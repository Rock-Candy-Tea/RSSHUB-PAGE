<p>普陀区甘泉路街道的黄陵路通过一系列精心综合整治与改造，已经从“小马路”更新为“网红街”，为人们所称赞，并获得“2020上海市户外招牌特色道路”荣誉。我们一起来看看吧↓</p><p><img src="http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2021/03/22/cde14df7a8211e831718fdb39a52da73.jpeg" data-size="900,562" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2021/03/22/083436482361c6067977931208464241.jpeg" data-size="900,600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>走在街上，你可以看到干净平整的路面，错落有致的景观绿化，格调一致又各具特色的店铺招牌以及独具特色的街边彩绘。</p><p><img src="http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2021/03/22/d6fdfded6b54d5edc72a63eda406a74e.jpeg" data-size="900,600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2021/03/22/abfe8c1c8a1be5e6f4d3483e4b7bf7d8.jpeg" data-size="892,594" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2021/03/22/382e6c1feb1f14b329140902c667261b.jpeg" data-size="900,600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>为了更好地增添城市的诗情画意，黄陵路街道将原本种植的香椿树全部换成了银杏树。原本的树木较为单调无法形成景观特色而改种银杏树待到深秋时节，两旁变黄的银杏将成为一道亮丽的风景线。</p><p><img src="http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2021/03/22/1b34193cab76a3bac6e945b536078a23.jpeg" data-size="900,584" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2021/03/22/834c627884f4dd574452e7c660700746.jpeg" data-size="900,600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2021/03/22/ee8c3ef7dfc14da5ada32e57aea89d65.jpeg" data-size="900,601" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2021/03/22/3fd1813207f580fba58fb71f22105372.jpeg" data-size="900,584" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2021/03/22/0b4595e4c1f8640206b5c4073c31c8b6.gif" data-size="1079,719" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
