<p class="p3">3 <span class="s1">月</span> 11 <span class="s1">日晚，微信</span> for Mac <span class="s1">突然发布</span> 3.0.0 <span class="s1">内测版。</span></p>
<p class="p4">相比之前不痛不痒的功能，这次的更新十分「有料」，不仅适配了深色模式，还增加了最为关键的朋友圈浏览功能。</p>
<p class="p4">接下来，就跟着我们共同体验一番。<span class="s2"><b>（</b></span><span class="s3">🔗</span><b> </b><span class="s2"><b>文末有安装包）</b></span></p>
<p class="p5"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1405385" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/image.png!720" alt width="1100" height="820" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/image.png!720 1100w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/image-360x268.png 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/image-768x573.png 768w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/image-1024x763.png 1024w" sizes="(max-width: 1100px) 100vw, 1100px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h3 class="p6"><span class="s5"><b>懒人目录</b></span></h3>
<ol class="ol1">
<li class="li7"><span class="s7">可以浏览朋友圈</span></li>
<li class="li7"><span class="s7">可以浏览聊天中分享的视频号视频和视频号直播</span></li>
<li class="li5"><span class="s8">在</span><span class="s7"> OS X 10.14 </span><span class="s8">版本及以上系统支持深色模式</span></li>
<li class="li7"><span class="s7">可以打开小程序（除小游戏外）</span></li>
<li class="li7"><span class="s7">可以进行群直播</span></li>
<li class="li7"><span class="s7">可以将公众号推文等链接添加到浮窗</span></li>
</ol>
<h3><span class="s10"><b>1. 可以浏览朋友圈</b></span></h3>
<p class="p4">上班摸鱼不用再打开手机了！有了微信<span class="s6"> for Mac 3.0.0 </span>内测版，电脑就能刷朋友圈。</p>
<p class="p4">点击微信左栏的「朋友圈」图标，即可打开一个独立的朋友圈窗口。</p>
<p class="p5"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1405374" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/100.jpg!720" alt width="928" height="954" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/100.jpg!720 928w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/100-360x370.jpg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/100-768x790.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 928px) 100vw, 928px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="p4">在这个独立窗口中，能够正常点赞、评论、查看消息，同时支持刷新。</p>
<p class="p2">窗口可以拉伸长度，宽度固定。</p>
<p class="p4">不过目前这个测试版中，你只能查看自己的朋友圈时间线，无法点击微信头像进入个人朋友圈，甚至不能发布动态。</p>
<p class="p5"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1405380" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/1-4.gif" alt width="1062" height="805" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/1-4.gif 1062w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/1-4-360x273.gif 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/1-4-768x582.gif 768w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/1-4-1024x776.gif 1024w" sizes="(max-width: 1062px) 100vw, 1062px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="p4">朋友圈内的图片<span class="s6">/</span>视频支持点击预览，可缩放、旋转、收藏、存储、复制、共享，但无法识别图片二维码。</p>
<p class="p2">值得一提的是，微信<span class="s6"> for Mac 3.0.0 </span>内测版支持在朋友圈打开视频号内容，包括视频号直播，直播和视频号会分别存在于独立窗口，互不影响。</p>
<p class="p4">视频号直播支持分享、旋转、放大、置顶，无法评论点赞，操作体验非常不错。</p>
<p class="p5"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1405376" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/3o44.jpg!720" alt width="1784" height="1192" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/3o44.jpg!720 1784w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/3o44-360x241.jpg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/3o44-768x513.jpg 768w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/3o44-1024x684.jpg 1024w" sizes="(max-width: 1784px) 100vw, 1784px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h3><b>2. 可以浏览聊天中分享的视频号视频和视频号直播</b></h3>
<p class="p4">除了朋友圈，微信<span class="s6"> for Mac 3.0.0 </span>内测版中，聊天窗口也可以查看视频号内容和视频号直播了。</p>
<p class="p5"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1405372" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/00.jpg!720" alt width="1337" height="972" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/00.jpg!720 1337w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/00-360x262.jpg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/00-768x558.jpg 768w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/00-1024x744.jpg 1024w" sizes="(max-width: 1337px) 100vw, 1337px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="p4">同样，视频号直播支持分享、旋转、放大、置顶，无法评论点赞；视频号内容只支持拉进度条、关闭浮评、分享、投诉，无法评论点赞，甚至无法看到完整的评论区。</p>
<p class="p5"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1405379" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/2-1.gif" alt width="724" height="803" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/2-1.gif 724w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/2-1-360x399.gif 360w" sizes="(max-width: 724px) 100vw, 724px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="p4">需要说明的是，视频号名片不支持在聊天窗口分享，点击视频号内的头像也无法跳转到博主首页，你能做的，只是观看朋友分享过来的内容。</p>
<h3><b></b><span class="s10"><b>3. 在 OS X 10.14 版本及以上系统支持深色模式</b></span><span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span></h3>
<p class="p4">除了功能更新，<span class="s6">Mac </span>版微信的外观也有了变化。</p>
<p class="p4">首先，它的图标更「macOS Big Sur」了，圆角矩形的全新设计，在一众符合新系统风格的 app 中显得非常和谐。</p>
<p class="p2"><span class="Apple-converted-space"> <img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1405386" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/logo.jpg!720" alt width="1000" height="357" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/logo.jpg!720 1000w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/logo-360x129.jpg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/logo-768x274.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p class="p4">其次，最为直观的变化是，我们可以通过左下角的菜单进入设置，在「通用设置」中自定义深色或浅色外观了。<span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span></p>
<p class="p5"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1405377" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/1300.jpg!720" alt width="931" height="911" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/1300.jpg!720 931w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/1300-360x352.jpg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/1300-768x752.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 931px) 100vw, 931px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="p4">你也可以让外观跟随系统设置，随着夜色降临披上深色外衣。</p>
<p class="p5"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1405387" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/1345.gif" alt width="640" height="495" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/1345.gif 640w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/1345-360x278.gif 360w" sizes="(max-width: 640px) 100vw, 640px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h3><b></b><span class="s10"><b>4. 可以打开小程序（除小游戏外）</b></span><span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span></h3>
<p class="p4">使用桌面端微信的朋友，一定没少见到这句话：「当前版本不支持，请在手机上查看」。</p>
<p class="p4">这是因为微信<span class="s6"> for Mac </span>正式版尚未支持小程序，而在微信<span class="s6"> for Mac 3.0.0 </span>内测版中，查看小程序已经不是问题了。<span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span></p>
<p class="p5"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1405371" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/0.jpg!720" alt width="830" height="888" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/0.jpg!720 830w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/0-360x385.jpg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/0-768x822.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 830px) 100vw, 830px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="p3"><span class="s1">这个功能并非</span> 3.0.0 <span class="s1">才有，早在</span> 2020 <span class="s1">年</span> 1 <span class="s1">月，微信</span> for Mac <span class="s1">的测试版中就已经可以使用小程序了，我们也对<a href="https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s/93pHo9GVOR2yTOUzyb2Dtw">相关功能</a>进行了测试。</span><span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span></p>
<p class="p4">在之前的测试中，我们发现微信<span class="s6"> for Mac </span>暂不支持小游戏的分享，到了微信<span class="s6"> for Mac 3.0.0 </span>内测版，小游戏依然无法在桌面端打开。</p>
<h3 class="p4"><b>5. 可以进行群直播</b></h3>
<p class="p2">在微信<span class="s6"> for Mac 3.0.0 </span>内测版中，还加入了群直播的功能。</p>
<p class="p2">在输入框上方左侧你可以看到一个圆环图标，它就是群直播的入口了。不过此项功能还在灰度中，并不支持所以群聊，即使在最新的微信 for Mac 3.0.0 内测版中，一些群聊也尚未加入此功能。</p>
<p class="p2"><span class="Apple-converted-space"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1405389" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/12-1.png!720" alt width="1000" height="632" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/12-1.png!720 1000w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/12-1-360x228.png 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/12-1-768x485.png 768w" sizes="(max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p class="p2">点击直播按钮，同意直播使用条款后你就可以发起直播了，直播可以通过三种形式进行：语音、视频、投屏（如下图）。</p>
<p class="p5"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1405390" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/123.png!720" alt width="926" height="907" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/123.png!720 926w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/123-360x353.png 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/123-768x752.png 768w" sizes="(max-width: 926px) 100vw, 926px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="p5">在直播的过程中，可以非常便捷地切换语音、视频或者投屏模式，而观众也可以通过连麦、评论等形式与主播进行互动。</p>
<p class="p2"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1405392" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/42321.gif" alt width="640" height="360" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/42321.gif 640w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/42321-360x203.gif 360w" sizes="(max-width: 640px) 100vw, 640px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="p4">这项功能可以在群内方便的进行视频或语音会议、软件教学等工作。同时，微信群直播不支持分享，也就是说，除了群成员，没有人可以通过链接进入直播，也保证了直播的私密性。</p>
<h3><b></b><span class="s10"><b>6. 可以将公众号推文等链接添加到浮窗</b></span></h3>
<p class="p4">在手机上，你可以通过把公众号文章等链接，以拖动到屏幕边缘的方式暂时存储在浮窗中。</p>
<p class="p4">而在微信<span class="s6"> for Mac 3.0.0 </span>测试版里，你也可以通过点击文章右上角的「浮窗」按钮，将文章收纳进浮窗了。</p>
<p class="p5"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1405378" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/4345.jpg!720" alt width="906" height="868" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/4345.jpg!720 906w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/4345-360x345.jpg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/4345-768x736.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 906px) 100vw, 906px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="p4">加入浮窗后，你可以在微信<span class="s6"> for Mac 3.0.0 </span>测试版左下角的手机图标里找到这篇文章，值得一提的是，手机端的浮窗里也会同步显示文章。</p>
<p class="p5"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1405393" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/12345.gif" alt width="700" height="448" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/12345.gif 700w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/12345-360x230.gif 360w" sizes="(max-width: 700px) 100vw, 700px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="p4">以上就是我们在微信<span class="s6"> for Mac 3.0.0 </span>测试版中体验到的新功能，如果你还有其他发现，欢迎在留言区告诉我们。</p>
