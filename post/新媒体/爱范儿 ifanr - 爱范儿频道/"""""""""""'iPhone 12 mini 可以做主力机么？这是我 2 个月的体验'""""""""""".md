<div>   
<p class="p3"><span class="s2">近日，来自数据机构</span> CIRP <span class="s2">的一份报告显示，</span>2020 <span class="s2">年</span> 10 <span class="s2">月</span>-11 <span class="s2">月的美国市场，</span>iPhone 12 mini <span class="s2">仅占</span> iPhone 12 <span class="s2">系列总销量的</span> 6%<span class="s2">。在</span> iPhone 12 <span class="s2">发布会之前，最受关注的机型是</span> iPhone 12 mini<span class="s2">；在</span> iPhone 12 <span class="s2">发布会后，销量最差的也是</span> iPhone 12 mini<span class="s2">。说好的出小屏就买爆呢？</span></p>
<p class="p3"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1396150" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/1-4.jpg!720" alt width="1000" height="667" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/1-4.jpg!720 1000w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/1-4-360x240.jpg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/1-4-768x512.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="p1">喊了多年「苹果出小屏旗舰一定买」的我，在第一时间购入了<span class="s1"> iPhone 12 mini</span>，并当作主力机使用至今。接下来就用我两个月的使用体验，来告诉你这款在商业上几乎注定「失败」的小屏旗舰，到底好不好用。</p>
<h3 class="p1">单卡续航短，这些硬伤怎么办？</h3>
<p class="p1">「可惜它不支持双卡」和「可惜它续航太差」，让无数喜欢小屏手机的用户望而却步，但<span class="s1"> iPhone 12 mini </span>的这些硬伤，并不足以让所有人放弃它。作为双卡用户，我也很遗憾它只是一款单卡手机，我只好把副卡留在旧手机里用来接受验证码，虽说没有双卡方便，但也不至于影响工作生活。</p>
<p class="p1"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1396151" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/2-1.jpeg!720" alt width="1000" height="750" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/2-1.jpeg!720 1000w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/2-1-360x270.jpeg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/2-1-768x576.jpeg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="p1">限于机身尺寸，<span class="s1">iPhone 12 mini </span>的电池容量是系列最小的，续航的确比较差。使用<span class="s1"> iPhone 12 mini </span>的第一天，我故意不充电，正常刷微博、看视频、拍照片、玩游戏，从早<span class="s1"> 8 </span>点<span class="s1"> 100% </span>电量出门到晚<span class="s1"> 7 </span>点电量用完关机，虽然有些勉强，但它的续航还是可以满足我一整天使用需求的。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1396152" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/3.jpeg!720" alt width="1000" height="1354" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/3.jpeg!720 1000w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/3-360x487.jpeg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/3-768x1040.jpeg 768w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/3-756x1024.jpeg 756w" sizes="(max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<div class="editor-image-source">
<p>▲ 18:58 <span class="s2">分，</span>mini <span class="s2">彻底没电</span></p>
</div>
<p class="p1">而作为上班族，每天坐在格子间里办公，其实我的手机几乎不会离开无线充电板，下班回家，不用手机时它也静静地躺在充电板上。你以为<span class="s1"> iPhone 12 mini </span>的手机电量撑不够一天，实际上，使用一天到睡前依然保持<span class="s1"> 100% </span>电量才是常态。</p>
<p class="p1"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-1396153" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/4-6.jpg!720" alt width="1019" height="1820" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/4-6.jpg!720 1457w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/4-6-360x643.jpg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/4-6-768x1372.jpg 768w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/4-6-573x1024.jpg 573w" sizes="(max-width: 1019px) 100vw, 1019px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="p1">虽然会遇到需要外出拍摄一整天的情况，但即使之前使用大屏大电量的手机，我也会随身携带移动电源，换为<span class="s1"> iPhone 12 mini </span>后，手机<span class="s1">+</span>移动电源的出行负担反而因为<span class="s1"> iPhone 12 mini </span>的轻便而小了不少。</p>
<p class="p1">单卡我不在乎，续航几乎不是问题，关于<span class="s1"> iPhone 12 mini</span>，我体验到的只有它小巧的机身和强大的性能所带来的出色手感和迅捷表现。</p>
<h3 class="p3">mini <span class="s2">的手机，</span>Max <span class="s2">的生活</span></h3>
<p class="p1">在使用<span class="s1"> iPhone 12 mini </span>前，我一直担心小屏幕显示的内容会比大屏幕少很多，从而导致我获取信息的效率降低，实际进行对比后，发现在我们常用的一些界面中，<span class="s1">iPhone 12 mini </span>显示的内容的确要比大屏手机少一两行。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1396252" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/IMG_9CA7B547D30F-1.jpg!720" alt width="1000" height="1124" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/IMG_9CA7B547D30F-1.jpg!720 1000w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/IMG_9CA7B547D30F-1-360x405.jpg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/IMG_9CA7B547D30F-1-768x863.jpg 768w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/IMG_9CA7B547D30F-1-911x1024.jpg 911w" sizes="(max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<div class="editor-image-source">
<p>▲ <span class="s2">微博，左为</span> iPhone 12 mini<span class="s2">，右为</span> iPhone 11 Pro Max</p>
</div>
<p class="p1"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1396155" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/6-5.jpg!720" alt width="1000" height="1124" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/6-5.jpg!720 1000w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/6-5-360x405.jpg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/6-5-768x863.jpg 768w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/6-5-911x1024.jpg 911w" sizes="(max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>

<p class="p1">内容的缺失，会降低阅读文章的效率么？其实，在我们使用手机时，除了模拟真书的阅读类<span class="s1"> app</span>。并不会从屏幕的顶部一直看到底部再翻页，而是不断地滑动，眼睛看的，只是屏幕中间区域。虽然<span class="s1"> iPhone 12 mini </span>的小屏幕让每行的字数少了几个，但几乎没有对我产生什么影响。</p>
<p class="p3"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1396156" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/7-2.gif" alt width="800" height="1733" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/7-2.gif 800w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/7-2-360x780.gif 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/7-2-768x1664.gif 768w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/7-2-473x1024.gif 473w" sizes="(max-width: 800px) 100vw, 800px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<div class="editor-image-source">
<p>▲ 阅读时，我们只会盯着屏幕中间的三五行</p>
</div>
<p class="p1">相比阅读，游戏体验的降级才是最实在的。现在的流行游戏，早已不像多年前《水果忍者》《神庙逃亡》那样，只需滑动屏幕就可以轻松闯关。以我经常玩的《王者荣耀》为例，游戏中一个界面里可以操作的按钮超过<span class="s1"> 20 </span>个，再加上各种伤害、控制、信号的标示，<span class="s1">iPhone 12 mini </span>又不像<span class="s1"> iPhone 8 Plus </span>那样可以用额头和下巴支撑，实际游玩时，小小的屏幕着实没剩下多少视野。</p>
<p class="p1"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1396157" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/8-2.jpg!720" alt width="1000" height="667" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/8-2.jpg!720 1000w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/8-2-360x240.jpg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/8-2-768x512.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<div class="editor-image-source">
<p>▲ 看来这个赛季的王者路会漫长一些</p>
</div>
<p class="p1">可除了游戏，<span class="s1">iPhone 12 mini </span>在其他方面的体验几乎全都超出了预期。</p>
<p class="p1">通勤路上，<span class="s1">iPhone 12 mini </span>单手操作毫无压力，全键盘输入虽然有时会出现误触的情况，不过在拥挤的地铁里，滑动输入还是要比双手输入要便捷许多；睡前在床上玩手机时，<span class="s1">133 </span>克的重量并不会给我太大压力，即使砸到脸上，比起之前动辄半斤的大屏手机，也没那么疼；在跑步时，把它放在裤子口袋中并不会感觉下坠，随身携带轻松了不少<span class="s1">……</span></p>
<p class="p1">随着行业的发展，手机的屏幕越来越大、功能越来越多，变成了一个全能的「机」器，可<span class="s1"> iPhone 12 mini </span>在做到尽可能全能的同时，还照顾到了「手机」里「手」的部分。</p>
<p class="p1"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1396158" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/9-3.jpg!720" alt width="1000" height="563" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/9-3.jpg!720 1000w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/9-3-360x203.jpg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/9-3-768x432.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<div class="editor-image-source">
<p>▲ 上一个可以这样拿起来的手机是哪款呢？</p>
</div>
<p class="p1">你每天有多少时间在使用手机呢？问了问身边的同事，大家每天使用手机的时长都在<span class="s1"> 7</span>～<span class="s1">8 </span>个小时，而在使用了一段时间<span class="s1"> iPhone 12 mini </span>后，我使用手机的平均时长基本维持在<span class="s1"> 4 </span>个小时左右。</p>
<p class="p1"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1396159" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/10-2.jpeg!720" alt width="1000" height="1790" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/10-2.jpeg!720 1000w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/10-2-360x644.jpeg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/10-2-768x1375.jpeg 768w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/10-2-572x1024.jpeg 572w" sizes="(max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="p1">不得不承认，大屏可以带来更好的视听和游戏体验，小屏幕的<span class="s1"> iPhone 12 mini </span>让我对玩手机的兴趣降低了一个档次。比如之前经常会在饭后和同事们开黑玩一局《王者荣耀》，因为<span class="s1"> iPhone 12 mini </span>的屏幕太小，慢慢便失去了兴趣<span class="s1">——</span>我把本来玩游戏的时间，交给了电纸书。</p>
<p class="p3"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1396160" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/11-2.jpg!720" alt width="1000" height="750" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/11-2.jpg!720 1000w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/11-2-360x270.jpg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/11-2-768x576.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="p1">慢慢的，吃饭时追剧变成了听歌，上下班刷微博变成了听播客<span class="s1">——</span>这和<span class="s1"> iPhone 12 mini </span>屏幕承载信息多寡无关，和电量适不适合看视频玩游戏无关。只是在玩手机的时间变短后，看书、健身、听播客，我发现了生活中更多有趣的事情。</p>
<h3 class="p1"><span class="s1">mini </span>好用，但它适合你么？</h3>
<p class="p1">可能看完我的描述，你心中那个关于小屏的梦又发了新芽。虽然我很喜欢<span class="s1"> iPhone 12 mini </span>这款机器，同时也因它改变了一些生活的习惯，但<span class="s1"> iPhone 12 mini</span>，可能更适合下面提到的这几类人群。</p>
<p class="p3"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1396161" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/12-2.png!720" alt width="1000" height="499" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/12-2.png!720 1000w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/12-2-360x180.png 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/12-2-768x383.png 768w" sizes="(max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="p1">首先就是像我这样的上班族，日常可以随时充电，除了游戏时按键稍微有些局促，<span class="s1">iPhone 12 mini </span>的日常体验还是非常出色的。如果你是需要兼顾工作和生活的双机党，<span class="s1">iPhone 12 mini </span>也是工作机和测试机的优选。</p>
<p class="p1">其次就是学生党了，每天时间紧、课业压力大，续航短反而成了优点：快去学习！不然没到宿舍手机就没电啦！</p>
<p class="p1">最后就是「真正的」手机用户：打电话、聊微信，不太玩游戏、不太拍照片，就想购买一款轻便的通信工具，那<span class="s1"> iPhone 12 mini </span>一定很适合你。</p>
<p class="p3"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1396162" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/13.png!720" alt width="1000" height="562" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/13.png!720 1000w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/13-360x202.png 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/13-768x432.png 768w" sizes="(max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="p1">不要因为想少玩游戏、想少刷视频而购买<span class="s1"> iPhone 12 mini</span>，就像不要因为想要多看书而购买<span class="s1"> Kindle </span>一样，「少玩手机」是你的自我约束，手机可没这个功能。</p>
<h3 class="p1">即便大势所趋，小屏依然精彩</h3>
<p class="p1">「再怎么夸，它也不过是曲曲<span class="s1"> 6% </span>小屏用户的自嗨罢了」<span class="s1">——</span>小屏旗舰，真的有市场么？在回答这个问题之前，我要先问另一个问题：「对你来说，多小的屏幕算小屏呢？」</p>
<p class="p3">iPhone 8 Plus <span class="s2">的长度是</span> 158.4mm<span class="s2">，而</span> iPhone 12 <span class="s2">的长度是</span> 146.7mm<span class="s2">，即使拥有</span> 6.1 <span class="s2">寸大屏，</span>iPhone 12 <span class="s2">也比</span> 5.5 <span class="s2">寸屏幕的</span> iPhone 8 Plus <span class="s2">更迷你。与其说我们追求的是更小的屏幕，不如说我们追求的是更轻便的握持手感，比</span> iPhone 8 Plus <span class="s2">更小的</span> iPhone 12<span class="s2">，已经可以满足很多人对「小屏幕」的需求了。</span></p>
<p class="p3"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1396163" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/14-1.jpg!720" alt width="1000" height="563" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/14-1.jpg!720 1000w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/14-1-360x203.jpg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/14-1-768x432.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="p1">单卡和续航短是许多「被迫」购买<span class="s1"> iPhone 12 </span>用户的说辞，但最主要的原因，可能还是价格。首发价<span class="s1"> 6499 </span>元的<span class="s1"> iPhone XR</span>，边框宽、屏幕差、信号烂，<span class="s1">4999 </span>的<span class="s1"> iPhone XR</span>，买买买。相比<span class="s1"> iPhone 12</span>，少了双卡、少了续航、小了屏幕，却仅有<span class="s1"> 24 </span>期分期下「四舍五入不要钱」的<span class="s1"> 800 </span>元差价，如果<span class="s1"> iPhone 12 mini </span>售价降到<span class="s1"> 3999 </span>元，可能「真香定理」就又会在它身上上演了。</p>
<p class="p1">手机承载着我们的生活、工作、娱乐，更大的屏幕不仅能带来更好的观感，也能让我们在处理问题、享受生活时更加游刃有余。可即便大势所趋，还是有许多人希望把自己从手机里解放出来。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1396164" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/15-1.png!720" alt width="1000" height="489" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/15-1.png!720 1000w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/15-1-360x176.png 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/15-1-768x376.png 768w" sizes="(max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="p3">从没有小屏旗舰时「出小屏旗舰必买爆」的发言到<span class="s1"> iPhone 12 mini </span>销量不佳时「要买爆的那些人在哪里呢？」的嘲讽，对立的言论组成了我们所处的网络环境，但却无法代表真实的用户。</p>
<p class="p1">即使只有<span class="s1"> 6% </span>的销量，小屏手机依然有<span class="s1"> 100% </span>的精彩。</p>
