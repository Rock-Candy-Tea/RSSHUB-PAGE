<div>   
<p class="p1">你使用过<span class="s1"> iPhone </span>的「捷径」吗？只需一次按键，就可以帮我们扫码拼图打电话，翻译阅读查快递。本来复杂的操作，通过《快捷指令》，一次点击、一句语音就可以轻松完成、一步直达。</p>
<p class="p1"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1394124" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/1-3.png!720" alt width="1000" height="690" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/1-3.png!720 1000w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/1-3-360x248.png 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/1-3-768x530.png 768w" sizes="(max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="p1">这样方便的操作并不只限于<span class="s1"> iPad </span>和<span class="s1"> iPhone</span>，<span class="s1">Apple Watch </span>也可以使用《快捷指令》，让本来繁琐的操作变得更加简单。</p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">Apple Watch</span>、《快捷指令》？难道可以让<span class="s1"> Apple Watch </span>帮我拼图查快递么？为什么不用手机？相信看到「在<span class="s1"> Apple Watch </span>上使用《快捷指令》」这这句话时，你的心中会一下子冒出许多疑问。接下来，就让我们一一解答。</p>
<p class="p3"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1394125" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/2.jpeg!720" alt width="1000" height="667" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/2.jpeg!720 1000w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/2-360x240.jpeg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/2-768x512.jpeg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h3 class="p3"><span class="s2">哪些快捷指令适合</span> Apple watch<span class="s2">？</span></h3>
<p class="p1">相比手机，<span class="s1">Apple Watch </span>不止是硬件上没有相机、屏幕更小，在软件上存在的问题更多。首先是<span class="s1"> app </span>的缺失，相比每周都会增加<span class="s1"> 10 </span>万的手机版<span class="s1"> App Store</span>，手表版<span class="s1"> App Store </span>里的<span class="s1"> app </span>在数量上差了不止一个数量级。</p>
<p class="p1"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1394126" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/3-4.jpg!720" alt width="1000" height="1074" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/3-4.jpg!720 1000w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/3-4-360x387.jpg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/3-4-768x825.jpg 768w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/3-4-953x1024.jpg 953w" sizes="(max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<div class="editor-image-source">
<p>▲ Apple Watch <span class="s2">商店（右）可以轻易拉到底</span></p>
</div>
<p class="p1">其次是<span class="s1"> app </span>的功能性，虽然我们常用的一些<span class="s1"> app </span>也都推出了<span class="s1"> Apple Watch </span>的版本，但功能方面差得很远。微信只能简单回复、微博只能看步数，支付宝功能相对全面一些，但也主要是展示二维码的功能（付款、乘车），转账还款还是需要在手机上操作。</p>
<p class="p3"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1394127" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/4-1.jpg!720" alt width="1000" height="546" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/4-1.jpg!720 1000w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/4-1-360x197.jpg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/4-1-768x419.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<div class="editor-image-source">
<p>▲ 微信、微博、支付宝</p>
</div>
<p class="p1">软硬件的缺失，让<span class="s1"> Apple Watch </span>在使用《快捷指令》时有了许多限制，所以在回答「<span class="s1">Apple Watch </span>适合哪些快捷指令」之前，想要搞清楚<span class="s1"> Apple Watch </span>可以做什么，翻阅苹果官网，我们可以快速了解到<span class="s1"> Apple Watch </span>的主要功能：</p>
<ul>
<li class="p1">信息查看提醒：时间、天气、<span class="s1">app </span>通知、健康信息等</li>
<li class="p1">体能训练：自带丰富的体能训练项目，精确记录训练情况</li>
<li class="p1">各类<span class="s1"> App</span>：电话短信、音乐播放、即时通信、待办备忘、定时闹钟等</li>
</ul>
<p class="p1">拨打电话、查询天气、添加备忘<span class="s1">……</span>相比在<span class="s1"> Apple Watch </span>的小屏幕上滑动点击，快捷指令只需一步就可以实现一整套操作<span class="s1">——</span>适合<span class="s1"> Apple Watch </span>的快捷指令，就在上面这些功能点中。</p>
<h3 class="p1">这些「捷径」值得一试</h3>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">Apple Watch </span>会同步<span class="s1"> iPhone </span>上的指令，但可以显示不代表可以使用。比如拼图、扫码类的指令是无法正常在<span class="s1"> Apple Watch </span>上运行的。接下来为大家介绍几款适合在<span class="s1"> Apple Watch </span>上使用的快捷指令。</p>
<ul>
<li class="p1">自动发短信</li>
<li>刷牙计时器</li>
<li>朗读刷牙流程</li>
<li>洗手计时器</li>
<li>听歌识曲</li>
<li>播放 Apple Music 列表</li>
<li>控制智能家居</li>
<li>洗衣计时器</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>通讯必备：发送短信，一步直达</strong></p>
<p class="p1">从<span class="s1"> Apple Watch S3 </span>开始，如果你的城市支持<span class="s1"> eSIM </span>卡，就可以使用<span class="s1"> Apple Watch </span>打电话、发短信。不过在<span class="s1"> Apple Watch </span>的小屏幕上拨打电话和发送短信的操作都略显繁琐，使用《快捷指令》就会方便很多。</p>
<p class="p1">当然，拨打电话可以把联系人添加到表盘中，需要通话时一步直达。但习惯给好友发短信的话，新建一个快捷指令，把「发送信息」的联系人设置成<span class="s1"> TA</span>，发送短信就会方便许多。</p>
<p class="p1"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1394129" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/6-1.jpg!720" alt width="1000" height="713" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/6-1.jpg!720 1000w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/6-1-360x257.jpg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/6-1-768x548.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="p1">如果你经常遇到家人或者男（女）友在上班时打电话给你的情况，也可以直接设置一个发送「晚点回电」到指定联系人的快捷指令，一键让<span class="s1"> TA </span>知道现在不适合通话。</p>
<p class="p1"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1394130" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/7.jpg!720" alt width="1000" height="712" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/7.jpg!720 1000w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/7-360x256.jpg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/7-768x547.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1394131" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/8-1.gif" alt width="640" height="360" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/8-1.gif 640w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/8-1-360x203.gif 360w" sizes="(max-width: 640px) 100vw, 640px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<ul>
<li class="p1">洗漱必备：认真刷牙，有它就行</li>
</ul>
<p class="p1">我们都知道刷牙要<span class="s1"> 3 </span>分钟，但到了刷牙时，却总在「差不多行了」的心态中刷不够时间。如果你想认真刷牙，那「朗读刷牙流程」和「刷牙计时器」这两个快捷指令值得一试。</p>
<p class="p1"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1394132" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/9.jpg!720" alt width="1000" height="1074" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/9.jpg!720 1000w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/9-360x387.jpg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/9-768x825.jpg 768w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/9-953x1024.jpg 953w" sizes="(max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="p1">「朗读刷牙流程」非常直观，从「拿出牙刷牙膏」到「以打圈的方式刷牙」再到「擦干脸和手」，跟着它你可以非常顺利且按时的完成整个刷牙过程。「刷牙计时器」就简单许多了，它会在刷牙进行的时间中用通知和提示音来提醒你是否刷够时间。</p>
<p class="p3"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1394133" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/10.jpg!720" alt width="1000" height="821" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/10.jpg!720 1000w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/10-360x296.jpg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/10-768x631.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<div class="editor-image-source">
<p>▲「刷牙计时器」快捷指令</p>
</div>
<p>健康生活当然不止刷牙，watchOS 7 更新后，增加了一个监控洗手的功能，当你洗手时，Apple Watch 会自动开启一个 20 秒的倒计时，监督你的洗手时长。不过，Apple Watch 识别洗手并不准确，有时洗手很久都没有提示，有时没有洗手却问你是否在洗手。这时，你就可以在快捷指令中开启「洗手倒计时」，只需一点，Apple Watch 就会自动开启一个 20 秒的倒计时，手表会通过声音和震动来提醒你已经洗够了 20 秒。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1396186" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/a-1.jpg!720" alt width="1000" height="794" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/a-1.jpg!720 1000w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/a-1-360x286.jpg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/a-1-768x610.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<ul>
<li class="p1">听歌必备：听音识曲，快速播放</li>
</ul>
<p class="p1">《<span class="s1">Shazam</span>》是一款用于识别音频的<span class="s1"> app</span>，当你听到一首歌曲时，可以通过这款<span class="s1"> app </span>来联网快速识别，获取歌曲的各项信息。在<span class="s1"> Apple Watch </span>上，即使你不下载这款<span class="s1"> app</span>，也可以通过「<span class="s1">Shazam shortcut</span>」这个快捷指令来听歌识曲。</p>
<p class="p3"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1394134" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/11-1.gif" alt width="640" height="360" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/11-1.gif 640w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/11-1-360x203.gif 360w" sizes="(max-width: 640px) 100vw, 640px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="p1">如果你使用<span class="s1"> Apple Music</span>，还可以使用「播放播放列表」这个快捷指令，通过手表控制<span class="s1"> iPhone </span>播放你设定好的播放列表。很适合开车或者骑行中不方便直接操作手机时使用。</p>
<p class="p3"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1394135" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/12-1.gif" alt width="640" height="360" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/12-1.gif 640w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/12-1-360x203.gif 360w" sizes="(max-width: 640px) 100vw, 640px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<ul>
<li class="p1">生活必备：开灯关灯，洗衣定时</li>
</ul>
<p class="p1">智能家居已经不是什么小众物品，我们同样可以使用<span class="s1"> Apple Watch </span>的快捷指令来控制。以控制米家电暖器为例，打开《米家》<span class="s1">app</span>，在场景中选择「<span class="s1">+></span>手动执行<span class="s1">></span>电暖器<span class="s1">></span>开机」。</p>
<p class="p3"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1394136" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/13-1.jpg!720" alt width="1000" height="713" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/13-1.jpg!720 1000w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/13-1-360x257.jpg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/13-1-768x548.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="p1">返回「场景」选择「添加到<span class="s1"> Siri></span>电暖器开」，再为这项操作添加一个唤醒语。这样，你不仅可以使用<span class="s1"> Siri </span>控制智能家居，还能在《快捷指令》<span class="s1">app </span>中看到它<span class="s1">——</span>只要在手表上点击这个指令，就能控制智能家居了。</p>
<p class="p3"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1394137" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/14.jpg!720" alt width="1000" height="715" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/14.jpg!720 1000w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/14-360x257.jpg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/14-768x549.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="p1">如果你在生活中会出现把衣服放到洗衣机里，却忘记把它们拿出来的问题，「洗衣计时器」这个快捷指令就是为你准备的。点击它，输入时间，《提醒事项》中会为你创建一个洗衣的待办。当设定的时间到了，<span class="s1">Apple Watch </span>的通知会告诉你衣服已经洗好了。</p>
<p class="p3"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1394138" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/15.jpeg!720" alt width="1000" height="1582" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/15.jpeg!720 1000w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/15-360x570.jpeg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/15-768x1215.jpeg 768w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/15-647x1024.jpeg 647w" sizes="(max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="p1">除了拨打电话、发送短信和控制智能家居，其他快捷指令均可在《快捷指令》<span class="s1">app </span>的「快捷指令中心」搜索到，想要尝试的朋友快去搜索添加吧。</p>
<p class="p3"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1394139" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/16.jpg!720" alt width="1000" height="713" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/16.jpg!720 1000w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/16-360x257.jpg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/16-768x548.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h3 class="p1">这样设置，让快捷指令更好用</h3>
<p class="p1">指令越来越多，在《快捷指令》里选择点击指令也会越来越繁琐，这就违背了我们使用快捷指令的初心。接下来教大家两个小方法，提高在<span class="s1"> Apple Watch </span>上使用《快捷指令》的效率。</p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">Apple Watch </span>上快捷指令的排序与<span class="s1"> iPhone </span>上一致，所以第一个方法就是在<span class="s1"> iPhone </span>上将经常使用的快捷指令移动到最顶端，这样，在<span class="s1"> Apple Watch </span>上打开《快捷指令》<span class="s1">app </span>时，常用些指令就会出现在最上方。</p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">Apple Watch </span>有非常丰富的表盘可供我们选择，其中有不少表盘可以添加复杂功能，比如「子午线」、「图文」等，我们可以将最常用的快捷指令添加到表盘上，也可以更高效的使用《快捷指令》。</p>
<p class="p1"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1394140" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/17.jpg!720" alt width="999" height="712" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/17.jpg!720 999w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/17-360x257.jpg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/17-768x547.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 999px) 100vw, 999px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="p1">从<span class="s1"> iOS 13 </span>开始，《快捷指令》成为了不少朋友的效率神器，而在<span class="s1"> Apple Watch </span>上，它也可以有效的提高你的工作、生活效率，让你拥有更加智能、便捷的使用体验。从沟通到娱乐，从生活到工作，<span class="s1">Apple Watch </span>上的快捷指令一定会在某个时间帮助到你。</p>
