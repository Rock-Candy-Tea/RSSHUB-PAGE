<p class="p1">如果问我最期待苹果的哪款新品，那一定是<span class="s1"> iPad Pro</span>。</p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">2015 </span>年，第一代<span class="s1"> iPad Pro </span>为我们带来了<span class="s1"> Apple Pencil</span>；<span class="s1">2018 </span>年，全面屏设计的它再度惊艳全场。苹果总会在<span class="s1"> iPad Pro </span>上为我们带来不一样的体验。</p>
<p class="p3"><span class="s2">而首发在</span> iPad Pro <span class="s2">上的技术，也会逐渐应用到苹果的其他产品线上，</span>2020 <span class="s2">款</span> iPad Pro <span class="s2">上新增加的激光雷达传感器，成为了</span> iPhone 12 Pro <span class="s2">系列上的新功能，而曾经</span> iPad Pro <span class="s2">专属的</span> Apple Pencil<span class="s2">，现在也成了</span> iPad 系列产品<span class="s2">的必备。</span></p>
<p class="p3"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1401384" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1-6.jpg!720" alt width="1000" height="576" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1-6.jpg!720 1000w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1-6-360x207.jpg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1-6-768x442.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="p1">等等，必备？我写字又丑又不会画画，<span class="s1">Apple Pencil </span>在我手里可没法妙笔生花，买回来也不过吃灰罢了</p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">——</span>相信许多人会有这样的想法，不过，如果你掌握了下面几个小技巧和<span class="s1"> app</span>，即使不擅长写写画画，<span class="s1">Apple Pencil </span>也能为你创造出价值。</p>

<h3 class="p3">iPadOS 14 <span class="s2">升级，你的笔还能这么用</span></h3>
<p class="p1">不用来写字画画，<span class="s1">Apple Pencil </span>还能做什么？</p>
<p class="p1">如果是更新系统之前的<span class="s1"> iPad</span>，<span class="s1">Apple Pencil </span>可以让你打开<span class="s1"> app </span>更快速、拖动进度条更精准，而最有作用的功能，也不过是从左下角划入屏幕，截取当前画面而已。</p>
<p class="p3"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1401385" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/2.gif" alt width="640" height="360" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/2.gif 640w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/2-360x203.gif 360w" sizes="(max-width: 640px) 100vw, 640px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="p1">而<span class="s1"> iPad </span>升级到最新的<span class="s1"> iPadOS 14 </span>后，全新的「随手写」功能，一定会让对书写绘画没有自信的你，重新爱上<span class="s1"> Apple Pencil</span>。</p>
<p class="p1">简单来说，随手写就是将你手写的文字转化为可编辑的文本，<span class="s1">iPadOS 14 </span>对随手写功能的支持是「全局」，不论是备忘录还是浏览器，不论是搜索框还是聊天框，它都可以让你通过手写快速输入文本。随手写能识别的不仅是一笔一画的正楷，中文的一些简化、连笔也难不倒它。</p>
<p class="p1"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1401386" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/3-1.gif" alt width="640" height="360" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/3-1.gif 640w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/3-1-360x203.gif 360w" sizes="(max-width: 640px) 100vw, 640px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="p1">输入好了文字，要如何修改呢？随手写提供了删除、选定、插入等多种修改方法。划掉就是删除，框起来就是选定，这些修改方法不仅实用，而且非常直观。</p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1401387" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/4-3.gif" alt width="640" height="360" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/4-3.gif 640w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/4-3-360x203.gif 360w" sizes="(max-width: 640px) 100vw, 640px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p class="p1">除了文字输入，<span class="s1">iPadOS 14 </span>还对形状输入进行了一定的优化，如果你一直在纠结画线不直、画圆太扁，只需在绘制完成后停留片刻，你绘制的不规则形状就会变成标准形状了。</p>
<p class="p3"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1401388" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/4a.gif" alt width="640" height="360" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/4a.gif 640w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/4a-360x203.gif 360w" sizes="(max-width: 640px) 100vw, 640px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="p1">随手写、图形绘画等功能的加入，已经足够你使用<span class="s1"> iPad </span>来写会议记录、记笔记，甚至画简笔画了。学会这些功能，就是让<span class="s1"> Apple Pencil </span>告别吃灰的第一步。</p>
<h3 class="p3"><span class="s2">搭配系统</span> App<span class="s2">，玩转</span> Apple Pencil</h3>
<p class="p1">用<span class="s1"> Apple Pencil </span>点击屏幕，就可以快速打开备忘录，随时记录自己的所见所想<span class="s1">——</span>当朋友问我「<span class="s1">Apple Pencil </span>有什么用」的时候，这是我一定会用来展示的操作。</p>
<p class="p1">除了备忘录，还有不少自带<span class="s1"> app </span>针对<span class="s1"> Apple Pencil </span>进行了优化，比如照片、<span class="s1">iWork </span>办公套件，想要发挥这些系统<span class="s1"> app </span>的全部实力，那就拿起你的<span class="s1"> Apple Pencil </span>吧！</p>
<ul>
<li class="p3"><span class="s2">备忘录：发挥</span> Apple Pencil <span class="s2">的全部潜能</span></li>
</ul>
<p class="p1">笔要搭配什么使用呢？当然是本子啦！打开苹果官网，我们也可以看到使用<span class="s1"> Apple Pencil </span>制作出的精致笔记。</p>
<p class="p1"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1401389" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/5-5.png!720" alt width="1000" height="511" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/5-5.png!720 1000w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/5-5-360x184.png 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/5-5-768x392.png 768w" sizes="(max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="p1">除了前文提到的熄屏直接唤醒直接做笔记，随手写、图形识别等功能，都非常完美的适配了备忘录，上文中讲解功能的的所有图片，正是来自<span class="s1"> iPad </span>自带的备忘录。</p>
<p class="p1">如果你的是第二代<span class="s1"> Apple Pencil</span>，还可以轻点笔身，切换工具。在设置中，你可以选择「当前工具与橡皮擦」、「当前工具与上一个工具」、「调色板」三种不同的功能。</p>
<p class="p3"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1401390" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/6-6.jpg!720" alt width="1000" height="700" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/6-6.jpg!720 1000w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/6-6-360x252.jpg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/6-6-768x538.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<ul>
<li class="p1">照片：快速截图，精确标注</li>
</ul>
<p class="p1">打开<span class="s1"> Safari</span>，浏览到一篇不错的文章，想要截图分享个朋友。这时，你只需使用<span class="s1"> Apple Pencil </span>从左下角向上划入屏幕，轻松获取当前页面的截图。</p>
<p class="p1">获取了截图不进行标注怎么让朋友快速看到重点内容呢？在框选、划线后稍加停顿，你的标注就会变成标准图形，不仅美观便捷，也更加准确实用。</p>
<p class="p1"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1401391" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/7-4.jpg!720" alt width="1000" height="1324" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/7-4.jpg!720 1000w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/7-4-360x477.jpg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/7-4-768x1017.jpg 768w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/7-4-773x1024.jpg 773w" sizes="(max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="p1">在工作生活中，难免遇到签订合同的情况，将合同的图片导入到照片里打开，点击编辑界面顶部的「<span class="s1">…</span>」，选择「标记」。点击下方的「<span class="s1">+</span>」，你就会看到「签名」标签了。</p>
<p class="p1">使用<span class="s1"> Apple Pencil </span>将名字写到这里，你就获得了一个可以改变大小、颜色、位置的签名图片，随时随地，方便签署各类文件。</p>
<p class="p1"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1401392" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/8-3.jpg!720" alt width="1000" height="711" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/8-3.jpg!720 1000w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/8-3-360x256.jpg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/8-3-768x546.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<ul>
<li class="p1"><span class="s1">iWork</span>：除了绘图，还能当作激光笔</li>
</ul>
<p class="p3"><span class="s2">作为苹果生态的办公三件套，在全新的</span> 10.0 <span class="s2">版本更新后，</span>Pages<span class="s2">、</span>Numbers <span class="s2">和</span> Keynote <span class="s2">这三款</span> app <span class="s2">全面支持了随手写功能，你可以轻松的使用</span> Apple Pencil <span class="s2">在</span> Pages <span class="s2">里撰写文稿，在</span> Numbers <span class="s2">里录入表格，在</span> Keynote <span class="s2">里绘制图形。</span></p>
<p class="p3"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1401393" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/9-3.jpg!720" alt width="1000" height="547" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/9-3.jpg!720 1000w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/9-3-360x197.jpg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/9-3-768x420.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<div class="editor-image-source">
<p>▲ Pages <span class="s2">的</span> Apple Pencil <span class="s2">设置栏</span></p>
</div>
<p class="p1">而在<span class="s1"> Keynote </span>上，还有一个专属功能，那就是激光笔。当我们进入<span class="s1"> Keynote </span>的演示模式后，除了标注，你可以点击下方的激光笔图标，把<span class="s1"> Apple Pencil </span>变成一个激光点，用来指示重点内容。在开会或者课堂上讲解演示文稿时，这个功能非常实用。</p>
<p class="p3"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1401394" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/10-1.gif" alt width="640" height="447" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/10-1.gif 640w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/10-1-360x251.gif 360w" sizes="(max-width: 640px) 100vw, 640px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h3 class="p1">标注、提醒、涂颜色，这些<span class="s1"> App </span>值得一试</h3>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">iPad </span>的系统<span class="s1"> app </span>针对<span class="s1"> Apple Pencil </span>进行了或多或少的适配，让我们在使用时游刃有余。那在第三方<span class="s1"> app </span>里，<span class="s1">Apple Pencil </span>难道只能用来拖动进度条么？</p>
<p class="p1">当然不是，在这里，我们介绍几款针对<span class="s1"> Apple Pencil </span>进行了适配的第三方<span class="s1"> app</span>，除了拖动进度条，<span class="s1">Apple Pencil </span>还能做这些事。</p>
<p class="p1"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1401395" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/11-5.png!720" alt width="1000" height="403" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/11-5.png!720 1000w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/11-5-360x145.png 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/11-5-768x310.png 768w" sizes="(max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<ul>
<li class="p3">Notability<span class="s2">：演示模式的激光笔</span></li>
</ul>
<p class="p1">在每一个「<span class="s1">iPad </span>必备的<span class="s1"> N </span>个<span class="s1"> app</span>」推荐中，<span class="s1">Notability </span>总会位列其中，作为笔记<span class="s1"> app</span>，从文字、图片到录音、绘图，它强大的功能几乎可以满足所有人的需求，那<span class="s1">……</span>你尝试过在外接显示屏时使用<span class="s1"> Notability </span>么？</p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1401396" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/12.jpeg!720" alt width="1000" height="750" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/12.jpeg!720 1000w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/12-360x270.jpeg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/12-768x576.jpeg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p class="p1">外接显示屏后，<span class="s1">Notability </span>就拥有了两个模式，一个是同步<span class="s1"> iPad </span>屏幕的编辑模式，与未外接显示屏时的体验一致。</p>
<p class="p1">另一种就是演示模式，这时，<span class="s1">Notability </span>可以在与<span class="s1"> iPad </span>屏幕比例不同的显示屏上全屏显示，而你手中的<span class="s1"> Apple Pencil </span>就变成了一只激光笔，可以在笔记中进行指示操作。</p>
<p class="p3"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1401397" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/13.gif" alt width="640" height="360" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/13.gif 640w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/13-360x203.gif 360w" sizes="(max-width: 640px) 100vw, 640px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<ul>
<li class="p1"><span class="s1">Lake</span>：简单治愈的涂色书</li>
</ul>
<p class="p1">当我们搜索「送女生的礼物」时，填色本总会占据一个推荐位置。而《<span class="s1">Lake</span>》就是<span class="s1"> iPad </span>上的填色本。释放创意、放松自我，填色就够了。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1401398" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/14-4.jpg!720" alt width="1000" height="667" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/14-4.jpg!720 1000w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/14-4-360x240.jpg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/14-4-768x512.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="p3">作为<span class="s1"> 2017 </span>年苹果设计大奖的得主，《<span class="s1">Lake</span>》提供了工具箱、定制调色板以及精美的绘本，为用户提供了来自世界各地艺术家的全新作品，不会画画，也能通过涂色来释放自己的创意。</p>
<p class="p3"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1401399" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/15-1.gif" alt width="640" height="447" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/15-1.gif 640w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/15-1-360x251.gif 360w" sizes="(max-width: 640px) 100vw, 640px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<ul>
<li class="p1"><span class="s1">MindNode</span>：如同纸张般的体验</li>
</ul>
<p class="p1">在<span class="s1"> Windows </span>平台上，有一款名为<span class="s1"> MindMaple Pen </span>的思维导图<span class="s1"> app</span>，可以使用<span class="s1"> Surface Pen </span>拖出分支，使用它就像在纸张上绘制思维导图一般。而拥有了随手写功能的《<span class="s1">MindNode</span>》，让<span class="s1"> Apple Pencil </span>也有了这样的体验。</p>
<p class="p3"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1401400" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/16.jpeg!720" alt width="1000" height="700" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/16.jpeg!720 1000w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/16-360x252.jpeg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/16-768x538.jpeg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="p1">双击进行编辑、拖动创建新节点，再搭配随手写功能，《<span class="s1">MindNode</span>》让思维导图的创建更加随心所欲。</p>
<p class="p3"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1401401" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/17-1.gif" alt width="640" height="360" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/17-1.gif 640w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/17-1-360x203.gif 360w" sizes="(max-width: 640px) 100vw, 640px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<ul>
<li class="p1">格志：随手写日记</li>
</ul>
<p class="p1">在<a href="https://www.ifanr.com/app/1399745">之前的文章</a>中，我们介绍过这款用格子写日记的<span class="s1"> app</span>，让记录一天的生活变得简单又快捷。支持多平台的《格志》针对不同的平台都进行了优化，比如在<span class="s1"> macOS </span>端优化了界面，比如在<span class="s1"> iPadOS </span>端支持了随手写。</p>
