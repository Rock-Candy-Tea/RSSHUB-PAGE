<p>随着欢乐的春节结束，一年一度的求职高峰期又来了，本想为过年后精神爽利的自己重新换上证件照附在简历上，但来到图文店门前，我犹豫了……</p>
<p>倒不是因为我担心影印照片的时间太长或者不想花钱，纯粹是尴尬癌又犯了：每次去拍照，尽管已经妆容完备、得体微笑，但拍摄的结果却总是和理想的自己有点差距，由于实在抹不开面子让老板 P 图，多数情况下只好拿着不满意的照片悻悻而归。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1402072" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/01.png!720" alt width="540" height="790" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/01.png!720 540w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/01-360x527.png 360w" sizes="(max-width: 540px) 100vw, 540px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>求人不如求己，家里也不是没有打印机，想要证件照为何不自己制作呢？</p>
<p>手机上的自助证件照制作工具其实不少，但大部分情况是我们摆好了架势照相，并费尽心思调了色调、背景去到导出照片一步才猛然弹出「付费去水印」的页面，而且一看价格也并不实惠。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1402603" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/2.8-1.jpg!720" alt width="1411" height="1444" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/2.8-1.jpg!720 1411w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/2.8-1-360x368.jpg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/2.8-1-768x786.jpg 768w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/2.8-1-1001x1024.jpg 1001w" sizes="(max-width: 1411px) 100vw, 1411px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>为服务花费本也合理，但多数工具并没有提前告知用户付费的情况，它们的付费窗口总是悄然躲在背后看着用户拍照、调整，等到即将大功告成时再及时地弹出来拦截，颇有种「要从此路过，留下买路财」的气势。</p>
<p>有没有好用又免费的良心证件照制作工具呢？这次为大家测试几款，工具的传送门放在了本文的末尾。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1402649" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/333-1.png!720" alt width="942" height="243" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/333-1.png!720 942w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/333-1-360x93.png 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/333-1-768x198.png 768w" sizes="(max-width: 942px) 100vw, 942px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>为了考察各款证件照制作工具的抠图能力，我特意选择了一张不是站在白墙前的小姐姐照片作为测试对象，以下便是各款工具的具体测评情况。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1402073" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1.0.jpg!720" alt width="489" height="637" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1.0.jpg!720 489w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1.0-360x469.jpg 360w" sizes="(max-width: 489px) 100vw, 489px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>

<h3>证件照编辑器</h3>
<p>推荐指数：★★★</p>
<p>这是一款微信上的小程序，无需额外下载 app 就可免费拍摄下载证件照，从小一寸到二寸，从四六级到签证，它能满足绝大多数证件照的需求。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1402074" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1.1.png!720" alt width="749" height="837" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1.1.png!720 749w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1.1-360x402.png 360w" sizes="(max-width: 749px) 100vw, 749px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>这款小程序对上传的照片要求比较高，过于复杂的背景将导致照片上传失败，因此建议选择在白背景下拍摄的照片。经检验，它还能检测出脸部倾斜和眯眼睛等照片不合格的问题。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1402075" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1.2.jpg!720" alt width="1080" height="1985" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1.2.jpg!720 1080w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1.2-360x662.jpg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1.2-768x1412.jpg 768w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1.2-557x1024.jpg 557w" sizes="(max-width: 1080px) 100vw, 1080px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>成功上传照片后就可以选择背景颜色，照片可以在小程序里保存 24 小时，观看视频后将开放下载。尽管免费，但证件照编辑器制作的寸照下载后清晰度不高、边缘较模糊。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1402076" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1.3.jpg!720" alt width="1790" height="1528" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1.3.jpg!720 1790w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1.3-360x307.jpg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1.3-768x656.jpg 768w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1.3-1024x874.jpg 1024w" sizes="(max-width: 1790px) 100vw, 1790px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h3></h3>
<h3>小米云证件照</h3>
<p>推荐指数：★★★★★</p>
<p>「小米云证件照」也是微信小程序，它页面无任何广告，程序界面干净清爽，支持超过 40 种证件照规格，还支持自定义尺寸，十分的人性化。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1402077" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1.4.png!720" alt width="799" height="811" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1.4.png!720 799w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1.4-360x365.png 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1.4-768x780.png 768w" sizes="(max-width: 799px) 100vw, 799px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>比起「证件照编辑器」，它可选择的背景色会少一些，只有红蓝白灰四种，但照片下载过程干脆利落，无需看视频就可以保存到本地。小米证件照的抠图效果不错，基本无白边、边缘清晰，人像位置适中。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1402078" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1.5.jpg!720" alt width="1072" height="2132" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1.5.jpg!720 1072w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1.5-360x716.jpg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1.5-768x1527.jpg 768w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1.5-515x1024.jpg 515w" sizes="(max-width: 1072px) 100vw, 1072px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h3><strong>小面条证件照</strong></h3>
<p>推荐指数：★★★★</p>
<p>这款微信小程序是一位不愿使用付费和广告满天飞的证件照生成工具的程序员，推己及人、为造福群众而独立开发的。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1402631" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1-13.jpg!720" alt width="1332" height="999" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1-13.jpg!720 1332w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1-13-360x270.jpg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1-13-768x576.jpg 768w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1-13-1024x768.jpg 1024w" sizes="(max-width: 1332px) 100vw, 1332px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>只需要在微信小程序中的搜索栏输入「小面条证件照」或者扫描本文末尾的二维码就能找到它。此工具的界面朴实无华，「拍照上传」和「背景颜色」都在同一个页面，幽默的文案和活泼的背景色让已经测试了几款证件照工具的我耳目一新。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1402632" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/2-6.png!720" alt width="788" height="824" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/2-6.png!720 788w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/2-6-360x376.png 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/2-6-768x803.png 768w" sizes="(max-width: 788px) 100vw, 788px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>经测试，三色背景的证件照都有良好的表现，不仅照片清晰，而且人物抠图几乎无白边。证件照免费导出无水印，还增设了免费的证件照排版功能，方便大家冲洗照片。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1402633" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/3-5.png!720" alt width="1114" height="442" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/3-5.png!720 1114w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/3-5-360x143.png 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/3-5-768x305.png 768w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/3-5-1024x406.png 1024w" sizes="(max-width: 1114px) 100vw, 1114px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1402634" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/4-3.png!720" alt width="887" height="772" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/4-3.png!720 887w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/4-3-360x313.png 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/4-3-768x668.png 768w" sizes="(max-width: 887px) 100vw, 887px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>但它也并非十全十美，非排版保存时「小面条证件照」没有照片裁剪功能，也没有尺寸选择，作者把这个功能放进了其个人公众号「小面条工具」里，需要回复「尺寸」才能进一步使用修改功能。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1402635" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/5-6.png!720" alt width="811" height="794" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/5-6.png!720 811w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/5-6-360x352.png 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/5-6-768x752.png 768w" sizes="(max-width: 811px) 100vw, 811px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h3>一键抠图</h3>
<p>推荐指数：★★★★</p>
<p>「一键抠图」是微信公众号 <span style="float: none; background-color: #ffffff; color: #333333; cursor: text; font-family: Georgia,'Times New Roman','Bitstream Charter',Times,serif; font-size: 16px; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; text-align: left; text-decoration: none; text-indent: 0px;">「搞定设计」</span>开发的在线抠图工具，关注该公众号后就可在对话界面中找到传送门，直接点击本文末尾的链接打开抠图页面也是可以的。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1402623" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/748E2393DEAADEEF3C142D572E1AF05F.jpg!720" alt width="764" height="842" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/748E2393DEAADEEF3C142D572E1AF05F.jpg!720 764w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/748E2393DEAADEEF3C142D572E1AF05F-360x397.jpg 360w" sizes="(max-width: 764px) 100vw, 764px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>面对比较复杂的背景，「一键抠图」都能迅速地识别并将人像抠出，并且边缘过度自然，照片无损，不愧于是专业的抠图程序。此外，它还提供更丰富的底色进行替换，也能自行裁切，下载也是免费的。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1402080" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1.7.jpg!720" alt width="1073" height="2038" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1.7.jpg!720 1073w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1.7-360x684.jpg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1.7-768x1459.jpg 768w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/1.7-539x1024.jpg 539w" sizes="(max-width: 1073px) 100vw, 1073px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>「一键抠图」的抠图效果是此次测试中最好的，就算是凑近手机屏幕，也看不见白边，但遗憾的是它没有提供照片规格的选择，也不支持修容，如果平时用作快速抠图工具的话应该会很不错，要制作证件照，特别是要打印粘贴照片的话可能需要自己再裁剪。</p>
<h3>证件照制作大师</h3>
<p>推荐指数：★★★★</p>
<p>本次测试还发现了一款 iOS 平台上的、能够换装和美颜的免费证件照 app，名为《证件照制作大师》，它免费保存各种的照片尺寸和排版照，付费 15 元后还可将一版照片打印邮寄到家。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1402595" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/F10F0D82F59404A94F592A727EE54555.jpg!720" alt width="1274" height="1133" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/F10F0D82F59404A94F592A727EE54555.jpg!720 1274w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/F10F0D82F59404A94F592A727EE54555-360x320.jpg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/F10F0D82F59404A94F592A727EE54555-768x683.jpg 768w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/F10F0D82F59404A94F592A727EE54555-1024x911.jpg 1024w" sizes="(max-width: 1274px) 100vw, 1274px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>此款 app 界面无广告，且功能精简好用，支持一键美颜和换装功能，美颜功能比较简单粗暴，只有开或不开两种，使用换装功能时，用户只需挑好正装，再拉伸到合适的尺寸即可。</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1402597" src="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/D7DBD51D2B37AB5047FF2D75220AB0A0.jpg!720" alt width="2033" height="920" srcset="https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/D7DBD51D2B37AB5047FF2D75220AB0A0.jpg!720 2033w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/D7DBD51D2B37AB5047FF2D75220AB0A0-360x163.jpg 360w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/D7DBD51D2B37AB5047FF2D75220AB0A0-768x348.jpg 768w, https://s3.ifanr.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/D7DBD51D2B37AB5047FF2D75220AB0A0-1024x463.jpg 1024w" sizes="(max-width: 2033px) 100vw, 2033px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>作为一款专门的证件照制作工具，《证件照制作大师》的抠图能力是过关的，边缘清晰无白边，只是线条比较僵硬，这一点在红底照片下比较明显。</p>
<p>另外还有一点需要提醒大家注意的，Android 端也有一款同名的 app，但是发开商似乎不同，并且需要付费使用。</p>
<p>其实除了专门用来制作证件照的 app 、小程序和在线工具，不少手机热门 app 也自带证件照制作的插件，例如支付宝、夸克、美图秀秀、扫描全能王等等。</p>
