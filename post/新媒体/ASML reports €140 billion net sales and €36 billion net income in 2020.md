<p>Today ASML Holding NV (ASML) has published its 2020 fourth-quarter and full-year results.</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Q4 net sales of €4.3 billion, gross margin of 52.0%, net income of €1.4 billion</p>
 </li>
<li>
<p>Q4 net bookings of €4.2 billion </p>
</li>
<li>
<p>2020 net sales of €14.0 billion, gross margin of 48.6%, net income of €3.6 billion</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>ASML expects Q1 2021 net sales of between €3.9 billion and €4.1 billion and a gross margin between 50% and 51% </p>
</li>
<li>
<p>ASML intends to declare a total dividend over 2020 of €2.75 per ordinary share (15% increase)</p>
</li>
</ul>
