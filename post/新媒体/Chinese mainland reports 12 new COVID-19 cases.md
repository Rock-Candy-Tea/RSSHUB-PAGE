<div class="text  en"><p>The Chinese mainland recorded 12 new COVID-19 cases on Saturday, all from overseas, data from the National Health Commission (NHC) showed on Sunday.</p><p>No deaths related to COVID-19 were reported on Saturday, the NHC said.</p><p>Eight new asymptomatic cases were recorded on Saturday, while 238 asymptomatic patients are under medical observation.</p><p>This brought the total number of confirmed COVID-19 cases on the Chinese mainland to 90,099, with the death toll unchanged at 4,636.</p><p>The total number of confirmed cases in the Hong Kong and Macao special administrative regions and the Taiwan region are as follows:</p><p>Hong Kong: 11,371 (10,863 recoveries, 203 deaths)</p><p>Macao: 48 (47 recoveries)</p><p>Taiwan: 1,005 (961 recoveries, 10 deaths)</p><p>(Cover: Teachers and students are receiving COVID-19 vaccine shots in an auditorium at the China University of Geosciences, Beijing, China, March 20, 2021./ CFP)</p><p> </p></div>
