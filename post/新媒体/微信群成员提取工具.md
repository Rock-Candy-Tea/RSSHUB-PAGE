<p>最近在研究微信逆向。刚好学习到微信群相关内容。特做一个微信群成员账号提取工具。献给有需要的人。使用方式如下：</p>
<p>第一步，先退出电脑上登录的微信。然后运行软件，点击登录微信<br>
<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3660" src="http://www.fulinian.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/1-1.png" alt="微信群成员提取工具" width="774" height="571" srcset="https://www.fulinian.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/1-1.png 774w, https://www.fulinian.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/1-1-300x221.png 300w, https://www.fulinian.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/1-1-768x567.png 768w" sizes="(max-width: 774px) 100vw, 774px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br>
第二步、登录成功后，在左边的列表中会显示当前已加入的所有群列表<br>
<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3661" src="http://www.fulinian.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/2-1.png" alt="微信群成员提取工具" width="774" height="571" srcset="https://www.fulinian.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/2-1.png 774w, https://www.fulinian.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/2-1-300x221.png 300w, https://www.fulinian.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/2-1-768x567.png 768w" sizes="(max-width: 774px) 100vw, 774px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br>
第三步、选择其中一个群，详细群成员信息会在右边列表中显示出来<br>
<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3662" src="http://www.fulinian.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/3-1.png" alt="微信群成员提取工具" width="774" height="562" srcset="https://www.fulinian.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/3-1.png 774w, https://www.fulinian.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/3-1-300x218.png 300w, https://www.fulinian.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/3-1-768x558.png 768w" sizes="(max-width: 774px) 100vw, 774px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br>
第四步，点击导出按钮，即可导出当前群成员的所有信息<br>
<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3663" src="http://www.fulinian.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/4-1.png" alt="微信群成员提取工具" width="1017" height="556" srcset="https://www.fulinian.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/4-1.png 1017w, https://www.fulinian.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/4-1-300x164.png 300w, https://www.fulinian.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/4-1-768x420.png 768w" sizes="(max-width: 1017px) 100vw, 1017px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3664" src="http://www.fulinian.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/5-1.png" alt="微信群成员提取工具" width="1490" height="854" srcset="https://www.fulinian.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/5-1.png 1490w, https://www.fulinian.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/5-1-300x172.png 300w, https://www.fulinian.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/5-1-768x440.png 768w, https://www.fulinian.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/5-1-1024x587.png 1024w" sizes="(max-width: 1490px) 100vw, 1490px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
