                        <p>
            Public servers are what most Java players use to link up with others. Servers are free to join and you can find them by using any of the many server-listing sites such as <a href="https://minecraftservers.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">MinecraftServers.org</a> (just search "Minecraft Servers" in your web browser to find more listing sites).
    </p>


    


                
                        
                                                        
        
            
            
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
            
                                                                                                
                                
                                                



            
                        <p>
            We've covered an in-depth guide on <a href="https://www.makeuseof.com/how-to-join-minecraft-server/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">How to Join a Minecraft Server</a> already, but here's a quick rundown:
    </p>


    


                
                        
            
        
            
            
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                
            
            
                            <ol>
                    <li>
                        Copy the IP Address of the server you want to join. This will look something like "makeuseof.example.com"
            </li>
                    <li>
                        Navigate to the <strong>Multiplayer</strong> screen in your Minecraft client. Click on <strong>Add Server</strong> or <strong>Direct Connection</strong>.
            </li>
                    <li>
                        Paste the IP address into the box labeled <strong>Server Address</strong>.
            </li>
                    <li>
                        If you chose <strong>Add Server</strong> on step 3, give the server a name and click <strong>Done</strong>, then double click on the server from your list or select it and choose <strong>Join Server</strong>. If you chose <strong>Direct Connection</strong>, click <strong>Join Server</strong>.
            </li>
            </ol>

                
                    
                        
            
        
            
            
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
            
                                                                                                
                                
                                                



            
                        <p>
            Enjoy playing Minecraft with friends!
    </p>

                        <p>
            While your range is narrower than Java Edition players', there are definitely public servers you can join to play Minecraft with your friends in Bedrock Edition.
    </p>


    


                
                        
                                                        
        
            
            
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
            
            
                            <ol>
                    <li>
                        Start Minecraft BE. Click <strong>Play</strong> and navigate to the <strong>Servers</strong> tab.
            </li>
                    <li>
                        You can choose from the list of <strong>Featured Servers</strong> which have a high player count and feature game modes like Skywars, Build Battle, and more.
            </li>
                    <li>
                        You can also add another server by clicking <strong>Add Server</strong>.
            </li>
                    <li>
                        Enter the server's name, IP address, and port. Click <strong>Save</strong> to keep this server bookmarked.
            </li>
            </ol>

                
                        
            
        
            
            
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
            
                                                                                                
                                
                                                



            
                        <p>
            You're all set! Enjoy Minecraft multiplayer in Bedrock Edition.

                        <h2 id="minecraft-multiplayer-for-private-servers">

                        <p>
            If you're not sure how to find an IP address for a public server, it's covered in <a href="https://www.makeuseof.com/how-to-join-minecraft-server/">How to Join a Minecraft Server</a>.
    </p>

                        <h2 id="make-minecraft-multiplayer-easy-with-realms">
                    Make Minecraft Multiplayer Easy With Realms</h2>

                
                        
            
        
            
            
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
            
                                                                                                
                                
                                                



            
                        <p>
            Realms work for both Minecraft Bedrock Edition and Minecraft Java Edition, though they are not cross-compatible (a friend who plays on Java Edition cannot play on a Bedrock Edition player's realm).
    </p>


    


                
                        
            
        
            
            
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
            
                                                                                                
                                
                                                



            
                        <p>
            <span class="related-single">Related: <a href="https://www.makeuseof.com/tag/minecraft-modpacks-to-try/">Bring Back the Magic With Minecraft Modpacks</a></span>
    </p>


    


                
                        
            
        
            
            
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
            
                                                                                                
                                
                                                



            
                        <p>
            Realms are Minecraft's version of personal servers. This is the easiest way to create a private world to craft and build with your friends.
    </p>


    


                
                        
            
        
            
            
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
            
            
                        <h3 id="how-to-create-a-realm">How to Create a Realm</h3>
                
                        
            
        
            
            
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
            
                                                                                                
                                
                                                



            
                        <p>
            <strong>Java Edition</strong>: First, you'll need to purchase a subscription for a Realm (you can check the pricing plans on <a href="https://www.minecraft.net/en-us/realms-for-java/plans/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Minecraft's website</a>). You can activate a 30-day free trial to test the waters and cancel anytime.
    </p>


    


                
                        
            
        
            
            
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                
            
                                                                                                
                                
                                                



            
                        <p>
            Once you have a subscription plan, open Minecraft and click <strong>Minecraft Realms</strong>. If this is your first time creating a Realm, click on <strong>click to start your new Realm</strong>. Here you can name your Realm and enter a brief description before choosing your world type.
    </p>


    


                
                    
                        
            
        
            
            
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
            
                                                                                                
                                
                                                



            
                        <p>
            Choose between generating a <strong>new world</strong>, uploading a previous world save, or explore Realms' <strong>World templates</strong>, <strong>Adventures</strong>, and <strong>Experiences</strong>.
    </p>

Now all you have to do to whitelist a friend on your server is click <strong>Invite player</strong> and type in their username before clicking <strong>Invite player</strong> again. Your friend will receive an invitation to join your Realm.
