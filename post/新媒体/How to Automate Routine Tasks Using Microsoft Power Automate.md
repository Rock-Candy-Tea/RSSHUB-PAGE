    </p>

                        <h2 id="what-is-microsoft-power-automate">
                    What is Microsoft Power Automate?</h2>

                
                        
            
        
            
            
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
            
                                                                                                
                                
                                                



            
                        <p>
            Microsoft Power Automate is a cloud-based service that allows you to automate workflows. Say you have a routine task like checking for an email from your manager every day. It's easy to forget this simple task. Using Microsoft Power Automate you can schedule a reminder notification to check for your manager’s email at a set time.
    </p>


    


                
                        
            
        
            
            
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                
            
                                                                                                
                                
                                                



            
                        <p>
            There are also many more workflows you can automate including complex data collection business processes. In other words, Microsoft Power Automate is more than a simple task scheduler. It is a <a href="https://www.makeuseof.com/tag/secure-cloud-storage-services/">secure cloud service</a> that can help you streamline your business.
    </p>

                        <h2 id="how-does-microsoft-power-automate-work">
                    How Does Microsoft Power Automate Work?</h2>

                
                        
            
        
            
            
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
            
                                                                                                
                                
                                                



            
                        <p>
            Microsoft Power Automate takes advantage of the cloud to run a set of predetermined actions. The actions can range from sending an email to collecting data from third-party services like Twitter. The cool thing about automation with Power Automate is that you don’t need to know how to code.
    </p>


    


                
                        
            
        
            
            
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
            
                                                                                                
                                
                                                



            
                        <p>
            You can create three types of workflows in Power Automate:
    </p>


    


                
                        
            
        
            
            
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
            
            
                            <ol>
                    <li>
                        <strong>Automated Workflows</strong> that work anytime a specified event takes place.
            </li>
                    <li>
                        <strong>Scheduled Workflows</strong> that you schedule to run for a specific time.
            </li>
                    <li>
                        <strong>Button Workflows</strong> that you trigger by pressing a button.
            </li>
            </ol>

                
                        
            
        
            
            
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                
            
                                                                                                
                                
                                                



            
                        <p>
            When you first open the <a href="https://flow.microsoft.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Power Automate homepage</a>, you will need to sign in with an account. If you already have an Office 365 account then you can go straight to creating flows. If you don't have an account, click on <strong>Try Free</strong> in the upper right corner.
    </p>


    


                
                    
                        
            
        
            
            
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
            
                                                                                                
                                
                                                



            
                        <p>
            You can use Power Automate from within your web browser, a desktop app, and a mobile app. While the desktop app and the browser are on par in terms of the feature set, the mobile app provides limited functionality.
    </p>


    


                
                        
            
        
            
            
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
            
                                                                                                
                                
                                                



            
                        <p>
            Register with your email address, and you are good to go. After signing in, you will find a number of pre-built templates on the homepage.
    </p>


    


                
                        
            
        
            
            
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
            
                                                                                                
                                
                                                



            
                        <p>
            For instance, on the homepage, you can see <strong>Schedule a meeting with a message sender. </strong>With just a couple of clicks, this template enables you to schedule a meeting with anyone that sends you a message on Microsoft Teams.
    </p>

                        <h2 id="automating-tasks-in-power-automate-using-pre-built-templates">
                    Automating Tasks in Power Automate Using Pre-Built Templates</h2>

                
                        
            
        
            
            
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
            
                                                                                                
                                
                                                



            
                        <p>
            Microsoft Power Automate makes it easy to automate common tasks like email reminders, important events, and data collection. Let us automate some common workflows using ready-to-use templates.

                        <h3 id="sending-an-email-whenever-a-new-tweet-uses-a-keyword">Sending an Email Whenever a New Tweet Uses a Keyword</h3>
                
                        
            
        
            
            
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
            
                                                                                                
                                
                                                



            
                        <p>
            Suppose you run a business that sells gaming hardware like graphics cards. Naturally, you want to target people that want to buy a GPU. So, you will keep an eye out for customers interested in gaming hardware. One way to do this is to track certain keywords that match your business interests.
    </p>


    


                
                        
            
        
            
            
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                
            
                                                                                                
                                
                                                



            
                        <p>
            On the Power Automate homepage, use the search box and search for ‘Twitter". Click on <strong>Send an email for new Tweets about a certain keyword</strong>. Connect your Twitter account by clicking on the <strong>+ </strong>sign and entering your credentials. Clicking on <strong>Continue</strong> will take you to the flow customization screen.
    </p>

                        <h3 id="save-email-attachments-to-onedrive">Save Email Attachments to OneDrive</h3>
                
                        
            
        
            
            
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
            
                                                                                                
                                                                    
                                                



            
                        <p>
            Deleting old emails comes with the risk of losing important email attachments like contracts. Using Microsoft Power Automate you can create a workflow to automatically save new attachments to your OneDrive cloud storage.
    </p>


    


                
                        
                                                        
        
            
            
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                
            
                                                                                                
                                
                                                



            
                        <p>
            Search for "OneDrive" using the search box and click on <strong>Save Outlook.com email attachments to your OneDrive</strong>. Afterward, enter your email and OneDrive credentials. Click on <strong>Create Flow </strong>to set up your workflow.
    </p>


    


                
                    
                        
            
        
            
            
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
            
                                                                                                
                                
                                                



            
                        <p>
            <span class="related-single">Related: <a href="https://www.makeuseof.com/how-to-archive-emails-in-outlook/">How to Archive Emails in Outlook</a></span>
    </p>


    


                
                        
            
        
            
            
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
            
                                                                                                
                                
                                                



            
                        <p>
            After you have created your flow, you will see a flow screen where you can manage your flow. For instance, you can delete, modify, and see the history of your current flows. Furthermore, you can also publish it as a template by clicking on <strong>Submit as template.</strong>
    </p>

                        <h2 id="creating-custom-tasks-in-power-automate">
                    Creating Custom Tasks in Power Automate</h2>

                
                        
            
        
            
            
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
            
                                                                                                
                                                                    
                                                



            
                        <p>
            Workflows from existing templates simplify many tasks but they can also be limiting. There are situations when you want to automate a task that doesn’t have a pre-built template. In such situations, you can create your own flows.
    </p>


    


                
                        
                                                        
        
            
            
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                
            
                                                                                                
                                
                                                



            
                        <p>
            Let's create a custom flow to send a birthday wish to a person every year. To do this, click on <strong>Create</strong> in the left sidebar. This will take you to the flow creation screen where you can choose the type of flow you want to create.
    </p>


    


                
                    
                        
            
        
            
            
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
            
                                                                                                
                                
                                                



            
                        <p>
            Because we want to send a birthday wish, it will be a scheduled flow that runs at a specified date. So, click on <strong>Scheduled Cloud Flow</strong>.
    </p>
