    In 2018, CLW released a report on minimum wage standards in China. This is an updated report compiled by CLW volunteers. The tables in this report demonstrate the current minimum wage standards for all provinces, autonomous regions and municipalities in China. All wages are in Chinese currency. (One Chinese yuan = 0.15 US dollar in September 2020.) For detailed information, please read the report.
