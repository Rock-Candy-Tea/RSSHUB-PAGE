            <div class="text  en"><p>Technical experts from the China Media Group 'revived' the 2.6-meters-tall bronze figure discovered at Sanxingdui Ruins site with motion tracking technology. Over 3,000 years old, the figure is clad in robes decorated with dragons and birds and is wearing a crown with a sun motif. He may have been a king or priest-king of Sanxingdui.</p></div>
