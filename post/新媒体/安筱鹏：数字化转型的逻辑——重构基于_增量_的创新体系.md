<p style="margin-bottom: 15px; max-width: 100%; text-indent: 2em; margin-top: 20px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px;">客户运营商将带来三个重要变化：</span></p>
<section style="margin-bottom: 15px; max-width: 100%; text-indent: 2em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #021eaa;"><strong style="max-width: 100%; text-indent: 0em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">■ </span></strong><strong style="max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">一是企业经营中轴从过去以产品为中心到以消费者为中心。</span></strong></span><span style="font-size: 16px; max-width: 100%; text-indent: 0em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">以产品为中心的核心逻辑是关注产品的质量、效益、交期、成本，以消费者为中心的核心理念是持续关注消费者从购买到使用全生命周期的体验。</span></section>
<section style="margin-bottom: 15px; max-width: 100%; text-indent: 2em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #021eaa;"><strong style="max-width: 100%; text-indent: 0em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">■</span></strong><span style="font-size: 16px; max-width: 100%; text-indent: 0em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"> </span><strong style="max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">二是从产品功能导向到功能+体验导向转变。</span></strong></span><span style="font-size: 16px; max-width: 100%; text-indent: 0em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">从关注性价比、产品功能、耐用性，到关注参与感、交付体验、文化认同、分享与交流等新感受。</span></section>
<p style="max-width: 100%; text-indent: 2em; margin-bottom: 15px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #021eaa;"><strong style="max-width: 100%; text-indent: 0em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">■ </span></strong><strong style="max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">三是从关注海量消费群体到个性化消费群体。</span></strong></span><span style="font-size: 16px; max-width: 100%; text-indent: 0em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">比如从关注5000万人的共同需求到关注500人的小众需求，从规模化到定制化。</span></p>

<p style="margin-top: 15px; margin-bottom: 15px; max-width: 100%; min-height: 1em; text-indent: 2em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px;">对于企业来说，产品开发、客户定位、营销策略、库存管理、排产计划、商业模式选择都是决策。</span><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #021eaa;"><strong style="max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">在不确定性的世界中进行决策是企业的一个本质属性</span></strong>。</span></p>
<p style="margin-top: 15px; margin-bottom: 15px; max-width: 100%; min-height: 1em; text-indent: 2em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #021eaa;"><strong style="max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">数字化本质上给企业带来两场革命：工具革命和决策革命。</span></strong></span></p>
<p style="margin-top: 15px; margin-bottom: 15px; max-width: 100%; min-height: 1em; text-indent: 2em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px;">决策革命就是企业每个决策主体面对各种决策的时候，系统能把正确的数据在正确的时间以正确的方式传递给正确的人和机器，可以定义为</span><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #021eaa;"><strong style="max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">数据的自动流动</span></strong></span><span style="font-size: 16px;">。数据的自动流动的背后就在于能够</span><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #021eaa;"><strong style="max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">基于数据+算法</span></strong></span><span style="font-size: 16px;">，把精准的决策告诉机器、生产线、立体仓库、操作人员、企业会计，乃至CEO、CIO。</span></p>
<p style="margin-top: 15px; margin-bottom: 15px; max-width: 100%; min-height: 1em; text-indent: 2em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px;">什么是数字化转型的本质？就是</span><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #021eaa;"><strong style="max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">在数据+算法定义的世界中，以数据的自动</span></strong></span><span style="letter-spacing: 2px;"><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #021eaa;"><strong style="max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">流动去消解复杂系统的不确定性</span></strong></span><span style="font-size: 16px;">，最终的目的在于提高资源配置的效率。</span></span></p>

<section style="margin-bottom: 15px; max-width: 100%; text-indent: 2em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px;">市场为什么能不断扩张？我们来看互联网平台是如何促进市场扩张的。</span><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #021eaa;"><strong style="max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">互联网降低了交易成本，使原来无法通过市场进行交易的产品变得有利可图</span></strong></span><span style="font-size: 16px;">。麦肯锡在2013年曾经做过一个报告，网络零售每100元的交易中，61元是从线下转移到线上，39元是互联网平台创造的。以此类推，我们可以大致估算：2019年网络零售10万亿，其中3.9万亿可能是由互联网平台创造的，技术极大地拓展了市场边界，</span><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #021eaa;"><strong style="max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">大量的长尾需求被开发出来</span></strong></span><span style="font-size: 16px;">。</span></section>

<section style="margin-bottom: 15px; max-width: 100%; text-indent: 2em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px;">最后我们看看生产组织方式创新。信息通信技术正在构造一个数字孪生的世界，通过物理世界与数字孪生世界的相互映射、实时交互、高效协同，在比特的世界中构建物质世界的运行框架和体系，构建人类社会大规模协作新体系。</span></section>
<section style="margin-bottom: 15px; max-width: 100%; text-indent: 2em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px;">面对物理世界，人们需要把物理世界跟赛博空间连接，通过对物理世界的状态感知、实时分析、科学决策、精准执行四个环节，来帮助人们描述、诊断、预测、决策，这需要突破隐性数据显性化和隐性知识显性化两大环节，通过构建数据闭环流动，</span><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #021eaa;"><strong style="max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">把数据变成信息，信息变成知识，知识变成决策，最终优化资源的配置效率</span></strong></span><span style="font-size: 16px;">。</span></section>
<section style="margin-bottom: 15px; max-width: 100%; text-indent: 2em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px;">制造业的生产方式正在从传统的试错法变为模拟择优法。一百多年前爱迪生发明电灯泡实验了几千种材料，每一种材料又实验了几百次、上千次，我们把它定义为试错法，今天正在演变成模拟择优法。</span></section>

<p style="max-width: 100%; min-height: 1em; text-indent: 0em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #021eaa;"><strong style="max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">■</span></strong></span><span style="font-size: 16px;"><strong style="max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="max-width: 100%; color: #fa6a00; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"> </span></strong><strong style="max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">数字孪生实践</strong></span></p>
<p style="margin-top: 15px; margin-bottom: 15px; max-width: 100%; min-height: 1em; text-indent: 2em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #021eaa;"><strong style="max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">高铁的研发过程中，在数字世界有一个虚拟京沪线</span></strong></span><span style="font-size: 16px;">，一个数字化高铁在数字化京沪线上跑起来，100公里、200公里、300公里、500公里，工程师们测不同速度下的电流、电压、稳定、安全、可靠，包括每个座位上的噪音，然后快速优化迭代。</span><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #021eaa;"><strong style="max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">飞机的研发周期从15年缩短到10年</span></strong></span><span style="font-size: 16px;">，高铁等高端复杂产品的研发周期都大幅缩短，是因为人们构造了一种新的方法论，在虚拟世界里模拟择优。</span></p>
<p style="margin-top: 15px; margin-bottom: 15px; max-width: 100%; min-height: 1em; text-indent: 2em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px;">淘宝天猫新产品上市速度越来越快，从2018年的5000万件新品增长到2019年1亿件新品。天猫新产品销售增速是77%，为整个大盘增速的两倍，占比从2018的24%提高到2019年的31%。</span></p>
<p style="margin-top: 15px; margin-bottom: 15px; max-width: 100%; min-height: 1em; text-indent: 2em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px;">新品创新速度为什么这么快？因为它构造了同样的一套模拟择优的方法：</span><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #021eaa;"><strong style="max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="color: #021eaa; font-size: 16px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">商家可以在天猫平台上实现对新产品的概念设计、成品上市测试、销售数据监测的模拟仿真，帮助企业决策者进行精准高效的决策</span></strong>。</span></p>

<section style="margin-top: 20px; max-width: 100%; text-indent: 2em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px;">数字孪生带来最大的价值是什么呢？就是人们正在构造一个通向</span><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #021eaa;"><strong style="max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">零成本的试错</span></strong></span><span style="font-size: 16px;">之路。</span></section>
<p style="margin-top: 15px; max-width: 100%; min-height: 1em; text-indent: 2em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #021eaa;"><strong style="max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">创新就是一个试错的过程</span></strong></span><span style="font-size: 16px;">，汽车需要做多次碰撞试验，飞机要做几万小时的风洞实验。产品如何制造加工，选用什么材料，采用什么工艺等等，都是一个个不断试错的过程。质量有一个爬坡期，在质量的爬坡期，从60%的成品率提高到99%的成品率的背后，是一个试错的过程。</span></p>
<section style="margin-top: 15px; max-width: 100%; text-indent: 2em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #021eaa;"><strong style="max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">客户需求的判断分析，也是一个试错的过程</span></strong></span><span style="font-size: 16px;">。开发一辆汽车、一件衣服，这些产品是不是客户所需要的，需要不断地去派样，先让客户体验一下，客户认可之后，再大规模地生产。今天可以通过天猫平台快速试错与迭代，找到一个最优的模式。</span></section>
<section style="margin-top: 15px; max-width: 100%; text-indent: 2em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px;">对于一个企业来说，技术方案是试错，工艺路线是试错，客户需求是试错，商业模式是试错。</span><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #021eaa;"><strong style="max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">衡量企业创新能力的重要标志就体现在企业试错能力：试错成本是不是足够低、试错效率是不是足够</span></strong></span><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #021eaa;"><strong style="max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">高</span></strong></span><span style="font-size: 16px;">。</span></section>
<section style="margin-top: 15px; max-width: 100%; text-indent: 2em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px;">在过去的50年，1GB的存储成本从1000万美元降到了目前的0.02美元，但我们今天思考的问题不是存储成本如何降低，而是试错成本怎么降低、创新效率如何去提高。概括地看，</span><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #021eaa;"><strong style="max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="color: #021eaa; font-size: 16px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">数字化正在带给人们认识和改造世界的新方法论：从理论推理、实验验证到模拟择优和大数据分析。</span></strong></span></section>
