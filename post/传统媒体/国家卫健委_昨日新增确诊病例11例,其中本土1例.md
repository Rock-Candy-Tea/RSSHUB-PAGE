<p style="text-align:justify;margin-bottom: 20px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-size: 17px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-indent: 2em; line-height: 1.75em; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">当日新增治愈出院病例16例，解除医学观察的密切接触者696人，重症病例与前一日持平。</p>

<p style="text-align:justify;margin-bottom: 20px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-size: 17px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-indent: 2em; line-height: 1.75em; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">境外输入现有确诊病例162例（无重症病例），现有疑似病例4例。累计确诊病例5179例，累计治愈出院病例5017例，无死亡病例。</p>

<p style="text-align:justify;margin-bottom: 20px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-size: 17px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-indent: 2em; line-height: 1.75em; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">截至3月18日24时，据31个省（自治区、直辖市）和新疆生产建设兵团报告，现有确诊病例164例（无重症病例），累计治愈出院病例85283例，累计死亡病例4636例，累计报告确诊病例90083例，现有疑似病例4例。累计追踪到密切接触者984534人，尚在医学观察的密切接触者3552人。</p>

<p style="text-align:justify;margin-bottom: 20px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-size: 17px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-indent: 2em; line-height: 1.75em; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">31个省（自治区、直辖市）和新疆生产建设兵团报告新增无症状感染者5例（均为境外输入）；当日无转为确诊病例；当日解除医学观察14例（均为境外输入）；尚在医学观察无症状感染者252例（均为境外输入）。</p>

<p style="text-align:justify;margin-bottom: 20px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-size: 17px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-indent: 2em; line-height: 1.75em; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">累计收到港澳台地区通报确诊病例12396例。其中，香港特别行政区11350例（出院10824例，死亡203例），澳门特别行政区48例（出院47例），台湾地区998例（出院958例，死亡10例）。</p>
