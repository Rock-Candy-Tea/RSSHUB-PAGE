    <p><b>Raising Ambitions: A new roadmap for the automotive circular economy</b><br>A meaningful transition to the circular economy will require players along the automotive value chain to establish a common language. To that end, this report proposes a taxonomy based on two primary measures – carbon and resource efficiency – and identifies key transformation pathways to circularity. This comprehensive and future-looking framework can help improve both materials and use-phase efficiency in the automobile sector, increasing circularity and dramatically reducing carbon emissions.</p>
</div>
<div class="st__content-block st__content-block--text">
    <p><br><i>The report is the second of three framework reports developed by the Circular Cars Initiative to help the sector and its regulators better understand automotive’s new sustainable future.</i></p>
