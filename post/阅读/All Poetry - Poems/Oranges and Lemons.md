<div>   
<div class="tr_11611701"></div><div class="orig_11611701"><p>
"Oranges and lemons,"<br>
Say the bells of St. Clement's.</p>
<p>
 </p>
<p>
"You owe me five shillings,"<br>
Say the bells of St. Helen's.</p>
<p>
 </p>
<p>
"When will you pay me?"<br>
Say the bells of Old Bailey.</p>
<p>
 </p>
<p>
"When I grow rich,"<br>
Say the bells of Shoreditch.</p>
<p>
 </p>
<p>
"When will that be?"<br>
Say the bells of Stepney.</p>
<p>
 </p>
<p>
"I do not know,"<br>
Says the great Bell of Bow.</p>
<p>
 </p>
<p>
"Two sticks in an apple,"<br>
Ring the bells of Whitechapel.</p>
<p>
 </p>
<p>
"Halfpence and farthings,"<br>
Say the bells of St. Martin's.</p>
<p>
 </p>
<p>
"Kettles and pans,"<br>
Say the bells of St. Ann's.</p>
<p>
 </p>
<p>
"Brickbats and tiles,"<br>
Say the bells of St. Giles.</p>
<p>
 </p>
<p>
"Old shoes and slippers,"<br>
Say the bells of St. Peter's.</p>
<p>
 </p>
<p>
"Pokers and tongs,"<br>
Say the bells of St. John's.</p></div><div class="copyright"><p>
