<p></p>
<h2 style="text-align: center;">听Dプ竹崎讲述「油库里」的故事</h2><blockquote>
<p><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-11361" src="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/07170930/13fa3943ca3d107166aef6c31e729220.png" alt width="400" height="400" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p><span style="color: #d24519;"><strong><span class="im">Dプ竹崎</span></strong></span>：也以<span class="im">「深い」「深い㌧」的ID活动。<br>
</span>爱好是画画，创作ASCII字符画，玩游戏，做菜。最近还在玩水族箱。</p>
<p>本职是游戏公司的美工。 <br>
<a href="https://www.pixiv.net/users/7335954" target="_blank" rel="noopener" data-saferedirecturl="https://www.google.com/url?q=https://www.pixiv.net/users/7335954&source=gmail&ust=1602144043304000&usg=AFQjCNG8VrE-bXjtM-7PFfXkrkKwPDfA8Q">https://www.pixiv.net/users/<wbr>7335954</a><br>
<a href="https://twitter.com/fukaiton" target="_blank" rel="noopener" data-saferedirecturl="https://www.google.com/url?q=https://twitter.com/fukaiton&source=gmail&ust=1602144043304000&usg=AFQjCNGp1c_s8taBFmSjAbVACQAZ695eew">https://twitter.com/fukaiton</a></p></blockquote>
<p><strong>——以东方角色为原型，通称「油库里」的AA（ASCII字符画），以及以此为基础的作品，</strong><strong style="letter-spacing: 0.28px;">请谈一谈</strong><strong style="letter-spacing: 0.02em;">您在这次给出可以自由使用、创作的许可时，心里是怎样想的呢？</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="color: #d24519;"><strong>Dプ竹崎：</strong></span><br>
　魔理沙的AA、灵梦的AA确实是我制作的，但是将这不同的A</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">A拼接在一起，创造那个名台词「馒馒来吧（ゆっくりしていってね）」的人都不是我。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　油库里将各种各样的要素以一种复杂的方式结合起来，变成了一个全新的角色，已经不能称得上是我个人的作品了。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　归根结底，论坛上的AA就是被复制粘贴，出现在各种地方从而扩散开来的事物。想要限制这种</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">AA文化，至少我是做不到也不会去做的。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>——我想了解那个ASCII字符画诞生的契机。在这之前，我想了解「当时的2ch」文化。您还记得最一开始创作ASCII字符画的时候吗？</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="color: #d24519;"><strong>Dプ竹崎：</strong></span><br>
　实际上最一开始我活动的地点不是2ch，是ふたば☆ちゃんねる【※】。</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #707c74;">※ふたば☆ちゃんねる（下称双叶）：始于2001年的图片论坛。最有名的板块是「二次裏（二次元裏@ふたば）」，有无数meme与P图从这里诞生，在互联网上广泛流传。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　当时红魔乡刚推出，我在双叶的绘画版上从灵梦开始画了红魔乡的全角色。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_11361" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11361" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img class="wp-image-11361 size-full" src="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/07170930/13fa3943ca3d107166aef6c31e729220.png" alt width="400" height="400" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11361" class="wp-caption-text">灵梦.PNG 2003/6/16</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　2ch上东方串的人看到后去推荐了我的作品，那之后我也开始偷偷看2ch了</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　但是，2ch不是图像论坛，很难发布自己的画作，我就想到了，「画字符画不就好了？」。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p></p>
<h2 style="text-align: center;">「改造后用来表现其他事物」――在油库里成为“油库里”之前</h2><p><strong>——你还记得最开始画的AA吗？</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="color: #d24519;"><strong>Dプ竹崎：</strong></span><br>
　我已经记不清哪个是严格来说的最早作品了，印象里最早期的应该是灵梦</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>
<p><img class="alignnone wp-image-11368 size-full" src="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/07171710/5ae2938932c71495cfc5af17f5ef0086.png" alt width="934" height="758" srcset="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/07171710/5ae2938932c71495cfc5af17f5ef0086.png 934w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/07171710/5ae2938932c71495cfc5af17f5ef0086-512x416.png 512w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/07171710/5ae2938932c71495cfc5af17f5ef0086-768x623.png 768w" sizes="(max-width: 934px) 100vw, 934px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p><strong>——还有，您现在是如何看待当时的2ch与AA文化的？</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="color: #d24519;"><strong>Dプ竹崎：</strong></span><br>
　当时网络上有传言说2ch是法外之地，说实话，我还记得最开始写完时不敢按下发布按钮</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">（笑）。但是真实情况是，其中的用户自然形成了某种秩序，并没有传说中那样无法无天。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　无论评价如何，我都认为那里是「自由」的。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　AA是文字，发布了原型的话，不需要特殊的软件或技术也能进行一定程度的改造。因此在扩散时，不单单只有原本发布的作品。只改变了台词、将部件换成其他的AA、将复数个AA作品结合起来，表达一个拥有起承转合的故事，能看到这些我非常开心</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　这种改造后用来表现其他事物的部分，我认为就是油库里诞生的根源。</span></p>
<p><strong>——「油库里」AA又是如何诞生的？</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="color: #d24519;"><strong>Dプ竹崎：</strong></span><br>
　在我回答最早的问题时也说了，这原本是不同的作品。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　1.最早的是灵梦的站姿AA。</span></p>
<p><img class="alignnone wp-image-11363 size-full" src="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/07171254/9f79a01cdce9fefe5632ea689c58a856.png" alt width="934" height="758" srcset="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/07171254/9f79a01cdce9fefe5632ea689c58a856.png 934w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/07171254/9f79a01cdce9fefe5632ea689c58a856-512x416.png 512w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/07171254/9f79a01cdce9fefe5632ea689c58a856-768x623.png 768w" sizes="(max-width: 934px) 100vw, 934px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　2.没过多久，东方串的人将AA改造，变成了拿菜刀的「咕嘿嘿灵梦」。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><img class="alignnone wp-image-11365 size-full" src="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/07171328/197e47fd0cfd8af1911ad4b9e5dfd190-1.png" alt width="934" height="758" srcset="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/07171328/197e47fd0cfd8af1911ad4b9e5dfd190-1.png 934w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/07171328/197e47fd0cfd8af1911ad4b9e5dfd190-1-512x416.png 512w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/07171328/197e47fd0cfd8af1911ad4b9e5dfd190-1-768x623.png 768w" sizes="(max-width: 934px) 100vw, 934px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　3.之后，出现了只保留咕嘿嘿灵梦头部的改造AA。</span></p>
<p><img class="alignnone wp-image-11364 size-full" src="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/07171312/806c93c0b10d66aa50aa095ede8ddf70.png" alt width="934" height="758" srcset="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/07171312/806c93c0b10d66aa50aa095ede8ddf70.png 934w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/07171312/806c93c0b10d66aa50aa095ede8ddf70-512x416.png 512w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/07171312/806c93c0b10d66aa50aa095ede8ddf70-768x623.png 768w" sizes="(max-width: 934px) 100vw, 934px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　4.由于只有头的灵梦太好玩了，我想着让它变得更圆一点，就做出了所谓的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">「馒头灵梦」。台词里的</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">「一起吃甜食吧（いっしょにお菓子を食べませんか）」是我在看其他AA台词时觉得这句话感觉不错，就借用了过来。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_11366" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11366" style="width: 934px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img class="wp-image-11366 size-full" src="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/07171428/264ae462ee9c2a99133d8433ff9181e2.png" alt width="934" height="758" srcset="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/07171428/264ae462ee9c2a99133d8433ff9181e2.png 934w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/07171428/264ae462ee9c2a99133d8433ff9181e2-512x416.png 512w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/07171428/264ae462ee9c2a99133d8433ff9181e2-768x623.png 768w" sizes="(max-width: 934px) 100vw, 934px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11366" class="wp-caption-text">制作时期未知 2005年前后？</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　5.那之后大概过了两年，我创作了全篇的AA漫画同人志。很遗憾，当时的原稿已经不在了，当时的一格里就是油库里魔理沙的原型AA</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。我后来也向2ch的东方串里投稿了这个AA。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_11367" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11367" style="width: 934px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img class="wp-image-11367 size-full" src="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/07171509/65b87c6d7f275d2a738c2c4398f1fe4f.png" alt width="934" height="758" srcset="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/07171509/65b87c6d7f275d2a738c2c4398f1fe4f.png 934w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/07171509/65b87c6d7f275d2a738c2c4398f1fe4f-512x416.png 512w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/07171509/65b87c6d7f275d2a738c2c4398f1fe4f-768x623.png 768w" sizes="(max-width: 934px) 100vw, 934px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11367" class="wp-caption-text">2007年夏</figcaption></figure>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　在那之后，我也不知道</span><span style="letter-spacing: 0.28px;">这两者结合在一起时经历了怎样的过程</span><span style="letter-spacing: 0.02em;">。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　所以，我本人只是分别投稿了作为源头的素材，和油库里本身的诞生实际上几乎没有关系。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>——现在油库里被许多人爱着（常听说中小学女生经常看「油库里实况」），您对这点怎么想？</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="color: #d24519;"><strong>Dプ竹崎：</strong></span><br>
　我很开心，油库里已经脱离了其源头AA，作为一个独立的角色存在并活跃。说不定现在的小孩子们在听说其原本是AA后，都得问</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">「AA是什么？」了。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　我本人也是完全站在”观众”的角度享受这一切。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　无来由地觉得有些恶心但又生气不起来的暧昧表情，棒读的说话方式等等……真可爱。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　虽然这样说有些傲慢，不过我对油库里还是有着零星的「亲情」一样的感情。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">「这孩子，长大了啊……」这种。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　说起来油库里AA从诞生起也已经过了10年以上了，当年的小朋友现在可能真的成了中小学生吧。</span></p>
<p><strong>——最后请对喜欢「油库里」的人们说几句话吧！</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="color: #d24519;"><strong>Dプ竹崎：</strong></span><br>
　到了现在，我也只是油库里众多爱好者之一罢了，说不了太多。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　「今后也要馒馒来哦！！！」</span></p>
