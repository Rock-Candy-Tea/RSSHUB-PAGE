<h2 style="text-align: center;">前情回顾</h2><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　开场是在冥界·白玉楼。对和平的日常感到些许厌倦的妖梦，从到访的四季映姬·夜摩仙那度那里接受了一个委托。那就是「与部下小野塚小町一起，去调查从地狱中消失的大量灵魂的去向及其原因」。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">面对妖梦「解决异变的话拜托灵梦不是更好吗」的询问，四季回答是「因为是已经进入冥界的灵魂，我想尽可能由冥界这边来处理。并且我认为妖梦更能胜任应对这次异变的任务」。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　由于也受到了主人·西行寺幽幽子的请求，妖梦便同小町一起前往了幻想乡。</span></p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-3050" src="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/05213014/image10-3-734x1024.jpg" alt width="734" height="1024" srcset="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/05213014/image10-3-734x1024.jpg 734w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/05213014/image10-3-367x512.jpg 367w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/05213014/image10-3-768x1072.jpg 768w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/05213014/image10-3-1146x1600.jpg 1146w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/05213014/image10-3.jpg 1200w" sizes="(max-width: 734px) 100vw, 734px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　二人首先前往的，是博丽神社的方向。虽然四季映姬没有委托灵梦，但如果她注意到了异变的话，也可以从她那里收集信息。这一判断是否正确尚不清楚，但走在夜晚森林中的二人，遭到了已开始白骨化的仿佛尸体一般的敌人的袭击。在遭到并非弹幕游戏的攻击——而是枪击之后，二人仔细观察敌人，发现它们是把某种东西作为依附对象从而实体化的亡灵士兵。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　小町提议，用妖梦持有的白楼剑斩杀亡灵。虽然对于能斩断迷惘的白楼剑是否真的有效，妖梦有些将信将疑，但实践后确实成功地把灵魂从依附对象上剥离了下来。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　等到把袭来的亡灵士兵全部击倒时，听闻骚动的灵梦也飞了过来。小町以将灵魂送回去为由暂时逃回了冥界，妖梦则代替她被灵梦拉到了博丽神社。</span></p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-3049" src="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/05213014/image9-2-734x1024.jpg" alt width="734" height="1024" srcset="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/05213014/image9-2-734x1024.jpg 734w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/05213014/image9-2-367x512.jpg 367w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/05213014/image9-2-768x1072.jpg 768w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/05213014/image9-2-1146x1600.jpg 1146w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/05213014/image9-2.jpg 1200w" sizes="(max-width: 734px) 100vw, 734px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　被灵梦问及缘由的妖梦讲述了事情的经过，但灵梦表示并没有听到过类似的消息。期待落空，妖梦听从回来的小町的意见，决定访问魔法森林附近的道具店·香霖堂。由于亡灵士兵的依附对象看起来像是从外面世界流入的，所以她认为的确应该去问问森近霖之助。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　就结果来说，小町的预想是正确的。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　幻想乡的大贤者·八云紫正在拜访霖之助，她委托霖之助处理掉从外面世界流入幻想乡的武器、兵器堆。其中还有第二次世界大战后开发的沙皇炸弹。</span></p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-3052" src="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/05213013/image12-2-734x1024.jpg" alt width="734" height="1024" srcset="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/05213013/image12-2-734x1024.jpg 734w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/05213013/image12-2-367x512.jpg 367w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/05213013/image12-2-768x1072.jpg 768w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/05213013/image12-2-1146x1600.jpg 1146w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/05213013/image12-2.jpg 1200w" sizes="(max-width: 734px) 100vw, 734px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　由于需要处理的武器、兵器的数量过于庞大，他拜托了以河城荷取为首的河童们，花费了好几天才大致处理完。但对于能把幻想乡全域甚至博丽大结界都烧成灰烬的核武器，要想安全分解还需要专业人员的协作。而那个人就是灵乌路空，操纵核聚变的地狱鸦。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　终于到了准备开始分解作业的阶段，突然出现了一个亡灵。亡灵身着漆黑的军服，单是站在那里便散发出恶狠狠的气息。打一照面霖之助就作出了「危险」的判断。</span></p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-3051" src="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/05213013/image11-3-734x1024.jpg" alt width="734" height="1024" srcset="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/05213013/image11-3-734x1024.jpg 734w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/05213013/image11-3-367x512.jpg 367w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/05213013/image11-3-768x1072.jpg 768w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/05213013/image11-3-1146x1600.jpg 1146w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/05213013/image11-3.jpg 1200w" sizes="(max-width: 734px) 100vw, 734px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　亡灵自称名叫简·卡奈尔·奥斯缇娜，是率领亡灵兵团的首领。她迅速地迫近而来并将灵乌路空击昏，然后连同沙皇炸弹一起带走了。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　为了消灭留下的荷取与霖之助，亡灵士兵奔袭而来。妖梦和小町与四处逃窜的荷取他们会合，收拾掉亡灵士兵，并听闻了事情的经过。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　于是荷取也与妖梦和小町一同前行，追着空和炸弹及幕后黑手的去向，向山上进发。</span></p>
<h2 style="text-align: center;">登场人物介绍</h2><p style="text-align: center;"><strong>魂魄妖梦</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;"><img class="alignnone wp-image-3042 size-full" src="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/05213015/image2-3.jpg" alt width="394" height="394" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br>
</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">半人半灵，西行寺幽幽子的剑术指导及白玉楼的园艺师。使用长刀·楼观剑与短刀·白楼剑，无论是亡灵还是子弹还是近代兵器都能一刀两断。在与亡灵士兵的战斗中透露出以往试刀杀人魔的气质，三头六臂大展身手。虽然在与小町的对话中担当吐槽角色，但由于荷取的加入，也渐渐地走上了卖蠢的道路。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>小野塚小町</strong><br>
<img class="size-full wp-image-3041 alignnone" src="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/05213015/image1-5.jpg" alt width="388" height="388" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br>
地狱的审判长·四季映姬·夜摩仙那度的部下。死神。负责将灵魂渡到彼岸，作为这次灵魂大量消失事件的相关调查员与妖梦一同行动。虽然依旧喜欢偷懒，不过像是把抓到的灵魂送回冥界时，或是在妖梦危难当头需要援助时，她也会好好地工作。标志性的镰刀似乎试验性地换成了带刃的。<br>
</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>河城菏取<br>
<img class="size-full wp-image-3044 alignnone" src="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/05213015/image4-3.jpg" alt width="389" height="389" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br>
</strong>住在妖怪之山上的河童。对人造物充满兴趣的超级工程师。是从森近霖之助那里接受了武器堆分解委托的人之一。本来是要与灵乌路空一起分解沙皇炸弹的，但因受到了黑衣亡灵的袭击而计划受挫。在得知妖梦与小町正在调查亡灵士兵后，申请一同帮忙。同时也负责对二人的卖蠢进行吐槽。</p>
<p> </p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>八云紫</strong><br>
<img class="size-full wp-image-3043 alignnone" src="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/05213015/image3-4.jpg" alt width="391" height="391" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br>
</span>幻想乡最强的妖怪，大贤者。知晓一切，只根据需要而行动的勤奋的懒惰者。一边委托霖之助处理从外面世界流入幻想乡的大量武器与兵器堆，一边叮嘱灵梦不要对这次的异变展开行动。虽然她看起来已经掌握了迫近幻想乡的危机，但……。</p>
<p> </p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>博丽灵梦<br>
<img class="size-full wp-image-3047 alignnone" src="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/05213014/image7-3.jpg" alt width="396" height="396" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br>
</strong>博丽神社的巫女，解决异变的专家。从妖梦和小町那里得知了有什么事在发生，但正当她犹豫自己该不该行动时，却被紫叮嘱不要行动。也许是对此感到不满，抑或只是乱撒气，总之灵梦也独自开始了对异变的调查。</p>
<p> </p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>森近霖之助</strong><br>
<img class="size-full wp-image-3045 alignnone" src="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/05213014/image5-4.jpg" alt width="394" height="394" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br>
　在魔法森林附近开店的道具店店主。尽管各种道具齐备，但却是个完全不适合做生意的收藏家。从紫那里接受了处理外面世界流入的兵器堆这一委托，又转包给了河童。关于兵器分解，他向河童们教授了武器的名字和用途。<br>
</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>亡灵士兵</strong><br>
　从地狱逃走的灵魂依附在从外面世界流入的武器、兵器上而实体化的产物。似乎还记得战斗的方法，毫不顾忌地将武器、兵器对准了妖梦等人。不知是记忆的关系还是战斗的后遗症，身上有些部分无法保持人形。</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br>
<strong>简·卡奈尔·奥斯缇娜</strong><br>
<img class="size-full wp-image-3046 alignnone" src="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/05213014/image6-3.jpg" alt width="393" height="393" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br>
　自称亡灵兵团的首领。左眼的眼窝之中只有黑暗，不管是从黑色的军服、军帽还是军刀，怎么看都是军人样貌的亡灵。背负着独特的长矛。身为亡灵却有着明确的意志，巧舌如簧。拥有瞬间迫近灵乌路空（尽管有大意的成分），并一击将其击昏的能耐。抢走空和沙皇炸弹后，就消失到了别处。<br>
</span></p>
