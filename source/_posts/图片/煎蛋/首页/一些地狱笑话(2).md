<blockquote><p>迟到总比不到强</p></blockquote><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://img.jandan.net/news/2022/08/f25acb2d91005678edd2b94b17dfa72e.jpg!custom" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><blockquote><p>
<strong>玩儿跳伞其实不用带降落伞。但是想再玩儿一次的话就需要了。</strong><br>
<em>You don't need a parachute to go skydiving. You need a parachute to go skydiving twice.</em>
</p></blockquote>
<blockquote><p>
<strong>一个男人领着一个小男孩走进森林。小男孩抬头对他说：“先生，这里越来越黑了，我好怕。”男人回答说：“那你想过我的感受吗？我还得一个人走回去。”</strong><br>
<em>A guy walks with a young boy into the woods. The boy turns to him and says, "Hey mister, it's getting really dark and I'm scared." The man replies, "How do you think I feel? I have to walk back alone."</em>
</p></blockquote>
<blockquote><p>
<strong>最近，一项新的重大研究发现，人类吃香蕉比猴子还要多。雀食。我已经记不起来自己上次吃猴子是什么时候了。</strong><br>
<em>Turns out a major new study recently found that humans eat more bananas than monkeys. It’s true. I can’t remember the last time I ate a monkey.</em>
</p></blockquote>
<blockquote><p>
<strong>随着老去，我想起了这一路来失去的人们。或许当导游不是个正确的选择。</strong><br>
<em>As I get older, I remember all the people I lost along the way. Maybe a career as a tour guide was not the right choice.</em>
</p></blockquote>
<blockquote><p>
<strong>我今天做了新冠检测，结果显示50。这是啥意思？还有，我的智商检测结果也出来了，是阳性。</strong><br>
<em>I got my COVID test today, it says 50. What does that mean? Also, my IQ test came back positive.</em>
</p></blockquote>
<blockquote><p>
<strong>老婆和我在餐厅吃饭，服务员开始挑逗我。我老婆就说：“这姑娘肯定得新冠了。”我问：“为什么呢？”老婆说：“因为她一点品味(味觉)都没有。”</strong><br>
<em>My wife and I were out to dinner and the waitress started flirting with me. "She obviously has COVID," my wife said. "Why?" I asked. My wife replied with a sneer, "Because she has no taste."</em>
</p></blockquote>
<blockquote><p>
<strong>今天真是糟糕的一天。我的前任被公交车撞了。而我丢掉了公交车司机的工作！</strong><br>
<em>Today was a terrible day. My ex got hit by a bus. And I lost my job as a bus driver!</em>
</p></blockquote>
<blockquote><p>
<strong>昨天我把丈母娘给送走了。当狙击手真特么的爽！</strong><br>
<em>I took my mother-in-law out yesterday morning. Being a sniper is awesome.</em>
</p></blockquote>
<blockquote><p>
<strong>孤儿自拍叫什么？全家福。</strong><br>
<em>What do you call an orphan taking a selfie? A family photo.</em>
</p></blockquote>
<blockquote><p>
<strong>我很震惊，这吐司机竟然不防水。</strong><br>
<em>I was shocked when I found out my toaster was not waterproof.</em>
</p></blockquote>
<blockquote><p>
<strong>我有一条会跳霹雳舞的鱼！但只能跳20秒，而且就能跳一回。</strong><br>
<em>I have a fish that can breakdance! Only for 20 seconds though, and only once.</em>
</p></blockquote>
<blockquote><p>
<strong>我和女朋友分手了，于是我就偷走了她的轮椅，猜猜谁爬着回来找我了？</strong><br>
<em>I broke up with my girlfriend so I stole her wheelchair and guess who came crawling back.</em>
</p></blockquote>
<blockquote><p>
<strong>妈妈快要不行了，而我们却记不清她的血型。在弥留之际，她不停的叮嘱我们“要乐观(B型Rh阳性)”，但她都不在了，我们又怎么可能做得到。</strong><br>
<em>My mom died when we couldn't remember her blood type. As she died, she kept telling us to “be positive,” but it's hard without her.</em>
</p></blockquote>
<blockquote><p>
<strong>为什么孤儿的日历上一年只有363天？他们没有父亲节和母亲节。</strong><br>
<em>Why are there only 363 days in an orphans calendar？ They don’t have fathers or Mother’s Day.</em>
</p></blockquote>
<blockquote><p>
<strong>俩黑人在街上走，然后看到一个牌子写着，想要变白吗？只要75块钱。<br>
黑人1：“你觉得这玩意儿管用吗？”<br>
黑人2：“只有试试才知道。”<br>
黑人1：“但我只有50块。”<br>
黑人2：“我有100块，我先去试试管用不，然后把剩下来的钱给你用。”<br>
黑人1：“行啊，就这么整。”<br>
黑人2走了进去，15分钟后走了出来，身上穿着一套崭新的西服，手里提着一个公文包，皮肤白的吓人。<br>
黑人1：“卧槽，还真特么管用啊！快把剩下的25块钱给我。”<br>
黑人2：“去你码的黑鬼，找份工作去吧。”<br>
</strong><br>
<em>Two blacks are walking down the street and see a sign that says turn white for $75.<br>
Black1: "Do you think it will work?<br>
Black2: "Only one way to find out."<br>
Black1: "I only have $50."<br>
Black2: "Well, I have $100, I'll go do it then give you my change."<br>
Black1: "Let's do it then."<br>
Black2 goes in and fifteen minutes later comes out white as a ghost, wearing a brand new suit and carrying briefcase.<br>
Black1: "Holy shit, it actually worked! Let me get that $25."<br>
Black2: "Fuck you, nigger. Get a job."</em>
</p></blockquote>
<blockquote><p>
<strong>我问威尔士朋友有过多少床伴。他就开始跟我数，但很快就睡着了。</strong><br>
<em>I asked my Welsh friend how many sexual partners he'd had. He started counting but he fell asleep.</em>
</p></blockquote>
<blockquote><p>
<strong>《每日邮报》在网上说：“自慰有助于预防感冒”。希望如此，我可一张纸巾都不剩了。</strong><br>
<em>Daily Mail online: "Masturbation may help prevent the common cold." Hope so, I've got no tissues left</em>
</p></blockquote>
<blockquote><p>
<strong>在图书馆，一个黑人兄弟问我，彩色(有色)打印机在哪里。<br>
我说：“哥们儿，现在已经是2022年了，随便你想用什么打印机都没问题的。”</strong><br>
<em>A black guy in a library asked me where the coloured printer was.<br>
I replied, "Mate, it's 2022, you can use any printer you want."</em>
</p></blockquote>
<blockquote><p>
<strong>一张普通的沙发秒变沙发床只需要简单的一步：忘了你老婆的生日是哪天。</strong><br>
<em>Turn a regular sofa into a sofa bed by simply forgetting your wife's birthday.</em>
</p></blockquote>
<blockquote><p>
<strong>一名牧师犯了心脏病，被紧急送到了医院。他醒来后发现自己正躺在轮床上，两名护士脚步匆忙的推着他在医院中穿梭。<br>
“我这是在天堂吗？”迷糊中的牧师问道。<br>
“不是，”一名护士回答说，“我们只是从儿科病房抄个近道。”</strong><br>
<em>A priest has a heart attack, and is rushed to the hospital.<br>
He wakes up as he's being rushed through the hospital on a gurney by two nurses.<br>
"Am I in heaven?" asks the disoriented priest.<br>
"No" says one of the nurses. "We're just taking a short cut through the children's ward."</em>
</p></blockquote>
