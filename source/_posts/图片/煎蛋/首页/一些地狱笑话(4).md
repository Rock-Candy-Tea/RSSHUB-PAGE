<blockquote><p>经过精心挑选和净化</p></blockquote><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://img.jandan.net/news/2022/08/f25acb2d91005678edd2b94b17dfa72e.jpg!custom" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><blockquote><p>
<strong>在我的柠檬水摊子，第一杯柠檬水是免费的，第二杯收费5美元。续杯里面有解药。</strong><br>
<em>At my lemonade stand, I used to give the first glass away free and charge five dollars for the second glass. The refill contained the antidote.</em>
</p></blockquote>
<blockquote><p>
<strong>90岁时的滚床单就像是用根儿绳子打台球。</strong><br>
<em>Sex at age 90 is like trying to shoot pool with a rope.</em>
</p></blockquote>
<blockquote><p>
<strong>-洋葱和孩子有什么不同点？<br>
-剥洋葱的时候会流泪。</strong><br>
<em>-What’s the difference between a baby and an onion?<br>
-I cry peeling Onions!</em>
</p></blockquote>
<blockquote><p>
<strong>爆炸发生后露西去哪儿了？遍地都是。</strong><br>
<em>Where did Lucy go after the bombing?<br>
Everywhere.</em>
</p></blockquote>
<blockquote><p>
<strong>在死后，尸体中唯一温热的器官是哪个？我的小和尚。</strong><br>
<em>After death, what is the only organ in the  body which remains warm? My penis.</em>
</p></blockquote>
<blockquote><p>
<strong>一名黑人男性身中15枪，警方称这是他们见过最惨的自杀案。</strong><br>
<em>A black man was shot 15 times. Police said it was the worst case of suicide they have ever seen.</em>
</p></blockquote>
<blockquote><p>
<strong>耶稣和耶稣画像有什么区别？画像只需要一颗钉子就能挂住。</strong><br>
<em>What is the difference between Jesus and a painting of Jesus? It only takes one nail to hang the painting.</em>
</p></blockquote>
<blockquote><p>
<strong>该怎么称呼一位耳聋的妇科医生？唇语专家。</strong><br>
<em>What do you call a deaf gynecologist?<br>
A lip reader.</em>
</p></blockquote>
<blockquote><p>
<strong>有天一人问我：“春袋怎么拼？”我说：“你该昨儿晚上问我，当时就在我嘴边儿。”</strong><br>
<em>Someone asked the other day how you spell “scrotum”, I replied ” you should have asked me last night as it was on the tip of my tongue”</em>
</p></blockquote>
<blockquote><p>
<strong>如果人是被烧死的，那火化时有折扣吗？</strong><br>
<em>If someone burns to death, do they get a discount at the crematorium? </em>
</p></blockquote>
<blockquote><p>
<strong>爷爷说我太依赖科技了。我说他是个伪君子，然后拔了他维生装置的电源。</strong><br>
<em>My grandfather says I am too reliant on technology. I called him a hypocrite and unplugged his life support.</em>
</p></blockquote>
<blockquote><p>
<strong>我的老板在一个犹太客户面前放了个屁，说：“一点点气儿要不了人命的。”</strong><br>
<em>My boss farted in front of a Jewish client, "A little gas never killed anybody."</em>
</p></blockquote>
<blockquote><p>
<strong>怎么称呼一位比她兄弟们跑的更快的白人女孩？红脖儿老姑娘。</strong><br>
<em>What do you call a White girl that can run faster than her brothers? The redneck spinster. </em>
</p></blockquote>
<blockquote><p>
<strong>我给一帮坐在长凳上的白人整了个新定义——NBA。</strong><br>
<em>I got a new definition for many White guys seated on a bench, NBA.</em>
</p></blockquote>
