<p>身为《假面骑士W》的女主角，鸣海亚树子经常携带一只绿色的拖鞋，拿它来敲各种人或者不是人的头【大部分是翔太郎的头】，绿色拖鞋也成为她他的标志。</p>
<p class="GsImageLabel" align="center"><a href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/09/20220906_ls_red_141_4/14643.jpg" target="_blank" _cke_saved_href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/09/20220906_ls_red_141_4/14643.jpg"><img class="picact" border="0" alt="游民星空" src="https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/09/20220906_ls_red_141_4/14643_S.jpg" _cke_saved_src="https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/09/20220906_ls_red_141_4/14643_S.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="GsImageLabel" align="center"><a href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/09/20220906_ls_red_141_4/14819.jpg" target="_blank" _cke_saved_href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/09/20220906_ls_red_141_4/14819.jpg"><img class="picact" border="0" alt="游民星空" src="https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/09/20220906_ls_red_141_4/14819_S.jpg" _cke_saved_src="https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/09/20220906_ls_red_141_4/14819_S.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>初期聒噪又不识时务，还非常的不正经。但随着剧情发展变得坚强、勇敢和可靠，有时经常能够提醒菲利普和翔太郎，促进案件的进展。在两个人变身假面骑士W的时候负责保护菲利普的身体。</p>
<p style="TEXT-ALIGN: center"><a href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/09/20220906_ls_red_141_4/15409.jpg" target="_blank" _cke_saved_href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/09/20220906_ls_red_141_4/15409.jpg"><img class="picact" border="0" alt="游民星空" src="https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/09/20220906_ls_red_141_4/15409_S.jpg" _cke_saved_src="https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/09/20220906_ls_red_141_4/15409_S.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>

<p style="TEXT-ALIGN: center"><a href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/09/20220906_ls_red_141_4/15656.jpg" target="_blank" _cke_saved_href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/09/20220906_ls_red_141_4/15656.jpg"><img class="picact" border="0" alt="游民星空" src="https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/09/20220906_ls_red_141_4/15656_S.jpg" _cke_saved_src="https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/09/20220906_ls_red_141_4/15656_S.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
