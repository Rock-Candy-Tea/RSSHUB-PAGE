<p>日本公信榜每一年都会评选，拥有理想身材的日本女星。近日发布第七届榜单，有不少去年在榜外的排进前十名。</p>
<p>今年新入榜的有小岛阳菜、荣仓奈奈和池田依来沙，这三位在日本都因为身材非常有名，但是这次是这几年首次排进前十。</p>
<p class="GsImageLabel" align="center"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/08/20220816_zy_red_164_4/1107.jpg"><img class="picact" border="0" alt="游民星空" src="https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/08/20220816_zy_red_164_4/1107_S.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">TOP 10 池田依来沙</span></p>
<p>1996年出生于日本福冈县，日本女演员、模特、导演，隶属于Ever Green Entertainment事务所。</p>
<p class="GsImageLabel" align="center"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/08/20220816_zy_red_164_4/1180.jpg"><img class="picact" border="0" alt="游民星空" src="https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/08/20220816_zy_red_164_4/1180_S.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="GsImageLabel" align="center"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/08/20220816_zy_red_164_4/1182.jpg"><img class="picact" border="0" alt="游民星空" src="https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/08/20220816_zy_red_164_4/1182_S.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">TOP 9 荣仓奈奈</span></p>
