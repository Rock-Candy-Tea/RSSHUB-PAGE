<p>疫情肆虐，最近大有反扑趋势。而国外一直处于疫情中，许多旅游景点无法开放。在韩国，一位vlog博主——Velvet 벨벳
就因为在疫情中拍摄当地景点介绍而走红。最近，她的一部参观「首尔龙山海绵宝宝展览」的视频又令她走红网络。</p>
<p class="GsImageLabel" align="center"><a href="http://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?http://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/08/20210808_zy_red_164_1/image001.jpg" target="_blank"><img class="picact" border="0" alt="游民星空" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/08/20210808_zy_red_164_1/image001_S.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="GsImageLabel" align="center"><img alt="游民星空" border="0" class="picact" width="550" src="https://imggif.gamersky.com/upimg_news/2021/20210807_zy_red_164_10/pZmZnaOZkaWbqA.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在当地这个「首尔龙山海绵宝宝展览」是去年8月-12月举办的，当然在疫情中，也没有什么游客。这让Velvet可以尽情拍摄场馆内的视频和展物。</p>
<p class="GsImageLabel" align="center"><a href="http://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?http://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/08/20210808_zy_red_164_1/image003.jpg" target="_blank"><img class="picact" border="0" alt="游民星空" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/08/20210808_zy_red_164_1/image003_S.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="GsImageLabel" align="center"><a href="http://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?http://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/08/20210808_zy_red_164_1/image005.jpg" target="_blank"><img class="picact" border="0" alt="游民星空" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/08/20210808_zy_red_164_1/image005_S.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="GsImageLabel" align="center"><img alt="游民星空" border="0" class="picact" width="550" src="https://imggif.gamersky.com/upimg_news/2021/20210807_zy_red_164_10/pZmZnaOZkaGWqA.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>这个海绵宝宝展一共有12个展区，这里面包含海绵宝宝的家，还有章鱼哥的住所，海绵宝宝与派大星常常出门玩耍的地方，总之里面还原了许多比奇堡海底世界</p>
<p class="GsImageLabel" align="center"><a href="http://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?http://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/08/20210808_zy_red_164_1/image006.jpg" target="_blank"><img class="picact" border="0" alt="游民星空" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/08/20210808_zy_red_164_1/image006_S.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="GsImageLabel" align="center"><a href="http://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?http://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/08/20210808_zy_red_164_1/image008.jpg" target="_blank"><img class="picact" border="0" alt="游民星空" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/08/20210808_zy_red_164_1/image008_S.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>这次的展览，基本以多个拍照打卡区做卖点，各种超大型的立体摆设，除了一边还原作品中的海底世界外，也让大家充份的置身其中。</p>
<p class="GsImageLabel" align="center"><a href="http://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?http://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/08/20210808_zy_red_164_1/image009.jpg" target="_blank"><img class="picact" border="0" alt="游民星空" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/08/20210808_zy_red_164_1/image009_S.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center" class="GsImageLabel"><img alt="游民星空" border="0" class="picact" width="550" src="https://imggif.gamersky.com/upimg_news/2021/20210807_zy_red_164_10/pZmZnaOZkaSXqQ.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>里面除了有各式各样的海绵宝宝周边，还有各式各样的角色甜品蛋糕、汉堡、松饼以及饮料咖啡等等，逛累了就可以好好用餐。</p>
