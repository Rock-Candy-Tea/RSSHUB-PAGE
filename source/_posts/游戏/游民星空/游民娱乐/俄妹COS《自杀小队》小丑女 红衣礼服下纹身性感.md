<p>小丑女是DC漫画中的经典形象，而在近年《自杀小队》中由玛格特·罗比饰演更是让她火出圈。最近，来自俄罗斯的COSER：ILONA
BUGAEVA就COS了在《X特遣队:全员集结》中红衣礼服版的小丑女。</p>
<p class="GsImageLabel" align="center"><a href="http://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?http://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/12/20211208_zy_red_164_7/image001.jpg" target="_blank"><img class="picact" border="0" alt="游民星空" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/12/20211208_zy_red_164_7/image001_S.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="GsImageLabel" align="center"><a href="http://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?http://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/12/20211208_zy_red_164_7/image002.jpg" target="_blank"><img class="picact" border="0" alt="游民星空" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/12/20211208_zy_red_164_7/image002_S.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="GsImageLabel" align="center"><a href="http://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?http://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/12/20211208_zy_red_164_7/image003.jpg" target="_blank"><img class="picact" border="0" alt="游民星空" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/12/20211208_zy_red_164_7/image003_S.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>ILONA BUGAEVA本身就是非常厉害的化妆师，无论是形象还是身材都相当还原影视版。此前她也曾COS了不同版本的小丑女，同样非常高质。</p>
