<p>关于“化妆术”相信已经大家看到不少了，今天和大家分享的是来自日本的小姐姐谷口布实，凭借高超的化妆技巧，吸引了大批粉丝。</p>
<p class="GsImageLabel" align="center"><a href="http://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?http://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/07/20210715_zy_red_164_4/image001.jpg" target="_blank"><img class="picact" border="0" alt="游民星空" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/07/20210715_zy_red_164_4/image001_S.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="GsImageLabel" align="center"><a href="http://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?http://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/07/20210715_zy_red_164_4/image003.jpg" target="_blank"><img class="picact" border="0" alt="游民星空" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/07/20210715_zy_red_164_4/image003_S.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>谷口布实经常会在自己的YouTube频道发布美妆视频，而她的作品也都是不同风格的整容级化妆术。每次都会令粉丝惊叹，实在太神奇了，简直换了一个人。</p>
<p class="GsImageLabel" align="center"><a href="http://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?http://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/07/20210715_zy_red_164_4/image005.jpg" target="_blank"><img class="picact" border="0" alt="游民星空" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/07/20210715_zy_red_164_4/image005_S.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="GsImageLabel" align="center"><a href="http://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?http://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/07/20210715_zy_red_164_4/image007.jpg" target="_blank"><img class="picact" border="0" alt="游民星空" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/07/20210715_zy_red_164_4/image007_S.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="GsImageLabel" align="center"><a href="http://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?http://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/07/20210715_zy_red_164_4/image009.jpg" target="_blank"><img class="picact" border="0" alt="游民星空" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/07/20210715_zy_red_164_4/image009_S.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="GsImageLabel" align="center"><a href="http://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?http://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/07/20210715_zy_red_164_4/image011.jpg" target="_blank"><img class="picact" border="0" alt="游民星空" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/07/20210715_zy_red_164_4/image011_S.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>谷口布实今年20岁，在五年前接触化妆。因为出演舞台剧经常需要化妆，所以她就开始喜欢并自学化妆。后来还建立了自己的美妆频道，现在已经成了拥有21万多的美妆博主。</p>
