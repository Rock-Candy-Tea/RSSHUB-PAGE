<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/03/20210327_ls_red_141_2/gamersky_040origin_079_20213271842157.jpg" target="_blank" _cke_saved_href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/03/20210327_ls_red_141_2/gamersky_040origin_079_20213271842157.jpg"><img class="picact" border="0" alt="游民星空" src="https://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/03/20210327_ls_red_141_2/gamersky_040small_080_2021327184249B.jpg" _cke_saved_src="https://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/03/20210327_ls_red_141_2/gamersky_040small_080_2021327184249B.jpg" original-image-height="720" original-image-width="1280" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>

<p>突然遇到地震快讯，一秒瞬间收起笑容切换严肃表情。</p>
