<p>积极经营Twitter、Ins、YT等社群的三上悠亚老师，最近开通了TikTok，跟着短视频热潮模仿了抖音上的热门舞蹈。</p>
<p style="TEXT-ALIGN: center"><a href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/04/20210414_ls_red_141_2/gamersky_21origin_41_20214141918DFE.jpg" target="_blank" _cke_saved_href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/04/20210414_ls_red_141_2/gamersky_21origin_41_20214141918DFE.jpg"><img class="picact" border="0" alt="游民星空" src="https://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/04/20210414_ls_red_141_2/gamersky_21small_42_20214141918242.jpg" original-image-height="1504" original-image-width="1128" _cke_saved_src="https://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/04/20210414_ls_red_141_2/gamersky_21small_42_20214141918242.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>随便一拍就美丽又大方的三上老师，身穿青春可爱的女高中生制服扭腰摆臀的甜美模样，网友观赏之余也赞叹，有偶像底子的三上悠亚真的好棒。</p>
<p style="TEXT-ALIGN: center"><a href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/04/20210414_ls_red_141_2/gamersky_22origin_43_202141419185B7.jpg" target="_blank" _cke_saved_href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/04/20210414_ls_red_141_2/gamersky_22origin_43_202141419185B7.jpg"><img class="picact" border="0" alt="游民星空" src="https://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/04/20210414_ls_red_141_2/gamersky_22small_44_2021414191892C.jpg" original-image-height="1043" original-image-width="720" _cke_saved_src="https://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/04/20210414_ls_red_141_2/gamersky_22small_44_2021414191892C.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
