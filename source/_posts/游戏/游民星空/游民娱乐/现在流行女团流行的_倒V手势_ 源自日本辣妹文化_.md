<p>偶像文化源自于日本，后来影响至韩国后又演变成另一种型态，现今更带来全球性的影响力，不同国家文化的交融现象让我们看到全新的流行色彩。</p>
<p class="GsImageLabel" align="center"><a href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/04/20220414_ls_red_141_1/16121.jpg" target="_blank" _cke_saved_href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/04/20220414_ls_red_141_1/16121.jpg"></a> </p>
<p class="GsImageLabel" align="center"><a href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/04/20220414_ls_red_141_1/16123.jpg" target="_blank" _cke_saved_href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/04/20220414_ls_red_141_1/16123.jpg"><img class="picact" border="0" alt="游民星空" src="https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/04/20220414_ls_red_141_1/16123_S.jpg" _cke_saved_src="https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/04/20220414_ls_red_141_1/16123_S.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="GsImageLabel" align="center"><span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); text-align: start;">韩女团TWICE内就有3名日本籍成员。</span></p>
<p>近期在社交媒体上，偶像拍照出现了一个新的趋向手势，当然不是已经在亚洲地区已经风行一阵子的「ღ( ´･ᴗ･` )比心」，而是上下翻转的「倒V」</p>
<p>这个姿势又称 ギャルピース（Gyaru Peace），有「Girl Peace」的意思，最早是日本90年代的辣妹流行文化，最近韩团中的日籍成员带来的这个复古流行，在韩国娱乐圈里带来了新的一波风潮。</p>
<p class="GsImageLabel" align="center"><a href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/04/20220414_ls_red_141_1/16289.jpg" target="_blank" _cke_saved_href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/04/20220414_ls_red_141_1/16289.jpg"><img class="picact" border="0" alt="游民星空" src="https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/04/20220414_ls_red_141_1/16289_S.jpg" _cke_saved_src="https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/04/20220414_ls_red_141_1/16289_S.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="GsImageLabel" align="center"><a href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/04/20220414_ls_red_141_1/16290.jpg" target="_blank" _cke_saved_href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/04/20220414_ls_red_141_1/16290.jpg"><img class="picact" border="0" alt="游民星空" src="https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/04/20220414_ls_red_141_1/16290_S.jpg" _cke_saved_src="https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/04/20220414_ls_red_141_1/16290_S.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 9pt;">这个潮流会蔓延可说是从</span>韩国新一代人气女团的IVE日籍成员Rei（直井怜）开始。</p>
<p class="GsImageLabel" align="center"><a href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/04/20220414_ls_red_141_1/16372.jpg" target="_blank" _cke_saved_href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/04/20220414_ls_red_141_1/16372.jpg"><img class="picact" border="0" alt="游民星空" src="https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/04/20220414_ls_red_141_1/16372_S.jpg" _cke_saved_src="https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/04/20220414_ls_red_141_1/16372_S.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
