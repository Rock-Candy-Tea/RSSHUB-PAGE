<p>日本女星天木纯Jun Amaki，今年已25岁，是“假面女子”组员出道的偶像，通过拍写真成功爆红，变成人气值女星。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/08/20210821_ls_red_141_1/gamersky_137origin_273_20218211716BC.jpg" target="_blank" _cke_saved_href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/08/20210821_ls_red_141_1/gamersky_137origin_273_20218211716BC.jpg" style="color: rgb(225, 29, 3); text-decoration-line: underline; font-size: 14px;"><img class="picact" border="0" alt="游民星空" src="https://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/08/20210821_ls_red_141_1/gamersky_137small_274_2021821171A00.jpg" _cke_saved_src="https://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/08/20210821_ls_red_141_1/gamersky_137small_274_2021821171A00.jpg" original-image-height="800" original-image-width="640" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>她以不会改变的美肌和较好身型知名，个子仅有148cm，却是童颜巨R，因为实在太大了，不少人质疑她的胸是假的。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/08/20210821_ls_red_141_1/gamersky_123origin_245_2021821171FBF.jpg" target="_blank" _cke_saved_href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/08/20210821_ls_red_141_1/gamersky_123origin_245_2021821171FBF.jpg" style="color: rgb(225, 29, 3); text-decoration-line: underline; font-size: 14px; text-align: -webkit-center;"><img class="picact" border="0" alt="游民星空" src="https://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/08/20210821_ls_red_141_1/gamersky_123small_246_2021821171332.jpg" _cke_saved_src="https://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/08/20210821_ls_red_141_1/gamersky_123small_246_2021821171332.jpg" original-image-height="813" original-image-width="650" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
