<p>《电锯人》中除了高人气的“坏女人”玛奇玛，性情暴躁“屑女人”帕瓦也是众多COSER喜欢的对象。今天和大家分享的来自身居日本的英国COSER：Peach的帕瓦COS。</p>
<p class="GsImageLabel" align="center"><a href="http://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?http://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/09/20210915_zy_red_164_4/image001.jpg" target="_blank"><img class="picact" border="0" alt="游民星空" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/09/20210915_zy_red_164_4/image001_S.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="GsImageLabel" align="center"><a href="http://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?http://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/09/20210915_zy_red_164_4/image003.jpg" target="_blank"><img class="picact" border="0" alt="游民星空" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/09/20210915_zy_red_164_4/image003_S.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>帕瓦也被称为“血之恶魔”的魔人，擅长用血液战斗。头上明显的恶魔之角和眼中有红圈十字是她十分鲜明的特点，除了喜欢战斗，还是一位猫奴。</p>
<p class="GsImageLabel" align="center"><a href="http://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?http://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/09/20210915_zy_red_164_4/image005.jpg" target="_blank"><img class="picact" border="0" alt="游民星空" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/09/20210915_zy_red_164_4/image005_S.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="GsImageLabel" align="center"><a href="http://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?http://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/09/20210915_zy_red_164_4/image007.jpg" target="_blank"><img class="picact" border="0" alt="游民星空" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/09/20210915_zy_red_164_4/image007_S.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="GsImageLabel" align="center"><a href="http://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?http://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/09/20210915_zy_red_164_4/image009.jpg" target="_blank"><img class="picact" border="0" alt="游民星空" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/09/20210915_zy_red_164_4/image009_S.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>Peach是一位目前身居日本的英国妹子，twitter账号为PeachMilky_。长相甜美的她还经营着一个VTuber形象：Peach2D。之前也COS过多个动漫和游戏角色，一起拉看看吧。</p>
