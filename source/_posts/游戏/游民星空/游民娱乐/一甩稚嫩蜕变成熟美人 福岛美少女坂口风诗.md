<p>今天介绍的这位福岛美少女坂口风诗，2018年18岁时入选《福岛美少女图鉴》，在周刊的制服大赛“制コレ18”进入决赛，虽未获奖，但此后便正式进入她的演艺生涯。</p>
<p style="TEXT-ALIGN: center"><a href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/03/20220303_ls_red_141_1/11445.jpg" target="_blank" _cke_saved_href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/03/20220303_ls_red_141_1/11445.jpg"><img class="picact" border="0" alt="游民星空" src="https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/03/20220303_ls_red_141_1/11445_S.jpg" _cke_saved_src="https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/03/20220303_ls_red_141_1/11445_S.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p style="TEXT-ALIGN: center"><a href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/03/20220303_ls_red_141_1/121.jpg" target="_blank" _cke_saved_href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/03/20220303_ls_red_141_1/121.jpg"><img class="picact" border="0" alt="游民星空" src="https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/03/20220303_ls_red_141_1/121_S.jpg" _cke_saved_src="https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/03/20220303_ls_red_141_1/121_S.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
