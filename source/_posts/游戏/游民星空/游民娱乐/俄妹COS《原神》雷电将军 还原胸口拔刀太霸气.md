<p>《原神》可以说是近年火出国门的手游，而在国外也有不少COSER带来了原神正片。今天和大家分享的是来自俄罗斯的COSER：melamori的雷电将军COS。</p>
<p class="GsImageLabel" align="center"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/01/20220128_zy_red_164_1/180.jpg"><img class="picact" border="0" alt="游民星空" src="https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/01/20220128_zy_red_164_1/180_S.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="GsImageLabel" align="center"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/01/20220128_zy_red_164_1/182.jpg"><img class="picact" border="0" alt="游民星空" src="https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/01/20220128_zy_red_164_1/182_S.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>雷电将军因为其形象和特殊的胸口拔剑招式迅速走红，成为COS圈的大热角色，本次melamori也还原了“奶香一刀”，十分惊艳。</p>
