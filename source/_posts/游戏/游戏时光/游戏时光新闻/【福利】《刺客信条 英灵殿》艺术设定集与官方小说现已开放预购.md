<div class="topicContent front_content"><p>　　育碧宣布，<span style="letter-spacing: 1.3px;">由育碧娱乐公司打造、次元书馆引进的</span><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">《刺客信条 英灵殿》艺术设定集与官方小说</span><span style="letter-spacing: 1.3px;">《盖尔蒙德之章》现已在官方商城上架并开启预售。</span></p><p><span style="letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　购买链接：<a href="https://item.taobao.com/item.htm?spm=2013.1.0.0.3c326018Xp2vGs&id=651506901190&scm=1007.12144.95220.42296_0_0&pvid=45c56f43-3886-4f7d-801a-8474ef5ae902&utparam=%7B%22x_hestia_source%22%3A%2242296%22%2C%22x_object_type%22%3A%22item%22%2C%22x_hestia_subsource%22%3A%22default%22%2C%22x_mt%22%3A0%2C%22x_src%22%3A%2242296%22%2C%22x_pos%22%3A2%2C%22wh_pid%22%3A-1%2C%22x_pvid%22%3A%2245c56f43-3886-4f7d-801a-8474ef5ae902%22%2C%22scm%22%3A%221007.12144.95220.42296_0_0%22%2C%22x_object_id%22%3A651506901190%7D">艺术设定集</a>|<a href="https://item.taobao.com/item.htm?spm=a1z10.3-c-s.w4002-21482603314.46.7d4a671bJdTgWr&id=652197787038">官方小说</a>|<a href="https://m.tb.cn/h.4AMv3WJ?sm=2b3580">两本一套合集</a></span></p><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/08/10/210810181934634_u8855.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　从挪威到韦塞克斯，从九世纪的简朴村镇到失落的古罗马建筑，这本艺术设定集还展现了《刺客信条》史上最为多样的自然风光。</span></p><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/08/10/210810182058125_u8855.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><p>　　《刺客信条 英灵殿》的官方小说《盖尔蒙德之章》也同步上市，读者将从全新的视角见证艾沃尔和维京勇士们的纠葛与荣耀。<br></p><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/08/10/210810182252114_u8855.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><p>
