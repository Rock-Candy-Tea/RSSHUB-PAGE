<div class="topicContent front_content"><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　CD Projekt Red 最近开启了《巫师》系列衍生漫画《巫师：浪人》（The Witcher: Ronin）的<a href="https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/witcherronin/the-witcher-ronin-a-hard-copy-collectors-edition-manga"> Kickstarter 众筹</a>。</span><br></p><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/09/08/210908165812589_u202875.jpg" class="vg_insert_img_onfocus" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　《巫师：浪人》是一部原创漫画。故事的舞台设定在古代日本，斩妖者杰洛特必须冒险穿越这片妖怪出没的土地，同时追查神秘「雪女」的下落。</span><br></p><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/09/08/210908165822251_u202875.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　CDPR 在公告中表示，《巫师：浪人》的主体内容已经完成，此次众筹是为了发行这篇漫画的特制收藏版。特制版除了收录主线故事，还包含有3个短篇漫画与幕后内容。</span><br></p><p>　　此外捐助者还能得到其它精美赠品，首日支持者可以获得一款 12 厘米规格的微缩模型，模型展示了杰洛特与致命天狗战斗的场景。</p><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/09/08/210908165841323_u202875.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　截至发稿前，《巫师：浪人》项目已筹得资金将近 300 万元。</span><br></p><p style="text-align: right;">来源：<a href="https://en.cdprojektred.com/news/the-witcher-ronin-has-arrived-on-kickstarter/">CDPR</a>/<a href="https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/witcherronin/the-witcher-ronin-a-hard-copy-collectors-edition-manga">Kickstarter

</a></p></div>
