<div class="topicContent front_content"><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　方块游戏平台今日（11 月 25 日）宣布，团队已与游戏开发商 Daedalic Entertainment 达成战略合作。方块游戏通过非股权投资参与《指环王：咕噜》的研发。本作预计于 2022 年登陆 PlayStation / Xbox / Switch / PC 平台。</span><br></p><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/11/25/211125133043314_u202875.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　《指环王：咕噜》是一款以《魔戒》故事为题材的动作冒险游戏。玩家将以咕噜的身份开启一场邪恶之旅，追寻他唯一珍视的至尊魔戒。咕噜诡计多端、阴险狡诈，同时也挣扎于分裂人格之间。玩家将在游戏过程中面临各种各样的选择。</span></p><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/11/25/211125133107884_u202875.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><p style="text-align: right;">来源：<a href="https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s?__biz=MzIxODA5MzcwNQ==&mid=2247484375&idx=1&sn=e900056284b4318fc15c33020ba60f78&chksm=97ee8231a0990b27f34c8f75162b416e6d64e7e6475fc4f2f28dce332025973d454eff9d8345&mpshare=1&scene=23&srcid=112557ALeo8igPOhoKgPUh4W&sharer_sharetime=1637812399417&sharer_shareid=ea5a0ce627f0cdf9377bc8ef8b415fa9#rd">方块游戏平台</a></p></div>
