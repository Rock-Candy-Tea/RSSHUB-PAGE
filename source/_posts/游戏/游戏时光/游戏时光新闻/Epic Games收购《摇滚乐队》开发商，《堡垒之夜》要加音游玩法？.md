Epic Games 正在打造的元宇宙中。</span></p><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width: 600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/11/24/211124120318639_u307326.jpg" title referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;"><br></span></p><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">      Epic 首席执行官 Tim Sweeney 曾表示会打造为一个开放的元宇宙平台。至今为止，</span><span style="letter-spacing: 1.3px;">《堡垒之夜》中已经举办过</span><span style="background-color: initial;
