<div class="topicContent front_content"><p>　　良笑社 Good Smile Company 现已开放一款全新手办的预售页面，为育碧旗下动作游戏系列新作《刺客信条：英灵殿》中的主角“艾沃尔”粘土人。官方旗舰店定价 383 元，预计将于 2022 年 1 月发货。  <br></p><p style="text-align: center;"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/07/15/210715115053821_u162815.jpg" class referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></p><p>　　这款男性艾沃尔手办的头部的纹身以及武器的细节均得到了精致还原，附赠配件为艾沃尔的主武器手斧（两把）、盾、袖剑，可用以再现游戏内激烈的战斗动作或潜行战斗风格。此外艾沃尔另有配件“渡鸦”，玩家可通过支撑架将它们摆饰在一起。  <br></p><p style="text-align: center;"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/07/15/210715115119619_u162815.jpg" class referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/07/15/2107151151403_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/07/15/210715115141875_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/07/15/210715115141530_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/07/15/210715115141490_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/07/15/210715115153672_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></p></div>
