<div class="topicContent front_content"><p>　　《真·三国无双8 帝国》将于 12 月 23 日登陆 PC、Xbox Series、Xbox One、PS5、PS4、NS 平台，光荣特库摩近日公布了本作的政略系统新要素“散步”以及武将编辑系统的情报。</p><h4><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">政略系统</span></h4><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　为了备战，将有多种方法可以强化己方势力。</span></p><h3><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">通过内政强化军备</span></h3><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　为了进行战斗，必须要充实「金钱」、「兵粮」及「兵力」。</span></p><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　除了通过金策及领内征集外，也能够通过掠夺国民等多种方法来达到目的。</span></p><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/10/28/211028180248467_u8855.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><h3>武将登用</h3><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　集结有能武将也十分重要，将可以通过政略指令以及战斗结束后的俘虏登用与</span><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">新要素「散步」等多种方式来登用喜欢的武将。</span></p><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　本作与前作不同，本作采取将武将配置在各个城市的形式，因此该城市发起的侵略作战(或是该城市的防卫战)除了玩家武将之外，</span><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">只有该城市所配置的武将能参加，因此登用众多武将，将其配置在各城市，将会使战局更加有利。</span></p><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/10/28/211028180544423_u8855.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/10/28/211028180552667_u8855.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/10/28/211028180558316_u8855.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><h4>新要素「散步」</h4><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　可以在自身的领地中，与各武将进行交流的新要素。</span></p><h3><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">与武将的交流</span></h3><p>　　除了可以与武将通过聚餐或是下围棋等来交流感情之外，也能看到武将们战场之外的另一面。</p><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/10/28/211028180734477_u8855.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/10/28/211028180740990_u8855.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/10/28/211028180745978_u8855.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><h3>登用在野武将</h3><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　在散步时，将有可能登用不属于势力的在野武将。</span></p><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　提高与武将的友好度，对方同意登用的概率也会增大。</span></p><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　除此之外，也存在玩家在大陆行走时，首次可登用的特殊在野武将。</span></p><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/10/28/211028181323978_u8855.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/10/28/21102818132926_u8855.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><h3>在散步中与武将同行</h3><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　可以邀请友好度达到一定程度的武将同行，享受散步的乐趣。</span></p><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　在同行中击破超过一定数量的山贼或野兽，还有可能从同行武将处获得礼物。</span></p><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　使用摄影模式的话，将可以调整镜头角度及自由设定时间及天气等，让玩家拍摄出喜欢的照片。</span></p><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/10/28/211028181544969_u8855.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/10/28/211028181549937_u8855.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><h4>与武将的友好度</h4><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　在本作中，将有显示玩家与各武将的信赖关系的「友好度」，当</span><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">「友好度」提高后，将获得武将协助强化势力等各式各样的帮助。</span></p><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　「友好度」除了通过新要素「散步」可以提升之外，也可以通过共赴战场，</span><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">或是采用武将所提案的「作战秘计」等方式来提升。</span></p><h3><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">提案作战秘计</span></h3><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　随着与武将的友好度上升，在准备战斗时他们将可能提案各种作战秘计。</span></p><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　当中也有强大的作战秘计，如果采用武将所提案的作战秘计，将能再次提升武将的「友好度」。</span></p><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/10/28/211028181942698_u8855.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/10/28/211028181948971_u8855.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><h3>合作完成内政指令获得更佳的效果</h3><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　在内政中，其他我方武将也会各自进行指令，而当与其他武将进行同一项指令时，将发动合作。</span></p><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　除了具有提升「友好度」的效果之外，也能看到特殊台词。</span></p><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/10/28/211028182145525_u8855.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/10/28/211028182150216_u8855.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><h3>可以超越身份作出指示</h3><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　一般只可对比自己身份低的武将作出个别指示，</span><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">但是对高友好度的武将可以无视身份作出个别指示。</span></p><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/10/28/21102818225210_u8855.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/10/28/211028182300233_u8855.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><h4>武將编辑</h4><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　本作中，武将编辑系统较前作大幅升级，将可以调整头发、脸庞、胡须及化妆等细微的设定。</span></p><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　此外服装部分也将提供新要素而可以进行「重叠穿戴」。</span></p><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　与前作一样，玩家可以自由设定各个部位的防具，本作中允许玩家可以在衣服外再穿戴铠甲。</span></p><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　另外，散步及特殊事件中，将可以以卸下铠甲的装扮进行游玩。</span></p><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/10/28/211028182523954_u8855.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/10/28/211028182529672_u8855.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/10/28/211028182534671_u8855.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/10/28/211028182540665_u8855.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/10/28/211028182548585_u8855.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/10/28/211028182554502_u8855.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><p><br></p></div>
