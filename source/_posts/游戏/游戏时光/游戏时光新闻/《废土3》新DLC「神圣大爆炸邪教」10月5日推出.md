<div class="topicContent front_content"><p>　　开发商 inXile 宣布旗下后启示录风格 RPG《废土3》即将迎来全新 DLC“神圣大爆炸邪教”（Cult of the Holy Detonation计，预计将于 10 月 5 日推出，预告片也一并公布。</p><p><iframe class="new_bilibili_player" src="http://player.bilibili.com/player.html?aid=762551061&bvid=BV1T64y1v7Tb&cid=396168316&page=1" scrolling="no" border="0" frameborder="no" framespacing="0" allowfullscreen="true" style="width: 819px;
