<div class="topicContent front_content"><p>曾开发了《黑暗之魂》桌游的开发商 Steamforged 宣布再次与万代南梦宫合作，推出《艾尔登法环》桌游，目前该游戏已在<a href="https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/steamforged/elden-ring-the-board-game">Kickstarter</a>上开启众筹页面。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/09/19/220919110914693_u162815.jpg" class referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></p><p>《艾尔登法环》桌游支持 1~4名褪色者在桌面上探索交界地的著名景点，并面对众多标志性的敌人，小到葛瑞克士兵，大至接肢君王等等，游戏采用非掷骰的战斗机制，玩家需要集中精力面对每一场遭遇战，制定好战斗策略并建立大局规划。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/09/19/220919110924347_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/09/19/220919110932590_u162815.jpg" class referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></p><p style="text-align: right;">来源：<a href="https://www.ign.com/articles/elden-ring-the-board-game-is-coming-to-kickstarter">IGN</a></p></div>
