<div class="topicContent front_content"><p>　　索尼 PlayStation Plus 会员新订阅服务在港服上线以来，补差价升档政策引起了许多争议。现在又有多名网友发现补差价升级政策悄悄发生了变化，无论剩余会员时长是多少，升级到二档三档会员需要补的差价有封顶，分别是 518 港币和 718 港币。一经付费升档则可以将所有剩余会员时长转换为更档位的订阅服务。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img style="width: 600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/06/04/22060411345882_u292926.jpg" title class referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width: 600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/06/04/220604113458209_u292926.jpg" title referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width: 600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/06/04/220604113458848_u292926.jpg" title referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></p><p>　　B 站用户 <a href="https://space.bilibili.com/279007309">@养蚂蚁的豆豆</a> 致电了 PlayStation 客服，询问该情况是系统出错还是正常情况，客服回应是「只要是系统算出的价格就没有问题」。</p><div class="vg_insert_video"><figure contenteditable="false"><iframe class="vg_insert_video_iframe new_bilibili" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen src="https://player.bilibili.com/player.html?aid=639680771&bvid=BV13Y4y1x71G&cid=737561828&page=1" height="640.125"></iframe><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_video_notice"></figcaption></figure><div class="vg_insert_video_cover bili_cover bili_cover_new" contenteditable="false" style="height:640.125px"><b class="bili_play"></b><span class="vg_insert_video_del" contenteditable="false"></span></div></div><p style="text-align: right;
