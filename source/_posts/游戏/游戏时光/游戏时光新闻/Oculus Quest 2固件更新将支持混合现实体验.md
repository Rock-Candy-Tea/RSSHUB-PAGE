<div class="topicContent front_content"><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　Oculus 日前发布公告，称旗下 VR 产品 Oculus Quest 2 将引入实验性应用编程接口</span>「Passthrough API」<span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">，为用户带来混合现实（Mixed Reality）体验。</span><br></p><p style="text-align: center;"><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/07/26/210726111829708_u202875.jpg" class referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></span></p><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　相关功能实装后，Oculus Quest 2 用户可以在现实环境中实现虚拟互动，例如在虚拟平面上绘画。</span></p><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/07/26/210726112447651_u202875.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　或是让自己的房间成为一间“远程会议室”。</span><br></p><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/07/26/210726112456910_u202875.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　Oculus </span><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">官方表示，混合现实功能不仅可用于协同办公，还能为玩家带来独特的游戏体验。遗憾的是，目前仅 Quest 2 用户可以完整体验全部功能。</span></p><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　「Passthrough API」接口将首先提供给 Unity 开发人员使用，日后将支持其它开发平台。官方将在今年晚些时候推出生产力版本，方便游戏开发者进行创作。</span></p><p style="text-align: center;"><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/07/26/21072611294621_u202875.jpg" class referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></span></p><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　最后，官方还提到「Passthrough API」注重保护个人隐私，用户的实景信息不会被窃取。  </span></p><p style="text-align: right;">来源：<a href="https://developer.oculus.com/blog/mixed-reality-with-passthrough/">Oculus</a>/<a href="https://gizmodo.com/oculus-new-experimental-api-blends-virtual-reality-with-1847356205">Gizmodo</a></p></div>
