<div class="topicContent front_content"><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　《黎明杀机》官推今日（5 月 18 日）宣布，游戏将与《生化危机》系列再次展开联动。联动新章节将于 2022 年内上线。</span><br></p><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/05/18/220518113423630_u202875.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　本次联动章节的标题为「W 计划」（Project W）。虽然官方尚未公布相关细节，但结合《生化危机》系列的剧情，「W 计划」与威斯克高度相关。也许在将来，我们能看到威斯克登上《黎明杀机》的舞台。</span><br></p><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/05/18/220518113442763_u202875.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　去年 6 月，《黎明杀机》曾与《生化危机》进行过联动。作为联动内容，《黎明杀机》添加了「里昂」「吉尔」等逃生者，并且上线了新地图「浣熊市警局」。</span><br></p><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/05/18/220518113454768_u202875.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><p style="text-align: right;">来源：<a href="https://twitter.com/DeadByBHVR/status/1526646323428171778">Twitter</a>/<a href="https://www.polygon.com/23075249/dead-by-daylight-resident-evil-project-w-new-chapter">Polygon</a></p></div>
