<div class="topicContent front_content"><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　EA 官网近期发布了旗下工作室，《尘埃5》开发商 Codemasters 的招聘公告。从公告内容来看，Codemasters 正在筹备某款新游戏。</span><br></p><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/10/11/211011180519983_u202875.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　</span><a href="https://ea.gr8people.com/jobs/167857/development-manager" style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">在招聘开发经理的公告中，</a><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">Codemasters 表示团队准备在《尘埃5》后投入下一个项目。Codemasters 将新项目描述为“十多年来团队最具野心且规模最大的游戏”。</span><br></p><p>　　此外 Codemasters 还在<a href="https://ea.gr8people.com/jobs/167843/senior-manager-engineering">另一份招聘公告</a>中提到，团队先前的工作重心一直放在《尘埃5》上。随着《尘埃5》的开发工作逐渐收尾，团队已在努力构思下一款 3A 级项目。<br></p><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/10/11/211011180634587_u202875.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　综上所述，Codemasters 的新作应该是一款颇具野心的 3A 级作品。不过现有情报终归有限，更多信息需等待后续官宣。</span><br></p><p style="text-align: right;">来源：<a href="https://ea.gr8people.com/jobs/167857/development-manager">EA</a>/<a href="https://exputer.com/news/games/codemasters-working-biggest-aaa-game/">Exputer</a>/<a href="https://www.vg247.com/codemasters-chesire-hiring-job-ad-biggest-game">VG247</a></p></div>
