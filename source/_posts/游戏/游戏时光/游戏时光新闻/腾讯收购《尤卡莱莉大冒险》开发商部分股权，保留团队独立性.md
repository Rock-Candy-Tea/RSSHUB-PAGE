Playtonic Friends 也会得到支持。</span><br></p><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/11/19/211119174307825_u202875.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　另据外媒 </span><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">Games Industry 报道，Playtonic 打算将员工人数增加一倍以上，未来将有三支开发团队制作新游戏。其中一款新作正是《尤卡莱莉大冒险》的续集。</span><br></p><p style="text-align: right;"><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">来源：</span><span style="letter-spacing: 1.3px;"><a href="https://www.playtonicgames.com/news/playtonic-is-levelling-up/">Playtonic Games</a>/</span><span style="letter-spacing: 1.3px;"><a href="https://www.gamesindustry.biz/articles/2021-11-18-yooka-laylee-developer-playtonic-plots-new-studios-following-major-tencent-investment">Games Industry</a></span><br></p></div>
