<div class="topicContent front_content"><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">索尼美服官网上线了 PlayStation PC games </span><a href="https://www.playstation.com/en-us/games/pc-games/" style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">标签页</a><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">，该网页展示了已经或即将登陆 PC 平台的由索尼发行的游戏，还包含了对这些游戏的疑问解答。</span><br></p><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/08/11/220811114242888_u202875.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><p><span style="background-color: initial;
