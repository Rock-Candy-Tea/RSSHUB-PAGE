<div class="topicContent front_content"><p><span style="letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　</span><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">根据官方社交媒体暗示，太空狼人杀《Among Us》将增加知名系列联动皮肤。即将到来的形象是《光环》中的「士官长」和《瑞奇与叮当》中的「瑞奇」。</span><br></p><p style="text-align: center;"><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;"><img style="width: 480px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/03/31/220331105646715_u77470.jpg" class referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width: 480px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/03/31/220331105704980_u77470.jpg" class referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></span></p><p style="text-align: left;"><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　在《Among Us》官推预告后，「士官长」皮肤由《光环》官方正式公布，而《瑞奇与叮当》联动则由《Among Us》官推自己公布。这些新皮肤的价格和具体推出日期尚未明确。</span><br></p><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/03/31/220331112524916_u202875.jpg" class referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class>「士官长」联动皮肤</figcaption></figure></div><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/03/31/220331112619129_u202875.jpg" class referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class>「瑞奇与叮当」联动皮肤</figcaption></figure></div><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　《Among Us》此前还出过不少联动皮肤，比如和《英雄联盟》的联动，还有来自《惊声尖叫》的「鬼面杀手」。《Among Us》的角色也会在其它游戏亮相，例如 Owlchemy Labs 开发的 VR 游戏《Cosmonious High》。</span><br></p><p style="text-align: right;"><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">来源：<a href="https://www.gamespot.com/articles/among-us-is-adding-master-chief-and-ratchet-clank-dlc-skins-it-seems/1100-6502040/">Game Spot</a></span></p></div>
