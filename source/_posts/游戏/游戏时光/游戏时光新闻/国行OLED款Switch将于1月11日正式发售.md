<div class="topicContent front_content"><p>　　腾讯今日官宣将在国内发售 OLED 款任天堂 Switch 主机，预计将于 1 月 11 日发售，今日起（1 月 4 日）开放预售，有“白色”和“电光蓝·电光红”两款配色套装可供选择，官方定价每套 2599 元。  <br></p><p style="text-align: center;"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/01/04/220104093438355_u162815.jpg" class referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></p><p>　　OLED 款 Switch 主机采用了色彩更艳丽的 7.0 寸 OLED 屏幕，采用了更加时尚的外观，并相较原版增加了角度调节更自由宽广的转轴式支架、全新扬声器、增至64GB的主机内存。OLED 套装将包含一对 Joy-Con、Joy-Con 握把、Joy-Con 腕带、Switch 基座、电源适配器、高速 HDMI 线。  </p><p style="text-align: center;"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/01/04/220104093445504_u162815.jpg" class referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/01/04/220104093451683_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/01/04/220104093451250_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/01/04/220104093451151_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/01/04/220104093454219_u162815.jpg" class referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/01/04/220104094226230_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/01/04/220104094226996_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/01/04/220104094226333_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></p></div>
