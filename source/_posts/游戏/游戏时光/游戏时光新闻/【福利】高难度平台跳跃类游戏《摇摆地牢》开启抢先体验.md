平台跳跃类独立游戏《摇摆地牢》现已在 Steam 平台开启抢先体验，国区售价 6 元，首发期间有 9 折特惠。</p><p>　　Steam 商店页：<a href="https://store.steampowered.com/app/1331240/_Swing_Dungeon/">传送门</a></p><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/09/14/210914112052929_u524.jpg" class="vg_insert_img_onfocus" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><p>　　根据介绍，本作创作灵感来源于《像素鸟》《掘地求生》等作品，<span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">玩家将扮演一只爱吐舌头的青蛙，靠自己灵活的舌头自由探索地牢，寻找稀奇古怪的道具，挑战可爱多变的怪物，解谜创意的机关，获得丰厚的宝藏。</span></p><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/09/14/210914112122606_u524.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><p><span style="background-color: initial;
