<div class="topicContent front_content"><p>　　新一周的英国实体游戏销量榜已经公布，本周榜单的最大影响因素是<span style="letter-spacing: 1.3px;">亚马逊的 Prime Day 促销，让许多老游戏重回榜单。《</span><span style="letter-spacing: 1.3px;">乐高星球大战 天行者传奇</span><span style="letter-spacing: 1.3px; background-color: initial;">》重回榜首，销量同比增长了 83%。</span></p><p style="text-align: center;"><span style="letter-spacing: 1.3px; background-color: initial;"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/07/19/220719110552595_u77470.jpg" class referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></span></p><p><span style="letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　</span><span style="letter-spacing: 1.3px; background-color: initial;">这些老游戏也得到了 </span><span style="letter-spacing: 1.3px;">Prime Day 促销的助力，比如第 6 位的《</span><span style="letter-spacing: 1.3px;">孤岛惊魂6</span><span style="letter-spacing: 1.3px; background-color: initial;">》销量同比增加 </span><span style="letter-spacing: 1.3px;">275%，两周前被挤出榜单的《</span><span style="letter-spacing: 1.3px;">刺客信条 英灵殿》</span><span style="letter-spacing: 1.3px;">销量激增 441% 后，重新回到第 11 位。</span><span style="letter-spacing: 1.3px;">第 29 位的《漫威复仇者》亦有 478% 的同比增长。</span></p><p style="text-align: center;"><span style="letter-spacing: 1.3px;"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/07/19/220719110824626_u77470.jpg" class referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></span></p><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　</span><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">还有一些游戏的销量增长更为惊人，第 14 位的《</span><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">光环 无限</span><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">》录得 </span><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">692% 的同比增长，</span><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">《看门狗：军团》甚至以 777% 的同比增长重回第 20。</span></p><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/07/19/220719110858178_u77470.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　过去两周的榜首《F1 2022》以同比下降 46% 的销量退居第二，上周发售的《风之少年 克罗诺亚1&2 乘风归来》销量同比下降 77%，从第 5 位跌至第 28 位。</span></p><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/07/19/22071911001615_u77470.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><p><span style="background-color: initial;
