<div class="topicContent front_content"><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">      《战地 2042》在本周开启了小规模 Beta 测试，虽然参加内测的玩家签署了保密协议，但本作已经有</span><a href="https://www.videogameschronicle.com/news/30-mins-more-battlefield-2042-gameplay-has-leaked-from-the-private-playtest/" style="background-color: initial;
