<div class="topicContent front_content"><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　为庆祝全球无障碍宣传日（5 月 19 日），PlayStation 官博日前发布了《战神 诸神黄昏》辅助功能介绍文。</span><br></p><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/05/20/220520133417733_u202875.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><p><span style="background-color: initial;
