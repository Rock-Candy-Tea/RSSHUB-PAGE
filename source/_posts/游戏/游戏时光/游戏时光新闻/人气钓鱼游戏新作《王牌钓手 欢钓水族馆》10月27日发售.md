<div class="topicContent front_content"><p>　　继街机游戏系列“王牌钓手”（Fishing Spirits）于 2019 年 7 月在日本发售推出 Switch 平台移植作品后，本作在日本玩家间掀起了一场钓鱼热潮，游戏累计销量突破 80 万份，万代南梦宫现宣布 Switch 新作《王牌钓手 欢钓水族馆》即将到来，将于 10 月 27 日发售，支持中文。</p><p><iframe class="new_bilibili_player" src="http://player.bilibili.com/player.html?aid=556620995&bvid=BV1Je4y1D7gj&cid=790284490&page=1" scrolling="no" border="0" frameborder="no" framespacing="0" allowfullscreen="true" style="width: 1035px; height: 646.875px;"> </iframe></p><p style="text-align: right;"><a href="https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1Je4y1D7gj?share_source=copy_web">视频地址</a></p><p>　　《王牌钓手 欢钓水族馆》游戏舞台来到了“欢钓水族馆主题乐园”，在这里多达 4 名玩家可以一同享受钓鱼的乐趣，或是各种水上小游戏，玩家也可以享受以保护海洋生态可持续发展主题的剧情模式，多达 250 种鱼类等待玩家捕获，最终填充满自己的水族馆。本作还将同步推出包含“钓竿” Joy-Con 外界拓展的实体套组版。<br></p><hr class="vg_hr" contenteditable="false"><p style="text-align: center;"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/08/01/220801114918469_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/08/01/220801114922506_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/08/01/220801114928793_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/08/01/220801114928949_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/08/01/220801114932659_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/08/01/220801114932699_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/08/01/220801114932424_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/08/01/220801114932804_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/08/01/220801114937415_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/08/01/220801114939493_u162815.jpg" class referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></p><p style="text-align: right;">来源：<a href="https://nintendoeverything.com/bandai-namco-bringing-ace-angler-fishing-spirits-to-switch/">Nintendo Everything</a></p></div>
