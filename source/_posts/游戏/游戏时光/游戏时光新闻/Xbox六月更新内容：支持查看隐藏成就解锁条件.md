<div class="topicContent front_content"><p>　　微软发布了 6 月的 Xbox 更新内容公告，宣布在本次更新后，将允许玩家查看游戏的隐藏成就解锁条件。</p><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/06/01/220601104326751_u8855.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><p>　　在更新完成后，玩家可以在游戏中打开「游戏指南」，选择「游戏活动」并进入「成就」页面，随后通过「查看详情信息」来揭露任何成就的名称、描述、点数等内容。</p><p>　　在查看过后，玩家可以选择重新隐藏或继续显示详情信息。</p><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/06/01/22060110485683_u8855.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/06/01/220601104902827_u8855.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><p style="text-align: right;">来源：<a href="https://news.xbox.com/en-us/2022/05/31/june-xbox-update/?ranMID=24542&ranEAID=kXQk6*ivFEQ&ranSiteID=kXQk6.ivFEQ-H7brlirejxCWqXLTQELHZg&epi=kXQk6.ivFEQ-H7brlirejxCWqXLTQELHZg&irgwc=1&OCID=AID2200057_aff_7593_1243925&tduid=%28ir__ozm12adfc0kf6khjbqeacrzpy22xvy3b1gv6xg2y00%29%287593%29%281243925%29%28kXQk6.ivFEQ-H7brlirejxCWqXLTQELHZg%29%28%29&irclickid=_ozm12adfc0kf6khjbqeacrzpy22xvy3b1gv6xg2y00">Xbox Wire</a></p></div>
