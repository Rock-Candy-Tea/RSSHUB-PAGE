<div class="topicContent front_content"><p>　　2021 E3 上育碧曾与任天堂宣布合作推出“马力欧+疯狂兔子”系列新作《马力欧+疯狂兔子：星耀之愿》 ，育碧官方商城现提前本作将于 10 月 20 日在 Switch 平台推出。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/06/28/220628075720753_u162815.jpg" class referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></p><p>　　《马力欧+疯狂兔子：星耀之愿》是一款回合制策略战棋游戏，玩家可以将马力欧、路易吉、桃花公主和疯兔们组建成战斗小队，乘上太空船前往银河的各个角落，与众多头目作战并救出“星耀”们。官方承诺本作将在发售后推出后续内容更新，包含游戏季票的黄金版游戏也将同步发售。<br></p><p style="text-align: center;"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/06/28/220628075858432_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/06/28/220628075858599_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/06/28/220628075858566_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/06/28/220628075858867_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/06/28/22062807585879_u162815.jpg" class referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/06/28/22062807585898_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></p><p style="text-align: right;">来源：<a href="https://www.gematsu.com/2022/06/mario-rabbids-sparks-of-hope-launches-october-20">Gematsu</a></p></div>
