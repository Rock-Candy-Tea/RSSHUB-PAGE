<div class="topicContent front_content"><p>　　良笑社（Good Smile Company）最新商品现已到货，今日（7 月 6 日）开启预售的有“希尔瓦娜斯·风行者”“毒液”两款粘土人手办以及“索菲亚· F· 希琳”figma 手办。  <br></p><h4>希尔瓦娜斯·风行者  </h4><p>  <br>　　人气 MMORPG《魔兽世界》中率领着被遗忘者的女妖之王“希尔瓦娜斯·风行者”化身为超可动式样粘土人登场，这款手办将拥有“普通脸”和“女妖脸”两个脸部替换配件，手部配件则有“弓”和“箭矢”“光环”三种。官方旗舰店定价 357 元，预计将于 2022 年 1 月发货。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/07/06/210706133945253_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/07/06/210706133945616_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/07/06/210706133945289_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/07/06/210706133945690_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/07/06/210706133945309_u162815.jpg" class referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></p><h4>毒液

</h4><p>　　漫威漫画中的经典角色“毒液”也化身成为了粘土人，该手办有“普通脸”和“瞪视脸”两个脸部配件可选，身体配件则有“网配件”“共生体配件”“肩用头部”“死灵剑”“带粘液的舌头”五种，可用其搭配出剧中经典情景，也可与预订未来推出的漫威角色组合摆放。官方旗舰店定价 547 元，预计将于 2022 年 3 月发货。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/07/06/210706134001869_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/07/06/210706134002731_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/07/06/210706134002984_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/07/06/210706134002211_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/07/06/210706134002678_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/07/06/2107061340022_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/07/06/210706134003121_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/07/06/210706134009725_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/07/06/210706134015497_u162815.jpg" class referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></p><h4>索菲亚· F· 希琳（泳装兔女郎）

</h4><p>　　该手办的角色为画师高峰ナダレ的原创作品《BUNNY SUIT PLANNING》中的“索菲亚· F· 希琳”，身穿泳装风格的兔女郎装，表情配件有“笑脸”“得意脸”“焦急脸”“闭目笑脸”四种，身体配件有“盾板冲浪板”“水枪长枪”“手持水枪”三种，其中长枪的各部分可以分离，并搭配上延长配件或手枪配件可改造成玩家原创的武器，此外还有一个可大幅改变形象的“戴墨镜的替换前发”配件。该手办旗舰店定价 655 元，将附带“专用底座”特典，搭配 figma 支架可再现出各种姿势，预计将于 2022 年 6 月发货。  <br></p><p style="text-align: center;"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/07/06/210706134038699_u162815.jpg" class referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/07/06/210706134038979_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/07/06/210706134038618_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/07/06/210706134038188_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/07/06/210706134038147_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/07/06/210706134038915_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/07/06/210706134038471_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/07/06/210706134038129_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/07/06/210706134038735_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/07/06/210706134101428_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/07/06/21070613410724_u162815.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></p></div>
