<div class="topicContent front_content"><p>　　《指环王》动画电影《The Lord of the Rings: The War of the Rohirrim（指环王：洛汗人之战）》制作决定。这是华纳兄弟与新线影业的一个合作项目，该片由神山健治执导，华纳计划将在全球发行该片。</p><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/06/11/210611105619523_u93176.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　根据外媒 Polygon 的消息，这部动画将讲述“圣盔谷要塞背后不为人知的故事”，“深入探究中土世界最传奇人物之一的生活和血泊时代，强大的洛汗国国王”。</span><br></p><p>　　另外，今年是电影《指环王：护戒使者》上映 20 周年。</p><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/06/11/210611105732355_u93176.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><p style="text-align: right;"><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">来源：<a href="https://www.polygon.com/movies/22528048/lord-of-the-rings-war-of-the-rohirrim-animated-movie">Polygon</a></span><br></p></div>
