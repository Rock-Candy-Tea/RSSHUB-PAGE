<div class="topicContent front_content"><p>　　有网友发现《DC Universe Online》的开发商 Dimensional Ink Games 正在制作一款漫威题材的 MMO 游戏，他们在其母公司 Enad Global 7 的<a href="https://www.enadglobal7.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/11/Q3-2021-Investor-Presentation-November.pdf?fbclid=IwAR20HHFYp81pBDkzFt8tUWcAhldNK9sd0zwRKyy801wf1tSTtO6TeiMnde0">投资者简报</a>中找到了这款游戏的一些细节。</p><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/11/30/211130113903936_u93176.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　投资者简报中显示，Dimensional Ink Games 正在开发一款基于漫威IP的3A级别多人在线网游，游戏由《城市英雄》的制作人 Jack Emmert 领导。</span><br></p><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2021/11/30/211130113748550_u93176.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　在 Jack Emmert 的个人<a href="https://www.linkedin.com/authwall?trk=gf&trkInfo=AQF1yGf2Sqsf_gAAAX1u7Rkg6AJEUH0J-A06d0P-CVmntrL4AJUq3tnOtT9OUljisuuuAHgVf43waRmsF4id2CmKrSlwb3lD1HPeVIPrM36thauZZTmTAM8WvICaRIROxWwtB-g=&originalReferer=https://www.gamespot.com/articles/dc-universe-online-devs-are-creating-a-new-marvel-mmo/1100-6498408/&sessionRedirect=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.linkedin.com%2Fin%2Fjackemmert%2F">领英页面</a>中也提到，他正在负责一款「全新的、未公开的 MMORPG」。</span><br></p><p>　　除了投资者简报外，这款漫威 MMO 并没有更多消息，也许它还有很长一段时间才能与玩家们见面。</p><p style="text-align: right;">来源：<a href="https://www.gamespot.com/articles/dc-universe-online-devs-are-creating-a-new-marvel-mmo/1100-6498408/">gamespot</a></p></div>
