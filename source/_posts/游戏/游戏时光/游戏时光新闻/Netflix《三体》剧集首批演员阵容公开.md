Weiss 担任制片人。</p><p style="text-align: right;">来源：<a href="https://deadline.com/2021/10/3-body-problem-benedict-wong-tsai-chin-john-bradley-liam-cunningham-among-12-cast-netflix-1234864582/">deadline</a></p></div>
