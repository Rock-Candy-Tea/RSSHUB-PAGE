<div class="topicContent front_content"><p><font style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">      《最后生还者》真人剧中</font><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">乔尔的演员 </span><font style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">Pedro Pascal</font><font style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">（代表作《权力的游戏》《毒枭》等）在接受 </font><font style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;"></font><a href="https://www.gq-magazine.co.uk/culture/article/pedro-pascal-interview-2022"><font style="background-color: initial;
