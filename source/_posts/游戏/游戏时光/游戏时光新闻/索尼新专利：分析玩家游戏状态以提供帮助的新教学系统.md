<div class="topicContent front_content"><p>　　索尼的又一份新专利在近日被发现，该专利为分析玩家的游戏状态以提供相应帮助的新教学系统。</p><div class="vg_insert_img"><figure contenteditable="false"><img style="width:600px;" src="https://img01.vgtime.com/game/cover/2022/01/05/220105123700632_u8855.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption contenteditable="true" class="vg_insert_img_notice"></figcaption></figure></div><p>　　这项专利在去年 6 月被提交，并于 12 月 30 日正式公布。索尼称，该系统可用于“减少各类型游戏的玩家在尝试新游戏时的挫败感”。<span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;"><br></span></p><p><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">　　据</span><a href="https://pdfaiw.uspto.gov/.aiw?PageNum=0&docid=20210402302&IDKey=73EFC212500F&HomeUrl=http%3A%2F%2Fappft.uspto.gov%2Fnetacgi%2Fnph-Parser%3FSect1%3DPTO1%2526Sect2%3DHITOFF%2526d%3DPG01%2526p%3D1%2526u%3D%2Fnetahtml%2FPTO%2Fsrchnum.html%2526r%3D1%2526f%3DG%2526l%3D50%2526s1%3D20210402302.PGNR.%2526OS%3D%2526RS%3D" style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">专利文件</a><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">描述，这项设计能够识别玩家在游戏过程中何时“低于所需的能力阈值”，然后向玩家提供相应的提示。而专利说明图显示，系统会录制并分析玩家的游戏行为与过程，随后将教学信息以文字框的形式呈现在游戏画面内。</span></p><p style="text-align: right;"><span style="background-color: initial; letter-spacing: 1.3px;">来源：<a href="https://www.videogameschronicle.com/news/a-new-playstation-patent-describes-a-system-for-coaching-players/">VGC</a></span></p></div>
