【巴黎世家推出PS5联动服装，比PS5主机还要贵】知名时尚品牌巴黎世家推出与PS5联动服装，分别是黑红两种颜色的T恤和一套卫衣。这套联动服装前身和后背印有PS5的Logo和发售日的纪念字样，侧臂上则写有PlayStation字体样式的巴黎世家。不过，这套联动服装的价格也是令人咋舌，其中T恤官方售价675美元（约4307元），卫衣官方售价875美元（约5584元）。相比之下，一台PS5主机还算是便宜的了（PS5官方售价499美元，约3184元）。不知道有没有玩家已经想要入手的呢？<br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2021/06/02/1419170142.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2021/06/02/1419189311.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2021/06/02/1419190974.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2021/06/02/1419573485.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2021/06/02/1419204190.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2021/06/02/1419210985.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2021/06/02/1419220878.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2021/06/02/1419235303.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2021/06/02/1419243665.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2021/06/02/1419250347.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
