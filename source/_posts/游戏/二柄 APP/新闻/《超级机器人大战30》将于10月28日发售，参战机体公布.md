【《超级机器人大战30》将于10月28日发售，参战机体公布】在刚刚万代南梦宫所举办的“钢之超感谢祭2021”直播活动上，公布了30周年纪念作《超级机器人大战30》的最新情报。本作将于2021年10月28日登录PS4，Switch，Steam平台。男女主角以及敌方人物的人设和机甲设定公布。本作参战机体除了此前公布的《高达Z》《叛逆的鲁鲁修》等作品还有新登场的《古力特》等。<br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202107111958501175html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202107111959105322html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202107111959106859html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202107111959104462html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202107111959130434html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202107111959149057html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202107111959331257html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202107111959331690html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202107111959330904html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202107111959331709html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202107111959339802html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
