【CDPR官方商店上架叶奈法日本主题雕像】今日，CDPR终于上架了叶奈法的日本系列雕像，在此前，官方就曾推出过杰洛特和希里的同系列雕像。CDPR将这个 17 英寸雕像塑造成一位来自古代日本的强大女忍者。叶奈法站立在一座被毁坏的寺庙屋顶上，屋顶即将淹没在一个废弃战场的水域中。背负忍刀与手爪，正计划下一步行动……本款雕像目前可以在CDPR官方商店预定，预定价格380美元，预计将于2021年12月发货。<br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202107111827419376html_img.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202107111827417096html_img.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202107111827426274html_img.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202107111829530557html_img.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202107111829532984html_img.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202107111829539632html_img.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202107111829530624html_img.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202107111829530248html_img.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202107111829530284html_img.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202107111829540466html_img.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202107111829540555html_img.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202107111829542894html_img.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202107111829549504html_img.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202107111829546786html_img.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202107111829544736html_img.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
