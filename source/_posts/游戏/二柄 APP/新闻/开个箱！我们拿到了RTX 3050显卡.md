【 开个箱！我们拿到了RTX 3050显卡】英伟达在本月初的CES上公布了全新的入门级桌面显卡RTX 3050。感谢影驰的支持，我们也已经提前拿到了这张显卡。这里为大家带来影驰RTX 3050金属大师Mini的开箱。影驰的这张RTX 3050属于金属大师系列，所以从外观上就可以看出非常浓厚的硬派金属风，银灰色的外观更能勾勒出硬朗的线条，同时全金属外壳和背板也让这张卡更具分量。RTX 3050拥有2560个CUDA核心，加速频率1.78 GHz，配备8GB DDR6显存。晚些时候，我们也会在实机的游戏中来测试一下RTX 3050这张显卡的实际游戏性能，敬请关注。<br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2022/01/26/1128540160.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2022/01/26/1128564743.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2022/01/26/1129048784.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2022/01/26/1129008064.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2022/01/26/1128597383.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2022/01/26/1129038199.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2022/01/26/1129013588.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2022/01/26/1128572915.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2022/01/26/1129052082.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
