【《艾尔登法环》玩家评价展示】开放世界动作游戏《艾尔登法环》已经发售快一周了，各大平台的玩家正在体验这款人气游戏的超凡魅力，我们挑选了不同平台的玩家评价给大家展示（仅作为活动作品展示，并非为最后中奖作品）。同时我们的有奖评价活动将在下周一3月7日零点截止，各位柄友记得给《艾尔登法环》撰写原创评价（注：无效评论会被清理，从而无法获取参与活动资格）！<br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/3/2022/03/01/1455041361.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/3/2022/03/01/1455062643.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/3/2022/03/01/1455069520.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/3/2022/03/01/1455070398.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/3/2022/03/01/1455099978.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/3/2022/03/01/1455107686.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/3/2022/03/01/1455111693.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/3/2022/03/01/1455126777.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/3/2022/03/01/1455135303.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/3/2022/03/01/1455142649.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
