【经典国产RPG《复活之秦殇前传》上架Steam，9月发售】在《秦殇》上架Steam之后，另外一部作品《复活之秦殇前传》也同样上架了Steam平台，游戏也将于2021年9月份发售。游戏讲述了战国末期，蓝雄的女儿蓝薇为了寻找传说中能够使亡人复活的神器而踏入中原大地，开始了一段不寻常的旅程。由于本作的发行商是早年海外版的GMX Media，因此游戏暂时不支持中文。<br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2021/09/17/1025484493.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2021/09/17/1025492832.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2021/09/17/1025509636.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2021/09/17/1025514414.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2021/09/17/1025528284.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2021/09/17/1025538321.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2021/09/17/1025544482.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2021/09/17/1025559191.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2021/09/17/1025563954.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
