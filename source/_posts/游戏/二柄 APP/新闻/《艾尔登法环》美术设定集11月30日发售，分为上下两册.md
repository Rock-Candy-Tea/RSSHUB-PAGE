【《艾尔登法环》美术设定集11月30日发售，分为上下两册】《艾尔登法环》官方美术设定集将于2022年11月30日发售，分为“Volume I”和“Volume II”两册，每册约400页，售价均为4400日元。“Volume I”包含游戏开场OP、角色/盔甲艺术设定画等内容；“Volume II”包含敌人和各种武器艺术设定画等内容；另有两册成套，包装与印刷更加精美，适合收藏的豪华版，售价17600日元。<br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2022/09/02/1652527868.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2022/09/02/1652522511.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2022/09/02/1652524441.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2022/09/02/1652528010.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2022/09/02/1652523552.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2022/09/02/1652528403.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2022/09/02/1652523690.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2022/09/02/1652528474.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2022/09/02/1652520461.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
