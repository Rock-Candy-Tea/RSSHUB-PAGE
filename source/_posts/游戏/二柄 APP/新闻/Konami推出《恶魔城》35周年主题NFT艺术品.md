【Konami推出《恶魔城》35周年主题NFT艺术品】Konami近日在《恶魔城》35周年来临之际，宣布推出主题纪念NFT（非同质化代币Non-Fungible Token的简称）艺术品。据悉作为KONAMI MEMORIAL NFT产品，本次推出的十四件作品均采用了《恶魔城》历代经典游戏场景以及BGM重新绘制而成，预计将于1月13日在NFT市场OpenSea上开始拍卖。<br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202201061751470956html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202201061751492778html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202201061751534976html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202201061751564638html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202201061751570892html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
