【五菱宏光S上线《地平线 极限竞速5》，大量本土气息涂装登场】“昨晚我在秋名山输给一辆五菱宏光，他的速度很快，用惯性漂移过弯...”五菱宏光S（Wuling Sunshine）已经在《地平线 极限竞速5》中正式上线。通过完成秋季系列赛的赛事活动，得到25分即可获得这台“国产战神”。而在五菱宏光S上线之后，游戏中的涂装社区也是涌现出了一大批富含本土气息的涂装，像是什么贴着专业补漏、阳台防水招牌，或是写着“决战秋名山，败者留下车标”，又或者是印着“XX快递”的涂装引入眼帘，几乎毫无违和感！<br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2022/02/11/1955323831.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2022/02/11/1955250572.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2022/02/11/1955268120.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2022/02/11/1955281486.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2022/02/11/1955275799.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2022/02/11/1955296267.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2022/02/11/1955306934.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2022/02/11/1955313010.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2022/02/11/1955324253.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
