【“J.F.REY”推出由小岛秀夫监制的《死亡搁浅》山姆同款眼镜】今日法国眼镜品牌“J.F.REY”宣布由旗下知名设计师jean - francois REY设计，小岛秀夫监制的《死亡搁浅》联动眼镜即将上市。本次共推出了四款产品，其中HKxJF01是可以变形的款式，共两种颜色可选；HKxJF02为时尚型，提供三色可选；而HKxJF03则为套装版，共为眼罩+眼镜，也就是游戏中山姆的款式。据悉“J.F.REY”是小岛爱用的品牌，不仅有着时尚且独特的设计，并且在日常生活中的使用也十分方便。<br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202110181903200826html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202110181903230166html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202110181903260419html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202110181903344867html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202110181903349138html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202110181903421886html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202110181903516764html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202110181903561837html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202110181903565597html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202110181903582807html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
