【《破晓传说》舞台剧已在日本开始公演】万代南梦宫已于3月26日首演了由人气RPG游戏改编而成的舞台剧《破晓传说：被赋予希望的解放者们New Attempt》。本次由万代南梦宫旗下专门负责动作捕捉的部门“MIRAIKEN studio”负责搭设演出中的场景，通过立体屏显来投射出游戏中的各个场景，从图片来看还是十分还原的。公演的形式为线上，全球玩家可以汇聚一起观看并随时在聊天栏中沟通。公演期截至至2022年4月27日。<br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202203281906586736html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202203281907017200html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202203281907127469html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202203281907123901html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202203281907133336html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202203281907131282html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202203281907121348html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202203281907197575html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202203281907244116html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202203281907275368html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
