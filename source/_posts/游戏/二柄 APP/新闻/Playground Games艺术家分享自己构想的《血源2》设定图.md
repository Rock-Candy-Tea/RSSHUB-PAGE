【Playground Games艺术家分享自己构想的《血源2》设定图】Playground Games（《极限竞速:地平线》系列开发商）的概念艺术家Thomas Chamberlain-Keen在个人ArtStation主页上，分享了一批自己所构想的《血源2》设定图，这其中不仅在所有设定图上都添加了UI系统，甚至还专门基于游戏玩法详细设计了一些武器。Thomas Chamberlain-Keen所构想的《血源2》艺术风格与原作的黑暗氛围相距甚远，并不像维多利亚时代风格的亚楠，而是具有更加开放和明亮的环境。<br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2021/04/28/1520325051.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2021/04/28/1520337019.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2021/04/28/1520356407.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2021/04/28/1520360445.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2021/04/28/1520372757.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2021/04/28/1520389149.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2021/04/28/1520397508.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2021/04/28/1520400148.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2021/04/28/1520413677.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2021/04/28/1520420130.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2021/04/28/1520433637.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2021/04/28/1520452782.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
