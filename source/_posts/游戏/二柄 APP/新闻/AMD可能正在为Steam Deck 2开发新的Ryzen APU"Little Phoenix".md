【AMD可能正在为Steam Deck 2开发Ryzen APU】PCGamesN报道，据科技播主Moore's Law Is Dead消息，AMD正在为Steam Deck 2打造一款代号为"Little Phoenix"的Ryzen APU，该芯片将采用Zen 4和RDNA 3架构，并拥有4核8线程。当然这并不意味着Steam Deck的下一代会很快推出。因为新机型的规划一般会在很早就展开，并且会持续多年的开发。且目前Steam Deck尚处于供不应求的状态，Valve并不急于发布新的掌上设备。<br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202206180954198557html_img.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202206180955324362html_img.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202206180955364039html_img.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202206180955397349html_img.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
