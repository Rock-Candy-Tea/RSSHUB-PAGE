【《艾尔登法环》Steam首日在线玩家峰值突破76万】据Steam官网显示，截止至目前《艾尔登法环》首日在线玩家峰值已突破761,972人，排名Steam热门游戏榜单第一，该数据也超越了之前FromSoftware其他游戏的首日在线玩家峰值记录（例如之前《只狼》首日在线玩家峰值约12万）。<br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2022/02/25/1007313874.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2022/02/25/1007320811.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2022/02/25/1007325495.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2022/02/25/1007330139.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
