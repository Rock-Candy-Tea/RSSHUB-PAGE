【萌系种田模拟养成游戏《PuffPals: Island Skies》公布】萌系种田模拟养成游戏《PuffPals: Island Skies》宣布将于3月下旬在Kickstarter平台开启众筹，玩家在游戏中除了可以种田模拟经营自己的农场之外，还可以前往全新区域进行冒险探索，或是邀请遇到的NPC角色前往自己的农场。游戏目前已确认会登陆PC/移动端平台，后续将根据众筹情况再考虑是否正式推出Switch版本，游戏具体推出时间待定。<br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2022/03/16/1555292938.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2022/03/16/1555303378.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2022/03/16/1555318357.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2022/03/16/1555327043.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2022/03/16/1555337579.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2022/03/16/1555344233.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2022/03/16/1555353985.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2022/03/16/1555381612.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
