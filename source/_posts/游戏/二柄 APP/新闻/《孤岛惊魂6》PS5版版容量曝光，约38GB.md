【《孤岛惊魂6》PS5版版容量曝光，约38GB】PlayStation Game Size发文表示，目前在PlayStation数据库中发现了PS5版《孤岛惊魂6》容量为38.945 GB，但这是没有包含首日补丁的容量。作为对比前作《孤岛惊魂5》在发布时大约30GB，然后经过各种更新达到了大约50GB。游戏正式版将于10月7日登陆Xbox Series、Xbox One、PS5、PS4、PC平台。<br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202109091147066264html_img.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202109091147065594html_img.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202109091148293128html_img.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2021/09/09/1158298654.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2021/09/09/1158293701.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2021/09/09/1158293693.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
