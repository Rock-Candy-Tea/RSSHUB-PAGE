【2021 英雄联盟全球总决赛将在上海、武汉、成都等五个城市举办】据英雄联盟官方微博消息，2021英雄联盟全球总决赛将在上海、青岛、武汉、成都和深圳等五座中国城市举办，具体赛程地点将于随后公布。来自各地区联赛的顶级队伍将共同争夺11月6日于深圳举办的决赛席位，届时各队伍也将在此一决雌雄。<br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2021/06/16/1703190925.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2021/06/16/1703155060.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2021/06/16/1703154312.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2021/06/16/1703164443.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2021/06/16/1703178368.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2021/06/16/1703187093.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2021/06/16/1703218111.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
