【二柄鉴赏团《创尸纪》评测】游戏中收集养成加上僵尸的元素，让游戏令人耳目一新。拥有完善的引导教学也十分适合新手玩家上手。但在操作和剧情上还有不少需要打磨的地方。游戏中收集和种植的比重太高，NPC又总让玩家制作一些用处不大的物品来拖延游戏时长，导致战斗失去了本该带给玩家的刺激感。总体来说本作适合喜欢慢节奏但又想拥有难度不高的战斗元素的玩家。在后续更新中，如果能解决键鼠操作问题并加入更直观的物品获取引导或许可以吸引更多的玩家。<br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/2orHJySONiy2tBet39HBg/2022/05/23/1029327058.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/2orHJySONiy2tBet39HBg/2022/05/23/1031023933.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/2orHJySONiy2tBet39HBg/2022/05/23/1031113103.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/2orHJySONiy2tBet39HBg/2022/05/23/1032296656.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/2orHJySONiy2tBet39HBg/2022/05/23/1032293065.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/2orHJySONiy2tBet39HBg/2022/05/23/1033425519.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/2orHJySONiy2tBet39HBg/2022/05/23/1033523848.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/2orHJySONiy2tBet39HBg/2022/05/23/1034008190.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/2orHJySONiy2tBet39HBg/2022/05/23/1034085754.png" " referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://news.diershoubing.com/article_info/ios/51983/" video": null" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://news.diershoubing.com/article_info/ios/51983/" article_id": 51983&#125;" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
