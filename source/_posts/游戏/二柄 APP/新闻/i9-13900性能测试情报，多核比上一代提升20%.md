【i9-13900性能测试情报，多核比上一代提升20%】近日，外媒Videocardz带来了非超频型号的i9-13900的相关情报。据悉，英特尔i9-13900将会是英特尔推出的首批65W型号CPU之一，i9-13900为8大核+16小核总共24核32线程设计，配有8MB L2缓存，36MB L3缓存。根据测试，i9-13900达到了5.57GHz，单核测试成绩2130分，多核测试成绩20131分。与i9-12900相比，单核测试成绩提高了10％，多核测试成绩提高了21％。<br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202208281508357226html_img.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202208281508355175html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202208281508359696html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202208281508353990html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
