【良笑正式公布Max Factory粘土人系列“大神“白野威】良笑近日正式公布由Max Factory所打造的的粘土人系列最新手办“大神“白野威。以Capcom旗下的人气游戏《大神》中的初代大神，天照白野威为原型，并由原角色设计师吉村健一郎负责检修制作。本次分为通常版和DX版可供选择，普通版提供一个可换脸型，一对可变为坐姿的可替换后肢，神器“八咫镜”以及地台；而DX版在此基础上提供了完整的三神器“天业云剣”“八尺瓊勾玉”，售价分别为7000日元&8800日元（约420元/520元，均含税），现已开启预约，预计2022年6月出货。<br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202108281109231212html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202108281109238492html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202108281109236838html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202108281109420280html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202108281109498933html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202108281109497471html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202108281109506323html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202108281109503084html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202108281109533258html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202108281109573819html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202108281109570902html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
