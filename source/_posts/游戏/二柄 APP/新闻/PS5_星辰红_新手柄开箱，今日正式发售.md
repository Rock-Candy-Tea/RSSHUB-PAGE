【PS5“星辰红”新手柄开箱，今日正式发售】PlayStation国行PS5新配色手柄“星辰红”与“午夜黑”今日正式发售，我们也为大家带来了“星辰红”这款配色的手柄开箱。这款手柄深红中带有微微的紫色，反光之处又衬出些许粉红。在不同的光照情况下颜色也会有少许的变化。手柄正面水晶按键中间的图标也是用粉红色来点缀。和之前一样，手柄底部依旧是使用了圈、叉、方块、三角的凸起组合来营造出磨砂的手感。国行星辰红手柄官方售价559元，午夜黑则是529元。这两款手柄都于今日在PlayStation天猫官方旗舰店和京东自营店开售，不过截止发稿时星辰红配色都已售罄，午夜黑在京东官方渠道还有余货。<br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2021/06/16/1640400705.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2021/06/16/1625267210.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2021/06/16/1625286270.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2021/06/16/1625278879.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2021/06/16/1625250829.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2021/06/16/1625251091.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2021/06/16/1625299672.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2021/06/16/1625240399.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/19/2021/06/16/1625236623.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
