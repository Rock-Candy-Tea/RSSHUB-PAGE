【《拳皇15》发布新预告片和截图介绍龙虎队的坂崎良、罗伯特·加西亚】SNK发布了《拳皇15》的新预告片和截图，介绍了可玩角色“坂崎亮”和“罗伯特·加西亚”。坂崎亮、罗伯特·加西亚和之前透露的琼组成了游戏的第五支人员完全曝光的团队“龙虎队”。之前的人物预告片已经介绍了克里斯、夏米尔、不知火舞、琼、七枷社、特瑞·博加德、坂崎百合、安迪·博加德、神乐千鹤、草薙京、东丈、八神庵、二阶堂红丸、明天君和瞬影。《拳皇15》将于2021年发布，平台尚未公布。<br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202105061102352958html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202105061102383833html_img.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202105061102421791html_img.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202105061102457301html_img.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202105061102488558html_img.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202105061102510199html_img.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202105061102548026html_img.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202105061102586741html_img.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202105061103012432html_img.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202105061103043135html_img.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202105061103073566html_img.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202105061103109085html_img.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
