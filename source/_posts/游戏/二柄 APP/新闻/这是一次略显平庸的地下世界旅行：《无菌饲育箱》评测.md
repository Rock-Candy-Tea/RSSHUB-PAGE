鉴赏团柄友“赤い”分享了《无菌饲育箱》的游戏评测：作为日本一的最新作品，该作的2D场景保持了一贯的高水准。然而作为一款Roguelike玩法为主体的游戏，该作的怪物设计，装备设计和技能设计十分单一，导致游戏整体流程单调且无趣。而且整体游戏内容也较为单薄。所以就目前的价格而言，或许不是合适的入手时机。接下来希望能够通过评测，带各位一窥该作的风貌。<br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/z0Vse2T91TzgTjLuvX/2021/07/31/1206079498.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/z0Vse2T91TzgTjLuvX/2021/07/31/1207030118.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/z0Vse2T91TzgTjLuvX/2021/07/31/1207301233.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/z0Vse2T91TzgTjLuvX/2021/07/31/1208238273.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/z0Vse2T91TzgTjLuvX/2021/07/31/1209133578.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/z0Vse2T91TzgTjLuvX/2021/07/31/1209584690.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/z0Vse2T91TzgTjLuvX/2021/07/31/1213109131.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/z0Vse2T91TzgTjLuvX/2021/07/31/1213369927.png" " referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://news.diershoubing.com/article_info/ios/40746/" article_id": 40746&#125;" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
