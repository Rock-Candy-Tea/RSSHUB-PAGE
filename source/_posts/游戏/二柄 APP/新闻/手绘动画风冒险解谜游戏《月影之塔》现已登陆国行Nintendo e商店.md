【手绘动画风冒险解谜游戏《月影之塔》现已登陆国行Nintendo e商店】由冉灯工作室制作、椰岛游戏发行的《月影之塔》现已登陆国行Nintendo e商店，售价68元。《月影之塔》是一款以手绘逐帧动画风格为特色的冒险解谜独立游戏，在现实与魔法的边缘，一场意外的发生打破了世界的宁静。为了找回丢失的记忆，游戏的主角小男孩将与他的神秘小伙伴一道， 进入被混沌笼罩的古老高塔，开启一段寻找真相的冒险旅程。<br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2021/04/15/2001041635.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2021/04/15/2001050013.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2021/04/15/2001072363.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2021/04/15/2001084893.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2021/04/15/2001108879.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2021/04/15/2001119270.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2021/04/15/2001139622.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2021/04/15/2001148299.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2021/04/15/2001154494.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
