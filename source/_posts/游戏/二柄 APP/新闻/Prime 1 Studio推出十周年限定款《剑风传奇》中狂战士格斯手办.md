【Prime 1 Studio推出十周年限定款《剑风传奇》中狂战士格斯手办】为迎接Prime 1 Studio成立十周年，今日官方公布了来自人气作品《剑风传奇》中格斯的狂战士甲冑形态的纪念限定款手办。产品附带两种可替换手臂，可以实现肩扛“斩龙剑”或者霸气的插向地面。在专属地台上围绕着岩壁周围散落着鬼兵们的亡骸，360度无死角的以极致的高精细度再现了这个如噩梦一般的世界。手办全高95cm，售价161590日元（含税，约8400元），即日起开启接受预约，预计2022年11月~2023年1月出货。<br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202203300924362839html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202203300924422953html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202203300924433728html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202203300924438780html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202203300924432923html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202203300924434786html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202203300924562112html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202203300924561506html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202203300924565479html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202203300924567043html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202203300924569322html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202203300925307542html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202203300925300694html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
