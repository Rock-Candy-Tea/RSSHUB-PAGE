【《宝可梦》最新周边：完美复刻的“精灵球”上线】宝可梦中心今日公布了《宝可梦》最新周边——真实比例还原的“精灵球”。产品除了经典红白球以外，还有超级球，高级球，黑暗球，纪念球共五种款式可选。外表采用了金属外壳涂装，并配备触摸传感器和接近传感器，当手靠近或者触摸球体按钮时就会发出闪光（PS：图中的外展示盒也会随商品附送~）。预计12月1日正式上架，目前售价还尚未公布。<br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202111131820007501html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202111131820075177html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202111131820072081html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202111131820118606html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202111131820111093html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202111131820146675html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202111131820147470html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202111131820188402html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202111131820182332html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202111131820227350html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202111131820225959html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
