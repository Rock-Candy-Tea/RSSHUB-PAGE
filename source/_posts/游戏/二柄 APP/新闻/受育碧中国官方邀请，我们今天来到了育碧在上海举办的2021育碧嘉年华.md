受育碧中国官方邀请，我们今天来到了育碧在上海举办的2021育碧嘉年华。在活动现场，一些大家熟悉的作品悉数亮相。我们在这里体验了《刺客信条》中多个经典名场景拍照，《孤岛惊魂6》开发过程现场演示和讲解，还有《刺客信条 英灵殿》中“命运之戏”的实体桌游。此外，这次《孤岛惊魂6》中的DLC内容也将由育碧上海工作室操刀，官方透露本作将会在下半年陆续放出更多的情报和展示。<br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2021/07/31/1151505122.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2021/07/31/1151528625.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2021/07/31/1151564799.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2021/07/31/1151598694.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2021/07/31/1152017233.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2021/07/31/1152043043.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2021/07/31/1152068797.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2021/07/31/1152095379.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/6/2021/07/31/1152125413.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
