【灶门炭治郎（火之神神乐）参战《鬼灭之刃:火神血风谭》】据《少年周刊Jump》显示，灶门炭治郎（火之神神乐）将参战CyberConnect2的最新作《鬼灭之刃:火神血风谭》。本作此前已公开人物有：炭治郎三人组，祢豆子，富冈义勇，炼狱杏寿郎，蝴蝶忍，真菰，锖兔，鳞泷。游戏将于年内登陆PS4、PS5、Xbox One、Xbox Series X/S以及Steam平台。<br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202105211110140734html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202105211110164679html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202105211110187836html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://erbingeditor.diershoubing.com/202105211110208114html_img.blob" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
