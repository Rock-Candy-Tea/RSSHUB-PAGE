<p><img class="lazy" data-height="781" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/03/15/e474e20c77cde1c58ce21d73aa561ec1/thumb.jpeg" data-width="1260" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>“若非艺术家，何为神明？” ----------席奥 </p><p>骨钉大师席奥，三位骨钉大师的大师兄，也是唯一一个放下骨钉去追随自己本心的虫子。席奥深得斯莱真传，马托说他是斯莱最喜欢的徒弟，但是，他的学习优秀不止来源于头脑聪明，更来源于他的悟性。正是因为他的悟性，在放下骨钉这么久仍然技艺不减，用一只画笔便能当做骨钉一样挥舞自如，其融会贯通的本领自不必多说。他追随本心，当骨钉不再能满足他的精神需求后，他并没有被自己骨钉大师的名号所束缚，毅然决然地弃武从艺，隐居在苍绿之径的一个角落。但即使如此，他并没有远离世俗，当虫子们不远万里来到他的居所，他仍然会慷慨地授予他的技艺。当迷失的灵魂在圣巢里游荡，想要找回自我时，也是他敞开了家门，让常年辛苦工作的钉子匠得到陪伴与慰藉。他热爱艺术，因为他在其中找到了自我的归宿，他明白艺术在生活中随处可见，一切自然行为的造物都可以视为艺术。虽然隐居，但他从未忘记过他的兄弟，一直在挂念着奥罗的近况。对于过往的虫子，席奥都会提醒他们坚守自己的本心，不要被外界束缚。在萌德转载的塔罗牌中，“隐士”给了席奥，这正是席奥的写照，远离人群孤独自省，引领后辈追随初心。席奥，一位开悟的骨钉大师，一位智慧思辨的艺术家，一位温暖和蔼的隐士，一位哲思的巨人。 </p><p>席奥与其兄弟奥罗和马托都是斯莱的徒弟。 虽然他显得很冷漠和善良，但他却是斯莱最得意的弟子。 然而，随着时间的流逝，他对骨钉技艺的热情渐渐褪去，拿起了画笔，成为了一名艺术大师，在苍绿之径的深处，他搭了一个小屋，在那里安静地进行着他的艺术创作。</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="701" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/03/15/f4c9f89b346595aa5315c59aa5db9eb9/thumb.jpeg" data-width="1243" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>对话</p><p>一个拜访者！你为什么溜进了这里，小虫子？也许，像我一样，你喜欢发现新事物？</p><p>A visitor! Why have you crept in here, little grub? Perhaps, like me, you have a taste for discovering new things? </p><p>我是席奥，在以前的生活中，我是一名骨钉大师。是的，唯一给我动力的事情就是变得越来越强大，并用剑来磨练我的技能。</p><p>I am Sheo, and in a previous life I was a Nailmaster. Yes, the only thing that drove me was becoming stronger and honing my skills with a nail. </p><p>我以为这是我生命中唯一的呼唤，但这种感觉随着时间的流逝消失了。现在我有一个新的呼唤，它可以与我的灵魂对话，并迫使我前进。 </p><p>I imagined it was my one calling in life, but that feeling faded over time. Now I seek a new calling, something that speaks to my soul and compels me forward. </p><p>所以我才会在这里，与世隔绝，试图掌握创作的艺术。绘画、雕刻…这些东西带来不同的乐趣。 </p><p>That's why I spend my time here, secluded, trying to master the art of creation. Painting, sculpting... these things bring a different kind of pleasure. </p><p>嗯。你似乎并没有被我所说的打动。你要找的是骨钉大师席奥吗？你是来学骨钉技艺的吗？如果你坚持，我会教你… </p><p>Mmm. You don't seem very moved by what I've said. Was it Sheo the Nailmaster you were looking for? Did you come to learn a Nail Art? I'll teach you if you insist... </p><p>（拒绝） 哦？你也厌倦了骨钉的重荷吗？我明白。如果你想，就留下来，我们一起搞艺术。 Oh? Have you, too, grown weary of the weight of a nail? I understand. Stay, if you like, and we can make art together. </p><p>（再次对话接受） 关于你的举止的某些东西…我觉得你好像还没有放弃骨钉的艺术。我可以教你一些东西，如果你喜欢的话…</p><p>Something about your bearing... I feel as though you haven't yet abandoned the way of the nail. I can teach you something, if you like... </p><p>（学习） 所以！我的工作，你有没有为之所动？ </p><p>So! My work, does it move you? </p><p>挥剑、挥笔…这些东西没有太大的不同。</p><p>The wielding of a nail, the wielding of a brush... these things are not so different. </p><p>我们闯进那个世界，以便窥探到更深处。</p><p> We cut into the world so that we can peer deeper inside. </p><p>尽管现在，我没有什么可以教你的。你前方的路…只有你可以看到它。祝你好运。 </p><p>For now though, I have nothing more to teach you. Your path forwards... only you can see it. Good luck. </p><p>（关于马托） 你学到的骨钉技艺，旋风劈砍。这一直是马托的最爱。 </p><p>That Nail Art you've learnt, the Cyclone Slash. That was always Mato's favourite. </p><p>有个像你这样的学生一定让他欣喜若狂。他一直想把他所学到的东西传给新一代。 </p><p>It must have made him ecstatic to have a pupil like you. He always wanted to pass down what he'd learnt to a new generation. </p><p>他本人不是最聪明的学生，但他的热情和善良让他脱颖而出。我很高兴他实现了他所期望已久的东西。成为老师… </p><p>He was not the most skilled pupil himself, but his enthusiasm and his kindness set him apart. I'm glad he achieved what he desired for so long. To be a teacher... </p><p>（关于奥罗） 所以你遇到了我的兄弟奥罗，并说服他教你骨钉技艺。嗯。 </p><p>So you met my brother Oro and convinced him to teach you his Nail Art. Hmm. </p><p>我很久之前见过他。我想知道他改变了多少。他似乎总是被一些东西困扰，虽然我从来不知道是什么。 </p><p>It's been a long time since I've seen him. I wonder how much he's changed. He always seemed to be haunted by something, though I never learned what. </p><p>不管我们之间有多大的距离，我们还是兄弟。我希望把他所学的东西传给你会让他高兴点。</p><p>No matter the distance between us though, we are still brothers. I hope that passing down his learnings to you brought him some happiness. </p><p>（关于斯莱） 那个护符！只有伟大的骨钉贤者可以给你… </p><p>That charm! Only the Great Nailsage could have given that to you... </p><p>看到你变得多么强大…这几乎让我想要再次拿起我的骨钉。 Seeing how strong you've become... it almost makes me want to take up my nail once more. </p><p>但是不，我应该继续走现在的路，继续我的艺术工作。如果伟大的骨钉贤者来了，我想告诉他我走得有多远。 </p><p>But no, I should stay on my current path and keep working at my art. If the Great Nailsage ever comes by, I want to show him how far I've come. </p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="705" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/03/15/ac6a730536630a31214bef4486a6e7e6/thumb.jpeg" data-width="1248" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>（关于骨钉匠） 哦，看看谁加入了我们！现在我们有了一个快乐的团队，不是吗？ </p><p>Oh, look who's joined us! We have quite a merry crowd now, don't we? </p><p>这个家伙在我的小屋里彷徨不安，心烦意乱，追求自己生命中新的呼唤。 </p><p>This fellow came wandering in to my hut, quite distraught and on his own quest for a new calling in life. </p><p>我们聊了起来，并发现了我们都热爱创造以及建造和珩磨工艺。所以现在我们一起搞艺术！ We talked, and found a common love of creating and building and honing a craft. So now we make art together! </p><p>有伴真好，我之前没有意识到我是多么孤独。就像又和我的兄弟在一起一样！如果你喜欢，可以留在我们这里！ </p><p>It's good to have company, I hadn't realised how lonely I was. It's like being with my brothers again! Stay with us, if you like! </p><p>（建模） 建造这些模型需要耐心和轻柔的动作。但看到古老而伟大的圣巢骑士出现在我们眼前也是值得的。 </p><p>Building these models takes patience and a light touch. It's worth it though, seeing the grand old Knights of Hallownest come to life before our eyes. </p><p>勇武的赫格莫永远是我的最爱。他如此高大威猛，但令人惊讶的是，这个高大的人物说起话来轻声细语。 </p><p>Mighty Hegemol was always my favourite. What a towering figure he was, though surprisingly soft spoken for one so large. </p><p>当然，他们本身就个性鲜明。凶猛的德莱娅、善良的伊思玛、神秘的泽摩尔…嗯，还有一个。 </p><p>They were all impressive in their own right, of course. Fierce Dryya, kindly Isma, mysterious Ze'mer... erm, and the other one. </p><p>所以，你因为旅行又回来了。你学到了什么?你是怎么成长的? </p><p>So, your travels have brought you back. What have you learnt? How have you grown? </p><p>我自己？我仍然试图用画笔来捕捉东西…虽然我不太确定那是什么。 </p><p>Myself? I am still trying to capture something with my brush... although I'm not quite sure what that thing is. </p><p>我有备用的画笔和大量的油漆。如果你对骨钉厌倦了，可以随时加入我的艺术世界。 </p><p>I have spare brushes and plenty of paint. Once you tire of the nail, feel free to join me in my art. </p><p>梦语： 我的兄弟，奥罗…你为什么要深藏在那个灰尘飞扬的坟墓边缘？ </p><p>My brother, Oro... what drove you to hide away deep down at the very edge of that ash swept grave? </p><p>是骄傲还是恐惧，还是耻辱？如果你的剑让你苦恼，也许你应该放下它，并找到一种新的呼唤，让你的灵魂得到放松。 </p><p>Was it pride, or fear, or shame? Perhaps you should lay down your nail if it haunts you so, and find a new calling that eases your soul.</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="705" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/03/15/90122f2cb46a16787887e0393186e171/thumb.jpeg" data-width="1244" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><a href="heybox://open_subject" target="_blank">#空洞骑士#</a> <br></p><p><a href="heybox://open_subject" target="_blank">#steam游戏#</a> <br></p><p><a href="heybox://open_subject" target="_blank">#单机游戏#</a> <br></p>
