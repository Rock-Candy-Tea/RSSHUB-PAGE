<p>平时的「长野原烟花店」除了贩卖烟花，还会出售各种<b>精巧的纪念品</b>。可以说，这些纪念品就是宵宫的第二事业。</p><p><b>精巧的纪念品intricate knickknacks</b></p><p>Apart from selling fireworks, Naganohara Fireworks also sells various <b>intricate knickknacks</b>. This can be said to be Yoimiya's second career.</p><p><br></p><p>货架上的纪念品五花八门，一部分是北斗带给宵宫的<b>舶来品</b>，经过适当改造，成了很有特色的工艺品。另一部分则是宵宫<b>灵感爆发</b>做出的练手作品。</p><p><b>舶来品imports</b></p><p><b>灵感爆发bursts of explosive inspiration</b></p><p>All manner of trinkets adorn the shelves, with some of them being exotic <b>imports </b>from Beidou that were modified and made into highly distinctive handicrafts, and others being practice works created during <b>bursts of Yoimiya's explosive inspiration</b>.</p><p><br></p><p>碰后绽放出花瓣的「<b>散华弹珠</b>」，旋转时发出清亮鸟鸣的「<b>雀跃陀螺</b>」，升空后<b>不断变色</b>的「霓光蜻蜓」，还有实在算不上成功的「<b>鬼兜虫战车</b>」…玩具的灵感全都来源于民间盛行的小游戏。</p><p><b>散华弹珠Scatterbloom Marbles</b></p><p><b>雀跃陀螺Leaping Tops</b></p><p><b>不断变色changes hue</b></p><p>鬼兜虫战车Onikabuto Chariot</p><p>From the <b>Scatterbloom Marbles </b>that will blossom when they knock against each other,<b>Leaping Tops </b>that chirp like birds as they spin, the Neon Dragonfly that <b>changes hue </b>as it ascends into the air, and the somewhat-failed <b>Onikabuto Chariot</b>... All these toys are inspired by the little games that the common people play.</p><p><br></p><p>它们不仅受到孩子的喜爱，很多成年人也对它们爱不释手。甚至连「荒泷派」的头目荒泷一斗都专程前来购买宵宫制作的纪念品，带去和他口中的<b>劲敌</b>一较高下。</p><p><b>劲敌nemesis</b></p><p>They are not only popular among the children, but are much-loved by adults as well. Even Arataki Itto, the head of the Arataki Gang, comes over to buy her specially-made knickknacks, which he then brings to go challenge the one he has declared his <b>nemesis</b>.</p><p><br></p><p>宵宫做的纪念品不只是玩具。只要加入一些巧思，就能在生活中派上大用场。最典型的当属「<b>螽退轮</b>」，一种装有多种燃剂和香料的小圆环，夏夜里将它点燃挂起，就能<b>驱赶蚊虫</b>。</p><p><b>螽退轮Katydid Coil</b></p><p><b>驱赶蚊虫repel mosquitoes</b></p><p>Yoimiya's knickknacks are not just toys. With some thought, they can be very useful in one's daily life. The most classic example of this is the <b>Katydid Coil</b>, which is a ring that can contain a variety of flammables and spices. When ignited and hung up in the summer, it can be used to <b>repel mosquitoes</b>.</p><p><br></p><p>有些邻居看中宵宫手艺精湛，请她上门帮忙。尽管宵宫口口声声说着「烟花店可不是万事屋，我也不是有求必应的」，但每次接到委托，依然会尽心尽力帮助对方，哪怕忙活一天的报酬只能买下几份小吃，宵宫也乐在其中。</p><p>Many people have come to ask Yoimiya for help on account of her excellent craftsmanship. And though she protests, saying, "Hey now, this is a fireworks shop, not an everything shop. Even I can't possibly do it all," she will still try her best to help, even if her earnings for the whole day are only enough to buy a few snacks. She'll enjoy the whole thing, too —</p><p><br></p><p>毕竟，花见坂的大家都是宵宫的好朋友嘛。</p><p>everyone in Hanamizaka is her friend, after all.</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="1143" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2022/08/14/288d3211c44d49999e2d96c5a80871981660484029.png" data-width="800" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><a href="heybox://open_subject" target="_blank">#原神2.8版本创作者征集活动#</a><br></p>
