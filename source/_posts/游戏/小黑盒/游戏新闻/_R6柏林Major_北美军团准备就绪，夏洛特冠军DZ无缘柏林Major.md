class="lazy" data-height="2700" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/07/13/d0746011c26f04f239561ebd48d9751d/thumb.jpeg" data-width="2160" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>　　混血巴西队<b>XSET</b>依旧稳定发挥，在最后一天击败Oxygen积得3分，以总分15分踩着及格线晋级柏林Major。<br></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="1920" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/07/13/de067ef7870715543daea06f917d5d20/thumb.jpeg" data-width="1536" data-height="1755" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/07/14/7c2a179271b90854051ca8c9e883e525/thumb.jpeg" data-width="1404"
