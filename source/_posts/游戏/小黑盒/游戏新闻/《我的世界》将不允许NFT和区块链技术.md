<blockquote><p>来源：gamingbolt</p><p><a href="https://gamingbolt.com/minecraft-will-not-have-nfts-or-other-blockchain-technologies-mojang" target="_blank">点击此处查看原文>>></a></p></blockquote><p>《我的世界》开发商Mojang发布一则声明，明确表明不看好在《我的世界》里面加入NFT，尤其是在一些Minecraft服务器为参与游戏的人提供NFT之后，这明显违反了游戏的相关规定。<br></p><p>"为了确保《我的世界》玩家们能够拥有安全的游玩环境和良好的游戏体验，区块链技术不允许在我们的客户端和服务器应用程序内集成（即捆绑区块链），《我的世界》游戏内的内容，如世界、皮肤、角色物品或其他MOD，也不得利用区块链技术来创造稀缺的数字资产，"Mojang的声明说。<br></p><p>"鉴于NFT的投机性的价格和人们对此的投资心态，会致使玩家们的注意力从玩游戏上转移到获取暴利上，我们认为这与我们玩家的长期快乐和成功不符。"<br></p><p>归根结底，Mojang不想让《我的世界》因为NFT将玩家区分成“有”或“无”的两个群体（类似富人跟穷人）。该工作室还指出，NFT市场也充满了骗局，如欺诈性的价格膨胀。<br></p><p>虽然目前不允许对NFT或其他区块链技术加入《我的世界》，但游戏已经支持这项技术。如果之后发生变化，有了新的用途，或许能够真正提高《我的世界》游戏性。<br></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="577" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/web/2022/07/21/78bb561635a70b2028f57ef517548a44/thumb.jpeg" data-width="1024" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><br><p class="original-news">
Minecraft developer Mojang has released a statement about its position on NFTs (non-fungible tokens) in Minecraft. The statement is generally quite negative on the entire idea, and comes on the heels of a few Minecraft servers offering NFTs related to the game for those who participate.
Mojang doesn’t want to include NFTs in Minecraft because it goes against the Minecraft Usage Guidelines.
“To ensure that Minecraft players have a safe and inclusive experience, blockchain technologies are not permitted to be integrated inside our client and server applications, nor may Minecraft in-game content such as worlds, skins, persona items, or other mods, be utilized by blockchain technology to create a scarce digital asset,” says the statement by Mojang.
“The speculative pricing and investment mentality around NFTs takes the focus away from playing the game and encourages profiteering, which we think is inconsistent with the long-term joy and success of our players.”
Ultimately, Mojang doesn’t want Minecraft to harbour a culture of separating its playerbase into “haves” and “have nots”. The studio also points out that the NFT market is also full of scams, such as fraudulently inflated prices.
While currently saying no to any possibilities of NFTs or other blockchain technologies coming to Minecraft, Mojang has left the door open for the future if the technology changes and new uses of the technology are found that would actually enhance the gameplay.</p>
