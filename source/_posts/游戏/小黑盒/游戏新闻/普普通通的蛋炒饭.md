<p>食材：剩米饭，鸡蛋4个，半个玉米，2-3根葱，甜豆30-40粒，洋葱一个，火腿肠2根【搭配自己爱吃配菜6】</p><p>调料品：食用油，盐，胡椒粉，剁辣椒（爱吃放），鸡精（爱吃就放）</p><p>鸡蛋蛋白，蛋黄分离，蛋黄放米饭里➕半勺盐➕半勺胡椒粉，抓拌均匀，蛋白留着。</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="3264" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/08/20/3f517c7d077085bf6e23ac93d504e04e/thumb.jpeg" data-width="2176" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>洋葱切小块，火腿肠切小丁。玉米对半切，方便好剥。</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="3264" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/08/20/0490734ff822afaca87bc501551987b0/thumb.jpeg" data-width="2176" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>甜豆焯下水</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="1920" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/08/20/a97ba5a8d284ade308459c1931414233/thumb.jpeg" data-width="1440" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>锅中放油把蛋白炒好盛出来备用【想要颜色更好看，可以不放蛋白，蛋白可以做个汤】锅中放油把甜豆炒下，放入玉米加一点点盐，盛出来备用。</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="3264" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/08/20/cba1cfaf4e4e53610f1e7d40594f35f1/thumb.jpeg" data-width="2176" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>锅中放油把火腿肠炒香，再放火腿肠，盛出来备用。</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="3264" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/08/20/97220ed76cd0aaca0eb332b973e00fa8/thumb.jpeg" data-width="3264" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>锅中放油，把饭炒干水分，多翻动，倒入之前炒好配菜，放1勺剁辣椒。</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="3264" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/08/20/a0d84aa8e39b2cfbaceb69585a589ccd/thumb.jpeg" data-width="3264" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>出锅之前放葱花</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="1920" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/08/20/36df0657bf608f6496e94ac324629a6b/thumb.jpeg" data-width="1440"
