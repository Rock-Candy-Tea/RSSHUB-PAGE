<p>大家好，我是分享星露谷模组内容的流木。大家都知道“<b>绿帽谷</b>”这个梗<span data-emoji="cube_doge" class="hb-emoji"></span>在2018年的一篇帖子中，有人给星露谷作者发了邮件并得到了回复，问的就是绿帽谷的问题，作者是这样回复的：</p><blockquote><p>It's very interesting to me to hear that the game is being interpreted this way but I don't want this interpretation to prevent people from playing the game. I have intentionally not answered questions in the past because I like to let people come up with their own interpretations...me not being able to remember every detail I had put into the game...I will stand up for the townsfolks and say that I think they are good people.</p></blockquote><p>原文较长，大意就是游戏开发周期长，有些细节作者没有注意到，导致玩家起了误会。作者说他喜欢让玩家自由想象，但绝不是有意将剧情这样安排。他希望星露谷里的一切都有真实感，所以有些人遇到不幸也在情理之中。</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="233" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/web/2022/01/09/425bf97ae6099ce7f962aa3c3c77df16/thumb.png" data-width="468" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><h4 class="img-desc">贴吧原文</h4><p>但是既然误会已经产生了，想忘掉就有些困难，还好我们的模组作者们为我们提供了更多的选择。没错，这个系列的内容就是为大家介绍独立的新增NPC模组，本期主要介绍女NPC！<span data-emoji="cube_滑稽" class="hb-emoji"></span>让我们马上开始吧</p><p><br></p><h3>1.
