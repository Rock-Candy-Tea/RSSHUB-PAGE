<p><b>配方材料：</b>土豆*2，秃秃豆*2，香辛料*1</p><p><b>星级</b><b>：</b>★★</p><p><b>食谱获取方法：</b>须弥奥摩斯港购买获得（人物：贾汗吉尔）</p><p><br></p><p>Panipuri是一种街头小吃，在印度、孟加拉国、巴基斯坦和尼泊尔非常流行。它是一种酥脆、中空的油炸面团（puri），里面塞满了煮熟的土豆或蒸好的月芽、煮熟的鹰嘴豆或白豌豆咖喱（也称ragda），还有辛辣的水和甜酸辣酱、罗望子酸辣酱、查特马沙拉（chaat masala）、洋葱、热辣椒和鹰嘴豆的调味水。当你吃Panipuri时，嘴里会爆发出如此多的味道，绝对是你此前没有感受过的美味。</p><p>在印度北部，panipuri被称为golgappa，gol指的是脆壳，gappa指的是吃的过程，因为这些小零食通常一次吃一种。据信，帕尼普里起源于北方邦，并逐渐在印度和国外流行开来。</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="500" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2022/09/06/50d96550a499422793099bc05e0215c01662454783.jpg" data-width="750" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="247" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2022/09/06/e601e758fed04de6b6f7b5b76ee37a301662454785.png" data-width="410" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><h4 class="img-desc">绿汁脆球</h4><p>An appetizer served with a green dipping sauce. The crispy balls are first deep-fried, then stuffed with a delicious filling through a hole on the top. The <b>bite-sized </b>snack is a common street food in Sumeru.</p><p><b>bite-sized 一口大小的</b></p><p>蘸汁食用的小吃。用热油先炸好脆球，戳个洞塞入配料，搭配绿汁一起食用。大小适中，一口一个，是须弥街头常见的特色小吃。</p><p><br></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="237" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2022/09/06/a7cd75f6605f4f68bd3f1227882a90741662454786.png" data-width="410" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><h4 class="img-desc">奇怪的绿汁脆球</h4><p>A delicacy served with dipping sauce. The green sauce gives off a slightly unusual smell, and its color looks a bit off... Maybe it is a good idea to forget about the sauce and directly go for the panipuri balls.</p><p>蘸汁食用的小吃。调配出的绿汁散发着些许不同寻常的微妙气味，颜色似乎也不太对劲…或许放弃掉蘸汁，直接吃脆球会比较好。</p><p><br></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="236" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2022/09/06/1f91e3fd800741f682b8bbbd4f17f5521662454789.png" data-width="411" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><h4 class="img-desc">美味的绿汁脆球</h4><p>A delicacy that is served with dipping sauce. The round panipuri balls remind you of Slimes. Anticipate the mysterious, bright-colored juice within, and bring it to your mouth — the coolness, sourness, and tingling sensations are bouncing freely on your tongue as if someone has ignited a stick of dreamy <b>dynamite</b>. No wonder that some people in Sumeru are so obsessed with it...</p><p><b>dynamite/ˈdaɪnəmaɪt/n.<非正式>轰动一时的人（或事物），极其令人兴奋的人（或事物）</b></p><p>蘸汁食用的小吃。如同史莱姆一般圆滚滚的脆球，盛着鲜艳神秘的汁水。刚一入口，清凉、刺激、酸爽等多重口味，瞬间在舌尖炸开，带给人如梦似幻般的冲击感。难怪有的须弥人会对此如痴如醉…</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="963" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2022/09/06/eb8343448dca45b985e185a4adf941f91662454791.png" data-width="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><a href="heybox://open_subject" target="_blank">#原神3.0版本创作者征集活动#</a><br></p>
