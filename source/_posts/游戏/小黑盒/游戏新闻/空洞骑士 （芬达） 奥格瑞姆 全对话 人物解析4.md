<p>“国王陛下…圣巢将会重生！变得辉煌、闪耀！就像原本的样子…” ---------奥格瑞姆<br></p><p><br></p><p>朋友们……我会保护好我们伟大的王国。相信我！<br></p><p> ---------奥格瑞姆<br></p><p><br></p><p>圣巢伟大骑士之列将终结于何处？会终结在我这里吗？这种意志能传承下去吗？ ---------奥格瑞姆</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="767" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/03/06/1c3c5fb53d567a291d3b9b8cb90c1894/thumb.jpeg" data-width="1195" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>芬达哥一直是我很喜欢的一位角色，在圣巢阴暗沉的图景里，他是一抹不可多得的亮色。在下水道里遇见他，总能让人精神一振。他豪迈乐达，他忠于职守，即使王国已然衰落，他依然惦记着曾经的战友与敬爱的国王，他依然铭记着骑士的荣耀与王国的辉煌。但在他爽朗的笑声中，掩藏的是一颗细腻的心。正如他自己所言，总是会陷入过往的回忆中，爱人的逝去，家国的幻灭，这一切都一直存放在他心里，但他用乐观压制了悲伤，用希望战胜了荒凉。即使自己是五骑士中的最后一员，也要把骑士精神传承下去；即使王国已经衰败，仍然要守护着这片土地。他在战斗中高呼“为了五骑士的荣耀”，他在梦境中低语“一切都会完好如初”，他拥有着对圣巢最为深沉的热爱，有着对职责最为忠实的坚守，有着对身边虫最为真挚的情感，同时，他还有着对未来最为充盈的希望。奥格瑞姆，在王国强盛时，是土地的防御者；在王国衰颓后，是梦想的守护者。他乐达的胸襟、细腻的情思，以及他威严的骑士之道，都是这永恒的废墟中最为耀眼的光辉，点染着山河悲壮，彰显着人性伟大。</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="697" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/03/06/2a4f9b9b2e11fa3a3ae146b715ac80e6/thumb.jpeg" data-width="1243" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>对话：</p><p>（奥格瑞姆）</p><p>啊，又是你，真高兴看到你回来。</p><p>Oh, it's you again. I'm glad to see you return.</p><p>不，不，拜托了……什么都别说。我必须为之前的事道歉。</p><p>No, no, please... Don't say a word. I must apologise for my previous behaviour.</p><p>都是我的错。我以为你也是那些没有心智的鬼祟躯壳，但你用最威严的骑士之道超越了我。</p><p>Entirely my fault. I mistook you for one of those mindless skulking husks, yet you bested me in the most majestic knightly fashion.</p><p>多么愉悦激烈的战斗！我几乎都忘记了这种感觉。</p><p>Such joyful, vigorous combat. I'd almost forgotten the feeling.</p><p>你也看到了，我以前是位骑士，承蒙国王恩典，我能骄傲在王座前目睹圣巢日渐伟大。</p><p>I was once a Knight you see. By the grace of our good King, I stood proudly before the throne, watching Hallownest swell to greatness.</p><p>但接着就出现了可怕的瘟疫……</p><p>Then that nasty business with the affliction...</p><p>我们这些骑士对抗起有形体却没有形态的敌人，但怎么才能打败这种敌人呢？</p><p>We knights defend against the physical, but a formless enemy. How to defeat such a foe?</p><p>国王尝试了自己的办法，那手段着实残酷……但我们最后还是没逃脱衰落的命运。</p><p>Our King tried in his own way. A cruel means it was... And still, eventually, we were brought low.</p><p>唉！我不该这么闷闷不乐，你给了我很大鼓舞。能遇见势均力敌的对手真让人开心。</p><p>Bah! I shouldn't be so morose. You've invigorated me. Tis' truly a delight to meet one whose strength can match my own.</p><p>去吧，圣巢的强大战士！你为自己赢得了荣耀！祝你好运。</p><p>Go now, mighty warrior of Hallownest! You've proven your honour! Good fortune on the path ahead.</p><p>梦语：</p><p>……原来还有其他虫子……</p><p>...So there were others...</p><p>……伊斯玛……好强……教我……</p><p>...Isma... So strong.... Teach me...</p><p>（佩戴防御者护符）</p><p>你散发着正义的骄傲气息，的确是一位强大的骑士。</p><p>Ahh. You exude the proud odour of the Just. Truly, a mighty Knight you are.</p><p>（拥有伊斯玛之泪）</p><p>啊啊！那滴泪水……你去过她的树林了！</p><p>Ahh! That tear... You've been to her grove!</p><p>我过发誓要去探访，但……我的职责……我的誓言……</p><p>I swore I'd visit, but... my duties... my oath...</p><p>抱歉，别在意我，我真是个傻瓜。不小心点的话我就会沉溺到过去的回忆中，再也不能回来。</p><p>Oh, I'm sorry. Ignore me, I'm being foolish. If I'm not careful I'll sink down into old memories and won't ever come back again.</p><p>她看人很准，既然她祝福了你，那你肯定有特别之处。我在你面前简直不值一提！</p><p>She's a great judge of character, so if she gave you her blessing you must be something special indeed. I'm barely worthy to be in your p</p><p>resence!</p><p>（地下室）</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="701" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/03/06/52b5b2cbc9a2b3e5b43873ca8542415e/thumb.jpeg" data-width="1235" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>啊！你醒了，而且看起来很有精神！</p><p>Ahh, you're awake, and looking well-rested!</p><p>我起来的时候发现你蜷着身子躺在我旁边，吓了我一跳。不过，我倒是很欢迎你在对这个世界感到厌倦的时候到这里来休息的。</p><p>I was startled when I woke to find you curled up beside me, but of course you're welcome to rest here whenever the world wearies you.</p><p>正如国王将流浪者收留在这个国度一样，我的住所也向你敞开。这里温暖、湿润又舒适，你想待多久就可以待多久。</p><p>As the King granted his kingdom, to shelter to those who needed it, I offer my home. Stay as long as you like, down here where it's cosy and warm and moist.</p><p>这个洞穴大概和宫殿的精致华丽差得很远，不过即使如此，我曾经的战友和我们尊敬的国王的种种回忆，仍然深深刻印在我的脑海中。</p><p>My cave may seem a far cry from the finery of the palace but even still the memories of my former comrades, and our glorious King, remain fresh in my mind.</p><p>苍白之殿是一个奇伟的地方，那个时代最伟大的英雄都汇聚在那里。啊！看看你现在的模样，你立于他们之中的场景仿佛就近在眼前。</p><p>The Pale Court was a wondrous place full of the age's greatest heroes. Ahh! Looking at you now, I can easily imagine you standing amongst them.</p><p>梦语：</p><p>圣巢伟大骑士之列将终结于何处？会终结在我这里吗？这种意志能传承下去吗？</p><p>Where will the line of Hallownest's Great Knights end? With me? Can such a thing be passed on?</p><p>（白色防御者）</p><p>白色防御者</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="653" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/03/06/44e7b562a10d5ba5621bfbef0531e94f/thumb.jpeg" data-width="1126" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>朋友们……我会保护好我们伟大的王国。相信我！</p><p>My friends... I will protect our dear Kingdom. Have faith in me!</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="363" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/03/06/8e79a51f49cadf7c4746f68a1325d68a/thumb.jpeg" data-width="580" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>我们会再次于此相聚。我保证……</p><p>We will all meet again. I promise it...</p><p>（第二次）</p><p>国王陛下……我相信您，也相信圣巢。为了这个王国，我会竭尽全力！</p><p>My King... I believe in you. I believe in Hallownest! My devotion will never falter...</p><p>（第三次）</p><p>国王陛下……圣巢将会重生！变得辉煌、闪耀！就像原本的样子……</p><p>My King... Hallownest will be reborn! Glorious and bright! Just as it always was...</p><p>（第四次）</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="363" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/03/06/fb78f15bedd4ae8084e0540595b93eba/thumb.jpeg" data-width="580" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>国王陛下……一切都会完好如初……</p><p>My King... We will all be together once more...</p><p>（第五次）</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="363" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/03/06/cc632844bbf6b18b5819d337e68251ea/thumb.jpeg" data-width="580" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>小家伙，你出现在了我的梦中，真有意思。你高贵的举止风度让我想起了敬爱的国王。他回来之后，我会请求封你为骑士！这样我们就能愉快地冒险了……</p><p>How funny that I see you in my dreams too, little one. Your noble bearing reminds me of our dear King. When he returns, I'll ask him if you can become a Knight! What jolly adventures we will have...</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="363" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/03/06/70ace5566087deecb4e68a7767545eb6/thumb.jpeg" data-width="580" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>————————————————————</p><p><br></p><p>乐观的另一面，隐藏着悲情</p><p><br></p><p>战斗梦语：</p><p>粪球防御者</p><p>……为了国王的荣耀！……</p><p>...For the honour of the King!..</p><p>……为了五骑士的荣耀！……</p><p>...For the honour of the Five!..</p><p>……为了圣巢的荣耀！</p><p>...For the honour of Hallownest!</p><p>白色防御者</p><p>海格默……我想念你的幽默……</p><p>Hegemol...
