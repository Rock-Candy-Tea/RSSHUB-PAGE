class="lazy" data-height="209" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2021/07/17/33b9e20df817262c20979d1386c82f18.obj" data-width="372" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>激光制导，三点定位锁定旅行者所处位置，随后造成猛烈的激光炮击。</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="214" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2021/07/17/c71cb14d0e2e41abe972b74ec9cdced4.obj"
