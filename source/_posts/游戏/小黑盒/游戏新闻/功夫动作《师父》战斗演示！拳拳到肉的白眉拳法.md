<p><img data-gameid="900008724" src="https://cdn.max-c.com/pc_game/head/85fd31680863de9578a9cb6aa9453256.webp" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>由Sloclap（截拳道功夫动作游戏《赦免者》）开发的全新功夫游戏《师父（SIFU）》发布了新的战斗演示，为玩家们展示了闪转腾挪的流畅打斗。</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="null" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2021/05/17/d222490bf4514e3dbb836eab0a0934e61621217084.gif" data-width="null" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>本次演示中，特技演员在Alpha动作捕捉系统下展示了白眉拳的闪避以及投掷等格斗动作，不过在向敌人投掷物品时要注意，一不小心可能会被敌人反制，成为敌人攻击的武器。</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="null" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2021/05/17/70793e376aaf46ad91cc904e159267e01621217098.gif" data-width="null" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>《师父》是一款基于“白眉拳法”的动作游戏，游戏受成龙电影启发，玩家将扮演一位白眉拳功夫传人，向无名杀手报仇。玩家可以在游戏中体验到一对多战斗，而且还可以利用环境和物体等对敌人造成攻击，如果玩家在战斗中阵亡，复活后角色的年纪就会增大。</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="null" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2021/05/17/f5c7414e4b7a48cda5b670491cf8ea7b1621217106.gif" data-width="null" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="null" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2021/05/17/b9799f23c89a40e7954e71f99100481c1621217115.gif" data-width="null" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><br></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="null" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2021/05/17/b998c1ea1e5741a89dfab2f02bf7ac4d1621217120.gif" data-width="null" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="null" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2021/05/17/8b10983fc7074498b75cf6f8225659131621217128.gif" data-width="null" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="null" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2021/05/17/6569438832f84ade8c0ea2d4668a11801621217139.gif" data-width="null" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
