<p>日前，Xbox负责人Phil Spencer在接受GQ杂志采访时表示，《上古卷轴6》会和明年即将发售的《星空》（Starfield）一样，在Xbox和PC平台独占。他认为，Xbox有着完整的游戏体验，包括Xbox Live、Game Pass、云游戏、好友、存档等。</p><p>“这和制裁其他平台无关，我坚信所有平台都会继续成长，”Phil Spencer说，“为了登陆Xbox，我希望我们能带来全套产品。对于《上古卷轴6》，我们就是这么做的。对于我们的其他系列也是如此。”</p><blockquote><p>It’s been confirmed that next year’s Starfield, arguably the biggest game of 2022, will be Xbox and PC only. Spencer says he sees the same for The Elder Scrolls VI. In his eyes, Xbox is the whole experience. Xbox Live. Game Pass. Cloud Gaming. Friends lists. Save states. “It’s not about punishing any other platform, like I fundamentally believe all of the platforms can continue to grow,” he says. “But in order to be on Xbox, I want us to be able to bring the full complete package of what we have. And that would be true when I think about Elder Scrolls VI. That would be true when I think about any of our franchises.”</p></blockquote><p>《上古卷轴6》是由微软旗下Bethesda游戏工作室开发的一款角色扮演游戏，在2018年E3上首次公布，发售时间暂未公布。</p><p>原文链接<br></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="532" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2021/11/15/b36967ef139f83309550ccdd85e9850d/thumb.jpeg" data-width="1143" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
