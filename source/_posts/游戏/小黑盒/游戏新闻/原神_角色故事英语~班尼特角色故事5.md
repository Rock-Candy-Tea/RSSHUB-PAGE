<p>死亡究竟是什么呢？总是<b>游离在死亡边缘</b>的班尼特不禁思考。</p><p><b>游离在死亡边缘walking the thin line between life and death</b></p><p>What does death mean, exactly? Always <b>walking the thin line between life and death</b>, Bennett can't help but wonder.</p><p><br></p><p>班尼特知道将他「捡」回来的那位冒险家「老爹」，在死后留下诸多冒险传说供人<b>称颂</b>。</p><p><b>称颂praiseworthy</b></p><p>Bennett knows that the old adventurer who saved him has left many <b>praiseworthy </b>stories and legends after his death.</p><p><br></p><p>班尼特知道无妻无子孑然一 身的冒险家，在他的葬礼上不会有<b>哀声泣泪</b>，只有众多老友碰杯豪饮为他送别。</p><p><b>哀声泣泪weeping</b></p><p>He knows that an adventurer will not have <b>weeping </b>at their funerals, only toasts made in their names by old comrades.</p><p><br></p><p>班尼特知道蒙德冒险家最好的归宿，便是献身于追求宝藏与大地的秘密之路，再由风神将其灵魂带回故乡。</p><p>He knows, to the adventurers of Mondstadt, a "good death" comes at the end of a life given to the pursuit of treasure and the secrets of the world. Once over, their souls are brought back to their homeland by Barbatos.</p><p><br></p><p>他曾十分畏惧死亡。</p><p>He once feared death.</p><p><br></p><p>但<b>思考</b>过后的班尼特认为，死亡对冒险家而言或许是一件幸事。</p><p><b>思考pondering on</b></p><p>However, after <b>pondering on </b>this matter some more, he now feels death may be something fortunate for an adventurer...</p><p><br></p><p>一一可幸运的事与我无关啊？</p><p>Not that fortune has anything to do with him.</p><p><br></p><p>「启程吧，去找宝藏！」于是班尼特放弃了思考。</p><p>"Onto the next treasure!"
