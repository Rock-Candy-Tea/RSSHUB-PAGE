<p>《死亡细胞（Dead Cells）》公布了新DLC“女王与海”的全新实机宣传片，该DLC将于2022年1月6日推出，登陆PC和各大主机平台。</p><p><img data-gameid="1580050" src="https://cdn.akamai.steamstatic.com/steam/apps/1580050/header.jpg?t=1639674750" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p contenteditable="false"><iframe allowfullscreen="true" border="0" frameborder="no" framespacing="0" height="270" scrolling="no" src="https://player.bilibili.com/player.html?aid=337449877&bvid=BV1pR4y1W7fP&cid=462551623&page=1" width="480"></iframe></p><p>据介绍，该DLC中包含了9件新武器、新的宠物、敌人、装束、新地图感染船骸、灯塔等内容。</p><p>《死亡细胞》是一款2D平台动作游戏，将Roguelite与银河战士恶魔城类特点融合在了一起。本作目前在Steam商城售价80元，游戏支持简体中文。</p><p><img data-gameid="588650" src="https://cdn.max-c.com/heybox/game/header/588650_ALIaT.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="338" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2021/12/17/e3817af445584fabb413eafc65bc17921639701784.png" data-width="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="338" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2021/12/17/4b6cdd207a4b4bd99720d5bc25ad17831639701784.png" data-width="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="338" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2021/12/17/5cf34b0579724ff6aae4e7e32aa227641639701785.png" data-width="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="338" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2021/12/17/98ab0b8f59304ea5a42c158014d64d541639701785.png" data-width="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="338" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2021/12/17/9a07a92df9d44e609398f1e3082656701639701785.png" data-width="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><blockquote><p>来源：电玩巴士</p></blockquote>
