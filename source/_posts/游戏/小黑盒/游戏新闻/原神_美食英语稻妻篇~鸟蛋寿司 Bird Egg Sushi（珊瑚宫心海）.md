<p><b>配方材料：</b>鸟蛋*1、糖*1</p><p><b>星级：</b>★</p><p><b>食谱获取方法：</b>稻妻志村屋购买</p><p><br></p><p>稻妻的菜免不了要啃一下假名。Sushi很简单，寿司 すし。珊瑚宫心海 Sangonomiya Kokomi……特别长是吧，我们来一点点看。我日语很烂，所以我大胆地猜测一下，“珊瑚”是さんご sango，“宫”是みや miya。日语两个词之间通常加个の no，比如宵宫的姓氏长野原是長野の原 ながのはら，所以“珊瑚宫”就是珊瑚の宮 Sangonomiya。“心”是こころ，“海”是うみ，那么“心海”就是ここみ Kokomi。</p><p><br></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="245" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2022/02/06/ce6e555c37394d9b8756499c5e4a89321644112155.png" data-width="412" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><h4 class="img-desc">鸟蛋寿司</h4><p>An everyday Inazuman dish. The sliced Egg Roll is placed over <b>seasoned </b>rice. Simply made, yet full of flavor.</p><p><b>seasoned  /ˈsiːznd/ adj. 调好味的</b></p><p>稻妻的家常料理之一。 将切成片的鸟蛋烧放在调味过的米饭.上，做法简单，美味却丝毫不打折扣。</p><p><br></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="235" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2022/02/06/eefe588c7fa343898dc14d995352dbff1644112156.png" data-width="412" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><h4 class="img-desc">奇怪的鸟蛋寿司</h4><p>An everyday Inazuman dish. The egg roll was not <b>beated </b>evenly and the <b>vinegared </b>rice was also over-seasoned... Even simple dishes leave no room for carelessness.</p><p><b>beat /biːt/  v. 搅拌，混合（打蛋器叫做eggbeater)</b></p><p><b>vinegared  /ˈvɪnɪɡərd/ adj. 醋腌的</b></p><p>稻妻的家常料理之一。 制作鸟蛋烧时没有搅拌均匀，醋饭的调味也稍显过头.看来再简单的料理也不能掉以轻心。</p><p><br></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="236" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2022/02/06/46385ed16c4443a99e1637babec08a041644112156.png" data-width="411" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><h4 class="img-desc">美味的鸟蛋寿司</h4><p>An everyday Inazuman dish. The tender egg <b>bounces </b>on the tongue together with the rich grains, bringing to mind a joy that recalls one's childhood. Eager to <b>relive </b>that sweetness, you pick up another before your brain can even <b>catch up</b>.</p><p><b>bounce /baʊns/ v. 跳动，蹦</b></p><p><b>relive /ˌriːˈlɪv/  vt. 再体验</b></p><p><b>catch up 赶上</b></p><p>稻妻的家常料理之一。 嫩滑的鸟蛋牵着饱满的米粒一起在舌尖蹦跳，快活得仿佛重回童年..为了让嘴中的甜蜜延续下去，不知不觉已经拿起了下一个。</p><p><br></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="249" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2022/02/06/a2da343194b64ec49b73aeec3ee11a831644112157.png" data-width="436" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><h4 class="img-desc">「奇策」</h4><p>Sangonomiya Kokomi's specialty. The egg is wrapped up almost like a package, and the fish tail at the end is both small and very cute. It really makes you wonder what's inside the package, but at the same time, you can't bear to eat such a beautiful-looking thing... You <b>glance at </b>the <b>crystal-clear </b>smile in Kokomi's eyes. Ah, darn it, you've fallen for her <b>trick</b>, haven't you?</p><p><b>glance at 匆匆一瞥</b></p><p><b>crystal-clear /ˌkrɪstl ˈklɪr/ adj. 透明如水晶的</b></p><p><b>trick /trɪk/ n. 花招，诡计，骗局（中计了，，中了美人计）</b></p><p>珊瑚宫心海的特色料理。蛋皮包成锦囊之形，末端鱼尾小巧而又可爱。如此珍品让人好奇「锦囊」中包裹之物，却又捧在掌心不忍下口…看着心海脸上盈盈的笑意，可恶，已经中计了吗？</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="1920" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2022/02/06/b414c6d17e9b4d54893b82a3345726d61644112157.jpg" data-width="979" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="1701" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2022/02/06/5850c6c24bb244b99c3594a3c6d2ec141644112157.jpg" data-width="1210" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><h4 class="img-desc">大家不要中计啊！</h4><p><a href="heybox://open_subject" target="_blank">#神来之作新年特别篇#</a><br></p>
