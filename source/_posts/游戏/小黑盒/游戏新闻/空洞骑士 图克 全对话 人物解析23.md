<p>“你再见到他的时候，朋友啊……帮我说声谢谢。他帮助过我。”——————图克</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="616" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/04/22/237acf7cfe023e6973e739e080f2fa5e/thumb.jpeg" data-width="998" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>生平<br></p><p>图克是一个在下水道卖腐臭蛋的外来虫，他的蛋似乎就是从下水道捡的（水流给我带来了所有我想要的东西）。</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="927" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/04/22/f314265c4b2fbb5b6f259d590ff4944e/thumb.jpeg" data-width="580" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>从对话和梦语看，图克在寻找着谁，并且希望找到他后，它能够向他道歉，极有可能是下面这位（奎若官方漫画中出现的布恩）。</p><p>钢魂模式下，由于没有吉吉收腐臭蛋把阴影招过来，图克就变成了一具尸体。</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="622" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/04/22/bc6e11297584401866b0c0d063859210/thumb.jpeg" data-width="1003" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>腐臭蛋的携带上限为80个，当你有80个蛋，图克会拒绝再卖给你蛋。</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="432" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/04/22/8d8c28b9a11b2e8736659ed13f8e4681/thumb.jpeg" data-width="853" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="564" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/04/22/93fca5c9aff7bdf8595a10099699dcbe/thumb.jpeg" data-width="996" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>（对话）</p><p>嗯嗯嗯嗯嗯嗯……水流给我带来了所有我想要的东西……我只要把它们找出来就行了。水流也带来了你。你饿了吗？我可以把吃的分给你……不过你得先给吉欧。</p><p>Mmmnnnnnnngghhh... The water brings me everything I wish for... I just need to find it.It brought you here, too. Are you hungry? I'll share my food with you... if you give me Geo.</p><p>我可以把吃的分给你……只要你先给吉欧。</p><p>I'll share my food with you... if you give me Geo</p><p>（吉欧不够）</p><p>你怎么会吉欧不够呢？！哼！不给吉欧我就不分给你吃的。</p><p>Why don't you have enough Geo?!Mnnghh! I won't share my food if you can't give me Geo.</p><p>（购买）</p><p>嗯……吉欧现在是我的了。别想拿回去。给你，走得时候把吃的带上。</p><p>Mnngh... this Geo is mine now. Don't try to take it back.Here, take your food with you when you leave.</p><p>（拒绝）</p><p>嗯？你不要的话我就拿走了。</p><p>Mngh? I'll share my food with you... if you give me Geo.</p><p>（携带腐臭蛋数量达到上限）</p><p>你有很多食物是不是？ 哎……我可以闻到你的气味。你很贪婪……我不把吃的分给你。</p><p>You have a lot of food, don't you? Mnngh... For helping me.</p><p>（梦语）</p><p>嗯嗯嗯嗯……我们会再见面的。水会把你带到我身边。我只要不断寻找……不断祈祷。下次见面的时候……我希望你能……道歉。</p><p>Mmmnnnnnnngghhh... I'll find you again. The water will bring you to me.
