<p>没有人知道多莉与艾莉丝相遇的具体过程，但她们确实是一对让人艳羡的合作伙伴。</p><p>No one knows how exactly Dori met Alice, but they are truly a pair of business partners that could make all merchants jealous.</p><p>艾莉丝周游世界各地，不会一直停留在须弥，但每过一段时间，她就会托人将自己的新发明寄给多莉。</p><p>Alice travels the world, so she does not stay for long every time she visits Sumeru. However, every once in a while, Alice will ask someone to send her new invention to Dori.</p><p>而多莉也会在售卖之后，想办法将摩拉送到艾莉丝手上。</p><p>Dori, for her part, will sell it and find a way to send the Mora to Alice.</p><p>有时艾莉丝会附一封信，告诉多莉自己最近身处险地，摩拉可以先存放在多莉那里。</p><p>Sometimes, Alice sends the invention with an attached letter that tells Dori to keep the Mora for the time being because Alice is currently in a dangerous area.</p><p>多莉却执意派人携带摩拉赶往距离艾莉丝最近的安全地区，以保证艾莉丝能够第一时间拿到摩拉。</p><p>However, Dori insists on getting someone to send the Mora to safe zones closest to Alice's position, making sure that Alice will receive the Mora at her earliest convenience.</p><p>人工费、运输费、保险费…各项费用相加并非一笔小钱，多莉却<b>毫不吝啬</b>，主动掏腰包提前支付。</p><p><b>毫不吝啬never stingy</b></p><p>The labor, shipment, insurance... The various costs tend to amount to a fairly large expenditure, but Dori is <b>never stingy </b>in this regard. She even pays in advance.</p><p>「大善人多莉从不<b>拖欠回款</b>。」</p><p><b>拖欠回款default on payment</b></p><p>"I, Dori, never <b>default on payment</b>."</p><p>「而且，艾莉丝女士的开销也不小，必须保证她时时刻刻有钱可用。」</p><p>"Also, Ms. Alice has expenses, too. I must make sure that she has Mora at hand whenever she needs it."</p><p>多莉总是念叨着这些话，不厌其烦地反复确认摩拉是否送到。</p><p>Dori often repeats these words and, despite the trouble, repeatedly makes sure that Alice's payment is delivered.</p><p>即使现在艾莉丝已经不是多莉唯一的<b>供货商</b>，多莉仍将这视为最重要的事。</p><p><b>供货商supplier</b></p><p>Though Alice is no longer Dori's only <b>supplier</b>, Alice's payment is still a top priority for Dori.</p><p>也许在她心里，一直记得相遇那天许下的承诺。</p><p>Perhaps it's because Dori keeps a certain promise embedded in her heart — the promise she made to Alice the day they met.</p><p>「你想要<b>数不清的</b>摩拉？可以呀。我可以提供给你各种神奇道具，卖出去多少就看你的本事啦。」</p><p><b>数不清的limitless</b></p><p>"You want <b>limitless </b>Mora? Sure. I can supply you with marvelous items of all kinds. But how much Mora you're going to make will depend on how good you are at sales."</p><p>「请放心。我会卖出高高的价格，换来多多的摩拉，以最快的速度送到你的手上…我会成为你最好的合作伙伴。」</p><p>"Don't worry. I'll sell them for a good price, earn a large sum of Mora, and deliver your share as quickly as possible... I'll be your greatest business partner."</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="791" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2022/09/22/4e99403ef3714a38896a2f33e8f0a3e61663836066.png" data-width="560" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><a href="heybox://open_subject" target="_blank">#原神3.0版本创作者征集活动#</a><br></p>
