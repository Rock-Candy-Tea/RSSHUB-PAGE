<p>视频游戏开发者Vintage Digital Revival发布了以豪华客船泰坦尼克号为舞台的视频冒险游戏《泰坦尼克号：荣耀》（暂译）的最新体验版Demo。</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="377" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/web/2022/02/01/064baf93a3e9083bf53129c89737da7e/thumb.jpeg" data-width="670" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>本作是以再现1912年迎来悲剧结局的豪华客船泰坦尼克号为目标进行的开发项目。这款游戏不仅再现了船舱,还再现了甲板、船舱、锅炉室等，玩家可以体验与乘员交流、并在船内进行探索和收集,甚至到船的“终结”。<br></p><p>这次发布的“MegaDemo(DEMO 401)”是能够让玩家体验船上25%以上部分的史上最高级别的体验版。可以探索各个等级的船舱和船员室等几个区域。<br></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="361" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/web/2022/02/01/687926b49e622416b7bb3f44826c1c15/thumb.jpeg" data-width="670" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="361" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/web/2022/02/01/f5692b37376bc4f481a40e9a6e644d4c/thumb.jpeg" data-width="670" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="361" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/web/2022/02/01/c4eec5691595676bfe61a810462ae515/thumb.jpeg" data-width="670" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="378" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/web/2022/02/01/01a5c6e383c7cbfcd2dae6829a9ecf42/thumb.jpeg" data-width="670" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="377" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/web/2022/02/01/10918bcaf06fd19ad567fa5bc3b14d0f/thumb.jpeg" data-width="670" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="377" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/web/2022/02/01/bd1171fd68b98a42ea8f01d17932f6f1/thumb.jpeg" data-width="670" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><a href="heybox://open_subject" target="_blank">#神来之作新年特别篇#</a><br></p>
