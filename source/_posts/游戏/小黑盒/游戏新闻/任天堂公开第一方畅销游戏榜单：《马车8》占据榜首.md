<blockquote><p>来源：gamingbolt</p><p><a href="https://gamingbolt.com/mario-kart-8-deluxe-hits-46-82-million-units-sold-animal-crossing-new-horizons-at-39-38-million-sales" target="_blank">点击此处查看原文>>></a></p></blockquote><p>除了更新的任天堂Switch设备销售量，任天堂还更新了其最畅销的第一方游戏销量。截至2022年6月30日，《马里奥卡丁车8豪华版》以4682万台的销量继续领先。这款历史悠久的卡丁车赛车在8月4日发布了通行证中含有的第二波更新，增加了一条新的和七条重制的赛道。<a href="https://gamingbolt.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/mario-kart-8-deluxe.jpg" target="_blank"></a></p><p>《集合啦!动物森友会》以3938万的销量位居第二，其次是《超级大乱斗：终极版》，销量为2882万。《塞尔达传说：荒野之息》目前的销量为2714万；《口袋妖怪：剑与盾》的销量达到2450万；《超级马里奥：奥德赛》的销量为2393万。<br></p><p>榜单的最后四名中，《超级马里奥派对》以1806万的销量排在第七位。口袋妖怪《辉煌钻石和闪亮珍珠》的销量为1479万，其次是口袋妖怪《Let's Go, 皮卡丘！/Let's Go, 伊布！》销量为1466万。最后《Ring Fit Adventure》以1454万套的销量位列前十。<br></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="576" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/web/2022/08/03/03ca34ec82456f395e25d033c5ba7c27/thumb.jpeg" data-width="1024" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="original-news">
In addition to updated Nintendo Switch hardware sales, Nintendo also updated its list of top-selling first-party titles. As of June 30th, 2022, Mario Kart 8 Deluxe continues to lead the pack with 46.82 million units sold. The long-standing kart racer sees the release of Wave 2 from its Booster Course Pass on August 4th, which adds one new and seven remastered tracks.
Animal Crossing: New Horizons is in second place with 39.38 million units sold, followed by Super Smash Bros. Ultimate at 28.82 million sales. The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild is currently at 27.14 million units sold; Pokemon Sword and Shield have hit 24.50 million sales; Super Mario Odyssey is at 23.93 million units sold.
The final four of the list include Super Mario Party in seventh with 18.06 million sales. Pokemon Brilliant Diamond and Shining Pearl are at 14.79 million sales, followed by Pokemon: Let’s Go, Pikachu! and Let’s Go, Eevee! at 14.66 million sales. Finally, Ring Fit Adventure rounds out the top 10 at 14.54 million units sold.</p>
