23/2/16<br></p><p>樱桃队</p><p>嘿大家好，在今天的更新中，我们将谈论到目前为止我们还没有展示的游戏的一部分:护符系统！</p><p>这些神秘的符咒遍布整个圣巢，每一个都包含一个强大的符咒，一旦装备就会生效。你可以在被遗忘已久的宝藏室中找到它们，它们被可怕的生物守卫着，或者被你遇到的奇怪的朋友拥有(最好保存你的地理位置！).</p><p>当你在长椅上休息时，你可以进入你的护身符菜单来装备你捡到的护身符。</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="566" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/03/26/8f7ec7f9e874cb05a4dfc4b379ec84cc/thumb.jpeg" data-width="1070" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>看看那些空位置！让你想把它们都收集起来，对吗？</p><p>每种护符都要花费一定数量的（护符）槽位，这取决于它们的威力。这限制了你一次可以装备多少，所以你必须做出一些战术决定，找到适合你的游戏风格的装备。</p><p>当你继续你的冒险，你可以想办法扩大你的（护符）槽位，让你一次装备更多的护身符。你很快就会成为一个精力充沛的人！</p><p>那么魔咒到底是做什么的呢？各种各样的事情！看看这个:</p><p>你现在看到的是最早的咒语之一“聚群”的效果。有没有发现自己迷失在裂缝或深坑里？就不能停下来自己捡起来吗？别担心！有了这个护身符，乐于助人的小虫子会捡起所有的零钱并带给你。太方便了！</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="655" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/03/26/68f053a0d11f0c87a11a2133b47de427/thumb.jpeg" data-width="1200" THE POWER OF THE CHARMS<br></p><p>Team Cherry Games</p><p>Hey Everyone,</p><p>For today’s update, we’re going to talk about a part of the game that we haven’t shown at all so far: The charm system!</p><p>These mystical charms can be found throughout Hallownest, each of them containing a powerful spell that will take effect once equipped. You can find them hidden in long-forgotten treasure chambers, guarded by fearsome creatures, or in the possession of the weird friends you meet (better save up your Geo!).</p><p>When you’re resting at a bench, you can enter your Charms Menu to equip the charms you’ve picked up.</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="566" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/03/26/1ac4f7647c73d54278f3fa407449e629/thumb.jpeg" data-width="1070" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p> Look at all those empty slots! Makes you want to collect them all, right?</p><p>Each charm costs a certain amount of notches, depending on how powerful they are. This limits how many you can equip at once, so you’ll have to make some tactical decisions and find a load-out that suits your playstyle.</p><p>As you continue on your adventure, you can find ways to expand your notches, allowing you to keep more charms equipped at once. You’ll be a powerhouse in no time!</p><p>So what do charms actually do? All sorts of things! Check this out:</p><p> What you’re seeing is the effect of one of the earliest charms, ‘Gathering Swarm’. Ever find yourself losing stray Geo down cracks or pits? Can’t be bothered to stop and pick ’em up yourself? No worries! With this charm equipped, helpful little bugs will pick up all that loose change and bring it to you. So convenient!</p><p>Here are just a few more charms to find amidst the ruins of Hallownest:</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="655" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/03/26/d8cca159f2aaead5a8f0c1b047088628/thumb.jpeg" data-width="1200" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>Stalwart Shell</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="125" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/03/27/3e742d0c7cf23eb23d3a580a0907e450/thumb.png" data-width="131" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>Wayward Compass</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="122" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/03/27/b4a8bf23bdefbba628328b4a854c0173/thumb.png" data-width="121" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>Fury of the Fallen</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="170" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/03/27/875e27680a195b0ffa2a67c903f05c25/thumb.png" data-width="160" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>Vengeful Swarm</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="136" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/03/27/f5c4f86e77c5d4394f98b72c856490c3/thumb.png" data-width="132" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>Unending Greed</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="129" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/03/27/7d985578188672b611552bc17b209530/thumb.png" data-width="127" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>Stalwart Shell: Increases the length of your recovery time after taking damage, allowing you to escape from nasty situations.</p><p>Wayward Compass: Shows you your position on the map. Useful for finding your way, but is the cost worth it?</p><p>Fury of the Fallen: When you’re down to only one HP, you deal extra damage with your nail. Risky, but powerful in the hands of an expert!</p><p>Vengeful Swarm: When taking damage, spawn angry hatchlings that attack nearby enemies.</p><p>Unending Greed (Glass): Causes much more Geo to drop from enemies, but will break if you’re defeated while it’s equipped! Be careful and don’t let your greed blind you to the dangers ahead.</p><p>We’ve got a whole bunch more planned, so we hope you’ll look forward to hunting these stylish charms down and experimenting with their powerful, sometimes weird, abilities!</p><p>Development Update</p><p>To finish off, a quick update on overall development. We’re polishing up the last of the content so we can move on to the testing phase and things are going really well. We’ve also been playing around with the Wii U build, which is looking really promising (bring on the gamepad features!). What about other console platforms? We’re definitely still looking into them and will hopefully have some more to say soon.</p><p>And lastly, we have another trailer in the works which we’ll be releasing soon, so look forward to it. Let’s see if we can hit the front page of Reddit again!</p><p>Hollow Knight</p>
