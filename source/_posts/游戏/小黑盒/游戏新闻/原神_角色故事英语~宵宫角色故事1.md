<p>烟花对于稻妻人而言，就像是夏日的符号。</p><p>To Inazumans, fireworks symbolize summer.</p><p><br></p><p><b>定期</b>举办的各式庆典中，多种活动<b>轮流</b>上场，唯有烟花表演是<b>不变的</b>惯例。</p><p><b>定期at fixed times</b></p><p><b>轮流a rotation of</b></p><p><b>不变的sole constant</b></p><p>The various festivals that take place <b>at fixed times </b>feature <b>a rotation of </b>various events, with fireworks being the <b>sole constant</b>.</p><p><br></p><p>因此百余年前，<b>社奉行</b>以「<b>纪念</b>」为主题，筹办了<b>令人目不暇接的</b>烟花表演。</p><p><b>社奉行the Yashiro Commission</b></p><p><b>纪念commemoration</b></p><p><b>令人目不暇接的dazzling</b></p><p>Over a century ago,<b>the Yashiro Commission </b>once organized a <b>dazzling </b>fireworks display with "<b>commemoration</b>" as the theme.</p><p><br></p><p>它们都被交给本地最负盛名的烟花世家主办，这就是「长野原烟花会」的起源。</p><p>The fireworks then were made by the greatest pyrotechnicians in the land, and this was the origin of the Naganohara Fireworks Show.</p><p><br></p><p>此后，烟花这一事物普及开来，「长野原烟花会」逐渐变成<b>全民参与</b>的活动。</p><p><b>全民参与all people would participate</b></p><p>In latter days, fireworks would become more common, and the Naganohara Fireworks Show would slowly become an event in which <b>all people would participate</b>.</p><p><br></p><p>所有人都能在烟花会上亲手燃放最中意的烟花，而「长野原烟花店」的店长会选择合适的时机，<b>安排</b>最为盛大的表演。</p><p><b>安排set up</b></p><p>During the Fireworks Show, everyone can light their favorite fireworks, and the owner of the Naganohara Fireworks store will pick an appropriate time to <b>set up </b>a most lavish performance.</p><p><br></p><p>宵宫接任店长那年，她举办的第一场<b>盛典</b>就已令人<b>叹为观止</b>。</p><p><b>盛典fiesta</b></p><p><b>叹为观止jaw-droppingly</b></p><p>The year Yoimiya took over, her first <b>fiesta </b>was already <b>jaw-droppingly </b>good.</p><p><br></p><p>那次烟花会前夕，神里绫华曾向宵宫<b>叮嘱</b>，「大御所大人也会观看这次的烟花表演，所以还请务必在烟花中加入大御所大人的<b>家纹</b>——<b>雷之三重巴</b>。」</p><p><b>叮嘱enjoin</b></p><p><b>家纹emblem</b></p><p><b>雷之三重巴the Electro Mitsudomoe</b></p><p>On the night before that Fireworks Show, Kamisato Ayaka <b>enjoined </b>Yoimiya thusly: "Her Excellency will be watching this fireworks display personally. As such, please add Her Excellency's <b>emblem</b>—<b>the Electro Mitsudomoe</b>— into the fireworks repertoire, if you would."</p><p><br></p><p>宵宫信心满满地答应下来：「好好期待吧!」</p><p>"Haha, just wait for it!" Yoimiya replied, agreeing with great confidence.</p><p><br></p><p>表演开始前，宵宫跑遍城区每个角落，布置了数以千记的<b>发射筒</b>。作为烟花会<b>压轴节目</b>，燃绽的焰火构成了庞大绘卷，描摹了一整座倒悬天空的稻妻城。</p><p><b>发射筒launch tube</b></p><p><b>压轴节目finale</b></p><p>Before the show could begin, Yoimiya ran all about the city, placing thousands of <b>launch tubes </b>in every nook and cranny. At the festival's <b>finale</b>, the blazing fireworks formed a gigantic picture scroll that depicted the city of Inazuma in the skies.</p><p><br></p><p>可绫华并未在任何一枚烟花中找到<b>关键的</b>家纹，以为宵宫的计划出了差池。</p><p><b>关键的critical</b></p><p>Yet Ayaka did not find the <b>critical </b>emblem in any one of these fireworks, and wondered if Yoimiya had made an error in her arrangements.</p><p><br></p><p>事后询问宵宫，绫华才得知，原来那座倒悬空中的城池，被宵宫赋予了名为「<b>视角</b>」的戏法。</p><p><b>视角 angling</b></p><p>It was only after speaking with Yoimiya that Ayaka discovered that the image of Inazuma in the skies had an "<b>angling</b>" trick added to it.</p><p><br></p><p>从<b>天守阁</b>向下观望时，齐放的烟花恰好构成了雷电将军的<b>家纹</b>，庞大清晰而规整，甚至还有<b>俏皮的花边</b>作为装饰。</p><p><b>天守阁Tenshukaku</b></p><p><b>家纹house-crest</b></p><p><b>俏皮的花边playfully lacy</b></p><p>If viewed from the <b>Tenshukaku</b>, the fireworks would form a huge, clear, and orderly image of the Raiden Shogun's <b>house-crest</b>— with some <b>playfully lacy </b>decorations on the side to boot.</p><p><br></p><p>宵宫怎么会在烟花表演中失手呢？她同时赢取了民众与雷电将军的赞赏，这场盛宴亦成为无数人记忆中的<b>传世经典</b>。</p><p><b>传世经典timeless classic</b></p><p>Of course, how could Yoimiya miss a beat at a fireworks performance?
