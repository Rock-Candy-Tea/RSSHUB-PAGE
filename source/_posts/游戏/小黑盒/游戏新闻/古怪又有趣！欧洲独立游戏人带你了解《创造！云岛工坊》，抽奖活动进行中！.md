I’m excited to see the game release in China!</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="530" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/web/2021/12/10/da24e225653db6212a2983d281ac213a/thumb.png" data-width="1213" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p> </p><p><br></p><p><b>Q:那么请问作为游戏的开发者，您会如何向中国玩家介绍《创造！云岛工坊》呢？</b><br>Please introduce the game Craftlands Workshoppe to Chinese players from the developer's point of view.</p><p> A:《创造！云岛工坊》是一个激情满满的项目。在这么多年致力于开发（风格相似的）《冒险者商店》系列作品后，我想创造些稍微有些不一样的东西，一款更注重叙事，但同样有着绝佳创造和经营体验的游戏作品。在创造这部叙事导向模拟经营游戏时，那部曾火爆一时的《守墓人》给了我很大的启发，因此我相信，这款《创造！云岛工坊》应该也会给玩家们带来与之类似的，有趣而古怪的游戏体验。<br>Answer: Craftlands Workshoppe is very much a passion project. After having learnt a lot working on the Shoppe Keep series of games, I wanted to do something slightly different. A game more focused on the story, but with some good crafting and management game loops. I took a lot of influence from Graveyard Keeper and created my own story-based management game. The result, I believe, is a fun and quirky experience.</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="714" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/web/2021/12/10/51590295fbe93dfc1341c8754db16b75/thumb.png" data-width="1268" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><b><br></b></p><p><b>Q：是什么启发了您和您的团队将工坊开到了云端之上？在您看来Craftlands Workshoppe应该是一个怎样的世界呢？</b></p><p>What inspired you and your team to move the workshop to the cloud? What kind of world should Craftlands Workshoppe be in your opinion?</p><p> A：云端世界更有神秘感嘛~而且这样的设定也让我们可以赋予游戏中那些可探索地点以鲜明的特色，也让我在设计云岛世界观——大到解释诸岛之间的交通运输体系，小到详述云岛居民获取饮用水这类的问题时可以更有创意地玩儿些花活儿。</p><p>Answer: Having the world set in the clouds gave it a sense of mystery. This also allowed us to clearly define areas of the world which you get to explore throughout the game. It also allowed me some fun creative liberties with the lore, from modes of transportation to detailing how the residents of Craftlands obtain water.</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="644" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/web/2021/12/10/14cce445211ac27bb8b3015b09a3014b/thumb.png" data-width="1104" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p> </p><p><b>Q:您认为《创造！云岛工坊》是否成功地表达出了您渴望展现的内容？在多大程度上实现了您的构想？如果十分代表“非常成功”的话，您愿意给现在的《创造！云岛工坊》打几分？<br></b>Do you think Craftlands Workshoppe has succeeded in expressing what you want to show? To what extent did you realize your idea? If 10 stands for "very successful", how would you rate the current Craftlands Workshoppe?</p><p>A：我觉得这部《创造！云岛工坊》很接近我的最初想法。但是吧，听取了玩家们和发行商的反馈意见后，我觉得还是得做一款能真正让玩家们乐在其中的游戏作品。所以几年前我们在steam上开启了游戏的抢先体验，也收集了很多之前测试版的反馈意见（来让这部作品日臻完美）。<br>Answer: I feel the game is quite close to my original idea. However, through feedback from players and the publishing team at Excalibur, we crafted a game that I believe players can really enjoy. That’s why we launched in Early Access and also gathered lots of feedback from players from betas even earlier.</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="714" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/web/2021/12/10/439dbb61add05aaadc6ecc5b50bfbdf6/thumb.png" data-width="1268" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><br></p><p><b>Q:《创造！云岛工坊》中您哪些内容细节或者游戏机制最为满意？最喜欢哪些NPC？我们能在游戏中能找到您自己或是某些朋友的原型或是小彩蛋吗？</b></p><p>Which details or game mechanism are you most satisfied with in "Craftlands Workshoppe"?<br>Which NPC do you like best?<br>Can we find prototypes or small eggs of yourself or some of your friends in the game?</p><p> </p><p>A:我对这游戏的故事很满意，还有我在游戏文本和对话中展现自己幽默感的方式~~</p><p>你会发现岛上有几个代表着云岛社区核心成员的NPC，目前游戏的彩蛋也和他们息息相关。（这些NPC中）我尤其欣赏Mr Boogeyman这个角色，因为他在故事中完成了自己的开悟。<br>Answer: I am very satisfied with the story of the game, but also with how I was able to put my own sense of humour in dialogue and text. You can find a few NPCs around the islands who represent core members of the community, and easter eggs relating to team members. I'm especially proud of Mr Boogeyman, a character who goes through their own enlightenment throughout the story.</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="487" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/web/2021/12/10/5b537047e8848699a548f40d6ae00394/thumb.png" data-width="865" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p> </p><p><b>Q:有些中国玩家表示当他们进入游戏后，很难迅速体验到游戏最好玩的部分，请问您有什么实用的建议能帮助改善这些朋友们的游戏体验吗？还是说《创造！云岛工坊》就是这样一部硬核的，必须沉下心静静体验才能感受到乐趣的作品？</b><br>Some Chinese players say that when they enter the game, it is difficult to experience the most fun part of the game quickly. Do you have any practical suggestions that can help improve the game experience of these friends?Or Craftlands Workshoppe is such a hard-core game that you must settle down and experience it quietly in order to have fun.</p><p>A:我对游戏的核心体验也很满意，我想在社区的帮助和反馈下，我们已经（为游戏）开发了一个有趣的核心体验——对一款游戏而言，这才是最重要的内容。我们强烈建议（玩家）以炼金术士的身份开始游戏，这是游戏中最简单的也最有前途的职业，（它为角色提供的种种便利）能帮助你快速而顺利地步入游戏的进阶阶段。<br>Answer: I’m also happy with the core game loops – I think, with help and feedback from the community, that we’ve developed a fun core game loop, which is arguably the most important aspect of a game.We highly recommend starting the game as an alchemist. This is the easiest route to advance within the game, giving you a quick head start to the later stages of the game.</p><p> </p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="714" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/web/2021/12/10/2c510e854d41935d895da52837aa81c2/thumb.png" data-width="1268" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p> </p><p><b>Q:未来的《创造！云岛工坊》还会有什么重大更新吗？是否会增加冒险和战斗部分的内容吗？<br></b>The creation of the future! Will there be any major updates to Craftlands Workshoppe? Will the content of adventures and battles be added?</p><p>A:我们已经在《创造！云岛工坊》的1.0版本里以一种，我们相信能让玩家们心满意足结束云岛之旅的方式把整个故事讲完了，所以恐怕是没什么升级游戏的计划了。这游戏的1.0版本已经相当完整了。<br></p><p>Answer: For v1.0, we have rounded off the story in a way we believe will give a satisfying end to players’ experience of Craftlands Workshoppe.
