<p>经典街机游戏《雪人兄弟》确认将在NS平台上重制。重制版标题为《雪人兄弟 Special》，包含数字版和实体收藏版，具体发售日和售价未定。</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="1350" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2022/02/16/0402c5c10cc7463b84af3009b0c0aed21645004582.jpg" data-width="822" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>重制版和街机版一样支持双人游玩，同时在原版的50个关卡上新增30个关卡，新怪物和新boss也会出现。本作还将加入「怪物挑战」模式，让玩家可以操控怪物进行游戏。</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="757" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2022/02/16/38505d14c9014cdda5e9f5d1783c422b1645004583.jpg" data-width="1345" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="776" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2022/02/16/f1d61e8e98fa441bb5d15ead05cedbdd1645004583.jpg" data-width="1380" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="745" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2022/02/16/5531b2604ce944b3985b43327e53801b1645004584.jpg" data-width="1324" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="745" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2022/02/16/1ded77090d74487da93db5ec96e4e9501645004584.jpg" data-width="1324" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="1080" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2022/02/16/1a5607e605174ee49eafa6eae3cf757a1645004584.jpg" data-width="1743" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
