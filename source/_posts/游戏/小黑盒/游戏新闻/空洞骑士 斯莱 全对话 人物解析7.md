<p>“骨钉所指，即为目标！” <br></p><p> ---------------------------斯莱</p><p>斯莱，你可以叫他奸商，也可以叫他祖师爷，讲真，最开始在十字路遇到祖师爷的时候，我还真以为他就像他说的一样是一个“在德特茅斯过着平静的生活”的良民，结果嘛…大家都知道了。乍一看，斯莱似乎很爱财，但再仔细相处一段时间，便会发现…他是真的爱财。不过，爱财不过是他给世人展现的伪装，骨钉贤者的荣耀与对徒弟的关切始终在他心间。当瘟疫夺去了他清醒的神智，他口中叨念着的仍是对学生的训诫；而当他回到德特茅斯开始了他的奸商生涯，他心中所想的，也仍然是“无论你们仨在哪里，我都希望你们能照顾好自己”。作为骨钉贤者，他有自己的准则，根据奥罗所说，他教导他们要把技艺传授给值得的人，要学会“分享力量”，而当小骑士学会了三个剑技，已经放下了贤者身份的他仍然遵照传统，把最后的护符赠予他。在神居里，我们得以一睹祖师爷年轻时的风貌，他心中充满了对骨钉技艺的热爱与荣耀，同时也为弟子的决裂感到深深的遗憾与悲叹。“记住，你们是兄弟…”这一句话里，包含着多少的关怀与悔恨！斯莱作为他们的师父，总是隐藏自己的真实情感，对于外人，更是用一副奸商嘴脸不让他们过多了解自己，可他仍然关注着小骑士，也仍然时时刻刻怀念着他的学生们。一钉寒芒手中握，万缕柔肠心间存，斯莱，无愧为伟大的骨钉贤者！</p><p>斯莱是空洞骑士中的一名商人。他在德特茅斯的小店出售许多商品。 斯莱还以骨钉贤者的姿态出现在神居中。</p><p>斯莱曾经是伟大的骨钉大师，在他还是骨钉大师的时候，她收了三个徒弟：马托，奥罗和席奥。也就是我们的学习剑技的三位剑技大师。</p><p>在深巢衰落后，并且和他的学生分别之后，斯莱放弃了他的骨钉大师的身份，转而从商，从他在的德特茅斯的小店中卖一些东西。但是，他并没有忘记他炉火纯青的骨钉技艺，并将他的大钉子留在了商店的地下室。</p><p>在某个时刻，一个梦使他关闭了商店，在德特茅斯下面的洞穴中徘徊。他还丢失了他的店主钥匙，该钥匙最终出现在水晶山峰的某一处地方。</p><p>击败位于遗忘十字路的格鲁兹之母后，在一个地洞中首次遇见斯莱。此时他头上笼罩着黄雾，似乎是正在被感染，或者是在做梦。和他对话，唤醒他，之后他就会回到德特茅斯小镇开店。</p><p>给予他店主的钥匙之后，就能解锁更多售卖的物品种类。</p><p>学习完全部三种骨钉技艺之后来找他对话，他会出现他商店的地下储藏间，此时我们会发现他居然是伟大的骨钉贤者。是他向骨钉大师传授了骨钉技艺。他承认我们的技艺，并且赠送一枚护符：骨钉大师的荣耀。</p><p>对话</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="647" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/03/10/2aec5aa2a28446b418f9a458b2871217/thumb.jpeg" data-width="1225" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>遗忘十字路</p><p>…啊啊，奥罗，你个**…你把你的骨钉挥舞得…像棒槌一样…</p><p>...ugghh, Oro you oaf.... You wield your nail... like a club...</p><p>…埃斯米（哎呦妈呀）…我们还得走多远…</p><p>...Esmy... how much deeper do we have to go...</p><p>噢！什么？！你是谁？！</p><p>Oh! What?! Who are you?!</p><p>…我明白了。这个老村庄。能把我带到这下面来，真是个奇怪的梦！如果你没有找到我，我觉得我永远都不会醒来了。</p><p>...I see. This old village. What a strange dream, to have led me down here! If you hadn't found me, I don't think I would've ever woken.</p><p>我是斯莱。正常情况下，我在泥口镇只不过过着一个平淡无奇的生活。这些遗迹里的空气不适合我，所以我最好还是回去。</p><p>I'm Sly. Usually, I live an uneventful life up in Dirtmouth. The air in these ruins doesn't agree with me, so I'd best be getting back.</p><p>如果你回到上面了，就来看我。我或许是那里最友好的一张面孔了，而且我能好好感谢你你做的好事。</p><p>If you return above, come and see me. I'm probably the friendliest face left there, and I can thank you properly for your good deed.</p><p>你在探索吗？非常勇敢！许多有胆量的流浪者已经迷失在了这些古老洞穴的饥饿。</p><p>You're exploring then? Very brave! Plenty of courageous wanderers have been lost to the hunger of these old caverns.</p><p>不过你有你的骨钉，而且我看得出来你知道怎么挥舞它。</p><p>You have your nail though, and I can tell just by looking that you know how to wield it.</p><p>梦语</p><p>…吉欧，吉欧，全世界都是吉欧。没有哪根骨钉能抵挡它的威力…</p><p>...Geo. Geo. All the world is Geo. No nail can withstand its might...</p><p>商店</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="683" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/03/10/5c6601d221a100c62bac824ecc857886/thumb.jpeg" data-width="1226" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>啊，你好！我就知道我们还会再见的。你喜不喜欢我这个舒适的小店？我让自己在这里过得非常舒服，出售一些古老的玩意儿给你这样的旅行者。</p><p>Ah, hello! I knew we'd meet again. How do you like my cosy little store? I've made myself pretty comfortable here, selling old trinkets to travellers like yourself.</p><p>如果你计划去下面旅行，我有几个东西可能能提高你活下去的机率。</p><p>If you're planning a trip below, I've several items that may improve your chance of survival.</p><p>啊，还在找要买的东西吗？你的吉欧够你去下面的花费吗？很遗憾，你似乎清理了除了我愿意卖掉的所有东西。一无所有！</p><p>Ah, still looking for something to buy? Is your Geo weighing you down? Unfortunately, you seem to have cleared out everything I'd be willing to part with. Nothing left at all!</p><p>我想我下面的库房里有几个旧玩意儿，但是我不久之前把钥匙丢了。</p><p>I think there might be a few old trinkets down in my storeroom, but I lost the key a while ago.</p><p>所以，恐怕我们之间没有生意可谈了！至少现在是。从这出去，让我跟你送给我的吉欧好好享受一点美好的时光。</p><p>So, no more business between us I'm afraid! For now at least. Get out of here and let me spend some quality time with all the Geo you've gifted me.</p><p>你那儿有什么？为什么，那是我的储藏室的钥匙！我以为我丢了呢。</p><p>What do you have there? Why, that's the key to my storeroom! I thought I'd lost it for good.</p><p>嗯？难道你是在这些老矿山里找到的？非常好奇。我不知道它为什么会出现在这么遥远的地方。</p><p>Hmm? Did you find it in those old mines perhaps? Very curious. I've no idea how it ended up in such a remote place.</p><p>你能把它还回来真是太诚实了，是的。非常善良。现在，我可以打开我的老储藏室了，我可以把里面一些积满灰尘的稀有化石出售给你。</p><p>Very honest of you to return it to me, yes. Very kind. And now that I can open my old storeroom up, I can sell you some of the rare stock that's been gathering dust in there.</p><p>这些东西可能有点过时，但是它们仍然保留着它们完整的市场价值。非常珍贵的东西！看一下…</p><p>These items may be a little time-worn, but they still retain their full market value. Very precious stuff! Take a look...</p><p>口袋空了吗？如果你还需要吉欧，可以去我们脚下的遗迹里看看，四处找找。只是要小心，不要陷入迷雾。我不会下去救你的。</p><p>Pockets feeling empty? If you need some more Geo, take a trip into the ruins below us and hunt around. Just take care not to fall into a haze. I won't be coming down to rescue you.</p><p>（地图店开门）</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="709" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/03/10/60c2732eb03092f4c0e9a8cb0b7fe192/thumb.jpeg" data-width="871" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>我看到他们在路对面开了那个地图商店。我想有点竞争是不错的。</p><p>I see they've opened that mapping shop across the way. I suppose a little competition is healthy.</p><p>或许我也应该开始买地图了…这是不是有点咄咄逼人？呵呵。在用吉欧组成的世界里，所有一切都很公平。</p><p>Perhaps I should start to sell maps as well... or is that a little aggressive? Heh. All's fair in a world made of Geo.</p><p>（第一次升级骨钉）</p><p>你已经找到了一把新骨钉，对吗？它看上去比上一把还要精良。</p><p>You've found a new nail, then? It looks like a much finer weapon than the last.</p><p>嗯？噢，这是同一把骨钉…但是它已经被磨光了！是一些技艺非常高超的人磨的…</p><p>Hmm? Oh, this is the same nail... but it's been honed! By someone extremely skilled...</p><p>骨钉是我们自己身体的延伸，它也是我们构造周围世界的工具。让它的边缘保持锋利是个神圣的职责。</p><p>A nail is an extension of ourselves, it is the instrument by which we shape the world around us. It's a sacred duty to keep its edges sharp.</p><p>…嗨，不好意思。我不应该那样继续东拉西扯地说下去了。我们还是来谈谈吉欧吧。</p><p>...Heh, sorry. I shouldn't ramble on like that. Let's talk Geo instead.</p><p>（学习剑技后）</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="705" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/03/10/ac6d3231db489f7d3a5ebdaa7831c2e0/thumb.jpeg" data-width="1244" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>嗯，你似乎有点不一样了。你的举止有些变化。</p><p>Hmm, you seem a little different. Something has changed in your bearing.</p><p>啊！你握骨钉的方式！所以你已经学到了骨钉技艺…</p><p>Ah! The way you hold your nail! So you've learnt a Nail Art...</p><p>非常有趣。我想知道，你还有继续训练的激情吗？</p><p>Very interesting. I wonder, do you have the passion to continue your training?</p><p>…所以，另一个家伙将会加入我们的队伍。</p><p>...So, another would join our group.</p><p>我有个礼物要给你，勇士。作为店主，我承认把它免费送出去是相当的不舍。</p><p>I have a gift for you, warrior. As a shopkeeper, I admit parting with it for free is rather difficult.</p><p>但是作为伟大的骨钉圣人，我必须认可你的技能。你已经掌握了我其余的学生的技艺。现在按照惯例，你也可以拥有那个标志。</p><p>But as the Great Nailsage I must recognise your skill. You have mastered the arts of my remaining pupils. Now tradition decrees you too may hold the symbol.</p><p>骄傲地带上它，骨钉大师。你应该是最后一个收到它的人了。</p><p>Wear it proudly, Nailmaster. You shall be the last to receive its like.</p><p>你可以走了，骨钉大师。我没有什么可给你的了…</p><p>Off you go Nailmaster. I've nothing more to give you...</p><p>至少没有免费的了。我暂时要上去，用我珍贵的商品换你大把的吉欧。</p><p>Nothing for free at least. I'll be up briefly and eager take your Geo in trade for my precious wares.</p><p>梦语</p><p>…这些精炼的武器…吉欧的战争要更加激烈了…</p><p>...These refined weapons...The battles of Geo are much deadlier...</p><p>你的旅行怎么样，骨钉大师？随着时间的过去，我相信你只会变得越来越强大。</p><p>How are your travels, Nailmaster? As time goes by, I'm sure you'll only grow stronger.</p><p>如果你见到其他的骨钉大师，告诉他们，我的脑海里会永远记住他们。而且他们应该来从他们</p><p>年长的老师这里买些东西，以示尊敬。</p><p>If you see your fellow Nailmasters again, tell them I carry them in my thoughts always. And </p><p>that they should come and buy something from their old teacher as a mark of respect.</p><p>（未获得剑技护符时买完所有东西）</p><p>又回来了？你知道的，我已经没有什么库存了，对吗？你已经把我这里扫荡干净了。</p><p>Back again? You know I haven't any stock left, yes? You've cleaned me out.</p><p>还是你想偷偷窥视我的吉欧山？对，它现在是我的了！这是公平交易得来的。</p><p>Or were you hoping to sneak a peek at my mountain of Geo? Well it's mine now! And fairly traded.</p><p>梦语</p><p>…不管你们在哪里…不管你们现在在干什么…我都希望你们三个能照顾好自己…</p><p>...Wherever you are... Whatever it is you do now...
