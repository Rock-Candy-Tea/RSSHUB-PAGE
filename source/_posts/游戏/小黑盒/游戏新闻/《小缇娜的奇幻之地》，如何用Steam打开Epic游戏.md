<p>3月25日，无主之地系列的衍生作《小缇娜的奇幻之地》，已经在主机端和PC端发售，其中PC端会在Epic独占一段时间，选用本文的方法可以快速用Steam启动Epic上的《小缇娜的奇幻之地》，同时可以享用比如Steam快捷键截图等功能，也不会影响Shift联机功能！</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="1080" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/web/2022/03/26/9c607e588915241f692e30911f610b8c/thumb.png" data-width="1920" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><br></p><hr><h2>步骤</h2><p>1. 下载legendary.exe</p><blockquote><p>https://github.com/derrod/legendary/releases<br></p></blockquote><p><img class="lazy" data-height="332" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/web/2022/03/26/bf56cfb903c58ce80e5593c22fb0ae7b/thumb.png" data-width="828" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>2. 运行</p><p>打开电脑Windows自带的CMD命令行，进入legendary.exe所在的文件夹，比如在H盘就先输入H:，然后进入程序所在的文件夹，输入legendary</p><blockquote><p>legendary</p></blockquote><p><img class="lazy" data-height="659" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/web/2022/03/25/f65fdc93f092ffd13d4cf7f9c816604f/thumb.png" data-width="622" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>3. 绑定Epic</p><p>输入legendary auth，会自动弹出Epic的登录程序，输入账号密码登录，即可完成绑定！</p><blockquote><p>legendary auth</p></blockquote><p><img class="lazy" data-height="1021" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/web/2022/03/25/ab270657ae0d3b9cec2170219e1b2d91/thumb.png" data-width="746" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>4. 获取游戏名称</p><p>在CMD中继续输入legendary list-games，可以查看所有的游戏名！</p><blockquote><p>legendary list-games</p></blockquote><p><img class="lazy" data-height="596" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/web/2022/03/25/767f96f5ff3100b4d22874127a0b4cc0/thumb.png" data-width="889" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>5. 复制游戏名</p><p>Tiny Tina's Wonderlands</p><blockquote><p>Tiny Tina's Wonderlands (App name: eb6ddfb245f647e2bb6902f52f38a535 | Version: 1.0.0_CL_2735735_Wonderlands)<br></p></blockquote><p><img class="lazy" data-height="21" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/web/2022/03/25/175675e66e1e6e5f56e14714de930925/thumb.png" data-width="902" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>6. 进入Steam添加legendary.exe</p><p>Steam左下角添加游戏->添加非Steam游戏->浏览找到legendary.exe的位置->添加程序</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="516" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/web/2022/03/25/15d020ba21fe79175b6b7b66d501e376/thumb.png" data-width="791" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>7. 设置启动选项</p><p>搜索legend找到程序->右键找到属性->找到启动选项，输入launch Tiny Tina's Wonderlands</p><blockquote><p>launch Tiny Tina's Wonderlands<br></p></blockquote><p><img class="lazy" data-height="741" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/web/2022/03/25/b39bfb7b670ee5e71cf6310d268efea9/thumb.png" data-width="1146" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>8. 同步游戏存档</p><p>①做到上面这一步如果不能打开游戏，可以继续在CMD中测试legendary list-installed，看看自己是否安装好小缇娜</p><blockquote><p>legendary list-installed</p></blockquote><p><img class="lazy" data-height="92" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/web/2022/03/25/11a64f5d7b4dbff4b4cdc5bc1fd13162/thumb.png" data-width="344" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>②如果为0，则先同步游戏 legendary -y egl-sync，然后再同步所有的运存档 legendary sync-saves，一路y点到底</p><blockquote><p>legendary -y egl-sync<br></p><p>legendary sync-saves</p></blockquote><p><img class="lazy" data-height="887" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/web/2022/03/25/acfc1bf43c1a4b2e5f74d713ff97e267/thumb.png" data-width="1317" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>③再次测试已下载游戏</p><p>如果小缇娜显示在下方，即可顺利在Steam中运行游戏！</p><blockquote><p>legendary list-installed</p></blockquote><p><img class="lazy" data-height="186" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/web/2022/03/25/c4bb28fd235ed74a4d591b7a1dec55e2/thumb.png" data-width="1155" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>9.
