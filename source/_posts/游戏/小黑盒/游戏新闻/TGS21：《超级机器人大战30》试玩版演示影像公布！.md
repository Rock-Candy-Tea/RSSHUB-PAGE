<blockquote><p>来源：游侠网</p></blockquote><p>电击Online今天公布了《<b>超级机器人大战30</b>（Super Robot Wars 30）》TGS2021试玩版演示影像。</p><p contenteditable="false"><iframe allowfullscreen="true" border="0" frameborder="no" framespacing="0" height="270" scrolling="no" src="https://player.bilibili.com/player.html?aid=720823209&bvid=BV17Q4y167Lj&cid=416882797&page=1" width="480"></iframe></p><p>该影像约20分钟，向玩家展示了本作中的游戏系统与战斗画面。</p><p>《超级机器人大战30》是《超级机器人大战》系列游戏作品的30周年纪念作，本质上是一款策略角色扮演类机甲游戏，《霸界王～GaoGaiGar对Betterman～》、《勇者警察》、《骑士与魔法》、《魔神凯撒INFINITISM》、《SSSS.GRIDMAN》将以新角色的形式登场。为了让没有玩过前作的人也能享受到乐趣，“超级机器人大战”中首次搭载了“自动战斗”。</p><p>《超级机器人大战30》将于10月28日发售，登陆PS4/Switch/PC平台，预购现已开启。</p><p><b>视频截图：</b></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="720" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2021/09/30/66ebaaa7ca8b459e9f1f9140d7f28f3e1633000923.jpg" data-width="1280" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="720" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2021/09/30/2299bb8663f84c7898116166d61080311633000923.jpg" data-width="1280" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="720" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2021/09/30/9d0288f8bee5489f87b3eb9838a757941633000924.jpg" data-width="1280" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="720" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2021/09/30/c35e91a989534f56ba0a5ab7d8f51fd91633000925.jpg" data-width="1280" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="720" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2021/09/30/0900fecf7cf040d39aff47b9ef6de70d1633000926.jpg" data-width="1280" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="720" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2021/09/30/c43273901952487792ee8ee417f503b31633000926.jpg" data-width="1280" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="720" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2021/09/30/5478369e2c2a4ed5ad000975a71ceac21633000927.jpg" data-width="1280" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
