<blockquote><p>来源：gematsu</p><p><a href="https://www.gematsu.com/2022/09/dragon-ball-xenoverse-2-dlc-character-gamma-2-announced-alongside-dragon-ball-super-super-hero-pack-set" target="_blank">点击此处查看原文>>></a></p></blockquote><p>发行商Bandai Namco和开发商Dimps宣布了《龙珠：超宇宙2》的新DLC“龙珠超：超级英雄套装”。其中包括两个新的可玩人物包。第一个包将在今年秋天推出，将增加伽马2和两个未公布的角色，第二个包还没有详细说明。</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="372" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/web/2022/09/05/feddc4869bed0669cd2a75d9fe70e56d/thumb.png" data-width="660" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>《龙珠：超宇宙2》游戏平台PC/PS4/XboxOne/Switch<br></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="717" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/web/2022/09/05/c3b640ad598143daff89ea61132ad0a7/thumb.png" data-width="1337" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="original-news">Publisher Bandai Namco and developer Dimps have announced Dragon Ball Xenoverse 2 downloadable content “Dragon Ball Super: Super Hero Pack Set,” which includes two packs of new playable characters. The first pack is due out this fall and will add Gamma 2 alongside two unannounced characters. The second pack has yet to be detailed. 
Dragon Ball Xenoverse 2 is available now for PlayStation 4, Xbox One, Switch, PC via Steam, and Stadia.
