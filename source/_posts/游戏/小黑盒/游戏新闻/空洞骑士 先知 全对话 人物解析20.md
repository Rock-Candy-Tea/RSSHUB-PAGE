<p>“将事物留存于心，它就随你继续活着。但如果忘记了它，它就被永远封存，那才是真正的死亡。”                       -------------------先知</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="727" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/04/16/35a7406a39dbecf9bbde56bd7fa047e7/thumb.jpeg" data-width="1220" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>先知，蛾族部落最后的幸存者，等待挥舞者并引导之，以忏悔往日的罪行。就像所有蛾子一样，先知对于记忆有着深层的理解。她明白，一件事终结于彻底的遗忘，纵观历史，多少英雄豪杰，多少情仇恩怨，有的被记载于史册，名垂千古，而有的则被埋没于时间的长河中，不见踪影。记忆，是一个人生命中最宝贵的东西，是过往的记忆塑造了现在的自我，是过往的记忆积累了智慧的宝库。诚然，有时记忆也会让人刻骨铭心，但先知面对同族犯下的罪行并没有逃避，而是通过引导挥舞者，照看灵魂沼地来维持平衡。先知有一种超然的智慧，即使对于辐光十分尊崇，但她明白，现在的辐光已经不是曾经那位爱好和平的梦境之神了，所以才会引导小骑士去封印瘟疫。但同时，她的忏悔既表现了她不逃避过去，也体现了她的较为消极的一面，因为一个失误而把自己和族群几乎全盘否定。但换一个角度来想，先知这样的举动正说明了她的怀旧与重情，所以才会有如此大的负罪感。先知的遗言也尤为触动人心，一位垂暮的老人在弥留之际忏悔往日的罪行，仿佛一声世事沧桑的叹息。岁月在她身上刻下了衰老的印记，但没有磨灭她那颗感情充沛的心灵。我相信，在最后的时刻，先知也应当完成了悔罪，回归到了梦境的怀抱中。</p><p>对话</p><p>那边的的门已经开了，你可以去那上面的空地看看。在那儿用心聆听，或许你可以听到那些逝去已久的虫的声音。</p><p>There, the door is open and you can enter the glade beyond it. Listen closely in there and you may hear the utterings of bugs long gone.</p><p>在梦境生根的地方你可以找到精华。这些低语着的植物遍布整个王国，你遇到过吗？外面就有一个。要不你用梦之钉去砍它，然后看会发生什么？</p><p>Essence can be found wherever dreams take root. Have you seen them? Those whispering plants that grow all over this old Kingdom? I believe there is one just outside. Why not strike it with your Dream Nail, and see what happens?</p><p>有时梦境会变为已经逝去之人的外形。这样的梦境蕴含着丰富的精华！ 你应该仔细搜索附近的坟墓和纪念碑。我在安息之地里看到过一个很有意思的墓碑。 一旦你下定决心要去惊扰沉眠之人的梦境，你就要做好战斗的准备……</p><p>Sometimes dreams take the shape of those who have passed away. Such dreams are bountiful sources of Essence! You should search carefully near graves and other monuments. Why, I believe I saw an interesting gravestone here in the Resting Grounds. If you do decide to disturb those dreams though, be prepared for a fight...</p><p>这个王国充满了古老的回忆，它们既美丽又奇特。找到它们，揭露它们，收集精华。</p><p>This kingdom is full of old memories, both beautiful and grotesque. Seek them out, reveal them, and gather Essence.</p><p>嗯，看来在收集梦境精华这件事上你还是没什么经验。用梦之钉收集 100 精华之后再过来找我，我会告诉你一些小知识。</p><p>Hm. You are still unpracticed in the way of dreams. Collect 100 Essence with the Dream Nail; visit me again and I will share what little wisdom I have.</p><p>你和梦之钉看起来都更具锋芒了，但你还有很长的路要走。等你收集了 200 精华之后再回到我这里。</p><p>You and the Dream Nail both seem a little sharper, but you still have a long way to go. Return to me once you have collected 200 Essence.</p><p>你和梦之钉看起来都更具锋芒了，但你还有很长的路要走。等你收集了 300 精华之后再回到我这里。</p><p>You and the Dream Nail both seem a little sharper, but you still have a long way to go. Return to me once you have collected 300 Essence.</p><p>啊，你又学到了很多使用梦之钉的技巧啊，不是吗？当你收集了 500 精华之后我会教你更多东西。</p><p>Ah, you have learnt a lot about wielding the Dream Nail, haven';t you? Return to me once you have collected 500 Essence and I will teach you more.</p><p>啊，你又学到了很多使用梦之钉的技巧啊，不是吗？当你收集了 700 精华之后我会教你更多东西。</p><p>Ah, you have learnt a lot about wielding the Dream Nail, haven';t you? Return to me once you have collected 700 Essence and I will teach you more.</p><p>啊，你已经开始变得梦幻起来了！等你收集到 900 精华，我会教授你一个“独门秘技”……</p><p>My my, you';re almost starting to become a dream yourself! Once you have collected 900 Essence I';ll teach you a trick that I';ve kept hidden for a long time...</p><p>啊，梦之钉散发着如此明亮的光辉！继续努力。等你收集了 1200 精华之后再回到我这里。</p><p>Ahhh, the Dream Nail is glowing so brightly! Keep it up. Return to me once you have collected 1200 Essence.</p><p>啊，梦之钉散发着如此明亮的光辉！继续努力。等你收集了 1500 精华之后再回到我这里。</p><p>Ahhh, the Dream Nail is glowing so brightly! Keep it up, return to me once you have collected 1500 Essence.</p><p>难以置信……梦之钉已经快要完全觉醒了！收集更多的精华！等你拥有 1800 精华之后，会出现不可思议的奇迹！</p><p>Incredible... the Dream Nail, it is so close to fully awakening! Collect more Essence! Once you have 1800 Essence, something truly marvellous will happen!</p><p>梦之钉已经完全觉醒了，但你却还在收集精华。也许我的执念也影响到了你？ 继续收集吧！看到你如今的成就，我觉得等待也是有价值的。 等你收集了 2400 精华之后再回到我这儿吧。虽然我没有礼物可以给你了……但只有这样。你才能证明自己是我们部落所等待的那个人。</p><p>The Dream Nail has fully awoken, yet still you collect Essence. Perhaps I';ve instilled an obsession in you? Keep at it, then! I';m happy to wait and watch your progress. Once you have collected 2400 Essence, return to me. I';ve no more gifts to give... but you';d prove yourself the Wielder my tribe awaited.</p><p>啊啊……我很抱歉…… 光芒……辐光…… 我……记得你。</p><p>Aahh... I';m sorry... Light... Radiance... I... remember you.</p><p>这么多的精华……如此明亮……毫无疑问，你就是我们部落期盼已久的挥舞者。 我们部落的民众都是从一道光中诞生的。那光很像是精华，也像那把强大的刃剑，但比它们都明亮得多。 有那么一段时期，民众无比崇敬那光辉并从沐浴其中感到无上的喜悦。 但另一道光出现在了这个世界上……化为王之形的沃姆。 我的祖先，无情的抛弃了孕育他们的光芒。他们不仅将其背叛……甚至将其遗忘。</p><p>So much Essence... So bright.... You truly are the Wielder my tribe so long has dreamed of. The folk of my tribe were born from a light. Light similar to Essence, similar to that powerful blade, though much brighter still. They were content to bask in that light and honoured it... for a time. But another light appeared in our world... A wyrm that took the form of a king. How fickle my ancestors must have been. They forsook the light that spawned them. Turned their backs to it... Forgot it even.</p><p>这个王国就是在那次背叛之中诞生的。但有关于那古老光辉的记忆却依旧留存了下来，传播着信仰的细语……直到所有圣巢的虫子都开始梦到那被遗忘的光芒。 啊，过去的就让它过去吧。我也应当如此。我已经保留着部落的记忆活了太久太久。就是为了等到你的出现，是时候了，我们也应当被遗忘了。 不要怀念我们，挥舞者，不要敬重我们，我们不配……</p><p>And so this kingdom was born from that betrayal. But the memories of that ancient light still lingered, hush whispers of faith... Until all of Hallownest began to dream of that forgotten light. Ah, but what';s done is done. And so am I. The Wielder has at last appeared and I';ve held the memories of my tribe for long enough. It is time for us to be forgotten too. Don';t remember us, Wielder. Don';t honour us. We do not deserve it...</p><p>啊，你回来了。欢迎回到清醒的世界。</p><p>Ahhhh, you';ve returned. Welcome back to the waking world.</p><p>啊，你回来找我了。当你醒来什么都不说，只是起身离开的时候。我还以为是我吓到你了。</p><p>Ahhhh, you';ve found your way back to me. When you awoke you just up and left. I thought perhaps I';d frightened you.</p><p>是的。时机已到。挥舞者，现在是你和梦之钉觉醒的时刻了。 你收集的精华……那依旧遗存在古老王国中的希望。是纯粹的潜力！你要引导它们，将它们注入到梦之钉中。 将它举起，挥舞者！觉醒吧！ </p><p>Yes. The time has come. The Dream Nail... And you as well, Wielder. It is time for you both to awaken. The Essence you have collected...The hope that still remains in this old Kingdom. Pure potential! Let it course through you and into the Dream Nail. Hold it aloft, Wielder! Awaken!</p><p>啊哈，啊哈，是的…… 现在所有的梦境在你面前都无处可藏了。你可以窥视那些最黑暗的地方……只要找到正确的缝隙。 挥舞者，你会用这样伟大的力量做什么？你会去探求谁的记忆？ 啊，如你的期望一般，我践行了诺言。愿我部落的罪行能够尽快消散，就像这个王国剩余的部分一样……</p><p>Ahhhh, ah ha, yes... No dream can hide itself from you now. You can peer into the darkest places... You just need to find the right crack. What will you do with such a power, Wielder? Whose memories will you hunt down? Hah. Do as you wish, my promise is fulfilled. May the crimes of my tribe be washed away in time, like the rest of this Kingdom';s...</p><p>虽然你可能会陨落，但你的意志会指引你前行。 梦境是无止境的，而王国不是。 你只要伸出手就能得到……这份能够将沉睡的世界唤醒的力量。</p><p>Though you may fall, your will shall carry you forward. A dream is endless, but a Kingdom is not. The power to wake this world from its slumber... you only need to reach out and take it.</p><p>那些守梦者……他们使用了仅存的一点力量到达外界，把你拖进了这个属于他们的隐藏国度。 但现在你有梦之钉，这是件不可思议的宝物……它可以切开分隔梦境与现实的屏障。即使是守梦者，在这件武器面前也无处遁形。 虽然我必须承认，随着时间推移，这把神圣的刀刃已经变钝了。但也许我们可以让它重新拥有力量。你只需要给我精华。 精华……是构成梦境的珍贵光之碎片。不管你在哪里找到它，把它们收集起来带给我。如果我们有足够的精华，就可以一起创造奇迹。 挥舞者，出去探索世界吧。寻找残留的精华。 收集到 100 精华后再回到我这儿。我会教你更多东西……</p><p>Those figures, those Dreamers... they reached out with what little power they still have and dragged you into that hidden place. But that talisman you now wield, the Dream Nail... it can cut through the veil that separates the waking world from our dreams. Even the Dreamers themselves can not hide from such a weapon. Though I must admit, that sacred blade has dulled over time. Together perhaps, we can restore its power. You only have to bring me Essence. Essence... the precious fragments of light that dreams are made of. Collect it wherever you find it, and bring it to me. Once we have enough, we can work wonders together. Go out into the world, Wielder. Hunt down the Essence that lingers there! Collect 100 Essence and return to me. I will teach you more...</p><p>啊，挥舞者，你回来了。让我看看梦之钉……</p><p>Ah, Wielder, you';ve returned. Let me have a look at the Dream Nail...</p><p>嗯，你这么快就收集到 100 精华了！是不是感觉好像你天生就会做这个？ 难怪守梦者想把你埋葬在那古旧的梦境中。也许你的存在让他们感到畏惧？又或许身为囚徒的他们希望与你合作？ 无论如何，你都还有很长的路要走。这个旧饰品就当我给你的鼓励了，当你收集到 200 精华时再到我这里。</p><p>Hmm, already you';ve collected 100 Essence. Quick work! Things come naturally to you, don';t they? No wonder the Dreamers tried to bury you in that old dream. Perhaps you frighten them? Or perhaps, being prisoners themselves, they desired your company? In any case, you still have a long way to go. Take this old trinket as encouragement from me, and return when you have collected 200 Essence.</p><p>啊。你的梦之钉已经拥有超过 200 精华了。你能收集这么多，说明你是有天赋的。 你看到外面那个巨大的门了吗？很久以前我的部落就将其关闭，并禁止打开它。 啊，但据我所知，我是我们部落唯一一个还活着的了。所以我不必为打破部落的规矩感到自责。为了证明我对你的信任，我会打开那扇门。</p><p>Ahhh... Your Dream Nail holds over 200 Essence. You';re proving your talent in its collection. Have you seen that great door just outside? My tribe closed it long ago and forbade its opening. Ah, but as far as I can tell I';m the only member of my tribe still breathing. That means I needn';t feel bad about breaking a taboo. As proof of my belief in you, I';ll open the door.</p><p>所以你已经收集到 300 精华了……是的，我看到这个古老王国的梦境开始依附于你。 你确实给我枯燥的工作添加了一些乐趣。在这个地方当守护者可没什么意思。 我不应该抱怨的。照料这些墓碑，引导挥舞者……这都是为了忏悔昔日的罪行。某些事情必须保持平衡。 为了奖励你的努力，拿走这个小饰品吧，等你收集到 500 精华后再回来，继续不懈努力！</p><p>So you';ve collected 300 Essence... Yes, I can see the dreams of this old Kingdom are starting to cling to you. Very encouraging. You';ve certainly made my dreary work here more exciting. Being the caretaker of this place hasn';t been very stimulating. I shouldn';t complain though. Tending these graves and guiding the Wielder... all penitence for old crimes. Certain things have to be kept in balance. Take this trinket for your efforts, and return with 500 Essence. Keep up your tireless work!</p><p>啊~ 已经收集到 500 精华了。在收集精华这件事上你真是个行家。做得好！做得好！我有一个小奖励要给你。 这是从我最珍贵的回忆中挑选出来的，这个护符会让你与梦之钉更加契合。你很快就能知道王国所有的秘密了！ 拿走它吧，收集到 700 精华再回来。我还有很多礼物要给你……</p><p>Ahhhhh. 500 Essence. You';re a master in the making. Well done! Well done! I';ve a small reward for you. Plucked from one of my most precious memories, this Charm will bring you and the Dream Nail closer together still. The secrets of this kingdom won';t be able to hide from you any longer! Take it, and return once you have collected 700 Essence. More gifts await you...</p><p>所以你已经收集到 700 精华了。我不知道那些守梦者是否在你收集的时候观察着你，甚至旧王都可能注意到了你。 他们的那个封印，尽管不会有什么希望，但却很高明。不知道他们觉得封印能持续多久？ 我并不是轻视他们。只是光阴荏苒，往事一再重复，我对这些已经麻木了。 带走这份礼物，继续收集精华。当你收集到 900 精华后再来拜访我。</p><p>So, you already have 700 Essence. I wonder whether the Dreamers are watching you as you work. Or even that old King. That lock of theirs. Desperate, but very clever. I wonder how long they thought it could hold? I hold no disdain for them though. As history goes on I grow numb to its repetitions. Take your gift and continue collecting Essence. Once you have 900, come back and visit me.</p><p>梦之钉内蕴含着 900 精华了！这个古老的王国竟然隐藏了这么多梦境，你有没有感到惊讶？ 是的，你已经开始发现了，那些曾经去过的梦境与我们之间，仅仅隔着一层窗户纸。是时候学习如何打通去往那些地方的道路了！ 挥舞者，握紧你的梦之钉，想象有一扇大门在你的面前开启！</p><p>The Dream Nail now holds 900 Essence within its core! Are you surprised that this old kingdom has so many hidden dreams? Yes, you';re starting to see them. The connections between us and the dreams we leave behind, like prints in the dust. The time has come for you to learn how to revisit the places connected to you! Hold the Dream Nail tight, wielder, and imagine a great gate opening before you!</p><p>梦之钉散发着光辉……它蕴含着超过 1200 精华。看着它，我能感受到那么多的记忆也在凝视着我。那么多祈求被铭记的往事。  没有虫子可以永远活着，所以我们希望让活着的人记住我们。 将事物留存于心上，它就随你继续活着。但如果忘记了它，它就被永久封存。那才是真正的死亡。 哈，他们是那么讲的，说的够多了。拿走这个纪念品，收集到 1500 精华之后再回到我这儿，去吧！</p><p>The Dream Nail glows bright... It holds over 1200 Essence. Looking into it I can see so many memories peering back at me. So many asking to be remembered. None of us can live forever, and so we ask those who survive to remember us. Hold something in your mind and it lives on with you, but forget it and you seal it away forever. That is the only death that matters. Huh, so they say! Enough of that though. Take this relic and come back to me with 1500 Essence. Go!</p><p>是的，我想是这样。你已经收集了超过 1500 精华了。梦之钉已经……快要完全觉醒了！ 继续你的旅程吧，挥舞者。我不知道是什么指引着你，也不知道你前进的动力是什么。收集了 1800 精华之后再回来吧，我会在这等着的。 带走这个礼物吧，它拥有帮助你收集精华的力量！</p><p>Yes, I thought so. More than 1500 Essence. The Dream Nail is close... so close to fully awakening! Continue on your path, Wielder. I know not what guides you, nor what will it is that drives you forward. Once you have collected 1800 Essence though, I will be here waiting. Take this gift, may it grant you strength and help you to collect the Essence of this world!</p><p>(梦语)</p><p>你在做什么，探查我它那老到布满灰尘的内心？相信我，里面没有藏着什么有价值的东西。 但如果你继续用梦之钉窥探周围的世界……隐藏起来的事物将无处遁形。</p><p>What are you doing, poking about in a dusty old mind like mine? Nothing of worth is hidden inside, believe me. But keep probing the world around you with your Dream Nail... Hidden things will reveal themselves.</p><p>他们带给你的命运是多么残酷。 将你抛在了肉体与灵魂的间隙之中。 你会接受他们的裁决，逐渐枯朽殆尽吗？ 又或者你会举起手中的武器，从这个被遗忘的悲伤梦境中开辟出你的道路？</p><p>What a terrible fate they';ve visited upon you. To cast you away into this space between body and soul. Will you accept their judgement and fade slowly away?
