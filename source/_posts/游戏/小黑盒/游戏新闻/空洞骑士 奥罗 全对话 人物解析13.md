<p>无论代价是什么…我都会证明自己！" --------奥罗</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="771" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/03/19/f20b6f0b48e90003f315d7b7dc47b55f/thumb.jpeg" data-width="1260" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>骨钉大师奥罗，无疑是斯莱的三个徒弟中技艺最差的（斯莱：奥罗，你把骨钉挥得跟棒槌似的），也正因如此，他是如此渴望证明自己，就连他居住的小屋边上的训练人偶的梦语都是"更强...兄弟..."。他为什么要隐居到国王边境，三兄弟为何决裂，斯莱口中的埃斯米是谁，虽然这一切非常模糊，但我们仍然能从对话中感受到，三兄弟原先的友谊之深，以及后来发生的事是多么悲伤和绝望，以至于师徒四人从此决裂，各自走自己的路。其实奥罗可能是每个奋斗着的普通人的真实写照，资质不够，唯有努力才能证明自己。由于一些不可控的因素的，一些悲剧发生了，那么可能这一生也随着变得黯淡了，毕竟我们没有那么大的能力去改变这一切，能做的，只有像奥罗一样，隐居边境，静静等待时间来磨平这一切罢了。愿你的努力终将得到回报，希望你最终能证明自己，奥罗如此，所有人也是如此。</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="705" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/03/19/14a270f0c34964acbba0feb1b5f46143/thumb.jpeg" data-width="1248" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>对话</p><p>哇，你是怎么在世界尽头找到我的？</p><p>Hmph. How did you find me, down here where the world ends?</p><p>没错，我知道你为何而来。你想学传说中骨钉大师奥罗的骨钉之技。</p><p>Yes, I know why you're here. You came to learn a Nail Art from the legendary Nailmaster Oro.</p><p>伟大骨钉贤者规定我必须将自己的本领传授给值得教授的人。</p><p>It is the law of the Great Nailsage that I must pass down my teachings to those who are worthy.</p><p>但没人规定我要无偿传授。如果你真的想当我的徒弟，那就用吉欧来证明吧!</p><p>But no law forbids me from exacting a payment for my time. If you truly wish to train under me, prove it with Geo!</p><p>（拒绝）</p><p>“不分享力量的人永远学不到的东西。”这是我师父说过的话。</p><p>"Those who do not share their strength will never learn."My master used to say that.</p><p>用“吉欧”来代替“力量"就是我的观点了。滚开!</p><p>Substitute "Geo" for "strength" and you'll have my own views on the matter. Begone!</p><p>（接受）</p><p>怎么样，旅行者？打算付账吗，还是准备懵懵懂懂地离开？</p><p>So what will it be, traveller? Will you pay the price asked of you, or will you leave this place unenlightened?</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="700" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/03/19/a70f6ca358c2e62ed8e37c4e46416474/thumb.jpeg" data-width="1244" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>（学习）</p><p>啊，我要承认你是个杰出的徒弟。</p><p>Hmph. I must say, you are an excellent pupil.</p><p>你对骨钉的驾驭，对变强的渴望……让我想起了我的兄弟们。</p><p>Your skill with the nail, and your thirst to become stronger... you remind me of my brothers.</p><p>我已经对你倾囊相授。继续去探索世界吧，旅行者。别再打扰我。</p><p>I have taught you all I can. Go back into the world, traveller, and leave me to my solitude.</p><p>（关于马托）</p><p>这骨钉之技……你接受过我兄弟马托的指导。</p><p>That Nail Art... you have been training with my brother Mato.</p><p>他对忠诚和荣誉有着古怪的念头。当他的徒弟肯定很艰难很不愉快。</p><p>He has strange notions of loyalty and honour. I'm sure he was difficult and unpleasant to train under.</p><p>他对你提到过我吗？提到的话跟他说要耐心点。得再等一段时间我才能把想要的东西给他。</p><p>Did he mention my name to you? If he does, tell him to be patient. He'll be waiting a while longer before I give him what he wants.</p><p>（关于席奥）</p><p>看来我的兄弟席奥传授过你骨钉之技。你真是走运。</p><p>I see you've been taught a Nail Art by my brother Sheo. How fortunate of you.</p><p>没错，他是伟大的骨钉贤者最得意的徒弟，马托和我都难以望其项背。我的兄弟和师父都很喜欢他。</p><p>Yes, he was the mightiest of the Great Nailsage's pupils, far outshining Mato and myself. My brother and my master loved him very much.</p><p>他总是很冷淡，但是…… 人不坏。我们最后一次说话的时候，他说他要去学全新的技艺。不知道他有没有达成目标。</p><p>He was always aloof, but... not unkind. When we last spoke, he said he was going to teach himself something completely new. I wonder if he achieved that goal.</p><p>（关于斯莱）</p><p>你身上的护符……</p><p>看来伟大的骨钉贤者承认你的骨钉技艺已经炉火纯青，可以获得最高尊荣。</p><p>That charm you bear... It seems the Great Nailsage has deemed you skilled enough in the Nail Arts to earn his highest esteem.</p><p>我不嫉妒他对你的喜爱，这是你亲手挣来的。</p><p>I do not begrudge you his love. You have earned it.</p><p>也许我对你的帮助也为我自己赢得了荣耀……哪怕只有一点点。</p><p>And perhaps by helping you along your path, I have redeemed myself... if only a little.</p><p>虽然没被邀请，但你还是回来了。</p><p>You've returned, though you were not invited.</p><p>作为我的徒弟，我有义务为你提供栖身之所，但休息之后就立刻动身吧。</p><p>As my pupil, it is lawful that I allow you rest and shelter, but do not stay longer than you need to.</p><p>必须休息的话就在这里休息，但充分休息之后就马上离开。</p><p>Rest here if you must, but do not stay longer than you need to.</p><p>（送花）</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="701" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/03/19/2a72403bef4518082f36816801aac425/thumb.jpeg" data-width="1247" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>这是什么？你觉得学生在老师家里扔垃圾像话吗？还是说你觉得这朵俗气的花可以当做礼物？</p><p>What is this?Do you think it becoming for a pupil to drop rubbish in their master's home?Or is this gaudy flower your idea of a gift?</p><p>这东西对我毫无用处。把它带走，否则我就把它扔到外面喂害虫。</p><p>I've no use for such things.Take it with you,or else I'll toss it outside to be caten by vermin.</p><p>梦语</p><p>马托兄弟，孤独地生活在世界顶端的你……还把我看作敌人么？</p><p>Brother Mato, in your lonely home at the top of the world... do you still think of me as an enemy?</p><p>如果有一天你愿意倾听……我会告诉你为什么我选择这条道路。</p><p>In the future, if you will listen... I will tell you why I chose to tread this path.</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="326" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/03/19/4b7b7f122976df9bb289753a314a979a/thumb.jpeg" data-width="580" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>神居梦语：</p><p>无论代价是什么…我都会证明自己！</p><p>Whatever it takes... I'll prove myself!</p><p>我的路只有我自己走…</p><p>My path is mine alone...</p><p><a href="heybox://open_subject" target="_blank">#steam游戏#</a>   <a href="heybox://open_subject" target="_blank">#单机游戏#</a>  <a href="heybox://open_subject" target="_blank">#空洞骑士#</a> <br></p>
