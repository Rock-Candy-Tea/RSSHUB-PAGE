<blockquote><p>来源：游侠网</p></blockquote><p data-from-paste="1">近日，Capcom公布了旗下共斗游戏《怪物猎人崛起》的游戏幕后配乐制作花絮，玩家们可以观看到制作团队给大家创作留下深刻印象的音乐时的珍贵画面。</p><p><img data-gameid="900007596" src="https://cdn.max-c.com/pc_game/head/b3d52963939166b84c16e1469657b05b" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>从此次花絮影像来看，配音团队很好地利用了日本传统音乐的长处，还让人声演唱、管弦乐以及多种乐器配乐相互交融，达到了非常理想的效果。</p><p>《怪物猎人崛起》本作再次为狩猎掀起全新风潮，可无拘无束尽情跃动的动作，可随心所欲四下驰骋的原野。能带来未知兴奋与惊喜的全新怪物。前所未有的狩猎体验正等待着猎人们。该作采用RE引擎打造，最多支持四人合作。</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="null" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2021/07/01/bcdd95cef46643ae91796db4083304ba1625097600.png" data-width="null" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="null" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2021/07/01/f50e7db2c7624f968ded602aa6ff95711625097600.png" data-width="null" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="null" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2021/07/01/3ecb0f5f63044a1bb3801060643e33231625097601.jpeg" data-width="null" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p id="No_607133"><b>配乐制作花絮视频截图一览：</b></p><p><br></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="null" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2021/07/01/de3b3340ed1b47c597afa941a06388a11625097601.png" data-width="null" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="null" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2021/07/01/0a20ea854ad14835b9aba568502664271625097602.png" data-width="null" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="null" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2021/07/01/29d7a2c6de234f59a3ca54b9bd5b9a4a1625097602.png" data-width="null" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="null" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2021/07/01/437275e868804b0bb2c60c9f2a92f6671625097603.png" data-width="null" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p id="No_607133"><br></p>
