This shape-shifting race appears to have been on Earth for many years, infiltrating key positions of power and influence.  The location of Secret Invasion in the wider Marvel Cinematic Universe is currently unknown, but many fans believe that the film will be set during the so-called “surge”, the five-year time gap between Avengers: Infinity War and Avengers: Endgame.
