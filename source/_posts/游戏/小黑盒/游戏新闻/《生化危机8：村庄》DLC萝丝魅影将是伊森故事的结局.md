AT conversations with the Japanese division of IGN, Kento Kinoshita – the head of Capcom – said that the Shadows of Rose DLC for Resident Evil: Village will be the final part of the story about the family of Ethan Winters – the protagonist of Resident Evil 7 and Resident Evil: Village.
However, Capcom representatives did not answer the question of what will happen in Resident Evil next.  As they said, they cannot share the company’s plans for the series.
The Shadows of Rose DLC, starring Ethan Winters’ daughter and taking place after the events of the main game, releases October 28 alongside the release of the Gold Edition of Resident Evil: Village.
