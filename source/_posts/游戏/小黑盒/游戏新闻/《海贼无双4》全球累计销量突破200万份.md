Bandai Namco has announced that One Piece: Pirate Warriors 4 has surpassed two million units in digital sales and worldwide shipments. The publisher announced the milestone on Twitter on the game’s official account.
According to the translated tweet by Bandai Namco, One Piece: Pirate Warriors 4 is fastest game in the Pirate Warriors franchise to reach the milestone.
One Piece: Pirate Warriors 4 was released back in 2020. The beat ’em up game was developed by Koei Tecmo, and is available on PC, PS4, Xbox One, and Nintendo Switch.
The game features the largest playable roster of its franchise, with more than 40 playable characters in its roster. It also has the Dramatic Log that can be used by players to experience specific episodes from the series.
Playable characters in One Piece: Pirate Warriors 4 incclude Monkey D. Luffy, Rorona Zoro, and the rest of the Strawhat Pirates, as well as other characters like Boa Hancock, Jinbe, and Bartolomeo, among others.
