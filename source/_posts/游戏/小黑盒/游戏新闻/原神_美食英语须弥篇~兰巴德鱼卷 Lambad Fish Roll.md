It is said that Lambad once adventured far and wide in his youth. Everywhere he went, he would taste the grilled fish at local taverns. This dish was not a signature food in Sumeru, and Lambad thought it was a pity, so he invented his own fish roll. According to the <b>gourmets </b>who often dine at his tavern, the tales of his adventures aren't necessarily true, but his fish roll is undoubtedly delicious.</p><p><b>gourmet/ˈɡʊrmeɪ/n.美食家；美食家</b></p><p>略带花香的烤鱼。据说兰巴德大叔在年轻的时候，曾四处冒险，吃遍了各地的烤鱼。他遗憾于须弥没有代表性的烤鱼，亲自创作了这道菜肴。酒馆内的<b>老饕</b>们一致认为，冒险故事的真实性有待考虑，但鱼卷的美味是毋庸置疑的。</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="236" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2022/09/16/47b7f451edcb432db07dd2bcd7cac8041663346794.png" data-width="410" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><h4 class="img-desc">奇怪的兰巴德鱼卷</h4><p>A grilled fish dish with a faint floral scent. Oh dearie me. The <b>rank </b>smell of fish and the stench of charred meat are the most telling signs of your terrible skills. Take the dish away before Lambad sees it, or he might make you pay twice as much for your next order.</p><p><b>rank/ræŋk/adj.难闻的，恶臭的（熟词生义）</b></p><p>略带花香的烤鱼。糟了，与腥气纠缠的焦味已经暴露了厨艺的不精。如果不想在下次点单的时候被兰巴德大叔打二十折的折扣，最好不要让他看到…</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="234" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2022/09/16/bd4d254cbe4d40e3ba877f9f25ad3b801663346797.png" data-width="409" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><h4 class="img-desc">美味的兰巴德鱼卷</h4><p>A grilled fish dish with a faint floral scent.
