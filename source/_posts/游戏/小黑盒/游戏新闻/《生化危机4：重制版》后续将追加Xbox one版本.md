According to Idle Sloth, the Xbox Series X version of Resident Evil 4 Remake |  S and Xbox One appeared in list Amazon’s English division, thus confirming that Capcom’s survival horror will also appear on older Microsoft consoles.
Resident Evil 4 Remake was originally conceived as an exclusive project for PC and current generation consoles.  However, during Tokyo Game Show 2022, Capcom revealed that a remake of Leon’s misadventures will also be coming to PS4.  In that sense, the Xbox One version was desirable, but not entirely obvious.  In short, there was no official confirmation, which has now appeared, albeit indirectly, from Amazon UK.



As a reminder, Resident Evil 4 Remake will be available from March 24th.  In October, Capcom will share new details about this highly anticipated remastered edition at a special event called the Resident Evil Showcase, with more details on the date and time to be revealed in the coming days.</p>
