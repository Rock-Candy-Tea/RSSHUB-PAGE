In its coverage for Gotham Knights as part of its IGN First series, the map for Gotham Knights inadvertently got revealed. Check out the map below.
The map shows five distinct boroughs in the Gotham City envisioned for Gotham Knights: North Gotham, New Gotham, Downtown Gotham, Historic Gotham, and Lower Gotham.
IGN goes deeper through the history of Gotham City to help further embellish the map, from the arrival of five families from Europe, all the way through Gotham City’s founding, and how the five zones each have inherited the architectural stylings of one of the five families.
In terms of affecting gameplay, the revealed map doesn’t really show much aside from a few icons. Unfortunately, there aren’t really any details on what these icons might mean.
A recent trailer for Gotham Knights showcased the gameplay for Red Hood. Real name Jason Todd, Red Hood’s gameplay revolves around using his dual handguns (with non-lethal ammo), and mystical powers like the Mystic Leap.
Gotham Knights is coming to PC, PS5, and Xbox Series X/S on October 25.
