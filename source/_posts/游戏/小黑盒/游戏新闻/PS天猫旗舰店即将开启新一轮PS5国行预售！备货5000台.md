<p>临近圣诞节和元旦，PlayStation天猫旗舰店天猫官方旗舰店近日开启了新一轮的PS5国行预售，本轮预售PS5备货共5000台，光驱版PS5单机备货1000台。</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="800" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2021/12/22/bf3bc1ba805146448f70780dc55b20fe1640138523.jpg" data-width="1200" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>根据官方标价，光驱版PS5单机套餐售价3899元，光驱版PS5双手柄（黑+白）套餐售价4428元，备货共计5000台。本轮双旦的预售时间将从12月25日早上10点击开始，光驱版PS5单机备货1000台，光驱版PS5双手柄（黑+白）套餐备货4000台，抢购完成后45天内发货。</p><p>预售地址：<a href="https://detail.tmall.com/item.htm?spm=a220m.1000862.1000725.17.34da7e043CFBcr&id=642437628619&areaId=310100&is_b=1&cat_id=2&rn=a77946b78ed992dae07e8795cccfa0d1&skuId=4634625721096" target="_blank">点击前往>>></a></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="784" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2021/12/22/33768e6897b14226b2e64f1929cb11041640138523.png" data-width="1422" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="418" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2021/12/22/3a3e3dcd4d3a434eb0af21633166c7091640138524.jpg" data-width="700" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><blockquote><p>来源：游侠网</p></blockquote>
