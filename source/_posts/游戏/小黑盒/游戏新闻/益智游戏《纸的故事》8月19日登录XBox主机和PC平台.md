developer Open House Games announced. The PlayStation 5 and Switch versions will follow later in 2022. 
The original A Tale of Paper is currently available for PlayStation 4. The Refolded edition adds three new prequel chapters starring a new character with its own unique transforming abilities.
Here is an overview of the game, via Digerati:
About
A Tale of Paper: Refolded is a puzzle platformer that tells the story of Line, a magical character made of paper who can use origami to change its shape. Transform into a frog, a rocket, a bird and more as Line embarks on an emotional journey to fulfill the dream of its creator.
