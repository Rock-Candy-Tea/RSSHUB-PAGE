Aliens: Fireteam Elite might not be the most massive multiplayer game around, but it does seem to be intent to keep its existing player base happy with regular new content drops. Developer Cold Iron Studios has now launched the fourth season for the co-op shooter, and released a launch trailer to commemorate the same. Check it out below.
Season 4: Prestige adds quite a bit to Aliens: Fireteam Elite. As its name suggests, it adds a new Prestige system to the game that allows you to go up a hundred Prestige ranks after hitting level 20. There’s also a new tower defense-style game mode called Restock Turrets for players to dive into.
In addition to that, Season 4 brings Challenge Cards to all modes in the game, the new Fabrication Reprocessor, and four new weapons.
Aliens: Fireteam Elite is available on PS5, Xbox Series X/S, PS4, Xbox One, and PC. It’s also getting a major new update with the Pathogen expansion on August 30.
