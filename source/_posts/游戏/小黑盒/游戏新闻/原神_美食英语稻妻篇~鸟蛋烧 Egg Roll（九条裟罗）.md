<p><b>配方材料：</b>鸟蛋*1、糖*1</p><p><b>星级：</b>★</p><p><b>食谱获取方法：</b>稻妻志村屋购买</p><p><br></p><p>菜名简单，营养丰富。倒是可以看看九条裟罗的假名。九条 くじょう Kujou，裟羅 さら Sara。 日语中“r"读作”l"，否则Sara读起来像个蒙德名字。貌似猎鹿人的老板就叫Sara。</p><p><br></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="250" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2022/02/07/5b3ff84829ec455592b5e412372704551644198303.png" data-width="411" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><h4 class="img-desc">鸟蛋烧</h4><p>A fried egg dish. Beat eggs well, season and fry them slowly in a <b>wok</b>, roll up into a fixed shape. Pour our egg liquid in again and continue until it has all reached a certain thickness, then get ready to <b>plate up</b>. This is an ordinary yet welcoming Inazuman delicacy.</p><p><b>wok  /wɑːk/ n. 锅（源自广东话）；炒菜锅</b></p><p><b>plate up 摆盘</b></p><p>煎制而成的蛋料理。将搅拌均匀的蛋液先行调味，倒入锅中小火慢煎，卷至一边定型，再次倒入蛋液继续…重复至一定厚度，便可摆盘作为成品。是稻妻地区平凡而又亲切的美味。</p><p><br></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="236" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2022/02/07/d157fa6c28224246b4b8da8ae07ea0f61644198304.png" data-width="411" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><h4 class="img-desc">奇怪的鸟蛋烧</h4><p>A fried egg dish. It looks way too soft... and what do you know, it <b>comes apart </b>the instant it so much as touches chopsticks. Might as well use a spoon at this point.</p><p><b>come apart 破碎；崩溃；瓦解</b></p><p>煎制而成的蛋料理。看起来过于软嫩，用筷子轻轻一碰就会松散开来，或许用勺子挖着吃会更方便。</p><p><br></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="236" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2022/02/07/dc79fc9ca3c44014a8b9e6fadbe965441644198304.png" data-width="411" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><h4 class="img-desc">美味的鸟蛋烧</h4><p>A fried egg dish. The shape of the dish as a whole, the golden color, the <b>fluffy </b>mouthfeel... a small piece of this may not fill your stomach, but it will fill you with warmth. Not to mention that sweetness that you can almost <b>reach out </b>and touch — simply <b>irresistible</b>.</p><p><b>fluffy /ˈflʌfi/ adj.（食物等）松软的，透气的；轻软状的</b></p><p><b>reach out vt. 伸出</b></p><p><b>irresistible /ˌɪrɪˈzɪstəbl/  adj. 无法抗拒的，富有诱惑力的</b></p><p>煎制而成的蛋料理。规整的造型、金黄的色泽、松软的口感…小小一块，虽不及填饱肚子，却足以品出温馨。这一份唾手可得的香甜，惹人迷恋。</p><p><br></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="237" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2022/02/07/632512d27c3b46fab745e3248792787a1644198304.png" data-width="409" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><h4 class="img-desc">「永恒的信仰」</h4><p>Kujou Sara's specialty. Forget calling it a dish, this is really more like a snack. The sweet, fluffy flavor really doesn't seem to suit Sara's own <b>preferences</b>. Wait a moment. That symbol on the snack — does this have to do with a certain other person's tastes instead?</p><p><b>preference /ˈprefrəns/ n. 偏爱，偏好</b></p><p>九条裟罗的特殊料理。与其说成菜肴，不如说是点心。甜蜜软绵的口味似乎与裟罗本人的喜好不大相同。等等，这上面的印记，莫非是与【那位大人】有关。</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="1815" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2022/02/07/66a8d750f30745cc8dae5940e1d990b81644198305.jpg" data-width="1060" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="1962" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2022/02/07/f9c5f132655543a686f57c80ff1bbbd11644198305.jpg" data-width="1170" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="1781" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2022/02/07/dcc4d9b41ac94a66b03bb15bfb4593e71644198306.png" data-width="1580" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>据说九条是女角色身高最高，以及……最大的。<a href="heybox://open_subject" target="_blank">#原神2.4创作激励计划#</a></p>
