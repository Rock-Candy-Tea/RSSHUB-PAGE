<blockquote><p>来源：gematsu</p><p><a href="https://www.gematsu.com/2022/08/life-is-strange-arcadia-bay-collection-for-switch-launches-september-27" target="_blank">点击此处查看原文>>></a></p></blockquote><p>《奇异人生: 阿卡迪亚湾合集》将于9月27日上线Switch<br></p><p>发行商Square Enix和开发商DON'T NOD及Deck Nine Games将于9月27日于Switch发布《奇异人生：阿卡迪亚湾合集》。<br></p><p>该合集包括《奇异人生》重制版和前传《奇异人生：暴风前夕》重制版，将同时提供实体和数字版本。然而，实体版将只包括《奇异人生》重制版，《奇异人生：暴风前夕》重制版将以下载代码的形式发放。<br></p><p>《奇异人生》重制版已于2月1日登录PS,xbox以及PC平台。</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="500" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/web/2022/08/17/6c397be5963c6d974475b9fb8e54aaad/thumb.jpeg" data-width="839" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="original-news">Publisher Square Enix and developers DON’T NOD and Deck Nine Games will release Life is Strange: Arcadia Bay Collection for Switch on September 27, the companies announced. 
The collection, which includes both Life is Strange Remastered and Life is Strange: Before the Storm Remastered, will be available both physically and digitally. However, the physical version will only include Life is Strange Remastered on the cart, while Life is Strange: Before the Storm Remastered is included as a download code.
The same collection launched as Life is Strange: Remastered Collection for PlayStation 4, Xbox One, PC via Steam (1, Before the Storm), and Stadia on February 1.
