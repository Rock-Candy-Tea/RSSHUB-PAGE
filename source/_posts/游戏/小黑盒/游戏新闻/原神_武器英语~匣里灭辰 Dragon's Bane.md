In the end, both the legend and the dragon-slaying polearm were lost.</p><p>英雄已然成为了街巷间的传说，灭龙的长枪也随着传说失落，</p><p><br></p><p>Freed at last from the shackles of its past, it displayed the full force of its <b>ferocity </b>once and for all, as if to mark the dragon's return to the ocean where it belonged.</p><p><b>ferocity/fəˈrɑːsəti/n.凶猛；残忍；暴行</b></p><p>一朝内失去了来自过去的束缚，便如龙归大海一般尽显锋芒。</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="2480" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2022/08/18/0e3c0a6d186c4cb28edd5ca9a5aac8691660810240.png" data-width="1754" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><a href="heybox://open_subject" target="_blank">#原神2.8版本创作者征集活动#</a><br></p>
