December will mark two years since the release of Cyberpunk 2077, and while we’ve only received patches and updates so far that haven’t brought much additional content that could extend the game for many hours, there are still a lot of players entering the game on a daily basis.  The popularity of Cyberpunk has increased significantly in recent times, as evidenced by statistics from the Steam database, as well as a message from the developers from CD Projekt RED.
Every day of this week, Night City is visited by 1 million players, both new and returning.  We wanted to take this opportunity to thank you for being with us and playing the game.  Thank you, chumps!
At the same time, it is worth noting that the game returned to the group of the most popular single-player games on Steam, ahead of even Elden Ring.
