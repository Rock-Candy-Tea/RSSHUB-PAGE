Well, after the first free update that added Daisy and Shy Guy, the second, due out later this month, has confirmed Nintendo and Next Level Games’ post-release plans for its sports title.
This upcoming update (for which we currently lack an exact launch date) will add two new characters: Pauline and Diddy Kong.  In the announcement trailer, which you can find below, we’re told that Pauline is a “physical powerhouse” with quick movements and strong tackles, while Diddy Kong is a “midfielder” with great technique and passing skills.
But this pair won’t be all of the new content the game is getting in this update, as a new stadium is arriving (dubbed Planetoid), as is a new gear set (the Barrel set).
