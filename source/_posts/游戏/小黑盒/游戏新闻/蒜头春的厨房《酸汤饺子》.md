<p>大家好我是蒜头春。</p><p>前两天吃饺子时候看到有hxd提到了酸汤饺子，勾起了我大学时候的一些回忆。</p><p>今天给hxd们整个简单至极的酸汤饺子，预祝诸位生活顺利。</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="4344" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2021/08/11/99200166d03449ce1a7f94cc0f346e52/thumb.jpeg" data-width="5792" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>首先我们调制一个简单的酸汤底子。<br></p><p>两勺醋</p><p>一勺酱油</p><p>三勺油辣子</p><p>适量的盐和胡椒粉。</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="4344" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2021/08/11/f86e2b16c773f11c19975121cecdfd06/thumb.jpeg" data-width="5792" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>这里的醋如果有条件可以换成呛醋。（呛醋：将醋与香料同煮，煮开后自然晾凉，滤出料渣即可）汤底里可以加虾皮、紫菜等提鲜。</p><p>汤底准备好后开始煮饺子。</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="4344" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2021/08/11/f545f28289cadac650fba8a0c6163966/thumb.jpeg" data-width="5792" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>将包好的饺子开水下锅，煮至沸腾后下一碗冷水，再次煮至开锅。总共点三次水即可。<br></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="225" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2021/08/11/f11708fbc6ea66f0aa692a33aeb921b9.gif" data-width="401" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>将滚烫的饺子汤冲入酸汤汤底。</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="459" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2021/08/11/089aa2abfdc7ba0b85dbf0ab4d5b022c.gif" data-width="295" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>将煮熟的饺子捞进去。</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="289" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2021/08/11/f0316c616abdb7f67d57db80e6e7e7bb.gif" data-width="392" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>一碗酸辣开胃的酸汤水饺就做好了。</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="4344" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2021/08/11/0607305f8ae12bbfee1db12c8c6cc955/thumb.jpeg" data-width="5792" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>一碗饺子，一段无法回去的青春。<br></p><p><a href="heybox://open_subject" target="_blank">#美食#</a> <br></p><p><a href="heybox://open_subject" target="_blank">#黑盒美食#</a> <br></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="419" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2021/08/11/125be988e49713e5d8e7f49fd952c8cd.gif" data-width="960" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
