The Saints Row reboot is not far away from launch, and ahead of its release, developer Volition and publisher Deep Silver are continuing to reveal more and more details and show more new gameplay. As part of that info rollout, we got a recent round of previews and hands-on impressions on the game from various outlets, courtesy of which we’ve got quite a new bit of gameplay footage to check out.
A new video, for instance, was recently uploaded by Game Informer, showcasing over 15 minutes of new gameplay footage, covering vehicular combat, regular combat, and various locations in the game’s open world, among other things. Meanwhile, IGN has also uploaded a video that shows the first 15 minutes of the game. Check out both videos below.
Recently, it was confirmed that while Saints Row will permit cross-gen multiplayer, cross-platform play across PlayStation and Xbox will not be supported. Read more on that through here.
Saints Row recently went gold, and launches on August 23 for PS5, Xbox Series X/S, PS4, Xbox One, PC, and Stadia. Check out the game’s minimum PC requirements through here.
