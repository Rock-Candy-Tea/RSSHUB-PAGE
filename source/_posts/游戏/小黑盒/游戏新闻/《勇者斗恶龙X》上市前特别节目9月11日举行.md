Pre-Launch Special!” live stream on September 11 at 20:00 JST, the company announced. You will be able to watch it on YouTube. 
The broadcast will feature the latest information on Dragon Quest X: Rise of the Five Tribes Offline, including first-time reveals, as well as live gameplay and more.
Presenters include host Jun Fukuyama (voice actor) and guests Kentaro Ito (Dasuton voice actor), Aya Endo (Maille voice actor), Mitsuki Saiga (Fostale voice actor), and Takuma Shiraishi (Dragon Quest X: Rise of the Five Tribes Offline producer).
