<p>在外人看来，多莉的生意似乎一直在<b>蒸蒸日上</b>，然而只有多莉自己清楚，她其实多次遇到过旁人<b>无法想象的瓶颈</b>。</p><p><b>蒸蒸日上on the up and up</b></p><p><b>无法想象的瓶颈unimaginable bottlenecks</b></p><p>From the perspective of others, Dori's business has always been <b>on the up and up</b>. However, only Dori herself knows how many <b>unimaginable bottlenecks </b>she's struggled through.</p><p>曾经有一段时间，来自须弥各地的订单<b>源源不断地</b>送到多莉的手上，但多莉却无法<b>按期</b>交付这么多的货物。</p><p><b>源源不断地keep flooding in</b></p><p><b>按期in a timely fashion</b></p><p>There was a time when orders from all over Sumeru just <b>kept flooding in </b>and Dori couldn't deliver such a giant number of orders <b>in a timely fashion</b>.</p><p>因为周边几条商道的货运量十分有限，难以承载如此规模的<b>大宗商品</b>运输。</p><p><b>大宗商品commodities</b></p><p>Unfortunately, the capacity of the usual merchant routes was too limited for the delivery of <b>commodities </b>in such a gigantic quantity.</p><p>商队的成员劝多莉回绝部分订单，这样依旧能凭借现有的商道维持稳定收益。假以时日，富甲一方应该不成问题。</p><p>Many members of Dori's merchant caravans suggested rejecting certain orders to dial down the scale of delivery and maintain a decent, stable profit — one that already seemed set to make them immensely wealthy, given time.</p><p>但多莉却做了一个非常冒险的举措：她一边接下订单，一边与妙论派展开合作，在公认的危险地区开辟新的商道。</p><p>But Dori took a very bold step: She accepted all the orders while starting cooperation with the Kshahrewar to establish new trade routes in areas that were known to be dangerous.</p><p>她将这些新商道开放给周边的商队，并借此成立商会、整合资源，终于突破了原先的瓶颈。</p><p>She opened the new routes up to nearby merchant caravans, took the opportunity to found a commerce guild and put the relevant resources to good use, and finally eliminated the bottleneck that her business was stuck in.</p><p>时至今日，整个须弥境内都能见到多莉新修建的商道，「桑歌玛哈巴依老爷」的名声也<b>越传越广</b>。</p><p><b>越传越广spread far and wide</b></p><p>Today, Dori's new, built-from-scratch merchant routes are seen all across Sumeru, and the good reputation of "Lord Sangemah Bay" has <b>spread far and wide</b>.</p><p>与之一同流传的，还有多莉当初对商队成员说的话： 「我不会放弃任何订单，那是<b>蠢蘑菇</b>才会做的事。」</p><p><b>蠢蘑菇toadstool</b></p><p>The words Dori said to her caravan members have also become common knowledge:</p><p>"I'm not some stupid <b>toadstool</b>. I don't cancel or reject any orders."</p><p>「我会赚到所有能赚的摩拉，要问为什么的话，因为摩拉就在那里。」</p><p>"Every Mora that can be earned, will be earned by me. Why? Well, because it's there, of course."</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="3831" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2022/09/18/41f515531c9b492a852ee3abcce44ac71663509618.png" data-width="3060" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><a href="heybox://open_subject" target="_blank">#原神3.0版本创作者征集活动#</a><br></p>
