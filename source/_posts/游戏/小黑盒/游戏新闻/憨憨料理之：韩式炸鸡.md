class="hb-emoji"></span></p><h2>part1:准备材料</h2><p>鸡腿</p><p>胡椒，盐</p><p>牛奶，油</p><p>淀粉，鸡蛋，芝麻</p><p>番茄酱，韩式辣酱，芝士片</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="620" class="lazy" data-height="620" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/02/02/1c27e31ee1d918fa9baba5425102936f/thumb.jpeg" data-width="828" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>拿一个盆，里面倒入牛奶和胡椒粉，加一点点盐腌制一下鸡肉</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="620" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/02/02/5d0f40de8baae57ec2285ba2f3f19f6f/thumb.jpeg" data-width="828" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="620" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/02/02/f1ad035df9a8ce2cc0e537bdc1dd04c0/thumb.jpeg" data-width="828" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>腌大概20分钟左右，倒出牛奶，加一个鸡蛋和淀粉，让肉挂上淀粉糊</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="1104" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/02/02/d8bc93f35c594a24fa7f1a450844c12f/thumb.jpeg" data-width="828" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>疯狂rua它，让鸡蛋和淀粉完全裹住每一块肉</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="1104" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/02/02/144ce7bb11ee3e1dae2a691192a79da7/thumb.jpeg" data-width="828" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>rua成这样差不多了，可以起锅烧油了<span data-emoji="cube_doge" class="hb-emoji"></span></p><h2>part3:炸鸡</h2><p>油温170度</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="620" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/02/02/b814319db59e8565b1155fb64637acfb/thumb.jpeg" data-width="828" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>鸡肉块裹一层淀粉</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="620" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/02/02/bf841e0fa6bd29fbe667cec441d61fc0/thumb.jpeg" data-width="828" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>淀粉就可以，不用加泡打粉</p><p>下油锅咯！<span data-emoji="cube_喜欢" class="hb-emoji"></span></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="1104" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/02/02/d9c8d2ca4d86e07da10036082bac0176/thumb.jpeg" data-width="828" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>炸到这个样子，差不多10分钟左右</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="1104" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/02/02/bb1d57f01e497d406c83cee0bc43df7f/thumb.jpeg" data-width="828" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>升高油温至190度，复炸三分钟左右，看到变成这样子的金黄色就好了</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="1104" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2022/02/02/af39a1e0ae189e7db2f306a68f69710d/thumb.jpeg" data-width="828"
