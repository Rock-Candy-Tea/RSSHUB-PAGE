<p><b>配方材料：</b>墩墩桃*2，薄荷*2，须弥蔷薇*2</p><p><b>星级</b><b>：</b>★★</p><p><b>食谱获取方法：</b>森林书系列任务「愿为一炊之梦」，「料理是快乐的回忆」完成获得。</p><p>虽然沙拉有很多种，但是看这个描述很像Tabbouleh沙拉，由西红柿、洋葱、黄瓜和布格麦（中东菜常用到的一种小米）制成，并用新鲜的薄荷和柠檬汁调味。属于黎巴嫩等地的菜肴。</p><p><br></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="877" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2022/09/12/950229eac3b94f2f836969729c026a101662995525.jpg" data-width="1080" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="234" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2022/09/12/787298a7c778443cb81fe9c7296f90581662995526.png" data-width="410" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><h4 class="img-desc">雨林沙拉</h4><p>An Aranara-style salad that Paimon drained her brain juice dry to improve. It is, in fact, a rather traditional recipe in Sumeru. Fresh and crispy Zaytun Peach slices and mixed with gently squeezed <b>Sumeru Roses</b>, which adds a touch of distinctive flavor. Mint is used instead of sugar to avoid getting a sickening sweetness. A refreshing dish that pleases both the eyes and the nose.</p><p><b>Sumeru Roses须弥蔷薇</b></p><p>经过派蒙苦思冥想改良后的兰那罗沙拉，但实际上在须弥却是早已有之的做法。墩墩桃十分鲜脆，替代国甜白糖的是口感清爽的薄荷，为增加风味放入略加挤压的<b>须弥蔷薇</b>。是兼具味觉和嗅觉双重享受的清新菜肴。</p><p><br></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="235" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2022/09/12/89f25c288ec64ea0959f12090a80354c1662995528.png" data-width="409" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><h4 class="img-desc">奇怪的雨林沙拉</h4><p>An Aranara-style salad that Paimon drained her brain juice dry to improve. It is, in fact, a rather traditional recipe in Sumeru. Ripe — "slightly fermented" to be more precise — Zaytun Peaches give off a bizarre smell. And this is to say nothing of the <b>disproportionate </b>use of mints and roses...</p><p><b>disproportionate/ˌdɪsprəˈpɔːrʃənət/adj.不成比例的</b></p><p>经过派蒙苦思冥想改良后的兰那罗沙拉，但实际上在须弥却是早已有之的做法。墩墩桃散发着近乎熟烂到发酵的古怪味道，薄荷与蔷薇的比例也相当失衡…</p><p><br></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="233" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2022/09/12/9f1dc35aeb7242c38b1ecc465c5fd56a1662995535.png" data-width="405" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><h4 class="img-desc">美味的雨林沙拉</h4><p>An Aranara-style salad that Paimon drained her brain juice dry to improve. It is, in fact, a rather traditional recipe in Sumeru. Crispy and juicy Zaytun Peaches are served with fresh mints and blooming roses. This delicacy tastes <b>refreshingly </b>sweet and feels pleasantly cool on the tongue — this must be "the Taste of Happiness," as Aranara say.</p><p><b>refreshingly/rɪˈfreʃɪŋli/adv.清爽地；有精神地</b></p><p>经过派蒙苦思冥想改良后的兰那罗沙拉，但实际上在须弥却是早已有之的做法。选用的墩墩桃质地脆嫩，入口多汁，伴随薄荷的清甜和蔷薇成熟后散发的沁香直入腹中。具备适宜的甜度与恰到好处的冰爽，这就是所谓的「快乐的味道」吧。</p><p><br></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="1228" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2022/09/12/a1c958f5b750427a8706b3f19d4a60d71662995540.png" data-width="700" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><a href="heybox://open_subject" target="_blank">#原神3.0版本创作者征集活动#</a><br></p>
