<h2>今天给大家分享的菜是排骨炖土豆，下面介绍这道菜的做法<span data-emoji="cube_开心" class="hb-emoji"></span>。</h2><p><b>‌‌1.首先准备食材。准备排骨大约2斤，土豆2个，葱姜蒜干辣椒适量。</b></p><p><b>2.排骨用水浸泡去除血水，土豆切块浸泡去除表面淀粉，葱姜蒜干辣椒切好备用。</b></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="626" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2021/08/04/0cc167ff3c268f8924b451011e6434a7/thumb.jpeg" data-width="834" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><b><br></b></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="626" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2021/08/04/df3eb13f08656dec11276db3e3a07829/thumb.jpeg" data-width="834" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><b><br></b></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="626" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2021/08/04/2d89e0175c33151222e435353fb9400d/thumb.jpeg" data-width="834" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><b>2.烧一锅水，水开后倒入排骨，焯1至2分钟，撇去浮末，捞出排骨备用。</b></p><p><b>3.热锅凉油，放入花椒、八角爆香，倒入排骨翻炒均匀，翻炒至排骨表面变色。</b></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="626" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2021/08/04/d62f921ea1e8d0528df8347f6deea1e3/thumb.jpeg" data-width="834" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><b>4.倒入葱姜蒜干辣椒翻炒均匀。</b></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="626" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2021/08/04/f4ad32b1cbec827578d107754425f245/thumb.jpeg" data-width="834" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><b>5.倒入料酒、老抽翻炒均匀，料酒去腥提鲜，老抽提色增香。</b></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="626" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2021/08/04/7f20bf963ded5b165c66305d93ef00a1/thumb.jpeg" data-width="834" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><b>6.倒入事先烧好的热水，水开后加入一勺食盐，盖上锅盖，小火慢炖30分钟。</b></p><p><b>7.倒入土豆块，炖大概10分钟。</b></p><p><b><br></b></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="626" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2021/08/04/ee3817c5d36176045f9fb8cdbb0f5468/thumb.jpeg" data-width="834" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><b>8.放入鸡精、陈醋，转大火收汁，炖至土豆块软烂。</b></p><p><b><br></b></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="626" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2021/08/04/c1f0e508918b3a9e24010fb83cc39802/thumb.jpeg" data-width="834" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><b>9.装盘后撒上葱花点缀。</b></p><p><b><br></b></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="626" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2021/08/04/e389218e8eea2098bbc8a8530ff9b3e5/thumb.jpeg" data-width="834" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><b>10.最后蒸好香喷喷的米饭。</b></p><p><b><br></b></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="626" src="https://imgheybox.max-c.com/bbs/2021/08/04/89c74d901241a8fc7f842cd9dc2a1b24/thumb.jpeg" data-width="834" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><h2>不得不说，土豆永远的神<span data-emoji="cube_哇" class="hb-emoji"></span>，排骨可以和土豆搭配，也可以和芸豆、粉条搭配，如果不喜欢干辣椒也可以不加。下期有空给大家分享米饭的蒸法，蒸出来的米饭真的很香<span data-emoji="cube_滑稽" class="hb-emoji"></span>。<a href="heybox://open_subject"
