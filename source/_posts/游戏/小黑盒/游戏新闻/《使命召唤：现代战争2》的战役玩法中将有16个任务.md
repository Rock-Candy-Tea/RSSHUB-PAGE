The Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2 campaign, which will be available in Early Access for those who pre-order, promises to deliver a compelling story filled with highly entertaining, surprising, and dramatic episodes.
Several mission titles are clearly reminiscent of the events of the original Modern Warfare 2, notably “Backstabbed”, which may refer to a level where a certain betrayal occurs and two characters in the squad suffer a chilling fate.
The open beta starts on September 24, and the game itself will be released on October 28 on PC, PlayStation 4, Xbox One, PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X.</p>
