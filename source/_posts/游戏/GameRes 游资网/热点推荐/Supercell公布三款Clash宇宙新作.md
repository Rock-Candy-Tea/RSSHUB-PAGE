Supercell 一连公布了三款新作：《部落战线》、《皇室奇兵》和《部落传说》。<br>
<br>
这三款游戏均为Clash宇宙系列的作品，我们熟悉的王子、巨人、骷髅兵等角色仍然会出席，而其中其中《部落战线》不久将会展开（海外）测试，其他两款由Supercell上海工作室开发。<br>
<br>
<strong><font color="#de5650">《部落战线》</font></strong><br>
<br>
<div align="center">
<img id="aimg_970240" aid="970240" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/06/101826x90p5n9o050g9q5p.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/06/101826x90p5n9o050g9q5p.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/06/101826x90p5n9o050g9q5p.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div><br>
<br>
回合制战术策略冒险游戏。对抗系统预设的防御阵型，玩法是消耗队伍中不同的兵种进行攻击，兵种运行机制和《皇室战争》《部落冲突》中的类似。<br>
<br>
<div align="center">
<img id="aimg_970241" aid="970241" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/06/101827xvf4ibh2be88n4l4.png" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/06/101827xvf4ibh2be88n4l4.png" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/06/101827xvf4ibh2be88n4l4.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div><br>
<br>
<div align="center">
<img id="aimg_970242" aid="970242" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/06/101828kxibxehxoddx73ix.gif" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/06/101828kxibxehxoddx73ix.gif" width="200" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/06/101828kxibxehxoddx73ix.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div><br>
<br>
比如，每场对战里，如果提前消耗完队伍里的兵种，就会输掉比赛；兵种消耗也有一定的规则，比如出击的同种兵种越多，效果就越好。<br>
<br>
巨大BOSS和道具系统也是本作的特色，不同道具的触发方式不同，比如同时出击5名王子才会生效的AOE道具。<br>
<br>
<div align="center">
<img id="aimg_970243" aid="970243" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/06/101830vecx2wuekxs66sch.gif" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/06/101830vecx2wuekxs66sch.gif" width="372" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/06/101830vecx2wuekxs66sch.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div><br>
<br>
<strong><font color="#de5650">《皇室奇兵》</font></strong><br>
<br>
玩家双方同时在桌面布置可可爱爱的迷你奇兵，他们将自动攻击。<br>
<br>
<div align="center">
<img id="aimg_970244" aid="970244" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/06/101832iu3h6ru5t62h55ee.png" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/06/101832iu3h6ru5t62h55ee.png" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/06/101832iu3h6ru5t62h55ee.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div><br>
<br>
<div align="center">
<img id="aimg_970245" aid="970245" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/06/101835p0zopsh7sc7qxcph.gif" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/06/101835p0zopsh7sc7qxcph.gif" width="220" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/06/101835p0zopsh7sc7qxcph.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">

<br>
在游戏中，玩家可以将精力集中于策略部分，而不是实时控制，各种迷你奇兵十分可爱。<br>
<br>
<div align="center">
<img id="aimg_970246" aid="970246" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/06/101835z50aww04wk4a589a.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/06/101835z50aww04wk4a589a.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/06/101835z50aww04wk4a589a.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div><br>
<br>
<strong><font color="#de5650">《部落传说》</font></strong><br>
<br>
一款虚幻引擎打造的动作类游戏，玩家们使用不同的角色组建队伍，在地图上冒险。<br>
<br>
<div align="center">
<img id="aimg_970247" aid="970247" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/06/101836ljxizha86i9zcgwe.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/06/101836ljxizha86i9zcgwe.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/06/101836ljxizha86i9zcgwe.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div><br>
<br>
从公布的资料可以看出，玩家可以使用双摇杆，控制前进方向和技能释放及技能方向；<br>
<br>
<div align="center">
<img id="aimg_970248" aid="970248" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/06/101838evbdt1dew6dv9taw.gif" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/06/101838evbdt1dew6dv9taw.gif" width="350" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/06/101838evbdt1dew6dv9taw.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div><br>
<br>
游戏内还分有不同的地域，有草原、有水上城市，而且地图上的部分建筑物甚至能被破坏。<br>
<br>
