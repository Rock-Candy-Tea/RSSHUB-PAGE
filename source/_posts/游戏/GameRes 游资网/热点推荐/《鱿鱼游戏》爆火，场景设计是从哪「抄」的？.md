近期，一部惊悚悬疑神剧，《鱿鱼游戏》持续霸占网飞（Netflix）热播剧榜首。故事讲述了456名为生活所困的人，为了456亿韩元的奖金参加了6个生死逃杀的游戏。<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1014992" aid="1014992" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134212n21fzrg8mu111cfk.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134212n21fzrg8mu111cfk.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134212n21fzrg8mu111cfk.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br>
伴随着这个剧爆火的除了精彩的故事情节与游戏外，莫过于整部剧的场景设计。其中最为引起共鸣的莫过于闯关者出场的彩色立体走廊。<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1014993" aid="1014993" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134212q7azjmj77ol6yjmy.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134212q7azjmj77ol6yjmy.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134212q7azjmj77ol6yjmy.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br>
有的人说，像「纪念碑谷」，也有的人一眼就认出这是西班牙的红墙公寓。今天我们就来盘一盘这部热播剧中的美学空间。<br><br><strong><font color="#de5650">01、视觉美学·鱿鱼空间</font></strong><br><br><strong><font color="#de5650"># 概念来源</font></strong><br><br>
在《鱿鱼游戏》中，这个巨大而又错综复杂的高饱和彩色楼梯迷宫，给人一种童真的画面感。<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1014994" aid="1014994" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134213rt73atmt946ymt4m.gif" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134213rt73atmt946ymt4m.gif" width="320" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134213rt73atmt946ymt4m.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br>
然而这个场景所参考的便是位于西班牙卡尔贝的红墙公寓「La Muralla Roja」。<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1014995" aid="1014995" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134213jwn5zrkngxtwvwwq.gif" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134213jwn5zrkngxtwvwwq.gif" width="320" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134213jwn5zrkngxtwvwwq.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br><strong><font color="#de5650"># 视觉美学之对称</font></strong><br><br>
除了建筑整体外，整部电影的对称美学的运用，让人看的畅快淋漓。游戏空间则充分将工业风融入到对称美学中。<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1014996" aid="1014996" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134214spkraza32spzzz35.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134214spkraza32spzzz35.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134214spkraza32spzzz35.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br>
最有寓意的莫过于宿舍的仓库化，床铺的货架化，表达了人在社会的阶级关系。<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1014997" aid="1014997" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134214gtxfao1mmw8qqm78.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134214gtxfao1mmw8qqm78.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134214gtxfao1mmw8qqm78.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br>
当参与者接二连三的死去，阶级逐渐被清除，那个被大家以命相争的“线索”其实一直隐藏于床后。<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1014998" aid="1014998" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134215jm3q5q16tqz3bgnn.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134215jm3q5q16tqz3bgnn.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134215jm3q5q16tqz3bgnn.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br><strong><font color="#de5650"># 视觉美学之超现实</font></strong><br><br>
不知道大家在看游乐场时，会不会觉得童真的背景，又充斥着一种超现实主义感？<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1014999" aid="1014999" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134215wtyzhvvjuquh99h4.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134215wtyzhvvjuquh99h4.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134215wtyzhvvjuquh99h4.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br>
简化的房屋，虚假而又封闭的天空，像极了《楚门的世界》。被人观赏，监督，成为别人的“玩具”。<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1015000" aid="1015000" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134216nz75al8r588388zs.gif" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134216nz75al8r588388zs.gif" width="320" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134216nz75al8r588388zs.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br>
在木头人游戏场所里，导演用一个放大的人偶娃娃，在观众中营造一种怪诞、不安的感觉。<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1015001" aid="1015001" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134216xxxjjfdxcr2r41h4.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134216xxxjjfdxcr2r41h4.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134216xxxjjfdxcr2r41h4.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br>
第二个游戏空间同样也是一个超现实主义场所。看似简单的线条寥寥几笔，勾勒出一个似梦非梦的空间。<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1015002" aid="1015002" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134217s9ufz7x45hp1mf4u.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134217s9ufz7x45hp1mf4u.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134217s9ufz7x45hp1mf4u.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br>
其实这个风格就是借鉴了超现实主义画家雷内·玛格利特的作品，毕竟蓝天白云就是她的标志风格。<br><br><div align="center"><font size="2"><font color="#808080">
<img id="aimg_1015003" aid="1015003" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134217us1t9yxsyxy9swts.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134217us1t9yxsyxy9swts.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134217us1t9yxsyxy9swts.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></font></font></div>
<div align="center"><font size="2"><font color="#808080">左：《虚假的镜子》  右：《海岸构图》</font></font></div>
<br>
精巧的将超现实主义融入现实，如放大的儿童玩具，让人梦与现实难解难分。难怪这里的空间即使发生“暴力事件”后，也不觉得不和谐。<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1015004" aid="1015004" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134217jlx000owvllwe3ew.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134217jlx000owvllwe3ew.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134217jlx000owvllwe3ew.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<div align="center"><font size="2"><font color="#808080">正常儿童尺寸的游乐园和放大版成人游乐园</font></font></div>
<br>
同样，拔河比赛的空间也像是进入到一个放大的、儿童搭建玩具中的其中一部分。黑色的幕布，强烈的空间感，仿佛失败便是堕入万劫不复的虚空。<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1015005" aid="1015005" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134218ijccc49cdhckerr9.gif" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134218ijccc49cdhckerr9.gif" width="320" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134218ijccc49cdhckerr9.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br><strong><font color="#de5650"># 视觉美学之现实</font></strong><br><br>
除了刻意营造出的超现实空间外，也有一些空间灵感完全取自于现实。比如玩弹珠游戏的小巷。<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1015006" aid="1015006" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134218h5x548f6fhci294q.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134218h5x548f6fhci294q.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134218h5x548f6fhci294q.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br>
这个空间的搭建是所有场景中花费时间最长的空间，场景细节从“大理石、到生锈的大门，甚至是门板，制作组细化了几乎所有细节...<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1015007" aid="1015007" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134220l69n116nv65eiaw7.gif" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134220l69n116nv65eiaw7.gif" width="320" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134220l69n116nv65eiaw7.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br>
这一切都是为了制造游戏的冲突感。为了进一步提升气氛，鱿鱼的剧组还选择了深红色的日落作为背景，以模糊现实与梦境的边界。<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1015008" aid="1015008" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134221t2y2nykj3y3ny3sj.gif" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134221t2y2nykj3y3ny3sj.gif" width="320" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134221t2y2nykj3y3ny3sj.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br>
另一个白空间“等待室”可以看到对当代建筑的参考，纯白、明亮的空间让人想起扎哈·哈迪德或圣地亚哥·卡拉特拉瓦的设计。<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1015009" aid="1015009" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134222th99gshrhvv66vi6.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134222th99gshrhvv66vi6.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134222th99gshrhvv66vi6.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<div align="center"><font size="2"><font color="#808080">左：盖达尔·阿利耶夫中心由扎哈·哈迪德建筑师事务所; 右：圣地亚哥·卡拉特拉瓦 (Santiago Calatrava) 的世贸中心站</font></font></div>
<br>
弯曲的墙壁和冷峻的灯光与盖达尔·阿利耶夫中心或世界贸易中心内部相呼应。这里的白色空间营造的未知与不安感，渲染了游戏紧张的氛围。<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1015010" aid="1015010" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134222vbzmkvqs065s6ggn.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134222vbzmkvqs065s6ggn.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134222vbzmkvqs065s6ggn.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br>
玻璃桥淘汰赛空间，玲琅满目的霓虹灯与帷幔，像极了剧院或马戏团大秀的场景。<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1015011" aid="1015011" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134223zii4aahd9ias8cdf.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134223zii4aahd9ias8cdf.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134223zii4aahd9ias8cdf.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br>
结合游戏又会发现这样的布置与其说是剧院，更像是来自拉斯维加斯的赌场。毕竟，这是一场终极赌博游戏，这样的设计，才会让「VIP」们更有凌驾一切的观赏与优越感。<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1015012" aid="1015012" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134223zouuevyiu5ozqzuo.gif" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134223zouuevyiu5ozqzuo.gif" width="320" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134223zouuevyiu5ozqzuo.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br>
在游戏结束之前，最后还有三个人的时候，享受的晚宴的三角桌也颇有渊源。<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1015013" aid="1015013" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134224nnwollys27vil7bk.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134224nnwollys27vil7bk.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134224nnwollys27vil7bk.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br>
三角形晚宴桌设计，灵感来自女权鼻祖艺术家朱迪·芝加哥(Judy Chicago)的大型视觉艺术作品晚宴（Dinner Party）。<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1015014" aid="1015014" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134224yu853kbuuulu8eqz.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134224yu853kbuuulu8eqz.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134224yu853kbuuulu8eqz.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br>
这个三角桌在艺术家的表达中象征了女权运动中“平等”的概念，而“平等”这个概念，又是贯穿《鱿鱼游戏》的核心主导。<br><br><div align="center"><font size="2"><font color="#808080">
<img id="aimg_1015015" aid="1015015" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134224shme1ipc1okho4kz.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134224shme1ipc1okho4kz.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134224shme1ipc1okho4kz.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></font></font></div>
<div align="center"><font size="2"><font color="#808080">《晚宴（Dinner Party）》</font></font></div>
<br>
除游戏场景外，VIP看客们的观赏大厅也是剧组重点设计的场景。昏暗的灯光，茂密的丛林，让人不禁联想到卢梭的作品-《梦境》。女人在不知情的情况下被带入森林中，依靠在沙发上，看一条蛇为她吹奏。<br><br><div align="center"><font size="2"><font color="#808080">
<img id="aimg_1015016" aid="1015016" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134225dyl8aalhlat7ay2d.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134225dyl8aalhlat7ay2d.jpg" width="460" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134225dyl8aalhlat7ay2d.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></font></font></div>
<div align="center"><font size="2"><font color="#808080">卢梭 《梦境》 1910年</font></font></div>
<br>
圣经中，蛇代表“撒旦”，而蛇这个元素在VIP大厅里可是高频出现。VIP们带着兽首面具。<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1015017" aid="1015017" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134225jzmkrmkdh782tvi4.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134225jzmkrmkdh782tvi4.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134225jzmkrmkdh782tvi4.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br>
而其他人则扮演丛林中的动物，处处暗示这是一个弱肉强食的邪恶世界，“财富”这个伊甸园，是一去不返的“地狱”。<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1015018" aid="1015018" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134226nh251zcc2522h1m2.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134226nh251zcc2522h1m2.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134226nh251zcc2522h1m2.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br><strong><font color="#de5650">02、建筑美学·红墙公寓</font></strong><br><br>
除了视觉彩蛋外，应该就是大家津津乐道的，好看而有梦幻的“迷宫走廊”现实版了。这个建筑是由建筑大师 Ricardo Bofill （里卡多·波菲）于1968年设计，1973年完工的公寓。<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1015019" aid="1015019" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134226hli4z607a95i342b.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134226hli4z607a95i342b.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134226hli4z607a95i342b.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br>
这栋公寓建造在海岸线边的峭壁上，与周围环境格格不入。说它是公寓，其实更像是一座红色的堡垒。<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1015020" aid="1015020" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134227kqa5ru5h9lzdfa5p.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134227kqa5ru5h9lzdfa5p.jpg" width="576" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134227kqa5ru5h9lzdfa5p.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br>
该建筑受到北非国家的「卡斯巴哈山城」启发，借鉴了互锁楼梯等建筑结构，所以看起来魔幻感十足。也难怪《鱿鱼游戏》会借鉴。<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1015021" aid="1015021" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134227vbx9bazmuszss6ag.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134227vbx9bazmuszss6ag.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134227vbx9bazmuszss6ag.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br>
里卡多·波菲痴总是迷于用奇特的几何体块、大胆艳丽的颜色，塑造出史诗般宏大的建筑体块，并让它完全脱离于环境。<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1015022" aid="1015022" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134230xasqhj33q3kgjd3m.gif" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134230xasqhj33q3kgjd3m.gif" width="320" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134230xasqhj33q3kgjd3m.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br>
虽然画风诡异，但他的视觉风格总能将传统和神秘多变、严谨理性都无缝结合在一起。<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1015023" aid="1015023" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134231hcczdcv4jnoufoud.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134231hcczdcv4jnoufoud.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134231hcczdcv4jnoufoud.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br>
波菲希望能够通过这种建筑，令人们意识到一种「后立体主义」的视觉语言。<br><br>
同时，还想要打破自文艺复兴时期以来，对公共空间与私人空间越来越强烈的区隔，建造一个「人人都生活在一起」的乌托邦世界。<br><br><div align="center"><font size="2"><font color="#808080">
<img id="aimg_1015024" aid="1015024" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134231ogb4hgg7bds7tmjs.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134231ogb4hgg7bds7tmjs.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134231ogb4hgg7bds7tmjs.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></font></font></div>
<div align="center"><font size="2"><font color="#808080">图片：Andrés Gallardo</font></font></div>
<br>
对比之下，《鱿鱼游戏》里的应用，绝对是反讽高手了！<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1015025" aid="1015025" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134231ov6pqywzpeq0o9hp.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134231ov6pqywzpeq0o9hp.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134231ov6pqywzpeq0o9hp.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br><strong><font color="#de5650"># 复杂形态</font></strong><br><br>
这栋公寓复杂的建筑溢于言表。从平面来看，它是基于希腊十字架，用多个十字架以不同方式组合而成。<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1015026" aid="1015026" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134231ww25o51wnsx72w2m.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134231ww25o51wnsx72w2m.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134231ww25o51wnsx72w2m.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1015027" aid="1015027" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134233jeidgttl00cltl3g.gif" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134233jeidgttl00cltl3g.gif" width="320" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134233jeidgttl00cltl3g.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br>
这个布局的几何基础和「构成主义理论」很相近，体现出了一种构成主义的美学。<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1015028" aid="1015028" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134233q0ob0qmgvm2lqg3f.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134233q0ob0qmgvm2lqg3f.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134233q0ob0qmgvm2lqg3f.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br>
这种环环相扣的楼梯，交错有序的平台，里三层外三层的结构就像迷宫，让人不禁联想到「卡斯巴循环」。<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1015029" aid="1015029" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134234qoosf2rrmjrzr1vx.gif" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134234qoosf2rrmjrzr1vx.gif" width="320" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134234qoosf2rrmjrzr1vx.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1015030" aid="1015030" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134234gjmb57gwojjs9li5.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134234gjmb57gwojjs9li5.jpg" width="548" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134234gjmb57gwojjs9li5.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br>
纵横狭窄的阶梯，是红墙内部最大的特征。如果不熟悉地形，很容易在城堡内的迷宫世界迷路，这也是为什么红墙和《纪念碑谷》相似的原因之一。<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1015031" aid="1015031" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134235cnd2zynn2nd9bw9e.gif" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134235cnd2zynn2nd9bw9e.gif" width="320" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134235cnd2zynn2nd9bw9e.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1015032" aid="1015032" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134235x27ad2wb8o68pd11.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134235x27ad2wb8o68pd11.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134235x27ad2wb8o68pd11.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<div align="center"><font size="2"><font color="#808080">左：红墙公寓    右：《纪念碑谷3》</font></font></div>
<br>
这座建筑内有 50 间公寓，3 种规格：60㎡ 一居室、80㎡ 两室和 120㎡ 的三室。<br><br><div align="center"><font size="2"><font color="#808080">
<img id="aimg_1015033" aid="1015033" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134236g7d7ivi8kdb8ji7d.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134236g7d7ivi8kdb8ji7d.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134236g7d7ivi8kdb8ji7d.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></font></font></div>
<div align="center"><font size="2"><font color="#808080">图片：Andrés Gallardo</font></font></div>
<br>
另外也有包括互相连接的公共庭院，在屋顶上有日光浴室和游泳池。<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1015034" aid="1015034" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134236bvp7w1aa2afip23z.gif" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134236bvp7w1aa2afip23z.gif" width="320" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134236bvp7w1aa2afip23z.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br>
复杂但功能完备的建筑结构，是红墙设计上最出彩的一环，让公寓呈现出充满未来感的氛围。<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1015035" aid="1015035" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134236zp2o0mj6rw669mkg.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134236zp2o0mj6rw669mkg.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134236zp2o0mj6rw669mkg.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br>
虽然《鱿鱼游戏》的空间看起来很像红墙公寓，但是《鱿鱼游戏》的导演黄东赫明确表示，自己是受到荷兰版画家：莫里茨·科内利斯·埃舍尔（Maurits Cornelis Escher）1953年的一幅画——《相对性》（Relativity）的启发，因此有了如此的建筑演绎。<br><br><div align="center"><font size="2"><font color="#808080">
<img id="aimg_1015036" aid="1015036" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134237r5bfb4qqzqi90yuq.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134237r5bfb4qqzqi90yuq.jpg" width="493" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134237r5bfb4qqzqi90yuq.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></font></font></div>
<div align="center"><font size="2"><font color="#808080">《相对性》（Relativity）</font></font></div>
<br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1015037" aid="1015037" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134237dc26r86cix56v8um.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134237dc26r86cix56v8um.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134237dc26r86cix56v8um.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br><strong><font color="#de5650"># 高饱和颜色</font></strong><br><br>
虽然叫做「红墙」，但是红墙不止有一种颜色而已。<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1015038" aid="1015038" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134237ahf3asss8hw8khwh.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134237ahf3asss8hw8khwh.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134237ahf3asss8hw8khwh.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br>
不要以为这些颜色只是波菲高兴所以随随便便搞出来的，事实上这些颜色都有不同的功用。<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1015039" aid="1015039" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134238apqvvl0b6llzq0jj.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134238apqvvl0b6llzq0jj.jpg" width="507" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134238apqvvl0b6llzq0jj.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br>
红墙的外立面采用各种色调的红色，与周围蓝色的海景形成鲜明的对比，以此来「刷存在感」，把建筑本身在环境中凸显出来。<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1015040" aid="1015040" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134238ps0ekpp5s0p3p3r1.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134238ps0ekpp5s0p3p3r1.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134238ps0ekpp5s0p3p3r1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br>
楼梯和各处通道则是用了天蓝、靛蓝、紫罗兰等不同色调的蓝色。<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1015041" aid="1015041" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134239tgizch456udhc5xl.gif" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134239tgizch456udhc5xl.gif" width="320" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134239tgizch456udhc5xl.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br>
当阳光照射下来，建筑表面的颜色随着日光而变化，形成丰富的层次感，创造出浪漫又迷幻的效果。<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1015042" aid="1015042" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134240b61ir6rrp2unn1m9.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134240b61ir6rrp2unn1m9.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134240b61ir6rrp2unn1m9.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br>
撞色效果能够产生强烈的视觉冲击，有种身处异度空间的迷失感。绿植的点缀为未来感的空间增加点现实感。<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1015043" aid="1015043" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134240ttn1a11r5f8ey5tr.gif" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134240ttn1a11r5f8ey5tr.gif" width="320" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134240ttn1a11r5f8ey5tr.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br>
当然也有在建筑物的表面颜色上进行的冲突。<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1015044" aid="1015044" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134240dglwbl6f8jbc8glj.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134240dglwbl6f8jbc8glj.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134240dglwbl6f8jbc8glj.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br>
红墙的颜色是设计中最出彩的环节之一。大量使用低纯度但明度高的颜色，让整栋楼显得既优雅沉稳，又不失去活泼有朝气。<br><br><div align="center">
<img id="aimg_1015045" aid="1015045" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134240ixtkekjaxhxf2eaa.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134240ixtkekjaxhxf2eaa.jpg" width="507" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202110/15/134240ixtkekjaxhxf2eaa.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div>
<br><strong><font color="#de5650"># 小结</font></strong><br><br>
「La Muralla Roja」红墙迄今为止已经超过 50 年历史，是波菲在早期崇尚后现代主义时的巅峰作品。尽管他早已回归经典，转变为新古典主义设计，但他设计出来的这面「红墙」，过了半个世纪依然是后生津津乐道的艺术灵感源泉。<br><br>
电影也一样，电影中的空间是一种综合美学，好的场景设计能够有效地烘托氛围，让观众沉浸其中达到共情。当然，最主要的是，可能会和建筑一样，成为一代经典。<br><br><font size="2"><font color="#808080"></font></font><br><font size="2"><font color="#808080">来源：一起设计</font></font><br><font size="2"><font color="#808080">原文：https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s/QcZmI_td7HXEzuIsqMFCyA</font></font><br><br>
