美好的一天从玩“黑神话”手游开始。<br>
<br>
近日，茶馆发现一款名叫《浮生妖绘卷：黑神话》的游戏在国区App Store付费榜已经连续霸榜十天了，隔壁的《王者荣耀》与《和平精英》都没他猛。<br>
<br>
<div align="center">
<img id="aimg_973765" aid="973765" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/22/103823szszj05153bsuj3m.png" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/22/103823szszj05153bsuj3m.png" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/22/103823szszj05153bsuj3m.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div><br>
<div align="center">
<img id="aimg_973766" aid="973766" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/22/103823q3ovc5szoyh5ossc.png" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/22/103823q3ovc5szoyh5ossc.png" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/22/103823q3ovc5szoyh5ossc.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div><br>
一说起黑神话我们难免会想起来游戏科学的《黑神话：悟空》，这款游戏自发布宣传片以来受到海外内游戏圈的大量好评，是最有希望冲击国产3A游戏的作品之一。<br>
<br>
<div align="center">
<img id="aimg_973774" aid="973774" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/22/103858vfyxq2ll7l2lkmmz.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/22/103858vfyxq2ll7l2lkmmz.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/22/103858vfyxq2ll7l2lkmmz.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div><br>
《浮生妖绘卷：黑神话》当然不是黑悟空的手游版，这显然是一款蹭热度游戏。在国家新闻出版署搜索版号，发现游戏的名字只有前半部分，后面的“黑神话”是强行加上去的。<br>
<br>
<div align="center">
<img id="aimg_973767" aid="973767" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/22/103823txiahk70ha0pm8oa.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/22/103823txiahk70ha0pm8oa.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/22/103823txiahk70ha0pm8oa.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div><br>
<div align="center">
<img id="aimg_973768" aid="973768" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/22/103824pz3uaylwx61zd38d.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/22/103824pz3uaylwx61zd38d.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/22/103824pz3uaylwx61zd38d.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div><br>
这款游戏无法通过TapTap下载。而在TapTap上，游戏的评分仅为1.7。<br>
<br>
<div align="center">
<img id="aimg_973769" aid="973769" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/22/103824q3t3d5w4n5h8da51.png" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/22/103824q3t3d5w4n5h8da51.png" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/22/103824q3t3d5w4n5h8da51.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div><br>
冒着很大的风险，我的vivo应用商店里下载了这款游戏，尽管我能预见到游戏的体验会很糟糕，但游戏无法登陆疑似停服我是没想到的。<br>
<br>
<div align="center">
<img id="aimg_973770" aid="973770" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/22/103824gcrm0mumfmlxw7l5.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/22/103824gcrm0mumfmlxw7l5.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/22/103824gcrm0mumfmlxw7l5.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div><br>
游戏400个服务器全部爆满，创建角色需要选择未满的服务器。我们连续选择在多个服务器之间创建角色，结果都是失败的。有意思的是，官方公告里并未通知游戏今天维护。<br>
<br>
<div align="center">
<img id="aimg_973771" aid="973771" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/22/103825e2t1hdx41x41ctj9.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/22/103825e2t1hdx41x41ctj9.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/22/103825e2t1hdx41x41ctj9.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div><br>
根据显示，游戏初始可选择的角色只有灵明石猴，剩下的六耳猕猴、心月狐、七公主等得在游戏中通关主线章节后才能解锁。<br>
<br>
<div align="center">
<img id="aimg_973772" aid="973772" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/22/103825uzuuiyozy93cmxmg.jpg" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/22/103825uzuuiyozy93cmxmg.jpg" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/22/103825uzuuiyozy93cmxmg.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div><br>
最终，借同事的手机花了1块钱的血本在ios下载了这款游戏并且顺利登陆了。<br>
<br>
没有什么惊喜，就和大部分商业ARPG游戏一样，自动巡礼-升级-打怪-提升战斗力类似的循环，游戏的画质说不上糟糕，甚至还可以在3D与2.5D之间切换。<br>
<br>
<div align="center">
<img id="aimg_973773" aid="973773" zoomfile="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/22/103826a17q20nja0q7si0n.png" data-original="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/22/103826a17q20nja0q7si0n.png" width="600" inpost="1" src="https://di.gameres.com/attachment/forum/202104/22/103826a17q20nja0q7si0n.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</div><br>
剧情不用深究，毕竟悟空也可以跟六耳猕猴是好战友。<br>
<br>
我很快卸载了《浮生妖绘卷》，我想，如果没有在名字里加上黑悟空，我大概也不会注意到这款游戏。而《浮生妖绘卷：黑神话》也会安然的坐在付费榜第一的位置，俯瞰风景。（狗头.jpg）<br>
