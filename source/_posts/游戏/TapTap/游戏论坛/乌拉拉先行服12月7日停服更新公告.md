乌拉拉先行服本次停服维护更新时间预计延长1小时，维护至12月7日24:00。<br class="bbcode-paragraph-br"><br class="bbcode-paragraph-br">维护完成后将为大家发放停服维护补偿。<br class="bbcode-paragraph-br"><br class="bbcode-paragraph-br">——————————————————————————————————————————————<br class="bbcode-paragraph-br"><br class="bbcode-paragraph-br">乌拉拉的冒险者们：<br class="bbcode-paragraph-br"><br class="bbcode-paragraph-br">大家好~<br class="bbcode-paragraph-br"><br class="bbcode-paragraph-br">乌拉拉先行服将在12月7日（周一）下午20:00-23:00进行一次停服维护更新。<br class="bbcode-paragraph-br"><br class="bbcode-paragraph-br">本次更新将新增宠物星级回退功能、时装预览功能，同时新增天空树大陆关卡50关、新增赛季副本关卡及进行部分数值调整、新增冰霜节礼包。<br class="bbcode-paragraph-br"><br class="bbcode-paragraph-br"><br class="bbcode-paragraph-br">(2) 高于0星的宠物可进行回退操作，星级越高，回退操作消耗越高；<br class="bbcode-paragraph-br"><br class="bbcode-paragraph-br">(3) 低于5星的宠物回退后为0星， 高于5星的宠物回退后为5星；<br class="bbcode-paragraph-br"><br class="bbcode-paragraph-br">(4) 退星时，将根据宠物的星级返还对应数量的金色猪布林宠物蛋。<br class="bbcode-paragraph-br"><br class="bbcode-paragraph-br">2. 游戏界面整体升级<br class="bbcode-paragraph-br"><br class="bbcode-paragraph-br">(1) 整合部分功能入口，优化部分小红点提示逻辑<br class="bbcode-paragraph-br"><br class="bbcode-paragraph-br">3. 增加时装预览功能<br class="bbcode-paragraph-br"><br class="bbcode-paragraph-br">(1) 可以预览时装的穿戴效果，并且可以查看设计师精心搭配的套装效果；<br class="bbcode-paragraph-br"><br class="bbcode-paragraph-br">4. 新增天空树大陆幻影石窟关卡，共计50关；<br class="bbcode-paragraph-br"><br class="bbcode-paragraph-br">5. 赛季副本更新：<br class="bbcode-paragraph-br"><br class="bbcode-paragraph-br">(1) 开放赛季副本151-200层<br class="bbcode-paragraph-br"><br class="bbcode-paragraph-br">(2) 赛季副本1-150层怪物强度下调及推荐战力调整<br class="bbcode-paragraph-br"><br class="bbcode-paragraph-br">(3) 赛季副本战斗增益组合数值调整：<br class="bbcode-paragraph-br"><br class="bbcode-paragraph-br">a) 霸王龙头骨：宠物获得主人攻击值比例由400%下调为300%<br class="bbcode-paragraph-br"><br class="bbcode-paragraph-br">b) 雷王龙之角：宠物造成伤害比例由400%下调为300%<br class="bbcode-paragraph-br"><br class="bbcode-paragraph-br">c) 女王龙之羽：每次释放技能宠物攻击力提高比例由100%下调为75%<br class="bbcode-paragraph-br"><br class="bbcode-paragraph-br">d) 兔晨晨的账本：每拥有1个神器，角色攻击力提高比例由10%下调为5%，角色攻击力提高40%需求累计获得神器数量由3个提升为6个<br class="bbcode-paragraph-br"><br class="bbcode-paragraph-br">e) 战斗日记：每拥有4级神佑，角色攻击力提高比例由10%下调为8%<br class="bbcode-paragraph-br"><br class="bbcode-paragraph-br">f) 大酋长的腰带：角色对boss造成伤害提高比例由100%下调为80%<br class="bbcode-paragraph-br"><br class="bbcode-paragraph-br">g) 乌拉拉棋盘：每1关攻击力提升比例由4%下调为3.5%<br class="bbcode-paragraph-br"><br class="bbcode-paragraph-br">h) 爱心吊坠：每1颗红心提高攻击力比例由10%下调为8%，满红心加成攻击力比例由60%下调为50%<br class="bbcode-paragraph-br"><br
