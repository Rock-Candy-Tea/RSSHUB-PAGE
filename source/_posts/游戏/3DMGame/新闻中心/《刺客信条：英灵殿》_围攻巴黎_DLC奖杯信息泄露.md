<p style="text-indent:2em;">
之前我们报道了《<a target="_blank" href="https://www.3dmgame.com/games/acvalhalla/">刺客信条：英灵殿</a>》“围攻巴黎”DLC或<a href="https://www.3dmgame.com/news/202107/3819627.html" target="_blank"><u>将于</u></a><a href="https://www.3dmgame.com/news/202107/3819627.html" target="_blank"><u>8月5</u></a><a href="https://www.3dmgame.com/news/202107/3819627.html" target="_blank"><u>日正式上线</u></a>的消息。此次该DLC的PS主机版奖杯消息也已经泄露。
</p>
<p align="center">
<img src="https://img.3dmgame.com/uploads/images/news/20210724/1627096480_597965.jpg" alt="《刺客信条：英灵殿》“围攻巴黎”DLC奖杯信息泄露" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"> 
</p>
<p style="text-indent:2em;">

</p>
<p align="center">
<img src="https://img.3dmgame.com/uploads/images/news/20210724/1627096487_217703.png" alt="《刺客信条：英灵殿》“围攻巴黎”DLC奖杯信息泄露" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"> 
</p>
