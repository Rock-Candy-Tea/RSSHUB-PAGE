<div>   
<p style="text-indent:2em;">
国行版Nintendo Switch OLED于北京时间今日（1月4日）上午9点在京东Nintendo Switch官方旗舰店正式开启预购，并将于1月11日正式发售。<span>价格为2599元，预购定金300元。目前有</span><span>“</span><span>白色</span><span>” “</span><span>电光蓝・电光红</span><span>”</span><span>两款配色版本。</span> 
</p>
<p style="text-indent:2em;">
<strong>预购地址：</strong><a href="https://item.jd.com/100031512416.html#" target="_blank"><strong>点击此处</strong></a> 
</p>
<p align="center">
<a href="https://item.jd.com/100031512416.html#" target="_blank"><img src="https://img.3dmgame.com/uploads/images/news/20220104/1641260139_299242.png" alt="国行版Switch OLED今日开启预售 官方价格2599元" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a> 
</p>
<p style="text-indent:2em;">
任天堂Switch OLED（Nintendo Switch OLED），简称NS OLED，是任天堂公司于2021年10月8日发布的主机。采用了7英寸OLED屏幕，设计上提高了屏占比，内部存储由32G提高到64G，搭配新式支架，采用家用机、掌机一体化设计。国行版的Switch OLED在大陆市场依旧是由腾讯代理。
</p>
<p>
<span><strong>套装内含：</strong></span> 
</p>
<p>
·Nintendo Switch<span>（</span><span>OLED</span><span>版）主机</span> 
</p>
<p>
·Joy-Con™(<span>左</span><span>)</span><span>、</span><span>Joy-Con™</span><span>（右）</span> 
</p>
<p>
·*<span>如主机颜色是</span><span>“</span><span>白色</span><span>”</span><span>，</span><span>2</span><span>只</span><span>Joy-Con</span><span>的颜色则同为</span><span>“</span><span>白色</span><span>”</span><span>；如主机颜色是</span><span>“</span><span>电光蓝・电光红</span><span>”</span><span>，则</span><span>Joy-Con</span><span>（左）的颜色是</span><span>“</span><span>电光蓝</span><span>” </span><span>，</span><span>Joy-Con</span><span>（右）的颜色是</span><span>“</span><span>电光红</span><span>”</span><span>。</span> 
</p>
<p>
·Joy-Con<span>握把</span> 
</p>
<p>
·Joy-Con<span>腕带</span> 
</p>
<p>
·Nintendo Switch<span>基座</span> 
</p>
<p>
·Nintendo Switch<span>电源适配器</span> 
</p>
<p>
<span>·高速</span><span>HDMI</span><span>线</span> 
</p>
