<p style="text-indent:2em;">
据Twitter用户Jason Is Steam Decking测试，《赛博朋克2077》在Steam Deck上，Steam OS的性能比Windows 
10快17%。
</p>
<p align="center">
<img src="https://img.3dmgame.com/uploads/images/news/20220312/1647087442_948310.jpg" alt="《赛博朋克2077》Steam Deck测试：Steam OS比Win10快17%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
</p>
<p style="text-indent:2em;">
这次测试采用了预设的低/中等画质，Steam OS上的最低帧数为22.42FPS，而Windows 10上只有7.33，所以Steam 
OS的最低帧数比Windows 10高很多。
</p>
<p align="center">
<img src="https://img.3dmgame.com/uploads/images/news/20220312/1647087450_124607.jpg" alt="《赛博朋克2077》Steam Deck测试：Steam OS比Win10快17%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
