<h2 style="text-align: center;">拍摄当天，化妆的幕后花絮</h2><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">――拍摄的某一天。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　向进入了东京内某家工作室的上坂小姐自我介绍「我是专门做东方的媒体」后，得到了「能与您合作，我十分高兴」的回答，于是就开始了在化妆的间隔中的采访取材。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　首先从换上辉夜的衣服和化妆开始。<br>
</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">　几乎是以素颜登场，在化妆下渐渐变成辉夜的上坂小姐。在她的旁边，我们询问了许多东方相关的回忆。</span></p>
<p><a href="https://edit.touhougarakuta.com/interview/cn_uesakasumire_3">「还有谁记得我那时候的Cosplay吗？」上坂堇的高中时代与例大祭的回忆</a></p>
<p><a href="https://edit.touhougarakuta.com/interview/cn_uesakasumire_4">「再没有其他世界能让二次创作变得如此快乐！」上坂堇漫谈东方，从东方的魅力到弹幕之美</a></p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-11068" src="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/23181532/20200607_us_9271_-1024x683.jpg" alt width="1024" height="683" srcset="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/23181532/20200607_us_9271_-1024x683.jpg 1024w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/23181532/20200607_us_9271_-512x341.jpg 512w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/23181532/20200607_us_9271_-768x512.jpg 768w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/23181532/20200607_us_9271_-1600x1067.jpg 1600w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/23181532/20200607_us_9271_-1568x1045.jpg 1568w" sizes="(max-width: 1024px) 100vw, 1024px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　通过在事前与发型师的交流，为了更好地配合辉夜的印象，做了不少如颜色上的细微调整。<br>
</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">　在化妆中渐渐用上粉色了，变成了能在某些地方感觉到妖艳的辉夜。在一些细节上则由上坂小姐自己化妆，一起制造着辉夜。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　然后，是与平时的上坂印象更加不一样的，蕾米莉亚！<br>
</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">　为了更加还原于是决定戴上红色美瞳，但对平常都是裸眼的上坂小姐，戴上美瞳是一场苦战……！</span></p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-11067" src="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/23181526/20200607_us_0021_-1024x683.jpg" alt width="1024" height="683" srcset="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/23181526/20200607_us_0021_-1024x683.jpg 1024w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/23181526/20200607_us_0021_-512x341.jpg 512w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/23181526/20200607_us_0021_-768x512.jpg 768w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/23181526/20200607_us_0021_-1600x1067.jpg 1600w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/23181526/20200607_us_0021_-1568x1045.jpg 1568w" sizes="(max-width: 1024px) 100vw, 1024px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　工作人员们一起「这样把眼皮抬起来能更容易戴进去哦！」「啊，挺好！能戴进去！」应援，最终终于戴上去了。为了完成这细致的蕾米莉亚，真的非常努力……十分感谢。</span></p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-11066" src="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/23181520/20200607_us_0006_-1024x683.jpg" alt width="1024" height="683" srcset="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/23181520/20200607_us_0006_-1024x683.jpg 1024w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/23181520/20200607_us_0006_-512x341.jpg 512w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/23181520/20200607_us_0006_-768x512.jpg 768w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/23181520/20200607_us_0006_-1600x1067.jpg 1600w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/23181520/20200607_us_0006_-1568x1045.jpg 1568w" sizes="(max-width: 1024px) 100vw, 1024px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　与辉夜相比，蕾米莉亚显然地在配色上更鲜艳，化妆上也注意到了不要被鲜艳的水色假发夺去眼球。假发其实也是事前就精心处理过的宝贵道具。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">「化妆好艳！不过也很可爱」上坂小姐给我们展现出了与平时不一样的表情。</span></p>
<h2 style="text-align: center;">与衣服的初次见面，一切的一切都是上坂堇“专用”</h2><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　在摄影开始的前一个月。<br>
</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">　这是在拍摄前将这两件衣服给上坂堇看的初次见面。</span></p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">「哇，真的是辉夜，超级豪华的衣服啊！　这些全都是制作出来的吗？」</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　与衣服的初次见面，被惊讶到的上坂小姐。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　虽然是根据事前就询问到的身材数据做的，但真穿上之后发现比想象中还要瘦（！），明白了哪些细节需要修正。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　坐下的时候的衣服的扩展的方法，也比预定的要更小。</span></p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">「比想象中的要更重，但是很合身，真的好豪华……我手脚笨，COS服基本上都是在专门店买的，为我专门做衣服真的非常感谢」</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　那也是当然的。本次是为了得到理想的光泽和色感，从布料开始就使尽全力，以上版小姐为印象设计，衣服尺寸也来回调整为上坂小姐特制的专用服装，能收到这样的感谢，我们编辑部真的是感谢无尽……</span></p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-11063" src="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/23181506/IMG_2863_-1024x819.jpg" alt width="1024" height="819" srcset="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/23181506/IMG_2863_-1024x819.jpg 1024w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/23181506/IMG_2863_-512x410.jpg 512w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/23181506/IMG_2863_-768x614.jpg 768w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/23181506/IMG_2863_.jpg 1525w" sizes="(max-width: 1024px) 100vw, 1024px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　实际上这次，不仅衣服，从饰品到手上的小道具都是“为了这次摄影”，从零开始（！）制作的。这一根蓬莱玉枝，也当然是亲手制作的。为制造出幻想的世界观的氛围发挥了重要的作用。</span></p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-9940" src="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/08/12211750/20200607_us_9288_-1024x683.jpg" alt width="1024" height="683" srcset="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/08/12211750/20200607_us_9288_-1024x683.jpg 1024w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/08/12211750/20200607_us_9288_-512x341.jpg 512w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/08/12211750/20200607_us_9288_-768x512.jpg 768w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/08/12211750/20200607_us_9288_-1600x1067.jpg 1600w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/08/12211750/20200607_us_9288_-1568x1045.jpg 1568w" sizes="(max-width: 1024px) 100vw, 1024px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　本次并非想要做完全忠实于原作的COS服，而是想要制作专属于上坂小姐的COS服……抱着这种想法，是否要在服装设计中加入苏联（俄罗斯）主题的设计，在编辑部内多次讨论过。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　顺带一提，蕾米莉亚的服装就是饱含苏联主题而设计出来的。<br>
</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">　在草稿阶段大概就是这么一个状态。</span></p>
<p><img class="aligncenter wp-image-11062 size-medium" src="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/23181504/IMG_2866_-725x1024.jpg" alt width="725" height="1024" srcset="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/23181504/IMG_2866_-725x1024.jpg 725w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/23181504/IMG_2866_-362x512.jpg 362w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/23181504/IMG_2866_-768x1085.jpg 768w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/23181504/IMG_2866_.jpg 906w" sizes="(max-width: 725px) 100vw, 725px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　本次从细节就开始挑选材料，精心制作的两套衣服，是根据上坂小姐的尺寸而专门定做的。因此，在摄影结束后上坂小姐也说想要，就作为礼物送给了本人。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　搞不好在不久的将来，<strong>能在其他地方也看到这两套衣服……也说不好！</strong></span></p>
