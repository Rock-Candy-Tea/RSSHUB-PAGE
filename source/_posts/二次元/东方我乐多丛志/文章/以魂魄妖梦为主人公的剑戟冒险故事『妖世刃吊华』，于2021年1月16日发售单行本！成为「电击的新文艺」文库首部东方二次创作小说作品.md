<img src="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/10235913/top_jinntyouge_book.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><hr><p>正连载于「东方我乐多丛志」的二次创作小说『妖世刃吊华』，已决定通过「电击的新文艺」文库书籍化！</p>
<p>『东方Project二次创作系列 妖世刃吊华 吾爱之地 而今安在』预定于2021年1月16日（周六）发售。</p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-12886" src="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/10235128/yousei_01_cover-illust-1-734x1024.jpg" alt width="734" height="1024" srcset="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/10235128/yousei_01_cover-illust-1-734x1024.jpg 734w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/10235128/yousei_01_cover-illust-1-367x512.jpg 367w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/10235128/yousei_01_cover-illust-1-768x1072.jpg 768w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/10235128/yousei_01_cover-illust-1-1146x1600.jpg 1146w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/10235128/yousei_01_cover-illust-1.jpg 1200w" sizes="(max-width: 734px) 100vw, 734px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>本作是以魂魄妖梦为主人公的剑戟冒险故事。讲述为了拯救幻想乡的灭亡危机，妖梦一边与突如其来的亡灵兵和近代兵器战斗，一边追逐敌方势力魁首的故事。原作中的众多角色纷纷登场，灵活运用各自的能力来帮助妖梦。</p>
<p>现在连载至<a href="https://edit.touhougarakuta.com/novel/youseijintyouge_46">第46回</a>，妖梦已经与战车和战斗机，甚至战舰展开了激烈的战斗。</p>
<p>单行本收录了本作连载的第1回至第21回，并加入了新创作的章节，逐渐揭开可以说是本作最大特色的原创角色「奥斯缇娜」身上的谜团。</p>
<p>封面插画自然也是全新绘制。卷首插画除1张旧视觉图外，还收录了2张新作！</p>
<p>开本为B6开，价格为本体价格1,300日元+税，预定于日本全国书店、通贩网站、电子书商店售卖。</p>
