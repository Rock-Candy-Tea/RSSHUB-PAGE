<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　　这次，<span style="color: #c35330;" data-darkreader-inline-color><strong>本文将以购买和启动Steam版『东方风神录　～ Mountain of Faith.』的流程</strong></span>，进行介绍。</span></p>

<h3><span style="font-weight: 400;">「Steam」是什么？</span></h3>
<p><img class="alignnone size-thumbnail wp-image-24685" src="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/07173439/pic_steam_000-512x441.png" alt width="512" height="441" srcset="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/07173439/pic_steam_000-512x441.png 512w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/07173439/pic_steam_000-768x662.png 768w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/07173439/pic_steam_000.png 975w" sizes="(max-width: 512px) 100vw, 512px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">「Steam」，一个发行各种PC游戏的「游戏平台（简单地说，就是游戏发行网站）」，在2021年2月的时候同时登录人数突破了2600万人，是规模最大的PC游戏平台之一。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　　Steam的特点是，可以在专用软件上购买游戏，并且通过该软件轻松启动游戏。你自己的个人电脑，摇身一变成为游戏机了！</span></p>
<p> </p>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400;">必需品、不需要的物品、最好拥有的物品</span></h3>
<h2 style="text-align: center;">必需品</h2>
<p><span style="color: #5056d2;" data-darkreader-inline-color><strong>个人电脑（笔记本式也行，台式也行）</strong></span></p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-7322" src="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/05211849/kaden_PC-1.png" alt width="500" height="177" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　　东方原作的话，对PC的配置要求并不高。从市场上购买的较新的PC，都可以满足在PC上启动游戏的要求。</span></p>
<blockquote>
<p><strong>最新作『东方虹龙洞　～ Unconnected Marketeers.』所需的配置</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">操作系统：Windows 8.x/10<br>
</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">处理器：Core 2 Duo 以上<br>
</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">内存：1024 MB RAM<br>
</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">显卡：兼容Shader Model 2.0<br>
</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">DirectX：9.0版<br>
</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">声卡：兼容DirectSound<br>
</span></p>
</blockquote>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　　请注意，东方原作是作为「Windows专用软件」而制作的，在MacBook所搭载的操作系统「macOS」上是不能运行的。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　　虽然有些兼容程序可以实现在Mac上运行Windows专用软件，但是在虚拟环境下运行可能会出现难以预料的状况，因此如果说“我一定要在Mac上运行东方！”的话还请自行解决相关的问题。</span></p>
<h2 style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">不需要的物品</span></h2>
<p><span style="color: #5056d2;" data-darkreader-inline-color><strong>信用卡</strong></span></p>
<p><img class="alignnone wp-image-24686 size-thumbnail" src="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/07173604/pic_creditcard-512x316.png" alt width="512" height="316" srcset="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/07173604/pic_creditcard-512x316.png 512w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/07173604/pic_creditcard-768x474.png 768w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/07173604/pic_creditcard.png 800w" sizes="(max-width: 512px) 100vw, 512px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　　在Steam，您无需信用卡也可以购买软件。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　　本文建议的购买方式是<span style="color: #c35330;" data-darkreader-inline-color><strong>「Steam预付卡」</strong></span>。购买一份东方原作的话只需要购买3,000日元的预付卡即可。</span></p>

<h2 style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">最好拥有的物品</span></h2>
<p><span style="color: #5056d2;" data-darkreader-inline-color><strong>可以连接到电脑的手柄</strong></span></p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-7323" src="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/05211849/9e0acea933a2473c1c8f81e171846004.png" alt width="500" height="226" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　　东方原作可以使用键盘进行游戏，但是熟悉任天堂Switch、PS4等家用游戏机的人士，初试键盘却发现玩不惯而感到挫折的话……对于这样的人士，可以尝试使用连接到电脑上的手柄来进行游戏。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br>
</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br>
</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">　　本文推荐的手柄是「<a href="https://gaming.logicool.co.jp/ja-jp/products/gamepads/f310-gamepad.940-000137.html" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Logicool F310游戏手柄</a>」。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　　价格相对便宜，而且同时支持XInput和DirectInput两种数据输入方式，一般也可以在Steam上游玩东方以外的游戏这点值得推荐（不过<span class="VIiyi" lang="zh-CN"><span class="JLqJ4b ChMk0b C1N51c" data-language-for-alternatives="zh-CN" data-language-to-translate-into="ja" data-phrase-index="2" data-number-of-phrases="3"><span class="Q4iAWc">由于价格原因耐用性没有那么高</span></span></span>）。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<h3 style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">在Steam上登录帐户，并购买『东方风神录』</span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　　本次的说明，面向的是没有接触过Steam的人士，或者没有相关帐户的人士。如果是拥有Steam只是还没有购买东方原作，对于这样的人士，请从“3、”开始继续阅读</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>
<h2 style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-weight: 400; color: #5056d2;" data-darkreader-inline-color>１，安装Steam</span></h2>
<blockquote>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://store.steampowered.com/?l=schinese" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><span style="font-weight: 400;">https://store.steampowered.com/?l=schinese</span></a></p>
</blockquote>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　　点击<a href="https://store.steampowered.com/?l=schinese" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Steam主页</a>上的<span style="color: #92ae7d;" data-darkreader-inline-color><strong>「安装Steam」</strong></span>按钮，然后点击</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="color: #5056d2;" data-darkreader-inline-color>蓝色的按钮「安装Steam」</span>。</span></p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-24688" src="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/07174334/pic_steam_001.png" alt width="980" height="168" srcset="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/07174334/pic_steam_001.png 980w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/07174334/pic_steam_001-512x88.png 512w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/07174334/pic_steam_001-768x132.png 768w" sizes="(max-width: 980px) 100vw, 980px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-24689" src="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/07174357/pic_steam_002.png" alt width="953" height="611" srcset="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/07174357/pic_steam_002.png 953w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/07174357/pic_steam_002-512x328.png 512w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/07174357/pic_steam_002-768x492.png 768w" sizes="(max-width: 953px) 100vw, 953px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">下载「SteamSetup.exe」后，启动<strong>SteamSetup.exe</strong>。</span></p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-24690" src="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/07174407/pic_steam_003.png" alt width="578" height="353" srcset="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/07174407/pic_steam_003.png 578w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/07174407/pic_steam_003-512x313.png 512w" sizes="(max-width: 578px) 100vw, 578px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　　启动后将开启Steam的设置向导。根据其内容，点击<strong>「下一步」</strong>。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　　此时可以修改安装位置等内容，不熟悉相关操作的人士可以保持默认设定并点击「下一步」。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br>
<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-24691" src="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/07174423/pic_steam_004.png" alt width="579" height="357" srcset="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/07174423/pic_steam_004.png 579w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/07174423/pic_steam_004-512x316.png 512w" sizes="(max-width: 579px) 100vw, 579px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　　这个画面标志着Steam安装完毕了。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>　　在「□运行Steam」上勾选为☑并点击「完成」</strong>后，Steam即可启动。</span></p>
<h2 style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-weight: 400; color: #5056d2;" data-darkreader-inline-color>２，登录帐户</span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　　在Steam启动之后，首先建立一个Steam帐户。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">在启动画面中点击<strong>「创建一个新的帐户」</strong>。</span></p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-24692" src="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/07174452/pic_steam_005.png" alt width="478" height="326" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　　在弹出的帐户创建窗口中，<strong>输入登录用的电子邮箱地址后</strong>点击<strong>「继续」</strong>。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　　对于不在个人电脑上使用电子邮箱地址的人士，您可以趁此机会创建一个电子邮箱地址（Gmail等）。</span></p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-24693" src="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/07174501/pic_steam_006.png" alt width="710" height="860" srcset="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/07174501/pic_steam_006.png 710w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/07174501/pic_steam_006-423x512.png 423w" sizes="(max-width: 710px) 100vw, 710px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　　在您登录电子邮箱地址后会收到一封名为「新Steam帐户的电子邮件确认」的电子邮件。打开电子邮件后<strong>点击</strong><strong>「<span class="VIiyi" lang="zh-CN"><span class="JLqJ4b ChMk0b C1N51c" data-language-for-alternatives="zh-CN" data-language-to-translate-into="ja" data-phrase-index="1" data-number-of-phrases="2"><span class="Q4iAWc">确认电子邮件地址</span></span></span>」</strong>。</span></p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-24695" src="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/07174548/pic_steam_009.png" alt width="861" height="744" srcset="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/07174548/pic_steam_009.png 861w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/07174548/pic_steam_009-512x442.png 512w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/07174548/pic_steam_009-768x664.png 768w" sizes="(max-width: 861px) 100vw, 861px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"> <img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-24696" src="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/07174549/pic_steam_010.png" alt width="971" height="319" srcset="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/07174549/pic_steam_010.png 971w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/07174549/pic_steam_010-512x168.png 512w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/07174549/pic_steam_010-768x252.png 768w" sizes="(max-width: 971px) 100vw, 971px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　　确认电子邮件地址后，<strong>接下来就是创建帐户名称和密码</strong>了。输入您喜欢的帐户名称，以及密码吧</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span></p>

<h3><span style="font-weight: 400;">在Steam看设定文档！</span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　　有很多人说“安装原作之后，我想看的是<strong>东方角色设定和故事梗概，以及ZUN先生所写的<span style="color: #5056d2;" data-darkreader-inline-color>「omake.txt」「<ruby>キャラ<rt></rt></ruby>設定.txt」</span></strong>。但是</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">、在Steam上就不知道怎么看了……！”。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">在Steam上想要看「omake.txt」的话、</span></p>
<p><strong>・「库」打开软件界面<br>
・点击右侧的「⚙」<br>
・将鼠标放在「管理」上，然后点击「浏览本地文件」</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　　<span class="VIiyi" lang="zh-CN"><span class="JLqJ4b ChMk0b C1N51c" data-language-for-alternatives="zh-CN" data-language-to-translate-into="ja" data-phrase-index="0" data-number-of-phrases="1"><span class="Q4iAWc">这样，您就可以打开安装了东方原作的文件夹了。</span></span></span></span></p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">　　在弹出的文件夹中可以看到「omake.txt」「キャラ設定.txt」了！接下来就</span><span class="VIiyi" lang="zh-CN"><span class="JLqJ4b ChMk0b" data-language-for-alternatives="zh-CN" data-language-to-translate-into="ja" data-phrase-index="1" data-number-of-phrases="2"><span class="Q4iAWc">让我们尽情品味ZUN先生的文字吧。</span></span></span></p>
