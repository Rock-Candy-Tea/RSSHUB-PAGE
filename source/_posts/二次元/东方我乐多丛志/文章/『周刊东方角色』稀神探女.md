<blockquote>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>「东方」，<span style="color: #d24519;">到底要怎么了解</span>？</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>「○○○○」<span style="color: #d24519;">是什么角色</span>？</strong></p>
</blockquote>
<p>我认识不少人都提出过这样的问题，这个问题可以说越了解东方反而越难回答。这种问题到底要怎么回答才好……原作的设定当然要说，但也想要传达出从中发祥出来的种种创作的魅力……！这就是<strong>「从二次创作谈角色」图鉴</strong>这一栏目的设立理由！</p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>「周刊东方角色」</strong>指的是、<br>
<strong>从二次创作谈角色“很</strong><span style="color: #d24519;">东方我乐多<span style="color: #d3c57d;">丛</span>志</span><strong>”</strong><strong>的角色图鉴</strong>。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">本次要谈的角色，是<strong>稀神探女</strong>！</span></p>
<h2 style="text-align: center;">『周刊东方角色』　稀神探女</h2><p><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-17754" src="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2021/07/09110114/chara_sagume-709x1024.jpg" alt width="709" height="1024" srcset="https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2021/07/09110114/chara_sagume-709x1024.jpg 709w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2021/07/09110114/chara_sagume-354x512.jpg 354w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2021/07/09110114/chara_sagume-768x1110.jpg 768w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2021/07/09110114/chara_sagume-1107x1600.jpg 1107w, https://s3-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/touhougarakuta-statics/putfykzd/wp-content/uploads/2021/07/09110114/chara_sagume-1568x2266.jpg 1568w" sizes="(max-width: 709px) 100vw, 709px" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p><strong>稀神探女</strong>，是于东方Project第15弹『<a href="https://store.steampowered.com/app/937580/__Legacy_of_Lunatic_Kingdom/"><strong>东方绀珠传</strong><strong>　～</strong><strong> Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom.</strong></a>』作为4面BOSS登场的角色。拥有「从口中说出便可使事态逆转程度的能力」。</p>
<p>有着「不管是任何事态或者事件，只要说出口就会使事态逆转」非常难以理解的能力。和前一作中登场的天邪鬼的能力又不一样，有着非常像月之民的违规级别的能力。</p>
