<h2 data-id="heading-0">1. hover 图片放大缩小</h2>
<p><img src="https://p6-juejin.byteimg.com/tos-cn-i-k3u1fbpfcp/04d473ef9a3a4ff38f730820d74fc841~tplv-k3u1fbpfcp-watermark.image" alt="001-图片缩放效果.gif" loading="lazy" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h3 data-id="heading-1">方法1 ：在鼠标hover上去的时候，使用transform的scale()来缩放图片。</h3>
<pre><code class="copyable"> <style>
    div &#123;
        width: 400px;
        height: 300px;
        overflow: hidden;
    &#125;

    img &#123;
        width: 100%;
        height: 100%;
        transition: all .5s;
    &#125;

    div:hover img &#123;
        transform: scale(1.2);
    &#125;
</style>

 
 <div>
    <img src="./images/1.jpg" alt="">
 </div>
<span class="copy-code-btn">复制代码</span></code></pre>
<h3 data-id="heading-2">方法2：将图片设置为背景图片，鼠标hover上去的时候，调整背景大小background-size，这里要把背景位置设置为居中（background-position:center center）。</h3>
<pre><code class="copyable">  <style>
    div &#123;
        width: 400px;
        height: 300px;
        background: url(./images/1.jpg) no-repeat;
        background-size: 100% 100%;
        background-position: center;
        transition: all .5s;
    &#125;

    div:hover &#123;
        background-size: 135% 135%;
    &#125;
</style>

<p><img src="https://p3-juejin.byteimg.com/tos-cn-i-k3u1fbpfcp/cb176ef96e2a4562a75121298062433d~tplv-k3u1fbpfcp-watermark.image" alt="002-hover显示二维码.gif" loading="lazy" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>

<h2 data-id="heading-5">3. 图片hover上去会变暗</h2>
<p><img src="https://p6-juejin.byteimg.com/tos-cn-i-k3u1fbpfcp/abf9c918a75342dda25d3f727626ad71~tplv-k3u1fbpfcp-watermark.image" alt="003-图片hover上去会变暗.gif" loading="lazy" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h3 data-id="heading-6">方法1：实现给装图片的盒子设置一个背景色（比如黑色），图片被hover的时候，加大透明度（opacity），图片就会透出后面的背景色。</h3>
<pre><code class="copyable">  <style>
    .box &#123;
        width: 300px;
        height: 300px;
        background-color: #000;
    &#125;

    .box img &#123;
        width: 100%;
        height: 100%;
        transition: all 0.5s;
    &#125;

    .box:hover img &#123;
        opacity: 0.5;
    &#125;
</style>
