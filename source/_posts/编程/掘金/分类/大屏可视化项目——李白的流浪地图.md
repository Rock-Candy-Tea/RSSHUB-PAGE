<h2 data-id="heading-0">适配屏幕</h2>
<h3 data-id="heading-1">算法</h3>
<p>Wp为页面有效宽度，Hp为页面有效高度。</p>
<p><img src="https://p9-juejin.byteimg.com/tos-cn-i-k3u1fbpfcp/6e6cfa9a280a4cd4b50705e15cfb3185~tplv-k3u1fbpfcp-watermark.image" alt="image.png" loading="lazy" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>页面左右居中，上下居中。</p>
<p>若页面<code>宽/高</code>＞<code>16/9</code>，就左右留白。若页面<code>宽/高</code>＜<code>16/9</code>，就上下留白。</p>
