<h1 data-id="heading-0">8月3日</h1>
<p>8月3日，我们向社区同步了Vue DevUI的最新进展：</p>
<p><a href="https://juejin.cn/post/6992233443585163300" target="_blank" title="https://juejin.cn/post/6992233443585163300">Vue DevUI 已经有10个组件成员啦～🥳😋</a></p>
<p>第二天，我们的<a href="https://link.juejin.cn/?target=https%3A%2F%2Fdevui.design%2F" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer" title="https://devui.design/" ref="nofollow noopener noreferrer">官网</a>访问量就达到历史最高的峰值：<code>20213</code></p>
<p>第三天一早，<a href="https://link.juejin.cn/?target=https%3A%2F%2Fgitee.com%2Fzzzautumn" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer" title="https://gitee.com/zzzautumn" ref="nofollow noopener noreferrer">Lucky</a>小姐姐就提交了新组件的<a href="https://link.juejin.cn/?target=https%3A%2F%2Fgitee.com%2Fdevui%2Fvue-devui%2Fpulls%2F14" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer" title="https://gitee.com/devui/vue-devui/pulls/14" ref="nofollow noopener noreferrer">PR</a></p>
<p>一周之内，<a href="https://link.juejin.cn/?target=https%3A%2F%2Fgitee.com%2Fdevui%2Fvue-devui" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer" title="https://gitee.com/devui/vue-devui" ref="nofollow noopener noreferrer">Vue DevUI</a>新增<code>109</code>颗star🌟，微信群新增<code>153</code>名成员，诞生了<code>30</code>位新田主，新增了<code>9</code>名组件成员。</p>
<p><img src="https://p1-juejin.byteimg.com/tos-cn-i-k3u1fbpfcp/4dfaa41af15748f08fdb1bd940f71723~tplv-k3u1fbpfcp-watermark.image" alt="vue devui star.png" loading="lazy" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h1 data-id="heading-1">8月9日</h1>
<p>8月9日，DevUI 12 版本正式发布，升级了最新的Angular 12，全面拥抱Ivy新引擎。</p>
<p>这次发版我们使用了去年掘金年度打榜活动获得的推广位，8月10日在掘金首页停留了一天，不知道小伙伴们有没有看到呢😋。</p>
<p><img src="https://p3-juejin.byteimg.com/tos-cn-i-k3u1fbpfcp/375c8161e9e94a7596b07666a72419df~tplv-k3u1fbpfcp-watermark.image" alt="掘金推广位.png" loading="lazy" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p><a href="https://juejin.cn/post/6994376794073727007" target="_blank" title="https://juejin.cn/post/6994376794073727007">📧叮~你有一份 DevUI 12 新版本待查收~😋</a></p>
<p>第二天，<a href="https://link.juejin.cn/?target=https%3A%2F%2Fdevui.design%2F" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer" title="https://devui.design/" ref="nofollow noopener noreferrer">DevUI</a>官网访问量再次达到峰值：<code>15088</code></p>
<p>一周内，<a href="https://link.juejin.cn/?target=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FDevCloudFE%2Fng-devui" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer" title="https://github.com/DevCloudFE/ng-devui" ref="nofollow noopener noreferrer">NG DevUI</a>新增<code>87</code>颗star🌟，此时star数已经到<code>972</code>。</p>
<h1 data-id="heading-2">8月17日</h1>
<p>8月17日，<a href="https://link.juejin.cn/?target=https%3A%2F%2Fdevui.design%2Fadmin-page%2Fhome" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer" title="https://devui.design/admin-page/home" ref="nofollow noopener noreferrer">DevUI Admin 2.0</a>重磅发布！推出了一项黑科技：可以通过CLI命令，快速增加区块。</p>
