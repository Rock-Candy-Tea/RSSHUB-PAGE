<h3 data-id="heading-1">一、下载文件显示找不到页面</h3>
<h4 data-id="heading-2">1、定位问题</h4>
<p>查看需要下载的文件静态资源是否乱码，在windows系统下eclipse编码是默认GBK的编码格式，linux上默认为默认语言的编码格式。所以windows上传文件到Linux服务器会被转译，也就是我们看到的乱码,去下载文件的时候就会提示找不到页面。</p>
<p><img src="https://p1-juejin.byteimg.com/tos-cn-i-k3u1fbpfcp/d29680d407cd46119cefa9ee68d12e5c~tplv-k3u1fbpfcp-watermark.image" alt="image.png" loading="lazy" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 data-id="heading-3">2、查看自己是用的Xftp工具还是SecureFX</h4>
<h4 data-id="heading-4">3、Xftp工具解决方案</h4>
<h6 data-id="heading-5">步骤一：打开 Xftp软件，在菜单栏单击[文件]菜单，在其下拉菜单选择[属性]，如下图所示。</h6>
<p><img src="https://p9-juejin.byteimg.com/tos-cn-i-k3u1fbpfcp/f0ab8238ccb642659f30fb9e820b5db7~tplv-k3u1fbpfcp-watermark.image" alt="image.png" loading="lazy" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>或者单击软件主界面快捷工具栏中的属性按钮(带齿轮的)，从而来打开默认会话属性对话框。</p>
<p><img src="https://p6-juejin.byteimg.com/tos-cn-i-k3u1fbpfcp/0ab113bd1bca411a8ca32cb8ebb0f26a~tplv-k3u1fbpfcp-watermark.image" alt="image.png" loading="lazy" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h6 data-id="heading-6">步骤二：执行以上操作后，就会打开默认会话属性对话框，选择“选项”选项卡，勾选“使用UTF-8编码”，如下图所示。</h6>
<p><img src="https://p6-juejin.byteimg.com/tos-cn-i-k3u1fbpfcp/140d4f65f52c4f9a9a83c5ba838cfb0f~tplv-k3u1fbpfcp-watermark.image" alt="image.png" loading="lazy" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h6 data-id="heading-7">步骤三：重新上传文件。</h6>
<h4 data-id="heading-8">4、SecureFX工具解决方案</h4>
<h6 data-id="heading-9">步骤一：打开SecureFX，找到含有中文名的路径，查看还有中文名的文件，确认被转译过。</h6>
<p><img src="https://p3-juejin.byteimg.com/tos-cn-i-k3u1fbpfcp/e3207aabf12746ac96b56e0c61d21812~tplv-k3u1fbpfcp-watermark.image" alt="image.png" loading="lazy" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h6 data-id="heading-10">步骤二：在SecureFX菜单中选择“Options”-"Global Options…"。</h6>
<p><img src="https://p1-juejin.byteimg.com/tos-cn-i-k3u1fbpfcp/3981b94746b4470eb8695f7a2b3aa41a~tplv-k3u1fbpfcp-watermark.image" alt="image.png" loading="lazy" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h6 data-id="heading-11">步骤三：在“Global Options”窗口中，选择“General”-"Configuration Path"，找到SecureFX配置文件所在的路径，并复制该路径。</h6>
<p><img src="https://p1-juejin.byteimg.com/tos-cn-i-k3u1fbpfcp/7a1d51d7e15a4a3e9b1c4931d8067890~tplv-k3u1fbpfcp-watermark.image" alt="image.png" loading="lazy" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h6 data-id="heading-12">步骤四：Win+E打开文件管理器，在地址栏中粘贴SecureFX配置文件所在的路径，进入到该目录中，选择“Sessions”。</h6>
<p><img src="https://p9-juejin.byteimg.com/tos-cn-i-k3u1fbpfcp/cbe3643fd46a4a3391b31211fa81e9a5~tplv-k3u1fbpfcp-watermark.image" alt="image.png" loading="lazy" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h6 data-id="heading-13">步骤五：在“Sessions”中找到链接地址的ini文件，并用文本编辑器打开。</h6>
<p><img src="https://p3-juejin.byteimg.com/tos-cn-i-k3u1fbpfcp/ab9f3b798c584e25b8fb68f2ccfd95ec~tplv-k3u1fbpfcp-watermark.image" alt="image.png" loading="lazy" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h6 data-id="heading-14">步骤六：在打开的ini文件中，查找：Filenames Always Use UTF8。</h6>
<p><img src="https://p1-juejin.byteimg.com/tos-cn-i-k3u1fbpfcp/b4955689a2264999875eab0a2e9332d8~tplv-k3u1fbpfcp-watermark.image" alt="image.png" loading="lazy" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h6 data-id="heading-15">步骤七：将Filenames Always Use UTF8后面的值修改为：00000001，保存退出。</h6>
<p><img src="https://p9-juejin.byteimg.com/tos-cn-i-k3u1fbpfcp/cbe29cc68da348d69f6919de63b84559~tplv-k3u1fbpfcp-watermark.image" alt="image.png" loading="lazy" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h6 data-id="heading-16">步骤八：再次打开SecureFX，进入到含有中文名称的文件目录中，可以看到含有中文名称的文件已经能够正常显示了。</h6>
