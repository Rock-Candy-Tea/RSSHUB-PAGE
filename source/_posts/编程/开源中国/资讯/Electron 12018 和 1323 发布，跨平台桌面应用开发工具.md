                                                                                            <p>Electron 12.0.18 和 13.2.3 现已发布，主要更新内容如下： </p> 
<p><strong>v12.0.18：</strong></p> 
<p><strong>Fixes</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>修复了<code>webView</code>中的一个问题，即如果用户用<code>esc</code>c而不是点击进入<code>webView</code>退出全屏，则不会发出<code>leave-html-full-screen</code>事件。 <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F30669" target="_blank">#30669</a></li> 
</ul> 
<p><strong>Other Changes</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>Security：1196853 的反向移植修复。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F30581" target="_blank">#30581</a></li> 
 <li>Security：1227933 的反向移植修复。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F30615" target="_blank">#30615</a></li> 
 <li>Security：1229298 的反向移植修复。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F30580" target="_blank">#30580</a></li> 
</ul> 
<p>更新说明：<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Freleases%2Ftag%2Fv12.0.18" target="_blank">https://github.com/electron/electron/releases/tag/v12.0.18</a></p> 
<p><strong>v13.2.3：</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>修复了如果<code>items</code>是空数组，当<code>showArrowButtons</code>被启用时， TouchBarScrubber 会崩溃的问题。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F30680" target="_blank">＃30680 </a></li> 
 <li>修复了在简单全屏模式下标题栏的显示。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F30706" target="_blank">＃30706 </a></li> 
</ul>
