<p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><span style="color:#333333">更新内容：</span></p> 
<p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><strong>v5.3.1</strong></p> 
<ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
 <li>优化 
  <ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
   <li>Calendar 如果有默认的选中值，那么现在会在组件创建时，自动跳转到选中日期的当月<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fant-design%2Fant-design-mobile%2Fcommit%2F7f289aa6921b743c43e65f697e5330e4abdf0a2d" target="_blank">7f289aa</a></li> 
  </ul> </li> 
 <li>修复 
  <ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
   <li>修复了 Avatar 的<span> </span><code>fit</code><span> </span>属性没有生效的问题<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fant-design%2Fant-design-mobile%2Fpull%2F4872" target="_blank">#4872</a> </li> 
  </ul> </li> 
</ul> 
<p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start">更新公告：<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fant-design%2Fant-design-mobile%2Freleases%2Ftag%2Fv5.3.1" target="_blank">https://github.com/ant-design/ant-design-mobile/releases/tag/v5.3.1</a></p> 
<p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><strong>v5.4.0</strong></p> 
<ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
 <li>特性 
  <ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
   <li>TextArea 增加了<span> </span><code>--text-align</code><span> </span><code>--count-text-align</code><span> </span>CSS 变量<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fant-design%2Fant-design-mobile%2Fpull%2F4875" target="_blank">#4875</a></li> 
   <li>FloatingBubble 增加了<span> </span><code>axis</code><span> </span>和<span> </span><code>magnetic</code><span> </span>属性，支持沿多种方向拖动和磁吸效果<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fant-design%2Fant-design-mobile%2Fcommit%2F81d7fa7ff9e32f4e281d177c5326d8e3b4428124" target="_blank">81d7fa7</a></li> 
  </ul> </li>

</ul>
