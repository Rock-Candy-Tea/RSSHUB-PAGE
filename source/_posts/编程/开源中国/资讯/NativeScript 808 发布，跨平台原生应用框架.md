                                                        <p>NativeScript 8.0.8 现已发布。NativeScript 使用户能够直接从 JavaScript 访问 native API。目前，该框架为丰富的移动开发提供了 iOS 和 Android 运行时，并可用于多种不同的用例。</p> 
<p>此版本主要更新内容如下：</p> 
<p><strong>Bug 修复</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>停止 leaking style scopes（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FNativeScript%2FNativeScript%2Fissues%2F9444" target="_blank">#9444</a>）（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FNativeScript%2FNativeScript%2Fcommit%2Fb8d8110994ecb5a882e89ae10e7b13070ae4d709" target="_blank">b8d8110</a>），closes <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FNativeScript%2FNativeScript%2Fissues%2F9311" target="_blank">#9311</a></li> 
 <li>android：accessibilityIdentifier（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FNativeScript%2FNativeScript%2Fissues%2F9432" target="_blank">#9432</a>）（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FNativeScript%2FNativeScript%2Fcommit%2F9f582ba168dd6f4366e2591a9fed10e1c2443ca4" target="_blank">9f582ba</a>）</li> 
</ul>
