<div class="content">
                                                                                            <p>Zotero 是一个参考文献管理软件，也是一个免费 Firefox 和 Google 浏览器扩展。Zotero 可以协助用户收集、管理及引用研究资源，包括期刊、书籍等各类文献和网页、图片等。</p> 
<p style="color:#333333; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:left">Zotero 6.0 现已发布，这是 Zotero 历史上最大的一次升级；引入了一系列新功能来改变你做研究的方式，包括一种处理 PDF 和笔记的全新方式。具体更新内容如下：</p> 
<p style="text-align:left"><strong><span><span><span><span><span><span style="color:#222222"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#ffffff"><span><span><span>PDF 阅读器和新的 </span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span>Note Editor</strong></p> 
<p style="text-align:left"><img alt height="326" src="https://oscimg.oschina.net/oscnet/up-138c8e1e0ffee131e7511ecd0cc713d0ff2.jpg" width="500" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p> 
<p style="text-align:left"><span><span><span><span style="color:#222222"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#ffffff"><span><span><span>使用 Zotero 6，你现在可以：</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>在 Zotero 主窗口的新内置阅读器中，在新的选项卡式界面中打开 PDF</li> 
 <li>在查看项目的 PDF 时清理项目的元数据</li> 
 <li>使用高亮、注释和图像注释标记 PDF</li> 
 <li>在功能强大的新笔记编辑器中，使用自动引用向 Zotero <span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#222222">notes </span>添加注释</li> 
 <li>使用 Zotero 熟悉的引文对话框直接在笔记中引用其他项目</li> 
 <li>在你的 Word、LibreOffice 和 Google Docs 文档中插入带有活跃 Zotero 引文的注释，以自动生成参考书目</li> 
 <li>将注释导出到外部 Markdown 编辑器，并带有指向 Zotero 项目和 PDF 的链接</li> 
</ul> 
<p style="text-align:left"><span><span><span><span style="color:#222222"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#ffffff"><span><span><span>结合 Zotero 强大的保存功能和文字处理器插件，Zotero 现在可以帮助你完成从在线有趣项目到最终文档中引用的引用的整个路径。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p> 
<p style="text-align:left"><strong><span><span><span><span><span><span style="color:#222222"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#ffffff"><span><span><span>无缝注释同步</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></strong></p> 
<p style="text-align:left"><span><span><span><span style="color:#222222"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#ffffff"><span><span><span>与所有 Zotero 库数据一样，你创建的注释将在所有 Zotero 设备之间自动同步。无需在一台计算机上关闭 PDF，也无需担心由于同步冲突而丢失注释。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span><span><span><span><span style="color:#222222"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#ffffff"><span><span><span>除了注释自己的文件，你还可以在群组库中创建 PDF 注释，让你与其他群组成员协作标记 PDF 并立即看到彼此的更改。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p> 
<p style="text-align:left"><span><span><span><span style="color:#222222"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#ffffff"><span><span><span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fapps.apple.com%2Fus%2Fapp%2Fzotero%2Fid1513554812" target="_blank">Zotero 的 PDF 阅读器与新的Zotero iOS 应用程序</a>同步，允许你在 iPad 或 iPhone 上突出显示和做笔记，然后当你回到电脑前时快速将这些注释拉入笔记或文字处理器文档。你甚至可以查看使用 Apple Pencil 进行的墨迹注释。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p> 
<p style="text-align:left"><span><span><span><span style="color:#222222"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#ffffff"><span><span><span>无论你上次使用什么设备，PDF 都会自动打开到你的当前页面。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p> 
<p style="text-align:left"><strong><span><span><span><span><span><span style="color:#222222"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#ffffff"><span><span><span>Annotations in Notes</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></strong></p> 
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span style="color:#222222"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#ffffff"><span><span><span><img alt height="300" src="https://oscimg.oschina.net/oscnet/up-c8d94127489f052a6ea1206c96817da1203.png" width="310" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p> 
<p style="text-align:left"><span><span><span><span style="color:#222222"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#ffffff"><span><span><span>标记 PDF 后，你可以快速<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.zotero.org%2Fsupport%2Fpdf_reader%23adding_annotations_to_notes" target="_blank">将部分或全部注释添加到 Zotero note</a>。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p> 
<p style="text-align:left"><span><span><span><span style="color:#222222"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#ffffff"><span><span><span>添加到笔记中的注释不仅仅是纯文本：它们包括有关 source PDF 的信息，让你快速跳回原始页面查看上下文，使用 Zotero 的文字处理器插件生成参考书目条目，切换注释的颜色，在导出到 Markdown 时包括返回 Zotero 的链接，甚至恢复意外删除的引文以确保你已经正确归属了一个引文。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p> 
<p style="text-align:left"><span><span><span><span style="color:#222222"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#ffffff"><span><span><span>使用 <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.zotero.org%2Fsupport%2Fnote_templates" target="_blank">note templates</a>，你可以自定义添加的注释格式。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p> 
<p style="text-align:left"><span><span><span><span style="color:#222222"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#ffffff"><span><span><span>如果你之前使用 ZotFile 扩展将 PDF 注释提取到 Zotero notes，你现在可以直接使用 Zotero 并从这些新功能中受益。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p> 
<p style="text-align:left"><strong><span><span><span><span style="color:#222222"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#ffffff"><span><span><span>Adding Notes to Word Processors</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></strong></p> 
<p style="text-align:left"><strong><span><span><span><span style="color:#222222"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#ffffff"><span><span><span><img alt height="136" src="https://oscimg.oschina.net/oscnet/up-76970df6ffeed34cc83c911d69727888261.png" width="300" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></strong></p> 
<p style="text-align:left"><span><span><span><span style="color:#222222"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#ffffff"><span><span><span>使用 Zotero 文字处理器插件之一时，新的“Add Note”按钮可让你将任何 Zotero note 直接插入到文档中。Note 中的引用，包括那些从 PDF 注释生成的引用，将保持活动状态，因此它们将自动添加到你的参考书目中。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p> 
<p style="text-align:left"><strong>Markdown Export</strong></p> 
<p style="text-align:left"><strong><img alt height="58" src="https://oscimg.oschina.net/oscnet/up-b608926e3f064cfefffbf2abcd3961127eb.png" width="500" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></strong></p> 
<p style="text-align:left">Notes 现在有他们自己的快速复制设置，可以选择 Markdown+Rich Text 或原始 HTML。你<span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#222222">可以将项目列表中的注释，甚至是 PDF 阅读器中的单个注释直接复制或拖动到外部编辑器，并使用 Markdown 链接返回与注释关联的项目和 PDF 页面。</span></p> 
<p style="text-align:left"><strong>Embedded Images in Notes</strong></p> 
<p style="text-align:left"><span><span><span><span style="color:#222222"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#ffffff"><span><span><span>Zotero notes 现在支持嵌入图像。你可以从计算机或 Web 中拖入图像，它们会同步到你的其他设备。你甚至可以在 PDF 阅读器中创建图像注释并将其直接拖到注释中以在文档中创建引用的图像。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
