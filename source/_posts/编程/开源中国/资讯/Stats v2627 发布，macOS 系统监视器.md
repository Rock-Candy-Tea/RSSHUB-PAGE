                                                                                            <p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><span style="color:#333333">Stats 是一款开源的 macOS 系统监控工具，可以驻留在菜单栏中对 CPU、GPU、内存、磁盘和网络等进行实时监测。</span></p> 
<p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><span style="color:#333333">目前 Stats </span>v2.6.27<span style="color:#333333"><span> </span>发布了 ，此版本带来如下变更:</span></p> 
<h3 style="margin-left:.6em; margin-right:0; text-align:start">关闭 I<strong>ssues</strong></h3> 
<ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
 <li>feat:<span> </span><span style="color:#2e3033">增加了电流传感器</span><span> </span>(<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fexelban%2Fstats%2Fissues%2F766" target="_blank">#766</a>)</li> 
 <li>feat:<span> </span><span style="color:#2e3033">将 kill 进程添加到顶部进程，仅在进程被锁定时可用。</span><span> </span>(<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fexelban%2Fstats%2Fissues%2F569" target="_blank">#569</a>,<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fexelban%2Fstats%2Fissues%2F764" target="_blank">#764</a>)</li> 
 <li>feat:<span> </span><span style="color:#2e3033">增加了网络小部件的下载/上传颜色选择器。</span>(<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fexelban%2Fstats%2Fissues%2F772" target="_blank">#772</a>)</li> 
 <li>fix:<span> </span><span style="color:#2e3033">试图修复应用程序崩溃时，停止电池的顶部进程 reader</span><span> </span>(<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fexelban%2Fstats%2Fissues%2F781" target="_blank">#781</a>)</li> 
</ul> 
<h2 style="margin-left:.6em; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><strong>Bug 修复与新特性</strong></h2> 
<ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
 <li>feat:<span> </span><span style="color:#2e3033">增加了一个锁定顶部进程的选项</span></li> 
 <li>feat:<span> </span><span style="color:#2e3033">在没有图标的顶部进程中添加默认图标</span></li> 
</ul> 
<h2 style="margin-left:.6em; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><strong>本地化</strong></h2> 
<ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0">
