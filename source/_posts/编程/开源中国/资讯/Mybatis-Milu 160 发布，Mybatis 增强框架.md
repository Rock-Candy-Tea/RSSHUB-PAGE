                                                                                            <p>Mybatis-Milu 1.6.0发布，更新内容</p> 
<p>新特性</p> 
<ul> 
 <li><span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#40485b">增加逻辑删除通用接口</span> 
  <ul> 
   <li>logicDeleteById</li> 
   <li>logicDeleteByIds</li> 
   <li>logicDeleteByCriteria</li> 
   <li>logicDeleteByLambdaCriteria</li> 
   <li>resumeLogicDeleteById</li> 
   <li>resumeLogicDeleteByIds</li> 
   <li>resumeLogicDeleteByCriteria</li> 
   <li>resumeLogicDeleteByLambdaCriteria</li> 
  </ul> </li> 
</ul> 
<p><span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#40485b">优化与修复</span></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>修正乐观锁值会被重置的问题</li> 
 <li>优化自动化配置</li> 
</ul> 
<p>Mybatis-Milu是在Mybatis的基础上，<span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#40485b">遵循JPA注解规范，参考Spring-Data-Jpa实现的增强型框架，极大提高了数据库操作的便捷性，又保留了Mybatis的灵活性，学习成本低。</span></p>
