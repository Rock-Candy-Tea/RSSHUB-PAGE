                                                                                            <p>Traefik 2.4.8 现已发布，该版本更新内容如下：</p> 
<p><strong>Bug fixes：</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li><strong>[acme]</strong> 更新 go-acme/lego 至 v4.3.1（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Ftraefik%2Ftraefik%2Fpull%2F7980" target="_blank">＃7980</a>）</li> 
 <li><strong>[acme]</strong> 更新 go-acme/lego 至 v4.3.0（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Ftraefik%2Ftraefik%2Fpull%2F7975" target="_blank">＃7975</a>由<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fldez" target="_blank">ldez</a>）</li> 
 <li><strong>[k8s,k8s/gatewayapi]</strong>  更新到 gateway-api v0.2.0（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Ftraefik%2Ftraefik%2Fpull%2F7943" target="_blank">＃7943</a>）</li> 
 <li><strong>[pilot,webui]</strong> 在 Traefik 仪表板中添加一个选项来（取消）激活 Pilot 集成（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Ftraefik%2Ftraefik%2Fpull%2F7994" target="_blank">＃7994</a>）</li> 
 <li><strong>[rules]</strong> 对路由器规则中的非 ASCII 域名进行报错（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Ftraefik%2Ftraefik%2Fpull%2F7986" target="_blank">＃7986</a>）</li> 
 <li><strong>[server]</strong> 更新pires/go-proxyproto 至 v0.5.0（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Ftraefik%2Ftraefik%2Fpull%2F7948" target="_blank">＃7948</a>）</li> 
</ul> 
<p><strong>Documentation:</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li><strong>[middleware]</strong> 改进基本认证中间件 httpasswd 示例（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Ftraefik%2Ftraefik%2Fpull%2F7992" target="_blank">＃7992</a>）</li> 
 <li><strong>[middleware]</strong> 在示例配置中添加缺失的<code>traefik.</code>前缀（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Ftraefik%2Ftraefik%2Fpull%2F7990" target="_blank">＃7990</a>）</li> 
 <li><strong>[middleware]</strong> 删除不再需要的 note（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Ftraefik%2Ftraefik%2Fpull%2F7979" target="_blank">＃7979</a>）</li> 
</ul>
