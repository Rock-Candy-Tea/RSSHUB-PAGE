                                                                                            <p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><span style="color:#333333">Laravel v8.69.0 已发布，主要更新如下：</span></p> 
<h3 style="margin-left:.6em; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><strong>新特性</strong></h3> 
<ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
 <li>改进异常处理的内容协商 (<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Flaravel%2Fframework%2Fpull%2F39385" target="_blank">#39385</a><span> </span>)</li> 
 <li>添加了对 MariaDB 的 SKIP LOCKED 的支持 (<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Flaravel%2Fframework%2Fpull%2F39396" target="_blank">#39396</a><span> </span>)</li> 
 <li>自定义将字符串转换为 Stringable (<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Flaravel%2Fframework%2Fpull%2F39410" target="_blank">#39410</a><span> </span>)</li> 
 <li>添加<code>Illuminate/Support/Str::mask()</code>(<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Flaravel%2Fframework%2Fpull%2F39393" target="_blank">#39393</a><span> </span>)</li> 
 <li>允许将模型属性转换为 Enum (<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Flaravel%2Fframework%2Fpull%2F39315" target="_blank">#39315</a><span> </span>)</li> 
 <li>添加了 Enum 验证规则 (<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Flaravel%2Fframework%2Fpull%2F39437" target="_blank">#39437</a><span> </span>)</li> 
 <li>Auth：允许在凭据数组中使用回调（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Flaravel%2Fframework%2Fpull%2F39420" target="_blank">#39420</a>）</li> 
 <li>添加成功和失败命令断言 (<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Flaravel%2Fframework%2Fpull%2F39435" target="_blank">#39435</a><span> </span>)</li> 
</ul> 
<h3 style="margin-left:.6em; margin-right:0; text-align:start">修复</h3> 
<ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
 <li>更改列时将 CURRENT_TIMESTAMP 固定为默认值 (<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Flaravel%2Fframework%2Fpull%2F39377" target="_blank">#39377</a><span> </span>)</li> 
 <li>现在接受的 header 比较时不区分大小写（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Flaravel%2Fframework%2Fpull%2F39413" target="_blank">#39413</a>）</li> 
 <li>使用大写的翻译参数来修复回归（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Flaravel%2Fframework%2Fpull%2F39424" target="_blank">#39424</a>）</li> 
</ul>

</ul>
