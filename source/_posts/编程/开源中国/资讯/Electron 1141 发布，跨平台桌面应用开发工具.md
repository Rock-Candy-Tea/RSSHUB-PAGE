                                                                                            <p>Electron 11.4.1 现已发布，主要更新内容如下： </p> 
<p><strong>Fixes</strong> </p> 
<ul> 
 <li>修复了<code>desktopCapturer.getSources()</code>promise result 有时从不解析的问题。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F28282" target="_blank">＃28282</a></li> 
 <li>修复了 macOS 上 BrowserViews 中的拖动区域可能偏离其 y 轴的问题。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F28297" target="_blank">＃28297 </a></li> 
 <li>修复了上下文菜单靠近屏幕边缘时无法正确定位的问题。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F28278" target="_blank">＃28278 </a></li> 
 <li>修复了从 asar 文件中密集 I/O，导致一段时间后出现 ERR_FILE_NOT_FOUND 的问题。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F28201" target="_blank">＃28201</a></li> 
 <li>修复了当启用 contextIsolation 且禁用 nativeWindowOpen 时，window.open() 不会返回一个带有 location.href 设置器的对象的问题。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F28161" target="_blank">＃28161</a></li> 
 <li>在 Windows 上传递给<code>shell.openExternal</code>的 URL 现在可以正确地进行 URI 编码。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F28340" target="_blank">＃28340 </a></li> 
</ul> 
<p>更多详情可查看更新说明：<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Freleases%2Ftag%2Fv11.4.1" target="_blank">https://github.com/electron/electron/releases/tag/v11.4.1</a></p>
