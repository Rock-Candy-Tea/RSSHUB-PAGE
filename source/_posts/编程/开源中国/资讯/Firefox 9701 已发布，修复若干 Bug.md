                                                                                            <p>Firefox 97.0.1 已发布，该版本修复了若干 Bug ：</p> 
<ul> 
 <li>修复了 TikTok 有时无法从个人资料主页加载视频的 Bug 。<span style="color:#42425a">(</span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fbugzilla.mozilla.org%2Fshow_bug.cgi%3Fid%3D1750973" target="_blank">bug 1750973</a><span style="color:#42425a">) </span></li> 
 <li><span style="color:#42425a">修复了无法在 Hulu 上切换画中画模式的问题。(</span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fbugzilla.mozilla.org%2Fshow_bug.cgi%3Fid%3D1753401" target="_blank">bug 1753401</a><span style="color:#42425a">)</span></li> 
 <li><span style="color:#42425a">解决 WebRoot SecureAnywhere 防病毒软件在某些情况下导致 Firefox 无法使用的问题。(</span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fbugzilla.mozilla.org%2Fshow_bug.cgi%3Fid%3D1752466" target="_blank">bug 1752466</a><span style="color:#42425a">)</span></li> 
 <li><span style="color:#42425a">修复了导致用户在启动 Firefox 时意外看到“恢复会话”屏幕的问题。(</span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fbugzilla.mozilla.org%2Fshow_bug.cgi%3Fid%3D1749996" target="_blank">bug 1749996</a><span style="color:#42425a">)</span></li> 
</ul>
