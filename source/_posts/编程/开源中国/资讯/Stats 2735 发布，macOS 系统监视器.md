                                                                                            <p><span style="color:#000000">Stats 是一款开源的 macOS 系统监控工具，可以驻留在菜单栏中对 CPU、GPU、内存、磁盘和网络等进行实时监测。目前 Stats </span><span style="color:#333333">v2.7.35</span><span style="color:#000000"> 发布了 ，此版本带来如下变更：</span></p> 
<h3><strong>关闭的问题</strong></h3> 
<ul> 
 <li>修复：重新启动后，更正了网络图表中颜色的顺序（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fexelban%2Fstats%2Fissues%2F1080" target="_blank">#1080</a>）</li> 
 <li>修复：如果不再可用，则从弹出视图中删除电池电量（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fexelban%2Fstats%2Fissues%2F1083" target="_blank">#1083</a>）</li> 
</ul> 
<h3><strong>错误修复和新功能</strong></h3> 
<ul> 
 <li>修复：网络模块中的固定数据竞速</li> 
 <li>修复：访问读卡器状态时，修复数据竞争</li> 
 <li>特性：向网络模块添加了一个新的连接阅读器。它向 1.1.1.1 发送低级 (ICMP) ping 消息。（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fexelban%2Fstats%2Fissues%2F1082" target="_blank">#1082</a>）</li> 
 <li>特性：将连接状态添加到网络弹出视图（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fexelban%2Fstats%2Fissues%2F1082" target="_blank">#1082</a>）</li> 
</ul> 
<h3><strong>本土化</strong></h3> 
<ul> 
 <li>特性：更新简体中文翻译（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fexelban%2Fstats%2Fpull%2F1078" target="_blank">#1078</a>）</li> 
 <li>特性：更新繁体中文翻译（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fexelban%2Fstats%2Fpull%2F1079" target="_blank">#1079</a>）</li> 
 <li>特性：更新德语本地化（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fexelban%2Fstats%2Fpull%2F1085" target="_blank">#1085</a>）</li> 
</ul>
