                                                        <p>OpenSearch 首个 GA 版本已正式<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fopensearch.org%2Fblog%2Fupdates%2F2021%2F07%2Fopensearch-general-availability-announcement%2F" target="_blank">发布</a>，GA 即意味着可在生产环境中使用。</p> 
<p>根据官方的介绍，<span style="color:#002a3a"><span style="background-color:#ffffff">OpenSearch 是一个由社区驱动的开源搜索和分析套件，fork 自 Apache 2.0 许可的 Elasticsearch 7.10.2 和 Kibana 7.10.2。它由一个搜索引擎守护程序 (OpenSearch)、一个可视化和用户界面 (OpenSearch Dashboards) 以及 Open Distro for Elasticsearch 的高级功能（如安全性、警报、异常检测等）组成。</span></span></p> 
<p>OpenSearch 1.0 GA 对原分支的代码进行了更深一步的清理，其开发团队表示已删除了绝大多数的专有代码和相关引用，现在任何人都可以放心使用而无需担心侵权问题。在升级版本方面，团队也做了一些工作，可方便用户轻松地从 Elasticsearch 升级到 OpenSearch，因为这与在 Elasticsearch 版本之间升级的方式完全相同。</p> 
<p style="text-align:start"><span style="color:#002a3a"><span style="background-color:#ffffff">自测试版以来，OpenSearch 1.0 GA 版本还新增了如下的增强功能：</span></span></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>支持运行于 ARM64 架构之上的 Linux</li> 
 <li>新增将 OpenSearch 和 OpenSearch Dashboards 嵌入现有产品和服务的最小工件</li> 
 <li>OpenSearch Dashboards 支持数据流</li> 
 <li>Trace Analytics 插件支持跨属性的可见性和过滤</li> 
 <li>Reporting 插件支持调度和租户</li> 
</ul> 
<p><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fopensearch-project%2Fopensearch-build%2Fblob%2Fmain%2Frelease-notes%2Fopensearch-release-notes-1.0.0.md" target="_blank">详情查看发布说明</a>。</p> 
<p><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fopensearch.org%2Fdownloads.html" target="_blank">下载地址</a> | <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fopensearch.org%2Fblog%2Ftechnical-posts%2F2021%2F07%2Fhow-to-upgrade-from-opendistro-to-opensearch%2F" target="_blank">升级指南</a></p> 
<p>下一个重要里程碑是 OpenSearch 1.1，预计将于 8 月 30 日发布。 除了更多的工件覆盖（例如 RPM X64、DEB X64、DEB ARM64），OpenSearch 1.1 还将对异常检测进行改进（例如 <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fopendistro-for-elasticsearch%2Fanomaly-detection%2Fissues%2F380" target="_blank">universal flow</a>），新增<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fopensearch-project%2FOpenSearch%2Fissues%2F478" target="_blank">索引压力保护</a>和<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fopensearch-project%2Fnotifications%2Fissues%2F181" target="_blank">通知支持</a>等特性。查看<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Forgs%2Fopensearch-project%2Fprojects%2F1" target="_blank">公开路线图</a>了解更多详情。</p>
