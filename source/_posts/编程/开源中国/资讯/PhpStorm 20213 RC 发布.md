<div class="content">
                                                                                            <p><span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#27282c">PhpStorm 2021.3 RC 现已发布，这是 PhpStorm 第三次主要更新之前的最终版本。</span>值得注意的是，与以前的 EAP 版本不同，此 RC 要求用户拥有有效的 PhpStorm 许可证。</p> 
<p style="color:#27282c; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start">除了即将发布的版本的新功能之外，此版本还介绍了 Remote Development。</p> 
<p style="color:#27282c; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><strong>Remote Development: JetBrains Gateway</strong></p> 
<p style="color:#27282c; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#27282c">通过 </span>Remote Development<span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#27282c">，用户将可以连接到运行 IDE 后端的远程计算机，并处理位于该后端的项目，就像在本地计算机上一样。不过此功能仍在大量开发中，现在处于测试状态。可参阅</span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.jetbrains.com%2Fhelp%2Fphpstorm%2F2021.3%2F%3FRemote_development_starting_page" target="_blank">文档</a><span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#27282c">以了解有关该功能的更多信息。</span></p> 
<p style="color:#27282c; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#27282c"><img alt height="362" src="https://oscimg.oschina.net/oscnet/up-759dc585f4a545be15137e949644e12e9d5.png" width="500" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p> 
<p style="color:#27282c; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#27282c">自 Beta 以来引入的更改列表可在</span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fconfluence.jetbrains.com%2Fdisplay%2FPhpStorm%2FPhpStorm%2B213.5744.147%2BRelease%2BNotes" target="_blank">发行说明</a><span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#27282c">中找到。</span></p> 
<p style="color:#27282c; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.jetbrains.com%2Fphpstorm%2Fnextversion%2F" target="_blank"><span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#27282c">下载地址</span></a></p>
