                                                                                            <p style="color:#24292f; text-align:start">PaddleOCR旨在打造一套丰富、领先、且实用的OCR工具库，助力开发者训练出更好的模型，并应用落地。</p> 
<p style="color:#24292f; text-align:start">最新版本 2.1.0 主要更新内容包括：</p> 
<ol> 
 <li style="color: rgb(36, 41, 47); text-align: start;">新发布的端到端文本识别算法<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.aaai.org%2FAAAI21Papers%2FAAAI-2885.WangP.pdf" target="_blank">PGNet</a>，发布在AAAI 2021中。在<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=http%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FPaddlePaddle%2FPaddleOCR%2Fblob%2Frelease%2F2.1%2Fdoc%2Fdoc_en%2Fpgnet_en.md" target="_blank">此处</a>查找教程。</li> 
 <li style="color: rgb(36, 41, 47); text-align: start;">新发布多语言识别模型，支持80多种语言识别。在<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=http%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FPaddlePaddle%2FPaddleOCR%2Fblob%2Frelease%2F2.1%2Fdoc%2Fdoc_en%2Fmulti_languages_en.md" target="_blank">此处</a>查找教程。</li> 
 <li style="color: rgb(36, 41, 47); text-align: start;">优化<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=http%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FPaddlePaddle%2FPaddleOCR%2Fblob%2Frelease%2F2.1%2Fdoc%2Fdoc_en%2Fmodels_list_en.md%23English" target="_blank">英文识别模型</a>的性能。</li> 
</ol>
