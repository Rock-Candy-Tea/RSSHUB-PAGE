                                                        <p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start">Fastlane 2.196.0 发布了，<span style="color:#333333">Fastlane 是一个针对 iOS 和 Android 全方位开发自动化流程的工具。利用目前支持的工具可以做包含自动化和可持续化构建的每个环节，比如单元测试、截图、分发渠道、上传元数据和 ipa 包提交审核等等。</span></p> 
<p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><strong>Fastlane 2.196.0 版本主要更新内容</strong></p> 
<ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
 <li><span style="color:#24292f">[fastlane_core]<span> </span></span>修复了哈希选项环境中，自动把键值转换成符号的问题<span> </span><span style="color:#24292f">(</span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Ffastlane%2Ffastlane%2Fpull%2F19424" target="_blank">#19424</a><span style="color:#24292f">) </span></li> 
 <li><span style="color:#24292f">[snapshot] 快照功能 已支持 i</span>Pad Mini<span> </span><span style="color:#24292f">(</span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Ffastlane%2Ffastlane%2Fpull%2F19460" target="_blank">#19460</a><span style="color:#24292f">) </span></li> 
 <li><span style="color:#24292f">[pem] 修复 OpenSSL 的空密码问题 (</span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Ffastlane%2Ffastlane%2Fpull%2F19459" target="_blank">#19459</a><span style="color:#24292f">) </span></li> 
 <li><span style="color:#24292f">[supply]<span> </span></span>将“未正式发行”公告作为提醒，而不是错误(<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Ffastlane%2Ffastlane%2Fpull%2F19425" target="_blank"><u>#19425</u></a><span> </span>)</li> 
 <li><span style="color:#24292f">[sigh] 添加 --generate-entitlement-der 以退出数字签名的调用<span> </span></span>(<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Ffastlane%2Ffastlane%2Fpull%2F19423" target="_blank"><u>#19423</u></a><span> </span>)</li> 
 <li><span style="color:#24292f">[action][latest_testflight_build]<span> </span></span><code>LatestTestflightBuildNumberAction</code>不再覆盖现有的 Actions.lane_context (<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Ffastlane%2Ffastlane%2Fpull%2F19421" target="_blank">#19421</a><span> </span>)</li> 
 <li><span style="color:#24292f">[snapshot]<span> </span></span>HTML 报告页面中加入 iPhone 13 设备<span> </span><span style="color:#24292f">(</span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Ffastlane%2Ffastlane%2Fpull%2F19420" target="_blank">#19420</a><span style="color:#24292f">)</span></li> 
 <li><span style="color:#24292f">[screengrab] 修正 Screengrab 选项中的错别字<span> </span></span>(<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Ffastlane%2Ffastlane%2Fpull%2F19426" target="_blank">#19426</a><span> </span>)</li> 
 <li><span style="color:#24292f">[supply] 修复更新 rollout 时<span> </span></span><code>track</code><span style="color:#24292f"><span> </span> 不对应的问题<span> </span></span>(<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Ffastlane%2Ffastlane%2Fpull%2F19411" target="_blank"><u>#19411</u></a><span> </span>)</li> 
</ul>
