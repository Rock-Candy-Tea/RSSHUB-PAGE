<h2 style="margin-left:0.8em; margin-right:0.8em; text-align:start">简介</h2> 
<p data-darkreader-inline-color style="--darkreader-inline-color:#c8c3bc; color:#333333; margin-left:0.8em; margin-right:0.8em; text-align:start"><span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fvran-dev%2FPrettyZoo" target="_blank"><span>PrettyZoo</span></a></span><span> 是一款基于 JavaFX 实现的 Zookeeper 桌面图形化工具，不仅颜值极高，而且功能齐全，支持 Windows / Mac / Linux 等主流平台。</span></p>
