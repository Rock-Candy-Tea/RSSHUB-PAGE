<ul> 
 <li> <p>增加了对正则表达式的验证器 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fistio%2Fistio%2Fissues%2F34065" target="_blank">Issue 34065</a>)</p> </li> 
 <li> <p>修复导致错误状态的<code>EndpointSlice</code> races (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fistio%2Fistio%2Fissues%2F33672" target="_blank">Issue 33672</a>)</p> </li> 
 <li> <p>修复<code>EndpointSlice</code> 在服务更新时创建了重复 IP</p> </li> 
</ul>
