                                                                                            <p>Firefox 88.0.1 版本现已<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.mozilla.org%2Fen-US%2Ffirefox%2F88.0.1%2Freleasenotes%2F" target="_blank">发布</a>，具体更新内容如下：</p> 
<p><strong>Fixed</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>解决了最近 Widevine 插件更新引起的问题，该问题使一些购买的视频内容无法正确播放（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fbugzilla.mozilla.org%2Fshow_bug.cgi%3Fid%3D1705138" target="_blank">bug 1705138</a>）</li> 
 <li>修复了在某些 Gen6 英特尔图形芯片组上播放 Twitter 或 WebRTC 电话的视频损坏问题（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fbugzilla.mozilla.org%2Fshow_bug.cgi%3Fid%3D1708937" target="_blank">bug 1708937</a>）</li> 
 <li>修复了在启用高对比度模式的情况下，偏好设置中的菜单无法阅读的问题（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fbugzilla.mozilla.org%2Fshow_bug.cgi%3Fid%3D1706496" target="_blank">bug 1706496</a>）</li> 
 <li> <p>各种稳定性和<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.mozilla.org%2Fsecurity%2Fadvisories%2Fmfsa2021-20%2F" target="_blank">安全性修复</a></p> </li> 
</ul>

</ul>
