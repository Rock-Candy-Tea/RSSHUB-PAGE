                                                        <p>Kotlin 1.5.0-M2 已发布，此版本是 Kotlin 1.5.0 的最后一个里程碑版本，正式版将于春季发布。</p> 
<p>发布公告显示，Kotlin 1.5.0-M2 简化了使用配置。在之前的版本中，如果决定在现有项目中使用预览版本，则必须在 Gradle 文件中指定一个额外的 Bintray 仓库。现在，所有的 Kotlin 预览构件都存储在 Maven Central 中，无需手动添加仓库。</p> 
<p>相关文档：<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fkotlinlang.org%2Fdocs%2Finstall-eap-plugin.html" target="_blank">https://kotlinlang.org/docs/install-eap-plugin.html</a></p> 
<p>Kotlin 1.5 部分新特性：</p> 
<ul> 
 <li>默认使用<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fblog.jetbrains.com%2Fkotlin%2F2021%2F02%2Fthe-jvm-backend-is-in-beta-let-s-make-it-stable-together%2F" target="_blank">新的 JVM IR 后端</a>（已处于稳定阶段）</li> 
 <li>改进内联 class，目前已处于<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fblog.jetbrains.com%2Fkotlin%2F2021%2F02%2Fnew-language-features-preview-in-kotlin-1-4-30%2F%23inline-value-classes-stabilization" target="_blank">稳定阶段</a></li> 
 <li>实验性支持 <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fblog.jetbrains.com%2Fkotlin%2F2021%2F02%2Fnew-language-features-preview-in-kotlin-1-4-30%2F%23jvm-records" target="_blank">JVM records</a></li> 
 <li>实验性支持<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fblog.jetbrains.com%2Fkotlin%2F2021%2F02%2Fnew-language-features-preview-in-kotlin-1-4-30%2F%23sealed-interfaces" target="_blank">密封接口</a>以及对密封类的其他改进</li> 
</ul>
