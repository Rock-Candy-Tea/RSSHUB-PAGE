                                                        <p>Firefox 90.0.1 版本现已发布，具体更新内容如下：</p> 
<p><strong>Fixed</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>修复了在 Windows 上使用某些 accessibility clients 时发生的崩溃（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fbugzilla.mozilla.org%2Fshow_bug.cgi%3Fid%3D1720696" target="_blank">bug 1720696</a>）</li> 
 <li>修复了 busy looping 处理一些 HTTP3 响应（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fbugzilla.mozilla.org%2Fshow_bug.cgi%3Fid%3D1720079" target="_blank">bug 1720079</a>）</li> 
 <li>修复了使用某些智能卡进行身份验证的瞬态错误（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fbugzilla.mozilla.org%2Fshow_bug.cgi%3Fid%3D1715325" target="_blank">bug 1715325</a>）</li> 
 <li>修复了关机时罕见的崩溃（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fbugzilla.mozilla.org%2Fshow_bug.cgi%3Fid%3D1707057" target="_blank">bug 1707057</a>）</li> 
 <li>修复了一个启动时的 race，其导致了 about:support 在升级后最终为空（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fbugzilla.mozilla.org%2Fshow_bug.cgi%3Fid%3D1717894" target="_blank">bug 1717894</a>）</li> 
</ul> 
<p><strong>unresolved</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>打印带有缩放的页面可能会导致输出被截断（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fbugzilla.mozilla.org%2Fshow_bug.cgi%3Fid%3D1720621" target="_blank">bug 1720621</a>）</li> 
</ul> 
<p>更新说明：<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.mozilla.org%2Fen-US%2Ffirefox%2F90.0.1%2Freleasenotes%2F" target="_blank">https://www.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/90.0.1/releasenotes/</a></p>
