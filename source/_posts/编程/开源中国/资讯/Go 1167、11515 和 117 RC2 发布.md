                                                                                            <p>Go 开发团队近日为三个分支发布了更新，分别是 <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgroups.google.com%2Fg%2Fgolang-announce%2Fc%2FuHACNfXAZqk" target="_blank">Go 1.16.7、1.15.15</a> 和 <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgroups.google.com%2Fg%2Fgolang-announce%2Fc%2Fyk30ovJGXWY" target="_blank">1.17 RC2</a>。</p> 
<p>Go 1.16.7 和 1.15.15 是次要版本更新，更新内容包括根据新安全策略进行的安全问题修复。</p> 
<ul> 
 <li>Go 1.16.7 包含对<code>net/http/httputil</code>软件包的安全修复，以及对编译器、链接器、运行时、<code>go</code>命令和<code>net/http</code>包的错误修复。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fgolang%2Fgo%2Fissues%3Fq%3Dmilestone%253AGo1.16.7%2Blabel%253ACherryPickApproved" target="_blank">点此查看详细内容</a>。</li> 
 <li>Go 1.15.15 的更细内容与 1.16.7 的一致，<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fgolang%2Fgo%2Fissues%3Fq%3Dmilestone%253AGo1.15.15%2Blabel%253ACherryPickApproved" target="_blank">详情点此查看</a>。</li> 
</ul> 
<p>release note：<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgolang.org%2Fdoc%2Fdevel%2Frelease%23go1.16.minor" target="_blank">https://golang.org/doc/devel/release#go1.16.minor</a>。</p> 
<hr> 
<p>Go 1.17 计划在本月正式发布，因此 RC2 应该是正式发布前的最后一个候选版本。</p> 
<p>Go 1.17 包含三个针对语言特性的改进：</p> 
<ul> 
 <li>支持<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Ftip.golang.org%2Fref%2Fspec%23Conversions_from_slice_to_array_pointer" target="_blank">从 slice （切片）到数组指针的转换</a></li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Ftip.golang.org%2Fpkg%2Funsafe%23Add" target="_blank"><code>unsafe.Add</code></a>：<code>unsafe.Add(ptr, len)</code>将<code>len</code>添加<code>ptr</code>并返回更新的指针<code>unsafe.Pointer(uintptr(ptr) + uintptr(len))</code></li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Ftip.golang.org%2Fpkg%2Funsafe%23Slice" target="_blank"><code>unsafe.Slice </code></a>: 对于<code>*T</code>类型的<code>ptr</code>表达式，<code>unsafe.Slice(ptr, len) </code>返回一个<code>[]T</code>类型的切片，其底层数组从<code>ptr</code>开始， 其长度和容量为<code>len</code></li> 
 <li>……</li> 
</ul> 
<p>此外，对于 Mac 平台，Go 1.17 要求在 macOS 10.13 High Sierra 或更高版本上运行，并已停止支持以前的系统版本。对于 Windows 平台，Go 1.17 添加了对 64 位 ARM 架构的支持。对于 OpenBSD，Go 1.17 添加了对 64 位 MIPS 架构的支持。</p> 
<p><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Ftip.golang.org%2Fdoc%2Fgo1.17" target="_blank">更多内容查看 release notes</a>。</p>
