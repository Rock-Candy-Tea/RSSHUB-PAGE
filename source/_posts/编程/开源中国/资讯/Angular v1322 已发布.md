                                                                                            <p><span style="color:#333333">Angular 是一个基于 TypeScript 的开源前端框架，由 Google 的 Angular 团队以及社区共同领导，从 AngularJS 完全重写而成。</span></p> 
<p>Angular v13.2.2 发布了，该版本带来少量修复：</p> 
<ul> 
 <li><strong>编译器</strong>：允许 banana-in-a-box 绑定以非空断言结束 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fangular%2Fangular%2Fpull%2F37809" target="_blank">#37809</a>)</li> 
 <li><strong>表单</strong>：修复在表单中错误地使用 ngFor 跟踪 ngModel 的问题 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fangular%2Fangular%2Fpull%2F40459" target="_blank">#40459</a>)</li> 
 <li><strong>http：</strong>移除 IE 特殊状态处理 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fangular%2Fangular%2Fpull%2F44354" target="_blank">#44354</a>)</li> 
 <li><strong>upgrade：</strong>不再从 Angular 路由器触发重复的导航事件 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fangular%2Fangular%2Fpull%2F43441" target="_blank">#43441</a>)</li> 
</ul>
