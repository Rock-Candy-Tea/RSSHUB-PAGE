                                                        <p>PhpStorm 2021.1.3 的预览版已经发布，其中有大量的错误修复和稳定性改进。</p> 
<p>在该预览版中，值得关注的更新包括：</p> 
<ul> 
 <li>修复：编辑器配色方案一直被切换到 Darcula (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FIDEA-265169" target="_blank">IDEA-265169</a> +73)</li> 
 <li>更新了捆绑的 webpack schema (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FWEB-49925" target="_blank">WEB-49925</a> +13)</li> 
 <li>修复：标签和缩进设置拒绝保存 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FIDEA-267368" target="_blank">IDEA-267368</a> +9)</li> 
 <li>修复：未锁定的 Git 工具窗口不断缩小 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FIDEA-266173" target="_blank">IDEA-266173</a> +7)</li> 
 <li>新增： 支持 PostgreSQL 的行安全策略 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FDBE-6276" target="_blank">DBE-6276</a> +5)</li> 
 <li>新增： 自动配置 PHPCS Fixer 3.0 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FWI-60300" target="_blank">WI-60300</a>)</li> 
 <li>修复：在尝试以 cs-fixer < 2.19 时加载 cs-fixer 的规则集时出现异常 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FWI-60402" target="_blank">WI-60402</a>)</li> 
 <li>修复：只有名称为 <code>.php_cs</code>、 <code>.php-cs-fixer.php</code>、 <code>.php_cs.dist</code>、 <code>.php-cs-fixer.dist.php</code> 的文件被接受为自定义规则集 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FWI-60454" target="_blank">WI-60454</a>)</li> 
</ul>
