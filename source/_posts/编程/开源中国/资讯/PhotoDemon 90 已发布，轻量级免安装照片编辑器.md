<div class="content">
                                                                    
                                                        <p><span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#333333">PhotoDemon 是一个超轻量级、免安装的开源照片编辑器，它体积仅有 14 MB ，但提供了全面的照片编辑工具。PhotoDemon 不需要安装，也不需要管理员权限即可运行，可以作为便携式应用程序在任何 Windows 机器（XP 到 Win 11）上运行。</span></p> 
<p>目前 <span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#333333">PhotoDemon 9.0 已发布，</span><span style="background-color:#fdfdfd; color:#111111">此版本的亮点包括：改进的用户界面、新的选择工具（包括对多项选择的支持）、对许多新图像格式（AVIF、Paintshop Pro (PSP)、GIMP (XCF)、SVG 等）的支持)、内容感知填充、新的过滤器和效果等等。</span></p> 
<h3 style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><span><span><span style="color:#111111"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#fdfdfd"><span><span><span>改进的主用户界面</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></h3> 
<p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><span><span style="color:#111111"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#fdfdfd"><span><span><span>在 9.0 版中，PhotoDemon 的主界面提供更多工作空间，新的 UI 占用的垂直空间是之前的一半。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p> 
<p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><span><span style="color:#111111"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#fdfdfd"><span><span><span>工具选项与菜单栏顶部对齐，常用的设置显示在工具栏上，扩展选项位于弹出面板中。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p> 
<p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><span><span style="color:#111111"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#fdfdfd"><span><span><span>与画布交互时，这些弹出面板会自动消失。如果需要固定特定的面板，可以单击右下角的“图钉”图标，使其保持打开状态。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p>

<h2 style="margin-left:0px; margin-right:0px; text-align:start"><span><span><span style="color:#111111"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#fdfdfd"><span><span><span>新的选择工具（包括复合选择）</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></h2> 
<p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><span><span style="color:#111111"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#fdfdfd"><span><span><span>PhotoDemon 一直支持可编辑的选择。创建选区后，可以通过单击拖动选区的角或边来进一步优化选区，或使用文本框进行精确调整。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p> 
<p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><span><span style="color:#111111"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#fdfdfd"><span><span><span>PhotoDemon 的可编辑选择现在带有多选支持。可以使用“添加”、“减去”和“相交”模式组合选择，<strong>即使在多个选择处于活动状态时</strong>，您的最后一个选择仍可编辑。例如，这是一个套索选择，它与之前的矩形和椭圆选择实时合并：</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p> 
<p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><img alt height="414" src="https://oscimg.oschina.net/oscnet/up-e5515e7fe959261ce2e2a4dac5c99f54be3.webp" width="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p> 
<h3 style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><span><span><span style="color:#111111"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#fdfdfd"><span><span><span>内容感知填充</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></h3> 
<p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><span><span style="color:#111111"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#fdfdfd"><span><span><span>基于这些新的选择工具功能，PhotoDemon 现在配备了功能强大的内容感知填充工具。（此功能也称为“智能对象移除”或“修复”。）</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p> 
<p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><span><span style="color:#111111"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#fdfdfd"><span><span><span>只需选择要从照片中删除的一个或多个对象，单击<code class="language-plaintext">Edit > Content-aware fill</code>（或<code class="language-plaintext">Select > Heal selected area</code>），PhotoDemon 会处理剩下的事情。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p> 
<p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><span><span style="color:#111111"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#fdfdfd"><span><span><span><img alt="内容感知填充" height="371" src="https://photodemon.org/media/images/content-aware-fill.webp" width="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p> 
<p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><span><span style="color:#111111"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#fdfdfd"><span><span><span>PhotoDemon 的内容感知填充没有限制。它适用于<strong>任何 PC 上的</strong><strong>所有</strong>选择工具，即使在 Windows XP 上也是如此。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p> 
<p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><span><span style="color:#111111"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#fdfdfd"><span><span><span>高级用户可以进一步调整工具的行为。例如，在这张缺少像素的全景照片中，要求修复器避免对左侧的像素进行采样。这可以防止那较暗的云层渗我想要匹配的较轻的云层中：</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p> 
<p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><img alt="内容感知填充" height="338" src="https://photodemon.org/media/images/content-aware-fill-panorama.webp" width="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p> 
<h3 style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><span><span><span style="color:#111111"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#fdfdfd"><span><span><span>对新图像格式的支持</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></h3> 
<p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><span><span style="color:#111111"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#fdfdfd"><span><span><span>扩展的图像支持是此版本的一大重点。PhotoDemon 现在提供：</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p> 
<ul style="margin-left:30px; margin-right:0"> 
 <li><strong>对 Corel Paintshop Pro ( PSP</strong> ) 图像的导入和导出支持。新的导入器甚至可以将 PSP 文本图层保存为 PhotoDemon 中的可编辑文本图层！</li> 
 <li><strong>对 GIMP ( XCF</strong> ) 图像的导入支持，包括全面覆盖所有颜色模式、整数和浮点精度以及 GIMP 版本的图层。</li> 
 <li>对全新<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fen.wikipedia.org%2Fwiki%2FAV1%23AV1_Image_File_Format_%28AVIF%29" target="_blank"><strong>AVIF</strong>文件格式</a>的导入和导出支持，c/o<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FAOMediaCodec%2Flibavif" target="_blank">开源 libavif 库</a>。由于 AVIF 文件支持非常复杂（编码器+解码器应用程序比 PhotoDemon 大 3 倍！）并且它们仅适用于 64 位系统，因此 PhotoDemon 默认不提供这些库。如果您尝试在兼容的 PC 上打开或保存 AVIF 文件，PhotoDemon 将提供自动下载和配置 libavif 的便携式副本。</li> 
 <li><strong>对 WebP </strong>图像的导入和导出支持。此页面上的许多屏幕截图动画都是使用此功能创建的。</li> 
 <li>对 <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fen.wikipedia.org%2Fwiki%2FScalable_Vector_Graphics" target="_blank"><strong>SVG 和 SVGZ</strong>图像</a>的导入支持，由<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FRazrFalcon%2Fresvg" target="_blank">开源 resvg 库提供</a>。</li> 
</ul> 
<p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><span><span style="color:#111111"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#fdfdfd"><span><span><span>PhotoDemon 支持的其他新格式包括无损 JPEG ( <strong>JPEG-LS</strong> )、漫画书档案 ( <strong>CBZ</strong> )、旧 Symbian 图像（<strong>MBM</strong>、<strong>AIF</strong>）和无损“相当棒的”图像（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/news/174927/new-image-format-qoi"><strong>QOI</strong></a>）。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
