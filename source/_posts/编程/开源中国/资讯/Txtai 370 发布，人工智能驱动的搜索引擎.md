                                                        <p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><span style="color:#333333">Txtai 3.7.0 现已发布。Txtai 是一个人工智能驱动的搜索引擎，可以在文本的各个部分上建立了一个 AI 驱动的索引。</span><span style="color:#24292f">此版本添加了以下新功能、改进和错误修复：</span></p> 
<p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><span style="color:#24292f"><strong>新功能</strong></span></p> 
<ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
 <li>添加对象检测管道（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fneuml%2Ftxtai%2Fissues%2F148" target="_blank">#148</a>）</li> 
 <li>添加图像标题管道（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fneuml%2Ftxtai%2Fissues%2F149" target="_blank">#149</a>）</li> 
 <li>添加检索任务（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fneuml%2Ftxtai%2Fissues%2F150" target="_blank">#150</a>）</li> 
 <li>添加 no-op pipeline（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fneuml%2Ftxtai%2Fissues%2F152" target="_blank">#152</a>）</li> 
 <li>添加新的工作流程功能 ( <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fneuml%2Ftxtai%2Fissues%2F155" target="_blank">#155</a> )</li> 
</ul> 
<p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><span style="color:#24292f"><strong>改进</strong></span></p> 
<ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
 <li>在 README.md 中添加韩语翻译（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fneuml%2Ftxtai%2Fissues%2F138" target="_blank">#138</a>）</li> 
 <li>添加指向外部文章的链接（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fneuml%2Ftxtai%2Fissues%2F139" target="_blank">#139</a>）</li> 
 <li>更新应用实例以保持一致（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fneuml%2Ftxtai%2Fissues%2F140" target="_blank">#140</a>）</li> 
 <li>添加文章摘要示例（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fneuml%2Ftxtai%2Fissues%2F144" target="_blank">#144</a>）</li> 
 <li>为 texttractor 添加回退模式（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fneuml%2Ftxtai%2Fissues%2F145" target="_blank">#145</a>）</li> 
 <li>重组 pipeline package（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fneuml%2Ftxtai%2Fissues%2F147" target="_blank">#147</a>）</li> 
 <li>更新可选包测试以模拟丢失的包（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fneuml%2Ftxtai%2Fissues%2F154" target="_blank">#154</a>）</li> 
 <li>添加扁平化标签输出的参数（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fneuml%2Ftxtai%2Fissues%2F153" target="_blank">#153</a>）</li> 
 <li>使用最新更改更新文档 ( <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fneuml%2Ftxtai%2Fissues%2F156" target="_blank">#156</a> )</li> 
</ul> 
<p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><span style="color:#24292f"><strong>Bug 修复</strong></span></p> 
<ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
 <li>修复未安装 workflow extra 时导入服务任务的错误 ( <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fneuml%2Ftxtai%2Fissues%2F146" target="_blank">#146</a> )</li> 
 <li>修复基于 url 的任务的不一致性（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fneuml%2Ftxtai%2Fissues%2F151" target="_blank">#151</a>）</li> 
</ul>
