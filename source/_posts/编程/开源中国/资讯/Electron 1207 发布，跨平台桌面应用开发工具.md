                                                        <p>Electron 12.0.7 现已发布，主要更新内容如下： </p> 
<p><strong>Fixes</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>允许 Node.js 在调用<code>uv_run()</code>之前通过使用显式微任务策略来管理微任务队列。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F28972" target="_blank">＃28972 </a></li> 
 <li>修复<code>&lt;webview&gt;</code> <code>focus</code>/<code>blur</code>事件在启用了<code>contextIsolation</code>的情况下无法工作的问题。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F29024" target="_blank">＃29024 </a></li> 
 <li>修复了在未设置拖动区域的情况下 macOS 上的拖动区域将被错误地偏移的问题。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F29019" target="_blank">＃29019</a></li> 
 <li>修复了以下问题：即如果用户在显示信息框的情况下试图退出，则窗口无法关闭。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F28989" target="_blank">＃28989 </a></li> 
</ul> 
<p><strong>Other Changes</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li> <p>改进了<code>napi_threadsafe_function</code>的性能。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F29047" target="_blank">＃29047 </a></p> </li> 
</ul> 
<p>更新说明：<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Freleases%2Ftag%2Fv12.0.7" target="_blank">https://github.com/electron/electron/releases/tag/v12.0.7</a></p>
