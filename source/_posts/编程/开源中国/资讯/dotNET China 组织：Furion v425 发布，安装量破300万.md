<h2>dotNET China 组织</h2> 
<p style="color:#40485b; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:left">dotNET China 社区是国内一群爱好 .NET/C# 技术的开发者自发组织成立的<strong>非微软官方社区组织</strong>。</p> 
<h3 style="margin-left:0px; margin-right:0px; text-align:left">标志理念</h3> 
<p style="color:#40485b; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:left">dotNET China 社区标志设计由其名称中两个单词组成，同时将<span> </span><strong>NET</strong><span> </span>单词进行了蓝色高亮，<strong>C</strong>hina 单词中的<span> </span><strong>i</strong><span> </span>和<span> </span><strong>#</strong><span> </span>符号进行结合，既有<span> </span><strong>C#</strong><span> </span>的视感，又有<span> </span><strong>I love .NET/C#</strong><span> </span>的寓意。</p>
