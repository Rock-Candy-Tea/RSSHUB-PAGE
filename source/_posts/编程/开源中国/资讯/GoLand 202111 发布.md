                                                                                            <p>GoLand 2021.1.1 正式发布，以下是该版本的主要亮点：</p> 
<ul> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FGO-10841" target="_blank">GO-10841</a> - 修复了运行目标的问题，该问题在 WSL 2 上构建非根目录时导致编译失败；</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FGO-9851" target="_blank">GO-9851</a> - 我们通过修复一定数量的错误，提高了冗余类型转换代码检查的质量；</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FGO-10804" target="_blank">GO-10804</a> - 调试时 IDE 在进度条中显示无尽状态计算变量呈现的UI问题已经修复；</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FIDEA-264567" target="_blank">IDEA-264567</a>, <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FIDEA-265753" target="_blank">IDEA-265753</a> 修复了几个导致共享索引工作不正常的 bug，其目的是使进程在后台更快速、更智能地执行；</li> 
</ul>
