 <li><strong>Alerting</strong>：在 InfluxDB 中添加了在连字符中包含别名的功能。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fgrafana%2Fgrafana%2Fpull%2F32262" target="_blank">＃32262</a></li>

</ul> 
<p><strong>Bug 修复</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li><strong>Alerting</strong>：在 InfluxDB 中添加了在连字符中包含别名的功能。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fgrafana%2Fgrafana%2Fpull%2F32224" target="_blank">＃32224</a></li>

</ul>
