                                                        <p><span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#333333">Electron 15.1.2 和 14.1.1 现已发布。</span></p> 
<p><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Freleases%2Ftag%2Fv15.1.2" target="_blank">v15.1.2</a> <span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#333333">主要更新内容如下：</span></p> 
<p><strong style="color:#333333">Fixes</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>修复了<code>WebContents::MessageTo</code>在渲染帧被破坏时可能出现的崩溃。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F31334" target="_blank">#31334</a></li> 
 <li>修复了不可调整大小的无框窗口不可拖动的问题。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F31299" target="_blank">＃31299 </a></li> 
</ul> 
<p><strong style="color:#333333">Other Changes</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>1251688 的反向移植修复。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F31297" target="_blank">#31297</a></li> 
 <li>将 Chromium 更新为 94.0.4606.71。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F31234" target="_blank">#31234</a></li> 
</ul> 
<p><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Freleases%2Ftag%2Fv14.1.1" target="_blank">v14.1.1</a> <span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#333333">主要更新内容如下：</span></p> 
<p><strong style="color:#333333">Fixes</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>修复了不可调整大小的无框窗口不可拖动的问题。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F31298" target="_blank">＃31298 </a></li>
