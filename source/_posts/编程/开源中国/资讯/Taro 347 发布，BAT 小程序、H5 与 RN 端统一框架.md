                                                                                            <p><span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#333333">Taro 3.4.7 现已发布。Taro 是一个开放式跨端跨框架解决方案，支持使用 React/Vue/Nerv 等框架来开发微信/京东/百度/支付宝/字节跳动/ QQ 小程序/H5 等应用。具体更新内容如下：</span></p> 
<h4><strong>特性</strong></h4> 
<p style="text-align:start"><strong>H5</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>添加非微信 JS-SDK 下的<span> </span><code>getLocation</code><span> </span>API实现，用于在非微信 JS-SDK 环境下获取 GPS 定位，<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FNervJS%2Ftaro%2Fissues%2F11613" target="_blank">#11613</a><span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FNervJS%2Ftaro%2Fissues%2F11431" target="_blank">#11431</a></li> 
 <li>支持<span> </span><code>PickerView</code>、<code>PickerViewColumn</code><span> </span>组件</li> 
</ul> 
<h4><strong>修复</strong></h4> 
<ul> 
 <li>修改了默认模板中<span> </span><code>tsconfig.json</code><span> </span>的<span> </span><code>jsx</code><span> </span>设置，以便 Vue 框架用户更方便地使用 tsx 语法</li> 
</ul> 
<p style="text-align:start"><strong>CLI</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>修复 Taro 插件报方法缺失的问题</li> 
</ul> 
<h4><strong>Typings</strong></h4> 
<ul> 
 <li>增加 Vue3 tsx 组件类型定义</li> 
 <li>app 配置的<span> </span><code>subPackages</code><span> </span>选项增加<span> </span><code>plugins</code><span> </span>选项</li> 
</ul> 
<p>更新说明：<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FNervJS%2Ftaro%2Freleases%2Ftag%2Fv3.4.7" target="_blank">https://github.com/NervJS/taro/releases/tag/v3.4.7</a></p>
