                                                                                            <p style="color:#333333; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:left">OnlySwitch 是一个适用于 macOS 的菜单栏应用程序，其将数十个 Mac 常用功能集合到一个应用中，提升了使用便利性和操作效率。具备的功能包括：隐藏桌面图标、开启 / 关闭黑暗模式、清空剪贴板、快速连接 AirPods、清理 Xcode 衍生数据等。</p> 
<p style="color:#333333; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:left"><img alt height="330" src="https://oscimg.oschina.net/oscnet/up-13089189ae78745acbc1f032007f3c96f23.png" width="500" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p> 
<p style="color:#333333; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:left">OnlySwitch 2.3.3 已发布，带来如下改动：</p> 
<ul> 
 <li>更新屏幕测试开关（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fjacklandrin%2FOnlySwitch%2Fissues%2F34" target="_blank">#34</a>）</li> 
 <li>Radio 支持使用媒体键来控制播放/暂停（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fjacklandrin%2FOnlySwitch%2Fissues%2F36" target="_blank">#36</a>）和切换下/上一个频道</li> 
 <li>更改了版本历史外观</li> 
 <li>更改设置窗口的显示逻辑</li> 
 <li>修复错误：Ventura 中的 Radio 崩溃</li> 
</ul> 
<p>更新公告：<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fjacklandrin%2FOnlySwitch%2Freleases%2Ftag%2Frelease_2.3.3" target="_blank">https://github.com/jacklandrin/OnlySwitch/releases/tag/release_2.3.3</a></p>
