<p>此版本更新内容包括：</p> 
<h3>电脑端</h3> 
<p><code>修复</code></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>修复Form组件字典dataType属性的问题<a href="https://gitee.com/smallweigit/avue/issues/I3QFMV" target="_blank">gitee_I3QFMV</a></li> 
 <li>修复Form组件首次进来出发验证问题<a href="https://gitee.com/smallweigit/avue/issues/I3SATC" target="_blank">gitee_I3SATC</a></li> 
 <li>修复Curd组件二次打开弹框的数据问题<a href="https://gitee.com/smallweigit/avue/issues/I3RA4X" target="_blank">gitee_I3RA4X</a>,<a href="https://gitee.com/smallweigit/avue/issues/I1RUHM" target="_blank">gitee_I1RUHM</a></li> 
 <li>修复Crud组件height高度auto时的问题<a href="https://gitee.com/smallweigit/avue/issues/I3SBTN" target="_blank">gitee_I3SBTN</a></li> 
 <li>修复Crud组件字典筛选的问题<a href="https://gitee.com/smallweigit/avue/issues/I3QSFI" target="_blank">gitee_I3QSFI</a></li> 
 <li>修复Crud组件order排序错乱问题<a href="https://gitee.com/smallweigit/avue/issues/I3QS63" target="_blank">gitee_I3QS63</a>,<a href="https://gitee.com/smallweigit/avue/issues/I3QCF9" target="_blank">gitee_I3QCF9</a></li> 
 <li>修复Cascader组件的emitPath属性失效问题<a href="https://gitee.com/smallweigit/avue/issues/I3S4X1" target="_blank">gitee_I3S4X1</a></li> 
 <li>修复Export导出插件的数据0的问题<a href="https://gitee.com/smallweigit/avue/issues/I3R9ZG" target="_blank">gitee_I3R9ZG</a></li> 
</ul> 
<p><code>优化</code></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>优化Crud组件和Form组件的defaults属性的<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Favuejs.com%2Fdoc%2Fform%2Fform-control1" target="_blank">在线例子1</a>,<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Favuejs.com%2Fdoc%2Fcrud%2Fcrud-control" target="_blank">在线例子2</a></li> 
 <li>优化Crud组件列拖动问题，只能在弹窗中进行拖拽排序<a href="https://gitee.com/smallweigit/avue/issues/I3PZDE" target="_blank">gitee_I3PZDE</a>,<a href="https://gitee.com/smallweigit/avue/issues/I3OTFK" target="_blank">gitee_I3OTFK</a></li> 
 <li>优化Data数据展示类组件的单独配置decimals属性<a href="https://gitee.com/smallweigit/avue/issues/3R3CU" target="_blank">gitee_3R3CU</a></li> 
</ul> 
<p><code>新增</code></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>新增Form组件smSpan和xsSpan属性<a href="https://gitee.com/smallweigit/avue/issues/I3QTQ6" target="_blank">gitee_I3QTQ6</a></li>
