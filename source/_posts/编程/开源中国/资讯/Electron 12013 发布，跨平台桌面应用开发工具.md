                                                        <p>Electron 12.0.13 现已发布，主要更新内容如下： </p> 
<p><strong>Fixes</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>修复了当<code>nativeWindowOpen: false</code>时，使用<code>setWindowOpenHandler</code>拒绝<code>window.open</code>的挂起。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F29881" target="_blank">＃29881</a></li> 
 <li>修复了如果 webRequest.onBeforeSendHeaders 回调响应对象中不包含可选的"requestHeaders"参数，request headers 会被清除的问题。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F29834" target="_blank">＃29834 </a></li> 
</ul> 
<p><strong>Other Changes</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>chromium 的反向移植修复：1211215。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F29784" target="_blank">#29784</a></li> 
 <li>安全性：针对 CVE-2021-30547 的反向移植修复。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F29789" target="_blank">#29789</a></li> 
 <li>安全性：CVE-2021-30553 的反向移植修复。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F29818" target="_blank">#29818</a></li> 
 <li>安全性：CVE-2021-30554 的反向移植修复。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F29822" target="_blank">#29822</a></li>
