                                                                                            <p>GoLand 2021.2.3 已发布。这是一个次要更新版本，更新内容包括部分改进和 bug 修复。</p> 
<p>升级方式：</p> 
<ul> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.jetbrains.com%2Fgo%2Fdownload%2F" target="_blank">直接下载</a>新版的安装包</li> 
 <li>通过<span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#27282c"><span> </span></span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.jetbrains.com%2Ftoolbox%2Fapp%2F" target="_blank">Toolbox App</a><span style="color:#27282c"> 进行升级</span></li> 
 <li>在 <span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#27282c">2021.2.2 或 2021.1.3<span> 基础上应用更新补丁（路径：</span></span>GoLand | Check for Updates<span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#27282c"><span>）</span></span></li> 
 <li>使用 snap 软件包进行升级（针对 Ubuntu）</li> 
</ul> 
<p>更新亮点：</p> 
<ul> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FGO-11632" target="_blank">GO-11632</a> – 修复在某些机器上搜索接口实现时出现的延迟问题</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FGO-11372" target="_blank">GO-11372</a> – “Convert path to absolute” 不再在 go.mod 中显示</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FWEB-52236" target="_blank">WEB-52236</a> – ESLint 8.0 支持正常运行</li> 
</ul> 
<p>完整更新说明查看  <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fconfluence.jetbrains.com%2Fdisplay%2FGOLAND%2FGoLand%252B2021.2.3%252BRelease%252BNotes" target="_blank">release notes</a><span style="color:#27282c">。</span></p>
