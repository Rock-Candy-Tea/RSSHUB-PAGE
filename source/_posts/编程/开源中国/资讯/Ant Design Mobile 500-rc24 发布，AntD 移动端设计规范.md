                                                                                            <p style="color:#333333; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:left">Ant Design Mobile 5.0.0-rc.24 发布了。Ant Design Mobile 即 Ant Design 移动端设计规范，<code>antd-mobile</code> 是 Ant Design 的移动规范的 React 实现，服务于蚂蚁及口碑无线业务。</p> 
<p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><strong>更新内容如下：</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>特性 
  <ul> 
   <li><code>Form.Item</code><span> </span>增加了<span> </span><code>arrow</code><span> </span>属性<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fant-design%2Fant-design-mobile%2Fpull%2F4537" target="_blank">#4537</a></li> 
   <li>Picker 增加了<span> </span><code>--header-button-font-size</code><span> </span><code>--title-font-size</code><span> </span><code>--item-font-size</code><span> </span>CSS 变量<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fant-design%2Fant-design-mobile%2Fpull%2F4744" target="_blank">#4744</a></li> 
   <li>Input 和 TextArea 增加了<span> </span><code>onCompositionStart</code><span> </span>和<span> </span><code>onCompositionEnd</code><span> </span>属性</li> 
  </ul> </li> 
 <li>优化 
  <ul> 
   <li>优化了 Popover 和 Form 的样式</li> 
   <li>调整了 Popover 和 Popover.Menu Props 的 ts 定义，和文档保持一致</li> 
   <li>重构了 Popover.Menu 的内部实现逻辑</li> 
   <li>完善了 Form 组件的一系列 ts 类型定义，并且更新了对应的文档</li> 
  </ul> </li> 
 <li>修复 
  <ul> 
   <li>修复了 TextArea 对 emoji 等特殊内容的字数统计在不同浏览器下行为不一致的问题</li> 
  </ul> </li> 
</ul> 
<p>更新说明：<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fant-design%2Fant-design-mobile%2Freleases%2Ftag%2Fv5.0.0-rc.24" target="_blank">https://github.com/ant-design/ant-design-mobile/releases/tag/v5.0.0-rc.24</a></p>
