                                                        <p>MongoDB 5.0.1 现已正式发布，该版本部分更新内容如下：</p> 
<p><strong>Replication</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li> <p><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fjira.mongodb.org%2Fbrowse%2FSERVER-58398" target="_blank">SERVER-58398</a> Tenant migration 无限期挂起</p> </li> 
</ul> 
<p><strong>Internals</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fjira.mongodb.org%2Fbrowse%2FSERVER-58142" target="_blank">SERVER-58142</a> TenantOplogBatcher 应该在下一个批处理回调被调用并出现错误状态时过渡到完成</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fjira.mongodb.org%2Fbrowse%2FSERVER-58171" target="_blank">SERVER-58171</a> 更改 time-series granularity 不会更新视图定义</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fjira.mongodb.org%2Fbrowse%2FSERVER-58489" target="_blank">SERVER-58489</a> 当有一个重复的名字作为视图时，集合的创建卡在一个无限的 writeConflictRetry 循环中</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fjira.mongodb.org%2Fbrowse%2FSERVER-58546" target="_blank">SERVER-58546</a> 为 applyOps 添加日志消息以预览将应用哪些 oplog 条目</li> 
</ul>
