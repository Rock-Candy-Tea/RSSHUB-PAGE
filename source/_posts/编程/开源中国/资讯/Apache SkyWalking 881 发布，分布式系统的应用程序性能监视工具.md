 <div style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
  <div style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
   <p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0">Apache SkyWalking 8.8.1 已正式<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fskywalking.apache.org%2Fevents%2Frelease-apache-skywalking-apm-8-8-1%2F" target="_blank">发布</a>，这是针对<a href="https://www.oschina.net/news/162053/apache-skywalking-8-8-0-released"><span> </span>8.8.0<span> </span></a>的错误修复版本。</p> 
   <p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0">主要更新内容如下：</p> 
   <p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"><strong>OAP Server</strong></p> 
   <ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
    <li>修复用于 OpenSearch 存储的 ElasticSearch 客户端的错误（反）序列化程序</li> 
    <li>修复使用带有标签的 traces 查询会导致报错的问题</li> 
    <li>使用 e2e simple cases 替代 e2e-v2</li> 
    <li>修复端点依赖被破坏的问题</li> 
   </ul> 
   <p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"><strong>UI</strong></p> 
   <ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
    <li>删除端点依赖的重复调用</li> 
   </ul> 
   <p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fapache%2Fskywalking%2Fmilestone%2F104%3Fclosed%3D1" target="_blank">更多内容点此查看</a>。</p> 
   <p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0">SkyWalking 是分布式系统的应用程序性能监视工具，专为微服务、云原生架构和基于容器（Docker、K8s、Mesos）架构而设计，提供分布式追踪、服务网格遥测分析、度量聚合和可视化一体化解决方案。</p> 
   <p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0">下载地址：<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fskywalking.apache.org%2Fdownloads%2F" target="_blank">https://skywalking.apache.org/downloads/</a></p>
