                                                                                            <p>txtai 4.4 已发布。txtai 是一个人工智能驱动的搜索引擎，可以在文本的各个部分上建立一个 AI 驱动的索引。 txtai 支持构建文本索引以执行相似性搜索并创建基于问-答的系统。此外，txtai 还具有用于 zero-shot 分类的功能。</p> 
<p><img src="https://static.oschina.net/uploads/space/2021/0125/132438_duPt_2744687.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p> 
<p style="color:#333333; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><strong>新特性</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>增加语义搜索的可解释性 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fneuml%2Ftxtai%2Fissues%2F248" target="_blank">#248</a>)</li> 
 <li>增加笔记本覆盖模型的可解释性 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fneuml%2Ftxtai%2Fissues%2F249" target="_blank">#249</a>)</li> 
 <li>增加 txtai 控制台 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fneuml%2Ftxtai%2Fissues%2F252" target="_blank">#252</a>)</li> 
 <li>增加序列 pipeline (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fneuml%2Ftxtai%2Fissues%2F261" target="_blank">#261</a>)</li> 
 <li>添加脚本来训练查询翻译模型 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fneuml%2Ftxtai%2Fissues%2F265" target="_blank">#265</a>)</li> 
 <li>在嵌入搜索中添加查询翻译逻辑 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fneuml%2Ftxtai%2Fissues%2F266" target="_blank">#266</a>)</li>
