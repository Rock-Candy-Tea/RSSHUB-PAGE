 <p><span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#333333">Electron 15.0.0 现已发布，主要更新内容如下： </span></p> 
 <p><strong>Fixes</strong></p> 
 <ul> 
  <li>修复了在 macOS 上多次调用<code>BrowserWindow.setResizable()</code>可能导致缩放按钮被错误禁用的问题。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F31014" target="_blank">#31014</a> </li> 
  <li>修复了子窗口不能继承正确的透明度和背景颜色设置的问题。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F31049" target="_blank">#31049</a></li> 
  <li>修复了以编程方式关闭可拖动无框子窗口时潜在的崩溃问题。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F31027" target="_blank">#31027</a></li> 
  <li>None <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F30995" target="_blank">#30995</a></li> 
 </ul> 
 <p><strong>Other Changes</strong></p> 
 <ul> 
  <li>将 Chromium 更新为 94.0.4606.51。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F30895" target="_blank">#30895</a></li> 
 </ul>
