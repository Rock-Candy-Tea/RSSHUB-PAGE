                                                                                            <p>openSUSE Leap 15.4 已<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fnews.opensuse.org%2F2022%2F06%2F08%2Fleap-offers-new-features-familiar-stability%2F" target="_blank">发布</a>，<span style="color:#333333">openSUSE 是基于企业发行版 SUSE Linux Enterprise (SLE) 的社区发行版。</span></p> 
<p>Leap 15.4 的新功能是 <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fen.opensuse.org%2FopenSUSE%3ARoadmap%23Schedule_for_Leap_Micro_5.2" target="_blank">Leap Micro 5.2</a>。Leap Micro 是一个现代轻量级操作系统，非常适合主机容器和虚拟化工作负载。Leap Micro 非常适合分散式计算环境、边缘使用和嵌入式/物联网部署，它提供自动化管理和补丁。</p> 
<p>OpenSUSE Leap 15.4 还通过 Cisco OpenH264 集成引入了改进的多媒体编解码器安装、开源图形驱动程序改进、提供 NVIDIA RTX 30 签名固件包、打包 PHP 8.1 等等。</p> 
<p>软件也得到了更新，KDE Plasma 5.24、GNOME 41、Enlightenment 0.25 和 MATE 1.26 可作为桌面选项使用。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fsystemd%2Fsystemd" target="_blank">systemd</a> 更新到了 249 版本。</p> 
<p>更多版本更新内容可查看<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fnews.opensuse.org%2F2022%2F06%2F08%2Fleap-offers-new-features-familiar-stability%2F" target="_blank">发布公告</a>，<span style="color:#000000">要</span>下载 ISO 映像，请访问 <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fget.opensuse.org%2Fleap%2F%E3%80%82" target="_blank">https://get.opensuse.org/leap/。</a></p> 
<p><span style="color:#000000">随着 15.4 的发布，openSUSE Leap 15.3 将在六个月后 EOL。</span> </p>
