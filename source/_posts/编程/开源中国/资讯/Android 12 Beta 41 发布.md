                                                        <p>如果用户手中的设备已经在运行开发者预览版或测试版，将可以直接通过 OTA 的方式更新到 Beta 4.1 版本。</p> 
<p>Android 12 Beta 4 的这个小更新包括以下修复：</p> 
<ul> 
 <li>修正了一个导致一些设备在重启设备后卡在启动循环中的问题 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fissuetracker.google.com%2Fissues%2F193789343" target="_blank">Issue #193789343</a>)</li> 
 <li>修正了一个问题，该问题会导致设备的提示通知有时无法显示，需要用户手动下拉通知栏来查看 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fissuetracker.google.com%2Fissues%2F196264672" target="_blank">Issue #196264672</a>)</li> 
 <li>修正了一个在投屏时，设备音量控制有时无法正常使用的问题 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fissuetracker.google.com%2Fissues%2F196528545" target="_blank">Issue #196528545</a>)</li> 
 <li>修正了面部解锁在某些设备上无法使用的问题 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fissuetracker.google.com%2Fissues%2F189819846" target="_blank">Issue #189819846</a>)</li> 
 <li>修正了一个用户无法在设置菜单中向电话应用授予 "附近设备" 权限的问题。该问题会导致用户试图通过蓝牙设备拨打电话时，电话应用出现循环崩溃 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fissuetracker.google.com%2Fissues%2F196325588" target="_blank">Issue #196325588</a>)</li> 
 <li>修正了将手机连接到 VPN 后，手机无法上网的问题 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fissuetracker.google.com%2Fissues%2F196237480" target="_blank">Issue #196237480</a>)</li> 
</ul> 
<p>更多详情可查看：<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fdeveloper.android.com%2Fabout%2Fversions%2F12%2Frelease-notes%23beta-2.1" target="_blank">https://developer.android.com/about/versions/12/release-notes#beta-2.1</a></p>
