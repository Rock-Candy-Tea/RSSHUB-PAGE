  <div style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
   <p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0">webpack 5.64.4 现已发布。webpack 是一个模块打包器，主要目的是在浏览器上打包 JavaScript 文件。</p> 
   <p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0">具体更新内容如下：</p> 
   <p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"><span style="color:#24292f"><strong>Bug 修复</strong></span></p> 
   <ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
    <li>修复了标记的模板文字评估</li> 
    <li>修复带有 ESM 的 ModuleFederation</li> 
    <li>修复带有 intial splitChunks 的 outputModule</li> 
   </ul> 
   <p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"><strong>Performance</strong></p> 
   <ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
    <li>升级 watchpack 以加快 watcher 的更新</li> 
    <li>在 watchpack 和 webpack 中分别跟踪文件和目录时间戳</li> 
   </ul> 
   <p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"><strong>Developer Experience</strong></p> 
   <ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
    <li>在 mismatch warning 中显示单例共享模块的来源</li> 
   </ul> 
   <p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0">更新说明：<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fwebpack%2Fwebpack%2Freleases%2Ftag%2Fv5.64.4" target="_blank">https://github.com/webpack/webpack/releases/tag/v5.64.4</a></p>
