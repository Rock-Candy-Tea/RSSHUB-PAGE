<div class="content">
                                                                                            <p><span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#333333">Qt Creator 5.0.3 现已</span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.qt.io%2Fblog%2Fqt-creator-5.0.3-released" target="_blank">发布</a><span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#333333">。</span>此版本修复了在 ARM Mac 上读取 qmake 项目时发生的崩溃，以及 <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fcode.qt.io%2Fcgit%2Fqt-creator%2Fqt-creator.git%2Ftree%2Fdist%2Fchanges-5.0.3.md%3Fh%3D5.0" target="_blank">change log</a><span style="color:#09102b"> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fcode.qt.io%2Fcgit%2Fqt-creator%2Fqt-creator.git%2Ftree%2Fdist%2Fchanges-5.0.3.md%3Fh%3D5.0" target="_blank">中</a>描述的其他一些问题。</p> 
<p><strong>主要更新内容</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>修复了关机时可能出现的崩溃</li> 
 <li>修复了打开 invalid movie 时的崩溃</li> 
 <li>修复了对 QMAKE_EXTRA_COMPILERS 的处理（QTCREATORBUG-26323）</li> 
 <li>修复了在 ARM Macs 上打开 qmake 项目时的崩溃（QTBUG-97085）</li> 
 <li>修复了 qmake 项目安装步骤中的问题（QTCREATORBUG-26357）</li> 
</ul>
