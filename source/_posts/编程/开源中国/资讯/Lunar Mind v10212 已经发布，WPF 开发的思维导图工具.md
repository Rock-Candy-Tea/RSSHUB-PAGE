                                                        <p>Lunar Mind v1.0.2.12 已经发布，WPF 开发的思维导图工具</p> 
<p>此版本更新内容包括：</p> 
<p>Syncfusion 的 <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.syncfusion.com%2Fdownloads%2Fmetrostudio" target="_blank">Metro Studio</a> 提供了数以千计的矢量图标，而且提供了这些图标的 Xaml SVG 描述字符串。</p> 
<blockquote> 
 <p><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.syncfusion.com%2Fdownloads%2Fmetrostudio" target="_blank">https://www.syncfusion.com/downloads/metrostudio</a> 页面上有：</p> 
 <ul> 
  <li>Can these icons be used in commercial applications? Yes, the icons are available royalty-free and can be used in commercial applications. Icons can also be used in open source projects and hosted on sites including but not limited to CodePlex, GitHub, BitBucket, etc.</li> 
 </ul> 
</blockquote> 
<p>现在 可以从 <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.syncfusion.com%2Fdownloads%2Fmetrostudio" target="_blank">Metro Studio</a> 中找到某个图标，设置好需要的属性之后，打开 Xaml 代码面板，从中复制这串代码并 <strong>直接粘贴到 Lunar Mind 的某个标题、标题内置图标框、外置图标框</strong> 。</p> 
<p>在 <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.syncfusion.com%2Fdownloads%2Fmetrostudio" target="_blank">Metro Studio</a> 中找到需要的图标，点击“编辑”按钮：</p> 
<p><img alt="Metro Studio" src="https://images.gitee.com/uploads/images/2021/0415/090422_42f5c5e4_310357.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p> 
<p>设置好需要的尺寸、色彩等：</p> 
<p><img alt="设置图标属性" src="https://images.gitee.com/uploads/images/2021/0415/090541_bb101999_310357.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p> 
<p>然后点击“Code”按钮：</p> 
<p><img alt="复制 Xaml 字符串" src="https://images.gitee.com/uploads/images/2021/0415/090711_5edb47b4_310357.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p> 
<p>然后在 Lunar Mind 中选择某个标题或内置（外置）图片框，并按 <code>Ctrl+V</code> 粘贴：</p> 
<p><img alt="粘贴到 Lunar Mind 中的效果" src="https://images.gitee.com/uploads/images/2021/0415/090901_a7289b64_310357.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p> 
<p>可以看到：</p> 
<ul> 
 <li>直接粘贴到标题中，不能改变图标位置（标题文本一定在顶部）</li> 
 <li>粘贴到内置图标框中，可以设置图标与标题文本的相对位置（通过右键菜单）</li> 
 <li>粘贴到外置图标框中，可以随意拖动位置（不支持文本）</li> 
</ul> 
<p>详情查看：<a href="https://gitee.com/lunarsf/lunarmind/releases/v1.0.2.12">https://gitee.com/lunarsf/lunarmind/releases/v1.0.2.12</a></p>
