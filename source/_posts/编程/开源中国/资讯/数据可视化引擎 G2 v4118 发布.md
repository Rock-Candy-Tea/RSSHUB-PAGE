                                                        <p>数据可视化引擎 G2 4.1.18 版本现已发布，更新内容包括： </p> 
<p><strong>New Features</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>legend: 图例支持回调设置 marker (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fantvis%2Fg2%2Fpull%2F3448" target="_blank">#3448</a>) (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fantvis%2Fg2%2Fcommit%2Fed3047e7823470de135a24ad854597316af8a7cc" target="_blank">ed3047e7</a>)</li> 
</ul> 
<p><strong>Bug Fixes</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>修复 view 上没有正确的 scale 时，绘制 annotation 出错 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fantvis%2Fg2%2Fpull%2F3450" target="_blank">#3450</a>) (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fantvis%2Fg2%2Fcommit%2Fd0af40954a574948e34d6132f852ffb4e9bc0bd0" target="_blank">d0af4095</a>)</li> 
</ul> 
<p><strong>Other Changes</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>修复 view.changeData 在重新渲染的时候 报 toFront 为 underfind 的错误 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fantvis%2Fg2%2Fpull%2F3438" target="_blank">#3438</a>) (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fantvis%2Fg2%2Fcommit%2F76e08d066a932c402e0acc57c22d8aed4bc9b3b4" target="_blank">76e08d06</a>)</li> 
</ul>
