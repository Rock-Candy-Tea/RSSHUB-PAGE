                                                                                            <p>ImageMagick 7.1.0-6 已发布，该版本可以在 <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimagemagick.org%2Fscript%2Fdownload.php%23unix">Linux</a>，<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimagemagick.org%2Fscript%2Fdownload.php%23windows">Windows</a>，<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimagemagick.org%2Fscript%2Fdownload.php%23macosx">Mac Os X</a>，<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimagemagick.org%2Fscript%2Fdownload.php%23iOS">iOS</a>，<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fcherryleafroad%2FAndroid-ImageMagick7">Android</a> OS 等平台上运行。</p> 
<p>ImageMagick 是一个用来创建、编辑、合成图片的软件。它可以读取、转换、写入超过 200 种格式的图片，包括 PNG、JPEG、GIF、HEIC、TIFF、DPX、EXR、WebP、Postscript、PDF 和 SVG 等等。</p> 
<p>ImageMagick 可被用于图片切割、颜色替换、各种效果的应用，图片的旋转、组合，文本，直线， 多边形，椭圆，曲线，附加到图片伸展旋转等。支持 Linux、Windows、Mac OS X、iOS、Android OS 平台。</p> 
<p>7.1.0-6 版本的更新内容包括有：</p> 
<ul> 
 <li>调整 bilevel 图像类型（参考 <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FImageMagick%2FImageMagick%2Fissues%2F4121" target="_blank">https://github.com/ImageMagick/ImageMagick/issues/4121</a>）。</li> 
 <li>从 LAB 转换到 RGB 会导致错误的颜色（参考 <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FImageMagick%2FImageMagick%2Fissues%2F4105" target="_blank">https://github.com/ImageMagick/ImageMagick/issues/4105</a>）。</li> 
</ul> 
<p>详细更新内容，以及了解以往版本更新可查阅<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimagemagick.org%2Fscript%2Fchangelog.php">更新记录</a>。</p>
