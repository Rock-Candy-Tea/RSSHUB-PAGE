                                                        <p>Eclipse Jetty 9.4.40 已发布，此版本包含 bugfix 和功能改进，可从项目下载页面或 Maven Central 仓库获取：</p> 
<ul> 
 <li> <p>Eclipse: <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.eclipse.org%2Fjetty%2Fdownload.html" target="_blank">https://www.eclipse.org/jetty/download.html</a></p> </li> 
 <li> <p>Maven Central: <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Frepo1.maven.org%2Fmaven2%2Forg%2Feclipse%2Fjetty%2F" target="_blank">https://repo1.maven.org/maven2/org/eclipse/jetty/</a></p> </li> 
</ul> 
<p><strong>主要变化</strong></p> 
<div style="text-align:start"> 
 <ul> 
  <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Feclipse%2Fjetty.project%2Fissues%2F6168" target="_blank"><span style="color:null">#6168</span></a><span style="color:null"> - 改进对未消费内容的处理，<span style="background-color:null">GzipHandler 用户应该升级</span></span></li> 
  <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Feclipse%2Fjetty.project%2Fissues%2F6148" target="_blank"><span style="color:null">#6148</span></a><span style="color:null"> - </span>修复 Jetty start.jar 总是将 jetty.tag.version 报告为<span style="color:null"><code>master</code></span>的问题</li> 
  <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Feclipse%2Fjetty.project%2Fissues%2F6105" target="_blank"><span style="color:null">#6105</span></a><span style="color:null"> - </span>修复 <span style="color:null">HttpConnection.getBytesIn() </span>对于 chunk 内容的请求不正确的问题</li> 
  <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Feclipse%2Fjetty.project%2Fissues%2F6082" target="_blank"><span style="color:null">#6082</span></a><span style="color:null"> - 压缩 SSL 连接，<span style="background-color:null">在 jetty 服务器或 jetty 客户端上使用 SSL/TLS 的用户应该升级</span></span></li> 
 </ul>
