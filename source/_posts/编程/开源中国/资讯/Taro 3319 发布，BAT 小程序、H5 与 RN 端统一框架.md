                                                                                            <p style="color:#333333; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:left"><span style="color:#333333">Taro 3.3.19 发布了。Taro 是一个开放式跨端跨框架解决方案，支持使用 React/Vue/Nerv 等框架来开发微信/京东/百度/支付宝/字节跳动/ QQ 小程序/H5 等应用。</span></p> 
<p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><span style="color:#333333">此版本更新内容包括：</span></p> 
<h4 style="text-align:start"><strong>修复</strong></h4> 
<p style="text-align:start"><strong>RN</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>优化 Android Input 组件默认 padding 参数</li> 
 <li>修复 Swiper 组件只有一个字节点问题</li> 
 <li>修复 Swiper 组件 current > count -1 问题</li> 
</ul> 
<p style="text-align:start"><strong>H5</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>修复 Vue3 AppId 配置问题，fix<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FNervJS%2Ftaro%2Fissues%2F10974" target="_blank">#10974</a></li> 
 <li>修复 Taro-H5 rollup Shaking 删除样式问题，fix<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FNervJS%2Ftaro%2Fissues%2F10958" target="_blank">#10958</a></li> 
 <li>修复 uploadFile、downloadFile task 错误</li> 
 <li>修改 Taro-H5 正常打包和引用方式</li> 
</ul> 
<p>更新说明：<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FNervJS%2Ftaro%2Freleases%2Ftag%2Fv3.3.19" target="_blank">https://github.com/NervJS/taro/releases/tag/v3.3.19</a></p>
