                                                                                            <p><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fblog.jetbrains.com%2Fwebstorm%2F2022%2F02%2Fwebstorm-2021-3-2%2F" target="_blank">WebStorm 2021.3.2 已发布</a>，这是 <a href="https://www.oschina.net/news/171492/webstorm-2021-3-released">WebStorm 2021.3</a> 的第二个 bug-fix 更新版本。</p> 
<p>此版本主要修复了以下问题：</p> 
<ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
 <li>解决了关于 Vue 支持的一些问题 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FWEB-50085" target="_blank">WEB-50085</a>,<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FWEB-46606" target="_blank">WEB-46606</a>,<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FWEB-52858" target="_blank">WEB-52858</a>,<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FWEB-54223" target="_blank">WEB-54223</a>, and<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FWEB-54109" target="_blank">WEB-54109</a>)</li> 
 <li>对于托管在 WSL 2 中的项目，WebStorm 在索引文件时不再冻结 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FIDEA-286059" target="_blank">IDEA-286059</a>)</li> 
 <li>在 Angular 中，运行工具窗口中显示的编译器错误现在可被点击 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FWEB-49515" target="_blank">WEB-49515</a>).</li> 
 <li>修复在相应对话框中禁用 Rebase 按钮的问题 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FIDEA-282538" target="_blank">IDEA-282538</a>)</li> 
</ul>
