                                                        <p>Android Studio 是开发 Android 应用的官方 IDE，包含构建 Android 应用所需的所有功能。Android Studio 4.2.2 稳定版本已正式推出。本次更新包括对以下问题的修复：</p> 
<p><strong>Android Gradle 插件</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fissuetracker.google.com%2Fissues%2F187353303" target="_blank">Issue #187353303</a>: AGP 4.2.0 产生的测试 APK 缺少一个类，可能是由于混合 java/kotlin 源集造成的；</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fissuetracker.google.com%2Fissues%2F187420313" target="_blank">Issue #187420313</a>: buildConfigField 替换警告打印出的值，该值可能包含敏感信息提示；</li> 
</ul> 
<p><strong>Lint</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fissuetracker.google.com%2Fissues%2F187851920" target="_blank">Issue #187851920</a>: 使用 lint.do.not.reuse.uast.env 时，Lint 在 PrivateApiDetector 中崩溃</li> 
</ul> 
<p><strong>Shrinker (R8)</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fissuetracker.google.com%2Fissues%2F187977749" target="_blank">Issue #187977749</a>: desugar 覆盖 PriorityQueue 中的最终方法 spliterator；</li> 
</ul> 
<p><strong>Tool Windows</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fissuetracker.google.com%2Fissues%2F187415389" target="_blank">Issue #187415389</a>: 当 Gradle 任务列表未填充时，添加带有链接的指示器；</li> 
</ul>
