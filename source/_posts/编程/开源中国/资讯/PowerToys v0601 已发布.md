<div class="content">
                                                                    
                                                        <p>PowerToys v0.60.1 已发布，这是一个补丁版本，用于修复 v0.60.0 中的问题，以修复一些对稳定性很重要的错误。</p> 
<ul> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fmicrosoft%2FPowerToys%2Fpull%2F19253" target="_blank">#19253</a> - 更新了 PowerToys 的 Microsoft Store 提交，在安装时不显示 UI。</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fmicrosoft%2FPowerToys%2Fissues%2F19023" target="_blank">#19023</a> - 修复了当多个显示器共享相同序列号时，导致 FancyZones 无法正确应用模板的错误。</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fmicrosoft%2FPowerToys%2Fissues%2F19233" target="_blank">#19233</a> - 修复了 PowerRename 和 ImageResizer Windows 11 第 1 层上下文菜单在开发内幕上的不兼容性问题。</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fmicrosoft%2FPowerToys%2Fissues%2F19219" target="_blank">#19219</a> - 旧的上下文菜单现在也显示在 Windows 11 上，以说明其他第三方软件可能覆盖 Windows 11 上下文菜单的配置。</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fmicrosoft%2FPowerToys%2Fissues%2F19263" target="_blank">#19263</a> - 修复了在屏幕左上角激活“查找我的鼠标”覆盖时挂起的错误。</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fmicrosoft%2FPowerToys%2Fissues%2F19223" target="_blank">#19223</a> - FancyZones 编辑器在未检测到显示器连接的配置上崩溃的问题。</li> 
</ul> 
<p>更新公告：<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fmicrosoft%2FPowerToys%2Freleases%2Ftag%2Fv0.60.1" target="_blank">https://github.com/microsoft/PowerToys/releases/tag/v0.60.1</a></p>
