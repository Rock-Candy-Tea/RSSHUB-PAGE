                                                        <p>Electron 13.1.5 现已发布，主要更新内容如下： </p> 
<p><strong>Fixes</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>修复了当<code>nativeWindowOpen: false</code>时，使用<code>setWindowOpenHandler</code>拒绝<code>window.open</code>的挂起问题。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F29882" target="_blank">＃29882 </a></li> 
 <li>修复了在启用<code>contextIsolation</code>时下拉菜单在 DevTools 中不起作用的问题。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F29885" target="_blank">#29885</a> </li> 
 <li>修复了一个问题：如果 webRequest.onBeforeSendHeaders 回调响应对象中没有包含可选的"requestHeaders "参数，request headers 会被清除。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F29836" target="_blank">＃29836 </a></li> 
 <li>修复了有时不会为 macOS 上的子窗口保留<code>setAlwaysOnTop</code>值的问题。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F29956" target="_blank">＃29956 </a>（同样在<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F29957" target="_blank">12</a>，<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F29855" target="_blank">14</a>）</li> 
 <li>修复了在 macOS 上使用<code>Geolocation</code>时崩溃的问题。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F29912" target="_blank">#29912</a></li> 
</ul> 
<p><strong>Other Changes</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>将 Chromium 更新为 91.0.4472.124。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F29774" target="_blank">#29774</a></li> 
</ul>
