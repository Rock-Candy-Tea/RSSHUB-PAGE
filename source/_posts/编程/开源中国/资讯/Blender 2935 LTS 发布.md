                                                        <p>Blender 是一个免费和开源的 3D 计算机图形软件工具集，用于创建动画电影、视觉效果、艺术、3D 打印模型、交互式 3D 应用、VR 和计算机游戏。</p> 
<p>近日，Blender 2.93.5 LTS 正式发布，该版本的更新内容如下：</p> 
<ul> 
 <li>选择 3D 跟踪标记时发生崩溃 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fdeveloper.blender.org%2FT90651" target="_blank">T90651</a>)</li> 
 <li>图像编辑器没有从当前选定的通道中采样颜色 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fdeveloper.blender.org%2FT90772" target="_blank">T90772</a>)</li> 
 <li>PulseAudio 在新场景中中断了动画播放 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fdeveloper.blender.org%2FT90887" target="_blank">T90887</a>)</li> 
 <li>Blender 的弹出窗口在错误的屏幕上打开 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fdeveloper.blender.org%2FT90752" target="_blank">T90752</a>)</li> 
 <li>修复 SCRIPT_OT_python_file_run 中的缓冲区大小不匹配 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fdeveloper.blender.org%2FrB515bdda3d9c" target="_blank">rB515bdda3d9c</a>)</li> 
 <li>修复 "文本到对象" 创建不可见对象的问题 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fdeveloper.blender.org%2FrB933c6b7d8a3" target="_blank">rB933c6b7d8a3</a>)</li> 
 <li>修复 T88909：Win32 getTitle() UTF8 支持 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fdeveloper.blender.org%2FrBd6facd44b53" target="_blank">rBd6facd44b53</a>)</li> 
 <li>修复将缩略图缩放为零尺寸的错误 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fdeveloper.blender.org%2FrB16637e7ff40" target="_blank">rB16637e7ff40</a>)</li> 
 <li>当电脑进入睡眠、休眠状态或出现屏保时，音频导致的播放问题 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fdeveloper.blender.org%2FT88887" target="_blank">T88887</a>)</li> 
 <li>Grease Pencil：在 Transform 中添加驱动程序，在播放过程中不会按预期运行 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fdeveloper.blender.org%2FT88433" target="_blank">T88433</a>)</li> 
 <li>自 2.93.4 版本起，“Scale to Fit” 无法正常使用 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fdeveloper.blender.org%2FT91401" target="_blank">T91401</a>)</li> 
 <li>修复在分配 id-property 序列时发生错误所引起的内存泄漏 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fdeveloper.blender.org%2FrBec448d5a6ac" target="_blank">rBec448d5a6ac</a>)</li> 
 <li>Workbench 在渲染时不遵循 GP 简化选项 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fdeveloper.blender.org%2FT91448" target="_blank">T91448</a>)</li> 
 <li>GPencil：Scripting weight_get 和 Vertex Groups 未按预期运行 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fdeveloper.blender.org%2FT91511" target="_blank">T91511</a>)</li> 
 <li>GPencil：Interpolate Sequence 崩溃并退出 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fdeveloper.blender.org%2FT91534" target="_blank">T91534</a>)</li> 
 <li>Intel HD graphics 显卡：将 Roughness Value 改为 0 会导致网格变黑 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fdeveloper.blender.org%2FT88754" target="_blank">T88754</a>)</li> 
 <li>VSE 播放周期性地拒绝运行 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fdeveloper.blender.org%2FT89045" target="_blank">T89045</a>)</li> 
 <li>GPencil: Tint 修改器 "应用" 会删除效果 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fdeveloper.blender.org%2FT91607" target="_blank">T91607</a>)</li> 
 <li>Texture Paint Stencil 不使用指定的 UV Layer. (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fdeveloper.blender.org%2FT91557" target="_blank">T91557</a>)</li> 
 <li>修复 'WM_window_find_under_cursor'. (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fdeveloper.blender.org%2FrB1ff10bb6d18" target="_blank">rB1ff10bb6d18</a>)</li> 
 <li>……</li> 
</ul>
