  <div style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
   <p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0">Wekan 5.88 发布了，Wekan 是模仿 Trello 且与它最接近的开源 kanban，它的前后端都是使用 JavaScript 编写，基于 Meteor 框架。Wekan 简化界面、支持中文、拒绝其它花哨的功能，支持项目管理所需的标签、注释、添加成员等功能，而且它还有一个免费托管的 demo 站点，除了可以下载安装使用，还可以将 Wekan 通过 Dockerfile 镜像在一个容器里安全运行。</p> 
   <p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0">此版本带来以下更新项：</p> 
   <p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"><strong>关键安全修复</strong></p> 
   <ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
    <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fwekan%2Fwekan%2Fpull%2F4252" target="_blank">用户只能在他们有权限的看板查看存档卡。</a></li> 
    <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fwekan%2Fwekan%2Fpull%2F4252" target="_blank">修复<span> </span><code>Boards.userBoards()</code><span> </span>方法，以在确定看板可访问性时可以考虑到用户的组织和团队。</a></li> 
   </ul> 
   <p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"><span data-darkreader-inline-color style="--darkreader-inline-color:#cecac3; color:#24292f"><strong>添加新功能</strong></span></p> 
   <ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
    <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fwekan%2Fwekan%2Fpull%2F4252" target="_blank">新的<span> </span><code>debug</code><span> </span>搜索操作符，有助于调试搜索问题</a></li> 
    <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fwekan%2Fwekan%2Fpull%2F4252" target="_blank">新的<span> </span><code>org</code><span> </span>和<span> </span><code>team</code><span> </span>搜索运算符，用于查找属于给定组织或团队的看板卡</a></li> 
    <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fwekan%2Fwekan%2Fpull%2F4252" target="_blank">列表看板有新的管理报告</a></li> 
   </ul> 
   <p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"><strong>修复 bug</strong></p> 
   <p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"><span data-darkreader-inline-color style="--darkreader-inline-color:#c9c5be; color:#2e3033">修正美人鱼图错误：超过最大的可调用堆栈大小</span></p> 
   <ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0">
