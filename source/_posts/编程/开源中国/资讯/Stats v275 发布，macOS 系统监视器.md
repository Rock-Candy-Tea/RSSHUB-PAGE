                                                                                            <p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start">Stats 是一款开源的 macOS 系统监控工具，可以驻留在菜单栏中对 CPU、GPU、内存、磁盘和网络等进行实时监测。</p> 
<p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start">目前 Stats v2.7.5 发布了 ，此版本带来如下变更:</p> 
<ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
 <li>在低电量通知中添加了 60% 的电池电量（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fexelban%2Fstats%2Fissues%2F825" target="_blank">#825</a>）</li> 
 <li>将 10.13 macOS High Sierra 添加到操作系统列表 (<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fexelban%2Fstats%2Fissues%2F832" target="_blank">#832</a><span> </span>)</li> 
 <li><span style="color:#2e3033">将 CPU 和 GPU 的平均值和最热值(计算后)加入传感器列表</span>（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fexelban%2Fstats%2Fissues%2F826" target="_blank">#826</a>）</li> 
 <li>删除了 DMG 中的重复图标（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fexelban%2Fstats%2Fissues%2F835" target="_blank">#835</a>）</li> 
</ul> 
<p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start">更新公告：<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fexelban%2Fstats%2Freleases%2Ftag%2Fv2.7.5" target="_blank">https://github.com/exelban/stats/releases/tag/v2.7.5</a></p>
