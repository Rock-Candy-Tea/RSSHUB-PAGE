<div class="content">
                                                                                            <p>基于 Chromium 的 Vivaldi 浏览器已<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fvivaldi.com%2Fblog%2Fvivaldi-5-2%2F" target="_blank">发布</a> 5.2 版本，该版本引入了侧边栏阅读列表、隐私统计等功能，桌面版本可用于 Windows、Mac OS 和 Linux。</p> 
<h2>侧边栏阅读列表</h2> 
<h2><img alt height="394" src="https://oscimg.oschina.net/oscnet/up-2960b3ecd84a375938538436a0b627ab696.webp" width="701" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></h2> 
<p>可以在这个滑出式侧边栏（面板）查看和管理希望阅读的内容，该面板还包含一系列按钮，包括<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fhelp.vivaldi.com%2Fdesktop%2Fbookmarks-speed-dial%2Fbookmarks%2F" target="_blank">书签</a>、<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fhelp.vivaldi.com%2Fdesktop%2Fpanels%2Fdownloads%2F" target="_blank">下载</a>、<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fhelp.vivaldi.com%2Fdesktop%2Fnavigation%2Fhistory%2F" target="_blank">历史记录</a>、<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fvivaldi.com%2Fblog%2Fvivaldi-gets-notes-manager-and-configurable-menus%2F" target="_blank">注释</a>、<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fhelp.vivaldi.com%2Fdesktop%2Ftabs%2Fwindow-panel%2F" target="_blank">窗口面板</a>、<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fvivaldi.com%2Ffeatures%2Ftranslate%2F" target="_blank">翻译</a>和 <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fhelp.vivaldi.com%2Fdesktop%2Fpanels%2Fweb-panels%2F" target="_blank">Web 面板</a> 。</p> 
<ul> 
 <li><strong>直观地管理列表</strong>：双击所选项目，相应页面会立即在新选项卡中打开。</li> 
 <li><strong>轻松搜索和过滤：</strong>面板中的搜索栏可以帮助过滤阅读列表，识别页面标题中的 URL 和字词。 </li> 
 <li><strong>高效排序和组织</strong>：菜单排序可以选择按标题、阅读状态、更新日期或地址重新排序列表。</li> 
</ul> 
<p><img alt height="394" src="https://oscimg.oschina.net/oscnet/up-11b5ec89fce04a3dad9dba67e7c619477c2.webp" width="700" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p> 
<p><span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#2f374f">可以使用右键菜单打开所选项目（在新选项卡中）、复制其地址、将条目标记为已读或从列表中删除已保存的项目，支持右键快速添加站点到阅读列表中：</span></p> 
<p><span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#2f374f"><img alt height="394" src="https://oscimg.oschina.net/oscnet/up-f6ad61e2e1620b75cc416f0004b32b0b5a6.webp" width="700" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p> 
<p><span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#2f374f">同时，该功能支持在 Android 手机、平板电脑、Chromebook 、以及所有 PC 端同步。</span></p> 
<h2><span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#2f374f">隐私统计功能</span></h2> 
<p><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fhelp.vivaldi.com%2Fdesktop%2Fprivacy%2Ftracking-and-ad-blocking%2F" target="_blank">广告和跟踪器拦截器</a>可以<span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#2f374f">看到阻止跟踪的广告和跟踪器的数量，点击地址栏上的盾牌图标，可以看到每次访问每个站点的广告和跟踪器拦截器统计信息。</span></p> 
<p><span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#2f374f"><img alt height="394" src="https://oscimg.oschina.net/oscnet/up-0c0a755e52ea9a88cbf78571ea662d59715.webp" width="700" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p> 
<p style="margin-left:auto; margin-right:auto; text-align:left"><span><span><span style="color:#2f374f"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#ffffff"><span><span><span>还有隐私详情信息页面，可提供三个功能：</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>跟踪器和广告的每个网站细分 </li> 
 <li>每个“跟踪公司”的细分</li> 
 <li>重置累积隐私统计数据</li> 
</ul> 
<h2>其他更改</h2>

 <li><strong><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.qwant.com%2F" target="_blank">Qwant</a> 隐私搜索：</strong>将注重隐私的搜索 Qwant 作为默认设置之一，Qwant 可以在不涉及私人数据得情况下提供优质搜索结果</li> 
 <li><strong>更快的标签拖动：</strong>打磨了一些粗糙的边缘，使标签的拖放更快、更流畅。</li> 
 <li><strong>Vivaldi 邮件、日历和提要阅读器（测试版）</strong></li> 
 <li><strong>...</strong></li> 
</ul>
