                                                        <p style="color:#333333; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:left">carbon 是一个轻量级、语义化、对开发者友好的 Golang 时间处理库，支持链式调用、农历和 gorm、xorm 等主流 orm</p> 
<p style="color:#333333; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:left">目前已被 <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Favelino%2Fawesome-go%23date-and-time" target="_blank">awesome-go</a> 收录，如果您觉得不错，请给个 star 吧 <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fgolang-module%2Fcarbon" target="_blank">github.com/golang-module/carbon</a> <a href="https://gitee.com/golang-module/carbon">gitee.com/golang-module/carbon</a></p> 
<p style="color:#333333; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:left"><strong>更新日志</strong></p>

 <li>修复 ToFormatString() 方法对于支持 i18n 的特殊字符(D, F, M, l)不能输出本地化翻译的 bug(感谢 <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FDrOctavius" target="_blank">DrOctavius</a>)</li> 
 <li>修复 lang/jp.json 语法错误(感谢 <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FDrOctavius" target="_blank">DrOctavius</a> )</li> 
 <li>修复 lang/ro.json 语法错误(感谢 <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fbohehe" target="_blank">bohehe</a>)</li> 
 <li>优化 IsSameXXX() 判断逻辑</li> 
 <li>优化单元测试</li> 
 <li>统一私有方法注释格式</li>
