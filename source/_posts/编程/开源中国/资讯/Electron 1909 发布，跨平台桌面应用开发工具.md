                                                                                            <p><span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#333333">Electron 19.0.9 现已发布，具体更新内容如下：</span></p> 
<p style="color:#333333; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:left"><strong><span><span><span><span><span style="color:#24292f"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#ffffff"><span><span><span>Fixes</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>确保<code>requestMediaKeySystemAccess</code>最终 resolves / rejects。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F34890" target="_blank">#34890 </a></li> 
 <li>修复了在使用无效的<code>titleBarStyle</code>初始化 WCO 后更改窗口设置时可能发生的崩溃。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F34874" target="_blank">#34874</a></li> 
 <li>修复了 X11 Linux 的 alwaysOnTop BrowserWindow 选项。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F34912" target="_blank">#34912</a></li> 
 <li>修复了 macOS 上的 BrowserWindows 被错误地标记为可调整大小的问题。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F34906" target="_blank">#34906</a></li> 
 <li>修复了在 macOS 上调用<code>BrowserWindow.setRepresentedFilename</code>时，<code>titlebarStyle: 'hiddenInset'</code> 或<code>titlebarStyle: 'hidden'</code>无意中移动 traffic light 位置的问题。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F34848" target="_blank">#34848 </a></li> 
 <li>修复了在 Linux 上使用无效的<code>deviceName</code>printing 导致 silent failures 的问题。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F34900" target="_blank">#34900</a></li> 
 <li>修复了启用 WCO 的最小化按钮在某些情况下会被错误地突出显示的问题。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F34839" target="_blank">#34839</a></li> 
 <li>修复了在 Wayland 下运行时为空的 app_id。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F34878" target="_blank">#34878</a></li> 
 <li>防止在 Linux 上的窗口标题中短暂显示“Ozone X11”。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F34929" target="_blank">#34929</a></li> 
</ul>
