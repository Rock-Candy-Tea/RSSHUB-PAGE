 <li>添加了有关从外部路由处理程序进行日志记录的信息（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Ffastify%2Ffastify%2Fpull%2F2982" target="_blank">＃2982</a>）</li>
