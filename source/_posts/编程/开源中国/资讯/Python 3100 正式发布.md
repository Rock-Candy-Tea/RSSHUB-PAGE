                                                        <p>Python 3.10.0 正式发布，Python 3.10.0 是 Python 编程语言的最新主要版本，它包含了许多新特性和优化。</p> 
<p>与 3.9 相比，3.10 系列的主要新特性包括：</p> 
<ul> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.python.org%2Fdev%2Fpeps%2Fpep-0623%2F" target="_blank">PEP 623</a> – 弃用并准备删除 PyUnicodeObject 中的 wstr</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.python.org%2Fdev%2Fpeps%2Fpep-0604%2F" target="_blank">PEP 604</a> – 允许将 union 类型写成 X | Y</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.python.org%2Fdev%2Fpeps%2Fpep-0612%2F" target="_blank">PEP 612</a> – 参数规范变量</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.python.org%2Fdev%2Fpeps%2Fpep-0626%2F" target="_blank">PEP 626</a> – 用于调试和其他工具的精确的行号</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.python.org%2Fdev%2Fpeps%2Fpep-0618%2F" target="_blank">PEP 618</a> – 在 zip 中增加可选的长度检查功能</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fbugs.python.org%2Fissue12782" target="_blank">bpo-12782</a>: 现在正式允许使用带括号的上下文管理器</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.python.org%2Fdev%2Fpeps%2Fpep-0632%2F" target="_blank">PEP 632</a> – 弃用 distutils 模块</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.python.org%2Fdev%2Fpeps%2Fpep-0613%2F" target="_blank">PEP 613</a> – 显式类型别名</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.python.org%2Fdev%2Fpeps%2Fpep-0644%2F" target="_blank">PEP 644</a> – 需要 OpenSSL 1.1.1 或更新版本</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.python.org%2Fdev%2Fpeps%2Fpep-0624%2F" target="_blank">PEP 624</a> – 删除 Py_UNICODE 编码器的 API</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.python.org%2Fdev%2Fpeps%2Fpep-0597%2F" target="_blank">PEP 597</a> – 增加可选的 EncodingWarning</li> 
</ul>
