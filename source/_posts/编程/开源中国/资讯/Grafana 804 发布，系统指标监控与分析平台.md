                                                        <p>Grafana 8.0.4 现已发布。Grafana 是一个功能丰富的指标标准仪表板和图形编辑器，用于分析和监控 Graphite、Elasticsearch、OpenTSDB、Prometheus 和 InfluxDB。</p> 
<p>具体更新内容如下：</p> 
<h4><strong>Features and enhancements</strong></h4> 
<ul> 
 <li><strong>Live：</strong>依靠应用 url 进行来源检查。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fgrafana%2Fgrafana%2Fpull%2F35983" target="_blank">#35983</a></li> 
 <li><strong>PieChart：</strong>图例降序排序，更新占位符以显示默认值…… <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fgrafana%2Fgrafana%2Fpull%2F36062" target="_blank">#36062</a></li> 
 <li><strong>TimeSeries panel：</strong>当阈值模式改变时不要重新初始化绘图。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fgrafana%2Fgrafana%2Fpull%2F35952" target="_blank">#35952</a></li> 
</ul> 
<h4><strong>Bug 修复</strong></h4> 
<ul> 
 <li> <p><strong>Elasticsearch：</strong>允许在 date_histogram 区间内区分大小写的自定义选项。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fgrafana%2Fgrafana%2Fpull%2F36168" target="_blank">#36168</a></p> </li> 
 <li><strong>Elasticsearch：</strong>使用变量时恢复以前的字段命名策略。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fgrafana%2Fgrafana%2Fpull%2F35624" target="_blank">#35624</a></li> 
 <li><strong>Explore：</strong>修复 SQL 数据源之间的查询导入。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fgrafana%2Fgrafana%2Fpull%2F36210" target="_blank">#36210</a></li> 
 <li><strong>InfluxDB：</strong> InfluxQL 查询编辑器：修复保留策略处理。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fgrafana%2Fgrafana%2Fpull%2F36022" target="_blank">#36022</a></li> 
 <li><strong>Loki：</strong>在模板变量查询中发送正确的时间范围。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fgrafana%2Fgrafana%2Fpull%2F36268" target="_blank">#36268</a></li> 
 <li><strong>TimeSeries：</strong>从旧图形面板迁移时保留 RegExp series overrides。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fgrafana%2Fgrafana%2Fpull%2F36134" target="_blank">#36134</a></li> 
</ul>
