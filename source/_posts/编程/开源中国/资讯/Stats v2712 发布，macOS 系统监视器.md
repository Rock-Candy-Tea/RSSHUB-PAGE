                                                        <p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><span style="color:#000000">Stats 是一款开源的 macOS 系统监控工具，可以驻留在菜单栏中对 CPU、GPU、内存、磁盘和网络等进行实时监测。目前 Stats v2.7.12 发布了 ，此版本带来如下变更：</span></p> 
<h2 style="margin-left:.6em; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><strong>关闭的问题</strong></h2> 
<ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
 <li>修复：更正了 Web 图表中颜色的顺序（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fexelban%2Fstats%2Fissues%2F869" target="_blank">#869</a>）</li> 
 <li>修复：在初始化平均传感器时，修复 CPU 和 GPU 传感器的列表过滤（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fexelban%2Fstats%2Fissues%2F892" target="_blank">#892</a>）</li> 
 <li>特性：将 mac studio 添加到设备列表（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fexelban%2Fstats%2Fissues%2F903" target="_blank">#903</a>）</li> 
 <li>特性：添加了一种从<span> </span><span style="color:#24292f">system_profiler</span><span> </span>获取 BLE 电池电量的新方法 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fexelban%2Fstats%2Fissues%2F859" target="_blank">#859</a>)</li> 
</ul> 
<h2 style="margin-left:.6em; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><strong>新功能</strong></h2> 
<ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
 <li>特性：<span style="color:#24292f">增加了平均和最热 SOC 的更新 </span></li> 
</ul>
