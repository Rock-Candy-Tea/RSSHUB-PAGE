                                                                                            <p>Ant Design 4.17.2 现已发布，主要变化如下：</p> 
<ul> 
 <li>修复 Form <code>wrapperCol=&#123;&#123; span: 24 &#125;&#125;</code> 时样式错乱的问题。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fant-design%2Fant-design%2Fpull%2F32981" target="_blank">#32981</a></li> 
 <li>修复 Modal <code>centered</code> 略微偏上的问题。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fant-design%2Fant-design%2Fpull%2F33022" target="_blank">#33022</a></li> 
 <li>Cascader 丢失 <code>suffixIcon</code> 定义问题并且支持 <code>options</code> 泛型定义。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fant-design%2Fant-design%2Fpull%2F33008" target="_blank">#33008</a></li> 
 <li>修复 Input.Search 下 <code>enterButton</code> 内元素上的 <code>onClick</code> 未被触发的问题。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fant-design%2Fant-design%2Fpull%2F32999" target="_blank">#32999</a></li> 
 <li>补充爱沙尼亚语言包。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fant-design%2Fant-design%2Fpull%2F33005" target="_blank">#33005</a></li> 
 <li>Tree 支持泛型以更好的配合 <code>fieldNames</code>。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fant-design%2Fant-design%2Fpull%2F32992" target="_blank">#32992</a></li> 
</ul>
