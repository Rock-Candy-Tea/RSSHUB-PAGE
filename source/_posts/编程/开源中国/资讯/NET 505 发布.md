 <li>EntityFramework Core : <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fdotnet%2Fefcore%2Fissues%3Fq%3Dmilestone%253A5.0.5%2Bis%253Aclosed%2Blabel%253Aservicing-approved" target="_blank">5.0.5</a></li>
