                                                                                            <p>PhpStorm 2022.1.2 预览版日前发布，这是 PhpStorm 即将发布的一个小版本更新。它带来了大量的错误修复和质量的改进。</p> 
<p>以下是最值得注意的变化和错误修复：</p> 
<ul> 
 <li>已修复创建新类对话框中失效的键盘导航 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FWI-66414" target="_blank">WI-66414</a>)</li> 
 <li>已删除向 __destruct() 函数添加返回类型的无效建议 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FWI-66138" target="_blank">WI-66138</a>)</li> 
 <li>在 Blade 文件的特定位置粘贴的一些问题已得到修复 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FWI-66304" target="_blank">WI-66304</a>)</li> 
 <li>在某些情况下与 UML 图交互时，无法关闭或最小化 PhpStorm，该问题已得到修复 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FIDEA-292463" target="_blank">IDEA-292463</a>)</li> 
 <li>当终端有焦点时，Search Everywhere 无法正常使用，该问题已被修复 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FIDEA-290102%2FSearch-Everywhere-double-shift-stopped-working-when-Terminal-has" target="_blank">IDEA-290102</a>)</li> 
</ul>
