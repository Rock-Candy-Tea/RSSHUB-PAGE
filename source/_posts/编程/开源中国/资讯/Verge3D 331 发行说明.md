                                                                                            <p>本次更新主要修复了Verge3D 3.3 for Blender, 3ds Max和Maya中出现的几个错误。在3ds Max版中引入了新的OSL节点，并且通过优化进一步提升了应用的加载速度。</p> 
<h2>OSL 节点</h2> 
<p>OSL着色器列表中添加了新的OSL节点。</p> 
<p style="text-align:center"><img alt="image" src="https://oscimg.oschina.net/oscnet/up-e9eab33ec9c216eeae6b361d5fcfc72e51c.JPEG" width="1024" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p> 
<p>此次新增节点包括：<strong>Mandelbro</strong><strong>t(曼德勃罗</strong><strong><span style="color:#333333">集)</span></strong>, <strong>Digits(数字)</strong> (如上图所示), <strong>Rivets</strong><strong>(</strong><strong>铆钉</strong><strong>)</strong> (如下图所示), <strong>Radians-to-Degrees(</strong><strong>弧度转角度</strong><strong>)</strong>, <strong>UVW Row Of</strong><strong>fset(</strong><strong>UVW行偏移)</strong>, <strong>Toon Width(卡通宽度)</strong>, <strong>Wave Length</strong><strong>(</strong><strong>波长</strong><strong>)</strong>, <strong>Half Tone Dot</strong><strong>s(半色调点</strong><strong>)</strong>, <strong>Weave(半色调点)</strong>, <strong>Candy(</strong>糖果<strong>)</strong>, <strong>UVW Environment(UVW环境)</strong>, <strong>Threads(线程)</strong>, and <strong>Atan(反正切)。</strong></p> 
<p style="text-align:center"><img alt="image" src="https://oscimg.oschina.net/oscnet/up-8962f6c37e3a667fb190bd42c86aaecc18e.JPEG" width="836" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p> 
<h2>故障修复</h2> 
<p>尽管大多数用户都对加载速度显著提升给予好评，但当场景中存在许多着色器时，会出现加载速度变慢的情况。目前，此问题已得到解决。</p> 
<p>此外，此次更新还修复了以下错误：</p> 
<ul> 
 <li>修复了论坛中上报的<strong>get object transform/rotat</strong><strong>ion(获取对象变换/旋转</strong><strong>)</strong>拼图产生的返回弧度而非度数的<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.soft8soft.com%2Ftopic%2Fget-rotation-and-set-rotation-in-3-3-0%2F" target="_blank">错误</a>。</li> 
 <li>修复了论坛中上报的使用剪切平面时的<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.soft8soft.com%2Ftopic%2Fobject-clipping-issues%2F" target="_blank">崩溃问题</a>。</li> 
 <li>修复了与材质相关的多种拼图潜在问题。</li> 
 <li>修复了从Blender错误导出的几何问题。</li> 
 <li>修复了3ds Max的OSL节点的纹理环绕模式。</li> 
 <li>修复了Maya导出器因驱动关键帧动画以及因缺少阴影组而导致的崩溃问题。</li> 
</ul>
