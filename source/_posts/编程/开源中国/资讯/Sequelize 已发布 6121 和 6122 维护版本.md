   <p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"><span style="color:#333333">Sequelize 6.12.1 和 6.12.2 发布了，Sequelize 是一款基于 Nodejs 的异步 ORM 框架，它同时支持 PostgreSQL、MySQL、SQLite 和 MSSQL 多种数据库，很适合作为 Nodejs 后端数据库的存储接口，为快速开发 Node.js 应用奠定扎实、安全的基础。</span></p> 
   <p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"><span style="color:#333333">更新内容：</span></p> 
   <h3 style="margin-left:.6em; margin-right:0"><span style="color:#333333">Sequelize 6.12.1</span></h3> 
   <ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
    <li>允许深度导入<span> </span><span style="color:#24292f">(</span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fsequelize%2Fsequelize%2Fissues%2F13795" target="_blank">#13795</a><span style="color:#24292f">)</span></li> 
    <li><span style="color:#2e3033">修复 lib/</span><span style="color:#24292f">operators </span><span style="color:#2e3033"><span> </span>的无效 TypeScript 导入样式。</span><span style="color:#24292f">(</span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fsequelize%2Fsequelize%2Fissues%2F13797" target="_blank">#13797</a><span style="color:#24292f">)</span></li> 
   </ul> 
   <p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"><span style="color:#24292f">更新公告：</span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fsequelize%2Fsequelize%2Freleases%2Ftag%2Fv6.12.1" target="_blank">https://github.com/sequelize/sequelize/releases/tag/v6.12.1</a></p> 
   <h3 style="margin-left:.6em; margin-right:0"><span style="color:#333333">Sequelize 6.12.2</span></h3>

    <li><strong>abstract:</strong><span> </span>如果值为 json，则为 pg 修补 jsonb 运算符 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fsequelize%2Fsequelize%2Fissues%2F13780" target="_blank">#13780</a>) </li>

    <li><strong>postgres:</strong><span> </span><span style="color:#2e3033">允许 ARRAY(ENUM) 类型名称使用 schema</span><span> </span>(<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fsequelize%2Fsequelize%2Fissues%2F13807" target="_blank">#13807</a>) </li>
