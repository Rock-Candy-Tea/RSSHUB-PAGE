                                                                                            <p><span style="color:#000000">Stats 是一款开源的 macOS 系统监控工具，可以驻留在菜单栏中对 CPU、GPU、内存、磁盘和网络等进行实时监测。目前 Stats </span><span style="color:#333333">v2.7.34</span><span style="color:#000000"> 发布了 ，此版本带来如下变更：</span></p> 
<h3><strong>关闭的问题</strong></h3> 
<ul> 
 <li>功能：添加了一个选项，可对传感器小部件中的项目进行排序（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fexelban%2Fstats%2Fissues%2F928" target="_blank">#928</a>）</li> 
 <li>功能：添加了暂停/恢复应用程序的选项（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fexelban%2Fstats%2Fissues%2F834" target="_blank">#834</a>）</li> 
</ul> 
<h2><strong>错误修复和新功能</strong></h2> 
<ul> 
 <li>修复：固定 NSStatusItem (widget) id</li> 
 <li>功能：在启用/禁用模块时，添加了小部件位置恢复功能</li> 
</ul> 
<h2><strong>本土化</strong></h2> 
<ul> 
 <li>修复：修复 了<code>Thunderbolt</code> 中的错字 （<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fexelban%2Fstats%2Fissues%2F1072" target="_blank">#1072</a>）</li> 
 <li>功能：添加 <code>Pause/Resume the Stats</code> 翻译（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fexelban%2Fstats%2Fissues%2F834" target="_blank">#834</a>）</li> 
 <li>功能：更新了简体中文翻译（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fexelban%2Fstats%2Fpull%2F1076" target="_blank">#1076</a>）</li> 
 <li>功能：更新繁体中文翻译（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fexelban%2Fstats%2Fpull%2F1077" target="_blank">#1077</a>）</li> 
</ul>
