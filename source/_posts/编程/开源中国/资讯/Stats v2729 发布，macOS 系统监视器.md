                                                                                            <p><span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#000000">Stats 是一款开源的 macOS 系统监控工具，可以驻留在菜单栏中对 CPU、GPU、内存、磁盘和网络等进行实时监测。目前 Stats </span><span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#333333">v2.7.29</span><span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#000000"> 发布了 ，此版本带来如下变更：</span></p> 
<h2 style="text-align:start"><span><span><span><span><span style="color:#24292f"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#ffffff"><span><span><span>关闭的问题</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></h2> 
<ul> 
 <li>功能：在弹出视图中添加了模糊的背景（仅在启用暗模式时）（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fexelban%2Fstats%2Fissues%2F964" target="_blank">#964</a>）</li> 
 <li>功能：添加了一个选项来选择折线图和网络图小部件的缩放比例（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fexelban%2Fstats%2Fissues%2F844" target="_blank">#844</a>）</li> 
</ul> 
<h2 style="text-align:start"><span><span><span><span><span style="color:#24292f"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#ffffff"><span><span><span>错误修复和新功能</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></h2> 
<ul> 
 <li>功能：向图表添加了不同的缩放类型（正方形、立方体、对数）</li> 
</ul> 
<h2 style="text-align:start"><span><span><span><span><span style="color:#24292f"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#ffffff"><span><span><span>本土化</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></h2> 
<ul> 
 <li>功能：添加了缩放的翻译</li> 
</ul>
