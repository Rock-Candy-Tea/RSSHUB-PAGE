                                                        <p>Electron 13.1.6 现已发布，主要更新内容如下： </p> 
<p><strong>Fixes</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>修复了在 macOS 上调用<code>app.quit()</code>时潜在的崩溃问题。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F29961" target="_blank">#29961</a></li> 
 <li>修复了<code>self.module.paths</code>在 Workers 中不起作用的问题。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F30013" target="_blank">＃30013 </a></li> 
 <li>修复了从 webview 单击<code>target=_blank</code>链接时崩溃的问题。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F29951" target="_blank">＃29951 </a></li> 
 <li>修复了在 macOS 上创建通知时的内存泄漏。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F30009" target="_blank">#30009</a></li> 
 <li>修复了在 nativeWindowOpen 下，没有导航项的子窗口的页面标题没有更新。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F29958" target="_blank">＃29958</a></li> 
</ul> 
<p><strong>Other Changes</strong> </p> 
<ul> 
 <li>1090000 的反向移植修复。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F29997" target="_blank">#29997</a></li> 
</ul>
