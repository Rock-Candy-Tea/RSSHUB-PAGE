                                                                                            <p>GoLand 2021.3.1 正式发布，该版本是 GoLand 2021.3 的第一个修复版本，包含有一些改进和错误修复。</p> 
<p>开发者可以通过以下方式更新：</p> 
<ul> 
 <li>从<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.jetbrains.com%2Fgo%2Fdownload%2F%3F_gl%3D1*sxqin9*_ga*MTMzMTM3NzQ1MS4xNjIxOTM2MTky*_ga_0WQ2ZF5VGT*MTYyOTM4MjUwMy4yNTEuMS4xNjI5MzgyNzg3LjA.%26_ga%3D2.87053848.1897507604.1628341259-1331377451.1621936192%26_gac%3D1.93597167.1629042383.CjwKCAjw9uKIBhA8EiwAYPUS3IH6FMaUHhwBUM8VX-YUDQiQ5H6pspefC4gEhF4ZYWPE5QJbaVJKPBoCsw0QAvD_BwE" target="_blank">官方网站下载</a>；</li> 
 <li>通过 Toolbox App 进行更新；</li> 
 <li>进入 GoLand —— 检查更新；</li> 
 <li>使用 snap 更新（针对 Ubuntu）；</li> 
</ul> 
<p>以下是该版本值得关注的更新：</p> 
<ul> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FGO-12060" target="_blank">GO-12060</a> - 修复一个误报问题，即 GoLand 不再警告你在使用 protocol buffers（protobuf）时出现不存在的错误。</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FGO-12066" target="_blank">GO-12066</a> - 调试器中的 "Evaluate Expression" 再次按预期工作。</li> 
</ul> 
<p>更多详情可查看：<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fblog.jetbrains.com%2Fgo%2F2021%2F12%2F20%2Fgoland-2021-3-1%2F" target="_blank">https://blog.jetbrains.com/go/2021/12/20/goland-2021-3-1/</a></p>
