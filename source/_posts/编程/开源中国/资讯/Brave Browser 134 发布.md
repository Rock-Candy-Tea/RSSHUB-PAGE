                                                                                            <p>Brave 是基于 Chromium 的一款免费开源网络浏览器，主打隐私保护，其默认设置就会自动阻止在线广告和网站跟踪。Brave Software（Brave 浏览器所属公司）于 2015 年由 Brendan Eich 和 Brian Bondy 联合创立，其中前者是 JavaScript 的创造者，同时也是 Mozilla 联合创始人。</p> 
<p>Brave 1.34 发布，更新内容如下：</p> 
<ul> 
 <li>增加了通过使用 <code>brave://flags/#tab-audio-icon-interactive</code> 使标签页上的音频图标不可点击的功能 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fbrave%2Fbrave-browser%2Fissues%2F19979" target="_blank">#19979</a>)</li> 
 <li>增加了对选定网络的指示 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fbrave%2Fbrave-browser%2Fissues%2F19651" target="_blank">#19651</a>)</li> 
 <li>更新了 Trezor 未连接时的错误消息文本 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fbrave%2Fbrave-browser%2Fissues%2F19351" target="_blank">#19351</a>)</li> 
 <li>删除了隐私窗口上图标的文本标签</li> 
 <li>修正了在某些情况下禁用 Brave Shields 时的崩溃 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fbrave%2Fbrave-browser%2Fissues%2F19958" target="_blank">#19958</a>)</li> 
 <li>修正了无法用 WebTorrent 下载 torrents 的问题 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fbrave%2Fbrave-browser%2Fissues%2F19818" target="_blank">#19818</a>)</li> 
 <li>修正了导入 Ledger 账户时的错误 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fbrave%2Fbrave-browser%2Fissues%2F19451" target="_blank">#19451</a>)</li> 
 <li>修正了 Trezor 弹出窗口在某些情况下不正确地重新打开 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fbrave%2Fbrave-browser%2Fissues%2F19858" target="_blank">#19858</a>)</li> 
 <li>修正了编辑网络时不显示 RPC URLs 的加号图标的问题 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fbrave%2Fbrave-browser%2Fissues%2F19717" target="_blank">#19717</a>)</li> 
 <li>修正了无法删除以前输入的 RPC URL 的问题 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fbrave%2Fbrave-browser%2Fissues%2F19782" target="_blank">#19782</a>)</li> 
 <li>修正了 brave://wallet 上不支持的网络的工具提示被保留在屏幕上的问题 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fbrave%2Fbrave-browser%2Fissues%2F19400" target="_blank">#19400</a>)</li> 
 <li>修正了 IPNS 地址的路径名称没有出现在地址栏中的问题 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fbrave%2Fbrave-browser%2Fissues%2F18888" target="_blank">#18888</a>)</li> 
 <li>将 Chromium 升级到 97.0.4692.71 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fbrave%2Fbrave-browser%2Fissues%2F20269" target="_blank">#20269</a>) (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fchromium.googlesource.com%2Fchromium%2Fsrc%2F%2Blog%2F96.0.4664.110..97.0.4692.71%3Fpretty%3Dfuller%26n%3D1000" target="_blank">Changelog for 97.0.4692.71</a>)</li> 
</ul> 
<p>更多详情可查看：<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fbrave.com%2Flatest%2F" target="_blank">https://brave.com/latest/</a></p>
