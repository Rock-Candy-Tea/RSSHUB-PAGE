                                                                                            <p><span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#333333">DataGrip 2022.1.5 现已</span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fblog.jetbrains.com%2Fdatagrip%2F2022%2F06%2F07%2Fdatagrip-2022-1-5%2F" target="_blank">发布</a><span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#333333">，这是 DataGrip 2022.1</span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fblog.jetbrains.com%2Fdatagrip%2F2022%2F06%2F07%2Fdatagrip-2022-1-5%2F" target="_blank"><span> </span></a><span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#333333">的一个小更新版本。</span></p> 
<p><strong><span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#27282c">Bug 修复：</span></strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FDBE-14378" target="_blank">DBE-14378</a>：现在可以检测到 TiDB，DataGrip 只使用数据库支持的语句。</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FDBE-15553" target="_blank">DBE-15553</a>：[Exasol] 现在支持 IMPERSONATE 命令。</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FDBE-15322" target="_blank">DBE-15322</a>：[ClickHouse] 修复了地图文字的类型推导。</li> 
 <li>[Snowflake] 添加了在获取属性时间过长时跳过表格阶段属性自省的功能。</li> 
 <li>[PostgreSQL, Azure] 修复了在一个数据源中选择多个数据库时发生的自省问题（也修复了 <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FDBE-15360" target="_blank">DBE-15360</a>）。</li> 
 <li>[Oracle]：修复了 Oracle v.18 及更高版本中的数值溢出问题。</li> 
</ul>
