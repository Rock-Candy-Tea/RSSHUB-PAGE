<div class="content">
                                                                    
                                                        <p>openSUSE Leap 15.3 <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fnews.opensuse.org%2F2021%2F06%2F02%2Fopensuse-leap-bridges-path-to-enterprise%2F" target="_blank">已正式发布</a>，openSUSE 是基于企业发行版 SUSE Linux Enterprise (SLE) 的社区发行版。</p> 
<p>官方的发布公告<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fzh.opensuse.org%2F%25E5%258F%2591%25E8%25A1%258C%25E5%2585%25AC%25E5%2591%258A_15.3" target="_blank">写道</a>：“作为坚如磐石的 openSUSE 15.x 系列的新成员，它继承了前代产品的所有优秀特性。这次的版本相比较之前的 Leap 有了一个巨大的变化，openSUSE Leap 15.3 不仅仅像之前的版本一样编译自 <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.suse.com%2Fproducts%2Fserver%2F" target="_blank">SUSE Linux Enterprise</a> 的代码，还使用了和 SLE 完全一样的二进制包进行了构建，从而使 Leap 和 SLE 之间能<strong>像<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutu.be%2FezmR9Attpyc" target="_blank">阴阳</a>一样对立而又统一</strong>。”</p> 
<p><img alt src="https://oscimg.oschina.net/oscnet/up-47a97a161c00873662e127509cc1bb8b801.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p> 
<p>升级到 openSUSE Leap 15.3 的用户需要注意，官方不建议从 openSUSE Leap 15.2 之前的版本直接升级。由于升级路径的原因，强烈建议在升级到 Leap 15.3 之前先升级到 Leap 15.2。该版本只支持从 openSUSE Leap 15.2 升级到 15.3，正如<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fdoc.opensuse.org%2Frelease-notes%2Fx86_64%2FopenSUSE%2FLeap%2F15.3%2FRELEASE-NOTES.zh_CN.html%23upgrade" target="_blank">发行说明</a>中所强调的。</p> 
<p>如需下载 Leap 15.3 的镜像 ISO 文件，访问 <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fget.opensuse.org%2Fleap%2F" target="_blank">https://get.opensuse.org/leap/</a>。</p> 
<h1>主要变化</h1> 
<ul> 
 <li>Xfce 4.16：带来新的图标和调色板</li> 
 <li>GNU Health 3.8：增加新的牙科模块和牙形图</li> 
 <li>软件包管理器 DNF 升级到 4.7.0</li> 
 <li>增加对 <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fen.wikipedia.org%2Fwiki%2FLinux_on_IBM_Z" target="_blank">IBM Z 和 LinuxONE (s390x)</a> 架构的支持</li> 
</ul> 
<p>此外还提供了针对容器技术方面的更新，例如升级所有软件包。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fzh.opensuse.org%2F%25E5%258F%2591%25E8%25A1%258C%25E5%2585%25AC%25E5%2591%258A_15.3" target="_blank">详细更新说明点此查看</a>。</p>
