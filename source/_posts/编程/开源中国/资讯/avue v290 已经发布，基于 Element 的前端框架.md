<p>此版本更新内容包括：</p> 
<h2>v2.9.0</h2> 
<p><code>2022-03-10</code></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>重构组件内大量方法和逻辑优化</li> 
 <li>优化了打包方式</li> 
 <li>重构Crud组件文档</li> 
 <li>新增全局$DialogForm方法，直接弹窗打开表单<a href="https://gitee.com/link?target=https%3A%2F%2Favuejs.com%2Fform%2Fform-dialog.html" target="_blank">在线例子</a></li> 
 <li>新增Upload组件拖拽排序<a href="https://www.oschina.net/form/form-upload.html#%E6%8B%96%E6%8B%BD%E6%8E%92%E5%BA%8F">在线例子</a></li> 
 <li>修复许多已知和未知的问题</li> 
 <li>字典级联动cascaderItem -> cascader属性，同时支持一对多的关系<a href="https://gitee.com/link?target=https%3A%2F%2Favuejs.com%2Fform%2Fform-dic.html%23%25E5%25AD%2597%25E5%2585%25B8%25E8%2581%2594%25E5%258A%25A8" target="_blank">在线例子</a></li> 
 <li>表格行和列拖拽单独属性控制rowSort和columnSort<a href="https://gitee.com/link?target=https%3A%2F%2Favuejs.com%2Fcrud%2Fcrud-row.html%23%25E8%25A1%258C%25E6%258B%2596%25E6%258B%25BD%25E6%258E%2592%25E5%25BA%258F" target="_blank">行拖动</a>，<a href="https://gitee.com/link?target=https%3A%2F%2Favuejs.com%2Fcrud%2Fcrud-column.html%23%25E5%2588%2597%25E6%258B%2596%25E6%258B%25BD%25E6%258E%2592%25E5%25BA%258F" target="_blank">列拖动</a></li> 
</ul>
