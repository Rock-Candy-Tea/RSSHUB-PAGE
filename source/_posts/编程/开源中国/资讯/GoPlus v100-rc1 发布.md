                                                                                            <p>GoPlus v1.0.0-rc1 现已发布。GoPlus 是一门静态类型语言，与 Go 语言完全兼容。其代码样式类似脚本，并且比 Go 更具可读性，专门用于数据科学。</p> 
<p>具体更新内容如下： </p> 
<p><strong>新特性</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>gop version</li> 
 <li>spx/gmx: main func</li> 
</ul> 
<p><strong>changes</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>错误修复：toRecv nil 名称检查</li> 
 <li>错误修复：ErrWrap 表达式  (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fgoplus%2Fgop%2Fissues%2F755" target="_blank">#755</a>)</li> 
 <li>错误修复：结构/接口方法调用 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fgoplus%2Fgop%2Fissues%2F757" target="_blank">#757</a>)</li> 
 <li>错误修复：struct.field  (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fgoplus%2Fgop%2Fissues%2F767" target="_blank">#767</a>)</li> 
 <li>persist bugfix：Interface Complete</li> 
 <li>go run bugfix：支持 stdin</li> 
</ul>
