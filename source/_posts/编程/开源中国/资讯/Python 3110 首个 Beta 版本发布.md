                                                                                            <p>Python 3.11.0 <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fpythoninsider.blogspot.com%2F2022%2F05%2Fpython-3110b1-is-now-available.html" target="_blank">发布</a>了首个 Beta 版本。</p> 
<p>开发团队称，虽然该版本计划在功能完整的情况下进入 Beta 阶段，但在发布候选阶段之前，功能可能会被修改或在极少数情况下被删除。他们的目标是在 beta 4 之后没有 ABI 更改，并且在第一个候选版本 3.11.0rc1 之后尽可能少地更改代码。为实现这一目标，在 Beta 阶段尽可能多地进行测试非常重要<strong style="color:#111111">。</strong></p> 
<p style="color:#333333; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:left"><span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#333333">请记住，这是一个预览版本，不建议在生产环境中使用。</span></p> 
<p style="color:#333333; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:left"><strong>Python 3.11.0 主要变化</strong></p>

 <li><span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.python.org%2Fdev%2Fpeps%2Fpep-0654%2F" target="_blank">PEP 654</a> –  Exception Groups and except*</span></li> 
 <li><span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.python.org%2Fdev%2Fpeps%2Fpep-0673%2F" target="_blank">PEP 673</a>  - Self 类型</span></li> 
 <li><span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.python.org%2Fdev%2Fpeps%2Fpep-0646%2F" target="_blank">PEP 646</a>  - 可变参数泛型 (</span>Variadic Generics<span>)</span></li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.python.org%2Fdev%2Fpeps%2Fpep-0680%2F" target="_blank">PEP 680</a> - tomllib：支持在标准库中解析 TOML</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.python.org%2Fdev%2Fpeps%2Fpep-0675%2F" target="_blank">PEP 675</a> - 任意文字字符串类型</li>

 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fbugs.python.org%2Fissue46752" target="_blank">bpo-46752</a> – 将任务组引入 asyncio</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Ffaster-cpython" target="_blank">Faster Cpython Project</a> 已经出现了一些令人振奋的结果：Python 3.11 比 Python 3.10 快 10%~60%</li>
