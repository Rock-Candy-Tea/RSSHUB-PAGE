                                                                                            <p style="margin-left:0px; margin-right:0px; text-align:start">Spring Boot 2.6.0-RC1, 2.5.6 & 2.4.12 已发布，更新内容包括修复安全问题、改进文档，以及升级依赖项。</p> 
<p style="margin-left:0px; margin-right:0px; text-align:start"><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fspring.io%2Fblog%2F2021%2F10%2F21%2Fspring-boot-2-6-0-rc1-is-available-now" target="_blank"><strong>Spring Boot 2.6.0-RC1</strong></a></p> 
<p style="margin-left:0px; margin-right:0px; text-align:start">此版本增加了如下新特性</p> 
<ul> 
 <li>支持在<code>/actuator/info</code><span> 端点中选择 </span>Java runtime 信息</li> 
 <li><span style="color:#191e1e">添加对 Log4j2 复合配置的支持</span></li> 
 <li>为 buildpack 添加了其他配置选项</li> 
 <li>servlet 已支持 SameSite cookie 属性</li> 
</ul> 
<p style="text-align:start"><strong><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fspring.io%2Fblog%2F2021%2F10%2F21%2Fspring-boot-2-5-6-is-now-available" target="_blank">Spring Boot 2.5.6</a> & <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fspring.io%2Fblog%2F2021%2F10%2F21%2Fspring-boot-2-4-12-is-now-available" target="_blank">Spring Boot 2.4.12</a></strong></p> 
<p>这两个版本主要修复了以下安全问题：</p>

 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Ftanzu.vmware.com%2Fsecurity%2Fcve-2021-22097" target="_blank">CVE-2021-22097</a><span> </span>for Spring AMQP</li> 
</ul>
