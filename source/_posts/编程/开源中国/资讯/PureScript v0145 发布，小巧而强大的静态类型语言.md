                                                        <p>PureScript  v0.14.5 发布了。PureScript 是个小巧而强大的静态类型语言，可以编译成 JavaScript。PureScript 主要是由 Haskell 和 PureScript 编写的。</p> 
<p style="color:#333333; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:left"><strong>Bug 修复：</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li> <p><span>修复类型推断为类型同义词推断错误类型的情况（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fpurescript%2Fpurescript%2Fpull%2F4184" target="_blank">#4184</a>）</span></p> </li> 
 <li> <p><span>在与声明冲突的情况下正确重命名模块导入（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fpurescript%2Fpurescript%2Fpull%2F4188" target="_blank">#4188</a>）</span></p> </li> 
</ul> 
<p style="text-align:start"><strong style="color:#333333">Internal：</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li> <p><span>修复命令并澄清发布指南中的其他一些要求（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fpurescript%2Fpurescript%2Fpull%2F4177" target="_blank">#4177</a></span><span>）</span></p> </li> 
 <li> <p><span>为<code>Language.PureScript.CoreFn.Module</code>添加<code>Functor</code>实例。（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fpurescript%2Fpurescript%2Fpull%2F4186" target="_blank">#4186</a>）</span></p> </li> 
</ul>
