                                                                                            <p>PhpStorm 2021.2 的第四个错误修复更新现在已经正式推出，PhpStorm 2021.2.4 中值得注意的变化包括：</p> 
<ul> 
 <li>修复：主菜单构建缓慢，可能导致崩溃 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FIDEA-277709" target="_blank">IDEA-277709</a> +35)</li> 
 <li>修复：更新到 IntelliJ IDEA 2021.2.1 后，代理认证不起作用 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FIDEA-276949" target="_blank">IDEA-276949</a> +19)</li> 
 <li>修复：当试图删除其他标签时，当前活动的结果标签被意外删除 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FIDEA-274154" target="_blank">IDEA-274154</a> +17)</li> 
 <li>修复：ESLint 8.0：与 YarnPnP 不兼容 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FWEB-52594" target="_blank">WEB-52594</a> +13)</li> 
 <li>修复：没有波浪号的 Sass @use / @import 语句没有被正确解析 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FWEB-53120" target="_blank">WEB-53120</a> +13)</li> 
 <li>修复：恢复移到单独窗口的 Diff 可能会产生一个空窗口 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FIDEA-277226" target="_blank">IDEA-277226</a> +12)</li> 
 <li>修复：如果省略了 <code>~</code>，LESS 中的 @import 不能解析 node_modules 中的包 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FWEB-49795" target="_blank">WEB-49795</a> +7)</li> 
 <li>修复：Stylelint 插件与 Stylelint v14 + SCSS 不兼容 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FWEB-53225" target="_blank">WEB-53225</a> +7)</li> 
 <li>修复：scss 中的 @import 不能解析 node_modules 中的包 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FWEB-30607" target="_blank">WEB-30607</a> +5)</li> 
</ul>
