                                                                                            <p>JavaScript 日期选择器 flatpickr 发布了 4.6.10 版本，这是自 2019 年 9 月 flatpickr <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fflatpickr%2Fflatpickr%2Freleases%2Ftag%2Fv4.6.3" target="_blank"><strong>v4.6.3</strong></a><strong> 之后的首个新版本，</strong>带来了一些改动和修复：</p> 
<ul> 
 <li><span style="color:#2e3033">支持 Z&GMT 自定义解析 </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fflatpickr%2Fflatpickr%2Fpull%2F2369" target="_blank">#2369</a></li> 
 <li><span style="color:#2e3033">修复:限制 onKeyDown 监听 Flatpickr </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fflatpickr%2Fflatpickr%2Fpull%2F2329" target="_blank">#2329</a></li> 
 <li><span style="color:#24292f">支持 minTime 和 maxTime 的反转范围 </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fflatpickr%2Fflatpickr%2Fpull%2F2482" target="_blank">#2482</a></li> 
 <li><span style="color:#24292f">将所有 ES6+ 语法编译为 ES5 </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fflatpickr%2Fflatpickr%2Fpull%2F2469" target="_blank">#2469</a></li> 
 <li>修复 <span style="color:#24292f">rangePlugin 选择问题 </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fflatpickr%2Fflatpickr%2Fpull%2F1795" target="_blank">#1795</a></li> 
 <li><span style="color:#2e3033">修复 maxTime 受限制的 bug </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fflatpickr%2Fflatpickr%2Fpull%2F2483" target="_blank">#2483</a></li> 
 <li><span style="color:#2e3033">修复相对输入的 parseDate </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fflatpickr%2Fflatpickr%2Fpull%2F2475" target="_blank">#2475</a></li> 
 <li><strong>...</strong></li> 
</ul>
