                                                        <p>Electron 12.0.14 现已发布，主要更新内容如下： </p> 
<p><strong>Fixes</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>修复了<code>requestFullscreen</code>在 webview 内不会使 element 全屏显示的问题。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F29988" target="_blank">＃29988 </a></li> 
 <li>修复了使用<code>fsPromises.readFile</code>时可能出现的 renderer 崩溃。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F29929" target="_blank">#29929</a></li> 
 <li>修复了 Electron 有时不支持用户定义的下载目录的问题。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F29966" target="_blank">＃29966</a></li> 
 <li>修复了<code>self.module.paths</code>在 Workers 中不起作用的问题。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F30012" target="_blank">＃30012</a></li> 
 <li>修复了在 macOS 上 setAlwaysOnTop value 有时不会为子窗口保留的问题。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F29957" target="_blank">＃29957 </a></li> 
 <li>修复了从 webview 单击带<code>target=_blank</code>的链接时崩溃的问题。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F29950" target="_blank">＃29950</a></li> 
 <li>修复了在 macOS 上创建通知时的内存泄漏。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F30007" target="_blank">＃30007</a></li> 
 <li>修复了在 nativeWindowOpen 下没有导航条目的子窗口上不会更新页面标题的问题。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F29960" target="_blank">＃29960 </a></li> 
</ul> 
<p><strong>Other Changes</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>安全性：CVE-2021-30522 的反向移植修复。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F29878" target="_blank">#29878</a></li> 
 <li>安全性：CVE-2021-30523 的反向移植修复。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F29876" target="_blank">#29876</a></li> 
</ul>
