                                                                                            <p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start">JetBrains 发布了其 .NET 跨平台集成开发环境 Rider  2021.2.3 版本。</p> 
<p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start">从 Rider 2021.2.3 开始，后续的 Rider 维护版本都要求登陆 JetBrains 帐户才能开始试用，此举的目的是一定程序上打击盗版软件，另一方面是帮助新人快速入门<span> </span><span style="color:#27282c">JetBrains</span><span> </span>产品。</p> 
<p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start">此外，版本修复了一些烦人的小问题，如：</p> 
<ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
 <li>打开解决方案时出现两个安全通知对话框的问题 (<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FRIDER-70825" target="_blank">RIDER-70825</a><span> </span>)。</li> 
 <li>修复了子菜单中的低分辨率图标 (<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FIDEA-283733" target="_blank">IDEA-283733</a><span> </span>)。</li> 
 <li>修复了在构建和渲染 IDE 的主菜单 时可能发生的性能滞后和崩溃(<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FIDEA-277709" target="_blank">IDEA-277709</a><span> </span>) 。</li> 
 <li>现在可以使用 [SuppressMessage] 属性抑制 Roslyn Analyzers 的诊断 (<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FRIDER-65391" target="_blank">RIDER-65391</a><span> </span>)。</li> 
</ul>
