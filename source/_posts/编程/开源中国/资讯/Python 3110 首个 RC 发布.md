                                                                                            <p>Python 3.11.0 首个 RC <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.python.org%2Fdownloads%2Frelease%2Fpython-3110rc1%2F" target="_blank">已发布</a>。按照发布日程，RC 阶段会发布两个版本，在正式版发布前，开发团队会对明确的错误进行修复。此外，进入 RC 阶段意味着不会再改动 ABI，开发团队也会尽可能少地更改代码。</p> 
<p style="color:#333333; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:left">与 3.10 相比，Python 3.11 的主要变化</p> 
<ul style="list-style-type:disc; margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.python.org%2Fdev%2Fpeps%2Fpep-0657%2F">PEP 657</a> – 在错误信息报告中引入细粒度的 Error Locations</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.python.org%2Fdev%2Fpeps%2Fpep-0654%2F">PEP 654</a> –  Exception Groups and except*</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.python.org%2Fdev%2Fpeps%2Fpep-0673%2F">PEP 673</a>  - Self 类型</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.python.org%2Fdev%2Fpeps%2Fpep-0646%2F">PEP 646</a>  - 可变参数泛型 (Variadic Generics)</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.python.org%2Fdev%2Fpeps%2Fpep-0680%2F">PEP 680</a> - tomllib：支持在标准库中解析 TOML</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.python.org%2Fdev%2Fpeps%2Fpep-0675%2F">PEP 675</a> - 任意文字字符串类型</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.python.org%2Fdev%2Fpeps%2Fpep-0655%2F">PEP 655</a> - 将单个 TypedDict 项目标记为必需或可能缺失</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fbugs.python.org%2Fissue46752">bpo-46752</a> – 将任务组引入 asyncio</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.python.org%2Fdev%2Fpeps%2Fpep-0681%2F">PEP 681</a>  - Data Class 转换</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fpython%2Fcpython%2Fissues%2F34627%2F" target="_blank">bpo-433030 –</a> 正则表达式现在支持原子分组 (Atomic grouping)：((?>...))，以及占有型量词<span style="color:#222222"> (</span><code>*+, ++, ?+, &#123;m,n&#125;+</code><span style="color:#222222">) </span></li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Ffaster-cpython">Faster Cpython Project</a> 已经出现了一些令人振奋的结果：Python 3.11 比 Python 3.10 快 10%~60%</li> 
</ul> 
<p style="color:#333333; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:left">Python 3.11 的下一个预览版本将是 3.11.0rc2，目前计划于 2022 年 9 月 5 日发布。正式版则计划于 2022 年 10 月 3 日发布。</p>
