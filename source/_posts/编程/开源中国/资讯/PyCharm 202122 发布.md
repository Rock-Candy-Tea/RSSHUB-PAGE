 <p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start">PyCharm 2021.2.2 现已<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fblog.jetbrains.com%2Fpycharm%2F2021%2F09%2F2021-2-2%2F" target="_blank">发布</a>。这是 PyCharm 2021.2 的第二个次要版本，包含了多个错误修复。</p> 
 <p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start">具体更新内容如下：</p> 
 <ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
  <li>cProfile 调用图永远不会加载。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FPY-49917" target="_blank">[PY-49917]</a></li> 
  <li>正在创建 Requirements.txt 空白文件。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FPY-41953" target="_blank">[PY-41953]</a></li> 
  <li>WSL：无法在 WSL 项目中检测到任何 SDK。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FPY-48100" target="_blank">[PY-48100]</a></li> 
  <li>PyCharm 不再将模式匹配与类型提示混为一谈。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FPY-49990" target="_blank">[PY-49990]</a></li> 
  <li>Python 3.10：快速修复以添加“from __future__ import”注释 <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FPY-49283" target="_blank">[PY-49283]</a></li> 
  <li>React Native 应用程序中样式表属性的值没有完成。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FWEB-43144" target="_blank">[WEB-43144]</a></li> 
  <li>Inspections：代码中内联的 HTML 属性的误报。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fyoutrack.jetbrains.com%2Fissue%2FWEB-26240" target="_blank">[WEB-26240]</a></li> 
 </ul> 
 <p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start">有关 PyCharm 2021.2.2 中解决的问题的完整列表，可参阅<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fconfluence.jetbrains.com%2Fdisplay%2FPYH%2FPyCharm%2B2021.2.2%2BRelease%2BNotes" target="_blank">发行说明</a>。</p>
