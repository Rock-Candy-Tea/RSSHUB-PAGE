                                                        <p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start">Brave 是基于 Chromium 引擎开发的免费开源网络浏览器。Brave 是一个注重隐私的浏览器，它通过在默认设置中自动阻止在线广告和网站跟踪器来将自己与其他浏览器区分开来。</p> 
<p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start">Brave Browser 1.32 正式发布，该版本更新内容如下：</p> 
<ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
 <li>iOS：阐明了同步设置说明（这将是 Brave 浏览器支持 iOS 13 的最后一个版本） (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fbrave%2Fbrave-ios%2F4470" target="_blank">#4470</a>)</li> 
 <li>Desktop：将 Chromium 升级到 96.0.4664.45 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fbrave%2Fbrave-browser%2Fissues%2F19387" target="_blank">#19387</a>) (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fchromium.googlesource.com%2Fchromium%2Fsrc%2F%2Blog%2F95.0.4638.69..96.0.4664.45%3Fpretty%3Dfuller%26n%3D1000" target="_blank">Changelog for 96.0.4664.45</a>)</li> 
 <li>Desktop：增加了本地 Brave Wallet (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fbrave%2Fbrave-browser%2Fissues%2F17081" target="_blank">#17081</a>)</li> 
 <li>Desktop：在已验证用户的月度 Rewards 报表中增加了托管人仪表板的链接 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fbrave%2Fbrave-browser%2Fissues%2F9211" target="_blank">#9211</a>)</li> 
 <li>Desktop：增加了 macOS 的 Apple Silicon IPFS 二进制文件 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fbrave%2Fbrave-browser%2Fissues%2F16912" target="_blank">#16912</a>)</li> 
 <li>Desktop：改进了添加无源隔离的 IPFS 网关时的错误信息 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fbrave%2Fbrave-browser%2Fissues%2F18212" target="_blank">#18212</a>)</li> 
 <li>Desktop：更新了支付横幅消息 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fbrave%2Fbrave-browser%2Fissues%2F17943" target="_blank">#17943</a>)</li> 
 <li>Desktop：删除了标签组中的 "发送反馈" (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fbrave%2Fbrave-browser%2Fissues%2F18314" target="_blank">#18314</a>)</li> 
 <li>Desktop：当钱包无法被读取时，修复了 Brave Rewards 钱包的再次生成 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fbrave%2Fbrave-browser%2Fissues%2F18717" target="_blank">#18717</a>)</li> 
</ul> 
<p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start">更多详情可查看：<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fbrave.com%2Flatest%2F" target="_blank">https://brave.com/latest/</a></p>
