                                                                                            <p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><span style="color:#000000">Fastlane<span> </span></span>2.203.0<span style="color:#000000"><span> </span>发布了，</span><span style="color:#333333">Fastlane 是一个针对 iOS 和 Android 全方位开发自动化流程的工具。利用目前支持的工具可以做包含自动化和可持续化构建的每个环节，比如单元测试、截图、分发渠道、上传元数据和 ipa 包提交审核等等。</span></p> 
<p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><strong>Fastlane 2.203.0 版本主要更新内容：</strong></p> 
<ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
 <li>[scan] 复如果没有派生数据的崩溃 (<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Ffastlane%2Ffastlane%2Fpull%2F19871" target="_blank">#19871 ) </a></li> 
 <li>[scan] 运行扫描时返回结果 (<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Ffastlane%2Ffastlane%2Fpull%2F19860" target="_blank">#19860 ) </a></li> 
 <li>[scan] 添加 parallel_testing 选项，以进行扫描 (<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Ffastlane%2Ffastlane%2Fpull%2F19857" target="_blank">#19857 )</a></li> 
</ul>
