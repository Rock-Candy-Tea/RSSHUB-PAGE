<div class="content">
                                                                                            <p><span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#333333">EndeavorOS 是一个基于 Arch Linux 的滚动式 Linux 发行版，但入门难度比 Arch 低很多，该项目旨在继承 Antergos 的衣钵，基于 Arch 来提供方便的安装及预配置好的桌面环境。</span></p> 
<p><img src="https://oscimg.oschina.net/oscnet/up-e762e9d87d6f76c3a73700930c867f0ed06.webp" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p> 
<p>目前 Endeavor OS 22.9 已发布，该版本除了常规的上游更新外，还附带了一些额外的内部变化。此版本更新了软件包，<span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#1f1f1f">新包版本为：</span></p> 
<ul> 
 <li><strong>Calamares</strong><span> </span>3.2.61</li> 
 <li><strong>Firefox</strong><span> </span>104.0.2-1</li> 
 <li><strong>Linux kernel</strong><span> </span>5.19.7.arch1-1</li> 
 <li><strong>Mesa</strong><span> </span>22.1.7-1</li> 
 <li><strong>Xorg-Server</strong><span> </span>21.1.4-1</li> 
 <li><strong>nvidia-dkms</strong><span> </span>515.65.01-2</li> 
 <li><strong>Grub</strong><span> </span>2:2.06.r322.gd9b4638c5-4</li> 
</ul> 
<p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><span><span><span><span style="color:#1f1f1f"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#ffffff"><span><span><span><strong>社区版改进</strong></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>由于上游包更改， <strong>ttf-nerd-fonts-symbols </strong>更改为 <strong>ttf-nerd-fonts-symbols-2048-em 。</strong></li> 
</ul> 
<p>由于 Endeavor OS 跟随 Arch 的滚动时更新，因此用户无需安装新版本。</p>
