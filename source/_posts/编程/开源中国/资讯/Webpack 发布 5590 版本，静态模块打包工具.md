                                                        <p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><span style="color:#333333">Webpack v</span>5.59.0<span style="color:#333333"><span> </span>发布了！Webpack 是一个常见的静态模块打包工具，主要用途是在浏览器上打包 JavaScript 文件。</span></p> 
<p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start">此版本主要更新内容如下：</p> 
<h3 style="margin-left:.6em; margin-right:0; text-align:start">新特性</h3> 
<ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
 <li>生成代码的时候为<span> </span><code>Object()</code>加入<code>/*#__PURE__*/</code><span style="color:#24292f"><span> </span>注释</span></li> 
 <li>为<span> </span><code>managed/immutablePath</code><span> </span>加入正则表达式和函数支持。</li> 
 <li>在构建组件的多个阶段加入 Hooks 。</li> 
 <li>优化<span> </span><code>experiments.buildHttp</code>： 
  <ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
   <li>允许共享缓存</li> 
   <li>添加许可名单</li> 
  </ul> </li> 
 <li>加入<span> </span><code>splitChunks.minSizeReduction</code><span> </span>选项。</li> 
</ul> 
<h3 style="margin-left:.6em; margin-right:0; text-align:start">bug 修复</h3> 
<ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
 <li>修复了<span> </span><span style="color:#24292f">Data URLs 的内存缓存问题 。</span></li> 
 <li>修复了组件使用<span> </span><span style="color:#24292f"><code>unsafe cached</code><span> </span>这种缓存</span>方法时，<span> </span><code>waitFor</code><span> </span>会崩溃的问题。</li> 
 <li>修复了构建循环检测时的 bug 。</li> 
</ul> 
<p>更新公告：<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fwebpack%2Fwebpack%2Freleases%2Ftag%2Fv5.59.0" target="_blank">https://github.com/webpack/webpack/releases/tag/v5.59.0</a></p>
