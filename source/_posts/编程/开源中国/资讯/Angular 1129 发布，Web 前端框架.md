 <li>compiler-cli：允许继续分析无效的样式 url（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fangular%2Fangular%2Fissues%2F41403" target="_blank">＃41403</a>）（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fangular%2Fangular%2Fissues%2F41489" target="_blank">＃41489</a>）（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fangular%2Fangular%2Fcommit%2F07131fa8ef9f9e6930cf297c27e450c15fb4f10b" target="_blank">07131fa</a>）</li> 
 <li>compiler-cli：修复从非相对扩展较少的 TypeScript 配置文件中扩展 angularCompilerOptions 的问题（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fangular%2Fangular%2Fissues%2F41349" target="_blank">＃41349</a>）（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fangular%2Fangular%2Fcommit%2Fe0165fd7f8374694c1f9d74ecdea5af4f8bba4c7" target="_blank">e0165fd</a>），closes <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fangular%2Fangular%2Fissues%2F41343" target="_blank">＃41343</a></li>
