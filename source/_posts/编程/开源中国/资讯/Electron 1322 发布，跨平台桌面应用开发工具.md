                                                        <p>Electron 13.2.2 现已发布，主要更新内容如下： </p> 
<p><strong>Fixes</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>修复了<code>webView</code>中的一个问题，即如果用户用<code>esc</code>c而不是点击进入<code>webView</code>退出全屏，则不会发出<code>leave-html-full-screen</code>事件。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F30560" target="_blank">＃30560 </a></li> 
 <li>修复了一个问题：当 x 或 y 坐标为负数（off-screen）时，背景色不能正确应用于 Windows 上的 BrowserViews。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F30540" target="_blank">＃30540 </a></li> 
 <li>修复了一个问题：在 macOS 上用<code>titlebarStyle: 'hiddenInset'</code>切换<code>documentEdited</code>状态时会无意中移动了 traffic light。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F30603" target="_blank">＃30603 </a></li> 
</ul> 
<p><strong>Other Changes</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>Security：1227933 的反向移植 修复。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F30583" target="_blank">#30583 </a></li> 
 <li>Security：1231950 的反向移植修复。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F30584" target="_blank">#30584</a></li> 
 <li>Security：1234764 的反向移植修复。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F30587" target="_blank">#30587</a></li> 
 <li>Security：1234770 的反向移植修复。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F30586" target="_blank">#30586</a></li> 
 <li>Security：1234829 的反向移植修复。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Felectron%2Felectron%2Fpull%2F30585" target="_blank">#30585</a></li> 
</ul>
