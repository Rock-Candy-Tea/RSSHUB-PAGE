                                                        <p><span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#333333">Gradle 7.3.1 现已发布。Gradle 是一个基于 Apache Ant 和 Apache Maven 概念的项目自动化构建工具，支持依赖管理和多项目，类似 Maven，但比之简单轻便。它使用一种基于 Groovy 的特定领域语言来声明项目设置，而不是传统的 XML。</span></p> 
<p style="text-align:start"><span><span><span style="color:#24292f"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#ffffff"><span><span><span>这是 Gradle 7.3 的补丁版本，官方建议用户进行升级。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span><span><span><span style="color:#24292f"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#ffffff"><span><span><span>它修复了以下问题：</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p> 
<ul> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fgradle%2Fgradle%2Fissues%2F19058" target="_blank">#19058</a> 考虑恢复有关测试配置的突破性变化</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fgradle%2Fgradle%2Fpull%2F19067" target="_blank">#19067</a> 修复 Micronaut 发现的多个注释处理问题</li> 
</ul> 
<p style="color:#333333; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:left"><strong>升级说明</strong></p> 
<p style="color:#333333; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:left">通过更新包装器将构建切换为使用 Gradle 7.3.1：</p> 
<div> 
 <pre><code>./gradlew wrapper --gradle-version=7.3.1
</code></pre>
