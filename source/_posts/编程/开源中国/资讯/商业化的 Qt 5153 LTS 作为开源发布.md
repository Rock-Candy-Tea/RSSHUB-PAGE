<div class="content">
                                                                                            <p><span style="background-color:#ffffff"><span style="color:#121212">Qt 公司<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Flists.qt-project.org%2Fpipermail%2Fdevelopment%2F2022-March%2F042262.html" target="_blank">发布</a>了 Qt 5.15.3 LTS 的免费开源版本。2021 年，该公司<a href="https://www.oschina.net/news/125661/qt-5-15-lts-commercia">宣布</a></span></span><span style="background-color:#ffffff"><span style="color:#333333">从 1 月 5 日起将 Qt 5.15 作为仅供商业化的 LTS，彼时现有的 Qt 5.15 分支将公开可见，但不会看到任何新补丁，只有付费账户才可以使用长期支持版本的 Qt 5.15。</span></span></p> 
<p><a href="https://www.oschina.net/news/132032/qt-5-15-3-released" target="_blank">Qt 5.15.3 LTS </a><span style="color:#000000"><span style="background-color:#ffffff">则作为 Qt 5.15 LTS 系列的第一个商业专用补丁版本发布，该版本并没有增加任何新的功能，</span>包含<span style="background-color:#ffffff">了 200 多个错误修复</span><span style="background-color:#ffffff">和一些其他改进</span><span style="background-color:#ffffff">。</span></span></p> 
<p><img height="334" src="https://oscimg.oschina.net/oscnet/up-b9aae40812ef39d606473976dc056771498.png" width="500" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p> 
<p><span style="color:#121212">目前，</span><span style="color:#121212">开源 Qt 5.15.3 版本的源代码包已可供下载，并有一个新的"v5.15.3-lts-lgpl" Git 分支。事实上，该 Git </span><span style="background-color:#ffffff"><span style="color:#121212">tag </span></span><span style="color:#121212">早在去年 11 月就已完成，但直到如今才公开推送和公布。</span></p> 
<ul> 
 <li style="text-align:start"><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fdownload.qt.io%2Fofficial_releases%2Fqt%2F5.15%2F5.15.3%2F" target="_blank">https://download.qt.io/official_releases/qt/5.15/5.15.3/</a></li> 
 <li style="text-align:start"><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fdownload.qt.io%2Fofficial_releases%2FQtForPython%2Fpyside2%2FPySide2-5.15.3-src%2F" target="_blank">https://download.qt.io/official_releases/QtForPython/pyside2/PySide2-5.15.3-src/</a></li> 
</ul> 
<p><span style="color:#000000">Qt 5.15.8 LTS 将是最新的商业版本，该版本</span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.phoronix.com%2Fscan.php%3Fpage%3Dnews_item%26px%3DQt-5.15.3-Open-Source" target="_blank">预计</a><span style="color:#000000">会在明年一月开源。</span></p> 
<p>详情可<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Flists.qt-project.org%2Fpipermail%2Fdevelopment%2F2022-March%2F042262.html" target="_blank">查看邮件列表</a>。</p>
