                                                                                            <p>Spring Security 6.0.0-M1 & 5.7.0-M1<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fspring.io%2Fblog%2F2022%2F01%2F17%2Fspring-security-6-0-0-m1-and-5-7-0-m1-available-now" target="_blank"> 已发布</a>。</p> 
<p>其中，6.0.0 M1 是 Spring Security 6 发布的首个更新，主要是升级底层组件，特别是要求 JDK 17+，以及迁移到 Jakarta EE 9 APIs。</p> 
<p>6.0.0 M1 新特性</p> 
<ul> 
 <li>为 RelyingPartyRegistration 添加 NameIdFormat 支持<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fspring-projects%2Fspring-security%2Fissues%2F9115" target="_blank">#9115</a></li> 
 <li>优化参考文档<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fspring-projects%2Fspring-security%2Fpull%2F9668" target="_blank">#9668</a></li> 
 <li>清除 null 身份验证以修复 ThreadLocal 泄漏<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fspring-projects%2Fspring-security%2Fpull%2F9877" target="_blank">#9877</a></li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fspring-projects%2Fspring-security%2Fissues%2F10333" target="_blank">Gh-10333</a><span> </span>将 HttpSecurityDsl 移动到另一个包<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fspring-projects%2Fspring-security%2Fpull%2F10429" target="_blank">#10429</a></li> 
 <li>对 LdapAuthoritiesPopulator 进行 postProcessed<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fspring-projects%2Fspring-security%2Fpull%2F9276" target="_blank">#9276</a></li> 
 <li>使 SP NameIDPolicy 在 RelyingPartyRegistration 中可配置<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fspring-projects%2Fspring-security%2Fpull%2F9227" target="_blank">#9227</a></li> 
 <li>PermitAllSupport 支持 AuthorizeHttpRequestsConfigurer<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fspring-projects%2Fspring-security%2Fpull%2F10543" target="_blank">#10543</a></li> 
 <li>升级 Authorization 文档<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fspring-projects%2Fspring-security%2Fissues%2F10442" target="_blank">#10442</a></li> 
</ul>
