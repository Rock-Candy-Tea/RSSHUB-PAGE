                                                                                            <p>GNS3 是一个图形化的网络拓扑逻辑模拟工具。GNS3 2.2.20 现已发布，具体更新内容如下：</p> 
<p><strong>GUI</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>修复项目无法加载的问题。修复<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FGNS3%2Fgns3-gui%2Fissues%2F3140" target="_blank">＃3140</a></li> 
 <li>在等待用户接受/拒绝 SSL 证书时，不能连接服务器。修复<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FGNS3%2Fgns3-gui%2Fissues%2F3144" target="_blank">＃3144</a></li> 
 <li>修复无效的服务器版本检查请求。修复<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FGNS3%2Fgns3-gui%2Fissues%2F3144" target="_blank">＃3144</a></li> 
 <li>升级 dependencies</li> 
 <li>将 terminator 添加为预定义的自定义控制台选项</li> 
</ul> 
<p><strong>Server</strong> </p> 
<ul> 
 <li>发布 Web UI 2.2.20 版本</li> 
 <li>修复使用 HTTPS 远程服务器捕获数据包的问题。修复<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FGNS3%2Fgns3-gui%2Fissues%2F1882" target="_blank">＃1882</a></li> 
 <li>与在线仓库同步后，同步设备文件并删除旧文件。修复<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FGNS3%2Fgns3-gui%2Fissues%2F1876" target="_blank">＃1876</a></li> 
 <li>升级 dependencies</li> 
 <li>修复导出丢失文件的问题</li> 
 <li>解决了尝试导出临时 Dynamips 文件时的问题。</li> 
</ul> 
<p>更新说明：<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FGNS3%2Fgns3-gui%2Freleases%2Ftag%2Fv2.2.20" target="_blank">https://github.com/GNS3/gns3-gui/releases/tag/v2.2.20</a></p>
