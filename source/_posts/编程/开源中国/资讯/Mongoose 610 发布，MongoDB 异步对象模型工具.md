                                                        <p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start">Mongoose 是设计用于异步环境的 MongoDB 对象模型工具，支持 promises 和 callbacks。Mongoose 6.1.0 正式发布，本次更新内容如下：</p> 
<ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
 <li>feat(populate):<span> </span><span style="color:#2e3033">支持子文档的引用</span><span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FAutomattic%2Fmongoose%2Fissues%2F10856" target="_blank">#10856</a></li> 
 <li>feat(document): 添加<span> </span><code>ownerDocument()</code><span> </span>方法<span style="color:#2e3033">到顶层文档，与子文档保持一致</span><span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FAutomattic%2Fmongoose%2Fissues%2F10884" target="_blank">#10884</a> </li> 
 <li>fix: mongodb 驱动升级到 4.2.1<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FAutomattic%2Fmongoose%2Fpull%2F11032" target="_blank">#11032</a><span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FAutomattic%2Fmongoose%2Fissues%2F10985" target="_blank">#10985</a> </li> 
 <li>feat(schema): MongoDB 5 时间序列集合支持<span> </span><code>timeseries</code><span> </span>选项<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FAutomattic%2Fmongoose%2Fissues%2F10611" target="_blank">#10611</a></li> 
 <li>feat(mongoose): 加入全局<span> </span><code>strictPopulate</code><span> </span>选项<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FAutomattic%2Fmongoose%2Fissues%2F10694" target="_blank">#10694</a> </li> 
 <li>feat(mongoose+connection): 加入全局<span> </span><code>mongoose.syncIndexes()</code><span> </span>和<span> </span><code>Connection.prototype.syncIndexes()</code><span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FAutomattic%2Fmongoose%2Fissues%2F10893" target="_blank">#10893</a> </li> 
 <li>feat(query):<span> </span><span style="color:#2e3033">支持从推测（</span><span style="color:#24292f">projections</span><span style="color:#2e3033">）中移除字段</span><span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FAutomattic%2Fmongoose%2Fpull%2F10630" target="_blank">#10630</a> </li> 
 <li>feat(aggregate): 为聚合添加<span> </span><span style="color:#24292f">unionWith 方法</span><span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FAutomattic%2Fmongoose%2Fpull%2F10961" target="_blank">#10961</a> </li>
