                                                                                            <p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start">Snapcraft 是一个用于 Linux 系统上的打包、分发与更新工具，由于绑定了依赖项，所以不需要修改就可以在所有主要 Linux 系统上运行。</p> 
<p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start">目前 Snapcraft 7.0.8 已正式发布，更新内容如下：</p> 
<ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
 <li>store: 使用 legacy 导出登录<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fsnapcore%2Fsnapcraft%2Fpull%2F3797" target="_blank">#3797</a></li> 
 <li>状态命令：静态 linting<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fsnapcore%2Fsnapcraft%2Fpull%2F3802" target="_blank">#3802</a></li> 
 <li>包存储库：确保在使用之前安装依赖项<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fsnapcore%2Fsnapcraft%2Fpull%2F3801" target="_blank">#3801</a></li> 
 <li>包存储库：强制包刷新<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fsnapcore%2Fsnapcraft%2Fpull%2F3804" target="_blank">#3804</a></li> 
 <li>传播：将构建包添加到包存储库测试中<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fsnapcore%2Fsnapcraft%2Fpull%2F3806" target="_blank">#3806</a></li> 
 <li>store：支持 configparser 凭据<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fsnapcore%2Fsnapcraft%2Fpull%2F3805" target="_blank">#3805</a></li> 
 <li>提升：将 snap-name 添加到 args  <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fsnapcore%2Fsnapcraft%2Fpull%2F3807" target="_blank">#3807</a></li> 
 <li>商店：登录不需要导出凭据<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fsnapcore%2Fsnapcraft%2Fpull%2F3808" target="_blank">#3808</a></li> 
 <li>商店：正确登录注册密钥<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fsnapcore%2Fsnapcraft%2Fpull%2F3809" target="_blank">#3809</a></li> 
</ul>
