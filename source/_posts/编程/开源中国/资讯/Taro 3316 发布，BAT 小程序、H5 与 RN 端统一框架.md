<p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><span style="color:#333333">此版本更新内容包括：</span></p>

<p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><strong>RN</strong></p> 
<ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
 <li>支持 backgroundTextStyle 设置, <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FNervJS%2Ftaro%2Fpull%2F10739" target="_blank">#10739</a></li> 
 <li>postcss 支持插件自定义, <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FNervJS%2Ftaro%2Fpull%2F10775" target="_blank">#10775</a></li> 
 <li>富文本支持自动宽高, <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FNervJS%2Ftaro%2Fpull%2F10794" target="_blank">#10794</a> <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FNervJS%2Ftaro%2Fpull%2F10807" target="_blank">#10807</a></li> 
</ul>

 <li>tabbar 初始配置修复, <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FNervJS%2Ftaro%2Fissues%2F10736" target="_blank">#10736</a> <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FNervJS%2Ftaro%2Fissues%2F10758" target="_blank">#10758</a></li>
