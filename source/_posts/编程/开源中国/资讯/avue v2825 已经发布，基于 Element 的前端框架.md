                                                        <p>avue v2.8.25 已经发布，基于 Element 的前端框架</p> 
<p>此版本更新内容包括：</p> 
<h2>v2.8.25</h2> 
<p><code>2021-12-114</code></p> 
<p><code>新增</code></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>新增Crud组件searchType属性，可以重新定义搜索组件的类型，可以和表单不一致<a href="https://gitee.com/smallweigit/avue/issues/I4IRHU" target="_blank">gitee_I4IRHU</a>,<a href="https://gitee.com/link?target=https%3A%2F%2Favuejs.com%2Fcrud%2Fcrud-search" target="_blank">在线例子</a></li> 
 <li>新增Form组件mockText和printText属性<a href="https://gitee.com/smallweigit/avue/issues/I4J9X3" target="_blank">gitee_I4J9X3</a></li> 
 <li>新增Select、cascader、date、inputColor、time组件的popperClass下拉框class的属性<a href="https://gitee.com/smallweigit/avue/issues/I4JE88" target="_blank">gitee_I4JE88</a></li> 
</ul> 
<p><code>修复</code></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>修复Crud组件menuType为menu时候文字不能定义的问题<a href="https://gitee.com/smallweigit/avue/issues/I4LFTF" target="_blank">gitee_I4LFTF</a></li> 
 <li>修复InputTree组件不能选值为0的情况<a href="https://gitee.com/smallweigit/avue/issues/I4L48X" target="_blank">gitee_I4L48X</a>,<a href="https://gitee.com/link?target=https%3A%2F%2Favuejs.com%2Fcomponent%2Finput-tree" target="_blank">在线例子</a></li> 
 <li>修复InputTable组件的清空后不能选择问题<a href="https://gitee.com/smallweigit/avue/issues/I4L2OC" target="_blank">gitee_I4L2OC</a>,<a href="https://gitee.com/link?target=https%3A%2F%2Favuejs.com%2Fcomponent%2Finput-table" target="_blank">在线例子</a></li> 
 <li>修复Tree组件的菜单不消失问题<a href="https://gitee.com/smallweigit/avue/issues/I4LYPW" target="_blank">gitee_I4LYPW</a>,<a href="https://gitee.com/link?target=https%3A%2F%2Favuejs.com%2Fdefault%2Ftree" target="_blank">在线例子</a></li> 
 <li>修复核心字典的逻辑报错问题<a href="https://gitee.com/smallweigit/avue/issues/I4KPHW" target="_blank">gitee_I4KPHW</a>,<a href="https://gitee.com/link?target=https%3A%2F%2Favuejs.com%2Fform%2Fform-dic.html" target="_blank">在线例子</a></li> 
</ul>

 <li>优化dynamic组件的卡槽模板问题</li> 
</ul>
