                                                                                            <p>星际译王近日发布了面向 Windows 平台的新版本，包含三个分支：</p> 
<ul> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fsourceforge.net%2Fprojects%2Fstardict-4%2Ffiles%2F3.0.6.2%2Fstardict-3.0.6.2.exe%2Fdownload" target="_blank">stardict-3.0.6.2.exe</a></li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fsourceforge.net%2Fprojects%2Fstardict-4%2Ffiles%2F3.0.6%2Fstardict-3.0.6.exe%2Fdownload" target="_blank">stardict-3.0.6.exe</a></li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fsourceforge.net%2Fprojects%2Fstardict-4%2Ffiles%2F3.0.1.2%2Fstardict-3.0.1.2-wordnet.exe%2Fdownload" target="_blank">stardict-3.0.1.2-wordnet.exe</a></li> 
</ul> 
<p>更新内容包括添加更多词典和插件、支持压缩字典格式，以及客户端支持更多加密协议等。</p> 
<p><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=http%3A%2F%2Fstardict-4.sourceforge.net%2FChangeLog" target="_blank">详情查看 Changelog</a>。</p> 
<p style="color:#333333; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:left">以下内容来自作者的介绍：</p> 
<blockquote> 
 <p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0">星际译王是跨平台的国际词典软件！它功能强大，实用性强，“通配符匹配”，“鼠标查词”，“模糊查询” 等功能倍受青睐！星际译王 3.0 版更增加了全文翻译，网络词典等新功能。</p> 
</blockquote>
