                                                        <p>Apache Camel 发布了长期支持版本(LTS) 3.7.5，此版本是 3.7.x LTS 分支的一个补丁版本，包含 <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fcamel.apache.org%2Freleases%2Frelease-3.7.5%2F" target="_blank">17 个</a>错误修复和功能改进，主要如下：</p> 
<ul> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fissues.apache.org%2Fjira%2Fbrowse%2FCAMEL-16718" target="_blank">CAMEL-16718</a> 修复与 Netty TCP + Resilience4J 断路器的冲突</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fissues.apache.org%2Fjira%2Fbrowse%2FCAMEL-16707" target="_blank">CAMEL-16707</a> 修复 camel-rabbitmq 连接在“声明”过程中出现泄露错误的问题</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fissues.apache.org%2Fjira%2Fbrowse%2FCAMEL-16699" target="_blank">CAMEL-16699</a> 修复 HttpProducer 在桥接端点上跳过查询参数的请求 header，被常见类型而损坏的问题</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fissues.apache.org%2Fjira%2Fbrowse%2FCAMEL-16785" target="_blank">CAMEL-16785</a> camel 3.7.x - 依赖升级</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fissues.apache.org%2Fjira%2Fbrowse%2FCAMEL-16619" target="_blank">CAMEL-16619</a> camel-rabbitmq - Producer 在将 rabbit 频道返回到数据池中时将其销毁</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fissues.apache.org%2Fjira%2Fbrowse%2FCAMEL-16686" target="_blank">CAMEL-16686</a> camel-website - 构建期间的 Asciidoc ERROR/WARN</li> 
</ul> 
<p>下载地址：<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fcamel.apache.org%2Fdownload%2F" target="_blank">https://camel.apache.org/download/</a></p> 
<p>Apache Camel 是一个开源的集成框架，可快速、轻松地集成各种消费或产生数据的系统。它针对应用集成场景抽象出了一套消息交互模型，通过组件的方式进行第三方系统的接入，目前 Apache Camel 已经提供了 300 多种组件能够接入 HTTP，JMS，TCP，WS-*，WebSocket 等多种传输协议。</p>
