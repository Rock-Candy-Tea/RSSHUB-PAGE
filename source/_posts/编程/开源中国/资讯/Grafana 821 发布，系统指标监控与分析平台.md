                                                        <p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start">Grafana 8.2.1 现已发布。Grafana 是一个功能丰富的指标标准仪表板和图形编辑器，用于分析和监控 Graphite、Elasticsearch、OpenTSDB、Prometheus 和 InfluxDB。</p> 
<p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start">具体更新内容如下：</p> 
<h4 style="margin-left:.6em; margin-right:0; text-align:start">Bug fixes</h4> 
<ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
 <li><strong>Dashboard：</strong>修复重复面板的渲染。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fgrafana%2Fgrafana%2Fpull%2F39991" target="_blank">#39991</a></li> 
 <li><strong>Datasources：</strong>修复在未找到插件时删除数据源的问题。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fgrafana%2Fgrafana%2Fpull%2F40095" target="_blank">#40095</a></li> 
 <li><strong>Packaging：</strong>从 systemd 单元文件中删除 systemcallfilters 部分。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fgrafana%2Fgrafana%2Fpull%2F40176" target="_blank">#40176</a></li> 
 <li><strong>Prometheus：</strong>向 HTTP 客户端选项添加 Headers。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fgrafana%2Fgrafana%2Fpull%2F40214" target="_blank">#40214</a></li> 
</ul>
