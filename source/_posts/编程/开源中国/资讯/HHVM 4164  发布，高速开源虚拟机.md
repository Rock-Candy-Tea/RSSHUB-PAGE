                                                                                            <p>HHVM 4.164 现已发布，HHVM<span style="color:#000000"> 4.161 - 4.163、以</span>及 4.128 和 4.153 LTS 版本还会继续得到支持。</p> 
<p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><span style="color:#333333">更新内容如下：</span></p> 
<p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><strong>Highlights</strong></p> 
<ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
 <li><code class="language-plaintext">hh</code>现在拒绝<code class="language-plaintext">catch</code>块中的类型别名。这些已经在运行时被禁止（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Ffacebook%2Fhhvm%2Fcommit%2F7fff3fda12e627be7fa18750677ea0f0ada8f172" target="_blank">commit</a>）。</li> 
 <li>修复了增量类型检查的问题，其中要求 XHP 属性并不总是显示类型错误（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Ffacebook%2Fhhvm%2Fcommit%2F59f6ba82d11ba1c6e0eb7867a04b96bc49581e5a" target="_blank">commit</a>）</li> 
 <li>在类上引入了<code class="language-plaintext">__Docs</code>属性（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Ffacebook%2Fhhvm%2Fcommit%2F9e284df22eb12fbcb17e1dcf7e10ec632d17d607" target="_blank">commit</a>）</li> 
</ul>
