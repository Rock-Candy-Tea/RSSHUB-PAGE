                                                        <p>开源监控项目frostmourne最新发布版本0.7.1-RELEASE，带来了很多核心功能，欢迎使用。</p> 
<h1>更新内容：</h1> 
<pre><span style="color:#cc7832">* </span><span>【</span>0.7<span>】静默优化，支持字段判断表达式 </span><span style="color:#cc7832">[2022-05-05]
</span><span style="color:#cc7832">* </span><span>【</span>0.7<span>】</span>mysql, clickhouse<span>监控增加表达式监控规则 </span><span style="color:#cc7832">[2022-05-06]
</span><span style="color:#cc7832">* </span><span>【</span>0.7<span>】增加</span>ping<span>监控报警 </span><span style="color:#cc7832">[2022-05-07]
</span><span style="color:#cc7832">* </span><span>【</span>0.7<span>】增加 </span><span style="color:#cc7832">[skywalking]</span>(https://github.com/apache/skywalking) <span>日志数据监控 </span><span style="color:#cc7832">[2022-05-09]
</span><span style="color:#cc7832">* </span><span>【</span>0.7<span>】增加 </span><span style="color:#cc7832">[skywalking]</span>(https://github.com/apache/skywalking) <span>报警数据监控 </span><span style="color:#cc7832">[2022-05-10]
</span><span style="color:#cc7832">* </span><span>【</span>0.7<span>】增加 </span><span style="color:#cc7832">[prometheus]</span>(https://github.com/prometheus/prometheus) <span>数据监控报警支持 </span><span style="color:#cc7832">[2022-05-12]
</span><span style="color:#cc7832">* </span><span>【</span>0.7.1<span>】增加判断规则：持续</span>n<span>次满足规则时报警 </span><span style="color:#cc7832">[2022-05-13]</span></pre> 
<h1>项目介绍：</h1> 
<pre><span style="color:#808080">`Frostmourne`</span>(<span>霜之哀伤</span>)<span>是汽车之家经销商技术部监控系统的开源版本，用于帮助监控几乎所有数据库</span>(<span>包括</span><span style="color:#808080">`Elasticsearch`</span>, <span style="color:#808080">`Prometheus`</span>, <span style="color:#808080">`SkyWalking`</span>, <span style="color:#808080">`MySql` </span><span>等等</span>)
<span>数据。如果你已经建立起了日志系统，</span><span>指标体系，却苦恼于没有一个配套监控系统，也许它能帮到你。</span></pre> 
<h1>主要功能：</h1> 
<pre><span style="color:#cc7832">* </span><span>只需要写一条数据查询就可以轻松搞定监控
</span><span style="color:#cc7832">* </span><span>多种数据源支持：</span><span style="color:#808080">`Elasticsearch, HTTP, SkyWalking, Prometheus, InfluxDB, MySQL/TiDb, ClickHouse, PING`
</span><span style="color:#cc7832">* </span><span>数值计算类型监控：</span><span style="color:#808080">`count, min, max, avg, sum, unique count, percentiles, standard deviation`</span>; <span style="color:#808080">`Elasticsearch`</span><span>数据支持分桶
</span><span style="color:#cc7832">* </span><span>报警消息发送方式：钉钉</span>(<span>机器人</span>)<span>、企业微信</span>(<span>机器人</span>)<span>、飞书机器人、</span>Email<span>、短信、</span>HTTP
<span style="color:#cc7832">* </span><span>支持消息格式：</span><span style="color:#808080">`text, markdown`
</span><span style="color:#cc7832">* </span><span>灵活的报警消息</span><span style="color:#808080">`Freemarker`</span><span>模板定制，支持变量占位符；消息模板管理
</span><span style="color:#cc7832">* </span><span>分布式调度实现，每个监控都是独立调度，互不影响
</span><span style="color:#cc7832">* </span><span>报警消息附带日志查询短链接，直达报警原因
</span><span style="color:#cc7832">* </span><span>数值同比监控
</span><span style="color:#cc7832">* </span><span style="color:#808080">`HTTP`</span><span>数据监控</span>, <span style="color:#808080">`Javascript`</span><span>表达式判断是否报警</span>; <span style="color:#808080">`PING`</span><span>连通监控
</span><span style="color:#cc7832">* </span><span>前端简单易用：监控管理、测试、另存、执行日志和历史消息
</span><span style="color:#cc7832">* </span><span style="color:#808080">`Elasticsearch`</span><span>数据查询、分享和下载
</span><span style="color:#cc7832">* </span><span>报警消息抑制功能，防止消息轰炸
</span><span style="color:#cc7832">* </span><span>自带账号，团队，部门信息管理模块，也可自己实现内部对接
</span><span style="color:#cc7832">* </span><span>集成</span><span style="color:#808080">`LDAP`</span><span>登录认证
</span><span style="color:#cc7832">* </span><span>权限控制，数据隔离，各团队互不影响</span></pre>
