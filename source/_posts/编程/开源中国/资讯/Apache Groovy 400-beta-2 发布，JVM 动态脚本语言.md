                                                        <p>Apache Groovy 4.0.0 第二个 Beta 测试版已发布，Apache Groovy 是用于 JVM 的多面性编程语言。</p> 
<p>新特性</p> 
<ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
 <li>[<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fissues.apache.org%2Fjira%2Fbrowse%2FGROOVY-5450" target="_blank">GROOVY-5450</a>] - 静态编译时支持对 final 字段分配进行检查</li> 
 <li>[<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fissues.apache.org%2Fjira%2Fbrowse%2FGROOVY-10240" target="_blank">GROOVY-10240</a>] - 支持 record 语法</li> 
 <li>[<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fissues.apache.org%2Fjira%2Fbrowse%2FGROOVY-10274" target="_blank">GROOVY-10274</a>] - 对 DGM 的改进：提供 set union 和 minus，其采用了 closure/comparator</li> 
 <li>支持 sealed classes</li> 
 <li>支持 JDK 17</li> 
</ul>
