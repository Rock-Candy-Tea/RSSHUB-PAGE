                                                        <p>Firefox 89.0.1 版本现已发布，具体更新内容如下：</p> 
<p><strong>Fixed</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li> <p>Windows：解决了导致某些 screen readers 无法再与 Firefox 正确交互的问题（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fbugzilla.mozilla.org%2Fshow_bug.cgi%3Fid%3D1714212" target="_blank">bug 1714212</a>）</p> </li> 
 <li> <p>更新翻译，包括完整的西班牙语（墨西哥）本地化和其他改进（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fbugzilla.mozilla.org%2Fshow_bug.cgi%3Fid%3D1714946" target="_blank">bug 1714946</a>）</p> </li> 
 <li> <p>修复各种字体相关的 regressions（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fbugzilla.mozilla.org%2Fshow_bug.cgi%3Fid%3D1694174" target="_blank">bug 1694174</a>）</p> </li> 
 <li> <p>Linux：修复 WebRender 的性能和稳定性 regressions（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fbugzilla.mozilla.org%2Fshow_bug.cgi%3Fid%3D1715895" target="_blank">bug 1715895</a>、<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fbugzilla.mozilla.org%2Fshow_bug.cgi%3Fid%3D1715902" target="_blank">bug 1715902</a>）</p> </li> 
 <li> <p>macOS：修复在外部显示器上滚动页面时屏幕闪烁的问题（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fbugzilla.mozilla.org%2Fshow_bug.cgi%3Fid%3D1715452" target="_blank">bug 1715452</a>）</p> </li> 
 <li> <p>Enterprise：修复<code>DisableDeveloperTools</code>策略不再有效的问题（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fbugzilla.mozilla.org%2Fshow_bug.cgi%3Fid%3D1715777" target="_blank">bug 1715777</a>）</p> </li> 
 <li> <p>Linux：修复某些 GTK 主题上损坏的 scrollbars（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fbugzilla.mozilla.org%2Fshow_bug.cgi%3Fid%3D1714103" target="_blank">bug 1714103</a>）</p> </li> 
 <li> <p>各种稳定性和<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.mozilla.org%2Fsecurity%2Fadvisories%2Fmfsa2021-27%2F" target="_blank">安全性修复</a></p> </li> 
</ul>

</ul>
