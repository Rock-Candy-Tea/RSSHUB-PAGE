 <div style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
  <div style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
   <p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"><span style="color:#333333">Serverless 发布了<span> </span></span><span style="color:#24292f"><strong>2.68.0</strong></span><span style="color:#333333"> 版本，该框架使用 AWS Lambda、Azure Functions、Google CloudFunctions 等技术，可以构建 Serverless 架构的 Web、移动和 IoT 应用。 </span></p> 
   <p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"><span style="color:#333333">此版本主要有以下改动：</span></p> 
   <h3 style="margin-left:.6em; margin-right:0"><strong>特征</strong></h3> 
   <ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
    <li><strong>AWS 部署：</strong> 
     <ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
      <li>确保<span> </span><code>deploy function</code><span> </span>中的函数状态一致 (<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fserverless%2Fserverless%2Fpull%2F10288" target="_blank">#10288</a><span> </span>)</li> 
      <li>支持所有区域的<span> </span><code>iso</code><span> </span>和<span> </span><code>isob</code><span> </span>分区  (<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fserverless%2Fserverless%2Fissues%2F10299" target="_blank">#10299</a><span> </span>)</li> 
     </ul> </li> 
    <li><strong>AWS SQS：</strong>支持<span> </span><code>filterPatterns</code><span> </span>(<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fserverless%2Fserverless%2Fpull%2F10297" target="_blank">#10297</a><span> </span>)</li> 
    <li><strong>AWS Stream：</strong>支持<span> </span><code>filterPatterns</code><span> </span>(<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fserverless%2Fserverless%2Fissues%2F10285" target="_blank">#10285</a><span> </span>)</li> 
   </ul> 
   <h3 style="margin-left:.6em; margin-right:0"><strong>Bug修复</strong></h3> 
   <ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
    <li><strong>AWS SQS：</strong>仅接受直接 ARN 分配中的纯字符串形式 (<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fserverless%2Fserverless%2Fissues%2F10263" target="_blank">#10263</a><span> </span>)</li> 
    <li><strong>变量：</strong>解析地址和参数值中的变量 (<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fserverless%2Fserverless%2Fpull%2F10296" target="_blank">#10296</a><span> </span>)</li> 
   </ul> 
   <h3 style="margin-left:.6em; margin-right:0"><strong>维护改进</strong></h3> 
   <ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
    <li><strong>变量：</strong>隔离内部逻辑以供复用 (<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fserverless%2Fserverless%2Fpull%2F10296" target="_blank">#10296</a><span> </span>)</li> 
   </ul> 
   <h3 style="margin-left:.6em; margin-right:0"><strong>模板</strong></h3> 
   <ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
    <li>更新<span> </span><code>aws-java-maven</code><span> </span>中的依赖项 (<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fserverless%2Fserverless%2Fissues%2F10289" target="_blank">#10289</a><span> </span>)</li> 
   </ul> 
   <p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0">更新公告：<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fserverless%2Fserverless%2Freleases%2Ftag%2Fv2.68.0" target="_blank">https://github.com/serverless/serverless/releases/tag/v2.68.0</a></p>
