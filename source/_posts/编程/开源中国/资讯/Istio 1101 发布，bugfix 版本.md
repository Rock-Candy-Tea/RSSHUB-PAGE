                                                        <p>Istio 1.10.1 已发布，此版本修复了 1.10 中的错误，以提升稳定性。</p> 
<ul> 
 <li> <p>修复了导致<code>VirtualService</code>中的特定目标无法修改<code>Host</code>标头的问题 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fistio%2Fistio%2Fissues%2F33226" target="_blank">Issue #33226</a>)</p> </li> 
 <li> <p>修复了无法在<code>IstioOperator</code>中设置 PDB<code>maxUnavailable</code>字段的问题 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fistio%2Fistio%2Fissues%2F31910" target="_blank">Issue #31910</a>)</p> </li> 
</ul> 
<p><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fistio.io%2Flatest%2Fnews%2Freleases%2F1.10.x%2Fannouncing-1.10%2F" target="_blank">Istio 1.10 </a>是上个月发布的大版本更新，主要亮点包括：</p> 
<ul> 
 <li>Discovery Selectors</li> 
 <li>稳定修订标签</li> 
 <li>Sidecar 网络变更</li> 
 <li>全新的 Istio.io 网站外观</li> 
 <li>全新开放设计文档</li> 
</ul> 
<p>下载地址：<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fistio.io%2Flatest%2Fdocs%2Fsetup%2Fgetting-started%2F%23download" target="_blank">https://istio.io/latest/docs/setup/getting-started/#download</a></p>
