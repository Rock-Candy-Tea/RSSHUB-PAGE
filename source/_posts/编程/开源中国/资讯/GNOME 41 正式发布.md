<div class="content">
                                                                                            <p><span style="color:#000000">GNOME 41 现已发布。这是开发团队历时 6 个月的产物，包括了许多重大改进和新功能、以及大量的增强功能。此版本中最显着的变化包括改进的软件应用程序、新的多任务设置和增强的电源管理功能。“通过这些变化，GNOME 变得更智能、更灵活，并提供比以往更丰富、更引人入胜的体验。”</span></p> 
<p style="text-align:start"><span><span><span style="color:#000000"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span>新版本还<span>为开发人员</span>带来了重大<span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fhelp.gnome.org%2Fmisc%2Frelease-notes%2F41.0%2Fdevelopers.html" target="_blank">改进</a></span>，包括新的开发人员文档网站、一个重要的新版 Human Interface Guidelines、Builder IDE 中的新功能、GTK 4 增强功能等等。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p> 
<p style="text-align:start"><span><span><span style="color:#000000"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span>且 GNOME 41 还支持 <span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fhelp.gnome.org%2Fmisc%2Frelease-notes%2F41.0%2Fi18n.html" target="_blank">38 种语言</a></span>。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span><span><span><span style="color:#000000"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span>GNOME 41 是第二个使用 GNOME 新版本编号方案的版本，下一个版本 42 计划于 2022 年 3 月发布。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p> 
<h4><strong><span>More Power To You</span></strong></h4> 
<p><strong><span><img alt height="368" src="https://oscimg.oschina.net/oscnet/up-6e65ad7d6d1f94268afa5214757f1ab298b.png" width="500" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></strong></p> 
<p style="text-align:start"><span><span><span style="color:#000000"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span>在 GNOME 41 中，电源模式功能得到了改进。现在可以从“<span>System Status”</span>菜单快速更改电源模式，并且增强了<span>节电</span>模式。因此当屏幕处于活动状态时，屏幕会更快地变暗和消失。电池电量低时，<span>节电模式</span>也会自动开启。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p> 
<p style="text-align:start"><span><span><span style="color:#000000"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span>还添加了初始支持以允许应用程序请求特定的电源模式，这与允许对性能敏感的应用程序（例如游戏）请求性能模式最​​为相关。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p>

<h4><span>Mobile Settings</span></h4> 
<p><span><img alt height="358" src="https://oscimg.oschina.net/oscnet/up-8d85775214259543dd077b9a8b80c26e287.png" width="500" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p> 
<p style="text-align:start"><span><span><span style="color:#000000"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span>GNOME 41 包括一个新的<span>移动网络</span>设置面板。这允许配置移动网络连接，并与 2G、3G、4G 和 GSM/LTE 调制解调器配合使用。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p> 
<p style="text-align:start"><span><span><span style="color:#000000"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span>仅当存在受支持的调制解调器时才会显示新的<span>移动网络</span>设置。它们允许设置网络类型、选择是否应使用移动数据以及漫游时是否应使用数据。它们还支持使用多个 SIM 卡和调制解调器，并允许在网络之间轻松切换。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span><span><span><span style="color:#000000"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span>新设置取代了之前的移动网络设置。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
