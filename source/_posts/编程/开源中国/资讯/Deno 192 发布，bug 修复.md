                                                                                            <p>Deno 1.9.2 现已发布，此版本包含了一些错误修复，具体更新内容如下：</p> 
<ul> 
 <li>修复：正确解析 websocket 消息（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fdenoland%2Fdeno%2Fpull%2F10318" target="_blank">＃10318</a>）</li> 
 <li>修复：M1 上的 standalone bin 损坏（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fdenoland%2Fdeno%2Fpull%2F10311" target="_blank">＃10311</a>）</li> 
 <li>修复：不要让内部错误 frames gray-out（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fdenoland%2Fdeno%2Fpull%2F10293" target="_blank">＃10293</a>）</li> 
 <li>fix(op_crates/fetch)：响应 inspect regression（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fdenoland%2Fdeno%2Fpull%2F10295" target="_blank">＃10295</a>）</li> 
 <li>fix(runtime)：在未找到 cwd 时不要 panic（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fdenoland%2Fdeno%2Fpull%2F10238" target="_blank">＃10238</a>）</li> 
 <li>fix(op_crates/webgpu)：必要时移动非空操作缓冲区参数检查（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fdenoland%2Fdeno%2Fpull%2F10319" target="_blank">＃10319</a>）</li> 
 <li>fix(lsp)：文档符号性能标记（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fdenoland%2Fdeno%2Fpull%2F10264" target="_blank">＃10264</a>）</li> 
</ul>
