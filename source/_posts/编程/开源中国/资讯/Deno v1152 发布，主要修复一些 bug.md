                                                        <p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start">Deno v1.15.2 发布，<span style="color:#333333">此版本包含了一些错误修复，具体更新内容如下：</span></p> 
<ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
 <li>eat(unstable):<span> </span><span style="color:#24292f">用于兼容模式的节点 CJS 和 ESM 解析器。</span>(<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fdenoland%2Fdeno%2Fpull%2F12424" target="_blank">#12424</a>)</li> 
 <li>fix(cli):<span> </span><span style="color:#24292f">为 tsc 重新启用<span> </span><code>allowSyntheticDefaultImports</code><span> </span>。</span>(<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fdenoland%2Fdeno%2Fpull%2F12435" target="_blank">#12435</a>)</li> 
 <li>fix(cli/fmt_errors):  无需担心源代码的<span style="color:#24292f">行格式错误。</span><span> </span>(<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fdenoland%2Fdeno%2Fpull%2F12449" target="_blank">#12449</a>)</li> 
 <li>fix(cli/tests): 从消息处理程序中移除 worker 测试声明。 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fdenoland%2Fdeno%2Fpull%2F12439" target="_blank">#12439</a>)</li> 
 <li>fix(console): 修复<span style="color:#2e3033">原始包装器对象的显示方式。<span> </span></span>(<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fdenoland%2Fdeno%2Fpull%2F12425" target="_blank">#12425</a>)</li> 
 <li>fix(core): 主动取消后不再轮询。(<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fdenoland%2Fdeno%2Fpull%2F12385" target="_blank">#12385</a>)</li> 
 <li>fix(core): 主<span style="color:#2e3033">动轮询异步操作</span><span> </span>(<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fdenoland%2Fdeno%2Fpull%2F12385" target="_blank">#12385</a>)</li> 
 <li>fix(fmt):<span> </span><span style="color:#24292f">保留 JS 文档类型声明的括号。 (</span>(<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fdenoland%2Fdeno%2Fpull%2F12475" target="_blank">#12475</a>)</li> 
 <li>fix(fmt): 不应该<span style="color:#2e3033">删除序列表达式周围的括号。</span>(<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fdenoland%2Fdeno%2Fpull%2F12461" target="_blank">#12461</a>)</li> 
 <li>fix(runtime/ops/worker_host):<span> </span><span style="color:#2e3033">将权限参数解析移到 Rust。<span> </span></span>(<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fdenoland%2Fdeno%2Fpull%2F12297" target="_blank">#12297</a>)</li> 
</ul>
