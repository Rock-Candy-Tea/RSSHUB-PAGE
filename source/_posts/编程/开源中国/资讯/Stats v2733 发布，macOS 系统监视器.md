                                                                                            <p><span style="color:#000000">Stats 是一款开源的 macOS 系统监控工具，可以驻留在菜单栏中对 CPU、GPU、内存、磁盘和网络等进行实时监测。目前 Stats </span><span style="color:#333333">v2.7.33</span><span style="color:#000000"> 发布了 ，此版本带来如下变更：</span></p> 
<h2><strong>已关闭的问题</strong></h2> 
<ul> 
 <li>特性：在网络图表中添加了一个选项， 可分离下载和上传的规模（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fexelban%2Fstats%2Fissues%2F966" target="_blank">#966</a>）</li> 
 <li>特性：添加 <code>Widget activation threshold option</code> 到网络模块（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fexelban%2Fstats%2Fissues%2F931" target="_blank">#931</a>）</li> 
</ul> 
<h2><strong>错误修复和新功能</strong></h2> 
<ul> 
 <li>修复：重启后，恢复网络图表中的比例</li> 
 <li>特性：启用图标内百分比时，禁用电池小部件着色</li> 
 <li>修复：调整网络图基线</li> 
</ul> 
<h2><strong>本地化</strong></h2> 
<ul> 
 <li>特性：添加了缺失的翻译（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fexelban%2Fstats%2Fissues%2F1061" target="_blank">#1061</a>）</li> 
 <li>特性：更新了简体中文翻译（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fexelban%2Fstats%2Fpull%2F1067" target="_blank">#1067</a>）</li> 
 <li>特性：更新繁体中文翻译（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fexelban%2Fstats%2Fpull%2F1066" target="_blank">#1066</a>）</li> 
</ul>
