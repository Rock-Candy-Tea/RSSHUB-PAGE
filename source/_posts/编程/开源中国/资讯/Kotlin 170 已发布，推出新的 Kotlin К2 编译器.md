                                                                                            <p>Kotlin 1.7.0 已经<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fblog.jetbrains.com%2Fkotlin%2F2022%2F06%2Fkotlin-1-7-0-released%2F" target="_blank">发布</a>啦。新版本推出了新的 Kotlin/JVM K2 编译器的 Alpha 版本，稳定了语言特性，并为 JVM、JS 和 Native 平台带来了性能改进。</p> 
<p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><span><span><span><span><span style="color:#27282c"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span style="background-color:#ffffff"><span><span><span>以下是此版本的主要更新：</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p> 
<ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fkotlinlang.org%2Fdocs%2Fwhatsnew17.html%23new-kotlin-k2-compiler-for-the-jvm-in-alpha" target="_blank">新的 Kotlin К2 编译器现在处于 Alpha 阶段</a>，它提供了显着的性能改进。它目前仅适用于 JVM，包括 kapt 在内的所有编译器插件都无法使用它。</li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fkotlinlang.org%2Fdocs%2Fwhatsnew17.html%23a-new-approach-to-incremental-compilation" target="_blank">Gradle 中增量编译的一种新方法</a>。增量编译现在也支持在依赖的非 Kotlin 模块中进行更改，并且与 Gradle 兼容。</li> 
 <li>已经稳定<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fkotlinlang.org%2Fdocs%2Fwhatsnew17.html%23stable-opt-in-requirements" target="_blank">了选择性加入需求注释</a>、<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fkotlinlang.org%2Fdocs%2Fwhatsnew17.html%23stable-definitely-non-nullable-types" target="_blank">绝对不可空的类型</a> 和 <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fkotlinlang.org%2Fdocs%2Fwhatsnew17.html%23stable-builder-inference" target="_blank">构建器推断</a>。</li> 
 <li>现在有一个<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fkotlinlang.org%2Fdocs%2Fwhatsnew17.html%23underscore-operator-for-type-arguments" target="_blank">用于 args 类型的下划线运算符</a>。可以使用它在指定其他类型时<strong>自动推断参数类型</strong>。</li> 
 <li>此版本<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fkotlinlang.org%2Fdocs%2Fwhatsnew17.html%23allow-implementation-by-delegation-to-an-inlined-value-of-an-inline-class" target="_blank">允许通过委托（delegation）实现内联类的内联值</a>。您现在可以创建在大多数情况下不分配内存的轻量级包装器。</li> 
</ul>
