                                                                                            <p>Taro 3.3.7 发布了。Taro 是一个开放式跨端跨框架解决方案，支持使用 React/Vue/Nerv 等框架来开发微信/京东/百度/支付宝/字节跳动/ QQ 小程序/H5 等应用。此版本更新内容包括：</p> 
<h4>修复</h4> 
<p><strong>小程序</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li> <p>修复字节小程序不支持 <code>onLongPress</code> 事件的问题，<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FNervJS%2Ftaro%2Fissues%2F10069" target="_blank">#10069</a></p> </li> 
 <li>补全字节小程序 <code>TextArea</code> 组件的属性，<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FNervJS%2Ftaro%2Fissues%2F10083" target="_blank">#10083</a></li> 
 <li>补全支付宝小程序 <code>Canvas</code> 组件的 <code>type</code> 和 <code>onReady</code> 属性，<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FNervJS%2Ftaro%2Fissues%2F9990" target="_blank">#9990</a></li> 
</ul> 
<p><strong>H5</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li> <p>修复 <code>taro-components-react</code> 包下 <code>swiper</code> 组件 <code>disconnect</code> 报错的问题</p> </li> 
</ul> 
<p><strong>RN</strong></p> 
<ul> 
 <li>修复 <code>Slider</code> 组件设置 <code>showValue</code> 时, 其 <code>value</code> 不随滑动而改变的问题</li> 
 <li>修复压缩图片时尺寸问题，并更新代码风格</li> 
</ul> 
<h4>Types</h4> 
<ul> 
 <li>补全 <code>Text</code> 组件的 <code>userSelect</code> 属性的类型，<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FNervJS%2Ftaro%2Fissues%2F10058" target="_blank">#10058</a></li> 
</ul>
