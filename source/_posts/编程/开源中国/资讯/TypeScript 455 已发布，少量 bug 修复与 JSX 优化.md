                                                                                            <p>TypeScript 4.5.5 发布了，这是一个维护版本，修复了社区报告的一些问题，以及对 JSX 自动补全功能进行了优化：</p> 
<ul> 
 <li>修复JSX 的结束标记 <code>></code> 中的快速信息查询崩溃问题 。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fmicrosoft%2FTypeScript%2Fissues%2F47446" target="_blank">#47446</a></li> 
 <li>修复 completionEntryDetails 崩溃问题。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fmicrosoft%2FTypeScript%2Fissues%2F47428" target="_blank">#47428</a></li> 
 <li>使用 strictPropertyInitialization 修复 JSDoc 中的代码崩溃问题。 <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fmicrosoft%2FTypeScript%2Fissues%2F47417" target="_blank">#47417</a></li> 
 <li>修复使用AMD + Classic + rootDirs 条目时，补全功能崩溃的问题。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fmicrosoft%2FTypeScript%2Fissues%2F47398" target="_blank">#47395</a></li> 
 <li>修复重复的无主体环境模块导致导航栏崩溃的问题。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fmicrosoft%2FTypeScript%2Fissues%2F47394" target="_blank">#47394</a></li> 
 <li>修复TS服务器错误：无法读取未定义的属性“标签”。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fmicrosoft%2FTypeScript%2Fissues%2F47299" target="_blank">#47299</a></li> 
 <li>修复 Jsx 属性补全和预期不符的问题。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fmicrosoft%2FTypeScript%2Fissues%2F47280" target="_blank">#47280</a></li> 
 <li>修复在 'navtree' 期间无法读取 'areSameModule' 中未定义的属性 'kind'  。 <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fmicrosoft%2FTypeScript%2Fissues%2F38058" target="_blank">#38058</a></li> 
 <li>修复一些调试失败问题。<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fmicrosoft%2FTypeScript%2Fissues%2F32860" target="_blank">#32860</a></li> 
</ul>
