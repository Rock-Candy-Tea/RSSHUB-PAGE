  <div style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
   <p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0">MJRefresh 是一个<span style="color:#4d4d4d">上下拉刷新效果控件，</span><span style="color:#222222">可以控制下拉刷新和上拉加载的文案的内容、文案是否显示，以及文案的颜色和字体。</span>MJRefresh 3.7.3 发布了，主要带来如下改变：</p> 
   <ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
    <li><span style="color:#24292f">修复初始化<span> </span></span><code>Trailer</code><span style="color:#24292f"><span> </span>后，设置<span> </span></span><code>MJRefreshStateIdle</code><span style="color:#24292f"><span> </span>的标题，不会立即生效的问题<span> </span></span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FCoderMJLee%2FMJRefresh%2Fissues%2F1533" target="_blank">#1533</a> </li> 
    <li><span style="color:#2e3033">删除 SPM 中不必要的定义<span> </span></span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FCoderMJLee%2FMJRefresh%2Fpull%2F1544" target="_blank">#1544</a> </li> 
    <li>修复<span> </span><span style="color:#2e3033">iOS15 的导航栏问题<span> </span></span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FCoderMJLee%2FMJRefresh%2Fpull%2F1553" target="_blank">#1553</a> </li> 
    <li>修复 iOS 15下使用 MJRefreshAutoFooter/MJRefreshBackFooter，结束刷新时可能会有一些不符合预期的动画效果<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FCoderMJLee%2FMJRefresh%2Fpull%2F1554" target="_blank">#1554</a> </li> 
    <li><span style="color:#2e3033">为框架和示例重新排序项目工作区<span> </span></span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FCoderMJLee%2FMJRefresh%2Fpull%2F1555" target="_blank">#1555</a> </li> 
    <li>修复了在设置 header 展示刷新文字的 block 后，第一次展示不会刷新文字的问题<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FCoderMJLee%2FMJRefresh%2Fpull%2F1404" target="_blank">#1404</a> </li> 
    <li><span style="color:#2e3033">修正了初始化函数的误用问题<span> </span></span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FCoderMJLee%2FMJRefresh%2Fpull%2F1558" target="_blank">#1558</a></li> 
   </ul>
