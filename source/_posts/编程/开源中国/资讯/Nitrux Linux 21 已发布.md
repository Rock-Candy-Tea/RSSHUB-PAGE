                                                                                            <p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start">Nitrux 是一个基于 Debian 的完整操作系统，拥有自己的一套 Nitrux 应用程序，建立在 Maui 套件和 Qt 上。</p> 
<p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start">Nitrux 2.1 已发布，该版本由<span> </span><strong>Nitrux 2.0<span> </span></strong>中引入的 XanMod 风格的 Linux 5.16 内核提供支持，该内核已更新到版本 5.16.16，同时增加了对最新的<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2F9to5linux.com%2Flinux-kernel-5-17-officially-released-this-is-whats-new" target="_blank"><strong>Linux 5.17</strong></a><strong><span> </span></strong>内核系列的支持。</p> 
<p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><strong>组件更新</strong></p> 
<p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start">更新了分发的以下组件：</p> 
<ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fkde.org%2Fannouncements%2Fplasma%2F5%2F5.24.4%2F" target="_blank">KDE Plasma 到 5.24.4 版</a>，<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fkde.org%2Fannouncements%2Fframeworks%2F5%2F5.91.0%2F" target="_blank">KDE Frameworks 到 5.92.0 版</a>，<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fkde.org%2Fannouncements%2Fgear%2F21.12.4%2F" target="_blank">KDE Gear 到 21.12.3 版</a>。</li> 
 <li>Firefox 到版本 98.0.2。</li> 
 <li>LibreOffice 到版本 7.3.1.3。</li> 
 <li>在应用程序菜单中添加了安装 Steam 的快捷方式，类似于安装 itch.io 的快捷方式。Steam 的安装过程将在后台进行。完成后，将出现一条通知。</li> 
 <li>更改了 ISO 文件的文件名以避免混淆。</li> 
 <li>添加了一个包含 Broadcom 43xx 设备固件的软件包。</li> 
 <li>添加了一个包含来自英特尔 SOF 设备的固件包。</li> 
 <li>添加了各种包，包括用于 iPhone 和 iPod Touch 设备的 ifuse FUSE 模块以及<span> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Flibimobiledevice.org%2F" target="_blank">libmobiledevice</a><span> </span>提供的其他软件。</li> 
</ul> 
<p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><strong>问题修复</strong></p> 
<ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
 <li>iPad 未被检测到 <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FNitrux%2Fnitrux-bug-tracker%2Fissues%2F56" target="_blank"><span> </span>#56</a> </li> 
 <li>在 Plasma 5.24 中不能配置 KWin 脚本 <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FNitrux%2Fnitrux-bug-tracker%2Fissues%2F53" target="_blank"><span> </span>#53</a></li> 
 <li>选择“擦除磁盘”时，Calamares 创建新分区失败<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FNitrux%2Fnitrux-bug-tracker%2Fissues%2F54" target="_blank"><span> </span>#54</a></li> 
 <li>选择“替换分区”时，Calamares 创建新分区失败<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FNitrux%2Fnitrux-bug-tracker%2Fissues%2F55" target="_blank"><span> </span>#55</a> </li> 
</ul>
