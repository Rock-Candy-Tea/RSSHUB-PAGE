 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FRediSearch%2FRediSearch%2Fpull%2F2736" target="_blank">#2736</a>，<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FRediSearch%2FRediSearch%2Fpull%2F2782" target="_blank">#2782</a><span> </span>向量相似度索引的内存分配限制（导致 OOM）（MOD-3195）</li>
