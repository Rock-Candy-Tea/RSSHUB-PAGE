                                                                                            <p><span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#000000">Rust 团队发布了 Rust 的新版本 1.62.1，该版本解决了编译器和标准库中最近的一些回归问题，并且还缓解了 Intel SGX 上的 CPU 漏洞。</span></p> 
<p><span style="background-color:#ffffff; color:#000000">具体更新内容包括：</span></p> 
<ul> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Frust-lang%2Frust%2Fissues%2F98608" target="_blank">编译器修复了涉及<code>impl Trait</code>返回类型的不健全的函数强制。</a></li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Frust-lang%2Frust%2Fissues%2F98890" target="_blank">编译器修复了一个关于<code>async fn</code>lifetimes 的增量编译错误。</a></li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Frust-lang%2Frust%2Fpull%2F98950" target="_blank">Windows 为同步读写中的重叠 I/O 添加了回退。</a></li> 
 <li><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Frust-lang%2Frust%2Fpull%2F98126" target="_blank"><code>x86_64-fortanix-unknown-sgx</code>target 添加了针对 MMIO 陈旧数据漏洞的缓解措施，即</a> <a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.intel.com%2Fcontent%2Fwww%2Fus%2Fen%2Fsecurity-center%2Fadvisory%2Fintel-sa-00615.html" target="_blank">INTEL-SA-00615</a>。</li> 
</ul> 
<p>详情可查看：<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fblog.rust-lang.org%2F2022%2F07%2F19%2FRust-1.62.1.html" target="_blank">https://blog.rust-lang.org/2022/07/19/Rust-1.62.1.html</a></p>
