                                                        <p>Micronaut 是 Grails 框架作者打造的开源项目，也是新一代基于 JVM 的全栈微服务框架，用于构建模块化的、易于测试的微服务应用。有关 Micronaut 的特性介绍<a href="https://www.oschina.net/news/96381/micronaut-open-sourced">点此查看</a>。</p> 
<p>近日，Micronaut 2.4.2 正式发布，本次更新内容如下：</p> 
<h3>变化：</h3> 
<ul> 
 <li>在调试 SSL 异常时记录没有请求状态；</li> 
 <li>修正带有连字符的数字处理；</li> 
 <li>将 micronaut-aws 升级至 2.4.2 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fmicronaut-projects%2Fmicronaut-core%2Fpull%2F5193" target="_blank">#5193</a>)；</li> 
 <li>将 micronaut-security 升级至 2.3.2 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fmicronaut-projects%2Fmicronaut-core%2Fpull%2F5188" target="_blank">#5188</a>)；</li> 
 <li>日志级别文档修复 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fmicronaut-projects%2Fmicronaut-core%2Fissues%2F5177" target="_blank">#5177</a>)；</li> 
 <li>修复注释元数据层次结构不传递默认值 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fmicronaut-projects%2Fmicronaut-core%2Fpull%2F5183" target="_blank">#5183</a>)；</li> 
 <li>将 micronaut-test 升级至 2.3.3 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fmicronaut-projects%2Fmicronaut-core%2Fpull%2F5182" target="_blank">#5182</a>)；</li> 
 <li>支持碎片化框架 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fmicronaut-projects%2Fmicronaut-core%2Fpull%2F5167" target="_blank">#5167</a>)；</li> 
 <li>javadoc: micronaut.config-client.enabled (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fmicronaut-projects%2Fmicronaut-core%2Fpull%2F5178" target="_blank">#5178</a>)；</li> 
 <li>当 snake_case 和 bean 自省同时启用时，修复 JsonView 过滤 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fmicronaut-projects%2Fmicronaut-core%2Fpull%2F5170" target="_blank">#5170</a>)；</li> 
 <li>将 micronaut-redis 升级至 4.0.1 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fmicronaut-projects%2Fmicronaut-core%2Fpull%2F5163" target="_blank">#5163</a>)；</li> 
 <li>将 micronaut-jackson-xml 升级至 2.3.0 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fmicronaut-projects%2Fmicronaut-core%2Fpull%2F5162" target="_blank">#5162</a>)；</li> 
</ul> 
<p>更多详情可查看：<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fmicronaut-projects%2Fmicronaut-core%2Freleases%2Ftag%2Fv2.4.2" target="_blank">https://github.com/micronaut-projects/micronaut-core/releases/tag/v2.4.2</a></p>
