<h3 style="margin-left:.6em; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><strong>Bug修复</strong></h3> 
<ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
 <li>重构以替换不推荐使用的<span> </span><code>String.prototype.substr()</code><span> </span>(<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fiamkun%2Fdayjs%2Fissues%2F1836" target="_blank">#1836</a>) </li> 
 <li>添加孟加拉语 [bn-bd] 语言环境（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fiamkun%2Fdayjs%2Fissues%2F1806" target="_blank">#1806</a>）</li> 
 <li>更新德语 [de] 语言环境：添加月份的缩写（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fiamkun%2Fdayjs%2Fissues%2F1831" target="_blank">#1831</a>）</li> 
 <li>更新意大利语 [it-ch]： 时区 relativeTime (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fiamkun%2Fdayjs%2Fissues%2F1829" target="_blank">#1829</a>) </li> 
 <li>更新库尔德语言环境 [ku] ：字符串和格式化输出包含非标准库尔德字符 (<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fiamkun%2Fdayjs%2Fissues%2F1848" target="_blank">#1848</a>) </li> 
 <li>更新藏语环境 [Tibetan]：更正了格式中的命令（<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fiamkun%2Fdayjs%2Fissues%2F1823" target="_blank">#1823</a>）</li> 
</ul>
