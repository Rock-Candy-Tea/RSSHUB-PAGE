<div class="content">
                                                                    
                                                        <p>Kali Linux 2021.2 稳定版已于上周<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.kali.org%2Fblog%2Fkali-linux-2021-2-release%2F" target="_blank">发布</a>。</p> 
<p>主要变化：</p> 
<ul> 
 <li><strong><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.kali.org%2Fblog%2Fkali-linux-2021-2-release%2F%23introducing-kaboxer-v10-again" target="_blank">Kaboxer v1.0</a></strong> - 引入 Kali Applications Boxer v1.0，用于容器的应用</li> 
 <li><strong><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.kali.org%2Fblog%2Fkali-linux-2021-2-release%2F%23releasing-kali-tweaks-v10" target="_blank">Kali-Tweaks v1.0</a></strong> - 让用户更轻松地根据自己的喜好配置 Kali Linux</li> 
 <li><strong><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.kali.org%2Fblog%2Fkali-linux-2021-2-release%2F%23refreshed-bleeding-edge-branc" target="_blank">更新 Bleeding-Edge 分支</a></strong> - 对后端进行了彻底改造，为最新的更新提供了安装包</li> 
 <li><strong><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.kali.org%2Fblog%2Fkali-linux-2021-2-release%2F%23disabled-privileged-ports" target="_blank">禁用特权端口</a></strong> - 在 1024/TCP-UDP 及以下端口打开侦听器不再需要超级用户访问权限</li> 
 <li><strong><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.kali.org%2Fblog%2Fkali-linux-2021-2-release%2F%23new-tools-in-kal" target="_blank">添加新工具</a></strong> - Ghidra & Visual Studio Code. Along with CloudBrute, Dirsearch, Feroxbuster, pacu, peirates, & Quark-Engine</li> 
 <li><strong><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.kali.org%2Fblog%2Fkali-linux-2021-2-release%2F%23theme-enhancement" target="_blank">增强主题</a></strong> - 添加了一种在双行和单行终端提示之间快速切换的方法，并对 Xfce4 快速启动 + 文件管理器进行了调整</li> 
 <li><strong><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.kali.org%2Fblog%2Fkali-linux-2021-2-release%2F%23desktop-wallpaper--login-background" target="_blank">桌面壁纸 & 登录背景更新</a></strong> - 默认图像已更改，提供了更多选择</li> 
</ul> 
<p><img alt src="https://oscimg.oschina.net/oscnet/up-df3d8c0d9b996dc4afe682427c5fabb383c.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p> 
<ul> 
 <li><strong><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.kali.org%2Fblog%2Fkali-linux-2021-2-release%2F%23raspberry-pi-recharged" target="_blank">Raspberry Pi images recharged</a></strong> - 现已完全支持 RPi 400，内置蓝牙工作，以及第一次运行等待时间大幅减少</li> 
 <li><strong><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.kali.org%2Fblog%2Fkali-linux-2021-2-release%2F%23kali-nethunter-updates" target="_blank">Kali NetHunter 支持 Android 11</a></strong> - Android 11 支持以及 NetHunter 平台的多项其他改进</li> 
 <li><strong><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.kali.org%2Fblog%2Fkali-linux-2021-2-release%2F%23more-docker-supportparallels-supportbug-fixes" target="_blank">更多的 Docker 支持</a></strong> - 支持 ARM64 和 ARM v7（以及之前的 AMD64）</li> 
 <li><strong><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.kali.org%2Fblog%2Fkali-linux-2021-2-release%2F%23more-docker-supportparallels-supportbug-fixes" target="_blank">支持 Parallels</a></strong> - 安装了 Parallels 的 Apple M1 完全支持运行 Kali Linux</li> 
 <li><strong><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.kali.org%2Fblog%2Fkali-linux-2021-2-release%2F%23more-docker-supportparallels-supportbug-fixes" target="_blank">多项 bug 修复</a></strong> - 修复了 Pkexec 补丁、Wireshark 权限、未找到命令行以及其他可访问性问题</li> 
</ul> 
<p><img alt src="https://oscimg.oschina.net/oscnet/up-5d0a8c917716447a07652eab422143bf52c.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p> 
<p>Kali Linux（以前叫做 BackTrack）是一份基于 Debian 的发行，它带有一套安全和计算机取证工具。其特色在于及时的安全更新，对 ARM 架构的支持，有四种流行的桌面环境供选择，以及能平滑升级到新版本。</p> 
<p>下载地址：<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.kali.org%2Fdownloads%2F" target="_blank">https://www.kali.org/downloads/</a><br> 发布公告：<a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.kali.org%2Fblog%2Fkali-linux-2021-2-release%2F" target="_blank">https://www.kali.org/blog/kali-linux-2021-2-release/</a></p>
