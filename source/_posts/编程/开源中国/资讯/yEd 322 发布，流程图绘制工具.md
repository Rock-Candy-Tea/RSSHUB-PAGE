                                                                                            <p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><span style="color:#000000">yEd 3.22 现已发布，该版本更新内容如下：</span></p> 
<h4 style="margin-left:.6em; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><span style="color:#000000"><strong>New Features</strong></span></h4> 
<ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
 <li><span style="color:#000000">添加了新的 Compact Disk 布局算法，将节点紧密地放在一起。</span></li> 
</ul> 
<h4 style="margin-left:.6em; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><span style="color:#000000"><strong>Improvements</strong></span></h4> 
<ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
 <li><span style="color:#000000">除了链、星、圆和平行子结构外，Organic layout 现在还支持组和树子结构。</span></li> 
 <li><span style="color:#000000">为圆形和径向布局算法添加了处理节点标签的新策略。</span></li> 
 <li><span style="color:#000000">为圆形和径向布局算法添加了新的 edge routing 选项。</span></li> 
 <li><span style="color:#000000">将节点类型作为附加优化标准添加到 Balloon 布局算法。相同类型的节点更有可能彼此相邻放置。</span></li> 
</ul> 
<h4 style="margin-left:.6em; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><span style="color:#000000"><strong>Bug 修复</strong></span></h4> 
<ul style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0"> 
 <li><span style="color:#000000">现在，使用 tab 键逐步浏览布局设置可以选择可编辑的值。</span><span style="color:#242265"> </span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=http%3A%2F%2Fyed.yworks.com%2Fsupport%2Fqa%2F23479" target="_blank">[Q&A 23479]</a></li> 
 <li><span style="color:#000000">许多大大小小的错误修正。</span></li> 
</ul> 
<p style="color:#000000; margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:start"><span style="color:#000000">更新说明：</span><a href="https://www.oschina.net/action/GoToLink?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.yworks.com%2Fproducts%2Fyed%2Fdownload%23ReleaseNotes" target="_blank">https://www.yworks.com/products/yed/download#ReleaseNotes</a></p>
