<p>第一次看到这个问题是在<strong>matrix67</strong>的博客上，标题为“<strong>44 个精彩的物理趣题</strong>”。</p>
<p>这篇文章至今仍然能搜索到。看了解题思路后，觉得自己应该一辈子都想不出来这个解答。</p>
<p>题目如下：</p>
<p><strong>考虑某颗星球，它由某种密度均匀的物质组成，其质量为 </strong></p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=M" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p><strong> ，体积为 </strong></p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p><strong> 。</strong></p>
<p><strong>如果这颗星球是一个球体，那么它的半径 </strong></p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=R%3D%5Csqrt%5B3%5D%7B3V%2F4%5Cpi%7D" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p><strong>，星球表面上的</strong></p>
<p><strong>重力加速度则为</strong></p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g_s%3DGM%2FR%5E2%3D2%5Crho+G%5Csqrt%5B3%5D%7B%7B2V%5Cpi%5E2%7D%2F%7B9%7D%7D" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>,<strong>其中 </strong></p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=G" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p><strong> 是万有引力常数。</strong></p>
<p><strong>考虑这颗星球所有可能的形状，怎样的形状才会让星球表面的某一点重力加速度达到最大？</strong></p>
<p><strong>最大值是多少？</strong></p>
<p>下图就是让表面某处的重力加速度达到最大的星球形状。这个图形是一个稍微有些变形的球体，整个图形是一个以</p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=z" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>方向为轴的旋转体，顶端的</p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>点即是重力加速度最大的点，它的重力加速度为</p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%3D2%5Crho+G%5Csqrt%5B3%5D%7B%7B6V%5Cpi%5E2%7D%2F%7B25%7D%7D" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>，只比球形星体的重力加速度大</p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2.6%5C%25" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>。这是又一个经典的例子——圆形似乎并不是那么完美。</p>
<figure><img class="content-image" src="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-8e699e597c2f62dad39a71d4c6a24c6a_720w.jpg?source=8673f162" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p>这个问题的解法非常漂亮。首先，假设我们想要让星体表面上的某个点</p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>(图中</p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=O" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>点) 的重力加速度最大，并且所受重力方向在</p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=z" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>轴上，那么这个星体必然是沿</p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=z" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>轴方向对称的。否则，取出不对称的一层，把多的部分填进少的部分让它变成一个完全对称的圆盘，这将会让</p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>点在竖直方向上的受力变大。不断这样做直到这个图形沿</p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=z" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>轴完全对称，显然就得到了一个更优的形状。</p>
<p>接下来的步骤就真的神了。现在，在星体上取一个非常细的圆环，假设它的质量是</p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=dM" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>。那么，这个圆环所贡献的重力加速度大小就是</p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=G%C2%B7dM%C2%B7%5Ccos%7B%CE%B8%7D+%2Fr%5E2" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>。如果把这个圆环从星体中挖掉，放到其它的位置上，那么新的圆环将会有新的</p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>值和</p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctheta" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>值。当整个形状达到最优时，这个形状将位于“极值点”的位置，也就是说它的“微分”为</p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>，任何微小的变动都不会改变</p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>的加速度。这就意味着，</p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=cos%CE%B8+%2F+r%5E2" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>是一个常数。这个条件就确定出整个星体的形状。</p>
<p>自然这个问题也可以用变分法来求解。</p>
<p>容易分析，这个星球应该是旋转对称体。体积微元可表示为</p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=dV%3D2%5Cpi+r%5E2%5Csin%5Ctheta+drd%5Ctheta" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>任一体积微元对选取点的重力加速度的贡献为</p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=dg%3D%5Cfrac%7BG%5Crho+dV+%5Ccos%5Ctheta%7D%7Br%5E2%7D%3D2%5Cpi+%5Crho+G+%5Csin%5Ctheta+%5Ccos%5Ctheta+drd%5Ctheta" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>那么这点的重力加速度可以写成积分形式</p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%3D2%5Cpi+%5Crho+G+%5Cint_0%5E%7B%5Cpi%2F2%7D+d%5Ctheta%5Cint_0%5E%7Br%28%5Ctheta%29%7D++%5Csin%5Ctheta+%5Ccos%5Ctheta+dr%3D2%5Cpi+%5Crho+G+%5Cint_0%5E%7B%5Cpi%2F2%7D+r%28%5Ctheta%29+%5Csin%5Ctheta+%5Ccos%5Ctheta+d%5Ctheta+" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>当然还要考虑它的质量约束条件。</p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=M%3D2%5Cpi+%5Crho++%5Cint_0%5E%7B%5Cpi%2F2%7D+d%5Ctheta%5Cint_0%5E%7Br%28%5Ctheta%29%7D++r%5E2%5Csin%5Ctheta++dr%3D%5Cfrac%7B2%5Cpi+%5Crho+%7D%7B3%7D+%5Cint_0%5E%7B%5Cpi%2F2%7D+r%28%5Ctheta%29%5E3%5Csin%5Ctheta+d%5Ctheta" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>定义</p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F%28r%2C%5Ctheta%29%3DGr%28%5Ctheta%29%5Csin%5Ctheta%5Ccos%5Ctheta" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>以及</p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarphi%28r%2C%5Ctheta%29%3D%7Br%28%5Ctheta%29%5E3%5Csin%5Ctheta%7D%2F%7B3%7D" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>考虑泛函</p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H%28r%2C%5Ctheta%29%3DF%28r%2C%5Ctheta%29%2B%5Clambda+%5Cvarphi%28r%2C%5Ctheta%29" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>，</p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>为一常数。</p>
<p>那么根据欧拉 - 拉格朗日方程</p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+H%7D%7B%5Cpartial+r%7D-%5Cfrac%7Bd%7D%7Bd%5Ctheta%7D%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+H%7D%7B%5Cpartial+r%27%7D%3D0" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>可以解得</p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r%3Dk%5Csqrt%7B%5Ccos%7B%5Ctheta%7D%7D" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>，</p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>为一正常数。</p>
<p>根据前述的质量条件，可以求出</p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%3D%5Csqrt%5B3%5D%7B%7B15M%7D%2F%7B4%5Crho+%5Cpi%7D%7D" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>那么重力加速度可以表示为</p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%3D2%5Crho+G%5Csqrt%5B3%5D%7B%5Cfrac%7B6V%5Cpi%5E2%7D%7B25%7D%7D" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>如果星球为球体的话，重力加速度为</p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g_%7Bs%7D%3D2%5Crho+G%5Csqrt%5B3%5D%7B%5Cfrac%7B2V%5Cpi%5E2%7D%7B9%7D%7D" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>两者之间相差</p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Bg-g_s%7D%7Bg_s%7D%3D%5Cfrac%7B3%7D%7B%5Csqrt%5B3%5D%7B25%7D%7D-1%5Capprox+2.6%5C%25" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>它的侧面轮廓是这个样子，怎么感觉像个馒头一样，难道馒头的烹制也蕴含着精妙的物理规律吗？</p>
