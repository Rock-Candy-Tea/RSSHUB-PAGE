<p>一日在那瑞文戴尔内，各大种族齐聚一堂，甘道夫与精灵王面露难色。</p>
<figure><img class="content-image" src="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-83a90c9cbb89280f5d8d9d23a309457c_720w.jpg?source=8673f162" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p>至尊魔戒重现江湖，索伦即将复兴，中土浩劫将至。</p>
<figure><img class="content-image" src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-595a40479dabc4b59627d71fa85f1efc_720w.jpg?source=8673f162" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p>唯有将其投入末日火山方可销毁，然而此去困难重重，九死一生，能否成功尚未可知。</p>
<figure><img class="content-image" src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-93bfaa057333daad5ac5f9ebd14f8ae4_720w.jpg?source=8673f162" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p>然而天空一声巨响，BOSS 闪亮登场，原来是在电影院看电影时不慎穿越进了电影中。</p>
<figure><img class="content-image" src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-bc15d1e5e0baf8b39bc7eee60760fff6_720w.jpg?source=8673f162" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p>BOSS 见眼前场景，便道不忙，即取来清水一杯，将魔戒放入。</p>
<figure><img class="content-image" src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-e47f7dc6e1d954fc11e1b431eeb4644e_720w.jpg?source=8673f162" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p>又取食盐一袋倒入水中搅拌均匀。</p>
<figure><img class="content-image" src="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-beb3234709c002f209e5e09263254f05_720w.jpg?source=8673f162" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p>紧接着寻来一块石墨板亦放入水中，取铜导线两根，分别连接魔戒与石墨板</p>
<figure><img class="content-image" src="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-86d5935aed42753be2e82dd631b312da_720w.jpg?source=8673f162" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p>当即唤来甘道夫，运起闪电魔法，此魔法加于两导线间，以石墨为阴极，魔戒为阳级，运起 24V 直流电。</p>
<figure><img class="content-image" src="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-fcdb41cafc402e83014bbc9ef3fddc32_720w.jpg?source=8673f162" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p>只见一盏茶功夫，那魔戒便消失不见。</p>
<figure><img class="content-image" src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-ff8f5188d76634f2c27f3739c69dc68d_720w.jpg?source=8673f162" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p>随着一声凄厉地呼喊。</p>
<figure><img class="content-image" src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-2c913603598da1077ac62d1c4cf6c1e1_720w.jpg?source=8673f162" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p>正义就这样战胜了邪恶</p>
<figure><img class="content-image" src="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-f87b62a8692d1210a6c7175371a5ef19_720w.jpg?source=8673f162" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p>抑或是寻来魔戒，将其四周以铜线环绕形成线圈。</p>
<figure><img class="content-image" src="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-05fde4d8f83db3a9dd314c739df391d8_720w.jpg?source=8673f162" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p>便唤来甘道夫，运起闪电魔法，此魔法加于铜线圈之上，电压为 220V，每秒钟方向变换 50 余次</p>
<figure><img class="content-image" src="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-046e5e27fcbff9e52fba875902de53b1_720w.jpg?source=8673f162" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p>只见刹那间，那魔戒放出红光，不多时火光崩裂，温度已不知高出那末日火山几倍，魔戒登时化作一滩铁水销毁殆尽。</p>
