<p>显然当你点开这个回答，说明做成不同形状吸引了你的关注。</p>
<p>具体原因摘自 2018 年报道的加拿大麦当劳公司对该问题的回复：</p>
<blockquote><em>The 4 shapes we make Chicken McNuggets in was the perfect equilibrium of dipability and fun. 3 would’ve been too few. 5 would’ve been, like, wacky. For now, our McNugget shapes are: the ‘ball,’ the ‘boot,’ the ‘bow-tie’ and the ‘bell.’ We also make them similar in size to ensure consistent cooking times for food safety.</em></blockquote>
<p>4 种形状的麦鸡块完美平衡了鸡块的可蘸性和趣味性。至于为什么是 4 种形状，因为 3 种就太少了。5 种有点古怪。目前，我们的麦乐鸡块的形状是：球、靴子、领结和铃铛。我们还使它们的尺寸相似，以确保一致的烹饪时间，确保食品安全。</p>
<figure><img class="content-image" src="https://pica.zhimg.com/v2-a9e76282e323421dff49a99c1b977cdd_720w.jpg?source=8673f162" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure>
<p>在裹上面糊后，鸡块的形状并不明显。鸡块在制作时首先被压成等宽的饼干大小，形状更接近他们所描述的样子。</p>
<figure><img class="content-image" src="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-bdbc9b6c4bfc88ae3bc823335d81e267_720w.jpg?source=8673f162" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure>
<p>这些形状是为孩子设计。</p>
