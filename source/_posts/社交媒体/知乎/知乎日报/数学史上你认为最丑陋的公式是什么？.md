<p><strong><strong>波尔文积分（Borwein integral）</strong></strong></p>
<p>此特殊积分由波尔文父子（David Borwein & Jonathan Borwein）于 2001 年首次提出，用于举例说明【<strong>看似成立的数学规律会在某个时刻突然失效】</strong>。</p>
<p>这是一个涉及 sinc 函数的积分，常见的例子为：</p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint_%7B0%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%5Cfrac%7Bsin%28x%29%7D%7Bx%7Ddx%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%7D%7B2%7D" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint_%7B0%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%5Cfrac%7Bsin%28x%29%7D%7Bx%7D%5Cfrac%7Bsin%28x%2F3%29%7D%7Bx%2F3%7Ddx%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%7D%7B2%7D" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint_%7B0%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%5Cfrac%7Bsin%28x%29%7D%7Bx%7D%5Cfrac%7Bsin%28x%2F3%29%7D%7Bx%2F3%7D%5Cfrac%7Bsin%28x%2F5%29%7D%7Bx%2F5%7Ddx%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%7D%7B2%7D" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint_%7B0%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%5Cfrac%7Bsin%28x%29%7D%7Bx%7D%5Cfrac%7Bsin%28x%2F3%29%7D%7Bx%2F3%7D%5Cfrac%7Bsin%28x%2F5%29%7D%7Bx%2F5%7D%5Cfrac%7Bsin%28x%2F7%29%7D%7Bx%2F7%7Ddx%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%7D%7B2%7D" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>此规律一直到：</p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint_%7B0%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%5Cfrac%7Bsin%28x%29%7D%7Bx%7D%5Cfrac%7Bsin%28x%2F3%29%7D%7Bx%2F3%7D...%5Cfrac%7Bsin%28x%2F13%29%7D%7Bx%2F13%7Ddx%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%7D%7B2%7D" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>依然成立。</p>
<p>好了，算到这里你是不是会想：<strong>哦，多棒的规律啊！</strong>然后得意洋洋地开始测试下一个数。</p>
<p>然而，打脸来得就是这么猝不及防：</p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint_%7B0%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%5Cfrac%7Bsin%28x%29%7D%7Bx%7D%5Cfrac%7Bsin%28x%2F3%29%7D%7Bx%2F3%7D...%5Cfrac%7Bsin%28x%2F15%29%7D%7Bx%2F15%7Ddx%3D%5Cfrac%7B467807924713440738696537864469%7D%7B935615849440640907310521750000%7D%5Cpi" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>

<p><strong>烂，烂尾了……</strong></p>
<p><strong>令人头大。</strong></p>

<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint+_%7B0%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty+%7D%5Cprod+_%7Bk%3D0%7D%5E%7Bn%7D%7B%5Cfrac+%7B%5Csin%28a_%7Bk%7Dx%29%7D%7Ba_%7Bk%7Dx%7D%7D%5C%2Cdx%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%7D%7B2a_%7B0%7D%7D%C2%B7%7B%5Cfrac+%7B1%7D%7B2%5E%7Bn%7Dn%21%5Cprod+_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5E%7Bn%7Da_%7Bk%7D%7D%7D%5Csum+_%7B%5Cgamma+%5Cin+%5C%7B%5Cpm+1%5C%7D%5E%7Bn%7D%7D%5Cvarepsilon+_%7B%5Cgamma+%7Db_%7B%5Cgamma+%7D%5E%7Bn%7D%5Coperatorname+%7Bsgn%7D%28b_%7B%5Cgamma+%7D%29%2C+a_%7Bk%7D%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2k%2B1%7D%EF%BC%8C" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cgamma+%3D%28%5Cgamma+_%7B1%7D%2C%5Cgamma+_%7B2%7D%2C%5Cldots+%2C%5Cgamma+_%7Bn%7D%29%5Cin+%5C%7B%5Cpm+1%5C%7D%5E%7Bn%7D%2Cb_%7B%5Cgamma+%7D%3Da_%7B0%7D%2B%5Cgamma+_%7B1%7Da_%7B1%7D%2B%5Cgamma+_%7B2%7Da_%7B2%7D%2B%5Ccdots+%2B%5Cgamma+_%7Bn%7Da_%7Bn%7D%2C%5Cvarepsilon+_%7B%5Cgamma+%7D%3D%5Cgamma+_%7B1%7D%5Cgamma+_%7B2%7D%5Ccdots+%5Cgamma+_%7Bn%7D" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>从仅有</p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_%7Bk%7D" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>一个参数，硬生生地加到</p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_%7Bk%7D%2C%5Cgamma%2Cb_%7B%5Cgamma+%7D%2C%5Cvarepsilon+_%7B%5Cgamma+%7D" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>四个参数，运算有幂、阶乘、累乘、累和，甚至还包含符号函数 sgn，实在是……</p>
<p><strong>复杂又丑陋。</strong></p>
<p>哦对了，如果你增加附加因子</p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2cos%28x%29" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>，那么这个序列一直坚持到 k=55，即：</p>
<p><img class="content-image" src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint_%7B0%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D2cos%28x%29%5Cfrac%7Bsin%28x%29%7D%7Bx%7D%5Cfrac%7Bsin%28x%2F3%29%7D%7Bx%2F3%7D...%5Cfrac%7Bsin%28x%2F111%29%7D%7Bx%2F111%7Ddx%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%7D%7B2%7D" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>

<p><strong>直觉和经验，其实并不总是那么可靠。</strong></p>
