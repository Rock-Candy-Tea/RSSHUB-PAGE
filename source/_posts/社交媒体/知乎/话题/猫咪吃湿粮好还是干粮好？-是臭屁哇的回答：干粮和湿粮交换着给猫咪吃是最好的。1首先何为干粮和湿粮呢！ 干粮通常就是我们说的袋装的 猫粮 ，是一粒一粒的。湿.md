<strong>猫咪吃湿粮好还是干粮好？</strong><br>是臭屁哇的回答<br><h2><b>干粮和湿粮交换着给猫咪吃是最好的。</b></h2><h3>1.首先何为干粮和湿粮呢！</h3><p data-pid="YEPHkmc3">　　干粮通常就是我们说的袋装的<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.so.com/s%3Fq%3D%25E7%258C%25AB%25E7%25B2%25AE%26ie%3Dutf-8%26src%3Dinternal_wenda_recommend_textn" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">猫粮</a>，是一粒一粒的。湿粮，也就是通常说的<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.so.com/s%3Fq%3D%25E7%25BD%2590%25E5%25A4%25B4%26ie%3Dutf-8%26src%3Dinternal_wenda_recommend_textn" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">罐头</a>和妙鲜包以及自己做的饭之类的啦！不过这里讨论的仅仅是罐头哦！</p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-a073ad7e1000bbd08178509c9c08ba32_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="268" data-rawheight="268" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-96cbc91f9b24fb2a9ac9b8ea5d63a060_b.jpg" class="content_image" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><h3>2.干粮有什么优势呢？</h3><p data-pid="gTOWskjg"><b>　　1）价格便宜</b></p><p data-pid="dSbO1Mpd">　　通个品牌的罐头和<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.so.com/s%3Fq%3D%25E5%2590%258C%25E9%2587%258F%26ie%3Dutf-8%26src%3Dinternal_wenda_recommend_textn" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">同量</a>的干粮，干粮的价格要便宜一半！</p><p data-pid="OILop1kf"><b>　2）便于保存和清理</b></p><p data-pid="RA0j2meW">　　干粮通常保质期内不用太担心变质问题，而且即使洒到地板和地毯上也容易清理！至于湿粮就不用说了吧！</p><p data-pid="cl1gO2wa"><b>　3）可以锻炼猫咪的牙齿</b></p><p data-pid="jLz6S0PA">　　干粮是固体食物，正常在吃的同时也可以<b>锻炼猫咪的牙齿</b>，<b>不过这个效果不是太明显！</b></p><p data-pid="q-3zzZLb">猫咪长期吃干粮可能带来的主要问题就是脱水，因为干粮的含水量只有10%。<b>所以长期吃干粮的猫咪可能会慢性脱水，甚至可能会导致肾衰竭和尿结石，</b>所以在<b>喂食干粮时一定要保证猫咪充足的饮水</b>。</p><h3>3.然后湿粮有哪些优势呢？</h3><p data-pid="Thc6y5_0"><b>　1）含水分高</b></p><p data-pid="zzonUgOJ">　　在湿粮中含有较高的水分，对于那些不爱喝水的猫咪很管用，另外对于肠胃肾脏不好的猫咪吃干粮会造成病情的加重！而罐头中的水分有利于猫咪排尿。</p><p data-pid="Ik7Tqqs9"><b>　2）含有碳水化合物和<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.so.com/s%3Fq%3D%25E8%25B0%25B7%25E7%2589%25A9%26ie%3Dutf-8%26src%3Dinternal_wenda_recommend_textn" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">谷物</a>少</b></p><p data-pid="PSBLvFuz">　　猫咪作为<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.so.com/s%3Fq%3D%25E9%25A3%259F%25E8%2582%2589%25E5%258A%25A8%25E7%2589%25A9%26ie%3Dutf-8%26src%3Dinternal_wenda_recommend_textn" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">食肉动物</a>，基本是不需要补充碳水化合物和谷物的，而湿粮中比干粮中含有的这两样东西少的多。便宜的干粮中更是含有大量的这些成分，这个让猫咪更本补充不了需要的营养。</p><p data-pid="cmMWEiX7"><b>3）成分和口感比较好</b></p><p data-pid="JbcpI9SI">　　如果同时放干粮和湿粮，相信很多猫咪会毫无疑问的选择湿粮吧！因为湿粮的<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.so.com/s%3Fq%3D%25E5%2591%25B3%25E9%2581%2593%26ie%3Dutf-8%26src%3Dinternal_wenda_recommend_textn" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">味道</a>更鲜更香，并且成分也最贴近<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.so.com/s%3Fq%3D%25E5%25A4%25A7%25E8%2587%25AA%25E7%2584%25B6%26ie%3Dutf-8%26src%3Dinternal_wenda_recommend_textn" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">大自然</a>，基本罐头都是用鲜肉做的，可以满足猫咪所需要的营养。</p><p data-pid="-b4fVnLa"><b>而长期吃湿粮则有可能会导致猫咪更容易得口腔疾病和养成挑食的习惯。所以最好的办法就是交换着给猫咪喂食干粮和湿粮。</b><br></p><p
