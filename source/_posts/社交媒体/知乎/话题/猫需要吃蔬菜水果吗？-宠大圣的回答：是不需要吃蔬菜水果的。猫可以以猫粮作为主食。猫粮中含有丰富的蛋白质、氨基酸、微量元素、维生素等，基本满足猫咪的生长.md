<strong>猫需要吃蔬菜水果吗？</strong><br>宠大圣的回答<br><p>是不需要吃蔬菜水果的。猫可以以猫粮作为主食。猫粮中含有丰富的蛋白质、氨基酸、微量元素、维生素等，基本满足猫咪的生长需求；并且猫咪是肉食动物，猫咪对于蔬菜和水果等素食是不喜欢的。平时可以给猫咪一周吃3-4次零食，比如罐头、鸡胸肉、鸡蛋黄、营养膏等。</p><p><br></p><p><strong>9.9元定量速抢区：</strong> <strong><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//detail.tmall.com/item.htm%3Fspm%3Da1z10.1-b-s.w4004-21093901541.30.71ab440emFodRK%26pvid%3D4a4000cc-97d9-4895-b244-774ddbc408f4%26pos%3D15%26acm%3D03068.1003.1.702815%26id%3D626960711983%26scm%3D1007.12941.156882.100200300000000%26sku_properties%3D10723838%3A106826" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">猫条</a></strong></p><p><strong>营养猫粮推荐：<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//detail.tmall.com/item.htm%3Fspm%3Da1z10.1-b-s.w4004-21093901541.1.4a03440ew1J2bp%26pvid%3D719d7645-2e24-48ee-bb03-abed8d07a503%26pos%3D1%26acm%3D03068.1003.1.702815%26id%3D582309109556%26scm%3D1007.12941.156882.100200300000000" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">宠大圣经典系列</a></strong></p><p><strong>天猫/淘宝：<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//petwukong.tmall.com/" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">宠大圣旗舰店</a></strong></p>
