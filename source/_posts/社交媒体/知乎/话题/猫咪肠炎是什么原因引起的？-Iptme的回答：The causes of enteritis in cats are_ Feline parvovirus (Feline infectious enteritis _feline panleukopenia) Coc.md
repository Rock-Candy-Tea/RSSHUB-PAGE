<strong>猫咪肠炎是什么原因引起的？</strong><br>Iptme的回答<br><p data-pid="w4e8FpAz">The causes of enteritis in cats are:</p><p data-pid="ccexMlpQ">Feline parvovirus (Feline infectious enteritis /feline panleukopenia)</p><p data-pid="yTv-YpuK">Coccidia (<i>Isospora spp.</i>)</p><p data-pid="GVwOI5gX">Protozoa (<i>Giardia spp.</i>, <i>Tritrichomonas fetus, Toxoplasma gondii</i> or <i>Cryptosporidium parvum)</i></p><p data-pid="VWrlbwmZ">Parasitic worms (<i>Toxocara cati, Toxascaris leonina, Dipylidium caninum, Ancylostoma tubaeformae</i>)</p><p data-pid="QKrbKukW">Enteropathogenic bacteria (<i>Campylobacter jejuni, Salmonella, Escherichia coli and Clostridium</i>)</p><p data-pid="SqMfxClU">Inflammatory bowel disease (IBD)</p><p data-pid="r3IvO4lN">Food hypersensitivity (skin signs with itching and external otitis)</p><p data-pid="7EXXCtdd">Plant poisoning </p><p data-pid="X80iSqs0">Feline coronavirus (mild self-limiting diarrhea in kittens).</p>
