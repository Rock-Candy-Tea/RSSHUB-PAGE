<strong>猫菊酯中毒了，怎么办？</strong><br>从从猫-在线咨询的回答<br><p data-pid="94N89a1b"><b>驱蚊产品的有效成分都是菊酯，通常低于0.65%，毒性较低，人几乎无害，猫咪虽然也是哺乳动物，但是肝脏中没有这种溶解酶。呼吸进去的毒性全部留存，慢慢积累，排不出去。</b></p><p data-pid="OTeM96Md"><b>一、中毒表现：</b></p><p data-pid="7b0s7vf6">1.轻者<b>流口水</b>，严重者吐泡沫</p><p data-pid="UXuOyA9A">2.走路不稳</p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-8a36017b9668ceb9763ecc35ce6590dc_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1280" data-rawheight="853" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-f58e16894b1fcc8cf3679497fc9db1fc_b.jpg" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-8a36017b9668ceb9763ecc35ce6590dc_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p data-pid="bWcKFIFp">3.高于平常兴奋</p><p data-pid="yJmJ3iQ0">4.呕吐</p><p data-pid="QmQTvbDs">5.食欲不佳</p><p data-pid="pC24sdOS"><b>二、解决办法</b></p><p data-pid="YADd8zMg">1.多喝绿豆水，立即催吐以防止吸收。</p><p data-pid="9X4-JaJn">2.需要在医院进行洗胃,以加速从体内清除毒物。</p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-2bcc4ebf1cb72e4d8b9cace390e6354f_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1280" data-rawheight="853" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-73337cd9790022a9066932a8e15cbc65_b.jpg" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-2bcc4ebf1cb72e4d8b9cace390e6354f_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p data-pid="yn7z0G_p"><b>三、那些东西会让猫中毒？</b></p><p data-pid="6EqNzIgr"><b>杜鹃花、夹竹桃、百合花、万年青、水仙花、茉莉花、牵牛花、郁金香、牡丹、洋葱、巧克力、猫咪喝到有消毒剂或清洁剂的脏水等。</b></p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-f8c75ae7cb284a1fefe0f442ae4580b7_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1070" data-rawheight="1280" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-dabc728583e7d6ead6c34bb6e97c3bd9_b.jpg" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-f8c75ae7cb284a1fefe0f442ae4580b7_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p></p>
