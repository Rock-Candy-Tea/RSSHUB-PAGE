<strong>怎么给猫清理耳朵，多久清理一次？</strong><br>牙臣的回答<br><p data-pid="9bzAaXrl">如果是耳螨，直接上大宠爱，不用在耳朵上药，很快就好了。</p><p data-pid="WzV-bnKp">如果是某某菌感染，必须要滴药的，如下图所示操作。单手用虎口托住猫下巴，手指抓住猫脑袋两侧，中指按住猫耳朵尖以露出耳道口。猫很享受这个被握住下巴的姿势，基本不会挣扎。</p><p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-a591c595923c42d7edb6b7f6eab9c4f8_1440w.jpg" data-rawwidth="3456" data-rawheight="4608" data-size="normal" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-52f3dc819073c311920289791f32db9a_b.jpg" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-a591c595923c42d7edb6b7f6eab9c4f8_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p><p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-1d6678d58a28e2415de017b7b696183f_1440w.jpg" data-rawwidth="2736" data-rawheight="3648" data-size="normal" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-029b89915d63ce60587a761e60a3b3e3_b.jpg" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-1d6678d58a28e2415de017b7b696183f_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p><p data-pid="zFwN47YX">你看，在手指松开的情况下，猫也乖乖的把下巴放在虎口上挨着，它享受这种姿势。用这个手势给猫耳朵上药，单人就可以完成。</p>
