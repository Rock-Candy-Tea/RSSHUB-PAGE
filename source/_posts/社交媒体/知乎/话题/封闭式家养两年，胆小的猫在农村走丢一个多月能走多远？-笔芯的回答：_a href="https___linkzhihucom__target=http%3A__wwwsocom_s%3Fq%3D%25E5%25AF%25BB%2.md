<strong>封闭式家养两年，胆小的猫在农村走丢一个多月能走多远？</strong><br>笔芯的回答<br><p><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.so.com/s%3Fq%3D%25E5%25AF%25BB%25E7%258C%25AB%26ie%3Dutf-8%26src%3Dinternal_wenda_recommend_textn" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">寻猫</a>的时间分为3个阶段 <br>第一阶段： 猫丢失的前3天，也就是最开始的72小时可以说是找猫的黄金时间，这时猫一般就在家<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.so.com/s%3Fq%3D%25E9%2599%2584%25E8%25BF%2591%26ie%3Dutf-8%26src%3Dinternal_wenda_recommend_textn" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">附近</a>，甚至近在咫尺（例如楼道里），所以应该抓紧时间密集寻找，找回的可能最大。 <br>第二阶段： 丢失后的前7－10天，仍然是找回猫至关重要的时间。猫在很大程度上仍然在附近，不会走远。特别是附近有<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.so.com/s%3Fq%3D%25E6%25B5%2581%25E6%25B5%25AA%25E7%258C%25AB%26ie%3Dutf-8%26src%3Dinternal_wenda_recommend_textn" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">流浪猫</a>喂食点或者聚集的地方。猫的<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.so.com/s%3Fq%3D%25E5%25A4%25A9%25E6%2580%25A7%26ie%3Dutf-8%26src%3Dinternal_wenda_recommend_textn" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">天性</a>很容易把他引向同类聚集的地方，即便他从没去过。 <br>这里所说的“附近” ，一般情况下，对绝育的猫来说是方圆400m－1km/2km内。当然这个只是供参考的数据，具体还要看周围的环境，<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.so.com/s%3Fq%3D%25E5%25B0%258F%25E5%258C%25BA%26ie%3Dutf-8%26src%3Dinternal_wenda_recommend_textn" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">小区</a>是不是封闭等等也会有影响 <br>第三阶段： 7或者<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.so.com/s%3Fq%3D10%25E5%25A4%25A9%25E4%25BB%25A5%25E5%2590%258E%26ie%3Dutf-8%26src%3Dinternal_wenda_recommend_textn" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">10天以后</a>，如果<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.so.com/s%3Fq%3D%25E7%258C%25AB%25E7%258C%25AB%26ie%3Dutf-8%26src%3Dinternal_wenda_recommend_textn" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">猫猫</a>在附近找不到吃喝的地方，可能就会选择去更远的地方，因此找回的概率减低，但是并不是没有可能，很多例子都是走丢<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.so.com/s%3Fq%3D%25E4%25BA%2586%25E4%25B8%2580%26ie%3Dutf-8%26src%3Dinternal_wenda_recommend_textn" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">了一</a><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.so.com/s%3Fq%3D%25E4%25B8%25A4%25E4%25B8%25AA%25E6%259C%2588%26ie%3Dutf-8%26src%3Dinternal_wenda_recommend_textn" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">两个月</a>找回来的，所以这些数字只能作为参考 <br>2 平时住<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.so.com/s%3Fq%3D%25E5%259C%25A8%25E5%25AE%25B6%25E9%2587%258C%26ie%3Dutf-8%26src%3Dinternal_wenda_recommend_textn" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">在家里</a>的猫，因为不出门，所以即便是一个住了很久的地方，可能一旦出去也不认识了。特别是现在的<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.so.com/s%3Fq%3D%25E6%25A5%25BC%25E6%2588%25BF%26ie%3Dutf-8%26src%3Dinternal_wenda_recommend_textn" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">楼房</a>和单元都很近似。所以如果不是一直经常出门的 猫，其实并不存在是否对<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.so.com/s%3Fq%3D%25E5%25AE%25B6%25E9%2597%25A8%26ie%3Dutf-8%26src%3Dinternal_wenda_recommend_textn" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">家门</a>认识的问题 <br>如果你住在一楼，可以把他用过的猫砂（有他的气味）撒一些在周围，特别是<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.so.com/s%3Fq%3D%25E7%25AA%2597%25E6%2588%25B7%26ie%3Dutf-8%26src%3Dinternal_wenda_recommend_textn" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">窗户</a>下什么的可以多撒一些，注意不要让清洁工直接扫走了。 <br>还有他的饭碗水盆，也可以放<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.so.com/s%3Fq%3D%25E7%25AA%2597%25E5%258F%25B0%26ie%3Dutf-8%26src%3Dinternal_wenda_recommend_textn" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">窗台</a>。总之是有他的气味的，例如他尿过的垫子，或是他熟悉的气味，例如他特别喜欢的吃的，<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.so.com/s%3Fq%3D%25E7%25BD%2590%25E5%25A4%25B4%26ie%3Dutf-8%26src%3Dinternal_wenda_recommend_textn" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">罐头</a>之类 <br>4. 猫在走失后，基本有两种不同的反应：一种是害怕但是仍然认识<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.so.com/s%3Fq%3D%25E4%25B8%25BB%25E4%25BA%25BA%26ie%3Dutf-8%26src%3Dinternal_wenda_recommend_textn" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">主人</a>，听到主人的<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.so.com/s%3Fq%3D%25E5%258F%25AB%25E5%25A3%25B0%26ie%3Dutf-8%26src%3Dinternal_wenda_recommend_textn" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">叫声</a>会出来。 这样的话比较幸运。第二种是吓得已经不知道谁是谁了，见到谁都跑。这样就要有耐心。千万不能因为自己急迫的心情把猫吓得跑更远了 <br>不管是哪种情况，猫在<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.so.com/s%3Fq%3D%25E5%25A4%2596%25E9%259D%25A2%26ie%3Dutf-8%26src%3Dinternal_wenda_recommend_textn" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">外面</a>陌生的环境里都是很惊恐的，一旦见到了，切忌惊喜大叫。一定要尽可能平静，就像平时在家一样慢慢接近！ <br>随身带猫包<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.so.com/s%3Fq%3D%25E7%25AC%25BC%25E5%25AD%2590%26ie%3Dutf-8%26src%3Dinternal_wenda_recommend_textn" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">笼子</a>最好，因为一旦抓住猫常会发疯挣扎，千万不能在这个时候松脱！ <br>或者用<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.so.com/s%3Fq%3D%25E8%25A2%25AB%25E5%25AD%2590%26ie%3Dutf-8%26src%3Dinternal_wenda_recommend_textn" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">被子</a><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.so.com/s%3Fq%3D%25E6%25AF%25AF%25E5%25AD%2590%26ie%3Dutf-8%26src%3Dinternal_wenda_recommend_textn" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">毯子</a>一下蒙上。 <br><br><br><br><br><br><br><br><br><br><br></p><p>5. 找猫的时间，除了推荐夜深人静。晚上10点以后。还可以根据自己<a
