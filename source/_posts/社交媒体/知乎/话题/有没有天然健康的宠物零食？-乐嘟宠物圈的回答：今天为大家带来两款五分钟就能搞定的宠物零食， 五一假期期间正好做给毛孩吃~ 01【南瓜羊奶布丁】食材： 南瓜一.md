data-pid="WmtMwWl4">食材：</p><ol><li data-pid="4KzS-s0i">南瓜一小块</li><li data-pid="q8CXMB13">羊奶一袋（宠物羊奶粉也可以）</li><li data-pid="x0o34dmt">吉利丁片2片</li></ol><figure data-size="normal"><img data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-08937785eeb9e11e5a5b271d13de38e3_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="585" data-rawheight="439" class="origin_image
