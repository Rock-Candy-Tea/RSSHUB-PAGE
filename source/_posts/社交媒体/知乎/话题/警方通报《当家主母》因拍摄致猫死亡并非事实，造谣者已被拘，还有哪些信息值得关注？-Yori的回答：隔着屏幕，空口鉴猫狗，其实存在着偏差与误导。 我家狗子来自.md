data-pid="6H2_Vl_J">在犬舍时，日常照片是这样的：</p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-e88e82c675ef2025730faaa5f27c8229_1440w.jpg" data-rawwidth="1198" data-rawheight="1600"
