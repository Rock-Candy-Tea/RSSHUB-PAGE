<strong>狗狗从小就经常流眼泪，怎么办？</strong><br>宠大圣的回答<br><p>狗狗流泪的原因有很多，比如，狗狗的鼻泪管堵塞或者狭窄导致其流眼泪；</p><p>狗狗眼部受到异物刺激而流泪，如香水、沙子、毛发、灰尘等；</p><p>狗狗患有角膜炎、结膜炎等疾病引起的流泪；</p><p>狗狗因饮食不当而上火，从而引发流眼泪；</p><p>狗狗因患有犬瘟热、感冒、耳部疾病而引起眼部流泪。</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p><strong>9.9元定量速抢区</strong>：<strong><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//detail.tmall.com/item.htm%3Fspm%3Da1z10.1-b-s.w4004-21093901541.29.2045440ewSmEaz%26pvid%3Dfe3d6b53-0368-4f09-b28b-c9f4e7adb81e%26pos%3D15%26acm%3D03068.1003.1.702815%26id%3D626960711983%26scm%3D1007.12941.156882.100200300000000%26sku_properties%3D10723838%3A106826" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">零食肉条</a></strong></p><p><strong>营养犬粮推荐：<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//detail.tmall.com/item.htm%3Fspm%3Da1z10.1-b-s.w4004-21093901541.3.18d0440eKiUD7q%26pvid%3D735b9899-03ad-4bb7-9b26-6c9cecd273a7%26pos%3D2%26acm%3D03068.1003.1.702815%26id%3D582310721085%26scm%3D1007.12941.156882.100200300000000" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">宠大圣经典系列</a></strong></p><p><strong>淘宝/天猫：<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//petwukong.tmall.com/" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">宠大圣旗舰店</a></strong></p>
