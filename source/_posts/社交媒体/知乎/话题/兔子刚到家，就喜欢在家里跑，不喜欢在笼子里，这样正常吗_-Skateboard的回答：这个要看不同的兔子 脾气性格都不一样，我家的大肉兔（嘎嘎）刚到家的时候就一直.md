<strong>兔子刚到家，就喜欢在家里跑，不喜欢在笼子里，这样正常吗?</strong><br>Skateboard的回答<br><p data-pid="Qc9mrquS">这个要看不同的兔子 脾气性格都不一样，我家的大肉兔（嘎嘎）刚到家的时候就一直在笼子里面不敢出来，过了好久才敢没人在笼子旁边自己跑出来玩</p><a class="video-box" href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.zhihu.com/video/1482292299330105344" target="_blank" data-video-id data-video-playable data-name data-poster="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-faf1645a2ca86e36c39aff5b76a6be86.png" data-lens-id="1482292299330105344"><img class="thumbnail" src="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-faf1645a2ca86e36c39aff5b76a6be86.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="content"><span class="title"><span class="z-ico-extern-gray"><span class="z-ico-extern-blue"></span><span class="url"><span class="z-ico-video">https://www.zhihu.com/video/1482292299330105344</span></span></span></span></span></a><p data-pid="YBzIT4QJ">在看我们家刚接来的小兔子（元宵）刚到家就和题主家兔子一样喜欢到处跑。特别是我家床底下，然后会咬墙角，但是兔子很聪明会在自己厕所尿尿，很少在外面拉屎。</p><a class="video-box" href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.zhihu.com/video/1482292454963982337" target="_blank" data-video-id data-video-playable data-name data-poster="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-4a59e3de11d4165a06dfa6074de9e6c4.jpeg" data-lens-id="1482292454963982337"><img class="thumbnail" src="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-4a59e3de11d4165a06dfa6074de9e6c4.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="content"><span class="title"><span class="z-ico-extern-gray"><span class="z-ico-extern-blue"></span><span class="url"><span class="z-ico-video">https://www.zhihu.com/video/1482292454963982337</span></span></span></span></span></a><p data-pid="o-tjXS_y">  我给题主的建议是，这种活泼的小兔子刚到家先静养两天，关在笼子不要放出来，除了喂草也不要去碰它。让它自己熟悉一下笼子里面的情况，不然以后放养他不会自己回笼子里面的，脾气也会变大。</p>
