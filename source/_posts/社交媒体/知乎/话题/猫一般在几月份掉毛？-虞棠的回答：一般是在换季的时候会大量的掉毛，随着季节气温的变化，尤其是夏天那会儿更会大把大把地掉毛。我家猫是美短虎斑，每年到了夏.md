<strong>猫一般在几月份掉毛？</strong><br>虞棠的回答<br><p>一般是在换季的时候会大量的掉毛，随着季节气温的变化，尤其是夏天那会儿更会大把大把地掉毛。</p><p>我家猫是美短虎斑，每年到了夏天，已经不是蒲公英可以形容掉毛的了，换完毛后可以明显看到毛色浅了很多，猫毛也有变短一些些。</p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic4.zhimg.com/50/v2-1eb6e419da438f0b1f988718bb709ce9_720w.jpg?source=54b3c3a5" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="1440" data-default-watermark-src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-e637e2f91f3d517a5ef94bebbce47dab_720w.jpg?source=54b3c3a5" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb lazy" data-original="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-1eb6e419da438f0b1f988718bb709ce9_720w.jpg?source=54b3c3a5" data-actualsrc="https://pic4.zhimg.com/50/v2-1eb6e419da438f0b1f988718bb709ce9_720w.jpg?source=54b3c3a5" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p>（上图只是某一次随手扒拉下来，团成团的）</p><p>其实，猫每天都在掉毛，“毛孩子”只要在床铺沙发上等滚过一圈，基本就是猫过留毛了，所以家里一定要准备好扫床仪和吸尘器。</p><a class="video-box" href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.zhihu.com/video/1437400289444196352" target="_blank" data-video-id data-video-playable="true" data-name data-poster="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-a3c912960fddab646696534629d75271.jpeg" data-lens-id="1437400289444196352">              <img class="thumbnail" src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-a3c912960fddab646696534629d75271.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">              <span class="content">                <span class="title"><span class="z-ico-extern-gray"><span class="z-ico-extern-blue"></span>                <span class="url"><span class="z-ico-video">https://www.zhihu.com/video/1437400289444196352</span>              </span>            </span></span></span></a>            <p>建议：养了花色毛孩子的小伙伴，建议你们别买深色衣服！</p>
