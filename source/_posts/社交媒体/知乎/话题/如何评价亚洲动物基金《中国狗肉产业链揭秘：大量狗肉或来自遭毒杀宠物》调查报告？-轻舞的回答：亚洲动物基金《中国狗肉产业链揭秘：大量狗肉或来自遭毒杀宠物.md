data-pid="L1lRga8Y">多年以前，高举反对“活熊取胆”旗帜的亚洲动物基金就遭到过质疑，认为其涉嫌非法集资。</p><p data-pid="qp1iQXkr">亚洲动物基金AAF 曾接受西方药企<a href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E4%BC%98%E6%80%9D%E5%BC%97&search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A2683848506%7D"
