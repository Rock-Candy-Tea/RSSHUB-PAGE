<strong>如何治疗猫便秘？</strong><br>从从猫-在线咨询的回答<br><p data-pid="ETkbkBT0">猫便秘是一种由于肠管运动障碍和分泌紊乱，肠内物停滞、变干,而造成的肠道阻塞的消化系统疾病。</p><p data-pid="AZ179Avh"><b>一、猫便秘通常有以下病症：排便频率增加2倍、粪便量减少、腹部触诊疼痛、腹部触诊不明团块;腹腔内团块、食欲减少、被毛杂乱等。</b></p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-8b8dde4ebee1351173d77498bba59f75_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1280" data-rawheight="1016" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-0cd2d286d8833ee2beab2d4f98425790_b.jpg" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-8b8dde4ebee1351173d77498bba59f75_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p data-pid="BES2chgB"><b>二、什么情况会造成猫便秘？</b></p><p data-pid="Fnm0y5BV">1.不爱喝水，喝水不规律</p><p data-pid="PeIrM-m1">2.不带运动，天天躺</p><p data-pid="j0xesgL1">3.猫食物中纤维素的含量过低</p><p data-pid="wO3uBrIR">4.猫患腰椎疾病,由于疼痛引起不敢排便;或支配神经和排便中枢受损后不能支配,引起便秘</p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-3127e94d9d0bf3e415035ccb945bc460_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1280" data-rawheight="853" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-8fc009b3cc32c1a3c94b3be52d06b38f_b.jpg" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-3127e94d9d0bf3e415035ccb945bc460_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p data-pid="HeQUjqew"><b>三、预防猫便秘</b></p><p data-pid="ncKMT1rK">1.多喝水或者多喂一些妙鲜包、猫罐头之类的湿粮</p><p data-pid="pNeIdNNI">2.多运动，买一些猫玩具或是逗猫棒和它一起玩</p><p data-pid="mk0XvaWZ">3.多喂纤维素多的食物</p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-d109a652860cc876af329223d1c07df0_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1280" data-rawheight="882" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-09b01098b46643606965572f0dfd17b1_b.jpg" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-d109a652860cc876af329223d1c07df0_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p></p>
