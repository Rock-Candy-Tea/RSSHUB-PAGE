<strong>请问国内哪里有比较好并且近期有繁育陨石色的边牧犬舍？</strong><br>苏秦背剑的回答<br><p data-pid="7oEF5dtj">陨石边牧没那么贵，再说边牧也不用一万多块钱啊</p><p data-pid="-8rWMUPD">我家就繁育边牧，陨石，黑白，咖啡，金色都有，陨石黑白是主流，因为容易繁育，另外提醒一下你，如果你你想买陨石色边牧，卖边牧的犬舍父母都是陨石，千万别买~因为陨石边牧不能父母都用陨石搭配，那样会出现概率性失聪，影响狗狗以后的听力，还容易出现色盲，失明！</p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-e7935d7f5ca65ecf260400e2225c1a7f_1440w.jpg" data-rawwidth="1620" data-rawheight="1080" data-size="normal" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-b1a6fb50c40cacfc6c01590e7d371171_b.jpg" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-e7935d7f5ca65ecf260400e2225c1a7f_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-b90b42bf85e8722e9676c357faa27f38_1440w.jpg" data-rawwidth="1620" data-rawheight="1080" data-size="normal" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-27051793091c65ce5f8e51c81a543b3d_b.jpg" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-b90b42bf85e8722e9676c357faa27f38_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p></p>
