href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E7%8C%AB%E5%92%AA&search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A1972087114%7D" class="internal">猫咪</a>打喷嚏，也要根据它打喷嚏的个数、频率等来判断它是否感冒或者患其他病症。万一猫咪只是突然的打了一个喷嚏，那也有可能是和我们人一样，突然觉得<b>鼻子痒痒</b>，或者是<b>被外界的气味等刺激到</b>了。</p><p data-pid="y492zeAq">有一些<b>猫咪鼻子的鼻道天生就比较小</b>，这样也很容易打喷嚏。</p><p data-pid="rlY4JtXw">如果猫咪一直打喷嚏，还一直用爪子洗脸挠鼻子，铲屎官就可以检查一下鼻子中是否进了<b>异物</b>。</p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-261b291161d466b3a917eb7e1636dd1e_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-pid="5q_hof57"><b><a href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E8%BF%87%E6%95%8F%E6%80%A7%E6%89%93%E5%96%B7%E5%9A%8F&search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A1972087114%7D" href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E6%89%93%E5%96%B7%E5%9A%8F&search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A1972087114%7D" class="internal">打喷嚏</a>。即使让猫咪离开过敏源，或者给猫咪少量服用地米、<a href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E6%89%91%E5%B0%94%E6%95%8F&search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A1972087114%7D" class="internal">扑尔敏</a>等抗过敏药物即可。</li><li data-pid="SHijJ2vg"><b>外界刺激</b>：猫咪嗅觉灵敏，有些我们闻着好闻的味道，在猫咪闻来可能就是刺激性气味，也会引起猫咪打喷嚏。所以铲屎官在使用空气清新剂、香氛、香水等味道较重的物品时，最好避开猫咪且少量使用。</li><li data-pid="va9CrAZ8"><b><a href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E7%97%85%E6%AF%92%E6%84%9F%E6%9F%93&search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A1972087114%7D" class="internal">病毒感染</a></b>。这里包括感冒和猫鼻支，这两种情况都是猫咪感染病毒，患病之后会出现打喷嚏的症状。但是除此之外，猫咪还可能同时出现情绪低落、食欲下降等症状。比较严重的还有可能伴随流鼻涕、流口水、发烧等症状，需要尽快就医。</li></ol><figure data-size="normal"><img
