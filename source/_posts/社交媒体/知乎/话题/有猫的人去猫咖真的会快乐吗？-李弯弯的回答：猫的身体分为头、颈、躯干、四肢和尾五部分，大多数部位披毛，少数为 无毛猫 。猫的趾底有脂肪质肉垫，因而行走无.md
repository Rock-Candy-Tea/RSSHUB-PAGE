<strong>有猫的人去猫咖真的会快乐吗？</strong><br>李弯弯的回答<br><p data-pid="gZDw2r04">猫的身体分为头、颈、躯干、四肢和尾五部分，大多数部位披毛，少数为<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//baike.baidu.com/item/%25E6%2597%25A0%25E6%25AF%259B%25E7%258C%25AB" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">无毛猫</a>。</p><p data-pid="yHWfhJro">猫的趾底有脂肪质肉垫，因而行走无声，捕鼠时不会惊跑鼠，趾端生有锐利的指甲。爪能够缩进和伸出。猫在休息和行走时爪缩进去，只在捕鼠和攀爬时伸出来，防止指甲被磨钝。猫的前肢有五指，后肢有四指。猫的牙齿分为<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//baike.baidu.com/item/%25E9%2597%25A8%25E9%25BD%25BF" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">门齿</a>、<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//baike.baidu.com/item/%25E7%258A%25AC%25E9%25BD%25BF" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">犬齿</a>和<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//baike.baidu.com/item/%25E8%2587%25BC%25E9%25BD%25BF" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">臼齿</a>。犬齿特别发达，尖锐如锥，适于咬死捕到的鼠类，臼齿的咀嚼面有尖锐的突起，适于把肉嚼碎；门齿不发达。</p><p data-pid="9yaclsdY"><b>猫还能从它的牙齿来判断它的年龄</b></p><p data-pid="gqQiQPu-">成年猫咪的牙齿共30枚。幼年猫咪的牙齿共26枚。</p><p data-pid="v6SIb8W5">猫的牙齿从两边往中间分别是：</p><p data-pid="Ac8eK0jy">上排——臼齿大臼齿前臼齿犬齿6颗门齿</p><p data-pid="2TS80Uor">下排——大臼齿前臼齿犬齿6颗门齿</p><p data-pid="Oq54D5Vl">14天左右开始长牙</p><p data-pid="AM394x99">2～3周龄乳门牙长齐。近两月龄时，乳牙全部长齐，呈白色，细而尖</p><p data-pid="-M1YnCvY">3～4月龄更换第一乳门牙</p><p data-pid="ETaEIq_J">5～6月龄换第二三<a href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E4%B9%B3%E9%97%A8%E9%BD%BF&search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A2567642363%7D" class="internal">乳门齿</a>及乳犬牙</p><p data-pid="nBlkEci9">6月龄以后全部换上<a href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E6%81%92%E9%BD%BF&search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A2567642363%7D" class="internal">恒齿</a></p><p data-pid="IWjTj2oD">8月龄恒齿长齐，洁白光亮，门齿上部有尖凸</p><p data-pid="8CuvXaHm">1岁下颌第二门齿大尖峰，磨损至小尖峰平齐，此现象称为尖峰磨灭</p><p data-pid="7Oe4a-F5">2岁下颌第二门齿尖峰磨灭</p><a
