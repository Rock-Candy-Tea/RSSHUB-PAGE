<strong>狗狗洋葱中毒，现在严重溶血性贫血，红细胞才3.9克怎么办？</strong><br>宠壹生的回答<br><p data-pid="rqVcj4vm">哪些人类食物狗狗吃了会中毒呀?</p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-b501c31dacec8f785672d5d744999f2e_1440w.jpg" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="623" data-size="normal" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-b501c31dacec8f785672d5d744999f2e_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p data-pid="-_CVLH65">       很多人类食物不适合饲喂狗狗,特别是洋葱、巧克力、葡萄、乳酪、鸡鸭骨头等,会严重影响狗狗的健康。</p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-106953fa620ac466dfe43f03b862538c_1440w.jpg" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="710" data-size="normal" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-106953fa620ac466dfe43f03b862538c_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p data-pid="YVLAyonQ">        巧克力中的可可碱对狗狗的中枢神经及心肌有非常强的神经作用,巧克力的纯度越高,危险性越大。</p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-2c1bb66bb867d34c098a7e33f8750351_1440w.jpg" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="941" data-size="normal" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-2c1bb66bb867d34c098a7e33f8750351_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p data-pid="6YzZOwZg">洋葱、大葱和大蒜,含有二硫化物会损伤狗狗的红细胞,引起溶血性贫血。</p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-14f7fa6a4852756cb5ef77226ea5f2a6_1440w.jpg" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="668" data-size="normal" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-14f7fa6a4852756cb5ef77226ea5f2a6_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p data-pid="D1wtoqi3">       另外部分的人类药物也会引起狗狗中毒,包括阿司匹林、扑热息痛、布洛芬等药物,因此也尽量不要用人类的药物给宠物使用。</p>
