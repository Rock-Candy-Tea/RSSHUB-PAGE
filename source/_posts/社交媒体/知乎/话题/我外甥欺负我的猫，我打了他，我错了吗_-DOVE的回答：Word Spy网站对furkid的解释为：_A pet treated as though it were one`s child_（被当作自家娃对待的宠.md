<strong>我外甥欺负我的猫，我打了他，我错了吗?</strong><br>DOVE的回答<br><p data-pid="Bf7H4RdO"><b>Word Spy</b>网站对<b>furkid</b>的解释为：“<b>A pet treated as though it were one`s child.</b>”（被当作自家娃对待的宠物。）<b>furkid</b>由<b>fur</b>（软毛，毛皮）和<b>kid</b>组成，为可数名词，也可写作<b>fur-kid</b>、<b>fur kid</b>、<b>furbaby</b>和<b>furchild</b>等形式。<br>  美国《南佛罗里达太阳哨兵报》（<b><i>Sun-Sentinel</i></b>）2000年3月26日刊登的一篇文章中最早使用了这一表达方式。文章指出：“<b>At least one major consumer advocacy organization advised against pet health insurance. Robert Hunter, director of insurance for the Washington, D.C.-based Consumer Federation of America, one of the nation`s largest consumer advocacy groups, expressed his group`s view, which is likely to outrage animal lovers who think of their dogs and cats as their 'furkids.</b>'”（至少有一个大型消费者权益保护组织建议不要使用宠物健康保险。全国最大的消费者权益保护团体之一、总部位于华盛顿特区的美国消费者联盟的保险事务主管罗伯特·亨特表达了其所在团体的观点，该观点有可能激怒将其猫和狗视为自家“毛小孩”的动物爱好者。）<br>  美国《新闻日报》（<b><i>Newsday</i></b>）特约撰稿人丹尼斯·费雷姆（<b>staff writer Denise Flaim</b>）2004年5月17日为该报撰文写道：“<b>Although I never went as far as calling them my 'fur kids,' dogs long predated babies in my life. Indeed, in other pages of this newspaper, I am a pets columnist, writing about everything from bearded dragons to border collies. But as I begin to compose the occasional mothering column as well, I`ve discovered that there`s plenty of crossover wisdom between species.</b>”（尽管我还没到把狗叫作我的‘毛小孩’的地步，但它们在我生儿育女之前很早就已出现在我的生活中。实际上，我是这份报纸其他版面的宠物文章专栏作家，从鬃狮蜥到博德牧羊犬，所有宠物都写。不过，由于我开始偶尔也撰写育儿专栏文章，我认识到物种之间存在大量交叉的智慧。）</p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-dccdf9db2232360979341885e7f61774_1440w.jpg" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="6000" data-rawheight="4000" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-dccdf9db2232360979341885e7f61774_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption>宠物</figcaption></figure><p data-pid="EYfWujTT">猫猫真可爱~</p>
