Denis Villeneuve的回答<br><br><p>The first book of <i>Dune</i> was too dense of a story to be told in one movie. I needed to do a massive movie that is split in two.  For me, <i>Dune: Part 1</i> was by far the biggest movie I've ever made, and the most challenging one. But, the second part might be an even bigger challenge. </p><p>Hans Zimmer who composed the score for <i>Dune</i> is already working on <i>Dune: Part 2</i>. Every now and then, I receive new music that he created. It feels like receiving Christmas gift every time. I'm also writing the script for <i>Dune: Part 2</i>, and his music inspires me tremendously. I hope to create a second movie that is even more cinematic than <i>Dune: Part 1</i>.</p><p>《沙丘》原著的第一卷是一个非常宏伟的故事，一部电影放不下。我需要完成一部分成上下部的长篇巨制。对我来说，《沙丘》是我迄今为止最大的一部制作，也是最具挑战性的。但是，第二部可能是更大的挑战。</p><p>为《沙丘》创作了配乐的汉斯·季默还在为《沙丘2》继续创作。我隔一段时间就会收到他创作出来的新的音乐，那种感觉就像收到圣诞礼物一样——我正在写《沙丘2》的剧本，他的音乐极大地鼓舞了我。我希望能够创作出比《沙丘》更有电影感的《沙丘2》。</p>
