GT以及realme GT Neo这对双生子。</p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-f072373f5533ca3e7d4f35bf3c9dc7d9_1440w.jpg" data-rawwidth="600" data-rawheight="257" data-size="normal"
