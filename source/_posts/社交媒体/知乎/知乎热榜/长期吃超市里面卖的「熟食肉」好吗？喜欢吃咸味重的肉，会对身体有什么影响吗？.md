global  sodium  benchmarks  for different  food  categories”<sup data-text="WHO global  sodium  benchmarks  for different  food  categories" data-url="https://apps.who.int/iris/bitstream/handle/10665/341081/9789240025097-eng.pdf" data-draft-node="inline" data-draft-type="reference" data-numero="2">[2]</sup>翻译过来的意思是“世卫组织各类食品的全球钠基准”</p><p>其中有提到：</p><blockquote>An estimated 11 million deaths globally are associated with poor diet, 3 million  of which are attributable to high sodium intakes (2). Excess dietary sodium intake  increases blood pressure and consequently increases the risk of cardiovascular  diseases (3). Cardiovascular diseases are the leading cause of NCD deaths worldwide,  responsible for 32% of all deaths (4).</blockquote><p>大致意思是说：“全球大约有一千一百万人死于不良饮食，在这其中有三百万是死于高钠饮食（也可以说是高盐饮食，因为盐大多数时候生活中最常见的钠盐摄入渠道）”</p><blockquote>Reducing sodium intake is an effective way to lower blood pressure and thus reduce  NCDs such as cardiovascular diseases;it also reduces other complications associated  with high sodium intakes such as chronic kidney disease, obesity, gastric cancer and  liver diseases.</blockquote><p>减少钠摄入是降低血压从而减少其他心脑血管疾病的好方法。同时减少钠摄入，还可以降低与之相关的如慢性肾病、胃癌、肝癌等疾病的发病率。</p><p>世卫组织除了上面这个，还制定了《成人和儿童钠摄入量指南》，我们之前所说的每日摄入少于五克盐或者少于两克钠，就是从这里面来的。</p><p>但是对于经常吃的口味比较重的朋友来说，吃得咸的肉，往往是超过这个标准的。（有兴趣的，可以去找一些上面世卫组织建议文章中所引用的文献，这里就不贴了。）</p><p>说起来，随手拿起身边一包新疆朋友送的熟牛肉。</p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-9212f7a933ac112685586c0063f09118_720w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="249" data-rawheight="332" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-5efdcc74a8301767197c29e649d71383_720w.jpg" class="content_image" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p>这一包有五十克牛肉，就一点点，而且味道不算咸，很香，新疆的牛羊肉确实很不错，但就这样吃下去就摄入了400毫克钠，已经占了世卫组织推荐的五分之一，然而也就打个牙祭的，再随便吃一点东西，很容易就会超了……</p><p>又是不健康的一天呐！</p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-aacdf74099bf94c5362e97be08c30cb8_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="440" data-rawheight="440" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-2ad5c9d977513de308ff6ebb7500c014_720w.jpg" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-aacdf74099bf94c5362e97be08c30cb8_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p></p>
