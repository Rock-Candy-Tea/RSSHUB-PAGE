(±0.1mm)。</p><p>这么直接说大家可能还不清楚，直接上图。（图一）</p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-b7946a05eab29ceb3c1dce3aa1588b7c_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal"
