x的小米8系列，首月销量就破了118万台。</p><figure data-size="small"><img src="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-3d0e0831063fde7eba8d235e7f58259f_1440w.jpg" data-rawwidth="640" data-rawheight="1052" data-size="small"
