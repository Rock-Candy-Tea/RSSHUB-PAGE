referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>做法简单跟你们汇报下：</p><p>1、先用十来颗糯米糍去皮去核，放入料理机充分打碎。稍微过一下残渣，保留乳白色的混合液。</p><figure data-size="normal"><img data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-f4b8aa8572d5d678af0b92db86ca88c3_1440w.jpg" data-rawwidth="773" data-rawheight="515" data-size="normal" data-caption class="origin_image
