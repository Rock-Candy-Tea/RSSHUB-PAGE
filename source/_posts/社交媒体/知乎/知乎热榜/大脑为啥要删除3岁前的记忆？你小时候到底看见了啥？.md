<div>   
瞻云的回答<br><br><p><strong>从某种意义上来说<span><span>，</span></span>为了成长<span><span>，</span></span>我们献祭了童年的自己<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
<p>我们常常把婴儿的记忆比喻成一张白纸<span><span>，</span></span>其实比喻成自适应的硬盘更加的恰当<span><span>。</span></span></p>
<p>就像电脑硬盘在厂里造出来<span><span>，</span></span>通电后就开始损耗一样<span><span>。</span></span>你的大脑也还在妈妈肚子里的时候<span><span>，</span></span>也已经开始损耗<span><span>。</span></span></p>
<p>这是你 14 周时候的样子<span><span>：</span></span></p>
<figure><img alt data-rawheight="1440" data-rawwidth="2091" src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-390b927b4fad5e6674fbc71b397ff99d_1440w.jpg?source=b1748391" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-390b927b4fad5e6674fbc71b397ff99d_r.jpg?source=b1748391" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure>
<p>对于以神经元个数论英雄的人来说<span><span>，</span></span>此时你已经达到了人生巅峰<span><span>。</span></span></p>
<p>是的<span><span>，</span></span>当你在妈妈体内 3~4 个月的时候<span><span>，</span></span>随着神经系统的发育<span><span>，</span></span>神经元个数达到了此生的极限<span><span>。</span></span>从此以后<span><span>，</span></span>神经元个数开始做减法<span><span>，</span></span>然后持续一生<span><span>。</span></span></p>
<p>成年后你的神经元总个数<span><span>，</span></span>仅仅只有胎儿时期的一半<span><span>。</span></span></p>
<p>虽然怀胎 10 月<span><span>，</span></span>但大脑神经元数目的爆发<span><span>，</span></span>却主要集中在 2~3 月<span><span>。</span></span></p>
<p>第 1 个月的时候<span><span>，</span></span>你的神经系统还是雏形<span><span>，</span></span>简单得犹如原始鱼类的大脑[1]<span><span>。</span></span></p>
<figure><img alt data-rawheight="1076" data-rawwidth="2560" src="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-4d9e4267673883544b9e6c518d635e15_1440w.jpg?source=b1748391" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-4d9e4267673883544b9e6c518d635e15_r.jpg?source=b1748391" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure>
<p>但仅仅 1 周之后<span><span>，</span></span>就会形成端脑<span><span>、</span></span>间脑<span><span>、</span></span>中脑<span><span>、</span></span>后脑<span><span>、</span></span>末脑<span><span>。</span></span></p>
<figure><img alt data-rawheight="1349" data-rawwidth="2560" src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-493f66e0bc727922839ce7154e1e6769_1440w.jpg?source=b1748391" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-493f66e0bc727922839ce7154e1e6769_r.jpg?source=b1748391" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure>
<p>2 个月前后<span><span>，</span></span>发育出五脏六腑<span><span>、</span></span>生殖系统<span><span>。</span></span></p>
<p>到了第 3 个月<span><span>，</span></span>除了肺部需要在 180 天之后才开始形成外<span><span>，</span></span>基本上所有的器官都有了雏形<span><span>。</span></span></p>
<p>这个时候<span><span>，</span></span>你的大脑才是货真价实的<span><span>「</span></span><strong>原厂配置</strong><span><span>」</span></span><span><span>。</span></span></p>
<p>因为从第 2 个月的时候<span><span>，</span></span>你开始出现触觉<span><span>，</span></span>第 3 个月出现味觉<span><span>，</span></span>第 5 个月出现听觉<span><span>，</span></span>第 7 个月出现视觉…</p>
<p>总之<span><span>，</span></span>大约从 14 周前后<span><span>，</span></span>随着各类感官系统的发育<span><span>，</span></span>妈妈体内的信息开始通过神经元传输<span><span>，</span></span>在你的大脑中留下痕迹<span><span>，</span></span>产生记忆<span><span>。</span></span></p>
<p><strong>人的记忆是通过什么储存的<span><span>？</span></span></strong></p>
<p>并不是神经元胞体<span><span>，</span></span>而是神经元与神经元之间的突触连接<a href="https://www.zhihu.com/question/518355959/answer/2411189004#ref_2">[2]</a><span><span>。</span></span></p>
<figure><img alt="两条相连的神经通路的强度被认为导致信息的储存，从而产生记忆。" data-rawheight="1440" data-rawwidth="2553" src="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-f8295924678ed5912963723fae7b715a_1440w.jpg?source=b1748391" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-f8295924678ed5912963723fae7b715a_r.jpg?source=b1748391" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure>两条相连的神经通路的强度被认为导致信息的储存<span><span>，</span></span>从而产生记忆<span><span>。</span></span>
<p>但胎儿<span><span>（</span></span>婴儿<span><span>）</span></span>大脑只有一个原始的智能学习系统<span><span>，</span></span>无法处理任何事情<span><span>，</span></span>不像电脑可以装载高级软件和资料<span><span>，</span></span>直接进行复杂工作<span><span>。</span></span></p>
<p>人类刚出厂的时候<span><span>，</span></span>除了一味的储存大量信息外<span><span>，</span></span>对外界信息的处理能力十分的低下<span><span>。</span></span></p>
<p>现在的 AI 就是模仿人类大脑的学习网络<span><span>，</span></span>一开始就是大量的信息摄入<span><span>，</span></span>然后不断学习<span><span>，</span></span>不断优化<span><span>。</span></span>一开始的人工智能总是给人留下又傻又蠢的印象<span><span>，</span></span>但随着几代升级后<span><span>，</span></span>很多细节就变得越来越智能<span><span>。</span></span></p>
<p>人一出生的时候<span><span>，</span></span>学习才刚刚开始<span><span>。</span></span></p>
<p>一出生的刹那<span><span>，</span></span>光进入你的眼睛<span><span>，</span></span>声音进入你的耳朵<span><span>，</span></span>空气进入你的鼻子<span><span>、</span></span>奶水滑过你的舌尖<span><span>，</span></span>以及妈妈在你皮肤上的抚摸<span><span>，</span></span>无数的感觉转化成神经电信号<span><span>，</span></span>到达你的大脑……</p>
<p>一方面你大脑处理能力低下<span><span>，</span></span>眼前任何事物<span><span>，</span></span>都足以让你的感官超载<span><span>。</span></span>而另一方面<span><span>，</span></span>你的大脑每时每刻<span><span>，</span></span>都在不断记录这些新奇的信息<span><span>，</span></span>建立你自己的专属神经网络<span><span>。</span></span></p>
<p>仅仅每一秒钟<span><span>，</span></span>你的大脑便可以产生上百万个突触连接<span><span>。</span></span></p>
<p>但对于至少需要 100 万亿突触连接<span><span>（</span></span>大脑皮层 140~160 亿神经元[3]<span><span>，</span></span>每个的突触连接可多达数千<span><span>）</span></span>的你<span><span>，</span></span>你那点记忆<span><span>，</span></span>此时简直犹如储存在巨大的黑暗匣子内<span><span>。</span></span></p>
<p>所以<span><span>，</span></span>作为新生儿的你<span><span>，</span></span>记忆是碎片式的<span><span>，</span></span>还没有连续记忆<span><span>。</span></span>一开始主要都是 5~15 秒钟的短时记忆<span><span>，</span></span>超过 2 分钟以上的记忆还很少很少<span><span>。</span></span></p>
<p><strong>那如此短的记忆是如何延长的呢<span><span>？</span></span></strong></p>
<p>这是因为人类的突触连接具有可塑性<a href="https://www.zhihu.com/question/518355959/answer/2411189004#ref_4">[4]</a><span><span>。</span></span>当建立突触连接之后<span><span>，</span></span>如果你再接收到相同的信号刺激<span><span>，</span></span>突触连接就会加强<span><span>，</span></span>相反长期不接收到相关的刺激<span><span>，</span></span>信息就会减弱<a href="https://www.zhihu.com/question/518355959/answer/2411189004#ref_5">[5]</a><span><span>。</span></span></p>
<figure><img alt="一个神经元发出的突触连接总是有强有弱" data-rawheight="1440" data-rawwidth="1920" src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-a28411a366d5704e7638aae133ebfd21_1440w.jpg?source=b1748391" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-a28411a366d5704e7638aae133ebfd21_r.jpg?source=b1748391" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure>一个神经元发出的突触连接总是有强有弱
<p>我们从语言的建立过程来举例<span><span>，</span></span>a<span><span>、</span></span>o 等元音是我们最容易也是最先掌握的声音<span><span>，</span></span>我们对此最为熟悉<span><span>，</span></span>所以大脑最开始建立的相关神经通路<span><span>，</span></span>也最为强壮<span><span>。</span></span>随着<span><span>，</span></span>我们从父母的声音中积累越来越多的元音<span><span>，</span></span>随后能掌握的单字往往也是单元音的<span><span>，</span></span>而且是叠词<span><span>，</span></span>例如妈妈<span><span>、</span></span>爸爸<span><span>。</span></span></p>
<p>随着相关神经网络的加强<span><span>，</span></span>自然也就有了形成长期记忆的基础<span><span>。</span></span></p>
<p>2 个月大的婴儿<span><span>，</span></span>一般能有一两天的记忆<span><span>，</span></span>有人认为婴儿的驱动还没有装好<span><span>（</span></span>没有神经基础<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>然而早在 1999 年<span><span>，</span></span>就有人通过实验研究发现<span><span>，</span></span>2 个月大的婴儿通过周期性的反复提醒<span><span>，</span></span>便能够一直保持记忆[6]<span><span>。</span></span></p>
<figure><img alt data-rawheight="2111" data-rawwidth="1440" src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-1f562c557c8ad52bc1815def4060bfe5_1440w.jpg?source=b1748391" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-1f562c557c8ad52bc1815def4060bfe5_r.jpg?source=b1748391" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure>
<p>这说明<span><span>，</span></span>并非大脑没有相关的<a class="internal" href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E8%AE%B0%E5%BF%86%E6%9C%BA%E5%88%B6&search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_extra=%7B:,:2411189004%7D">记忆机制</a><span><span>，</span></span>而是大脑主动把这些记忆清除了<span><span>。</span></span></p>
<p>大约 6 个月的时候<span><span>，</span></span>长期记忆越来越多<span><span>，</span></span>少部分能持续 1 个月之久<span><span>。</span></span></p>
<p>到达 1 岁的时候<span><span>，</span></span>比起出生时<span><span>，</span></span>大脑中的突触连接已经复杂了很多倍<span><span>。</span></span></p>
<p>人小脑中的浦肯野细胞<span><span>，</span></span>随着你的成长<span><span>，</span></span>在你学会爬行<span><span>、</span></span>走路<span><span>、</span></span>奔跑<span><span>，</span></span>以及各种大动作<span><span>、</span></span>精细动作的学习之后<span><span>，</span></span>最终每个神经细胞会建立多达 10 万个的突触连接[7]<span><span>。</span></span></p>
<p>简直犹如鬼斧神工般的惊艳<span><span>：</span></span></p>
<figure><img alt data-rawheight="1440" data-rawwidth="2099" src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-3011400ff4ecdfeb344cac5d6fab1837_1440w.jpg?source=b1748391" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-3011400ff4ecdfeb344cac5d6fab1837_r.jpg?source=b1748391" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure>
<p>突触连接的增加<span><span>，</span></span>也令脑子迅速膨大<span><span>。</span></span></p>
<p>刚出生的时候<span><span>，</span></span>你的脑容量只有 350g<span><span>，</span></span>一岁便达到 950g<span><span>，</span></span>2 岁达到 1100g<span><span>，</span></span>3 岁便有了 1260g<span><span>，</span></span>达到成年人的 90%<span><span>。</span></span></p>
<p><strong>既然大脑发育得如此之快<span><span>，</span></span>记忆又是如何消失的呢<span><span>？</span></span></strong></p>
<p>还记得一开始<span><span>，</span></span>我把记忆比喻成硬盘吗<span><span>？</span></span></p>
<p>但人类的神经元或者突触连接<span><span>，</span></span>并不像电脑的储存单元一样被晶体管数目和布局限定死的<span><span>。</span></span></p>
<p>储存空间是随着神经元个数<span><span>、</span></span>以及突触连接情况而自适应的<span><span>。</span></span></p>
<p>虽然在 14 周的时候<span><span>，</span></span>我们会诞生数量最多的神经元<span><span>，</span></span>但神经元质量参差不齐<span><span>。</span></span>人脑的空间就只有 1400ml 左右<span><span>，</span></span>你让 10 个低质量的神经元建立 1 万个神经连接<span><span>，</span></span>可能还不如一个神经元建立 5000 个神经连接更加的节能高效<span><span>。</span></span></p>
<p>人类的大脑其实已经是一个十分优化的节能系统了<span><span>，</span></span>但哪怕如此<span><span>，</span></span>也需要消耗人体 20% 的能量<span><span>。</span></span>所以<span><span>，</span></span>一开始足够多的神经元储备<span><span>，</span></span>就是为了后续优化的<span><span>。</span></span></p>
<p>而人类童年记忆的消失<span><span>，</span></span>同样在于大脑的优化<span><span>。</span></span></p>
<p>两三岁之前<span><span>，</span></span>记忆信息来者不拒<span><span>，</span></span>会形成十分纷乱的神经网络<span><span>。</span></span></p>
<p>两三岁的孩子基本停不下来<span><span>，</span></span>这里戳一戳<span><span>，</span></span>那里爬一爬<span><span>。</span></span>杂乱的神经网络<span><span>，</span></span>不仅不能形成稳定的人格<span><span>，</span></span>信息之间也会互相争夺感官资源<span><span>，</span></span>造成注意力的不集中<span><span>。</span></span></p>
