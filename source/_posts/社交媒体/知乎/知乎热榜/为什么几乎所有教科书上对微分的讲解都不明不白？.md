Leonhard的回答<br><br><p data-pid="oXMuKFfb">在数学分析范围内, 微分就是一种线性映射.</p><p data-pid="XYcD9Qf2">设 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D%5Csubset%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En" alt="D\subset\mathbb&#123;R&#125;^n" eeimg="1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"> 为开集, <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3A+D%5Cto%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5Em" alt="f: D\to\mathbb&#123;R&#125;^m" eeimg="1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"> 是我们考虑的函数. 设 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_0%5Cin+D" alt="x_0\in D" eeimg="1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"> , 若存在线性映射 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A" alt="A" eeimg="1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"> , 使得对 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_0" alt="x_0" eeimg="1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"> 的某个邻域内的所有点 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x" alt="x" eeimg="1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">, 均有</p><p data-pid="KE6_XP3m"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29-f%28x_0%29%3DA%5Ccdot%28x-x_0%29%2Bo%28%7C%7Cx-x_0%7C%7C%29" alt="f(x)-f(x_0)=A\cdot(x-x_0)+o(||x-x_0||)" eeimg="1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p data-pid="KFMFux8A">那么称线性映射 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7Bd%7Df%28x_0%29%3A%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%5Cto%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5Em%2C+h%5Cmapsto+Ah" alt="\text&#123;d&#125;f(x_0):\mathbb&#123;R&#125;^n\to\mathbb&#123;R&#125;^m, h\mapsto Ah" eeimg="1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"> 为 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f" alt="f" eeimg="1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"> 在 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_0" alt="x_0" eeimg="1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"> 处的微分.</p><p data-pid="V9FAPAq3">用线性映射去逼近是贯穿整个数学分析的灵魂. 暂时可以这样理解.</p>
