other studies that it was more transmissible but less severe.</p><p data-pid="lMl2RdQh">5月5日（路透社） - 根据美国一<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.researchsquare.com/article/rs-1601788/v1" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">项大型研究</a>的预印本版本，SARS-CoV2病毒的Omicron变体本质上与以前的变体危害一样严重，该研究反驳了其他研究中的假设，即它更容易传播但不太严重。</p><p data-pid="KiO0LUoA">The findings, which estimated Omicron's severity after accounting for the impact of vaccines, should reinforce the importance of inoculations and booster shots, experts said. Vaccines helped keep hospitalizations and deaths relatively low during the Omicron surge compared with previous variants.</p><p data-pid="p-9aBGmx">专家说，这些发现在考虑了疫苗的影响后估计了Omicron的严重程度，说明了接种和加强针的重要性。与以前的变体相比，疫苗有助于在Omicron激增期间保持相对较低的住院率和死亡人数。</p><p data-pid="No3Al6D8">The study, which is undergoing peer review at Nature Portfolio, was posted on Research Square on May 2. The authors, from Massachusetts General Hospital, Minerva University and Harvard Medical School, declined to comment until peer review is completed.</p><p data-pid="jeQvWNO3">该研究正在Nature Portfolio进行同行评审，于5月2日发布在Research Square上。来自马萨诸塞州总医院，密涅瓦大学和哈佛医学院的作者拒绝在同行评审完成之前发表评论。</p><p data-pid="HOEZWqPW">"We found that the risks of hospitalization and mortality were nearly identical" between the Omicron era and times in the past two years when different variants were dominant, the researchers said in their report.</p><p data-pid="v_pNTFgb">研究人员在他们的报告中说，“我们发现，Omicron时代和过去两年中不同变体占主导地位的时代之间的住院和死亡风险几乎相同”。</p><p><br></p><p data-pid="yvOHzxvw">The new study, based on records of 130,000 COVID patients in Massachusetts, is unique and "pretty strong," said Dr. Arjun Venkatesh of Yale School of Medicine and the Yale Center for Outcomes Research and Evaluation, who was not involved in the research.</p><p data-pid="y8AgNAag">这项新研究基于马萨诸塞州130，000名COVID患者的记录，是独特且“相当强大的”，耶鲁大学医学院和耶鲁大学结果研究与评估中心的Arjun Venkatesh博士说，他没有参与这项研究。</p><p data-pid="8OX5Pkd1">Rather than just looking at numbers of deaths and hospitalizations, as earlier studies have done, it accounted for patients' vaccination status and medical risk factors and compared similar groups of people, Venkatesh said.</p><p data-pid="SeezXu3w">Venkatesh说，它不像早期的研究那样只看死亡和住院人数，而是考虑了患者的疫苗接种状况和医疗风险因素，并比较了类似的人群。</p><p><br></p><p data-pid="r_0CDHMq">The authors cited potential limitations in their report, including the possibility that the analysis underestimated the number of vaccinated patients in more recent COVID waves, and the total number of infections, because it excluded patients who performed at-home rapid tests.</p><p data-pid="JW7m7BLA">作者在他们的报告中引用了潜在的局限性，包括分析可能低估了最近COVID浪潮中接种疫苗的患者数量以及感染总数，因为它排除了在家中进行快速检测的患者。</p><p data-pid="YuCnPiYr">The study did not account for treatments patients may have received, such as monoclonal antibodies or antiviral drugs "that are known to reduce hospitalizations," Venkatesh noted. "It's possible that if we didn't have these treatments available today, Omicron would be even worse."</p><p data-pid="tILJXqq1">Venkatesh指出，该研究没有考虑患者可能接受的治疗，例如“已知可以减少住院治疗”的单克隆抗体或抗病毒药物。“如果我们今天没有这些治疗方法，Omicron可能会更糟。</p><p data-pid="Jur-yKcM">Countries around the world have found that a significant percentage of their citizens were unwilling to get a COVID vaccine, even during surges of apparently deadlier variants.</p><p data-pid="WdS0D7nc">世界各国发现，其公民中有很大一部分不愿意接种COVID疫苗，即使在明显更致命的变体激增期间也是如此。</p><p data-pid="FkzHVFYA">When the Omicron variant was first identified late in 2021, public health officials said it caused much milder symptoms in the vast majority of infected people. That may have encouraged the vaccine hesitant that they were less in need of a shot.</p><p data-pid="lHxtSN7-">当Omicron变体在2021年底首次被发现时，公共卫生官员表示，它在绝大多数感染者中引起更轻微的症状。这样的言论可让很多人觉得他们不太需要接种。</p><p data-pid="s23ccerN">But Venkatesh said the new preprint adds to evidence that vaccines helped spare people from the worst impacts of Omicron.</p><p data-pid="876aukQR">但Venkatesh表示，新的预印本增加了疫苗帮助人们免受Omicron最严重影响的证据。</p><p data-pid="FZhwJMTq">"Don't make the mistake" of thinking vaccines and boosters are not important, he said.</p><p data-pid="d4LPHSUP">他说，“不要犯错误”，认为疫苗和加强剂并不重要。</p><p><br></p><h3>2.马萨诸塞州数据显示，Omicron期间住院人数和死亡人数都超过了前几波</h3><p data-pid="Ej04JPHx">感染数据</p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-7c7e24141e9ee62eba3258d7725f50e6_1440w.jpg?source=b1748391" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="818" data-rawheight="462" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-7c7e24141e9ee62eba3258d7725f50e6_r.jpg?source=b1748391" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p data-pid="jGhvcF04">马萨诸塞州死亡人数和住院人数</p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-400289583341029222cd95a52eec4be5_1440w.jpg?source=b1748391" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="819" data-rawheight="462" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-400289583341029222cd95a52eec4be5_r.jpg?source=b1748391" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p data-pid="1at2gVSx">本段来源：SARS-CoV-2 Omicron Variant is as Deadly as Previous Waves After Adjusting for Vaccinations, Demographics, and Comorbidities</p>
