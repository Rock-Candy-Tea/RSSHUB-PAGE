href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E9%A9%AC%E6%96%AF%E6%B4%9B&search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A2190689103%7D" class="internal">马斯洛</a>把需要分为五个层次，由低到高依次是：生理需要，安全的需要，归属和爱的需要，尊重的需要，自我实现的需要。马斯洛的<a href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E9%9C%80%E8%A6%81%E5%B1%82%E6%AC%A1%E7%90%86%E8%AE%BA&search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A1147582843%7D" class="internal">需要层次理论</a>认为，只有低级需要基本满足后，才会出现高一级需要。<br><br><b>（二十四）气质类型与教育</b><br><br><b>我们克服气质偏见，根据学生的气质特征因材施教：</b><br><br>1.对<b>多血质类型的学生</b>，在严格其<a href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E7%BB%84%E7%BB%87%E7%BA%AA%E5%BE%8B&search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A1147582843%7D"
