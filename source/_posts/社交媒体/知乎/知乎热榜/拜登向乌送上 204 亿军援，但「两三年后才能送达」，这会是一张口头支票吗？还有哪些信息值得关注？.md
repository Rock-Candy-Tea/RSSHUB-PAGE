月塔的回答<br><br><p data-pid="XNdcttRU">就看你怎么解读这个问题了；</p><p data-pid="3Np6dyv3">美国的援助分为两种形式，一种是从国防部的仓库里提取装备，然后立刻送往前线，这种援助美国今年执行了19次，最近一次在8月19日，拜登授权了价值7.75亿美元的援助，其中包括：</p><ul><li data-pid="C17gAkBi">高机动火箭炮系统（HIMARS）的额外弹药；</li><li data-pid="XR9C6fXb">16 门 105 毫米榴弹炮和 36,000 发 105 毫米火炮；</li><li data-pid="WOlTR550">15 Scan Eagle无人机系统；</li><li data-pid="414wqQuT">40 辆 MaxxPro 防地雷伏击保护车辆，配备矿山滚轮；</li><li data-pid="oZX0htQA">额外的高速反辐射导弹；</li><li data-pid="NZNPQgVR">50辆装甲高机动多用途轮式车辆（HMMWV）；</li><li data-pid="gAqx7S8Z">1,500 枚管发射、光跟踪、线制导 (TOW) 导弹；</li><li data-pid="IeqbAjjW">1,000 套标枪反装甲系统；</li><li data-pid="erSyEk_s">2,000 发反装甲弹；</li><li data-pid="HZ1yvy6U">扫雷设备和系统；</li><li data-pid="oU2JE9vB">爆破弹药；</li><li data-pid="eLMjbczf">战术安全通信系统；</li><li data-pid="4tXsEnmy">夜视设备、热成像系统、光学和激光测距仪。</li></ul><p data-pid="X6PrjIbN">另一种则是美国政府出钱，从工业界采购然后再送往前线，这应该是题目中所说的「两三年后才能送达」的来源。关于这件事，美国国防部是这么说的：</p><blockquote data-pid="x6yOI0PS">这是自六个月前俄罗斯入侵乌克兰以来，美国对乌克兰最大的一揽子援助计划。 <br>五角大楼新闻秘书说，这笔援助“强调了美国对长期支持乌克兰的承诺，代表了对关键能力的多年投资，以建立乌克兰武装部队的持久实力，因为它在面对俄罗斯的侵略时继续捍卫乌克兰的主权”空军双桅船。帕特莱德将军在一份书面声明中说。 <br>这种援助是美国对乌克兰人民的信任的具体证明。与允许几乎立即向乌克兰交付现有武器库存的总统提款授权不同，这些资金是乌克兰安全援助倡议的一部分。<br>这一过程使美国能够从工业界采购能力。莱德说：“该公告代表了一项合同流程的开始，旨在为乌克兰提供中长期的额外优先能力。”  <br>这些能力包括六个额外的国家先进地对空导弹系统和弹药。它还包括多达 245,000 发 155 毫米火炮弹药和多达 65,000 发 120 毫米迫击炮弹药。 <br>该工艺还将提供多达 24 台反炮雷达、Puma 无人机系统和扫描鹰无人机系统的支持设备。 <br>俄罗斯也在使用无人机系统，援助计划将向乌克兰人提供 VAMPIRE 反无人机系统。 <br>该援助还将提供激光制导火箭系统。 <br>这笔钱还将用于培训、维护和维持。<br><br><br><br><br><br><br><br></blockquote><p data-pid="bJva6ZV4">这是声明的原文：</p><blockquote data-pid="2fuVFXWg">It is the largest single U.S. aid package to Ukraine since the Russians invaded the nation six months ago. <br>The aid "underscores the U.S. commitment to supporting Ukraine over the long term, representing multi-year investments in critical capabilities to build the enduring strength of Ukraine's armed forces as it continues to defend Ukraine's sovereignty in the face of Russian aggression," Pentagon Press Secretary Air Force Brig. Gen. Pat Ryder said in a written statement. <br>This aid is tangible proof of America's belief in the Ukrainian people. Unlike the presidential drawdown authority that allows the almost immediate delivery to Ukraine of existing stocks of weapons, these funds are part of the Ukraine Security Assistance Initiative.<br>This process allows the United States to procure capabilities from industry. "This announcement represents the beginning of a contracting process to provide additional priority capabilities to Ukraine in the mid- and long-term," Ryder said.  <br>These capabilities include six additional National Advanced Surface-to-Air Missile Systems and munitions. It also includes up to 245,000 rounds of 155 mm artillery ammunition and up to 65,000 rounds of 120 mm mortar ammunition. <br>The process will also supply up to 24 counter-artillery radars, Puma Unmanned Aerial Systems and support equipment for Scan Eagle unmanned aerial systems. <br>Russia is using unmanned aerial systems, too, and the aid package will provide VAMPIRE Counter-Unmanned Aerial Systems to the Ukrainians. <br>The aid will also provide laser-guided rocket systems. <br>The money will also be used for training, maintenance and sustainment.  <br><br><br><br><br><br><br><br></blockquote><a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.defense.gov/News/News-Stories/Article/Article/3138602/us-announces-298-billion-in-aid-to-ukraine/" data-draft-node="block" data-draft-type="link-card" class=" external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer"><span class="invisible">https://www.</span><span class="visible">defense.gov/News/News-S</span><span class="invisible">tories/Article/Article/3138602/us-announces-298-billion-in-aid-to-ukraine/</span><span class="ellipsis"></span></a><p></p>
