张萌的回答<br><br><p>据以色列卫生部称，最近几周，辉瑞的Covid-19疫苗在防止人们感染以色列传染性德尔塔变种方面仅有效39%，但为防止住院和更严重的病毒形式提供了强有力的防护。</p><p>卫生部周四的一份报告称，这种疫苗为6月20日至7月17日期间研究的未指明人数的人提供了88%的预防住院治疗的保护，91.4%的疫苗可以预防重病。</p><p>报告说，由于对接种过疫苗的群体和未接种疫苗人群的检测方式不同，数据可能会出现偏差。</p><blockquote><a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.bloomberg.com/quote/PFE%3AUS" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">Pfizer Inc.</a>’s Covid-19 vaccine was just 39% effective in keeping people from getting infected by the contagious delta variant in Israel in recent weeks, according to the country’s health ministry, but provided a strong shield against hospitalization and more severe forms of the virus.<br>The vaccine provided 88% protection against hospitalization and 91.4% against severe illness for an unspecified number of people studied between June 20 and July 17, according to a report Thursday from the health ministry.<br><br>The report said that the data could be skewed because of different ways of testing vaccinated groups of people versus those who hadn’t been inoculated.<br><br><br></blockquote><p>另外，英国的数据也基本说明了，只打疫苗不防疫，不能阻断病毒传播。</p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-2d3989e7dd5a485323e81043ad9c492d_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="699" data-rawheight="593" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-0e01b89b117b8ca24215c70020e937e9_720w.jpg" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-2d3989e7dd5a485323e81043ad9c492d_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p></p>
