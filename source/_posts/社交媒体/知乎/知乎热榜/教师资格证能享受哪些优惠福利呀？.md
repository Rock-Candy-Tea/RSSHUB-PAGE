class="internal">教师资格证</a>，相当于已经拿到了教师行业的入场券，能从事的教师选择也非常多：</b></p><p data-pid="rufRNZkC"><b>第一种选择，编制教师。</b>长辈口中的“铁饭碗”，相亲市场上的“香饽饽”，五险一金齐全，有寒暑假，<a href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E9%80%80%E4%BC%91&search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A765957530%7D" class="internal">退休</a>没有经济压力，国家会给你发工资。这个工作好吧？但是好的工作大家都想去啊，竞争的激烈程度和<a href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E5%85%AC%E5%8A%A1%E5%91%98%E8%80%83%E8%AF%95&search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A765957530%7D" href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E5%90%88%E5%90%8C%E5%88%B6%E6%95%99%E5%B8%88&search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A765957530%7D" href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E4%BB%A3%E8%AF%BE%E6%95%99%E5%B8%88&search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A765957530%7D"
