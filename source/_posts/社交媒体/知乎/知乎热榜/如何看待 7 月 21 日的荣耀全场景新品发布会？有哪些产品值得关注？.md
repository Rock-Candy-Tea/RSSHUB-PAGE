RDNA 2核显技术，性能表现非常出色Time Spy跑分2800+,Time Spy Extreme 1300+，基本是MX550的表现了。</p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pica.zhimg.com/v2-8ac7e64d68bb7505c22e3b743a505220_1440w.jpg?source=b1748391"
