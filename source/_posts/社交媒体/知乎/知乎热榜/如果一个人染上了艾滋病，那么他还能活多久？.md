不想上吊王承恩的回答<br><br><p data-pid="Y5Kp1a7k">看到这么多声称艾滋病可控、艾滋病人与普通人无异的回答真是让我大开眼界、叹为观止，我建议卫生健康委请知乎网友来编纂一版真正的《传染病学》，当然最好是请这些答主亲自感染一下艾滋病，然后给我们作一下正确的示范。</p><p data-pid="fgBEXZK2">————以下内容来源于虚假的《传染病学》————</p><h2>临床表现</h2><p data-pid="PgRn8ZWq">本病<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E6%25BD%259C%25E4%25BC%258F%25E6%259C%259F" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">潜伏期</a>较长，感染病毒后需2～10年才发生以机会性感染及<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E8%2582%25BF%25E7%2598%25A4" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">肿瘤</a>为特征的艾滋病。</p><p data-pid="La89v4yD">（一）<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E6%2580%25A5%25E6%2580%25A7%25E6%2584%259F%25E6%259F%2593" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">急性感染</a> 部分病人感染后2～6周，可出现一过性类似<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E4%25BC%25A0%25E6%259F%2593%25E6%2580%25A7%25E5%258D%2595%25E6%25A0%25B8%25E7%25BB%2586%25E8%2583%259E%25E5%25A2%259E%25E5%25A4%259A%25E7%2597%2587" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">传染性单核细胞增多症</a>的<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E7%2597%2587%25E7%258A%25B6" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">症状</a>，持续3～14天后进入<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/index.php%3Ftitle%3D%25E6%2597%25A0%25E7%2597%2587%25E7%258A%25B6%25E6%259C%259F%26action%3Dedit%26redlink%3D1" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">无症状期</a>，少数病人可持续发展。起病多急骤，有<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E5%258F%2591%25E7%2583%25AD" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">发热</a>、出汗、不适、<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E5%258E%258C%25E9%25A3%259F" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">厌食</a>、<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E6%2581%25B6%25E5%25BF%2583" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">恶心</a>、<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E5%25A4%25B4%25E7%2597%259B" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">头痛</a>、<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E5%2592%25BD%25E7%2597%259B" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">咽痛</a>及关节肌肉痛等症状，同时可有<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E7%25BA%25A2%25E6%2596%2591" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">红斑</a>样<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E7%259A%25AE%25E7%2596%25B9" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">皮疹</a>和淋巴结肿大，<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E8%25A1%2580%25E5%25B0%258F%25E6%259D%25BF" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">血小板</a>可减少，CD4：CD8比值下降或倒置。</p><p data-pid="7Ds1lXDe">（二）无症状感染 持续1～10年，平均5年，无自觉症状，仅血清抗HIV抗体阳性。</p><p data-pid="FWQz10B4">（三）艾滋病相关综合征 主要表现为持续性淋巴结肿大。全身包括<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E8%2585%25B9%25E8%2582%25A1%25E6%25B2%259F" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">腹股沟</a>有两处以上淋巴结肿大，持续三个月以上，且无其它原因可以解释。肿大的淋巴结多对称发生，直径1cm以上，质地韧，可移动，无<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E5%258E%258B%25E7%2597%259B" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">压痛</a>。部分病例4月至5年后，可发展为艾滋病。常伴有间歇性发热、<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E4%25B9%258F%25E5%258A%259B" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">乏力</a>、<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E7%259B%2597%25E6%25B1%2597" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">盗汗</a>、<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E6%25B6%2588%25E7%2598%25A6" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">消瘦</a>和<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E8%2585%25B9%25E6%25B3%25BB" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">腹泻</a>，<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E8%2582%259D%25E8%2584%25BE%25E8%2582%25BF%25E5%25A4%25A7" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">肝脾肿大</a>，亦可出现原因不明的<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E7%25A5%259E%25E7%25BB%258F%25E7%25B3%25BB%25E7%25BB%259F" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">神经系统</a>症状。</p><p data-pid="iKhQ1riD">（四）典型艾滋病（真性艾滋病、艾滋病全盛期）主要表现为由于免疫功能缺陷所导致的<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E7%25BB%25A7%25E5%258F%2591%25E6%2580%25A7" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">继发性</a>机会性感染或恶性肿瘤的症状。</p><p data-pid="YOHImyDK">1．机会性感染机会性感染是艾滋病患者最常见的且往往最初的临床表现。主要病原体有<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/index.php%3Ftitle%3D%25E5%258D%25A1%25E6%25B0%258F%25E8%2582%25BA%25E5%259B%258A%25E8%2599%25AB%26action%3Dedit%26redlink%3D1" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">卡氏肺囊虫</a>、<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/index.php%3Ftitle%3D%25E5%25BC%2593%25E5%25BD%25A2%25E4%25BD%2593%26action%3Dedit%26redlink%3D1" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">弓形体</a>、隐孢子虫、念珠菌、组织胞浆菌，<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E9%25B8%259F%25E5%2588%2586%25E6%259E%259D%25E6%259D%2586%25E8%258F%258C" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">鸟分枝杆菌</a>、<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E5%25B7%25A8%25E7%25BB%2586%25E8%2583%259E%25E7%2597%2585%25E6%25AF%2592" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">巨细胞病毒</a>、<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E7%2596%25B1%25E7%2596%25B9" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">疱疹</a>病毒等。其中卡氏肺囊虫性<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E8%2582%25BA%25E7%2582%258E" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">肺炎</a>最为常见，起病缓慢，以发热乏力、<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E5%25B9%25B2%25E5%2592%25B3" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">干咳</a>和进行性<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E5%2591%25BC%25E5%2590%25B8%25E5%259B%25B0%25E9%259A%25BE" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">呼吸困难</a>为主要症状，而肺部<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E4%25BD%2593%25E5%25BE%2581" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">体征</a>不明显。<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E8%25A1%2580%25E6%25B0%2594%25E5%2588%2586%25E6%259E%2590" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">血气分析</a>常有低氧<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E8%25A1%2580%25E7%2597%2587" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">血症</a>。诊断可作痰液检查及经支气管镜活检或<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E8%2582%25BA%25E6%25B3%25A1" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">肺泡</a>灌洗，必要时开胸活检。其它机会性感染临床表现常呈多系统、播散性、进行性和复发性<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E7%2582%258E%25E7%2597%2587" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">炎症</a>，可引起肺炎、<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E9%25A3%259F%25E7%25AE%25A1%25E7%2582%258E" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">食管炎</a>、<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E8%2582%25A0%25E7%2582%258E" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">肠炎</a>、<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E7%259B%25B4%25E8%2582%25A0" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">直肠</a>肛管炎，皮肤损害、<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E8%2584%2591%25E7%2582%258E" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">脑炎</a>、<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E8%2584%2591%25E8%2586%259C%25E7%2582%258E" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">脑膜炎</a>、<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/index.php%3Ftitle%3D%25E9%25A2%2585%25E7%25A5%259E%25E7%25BB%258F%25E7%2582%258E%26action%3Dedit%26redlink%3D1" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">颅神经炎</a>甚至<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E5%2585%25A8%25E8%25BA%25AB%25E6%2580%25A7%25E6%2584%259F%25E6%259F%2593" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">全身性感染</a>等，并常有多种感染及肿瘤同时存在，使临床表现复杂多样。</p><p data-pid="y8Ck49pN">2．恶性肿瘤</p><p data-pid="Kf3BfDF-">（1）卡氏肉瘤　最为常见，多见于青壮年，起病缓隐袭，肉瘤呈多灶性，不痛不痒，除皮肤广泛损害外，常累及<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E5%258F%25A3%25E8%2585%2594" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">口腔</a>、吸肠道、<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E6%25B7%258B%25E5%25B7%25B4" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">淋巴</a>等。</p><p data-pid="JDdvhWwu">（2）其它恶性肿瘤包括<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E5%258E%259F%25E5%258F%2591%25E6%2580%25A7" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">原发性</a>脑淋巴瘤、<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E4%25BD%2595%25E6%259D%25B0%25E9%2587%2591%25E6%25B0%258F%25E7%2597%2585" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">何杰金氏病</a>，<a href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E9%259D%259E%25E4%25BD%2595%25E6%259D%25B0%25E9%2587%2591%25E6%25B7%258B%25E5%25B7%25B4%25E7%2598%25A4" class=" wrap external" href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E8%2587%25AA%25E8%25BA%25AB%25E5%2585%258D%25E7%2596%25AB%25E6%2580%25A7%25E8%25A1%2580%25E5%25B0%258F%25E6%259D%25BF%25E5%2587%258F%25E5%25B0%2591%25E6%2580%25A7%25E7%25B4%25AB%25E7%2599%259C" href="http://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.a-hospital.com/w/%25E9%2597%25B4%25E8%25B4%25A8%25E6%2580%25A7%25E8%2582%25BA%25E7%2582%258E" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">间质性肺炎</a>等。</p><p data-pid="C68ybAAr">HIV感染者在5年内有20～50%发展为艾滋病相关综合征，10～30%发展为典型艾滋病。一旦发生并发有机会性感染及恶性肿瘤的典型艾滋病，则预后极差。发病后1年病死率50%以上，4～5年几近100%。</p>
