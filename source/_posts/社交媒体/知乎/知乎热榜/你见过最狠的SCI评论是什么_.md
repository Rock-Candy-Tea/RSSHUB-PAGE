自然卷的回答<br><br><p>1.The results are as weak as wet noodles.（结论跟湿面条一样软弱无力）</p><p>2.The authors are amateur.（作者怕不是个兼职的）</p><p>3.It is a bit for me that authors have used Python statistical analysis instead of SPSS or Matlab as usual.Please,explain.（本文用了Python而不是像通常用SPSS和Matlab处理数据，为啥）</p><p>4.Did you have seizure while writing this sentence?Because I fell like I had one while reading it.（你写文章的时候犯癫痫了吗？反正我觉得我读的时候犯了）</p><p>5.Why chase a gene in this ridiculous organism?（为什么在这种荒诞的生物里追求一个基因）</p><p>6.This paper introduce tools to answer questions which it does not seem many people are interested in.（本文介绍了些解决问题的工具，然而人们并不感兴趣）</p><p>7.The English language ranks this manuscript among the top 5 worst manuscripts I have ever reviewed.（这篇文章的英语水平可以排进我看过的最烂的前五）</p><p>8.I started reading this manuscript with much anticipation.But my enthusiasm was short lived.（我满怀期待读这篇文章，但热情很短）</p><p>9.I have rarely a more blown-up and annoy paper in the last couple of years than this hot-air balloon manuscript.（我过去几年都没读过这篇像屁一样烦人的文章）</p><p>10.Figure3.This figure is silly.（这图好蠢呐）</p><p>11.This paper makes no contribution.（这文章有锤子贡献）</p><p>12.But now, there are over 1000 articles on this topic. But this author have not read a single.（这个方向至少有一千篇文章，作者没读过一篇）</p><p>更一下</p><p>13.Reject-More holes than my grandads' string vest.（拒了，漏洞比我爷爷的背心上的网眼还多）</p><p>14.The biggest problem with manuscript, which has nearly sucked the will to live out of me, is the terrible writing style.（这篇稿子审得我都想自挂东南枝了）</p><p>15.This paper is desperate. Please reject it complete and then block The author's email ID so they can't use The online system future.（文章太差了，麻烦封掉作者的账号以免他以后再投稿）</p><p>16.The writing and data presentation are so bad that I had to leave and go home early and then spend time to wonder what life is about.（手稿和数据显示让我整个人都不好了，不得不提前下班，开始怀疑人生）</p><p>17.The presentation is of a standard that I would reject from an undergraduate
