卢健龙的回答<br><br><p data-pid="8fr6U-fx">单纯从数学的角度讲，洛必达法则的适用是有严格条件的，<b>在对问题适用性不加以证明的情况下</b>盲目使用洛必达法则很容易得到错误的结果。</p><p data-pid="zr-Az88R">举一个简单的反例：</p><p data-pid="V-_ZVJNn">考虑函数 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29+%3D+x+%2B+%5Csin%28x%29%5Ccos%28x%29" alt="f(x) = x + \sin(x)\cos(x)" eeimg="1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"> 和 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%28x%29+%3D+e%5E%7B%5Csin%28x%29%7D%5Cbig%5Bx+%2B+%5Csin%28x%29%5Ccos%28x%29%5Cbig%5D" alt="g(x) = e^&#123;\sin(x)&#125;\big[x + \sin(x)\cos(x)\big]" eeimg="1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"> 。</p><p data-pid="HRG8ZRkV">显然的，我们有</p><p data-pid="MKvfB4wa"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bx%5Crightarrow+%5Cinfty%7Df%28x%29+%5Crightarrow+%5Cinfty+" alt="\lim_&#123;x\rightarrow \infty&#125;f(x) \rightarrow \infty " eeimg="1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"> ，</p><p data-pid="M8eIuhMU"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bx%5Crightarrow+%5Cinfty%7Dg%28x%29+%5Crightarrow+%5Cinfty" alt="\lim_&#123;x\rightarrow \infty&#125;g(x) \rightarrow \infty" eeimg="1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"> ，</p><p data-pid="gE11YdzX"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Bf%28x%29%7D%7Bg%28x%29%7D%3De%5E%7B-%5Csin%28x%29%7D" alt="\frac&#123;f(x)&#125;&#123;g(x)&#125;=e^&#123;-\sin(x)&#125;" eeimg="1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"> 。</p><p data-pid="ouNEK1Sq">其中 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Bf%28x%29%7D%7Bg%28x%29%7D%3De%5E%7B-%5Csin%28x%29%7D" alt="\frac&#123;f(x)&#125;&#123;g(x)&#125;=e^&#123;-\sin(x)&#125;" eeimg="1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"> 在 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5Crightarrow+%5Cinfty" alt="x\rightarrow \infty" eeimg="1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"> 的时候不存在极限，但如果对 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bx%5Crightarrow+%5Cinfty%7D+%5Cfrac%7Bf%28x%29%7D%7Bg%28x%29%7D" alt="\lim_&#123;x\rightarrow \infty&#125; \frac&#123;f(x)&#125;&#123;g(x)&#125;" eeimg="1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"> 盲目使用洛必达法则，就会得到</p><p data-pid="WFNJe8bs"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bx%5Crightarrow+%5Cinfty%7D+%5Cfrac%7Bf%E2%80%99%28x%29%7D%7Bg%E2%80%99%28x%29%7D+%3D+%5Clim_%7Bx%5Crightarrow+%5Cinfty%7D+%5Cfrac%7B2%5Ccos%28x%29%7D%7Bx+%2B+%5Ccos%28x%29%5Csin%28x%29+%2B+2+%5Ccos%28x%29%7De%5E%7B-%5Csin%28x%29%7D+%3D+0" alt="\lim_&#123;x\rightarrow \infty&#125; \frac&#123;f’(x)&#125;&#123;g’(x)&#125; = \lim_&#123;x\rightarrow \infty&#125; \frac&#123;2\cos(x)&#125;&#123;x + \cos(x)\sin(x) + 2 \cos(x)&#125;e^&#123;-\sin(x)&#125; = 0" eeimg="1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"> ，</p><p data-pid="PLxLjkg2">于是错误的结果就产生了。</p><p data-pid="dcTZUM9U">所以在理直气壮地使用洛必达法则（以及其他数学定理）之前，请扪心自问一句：</p><p data-pid="pWHP6_4P"><b>这个定理在你面对的问题中的适用性，你证明过了吗？</b></p><p></p><p></p><p></p><p></p>
