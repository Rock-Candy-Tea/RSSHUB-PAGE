予一人的回答<br><br><p></p><p data-pid="Cy1HHLyA">这里利用<b>无穷递降</b>的思想给出与常见证法不同的另一种<b>反证法</b>。</p><p data-pid="G7MTX9oW">设若 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csqrt%7B2%7D" alt="\sqrt&#123;2&#125;" eeimg="1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"> 是有理数，可记 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csqrt%7B2%7D%3D%5Cfrac%7Bm%7D%7Bn%7D" alt="\sqrt&#123;2&#125;=\frac&#123;m&#125;&#123;n&#125;" eeimg="1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"> 也即 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m%5E2%3D2n%5E2%2C" alt="m^2=2n^2," eeimg="1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"> 其中 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m%2Cn%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BZ_%2B%7D." alt="m,n\in \mathbb&#123;Z_+&#125;." eeimg="1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">  很清楚，由于 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1%3C2%3C4%2C" alt="1<2<4," eeimg="1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"> 所以 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1%3C%5Csqrt%7B2%7D%3D%5Cfrac%7Bm%7D%7Bn%7D%3C2%2C" alt="1<\sqrt&#123;2&#125;=\frac&#123;m&#125;&#123;n&#125;<2," eeimg="1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"> 于是 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0%3Cm-n%3Cn." alt="0<m-n<n." eeimg="1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"> </p><p data-pid="EgCeIrw3">接下来，注意到 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Bm%7D%7Bn%7D%3D%5Cfrac%7B2n-m%7D%7Bm-n%7D%2C" alt="\frac&#123;m&#125;&#123;n&#125;=\frac&#123;2n-m&#125;&#123;m-n&#125;," eeimg="1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"> 这表明 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csqrt%7B2%7D" alt="\sqrt&#123;2&#125;" eeimg="1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"> 除可表为 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Bm%7D%7Bn%7D" alt="\frac&#123;m&#125;&#123;n&#125;" eeimg="1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"> 外，还可表为 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Bp_1%7D%7Bq_1%7D%2C" alt="\frac&#123;p_1&#125;&#123;q_1&#125;," eeimg="1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"> 其中 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m%3E2n-m%3Dp_1%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BZ_%2B%7D%2C%7En%3Em-n%3Dq_1%5Cin%5Cmathbb%7BZ_%2B%7D." alt="m>2n-m=p_1\in \mathbb&#123;Z_+&#125;,~n>m-n=q_1\in\mathbb&#123;Z_+&#125;." eeimg="1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"> </p><p data-pid="dprZ8tzk">重复这样的论证，就可求得一列无限延伸的等式</p><p data-pid="J7QCqggS"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csqrt%7B2%7D%3D%5Cfrac%7Bm%7D%7Bn%7D%3D%5Cfrac%7Bp_1%7D%7Bq_1%7D%3D%5Cfrac%7Bp_2%7D%7Bq_2%7D%3D%5Ccdots%2C%5C%5C" alt="\sqrt&#123;2&#125;=\frac&#123;m&#125;&#123;n&#125;=\frac&#123;p_1&#125;&#123;q_1&#125;=\frac&#123;p_2&#125;&#123;q_2&#125;=\cdots,\\" eeimg="1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"> 其中 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m%3Ep_1%3Ep_2%3E%5Ccdots%2C%7E%7E%7E%7E%7E%7E%7En%3Eq_1%3Eq_2%3E%5Ccdots%2C%5C%5C" alt="m>p_1>p_2>\cdots,~~~~~~~n>q_1>q_2>\cdots,\\" eeimg="1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"> 但这明显不可能，因为 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Bp_n%5C%7D%2C%5C%7Bq_n%5C%7D" alt="\&#123;p_n\&#125;,\&#123;q_n\&#125;" eeimg="1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"> 这样的正整数序列充其量只能减少到 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1%2C" alt="1," eeimg="1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"> 不可能无限递降下去，于是推翻反设，命题得证。</p><p data-pid="p5CFUoor">事实上，将上述证法稍加改造，即可推广地证明：</p><blockquote data-pid="KE6-ahU1">若 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n" alt="n" eeimg="1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"> 不是完全平方数，则 <img
