<hr><p>披萨的好处是啥都能往上放，坏处也是，当回过神来时，做出来的东西已经脱离了人类味觉的极限。<br>发酵24小时后的面团，更硬，但空气炸锅无法烤脆，下次还是别放24小时了，发起来就做成软底披萨吧。<br>菠萝，应该切成薄片放在最上方以便烤熟，或者炒熟后再放上去（但更明智的做法是再也别吃菠萝了）<br>200°烤30分钟更像熟的。</p><br><img src="https://img.pawoo.net/media_attachments/files/108/870/454/225/353/767/original/c6a1fb019b1f38a0.jpeg" alt="image " referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://img.pawoo.net/media_attachments/files/108/870/454/846/827/143/original/ed6bc0aa619e7b7e.jpeg" alt="image " referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://img.pawoo.net/media_attachments/files/108/870/455/269/446/319/original/94b7e5d8342f5928.jpeg" alt="image " referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><img src="https://img.pawoo.net/media_attachments/files/108/870/455/717/223/689/original/9351a19fdaa29946.jpeg" alt="image " referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
