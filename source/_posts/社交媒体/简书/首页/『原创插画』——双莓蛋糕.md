<div class="image-caption"></div>
</div><blockquote>①线稿   这是一张和完成图有出入的线稿，请大家自觉忽略，，，先画出双层蛋糕的轮廓，再给蛋糕加一些装饰，就像做蛋糕一样，嘻嘻，右下角放了色卡哦。</blockquote><div class="image-package">
<div class="image-container">
<div class="image-container-fill"></div>
<div class="image-view" data-width="411" data-height="583"><img data-original-src="//upload-images.jianshu.io/upload_images/10888987-9e3f716c39ce7d42.png" data-original-width="411" data-original-height="583" data-original-format="image/png" data-original-filesize="98001" src="https://upload-images.jianshu.io/upload_images/10888987-9e3f716c39ce7d42.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></div>
</div>
<div class="image-caption"></div>
</div><blockquote>②铺色  蛋糕分两层铺，再细致一点可以把蛋糕的面再分一层铺色，画好看不出来区别，但是画到后面给蛋糕画装饰会比较好画。</blockquote><div class="image-package">
<div class="image-container">
<div class="image-container-fill"></div>
<div class="image-view" data-width="413" data-height="581"><img data-original-src="//upload-images.jianshu.io/upload_images/10888987-701c925f248c1162.png" data-original-width="413" data-original-height="581" data-original-format="image/png" data-original-filesize="215916" src="https://upload-images.jianshu.io/upload_images/10888987-701c925f248c1162.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></div>
</div>
<div class="image-caption"></div>
</div><blockquote>③底层蛋糕画上深浅的层次，是各种口味的夹心，给蛋糕上的水果铺色，这一步要有耐心，零碎的会很多，比较花时间。配色，摆放位置，遮挡关系都需要注意。</blockquote><div class="image-package">
<div class="image-container">
<div class="image-container-fill"></div>
<div class="image-view" data-width="411" data-height="583"><img data-original-src="//upload-images.jianshu.io/upload_images/10888987-f951a59c7bd6715f.png" data-original-width="411" data-original-height="583" data-original-format="image/png" data-original-filesize="261391" src="https://upload-images.jianshu.io/upload_images/10888987-f951a59c7bd6715f.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></div>
</div>
<div class="image-caption"></div>
</div><blockquote>④蛋糕面上用喷枪喷出蛋糕铺面的效果，画上装饰植物主干。上层蛋糕体有些空，画上一些小樱花，降低透明度，很美的效果。</blockquote><div class="image-package">
<div class="image-container">
<div class="image-container-fill"></div>
<div class="image-view" data-width="411" data-height="581"><img data-original-src="//upload-images.jianshu.io/upload_images/10888987-8afac478106a228c.png" data-original-width="411" data-original-height="581" data-original-format="image/png" data-original-filesize="309856" src="https://upload-images.jianshu.io/upload_images/10888987-8afac478106a228c.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></div>
</div>
<div class="image-caption"></div>
</div><blockquote>⑤继续优化水果部分，画的更紧凑些，画上植物叶片，在蛋糕托盘上画出植物的影子。</blockquote><div class="image-package">
