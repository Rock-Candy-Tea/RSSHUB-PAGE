font-size: 14px;">人们一直认为，金星高压、高温，其表面几乎不可能存在生命。去年 9 月，科学家宣布金星大气层中可能存在磷化氢气体，这是生命存在的一种指标。因此，有研究人员好奇金星云层中是否可能存在细菌等生命。</span></span></p><p data-track="4" class="syl-page-br" style="margin-top: 18px; margin-bottom: 18px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: rgb(89, 89, 89); --tt-darkmode-color: #595959; font-size: 14px;">科学家们可能想降低期望值。期刊《自然·天文学》上的一项新研究表明，金星上没有足够的水能让云层中的已知生命存活。</span></span></p><p data-track="6" class="syl-page-br" style="margin-top: 18px; margin-bottom: 18px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: rgb(34, 34, 34); font-family: "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", "Helvetica Neue", Arial, sans-serif; white-space: normal;"><br></p><p data-track="7" style="margin-top: 20px; margin-bottom: 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: rgb(34, 34, 34); font-family: "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", "Helvetica Neue", Arial, sans-serif; white-space: normal;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: rgb(89, 89, 89); --tt-darkmode-color: #595959; margin-bottom: 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: rgb(34, 34, 34); font-family: "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", "Helvetica Neue", Arial, sans-serif; white-space: normal;"><span style="margin: 0px; margin-bottom: 18px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: rgb(34, 34, 34); font-family: "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", "Helvetica Neue", Arial, sans-serif; white-space: normal;"><br></p><p data-track="13" style="margin-top: 20px; margin-bottom: 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: rgb(34, 34, 34); font-family: "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", "Helvetica Neue", Arial, sans-serif; white-space: normal;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: rgb(89, 89, 89); --tt-darkmode-color: #595959; font-size: 14px;">快进到 2020 年，金星大气层中探测到了磷化氢的潜在踪迹。科学家由此设想了一个场景：金星浓硫酸云层中潜在的水循环能够让金星上的微生物在高海拔的水滴中生存，并产生潮湿的孢子，从而继续繁殖生命的周期。虽然金星的表面如同地狱，但其云层是稳定温和的。</span></span></p><p data-track="14" class="syl-page-br" style="margin-top: 18px; margin-bottom: 18px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: rgb(34, 34, 34); font-family: "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", "Helvetica Neue", Arial, sans-serif; white-space: normal;"><br></p><p data-track="15" style="margin-top: 20px; margin-bottom: 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: rgb(34, 34, 34); font-family: "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", "Helvetica Neue", Arial, sans-serif; white-space: normal;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: rgb(89, 89, 89); --tt-darkmode-color: #595959; font-size: 14px;">但是，最新的研究论文却表明存活的可能性很小。该研究的重点是 “水的活度”，也就是微生物需要的水量，以 0 到 1 的标准来衡量。</span></span></p><p data-track="16" class="syl-page-br" style="margin-top: 18px; margin-bottom: 18px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: rgb(34, 34, 34); font-family: "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", "Helvetica Neue", Arial, sans-serif; white-space: normal;"><br></p><p data-track="17" style="margin-top: 20px; margin-bottom: 18px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: rgb(34, 34, 34); font-family: "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", "Helvetica Neue", Arial, sans-serif; white-space: normal;"><br></p><p data-track="19" style="margin-top: 20px; margin-bottom: 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: rgb(34, 34, 34); font-family: "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", "Helvetica Neue", Arial, sans-serif; white-space: normal;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: rgb(89, 89, 89); --tt-darkmode-color: #595959; white-space: normal;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: rgb(89, 89, 89); --tt-darkmode-color: #595959; white-space: normal;"><br></p><p data-track="27" style="margin-top: 20px; margin-bottom: 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: rgb(34, 34, 34); font-family: "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", "Helvetica Neue", Arial, sans-serif;
