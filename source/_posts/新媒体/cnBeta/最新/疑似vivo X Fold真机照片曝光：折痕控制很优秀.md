今天，酷安网用户良风爆料，称看到了一款带着保密壳的折叠屏手机，<strong>从近期各大厂商的宣发信息来看，这款手机很有可能是vivo X Fold。</strong>根据照片，这款折叠屏手机整体设计较为方正，<strong>并且折痕控制的很不错，在大仰角下依旧几乎看不到明显痕迹。</strong><br>
 <p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220314/fd6e5bc152844db38a7ff79f152df794.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0314/8a5a35902cc7f79.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220314/fa330e0eeded40798b835e5c061d0d26.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0314/905343a026b26a7.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>根据此前的消息，vivo计划于下月发布旗下首款折叠屏<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://shouji.jd.com/" target="_blank">手机</a>X Fold，手机代号“蝴蝶”，<strong>在核心的铰链上有很大的技术突破</strong>，并且产品性能全面对标<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?k=WP0ZSPklWdlZyN4MTPklWYmYDO5IDNy0DZp9VZ0l2cmYiRyUSbvNmLsxWYtZnL3d3dGJTJGJTJBNTJzBHd0h" target="_blank">华为</a>、<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://samsung.jd.com/" target="_blank">三星</a>。</p><p>跟据@数码闲聊站此前爆料的信息，vivo X Fold将采用一块居中挖孔单<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C794%2C798%26ev%3D4155_92263%26sort%3Dsort_rank_asc%26trans%3D1%26JL%3D3_%25E7%2594%25B5%25E8%25A7%2586%25E7%25B1%25BB%25E5%259E%258B_%25E6%259B%25B2%25E9%259D%25A2%23J_crumbsBar" target="_blank">曲面</a>屏作为外屏，后置全焦段四摄模组，并且没有采用侧面指纹。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220314/d4b7c11457b74be4bcb3b58f4a80031c.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0314/4e21dcb444c6148.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>而在内屏上，vivo X Fold将采用一块来自三星的LTPO 120Hz高刷柔性屏，且覆盖了UTG超薄玻璃，用于增加内屏的强度。</p><p>配置上，<strong>vivo X Fold在配置上将搭载高通8 Gen 1处理器</strong>、内置4600mAh的电池，支持80W有线和50W无线快充。</p><p>目前，vivo X Fold已经获得了入网许可，并得到了3C认证。</p>
