<strong>Native Union 本周推出了 </strong><a href="https://www.nativeunion.com/products/airpods-beanies" target="_blank"><strong>AirPods Beanies</strong></a><strong>，这是一款仿照经典 iPod Sock 设计的 AirPods 新便携包。</strong>AirPods Beanies 提供了双色针织设计，有 Glacier, Peach, Indigo 和 Sage 四种颜色。该尺寸适合所有 AirPods 型号，包括初代 AirPods、AirPods 2、AirPods 3和AirPods Pro。<br>
