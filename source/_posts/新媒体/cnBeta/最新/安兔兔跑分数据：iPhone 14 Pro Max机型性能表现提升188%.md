<strong>今天，搭载苹果最新一代A16处理器的iPhone 14 Pro及iPhone 14 Pro Max两款机型正式发售</strong>，安兔兔也同步更新了这两款机型的跑分成绩。根据跑分成绩来看，<strong>iPhone 14 Pro的总分为978147分，iPhone 14 Pro Max的总分则达到了972936分。</strong><br>
 <p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220913/146f45897aee4098b75c8d0460ac8b3f.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0913/8a2ece0060dfa42.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>与此前的<a data-link="1" href="https://apple.pvxt.net/c/1251234/435400/7639?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.apple.com%2Fcn%2Fiphone%2F" target="_blank">iPhone</a> 13 Pro系列机型相比，<strong>A16为iPhone 14 Pro系列带来了18.8%左右的涨幅，相当出众。</strong></p><p>结合具体数据来看，A16相比A15在CPU性能上提升了17%，GPU性能则提升了高达28%，是近年来<a data-link="1" href="https://apple.pvxt.net/c/1251234/435400/7639?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.apple.com%2Fcn%2Fmusic%2F" target="_blank">苹果</a>A系处理器GPU性能提升最高的一次。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220913/c9284066b658462f993a877ab7e2ea87.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0913/65180644a573f9f.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0913/65180644a573f9f.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0913/65180644a573f9f.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>据悉，<strong>A16处理器采用4nm工艺，CPU依然2性能核+4效能核，GPU是5核，AI引擎还是16核，晶体管数量比A15增加6.6%、每秒操作次数增加8%、主频增加7%。</strong></p><p>同时，根据苹果官方说法，A16的大小核在同性能下，能够降低20%的功耗。</p><div class="article-relation"><p><strong>相关文章:</strong></p><p><a href="https://www.cnbeta.com/articles/tech/1315497.htm" target="_blank">台积电4nm工艺外 A16 Bionic性能提升的另一因素是采用新核心</a></p><p><a href="https://www.cnbeta.com/articles/tech/1315543.htm" target="_blank">IPC仅提升1% 苹果A16处理器性能狂挤牙膏</a></p></div>
