荣耀70系列已定于5月30日正式发布，届时将推出荣耀70、荣耀70 Pro以及荣耀70 Pro+三款机型。今日，知名数码博主 @WHYLAB 曝出了荣耀70的官方渲染图，<strong>将提供亮黑、流光水晶、墨玉青、冰岛幻境 4 款配色</strong>。<br><br>
<p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220520/b7ef7e6120bb4ac2a4b5e8c2e98125ee.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0520/22acfebf71bcc4f.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0520/22acfebf71bcc4f.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0520/22acfebf71bcc4f.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>从渲染图中可以看出，<strong>荣耀70标准版采用了双<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C794%2C798%26ev%3D4155_92263%26sort%3Dsort_rank_asc%26trans%3D1%26JL%3D3_%25E7%2594%25B5%25E8%25A7%2586%25E7%25B1%25BB%25E5%259E%258B_%25E6%259B%25B2%25E9%259D%25A2%23J_crumbsBar" target="_blank">曲面</a>屏的设计，正面屏占比极高，继承了前代的居中打孔前置摄像头的设计</strong>。</p><p>在荣耀70机身背部，<strong>保留了上一代经典的双环镜头，后置三摄像头+闪光灯模组居于左上角</strong>，<strong>其主摄像头将首发搭载1/1.49英寸的索尼IMX800传感器</strong>。</p><p>据悉，<strong>荣耀70标准版将搭载骁龙7 Gen 1移动平台，基于台积电4nm制程工艺，由4颗CortexA710大核和4颗CortexA510小核组成，GPU采用Adreno662，综合性能较上代骁龙778G提升明显，但不及骁龙870及天玑8100</strong>。</p><p>根据此前的爆料信息来看，荣耀70标准版将会搭载一块类钻排屏幕，由<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?k=nJ9QWa1VmJxYTPklWYmYDO5IDNy0DZp9VZ0l2cmYiJt92YuQmauc3d3ZkMlYkMlE0MlMHc0RHa9Q" target="_blank">京东</a>方独家供应，当然1920MHz的高频PWM调光技术也会有所搭载，同时辅以4800mAh电池，支持66W有线快充。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220520/53070bf41ef046328a7b5bba256def72.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0520/d4092ec75dbb99b.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0520/d4092ec75dbb99b.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0520/d4092ec75dbb99b.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
