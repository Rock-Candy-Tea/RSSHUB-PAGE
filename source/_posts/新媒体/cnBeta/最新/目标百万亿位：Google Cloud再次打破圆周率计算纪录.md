早在 2019 年，谷歌云服务就已经首次打破了圆周率（Pi）的计算纪录，并达到了 31.4 万亿位。<strong>不过在周三的官方新闻稿中，Google Cloud 再次宣布打破了百万亿位的圆周率计算纪录。</strong><br><br>
 <p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0609/8b7bff53c512dbd.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0609/8b7bff53c512dbd.jpg" alt="1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>Google Cloud 团队<a href="https://cloud.google.com/blog/products/compute/calculating-100-trillion-digits-of-pi-on-google-cloud" target="_self">表示</a>，其借助计算引擎（Compute Engine）服务完成了本次新挑战。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0609/f7cd5a204a9f7f9.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0609/f7cd5a204a9f7f9.jpg" alt="2.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p style="text-align: center;">计算机 π 计算历史</p><p>期间用到了 <a href="https://cloud.google.com/compute/docs/general-purpose-machines#n2_machines" target="_self">N2 机器家族</a>、<a href="https://cloud.google.com/compute/docs/networking/using-gvnic" target="_self">虚拟 NIC</a> @ <a href="https://cloud.google.com/compute/docs/networking/configure-vm-with-high-bandwidth-configuration" target="_self">100 Gbps</a> 出口带宽，以及平衡持久磁盘（<a href="https://cloud.google.com/compute/docs/disks" target="_self">balanced Persistent Disks</a>）等新功能。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0609/9a61d4fe02d462a.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0609/9a61d4fe02d462a.jpg" alt="3.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p style="text-align: center;">架构节点概述</p><p>最终 Google Cloud 计算到的百万亿位圆周率的最后 100 位数字为：</p><blockquote><p>4658718895 1242883556 4671544483 9873493812 1206904813
2656719174 5255431487 2142102057 7077336434 3095295560 。</p></blockquote><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0609/79b6013c45fa66b.png" alt="4.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p style="text-align: center;">（演示传送门：<a href="https://pi.delivery/" target="_self">pi.delivery</a>）</p><p>目前 Google Cloud 团队已同吉尼斯世界纪录官方取得了联系，以验证其最新壮举。</p>
