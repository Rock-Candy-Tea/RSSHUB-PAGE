9月23日，沈阳市沈北新区、浑南新区部分区域限制供电，甚至导致部分道路的红绿信号灯也没电了，产生严重拥堵(<a class="mydrivers_player" href="https://weibo.com/6124642021/KzXOCkvhh">视频</a>)。当地一商户业主表示，下午4点多，突然就停电了，一直到半夜11点多，一般的饭店酒店都没电，工人吃饭的地方都没有。<br>
<p>据沈阳市工信局工作人员透露，<strong>因电力缺口特别巨大，风电量少，火电储力不足，煤还要留着供暖，为保证电网安全，做出了限制供电的举措。</strong></p><p>此外因为，限电通知紧急，影响到部分居民、医院用电，相关部门正在协调此事，<strong>后续将全力保障民生用电</strong>，但工业可能会受到一些影响。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210926/239005d200bc4146bae46e03ddb9bef3.png" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0926/b6da4e2553f1561.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0926/b6da4e2553f1561.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0926/b6da4e2553f1561.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210926/af44d48e3d0a40f7bd39d1c93e739928.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0926/27bded7a3edc015.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0926/27bded7a3edc015.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0926/27bded7a3edc015.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210926/0243c565c72a45d8947a023f6a70d991.png" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0926/fd0d252b635e675.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0926/fd0d252b635e675.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0926/fd0d252b635e675.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210926/6db67307368c4a35beb67216bde2466e.png" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0926/f22147795e17e5c.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0926/f22147795e17e5c.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0926/f22147795e17e5c.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
