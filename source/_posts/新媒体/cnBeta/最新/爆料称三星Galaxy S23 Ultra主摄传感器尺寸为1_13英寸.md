今日消息，博主i冰宇宙在社交平台爆料，<strong>三星Galaxy S23 Ultra主摄是2亿像素，传感器尺寸为1/1.3英寸，和iPhone 14 Pro一致（iPhone 14 Pro主摄是4800万像素，尺寸为1/1.3英寸）。</strong><br>
 <p>不仅如此，Galaxy S23 Ultra主摄单位像素面积为0.6μm，<strong>光圈为f/1.7，对比上一代Galaxy S22 Ultra，提升幅度很小。</strong>PS：Galaxy S22 Ultra主摄为1.08亿像素，传感器尺寸为1/1.33英寸，单位像素面积0.8µm，光圈为f/1.8。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220912/c0ecb5b356ec4dfc9b8590e492af5281.png" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0912/9b1a481ec44acd1.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0912/9b1a481ec44acd1.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0912/9b1a481ec44acd1.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p style="text-align: center;">三星Galaxy S22 Ultra右</p><p>上述机型的传感器尺寸都不如<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&aid=450&euid=&t=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.mi.com%2F" target="_blank">小米</a>12S Ultra，后者首发索尼IMX989，<strong>拥有完整的1英寸超级大底，</strong>这意味着12S Ultra领先三星一代旗舰。</p><p>另外，传闻Galaxy S23 Ultra配备了1000万像素长焦摄像头，支持3倍光学变焦，采用6.8英寸微曲<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C794%2C798%26ev%3D4155_88234%26sort%3Dsort_rank_asc%26trans%3D1%26JL%3D2_1_0%23J_crumbsBar" target="_blank">OLED</a>屏幕，搭载高通骁龙8 Gen2旗舰处理器，电池为5000mAh。该机有望在明年年初登场。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220912/84fd67d576684ced8fd44a15e3215b4f.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0912/fd22cc9b3e23958.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0912/fd22cc9b3e23958.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0912/fd22cc9b3e23958.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
