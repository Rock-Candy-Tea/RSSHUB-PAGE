<strong>HBO打造的《西部世界》第四季今日首曝海报，海报封面写着“适应亦或死亡”，从头骨的眼眶中可以看到人类的城市。</strong>HBO美剧《西部世界》于上月公布了第四季预告，主演埃文·蕾切尔·伍德、坦迪·牛顿、“女武神”泰莎·汤普森、艾德·哈里斯回归，继第三季后，“小粉”亚伦·保尔也继续在第四季登场。<br>
 <p style="text-align:center"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/upimg/pic/2022/06/05/origin_202206051104244235.jpg"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0605/51336b8e14c1a8c.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p style="text-align: left;">本剧将在今年6月26日播出。</p><p style="text-align:center"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/upimg/pic/2022/06/05/origin_202206051105096952.jpg"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0605/3fddc390d009083.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0605/3fddc390d009083.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0605/3fddc390d009083.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p style="text-align: left;">在首支预告中揭露了各主角的近况，隐藏在人群中的女主，失落的“小粉”，还有“大冤种”威廉。值得一提的是，预告中一闪而过上世纪早期的城市街景，车中的匪徒在老爷车中手持汤普森射杀警察，看来这一季会有“黑帮世界”亮相。</p><p style="text-align:center"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/upimg/pic/2022/06/05/origin_202206051105177909.jpg"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0605/8328c921d60b0fc.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0605/8328c921d60b0fc.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0605/8328c921d60b0fc.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p style="text-align: center;"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/upimg/pic/2022/06/05/origin_202206051105179101.jpg"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0605/5d4cb4a3af432bf.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0605/5d4cb4a3af432bf.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0605/5d4cb4a3af432bf.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
