小米与徕卡合作的首款高端产品将在7月份推出，外界普遍猜测会是小米12 Ultra。日前，爆料大神Onleaks联手Zouton分享了小米12
Ultra的最新高清渲染图，360度外形一览无余。从正面来看，小米12
Ultra采用一块曲面挖孔屏，上下黑边几乎等宽，整机屏占比很高，颇具视觉冲击力。<br>
 <p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220607/eca0902d493a432ab70ee3c693d918b1.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0607/af2a6eb168bcc8f.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>背面则更激进，<strong>白色陶瓷机身的几乎整个上半部都被硕大的黑色相机模组占据，右下角是徕卡“可乐标”。</strong></p><p>从图上来看摄像头共四颗，其中3颗是圆形镜头，最下方一颗是矩形潜望式变焦镜头，主摄据说是第二代液态镜头，广角畸变更小。圆形结构上，还有三个开孔分别对应闪光灯以及辅助传感器（对焦/色温/ToF等）。</p><p>据称<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&aid=450&euid=&t=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.mi.com%2F" target="_blank">小米</a>12 Ultra的三围数据是161.9 x 74.3 x 9.5mm，重量234g。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220607/0cd8ba07998746cc93b50e59cd44e391.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0607/5c3f2194f54a585.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0607/5c3f2194f54a585.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0607/5c3f2194f54a585.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220607/c575ed78964a44ff9a7c4ded548801fc.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0607/aac9091c442d004.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0607/aac9091c442d004.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0607/aac9091c442d004.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220607/c32828f1765b492f922628b1623404aa.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0607/148da804317b7cc.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0607/148da804317b7cc.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0607/148da804317b7cc.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
