今天，知名数码博主@数码闲聊站爆料，称“蓝厂”vivo将推出首款折叠屏手机X Fold，并放出了手机的外观设计图。根据爆料的外观设计图，<strong>vivo X Fold将采用一块居中挖孔单曲面屏作为外屏，后置全焦段四摄模组，并且没有采用侧面指纹。</strong><br>
 <p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220307/b6d036ef6181470dbf2964975cba0972.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0307/3bc901fb914670f.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220307/913e2951dd694b87ae17e539ac816afe.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0307/2caf3103da18fd2.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0307/2caf3103da18fd2.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0307/2caf3103da18fd2.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>此前，数码闲聊站曾爆料，称X系列将推出一款定位超高端的超声波屏下指纹折叠屏<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://shouji.jd.com/" target="_blank">手机</a>，<strong>从目前的信息来看，<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://vivo.jd.com/" target="_blank">vivo</a> X Fold很可能就是这款超声波指纹手机。</strong></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220307/e8d3026855c545479d8808234efe47e4.png" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0307/33a35ab2f1341e7.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0307/33a35ab2f1341e7.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0307/33a35ab2f1341e7.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>在内屏上，根据此前显示器行业分析师Ross Young透露的消息，<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://samsung.jd.com/" target="_blank">三星</a>在今年一月左右，已经开始为vivo的首款折叠屏手机生产屏幕面板。</p><p>这块来自三星的面板大小为8.03英寸，<strong>为一块LTPO 120Hz高刷屏，且覆盖了UTG超薄玻璃，用于增加内屏的强度。</strong></p><p>目前，vivo X Fold的具体配置信息尚未公布，但数码闲聊站在爆料中提到，<strong>这款手机的硬件配置绝对是TOP水准，相信不会让用户失望。</strong></p>
