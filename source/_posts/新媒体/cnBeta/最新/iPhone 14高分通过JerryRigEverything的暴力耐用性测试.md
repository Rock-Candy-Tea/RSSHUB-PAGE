在三星 Galaxy Z Flip4 和 Galaxy Z Fold4 折叠屏新机后，<strong>油管知名毁机频道 JerryRigEverything，又分享了针对新上市的苹果 iPhone 14 智能机的暴力测试结果。</strong>可知作为 iPhone 13 的衍生 SKU，苹果仅针对 iPhone 14 加以微小的改进，所以我们对新机的耐刮擦、抗热和抵御弯折的性能表现也并不感到意外。<br>
<p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0920/0883be2f96dd289.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0920/0883be2f96dd289.jpg" alt="6.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>不过一项显著的硬件差异，就是 iPhone 14 的玻璃后盖变得更易拆卸、且维修成本显著降低。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><iframe src="//tv.sohu.com/s/sohuplayer/iplay.html?bid=384706280&autoplay=false&disablePlaylist=true" width="720" height="480" frameborder="0"></iframe></p><p style="text-align: center;">iPhone 14 Durability Test - APPLE FINALLY FIXED IT（<a href="https://tv.sohu.com/v/dXMvODIyMjQwNTMvMzg0NzA2MjgwLnNodG1s.html" target="_self">via</a>）</p><p>一如既往，本次耐用性测试依然从屏幕划痕开始上手，选用的配色版本为午夜黑。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0920/f38df7b8cc38459.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0920/f38df7b8cc38459.jpg" alt="1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>可知 iPhone 14 会在 6 级莫氏硬度下产生轻微划痕，且 7 级就会产生较深的刮槽。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0920/547d760fa5ae0a1.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0920/547d760fa5ae0a1.jpg" alt="2.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>铝制中框能够应付几次尖锐的刀刮，但后摄的所谓蓝宝石镜头盖竟然在 6 级硬度下就开始产生了刮痕。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0920/4bc81354a97cfa8.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0920/4bc81354a97cfa8.jpg" alt="3.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>接下来是“屏幕老化”测试，长时间火烤不出意料地导致了不可逆的坏点。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0920/67b8aecfa6af151.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0920/67b8aecfa6af151.jpg" alt="4.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>最后是暴力弯折测试，iPhone 14 并未在此项测试过后出现显著的弯曲。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0920/5bd04d30fe6baee.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0920/5bd04d30fe6baee.jpg" alt="5.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>综上所述，Zach 对 iPhone 14 的总体评价还是相当之高的。</p>
