苹果秋季新品发布会定档北京时间9月15日（周三）凌晨1点，在邀请函上，苹果通过AR实景互动的小心思来预热。不出意外的话，iPhone
13系列将是本次活动的绝对主角。其它可能的新品还有Apple Watch 7、AirPods 3、MacBook Pro 14/16、iPad
mini 6、iPad 9等。<br>
 <p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0908/9a9754ac947b93d.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0908/9a9754ac947b93d.jpg" title alt="44274-85987-210907-Siri-xl.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>考虑到Siri助手的“泄密传统”，老外也赶紧测试了一番，结果发现这次Siri学精明了，<strong>回应是“你不能赶消息，你得耐心等待”。原话“You can't hurry news. No, you'll just have to wait.”来自摩城唱片The Supremes组合的经典歌曲《You Can't Hurry Love》。</strong></p><p>之所以说Siri被认为是突破点，原因在于此前曾准确抢曝了<a data-link="1" href="https://apple.pvxt.net/c/1251234/435400/7639?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.apple.com%2Fcn%2Fmusic%2F" target="_blank">苹果</a>的春季发布会，在此次活动中，苹果发布了紫色<a data-link="1" href="https://apple.pvxt.net/c/1251234/435400/7639?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.apple.com%2Fcn%2Fiphone%2F" target="_blank">iPhone</a> 12、AirTag追踪器、M1处理器<a data-link="1" href="https://apple.pvxt.net/c/1251234/435400/7639?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.apple.com%2Fcn%2Fmac%2F" target="_blank">iMac</a>/<a data-link="1" href="https://apple.pvxt.net/c/1251234/435400/7639?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.apple.com%2Fcn%2Fipad%2F" target="_blank">iPad</a> Pro等。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210908/6213fa5dffd4464394f0e25d63d4ecd3.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0908/9957ab4b7dc8e0a.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0908/9957ab4b7dc8e0a.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0908/9957ab4b7dc8e0a.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
