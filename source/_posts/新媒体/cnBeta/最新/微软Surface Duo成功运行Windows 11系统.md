2月3日消息，开发者Gustave Monce成功<strong>将微软Surface Duo手机刷入Windows 11 ARM64操作系统</strong>，该手机出厂预装Android
10，现已推送升级到Android 11。此前这位开发者曾将Windows 10移植到了Lumia 950 XL手机上，后者预装Windows
10 Mobile（前身为Windows Phone），目前微软已经停止维护Windows 10 Mobile。<br>
 <p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/02/dbd27c0451c0d1e.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/02/dbd27c0451c0d1e.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>值得注意的是，运行<a data-link="1" href="https://microsoft.pvxt.net/x9Vg1" target="_blank">Windows</a> 11系统的Surface Duo目前有较多Bug，但我们已经见证了“Windows 11智能<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://shouji.jd.com/" target="_blank">手机</a>的诞生”。</p><p>公开资料显示，<strong>Surface Duo于2020年9月份上市，这是<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://www.microsoftstore.com.cn/" target="_blank">微软</a>Surface系列首款双屏手机。</strong></p><p><strong><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0203/6592a42cb864b99.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0203/6592a42cb864b99.jpg" title alt="02ea3824-1c50-4b8a-8c08-b6d81bbc500f[1].jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0203/bb7570f352ef778.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0203/bb7570f352ef778.png" title alt="f0af8500-6c75-4bdf-b482-2b300d172a79[1].png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0203/27869eeafd028d9.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0203/27869eeafd028d9.jpg" title alt="fc08dfdf-84af-4182-b664-c97517036c68[1].jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></strong></p><p>该机通过铰链将两块5.6英寸AM<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C794%2C798%26ev%3D4155_88234%26sort%3Dsort_rank_asc%26trans%3D1%26JL%3D2_1_0%23J_crumbsBar" target="_blank">OLED</a>屏连接在一起，每块显示屏的分辨率为1800×1350，展开之后即可获得8.1英寸的视野，分辨率为2700×1800，屏幕纵横比为3:2。</p><p>核心配置上，<strong>Surface Duo搭载高通骁龙855旗舰处理器</strong>，配备6GB内存，最高配备256GB存储，不支持5G。</p>
