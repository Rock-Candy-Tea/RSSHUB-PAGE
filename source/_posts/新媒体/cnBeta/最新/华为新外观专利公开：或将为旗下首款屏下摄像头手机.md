近日，根据国家知识产权局公开的信息，<strong>华为申请的一项手机外观专利正式获得授权</strong><strong>，授权公告号为“CN307142027S”。</strong>根据专利图片来看，这款这款手机后置镜头模组位于机身右侧，设计与目前市面上的产品并无明显区别，不过这款手机的正面屏幕却颇为引人瞩目。<br>
  <p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220307/50e0929924124767bef61a7687d283e8.png" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0307/6df6d496b63ff57.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0307/6df6d496b63ff57.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0307/6df6d496b63ff57.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220307/e0e7bd1fcb374718abb1fb994b74b628.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0307/b2900ee49ae1561.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>这款<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://shouji.jd.com/" target="_blank">手机</a>的正面为一块完整的全面屏，没有进行任何的打孔开槽，<strong>且从俯视图来看，它也并没有采用升降或滑盖等机械机构隐藏摄像头。</strong></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220307/a29882bc0a8b4d2da88e0585b3a56af2.png" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0307/65635ed11de3e1c.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0307/65635ed11de3e1c.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0307/65635ed11de3e1c.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>这意味着，此次通过专利的这款手机，<strong>很可能将会是<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?k=WP0ZSPklWdlZyN4MTPklWYmYDO5IDNy0DZp9VZ0l2cmYiRyUSbvNmLsxWYtZnL3d3dGJTJGJTJBNTJzBHd0h" target="_blank">华为</a>旗下的第一款采用屏下摄像头技术的，真全面屏手机。</strong></p><p>在其他方面，这款手机正面采用了<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C794%2C798%26ev%3D4155_92263%26sort%3Dsort_rank_asc%26trans%3D1%26JL%3D3_%25E7%2594%25B5%25E8%25A7%2586%25E7%25B1%25BB%25E5%259E%258B_%25E6%259B%25B2%25E9%259D%25A2%23J_crumbsBar" target="_blank">曲面</a>屏设计，后壳也同样采用了弧形过渡，整体观感较为圆润，且摄像头凸起较小，理论上能够带来较好的握持感受。</p><p>不过，<strong>一款手机从获批专利到正式投产，进入宣发阶段有着较长的时间跨度</strong>，这款手机具体何时能够与消费者见面尚不得而知。</p>
