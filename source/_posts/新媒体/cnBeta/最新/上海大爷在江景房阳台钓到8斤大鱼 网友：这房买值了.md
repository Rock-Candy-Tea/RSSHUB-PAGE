封控在家做什么？上海一位居住在江景房里的大爷居然玩起了钓鱼，而且手气爆棚。
网上流传的一则小视频显示，<strong>在一栋临江的楼房上，一位大爷淡定地坐在窗前阳台上，朝窗外的江水甩出鱼线，不久后就钓出一条大鱼，拿回家一称足有8斤重。</strong><br>
<p>视频中还有一位女子表示，在家封控已经一个月了，突发奇想在楼上钓鱼，没想到钓到这么大一条。</p><p><iframe src="//player.bilibili.com/player.html?bvid=BV1CZ4y1h7T8&page=1" width="750" height="480" frameborder="0"></iframe></p><p>这让众多钓友们心里可痒痒了，感叹大爷手气爆棚，网友更是直言这江景房买值了，不过也有人在怀疑这件事的真伪。</p><p>你觉得呢？</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/a68d77ecf93f49e3bdb2e2473475f7f5.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0505/e5a0482648518bc.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/4f9183a31f82408283b36d6bd6befea4.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0505/f7301c37d8ac663.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/becf4364dd074ba3a40c41a9b52c78c4.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0505/1399452743b9cd8.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/e61604e23db148448be02daa3f57aff0.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0505/57efb606e6d932d.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/60c4465015034e89ac22e17f29f90e85.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0505/1321e31057c0e37.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></a></p>
