小米12S Ultra发布前，<strong>雷军曾公开表示，这款手机将不会送测DoXMark</strong>，而在手机发布后，DxO也确实迟迟没有发布小米12S Ultra的评测结果。但今天，<strong>DoXMark官方微博发布了小米12S Ultra的开箱视频，并宣布正在进行测试，结果敬请期待。</strong><br>
 <p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220808/4425a5f87bd24785a739a0ddfa516239.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0808/9c44bc51c4e3a7b.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>根据DxO此前发表的声明来看，<strong>这款跨越千山来到法国的小米12S Ultra，大概率是其自行购买，并主动测评</strong>，这也让这次评测显得更加“公正”。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220808/66e08df9de0a439fa0aa9f80c105f15c.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0808/c77eac7a84a41b1.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>目前看来，<strong>小米12S Ultra绝对称得上是小米今年推出的，影像表现最好的一款<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://shouji.jd.com/" target="_blank">手机</a>。</strong></p><p>硬件方面，它后置三颗摄像头，包括超大底主摄、超广角和潜望式长焦，并支持OIS光学防抖。</p><p>软件方面，它加入了徕卡影像算法，支持8K电影大师徕卡视频滤镜，支持徕卡自然色彩和黑白滤镜。</p><p>核心配置上，小米12S Ultra则采用2K AM<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C794%2C798%26ev%3D4155_88234%26sort%3Dsort_rank_asc%26trans%3D1%26JL%3D2_1_0%23J_crumbsBar" target="_blank">OLED</a>柔性<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C794%2C798%26ev%3D4155_92263%26sort%3Dsort_rank_asc%26trans%3D1%26JL%3D3_%25E7%2594%25B5%25E8%25A7%2586%25E7%25B1%25BB%25E5%259E%258B_%25E6%259B%25B2%25E9%259D%25A2%23J_crumbsBar" target="_blank">曲面</a>屏，形态为中置挖孔，搭载高通骁龙8+旗舰处理器，支持67W有线闪充。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220808/0078e41f60dd4c05b384152f78511584.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0808/013190bf1d46e33.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0808/013190bf1d46e33.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0808/013190bf1d46e33.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
