与全新 MacBook Pro 和 AirPods 3 一起推出的，还有苹果抛光布，价格为 145 元。这款抛光布销售火爆，发货时间已经推迟至 2022 年。一些顾客已经拿到了这款产品，并分享了照片。<br>
 <p>推特上 Karagana 分享了抛光布实物照片，包装盒比抛光布还大，只有抛光布本体和一张介绍卡纸。这款抛光布质地柔软，可安全有效地清洁各款 Apple 显示屏，包括 Nano-texture 纳米纹理玻璃面板。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/10/d9692010e6718a3.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/10/d9692010e6718a3.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/10/5dbbd745fbf7790.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/10/5dbbd745fbf7790.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/10/512e434dab65298.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/10/512e434dab65298.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/10/03f34abb90ba262.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/10/03f34abb90ba262.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/10/2195bdd00590339.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/10/2195bdd00590339.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
