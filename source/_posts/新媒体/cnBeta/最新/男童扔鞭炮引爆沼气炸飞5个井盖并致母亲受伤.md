又有熊孩子往水下放鞭炮了，然后结果也是很严重的。<strong>1月24日，四川巴中上午10时许，平昌县一10岁男孩将鞭炮扔进广场下水道内，导致5个下水道井盖被炸飞，男孩及其母亲受伤倒地。消防员到场后迅速将伤者送医救治，随后利用水枪对爆炸井口进行稀释，防止二次事故发生。</strong><br>
 <p style="text-align: center;"><a class="mydrivers_player" href="https://weibo.com/6124642021/Lcmpl8UQ8"><iframe src="//interface.sina.cn/wap_api/video_location.d.html?cid=&table_id=&did=kyakumy2349386&vt=4&creator_id=9805&vid=44579040201&video_id=445790402&r=video.sina.cn%2Fnews%2F2022-01-24%2Fdetail-ikyakumy2349386.d.html" width="640" height="480" frameborder="0"></iframe></a></p><p>现场目击者透露，事故是因男孩贪玩将鞭炮投入广场下水道内，导致下水道内部沼气发生爆燃，附近5个井盖、地砖瞬间被炸飞，小孩及其母亲因来不及躲闪崩裂的地砖和井盖而被炸伤，现场群众立即拨打110、120、119报警。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/01/5ca55b07479c3e2.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/01/5ca55b07479c3e2.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>据了解，事发广场下面是附近小区的化粪池，大约2000平方米，广场上密布十几个井盖，其中5个被炸飞。目前，伤者正在医院接受治疗。</p><p>消防提示：春节、元宵节临近，孩子燃放烟花爆竹时，大人务必到场看护指导，同时一定要远离或避开污水井、化粪池和下水道口，以免引爆沼气造成伤害。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220125/0d9713dc6a6c470ba18edfd737cda52c.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0125/36e946c87d66ace.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></a></p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0125/73b8a5dd88fe22b.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0125/73b8a5dd88fe22b.png" title alt="c177c9e021114f0b952f6f5854a2b583.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0125/e200978c4a6ca83.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0125/e200978c4a6ca83.png" title alt="70f358d04aac410f970c1d2b1f5b318e.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0125/783943ee24029a4.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0125/783943ee24029a4.png" title alt="b45d1db804404370831362927ee3cced.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
