赶在明天下午14点苏姿丰博士的台北电脑展演讲前，适配Zen 4锐龙7000的X670系列主板已经提前抢跑了。已经泄露的型号有X670E
GODLIKE, X670E ACE, X670E Carbon WIFI, X670P-WIFI，其中X670E Carbon
WIFI采用18+2相90A直连供电，X670P-WIFI是14+2相80A供电。<br>
<p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220522/7c0de2f077a0417d8a342f8162b4460d.png" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0522/68fb2187e298be3.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0522/68fb2187e298be3.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0522/68fb2187e298be3.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>接口方面，虽然没有USB 4，但HDIMI 2.1、DP 1.4 HBR（<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C794%2C798%26ev%3D4155_110018%26sort%3Dsort_rank_asc%26trans%3D1%26JL%3D2_1_0%23J_crumbsBar" target="_blank">4K</a> 120Hz）、2.5G网口、支持Wi-Fi 6E等也都非常实用。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0522/860a1f4932ae17b.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0522/860a1f4932ae17b.png" title alt="FTWAAGzaUAAXNgv-very_compressed-scale-2_00x-740x495.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220522/9311d7c60ae6439e80bb5098936e5552.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0522/491a6648c84b4bb.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>根据现在的爆料，首批Zen 4锐龙7000将有X670E（Extreme）、X670和B650可选，<strong>其中透视图确认，X670为双芯（双南桥）设计，B650则是传统的单芯。双芯显然会带来更多发热，不过好处在于有着更稳定更多通道的PCIe 5.0、I/O接口等支持。</strong></p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0522/a094e7059ffef5b.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0522/a094e7059ffef5b.jpg" title alt="FTR6EPaagAAKpzX-very_compressed-scale-2_00x-740x306.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0522/4b1eb236d32f1fa.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0522/4b1eb236d32f1fa.png" title alt="FTR6CdBaAAEzIKY-low_res-scale-4_00x-740x315.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
