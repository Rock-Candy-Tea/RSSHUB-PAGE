这几天关于摩托罗拉新手机moto G Stylus的消息越来越多，Evan Blass更是直接放出了这款手机的促销视频。在视频中可以看到，moto G Stylus系列手机将配备手写笔，集成在底部边框的扬声器开孔旁边，不过看不出手写笔是否为伸缩设计。<br>
 <p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220116/13b2b15a-3cbc-4d44-a707-f06db68e10e8.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0116/8878ca51a748268.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0116/8878ca51a748268.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0116/8878ca51a748268.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>规格方面，moto G Stylus使用了一块6.8英寸的挖孔屏，开孔位置在屏幕正上方，屏幕刷新率为90Hz。<br></p><p>搭载高通骁龙480处理器，后置三摄拍摄组合，支持物理指纹识别，识别模块在<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://shouji.jd.com/" target="_blank">手机</a>的右边框，或将配备4500mAh电池，并搭载Android 12系统。</p><p>从配置上来看，moto G Stylus很可能是一款针对入门机市场推出的机型。作为少见的配备手写笔的手机，如果moto G Stylus销量出色，不排除摩托罗拉推出配备手写笔旗舰手机的可能。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0116/4adcf2a03b7f061.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0116/4adcf2a03b7f061.jpg" title alt="gsmarena_005.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0116/e82135726ed2c58.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0116/e82135726ed2c58.jpg" title alt="gsmarena_004.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0116/db21e23710e91b9.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0116/db21e23710e91b9.jpg" title alt="gsmarena_003.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0116/2b691c07da5cbad.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0116/2b691c07da5cbad.jpg" title alt="gsmarena_002.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
