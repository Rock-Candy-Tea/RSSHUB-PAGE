今天好评第三方开发者PMDG为《微软飞行模拟》制作了波音737-600飞机。除了上架时间出人意料之外，这款产品的价格也非常出人意料，标价的34.99美元比之前PMDG的暂定价格低了15美元。<br>
 <p>这个价格是PMDG 737-700飞机定价（69.99美元）的一半，区别就是新飞机要短一点，人气也要低一点。</p><p>开发者还发布了波音737-600的游戏截图，一起来欣赏一下：</p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0731/84fc1b911420a74.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0731/84fc1b911420a74.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0731/84fc1b911420a74.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0731/63ca8aeadbc2cc3.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0731/63ca8aeadbc2cc3.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0731/63ca8aeadbc2cc3.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0731/8a8229565e45bb7.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0731/8a8229565e45bb7.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0731/8a8229565e45bb7.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0731/eb18d293967efe2.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0731/eb18d293967efe2.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0731/eb18d293967efe2.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0731/7a65103a3e50153.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0731/7a65103a3e50153.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0731/7a65103a3e50153.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0731/fb899e166d5c381.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0731/fb899e166d5c381.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0731/fb899e166d5c381.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
