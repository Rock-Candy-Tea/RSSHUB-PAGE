今天，知名爆料人士evleaks曝光了ROG游戏手机6渲染图。如图所示，<strong>其正面为上下对称式设计，无刘海、无挖孔，是业界第一款无挖孔的骁龙8+电竞手机。</strong>背部共有三颗摄像头，<strong>呈横向排布，左下角是ROG的Logo，同时印有“腾讯游戏”标识，</strong>表明这款手机由ROG和腾讯双方联合研发，有黑色和白色两种配色，极具辨识度。<br>
 <p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0702/a7610d4d1a60352.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0702/a7610d4d1a60352.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0702/a7610d4d1a60352.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>核心配置上，ROG游戏<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://shouji.jd.com/" target="_blank">手机</a>6采用6.78英寸AM<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C794%2C798%26ev%3D4155_88234%26sort%3Dsort_rank_asc%26trans%3D1%26JL%3D2_1_0%23J_crumbsBar" target="_blank">OLED</a>全面屏，<strong>刷新率为165Hz，最高配备了18GB内存，电池容量为6000mAh，预装Android 12操作系统。</strong></p><p>此外，ROG游戏手机6将会搭载腾讯定制Solar Core游戏加速引擎，该加速引擎在游戏中通过分场景优化，让《王者<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?k=WP0ZSPklWdlZyN4MTPklWYmYDO5IDNy0DZp9VZ0l2cmYiRyUSbvNmLsxWYtZnL3d3dGJTJGJTJBNTJzBHd0h" target="_blank">荣耀</a>》、《和平精英》等热门手游，能够保持高画质、高帧率运行。</p><p>该机将于7月份正式发布。</p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0702/b38f2cd9dd1b2e1.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0702/b38f2cd9dd1b2e1.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0702/b38f2cd9dd1b2e1.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0702/54a9a9377066331.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0702/54a9a9377066331.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0702/54a9a9377066331.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0702/71e7e778b984bd1.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0702/71e7e778b984bd1.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0702/71e7e778b984bd1.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
