虽然配备M2芯片的新MacBook Air本周五正式发布，但一位幸运的客户提前一天收到了这款来自2022年的笔记本。这位顾客在Reddit上分享了一些照片，我们可以得以仔细观察这台银色外观的新MacBook Air真机。<br>
 <p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/07/63f398b6fecf74d.webp" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/07/63f398b6fecf74d.webp" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/07/07a0e1eafaa062b.webp" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/07/07a0e1eafaa062b.webp" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/07/de5a4d3754cc123.webp" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/07/de5a4d3754cc123.webp" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/07/29be0c7f08dfa09.webp" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/07/29be0c7f08dfa09.webp" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>苹果在今年重新设计了<a data-link="1" href="https://apple.pvxt.net/c/1251234/435400/7639?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.apple.com%2Fcn%2Fmac%2F" target="_blank">MacBook</a> Air，配备了稍大的13.6英寸显示屏、刘海、更平整的机身边缘、支持MagSafe充电、安装了升级的1080p摄像头，以及除银色和太空灰之外的新的星光和午夜颜色选择。该笔记本还配备了两个Thunderbolt 3端口和一个支持高阻抗耳机的3.5毫米耳机插孔。<br></p><p>这位客户的照片还完整展示了新MacBook Air的包装盒，里面有手册、苹果贴纸和一条颜色匹配的MagSafe 3充电线。</p><p>新款MacBook Air的首批媒体评测预计将在周五的笔记本发布前不久解禁。</p>
