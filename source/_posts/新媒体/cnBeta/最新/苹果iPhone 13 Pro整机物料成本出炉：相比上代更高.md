每年新iPhone发布上市后，不少网友最期待的一个项目，就是新机的物料成本是多少，现在我们来感受下。第三方拆解机构iFixit送出了iPhone13 Pro成本分析，当然也只是分析，毕竟整机不能这么计算，里面还包含了苹果的设计、运输、加工等多个环节。<br>
<p><strong>从iFixit公布的数据来看，<a data-link="1" href="https://apple.pvxt.net/c/1251234/435400/7639?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.apple.com%2Fcn%2Fiphone%2F" target="_blank">iPhone</a>13 Pro的BOM总成本为570美元，约合人民币3674元，去年发布的iPhone12 Pro的BOM成本大约为548.5美元（约合人民币3535元）。</strong></p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/10/d81109e03bfe5cb.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/10/d81109e03bfe5cb.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>作为对比，安卓阵营旗舰<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://samsung.jd.com/" target="_blank">三星</a>S21+的BOM成本则是508美元，约合人民币3274元。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/10/0edb6df828e19a5.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/10/0edb6df828e19a5.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>需要注意的是，iPhone 13 Pro国行的售价是7999元起步，最高的1TB版本达到了11999元。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/10/5770f85c8551783.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/10/5770f85c8551783.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/10/140339f6b9e27de.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/10/140339f6b9e27de.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/10/dc91d2ed7f9caa0.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/10/dc91d2ed7f9caa0.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/10/bbaa7e8afd6c2e4.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/10/bbaa7e8afd6c2e4.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/10/e83f1865b1edd72.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/10/e83f1865b1edd72.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
