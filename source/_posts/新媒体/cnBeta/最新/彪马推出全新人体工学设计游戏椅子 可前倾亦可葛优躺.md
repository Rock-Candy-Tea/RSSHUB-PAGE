著名运动品牌彪马近日宣布推出一款全新人体工学设计游戏椅子《Playseat PUMA Active Gaming Seat Red》，预定4月26日发售，玩家既可以前倾身体专注游玩，也可以“葛优”躺休闲玩游戏，一起来了解下。<br>
<p style="text-align:center"><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0420/f9a1b7239f95d4c.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0420/f9a1b7239f95d4c.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0420/f9a1b7239f95d4c.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p style="text-align: left;">·《Playseat PUMA Active Gaming Seat Red》号称采用最先端的人体工学设计，看上去平平无奇，实际上每个部件的角度都经过精心论证，游戏玩家坐在椅子上，不论是前倾身体专注游玩还是“葛优”躺休闲玩游戏都能找到最舒服姿势。</p><p style="text-align:center"><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0420/f49878379bdf514.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0420/f49878379bdf514.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0420/f49878379bdf514.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p style="text-align:center"><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0420/65925749f69f639.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0420/65925749f69f639.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0420/65925749f69f639.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p style="text-align:center"><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0420/dc8b6d798c5d693.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0420/dc8b6d798c5d693.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0420/dc8b6d798c5d693.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p style="text-align: left;">·椅子面材料采用高科技面料，强固且透气，适合玩家久坐而不累，《Playseat PUMA Active Gaming Seat Red》预定4月26日发售，感兴趣的玩家可以关注：官方页。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0420/b4f56142fa01573.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0420/b4f56142fa01573.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0420/b4f56142fa01573.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
