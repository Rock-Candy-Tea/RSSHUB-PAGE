自从B社官方公布《星空》消息已经过去了很长时间，最近有一些早期的素材被泄露在网络上。这些泄露的素材展示了两套太空服。根据报道，这些太空服将装备喷气背包。不仅如此，玩家还可以选择变成太空海盗。<br>
<p>《星空》是B社游戏工作室25年来推出的首个新宇宙。在这款设定在群星中的次世代RPG游戏中，玩家能创造他们想要的角色，无拘无束的探索，开启一个回答人类最大疑问的旅程。</p><p>《星空》还是首个采用Creation Engine 2引擎开发的游戏，其E3 2021预告片全部为游戏内画面，没有采用任何的电影工具。</p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1009/9fa4c40acd61096.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://img.3dmgame.com/uploads/images/news/20211009/1633780309_950331.png" alt="B社《星空》新图泄露 展示两套太空服" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1009/22e68e1fe9f0cd9.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1009/22e68e1fe9f0cd9.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/1009/22e68e1fe9f0cd9.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1009/3c15fff47f0c2e5.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1009/3c15fff47f0c2e5.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/1009/3c15fff47f0c2e5.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1009/824be961062e533.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1009/facacc7e305995a.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1009/4b8877337555959.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1009/fec2f7a96e39c26.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1009/86057a570a3d01a.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1009/1218c8d8eff3eb9.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1009/1218c8d8eff3eb9.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/1009/1218c8d8eff3eb9.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
