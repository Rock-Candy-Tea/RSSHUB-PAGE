来自TheElec的最新报道称，<strong>关于为iPhone 14制造OLED面板一事，京东方仍未获得苹果批准。</strong>此前，同一个消息源称京东方今年基本没为iPhone供货OLED，原因是“擅自”修改了iPhone 13所需OLED的薄膜晶体管线宽以提高收益，但遭到苹果“退货”。<br>
<p>报道称，尽管<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?k=nJ9QWa1VmJxYTPklWYmYDO5IDNy0DZp9VZ0l2cmYiJt92YuQmauc3d3ZkMlYkMlE0MlMHc0RHa9Q" target="_blank">京东</a>方委派高管向<a data-link="1" href="https://apple.pvxt.net/c/1251234/435400/7639?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.apple.com%2Fcn%2Fmusic%2F" target="_blank">苹果</a>沟通解释并试图争取<a data-link="1" href="https://apple.pvxt.net/c/1251234/435400/7639?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.apple.com%2Fcn%2Fiphone%2F" target="_blank">iPhone</a> 14的订单，可至今悬而未决。</p><p>就目前的迹象来看，iPhone 14的屏幕订单，京东方可能已基本出局，苹果甚至在考虑将原先规划的今年3000万块<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C794%2C798%26ev%3D4155_88234%26sort%3Dsort_rank_asc%26trans%3D1%26JL%3D2_1_0%23J_crumbsBar" target="_blank">OLED</a>（iPhone 12/13/14）订单，转交给<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://samsung.jd.com/" target="_blank">三星</a>和LG分享。</p><p><a Pro所需的6.1寸OLED，LG则将首次为苹果供货LTPO OLED，对象是6.7寸iPhone 14 Pro Max。</p><p>值得一提是，<strong>关于苹果取消京东方<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://shouji.jd.com/"
