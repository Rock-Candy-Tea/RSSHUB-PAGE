22岁的日本知名女星桥本环奈最近刚刚发布了《千与千寻》舞台剧定妆照，近日，据日媒报道，桥本环奈在去年10月在东京买一下一套价值约3亿日元的高级公寓，为此其惊人的收入也被广告人士曝光。<br>
 <p><a href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/11/20211118_zy_red_164_9/gamersky_10origin_19_2021111823409F7.jpg" target="_blank" style="font-size: 14px;"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1119/0d4508da2d8a119.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>桥本环奈在福冈以「Rev. from DVL」偶像女团的身份出道，随后一张宛如天使下凡的美照爆红，也让她收获了“千年一遇美少女”的称号。</p><p><a href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/11/20211118_zy_red_164_9/gamersky_01origin_01_202111182340A2E.jpg" target="_blank" style="font-size: 14px;"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1119/8ad5de65270d3c1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>据报道，还在高中时代桥本环奈就从福冈搬到东京打拼，前三年都是和经纪人合住，但是对居住环境要求相当苛刻的她，一赚钱就开始了自己的独居生活，并且还不断搬家寻找理想的居住环境。</p><p><a href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/11/20211118_zy_red_164_9/gamersky_09origin_17_20211118234036F.jpg" target="_blank" style="color: rgb(225, 29, 3); text-decoration-line: underline; font-size: 14px;"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1119/6075d9c38a3f5b2.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>据说已经搬了四、五次家的她，终于在去年的东京看到了理想的住所，并豪置3亿日元（约合1682万元人民币）买下了这栋超高级公寓。她曾表示，一有时间就忍不住看各种房子，然后想马上搬家！</p><p>在8月份参加电影《辉夜姬大小姐》的宣传节目上，她透露出一些居家习惯的强迫症小细节。比如：<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C794%2C798%26ev%3D4155_76344%26sort%3Dsort_rank_asc%26trans%3D1%26JL%3D2_1_0%23J_crumbsBar" target="_blank">电视</a>遥控器、<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C794%2C870%26ev%3D1554_584894%26JL%3D3_%25E7%25A9%25BA%25E8%25B0%2583%25E7%25B1%25BB%25E5%2588%25AB_%25E7%25AB%258B%25E6%259F%259C%25E5%25BC%258F%25E7%25A9%25BA%25E8%25B0%2583%23J_crumbsBar" target="_blank">空调</a>遥控器之类的都不拿出来，而是全部放进柜子里。纸巾也是这样。然后玄关那里一双鞋都不会放在明面上。</p><p><a href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/11/20211118_zy_red_164_9/gamersky_02origin_03_202111182340F4F.jpg" target="_blank" style="font-size: 14px;"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1119/b4902838352fe53.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>去参观过她家的演员浅川梨奈吐槽，“一点生活感都没有，就像样板房一样！”有人还爆料称，桥本环奈是演艺圈著名的爱酒人士，经常与小栗旬、室刚等人一同相聚品酒。但疫情爆发之后，只能居家独自饮酒，为了提升品酒环境，才特意选了理想的住户。</p><p><a href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/11/20211118_zy_red_164_9/gamersky_04origin_07_202111182340269.jpg" target="_blank" style="color: rgb(225, 29, 3); text-decoration-line: underline; font-size: 14px;"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1119/63aa80b7dd984a8.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>如此购置豪宅，也让人对桥本环奈的收入感到好奇。业内人士称：桥本环奈拍摄广告的费用大概是2500~3000万日元（约合140万~168万元人民币）一条，去年她拍摄了22条广告，这么计算下来，每年她光是拍广告就能进账超过5亿日元（约合2796万元人民币）。</p><p><a href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/11/20211118_zy_red_164_9/gamersky_03origin_05_20211118234044A.jpg" target="_blank" style="font-size: 14px;"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1119/4188ca17967017b.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>虽然不是全部收入她的囊中，但加上广告、电视剧、电影等的出场费，保守估计去年的年收入应该有2亿日元左右（约合1117万元人民币），无论是贷款还是购买公寓都绰绰有余。</p><p><a href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/11/20211118_zy_red_164_9/gamersky_05origin_09_20211118234074F.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1119/04dc674fa21ac07.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/11/20211118_zy_red_164_9/gamersky_06origin_11_202111182340DD7.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1119/fa3e7eeac412a2d.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/11/20211118_zy_red_164_9/gamersky_07origin_13_20211118234055F.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1119/deebec178633e32.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2021/11/20211118_zy_red_164_9/gamersky_08origin_15_202111182340BE7.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1119/4fc65c355f51991.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
