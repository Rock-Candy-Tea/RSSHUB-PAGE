由王晶执导、林峰、文咏珊、古天乐、云千千、甄子丹等位主演的新版《倚天屠龙记》第一部《倚天屠龙记之九阳神功》今日首播，刚刚豆瓣平台也公布了本片的用户评分。<br>
 <p><a target="_blank" href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/upimg/pic/2022/01/31/origin_202201312105187511.png"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0201/3607ebfb4f5e1c6.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0201/3607ebfb4f5e1c6.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0201/3607ebfb4f5e1c6.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>本片目前在豆瓣获得3.5分评分（2203人评价），1星率47.9%、4星率4.2%、5星率仅有0.7%。</p><p>大部分观众认为该片的化妆造型“很难看”，对原著的剧情也进行了魔改。武侠小说改编的电影在武术方面表现也一般，有网友直接评价该作是“包工头王晶带领中老年港星的一次集体再就业”。</p><p>部分用户评价：</p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/upimg/pic/2022/01/31/origin_202201311856038996.png"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0201/74a3bd65a5e3384.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0201/74a3bd65a5e3384.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0201/74a3bd65a5e3384.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/upimg/pic/2022/01/31/origin_202201311856107554.png"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0201/927d42b4c33d483.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0201/927d42b4c33d483.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0201/927d42b4c33d483.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
