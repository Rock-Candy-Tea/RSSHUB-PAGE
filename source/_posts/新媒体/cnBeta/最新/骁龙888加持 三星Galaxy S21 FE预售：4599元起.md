1月6日消息，据三星GALAXY盖乐世官微消息，三星Galaxy S21 FE现已开启预售，<strong>提供8GB+128GB、8GB+256GB两款配置，售价分别为4599元和4999元。</strong>ID设计上，三星Galaxy S21 FE延续S21系列外观方案，背部相机模组与后盖融为一体，拥有霜糖白、香提绿、浅芋紫、松露灰四款配色。<br>
 <p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220106/7c31ea5235484a9e9fecdf9ab149072e.png" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0106/d912b500b1eb777.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0106/d912b500b1eb777.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0106/d912b500b1eb777.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>三星Galaxy S21 FE配备一块6.4英寸AM<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C794%2C798%26ev%3D4155_88234%26sort%3Dsort_rank_asc%26trans%3D1%26JL%3D2_1_0%23J_crumbsBar" target="_blank">OLED</a>中置挖孔屏，支持120Hz高刷、240Hz触控采样率，康宁Victus大猩猩玻璃。</p><p>核心配置方面，<strong>三星Galaxy S21 FE搭载S21同款骁龙888芯片，</strong>内置4500mAh电池，支持25W有线快充、15W无线充电。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220106/8ea3f8664af24df5822a1ad0f1822410.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0106/2fff994c7e57f00.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0106/2fff994c7e57f00.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0106/2fff994c7e57f00.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>影像上，新机后置1200万像素超广角摄像头+1200万像素广角摄像头+800万像素长焦摄像头，支持30倍空间变焦，前置3200万像素自拍摄像头。</p><p>值得一提的是，与S21相比，<strong>S21 FE从1000万像素自拍镜头升级至3200万像素，但长焦镜头从6400万像素降至800万像素。</strong></p><p>另外，三星Galaxy S21 FE包装内不附送充电器，需用户自行购买。</p>
