目前，小米在国内的笔记本产品线已十分全面，小米笔记本、RedmiBook两大体系可满足不同消费者需求。前不久，小米在海外发布Xiaomi Book S，<strong>搭载高通骁龙8cx Gen2处理器，平板+键盘售价799欧元（约合5641元人民币），</strong>这也是小米首款二合一笔记本。<br>
 <p>今日，数码博主@数码闲聊站 透露，<strong>小米这款Xiaomi Book S二合一笔记本有备案国行版，配置为商务取向。</strong></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220622/11f3d805c1c04d39a311c49ac76773f6.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0622/68f082e86c2f404.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>据悉，<strong>Xiaomi Book S采用一块12.4英寸LCD屏，分辨率为2560* 1600，</strong>峰值亮度500尼特，拥有100% DCI-P3色域，支持触控、手写笔。</p><p>核心性能上，Xiaomi Book S搭载的骁龙8cx Gen2处理器采用<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://samsung.jd.com/" target="_blank">三星</a>7nm工艺，CPU由4个Kryo 495大核（Cortex-A76）+4个Kryo 495小核组成，频率3.15 GHz，热设计功耗7W。内置38.08Wh/4920mAh电池，支持65W快充。</p><p>同时，Xiaomi Book S内置一个USB-C和3.5mm接口，运行基于ARM平台的<a data-link="1" href="https://microsoft.pvxt.net/x9Vg1" target="_blank">Windows</a> 11操作系统，支持512 GB micro SD卡扩展。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220622/52e1e9f6eba04552877d598e1368ef82.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0622/b3b8dab8745c8e3.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220622/24d72f55de47478081df5e32488a646e.png" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0622/6415d7dce61f920.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0622/6415d7dce61f920.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0622/6415d7dce61f920.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220622/e78510765cca4b138728f377c2255531.png" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0622/485bc66b69d9ab9.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0622/485bc66b69d9ab9.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0622/485bc66b69d9ab9.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
