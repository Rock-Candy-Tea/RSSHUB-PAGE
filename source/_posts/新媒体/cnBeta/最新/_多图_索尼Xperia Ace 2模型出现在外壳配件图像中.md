<strong>最初被称为索尼Xperia Compact的传闻中的Xperia Ace
2外观模型出现在一系列的外壳配件图像中。</strong>一些人怀疑这款手机将是一款日本市场专用设备，但只有时间才能证明这是否是真的。外壳厂商揭示了这款新品背面的双摄像头模组，似乎机身还包含一个侧面的指纹识别器和相机快门的专用按钮。<br>
<p>但不幸的是，我们无法知道它是否会采用正面打孔设计或坚持一贯特色，继续设计较厚的上边框。</p><p>如果我们从一些旧的渲染图来看，它可能会有水滴式的刘海。话说回来，刘海的时代已经过去，所以索尼不太可能采取这种方式。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/05/26605d215ce1d63.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/05/26605d215ce1d63.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/05/177a94a26548261.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/05/177a94a26548261.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/05/58044a1e684e86a.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/05/58044a1e684e86a.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/05/7a5b9dd3541fe81.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/05/7a5b9dd3541fe81.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/05/29db496b26bf5a3.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/05/29db496b26bf5a3.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/05/0175b900827b8d1.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/05/0175b900827b8d1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/05/301d50348d3d357.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/05/301d50348d3d357.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/05/9dc3a06a30ee483.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/05/9dc3a06a30ee483.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/05/51b640bc24efc74.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/05/51b640bc24efc74.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
