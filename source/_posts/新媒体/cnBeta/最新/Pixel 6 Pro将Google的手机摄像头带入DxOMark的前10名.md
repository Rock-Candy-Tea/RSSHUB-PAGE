<strong>DxOMark已经完成了对GooglePixel 6 Pro主摄像头的评估，结果成绩不俗，这家评测机构将其列入前十名。</strong>项目得分分别是照片143分，变焦71分，视频115分，合计135分，这使Pixel Pro仅次于Mate 40 Pro和iPhone 13 Pro和13 Pro Max。<br>
 <p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1104/eb6cacd580a8bd2.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/1104/eb6cacd580a8bd2.jpg" title alt="gsmarena_001.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>在他们的测试中，DxOMark满意Pixel 6 Pro在所有照明条件下的细节水平，其宽广的动态范围和远距离拍摄画质。特别是在相同的照明条件下，画面细节甚至超过了<a data-link="1" href="https://apple.pvxt.net/c/1251234/435400/7639?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.apple.com%2Fcn%2Fiphone%2F" target="_blank">iPhone</a> 13 Pro和<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?k=WP0ZSPklWdlZyN4MTPklWYmYDO5IDNy0DZp9VZ0l2cmYiRyUSbvNmLsxWYtZnL3d3dGJTJGJTJBNTJzBHd0h" target="_blank">华为</a>的P50 Pro。</p><p>缺点包括景深较窄，这种问题是大传感器的相机所固有的，特别是在低光下。在光线较暗的环境中存在噪点，还有就是广角相机的16毫米等效值相对不太出彩。</p><p><strong>具体评测参见：</strong></p><p><a href="https://www.dxomark.com/google-pixel-6-pro-camera-review-a-big-leap-in-image-quality/" _src="https://www.dxomark.com/google-pixel-6-pro-camera-review-a-big-leap-in-image-quality/" target="_blank">https://www.dxomark.com/google-pixel-6-pro-camera-review-a-big-leap-in-image-quality/</a><br></p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1104/ce228edce845ed5.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/1104/ce228edce845ed5.jpg" title alt="gsmarena_002.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
