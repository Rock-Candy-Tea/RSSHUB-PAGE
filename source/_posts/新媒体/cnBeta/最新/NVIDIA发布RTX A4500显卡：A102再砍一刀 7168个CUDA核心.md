前几天有消息称 NVIDIA 即将发布 RTX A4500 工作站显卡，现在已经得到证实了。<strong>PNY 及官方都已经公布了这款显卡的详细规格，基于 GA102 核心，不过 CUDA 核心只有 7168 个。</strong>20GB显存，浮点性能 23.7 TFLOPS、TDP 功耗 200W 。<br>
 <p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1124/29c216f6688314e.png" alt="1.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>RTX A系列工作站显卡此前已经有A6000及A5000，都是基于GA102核心，CUDA核心分别是10752、8192。</p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1124/73f877305f45335.png" alt="2.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>A4500显卡这次又砍到了7168个CUDA核心，224个Tensor核心及56个RT核心。</p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1124/01c3183baf941f3.png" alt="3.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>显存位宽也从384bit砍到了320bit，搭配20GB GDDR6 ECC显存，位宽640GB/s。</p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1124/124ac8d5c0450f7.png" alt="4.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>性能方面，单精度23.7TFLOPS，RT光追性能46.2TFLOPS，Tensor性能189.2TFLOPS。</p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1124/74662ba3bb56e4b.png" alt="5.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>好处是功耗也从300W、230W降低到了200W。其它方面，A4500显卡位双插槽单<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C738%2C751" target="_blank">风扇</a>设计，支持4个DP1.4接口。</p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1124/027efbf69d4a9c6.png" alt="6.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>最多支持4路<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C794%2C798%26ev%3D4155_110018%26sort%3Dsort_rank_asc%26trans%3D1%26JL%3D2_1_0%23J_crumbsBar" target="_blank">4K</a> 120Hz输出，4路5K 60Hz输出或者2路8K 60Hz输出。单8pin供电，PCIe 4.0 x16等等。</p>
