尽管M.2 SSD在当下已经司空见惯，但对于20年前的PC来说，就是无法高攀的奢望了。不过，日前民间大神Pierre Dandumont出手，将一台苹果发布于1999年的PowerMac G4电脑，成功加装M.2 SSD。<br>
 <p><strong>访问原文：</strong></p><p><a href="https://www.journaldulapin.com/2021/12/18/m-2-pci-g4/" _src="https://www.journaldulapin.com/2021/12/18/m-2-pci-g4/" target="_blank">https://www.journaldulapin.com/2021/12/18/m-2-pci-g4/</a><br></p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/12/09ad88ce7e074d9.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/12/09ad88ce7e074d9.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>说来也并不复杂，Dandumont借助的是PCI（非PCIe）扩展卡，搭配<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://samsung.jd.com/" target="_blank">三星</a>SM951 <a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://list.jd.com/list.html?cat=670,677,11303" target="_blank">SSD</a>。并非所有的SSD都能用，<strong>M.2 SATA可以，但M.2 NVMe型号则必须选择支持AHCI的款式。</strong></p><p>要想成功启动运行SSD，还需要特定版本的Mac OS X系统以及编译过的BIOS，<strong>最终测得速度为133MB/s。</strong></p><p>由于Power Mac G4仅配备了USB 1.1接口，速度最高1.5MB/s，即便是更高速的FireWire 400，也不过50MB/s，所以加装M.2并非负优化。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/12/5699b9ef8f800b7.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/12/5699b9ef8f800b7.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/12/763c24a5ac23c1c.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/12/763c24a5ac23c1c.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/12/67c26151eb9e451.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/12/67c26151eb9e451.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/12/d248144657f0ecd.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/12/d248144657f0ecd.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
