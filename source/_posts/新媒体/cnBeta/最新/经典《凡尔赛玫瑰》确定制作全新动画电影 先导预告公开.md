<strong>2022年是日本经典少女漫画《凡尔赛玫瑰》诞生50周年，官方宣布确定制作全新动画电影，上映日期未定，同时最新海报以及先导预告公开，一起来先睹为快。</strong>《凡尔赛玫瑰》是池田理代子于1972年开始在周刊《玛格丽特》上连载以法国大革命为背景的浪漫历史剧，作品获得巨大成功并被改编为TV动画、真人电影以及舞台剧。<br>
 <p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0907/ff0884c33dc5d9b.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>打破了长期垄断日本漫画界的手冢治虫少女漫画表现形式，与手冢治虫的《怪医黑杰克》和永井豪的《恶魔人》并称为20世纪70年代三大不朽杰作。</p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0907/8b40b8588987fe0.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0907/e10052e8230a80b.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>•《凡尔赛玫瑰》全新动画电影先导预告：</p><p style="text-align: center;"><iframe src="//player.youku.com/embed/XNTkwMTM1NjcwMA==?client_id=5a73c0df8eb0d91d" width="640" height="480" frameborder="0"></iframe></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0907/1d143915b492133.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0907/1d143915b492133.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0907/1d143915b492133.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0907/9033a3486e3f288.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0907/9033a3486e3f288.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0907/9033a3486e3f288.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0907/db3b04a47d6d951.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0907/db3b04a47d6d951.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0907/db3b04a47d6d951.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0907/72725d5bff75180.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0907/72725d5bff75180.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0907/72725d5bff75180.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
