企查查APP显示，北京市第一中级人民法院将于2022年10月10日拍卖乐视控股（北京）有限公司名下的908件商标，起拍价格约为83.79万元，关联案号为（2022）京01执恢209号。据了解，<strong>本次拍卖的908件商标中包含乐视公司多款“Le”经典商标、乐视手机“LeMobile”、乐视超级电视“UMax120”等</strong>，国际分类包括广告销售、办公用品、金融物管等。<br>
 <p>目前，相关拍品已经在<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?k=nJ9QWa1VmJxYTPklWYmYDO5IDNy0DZp9VZ0l2cmYiJt92YuQmauc3d3ZkMlYkMlE0MlMHc0RHa9Q" target="_blank">京东</a>拍卖上架，本次拍卖不限制竞买人数量，保证金8万元，加价幅度4千元。</p><p>据企查查显示，乐视控股（北京）有限公司成立于2011年，法定代表人为吴孟，注册资本10亿元，贾跃亭持股92.07%为实际控制人。</p><p>目前该企业被执行总金额约为22.97亿元，失信被执行总金额约为9.55亿元。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220926/b1acd2abd7a74b88b1cb3a06178b7e14.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0926/aebac61424994ea.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0926/aebac61424994ea.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0926/aebac61424994ea.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220926/b15a9d3e5e44424b98802c067e42efac.png" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0926/189695483350c38.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0926/189695483350c38.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0926/189695483350c38.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220926/48f9fcd3e8fe4ac8a88d78cf5a103c99.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0926/c35ede597dda9f0.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0926/c35ede597dda9f0.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0926/c35ede597dda9f0.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220926/04517d1be9a7480897562776cc7d194a.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0926/4dd8e54fafcdc9b.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0926/4dd8e54fafcdc9b.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0926/4dd8e54fafcdc9b.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
