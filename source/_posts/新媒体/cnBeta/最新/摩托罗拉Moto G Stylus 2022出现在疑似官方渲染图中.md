<strong>据传，摩托罗拉Moto G Stylus 2022将在2022年中期的某个时候发布，但第一批渲染图已经揭示了其设计。</strong>这些图片是由@OnLeaks和Prepp提供的，它似乎来自于摩托罗拉官方的宣传资料，向我们展示了熟悉的Moto外形设计，带有椭圆形、长方形的摄像头凸起和略微突出的镜头。<br>
 <p style="text-align: center;"><iframe src="//tv.sohu.com/s/sohuplayer/iplay.html?bid=312536207&autoplay=false&disablePlaylist=true" width="640" height="480" frameborder="0"></iframe></p><p>我们还可以看到机身前方是平面显示屏和一个居中的前置摄像头刘海。电源键和音量摇杆在右侧，而前者似乎兼作指纹识别器，表明是其采用的是LCD而不是<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C794%2C798%26ev%3D4155_88234%26sort%3Dsort_rank_asc%26trans%3D1%26JL%3D2_1_0%23J_crumbsBar" target="_blank">OLED</a>面板。</p><p>根据之前的传言，这款<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://shouji.jd.com/" target="_blank">手机</a>将配备6.81英寸屏幕，4800万像素主摄像头，4500毫安时或5000毫安时电池，基础配置为6GB/128GB，骁龙480+ 5G可能会成为其核心，这要比比2021年的骁龙678 SoC退步。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1221/1b80cef09045bf4.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/1221/1b80cef09045bf4.jpg" title alt="gsmarena_004.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1221/848e9b8799e0ce8.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/1221/848e9b8799e0ce8.jpg" title alt="gsmarena_003.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1221/fe343ba0640c6bb.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/1221/fe343ba0640c6bb.jpg" title alt="gsmarena_002.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1221/d095de6be156618.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/1221/d095de6be156618.jpg" title alt="gsmarena_001.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
