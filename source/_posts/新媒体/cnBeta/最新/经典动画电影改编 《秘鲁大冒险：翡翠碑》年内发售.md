开发商Gammera Nest近日宣布，将把动画电影作品《秘鲁大冒险》改编为游戏，名为《秘鲁大冒险：翡翠碑》（Tad the Lost
Explorer and the Emerald
Tablet）。在本作中，主角泰德和他的好友们将踏上全新冒险旅程，在不同城市中解开谜题，最终完成任务，拿到翡翠碑。<br>
 <p>本作有大量动作和解谜元素，主角泰德要完成奔跑、跳跃、攀爬和游泳等动作，同时运用智慧解开谜题。在旅途中，泰德还将遭遇妄图阻挠其计划的敌人。</p><p>厂商近日发布本作的预告片和游戏截图。《秘鲁大冒险：翡翠碑》将于2022年发售，登陆PC、PS4和PS5平台。</p><p>感兴趣的玩家可<a href="https://store.steampowered.com/app/1966690/Tad_the_Lost_Explorer_and_the_Emerald_Tablet/" target="_blank"><strong>点此访问</strong></a>本作Steam商城页面。</p><p>预告片：</p><p style="text-align: center;"><iframe src="//player.youku.com/embed/XNTg3NTc1MjI5Ng==?client_id=5a73c0df8eb0d91d" width="640" height="480" frameborder="0"></iframe></p><p>游戏截图：</p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0605/dcec2b1e9e5ef65.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0605/dcec2b1e9e5ef65.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0605/dcec2b1e9e5ef65.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0605/4b301d0588514a1.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0605/4b301d0588514a1.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0605/4b301d0588514a1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0605/30985ac2f18c926.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0605/30985ac2f18c926.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0605/30985ac2f18c926.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0605/2f25285cd648966.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0605/2f25285cd648966.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0605/2f25285cd648966.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0605/a93551ce1121e97.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0605/a93551ce1121e97.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0605/a93551ce1121e97.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
