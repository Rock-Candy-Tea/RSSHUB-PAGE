根据北京冬奥组委与特许生产商的特许经营协议，今天是最后一天特许经营企业可以领取防伪标签。也就是说，从明天开始，包括冰墩墩公仔等在内的2022北京冬奥会各种特许商品都将停止生产。<br>
 <p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0630/77e9f542ca5a84a.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0630/77e9f542ca5a84a.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0630/77e9f542ca5a84a.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a><br></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0630/6e608233c52e4e0.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0630/6e608233c52e4e0.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0630/6e608233c52e4e0.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a><br></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0630/ce4f7905bf9d663.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0630/ce4f7905bf9d663.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0630/ce4f7905bf9d663.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a><br></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0630/17fd8d3ed9f43a1.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0630/17fd8d3ed9f43a1.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0630/17fd8d3ed9f43a1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a><br></p><p>在位于王府井的工美大厦冬奥特许商品旗舰店，记者看到，一位刚刚从大连坐火车赶到北京的旅客，第一时间来到这里。他买下了所有类型的冰墩墩，为了给自己留下一个美好的冬奥记忆。</p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0630/502eca3d34a36f5.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0630/502eca3d34a36f5.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0630/502eca3d34a36f5.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a><br></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0630/f05b64c381bf1d1.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0630/f05b64c381bf1d1.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0630/f05b64c381bf1d1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a><br></p><p>记者从冬奥特许商品旗舰店了解到，包括冰墩墩在内的各类特许商品还将持续销售到9月份。</p>
