今日开始，此前参与订购Switch OLED游戏机的用户开始陆续收货。同行Sam Byford收到后，拍摄了Switch OLED的屏幕微距，结果如图：虽然排列方式并非标准RGB、<strong>也非常见的钻石排列、Delta排列或者“周冬雨”排列，但可以确认的是，并没有共用绿色像素的现象，也就是并非“臭名昭著”的Pentile排列。</strong><br>
<p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211008/bf4ca81efdae4563b8ddf6715b3e193d.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1008/c748afa0b1a3d03.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1008/c748afa0b1a3d03.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/1008/c748afa0b1a3d03.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>换言之，尽管Switch <a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C794%2C798%26ev%3D4155_88234%26sort%3Dsort_rank_asc%26trans%3D1%26JL%3D2_1_0%23J_crumbsBar" target="_blank">OLED</a>仍是720P分辨率，但PPI并没有P排OLED那样对比LCD直接缩水1/3。</p><p>感兴趣的不妨看一下<a data-link="1" href="https://apple.pvxt.net/c/1251234/435400/7639?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.apple.com%2Fcn%2Fiphone%2F" target="_blank">iPhone</a> 13 Pro的屏幕微距，标准的<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://samsung.jd.com/" target="_blank">三星</a>钻石排列。也就是四个子像素为一组，每一组呈菱形排列。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211008/36f7d4d0ab0442b0b35786beaaee218a.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1008/75803c1d7d235f9.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1008/75803c1d7d235f9.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/1008/75803c1d7d235f9.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>从老外的评测来看，普遍对Switch OLED的屏幕赞赏有加，毕竟看惯了之前Switch的屏幕，也许早就习惯了那种“颗粒感”吧。</p>
