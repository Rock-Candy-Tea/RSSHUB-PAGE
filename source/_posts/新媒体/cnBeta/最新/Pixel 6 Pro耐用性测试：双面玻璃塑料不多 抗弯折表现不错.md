<strong>油管知名“毁机”科技频道 JerryRigEverything，刚刚分享了 Google Pixel 6 Pro 旗舰智能机的“耐用性测试”视频。</strong>副标题写到了“这次用了多少塑料？”，但 Pixel 6 Pro 总体还是通过了 Zack 的暴力弯折测试 —— 尽管屏幕因为打火机的火焰烧灼而留下了损伤。<br>
 <p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1106/2a1053301067288.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/1106/2a1053301067288.jpg" alt="0.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p style="text-align: center;">视频截图（来自：JerryRigEverything）</p><p>本次耐用性测试从 Pixel 6 Pro 凸出约 2.5mm 的后摄模组开始着手，然后遍历了机身、后盖、屏幕玻璃。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><iframe src="//tv.sohu.com/s/sohuplayer/iplay.html?bid=301039015&autoplay=false&disablePlaylist=true" width="640" height="480" frameborder="0"></iframe></p><p style="text-align: center;">Pixel 6 Pro Durability Test - How much Plastic this time？（<a href="https://tv.sohu.com/v/dXMvODIyMjQwNTMvMzAxMDM5MDE1LnNodG1s.html" target="_self">via</a>）</p><p>相机模组外层的平坦部分为玻璃材质，但弯曲部分由塑料制成。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1106/998d9ce81e7c1a4.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/1106/998d9ce81e7c1a4.jpg" alt="1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>在放置于桌面上时，横向的后摄模组设计不至于让<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://shouji.jd.com/" target="_blank">手机</a>容易晃动。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1106/3411994d8536ec2.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/1106/3411994d8536ec2.jpg" alt="2.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>Pixel 6 Pro 的侧边、底部、按键、以及 SIM 卡槽，均采用铝制材料。</p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1106/a531f836138bfc6.gif" alt="4-0.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>但由于放置了天线（大概为毫米波），中框顶部换用了塑料材质。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1106/3316c19b0b4f73c.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/1106/3316c19b0b4f73c.jpg" alt="3.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>机身前后面板均由康宁大猩猩 Victus 玻璃提供防护，在 6 级莫氏硬度下可看到轻微划痕，7 级状况下会伤得更深。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1106/3baabd196724813.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/1106/3baabd196724813.jpg" alt="5.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>经过“像素燃烧测试”后，Zack 在 Pixel 6 Pro 屏下指纹传感器上方显示区域留下了打火机火焰的烧灼伤。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1106/8a3bc5f2feca422.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/1106/8a3bc5f2feca422.jpg" alt="6.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>虽然“双面玻璃三明治”的结构设计让我们一开始对 Pixel 6 Pro 的抗弯折设计感到有些担心，但所幸最终有惊无险。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1106/6b23a8bee4568d2.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/1106/6b23a8bee4568d2.jpg" alt="7.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>最后，Zack 分享了卸下 Pixel 6 Pro 后盖后的内部结构，电池和无线充电感应线圈等组件清晰可见。</p>
