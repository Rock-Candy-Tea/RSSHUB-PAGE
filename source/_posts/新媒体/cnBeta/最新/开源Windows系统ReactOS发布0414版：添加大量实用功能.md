开源Windows系统，听说过吗？实际上，从1996年起，ReactOS项目就启动了，目的在于通过开源、免费的方式重建Windows NT内核以及系统层，<a class="f14_link" href="https://reactos.org/project-news/reactos-0414-released/" target="_blank">ReactOS 0.4.14已经发布并提供免费下载</a>。<br>
 <p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211221/03cb97b457504cdbbc7ad2722c706f55.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1221/35b08b9fc4f380c.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1221/35b08b9fc4f380c.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/1221/35b08b9fc4f380c.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>对于<a data-link="1" href="https://microsoft.pvxt.net/x9Vg1" target="_blank">Windows</a>老鸟来说，新系统添加的功能可能有些“贻笑大方”，但从开源免费的角度看，已经是进步。<strong>比如早在Windows 2000时代就上线的右键“发送到……”功能，还有“打开方式”、“以命令行运行”等，基于Wine项目更新了一批DLL文件、添加对NEC PC-9800的启动支持等。</strong></p><p><strong><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1221/bc8eebb232aa71a.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/1221/bc8eebb232aa71a.png" title alt="shell.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></strong></p><p>此外，引入互联网消息控制协议、优化了C语言编写程序的运行框架、更新屏幕键盘、字体等。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1221/cfb180ff3a26f07.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/1221/cfb180ff3a26f07.png" title alt="nec98.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>总的来说，新系统运行更加稳定，<strong>闲置时占用的内存仅100MB。</strong></p><p><strong><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1221/989168ba63bd2f1.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/1221/989168ba63bd2f1.png" title alt="misc.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1221/9e89784805a5196.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/1221/9e89784805a5196.png" title alt="icmp.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></strong></p><p>软硬件兼容性方面，ReactOS团队经常在官方账号更新进展，前不久还成功在Z690A主板和12代酷睿i7的平台上跑了起来，也能运行《暗黑破坏神2》《<a data-link="1" href="https://microsoft.pvxt.net/P0JMe" target="_blank">Office</a> 97》《VLC Media Player》等程序。</p>
