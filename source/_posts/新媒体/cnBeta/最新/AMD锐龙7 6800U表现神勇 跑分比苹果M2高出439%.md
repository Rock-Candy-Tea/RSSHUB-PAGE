AMD锐龙7 6800U是今年1月发布的移动端低电压APU产品，家族代号Rembrandt，CPU架构升级到了Zen
3+，并采用台积电6nm工艺制造。在Passmark的最新测试中，AMD锐龙7
6800U表现神勇，连胜Intel酷睿i7-1280P以及苹果M2。<br>
 <p>具体来说，<strong>锐龙7 6800U的CPU Mark跑分比5800U高出13.7%，比i7-1280P（14核，6P+8E）高出12.3%，比i7-1260P（12核，4P+8E）高出26.2%，比<a data-link="1" href="https://apple.pvxt.net/c/1251234/435400/7639?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.apple.com%2Fcn%2Fmusic%2F" target="_blank">苹果</a>M2更是高出43.9%。</strong></p><p>实际上，<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://amd-cpu.jd.com/" target="_blank">AMD</a>锐龙7 6800U和苹果M2都是8核设计，前者频率为2.7~4.7GHz，后者则是3.5GHz。</p><p>当然，跑分只是一方面，不完全对等各场景下的体验。</p><p>目前，锐龙7 6800U已经可以在ZenBook S 13 <a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C794%2C798%26ev%3D4155_88234%26sort%3Dsort_rank_asc%26trans%3D1%26JL%3D2_1_0%23J_crumbsBar" target="_blank">OLED</a>、联想ThinkBook 13s、<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://mall.jd.com/index-1000000155.html" target="_blank">惠普</a>EliteBook 865 G9等笔记本上搭载，供消费者选购。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220714/6853e701dba541faa78838de7d3737bc.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0714/951d5b59b07c2d3.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0714/951d5b59b07c2d3.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0714/951d5b59b07c2d3.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
