<strong>发表在《英国药理学杂志》上的新研究表明，目前有两种药物 -
一种抗真菌药物和一种抗抑郁药物可以有效抑制实验室细胞中引起COVID-19的病毒。</strong>研究人员发现，抗真菌药伊曲康唑和抗抑郁药氟西汀在细胞培养实验室试验中分别阻止了传染性SARS-CoV-2病毒的产生。<br>
<p>当任何一种药物与抗病毒药物雷米西韦联合使用时，该组合便会显示出协同作用，且对SARS-CoV-2的产生抑制率超过90%。</p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0408/9f2d4791f95e7a5.webp" title alt="bph15418-fig-0004-m.webp" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0408/52e4861553ff5a4.webp" title alt="bph15418-fig-0005-m.webp" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0408/644040199c7962c.webp" title alt="bph15418-fig-0002-m.webp" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0408/7f3d6bf7ca03d05.webp" title alt="bph15418-fig-0003-m.webp" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>"预防接种和针对COVID-19的治疗药物都是有效对抗SARS-CoV-2等新兴人畜共患病病毒引起的大流行所必需的。"资深作者德国明斯特大学的Ursula Rescher博士说。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0408/ecb2160ad2d6b8c.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0408/ecb2160ad2d6b8c.jpg" title alt="itraconazole-1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0408/20bb887b3d7ab3d.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0408/20bb887b3d7ab3d.jpg" title alt="Fluoxine.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><strong>参考文献：</strong></p><p>DOI：10.1111/bph.15418，《英国药理学杂志》</p><p><a href="https://doi.org/10.1111/bph.15418" _src="https://doi.org/10.1111/bph.15418" target="_blank">https://doi.org/10.1111/bph.15418</a><br></p>
