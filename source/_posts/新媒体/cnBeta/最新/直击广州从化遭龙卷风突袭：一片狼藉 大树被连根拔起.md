6月16日晚19点半左右，广州市从化区太平镇出现龙卷风。经从化区气象局工作人员现场确认，<strong>龙卷风出现的时间为19时25分左右，不过尺度很小，距离龙卷风最近的(约700米)的太平二中气象站，记录下了6级阵风，相当于风速12.6米/秒。</strong><br>
 <p>据现场目击者反馈，这次龙卷风历时3分钟左右。</p><p>尽管尺度不大、时间不长，但由于穿过建筑密集地带，<strong>这次龙卷风还是造成了明显的破坏，当地街道一片狼藉，路边有的大树都被连根拔起，还有停放的汽车被砸中。</strong></p><p><a class="f14_link" href="https://live.baidu.com/m/media/pclive/pchome/live.html?type=live&action=liveshow&room_id=7433474865&source=search" target="_blank">点击看现场直击</a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220616/57fc8a2c-29f6-4fdd-b453-9c5df6b248e1.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0616/758cc0ae585f826.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0616/758cc0ae585f826.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0616/758cc0ae585f826.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220616/b95605ce-4a1f-4d06-993c-c93d0bf6b7ae.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0616/9102d019b8af4a9.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0616/9102d019b8af4a9.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0616/9102d019b8af4a9.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220616/305bffe4-4fdf-43f2-b306-14b9e5ce4529.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0616/1954f648d3a555a.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0616/1954f648d3a555a.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0616/1954f648d3a555a.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220616/9b5d0200-7d31-4a2e-8277-08979a47e09b.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0616/c9c0f801c79682a.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0616/c9c0f801c79682a.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0616/c9c0f801c79682a.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
