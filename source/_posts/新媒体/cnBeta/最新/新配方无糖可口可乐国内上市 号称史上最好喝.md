为降低热量摄取，现在很多朋友会选择无糖饮料。日前，<strong>可口可乐宣布新配方无糖可口可乐（零度）上市，同时全球启用新包装。</strong>由于配方绝密，具体升级之处不详。官方配料表中显示仍是水、食品添加剂（二氧化碳、焦糖色、磷酸、苯甲酸钠、阿斯巴甜、安赛蜜、柠檬酸钠、咖啡因、蔗糖素）和食用香精。<br>
 <p>在宣传页面，可口可乐甚至用了“史上最好喝的可口可乐？”来宣传。</p><p>新配方无糖可口可乐330mLx24罐售价59元，300mLx24瓶装售价45.9元。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210908/06f1de628372437885cde791cc3fcfe6.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0908/84bfe5529abcd40.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0908/84bfe5529abcd40.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0908/84bfe5529abcd40.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210908/86a4b452e7ca4af2b6d3d0b861deb846.png" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0908/8ea5e9de699624a.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0908/8ea5e9de699624a.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0908/8ea5e9de699624a.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
