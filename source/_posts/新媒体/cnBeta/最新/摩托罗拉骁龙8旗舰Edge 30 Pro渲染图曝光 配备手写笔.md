今天，知名爆料人evleaks曝光了摩托罗拉Edge 30 Pro的渲染图。爆料图显示，<strong>摩托罗拉为这款高端旗舰配备了手写笔，这是业界第二款支持手写笔的骁龙8旗舰。</strong>众所周知，<strong>三星本月发布的Galaxy S22 Ultra便支持手写笔，</strong>这是首款支持手写笔的骁龙8旗舰手机。<br>
<p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0211/a32e15efdedfbfa.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0211/a32e15efdedfbfa.jpg" title alt="gsmarena_002.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>通过S Pen，<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://samsung.jd.com/" target="_blank">三星</a>Galaxy S22 Ultra解锁更多玩法。比如按住S Pen上的按钮，<strong>就可轻松完成遥控拍照，</strong>如果左、右或上、下挥动，还可以切换拍照模式或转换前、后置摄像头。</p><p>在摩托罗拉Edge 30 Pro上，预计摩托罗拉同样会为这支手写笔赋予新玩法，值得期待。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><video src="//fdn.gsmarena.com/imgroot/news/22/02/moto-edge-30-pro-case-color/video/gsmarena_001.mp4" controls="controls" width="640" height="480"></video></p><p>核心配置上，摩托罗拉Edge 30 Pro采用6.7英寸<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C794%2C798%26ev%3D4155_88234%26sort%3Dsort_rank_asc%26trans%3D1%26JL%3D2_1_0%23J_crumbsBar" target="_blank">OLED</a>显示屏，分辨率为1080×2400，刷新率为144Hz，<strong>后置5000万AI三摄，电池为5000mAh，支持68W快充。</strong></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220211/5f41bd214f9e47548e267bbd2a106c27.png" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0211/ccae24af0f1b188.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0211/ccae24af0f1b188.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0211/ccae24af0f1b188.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
