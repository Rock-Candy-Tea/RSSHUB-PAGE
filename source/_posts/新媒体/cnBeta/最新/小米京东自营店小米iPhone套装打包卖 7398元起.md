3月18日消息，有网友发现，<strong>小米京东自营旗舰店上架了多款“小米+iPhone”套装。</strong>简单来说，就是把小米手机和苹果iPhone打包在一起开卖。网友调侃：这是买小米送iPhone的节奏？其中的<strong>小米手机包括小米11青春版、Redmi Note 10 Pro、Redmi Note 9 Pro三款机型可选。</strong><br>
 <p>而<strong>iPhone方面则都是iPhone 13（256GB）一款机型</strong>，多种颜色可选。</p><p>在商品界面，目前尚未有详细介绍，只显示一个漏出一角的<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&aid=450&euid=&t=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.mi.com%2F" target="_blank">小米</a>包装盒，看来两部<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://shouji.jd.com/" target="_blank">手机</a>会放在一个盒子里。</p><p>对此，据新浪科技报道，该旗舰店客服回应：是<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?k=nJ9QWa1VmJxYTPklWYmYDO5IDNy0DZp9VZ0l2cmYiJt92YuQmauc3d3ZkMlYkMlE0MlMHc0RHa9Q" target="_blank">京东</a>推出的组合，具体时间需咨询京东。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220318/2ba0f2eaa02844cdb2e33f6899028d08.png" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0318/4215d1330266136.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0318/4215d1330266136.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0318/4215d1330266136.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220318/2c57c34c4a92402fa6860e679118c424.png" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0318/b246bbf1346e19e.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0318/b246bbf1346e19e.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0318/b246bbf1346e19e.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220318/e66093719e7a458db2e8de2872ef26ce.png" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0318/b6e97cde23ab706.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0318/b6e97cde23ab706.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0318/b6e97cde23ab706.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
