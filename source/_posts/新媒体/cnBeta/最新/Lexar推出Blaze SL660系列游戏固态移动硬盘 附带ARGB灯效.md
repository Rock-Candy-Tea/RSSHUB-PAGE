<strong>雷克沙（Lexar）刚刚发布了 Blaze SL660 系列游戏固态移动硬盘，特点是采用了独特的非对称外形和 ARGB 灯效设计。</strong>长宽高为 112.6 × 57.4 × 10.6 毫米，重 80 克。此外通过 USB 3.2 Gen 2×2 接口，还可实现高达 2000 MB/s 的顺序读取、以及 1900 MB/s 的顺序写入。<br>
 <p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1118/5b64e869b38b080.png" alt="0-1.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p style="text-align: center;">（来自：Lexar <a href="https://lexar.com/product/lexar-sl660-blaze-gaming-portable-ssd/" target="_self">官网</a>）</p><p>容量方面，Lexar 为 Blaze SL660 提供了 512GB / 1TB 两档。</p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1118/9fea0e9873f951d.png" alt="0-2.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>Blaze SL660 通过单根线缆连接电源和主机，且支持 USB-C 和 Type-A 端口。</p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1118/3574e1cb010ba9d.jpg" alt="1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>为方便桌面侧置使用，包装内还附带了一个塑料支架。</p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1118/751264c7572413f.jpg" alt="2.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>如需收纳，你也可以将 Lexar Blaze SL660 全都放进人造皮革小袋子里。</p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1118/6e34ace03dc7bf4.jpg" alt="5.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>目前 Lexar Blaze SL660 游戏移动固态硬盘已在国内上市，辅以 5 年有限质保，国外售价尚未公布。</p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1118/dfd2e93f1496af0.jpg" alt="3.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>目前 <a href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://item.jd.com/100028690396.html" target="_self">512GB</a> / <a href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://item.jd.com/100028690402.html" target="_self">1TB 版</a> Lexar Blaze SL660 移动固态硬盘在<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?k=nJ9QWa1VmJxYTPklWYmYDO5IDNy0DZp9VZ0l2cmYiJt92YuQmauc3d3ZkMlYkMlE0MlMHc0RHa9Q" target="_blank">京东</a>自营旗舰店的参考价格为 ￥899 / 1599 RMB 。</p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1118/4b6204a73dbe1e7.jpg" alt="4.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
