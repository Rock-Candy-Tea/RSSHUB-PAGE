不知不觉，经典美剧《行尸走肉》已经开播10年多了，粉丝们关心的最终第11季终于有了确切消息了，AMC在4月4日晚上宣布，《行尸走肉》最终第11季确定8月22日开播，总集数依然如之前的消息为24集。<br>
 <p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0405/17c91e4347172a9.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0405/17c91e4347172a9.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0405/17c91e4347172a9.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>·官方表示，虽然《行尸走肉》最终第11季已经确定完结板上钉钉，不过这“僵尸世界”并不会结束，AMC将继续《行尸走肉》中超人气角色弩哥与战神卡罗尔的冒险，预定2023年开播二人的衍生系列剧《Tales of The Walking Dead》，敬请期待。</p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0405/6019c415e8385f5.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0405/10777eb2d9a6a4c.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><strong>《行尸走肉》最终第11季先导预告：</strong></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0405/b01a2f43c67537f.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0405/b01a2f43c67537f.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0405/b01a2f43c67537f.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0405/8fad2aaa597f016.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0405/8fad2aaa597f016.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0405/8fad2aaa597f016.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0405/acfee0bb9e344fe.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0405/acfee0bb9e344fe.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0405/acfee0bb9e344fe.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0405/c9aabbabd7fe241.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0405/c9aabbabd7fe241.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0405/c9aabbabd7fe241.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
