今年4月份发布的华为Mate Xs 2折叠屏手机在重量上有重大突破，<strong>这款手机只有255g重，跟戴壳的iPhone 13 Pro Max重量相当。</strong>据悉，Mate Xs 2采用超轻玻璃纤维，结构中采用航空级钛合金和超轻超强钢等创新材质，在实现高强度高韧性的同时，机身做到了超轻。作为华为的老对手，三星将发布的Galaxy Z Fold4在重量上同样有重大突破。<br>
 <p>今天，博主@i冰宇宙在社交平台爆料，<strong>三星Galaxy Z Fold4重量只有254g，比<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?k=WP0ZSPklWdlZyN4MTPklWYmYDO5IDNy0DZp9VZ0l2cmYiRyUSbvNmLsxWYtZnL3d3dGJTJGJTJBNTJzBHd0h" target="_blank">华为</a>Mate Xs 2轻1g。</strong><strong>对比上一代Galaxy Z Fold3 271g的重量，Galaxy Z Fold4 254g有了明显进步。</strong></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220602/9e0d5b5de9134b52983fbfbb7b25278f.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0602/12c646f494f6a5f.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0602/12c646f494f6a5f.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0602/12c646f494f6a5f.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>据爆料，三星Galaxy Z Fold4内屏尺寸为7.6英寸，刷新率为120Hz，尺寸接近<a data-link="1" href="https://apple.pvxt.net/c/1251234/435400/7639?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.apple.com%2Fcn%2Fipad%2F" target="_blank">iPad</a> mini，后者尺寸为8.3英寸。</p><p>该机外屏尺寸为6.2英寸，刷新率为120Hz，搭载高通骁龙8+旗舰处理器，配备12GB内存，提供256GB和512GB两种选择，后置5000万主摄、1200万超广角和1000万长焦三摄。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220602/e4ba94464bc844d7b732f4d58adada1f.png" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0602/f2dae4f1fbf7821.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0602/f2dae4f1fbf7821.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0602/f2dae4f1fbf7821.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>三星Galaxy Z Fold4</p>
