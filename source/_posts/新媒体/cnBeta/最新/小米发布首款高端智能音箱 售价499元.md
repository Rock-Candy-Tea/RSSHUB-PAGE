<strong>8月10日晚，小米发布了旗下首款高端智能音箱——小米Sound</strong>，拥有小巧身材的同时，还能提供震撼音质。其内置钕铁硼强力磁体，<strong>搭配双悬挂无源辐射器，可实现90dB震撼声压，70Hz饱满低音。</strong>同时采用360°全向出音，顶置导音锥设计，Hi-Res高解析认证。<br>
 <p>小米Sound提供银色、黑色双色可选，金属质感知足，雷军表示这是小米10年来最漂亮、最高端的智能音箱。</p><p>小米Sound还支持计算音频功能，每秒330次计算，实现动态调音。同时夜莺算法加持，夜间小音量也有饱满音质。</p><p>值得一提的是，小米Sound还支持两台互联，组合立体声，<strong>更是支持8台互联，放在家里不同的位置，可以让动听的音乐弥漫在家中每个角落。</strong></p><p>小米Sound音箱售价499元。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210810/b11f128cd7e145dabf886af2160686f5.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0810/9afeb51c681eb50.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210810/5cd53befdb0540eea3d6dd9ad5940a8f.png" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0810/b8ae15bd1a834f9.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0810/b8ae15bd1a834f9.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0810/b8ae15bd1a834f9.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210810/784bcb7b8e4847c59b878f956b6f8571.png" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0810/c749ed72f010062.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0810/c749ed72f010062.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0810/c749ed72f010062.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210810/f5603d635ac94c80854b537e1f0dc768.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0810/adb8ad7b7922b31.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210810/1f140b266e83453ea02a5decdd28041a.png" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0810/6928ad29e56407a.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0810/6928ad29e56407a.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0810/6928ad29e56407a.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
