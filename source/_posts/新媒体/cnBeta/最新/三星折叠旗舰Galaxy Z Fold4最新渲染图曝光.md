<strong>三星的新款折叠旗舰Galaxy Z Fold4快要来了，预计8月份登场。此前该机已获得3C认证。</strong>Onleaks根据官方CAD制作了一组渲染图，<strong>可以看出整机采用和上一代一样的内外双屏设计，正面展开更加方正了</strong>，和此前爆料的6：5比例相符合。<br><br>
<p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220511/18b29d315a03414cb3175495ee550e82.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0511/2d6878f212fe7f1.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0511/2d6878f212fe7f1.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0511/2d6878f212fe7f1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220511/aab8e91d06564973b0b5620d5ca22c7d.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0511/481f0924fd4b1ef.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0511/481f0924fd4b1ef.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0511/481f0924fd4b1ef.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><strong>同时，侧面更加纤薄，即使折叠起来也不显得臃肿。</strong></p><p>据悉，Galaxy Z Fold4将会搭载<strong>高通骁龙8 Plus旗舰处理器，并且会配备屏下摄像头</strong>，这是业界第一款采用屏下摄像技术的骁龙8 Plus旗舰，将会成为史上最强悍的折叠屏旗舰。</p><p><strong>此外，该机重量不到260g，比<a data-link="1" href="https://apple.pvxt.net/c/1251234/435400/7639?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.apple.com%2Fcn%2Fiphone%2F" target="_blank">iPhone</a> 13 Pro Max还要轻，并且和<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?k=WP0ZSPklWdlZyN4MTPklWYmYDO5IDNy0DZp9VZ0l2cmYiRyUSbvNmLsxWYtZnL3d3dGJTJGJTJBNTJzBHd0h" target="_blank">华为</a>Mate Xs 2近乎相同，后者重255g。</strong></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220511/1ec8e63a6f754cbcae6db6191e26e046.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0511/549b5b2181b4706.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0511/549b5b2181b4706.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0511/549b5b2181b4706.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
