2月4日消息，科技媒体Phone Arena带来了骁龙8旗舰一加10 Pro与竞品的续航对比测试。在相同屏幕亮度条件下，一加10 Pro、一加9
Pro、iPhone 13 Pro Max、三星Galaxy S21 Ultra、谷歌Pixel 6
Pro等高端旗舰均同时播放YouTube视频。<br>
 <p>经过测试，<strong>一加10 Pro以10小时47分钟的好成绩拔得头筹，</strong><strong>超越了<a data-link="1" href="https://apple.pvxt.net/c/1251234/435400/7639?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.apple.com%2Fcn%2Fiphone%2F" target="_blank">iPhone</a> 13 Pro Max</strong>，后者的测试成绩是10小时23分钟。</p><p>值得注意的是，<strong>一加10 Pro、iPhone 13 Pro Max是唯二两款续航成绩突破10小时的高端旗舰，</strong>远远好于<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://samsung.jd.com/" target="_blank">三星</a>Galaxy S21 Ultra、谷歌Pixel 6 Pro和一加9 Pro。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220204/1dc11937c6a24c0eab2e84042d206246.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0204/5664534056b53b0.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0204/5664534056b53b0.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0204/5664534056b53b0.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>据悉，一加10 Pro电池容量为5000mAh，支持80W有线快充。虽然三星Galaxy S21 Ultra也是5000mAh大电池，谷歌Pixel 6 Pro电池容量为5003mAh。</p><p>但是从实测结果来看，<strong>一加10 Pro的续航远好于同样配备大电池的Galaxy S21 Ultra和谷歌Pixel 6 Pro。</strong>该机国行首发起售价为4699元。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220204/0517d6dc44c442cabee9569499af5981.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0204/6e2d19bc72e48bf.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0204/6e2d19bc72e48bf.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0204/6e2d19bc72e48bf.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
