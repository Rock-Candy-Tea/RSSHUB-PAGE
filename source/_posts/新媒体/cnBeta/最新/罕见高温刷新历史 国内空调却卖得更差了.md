虽然已经进入三伏里的末伏，而且过了立秋节气，可华夏大地的高温天气并无明显减退的痕迹。事实上，截至今天6时，中央气象台连续第6天发布高温红色预警，连续第28天发布高温预警。按理说，在这个炎炎夏日，身担“救命神器”的空调该空前火爆才是，可市场情况似乎并非如此。<br>
 <p>据<a class="f14_link" href="https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s/4F_0il9CYEToAkfP3OKT3A" target="_blank">奥维云网</a>发布的数据，<strong>上半年，<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C794%2C870%26ev%3D1554_584894%26JL%3D3_%25E7%25A9%25BA%25E8%25B0%2583%25E7%25B1%25BB%25E5%2588%25AB_%25E7%25AB%258B%25E6%259F%259C%25E5%25BC%258F%25E7%25A9%25BA%25E8%25B0%2583%23J_crumbsBar" target="_blank">空调</a>全渠道零售额727亿元，同比下降15.3%。以往作为行业波动中“稳定器”的线上渠道，零售额380亿元，同比下滑10.1%。</strong></p><p>工程渠道同样低迷，上半年精装修市场空调配套项目个数516个，同比下降26.5%；配套规模为36.24万套，同比下降32.9%。</p><p>精装市场配套品牌方面，日立、<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://mideajiadian.jd.com/" target="_blank">美的</a>和大金的份额位列三甲，接下来还有<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://greedianqi.jd.com/" target="_blank">格力</a>、海尔、<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://toshiba-storage.jd.com/" target="_blank">东芝</a>和<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://hisense-tv.jd.com/" target="_blank">海信</a>等。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220817/04c91886ddf74bbd979b8a3b0949a8e1.png" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0817/813a611e9bd9f95.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0817/813a611e9bd9f95.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0817/813a611e9bd9f95.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220817/76bdc96e12ab4ef6a85254e755873ec3.png" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0817/71876d5ecf52612.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0817/71876d5ecf52612.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0817/71876d5ecf52612.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
