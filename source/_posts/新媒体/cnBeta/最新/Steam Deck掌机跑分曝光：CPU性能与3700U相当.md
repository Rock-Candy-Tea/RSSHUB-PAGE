<strong>据Benchleaks消息，即将上市的Steam Deck掌机现已现身Geekbench跑分平台。</strong>Steam Deck搭载的这款AMD定制处理器为4核8线程，主频为2.8GHz，最高3.5GHz，4MB三级缓存。Steam Deck处理器单核830分，多核3666分，远不及最新的笔记本处理器，与AMD 2019年发布的R7 3700U跑分类似。<br>
 <p><a target="_blank" href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/upimg/pic/2022/02/17/origin_202202170023321351.jpg"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0217/bd1ddac6ad3c1a4.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0217/bd1ddac6ad3c1a4.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0217/bd1ddac6ad3c1a4.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/upimg/pic/2022/02/17/origin_202202170018265109.png"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0217/ceb2a69d0f39fa4.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>Steam Deck搭载了<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://amd-cpu.jd.com/" target="_blank">AMD</a>的Van Gogh APU，Zen2架构，2.4GHz到3.5GHz。采用集成了8CU的RDNA2 GPU，频率为1.0GHz到1.6GHz，性能为1.6TFops FP32。整个SoC的TDP为4到15W，支持16GB LPDDR5内存。存储可选64GB eMMC和256GB、512GB NVMe <a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://list.jd.com/list.html?cat=670,677,11303" target="_blank">SSD</a>。消息称，该游戏掌机将于2月25日发售。</p>
