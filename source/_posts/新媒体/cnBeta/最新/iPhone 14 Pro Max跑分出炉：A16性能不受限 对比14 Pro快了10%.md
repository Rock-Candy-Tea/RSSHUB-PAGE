尽管同为A16处理器，但从GeekBench 5的跑分成绩来看，iPhone 14 Pro Max的表现比iPhone 14 Pro更好。具体来说，iPhone 14 Pro Max幸好识别为iPhone15,2，主板代号D73AP，运行iOS 16.0系统。CPU 6核设计，最大主频3.46GHz，匹配6GB RAM。<br>
 <p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220909/7274fcafe8fa400c86ce5db6f495ca3b.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0909/b5f518af911a942.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0909/b5f518af911a942.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0909/b5f518af911a942.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><strong>单核1887，多核5455，对于<a data-link="1" href="https://apple.pvxt.net/c/1251234/435400/7639?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.apple.com%2Fcn%2Fiphone%2F" target="_blank">iPhone</a> 14 Pro（iPhone15,3）的成绩，单核持平，多核居然高出13.4%。</strong></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220909/a5b126864e8941309f3f18803c0a77f2.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0909/faea7d3c2528411.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0909/faea7d3c2528411.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0909/faea7d3c2528411.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>对比对象换成iPhone 13 Pro Max上的满血A15的话， 单核提升8.9%、多核提升10.4%。不知道是不是因为iPhone 14 Pro受到机身空间的影响，导致无法释放最大性能潜力，多核甚至不如A15。</p><p>据了解，A16处理器采用4nm工艺，CPU依然2性能核+4效能核，GPU是5核，AI引擎还是16核，晶体管数量比A15增加6.6%、每秒操作次数增加8%、主频增加7%。</p><p>此前<a data-link="1" href="https://apple.pvxt.net/c/1251234/435400/7639?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.apple.com%2Fcn%2Fmusic%2F" target="_blank">苹果</a>曾强调，A16的大核同性能下功耗降低了20%。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220909/073918105efa48c09860a1c719147a7d.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0909/83da87c4deaeb2c.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0909/83da87c4deaeb2c.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0909/83da87c4deaeb2c.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
