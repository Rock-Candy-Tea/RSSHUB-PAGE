<strong>Google已经为他们的Chrome浏览器桌面用户发布了一个紧急修复程序，以应对最近发现的一个正在被积极利用的漏洞。</strong>CVE-2021-30563已经被判定是Chrome的Javascript引擎V8中的一个类型混淆漏洞，但Google没有公布有关该漏洞的更多细节。<br>
 <p>Google已经了解有报告说CVE-2021-30563的漏洞存在于外部，这使得它的危险性大大提高。<br></p><p>新的补丁会将Chrome浏览器升级到<a data-link="1" href="https://microsoft.pvxt.net/x9Vg1" target="_blank">Windows</a>、Mac和Linux的Chrome 91.0.4472.164，总共修补了其他七个安全漏洞。</p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0716/cc8ed30eb5b62d3.webp" title alt="Google___Chrome.webp" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>修复的其他漏洞包括：</p><ul class=" list-paddingleft-2"><li><p>[$7500][<a href="https://crbug.com/1219082" target="_blank" rel="noopener">1219082</a>] High CVE-2021-30559: Out of bounds write in ANGLE. Reported by Seong-Hwan Park (SeHwa) of SecunologyLab on 2021-06-11</p></li><li><p>[$5000][<a href="https://crbug.com/1214842" target="_blank" rel="noopener">1214842</a>] High CVE-2021-30541: Use after free in V8. Reported by Richard Wheeldon on 2021-05-31</p></li><li><p>[$N/A][<a href="https://crbug.com/1219209" target="_blank" rel="noopener">1219209</a>] High CVE-2021-30560: Use after free in Blink XSLT. Reported by Nick Wellnhofer on 2021-06-12</p></li><li><p>[$TBD][<a href="https://crbug.com/1219630" target="_blank" rel="noopener">1219630</a>] High CVE-2021-30561: Type Confusion in V8. Reported by Sergei Glazunov of Google Project Zero on 2021-06-14</p></li><li><p>[$TBD][<a href="https://crbug.com/1220078" target="_blank" rel="noopener">1220078</a>] High CVE-2021-30562: Use after free in WebSerial. Reported by Anonymous on 2021-06-15</p></li><li><p>[$TBD][<a href="https://crbug.com/1228407" target="_blank" rel="noopener">1228407</a>] High CVE-2021-30563: Type Confusion in V8. Reported by Anonymous on 2021-07-12</p></li><li><p>[$TBD][<a href="https://crbug.com/1221309" target="_blank" rel="noopener">1221309</a>] Medium CVE-2021-30564: Heap buffer overflow in WebXR. Reported by Ali Merchant, iQ3Connect VR Platform on 2021-06-17</p></li></ul><p>鉴于该漏洞在外部被利用，建议所有Chrome用户尽快通过进入设置>帮助>"关于Google浏览器"来更新以防止遭受攻击。</p>
