7月29日晚，华为终于正式发布了新一代P50系列旗舰机，包括P50、P50 Pro。现在，大家最关心的价格来了：<br>
<p><strong>P50 Pro：8GB+128GB 5988元、8GB+256GB 64888元、8GB+512GB 7488元。128GB版本标配骁龙888 4G。256/512GB版本首发麒麟9000，年底更换骁龙888 4G。</strong>拂晓粉、雪域白、曜金黑、可可茶金四种配色。7月30日0点预售，8月12日10:08开售。</p><p><strong>12GB+521GB 7988元、12GB+512GB典藏版8488元。配色分别为涟漪云波、曜金黑，标配麒麟9000处理器。</strong>9月开卖。</p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0729/dc328e9028c6ecd.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0729/dc328e9028c6ecd.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0729/dc328e9028c6ecd.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0729/5a2900531c47e8e.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0729/5a2900531c47e8e.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0729/5a2900531c47e8e.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0729/ebb2473317cc2ed.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0729/ebb2473317cc2ed.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0729/ebb2473317cc2ed.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0729/43e6e2c4abdf091.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0729/43e6e2c4abdf091.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0729/43e6e2c4abdf091.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0729/8f1ddd59e3d9919.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0729/8f1ddd59e3d9919.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0729/8f1ddd59e3d9919.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
