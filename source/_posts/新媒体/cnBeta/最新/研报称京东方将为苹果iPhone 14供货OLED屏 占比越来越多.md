往年的拆解显示，iPhone上最贵的几大元器件分别是屏幕、相机模块和处理器。继iPhone 12/13后，调研机构Omdia在研报中表示，预计三季度推出的iPhone 14系列上，<strong>三星、LG和京东方仍将作为三大柔性OLED面板主要供应商</strong>。<br>
 <p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220617/4aecef0504d3468399ccaf18f5a128f8.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0617/0c7ca0f5bbf2fb3.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>不过，<a data-link="1" href="https://apple.pvxt.net/c/1251234/435400/7639?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.apple.com%2Fcn%2Fmusic%2F" target="_blank">苹果</a>仍旧仅仅是从<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?k=nJ9QWa1VmJxYTPklWYmYDO5IDNy0DZp9VZ0l2cmYiJt92YuQmauc3d3ZkMlYkMlE0MlMHc0RHa9Q" target="_blank">京东</a>方采购6.06英寸的LTPS刘海屏，只用于标准版<a data-link="1" href="https://apple.pvxt.net/c/1251234/435400/7639?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.apple.com%2Fcn%2Fiphone%2F" target="_blank">iPhone</a> 14型号。6.68英寸的iPhone 14 Plus屏幕和6.12英寸的iPhone 14 Pro屏幕均由<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://samsung.jd.com/" target="_blank">三星</a>独家供应。</p><p>研报也确认，iPhone 14 Pro/Pro Max会首次采用双挖孔叹号屏，可因为屏下Face ID难度高，叹号屏会在未来几年的iPhone上持续保留。</p><p>出货比例方面，今年，京东方供屏占比翻番到15%，LGD占比25%，三星依旧最多，达到60%。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220617/eeeb23a4fb0c421895a921e267ce73c0.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0617/903294237da0c09.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220617/7f328b0ddbb043eeab116e3ef65ac341.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0617/94e1a52cac60c90.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
