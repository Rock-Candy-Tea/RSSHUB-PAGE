11月4日晚间，博主@数码闲聊站爆料，<strong>OPPO会在明年年初发布代号蝴蝶的骁龙898旗舰手机。</strong>考虑到OPPO Find X系列是OPPO的高端旗舰产品线，<strong>因此这款骁龙898新机可能是OPPO Find X4，这将是OPPO最强悍的王牌旗舰。</strong><br>
 <p>从OPPO Find X开始，每一代产品都有重要突破。以OPPO Find X3系列为例，这款高端旗舰挑战复杂工艺，在<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://shouji.jd.com/" target="_blank">手机</a>背部实现了“不可能的<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C794%2C798%26ev%3D4155_92263%26sort%3Dsort_rank_asc%26trans%3D1%26JL%3D3_%25E7%2594%25B5%25E8%25A7%2586%25E7%25B1%25BB%25E5%259E%258B_%25E6%259B%25B2%25E9%259D%25A2%23J_crumbsBar" target="_blank">曲面</a>”设计。</p><p>OPPO介绍，Find X3通过2000+控制点精密调整后盖曲线，<strong>整体采用艺术级热锻工艺使玻璃热弯、一体成型，</strong>再配合曲面玻璃镀膜工艺，最终实现了手感温润的釉质镜黑效果。</p><p>展望Find X4，该机除了拥有令人期待的工业设计之外，<strong>传闻它还会搭载OPPO自研影像芯片，</strong>影像表现可能会有大幅提升。</p><p>另外，高素质柔性<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C794%2C798%26ev%3D4155_88234%26sort%3Dsort_rank_asc%26trans%3D1%26JL%3D2_1_0%23J_crumbsBar" target="_blank">OLED</a>屏幕、超级闪充、优秀散热这些旗舰规格预计也不会缺席。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211105/904c443ad39f48ccb8ef141f110d22ef.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1105/8be65cf351edf68.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1105/8be65cf351edf68.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/1105/8be65cf351edf68.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
