育碧著名游戏系列新作《孤岛惊魂6》将于10月7日发售，媒体评分将于6日解禁。<strong>目前育碧允许游戏媒体将部分试玩视频上传至网络，IGN今日发布《孤岛惊魂6》的开场17分钟游戏视频，我们可以从中看到这款新作的实际表现。</strong><br>
<p>《孤岛惊魂6》将呈现迄今为止最大规模的孤岛惊魂战场。游戏中将会有丛林、海滩和亚拉首都等场景。在革命中心地带，玩家将投入到混乱的游击战中。玩家将使用独特和令人惊讶的新武器、载具和动物伙伴，同顽固的军事政权进行不懈战斗。</p><p>《孤岛惊魂6》将于10月7日发售，登陆PS4、PS5、<a data-link="1" href="https://microsoft.pvxt.net/e4yLO" target="_blank">Xbox</a> One、XSX/S和PC平台。</p><p>演示视频：</p><p style="text-align: center;"><iframe src="//player.youku.com/embed/XNTgxMTA2NjIyMA==?client_id=5a73c0df8eb0d91d" width="640" height="480" frameborder="0"></iframe></p><p>视频截图：</p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1005/dcc6343c9a9e91f.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1005/dcc6343c9a9e91f.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/1005/dcc6343c9a9e91f.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1005/d43af41893c61f6.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1005/d43af41893c61f6.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/1005/d43af41893c61f6.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1005/5b73f42e0a00dc6.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1005/5b73f42e0a00dc6.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/1005/5b73f42e0a00dc6.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1005/0cacc091ed3394c.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1005/0cacc091ed3394c.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/1005/0cacc091ed3394c.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1005/863ae262cd00771.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1005/863ae262cd00771.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/1005/863ae262cd00771.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
