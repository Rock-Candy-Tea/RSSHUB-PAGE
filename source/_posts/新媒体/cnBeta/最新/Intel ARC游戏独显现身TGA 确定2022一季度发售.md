尽管AMD和NV双双缺席，但Intel却低调在刚刚结束的“游戏奥斯卡”TGA颁奖礼上出镜。既然是游戏盛会，Intel的主角当然就放在了ARC锐炫显卡上。Intel ARC宇宙将覆盖台式机、笔记本和显示器（自适应刷新技术相关），<strong>官方还承诺会在明年一季度发售ARC独显。</strong><br>
 <p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211210/8625226ebb494d9aa4cf777d33435fb3.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1210/7360258e557d9f2.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1210/7360258e557d9f2.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/1210/7360258e557d9f2.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>预告视频中出现的ARC显卡采用单8Pin外接供电，从功耗判断，定位应该在RTX 3060/3070类似级别。</p><p>爆料称，Intel ARC显卡预计包含A350、A380、A550、A580、A750和A780等几款型号，集成的EU单元从96组到做多512组，功耗跨度50~275W。</p><p>值一提的是，ARC显卡的GPU核心采用台积电6nm工艺制造。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211210/25934c93010e49d78e844dd73316bf9c.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1210/0ae03b80776405c.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1210/0ae03b80776405c.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/1210/0ae03b80776405c.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
