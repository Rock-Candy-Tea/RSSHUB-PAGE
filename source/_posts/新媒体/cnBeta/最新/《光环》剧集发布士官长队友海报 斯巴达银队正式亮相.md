<strong>在此前公布的《光环》真人剧集完整预告中，士官长的新队友Riz-028、Vannak-134和Kai-125得以亮相，士官长也将和三人组成斯巴达银队。</strong>官方本次放出了士官长三名队友的角色海报，一起来欣赏一下。<br>
 <p style="text-align:center"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/upimg/pic/2022/02/24/origin_202202241441334779.jpg"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0224/0db5ad02761f6fd.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0224/0db5ad02761f6fd.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0224/0db5ad02761f6fd.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p style="text-align:center"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/upimg/pic/2022/02/24/origin_202202241441396019.jpg"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0224/000087244859119.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0224/000087244859119.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0224/000087244859119.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p style="text-align:center"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/upimg/pic/2022/02/24/origin_202202241441445647.jpg"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0224/a3cbb179d0c74f0.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0224/a3cbb179d0c74f0.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0224/a3cbb179d0c74f0.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p style="text-align: left;">《光环》剧集将于今年3月24日在派拉蒙+平台首播，士官长将由演员巴勃罗·施雷伯饰演。据此前的报道称《光环》<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C794%2C798%26ev%3D4155_76344%26sort%3Dsort_rank_asc%26trans%3D1%26JL%3D2_1_0%23J_crumbsBar" target="_blank">电视</a>剧集将在该系列历史上首次披露主角“士官长”的面孔，他将在本剧中摘下其标志性的头盔，露出他的面容。</p><p style="text-align: left;">剧集根据经典游戏《光环》改编，系列主要故事围绕26世纪时，人类与外星种族之间的史诗级宇宙战争展开。</p>
