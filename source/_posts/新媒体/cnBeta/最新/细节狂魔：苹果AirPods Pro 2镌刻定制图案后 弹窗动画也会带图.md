昨晚和iPhone 14系列同时开启预定的，还有全新升级的AirPods Pro 2无线耳机，官网价格1899元。这次除了硬件上的升级之外，还有一个非常细节的功能，<strong>苹果为弹窗动画也同步了定制图案。</strong><br>
 <p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220910/024ae66cec0047c5ab7dd4105522e764.png" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0910/a587fb6ddfb453a.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0910/a587fb6ddfb453a.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0910/a587fb6ddfb453a.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>苹果介绍中提到，将AirPods Pro靠近你的<a data-link="1" href="https://apple.pvxt.net/c/1251234/435400/7639?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.apple.com%2Fcn%2Fiphone%2F" target="_blank">iPhone</a>，然后轻点“连接”，它就会顺畅地与你iCloud账户中的每个设备配对。</p><p><strong>这时如果你的充电盒上镌刻了定制内容，该镌刻内容也会呈现在配对设备的屏幕上，整个体验更加个性化。</strong></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220910/df79b4ee1b2e4e609ff0045e240d7698.png" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0910/0733e1d82e756a4.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0910/0733e1d82e756a4.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0910/0733e1d82e756a4.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>同时，之前的镌刻只能单独选择文字或符号或表情，<strong>现在可以混搭了，文字、数字和表情符号能同时出现，个性化属性更强。</strong></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220910/15805c23ce8046a6af69a0f9762b39ab.png" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0910/2e169356284026c.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0910/2e169356284026c.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0910/2e169356284026c.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><br>
