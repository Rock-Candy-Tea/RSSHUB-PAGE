6月10日，奥迪A8L Horch创始人版正式在中国市场上市，<strong>新车共推出四款车型，售价127.48-173.56万元</strong>。作为奥迪当之无愧的旗舰轿车，奥迪A8L Horch创始人版就是为了对标迈巴赫S级而生的。<br>
 <p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220611/2a95d0fc32c24638b520f7b7227971ab.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0611/eb1ae9541a53211.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0611/eb1ae9541a53211.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0611/eb1ae9541a53211.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>该车基于普通版A8L打造而来，采用了相似的设计语言，多边形前格栅搭配镀铬的条幅式的中网，并搭载了数字矩阵式LED大灯，看上去格外闪亮。</p><p>此外，Horch版还增加了云雨灰和橡木绿两种专属车漆，以彰显新车的独特身份。</p><p><a class="impage" href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220404/a790ac6d5bd845e98a46e5be67955f1f.jpg" style="color: rgb(0, 153, 255);" target="_self"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0611/5c24223b6cdec96.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0611/5c24223b6cdec96.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0611/5c24223b6cdec96.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a class="impage" href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220404/349207af8ecf4e50ba24736295006b5f.jpg" style="color: red; text-decoration-line: none;" target="_self"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0611/c32bd3528f9a9ee.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>新车通过加长车身后半部分来提升整车长度以及轴距，长度为5.45米，<strong>相比普通版的奥迪A8L加长了130mm</strong>，加长的部分使得整体车身比例更加修长，后排空间也进一步增加。</p><p>值得一提的是，新车使用了镀铬装饰与大饼镀铬样式的20英寸轮圈，使得气场得到进一步提升。</p><p><a class="impage" href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220404/0a4078ebb9864a5e8fd5a7b8f27dd13e.jpg" style="color: red; text-decoration-line: none; font-family: " open hiragino sans microsoft wenquanyi micro font-size: target="_self"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0611/1a2e91e07049e89.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a class="impage" href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220404/8272286cc5294046b8a37a718e945054.jpg" style="color: red; text-decoration-line: none; font-family: " open hiragino sans microsoft wenquanyi micro font-size: target="_self"><img alt="对标迈巴赫S级 奥迪A8L Horch创始人版预定：限106台、售130万起" h="391" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220404/8272286cc5294046b8a37a718e945054.jpg" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>该车依旧使用了奥迪数字虚拟座舱，<strong>配有全液晶显示屏以及三辐式多功能方向盘，全触控MMI操作系统</strong>，中控多媒体显示屏采用内嵌式设计，下方还搭配有触摸式的车内功能控制系统。</p><p>另外，新车的<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C794%2C870%26ev%3D1554_584894%26JL%3D3_%25E7%25A9%25BA%25E8%25B0%2583%25E7%25B1%25BB%25E5%2588%25AB_%25E7%25AB%258B%25E6%259F%259C%25E5%25BC%258F%25E7%25A9%25BA%25E8%25B0%2583%23J_crumbsBar" target="_blank">空调</a>出风口采用隐藏式设计，开启时非常有仪式感。</p><p><a class="impage" href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220404/93c0284b32cc461180e78814033b6140.jpg" style="color: red; text-decoration-line: none;" target="_self"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0611/1875d7db976dbaf.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>动力方面，新车将延续现款车型上的<strong>3.0T涡轮增压V6发动机+48V轻混，最大可输出340马力，峰值扭矩500牛米</strong>，匹配8速手自一体变速箱，并标配quattro四驱系统。</p>
