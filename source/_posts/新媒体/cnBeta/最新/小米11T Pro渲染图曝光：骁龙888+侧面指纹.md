9月1日消息，推特上曝光了小米11T Pro渲染图。如图所示，小米11T Pro背部为矩阵相机设计，同时采用侧面指纹方案。核心配置上，与上一代小米10T Pro对比，<strong>小米11T Pro屏幕最大的变化是采用了OLED全面屏（小米10T Pro采用LCD全面屏），二者都是FHD+分辨率。</strong><br>
 <p>此外，小米11T Pro搭载高通骁龙888旗舰处理器，电池容量为5000mAh，传闻支持120W快充。</p><p>值得注意的是，<strong>小米11T系列除了Pro款之外还有标准版小米11T。</strong></p><p>预计小米11T的工业设计与小米11T Pro不会有太大差异，区别在于规格。爆料称小米11T采用的是联发科旗舰处理器，可能是联发科天玑1200，二者都是<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C794%2C798%26ev%3D4155_88234%26sort%3Dsort_rank_asc%26trans%3D1%26JL%3D2_1_0%23J_crumbsBar" target="_blank">OLED</a>高刷屏幕。</p><p>该机有可能会在本月下旬发布，<strong>目前已经获得了FCC认证，</strong>尚不确定是否会在国内亮相。</p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0901/3a33441e1a9f45f.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0901/3a33441e1a9f45f.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0901/3a33441e1a9f45f.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0901/26f3d573b0f2b5a.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0901/26f3d573b0f2b5a.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0901/26f3d573b0f2b5a.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0901/5b63fecef1b114b.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0901/5b63fecef1b114b.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0901/5b63fecef1b114b.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0901/232aa198250e153.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0901/232aa198250e153.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0901/232aa198250e153.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
