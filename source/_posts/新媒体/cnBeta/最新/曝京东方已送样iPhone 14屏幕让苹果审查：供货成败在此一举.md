京东方今年能否为iPhone 14供应屏幕，成败似乎在此一举了！据TheElec报道，<strong>苹果将在本周开始审查京东方送样的iPhone 14 OLED面板试制品。</strong>如果一切顺利的话，京东方将在7月、8月开始大规模量产备货相关面板。<br>
 <p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0529/b2892ebc97b828d.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0529/b2892ebc97b828d.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>当然，在此之前，<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://samsung.jd.com/" target="_blank">三星</a>和LG已经获得<a data-link="1" href="https://apple.pvxt.net/c/1251234/435400/7639?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.apple.com%2Fcn%2Fmusic%2F" target="_blank">苹果</a>点头，开始热火朝天地投产<a data-link="1" href="https://apple.pvxt.net/c/1251234/435400/7639?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.apple.com%2Fcn%2Fiphone%2F" target="_blank">iPhone</a> 14系列所需屏幕了。</p><p>本次报道依然强调，<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?k=nJ9QWa1VmJxYTPklWYmYDO5IDNy0DZp9VZ0l2cmYiJt92YuQmauc3d3ZkMlYkMlE0MlMHc0RHa9Q" target="_blank">京东</a>方此前因调整了iPhone 13 <a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C794%2C798%26ev%3D4155_88234%26sort%3Dsort_rank_asc%26trans%3D1%26JL%3D2_1_0%23J_crumbsBar" target="_blank">OLED</a>面板的薄膜晶体管线宽，导致被苹果叫停。但后续沟通后，京东方依然争取到了为iPhone 14供货的机会，且iPhone 13的供货也开始恢复。</p><p>不过，因为iPhone 13的风波，这次京东方预计将只为苹果供应500万块左右的OLED面板。</p><p>根据此前Omdia的研报，这一代iPhone 14系列上，京东方将仅参与6.1寸iPhone 14的屏幕供货（刘海屏），6.7寸iPhone 14 Plus、6.1寸iPhone 14 Pro均由三星独家供应，6.7寸的iPhone 14 Pro Max则是三星、LG双供。</p>
