近日，网络上曝光了smart精灵#1实车照片，<strong>该车车身采用明快的黄色，看起来十分抢眼。</strong>此次曝光的实车与此前发布的官图基本一致，整体圆润柔和，全新的设计语言让它不再是曾经smart车型的样子。概念车上贯穿式的前大灯得以保留，<strong>但曝光的车型并未采用官图的透镜式大灯</strong>，所以推测为低配版车型。<br>
 <p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220424/ad3e62e23ee14fe0b27e7b630e9a5d4f.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0424/81e731f547c2694.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0424/81e731f547c2694.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0424/81e731f547c2694.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220424/8ece4ff81a624428af2623e210a965f0.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0424/cc43ba9ad022d2b.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0424/cc43ba9ad022d2b.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0424/cc43ba9ad022d2b.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>同时新车配备了隐藏式门把手和无框车门，使得档次感得到进一步提升，此外，实车采用了与官图、此前曝光的高性能版本都不同的轮圈样式，看起来更加简洁。</p><p>值得注意的是，该车后方还有一台黑红搭配的smart精灵#1，而它的身份大概率就是高性能版。</p><p>相比于普通版本的smart精灵#1，<strong>新车车身增加了红色撞色设计</strong>，在车头增加了进气口，并且保险杠两侧的进气造型也更加运动。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220424/527f6374bec94cf7b99779edcf123403.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0424/80aaadd921cde66.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220424/c0b1fc5522c140aaa36d64b0f989d418.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0424/bbedd10601c26a2.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220424/6cdc6ad92cd14fb1a023e05442e21688.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0424/196bc47ab628043.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>据了解，smart精灵#1由梅赛德斯-奔驰全球设计团队操刀设计的smart精灵#1，基于吉利浩瀚SEA架构打造而来并在华实现国产化生产。</p><p>其<strong>长宽高分别为4270*1822*1636mm，轴距2750mm</strong>，定位于小型纯电动SUV，整车风阻系数仅为0.29Cd。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220408/5da70a46330544c59e6c74ebc3b5ce4c.png" style="color: red; text-decoration-line: none;" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0424/f68b703ec0a8d76.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0424/f68b703ec0a8d76.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0424/f68b703ec0a8d76.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220408/bafb812cdba045d89aa059e4defbd248.png" style="color: rgb(0, 153, 255); text-decoration-line: none;" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0424/03861b3aaf2f2af.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0424/03861b3aaf2f2af.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0424/03861b3aaf2f2af.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>普通版搭载了一台后置电机，最大功率200kW，峰值扭矩343Nm，配备有66kWh三元锂电池组，<strong>CLTC最大续航里程560km，官方公布的0-100km/h加速时间6.7秒</strong>。</p><p>而性能版大概率会搭载前后双电机，零百加速时间预计会进入到5秒之内，甚至是4秒俱乐部，当然价格估计也会水涨船高。</p>
