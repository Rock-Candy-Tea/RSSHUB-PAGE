威刚科技今天宣布推出新的威刚Premier Extreme SDXC SD Express卡。得益于最新的SD
7.0规范，支持PCIe和NVMe，Premier Extreme SDXC SD 7.0
Express卡在性能上与SSD相当，但却有着SD卡的超便携外形。<br>
<p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1006/8709456c1ee2bb4.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/1006/8709456c1ee2bb4.jpg" title alt="yc6O8So55ycLds7X.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>由于支持PCIe Gen 3 x1和NVMe，Premier Extreme SDXC SD Express卡的读写性能高达800/700 MB/s，大约是SATA <a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://list.jd.com/list.html?cat=670,677,11303" target="_blank">SSD</a>的1.5倍。当与UHS-II SD和UHS-I SD卡相比，它的速度分别约为2.7和8倍。该存储卡向后兼容UHS-I设备，读写速度100MB/s，符合V30视频速度等级。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1006/dac06c01d4b0607.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/1006/dac06c01d4b0607.jpg" title alt="db0gfkULzpZPMHg9.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>它可以直接插入数码相机拍摄高质量的照片或<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C794%2C798%26ev%3D4155_110018%26sort%3Dsort_rank_asc%26trans%3D1%26JL%3D2_1_0%23J_crumbsBar" target="_blank">4K</a>视频，然后插入支持SD 7.0的PC读卡器，进行无缝编辑或播放。容量从256GB到512GB不等。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1006/d477d2df82983f8.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/1006/d477d2df82983f8.jpg" title alt="wUNXPU4DHI4L1Mxt.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>Premier Extreme SDXC SD Express卡里的数据安全有额外保障的。它具有纠错代码，以确保数据的准确性。更重要的是，它防水、防震、防X射线、防静电，并经过测试在高温和低温下都能保持最佳运行状态。</p><p>ADATA Premier Extreme SDXC SD 7.0 Express卡提供终身保修服务，目前价格暂未公布。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1006/d3fe3b110165c67.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/1006/d3fe3b110165c67.jpg" title alt="eRkGvcuHKwK11Jt0.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1006/55364f87e344802.jpg" target="_blank"><img
