<strong>在本月初做过预告之后，一加今天宣布一加 10R 的“Prime Blue Edition”新蓝配色将于 9 月 22 日在印度市场发售。</strong>根据亚马逊印度产品页提供的信息，Prime Blue Edition 只是在颜色方面有所差异，外观设计和内部规格都基本保持相同。<br>
<p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0920/6de3a83783707e8.webp" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p style="text-align: left;">OnePlus 10R Prime Edition 将配备 Dimensity 8100 Max SoC、OxygenOS 12 和后置三摄像头设置，包括 50MP 主摄像头、8MP 超宽摄像头和 2MP 微距单元。</p><p style="text-align: left;">促销页面还提到了 4,500 mAh 电池和 150W 充电，但它指出这是 Sierra Black 型号的 12GB/256GB 版本独有的。因此，OnePlus 10R Prime Edition 很有可能会匹配充电速度更快的 10R 版本，而不是更大的电池版本。亚马逊声称OnePlus 10R Prime Blue Edition将配备“impossibly-fast charging”（超快的快充）。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0920/3e0f94031eeebc1.webp" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0920/9625ec8cb14b264.webp" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0920/6e3fd59cec6f4b8.webp" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0920/7775699dbb45dba.webp" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0920/a92dd6dc328d2ae.webp" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0920/19841aea1e56376.webp" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0920/c9e2f3cad4aa990.webp" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0920/77ee27667efbb20.webp" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0920/3ead1df62da52f8.webp" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0920/26ac9f4f17e2635.webp" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0920/08a0509942f567f.webp" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
