在网飞举行的网络发布会“噔噔”上，该公司带来了根据游戏《英雄联盟》改编的动画作品《奥术（Arcane）》的首个预告片。<br>
<p style="text-align: center;"><iframe src="//player.youku.com/embed/XNTgwOTEyMTg4NA==?client_id=5a73c0df8eb0d91d" width="640" height="480" frameborder="0"></iframe></p><p>作为《英雄联盟》改编的首部动画，《奥术》首次于2019年公开，故事场景设在宛如乌托邦的皮尔托福，以及饱受压迫的地下城佐恩，叙述两个著名联盟冠军的起源，以及在未来摧毁他们的强权。</p><p>《奥术》将在2021年11月6日上线Netflix。</p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0926/667b87911893e06.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0926/667b87911893e06.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0926/667b87911893e06.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0926/2a776db88691cdc.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0926/2a776db88691cdc.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0926/2a776db88691cdc.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0926/0792768a663f99b.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0926/0792768a663f99b.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0926/0792768a663f99b.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0926/1ac37e9040e44fe.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0926/1ac37e9040e44fe.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0926/1ac37e9040e44fe.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0926/2b02e7bcc97fa8f.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0926/2b02e7bcc97fa8f.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0926/2b02e7bcc97fa8f.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
