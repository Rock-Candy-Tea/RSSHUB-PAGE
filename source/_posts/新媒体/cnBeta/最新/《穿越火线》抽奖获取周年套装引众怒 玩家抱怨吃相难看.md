根据玩家反馈，《穿越火线》今年的周年套装获取方式从商城直售改为抽奖，这引发了大量玩家不满，玩家们发起了<strong>#万人请辞穿越火线策划#</strong>，随后，话题<strong>#穿越火线#</strong>也登上了微博热搜。<br>
 <p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0801/4f30d8ef830ed59.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0801/7ca529880a47a58.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0801/d07a60c8ea6118a.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>而根据贴吧用户“王者之契”的分析，玩家脸黑保底一把“M200-竞技荣光”可能需要花2k以上，套装集齐估计在3k左右，而此前年套直售时总价为1400元，现在价格十分劝退。并且“王者之契”还表示这次的套装武器性能也不够强大。</p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0801/a29996e67ad4072.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0801/a29996e67ad4072.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0801/a29996e67ad4072.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0801/5544799dee08e9f.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0801/5544799dee08e9f.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0801/5544799dee08e9f.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0801/e0bebd07e8cb9d6.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0801/e0bebd07e8cb9d6.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0801/e0bebd07e8cb9d6.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
