路特斯将于<strong>6月1日</strong>于国内正式发布旗下首款纯电车型ELETRE。该车在3月底已全球首发，其将在<strong>武汉智能工厂实现国产化生产</strong>，首批车辆将于2023年开启交付，价格或足够惊喜。ELETRE定位于中大型SUV，尺寸为5103/2019/1630mm，轴距为3019mm，基于EPA平台打造，采用前后布局的双电动机四轮驱动。<br>
 <p><a class="impage" href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220330/f0b54978504a4f2f92939e0a5c6becb8.png" style="color: red; text-decoration-line: none; font-family: " open hiragino sans microsoft wenquanyi micro font-size: target="_self"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0524/4ba2177ec0888b0.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0524/4ba2177ec0888b0.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0524/4ba2177ec0888b0.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>新车采用了极富张力的设计，配有分体式灯组，上方是造型犀利的LED日间行车灯，内部采用了纵向布局排列的设计；下方的远近光灯组隐藏在前包围两侧，而前包围整体环抱式的造型设计使其拥有强烈的攻击属性。</p><p><a class="impage" href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220330/7de6bfab1d714df8b4f67cb63998426a.png" style="color: red; text-decoration-line: none; font-family: " open hiragino sans microsoft wenquanyi micro font-size: target="_self"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0524/aa78bed0d304ec8.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0524/aa78bed0d304ec8.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0524/aa78bed0d304ec8.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>此外，新车前唇部分采用了主动式散热开口设计，保证了车身良好的空气动力学属性，<strong>可提供51公斤的下压力</strong>。</p><p>侧面采用了撞色设计，同时通过特殊设计，营造了非常硬朗且流畅的视觉效果，与兰博基尼Urus倒是有异曲同工之妙。</p><p><a class="impage" href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220330/2b9c969bf08944b2b3e7ac0a212d3587.png" style="color: red; text-decoration-line: none; font-family: " open hiragino sans microsoft wenquanyi micro font-size: target="_self"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0524/af3f9bfcd9e6a21.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0524/af3f9bfcd9e6a21.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0524/af3f9bfcd9e6a21.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>其还配备了23英寸的双色刀锋式五辐轮圈、隐藏式门把手以及小尺寸的电子后视镜，细节搭配均是让车身能够营造出更小的风阻系数。</p><p>新车尾部同样富有设计感，车顶配有双片式后扰流板，用上了贯穿式LED尾灯，两侧则拥有“气流通道”，视觉效果霸气，还能保证良好的空气动力学。</p><p><a class="impage" href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220330/da6deabb211143f8b82f9b86d60ea0c0.png" style="color: red; text-decoration-line: none; font-family: " open hiragino sans microsoft wenquanyi micro font-size: target="_self"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0524/761cd3ad6582688.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0524/761cd3ad6582688.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0524/761cd3ad6582688.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>内部则是极致的科技风，<strong>除配备5块<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C794%2C798%26ev%3D4155_110018%26sort%3Dsort_rank_asc%26trans%3D1%26JL%3D2_1_0%23J_crumbsBar" target="_blank">4K</a>液晶屏</strong>，位于中控台的贯穿式灯带可通过色彩变幻反映不同的车辆状态，而车内配备的中控大屏支持折叠开启，内置双高通8155车机芯片兼容5G网络，支持OTA升级及语音识别技术。</p><p><a class="impage" href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220330/2e24b62da0234423a2b43319e0798525.png" style="color: rgb(0, 153, 255);" target="_self"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0524/cc07d374c7f7ead.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0524/cc07d374c7f7ead.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0524/cc07d374c7f7ead.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a class="impage" href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220330/155fa0122ae14b26b479e4d50b9f6e51.png" style="color: rgb(0, 153, 255);" target="_self"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0524/741c8b24786d348.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0524/741c8b24786d348.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0524/741c8b24786d348.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a class="impage" href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220330/1aac6086a28742baa4db9859c2692fee.png" style="color: red;
