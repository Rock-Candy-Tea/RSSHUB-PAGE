今日消息，Phone Arena测试了华硕Zenfone 9的续航，称赞“<strong>Zenfone 9迷你机身、Pro Max级续航</strong>”，表现令人惊讶、难以置信。先来看下华硕Zenfone 9和竞品的电池数据，<strong>Zenfone 9电池容量为4300mAh，iPhone 13点电池容量为3227mAh，三星Galaxy S22电池容量为3700mAh。</strong><br>
 <p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220801/5ff7958508c64e059ff8c6bc05ff3b71.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0801/2f1e5ab607f2926.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0801/2f1e5ab607f2926.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0801/2f1e5ab607f2926.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>在YouTube视频播放测试环节，<strong>华硕Zenfone 9续航长达12小时14分钟，轻松击败<a data-link="1" href="https://apple.pvxt.net/c/1251234/435400/7639?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.apple.com%2Fcn%2Fiphone%2F" target="_blank">iPhone</a> 13、<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://samsung.jd.com/" target="_blank">三星</a>Galaxy S22，iPhone 13 mini自然也被碾压。</strong></p><p>在3D Gaming测试中，华硕Zenfone 9续航仍然是完胜，表现大幅领先三星Galaxy S22和iPhone 13 mini，略微领先iPhone 13。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220801/d39090afdd81400dbb482beb3b770a46.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0801/8ac3f717bd91156.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0801/8ac3f717bd91156.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0801/8ac3f717bd91156.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>Phone Arena表示，华硕Zenfone 9虽然尺寸很小（其屏幕尺寸为5.9英寸），但是它提供了令人难以置信的电池续航。</p><p>该机起售价799欧元（约合人民币5500元）。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220801/de276b8bd02b4c5a8c48b3d233c4975b.png" target="_blank"><img alt="小屏旗舰华硕Zenfone 9续航令老外惊讶：比iPhone 13和三星S22都牛" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220801/de276b8bd02b4c5a8c48b3d233c4975b.png" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
