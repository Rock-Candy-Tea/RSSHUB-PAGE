<div>   
在 Realme GT 新品之后，该公司似乎也做好了推出所谓“大师版”的准备。<strong>@OnLeaks 与 91Mobiles 刚刚分享了 Realme GT“Master Edition”的设计、规格与售价等详情，据说该机的起价为 399 欧元（约 3056 RMB）。</strong>预计它将配备打孔屏前摄 + 后置三摄的方案，辅以皮革饰面的选项。<br>
<p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0705/27b596252befbfe.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0705/27b596252befbfe.jpg" alt="1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>除了高清分辨率的渲染图，@OnLeaks 还携手 91Mobiles 分享了 Realme GT“Master Edition”的定价和主要规格。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0705/d0e405b00ae3e2b.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0705/d0e405b00ae3e2b.jpg" alt="2.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>渲染图完整展示了 Realme GT“Master Edition”的完整设计与三总配色选项，且其中一种带有皮革饰面。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0705/58fed91db2efc0b.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0705/58fed91db2efc0b.jpg" alt="3.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>拍照方面，该机采用了打孔屏前摄 + 矩形后置三摄的方案，整体设计看起来相当养眼。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0705/ba338a8a4699609.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0705/ba338a8a4699609.jpg" alt="4.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>其它主要规格包括高通骁龙 778 5G 芯片组，支持 120Hz 高刷新率的 Super AM<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C794%2C798%26ev%3D4155_88234%26sort%3Dsort_rank_asc%26trans%3D1%26JL%3D2_1_0%23J_crumbsBar" target="_blank">OLED</a> 屏幕，以及 65W Warp 闪充等。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0705/1fb7b9bcc0611cd.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0705/1fb7b9bcc0611cd.jpg" alt="5.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>除了哑光饰面的纯白与黑色外观，Realme GT“大师版”还提供了带有人造皮革饰面的选项。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0705/200264788444dee.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0705/200264788444dee.jpg" alt="6.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>据说皮革饰面版本是与日本设计师深泽直人（Naoto Fukusawa）联手打造。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0705/9508001fab688b1.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0705/9508001fab688b1.jpg" alt="7.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>机身正面采用了无边框全面屏设计，机身背部边缘则带有柔和的弧形，并于左上角嵌入了矩形的后置三摄 + LED 闪光灯模块。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0705/6c88e0a34cf2761.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0705/6c88e0a34cf2761.jpg" alt="8.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>机身底部可见到 3.5mm 耳机插孔、麦克风、USB-C 充电 / 数据端口、以及扬声器格栅。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0705/f96670ab75b5618.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0705/f96670ab75b5618.jpg" alt="9.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>电源键安排在了机身右侧，而音量调节和 SIM 卡槽则被安排在了机身左侧。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0705/662bfcef19944f1.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0705/662bfcef19944f1.jpg" alt="10.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>配置方面，据说 Realme GT“大师版”提供了 8GB RAM + 256GB ROM / 12GB RAM + 256GB ROM 的选项，定价分别为 399 / 449 欧元（约 3056 / 3439 RMB）。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0705/2ec4bfadec57f90.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0705/2ec4bfadec57f90.jpg" alt="11.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>虽然爆料人 Steve Hemmerstoffer 透露 Realme 曾计划提供 349 欧元（约 2673 RMB）的 6GB RAM + 128GB ROM 版本，但该机型可能已被取消。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0705/bb2eddf05d32f50.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0705/bb2eddf05d32f50.jpg" alt="12.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>其它方面，Realme GT“大师版”采用了支持 120Hz 高刷新率的 6.43 英寸 @ FHD+ 分辨率的 Super AMOLED 屏 + 屏下指纹传感器方案。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0705/2d8281559f28023.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0705/2d8281559f28023.jpg" alt="13.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>Realme GT“大师版”机身厚度为 8mm、重 174g，且预装了基于 Android 11 定制的 Realme UI 2.0 。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0705/6b6d4c9d319c5fd.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0705/6b6d4c9d319c5fd.jpg" alt="14.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>预计前置单摄为 32MP 传感器，后置则是 64MP 主摄 + 8MP 超广角 + 2MP 深度传感器的三摄组合。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0705/7957e8f4af876a8.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0705/7957e8f4af876a8.jpg" alt="15.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>不过在早前的一份报告中，爆料人曾声称该机有望采用与 Realme GT 旗舰机相同的 1.08 亿像素的主摄。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0705/84ab2143f30602d.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0705/84ab2143f30602d.jpg" alt="22.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>最后，爆料称 Realme GT“大师版”还内置了支持 65W SuperDart 闪充的 4300 mAh 电池。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0705/e5fddf12199b481.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0705/e5fddf12199b481.jpg"
