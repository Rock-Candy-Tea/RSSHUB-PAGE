12代酷睿的秘密越来越少，随着发布时间的临近，相关型号的“出镜”频率也越来越高。<strong>PugetBenchmark官网出现了一台搭载酷睿i9-12900K处理器的平台电脑，主板ROG
STRIX Z690-E Gaming WiFi，搭配64GB DDR5-4800内存，RTX 3090独显。</strong><br>
 <p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0822/a94dfffdc39f150.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0822/a94dfffdc39f150.jpg" title alt="Intel-Core-i9-12900K-Alder-Lake-Desktop-CPU.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>三大件都是最先进的存在，Adobe After Effects典型跑分1541，最高1575。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0822/c1bf35ebab0a75a.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0822/c1bf35ebab0a75a.jpg" title alt="Intel-Architecture-Day-2021_Pressdeck_Final_EMBARGO-compressed-075.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><strong>对比酷睿i9-11900K平台（1548），成绩相差微乎其微，略微有些让人失望，目前只能用工程版需要进一步调优来自我安慰了。</strong></p><p>据已经掌握的资料，Core i9-12900K为16核设计，基于Intel 7工艺（10nm），集成8个Golden Cove大核，8个Gracemont小核，总计24逻辑线程，双核加速频率5.3GHz，功耗125瓦。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0822/49b6d83328778be.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0822/49b6d83328778be.png" title alt="XPH)$3&#123;HYMYF1~$6%3X@@NR.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>Intel宣称，Golden Cove的IPC提升了19%。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210822/e45dab9f1e5a4b0b9438ba6d82cca24b.png" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0822/84e3cdebbb5f845.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0822/84e3cdebbb5f845.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0822/84e3cdebbb5f845.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
