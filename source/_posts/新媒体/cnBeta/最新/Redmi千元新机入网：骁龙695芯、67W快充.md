Redmi K50宇宙首款新机将在虎年正式发布，凭借新骁龙8、120W神仙秒充等旗舰配置，该机受到众多消费者期待。值得一提的是，除了这款旗舰机型外，<strong>爆料称Redmi还将带来一款中低端机型，预计会在千元档价位。</strong><br>
 <p>日前，<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&aid=450&euid=&t=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.mi.com%2F" target="_blank">Redmi</a>一款5G新机入网，从配置来看该机正是此前曝光的中低端机型。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220129/25e665765ceb427fb7997f6b33aa6912.png" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0129/13bc9eea7da3293.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0129/13bc9eea7da3293.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0129/13bc9eea7da3293.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>结合入网信息与爆料来看，<strong>该机将搭载骁龙695芯片，拥有一块6.67英寸<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C794%2C798%26ev%3D4155_88234%26sort%3Dsort_rank_asc%26trans%3D1%26JL%3D2_1_0%23J_crumbsBar" target="_blank">OLED</a>屏，</strong>分辨率为2400*1080，支持120Hz高刷，内置5000mAh大容量电池，支持67W快充，后置1.08亿主摄。</p><p>其他方面，新机可选6/8/12/16GB内存，64/128/256/512GB存储，重量为202g，8.12mm厚，提供红、橙、黄、蓝等多款配色。</p><p>从博主@数码闲聊站 晒出的渲染图来看，<strong>该机采用类似Redmi Note 11系列</strong><strong>外观</strong><strong>设计。</strong></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220129/80c4ae2b841443aea4a3e7977e02a34d.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0129/9b8277b978d3fac.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>值得一提的是，此前Redmi在海外推出了Redmi Note 11 Pro，海外版与国行最大的变化在于处理器，海外版搭载高通骁龙690芯片，而国行版搭载联发科天玑920芯片。</p><p>不出意外，Redmi这款5G新机将会成为国内市场的新一代千元爆款机型。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220129/2d1d32472b4a4dc392eb9c071e3f65eb.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0129/bd493a8ec80c89d.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
