今日，上海下冰雹冲上热搜，目前上海处于<strong>冰雹、暴雨、大风、雷电、高温“三黄两橙”五预警天气。</strong>从新闻晨报放出的视频中可以看到，<strong>上海部分地区已经下起了冰雹，尺寸大如鹅卵石。</strong>据了解，冰雹属于强对流天气的一种，一般发生在春季、夏季和秋季。<br>
 <p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220806/deba1a0dca7948a5a1ad26b1abeb5581.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0806/55e48760439f6bb.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>其中，夏天天气炎热，太阳把大地烤得滚烫，近地面极易形成不稳定的湿热空气，当高空有弱冷空气时，<strong>“上冷下暖”将引起空气的强烈对流，为冰雹的形成创造了极佳的条件。</strong></p><p>不少网友还晒出了冰雹图并表示，“大风大雨，还飘点冰渣子，看不清”、“上午还艳阳高照，下午就狂风暴雨”、“晾晒的衣服要重新淘洗了”。</p><p>应急管理部门提醒，突遇冰雹后，<strong>室外人员应及时躲进建筑物，避免高压线、电线杠以及大树下躲避冰雹，室外停放的车辆，及时移进地库。</strong></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220806/cae3a74511744ceaa7bd810e91254719.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0806/bd4b4c56298cafb.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220806/eac7cba8a29346c5a47ca0c6aacfae41.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0806/17a1efbf21cdef9.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220806/53c6926b17fe4144b0e6fea08317fa62.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0806/809732fbd3fe04e.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220806/783fc3ac8245476e940afc2c43798f05.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0806/af923cdcd9cecf7.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220806/eb5975bdd8334feab8e8468664e5e40c.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0806/66c007486a29dba.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
