苹果秋季新品发布会定档北京时间9月15日凌晨1点，iPhone 13无疑将是绝对主角。配套iPhone 13的，当然就是iOS 15正式版系统了。部分用户发现，<strong>Tips APP（“提示”APP）已经开始推送iOS 15的预热通知，题为“来瞧瞧iOS 15的新鲜元素”。</strong><br>
 <p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0908/85cf0625a1e5641.webp" title alt="ios-15-previewtips.webp" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>点击通知横幅后会进入iOS 15新功能特性的介绍，包括Safari标签分组、专注模式、系统级翻译、AR地图等。</p><p>之后，还有名为iOS 15预览版的体验，通过模拟新功能界面，教你快速上手iOS 15。</p><p>目前iOS 15已经发布了八个测试版，看起来<a data-link="1" href="https://apple.pvxt.net/c/1251234/435400/7639?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.apple.com%2Fcn%2Fmusic%2F" target="_blank">苹果</a>的准备工作比较充分，希望系统的最终稳定性会高些。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210908/5f03c759231a47a986f2e744b0828fc3.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0908/a3e4c03b4cb2327.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210908/b127525aadec47f68e29a525f13a82c9.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0908/66265b15043944b.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210908/c412a9fe6ddf4aa58c5794ec0a5db0af.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0908/4594bd1164d638b.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210908/3f8753f7542f4af780eecddc938b2704.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0908/27742c2f97d3977.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
