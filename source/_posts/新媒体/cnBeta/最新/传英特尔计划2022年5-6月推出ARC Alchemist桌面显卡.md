<strong>Igor's Lab 爆料称，英特尔计划在 2022 年初夏，推出基于 Xe-HPG 图形架构的 ARC“Alchemist”台式游戏显卡。</strong>如果能够在 5 月 2 日 - 6 月 1 日之间发布，ARC Alchemist 有望在夏季满足部分市场需求。<br>
 <p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0309/173c6e2d3ebe7af.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0309/173c6e2d3ebe7af.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p style="text-align: center;">（资料图 via <a href="https://www.igorslab.de/en/the-geforce-rtx-3070-ti-16gb-doesnt-come-into-arc-promises-again-and-the-gddr6x-is-itching/" target="_self">Igor's Lab</a>）</p><p>销售渠道方面，推测<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://intel.jd.com/" target="_blank">英特尔</a>会同已经与其建立牢固合作伙伴关系的少数主板厂商来提供。</p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0309/a9f2f3cff9f57a0.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p style="text-align: center;">（截图 via <a href="https://www.techpowerup.com/292776/intel-plans-may-june-2022-launches-of-arc-alchemist-desktop-graphics-cards" target="_self">TechPowerUp</a>）</p><p>定位方面，ARC Alchemist 将瞄准从性能到入门的四个细分市场。高端型号能够最大限度发挥 AAA 游戏的性能，而 XeSS 又可缓解 <a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C794%2C798%26ev%3D4155_110018%26sort%3Dsort_rank_asc%26trans%3D1%26JL%3D2_1_0%23J_crumbsBar" target="_blank">4K</a> 超高清游戏的流畅运行压力。</p>
