这两年，没有一款显卡可以正常轻松买到，玩家也无奈地将它们称为“空气卡”，看不见也摸不着。最近，一大波新卡陆续发布，NVIDIA这边从上到下有RTX
3090 Ti、RTX 3080 12GB、RTX 3070 Ti 16GB、RTX 3050，AMD则是入门级的RX 6500 XT、RX
6400。<br>
 <p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0113/e9adb855da2577d.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0113/e9adb855da2577d.png" title alt="2022-01-04_21-18-25-1480x833.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>按理说，低端显卡一般不会如矿主们的“法眼”，会不会更好买一些呢？</p><p>据业内人士称，<strong>NVIDIA会针对RTX 3050系列加大首批货源的供应量、铺货力度，甚至要超过RTX 3060、RTX 3060 Ti。</strong></p><p><strong><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0113/445b35a0aea1a8d.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0113/445b35a0aea1a8d.png" title alt="2022-01-04_21-17-57-1480x833.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></strong></p><p>不过，RTX 3050现在只是纸面宣布，27日才会解禁上市，也就是距离春节只差一周，因此<strong>预计渠道方面大量开卖要等到春节后了。</strong></p><p><strong><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0113/e5cfc3e6d140059.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0113/e5cfc3e6d140059.png" title alt="2022-01-04_21-18-17-1480x833.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></strong></p><p>RTX 3050显卡基于Ampere架构、GA106核心，2560个流处理器、20个光追单元、80个张量单元，核心频率1550-1780MHz(市售均为非公版)，搭配128-bit 8GB GDDR6显存，等效频率14GHz，整卡功耗130W，浮点性能相比RTX 2060提升接近40％，<strong>国内定价1899元起。</strong></p><p><strong><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0113/6223f03523fbf81.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0113/6223f03523fbf81.png" title alt="NVIDIA-GeForce-RTX-3050-Performance.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></strong></p>
