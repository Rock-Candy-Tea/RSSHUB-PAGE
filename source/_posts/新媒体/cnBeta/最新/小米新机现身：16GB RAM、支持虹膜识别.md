因为耗电、成本等问题，4K分辨率在手机显示屏上仍旧是稀罕物，迄今也只有索尼独家采用。在工信部通信设备管理网站，型号2109119BC的设备入网，识别信息显示其搭载了一块3840x2160分辨率的OLED屏，也就是4K级别，尺寸为6.55英寸。<br>
<p>从工信部给出的“证件照”来看，这款<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://shouji.jd.com/" target="_blank">手机</a>似乎并非<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&aid=450&euid=&t=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.mi.com%2F" target="_blank">小米</a>12，而和刚刚发布的小米civi简直一个模子刻出来，背部为三摄，正面是<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C794%2C798%26ev%3D4155_92263%26sort%3Dsort_rank_asc%26trans%3D1%26JL%3D3_%25E7%2594%25B5%25E8%25A7%2586%25E7%25B1%25BB%25E5%259E%258B_%25E6%259B%25B2%25E9%259D%25A2%23J_crumbsBar" target="_blank">曲面</a>屏。</p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1001/c7bc0aeffa61e87.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1001/c7bc0aeffa61e87.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/1001/c7bc0aeffa61e87.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>其它方面，<strong>内存容量最大16GB，机身存储最大512GB</strong>，整机三围158.34×71.5×6.98，重量166g，处理器主频2.4GHz，特色功能甚至包括虹膜识别。</p><p>从小米civi的定位来看，未来推出<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C794%2C798%26ev%3D4155_110018%26sort%3Dsort_rank_asc%26trans%3D1%26JL%3D2_1_0%23J_crumbsBar" target="_blank">4K</a>屏、16+512GB的配置几乎不可能。所以要么只是象征性入网，要么它是某款小米新旗舰？</p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1001/ea734fad60b9726.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1001/ea734fad60b9726.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/1001/ea734fad60b9726.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
