搜狗输入法出机械键盘了！搜狗输入法陪伴用户15年，终从虚拟到实体。这款电竞级键盘，号称专为个性潮趣年轻人打造。<strong>搜狗输入法机械键盘采用标准的114键布局方案</strong>，具备小键盘，在Esc和空格键上，还有搜狗输入法的Logo。<br>
 <p>该键盘采用客制化级调校卫星轴，顺滑之趣宛如天鹅绒上打字。支持全轴热插拔，磁吸上盖，方便DIY。</p><p><strong>它还采用电竞级键盘芯片，支持全键无冲</strong>，WIN键锁定，游戏时可快速触发连续技能。</p><p>外观设计上，搜狗输入法机械键盘采用专属橙白配，色彩亮眼，<strong>同时具备1680万色全彩灯效。</strong></p><p>键帽为双色注塑、五面热升华，材质细腻。整体符合人体工学，斜坡和两段式撑脚，多功能Type-C键线分离。</p><p>官方尚未公布其售价及开售日期，我们也将持续关注。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220110/a05ef292ad0142f9abd0fd8108ed1ed7.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0110/a56156f8f684f42.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220110/629e7007618d4bbc9b23a24665ef7d1e.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0110/8da770b6625d298.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220110/fda57c5f4ad84e92831c1aaab4451232.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0110/951d3ff01af79c0.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220110/646c260741e743bc9c6c4def260b5345.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0110/9705a3377ae82d3.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220110/4aa4f8bef0d64c8d9d057c297c304c39.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0110/d0ef0f25cb4e991.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
