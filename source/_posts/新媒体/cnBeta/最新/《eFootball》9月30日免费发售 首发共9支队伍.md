Konami官博宣布《实况足球》新作《eFootball》将在9月30日发售，免费登上主机及PC平台。首发时仅支持线下本地对战及线上活动模式。线下与AI或朋友对战有9个真实球会及6个球场供选择。玩家也可与上一代/下一代主机对战。<br>
 <p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0902/d49bc4ea1540a30.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0902/d49bc4ea1540a30.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0902/d49bc4ea1540a30.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>根据Konami，首发时支持9个队伍：</p><p>Arsenal</p><p>Barcelona</p><p>Bayern Munich</p><p>Corinthians Paulista</p><p>Flamengo</p><p>Juventus</p><p>Manchester United</p><p>River Plate</p><p>São Paulo</p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0902/a7f750df210a373.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0902/a7f750df210a373.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0902/a7f750df210a373.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0902/9a1012dc459bc5c.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0902/9a1012dc459bc5c.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0902/9a1012dc459bc5c.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>6个球场：</p><p>Camp Nou</p><p>Old Trafford</p><p>Allianz Stadium</p><p>Emirates Stadium</p><p>Allianz Arena</p><p>eFootball Stadium</p><p>Konami表示《eFootball》的首个重要更新将在秋季到来，包括多个新模式，其中就有创意队伍模式，在里面玩家可以打造他们自己的队伍，类似于EA《FIFA》游戏中的终极球队模式。</p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0902/e3b8e8d0546ebf4.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0902/e3b8e8d0546ebf4.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0902/e3b8e8d0546ebf4.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0902/b511f67f6d12c80.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0902/b511f67f6d12c80.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0902/b511f67f6d12c80.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
