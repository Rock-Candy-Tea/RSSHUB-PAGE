<strong>据灯塔数据显示，截至8月3日13时49分，动作电影《怒火·重案》上映5天，票房正式突破3亿。</strong>本片已连续数日登顶内地票房榜，8月3日实时票房占比达到44.4%，排片占比为35%。<br>
<p style="text-align:center"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/upimg/pic/2021/08/03/origin_202108031441304348.jpg"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0803/0b34321c5a9e102.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p style="text-align: left;">目前电影在豆瓣上的评分为7.7分，共有超过66000名用户评价，其中四星评价占比为52.8%。</p><p style="text-align:center"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/upimg/pic/2021/08/03/origin_202108031443201335.jpg"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0803/4f641598622a1ac.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0803/4f641598622a1ac.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0803/4f641598622a1ac.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p style="text-align:center"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/upimg/pic/2021/08/03/origin_202108031443214835.jpg"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0803/884aaee30b9b303.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0803/884aaee30b9b303.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0803/884aaee30b9b303.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p style="text-align: left;">《怒火·重案》由陈木胜执导，甄子丹、谢霆锋、秦岚领衔主演。电影讲述甄子丹饰演的重案组警察，在围剿国际毒枭时，遇上了一帮“黑吃黑”的蒙面悍匪，冷血屠杀警察。而悍匪的首领竟是甄子丹昔日战友谢霆锋。宿命令二人再次纠缠，一切恩怨张崇邦如何作出了断……</p>
