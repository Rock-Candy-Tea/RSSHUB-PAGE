<strong>讯景（XFX）官网刚刚放出了三款 AMD Radeon RX 6600 XT 系列显卡新品，分别为 MERC308、QICK308、以及 SWFT210 。</strong>规格方面，Navi 23 GPU 拥有 2048 个流处理器，搭配 8GB @ 128-bit 的 GDDR6 显存，辅以 32MB Infinity Cache 无限缓存。<br>
<p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0802/37f903b84c0998b.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0802/37f903b84c0998b.png" alt="0.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p style="text-align: center;">（来自：XFX <a href="https://www.xfxforce.com/" target="_self">官网</a>）</p><p>首先介绍旗舰款 XFX RX 6600 XT Speedster MERC308 Black 。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0802/1b4d4547a3830c8.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0802/1b4d4547a3830c8.jpg" alt="1-1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>其采用了定制的双槽三<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C738%2C751" target="_blank">风扇</a>散热器、并有四根镀镍铜热管穿过铝制散热鳍片。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0802/24ec410e5927f5b.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0802/24ec410e5927f5b.jpg" alt="1-2.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>显卡的散热器底下，采用了完全覆盖的设计方案（包括了 VRM 供电模块），且卡座设计在显存和 GPU 稍上方。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0802/8f69c9faf385e37.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0802/8f69c9faf385e37.jpg" alt="1-3.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>XFX RX 6600 XT Speedster MERC308 Black 显卡的侧边，还带有双 BIOS 切换开关和 LED 灯效。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0802/0a046786269e849.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0802/0a046786269e849.jpg" alt="1-4.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>遗憾的是，讯景尚未披露有关 XFX RX 6600 XT Speedster MERC308 Black 显卡规格的更多细节。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0802/756a236fbf4198c.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0802/756a236fbf4198c.jpg" alt="1-5.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>感兴趣的朋友，还请留意正式上市时的规格与售价公告。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0802/1d9558be28c5f63.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0802/1d9558be28c5f63.jpg" alt="2-1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>其次是 RX 6600 XT Speedster QICK308 Black 。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0802/a1d9d0888ed17c5.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0802/a1d9d0888ed17c5.jpg" alt="2-2.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>它与 MERC308 Black 版本没有太大差别。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0802/2c44a357a32add4.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0802/2c44a357a32add4.jpg" alt="2-3.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>主要缺少了双 BIOS 切换支持。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0802/565975f928c2d32.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0802/565975f928c2d32.jpg" alt="2-4.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>并且选用了纯黑的散热器外饰面。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0802/7c92908b05fba88.jpg" target="_blank"><img 相同。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0802/ab98c8f78b3927c.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0802/ab98c8f78b3927c.jpg" alt="3-1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>最后介绍的是 XFX RX 6600 XT Speedster SWFT210 Core 。其采用了双槽双风扇的设计，且只需单 8-pin 辅助供电。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0802/34a71fddcacfb99.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0802/34a71fddcacfb99.jpg" alt="3-2.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>此外与 QICK308 Black 一样，SWFT210 Core 也未配备双 BIOS 切换功能。至于更多细节，还请耐心等待 8 月 11 日的正式上市公告。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0802/f5f52f33dbd7d4a.jpg" target="_blank"><img data-link="1" href="https://microsoft.pvxt.net/Xx07X" target="_blank">配件</a>产品线。</p><p>XFX 在中国大陆地区拥有先进的生产设施，并在北美 / 欧洲 / 亚洲市场建立了销售、营销与技术支持中心。</p>
