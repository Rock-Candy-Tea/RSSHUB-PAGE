今天，Intel的12代酷睿i5处理器正式在各大电商平台上架，<strong>此次上架的除了i5-12400、i5-12400F两款外，还有i5-12490F这一国内特供型号。</strong>根据官方提供的参数，i5-12490F是针对中国大陆游戏市场的特供型号，<strong>拥有6核12线程、最高4.6GHz、20MB三级缓存，其中三级缓存达到i5K级别</strong>，相比i5-12400和i5-12400F有更快的频率和更大的三级缓存。<br>
 <p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220105/73e5f979c8d742ae83d1e1da77137daf.png" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0105/49b81fd7056a389.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0105/49b81fd7056a389.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0105/49b81fd7056a389.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220105/454cb15339324afea99794e8ac9da2ad.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0105/762781186352cb4.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>官方参数信息</p><p>i5-12400和i5-12400F均为6核12线程、最高4.4GHZ、18MB三级缓存，并支持DDR5-4800和DDR4-3200内存，两者区别在于i5-12400F未配备核显。</p><p>此次上架的三款12代酷睿i5处理器均采用Intel 7制程工艺打造，最大睿频功耗可达117W。</p><p>在外观上，<strong>国内特供的i5-12490F采用了黑色包装</strong>，而i5-12400和i5-12400F则依旧采用Intel标志性的蓝色包装，相当容易辨识。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220105/6418a9ed3da7454fb383d5bd2d10c570.png" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0105/ec14fb485c87048.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0105/ec14fb485c87048.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0105/ec14fb485c87048.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>在价格上，i5-12400售价1699元，i5-12400F售价1499元，i5-12490F售价1599元，<strong>需要注意的是，两款后缀“F”的版本均需要自行配备独立显卡。</strong></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220105/36bda9e128f7499282b6ce38165fedbb.png" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0105/835ba349f2e1a85.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0105/835ba349f2e1a85.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0105/835ba349f2e1a85.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
