2月28日晚上，荣耀带来了新一代荣耀Earbuds 3 Pro，售价199欧元（约合人民币1400元）。<strong>荣耀Earbuds 3 Pro最大的特色就是支持测体温功能，是全球首款支持测温的TWS耳机。</strong>据介绍，它能持续坚持体温，测温误差仅±0.3℃，当用户体温过高或过低时，可通过手机或手表发出提醒。<br>
<p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220228/d881bd2bca104952b7f3afa9c863a455.png" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0228/717fe4490d2b7a4.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>作为一款真无线降噪耳机，<strong>荣耀Earbuds 3 Pro支持场景化智慧降噪（降噪深度最高可达46dB）</strong>，可根据不同嘈杂环境，智能调节轻度/中度/深度三种降噪档位。</p><p>音质方面，荣耀Earbuds 3 Pro拥有11mm超大振幅动圈和陶瓷高音单元，低音扎实，高音明亮，它还是全球首款采用超大振幅动圈与陶瓷高音单元的同轴双单元TWS耳机</p><p>荣耀Earbuds 3 Pro搭配充电仓可连续听歌24小时。<strong>同时它也是全球首款支持5C快充的TWS耳机</strong>，只需要充电5分钟就能听歌2小时。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220228/0d1109531c0d486b8fc81299bb1ab39b.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0228/461f74c23309643.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220228/33ed9916ec404759abae42d9496d70d8.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0228/dfc44463047a203.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220228/9137202eaf764b038d72782fa8d0c327.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0228/88374b7165918a9.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220228/de39cb902f2440129945940d62ac331c.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0228/c1970e2f79c55f4.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
