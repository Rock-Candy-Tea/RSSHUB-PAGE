近日消息，一汽红旗旗下最豪华的轿车红旗H9+正式上市，<strong>新车共推出基础款一款车型，售价86万元</strong>，但可支持定制选装方案，包括至尚风范包、舒享内饰包、专属个性包。相较于普通版车型，红旗H9+的尺寸变化尤为明显，其<strong>长宽高为5337/1904/1493mm，轴距为3260mm，车身加长200mm，全面超越奔驰S级</strong>，妥妥达到了行政级别。<br>
<p><a class="impage" href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220510/b4fa6abb7d0c42c88ba7dd87e5d7e803.jpg" style="color: red; text-decoration-line: none; font-family: " open hiragino sans microsoft wenquanyi micro font-size: target="_self"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0521/98a03ce2a2ecbff.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0521/98a03ce2a2ecbff.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0521/98a03ce2a2ecbff.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>车身外观上与普通版相差不大，前脸均为大尺寸直瀑式进气格栅，加长部分位于车身B柱，同时镶嵌有红旗H9+的专属徽标，凸显尊贵感。</p><p><a class="impage" href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220508/4e332b90aeee43fcbfe7292ad219bec6.png" style="color: rgb(0, 153, 255); font-family: " open hiragino sans microsoft wenquanyi micro font-size: target="_self"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0521/21e079aa52024b7.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0521/21e079aa52024b7.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0521/21e079aa52024b7.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>车内整体造型差异不大，但在细节设计上注重表现尊贵豪华，如大量采用木纹饰板，车门内饰还配有手工打造的梅花刺绣，<strong>车顶为劳斯劳斯同款星空顶</strong>，实现了高贵和典雅有机结合。</p><p><a class="impage" href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220510/fd2085b340f5481db3ce45d7554bf471.jpg" style="color: red; text-decoration-line: none; font-family: " open hiragino sans microsoft wenquanyi micro font-size: target="_self"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0521/7165ec395256c63.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0521/7165ec395256c63.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0521/7165ec395256c63.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>另外，红旗H9+后排使用的是两个独立的航空座椅，配有隐藏式酒柜，支持同时存放两瓶香槟，并自带两个酒杯，并且中间扶手显示屏，可控制车内多媒体系统及座椅调节等。</p><p><a class="impage" href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220508/0eaca948caf441b1916f8967ab0c3f3f.jpg" style="color: red;
