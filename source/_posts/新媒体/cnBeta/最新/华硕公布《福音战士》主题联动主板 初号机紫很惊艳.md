华硕于4月29日宣布，将推出与《新世纪福音战士》联动新主板，首款产品为初号机主题紫色很惊艳，目前华硕暂未公开该款特别版主板的销售区域以及上市时间，敬请期待后续报道。<br>
<p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0502/9345446f12d11cd.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>•话说新主板型号为ROG MAXIMUS Z690 HERO，整体色调为初号机紫色，性能方面DDR5-6400×4、PCIe 5.0 x16×2、M.2×5、SATA 6Gbps×6、eSATA 3Gbps×2、Thunderbolt 4×2、USB 3.2×7、USB 2.0×2、2.5 Gigabit Ethernet主流配置一应俱全，价格在560美元左右。</p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0502/9a657540e50676f.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0502/9a657540e50676f.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0502/9a657540e50676f.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0502/75a2dc120948738.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0502/75a2dc120948738.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0502/75a2dc120948738.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0502/f7c8affc625f66b.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0502/f7c8affc625f66b.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0502/f7c8affc625f66b.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><div class="article-relation"><p><strong>相关文章:</strong></p><p><a href="https://www.cnbeta.com/articles/tech/1264581.htm" target="_blank">RTX 3090新版登场 ROG宣布推出EVA联名定制版</a></p></div>
