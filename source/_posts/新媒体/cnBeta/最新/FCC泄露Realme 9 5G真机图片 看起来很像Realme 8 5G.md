我们知道Realme 9 Pro和Pro+即将面世，有理由相信会有一款普通的机型加入。<strong>现在，一份FCC设备入网审批清单显示，我们也将得到一款Realme 9 5G机型。FCC文件指出，Realme 9 5G有着不同的营销名称和型号，但从设计上看，它与Realme 8 5G基本相同。</strong><br>
 <p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/01/d189ed9aa683751.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/01/d189ed9aa683751.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>从现场照片本身来看，<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://realme.jd.com/" target="_blank">Realme</a> 9 5G与Realme 8 5G没有太大区别。但Realme 9 5G会自带一个18W的充电器，而不是两个Pro型号的33W充电器。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/01/ee4ae8c57d43358.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/01/ee4ae8c57d43358.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/01/6e3bd955e638770.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/01/6e3bd955e638770.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/01/5b2c6236115ae35.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/01/5b2c6236115ae35.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0124/d4be5e2758462e5.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0124/d4be5e2758462e5.jpg" title alt="gsmarena_001.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
