1月5日消息，爆料人士@evleaks在本月曝光了三星Galaxy S22 Ultra最终外形，这一爆料被三星投诉。<strong>三星这次投诉，关于Galaxy S22 Ultra的外形图在推特平台上已删除。</strong>事实上，备受泄密困扰的不只是三星一家品牌，苹果、谷歌等厂商新品在尚未发布之前也屡次被外泄。<br>
 <p>回到Galaxy S22 Ultra上，<strong>这是Galaxy S22系列的超大杯旗舰，主摄为一亿像素，</strong>型号为<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://samsung.jd.com/" target="_blank">三星</a>HM3的改良款，拥有1/1.33英寸超大底，支持OIS光学防抖，这是2022年唯一一款坚持一亿像素的高端旗舰。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220105/97330d5285fa43ee8ffddf00883de93c.png" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0105/fd9129ada4fab9f.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0105/fd9129ada4fab9f.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0105/fd9129ada4fab9f.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>Galaxy S22 Ultra</p><p>规格方面，<strong>Galaxy S22 Ultra首批搭载高通骁龙8旗舰处理器，部分版本搭载Exynos 2200旗舰处理器，</strong>它采用微曲AM<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C794%2C798%26ev%3D4155_88234%26sort%3Dsort_rank_asc%26trans%3D1%26JL%3D2_1_0%23J_crumbsBar" target="_blank">OLED</a>柔性屏，分辨率预计为2K+，支持无线充电。</p><p>该机将于2月份上市发售，国行版定价可能在万元左右。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220105/7aaa52bba8054e8c9c6d15e2ae77c1a0.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0105/e576c87195b0cfd.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0105/e576c87195b0cfd.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0105/e576c87195b0cfd.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
