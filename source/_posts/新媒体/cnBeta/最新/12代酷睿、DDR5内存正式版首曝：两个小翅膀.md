<strong>Intel Alder Lake 12代酷睿马上就要来了，之前已经曝光得七七八八，今天第一次见到了正式零售版。</strong>根据知乎网友@DDAA117曝出的谍照，我们看到了零售版的i9-12900K、i5-12600K，正面都印上了完整型号、S-Spec编号（SRL4H/SRL4T）。<br>
 <p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1021/7b905034dbf72da.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>一款新处理器从诞生到正式版，需要经过多个样品阶段，一般至少两版ES工程样品、一版QS验证样品，之前已经陆续见到过一些，如今终于修成正果。</p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1021/d61c0cb07f4f0f5.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1021/f172cec86338e88.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p style="text-align: center;">工程样品</p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1021/ceecb880ef5f4fb.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>同时，这一代还会有新的包装盒设计。</p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1021/f35f3c059b73a09.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1021/2933a731c06a41b.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1021/e7a174596bc2f73.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>12代酷睿更换新的LGA1700接口，<strong>长宽尺寸为37.5×45.0毫米</strong>，整体呈长方形，左右两个突出，有点像两个小翅膀，也有点像……那啥。</p><p>现在的10/11代酷睿接口都是LGA1200，尺寸37.5×37.5毫米，也就是个正方形。</p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1021/3aaded13d09d3e5.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p style="text-align: center;">方方正正的10代酷睿</p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1021/2dd2ab4b30e9c44.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>同时曝光的还有来自<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://adata.jd.com/" target="_blank">威刚</a>的DDR5内存，无散热片普条，<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://mall.jd.com/index-1000098521.html" target="_blank">美光</a>颗粒，单条容量8GB，标准频率4800MHz，标准电压1.1V。</p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1021/28575b2c90b0b74.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1021/201cc5e4af36bbe.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
