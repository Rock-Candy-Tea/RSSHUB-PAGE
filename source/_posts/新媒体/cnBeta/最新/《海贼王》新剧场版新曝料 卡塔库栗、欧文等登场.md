《海贼王》新剧场版《ONE PIECE FILM RED》，公开新情报，大妈海贼团的卡塔库栗、欧文、布蕾确定将在《ONE PIECE FILM
RED》中登场，并且公开了三人的设定图，不会知道他们会和路飞等人有怎样的交集。剧场版动画《ONE PIECE FILM
RED》将于2022年8月6日在日本上映。<br>
 <p>剧场版《ONE PIECE FILM RED》新情报：</p><p style="text-align: center;"><iframe src="//player.youku.com/embed/XNTg4MDIzMjY2OA==?client_id=f7d81b29f4146ce2" width="640" height="480" frameborder="0"></iframe></p><p>剧场版动画《ONE PIECE FILM RED》，由谷口悟朗担任本作的监督，黑岩勉担任脚本，尾田荣一郎担任制作人。本次剧场版将讲述红发香克斯的“女儿”UTA（乌塔）的故事，以及她和草帽团相遇之后的冒险。</p><p><a href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/06/20220622_lr_176_1/gamersky_01origin_01_202262214485D4.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0622/41b9256c4622359.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0622/41b9256c4622359.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0622/41b9256c4622359.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/06/20220622_lr_176_1/gamersky_02origin_03_20226221448C8D.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0622/a9248589218095c.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0622/a9248589218095c.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0622/a9248589218095c.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/06/20220622_lr_176_1/gamersky_03origin_05_20226221448474.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0622/080565d35c9dc2a.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0622/080565d35c9dc2a.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0622/080565d35c9dc2a.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
