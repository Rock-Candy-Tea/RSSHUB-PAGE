据守望好莱坞报道，<strong>盖尔·加朵主演间谍惊悚片《铁石心肠》杀青，历时5个月，在5个国家拍摄。</strong>《铁石心肠》由Netflix和Skydance出品，盖尔加朵与《五十度灰》男主詹米·多南主演。<br>
 <p style="text-align:center"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/07/20220728_zy_red_164_8/145.jpg"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0729/8728ba5475b8c48.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p style="text-align: left;">该片由汤姆·哈伯(《浴血黑帮》《战争与和平》)执导，格雷格·鲁卡(《永生守卫》)和艾莉森·施罗德(《隐藏人物》《克里斯托弗·罗宾》)撰写剧本，目前剧情暂时保密中。</p><p style="text-align: left;">计划以该片为起点打造一个以女性为主角的动作片系列，类似《碟中谍》和《007》。</p><p style="text-align:center"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/07/20220728_zy_red_164_8/1220.jpg"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0729/57531a7866cd92d.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p style="text-align:center"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/07/20220728_zy_red_164_8/1222.jpg"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0729/92f439ad623b489.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p style="text-align: center;"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/image2022/07/20220728_zy_red_164_8/1225.jpg"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0729/55bdfc8d05f128b.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
