慧荣科技今天宣布，推出全新的SSD主控方案“<strong>SM2320</strong>”，面向外置便携式SSD，采用单芯片设计，性能高、功耗低、性价比高。<strong>慧荣SM2320主控将原来分离的桥接芯片、NAND控制器集成在一颗芯片中</strong>，PCB面积控制在26×60毫米，同时成本、发热也更低。<br>
 <p>它支持<strong>四个NAND闪存通道</strong>，支持<strong>USB 3.0 Gen2x2接口</strong>，理论带宽20Gbps，<strong>实测连续读写速度最高分别可超2.1GB/s、2.0GB/s，混合读写速度也能超过1.8GB/s，最大容量可达4TB，TLC、QLC闪存均可。</strong></p><p>技术方面，它支持慧荣科技独家的NANDXtend ECC、端到端数据路径保护、AES-256硬件加密、真随机数生成、TCG Opal 2.0安全规范、指纹安全。</p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0901/f32920a159dca68.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0901/f32920a159dca68.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0901/f32920a159dca68.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0901/61cd893c895387d.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0901/61cd893c895387d.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/0901/61cd893c895387d.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><img alt="慧荣发布史上最快外置SSD单芯片主控：读写都超2GB/s" h="272" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210901/9c7c4cc8d0474018925f1f3704839c0b.png" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><strong>金士顿外置<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://list.jd.com/list.html?cat=670,677,11303" target="_blank">SSD</a> XS2000已经首发慧荣SM2320，容量500GB、1TB、2TB，接口USB-C，标称速度最高2GB/s。</strong></p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/0901/16fb0d3b97381dd.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
