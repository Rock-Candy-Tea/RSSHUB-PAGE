今天，知名爆料人士OnLeaks曝光了摩托罗拉新旗舰的更多细节。OnLeaks爆料，摩托罗拉新旗舰代号为Frontier，全球首发高通骁龙8  Plus旗舰处理器，<strong>命名为摩托罗拉Edge 30 Ultra，国行版会命名为摩托罗拉edge X30 Pro。</strong><br>
 <p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0328/a75586b24c35cca.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0328/a75586b24c35cca.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>爆料图显示，摩托罗拉Edge 30 Ultra采用了3D<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C794%2C798%26ev%3D4155_92263%26sort%3Dsort_rank_asc%26trans%3D1%26JL%3D3_%25E7%2594%25B5%25E8%25A7%2586%25E7%25B1%25BB%25E5%259E%258B_%25E6%259B%25B2%25E9%259D%25A2%23J_crumbsBar" target="_blank">曲面</a>柔性屏，形态为中置挖孔，背部采用三摄方案，主摄位于顶部，摄像头依然凸起，SIM卡插槽位于机身底部，视觉上较为纤薄。</p><p>据悉，该机采用6.67英寸FHD+全面屏，刷新率为144Hz，<strong>前置6000万像素，后置主摄可能是2亿像素，同时配备5000万和1200万副摄，电池为4500mAh，支持125W超级快充。</strong></p><p>作为年度旗舰，摩托罗拉Edge 30 Pro Ultra最大的看点就是<strong>搭载了骁龙8 Plus旗舰处理器</strong>，这是一颗比高通骁龙8更为强悍的<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://shouji.jd.com/" target="_blank">手机</a>芯片，也将是安卓阵营最强悍的性能旗舰。</p><p>这款芯片采用的是“1+3+4”三丛集架构，由超大核Cortex X2、大核Cortex A710和小核Cortex A510组成，CPU主频为2.99GHz，GPU有小幅升级，基于台积电4nm工艺制程打造。</p><p>该机预计在今年Q2登场。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220330/642cdfb70a5f45fab924c0ef2a76a75d.png" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0330/35384fbc078d1e1.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0330/35384fbc078d1e1.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0330/35384fbc078d1e1.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p style="text-align: center;">摩托罗拉Edge 30 Ultra</p>
