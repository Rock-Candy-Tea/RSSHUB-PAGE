<a href="https://weibo.com/1623886424/LncnZ4Plh" target="_blank" class="n1">新浪电影</a>消息，邱礼涛(《扫毒2》《拆弹专家》系列)导演的新动作片《海关战线》今日正式开机，主演张学友、谢霆锋、林嘉欣、刘雅瑟、关智斌、陈家乐、卫诗雅、杨天宇等以片中造型亮相开机仪式。<br>
 <p>该片是张学友、谢霆锋时隔12年再度合作，聚焦海关题材，揭秘令人震惊的跨国走私大案。据片方信息，谢霆锋在片中饰演的中国香港海关调查员周正礼特立独行，幸得张学友饰演的上司张允南包容指导，立下不少功劳，两人在侦办一宗军火走私案的过程中，被迫卷入海外势力的暴力袭击。</p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/upimg/pic/2022/04/06/origin_202204061625457074.jpg"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0406/91d2a51d74da2ea.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/upimg/pic/2022/04/06/origin_202204061625465732.jpg"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0406/bcedf1120d0f4b2.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/upimg/pic/2022/04/06/origin_202204061625464372.jpg"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0406/00d9e3c05602829.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/upimg/pic/2022/04/06/origin_202204061625467248.jpg"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0406/afc4e70de1a20bf.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
