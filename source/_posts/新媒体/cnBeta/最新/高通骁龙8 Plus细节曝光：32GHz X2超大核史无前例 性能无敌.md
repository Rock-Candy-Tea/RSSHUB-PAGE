今天上午，博主@数码闲聊站曝光了高通骁龙8 Plus旗舰处理器的关键参数，<strong>这颗芯片由超大核Cortex X2、大核Cortex A710和小核Cortex A510组成，GPU为Adreno 730。</strong>其中<strong>超大核主频达到了3.2GHz，</strong>超过了自家的高通骁龙8和对手联发科天玑9000。PS：高通骁龙8 Cortex X2超大核主频为3.0GHz，联发科天玑9000 Cortex X2超大核主频为3.2GHz。<br>
<p>毫无疑问，这将是安卓阵营最强悍的<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://shouji.jd.com/" target="_blank">手机</a>芯片，<strong>安兔兔跑分将会再创新高，</strong>这也是安卓阵营下半年旗舰手机的标配。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220519/ca9f786d39cd4fbfbda0a31a62493bfa.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0519/17f7a09a8e109a1.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0519/17f7a09a8e109a1.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0519/17f7a09a8e109a1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>值得注意的是，骁龙8 Plus采用了台积电4nm工艺，博主@数码闲聊站爆料，<strong>由台积电代工的骁龙8 Plus功耗降低30%左右，能效提升30%左右。</strong></p><p>这颗芯片将于明天正式发布，摩托罗拉可能会全球首发骁龙8 Plus。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220519/6821697b870b4b58a80b4d560d95e22b.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0519/982e7dcc9fc41a9.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0519/982e7dcc9fc41a9.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0519/982e7dcc9fc41a9.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
