苹果刚刚发布了M2处理器，首发搭载于新款MacBook Air和13寸MBP上。这次M2依然是5nm工艺，CPU依然最高8核，说实话有点“挤牙膏”的意味。不过，<strong>苹果在营销方面倒是很鸡贼，摆出这种数据吊打x86竞品，比如号称剪视频比Intel快了15倍,图片放大快了26倍。</strong><br>
 <p>NBcheck在仔细查看尾注后发现，<a data-link="1" href="https://apple.pvxt.net/c/1251234/435400/7639?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.apple.com%2Fcn%2Fmusic%2F" target="_blank">苹果</a>居然拿M2处理器和4年前的8代酷睿对比。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220608/20db464fd04946eea6039e8bdde20fc8.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0608/3effbca2b4da7d0.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0608/3effbca2b4da7d0.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0608/3effbca2b4da7d0.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>具体来说，苹果选取的是2019款<a data-link="1" href="https://apple.pvxt.net/c/1251234/435400/7639?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.apple.com%2Fcn%2Fmac%2F" target="_blank">MacBook</a> Air，处理器为酷睿i5-8210Y，基频只有可怜的1.6GHz，这不仅是一颗14nm双核产品，而且核显也是弱鸡的UHD 617，测试场景是使用Final Cut剪一段2分钟的<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C794%2C798%26ev%3D4155_110018%26sort%3Dsort_rank_asc%26trans%3D1%26JL%3D2_1_0%23J_crumbsBar" target="_blank">4K</a> 422视频。</p><p>这里的问题在于，明明最近的一代MBA搭载的是10代酷睿i5-1030G7，可苹果偏偏要挑更老的来比，难道知道后者4核更猛？</p><p>老外不无讽刺地指出，苹果若是再Low点，何不找2008款MBA，也是双核处理器（Intel Core 2 Duo SP7500)）呢？</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220608/310bb93959614b98babaf4df77be2f0f.png" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0608/8ecdcbe5345fd47.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0608/8ecdcbe5345fd47.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0608/8ecdcbe5345fd47.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
