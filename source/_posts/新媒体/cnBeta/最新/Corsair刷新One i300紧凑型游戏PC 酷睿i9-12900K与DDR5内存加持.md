<strong>CES 2022 期间，美商海盗船推出了新款 Corsair One i300 紧凑型游戏 PC 。</strong>配置方面，该机升级了最新的英特尔 12 代酷睿 i9-12900K 处理器、Corsair Vengeance DDR5 内存、水冷版英伟达 GeForce RTX 3080 Ti 独显，并且支持 2.5 GbE 有线以太网 + Wi-Fi 6 无线网络。<br>

referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p style="text-align: center;">（来自：Corsair <a PC，它的体型却相当小巧。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0106/c800110c534f2cf.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0106/c800110c534f2cf.png" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0106/1bf72f5c01ffe83.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0106/1bf72f5c01ffe83.png" alt="0-3.png"
