8月26日消息，在成都车展期间，全新一汽奥迪A8L、S8L正式上市。其中A8L家族共提供5种车型<strong>，售价82.98~195.48万元，S8L售价207.68万元。</strong>一汽奥迪给A8L的定位是豪华D级智慧旗舰座驾，严格来说它是2018年底盘代号D5车型的中期改款，外观进行了巨大改变。<br>
 <p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220826/23ce13497fbf491bbcc587ee388305b8.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0826/3008acf59d299a9.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0826/3008acf59d299a9.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0826/3008acf59d299a9.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>其中前脸采用点阵式银翼格栅，中网面积更大，家族折角式数字矩阵LED大灯。车尾是一体贯穿式<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C794%2C798%26ev%3D4155_88234%26sort%3Dsort_rank_asc%26trans%3D1%26JL%3D2_1_0%23J_crumbsBar" target="_blank">OLED</a>尾灯，分段式光带，且支持定制化服务。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220826/b415389ab9d14a0fa0566096ca610075.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0826/b89b4cd5232691a.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0826/b89b4cd5232691a.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0826/b89b4cd5232691a.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>内饰方面，全新A8L搭载飞机节流阀造型挡把，内有三块大屏。后排空间支持触摸控制，座椅拥有8种模式<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C1276%2C795" target="_blank">按摩</a>功能，可调节多种角度，脚踏板还可开启足底加热和按摩。搭配B&O音箱，全景超大天窗等。</p><p>动力方面，全系标配quattro全时四驱，智能稳姿系统。入门款45TFSI为265马力的2.0T发动机，搭配ZF的8AT变速箱，向上则是3.0T V6发动机（低功+高功）+48V轻混系统，顶配为4.0T。</p><p>至于S8L则是搭载4.0T V8发动机+48V轻混系统，同样采用ZF的8速手自一体变速器和quattro四驱系统。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220826/ea270300ccc14779801ddae2ea0a1dc2.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0826/a8a0f05e5270d39.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0826/a8a0f05e5270d39.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0826/a8a0f05e5270d39.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
