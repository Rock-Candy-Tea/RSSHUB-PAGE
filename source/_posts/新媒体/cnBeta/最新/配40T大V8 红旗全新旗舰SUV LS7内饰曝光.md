近日，一汽红旗发布了旗下全新旗舰SUV车型LS7的官图，4月14日下午，“悦读一汽”官方就该车的内饰做出了解读。据了解，作为顶尖旗舰SUV，LS7的内饰简单而言就是“奢华”。<strong>其配备了大量的红木装饰</strong>，包括仪表盘两侧、换挡杆区域、扶手箱后方、座椅靠背、第二排扶手等，红木实木经过了特殊工艺的处理，看起来格外细腻，想必触感也是顶级。<br>
<p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220414/c5d6be2efb874376b639a8c235b15641.png" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0414/4ee48e2e4932031.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0414/4ee48e2e4932031.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0414/4ee48e2e4932031.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220414/ddfe1721aac54845aae3002a14e5003d.png" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0414/c3753168ed79f84.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0414/c3753168ed79f84.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0414/c3753168ed79f84.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>此外，该车还使用了金色装饰，<strong>出风口中央位置还配有镀铬石英表，</strong>非常具有奢华车的典范<strong>。</strong></p><p>值得一提的是，该车方向盘下方镀铬装饰实际上是喇叭的控制开关，设计相当巧妙。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220413/12c9189626924478a4b043485766b62b.png" style="color: red; text-decoration-line: none; font-family: " open hiragino sans microsoft wenquanyi micro font-size: target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0414/c2c3a1744354908.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0414/c2c3a1744354908.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0414/c2c3a1744354908.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>外观来看，该车采用了红旗家族经典的直瀑式进气格栅，并配用分体式大灯，其车标虽然为红旗最新的“红旗标”，<strong>但是车辆轮毂和方向盘中间，均为红旗最尊贵的L系列才使用的“葵花LOGO”</strong>。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220413/b202a6da15844e1fb2d48281c00df7a8.png" style="color: red; text-decoration-line: none; font-family: " open hiragino sans microsoft wenquanyi micro font-size: target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0414/1d9b694543eee13.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0414/1d9b694543eee13.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0414/1d9b694543eee13.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220413/5ce860a3296f4f39a2958d7ef857d81b.png" style="color: red; text-decoration-line: none; font-family: " open hiragino sans microsoft wenquanyi micro font-size: target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0414/e986d5b1e81a932.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0414/e986d5b1e81a932.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0414/e986d5b1e81a932.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>动力方面，新车<strong>搭载了一台4.0T V8涡轮增压发动机，最大功率为265千瓦（360马力）</strong>，其极速达到200km/h。</p><p>新车售价未知，起售价预计会突破150万元，如此才能配得上其东方“劳斯莱斯”的称号。</p>
