<strong>三星刚刚发布了其最新的入门级智能手机，名为Galaxy A03
Core。A03拥有一面6.5英寸HD+分辨率的LCD，在水滴状刘海上安装了一个500万像素的自拍摄像头。</strong>这款手机由八核处理器驱动，其中四个单元以1.6GHz运行，另外四个单元以1.2GHz运行。<br>
 <p>这种核心配置、时钟速度与Unisoc SC9863A芯片组完全相匹配，因此可以肯定就是这款来自紫光展锐的芯片，它被广泛用在全球多个品牌在各国市场销售的低端机型上，在A03 Core型号上，它与2GB的内存和32GB的存储空间搭配。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/11/b03329bbf6db452.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/11/b03329bbf6db452.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></a></p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/11/35d3083d0d28678.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/11/35d3083d0d28678.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></a></p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/11/0322c3867353ade.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/11/0322c3867353ade.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></a></p><p>三星Galaxy A03 Core背面的摄像头是一个800万像素的传感器，机身内置5000mAh的电池，由于参数列表没有提到任何快速充电支持，我们暂且认为它的充电速度被限制在10W。</p><p>三星Galaxy A03 Core可以插入两张nano-SIM卡，一张microSD卡，并将提供蓝色和黑色可选，它的定价和供应细节尚未确认。</p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/11/197bd195e0cd3bc.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/11/197bd195e0cd3bc.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></a></p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/11/1df586a60bf5ed0.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/11/1df586a60bf5ed0.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></a></p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/11/b8067884beb548f.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/11/b8067884beb548f.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></a></p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/11/841b77b942cd1af.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/11/841b77b942cd1af.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></a></p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/11/30b1c91e43ca764.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/11/30b1c91e43ca764.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></a></p>
