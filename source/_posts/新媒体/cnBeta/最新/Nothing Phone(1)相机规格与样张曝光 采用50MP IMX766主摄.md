<strong>Nothing 即将于明日（7 月 12 号）正式推出首款 Android 智能机，但该品牌已抢先放出了它的相机规格。</strong>据悉， Nothing Phone（1）采用了后置双摄方案，且认为两枚好相机要远胜于通过数量堆砌参数卖点。<br>
 <p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0711/e4b3d362c7ef912.jpg" alt="1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>可知该机主摄为 50MP @ 1/1.56 英寸的索尼 IMX766 传感器，辅以 f/1.8 大光圈。</p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0711/e089ddd07051506.webp" alt="2.webp" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>副摄则是具有 114° 视野的超广角镜头，且设备支持夜间模式和场景检测等特色功能。</p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0711/f6d8ed178075d32.webp" alt="3.webp" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>【其它配置】Nothing Phone（1）采用了支持 120Hz 高刷的 6.55 英寸 <a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C794%2C798%26ev%3D4155_88234%26sort%3Dsort_rank_asc%26trans%3D1%26JL%3D2_1_0%23J_crumbsBar" target="_blank">OLED</a> 屏。</p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0711/cccc84752bd6a8d.webp" alt="4.webp" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>处理器为高通骁龙 778G+ SoC，辅以 12GB RAM + 256GB ROM 存储组合。</p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0711/8334bdde687cfe8.webp" alt="5.webp" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>软件方面，该机预装了基于 Android 12 定制的 Nothing OS 。</p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0711/693f2febd2d85db.webp" alt="6.webp" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>除了 50MP 后置主摄，还有一枚 16MP 的前置自拍摄像头。</p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0711/d719e16f3cfb486.webp" alt="7.webp" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>最后，预计 Nothing Phone（1）内置了支持 33W 快充的 4500 mAh 电池。</p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0711/f21ecd3e54b99bd.webp" alt="8.webp" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>至于更多细节，还请耐心等待明日的官宣。</p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0711/37208eab0059da4.webp" alt="9.webp" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
