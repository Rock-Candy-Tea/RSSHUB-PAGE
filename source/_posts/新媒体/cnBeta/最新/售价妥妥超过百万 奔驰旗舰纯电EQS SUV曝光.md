近日，网络上曝光了奔驰旗舰纯电车型EQS SUV无伪装实车图片，作为奔驰EQ家族的新成员，定位于大型豪华纯电SUV的奔驰EQS SUV<strong>将于4月19日全球首发</strong>。不过实车照片曝光后，引起了不少网友的吐槽，<strong>更有甚者称其设计连二十多万的国产车都不如</strong>！<br>
 <p>外观来看，与EQS纯电轿车相同，EQS SUV同样诞生自EVA电驱平台，延续了奔驰EQ家族的设计理念，前脸采用了所谓的暗夜星阵格栅封闭式前脸，并与大灯组融为一体。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220411/301bc5db0b134fc5b6bd5f4c786cc870.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0411/eb1cda31e12a576.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>车身侧面没什太多亮点，或许是由于拍摄角度的问题，<strong>感觉没比自家的GLC大气</strong>。</p><p>尾部依旧采用了圆润的设计风格，用上了时下流行的家族化贯穿式尾灯，两侧的光源倒是足够精致。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220411/9adc869f8bb9423d873b22c7992f4d11.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0411/9dc5c3b8ed9761c.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>内饰官图日前已经发布，奔驰EQS SUV与EQS的内饰设计风格保持一致，皆是奔驰EQ系列电动车最新的设计语言。</p><p>新车<strong>同样采用了MBUX Hyperscreen系统的<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C794%2C798%26ev%3D4155_88234%26sort%3Dsort_rank_asc%26trans%3D1%26JL%3D2_1_0%23J_crumbsBar" target="_blank">OLED</a>大屏，贯穿整个中控台，总尺寸宽度超过141厘米</strong>，由非规则的<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C794%2C798%26ev%3D4155_92263%26sort%3Dsort_rank_asc%26trans%3D1%26JL%3D3_%25E7%2594%25B5%25E8%25A7%2586%25E7%25B1%25BB%25E5%259E%258B_%25E6%259B%25B2%25E9%259D%25A2%23J_crumbsBar" target="_blank">曲面</a>玻璃打造而成，内部集成了主驾驶仪表、中控大屏、副驾驶娱乐屏三块屏幕，可以带来更强的沉浸体验感。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220316/580560351c114e15a01943e5035587e7.png" style="color: rgb(0, 153, 255);" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0411/c3057ab104642ff.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220316/0b7ad3e3f8c34aedb3196f9d168cd114.jpg" style="color: red; text-decoration-line: none;" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0411/239d5980cf72e29.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>不出意外的话，EQS SUV的这套系统同样也会配备8核CPU和24GB内存，内含英伟达Xavier AI芯片，它可以学习驾驶者的用车习惯，主动进行座椅加热、<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C794%2C870%26ev%3D1554_584894%26JL%3D3_%25E7%25A9%25BA%25E8%25B0%2583%25E7%25B1%25BB%25E5%2588%25AB_%25E7%25AB%258B%25E6%259F%259C%25E5%25BC%258F%25E7%25A9%25BA%25E8%25B0%2583%23J_crumbsBar" target="_blank">空调</a>等功能的提示推送，让汽车变得更智能，更简单。</p><p>此外，该车内部的豪华程度保持了奔驰一贯的高水准，大面积使用真皮材质，还配有多色氛围灯、后排娱乐系统等等，该车提供5座和7座两种车型，以供不同消费者的使用需求。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220316/3743ef4f21de457a92e421996e7dcf09.jpg" style="color: rgb(0, 153, 255);" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0411/8ee413c5069653c.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220316/ad4092ee81a743ac93130299b79f9f92.jpg" style="color: red; text-decoration-line: none;" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0411/0ae61504f319969.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220316/80419b73c15143cc855cb7bbaca1cd40.jpg" style="color: rgb(0, 153, 255);" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0411/2d830a159b01660.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220316/65847dc3eff046248c1b5b61cc3cfde0.jpg" style="color: rgb(0, 153, 255);" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0411/aa26bb2893641b0.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>动力部分，预计其也将提供了单电机与双电机两个版本，<strong>其中单电机版本最大可输出329马力，配111.8kWh三元锂电池，WLTP标准续航里程最远为800里</strong>。</p><p><strong>双电机版最大可输出516马力，峰值扭矩828牛米</strong>，配备111.8kWh三元锂电池，0-96km/h加速仅有4.1秒，预计WLTP标准续航里程为750公里。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220316/85de8f811293453895e895c77505ee09.jpg" style="color: red; text-decoration-line: none; font-family: " open hiragino sans microsoft wenquanyi micro font-size: target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0411/d2e33c80e274432.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>至于售价，同级车型，SUV一般要比轿车卖的更贵，作为参考，<strong>EQS轿车在国内的售价为107.96-151.86万</strong>，那么EQS SUV的售价势必也会轻松破百万元。</p>
