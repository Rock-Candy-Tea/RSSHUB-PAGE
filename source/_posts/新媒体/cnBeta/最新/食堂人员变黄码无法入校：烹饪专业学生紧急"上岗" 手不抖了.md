11月6日，在四川成都，某校的食堂工作人员因为疫情不能入校，烹饪专业的同学紧急提前上岗，没想到广受好评（<a class="mydrivers_player" href="https://weibo.com/1659643027/L0oBIjCA4">视频</a>）。据悉，由于疫情，食堂的叔叔阿姨健康码变为黄码，无法进入学校，于是紧急集合烹饪专业同学，承担食堂做饭等工作。<br>
 <p>别看是初次“上岗”，这些学生的表现相当惊艳，做的菜从视频上看就觉得色香味俱全，业务能力也得到了同学们的交口称赞，尤其是……</p><p>－ 我说今天的饭怎么那么好吃，原来是烹饪班的同学去帮忙了。手不抖了，分量也很充足！</p><p>－ 这分量，果然自己人才最懂自己人。</p><p>－ 味道超级好。天天这样，一个月能长胖20斤。</p><p>－ 伙食情况大为改观，终于吃饱了。</p><p>－ 这实践课程学校不给满分说不过去啊。</p><p></p><p><a href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/11/62db31d8bac7547.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/11/62db31d8bac7547.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><br></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1107/490534a43068d7c.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1107/490534a43068d7c.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/1107/490534a43068d7c.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1107/53070f943b93149.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1107/53070f943b93149.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/1107/53070f943b93149.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1107/5ab169498c2001c.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1107/5ab169498c2001c.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/1107/5ab169498c2001c.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1107/8e175c8ab0d26d9.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1107/8e175c8ab0d26d9.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/1107/8e175c8ab0d26d9.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1107/153ee18e66620f4.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2021/1107/153ee18e66620f4.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2021/1107/153ee18e66620f4.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
