<strong>4月26日，紫米推出了ZMI GaN3充电器30W，现已正式开售，价格69元。</strong>小个子大能量，第三代氮化镓黑科技加持，果粉福音！苹果全系列，一个全搞定，PD认证协议芯片，可为苹果全系列设备提供30W MAX的充电功率。<br>
 <p><strong>可为<a data-link="1" href="https://apple.pvxt.net/c/1251234/435400/7639?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.apple.com%2Fcn%2Fiphone%2F" target="_blank">iPhone</a> 13 Pro MAX提供27W MAX快充，早上洗漱吃早餐的时间，30分钟即可充至48%电量。</strong></p><p>采用第三代GaN氮化镓材料，相比第二代体积更小。更小的体积，节省更多空间，多个充电器插在插排上也不会拥挤。而且可折叠插脚易收纳。</p><p>同时，高功率充电时也能保持低发热，时刻保障充电安全；<strong>低电量充电时自动开启快充，满电时自动切换至涓流充电</strong>，为使用者提供安心的充电保障。</p><p>100-240V宽电压，覆盖全球绝大部分地区市。多项充电硬核认证加持，果粉带着它行万里都不怕。适配<a data-link="1" href="https://apple.pvxt.net/c/1251234/435400/7639?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.apple.com%2Fcn%2Fmusic%2F" target="_blank">苹果</a>全家桶，音箱、<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://shouji.jd.com/" target="_blank">手机</a>、电脑、平板、耳机、头戴耳机都能充。</p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220426/6cbbd3486de64fa2a29adb0ae1beb372.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0426/963ddd3df4fb736.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220426/70166166bbc3410a8b0d52308c70a2b3.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0426/eb04f5893f90aab.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220426/6a43964410c14f558ab92259d2a90af7.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0426/6639d47b03e8008.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220426/4be7e1d12cb042f48ea9a6c2ac4bc283.png" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0426/5c69da0fa8a9af4.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0426/5c69da0fa8a9af4.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0426/5c69da0fa8a9af4.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
