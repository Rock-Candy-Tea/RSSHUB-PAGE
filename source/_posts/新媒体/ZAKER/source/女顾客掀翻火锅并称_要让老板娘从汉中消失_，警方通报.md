<p>7 月 19 日，陕西汉中。汉中市公安局发布一则通报称 7 月 18 日西一环路一火锅店发生纠纷。经查系某私企员工牛某（女）因餐资与火锅店老板赵某（女）发生纠纷。目前公安机关已受案，调查正在进行，待查明事实后，将严格依法处理。</p><p>此前该火锅店老板娘赵某反映，牛某与朋友吃饭后，不愿意买单，将火锅油泼出洒在墙上。</p><p></p><div class="zaker_div"><div id="zkvideo16582847219957" class="zk_upvideo" data-zkinfo="%7B%22status%22%3A%22finished%22%2C%22imgUrl%22%3A%22http%3A%2F%2Fzkres.myzaker.com%2Fimg_upload%2Feditor%2F2022%2F07%2F20%2F1658284724746_W1280H720S.jpeg%22%2C%22videoUrl%22%3A%22http%3A%2F%2Fzkres.myzaker.com%2Fimg_upload%2Feditor%2Farticle_video%2F2022%2F07%2F20%2F16582847246213.mp4%22%2C%22name%22%3A%2216582847246213.mp4%22%2C%22width%22%3A1280%2C%22height%22%3A720%7D"></div><div class="edi_oper"></div></div><p></p><div id="recommend_bottom"></div><div id="article_bottom"></div>
