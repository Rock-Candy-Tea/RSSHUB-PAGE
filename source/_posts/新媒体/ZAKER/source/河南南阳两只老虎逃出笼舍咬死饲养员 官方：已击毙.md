<p>5 月 25 日，河南南阳淅川县丹江孔雀谷景区内两只老虎逃出笼舍，大批警力到场处置。</p><p>@紧急呼叫 从淅川林业局获悉，老虎逃出笼舍但未逃出景区，出逃过程中将一名饲养员咬伤，后经抢救无效死亡。</p><p>林业局称，警方到场后因抓捕困难，为避免老虎再次伤人将其击毙。</p><p></p><div class="zaker_div"><div id="zkvideo16219368886355" class="zk_upvideo" data-zkinfo="%7B%22status%22%3A%22finished%22%2C%22imgUrl%22%3A%22http%3A%2F%2Fzkres.myzaker.com%2Fimg_upload%2Feditor%2F2021%2F05%2F25%2F1621936887072_W853H480S.jpeg%22%2C%22videoUrl%22%3A%22http%3A%2F%2Fzkres.myzaker.com%2Fimg_upload%2Feditor%2Farticle_video%2F2021%2F05%2F25%2F16219368769002.mp4%22%2C%22name%22%3A%2216219368769002.mp4%22%2C%22width%22%3A853%2C%22height%22%3A480%7D"></div></div><p></p><div id="recommend_bottom"></div><div id="article_bottom"></div>
