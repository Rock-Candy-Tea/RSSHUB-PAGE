<p>近日，上海。此前一段监控视频显示，一辆停在单元门口的摩托车被一名行动不便的老人故意推到，损失近万元。但老人否认且态度强硬，时至今日车主依旧没有收到赔偿。11 日，该小区又一辆车子被推倒，疑似该老人所为，知情人称送外卖的小哥直接被吼走。车主担心再次发生同类事件，已经主动搬离该小区。</p><p></p><div class="zaker_div"><div id="zkvideo16576774247519" class="zk_upvideo" data-zkinfo="%7B%22status%22%3A%22finished%22%2C%22imgUrl%22%3A%22http%3A%2F%2Fzkres.myzaker.com%2Fimg_upload%2Feditor%2F2022%2F07%2F13%2F1657677425635_W1920H1080S.jpeg%22%2C%22videoUrl%22%3A%22http%3A%2F%2Fzkres.myzaker.com%2Fimg_upload%2Feditor%2Farticle_video%2F2022%2F07%2F13%2F16576774252928.mp4%22%2C%22name%22%3A%2216576774252928.mp4%22%2C%22width%22%3A1920%2C%22height%22%3A1080%7D"></div><div class="edi_oper"></div></div><p></p><div id="recommend_bottom"></div><div id="article_bottom"></div>
