<p>8 月 28 日，河北石家庄。男子孟令某在赵县范庄镇早餐摊点吃饭时，与在同一摊点吃饭的孟祥某因口角发生打斗，孟令某持板凳砸中孟祥某头部，又持铁铲将孟祥某妻子头部打伤，二人当场死亡。</p><p>警方通报：案发后，犯罪嫌疑人孟令某到当地派出所投案，随即被公安机关控制。目前，案件正在进一步侦办中。</p><p></p><div class="zaker_div"><div id="zkvideo16305886388648" class="zk_upvideo" data-zkinfo="%7B%22status%22%3A%22finished%22%2C%22imgUrl%22%3A%22http%3A%2F%2Fzkres.myzaker.com%2Fimg_upload%2Feditor%2F2021%2F09%2F02%2F1630588614176_W720H1280S.jpeg%22%2C%22videoUrl%22%3A%22http%3A%2F%2Fzkres.myzaker.com%2Fimg_upload%2Feditor%2Farticle_video%2F2021%2F09%2F02%2F16305886102612.mp4%22%2C%22name%22%3A%2216305886102612.mp4%22%2C%22width%22%3A720%2C%22height%22%3A1280%7D"></div><div class="edi_oper"></div></div><p></p><div id="recommend_bottom"></div><div id="article_bottom"></div>
