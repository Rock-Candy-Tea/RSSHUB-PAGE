<p>火锅吃到一半突然喷火，5名游客被烧伤紧急跳河逃生！昨天，杭州西溪湿地的网红项目——“摇摇锅”吃出事情来了，相关部门介入调查，“摇摇锅”项目被停运。</p><p><span><span><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/images/2021/10/17/1634470792140866829water.jpg" data-size="1080,720" alt="5ff351bec98daf136f9b8728574cc65f.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></span></p><p><span><span><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/images/2021/10/17/1634470792903478227water.jpg"
