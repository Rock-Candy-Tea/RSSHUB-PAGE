<p>原标题：再关停9家门店！荥阳严查疫情防控风险漏洞</p><p>当前疫情防控形势严峻，为达到发现一起、查处一起、曝光一起、震慑一片的目的，1月6日，荥阳市市场监管局继续重拳出击，严查流通、餐饮、药店等重点场所的疫情防控风险漏洞。</p><p>当日共责令整改问题单位39家，对京城街道佰味轩串串香火锅、北舞渡优质胡辣汤、索河街道老武汉热干面、骨汤包、傻子张大盘鸡、广武镇晓田祥小吃、乔楼镇魏记道口烧鸡猪蹄、豫龙镇果然好水果店、高村镇李老三老式扣碗等9家问题严重的食品经营单位责令停业整顿。</p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/01/06/d815a18900f255a4512746563bb2d797.jpeg" data-size="480,360" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/01/06/83c8c6f852f321847f5dd24fb538691e.jpeg" data-size="480,360" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/01/06/c0b392eb4580047ce613da534c9a8aba.jpeg" data-size="480,360" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/01/06/b70c8aaba523d6062e3c695bb137bdee.jpeg" data-size="480,640" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/01/06/cc6e8f53a0fef3676903aa1d1663f957.jpeg" data-size="480,360" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/01/06/a117fa388adb5822fe1886215b2b70ce.jpeg" data-size="480,640" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/01/06/5753642e5e9dfdea0c4e4450a2efb336.jpeg" data-size="480,221" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/01/06/da49c5d6eda3ec4eaf8bb5fd3061c66b.jpeg" data-size="480,360" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>荥阳市市场监管局有关人员表示，希望全市各相关商户引以为戒，认真落实疫情防控主体责任，夯实各项防疫举措，共同筑牢疫情防控防线。（介敏）</p><p>来源：大河网</p>
