<p><span>齐鲁晚报·齐鲁壹点记者 张中 通讯员 姜晓</span></p><p>1月30日，济南善德养老院内一片喜气洋洋，医护人员和老人共同走秀、包水饺，将虎年新春联欢会推向高潮，让老人在欢欢喜喜中过大年。善德养老院严格落实疫情防控措施，实行“非必要不探视、非预约不接待”，亲友预约探视不允许进入生活区，为了让老人在春节期间不孤单寂寞，养老院医护人员为老人准备了丰富多彩的节目，让老人在浓浓的年味中快快乐乐过大年。</p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/02/02/90848504d9d11977df0e8642cf98bc64.jpeg" data-size="720,480" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/02/02/f2f126b0cf404e4b9fa7a8505255954e.jpeg" data-size="720,960" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/02/02/a1055aacf91771f682d0001ce2642bc0.jpeg" data-size="720,540" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/02/02/1de4b0325a5967cdacfaef7c6675799c.jpeg" data-size="720,480" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/02/02/4dbc58b3327f95115507fb406a8814cd.jpeg" data-size="720,480" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/02/02/5f663bf580c89e57a957e921e33ec806.jpeg" data-size="720,480" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/02/02/cd2e24bec6c9066030b84e52f1b0fa5a.jpeg" data-size="720,540" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/02/02/9f88c555d5a4706559c39eb0a99bca47.jpeg" data-size="720,480" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
