<p>4月6日9时，宁马高速往南京方向，高速九大队民警巡逻中发现路面散落多块木板，立即上前搬运清理。民警通过监控查到了抛洒滴漏的货车，联系上驾车人依法对其处以罚款200元处罚并责令整改。蜀黍提醒，行车上高速一定要提前对车辆货物做好捆扎工作，以免抛洒的障碍物影响道路交通安全。</p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/04/07/efe4f29b8e180657918085cb291c2604.jpeg" data-size="440,330" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/04/07/8faaa01380c9544109fc17458828bb33.jpeg" data-size="440,330" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/04/07/86c07fcb9c458da0986e40cabb7c8887.jpeg" data-size="440,330" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
