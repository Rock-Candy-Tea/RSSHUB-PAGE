<p>1月16日上午，在海南热带野生动物园举办了欢迎雌性白颊长臂猿“命大”仪式。15日，经过一天的奔波，“命大”从杭州安全抵达海口，经过一晚的适应期后，“命大”精神抖擞地在枝头上跳跃。据悉，“命大”将单独在展区内度过适应期，预计将于2月14日当天安排雄性白颊长臂猿“亮亮”和“命大”进行合笼。（记者 刘孙谋 通讯员 李冬林）</p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/01/16/c0db35a47f1b51ec630389956ec44e81.jpeg" data-size="440,230" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/01/16/b9427e837b1827af33bdb03f06fe572e.jpeg" data-size="440,237" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/01/16/7779e8352d6e271c82c9db109b7342b2.jpeg" data-size="440,234" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/01/16/0700577eb14b2e40c725d752967960f2.jpeg" data-size="440,241" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/01/16/76202fba296c50da2eaebe2515c66507.jpeg" data-size="440,240" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/01/16/dd5b42d2b7c198eba543a5d897920b96.jpeg" data-size="440,252" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/01/16/30d2d6e3933122140c4b1425f8b6d7fd.jpeg" data-size="440,234" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/01/16/16e4d7f14f106916fa9a84d355eb54b8.jpeg" data-size="440,248" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/01/16/2564bbea7a05a51f93f847acf5b72f3d.jpeg" data-size="440,288" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
