<p>3月5日12时许，兴山县古夫水陆派出所民警杜承诚、辅警董陈在刘家湾小区走访调查时，突遇谭某家厨房失火。只见厨房内火光闪闪，烟雾弥漫。在对现场火情快速分析评估后，杜承诚、董陈二人迅速冲进厨房将煤气罐拖出，消除最大隐患，之后使用灭火器迅速将火扑灭。因处置及时得当，火情没有扩大造成更严重后果。</p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/03/07/d4bd0167e2553c78b2ff10bf9f1accea.jpeg" data-size="440,198" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/03/07/780087870b9683a85a3e98793d031032.jpeg" data-size="440,198" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/03/07/533229fffafca327182d588f1f720807.jpeg" data-size="440,198" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/03/07/6283add7b1b3d929bc1686b92750a5fa.jpeg" data-size="440,198" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
