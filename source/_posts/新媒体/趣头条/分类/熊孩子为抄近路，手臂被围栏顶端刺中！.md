src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/06/07/f340839ee1c2d19559fde85c7f86d950.gif" data-size="281,202" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/06/07/e515134eb1fd3c1a21f45134227cdb35.png" data-size="992,564" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/06/07/64103e4f2d1dac4aed626f7aa530030a.gif" data-size="319,181" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>消防员使用液压破拆工具对男孩被卡手臂四周的围栏进行破拆，将男孩救下，并移交给现场急救人员救治。</p><p><img
