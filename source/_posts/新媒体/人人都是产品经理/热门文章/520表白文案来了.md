<p>
</p><p>又快到520表白日了。</p>
<p>我已经准备GUCCI和520红包，现在就差一个对象了。而作为品牌，消费者就是我们的情人。趁着这个好机会，给大家多点产品优惠的同时，再来一记暖心的告白吧。</p>
<p>这里我写了10个行业的借势文案，抛砖引你。如果能帮到你，那最好不过了。</p>
<h2 id="toc-1">1. 理财</h2>
<p>文案：从一点点喜欢，变成满是欢喜。</p>
<p><img data-action="zoom" class=" wp-image-1030398 aligncenter" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.woshipm.com/wp-files/2018/05/YMN3MlyLvYL2AtF1QBQF.jpg" alt width="800" height="375" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h2 id="toc-2">2. 护肤品</h2>
<p>文案：你的美，从未改变。</p>
<p><img data-action="zoom" class=" wp-image-1030401 aligncenter" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.woshipm.com/wp-files/2018/05/ZoJQaPtoDcEfm6geV5m9.jpg" alt width="800" height="340" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h2 id="toc-3">3. 洗发水</h2>
<p>文案：去你心里的路，我准备一头走到黑。</p>
<p><img data-action="zoom" class=" wp-image-1030403 aligncenter" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.woshipm.com/wp-files/2018/05/jpT9n9Ta9JKjaW0gC5Yh.jpg" alt width="800" height="340" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h2 id="toc-4">4. 空调</h2>
<p>文案：想在你心底下，乘凉。</p>
<p><img data-action="zoom" class=" wp-image-1030404 aligncenter" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.woshipm.com/wp-files/2018/05/guJ3efMr1BPm8HualvLU.jpg" alt width="798" height="374" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h2 id="toc-5">5. 手机</h2>
<p>文案：放得下手机，放不下你。</p>
<p><img data-action="zoom" class=" wp-image-1030406 aligncenter" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.woshipm.com/wp-files/2018/05/YYRzzg9p81a1LrvJQQMA.jpg" alt width="805" height="342" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h2 id="toc-6">6. 酒店</h2>
<p>文案：想在你的左心房睡懒觉。</p>
<p><img data-action="zoom" class=" wp-image-1030407 aligncenter" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.woshipm.com/wp-files/2018/05/Sddz1pHKCUhZh0luMC3G.jpg" alt width="801" height="342" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h2 id="toc-7">7. 墨镜</h2>
<p>文案：你的出现，周围的一切都黯然失色。</p>
<p><img data-action="zoom" class=" wp-image-1030408 aligncenter" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.woshipm.com/wp-files/2018/05/CoXMq1MRS59J5kxwNV7O.jpg" alt width="800" height="340" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h2 id="toc-8">8. 内衣</h2>
<p>文案：因为你，我可以托起整个世界。</p>
<p><img data-action="zoom" class=" wp-image-1030410 aligncenter" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.woshipm.com/wp-files/2018/05/7tvhRthu0aQLzHwkp5tj.jpg" alt width="798" height="339" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h2 id="toc-9">9. 口红</h2>
<p>文案：稀里糊涂，爱上你。</p>
<p><img data-action="zoom" class=" wp-image-1030411 aligncenter" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.woshipm.com/wp-files/2018/05/G7l3uh0PRGbVsLYAEp8Q.jpg" alt width="800" height="340" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h2 id="toc-10">10. 地产</h2>
<p>文案：落叶归根，我归你。</p>
<p><img data-action="zoom" class=" wp-image-1030412 aligncenter" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.woshipm.com/wp-files/2018/05/5INUKq0H3MFwyfFqL5uy.jpg" alt width="801" height="342" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>我觉得520这种日子，挺有意义的。一方面，品牌需要这种的契机，让促销活动显得更合理。另一方面，我们也需要这种仪式感，去做一些润滑日常的事。</p>
<p>去表白，去制造惊喜，去热爱大胆一回。</p>
