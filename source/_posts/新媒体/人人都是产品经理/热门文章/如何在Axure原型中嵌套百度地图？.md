<blockquote><p>如何在Axure原型中嵌套百度地图？一起来文中看看~</p></blockquote>
<p><img data-action="zoom" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1836097" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.woshipm.com/wp-files/2019/01/DpUyBT2VhHUsgUmDSekC.jpg" alt width="800" height="450" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>首先我们要准备好页面的元件，从元件库中拉出内联框架。</p>
<p>我们先给内联框架一些基本配置：</p>
<p><img data-action="zoom" class=" aligncenter" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.woshipm.com/wp-files/2019/01/q2IYJ4oNX8hc3SGE44hc.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>然后，打开百度地图开放平台。</p>
<p>网址：http://lbsyun.baidu.com/，在顶部开发文档的菜单中选择地图生成器。</p>
<p><img data-action="zoom" class=" aligncenter" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.woshipm.com/wp-files/2019/01/KpwnwRCtliq32KOjBmhQ.jpg" width="764" height="281" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>接下来来配置，创建地图的HTML文件，先给地图设置中心点。</p>
<p><img data-action="zoom" class=" aligncenter" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.woshipm.com/wp-files/2019/01/G6WLCV8TioXLm9aGL4Kw.jpg" width="785" height="498" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>再设置地图的尺寸、显示和功能组件。</p>
<p><img data-action="zoom" class=" aligncenter" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.woshipm.com/wp-files/2019/01/TbfYjjNbCR9XcJ3nKtZR.jpg" width="734" height="450" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>点击下方获取代码，将代码全选复制下来。</p>
<p><img data-action="zoom" class=" aligncenter" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.woshipm.com/wp-files/2019/01/v0aznhs4cFdYsxecKI2A.jpg" width="712" height="543" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在本地新建一个文本，将复制到代码粘贴进去。</p>
<p><img data-action="zoom" class=" aligncenter" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.woshipm.com/wp-files/2019/01/0cQCaLtedvTGIahQjQ1d.jpg" width="728" height="594" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>再反回页面点击申请密匙。</p>
<p><img data-action="zoom" class=" aligncenter" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.woshipm.com/wp-files/2019/01/spyx4WIJeXllJx8QOfp3.jpg" width="720" height="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>点击后跳转到创建应用页面，点击创建应用。</p>
<p><img data-action="zoom" class=" aligncenter" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.woshipm.com/wp-files/2019/01/c0Dzt2SBZFX9UQocqBsU.jpg" width="718" height="343" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>接下来配置应用，结束后提交。</p>
<p><img data-action="zoom" class=" aligncenter" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.woshipm.com/wp-files/2019/01/gwFQxWASBEmZXyG83sPk.jpg" width="725" height="519" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>将密匙复制下来。</p>
<p><img data-action="zoom" class=" aligncenter" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.woshipm.com/wp-files/2019/01/qa05LkYh0OQzFMze5oOo.jpg" width="739" height="535" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>粘贴到刚才创建的文本的指定位置。</p>
<p><img data-action="zoom" class=" aligncenter" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.woshipm.com/wp-files/2019/01/XFJvdelCHMkkLXJOuo0g.jpg" width="754" height="616" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>然后将文件保存，且命名为：mymap.html。</p>
<p><img data-action="zoom" class=" aligncenter" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.woshipm.com/wp-files/2019/01/gsldRnDhnGDFq38MlNPc.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>然后将文件打开就是地图了。</p>
<p><img data-action="zoom" class=" aligncenter" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.woshipm.com/wp-files/2019/01/TuUmIFCP0kzHVbugPNCk.jpg" width="736" height="528" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>如果想将地图底下的文字删除，将代码中的这段代码删除。</p>
<p><img data-action="zoom" class=" aligncenter" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.woshipm.com/wp-files/2019/01/eHLXKNLlnlNkUnpohFVo.jpg" width="677" height="417" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>接下来将文件的具体地址放进内联框架的目标超链接中。</p>
<p>（<strong>具体地址：</strong>打开地图的html文件，浏览器上显示的链接就是具体地址。）</p>
<p><img data-action="zoom" class=" aligncenter" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.woshipm.com/wp-files/2019/01/wIRcT0ICWJWLEgjBOLE3.jpg" width="705" height="431" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>放进去后，文件预览是看不见效果的，只有将文件生成HTML文件才能预览的效果。</p>
<p><img data-action="zoom" class=" aligncenter" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.woshipm.com/wp-files/2019/01/y91s2dwqHDMssXmqCduS.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p><img data-action="zoom" class=" aligncenter" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.woshipm.com/wp-files/2019/01/ESsbvSq4HjBh9vTxxQbN.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p><img data-action="zoom" class=" aligncenter" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.woshipm.com/wp-files/2019/01/Z9hxH2PawbFQH618qa62.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
