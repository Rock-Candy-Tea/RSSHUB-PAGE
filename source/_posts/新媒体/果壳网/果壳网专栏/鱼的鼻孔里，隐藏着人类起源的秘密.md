style="color: rgb(154, 131, 88);box-sizing: border-box;"><strong style="box-sizing: border-box;">不厌其烦地进行磨片、画图</strong></span>，常常工作到深夜。</p><p style="white-space: normal;box-sizing: border-box;"><br
