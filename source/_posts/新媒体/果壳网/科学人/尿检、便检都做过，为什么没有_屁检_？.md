style="white-space: normal;margin: 0px;padding: 0px;box-sizing: border-box;"><strong style="box-sizing: border-box;"><span style="color: rgb(52, 52, 52);box-sizing: border-box;">数量的不对劲</span></strong><span style="color: rgb(52, 52, 52);box-sizing: border-box;">，比如尿多或尿少，腹泻或便秘；</span></p><p style="white-space: normal;margin: 0px;padding: 0px;box-sizing: border-box;"><strong style="box-sizing: border-box;"><span style="color: rgb(52, 52, 52);box-sizing: border-box;">性质的不对劲</span></strong><span style="color: rgb(52, 52, 52);box-sizing: border-box;">，比如尿液的五彩斑斓，便便的软硬稀稠；</span></p><p style="white-space: normal;margin: 0px;padding: 0px;box-sizing: border-box;"><strong style="box-sizing: border-box;"><span style="color: rgb(52, 52, 52);box-sizing: border-box;">过程的不对劲</span></strong><span style="color: rgb(52, 52, 52);box-sizing: border-box;">，比如尿尿时觉得尿痛，拉粑粑时觉得腹痛；</span></p><p style="white-space: normal;margin: 0px;padding:
