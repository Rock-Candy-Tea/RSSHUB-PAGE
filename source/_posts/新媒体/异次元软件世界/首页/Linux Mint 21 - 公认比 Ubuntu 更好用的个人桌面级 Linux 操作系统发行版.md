2。<strong>MATE</strong> 随 Linux Mint12 投入使用。</p><p><img class="aligncenter size-medium" src="https://img.iplaysoft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/linuxmint/linux_mint_anime_girl.jpg" alt="Linux Mint
