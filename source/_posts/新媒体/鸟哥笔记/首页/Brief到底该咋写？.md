<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-size: 20px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; color: #7a4442; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">一、brief到底是什么？</span></strong></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; visibility: visible;">brief又称<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E5%88%9B%E6%84%8F%E7%AE%80%E6%8A%A5" target="_blank">创意简报</a>，也叫工作简报；在小红书投放过程中，首先由品牌方输出要求，整合成brief传递给达人，达人了解到品牌方诉求后，再根据brief展开创作，将品牌理念、产品信息等传递给用户</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; visibility: visible;">简单来说，brief就是连接品牌方、达人、用户三方的桥梁，一个好的brief，应该能够清楚表达品牌诉求、利于达人理解和创作，并有可以清晰的传递给用户的品牌信息</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0.5em; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; background-color: #ffffff; text-align: inherit; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><img class="rich_pages wxw-img" style="padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; vertical-align: bottom; box-shadow: #969696 0em 0em 0.5em 0px; border-radius: 9px; width: 563px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; height: 400px; visibility: visible !important; margin: 0px;" src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/5cbcd79fd63dc4d72595fa43f6df0dd4.png" alt="图片" crossorigin="anonymous" data-galleryid data-ratio="0.7105508870214753" data-s="300,640" data-src="https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_png/C1ptrBKcREogQopRXzGvek5cOdzAyYJEeTobUbzC9jhTOQY9aayMI6QxwmAPibTO46R81nHg2jvJlqr8OwcRKug/640?wx_fmt=png" data-type="png" data-w="1071" data-fail="0" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-size: 20px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; color: #7a4442; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">二、先明确投放诉求</span></strong></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; visibility: visible;">在达人投放这个问题上，首先要明确投放的主题，确定我们要投放给谁看，预期达到什么样的效果，根据这两点得出品牌的brief会更加具体，也更利于达人创作</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; visibility: visible;">1）诉求对象</span></strong><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; visibility: visible;"></span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; visibility: visible;">诉求对象其实就是我们的用户群体，明确我们的用户画像，进行精准的用户洞察，让达人进行针对性的创作，使作品最终可以精准投放到我们的目标受众</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; visibility: visible;">2）诉求点</span></strong><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; visibility: visible;"></span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px;">量</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px;">宣传</span><span style="margin: 0px; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px;">不同</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; <p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-size: 20px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; color: #7a4442; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">三、Brief具体怎么写？</span></strong></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; visibility: visible;">一份成熟的brief需要把品牌想让消费者了解到的信息全面清晰的展现出来，具体内容包括但不限于：品牌信息、产品介绍、内容要求、及其他合作要求等等</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; visibility: visible;">1）品牌信息</span></strong><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; visibility: visible;"></span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; visibility: visible;">品牌信息主要包括品牌历史、品牌故事、品牌地位、创始人和代言人等等</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; visibility: visible;"> </span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;
