<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="12"> </p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="13"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;">整体来看，二季度快手电商业务的表现关键词是强韧。</span></p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="14"> </p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="15"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;">先看核心数据。GMV方面，Q2环比Q1增加161亿，增速9.2%；商家方面，月动销快品牌商家数量环比翻倍增长，月动销品牌商家数量环比增长超2倍；用户方面，重复购买率同环比均有上升。</span></p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="16"> </p>

<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="18"> </p>

<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="20"> </p>
<h2 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; border: 0px; color: #212629; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: left; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;" data-track="21"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-weight: bold;">一、流量飞轮：公私域循环</span></span></h2>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="22"> </p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="23"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;">移动互联网红利见顶，电商的流量焦虑在加重。一方面，公域流量越来越贵；一方面，私域流量沉淀并不容易。</span></p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="24"> </p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="25"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;">快手长期以私域见长，但现在公域流量也很大：2022Q2财报显示，快手平均DAU同比增长18.5%达到3.47亿；总流量同比增长38.7%；每DAU日均使用时长提高到125.2分钟，同比提高18.3分钟。在今年2月Quest Mobile发布的《2021中国移动互联网年度大报告 》，从用户使用总时长看，快手位列第三。</span></p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="26"> </p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="27"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;">因此，现在快手电商的进化思路是融合公私域，破除公私域流量的流动隔阂。8月快手在电商服务商大会上公开了快手电商的新流量策略和新经营逻辑，快手高级副总裁、快手电商负责人笑古说，“我们要让发现页和关注页的公私域流量循环形成双轮驱动，让生意持续稳定增长。”</span></p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="28"> </p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="29"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-weight: bold;">在全新的流量驱动逻辑下，快手商家一方面能够持续从超级公域获取流量，提高商品曝光，获得直接的销量，另一方面能够将公域流量沉淀成自己的私域流量池，实现粉丝增长和长期的私域复购，形成稳定利润。</span></span></p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="30"> </p>
