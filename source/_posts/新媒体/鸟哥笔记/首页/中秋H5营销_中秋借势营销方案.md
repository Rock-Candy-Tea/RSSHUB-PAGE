                        <p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E4%B8%AD%E7%A7%8B%E8%8A%82" target="_blank">中秋节</a>追热点已经成为许多品牌商家的常态化工作，虽然中秋节有祭月、赏月、吃月饼、看花灯、赏桂花、饮桂花酒等多种民俗，能为商家活动策划提供丰富的主题，但开展中秋节营销活动的商家太多太多，在这习以为常的节日设计中，怎样做到吸引更多用户，从中脱颖而出却是一件相当不易之事。中秋就要到了，就让我们来看看往年中秋都是如何用H5吸引用户的。</p>
<h1 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: 30px; color: #212629; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 20px; text-align: center; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); font-weight: 600; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;">案例一：特种饼登月计划-和平精英</strong></h1>
<h2 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; line-height: 30px; color: #212629; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); text-align: left; font-weight: 600; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;">品牌：腾讯游戏</strong></h2>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/089508cbd9a41cb209843ccfc6ce4353.png" alt width="700" height="440" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></strong></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;">案例介绍：</strong>腾讯游戏借中秋节品牌宣传H5，设计上运用了月饼、天灯、夜晚、月亮等中秋元素，以登月计划（小游戏）的形式，宣传和平精英游戏。用户需要在4个特种饼（巧克力、樱花、五仁、抹茶）中选择一个自己喜欢的形象进行游戏；确认好后点击“开始”按钮，根据提示点击“跳跃”按钮操作特种饼起跳，特种饼找准时机跳在移动的天灯上，在60秒内抵达月宫即可捡到登月空投奖励。</p>
<h1 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: 30px; color: #212629; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 20px; text-align: center; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); font-weight: 600; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;">案例二：中秋，假如思念有声音......</strong></h1>
<h2 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; line-height: 30px; color: #212629; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); text-align: left; font-weight: 600; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;">品牌：喜马拉雅</strong></h2>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/e7205bc51d202cd11258b8e9a528105d.png" alt width="700" height="445" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></strong></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;">案例介绍：</strong>喜马拉雅推出的中秋节作品，从平台“听”的角度出发，让用户聆听生活中代表思念的声音，感受生活的美好，还可以点击触点加入会员，聆听更多声音节目。H5通过展示一幅幅手绘的生活图，如妈妈做饭，火车飞驰，街头熙攘，晚上赏月，家人散步，静谧农村等，让用户聆听图中场景的相应声音，营造意境，表达中秋思念主题。</p>
<h1 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: 30px; color: #212629; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 20px; text-align: center; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); font-weight: 600; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;">案例三：中秋奇妙夜</strong></h1>
<h2 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; line-height: 30px; color: #212629; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: left; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-weight: 600; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;">品牌：腾讯新闻</strong></h2>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/55f049cd928593fd6962710b93f8cba5.png" alt width="700" height="441" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></strong></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;">案例介绍：</strong>由腾讯出品重力感应类H5，邀请用户感受不同地方的赏月体验，融入中国古诗词，国风浓郁，赏美景，学古句，意境绝佳。用户进入H5游戏后，通过重力感应来选择一个观景地，包含大漠敦煌、万里长城、梦回古都、平湖秋月、太空奇旅等景点。选择好后将穿过云雾，通过转动手机改变观景角度，欣赏VR美景。遇上喜欢的美景可点击照相触点，生成VR赏月海报，然后可关闭海报继续观赏，也可点击海报上的触点登陆腾讯新闻APP或领取福包。</p>

<h2 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; line-height: 30px; color: #212629; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); text-align: left; font-weight: 600; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;">品牌：天目新闻</strong></h2>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/6fd0766fbec9f90e6322db91dc2abeae.png" alt width="700" height="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></strong></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">案例介绍：</strong><span style="font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-weight: 400;">中秋吃月饼是传统习俗，天目新闻邀请用户参与性格测试，生成代表自己性格的特殊月饼，切合中秋营销节点，表现平台的创意思维，吸引用户关注和登陆浏览。H5将传统月饼与空旷宇宙结合，营造意境感，有点若虚若实的感觉，题目设置得也比较文艺和有诗意。测试结果为每个性格属性设计了不同的色系和样式，搭配同色系海报，突出金属月饼的高颜值，360度的滑动欣赏设置增加互动，丰富视觉体验，好看又好玩。</span></strong></p>                        <div style="height: 1px"></div>
