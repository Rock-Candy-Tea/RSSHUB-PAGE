<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">在广告或者营销行业，<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/brief" target="_blank">brief</a>是从品牌方向服务商、继而向创意生产者（如KOL达人）传递信息的媒介。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">那么，对于出海品牌来说，应该如何撰写一份优质的brief文档呢？</p>
</section>
<section>
<section>
<section></section>
</section>
</section>
<section>
<section>
<section></section>
</section>
</section>
<section>
<section>
<section>
<h2 id="toc-1" style="font-size: 18px; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); padding-left: 12px; background-position: 0px 5px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; line-height: 30px; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-size: 8px 20px; color: #212629; font-weight: 600; text-align: left;">为什么你需要brief？</h2>
</section>
</section>
</section>
<section>
<p style="margin-bottom: 18px; margin-top: 0px;">Brief又名“简报”，是品牌方发送给合作机构和博主的说明性文档。通常包含本次KOL活动的目标、时间表、付款条件、KPI等内容。</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 18px; margin-top: 0px;">在KOL营销活动中，brief是品牌、机构、合作KOL拉平信息，建立信息对称的一种方式。如果没有Brief，相当于高楼没打地基，盖到一半会翻车</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 18px; margin-top: 0px;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">除了信息对齐之外，brief还能帮您：</span></p>
</section>
<section>
<section>
<section>
<h3 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; line-height: 30px; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//1bb91f73e9d31ea2830a5e73ce3ed328.png'); padding-left: 12px; background-position: 0px 5px; font-size: 16px; color: #212629; background-size: 8px 20px; font-weight: 600; text-align: left;">宣传品牌</h3>
</section>
</section>
</section>
<section>
<p style="margin-bottom: 18px; margin-top: 0px;">有才华的创作者每天都会收到品牌合作的请求。那么如何在人群中脱颖而出呢？创建一个强有力的brief可以让您以创造性的方式宣传您的品牌并说服他们与您合作。</p>
</section>
<section>
<section>
<section>
<h3 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; line-height: 30px; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//1bb91f73e9d31ea2830a5e73ce3ed328.png'); padding-left: 12px; background-position: 0px 5px; font-size: 16px; color: #212629; background-size: 8px 20px; font-weight: 600; text-align: left;">明确目标</h3>
</section>
</section>
</section>
<section>
<p style="margin-bottom: 18px; margin-top: 0px;">明确您的KOL营销目标、目标、策略和 KPI，这将使您更容易将您的计划和结果传达给您的团队和管理层。</p>
</section>
<section>
<section>
<section>
<h3 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; line-height: 30px; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//1bb91f73e9d31ea2830a5e73ce3ed328.png'); padding-left: 12px; background-position: 0px 5px; font-size: 16px; color: #212629; background-size: 8px 20px; font-weight: 600; text-align: left;">提高效率</h3>
</section>
</section>
</section>
<section>
<p style="margin-bottom: 18px; margin-top: 0px;">通过为KOL营销人员关系和活动创建标准文档和流程来简化和改进您的KOL营销（这将为您节省大量时间和精力！）。</p>
</section>
<section>
<section>
<section>
<h3 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; line-height: 30px; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//1bb91f73e9d31ea2830a5e73ce3ed328.png'); padding-left: 12px; background-position: 0px 5px; font-size: 16px; color: #212629; background-size: 8px 20px; font-weight: 600; text-align: left;">吸引合作</h3>
</section>
</section>
</section>
<section>
<p style="margin-bottom: 18px; margin-top: 0px;">通过明确设置和管理围绕活动指南和可交付成果的期望，吸引并与合适的KOL建立更好的关系。</p>
</section>
<section>
<section>
<section>
<h3 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; line-height: 30px; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//1bb91f73e9d31ea2830a5e73ce3ed328.png'); padding-left: 12px; background-position: 0px 5px; font-size: 16px; color: #212629; background-size: 8px 20px; font-weight: 600; text-align: left;">少走弯路</h3>
</section>
</section>
</section>
<section>
<p style="margin-bottom: 18px; margin-top: 0px;">让内容与品牌愿景和目标保持一致，让您高枕无忧。</p>
</section>
<section>
<section>
<section></section>
</section>
</section>
<section>
<section>
<section></section>
</section>
</section>
<section>
<section>
<section>
<h2 id="toc-2" style="font-size: 18px; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); padding-left: 12px; background-position: 0px 5px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; line-height: 30px; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-size: 8px 20px; color: #212629; font-weight: 600; text-align: left;">brief中应该传递哪些信息？</h2>
</section>
</section>
</section>
<section>
<p style="margin-bottom: 18px; margin-top: 0px;">有效的KOL营销简报应包括以下 5 个要素：</p>
</section>
<section>
<section>
<h3 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; line-height: 30px; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//1bb91f73e9d31ea2830a5e73ce3ed328.png'); padding-left: 12px; background-position: 0px 5px; font-size: 16px; color: #212629; background-size: 8px 20px; font-weight: 600; text-align: left;">1.<span style="font-size: 16px;">品牌/产品介绍</span></h3>
</section>
</section>
<section>
<p style="margin-bottom: 18px; margin-top: 0px;">KOL需要知道他们在与谁合作，因此品牌商需要做一个简单的品牌&产品介绍：介绍一下你是谁？你想宣传什么，以及博主们为什么要宣传你。</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 18px; margin-top: 0px;">同时，不要忘记添加官网链接、社交媒体账号以及任何其他照片或创意的链接，以更好地展示您的品牌。</p>
</section>
<section>
<section>
<h3 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; line-height: 30px; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//1bb91f73e9d31ea2830a5e73ce3ed328.png'); padding-left: 12px; background-position: 0px 5px; font-size: 16px; color: #212629; background-size: 8px 20px; font-weight: 600; text-align: left;">2.<span style="font-size: 16px;">活动总结</span></h3>
</section>
</section>
<section>
<p style="margin-bottom: 18px; margin-top: 0px;">在描述活动执行细节之前，品牌方需用 2-3 句话在开头清晰简洁对活动进行总结，内容需包含：此活动的产品（或服务、活动等）是什么？本次活动目的是？是新品上市吗？这是节日优惠活动呢？是提升品牌知名度还是增加产品销量？</p>
</section>
<section>
<section>
<section>
<section>
<p style="margin-bottom: 18px; margin-top: 0px;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">举个栗子</span></p>
</section>
</section>
</section>
<section></section>
<section>
<section>
<p style="margin-bottom: 18px; margin-top: 0px;">Brand X is partnering with Influencer Z to promote the launch of its new mascara, designed specifically for people who wear contact lenses. The goal of this campaign is to reach a new customer audience and boost the number of purchases the day of launch.</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 18px; margin-top: 0px;">（Brand X 与 Influencer Z 合作推广其新睫毛膏的推出，该睫毛膏专为佩戴隐形眼镜的人设计。该活动的目标是吸引新的客户受众并增加发布当天的购买数量。）</p>
</section>
</section>
</section>
<section>
<section>
<h3 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; line-height: 30px; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//1bb91f73e9d31ea2830a5e73ce3ed328.png'); padding-left: 12px; background-position: 0px 5px; font-size: 16px; color: #212629; background-size: 8px 20px; font-weight: 600; text-align: left;">3.<span style="font-size: 16px;">执行细节</span></h3>
</section>
</section>
<section>
<p style="margin-bottom: 18px; margin-top: 0px;">概述协作基础知识。他们需要明确的方向和对您成功期望的理解，因此请务必涵盖：</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 18px; margin-top: 0px;">(1) 投放频道：<span style="font-size: 16px;">您希望在哪些社交媒体平台进行投放</span></p>
</section>
<section>
<section>
<section>
<p style="margin-bottom: 18px; margin-top: 0px;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">(2) 帖子数量：</span><span style="font-size: 16px;">您要创建的帖子数</span></p>
</section>
</section>
</section>
<section>
<section>
<section>
<p style="margin-bottom: 18px; margin-top: 0px;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">(3) 时间表：</span><span style="font-size: 16px;">发布日期、截止时间等</span></p>
</section>
</section>
</section>
<section>
<section>
<section>
<p style="margin-bottom: 18px; margin-top: 0px;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">(4) 活动目标：</span><span style="font-size: 16px;">您的活动目标和 KPI等</span></p>
</section>
</section>
</section>
<section>
<section>
<section>
<p style="margin-bottom: 18px; margin-top: 0px;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">(5) 内容交付：</span><span style="font-size: 16px;">KOL在项目完成应如何向您发送他们的内容和结果？你期望什么？你喜欢的任何文件格式？他们需要什么时候发送？</span></p>
</section>
</section>
</section>
<section>
<section>
<section>
<p style="margin-bottom: 18px; margin-top: 0px;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">(6) 付款条件：</span><span style="font-size: 16px;">如果您的活动中涉及到付款，请注明会涉及到的费用以及达到什么要求才会付款，以便博主知道会发生什么。</span></p>
<ul style="padding-left: 40px; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.6); list-style: none;">
<li style="position: relative; padding-left: 20px; margin-bottom: 5px;"><span style="font-size: 16px;">报酬类型：固定金额、佣金、其他等</span></li>
<li style="position: relative; padding-left: 20px; margin-bottom: 5px;"><span style="font-size: 16px;">付款方式</span></li>
<li style="position: relative; padding-left: 20px; margin-bottom: 5px;"><span style="font-size: 16px;">付款日期</span></li>
<li style="position: relative; padding-left: 20px; margin-bottom: 5px;"><span style="font-size: 16px;">付款条款和条件</span></li>
</ul>
</section>
</section>
</section>
<section>
<section>
<section>
<p style="margin-bottom: 18px; margin-top: 0px;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">(6)品牌支持：</span><span style="font-size: 16px;">本次活动品牌可以提供哪些资源，如有请注明</span></p>
</section>
</section>
</section>
<section>
<section>
<section>
<p style="margin-bottom: 18px; margin-top: 0px;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">(7)取消政策：</span><span style="font-size: 16px;">如果博主想取消合作，他们应该怎么做？</span></p>
</section>
</section>
</section>
<section>
<section>
<section>
<p style="margin-bottom: 18px; margin-top: 0px;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">(8)保密：</span><span style="font-size: 16px;">让KOL知道这此合作是否需要提前保密机密。他们是否应该在特定日期之前避免展示或提及某一产品或服务？</span></p>
</section>
</section>
</section>
<section>
<section>
<h3 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; line-height: 30px; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//1bb91f73e9d31ea2830a5e73ce3ed328.png'); padding-left: 12px; background-position: 0px 5px; font-size: 16px; color: #212629; background-size: 8px 20px; font-weight: 600; text-align: left;">4.<span style="font-size: 16px;">内容要求</span></h3>
</section>
</section>
<section>
<p style="margin-bottom: 18px; margin-top: 0px;">如果有要求，最好提前一次写明，减少后续改稿沟通返工的成本，包括但不限于：</p>
</section>
<section>
<section>
<section>
<p style="margin-bottom: 18px; margin-top: 0px;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">（1）内容形式：</span><span style="font-size: 16px;">如图片、视频、长文，长文需要含几张图等。</span></p>
</section>
</section>
</section>
<section>
<section>
<section>
<p style="margin-bottom: 18px; margin-top: 0px;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">（2）内容风格：</span><span style="font-size: 16px;">科普、评测、体验、故事……在搭配博主环节也会考虑到内容风格的要求进行适配。</span></p>
</section>
</section>
</section>
<section>
<section>
<section>
<p style="margin-bottom: 18px; margin-top: 0px;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">（3）期望的视觉效果:</span><span style="font-size: 16px;">您可以通过情绪版向博主介绍品牌视觉要求。</span></p>
</section>
</section>
</section>

<section>
<section>
<section>
<p style="margin-bottom: 18px; margin-top: 0px;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">（4）创意方向：</span><span style="font-size: 16px;">比如5W1H（谁、在什么时间、什么场景、因为什么、发生了什么、结果怎样），或者此前的活动案例链接。</span></p>
</section>
</section>
</section>
<section>
<section>
<section>
<p style="margin-bottom: 18px; margin-top: 0px;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">（5）内容长度：</span><span style="font-size: 16px;">字数、图片数、视频长度等。</span></p>
</section>
</section>
</section>
<section>
<section>
<section>
<p style="margin-bottom: 18px; margin-top: 0px;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">选题：</span><span style="font-size: 16px;">如果有明确选题可直接给出，如果没有可以给个方向或要求，由服务商或达人选择后在正式写作前确认。因为内容要扣题，所以在定大纲或初稿之前选题是第一步要确认的。</span></p>
</section>
</section>
</section>
<section>
<section>
<section>
<p style="margin-bottom: 18px; margin-top: 0px;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">（6）帖子标题说明</span></p>
</section>
</section>
</section>
<section>
<p style="margin-bottom: 18px; margin-top: 0px;">是否需要注明的品牌主题标签、链接、折扣代码、附属链接或登录页面</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 18px; margin-top: 0px;">是否需要在其帖子中@的社交媒体帐户或使用品牌标签</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 18px; margin-top: 0px;">是否需要披露品牌赞助信息，请根据当地法规要求决定是否添加；</p>
</section>
<section>
<section>
<section>
<p style="margin-bottom: 18px; margin-top: 0px;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">（7）互动要求：</span><span style="font-size: 16px;">比如对评论区的维护；</span></p>
</section>
</section>
</section>
<section>
<section>
<section>
<p style="margin-bottom: 18px; margin-top: 0px;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">（8）话术要求：</span><span style="font-size: 16px;">有些特殊信息的标准表达、要避讳的点、关键词（要埋的关键词–比如要看回搜率的锚点词、要避的关键词—比如平台敏感词）等。</span></p>
</section>
</section>
</section>
<section>
<section>
<h3 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; line-height: 30px; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//1bb91f73e9d31ea2830a5e73ce3ed328.png'); padding-left: 12px; background-position: 0px 5px; font-size: 16px; color: #212629; background-size: 8px 20px; font-weight: 600; text-align: left;">5.注意事项</h3>
</section>
</section>
<section>
<p style="margin-bottom: 18px; margin-top: 0px;">在向博主介绍情况时，添加您希望博主在其内容中需要做到或需要避免的任何特定要求。</p>
