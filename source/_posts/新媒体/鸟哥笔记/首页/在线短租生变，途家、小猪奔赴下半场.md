<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="4"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="5"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;">2022年5月24日，爱彼迎的一封“退出信”拨动了国内在线<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E7%9F%AD%E7%A7%9F" target="_blank">短租</a>市场的神经。自疫情突袭以来，在线短租生意难做已成行业共识，巨头的撤退更是侧面坐实了这一看法。</span></p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="6"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="7"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;">同样，纵观国内各大头部品牌的发展历程，也能一窥在线短租市场的疲态。在经历了大肆烧钱营销、疫情重创后的途家和小猪，或断臂减亏，或艰难回血。比较乐观的是，途家、小猪等对市场的不利走向也做出了积极反应，提前转变策略提质升级。</span></p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="8"> </p>
<h2 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; border: 0px; color: #212629; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: left; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px;" data-track="9"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-weight: bold;">在线短租步履蹒跚</span></h2>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="10"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="11"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;">中国作为人口大国，市场需求基数庞大，爱彼迎忍痛割舍，排除自身水土不服的内因，最关键的还是国内市场难以攻略。</span></p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="12"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="13"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-weight: bold;">一方面，国内强者选手扎堆，在线短租挑战增多。</span>一边是稳占头部地位的木鸟、美团等实力平台，一边是抖音、小红书等跨界侵入，在平台众多、市场越来越饱和的现实情况下，各平台的用户流量增长空间被分割得越来越小。</span></p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="14"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="15"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;">除此之外，近一年来，帐篷、房车露营等出游活动的走红不断拉拢旅游短租人群，长期以往可能成长为短租市场强有力对手，给在线短租行业形成新的冲击。</span></p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="16"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="17"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-weight: bold;">另一方面，市场大盘“失温”，增速减缓。</span>前瞻产业研究院数据显示，2020年中国在线短租用户和在线<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E6%B0%91%E5%AE%BF" target="_blank">民宿</a>市场交易规模分别下滑至1.82亿人和125.8亿元。2021年交易规模回升至201亿元，不过依然没有达到2019年209亿元的水平。</span></p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="18"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="19"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;">整体来看，目前疫情零星反弹，各地旅游市场仍在恢复状态，最近几年与之大有相关的民宿行业更是处于冰火交替的局面，复苏情况跌宕起伏，在线短租市场的运作也是深受这些因素牵制而缓慢愈合。</span></p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="20"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="21"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;">疫情给民宿行业带来了许多变量，在线短租困境丛生，爱彼迎在全球范围的影响力相对较大，可以选择在不影响整体经营的前提下退出国内市场，及时止损。而以中国市场为主要创收来源的途家、小猪们自然不能如此爽快，只能通过调整策略，让自身保持住持续的造血能力，为后续的长足发展创造良机。</span></p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="22"> </p>
<h2 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; border: 0px; color: #212629; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: left; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px;" data-track="23"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-weight: bold;">途家：由重转轻</span></h2>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="24"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="25"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;">一直以来，重资产模式的运营成本都比较高，这也使得途家在疫情期间所承受的营收压力相比之下要远大于同行。正因如此，在2021年途家不得不砍掉超20个城市的自营房源，尝试以C2C轻资产模式增强抗风险能力，着重在技术、规则、营销渠道以及社区上深耕。</span></p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="26"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="27"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-weight: bold;">其一，提供专业的前端后台系统，支持接入民宿主在其他平台的经营数据，给足民宿主进入新平台的安全感，在一定程度上也为途家后续招揽新房源减少困难。</span></span></p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="28"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="29"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;">据相关报道，在爱彼迎宣布退出中国市场之后，国内其他同行纷纷争抢吸纳爱彼迎的房源。其中，途家迅速开通了“绿色审核通道”，并且推出“一键上线”、点评内容同步等多项服务接入爱彼迎在中国区的民宿主。截止目前，途家已接入爱彼迎平台上超九成的优质房源。</span></p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="30"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="31"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-weight: bold;">其二，在系统以及服务规则上形成体系，不断完善自己的《民宿分级标准》，通过建立评估体系、上线民宿钻级，帮助消费者和民宿主完成消费和经营的高效匹配，有效帮助民宿主提升房源运营效率。</span>比如被授予途家“超赞新房”称号的房源，可以获得多平台流量推广位，同时也会被优先纳入平台严选流量池，推荐到精准客群。</span></p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="32"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="33"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-weight: bold;">其三，依靠携程助力打通去哪儿、同程艺龙、蚂蚁短租等多个渠道，途家更容易触及到精准的目标旅游客群，提高转化率，实现降本提效，这对途家平台的运营来说无疑是锦上添花。</span>此外，在打造房东社区、在营造社区氛围上，途家也在致力向爱彼迎看齐。</span></p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="34"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="35"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;">照常来说，途家以重资产模式起家，由“重”转“轻”会需要一定时间进行过渡，但因其背靠携程这一大平台，可以轻易整合到平台内外资源，快速形成竞争优势，而且途家本身耕耘民宿领域多年，早已积累了厚实的品牌口碑和高粘性客户。所以对于模式切换，途家完全可以加速跨过磨合期，在轻资产运营方面以品牌为利器，高筑壁垒。</span></p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="38"> </p>
<h2 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; border: 0px; color: #212629; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: left; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px;" data-track="39"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-weight: bold;">小猪：由轻转重</span></h2>

<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="41"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;">对比之下，途家的改变是为了缓解成本的忧虑，而小猪则是急于扩张短租房源。</span></p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="42"> </p>

<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="44"> </p>

<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="46"> </p>

<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="48"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="49"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-weight: bold;">其次，加速布局乡村民宿。</span>乡村民宿是国家鼓励农业现代化的一环，政策利好明显，有望成为民宿行业的重要需求增量点。2021年，小猪民宿依托“互联网+乡村产业”打造乡村IP，重点联合飞猪在全国打造乡村民宿集群项目，进一步帮助民宿主解决在营销、获客和盈利等方面存在的痛点。</span></p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="50"> </p>

<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="52"> </p>

<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="60"> </p>
