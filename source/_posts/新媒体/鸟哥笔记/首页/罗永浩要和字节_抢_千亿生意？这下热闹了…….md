<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>7 月 10 日，在交个朋友直播间，<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E7%BD%97%E6%B0%B8%E6%B5%A9" target="_blank">罗永浩</a>官宣其新<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E5%88%9B%E4%B8%9A" target="_blank">创业</a>公司名为「Thin Red Line」</span><span>（翻译为细红线，未公布正式中文名）</span><span>，创业方向剑指 AR 领域。不仅如此，罗永浩本人在交个朋友直播间里欲广纳贤才，招聘产品经理和设计师。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/145248037062cd81221714b0.59662429.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>

<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在「Thin Red Line」的官网中配图为一条细红线，配有一句话：An Opportunity to Participate Directly in the Platform-level Revolution</span><span>（直接参与平台级革命的机会）</span><span>。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/107331650062cd812248cd82.69982439.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span>图源：Thin Red Line 官网</span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>关于 AR 的生意，各家大厂动作也一直不断：国内有腾讯、<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E5%AD%97%E8%8A%82" target="_blank">字节</a>等大厂收购 AR 公司；海外有Meta</span><span>（原 Facebook）</span><span>，Google 等大力进军元宇宙，也做 AR 相关业务。而罗永浩试图和大厂争抢生意，到底怎么回事呢？</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>接下来，运营社就聊聊，临近五十岁的罗老师又开始折腾什么新业务？又在抢谁的生意呢？</span></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span><em><span><strong>01、</strong></span></em></span><strong><span>“细红线”，罗永浩的新船票</span></strong></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>「Thin Red Line」官网相关联的企业为“北京细红线科技有限公司”，这家公司早在 2018 年就已成立，且股东信息上并未显示罗永浩。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>据天眼查显示，北京细红线科技有限公司注册资本 10 万元，法定代表人为徐寒</span><span>（曾任老罗英语培训学校副总、锤子科技有限公司董事）</span><span>，股东信息显示，该公司由徐寒、黄贺</span><span>（北京交个朋友数码科技有限公司创始人）</span><span>联合控股，持股比例分别为99%、1%。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/188514887662cd81228d6244.85489306.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span>图源：天眼查</span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>但从这家公司的名字来看，其和罗老师过去的创业经历有着千丝万缕的联系。</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>有些用户可能初看“细红线”这一名字有点不明白，但<span>罗永浩</span>实际上<span>曾在 2017 年的锤子手机发布会上提到</span>“细红线”这个名称的由来：</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<blockquote>
<section><span>在观看电影《细细的红线》时，想到电影中的战役为以少胜多的防卫战，与锤子科技现在的处境很像，于是取了这个名字。</span></section>
</blockquote>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/91825757962cd8122bcd436.25457633.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>“细红线”也是是个英国的俚语，thin red line，意思很简单 —— “背水一战”，在 17 年的发布会上，罗老师也展示了这篇英军拼死抵抗俄军冲锋的油画作品。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/116591370162cd81231a6584.23295393.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>阻挡在排山倒海般冲杀而来的俄军骑兵与英军基地之间的，只有“一道细细的红线”</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>而老罗究竟对这个“细红线战役”有多中意呢？</span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>锤子就推出过坚果Pro 细红线特别版，之后的坚果Pro2 也有细红线版，细红线指的是手机中框的切割线条。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/18984274662cd8123926944.93166204.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>除此之外，罗永浩曾发微博支持，由 Smartisan Studio 设计，归属于锤子科技前总裁</span><span>@彭锦洲</span><span> 的创业项目小野电子烟，旗下有一款产品也叫细红线。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/192264405462cd8123c5cd59.27904544.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>而此番创业，罗永浩再次搬出了这个“细红线”的名称，可见其在老罗心中的份量。</span></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span><em><span><strong>02、</strong></span></em></span><strong><span>罗永浩，究竟要抢谁的生意?</span></strong></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在 6 月 13 日，罗永浩发布微博表示，“真还传“接近尾声，自己要再创业，是一家 AR 科技公司。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/163129581162cd8124059008.06009502.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>给不了解 AR 的用户科普一下：AR</span><span> ( Augment Reality 增强现实）</span><span>，一般可分为硬件、内容、平台 3 大领域。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<blockquote>
<section><span>硬件：AR 眼镜、AR 平板电脑等...；<br></span></section>
<section><span>内容：AR 游戏、应用 AR 的各类 App...；</span></section>
<section><span>平台：类似于 iOS、安卓的 AR 操作系统、计算平台。</span></section>
</blockquote>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/75437506262cd8124496189.89918313.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span>来源：公开资料整理、虎嗅智库</span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>不少用户可能会发出疑问，那罗老师究竟要做那个领域和方向的 AR 呢？</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在</span><span>@晚点LatePost </span><span>的文章中，罗老师明确表示，AR 就是下一代计算平台：</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<blockquote>
<section><span>我们觉得 VR 的本质更像是游戏主机，它可能是史上最畅销的游戏机。我们相信 AR 就是下一代计算平台，这在科技界很大程度上也是共识了，并不是我们的创见。</span></section>
</blockquote>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>也就是说，罗老师可能想打造出类似“ iPhone+iOS ”一样的东西，即既有 AR 的硬件也有配套的软件平台，想要成为下一个时代的“开创者”。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>具体是什么产品形态呢？罗永浩在</span><span>@晚点LatePost</span><span> 采访中表示，商业机密不能透露。<strong>而运营社猜测，罗老师想做的「平台性产品」很可能是类似 AR 眼镜的产品，不仅有硬件，还有一套完备的软件平台为用户提供相对应的服务。</strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>外界有一种说法，AR 眼镜或许可以成为突破智能手机交互形式的一种产品。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>我们不妨想想，某一天你带着 AR 眼镜逛超市，所有映入眼帘的食品都自动弹出热量、生产日期、做饭等信息，而不需要你再打开手机 App 去查询。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>而类似 AR 眼镜的产品形态和罗老师目前透露的出的创业信息算是不谋而合。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>那目前在 AR 这条赛道上，罗永浩到底有多少对手呢？</span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在国内，不论是字节、腾讯、阿里等投资频繁的互联网大厂，还有华为、小米、OPPO 等手机厂商都开始进攻 AR领域：</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<blockquote>
<section><span>字节在 2022 年 1 月，披露投资“李未可”团队，该团队主要布局虚拟数字人和 AR 眼镜领域；</span></section>
<section><span>腾讯在 2022 年 3 月，披露投资 AR 眼镜厂商蜂巢科技；</span></section>
<section><span>阿里在 2022 年 4 月，宣布投资 AR 眼镜制造商 Nreal；</span></section>
<section><span>小米在 2021 年 9 月，发布智能眼镜概念视频；</span></section>
<section><span>华为在 2021 年 12 月，发布第三代华为智能眼镜，可以用来听歌、接听电话等；</span></section>
<section><span>OPPO 在 2022 年 3 月，开售智能眼镜 OPPO Air Glass，支持天气提醒、实时翻译等功能……</span></section>
</blockquote>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>从目前公开媒体报道和信息看，大厂们都试图争抢下一个科技新风口，进军 AR 提前占领有利地位，而罗永浩要面对的，显示是资金雄厚、人才储备更强的大厂。想要在激烈的市场竞争环境中闯出一番天地，初创企业着实不容易。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>而像 Google、微软、苹果、Meta</span><span>（原名Facebook）</span><span>、Snap 等海外科技巨头在 AR 硬件、软件、系统等方面，也早早布局。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>但心态积极的罗老师，在谈及目前大厂在 AR 业务上的发展境况时说道，可能目前他现在的敌人只有苹果，因为：</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<blockquote>
<section><span>“大的科技公司基本上没有 all-in AR 的，唯一号称重兵做 AR 的只有苹果。”</span></section>
</blockquote>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>从硬件上，虽然苹果一直没有明确表示自己要出 AR 眼镜，不过长期跟踪苹果的彭博社记者</span><span> @Mark Gurman </span><span>和行业分析师 </span><span>@郭明池</span><span> 都说苹果将在明年发布 AR/MR</span><span>（Mixed Reality 混合现实技术）</span><span>眼镜。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>从开发生态上，苹果自 2017 年以来一直在推进的 AR 应用开发工具包 —— ARKit，WWDC 2022 期间苹果首次向开发者展示了用于在 iOS 设备上构建 AR 应用程序的改进版本 Apple ARKit 6 工具包。</span></p>

<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span> </span><strong><span>不过，即便对手是大厂苹果，罗老师仍非常自信：</span></strong><span> </span></p>
<blockquote>
<section><span> </span><span>“</span><span>新世界的主宰者几乎从来都不是旧世界的霸主。”</span></section>
</blockquote>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span><em><span><strong>03、</strong></span></em></span><strong><span>AR领域，“行业冥灯”还奏效么？</span></strong></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>互联网上关于罗永浩创业冥灯的热梗一直存在，他本人也回应过：“我完全不同意这种说法，它更多只是一个时间上的巧合。”</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>再度创业，罗永浩盯上的这块 AR 蛋糕，前景到底怎么样？“行业冥灯”还会奏效吗？</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>@刘润 </span><span>曾分享过一个观点，</span><span><strong>当一个新技术、新产品出现后，一个公司能否存活的关键在是否可以跨越“死亡之井”。</strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<blockquote>
<section><span></span><span>如果将“技术采用生命周期”和“技术成熟度曲线”相结合后，不难发现，正是因为创新者等人的疯狂追捧，概念被迅速炒热，将其推到了期望的膨胀期，甚至是泡沫的幻灭期 —— 也就是会“死”掉大多数公司的时期，死亡之井。</span></section>
</blockquote>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/109581758362cd8125432dc4.24770055.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>

<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>@刘润</span><span> 分享到，想要跨越“死亡之井”，科技公司们要将新的概念，从意见领袖喜欢的“有趣的科技”，变成普罗大众喜欢的“有用的产品”。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>再简单一点儿来说，罗永浩所在 AR 领域能否成功的关键，是能不能创造出一个软件或者产品，让大众有购买设备的理由。</span></strong></p>

<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>据任天堂发布 2021 - 2022 财年年度财报显示，Switch 主机销量达到 1.7 亿台,成为全球第七款销量破亿的主机。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>同时，根据任天堂 2021 年 4 月至 2022 年 3 月的业绩报告显示，《塞尔达传说：旷野之息》游戏推出至今已经五年多了，作为许多人心目中非常棒的开放世界游戏，依旧在 22 年度卖出去了 428 万套，累计总销量为 2655 万套。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>甚至不少网友打趣：“每卖出一份旷野之息，就多卖出一台 Switch。”</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/139472239062cd8126aafe66.01044074.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>同理，这套逻辑也可以套用在 AR 眼镜产品上 —— 罗老师能否创造出一个可以护航 AR 设备的软件产品?</span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>而通过前面的支付宝和任天堂的例子，不难发现，作为“护航产品”，社交和游戏是两个非常合适的方向。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<blockquote>
<section><span>社交软件：</span><span>流量导向，通过社交关系链圈住海量的用户；</span></section>
<section><span>游戏软件：</span><span>兴趣导向，通过游戏吸引用户购买产品。</span></section>
</blockquote>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>但别忘了，社交产品也是罗老师的主场：</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<blockquote>
<section><span>聊天宝是罗永浩在子弹短信上所延伸出来的一个社交 App，子弹短信连续 13 天登顶 Appstore 免费榜，并经历了多次融资。</span></section>
</blockquote>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>同时，营销能力也是罗老师的优势所在，</span></strong><span>从以往的锤子科技的几次爆火的发布会，到交个朋友直播间直奔抖音第一，都能看出罗老师对于营销内容的把控上非常有经验。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>不过，运营社认为，在硬件规模上，AR 设备目前还不能成气候。</span></strong></span></p>
<section>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>根据</span><span>@VR陀螺 研究院 </span><span>发布的《2021年全球VR/AR行业年度发展报告》显示，2021 年全球 VR 头显出货量为 1110 万台，较 2020 年增长了 66%；2021 年全球 AR 出货量 57 万台，较 2020 年增长 44%，</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>虽然增长幅度看起来很美好，但是，这对于罗老师想做的「平台型产品」来说，明显规模还远达不到预期。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在 iPhone 出现前，智能手机就已经有所规模，在 2006 年全球出货量达到 8100 万台，全球手机出货量达 9.9 亿部。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/33927279662cd8126dc90e8.44524092.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>正如 2020 年初，Facebook</span><span>（现 Mate）</span><span>首席 VR 科学家 </span><span>@MichaelAbrash </span><span>曾表示，AR/VR 对现在的市场来说，太早了。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<section><span>真正的 AR 眼镜进入市场还需要 5 到 10 年时间。</span></section>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>所以如果罗老师所创造的产品想取代手机，成为新的传播设备，必定是一个漫长又复杂的战争。</span></strong></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span><em><span><strong><span>04、</span></strong></span></em></span><strong><span>结语</span></strong></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>不少 AR 行业的从业者表示：</span><span><strong>AR 行业最大的敌人不是竞争者，而是时间。</strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>AR 行业只需要等技术在时间的催化下成熟，会极有可能成为代替手机行业的科技终端产品。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在时代的浪潮翻滚下，任何公司都有可能被卷走，所以，不论是还是苹果、Mate，还是即将进入的罗老师，都需要不停积累，等待迎接时代的冲击。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>不过，不管结果如何，五十岁还在奋斗一线创业的罗老师，都值得被尊敬。</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>正如某些网友所讲：“罗永浩这三个字都已经不是一个名字，而是可以称之为一种精神。”</span></p>
