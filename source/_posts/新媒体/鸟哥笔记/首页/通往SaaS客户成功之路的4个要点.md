<section style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; background-color: #ffffff; color: #222222; font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 1.75em; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box !important; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px; visibility: visible; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"></span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">早在2001年，自Salesforce公司创立客户成功团队以来，客户成功就已经开始被国内的SaaS厂商所关注。</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">到2015年，市场上有很多SaaS厂商也开始或者已经搭建了自己的客户成功团队。</span></section>
<section style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; background-color: #ffffff; color: #222222; font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 1.75em; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">但是中美SaaS服务商对客户成功的理解相信还是有差异的。国内SaaS起步比较晚，SaaS理念和产品相对还在完善的过程中，</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #103a9a; text-align: justify; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">在不能依靠PLG（产品驱动增长）的策略下，</strong></span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">往往需要通过客户成功团队来为客户提供产品使用、实施交付等持续服务，以期实现客户的增购和续约。</span></section>
<section style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; background-color: #ffffff; color: #222222; font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 1.75em; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">在客户成功愈发受SaaS厂商重视的现状下，</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #103a9a; text-align: justify; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">企业不再讨论是否要做客户成功，而是考虑如何建立成体系的客户成功打法。</strong></span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">虽然我们都知道客户成功的概念，但是到现在为止，我们国内SaaS厂商能把客户成功服务做好的基本上非常少。</span></section>
<section style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; background-color: #ffffff; color: #222222; font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 1.75em; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #103a9a; text-align: justify; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">ToC业务通过用户运营来传递服务质量，ToB业务通过客户成功来持续服务。</strong></span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;"></span><img class="rich_pages wxw-img js_insertlocalimg" style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px 5px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; max-width: 100%; vertical-align: middle; cursor: pointer;" src="https://img.pmtalk.club/sz_mmbiz_png/z2Z9HicDWWsZW4KPRLYgf8Gy8HCBELEkichqvoWJyYZO1EFOqJOkm28fMWkXxdv2b51KS1Rib7pJ242XFnajQdzIQ/640?wx_fmt=png" data-ratio="0.4375" data-s="300,640" data-type="png" data-w="800" title="通往SaaS客户成功之路的4个要点" alt="通往SaaS客户成功之路的4个要点" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></section>
<section style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; background-color: #ffffff; color: #222222; font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 1.75em; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #103a9a; text-align: justify; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">那么，通往SaaS客户成功之路上要扫清哪些障碍呢？</strong></span></section>
<section style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; background-color: #ffffff; color: #222222; font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 1.75em; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">我们一点点讲。</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;"></span></section>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; color: #222222; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 1.75em; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0.8em 8px 5px; padding: 0px; color: #222222; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; background-color: #ffffff; letter-spacing: 1px; font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; max-width: 100%; font-size: 36px; color: #ee5408;"><em style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong style="margin: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"> </strong></strong></em></span></span><span style="box-sizing: border-box !important; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 36px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"> <span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-size: 36px; color: #f4f2f2;"><em style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">—</em></span></span></p>
<h3 style="margin: 0.8em 8px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-weight: normal; line-height: 25.6px; font-size: 20px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; background-color: #ffffff; text-align: center; text-size-adjust: auto; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #07379b; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 1px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">为什么需要客户成功</span></strong></span></h3>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; letter-spacing: 0.5px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; color: #222222; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 1.75em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;"></span><strong style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">（1）SaaS为什么需要客户成功</span></strong><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;"></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; color: #222222; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 1.75em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">在软件产品的交付和服务过程中，传统软件公司更多是由实施人员来负责产品交付，为什么没有客户成功？</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; color: #222222; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 1.75em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">这是因为传统软件公司在收取客户的一次性采购费用之后，后续的服务更偏向于实施交付和售后答疑，说的更直接就是：</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #103a9a; text-align: justify; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">客户成不成功已经和你没有太大的关系，因为客户的付费价值基本上已经挖掘完了。</strong></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; color: #222222; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 1.75em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">但是因为SaaS厂商采取的是订阅式收费，必须得依赖客户来年的续费收入才能保持正向盈利，如果客户在采购SaaS系统之后，SaaS厂商不能够很好的为客户做好系统交付和持续服务直至客户成功，来年的续费是没保障的，当续费没保障，SaaS厂商必然会依靠大量的新客签约才能维持正向盈利。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; color: #222222; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 1.75em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">这有点像单次博弈和多次博弈，</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #103a9a; text-align: justify; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">SaaS厂商和客户的关系就是多次博弈。</strong></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; color: #222222; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 1.75em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">在客户成功还未出现之前，ToB公司更多通过运营、市场销售或实施团队来完成客户成功服务，有的会称之为商户运营（或商家运营）。我们有一部分厂商，尤其是ToC转型ToB业务的公司，甚至会让用户运营的岗位来承担客户成功的职责。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; color: #222222; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 1.75em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #103a9a; text-align: justify; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">同样是围绕“用户”做服务，C端面向个人用户，B端面向企业客户。</strong></span></p>

<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px 0px 0em; padding: 0px; color: #333333; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: center;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; color: #222222; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 1.75em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">C端场景下的用户运营服务于用户拉新、活跃、留存、转化、裂变等目的，B端更多集中在获客、转化、服务的环节，其中服务环节是CSM（客户成功经理）们的重点工作。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; color: #222222; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 1.75em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">（2）客户成功的目的是什么</span></strong><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;"></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; color: #222222; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 1.75em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">客户成功的本质是服务，SaaS厂商做客户成功的目的，简单来讲就是：</span></p>

<p style="margin: 0px 8px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; letter-spacing: 0.5px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; color: #222222; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 1.75em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box !important; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px; color: #ff6827; visibility: visible; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"></span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">我们可能会把客户成功和客户服务混淆，虽然两者在一定程度上有交叉的部分，但实际上他们并不是一回事。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; color: #222222; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 1.75em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">客户成功和客户服务存在着如下差异：</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; color: #222222; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 1.75em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">（1）关注的指标不同</span></strong></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; color: #222222; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 1.75em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">客户服务关注的更多是客户服务质量，如响应速度、处理时长、解决率、满意度等；客户成功关注的是续约率、续费率、活跃度、ARR（年度经常性收入）、NDR（收入留存率）、LTV（客户终身价值）等。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; color: #222222; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 1.75em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">（2）服务出发点不同</span></strong></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; color: #222222; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 1.75em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #103a9a; text-align: justify; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">客户服务是被动触发的，</strong></span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">一般是在客户有问题的时候才会介入；</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #103a9a; text-align: justify; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">客户成功是主动的，</strong></span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">关注持续为客户服务，直至客户成功上线并持续良好的使用产品。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; color: #222222; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 1.75em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">（3）接触的时段不同</span></strong></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; color: #222222; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 1.75em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">当客户与我们的产品开始接触的时候，就已经有客户服务的动作开始介入。而客户成功更多是在客户完成商务环节之后才开始。</span></p>

<h3 style="margin: 0.8em 8px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-weight: normal; line-height: 25.6px; color: #000000; font-size: 20px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: center; text-size-adjust: auto; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #07379b; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 1px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">拿什么让客户成功</span></strong></span></h3>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; line-height: 1.75em; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; line-height: 1.75em; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">（1）功能足够完善的产品是保障</span></strong><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;"></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; line-height: 1.75em; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">面向市场的SaaS产品，应该要有足够的差异化竞争力，尤其是在产品的功能上一定要能满足客户使用需求，如果说B端客户对SaaS产品易用性还能相对容忍，但是在产品功能上至少是不能打马虎眼的。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; line-height: 1.75em; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #103a9a; text-align: justify; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">所以功能足够完善的产品是保障，至少客户有个称手的武器可以使用。</strong></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; line-height: 1.75em; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">（2）相对精准的签约客户是前提</span></strong><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;"></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; line-height: 1.75em; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">经常会有这样的现象，销售人员为了完成公司KPI考核，在面向客户签约的时候，经常会允诺客户一些实际产品还不具备的功能，甚至有</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #103a9a; text-align: justify; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">“概念造航母，量产螺丝钉”之嫌。</strong></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; line-height: 1.75em; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">当这类客户已经完成商务环节并且交付给客户成功团队之后，压力就转移到客户成功团队身上。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; line-height: 1.75em; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">如果客户所需的产品功能正好是在待开发的产品规划中，客户还能相对容忍等待；如果客户需求和SaaS产品功能的匹配度低于30%，那么这个客户不管客户成功团队如何去努力，甚至是代运营，都极难做好客户的上线使用。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; line-height: 1.75em; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">就好比你需要M8型号（直径12.5mm）的螺丝钉，但是只能给你提供M10型号（直径15mm），不管你怎么努力，这个钻孔就是很难吻合，结果无非两个选择，换一个M8型号的螺丝钉；或者拓宽孔洞到15mm，</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #103a9a; text-align: justify; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">但是客户的初衷并不是为此而买单，</strong></span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">结果显而易见。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; line-height: 1.75em; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">（3）良好的数据化运营是续航</span></strong><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;"></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; line-height: 1.75em; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">作为客户成功团队，应该要有良好的数据化运营意识，</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #103a9a; text-align: justify; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">即可以通过对客户活跃度及健康度等方面的数据观察分析，来为关键节点上的客户服务动作做指导，提升客户使用粘性。</strong></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; line-height: 1.75em; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">常见的有：</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; line-height: 1.75em; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">a.活跃度</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; line-height: 1.75em; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">账号激活率、登录比例、在线时长、DAU/WAU/MAU等</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; line-height: 1.75em; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">b.健康度</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; line-height: 1.75em; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">客户是否使用核心产品功能、客户使用产品功能的广度和深度等</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; line-height: 1.75em; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #103a9a; text-align: justify; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">现实是很多SaaS厂商忙于自身产品的功能开发和规划迭代，还不能够为客户成功团队提供强有力的系统支撑。</strong></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; line-height: 1.75em; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">除了自建运营后台管理系统之外，常用的BI大数据分析平台也可以提供完整的成熟解决方案。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; line-height: 1.75em; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">（4）创新的陪跑式培训是加分项</span></strong><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;"></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; line-height: 1.75em; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">ToB行业的SaaS产品，面向的客户群体虽然以B端客户为主，但是B端客户的服务群体差异性还是蛮大的。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; line-height: 1.75em; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">以现在比较流行的私域商城为例，SaaS产品是卖给B端企业的，但是B端企业要使用的好，还必须得在商城中做好私域运营，这个时候作为SaaS厂商可能往往还需要提供更多的私域运营打法和案例，甚至是行业培训或者教练式服务，来辅助客户借助SaaS产品获得成功。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; line-height: 1.75em; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">同样，我们在信息化基础比较薄弱的B端客户群体中，创新式的陪跑型培训也是我们客户成功团队服务客户的一个加分项。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; line-height: 1.75em; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">后面我们还会针对这一板块进行深入探讨。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; line-height: 1.75em; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;"> </span></p>
<p style="margin: 5px 8px 0em; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box !important; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-size: 24px; color: #07379b; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box !important; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #07379b; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-size: 24px; letter-spacing: 0.5px; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">最后的话</span></strong></span></p>
<p style="margin: 5px 8px 0em; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box !important; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-size: 24px; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box !important; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-size: 24px; letter-spacing: 0.5px; color: #d92142; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; color: #000000; font-size: 16px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box !important; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-size: 24px; color: #ff0000; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"> </span></strong></span></strong></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; line-height: 1.75em; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;"></span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">SaaS客户成功之路必然是需要长周期的服务才能显现效果，</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #103a9a; text-align: justify; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">客户成功的本质是服务，</strong></span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">作为SaaS陪跑人要明白客户成功的目的和初衷，区分清楚客户成功和客户服务的差异点。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 1em; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; line-height: 1.75em; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #103a9a; text-align: justify; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">客户成功不是简单的改个部门名字就可以，</strong></span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px;">作为SaaS厂商，产研团队要做实产品给予保障，销售团队要针对精准客户画像落地签约，加之以客户成功团队良好的数据化运营和SOP流程管理，相信在SaaS客户成功之路上，你会少走很多弯路。</span></p>
