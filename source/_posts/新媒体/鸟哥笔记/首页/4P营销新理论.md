                        <p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; margin: 0px !important 0px 20px 0px;" data-track="1"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">搞<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E8%90%A5%E9%94%80" target="_blank">营销</a>的都知道营销4P是什么？即产品 ( Product )、价格( Price )、促销( Promotion)、渠道（Place )。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify;" data-track="2"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">要说营销4P真正的价值其实是在乙方忽悠客户时，而真正懂价格的定价机制，懂促销核心原理的营销大师，其实并不多，</span><strong style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">没有十年以上对人性的揣摩和理解，是无法get到4P的真正奥义的</span></strong><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify;" data-track="3"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">现在搞营销的动不动讲4P，用我的话来讲，其实是</span><strong style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">骗甲方、嫖方案、编故事、方案飘着落不了地的新4P</span></strong><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">。接下来我就用我的所见所闻，来为大家揭开营销圈里这广为人知的4P营销新理论。</span></span></p>
<h2 style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #212629; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: left; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px;" data-track="6"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><strong style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #ff0000; --tt-darkmode-color: #FF0C00;">第1P：骗甲方</span></strong></span></h2>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify;" data-track="7"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">准确的说法其实是忽悠，还到不了骗的地步。因为做营销案子的人，即便99%的客户都没做起来，也总会有一个因为甲方本身的努力，硬是把一个毫无价值的营销方案给做起来的，而</span><strong style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">这种钉子汤式的功劳，自然会被乙方大书特书</span></strong><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify;" data-track="8"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">试想一下，如果成功有方法论，那学会这方法就好了，为什么还有那么多企业做不起来，不是因为笨，而是想做起来一个企业天时地利人和三者缺一不可。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify;" data-track="9"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">就好比你现在去做一个新的网红品牌，用过去完美日记和花西子的那套打法，或者极端来讲，还是完美日记和花西子的那拨人，再造一个同样品牌，可能吗？当然不可能了，时过境迁，已经没有那样的低价渠道了。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify;" data-track="10"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">营销机构则不然，不会把外部环境考虑进去，而是一套营销理论吃一生，社会是进步的，市场是动态的，此时此地此景所需要的营销案子，一定与过往的有所不同，不是想几句口号，设计几个海报就万事大吉了。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify;" data-track="11"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">大家想想，今年新出的让你印象深刻的广告语有吗？消费者都记不住，</span><strong style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">为什么甲方还要被乙方忽悠定广告语呢？因为乙方只会这个</span></strong><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">。</span></span></p>
<h2 style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #212629; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: left; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px;" data-track="14"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><strong style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #ff0000; --tt-darkmode-color: #FF0C00;">第2P：嫖方案</span></strong></span></h2>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify;" data-track="15"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">我最烦的就是那种嫖方案的人，哪像我这样洁身自好的人儿，我做的方案从来不嫖，我只借鉴和致敬。就像前几天被炒得沸沸扬扬的小满，还有短视频的模仿，有的情节是一模一样的，甚至火过原版。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify;" data-track="16"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">这还不是最气人的，最可气的是我们做的方案没落地就被别人嫖过去了，那才是心里有苦说不出。据说小扎的脸书还是抄袭了朋友的方案，反正电影里是这样讲的，真相如何，我也不晓得。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify;" data-track="17"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">还是那句老话，天下方案一大抄。我们在做新项目时，一般都会做竞品分析，看竞品做的方案，然后从中吸取灵感，所以嫖方案是很正常的行业现象了。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify;" data-track="18"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">问题在于，你参考的百分比有多少？如果是一两个镜头，别人说你是致敬，如果是完全照搬，除非你是漫威，别人会说你这叫复刻，不然绝对冠以抄袭的骂名。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify;" data-track="19"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">有时就连乙方出方案时，也会借鉴自己过往做得好的案子，比如电梯广告，洗脑广告可真多哇。为什么每个企业都做洗脑广告？是因为他们都不约而同地有了同样的觉悟吗？当然不是了，我推测肯定是乙方参考了过去成功的案例，发现众多的广告形式中也就洗脑广告能跳出来。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify;" data-track="20"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">所以，当来了新的客户时，自然会推荐洗脑广告案子了。有一次，我在电梯口数了数广告，3个广告，有2个是一句话重复讲八百遍以上的。</span><strong style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">我很喜欢吃红烧肉，但是吃多了，我也会觉得腻，用户对洗脑广告的认知也是如此，劝乙方早点收手吧</span></strong><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">。</span></span></p>
<h2 style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #212629; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: left; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px;" data-track="23"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><strong style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #ff0000; --tt-darkmode-color: #FF0C00;">第3P：编故事</span></strong></span></h2>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify;" data-track="24"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">为什么B字开头的编故事也会收集到P里面，因为历史上知名的BP营销事件，某营销大师帮助荷兰一个初创的五人小团队，通过一个惊为天人的营销案子，三年之间登陆纳斯达克。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify;" data-track="25"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">为什么他们团队能有这样的运气，是因为在做产品推广时，团队误将广告语中的pa写成了ba结果引发全球传播，用户量暴涨，三年录得10亿用户，因祸得福，上市成功，这就是BP的故事。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify;" data-track="26"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">没错，这个故事是我编的，不像其他做营销的，明明是编的故事，非要一本正经地说我们是有据可查的。编故事是每个搞营销的基本功，</span><strong style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">相比枯燥乏味的产品介绍，一个好的故事确实很能打动消费者</span></strong><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify;" data-track="27"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">我曾经把故事比喻成可以传递的介质，有了故事，用户与用户之间就可以传递该品牌，形成品牌的自传播。而大多品牌本身并没有故事可言，创始人为什么创办这个企业，大概率都是因为一个原因：为了赚钱嘛。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify;" data-track="28"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">那些说是为了让自己老婆一样的人不再受分娩之痛，不再像过去的自己一样受皮肤暗沉影响等等，都是噱头来着，莫当真。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify;" data-track="29"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">还有的编故事是引经据典，说创始人是某文化的第几代传承人，这个就有点真实度的感觉了，如果没有依据，企业万万不敢这样讲的，但是让我信，还缺点证据。</span></span></p>

<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify;" data-track="34"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">做营销的最常说的一句话是：我的方案没问题，就是执行不到位，所以效果不行。这话有道理，但也未必全对。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify;" data-track="35"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">并不是每一个企业都有一个牛掰的马云助理，能把马云吹过的牛都给落地了，就单单一个线下抽奖活动，你让十个实体店去做，会有十种结果，做的好的难道是方案选的好吗？那做得不好，又怎样解释呢？</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify;" data-track="36"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">营销人做的案子要切实考虑到企业的自身实力，团队能力，有的企业商务团队强，搞营销未必擅长，有的企业<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E8%BF%90%E8%90%A5" target="_blank">运营</a>能力强，落地效率高，案子就可以做的天马行空一些。</span><strong style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">没有什么好不好的案子，只有落地执行到不到位</span></strong><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">。</span></span></p>
