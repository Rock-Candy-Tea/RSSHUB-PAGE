<p class="article-meta" style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; font-size: 16px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, 'Segoe UI', Rototo, Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="background-color: #f8f8f8; color: #8a8989; letter-spacing: 0.5px; font-weight: inherit; text-align: inherit; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif;"><a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E8%85%BE%E8%AE%AF%E5%BF%AB%E6%8A%A5" target="_blank">腾讯快报</a>停运，源于<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E4%BB%8A%E6%97%A5%E5%A4%B4%E6%9D%A1" target="_blank">今日头条</a>失去了“头条”？</span></p>
<p class="article-meta" style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; font-size: 16px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, 'Segoe UI', Rototo, Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="color: #333333; letter-spacing: 0.5px; font-weight: inherit; text-align: inherit; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif;">互联网大厂降本增效，一些失去风口的业务逐渐成为弃子，最近的一个是腾讯“看点快报”。</span></p>
<p class="article-meta" style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; font-size: 16px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, 'Segoe UI', Rototo, Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">7月18日起，</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #00385b; --tt-darkmode-color: #66B5E6;">腾讯旗下“看点快报”APP宣布停止运营，</span></span><span style="letter-spacing: 0.5px; font-weight: inherit; text-align: inherit; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif;">从各大应用商店下架。作为“头腾大战”的先锋，“天天快报”（看点快报的前身）上线于2015年6月，是当时腾讯为狙击今日头条而推出的个性化推荐新闻资讯APP。</span></p>
<p class="article-meta" style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; font-size: 16px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, 'Segoe UI', Rototo, Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">彼时，今日头条在国内资讯领域异军突起，掀起了根据用户兴趣推荐个性化内容的潮流，让门户网站沦为了传统媒体，也让腾讯倍感压力，快报就是在这个大背景下应运而生。</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #00385b; --tt-darkmode-color: #66B5E6;">2016初，天天快报日活量就达到了8700万，</span></span><span style="letter-spacing: 0.5px; font-weight: inherit; text-align: inherit; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif;">当时仅次于腾讯新闻、今日头条。</span></p>
<p class="article-meta" style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; font-size: 16px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, 'Segoe UI', Rototo, Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">只不过，这个时代变化太快，随着短视频时代的到来，当初为抢市场打得头破血流的“今日头条”们，如今都疲态尽显，就连</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #00385b; --tt-darkmode-color: #66B5E6;">今日头条自身也深陷流量瓶颈多年。</span></span></p>
<p class="article-meta" style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; font-size: 16px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, 'Segoe UI', Rototo, Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="color: #333333; letter-spacing: 0.5px; font-weight: inherit; text-align: inherit; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif;">去年11月，在字节新一轮架构调整中，作为曾经的骄子，今日头条被后起来的抖音并入。</span></p>
<p class="article-meta" style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; font-size: 16px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, 'Segoe UI', Rototo, Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="color: #333333; letter-spacing: 0.5px; font-weight: inherit; text-align: inherit; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif;">看点快报的下架，是图文资讯APP走向没落的标志性事件。作为图文资讯的领头羊，今日头条如何找到下一条增长曲线？</span></p>

<h2 style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; border: 0px; text-align: left; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;" data-track="24"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">曾经的字节骄子</span></span></span></h2>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="27"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">曾经，</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #00385b; --tt-darkmode-color: #66B5E6;">今日头条就是字节跳动，字节跳动就是今日头条。</span></span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="29"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">在2018年8月之前，字节全球总部大楼外立面悬挂的唯一品牌LOGO就是今日头条，这是长期处于C位的今日头条地位的象征。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="31"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">在很长一段时间里，外界对字节的感知一直停留在“今日头条”层面。由于用户认可度高，</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #00385b; --tt-darkmode-color: #66B5E6;">就连很多字节员工都以“头条人”自称，</span></span>字节APP矩阵，今日头条一直排首位。</span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="33"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">今日头条的地位来自曾经的辉煌战绩。2012年10月，今日头条上线，当时国内传统新闻资讯领域已是一片红海，但今日头条凭借兴趣算法向用户推荐高热度内容，硬是在已经固化的资讯市场敲开了一个口子。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="35"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">短短3个月内，今日头条注册用户数就突破了1000万人，</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #00385b; --tt-darkmode-color: #66B5E6;">五年之后，其日活数据达到了约1.2亿，</span></span>几乎是当时中国资讯市场日活总数的一半。今日头条成功接力内涵段子，为字节找到了新的增长曲线。</span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="37"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">现在回头来看，今日头条的成功，</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #00385b; --tt-darkmode-color: #66B5E6;">离不开对同时代新闻资讯产品的降维打击。</span></span>彼时，虽然也有不少移动资讯APP，但没有摆脱PC时代的套路：用户被动接受平台安排好的内容，每天更新的资讯量有限，已经无法满足人们对新鲜资讯、个性化内容的需求。</span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="39"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">而今日头条基于推荐引擎</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #00385b; --tt-darkmode-color: #66B5E6;">实现了“千人千面”的个性化展示，</span></span>并保证了用户能“随刷随有”获取信息。</span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="41"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">字节创始人张一鸣在《沸腾新十年》一书中透露，“随刷随有”是战略级的，是压倒一切的，为了保证用户的使用更流畅度，今日头条甚至设计了在有信号的地方缓存信息，以便在没有信号的地方继续“随刷随有”的机制。</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #00385b; --tt-darkmode-color: #66B5E6;">这为今日头条打开了市场，也让今日头条一度成为“行业公敌”。</span></span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="43"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">2014年，面对来势汹汹的今日头条，传统媒体以版权为武器，开始了反击。当年6月，搜狐对今日头条发起侵权诉讼，索赔1100万元；同一个月，《广州日报》起诉今日头条侵权；《新京报》等众多媒体纷纷要求今日头条停止侵权并赔偿损失。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="45"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">这应该是今日头条，</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #00385b; --tt-darkmode-color: #66B5E6;">或者说字节跳动成立以来遇到的最大一次危机。</span></span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="47"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">张一鸣后来在接受媒体采访时表示：当时不太景气的传统媒体和门户网站都把今日头条作为对手，当时头条估值达到5亿美元，自然会刺激到一些人。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="49"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">今日头条成为那条搅动了传统媒体和门户网站利益格局的鲇鱼，但最终趋势的力量让双方达成了妥协与和解，</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #00385b; --tt-darkmode-color: #66B5E6;">共同参与到由今日头条发起的新闻资讯变革中来。</span></span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="150"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">市场打开后，今日头条顺理成章成了字节最能“下金蛋的那只鸡”。</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #00385b; --tt-darkmode-color: #66B5E6;"><br style="box-sizing: border-box;"></span></span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">今日头条从2013年开始探索信息流广告商业变现。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="54"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">当时还处于移动互联网初期，不少企业对在手机上打广告仍心存疑虑，认为手机屏幕太小，不适合投放广告。但今日头条不久便打通了信息流广告通路，跻身国内最赚钱的互联网大厂之一，成为众多后来者模仿的对象。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="56"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">这个意义上说，和百度推动了竞价排名广告一样，今日头条后来的江湖地位，在一定程度上也得益于它所做的开创性尝试。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="58"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">在那段春风得意的日子里，今日头条对外攻城略地，对内则扮演了孵化器的角色。懂车帝、西瓜视频、悟空问答等APP，</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #00385b; --tt-darkmode-color: #66B5E6;">就是从今日头条汽车频道、头条视频、问答社区孵化而来。</span></span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="60"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">“钱袋子”鼓了，今日头条出手也相当阔绰。北京资深媒体人林哲向《豹变》回忆道：当年头条对这些新项目的扶持力度很大，悟空问答刚上线时，不仅张一鸣亲自站台，还花重金邀请众多大V加盟，</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #00385b; --tt-darkmode-color: #66B5E6;">每个月只要完成一定数量的问答内容，</span></span>就能获得三千多元奖励。</span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="62"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">“这应该是头条生态最风光的时候了。”林哲说。</span></span></p>

<h2 style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; border: 0px; text-align: left; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;" data-track="67"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">渐渐失宠的头条</span></span></span></h2>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="70"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">花无百日红，随着短视频时代的到来，</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #00385b; --tt-darkmode-color: #66B5E6;">今日头条的荣光逐渐被字节体系内的抖音，以及快手、B站取代。</span></span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="72"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">北京中航广场是字节全球总部，在大楼外立面左上方，曾经悬挂着巨大的“今日头条”LOGO。2018年8月，一辆黄色升降车把一位工人送至楼顶，</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #00385b; --tt-darkmode-color: #66B5E6;">今日头条LOGO被拆除，换上了“字节跳动”。</span></span></span></p>
<p class="pgc-img" style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><img class="syl-page-img" style="box-sizing: border-box; border: 0px; margin: 0px auto 8px; padding: 0px; cursor: zoom-in; max-width: 100%; display: block; border-radius: 4px; height: auto;" src="https://p3-sign.toutiaoimg.com/tos-cn-i-qvj2lq49k0/86a8d394a1794424b18559da0a959327~noop.image?_iz=58558&from=article.pc_detail&x-expires=1660012807&x-signature=LAUwoI8I9jZwTerEGs87kIj6abI%3D" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>

<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="75"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #ffffff; --tt-darkmode-color: #FFFFFF;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-color: #888888; --tt-darkmode-bgcolor: #898989;">中航广场大楼外立面今日头条LOGO/图片来源于网络</span></span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="77"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">也是在同一年，张一鸣以字节跳动CEO身份出席在山东举办的青年企业家创新发展国际峰会，此前很长一段时间，他的对外头衔一直是今日头条创始人兼CEO。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="79"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">字节去“头条化”，早在几年前就已经有预兆。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="81"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">2015年，今日头条APP上的短视频内容阅读占比超过了图文，达到了60%以上。在今日头条重新定义图文资讯分发规则后，短视频成为下一个被改造的对象，这才有了后来的西瓜视频和抖音。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="82"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #00385b; --tt-darkmode-color: #66B5E6;">随着短视频的兴起，今日头条不可避免地让出了C位。</span></span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="84"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">商业价值的下降是今日头条逐渐边缘化最直观的佐证。多位字节商业化员工对《豹变》表示，</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #00385b; --tt-darkmode-color: #66B5E6;">很少有客户会主动要求投头条，</span></span>现在只要不在抖音里，流量都很难起来。</span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="86"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">苏庆在字节电商业务线工作了四年，他告诉《豹变》，字节的电商业务是从今日头条起步的。“一开始头条电商业务叫‘放心购’，货到付款的形式。那时候用图片就很吃香，不用视频就可以直接出单，是最好操作的时候，好多客户都赚到钱了，因为很多人不知道怎么退货。”</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="88"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">“现在很少有客户主动要求投头条，基本都冲着抖音来的。”苏庆说。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="90"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">多位深耕字节的电商从业人士也称，现在短视频是风口，看图片和文字的人在减少。“头条的忠实粉丝还有，只是年纪偏大，一般在四十岁以上，年轻受众越来越少了。”上述人士表示。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="92"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #00385b; --tt-darkmode-color: #66B5E6;">放弃头条的除了商家，还有内容创作者。</span></span>陈怀五年前开始财经内容创业，对头条流量衰减有直观感受。他说，现在发布在头条号上的文章阅读普遍不高，一般只有几十、上百的阅读，能超过1000就算不错了。</span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="94"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">陈怀的头条号后台显示，最近几年，一共从今日头条获得创作补贴248元，最近几天的补贴在0.01-0.03元之间。而在2015年前后，有时候一条爆款的稿件，流量补贴就有好几百元。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="96"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">“现在也没太关注头条号的效果，发了就发了，复制粘贴一下而已。阅读量主要还是看内容和话题热度。”陈怀称。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="98"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">今日头条员工张洋回忆称，在2019年年中的一次CEO面对面会上，张一鸣曾直言，作为资讯APP，</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #00385b; --tt-darkmode-color: #66B5E6;">今日头条即将触及用户“天花板”，</span></span>如果没有新的增量，增长空间可能只剩小几千万DAU。</span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="100"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">“Kelly（抖音CEO张楠）在内部也提过，增长不是无限的，稳住也得付出努力。”张洋说。</span></span></p>

<h2 style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; border: 0px; text-align: left; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;" data-track="103"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">今日头条失去了头条</span></span></span></h2>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="107"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #00385b; --tt-darkmode-color: #66B5E6;">商业上的失意，让头条在字节体系内失位。</span></span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="109"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">2021年11月，字节宣布新一轮组织架构调整。抖音在字节体系内的地位进一步提升，“抖音”BU成为六大业务板块之一，今日头条、西瓜视频等“前辈”被并入了抖音。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="111"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">2022年5月，字节正式更名为“抖音集团”。与抖音蒸蒸日上相比，今日头条渐成明日黄花。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="113"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #00385b; --tt-darkmode-color: #66B5E6;">时代抛弃你时，连声招呼都不会打。</span></span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="115"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">“看点快报”APP宣布停运后，一位业界人士感叹：“</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #00385b; --tt-darkmode-color: #66B5E6;">原先快报要对标的是今日头条，现在对标的对象都快不行了，也就没有存在的价值了。”</span></span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="117"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">张洋认为，头条现在的处境还算可以，毕竟是相对独立的产品，收入贡献也还行。只是说资讯类APP天花板有限，在现在的大环境下，未来想要有很亮眼的增长比较难。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="119"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">而在外界，不少用户对头条未来的成长性表示了担忧。在职场社交APP脉脉上，多位用户发帖咨询头条的offer是否还值得去。对此，有认证字节跳动员工的用户回复称：不值得，还有增量吗？</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="121"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">被挤出头条位置的今日头条，</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #00385b; --tt-darkmode-color: #66B5E6;">要想避免边缘化，急需找到下一条增长曲线。</span></span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="123"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">今日头条的选择是抱紧抖音这条大腿。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="125"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">各方信息显示，今日头条正加速融入抖音生态，希望通过服务抖音突破自身流量瓶颈。如今在抖音搜索信息，有不少会推荐今日头条的内容。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="127"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">内容创业者陈怀就表示，现在之所以还坚持发头条号，</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #00385b; --tt-darkmode-color: #66B5E6;">是因为在抖音上能搜索到头条的内容，</span></span>希望能通过抖音为自己的内容导流。</span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="129"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">此外，电商业务也是今日头条一直放不下的执念。一般来说，资讯类APP主要收入依靠广告，但天花板很低。在并入抖音BU后，电商业务或许能为今日头条带来一丝转机。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="131"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">5月12日，今日头条发布公告称，将对直播板块进行整体升级，升级之后的直播服务将由抖音提供。这意味着，</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #00385b; --tt-darkmode-color: #66B5E6;">今日头条需要为抖音导流，也可以从抖音直播收益中分一杯羹。</span></span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="133"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">《豹变》在今日头条“直播”频道看到，页面顶部是一些媒体头条号直播间，之后便能刷到大量备注为“来自抖音”的直播间，占比约90%。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="135"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">在一个女装直播间，《豹变》点击“小黄车”即弹出“抖音电商”LOGO，尝试下单后，支付页面显示将由“抖音支付”付款，下单成功后，在今日头条APP里也能查看绑定的抖音账号全部电商订单。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="137"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">但是内容平台想做好电商不是一件容易的事，</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #00385b; --tt-darkmode-color: #66B5E6;">小红书、微博等也试水过电商，结果却不尽如人意。</span></span>更何况今日头条是在为抖音电商“做嫁衣”。在向抖音“输血”后，今日头条能否靠电商“造血”，还有待时间的检验。</span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="139"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">在抖音异军突起之前，今日头条一直在做加法，孵化新业务、探索新模式、上线新频道，“过重”的交互在一定程度上影响了用户体验。如今，抖音接替今日头条扮演了“试验田”的角色，本地生活服务、社交、种草、商城等新赛道，都依托抖音开展，今日头条参与度不高。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="141"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">当今日头条不再做加法，或许能为自身创造更纯粹的发展空间，</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #00385b; --tt-darkmode-color: #66B5E6;">但时间不等人，留给今日头条的时间不多了。</span></span></span></p>
