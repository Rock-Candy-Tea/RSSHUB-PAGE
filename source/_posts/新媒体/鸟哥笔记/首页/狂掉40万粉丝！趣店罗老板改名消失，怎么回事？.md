<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>7月17日<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E8%B6%A3%E5%BA%97" target="_blank">趣店</a>罗老板</span><span>的带货</span><span>直播</span><span>，堪称是“造神运动”</span><span>，一天暴涨将近500万粉丝，罗老板</span><span>自己把自己捧上了抖音主播的“神坛”。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>接着@趣店罗老板给东方甄选连刷了10个嘉年华后，被董宇辉“怼”了回去，导演小哥称和他有私人恩怨，并在直播间拉黑。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>随之而来的是傅首尔，贾乃亮等名人相继和他解除合作，<strong>@趣店罗老板抖音账号迅速掉粉，从高峰时期的508万迅速掉到468万，掉了约40万粉丝。</strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/140701899862ec601657d9b4.40852227.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/203002274662ec6016b40782.20487244.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>现在连抖音名字都改成了@趣店预制菜，抖音相关作品陆续删除，只剩7条视频。从全网爆红，到疯狂掉粉，这在抖音直播生态中是极其少见的现象。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span></span><span>这中间究竟发生了什么？</span></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span>1. 酸菜鱼预制菜 强推直播间</span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>7月17日当天，@趣店罗老板在抖音直播间拿出了“1000万份原价19.9元的酸菜鱼预制菜”，以及其他各类预制菜品，以1分钱或远低于市场的价格包邮“大甩卖”。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>通过这种低价补贴的方式，趣店吸引了一波又一波粉丝的关注，<span>大</span><span>家都</span><span>在疯狂</span><span>的</span><span>薅</span><span>羊毛，趣店</span>成为了当天抖音直播间排行榜NO.1。</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/203532857162ec60170b3435.23832689.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/209242789462ec60174ad086.42562107.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>一天暴涨450万粉，最高时总粉丝数达到508万。然而，一天后因为一场刷礼物的事件，罗老板又引发了一次轰动，不过这次却不是好事了。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>而是因为他进了东方甄选的直播间，连刷了10个嘉年华，主理人董宇辉和导演不欢迎他以这种方式支持，接着把他拉黑了。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>拉黑理由是导演小哥大学刚毕业，和他有私人恩怨，接着该事件就冲上了热搜。</span></strong><span>看到这里，肯定很多人不明白，这是个什么理由？</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>划重点，</span><span>大学毕业，私人恩怨</span><span>。</span></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span>2. 校园贷起家的罗老板</span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>虽然大家看到的都是罗老板趣店CEO的身份，抖音直播时也是宣传趣店上市公司的口号。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>但是如今的互联网是有记忆的，罗老板另一个身份被很多人发现了，就是校园贷项目“趣分期”的创始人。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>大学生可以通过趣分期进行贷款，进行消费购物等，趣分期收取一定的手续费。</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span></span><span>实际上趣店的前身就是“趣分期”，后来因为一些原因改名转型成了“趣店”。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>主要原因是，当时社会上出现了“非法借贷”、“强迫裸贷”、“暴力催收”等事件，将校园贷这种业务推向了舆论中心。<br></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>这其中最大的社会问题，就是多数学生都没有工作，没有收入来源。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>给学生放贷，赚学生的钱，<span>学</span><span>生们习惯性</span><span>超前消费后</span><span>，</span>显然触碰了社会道德的底线。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>接着有关部门发布通知，暂停网贷机构向大学生推广该业务。但因为<span>趣分期的“成功”</span><span>，趣店最终赴美上市成功。</span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>这些事件通通被媒体们挖了出来，很多和罗老板校园贷相关的文章同时出现。这些文章大多是质疑、批判或带有贬义性质。</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/51131934762ec601790c909.30365167.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>大家的关注点从罗老板直播，变成了罗老板曾经的放贷业务，罗老板瞬间处在了舆论的负面中心。</span><span></span></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span>3. 名人纷纷解除合作，黯然离场</span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>原本要和罗老板合作的傅首尔，贾乃亮，因为这个事件，也被卷进了舆论中心。原本双方之间是要合作的，最后选择向公众道歉，取消了合作。</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/98578696662ec6017dcd230.96901516.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/30727062962ec60182fda11.79095591.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>看到这里大家应该发现了，基于罗老板过去的商业行为事件。</span><span>从今以后，</span><span>无</span><span>论是哪</span><span>个明星或公司，恐怕都不会和</span><span>罗老板本人合</span><span>作了，这个事</span><span>件基本上</span><span>宣告了罗老板在</span><span>抖音甚至网络中寸步难行。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在直播中罗老板还称，</span><span>只要宝妈们在小区附近</span><span>开一家预制菜门店，每天卖出50份菜，每月即可轻松赚大几千的收入。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span></span><span>如果没钱开店，罗老板愿意为宝妈们提供一年免息贷款，所以表面上是在通过卖菜涨粉，最终目的还是靠放贷赚钱，老本行了。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>只不过这次是升级版了，喊了个口号“创业卖菜能赚钱”。要知道2015年那时候的大学生，不正是如今的宝妈吗，这就是“镰刀割了又想再割”。</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>与此同时，一条和罗老板有关的视频在全网迅速传播。<strong>在某个求职节目中，罗老板担任评委嘉宾，一位求职者质问罗老板，校园贷的意义是什么？</strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>罗老板回了一句“然后呢”，接着求职者怼了回去<strong>“看不到您项目的社会价值”</strong>，接着全场响起了掌声，甚至连主持人都和求职者拥抱了一下。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/12468042762ec60189d01e7.62428532.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>“校园贷业务”、“计划放贷给宝妈”、“过去的言论”，媒体们铺天盖地的报道，这些事件促使@趣店罗老板迅速掉粉，形象崩塌。</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>前些天，趣店抖音账号和罗老板相关的视频都被清空，抖音名字也改成了@趣店预制菜，头像也换掉了，</span><span>这似乎意味着</span><span>趣店抖音账号</span><span>和罗</span><span>敏没有了关系。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>目前趣店的单条视频，点赞数只有寥寥几百。没了罗老板，商业目的又被大家摸清了，趣店预制菜还能走远吗？目前趣店的市值较过去也没有什么变化，这波涨粉似乎是血亏的。</span></p>
