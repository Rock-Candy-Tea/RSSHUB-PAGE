<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>

<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>

<section style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 10px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; display: inline-block; min-width: 10%; vertical-align: top; border-style: solid; border-width: 0px 10px; border-radius: 0px; border-color: #f4dc32 #ff8124; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: PingFangSC-ultralight; letter-spacing: 1.4px; font-size: 24px;">两种误区</span></section>
</section>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">第一个误区，跟大家对品牌的认知有关。很多人对品牌的理解，就是一套视觉系统，甚至以为只是一个LOGO。我可以理解这种认知误区，毕竟，视觉就是最直观的、可感知的。好的设计，的确可以传递出品牌定位、品牌价值、品牌调性。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;"> </span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">但是，</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px; background-color: #888888; color: #ffffff;">这不意味着视觉与品牌之间可以划上等号。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">在整个品牌的构建过程中，视觉甚至是后置的，需要在前端将整个品牌屋的地基搭好，才能由设计师运用艺术化的手段通过设计方法将品牌呈现出来。</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px; background-color: #888888; color: #ffffff;">设计是重要的，但不要拔高这件事的价值</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">，其也只是整个品牌构建中后端的一个工作环节而已。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 0em; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: #333333; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; text-align: center;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">实际上，许多设计师是傲慢的，不愿意听取品牌方的需求，纯粹按照自己的“艺术/设计思维”去进行设计，一旦甲方不满意，只会说一句“不专业”来回怼。典型的例子，就是“彩色的黑”。网上已经有不少真正专业的设计师提出了<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">“彩色的黑”这一需求的设计</span>解决方案。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">因此，在品牌升级时也是一样，因为大家对外呈现时最容易接触到的是视觉，因此当然会更看重视觉层面的东西。但是，真正重要的，是藏在设计背后的内容。看热闹的事留给外行吧，内行要看门道。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">从“小米换logo”这件事的背后，就能看出这个误区有多深。当大家都在用“几百万就这？”的态度来吐槽之时，许多专业人士出来从品牌底层和设计逻辑的角度来表示这个logo多么好。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">实际上，他们是在两个维度争吵。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">第二个误区，涉及到</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px; background-color: #888888; color: #ffffff;">如何传递品牌升级</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">的问题。开个发布会、发点福利、拍个TVC，都是常用的手段。当将“品牌升级”仅仅窄化为一个事件营销之时，这个升级就失败了。实际上，在传播上来看的话，</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px; background-color: #888888; color: #ffffff;">好的品牌升级，是先感知后传播。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">也就是说，想用户/消费者先在其他的触点，比如产品、门店、物料等处感知到品牌已升级之后，再由品牌来进行大范围的主动传播。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">当然，也不是没有好的“事件营销型品牌升级”，最典型的莫过于</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px; background-color: #888888; color: #ffffff;">“滴滴打车升级为滴滴出行”</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">的案例。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">2015年9月4日开始，滴滴通过微博、微信公众号、滴滴APP等各个互联网渠道，向社会传递“滴滴打车再见”的信息，并发放大量福利，预告5天后的9月9日有“大事”发生。5天内，通过“辟谣”、倒计时海报、创意H5等各种手段一层层揭开面纱，将“产品升级为平台”的品牌升级新闻发布会做成了一个街知巷闻的社会化营销事件，获得巨大成功。</span></p>

</section>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px; background-color: #ffa900; color: #ffffff;">品牌升级，是细节（体现在每一处）也是全局（重投入）。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">在从0到1 的过程中，统一性是相对好把握的，因为一套品牌体系做完，后面的产品、门店、物料等触点，将体系呈现上去即可。但在品牌升级过程中，经常会出现不统一的情况。尤以加盟类品牌为主。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">我们常常看到，同一个品牌的门店，在一座城市可能出现两三种门头和空间效果。这时候就很容易造成品牌的混乱，消费者对这样的情况是很困惑的。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">因此，</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px; background-color: #888888; color: #ffffff;">品牌升级，不仅仅是垂直的覆盖，也是横向的覆盖。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">这里提到的细节，当然包括视觉，但不止于视觉。也包括产品、服务。是贯彻到每一个细节的。消费者在什么地方接触到品牌，就得在什么地方改变。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">而全局，意味着升级必须是全部的、彻底的。犹犹豫豫、拖泥带水的升级，只会给品牌认知带来混乱，不如不升级。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">如果不确定这次的升级方向是否正确，可以用一些概念店/门店来进行市场测试，一旦试对了路子，一定要进行快速而彻底的升级。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px; background-color: #ffa900; color: #ffffff;">品牌升级，是短期（事件）也是长期（战略）。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">品牌升级，最好是业务/产品先行。要抛开静态的认知，以为品牌升级，就是开一个发布会、发一篇公众号推文，告知一下消费者就完事了。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">品牌升级，是一个长期的过程。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">首先，</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px; background-color: #888888; color: #ffffff;">消费者对品牌的认知，不是突然改变的。</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">肯德基用了多久才建立起消费者心中“最本土化的洋品牌”的认知？而以肯德基的实力和地位，可不可以天天用广告轰炸这个认知呢？但它是怎么做的，不是嘴上说的，而是真正地从产品入手，为消费者研发、销售本土化的产品，才建立起这个认知的。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">其次，这个过程，可能</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px; background-color: #888888; color: #ffffff;">需要一直贯穿到下一次升级之前</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">。</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">美团历经过两次大的转型。第一次，是从团购平台升级为团购+外卖平台之时。在这个时候，便宜、省钱，依然还是大家对美团的认知，从业务上，由于外卖发展得太快，其与其他业务的融合也没有那么迅速，消费者的认知通过行为转变之后，没有跟原来的行为挂钩；到第二次，美团在业务上进行了大幅度调整，加入了酒店等业务，内部组织架构也进行了调整，才真正将外卖等其他业务一起考虑进大的品牌里。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>

<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">为什么要做品牌升级？</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">搞清楚这个问题，可能是这项工作的原点。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">可以用一句话来概述：跟不上发展。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">但如果不细究这句话，最终品牌升级又会变成“自嗨式升级”。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">跟不上谁/什么的发展？是同行？是时代？是消费者需求？</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">怎么定义这个“跟不上”？</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">实际上，这就是品牌升级的第一个步骤：</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px; background-color: #ffa900; color: #ffffff;">定义需求，尊重市场。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">品牌需要升级，可能是多方面的问题，先需要听取市场的意见，尤其要注重一线人员的意见。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">如果是推出了新的产品，突破了原有的品牌范畴，那么，品牌的边界就需要进一步去拓展了。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">比如，</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px; background-color: #888888; color: #ffffff;">支付宝在2019年时，进行过一次品牌升级</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">。在过去，大家提到支付宝时，对其的更多认知是“支付工具”，这是支付宝的功能体现，也是支付宝过去品牌传播中不断灌输的结果。那时，支付宝的slogan是“支付就用支付宝”。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 0em; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: #333333; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; text-align: center;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">2019年，支付宝早已不单纯是一个支付工具了，大家在上面可以缴费、可以查询便民服务、可以借钱、可以查快递。此时，支付宝需要进行品牌升级，他提出的新slogan是“生活好 支付宝”，将自己的边界从支付工具延展到数字生活开放平台。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">值得注意的是，在这个时候，</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px; background-color: #888888; color: #ffffff;">品牌升级是一个解决方案，并非是“万能钥匙”。</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">有时，为了保持品牌与产品的强关联，可以通过推出新子品牌的方式，与原品牌进行关联和区隔，既能保证原品牌的认知纯粹，又能让已有品牌为新子品牌赋能。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">比如，蜜雪冰城在推出咖啡品牌的时候，就没有用“蜜雪冰咖”这样的名字，而是重新构建了“幸运咖”的品牌。但，通过各种传播手段，让大家知道了这是蜜雪冰城旗下的品牌，不至于让大家产生“蜜雪冰城是奶茶还是咖啡？”产生混乱，又保证了大家立刻明白幸运咖的背景、定位和实力。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">需求只要找到了，答案不会太难回答。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">有新的产品吗？有新的场景吗？有新的服务吗？</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>

<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px; background-color: #888888; color: #ffffff;">市场需求，是品牌升级最重要的依据</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1.4px;">，消费者认可的升级，才是好的升级。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>

<p style="margin: 0px 16px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, 'system-ui', 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; caret-color: #000000; line-height: 2em;"> </p>
