<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>近日，<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E5%BE%AE%E4%BF%A1" target="_blank">微信</a><span>安卓版与苹果iOS版双双</span>迎来了最新的<span>8.0.27</span>内测版本，其中安卓版直接从8.0.25升级到了8.0.27。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>同时，PC端微信也迎来了3.7.6新内测版本，</span><span><strong>支持多端同步</strong></span><span>，这意味着可以将聊天记录在手机和电脑端互相迁移合并，之后在手机和电脑上均可浏览完整聊天记录，可以</span><span><strong>更方便的查找聊天信息和文件</strong></span><span>了。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>此外，见实也汇总了近期手机端<span>更新的</span>超实用的<span>5项</span>功能：</span><span><strong>消息通知横幅、全屏输入、新增视频通话悬浮窗、优化搜一搜、修复消息延迟</strong></span><span>。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>其中，消息通知横幅安卓版暂时未实现，还需再等等；而视频通话悬浮<span>窗</span>功能安卓则是早于IOS更早就实现了。另外，<span><span>优化搜一</span><span>搜、</span><span>修复消息延迟问题两项功能，对于</span><span>当前未更新版</span><span>微信</span><span>也已</span><span>实</span><span>现了，因为见实</span><span>留</span><span>意到很多老铁还未发现，</span><span>所以今天一起汇总方便老铁们用起来。</span></span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>接下来和见实一起体验这些超实用的功能吧！</span></p>
<h1 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); font-size: 20px; color: #212629; text-align: center; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span><strong><span>丨功能1：<strong>迁移和备份（</strong>多端同步）</span></strong></span><span><span></span></span></h1>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>内测的<span>3.7.6 </span>PC版微信拥有了</span><span><strong>【迁移和备份】</strong></span><span>功能，可以将聊天记录在 PC 和手机间同步互相迁移，迁移后</span><span><strong>在PC和手机上都可浏览合并后的聊天记录</strong></span><span>。</span><span>这弥补了PC 端微信离线无法完整接收微信消息的问题。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>需要注意的是，当使用该功能时PC和手机必须</span><span><strong>处于同一局域网中</strong></span><span>，并且迁移过程中手机微信将无法使用。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/82892236762f74e93ef7363.54845217.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>而此前，PC端微信拥有的<strong>【备份与恢复】</strong>功能，也可以将聊天记录备份到电脑上，但备份的聊天记录不能直接在电脑上打开，只能在手机上恢复。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/205696151662f74e94397375.11314940.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h1 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); font-size: 20px; color: #212629; text-align: center; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span><strong><span>丨功能2：消息通知横幅</span></strong></span></h1>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>适用机型：</span></strong><span>IOS</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>消息通知横幅的开启，可通过依次在微信内操作“</span><span><strong>设置—消息通知—开启‘消息通知横幅’</strong></span><span>”来实现。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>当消息通知横幅开启之后，可以在看文章、刷朋友圈、刷视频号等过程中，</span><span><strong>通过通知横幅回复新消息，且不中断当前进程</strong></span><span>，更高效利用时间。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>但，见实同学测试发现这一功能只能在微信内使用，退出到手机桌面之后就无法使用了。当前也只适用于IOS版。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/147197378962f74e946e7012.82355855.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h1 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); font-size: 20px; color: #212629; text-align: center; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span><strong><span>丨功能3：全屏输入</span></strong></span></h1>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>适用机型：</span></strong><span>安卓+IOS</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>全屏输入功能的开启有<strong>两种方式：</strong></span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>一是</span></strong><span>当对话框编辑内容达或超过三行时，输入框左侧会自动出现向上的箭头，点击后即可进行全屏输入。</span><strong><span>二是</span></strong><span>当长</span><span>按输入框2-3秒时</span><span>，也会弹出「全屏输入」选项，点击后</span><span>可进入全屏编辑模式。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>这一功能对于需要大量文字信息时确实方便，不用再借助外力来完成文字输入了。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/15436806062f74e94cda849.71713999.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h1 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); font-size: 20px; color: #212629; text-align: center; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span><strong><span>丨功能4：视频通话悬浮窗</span></strong></span></h1>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>适用机型：</span></strong><span>IOS+安卓</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><span>此前，安</span><span>卓版</span><span>微信</span><span>已实现了</span>视频通话悬浮功能，即<strong>接通视频后，点击聊天界面左上角的缩放按钮，视频窗口就会自动悬浮</strong>。但iOS微信悬浮窗只能在微信内展示，如果退回到手机桌面，悬浮窗就会消失，显示“<strong>对方未启用摄像头</strong>”，大大降低了通话体验。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/113594453262f74e953fd998.45434941.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在 iOS 微信最新版本内测中，悬浮窗功能终于可以实现。用新版本视频聊天即使从微信退回到手机桌面，视频聊天依然能够正常进行。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/153944784562f74e95eeda82.01958784.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h1 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); font-size: 20px; color: #212629; text-align: center; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span><strong><span>丨功能5：优化搜一搜</span></strong></span><span></span></h1>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>适用机型：</span></strong><span>IOS+安卓</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>搜一搜优化之后搜索将会</span><span><strong>更精准高效</strong></span><span>。在新的能力下当你进入「搜一搜」，输入关键词进行搜索后，点击全部旁边的“<strong>三横杠</strong>”，即可出现搜索排序方式：横轴为</span><span><strong>最新、最热</strong></span><span>；纵轴为</span><span><strong>类型、时间、范围</strong></span><span>，这时就可以根据需</span><span>求更精准地搜索内容了。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/14895264162f74ea0740d44.31120295.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h1 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); font-size: 20px; color: #212629; text-align: center; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span><strong><span>丨功能6：修复消息延迟问题</span></strong></span></h1>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>适用机型：</span></strong><span>IOS</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>微信8.0.26版本在使用过程中有很多用户反映消息延迟的问题，还因此上了热搜。关于这个问题官方表示已经修复了，大家可以按照下列步骤一次操作解决：</span><span><strong>设置—帮助与反馈—?—修复消息通知——完成修复</strong></span><span>。</span></p>
