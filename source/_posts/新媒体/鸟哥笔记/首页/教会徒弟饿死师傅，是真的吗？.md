                        <p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="1"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">我们都知道一句俗语：</span><strong style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">教会徒弟饿死师傅</span></strong><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">，这句话有没有道理呢？我给大家讲一个故事，我在一个传统企业做品宣推广，公司有十几个商务，也就是销售，在销售圈有个词叫“销冠”，大家知道销冠的意思吗？就是销售冠军。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="2"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">没有例外，每个公司都有他的销冠，我们公司也有自己的销冠。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="3"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">这个销冠厉害到什么程度呢？销售部三分之二的业绩都是他一个人贡献的，也就是他一个人的业绩不仅平了其余十来个人的业绩，还碾压式的超出很多，对于这么厉害的角色，公司自然是重点关照了，升职加薪是难免的。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="4"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">不到两年，就提拔为公司的销售部老大，本以为有这么牛掰的人带领，销售团队的业绩应该会再创新高，结果半年过去，销售部门的业绩基本横盘，不仅如此，销售部还走了一些人，这就奇怪了，</span><strong style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">为什么专业能力那么强的人，带不出一个好团队呢</span></strong><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">？</span></span></p>
<h2 class="syl-line-pure-english" style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #212629; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;" data-track="6"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><strong style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #ff2600; --tt-darkmode-color: #FF2600;">01、</span></strong></span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><strong style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">教会徒弟饿死师傅</span></strong></span></h2>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="8"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">我也反思过这个问题，虽然与我无关，但是我就喜欢为别人瞎操心，哎。我就有意无意地去听底下的销售是怎么说的，说来说去主要就一点，这个销冠并没有怎么带大家，而是让大家自己去跟客户，至于如何跟，怎么谈判，都没有很详细的指点。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="9"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">哦，原来问题出在了带人上，那么如此厉害的人为什么不会带人呢？大家要知道，销售岗不同于其他岗，销售是靠业绩提成来过活的，干的多赚的多，不干活就没钱拿，</span><strong style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">如果公司对于销售管理岗没有科学的薪资标准，一个管理者就很难把精力花在带人身上。</span></strong></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="10"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">之所以出现教会徒弟饿死师傅的心理，原因就像上面讲的，一方面是公司的薪资标准有问题，另一方面也就是大家常有的心理，把底下人教会了，抢了我的饭碗，那我咋办。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="11"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">对于第一个问题，只能让公司的老板意识到，比如把管理岗的工资变成固定薪资加基层业绩的提点，底下的人做得越高，个人的提点越高，这样一荣俱荣，一损俱损，销冠自然会想办法提升大家整体的能力，进而提升业绩了。</span></span></p>
<h2 class="syl-line-pure-english" style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #212629; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;" data-track="13"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><strong style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #ff2600; --tt-darkmode-color: #FF2600;">02、</span></strong></span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><strong style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">教会徒弟师傅才能爆赚</span></strong></span></h2>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="15"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">对于第二个问题，短视的人的确会把核心的经验藏着掖着，而我就不一样了，我从互联网公司出来的，我所在的公司开放到什么程度呢？不仅每个人在教别人时毫无保留，公司的后台数据也都是对每个人开放的，大家不管级别高低都可以查到公司最为核心的数据。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="16"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">所以，公司从上到下形成了一种良好的学习氛围，你不会的可以问我，但凡我知道的都会告诉你，如果我没告诉你，只能说明一点：我也不知道。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="17"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">不仅仅在公司内部，有其他公司上门来交流时，除了最为核心的产品数据外，我们是有什么说什么，给我的感受是，</span><strong style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">互联网企业的人都很实诚，不会藏着掖着</span></strong><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="18"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">我也养成了这样的习惯，所以有了感悟或者经验就会写出来，让大家一起学习成长，我也是一个乐于分享知识的人儿。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="19"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">而到了传统企业就不一样了，人与人之间互不信任，每个人说话都是说一半藏一半，久而久之，大家平时聊天说正经的事少了，胡吹海吹的事多了。这也让一些管理者理所当然地认为，我教会了别人，别人学去了，那我岂不是要失业了。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="20"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">上面提到这是很短视的想法，大家要知道，一个企业是动态发展的，发展意味着公司规模要扩张，</span><strong style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">扩张意味着部门要扩充人员或者增加新的业务模块，这些改变需要什么？钱？不是的，需要领导</span></strong><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="21"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">举一个例子，你是销冠，手底下带着5个业务员，你不怎么带底下的人，每个业务员跟你就拿个底薪加一点点提成，大家吭哧吭哧的过着低保一般的生活。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="22"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">有一天，公司规模变大了，老板说，我要开发新的市场了，要招兵买马，规模至少是现在部门的5倍，现在需要一个得力的助手来帮我开疆拓土，待遇开的很高。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="23"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">你也很心动，就跟老板说：我的能力足够可以，我想来担这个重任。老板看了下你的部门，底下5个业务员还没有成长起来，如果你调岗了，这5个人估计都要喝西北风了，于是否了你的提议，去外面花重金挖了一个销冠过来。看着这么好的机会被错过了，你是不是很心痛。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="24"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">从长远来看，教会徒弟后自己才能有更好的发展机会。如果史上最牛前台，只知道做好前台，不去带人，是绝对不会走到今天的。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="25"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">因为你的岗位离不开你，你就不会有更高的发展可能，为什么你的岗位离不开你，是因为你把个人技能看得比命还重，不忍心交给别人，</span><strong style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">你守住了岗位，也把自己牢牢的拴在了这个岗位上</span></strong><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">，这就是刚刚说的短视。</span></span></p>

<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="29"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">接下来讲理论了，我的三十年职场经验告诉我，一个人在职场的良好发展路径是螺旋上升的，你很难一步登天的从基层一下子坐到COO的位置，也很难持续稳定的向上攀升，因为任何人总会遇到瓶颈期。</span><strong style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">大多数人的发展都是磕磕碰碰，呈螺旋一样的上升</span></strong><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="30"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">上升的过程中，你掌握了各种技能，基础的做事技能，接人待物的技能，竞品调研的技能，协调资源的技能，管理的技能，项目统筹的技能等等，每一项技能的掌握都是你进一步上升的垫脚石。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="31"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">当你学会了某个技能时，只能应用于当下，你要是想继续地爬升，是需要更牛掰的技能来支撑的，而爬升除了需要运势与技能外，还要有能承接你原有工作的人。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="32"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">如何让他们能承接下你的工作，答案就是把你的所会所学，都毫无保留地交给下一个接棒者，用一个很形象的比喻来形容：</span><strong style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">你寻找到了火种，把火种交给下一代，一代一代的传递下去，最终你才能去敲响上市的那个锣</span></strong><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #515151; --tt-darkmode-color: #959595;">。</span></span></p>
