<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>先来看看一部全新的音乐MV</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>它的歌词总有那么一瞬间</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>让你想在炎炎夏日calm down</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>一如既往，酷感十足。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>这是<span>【雪碧】</span>邀请华晨宇演绎的2022年<span>【雪碧】</span>全新品牌单曲MV，没有复杂的编排，但却简单地唱出了品牌想要传达的理念：<strong>燥不可挡，酷爽以对。</strong></span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>这也是<span>【雪碧】</span>今年的全新主张。熟悉他们的Z世代一定记得那句：透心凉，心飞扬。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>只是，时代在变，品牌的主张也会变。</span><span>这一次，<span>【雪碧】</span>显然是捕捉到了喧嚣世界的燥热感，</span><strong>想用音乐的酷爽和无数的爱好者们一起去battle</strong><span>。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>毕竟，无论身上有着怎样的icon，Z世代都坚持自我，他们想带着BGM对抗世界上那些纷扰的声音，找到心中净土。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><span>【雪碧】</span>抓住的恰好是这点，他们试图通过“燥不可挡，酷爽以对”去沟通，<strong>为Z世代们提供一个保持自我的音乐解决方案</strong>。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>然而，理念如何才能入心，引起共情，这又是<span>【雪碧】</span>需要面对的一项课题，围绕这个课题，他们以逐层递进的方式给出了解法：</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/80860382360221a97c68298.05017712.png" title="【雪碧】2022年新主张：面对无可避免的燥热如何才酷爽？" alt="【雪碧】2022年新主张：面对无可避免的燥热如何才酷爽？" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><strong><span>打造开创性全球音乐平台，</span></strong><strong><span>凿开共情通道</span></strong><span></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>“燥不可挡，酷爽以对”是<span>【雪碧】</span>2022年的核心主张，但是落回到个体理解，他必须让人能够明白：酷爽的内涵以及如何酷爽。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>我们或许可以尝试从两个层面去解读：1.身体的酷爽；2.心灵的酷爽。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>身体的酷爽完全可以在燥热的夏天畅饮一杯yyds的雪碧就能实现，而心灵的酷爽，则需要品牌大开脑洞了。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>这一次，<span>【雪碧】</span>选择通过他们一直在深耕的领域——音乐去沟通，打造了一个开创性全球音乐平台：</span><span><strong>【雪碧】酷</strong></span><span><strong>光灯</strong>。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/84145914462b474854772e0.78541576.gif" title="【雪碧】2022年新主张：面对无可避免的燥热如何才酷爽？" alt="【雪碧】2022年新主张：面对无可避免的燥热如何才酷爽？" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span></span><span> </span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>实际上，品妹并不意外，从很早以前的“<span>【雪碧】</span>音乐季”，到冠名《我是唱作人2》《炙热的我们》等音综，<span>【雪碧】</span>绝对称得上是被主业耽误的“唱片公司”。</span><span>只是这一次，他们想用音乐玩出更多的“不一样”。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><span>【雪碧】</span>酷光灯邀请到了格莱美金奖制作人JAMES BLAKE，制作了一段2022年<span>【雪碧】</span>全球主题曲的主旋律，并由世界各地的先锋艺术家演绎。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在这里，音乐平台跳脱了乐评和对比，不再内卷，而成为了每一个先锋音乐家说出自己声音的归属地。</span><span>你可以理解为所有人都可以用音乐来表达“酷酷的自己”，那个对抗世界喧嚣，选择倾听内心声音的自己。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>当然，<span>【雪碧】</span>酷光灯不止是一个音乐平台。任何一个乐者的演绎，都在让<span>【雪碧】</span>“燥不可挡，酷爽以对”的理念无形地占据情绪心智。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>即，<span>【雪碧】</span>酷光灯承载品牌主张，凿开了共情的通道，也是每个人抵抗燥热，获得酷爽的路<span>径</span>。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/189383050560221a9860dbc2.87771537.png" title="【雪碧】2022年新主张：面对无可避免的燥热如何才酷爽？" alt="【雪碧】2022年新主张：面对无可避免的燥热如何才酷爽？" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><strong><span>演绎全新“酷爽”品牌单曲，</span></strong><strong><span>升华共情符号</span></strong></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>品牌共情，一定离不开沟通，而沟通的关键在于人物。</span><span>相对于品牌“自说”，利用关键人物的个性或故事进行传播，更能引起共鸣。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>华晨宇是主题曲的演绎者之一，他透过个体理解，将品牌主张升华成精神符号，用音乐让Z世代们“破防”：</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>① 唱出自我，释放最真实的共情因子</span></strong><span></span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>MV给人最直观的感觉就是“A爆了”。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>穿越内心世界</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在黑夜中奔跑</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>被聚光灯照耀</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span></span><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/31008897662b4748a9a7567.21859608.png" title="【雪碧】2022年新主张：面对无可避免的燥热如何才酷爽？" alt="【雪碧】2022年新主张：面对无可避免的燥热如何才酷爽？" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>它像那些外界燥热的声音</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>狠狠地打在我们身上</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>试图让我们迷茫</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/206114181462b4748b48dab1.42974674.png" title="【雪碧】2022年新主张：面对无可避免的燥热如何才酷爽？" alt="【雪碧】2022年新主张：面对无可避免的燥热如何才酷爽？" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>而华晨宇选择用音乐去对抗</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>击破这些聚光灯</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>内心酷爽</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/20867162662b4748bc94430.02330643.png" title="【雪碧】2022年新主张：面对无可避免的燥热如何才酷爽？" alt="【雪碧】2022年新主张：面对无可避免的燥热如何才酷爽？" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>任何一次共情的传播，其关键在于个体价值的彰显和精神的归属。面对聚光灯所隐喻的喧嚣世界，华晨宇在MV中用音乐去逐一击破，对抗噪音，再现个体价值。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>但同时，<strong>这种个体价值又呼应了每一个Z世代所想呐喊的声音：</strong>不管外界如何燥热，我才不会emo，而是用音乐表达，保持我内心的“酷”，从而碰撞出共情的火花。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>当然，MV中的歌词也是亮点满满。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>滚烫的气流</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>随它热切嘶吼</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>我在我的宇宙</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>任谁喋喋不休</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>I’m the one</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>通过隐喻回归自我的内心世界，MV对歌词进行了具像化，为我们<strong>创造了一块精神的归属地</strong>：用个性去抵御噪音，用内心去对抗炽热。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>因此，华晨宇的演绎连接了品牌与Z世代的情感，他作为“关键人物”，将共情的通道升华成了一种精神符号，既能帮助品牌焕发情感因子，同时又给予了每个人找寻自我世界的“酷爽”体验。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>② 音乐故事，辅助激活受众的情感认同</span></strong><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>华晨宇并非只是完全演绎音乐，相反，他也在赋予音乐故事性：</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>每当写歌遇到瓶颈时</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>他选择去做一些和音乐无关的事情</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>吹吹夜风，或者散散步</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>个体故事的加入是对品牌主张的一次深度强化。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>也许你无法很深地理解歌词表达，或者MV的画面意义，但当华晨宇说出自己的故事时，你一定会产生关联效应：原来，“酷爽以对”这么简单！</span><span>这种场景再现，能帮助品牌将主张真正钻入人心，从而在短时间内就实现情感认同。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>实际上，从个人价值→精神归属→故事加持，整个MV以华晨宇为纽带，让品牌用音乐带领每个人脱离了“燥”的现代生活。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/135701419260221a9c71d269.81480460.png" title="【雪碧】2022年新主张：面对无可避免的燥热如何才酷爽？" alt="【雪碧】2022年新主张：面对无可避免的燥热如何才酷爽？" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><strong><span>走出品牌共情的新路<span>径，</span></span></strong><strong><span>这三者缺一不可</span></strong><span></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>没有哪个品牌不想和自己的目标群体玩在一起。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>透过这波campaign，品妹始终觉得品牌制造共情，就像我们建造房屋：地基、框架、内饰，缺一不可。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>首先，地基是根本，没有长期的深耕就会“地动山摇”。显然，<span>【雪碧】</span>很懂这一点。他们持久地在音乐方面下功夫，可谓是已经到了掘地三尺的地步。</span><strong><span>这样的长期主义带来的好处是：</span><span>让Z世代们知道品牌不仅“懂音乐”，更懂爱音乐的他们。</span></strong><span></span></p>
