<section><span>这段时间大家都被二舅刷屏，钟薛高或许觉得热搜地位被威胁，被爆要推出低端版雪糕了。</span></section>

<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><span>虽然我的英语只有四级，但翻译一下：</span><span><strong>“钟薛低”，岂不是钟薛low。</strong></span></span></p>

<section></section>
<section><span>钟薛高之前在消费者的雷区反复跳舞，现在突然变身<span>小丑出来搞笑，</span>多少还是有被惊喜到的。</span></section>

<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>这让我想到一句话：<strong>高不成，低不就。</strong></span></p>

<section><span>不过这个<span>“钟薛低”，也不是空穴来风。</span></span></section>

<section><span><span>有网友从天眼查App<span>扒拉到</span>，</span><span><strong>钟薛高背后的公司——盘箸有喜生物科技有限公司，突然申请注册多个高高低低的<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E5%95%86%E6%A0%87" target="_blank">商标</a>。</strong></span></span></section>

<section></section>
<section><span>咱也不知道，这究竟是自嘲还是自咒。</span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span><span>虽然只是商标，但如果说钟薛要进军低价雪糕，向下沉市场进</span><span>攻，我还是相信的。</span></span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span>毕竟之前钟薛高就在落实单独冰柜，还有人发现钟薛高搞促销“低头”了。</span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<p><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/75631970262e44ce896cd50.53845557.png" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>

<section></section>
<section><span>要知道钟薛高之所以得罪年轻人，原因就两个：</span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span><span><strong>首先是</strong></span><span><strong>卖得贵</strong></span><span><strong>，三四块的成本，卖一二十块钱。</strong></span></span></section>
<section></section>
<section><span><span><strong><span>然后是</span></strong></span><span><strong><span>选错位</span></strong></span><span><strong><span>，</span></strong><strong><span>在下沉市场玩高端局</span></strong><strong><span>，挤占低端雪糕的空间。</span></strong></span></span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span><span>你贵我也贵。</span><span>那大家自然担心，因为钟薛高贵，带上其它雪糕一起贵。</span></span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span><span>高不成低不就的，<span>最后钟薛高</span>把两拨人都得罪了。</span><span>就像星巴克如果出现在普通商场，不标价格照样被骂。</span></span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/124683909462e44ce90548e6.37394365.jpeg" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></section>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin-bottom: 25px;"><span>图源：网友评论</span></h4>
<section></section>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><span>骂也骂过，烧也烧过，晒也晒过。。。</span><span><strong><span>钟薛高说不准要改过，专走低价路线。</span></strong></span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>看看人家蜜雪冰城，就是以下沉市场发家的。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>还有喜茶，有多少人跟我一样，第一次听到“喜小茶<span>”</span>，差点还以为哪里冒出来的山寨货。</span></p>
<section><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/12582095162e44ce9342219.52387541.jpeg" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></section>

<section><span><strong>但其实喜小茶，就是依靠大哥喜茶的品牌背书，打开下沉市场的子品牌。</strong></span></section>
<section><span><strong> </strong></span></section>
<section><span><strong><span>新茶饮碰到天花板，涨不动了就要找新的增量，钟薛高亦如此。</span></strong></span></section>
<section><span><strong><span> </span></strong></span></section>
<section><span>更何况，据说今年钟薛高搞下沉还不错。</span><span><strong><span>在三四线下沉市场的销量涨幅，就超过北上超一线城市；西南地区单是一季度销量，就超过去年全年。如今二三线城市的复购率，也可以跟大城市一争高下。</span></strong></span></section>
<section></section>
<section><span>钟薛高可能真的从理想的乌托邦醒来了，认真考虑国情和消费者，准备出大众能接受的低价雪糕了。</span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span><span>说不定</span><span><strong>既能挽回一线、二线城市低消费的人群，还可以讨好下沉市场。</strong></span></span></section>
<section></section>
<section><span>当然钟薛低能否把下沉搞出色是后话。起码从目前注册的动作来看，不排除是在偷偷试探平价。</span></section>

<section><span>如果<span>你</span>点了中杯咖啡，就会被“礼貌暗示”：“你确定是中杯吗？”、“中杯是我们最小的杯型喔”、“加3元咖啡就能升大杯” 。</span></section>

<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span>有没有一种可能，钟薛高担心消费者消费不起，干脆给咱们整个低的，将来钟薛高注册个“钟薛中”，再做差异化定价，<span>把<span>不同消费者</span>都拿捏住</span><span>。</span></span></section>

<section><span><strong><span>“钟薛中”就是参考标准，</span></strong><strong><span>吃得起的就吃钟薛高，吃不起的就吃钟薛低。</span></strong></span></section>

<section><span>难怪有人说，钟薛高是嫌一个单品不够割韭菜，开拓不同的产品线，把<span>高</span><span>低端</span>都包揽了，<span>到时候分档</span><span>割韭</span><span>菜</span>。</span></section>

<section></section>
<section><span>最后还有一种可能，就是大家想多了。</span><span><strong><span>钟薛高单纯就是怕别的商家来蹭热度。</span><span>这一招，就是跟阿里巴巴和老干妈学的。</span></strong></span></section>

<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>现在抢注相同近似商标的现象，是越来越普遍，就像一个段子说的：</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>想喝星巴克，结果去了一家星8克</span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>慕名去茶颜悦色，到手的却是一杯茶颜观色</span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>肯德基与麦当劳的合体成了“麦肯基”</span></strong></span></p>

<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong>上演我山寨自己，</strong></span><span><strong><span>走山寨的路，让山寨无路可走。</span></strong></span></p>
