<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="2"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="3"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;">在前一阵子乱翻书组织的《QQ元宇宙：频道、游戏引擎和超级QQ秀》主题直播活动中，科技主播们解析QQ群、QQ空间等传统功能的升级，探讨频道、QQ秀等新功能的传承，一个雏形QQ元宇宙世界展现了出来。</span></p>

<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="4"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;">科技主播们说了很多关于QQ的故事，比如二十年前QQ秀商业计划书就涉及元宇宙、虚拟世界等等，只不过受技术限制只做出2D版的QQ秀，随着移动互联网、3D引擎、人工智能技术的变化和发展，超级QQ秀诞生了，回头看来，这仿佛是轮回也是进步。轮回的是心智，进步的是技术。</span></p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="5"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="6"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-weight: bold;">其实，没有任何社交软件是不变的，变化是为了吸收旧事物的优点、克服旧事物的缺点，将旧事物的优点与新技术结合，才能创造一个既有旧事物优越性又有新技术先进性的现象级产品，拥有23年历史的QQ很好的印证了这一点。</span></span></p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="7"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="8"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;">关于QQ的谈论还有很多，前两天看到了一个挺有意思的视频，解答了我一些关于QQ的困惑。这些不同年纪、不同圈层的人似乎回答了QQ经久不衰的答案。很难在一个产品的用户群访中真正发现“再小的热爱都有回音”，但QQ的盘子和社群的筛选逻辑真正做到了。这些在各个平台的大V大神，莫名的都喜欢在QQ里互相交流。</span></p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="9"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="10"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;">除了兴趣，更有真情。视频中一个红斑狼疮患者在空间日志上记录姐姐倾囊支持她治病的故事，还因此认识了新朋友，在家人、朋友、医生的鼓励下，她有了与病魔抗争的勇气。彼时QQ对于她来说是情绪宣泄口，后来是工作工具，是日记本，是相册……</span></p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="11"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="12"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-weight: bold;">可以从中发现，尽管QQ功能在升级迭代，很多游戏和老旧的功能被淘汰，QQ用户对它的依赖性还是很强，因为00后、90后、80后群体都可以在QQ上找到自己的“位置”。QQ上，文字、语音、短视频、直播等等社交方式齐全，用户群体几乎覆盖了所有圈层，这一点笔者也深有体会。</span></span></p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="13"> </p>

<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="15"> </p>

<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="17"> </p>

<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="19"> </p>

<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="21"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="22"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;">使用QQ和弟弟、妹妹聊天是为了更快的找到他俩，有一次他们出去玩没回家，我用微信联系没人回复，换了QQ才有了回应，后来也就随他们使用QQ联系了。用QQ工作也比较偶然，有一次需要传输一个内存较大的视频素材，同事建议用QQ传输，后来就习惯用QQ和同事沟通工作进程、传输大文件，再后来公司开展了视频业务，我也跟着用上了QQ小世界，体验了超级QQ秀等新功能……着实让我体会了一把后浪推着前浪走的感觉。</span></p>

<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="29"> </p>

<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="31"> </p>

<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="49"> </p>
