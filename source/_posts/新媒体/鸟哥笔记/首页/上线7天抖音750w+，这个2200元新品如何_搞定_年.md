<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">“每个人都有自己偏爱的生活方式，每一件家具，都是和生活好好相处的见证。”</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">上周，品牌「林氏家居」推出新品“怎么坐都可以椅”，并携手品牌全球代言人@王一博 ，在天猫、抖音等平台进行多渠道联动营销。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">在抖音平台，品牌借势“抖音818发现好物节”开启了为期一周的新品推广活动，并通过话题#王一博的怎么坐都可椅，引爆品牌声量。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">据飞瓜显示，话题自上线开始热度就一路攀升，截止目前相关视频总播放超过1.2亿。</p>
<p class="pgc-img" style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><img class="syl-page-img" style="box-sizing: border-box; border: 0px; margin: 0px auto 8px; padding: 0px; cursor: zoom-in; max-width: 100%; display: block; border-radius: 4px; height: auto;" src="https://p3-sign.toutiaoimg.com/tos-cn-i-qvj2lq49k0/0dbe16b0d13b4d0789398676a4fd8321~noop.image?_iz=58558&from=article.pc_detail&x-expires=1661909379&x-signature=ZkoNQeq9ElcEBY848zBVNHuqm54%3D" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">这款“怎么坐都可椅”，从其名称上就直观传达了产品能适配各种坐姿的特性，因此推广达人在视频中也多围绕这一特点进行展开。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">其中，腰部达人@西米好宅 以当下大热的“宅文化”为切入点，展示了该产品在娱乐、工作等不同需求场景下的应用，并结合年轻人常见的追剧吃零食等场景，展示产品防水防污的亮点，种草力拉满。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">宠物号@喵小汪 则深谙“养宠一族”的痛点，在视频中放大了椅子耐磨又耐脏的特点，进一步扩宽产品的受众范围。</p>
