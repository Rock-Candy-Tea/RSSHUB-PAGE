<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="3">市场环境良性发展，抖音电商生态也一片向好，<strong>多位达人带货破亿，商品畅销千万，品牌直播也在行业中角力。</strong>具体有哪些突出主播和出圈现象，通过新抖统计的7月1日-7月31日的月榜数据，来看看吧。</p>
<p class="pgc-p" style="text-align: center; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="5"><strong>01</strong></p>
<p class="pgc-p" style="text-align: center; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="89"><strong>主播带货榜</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="6"><strong>单场带货2.51亿引发刷屏</strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="8">据新抖的<strong><span style="color: #ff7800; --tt-darkmode-color: #FF7800;">[主播带货榜]</span></strong>数据显示，<strong>7月带货销售额上亿的主播共有13位</strong>，对比有618大促加持的6月来说，破亿主播数略有减少。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="10">新东方前明星教师“董宇辉”带火的<strong><span style="color: #ff7800; --tt-darkmode-color: #FF7800;">@东方甄选</span></strong>连续两月斩获带货榜榜首，7月销售额6.18亿；8500w+粉丝的头部创作达人<strong><span style="color: #ff7800; --tt-darkmode-color: #FF7800;">@疯狂小杨哥</span></strong>排名升至榜二，直播销售额3.05亿；榜三则是<strong><span style="color: #ff7800; --tt-darkmode-color: #FF7800;">@董先生</span></strong>，直播销售额2.99亿。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="12">下面是主播带货榜TOP30的排名情况：</p>
<p class="pgc-img" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://mp.toutiao.com/mp/agw/article_material/open_image/get?code=NDQ4YWI4MjUzZWI3YWY1ZDY2OWMyYmJmNjU2OWUzMmYsMTY1OTUzNzM5OTEyOA==" data-ic="false" data-width="800" data-height="3642" data-ic-uri referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="pgc-img-caption"></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="15">从6月开始恢复直播的<strong><span style="color: #ff7800; --tt-darkmode-color: #FF7800;">@疯狂小杨哥</span></strong>在近2个月都进入带货榜前十，头部博主的号召力可见一斑。直播间开播频率稳定在一周两播，每周二和周六晚8点左右各有一场，小杨哥上月共开播9场，<strong>场均销售额高达3388.84w</strong>。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="17">直播选品以<strong>50元以下的福利款为主</strong>，占比50.7%，其中9.9元的口罩销售量达到15.41w；从销售额来看的话，<strong>高单价产品才是贡献GMV的大头</strong>，如售价999元的指纹锁销售额达到1480.42w，这也表明小杨哥直播间的粉丝具有较高的消费能力。</p>

<h4 style="text-align: center; background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;" data-track="19"><span style="color: #9b9b9b; --tt-darkmode-color: #9B9B9B;">▲ 新抖-账号详情-带货商品</span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="21">上月爆火刷屏的直播还属<strong><span style="color: #ff7800; --tt-darkmode-color: #FF7800;">@趣店罗老板</span></strong>。7月17日，罗老板结合抖音、微博等平台的开屏广告、热搜，以及明星助力等玩法为当日的直播造势，直播间则推出1分钱的酸菜鱼、1500台iPhone 13等丰厚福利引流，数据显示，<strong>直播当天共吸引到9098.59w人次观看，单场GMV高达2.51亿</strong>，一炮打响趣店的名号。</p>
<p class="pgc-img" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://mp.toutiao.com/mp/agw/article_material/open_image/get?code=YWU2Zjc1NTY5NDA1NDcyYjdiNTU5MWRiZDNiMzlkYjcsMTY1OTUzNzM5OTEyOA==" data-ic="false" data-width="1080" data-height="473" data-ic-uri referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="pgc-img-caption"></span></p>
<h4 style="text-align: center; background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;" data-track="23"><span style="color: #9b9b9b; --tt-darkmode-color: #9B9B9B;">▲ 新抖-直播详情页</span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="25">罗老板使用“钞能力”在抖音一战成名，但因其创办校园贷等经历，短短十余天风评就已翻车，从上市公司CEO变为卷走人民血汗钱的骗子，详情见《从狂卖2.5亿，到遭全网痛骂，趣店罗老板只用了13天》。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="27">目前，@趣店罗老板账号已<strong>改名为@趣店预制菜</strong>，淡化了企业创始人的存在感，但从该账号8月2日的直播仅10.33w的销售额来看，老板翻车对品牌形象的影响力还是不小。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="29">综合带货榜单之外，细分领域的直播带货情况如何？从新抖的<strong><span style="color: #ff7800; --tt-darkmode-color: #FF7800;">[直播带货风向]</span></strong>中可以看到，上月以<strong>服装鞋帽</strong>、<strong>食品饮料</strong>、<strong>美妆护肤</strong>、<strong>家居生活</strong>、<strong>钟表配饰</strong>五个品类的商品贡献超过80%的销售额。</p>

<p class="pgc-p" style="text-align: center; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="90"><strong>热门商品榜</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="38"><strong>去黑头鼻贴热销2118w</strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="40">就热销商品来说，纸杯、纸巾等日常用品，鼻贴、面膜等个护产品，以及猪肉脯、火锅底料等食品调料在平台畅销，价格多在1元内，作为直播间的福利引流款产品上架。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="42">具体到单品，通过<strong><span style="color: #ff7800; --tt-darkmode-color: #FF7800;">[热门商品排行]</span></strong>发现，TOP30单品中有7款产品均是<strong>“去黑头粉刺撕拉式鼻贴”</strong>，上月累计的抖音<strong>总销量预估在2118.55w</strong>。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="44">商品基本以直播形式售出，如榜一的鼻贴来自小店：OULIQI欧丽琦旗舰店，<strong>一个月关联476场直播</strong>，@晨泉集团CEO陈立、@晨泉创始人何胜强等达人均在带货。</p>

<p class="pgc-p" style="text-align: center; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="92"><strong>品牌自播榜</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="60"><strong>服装品牌推秋装月销7621w</strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="62"><a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E7%9B%B4%E6%92%AD%E7%94%B5%E5%95%86" target="_blank">直播电商</a>行业中的品牌入局者愈来愈多，品牌自播已成为企业展示品牌形象、推广优质产品的常规渠道，基于此新抖也推出<strong><span style="color: #ff7800; --tt-darkmode-color: #FF7800;">[品牌自播榜]</span></strong>，展示有自播动作的品牌在平台的带货情况，榜单显示，<strong>TOP30品牌店的带货销售额均在2600w以上</strong>。</p>
<p class="pgc-p" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="64">下面是带货销售额TOP30主播的排名情况：</p>

<p class="pgc-p" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="93"><strong><span style="color: #ff7800; --tt-darkmode-color: #FF7800;">@Teenie Weenie官方旗舰店</span></strong>以<strong>7621.18w的带货销售额</strong>位于榜单第二。直播间每日开播3-4次，场均直播时长3.79小时，基本可以从全时段覆盖更多的用户群体。</p>
