<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; letter-spacing: 1px; visibility: visible;">直播电商巨大红利的催促下，抖音电商高速内卷已经成为不争的现实，而平台愈加严苛的规则，让更多的商家在内卷的道路上越走越远。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; letter-spacing: 1px; visibility: visible;">立志于打造出线上商超，抖音电商平台对商家的直播时长也有考核，一般来说，直播时长越长，平台的流量倾斜也会越多，有效延展直播时长，努力打造成“日不落直播”或许能承接更多流量。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; letter-spacing: 1px; visibility: visible;">同时，刚入局或者还处于冷启动阶段的商家，为避免与头部、腰部主播争抢黄金时间段流量，转而向仍有转化空间的凌晨时间段，乃至不间断直播，或许是更为简单直接的冷启策略。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; background-color: #ffffff; text-align: center; line-height: inherit; visibility: visible; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; visibility: visible;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 20px; color: #6a49c5; visibility: visible;">直播间开启超长待机模式</span></strong></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; background-color: #ffffff; text-align: center; line-height: inherit; visibility: visible; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; visibility: visible;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 20px; color: #6a49c5; visibility: visible;">换时间为收益</span></strong> </p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; letter-spacing: 1px;">一年前的9月，@交个朋友直播间 首个宣布实行 7x24 小时直播制。通过延长直播时间去提高其它主播的出镜频率，以减小直播间对罗永浩个人IP的过度依赖。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; letter-spacing: 1px;">据报道，罗永浩的直播时长占比降低至3%，实现GMV占公司总GMV的比例不到5%。这证明，即使没有罗永浩，交个朋友直播间 也能正常运转。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; letter-spacing: 1px;">据壁虎看看数据显示，近7天（8月7日-13日），@交个朋友直播间 带货16场，累计直播销售额近6000万。而且该直播间是带货榜TOP10的常客之一，销售成绩可见一斑。</span></p>
