<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>如今，女性在社会中扮演的角色越来越多，也越来越重要。对于职场女性来说，平衡家庭和工作是一件非常困难的事，除了要照顾家庭，还得兼顾忙碌的工作，往往分身乏术。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>当带娃上班成为一种新的潮流时，职场妈妈背后的心酸和无奈应该被更多地看到和关注。<br></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>基于这一社会现实，<strong><a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E6%96%B9%E5%A4%AA%E7%B1%B3%E5%8D%9A" target="_blank">方太米博</a>拍了一支关于新手妈妈的品牌片，摒弃了传统的叙事手法，将妈妈的带娃经历职场化，包装成了一次述职的主题报告</strong>，从另类的视角，去诠释妈妈的不易。</span></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;">1</h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><strong>带娃经历职场化</strong></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong>诠释妈妈的伟大</strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>对于职场人来说，掌握述职报告是必备技能，它突出个人或者所在职位对于公司或者团队有怎样的贡献。目的是为了总结过去，思考未来，以更好的姿态迎接新的挑战。</span><span> </span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>但是如果将妈妈的带娃经历做成述职报告，将会怎样呈现？</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>视频中的新手妈妈将述职报告分为两部分，第一部分是「KPI指标内容及达成情况」，第二部分是「工作亮点」。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>妈妈给自己设立了三项KPI，第一项是给孩子编辫子，掌握五种辫子的打法；</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/175736711563285080d65869.12097917.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span> </span><span>第二项是考核对孩子发脾气的次数；</span><span> </span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/185207169563285081446b34.74445646.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span> </span><span>第三项是给孩子做健康餐，掌握三十道宝宝健康饮食菜谱。</span><span> </span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/45287329963285082259b53.57438710.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span> </span><span>当妈妈给自己设定个“带娃KPI”，为了培养出一个满级幼崽，就得付出双倍的努力。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在工作亮点部分，可以看到妈妈为了让孩子更好地成长，不仅给孩子补充各种营养素，让孩子一年内身高增长了8厘米，而且坚持每周产出照片50张，记录孩子的成长，最厉害的是让孩子在半年内荣获20朵小红花。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/184321527263285082d446b9.86444563.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span> </span><span>但基于上半年对孩子发了3次脾气，扣了15分，妈妈最后自评分为85分，直言自己做得还不够好。</span><span>在面对考核官亮出评分板时，明显能感受到妈妈的紧张；</span><span> </span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/110788527563285083cc2f80.61381041.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span> </span></strong><strong><span>米博洞察职场女性的困境，将妈妈的带娃经历做成述职报告，让叙事手法变得新颖，成功勾起职场人和新手妈妈的情感共鸣。</span></strong></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><strong><span>2</span></strong></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong>妈妈的爱虽有瑕疵</strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong>却不会因琐事减分</strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在短片的立意上，并没有鼓励妈妈去调节负面情绪，减少对孩子发脾气的次数，而是让孩子来打分，给予妈妈暖心回馈。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>妈妈也是第一次当妈妈，虽然不完美，但真的很努力。很多新手妈妈们第一做妈妈时，也在学习如何做好一个妈妈。<br></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>就像视频中的妈妈一样，学着给孩子做各种营养餐，让孩子努力长个，也学着给孩子编出好看的辫子，为孩子拍美美的照片。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>可即使这样，她依然觉得自己做得不够好，有时候做好了饭，孩子还会吵着要吃外卖；工作忙起来的时候没有耐心，容易对着孩子发脾气......</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/81314293763285084a55717.62803993.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/30282896563285085cc9093.32626271.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span> </span><span>原本以为会被考核官打低分，但是考核官却将这个决定权交给了孩子，因为只有孩子才是最懂妈妈的人</span><span>，别人没办法评价妈妈的付出，最后孩子给出了满分。</span><span> </span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/83565618163285086b66aa0.57296429.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>那些在妈妈眼中的“不完美”，可是在孩子眼里，妈妈就是最棒的。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/14293388126328508786b799.82734367.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span> </span><span>于</span><span>是，短片也提出了slogan：</span><strong>「妈妈，你值得满分。」</strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>当人们成为母亲的那一刻，总是希望自己可以做得更好，<strong>但爱不会因为琐事而减分，因为很爱，让妈妈少了信心，也因为爱，让妈妈值得满分。</strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/10022001486328508839ae22.13147728.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span> </span><span>在短片结尾，米博以真实镜头展示了人类幼崽们对妈妈的赞美，赞美妈妈的每一份付出。</span><span>米博想要传达的品牌主张是：</span><strong>「爱让妈妈值得最好的礼赞」</strong><span>。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/695869628632850888d5a04.35882808.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span> </span><span>这样的品牌主张，彰显出品牌的人文情怀、价值观和社会责任感。</span></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span>3</span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong>解放女性的双手</strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong>引领厨电新时代</strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在消费升级大背景下，消费者购买产品不再只是看中功能性，<strong>产品背后的情感因素往往比利益更容易打动人。</strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>为产品创造一个深入人心的故事，进而打动消费者“柔软”的内心，最后促进他们购买，是品牌惯用的营销策略。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>米博这支短片的情感因素是：妈妈们太辛苦了，又要照顾家人又要平衡工作，<strong>米博产品可以解放妈妈的双手，让她们时间去做自己想做的事，帮助她们变成自己最想要成为的样子。</strong><br></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>因此，米博这支片子可以通过软植入，自然带出产品。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>当妈妈为做营养餐犯愁时，米博蒸烤箱可以轻松搞定；当孩子把颜料弄得满地都是时，米博洗地机轻松帮妈妈解决问题。从生活出发，用智能科技为当代家庭创造幸福。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/97532957263285088e457c4.63181037.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/61309714863285089c50964.95836954.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>当下许多90、95 后的新兴消费群体已经成为厨电消费的主力人群。他们虽然缺乏烹饪经验，但却对此充满好奇与热情，米博多功能烹饪机的优势就是在一机多用的前提下，还可以多场景使用。让下厨不再成为一种负担，让消费者可以享受纯粹的烹饪乐趣。</span><span> </span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>这正契合了米博家电的角色定位。米博是方太推出的新生活方式品牌，立志成为高端智能生活电器领导者 ，为亿万家庭带来全新生活解决方案，打造健康有品位的新生活方式。通过这支片子所展示出的是米博可以成为妈妈日常生活中的好帮手，让妈妈们没有后顾之忧。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>此前，方太曾推出过《妈妈的时间机器》系列广告，鼓励妈妈们走出厨房，把时间花费在自己的梦想上，把洗碗、洗果蔬的琐碎家务交给方太。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>而方太米博这支广告，<strong>由《妈妈的时间机器》的原班人马打造，通过“职场述职”的创意形式，传递妈妈的爱值得满分的诉求。进一步诠释方太集团“因爱伟大”的<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E5%93%81%E7%89%8C%E7%90%86%E5%BF%B5" target="_blank">品牌理念</a>。</strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>愿妈妈们被这个世界温柔以待。</span></p>
