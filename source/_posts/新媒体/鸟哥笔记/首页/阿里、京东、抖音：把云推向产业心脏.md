<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="4"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="5"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;">腾讯关停企鹅电竞、看点快报、数字藏品业务“幻核”；阿里盒马鲜生、大润发以及旗下家居品牌homearch均有关店，社区团购业务十荟团全面撤退；<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E6%8A%96%E9%9F%B3" target="_blank">抖音</a>关停独立站项目Dmonstudio，Tiktok则收缩业务，将重点转向部分东南亚和中东市场。</span></p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="6"> </p>

<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="8"> </p>

<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="10"> </p>

<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="12"> </p>

<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="14"> </p>

<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="16"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="17"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;">当前产业端和企业端正在弹性复苏，其中存在大量可挖掘的机会，供大厂们施展拳脚。</span></p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="18"> </p>

<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="20"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="21"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;">疫情反复，企业支出态度谨慎，小微企业更在意经营存续，大中企业更在意稳定增长，但目前宏观经济复苏是大趋势，社零数据逐渐回暖。《中国小微经营者调查报告》显示，2022Q2小微经营者平均营业额为13.6万，比2022Q1高出1.3万元，环比增加10%。</span></p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="22"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="23"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;">从全局来看，企业端的机会主要聚焦于三个方面。</span></p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="24"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="25"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-weight: bold;">第一，财务综合管理意识和必要性的提升。</span>外部经营情况不确定性多，企业管理财税的需求增加，有较大现金流的传统企业，以及回款账期长的小微企业，是财税管理数字化服务目前的主要需求方。</span></p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="26"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="27"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;">加之国家大规模留抵退税政策加速实施，财务管理能力薄弱的企业对财税能力的建设更加敏感。</span></p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="28"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="29"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-weight: bold;">第二，数字化中台成为企业级新基建。</span>对大中企业来说，单纯上云已经无法满足长期的业务发展需求，将企业业务、资源、组织全面进行数字化和智能化改造，才能从基因层面塑造企业的长期竞争力。</span></p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="30"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="31"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;">建立数字中台，为企业定制数字化经营管理系统，将ERP、CRM、OA集成提供综合服务，对大中企业，特别是具备领先能力的企业，有着极高的战略意义。</span></p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="32"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="33"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-weight: bold;">第三，前沿科技制造产业走向全球化。</span>目前我国以光伏、动力电池为主的碳中和产业逐渐在全球竞争中占据主导优势，产业链企业要充分走出去捕获更多海外订单，需要扎实的数字化和智慧化能力支撑，比如高强度的IaaS基础设施、高安全性的网络防护能力、更高效的获客系统。</span></p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="34"> </p>

<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="36"> </p>

<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="59"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="60"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;">阿里云在十年之际曾立“不做saas，被集成”的生态定位，钉钉今年3月也公开表明“只做基础能力平台”，做好分内之事一直是阿里在面向B端时反复强调的价值观。</span></p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="61"> </p>

<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="93"> </p>

<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="95"> </p>

<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="97"> </p>
