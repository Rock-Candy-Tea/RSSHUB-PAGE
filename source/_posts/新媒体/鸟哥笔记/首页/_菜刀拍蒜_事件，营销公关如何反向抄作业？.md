                        <p><span style="caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.533333px; text-align: justify; text-size-adjust: auto; background-color: #ffffff;">近几天，刀具的直播号疯狂拍蒜，隔着屏幕，都仿佛被满桌子的蒜泥味儿熏染到了。甚至有网友调侃，蒜都被迫涨价了。与此同时，身处在舆论漩涡的“张小泉”和一众“王麻子”们，频上热搜。</span></p>
<p><span style="caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.533333px; text-align: justify; text-size-adjust: auto; background-color: #ffffff;">那么在这场由“拍蒜”引发的热点事件背后，有哪些值得我们营销人挖掘的重点呢？下面一起来剖析看看。</span></p>

<p> </p>
<h1 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; caret-color: #222222; color: #212629; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 20px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: center; text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 30px; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; font-weight: 600;">01菜刀不能拍蒜“反常识口号刷爆网络</h1>
<p> </p>
<p><span style="caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.533333px; text-align: justify; text-size-adjust: auto; background-color: #ffffff;"><span style="letter-spacing: 0.533333px; text-size-adjust: auto;">起初，是广州一位市民王女士，用张小泉菜刀拍蒜，菜刀段成两截。已知所购买的是斩切刀，使用已经超90天质保期。随后王女士联系了张小泉菜刀售后客服。在王女士询问“这刀好离谱，我拍蒜子，它能断裂？”，而客服的回应是发了一张产品规定图和一句“不建议拍蒜哦”。并没有得到满意回复的王女士将经历发到网上后引发网友热议。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.533333px; text-align: justify; text-size-adjust: auto; background-color: #ffffff;"><span style="letter-spacing: 0.533333px; text-size-adjust: auto;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/5be0a5649505d2049b16e8c5479800d7.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0pt 24px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-size-adjust: auto; text-align: center; visibility: visible; line-height: 27px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span mp-original-font-size="15" mp-original-line-height="24" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #808080; letter-spacing: 0.4pt; background-color: #ffffff; font-size: 15px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; visibility: visible; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">图片来源：上游新闻</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0pt 24px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: justify; text-size-adjust: auto; visibility: visible; line-height: 27px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span mp-original-font-size="15" mp-original-line-height="24" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.4pt; background-color: #ffffff; font-size: 15px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; visibility: visible; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">“菜刀不能拍蒜”为什么会火呢？</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0pt 24px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: justify; text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 27px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span mp-original-font-size="15" mp-original-line-height="24" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.4pt; background-color: #ffffff; font-size: 15px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">怎么会有不能拍蒜的菜刀呢？在笔者对于菜刀的认知里，从普通的小家庭到玉盘珍羞直万钱的美食界，一把菜刀可行天下。切、片、拍、剁、剔等料理处理方法，一把菜刀足以完成。而这一把有点贵、但又不能拍蒜的刀是在太奇怪。不禁疑惑：贵且易损，菜刀也走奢侈品的路线了？</span></p>
<h1 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; caret-color: #222222; color: #212629; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 20px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: center; text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 30px; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; font-weight: 600;">02公关第一要义：真诚</h1>
<p> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0pt 24px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: justify; text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 27px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span mp-original-font-size="15" mp-original-line-height="24" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.4pt; background-color: #ffffff; font-size: 15px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">在这一事件中我们可以看到，在王女士提出质疑时，商家并没有第一时间进行安抚和解释，而是直接给出了商品的质保规定和一句提醒，仅此而已。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0pt 24px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: justify; text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 27px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span mp-original-font-size="15" mp-original-line-height="24" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.4pt; background-color: #ffffff; font-size: 15px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">随着话题的不断发酵，媒体及部分网友也纷纷问及张小泉客服此类问题，得到的答复话语，在这时才逐渐在走上正轨。从简单的一句“不建议拍蒜”，发展到一篇小作文（如下）。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0pt 24px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: justify; text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 27px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span mp-original-font-size="15" mp-original-line-height="24" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.4pt; background-color: #ffffff; font-size: 15px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">“亲，我们不建议使用张小泉菜刀去横拍大蒜、黄瓜哦。因为硬度越高的刀具约锋利，当刀具横拍后，刀面的受力面积会变大，刀面承受的压强变大，硬度较高的刀具无法跟随变形于是发生断裂。硬度越高的刀具开刃之后的保持度越好，锋利度越高，切东西越顺手，但这种刀具相对而言会比较脆，磕碰或者重力拍打就容易断裂，家庭使用实在想要拍蒜的，可以将刀具放在蒜上，用手掌轻压刀面来达到拍蒜的效果。”</span><span mp-original-font-size="15" mp-original-line-height="24" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; background-color: #ffffff; color: #808080; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.4pt; text-align: center; line-height: 24px;"></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 24px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-size-adjust: auto; text-align: center; line-height: 27px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><img src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/725ca10ec1cd92b9556c73597ba74e66.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 24px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-size-adjust: auto; text-align: center; line-height: 27px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span mp-original-font-size="14" mp-original-line-height="22" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 14px; color: #888888; line-height: 22px;">图片来源：知乎@刘欢</span><span mp-original-font-size="17" mp-original-line-height="27" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; text-align: justify; line-height: 27px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0pt 24px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: justify; text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 27px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span mp-original-font-size="15" mp-original-line-height="24" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.4pt; background-color: #ffffff; font-size: 15px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">对于通过网络销售的的品牌而言，客服就是公关和服务的第一道防线。客服团队没有很好的处理问题的公关知识，硬生生把一个小小的品控问题催生成了一个公关危机。如果在质疑的初期就给出合理的解释，积极处理，自然大事化小、小事化了。甚至，是否可以顺势推荐一款品牌内更适合拍蒜的刀，岂不是更好？</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0pt 24px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: justify; text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 27px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span mp-original-font-size="15" mp-original-line-height="24" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.4pt; background-color: #ffffff; font-size: 15px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">营销服务上，对外沟通必须以诚信为前提，只有企业能够把公众利益放在首位，真诚地与消费者及外部公众沟通，才更能得到对方的理解和认可。而此时企业如果在危机发生后避免回答或者故意隐藏大量信息， 舆论场自然会向负面、不信任的漩涡偏移。</span></p>
<h1 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; caret-color: #222222; color: #212629; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 20px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: center; text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 30px; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; font-weight: 600;">03危机处理第一要素：黄金24小时</h1>
<p> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0pt 24px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: justify; text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 27px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span mp-original-font-size="15" mp-original-line-height="24" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.4pt; background-color: #ffffff; font-size: 15px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">7月13日，“菜刀不能拍蒜”事件在网络发布、发酵，张小泉品牌方此时并未正面公开回应。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0pt 24px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: justify; text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 27px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span mp-original-font-size="15" mp-original-line-height="24" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.4pt; background-color: #ffffff; font-size: 15px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">7月14日，停更了近一个月的老牌刀具品牌“王麻子”开启了直播，主播一边拍蒜一边介绍刀具，甚至在购物链接中也加上了“能拍蒜”字样。这一操作直接创下单日直播21小时44分钟、观看人次达45万的纪录。随后一系列刀具品牌争相模仿。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0pt 24px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 27px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span mp-original-font-size="15" mp-original-line-height="24" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.4pt; background-color: #ffffff; font-size: 15px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/ff141708fa635690cdd087976b75a6fc.png" alt width="576" height="576" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
