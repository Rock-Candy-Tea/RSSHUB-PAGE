<h2 id="toc-1" style="font-size: 18px; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); padding-left: 12px; background-position: 0px 5px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; line-height: 30px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; background-size: 8px 20px;">01 为什么要做私域呢？</h2>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">1）降低获客成本</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">开发一个新客户的成本是维护一个老客户成本的10倍，降低成本，不代表企业不用花钱就能带来很多客户，其实只是把投入的成本进行重新分配。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">打个比方，之前每个月花10w块去投广告，给你带来100个客户，现在你同样每个月投入10w，但是把这个钱花在老客户或者员工绩效，给你带来300个新客户，获客成本降低2/3。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">2）延长用户生命周期</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">通过精细化管理用户，提供个人化运营和惊喜化服务，清晰每个用户的痛点和需求，增强跟用户之间的粘度，让用户持续复购。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">3）高效管理客户</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">通过搭建VIP社群，把对我们产品/服务/品牌有一定认知度和认可度的用户，通过社群一对多进行管理，更加高效。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">4）优化服务流程提高用户的满意度</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">一切商业的本质都是品质+服务，只有把服务做到极致，用户才会对你产生好感，通过用户动向设计服务细节，提供满意度，增强品牌与客户的粘度，这是裂变的基础，服务没做好，上来就想裂变，必死。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">5）扩大品牌传播</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">通过公众号、视频号、小程序等触点，扩大品牌的传播度，当然，必须以服务作为支撑。只有好的产品/服务，才值得被传播。</p>
<h2 id="toc-2" style="font-size: 18px; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); padding-left: 12px; background-position: 0px 5px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; line-height: 30px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; background-size: 8px 20px;">02 存在问题</h2>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">我相信，做过微信运营的朋友，</span><span style="font-weight: bold;">都有下面的深刻感受：</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">1）</span><span style="font-weight: bold;">不知道怎么吸粉</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">很多人，不知道怎么吸引精准客户，平时主动到各个群里加好友，吸引来的不是发广告，就是僵尸粉。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">记住，被动吸引来的才是你的精准客户，微信好友不是越多越好，而是越精准越好，只有提升你的微信好友质量，你才能提升你的收入。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">2）</span><span style="font-weight: bold;">不知道怎么互动</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">很多人，要么一加上好友，就把广告全发一遍；要么直接把好友邀请进群，然后疯狂地在群里发产品广告；要么群建好了，不知道怎么互动，就放那边，群活跃度很差，很快群变得死气沉沉了。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">记住，互动，可以帮助你跟客户建立粘性、强关系，激活你的老客户，高效的互动，会给你带来高效的转化率。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">3）</span><span style="font-weight: bold;">不知道怎么成交</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">很多人，拥有了好几个微信号，上万微信好友，但是不知道怎么快速变现成交，平时想成交对方，就群发一遍广告信息，能成交一单算一单。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">记住，没有信任，就无法成交，成交是有流程的，一步到位的成交，只会把你的客户吓跑。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">4）</span><span style="font-weight: bold;">不知道怎么快速裂变</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">很多人，好几年微信好友就那么些人，客户一个都没增长，客户没增长，业绩就不会有增长，你再好的产品也卖不出去。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">所以裂变是整个社群发售中最重要的一环，客户裂变起来了，你才能卖出更多产品，赚钱就会变得更轻松。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">5）</span><span style="font-weight: bold;">不知道怎么设计社群发售流程</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">现在80%的企业、实体店老板，还是停留在过去老旧的经营模式。要么坐在店里等客户上门，成交一单算一单；要么就邀请客户加微信，有活动、新产品，就群发一遍广告。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">我相信你曾经在别人群里被成交过，但是估计你从来没有认真思考过你是如何被成交的。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">如果你研究透了那些社群发售操盘手的整个社群发售流程，那么我相信未来你想要在线上卖货、批量成交你的客户，将是轻而易举的事情。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">毫不夸张地说，如果你仔细观察一场火爆的社群发售流程，90%以上是遵循下方这张社群发售导图落地执行的！</p>

<h2 id="toc-3" style="font-size: 18px; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); padding-left: 12px; background-position: 0px 5px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; line-height: 30px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; background-size: 8px 20px;">03 社群自动化运营6步曲</h2>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">今天我就为您全面复盘我</span>操盘100多场社群发售活动，总结的社群自动化运营6步曲。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">只要你今天认真学习，我相信你也能学会！至少可以打败目前微信上90%以上的人！</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; line-height: inherit; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-image: none; background-position: 0px 6px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><strong>步骤1：筛选吸粉</strong></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">我曾经做过一个测试，就是用软件去各个群里加微信好友，发现加来的80%都是广告的，还有一大部分是僵尸粉，你发什么，他们都无动于衷。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">所以，懂事长还是建议各位，如果你用软件加粉的话，不仅吸引不来精准粉，可能还会被腾讯限制了各个功能权限。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">当然还有另外20%可能是你想要的精准粉，但是因为没有信任感，你也是非常难成交他们。除非他们有强烈的需求欲望，或者你有超强的成交技术。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">真正的高手，重来不会主动去吸粉，而是被动吸粉！只有主动加你的人，80%都是精准粉！当然里面有很多用软件加你的。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">今天简单跟大家总结一下，吸粉的几种最常见，最有效的方法：</p>

<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">获取知识：群内分享有你想要的知识</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">获取福利：群内会经常送福利，错过了就没了</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">获取价值：群内价值很高，不能错过</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">名师课程：群内名师大咖坐镇分享干货</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">混群吸粉：为了混群吸粉，多露脸</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">参与观摩：参与观摩团队/别人的方案、方法、指导</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">高级圈子：加入付费圈子、高级圈子</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">所以，不管你建群为了什么，都离不开一句话：就是提供别人想要的。提供别人想要的知识、福利、价值、课程、吸粉、方法、圈子。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">我们这次社群，采用的是签到送福利的方法，互动效果还可以，但是群内信息太多了，所以签到送福利这种方式，只能在正式分享干货之前做。</p>
