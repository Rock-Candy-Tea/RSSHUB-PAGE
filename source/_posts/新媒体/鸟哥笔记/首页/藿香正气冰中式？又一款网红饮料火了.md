<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>网红饮品的内卷，真是越来越让人看不懂了，就连曾经苦到喝不下去的中药，现在也成了年轻人追捧的爆款饮料。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>这不，继“一整根”人参水之后，年轻人开始跟风喝藿香正气口服液了。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>它还有个非常高大上的名字：</span><strong><span>藿香正气冰中式。</span></strong><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/19890403046315e992934733.32417542.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><strong><span><span>▲</span><span> </span><span>图源：小红书</span></span></strong></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/5611320262b974392829e0.57743105.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></strong></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><strong><span>“一身正气”的冰中式</span></strong></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>大概是今年夏天实在太热了，防中暑的藿香正气口服液销量猛增。对于年轻人来说，单纯喝藿香正气口服液难以下咽，但赋予藿香正气口服液网红饮品的属性，就不仅好喝，而且上头了。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在这种背景下，“藿香正气冰中式”诞生了。它的初版配方很简单，将藿香正气口服液加入到有冰块的水中，就可以直接饮用。由于很像冰美式咖啡，所以被命名为“冰中式”。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>而升级版，则是将藿香正气口服液直接加入到冰美式中，把藿香正气口服液和咖啡的味道结合在一起，同时达到提神醒脑和清热防暑的目的。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/2857250966315e993da7a72.79194828.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span>▲</span><span> </span><span>图源：小红书</span><span></span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>在小红书，有关“冰中式”的笔记已经达到了2W＋，足见“藿香正气冰中式”在年轻人中的风靡程度。不少人称其为“中西合璧”、“解暑神器”、“一身正气的冰中式”。</span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>“藿香正气冰中式”刮起的这股风潮，还吹火了瑞幸咖啡。在网友晒出的“藿香正气冰中式”中，我们会发现，瑞幸咖啡的出镜率非常高。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/9172524756315e9949834c1.25557445.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span>▲</span><span> </span><span>图源：微博@孤沽</span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>网络上，还流传着一张瑞幸咖啡的宣传海报，让很多人以为“藿香正气冰中式”是瑞幸咖啡的新品。但有媒体向瑞幸求证，得到了相关工作人员的否认。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/5298958656315e9977d8029.90481456.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span>▲</span><span> </span><span>图源：抖音新民晚报</span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/38794396662b974394a3da6.63273158.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></strong></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><strong><span>“西咖中做”成趋势</span></strong></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>人们由“藿香正气冰中式”联想到瑞幸咖啡，并非没有道理。在社交平台上，搜索相关关键词会发现，瑞幸咖啡的不少饮品会被网友形容为“有<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E8%97%BF%E9%A6%99%E6%AD%A3%E6%B0%94%E6%B0%B4" target="_blank">藿香正气水</a>的味道”，这些饮品基本指向瑞幸咖啡推出的夏日冰咖系列。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/16879153156315e9982ab786.61302256.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span>▲</span><span> </span><span>图源：微博网友</span><span></span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>荔枝气泡、橘金气泡、椰青冰萃、龙眼冰萃……尽管网友对这些饮品多是吐槽声，但瑞幸咖啡也算无意中和“藿香正气冰中式”这一网红饮品接轨了。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>事实上，瑞幸这种“鲜果＋咖啡”的创新组合，就是中式咖啡的一种。</span><span><strong>西咖中做，已经成为咖啡品牌的一大发展趋势。</strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>这一点，从茶颜悦色最近推出的子品牌</span><strong><span>“鸳央咖啡”</span></strong><span>表现得更加明显。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/11715781166315e999114050.24408161.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span>▲</span><span> </span><span>图源：鸳央咖啡官方微博</span><span></span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在品牌LOGO上，鸳央咖啡提出了</span><strong><span>“西学东渐，咖啡中式”</span></strong><span>的宣言。为此，茶颜悦色打出了“茶咖”的概念，即在咖啡饮品中加入“茶”作为基底，主推“咖啡+茶”的新中式饮品。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>饮品的命名更是充满了东方特色，比如空山新雨后、辣妹子辣、事宽则圆、蓉儿等。而辣妹子辣，则是在咖啡的雪顶上撒辣椒片，浓浓“无辣不欢”的湖南地方特色。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在场景上，鸳央咖啡的门店设计同样古色古韵，充满了中国风。“竹子”、“瓦片”、“蒲团”等文化符号，让鸳央咖啡可以有效区别于星巴克的西式风格。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>这种国潮风的咖啡店场景设计，鸳央咖啡并非独一份。</span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>位于深圳的</span><strong><span>红盏咖啡</span></strong><span>，最近在网络走红。门店设计上，满眼中国红的红色屋檐、百子柜；产品特色上，把黑芝麻糊、汤圆、豆腐花加入咖啡中，命名是“一根绿豆冰棍儿”、“黑芝麻糊你一脸”、“吃我豆腐花”、“一杯黑糖山水画”等。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/3812176006315e99ad9f069.69973587.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span>▲</span><span> </span><span>图源：公众号在创新</span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>这些国风风格，让红盏咖啡享有“国风咖啡馆”的名号，最高日销达700杯＋，不少顾客排队一个多小时，才能喝到一杯“国风特调”。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>位于东莞的</span><span><strong>竹隐公馆咖啡店</strong></span><span>，则是将门店开在了拥有百年历史的古宅中，充满了历史的厚重感。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/19246116006315e99cf0f4d6.84168841.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span>▲</span><span> </span><span>图源：大众点评</span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>无疑，无论产品还是场景，作为舶来品的咖啡，越来越中式化。</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/46978155362b9743fae3289.16804197.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></strong></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><strong><span>走向魔幻</span></strong></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>随着中国消费者文化自信的回归，西咖中做必然成为潮流趋势。但是，</span><strong><span>在“中式食材＋咖啡”的组合混搭中，也越来越疯魔。</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>就比如风靡网络的“藿香正气冰中式”，当年轻人都在争相效仿时，</span><strong><span>“医生建议勿跟风藿香正气冰中式”</span></strong><span>冲上了微博热搜，给年轻人浇了一盆冷水。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/5799848176315e99e03f9a3.14983486.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span>▲</span><span> </span><span>图源：微博</span><span></span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>藿香正气口服液说明书上有明确提示，服药期间需要忌生冷。否则，可能会降低药效，甚至会加重中暑等症状。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>有的人在一杯冰中式中，会加入好几支藿香正气口服液，这样很有可能每日剂量超标，出现副作用，没病也会喝出病来。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>目前，在小红书“冰中式”的搜索下，已经出现了薯队长</span><strong><span>“用药请遵医嘱，按需服用”</span></strong><span>的温馨提示。不少网友表示，“美食创新可以冷门，但不能邪门”、“好魔幻的吃法”、“暗黑料理”……</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/14062981246315e99e42bd60.90575156.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span>▲</span><span> </span><span>图源：小红书</span><span></span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>但由“藿香正气冰中式”引发的咖啡创新喝法却并没有止步。</span><strong><span>有人以此为灵感，将藿香正气口服液替换成了小柴胡或者风油精，加入到冰块中，做出了不一样的冰中式；有咖啡店还发明出了“咸菜咖啡”、“菜脯咖啡”。</span></strong><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/18692839836315e99eaf1233.12286547.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span>▲</span><span> </span><span>图源：抖音</span><span></span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>这种“万物皆可加咖啡”的混搭玩法，让人不由想到曾经“万物皆可联名”的跨界营销。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>国潮跨界的风潮，让李宁、旺旺、老干妈、大白兔奶糖、卫龙等老字号国货品牌走红网络，赢得了年轻消费者的喜爱，然而，随着杜蕾斯和喜茶联名翻车、马应龙卖口红引发吐槽、南京“洗衣液奶茶”被下架，品牌跨界不断挑战着消费者底线，营销价值逐渐失灵。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/9703789176315e99f276380.34988990.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
