<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>从1999年创立开始，</span><span>“<strong>买正版书上<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E5%BD%93%E5%BD%93%E7%BD%91" target="_blank">当当网</a></strong>”</span><span>，是喜爱阅读的网友们一致共识。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>树立了支持正版平台形象的当当网，最近还借华为与陈春花事件，喊话字节跳动创始人张一鸣：</span><span><strong>希望不被头条和抖音上的盗版书商淹没</strong></span><span>。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/23413828562d5f697428013.14169507.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>然而，就在喊话后不久，一则#</span><span><strong>女子称买9本书全盗版起诉当当</strong></span><span>#的消息冲上热搜。让这个曾有可能PK<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E4%BA%9A%E9%A9%AC%E9%80%8A" target="_blank">亚马逊</a>的网上图书交易平台，又一次站在舆论中心。</span></p>
<h1 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 20px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: center;">01</h1>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>7月14日，河南郑州的朱女士称在当当网买到盗版书，多次通过电话和当当APP上的客服系统沟通，皆无人应答。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>一气之下，朱女士将当当网告上法庭，认为当当网虚假宣传构成欺诈，要求三倍赔偿并赔礼道歉。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/191398578562d5f697aebcb4.87773579.png" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>众所周知，当当网打出的</span><strong><span>“金字招牌”</span></strong><span>就是只卖正版。这则“卖盗版”的新闻曝出，让信任平台的网友很受伤。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/152068341862d5f69911b463.54606923.png" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/122331281862d5f699385747.96611240.png" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>随着舆论发酵，网友纷纷吐槽——在当当网买到“疑似”盗版书的，不止一个朱女士。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/121642628162d5f6998b9202.52699127.png" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/140131245562d5f69a26ea37.65696504.png" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/160777770062d5f69a54c4e3.42515972.png" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>当然，也有网友指出，自己一直在当当自营店铺买书，从没买到盗版。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/85557905362d5f69a8f1f33.78349268.png" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>评论中提及最多的，是当当的服务和包装。</span><span>比如，原价买到没有塑封的旧书，四百多元买的书寄过来包裹全烂了等等。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/161897487662d5f69ace3351.02928557.png" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/146565407562d5f69b043fd4.49710065.png" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/113554094962d5f69b3367b7.98491593.png" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在黑猫投诉平台上，关于当当网的投诉有3000多条。除了盗版之外，也大多集中在服务、物流等问题。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/78002321162d5f69b650351.78905931.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>看来，当当网确实得好好反思，为什么近年来在市场上声势渐弱了。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/76460816062d5f69b8c8e76.86733124.png" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span> </span></p>
<h1 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); font-size: 20px; color: #212629; text-align: center; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;">02</h1>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>上一次当当网出现在舆论热议中，应该是2020年。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>创始人<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E6%9D%8E%E5%9B%BD%E5%BA%86" target="_blank">李国庆</a>和俞渝夫妻的矛盾升级，整整10个月占据社媒头条，从摔杯事件到抢公章，各路惊心情节悉数上演。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>一时间，众多媒体争相报道。网友们尽情吃瓜的同时，顺带回顾了当当网一次次错失良机的发展历程。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/31441086762d5f69bba6cf4.35384685.jpeg" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span><span>●</span><span>图源：当当网微博</span></span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>1995年，贝索斯在美国创立了网上图书交易平台——亚马逊。如今，亚马逊已经发展成为市值万亿的超级巨头。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>亚马逊成立四年后，想要打造“中国亚马逊”的李国庆夫妇，在多个知名风投的共同助力下，于1999年成功上线当当网。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>定位为</span><span><strong>中国最大图书资讯集成商和供应商</strong></span><span>的当当网，试图以国外现代图书的运营模式，结合先进的计算机网络技术，推动中国图书市场在“可供数目”和“网上书店”这两大板块的进程。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/203821570562d5f69c0741c3.64294969.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span>●</span><span>图源：当当网微博</span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>也正是在1999年，我们熟知的腾讯、搜狐、京东、阿里巴巴等平台，也先后开始了互联网领域的征战。</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>当时中国网民数量只有890万，与如今动辄十几亿的基数相较，用户规模小得可怜。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>可是，有着多年图书出版运营经验的李国庆，与毕业于纽约大学的俞渝“神雕侠侣”搭档，让当当网依托有限的用户基数，迅速成长为国内图书市场的头部平台。</span></p>
<h1 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); font-size: 20px; color: #212629; text-align: center; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;">03</h1>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>当当网成立仅一年，互联网领域就开始陷入破灭潮。根基不牢的互联网平台先后倒下，咬牙支撑的当当网，在三年之后迎来了新的市场红利期。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>2003年，受非典影响足不出户的网友们，将原本在线下进行的各类消费放到线上，助推着电商平台的飞速发展。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在这波市场红利的带动下，发展势头正猛的当当网获得了第二轮融资。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/5562565262d5f69cb01183.43596332.jpeg" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span><span>●</span><span>图源：当当读书汇微博</span></span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>彼时的当当网，既有着资金支持，又有着用户数量增长的无限潜力，吸引了巨头亚马逊的关注。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>计划登陆中国的亚马逊表示，</span><strong><span>希望在一亿与十亿的估值之间，收购当当网。</span></strong><span>不过，被李国庆拒绝了。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>2010年，当当网作为中国电商第一股赴美上市，比京东和阿里两大电商巨头还早了四年。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/194481471262d5f69cd94811.53455965.jpeg" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span><span>●</span><span>图源：当当读书汇微博</span></span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>上市之后，</span><span>以图书品类打响知名度的当当网，遇到的最大障碍，是在图书之外的品类突破。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>面对京东、阿里等电商平台的全品类竞争，“粮草充足”的当当网看到了服饰、3C、化妆品等领域的市场容量，决定上线更为丰富的产品。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>同时，也在被京东挤得几乎失去优势的图书领域，与亚马逊等巨头掀起了价格战。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/17619812762d5f69d13ce61.77305526.jpeg" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span><span>●</span><span>图源：当当读书汇微博</span></span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>一通混战，让当当网连续六个季度亏损，每个季度高达1亿。直到2014年才恢复盈利，实现了79.6亿的营收。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>只不过，当时京东的营收，已是当当网的18倍。</span></p>
<h1 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); font-size: 20px; color: #212629; text-align: center; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;">04</h1>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>2014年，腾讯曾想将旗下电商平台交给当当，并提供微信导流等支持服务，但李国庆夫妇拒绝了腾讯想占股33%的“企图”。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>有数据显示，与腾讯旗下电商平台展开合作的京东，在微信的流量加持下，仅2015年第一季度，就增加了770万活跃用户。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>当时，整个当当网的活跃用户也只是千万左右。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/136423367562d5f69d442e74.79067702.jpeg" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span><span>●</span><span>图源：当当读书汇微博</span></span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>拒绝亚马逊，又拒绝腾讯，与壮大机遇擦肩而过的当当网，上市六年后退市时，股价已跌至发行价的60%。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在此过程中，李国庆曾尝试开辟“新当当”，</span><strong><span>试图在自出版、电子书、实体书店等业务上掀起浪花。</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>可是，当李国庆夫妇之间的矛盾成为热议焦点时，当当网为了鼓励全民阅读展开的“网络书香节”“百万捐书计划”等创举，几乎在网友们围观“拉扯”中被淡忘。</span></p>
