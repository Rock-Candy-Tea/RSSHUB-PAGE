<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">随着直播达人数和开播频次的同步增长，带货直播热度持续攀升，2022年上半年带货直播流量同比增长53%，用户的看播需求仍然有可挖掘的潜力。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">但在直播内容的生产端，增长速度更加迅猛，流量增长速度逐渐难以匹配高速增长的开播场次，流量竞争愈发激烈，出现了供不应求的现状。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span id="article-img-14" class="a-image" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"><img style="margin: 0px auto 20px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: middle; border: none; max-width: 100%; display: block; background: url('//static.yidianzixun.com/apps/black_mamba_web_v1.0.0.177/black_mamba_web_main_pic_placeholder.png') 50% center no-repeat #e6e6e6;" src="https://i1.go2yd.com/image.php?url=YD_cnt_33_01DWyQ5Qv5Qn" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>

<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">直播：流量竞争加剧</strong></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">流量竞争加剧反映到每一场直播上的结果是：单场直播的平均流量持续走低；用户在直播间的平均停留时长也在下滑，从2021年1月的126秒，下降到2022年6月的86秒。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">其中，2021年10月-11月平台双十一大促期间，流量竞争最为明显，平均停留时长达到历史最低的58秒。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">停留时长下降的大背景下，直播间转化流量的窗口期变短，直播间装潢、主播话术、直播玩法和排品都受到了更严峻的考验。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span id="article-img-15" class="a-image" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"><img style="margin: 0px auto 20px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: middle; border: none; max-width: 100%; display: block; background: url('//static.yidianzixun.com/apps/black_mamba_web_v1.0.0.177/black_mamba_web_main_pic_placeholder.png') 50% center no-repeat #e6e6e6;" src="https://i1.go2yd.com/image.php?url=YD_cnt_33_01DWyQ6cvRAY" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>

<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">直播：垂直直播间拉动变现效率</strong></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">2022年上半年，平台带货直播间的货品矩阵，朝着行业垂直化发展，行业垂直直播间的数量占比进一步提升；</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">行业垂直直播间数量占比达84%，一级类目垂直直播间数量占比达76%，同时，相对综合类直播间，垂直直播间表现出了更高的流量变现效率。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span id="article-img-16" class="a-image" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"><img style="margin: 0px auto 20px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: middle; border: none; max-width: 100%; display: block; background: url('//static.yidianzixun.com/apps/black_mamba_web_v1.0.0.177/black_mamba_web_main_pic_placeholder.png') 50% center no-repeat #e6e6e6;" src="https://i1.go2yd.com/image.php?url=YD_cnt_33_01DWyQ7pXkIT" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>

<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">直播：中小行业展现带货增长潜力</strong></p>
