<h1 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; text-align: center; line-height: 30px; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 20px; font-weight: 600;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 20px; color: #6a49c5;">全网围攻钟薛高</span></strong> </h1>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; letter-spacing: 1px;">7月初，钟薛高雪糕在31℃的室温下放置1小时仍保持固体形状的新闻，冲上了微博热搜。而后，又有网友爆料钟薛高“雪糕烧不化”。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; letter-spacing: 1px;">尽管钟薛高官方及时回应澄清，还有专家出面科普，但“不信任”的种子已经埋下，网络上针对钟薛高“产品配不上价格”、“是不是智商税”的讨论仍不断发酵。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; letter-spacing: 1px;">钟薛高从建立以来就紧紧打上了“奢侈品”的标签，要说“贵”是原罪，那市场上高价雪糕那么多，又为何是钟薛高被骂上热搜呢？</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; letter-spacing: 1px;">这里概括了两个主要原因：</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; letter-spacing: 1px; color: #6a49c5;">1，高价雪糕侵占了平价雪糕的渠道资源</span></strong></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; letter-spacing: 1px;"> </span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; letter-spacing: 1px;">定位中高端的钟薛高，匹配的也是高消费人群。在电商平台明码标价的情况下，消费者都十分清楚它的价格和定位，所以即使会被吐槽贵，也不会带来舆论上的危机。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; letter-spacing: 1px;">比如<a class="weapp_text_link" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; color: #576b95; text-decoration-line: none; -webkit-tap-highlight-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0); cursor: pointer; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;" href="https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s?__biz=MzU5NjMxODA5OQ==&mid=2247491217&idx=1&sn=d8290ef6c564b754616c80f1f2ee9795&chksm=fe65dc6dc912557b15b63f4d0f8af1c2063352f520ac959b6aaadecef91c3d6aae3ca2a245a2&token=1157803239&lang=zh_CN" data-miniprogram-appid="wx486ef245f10a196f" data-miniprogram-path="pages/index/index" data-miniprogram-nickname="壁虎看看抖数" data-miniprogram-type="text" data-miniprogram-servicetype>钟薛高</a>曾推出过一款定价66元的限定款“厄瓜多尔粉钻”雪糕，仅15小时，就在天猫平台售罄2万只。这款高价雪糕还让钟薛高收获了“雪糕界爱马仕”的称号。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 0em; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; text-align: center; line-height: 1.75em;"><img src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/9729ff38b7b66eaaef510772cc01f52c.png" alt width="1080" height="721" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; letter-spacing: 1px;">但是，当像钟薛高这样的高价雪糕走进线下平价小店，在不标明价格的情况下混进了普通雪糕里，就带来了背刺普通消费者的机会。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; letter-spacing: 1px;"> </span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; letter-spacing: 1px;">并且由于线下小店中的价格标识混乱等原因，进一步加重了这个矛盾。</span></p>

<p style="margin: 0px 8px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; letter-spacing: 1px;">中高端产品如果走“价格高端化、场景低端化”的路线，不仅侵占了同一类目的平价产品的渠道资源，在价格不明的情况下容易引发消费者不满。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;"> </p>

<p style="margin: 0px 8px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; letter-spacing: 1px;">这里举一个较为成功的例子，就是辣条品牌-卫龙。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 0em; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; text-align: center; line-height: 1.75em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; letter-spacing: 1px;">作为平价零食代表，卫龙在产品方面，引进了欧洲生产线，又打造了全自动化无菌生产车间，为食品质量提供了保障。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; letter-spacing: 1px;">在营销方面，卫龙先是推出简约大气的全新包装来贴合年轻消费者的审美需求，之后借助直播电商、平台热点，成功实现品牌的再次爆红。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; letter-spacing: 1px;">如此操作之下，卫龙成为中国知名度最高，也最受消费者喜爱的辣味休闲食品品牌。</span></p>

<p style="margin: 0px 8px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; letter-spacing: 1px;">无论是钟薛高还是雪莲冰块，都提醒着我们：品牌需要热度，更需要产品力。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; letter-spacing: 1px;">当依靠营销打天下的时代已经过去，当消费者不再愿意为新鲜感付费，高价的网红产品就必须接受来自消费者更加严格的监督。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; letter-spacing: 1px;">消费者为产品付出更高的价格，就会对产品提出更高的要求，而高端品牌就需要更严格的控制产品质量的稳定性。这是理所当然的。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px 8px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; letter-spacing: 1px;">不管是老牌国货还是新兴品牌，一个共同的认知是，它们都需要通过持续的投入和长期的沉淀，让品牌建立起足够的品牌效应，经得起时间的检验，从而让消费者愿意为“老牌情怀”或“品牌溢价”买单。</span></p>
