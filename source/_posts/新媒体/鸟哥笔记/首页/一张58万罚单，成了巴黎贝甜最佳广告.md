<section data-role="paragraph" data-width="92%" data-mpa-powered-by="yiban.io">
<section><span>“朋友们，还不去支持<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E5%B7%B4%E9%BB%8E%E8%B4%9D%E7%94%9C" target="_blank">巴黎贝甜</a>吗？”、“中秋一定要买点巴黎贝甜吃吃”、“以后面包就巴黎贝甜了”……</span></section>
<p> </p>
<section><span>最近几天，上海巴黎贝甜的门店快要被消费者买爆了。这是一场消费者自发形成的购物潮，有人称其为</span><strong><span>“报恩性消费”</span></strong><span>。</span></section>
<p> </p>
<section><span>它的导火线，则是一条关于巴黎贝甜被罚款58万元的热搜。</span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/61010596363173b042b0b02.57492470.png" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></section>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin-bottom: 25px;"><span>▲</span><span> </span><span>图源：微博</span></h4>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 18px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;">1</h2>
<section><span>根据天眼查显示，</span><span><strong>巴黎贝甜关联公司上海艾丝碧西食品有限公司，因违反食品安全法，被上海市市场监督管理局罚款58万余元。</strong></span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/171915578063173b0467a969.31531058.png" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></section>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin-bottom: 25px;"><span>▲</span><span> </span><span>图源：新浪财经</span></h4>
<section><span>至于处罚事由，我帮大家画一下重点：</span></section>
<p> </p>
<section><span>①当事人在疫情封控期间封闭了位于景联路759号的工厂，安排部分因疫情防控措施无法回到住所的员工前往其培训中心（老虹井路100号）暂时过渡，并利用培训中心烘焙设备及物流中心配送的原材料制作面包自用。</span></section>
<p> </p>
<section><span>②随着封控持续，周边社区对糕点产品需求增大，当事人于4月23日至4月26日期间，在上述培训中心内从事糕点类食品生产经营活动，该地址未取得食品生产经营许可相关资质。</span></section>
<p> </p>
<section><span>③当事人的上述行为属于未经许可从事食品生产经营活动，对其处以没收物品、没收违法所得5.85万元、罚款58.5万元的处罚。</span></section>
<p> </p>
<section><span>这样的行政处罚，看起来一点毛病也没有。因为巴黎贝甜，的确违反了《食品安全法》的相关规定。</span></section>
<p> </p>
<section><span>但从处罚详情中，我们也可以看到品牌人性的光辉。</span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span><strong><span>①巴黎贝甜为无法返回住处的工人提供住宿和食物；②比如在疫情形势严峻下，周边居民对糕点需求增大，巴黎贝甜才开始向他们出售面包。</span></strong></span></section>
<p> </p>
<section><span>在很多人看来，尽管巴黎贝甜违反了相关规定，但它却是上海疫情期间的功臣企业。因此受到处罚，实在太冤了。</span></section>
<p> </p>
<section><span>所以，登上热搜的话题#巴黎贝甜疫情期间无证生产被罚58万#，竟然成了为巴黎贝甜反向带货的正面宣传，不少网友开始声援巴黎贝甜。</span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span><strong><span>一张58万的罚单，带来了580W的广告效果。</span></strong></span></section>
<p> </p>
<section><span><strong><span>众生皆苦，巴黎贝甜。</span></strong></span><span>有人感谢巴黎贝甜在疫情封控期间救了自己小命，维护了自己肚皮的尊严。</span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/214721546663173b05d6b941.92736421.png" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></section>
<section><span><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/87860244163173b061044f6.23418294.png" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></section>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin-bottom: 25px;"><span>▲</span><span> </span><span>图源：微博网友</span><span> </span></h4>
<section><span>有人称赞巴黎贝甜</span><span><strong>没有恶意涨价</strong></span><span>，非常良心。</span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/193973276663173b06521bd7.84786387.png" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></section>
<section><span><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/20040482663173b06a20884.13934096.png" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></section>
<section><span><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/27614539263173b070e3143.40697210.png" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></section>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin-bottom: 25px;"><span></span><span>▲</span><span> </span><span>图源：微博网友</span><span> </span></h4>
<section><span>有人自觉</span><strong><span>安利起了巴黎贝甜的产品</span></strong><span>。</span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/40401319063173b0765be90.98556817.png" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></section>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin-bottom: 25px;"><span></span><span>▲</span><span> </span><span>图源：微博网友</span><span> </span></h4>
<section><span>面对雪中送炭的巴黎贝甜，更多人选择</span><strong><span>用实际行动下单支持</span></strong><span>，报恩性消费。</span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/570911763173b07bde035.03750587.png" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></section>
<p><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/60748848463173b080d07a2.18913413.png" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<section><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/198059822263173b086e10b8.92876254.png" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></section>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin-bottom: 25px;"><span></span><span>▲</span><span> </span><span>图源：微博网友</span></h4>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section></section>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 18px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;">2</h2>
<section><strong><span>不得不说，每个消费者心中都有一杆秤。</span></strong></section>
<p> </p>
<section><span>巴黎贝甜此次被罚58万元，能够引发这么大的争议和讨论，足见上海疫情期间巴黎贝甜的举动，真正甜了人心，赢了民意。</span></section>
<p> </p>
<section><span>犹记得上海疫情最严重的4、5月份，青菜、鸡蛋、可乐等生活物资都成了硬通货，菜价一路飙升，上海的朋友们怨声载道。</span></section>
<p> </p>
<section><span>巴黎贝甜能够在此时向周边居民出售面包等食品，无疑是解了很多人的燃眉之急。甚至3天仅赚了5.85万元，也并没有趁火打劫。</span></section>
<p> </p>
<section><span>上海市监局处罚巴黎贝甜，有自己的理由。</span></section>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>但</span><strong><span>为众人抱薪者，不可使其冻毙于风雪</span></strong><span>。消费者选择帮巴黎贝甜将处罚的58.5万买回来，则是出于更善意、真诚的“知恩图报”。</span></p>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/209921061263173b08af0f69.75653807.png" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></section>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin-bottom: 25px;"><span></span><span>▲</span><span> </span><span>图源：微博网友</span><span> </span></h4>
<section><span>事实上，被官方通报处罚，却被消费者一边倒支持的品牌，巴黎贝甜并不是第一个。</span></section>
<p> </p>
<section><span>今年1月份，西安市市场监管综合执法支队经开区中队，曾通报西安<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E7%9B%92%E9%A9%AC" target="_blank">盒马</a>网络科技有限公司存在不规范行为。</span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span>前者在对西安盒马进行现场检查时，发现了烘焙间存在</span><strong><span>“操作台上放有未经清洗的鸡蛋”、“操作间摆放混乱”、“面包与工作人员衣服混放”</span></strong><span>等不符合规范的行为。综合执法支队认为其涉嫌违反《中华人民共和国食品安全法》相关规定，对西安盒马进行了立案查处。</span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/143800886163173b09928a19.48051586.jpeg" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></section>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin-bottom: 25px;"><span></span><span>▲</span><span> </span><span>图源：界面快报</span><span> </span></h4>
<section><span>消息一出，网络哗然。不少网友认为这是官方</span><span><strong>“鸡蛋里挑骨头”，矫枉过正。</strong></span><span>盒马之所以得到西安市民的支持，同样是因为在西安疫情期间，盒马坚持不涨价，始终平价供给老百姓日常生活物资，赢得了民心。</span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/152244744063173b09d8f7e9.48072057.jpeg" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin-bottom: 25px;"><span></span><span>▲</span><span> </span><span>图源：微博网友</span></h4>
<section><span>值得庆幸的是，这次面对关于巴黎贝甜的网络舆情，上海市市场监管局做出了回应，或许能减轻巴黎贝甜的行政处罚。</span></section>
<section></section>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/172319053363173b0a2c6ae8.05972656.jpeg" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin-bottom: 25px;"><span></span><span>▲</span><span> </span><span>图源：上海市场监管公众号</span></h4>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 18px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;"><span> 3</span></h2>
<section></section>
<section><span><strong><span>明朝的徽商许宪曾提出这样的经商之道：</span></strong></span><span><strong>唯诚待人，人自怀服，任术御物，物终不亲。</strong></span><span>意思是以诚心待人接物，与公相交的人才会逐渐信服。若是在贸易往来中以欺诈为本，以利图为上，既使商品货物也会对公敬而远之。</span></section>
<p> </p>
<section><span>诚然，商人喻于利，但能够做大做强的商人，必然不是唯利是图的。</span><span><strong>以诚待人，急人所急，需人所需，真正把顾客利益放在第一位，才能真正得到顾客的支持。</strong></span></section>
<p> </p>
<section><span>除了巴黎贝甜、盒马，那些引发人们野性消费、收获全网好评的企业，包括慷慨为河南水灾捐款5000万的鸿星尔克，三分之一员工为残疾人的白象，疫情期间不涨价、不裁员、不降薪的老乡鸡等，无一不是在自身利益之外，能够向普通人伸出缓手，为社会贡献力量的企业。</span></section>
<p> </p>
<section><span>法律无情，但法也不外乎人情。</span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span>希望有关部门在追求程序正义的同时，也能够多一些温情和包容，不要让那些良心企业寒了心。</span></section>
