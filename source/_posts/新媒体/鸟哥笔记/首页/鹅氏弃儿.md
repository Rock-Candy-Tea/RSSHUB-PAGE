<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="6"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #444444; --tt-darkmode-color: #A2A2A2;">近日，腾讯为之倾注6年流量和资源的游戏直播平台<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E4%BC%81%E9%B9%85%E7%94%B5%E7%AB%9E" target="_blank">企鹅电竞</a>黯然收场，宣布于2022年6月7日23:59关停服务器，终止平台运营。</span></span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="8"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #444444; --tt-darkmode-color: #A2A2A2;">这不是腾讯第一个沦为弃子的“亲儿子”，恐怕也不会是最后一个。</span></span></p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="10"> </p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; background-color: #ffffff; text-align: center;" data-track="11"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #ffffff; --tt-darkmode-color: #FFFFFF;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-color: #3daad6; --tt-darkmode-bgcolor: #3DAAD6;">01</span></span></span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; background-color: #ffffff; text-align: center;" data-track="12"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #ffffff; --tt-darkmode-color: #FFFFFF;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-color: #3daad6; --tt-darkmode-bgcolor: #3DAAD6;">高开低走，黯然退场</span></span></span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="15"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #444444; --tt-darkmode-color: #A2A2A2;">含着金汤匙出生的企鹅电竞，结束了开场即巅峰的尴尬一生。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="17"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #444444; --tt-darkmode-color: #A2A2A2;">2016年7月，腾讯内部孵化的企鹅电竞正式成立，比虎牙、斗鱼等当红平台晚了足足两年。彼时，游戏业务营收占据了腾讯总收入的半壁江山，时任QQ手游总经理刘宪凯的野心，是整合内外部资源，</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #444444; --tt-darkmode-color: #A2A2A2;">将企鹅电竞做成腾讯的移动电竞生态连接器。</span></span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="19"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #444444; --tt-darkmode-color: #A2A2A2;">然而，一步慢，步步慢。即使有腾讯的雄厚财力支持，也未能改变企鹅电竞因起步晚而先天不足的命运。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="21"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #444444; --tt-darkmode-color: #A2A2A2;">公开数据显示，2017年，企鹅电竞下载量只有5949万，在国内直播平台下载量排行榜中位居第六，而虎牙、斗鱼下载量分别超过4亿和3亿，对比之下堪称降维打击。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="23"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #444444; --tt-darkmode-color: #A2A2A2;">2018年，企鹅电竞发力内容，相继引入LOL“德云色”、原熊猫TV头牌主播若风、梦泪、人皇Sky等顶流主播，并重新举办重量级赛事WCG。</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #444444; --tt-darkmode-color: #A2A2A2;">然而，少数头部主播无法让平台的内容生态脱胎换骨，</span></span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #444444; --tt-darkmode-color: #A2A2A2;">短暂的热度消散后，企鹅电竞再次被“打回原形”。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="25"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #444444; --tt-darkmode-color: #A2A2A2;">同样是在2018年，腾讯对斗鱼和虎牙注资，开启了新的战局。在这场残酷的生存游戏中，嫡系出身的企鹅电竞再次落败。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="27"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #444444; --tt-darkmode-color: #A2A2A2;">一方面，在业务模式上，企鹅电竞未能跳出同质化陷阱，内容短板明显，盈利模式和主流电竞直播平台趋同，</span></span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #444444; --tt-darkmode-color: #A2A2A2;">主要依靠直播打赏服务和品牌广告推广，多年来并没有太多创新。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="29"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #444444; --tt-darkmode-color: #A2A2A2;">另一方面，行业洗牌加速，企鹅电竞面临的竞争愈发激烈。</span></span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #444444; --tt-darkmode-color: #A2A2A2;">熊猫、战旗、龙珠等直播平台相继停运，资源向斗鱼、虎牙等头部集中。2019年底，B站斥资8亿元拿下3年的《英雄联盟》S系列世界赛独播权，再次搅动游戏直播江湖。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="31"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #444444; --tt-darkmode-color: #A2A2A2;">持续掉队的企鹅电竞，成了腾讯“扶不起的阿斗”。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="33"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #444444; --tt-darkmode-color: #A2A2A2;">据艾媒数据，2021年5月，虎牙和斗鱼移动端的平均月活分别为7760万和6070万，企鹅电竞只有565.2万，不及前两者的1/10。前瞻产业研究院数据则显示，</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #444444; --tt-darkmode-color: #A2A2A2;">2020年，斗鱼和虎牙两家头部分割了游戏直播的半壁江山，企鹅电竞只有3.4%的市场份额。</span></span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="35"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #444444; --tt-darkmode-color: #A2A2A2;">废“太子”，成为腾讯减少内耗的必然选择。</span></span></p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="37"> </p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; background-color: #ffffff; text-align: center;" data-track="38"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #ffffff; --tt-darkmode-color: #FFFFFF;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-color: #3daad6; --tt-darkmode-bgcolor: #3DAAD6;">02</span></span></span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; background-color: #ffffff; text-align: center;" data-track="39"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #ffffff; --tt-darkmode-color: #FFFFFF;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-color: #3daad6; --tt-darkmode-bgcolor: #3DAAD6;">扶不起的阿斗们</span></span></span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="42"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #444444; --tt-darkmode-color: #A2A2A2;">高开低走的企鹅电竞并非孤例。纵观腾讯24年来的产品布局中，像企鹅电竞这样“扶不起的阿斗”还有不少。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="44"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #444444; --tt-darkmode-color: #A2A2A2;">2020年9月，腾讯为之砸钱无数、风光时日活曾是新浪微博两倍的腾讯微博，正式告别历史舞台。这款腾讯在2010年为应对新浪微博的威胁而仓促推出的产品，不仅在内容和运营上短板明显，也始终难以得到内部资源的持续支持。马化腾在与吴晓波对话中如是总结：“因为有微信，所以，微博的战争已经结束了。”</span></span></p>
