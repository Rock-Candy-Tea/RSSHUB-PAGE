<div class="Editable-unstyled" style="margin: 0px 0px 1.4em; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 15px; white-space: pre-wrap; background-color: #ffffff;" data-block="true" data-editor="5fesh" data-offset-key="8ok8-0-0">

<div class="Editable-unstyled" style="margin: 0px 0px 1.4em; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 15px; white-space: pre-wrap; background-color: #ffffff;" data-block="true" data-editor="5fesh" data-offset-key="ef1k9-0-0">
<h2 class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 18px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;" data-offset-key="ef1k9-0-0"><span data-offset-key="ef1k9-0-0" style="font-weight: bold;">1、想清楚做小红书目的</span></h2>
</div>
<div class="Editable-unstyled" style="margin: 0px 0px 1.4em; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 15px; white-space: pre-wrap; background-color: #ffffff;" data-block="true" data-editor="5fesh" data-offset-key="9od06-0-0">
<div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="9od06-0-0"><span data-offset-key="9od06-0-0">做小红书前，想清楚你的目的是什么？博主变现？引流到淘宝还是引流到微信？</span></div>
</div>
<div class="Editable-unstyled" style="margin: 0px 0px 1.4em; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 15px; white-space: pre-wrap; background-color: #ffffff;" data-block="true" data-editor="5fesh" data-offset-key="5ifvu-0-0">
<div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="5ifvu-0-0"><span data-offset-key="5ifvu-0-0">目的不同打法不同，举例引流到淘宝为例，你在小红书做的达人笔记、企业号笔记，核心获得更多曝光量，让用户看到你的笔记，然后去站外搜索，你要做的就是降低UV成本；</span></div>
</div>
<div class="Editable-unstyled" style="margin: 0px 0px 1.4em; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 15px; white-space: pre-wrap; background-color: #ffffff;" data-block="true" data-editor="5fesh" data-offset-key="68b0p-0-0">
<div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="68b0p-0-0"><span data-offset-key="68b0p-0-0">还比如，引流到私域，你要做收集同行是怎么在做引流，是小红书号？简介区？我的瞬间还是线下门店，在平台能接受的范围内做到商业目的。</span></div>
</div>
<div class="Editable-unstyled" style="margin: 0px 0px 1.4em; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 15px; white-space: pre-wrap; background-color: #ffffff;" data-block="true" data-editor="5fesh" data-offset-key="76ueg-0-0">
<h2 class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 18px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;" data-offset-key="76ueg-0-0"><span data-offset-key="76ueg-0-0" style="font-weight: bold;">2、干就完事了！</span></h2>
</div>
<div class="Editable-unstyled" style="margin: 0px 0px 1.4em; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 15px; white-space: pre-wrap; background-color: #ffffff;" data-block="true" data-editor="5fesh" data-offset-key="1nlo8-0-0">
<div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="1nlo8-0-0"><span data-offset-key="1nlo8-0-0">现在做小红书晚了吗？投广告还有红利吗？如何启动第一篇笔记？要不要买工具做小红书监控？</span></div>
</div>
<div class="Editable-unstyled" style="margin: 0px 0px 1.4em; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 15px; white-space: pre-wrap; background-color: #ffffff;" data-block="true" data-editor="5fesh" data-offset-key="b8ccr-0-0">
<div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="b8ccr-0-0"><span data-offset-key="b8ccr-0-0">当听到这样的问题，第一想法是劝你别做小红书，考虑的太多，真的就没机会，去年小红书企业号主页还可以绑定电话，今年就限制特别多，最大的认知就是，越早做，越好做，同时也能知道自己适不适合，不适合就放弃，降低沉没成本。</span></div>
</div>
<div class="Editable-unstyled" style="margin: 0px 0px 1.4em; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 15px; white-space: pre-wrap; background-color: #ffffff;" data-block="true" data-editor="5fesh" data-offset-key="6v7al-0-0">
<div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="6v7al-0-0"><span data-offset-key="6v7al-0-0">不知道发笔记，你发一篇看看就好了，不知道怎么做，去官方薯学习，进社群学习，市面海量的学习资料，迈出第一步，才会机会玩转平台。</span></div>
</div>
<div class="Editable-unstyled" style="margin: 0px 0px 1.4em; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 15px; white-space: pre-wrap; background-color: #ffffff;" data-block="true" data-editor="5fesh" data-offset-key="bv5qi-0-0">
<h2 class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 18px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;" data-offset-key="bv5qi-0-0"><span data-offset-key="bv5qi-0-0" style="font-weight: bold;">3、不猜疑平台，提高创作能力</span></h2>
</div>
<div class="Editable-unstyled" style="margin: 0px 0px 1.4em; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 15px; white-space: pre-wrap; background-color: #ffffff;" data-block="true" data-editor="5fesh" data-offset-key="dlsgo-0-0">
<div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="dlsgo-0-0"><span data-offset-key="dlsgo-0-0">啊啊啊！违规了怎么办？是不是限流了？笔记流量怎么这么差？</span></div>
</div>
<div class="Editable-unstyled" style="margin: 0px 0px 1.4em; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 15px; white-space: pre-wrap; background-color: #ffffff;" data-block="true" data-editor="5fesh" data-offset-key="7voni-0-0">
<div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="7voni-0-0"><span data-offset-key="7voni-0-0">首先，平台限流会有提醒，如果没有提醒流量不好，多半是内容问题，去看点击率、看互动率，分析整改就好。</span></div>
</div>
<div class="Editable-unstyled" style="margin: 0px 0px 1.4em; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 15px; white-space: pre-wrap; background-color: #ffffff;" data-block="true" data-editor="5fesh" data-offset-key="dlsog-0-0">
<div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="dlsog-0-0"><span data-offset-key="dlsog-0-0">也可看这篇账号数据分析；最差的结果，号不能用，那你就换一个号，重新做吧，一味地猜测平台规则、怀疑自己创作能力，反而是浪费自己时间。</span></div>
</div>
<div class="Editable-unstyled" style="margin: 0px 0px 1.4em; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 15px; white-space: pre-wrap; background-color: #ffffff;" data-block="true" data-editor="5fesh" data-offset-key="4bqpl-0-0">
<h2 class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 18px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;" data-offset-key="4bqpl-0-0"><span data-offset-key="4bqpl-0-0" style="font-weight: bold;">4、先过程，再结果</span></h2>
</div>
<div class="Editable-unstyled" style="margin: 0px 0px 1.4em; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 15px; white-space: pre-wrap; background-color: #ffffff;" data-block="true" data-editor="5fesh" data-offset-key="2nn89-0-0">
<div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="2nn89-0-0"><span data-offset-key="2nn89-0-0">如何保证ROI？这个问题是扯淡的，凡是能保证ROI，基本投产是不高的。</span></div>
</div>
<div class="Editable-unstyled" style="margin: 0px 0px 1.4em; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 15px; white-space: pre-wrap; background-color: #ffffff;" data-block="true" data-editor="5fesh" data-offset-key="bsv2-0-0">
<div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="bsv2-0-0"><span data-offset-key="bsv2-0-0">做小红书前期，一定不能唯ROI论，当天投广告，就要销量产出，基本判定很难玩好平台。代理商小伙伴说过一句话：“特别追求ROI的客户，其实是不适合小红书。”</span></div>
</div>
<div class="Editable-unstyled" style="margin: 0px 0px 1.4em; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 15px; white-space: pre-wrap; background-color: #ffffff;" data-block="true" data-editor="5fesh" data-offset-key="am30-0-0">
<div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="am30-0-0"><span data-offset-key="am30-0-0">小红书平台和其它平台相比，注重种草和追求调性，本质是投资品牌名，是一个长期价值。你一上来，你就要销量，没有销量，气凶凶说平台傻X，可能真的不太适合。</span></div>
</div>
<div class="Editable-unstyled" style="margin: 0px 0px 1.4em; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 15px; white-space: pre-wrap; background-color: #ffffff;" data-block="true" data-editor="5fesh" data-offset-key="1iuke-0-0">
<h2 class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 18px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;" data-offset-key="1iuke-0-0"><span data-offset-key="1iuke-0-0" style="font-weight: bold;">5、敬畏平台，不代表丧失目的</span></h2>
</div>
<div class="Editable-unstyled" style="margin: 0px 0px 1.4em; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 15px; white-space: pre-wrap; background-color: #ffffff;" data-block="true" data-editor="5fesh" data-offset-key="8cgft-0-0">
<div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="8cgft-0-0"><span data-offset-key="8cgft-0-0">“这容易被限流，这应该不能做吧？”当你说完这个话，我也不知道说啥了，这就像大学老师说，不能逃课，说句实话你上了这些课，也记不住什么，还不如看点想看的书。</span></div>
</div>
<div class="Editable-unstyled" style="margin: 0px 0px 1.4em; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 15px; white-space: pre-wrap; background-color: #ffffff;" data-block="true" data-editor="5fesh" data-offset-key="apu3s-0-0">
<div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="apu3s-0-0"><span data-offset-key="apu3s-0-0">对于平台也是如此，能引流的博主、企业仍旧存在，案例就在那，学呗，敬畏平台，不代表丧失商业目的，还是那句话，方法总比困难多。</span></div>
</div>
<div class="Editable-unstyled" style="margin: 0px 0px 1.4em; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 15px; white-space: pre-wrap; background-color: #ffffff;" data-block="true" data-editor="5fesh" data-offset-key="aaa6f-0-0">
<h2 class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 18px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;" data-offset-key="aaa6f-0-0"><span data-offset-key="aaa6f-0-0" style="font-weight: bold;">6、内容思维做小红书</span></h2>
</div>
<div class="Editable-unstyled" style="margin: 0px 0px 1.4em; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 15px; white-space: pre-wrap; background-color: #ffffff;" data-block="true" data-editor="5fesh" data-offset-key="e3vgr-0-0">
<div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="e3vgr-0-0"><span data-offset-key="e3vgr-0-0">“能用表单直接收割吧？”可以，但是不长久，可以说又贵又不长久。</span></div>
</div>
<div class="Editable-unstyled" style="margin: 0px 0px 1.4em; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 15px; white-space: pre-wrap; background-color: #ffffff;" data-block="true" data-editor="5fesh" data-offset-key="93d95-0-0">
<div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="93d95-0-0"><span data-offset-key="93d95-0-0">小红书本质是内容平台，内容创作是核心能力，你要做的是如何提升内容创作能力，竞价、线索这种方法能用，但效果逐渐降低，只有不间断打磨好你的内容，才有机会上场，因为比你有钱的客户太多了。</span></div>
</div>
<div class="Editable-unstyled" style="margin: 0px 0px 1.4em; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 15px; white-space: pre-wrap; background-color: #ffffff;" data-block="true" data-editor="5fesh" data-offset-key="dcpi3-0-0">
<h2 class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 18px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;" data-offset-key="dcpi3-0-0"><span data-offset-key="dcpi3-0-0" style="font-weight: bold;">7、高于内容，谈价值</span></h2>
