<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px; text-align: left;">前段时间，在小破站上，为感谢投币不停说话又好听的网友们，<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E5%AE%9D%E9%A9%AC" target="_blank">宝马</a>再续前缘，推出《高能的一匹》姊妹篇《高能的一币》。别看时长短了一倍，但观看量和营销看点却多了不少。</p>

<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: center; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">《高能的一币》</p>

<h1 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 20px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: center;"><span><strong><span>01</span></strong></span></h1>
<p style="text-align: center; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong>魔幻拼贴高能沟通</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong>拿捏调性斩获流量密码</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">什么是硬币？</p>
<p style="text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/182836226262cc91e67dcbd8.80760188.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">从初代硬币“贝币”的诞生，到硬币“成熟体”铜板、魏晋南北朝“一贯”、宋代交子、大清宝钞，再到“无现金时代”使用二维码及异世界花式玩币……</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/95065659962cc91e6d2c587.17207406.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">围绕并遵循硬币守恒定律，短片通过回溯货币的起源及历史进程，精准狙击B站观众姥爷们，获浏览量43.8万、点赞1.8万、投币3600+、收藏近2400。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/171457230862cc91e72b7321.70823629.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">其中，大量游戏、动漫等视觉元素（如《底特律变人》、《某科学的超电磁炮》）的填充，“好活不断”“SSR”等二次元旁白术语的融合，再加上贯穿始终的电玩音效，组合成“鬼畜、游戏、动漫、节奏、电音”五大关键词，搭配超现实主义拼贴画风，将品牌的高能有趣展现得淋漓尽致，贴合B站平台的沟通氛围，十分讨喜。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px; text-align: left;">而这种以素材的杂糅制造天马行空、荒诞不经的想象，把多重叙事变得轻巧活泼的魔幻表达，于宝师傅而言绝非首次。除两年的B站首秀，今年的虎年大吉新春刷屏祝福《宝马太虎了》也运用了该方式，玩的狂野不羁，令人上头。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: center; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span data-ratio="1" id="js_tx_video_container_0.5361814047297102"><iframe src="https://v.qq.com/txp/iframe/player.html?origin=https%3A%2F%2Fmp.weixin.qq.com&containerId=js_tx_video_container_0.5361814047297102&vid=p3320axoupw&width=637&height=477.75&autoplay=false&allowFullScreen=true&chid=17&full=true&show1080p=false&isDebugIframe=false" width="555" height="416" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></iframe></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: center; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">《宝马太虎了》</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: center; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/4575430662cc91e7d80ea0.90300152.png" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: center; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">虎虎生风</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: center; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/143842314862cc91e9093d30.50576851.png" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: center; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">如虎添翼</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: center; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="font-weight: inherit; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', Roboto, Oxygen, Ubuntu, Cantarell, 'Open Sans', 'Helvetica Neue', sans-serif;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/65340478362cc91ea9980c2.76863323.png" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: center; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">马-YI-A-HEI，马-YI-A-虎</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px; text-align: left;">挂在宝马B站主页的代表作实战成绩更加表明，当宝师傅一改往日端庄高雅的精英人设，在这里摇身一变担当宝藏UP主，画风越清奇就越对味，足够唤醒用户DNA。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px; text-align: inherit;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/29078766962cc91eb5e8132.04518872.png" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h1 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); font-size: 20px; color: #212629; text-align: center; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><strong><span> 02</span></strong></h1>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: center; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong>反向投币高能二创</strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: center; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong>契合社区文化圈粉受众</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">点赞+投币+收藏，通过长按点赞一次性完成三个动作，是给UP主的最高赞许，也是 B 站用户“用脚投票”的亲身实践。</p>
<p style="text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">同时，在B站规则里，用户等级升级大部分要依靠获取“硬币”。“硬币”是“硬通货”，“你币有了”是B站人的“礼赞”。</p>
<p style="text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">令人意想不到的是，这一次的视频里，宝马在大量铺垫后猝不及防插播广告，官宣限量款2022枚实体纪念币，并以反向投币赠送“福利”之名邀粉丝参与活动，亲自下场征集二创互动，颇具新意。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px; text-align: inherit;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/132343153162cc91ec47e237.20489850.jpeg" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">契合B站社区文化把币给粉丝，让过去“为爱发电”的用户来一次前所未有的“为币发电”。想来这两年持续不断的运营里，宝师傅不仅准确掌握对话B站用户的独特语种，做到了和Z世代“无障碍交流”；同时也悉心洞察出用户群感兴趣的话题内容，投其所好，践行着沟通“言之有物，引人入深”。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px; text-align: inherit;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/10774376662cc91ec92afe2.42278650.png" width="555" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px; text-align: inherit;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/99916358962cc91ecc2b652.10151094.png" width="555" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px; text-align: inherit;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/1599483562cc91eced3889.96793157.png" width="555" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: center; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">官方整活，评论区里大家积极响应</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px; text-align: inherit;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/207169718562cc91ed263f67.01156805.png" width="555" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px; text-align: inherit;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/206344197562cc91ed4fa717.20473826.png" width="555" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: center; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">还有其他同行玩家捧场给面子</p>
<p style="text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">活动期间，宝师傅不定期发布二创投稿主题，触达垂直目标受众；各路神仙应邀放马整活、互动求币，助推活动话题持续裂变，无形之中扩大了品牌声量，也向外辐散潜在用户，吸引了更多人的目光。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px; text-align: inherit;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/48414314762cc91ed746456.95882840.png" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h1 style="text-align: center; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 20px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px;"><span><strong><span> 03</span></strong></span></h1>
<p style="text-align: center; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong>面对新青年高能输出</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong>冲向B站还能怎么玩</strong></p>
<section>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">簇拥广大Z世代用户，自B站从小众二次元社区成长到如今愈发丰富多元的内容社区后，宝马、奔驰、大众、别克等汽圈大佬统统嗅到平台红利，争先在B站布局内容。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: center; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span data-ratio="1.7777777777777777" id="js_tx_video_container_0.154383903289792"><iframe src="https://v.qq.com/txp/iframe/player.html?origin=https%3A%2F%2Fmp.weixin.qq.com&containerId=js_tx_video_container_0.154383903289792&vid=y3329llbdqr&width=637&height=358.3125&autoplay=false&allowFullScreen=true&chid=17&full=true&show1080p=false&isDebugIframe=false" width="637" height="358.3125" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></iframe></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: center; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">《奔驰本驰，冲向B站》</p>
</section>
<p style="text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">为宣传新款梅赛德斯-AMG GT R，奔驰3月开启B站首秀，将车迷们的“梦中赛道”纽博格林赛道“复刻”其中，头文字D既视感瞬间拉满。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px; text-align: inherit;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/58782762462cc91edb97fb7.00731481.png" width="555" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">大众日前以标题党《什么？听说有500万人都选择了它？要不你也进来选一下》发布朗逸新系列，随后评论区亮了。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px; text-align: inherit;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/110716084062cc91ee4f95d3.60657056.png" width="666" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">沙雕梗王、高能反转、奇奇怪怪意想不到的知识点，有时候出现在品牌UP主身上，有时候空降粉丝“自来水”区。不知是B站魅力如此，还是入驻品牌进来自然就会改变，反正冲向B站的车企们不断在平台里贡献惊喜，舍弃了我们固有印象里的那些“大气”“奢华”的一面，都变得可可爱爱令人期待。</p>
<p style="text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">高能输出之外，也让人不禁畅想，下一次，他们还会玩出什么花样？</p>
</section>
