<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; visibility: visible; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; visibility: visible; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; color: #333333; letter-spacing: 0pt; vertical-align: baseline; font-size: 15px; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">在7月夏末阶段，防晒产品平销逐渐下滑，那些夏季防晒产品、夏季专用商品是如何做最后一波促销的？他们是如何出人意料、拿出不平常的刺激物吸引大众的？</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; visibility: visible; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; color: #333333; letter-spacing: 0pt; vertical-align: baseline; font-size: 15px; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">本期应对夏末秋初转季特殊节点，壁虎快跑带你从场景、营销、产品方面入手，来了解快手各夏季品牌的营销措施。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; visibility: visible; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"> </p>

<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; visibility: visible; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; color: #333333; letter-spacing: 0pt; vertical-align: baseline; font-size: 15px; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">近日，壁虎快跑在整理品牌自播榜时，发现有许多商家已经开启了夏末清仓，秋季上新活动，如快手百大快品牌之一的“金典家居官方旗舰店”。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; visibility: visible; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; color: #333333; letter-spacing: 0pt; vertical-align: baseline; font-size: 15px; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">与其他商家不同的是，金典此次活动还将七夕节点营销囊括其中，三大亮点齐发，截至目前，其粉丝仅为20.4万，但其在24号直播的清仓上新活动，拿下了</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-weight: bold; letter-spacing: 0pt; vertical-align: baseline; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; color: #ffa900; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">近170万</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; color: #333333; letter-spacing: 0pt; vertical-align: baseline; font-size: 15px; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">的销售额，远超平销百倍销售额。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; background-color: #ffffff; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/2b055eedce1c6f3df407afd6bd01072e.png" alt width="696" height="494" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-weight: bold; letter-spacing: 0pt; vertical-align: baseline; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; color: #ffa900; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">但降价不一定畅销，涨价也不见得难卖。</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-size: 15px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; color: #333333; letter-spacing: 0pt; vertical-align: baseline; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">一般来说，大部分人都喜欢性价比高的产品，因此一味的清仓降价并不见得能带来极高的收益，此次蕉下便在省钱活动中加入了一些日常的营销玩法，来实现账号声量与销量的双增长。</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #333333; letter-spacing: 0pt; vertical-align: baseline; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"></span></span></p>
<h2 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; outline: 0px; font-weight: 600; font-size: 18px; max-width: 100%; color: #212629; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; letter-spacing: 0.544px; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: 30px; text-align: left; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-weight: bold; letter-spacing: 0pt; vertical-align: baseline; font-size: 19px; color: #1b9bea; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">蕉下*快手超级省钱日</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-weight: bold; letter-spacing: 0pt; vertical-align: baseline; font-size: 19px; color: #1b9bea; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"> </span></h2>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; color: #333333; letter-spacing: 0pt; vertical-align: baseline; font-size: 15px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">近日，蕉下在快手开启了</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-weight: bold; letter-spacing: 0pt; vertical-align: baseline; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; color: #ffa900; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">“蕉下省钱日”</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; color: #333333; letter-spacing: 0pt; vertical-align: baseline; font-size: 15px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">主</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; color: #333333; letter-spacing: 0pt; vertical-align: baseline; font-size: 15px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">题直播活动，活动时间为7月19日-28日，官方爆品折上折，10天省钱小课堂、福利大放送，最后活动阶段整体销售额达到了186.99万，平销均18.7万，远超平日均10万的日销。</span></p>

<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; color: #333333; letter-spacing: 0pt; vertical-align: baseline; font-size: 15px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">我们都知道蕉下以DTC模式起家，主要依靠线上大量KOC、KOL、明星网红来做曝光营销，来提升品牌的知名度以及客群的粘性，蕉下吃到了线上发展的红利，自然也把线上平台作为了重要的销售渠道来进行布局，因此蕉下选择快手作为线上销售渠道之一，并非是意料之外的事。</span></p>
