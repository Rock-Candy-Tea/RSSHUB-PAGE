                        <p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-first-child data-pid="GIVUuJi6">遇到商家询问，如何在小红书做内容投放？怎么少走弯路？投放失败，多源于选品没竞争力、卖点不清，推广内容形式存在问题。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-pid="Nnpogf5I">在我看来，就是没有在不同阶段将事情做好，今天重点聊一下，品牌在小红书不同阶段做正确的内容，特别是新起盘的商家。</p>
<h1 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #212629; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 20px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); text-align: center; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;" data-pid="N-OTozIv"><span style="font-weight: 600; font-synthesis: style;">一、4类品牌不同需求</span></h1>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-pid="hjL0k5Vf">在小红书种草的商家，主要分为4个阶段。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-pid="YwlXccIf"><strong>第一阶段：</strong>无品牌心智，核心是带货，如淘系品牌或者拿私信转化的商家;</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-pid="xFosDP3C"><strong>第二阶段：</strong>新品0到1，核心目的是初始口碑积累，打下品牌基础，如Wonderlab，Herlab她妍社等品牌；</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-pid="7knJBFb9"><strong>第三阶段：</strong>新品1到10阶段，核心人群渗透，做心智渗透，核心抢占细分关键词，如三顿半、城野医生、小仙炖等品牌；</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-pid="6A7h1keC"><strong>第四阶段：</strong>成熟品牌，主做成熟单品口碑维护，心智巩固同时做好舆情控制，做好品牌宣传，如飞鹤、VIVO、雅诗兰黛等品牌。</p>
<p class="ztext-empty-paragraph" style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"> </p>
<h4 style="margin: 25px 0px; color: #818386; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 14px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px;" data-size="normal"><img class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb lazy" style="display: block; max-width: 100%; margin: 0px auto; cursor: zoom-in;" src="https://picx.zhimg.com/80/v2-589c928147bb69affc74f3a635bdec20_720w.jpg?source=d16d100b" width="1080" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="554" data-original="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-589c928147bb69affc74f3a635bdec20_720w.jpg?source=d16d100b" data-actualsrc="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-589c928147bb69affc74f3a635bdec20_720w.jpg?source=d16d100b" data-lazy-status="ok" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br>图1:引响不同阶段品牌诉求</h4>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-pid="B48s8F5A">不同阶段的品牌，目的是不同的，在小红书也需要不同的内容形式，如第一阶段核心是卖货，内容要围绕单品，更直接去吸引用户关注，下面着重对红书内容形式解读。</p>
<h1 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #212629; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 20px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); text-align: center; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;" data-pid="xTTQuDhC"><span style="font-weight: 600; font-synthesis: style;">二、小红书3大内容形式</span></h1>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-pid="QmbBy8cQ">在小红书需围绕品牌不同阶段，制定不同目标去产出对应的内容形式，目前小红书内容主要分为吸引型、热点型、导购型内容。</p>
<h4 style="margin: 25px 0px; color: #818386; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 14px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px;" data-size="normal"><img class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb lazy" style="display: block; max-width: 100%; margin: 0px auto; cursor: zoom-in;" src="https://pic2.zhimg.com/80/v2-b1753e0ab2bee6ebc4478ea3dd769ca2_720w.jpg?source=d16d100b" width="1080" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="541" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-b1753e0ab2bee6ebc4478ea3dd769ca2_720w.jpg?source=d16d100b" data-actualsrc="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-b1753e0ab2bee6ebc4478ea3dd769ca2_720w.jpg?source=d16d100b" data-lazy-status="ok" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br>图2:小红书主要内容形式</h4>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-pid="Ma8BBQP8"><strong>吸引型：</strong>指有兴趣的用户，有模糊需求但未锁定精准人群，内容形式围绕着好物分享、横向测评、场景类vlog等形式；</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-pid="kRglnIXm"><strong>热点型：</strong>针对潜在人群的弱需求进行产出，围绕着热点资讯、情景剧、故事性强一些的vlog等形式，此类用户核心诉求在消遣；</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-pid="QaOxj6fQ"><strong>导购型：</strong>针对高意向客户，此类人群目标明确，做购买前的深入了解，此品牌阶段需要点出差异化，内容形式围绕着经验分享，教程，单品推荐等形式。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-pid="ml5RQl36">以刷小红书为例，如我有时候想摸鱼，打开小红书随便刷刷，突然发现有个笔记分享的粉扑看着还不错，突然激发了我的购买欲，粉扑属于吸引型内容；</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-pid="nvy0zOIj">如突然刷到有博主扒正在热播的《二十不惑》的穿搭，这个时候即使我没有很想买东西，我也可能会因为喜欢关晓彤而买单，这种属于热点型；</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-pid="uvLhMQuW">导购型内容则我想去吃费大厨，但不知道应该点哪个菜，我去小红书搜一下，觉得不错我就想get同款菜品。</p>
<h1 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #212629; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 20px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); text-align: center; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;" data-pid="sHoG7hXU"><span style="font-weight: 600; font-synthesis: style;">三、两大阶段内容打法</span></h1>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-pid="Bx7qdhH7">在实际的运营中，商家应该选择吸引、热点、导购三类形式哪一类？下面针对两类商家进行重点解读。</p>
<h2 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: 18px; line-height: 30px; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; clear: left; font-synthesis: style; color: #212629; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); text-align: left; font-weight: 600; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><strong>第一阶段：带货商家</strong></h2>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-pid="z1IhzPv3">以淘系商家上，私域拿私信用户，核心是为了转化，内容要重点突出产品，让用户感知到产品的重点性，内容基本围绕单品/多篇+科普为主，把产品特点展示给有较强意愿的目标用户。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-pid="tP-ShA-U">举例「茶你喽」是做茶包的，核心是在小红书做转化，重点要突出产品，主要是吸引性内容，突出不同体质、场景，症状人群；</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-pid="HH36I1cj">做干货科普和搭配喝法,还比如拿私信的珠宝翡翠用户，内容形式集中在真假鉴别、价格、寓意等方面，通过吸引性内容快速锁客。</p>
<h4 style="margin: 25px 0px; color: #818386; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 14px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px;" data-size="normal"><img class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb lazy" style="display: block; max-width: 100%; margin: 0px auto; cursor: zoom-in;" src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/80/v2-a8a898a6943df1b7eea27fc00a214fc4_720w.jpg?source=d16d100b" width="1080" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="642" data-original="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-a8a898a6943df1b7eea27fc00a214fc4_720w.jpg?source=d16d100b" data-actualsrc="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-a8a898a6943df1b7eea27fc00a214fc4_720w.jpg?source=d16d100b" data-lazy-status="ok" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">图3：科普+单品/多品种草类笔记</h4>
<h2 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #212629; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 18px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;" data-pid="Zn8PBhlE"><span style="font-weight: 600; font-synthesis: style;">第二阶段：新品0到1</span></h2>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-pid="LEGUvWcr">此阶段在选择内容形式上，核心是以品类为判断基础，一种是无品类认知，另一种是有品类认知。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-pid="pjsgpTCP"><span style="font-weight: 600; font-synthesis: style;">1、无品类认知</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-pid="M9dk9HFb">特劳特的《定位》中指出：“消费者以品类来思考，以品牌来表达”，品类是用户购买的根！当一个品类消费者都不知道的时候，消费者自然不会为它买单。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-pid="Pze6XMeF">当品类认知度比较低的时候，品牌要做的是品类教育的工作。此阶段内容核心通过吸引和热点性内容。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-pid="cgdVonpX">如制冰机一开始是商用，现转为民用，消费者对这个品类认知比较弱，制冰机商家需要先给大家安利这个品类，让用户知道制冰机可以用在哪些场景，进而激发用户对这个品类的购买欲。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-pid="zCkEbumJ">制冰商家「惠康」在自己品牌号的内容形式上，主要通过分享宿舍制冰、家用制冰、公司制冰等场景，以及制冰机的各种饮品制作，向用户知道这个品类。</p>

<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-pid="J80BKYNr">品类度过初教育阶段，处于快速发展的时期，核心就要打造品牌差异化，完成用户在众多品类之间做出选择，内容形式围绕吸引性和导购性内容展开。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-pid="a1jnQTiz">以香薰品类为例，消费者更关注买哪个品牌更适合自己，品牌应该侧重看竞品在打哪些卖点，用户关注哪些点，自身和竞品有哪些不同。内容形式围绕着测评类，使用教程，单品种草。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-pid="YxHKT5vH">现在大家比较熟知的香薰品牌有观夏，野兽派，祖马龙等，在小红书的内容形式集中在香薰合集，单品种草，多品测评类。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-pid="-8KVsfLJ">如在观夏在达人笔记上更多是单品/多品合集/，由于香薰审美是比较私人的事情，香薰测评类的相对较少，更多是在价格上，味道上进行区分。品牌官方号上主要集中在品牌故事以及单品种草。</p>
