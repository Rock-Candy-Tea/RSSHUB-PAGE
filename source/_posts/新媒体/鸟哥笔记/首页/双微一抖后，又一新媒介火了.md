<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>大家好，这里是头条哥。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>对<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E5%9C%A8%E7%BA%BF%E6%96%87%E6%A1%A3" target="_blank">在线文档</a>，各位打工人一定不陌生，但你有想过每天和自己朝夕相处的Excel表格，也能成为广告的新媒介吗?</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>最近，<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/999%E6%84%9F%E5%86%92%E7%81%B5" target="_blank">999感冒灵</a>就和在线办公平台<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E9%87%91%E5%B1%B1%E6%96%87%E6%A1%A3" target="_blank">金山文档</a>合作，搭建起了一个名为<strong>“999许愿灵”</strong>的实时在线文档，以供大众分享生活，释放压力和情绪。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/191570644162de96666f0292.25493909.jpeg" width="555" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: center; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>（很难相信这是表格可以做出的效果）</span></strong></span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>而在999感冒灵之前，已经有很多品牌与在线文档平台合作的案例，比方说开心麻花X金山文档的“开心一夏摸会鱼吧”：<br></span></p>

<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><span>这些案例让我们看到，</span><span><strong>以功能性平台为大众印象的在线文档品牌，俨然朝着新品牌营销阵地的方向发展了起来。</strong></span></span><span></span></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span><strong><span> 从私域走向公域 </span></strong></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><span>在线文档，最初多应用于个人跨设备的文件处理，之后慢慢拓展到团队间的协作，</span><span>这种私域的传播为在线文档平台打下了用户基础，拓展了用户对于其功能性的认识。</span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><span>加上操作简单，易于管理查找的特点，之后的在线文档，</span><span><strong>就逐渐从私域的团队内部传播拓展到了人人都可参与的公域表达，</strong></span><span>比方说各种找工作避雷公司表格、河南暴雨事件中的救命文档，都让一个文档拓展出了无数个编辑者与观看者。</span></span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/193644641762de96683432c5.30476637.png" width="666" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: center; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>（河南暴雨事件中的“救命文档”）</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><span>在公共领域的表达传播中占有一席之地的在线文档，为自己成为品牌方青睐的平台打下了基础。而同时，</span><span><strong><span>借助在线文档进行</span><span>的营销之所以能够获得效果，在于它能给用户带来一种在当下互联网中没有的互动体验。</span></strong></span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span><span> 初代互联网的“再现” </span></span></strong><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>所有人都能进行编辑，</span></strong><strong><span>是公域化在线文档的灵魂。</span></strong></span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在和品牌搭建的在线文档的互动中，</span><strong><span>每一个用户都是以一种“完全平等”的身份介入的，在线文档清洗掉了现代媒介中常用的衡量用户文本价值的“点赞数”“回复数”等标准，</span></strong><span>“先来后到”是唯一的排序机制。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/73265069962de9668ca49c3.89504056.jpeg" width="666" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>这种类似于早期bbs论坛的互动模式，基本重现了早期互联网用户平等的网络身份，吸引着有怀旧情怀的80后90后，同时，也用一种新鲜感和较为自由的玩法赢得了Z世代和互联网原住民。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/12427340662de9669014a42.76617926.jpeg" width="666" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>品牌依托在线文档，确实可以用新颖的使用体验和自由的玩法获得良好的传播效果，而这也牵扯出另外一个在线文档营销中存在的问题。</span><span><span></span></span></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span><strong><span> 双赢还是喧宾夺主？</span></strong></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在线文档这种“准媒介”，与主流的“双微一抖”平台有很大区别。在后者上做内容，用户基本不会对媒介平台本身有较多的关注，而在线文档平台却恰恰相反。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/186899042762de96693f79a5.73396764.png" width="555" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><span>像一开头提到的999许愿灵案例，用户的</span><span>第一印象很可能在“<span>金山文档还能这么玩</span>”这个层面，这种打破既定认知的，对平台的记忆留存也很可能超过对品牌传播内容的记忆。</span></span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/177708915462de9669b6f3c6.27221551.png" width="555" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: center; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>（<span>999许愿灵中的某位用户发言</span>）</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>品牌做在线文档的营销，</span><strong><span>是与在线文档平台双赢，还是在线文档平台喧宾夺主，使品牌花了钱也没赚到吆喝，</span></strong><span>这是目前的在线文档营销绕不开的问题，这可能也是当下的在线文档案例多以联名合作方式推进的原因之一。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/184690035462de9669e3bed6.90410801.jpeg" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>但如果不想被在线文档平台喧宾夺主，就需要品牌的在线文档更加强调自身</span><span>（比方说放大logo</span><span>）</span><span>，将玩法与品牌内容相结合，不再是仅仅搭建一个论坛社区式的内容，</span><span><strong>不能直接套用主流平台上的内容方法论。</strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>品牌做在线文档营销，还有很长的路要走。</span><span></span></p>
