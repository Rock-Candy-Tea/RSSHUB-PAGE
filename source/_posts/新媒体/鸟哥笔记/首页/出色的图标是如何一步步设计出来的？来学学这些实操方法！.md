height: auto;" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></strong></p><p label="正文" style="font-size: 16px; color: rgb(33, 38, 41); margin-bottom: 25px;">▲ 通过在两个形状的相交处创建阴影，实现顶层形状悬浮的效果；使用三种黄色阴影--顶层较浅、底层较深和最深的阴影。</p><p label="正文" style="font-size: 16px; color: rgb(33, 38, 41); margin-bottom: 25px;"><strong>聊天图标</strong></p><p label="正文" style="font-size: 16px; color: rgb(33, 38, 41); margin-bottom: 25px; text-align: center;"><img src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/7936978606107c154822403.09002463.png" width="600" height="304" border="0" vspace="0" title="鸟哥笔记,产品设计,Clippp,UI设计,图标,品牌logo" alt="鸟哥笔记,产品设计,Clippp,UI设计,图标,品牌logo" style="width: 600px; color: rgb(33, 38, 41); margin-bottom: 25px;"><strong>标记图标</strong></p><p label="正文" style="font-size: 16px; color: rgb(33, 38, 41); margin-bottom: 25px; text-align: center;"><img src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/12625651336107c154ef1927.42124285.png" width="600" height="304" border="0" vspace="0" title="鸟哥笔记,产品设计,Clippp,UI设计,图标,品牌logo" alt="鸟哥笔记,产品设计,Clippp,UI设计,图标,品牌logo" style="width: 600px; color: rgb(33, 38, 41); margin-bottom: 25px;"><strong>旗帜图标</strong></p><p label="正文" style="font-size: 16px; color: rgb(33, 38, 41); margin-bottom: 25px; text-align: center;"><img src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/15554185656107c1556e4728.19278624.png" width="600" height="304" border="0" vspace="0" title="鸟哥笔记,产品设计,Clippp,UI设计,图标,品牌logo" alt="鸟哥笔记,产品设计,Clippp,UI设计,图标,品牌logo" style="width: 600px; color: rgb(33, 38, 41); margin-bottom: 25px;"><strong>心形图标</strong></p><p label="正文" style="font-size: 16px; color: rgb(33, 38, 41); margin-bottom: 25px; text-align: center;"><img src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/19794056676107c155f00579.77113642.png" width="600" height="304" border="0" vspace="0" title="鸟哥笔记,产品设计,Clippp,UI设计,图标,品牌logo" alt="鸟哥笔记,产品设计,Clippp,UI设计,图标,品牌logo" style="width: 600px;
