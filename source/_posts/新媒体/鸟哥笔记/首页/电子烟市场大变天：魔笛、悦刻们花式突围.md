<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="5"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="6">监管趋严是挑战也是机遇，中小型电子烟品牌被清退，头部电子烟品牌积极自救，悦刻、MOTI魔笛品牌加快出海步伐，试图在竞争激烈的份额之争中取胜。</p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="7"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="8">海外市场限制少、空间大，电子烟品牌出海是大势所趋。《2022年电子烟产业出口蓝皮书》报告显示，2022年全球电子烟市场规模将超过1080亿美元，预计2022年海外电子烟市场规模将保持35%的增长速度，总规模突破1000亿美金。</p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="9"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="10">电子烟品牌出海拓荒充满未知，行业热门词汇也暴露了国内电子烟市场极具挑战。</p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="11"> </p>
<h2 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; border: 0px; color: #212629; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: left; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px;" data-track="12"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-weight: bold;">电子烟市场变天了</span></h2>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="13"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="14">行业热门词汇反映行业发展趋势，从电子烟行业热门词汇来看，电子烟品牌们面临着新一轮行业变革大考。</p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="15"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="16"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-weight: bold;">热词一:限制口味。</span>近期，国家烟草专卖局发布了《电子烟国家标准》以及《电子烟管理办法》，规定：禁止销售除烟草口味外的调味电子烟和可自行添加雾化物的电子烟。</p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="17"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="18">电子烟主要的受众群体是年轻人，而年轻人最喜欢调味电子烟，禁调味电子烟会劝退大部分年轻消费者。据了解，电子烟市场上的调味产品的市占率接近九成，电子烟产品有约1.6万种口味，包括水果味、糖果味、各种甜品味等。</p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="19"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="20">对于电子烟品牌来说，口味限制会加剧产品同质化现象，原本靠产品口味取胜的电子烟品牌将要面临产品同质化和年轻消费者流失的难题。此外，禁令下电子烟品牌需要砍掉调味电子烟产品生产线，多元产品矩阵将化为乌有。</p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="21"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="22"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-weight: bold;">热词二：一烟一身份。</span>6月15日，全国统一电子烟交易管理平台正式上线，取得烟草专卖许可证的电子烟相关生产企业、批发企业和零售经营主体，可上平台进行交易会员注册登录，开展相关交易及进出口备案。</p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="23"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="24">全国统一电子烟交易管理平台电子上线，意味着烟生产、流通、销售变得透明可查，“一烟一身份”的溯源监管时代来了。对于电子烟品牌来说，电子烟入行门槛变高了，没有烟草专卖许可资质的企业将被淘汰，有资质的企业则要迎接更为严苛的技术和服务水平考核。</p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="25"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="26">电子烟监管政策层出不穷，电子烟品牌面临的监管压力越来越大，而在电子烟行业变局之时，悦刻、MOTI魔笛等头部品牌陷入了运营危机，正积极求变。</p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="27"> </p>
<h2 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; border: 0px; color: #212629; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: left; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px;" data-track="28"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-weight: bold;">集体陷入阵痛期</span></h2>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="29"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="30">以前，人们将电子烟比作“健康升级版”传统香烟，口味更多样、“不会上瘾”，传统烟民视电子烟为“戒烟神器”，年轻人视电子烟为时尚单品，一时间电子烟被推到风口上。现在科学证明，电子烟危害不亚于普通烟草，网红产品光环就此褪去。</p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="31"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="32">电子烟风口期吸引来众多创业者，监管政策趋严这些电子烟企业该何去何从？从悦刻、魔笛等头部品牌发展现状可以发现，电子烟行业监管趋严，产品口味无差异化、多元渠道行不通，电子烟品牌陷入阵痛。</p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="33"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="34">电子烟兴起之初，悦刻通过线上布局，迅速打开了市场。彼时，电子烟线上销售占比超过八成，而悦刻品牌店覆盖在京东、天猫等主流电商平台，还建立起近百个线上社群，提升了消费者认可度和品牌知名度。</p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="35"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="36"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-weight: bold;">线上渠道被禁后，悦刻大举布局线下专卖店、店中店、自动售卖机等等，守住了行业龙头的地位。据CIC报告数据显示，在2019年和2020年前9个月，按零售额计算，雾芯<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E7%A7%91%E6%8A%80" target="_blank">科技</a>（悦刻母公司）中国市场份额分别为48%和62.6%，稳居行业首位。</span></p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="37"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="38">好景不长，《电子烟管理办法》中的新规定写明：销售端的企业或个人需要具备从事电子烟零售业务资格，并不得排他性经营上市销售的电子烟产品。也就是说，未来的电子烟商店将不会出现专卖店的形式，“专卖店规模为王”成为过去式。</p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="39"> </p>
<p style="margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="40">方今，雾芯科技股价大跌，市值缩水约9成。据网络数据显示，上市当日，悦刻收盘股价29.51美元，暴涨145.92%，市值达458亿美元。上市第四日，悦刻就创造了35美元的股价高点，市值随之涨至583亿美元。而今（美东时间 6月21日），悦刻股价收盘报2.29美元，总市值仅35.45亿美元，下跌超90%。</p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="margin: 18px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; background-color: #ffffff;" data-track="41"> </p>
