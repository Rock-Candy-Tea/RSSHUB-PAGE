<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">在2022上半年整体呈下降趋势的美妆赛道，要如何突破，在自然流量与付费流量中寻找新的增长机会？</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span id="article-img-0" class="a-image" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"><img style="margin: 0px auto 20px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: middle; border: none; max-width: 100%; display: block; background: url('//static.yidianzixun.com/apps/black_mamba_web_v1.0.0.177/black_mamba_web_main_pic_placeholder.png') 50% center no-repeat #e6e6e6;" src="https://i1.go2yd.com/image.php?url=YD_cnt_41_01EtAA0Jm42D" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>

<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin-bottom: 25px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">▲图片来自蝉妈妈</strong></h4>

<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">本期依旧是蝉妈妈场控诊断师大卓，为新锐热门美妆品牌红地球进行直播间诊断，揭秘美妆赛道付费撬动自然流的关键方法论。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">红地球作为抖系美妆品牌的后起之秀，却有与其他百万GMV直播间截然不同的流量结构，作为期望跑量，却保持轻付费、高自然流占比的结构，让我们看看对其投流的拆解，能否得到增长的启发。</p>
<h1 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: 30px; color: #212629; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 20px; text-align: center; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); font-weight: 600; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">直播细节起量，</strong><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">开播不投放+破层防违规</strong></h1>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">在对红地球直播间进行诊断时，大卓首先分析了红地球直播间的人群与流量情况，红地球账号的视频用户画像与直播用户画像非常相似，所以直播间的自然流量非常的精准。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">但是在竞争激烈的美妆赛道，像红地球这样高自然流量占比的直播间，再进一步提升就存在很大瓶颈，针对于此大卓总结了红地球直播间在流量运营上的亮点与不足。</p>
<h2 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; font-weight: 600; color: #212629; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; text-align: left; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; line-height: 30px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">1、</strong><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">开播不介入付费精准流量</strong></h2>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">在复盘红地球8月5日的投流策略时，大卓通过蝉妈妈场控综合数据发现，在开场部分红地球做了一个9.9元眉笔的活动去承接开场流量，这时候没有付费流量的介入，对此他表示了认可：</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span id="article-img-1" class="a-image" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"><img style="margin: 0px auto 20px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: middle; border: none; max-width: 100%; display: block; background: url('//static.yidianzixun.com/apps/black_mamba_web_v1.0.0.177/black_mamba_web_main_pic_placeholder.png') 50% center no-repeat #e6e6e6;" src="https://i1.go2yd.com/image.php?url=YD_cnt_41_01EtAA3DOE6s" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<div>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin-bottom: 25px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">▲蝉妈妈场控直播间复盘诊断大会</strong></h4>
</div>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">付费流量有着20~30分钟的冷启动期，开场流量一般来自于关注粉丝、推荐流量、短视频流量、中心场流量等，开场不宜过早介入付费，以免付费流量的成单计划将9.9元吸引来的用户当做人群之一进行抓取，导致模型不精准，防止大量薅羊毛流量对直播间的进阶引流带来影响。</p>
<h2 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; font-weight: 600; color: #212629; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; text-align: left; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; line-height: 30px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">2、</strong><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">人气突破层级要谨防违规</strong></h2>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">除了开场的投流，在8月5号场直播的成交峰值处，大卓也发现了问题。通过蝉妈妈场控的5分钟看板，大卓观察了整场数据，发现在峰值的五分钟里，红地球出现了一次违规，而因为这次违规导致了直播间没能突破流量层级，获得平台更多的自然流奖励。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span id="article-img-2" class="a-image" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"><img style="margin: 0px auto 20px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: middle; border: none; max-width: 100%; display: block; background: url('//static.yidianzixun.com/apps/black_mamba_web_v1.0.0.177/black_mamba_web_main_pic_placeholder.png') 50% center no-repeat #e6e6e6;" src="https://i1.go2yd.com/image.php?url=YD_cnt_41_01EtAAAGFRAC" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<div>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin-bottom: 25px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">▲图片来自蝉妈妈</strong></h4>
</div>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">大卓表示，在抖音人气马上要破级的时候，平台会有管理者进行二审，除了机器的排查，还有人工的审核，当人工审核不通过时，就会通过违规降低直播间的流量，这在美妆行业很普遍，需要多注意。</p>
<h2 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; font-weight: 600; color: #212629; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; text-align: left; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; line-height: 30px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">3、</strong><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">抖音投放底层逻辑</strong></h2>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">在看完红地球整场的数据，大卓对当前的流量情况进行了总结，红地球直播间的整体ROI好，并不是投放优秀，而是因为长时间直播且付费占比低所致。而这种情况，是很难获得平台支持的，抖音付费投流的底层逻辑无非有二：</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">①抖音是一个讲求流量效率的平台，流量效率有投广告费的部分，如果无法成功跑量，平台直接收益会降低。</strong></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">②除了广告费，流量效率还有其本身转化成交的部分。从GPM来看，自然流量和付费流量的GPM在下降，则流量的利用效率也在下降，对平台来说，为什么不给其他直播间，获取更多的成交呢？</strong></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">基于抖音的付费逻辑，大卓建议红地球要优化投放策略，提升流量效率。</p>
<h1 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: 30px; color: #212629; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 20px; text-align: center; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); font-weight: 600; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">美妆投放标准答案，</strong><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">用付费节点撬动自然流</strong></h1>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">在整场诊断中，大卓多次提及流量优化的重要性，红地球直播间的负责人深有感触，她来到了现场连麦，并对付费流量如何跑量、免费流量如何提高转化等方面进行了咨询。对于此，大卓老师给出了答案：</p>
<h2 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; font-weight: 600; color: #212629; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; text-align: left; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; line-height: 30px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">1、</strong><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">视频跑量不是蓝海</strong></h2>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">首先，红地球直播间负责人表示，对于付费她们是不控制的，希望能够跑出更多量，目前他们主要依靠视频素材爆量，没有视频素材，就难以跑量。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span id="article-img-3" class="a-image" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"><img style="margin: 0px auto 20px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: middle; border: none; max-width: 100%; display: block; background: url('//static.yidianzixun.com/apps/black_mamba_web_v1.0.0.177/black_mamba_web_main_pic_placeholder.png') 50% center no-repeat #e6e6e6;" src="https://i1.go2yd.com/image.php?url=YD_cnt_41_01EtAACm8XTO" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<div>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin-bottom: 25px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">▲图片来自抖音</strong></h4>
</div>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">对此大卓表示，美妆赛道的素材跑量并不是蓝海，而且还容易卡审，建议优化直播间场景，尝试直投。</p>
<h2 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; font-weight: 600; color: #212629; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; text-align: left; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; line-height: 30px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">2、</strong><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">美妆红海要权衡成本增大投放</strong></h2>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">美妆类目在今年上半年整体呈下降趋势，在单彩妆、底妆的渠道里也是下降趋势，如今抖音是一个非常重要的增加销售额的方法，这个渠道在今天已经进入各美妆品牌的战略级规划中，即使你不进行投放，你的竞品也会通过投放抢占流量，其中红地球的付费占比仅有20%，而美妆行业的均值在50%以上。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span id="article-img-4" class="a-image" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"><img style="margin: 0px auto 20px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: middle; border: none; max-width: 100%; display: block; background: url('//static.yidianzixun.com/apps/black_mamba_web_v1.0.0.177/black_mamba_web_main_pic_placeholder.png') 50% center no-repeat #e6e6e6;" src="https://i1.go2yd.com/image.php?url=YD_cnt_41_01EtA9xiXOLk" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>

<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin-bottom: 25px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">▲图片来自蝉妈妈</strong></h4>

<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">大卓建议红地球将付费计划跑起来，可以适当降低对ROI的的期待值，让整个GMV提高，从红地球每天的高时长直播带来的人员成本等估算，在产出上至少要有5W才能收回成本，所以让投手减少控量，多跑些产出去平衡大账是有必要的。</p>
