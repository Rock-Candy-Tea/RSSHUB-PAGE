<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>继520后，又一个大型示爱节点七夕到了。作为传统节日，七夕的爱情属性更为纯粹鲜明，人们乐于在七夕送礼互表爱意，迎接一场浪漫狂欢。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><span><strong><span>七夕拉动的“浪漫经济”更是兵家必争之地，品牌们纷纷推出广告大片、限定礼盒、定制产品，以烘托浓郁的爱情氛围。</span></strong></span></span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>受固定的年度性营销命题限制，消费者已然产生审美疲劳。因此，为了突破营销瓶颈，今年品牌力求在内容创意和营销方式上进行革新，一起看看带来了哪些新玩法？</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/723115795623260770c18c4.52658385.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span><strong><span>创意脑洞整活，</span></strong></span><span><strong><span>撬动话题热度</span></strong></span><span><span></span></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>随着传统营销体系失效，<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E4%B8%83%E5%A4%95%E8%90%A5%E9%94%80" target="_blank">七夕营销</a>难度骤升。一些品牌打破常规，用创意释放营销活力，重构七夕营销场景，为品牌贡献讨论热度。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><span><strong>1、天猫开了间「七夕情蔬超市」</strong></span><span><span></span></span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>上海疫情爆发，一菜难求，推动了「蔬菜文学」的兴盛。今年七夕，天猫同样以炙手可热的蔬菜为营销主题，联手七大品牌发起七夕特别企划，当浪漫的七夕与接地气的蔬菜混搭，碰撞出不一样的火花。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>情书谐音情「蔬」，天猫从中找到连接的创意点，主张「写 N 行情书，不如种种情蔬」，让拟人化的蔬菜化身助攻神器，帮助用户传达难以开口的绵绵情话。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>同时，视频中呈现的蔬菜培育过程，隐喻爱情从萌芽到成长，同样需要彼此的用心栽培，寓意深刻。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong>2、金典奉上娟姗牛的「爱情宝典」</strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>影视剧中的丘比特大家见怪不怪，但是假如一头牛也能成为爱情导师呢？七夕到来之际，金典以娟姗牛为主角，拍摄了一支别开生面的七夕大片。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>嗑CP、神助攻、研读爱情名著，娟姗牛自创一套爱情哲学，为年轻人的爱情献计支招。在魔性沙雕的画风中，植入娟姗牛珍稀奶源的产品卖点，犹如爱情般独特而珍贵，从而达成产品与传播内容的深度捆绑。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong>3、Burberry 诠释「八芒星」神话</strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>“爱的尽头是什么？”</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>今年Burberry在七夕节点，抛出了一个发人深省的提问。邀请「W 野狗艺术舱·坨比」，以精品店星型窗玻璃为灵感，通过创意动画形式，讲述了一个饱含哲学思辨意义的神话故事。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>短片中，以Burberry标志性格纹元素构建神话世界观，集齐包容、责任、感恩、认真、真实、互助、积极、信赖八大美好特质，方能召唤「爱的八芒星」。透过虚构神话寓言，Burberry的品牌理念和爱情观深入用户心智。</span><span></span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong>4、旺旺上演浪味仙版「非诚勿扰」</strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>每逢重大营销节点，往往是品牌推出新品的绝佳时机。旺旺在七夕之际，上新玫瑰味粉色浪味仙，并拍摄了一支画风清奇的广告片，为新品预热。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>结合七夕的爱情特性，旺旺举办「相亲节目」，让番茄味、田园蔬菜味以及新人玫瑰味浪味仙化身女嘉宾，在趣味化的自我介绍中呈现浪味仙的美味口感，给观众留下深刻的印象记忆。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/205789392362326078647a25.53043458.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span><strong><span>聚焦新兴趋势，</span></strong></span><span><strong><span>探讨爱的新命题</span></strong></span><span><span></span></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>元宇宙热潮兴起，当全新的变革浪潮来临，势必会冲击和影响我们的生活，于爱情亦然。在此背景下，品牌试图探索科技与爱的边界，解析新时代爱情真相。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>1、巴黎世家演</span></strong><strong><span>绎A</span></strong><strong><span>I的爱情</span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>今年巴黎世家的七夕广告，依然备受争议。与往年土味复古画风截然不同，将视线放到了虚拟科技层面，颇具先锋性。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>巴黎世家首次启用AI机器人为主角，演绎七夕的浪漫穿搭。机器人机械性的动作，搭配经典情歌《甜蜜蜜》的旋律，萌生出一种时空交错的科幻感。在荒诞割裂的情绪中，诉说爱无边界的品牌态度，拉满视觉冲击效果。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>2、杰士邦高举</span></strong><strong><span>「</span></strong><strong><span>反元宇宙</span></strong><strong><span>」</span></strong><strong><span>旗帜</span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>关于元宇宙的概念，社会各界争论不休。随着科技的高度发展，人的注意力也会随之稀释，忽视真实世界的互动，人际距离<span>疏远</span>。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>七夕将至，杰士邦鼓励人们走出“恋爱的元宇宙”，放下对虚拟世界的依赖，在真实的触碰中享受爱情的美好，启发人们对虚拟时代的亲密关系的思考。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/134948845962326079426029.07408166.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span><strong><span>回归爱情主题，</span></strong></span><span><strong><span>拨动用户心弦</span></strong></span><span><span></span></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>自古以来，七夕的关键词非爱情莫属，在全民浪漫示爱的节日，“情感牌”仍然是营销的杀手锏，品牌细腻叙说爱情故事，唤起人们心中的点滴爱情回忆，搭建有效的情感沟通路径。</span><span><span></span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>1、野兽派见证爱的新生</span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>今年是野兽派认领大熊猫宝宝“噗噗”的一周年，正逢七夕，野兽派邀请代言人龚俊与吉祥物噗噗组成“俊俊噗噗”组合，以“爱的治愈 爱的新生”为主题推出七夕大片。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>黑白默剧的创意表现风格，增添幽默戏剧效果，让产品融入其中，窥见野兽派品牌内在的审美格调。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>2、华为寻找七夕的主角</span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>关于爱情的表达，就如一千的读者有一千个哈姆雷特，或许每个人的方式都不同。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><span>这个七夕，华为统一爱情的表达方式，邀请六组情侣/夫妻组合试镜七夕广告，用年轻人的方式拍摄爱情vlog，</span><span>让时光赋予影像情感沉淀，见证爱情的成长。</span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>3、奈雪心动上映《戒不掉的那杯茶》</span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>七夕来临，奈雪的茶贴心准备七夕礼，购买新品霸气草莓龙卷风的消费者，即可获得一枚「奈雪爱心戒」，为此，奈雪的茶还上映了一支七夕广告，演绎爱心戒的故事。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>相爱容易，相守很难。在苦苦寻觅无果后，奈雪爱心戒的出现连接了两颗坚定的心。有甜蜜，也有争吵，爱情的酸甜滋味正如奈雪新品的口感风味，珍藏心意。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/138833517962326079dd1f53.73402961.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span><strong><span>跨界梦幻联动，</span></strong></span><span><strong><span>焕发IP影响力</span></strong></span><span><span></span></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>七夕营销战场激烈，品牌放弃单打独斗，通过与品牌、艺术家、大众熟悉的IP开展联名合作，是七夕常见的营销打法。最大程度发挥品牌效应，打通目标受众圈层，倾斜流量池，增添营销砝码。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>1、瑞幸蛙瑞喜欢你</span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/48915332162ec27d63989b9.02113138.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>七夕演变至今，不再是情侣的狂欢，也可以是单身人士的玩梗专场。瑞幸联动悲伤蛙 PEPE ，推出七夕特定饮品，为单身人士奉送暖心关怀。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>悲伤蛙 PEPE 与女友翠花比心合影，传递瑞幸的美好祝福和陪伴：七夕不咕呱，蛙瑞喜欢你。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/21352461962ec27d6e4fb90.51169747.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>2、泡泡玛特 × ROSEONLY：爱·无畏</span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><span><span>泡泡玛特携手</span> ROSEONLY 推出<span>七夕</span></span><span>「爱·无畏」限定联名系列</span><span>，保留了SKULLPANDA标志性的双侧耳球宇航员头盔，荆棘玫瑰在娃娃脑后盛放，赋予穿越时空的魔力，在宇宙中建立爱情纪元，象征真爱恒久无处不在。</span></span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>年轻人喜爱的盲盒和一生一世人ROSEONLY相遇，深度融合潮流文化抢占年轻人心智，为双方加乘品牌价值，无疑是一次双赢的尝试。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/9674656862ec27d7bbb728.86865735.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>3、和平精英 × YSL空投爱意</span></strong></span><span><span></span></span></p>
