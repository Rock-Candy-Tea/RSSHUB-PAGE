<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><em style="font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', Roboto, Oxygen, Ubuntu, Cantarell, 'Open Sans', 'Helvetica Neue', sans-serif;">看起来，是个憨憨</em></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>——日站君</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/227795564631ea5741976c8.97514678.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E6%97%A5%E6%9C%AC" target="_blank">日本</a>，有一家杂货连锁，从物美价廉的角度夸张一点讲，可以说是<strong>能把<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/MUJI" target="_blank">MUJI</a>都“干碎”的存在</strong>。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>它就是ニトリ（Nitori）。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在谷歌日本上，如果你把Nitori和人気ない作为关键词结合搜索，你几乎是搜不到匹配的结果的，反之，多数搜索结果都会告诉你这个牌子简直太棒棒，必选！</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/1576169970631ea574766868.92767461.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>然而，就是这样一个在日本人气绝佳的杂货品牌，其实<strong>在中国已经开了6年</strong><strong>，但是仿佛没啥存在感……</strong></span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>你平常逛过Nitori吗？它究竟为啥没人气呢？</span><span>今天，日站君从各种角度，给大家胡乱分析一下。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/35467604662c705cad14f21.29628532.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span><strong> 设计上有什么问题 </strong></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>· Logo店头，缺乏辨识度 ·</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>作为一个Nitori铁粉，隔三差五都得去转一圈，曾经有朋友笑说日站君是不是入了股。然而，不得不说从<strong>国内的Nitori的设计，确实是缺乏辨识度</strong><strong>的</strong>。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>原本的设计，三个日文假名相当的鲜明，形状也经过了重新设计，显得很有个性。远远的竖在那里，让人可以一眼辨识出来。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/219511254631ea574a83603.75910859.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>不论是在室外还是室内，<strong>简洁明了</strong>，一眼就能抓住人的眼球。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/1968316755631ea574e1cc80.33706059.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/585915021631ea5758230f7.29721554.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>然而到了国内，设计则变得非常平庸，仅仅用NITORI的英文加底色标志设计出小LOGO，整体设计感降低了一截。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/1969896648631ea576b60d08.85306845.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>也许是由于NITORI本身的英文也不容易让人理解，所以还同时加上了“家具&家居”的字样，后期还改成了“日本著名家具&家居品牌”。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong>生硬的加字</strong><strong>，不假思索的设计</strong>，结果看起来就像家居大卖场里平平无奇的店一样，有时候和全棉时代傻傻分不清楚。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/1883928751631ea577f267f0.49104919.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/83902563631ea579362275.00354758.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>实际上，Nitori的名字确实是一大难题，由于原意是创始人似鸟昭雄的日语姓氏，结果很容易让人根本弄不清楚是干啥的。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>甚至连官方也费了不少劲，<strong>连改两次名，最初叫“尼达利”</strong>。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/1082150660631ea57aa3d629.09802646.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>直到2021年，又把名字改成了<strong>“宜得利”</strong>。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/1306896596631ea57c50e3c7.57773588.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>然而，与MUJI无印良品、IKEA宜家相比，Nitori在命名上终究是缺了那么一些感觉……</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>· 动线设计，打不过宜家 ·</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>既然说到了宜家，其实朋友们大概也看出来了。是的，Nitori这个自北海道札幌起家的品牌，其实<strong>在日本本土最开始对标的就是宜家。</strong></span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/923218125631ea57ce9aa74.43850460.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>但是，在<strong>设计陈列展示和动线</strong>这一环上，国内的Nitori却并没有像宜家一样非常深度。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>相信大家在逛宜家的时候就可以强烈的感受到一种感觉：“我是谁，我在哪，我在干什么”？</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/1527543004631ea57e9208e4.21590134.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>实际上，不论是国内还是国外的宜家，所有的<strong>动线都是经过潜心研究和设计过的</strong>。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/1518345643631ea57f543db7.47375711.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/1735229136631ea57fc5bc31.50756960.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>物品环环相扣产生关联，会给人一种“<strong>逛迷宫</strong>”的感受，但是也会<strong>升起人的探索欲和关联购买欲望</strong>。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/378947206631ea581a54f63.06611810.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/643573962631ea582788e69.44104296.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>然而，Nitori的区域动线，并没有这样的互相联系，多数门店仅仅是<strong>按照不同产品大类进行了区分</strong>。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>所有商品都堆在那里，互相没有联系，你可以在这个货架选购厨具的时候，下个货架就开始挑选卫浴用品。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/121445954631ea583480174.41221003.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>这固然对只想买了就走，有明确购物目标的人群来说是一件好事。</span><span>但是如此一来，也</span><strong>消减了人们“逛”的乐趣，更是无法培养出品牌记忆力。</strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>想想看，我们大概率不会记得偶然路过买瓶水的小店的名字。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/759316294631ea58478c9e6.04470274.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>· 生活方式的讲述，输了所有人 ·</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>由于这种性质，我们也可以发现<strong>Nitori几乎不讲生活方式</strong>。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>宜家曾凭借一本《家居指南》，让它成为万千室内设计爱好者、家居博主、北欧生活方式爱好者的唯一指定百科全书。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/2101824197631ea5856bbc01.07775132.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>无数人通过这本书获取灵感，打造出自己家理想的模样。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/1075189372631ea58780a3e2.50963424.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>它会设计一些好玩有趣的动图和漫画，从生活的各种角度吸引用户的兴趣。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/1942857791631ea588bd2610.17513187.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>还会以各种方言海报让人产生亲近感。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/627566523631ea58e238125.95447550.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/1042102409631ea58ed2b533.44394923.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>更会从各方面处处匹配你的生活方式。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/492352670631ea58f2906b2.40814864.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/2076588432631ea590e81f68.66179134.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>其他品牌，不论是<strong>MUJI、LoFt、niko and、蔦屋TSUTAYA哪怕最低调的中川政七</strong>，都会在生活方式上确立一个自己独特的观感。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/160648849631ea592b716f2.52120734.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/89944618631ea594683ac9.37689450.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>每个品牌都在奋力体现一种对于生活、家居、设计的理解。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/1476642781631ea59664ebf8.68278491.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/1278725930631ea59788cdd5.49266761.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>然而Nitori并不怎么讲这些——它完全没啥讨巧的、特别的营销设计。</span><span>“<strong>你买嘛，买完你就赶紧走了嘛</strong>”，它仿佛一个小卖铺阿姨，催你快快结账，然后赶紧离开。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>很难想这是不是一种创始人思维上的一种固执。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/1211587608608f8ba2bab225.33977934.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span><strong> 所以它强项到底是啥？ </strong></span><span></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>虽然Nitori仿佛几乎没有设计上的质感，但是为啥日站君会成为它的忠实粉丝。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>归根结底，还是它的产品确实<strong>能做到足够的物美价廉</strong>。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/1347655463631ea598a73796.63568610.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在油管上，你可以看到大量的推荐Nitori日用品的博主视频，一水儿的全是<strong>低价推荐</strong>。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/1765335431631ea599e9e1c4.98461989.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>品类几乎没有什么“神奇设计”，全是日常就会用的消耗品和生活用品，均价100日元，便宜到头了。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>所谓<strong>百元店的价格，品牌店的质量。</strong></span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/1506521494631ea59a65db35.29946709.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>之前，还有博主把<strong>德国高价品牌MAWA和Nitori的衣架</strong><strong>做了平行比较</strong>。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/776341785631ea59b62ba50.50225722.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>发现不论是从使用功能上、耐久度上、还是设计细部，都没啥大的区别。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/1984846686631ea59bcb5931.00222277.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/1327354602631ea59c1bced4.71796435.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>区别仅仅是在挂钩和区角的细部，然而<strong>MAWA的价格比Nitori足足贵了3倍</strong>。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/1362256806631ea59c8d0ff1.71059371.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>这也大概是为什么，<strong>Nitori也是日本主妇们最爱的品牌之一。</strong></span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>确实，妈妈们想买啥都是脑子里有谱的，节约时间直接一波带走，Nitori就是最好的存在。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/1807259686631ea59d06bbb4.60001512.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>而到了国内，其实Nitori也延续了这一品牌策略。实际上，正由于它是一家独资公司，<strong>内部很多服务，也都承继了日本本土的机制</strong>。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/1063799705631ea59e6fb680.34249285.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>不论是陈列、对店员的要求、收银、还是对购买产品包裹的精细，Nitori整体都比较符合日系品牌细致的作业方式。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>比如之前日站君为了买把菜刀，店员一时没查到货号，就一路小跑前后反复确认仓库库存最后找到，体贴的如同日本本土门店一样！</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>因此，<strong>从购物服务体验的角度来说，Nitori可以说是非常不错。</strong></span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/1446159369631ea5a006a501.99168614.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>同时，它的会员卡也是<strong>少数的积分可以直接抵现</strong>的存在，不像很多品牌的会员，花里胡哨整一堆，但是实际没什么实惠。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/741926698631ea5a09777a3.55332682.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>所以看到这里，大家似乎也能发觉Nitori很不错，但是又不火的原因了。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>它好像确实过于朴实了点……</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/956339854608f8ba3ea88c1.20596643.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span><strong> 这样低调的Nitori，能走下去吗？ </strong></span><span></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>自2016年进入中国，Nitori已经在大陆开出了30多家门店，然而自始至终秉承着低调、务实感的这个品牌，仿佛一直都不温不火，存在感很低。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>通过低价优质吸引人群的策略，是创始人的理念，但是在<strong>低价产品数量众多的中国市场上，似乎Nitori正处于一个尴尬的位置</strong>。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/223212653631ea5a0e45394.48302121.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在它的中国官网上，写着“物超所值的质感生活由此开始”和“站在使用、购买的立场来思考”。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>也许在消费下行的年代，它会迎来属于自己的新的机会，然而</span><span>不管怎么说，对于这样一家坚定固守的典型日式公司来说，</span><strong><span>如果不在设计和营销方式上做出一些变化，可能终究是难以迎来破局。</span></strong></p>
