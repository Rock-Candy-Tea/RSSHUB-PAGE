<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify;" data-track="2"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">本文就将通过大数据来“透视”今年6月份在抖音平台上进行投放的游戏概况，并分析部分游戏的投放策略。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify;" data-track="3"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1.5px;"><strong style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">本期月报亮点抢先看：</span></strong></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify;" data-track="4"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #0070c0; --tt-darkmode-color: #0070C0;">1）抖音游戏投放概况：投放≥15天的游戏广告主数量下降明显；</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify;" data-track="5"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #0070c0; --tt-darkmode-color: #0070C0;">2）6月抖音游戏投放榜单揭晓：《封灵诀》投放达人最多，《斗罗大陆：魂师对决》直播场数最多；</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify;" data-track="6"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #0070c0; --tt-darkmode-color: #0070C0;">3）网易游戏《无尽的拉格朗日》投放玩法解析；</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify;" data-track="7"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #595959; --tt-darkmode-color: #595959;">注：本文数据仅针对App Store的游戏，不包含安卓及其他平台的游戏数据。</span></span></p>
<h1 class="pgc-h-arrow-right" style="box-sizing: border-box; font-size: 1.056em; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; line-height: 1.579; position: relative; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; text-align: justify;" data-track="10">游戏投放概况</h1>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify;" data-track="12"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">根据蝉大师游戏直播数据显示，</span><strong style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">2022年6月份共有433款游戏App参与抖音游戏投放</span></strong><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">，与去年同期相比增长了34.47%，但较上一个月下降了6.7%。</span></span></p>

<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify;" data-track="15"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">1）</span><strong style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">游戏版号少</span></strong><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">，这也正是近些年游戏厂商们的最大痛处；</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify;" data-track="16"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">2）</span><strong style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">投放策略不断升级</span></strong><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">，现如今游戏厂商对于抖音游戏这个渠道的投放早已不再是入门级的摸索式投放，而是已进阶到了精细化投放。同时这也意味着，今后游戏厂商抢占抖音游戏市场的难度在不断增加。</span></span></p>
<h1 class="pgc-h-arrow-right" style="box-sizing: border-box; font-size: 1.056em; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; line-height: 1.579; position: relative; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; text-align: justify;">游戏投放榜单</h1>

<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 20px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; caret-color: #222222; color: #222222; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify;" data-track="22"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1.5px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">排在6月份抖音「投放天数榜」前列的游戏为网易旗下的《无尽的拉格朗日》、龙图游戏的《无神之界》、诗悦网络的《闪烁之光》、雷霆游戏的《问道》、球宝工作室的《球球英雄》以及林子互娱的《芒果斗地主》等。</span></span></p>
