<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>抖音、快手两大主播，最近因为一根玉米吵了起来。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>快手顶级主播<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E8%BE%9B%E5%B7%B4" target="_blank">辛巴</a>最近很火，刚刚宣战完抖音运动主播刘耕宏，又立马把矛头指向了抖音带货新星<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E8%91%A3%E5%AE%87%E8%BE%89" target="_blank">董宇辉</a>和东方甄选。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>辛巴隔岸在线炮轰东方甄选在直播间打着助农的名义，卖高达6元一根的玉米给用户，从而赚取高达40%以上的暴利。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>辛巴透露自己从一开始的喜欢东方甄选到现在的厌恶，认为东方甄选并没有实质性的助农，忘了初心。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>那么今天村长站在个人的角度和大家聊聊，这场玉米事件背后透露出的一些问题和现象。</span></p>
<h1 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); font-size: 20px; color: #212629; text-align: center; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span><strong><span>01、</span></strong></span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span>每个人的经历、认知不同</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></h1>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>这件事情，从本质上来说，辛巴没有错，董宇辉和东方甄选都没有。</span></strong><span></span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>因为辛巴和东方甄选接触的产品、客户、环境是不一样的，从而造成了认知上的冲突。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>就像同样是一双袜子、一个手机壳，有的卖几块钱，有的卖几十块钱。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>包括辛巴自己就是案例，一件普通T恤最多就二三百，但辛巴买一件名牌T恤就要三五千、一两万，你说它的材质真的就有那么大差别嘛？不见得，那为什么不去抨击别人赚取暴利呢？</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>包括我们平常线下买鸡蛋，有的地方茶叶蛋一个一块钱，有的地方三块钱，一定就是暴利吗?</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>一件产品会因为产地、技术、口感、工艺、销售渠道、制作师傅、季节等不同，造成价格上的差异。</span></p>
<h1 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); font-size: 20px; color: #212629; text-align: center; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span><strong><span>02、</span></strong></span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span>老百姓最终获利了没有</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></h1>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>无论是辛巴说的玉米七八毛一个还是董宇辉说的2块钱回收一个，如果大家的真心都是助农。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>就应该把问题关注到农民身上，如果真的是七八毛一个，农民真的赚钱了吗？</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>同样两块钱一根的玉米，农民拿到了多少钱，就算两块钱农民都拿到了，农民是不是赚钱的？</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>另外，一定都要卖越便宜越好吗？</span></strong><span></span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>玉米是不是非要卖1块钱或者两块钱一根才算合理，如果价格压到这么低，最终是不是农民受益。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>辛巴和东方甄选都在用自己的方式助农，那是不是要问问农民满不满意？</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>农民拿到更多钱了吗？农民卖出去更多货了吗？农民的种植积极性提高了吗？</span></strong><span></span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>否则这些争辩都毫无意义。</span></p>
<h1 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); font-size: 20px; color: #212629; text-align: center; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span><strong><span>03、</span></strong></span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span>服务好自己的消费人群</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></h1>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>其实辛巴在提出这样的问题之前，应该想一个问题：东方甄选6元一根的玉米卖了这么久，用户也有争议的情况，为什么还有那么多的消费者会下单和复购？</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>是不是所有的玉米都应该卖两三块一根才合理？那超市里面、餐馆里卖超过6块钱一根的玉米是不是都要被抨击？</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>辛巴在做对比的时候，是不是应该在同品质、同服务的产品下，再进行比较才更合理？</span></strong><span></span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>其实这还不是最终的，反而通过这个玉米事件，我想告诉各位。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>牢牢抓住属于你自己的那部分客户，并且服务好他们才是最重要的。</span></strong></span><span></span><span></span></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><strong><span>a、再贵的产品都会有人买</span></strong><span></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>因为每个人的消费能力、视野、认知、价值判断是不一样的，所以某种意义上不存在不合理。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>不是所有的东西，都有唯一的价格。</span><span></span></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><strong><span>b、大家的用户不同</span></strong><span></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>辛巴和东方甄选的客户是完全不同的，或者哪怕是同一类群体，但在消费不同产品上的决策是不同的。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>辛巴的粉丝更多像拼多多、淘特的用户，而东方甄选的客户类似京东、天猫的客户。</span></strong><span></span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>所以说，喜爱辛巴的客户有几百上千万，同样愿意在东方甄选、交个朋友直播间消费的客户也有几百万。</span></p>
<h1 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); font-size: 20px; color: #212629; text-align: center; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span><strong><span>04、</span></strong></span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span>消费者信任很重要</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></h1>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>这次玉米事件，有人觉得辛巴说的对，真正关心农民和考虑消费者，让消费者不吃亏。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>也有人说东方甄选没有错，买的玉米很好，已经连续复购了好几次。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在东方甄选的直播间买东西，不用担心产品质量和服务，只要需要和喜欢，可以闭着眼睛买。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>大家可以看看，在东方甄选的直播间几乎没有和其他主播一样，搞什么一分钱、一元、九块九秒杀。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>那为什么每天还有这么多人买呢？</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>我想更多还是信任，出于对品质、服务的相信，否则都火了三个月了，真的品质不好，早就爆了。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>因此，无论是辛巴还是东方甄选，做好选品，对自己消费者负责。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>持续构建消费者的信任才是最需要做的事情，比如把好选品关。</span></strong></span></p>
<h1 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); font-size: 20px; color: #212629; text-align: center; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span><strong><span>05、</span></strong></span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span>信息差、资源差永远存在</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></h1>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>那最有价值的一个问题来了？</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>为什么东方甄选6块钱的一根玉米有人买？而不是所有人都去辛巴那里买一两块一根的玉米？</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>这是我今天写这篇文章的根本原因。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>无论大家的消费能力有多大差距，对于多数人来说，信息差和资源差才是导致消费最大的不同。</span></strong></span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>信息差和资源差，也才是大家赚钱的核心条件</span></strong><span>，例举三个简单的例子。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>1、为什么抖音切片号能赚钱，许多人在剪辑小杨哥、郝邵文等直播视频，不就是许多人有消费能力和需求，但是之前没有看过吗？</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>这才有了流量的外溢，剪辑带货号才赚到了钱。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>2、为什么淘宝优惠券十几年了还能赚钱？不就是因为还有几亿用户不知道淘宝商家还设置了隐藏优惠券吗？用券后还能返佣金吗？</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>3、为什么以前海外代购很火，还有现在很多异地零食代购也很火。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>就是把盒马、山姆、鲍师傅等网红超市的产品从省会城市代购到周边的四五线城市。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>根本原因就是利用了信息差、资源差，从而赚到了钱。</span></strong><span></span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>所以希望大家能够深刻的认识到这一点，比辛巴说的更便宜的玉米还有，比东方甄选更贵的玉米也还有。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>而这些产品都通过各种渠道卖出去的，都让别人赚到钱了，这才是核心。</span></p>
