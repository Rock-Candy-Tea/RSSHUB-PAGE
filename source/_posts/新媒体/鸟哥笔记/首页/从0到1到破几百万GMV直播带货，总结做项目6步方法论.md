<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnGMsyqE6WguQCyiuBRVQBwc" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">为什么要复盘？</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnKEUcGESUa4a2KGDvVPS8zf" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">一来是沉淀下做项目的经验，二来体验做项目的节奏感把控感，三来希望能够对做项目的小伙伴有启发，少走弯路，少踩坑。</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnoCMmoqcIAc8I8d0dyUuGvg" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">那这个项目对我来说，有什么特别的地方呢？</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnm2Yw2U6Uqaa0pS1iLAuqrs" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">主要有2个挑战：</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">该项目相对没有平台优势，更多是从0资源起步</span>。之前做的项目，更多是有平台资源扶持，有品牌，有资源，有IP势能，那么做项目起来会比较容易。现在切换到不太依靠平台资源，可完整经历的0资源项目历程。<span style="font-weight: bold;">这对于小伙伴完全从0做项目，可能会有启发</span>。</p>
</li>
<li>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">自己之前未接触<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E7%9B%B4%E6%92%AD%E8%BF%90%E8%90%A5" target="_blank">直播运营</a>，直播带货，需快速上手，对自己也是一个小小的挑战。</span>之前更多是接触微信私域生态玩法，现在需要拓展到抖音，快手，视频号生态，同时流量性质完全不一样，量级也是不一样，也是一个不小的挑战。刚好可<span style="font-weight: bold;">借事修人，借假修真</span>。</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnQK8iCs8eSGcUauOj08MFDh" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">这个项目经历了很多次过山车似翻转，每次觉得快不行了，机会和运气总会跑到这边来，也踩中了一些红利。也是运气比较好，这个项目从去年9月份到今年6月份，GMV早就破了好几百万（需保密）。</p>

<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnUmG44ssiIQ8wakZNrfQxCg" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">从下面这个销售额&直播时长图中，你也能感受到项目是怎么一步步起来的。<img style="border: 0px; max-width: 100%; height: auto; cursor: pointer;" data-action="zoom" src="https://www.niaogebiji.com/undefined" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnMACmoOWCe0s049C5RYoOkf" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">需要说明的是：</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">项目并非一个人功劳。</span>这个项目并非是一个人就能完成，需要涉及到主播、内容运营、直播运营、私域运营等多部门的配合协同，<span style="font-weight: bold;">项目的功劳都是在于团队</span>，而并非在于某一个人。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">部分内容会省略。</span>考虑项目涉及部分隐私，部分内容和数据不方便透露，因此，部分具体操作细节，部分数据将会省略，敬请谅解。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">做项目有方法但没有捷径。</span>看完复盘后，并不会让你掌握流量密码，甚至马上复制一模一样的项目；也不会让你感觉学会这几招，你也能做出百万级/千万级GMV项目。做过项目的小伙伴都知道，做项目影响因素太多了，需要天时地利人和。所以要降低预期，就当做开拓视野罢了。</p>

<h1 id="toc-1" class="heading-2 ace-line old-record-id-doxcnWG0GGy8I0a8quoEAt8j0uh" style="font-size: 20px; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); padding-left: 25px; background-position: 0px 5px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; line-height: 30px; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-size: 16px 20px; color: #212629; font-weight: 600; text-align: center;">一、项目是如何从0到1开展起来？</h1>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnYEYaQuiI0kCmqUzIWehCIc" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">复盘路线就按照项目的前期，中期，和后期，分别对应“守”、“破”、“离”不同的策略。</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnW6mKWsykAk400eb8XWE5lg" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">那么接下来，就会详细复盘下具体的历经过程。</p>
<h2 class="heading-3 ace-line old-record-id-doxcnAYeYqAIOC262eq7Ohmmavg" style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; line-height: 30px; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); padding-left: 12px; background-position: 0px 5px; font-size: 18px; color: #212629; background-size: 8px 20px; font-weight: 600; text-align: left;">1.1 项目前期—— 守</h2>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcn0UO4kaOa2QWYsZhFjlcaAf" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">什么是“守”，守的意思是保底能够达到对方的模式。</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnSC6qww6Cs4aQq4WJxIr6sd" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">用在项目上，就是复制对方的模式，快速拿到数据反馈。</span></p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcn422koEowsiwUymmfVolaYc" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">用一个词就是“抄”。虽然不要求做到像素级“抄”，那也要做到1:1复制地“抄”。</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcn8yg8GUyCQ28aS4xBg3vGkb" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">看到这里，你千万不要有很强的道德感，说怎么能“抄”呢？不应该是原创吗？为什么要“抄”别人的呢？</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnEGekUO2UMuiqElG6PAoTpb" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">看看，这就是陷入思维误区，我之前也是有这想法。</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcn8IYECey60iwqAf2eKMWL7e" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">首先，记住，出社会后，做项目是“公开考试”，你可以公开借鉴别人的试题，这是完全没问题的，只要你能复制。</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnwecw28GoU8qIYf6ac2iZEf" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">其次，“抄”也是一门技术，至于抄的是否在表面，是否在底层，这就因人而异了。</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnEGESiUykuiU0M9vADL3EQh" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">最后，直接向有结果的项目学习，是能够更大概率提高成功概率。因为他们已经帮你排雷了，能够留下来的，基本都是被刷出出来的，相当于他们是先锋者，你直接跟在后面，一些不该踩的坑，就很大概率可以少走。</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnEkayGWikucskuk2sgg1EVd" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">好了，你以为“抄”就完了？抄也是有步骤的。在“抄”之前还是有一些前期准备的。</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnGksGAkcWwmgQu4FYK1OLeh" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">比如，这项目在正式开展时，boss 就留意快半年，初步觉得这个项目可长期做，于是再组建团队再慢慢“抄”作业。</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnIKess2QG6WcKqpCSo38ngv" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">这里简单放一些“抄“核心的步骤，注意不是所有步骤哈。</p>
<h3 class="heading-4 ace-line old-record-id-doxcnEakYKKYWyGWAGuVjG0xDec" style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//1bb91f73e9d31ea2830a5e73ce3ed328.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 16px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;">1.1.1 项目前期调研</h3>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnAgOYA08cASCe6xpJnglvAd" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">如果你是做电商的，你应该清楚，做电商除了供应链特别重要外，另外一个很重要的环节就是“选品”。</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnyiIOS2uioSU8qIzu3BDZPf" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">用在项目上，<span style="font-weight: bold;">项目前期调研就很关键，</span>类似于选赛道/选行业，<span style="font-weight: bold;">这属于最最最核心的决策。</span></p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnuYSG6iUmMCSyOEnQYBY8Ug" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">那怎样看对标账号/赛道有没有选对呢？</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcncwsUE8E8m0GY8R8MDUS81e" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">可以重点考虑看下面这4点：</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">看国家政策，平台政策，判断是否能做。</span>如果是国家政策平台政策重点打压的，那么需要谨慎选择，比如K12，黑五类商品等等。所以，这个环节可多看国家政策规定，和平台相关规则。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">看对标账号生命周期，判断能否长期做</span>。重点看对标账号是否活过一年或者半年，看他生命周期如何。如果对标账号1-2年都能正常做，而且销量没有出现巨大下滑，相对比较稳定，那么可考虑跟进。这样做目的是，为了避免自己做项目时，做到一半，突然项目没有了。这个关键步骤，是为了评估生存的可能性。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">看近期是否有新账号出现，判断可复制成功概率。</span>重点是看这个项目近期1-3个月有没有新起的账号，看他们销售额如何，目的是判断自己能否能够模仿做起来。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">看账号流量结构，盘点所需要资源是否欠缺。</span>比如该项目是以自然流量为主，还是短视频+自然流量，还是自然流量+投放流量，还是自然流量+短视频+投放流量等等，不同的流量结构所需要的资源配置也是不一样。</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnaysyQcMwi26625hS8u99Oe" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">这个调研步骤很关键，因为是第一步，项目的源头，直接决定了接下来项目的开展。</span></p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnIAiuMcG00cCkSmpRtqgCbe" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">所谓选择大于努力，这里也可套用下，所以要努力地做选择，需要花费更多的精力。</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnuQqMGyy6Iso0Qwh0wm0KX8" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">这个项目，因为 Boss 留意了很长时间，经过数据分析和请教行家，因此做了一个关键决策：可做。</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnAuwOskkY0mMO605rswfE3g" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">这里我再分享一下，这个选择赛道和类目，属于关键决策，那如何提高提高关键决策呢？其实还是有方法的。</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnSIAeE6Wykm6gQby7NqQ39g" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">刚好最近学到一个决策方法，分享出来：</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcn0MSgW4UAqIwqOmgwjs5zUb" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">（对关键决策感兴趣的，可略过，不看哈）</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">关键决策原则<span style="font-weight: bold;">：“不可回撤决策找外脑，可回撤决策找外包。”</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>

<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">亚马逊贝索斯有一个“单向门/双向门”决策工具。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">第一种：单向门</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">什么是单向门呢？意思就是你只要跨过这个门，退回来不太可能，或者退回来，你也要脱很多皮，你会损失很大。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">第二种门，双向门。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">你跨过了这个门，如果你觉得不行，随时可以切换回来，而且退回来的成本和风险不是很高。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">所以，我们做决策时，就要先问自己一个问题，这个决策是单向门决策还是双向门决策。尤其是，单向门决策，这就很重要，也叫关键决策。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">而这个单向问决策，就是不可回撤决策，有条件最好是要找垂直领域的外脑咨询，做分析后，再深思熟虑后，再做决策。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">如果双向门决策，也就是可回撤的决策，我们应该快速做好决策，边干边优化就行。或者是直接找外包，更快落地。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">举几个例子：比如，拍摄视频需要布置场地，需要特别精美的那种。现在还没购买设备，购买需要等3-4天怎么办？或者怎样能快速验证？</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">最好的方式是，不需要先购买场景设备，直接上“美团”搜索“拍摄场地”，直接外包就好，这个属于双向门决策。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">边干边优化，避免在不太重要的细节上，花费太多时间。又比如，公司要转型，要选择新的发展方向，新的类目项目。</p>

<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">这就是属于单向门决策，不可撤回决策，需要多咨询外脑专家顾问，谨慎做决策。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">因为一旦方向错了，除了大量的金钱成本付出外，还有大量的时间成本，还有团队士气状态等等。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">好了，一不小心就拓展了，现在我们再拉回来。</p>

<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnoo4kcUswQgMIY1KNLIThcd"><span style="font-weight: bold;">为了做好这决策，我们请教了很多行业内的专家，给出的大部分建议是可行，市场足够大，于是最终决定尝试涨价。</span>这也造成，这个商品客单价在当时大部分竞品类直播间，属于数一数二的客单价。</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnOGsokS62ei0s4dAIU0tCsb"> </div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnOqWEkggeUEImGquTmnnjif">当然，交付方式也是采用商品差异化，我们新增产品，增加了竞品没有的产品，满足用户其它更个性化需求。</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnEq464WsYW0KQWwJIpWTpDf"> </div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnS04qseA8SuMgKcgVQ0Ytye">这一个策略，也是让我第一次感觉到，原来涨价也是一种很好的策略。<span style="font-weight: bold;">尤其是在流量差不多不变情况下，哪怕转化率有所下滑，提高客单价是一种很好的提升销量的方法。</span></div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnS04qseA8SuMgKcgVQ0Ytye">
<h3 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//1bb91f73e9d31ea2830a5e73ce3ed328.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 16px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;">1.2.3 优化转化率</h3>
</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnwCecs8Cuq20C0ivdF4EUqd">因为直播间更多承载的是转化环节，所以谁的转化率高，在同等流量下，销售额更高，同时系统推荐的流量也会持续增加。用稍微相对比较专业的话描述就是<span style="font-weight: bold;">，谁的GPM、UV值越高，系统推荐更多的流量。</span></div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnYIqOiomYAsQScvWp9f520f"> </div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnYiCI62y4i6iyUJDgUuwDOd">为了优化直播间转化率，我们主要是从视觉和听觉两方面进行优化：</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnqyYUCOwYiu4QEhY1eEZFlh"> </div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcngyick2WwskUMgZbufh1XIb"><span style="font-weight: bold;">视觉</span>：<span style="font-weight: bold;">核心是人无我有，人有我优。</span>竞品没有的物料，我们有；竞品有的物料，我们做的更好，更吸引人，更有说服力。</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnicgCK40Eyw80KM6tXDrISd"> </div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnciCssWE2S2CMm0NiZcU4hd"><span style="font-weight: bold;">听觉</span>：核心是通过话术了解用户需求，提供产品满足用户需求。核心是话术结构，直播状态，直播语气，直播节奏等等。</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcn6O6y82QSYySMILWXU89hxe"> </div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnsKsGaGgmUAAW4yWG7hscQb"><span style="font-weight: bold;">做数据</span>：因为直播更多是采用店铺模式，做数据相对比较少。但如果靠更多系统直播间推荐流量，那么互动数据和成交数据就需要重点做。</div>
<h2 class="heading-3 ace-line old-record-id-doxcn4aQoyqAGSGMmw82Ao6B3wl" style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; line-height: 30px; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); padding-left: 12px; background-position: 0px 5px; font-size: 18px; color: #212629; background-size: 8px 20px; font-weight: 600; text-align: left;">1.3 项目后期 —— 离</h2>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnO0iWUOUQAmmwtygmZA8loR">到了这一个时期，就是尽可能跳出原来的业务，尽可能能够增加业务的壁垒，或者是放大业务模式，形成自身的优化。</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnI844Q82uww0kWOq7Qi7u1d"> </div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnUgOUaAiiYKoo2jVFugv6Yc">公司有个优势，获取信息能力很靠前，一些红利机会会比较容易踩到，基于此，我们做了一些关键动作，让项目进一步放大。</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnqSwAOYy6g66Qe85xYHYxId"> </div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnooIU6KWYygesCEGWxpZrHh">思考想后，我们主要做的一些动作如下。</div>
<h3 class="heading-4 ace-line old-record-id-doxcn28iuEQww6Kw4s1YqFSGkNg" style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//1bb91f73e9d31ea2830a5e73ce3ed328.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 16px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;">1.3.1 直播模式变化</h3>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcn2MGkEMI8KUEEITAIj8urff">常规的竞品直播模式是每人大约播1.5-2小时，再换主播继续播，同时下播后扮演场控继续直播，一直持续到10个小时以上。</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnSC4YQeUsIgKQOCvcmy97Ec"> </div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcn0w0aEyQ6S4eeoHLkrdxrTg">那这样会出现2个问题：</div>
<ul style="padding-left: 40px; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.6); list-style: none;">
<li style="position: relative; padding-left: 20px; margin-bottom: 5px;">主播的时间比较零碎，如果主播比较多，还存在在公司排队等主播的情况；</li>
<li style="position: relative; padding-left: 20px; margin-bottom: 5px;">主播直播1.5-2小时，本身比较疲惫，如果还需继续充当场控，精力不一定足够，状态会明显下降。</li>
</ul>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcn8cYeSi2YSwQGwlwTSH7ibf"> </div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnQikkisI0IW64iYZybX4J8e">基于此，通过借鉴公司其它项目直播模式，同时请教兄弟部门运营同学，我们改变了直播模式，通过该模式，极大解放了主播零碎的时间，方便安排主播排班。</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnsWiAGuM2S6ckp1ascxITrV"> </div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnOIAGImKIa6SyaQQbOifbsc">该模式就是：</div>
<ul>
<li>训练单个主播一场直播3.5-4小时，全程控制当下直播间，取消场控角色，方便主播更能以好状态进行直播。</li>
<li>排班也划分为凌晨场、上午场、下午场、傍晚场、晚上场，不同场次安排不同类型主播。</li>
</ul>
<p>确定该模式后，排班效率极大提高，主播直播效率也得到提升，同时也方便对主播进行考核和分析转化情况，该模式项目进入半自动运营阶段。</p>
<h3 class="heading-4 ace-line old-record-id-doxcne6oMwSCo4wGyyYdIirczId" style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//1bb91f73e9d31ea2830a5e73ce3ed328.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 16px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;">1.3.2 新增SKU</h3>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnqOgEuEAIyqYkglioKwFoEf">为了更高效转化流量，除了和竞品有类似商品外，我们也新上架了新SKU，如果用户还有其它需求，那么还可以用其它商品满足他们的需求。</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnAqiqu0WSSyiUwVct5CmIVc"> </div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnSa6yUgwkoyuEOaqd6IxNvh">突然想到，对公司来说，可能单独打爆一个商品会比较稳，毕竟可复制性更强。一旦某个模式跑通了，即可进行批量复制。</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcn4wGCKY8GMKkuuAoHsHfXNg"> </div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnu0EqCqKs0wAqm8DH3bbxBg">但对主播来说，如果长时间讲一个商品，确实很容易会厌倦，陷入疲劳期。如果又新增SKU，可以讲解不同的商品，同时还能创造新的收入，对主播来说，也是一个很不错的方式。</div>
<h3 class="heading-4 ace-line old-record-id-doxcn0UuqgMeyCoaGS0JERWquAf" style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//1bb91f73e9d31ea2830a5e73ce3ed328.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 16px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;">1.3.3 投放扩大</h3>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcn8g0aS02mUWUgKaJsaGP0Pg">在说投放之前，有个原则必须要永记心中，即：“<span style="font-weight: bold;">投放只能是锦上添花，而不是雪中送炭</span>”。</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnYGi8uOA8KQIcKOz32wWEDd">换句话说，如果直播间转化率就是渣的不行，那么投放就是加速项目的死亡。</div>
<p> </p>
<p style="color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img class="aligncenter" style="border: 0px; max-width: 100%; height: auto; display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; cursor: pointer;" src="https://image.woshipm.com/wp-files/2022/08/NRayXGYm0G4US2gQLWib.jpeg" alt width="828" height="1228" data-action="zoom" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>

<p style="color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img class="aligncenter" style="border: 0px; max-width: 100%; height: auto; display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; cursor: pointer;" src="https://image.woshipm.com/wp-files/2022/08/SBjp4I8yDMrIJbA96qPO.jpeg" alt width="828" height="605" data-action="zoom" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<div class="image-uploaded gallery old-record-id-doxcn2KiWGOOqmCAsgbrvx7jQ8f"><img style="border: 0px; max-width: 100%; height: auto; cursor: pointer;" data-action="zoom" src="https://www.niaogebiji.com/undefined" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnAYsaycCiESUMMV4NYxekQg">后面通过投放发现，其实投放最核心的是两部分，即：<span style="font-weight: bold;">直播间转化率+投放素材</span>。</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnAYsaycCiESUMMV4NYxekQg"> </div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnCE2Wq4WoGa4gaaR7lrt5fb">核心抓好这2个要点，投放就能少走一些弯路。</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcncqakoYms6sCs65sjUEqSWc"> </div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcn8w0wuiIKCw24SEHHXzLwRb">如果硬要总结归纳成3个要点，那就是：<span style="font-weight: bold;">直播间转化率+投放素材+撞运气</span>。</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcn82YAIWggoSoIeCj0qHcnDe"> </div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnoIKuwSY48GAuG0avYb71vg">为什么会撞运气？</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnUCUsIiey2YGwEFu96Wf9Be"> </div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnaEgG26Ko0GAMMTnKjqXHxh">做过投放的小伙伴可能知道，同样的投放计划，同样的投放素材包，同样的投放时间点，最终转化的效果很大概率是不一样的。</div>
<div> </div>
<div class="image-uploaded gallery old-record-id-doxcnmm02sEAu0Uiwu81UtS7ULh"><img style="border: 0px; max-width: 100%; height: auto; cursor: pointer;" data-action="zoom" src="https://www.niaogebiji.com/undefined" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">虽然，某些平台投放没有跑通，但是至少收获了关于投放的一些经验教训。</div>
<div class="image-uploaded gallery old-record-id-doxcnySmgWYMSccqKWPFLypPpus"><img style="border: 0px; max-width: 100%; height: auto; cursor: pointer;" data-action="zoom" src="https://www.niaogebiji.com/undefined" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnayCQqWkswy8schxIrntWzf">另外，<span style="font-weight: bold;">如果做投放，一定要得到上级/Boss 的高度支持</span>，这样你才能放得出手，才能更快拿到数据反馈，才能根据数据变化调整投放策略和投放计划，才会更快看到项目进展。</div>
<h3 class="heading-4 ace-line old-record-id-doxcnc8KEakYg0Iic2RUlu10Fah" style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//1bb91f73e9d31ea2830a5e73ce3ed328.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 16px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;">1.3.4 私域联动核武器</h3>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnc0WEKIoYgueOEpth0FPMmE">私域流量一词，从2020年以来就特火，尤其是在流量当下越来越贵，大家都想着如何高效使用私域流量。</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnWky66ayCoo4YI9G6IaSZcc"> </div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnsIaaSg40SI4WkBRlbpTKP0">那不用说，后面对私域流量重视度也是会越来越大。</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnIkoQuMuYKKk6y1QthMDZqa"> </div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnm0QEIoaimmGK2XbBnq9YSg">正如私域行业内有句话说的，<span style="font-weight: bold;">“做私域，把一次的生意做成一生一世的私域”</span>。虽然稍微有点夸张，这确实是一种相对底低价格高净值的流量。</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnGMIE6s4iy6u62Drqs1nwfh"> </div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnqUYaAGWSuEQc0soxfkU9ke">回过来，不得不说，结合平台的发展，<span style="font-weight: bold;">我们恰好又踩中私域联动的红利期</span>，进而能够快速放大私域+直播的价值。</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnMA2YyCACyEA2mAwcQAexqh"> </div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnmqU4MCaUMQCQaoohzG5NXb">这可能就是优质或者第一手信息的价值所在。</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnWcmwaUY0Y2sMgNkw2rHkQe"> </div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnWYkYsGyk6M2ywjfDkwfKFh">到了这一步，因为本身之前项目就有沉淀一部分私域流量，同时再结合做短视频不断沉淀新的私域流量，再用私域流量反哺直播间，最终效果是极好的，是直播中的核武器。</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcny0GSeWiIK8kO2NIM3FYx1f"> </div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnIIO8mA4cOGMKGi2JK0l43g">所以，在微信生态中，私域流量已经不是要不要做的问题，而是要持续做以及如何做得更好的问题。</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcns6k80M6wMC2WMRAG8prRdc"> </div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcniMWyq6uq6Q8AJPB049ucsJ">也就是说，到了这一步，我们通过直播私域联动，又可把规模持续不断放大很多倍。</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnckqKy4OG6kGoq2LdSGuTQh"> </div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnEIy2OSmomSSmml6leW3g0M">当然，涉及到具体操作流程，这里也写不完，就先写到这里，如有不清楚的，具体可详细再沟通交流。</div>

<h1 id="toc-2" class="heading-2 ace-line old-record-id-doxcnI2e2Ucs6YYmaAPevze8PQh" style="font-size: 20px; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); padding-left: 25px; background-position: 0px 5px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; line-height: 30px; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-size: 16px 20px; color: #212629; font-weight: 600; text-align: center;">二、做直播带货5个心得体会和避坑指南</h1>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnu44EsKU2omcEKEQIjdvjMb">到这一个环节，稍微理一下做直播带货的几个心得体会，做过项目的小伙伴可能会感同身受。</div>
<h2 class="heading-3 ace-line old-record-id-doxcnmWMUGoc2mCyAeygaM1wD4b" style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; line-height: 30px; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); padding-left: 12px; background-position: 0px 5px; font-size: 18px; color: #212629; background-size: 8px 20px; font-weight: 600; text-align: left;">2.1 项目前期阶段，无需全部准备充分，先快速拿到数据反馈很重要。</h2>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnqU22aS4eqkAYwnVVqUE4Of">这也是经常我会遇到的卡点，因为总想着先完美再完成，进而拖慢了一些进度。自从经历过这个项目后，更加坚定发现，项目先动起来会更重要，在做项目中不断进行调整和完善。</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcn0OOmwEmcowAAYt1SFIdcad"> </div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnA4WEiyE0G2i4IfytcPWXTd">尤其是最好刚开始就可以快速拿到数据反馈，这个会更有正向反馈。</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnOMmqiMw8cguMecemSsnp7d"> </div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnGkUCWGEc4uuWaKoNShWrMe">比如，这个项目在刚开始启动时，只有一个主播，还没有来得及招聘新的主播，那么boss甚至让我也充当几天的主播，甚至也出了几单，通过拿到了正向反馈，进而让团队更加有信心。</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcncOKcwy0Ua08iC0VPUMXy8e"> </div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnmasSgQsEOcU2KCRY1Zcyce">又比如，之前我们获取到一个优质信息，短视频涨粉会带动公众号涨粉。</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnYU2k4scSOkWoOw7XeihgAf"> </div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcns8ACU4GSUGas28hiV7Mr18">但收到这个信息到执行动作我还是慢了一天，为什么呢？</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcn642CYMYYWcgU6FLpGdgKYd"> </div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnMaw6KY4ymSKOgPTWTXVKLd">因为我觉得公众号现在文章啥都没有，这样对用户不太好，想着要不要花几天先发几篇文章后，再进行绑定。结果还是boss 再次强调可先绑定，后面再发文章，于是先照做了。</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcngM22MyqQiOyMolRCBaCLob"> </div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnyuKEW4ICQwuS4A1sfqDBge">最终，不如 boss 所料，哪怕是没有发文章，用户也能通过短视频大量关注到公众号，也不影响后续的文章推送。</div>
<h2 class="heading-3 ace-line old-record-id-doxcnUuiyggg2EI8YWmAnIEjVod" style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; line-height: 30px; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); padding-left: 12px; background-position: 0px 5px; font-size: 18px; color: #212629; background-size: 8px 20px; font-weight: 600; text-align: left;">2.2 做短视频内容核心抓住2个重要环节。</h2>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcneeqSquY2kmUU4HsGhokxch">内容生产是由内容运营的小伙伴负责的，我们知道，内容创作流程主要包括：选题、脚本、IP定位、拍摄、剪辑、账号运营 这6个大流程。</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnqYmYOwSSIAyq6DMw6AmX4d"> </div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnmeSGcCyIasw0a4x8proZPb">那么那几个流程是最最最重要的呢？</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnuOeSOeqkk4G6CybG9eOHwe"> </div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnAcm0sy8SCAOQWmkJLOb8Rd">很明显，<span style="font-weight: bold;">做内容最重要的是：选题和脚本，没有之一。</span>所以内容的核心就在于这两个环节，重要性占比80%以上。疯狂做好这两个环节就好，尤其是在人团队比较少的情况下，当然后面的4个大流程也不能忽视，更多的是锦上添花。</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnkEayaCmYE4GsAhXrseydbd"> </div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnGOO2uImOEWYaMhqdC2ivPb">之前看到张阳老师分享4种黄金选题，还是蛮有启发的，大家也可以去了解下，分别是：<span style="font-weight: bold;">创业故事、供应链优势、行业内幕、争议话题</span>。</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnyA80sCKY0OWYAB7pUE6Rgf"> </div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnSimE6kCWqKcAeohfb4DGuc">当然选题和脚本，这就是一门技术活了，这里就不继续展开了，我也正在学习中。</div>
<h3 class="heading-3 ace-line old-record-id-doxcn8akgg2YCssgqi8AMZ7XIo5" style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; line-height: 30px; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//1bb91f73e9d31ea2830a5e73ce3ed328.png'); padding-left: 12px; background-position: 0px 5px; font-size: 16px; color: #212629; background-size: 8px 20px; font-weight: 600; text-align: left;">2.3 做直播需要抓好3个点：视觉、听觉和做数据。</h3>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnSSEwMskGqGquEtUD4izqad">我们更多是在移动端刷到直播间，所以如果要简要分析直播间，那就从直播间视觉、听觉来吸引用户，通过数据来做更大系统推荐。（当然这个也可以用“人”、“货”“场”进行分析）</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnWa8Gy4oSI2Y8OxN1TZRB2o"> </div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnScC8Yw42yIaYk4sB9ra9ob">什么是视觉？视觉就是直播间场景搭建，如何能够吸引用户点击/停留。</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnGKqcqESiuyoGymsBSlp98g">什么是听觉？就是用户听到主播的脚本话术，愿意停留互动购买。</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcni64qsCYyqIQWGKe7vAs32f">什么是做数据？就是让系统觉得你的直播间是优质直播间，给直播间推荐更多流量。</div>
<h3 class="heading-3 ace-line old-record-id-doxcnCkoCUcME6ksG1CHq3EtWDQ" style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; line-height: 30px; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//1bb91f73e9d31ea2830a5e73ce3ed328.png'); padding-left: 12px; background-position: 0px 5px; font-size: 16px; color: #212629; background-size: 8px 20px; font-weight: 600; text-align: left;">2.4 私域和直播是协助关系，而不是竞争关系。</h3>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnowqOqoc4GqAeADsbWtx74e">私域和直播是一体的，如果转化端在直播间，那么私域更多是反哺流量到直播间。</div>
<div> </div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcngGmuseesqOAmEjNxvEo52g">而不是私域和直播间都来抢流量转化，否则会造成：私域部门叫用户在微信端成交，不要去直播间转化；直播间叫用户在直播间成交，不要在微信上购买，双方都出大矛盾，从整体来看，GMV是下滑的。</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnQwASWuuQ0isoYZBQSotOgc"> </div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnk2w84MgU0cmWOOgHfnS45b">那如何解决私域和直播间的问题呢？</div>
<div> </div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcn4M2Isyw8sGQiqkrwjBvE2g">核心方法是：<span style="font-weight: bold;">在私域+直播 设定一个共同的目标，双方都为这个目标发力。</span></div>
<h3 class="heading-3 ace-line old-record-id-doxcn86QEWu4QMy6KvFx2vf6ozB" style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; line-height: 30px; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//1bb91f73e9d31ea2830a5e73ce3ed328.png'); padding-left: 12px; background-position: 0px 5px; font-size: 16px; color: #212629; background-size: 8px 20px; font-weight: 600; text-align: left;">2.5 优秀的主播都具有类似特性，是筛选出来的，而不是培养出来的。</h3>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnI6awMiMw6OsuY545hmd5de">做过直播的小伙伴都知道，优秀的主播都有相同的共性，比如：迭代速度很快，学习力很强，求知欲很强，复盘能力很强，归因往内看等等。</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnQMIkIAKSaMcq0Emf1ybkBf"> </div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnsOom8qIeo2MsKoyI7FuZed">后面会发现，优秀的主播更多是筛选出来的，通过培养出来的，相对比较少。</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnmsIw6EWkCEumAjWBhx2ZAh"> </div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnSoMSoQeMGKiIUHhBY1VN4c">所以，把很大部分时间用于筛选优秀的主播，这一点很关键也很重要。</div>

<h1 id="toc-3" class="heading-2 ace-line old-record-id-doxcn0k28meCwMCWEE5fAARoWCf" style="font-size: 20px; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); background-position: 0px 5px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; line-height: 30px; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-size: 16px 20px; color: #212629; font-weight: 600; text-align: center;">三、初步总结做项目的6个步骤方法论</h1>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnUkOiOs6sq0aCoZqx8DNcQh" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">其实，从这个项目整个过程，你其实大概已经知道做整个项目，以及大致路径是什么。</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnYwAGISwiEieQWiOt773BNe" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">如果非要再总结归纳下，做项目的整体流程如下：</p>
<h2 class="heading-3 ace-line old-record-id-doxcnMEeiEYGqCkkmqewK6355yf" style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; line-height: 30px; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); padding-left: 12px; background-position: 0px 5px; font-size: 18px; color: #212629; background-size: 8px 20px; font-weight: 600; text-align: left;">第一步：选择赛道</h2>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnWi8kEgWSsG4aUxMVKRVxVh" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">这个无需再次多说，是个关键的一步，后面的所有努力都基于这个选项，所谓选择＞努力，自身的努力固然重要，也要考虑时代的进程。</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnsuI4SmQ2u0umwFaOJqb2od" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">你选择做什么行业，什么类目，什么人群的生意，决定着这个项目做到多大，能不能做起来。</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcn46UeU2emUwYOqMX1X5rCyh" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">因此，需要做好这个关键决策，可参考项目复盘前半部分：<span style="font-weight: bold;">亚马逊贝索斯“单向门/双向门”决策工具。</span></p>
<h2 class="heading-3 ace-line old-record-id-doxcn0Ik2Q8YuI4q24OhLUYQk2b" style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; line-height: 30px; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); padding-left: 12px; background-position: 0px 5px; font-size: 18px; color: #212629; background-size: 8px 20px; font-weight: 600; text-align: left;">第二步：提高审美能力</h2>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnq2cY4GSEG2QSkZr9b27gxh" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">选择好了赛道就可直接开干了吗？</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcniEAWuCiGsC4ycNlKzaaBSf" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">当然不是，你还要提高自身的审美能力。</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnAc4WwwgikYEmmUMPATZDzg" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">什么是审美能力？</span></p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnyEKeq2UoqE2wAbmEZXVazd" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">就是你知道什么是好的，什么是坏的，哪些可以参考，哪些是“只可远观不可亵玩焉”，目的是知道找哪些账号，哪些项目模式作为对标，谁可以作为竞争账号。</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcn268iEyYy4MmuWG2X9Cp2gh" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">而不是一上来就找到60分的账号就行模仿，再怎么模仿你最高也能只能达到60分。如果，你找到90分的账号，至少你可以努力达到80分左右，不会做的很差。</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnqOs0cyq0SogSQJtnPettle" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">那如何提高审美能力呢？</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcn8IyGAAecwQyCyakYK1Q2Bc" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">核心是多看，疯狂看。</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnw062oIIuCcUmuJIObZN2Vi" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">之前听过一位做抖音直播很厉害的操盘手分享，他说为了提高对内容的敏感度，他每天都要刷抖音。</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnQkKeYMMm4eOOOcDue1G1fd" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">你猜他一天要刷多少小时抖音？</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnWWy0qgimY2IUILOwfvP0jf" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">他说至少8小时。这样他的抖音推荐的账号，都是给他自动推荐对标账号。</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcn2wyMG2msKWuGCk4DHWYLcc" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">所以，<span style="font-weight: bold;">前期先看完100个对标账号，知道哪些账号做的好，哪些账号可以借鉴，哪些账号不适合复制，这样你才有上帝视角</span>。</p>
<h2 class="heading-3 ace-line old-record-id-doxcnK4WiIoIESMiMiO8tGtRsCf" style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; line-height: 30px; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); padding-left: 12px; background-position: 0px 5px; font-size: 18px; color: #212629; background-size: 8px 20px; font-weight: 600; text-align: left;">第三步：项目拆解</h2>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcn8IY8eIyiWOcQQ62RIPMNpb" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">看完了100多个账号后，其实你也知道了哪些账号是可以作为参考借鉴的，这时候可以再选择20多个账号进行拆解，拆解越细，复制概率就越大。</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnWOOGASi0cAGU6Xcd957jcf" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">那拆解什么呢？</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnMESkIgcugs4iuud1LRXeOg" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">核心还是围绕三大部分，即：<span style="font-weight: bold;">流量、变现、复购、裂变</span> 等维度去拆解。</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcn0UeyoA8YqIkUwXPqibC0uc" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">如果是直播带货，可从：<span style="font-weight: bold;">“人货场”、“短视频、直播间、商品交付</span>”等维度去拆解。</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnSIYuGWck2OswGsv5MELRFe" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">通过拆解，你就能发现项目是否可复制性，复制性概率如何等等，同时拆解颗粒度越详细，对于第四步特别关键。</p>
<h2 class="heading-3 ace-line old-record-id-doxcniGCuycmk42G2KYoC47XzRd" style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; line-height: 30px; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); padding-left: 12px; background-position: 0px 5px; font-size: 18px; color: #212629; background-size: 8px 20px; font-weight: 600; text-align: left;">第四步：复制模仿，精益执行，快速迭代</h2>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnIOGogI2WqYowS8hMb455ch" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">到了这一步，就是我们经常说的MVP环节，先快速跑起来，不一定要全面准备充分才去做，最核心的是快速拿到数据反馈，有了数据反馈才知道如何进行优化和调整。</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnseIsAGEgC0EwuOVqXJX3Fh" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">对了，对于这一步很关键，很多小伙伴就迫不及待要创新，要大改动。</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcn2oUikkgsKKgELxapg7Ewqh" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">其实，真实到这一步，<span style="font-weight: bold;">最核心的就是1:1复制</span>，哪怕你做不到像素级模仿，那至少先做到1:1复制吧。</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcniWIaOyek0e0eerO8Mah928" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">通过项目执行，再不断进行复盘，力争早日拿到数据反馈，无论是内容端流量涨了，还是直播间顺利成交1单，都是来自于项目的正向反馈。</p>
<h2 class="heading-3 ace-line old-record-id-doxcnw0cSokSaO6egOUpHE40Oaf" style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; line-height: 30px; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); padding-left: 12px; background-position: 0px 5px; font-size: 18px; color: #212629; background-size: 8px 20px; font-weight: 600; text-align: left;">第五步：超越对方</h2>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnOKoQOI4oiUYMOkTj3wB4Qg" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">到了这一步就是优化转化率，扩大流量。核心就是通过看更多的账号，找到自己直播间的优化点，把对方没有做好的，自己做的更好。</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnAeSuQcSmYQkwC45gF3q2Ef" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">比如，竞品只说案例没有展示案例，我们就展示案例；</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnA6kAyg8gI8aaQpv8URNnQe" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">比如，竞品只在某个平台分发内容，我们多增加一个平台分发内容；</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnwq6IcQAqEMuIeoAmayrvve" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">比如，竞品逼单营销环节没有做的很吸引人，我们就可特意优化该物料展示环节。</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcn0kwKc0E0EcE0y0Tncmlyuc" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">另外，其它行业的营销卖法，也可适当借鉴出来，底层的方法是想通的。</p>
<h2 class="heading-3 ace-line old-record-id-doxcnC2W8k6sIEesmmj3M265Ysb" style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; line-height: 30px; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); padding-left: 12px; background-position: 0px 5px; font-size: 18px; color: #212629; background-size: 8px 20px; font-weight: 600; text-align: left;">第六步：建立竞争壁垒</h2>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcncOAceOgKO0iUqCQyLzIndh" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">到了这一步后，你做的比同行更好后，现在要建立自己的项目壁垒。即，让同行的人复制起来比较难，或者做到知难而退。</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnEu6sYOG4s68Gykt4EP1gjd" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">比如，投放技能很牛，且能够投放ROI做到业内顶尖的水平，一般小玩家玩不起，复制起来比较难。</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnkO0AGYQgsGYUAddWp1TCLf" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">比如，做矩阵力很强，拥有工业化内容生产能力，一上来就是顶峰，基本上不给对方生存的空间。</p>
<p class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcn4O0uau4MsEIS80tfKjN2Db" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">至于如何构建竞争壁垒，我也没什么经验，还需要持续不断地学习当中。</p>
