<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-first-child data-pid="kqk-1DiO">随着营销渠道、营销方式的多元化，线上和线下交织互融，这其中，数据作为营销活动中最具说服力的构成元素，就成为营销活动中无可争议的关注点。需要说明的是，品牌方往往能够收集到各种类型的数据，但并非所有数据都是优质的、有价值的，如果只是重“量”而不重“质”，<span style="font-weight: 600; font-synthesis: style;">低质量数据做出的业务决策不仅会浪费营销预算，还可能损害品牌的声誉和形象。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-size="normal"><img class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb lazy" style="display: block; max-width: 100%; margin: 0px auto; cursor: zoom-in; background-color: transparent; animation: 0.5s ease-in 0s 1 normal none running fxRichTextFadeIn;" src="https://pic4.zhimg.com/80/v2-e721b90027522cb41b285d9c079ae0eb_720w.jpg" width="1080" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="606" data-original="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-e721b90027522cb41b285d9c079ae0eb_r.jpg" data-actualsrc="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-e721b90027522cb41b285d9c079ae0eb_b.jpg" data-lazy-status="ok" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-pid="e42JKtmr">数据显示，全球范围内，品牌所收集的数据中，有将近三分之一都是不准确的，同时有7成的企业表示他们很难获取到直接影响营销策略的重要数据——即<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E5%AE%A2%E6%88%B7%E6%95%B0%E6%8D%AE" target="_blank">客户数据</a>。那么，如何获取客户数据？可以获取哪些类型的客户数据？以及如何让客户数据发挥更大的价值？今天我们就来一起探讨下。</p>
<h1 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: 20px; line-height: 30px; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; clear: left; font-synthesis: style; color: #212629; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; font-weight: 600; text-align: center;"><span style="font-synthesis: style;">   01、</span><span style="font-synthesis: style;">客户数据有哪些类型？</span></h1>
<h2 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: 18px; line-height: 30px; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; clear: left; font-synthesis: style; color: #212629; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px; font-weight: 600; text-align: left;"><span style="font-synthesis: style;">客户个人信息数据</span></h2>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-pid="3eGijIQ2"><span style="font-weight: 600; font-synthesis: style;">客户个人信息数据是否完善和正确，决定了客户数据能否成为企业的有效资产。</span>这些数据大多都是通用的标准化字段。诸如客户姓名、手机号码、邮箱、职务等，可用于统计的联系人分类数据等（例如性别和收入）属于基本的客户数据。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-size="normal"><img class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb lazy" style="display: block; max-width: 100%; margin: 0px auto; cursor: zoom-in; background-color: transparent; animation: 0.5s ease-in 0s 1 normal none running fxRichTextFadeIn;" src="https://pic3.zhimg.com/80/v2-41f4613868c1cca2b9683618ee1eb396_720w.jpg" width="1080" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="605" data-original="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-41f4613868c1cca2b9683618ee1eb396_r.jpg" data-actualsrc="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-41f4613868c1cca2b9683618ee1eb396_b.jpg" data-lazy-status="ok" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-pid="XOvNc2sX">这类数据往往是描述客户基本信息的静态数据，获取也相对比较容易，<span style="font-weight: 600; font-synthesis: style;">当品牌对多个客户进行汇总和分析时，基本数据将为细分受众群奠定基础。</span></p>
<h2 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: 18px; line-height: 30px; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; clear: left; font-synthesis: style; color: #212629; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px; font-weight: 600; text-align: left;"><span style="font-synthesis: style;">行为数据</span></h2>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-pid="ySFJhAFL">行为数据的主要获取来源有<span style="font-weight: 600; font-synthesis: style;">线上线下</span>两种，线下主要源于线下实体店，线上渠道则包括微信公众号、H5、小程序、品牌APP等。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-size="normal"><img class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb lazy" style="display: block; max-width: 100%; margin: 0px auto; cursor: zoom-in; background-color: transparent; animation: 0.5s ease-in 0s 1 normal none running fxRichTextFadeIn;" src="https://pic2.zhimg.com/80/v2-26327b36e1daaaee4b6cc3c4911c5eed_720w.jpg" width="1080" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="615" data-original="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-26327b36e1daaaee4b6cc3c4911c5eed_r.jpg" data-actualsrc="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-26327b36e1daaaee4b6cc3c4911c5eed_b.jpg" data-lazy-status="ok" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-pid="g6HJzlMa"><span style="font-weight: 600; font-synthesis: style;">行为数据往往反应了客户的消费选择或是决策过程，可洞悉客户对实际产品、销售流程或服务的体验。</span>市场运营人员可通过<span style="font-weight: 600; font-synthesis: style;"><a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/CDP" target="_blank">CDP</a>等工具记录顾客的行为路径和情况并进行关联对比，全方位了解并追踪用户的行动，从而将数据真正“用起来”</span>。例如对一个在线上线下都会购买的客户，就可以根据数据记录其喜好，在线上进行个性化推荐，帮助用户买到喜欢的产品，减少决策时间，成功进行二次转化，反之亦然。</p>
<h2 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: 18px; line-height: 30px; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; clear: left; font-synthesis: style; color: #212629; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px; font-weight: 600; text-align: left;"><span style="font-synthesis: style;">反馈数据</span></h2>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-pid="dTyinAlG">与其他两种类型的数据不同，<span style="font-weight: 600; font-synthesis: style;">反馈数据是提供客户实际想法的第一手资料，帮助品牌了解客户对产品的看法</span>，一般通过销售、客服或是调查问卷获取。例如品牌发布新产品时往往会通过问卷进行市场调研，获取用户的使用感受。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-size="normal"><img class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb lazy" style="display: block; max-width: 100%; margin: 0px auto; cursor: zoom-in; background-color: transparent; animation: 0.5s ease-in 0s 1 normal none running fxRichTextFadeIn;" src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/80/v2-3105e40955265a66b247d5d68ee080f4_720w.jpg" width="1080" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="606" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-3105e40955265a66b247d5d68ee080f4_r.jpg" data-actualsrc="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-3105e40955265a66b247d5d68ee080f4_b.jpg" data-lazy-status="ok" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-pid="tHFYgAXE">值得一提的是，销售部门由于直接接触客户，因此他们获取到的数据更加生动有效，能够帮助分析客户在购买过程中的心路历程，影响未成交/最终成交的因素，从而促成下单或二次转化。但反馈数据也存在一些弊端，举例来说，来自不满意的人的严厉评论是否真的反映了整个客户群的情感？可能是也可能不是。因此<span style="font-weight: 600; font-synthesis: style;">品牌需要在重要的客户群中连续主动地收集反馈数据，识别出哪些话术对客户下单及服务满意度最有影响，从而进行优化。</span></p>
<h1 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: 20px; line-height: 30px; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; clear: left; font-synthesis: style; color: #212629; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; font-weight: 600; text-align: center;"><span style="font-synthesis: style;">   02、</span><span style="font-synthesis: style;">客户数据可以发挥哪些价值？</span></h1>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-pid="0WRwvmE9">对营销人员来说，学会考虑和改善客户体验无疑是工作中最为重要的一环。无论是内容还是多渠道的运营策略，<span style="font-weight: 600; font-synthesis: style;">客户体验始终是衡量运营活动成功与否的关键因素。创造吸引人的客户体验可以为品牌提供真正的竞争优势</span>。那么对于提升客户体验，客户数据到底可以发挥哪些价值呢？</p>
<h2 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: 18px; line-height: 30px; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; clear: left; font-synthesis: style; color: #212629; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px; font-weight: 600; text-align: left;"><span style="font-synthesis: style;">打造完整的客户画像</span></h2>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-pid="Z0lP-ZIh">营销的终极目标就是能够在合适的时间，通过合适的渠道，针对合适的人群推送或展示合适的内容，从而达到令人满意的营销效果，也就是所谓的精准营销，而精准营销的前提是有大量的数据作为判断依据。品牌在收集了上述数据后，可借助<span style="font-weight: 600; font-synthesis: style;">CDP等工具对客户数据进行归一化处理，形成完整的用户画像</span>，并可按照渠道、动作类型、时间等分类展示客户的完整行为数据，充分挖掘客户生命周期价值。</p>
<h4 style="margin: 25px 0px; color: #818386; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 14px; background-image: none; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px;" data-size="normal"><img class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb lazy" style="display: block; max-width: 100%; margin: 0px auto; cursor: zoom-in; background-color: transparent; animation: 0.5s ease-in 0s 1 normal none running fxRichTextFadeIn;" src="https://pic3.zhimg.com/80/v2-5f26ed840eff362613b78043e60c5996_720w.jpg" width="1080" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="607" data-original="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-5f26ed840eff362613b78043e60c5996_r.jpg" data-actualsrc="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-5f26ed840eff362613b78043e60c5996_b.jpg" data-lazy-status="ok" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">LinkFlow统一客户视图</h4>
<h2 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: 18px; line-height: 30px; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; clear: left; font-synthesis: style; color: #212629; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px; font-weight: 600; text-align: left;"><span style="font-synthesis: style;">精准营销，自动化触达用户</span></h2>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-pid="t_SQ_wSs">随着用户与企业的互动渠道激增，只了解客户的喜好并不足以构建准确的用户画像，还需要通过客户的行为数据加以完善。<span style="font-weight: 600; font-synthesis: style;">品牌可通过消费者行为数据洞察客户意向，基于不同阶段制定相应的运营策略，以便选择最佳的推送渠道、匹配用户需求的内容做触达</span>。此外，品牌也可通过CDP已对接的营销渠道，<span style="font-weight: 600; font-synthesis: style;">根据不同的用户标签组制定不同类型的客户旅程，为客户构建独特的自动化交互体验</span>，并将触达后的客户行为数据实时反馈到CDP中，用以及时调优，不断丰富用户画像。</p>
