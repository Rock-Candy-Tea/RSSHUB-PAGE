<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>7月6日，时隔6年周杰伦再发专辑，《最伟大的作品》引爆热搜。在视频号上，<a data-miniprogram-appid="wx52e2d0716c9ada5e" data-miniprogram-path="others/bloggerDetail/index?bloggerIdKey=01a289&bloggerId=11420" data-miniprogram-nickname="友望数据" data-miniprogram-type="text" data-miniprogram-servicetype href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/undefined">QQ音乐</a>发布的MV，也迅速获得了点赞、转发、收藏均10W+，近7日内，视频仍然霸屏。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/208009891662cfd028641b86.49423571.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span>△ </span><span>图片来源：友望数据-热门视频</span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>“杰作”爆火是意料之中的事，那最近有哪些“意料之外”的爆款视频呢？还有哪些点赞转发双10W+的视频？他们做对了什么？</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/2020343473624151d7a686a7.90569712.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><strong><span>意外爆火，</span></strong><strong><span>它们凭什么能10W+？</span></strong><span></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>相信经常刷短视频的小伙伴们，有时候看到一些点赞10W+的视频时候，难免会内心OS：我%￥&…这也能10W+？可以说是完全琢磨不透。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>我们在开头提到的【热门视频】中，QQ音乐下方，就发现一条<strong>只有7秒钟</strong>的视频，获得了</span><strong><span>点赞10W+、转发1.7W、收藏近7W</span></strong><span>。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><a data-miniprogram-appid="wx52e2d0716c9ada5e" data-miniprogram-path="others/hotContent/index?tab=1" data-miniprogram-nickname="友望数据" data-miniprogram-type="image" data-miniprogram-servicetype href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/undefined"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/44654098662cfd028988120.17051314.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span><span>△ </span><span>图片来源：友望数据-热门视频</span></span><span></span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>点开该条视频，我们发现，<strong>这条视频就一个镜头</strong>：一位穿着卫衣休闲裤运动鞋、左手拿着书、右手拿了个奖杯，看似普普通通的女生，朝着前方走了几步，没有说话，没有看镜头。就封面这几行字，镜头慢动作，背景音乐却很DJ。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/200021008262cfd028d0eaf8.38441195.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span><span>△ </span><span>图片来源：视频号</span></span><span></span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>对着视频，很多创作者可能不太理解这种视频为什么能火，看了评论后发现，大概就是“免费”这两个字眼吸引了用户吧。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>这条10W+的视频在7月7日发布的，紧接着，7月9日作者又发布了一条封面和文案都一模一样的视频，但在这条视频的基础上，后面又加了一段这位老师的介绍，但反响平平。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/194886630262cfd029b14d40.06614490.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span>△ </span><span>图片来源：友望数据-博主动态</span><span></span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>当天，又发布了一条类似的视频，也是这样的开头加上老师的介绍，只是开场音乐换成了稍显悲壮的，文案也所不同。隔天，还有一条类似的视频，数据会好些，但和第一条10W+相差甚远。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/105646633962cfd029de6925.42053051.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span><span>△ </span><span>图片来源：视频号</span></span><span></span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>或许这种视频的爆火只是意外，让人摸不着头脑，作者也想复制爆款，却难再造。<strong>可见，想维持热度，还需靠稳定且优质的内容才行</strong>。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/1059088702624151d9ba1ad0.27662037.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><strong><span>男版李子柒走红，</span></strong><strong><span>三农视频热度不减</span></strong><span></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在近30天的【热门视频】中，我们发现<a data-miniprogram-appid="wx52e2d0716c9ada5e" data-miniprogram-path="others/bloggerDetail/index?bloggerIdKey=9785fa&bloggerId=4662" data-miniprogram-nickname="友望数据" data-miniprogram-type="text" data-miniprogram-servicetype href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/undefined">一条匠心</a>发布的视频《张二冬离开城市，上山，花4000元租下一处院子，租期20年，从此过上每天种菜喂鸡，写书画画的生活。》，获得了</span><span><strong>点赞、转发、收藏均10W+。</strong></span><span></span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/141892048662cfd02a24b5d7.18040753.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span>△ </span><span>图片来源：友望数据-热门视频</span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>这条视频时长超过5分钟</span></strong></span><span>，</span><span>但传播指数很高，排名非常靠前。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>视频以第一人称口吻自述的方式，讲述了主人公二冬在深山隐居7年的生活。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>美术专业毕业三年后，二冬离开城市，上山，花4000元租下了一处院子，租期20年。年仅33岁的他，已经在山上住了7年，<span>每天种菜喂鸡、写书画画。</span></span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/44870843462cfd02a747861.14206146.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span>△ </span><span>图片来源：视频号</span><span></span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>与李子柒唯美的镜头不同，<span><a data-miniprogram-appid="wx52e2d0716c9ada5e" data-miniprogram-path="others/bloggerDetail/index?bloggerIdKey=9785fa&bloggerId=4662" data-miniprogram-nickname="友望数据" data-miniprogram-type="text" data-miniprogram-servicetype href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/undefined">一条匠心</a></span>的视频画面更为写实。虽然处在深山、破院，但经过美术生的改造，一人、一狗、几只鸡鹅，简简单单的生活也有了诗意。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>“之所以现在很多人逃离北上广，是因为当下的城市生活像一个巨大的幻象，被无数的人、事、物裹挟着，那种幻象让人身在其中很混沌，这个时候我们就需要不断去发现自己的真实，从混沌里出来，让自己清澈一会儿。”</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>或许这里也算不上什么世外桃源，但没有城市的喧嚣、没有快节奏生活的焦虑与烦躁、也没有复杂的人际关系，有的是“鸡犬不宁”般的娴静。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>“所谓空谷幽兰就是万籁俱寂里面有一个你，这就是孤独的清澈感。”</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>从李子柒到张同学，无论是仙境般的唯美还是朴实无华的农村生活写照，三农类的短视频依然非常受大众欢迎。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在友望数据此前的文章中</span><strong><span><a href="https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s?__biz=Mzg4MzUzNzM2NA==&mid=2247489030&idx=2&sn=4c3c24c21d94875c0119ca994d01693f&scene=21#wechat_redirect" target="_blank" rel="noopener" data-linktype="2">《频出10万+爆款、带货6位数，为何大家愿意为乡土记忆买单？》</a></span></strong><span>，我们也分析过视频号三农博主爆火的原因以及变现方式。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>无论是乡村美食、乡村生活还是乡村改造，960万平方公里、56个民族，广阔的农村天地、别样多彩的民俗、丰富诱人的美食，三农类的内容在背靠微信的视频号中，依然大有可为。</span><span></span></p>
