<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>七夕是情人之间的互通情谊的浪漫节日，但是在品牌看来，七夕是兵家必争之“节”，是一年营销中的一个重要节点。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>每年这时候，如何推出七夕限定、吸引消费者愿意购买，各大品牌都绞尽脑汁。接下来盘一盘今年的<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E4%B8%83%E5%A4%95%E8%90%A5%E9%94%80" target="_blank">七夕营销</a>，围观品牌是如何出招的。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/381440126233b25ace1df6.64458916.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span><strong><span>花西子用数字艺术，</span></strong></span><span><strong><span>献上七夕告白礼</span></strong></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>七夕是一年中重要的传统节日和送礼场景，而选择送女孩一份彩妆，是一次不会出错的礼物选择。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>中国早在4000年前的夏禹时代就开始使用珍珠了，而中国人对珍珠的喜爱是刻在DNA里的。这次七夕，新锐品牌花西子洞察到珍珠在东方浪漫中的地位，以「</span><span><strong>珍珠</strong></span><span>」为主题，与亚洲十大新锐设计师之一 SUSAN FANG 共同打造属于东方浪漫的「</span><span><strong>“珍”</strong><strong>心告白</strong></span><span>」礼。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>这款告白礼是以玻璃珠搭配柔光贝珠作“告白珠链”，串联定制成一份同心锁，寓意恋人双方同心同德、恋情圆满。除上线今年七夕限定之外，花西子还延续过去五年“为卿画眉”的七夕活动，将东方含蓄与浪漫传达出来。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>除此之外，花西子还邀请摄影师尹超、数字艺术工作室 Roubit ，用超现实虚拟场景，加上寓意美好的珍珠元素，虚构一个东方浪漫世界。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>此次，花西子采用数字科技的表达方式，成功将“z世代”的圈层爱好融入<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E5%93%81%E7%89%8C%E8%90%A5%E9%94%80" target="_blank">品牌营销</a>活动中。</span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/9225241376233b25c6ec6a4.50995726.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<section>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 18px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;"><span>巴黎世家以机器人，</span><span>演绎科幻七夕</span></h2>
</section>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>巴黎世家又一次在七夕营销上剑走偏锋。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>停止走乡村非主流的土味路线，巴黎世家这次选择了科技与时尚的交锋，以两位机器人为模特，进行新品穿戴。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>事实证明，七夕爱情不是人类独有，机器人也不能少。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>巴黎世家通过镜头语言，向受众呈现出一个荒诞却又现实的AI之间的爱情，同时，视频配上普罗大众所知晓的《甜蜜蜜》的旋律，呈现出爱情与科技时尚的碰撞之美。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/16900100706233b25e365e39.91683873.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span><strong><span>阿里妈妈与玩偶，</span></strong></span><span><strong><span>教蔬菜版情话</span></strong></span><span></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>蔬菜是我们生活必不可少的存在，近期，菜市场文学、书菜计划等品牌营销活动层出不穷。</span><span><strong><span>借助平台优势，阿里妈妈以蔬菜为媒介，线上开启一家特别的超市——七夕情蔬超市。</span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>阿里妈妈联合七大品牌共同打造七款不同的「</span><strong><span>情蔬</span></strong><span>」玩偶和一款隐藏款，以数字藏品的形式。结合品牌新品卖点，每款「</span><strong><span>情蔬</span></strong><span>」玩偶在设计上风格独特，有幽默风趣的「鬼脸豌豆」、专一忠诚的「鲜 love 椒」等等，并为其拍摄一支动画短片，在生活琐碎中传递出「写n行情书，不如送颗“情蔬”」的七夕主题。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>以刚需品「情蔬」代替奢侈品为七夕礼物，阿里妈妈带我们感受实用主义的中国式浪漫。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/144874037462390611b76b79.09484487.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span><strong>Burberry 召唤「八芒星」，</strong></span><span><strong>解读爱的寓意</strong></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>今年Burberry邀请「W 野狗艺术舱 · 坨比」，以品牌经典格纹设计为灵感，创作了《跨越爱的八芒星》童真动画，从天真可爱的儿童视角生动有趣地讲述古老爱情神话，诠释「爱之八芒星」的寓意。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><strong><span><strong><span><strong><span><strong><strong><span><strong><strong><span><strong><span><strong><span><strong><strong><span>爱是什么？短片告诉我们爱是要打破格纹边界，勇敢追求幸福，共同探寻爱的真谛。当人们召唤八芒星时，爱具有</span></strong><span>勇气、浪漫、理解、愉悦、赤诚、探索、珍惜、信任八个角组成的美好品质。</span></strong></span></strong></span></strong></span></strong></strong></span></strong></strong></span></strong></span></strong></span></strong></strong></span><span><strong><strong><span>故事结尾在揭示「爱的尽头是自由」的真谛的同时，以牛郎织女的爱情延续“中国风”路线。</span></strong></strong></span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>同时，Burberry邀请「W 野狗潮玩舱 · 艺术家 · 桃了桃」设计八芒星创意卡牌，延伸「爱之八芒星」的八个爱情品质。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/81402493962390612078889.98046962.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><strong><span>完美日记携手中国邮政，</span></strong><strong><span>以信「织爱」</span></strong></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>中国七夕是以牛郎织女鹊桥相会为传说的节日，完美日记延续「信封礼盒」的设计，回归中国传统七夕故事，从中提取出「织爱」的主题，推出全新的七夕限量礼盒。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>礼盒在设计上运用传统编织元素，打造中国式<span>浪漫</span>彩妆系列。礼盒内含立体爱心贺卡设计，语音留言功能还可录入专属告白情话。</span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>完美日记还携手中国邮政跨界联名，邀请知名艺术插画师 Jude Chan ，定制推出邮品插画的限定周边，传递出「</span><span><strong>七夕织爱 献给挚爱</strong></span><span>」的情谊。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/996765320623a1f7bd3a909.46127690.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span><strong><span>天猫写下99封情书，</span></strong></span><span><strong><span>用书信留下浪漫</span></strong></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><strong><span><strong><span><strong><span><strong><strong><span><strong><strong><span><strong><span><strong><span><strong><strong><span>天猫携手各大美妆个护品牌，在上海、北京、武汉、广州、深圳等六大城市的车窗上写下了 99 封情书，表达中国爱情的细腻而含蓄。</span></strong></strong></span></strong></span></strong></span></strong></strong></span></strong></strong></span></strong></span></strong></span></strong></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><strong><span>这次的99次告白没有直白的“我爱你”，而是见证藏在城市中的双向奔赴，是中国式的委婉表达。</span></strong></strong></span><span><span></span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>天猫以各大城市的地标建筑为见证者，洞察到每位再次奋斗过的年轻人的爱情感悟，写下对应的情书。</span></strong></span><span><span></span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><strong><span>不仅如此，在城市车窗情书线下活动的同时，天猫在线上制造话题，掀起满满的粉红浪漫氛围，为各大品牌七夕营销创造声量。</span></strong></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/1132444289623a1f7daa8cb2.63243718.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
