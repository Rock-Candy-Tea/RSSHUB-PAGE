<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; font-variant-ligatures: normal; orphans: 2; widows: 2; text-decoration-thickness: initial; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.9px; caret-color: #000000; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">这是过去6年我经历和观察到的现象。</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #000000; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.9px; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">如今，这种现象并没有随着私域的普及而减少，依然普遍存在。</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.9px; caret-color: #000000; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;"> </span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; font-variant-ligatures: normal; orphans: 2; widows: 2; text-decoration-thickness: initial; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #000000; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: 0.8999999761581421px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">这里，我要先声明的是，私域并非不能创造销售。相反，我有不少学员在上完私域课后，公司都获得了更持久且稳定的销售增长。</span></strong></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; font-variant-ligatures: normal; orphans: 2; widows: 2; text-decoration-thickness: initial; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.9px; caret-color: #000000; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">那出现这种现象，到底是什么原因呢？</span></strong></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #000000; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.8999999761581421px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">关于这个问题，我仔细分析过，原因在于：<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">那些越在乎销售结果的老板，他认知和擅长的是“直线式销售”，而对像私域这种“<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E6%9B%B2%E7%BA%BF%E5%BC%8F%E9%94%80%E5%94%AE" target="_blank">曲线式销售</a>”根本不了解，甚至不试图去理解。他们身陷思维惯性之中，却不自知。</strong></span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #000000; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.8999999761581421px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">这两种销售模式，虽然结果都是把产品卖掉，但销售逻辑却大不相同。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #000000; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.8999999761581421px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">这些老板因为不理解，看不懂，往往就容易主观臆断，不敢投入，也没有耐心投入。</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.9px; caret-color: #000000; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">这样的公司，私域自然就做不起来。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #000000; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.8999999761581421px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">要改变老板，说服他支持和投入，<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">那就</strong><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">要让他清楚，直线式销售与曲线式销售的区别，以及不同的价值。</strong></span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #000000; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.8999999761581421px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">这样企业的私域才能获得资源，做出成绩。</span></p>
<h1 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #222222; color: #212629; font-size: 20px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: center; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: 30px; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); background-size: 16px 20px; font-weight: 600; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><strong style="font-size: 16px; caret-color: #333333; orphans: 2; color: #000000; letter-spacing: 0.544px; widows: 2; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Apple Color Emoji', 'Emoji Symbols Font', 'Segoe UI Symbol', Arial, sans-serif; letter-spacing: 1px; caret-color: #ff0000; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">01、直线式销售VS曲线式销售</span></strong></h1>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #000000; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.8999999761581421px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">什么是直线式销售和曲线式销售？</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #000000; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.8999999761581421px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">你可能是头一次听，这很正常，因为这两个词是我自创的。相信曲线救国大家都知道，其实曲线式销售也是这么来的。</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.9px; caret-color: #000000; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">最终目的都是销售，只是采取的路径和形式不同。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #000000; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.8999999761581421px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">所谓直线式销售，顾名思义，就是见到客户，直接卖货，总是用最短路径，最直接的方式，最快把产品卖出去。没有铺垫，没有烘托，就是买卖。</strong></span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #000000; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.8999999761581421px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">过去很常见，像店里的导购，上门的推销员大多是这样。好一点，上来还先套个近乎，有些连近乎都不套，直奔主题，上来就一顿介绍产品，卖点，价格和优惠。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #000000; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.8999999761581421px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">你能很强烈感受到推销的火药，能成就成，不成拉倒，他们也不会在你身上浪费时间。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #000000; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.8999999761581421px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">直线式销售的收入公式，就是<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">「客户数量x客单价」</strong>，所以拼命缩短销售时间，争取销售给更多人。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #000000; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.8999999761581421px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">像过去，电话推销、陌拜销售、活动促销、互联网投广告买流量，基本都属于这种直接式销售逻辑。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #000000; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.8999999761581421px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">路径短，销售快，操作简单，容易复制规模化，整个销售环节就是3点一线，跟客户没什么多余的信任。</strong></span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0.5em; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><img src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/4d42f4c2cdd540f7a7240472f240a32a.png" alt width="567" height="167" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="margin: 25px 0px; padding: 0px 0.5em; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; caret-color: #222222; color: #818386; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: center; background-image: none; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-size: 12px; color: #888888; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: Arial, sans-serif; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-align: start; widows: 2; background-color: #ffffff; text-decoration-thickness: initial; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">△</span>直线式销售</span></strong></h4>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #000000; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.8999999761581421px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">而曲线式销售不一样，虽然最终目的也是成交卖货。但是它的出发点稍长远些，既关注眼前的销售，也重视离店后续的销售，换句话说，想销售更长久些，有二次，三次。</strong></span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #000000; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.8999999761581421px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">这样不仅考虑要卖产品，还要能跟客户建立信任关系，甚至哪怕这次客户不买，关系在，后面也有机会成交。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #000000; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.8999999761581421px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">基于这个思想，它销售路径就不能是<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">「客户——产品——销售」</strong>了。它的路径变长了，环节复杂了，花的时间精力多了，成交的时间周期也长了一些。用一张图来表示，</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.9px; caret-color: #000000; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">就是像下面这样。</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #000000; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.8999999761581421px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;"> </span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0.5em; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><img src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/76b905575c483ccadce486bb0c288e48.png" alt width="521" height="374" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="margin: 25px 0px; padding: 0px 0.5em; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; caret-color: #222222; color: #818386; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: center; background-image: none; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #333333; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, system-ui, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-variant-ligatures: normal; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; widows: 2; text-decoration-thickness: initial; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-size: 12px; color: #888888; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">△曲线式销售</span></strong></h4>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #000000; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.8999999761581421px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">所以很明显，相比直线式销售，<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">私域多了“养”的环节，这是一个建立，加强，修复信任的环节，信任越强，未来的成交就越多。</strong></span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #000000; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.8999999761581421px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">养这个环节，就是私域运营的工作，它涉及到内容，活动，互动，游戏，服务等运营动作。</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #000000; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.8999999761581421px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;"> </span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #000000; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.8999999761581421px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">这些动作虽不是直接推销，但它能加强客户关系，增加了信任分。</span><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.9px; caret-color: #000000; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">这个信任分，就好比是客户用来成交的货币。</span></strong></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #000000; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.8999999761581421px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">举个例子，每成交1次，需要2个信任分。企业本身的产品，品牌，门店各自算1个信任分，那就是每个新客有3分基础分。</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #000000; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.8999999761581421px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;"> </span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #000000; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.8999999761581421px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">那按照<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">直线式销售</strong>，每个新客户成交1次后，仅剩1分了，没法2次成交，除非再通过提升<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">产品，品牌</strong>来加信任分。这就需要大量投入研发，广告宣传费用。</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #000000; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.8999999761581421px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;"> </span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; background-color: #ffffff; caret-color: #000000; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.9px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">而同样情况下，按照<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">“曲线式销售”</strong>，每个新客户都有3分，当他们每</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #000000; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.9px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">收<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">到一次内容，活动，互动，游戏这些私域运营动作，都会增加1分好感，也就是1个信任分，这样每个新客户就积攒了7分，理论上他能重复买3.5次，相当于企业可以赚3次钱。</span></span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #000000; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.8999999761581421px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">这个简单的模型，很容易说明直线式销售和曲线式销售的区别。很明显，销售路径不同，采取的策略，需要时间，以及后续销售汇报也就都不相同了。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #000000; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.8999999761581421px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">搞清楚了2种销售思维的区别，可能有人问，<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">哪种模式更好，现在适合哪种呢？</strong>这就是下面的的话题了。</span></p>

<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #000000; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.8999999761581421px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">直线式销售，因为直接，所以<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">成交速度快，变现快，路径短，操作简单，容易规模化复制</strong>（增加销售员即可）。</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #000000; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.8999999761581421px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;"> </span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #000000; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.8999999761581421px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">缺点是跟客户只是交易关系，缺乏深层的信任关系和情感连接。</strong>要成交，就只能是更有竞争力的价格，更强势的品牌广告，这两种都是高投入。</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #000000; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.8999999761581421px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;"> </span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #000000; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.8999999761581421px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">随着竞争越来越激烈，高昂的营销费用，不仅降低企业利润，甚至带来亏损，影响企业健康发展。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #000000; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.8999999761581421px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">而曲线式销售，因为一开始就抱有长期主义价值，投入时间，精力跟客户建立信任关系。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #000000; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.8999999761581421px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">虽然早</strong><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">期成交速度不快，变现没那么及时，运营环节复杂，不容易复制。</strong></span><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.9px; caret-color: #000000; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">但是它跟客户建立了信任和情感连接，客户更相信企业，更愿意买我们的产品，企业从单个客户身上能获得更丰厚的利润。</span></strong></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #222222; color: #000000; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: inherit; font-family: -apple-system, system-ui, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; caret-color: #000000; color: #000000; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei UI', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.8999999761581421px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">因为运营复杂，一旦企业建立起来，壁垒就会很高，其它企业难以短期模仿复制。这就是曲线式销售的优点。</span></p>
