<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-mpa-powered-by="yiban.io"><span>预摘牌，<span lang="EN-US">+4</span>。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>时隔两个月，<span lang="EN-US">SEC</span>（美国证券交易委员会）再次大笔一挥，点名四家中概股企业进入“预摘牌名单”。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>继京东、拼多多、滴滴等之后，</span><span><strong>中国互联网双寡头之一的阿里，终于被盯上了。</strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/99323113621d859ad90f46.00495923.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span></span></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span><strong><span>阿里列入预摘牌，</span></strong></span><span><strong><span><span>市值闪崩</span><span lang="EN-US">2000</span><span>亿</span></span></strong></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，已经两个月没有动静的<span lang="EN-US">SEC</span>再度扩容了其预摘牌名单，这一批次中，共有<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E9%98%BF%E9%87%8C%E5%B7%B4%E5%B7%B4" target="_blank">阿里巴巴</a>、蘑菇街等<span lang="EN-US">4</span>家中概股企业上榜。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>作为我国<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E7%94%B5%E5%95%86" target="_blank">电商</a>龙头，阿里的上榜无疑备受关注。</span><span><strong>因为这意味着，阿里巴巴朝着被美国证券交易所强制退市的方向又近了一步。</strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在所有被列入预摘牌名单的中概股公司中，阿里也是规模最大的一家。受其自身知名度以及“预摘牌名单”的特殊性影响，这一事件迅速成为网络热点。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>与此同时，在此消息的利空影响下，阿里股价再度迎来大跌。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/99045095662e993b218b321.48664914.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span><span>●</span> 来源：雪球</span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>截止<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日美股收盘，阿里跌超<span lang="EN-US">11%</span>，报收<span lang="EN-US">89.37</span>美元<span lang="EN-US">/</span>股。</span><strong><span>短<strong>短一天时间内，阿里市值便蒸发了约<span lang="EN-US">300</span>亿美元，折合人民币约<span lang="EN-US">2000</span>亿元。</strong></span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>事实上，这已经不是<span lang="EN-US">SEC</span>对中概股的首次“制裁”了。自今年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月起至今，已有超<span lang="EN-US">150</span>家中概股企业被列入“预摘牌名单”，这一数字占据了所有在美上市中概股企业总量的一半。并且，由于无法在限期内证明不具备摘牌条件，已有<span lang="EN-US">153</span>家企业从“”预摘牌名单”被转入了“确定摘牌名单”。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>依据美方《外国公司问责法》，被列入预摘牌名单的上市公司需要在<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天内证明自己不具备被摘牌的条件，否则将会被转入“确定摘牌名单”。进入确定名单的公司若在<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年内无法满足检查要求，则会被强制退市。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>这其中的核心，则在于企业的“审计底稿”。受我国相关法律约束，作为呈现真实财务数据最重要文件之一的审计底稿，企业不得随意对外展示。</span><span><strong>而按照<span lang="EN-US">SEC</span>要求，这一数据则必须向其提交，否则将面临退市风险。</strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/200020560262e993b27eb9f7.99231178.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span><span>●</span> 来源：老板联播官方微博</span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>今天上午，阿里也对被列入预摘牌名单一事做出了回应，称“将继续留意市场动态，遵守适用的法律法规，并努力保持同时在纽交所和香港联交所的两地上市地位。”</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>然而关于这其中的博弈，并非几家公司可以扭转。对于列入“确定摘牌名单”的中概股公司未来是否会被强制退市，还需要取决于中美双方审计监管合作的结果。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>尽管目前而言，被列入预摘牌名单还不代表一定会被摘牌退市，但在如今的中美大环境下，风险不可不防。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong>换句话说，阿里必须做好未来从美股摘牌的准备。</strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/40300182621d859c4744b0.36029202.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span><strong><span>两手准备，</span></strong></span><span><strong><span>阿里申请双重主要上市</span></strong></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>毫无疑问，对于阿里，乃至整个中概股而言，在美退市都将是一项巨大的打击。一旦失去美股市场，阿里的庞大体量也将难以容身。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>并且，目前阿里在港股市场还处于二次上市地位，</span><span><strong>一旦其在主要上市地（纽约）被强制退市，那么在香港的上市地位也将受到影响。</strong></span><span>这柄达摩克利斯之剑，高悬于每个中概股公司头顶。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>为尽可能减少风险，应对单一市场的不确定性，回港上市已经成为中概股的新潮流。知乎、贝壳、小鹏等公司均是如此，阿里也作出了同样的选择，以对冲风险。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/207448693562e993b3057275.37880478.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/202958780662e993b3680c24.51054426.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span><span>●</span> 图源：阿里巴巴集团公告</span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，阿里巴巴发布公告称“将会按照香港上市规则申请香港为主要上市地，预期将于<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年年底前生效。”</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong>当其在港交所主要上市后，阿里将在港股和美股拥有同等的上市地位。</strong></span><span>这意味着，届时即便阿里在美股退市，也不会影响其在港股的上市地位。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>做足了两手准备，阿里才有继续前行的勇气。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>阿里公告表示，当相关流程完成后，阿里将成为在美国纽约和中国香港双重主要上市的公司。其于美国挂牌的存托股和在香港上市的普通股可以互相转换，投资者可自由选择一种形式继续持有阿里股票。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>对于阿里这一流通市值超<span lang="EN-US">2</span>万亿港元的优质龙头企业，此次回港主要上市也具有着标志性的意义，中概股的发展将走向一个新的篇章。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>然而即便找好了退路，相比于目前的美股主要上市形势而言，依旧属于“下策”。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/23241282862e993b3ecb3c3.35917135.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span><span>●</span> 图源：阿里巴巴集团官网</span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>相较于美股，香港市场对国际资金的吸纳度要明显不如。今年上半年，阿里在美国市场的日均交易量约<span lang="EN-US">32</span>亿美元，而在香港市场，则仅有<span lang="EN-US">7</span>亿美元左右。这意味着，</span><span><strong>一旦阿里美股退市，回归港股，势必将面临着流动性短期大幅萎缩、股价下跌等情况。</strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>对于内忧外患下的阿里而言，这绝不是个好消息。但在这一特殊时刻下，港股主要上市终归是种紧急避险的最好选择。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/2028633948621d859f7b3c30.12849065.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span></span></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span><strong><span>内忧外患，阿里黑暗时刻</span></strong></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>如今的阿里，确实处在一个较为黑暗的时刻，不复当年辉煌。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年便赴美上市的阿里，历经<span lang="EN-US">6</span>年迭荡，曾在<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年触及股价历史最高点，达<span lang="EN-US">319.32</span>美元<span lang="EN-US">/</span>股，市值高峰<span lang="EN-US">8600</span>亿美元，成为仅次于苹果、微软、谷歌、亚马逊和阿美的世界第六大巨头。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>然而在此之后，</span><span><strong>阿里一路狂跌，如今其市值仅剩不到<span lang="EN-US">2400</span>亿美元，跌去了整整<span lang="EN-US">7</span>成，几乎相当于两个“<span lang="EN-US">A</span>股股王”贵州茅台的体量。</strong></span><span>仅在<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月份，阿里股价累计跌幅便超过了<span lang="EN-US">20%</span>。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>除了在股市的持续遇冷，面临退市风险外，阿里自身业务发展也遭遇了瓶颈。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/4986467262e993b5086660.96106137.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span><span>●</span> 图源：雪球</span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>据其最新发布的<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>财年年报显示，尽管财年内阿里营收再度增长，达到了<span lang="EN-US">8530.62</span>亿元的新纪录，</span><span><strong>但净利润却大幅收缩，同比减少<span lang="EN-US">67%</span>达<span lang="EN-US">470.79</span>亿元；归属于普通股股东的净利润也同比下降了<span lang="EN-US">59%</span>。</strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>完全被腰斩的净利润，意味着阿里所遭受的竞争压力正越来越大。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在国内电商基本盘上，阿里正面临着京东、拼多多、抖音、快手等对手的持续施压。随着短视频、直播带货的兴起，阿里传统的“人找产品”平台模式不再成为唯一的电商选择，新玩家们正在不断蚕食老牌巨头的领地，许多消费者在休闲娱乐的同时，便完成了一整个电商购物流程。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>当这些映射到财报上，便出现了阿里核心电商业务的萎靡。</span><span><strong><span lang="EN-US">2022</span>财年，阿里核心电商业务<span lang="EN-US">GMV</span>为<span lang="EN-US">79760</span>亿，剔除其中的高鑫零售并表、批发业务影响后，相较于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>财年的<span lang="EN-US">78170</span>亿，首次出现了负增长现象。</strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/189093371062e993b56d5979.19643678.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span><span>●</span> 图源：阿里巴巴集团官网</span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>除电商业务外，被寄予厚望的云计算才刚刚实现全年的微弱盈利，距离扛起阿里的大旗还需要不短的时间；其他的本地生活、菜鸟等业务，全都处于亏损状态中。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>截止目前，核心电商业务依旧是阿里的营收支柱，然而随着其<span lang="EN-US">GMV</span>负增长、净利润贡献大幅下降等情况出现，可以说，阿里遇到了大难题。</span></p>
