<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span></span><span>不能忽视细节，很多情况下一些小的细节能决定使用体验的良好与否。不仅仅为了设计而设计，而是思考后设计，经过我们的思考将设计完整呈现。</span><span></span></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span><span><span><span> </span></span><span><span> </span></span></span></span><span><strong><span>使用容易理解的文案</span></strong></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>使用易理解、具有代入感的文案提示，能够让用户快速地明白文案的含义，提供良好的用户体验。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/1177264562d74816a756f4.89414088.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span><span><span><span><span> </span></span><span><span> </span></span></span></span></span><span><strong><span>水平对齐</span></strong></span><span><span><span></span></span></span><span><span><span></span></span></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>页面信息的有序排列能让我们看起来更轻松，增强内容的可读性。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>比如表单设计中，尤其每个选项的字数有多有少，左侧采用标题和输入框并列对齐的方式，页面看起来并不是很统一。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>如果想让页面更整齐，可以采用类似</span><span>右</span><span>侧水平对齐的排版方式，让表单看起来更统一</span><span>。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/43782054962d74816dd0562.72023946.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span><span><span><span><span><span> </span></span><span><span> </span></span></span></span></span></span><span><strong><span>使用图标/标签</span></strong></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在页面设计中，图标虽然尺寸很小但却有着不可替代的作用。如果页面中只有文字，没有图形化的元素，整个页面会显得很单调，很难吸引用户的注意力。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在文字的基础上，</span><span>增加图标、标签或者动效，能让页面内容看起来丰富、更精致</span><span>，能在第一时间达到吸引用户的目的。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/25092541562d7481719b623.89110821.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span><span><span><span><span> </span></span><span><span> </span></span></span></span><span><strong><span>合理拆分内容</span></strong></span></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在信息碎片化以及短视频流行的背景下，用户往往没有耐心去阅读页面中大段的文字。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>为了内容的展示和用户的使用体验，可以考虑将大段的内容拆分成几个小段内容，突出关键点，这样内容展示用更清晰，也方便用户一眼看到重点内容。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/25074636962d74817452375.60223715.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span><span><strong><span><span><span> </span></span><span><strong><span> 展示任务进程</span></strong></span></span></strong></span></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>展示任务进程的设计形式通常用在任务或者操作相对复杂的场景中。例如在用户需要填写长表单的场景中，展示任务进程就变得很有必要，能够告知用户当前已经填写了哪些内容，还差哪些内容需要填写。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>又或者在购物场景中，从商品加入购物车、填写收货人地址到确认支付，简化购物的流程，让用户更好理解。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/15406175462d74817844c71.12917542.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span><span><span><span><span> </span></span><span><span> </span></span></span></span><span><strong><span>精确的提示引导</span></strong></span></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>错误提示这点在用户日常的体验中常常遇到，设计师要格外重视。当用户的操作发生错误时，要明确告知用户错误的原因，并引导用户进行正确的操作。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>如果不了解页面中出现了什么问题，只是简单的报错，用户会对当前的操作感到困惑甚至放弃使用。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>因此在页面设计中，</span><span>在正确的位置提示正确的内容非常重要</span><span>。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/25384011762d74817b99e53.39349116.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span><span><span><span><span> </span></span><span><span> </span></span></span></span><span><strong><span>提升CTA转化率</span></strong></span></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>页面中CTA按钮的转化率和产品的商业价值息息相关。</span><span>在设计中，我们需要将用户的注意力集中到按钮的点击和转化中，因此CTA按钮的尺寸要易于点击，并放在用户最容易于注意到的位置。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span></span><span>对于CTA按钮的设计分析，可以快速回看这篇文章??<strong>如何设计出高转化率的CTA按钮？先学会这8个方法</strong></span></p>

<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span><strong><span>最后</span></strong></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>通过这些实用性的设计细节，希望能对你的工作带来切实的帮助。</span><span>在设计中想的更多、更全面一点，不仅能提升自己<strong>思考的维度和深度</strong>，还可能让产品和体验上升层次。</span></p>
