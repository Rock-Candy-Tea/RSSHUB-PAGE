<section><span>最近各大高校的<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E5%BD%95%E5%8F%96%E9%80%9A%E7%9F%A5%E4%B9%A6" target="_blank">录取通知书</a>陆续发出，迎来一年一度花式battle的时刻。准新生们纷纷晒照，表面上看似风平浪静，实际上也在暗自较劲。</span></section>
<section><span></span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span>众所周知，学生们的分享欲与创意程度成正比。有人收获羡慕目光，有人酸成柠檬精。</span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span>一起来看看这届高校录取通知书设计，又“卷”出了什么新高度？</span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 18px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;">01</h2>
<section><span></span></section>
<section><strong><span>最赛博朋克</span></strong></section>
<section><strong><span>哈尔滨工业大学</span></strong></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span>去年哈工大采用镭射材质打造的发光通知书，给人留下深刻的印象。今年，哈工大依然没让人失望，又带来了新意。</span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span>录取通知书的配色来自今年的流行色「克莱因蓝」，色彩饱和度高，契合理工学校的专业气息。</span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<p><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/195370253062e2fb66c89c95.22087340.png" width="455" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<section><span></span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span>通知书的主题为“耀·科技”，外轮廓的设计灵感来自于微电子芯片，封面雕印学校校徽，散发着科技感、现代感的气息。</span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<p><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/102405109562e2fb685beb50.81793096.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<section><span></span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span>最硬核的是，哈工大的每一份录取通知书中镶嵌着学校自主研制的金刚石和蓝宝石，封固于录取通知书的左上侧，对应着哈工大的校训“规格严格，功夫到家”。</span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<p><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/62267311662e2fb6969fbc6.61233587.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/9527277962e2fb6aef5723.56603613.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在高温高亮的照射环境下，录取通知书还会发出荧光，视觉效果相当酷炫。</span><span></span></p>
<p><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/209488601562e2fb6cc363c9.77502749.jpeg" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p> </p>
<section><span></span></section>
<section><span>哈工大还提取主题“耀·科技”的谐音词，向考生父母赠送“耀识”链 ，以此为连接彼此的纽带，处处体现着理工生的硬核浪漫，让细节控都爱了。</span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<p><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/93223311362e2fb6d55b569.44129326.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<section><span></span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 18px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;">02</h2>
<section><span></span></section>
<section><span><strong><span>最重量级</span></strong></span></section>
<section><span><strong><span>南方科技大学</span></strong></span></section>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>今年最“重量级”的录取通知书，当属南方科技大学。重达2.7公斤，让新生们感受到沉甸甸的知识力量，没点臂力还真扛不动。</span><span> </span></p>
<section><span>录取通知书以“南科大橙”为主色调，象征着“对话、体验、探索”，祝贺新生在南科大开启人生的新征程。</span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<p><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/134875516662e2fb6e571a41.65318083.jpeg" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<section><span></span></section>
<section></section>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>礼盒内还附有南科大校徽、新生入学指南、校园地图、定制防护口罩和水杯，实用性十足。</span><span> </span></p>
<section><span><span>通知书一式两份，一份用于新生报到，一份给新生放在家中纪念，可以说非常贴心了。</span></span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<p><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/51549351762e2fb6eba3b44.76382927.jpeg" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/10273535362e2fb6f2dbc05.96947079.jpeg" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/50572635262e2fb6f9e3258.60355266.jpeg" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>

<section></section>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 18px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;">03</h2>
<section><span></span></section>
<section><span><strong><span>最想“上天”</span></strong></span></section>
<section><span><strong><span>北京航空航天大学</span></strong></span></section>
<section></section>
<section><span>正值70周年校庆，北京航空航天大学今年的录取通知书设计自然融入了周年纪念元素。通知书以“向北航行”为主题，承载着少年们的飞天梦想。</span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<p><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/160538707262e2fb6fe16dc6.24757614.jpeg" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p> </p>
<section><span>通知书礼盒内放置着设计精美的亚克力板，将它们叠放拼接在一起，正好构成一幅飞向蓝天宇宙的图案，见证着北航人追逐飞天梦的成长历程。</span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/37163446262e2fb702ee896.25862005.jpeg" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></section>
<p><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/155033577062e2fb70a37652.37819124.jpeg" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<section><span></span></section>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>礼盒底部的“北航 70 载”徽章，以北斗七星的形状依序排列，和新生一同分享建校70周年的硕果和喜悦，充满纪念意义。</span><span></span></p>
<p><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/210716896262e2fb712ce337.93788622.jpeg" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p> </p>
<section><span></span></section>
<section></section>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 18px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;">04</h2>
<section><span></span></section>
<section><span><strong><span>最红最专</span></strong></span></section>
<section><span><strong><span>中国人民大学</span></strong></span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span>中国人民大学录取通知书采用函匣立体式设计，以“人大红”为主色调，突显神圣庄严感。</span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<p><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/195246281862e2fb71839600.16051934.jpeg" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/5451498362e2fb71e8bc80.42682415.png" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>上层主体为校史罗盘，随着罗盘转动，人大 1937 到 2022 的校史手绘画映入眼帘。</span><span></span></p>
<section><span><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/168261770262e2fb733cd2f9.14990150.gif" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></section>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>下层放置着通知书，各学院院徽的“一院一帜”定制书签，以及一封致新生的竖版信件。小小信纸书写满满情怀，细节还原特殊年代，让人秒回那段峥嵘岁月。</span><span></span></p>
<p><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/42021648362e2fb8063b5e0.09168288.gif" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<section><span></span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 18px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;">05</h2>
<section><span></span></section>
<section><strong><span>最简约</span></strong></section>
<section><strong><span>苏州大学</span></strong></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span>为了响应国家的简约号召，苏州大学今年的录取通知书放弃了奢华路线，直接采用标准版本。</span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span>通知书设计化繁为简，封面图案呈现金银两簇丝带交织，内部则是一幅 3D 立体浮雕图景，简约中又不失精致感。</span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<p><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/142620768962e2fb8f15bf09.32552422.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/204225675462e2fb8fa84d18.42638289.jpeg" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<section></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 18px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;">06</h2>
<section><span></span></section>
<section><span><strong><span>最喜气洋洋</span></strong></span></section>
<section><span><strong><span>复旦大学</span></strong></span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span>复旦大学通知书以古代捷报报喜的方法为设计灵感，采用红黄配色，封面图案由书法艺术体“捷”字以及校园的标志性建筑、校徽组成。</span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span>与之配套送达的，还有五大书院院长为新生特别赠予“来自复旦的第一本荐书”的合辑卡片，带领新生徜徉知识海洋。</span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/18137491062e2fb90063824.44153114.jpeg" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></section>
<p><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/171456893162e2fb9052c960.97858540.jpeg" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p> </p>
<section></section>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 18px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;">07</h2>
<section><span></span></section>
<section><span><strong><span>最原生态</span></strong></span></section>
<section><span><strong><span>浙江农林大学</span></strong></span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span>今年，浙江农林大学使出当家绝活，运用学校自主研发的刨切微薄竹技术制作印刷，打造了一封“竹子”通知书，蕴含着八项国家专利。</span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<p><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/168722139062e2fb90a93328.77204814.jpeg" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<section><span></span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span>以刨切微薄竹为原料制作，厚度仅有 0.28-0.3 毫米，可任意对折、侧压、卷曲，可像纸张一样卷曲成轴。</span><span></span></section>
<section><span></span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span>展开后，录取通知书上林立着一片金灿灿的银杏树林，搭配手绘的校园地图观看，增添了几分生动美感。</span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<p><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/57238289462e2fb914267e8.71032550.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/149176612462e2fb91efa9c5.67513643.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<section><span></span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span>即便只有一张纸，浙江农林大学的通知书也能够惊艳四方，兼具了科技发展和生态环保的意识，体现着学校在专业领域的雄厚实力。</span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 18px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;">08</h2>
<section><span></span></section>
<section><span><strong><span>最古典</span></strong></span></section>
<section><span><strong><span>四川大学</span></strong></span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span>四川大学的录取通知书以“锦凤传书”为主题，设计理念更为古雅，烘托出中式韵味。</span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span>采用卷轴的形式呈现，卷首印着四川大学标志性的“凤鸟”符号，卷尾由翻腾的海浪图案映衬。</span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<p><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/203147679862e2fb9325b5b3.83554321.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>

<section></section>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>新生打开卷轴，犹如古代才子接过金榜题名的“圣旨”，充满了仪式感。</span></p>
<p><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/48591995962e2fb94b0dda0.78214153.jpeg" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/78032820662e2fb952d96b4.59751632.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>

<section><span></span></section>
<section><span><strong><span>最公益</span></strong></span></section>
<section><span><strong><span>中山大学</span></strong></span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span>一向低调的中山大学，今年意外地加入了“内卷”队伍，在录取通知书的设计上下足了功夫。</span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span>首次采用礼盒套装样式，烫金工艺勾勒象征校园地标建筑怀士堂的轮廓，尽显中大历史底蕴。</span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<p><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/136381031962e2fb95a45227.43089907.jpeg" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
