<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-mpa-powered-by="yiban.io"><span>XX刺客这个梗最近很流行。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>最初流出来是雪糕冰柜里，有这样一个段子：不认识的雪糕不要拿，不然结账的时候，它就会跳出来给你的钱包一刀。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>很形象。我第一次听到的时候，想笑的点在于，各行皆刺客。<br></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>若干年前，我拿起3元一瓶的百岁山的时候，心想：天呐，啥水啊跟可乐一个价。<br></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/199482817762ced2f61dffb8.21850820.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>再后来在剧本杀店里拿到12一瓶的矿泉水也能觉得“正常”，让一个金牛座，对物价不敏感，那可太难了。说明矿泉水在消费认知里，高价，能接受。<br></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>为什么我们没说过“饮料刺客”？</span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>好像刺的次数多了，刺的人多了，也就习惯了。</span></p>
<section>
<section>
<section>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 18px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;"><strong>1、</strong><span style="font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', Roboto, Oxygen, Ubuntu, Cantarell, 'Open Sans', 'Helvetica Neue', sans-serif;">或许，本来也想当刺客</span></h2>
</section>
</section>
</section>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在近日，跟水有关，跟矿泉水有关的新闻是：</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>元气森林的发布会上我们听到很有意思的一句话：做水可以失败，但要让天下水厂卷起来，激起千层浪。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/122765559862ced2f865b873.92727632.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>元气森林旗下品牌有矿，推出的矿泉水好像是一个卷的存在。<br></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>跟所有走高端路线的矿泉水品牌一样，</span><span><strong>最初</strong><strong>都曾选择5元定价</strong></span><span>，有矿也不例外。<br></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>2021年7年，元气森林上线了首款<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E7%93%B6%E8%A3%85%E6%B0%B4" target="_blank">瓶装水</a>产品，并开始在电商渠道测试。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>测试进行得很低调，没有选择流量更大的官方旗舰店，而是选择上架旗下知名度较低的外星人品牌旗舰店。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>先说定价。当时非折扣正式定价为120元/箱/24瓶，定价为5元/瓶。去年的这个时候，我的钱包真的有被伤到，好在它明码标价，没有混在康师傅怡宝中浑水摸鱼。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/188383952962ced2f8a4f1e4.28103727.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>不过，高颜值真的没的说。产品营销方面，延续了细分品类故事化。在品牌故事中，该产品水源来自云南原始森林深层自涌泉，经地下深层玄武岩34年过滤，富含人体需要的钾、钙等微量矿物质元素，天然弱碱性，建议4℃饮用最佳。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>不过卖的不算好，相比起自家的明星产品气泡水，它跑的有点慢。</span></p>
<section>
<section>
<section>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 18px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;"><strong>2、</strong><span style="font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', Roboto, Oxygen, Ubuntu, Cantarell, 'Open Sans', 'Helvetica Neue', sans-serif;">刺不成，那就做卷王吧</span></h2>
</section>
</section>
</section>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>就是说，新消费产品很喜欢给自己弄个人设。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>比如0糖，比如0脂，比如低卡。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>通过娱乐圈我们看到，有人设的最容易翻车，饮料也不例外。比如元气森林乳茶说自己0蔗糖，但里面有结晶果糖，结果最终就是消费者明白了还是有糖的，只不过是代糖。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>有矿上来给自己讲的是一个精致瓶装水故事，重点在于消费升级后的品质生活。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>不过当来到瓶装水这个刚需赛道，情况发生了变化。元气森林以5元的差异化定价，进行瓶装水消费升级时才会发现，这个价格带的市场还未成熟。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>5元刺客来到赛道，发现“拔剑四顾心茫然”。</span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/143457023262ced2f8e644a8.75038717.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>于是它经过500天的摸索，最终重新定价到目前的3元主流价格带。在日前的沟通会上，有矿再次宣布降价，新版建议零售价直接调至3元。元气森林研究院院长王雪刚还强调，“想把国外进口矿泉水‘卷’到平民价位”。好处是定价更符合主流市场，同时劣势是元气森林也没能避开白刃战，一头扎入红海。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>那就卷起来。</span></p>

<section>
<section>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 18px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;"><strong>3、</strong><span style="font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', Roboto, Oxygen, Ubuntu, Cantarell, 'Open Sans', 'Helvetica Neue', sans-serif;">“价格下探”为抢地盘</span></h2>
</section>
</section>
</section>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>天然矿泉水在国内发展了几十年，一直发展较慢的原因跟品类发展路径有关。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>由于最初甚至长期被依云、巴黎水、斐泉等进口矿泉水占领，10-30元一瓶的高价令消费者望尘莫及，使得品类在消费端上的渗透较慢。更多中国消费者青睐定价在2元以下的饮用天然水、纯净水等，反而渐渐形成了对进口矿泉水是“智商税”的负面认知。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>亲民的价格，是本土化运作必走之路。</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>时至今日，在国内主流矿泉水平均单价不足5元/瓶的情况之下，众多矿泉水品牌还在进一步价格下探。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>据媒体FBIF，走访线下各大商超便利发现，标价4.6元的统一爱夸天然矿泉水，在喜士多促销6元两瓶，在全家促销2元/瓶。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/101200256262ced2f94441d9.32573404.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>

<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>刺客，或者卷王。<br></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>都是时代造就的，市场发展就必然会有高价位产品，而超高价位也会经过市场打磨开始价格下探。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>如开头所言，刺的人多了，刺的次数多了，也就无所谓刺客不刺客。<br></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>祝所有消费者都能买到心仪的商品，<br></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>也愿所有品牌都找对适合自己的消费人群。<br></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>市场海海，蛋糕很大，大家都能吃的开心。</span></p>
