                        <p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">自从6月份，<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E4%B8%9C%E6%96%B9%E7%94%84%E9%80%89" target="_blank">东方甄选</a>董宇辉等一众主播凭借知识带货强势出圈，东方甄选一夜持续爆火，销售额不断攀升。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">短短3个月时间内，东方甄选成长为抖音的超级头部直播间，场均销售额逼近2000万。蝉妈妈显示，过去的6月和7月，东方甄选表现出色，直播销售额力压罗永浩的交个朋友直播间，成为抖音新晋带货“一哥”。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">在这样良好的带货势头下，眼尖的朋友发现，东方甄选在各大应用平台上线了同名独立APP。<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">根据APP数据分析平台蝉大师显示，目前<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E4%B8%9C%E6%96%B9%E7%94%84%E9%80%89APP" target="_blank">东方甄选APP</a>的累计总下载量已接近20万，</strong>消息一出引发外界热烈讨论。</p>
<h1 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: 30px; color: #212629; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 20px; text-align: center; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); font-weight: 600; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">爆火之后热度持续，</strong><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">东方甄选销售额一路领先</strong></h1>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">此前曾有市场传言称，东方甄选直播间曾遭遇抖音限流。不过后续已经得到抖音电商相关负责人的辟谣，表示不存在对东方甄选限流的情况。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">通过蝉妈妈可以看到，除了6月中旬东方甄选爆火，进入流量巅峰期。进入7月之后，直播间的整体流量比较平稳，场均观看也突破2000万人次，并未出现大幅度持续低迷的情况。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span id="article-img-0" class="a-image" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"><img style="margin: 0px auto 20px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: middle; border: none; max-width: 100%; display: block; background: url('//static.yidianzixun.com/apps/black_mamba_web_v1.0.0.177/black_mamba_web_main_pic_placeholder.png') 50% center no-repeat #e6e6e6;" src="https://i1.go2yd.com/image.php?url=YD_cnt_38_01EN2QTwenFl" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>

<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin-bottom: 25px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">▲图片来自蝉妈妈</strong></h4>
</div>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">在极短的时间内，东方甄选很好地承接了涌来的流量，实现快速组品开播，目前东方甄选直播间稳定日播，保持每天18小时左右的开播时长。<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">在销售额方面，东方甄选一路领跑众多抖音带货达人，6月带货额突破7亿，7月销售额突破6亿持续霸榜，</strong>与其他头部主播拉开不小的距离。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">在带货类目方面，食品饮料类目依然是东方甄选的主推类目，上架占比48.87%，其次则是生鲜蔬果、日用百货，且多条商品链接来自抖店东方甄选企业店。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span id="article-img-1" class="a-image" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"><img style="margin: 0px auto 20px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: middle; border: none; max-width: 100%; display: block; background: url('//static.yidianzixun.com/apps/black_mamba_web_v1.0.0.177/black_mamba_web_main_pic_placeholder.png') 50% center no-repeat #e6e6e6;" src="https://i1.go2yd.com/image.php?url=YD_cnt_38_01EN2OwVkSr4" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<div>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin-bottom: 25px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">▲图片来自蝉妈妈</strong></h4>

<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">蝉妈妈显示，近30天东方甄选直播间销售额排行TOP10的产品中，9款产品商品来源都是东方甄选的抖店，可见在近期东方甄选自营的供应链已经搭建完善，可以支持大额订单。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span id="article-img-2" class="a-image" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"><img style="margin: 0px auto 20px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: middle; border: none; max-width: 100%; display: block; background: url('//static.yidianzixun.com/apps/black_mamba_web_v1.0.0.177/black_mamba_web_main_pic_placeholder.png') 50% center no-repeat #e6e6e6;" src="https://i1.go2yd.com/image.php?url=YD_cnt_38_01EN2OyG5RBX" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>

<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin-bottom: 25px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">▲蝉妈妈商品分析</strong></h4>

<h1 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: 30px; color: #212629; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 20px; text-align: center; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); font-weight: 600; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">积极建设自有平台，</strong><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">东方甄选独立APP已上线</strong></h1>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">东方甄选在经过流量巅峰之后，依然保持着强有劲的销售势头。新东方做带货直播，可以算是顺利转型。此前，俞敏洪就曾在个人公众号表示，“在东方甄选热闹的背后，也是我们对于农业和生活用品产业链以及供应链的更长远布局。毕竟，基于外部的平台所建立起来的热闹的商业模式，是有很强的脆弱性的，要夯实长期发展的基础，我们还有很长的路要走。”</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">对于东方甄选来说，自建平台不仅意味着只是自建供应链，深度发展电商事业，也包括了自建销售平台，脱离直播平台的影响与限制。蝉妈妈也下载了这款APP，可以看到目前页面暂无外部广告植入，页面相对比较简洁，页面底部设有“甄选”“分类”“购物车”和“我的订单”这四大入口。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span id="article-img-3" class="a-image" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"><img style="margin: 0px auto 20px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: middle; border: none; max-width: 100%; display: block; background: url('//static.yidianzixun.com/apps/black_mamba_web_v1.0.0.177/black_mamba_web_main_pic_placeholder.png') 50% center no-repeat #e6e6e6;" src="https://i1.go2yd.com/image.php?url=YD_cnt_38_01EN2P16T3rF" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<div>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin-bottom: 25px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">▲图片来自网络</strong></h4>
</div>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">商城内部的产品类目包括自营商品、生鲜果蔬、海鲜水产和图书文娱等，带货的产品跟抖音直播间销售额的类目高度相似，价格水平保持一致。<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">此外，我们也可以看到东方甄选积极布局私域运营，通过APP顶部的“东方甄选粉丝团”将用户导入私域</strong>，并通过发放优惠劵等方式，让用户开始在东方甄选APP内进行下单，以及创办专属社群。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">目前，东方甄选的抖音账号还在正常直播中，对于外界认为东方甄选将要完全独立，不在抖音做直播，业内人士都认为现阶段大概率不太可能，毕竟自建电商平台所耗费的资金和精力对于目前的新东方来说，不是一种有性价比的变现方式。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">且蝉妈妈观察到，东方甄选似乎走上了跟交个朋友类似的垂类矩阵账号的道路，<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">除了东</strong><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">方甄选，东方甄选之图书、东方甄选美丽生活、东方甄选自营产品多个矩阵账号都在稳定开播中，月销售额皆在千万级别以上</strong>，最高单个账号月销售额突破5000万。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span id="article-img-4" class="a-image" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"><img style="margin: 0px auto 20px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: middle; border: none; max-width: 100%; display: block; background: url('//static.yidianzixun.com/apps/black_mamba_web_v1.0.0.177/black_mamba_web_main_pic_placeholder.png') 50% center no-repeat #e6e6e6;" src="https://i1.go2yd.com/image.php?url=YD_cnt_38_01EN2OwUXZAs" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>

<h2 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; line-height: 30px; color: #212629; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 18px; text-align: left; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-weight: 600; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">小结</strong></h2>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">对于东方甄选来说，自建APP可能不会重点布局运营，但是它始终是新东方获取自主权的开始，也是自己为自己做的护城河。现阶段东方甄选在抖音做好直播带货，拓展更多产品类目，打造垂类矩阵账号，可能显得更为紧迫一点。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular, 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, 宋体, 'Arial Narrow'; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">也有媒体报道过，在东方甄选自建APP之前，其实已经开通了天猫旗舰店、京东旗舰店、东方甄选会员微信小程序等等。多平台联动自有平台，可能是未来东方甄选的业务核心。东方甄选在抖音开了个好头，未来还有很长的路要走，蝉妈妈也会持续关注，敬请期待。</p>                        <div style="height: 1px"></div>
