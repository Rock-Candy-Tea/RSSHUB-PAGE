<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-mpa-powered-by="yiban.io"><span>永远不要低估国家<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E5%8F%8D%E5%9E%84%E6%96%AD" target="_blank">反垄断</a>的决心！</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>自“防止资本无序扩张”、<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E9%98%BF%E9%87%8C" target="_blank">阿里</a>“二选一”案落锤后，互联网平台反垄断的序幕已经缓缓拉开。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>如今，铁拳再度砸向阿里。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/99323113621d859ad90f46.00495923.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span></span></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span><strong><span>阿里再遭反垄断处罚</span></strong></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>国家出手，法律的重剑正缓缓落下。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>日前，市场监管总局根据《中华人民共和国反垄断法》对28起未依法申报违法实施经营者集中案件作出行政处罚决定。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/146779082762ce0e1f8b02d4.43306359.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在这28起案件中，腾讯、阿里等巨头赫然上榜。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>其中，涉及腾讯交易共计12起，合计罚款600万元；涉及阿里巴巴交易共5起，合集罚款250万元。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/195748506862ce0e1fe43da1.79647109.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>对比此前阿里的182.28亿天价罚单，此次数百万的罚款可谓之毛毛雨。而事实上，罚款金额的如此悬殊，则与案件性质不同有关。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>依据反垄断法，达到一定门槛标准的经营者在实施集中（合并、收购等）时，需要事先向国务院反垄断执法机构申报，未经申报则不得实施集中。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>而此次涉及的28起案件中，不乏阿里收购百世集团、第一财经，以及腾讯收购有车股权等知名事件，依据反垄断法均需进行申报后进行交易。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>例如阿里收购百世案，作为专注于快递物流的百世集团，与电子商务巨头阿里明显属于产业上下游关系。在2017年，在认购百世的1000万新发行股后，阿里共持有后者27%的股份，实际投票权达46.2%，这也意味着阿里拥有了对百世集团的控制权。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>显然，此次交易符合反垄断法所规定的经营者集中情形，应依法进行申报，然而在当时的历史环境下，企业并未将向国家部门申报当做必要事情来做。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>巨头对法律的藐视，终将自食恶果。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/96768287962ce0e206d94d2.10516605.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>这也导致在去年3月，市场监管总局对该交易进行立案调查。在最终评估这桩交易不会产生排除、限制竞争的效果后，作出了50万元的顶格处罚。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>此次28起交易，均为未依法申报违法实施经营者集中案件，最终便是每桩交易均受到了50万元的罚款，阿里涉及5起案件，累计罚款250万元。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>罚款金额虽小，但意义重大！</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>法律范围内的“顶格处罚”，足以给这些互联网巨头们敲响警钟。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/40300182621d859c4744b0.36029202.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span></span></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span><strong><span>屠龙少年终成恶龙</span></strong></span><span></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>事实上，阿里因违反《反垄断法》被罚已非首次。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>从去年的182.28亿元天价罚单，到后续违法实施经营者集中案件，屠龙少年终成恶龙的故事让人惋惜。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>那个曾高喊着“让天下没有难做的生意”的阿里跌落神坛，那个曾被无数年轻人奉为“精神导师”的马云时代落幕。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/83349503362ce0e2146c672.96816708.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>故事，还要从2020年底开始讲起。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>彼时，中共中央政治局会议提出“要强化反垄断和防止资本无序扩张”，剑指阿里。大约两周后，阿里巴巴便因涉嫌垄断被立案调查。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>该案件的核心，便是被无数商家控诉的强制“二选一”行为。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>早在2015年，京东便怒而起诉天猫滥用市场支配地位，强迫商家“二选一”，揭开了行业的冰山一角，史称“东猫案”。2019年，格兰仕也揭竿而起，就天猫强制“二选一”行为发起诉讼。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>天下苦“二选一”久已。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>幸好，正义或许会迟到，但绝不会缺席。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>2021年4月，一则高达182.28亿元的天价反垄断处罚案引爆网络，阿里的反垄断调查结果正式出炉！</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>国家市场监管总局下达公告显示，自2015年以来，阿里滥用其市场支配地位，实施“二选一”垄断行为以获取不正当竞争优势。责令其停止违法行为，并处以其2019年销售额4%计182.28亿元罚款。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/142705492162ce0e219e1c75.80680983.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>一时间，身处风暴中心的阿里成为众矢之的。该案也凭借近200亿元的罚款金额，成为中国互联网史上反垄断第一大案。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>与此同时，这张天价罚单也如燎原之火，烧掉了阿里万亿江山，将其彻底钉在了国内互联网老二的位子上。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>百亿天价罚单，给所有巨头敲响了警钟。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>当巨头们凭借着自己的垄断地位和资本实力，把持市场门槛；当“互联网+”失去了应有的革新能力，成为创新的拦路虎。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>反垄断的利刃，当然会挥向互联网。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/2028633948621d859f7b3c30.12849065.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span><strong><span>互联互通新时代</span></strong></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在互联网领域，反垄断已经不是个新鲜话题。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>2012年，腾讯与奇虎360间的“3Q大战”最终对峙公堂，这场诉讼也被认为是中国互联网史上首起反垄断案。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>2021年，阿里被处以182.28亿天价罚单、国家反垄断局正式成立，中国互联网反垄断开启新的征程。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>除阿里这一老牌巨头外，美团、滴滴等后起之秀也均自食恶果，吃到了反垄断的罚单。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/95870915762ce0e230ccde8.50325236.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>甚至于腾讯也在去年年底“分红式”大举减持京东，持股比例从17%骤降至2.3%，以降低自身被反垄断监管的风险。业内人士表示，“现在主动分，总好过以后被强制要求卖掉好。”</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>巨头的纷纷转身，意味着互联网垄断的旧时代已经结束，互通互联将是新时代的主旋律。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>去年9月份，工信部召开行政指导会，明确要求阿里、腾讯、字节等平台限期解除屏蔽网址链接。这也意味着，国家正从监管层面发力，着手推进互联网平台互联互通。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>解除外链屏蔽也将成为中国互联网发展史上的一个历史性节点，它意味着在过去十年里，“平台垄断，赢家通吃”的资本游戏将得以终结。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在反垄断和互联互通的大趋势下，阿里和腾讯也开始彼此拥抱。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>今年618前夕，天猫正式进入微信朋友圈。朋友圈广告可以直接通往商家的天猫旗舰店。这意味着，微信与淘系<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E7%94%B5%E5%95%86" target="_blank">电商</a>之间多年的“外链封杀”或将走到尽头。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/121412480862ce0e2376aae5.96302343.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>随着微信庞大流量，面向阿里开放，双方合则两利，淘系商家距离“全域”获客的形式更近了一步，阿里则距离平台垄断的道路更远了一些。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>纵观近十年的互联网创新之路，巨头的垄断之势一旦形成，势必会将成本都摊在链条的所有参与者身上，无一例外。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>然而国家和社会需要的不是一枝独秀，而是春色满园。互联互通，才能给商家、消费者更良好的体验，更充分的选择。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>旧时代的落幕，意味着百花齐放的新时代来临。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>一只蝴蝶轻轻扇动翅膀，新时代的浪潮便喷涌而出。</span></p>
