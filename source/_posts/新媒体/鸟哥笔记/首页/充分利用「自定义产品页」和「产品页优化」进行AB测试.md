                        <blockquote>
<p><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #5f5f5f; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; font-variant-ligatures: normal; letter-spacing: 0.544px; orphans: 2; text-align: justify; widows: 2; background-color: #f4f4f4; text-decoration-thickness: initial;">App Store是iOS App下载的唯一出口。目前App Store的周活跃用户已超过6亿，如何提高App曝光率、影响用户产生下载是每个开发者和营销者的难题。</span></p>
</blockquote>
<p style="font-size: 16px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: #3e3e3e; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; font-variant-ligatures: normal; letter-spacing: 0.544px; orphans: 2; text-align: justify; widows: 2; background-color: #ffffff; text-decoration-thickness: initial; visibility: visible; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"> </p>
<p style="font-size: 16px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: #3e3e3e; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; font-variant-ligatures: normal; letter-spacing: 0.544px; orphans: 2; text-align: justify; widows: 2; background-color: #ffffff; text-decoration-thickness: initial; visibility: visible; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">众所周知，iOS 15及以上版本【<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E8%87%AA%E5%AE%9A%E4%B9%89%E4%BA%A7%E5%93%81%E9%A1%B5" target="_blank">自定义产品页</a>】和【<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E4%BA%A7%E5%93%81%E9%A1%B5%E4%BC%98%E5%8C%96" target="_blank">产品页优化</a>】新功能能够帮助开发者和营销者有效地管理自定义产品页面，根据不同受众群体打造不同的情景体验，以获得直接有效的营销转化。</p>
<p style="font-size: 16px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: #3e3e3e; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; font-variant-ligatures: normal; letter-spacing: 0.544px; orphans: 2; text-align: justify; widows: 2; background-color: #ffffff; text-decoration-thickness: initial; visibility: visible; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"> </p>
<p> </p>
<p style="font-size: 16px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: #3e3e3e; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; font-variant-ligatures: normal; letter-spacing: 0.544px; orphans: 2; text-align: justify; widows: 2; background-color: #ffffff; text-decoration-thickness: initial; visibility: visible; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">本文我们将阐述<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">如何充分利用【自定义产品页】进行有效的营销活动，以及【自定义产品页】和【产品页优化】之间的区别。</strong></p>
<p style="font-size: 16px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: #3e3e3e; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; font-variant-ligatures: normal; letter-spacing: 0.544px; orphans: 2; text-align: justify; widows: 2; background-color: #ffffff; text-decoration-thickness: initial; visibility: visible; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"> </p>
<h1 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); background-size: 16px 20px; color: #212629; font-size: 20px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: center; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat no-repeat;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; color: #0a49f5; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; font-size: 20px; font-variant-ligatures: normal; letter-spacing: 0.544px; orphans: 2; text-align: center; widows: 2; background-color: #ffffff; text-decoration-thickness: initial; visibility: visible; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">利用自定义产品页测试营销效果</strong></h1>
<p> </p>
<p> </p>
<ul>
<li>自定义产品页可以帮助开发者在App Store中创建多个不同的产品页；</li>
<li>可将不同的应用本地化；</li>
<li>每个版本可以通过设置App预览、截图、推广文本展示不同内容，例如：运动项目、角色、节目，以及游戏功能等，并通过唯一的 URL 以将用户引导至相关产品页面；</li>
<li>在 Apple Ads 账户中，可使用自定义产品页，针对不同受众创建更佳适合的广告版本</li>
</ul>
<p> </p>
<p><img src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/5515b6decaaffbbe4e7887ed61f2d889.png" alt width="2228" height="1238" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #3e3e3e; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; font-variant-ligatures: normal; letter-spacing: 0.544px; orphans: 2; text-align: justify; widows: 2; background-color: #ffffff; text-decoration-thickness: initial;">也就是说，【</span><strong style="font-size: 16px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #3e3e3e; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; font-variant-ligatures: normal; letter-spacing: 0.544px; orphans: 2; text-align: justify; widows: 2; background-color: #ffffff; text-decoration-thickness: initial; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">自定义产品页】是通过唯一的URL针对某一些指定用户群体投放的广告，一般用于推广渠道，例如：<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/ASA" target="_blank">ASA</a>广告投放、邮件营销、短信营销、官网投放等</strong><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #3e3e3e; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; font-variant-ligatures: normal; letter-spacing: 0.544px; orphans: 2; text-align: justify; widows: 2; background-color: #ffffff; text-decoration-thickness: initial;">。那如何利用改变元数据进行推广测试，我们应该测试哪些方面，以下是可以在【自定义产品页】的帮助下进行测试的实际示例。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<h3 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//1bb91f73e9d31ea2830a5e73ce3ed328.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; color: #212629; font-size: 16px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat no-repeat;"><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #3e3e3e; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; font-variant-ligatures: normal; letter-spacing: 0.544px; orphans: 2; text-align: justify; widows: 2; background-color: #ffffff; text-decoration-thickness: initial;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; color: #1f1e1e; font-size: 17px; font-variant-ligatures: normal; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: center; text-decoration-thickness: initial; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; text-align: justify; font-size: 18px; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">测试购买优化</span></strong></span></h3>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">了解受众兴趣和喜爱度，自定义产品页主题、宣传文字、截图等相关内容，制定用户能够接受的价格，吸引用户进行购买，带来有效转化。</p>
<p> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"> </p>
<h3 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//1bb91f73e9d31ea2830a5e73ce3ed328.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; color: #212629; font-size: 16px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat no-repeat;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; color: #1f1e1e; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; font-size: 18px; font-variant-ligatures: normal; letter-spacing: 0.544px; orphans: 2; text-align: center; widows: 2; background-color: #ffffff; text-decoration-thickness: initial; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; text-align: justify; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">测试细分受众群体</span></strong></h3>
<p> </p>
<p><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; color: #1f1e1e; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; font-size: 18px; font-variant-ligatures: normal; letter-spacing: 0.544px; orphans: 2; text-align: center; widows: 2; background-color: #ffffff; text-decoration-thickness: initial; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; text-align: justify; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-weight: 400; color: #3e3e3e; font-variant-ligatures: normal; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-decoration-thickness: initial;">我们用游戏App举例。每款玩家都有特定的需求和模板，我们可以根据用户个性、游戏风格对用户进行划分，比如：玩游戏用来解压、力争在游戏中击败他人获得升级、消磨时间等。开发商和营销者需要正确细分这些用户群体，分析他们能够带来的价值从而制定活动，来获得转化赢得ROAS。</span></span></strong></p>
<p> </p>
<h3 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//1bb91f73e9d31ea2830a5e73ce3ed328.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; color: #212629; font-size: 16px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat no-repeat;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; color: #1f1e1e; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; font-size: 18px; font-variant-ligatures: normal; letter-spacing: 0.544px; orphans: 2; text-align: center; widows: 2; background-color: #ffffff; text-decoration-thickness: initial; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; text-align: justify; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-weight: 400; color: #3e3e3e; font-variant-ligatures: normal; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-decoration-thickness: initial;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; color: #1f1e1e; font-size: 18px; font-variant-ligatures: normal; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: center; text-decoration-thickness: initial; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; text-align: justify; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">测试不同地区不同优惠</span></strong></span></span></strong></h3>
<p> </p>
<p style="font-size: 16px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: #3e3e3e; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; font-variant-ligatures: normal; letter-spacing: 0.544px; orphans: 2; text-align: justify; widows: 2; background-color: #ffffff; text-decoration-thickness: initial; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">地域、文化差异、消费能力等因素也是影响广告回报率的重要因素之一。借助自定义产品页的功能，针对不同国家/地区市场调整、突出某项特定内容。</p>
<p style="font-size: 16px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: #3e3e3e; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; font-variant-ligatures: normal; letter-spacing: 0.544px; orphans: 2; text-align: justify; widows: 2; background-color: #ffffff; text-decoration-thickness: initial; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"> </p>
<p> </p>
<section style="font-size: 16px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; color: #3e3e3e; font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light; font-variant-ligatures: normal; letter-spacing: 0.544px; orphans: 2; text-align: justify; widows: 2; background-color: #ffffff; text-decoration-thickness: initial; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">案例：今日头条</strong></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">头条在高考前借助高考热度创建了两个不同的产品页吸引用户。</p>

<p> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-variant-ligatures: normal; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">在App产品页的不同版本中，测试使用不同的价值主张、角色、功能、App图标、优惠活动等内容，这些版本会随机推给一定比例符合条件的用户</strong>，<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">通过“App分析”找到能够与目标受众产生共鸣的版本，并推广给App Store上的所有人显示。</strong></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-variant-ligatures: normal; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"> </p>
<p> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-variant-ligatures: normal; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">产品页优化即对元数据进行A/B测试，以便更直观的了解用户喜好，更好的进行归因。</p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-variant-ligatures: normal; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"> </p>
