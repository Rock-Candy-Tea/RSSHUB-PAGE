<h1 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); font-size: 20px; color: #212629; text-align: center; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span>01、</span><span>从想法到立项</span><span></span></h1>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/1064569326631f15e2938bb0.93591511.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>我们的想法通常来⾃于对产品的试⽤，product hunt，排⾏榜，同⾏交流等渠道信息，在有具体的产品想法后，我们会进⾏市场。</span><span>（九日补充：product hunt长期以来是一个非常好的冷启动平台，配合PH上的KOL一起做一波好的宣发能带来不少种子用户，值得注意的是这个平台通常对ToB的软件应用更加友好。我们龙总拿这个平台作为新品的一个灵感来源，对此我是认同的，但PH的产品未必都是经过市场检验的，大部分产品甚至在榜单排名都非常靠后，这个风险要学会自控）</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>研究，围绕新增⽤户量，变现规模和⽤户价值，⽇活规模，竞争程度等⻆度评估，是否进⼊内部⽴项研发。</span><span><span>（九日补充：关于第三方的一些数据监控平台，我推荐的是AppAnnie/SensorTower/AppStare。上面截图的就是来自AA的监控，把我们所在意的维度都统计了。</span><span>）</span></span></p>
<h1 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); font-size: 20px; color: #212629; text-align: center; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span>02、</span><span>立项的关键要素</span></h1>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>需求⾼频：D7使⽤频次 > 10</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><span>产品解决⽤户真实存在需求，D7⽤户使⽤</span><span>频次超过10次</span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>吸量：Top5竞品⽉新增 > 1000w</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><span>⽬标⽤户众多，⼴阔的市场空间，买量还</span><span>是⾃增都能逐步形成正向循环</span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>Top：有机会做到市场前5前3</span></strong><span></span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>有机会进⼊市场的Top5/Top3</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>九日补充：我这里和龙总会有一些些的小出入，我可能会根据更niche的一个指标来选品，比如商店页的某个有热度的关键词下面的竞品分布和排名是如何的。举个例子，比如说我们要做照片编辑类的软件。我不会去看照片和相机全频道的竞品，这可太难了。我会选择一个分类，比如“漫画脸”、“变老变年轻”，然后我会看看这些关键词下的竞品分布是如何的，评估我们有多大把握能狙击进前2。早期如果能有一个小突破口先稳定点流量带来点收入，对后续延展会有一定帮助。当然，这思路或许更适合个人工作室或小创业团队。</span><span></span></p>
<h1 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); font-size: 20px; color: #212629; text-align: center; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span>03、</span><span>3-5周度过冷启动Alpha阶段</span></h1>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/408614951631f15e2d054e7.07616467.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>投放先⾏⼀步</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>⼴告投放需要产品研发先⾏⼀步，解决受</span><span>众，成本，策略，素材</span><span>4</span><span>个基础问题</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>产品先</span><span>拷贝<span>⼀步</span></span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>分解竞品主流程，⼤胆模仿，⼩⼼验证，这</span><span>个阶段需要解决产品的</span><span>可</span><span>⽤性问题：</span><span>App</span><span>类产品的</span><span>D0</span><span>未卸载率、</span><span>游戏类产品的新⼿过关率</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>数据复盘⼀步</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>初步的投放后，回收1周左右的数据，这个阶</span><span>段要解决：</span><span>投放账户的构建；</span><span>产品北极星指标；</span><span>技术的稳定性</span><span>(</span><span>崩溃率等</span><span>)</span><span>；</span><span></span></p>
<h1 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); font-size: 20px; color: #212629; text-align: center; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span>04、</span><span>通过</span><span>Alpha</span><span>阶段的关键要素</span></h1>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>未卸载率：D0 > 60%</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>论证⽤户使⽤当⽇能理解产品需求，解决⽅案</span><span>真实有</span><span>效。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>投放：D7 ROI/ROAS > 40%</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>根据⽤户使⽤数据⼴告频次，订单率等数据核算⽤户⽣</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>命周期价值，⽣命周期价值能够⽀撑投放成本规模。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>上面只是一个起步的很基本阙值，已经被论证有效。补充一个背景，龙总的产品大多以休闲游戏和工具软件为主。</span><span></span></p>
<h1 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); font-size: 20px; color: #212629; text-align: center; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span>05、</span><span>从</span><span>1</span><span>到</span><span>10</span><span>的</span><span>Beta</span><span>阶段</span><span></span></h1>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/2083566495631f15e3117903.47723710.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>留存根据自己产品特性来看，这个阶段已经可以对齐行业平均水平了。像社交软件的次留在这个阶段如果没有65%以上的基本也可以算是嗝屁了，当然这些公式不适用于套路收割型产品或者强变现产品，强变现产品直接关注7日内ARPU和回本周期就行了，基本也别指望很大的自然流量。</span><span></span></p>
<h1 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); font-size: 20px; color: #212629; text-align: center; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span>06、</span><span><span><strong>通过Beta阶段的关键要素</strong></span></span><span></span></h1>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>留存：D1: 30%+，D7: 18%+，D30: 10%+</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>产品有良好的⽤户粘性，能够⽀撑⻓期的⽇活跃规模</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>商业化：D7 ROI/ROAS > 80%</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>商业模型正向，付费/复购、⼴告回收能够在预期内打平投放成本。九日补充：根据会议期间提供的情报，2位嘉宾（龙总和恋爱记创始人）都说广告变现能够在美区实现不错的收益，7日内可以回本。另一个个人开发者则表示，通过订阅+广告也能实现7日内回本。</span></p>
<h1 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); font-size: 20px; color: #212629; text-align: center; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span>07、</span><span><span><strong>有规模的市场化：账户，素材，数据</strong></span></span></h1>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>稳定出量的主投放账户体系及备份次投放账户体系</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>Facebook学习周期短，⾄少3个主投账户及3个备⽤账户</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>G</span><span>oogle机器学习时间⻓，投放稳定，也是主投账户的最佳选择代投及其他媒体渠道补量</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>九日推荐代投：</span></strong></span><span><strong><span>北京的AdsMarch</span></strong></span><span><span></span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>素材和创意</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>投放最核⼼的⽀撑基础</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>3-5天的素材alpha和beta测试周期</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>不断降低买量成本</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/1562950630631f15e33ed1f1.91368847.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>ROI/ROAS数据中台</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>能衡量⽤户级别的ROI/ROAS</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>能衡量媒体-⼴告系列-⼴告组-素材各个级别的ROI/ROAS</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>能计算及预测⽣命周期的⽤户付费率/复购率/⼴告价值</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>上面几个是海外的几个广告SDK，当然你也可以选择国内一些优秀的聚合广告SDK。<strong>九日这里推荐的是：TopOn和TradPlus。</strong></span><span></span></p>
<h1 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); font-size: 20px; color: #212629; text-align: center; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span>08、</span><span><span><strong>有营销点的产品：创新，推送，激励</strong></span></span></h1>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/614696361631f15e378f679.88551976.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>有独特的创新点，能够形成记忆点，有传播效应</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>产品的⽣命周期需要依靠创新来不断延⻓，此外，优秀的产品创新和投放买量相辅相成（上面这个活动就很有意思了，Podcast的产品和比特币挂钩）</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>推送</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>根据⽤户的特征和个性化画像，构建产品消息推送，养成⽤户习惯；留下⽤户联系⽅式，引导⽤户关注社交媒体</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>激励</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>短线波浪型的⾦钱物品激励</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>标志式的运营活动</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>⻓线的精神激励</span></p>
<h1 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); font-size: 20px; color: #212629; text-align: center; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span>09、</span><span><span><strong>可依赖的研发：执⾏⼒，壁垒，稳定</strong></span></span></h1>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>唯快不破</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>研发的执⾏⼒在这个阶段是最重要的，游戏副本，运营活动，beta版本等。都</span><span>需要能够⾼效响应和执⾏的研发</span></p>
