                        <p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="1" data-pm-slice="0 0 []"><span style="letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E6%8A%96%E9%9F%B3" target="_blank">抖音</a>近期又有哪些涨粉新案例？</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="3"><span style="letter-spacing: 0.5px;">近期，抖音有个账号3天涨粉90万，这样的涨粉速度迅速引起了运营人的注意！对于一夜爆粉或短时间内粉丝飙升的账号来说，博主们的吸粉特点其实各有千秋。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="5"><span style="letter-spacing: 0.5px;">平均每天增粉30万的<strong>@蒋明周</strong>，靠什么技能涨粉呢？</span></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;" data-track="8"><span style="font-size: 1.176em;"><span style="letter-spacing: 1.5px;"><strong>01、以声音圈粉，他靠一首歌爆火</strong></span></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="10">上周六，博主@蒋明周发了一条标题为<em><span style="color: #a0a0a0; --tt-darkmode-color: #A0A0A0;">“塔吊上的空调太难装了。爬上爬下好几次活没干呢累虚脱了#唱歌 #无声卡清唱 #经典老歌 #真人真唱 #高温下的劳动者 @DOU+小助手”</span></em>的视频，拿下了<strong>260万+</strong>的点赞量。</p>
<p class="pgc-img" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/6754a5edd36d0c9da7b5a4142d0c6605.png" width="354" height="559" data-ic="false" data-width="708" data-height="1118" data-ic-uri referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 class="pgc-img" style="text-align: center; background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span class="pgc-img-caption">▲图片来源：果集•飞瓜数据</span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="14">他在视频中清唱粤语歌《必杀技》，引发一批又一批用户感慨，“这技能实属了不得！”</p>
<p class="pgc-img" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/21e5aabeb70e72791ccdc1f2d5cc6f14.png" width="636" height="227" data-ic="false" data-width="1272" data-height="454" data-ic-uri referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="pgc-img-caption"></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="18">随后2天，蒋明周发布的2个视频，热度依旧不容小觑，而且还在不断攀升。</p>
<p class="pgc-img" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/0672d7ce1aaa6dd7c129f4b1aa87592b.png" width="1281" height="359" data-ic="false" data-width="1281" data-height="359" data-ic-uri referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="pgc-img-caption"></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="22">据博主在账号中的介绍以及爆火之后的采访视频显示，他的日常工作是负责在塔吊安装空调。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="24">拍出爆火视频那天，他安装空调太累了，休息的时候在工地即兴翻唱了一首粤语歌，没想到因此走红。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="26">蒋明周很喜欢唱歌，在此之前，也经常在抖音发布自己录制的唱歌作品，虽然没火之前粉丝不多，但依旧得到许多老粉肯定。</p>
<p class="pgc-img" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/874d0049df81546e041a1d82a75f5046.png" width="579" height="488" data-ic="false" data-width="1158" data-height="976" data-ic-uri referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 class="pgc-img" style="text-align: center; background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span class="pgc-img-caption">▲弹幕图片来源：果集•飞瓜数据</span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="30">而其中，他翻唱的粤语歌尤其受粉丝喜爱，粉丝们表示，虽然他的粤语不标准，但唱起来真的很好听。蒋明周在回复用户的评论中提到，自己是自学的粤语。</p>
<p class="pgc-img" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://mp.toutiao.com/mp/agw/article_material/open_image/get?code=YzU1NjVmNmY1N2NmMjE2Mjc3OGJlMjAxNjhhOGI0YWEsMTY1NzcwMTg2OTYxNg==" width="1148" height="410" data-ic="false" data-width="1148" data-height="410" data-ic-uri referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="pgc-img-caption"></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="33">火了之后，有不少粉丝跑到之前的作品中留言，自己是从《必杀技》追过来的。</p>
<p class="pgc-img" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/0d8b45d95dc4bd9712dc67f87db79a8e.png" width="770" height="407" data-ic="false" data-width="770" data-height="407" data-ic-uri referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="pgc-img-caption"></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="36">还有的粉丝表示，关注蒋明周的时候，他粉丝不多，但坚信他的歌好听，一定会火，没想到现在真的因为一首歌涨了80多万粉。</p>
<p class="pgc-img" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/d2abf274e3255e3f0ea3ae276c774ac6.png" width="586" height="300" data-ic="false" data-width="1172" data-height="600" data-ic-uri referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="pgc-img-caption"></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="39">而有的网友早就开启“点歌”模式了，会唱的，蒋明周后续就安排上了，不会的，学会了唱给大家听。</p>
<p class="pgc-img" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/498b71407ed107b5a3f83951be5b4df4.png" width="389" height="276" data-ic="false" data-width="778" data-height="552" data-ic-uri referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="pgc-img-caption"></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="42">还有的网友则不时抖机灵，如果是在车上旁录的视频，博主通常会在页面标注“安全道路”，因为翻唱实在好听，但是又没有歌名，于是引发众网友猜歌名。</p>
<p class="pgc-img" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/a4f87e21ec472bbff8435588d5b08a8f.png" width="367" height="264" data-ic="false" data-width="734" data-height="528" data-ic-uri referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="pgc-img-caption"></span></p>
<p class="pgc-p" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="45">因为一首歌蒋明周涨粉数十万，有多个原因的助力：</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="46"><strong><span style="color: #7f42f8; --tt-darkmode-color: #7F42F8;">1.拥有硬核技能</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="48">爆粉得益于他的唱功，在清唱的情况下他也依旧能让听者沉浸式听歌，同时，以唱歌为爱好的他，在高音、颤音等技巧上也表现得相当不错。</p>
<p class="pgc-img" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/8ab7f718012c87aad3887a7d136cdf53.png" width="282" height="74" data-ic="false" data-width="282" data-height="74" data-ic-uri referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="pgc-img-caption"></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="51"><strong><span style="color: #7f42f8; --tt-darkmode-color: #7F42F8;">2.存在反差感</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="53">“能有多好听啊？”</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="54">“我以为平平无奇，结果人家是王者！”。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="56">先入为主的观点让网友在听过蒋明周的歌声后被迅速圈粉，由此造成的反差让人留下深刻的印象，意外之余，也引发了粉丝们的讨论。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="58">有粉丝在评论区留言，为他的账号运营方向筹谋划策，建议他保持在工地唱歌的模式，才能留住用户。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="60"><strong><span style="color: #7f42f8; --tt-darkmode-color: #7F42F8;">3.情感输出</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="62">“唱得很有感情”，真挚的情感能拉近和粉丝的关系，在蒋明周的作品评论区，还可以看到粉丝发表自己听歌的感受。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="64">基于蒋明周因唱歌走红这件事，也有粉丝表示，仿佛又回到了抖音最初的时候。</p>
<p class="pgc-img" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/264a8d02fe9429236ad8796df3f16ec6.png" width="435" height="238" data-ic="false" data-width="870" data-height="476" data-ic-uri referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="pgc-img-caption"></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="67">抖音最初作为一个音乐创意短视频社交软件走入大众视野，历经多年发展，不断拓宽出新的内容边界。在抖音，因为跳舞、变装、卡点等爆火的视频层出不穷，而这背后，“BGM”一直是一个强大的因素。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="69">除了蒋明周，近期，也有不少账号通过“音乐”的路径打开涨粉大门，这些具备涨粉先天优势的BGM，也存在许多共性。</p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;" data-track="72"><span style="font-size: 1.176em;"><span style="letter-spacing: 1.5px;"><strong>02、热门BGM的吸引力</strong></span></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="73">被大量应用到短视频作品中的BGM各有其魅力，或魔性洗脑、可爱、或浪漫、激昂向上……现在提起抖音初期火起来的神曲《海草舞》时，很多人的脑海里就会蹦出刻在记忆里的动作。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="75">而这样具备刷屏属性的音乐，通常很容易就出现在抖音热搜榜。</p>
<p class="pgc-img" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/9cf230f44ce6eb2b360092d6ffbea827.png" width="366" height="762" data-ic="false" data-width="1080" data-height="2248" data-ic-uri referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 class="pgc-img" style="text-align: center; background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span class="pgc-img-caption">▲图片来源：抖音APP</span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="79">比如，最近热度挺高的《Upupu（剪辑版）》，相关话题#papapiu你会唱了吗#在7月10日上了热榜TOP1，总播放量约1.4亿+。</p>
<p class="pgc-img" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/79a5f1b63234a7ebe1dc171a7119b2c4.png" width="477" height="115" data-ic="false" data-width="954" data-height="230" data-ic-uri referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="pgc-img-caption"></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="83">这首歌的可爱程度爆表，引发5000多人参与合唱，其中，赵露思的合唱进一步拉高了歌曲热度。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="85">这些热门音乐又会反哺大众关注和搜索歌曲，关注到创作者和团队，可见音乐的传播力。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="87">近期，周杰伦时隔多年发布新专辑，专辑先行曲MV发布后，毫不意外地在多个平台激起热烈反响，新专辑预售额迅速破500万。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="89">7月18日，快手将联合周杰伦开启独家直播“可以说的秘密”，精准抓住粉丝们雀跃的心情。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="91">完全可以想象新专辑发布当天全网的讨论度，不少创作者可能也会链接热度发布相关作品，但与此同时，对于很多创作者来说，依旧要注意音乐版权问题。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="93">前不久，电竞选手PDD在直播中唱了一首《向天再借五百年》，被版权方起诉索赔10万，后经双方协商，已获得词曲作者谅解。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="95">基于此事件，博主PDD提到的“直播属于商业范畴，唱歌前要获得著作权人同意”也引发讨论。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="97">在日常演唱歌曲以及引用中，如何合理合法使用，避免侵权，也将覆盖到更多博主的创作过程中。</p>
