<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>这一段时间饮料界的大佬们，是有点任性在身上的。</span><span> </span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E9%9B%AA%E7%A2%A7" target="_blank">雪碧</a>要把绿瓶给丢了，<span><a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E5%85%83%E6%B0%94%E6%A3%AE%E6%9E%97" target="_blank">元气森林</a>要卖可乐味气泡水了，</span>真有一种“哀其不惜</span><span>（不珍惜品牌标识）</span><span>，怒其不真</span><span>（不卖真可乐）</span><span>”的戏剧感。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>也不知道<span>两个品牌，是</span>出于<span>“</span>同是天涯有气人<span>”</span>，还是对同行人兮兮相惜，竟然</span><span><strong>搞在一起拍了部大尺度“爽片<strong>”</strong>，看得网友们拍手叫爽。</strong></span><span><strong> </strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/58735563362fbcfa6944082.74737263.png" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: center; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>图源：微博网友评论</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>别误会，我说的爽片，不是那个“爽”。</span></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span>01 </span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>说起来还是雪碧先整活儿。虽然说听君一席话，如听一席话，但雪碧还是先提醒<span>这是雪碧的广告</span>。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>雪碧一上来就给镜头打满屏马赛克，<span>我</span>差点以为自己近视加重了。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>绿瓶当然是雪碧，白瓶看着像元气森林？但不会吧，雪碧是有钱没地方花，还便宜元气森林给它打广告，还是说两个品牌要搞联名了？</span><span> </span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/77027911162fbcfa70320c2.52996891.gif" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span> </span><span>等到镜头越拉越近，另一瓶色白色饮料的真身浮出水面。这.......居然也是雪碧，是雪碧<span>无糖纤维</span>。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/137063901362fbcfa777c152.06795739.gif" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/172492274762fbcfa85d9626.30178689.gif" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>伤害性不大，侮辱性极强，</span><span>句句不提元气森林，句句都是元气森林，<span>雪碧在内涵什么我不说。</span></span><span> </span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>这是不打码就能被元气森林看的吗？</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>想必元气森林看了都要说：“你礼貌吗？我真的谢谢你。”此时配上吴京的表情包</span><span>（你贱不贱呐）</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong>雪碧捆绑竞争对手打广告，营造犹抱琵琶半遮面的悬念，让大家误以为雪碧要翻车，忍不住把视频围观到最后。</strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>而之所以打元气森林的擦边球，<span>雪</span><span>碧</span>像是在暗示<span>无糖也不是元气森林一家独大，不信你尝尝雪碧的无糖系列，<span>光明正大地抢生意。</span></span></span><span> </span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>雪碧这波广告堪称是名副其实的“爽片<span>”</span>，</span><span><strong>一“爽”是宣传了自家饮料“酷爽”的卖点，二<span>“爽”是</span>不爽到了元气森林。</strong></span><span> </span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/186791244762fbcfa935dc64.16353176.png" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: center; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>图源：微博网友评论</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>谁看了不说一句：</span><span><strong>还得是雪碧。</strong></span></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span><strong>02</strong></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><span><span><span>本以为</span>事情到这儿</span><span>就告一段落，</span>但隔壁</span>元气森林真不爽了，决定以其人之道还治其人之身。</span><span> </span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/65085165462fbcfa9a39b58.73375472.png" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: center; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>图源：雪碧官微评论</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>元气森林出手了，拍了支更有梗的广告<span>回应。</span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><span>好笑的是，<span>元气森林</span></span>保留了雪碧原广告的前半部分，但从“别想太多”这句神转折文案开始，对视频后半部分二次剪辑，将内容替换成了元气森林的可乐味。</span><span> </span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/189089422062fbcfaa0faf65.57473913.gif" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span> </span><span>你说没有嘲讽吧，但是又好像有。这个回击调侃的场景，就像是说：你要记住，是你先招惹我的。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/172011101062fbcfac029ee7.66851358.png" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: center; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>图源：微博网友评论</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><span>道高一尺，魔高一丈，果然是</span>有仇必报元气森林。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>咱也不知道，这两条广告究竟是两家联合营销的阳谋，还是说意外相爱相杀的阴谋。但不得不说，</span><span><strong>两个品牌<strong>这一来一回</strong>紧凑发声，<strong>将营销尺度打开，</strong>碰</strong><strong>瓷点到即止，</strong><strong>确实赢在了大<strong>“</strong>气<strong>”</strong>层上。</strong></span></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;">03</h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>话说回来，没点品牌标识的自信，雪碧都不敢把尺度玩这么大。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E5%8F%AF%E5%8F%A3%E5%8F%AF%E4%B9%90" target="_blank">可口可乐</a>前总裁道格拉斯·达夫特说过一句话：</span><span><strong>如果可口可乐全球的工厂被一把火烧掉，只要留下“可口可乐”四个字，他依旧可以再造另一个可口可乐。</strong></span><span> </span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>别问谁给的勇气，问就是可口可乐百年经营的品牌视觉锤给的——用于品牌识别的视觉信息，看到这些标志我们都能联想起对应的品牌。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><span>就像很多人看完电影<span>《独行月球》时</span>，对</span>穿黄色的宇航服的刚子印象深刻，一度以为这是“美团外卖，月球也送”的广告，只怪美团的袋鼠logo形象和黄色太洗脑了。 </span><span> </span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/87333030862fbcfac4042c4.58810687.png" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: center; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>图源：微博网友评论</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>这次的广告主角，无论是雪碧的绿瓶，还是元气森林的白瓶，毫无疑问都是两个品牌鲜明的视觉锤，早已<span>刻进大家的DNA里，超越了视觉的存在。</span></span><span> </span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/3025526562fbcfaca97ab2.91527278.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>哪怕雪碧将绿瓶、白瓶模糊<span>打码</span>处理，大家远远<span>看到相近的颜色时</span>，大脑深处就会不自觉地产生条件反射：</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>猜到<span>分别</span>是雪碧和元气森林，并且也曾误会是其它品牌。</span><strong><span><strong><span> </span></strong></span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><span>所以</span>雪碧真是把品牌标识玩明白了，充分利用自身和对手的品牌标识，进行<span>视觉的刺激</span>，</span><span><strong>制造“雪碧给元</strong><strong>气森林打广告”的假象，让人浮想联翩，收获看到开头猜不到结局的惊喜。</strong></span></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;">04</h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>有句话怎么说来着，不怕神对手，就怕猪队友。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>元气森林能够蹭到雪碧，全靠雪碧带上一起玩，毕竟这也是可口可乐惯的，论内涵可口可乐当属第一名。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>比如去年欧洲杯，可口可乐是<span>欧洲杯的赞助商之一</span>，<span>在新闻发布会上展出产品。但“不识趣”的</span>C罗，却一脸嫌弃地将两瓶可口可乐移到一边，拿起一瓶矿泉水表示只喝水。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/67584684162fbcfad0ab844.21037173.gif" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: center; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>图源：央视财经</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>就是一个拒绝同框动作，<span>让</span>可口可乐股价<span>一夜</span>暴跌，损失高达人民币255亿元<span>，</span>还带坏了其他球员一起玩梗，挪走可口可乐、喜力啤酒等赞助商产品。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/78608054962fbcfadacfb89.24854875.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/122932134362fbcfae28c071.62173478.gif" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: center; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>图源：央视财经</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><span><span>虽然</span>可口可乐大方回应——“每个人都有权选择自己的饮料偏好”，但</span>后来却被爆“复仇”了。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><span><span>C罗的</span>葡萄牙队被淘汰出欧洲杯，</span><span>可口可乐疑似发布一则短视频：</span><span>一个人装扮成</span>巨型的可口可乐，<span>一言不合地</span>将桌上两个C罗<span>从桌面上移走</span>。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/10717944562fbcfb0b88d70.98967268.gif" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>

<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>这就叫“不是一家人,不进一家门”，可口可乐和旗下的雪碧，实力演绎啥叫大品牌的风范，<span>大尺度营销却不失大度，赢得</span>一定的关注度。 </span></p>

<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>毕竟雪碧今年在大力推广无糖系列，除了【雪碧】无糖原味，推出了无糖纤维+和无糖柠檬薄荷味<span>，还对整体配色进行</span>包装升级，采用黑色商标和瓶盖，市场教育正当时。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>而元气森林也忙着做可乐，刚刚宣布“可乐味苏打气泡水”<span>正式</span>上线。</span></p>
