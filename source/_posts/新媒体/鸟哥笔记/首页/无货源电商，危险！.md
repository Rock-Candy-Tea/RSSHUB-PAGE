<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>最近朋友圈一则聊天对话传得沸沸扬扬，大概意思就是因为搞<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E6%97%A0%E8%B4%A7%E6%BA%90" target="_blank">无货源</a>开店，涉嫌收集、交易公民信息，除了被<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E6%8A%96%E9%9F%B3" target="_blank">抖音</a>冻结账号、资金，还有可能面临被平台起诉坐牢的风险。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/108884536462eb46ee597ee9.15081804.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/104818086262eb46eeda6421.89058926.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/14254434562eb46ef31de30.81425630.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;">图为网传对话</h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E7%94%B5%E5%95%86" target="_blank">电商</a>圈里面有许多人都是搞无货源的，还有各种无货源的上传、拍单软件，如果这个事情成立的话，将会对许多人带来影响。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>所以今天，村长就和大家一起来聊聊无货源开店这件事情，以及最终会怎么样？</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>考虑一部分读者对于无货源不太了解，所以我先把什么是无货源介绍一下。</span></p>
<h1 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); font-size: 20px; color: #212629; text-align: center; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span><strong><span>01、</span></strong></span><span>无货源开店模式</span></h1>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span><strong><span>1、无货源开店</span></strong></span><span></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>所谓的无货源就是商家本人不需要囤货，自己在网上开设一家店铺，然后把别人店里面的商品信息采集到自己的店铺上进行发布。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>等有了订单以后，再去找其他的商家下单，获得订单号及下单的信息后，再填写到自己的店铺上。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>如果用户选择退款、换货或者是有纠纷，B商家再去找A商家进行处理。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>但是对于用户来说，是没有感知的。</span></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><strong><span>2、无货源开店如何赚钱</span></strong></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>无货源开店，主要是赚两份钱。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>a、佣金</span></strong><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><span>比如商家为了推广自己的产品会</span>在<span>淘宝联盟上设置推广佣金，有的是20%、有的是50%。</span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>目前像拼多多、淘宝、东京、视频号上都有官方的选品库。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/172296229962eb46ef7b6471.18103376.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>b、差价</span></strong><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>比如A商家原本设置的价格是29元，B商家会增加五块钱或者10块钱，再进行出售。</span></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><strong><span>3、无货源店群</span></strong></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>做无货源模式想要赚钱，核心的逻辑还是截流。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>想要截流就得店多、商品多，就是用户怎么搜索，怎么比价，都有你的店铺，但是用户并不知道。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>如果一家店一天赚100元，10家店铺就是1000，1000家店铺就是10000元。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>也可以把店群叫做矩阵，不管是淘宝、拼多多还是抖音、跨境电商，很多玩家都是这么玩的。</span></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><strong><span>4、无货源开店的成本</span></strong></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>想要做无货源赚到钱，有两个比较大的成本。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>a、人力成本</span></strong><span></span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>一个人最多可能管理一二十家店铺，不仅是下单售后的问题，还有店铺、商品的优化。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>店铺越多，团队的配置就越高。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>另外稍微聪明且有一些资金的成员，还会离职单干。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>b、资金成本</span></strong><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>开一家店需要账号、营业执照、类目保证金、还有自己下单、S单的垫资。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>所以搞店群再少投入三五万还是要的，稍微入门10万起步，再大的就更多了。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>有些店铺涉嫌违规，一个店铺的保证金就是2000-6000的损失。</span></p>
<h1 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); font-size: 20px; color: #212629; text-align: center; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span><strong><span>02、</span></strong></span><span>无货源的风险在哪里?</span></h1>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>无货源的确有很多人赚到了钱，不管是淘宝、闲鱼、抖音等等，不用自己担心发货，但是无货源的钱也不是那么好赚的。</span></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span><strong><span>1、开店风险</span></strong></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>刚才说了，要做无货源店群，就需要办理多张营业执照。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>有的平台一张营业执照只能开五个店铺，或者一个身份证下只能开设几个店铺。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>这就意味着需要找人来开店铺，<strong>其中就涉嫌买卖他人信息了。</strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>另外这么多的营业执照，每年涉及到的年审之类的，都是风险。</span></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span><strong><span>2、资金风险</span></strong></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>不同类目的保证金是不一样的，不同平台也是。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>有的1000、有的2000、还有的类目是6000，10000，尤其是抖音的审核比较严格。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>如果自己上传商品不规范或者被用户投诉举报，保证金就被扣除了，这个店铺也等于废了。</span></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span><strong><span>3、账号风险</span></strong></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>因为是店群模式，许多店铺的主体都是关联的，稍微严重一点，就是连坐。</span></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span><strong><span>4、交易风险</span></strong></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>抖音为什么会对部分的无货源店群打压，核心原因就是商家把抖音上的用户信息，收集并上传到了拼多多上。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>你自己解释是代下单，但在抖音的理解就是违法收集、出售用户个人信息，毕竟不是你发货、售后。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>因为你的确是把A平台上的用户的信息，发给了B的平台，从而进行了获利。</span></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span><strong><span>5、用户风险</span></strong></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>刚才说的都是从商家角度出发的，另外再从用户的角度出发。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>之前网上就有新闻，用户在京东下单的商品，收到的却是拼多多寄来的货物。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>亦或是在京东看起来很不错的商品，到手之后，差距甚大。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>因为很有可能就是部分不良的店群玩家收了正品的钱，却去拼夕夕某商家那里买了低质量的货物。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>所以，这就导致了用户的整个购物体验不好。</span></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span><strong><span>6、刷单风险</span></strong></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>做无货源的玩家应该都知道，店铺数量是一部分，商品、店铺的权重才是成交的关键。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>如何吸引用户下单，前期还是要做一些准备工作的，比如S单。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>但S单本身就是违法的，所以稍不注意，也会惹上麻烦。</span></p>
<h1 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); font-size: 20px; color: #212629; text-align: center; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span><strong><span>03、</span></strong></span><span>无货源开店模式还能做吗？</span></h1>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span><strong><span>1、抖音、拼多多开始清洗</span></strong></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>其实无货源对任何一个平台都是有伤害的，比如这么多的用户在抖音下单，但是商家一个账号就在拼多多完成了所有的交易。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>对于拼多多来说，当然期望100个真实的用户来下单，而不是一个或者100个机器人在拍单。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>所以从7月份开始，拼多多对诸多订单进行了清洗、自动退款，对于批量拍单的软件限制修改地址、上传数量等操作。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/161001896062eb46efd9b597.28324709.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>而抖音方面一方面针对大的商家进行封店、冻结、起诉等处理，另外也发布了关于《“商家—无货源店铺”细则》，对商品上传数量、违规行为进行限制、扣分、冻结等操作。</span></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span><strong><span>2、无货源模式不能做了吗？</span></strong></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>首先确定的第一点，从A平台把信息发布到B平台的这种模式，后续监管的力度会很大。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>尤其是抖音、天猫开店，然后去拼多多下单这种行为，还是要慎重。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>但是在淘宝、抖音、拼多多平台内进行分销带货还是可以的 ，这其实也是另外一种无货源的带货模式。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>比如在抖音里面有精选联盟，个人可以申请成为带货达人，可以自己去选择相应的佣金产品进行带货。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>这时候不管你是做好物推荐、探店、测评，还是做居家、美食矩阵号，书单号等等，都是符合要求的。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><span>顺便这里再强调一下，打着做无货源代理、培训等相关的服务也要注意了。</span></span><span><span></span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/87222502662eb46f050a162.98021811.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>最近湖北武汉警方就破获了一起无货源店铺代运营的骗局，号称缴纳4900元至9900元不等的费用后，可帮助无货源打造运营网络店铺，每月收益可轻松过万。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>但在经民警侦查发现，该公司所谓“运营”的数千家网店中，没有一个成功的案例。在受害人发现被骗要求退款时，客服人员采取拖延、屏蔽、拉黑等方式拒绝退款。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>最终警方抓获犯罪嫌疑人 98 ，扣押作案手机280余部，电脑105台，涉案金额高达1000余万元</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>所以说，各位做无货源培训的老师、待运营机构、以及无货源相关的软件，都要仔细排查和自身，看看是否有问题。</span></p>
