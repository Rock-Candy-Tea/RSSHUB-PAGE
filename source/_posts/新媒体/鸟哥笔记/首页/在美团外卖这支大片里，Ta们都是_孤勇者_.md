<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>一首《孤勇者》，让陈奕迅变身儿歌大王。“谁说站在光里的才算英雄”这句歌词更成为了“时代金句”，在互联网随处可见它的身影。<br></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>之所以能火爆，除了旋律抓耳之外，<strong>最重要的是它唱出了普通人为生活拼命的样子，抚慰着每一个在黑夜中奋斗的平凡人。</strong>无论是哪个领域的工作者，都有资格被称为“孤勇者”。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>有这么一群人，他们是大家眼中的“小蜜蜂”，是我们身边的平凡英雄，电动车是他的坐骑，他们不分昼夜在城市里穿行，无暇欣赏城市的繁华，只想尽快顺利送达，<strong>他们是平凡的骑士，也是暗夜孤勇者</strong>。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>最近，<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E7%BE%8E%E5%9B%A2%E5%A4%96%E5%8D%96" target="_blank">美团外卖</a>在717骑士节之际，就拍了一支外卖小哥<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/TVC" target="_blank">TVC</a>，讲述4个平凡的其实那不平凡的伟大壮举。</span><span> </span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>原来许多份外卖的背后，都蕴含着一个和“光”有关的故事，这些追光的骑手是每一个城市的守护者，送来城市的温暖。<br></span></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 18px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;">01</h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>他们是普通的骑手</span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>也是最伟大的英雄</span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>美团外卖这支视频分为4个篇章，每个篇章讲述一名外卖员的故事。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>他们心中有梦，身上有光，生有热烈，藏于市巷。</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>第一章 「刹」</span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/209542184962da69db551424.46445702.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>骑手罗虫董在送买卖的途中，目睹了一位年轻妈妈抛下刚出生的儿子准备轻生的一幕。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/112799284262da69dd5795d7.26687198.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>于是他赶紧停车把年轻妈妈拉了下来，直到交警过来才离开去送单。他的举动不仅救了这位年轻妈妈，也让刚出生的宝宝避免了失去母亲。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/25128044662da69ddeee7b9.72668965.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>第二章 「赴」</span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/131415355562da69deb6ff38.12708107.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>早上八点多骑手张献龙在店里等餐的时候，看到隔壁店里冒烟，他拿着灭火器冲进去，灭完火却发现头发和眉毛都被火撩了，但是顾客已经在催单，他顾不了形象只能赶紧送餐去了。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/38155373362da69df528ef4.28566289.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span> </span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>尽管并非自己职责所在，但</span><span>他依然选择义无反顾向前冲。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>第三章 「默」</span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/199161320362da69e01a2df0.28861671.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>之所以取名叫「默」，是因为外卖小哥唐箐存在听力障碍和语言障碍，于是采访全程用文字完成。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/130799475762da69e11c5488.71868060.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>他讲述自己到店取餐的时候，看到一个女孩倒在地上，于是赶紧去扶，女孩当时翻白眼还抽搐，他看到不对就出去喊人帮忙。隔壁邻居看不懂他在比划什么，还是被他拉进了店里，然后关掉了煤气。当他们发现了倒在地上的店主，立即拨打了120。看着他们上了救护车，他就离开了。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/29586400662da69e20115f3.28125165.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>后来店主写道：“谢谢外卖小哥救了我”，一次善举，影响了别人一生。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/191789645562da69e26b4fa8.75884706.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>第四章 「追」</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/27269327262da69e2c93f66.02001775.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>第四个故事里的骑手沈知音是一位女生，当时她遇到一个女生追劫匪，于是掉转头就追了上去，边追边喊，两个外卖员看到立马去帮她，三个人将小偷堵在巷子里成功抓住了他，甚至还劝小偷以后改邪归正。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/167194416762da69e3b710d6.83349734.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>以上四个骑手是众多骑手的缩影，<strong>他们不仅是外卖员，更在生活中扮演着多个角色。</strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>看完这支短片会发现，原来他们在每天频繁送外卖的过程中，也会遇到形形色色的人和事，当面对一些特殊情况时，他们会奋不顾身地冲到前面。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在现实中，<strong>没有从天而降的英雄，只有挺身而出的平凡人</strong>。骑手们有的只是最朴素的勇气和责任感，当遇到需要帮助的人，他们就会主动站出来，成为那束光。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>美团外卖作为全国领先的外卖平台，打造这样一支片子的意图也不难揣测，<strong>通过讲好骑手的故事，实现品牌心智占位，提升用户对品牌的认知与好感。</strong></span></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 18px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;">02</h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>只要去追光 </span></strong></span><strong><span>身上就有光</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>看完这支片子，不难发现，4个篇章的故事画面是<strong>由一张张定格照片组成，采用了黑白色调进行处理，更加具有故事感。</strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/43781842462da69e4a6b7d0.88124605.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>而当骑手救完人之后，画面中出现一抹亮眼的黄色，也是美团品牌色。</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>借助这样的创意表现形式，不仅能强化品牌视觉符号，更能凸显骑手<strong>「去追光，就会成为光」</strong>的广告主题。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/37962532362da69e5523664.10433214.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>最后，美团外卖引出slogan「不是每个人要成为那最亮眼的光，但只要你去追光，身上就会有光 」。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/85184919462da69e5bbbad5.05941816.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>以光寓意骑士，更加凸显骑手身上的伟大的闪光点。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>美团外卖强化骑士身上的色彩，通过骑手救人的故事建立品牌与消费者之间的情感纽带。</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>现如今，外卖小哥已经成为一个群体，一个符号，与我们每个人的生活密切相关。他们目睹了在这座城市奋斗的人们的故事，他们参与了我们生活，正是因为有这些骑手，才让人们的美好生活更便利。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在网上有一个说法，<strong>“请给外卖小哥一些理解和尊重，因为你永远不知道路上给你送餐的时候他经历了什么”</strong>，生活比你想象的难得多，多一份理解，也许世界就多一丝温暖。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>比如当你抱怨在下雨天外卖超时的时候，你可能不知道他们冒着大雨给你送外卖有多困难。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>就像视频中的四位骑手，他们分别遇到了<strong>母亲跳江、煤气中毒、店铺着火，小偷抢劫</strong>等事件，尽管他们只是一名骑手，但他们纷纷选择在危急时刻挺身而出，但即使冒险救了人，也要马不停蹄去送餐，因为怕顾客等太久。<br></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>每一个骑手都在用自己的微小力量为人民服务。一个订单的完成，就是一次‘使命必达’。”</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在视频中，美团骑手就像一个个无名英雄，不仅准时送餐，还为社会做了很多贡献。他们舍己救人，扮演着消防员、交警等多样化的角色。成为“城市守护者”。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/14354821462da69e62dc1a5.86386956.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>美团外卖选取这四个外卖员发光的故事，<strong>通过外卖小哥本职工作外的举动，深化美团「美好生活小帮手」的品牌心智，使得品牌的个性情感化色彩开始鲜明起来。</strong></span></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 18px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;">03</h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>致敬先锋骑手</span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>美团举办表彰活动</span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>值得一提的是，这支视频根据真实故事改编，在视频结尾，美团放出了故事原型，由他们本人讲述这些感人的事迹。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>最让人动容的是，视频结尾被救的店主亲自感谢救他的外卖小哥唐箐，这份最真挚的感谢，让无数人感同身受，也让无数人动容。</span></p>
