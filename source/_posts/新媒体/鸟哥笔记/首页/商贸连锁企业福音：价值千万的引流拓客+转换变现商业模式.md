<p class="p" style="text-indent: 0pt; padding: 0pt; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; background-color: #ffffff; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" align="justify"><strong><span class="15" style="font-family: Arial; color: #5d5d5d; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-size: 12pt; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;"><font face="helvetica">而，今年很多企业家朋友找到微三云小胡，阐述多的几个问题痛点：</font></span></strong><span style="font-family: helvetica; color: #5d5d5d; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-size: 12pt;"></span></p>
<p class="p" style="text-indent: 0pt; padding: 0pt; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; background-color: #ffffff; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" align="justify"><span style="font-family: Arial; color: #5d5d5d; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-size: 12pt; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">1<font face="helvetica">、受电商冲击，客流量下滑严重；</font></span><span style="font-family: helvetica; color: #5d5d5d; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-size: 12pt;"></span></p>
<p class="p" style="text-indent: 0pt; padding: 0pt; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; background-color: #ffffff; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" align="justify"><span style="font-family: Arial; color: #5d5d5d; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-size: 12pt; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">2<font face="helvetica">、单店标准化，导致运营不到位；</font></span><span style="font-family: helvetica; color: #5d5d5d; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-size: 12pt;"></span></p>
<p class="p" style="text-indent: 0pt; padding: 0pt; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; background-color: #ffffff; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" align="justify"><span style="font-family: Arial; color: #5d5d5d; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-size: 12pt; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">3<font face="helvetica">、产品更新迭代慢；</font></span><span style="font-family: helvetica; color: #5d5d5d; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-size: 12pt;"></span></p>
<p class="p" style="text-indent: 0pt; padding: 0pt; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; background-color: #ffffff; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" align="justify"><span style="font-family: Arial; color: #5d5d5d; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-size: 12pt; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">4<font face="helvetica">、客户粘性不足，缺乏好的商业营销手段；</font></span><span style="font-family: helvetica; color: #5d5d5d; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-size: 12pt;"></span></p>
<p class="p" style="text-indent: 0pt; padding: 0pt; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; background-color: #ffffff; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" align="justify"><span style="font-family: helvetica; color: #5d5d5d; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-size: 12pt; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;"><font face="helvetica">微三云小胡给河北石家庄段总，设计的顶层<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E5%95%86%E4%B8%9A%E6%A8%A1%E5%BC%8F" target="_blank">商业模式</a>规划（</font>F2B2C）架构，在这里拿出来分享给大家，用数字化新商业模式，重构企业的<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E7%9B%88%E5%88%A9%E6%A8%A1%E5%BC%8F" target="_blank">盈利模式</a>、服务模式、<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E7%BB%84%E7%BB%87%E6%A8%A1%E5%BC%8F" target="_blank">组织模式</a>，打造一套新的商业生态，具体详情如下：</span><span style="font-family: helvetica; color: #5d5d5d; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-size: 12pt;"></span></p>
<p class="p" style="text-indent: 0pt; padding: 0pt; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; background-color: #ffffff; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" align="justify"><img src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/91a70aa0f80e7c392d366c630b7c7ee0.png" alt="商贸连锁企业福音：价值千万的引流拓客+转换变现商业模式" title="商贸连锁企业福音：价值千万的引流拓客+转换变现商业模式" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span style="font-family: helvetica; color: #5d5d5d; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-size: 12pt;"> </span></p>
<h2 style="text-indent: 0pt; padding: 0pt 0pt 0pt 12px; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-color: #ffffff; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat no-repeat;"><strong><span class="15" style="font-family: helvetica; color: #5d5d5d; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-size: 12pt; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;"><font face="helvetica">一、商业模式创新</font></span></strong><span style="font-family: helvetica; color: #5d5d5d; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-size: 12pt;"></span></h2>
<p class="p" style="text-indent: 0pt; padding: 0pt; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; background-color: #ffffff; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" align="justify"><span style="font-family: helvetica; color: #5d5d5d; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-size: 12pt; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;"><font face="helvetica">解析：打造全链路的商业生态，把线上、线下、代理、渠道、员工，全部转换为自己的一个兵，人人皆兵，口碑传播。通过拼团、秒杀、竞拍、优惠券等等，设置低价、折扣、优惠、免费、赚钱，快速裂变。</font></span><span style="font-family: helvetica; color: #5d5d5d; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-size: 12pt;"></span></p>
<p class="p" style="text-indent: 0pt; padding: 0pt; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; background-color: #ffffff; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" align="justify"><strong><span class="15" style="font-family: helvetica; color: #5d5d5d; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-size: 12pt; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;"><font face="helvetica">比如：全民拼购，市场原价</font>150元，拼团价只需100元，10人成团，3人中奖，7人不中奖</span></strong><span style="font-family: helvetica; color: #5d5d5d; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-size: 12pt;"></span></p>
<p class="p" style="text-indent: 0pt; padding: 0pt; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; background-color: #ffffff; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" align="justify"><span style="font-family: helvetica; color: #5d5d5d; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-size: 12pt; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">1、拼中：①获得100元超值产品、②获得购物基金100元、③获得200平台积分、④可获得本月分红池的收益。</span><span style="font-family: helvetica; color: #5d5d5d; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-size: 12pt;"></span></p>
<p class="p" style="text-indent: 0pt; padding: 0pt; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; background-color: #ffffff; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" align="justify"><span style="font-family: helvetica; color: #5d5d5d; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-size: 12pt; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">2、拼不中：①用户投入本金退回、②获得红包补贴5元。</span><span style="font-family: helvetica; color: #5d5d5d; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-size: 12pt;"></span></p>
<p class="p" style="text-indent: 0pt; padding: 0pt; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; background-color: #ffffff; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" align="justify"><span style="font-family: helvetica; color: #5d5d5d; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-size: 12pt; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;"><font face="helvetica">疑问解析：</font></span><span style="font-family: helvetica; color: #5d5d5d; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-size: 12pt;"></span></p>
<p class="p" style="text-indent: 0pt; padding: 0pt; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; background-color: #ffffff; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" align="justify"><span style="font-family: helvetica; color: #5d5d5d; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-size: 12pt; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;"><font face="helvetica">购物基金作用：</font>①邀请好友参团，无论好友中奖与不中奖，都能把购物基金转为零钱体现；②自己参团复购，也可以转化一部分零钱。③用户每天看广告，每天可以静态释放千分之五，转化为零钱。</span><span style="font-family: helvetica; color: #5d5d5d; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-size: 12pt;"></span></p>
<p class="p" style="text-indent: 0pt; padding: 0pt; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; background-color: #ffffff; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" align="justify"><img src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/bac1ab2bf2200d07a0453d99b35553e1.png" alt="商贸连锁企业福音：价值千万的引流拓客+转换变现商业模式" title="商贸连锁企业福音：价值千万的引流拓客+转换变现商业模式" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span style="font-family: helvetica; color: #5d5d5d; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-size: 12pt;"> </span></p>
<h2 style="text-indent: 0pt; padding: 0pt 0pt 0pt 12px; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-color: #ffffff; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat no-repeat;"><strong><span class="15" style="font-family: helvetica; color: #5d5d5d; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-size: 12pt; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;"><font face="helvetica">二、盈利模式创新</font></span></strong><span style="font-family: helvetica; color: #5d5d5d; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-size: 12pt;"></span></h2>
<p class="p" style="text-indent: 0pt; padding: 0pt; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; background-color: #ffffff; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" align="justify"><span style="font-family: helvetica; color: #5d5d5d; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-size: 12pt; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;"><font face="helvetica">改变固有的商业模式，盈利模式不再是通过【产品】，而是通过流量，做商业变现，对接百业【吃、喝、住、行、穿、用】，不在局限一个品类，而是通过这种方式，锁定本土当地的所有地域流量。</font></span><span style="font-family: helvetica; color: #5d5d5d; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-size: 12pt;"></span></p>
<p class="p" style="text-indent: 0pt; padding: 0pt; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; background-color: #ffffff; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" align="justify"><span style="font-family: helvetica; color: #5d5d5d; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-size: 12pt; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;"><font face="helvetica">例如：拼团赠送的积分，可以充当现金，在百业联盟中，进行消费抵扣，原价</font>80元的，可以使用50积分，抵扣50元，用户只需要支付30元现金，就可以享受80元同等的服务。</span><span style="font-family: helvetica; color: #5d5d5d; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-size: 12pt;"></span></p>
<p class="p" style="text-indent: 0pt; padding: 0pt; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; background-color: #ffffff; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" align="justify"><img src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/3559cebef7d88eb4bec45fa85d650d4d.png" alt="商贸连锁企业福音：价值千万的引流拓客+转换变现商业模式" title="商贸连锁企业福音：价值千万的引流拓客+转换变现商业模式" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span style="font-family: helvetica; color: #5d5d5d; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-size: 12pt;"> </span></p>

<p class="p" style="text-indent: 0pt; padding: 0pt; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; background-color: #ffffff; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" align="justify"><span style="font-family: helvetica; color: #5d5d5d; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-size: 12pt; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">①根据市场潮流的新颖模式 ，放弃博弈思维，厂商要深度合作，有全局思维，要足够开发；</span><span style="font-family: helvetica; color: #5d5d5d; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-size: 12pt;"></span></p>
<p class="p" style="text-indent: 0pt; padding: 0pt; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; background-color: #ffffff; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" align="justify"><span style="font-family: helvetica; color: #5d5d5d; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-size: 12pt; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">②放弃低毛利跑量模式，逐步的转向高附加值，高毛利模式，降低渠道搭建费用，转向消费者互动沟通；</span><span style="font-family: helvetica; color: #5d5d5d; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-size: 12pt;"></span></p>
