<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>当很多品牌在为取悦年轻人绞尽脑汁的时候，<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E9%B8%BF%E6%98%9F%E5%B0%94%E5%85%8B" target="_blank">鸿星尔克</a>直接让Z时代主场了。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>当行业还在感叹“野性消费”终归平淡，留客还要靠产品体验的时候，鸿星尔克直接在中华世纪坛丢下一个重磅炸弹。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>这一次，不再是社会性新闻，而是大家翘首以盼的时装大秀。它是鸿星尔克晋级运动潮牌讲述的中国故事，是传统文化与当代审美融合的时尚盛宴，更是鸿星尔克科技新国货的炫酷蜕变，锋芒尽显。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/6251482662db2e883e36e5.24710351.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></strong></span></p>
<h1 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); font-size: 20px; color: #212629; text-align: center; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span><strong><span>星创 中国鸿，</span></strong></span><span><strong><span>在中华世纪坛的第一场人生大秀</span></strong></span><span></span></h1>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>2022年9月9日，鸿星尔克在中华世纪坛发布了「星创•中国鸿」主题大秀，鸿星尔克国货共创大师/著名服装设计师获得者计文波、鸿星尔克国货共创官马拉松运动员李芷萱、国货共创官南京Hero久竞战队队长无畏等明星达人莅临现场，与鸿星尔克品牌方以及官方直播间500多万观众一起，见证了「星创•中国鸿」的惊艳登场。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/263711704631e34e2e3d210.65729090.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>来到中华世纪坛不是巧合，其新世纪复兴中华民族的号召力，亦是鸿星尔克“星创”IP复兴科技新国货的心之所向。「星创•中国鸿」的主题，正是寓意着</span><strong><span>中国00后新一代设计力量的崛起，正以鸿鹄高飞、一举千里之势，开启着新国货的未来！</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>中国传统文化的融入是这场大秀可预见的惊喜。以“中”字为造型的秀场动线设计，见证着四大篇章的国潮时尚足迹，也表达了鸿星尔克的中国心。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/373463953631e34e3360b50.51718804.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>汉字“鸿”的解构图案散落于背景各处，诠释着俊彩星驰的星创格局。发布会现场，鸿星尔克正式宣布签约中国服装设计师协会副主席计文波先生为国货共创大师，开启鸿星尔克星创系列的大师时代。</span><span></span></p>

<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>“换了设计师的鸿星尔克，格局终于打开了”</span></strong></span></p>

<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>“服装很牛”</span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>“鸿星尔克变成了我买不起的样子”</span></strong></span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>网友把这次的喜欢和惊叹全部给到了鸿星尔克本克。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>众多赞誉背后，是鸿星尔克破茧成蝶的蜕变与新生，在星创“人生第一场大秀”的秀场中诠释的淋漓尽致。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/176078547762db2e8f874706.61176433.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></strong></span></p>
<h1 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); font-size: 20px; color: #212629; text-align: center; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span><strong><span>传统文化碰撞潮流语言，</span></strong></span><span><strong><span>鸿星破晓，鸿雁传书，惊鸿而出，鸿耀中国</span></strong></span><span></span></h1>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>「星创•中国鸿」系列立足于鸿星尔克专业运动科技的基础之上，注入中国传统文化，以新一代青年的创新力与独特审美共创呈现。通过系列化的主题服装，呈现科技新国货的未来潮流。</span><span></span></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><strong><span>鸿星破晓，溯源而来</span></strong><span></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>鸿星破晓，以大师级新作拉开星创发布大秀的序幕。由中国服装设计师协会副主席/鸿星尔克共创大师计文波亲自操刀设计的“源”系列，惊艳开场。扑面而来的高级感，刷新了鸿星尔克的产品在消费者心目中的形象。</span></p>

<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>当《我爱你中国》以浑厚饱满的音色响彻秀场，鸿耀中国开启了温暖人心的篇章，模特身着搭载燚能科技和暖绒科技材质的冬季保暖服饰步入秀场。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/134980375631e34e74fa9e2.05831930.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/1942500457631e34e7994508.79358463.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>五星、中国字样以及中国红为主要元素呈现，阐述鸿星尔克回归本心的家国情怀，以此表达诚挚的爱国之情。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/568255440631e34e7d70692.41995995.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>

<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/133117990631e34e8130262.30422645.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/995892623631e34e8433a57.22078971.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span></span></p>

<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>过去，网友经常调侃鸿星尔克是慈善家，品牌大，质量好，但是设计差强人意。然而事实并非全然如此，鸿星尔克早有爆款产品呈现。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>鸿星尔克的星星T恤，闪亮的中国红色星星印花，简单设计自带满满活力，上身秒变国货酷盖。独特的国潮属性颇受喜爱，成为明星达人分享的热销单品。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/628104393631e34e86df036.08524835.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span><span>●</span>现场无畏身穿鸿星尔克星星T恤走秀</span><span></span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>大秀现场，人气歌手、演员甘望星、著名演员代斯、中国马拉松国家队队员李芷萱所穿的星星系列风衣，也是走俏小红书的时尚穿搭爆款。因其潮感工装设计、个性国风色彩而席卷达人衣橱。</span><span></span></p>

<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>鸿星尔克青年共创设计大赛面向Z时代发出邀请。历时大半年，来自不同院校的新鲜血液——年轻的设计师带着他们的获奖作品，走上了人生第一场大秀——</span><strong><span>星芒秀的舞台，向世界展示中华民族生生不息的创造力。</span></strong><span></span></p>
