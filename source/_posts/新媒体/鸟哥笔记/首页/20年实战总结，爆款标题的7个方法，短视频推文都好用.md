                        <p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">甲方、乙方、媒体、<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E8%87%AA%E5%AA%92%E4%BD%93" target="_blank">自媒体</a>、短视频、公众号……只要是做传播，就有个共同的高频痛点，不知道怎么<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E8%B5%B7%E6%A0%87%E9%A2%98" target="_blank">起标题</a>。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">之所以对标题这么看重，其实源于大家的共识：好标题，确实能成百上千倍的吸引关注。很多自媒体大V都曾强调，要将50％的精力放在起标题上。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">但起标题这事儿，不是光熬夜就行的。一个好标题的背后，是一整套心理学、社会学、传播学、政治经济学……（巴拉巴拉）的总和。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">别划走！其实也没那么难。不要自己造轮子，站在巨人肩膀上。最近看了前奥美首席文案林桂枝的《秒赞》，在她基础上总结了7个简单上手、好记好用的方法，起标题时直接用就完事了。</p>
<h2 id="toc-1" style="font-size: 18px; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-position: 0px 5px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; line-height: 30px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; background-size: 8px 20px; padding-left: 12px;">一、你的标题必须要有针对性</h2>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">起标题得有一个总原则，就是标题必须与你想影响的人相关，一定要有针对性！</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">针对、对准、指向某一目标——就像一枚狙击子弹（当然是糖衣炮弹），能精准的射入对方的内心。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">想通过标题击中对方，你就必须知道对方内心渴求什么，你可以如何满足他的渴求，他需要解决什么问题，你可以为他提供什么方法。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">这个原则，可以说是颠扑不破的真理。无论有多少平台，会出多少新的应用，真正的终极媒体只有一个，就是人的内心。能击一下中对方内心，就能赢得所有平台的流量。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">所以，下面的这7个爆款标题的方法，都是建立在“你能足够了解你想要影响的人”之上。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">如果你对这点还不清楚，那请先去接近和了解你的传播对象。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">务必请首先思考：</p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">为什么他对你写的这篇文案会感兴趣？</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">你能赋予他什么？你能提供什么利益点，让他感到有所收获？</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">你的标题能怎样让他高兴，温暖，惊喜，恐惧，感到迫切，觉得安全？</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">你牵动了他心中的什么情感，让他感到非看不可？</span></p>
<h2 id="toc-2" style="font-size: 18px; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-position: 0px 5px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; line-height: 30px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; background-size: 8px 20px; padding-left: 12px;">二、创作爆款标题的7个方法</h2>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; line-height: inherit; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-image: none; background-position: 0px 6px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><strong>1. 特写镜头</strong></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">标题一定要直接针对目标客户，让他们看到后，第一时间觉得是和自己相关。</p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px;">a.女生显高的穿衣效果</p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px;">b.150~165的女生怎样变高挑？</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">大部分女生其实都在150~165这个区间，但不明确说出来，有时会太宽泛，不容易吸引关注。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">就像拍照片，取景太远，没法看清楚人群的特征，无法让对方看清楚与他们的相关性，所以要给特写镜头。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">那特写镜头给哪里呢？一方面是给到客户人群的特点，比如150<span style="font-weight: bold;">~</span>165的女生，一方面，可以给到客户人群的利益点、痛点。注意，这里是说客户的利益，而不是产品的性能。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">那比如你想推销一款洗衣粉:</p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px;">a.有效清除顽固污渍</p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px;">b.宝宝疯玩吧，现在多脏都不怕！</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">b标题是在说客户人群的利益点，而a标题说的是产品性能，哪个更能直接打动客户？</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">把你要推广的内容，带入到客户人群的使用场景中，从消费习惯上来找利益，找到后在标题里给特写镜头。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; line-height: inherit; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-image: none; background-position: 0px 6px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><strong>2. 超高回报</strong></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">人性是贪婪的、懒惰的、焦虑的、急切的，所以在标题里满足人性，让对方感到超高回报，少量投入便可收获可观效果，很难不上钩。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">当然不是教你骗人，这个标题适合确实能提供相应效果的内容，否则就是pianpao。</p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px;">弹尤克里里，从0基础到出道只需21天！</p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px;">这瓶大地鱼粉，放一点，鲜10倍</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">把你能给对方带来什么样的超高回报，直接写在标题上，就这么简单。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">当然，为了加强信任，你可以用下面这个句式，明确自己的承诺，就像婚礼上的宣誓：</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">超高回报+明确承诺</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">句式：你给我+我给你</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px;">你给我一个家，我陪你白头到老</p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px;">给我21天，让你0基础尤克里里出道！</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; line-height: inherit; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-image: none; background-position: 0px 6px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><strong>3. 客观来说</strong></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">人人都想听客观意见，客观的意见让事情变得更可信。那在标题里，我们就来强化一下，让自己想表达的东西，穿上客观的马甲。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">句式：产品+众人（都）+我+利益</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px;">用完这个，大家都说我的眼睛好有神！</p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px;">穿这个打底裤，家里人都说我瘦了！</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">每一个主观的人都希望用客观的方法来证明自己主观的正确。如果你想让他下决心，就把“大家”搬出来。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">喜欢听客观的意见，本质上就是从众心理。别人都这样，所以没啥不放心的。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">句式：100％+客户群体+产品</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px;">宿舍100%女生都喜欢的小摆设</p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px;">100%男生都喜欢看的一本书</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; line-height: inherit; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-image: none; background-position: 0px 6px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><strong>4. 心里有数</strong></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">数字这个东西很奇妙，可能最早就是用来计数的吧，所以人类遇到数字，本能的就多看两眼，或许是怕自己工资发少了的原因吧^_^</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">在传播领域，数字是最重要的工具。尤其是7这个数字，特别招人关注。也许是因为我们习惯了一周7天的生活吧。</p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px;">《高效能人士的7个习惯》</p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px;">《7天学会写动漫脚本》</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">数字，还能让事情变得更清晰和具体，增加获得感。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">比如，《高效能人士的7个习惯》这本畅销书，明确说明有7个习惯，让读者感到只需要养成7个习惯便能收获高效能。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; line-height: inherit; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-image: none; background-position: 0px 6px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><strong>5. 秘密在哪</strong></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">没有人不喜欢听秘密，所以没有人不喜欢看揭秘式标题。揭开秘密，满足好奇心。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">你觉得没有秘密，但可能别人并不知道，所以，大胆用揭秘、解密、奥秘、秘密这些词。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">句式：利益+解密</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px;">传说这家火锅香得让人上瘾，到底有啥秘诀？</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">你越说这是极少数人才能知道的秘密，就会有越多的人急着希望成为极少数人之一。最后，当全世界都知道这个秘密时，你的传播目的便达到了。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">句式：利益+少数人才知道+秘密</span></p>

<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">要让看你标题的人，觉得是和你在1对1聊天，有种被亲宠的感觉。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">想象着，她坐在你的对面，在和你聊天。而我们，要当她的知音。与对方靠近，将话吹进他的耳根。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">比如，用：</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">会不会觉得自己的腰有点不够细呢？</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">就比用“瑜伽教程”或“修腰有氧运动”作为标题更亲切。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">悄悄话：千万不要告诉别人，围巾这样结才好看</span></p>

<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; line-height: inherit; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-image: none; background-position: 0px 6px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><strong>7. 提个问题</strong></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">提问能引发对方思考，引起注意。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">书中总结了一些提问式标题的模板：</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">为什么+问题（痛点/爽点）</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px;">为什么你的显示屏不清晰？</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">A为什么比 B更（厉害/重要……）？</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px;">升级后的它为什么比过去更出色？</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">是否+问题</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px;">你是否为正在显示屏画质持续变差烦恼？</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">想不想+客户期待</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px;">想不想让画质变好？</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="font-weight: bold;">如何/怎样+客户期待</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px;">怎样让自己秒变白皙？</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">在“如何”之后加上“轻松”“方便”“容易”“快捷”“轻易”“简单”“3分钟”等词，便能令写标题变得更容易，加上“彻底”“绝对”“透顶”等词，更能加强效果。</p>

<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">就是找一些更知名的事物放入标题里，来吸引大家的关注。当然，一定要找那些正面的东西，才能让我们收获流量的同时，不至于损失品牌。</p>

<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; line-height: inherit; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-image: none; background-position: 0px 6px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><strong>10. 激发行动</strong></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">在标题中把事情的门槛降低，告诉对方事情没有想象中的困难，让观众感到小小个体可以成就非凡，这样能激发他的行动。</p>
