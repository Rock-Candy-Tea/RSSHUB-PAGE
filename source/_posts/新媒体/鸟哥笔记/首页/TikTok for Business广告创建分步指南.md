<p style="box-sizing: content-box; -webkit-tap-highlight-color: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0); margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; word-break: break-word; line-height: inherit; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit;"><img style="box-sizing: content-box; -webkit-tap-highlight-color: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0); transform: translateZ(0px); max-width: 100%; display: block; margin: 0px auto;" src="https://static.52by.com/yue/article/685_166148323073658473996.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>

<p style="box-sizing: content-box; -webkit-tap-highlight-color: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0); margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; word-break: break-word; line-height: inherit; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="box-sizing: content-box; -webkit-tap-highlight-color: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0);">创建广告之前，首先</span><span style="box-sizing: content-box; -webkit-tap-highlight-color: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0);">需要</span><span style="box-sizing: content-box; -webkit-tap-highlight-color: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0);">考虑</span><span style="box-sizing: content-box; -webkit-tap-highlight-color: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0);">的是推广目标，推广目标分为品牌认知、受众意向和转化量三类，广告主需要先确定希望受众看到你的广告后采取的动作，再选择广告目标。</span></p>
