<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: 16px; line-height: inherit; vertical-align: baseline; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, 'Microsoft YaHei', 微软雅黑, STHeitiSC-Light, simsun, 宋体, 'WenQuanYi Zen Hei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; word-break: break-word; overflow-wrap: break-word; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">种种环境非常有利于<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E7%A7%BB%E5%8A%A8%E5%BA%94%E7%94%A8" target="_blank">移动应用</a>程序的使用，所以今天，我们就来盘点一下巴西人最常用的手机APP，从中探寻巴西移动应用出海的机遇与挑战。</p>
<h2 style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: 18px; line-height: 30px; vertical-align: baseline; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, 'Microsoft YaHei', 微软雅黑, STHeitiSC-Light, simsun, 宋体, 'WenQuanYi Zen Hei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; word-break: break-word; color: #212629; position: relative; text-align: left; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-weight: 600; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;">移动搜索</h2>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: 16px; line-height: inherit; vertical-align: baseline; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, 'Microsoft YaHei', 微软雅黑, STHeitiSC-Light, simsun, 宋体, 'WenQuanYi Zen Hei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; word-break: break-word; overflow-wrap: break-word; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">谷歌是巴西人使用最多的搜索引擎，无论是个人使用还是工作，查询信息或者编辑共享文档组织在线会议，大多数巴西人还是会首选谷歌。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: 16px; line-height: inherit; vertical-align: baseline; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, 'Microsoft YaHei', 微软雅黑, STHeitiSC-Light, simsun, 宋体, 'WenQuanYi Zen Hei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; word-break: break-word; overflow-wrap: break-word; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">除了谷歌之外，当地用户还会使用一些较小型的本土搜索引擎读物，例如exploora、uol 、aonde、caribseek、globe等，这些搜索引擎可以通过.com域名访问。</p>
<h2 style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: 18px; line-height: 30px; vertical-align: baseline; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, 'Microsoft YaHei', 微软雅黑, STHeitiSC-Light, simsun, 宋体, 'WenQuanYi Zen Hei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; word-break: break-word; color: #212629; position: relative; text-align: left; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-weight: 600; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;">社交通讯</h2>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: 16px; line-height: inherit; vertical-align: baseline; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, 'Microsoft YaHei', 微软雅黑, STHeitiSC-Light, simsun, 宋体, 'WenQuanYi Zen Hei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; word-break: break-word; overflow-wrap: break-word; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">热情豪放的巴西，为社交产品的生根发芽带来了机会和潜力。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: 16px; line-height: inherit; vertical-align: baseline; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, 'Microsoft YaHei', 微软雅黑, STHeitiSC-Light, simsun, 宋体, 'WenQuanYi Zen Hei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; word-break: break-word; overflow-wrap: break-word; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">数据显示，巴西消费者是世界上最热衷社交媒体的消费群体之一，当地用户平均每天花在社交媒体上的时间为 3 小时 43 分钟，消费内容具有较高的转化销售潜力。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: 16px; line-height: inherit; vertical-align: baseline; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, 'Microsoft YaHei', 微软雅黑, STHeitiSC-Light, simsun, 宋体, 'WenQuanYi Zen Hei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; word-break: break-word; overflow-wrap: break-word; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: 600; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; vertical-align: baseline; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; word-break: break-word;">那么，巴西人活跃在哪些社交平台上呢？</span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: 16px; line-height: inherit; vertical-align: baseline; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, 'Microsoft YaHei', 微软雅黑, STHeitiSC-Light, simsun, 宋体, 'WenQuanYi Zen Hei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; word-break: break-word; overflow-wrap: break-word; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">与国内国内本土社交APP独占鳌头不同，巴西的社交媒体市场长期被来自美国的的meta系应用占领。meta旗下的 Facebook 以及 WhatsApp是巴西智能手机用户中最受欢迎的移动应用程序。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: 16px; line-height: inherit; vertical-align: baseline; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, 'Microsoft YaHei', 微软雅黑, STHeitiSC-Light, simsun, 宋体, 'WenQuanYi Zen Hei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; word-break: break-word; overflow-wrap: break-word; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">特别是Whatsapp，巴西互联网用户在日常交流中严重依赖这个与微信极为相似的通讯APP，据巴西当地媒体报道，<span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: 600; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; vertical-align: baseline; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; word-break: break-word;">WhatsApp在巴西当地互联网用户用户中的市场占有率早已超过90%</span>，用户早已超过1亿人。要知道，巴西的总人口也不过2亿左右。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: 16px; line-height: inherit; vertical-align: baseline; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, 'Microsoft YaHei', 微软雅黑, STHeitiSC-Light, simsun, 宋体, 'WenQuanYi Zen Hei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; word-break: break-word; overflow-wrap: break-word; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">值得注意的是，随着社交电商在巴西消费者中的迅速普及，Whatsapp还成为与品牌触达客户，进行各种产品营销活动的主要渠道，OpinionBox与MobileTime网站的一项调查显示，80%的WhatsApp用户使用信使与品牌卖家沟通，57%的用户选择在WhatsApp上购物。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: 16px; line-height: inherit; vertical-align: baseline; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, 'Microsoft YaHei', 微软雅黑, STHeitiSC-Light, simsun, 宋体, 'WenQuanYi Zen Hei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; word-break: break-word; overflow-wrap: break-word; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">除Whatsapp、Facebook之外，Instagram、Youtube是成为巴西月活量最多的社交通讯应用程序。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: 16px; line-height: inherit; vertical-align: baseline; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, 'Microsoft YaHei', 微软雅黑, STHeitiSC-Light, simsun, 宋体, 'WenQuanYi Zen Hei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; word-break: break-word; overflow-wrap: break-word; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;">另外，海外版贴吧Reddit以及来自中国的Litmatch、BIGO LIVE等社交产品也在巴西市场大放异彩。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: 16px; line-height: inherit; vertical-align: baseline; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, 'Microsoft YaHei', 微软雅黑, STHeitiSC-Light, simsun, 宋体, 'WenQuanYi Zen Hei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; word-break: break-word; overflow-wrap: break-word; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: 600; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; vertical-align: baseline; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; word-break: break-word;">Litmatch：</span>类似 Soul APP的玩法，产品的主要功能是 Avatar 语音匹配+语聊房+信息流。据白鲸研究院数据，Litmatch 的的出海首站是东南亚，在 2021年Q2 之后，Litmatch 的市场才开始扩散到巴西、沙特等地，位居当地下载榜前列</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: 16px; line-height: inherit; vertical-align: baseline; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, 'Microsoft YaHei', 微软雅黑, STHeitiSC-Light, simsun, 宋体, 'WenQuanYi Zen Hei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; word-break: break-word; overflow-wrap: break-word; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: 600; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; vertical-align: baseline; -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; word-break: break-word;">BIGO LIVE：</span>欢聚集团旗下，一款在海外炙手可热的直播社交App，2014年起步于新加坡，用户不仅可以在这一平台聊天、交友，还可以多人组团游戏、开会、祷 告、念经、欢聚节庆。</p>
