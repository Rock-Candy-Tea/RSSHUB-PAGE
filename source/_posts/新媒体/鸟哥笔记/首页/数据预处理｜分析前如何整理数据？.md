                        <p style="margin: 0px 0px 1.4em; caret-color: #121212; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; text-size-adjust: auto;" data-first-child data-pid="o89H243W"><span style="font-weight: 600; font-synthesis: style;">数据预处理</span></p>
<p style="margin: 1.4em 0px; caret-color: #121212; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; text-size-adjust: auto;" data-pid="GvHnqQev">在<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E6%95%B0%E6%8D%AE%E5%8F%AF%E8%A7%86%E5%8C%96" target="_blank">数据可视化</a>的过程中，我们经常会遇到几个情况：</p>
<p style="margin: 1.4em 0px; caret-color: #121212; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; text-size-adjust: auto;" data-pid="aTb9X9WT"><span style="font-weight: 600; font-synthesis: style;">存在个别空值导致整列无法计算、</span></p>
<p style="margin: 1.4em 0px; caret-color: #121212; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; text-size-adjust: auto;" data-pid="XrxzIAbr"><span style="font-weight: 600; font-synthesis: style;">第三方软件导出的数据格式有问题、</span></p>
<p style="margin: 1.4em 0px; caret-color: #121212; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; text-size-adjust: auto;" data-pid="itjdgi9i"><span style="font-weight: 600; font-synthesis: style;">存在重复值导致数据结果错误、</span></p>
<p style="margin: 1.4em 0px; caret-color: #121212; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; text-size-adjust: auto;" data-pid="Z2aaxHyU"><span style="font-weight: 600; font-synthesis: style;">异常值导致可视化效果不好等等</span></p>
<p style="margin: 1.4em 0px; caret-color: #121212; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; text-size-adjust: auto;" data-pid="dElkTN8N">这些问题都会影响我们的分析和可视化结果，这就需要我们在开始分析前，对数据进行清洗和整理。</p>
<p style="margin: 1.4em 0px; caret-color: #121212; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; text-size-adjust: auto;" data-pid="9YBnp4jD">专业的数据清洗和整理是指从数据库或数据表中更正和删除不准确数据记录的过程。</p>
<p style="margin: 1.4em 0px; caret-color: #121212; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; text-size-adjust: auto;" data-pid="a72f2OyZ">我们今天一起分享下广义的数据清洗，除了专业的数据库数据清洗外，一些本地的数据、第三方软件导出的数据等，在分析前，也要<span style="font-weight: 600; font-synthesis: style;">掌握数据预处理的思维，为后面的<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E6%95%B0%E6%8D%AE%E5%88%86%E6%9E%90" target="_blank">数据分析</a>和可视化提供支撑</span></p>
<h2 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; caret-color: #121212; color: #212629; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; text-size-adjust: auto; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; font-size: 18px; padding-left: 12px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;" data-pid="a72f2OyZ"><span style="font-synthesis: style;">01、确定字段含义、类型</span></h2>

<p style="margin: 1.4em 0px; caret-color: #121212; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; text-size-adjust: auto;" data-pid="I1uD118F">首先我们需要对数据有一个整体的了解</p>
<p style="margin: 1.4em 0px; caret-color: #121212; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; text-size-adjust: auto;" data-pid="V8r37Asd">1、了解每列数据的含义和表头是否对应</p>
<p style="margin: 1.4em 0px; caret-color: #121212; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; text-size-adjust: auto;" data-pid="NR5YGzac">2、确保没有重复的字段名</p>
<p style="margin: 1.4em 0px; caret-color: #121212; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; text-size-adjust: auto;" data-pid="uj8Mlih_">3、数据通常分为<span style="font-weight: 600; font-synthesis: style;">日期、数值、文本</span>3种类型</p>
<p style="margin: 1.4em 0px; caret-color: #121212; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; text-size-adjust: auto;" data-pid="C2XlerGE"><span style="font-weight: 600; font-synthesis: style;">日期：BI工具常用的</span>日期数据的标准格式：</p>
<p style="margin: 1.4em 0px; caret-color: #121212; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; text-size-adjust: auto;" data-pid="9SYn8AQV">“2015-01-01 00:00:00”</p>
<p style="margin: 1.4em 0px; caret-color: #121212; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; text-size-adjust: auto;" data-pid="uLzvzUDK">（时分秒部分为可选部分）excel单元格格式设置为日期</p>
<p style="margin: 1.4em 0px; caret-color: #121212; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; text-size-adjust: auto;" data-pid="vDFd5lyH"><span style="font-weight: 600; font-synthesis: style;">数值：</span>数值类型字段通常在系统中用来进行加减等数学计算，所以<span style="font-weight: 600; font-synthesis: style;">不能含有数字以外的符号或文本</span>，例如货币符号、千位分隔符等</p>

<h2 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 18px; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: 30px; clear: left; font-synthesis: style; caret-color: #121212; color: #212629; text-size-adjust: auto; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px; font-weight: 600; text-align: left;"><span style="font-synthesis: style;">02、转化为一维表</span></h2>
<p style="margin: 1.4em 0px; caret-color: #121212; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; text-size-adjust: auto;" data-pid="a02J2Dp1">二维表更符合我们日常的阅读习惯，信息更浓缩，适合展示分析结果，但作为源数据进行数据分析时，就需要使用一维表。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; caret-color: #121212; color: #000000; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; text-size-adjust: auto; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-pid="bk7o8jUL"><span style="font-weight: 600; font-synthesis: style;">如何区分二维表和一维表呢？</span></p>
<p style="margin: 1.4em 0px; caret-color: #121212; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; text-size-adjust: auto;" data-pid="rL_5wVnu">当多个字段属于同类型同含义时，例如都是数值字段且表达的含义差不多，那这个就是二维表;</p>
<p style="margin: 1.4em 0px; caret-color: #121212; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; text-size-adjust: auto;" data-pid="fuOxaVWg">而不存在任何相同性质的字段就是一维表。</p>

<h2 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; font-size: 18px; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: 30px; clear: left; font-synthesis: style; caret-color: #121212; color: #212629; text-size-adjust: auto; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px; font-weight: 600; text-align: left;"><span style="font-synthesis: style;">04、缺失值处理</span></h2>
<p style="margin: 1.4em 0px; caret-color: #121212; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; text-size-adjust: auto;" data-pid="jSo2stds">数据中出现缺失值会影响我们的分析结果，例如数值字段中存在空值，会导致无法计算等。</p>
<p style="margin: 1.4em 0px; caret-color: #121212; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; text-size-adjust: auto;" data-pid="2ysPPFcA"><span style="font-weight: 600; font-synthesis: style;">所以在数据预处理时，我们需要对缺失的数据进行处理</span></p>
<p style="margin: 1.4em 0px; caret-color: #121212; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; text-size-adjust: auto;" data-pid="60X2LVsj"><span style="font-weight: 600; font-synthesis: style;">1、筛选检查是否有空数据</span></p>
<p style="margin: 1.4em 0px; caret-color: #121212; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; text-size-adjust: auto;" data-pid="K5W5wh_I">在上传后，可以通过数据筛选，检查字段中是否有为空的数据</p>
<figure style="margin: 1.4em 0px; caret-color: #121212; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; text-size-adjust: auto;" data-size="normal"><img class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb lazy" style="display: block; max-width: 100%; margin: 0px auto; cursor: zoom-in; animation: 0.5s ease-in 0s 1 normal none running fxRichTextFadeIn;" src="https://pic3.zhimg.com/80/v2-c9206a650bb1f0c9abeb5c241c025812_1440w.jpg" width="1080" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="522" data-original="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-c9206a650bb1f0c9abeb5c241c025812_r.jpg" data-actualsrc="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-c9206a650bb1f0c9abeb5c241c025812_b.jpg" data-lazy-status="ok" title="数据预处理｜分析前如何整理数据？" alt="数据预处理｜分析前如何整理数据？" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure>
<p style="margin: 1.4em 0px; caret-color: #121212; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; text-size-adjust: auto;" data-pid="Fm5eN63X"><span style="font-weight: 600; font-synthesis: style;">2、对为空的数据赋值</span></p>
<p style="margin: 1.4em 0px; caret-color: #121212; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; text-size-adjust: auto;" data-pid="LpVGqAgK">数据中的空白数据，有可能是空值null，也有可能是空字符串，当是空字符串的时候可以用两个引号''表示</p>
<p style="margin: 1.4em 0px; caret-color: #121212; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; text-size-adjust: auto;" data-pid="fMcIIPKE">这里我们可以添加计算字段，对字段A进行判断，当字段A为空时，对其赋值0，使之不影响后续计算</p>
<p style="margin: 1.4em 0px; caret-color: #121212; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; text-size-adjust: auto;" data-pid="3tglIfqw"><span style="font-weight: 600; font-synthesis: style;">1）CCOALESCE：非空查找函数，返回参数中的第一个非空值或非空串；如果所有值都为NULL或空串，那么返回空串</span></p>
<p style="margin: 1.4em 0px; caret-color: #121212; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; text-size-adjust: auto;" data-pid="otQCgn20">示例：ccoalesce([字段A],0)</p>
<p style="margin: 1.4em 0px; caret-color: #121212; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; text-size-adjust: auto;" data-pid="LIjCayFD">当字段A中存在空值时，对于数值字段的计算会有影响，将空值替换成0，可以用这个函数，当字段A不为空时，返回字段A，当字段A为空时，返回0</p>
<p style="margin: 1.4em 0px; caret-color: #121212; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; text-size-adjust: auto;" data-pid="K488Z2KF"><span style="font-weight: 600; font-synthesis: style;">2）if函数：</span></p>
<p style="margin: 1.4em 0px; caret-color: #121212; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; text-size-adjust: auto;" data-pid="Ajd_VwTv"><span style="font-weight: 600; font-synthesis: style;">空值</span>：if([字段A] is null,0,[字段A])</p>
<p style="margin: 1.4em 0px; caret-color: #121212; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; text-size-adjust: auto;" data-pid="-kvhgtS2">含义：当字段A为空值时，返回0，否则返回字段A</p>
<p style="margin: 1.4em 0px; caret-color: #121212; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; text-size-adjust: auto;" data-pid="8-13NFMT"><span style="font-weight: 600; font-synthesis: style;">空字符串</span>：if([字段A]='',0,[字段A])<span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span></p>
<h2 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; caret-color: #121212; color: #212629; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; text-size-adjust: auto; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; font-size: 18px; padding-left: 12px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;" data-pid="8-13NFMT"><span style="font-synthesis: style;">05、异常值处理</span></h2>
<p style="margin: 1.4em 0px; caret-color: #121212; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; text-size-adjust: auto;" data-pid="AW5xROTf">数据中的异常值通常包含几种情况，例如部分数值极大极小、实际数据不应该有负数，但是出现数值为负等。</p>

<p style="margin: 1.4em 0px; caret-color: #121212; color: #121212; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Helvetica Neue', 'PingFang SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Source Han Sans SC', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', sans-serif; text-size-adjust: auto;" data-pid="jBwsLKGa">高质量的数据通常有一些相同特点：</p>
