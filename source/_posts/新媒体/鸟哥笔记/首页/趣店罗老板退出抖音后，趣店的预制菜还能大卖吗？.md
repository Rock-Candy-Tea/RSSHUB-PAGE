<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">7月17日，<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E8%B6%A3%E5%BA%97" target="_blank">趣店</a>罗老板的”一分钱送酸菜“直播间，刷屏抖音。当晚的直播间不仅豪送千台苹果手机，送出了 份1分钱的酸菜鱼，吸引了超过9000万人观看直播。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">经此一战，趣店做<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E9%A2%84%E5%88%B6%E8%8F%9C" target="_blank">预制菜</a>这一消息铺满全网。直播过后，趣店罗老板的抖音账号一夜涨粉超过400万，陆续有不少人收到趣店预制菜，对产品品质给予了肯定。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">在趣店预制菜势头正旺的时候，<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">据媒体报道，趣店CEO罗敏宣布退出抖音，并清空了微博的内容。</strong>蝉妈妈也发现，其抖音账号由“趣店罗老板”也悄然改名为“趣店预制菜”，这一转变不仅让外界开始猜测趣店接下来的动作。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><span id="article-img-0" class="a-image" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"><img style="margin: 0px auto 20px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: middle; border: none; max-width: 100%; display: block; background: url('//static.yidianzixun.com/apps/black_mamba_web_v1.0.0.177/black_mamba_web_main_pic_placeholder.png') 50% center no-repeat #e6e6e6;" src="https://i1.go2yd.com/image.php?url=YD_cnt_34_01DiKvVlTeHb" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>

<h4 style="margin: 25px 0px; padding: 0px; line-height: 30px; background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">▲图片来自微博</strong></h4>
<h2 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; line-height: 30px; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">趣店罗老板抖音账号改名，</strong><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">直播数据反映平平</strong></h2>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">据媒体报道，趣店罗老板在粉丝群中发布消息，称自己“可能有一些时间不会在镜头前直播了”，之后的工作将围绕供应链、菜品研发、食品质量等，但趣店做预制菜仍在继续。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">现在打开抖音的页面，趣店罗老板的抖音号已经换成了趣店预制菜，账号介绍、视频和评论区都不见罗老板的身影。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><span id="article-img-1" class="a-image" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"><img style="margin: 0px auto 20px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: middle; border: none; max-width: 100%; display: block; background: url('//static.yidianzixun.com/apps/black_mamba_web_v1.0.0.177/black_mamba_web_main_pic_placeholder.png') 50% center no-repeat #e6e6e6;" src="https://i1.go2yd.com/image.php?url=YD_cnt_34_01DiKvXPk0L0" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<div class="image-caption" style="margin: 20px 0px; width: 650px; color: #bfbfbf; text-align: center; font-size: 14px; line-height: 1.8; padding: 0px !important;"> </div>
<h4 style="margin: 25px 0px; padding: 0px; line-height: 30px; background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">▲图片来自抖音</strong></h4>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">蝉妈妈显示，趣店预制菜这个账号从6月开始开播带货，总共开播52场，<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">账号总销售额已经破2亿，累计销量千万级别。</strong></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><span id="article-img-2" class="a-image" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"><img style="margin: 0px auto 20px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: middle; border: none; max-width: 100%; display: block; background: url('//static.yidianzixun.com/apps/black_mamba_web_v1.0.0.177/black_mamba_web_main_pic_placeholder.png') 50% center no-repeat #e6e6e6;" src="https://i1.go2yd.com/image.php?url=YD_cnt_34_01DiKvYIfEms" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<div class="image-caption" style="margin: 20px 0px; width: 650px; color: #bfbfbf; text-align: center; font-size: 14px; line-height: 1.8; padding: 0px !important;"> </div>
<h4 style="margin: 25px 0px; padding: 0px; line-height: 30px; background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">▲图片来自蝉妈妈</strong></h4>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">在6月初刚开播时，趣店日销售额在10~280万之间徘徊，销售额并不稳定，且并未在业内引起广泛影响。<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">直到7月17日那场“一分钱送酸菜鱼”的直播场次，吸引了超过9000</strong><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">万人蹲守直播间，最终销售额突破2.5亿，让趣店预制菜一夜火出圈。</strong></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><span id="article-img-3" class="a-image" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"><img style="margin: 0px auto 20px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: middle; border: none; max-width: 100%; display: block; background: url('//static.yidianzixun.com/apps/black_mamba_web_v1.0.0.177/black_mamba_web_main_pic_placeholder.png') 50% center no-repeat #e6e6e6;" src="https://i1.go2yd.com/image.php?url=YD_cnt_34_01DiKvZrh1sJ" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<div class="image-caption" style="margin: 20px 0px; width: 650px; color: #bfbfbf; text-align: center; font-size: 14px; line-height: 1.8; padding: 0px !important;"> </div>
<h4 style="margin: 25px 0px; padding: 0px; line-height: 30px; background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">▲图片来自蝉妈妈</strong></h4>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">而在趣店罗老板的“撤离”之后<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">，直播间的销售额开始下降，</strong><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">蝉妈妈显示8月5日，趣店预制菜的两场直播销售总额仅为4万左右，且账号的粉丝数正在以一天几万的速度开始减少。</strong></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><span id="article-img-4" class="a-image" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"><img style="margin: 0px auto 20px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: middle; border: none; max-width: 100%; display: block; background: url('//static.yidianzixun.com/apps/black_mamba_web_v1.0.0.177/black_mamba_web_main_pic_placeholder.png') 50% center no-repeat #e6e6e6;" src="https://i1.go2yd.com/image.php?url=YD_cnt_34_01DiKvaWNa1r" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<div class="image-caption" style="margin: 20px 0px; width: 650px; color: #bfbfbf; text-align: center; font-size: 14px; line-height: 1.8; padding: 0px !important;"> </div>
<h4 style="margin: 25px 0px; padding: 0px; line-height: 30px; background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">▲图片来自蝉妈妈</strong></h4>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">目前，虽然趣店预制菜不再以“上市公司 CEO”人设开播，吸引外界的目光，但是趣店的账号依然在正常运营中。</p>
<h2 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; line-height: 30px; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">趣店流量与争议并存，</strong><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">而预制菜则走上风口</strong></h2>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">通过强人设和一分钱酸菜鱼送福利，趣店预制菜获得了千万级的曝光，虽然此前趣店也有多个项目，比如万里目、万里目少儿，并且斥重金邀请多位明星进行站台推广，却没有像这次一样能爆火出圈。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">但流量之下，趣店预制菜号召宝妈加盟趣店预制菜线下店，加上趣店之前的金融业务等接连引发争议，但趣店确实让更多人关注预制菜这个商品类目。而市面上各色各样的预制菜，正在走进千万人的餐桌，成为不可逆的饮食潮流。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">根据蝉妈妈行业类目趋势报告-食品饮料-方便速食/速冻食品-半成品菜/方便菜/快手菜中显示，<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">在过去的7月中，趣店超越叮叮懒人菜成为该品类销售额榜首。</strong></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><span id="article-img-5" class="a-image" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"><img style="margin: 0px auto 20px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: middle; border: none; max-width: 100%; display: block; background: url('//static.yidianzixun.com/apps/black_mamba_web_v1.0.0.177/black_mamba_web_main_pic_placeholder.png') 50% center no-repeat #e6e6e6;" src="https://i1.go2yd.com/image.php?url=YD_cnt_34_01DiKvbjanFV" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<div class="image-caption" style="margin: 20px 0px; width: 650px; color: #bfbfbf; text-align: center; font-size: 14px; line-height: 1.8; padding: 0px !important;"> </div>
<h4 style="margin: 25px 0px; padding: 0px; line-height: 30px; background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">▲图片来自蝉妈妈</strong></h4>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">除开趣店717的大场直播加持，<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">叮叮懒人菜在7月也表现不俗，单月销售额冲破4000万。</strong>相比于趣店，叮叮懒人菜在抖音平台则是深耕多时，21年就在更新账号，布局品牌自播，目前品牌自播销售额占比超过53%。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><span id="article-img-6" class="a-image" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"><img style="margin: 0px auto 20px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: middle; border: none; max-width: 100%; display: block; background: url('//static.yidianzixun.com/apps/black_mamba_web_v1.0.0.177/black_mamba_web_main_pic_placeholder.png') 50% center no-repeat #e6e6e6;" src="https://i1.go2yd.com/image.php?url=YD_cnt_34_01DiKvcgkiNO" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<div class="image-caption" style="margin: 20px 0px; width: 650px; color: #bfbfbf; text-align: center; font-size: 14px; line-height: 1.8; padding: 0px !important;"> </div>
<h4 style="margin: 25px 0px; padding: 0px; line-height: 30px; background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">▲蝉妈妈品牌详情页</strong></h4>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">且叮叮懒人菜开设了多个矩阵账号，无论是直播场观还是开播频率，销售额都保持着不错的趋势。此外深耕抖音预制菜的品牌还有皇家小虎、厨聪，海蓝厨房等，都在积极开播，或者寻找KOC进行带货。</p>
<h2 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; line-height: 30px; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">小结</strong></h2>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">近年来，因为疫情的影响，预制菜行业发展激增。据媒体报道，<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">国内预制菜企业注册量激增，仅2022年上半年就有1020家新注册企业</strong>，且消费市场还在进一步增长，投资机构、创业者看好预制菜也无可厚非。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">虽然预制菜目前如火如荼，但是依然有几大难关等待攻克。一是消费者的接受度，二则是并不算实惠的价格，一份椰子鸡预制菜价格在98~200元，价格可以比肩线下餐馆。三是物流运输、产品包装、产品口味差异造成的消费者售后服务等问题，以及产品同质化严重等等诸多难题。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">这些难题都预示着虽然预制菜有可观的前景，但并不是一个低门槛的赛道。蝉妈妈也会持续关注预制菜行业在抖音电商平台的长远发展，敬请期待。</p>
