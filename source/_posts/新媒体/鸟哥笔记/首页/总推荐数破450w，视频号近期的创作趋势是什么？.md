                        <p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="1" data-pm-slice="0 0 []">背靠微信强大的用户体量及强社交属性，<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E8%A7%86%E9%A2%91%E5%8F%B7" target="_blank">视频号</a>吸引着越来越多的创作者涌入其中。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="3">为帮助大家更好地洞察视频号平台的内容趋势及创作风向，<strong>新视推出7月「月度榜单」</strong>，结合近期热门内容，解析月榜数据，为从业者提供参考。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="5">据<span style="color: #1bb34d; --tt-darkmode-color: #1BB34D;">『新视』</span>统计，视频号7月TOP500账号中，<strong>影视娱乐、生活</strong>类账号数量依旧遥遥领先，特别是影视娱乐类账号，占据23.4%。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="7">从整体表现上看，<strong>影视娱乐、文化、健康</strong>等类型的账号热度上涨，而往期热门的<strong>生活、搞笑、母婴育儿</strong>类账号则占比明显下降。</p>
<p class="pgc-img" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://mp.toutiao.com/mp/agw/article_material/open_image/get?code=Mjk4MWNlYjZhZGJkN2E0MTYzZjllOTZhMTMxNjdiYTEsMTY1OTYwOTYwMTY3Mw==" data-ic="false" data-width="1080" data-height="608" data-ic-uri referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="pgc-img-caption"></span></p>
<h4 style="text-align: center; background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;" data-track="9"><span style="color: #a0a0a0; --tt-darkmode-color: #A0A0A0;">▲ 图源：新视</span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="11">7月视频号的内容生态中，<strong>有哪些创作者值得关注？他们又是如何打造爆款作品的？</strong>我们一起来看看。</p>
<h1 class="pgc-p" style="text-align: center; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); font-size: 20px; color: #212629; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;" data-track="15"><strong>01、</strong><strong>关注度飙升，</strong><strong>民生类账号势头强</strong></h1>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="18">7月，民生类账号表现突出，<strong>在总榜TOP10中占据5个席位</strong>，而在民生排行榜中，视频号<strong><span style="color: #1bb34d; --tt-darkmode-color: #1BB34D;">@极目新闻</span></strong>排名上升3位，位居榜首，<strong><span style="color: #1bb34d; --tt-darkmode-color: #1BB34D;">@民生频道</span></strong>、<strong><span style="color: #1bb34d; --tt-darkmode-color: #1BB34D;">@大象新闻</span></strong>则位列该榜第二和第三。</p>
<p class="pgc-img" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://mp.toutiao.com/mp/agw/article_material/open_image/get?code=MjE5M2M4NjJlMzYwZmMwYWZkZjc1ZWViMWEzNzQ3NGMsMTY1OTYwOTYwMTY3Mw==" data-ic="false" data-width="1000" data-height="2825" data-ic-uri referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="pgc-img-caption"></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="22">民生类账号发布的内容，<strong>以时事新闻、民生政策、百姓生活为主，且整体都保持着超高的更新频率，时常还会进行现场直播报道</strong>，在平台上获得了大量曝光。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="24">以<strong><span style="color: #1bb34d; --tt-darkmode-color: #1BB34D;">@极目新闻</span></strong>为例，7月该账号<strong>共计发布1081条作品，一周最多发布320条，10w推荐作品17条，且该账号总推荐数470.6w+，位于7月总榜第二</strong>。</p>
<p class="pgc-img" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://mp.toutiao.com/mp/agw/article_material/open_image/get?code=ODUyNmZjYWE4ODJlNzIyYjVhNjkzMzI1NWJiMmU1NjMsMTY1OTYwOTYwMTY3Mw==" data-ic="false" data-width="970" data-height="658" data-ic-uri referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="pgc-img-caption"></span></p>
<h4 style="text-align: center; background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;" data-track="26"><span style="color: #a0a0a0; --tt-darkmode-color: #A0A0A0;">▲ 图源：新视 - 视频号详情页</span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="28">新视发现，在民生类账号的简介中，通常还会留下电话号码、微信号、邮箱等联系方式，以供视频号用户联系或直接爆料投稿。</p>
<p class="pgc-img" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://mp.toutiao.com/mp/agw/article_material/open_image/get?code=YzdiY2U5OWNmODZhMTE2MzFlMTNjMTVjMjIzZTk1ODgsMTY1OTYwOTYwMTY3Mw==" data-ic="false" data-width="774" data-height="446" data-ic-uri referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="pgc-img-caption"></span></p>
<h4 style="text-align: center; background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;" data-track="30"><span style="color: #a0a0a0; --tt-darkmode-color: #A0A0A0;">▲ 图源：视频号</span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="32">民生类账号将视频号作为<strong>内容宣发新渠道</strong>，同时，视频号平台也成为了民生类账号的<strong>新闻线索来源</strong>。</p>
<h1 class="pgc-p" style="text-align: center; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); font-size: 20px; color: #212629; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;" data-track="34"><strong>02、</strong><strong>演的吧，</strong><strong>萌宠类账号真的卷</strong></h1>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="37">7月萌宠榜，视频号<strong><span style="color: #1bb34d; --tt-darkmode-color: #1BB34D;">@肉蛋球</span></strong>超过多次蝉联榜首的<strong><span style="color: #1bb34d; --tt-darkmode-color: #1BB34D;">@聪明旺豆豆</span></strong>，二者分别位居第一、第二，而<strong><span style="color: #1bb34d; --tt-darkmode-color: #1BB34D;">@小宠居家</span></strong>首次上榜，排名第三。</p>
<p class="pgc-img" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://mp.toutiao.com/mp/agw/article_material/open_image/get?code=NDQ3ODE1YTVlM2Q3YzlmM2MyYWExNDE5ZGEyNTY5YmMsMTY1OTYwOTYwMTY3Mw==" data-ic="false" data-width="1000" data-height="2816" data-ic-uri referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="pgc-img-caption"></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="41">我们曾分析过视频号<strong><span style="color: #1bb34d; --tt-darkmode-color: #1BB34D;">@聪明旺豆豆</span></strong>，其每条作品用简短的故事，展现金毛豆豆的聪明懂事，而<strong><span style="color: #1bb34d; --tt-darkmode-color: #1BB34D;">@肉蛋球</span></strong>的作品主角虽然同样是狗狗，并也展现出它的聪明机智，但却更令人啧啧称奇。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="43">如<strong><span style="color: #1bb34d; --tt-darkmode-color: #1BB34D;">@肉蛋球</span></strong>7月20日发布的作品<strong>《我说完都忘了，它记得比我清楚，到最后我又陪了...》</strong>，主人向狗狗肉球发起一项包含9个动作的复杂任务，在听一遍都不一定能记住的情况下，肉球不仅完美完成，还反将了主人一军，<strong>该视频不到2天就获得10w推荐</strong>。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="48">在以可爱治愈为主流的萌宠博主中，肉球的颜值并不高，但创作者另辟蹊径，<strong>通过展示肉球极通人性的新奇画面，引起平台用户的好奇，并获得喜爱</strong>。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="50">在作品评论区不乏有“送孩子去上清华北大吧！”、“太聪明了”的留言。</p>
<p class="pgc-img" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://mp.toutiao.com/mp/agw/article_material/open_image/get?code=ZmQ4NWZkY2VlNTdiNmVkZTIyYTEyY2QzMDllYmExZTAsMTY1OTYwOTYwMTY3Mw==" data-ic="false" data-width="680" data-height="724" data-ic-uri referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="pgc-img-caption"></span></p>
<h4 style="text-align: center; background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;" data-track="52"><span style="color: #a0a0a0; --tt-darkmode-color: #A0A0A0;">▲ 图源：视频号</span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="54">该账号创作者<strong>挖掘自身优势，打造机智萌宠“人设”</strong>，满足了萌宠类账号在另类娱乐属性上的需求。</p>
<h1 class="pgc-p" style="text-align: center; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); font-size: 20px; color: #212629; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;" data-track="56"><strong>03、</strong><strong>一招鲜，</strong><strong>搞笑类账号有奇招</strong></h1>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="59">7月搞笑榜出现黑马，视频号<strong><span style="color: #1bb34d; --tt-darkmode-color: #1BB34D;">@Ax阿羡搞笑</span>单月打造出14条爆款作品，空降榜首</strong>，<strong><span style="color: #1bb34d; --tt-darkmode-color: #1BB34D;">@何静同学</span></strong>排名上升3位，位居第二，<strong><span style="color: #1bb34d; --tt-darkmode-color: #1BB34D;">@沧州黑妹儿</span></strong>稳定发挥，稳居第三。</p>
<p class="pgc-img" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://mp.toutiao.com/mp/agw/article_material/open_image/get?code=NDRmMjM0MWNiYjM4ZmRhODg0N2YxNmY5ODNjMWYzZmIsMTY1OTYwOTYwMTY3NA==" data-ic="false" data-width="1000" data-height="2816" data-ic-uri referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="pgc-img-caption"></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="63">在竞争激烈的搞笑赛道，运营仅2个月的黑马账号<strong><span style="color: #1bb34d; --tt-darkmode-color: #1BB34D;">@Ax阿羡搞笑</span></strong>做了什么？</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="65"><strong><span style="color: #1bb34d; --tt-darkmode-color: #1BB34D;">固定发布频率，养成用户习惯</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="67"><span style="color: #333333; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">7月，</span><strong><span style="color: #1bb34d; --tt-darkmode-color: #1BB34D;">@Ax阿羡搞笑</span>共计发布90条作品，平均一天3条</strong>，从新视统计的发布时间分布可以看到，该账号在各时间段发布的频率较为平均，对于粉丝来说，无论何时查看该视频号，总能看到新内容，也会对账号更新产生期待。</p>
<p class="pgc-img" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://mp.toutiao.com/mp/agw/article_material/open_image/get?code=ODZiM2MwOWZiNDYwNWExOWIzNTU4ZDJhMGI3MzRjNjIsMTY1OTYwOTYwMTY3NA==" data-ic="false" data-width="751" data-height="206" data-ic-uri referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="pgc-img-caption"></span></p>
<h4 style="text-align: center; background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;" data-track="69"><span style="color: #a0a0a0; --tt-darkmode-color: #A0A0A0;">▲ 图源：新视 - 视频号详情页</span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="71"><strong><span style="color: #1bb34d; --tt-darkmode-color: #1BB34D;">重复内容特色，制造记忆点</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="73"><strong>红眼特效</strong>和<strong>熊猫表情包</strong>，看过<strong><span style="color: #1bb34d; --tt-darkmode-color: #1BB34D;">@Ax阿羡搞笑</span></strong>的作品，必定会对这两种元素记忆深刻，同样的搞笑视频内容，经过二次加工后，更具辨识度。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="78">并且除了在视频画面，其作品标题中，也一期不落地加上了话题<strong>#红眼#红眼特效</strong>，成为账号的独特标签。</p>
<p class="pgc-img" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://mp.toutiao.com/mp/agw/article_material/open_image/get?code=MDlkYjA4YWI5NjM1ZGRlMTdkMjBhMTYzMmMwODM2ZGQsMTY1OTYwOTYwMTY3NA==" data-ic="false" data-width="1362" data-height="334" data-ic-uri referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="pgc-img-caption"></span></p>
<h4 style="text-align: center; background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;" data-track="80"><span style="color: #a0a0a0; --tt-darkmode-color: #A0A0A0;">▲ 图源：新视 - 视频号详情页</span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="83">虽然贯穿始终的红眼特效，能够在早期，帮助账号快速给用户留下深刻影响，但用户的新鲜感是有限的。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="85">特别是<strong><span style="color: #1bb34d; --tt-darkmode-color: #1BB34D;">@Ax阿羡搞笑</span></strong>的内容原创度低，或许会限制账号的长期发展，想要保持黑马的劲头，<strong>在内容制作上还需要有更多原创性</strong>。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="88">以上就是7月细分领域榜单中，值得关注的优质视频号创作者，了解整体的榜单表现，可以查看下面的<strong><span style="color: #1bb34d; --tt-darkmode-color: #1BB34D;">『7月总榜』</span></strong>TOP50：</p>
<p class="pgc-img" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://mp.toutiao.com/mp/agw/article_material/open_image/get?code=Y2JjNWU5MmRiNjk4NDI0NDhmYTFkOWZhZjgwOTA5Y2YsMTY1OTYwOTYwMTY3NA==" data-ic="false" data-width="1000" data-height="6330" data-ic-uri referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="pgc-img-caption"></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="92">最后，我们再来总结回顾一下，本次榜单的重点信息：</p>
