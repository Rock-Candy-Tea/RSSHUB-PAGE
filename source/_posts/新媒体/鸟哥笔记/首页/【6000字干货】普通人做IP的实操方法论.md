                        <p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;"><a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E4%B8%AA%E4%BA%BAIP" target="_blank">个人IP</a>最近两年一直比较火，也是变现相对容易的方式。很多人想打造自己的IP却无从下手，很多人急于打造IP被割了韭菜。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">我从2019年开始用IP的形式做私域变现，也操盘过上百万用户的私域矩阵，算是有点心得和经验。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">今天就花点时间系统性的说一下：普通人如何打造自己的IP。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">想做个人IP，要先弄清楚几个问题：</span></p>
<h2 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; font-size: 18px; font-weight: 600; color: #212629; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; text-align: left; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); line-height: 30px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;"><strong style="margin-top: 0px !important;">1、如何定义个人IP？</strong></span></h2>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">个人IP这个词听起来很唬人，其实没有多么高大上，IP就是品牌，打造个人IP就是做自己的品牌。之所以说这事不高大上，是因为再大的IP也有覆盖不到的人群，再小的IP也有自己的受众用户。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">拿知识付费举例，做到100分的人可以教80分的，80分可以教60分，60分可以教40分...</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">只要你在某个领域有干货，就一定能找到愿意为你付费的人，所以个人IP不存在绝对专业一说，专业都是相对的。</span></p>
<h2 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; font-size: 18px; font-weight: 600; color: #212629; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; text-align: left; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); line-height: 30px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;"><strong style="margin-top: 0px !important;">2、是不是所有人都可以做个人IP？</strong></span></h2>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">先说结论，大部分人是不适合做个人IP的。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">原因不在于专业性是否够强，而在于是否具备持续输出好内容的能力。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">IP的养成首先需要足够多的曝光量，曝光靠的是信息的传递，而信息传递的媒介无非视频、音频、文字三种。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">所有做个人IP的一个前提就是至少擅长一种内容输出形式，且能坚持输出，以抖音为例，至少拍够了100条短视频才有资格说自己可以尝试做个人IP。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">知行合一，有知没有行的人是一定做不了成IP的。</span></p>
<h2 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; font-size: 18px; font-weight: 600; color: #212629; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; text-align: left; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); line-height: 30px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;"><strong style="margin-top: 0px !important;">3、做个人IP要不要先做粉丝量？</strong></span></h2>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">绝大多数情况下，做IP应该追求从第一个粉丝就开始变现，无需陷入粉丝增长的虚假繁荣里去，弄了10万粉丝，如果不能变现，那这些粉丝也毫无价值。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">只有一种情况除外，就是你不追求在粉丝身上变现，你想要粉丝数来证明自己的涨粉能力，或者增加自己在某个领域的背书，再通过其他方式变现。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">比如教别人如何做抖音，首先自己要先是一个大号才行，自己只有几万粉丝，还想教别人如何做到百万粉丝，多少是有点扯了，粉丝信任度这关你就过不去。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">搞清楚了这几个问题，我们可以对自己是否适合做个人IP有一个基本的判断，如果发现自己很适合做IP，那么先恭喜你，找到了一条快速致富之路。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">IP打造可以用一个公式来总结：IP价值=定位*产品*流量*转化。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">基于这个公式，我们按照定位、产品、流量、转化四个模块详细说说打造个人IP应该如何落地。</span></p>
<h2 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; font-size: 18px; font-weight: 600; color: #212629; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; text-align: left; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); line-height: 30px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;"><strong style="margin-top: 0px !important;">一、找准IP定位</strong></span></h2>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">定位是否准确，直接决定了这个IP的变现能力如何。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">给自己做定位时，不用过分关注名头是不是够响、听起来是不是很高大上。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">IP的定位过程是对自己能力模型的一次深度审视。要找到自己有相对优势的领域，而不是花心思去给自己包装出来一个听起来很唬人，但自己根本驾驭不了的花架子。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">做IP真实很重要，只有真实的才是最持久的。</span><span style="color: #000000;">不能因为看到某个行业赚钱，就硬往上面靠，假IP一旦崩盘露馅，基本也就互联网社死了。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">所以实事求是是第一要义，定位是筛选出来的，不能包装出来的。</span></p>
<h3 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; font-size: 16px; font-weight: 600; color: #212629; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; text-align: left; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//1bb91f73e9d31ea2830a5e73ce3ed328.png'); line-height: 30px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;"><strong style="margin-top: 0px !important;">1、找到自己有优势的领域</strong></span></h3>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">那如何找到自己有相对优势的领域呢？优先看自己从事了一定年限的职业。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">比如说：</span></p>

<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">理论上讲，大多数人都可以找到自己的专业定位，因为永远有人做的比你差。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">自己是60分选手时，要向80分选手学习，但是可以去赚40分选手的钱，生态位之间的差距就是你在该领域的套利空间。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">前不久，我在朋友圈看到一个大四学生的创业社群在招新，我很好奇自己还没赚到什么钱的人是怎么教别人创业的，就花点钱进群了，很快发现是自己肤浅了，他的群里都是混的不如他好的大学生。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">换言之，我根本不是他的目标用户，他自己是大四创业者，他就收大一、大二、大三学生的钱，教这些人如何创业，先不创业这事能不能教会，这个思路是非常对的，真正做到了去赚自己“影响圈”的钱。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">如果你把自己从头到脚审视了一遍，发现在所从事的职业实在没有值得讲的成绩，还可以从另外一个角度切入：把自己的爱好开发成IP定位。</span></p>

<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">找自己有优势或有资源的行业，这样做起来最容易，做IP是一件成功率挺低的事，所以在行业选择上一定要选择自己最擅长的，尽可能从个起点提高成功率。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">同时还要看这个行业是否离钱够近，一是变现路径短，二是付费意愿强。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">拿知识付费举例，理财、职场、星座离钱就近，娱乐、体育、三农离钱就远。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">如果不知道哪些行业离钱近，就看做这个行业的人多不多，做的人多说明变现容易，做的人少说明变现难。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">不要抱有侥幸自己能发现一片既赚钱又没人的蓝海，中国有14亿人口，能赚钱的行业一定都有人在做了。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">找到自己有优势的领域之后，接下来就是让自己的IP具像化起来。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">IP的定位相当于是一个人的灵魂，但是光有灵魂肯定不够，还得补充上骨架和血肉，再穿上衣服才能出去见人。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">这个过程就可以通过给IP打标签的形式来完成。</span></p>
<h3 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; font-size: 16px; font-weight: 600; color: #212629; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; text-align: left; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//1bb91f73e9d31ea2830a5e73ce3ed328.png'); line-height: 30px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;"><strong style="margin-top: 0px !important;">2、给IP打上准确的标签</strong></span></h3>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">第一步是起一个通俗易懂有辨识度的名字，相当于IP的骨架。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">从0起盘、没有影响力基础的IP最好不要用自己的本名，会影响用户的理解能力和记忆能力。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">比较好的起名方式是“花名+行业”，比如猴哥说车、达达聊私域、阿笨数码测评、花花教英语，可以让别人一眼就知道你是做什么的，再加上一个有辨识度的花名，有助于让别人把你从同行中区别出来。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">第二步是提炼自己的性格，相当于IP的血肉。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">人们更愿意为自己喜欢、认可的人付费，所以只有专业标签是不够的，还得有性格、有情绪。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">在性格的表现上最优解就是“做真实的自己”，IP是不能作假的，你可以选择性的展示你的某一面，不展示全部。但是不能硬装你具有某一面，装出来的迟早会露馅，做IP是一项长期工程，学会找到自己最舒服的姿势，要不然自己和观众都觉得拧巴。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">内向，外向；</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">多言，少语；</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">冷漠，热情；</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">社恐，社牛；</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">理性，感性。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">这些都是性格标签，而且没有对错，每种性格都能吸引一群特定的人。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">提炼性格标签的目的是获得客户的信任，所以只有真实是最重要的，不要追求完美，有瑕疵的性格更容易让人放下戒备。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">最后一步是特征，相当于给血肉之躯又套上了一层衣服。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">常用的表现特征标签的手法就是视觉锤和文字钉，作用是能够快速的让用户记住自己。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">视觉锤通常用穿衣风格来体现，比如摇滚教父崔健经常戴一顶红色五角星的帽子，比如360老总周鸿祎总是穿一件红色T恤，又因为敢说话，被称做“红衣大炮”。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">文字钉通常用口头禅来体现，比如李佳琦的“OMG，买它！”是近两年最成功的文字钉，只要一听到这句话你就可以联想到李佳琦。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">视觉锤和文字就是用最简单粗暴的手段在用户脑海里打下一个烙印，只要他看到类似的元素，就可以想起你来。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">到这一步，IP的定位工作就算是完成了，现在可以有一个完整立体的形象在用户面前展示了，接下来就是设计变现产品。</span></p>
<h2 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; font-size: 18px; font-weight: 600; color: #212629; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; text-align: left; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); line-height: 30px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;"><strong style="margin-top: 0px !important;">二、设计变现产品</strong></span></h2>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">设计产品，就是满足需求。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">人在两种情况下会特别难受，一是得不到，二是怕失去。所以只要回答以下任一问题即可：</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">1、我能为别人带来什么？</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">2、我能帮别人避免损失什么？</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">比如我是教英语的，我可以帮客户快速通过雅思考试；</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">比如我是做家政的，我可以帮客户节省时间和体力。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">找到这样的需求，就可以着手设计具体的变现产品了。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">变现产品可以分为引流品和利润品两大类，其中引流品不是必需品，可以没有；利润品可以分为标准化利润品和非标准化利润品。</span></p>
<h3 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; font-size: 16px; font-weight: 600; color: #212629; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; text-align: left; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//1bb91f73e9d31ea2830a5e73ce3ed328.png'); line-height: 30px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;"><strong style="margin-top: 0px !important;">1、引流品</strong></span></h3>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">我们知道，成交的前提是信任，而信任又和IP力是否够强有直接关系，当IP力不够强时，高价利润品很难直接成交，这时候就需要引流品起一个过渡作用。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">引流品通常具有“价格低、标准化、能有效解决单点问题”这三个特点，目的是让用户以较低的成本快速体验到产品的效果。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">例如知识付费的99元体验课；</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">例如大牌化妆品的小样、体验装；</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">例如超市各种新品的免费试吃。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">用户在消费引流品时，就是双方逐步建立信任的过程，如果体验满意，信任度+1，就有可能转到利润品；如果体验不好，信任度-1，用户就会直接流失掉。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">所以引流品的设计越简单越好，做到力出一孔，把单点打透。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">这样用户会形成心理暗示，低价品都可以把问题解决的这么好，那高价品只会更好，转化率自然就变高了。</span></p>
<h3 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; font-size: 16px; font-weight: 600; color: #212629; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; text-align: left; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//1bb91f73e9d31ea2830a5e73ce3ed328.png'); line-height: 30px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;"><strong style="margin-top: 0px !important;">2、利润品</strong></span></h3>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">从产品类型上看，引流品一定是标准化的。利润品在选择上则会多一些，可以标准化，也可以非标准化。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">标准化的利润品看重规模，哪怕每单的利润有限，但只要规模做大了，利润也非常的可观。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">这类产品随着规模变大边际成本会越来越低，最极端例子就是知识付费的课程，在课程制作成本上，卖出去1套和卖出去10000套成本是一样的。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">而非标准化的产品在交付上会更重，例如顾问、咨询，每一单解决的都是个性化需求，所以单价一定要高，否则很难赚钱。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">在自己的势能不足以收取超高客单价时，尽量把利润品做成标准化的方式会好一些。</span></p>
<h2 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; font-size: 18px; font-weight: 600; color: #212629; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; text-align: left; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); line-height: 30px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;"><strong style="margin-top: 0px !important;">三、选定流量平台</strong></span></h2>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">按照“IP价值=定位*产品*流量*转化”的公式，确定了IP定位、设计好了变现产品，接下来就要去获客了，获客最重要的就是选准适合自己的平台。</span></p>
<h3 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; font-size: 16px; font-weight: 600; color: #212629; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; text-align: left; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//1bb91f73e9d31ea2830a5e73ce3ed328.png'); line-height: 30px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;"><strong style="margin-top: 0px !important;">1、如何选择内容平台</strong></span></h3>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">平台从内容形态上可以分为三类：视频类、图文类、混合类。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; font-size: 16px; font-weight: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;"><strong style="margin-top: 0px !important;">1）视频类</strong></span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">主流的有：抖音、快手、视频号、B站。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">其中快手因为生态问题不适合素人去做IP，视频号目前的推荐算法还不完善，B站长视频的制作门槛又太高。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">抖音是目前流量最大、用户分布最均匀、推荐算法最成熟的的平台，推荐方式是选择抖音为主要阵地，然后把内容分发到其他平台上。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'Noto Sans CJK SC', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin-top: 0px !important; color: #000000;">自己是否适合抖音这个平台，可以看是否满足以下任一条件：</span></p>
