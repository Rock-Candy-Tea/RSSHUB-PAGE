<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E5%95%86%E4%B8%9A%E6%8A%95%E6%94%BE" target="_blank">商业投放</a>已成为提高品牌声量的重要手段，而低成本与高回报之间似乎存在一条鸿沟，那么跨越鸿沟，真正低成本高回报的打法是否存在？是否能成为品牌进入市场的长期打法？</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>新榜尝试在“种草圣地”<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E5%B0%8F%E7%BA%A2%E4%B9%A6" target="_blank">小红书</a>上寻找答案。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/996074115601215dbd1d874.12950565.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><strong>品牌尝试低成本“薅走”高声量</strong><span></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>据新红数据，小红书618预热阶段，<strong>“迪彩”“卓尔诗婷”“欧花集”“雾屿森林”</strong>四个品牌商业投放表现突出。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>一方面，在预估投放金额最低的50个美妆个护类品牌中，这四个品牌商业笔记总互动量超10万，数据上高于其他品牌。同时，通过对比商业笔记数量，其互动数据总量突出并非因为笔记数量多。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/149101322162bc336e66c3a3.49648458.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>另一方面，从高互动总量的区间来看，相较于资金实力雄厚的头部美妆大牌，<span>这四个品牌的</span>单次互动成本都相对极低。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/117400903162bc336e8d4294.73576012.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/2068046273601215dcef8457.71452222.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><strong><span>品牌声量数据“高增长”背后是什么</span></strong><span></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>基于新红平台的收录数据，可以观察到，这四个品牌多是从2021年Q4开始在小红书进行商业投放。</span><span>复盘整体投放趋势，仅商业投放半年左右，品牌互动量取得显著提升。</span><span>尤其是迪彩，商业投放周期不到3个月，618前就实现了互动量猛增。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/167595463162bc336edae337.03365900.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>根据新红数据，我们观察到这类品牌高速增长背后的一些投放特点：</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>达人账号类型：</span></strong><span>与长期霸榜的兰蔻、LA MER、完美日记、多芬等品牌存在较大差异的是，迪彩等并非以垂类的美妆个护为核心，而是投放账号类型更为分散，甚至在穿搭、美食、影视综等类别账号的投放力度高于美妆垂类，可谓是广撒网；</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>达人粉丝量级：</span></strong><span>四个品牌投放超九成都是5万以下，其中迪彩投放的尾部流量占比更是超过99%，小号内容强占坑；</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>笔记类型：</span></strong><span>图文居多，有利于“洗脑式”传播产品特点；</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>笔记场景：</span></strong><span>多为课堂、校园、宿舍、地铁等，切合年轻群体生活场景，切中目标群体需求；</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>笔记内容：</span></strong><span>持续强调产品效果明显，让粉丝觉得可以和博主一样成为“人群中最耀眼的美女”；</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>目标群体：</span></strong><span>经济能力一般、有强烈“美”需求的年轻女性群体，代表为女大学生。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/169998316162bc336f08a114.77894781.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/185001482862bc336f6714a6.08096316.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>显然，这些品牌种草的目的是最大程度促成转化，那么，他们是如何让目标受众完成购买交易的呢？</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>种草氛围浓厚：</span></strong><span>点赞量靠前的评论多是表达被种草、想要购买的想法，整体种草氛围浓厚；</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>粉丝间互动高于博主与粉丝互动：</span></strong><span></span><span>评论区粉丝热情度很高，对于使用体验、购买渠道等的疑问，甚至比博主回复速度更快；</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>多渠道购买转化</span></strong><span>：通过在品牌淘宝等相关渠道可以看到评论中看到“从小红书来”，得到转化链路的反向验证。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/213118661462bc336f8bb052.66704423.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/2012716452601215de253f83.29034753.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><strong><span>内容打法似早期<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E5%BE%AE%E5%95%86" target="_blank">微商</a>朋友圈</span></strong><span></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>通过观察以上四个品牌在小红书618期间的营销动作，我们可以看到很多熟悉的早期微商打法</span><span>。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>诸如，充分利用尾部小号流量、社交圈层化传播、以消费者影响消费者、重复性广告信息强化产品功效、持续性分享产品口碑打消用户顾虑等。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/123095769262bc336fb77380.01129204.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>整体而言，对于面向年轻女性用户群体的品牌，月活2亿、女性占比70%、90后占比72%的小红书的确是铺垫品牌声量、提升品牌认知度的重要渠道。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>但同时，品牌，尤其是新品牌，如何依托渠道对产品进行口碑效应的长期构筑，如何与渠道在营销打法上形成1+1＞2的效应，需要不断探索。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>迪彩等品牌的投放效果，在一定程度上印证了这类内容打法可以满足品牌</span><span>丰富社交平台内容生态、快速提升知名度的需求。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span></span><span>然而，其中有个别品牌在此前小红书开展的“虚假营销”治理专项中，涉嫌虚假营销被封禁，也说明<span>这</span><span>类</span>内容形式在实际运用中存在触碰平台风险的可能性。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/201969823562bc3370156414.70285374.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>如果从品牌口碑打造及长期建设角度来看，这是一次性的流量收割？</span><span>还是可以持续并促进品牌升级的捷径？</span><span>仍需要时间来验证。</span></p>
