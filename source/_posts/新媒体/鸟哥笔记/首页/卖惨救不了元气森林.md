<section><span>“早前我们主打日系品牌，是因为年轻人的偏好在那里。现在他们的喜好更多元，我们也会进一步调整策<strong><span>略。”</span></strong></span></section>
<section></section>
<section><span><span>潜台词：</span><span><strong>不是我们在装，而是你们变了。</strong></span></span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span>元气森林起初把自己包装成日系模样，误导消费者认为其是日本制品牌<span>。</span></span><span>现在怕是想变成更接地气的国货，<span>于是</span>撕掉伪日系标签。</span></section>
<section><span> </span></section>
<section><span><strong>这有点像把日系当夜壶，憋急了就拿来用一下，不用的时候就甩开。</strong></span></section>
<section></section>
<section><span><span>打脸那么快，消费者<span>难免</span>会</span>觉得元气森林是上演大型双标，自然对元气森林的国货牌<span>可乐</span>没啥什么好感了。</span></section>

<p style="text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>骗人长胖后，卖点变槽点 </span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>消费者对元气森林的反感，不单是伪日系，还有“伪零糖”。</span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>除了自毁日系，<span>元气森林</span>还自毁森林。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>元气森林最初将“0糖”当卖点宣传，还把<span>大大的</span>“0蔗糖”字眼印瓶上。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/174090106062ddb56c58e7f3.29958019.jpeg" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span><br></span></p>

<section><span><strong>这可逼得元气森林出来公关：</strong></span><span>换包装换话术，将“0蔗糖”改为“低糖”，还承诺原料不再加结晶果糖。</span></section>

<section><span>0蔗糖和0糖，消费者分不分得清不好说。但</span><span><strong>元气森林长期以“喝不胖的奶茶”做宣传，玩起文字游戏，容易让消费者误会乳茶就是0糖。</strong></span></section>
