<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="11"><span style="letter-spacing: 0.5px;">大家好，我是许钧展，今天和大家分享的课程是SEO的布局与优化。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="13"><span style="letter-spacing: 0.5px;">这一节课相对来说比较干，理论性较少，大都是操作类的内容，比较适合已经正在做抖音直播或者小店，或者以前做过传统电商的朋友，如果是完全没有电商经验的朋友，稍微有点门槛。但没关系，大家一起学习和了解。</span></p>

<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="17"><span style="letter-spacing: 0.5px;">我发两张对比图，大家看一下就知道了。</span></p>

<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="21"><span style="letter-spacing: 0.5px;">第一张图是没有做过优化的数据，搜索流量的中位数大概是十几，做过搜索优化之后，<strong>直播间搜索流量的数据翻了十倍多</strong>，数据大家可以看第二张图。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="23"><span style="letter-spacing: 0.5px;">它的流量上升非常快，到第三天，第四天左右，成交单价已经到了250元，流量质量本身是相当不错的。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="25"><span style="letter-spacing: 0.5px;">用这个案例是想告诉大家，做好SEO的优化和布局的重要性，那么具体怎么做呢？</span></p>
<p class="pgc-img" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/4bbba5edefd947d07aa2d7a800b7d15b.png" width="1950" height="390" data-ic="false" data-width="1950" data-height="390" data-ic-uri referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="pgc-img-caption"></span></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;" data-track="28"><span style="letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><strong><span style="color: #3e3e3e; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">如何选择与正确布局关键词</span></strong></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="30"><span style="letter-spacing: 0.5px;">做<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E6%8A%96%E9%9F%B3seo%E4%BC%98%E5%8C%96" target="_blank">抖音seo优化</a>，首先要做好<strong>选词</strong>的工作。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="32"><span style="letter-spacing: 0.5px;">为什么？因为<strong>选词本身代表着搜索量的高低</strong>。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="34"><span style="letter-spacing: 0.5px;"><span style="background-color: #f7f9fb; --tt-darkmode-bgcolor: #BDBEC0;">举个例子，一个选词搜索量如果有100万，转化率1%，就能够拦截到1万流量。如果选词选得不够好，比如只有1万流量，那么就算有20%拦截率，也只能拦截到2000个流量。</span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="36"><span style="letter-spacing: 0.5px;">这一对比，相差就非常大了。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="38"><span style="letter-spacing: 0.5px;">所以我们做SEO的布局与优化，第一步一定是做好选词的工作。那么，选词依据或者工具是什么呢？</span></p>
<p class="pgc-img" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/b88f43b1f8531a436a15661bb9737c51.png" width="705" height="174" data-ic="false" data-width="705" data-height="174" data-ic-uri referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 class="pgc-img" style="text-align: center; background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span class="pgc-img-caption">▲图片来源：老师课件 巨量算数后台</span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="42"><span style="letter-spacing: 0.5px;">这个工具其实就是抖音的一个官方网站——巨量算数。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="44"><span style="letter-spacing: 0.5px;">如上图，点击“算数指数”就能进入选词工具，其中又包含“综合指数”和“搜索指数”，我们<strong>主要看“搜索指数”</strong>。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="46"><span style="letter-spacing: 1.5px;"><strong><span style="color: #7e44f8; --tt-darkmode-color: #7E44F8;"><span style="background-color: #f4f1ff; --tt-darkmode-bgcolor: #BEBCC7;">“选词”三部曲</span></span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="47"><strong><span style="color: #ffffff; --tt-darkmode-color: #FFFFFF;"><span style="background-color: #7e44f8; --tt-darkmode-bgcolor: #7E44F8;">1.确定主关键词</span></span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="48">通过对比主关键词和联想词的搜索指数的高低，选择搜索指数高的词作为主关键词。</p>
<p class="pgc-img" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/c85196ee65db64d5955c31dd6c6d8690.png" width="549" height="283" data-ic="false" data-width="1098" data-height="566" data-ic-uri referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 class="pgc-img" style="text-align: center; background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span class="pgc-img-caption">▲图片来源：老师课件 巨量算数后台</span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="52">比如，以主关键词“盲盒”为例，查看“盲盒”的搜索指数以及下拉框中联想词的搜索指数。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="54">如果只要下拉框前10个关键词，那就记录这10个关键词的搜索指数，并<strong>挑选指数最高的词作为主关键词。</strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="56"><strong><span style="color: #ffffff; --tt-darkmode-color: #FFFFFF;"><span style="background-color: #7e44f8; --tt-darkmode-bgcolor: #7E44F8;">2.确定关联词</span></span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="57">还是以“盲盒”为例，它的关联词有可能是这些词，可能我们会觉得有些词的关联度不大，但是没关系，我们只要如实记录客观数据。</p>
<p class="pgc-img" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/20c8b2aa5b567ae19224040bc52b36f1.png" width="615" height="468" data-ic="false" data-width="1230" data-height="936" data-ic-uri referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 class="pgc-img" style="text-align: center; background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span class="pgc-img-caption">▲图片来源：老师课件 巨量算数后台</span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="61"><strong><span style="color: #ffffff; --tt-darkmode-color: #FFFFFF;"><span style="background-color: #7e44f8; --tt-darkmode-bgcolor: #7E44F8;">3.优化</span></span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="62">通过对“昵称、合集、话题、标题、视频文案”这5个要素，对SEO排名进行优化，这部分详见本文第二个模块内容。</p>
<p class="pgc-img" style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/b685ed5a90e50a5da1ee68da44ab612c.png" width="1950" height="390" data-ic="false" data-width="1950" data-height="390" data-ic-uri referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="pgc-img-caption"></span></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;" data-track="65"><strong><span style="color: #3e3e3e; --tt-darkmode-color: #A3A3A3;">如何卡排名与拍摄SEO视频</span></strong></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="67">影响SEO排名有以下4个要素：</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="69"><span style="letter-spacing: 1.5px;"><strong><span style="color: #7e44f8; --tt-darkmode-color: #7E44F8;"><span style="background-color: #f4f1ff; --tt-darkmode-bgcolor: #BEBCC7;">1.抖音流量机制</span></span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="70">抖音搜索结果千人千面，比如说，如果你关注的作者有发布过你想要搜索的内容，那么当你搜索时，他可能会排在搜索结果前排。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="72"><span style="letter-spacing: 1.5px;"><strong><span style="color: #7e44f8; --tt-darkmode-color: #7E44F8;"><span style="background-color: #f4f1ff; --tt-darkmode-bgcolor: #BEBCC7;">2.关键词堆叠</span></span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="73">其实就是我们所说的SEO优化，接下来会详细讲解。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="75"><span style="letter-spacing: 1.5px;"><strong><span style="color: #7e44f8; --tt-darkmode-color: #7E44F8;"><span style="background-color: #f4f1ff; --tt-darkmode-bgcolor: #BEBCC7;">3.视频播放量</span></span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="76">播放量也是影响搜索结果的一个关键因素。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="78"><span style="letter-spacing: 1.5px;"><strong><span style="color: #7e44f8; --tt-darkmode-color: #7E44F8;"><span style="background-color: #f4f1ff; --tt-darkmode-bgcolor: #BEBCC7;">4.视频数据</span></span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="79">比如，完播率、点击进入率、视频发布时间、视频相关反馈数据。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="81">那么，影响SEO排名的五要素，该如何做优化呢？</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="83"><span style="letter-spacing: 1.5px;"><strong><span style="color: #7e44f8; --tt-darkmode-color: #7E44F8;"><span style="background-color: #f4f1ff; --tt-darkmode-bgcolor: #BEBCC7;">1.昵称</span></span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="84">昵称的优化路径很简单，修改用户昵称，<strong>昵称和关键词吻合</strong>，让昵称里含有关键词。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="86">虽然昵称的操作难度低，但是对数据的影响力巨大。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="88"><span style="letter-spacing: 1.5px;"><strong><span style="color: #7e44f8; --tt-darkmode-color: #7E44F8;"><span style="background-color: #f4f1ff; --tt-darkmode-bgcolor: #BEBCC7;">2.合集</span></span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="89">做合集的好处就在于，<strong>合集里的视频播放量和点赞量可以累加</strong>，而视频的播放，点赞数据对优化SEO是至关重要的。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="91"><span style="letter-spacing: 1.5px;"><strong><span style="color: #7e44f8; --tt-darkmode-color: #7E44F8;"><span style="background-color: #f4f1ff; --tt-darkmode-bgcolor: #BEBCC7;">3.话题+标题</span></span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="92">一个抖音作品的标题或者话题一共能输入500个字符，我们可以<strong>将选词阶段确认的关键词和联想词都放进去</strong>。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="94">有的人可能会担心影响美观，但要注意，关键词其实是有助于系统识别关联度，提升优化效果。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;" data-track="96"><span style="letter-spacing: 1.5px;"><strong><span style="color: #7e44f8; --tt-darkmode-color: #7E44F8;"><span style="background-color: #f4f1ff; --tt-darkmode-bgcolor: #BEBCC7;">4.视频文案</span></span></strong></span></p>
