<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E6%96%B0%E8%8C%B6%E9%A5%AE" target="_blank">新茶饮</a>市场中，网红品牌层出不穷，在城市重要的商圈均可以看见令年轻人痴迷的新茶饮门店，如喜茶、兵立王、茶颜悦色、书亦烧仙草、沪上阿姨、<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E5%A5%88%E9%9B%AA%E7%9A%84%E8%8C%B6" target="_blank">奈雪的茶</a>等品牌。随着新茶饮品牌的崛起，中国的新茶饮市场呈现出高端化、标准化、时尚性与品牌态度明确等特征，同时不少新茶饮品牌被网红化，自带流量与传播力，也将新茶饮的营销与品牌形象、气质、名气推向了新高度。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>可以说，现在的新茶饮市场已经从高速发展趋于平稳，市场的竞争也愈加激烈，开始进入优胜劣汰时期。那些拥有规模化、时尚性、体验佳、个性化以及品牌营销特色鲜明的品牌，在市场中总能够占据优势地位。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>为了抢占更为优势的竞争地位，让品牌在消费者心中占领一席之地，品牌总是不遗余力的玩营销，希望在流量与声量上获得突破式的双增长。</span><span></span></p>

<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 18px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;"><strong>0</strong><span data-original-title title><strong>1、</strong></span><strong style="text-align: inherit; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', Roboto, Oxygen, Ubuntu, Cantarell, 'Open Sans', 'Helvetica Neue', sans-serif;">凭借「头像营销」成功出圈</strong></h2>

<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>继喜茶出猫窝、炖物24章出姨妈热饮后，兵法先生发现了新茶饮品牌收割年轻人钱包的营销大法，开始在品牌形象上玩突破，玩起了头像营销。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span><a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E8%9C%9C%E9%9B%AA%E5%86%B0%E5%9F%8E" target="_blank">蜜雪冰城</a>：雪王变黑</span></strong><span></span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>最近，「蜜雪冰城黑化」冲上了微博热搜榜NO.1，蜜雪冰城凭借具有人格化的可爱呆萌的品牌IP雪王，将其形象从之前的白色变成了黑色。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/52123012562b4c9412971e7.55844155.png" title="新茶饮找到了财富密码，蜜雪冰城变“黑”，奈雪的茶“摆烂”" alt="新茶饮找到了财富密码，蜜雪冰城变“黑”，奈雪的茶“摆烂”" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在微博、外卖平台以及App上，品牌的头像也随之发生了变化，均统一变成了黑色，而引发了网友的围观。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/27323094062b4c9414e5ad1.73667075.jpeg" title="新茶饮找到了财富密码，蜜雪冰城变“黑”，奈雪的茶“摆烂”" alt="新茶饮找到了财富密码，蜜雪冰城变“黑”，奈雪的茶“摆烂”" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>蜜雪冰城在其形象上改变后，并没有对外界公布品牌的营销动作。而是玩起了事件营销，在微博上推出了“盲猜”，「蜜雪冰城黑化」究竟为何？</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>随着关注此事件的网友越来越多，也将蜜雪冰城黑化推向了高潮。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/57582355362b4c9417aabd1.62749376.png" title="新茶饮找到了财富密码，蜜雪冰城变“黑”，奈雪的茶“摆烂”" alt="新茶饮找到了财富密码，蜜雪冰城变“黑”，奈雪的茶“摆烂”" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>网友也十分配合品牌，在网络上发散思维，研究品牌此举的目的，纷纷发表了自己的“见解”，评论也是妙趣横生。如，“黑色显瘦”、“众筹给雪王买瓶防晒喷雾”、“晒黑了”、“被收购”、“将出新品”等，还有网友猜测到品牌“是否要涨价”，等，随着盲猜的网友增加，也为品牌积攒了一大波流量。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/193319511462b4c94197c656.93590178.png" title="新茶饮找到了财富密码，蜜雪冰城变“黑”，奈雪的茶“摆烂”" alt="新茶饮找到了财富密码，蜜雪冰城变“黑”，奈雪的茶“摆烂”" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/64573956562b4c941bf9482.23334292.png" title="新茶饮找到了财富密码，蜜雪冰城变“黑”，奈雪的茶“摆烂”" alt="新茶饮找到了财富密码，蜜雪冰城变“黑”，奈雪的茶“摆烂”" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/212107736862b4c941ec8353.60828389.png" title="新茶饮找到了财富密码，蜜雪冰城变“黑”，奈雪的茶“摆烂”" alt="新茶饮找到了财富密码，蜜雪冰城变“黑”，奈雪的茶“摆烂”" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/133140392662b4c9421d4e80.77093782.png" title="新茶饮找到了财富密码，蜜雪冰城变“黑”，奈雪的茶“摆烂”" alt="新茶饮找到了财富密码，蜜雪冰城变“黑”，奈雪的茶“摆烂”" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/190091690962b4c9424b7a21.12528473.png" title="新茶饮找到了财富密码，蜜雪冰城变“黑”，奈雪的茶“摆烂”" alt="新茶饮找到了财富密码，蜜雪冰城变“黑”，奈雪的茶“摆烂”" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/80742562162b4c94283cae9.59645614.png" title="新茶饮找到了财富密码，蜜雪冰城变“黑”，奈雪的茶“摆烂”" alt="新茶饮找到了财富密码，蜜雪冰城变“黑”，奈雪的茶“摆烂”" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在<span>“盲猜”经过网络的发酵后，品牌找准时机</span>亲自下场，在微博上呼吁网友一起在线“破案”，究竟蜜雪冰城的变黑是为什么，而将事件推向了更高潮。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/92580317862b4c942bc8cc1.07900198.png" title="新茶饮找到了财富密码，蜜雪冰城变“黑”，奈雪的茶“摆烂”" alt="新茶饮找到了财富密码，蜜雪冰城变“黑”，奈雪的茶“摆烂”" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>而蜜雪冰城究竟为何变黑，品牌如柯南破案一样，在微博上表示“真相只有一个，雪王去桑葚园摘桑葚被晒黑了”而将事件营销推向了新的高潮。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/168830793762b4c942e92572.17761479.png" title="新茶饮找到了财富密码，蜜雪冰城变“黑”，奈雪的茶“摆烂”" alt="新茶饮找到了财富密码，蜜雪冰城变“黑”，奈雪的茶“摆烂”" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>一波又一波的关注度，也将#蜜雪冰城黑化#推向了更高的关注度，让此事件营销获得了5.4亿的阅读，7.4万的讨论，而蜜雪冰城也在继“蜜雪冰城甜蜜蜜”的爆火后，推出的具有教科书级别的案例。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/169918030562b4c94332be15.45367568.jpeg" title="新茶饮找到了财富密码，蜜雪冰城变“黑”，奈雪的茶“摆烂”" alt="新茶饮找到了财富密码，蜜雪冰城变“黑”，奈雪的茶“摆烂”" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>奈雪的茶：摆烂头像</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>无独有偶，除了蜜雪冰城，凭借「头像营销」成功出圈的，还有奈雪的茶。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>不知道从什么时候开始，摆烂成为了一些年轻人自黑的网络流行语，而为了更接近年用用户圈层，<span>奈雪的茶推出了“<span>摆</span><span>烂</span><span>头像</span>”，在其形象的设计中，品牌以“摆烂桃”为设计的主角，凭借俏皮可爱的形象，成功赢得了用户的注意。</span></span><span><span></span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/100107950462b4c9437f3218.99220063.jpeg" title="新茶饮找到了财富密码，蜜雪冰城变“黑”，奈雪的茶“摆烂”" alt="新茶饮找到了财富密码，蜜雪冰城变“黑”，奈雪的茶“摆烂”" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><span><span>蜜雪冰城的摆烂头像，并非一出现就刷爆了网络。首先，品牌在美团上日更头像，成功吸引了网友的注意，令不少消费者开始去关注品牌日更的头像，并产生了追更一族，，有人猜测，难道是要出新品？同时，还有人喊话奈雪的出表情包。</span></span></span><span><span><span></span></span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><span><span>作为十分宠粉的品牌，奈雪的茶自然有求必应，<span>「少来这桃</span><span>」<span>「今日宜摆烂</span><span>」<span>「桃<span>爸</span></span><span>」<span>「桃妈</span><span>」<span>「桃烂</span><span>」等表情包纷纷上线。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/11055457062b4c9439daa82.20092264.jpeg" title="新茶饮找到了财富密码，蜜雪冰城变“黑”，奈雪的茶“摆烂”" alt="新茶饮找到了财富密码，蜜雪冰城变“黑”，奈雪的茶“摆烂”" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span>这种迎合用户喜好的宠粉方式，更容易赢得用户的心，而粉丝也像追连续剧一样，去官方下载品牌的表情包，忙的不亦乐乎。也</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span><span>让以桃为原料的新品</span><span>「霸气一升桃</span><span>」成功出圈。具奈雪的茶数据显示，桃子系列的产品销售已经超100万杯。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/45083031962b4c943d9c5a7.79112574.jpeg" title="新茶饮找到了财富密码，蜜雪冰城变“黑”，奈雪的茶“摆烂”" alt="新茶饮找到了财富密码，蜜雪冰城变“黑”，奈雪的茶“摆烂”" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>奈雪的茶凭借营销，成功戳中年轻人的G点，实现品牌与消费者玩在了一起，为品牌赢得了流量，并成功实现动销。</span></p>
