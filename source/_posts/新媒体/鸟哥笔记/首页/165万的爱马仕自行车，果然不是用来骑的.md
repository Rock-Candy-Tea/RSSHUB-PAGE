<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E5%A5%A2%E4%BE%88%E5%93%81" target="_blank">奢侈品</a>界的<span>疯狂</span>故事，从不缺席。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span><a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E5%B7%B4%E9%BB%8E%E4%B8%96%E5%AE%B6" target="_blank">巴黎世家</a>1.2万元的垃圾袋刚退场，</span></strong><strong><span>爱马仕</span></strong><strong><span>16.5万元的自行</span></strong><strong><span>车又接力。</span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>前几天爱马仕上线了一款Odyssee Terre的载重小型单车，还配备了前后2个车筐。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/180194003662fa701645cc22.03918303.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span>图源：微博 股市论金</span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span> </span><span>当你还在好奇“这么贵的自行车谁会买<span>”时</span>，</span><span><strong>爱马仕消费者已经被告知，</strong><strong>北京已无库存也没展品<span>，</span>上海的线下门店已经售罄。</strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong> </strong></span><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/25500100862fa7016a0e642.09905177.png" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span>图源：微博网友评论</span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><span>爱马仕中国官网“查无此车<span>”</span>，而</span>客服对于抢光的回应，同样很凡尔赛<span>：</span></span><span><strong>没有调货服务，后续会从法国来货，来货时间无法确定。</strong></span></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;">01</h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>“配骑”还是“配货” </span></strong><span> </span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>把这辆天价自行车买回家，究竟是用来骑的摆的，还是供起来的？</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>懂行的早就看懂了，</span></strong><strong><span>低情商是捆绑销售，高情商则是配货。</span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>爱马仕<span>的</span>配货是奢侈品界的潜规则，还有长期配货、短期配货说法。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>所谓的“<span>短期配货<span>”，</span></span>简单来说，你可能不经常买爱马仕，有一天你去爱马仕专柜购买心仪的商品，</span><span><strong><span>那必须在门店消费和货品一定比例的杂物。</span></strong></span><span> </span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>比如配饰、皮具，这个比例1:0.5-1:2，热门商品的配货比列会设定更高，最高据说可以达到1:4。</span><span><strong><span> </span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/73364036062fa7016ee8b84.19788693.jpeg" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span>图源：TMagazineChina微博</span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>假设你看中定价为10万的手袋，以1:1.5的配货比<span>来计算的话</span>，<span>需要先购买价值</span>15<span>万</span>的其它商品，才能顺利将这个手袋拿下。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>长期配货，就是你不以买包为目的，但偶尔去爱马仕门店逛逛，买上各种东西，积累一定额度的消费记录。</span></strong></span><span> </span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>爱马仕的柜姐确认过消费记录，就会认为你<span>值得拥有爱马仕</span>。不是为<span>了买包而买包，而是真正</span>懂品牌，且具备一定的消费潜力<span>。</span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><span>那么柜姐</span>下次就会主动提醒你：“你心仪的包包这有”。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>所以买包加上配货，最终买单随时翻个倍。</span><span> </span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong>爱马仕的配货制度也常被诟病，每一只爱马仕包包背后，都有个配到哭的</strong><strong>故事。</strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>虽然爱马仕早就否认配货——只要有库存、没被预定、不是陈列品，就会卖给消费者。</span><span>但去年有消费者在爱马仕北京skp门店、上海国金店先后抗议，原因是他们在爱马仕按要求配货后，依旧没有拿到想要的手袋。</span><span> </span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/24361831562fa7017536096.15002016.jpeg" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: center; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>图源：壹矜</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>所以不要再怪奢侈品，总出一些怨种边角料产品。毕竟定价16.5万的爱马仕，不是用来考虑配不配骑的，而是用来配货的。</span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/10058066462fa701799ea87.04707374.jpeg" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: center; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>图源：玩转全球免税Globuy</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>你<span>可能</span>觉得爱马仕是在PUA消费者，<span>不配货就不让买，</span>但事实证明，爱马仕把配货套路玩明白了。</span><span> </span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>2022年上半年，<span>手袋等皮具作为爱马仕最主要的收入来源，同比大涨12%，<span>占总收入的</span>43%。而成衣和配饰类商品，同比大涨36%，占比为26.6%，珠宝和家居用品收入同样大涨32.5%。</span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>不难看出配货制度，除了让爱马仕<span>筛选出更精准的高净值人群</span>，还利用热门</span><span>商品带动周边商品，平衡了爱马仕不同品类的销售，保证了好看的业绩。</span></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span>02</span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>爱马仕不做“挨骂仕” </span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>爱马仕搞配货，不是为了落得个<span>“</span>挨骂仕”称号，更像是对身份象征<span>的坚持：让<span>多数人仰望，少数人拥有。</span></span></span><span> </span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>还记得《三十而已》里的名场面吗？</span><span> </span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/17199125562fa7017d2cca3.17737170.jpeg" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: center; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>图源：公众号 新饰觉时尚</span><span> </span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>顾佳背了一款限量款香奈儿包，但<span>在其他<span>太太</span>清一色的爱马仕面前，依然显得格格不入，还</span>被阔太们<span>从合影中裁掉</span>。</span><span>后来顾佳为了进入阔太的圈子，拜托王漫妮找买爱马仕的门路。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/185903117262fa70181aa8a9.50655618.jpeg" width="555" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: center; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>图源：小红书截图</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在电视剧里，爱马仕成了贵妇圈的入场券。</span><span> </span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>有网友曾制作奢侈品鄙视链图，爱马仕站在<span>鄙视链的顶端，将LV、迪奥等品牌甩在下面</span>。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/83680446962fa7018706c47.45599715.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span>图源：豆瓣</span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>现在我们形容某个东西是<span>品类中的顶级，就会戏称它</span>是<span>“xxx中的爱马仕”</span>，足以见得爱马仕的高贵地位。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>因为除了制定配货，爱马仕还讲了工匠精神的故事。</span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>1883年，爱马仕家族开了第一家店铺，主要卖马鞍、缰绳、骑马。</span><span> </span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>“马儿佩戴爱马仕的马鞍，是马主人地位的象征”。</span></strong></span><span>以马具起家的爱马仕，造就了品牌的高起点。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>马具制造对匠人的要求非常严苛，这为<span>爱马仕日后在皮具制作上的精益求精，奠定了技术基础。</span></span><span> </span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>爱马仕旗下有超过<span><strong>4500名</strong></span>工匠，新加入的工匠要接受长达5年的训练时间。</span></strong></span><span> </span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>不同于其他大牌，一个包包可能是由一群工匠们流水线分工。但爱马仕不仅</span><span>不走生产线，坚持纯手工制作，</span><span>而且一个手包从头至尾，都是由一个工匠负责。</span><span> </span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>制作完的包包，还要印上工匠名字和年份，就像是打上独一无二的身份证。<span>所以</span><span>即便卖出去，往后包包要维修，专柜也会找回对应工匠。</span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/130867725462fa7018cedfe2.89484182.jpeg" width="555" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px;"><span><span>①代表工匠 ②代表年份/</span>图源：ChannelChic时尚瑰视</span></h4>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>一个Birkin需要费一位工匠48小时，也就有了“一个爱马仕一个月仅能生产15只手袋”的说法。这在一定程度上，造就了产品的稀缺性。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>与其说爱马仕的配货初衷，</span></strong><strong><span>是为了促进销售，倒不如说爱马仕包包制作工艺复杂、周期长，有钱也未必能等到。</span></strong></span></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><span><strong><span>03</span></strong></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>包治百病不是假象 </span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>爱马仕“一包难求”从来不是假象。</span><span> </span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>爱马仕上半年财报数据显示，截至6月底的上半年内，爱马仕收入同比大涨23%至54.75亿欧元，营业利润率达42%。</span></p>
