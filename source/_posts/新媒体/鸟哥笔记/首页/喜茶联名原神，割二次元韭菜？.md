<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>各位好，这里是头条哥。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>国产大热游戏<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E5%8E%9F%E7%A5%9E" target="_blank">原神</a>又和<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E5%96%9C%E8%8C%B6" target="_blank">喜茶</a>联动了，推出了联动果汁的购买渠道和方法。虽然有变相清库存的嫌疑，但喜茶的果汁一上市，销量还是马上冲到了数万。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/212171110063173b2b9b3a33.65571176.jpeg" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>这已经不是原神第一次展现出它作为热门IP的强大号召力了，就在月初，原神和必胜客的联动活动导致多家必胜客爆满，</span><span><strong>现场排队人数堪比核酸队伍。</strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/115822253363173b2bc51ab7.19212441.jpeg" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>甚至活动刚一开始，必胜客的下单服务器就因承受不了巨大的访问量直接崩溃了。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/171065974663173b2bef1808.67847109.jpeg" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>早些时候，同是百胜中国旗下的餐饮品牌肯德基与原神联名，也收获到了相同的效果，原神主题店客流得到了极大的提升。<br></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/89914213863173b2c3c7001.01274156.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>品牌方们自然嗅到了原神ip背后的商业价值，与必胜客联动尚未结束，喜茶就宣布了和原神的联名。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/122289740863173b2cacd419.04499468.png" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>另外，便利店品牌罗森、高德APP也相继被曝出将与原神进行联动。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/91971099263173b2cf28cf3.60943960.jpeg" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>原神玩家9月现状：</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/65986568463173b2d33d960.51725055.jpeg" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>而原神为什么能成为品牌竞相合作的对象，头条哥认为有两方面的原因。</span></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><strong><span> 国产游戏最强IP </span></strong><span></span></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>原神作为一款开放世界游戏，</span><span><strong>拥有众多人气很高的角色，这可以使原神与各品牌之间的联动实现差异化，突出每次联动的独特性，</strong></span><span>不会使联动的主要受众——原神玩家有疲劳感。</span><span> </span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>比方说与肯德基联动时，游戏角色是诺尔艾和迪卢克：</span><span><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/147595130663173b2d54f4d4.13960905.jpeg" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>必胜客的联动角色是优菈和安柏：<br></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/118007321063173b2d823244.15768859.jpeg" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>喜茶的联动角色是神里绫人和神里绫华：<br></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/176650833363173b2dbd15c9.41237629.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>虽然角色众多，但完全不影响原神IP整体的号召力。</span><span><strong>原神带给品牌的流量，甚至能与当红明星代言代言相媲美。</strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>据易观千帆五月数据，原神玩家中，中等及中高消费者占据60%以上，集中于新一线、一线、二线城市，这些消费人群基本也和众多品牌的主要目标受众相重合，选择与原神联名，</span><span><strong>很快就能触达到两个品牌共同的用户。</strong></span></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-size: 18px; color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; padding-left: 12px;"><strong><span> 游戏机制带来线下红利 </span></strong></h2>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>作为开放世界游戏，原神的机制比较趋向于单机游戏，开发方米哈游主要看重的是玩家对开放世界的探索，而忽视了玩家交流和团队协作的需求，尽管游戏可以多人联机，但各种条件的限制无疑阻碍了玩家间的沟通。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><strong><span>这种游戏机制使得原神玩家很希望参与到线下的交流中，寻求相同爱好玩家的沟通，增加了原神玩家对线下联动活动参与的热情。</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>与原神联动的各个品牌方，也都会安排相应的线下活动参与方案，用以为玩家营造氛围感，提升玩家的参与感。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/1383120363173b2e0c80c7.44012621.jpeg" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>即便是听起来很社死的口号，在一众原神玩家的包围喊出来，反而会有不一样的感觉。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>玩家的这种心理为联动的品牌带来了持续稳定可预期的线下客流，可以说，只要和原神联名，线下店铺就能收获超出预期的ROI。<br></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>品牌与原神联名，是稳赚不赔的买卖。</span><strong><span>但对于原神的开发方米哈游来说，频繁而又欠缺规划的联名则是在贬损IP的商业价值。</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>众多玩家的热情在一次次方案欠妥的联动中被消耗殆尽，许多人也开始抱怨为联名花钱，不如把钱直接氪到游戏里去。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/47610465063173b2e3d1cd9.57002621.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/44307869863173b2e65d5f5.34572410.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>甚至当时被吐槽最多的肯德基，反而成了出道即巅峰的良心：</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/126937233163173b2e92e860.80875455.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>玩家的抱怨，也提醒着米哈游，在利开发自身IP商业价值的同时，也应有长远的眼光，慎重制定联动策略方案，维护好原神IP的价值。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>毕竟，无论是品牌方还是米哈游，都不能把玩家当傻子。</span></p>
