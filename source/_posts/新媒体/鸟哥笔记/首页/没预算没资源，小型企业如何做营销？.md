                        <p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">历来，我们谈营销，谈的都是大企业、大品牌、大预算。以大众熟悉的乳品企业来看，2021年伊利营销费用126亿、蒙牛营销费用72亿、飞鹤营销费用67亿。巨额的营销费用，可以换来奥运冠名、可以换来明星代言、也可以换来“不是所有牛奶，都叫特仑苏”。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">我们的小企业没这么多预算，还要不要做营销呢？</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">我的观点是要做，而且必须做！</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">目前市场上，产品同质化越来越严重，大家都是差不多的渠道资源、差不多的产品功能。如果拿不到好的渠道资源，也做不到自身的技术创新，就很难做到在行业内的差异化。最终只能陷入价格战，甚至在已经超低利润的底价情况下，客户仍不买账。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">我认为这种情况下，以打造“品牌差异化”为目的的营销方式。是解决此类困境，最好的答案了。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><img class="rich_pages wxw-img" style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 0px auto; list-style: none; border: 0px; vertical-align: middle; outline: none; position: relative; max-width: 100%; display: block;" src="https://file.adquan.com/pic/1661416092cd8664f5ef2a1259.jpg" data-ratio="0.4254335260115607" data-type="png" data-w="865" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h1 style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #212629; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 20px; background-color: #ffffff; text-align: center; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 0px; list-style: none; color: #00b0f0; font-size: 20px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 0px; list-style: none; font-weight: bold;">一、做品牌的价值</span></span></h1>
<h2 style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #212629; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 18px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 0px; list-style: none; font-weight: bold;">1、价值一：建立客户对产品的信任度</span></h2>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">我们日常消费的时候，为什么会优先选择熟悉的牌子（品牌）呢？最简单的想法，可能就是品质有保障，即使产品出了问题，我们能找到人去维权，他们还得做生意，不至于跑了。多么简单的想法。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">但简单想法背后，其实体现了品牌的第一个价值：信任度。</p>
<h2 style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #212629; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 18px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 0px; list-style: none; font-weight: bold;">2、价值二：降低用户的选择难度</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 0px; list-style: none; font-weight: bold;"> </span></h2>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">如果我们去吃四川火锅，我们经常喝的饮料是什么？很多人可能会想到是王老吉。为什么是王老吉，因为“怕上火，喝王老吉”。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">如果我们现在困得不行，后边又有重要的会议参加，怎么办？“累了、困了，喝红牛”可能会自动飘到我们的脑海。类似的还有“经常用脑，就喝六个核桃”、“送礼就送脑白金”。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">这些品牌，直接把产品使用的场景与品牌形象，深度捆绑在一起。当你处于什么场景，自然会想到这个品牌。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">这其实体现了品牌的第二个价值：降低用户的选择难度。</p>
<h2 style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #212629; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 18px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 0px; list-style: none; font-weight: bold;">3、价值三：增加产品的溢价</span></h2>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">赚取合理的利润，才能保证企业长久地经营下去，如果一味采用低价竞争的策略，本质是在透支企业的健康状态。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">下图是香奈儿2019年出的一款水晶拖鞋，售价675美元，实际产品成本，大家自行体会。但是品牌的理念，一旦受到客户的认可，与客户产生价值观层面的链接，客户是会自愿接受品牌溢价的。更准确地说，客户是在为自己的理念买单。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><img class="rich_pages wxw-img" style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 0px auto; list-style: none; border: 0px; vertical-align: middle; outline: none; position: relative; max-width: 100%; display: block;" src="https://file.adquan.com/pic/166141609293e3ad43ac61b304.jpg" data-backh="295" data-backw="327" data-ratio="0.9021406727828746" data-type="png" data-w="327" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>

<h1 style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #212629; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 20px; background-color: #ffffff; text-align: center; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 0px; list-style: none; font-size: 20px; color: #00b0f0;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 0px; list-style: none; font-weight: bold;">二、小企业如何做<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E5%93%81%E7%89%8C%E8%90%A5%E9%94%80" target="_blank">品牌营销</a></span></span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 0px; list-style: none; font-size: 20px; color: #00b0f0;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 0px; list-style: none; font-weight: bold;"> </span></span></h1>
<h2 style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #212629; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 18px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 0px; list-style: none; font-weight: bold;">1、预算有限下的“流量池思维”</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 0px; list-style: none; font-weight: bold;"> </span></h2>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">小企业没有大的预算做活动，吸引新客户，那我们更应该珍惜，已有的老客户了。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">老客户聚合为私域流量池，通过运营老客户带来新客户的成本，远比直接获得一位新客户的成本低得多。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">从“从存量中找增量”是流量池思维的核心理念。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">瑞幸咖啡以“流量池思维”作为核心的营销与用户增长的战略理念，从17年10月成立至20年5月，实现了用户从0-3000万的增长。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><img class="rich_pages wxw-img" style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 0px auto; list-style: none; border: 0px; vertical-align: middle; outline: none; position: relative; max-width: 100%; display: block;" src="https://file.adquan.com/pic/16614160935e9c7f380cbde0b0.jpg" data-ratio="0.42592592592592593" data-type="png" data-w="864" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h2 style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #212629; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 18px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 0px; list-style: none; font-weight: bold;">2、打造品牌需要做什么？</span></h2>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 0px; list-style: none; font-weight: bold;">（1）品牌定位</span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">品牌定位需要考虑三个维度，第一是企业自身，我们擅长或者拥有什么？技术、渠道、服务，或者独特的价值观；</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">第二是客户，我们的客户群是谁，他们的年龄、性别、城市、思考习惯等等；</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">第三是竞争对手，我们打造品牌的初衷，便是可以与竞争对手形成差异化，品牌定位中这是需要考虑的。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><img class="rich_pages wxw-img" style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 0px auto; list-style: none; border: 0px; vertical-align: middle; outline: none; position: relative; max-width: 100%; display: block;" src="https://file.adquan.com/pic/16614160933f9c3ea13d1158cc.jpg" data-ratio="0.4254335260115607" data-type="png" data-w="865" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 0px; list-style: none; font-weight: bold;">（2）打造品牌符号体系</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 0px; list-style: none; font-weight: bold;"> </span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">A、slogan：当我们对品牌进行定位之后，需凝炼出一句，易于企业宣传和客户记忆的slogan。好的slogan，可以让宣传效果加倍。比如，农夫山泉的“我们不生产水，我们只是大自然的搬运工”。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">B、Logo：logo主色调选择、使用文字还是图形等等，都要与整体品牌定位相符。可口可乐的红色代表着经典、百事可乐的蓝色代表年轻。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">C、产品包装：江小白的文案瓶“手机里的人已经坐在对面，你怎么还盯着手机”，茅台的经典白瓷瓶。江小白是年轻人小酌，茅台是成年人的情意，不同的包装同样传达着不同的理念。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">D、代言人：小企业固然请不起明星代言，但是老板或者每个在外的业务员，都是企业的代言人，你们的行为举止便代表着企业的文化理念。</p>
<h2 style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #212629; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 18px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 0px; list-style: none; font-weight: bold;">3、怎么才能将品牌推广出去</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 0px; list-style: none; font-weight: bold;"> </span></h2>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 0px; list-style: none; font-weight: bold;">（1）建设公司互联网媒体矩阵</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 0px; list-style: none; font-weight: bold;"> </span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">我们现在听说一个品牌之后，我们接下来会做什么，去百度搜一搜、在微信搜一搜、在小红书搜一搜、在抖音搜一搜。我们想通过各平台了解这个品牌，尤其做重大购买决策时，比如房产、汽车、数码产品、教育服务等。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">如果这时用户在百度搜索，有你的百度百科、有新闻资讯；在微信搜索，有你微信公众号发布的品牌介绍、还有某个好友转发过朋友圈；小红书有人分享使用的体验；抖音上你每天在分享公司的日常。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">信任度便会随内容的积累而积累，有时只靠销售员是不够的。我曾经为公司运营过20几个媒体账号，主要就是把公司公众号里的内容，复制到这20几个媒体账号。像搜狐号、网易号、百度号以及一些垂直类媒体，被百度收录的机会非常大。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">曾经我们部门一位同事，就是因为看到公司在各平台有这么多内容，便入职了公司，他后来一直说被我忽悠了，当然他后来也成了我工作以来最好的几个朋友之一。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">建设媒体矩阵对于小企业是友好的，账号都可免费注册，也不需要请太专业的人去运营，用最简单的方式，把品牌理念、品牌符号体系、品牌案例、品牌故事、品牌活动，这些传达给用户即可。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><img class="rich_pages wxw-img" style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 0px auto; list-style: none; border: 0px; vertical-align: middle; outline: none; position: relative; max-width: 100%; display: block;" src="https://file.adquan.com/pic/1661416093d524a50a40202e61.jpg" data-backh="244" data-backw="578" data-ratio="0.4212962962962963" data-type="png" data-w="864" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"> </p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 0px; list-style: none; font-weight: bold;">（2）寻找免费的资源互换机会</span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">可以找一些，同样有宣传需求的品牌，看是否有免费资源互换的机会。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">一类是，有共同客群，但业务不重合的品牌。比如一个啤酒品牌，新出了一款产品，那么可以找一家烤串店，联合做一个活动，烧烤店可以利用店内流量，换取啤酒的优惠，啤酒品牌得到曝光。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">再一类是，不同客群，业务重合的品牌。比如LV与Supreme的联名，LV想拓展喜欢潮牌的年轻客群，Supreme想要提升自己的高端品牌调性。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 0px; list-style: none; font-weight: bold;">（3）选择付费媒体时，以人为中心的媒介策略</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 0px; list-style: none; font-weight: bold;"> </span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">比如，瑞幸咖啡早期发展时的媒介策略，给人的观感是铺天盖地都是广告。但据说，其实瑞幸的广告投放是非常克制的，只是针对潜在用户群做了精细化投放策略，并没有做全媒体平铺轰炸的模式。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">但我们日常的印象就是，早上出门电梯能看到瑞幸、地铁里刷微博可以看到瑞幸、到公司楼下瑞幸又给你推送了优惠券、到了公司电梯又看到了的瑞幸。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; list-style: none; font-variant-ligatures: none; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Lantinghei SC', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'HanHei SC', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Open Sans', Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 微软雅黑, STHeiti, 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei'; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">总结去看，瑞幸就是针对其核心潜在用户群体（一二线城市白领为主），围绕其生活和工作的环境做了重复的媒体覆盖。传统的媒介策略往往是将一类媒体打透，比如在微博，将前200名的KOL都投一遍，再配合无数的KOC活跃互动，在微博平台形成品牌舆论场，从而引发微博用户的关注，再向外部平台扩展声量。</p>
