<p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.5; color: #5d5d5d; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;">ASA 广告上线中国大陆区已满一年，这一年来为了让广告主们更好地投放广告， ASA 广告后台多次进行调整，从多次更新广告指南，到新增多项功能，再到升级多项服务，都或多或少影响着广告投放效果。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.5; color: #5d5d5d; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">近日，为了帮助广告主更快一步发掘有效关键词，Apple ads 团队通过邮件发布了一份《持续发掘有效的关键词》的实践指南：</strong></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.5; color: #5d5d5d; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;">一是创建发现型广告，通过<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #027aff; box-sizing: border-box;">搜索匹配、广泛匹配、完全匹配</span>等匹配机制，拓展相关性更高的热门关键词，并利用关键词来保持获客势头，不断提高广告效果。<br style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.5; color: #5d5d5d; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;">二是善用“<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #027aff; box-sizing: border-box;">建议</span>”该实用工具，让用户更快一步发现广告主的 App，同时关键词建议可以为广告主提供与效果理想的关键词相仿的建议，帮助拓展更多关键词。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 1em 0px; padding-top: 0px; padding-right: 4px; padding-bottom: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.8; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; letter-spacing: 0px; background-color: #ffffff; font-size: 11px; color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box;"> </p>
<h2 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: left; line-height: 30px; color: #212629; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 18px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; font-weight: 600;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;">- No. -</span><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">01  </strong><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">创建</strong><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">发现型广告系列</strong></h2>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.5; color: #5d5d5d; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;">创建发现型广告系列，Apple Ads 能让广告主的广告自动匹配相关搜索，帮你触达更广泛的受众群体，并找到可转化为关键词的热门搜索词。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: center; line-height: 1.5; color: #5d5d5d; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 1em 0px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; display: inline-block; text-align: justify; line-height: 0; color: #5d5d5d; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; max-width: 100%; vertical-align: middle; border-radius: 10px; overflow: hidden; box-sizing: border-box; border: 1px solid #2ee8da;"><img class="rich_pages wxw-img" style="margin: 0px auto; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; display: block; clear: both;" src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/a3ae7f0aacb602cc12f1be609bb4aa21.png" width="1320" height="908" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.5; color: #5d5d5d; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.5; color: #5d5d5d; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">#1  确定方法</strong></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.5; color: #5d5d5d; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"> </strong></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.5; color: #5d5d5d; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;">单个发现型广告系列能助你轻松掌握 App 搜索趋势，并找到营销人员尚未考虑过的相关关键词变体。假若想按地点或营销目标分开管理预算，则需创建多个发现型广告系列。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.5; color: #5d5d5d; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;">此外，当营销人员开拓新市场，或推出新服务、新功能或新内容时，专用于发现关键词的广告系列或许会更加奏效。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.5; color: #5d5d5d; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 1em 0px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; display: inline-block; text-align: justify; line-height: 0; color: #5d5d5d; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; max-width: 100%; vertical-align: middle; box-sizing: border-box;"><img src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/ad02f0a6d7866ad4936601f4cceba42d.png" alt width="907" height="560" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.5; color: #5d5d5d; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.5; color: #5d5d5d; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">#2 创建发现型广告组</strong></span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 0px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.5; color: #5d5d5d; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.5; color: #5d5d5d; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;">广告主想让发现型广告系列取得出色效果，需在其中创建两个专用广告组，即搜索匹配广告组和广告匹配广告组。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.5; color: #5d5d5d; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 14px;"> </span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.5; color: #5d5d5d; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 14px;">（1）使用搜索匹配广告组</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.5; color: #5d5d5d; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 14px;"> </span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.5; color: #5d5d5d; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;">通过搜索匹配节省时间单独创建一个搜索匹配广告组，让广告自动匹配相关搜索词，并找到下载效果好的新关键词。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.5; color: #5d5d5d; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;">需要注意的是，搜索匹配机制是根据产品的元数据、同一类型中类似 App 的相关信息以及其他搜索数据，将广告与 App Store 中的相关搜索进行自动动匹配。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.5; color: #5d5d5d; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;"> </span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.5; color: #5d5d5d; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 14px;">（2）使用广泛匹配广告组</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.5; color: #5d5d5d; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 14px;"> </span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.5; color: #5d5d5d; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;">使用广泛匹配，广告主无需对详尽的关键词列表进行出价和管理，即可充分利用最新的搜索趋势、峰期和长尾词。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.5; color: #5d5d5d; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;">首先，广告主需要将其他广告系列中的完全匹配关键词，添加为发现型广告组中的广泛匹配关键词。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.5; color: #5d5d5d; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;">其次，所创建的广告将匹配与这些关键词紧密相关的搜索<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 12px; color: #027aff; box-sizing: border-box;">（例如单数、复数、拼写错误、同义词、相关搜索以及全部或部分包含该词语的词组）</span>，进而能轻松地发现更多效果良好的相关变体。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.5; color: #5d5d5d; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box;"> </p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.5; color: #5d5d5d; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">#3  添加完全匹配否定关键词</strong></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.5; color: #5d5d5d; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"> </strong></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.5; color: #5d5d5d; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;">为了避免广告匹配到已出价的关键词，广告主需要请将品牌和类别广告系列中所有的完全匹配关键词，作为完全匹配否定关键词，添加到发现型广告系列中。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.5; color: #5d5d5d; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;">需要注意的是，每次向其他广告系列添加新的完全匹配关键词时，请务必在发现型广告系列中将它们添加为完全匹配否定关键词。为了节省时间，你可以批量上传否定关键词。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 1em 0px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; display: inline-block; text-align: justify; line-height: 0; color: #5d5d5d; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; max-width: 100%; vertical-align: middle; border-radius: 10px; overflow: hidden; box-sizing: border-box; border: 1px solid #2ee8da;"><img src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/a5eb02f2e24db13fe5e4826cfd515b84.png" alt width="1101" height="411" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
