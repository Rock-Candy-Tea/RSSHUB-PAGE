<section>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; color: #212629; font-size: 18px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat no-repeat;">1、广告媒体的诉求</h2>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">广告受众、广告媒体和广告主是广告领域的3大主体。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">广告媒体基于一定规则，在流量变现和用户体验之间动态平衡获取广告收益。同时，广告受众和广告主各取所需并为之付出成本。</p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">广告请求数，即用户在浏览到广告位时，客户端向服务器发起广告请求数，服务器下发符合广告受众的广告。</p>

<h2 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; color: #212629; font-size: 18px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;">2、广告填充率</h2>
<p style="font-size: 16px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-variant-ligatures: normal; letter-spacing: 0.544px; orphans: 2; text-align: inherit; widows: 2; background-color: #ffffff; text-decoration-thickness: initial; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">影响广告填充率的因素有广告位、广告素材、广告曝光率、广告相关性、广告创意性等等。</p>
<section style="font-size: 16px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; font-family: PingFangSC-light; font-variant-ligatures: normal; orphans: 2; text-align: justify; widows: 2; background-color: #ffffff; text-decoration-thickness: initial; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">
<p style="color: #000000; letter-spacing: 0.544px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">媒体会通过较低的底价设置吸引大量广告主快速提高广告填充率、积累数据完善算法。广告主积累到一定数量后，媒体针对不同的资源位和流量设置底价梯度，影响广告填充率。</p>
<p style="color: #000000; letter-spacing: 0.544px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">你会发现在某一资源位上，有时可以花2元获得流量，有时需要更高的价钱才能获得流量。或受季节性影响，通常在热度营销节点上，广告主猛增导致根本抢不到流量，此时请勿盲目提高出价！而是围绕eCPM合理分析，做定向优化。</p>
<p style="color: #000000; letter-spacing: 0.544px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><img src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/c755bd37c461ef93b2fa134c636665ef.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; color: #212629; font-size: 18px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left; padding-left: 12px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;">3、通用法则eCPM</h2>
<p style="color: #000000; letter-spacing: normal; caret-color: #000000; text-align: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">此外广告素材质量也是影响用户是否愿意点击广告的重要因素。</p>
<p style="color: #000000; letter-spacing: normal; caret-color: #000000; text-align: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">Apple Ads新功能CPP帮助很多广告主提升了点击率和安装率，从媒体的视角看，苹果就是在丰富广告场景及样式，让广告受众，在不同搜索场景下看到不同样式的满足需求的素材。了解CPP?巧用【自定义产品页】和【产品页优化】提高转化率 </p>
<p style="color: #000000; letter-spacing: normal; caret-color: #000000; text-align: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><img src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/ac931edd0b34b20d78da32b7dc9a892e.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h1 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); background-size: 16px 20px; color: #212629; font-size: 20px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: center; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;">二、App Store的底层逻辑</h1>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; color: #212629; font-size: 18px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;">1、媒体流量-出货量持续上涨</h2>
<p style="color: #000000; letter-spacing: normal; caret-color: #000000; text-align: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="color: #3e3e3e; font-variant-ligatures: normal; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: justify; text-decoration-thickness: initial;">苹果设备的销量总体呈上涨趋势，苹果设备购买门槛高，决定了iOS用户质量和付费能力。</span></p>
<p style="color: #000000; letter-spacing: normal; caret-color: #000000; text-align: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="color: #3e3e3e; font-variant-ligatures: normal; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: justify; text-decoration-thickness: initial;"><img src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/9938bfadbd84a193f8acbbe9da726a83.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>

<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; color: #212629; font-size: 18px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat no-repeat;">2、App Store 流量入口</h2>
</section>
<p style="color: #000000; letter-spacing: 0.544px; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="color: #000000; letter-spacing: normal; caret-color: #000000; text-align: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">App Store的流量入口几经迭代，「搜索」依然是苹果主要的流量入口。「搜索广告」以“人找广告”的运行机制，决定了它在同个流量池中精准性更强、用户质量更高。如此而言，ASA和ASO在搜索广告中的重要性就不言而喻了。</p>
<p style="color: #000000; letter-spacing: normal; caret-color: #000000; text-align: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><img src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/ce02bc5189bdd5bdbafd8207324c1887.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; color: #212629; font-size: 18px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;">3、App Store 底层逻辑</h2>
<p style="color: #000000; letter-spacing: normal; caret-color: #000000; text-align: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">Less is more，Apple始终坚持做好原相机，做好原生系统。把功能上的补充交给开发者发布在App Store的App，并通过一定机制给优秀的App更多的曝光机会，这也正是小而美的拍照类App常常登上苹果推荐位的原因。</p>
<p style="color: #000000; letter-spacing: normal; caret-color: #000000; text-align: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">除了苹果推荐，如何在搜索结果中提升小而美的App排名也是众多开发者关心的话题，即优化关键词排名，也就是ASO时代常用的公式。</p>
<p style="color: #000000; letter-spacing: normal; caret-color: #000000; text-align: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><img src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/94cdfe322a0f1190dfb6e68287da4115.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="color: #000000; letter-spacing: 0.544px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-variant-ligatures: normal; text-decoration-thickness: initial; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">关键词排名涉及：下载量(一定时间)*元数据相关性*产品权重。</p>
<p style="color: #000000; letter-spacing: 0.544px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-variant-ligatures: normal; text-decoration-thickness: initial; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">通过优化App元数据相关性、提高词与产品之间的关联性，进而提高关键词排名。在短时间内获取大量的指向性搜索下载行为，获得曝光并得到自然新增用户。</p>

<h2 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; color: #212629; font-size: 18px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;">1、Apple Ads优化公式</h2>
<p style="color: #000000; letter-spacing: normal; caret-color: #000000; text-align: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">Apple Ads进入大陆1年以来，为iOS开发者带来大量高质量用户。同时，通过清晰的效果数据为ASO价值做了证明。</p>
<p style="color: #000000; letter-spacing: normal; caret-color: #000000; text-align: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">从前面结合广告运行基本原理eCPM公式和App Store底层逻辑公式，得出Apple Ads的优化公式。有了这个公式，我们投什么词、怎么投、什么时候投就都有了宗旨。</p>
