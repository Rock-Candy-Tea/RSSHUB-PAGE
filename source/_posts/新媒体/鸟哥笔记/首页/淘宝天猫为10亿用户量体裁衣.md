<p class="pgc-img-caption" style="box-sizing: border-box; padding: 0px; border: 0px; margin: 20px 0px 0px !important 0px;"><span style="color: #444444; font-weight: inherit; text-align: inherit;">最近，<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E6%B7%98%E5%AE%9D" target="_blank">淘宝</a>天猫动作频频——在服饰、家装、美妆、母婴等领域，分别上线服装新势力周、发布家装三大战略，举办金妆奖、金婴奖，再加上落地广州的造物节和创新大会，有数十万00后商家加入。线上线下同步，在行业里“刷足”存在感。</span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="8"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #444444; --tt-darkmode-color: #A2A2A2;">今年1月，阿里巴巴集团中国数字商业板块分管总裁戴珊发布内部信，宣布新组织架构——设立三大中心，最核心的淘宝和天猫两大业务在后台实现全面融合。</span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="10"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #444444; --tt-darkmode-color: #A2A2A2;">此轮调整以后，淘系一直在低调前行，这是近期少有的活跃时刻。实际上，融合的出发点在于大淘宝将形成统一的机制，确保消费者的购物体验更简单顺畅，更个性化的需求得到满足，中小商家的发展和成长更具确定性。</span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="12"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #444444; --tt-darkmode-color: #A2A2A2;">此次淘宝天猫的一系列动作，正是深化“从交易到消费”的路径优化与升级。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="14"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #444444; --tt-darkmode-color: #A2A2A2;">随着阿里系年度购买用户迎来10亿里程碑，淘宝天猫更注重从垂直化入手，满足细分人群，建设全域消费人群触达的能力,实现从品类人群到消费人群的升级。同时,根据每个品类差异化的服务,建设更符合消费者需求的平台服务规则。</span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="16"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #444444; --tt-darkmode-color: #A2A2A2;">业内人士分析，一系列动作背后，淘宝天猫聚焦在升级消费者体验，在不同领域根据消费需求“量体裁衣”，发力更深入的消费行业变革。</span></p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; text-align: inherit; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="18"> </p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; text-align: center; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="19"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #ffffff; --tt-darkmode-color: #FFFFFF;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-color: #3daad6; --tt-darkmode-bgcolor: #3DAAD6;">01</span></span></span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; text-align: center; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="20"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #ffffff; --tt-darkmode-color: #FFFFFF;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-color: #3daad6; --tt-darkmode-bgcolor: #3DAAD6;">商家主场优势持续</span></span></span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; text-align: center; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="21"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #ffffff; --tt-darkmode-color: #FFFFFF;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; background-color: #3daad6; --tt-darkmode-bgcolor: #3DAAD6;">年轻人涌入细分赛道</span></span></span></span></p>
<p class="syl-page-br" style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; height: 0px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="23"> </p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="24"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #444444; --tt-darkmode-color: #A2A2A2;">根据阿里巴巴集团最新财报显示，全平台年内购买用户首次超过10亿。这对于阿里电商来说，无疑是一个重要的发展节点。</span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="26"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #444444; --tt-darkmode-color: #A2A2A2;">此前，戴珊曾解析淘宝天猫的核心战略——围绕10亿消费者，从交易走向消费，做好、做深国内消费市场，在不确定性中锁定确定性，在确定性中发掘新商机。</span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="28"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #444444; --tt-darkmode-color: #A2A2A2;">众所周知，商家生意的主场就在淘系。当前，阿里电商的商家优势依然强劲——2022年商家数量也超过千万，其中GMV过亿商家数达到1000多家，并保持年增长50%以上，另有700多个新品牌生意增长超过200%。</span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="30"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #444444; --tt-darkmode-color: #A2A2A2;">不管是过去还是未来，要集合最有实力的老牌商家，挖掘最具潜力的新商家，首要是让商家看到确定性回报。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="32"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #444444; --tt-darkmode-color: #A2A2A2;">从几大主要垂直领域来看，淘宝天猫孵化超1万家年营收过百万的服饰新商新品牌；198万家装家居商家中，上财年成交过亿425家，上财年成交过千万1.2万家；2021年，天猫上有42个美妆新锐品牌成交额过亿。在全行业优势始终明显。</span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="34"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #444444; --tt-darkmode-color: #A2A2A2;">同时，淘宝天猫要保住领先地位，也需要不断挖掘新鲜的血液——发掘全新垂直赛道和品类，扶持年轻创业者。</span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="36"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #444444; --tt-darkmode-color: #A2A2A2;">在近期举办的淘宝造物节上，线上线下共计28万商家报名参加，是历年中人数最多的一次。与之对应的，“入淘”创业人数呈现持续增长态势。数据显示，截至7月31日的过去一年里，淘宝天猫净增了近120万商家，增量与增速均翻了一倍多。</span></p>

<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #000000; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: inherit; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;" data-track="66"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bold; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #444444; --tt-darkmode-color: #A2A2A2;">从新势力周已经能看到淘宝天猫服饰行业“从交易到消费”的变化：</span></span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; color: #444444; --tt-darkmode-color: #A2A2A2;">淘宝天猫服饰行业通过风格数字化洞察趋势风格和品类，商家通过平台提供的技术能力，朝着内容化品牌化方向升级店铺，展现在服饰行业的专业表达和不同见解，完成整体的商品内容化转型。这次淘宝天猫还升级了“魔镜”智能穿搭，“XR”等黑科技产品和服务，将开放给包括中小商家在内的所有商家。</span></p>
