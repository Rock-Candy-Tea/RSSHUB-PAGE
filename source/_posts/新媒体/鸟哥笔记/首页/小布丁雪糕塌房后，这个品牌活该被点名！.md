<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/17086755662d5be60bd3440.28107787.png" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: center; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>来自@统一酸爽宗师统一老坛</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>这倒不难理解，康师傅搞砸了老坛酸菜牛肉面，它还有红烧牛肉面、香辣牛肉、红烧排骨面撑住...</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><span>但老坛酸菜牛肉面，可是统一2008年就开创的，</span><span><strong>不仅是统一方便面<strong>的</strong>代名词，还默默地扛下了<strong>统一</strong>业绩的半壁江山。</strong></span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>不是我吹的，2012年统一的销售收入70多亿元，单是老坛酸菜牛肉面这个单品，就占了超过50%——整整<span>40亿。</span></span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><span>哪怕康师傅跟风推出老坛酸菜牛肉面品类，</span><span>统一也不忘改广告语强调正宗：</span><span><strong>“有人模仿我的脸，有人模仿我的面，模仿的再像也不是统一老坛！</strong></span></span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/6574474962d5be611fa7f5.18779329.gif" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: center; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>来自统一广告</span></strong></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>消费者早就不自觉地，将统一和老坛酸菜绑定在一起了。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>统一的品类销量冠军，要是真的输给了一脚酸菜，消费者想必会对统一的好感度大打折扣，就算是打折也救不了。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong>这不，</strong></span><span><strong>“老痰酸菜<strong>”</strong></strong></span><span><strong>品类的后遗症至今还在。</strong></span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>即使泡面被囤货人抢完，老坛酸菜泡面在货架上纹丝不动，这真的是大家最后的倔强。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/176008697762d5be63df8a25.10987480.jpeg" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: center; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>来自微博网友</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong>只能说踩踏酸菜一时爽，品类信任火葬场。</strong></span><span><span></span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><span><strong><span>我们熟悉的</span></strong></span><span><strong><span>可口可乐</span></strong></span></span><span><span><strong><span>亦如此，</span></strong></span></span><span><span><strong><span>对于多数人来说，最深刻的品类印象就是可乐碳酸饮料。</span></strong></span></span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>消费者这样的心理认知，大大限制了品牌的创新。所以<span>这几年，<span>哪怕</span><span>可口可乐</span>都尝试在果汁、乳制品、咖啡<span>领域</span>推新品，但出圈的很少。</span></span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>就看可口可乐推出夏枯草凉茶植物饮料，消费者都不好看，劝可口可乐还是别来凑热闹。</span><span></span></p>

<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/120398668462d5be64bfcd67.17199255.png" width="333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>

<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>过去在刀剪界，一直都流传着</span><span><strong>“南有张小泉、中有曹正兴、北有王麻子”</strong></span><span>的说法，王麻子也是刀剪品类的代名词。</span><span></span></p>

<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>对手出事翻车，消费者会把压力给到对手。王麻子自然要做点事，一边<span>挽回消费者对品类的信心，一边<span>稳住刀剪行业的话语权。</span></span></span></p>
<h1 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); font-size: 20px; color: #212629; text-align: center; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;">04</h1>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>看到这儿，就会知道伊利小布丁为啥慌了。</span><span><span></span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><span>这事说</span>大也不大，<span>说小也不小</span>!</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><span>在消费者的既有认知下，伊利蒙牛就是“布丁”雪糕品类的代名词。</span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>就像伊利也有苦咖啡、伊利心情等其它雪糕，虽然挂着小布丁名字的杂牌也不少。</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>但提及“小布丁”，我们还是<span>会第一时间</span>想到伊利小布丁；<span>一</span><span>旦“小布丁”被质疑，首当其冲的也</span><span>是</span><span>伊</span><span>利</span><span>。</span></span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/186496299262d5be653126e8.83045922.png" width="444" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: center; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>来自@伊小利冰激凌 微博</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><span><strong><span><span>从另一个角度来说，</span>伊利也是活该被点名，印证伊利</span></strong></span><span><strong><span>品类占位</span></strong></span><span><strong><span>的</span></strong><strong><span>成功。</span></strong></span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"> </p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong>所以品类心智是一把双刃剑。</strong></span><span><span></span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><span>一个品牌要是</span><span>成</span><span>功占领一个品类的关键词，让消费者<span>将其与某个品类联系到一块</span>，当然是赢麻了</span><span>。</span></span><span><span><span></span></span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><span><span>当消费者需求来了，想</span><span>要消费某</span><span>个产品时，就会产生条件反射优先选择这个品牌，而不会选择其它，</span><span>这就是“品牌即品类”的效果。</span></span></span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>但如果有朝一日，品牌想要推出别的品类新品，就要好好考虑消费者的接受度了，否则只会翻车。</span></p>
