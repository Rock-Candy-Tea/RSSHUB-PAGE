<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">当我们身处的各个角落都植入着广告，不少人表示早已对广告“免疫”，该如何在各式各样的广告中脱颖而出、让用户看到并为之买单？</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;">在研究了近期在<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E5%85%AC%E4%BC%97%E5%8F%B7" target="_blank">公众号</a>上热门的广告文章后，总结了6个热门广告的共同特点，这些特点同时也是可套用到实际工作的实用方法：</p>
</section>
</section>
</section>
</section>
<section><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/208275305963067a2d5d6822.42828957.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></section>
<h1 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 20px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: center;"><strong>设置“钩子”</strong><strong>吸引注意</strong></h1>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>如何吸引用户注意？</span><strong><span>设置广告钩子。</span></strong></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在研究了公众号上热门爆款广告文案后发现，常见并且很实用的方法之一便是利用时下最新新闻或者高热度话题。这个钩子可以放在哪些地方呢？</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>首先是标题</span></strong></span><span>，例如：</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/170836575863067a4067b272.92947711.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/66955085063067a40a49f39.23964042.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>

<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>打磨标题、吸引用户注意，增加文章打开率，更有利于后续的转化。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>还可以将这个钩子</span><strong><span>放在文章开头</span></strong><span>，通过解读热点吸引读者继续阅读，在合适的地方引出下文，应注意话题的转换要合理不生硬。</span></p>
<section><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/131084266363067a2e79c867.74435463.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></section>
<h1 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 20px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: center;"><strong>标题长度</strong><strong>应该长还是短呢？</strong></h1>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>以下两个标题，哪个会让你更想点进去呢？</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>（1）今年中秋送礼就选这款月饼！</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>VS</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>（2）去年卖爆全网的5W+的国潮风月饼又来了！纯素、低脂、低糖，好吃不长胖，今年中秋送礼就选她</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>大部分人应该会被第二个标题吸引点进去，因为临近中秋时购买月饼是刚需，5W+、低脂、低糖、不怕胖...等卖点通过标题直观地展现给目标用户，</span><span><strong>用户能一眼就看到自己所需求的卖点</strong></span><span>。相比之下，第一个标题便显得没有吸引力了。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>所以，以此得出的结论是：在明确目标用户的情况下，通过标题尽可能地展现卖点能更吸引用户购买。此时，标题便尽可能需要长一点。</span></p>
<section><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/80856107163067a30525939.71692382.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></section>
<h1 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 20px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: center;"><strong>从“自己说”</strong><strong>到“别人说”</strong></h1>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>“王婆卖瓜，自卖自夸”，在广告文案中，如果长篇大论都是从“我”的角度花式宣传产品，用户在阅读的过程中信任度会低于“他”说；</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>那么，</span><strong><span>如何从“自己说”变成“别人说”呢</span></strong><span>？</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在热门广告文章中，</span><span><strong>不少文案会引用“买家秀”</strong></span><span>，也就是让“别人说”，尤其是购买过的用户，那可信度便更高了。这种情况下，会增加用户购买转化的可能性。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/141663687763067a412b8b81.26459484.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>

<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>当这个“别人”是</span><span><strong>权威人士</strong></span><span>的时候，那刺激用户下单的效果就更明显了；在空口无凭的情况下，不少读者在看到产品价值后会产生这种质疑，此时在文案中加入专业认定的权威信息，能有效地提升产品信任感。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/21460779963067a41638c12.86413954.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>

<h4 style="background-image: none; font-size: 14px; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px; margin-bottom: 25px;">图 | 截自微信公众号</h4>

<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>在广告编写时，优秀的文案通常想的最多的就是：“如何让用户信任我？”而让“别人说”正是爆款文案中常见的特点之一。</span></p>
<section><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/115679805063067a41940097.73355344.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></section>
<h1 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 20px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: center;"><strong>善用比较</strong><strong>与别人比也要同自己比</strong></h1>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>好东西是对比出来的，好的广告文案可以利用“对比”将产品优势放大。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>一是通过对比不同</span></strong></span><span>。例如通过对比优劣性、使用效果、功效...甚至是价格、售后、物流，当用户通过文案看到了对比，那么产品的优势也就跃然纸上。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>常说“货比三家”，如果将对比结果能够明确地展示在用户面前，省去用户对比的时间，也能直接促成不知道该如何对比的用户下单。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>二是使用前后对比</span></strong></span><span>。产品的效果如何在文案中体现，最直观的方式之一便是通过使用前后对比。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/86778767263067a41d19172.70895011.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>

<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>无论是与别人比较还是与自己比较，善用比较这一技巧在热门广告文案中屡见不鲜，可见实用性之高。</span></p>
<section><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/96968355563067a420946f6.37394823.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></section>
<h1 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 20px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: center;"><strong>制造迫切感</strong><strong>刺激转化</strong></h1>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>当用户对产品产生兴趣的时候，此时</span><span><strong>通过文案制造迫切感则是刺激用户下单的关键点</strong></span><span>。刺激用户转化重要的一步就是活动信息的刺激，列出限时优惠的信息会让用户产生一种迫切感。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>常见的技巧是标红优惠内容，以及“名额有限”“先到先得”之类具有刺激性的字眼。</span></p>
<section><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://ngbjimg.xy599.com/111229615463067a422c1d19.75292141.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></section>
<h1 style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 20px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: center;"><strong>路径明确</strong><strong>告诉用户下一步该干嘛</strong></h1>
<section>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>优惠怎么领取？如何购买？在哪购买？</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span><strong><span>购买路径要越详细越好</span></strong></span><span>，如果在用户已产生购买欲望的时候，购买路径不明确会降低用户的购买欲，链路隐藏的太深，大部分用户是没有耐心找的；</span><span></span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>如果没有及时、明确地告知用户下一步该干什么，当用户冷静下来后，那大概率直接退出了。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>该在什么时候告诉用户呢？在通过大量研究热门广告文案后，我们发现</span><span><strong>可以在文章中多次提示购买路径</strong></span><span>。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>比如：你是通过二维码转化，那么可以在文章中多次插入二维码，一开始在前 10%内容不到的位置放一个二维码，然后在中间放一个二维码，并且结尾处再放一个二维码。</span></p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><span>以上就是总结的6个公众号热门广告文案的共同特点。总而言之，便是要从</span><span><strong>吸引注意、增加信任、刺激下单</strong></span><span>这</span><span>3个原则开始思考和展开。</span></p>
