<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnGAw62u84QWog06AVdklpIh">为了解<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E7%94%9F%E6%B4%BB%E6%9C%8D%E5%8A%A1" target="_blank">生活服务</a>达人对抖音平台侧资源的需求、对资源置换合作模式的接受度、合作周期/timline、接单范畴等，我们开展了生活服务达人市场调研。</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnGAw62u84QWog06AVdklpIh">* 调研报告中的“生活服务达人”，是指美食/旅行垂类达人</div>

<h1 class="heading-1 ace-line old-record-id-doxcnoeWgWqCMq6wk0i0yHRvGgc" style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 20px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: center;">一、总结</h1>
<h2 class="heading-2 ace-line old-record-id-doxcniYoyesm4i6eqgndhRBDnch" style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 18px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;">（一）调研结论</h2>
<h3 class="heading-3 ace-line old-record-id-doxcnqAsqKWOE4QI4gPj3cNIt8d" style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//1bb91f73e9d31ea2830a5e73ce3ed328.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 16px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;"><strong>1. </strong><strong>达人最</strong><strong>关注的资源</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnkO008K0Eiiy0UehvKapwrb">所有达人都关注<span style="color: #d83931;">流量</span>和<span style="color: #d83931;">现金</span>，超头达人还关注<span style="color: #d83931;">合作商家/内容素材</span></li>
<li class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnKOEwQIw6CMAEqCME93u3we">此外，其他重点关注因素包括<span style="color: #d83931;">与平台共建内容（提升知名度）、拓展商业化领域</span></li>
</ul>
<div class="heading-3 ace-line old-record-id-doxcnaWw2GUg6gQYyOwHuTrggSf"> </div>
<h3 class="heading-3 ace-line old-record-id-doxcnKeESgyEmkEm0fDWWjrBptc" style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//1bb91f73e9d31ea2830a5e73ce3ed328.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 16px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;"><strong>2. 达人对</strong><strong>资源置换合作</strong><strong>模式的</strong><strong>接受度</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>抖音官方合作中，达人对资源置换的<span style="color: #d83931;">接受度较高</span><span style="color: #d83931;">，</span>但也<span style="color: #d83931;">有明确的需求/条件</span>（比如，DOU+金额达到一定标准、优质合作商家/内容素材、认可项目等）</li>
<li>不接受资源置换的达人，希望<span style="color: #d83931;">有现金</span>（对 “平台资源+部分现金” 模式的接受度较高）</li>
</ul>
<blockquote class=" old-record-id-doxcnyISOsS0UeQIGCgoEC1ETfO" data-type="quote_container">
<div>
<div>举例：</div>
<ul>
<li>超头A：如果纯资源置换，希望视频流量能达到1000w</li>
<li>超头B：希望探店餐厅满足以下条件：1. 全网首发的新餐厅，或者经典餐厅（可提供可定制的美食/菜品）；2. 口碑/宝藏小店，大众评分4.8以上；3. 新式菜系</li>
<li>其他需求：能够客观评价餐厅/菜品</li>
</ul>
</div>
</blockquote>
<h3 class="heading-3 ace-line old-record-id-doxcn06WISsIKoqmwcVvG5RUM3d" style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//1bb91f73e9d31ea2830a5e73ce3ed328.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 16px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;">3. 达人的合作周期</h3>
<ul>
<li class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnoO8CwI2M8EoQyZNdkxqvib">内容制作周期大多为<span style="color: #d83931;">一周左右</span>（敲定合作后，从内容策划到视频上线）</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="heading-3 ace-line old-record-id-doxcnAmYQgAkqkSCMkvEVDq6Q4f"> </h3>
<h3 class="heading-3 ace-line old-record-id-doxcnAmYQgAkqkSCMkvEVDq6Q4f" style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//1bb91f73e9d31ea2830a5e73ce3ed328.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 16px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;">4. 达人的合作习惯&过往合作经历</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>异地合作：</strong>达人对异地合作的<span style="color: #d83931;">接受度很高</span>，但需要<span style="color: #d83931;">报销差旅费</span></li>
<li><strong>体裁（短视频/直播）：</strong>大多短视频达人无丰富的生活服务直播经验</li>
<li><strong>对</strong><strong>锚点</strong><strong>的接受度：</strong>超头达人对视频挂锚点的<span style="color: #d83931;">接受度一般，</span>其他达人的接受度相对较高</li>
<li><strong>接单范畴：</strong>集中在<span style="color: #d83931;">美食、探店、旅行</span>方向</li>
<li><strong>过往合作经历：</strong>
<ul>
<li>达人与商家的合作，大多是<span style="color: #d83931;">商家主动邀约、达人所在</span><span style="color: #d83931;">MCN</span><span style="color: #d83931;">推荐</span>，合作模式大多是<span style="color: #d83931;">一口价现金</span></li>
<li>大多达人参与过抖音官方合作，但部分达人<span style="color: #d83931;">对权益不满</span>，可能影响长期合作</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<blockquote class=" old-record-id-doxcncIYIiQ02CoWo47VSGESefg" data-type="quote_container">
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnaMmAak8CWq8aAR9ZwcoP4c">10位超头（抖音粉丝500w+，美食&旅行）：7位参与过抖音官方合作，其中3位认为权益少；3位未参与过，其中1位认为权益太少而拒绝合作</div>
</blockquote>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnoWQ0eGk8ASMU4iyg8TSvXe"> </div>
<h2 class="heading-2 ace-line old-record-id-doxcnuM4c6yMIIWyo00zryuH4Qe" style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 18px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;">（二）核心建议</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>平台资源：</strong><span style="color: #245bdb;">若达人对平台权益不满意，则可能影响长期合作，</span>合作权益总价值至少为<span style="color: #d83931;">星图</span><span style="color: #d83931;">报价50%以上</span></li>
</ul>
<ul class="list-bullet2">
<ul class="list-bullet2">
<li class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnmmcAyM8AQeii2DNiHHyAfe" data-list="bullet">
<div>由于平台扶持以外，达人和商家存在自然合作（以“一口价现金”为主），平台入场，最佳合作模式是<span style="color: #d83931;">有基础</span><span style="color: #d83931;">现金</span>（“一口价现金” or “现金+流量”），做商业权益以外的商单导向引导（KA商家扶持）+ 流量扶持</div>
</li>
<li class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcngGqWOoUEsKwWC0RnqvVH4b" data-list="bullet">
<div>若无招商进场，仅以<span style="color: #d83931;">资源置换</span>模式合作（纯流量），总金额建议<span style="color: #d83931;">不低于</span><span style="color: #d83931;">星图</span><span style="color: #d83931;">报价50%</span></div>
</li>
<li>如需达人异地合作，则<span style="color: #d83931;">报销差旅费</span></li>
</ul>
</ul>
<blockquote>
<p><strong>权益范围</strong><strong>粗调研</strong>：10w-粉丝的尾部达人<span style="color: #2ea121;">1k左右</span>，10w-100w粉丝的腰部达人<span style="color: #2ea121;">≈</span><span style="color: #2ea121;">1w</span>，100w-500w粉丝的头部达人<span style="color: #2ea121;">1w-5w</span>，500w+粉丝的超头达人<span style="color: #2ea121;">5w-12w。</span></p>
<p><strong>备注：</strong>调研时未强调挂POI锚点等商业化需求，so<span style="color: #2ea121;"> “权益范围粗调” ≈ “达人对资源需求的底线”</span>（不区分纯内容向合作 or 挂POI锚点的商业化合作）。</p>
<p><span style="color: #d83931;">一般来讲，对达人视频的要求越高，权益价值越接近</span><span style="color: #d83931;">星图</span><span style="color: #d83931;">报价。</span></p>
</blockquote>
<ul class="list-bullet1">
<li class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnkcI80sMya4ioaQTBytQmRL" data-list="bullet">
<div><strong>合作商家：</strong>最好选择<span style="color: #d83931;">行业头部</span><span style="color: #d83931;">KA</span>，达人期望优质的合作商家和内容素材</div>
</li>
<li class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnoMCiEecAS2OEqgnrETCvoh" data-list="bullet">
<div><strong>项目&达人匹配：</strong></div>
<ul class="list-bullet2">
<li class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnumOy6YyyWw8ei08ssHlr0g" data-list="bullet">
<div><span style="color: #d83931;">宣发向</span>项目，适合<span style="color: #d83931;">全网超头达人</span>（超头对团购POI锚点的接受度一般，部分达人只接受普通POI&活动锚点）</div>
</li>
<li class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnqwyKk4Q2GUq6U3822rhund" data-list="bullet">
<div><span style="color: #d83931;">带货向</span>项目，<span style="color: #d83931;">头部及以下达人</span>对视频挂POI锚点接受度较高</div>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnwIqi0yCOMYiWeqDUBG6Rac" data-list="bullet">
<div><strong>项目提需：</strong>一般而言，项目<span style="color: #d83931;">上线前3周</span>提需（常规流程：业务提需 --> 输出达人brief & bd建联 5-7天 --> 内容制作5-7天 --> 审核/修改3-5天 --> 项目上线）</div>
</li>
</ul>

<h1 class="heading-1 ace-line old-record-id-doxcn0giwSI8QciOQeOiopHUjMc" style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 20px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: center;">二、调研详情</h1>
<h2 class="heading-2 ace-line old-record-id-doxcngkwSO4c6MK2CKmZf4z109b" style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 18px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;">（一）1v1调研</h2>
<blockquote class=" old-record-id-doxcnYOwUcg6yiSUaiYlQiqG7Wd" data-type="quote_container">
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcny2406GCEoQISw9LBUjCtoc"><strong>调研对象：</strong>总计21位，其中超头达人10位，头部达人10位，腰部达人1位</div>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnyYMwGaOqk6UeKOzbvaj5dh">*备注：“超头达人”=“全网超头达人”，“头部达人”=粉丝量100w+且非超头，“腰部达人”=粉丝量10w-100w</div>
</blockquote>
<h3 class="heading-3 ace-line old-record-id-doxcnaOMaAwyU0qEIW8qRve5BXb" style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//1bb91f73e9d31ea2830a5e73ce3ed328.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 16px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;">1. 达人对平台侧资源的需求</h3>
<ul>
<li>超头达人：看重<span style="color: #d83931;">流量</span><span style="color: #d83931;">、</span><span style="color: #d83931;">现金</span><span style="color: #d83931;">、合作商家/内容素材</span></li>
<li>头部达人：看重<span style="color: #d83931;">流量和现金</span>，对合作商家/内容素材无较高要求</li>
</ul>
<blockquote class=" old-record-id-doxcn66yWMYa6sSee0DUIAvHKG3" data-type="quote_container">
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnOy42E8eYS0SAFZuv0LWOeO">超头达人对内容把控度高，希望与优质合作商家合作、视频内容有创意、内容不受过多干扰</div>
</blockquote>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnqAIIQOSiwIGCYxXnz3Cufc"> </div>
<h3 class="heading-3 ace-line old-record-id-doxcnQsgiWESYUmaa8JupzzpFSg" style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//1bb91f73e9d31ea2830a5e73ce3ed328.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 16px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;">2. 达人对资源置换合作模式的接受度</h3>
<ul>
<li>抖音官方合作中，达人对资源置换的<span style="color: #d83931;">接受度较高</span><span style="color: #d83931;">，</span>但也<span style="color: #d83931;">有明确的需求/条件</span>（比如，DOU+金额达到一定标准、优质合作商家/内容素材、认可项目等）</li>
</ul>
<blockquote class=" old-record-id-doxcnSEeeuiQ4GSS64tdAZBsOJu" data-type="quote_container">
<div>21位达人：</div>
<ul class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcn2QeSgw8ICgW2msDMh1cgKb">
<ul>
<li class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnOO0KammWyGGysFlYc204ig" data-list="bullet">17位接受无现金的资源置换模式，但也均有其他需求（比如，DOU+金额达到一定标准、优质合作商家/内容素材、认可项目等）</li>
<li class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcncqeiaOSuIS8IaELvCC1VFg" data-list="bullet">2位不接受，要求有<span style="color: #245bdb;">现金</span>（比如达人xx要求现金&少部分DOU+）</li>
<li class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcncKee64SuEuWIIDMqHvwwzg" data-list="bullet">2位不确定，需评估项目后再决定</li>
</ul>
</ul>
</blockquote>
<div class="heading-3 ace-line old-record-id-doxcniQ8AWIAYe2CqcpVWFE2nkh"> </div>
<h3 class="heading-3 ace-line old-record-id-doxcnG8MyKK0GO40ie83zkqBTZf" style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//1bb91f73e9d31ea2830a5e73ce3ed328.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 16px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;">3. 达人的合作周期</h3>
<ul>
<li>内容制作周期大多为<span style="color: #d83931;">一周左右</span>（敲定合作后，从内容策划到视频上线）</li>
</ul>
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnk8uQ8M0kaqEeq2SCdRYWvc"> </div>
<h3 class="heading-3 ace-line old-record-id-doxcnYMSwWmsKqeI4aeEuJxS3Of" style="background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//1bb91f73e9d31ea2830a5e73ce3ed328.png'); background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat; color: #212629; font-size: 16px; padding-left: 12px; margin: 25px 0px 20px; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; text-align: left;">4. 达人的合作习惯</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>体裁（短视频/直播）：</strong>大多达人无生活服务直播经验/生活</li>
</ul>
<blockquote class=" old-record-id-doxcncAyCCcqSqaq4Os7oWLDAbh" data-type="quote_container">
<div class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcncAm0kwmSYmCcwtCgXvlgng">21位达人：擅长直播的有1位，可以直播的有7位，不直播的有11位，未来计划直播的1位</div>
</blockquote>
<ul>
<li><strong>对</strong><strong>锚点</strong><strong>的接受度：</strong>超头达人对视频挂锚点的<span style="color: #d83931;">接受度一般，</span>其他达人的接受度相对较高</li>
</ul>
<blockquote class=" old-record-id-doxcnU4Qw08OecYa4UJsw64LXQg" data-type="quote_container">
<div>10位超头达人：</div>
<ul class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnCeUwUIyEUQmoYlxYQHf3Ed">
<ul>
<li class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnwuKQCmiQK4EiQH9nFZZnRb" data-list="bullet">6位接受，2位只接受普通POI锚点，1位不接受锚点，1位需要by case评估</li>
<li class="ace-line ace-line old-record-id-doxcnCoyOcKIG4EgkspRU7y2hih" data-list="bullet">如果商家付费/有现金激励，达人可以考虑接受锚点</li>
</ul>
</ul>
</blockquote>
<ul>
<li><strong>接单</strong><strong>范畴：</strong>集中在<span style="color: #d83931;">美食、探店、</span><span style="color: #d83931;">旅行</span>方向</li>
<li><strong>合作地域：</strong>达人对异地合作的<span style="color: #d83931;">接受度较高</span>，但需<span style="color: #d83931;">报销差旅费</span></li>
