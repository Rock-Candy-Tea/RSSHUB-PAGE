                        <p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;">近日，苹果发邮件表示已经在 ASA 后台推出了两项重要更新，目前广告预算管理已采用“<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/%E6%AF%8F%E6%97%A5%E9%A2%84%E7%AE%97" target="_blank">每日预算</a>”方式，而搜索标签广告位的计价方式已改用 CPT 计价模式。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;">6 月 23 日，3YData 开展的<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #027aff; box-sizing: border-box;">《ASA 推出重要更新，解锁广告投放新玩法》</span>线上活动完美收官。此次分享中，我们为大家解读了 ASA 推出的更新内容，在 ASA 广告的全新玩法，并通过不同场景进行分析，向广告主提供多种解决方案，帮助广告主们解决投放难题。</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;"> </span></p>
<h2 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; overflow-wrap: break-word; display: flex; text-align: left; line-height: 30px; color: #212629; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 18px; background-color: #ffffff; flex-flow: row nowrap; justify-content: flex-start; box-sizing: border-box; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-weight: 600; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"><br style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"></strong><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"><br style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"></strong><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"><br style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"></strong><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">01、<a href="https://www.niaogebiji.com/tag/ASA%E5%B9%BF%E5%91%8A" target="_blank">ASA广告</a>两项重要更新</strong></h2>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">#1 搜索标签广告更新为 CPT 竞价</strong></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; box-sizing: border-box; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;">在此前，搜索标签广告的竞价模式为 CPM，按千次展示竞价与计费。更新之后采用 CPT 计价模式，用户只有点击了广告才会收取费用。</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; box-sizing: border-box;"></span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; display: inline-block; text-align: inherit; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; max-width: 100%; vertical-align: middle; box-sizing: border-box; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><img src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/efd33731692792594017105dc0931699.png" alt="解锁ASA广告投放新玩法，利用ASA新功能获客！" width="871" title="解锁ASA广告投放新玩法，利用ASA新功能获客！" style="width:871px;height:auto;" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;">此前的搜索标签广告存在无效花费，即向用户展示了广告，但用户可能并没有留意到。但随着收费模式的变更，意味着广告仅有展示不会收取费用，只有用户点击后才会产生费用，在成本方面预计将会明显降低。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;">另外，在出价方面，搜索标签广告的 CPT 出价可以借鉴关键词广告的出价，但要考虑到搜索标签广告的 CR 将比预期要低得多。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">#2 广告预算管理改为“每日预算”</strong><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"> </strong></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;">在此前，ASA 广告后台广告预算管理，是<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #027aff; box-sizing: border-box;">总预算和日预算结合</span>的方式，即通过固定的消耗金额的生命周期预算。更新之后，新建广告系列的设置中移除广告系列的总预算，旧的广告系列已设置的总预算仍保留。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; display: inline-block; text-align: inherit; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; max-width: 100%; vertical-align: middle; box-sizing: border-box; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><img src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/2e8829c43bad5facecd0a4039c6494e0.png" alt="解锁ASA广告投放新玩法，利用ASA新功能获客！" width="869" title="解锁ASA广告投放新玩法，利用ASA新功能获客！" style="width:869px;height:auto;" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;">使用每日预算功能，只需要设置一个月内花费在广告系列上的每日平均金额。如若某一天有机会获得更多下载量，系统会自动适当提高当日的预算，但是每个月的总支出不会超过每日预算乘以一个日历月的平均天数（按 30.4 天计算）。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;">值得注意的是，增加每日预算时，当天生效；降低每日预算时，次日生效。</span></p>
<h2 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; overflow-wrap: break-word; display: flex; text-align: left; line-height: 30px; color: #212629; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 18px; background-color: #ffffff; flex-flow: row nowrap; justify-content: flex-start; box-sizing: border-box; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); font-weight: 600; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">02、如何利用新功能管理广告预算</strong><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"> </strong></h2>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">#1 预算跑不满的情况</strong><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"> </strong></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;">在预算跑不满的情况下，比如，设置预算 100元/天，实际消耗才 50-70元，则每日预算的调整对其基本没有影响。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">#2 预算跑满但不同日期预算要求不同</strong></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;">这类情况一般发生在<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #027aff; box-sizing: border-box;">金融类、直播类、交友类</span>等产品居多，以金融类产品为例，在交易日、股票行情好的情况下普遍会增加预算，相反的，在非交易日、股票行情差的情况下，则会减少预算。</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;"> </span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; display: inline-block; text-align: inherit; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; max-width: 100%; vertical-align: middle; box-sizing: border-box; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><img src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/146c17f5cc1fc1826576caa0d4fcb5c0.png" alt="解锁ASA广告投放新玩法，利用ASA新功能获客！" width="871" title="解锁ASA广告投放新玩法，利用ASA新功能获客！" style="width:871px;height:auto;" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;"> </span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;">针对日预算不同的情况，我们为广告主提供了两个方案：</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; display: flex; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; flex-flow: row nowrap; justify-content: flex-start; box-sizing: border-box; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">01.<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;">提前调整次日预算。</span></span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;"><br style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"></span></span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"><br style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"></span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; background-color: rgba(254, 255, 255, 0); font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;">02.创建多个广告系列，设置不同的日预算。</span></span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;">值得注意的是，方案二可能会导致预算难以控制的情况，因此还是需要根据实际情况灵活调整。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">#3 对特定系列有明确的预算要求</strong></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; box-sizing: border-box; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;">比如，一款游戏的测试预算是 100000 美金，按照此前的预算管理，只需设置广告系列预算为 100000 美金，改版之后，只能通过每天监控，直至消耗接近 100000 美金手动停止，对比下来，每日预算对这类产品不是很友好。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; display: inline-block; text-align: inherit; line-height: inherit; color: #000000; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; max-width: 100%; vertical-align: middle; box-sizing: border-box; background-image: none; font-weight: inherit;"><img src="https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com/23ca67cfd093157f2b1b68bafb1f0a3e.png" alt="解锁ASA广告投放新玩法，利用ASA新功能获客！" width="869" title="解锁ASA广告投放新玩法，利用ASA新功能获客！" style="width:869px;height:auto;" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<div style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #5d5d5d; font-family: helvetica, Arial, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', simsun; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff;">
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; box-sizing: border-box; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;">不过，针对这类情况也有相应的解决方案，广告主可以通过预算订单（Budget Order）控制总预算，但此功能仅对有授信的广告账户开放。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word; text-align: inherit; box-sizing: border-box; background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif;">或者是通过 management api创建，虽然 ASA 网页前端无法创建有总预算的广告系列，但 management api 在创建或修改广告系列时，仍可以设置、编辑总预算。</span></p>
