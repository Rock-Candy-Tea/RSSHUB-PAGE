                        <p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 微软雅黑, 微软雅黑, 'Microsoft YaHei', Helvetica, Tahoma, sans-serif; color: #000000; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">随着时代发展，带货已经成为一种潮流，越来越多的博主都在带货，B站是许多年轻人的聚集地，商品带货这块也是紧跟潮流，那么应该如何查看B站商品数据？发现带货优质视频呢？发掘优质带货达人？</p>
<h1 class="ql-align-justify" style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 微软雅黑, 微软雅黑, 'Microsoft YaHei', Helvetica, Tahoma, sans-serif; color: #212629; font-size: 20px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); text-align: center; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span style="font-weight: bolder;">一、商品搜索</span></h1>
<p class="ql-align-justify" style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 微软雅黑, 微软雅黑, 'Microsoft YaHei', Helvetica, Tahoma, sans-serif; color: #000000; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">通过商品关键词搜索来选择想要查看的商品内容，还可以通过商品分类细分品类、商品价格、关联商品、关联视频数、视频播放数、视频点赞数、视频评论数来筛选商品。</p>
<h4 class="ql-align-center" style="margin: 25px 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 微软雅黑, 微软雅黑, 'Microsoft YaHei', Helvetica, Tahoma, sans-serif; text-align: center; color: #818386; font-size: 14px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px;"><img style="border-style: none; display: block; margin: 10px auto 0px; max-width: 100%; height: auto;" src="https://p8.itc.cn/q_70/images03/20220913/87096037eb1d410f8d1114479b516f67.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img-desc" style="box-sizing: border-box; display: inline-block; width: 640px; margin: 5px auto 0px; line-height: 20px; color: #909090; padding: 6px 10px;">来源-果集·飞瓜数据（B站版）</span></h4>
<h1 class="ql-align-justify" style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 微软雅黑, 微软雅黑, 'Microsoft YaHei', Helvetica, Tahoma, sans-serif; color: #212629; font-size: 20px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); text-align: center; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span style="font-weight: bolder;">二、带货视频</span></h1>
<p class="ql-align-justify" style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 微软雅黑, 微软雅黑, 'Microsoft YaHei', Helvetica, Tahoma, sans-serif; color: #000000; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">发现近期up主带货视频，可通过视频标题和简介关键词、视频标签、视频评论来筛选带货视频，也可以根视频播放数、视频点赞数、视频评论数、视频收藏数、视频弹幕数、视频投币数进行筛选，快速定位到合适的带货视频，还可以自定义查询近24小时、近7天、近30天、近90天的带货视频数据情况，按照不同视频分类通过视频最新、最热来精准定位热门商品爆火当天的带货视频。</p>
<h4 class="ql-align-center" style="margin: 25px 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 微软雅黑, 微软雅黑, 'Microsoft YaHei', Helvetica, Tahoma, sans-serif; text-align: center; color: #818386; font-size: 14px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px;"><img style="border-style: none; display: block; margin: 10px auto 0px; max-width: 100%; height: auto;" src="https://p2.itc.cn/q_70/images03/20220913/a892a6be99394eee80b4f1af949dd4af.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img-desc" style="box-sizing: border-box; display: inline-block; width: 640px; margin: 5px auto 0px; line-height: 20px; color: #909090; padding: 6px 10px;">来源-果集·飞瓜数据（B站版）</span></h4>
<h1 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 微软雅黑, 微软雅黑, 'Microsoft YaHei', Helvetica, Tahoma, sans-serif; color: #212629; font-size: 20px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); text-align: center; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span style="font-weight: bolder;">三、商品分析</span></h1>
<p class="ql-align-justify" style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 微软雅黑, 微软雅黑, 'Microsoft YaHei', Helvetica, Tahoma, sans-serif; color: #000000; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">通过带货视频可查看该视频的关联商品分析，进入商品分析可以了解到该商品核心数据一览、关联视频、关联up主、观众画像、同类热门商品</p>
<h4 class="ql-align-center" style="margin: 25px 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 微软雅黑, 微软雅黑, 'Microsoft YaHei', Helvetica, Tahoma, sans-serif; text-align: center; color: #818386; font-size: 14px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px;"><img style="border-style: none; display: block; margin: 10px auto 0px; max-width: 100%; height: auto;" src="https://p2.itc.cn/q_70/images03/20220913/db619d24ddd14e8aa074c954f02d31aa.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img-desc" style="box-sizing: border-box; display: inline-block; width: 640px; margin: 5px auto 0px; line-height: 20px; color: #909090; padding: 6px 10px;">来源-果集·飞瓜数据（B站版）</span></h4>
<p class="ql-align-justify" style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 微软雅黑, 微软雅黑, 'Microsoft YaHei', Helvetica, Tahoma, sans-serif; color: #000000; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">通过关联UP主可以查看商品带货达人数据，了解up主的推广情况，分析视频的带货力，选出可以模仿的视频。</p>
<h4 class="ql-align-center" style="margin: 25px 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 微软雅黑, 微软雅黑, 'Microsoft YaHei', Helvetica, Tahoma, sans-serif; text-align: center; color: #818386; font-size: 14px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px;"><img style="border-style: none; display: block; margin: 10px auto 0px; max-width: 100%; height: auto;" src="https://p5.itc.cn/q_70/images03/20220913/2a06755576fe4c27b6f5e559e02697b0.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img-desc" style="box-sizing: border-box; display: inline-block; width: 640px; margin: 5px auto 0px; line-height: 20px; color: #909090; padding: 6px 10px;">来源-果集·飞瓜数据（B站版）</span></h4>
<p class="ql-align-justify" style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 微软雅黑, 微软雅黑, 'Microsoft YaHei', Helvetica, Tahoma, sans-serif; color: #000000; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">最后根据商品观众画像通过分析粉丝性别、等级、会员、星座、观众关注焦点等进一步确定商品所属的粉丝范围，更好的定位商品粉丝，做到精准带货。</p>
<h4 class="ql-align-center" style="margin: 25px 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 微软雅黑, 微软雅黑, 'Microsoft YaHei', Helvetica, Tahoma, sans-serif; text-align: center; color: #818386; font-size: 14px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px;"><img style="border-style: none; display: block; margin: 10px auto 0px; max-width: 100%; height: auto;" src="https://p0.itc.cn/q_70/images03/20220913/831b510e9a0a4b3986d29d84214f7398.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img-desc" style="box-sizing: border-box; display: inline-block; width: 640px; margin: 5px auto 0px; line-height: 20px; color: #909090; padding: 6px 10px;">来源-果集·飞瓜数据（B站版）</span></h4>
<h1 class="ql-align-justify" style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 微软雅黑, 微软雅黑, 'Microsoft YaHei', Helvetica, Tahoma, sans-serif; color: #212629; font-size: 20px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//48aedb8880cab8c45637abc7493ecddd.png'); text-align: center; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; background-size: 16px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span style="font-weight: bolder;">四、商品排行榜</span></h1>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 微软雅黑, 微软雅黑, 'Microsoft YaHei', Helvetica, Tahoma, sans-serif; color: #000000; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">商品排行榜可以筛选不同商品分类的周榜、月榜排行商品，还可以同步查看B站商品关联视频数、播放数、点赞数、评论数等数据，直观了解“声量效果”比较好的爆款商品，让你快速确认当下不同类型的账号推荐哪种品类商品在B站的投放效果最好</p>
<h4 class="ql-align-center" style="margin: 25px 0px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 微软雅黑, 微软雅黑, 'Microsoft YaHei', Helvetica, Tahoma, sans-serif; text-align: center; color: #818386; font-size: 14px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; font-weight: 300; line-height: 30px;"><img style="border-style: none; display: block; margin: 10px auto 0px; max-width: 100%; height: auto;" src="https://p4.itc.cn/q_70/images03/20220913/77492a9376894639a1371b5b257fa62d.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img-desc" style="box-sizing: border-box; display: inline-block; width: 640px; margin: 5px auto 0px; line-height: 20px; color: #909090; padding: 6px 10px;">来源-果集·飞瓜数据（B站版）</span></h4>
<h2 style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 微软雅黑, 微软雅黑, 'Microsoft YaHei', Helvetica, Tahoma, sans-serif; color: #212629; font-size: 18px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); text-align: left; font-weight: 600; line-height: 30px; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span style="font-weight: bolder;">总结</span></h2>
<p style="margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; font-family: 'PingFang SC', 微软雅黑, 微软雅黑, 'Microsoft YaHei', Helvetica, Tahoma, sans-serif; color: #000000; font-size: 16px; background-color: #ffffff; background-image: none; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">B站种草商品分析相关带货up主，种草视频，画像分析等数据，快速发现B站近期的热销商品，了解竞品投放策略，学习带货力强劲的爆款视频，挖掘商品背后的优质带货达人！</p>                        <div style="height: 1px"></div>
