                        <h2 style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; line-height: 30px; font-size: 18px; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">前言</span></h2>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">去年在金投赏大会上，小红书首次面向行业发布最新的品牌营销方法论—— IDEA方法论，为品牌成长梳理新思路。在此之后IDEA方法论频繁被提及，那么IDEA方法论究竟是什么呢，品牌又应该怎样通过IDEA方法论更好的与用户建立联系呢，我们一起往下看吧！</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="box-sizing: border-box; vertical-align: middle; border-style: none; margin: 6px 0px !important; padding: 0px; cursor: pointer; max-width: 100%;" src="https://yixiaoer-img.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/20220627/6d7eb028-e27d-4be8-bba7-5359652b5cc8.jpg" title="科学种草 | 品牌营销的新灯塔：IDEA方法论" alt="科学种草 | 品牌营销的新灯塔：IDEA方法论" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h2 style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; line-height: 30px; font-size: 18px; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">什么是IDEA方法论</span></h2>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">小红书IDEA方法论核心包含四个环节：Insight数据洞察、Define定义产品、Expand抢占赛道、Advocate拥护品牌。这四个环节形成一个营销阶段，品牌每完成一个营销阶段阶段后就可以启动下一个阶段，形成一个闭环。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="box-sizing: border-box; vertical-align: middle; border-style: none; margin: 6px 0px !important; padding: 0px; cursor: pointer; max-width: 100%;" src="https://yixiaoer-img.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/20220627/f01e45df-427b-40b0-85fa-e275ff05990b.jpg" title="科学种草 | 品牌营销的新灯塔：IDEA方法论" alt="科学种草 | 品牌营销的新灯塔：IDEA方法论" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">在IDEA方法论的指导下，品牌应该如何借力小红书的潮流趋势、利用优质内容与消费者进行更好的沟通，并进一步影响他们的消费决策的呢？下面我们就每一个环节具体分析。</p>
<h2 style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; line-height: 30px; font-size: 18px; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">INSIGHT 数据洞察</span></h2>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">Insight就是数据洞察。基于我们全量的数据、BrandTech算法和强大的自研BI分析工具可以帮助品牌诊断营销问题，定位产品痛点，找到营销机会，落地种草方案。具体的可以从以下三步进行分析。</p>
<h3 style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; line-height: 30px; font-size: 16px; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//1bb91f73e9d31ea2830a5e73ce3ed328.png'); color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">1、</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">行业分析</span></h3>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">行业分析从市场规模、行业增长、用户红利、平台流量四个方面洞悉市场情况，发现更多市场红利，可以用于指导新品研发，选择主推商品，制定品牌多产品联合推广策略等。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">市场规模和行业增长可以通过小红书笔记的规模和增长情况来看，用户红利可以通过素人笔记的占比来看，当一个品牌或一个行业素人笔记很多时，说明有很好的群众基础，种草效果往往也会较好，平台流量取决于平台算法的流量倾斜，平台觉得某一个品类或行业很热时，就会导入较多的流量。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="box-sizing: border-box; vertical-align: middle; border-style: none; margin: 6px 0px !important; padding: 0px; cursor: pointer; max-width: 100%;" src="https://yixiaoer-img.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/20220627/642cfc4a-2b8d-464d-9cf3-9cc8c6d3752a.jpg" title="科学种草 | 品牌营销的新灯塔：IDEA方法论" alt="科学种草 | 品牌营销的新灯塔：IDEA方法论" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px; padding: 0px; line-height: 30px; font-size: 14px; background-image: none; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300;">* 节选自《小红书行业品牌竞争格局分析报告》(共12页)</h4>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">举例：彩妆行业各品类中，虽然腮红的行业占比不及口红/唇膏，用户红利不及底妆，但是在行业趋势和平台流量方面表现特别好，所以综合得分很高。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="box-sizing: border-box; vertical-align: middle; border-style: none; margin: 6px 0px !important; padding: 0px; cursor: pointer; max-width: 100%;" src="https://yixiaoer-img.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/20220627/5ccade33-2822-47f2-8a2a-a85d0d58643b.jpg" title="科学种草 | 品牌营销的新灯塔：IDEA方法论" alt="科学种草 | 品牌营销的新灯塔：IDEA方法论" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h3 style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; line-height: 30px; font-size: 16px; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//1bb91f73e9d31ea2830a5e73ce3ed328.png'); color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">2、</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">竞争格局分析</span></h3>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">商品分为平价商品和贵价商品，平价商品的拔草逻辑与贵价商品不同，平价商品的拔草逻辑是冲动消费，用户在发现页刷到产品就容易下单，贵价商品用户习惯搜索之后反复比价，所以<span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">如果商品更加偏向于平价，则更多需要关注互动指标如果商品偏向于贵价，则更多的需要关注搜索指标。</span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">而小红书60%的流量来自于发现页，40%的流量来自于搜索页。发现页流量和搜索页流量的规模基本相当。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="box-sizing: border-box; vertical-align: middle; border-style: none; margin: 6px 0px !important; padding: 0px; cursor: pointer; max-width: 100%;" src="https://yixiaoer-img.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/20220627/a46af426-4bdc-4ff6-b667-f429c8d9a32b.jpg" title="科学种草 | 品牌营销的新灯塔：IDEA方法论" alt="科学种草 | 品牌营销的新灯塔：IDEA方法论" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px; padding: 0px; line-height: 30px; font-size: 14px; background-image: none; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300;">* 节选自《小红书行业品牌竞争格局分析报告》(共16页)</h4>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">我们的竞争格局分析报告就是从互动和搜索两个维度，分析各个品牌的竞争情况，评估要达到局部最优，需要的预算花费。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="box-sizing: border-box; vertical-align: middle; border-style: none; margin: 6px 0px !important; padding: 0px; cursor: pointer; max-width: 100%;" src="https://yixiaoer-img.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/20220627/8d4904f8-1135-4b19-b37d-4d8c4118828e.jpg" title="科学种草 | 品牌营销的新灯塔：IDEA方法论" alt="科学种草 | 品牌营销的新灯塔：IDEA方法论" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px; padding: 0px; line-height: 30px; font-size: 14px; background-image: none; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300;">* 节选自《小红书行业品牌竞争格局分析报告》(共16页)</h4>
<h3 style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; line-height: 30px; font-size: 16px; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//1bb91f73e9d31ea2830a5e73ce3ed328.png'); color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">3、</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">竞品分析</span></h3>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">竞品分析是对竞品一段时间内所有营销数据进行归纳和总结，通过赛马机制看竞品这段时间内的投放量、投放节奏、达人选择、笔记类目等。如果竞品投放的效果好，我们可以通过数据分析，学习竞品是以怎样的节奏投放，爆文都有哪些规律，选择了什么样的达人，制作了什么类型的笔记等积累经验；如果竞品投放的效果差，我们还可以精准避雷，吸取教训防止踩坑。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="box-sizing: border-box; vertical-align: middle; border-style: none; margin: 6px 0px !important; padding: 0px; cursor: pointer; max-width: 100%;" src="https://yixiaoer-img.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/20220627/eaff0859-cdaa-48af-bcd6-972e3a23a195.jpg" title="科学种草 | 品牌营销的新灯塔：IDEA方法论" alt="科学种草 | 品牌营销的新灯塔：IDEA方法论" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px; padding: 0px; line-height: 30px; font-size: 14px; background-image: none; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 12px;">* 节选自《小红书竞品分析报告》(共12页)</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 12px;"> </span></h4>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">另外竞品分析报告也可以用于品牌投放预算的预估，以及指导具体投放策略的制定。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="box-sizing: border-box; vertical-align: middle; border-style: none; margin: 6px 0px !important; padding: 0px; cursor: pointer; max-width: 100%;" src="https://yixiaoer-img.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/20220627/fc46923d-0b6d-4e9d-925b-9fe894f96ffe.jpg" title="科学种草 | 品牌营销的新灯塔：IDEA方法论" alt="科学种草 | 品牌营销的新灯塔：IDEA方法论" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px; padding: 0px; line-height: 30px; font-size: 14px; background-image: none; color: #818386; text-align: center; font-weight: 300;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 12px;">* 节选自《小红书竞品</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 12px;">分析报告》(</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 12px;">共12</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 12px;">页)</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 12px;"></span></h4>
<h2 style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; line-height: 30px; font-size: 18px; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">DEFINE 定义产品</span></h2>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">Define最主要的任务是确定营销的内容策略，包括怎么确定内容和角度、梳理卖点、挑选关键词、匹配黑马达人。小红书是一个内容平台，品牌无论营销最后落脚在内容上。</p>
<h3 style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; line-height: 30px; font-size: 16px; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//1bb91f73e9d31ea2830a5e73ce3ed328.png'); color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">1、</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">5A内容营销模型</span></h3>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">5A模型是由现代营销之父菲利普·科特勒提出。5A模型拥有消费者行为学AIPL模型的基础，根据阿里用户行为数据研究2021，一个消费者从引起注意到购买平均触点是5.2次。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">5A模型是对AIPL的内容表达，目标是通过优质内容运营让用户产生病毒式的传播，分别对应了Aware、Appeal、Ask、Act、Advocate这五个单词的缩写。从了解-吸引-问询-行动-拥护这5个环节来让用户记住品牌并下单购买，主动进行二次传播。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="box-sizing: border-box; vertical-align: middle; border-style: none; margin: 6px 0px !important; padding: 0px; cursor: pointer; max-width: 100%;" src="https://yixiaoer-img.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/20220627/5dae87d8-cab8-45b8-bbaf-7e0e14c4af7a.jpg" title="科学种草 | 品牌营销的新灯塔：IDEA方法论" alt="科学种草 | 品牌营销的新灯塔：IDEA方法论" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">了解</span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">让用户在不同场景下看到品牌，产生好奇，激发兴趣。在此环节，达人选择方面KOC和KOL均可，性价比优先，成熟品牌注重扩圈。笔记内容上应以合集内容为主，多与知名品牌合集，蹭取流量。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">吸引</span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">让用户反复看到大量不同角度的内容，吸引用户互动。在此环节达人选择应以KOC和素人为主，同样以性价比优先。笔记内容建议紧跟平台热度，结合热门话题，大量场景化种草。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">问询</span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">让用户在主动关注和搜索时看到优质内容增加信任度。此阶段需要投放一些高粉达人，注重企业账号内容运营，笔记内容以专家科普、测评，高质量单篇以及企业账号笔记等为主。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">行动</span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">挖掘用户心智，着力解决拥护购买决策面临的问题。此阶段如果从小红书到天猫的转化已经稳定了，可以考虑减少KOL的投入，笔记内容以单篇和一些场景化种草为主，加强评论的维护。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">拥护</span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">用户在购买产品后，不断受到优质内容激发，资源创作内容。此阶段可以少量稳定的投放KOC，保持着品牌在小红书上的内容沉淀。笔记内容应该多跟随平台的新玩法和新话题，触达更多人群。</p>
<h3 style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; line-height: 30px; font-size: 16px; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//1bb91f73e9d31ea2830a5e73ce3ed328.png'); color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">2、</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">笔记关键词</span></h3>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">笔记关键词是小红书上的流量密码，埋入合适的关键词有助于笔记获取更多流量。在小红书平台上，根据不同场景，关键词分为三种类型：</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">搜索红利词</span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">在平台上被大量搜索，相关笔记却很少的词，例如，痘印怎么去除，每月搜索曝光量约千万左右，但每月新笔记仅一两百篇。我们埋入这些词，就可以收获很多搜索流量。</p>

<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">搜索排位关键词</span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">品类词的搜索结果竞争往往非常激烈，如果品牌想让笔记在某些品类词下排名靠前，就可以考虑埋入搜索排位关键词。搜索排位关键词可以通过以下模型计算得到。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="box-sizing: border-box; vertical-align: middle; border-style: none; margin: 6px 0px !important; padding: 0px; cursor: pointer; max-width: 100%;" src="https://yixiaoer-img.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/20220627/fdb9b0cd-b7b9-4269-a68c-95441ccf4528.jpg" title="科学种草 | 品牌营销的新灯塔：IDEA方法论" alt="科学种草 | 品牌营销的新灯塔：IDEA方法论" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px; padding: 0px; line-height: 30px; text-align: center; font-size: 14px; background-image: none; color: #818386; font-weight: 300;">▲ 搜索排位关键词计算模型</h4>
<h2 style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 12px; line-height: 30px; font-size: 18px; background-image: url('https://qnssl.niaogebiji.com//6cdd60ea0045eb7a6ec44c54d29ed402.png'); color: #212629; text-align: left; font-weight: 600; background-size: 8px 20px; background-position: 0px 5px; background-repeat: no-repeat;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">热度关键词</span></h2>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">通过模型计算得到目前平台热度较高的关键词和话题，在笔记中埋入这些热度关键词，可以提升笔记的曝光。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="box-sizing: border-box; vertical-align: middle; border-style: none; margin: 6px 0px !important; padding: 0px; cursor: pointer; max-width: 100%;" src="https://yixiaoer-img.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/20220627/a2b868c7-fd51-4f88-9605-936264b49301.jpg" title="科学种草 | 品牌营销的新灯塔：IDEA方法论" alt="科学种草 | 品牌营销的新灯塔：IDEA方法论" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px; padding: 0px; line-height: 30px; text-align: center; font-size: 14px; background-image: none; color: #818386; font-weight: 300;">▲ 热度关键词的计算模型</h4>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">关键词既可以作为笔记的切入方向，又可以直接埋入，能够大幅提高笔记的搜索和推荐流量。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">3、</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">达人选择</span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">在投放的过程中，选择什么样的达人也是很重要的一部分，毕竟笔记是达人自己创造的，好的达人可以与品牌共创，生产出更多优质的内容。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">我们的大致选人过程是：</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">1. 从1.5亿的达人库中筛选出某个行业活跃达人，因为很多达人的活跃程度具有阶段性，可能之前更新频率较高的达人，近期活跃度有所下降所以需要实时筛选。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">2. 计算达人的各项指标，我们计算的达人指标有将近100个维度，可以根据品牌的投放要求，个性化定制指标条件，筛选出合适达人。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="box-sizing: border-box; vertical-align: middle; border-style: none; margin: 6px 0px !important; padding: 0px; cursor: pointer; max-width: 100%;" src="https://yixiaoer-img.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/20220627/1081941c-4c70-483f-8295-b650a481651d.jpg" title="科学种草 | 品牌营销的新灯塔：IDEA方法论" alt="科学种草 | 品牌营销的新灯塔：IDEA方法论" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<h4 style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px; padding: 0px; line-height: 30px; text-align: center; font-size: 14px; background-image: none; color: #818386; font-weight: 300;">▲ 火箭选人系统中的达人主要指标</h4>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">3. 通过算法对达人去水，提升种草效果。因为有一部分达人制作的笔记内容并不受用户喜欢，这类笔记存在达人内部互评互暖的情况，数据水分较大，不适合用于投放。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">4. 计算达人的商业化程度，排除广告接单号。我们前面的文章也有分析过，小红书是通过收录的方式来保证平台没有过度商业化，从分析的结果我们也可以看到，选号对于收录的影响要比内容对于收录的影响大得多，所以要多选择一些商业程度低的账号。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">5. 采用半监督的方式进行指标权重的分配，通过对种子达人进行排名校验，优化指标权重，最后得到达人列表。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="box-sizing: border-box; vertical-align: middle; border-style: none; margin: 6px 0px !important; padding: 0px; cursor: pointer; max-width: 100%;" src="https://yixiaoer-img.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/20220627/ac6de187-ca6e-4aee-aee8-b2d8d12065d0.jpg" title="科学种草 | 品牌营销的新灯塔：IDEA方法论" alt="科学种草 | 品牌营销的新灯塔：IDEA方法论" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">在定义产品的时候，需要做好以上三个部分，并非简单的从卖点出发设计内容，更多的需要使用赛马机制，充分利用平台红利，顺应用户需求，进行内容迭代，卖点需先被用户喜欢，才能被用户接受。</p>

<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">抢占赛道的核心是要提升效率与性价比，抢占赛道需要在最短时间或者最高性价比的前提下，尽可能多的增加品牌曝光，当曝光积累到一定程度，即可实现赢家通吃。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">1、</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">达人种草</span></p>

<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">常规的种草流程比较简单，通常都会用excel表格管理，一个种草项目可能会产生十多张表格，记录过程极易出错。</p>

<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">虽然CPC投放的性比价不及我们达人种草的性价比，但可以在短时间内精准触达更多用户。在日常的种草中也可以配合一定的CPC投放。很多品牌会疑惑我究竟该在什么时候投放CPC呢？</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">CPC的主要使用场景包括：</p>

<h4 style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px; padding: 0px; line-height: 30px; text-align: center; font-size: 14px; background-image: none; color: #818386; font-weight: 300;">▲ 星云CPC投放系统</h4>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">我们CPC的投放效果好主要<span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">是因为CPC的投放效果取决于两个方面，曝光率和点击率。</span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">曝光率</span>方面我们可以计算搜索词的红利，找到流量大而竞争小的词，以低价获得更多曝光。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">点击率</span>方面是因为我们种草时爆文率就极高，我们通过大数据挖掘怎样的笔记更容易被点击，容易获得更多的点击量。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">因为这两方面的明显优势，我们的CPC投放效果要远远领先其他机构，而我们的CPC投放系统将会进一步的扩大这一优势。</p>

<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">Advocate是将单品认知沉淀为品牌认知，进一步导向转化购买。通过持续种草，反复触达用户，加强用户复购，通过优质种草可以带来用户自发的二次分享，产生更多品牌内容沉淀。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">1、</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">转化归因</span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">虽然小红书种草并不能直接跳转到天猫，但用户的一般都是在社媒平台被种草后去天猫搜索下单，同时由于天猫中的搜索流量基本来自于站外，因此通过对搜索流量的分析就可以得出种草的效果。</p>

<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">小红书到天猫的转化一般有4部分，通过商卡转化、通过品销宝转化、通过购物车转化、通过淘内免费其他转化。</p>
<p style="background-image: none; font-size: 16px; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; margin: 25px 0px 20px;"><img style="box-sizing: border-box; vertical-align: middle; border-style: none; margin: 6px 0px !important; padding: 0px; cursor: pointer; max-width: 100%;" src="https://yixiaoer-img.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/20220627/0590d71a-4ced-458c-b122-9d54e296189c.jpg" title="科学种草 | 品牌营销的新灯塔：IDEA方法论" alt="科学种草 | 品牌营销的新灯塔：IDEA方法论" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>

<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">每完成一个营销阶段，我们可以收集到更多的用户行为数据。对用户行为进行分析，可以找到拥护品牌的那个TA。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">我们的小红书消费者分析报告，是通过大数据从性别、年龄、地区、类目、兴趣话题、关注品牌、评论内容以及发布笔记等维度对这些兴趣用户进行分析。并通过100多个维度对用户进行聚类，得到品牌各类典型用户，进一步分析这些用户的行为习惯，梳理品牌个性，反向指导下一步的运营策略。</p>

<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">对于品牌来说，种草是一个积累的过程，小红书有branding的效果，相比脉冲式投放，持续稳定的种草，使得基础更扎实且有长尾效应。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">持续种草可以带来更多爆文与互动，在不断种草的过程中，不断优化内容和选人，在赛马机制的作用下，爆率文会不断提升，我们合作的某些品牌在半年的持续种草后，单品KOC爆文率甚至可以达到40%。</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 25px 0px 20px; padding: 0px; line-height: inherit; font-size: 16px; background-image: none; color: #000000; text-align: inherit; font-weight: inherit;">持续种草可以给品牌带来更多的自发笔记，普通用户在互动之后有可能产生一篇自发笔记，通过下图的统计，我们得知平均约300个互动可以带来一篇自发笔记，持续种草可以提升篇均互动，带来更多自来水。</p>
