text-decoration-color: initial; color: rgb(0, 0, 0); text-align: center;">全景天幕</p><p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://mp.ofweek.com/Upload/News/Img/member44896/202206/wx_article__f7fc3bd7f2f17370914a6c9bad4be25c.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.6px; box-sizing: border-box; font-style: normal; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-indent: 2em; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial; color: rgb(0, 0, 0); text-align: center;">无框车门</p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.6px; box-sizing: border-box; font-style: normal; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-indent: 2em; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial; color: rgb(0, 0, 0); text-align: left;">不过，最让人感到“可恶”的并不是这两个配置，而是有网友提到的“自动启停”。这个配置，相信大家也不陌生，但将它称之为“过街老鼠”也不为过，因为不少车主用过后都深恶痛绝。</p><p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://mp.ofweek.com/Upload/News/Img/member44896/202206/wx_article__5333f58690c0c104705518f4d5f8b9d7.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.6px; box-sizing: border-box; font-style: normal; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-indent: 2em; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial; color: rgb(0, 0, 0); text-align: left;">顾名思义，自动启停就是一套能控制发动机在特定时刻自动熄火／点火的装置。通常情况下，驾驶员踩下刹车，车辆停下来之后，自动关闭发动机，以达到节省燃油的目的。在松开刹车踏板后，它又能重新启动发动机。</p><p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://mp.ofweek.com/Upload/News/Img/member44896/202206/wx_article__699563b0b22ca24c708f6a644a8fffa9.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.6px; box-sizing: border-box; font-style: normal; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-indent: 2em; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial; color: rgb(0, 0, 0); text-align: left;">所以，自动启停的初衷是为了节能环保，在长时间怠速停车等待的时候，关闭发动机，避免无意义的能耗和尾气排放。</p><p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://mp.ofweek.com/Upload/News/Img/member44896/202206/wx_article__b58f10701f7e7f6bb6b7ede2df9254c7.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.6px; box-sizing: border-box; font-style: normal; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-indent: 2em; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial; color: rgb(0, 0, 0); text-align: left;">理想是丰满的，现实却很骨感。从实际使用情况来看，自动启停往往会出现发动机抖动、车辆窜动等不好的体验，而且频繁熄火点火，会对<span class="hrefStyle"><a href="https://www.ofweek.com/auto/" target="_blank" title="汽车">汽车</a></span>零部件（发动机、起动机、皮带、电池）造成磨损，降低零部件的寿命。</p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.6px; box-sizing: border-box; font-style: normal; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-indent: 2em; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial; color: rgb(0, 0, 0); text-align: left;">因此，对于大多数拥有自动启停功能的车主来说，通常的操作是第一时间关掉这个功能。而且，让不少车主感到恶心的是，这个功能好像还不能永久关闭，只能每次上车启动时关闭。更过分的是，有的车辆没有设置关闭的按钮，车主只能默默忍受。</p><p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://mp.ofweek.com/Upload/News/Img/member44896/202206/wx_article__d3afa2b1c5fc2d21afe835d89525b166.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.6px; box-sizing: border-box; font-style: normal; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-indent: 2em; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial; color: rgb(0, 0, 0); text-align: left;">为什么会出现发动机抖动的不良体验呢？因为大多数燃油车，都是采用12V电气系统，在这样的电压下，起动机通常只能把发动机转速提到300rpm左右，想要拉到正常的怠速转速（800rpm左右），还需要发动机自身通过浓混合油气，将转速强行拉起，这个过程不可避免产生发动机抖动。</p><p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://mp.ofweek.com/Upload/News/Img/member44896/202206/wx_article__a651c377c770d546bb7897cb8c66cabc.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.6px; box-sizing: border-box; font-style: normal; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-indent: 2em; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial; color: rgb(0, 0, 0); text-align: left;">既然天生就有“缺陷”，为啥车企们还要给新车安装这样的配置呢？当然是为了降低车辆的油耗。要知道，国内目前采用的是NEDC工况测油耗，这种测试一个循环周期共1180秒，其中市区循环就占整个循环66％。所以，采用自动启停能有效降低怠速油耗，从而改善整车的综合油耗值。</p><p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://mp.ofweek.com/Upload/News/Img/member44896/202206/wx_article__af2ad23ca33ae1a8bd2e00ac94a40caa.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.6px; box-sizing: border-box; font-style: normal; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-indent: 2em; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial; color: rgb(0, 0, 0); text-align: left;">当然，不知道大家有没有发现，并不是所有的自动启停，体验都不好。有的新车上虽然搭载了自动启停系统，但停机的逻辑更加智能化，而且发动机也不会有明显的抖动，“存在感”也更低。</p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.6px; box-sizing: border-box; font-style: normal; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-indent: 2em; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial; color: rgb(0, 0, 0); text-align: left;">这意味自动启停的“缺陷”，还是有办法解决的，通常是采用48V轻混系统。</p><p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://mp.ofweek.com/Upload/News/Img/member44896/202206/wx_article__ce678e0c88c986412968e9e75c0cbc88.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.6px; box-sizing: border-box; font-style: normal; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-indent: 2em; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial; color: rgb(0, 0, 0); text-align: left;">相比于常规的12V电气系统，48V电压相对较高，可以很轻松的搭配大功率的BSG起发电一体机，也就可以轻松把发动机推到800rpm正常怠速，避开发动机启动瞬间的费油和抖动，改善自动启停的使用体验。</p><p style="text-indent: 2em; text-align: left;"><strong>小结</strong></p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.6px; box-sizing: border-box; font-style: normal; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-indent: 2em; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial; color: rgb(0, 0, 0); text-align: left;">回到最开始的问题，自动启停为啥会成为“过街老鼠”？疆哥认为，那些仅仅为了“应试教育”、“对付法规要求”而采用自动启停的车企，得负上不可推卸的责任。</p><p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.6px; box-sizing: border-box; font-style: normal; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-indent: 2em; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial; color: rgb(0, 0, 0); text-align: left;">当然，大家也不用一棒子把自动启停“否掉”，毕竟从出发点来看，自动启停还是一个不错的想法。对此，你怎么看呢？欢迎关注“车域无疆”，在下方评论区留言，分享你对自动启停的看法。</p><p>       <span style="color:#999999;font-family:  微软雅黑;font-size:14px;">原文标题 : 被车企视为“香饽饽”的自动启停，为啥如今却成了“过街老鼠”?</span></p>
