<p><strong>评选背景</strong></p><p>       为加速传统经济向数字经济转型融合，由中国高科技行业门户OFweek维科网主办的<strong>“OFweek2022中国智能制造<span class="hrefStyle"><a href="http://robot.ofweek.com/KW-shuzihua.html" target="_blank" class="hrefStyle"><a href="https://gongkong.ofweek.com/" target="_blank" title="自动化">自动化</a></span>及数字化行业年度评选（OFweek Industrial Automation & Digitization Awards
