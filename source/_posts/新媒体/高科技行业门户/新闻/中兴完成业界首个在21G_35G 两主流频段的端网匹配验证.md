box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; text-indent: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">据悉，</span><span style="letter-spacing: 0.544px;">这是业界唯一一个在2.1G、3.5G 两个主流频段进行的端网匹配验证。</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: left; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: left; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; text-indent: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">相比较之前的EPS Fallback 回落方案（5G用户会回落到4G网络，使用Vo<span class="hrefStyle"><a href="http://fiber.ofweek.com/KW-LTE.html" target="_blank" title="LTE">LTE</a></span>进行语音业务），<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">VoNR</span>通话方案呼叫建立时延更短、音质与画质更高、用户体验更优。</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: left; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); box-sizing: border-box !important; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: left; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: left; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; text-indent: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">同时，也验证了多种场景下的语音及数据的连续性，包括 2.1G与3.5G异频间的语音及数据业务连续性、VoNR到4G VoLTE的业务连续性；以及呼叫保持及呼叫前转等补充业务的性能等。<br>作为全球领先的5G市场，2021年国内5G SA网络商用进程进一步加快，VoNR今年也会在国内商用落地。</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: left; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);
