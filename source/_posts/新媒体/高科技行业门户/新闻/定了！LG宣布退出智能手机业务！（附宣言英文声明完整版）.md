<p style="text-indent: 2em; text-align: left;">4月5日，LG正式官宣退出手机业务！</p><p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://images.ofweek.com/Upload/News/2021-04/05/gui/1617602339223037166.jpg" title="定了！LG宣布退出智能手机业务！" alt="定了！LG宣布退出智能手机业务！" width="800" height="408" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p style="text-indent: 2em; text-align: center;">截图自engadget网站</p><p style="text-indent: 2em; text-align: left;">LG，今日（4月5日）正式宣布，全面退出手机市场。</p><p style="text-indent: 2em; text-align: left;">综合engadget和韩国媒体消息，此前传言的「LG将完全退出手机市场」消息，如今被LG官方证实了。LG官方消息指出，关闭手机业务后，相关员工将被转移至旗下其他单位。</p><p style="text-indent: 2em; text-align: left;">以下是LG官方声明英文全文：<br></p><p style="text-indent: 2em; text-align: left;">LG Electronics Inc． （LG） announced that it is closing its mobile business unit． The decision was approved by its board of directors earlier today．</p><p style="text-indent: 2em; text-align: left;">LG’s strategic decision to exit the incredibly competitive mobile phone sector will enable the company to focus resources in growth areas such as electric vehicle components， connected devices， smart homes， robotics， artificial intelligence and business－to－business solutions， as well as platforms and services．</p><p style="text-indent: 2em; text-align: left;">Current LG phone inventory will continue to be available for sale． LG will provide service support and software updates for customers of existing mobile products for a period of time which will vary by region． LG will work collaboratively with suppliers and business partners throughout the closure of the mobile phone business． Details related to employment will be determined at the local level．</p><p style="text-indent: 2em; text-align: left;">Moving forward， LG will continue to leverage its mobile expertise and develop mobility－related technologies such as 6G to help further strengthen competitiveness in other business areas． Core technologies developed during the two decades of LG’s mobile business operations will also be retained and applied to existing and future products．</p><p style="text-indent: 2em; text-align: left;">The wind down of the mobile phone business is expected to be completed by July 31， although inventory of some existing models may still be available after that．</p>
