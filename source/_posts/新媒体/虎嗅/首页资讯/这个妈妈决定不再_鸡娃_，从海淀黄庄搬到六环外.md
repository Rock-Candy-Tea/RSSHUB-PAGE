style="text-decoration: none;"><span class="text-remarks">谷雨影像-腾讯新闻（ID：ihuozhe）</span></a><span class="text-remarks">，作者：窝头，编辑：迦沐梓、周安，头图来自：视觉中国</span></p><p>自从搬到北京六环外的农村，几乎每周都有朋友到访。几位城里朋友路过村边的水库时正赶上日落，于是一起看着太阳一点一点掉到山的那一边。</p><p>没想到，如此每天都能看到的风景，成了大家口中不断喟叹的“奢侈”之事。“已经记不得上一次看日落是什么时候了。等想到该下班的时候，一抬头，呀！9点了。”一位来自互联网大厂的朋友说。</p><h3 label="大标题" class="text-big-title">内卷的生活里，没有“窗外”</h3><p>两年前的我，也在一家大厂工作。那时的我，每天的日常基本是这样的——一睁眼，面对的是手机上无数个未读消息和群消息红点。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100514267632.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="667" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100514267632.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100515478845.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="667" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100515478845.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>起床后到出门上班前的半小时，是女儿为数不多能看见我这个“大活人”的时间。她当然不知道“上班迟到”为何物，她需要妈妈多陪一会儿，我总要在孩子的哭闹声中强行告别。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100516632706.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="667" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100516632706.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>即使只有两公里的路，我也习惯了打车。一来为了争取时间不迟到，二来可以处理待回复的消息。</p><p>到了公司，刷卡，然后坐到自己的工位上。这一坐，大概得12个小时。甚至为了少上厕所，我会有意识地少喝水。可怕的是，要处理的事情越来越多，下班的时间被拉锯得越来越晚。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100518284687.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="667" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100518284687.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>等到终于下班回家，孩子早已由老人哄睡了。</p><p>每天“窗外”上演着晴空、晚霞、日落、月夜……而我压根儿意识不到这些的存在，甚至对四季也没有太强烈的感受。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100522487502.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="667" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100522487502.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>比晚下班更难受的，是这一天很忙，但并不知道自己忙了什么、为什么而忙。</p><p>我的很多同事，工作是他们实现梦想的重要路径之一，且游刃有余。但我自己，却成了那个每天生动演绎着“内卷”的人。</p><h3 label="大标题" class="text-big-title">一个春日的24小时</h3><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100524722259.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="667" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100524722259.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>直到有一天，我和先生去六环外参加了一所学校的开放日。不同于“鸡娃”模式的教育理念，他们尊重孩子的天性和成长规律，偏重自然教育。</p><p>仿佛自己身上积压已久的对另一种活法的渴望，在女儿的教育问题上找到了可以改变的火苗，我一下子被点燃了。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100525450581.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="667" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100525450581.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>当即，我们约看了一套多年没人住的农村小院，签下了10年的租房合同。在签合同后的第7天，我提了辞职，把家从海淀黄庄搬到了这里。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100526583231.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="667" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100526583231.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100528385534.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="667" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100528385534.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>前段时间，村子背靠的大山里，山桃花开了，这里一丛，那里一堆，开得满满当当。我们折了几支，摘下花骨朵，晒干了可泡山桃花茶。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100529716918.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="667" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100529716918.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100530549457.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="667" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100530549457.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>自从来了村里，跟着村民们，我们长了不少见识，仿佛万物皆可“吃”。</p><p>柳芽长出来了，有人摘了带回家，路边还有老头老太太在捡杨树花，也有人开始一把把地捋榆钱，随后是香椿叶、核桃芽、花椒芽、桑树叶，以及槐花……这些可以做成美味的馅饼，或炒鸡蛋，或做疙瘩汤，有着各自的风味。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100532003828.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="667" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100532003828.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>我妈每天乐此不疲地去挖荠菜。她曾对我的决定暗自流泪许久，直到她为了照顾我女儿，也来到这个农村生活，才理解了这一切。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100534654877.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="667" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100534654877.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100537493264.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="667" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100537493264.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>我家院子里有一小块土地，种了花，也种了些菜，一到春天，看着破土而出的各种植物，就像一次次揭开宝藏的谜底。冬天让一切看起来都死掉了，但总有新的生命又充满生机地长出来，就像拥有一次又一次失而复得的快乐。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100538095027.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="667" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100538095027.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>一小片土地，就令人如此感叹自然的神奇。因为有了停下来的心境，我也终于有了那双本就属于我的发现美的眼睛。</p><h3 label="大标题" class="text-big-title">不鸡娃，不鸡自己</h3><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100540147896.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="667" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100540147896.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>虽然是在农村，我们所在的也是一个独特的社区，聚齐了理念相同的家长。</p><p>在这里，家长褪去了各自的社会身份，不谈房子、车子、股票、名包，甚至很少谈及彼此的工作，更别说比较收入。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100541745992.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="667" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100541745992.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>大家聚在一起，聊得最多的是育儿，但毫无鸡娃属性——村里时常有育儿课程，邀请国内外专家来讲课，也有几个育儿的读书会，我参加过几次。在这里，家长们关注如何让自己成为更好的“环境”，如何理解和接纳孩子，让孩子“自我成长”，而不是去逼迫他们快速成长。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100543573651.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="667" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100543573651.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100544140183.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="667" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100544140183.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">  学校旁边有个果园，在天气好的春日周末，大人们带着孩子来野餐</p><p>亲子关系也异常和谐。因为7岁之前，学校不提倡孩子上课外班，没什么作业，考试也不多。如果放学后去学校，总能看到学生不肯走，因为学校可比家里有意思多了。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100546297511.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="667" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100546297511.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>孩子们在学校里，远远不是死板地坐在教室里学习。比如谷雨到小满这段时间，是植物生长最快的阶段。孩子们就会拿着尺子去校园里测量植物的生长速度。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100548292674.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="667" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100548292674.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>我先生去蹭过一节地理课，回来非常感慨。校园里有老师提前堆好的大土堆，老师让孩子们从土堆高处往下倒水。孩子们看到土也随着一起往下走，形成了一片平地，老师说，北京就是这样形成的“冲击平原”。</p><p>相信这样上课，孩子们应该都会爱上学习吧。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100554518667.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="667" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100554518667.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>当然，选择更接近自然的乡村生活，并不意味着这里就是桃花源。如何能既安定地生活在此，又能做一份养家的工作，焦虑依然还在。</p><p>如今我注册了自己的公司，虽然规模很小，但做着自己认为挺有意义的事。我先生是一名摄影师，时间也相对灵活。</p><p>我们都是那种能不去城里就坚决不去的人。在农村生活久了，对外出旅行也没多少渴望。因为生活本身，变成了可以随时进入大自然的日常旅程。一片野地的更迭，就足以让人心旷神怡了——</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100555771260.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="667" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100555771260.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>春风一吹，成片成片的蓝紫色花海，那是二月蓝。过段时间，蒲公英金灿灿的花包刚谢，白色的种子绒球就在夕阳下泛起光来。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100556851026.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="667" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100556851026.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100557814993.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="667" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100557814993.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>夏天的草，一下子能窜很高，一点点变成墨绿色。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100558811986.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="667" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100558811986.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100559568421.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="667" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100559568421.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>秋风一吹，苍耳牢牢地沾在孩子们的衣服上。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100601090761.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="667" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100601090761.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>冬天，雪积得比城里可厚多了，踩在上面咯吱咯吱响。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100603258451.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="667" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/14/100603258451.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>这是我们每天拥有，却总被忽视的四季。</p><p>我也是到现在，才真正明白“吾心安处是吾乡”这句话。</p><p><span class="text-remarks" label="备注">本文来自微信公众号：</span><a
