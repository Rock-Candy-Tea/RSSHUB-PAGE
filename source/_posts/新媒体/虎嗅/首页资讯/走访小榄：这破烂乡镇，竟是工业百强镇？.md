class="text-remarks">，作者：挖数，头图来自：作者拍摄</span></p><p>那天本来想去中山的，坐火车经过小榄镇，心想：“咦，这不是前同事的老家么？”很久前拜访过他，他住别墅，家里做种树生意的，比较富有，当年在广州打过几年工，后回乡继承家业了，让我好生羡慕。于是在小榄镇下车，我对乡镇的兴趣比城市大。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202111/05/173603694449.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="584" class="text-img-note">小榄镇夹在佛山和中山之间，隶属中山<br label="图片备注" class="text-img-note"></p><p>从火车站绕到大路，这里建筑低矮，楼跟楼的间隔很宽，人特别少，来来往往都是车。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202111/05/173604238364.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202111/05/173604238364.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>走了几步看到菊城小学，门前的吉祥物造型奇特，初以为是济公，细看原来是头顶菊花。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202111/05/173605461831.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202111/05/173605461831.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>看校门口的牌子，居然是广东省一级学校。挖数在广州的家半径7、8公里都找不到一所，整个广东也只有200多所省一级，这里居然镇上就有，不禁刮目相看。</p><p>学校往前走看到一座五金采购中心，规模挺大。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202111/05/173606450396.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202111/05/173606450396.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>此时是早上10点多，里边静悄悄。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202111/05/173609830248.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202111/05/173609830248.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>很好奇小榄怎么跟锁具扯上关系，一直以为这里主要生产蚊香，广东人经常用的榄菊牌蚊香就产自这里。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202111/05/173609197533.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="826" data-h="332" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202111/05/173609197533.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>上网一查，原来这里是全国锁具重镇，一镇的锁具生产就占了全国的23%，全镇有30%的企业生产锁具。</p><p>这里还有自己的村镇银行，很厉害。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202111/05/173611326765.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202111/05/173611326765.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>通常来说经济好的镇工资都不低，有些比市区还高，隔壁一物业公司招工，维修电工一个月5200－7000元，不知你们那怎么样，挖数住的小区电工只有4000多工资，坐标广州。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202111/05/173612965961.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202111/05/173612965961.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>我没有走太远，就在火车站附近兜，这里密密麻麻都是厂，除了锁具厂，看到最多还有脚轮厂、弹簧厂，都是跟五金相关。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202111/05/173614248306.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202111/05/173614248306.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202111/05/173615249504.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202111/05/173615249504.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">脚轮厂<br _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202111/05/173617928064.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202111/05/173617928064.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>五金厂招男工，制衣厂招女工，两口子能凑在一块工作，制衣厂工作不忙，一些女工还带了小孩过去。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202111/05/173618706521.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202111/05/173618706521.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>这里到处都是菊花标志，话说小榄为何叫菊城，查了下是公元1274年，一些难民跑到顺德县以打鱼为生，有一通天桥连接顺德和小榄两地，渔民过来见土地肥沃，漫山遍野都是黄菊，于是称之为菊城。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202111/05/173619988387.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202111/05/173619988387.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>锁具厂是真的多，厂门前停着许多车，有不少特斯拉、宝马和奔驰，当地人做锁都致富了。</p><p>照例看厂门口招聘广告，小型厂的工程师也能给到过万工资，且很多对学历没要求。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202111/05/173621937596.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202111/05/173621937596.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>摩托车送货员都能给到4000－8000元，着实不低。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202111/05/173622628358.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202111/05/173622628358.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>繁荣的五金业养活了工人、企业主、送货员，还养活了做金属回收生意的。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202111/05/173623390349.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202111/05/173623390349.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202111/05/173624586687.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202111/05/173624586687.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>回收的主要是风扇、煤气炉和电饭煲，都是工人丢弃的，2019年小榄有10万工人。</p><p>工人住在工厂附近的出租屋，出租屋也是低矮低矮的，间隔很宽，道路干净，井然有序，环境比广州城中村好太多。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202111/05/173626686580.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202111/05/173626686580.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202111/05/173630387099.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202111/05/173631207064.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202111/05/173633147139.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202111/05/173636798770.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750"
