_src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/13/001213079327.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="708" data-h="564" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>还有人认为，有这功夫不如去蚂蚁庄园养养鸡，顺便做做公益。</p><p>当然也有人说了大多数人都想说的话：<strong>求求</strong><strong>你出个实用的功能吧，别整天搞这些……</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/13/001213463516.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="754" data-h="284" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>虽然“微信农场”里，农场只是张图片，宠物也就是个表情包。但小辣椒刷着刷着发现，还真有一大堆网友觉得挺好玩，开始在朋友圈经营了农场。</p><p>比如有人在青青草原上养一头胖嘟嘟的猪。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/13/001215655959.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="555" data-h="665" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/13/001215655959.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>还有人在乡下的小道上放了一只大鹅，敢情这路是不准备让人过了。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/13/001217184794.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="632" data-h="742" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/13/001217184794.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>也有人在牧场里养了只梅花鹿，怕它跑出去还用围栏保护了起来。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/13/001231620525.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="637" data-h="671" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/13/001231620525.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>一开始，微信农场的画风还算正常，但玩着玩着，大家的脑洞大了起来。</p><p>有人把海绵宝宝请到了自己的农场。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/13/001233299477.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="747" data-h="903" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/13/001233299477.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>既然有了海绵宝宝，那怎么能少得了派大星呢。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/13/001236482937.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="640" data-h="807" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/13/001236482937.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>后来，网友们又把玩法扩展到了影视剧。</p><p>有人把一只鸡送到天宫里啄米。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/13/001238285537.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="634" data-h="746" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/13/001238285537.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>还有人把两只狗送到玉帝面前，表演个“二狗互殴”。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/13/001240140030.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="645" data-h="803" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/13/001240140030.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>当然，经典的张翰表情包也没逃过此劫。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/13/001242549836.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="635" data-h="701" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/13/001242549836.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>上面这几个还算好的，接下来就越来越离谱了。</p><p>比如有人打破了颜色的限制，给沐浴在阳光里的农场，硬塞进了一只蹦迪狗。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/13/001244278532.gif?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/gif/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="828" data-h="806" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/13/001244278532.gif?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/gif/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>但另外一只狗的运气，就没这么好了。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/13/001246362177.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="648" data-h="834" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/13/001246362177.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>如果你开始心疼上面那条狗，我只能说——早了，看看这只鹅吧。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/13/001248962649.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="736" data-h="726" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/13/001248962649.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>不过，今天的痛苦之王应该还是下面这只螳螂，即将献祭在了微信农场里。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/13/001250112790.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="740" data-h="655" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/13/001250112790.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>看看，这才半天，微信农场就已经被彻底玩坏了。以至于有人没跟上进度，不知道怎么设置农场，而遭到了众多网友的围观。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/13/001251747568.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="742" data-h="1092" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/13/001251747568.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>为了防止差友们被围观，小辣椒就先不带大家看别人的农场了，简单教学一波，自己做个农场！</p><p>首先我们需要在网上找一些背景图，可以是天空、草地啥的，你要想放你家厕所也行。</p><p>保存好背景图片后，打开 “微信 - 我” ，点击 “+ 状态” ，然后按照自己喜好随便选择一个状态。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/13/001253265485.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="762" data-h="703" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/13/001253265485.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>接着我们点击 “ 表情” ，选择 “搜索” 。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/13/001254438667.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="886" data-h="713" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/13/001254438667.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>在搜索框输入 “农场” ，然后滑到底，选择 “更多表情” 。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/13/001255706563.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="812" data-h="1069" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/13/001255706563.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>一秒之后，你就会发现有上百条宠物，扭动着屁股，等着你的挑选。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/13/001258062894.gif?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/gif/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="624" data-h="865" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/13/001258062894.gif?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/gif/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>选好后，我们再点击“背景”，安排一下背景图。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/13/001309150770.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="566" data-h="788" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/13/001309150770.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>到这里就大功告成啦！</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/13/001311683431.gif?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/gif/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="579" data-h="865" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/13/001311683431.gif?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/gif/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>其实微信农场热度有这么高，除了用户基数大外，小辣椒认为还有一个重要的原因。</p><p>这两年，不管是生活压力，工作压力，还是疫情，大伙儿每天都被很多事情困扰着。这时突然流行一个新玩法，可以在虚拟农场“养”个宠物，那花个2分钟时间，跟风参与，大家快乐放松一下，也未尝不可嘛。</p><p>可能有人会说，这花里胡哨的没意思，也没啥社会价值，明天就过时了。</p><p>是，我不否认这个看法。</p><p><strong>但小辣椒觉得，生活，还是不要在所有事情上都认真严肃，也不要在任何时候都追求有没有意义，有没有价值，那样很累。</strong></p><p><strong>人活着不就为了快乐嘛。</strong></p><p>偶尔乐呵乐呵，无聊两下，我没觉得哪里不好。</p><p>开心第一，别顾虑太多啦。</p><p
