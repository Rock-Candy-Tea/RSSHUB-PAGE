class="text-remarks">，作者：三折人生，头图来自：视觉中国</span></p><p>2021年7月7日，国务院常务会议决定，在坚持不搞大水漫灌的基础上，保持货币政策稳定性、增强有效性，适时运用<strong>降准</strong>等货币政策工具。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135804373859.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135804373859.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135805270749.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135805270749.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>你说的啥啊？这里的降准是<strong>降低存款准备金率</strong>。</p><p>为了解释清楚降准，我们从一个小故事开始——我们隔壁的王大妈有1000元，王大妈感觉放在家里不安全，又没利息，于是把钱存进银行。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135806935047.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135806935047.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>银行存款账本上就多了1000元。</p><p>如果银行一直把这钱锁在金库里，只等王大妈来取款——</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135807550247.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135807550247.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>于是银行决定先把这1000元钱贷给老吴，赚取贷款利息。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135808220459.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135808220459.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>老吴拿着借来的钱，从店家老李那买了双鞋，支付了1000元。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135809195073.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135809195073.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>于是，店家老李有了1000元，但老李感觉放在家里不安全，又没利息，于是老李也把钱存进银行。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135810005198.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135810005198.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>这时候，银行存款账本上就又多了1000元，存款共计2000元。</p><p>银行也不可能拿着这1000元钱只等店家老李来取款。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135807550247.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135807550247.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>于是银行又把钱贷了出去。</p><p>以此往复，货币每存入一次，银行就进行一次贷款，这样可以无限继续下去，一生十，十生百，无穷匮也，创造了<strong>无限</strong>的货币量。</p><p>这个过程就被是<strong>货币创造</strong>。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135811105898.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="757" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135811105898.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>这个游戏可以一直玩下去，但有个问题，就是——</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135812458519.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135812458519.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135813147623.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135813147623.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>银行毕竟还是要赚取利润的，把所有钱都闲置在金库中也没有必要。银行就是个大资金池，不断有客户把钱存进来，而客户也不是一次性把钱都取走。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135814596613.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="492" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135814596613.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>所以央行就要求银行把客户存进来的钱分成两部分，一部分用于发放贷款，赚取利润；另一部分存款放在央行，以便储户提取存款时使用。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135815157757.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135815157757.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>这部分<strong>以备客户提款需求</strong>而上交央行的钱就被称<strong>存款准备金。</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135816676915.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135816676915.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>不是，央行会规定银行必须持有的准备金的<strong>最低水平</strong>，这被称为<strong>法定</strong>存款准备金。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135817335644.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135817335644.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><strong>存款准备金</strong>占比存款总额的比例就被称为<strong>存款准备金率</strong>。</p><p>法定存款准备金率的公式如下：</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135817274471.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="441" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135817274471.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>因为银行有大有小，流动性风险各不相同，因此央行规定的各类银行法定存款准备金率有所不同。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135818089361.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135818089361.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>目前，<strong>大型银行</strong>法定存款准备金率为12.5%、<strong>中小银行</strong>为10.5%。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135819395099.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135819395099.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>当然可以，银行持有高于法定最低量的准备金，被称为超额存款准备金。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135819460137.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135819460137.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>在有<strong>准备金</strong>的情况下，<strong>货币的创造过程</strong>会发生什么变化呢？</p><p>我们再来模拟下，假设准备金率为10%。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135823220740.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135823220740.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>银行缴存完准备金后，剩下的存款就借给老吴。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135824821901.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135824821901.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>老吴在店家老李那消费900元买了鞋，店家老李就有了900元，又去存银行。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135825965054.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135825965054.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>银行缴存了10%准备金后，存款剩余900-90=810元继续用于贷款，以此往复，但因为有了准备金，存款不再是100%都能贷款出去，因此也没法创造出无限的货币量。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135825493353.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="990" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135825493353.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>在上面的例子中，1000元初始存款，10%的准备金率，最终能创造出了10000元<strong>货币供给</strong>，放大了10倍。</p><p>这个10倍我们称为<strong>货币乘数</strong>。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135827330554.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135827330554.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135827422627.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135827422627.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><strong>货币乘数的大小和准备金率有关系</strong>，我们在不考虑“私房钱被你塞在天花板上不再流通”这类<strong>现金漏损</strong>的情况下，货币乘数是准备金率的<strong>倒数</strong>。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135828574424.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="327" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135828574424.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>上述公式我们可以通过货币供给量S的等式，运用等比数列法则推算出来，我们就不展开说了，感兴趣同学可以算一算。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135829972310.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="506" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135829972310.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>货币乘数和准备金率的关系我们也可以这样理解，<strong>准备金率越低</strong>，每个存款银行能用于贷款的钱就越多，创造货币供给也越多，<strong>货币乘数自然也越高</strong>。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135830164404.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="521" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135830164404.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>降准的目的，是有效增加金融机构支持实体经济的<strong>稳定资金来源</strong>，降低<strong>资金成本</strong>，直接支持实体经济。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/08/135831816937.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>好了，今天就说到这吧。</p><p
