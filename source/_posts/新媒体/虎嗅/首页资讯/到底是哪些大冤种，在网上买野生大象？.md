class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/180141031591.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="855" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>不过鉴于这是大象，没有哪家快递小哥愿意把它扛上没有电梯的6楼，包邮只能包干粮，剩下的路都得靠它自己走。</p><p><strong>什么时候走到全看缘分</strong>，卖家唯一能保证的是你的大象虽迟但到。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/175637000756.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="552" data-h="906" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><strong> 如果不看售价，我几乎就要信以为真。</strong></p><p>以为斯拉夫人养熊，阿拉伯人养虎，而我们华夏人也终于告别网红羊驼，开始豢养猛兽。</p><p>毕竟饲养这种长鼻子怪兽的传统古已有之，河南开封的宋朝皇家动物园玉津园里，养过的大象就不下四十头。 </p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/180141824755.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="640" data-h="428" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/180141824755.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"> </p><p>但种瓜得瓜，种豆得豆，世间的价格和价值总不会相距太过离谱。</p><p><strong>1块钱的售价很快让我明白，就像当年的孤寡青蛙，这不过是又一场穷极无聊的赛博玩笑。</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/180438967337.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="1256" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/180438967337.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><strong>在掏包儿网一块钱买一只野生象，您买得了吃亏，买得了上当。</strong><br></p><p>大象自然是不会真的有，但你收不到货，商家也概不退款。</p><p>因为这就是场周瑜打黄盖的沙雕大考，没道理帮你确认了自己是沙雕学十级学者，你还要克扣主办方的考试费用。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/175637817698.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="480" data-h="206" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/175637817698.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><strong>大象交易的买卖双方，就像一对娴熟的国际政客，揣着一肚子的明白，装着满脸无辜的糊涂。</strong></p><p>当卖家说出“您的大象已发货”，就是表演开始的邀请信号。</p><p>买家立刻登上舞台，实力上演什么叫做现代版《等待戈多》。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/175637196389.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="2802" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/175637196389.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>绝对没有不识风情的憨子大喊出：</p><p>“骗子！哪有什么大象！”</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/180625771154.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="348" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/180625771154.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box" style="text-align: left;"><strong>买家总是彬彬有礼，不急不躁，替大象考虑很多。</strong></p><p>有人关心大象会不会迷路。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/175637656172.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="830" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/175637656172.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>有人关心大象有没有高等学历。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/175637812783.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="785" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/175637812783.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"> </p><p>还有人关心买下一对公母象，它们会不会在漫长的旅途中互生情愫。<br></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/175637447233.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="1492" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/175637447233.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><strong>没有人催促大象快些赶路，买家只是象征性地问上一句“我的大象到哪儿了”。</strong></p><p><strong>而卖家只需要回答“大象今晚不来，明天准来”，买家就会心满意足。</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/175637675486.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="610" data-h="624" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/175637675486.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>说实话，一头大象真的出现在四人宿舍里那才叫顶级灾难。没有人真的希望大象到货，大家只是闲得闷出屁来，想完成一场沙雕社群的精神团建。</p><p><strong>在掏包儿问答里，大象被越描越真。</strong></p><p>过来人一遍遍确认大象的真实可靠，声称为了迎接它进新家，不得不把门给卸了。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/175637113759.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="1008" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/175637113759.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>各人的大象有各人的特色：贤惠的会煮饺子，富裕的会开跑车，艺术细胞强的唱跳俱佳，操作意识好的称霸峡谷。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/180850859395.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="303" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/180850859395.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>在买家为小象搭建起的元宇宙里，它们可以慢慢走，永远晃着尾巴，悠闲地在路边踱步。</p><p>就像买下一颗天边星的浪漫计划。等到年华老去，时光褪色，你还可以在摇椅上眯着眼遥指远方，告诉身边的小屁孩：</p><p><strong>“那儿有爷爷当年买的一百头大象，等它们走到了，记得帮我签收。”</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/180141269394.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="666" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/180141269394.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>这些年，钱真是越来越当不得钱。<br></p><p>从前兜里揣着一块钱，还有在小卖部里挑挑拣拣的底气，现在甚至买不来半瓶农夫山泉。</p><p><strong>或许正因如此，1块钱的大象才卖得如此火爆。</strong></p><p><strong>最高的商家月销量过千，腰部卖家一个月也能有一百多单。</strong></p><p>1块钱就像当年丢进许愿池的1毛硬币，听个响声，图个乐呵，谁也不能好意思要求退款。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/175637616195.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="736" data-h="142" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/175637616195.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>如果你有特殊需求，可以购买更加昂贵、身怀绝技的马戏团象，它们将踩着滑板和自行车前来报到。</p><p>或者奇幻种蝴蝶象、以及最戳人肺管子的“对象”。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/181016387471.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="930" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/181016387471.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><strong>总之只要你想，就有人敢卖，限制这门生意的从来不是货源，而是想象。</strong></p><p>你可以买到朵拉，附带她的猴子，只要你不介意她故意对你视而不见，你们一定能成为best friend。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/175637985243.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="1738" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/175637985243.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>你也可以买到蚊子，发货超快，买一送十，超量特卖。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/180625017715.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="680" data-h="1584" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/180625017715.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>当然还有奥特曼，不论你以前信不信，现在你必须相信光，因为他正顺着光缆向你赶来。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/175637671241.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="1247" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/175637671241.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><strong>只需区区一块钱，就能拥有一张足以笑傲朋友圈的定制梗图，有效延长沙雕人设保质期至少三个月，这笔生意简直不要太划算。<br></strong></p><p><strong>然而等我真的下了一单才发现，不论对买家还是卖家来说，这都是一次堪称坑爹的大冤种体验。</strong></p><p>首先你发现，当你付完货款，摩拳擦掌准备和卖家来一番精彩对线时，卖家找不到了。</p><p>而当你艰难地试图按照别人的梗图挑逗卖家，卖家只会回复一句“好的”。</p><p src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/175637303814.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>好不容易聊上两句，眼看渐入佳境，卖家突然又不见了。过了很久你才收到他幽幽的道歉：对不起，刚刚在打游戏。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/175637537463.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="550" data-h="290" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/175637537463.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><strong>和大象卖家套磁，就像舔狗在追女神。</strong></p><p><strong>都说顾客就是上帝，但在大象卖家手里，你充其量只是个玩具。</strong></p><p>一个巴掌拍不响，一个人的独角戏唱不出趣儿，当你没法用那1块钱拿捏卖家，而又得求着他陪你演戏，就注定了一切大概率是个债主终成孙子的人生悲剧。</p><p><strong>有些卖家看起来很有职业道德，发货前还要先问问你的性别。</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/175637977553.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="1403" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/175637054002.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="400" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/175637054002.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><strong>对于卖家来说，这事儿同样挺烦。</strong></p><p>开始当然有趣，买家都是大学和高中生，思路活跃，脑洞清奇。</p><p>脚部卖家一个月也就成交十几单，总交易额不够买几杯奶茶，坚持干下来，谁还不是图个有趣的灵魂。</p><p>但接待的客人多了，发现大家也就是那么几句车轱辘话来回转，尤其是看过别人晒单再来的，思路大受局限——看来人类的本质还是复读机。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/175637425895.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="404" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/175637425895.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>有人还因为频繁触发关键词，被认定为倒卖野生动物和象牙制品的非法商家，一气之下，干脆改行倒卖外星人。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/20/175637478291.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="848"
