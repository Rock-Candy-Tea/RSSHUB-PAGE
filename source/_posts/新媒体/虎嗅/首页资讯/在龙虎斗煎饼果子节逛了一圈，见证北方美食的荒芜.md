class="text-remarks">，作者：廖信忠，题图来自视觉中国</span></p><p>前几天我去了北京，有人给我推荐了一个活动，名叫“北平机器煎饼节”，它的副标题“津京煎饼龙虎斗”。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/27/095904727403.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" class="text-img-note">简单地说，主办方邀请了北京天津各约十间煎饼店，在现场制作，比比看到底是谁家更受欢迎<br></p><p>主办方北平机器这店我知道，卖啤酒的店，但吸引我的是“津京煎饼龙虎斗”这几个字，我心里就呵呵了：北方美食果然荒芜啊！竟然连煎饼这玩意儿都能拿出来龙虎斗。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/27/095905216588.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="667" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/27/095905216588.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>后來我到现场逛了几圈，觉得这活动太魔幻了，想了几天，觉得还是应该给你们看看我到底都经历了些啥！</p><p>22、23日两天，活动地点在百子湾，就是那个无数北漂网红梦想起飞的百子湾，天气不错，这天北京已经33度，空气质量为轻度污染。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/27/095908692891.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="650" data-h="974" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/27/095908692891.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>入口处就有一幅大大的“煎饼思维导图”，借由各种口味材料不同选择，让你快速地找到最适合你的煎饼摊。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/27/095909516644.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="667" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/27/095909516644.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><video width="100%">您目前设备暂不支持播放</video><br></p><p>一进入口，就是好几间天津过来的煎饼摊，啥南楼煎饼、李向煎饼、老张煎饼.....那些天津人嘴里“家楼下的煎饼摊”。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/27/095911206811.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/27/095911206811.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>天津来的姐姐<span class="text-remarks" label="备注">（音：结界）</span>规规矩矩，行云流水般做好每一份煎饼，从现磨绿豆面开始，将绿豆面摊在铁板上，用T型铲流利地画了个圆弧型。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/27/095912086956.gif?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/gif/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="400" data-h="225" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/27/095912086956.gif?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/gif/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>磕了颗蛋，再用T字形小铲子将鸡蛋涂匀，就在那蛋液尚未凝固之时，金灿灿的煎饼已洒上脆绿的葱花。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/27/095916224701.gif?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/gif/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="418" data-h="237" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/27/095916224701.gif?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/gif/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>她迅速地涂上面酱、腐乳和辣椒酱，包裹着馃子或馃篦儿，于此同时，姐姐已经在制作下一份煎饼了。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/27/095920406632.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="667" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/27/095920406632.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>小铲子从中一截，两半的煎饼放进纸袋中递给你，那就是一份饱含着人间正道，令人热泪盈眶的天津煎饼果子。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/27/095922792855.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="650" data-h="866" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/27/095922792855.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>崔记煎饼也是天津有名煎饼店，这俩外派来京的大爷，就长得非常天津，简直是天津男人相貌模板：双眼皮，大眼袋。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/27/095923963103.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/27/095923963103.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>基本现场所有的天津煎饼果子摊，除了有没有洒芝麻，做法材料差不多都是一样，给人一种亲切而单调的古早味，那是有信仰，有执着的煎饼果子，让你身在百子湾也能梦回那津门。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/27/095925556735.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/27/095925556735.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>现场也卖着一些天津小吃，比如这个，有人管它叫钵仔糕，天津叫熟梨糕，更多人叫它嗡嗡糕。从他加热蒸熟的方式，就知道别小看天津人善用各种加热工具烹调的技能。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/27/095927845409.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="667" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/27/095927845409.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>用大米磨成粉渣，将米面置于木甑中，放在蒸锅上蒸一分钟，然后在其上涂抹各种小料即可。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/27/095928490822.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="667" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/27/095928490822.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>现在竟然也有推出煎饼果子大礼包，里面有各种调理包，有了它，全国人民都能在家自己做煎饼果子，这属于一种煎饼果子文创商品，天津文化输出的一环。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/27/095930892822.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/27/095930892822.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>一开始我以为是基于尊重客人，把天津的摊子安排在靠入口处，逛得越深入，我才觉得too simple，这真是一场机关算尽的活动。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/27/095931878638.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="667" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/27/095931878638.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>不愧是北京！就连这场批着煎饼节外衣实则藉机吃喝玩乐的活动中，都有人从容地下着象棋。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/27/095933087778.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="667" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/27/095933087778.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>主办方北平机器，以及协办的如公路商店、X博士、伍德吃托克......应该很多人知道关注这些号的都是些什么货，所以在煎饼节现场，你可以看到众多亚文化人士一边吃煎饼果子一边喝啤酒。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/27/095934313294.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="667" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/27/095934313294.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>既然这是场批着煎饼节外衣的亚X市集，忽然出现理发摊位也是相当正常的事了。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/27/095935049423.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="667" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/27/095935049423.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>现场也有许多漂亮姐姐，他们亲切地请我喝了酒，所以特别出镜广告作为感谢。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/27/095937030141.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>现场弥漫着爱与和平，以及各种煎饼的面酱味，后头墙外不断有列车轰隆隆地跑过去。各种有趣摊位，看着大家都新奇有趣的样子，惊觉自己已经失去了童心。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/27/095938678274.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="672" class="text-normal">相对于天津煎饼果子的本份老实，主场北京的煎饼摊，可就鸡贼多了，花样百出。</p><p>我看到上面几个大字“老北京风味”就觉得不妙！果然你看，竟然出现“金枪鱼虾”“夫妻肺片”“酱香牦牛”.....等魔性煎饼；我吓得花容失色，大饼可以包一切，但煎饼只能包果子或果蓖儿啊！</p><p class="img-center-box"><img _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/27/095942244161.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>大阪烧也属于北京煎饼的一种，天下武功出少林，世界煎饼出北京。</p><p>北京精神：创新、包容。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/27/100000377355.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/27/100008417198.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="752"
