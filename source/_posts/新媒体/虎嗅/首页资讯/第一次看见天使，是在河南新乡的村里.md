_src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094307177381.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="400" data-h="300" class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094310320898.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="612" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094310320898.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>有翅膀的天使才算持证上岗，而发证机关就在新乡。</p><p>“New York”是它直译的伪装，只有继续深入，你才能发现新乡的真正身份。</p><p>从这里天使种类的多样性和分布密度来看，很容易让人怀疑耶路撒冷是新乡人建的。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094316690025.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="655" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094316690025.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094319772342.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="669" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094319772342.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>河南新乡，是天使的故乡。</p><p>天使们主要出没于新乡市小店镇王连屯村，在当地劳动人民的长久努力下，基本做到了让天使都说河南话。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094323722941.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="640" data-h="360" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094323722941.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>与其他地方不同，作为翅膀的主要产区，凡是日常生活中能看得见的天使，几乎都背着河南大姐手工打造的翅膀，这是天使圈里公开的秘密。</p><p>连当地小朋友都知道天使不是从天而降，她们经常从村口走过来。</p><p>一旦明白了这一点，耶稣降生在驻马店的事也就有迹可循了。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094325820423.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="735" data-h="550" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094325820423.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>也许是勤劳的大姐们把秋收的劲头融进了创作，她们对翅膀的认识相当透彻，每一片羽毛都蕴含着人民群众对浪漫主义的理解。</p><p>自然界法则的局限并不影响她们的艺术热情，在真正务实的实干派看来，创造力的边界就是用来打破的。</p><p>插上这里的翅膀时，所有人的灵魂都将携手飞上平流层。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094327844521.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="640" data-h="426" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094327844521.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094329042258.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="564" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094329042258.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>“做出来的东西固然是死的，可人是活的，任何产品的用法都取决于使用者，一翅两用，只需要翻过来戴，气质就会完全不同。”</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094331328096.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="640" data-h="426" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094331328096.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094334452918.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="640" data-h="426" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094334452918.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">一款拥有垂直翻转功能的实体翅膀<br></p><p>开拓思维通路会激发艺术上的无限可能性，但提起原始创作理念，还是亲自上阵演示才能诠释的更清楚。</p><p>不过分修饰，没有做作的造型，这是原作者最朴素的情感体现。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094336607410.gif?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/gif/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="230" data-h="170" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094336607410.gif?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/gif/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">天使也可能是电动的<br></p><p>是金子就会发光，深谙此道的大姐们一直在用行动向世界证明，翅膀这种东西根本就没有隐形的。</p><p>“不管你带谁来这，最终他都会变成童话里你爱的那个天使，然后张开双手变成翅膀守护你。”</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094337676968.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="545" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094337676968.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094341238087.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="569" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094341238087.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">愿意的话也可以试试比翼双飞的感觉<br></p><p>当然这只是灵感的某种表现形式之一，也可以看作是河南老乡对世界的一种善意。</p><p>但精益求精的艺术工作者不会止步于此，在这里的语境中，当了天使也得讲究创新精神。</p><p>很多时候只需要改改颜色，就能带来全新的视觉冲击。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094345800702.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="674" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094345800702.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>你可以从中体会到隐藏在民间的先锋设计思维，比如红黑经典配色中点缀白色，亦正亦邪的气息隐喻人性的复杂。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094351813193.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="593" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094351813193.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">cosplay爱好者的福音<br></p><p>而在改用黄色和绿色时，又体现出新乡的国际视野，有人建议这些翅膀应该成为下届世界杯巴西队的标配。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094354416542.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="594" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094354416542.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094358419925.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="640" data-h="480" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094358419925.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">热情桑巴在河南<br></p><p>色彩的奥秘已经被她们参透，要是里面再加入蓝色，视角一下子拉回了田野，丰收的喜悦感扑面而来。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094400910682.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="439" data-h="439" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094400910682.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>从某种程度上说，她们掌握着翅膀领域的话语权，同时怀抱着一丝反传统的意味，给亲切包容的河南智慧插上新翅膀。</p><p>进入这种层面的创作者，想象力是不可能被束缚的。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094403578683.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="586" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094403578683.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">背后的红心可以放大所有爱<br></p><p>取材范围足够广阔，包含文化属性就越发多元，想做好翅膀，自然需要了解一点生物学。</p><p>有朋友看过之后说这两件翅膀应该是在野生山鸡的外形里融入吉普赛文化，但灵感也可能来自鸡毛掸子。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094406743783.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="640" data-h="360" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094406743783.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094409999443.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="544" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094409999443.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094414576540.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="891" data-h="1199" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094414576540.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>不过多狂野的艺术，在创作方式上都是严谨的，创新不是瞎创，老乡们很懂这一点。</p><p>比如能开屏的孔雀都是公的，从这个角度出发，保守男孩们也就有了指向比较明确的选择。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094422103343.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="640" data-h="480" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>她们显然在使用场景的问题上也做过深入研究，稍加改动就满足了夜间出行的用户需求，太阳落山后按下开关，LED会把梦想照进现实。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094425106864.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="640" data-h="426" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094427869469.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>在产品里注入对各个群体的人文关怀，新乡早已开展了关于人类多样性的探索。</p><p>外在表象只是传递内在精神的载体，在这些高阶翅膀专家看来，可能只要背后那个东西上有毛，都可以纳入翅膀的范畴。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094429901024.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="885" data-h="1109" class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094433454773.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="537" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094433454773.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094438699448.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="521" class="text-img-note">童趣与时尚的完美结合<br></p><p>通过这种逻辑的延展，翅膀不止处于简单的幻肢领域，它更像一种思维的具象化。</p><p>要知道，人类自古对征服天空充满畅想，而有的人起飞之前，已经给天空带去了足够的压迫感。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094441398239.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="925" data-h="839" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094441398239.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>很多时尚人士都曾被这种河南智慧加持过，早在2015年，湖南卫视《快乐大本营》就给一众明星插上了新乡的翅膀。</p><p>到2017年又给吴亦凡插了一次。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094448884242.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="923" data-h="2570" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/02/094505172177.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>由于采购时对方并没透露是给谁戴的，翅膀创始人江泉先生事后看到节目时反而有点惋惜，他表示早点说还能给他们设计一下。</p><p>但流量曝光带来了生意，他曾公开对媒体透露，当时卖过最火的单品，是吴亦凡穿过的同款，一下卖了好几百个。</p><p>因此他还有过找吴亦凡代言的念头。</p><p class="text-img-note">图片来源：猛犸新闻<br></p><p>同样是2017年，维密在上海搞了天使之路选秀，其中有好几款翅膀是从河南老乡手里买的。</p><p>甚至不光国内，全世界都有翅膀订单飞来，据说埃菲尔铁塔附近的影楼里，翅膀也是从新乡进口的。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg"
