<p><span class="text-remarks" label="备注">本文来自微信公众号：</span><a href="https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s/XGibCON-hMt40a-iR1OjbA" target="_blank" rel="nofollow" style="text-decoration: none;"><span class="text-remarks">天府最生活（ID：cdqqcom）</span></a><span class="text-remarks">，作者：匡匡，题图来自：作者</span></p><p>男人不够成熟、睿智的表现，一是爱幻想，二是乱花钱。</p><p>几年前的我，两种症状都很严重。</p><p>脑壳一热，掏空积蓄，在屋顶装了个花园，想拥抱阳光，亲近大自然。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/152301977046.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="980" data-h="542" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/152301977046.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>脑壳一冷，才发现，大自然没手机好玩。</p><p>又一个冬天过后，我忽然记起，我楼上是不是还有个花园？</p><p>上去看看，花园还在。不过树死花枯、野草茁壮，荒芜如我。</p><p>另外，还多了很多东西：塑料桶、破家具、烂木头、瓶瓶罐罐……</p><p>疏于管理的日子，有人把这当成了垃圾场。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/152302478325.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="559" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/152302478325.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>想找个装修公司，维护一下。</p><p>听完报价，我问，不要钱行不行，给你们写个表扬信，发朋友圈，我微信有5000个好友…</p><p>对面说，滚。</p><p>于是，我决定亲自动手。第一步，去趟三圣乡，搞点植物回来。</p><p>问题来了，三圣乡花市，拆拆搬搬好几回，现在去哪儿了？</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/152303080722.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="562" data-h="443" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/152303080722.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>朋友说，三圣乡花市，现在叫<strong>万福花卉市场</strong>，搬到一座铁路桥下面了。</p><p>顺着导航，我找到了成万路附近。</p><p>不显山不露水，甚至可以算隐蔽，没有想象中花枝招展的场面。</p><p>两位迎宾阿姨，拖着三轮车，兼职卖花洋芋和炸粉。</p><p>没有门口柱子上那几个绿字，我都以为是导航走偏了。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/152304281078.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="563" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/152304281078.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>市场里有免费停车场。不过，内部道路狭窄，进出不易。</p><p>建议停附近，车位充足，10元/车，不限时。</p><p>停车场门口，加6元可以洗车，自助。</p><p>不洗车也可以去耍水，假装在海边。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/152305911119.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="563" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/152305911119.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>进入花市。头顶，是一座铁路桥。</p><p>桥下，是与之平行、延伸的几百家花店。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/152306932751.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="563" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/152306932751.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>进入花市，起点到终点，一条直路，全长1公里。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/152307098909.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="844" data-h="629" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/152307098909.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>一边是棱角分明，钢筋水泥筑成的巨物。</p><p>一边是五彩斑斓、迎风开放的花海。</p><p>两种截然不同，甚至有些违和的场面，在这里融合、共生，略显神奇。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/152311501338.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="563" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/152311501338.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>几年前，我去泰国旅游，到网红景点“<strong>铁路市场</strong>”逛过。</p><p>论名气，它肯定比万福花市响亮。<strong>但比规模和气势，略逊一筹。</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/152313701846.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="542" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/152313701846.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">2019年末，在泰国铁路市场。<br></p><p>顺着路往前走，不要着急买东西。</p><p>根据我丰富的生活经验，离市场入口越近的摊位，价钱越贵。</p><p>一个成熟睿智的男人，要沉得住气。</p><p>货比三家，不动如山，不到最后不撒币。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/152314558622.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="563" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/152314558622.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>逛了一个小时，走到路尽头，我发现了设卡查健康码的保安大爷。</p><p>似乎，路尽头才是市场入口。</p><p>而我，好像是从后门进去的。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/152315420940.gif" data-w="640" data-h="360" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/152315420940.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">随风摆动的花朵，这是春天的感觉。<br></p><p>不过，这些都不重要了。</p><p>在万福花市，比价并非必要，因为都很便宜。</p><p>一个经过充分竞争的市场，价格透明且血腥。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/152318143809.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="563" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/152318143809.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>普通的当季鲜花，价格约8-15元/束，一束10来朵或更多。</p><p>最便宜的盆花，处理价2元/盆。</p><p>相比楼下花店，几乎就是白送。</p><p>在万福花市，预算100元，你就可以装满一个后备箱。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/152319608236.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="563" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/152319608236.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/152320129966.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>销售多肉的摊位，约占总数的1/3。</p><p>小号普通多肉，10元5盆是正常价格。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/152321042983.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="563" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/152321042983.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>三八节特价，10元6盆，堪比白菜。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/152322124897.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="563" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/152324881085.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="563" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/152324881085.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>但不是每个老板都有心情陪你砍。</p><p>他们的生意大头是批发，对鸡零狗碎的小单，态度比较冷淡。</p><p>下单之后，如果还要继续逛，可以把货寄存在店里，回来时再取。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/152326726604.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="563" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/152326726604.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>专业的多肉爱好者，可以到花市中间，那几家大型多肉园逛逛。</p><p
