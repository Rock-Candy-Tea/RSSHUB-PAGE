class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080609056393.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="600" data-h="210" class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080611475944.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="640" data-h="739" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>奶奶抱怨很累，爷爷便说他来扫吧。</p><p>我以为，爷爷会按照传统的套路把落叶扫好，来体现对奶奶的宠爱。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080613559655.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="640" data-h="400" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080613559655.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>但接下来发生的事，让我明白，我毕竟还是太年轻，不懂爱情……</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080615580311.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="640" data-h="400" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080615580311.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>爷爷把落叶扫成一堆之后，居然……</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080616995751.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="640" data-h="400" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080616995751.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>捧起一把落叶洒向奶奶啊！！！</p><p>这调皮的动作和开心的神情，果然在心爱的人面前，男人永远不满三岁。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080618871933.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="640" data-h="400" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080618871933.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>然后爷爷又去摘了花哄奶奶开心，两个人各自头戴两朵小花继续扫落叶……</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080619437630.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="640" data-h="1034" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080619437630.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>本来都已经扫好的落叶，因为两个人的玩闹又弄了一院子，但爷爷和奶奶乐在其中。</p><p>有了第一幕的经验，我已经成长了，于是可以相对冷静地面对之后出现的各种秀恩爱情节<span class="text-remarks" label="备注">（流泪）</span>。</p><p>奶奶上厕所的时候怕黑，总要爷爷陪着，絮絮叨叨地嘱咐爷爷不要走，爷爷就守在门口给奶奶唱歌。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080621940485.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="640" data-h="400" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080621940485.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>奶奶出来说：“爷爷唱的可真好听呀，爷爷冷不冷啊，我怕爷爷你冷呢。”</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080622567469.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="640" data-h="400" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080622567469.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>看到只有两个老人自己住在不方便的郊外，按照以往的经验，那应该是没有孩子或是孩子不孝顺吧。</p><p>但并非像我一开始想象的那样，是因为儿女不孝顺才只能相依为伴。</p><p>在家庭聚会上，爷爷奶奶的孩子们都来了，热热闹闹的一家子。</p><p>他们的孙女还在饭桌上谈起了爷爷对奶奶有多么体贴：</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080623939139.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="640" data-h="514" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080623939139.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>活泼的爷爷奶奶还对晚辈们比心：  <img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080625886863.gif?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/gif/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="19" data-h="19" style="text-align: center;" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080625886863.gif?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/gif/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080626533833.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="640" data-h="360" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080626533833.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>晚上奶奶给爷爷烤玉米吃，在烤炉边，两人回忆起他们年轻时是如何相爱的。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080627260872.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="640" data-h="1006" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080627260872.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>当年14岁时奶奶嫁给了爷爷，结婚到现在已经70多年了：</p><p>“遇到你之后，我就再也没觉得孤单。”</p><p>江原道的自然景色穿插在老人日常生活的点滴之间，一切温馨而平静。</p><p>导演的镜头只是单纯的记录，当谈及命运中的不幸时也依旧十分克制。</p><p>影片中段，爷爷和奶奶去给早年不幸去世的孩子买衣服。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080628483946.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="640" data-h="1024" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080628483946.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>两人面前摆着一堆给小孩子穿的秋衣裤，而孩子早已不在人间。隐隐的心酸。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080629729252.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="640" data-h="1024" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>灾难与死亡总是不期而至，两人一起养的狗“小不点”死去了。爷爷与奶奶哭着埋葬了它。</p><p>埋葬完陪伴自己多年的狗狗后，爷爷落寞地走远，留下一个背影，奶奶孤单地留在了原地。</p><p>这个镜头仿佛是一个悲伤的隐喻。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080630478671.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="600" data-h="337" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>后来爷爷的咳嗽日益加剧，整晚整晚地睡不着，咳得令人心惊。奶奶陪伴着痛苦的爷爷。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080631163440.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="600" data-h="337" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080631163440.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>子女闻讯赶来，奶奶说，没办法的，人太老了，没办法了，就这么平安地过吧。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080633244688.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="640" data-h="800" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080633244688.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>奶奶也就这么陪伴在爷爷的身边。</p><p>一天，趁爷爷睡着的时候，奶奶抱着一摞衣服悄悄走出卧室。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080634070168.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="640" data-h="400" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080634070168.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>奶奶独自坐在火光前喃喃自语：</p><p>“我得先烧一部分衣服过去，爷爷没有我，哪分得清哪些是冬天的衣服，哪些是夏天的衣服呀，我要先帮爷爷都整理好啊。”</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080635632917.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="640" data-h="384" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080635632917.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>家里另一条小狗生了宝宝，奶奶高兴地抱来给爷爷看。</p><p>而爷爷只能虚弱地躺在地上，连坐起来的力气都没有了。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080636036622.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="640" data-h="400" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080636036622.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>小狗崽在屋里爬来爬去，舔爷爷的脸。</p><p>一条小生命刚刚诞生，而同时另一条生命马上就要离去。</p><p><strong>在韩国，人们认为人死之后，会跨过一条江，到达彼岸的冥府。</strong></p><p>在爷爷临死前，奶奶躺在爷爷身旁，低声念着，我和爷爷你一起走，要是能一起走该有多好。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080637637328.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="640" data-h="453" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>这一年，爷爷98岁，他先渡过了那条江，帮奶奶探路去了。</p><p>他曾说：“人生呀，和花还有树叶都是一样的，我已经到了凋零枯萎的年纪咯。”</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080640487025.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="640" data-h="400" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080640487025.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>而如今，从医院回来的路上，只剩下奶奶自己一个人。</p><p>庭院里的椅子上，爷爷熟悉的身影渐渐消失。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080641961551.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="640" data-h="800" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>爷爷下葬后，奶奶来到爷爷的坟前，帮爷爷把剩下的冬天的衣服烧过去，念叨着“这是爷爷的衣服，冷的时候记得穿啊。”</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080644674394.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="640" data-h="358" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202104/04/080644674394.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85"
