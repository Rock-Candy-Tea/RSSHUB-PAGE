_src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085910424711.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" label="备注">（dot plot）</span>。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085910000890.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085910000890.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><strong>点阵图是对未来加息或者降息做预测的图谱，是一种前瞻性的信息。</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085910150088.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085910150088.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>美联储之所以发布点阵图，是因为美联储认为自己与市场的沟通太少，市场有时候不太能理解它的信息。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085911199157.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085911199157.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085912231544.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085912231544.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085912002699.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085912002699.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>因此，<strong>美联储希望通过公布点阵图，加强与市场的沟通。</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085913041718.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085913041718.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>别急，想要搞懂点阵图，我们先要知道美联储长啥样。</p><p>美联储全称是美国联邦储备系统。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085913792119.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085913792119.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>它主要由3大部分组成：</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085914430271.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="730" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085914430271.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>其中，<strong>美联储理事会是美联储的核心管理机构，共计有7名委员。</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085915534777.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="784" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085915534777.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>但由于理事辞职等等原因，理事的职位会有空缺，人数不总是7人。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085916864538.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="426" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085916864538.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>而其中的地区联邦储备银行有12家，分布在12个城市，对应的有12位行长。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085916982384.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="1047" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085916982384.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>可见，<strong>美联储的管理层中，理事会加上12家储备银行行长最多共计19人。</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085917504849.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="700" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085917504849.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>虽然19人都蛮重要，但不是每个人都具有利率决议的投票权。</p><p><strong>当决定货币供给等核心问题时，是由FOMC决定的。</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085918035590.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085918035590.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>FOMC全称是美国联邦公开市场委员会，主要负责公开市场操作的。</p><p><strong>它的成员才具有利率决议投票权。</strong></p><p>FOMC的成员一般有12人，是在19人基础上产生的。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085918107704.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="630" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085918107704.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>现在，我们就来看看哪些人可以登上FOMC的舞台。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085919127378.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="513" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085919127378.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>首先，美联储理事会的主席、副主席和理事全部是FOMC成员，拥有永久票权，最多拥有7张票。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085919904721.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="794" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085919904721.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>其次，<strong>在12家联邦储备银行中，由于纽约地位特殊，纽约储备银行的行长拥有永久票权。</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085920941877.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="842" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085920941877.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>再次，其他11个地方储备银行行长中，每年会按照一定规则，选出4名获得票权。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085921244915.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="842" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085921244915.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>拥有投票权的委员，我们就叫他们票委。</p><p>可见，FOMC共计有12名票委。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085922250885.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="804" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085922250885.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>但上面我们说过，美联储理事会的委员不总是7人，所以票委人数也往往会少于12人。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085923510041.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="669" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085923510041.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>台上的票委有权投票对利率调整做出决议，这次加息0.25%就是FOMC投票决定的。</p><p>而其他台下的委员没有投票权，只能参与讨论，表达意见。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085924216455.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="669" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085924216455.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>比如，<strong>点阵图就是由台上台下的委员共同预测出来的。</strong>下面我们就来看看点阵图的制作过程。点阵图的横轴是时间线，纵轴是未来利率的可能区间。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085925815604.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="665" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085925815604.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>由委员们对未来利率进行预测，反映在点阵图上就是实心小圆点。每1个小圆点代表1个做出预测的委员。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085925111983.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="552" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085925111983.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>一般来说，<strong>美联储平均每次加息0.25%。</strong>据此，通过点阵图我们可以看出委员们预测加息的次数。</p><p>假设目前利率是0～0.25%，1名委员预测利率会到0.5%～0.75%，那么就相当于需要加息2次。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085926273797.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="552" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085926273797.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>由于预测利率的人员，包括了台上台下所有委员，所以在点阵图上最多有19个点。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085926715620.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="581" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085926715620.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>但是由于美联储理事会时不时会缺人，所以点阵图也往往不是19个点，比如有可能只有17个点。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085927575624.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="552" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085927575624.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>美联储发布点阵图时，会公布利率中值，代表美联储未来的前瞻利率。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085928151396.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085928151396.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>中值并不是平均值，而是按照委员预测的数字，通过一定方式算出来：</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085928766714.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="908" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085928766714.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>比如，如果有17名委员，就取排在第9位委员的预测值作为中值。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085929386502.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="613" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085929386502.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>点阵图上的点虽然是委员们对利率的预测，但并不都代表委员们的个人判断，每名委员不仅需要根据自己对经济前景判断，还需要考虑对其他委员的认知，综合预测出未来会实行的利率。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085929868666.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="552" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085929868666.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>举个例子，假设有1名委员认为未来不需要加息：</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085930009026.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085930009026.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>但是具有投票权的票委大多认为需要加息：</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085930040988.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085930040988.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>那么这位委员就可能理性认为未来实际利率区间会高于自己认为合理的利率区间，反映在点阵图上这个点会上移一些。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085931084434.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/18/085931084434.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>可见，<strong>有投票权的票委，如果表达加息或者降息的观点后，对市场的影响还是蛮大的。</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg"
