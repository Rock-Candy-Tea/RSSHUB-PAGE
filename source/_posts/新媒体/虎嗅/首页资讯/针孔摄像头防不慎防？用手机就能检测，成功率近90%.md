target="_blank" rel="nofollow" style="text-decoration: none;"><span class="text-remarks" label="备注">量子位（ID：QbitAI）</span></a><span class="text-remarks" label="备注">，作者：丰色，原文标题：《针孔摄像头屡禁不止？用你手机自带的ToF传感器对付它，检测成功率近90%》，头图来自：视觉中国</span></p><p>不法分子用针孔摄像头违法偷拍的事儿，可以说是过段时间就上个新闻。<br></p><p>导致现在不少人出门住个酒店都要先用网上学来的方法查个遍，才能安心躺下。</p><p>然而像什么手机摄像头检测法、某宝上的检测仪等费时费力，主要效果还不一定好……</p><p>现在，来自新加坡国立大学的研究员们又开发出了一种新方法，用一部手机加一个智能应用程序，就能实现近90%的精准检测率。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/15/161303471221.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="549" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/15/161303471221.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>真的吗？来看。</p><h3 label="大标题" class="text-big-title">一部手机+机器学习APP揪出针孔摄像头<br></h3><p>首先，手机不是普通的手机，是带有ToF <span class="text-remarks" label="备注">（Time of light，飞行时间）</span>传感器的手机。</p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/15/161305949017.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="356" style="text-align: center;" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/15/161305949017.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">△ 论文来自ACM Sensys 2021 智能传感系统大会</p><p>所谓ToF传感器，就是传感器发出经调制的近红外光，遇物体后反射，然后通过计算光线发射和反射时间差或相位差，获得被拍摄景物的距离，以产生深度信息。</p><p>其测量范围可达数米、精度小于2cm，每秒更新频率可达30~60 fps。</p><p>可在手机上用于人脸识别、金融支付、摄像时精准抠像/探测景深、AR建模与体感游戏等。</p><p>现在有部分手机已经配有ToF传感器，比如iPhone 13、iPhone 12 Pro、Samsung S20+/Ultra、华为P30 Pro、OPPO RX17 Pro等。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/15/161307602109.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="516" data-h="650" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/15/161307602109.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>没有的也别着急，未来会渐渐成为一种趋势。<br></p><p>进入正题。</p><p>我们都知道，相比普通物体的“散射”，光线打在镜头这类物体上会形成强烈的逆反射，就像这样：</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/15/161308684986.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="796" data-h="358" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/15/161308684986.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>一个更形象的比喻：</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/15/161309842812.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="332" data-h="302" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/15/161309842812.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>而ToF传感器正好不仅能告诉你物体离摄像头有多远，还能反映图像中每个像素有多亮。</p><p>那就好办了，就比如下图最右，通过适当距离的ToF传感器反射，我们可以发现四个高亮点，而其中一个就是针孔摄像头藏身之处。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/15/161310939410.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="313" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/15/161310939410.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>但是，问题也来了，四个亮点长得差不多，凭什么说中间那个就是呢？</p><p>另外，如果传感器和嫌疑物的距离太近或太远，给出的成像会“糊”就会造成误报，那又不知道嫌疑物到底在哪，怎么选择一个理想位置进行检测呢？</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/15/161312336747.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="324" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/15/161312336747.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>以及目前的ToF传感器空间分辨率都非常低，只有240x320，这就导致一个针孔摄像头的图像可能就占1-2个像素；位深<span class="text-remarks" label="备注">（bit-depth）</span>也低，只能代表8种颜色，检测难度直线上升，误报率居高不下。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/15/161315395104.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="672" data-h="426" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/15/161315395104.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>最后，嫌疑摄像头反射的光只能在20°的视场角<span class="text-remarks" label="备注">（FoV）</span>范围内可见，操作不当也会导致漏报。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/15/161316580337.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="385" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/15/161316580337.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>好家伙，问题这么多，怎么解决？</p><p>这就得靠搭载了计算机视觉和机器学习的应用程序来帮忙了。</p><p>首先，使用手机摄像头对准一些你嫌疑的对象，然后APP通过计算引导你和嫌疑对象保持合适的检测距离<span class="text-remarks" label="备注">（一般是20厘米）</span>。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/15/161318633791.gif" data-w="500" data-h="432" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/15/161318633791.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>计算依据假定针孔摄像头的直径为1-2mm。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/15/161321244003.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="496" data-h="382" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/15/161321244003.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>在扫描过程中，ToF传感器的每一帧都给我们提供了激光强度图像和深度图像，程序将两者结合，提取候选区域，然后根据物理知识和机器学习过滤掉形状和距离基本不可能的选项。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/15/161323530602.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="290" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/15/161323530602.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>接着将过筛的图像通过坐标转换从2D转为3D，再通过FoV过滤器进行视场角过滤。</p><p>最终给出是否有针孔摄像头的结论。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/15/161324014275.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="291" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/15/161324014275.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>说了这么多，看看它的真本事吧。</p><h3 label="大标题" class="text-big-title">88.9%的检测率</h3><p>研究人员一共提供了21件不含和9件含针孔摄像头的物品进行测试。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/15/161328091561.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="324" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/15/161328091561.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>每件物品用三种检测方法：ToF手机/LAPD APP<span class="text-remarks" label="备注">（也就是他们的方法）</span>+一种叫做K18的市面检测仪+裸眼。</p><p>后两者需人为进行可疑摄像头的标注，一共379名志愿者参与。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/15/161330390058.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="274" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/15/161330390058.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>最终，他们的方法最高获得了88.9%的准确率，以及最低16.7%的误报率。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/15/161331241732.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="882" data-h="344" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/15/161331241732.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>比K18检测仪高了近30%。</p><p>并且，APP在实验用的Samsung S20+可以连续运行2个半小时。</p><p>看到这儿，网友都坐不住了，纷纷喊话：什么时候上线应用程序，戳我啊！</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/15/161333718089.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>好消息是，作者回应了：</p><blockquote><p>代码正在开源。</p></blockquote><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg"
