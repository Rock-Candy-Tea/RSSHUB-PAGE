电影《最后的决斗》</span></p><p><span class="text-remarks" label="备注">本文首发于虎嗅年轻内容公众号“那個NG”（ID：huxiu4youth）。在这里，我们呈现当下年轻人的面貌、故事和态度。</span></p><p>如果不是被按头推荐，我差一点就要错过这部年度必看的好电影了。</p><p>豆瓣8.5分，烂番茄新鲜度85%，成绩相当优秀。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/234213853594.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="475" class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/234213684803.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="300" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/234213684803.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>这部《最后的决斗》，卡司配置优秀到令人发指。口碑炸上天，热度却糊到底。</p><p>三个影帝级别的男演员：马特·达蒙<span class="text-remarks" label="备注">（《火星救援》）</span>、亚当·德莱弗<span class="text-remarks" label="备注">（《婚姻故事》《星球大战》）</span>和本·阿弗莱克<span class="text-remarks" label="备注">（《消失的爱人》）</span>，加上“小变态”朱迪·科默<span class="text-remarks" label="备注">（《杀死伊芙》）</span>。光是马达和大本继《心灵捕手》之后再次站在一起，就足够话题度。</p><p>再一看导演名字我就悟了——84岁的雷德利·斯科特<span class="text-remarks" label="备注">（《银翼杀手》《异形》《角斗士》）</span>现在拍个电影是连宣发都不搞了吗？</p><p>花了1亿制作，最后票房才收回来2900万。</p><p>虽然血本无归，但丝毫不影响它成为今年最好的女性电影。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/234213986522.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="667" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/234213986522.jpeg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpeg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">老骥伏枥的雷德利·斯科特<br></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/234157178235.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="214" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/234157178235.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>单看这部电影的设定外壳，我身边的直男观众第一反应就是雷德利·斯科特曾经拍过的那部《角斗士》，讲的是金戈铁马的男性荷尔蒙厮杀。</p><p>但这样想你就错了。</p><p label="正文" class="text-normal">真实的故事原型发生在中世纪，尚·德·卡鲁日<span class="text-remarks" label="备注">（马特·达蒙饰）</span>与贾克·勒格里<span class="text-remarks" label="备注">（亚当·德莱弗 饰）</span>，分别是领主<span class="text-remarks" label="备注">（本·阿弗莱克饰）</span>手下的骑士与乡绅。</p><p>卡鲁日和勒格里哥俩本是生死之交。有一天，勒格里趁卡鲁日不在家，强奸了他的妻子玛格丽特<span class="text-remarks" label="备注">（朱迪·科默 饰）</span>。</p><p>事后玛格丽特要控告勒格里，但勒格里声称自己是清白的。</p><p>而在欧洲中世纪，无法裁决的案件就交由上帝处理——卡鲁日与勒格里决斗。如果卡鲁日赢了，那便证明玛格丽特是清白的；如果卡鲁日输了，那便是玛格丽特诬告，她会被绑在火刑柱上活活烧死。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/234213328432.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="816" data-h="1244" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/234213328432.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">Eric Jager 原著 The Last Duel<br></p><p>一个简单的中世纪故事，雷德利·斯科特讲述它的方式是“罗生门”叙事——三位当事人，每个人都复述一遍。</p><p><strong>真正有趣的，这才开始。</strong></p><p><span class="text-remarks" label="备注">（以下涉及剧透）</span></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/234157666704.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="214" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/234157666704.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>在骑士卡鲁日的描述中，自己是一个温良恭俭让的好男人、好兄弟、好丈夫。<br></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/234228221767.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="393" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/234228221767.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">第一章：卡鲁日口中的真相</p><p>他在征战时骁勇孔武，还在危急关头救了勒格里一命，两个人是过命的交情。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/234223415767.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="417" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/234223415767.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>他和妻子玛格丽特，属于一见钟情。婚后得到了她娘家丰厚的嫁妆。<br></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/234235588073.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="417" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/234235588073.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>只要出差打仗，他都会和玛格丽特依依惜别：“放心在家待着，我来养你。”<br></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/234235824720.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="416" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/234235824720.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>玛格丽特在他眼里，是一个温顺、服从、懂事的金发美人。明眸善睐，要么爱意满满地看着他，要么羞涩地别过头。<br></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/235143277430.gif" data-w="890" data-h="371" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/235143277430.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>要么在他下班回家后，乖巧地跪在地上为他洗脚。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/235150754134.gif" data-w="890" data-h="371" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/235150754134.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>卡鲁日娶了美妇，在床笫之间更是大展雄风。凭着他健壮的体格，玛格丽特生一个大胖小子指日可待。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/234234104573.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="483" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/234234104573.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>而他曾经的好兄弟勒格里，在领主面前就知道拍马屁。仗都没打过几次，凭着嘴皮子就“截胡”了本应属于卡鲁日的土地和爵位。<br></p><p>但作为大善人的卡鲁日，当然是宰相肚里能撑船，主动在聚会上和勒格里握手言和。还大方地让自己的老婆去亲吻老友，以示他的气度。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/235311686556.gif" data-w="890" data-h="371" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/235311686556.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>没想到，卡鲁日一次出差回家后，听到了一个噩耗。<br></p><p>玛格丽特泪眼婆娑地哭诉：<strong>“我被强奸了”（I was raped）</strong>。</p><p>卡鲁日听完之后心疼万分，立刻愧疚地抱住妻子说道歉“对不起，没能保护好你”，坚定地支持妻子控告格里，他愿意为此付出一切代价。</p><p>卡鲁日的版本到此结束。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/234234318416.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="418" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/234234318416.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>而强奸犯勒格里的描述，为这则故事增添了许多讽刺的细节。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/234229701510.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="387" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/234229701510.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">第二章：勒格里口中的真相</p><p>在勒格里眼里，自己沉稳智慧，还在危急关头救过卡鲁日一命，两个人是过命的交情。<br></p><p>卡鲁日是一个鲁莽没脑子的武夫，干啥啥不行，人缘还特差。</p><p>而勒格里自己，是一个风流倜傥，会朗读拉丁文的青年才俊，哪个女人见了他都腿软。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/234235706200.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="416" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/234235706200.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>懂得人情世故的勒格里，每每在卡鲁日丢人现眼的时候，都帮他找台阶下，反倒是卡鲁日这个人小肚鸡肠，经常狗咬吕洞宾。</p><p>但勒格里是一个有气度的人，当然是宰相肚里能撑船，所以他主动在聚会上和卡鲁日握手言和。矮小懦弱的卡鲁日，只能唯唯诺诺地答应。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/235432972660.gif" data-w="890" data-h="371" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/235432972660.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>卡鲁日拽出自己的妻子玛格丽特献吻，结果这个女人也立刻为高大威猛的勒格里所倾倒。一吻之后，回味无穷地盯着他看个没完。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/235450884227.gif" data-w="890" data-h="371" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/235450884227.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>勒格里认定，这个女人一定是爱上自己了——这也不能怪她，毕竟自己的魅力太耀眼。<br></p><p>两个人在聚会上保持距离地调情，用拉丁文从诗词歌赋聊到人生哲学，浪漫又含蓄。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/235517526133.gif" data-w="890" data-h="371" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/235517526133.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>勒格里纳闷，这么有文化又美丽的玛格丽特，怎么偏偏就嫁给文盲糙汉卡鲁日了？</p><p>他一边贪恋着玛格丽特的美貌，一边承受着良心和道德的自责：朋友妻不可欺。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/234235051191.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="417" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/234235051191.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>但爱情至上的勒格里，最终还是没忍住闯进玛格丽特的闺房，对她深情表白。</p><p>“我爱你，我愿为你赴汤蹈火”，这么英俊潇洒的人能看上她，也是她的福气。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/234234451411.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="416" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/234234451411.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>玛格丽特欲拒还迎，她故意在楼梯口脱下鞋子，转身勾引勒格里上楼进卧室。两个人就像中世纪的西门庆和潘金莲，颠鸾倒凤，你情我愿。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/235603425507.gif" data-w="890" data-h="371" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/235603425507.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>大善人勒格里，事后还去教堂忏悔自己背叛兄弟的罪，为自己和玛格丽特的爱情备受煎熬。<br></p><p>神父苦口婆心地教育他：这是上帝对你的试炼。玛格丽特勾引你，就像夏娃当初勾引亚当，这种妖妇毒性很深，咱们男人一定要小心。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/234234071948.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="416" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/234234688571.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="413" class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/05/000004093580.gif" data-w="890" data-h="371" class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/04/234234826108.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="414"
