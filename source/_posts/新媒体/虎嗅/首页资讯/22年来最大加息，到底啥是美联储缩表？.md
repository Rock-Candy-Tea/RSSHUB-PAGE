class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202205/05/083129979513.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="720" data-h="360" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202205/05/083129979513.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>不是，<strong>主要是靠自己印的。<br></strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202205/05/083202557815.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="720" data-h="360" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202205/05/083202557815.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202205/05/083203864563.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202205/05/083203864563.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202205/05/083204482944.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202205/05/083204482944.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202205/05/083204003135.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202205/05/083204003135.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>我们继续说，央行可不是一般的<strong>商业银行</strong><strong>，央行管的是货币印刷和发行，不干吸储的事。</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202205/05/083205661825.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="720" data-h="360" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202205/05/083205661825.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>央行的资产负债表是根据IMF的口径来统计的，<strong>且央行本身没有股东，属于非盈利性机构。</strong></p><p>我们以美联储为例来说说央行缩表。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202205/05/083205899100.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202205/05/083205899100.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>央行发行的纸币，这张纸本身<strong>不值钱</strong>，只是印刷品而已，但<strong>国家信用</strong>赋予了它价值，所以我们现在使用的纸币叫<strong>“信用货币”</strong>。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202205/05/083206484983.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202205/05/083206484983.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>纸币其实就是一种债权证明，是一张特殊的欠条，是<strong>央行的负债</strong>。<br></p><p>所以央行的缩表要减少负债，这就相当于收回纸币，不再流通。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202205/05/083207121989.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202205/05/083207121989.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202205/05/083207407316.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202205/05/083207407316.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202205/05/083208096699.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202205/05/083208096699.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>就这样，<strong>美联储通过卖出各类债券，或者缩减规模，把钱从市场上回收到仓库里封存，完成了缩表。</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202205/05/083209934673.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202205/05/083209934673.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>本次美联储宣布缩减<strong>8.9万亿美元</strong>资产负债表规模，从6月1日开始每月475亿美元的步伐缩表，<strong>将在3个月内逐步提高缩表上限至每月950亿美元。</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202205/05/083209938109.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="720" data-h="360" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202205/05/083209938109.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><strong>美联储通过缩表，调节基础货币数量，是退出宽松措施的一种举措，属于紧缩政策。</strong></p><p>首先，美联储为了缩表，抛售资产。</p><p>比如抛售债券，使得市面上债券变多，债券利率上升。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202205/05/083210094753.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="720" data-h="360" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202205/05/083210094753.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>你想啊，市场上这么多债券，若你利率不提高，谁买你的啊？</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202205/05/083210300910.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="720" data-h="360" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202205/05/083210300910.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>这就是供过于求导致资产自身价格下跌，<strong>市场上对资产的收益率要价提高。</strong><br></p><p>而且利率具有<strong>传导性</strong>，整个市场利率都随之提高。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202205/05/083211691038.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="720" data-h="360" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202205/05/083211691038.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>其次，<strong>美联储抛售资产回收了基础货币，使得市场上的美元总量变少，美元就更值钱了，对黄金、大宗商品都会产生影响。</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202205/05/083211401336.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="720" data-h="360" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202205/05/083211401336.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>钱少了，整个市场利率又上升了，那么股市会出现一定程度的<strong>下跌预期</strong>。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202205/05/083212899700.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202205/05/083212899700.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>但是，<strong>本次美联储加息会议后，美股创下了44年来最大加息日涨幅！</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202205/05/083212596147.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202205/05/083212596147.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>本文结束，以下为彩蛋时间：</p><p>市场预估美联储6月会加息75个基点，但美联储主席表示，加息75个基点的激进方案不值得考虑。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg"
