referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><strong>2022年3月31日，观典防务公司成为“北交所转板第一股”，获批到科创板上市。</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/01/123027041553.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/01/123027041553.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>那可不一样。我们知道，资本市场被划分为不同的板块，不同板块的公司在规模、成长性等方面有诸多不同。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/01/123027920930.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="1255" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/01/123027920930.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>所谓转板，可以简单理解为<strong>在一个板块上市的公司，转向另一个板块上市或者挂牌转让。</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/01/123028637040.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/01/123028637040.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>比如在北交所上市的公司，可以申请到上交所的科创板上市。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/01/123029650324.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/01/123029650324.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>或者申请到深交所的创业板上市。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/01/123030204431.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/01/123030204431.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>这次，<strong>原来在北交所上市的“观典防务公司”就是转到上交所科创板上市。</strong></p><p>但是，北交所上市公司想转板，也不是想转就能转的，需要符合一定的条件。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/01/123031974919.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/01/123031974919.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><h3 label="大标题" class="text-big-title">一、需要在北交所上市已经连续满1年<br></h3><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/01/123031764797.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/01/123031764797.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/01/123032389989.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/01/123032389989.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>公司在北交所上市前，曾经在新三板原精选层挂牌的，挂牌时间和北交所上市时间可以合并计算。</p><h3 label="大标题" class="text-big-title">二、再次审核</h3><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/01/123033102121.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="527" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/01/123033102121.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/01/123034268020.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="532" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/01/123034268020.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><strong>公司提出转板申请的，由上交所或者深交所进行审核。</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/01/123035971954.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/01/123035971954.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>公司需要满足转入板块的上市条件。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/01/123035287135.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/01/123035287135.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>此外，<strong>公司还要满足转板办法的要求，</strong>要点包括：</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/01/123036830663.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="831" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/01/123036830663.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>但<strong>由于转板只是属于股票上市地的变更，因此不需要经过证监会核准或者注册。</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/01/123037415331.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/01/123037415331.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>上交所、深交所审核后同意上市的，<strong>公司要在北交所终止上市，然后再在科创板或创业板上市交易。</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/01/123038878577.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="532" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/01/123038878577.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><strong>但上交所、深交所作出转板审核的决定，需要及时向证监会备案。</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/01/123039365774.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/01/123039365774.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>此外，指导意见还对转板的股份限售做了安排，需要符合上交所、深交所相关限售规定。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/01/123040797977.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/01/123040797977.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>但限售期可以扣除在北交所已经限售的时间。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/01/123040427721.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/01/123040427721.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>最后，我们回顾下多层次资本市场的主要板块：</p><p
