<img src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/cover/202105/12/173936012491.jpg?imageView2/1/w/1440/h/810/|imageMogr2/strip/interlace/1/quality/85/format/jpg" alt="你可能有买房焦虑，但鸽子没有" data-v-30cd1b88 referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><p><span class="text-remarks" label="备注">本文来自微信公众号：</span><a href="https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s/53H5_xdlmnAX5jp4Q7ivlg" target="_blank" rel="nofollow" style="text-decoration: none;"><span class="text-remarks">地球人研究报告（ID：diqiuren005）</span></a><span class="text-remarks">，作者：你的外星小姨，题图来自：视觉中国</span></p><p>房子总是现代人生活中逃不出的坑。</p><p>之所以说是“坑”，主要是因为，想居住在一间属于自己的小窝里，属实是一件不太容易的事。</p><p>你不仅要背上天价的房贷，还会为这间房的一切而发愁。导致空了钱包，又难如意，这恐怕是当今社会上大部分成年人的共同困境。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171702325969.gif?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/gif/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="480" data-h="267" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171702325969.gif?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/gif/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><strong>但有一个群体是例外。</strong></p><p>这个群体，对房子不屑一顾，随遇而安，住不顺心就换，也不太操心自己的小孩。</p><p><strong>是的，那就是鸽子<span class="text-remarks" label="备注">（以及其他部分鸟类）</span>。</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171703474608.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="636" data-h="602" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171703474608.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><h3 label="大标题" class="text-big-title">一</h3><p>鸽子是城市里对住房要求最低的生物，这点毋庸置疑。</p><p>我们一般人对鸽子窝的展望常常是这样的：结实，牢固，可靠，能为鸽子宝宝遮风挡雨。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171708620966.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="270" data-h="180" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171708620966.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>事实上大部分鸽子窝也是如此，但这并不排除部分鸽子对筑巢的敷衍性态度。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171710387895.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="636" data-h="602" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171710387895.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>一些懒鸽对筑巢的态度非常明确：一树枝，一平地，生一蛋足矣。它们似乎并不像人类一样担心，自己的小孩长大之后会问自己“为什么咱家这么穷”。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171713745600.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="636" data-h="358" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171713745600.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>对鸽子来说，一个窝的定义基本上是这样的：有点树枝就行。在装修和户型这方面，它们不是非常讲究。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171715587437.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="636" data-h="846" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171715587437.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>由于鸽子是经常擅闯人类住宅来私搭自家违建鸽子窝的，所以在一些鸽子比较多的地区，房主经常能看见这样的情景：阳台上突然多了几根树枝，以为是风刮来的所以没太在意。没成想过了几天，一个浑圆的鸽子蛋赫然摆放其中。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171718564596.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="636" data-h="502" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171718564596.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>这就是这些鸽子窝的宗旨：干货不在多，看起来像回事就行。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171720897960.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="636" data-h="476" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171720897960.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>当然，这些简易鸽子住房的出现并不是毫无道理。</p><p>根据鸽子的习性，它们是采用的是夫妻共同努力买房制：雌雄鸽共同筑巢。并且是在交配后才开始寻找材料来搭窝，总体来说比较前卫。</p><p>所以我们或许可以大胆推测出原因：可能是有的鸽子交配的时候比较急了。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171723028910.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="636" data-h="476" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171723028910.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>值得一提的是，或许由于中国有着“基建狂魔”的这个称号，懒鸽懒窝在国内倒不是特别常见。但在欧美地区经常能看到：</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171727237740.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="636" data-h="490" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171727237740.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">你的毕业论文<br label="图片备注" class="text-img-note"></p><p>有的外国人还开始了“比比谁家鸽子更懒”的竞赛。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171729172563.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="636" data-h="1336" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171729172563.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><h3 label="大标题" class="text-big-title">二</h3><p>在住房要求这方面，人类不得不承认的是：的确是鸽子更朋克一些。</p><p>他们不仅对房子本身没什么特殊需求，连选址都非常洒脱。</p><p>在知乎上有这样一个提问：一位网友发现斑鸠<span class="text-remarks" label="备注">（鸽形目 ）</span>在自家阳台筑巢，随便找了个缝隙就准备生崽了。</p><p>结果这位网友本人似乎都更比鸽子担心这个住址的安全程度。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171739280899.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="636" data-h="188" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171739280899.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>类似如此的提问下，往往有数百位网友激情回复道，自己也遇到过同样的情况。根据他们晒出的图片可以总结出：鸽子们的确就是随便选个地方下蛋。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171741571362.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="636" data-h="682" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171741571362.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">以筑巢简陋为名的珠颈斑鸠<br label="图片备注" class="text-img-note"></p><p>对于同样需要繁衍后代的人类而言，人们总是会去考虑下一代的生存环境：周边环境好不好，有没有好学校，邻居怎么样……</p><p>鸽子的观点则是：不是很关心。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171745466682.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="636" data-h="846" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171745466682.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>前年还有一只鸽子直接闯进人类居住的宿舍，在拖把上搭窝的事件成为当地热议的新闻。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171749308935.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="636" data-h="452" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171749308935.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>不过虽说这种生活态度的确洒脱，但大城市对于鸽子来说，可能也是一个不那么容易生存的地方。</p><p>由于近年世界各地的城市化程度都在日益提高，对鸟类来说，找树枝已经变成了一项艰巨的任务。</p><p>所以我们不乏看到鸽子用铁丝搭窝的新闻图片出现。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171751741282.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="630" data-h="1014" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171751741282.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>这类图片往往会被用来警醒人类要注意环保。但除此之外，鸽子们随遇而安，能忍的特点也同样值得注意。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171754139791.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="636" data-h="1128" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171754139791.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>之所以说“能忍”，主要是因为，它们似乎已经开始不再要求建材无毒害了。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171800510159.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="636" data-h="476" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171800510159.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>甚至硌不硌得慌都不太在意，敷个蛋都能给人一种砥砺前行的励志感。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171802562036.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="636" data-h="358" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171802562036.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>只不过它们有时也会洒脱到一个令人心酸的程度。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171804435723.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="636" data-h="424" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171804435723.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><h3 label="大标题" class="text-big-title">三</h3><p>以上这些信息，可能会让你感觉家对鸽子来说并不是很重要。但实际情况正好相反，根据鸽子的习性，它们其实具有强烈的“归巢性”——鸽子会长期生活在自己出生的地方，且有些恋家，不愿在除自家以外的地方长期栖息。有些像现在的宅男宅女。</p><p>但即使如此，它们却仍然有这样洒脱和自由的生活态度，不免令人肃然起敬<span class="text-remarks" label="备注">（当然并不值得羡慕）</span>。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171808340178.gif?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/gif/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="455" data-h="244" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171808340178.gif?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/gif/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>除此之外，也有些鸽子还活出了几分叛逆的味道。</p><p>之前就有一位网友，家中窗外经常有鸽子光顾，弄成鸟叫不绝，鸟屎满天的场面。因不堪其扰，他在窗外放置了防止鸟类筑巢的防鸟刺。</p><p>结果没过几天，他就发现鸽子直接在刺上筑了巢，而且生米已煮成熟饭。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171812896544.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="800" data-h="600" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171812896544.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">鸟：好耶，有地基。<br label="图片备注" class="text-img-note"></p><p>叛逆只是初阶。而彻底活出自我的鸽子，已经逃脱了房的束缚。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171813635057.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="636" data-h="476" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171813635057.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>再超脱点的鸟类，已经做到了心中有蛋，万物皆蛋的境界。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171815625739.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="636" data-h="1130" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171815625739.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>至于最叛逆的那一只——</p><p>在几年前，一位外国网友回到家，发现家里厨房满地鸽子屎。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171822603502.gif?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/gif/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="443" data-h="341" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171822603502.gif?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/gif/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>而这只似乎在反抗人类强权的鸽子，则直接把蛋下在了他的平底锅里。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171825078660.gif?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/gif/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="511" data-h="321" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202105/12/171825078660.gif?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/gif/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><span class="text-remarks"
