<p><span class="text-remarks" label="备注">本文来自微信公众号：</span><a href="https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s/5SF1tTaPAyDejxADX2E6-g" target="_blank" rel="nofollow" style="text-decoration: none;"><span class="text-remarks">尤呢呢（ID：nene0217）</span></a><span class="text-remarks">，作者：尤呢呢，原文标题：《5+2基站扫地机化繁为简，解放双手60天！》</span></p><p>最初大家对于扫地机的需求就是扫地，吸力大即可。后来发现拖地一并解决了那更好，而且还要拖干净，因此震动拖布随即出现。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/06/115728585694.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/06/115728585694.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>再后来垃圾也不想天天倒，于是出现了集尘桩。既然垃圾不想倒，拖布自然也不想洗，所以自清洁拖布也成了必选功能。那么发展到现在功能最全面的基站到底都能具备哪些功能呢？今天就来一起看看行业顶配的5+2基站到底是否实用。</p><h3 label="大标题" class="text-big-title">基站功能</h3><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/06/115730131471.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="558" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/06/115730131471.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>随着扫地机的内卷，基站功能越来越多，而这次石头旗舰新品G10S系列升级的更加彻底，不但可以满足集尘和自清洗拖布的需求，还通过模块化设计增加自动上下水功能，连更换净污水箱的步骤都免了。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/06/115730113504.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/06/115730113504.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>并且在扫地机清洁过程中不但会自动回洗，而且在回洗时还可以给机身水箱自动补水，从而保证拖地的湿度统一。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/06/115733055154.gif" data-w="676" data-h="380" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/06/115733055154.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>并且每次自清洗拖布后，拖布会自动朝外抬升从而实现完全静音的自然风干。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/06/115734927221.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="563" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/06/115734927221.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>如果家里是梅雨天气，那么也可以通过模块的增加，实现加速烘干的功能。</p><p>除了可以通过模块化实现新功能的增加外，集尘功能和自清洗拖布的功能其实也有所升级。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/06/115735122358.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/06/115735122358.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>传统集尘桩需要定期倾倒垃圾，不但可能造成二次污染而且还需要动手清理十分麻烦。而这次G10S直接通过集尘袋来实现集尘需求，不但无需手动清理无粉尘飞出，而且集尘袋可以60天一换相比于半月一清理的集尘桩也更加方便。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/06/115736921160.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/06/115736921160.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>自清洁拖布的净水箱中增加了银离子模块，可以起到抑菌的作用。并且G10S系列可以在清理时给机身水箱自动补水，从而保证了拖布、水箱和地面的全链条安心。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/06/115737162784.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/06/115737162784.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>最后大多扫地机只能做到拖布自清洁，而基站还是需要手动清理的。但石头G10S系列不但能自清洁拖布，连基站也可以自动清洁。真的可以做到60天不用管。</p><h3 label="大标题" class="text-big-title">扫拖功能</h3><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/06/115738635051.gif" data-w="512" data-h="288" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/06/115738635051.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>聊完基站接下来我们就来看看主机功能，说起主机功能很多朋友只会追求纸面数据。例如这款扫地机就具备5100Pa的大吸力，确实吸力大了金属球都能吸起来，看似清理能力更强了。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/06/115740581309.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/06/115740581309.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>但是日常使用中其实4000Pa以上吸力就绝对够用了，而毛发缠绕才更让人头疼。因此G10S系列使用的是全胶主刷，相比于传统主刷来说不易缠绕毛发。</p><p>同时主刷的两端还可以拆卸，这样清理时更加的便利和彻底。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/06/115740076393.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/06/115740076393.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>拖地方面，目前扫地机有两种主流方式，分别是震动拖布和旋转拖布，其实我认为清洁力差距不大。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/06/115742521882.gif" data-w="565" data-h="318" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/06/115742521882.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>所以关键在于地毯的处理方式上，传统扫地机只能通过禁区设置避开地毯。而石头G10S系列则可以通过智能升降擦地模组，从而实现地毯的只吸不拖。</p><h3 label="大标题" class="text-big-title">建图避障</h3><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/06/115743809163.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="676" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/06/115743809163.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>最后来聊聊的就是建图避障能力，其实现在基于激光导航建图的大品牌一般算法都不差，建图都很完整。</p><p>而石头不但可以快速建图，还新增了3D地图和matrix地图可玩性更强，3D地图可以更立体化的表现房间结构，而matrix地图则是需要配合iphone 12 pro以上机型来创建。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/06/115744954390.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="614" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/06/115744954390.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>同时功能方面除了基础的定制清洁顺序、划区清洁等功能外，还能设置更个性化的需求，例如首页快捷指令功能等。</p><p>而且通过智能补光功能，不论是床下空间还是天黑后光线不足，都不必担心机器人迷路。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/06/115748976724.gif" data-w="483" data-h="272" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/06/115748976724.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>至于大家最关心的避障问题，石头G10S系列都可以精准测距，并准确绕过障碍物。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/06/115749003497.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/06/115749003497.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>除了基础款外，这次G10S系列还推出了Pro款，最大的区别就是搭载了RGB摄像头。基础款在避障时会标记障碍物，而Pro还会同时拍摄一张照片。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/06/115750174456.gif" data-w="388" data-h="218" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/06/115750174456.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>并且G10S Pro还可以进行实时视频，并进行双向语音，相当于多了一个可移动的摄像头。</p><p>至于隐私问题也不必担心想要开启实时视频需要通过主机来实现，并且会有视频运行的提示，而APP只能关闭视频功能想要再次开启还是需要主机来操作。</p><h3 label="大标题" class="text-big-title">总结</h3><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/06/115752095656.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/06/115752095656.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>不知道这次石头G10S系列强大的5+2基站是否能满足你的所有需求，其实除了基站功能的提升外，精准避障和拖布抬起功我也很感兴趣。</p><p>精准避障解决了地面杂物整理的问题，拖布抬起解决了地毯清理的问题，看似小的改变其实大大提升了使用便利度。</p><p><span class="text-remarks"
