class="text-remarks">，作者：三折人生，头图来自：视觉中国</span></p><p>据说，近日不少中小银行收到通知，鼓励它们把存款利率浮动上限下调约10个基点。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221049014142.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221049014142.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>为了搞清楚，我们要从头说起。我们之所以愿意把钱存在银行，是因为银行会给我们利息。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221050292937.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221050292937.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>至于利息给多少，是根据存款利率算出来的。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221051790405.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221051790405.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>在上世纪90年代，所有商业银行的存款利率都是一致的。就像去饭店吃饭，虽然饭店有大有小，可是价格竟然是一样的。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221101956773.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221101956773.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>因为当时我国存款利率改革尚未放开，<strong>存款利率由央行发布的基准利率决定。</strong></p><p>各家商业银行无权调整，存款利率不准上浮也不准下调。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221102107074.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221102107074.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>到了2004年10月，我国放开了，除城乡信用社以外的各商业银行存款利率的下限。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221103736773.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221103736773.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221104959731.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221104959731.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>2012年监管开始允许金融机构上浮存款利率，但上浮幅度最高不超过存款基准利率的1.1倍。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221105843520.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221105843520.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221106424788.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221106424788.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>这时候，各银行的存款利率上浮比例可以不一样。一般来说，大银行允许上浮的比例少，中小银行允许上浮的多。但都不能大于最高限额。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221106223137.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="247" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221106223137.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>等到2014年11月22日，上浮最高幅度进一步调整为1.2倍。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221107568904.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221107568904.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>2015年3月1日调整至1.3倍。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221107914573.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="520" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221107914573.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>2015年5月11日调整至1.5倍。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221108216205.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="586" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221108216205.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>到了2015年10月24日则全面放开了存款利率上限。这是利率市场化改革迈进了重要一步。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221109569795.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="586" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221109569795.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>此时，我国利率市场化进程在形式上，就可以宣告基本完成了。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221110151628.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="247" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221110151628.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221110453349.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221110453349.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>这是因为，虽然在存款利率上浮比例放开后，各银行对于自身的存款利率有了自主决定权，<strong>中小银行能通过提高自身的存款利率来吸引客户，增强与大银行的竞争力。</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221111112266.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="586" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221111112266.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>但是，有个问题：</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221112777208.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="684" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221112777208.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>实质上，<strong>很多银行还不具备存款利率的定价能力，如果个别银行一味提高存款利率只会导致恶性竞争。</strong></p><p>美国在推进利率市场化进程中就曾遇到类似问题，银行恶性竞争导致利差大幅下降，最终纷纷倒闭。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221113581446.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="684" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221113581446.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>所以，借鉴国外的经验教训，防范推进利率市场化过程中引发的金融风险，我国成立了市场利率定价自律机制组织，全行业继续实施存款利率行业自律。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221114419726.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221114419726.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>不同地区不同类型银行约定存款利率上浮的自律上限，对存款利率继续构成了一个无形的控制线。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221115153938.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="247" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221115153938.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>在这个控制线下，大部分银行按照基准利率上浮1.4倍，部分银行经申请后可上浮最高至1.5倍。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221115135875.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221115135875.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>定价自律机制有点类似“利率同盟”的市场自律组织，对各银行自主确定价格进行自律管理。</p><p><strong>定价自律机制的存在，为推进利率市场化的顺利实施起到了保驾护航的作用。</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221116138284.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="545" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221116138284.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><strong>2021年6月，存款利率定价方式，又进行了优化调整。</strong></p><p>存款利率的上限由“基准利率✖️倍数”调整为“基准利率+基点”。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221116974157.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="563" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/17/221116974157.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>而且对不同类型银行、不同的存款期限设置了不同的最高“基点”上限。</p><p referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>由于采用“基准利率+基点”模式定价是国际通行的定价惯例，这样调整是行业自律统一协商的结果。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>这次下调的目的，主要是通过降低银行资金成本，可以为银行进一步扩大信贷投放、推动贷款利率下行提供空间，从而加大金融对实体经济的支持力度。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg"
