<p label="正文" class="text-normal">作为一个败家之眼ROG的“中毒”用户，不免会安利身边的朋友也用上ROG。</p><p label="正文" class="text-normal">总有小伙伴问同系列产品间的差异在哪，就如ROG棱镜精英版耳机和ROG棱镜耳机，应该如何选择？是无脑选前者？还是根据自身需求将每一分都用到刀刃上，接下来我们就聊一聊，它们的差距体现在什么地方。</p><h3 label="大标题" class="text-big-title">外观细节区别</h3><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204001932345.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="572" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204001932345.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204002580069.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="572" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204002580069.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>从外包装盒来说，ROG棱镜白色与ROG棱镜精英版的区别不大，设计风格与元素均差不多，<strong>主要差异在一些小标注上，如对于PS4的支持，四路DAC芯片。</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204003615607.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="572" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204003615607.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>开盒方式没有啥不同，但ROG棱镜精英缺少一份欢迎卡，稍微少了一点仪式感。内部均为两层设计，上层是被托架支撑固定着的耳机主体，下层是配件盒。尝试两者互换摆放，托架可完美契合。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204003883190.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="572" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204003883190.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204004677891.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="572" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204004677891.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>配件方面，ROG棱镜白色的明显要多出许多，除了说明书和可拆麦克风以外，还配有延长线以及ROG混合材质织物耳罩。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204004387414.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="572" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204004387414.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>对于同为默认Typc-C接口的耳机来说，延长线的缺少无疑会影响到兼容性，用户得自己再去配一条Type-C转USB-A，倒是有些麻烦。而且ROG混合材质织物耳罩也给用户多一个选择，毕竟长久佩戴后耳罩不免会沾上汗渍油脂，清理时也有一个“备件”可替换。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204004950991.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="572" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204004950991.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>不过两者的非指向性降噪麦克风规格倒是相当的，实测也可互换插入。或许是因为我不常用麦克风的关系，会很喜欢这种可拆式麦克风的设计，因为它不会干扰到我的佩戴体验。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204005881451.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="572" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204005881451.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>无论是ROG棱镜白色还是ROG棱镜精英版，其单元外型均采用了D形设计，外壳有些像吉他拨片。不过外壳为不可更换设计，这倒让其可玩度不高，希望以后能更进。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204005914648.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="572" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204005914648.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>此外，音频线也是不可拆卸，但尼龙线材的耐用度较高，再加上引出端的防护措施，影响倒也不大。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204006116389.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="572" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204006116389.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>实际上ROG棱镜是灰+黑配色，整体与ROG棱镜精英外观一模一样。如果两者摆在一起时会很难分清彼此，这也是我为何用白色款ROG棱镜作为区别演示的关系。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204006302125.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="572" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204006302125.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204007408359.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="572" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204007408359.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>尽管ROG棱镜精英版的定位比ROG棱镜白色的要低一点，但并不意味着会偷工减料。头梁这块，ROG棱镜精英也是采用了传统的蛋白皮包裹，表面触感细腻，车缝线走势工整，两者的规格是一样的。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204007329070.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="600" data-h="324" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204007329070.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>按压后可感受到内部丰富的海绵填充物，回弹反馈不错，在缓解压力的同时又能很好地贴合头顶。从我对于ROG棱镜白色的佩戴体验来说，至少3个小时不会有泰山压顶的感觉。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204008330489.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="572" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204008330489.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204008762579.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="572" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204008762579.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>连接的转轴处，两者的区别只有ROG棱镜白色使用了塑料电镀而已。角度调节两者是相当的，都支持纵向30°与水平90°转动。灵活度很高，都可平置于桌面上，日常收纳很方便。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204009730686.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="572" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204009730686.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>控键和插口两者毫无区别，也都放在有音频线的左侧，依次为Aura灯效开关、音量/麦克风调节，麦克风插孔。其中音量/麦克风为拨杆设计，拨动是调节音量，按下为麦克风开关。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204009441259.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="572" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204009441259.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>同样的，默认耳罩部分与头梁一样也是蛋白皮，内里用海绵填充。两个的规格尺寸一样，也是可以互换。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204010115053.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="572" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204010115053.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>单只看耳罩下的驱动单元，其实也看不出所以然来，肉眼可见两个都是搭配的50mm驱动单元。既然外观无差，那么ROG棱镜白色与ROG棱镜精英的区别在哪呢？</p><p>根据官方参数显示两者的单元都采用铷磁铁材质，阻抗32欧姆，幅频响应20Hz-40kHz，单元内均应用了加大气密腔室腔体设计和音频导流技术，但是ROG棱镜白色拥有4路Hi-Fi ESS DAC解码，可带来更丰富的声音细节。</p><h3 label="大标题" class="text-big-title">应用支持</h3><p>除了硬件上的差异以外，在Armoury Crate奥创软件中也有些不一样，由于是型号不同，耳机在奥创软件中的识别名称也不一样。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204011025747.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="900" data-h="515" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204011025747.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204011182240.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="572" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204011182240.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>分别进入到“音效”界面时，两者的可调节项并无差别。我们都可以对耳机的声道、混响、均衡器、麦克风、虚拟环绕音效等进行调节。细节选择很多，可适配各类玩家的需求。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204011489728.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="572" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204011489728.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204011676077.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="900" data-h="515" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204011676077.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>但在“灯光”选择中，ROG棱镜白色可选的灯效更多，包括Aura Sync模式在内，总计有7种模式。而ROG棱镜精英只有恒亮、呼吸以及闪烁等3种。</p><p><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204011550417.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="572" style="text-align: center;" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204011550417.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></p><p>切换到“Aura Sync”功能时，我们可以看到同步设备下并无棱镜精英版勾选，也就是说ROG棱镜精英并不支持Aura Sync模式，这样就导致ROG棱镜精英的可玩性不如ROG棱镜白色限定版。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204012500724.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="572" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204012500724.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>尽管ROG棱镜精英的外壳也会亮，但却是单一的红色灯效。</p><p>当我们将所有ROG设备Aura Sync同步后，ROG棱镜白色、月刃鼠标、游侠键盘均会以彩虹色彩过渡，设备颜色会形成了互联统一，唯独ROG棱镜精英只能保持红色灯效，不参与其中。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204013778777.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="572" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204013778777.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>哪怕我们将同步灯效设定为“呼吸”或“闪烁”模式，ROG棱镜精英也只是保持着红灯长亮状态。在可玩度上，确实要弱于ROG棱镜白色耳机。</p><h3 label="大标题" class="text-big-title">佩戴与游戏体验</h3><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204014873027.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="439" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204014873027.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>实际佩戴体验，ROG棱镜白色和ROG棱镜精英的感受差不多，脸部两侧轮廓的贴合很高，头顶施加的压力不大。如果硬要说ROG棱镜精英要轻一些，就有些夸张了，只是4g的差异感知。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204015675727.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="572" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204015675727.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>两个耳机都支持7.1声道，当我们在游戏中设置后声场效果会有质的飞跃。如我们在游玩《极品飞车》时，当车辆加速，引擎高速转动后，轰鸣声会充斥着双耳。当超过车辆或被超越时，多声道所带来的方位感极强，游戏的空间与氛围感倍增。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204015451891.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="571" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204015451891.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>在玩FPS类游戏时，人物的脚步声远近也变得清晰，方向定位较精准。当出现双方交火时，能分辨出周遭的射击方位，并不会杂乱。此外，耳机的隔音效果相当优秀，可轻松隔绝周围的环境声。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204015727485.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="572" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/204015727485.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>而ROG棱镜白色的优势在于FPS游戏时，声道分离更透彻，听感上会有一点细微的区别，不过当游戏节奏变得相当快时，这些细节变得不容易被察觉。除非是追求氛围感的大作，例如在《生化危机》、《往日不在》、《求生之路》等游戏中，当出现僵尸低吼，怪物扑面时，可感觉到那种紧迫感，刺激度会有所提升，让我能更快地融入到游戏中。</p><p
