class="text-remarks">廖信忠（ID：lxztaiwan）</span></a><span class="text-remarks">，作者：廖信忠，头图来自：廖信忠</span></p><p>在上海最湿最冷的那几天又来了三亚，阳光和煦温暖。</p><p>啊！三亚！椰奶与蜜之地！</p><p>从机场到市区，经过三亚湾路，海滩边的椰梦长廊，是许多人的三亚初印象。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/072402402676.gif" data-w="410" data-h="230" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/072402402676.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><a href="https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s/Mdwe8Yf0vfGVFbnQPq-3Kw" target="_blank" rel="nofollow">去年来三亚</a>时我提到这条海边的带状公园，一路上你能见到各种中老年才艺展示。每回我到三亚一看这些跳舞的还在，我就安心了，三亚还是那个三亚。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/072403897457.gif" data-w="400" data-h="225" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/072403897457.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><strong>那是全球最长的带状才艺竞技区</strong>！来自天南地北<span class="text-remarks" label="备注">（主要地北）</span>的中老年，在这海风徐徐，温暖的季节，在这南方海滩边尽情燃烧旺盛的生命力。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/141259761267.gif" data-w="409" data-h="230" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/141259761267.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>椰梦长廊的白天不懂夜的黑，如果你白天过来，它只是普通的海滨公园。甚至你在春夏秋过来，天气热，游人寥寥可数。</p><p>然而每年到了十月底，约莫是北方晒起大白菜的季节，就有大批北方中老年南下，来到这片应许之城，椰奶与菠萝蜜之地，在这海边载歌载舞，直到隔年雪融才依依不舍地回去。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/072405809394.gif" data-w="395" data-h="224" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/072405809394.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>为了预习老年生活，这次我特别来考察，看以后我能够参加哪个团体。</p><p>白天的椰梦长廊一派祥和，除了海边日光浴和玩水，你能见到老大爷大妈在练习各种乐器，吹三个小节错两个小节那种。姑且称白天的椰梦长廊为新手村。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/072406875752.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="893" data-h="503" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/072406875752.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>如果你稍微进步一些了，也许能够结识些乐器爱好者，抱团取暖吹些有趣的曲子。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/072407004844.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="824" data-h="516" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/072407004844.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>弦乐与管乐合奏也是常见的组合，你撞你的树，我吹我的曲，愿我们都有美好的未来。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/072407946215.gif" data-w="400" data-h="225" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/072407946215.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>再进步一些，也有老年爵士大乐团，想必当年各个都是厂里的文艺骨干。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/142131683215.gif" data-w="350" data-h="197" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/142131683215.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>广场上少不了的当然是跳舞的大爷大妈，你想要练哪种舞都有相应的团体。你们这些整天没法运动的社畜扪心自问，到了这个年纪，有本事跳得那么激情吗？穿着高跟鞋膝盖还敢这么跳吗？</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/072409036441.gif" data-w="399" data-h="225" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/072409036441.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>你嫌节奏太快，动作太猛，也以选择跳些《可可托海的牧羊人》之类的和缓曲子。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/142227999621.gif" data-w="345" data-h="194" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/142227999621.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>这关刀耍得是虎虎生风，刀风生光，似乎要切断迎面而来的海风。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/142259444597.gif" data-w="402" data-h="226" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/142259444597.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>在波涛边翩翩起舞，也是极其开心，各位姑娘们，赶快跟闺蜜一起学舞去吧！可以一起跳到老那种。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/072413934263.gif" data-w="345" data-h="194" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>在这里活动的北方侯鸟老人，大约能分三个时段，早七点到十点半，下午两点到五点，晚六点半到九点半，依饭点而定，但你待得久了，大概可以区分三个时段活动的风格：上午多是唱唱歌做做操，下午跳跳舞，练练猫步。</p><p>傍晚五点半左右，你又可以看到一波波北方口音的人潮，从市区往海月广场走去；到了晚上九点半，又如同海潮退去，一波波地往市区方向走回。</p><p>晚上椰梦长廊那真的就是才艺竞技场，你没在白天练好一门技艺，绝对不敢轻易在晚上开台子。比如晚上经常可看到各种野生歌唱家演出，后面大乐队现场伴奏那种。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/142418963312.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/142418963312.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>歌曲间主持人报幕也非常专业，伴随温柔海风走出场，风姿绰约，用媲美央视主持的播报“感谢XXX老师的深情演唱，接下来，请听女声独唱《真情永远》，独唱：XXX，请您欣赏”。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>几位演唱老师，每天晚上六点半他们陆续到现场，开始串词与安排演唱流程，你很容易认出谁是演唱者，他们穿着跟一般匹夫匹妇就是不同，其实，在全年都是短裤拖鞋的三亚街头这么穿，大家都知道他们是要到海边唱歌的。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/072415367893.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>不要小看后面那些吹拉弹唱的乐队大爷们，他们分进合击，平常各自在这忘情滴玩乐器，接受路人仰慕的神情。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/072425735900.gif" data-w="353" data-h="199" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/072425735900.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>跳西藏舞，疯狂地跳啊跳啊，转啊转啊，好快乐。简直比迪厅还令人上头。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/144610329379.gif" data-w="400" data-h="224" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/144610329379.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>跟一般大广场不太一样的是，这是一条带状人形步道，所以一路走下去，你会陆续见到各种娱乐活动，就如同画卷般在你面前慢慢展开，五颜六色，多彩多姿，目不暇接，好像一整晚转了好几间live，不同的DJ，不同的风格，不同的快乐。</p><p>西藏舞旁是每天都受到许多人瞩目的秧歌队，在三亚海滩边椰子树下跳秧歌，本身就是件魔幻的事。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/144721887270.gif" data-w="399" data-h="225" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/144721887270.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>据说这秧歌队几年前本来只是几个人玩，跳着跳着，结果阵容一年比一年盛大，服化道一年比一年隆重。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/144721572693.gif" data-w="410" data-h="230" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/144721572693.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>现场一片传统喜庆服饰，但你很容易注意到这位踩着高翘，穿着白婚纱的舞者，舞姿相当特异华丽。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/072429303076.gif" data-w="400" data-h="224" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/072429303076.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>后来我才注意到那原来是一位阿叔反串，动作表情都相当娇媚，还经常跟现场观众互动<span class="text-remarks" label="备注">（主要是男观众）</span>。他经常自顾自开心地跳，不在主流队舞里，我问了其他秧歌阿姨，似乎都不太待见他，但也没人赶他走。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/072430208918.gif" data-w="246" data-h="366" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/072430208918.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>他是海月广场真正的drag queen！每天会准备两三套中西式新娘衣物替换，总是跟另一位阿叔来现场，替他保管这些变装道具及衣物，看得出他跳得是真的非常开心，在三亚彻底放飞自我那种开心。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/072432143560.gif" data-w="352" data-h="198" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/072432143560.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">不讲舞德啊！<br></p><p>秧歌旁相当多人围观的是一组残疾人团体，他们每年冬天就跨海南下到三亚，开始为期几个月的卖艺之行。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/072433457348.gif" data-w="400" data-h="225" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/072433457348.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>最欢乐的还是新疆舞，在三亚除了东北移民外，新疆移民也是一大宗，新疆舞可说是一种社交货币，只要展开手足，随时随地都能下去跳一曲，很容易就搭到伴。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/072434239527.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/072434239527.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>不如跳舞，来三亚不如跳舞！！</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/072435430486.gif" data-w="400" data-h="227" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/072435430486.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>新疆舞有好几个场子，跳舞的有汉族人也有少数民族同胞，五彩的霓虹灯照映着椰子树影，温柔海风轻抚着流汗的发稍，那是一派民族大团结的欢乐景象。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/072436464371.gif" data-w="400" data-h="225" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/072436464371.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>你在现场很容易被那种热烈的氛围感染。也许每座城市的广场都有民众载歌载舞，可是你想想，每年时候一到，来自全国各地的人，聚集在这海岛尽头的沙滩，跳着全世界离海最远区域的少数民族舞蹈，不禁会感慨：中国可真是伟大的国家呀！</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/145121560863.gif" data-w="390" data-h="220" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202202/24/145121560863.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>你可能会说，难道我去的是假三亚？<strong>其实我觉得三亚还挺圈层化的，年轻人现在喜欢去后海湾，中产阶级跑海棠湾亚龙湾，退休老人喜欢在三亚湾，不同湾区的人物风情完全不同。</strong></p><p>强烈推荐海月广场每年十月起固定上演的快活之夜，那是你到三亚一定要去看的魔幻风景。</p><p><span
