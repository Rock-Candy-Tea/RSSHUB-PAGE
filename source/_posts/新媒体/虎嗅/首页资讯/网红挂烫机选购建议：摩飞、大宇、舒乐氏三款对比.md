<p><span class="text-remarks" label="备注">本文来自微信公众号：</span><a href="https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s/RY1j0BM0GJcHI42ZM4bISw" target="_blank" rel="nofollow" style="text-decoration: none;"><span class="text-remarks">尤呢呢（ID：nene0217）</span></a><span class="text-remarks">，作者：尤呢呢，原文标题：《日常出差都精致？摩飞、大宇、舒乐氏三款网红挂烫机硬核对比！》，题图来自：尤呢呢</span></p><p>不论是日常上班，还是出差旅行，便携挂烫机都是必不可少的，所以今天我就来和大家对比下三台400元价位段的网红款便携挂烫机，分别是摩飞、大宇和舒乐氏。<br></p><p>接下来，我们就从<strong>颜值、便携性、蒸汽效果、使用效果、安全性、便利度</strong>这六个方面来看。</p><p label="小标题" class="text-sm-title">一、颜值</p><p>俗话说始于颜值，产品的第一感受一般来于颜值。但颜值对比的主观意识太强，所以大家可以把自己认为颜值最高的一款打在评论区，让我们来看看点赞最高的到底是哪一款，毕竟群众的眼睛是雪亮的。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095242093106.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095242093106.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095243276019.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095243276019.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="小标题" class="text-sm-title">二、便携性</p><p><strong>1、体积</strong></p><p>对于便携性挂烫机来说，收纳体积也至关重要，折叠后三款体积看似差不多，基本和一瓶矿泉水差不多大。但是大宇的电线和加水盒无法整体收纳，不但占用空间大，而且下次使用加水盒找不找到都是个问题。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095244218095.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095244218095.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><strong>2、重量</strong></p><p>影响便携的除了体积，重量也十分关键，为了测量准确和公平我都是连电线一起称重的。可以看到摩飞811g，大宇869g，舒乐氏799g，所以重量上来说舒乐氏最轻，而大宇最重。</p><p>因此总的来说便携性上舒乐氏和摩飞更胜一筹，而大宇要略微差一点，不过相比于传统挂烫机来说三款都很便携。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095245279679.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="444" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095245279679.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="小标题" class="text-sm-title">三、蒸汽效果</p><p><strong>1、预热速度</strong></p><p>只要是出蒸汽的挂烫机，在使用前都是需要预热的，所以预热速度也很关键。预热速度方面，摩飞较快仅需10s左右，舒乐氏和大宇都是半分钟左右。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095246595129.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095246595129.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><strong>2、蒸汽大小</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095250088555.gif" data-w="898" data-h="505" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095250088555.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095255043721.gif" data-w="856" data-h="482" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095255043721.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095259641511.gif" data-w="750" data-h="422" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095259641511.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>挂烫机的核心是蒸汽大小，只有蒸汽够大挂烫机熨烫的衣物才会更平整，从动图中可以看到三款挂烫机的蒸汽量基本一致，没有太大的区别。</p><p><strong>3、穿透性</strong></p><p>但是蒸汽不能只看大小，穿透力也需要考究。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095303137003.gif" data-w="770" data-h="433" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095303137003.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>摩飞虽然蒸汽量不小，但是只能影响两根蜡烛吹灭一根蜡烛。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095309110706.gif" data-w="577" data-h="325" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095309110706.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>而大宇虽然也只能吹灭一根蜡烛，但是可以影响3根蜡烛。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095314643267.gif" data-w="601" data-h="338" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095314643267.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>舒乐氏穿透性最强，还没靠近就吹灭一根蜡烛，总计吹灭两根蜡烛，并且影响了全部5根蜡烛。</p><p><strong>4、续航能力</strong></p><p>虽然三款挂烫机的水箱大小不同，但是使用时长都在6-7分钟左右，正常来说熨烫3-4件衣物没问题，持久性都不“凡”啊。</p><p><strong>5、功能性</strong></p><p>熨烫功能方面，三款挂烫机都支持干湿双烫，仅需一键即可切换，个人感觉适用性还是满广泛的，而摩飞还具备修毛球功能，不过这个功能略尴尬的点在于修剪毛球时，蒸汽口是对着人的。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095316545878.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095316545878.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095317490668.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095317490668.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="小标题" class="text-sm-title">四、使用效果</p><p><strong>1、熨烫效果</strong></p><p>使用范围方面不论是T桖、衬衣还西服，单纯熨烫效果来说都不错，但是效率和细节还是有差别的。</p><p><strong>2、喷水现象</strong></p><p>例如同样是熨烫T恤，可以看到舒乐氏和大宇熨烫的很平整，而且没有喷水现象。而摩飞虽然预热快，但是熨烫时偶有喷水的现象。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095318523969.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="1689" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095318523969.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><strong>3、熨烫效率</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095319038249.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095319038249.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>熨烫效率方面主要就是看熨烫面板的大小，相对来说舒乐氏和大宇的熨烫面板更大，所以熨烫效率也就较高，而摩飞的熨烫面板就比较平“凡”了。</p><p><strong>4、细节处理</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095320012759.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095320012759.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>针对细节处理上舒乐氏和大宇采用的尖角设计，而摩飞采用的凹槽烫头。个人觉得面对扣子等部位，还是尖角设计使用起来更便利。</p><p label="小标题" class="text-sm-title">五、安全性</p><p><strong>1、防烫底板</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095321686498.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095321686498.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>作为挂烫机来说，安全性也很重要，目前三款都具有防烫底板设计。在预热或者临时放置时，可以把挂烫机放到防烫底板上，防止烫伤或者损坏家具。</p><p><strong>2、按键出蒸汽</strong></p><p>老式的烫斗在预热完毕后会自动出蒸汽，这样就存在一定的安全隐患，而这三款挂烫机都是在使用时按键才会开始出蒸汽。</p><p><strong>3、自动断电</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095330994577.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095330994577.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>同时三款挂烫机也都具备自动断电功能，当你超过十分钟不使用时机器会自动断电防止干烧，个人觉得安全性上三款都没啥问题。</p><p label="小标题" class="text-sm-title">六、使用便利度</p><p><strong>1、手持便利度</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095332510102.gif" data-w="488" data-h="275" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095332510102.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>便利度方面首先来看手持便利度，舒乐氏和大宇手柄都是可以180°旋转的，挂烫平烫随意可以切换。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095334366155.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095334366155.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>但是大宇的手柄，没旋开的时候，中间无空隙没办法握紧。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095337465187.gif" data-w="748" data-h="421" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095337465187.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>而摩飞手柄属于折叠式的，更适合挂烫，平烫持握感略差。</p><p><strong>2、加水方式</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095340403586.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095340403586.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>加水方式上大宇和舒乐氏都是加水盒顶部加水，而摩飞是取下水箱加水。因为挂烫机需要添加纯净水，所以如果是净水器加水摩飞进水口大小正合适，但如果是倒水进去还是大宇和舒乐氏方便。</p><p><strong>3、可视便利度</strong></p><p>水箱的可视便利度方面三款差不多，都有可视窗可以进行当前水量多少的观察。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095341734981.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095341734981.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="小标题" class="text-sm-title">总结</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095342447748.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="549" data-h="325" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/09/095342447748.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>最后我把这总计18项的比较结果给大家列了一个表格，大家可以根据自己的需求选择最适合自己的一款。</p><p>如果大家有任何疑问欢迎评论区讨论，也欢迎点赞关注，并聊聊自己想了解的产品，下次对比的说不定就是你感兴趣的。</p><p><span class="text-remarks"
