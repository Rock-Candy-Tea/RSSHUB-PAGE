class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/09/120520683681.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="1333" class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/09/120521983974.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="1333" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/09/120521983974.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><strong>原来坯啦就是苤蓝。</strong>《中国植物志》上说，苤蓝的根须可以长到很长。于是我们找了一个稍大的透明玻璃瓶，既能给它足够的生长空间，也方便观察根须的成长。种上之后，苤蓝果然一天一个样，半透明的根，郁郁葱葱的叶子，看起来储存了很多能量。</p><p>那段时间，我还参加了盖娅自然学校举办的线上食育活动。其中一个小任务是发现不同萝卜的吃法，我们陆续解锁了白萝卜、青萝卜、卞萝卜、心里美、樱桃萝卜等各个品种，了解了萝卜的各种吃法，带领活动的蚊滋滋还在视频里示范了泡萝卜花。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/09/120522270010.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="609" data-h="810" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/09/120522270010.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/09/120523482017.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="1332" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/09/120523482017.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">卞萝卜做菜煮水后，剩下的萝卜头也可以利用起来。图片：盖娅自然学校的蚊滋滋<br></p><p>这也启发了我们，<strong>与其把萝卜头扔进堆肥桶，为什么不给它们一个开花的机会呢？</strong>于是我和橙子把切下的萝卜头都泡进了水里。</p><p>自此，我们正式开启了<strong>“吃什么种什么”</strong>的模式，任何菜头都不放过。白菜、胡萝卜、娃娃菜、卷心菜、紫甘蓝、芥菜，种满了半张桌子，在冬日里欣欣然地生长着，形成了一片厨余小森林。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/09/120524154016.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="1333" class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/09/120525459487.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="1333" class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/09/120526049661.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="1333" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/09/120526049661.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/09/120527045440.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="1333" class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/09/120528342249.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/09/120528342249.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">桌上的厨余小森林。<br></p><h3 label="大标题" class="text-big-title">一、菜头的生命力<br></h3><p>我们的小森林每天都有新的变化。胡萝卜头发芽后，长出了羽毛一样的叶子，茎有的毛茸茸，有的光溜溜。白菜头长得很快，开花后是特别好闻的油菜花味。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/09/120530329595.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="1333" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/09/120530329595.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/09/120531513392.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="1333" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/09/120531513392.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">胡萝卜叶和白菜花。<br></p><p>萝卜头们刚结花苞时我就非常期盼，开花后更是意想不到的惊喜：青萝卜花是紫色条纹的娇小花朵，心里美开出了带着婴儿粉的白色大花。不同品种的萝卜叶子手感、形状、颜色也不相同。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/09/120532995563.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="731" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/09/120532995563.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/09/120532339479.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="751" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/09/120532339479.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">萝卜花。别问，问就是：自己种了才知道哪花归哪妈！<br></p><p>最在我心尖尖上的还是苤蓝。一个多月，苤蓝长叶子、长根再到开第一朵花，观察它的变化给我带来了很多快乐。<strong>只是把菜头泡进水里面，不用怎么照顾就能开出这么美的花，我们平时真是太无视食物中巨大的生命能量了。</strong></p><p><video src="https://ali-video.huxiucdn.com/mda-nd9q3463bi1g9vqj/mda-nd9q3463bi1g9vqj.mp4" poster="https://v2-img.huxiucdn.com/mda-nd9q3463bi1g9vqj/mda-nd9q3463bi1g9vqj.jpg" controls="controls" width="100%">您目前设备暂不支持播放</video><br></p><p>其实，菜头开花的原理很简单。蔬菜根茎储存了很多营养和水分，根茎顶端<span class="text-remarks" label="备注">（根茎类蔬菜的菜头，叶菜类的菜根）</span>还集中着分生能力很强的组织。只要有水、空气和阳光，切下的菜头里的营养足以让植物继续生长，分生出叶和花来。</p><p>找出家里闲置的塑料盒或玻璃瓶，把菜头放进去，盛上点水浸没根须，摆在室内阳光充足的地方。北京的冬春季比较干燥，容器底1厘米高的存水，差不多第二天早上就会被“喝光”。</p><p>如果你放的水比较多，要记得每天换水。如果是洋葱、蒜、苤蓝这种根须长的蔬菜，可以找高一点的罐头瓶，把菜根坐上去，就像养风信子一样。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/09/120533275560.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/09/120533275560.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/09/120534603716.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="1333" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/09/120534603716.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>没有合适高度的容器也没关系，因为植物的适应性很强，它们会抓住一切机会拼命成长。这些菜头从长芽、出叶再到开花结果，每个瞬间都让人惊叹。</p><h3 label="大标题" class="text-big-title">二、赏花之外的收获</h3><p>泡了几周后，厨余森林里很多十字花科蔬菜都开花了。我忙不迭地假装小蜜蜂给花儿们授粉，拿毛笔把雄蕊上的花粉轻扫到雌蕊柱头上。没有授粉成功雌蕊的会萎缩掉落，而授粉成功的雌蕊，子房会逐渐膨大，结出肥硕的果实。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/09/120535737471.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="1333" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/09/120535737471.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/09/120536194196.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="1333" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/09/120536194196.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">给芥菜花授粉。<br></p><p>像很多十字花科蔬菜一样，白菜的果实是豆荚形的长角果。我剥开几颗果子，在十几粒暗红色的种子里挑出了三个最饱满的。<strong>至此，我也见证了一颗白菜完整的生命历程。</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/09/120537486926.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="921" data-h="615" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/09/120537486926.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/09/120538677149.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="668" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/09/120538677149.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><strong>水培菜头不光能开花，胡萝卜头长出的缨可以食用，用来给闽南咸饭提味再好不过了。</strong>水培的葱和蒜苗也可以继续吃，能割好多茬。我第一次知道，泡水的蒜头还会生出一颗圆鼓鼓的新蒜。我充满感恩地吃掉了它们，感谢它们成为我身体的一部分。</p><p><strong>除了美和生命力，厨余小森林还给了我们画画的灵感，我和橙子以它们为主角画了不少小画。</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/09/120538279167.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="693" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/09/120538279167.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/09/120539700474.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/09/120539700474.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>3月，我为新艺动大病儿童艺术陪伴项目做志愿者，就以“厨余小森林”为主题设计了一个月的自然观察活动，希望不方便出门的小朋友在家里也能感受春天，感受自然。参与了活动的大朋友说：“十几天前差点被我遗弃的菜头，现在看来太美了。每天早晚看看它们，心情真好！”</p><p><strong>除了水培菜头，还可以用豆类和谷物来发芽苗菜。</strong>参与的家长说，小朋友看到豆芽这么快就长出来，激动坏了。还有朋友说：“这个真的刷新我的认知，我以为米不可能是活的。”大家都被这股旺盛的生命力感染了。</p><p>现在，就像我对小朋友们说的一样，去到厨房里吧。把能发芽的种子和菜头都泡进水里，足不出户也能拥有春天！</p><p
