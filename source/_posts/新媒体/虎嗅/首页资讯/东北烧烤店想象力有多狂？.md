class="text-big-title">沈阳</h3><p>串之城，浴之都。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141404777451.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141404777451.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>你们都知道上个月我上热搜的事，谁知道那是我悲怆的天问。我心里苦啊！本来那几天就该在沈阳，这么一耽搁就是一个月。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141405580894.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="613" data-h="153" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141405580894.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>抢在真正入夏前，我终于又来了沈阳，我当了一周的干饭人，天天流连在各种串吧，沉溺在高蛋白的快乐当中。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141406955640.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141406955640.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>六月沈阳天气非常好，云卷云舒。下午忽然来了一场大雨，把空气洗得舒畅，所以晚上挺凉快。正值欧洲杯期间，有电视的串店基本上都在放球赛，许多烧烤店都把电视屏幕摆在室外，<strong>那个氛围有些像足球俱乐部，和三五好友相约吃串、看球、喝酒、瞎起哄，简直是种人生享受。</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141407313457.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="674" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141407313457.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>这次我去了几间串吧，基本都在铁西区。据说铁西区的球迷与曼彻斯特、利物浦的球迷是一类人，都是“working class hero”。但这些串吧平常播放的倒不是足球，而是CBA，篮球是老工业重镇球迷的慰藉。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141408170574.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141408170574.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>据《2020东北烧烤产业数据报告》显示，东三省烧烤店门店总数约有八万多间，占全国烧烤店总数16%。具体到城市来说，长春约有8千多家，其次是哈尔滨、沈阳及大连，都是五千多间。<strong>可能这些城市的沙县小吃和拉面店加起来，都没有烧烤店多。</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141410709843.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="599" data-h="400" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141410709843.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">串圈大佬高峰会</p><p>“东北烧烤”至少还可细分为锦州烧烤、丹东烧烤、延吉烧烤、长春烧烤、鸡西烧烤、齐齐哈尔烧烤......所用食材及烧烤方式略有不同。北境烧烤内战，谁也不服谁。</p><p>能把烧烤做得精细是东北人骄傲，去年上海大地方公众号“张江名媛”到沈阳，有感而发写了下面这段话，很多沈阳人就不乐意了：“喔？奥尼尔就算来打CBA也能算中上等了？”</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141411492284.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="600" data-h="558" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141411492284.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>沈阳作为东北最大城，自然汇聚了东三省各种流派的烧烤方式。沈阳的烧烤可能比不上长春的多样化，也比不上锦州的标准化，<strong>但在沈阳，串吧本身就是一种比串更神奇的存在。</strong></p><p>我认为沈阳这座城，总能将东北本土各种娱乐餐饮文化融合在一起，然后做到极致。所以当我看到沈阳现在出现各种莫兰迪元素、水磨石装修的ins风串吧时，竟然一点都不意外。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141412622686.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="1047" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141412622686.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">让你重新insins沈阳烧烤(图：大众点评)</p><p>当然很多人会说，最好吃的烧烤，就是家楼下那间烟燻火燎的烧烤店。街边三五好友，在烧烤这种最原始的烹饪法烟火中，模糊了面貌，卸下了心防，氛围融洽了起来，那是东北人迎接夏天来临的快乐仪式。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141414908104.gif?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/gif/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="266" data-h="401" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141414908104.gif?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/gif/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>东北地区烧烤为何如此火爆，很多人可能都会想到90年代末，用工厂废弃的金属器材及焦碳支棱起烧烤摊，那是一种谋生手段，也是老工业城市遍地烧烤狼烟的往事。</p><p>总之，烧烤摊当年不知撑起了多少家庭，如今却在东北遍地开花，还玩出了各种花样。但其他地方的串吧都只是纯粹串吧，沈阳串吧在拼美味之余，拼的已经是格局，比的是艺术。</p><p><strong>山海关外一只串，铁子馋得团团转。用我的手艺挑战你的生意，用我的生意直面你的创意。但凡沈阳的串吧走上品牌化，老板就开始思考宇宙观，如何创造一个烧烤宇宙。</strong></p><p>比如沈阳知名的野人串吧，它就企图营造在原始蛮荒环境中吃烧烤的氛围。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141416939986.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="674" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141416939986.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>里面是钟乳石洞，有各种莫名其妙的图腾、恐龙化石、香艳的灯光，还有一只不排除是风水讲究挂上去的中华巨龙。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141417470350.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="660" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141417470350.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>有一幅浮雕画，纪念人类第一次吃到熟食，任何行业都供奉着其守护神及开基先祖，烧烤店自然供奉野人，饮水思源。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141418693892.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141418693892.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">烧烤老祖</p><p>吃到一半，所有的服务员及厨师还会突然出现在你身旁，强行互动，开始跳起不知所云的舞。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141420523209.gif?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/gif/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="393" data-h="215" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141420523209.gif?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/gif/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>当然有些人知道，这种烧烤店跳舞的创始者，是哈尔滨的金钢山烧烤，号称东北最大烧烤店，老网红了。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141422544995.gif?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/gif/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="239" data-h="375" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141422544995.gif?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/gif/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">(快手用戶：金钢山烧烤楠哥)</p><p>泥炉烤肉这种原始烤肉方式可算是沈阳人的本命，许多泥炉烤肉店就企图造一种老沈阳的氛围。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141424768294.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="550" data-h="824" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141424768294.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">胡同泥炉烤肉(图：大众点评)</p><p>江东街的车友餐吧，这是一间开在4S店楼上的串吧，装修都是汽车主题，可以一边吃串一边等着爱车做保养。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141425505009.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="550" data-h="825" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141425505009.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">车友餐吧(图：大众点评)</p><p>在红梅文创区的一间列车厢烧烤店，客人坐在车厢里吃烧烤，我看了下车牌，是一列当年哈尔滨开往黑河的列车厢。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141427513601.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="600" data-h="904" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141427513601.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">(图：大众点评)</p><p>这里服务员穿着不怎么整齐的列车员制服，也不扎裤里，生无可恋地坐在车厢一角，这真是一场成功的cosplay，不仅坐进老车厢，让你身心灵感受当年列车员的服务态度。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141428364033.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141428364033.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">(图：瑞杉)</p><p>但你如果真的想要有坐在车厢里动态的感觉，沈阳还真的有这样的烧烤店，烧的是感觉，烤得是情调，不出沈阳，也能感受吃与远方。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141429721091.gif?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/gif/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="255" data-h="439" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141429721091.gif?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/gif/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">快手：大绿皮子火车烤肉</p><p>铁西区的第一大排档，以前的高压开关厂，他有个空间，用投影营造了一个在海底世界吃串的环境。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141431423407.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="550" data-h="1021" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141431423407.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>南方新媒体人来到这，看见大中午的就一堆年轻人扎堆吃烧烤，震惊之余高呼东北经济果然不行了。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141434306137.gif?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/gif/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="393" data-h="215" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141434306137.gif?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/gif/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>大一点的串吧里附设各种强行ins风的打卡点，这已经是种标配，免费游戏机台也成了许多店家留客的手段。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141435056670.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="674" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141435056670.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>总之，你在沈阳会看到许多与你刻板印象里“串吧”装修风格相悖的新形态串吧。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141436699403.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="600" data-h="1029" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141436699403.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">图：大众点评</p><p>边烧烤边唱KTV也毫无困难，这就是俗称的“烤TV”，你高兴的话可以拿起串当挥舞的荧光棒。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141439326653.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="656" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202107/01/141439326653.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">图：大众点评</p><p>烧烤干啤酒，一口接一口，音乐酒吧全国都有。<strong>但是在黑土地，是酒吧先卖串，还是串吧先卖酒，这是个终极问题。</strong></p><p>反正音乐串吧很自然而然地在东北首先出现了，就是下面撸着串，台上歌手驻唱，正常都会唱些民谣，不正常地会唱抖音神曲，有时会唱90年代港台流行乐，但大多时候没人理他唱些啥。</p><p
