class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/15/134602172563.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/15/134603782803.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/15/134603782803.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>其实，国家安全涵盖的内容很广。</p><p>在2015年7月1日，第十二届全国人民代表大会常务委员会第十五次会议上，通过了《中华人民共和国国家安全法》。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/15/134604203850.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/15/134604203850.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>根据《国家安全法》，国家安全是这个意思：</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/15/134605803230.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="997" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/15/134605803230.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>我们可以这么理解，<strong>凡是有可能影响国家政治社会稳定和有序发展的不利因素，都会纳入国家安全的范畴。<br></strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/15/134606752163.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/15/134606752163.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>《国家安全法》就从多个领域对国家安全任务进行了明确。</p><p>主要包括以下几个方面：</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/15/134606441215.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="325" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/15/134606441215.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/15/134607628792.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="1366" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/15/134607628792.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/15/134608170567.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="1319" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/15/134608170567.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/15/134609892384.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="1319" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/15/134609892384.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/15/134610898516.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="1038" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/15/134610898516.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/15/134610530244.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/15/134610530244.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>是的，国家安全无小事，这是一种新型的总体国家安全观。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/15/134611812838.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/15/134611812838.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>在我们日常生活中，有些危害国家安全的活动需要我们提高警惕。</p><p>比如：</p><p label="小标题" class="text-sm-title">一</p><p>遇到一些可疑人员，未经批准就到内部做调查，擅自进行科技、经济、企业等情况的搜集。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/15/134612268487.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/15/134612268487.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>当我们发现这种情况，就不能随意提供，并向当地公安机关或国家安全机关报告。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/15/134622087546.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/15/134622087546.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><h3 label="大标题" class="text-big-title">二</h3><p>一些境外组织和人员会到我国军事、保密单位周边晃悠，趁机盗取秘密情报和信息。</p><p>如果遇到这类可疑人员要立即报告。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/15/134623262677.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/15/134623262677.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="小标题" class="text-sm-title">三</p><p>如果发现有人买卖属于国家秘密的文件、资料和其他物品时，应当及时报告保密工作部门或者国家安全机关、公安机关处理。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/15/134624865368.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="500" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/15/134624865368.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>类似可能泄露国家秘密的线索，我们也应该及时向国家安全机关举报。</p><p label="小标题" class="text-sm-title">四</p><p>我们要警惕境外电视、电台、网络等传媒的煽动、造谣。一些有境外背景的组织和个人，往往会利用一些群众不满情绪，煽动与政府对抗。遇到这些情况，我们应该立即报告。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/15/134625897073.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="555" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/15/134625897073.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>破坏国家安全的，可能是来自外部的破坏，也可能是内部的原因；可能是天灾，也可能是人祸。维护国家安全，是我们每一个公民的责任。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/15/134625764900.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="449" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202204/15/134625764900.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85"
