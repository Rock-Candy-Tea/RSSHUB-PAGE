_src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142006077964.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="340" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>这座城市跟他的名字一样，有点小透明，大部分广东人听到他都会疑惑地摇摇头，云浮在哪？地级市都如此低调，更不用提其辖下的县——郁南县。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142008528863.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="462" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142008528863.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">郁南县位于云浮城区的西边，2018年时属于广东省山区贫困县。</p><p>下辖15个乡镇，总人口48万，我对小县城一直很感兴趣，这些地方除了家乡人，很少有人会过去。写了一年多走访文章，心里有种使命感驱使我去发掘这些不为人知的小城，加上郁南最近几年开通了火车，于是我来了。</p><p>相比山区贫困县的身份，郁南的火车站显得挺气派。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142009184247.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142009184247.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>火车站正对一个大广场，大广场的广告牌颇具煽动性。</p><blockquote><p>“衣锦还乡，归家有恒大。”</p><p>“衣锦荣归，安家甘露。”</p></blockquote><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142011660198.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="744" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142011660198.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142012959020.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="742" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142012959020.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>是号召那些归乡的人在当地买房，看招牌也能知道这座县城的房价，新房3000多元，湖景房4000多元。</p><p>郁南站是2014年底开通的，一般新开高铁房地产业都能拉一波，在离高铁站稍近的县城边缘也能看到一栋栋高楼在开工。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142013657618.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142013657618.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>跟三十几层的高楼形成鲜明对比的，是镇中心的矮房。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142015761369.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142015761369.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>都城镇是郁南县的县中心，也是县政府所在地，逛了几条街，这样的房子占大多数，五六层楼高，也不旧，估计就十几年的房龄。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142016110891.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142016110891.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>要说县里人的生活跟城里有什么不同，大概是这里没有万达那种大商场，从火车站坐大巴过来镇中心有个站叫时代广场，很多人下车，听名字我以为是那种大商超，结果就是一小超市。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142018864977.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142018864977.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/152544588803.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/152544588803.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>一楼是超市，二楼上不去，告示说二楼在转租，还没找到愿意进驻的商家。我走遍整个镇也没发现其他更繁华的商场。</p><p>这里没有肯德基麦当劳，吃炸鸡汉堡是去华莱士和美优乐，大城市那些fancy餐厅这里一间没有，什么必胜客、海底捞、九毛九这里通通不见，还好我对这些一点都不钟意，最喜欢的还是苍蝇馆子。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142021675860.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142021675860.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>这间潮汕猪杂汤粉的口味深得我心，可惜12元端上来份量有点少。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142022632733.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142022632733.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>没吃饱，2小时后又吃了一碗8元的，这个价格在广州城中村完全能拿到更大的份量。<strong>逛过这么多县城，我深深觉得县城的物价没有比大城市低，大城市的工资却是小县城几倍。</strong></p><p>以店员为例，这边步行街上的店员是2000元提成，换作广州一般是4000元提成。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142023054112.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142023054112.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>步行街给我的印象很深，时代广场往下走一点右拐就是。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142025749277.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142025749277.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>路边的招贴画惊艳到我。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142026364987.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142026364987.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>优雅的旗袍，复古的字体，再现了民国时代的网红产品。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142027366818.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142027366818.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142029700823.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142029700823.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>没想到在县城能见到这么讲究的步行街，每走一段我都会驻足观看，加上那些复古的招牌和老旧褪色的建筑，真有种置身民国的感觉。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142030550346.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142030550346.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>街上客流稀少，我迎着风，像哥伦布发现新大陆一样地狂笑，一遍遍跟自己说 “这里没人来过，就你来过！”</p><p>从招贴画我了解到都城镇又称 “小广州”，因为抗日战争时期，广州和沿海一带成为沦陷区，部分人逃难至此，在这安家并做起贸易。</p><p>读着历史，眼前浮现出当年车水马龙、商贾云集的样子，突然一间蜜雪冰城把我拉回现实，蜜雪果然是下沉之王，都开到这了，其他连锁奶茶店却一间不见。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142034167788.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142034167788.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>大城市步行街是刻意去复古，建筑都新盖的，这边没有，人们保留着原来的生活状态。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142036589968.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142036589968.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>惊讶的是这边餐馆居然是烧柴火的。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142037690780.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142037690780.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>大大的高炉，火焰旺盛，厨房一半面积堆着柴，隔壁就是柴房。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142040941581.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142040941581.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>理发店里搁着两张麻将桌。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142041808618.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>老宅门前挂着两黑板，黑板上用龙飞凤舞的字体写着古诗：</p><p>“宝剑锋从磨砺出，梅花香自苦寒来。”</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142042354938.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142043303434.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>再隔壁一点就是河堤了，河堤旁一排的肉摊。</p><p _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142046667897.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142048834957.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142049880780.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202108/25/142050522826.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750"
