<p>220218投资项目之夜</p> 
<p>生命力强，未来潜力高的企业是怎样的? </p> 
<p>投资总是押注在行业内高成长性、最有未来潜力的公司身上。而这些价值可以从具象的、已经被投资者关注的项目上有所体现。在本月18号，飞仕伯乐以跨境连线的形式举办了 <strong>一场投资项目的路演之夜</strong> 。在此次<a class="project-link" data-id="1679768002761480" data-name="路演中" data-logo="https://img.36krcdn.com/20220401/v2_c4315cf7425743b38690131ff039221b_img_000" data-refer-type="1" href="https://36kr.com/project/1679768002761480" target="_blank">路演中</a>，我们从 <strong>重要技术、商业领域中挖掘出5个未来可期的项目</strong> 。他们项目的未来价值是什么？又是如何斩获投资人芳心的？在这篇文章中，我们会为您进一步呈现。 </p> 
<p>Startups with future potential in the industry will receive investment. We can reflect this in the projects incubated by investors. On the 18th of this month, Fishburners hosted <strong>an Investor Ready Pitch Night</strong> for investment projects in the form of a cross-border link-up. In this roadshow, <strong>we identified five promising projects from key technology and business sectors</strong>. What is the future value of their projects? And how did they win the hearts of investors? In this article, we will tell you more.</p> 
<p class="image-wrapper"><img data-img-size-val="900,160" src="https://img.36krcdn.com/20220601/v2_5cfebfe8cfad476fa710c3905ff2876e_img_000" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p> 
<p class="img-desc">Trace | 致力于实现碳中和的数字平台</p> 
<p><strong>Trace 是一个致力于实现碳中和的数字平台</strong> ，它可以帮助有环保意识的个人或企业测量、减少甚至抵消他们自身产生的碳足迹，并通过简单而有吸引力的数字工具跟踪和监测他们， <strong>实现二氧化碳的“零排放”</strong> 。Tr<a class="project-link" data-id="1744024544877447" data-name="ace" data-logo="https://img.36krcdn.com/20220517/v2_7f3368f3935c45cfae94727c1a418ebd_img_000" data-refer-type="1" href="https://36kr.com/project/1744024544877447" target="_blank">ace</a>的使命是，通过数字化工具帮助人们采取方便、有效和节能减排的行动，最终实现碳中和的共同使命。 </p> 
<p><strong>Trace is a digital platform that helps climate conscious individuals and businesses measure</strong> , reduce and offset their carbon footprint and track their <a class="project-link" data-id="1743399881379972" data-name="impact" data-logo="https://img.36krcdn.com/20220516/v2_22943fd6ec6f4778a488ba10d258e39a_img_png" data-refer-type="1" href="https://36kr.com/project/1743399881379972" target="_blank">impact</a> over time through simple and engaging digital tools. Trace is on a mission to remove the barriers to becoming carbon neutral by making it accessible, effective and rewarding to take action. </p> 
<p class="image-wrapper"><img data-img-size-val="1080,608" src="https://img.36krcdn.com/20220601/v2_87aef6fd58af4ccca2d5ed7795ced2cf_img_000" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p> 
<p><strong>闪光点：</strong> Trace 会将目前通过会员制筹集的资金用于植树和致力于实现碳中和的项目上，会员也会在trace上看到自己钱的去向和项目的进展情况。 </p> 
<p><strong>Advantages:</strong> Trace pool together the funds from members to plant trees and invest in projects that reduce or remove carbon emissions from the atmosphere. Club will receive regular updates from us so you can see where your money is going and the impact it’s having. </p> 
<p class="image-wrapper"><img data-img-size-val="900,160" src="https://img.36krcdn.com/20220601/v2_857f9f3b686f4844968813743cff158b_img_000" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p> 
<p class="img-desc">Freeguides | 用户友好型的旅行移动平台</p> 
<p><strong>FreeGuides 是一个点对点的用户友好型移动平台</strong> ， <strong>用户可以利用 FreeGuides 创建自己的旅游线路，使用户能够经营和管理只属于自己的旅行体验</strong> 。FreeGuides 能够帮助用户充分利用未开发的资源，并引导他们形成对自己城市的独特知识。该应用程序涵盖了客户旅程的完整经历，包括旅游体验、打卡体验，用户需要支付体验的费用。 </p> 
<p>FreeGuides is a <strong>peer to peer platform, enabling locals to create, run and manage their own experiences</strong> . We empower locals to capitalize on an underutilized resource and their unique knowledge of their city. The app manages the entire customer journey, finding an experience, running an experience, and completing/tipping for an experience. </p> 
<p class="image-wrapper"><img data-img-size-val="1080,608" src="https://img.36krcdn.com/20220601/v2_360e273ec31f4babaacfc20c860db909_img_000" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p> 
<p><strong>闪光点：</strong> FreeGuides 的支付系统是完全无现金的，FreeGuides 允许每一位用户创造他们自己想象的任何体验，无论是摄影、宗教、建筑还是当地文化。 </p> 
<p><strong>Advantages: </strong>The payment system is exclusively cashless . FreeGuides allow guides to create any experience they could imagine whether that’s photography, religious, architectural or cultural. </p> 
<p class="image-wrapper"><img data-img-size-val="900,160" src="https://img.36krcdn.com/20220601/v2_6a456a146cdf47f68185705252f8dade_img_000" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p> 
<p class="img-desc">Joi | 用于活动策划和日程安排的应用软件</p> 
<p><strong>Joi 是一个专门为活动策划、日程安排所设计的云应用平台</strong> 。这个智能工作空间可以存放所有与用户活动有关的信息，并帮助用户 <strong>更好地协作与安排日程</strong> 。Joi 可以通过高准确性和高效率来管理活动策划的每一个元素，帮助用户掌握每个活动的细节之处。 </p> 
<p><strong>Joi is a cloud-based productivity platform designed specifically for planning and delivering events.</strong> A place to house all the information surrounding your event. A place to schedule, <strong>a place to brief and a place to collaborate</strong> . Manage every element of event planning with greater accuracy and efficiency, and have confidence that you have everything covered. </p> 
<p class="image-wrapper"><img data-img-size-val="1080,608" src="https://img.36krcdn.com/20220601/v2_6320add4220f4f20b41e8b9083a0e79c_img_000" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p> 
<p><strong>闪光点：</strong> Joi 可以增强用户的活动策划能力，让协作变得更加简单。 </p> 
<p><strong>Advantages: </strong>Supercharge your event planning capabilities, and collaboration has never been so easy. </p> 
<p class="image-wrapper"><img data-img-size-val="900,160" src="https://img.36krcdn.com/20220601/v2_9260cc4f62954a85b5efbb97dceb4144_img_000" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p> 
<p class="img-desc">UnicornShift | 随需应变的交通管制供应商</p> 
<p>难以想象，在7800家重型建筑公司中就有超过25万名交通管制工人，仅在澳大利亚地区就有超过13亿美元的成本。 <strong>UnicornShift 只需用户轻轻一点，便可呼叫附近的交通管制员一起为您的工作地点配备管理人员</strong> 。通过端到端的管理，UnicornShift 将交通控制员与工地连接起来。 </p> 
<p>There are over 7800 heavy construction companies, over 250000 traffic troll workers, and a sample of over $1.3 billion in Australia alone. <strong>With a single tap, crew your worksites with nearby Traffic Controllers.</strong> Eliminate delays with end-to-end performance management, connecting Traffic Controllers with worksites. </p> 
<p class="image-wrapper"><img data-img-size-val="1080,608" src="https://img.36krcdn.com/20220601/v2_a8360db8c3bf4979a06e91499cc346ad_img_000" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p> 
<p><strong>闪光点：</strong> 交通控制员拥有来自 Downer、Lendlease、Ventia 和其他许多公司的入职资格。 </p> 
<p><strong>Advantages:</strong> TCs hold a range of inductions from Downer, Lendlease, Ventia and many others. </p> 
<p class="image-wrapper"><img data-img-size-val="900,160" src="https://img.36krcdn.com/20220601/v2_28f96677a4e24303af195b57cf704c9b_img_000" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p> 
<p class="img-desc">Data Fetch | 协助用户搜索的数据平台</p> 
<p><strong>Data Fetch是一个 slack 应用程序</strong> ，不久将成为微软团队的应用程序，它允许你 <strong>从任何数据库和组织中的任何应用程序中提取数据</strong> 。寻找数据或许会占据你大量的时间，而实际上你可以在Data Fetch 直接得到你想要的数据。 </p> 
<p><strong>Data Fetch is a platform called Data Fetch, </strong>and it is a slack app and soon to be a Microsoft teams app, that allows you to <strong>pull data from any database and any application</strong> that you have in your organization. </p> 
<p class="image-wrapper"><img data-img-size-val="1080,608" src="https://img.36krcdn.com/20220601/v2_c19227399e1c4185b37bbf109a5c6011_img_000" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p> 
<p><strong>闪光点：</strong> 让搜索数据变得更加简单。 </p> 
<p><strong>Advantages: </strong>Making it easier to search for data. </p>
