 <p style="text-align:justify;vertical-align: inherit;margin-right: 16px;margin-left: 16px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: rgb(51, 51, 51);letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-size-adjust: auto;line-height: 1.75em;font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 11pt;caret-color: red;color: #494949;font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei", sans-serif;">在2021年11月举行的广州车展上，零跑汽车董事长朱江明对零跑汽车多年来推行的“全域自研”进行了详解。他说：“我们通过全域自研，来取得自己的核心竞争优势。”他表示，驱动总成、电池领域也已从外采电芯，发展到自己做模组、Pack和BMS（电池管理系统）。此外，</span><strong><span style="font-size: 11pt;letter-spacing: 0.544px;color: #494949;font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;">零跑汽车在车机系统、云平台、智能驾驶等都实现了自研</span></strong><span style="font-size: 11pt;caret-color: red;color: #494949;font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei", sans-serif;">。</span></p> 
 <p style="text-align:justify;vertical-align: inherit;margin-right: 16px;margin-left: 16px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: rgb(51, 51, 51);letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-size-adjust: auto;line-height: 1.75em;font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 11pt;caret-color: red;color: #494949;font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei", sans-serif;">此外，为了摆脱芯片“卡脖子”的困境，零跑与大华联手打造智能驾驶芯片“凌芯01”，并已搭载在零跑C11上。倘若凌芯01实现量产，有望成为中国首款自主研发的智能驾驶芯片。</span></p> 
 <p style="text-align:justify;vertical-align: inherit;margin-right: 16px;margin-left: 16px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: rgb(51, 51, 51);letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-size-adjust: auto;line-height: 1.75em;font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 11pt;caret-color: red;color: #494949;font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei", sans-serif;">对零跑来说，</span><strong><span style="font-size: 11pt;letter-spacing: 0.544px;color: #494949;font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;">“全域自研”的最大好处是未来规模效应带来的低成本</span></strong><span style="font-size: 11pt;caret-color: red;color: #494949;font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei", sans-serif;">。朱江明表示，尽管前期全域自研需要大量的成本投入，但是通过销量规模可以分担每辆车的边际成本，包括软件研发成本和硬件采购成本。</span></p> 
 <p style="text-align:justify;vertical-align: inherit;margin-right: 16px;margin-left: 16px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: rgb(51, 51, 51);letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-size-adjust: auto;line-height: 1.75em;font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 11pt;caret-color: red;color: #494949;font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei", sans-serif;">但另一面，</span><strong><span style="font-size: 11pt;letter-spacing: 0.544px;color: #494949;font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;">“全域自研”也给零跑带来了其它问题。首先，研发投入带给财务的压力是巨大的</span></strong><span style="font-size: 11pt;caret-color: red;color: #494949;font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei", sans-serif;">。虽然零跑没有公布每年研发投入的准确数字。但是，参照蔚小理三家，动辄一家企业在研发上每年投入就是10亿元计，零跑背后的大华股份2021年前三季度的净利润才只有24亿。</span></p> 
 <p style="text-align:justify;vertical-align: inherit;margin-right: 16px;margin-left: 16px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: rgb(51, 51, 51);letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-size-adjust: auto;line-height: 1.75em;font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 11pt;caret-color: red;color: #494949;font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei", sans-serif;">“全域自研”的庞大投入，</span><strong><span style="font-size: 11pt;letter-spacing: 0.544px;color: #494949;font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;">一方面可能让零跑的现金流变得紧张，一些营销或渠道拓展支出不得不为技术让路；另一方面，技术自研是一个较外采或联合开发更为漫长的过程</span></strong><span style="font-size: 11pt;caret-color: red;color: #494949;font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei", sans-serif;">。比如，重点打造的C11其宣传的L2辅助驾驶功能，事实上已经滞后了蔚来、小鹏近2年的时间。</span></p> 
 <p style="text-align:justify;vertical-align: inherit;margin-right: 16px;margin-left: 16px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: rgb(51, 51, 51);letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-size-adjust: auto;line-height: 1.75em;font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong style="letter-spacing: 0.544px;color: rgb(255, 192, 0);font-size: 11pt;caret-color: red;font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei", sans-serif;"><img class="rich_pages wxw-img" data-ratio="0.6554054054054054" data-type="png" data-w="1184" style="box-sizing: border-box;vertical-align: inherit;width: 100%;height: auto !important;" src="https://img.36krcdn.com/20220212/v2_0c72f25089a045859eb5f7269c7f50fa_img_000" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></strong></p> 
 <p style="text-align:justify;vertical-align: inherit;margin-right: 16px;margin-left: 16px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: rgb(51, 51, 51);letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-size-adjust: auto;line-height: 1.75em;font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;color: rgb(136, 136, 136);font-size: 12px;font-style: italic;letter-spacing: 1px;text-align: left;font-family: -webkit-standard;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">△零跑汽车自动驾驶技术介绍</strong></p> 
 <p style="text-align:justify;vertical-align: inherit;margin-right: 16px;margin-left: 16px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: rgb(51, 51, 51);letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-size-adjust: auto;line-height: 1.75em;font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 11pt;caret-color: red;color: #494949;font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei", sans-serif;">除了研发缓慢之外，</span><strong><span style="font-size: 11pt;letter-spacing: 0.544px;color: #494949;font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;">自研技术的实力是否过关，会不会导致产品品控出现问题，这些也是零跑要面对的问题</span></strong><span style="font-size: 11pt;caret-color: red;color: #494949;font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei", sans-serif;">。研发实力强是一方面，但要把实力转化为销量才是最关键的，就好像学生懂的再多，考的不好也白搭，而零跑欠缺的地方就在于此。看似准备大干一场，结果一到战场，销量还是差强人意。</span></p>

 <p style="text-align:justify;vertical-align: inherit;margin-right: 16px;margin-left: 16px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: rgb(51, 51, 51);letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-size-adjust: auto;line-height: 1.75em;font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 11pt;caret-color: red;color: #494949;font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei", sans-serif;">在渠道上，零跑采取最低成本的模式，</span><strong><span style="font-size: 11pt;letter-spacing: 0.544px;color: #494949;font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;">以加盟为主，直营为辅</span></strong><span style="font-size: 11pt;caret-color: red;color: #494949;font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei", sans-serif;">。与特斯拉和蔚来等以增加用户停留为理念，打造包括咖啡、特饮、阅读等在内的舒适休息区不同，零跑中心的设计理念更以销售为导向，不会有过多城市生活空间这样的属性。</span></p>
