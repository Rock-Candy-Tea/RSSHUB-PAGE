<p style="padding: 10px;white-space: normal;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-size-adjust: auto;font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;line-height: 2em;background-image: none;background-size: auto;text-align: left;visibility: visible;background-position: 0% 0%;background-repeat: repeat;color: rgb(163, 163, 163) !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="color: #000000;font-family: PingFangTC-light;font-size: medium;letter-spacing: -0.4px;text-indent: 21px;-webkit-text-stroke-color: #000000;caret-color: #000000;">投资者更为关注的是，小康股份2021年与华为合作推出的首款车型赛力斯SF5销量不佳，而在当初这款汽车发布时，曾将公司股价从22元直线拉升到近80元。</span></p> 
<p style="padding: 10px;white-space: normal;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-size-adjust: auto;font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;line-height: 2em;background-image: none;background-size: auto;text-align: left;visibility: visible;background-position: 0% 0%;background-repeat: repeat;color: rgb(163, 163, 163) !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="color: #000000;font-family: PingFangTC-light;font-size: medium;letter-spacing: -0.4px;text-indent: 21px;-webkit-text-stroke-color: #000000;caret-color: #000000;">目前，小康股份与华为再次合作的“问界M5”即将上市，但销量如何难以确定。而2021年业绩预告显示，小康股份净利润预计亏损15.5-19.5亿元。这种情势之下，小康股份未来的造车之路该如何走下去？连年亏损的业绩能否撑得住当下600亿的市值？</span></p> 
<p style="padding: 10px;white-space: normal;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-size-adjust: auto;font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;line-height: 2em;background-image: none;background-size: auto;text-align: left;visibility: visible;background-position: 0% 0%;background-repeat: repeat;color: rgb(163, 163, 163) !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="color: #000000;font-family: PingFangTC-light;font-size: medium;letter-spacing: -0.4px;text-indent: 21px;-webkit-text-stroke-color: #000000;caret-color: #000000;">这还要从华为看上小康股份之前开始说起。</span></p>

<p style="padding: 10px;white-space: normal;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-size-adjust: auto;font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;line-height: 2em;background-image: none;background-size: auto;text-align: left;visibility: visible;background-position: 0% 0%;background-repeat: repeat;color: rgb(163, 163, 163) !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="color: #000000;font-family: PingFangTC-light;font-size: medium;letter-spacing: -0.4px;text-indent: 21px;-webkit-text-stroke-color: #000000;caret-color: #000000;">如果用一句话来回顾小康股份的崛起之路，那应该是“大树底下好乘凉”。</span></p> 
<p style="padding: 10px;white-space: normal;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-size-adjust: auto;font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;line-height: 2em;background-image: none;background-size: auto;text-align: left;visibility: visible;background-position: 0% 0%;background-repeat: repeat;color: rgb(163, 163, 163) !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="caret-color: #000000; color: #000000; font-family: PingFangTC-light; font-size: medium; letter-spacing: -0.4px; text-indent: 21px; -webkit-text-stroke-color: #000000;">在牵手华为之前，小康股份的那棵“大树”是<a class="project-link" data-id="25766" data-name="东风汽车" data-logo="https://img.36krcdn.com/20210806/v2_189df15280ad48f38263ce40fb6816ad_img_000" data-refer-type="2" href="https://36kr.com/projectDetails/25766" target="_blank">东风汽车</a>。2003年，其联手东风汽车的东风小康有限公司成立。2005年，东风小康下线，两年内产销量双双突破10万辆。</span></p> 
<p style="padding: 10px;white-space: normal;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-size-adjust: auto;font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;line-height: 2em;background-image: none;background-size: auto;text-align: left;visibility: visible;background-position: 0% 0%;background-repeat: repeat;color: rgb(163, 163, 163) !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="color: #000000;font-family: PingFangTC-light;font-size: medium;letter-spacing: -0.4px;text-indent: 21px;-webkit-text-stroke-color: #000000;caret-color: #000000;">彼时，凭借“许三多”走红的王宝强成为东风小康代言人，“开东风，奔小康”的广告语传遍大江南北。也就是在这一时期，东风小康与五菱宏光、长安之星并称中国<a class="project-link" data-id="34785" data-name="微车" data-logo="https://img.36krcdn.com/20210806/v2_441fc6c8155d46cfa94b4e258f375330_img_000" data-refer-type="2" href="https://36kr.com/projectDetails/34785" target="_blank">微车</a>三巨头、三大拉货神车。</span></p> 
<p style="padding: 10px;white-space: normal;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-size-adjust: auto;font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;line-height: 2em;background-image: none;background-size: auto;text-align: left;visibility: visible;background-position: 0% 0%;background-repeat: repeat;color: rgb(163, 163, 163) !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong><span style="color: #000000;font-family: PingFangTC-light;font-size: medium;letter-spacing: -0.4px;text-indent: 21px;-webkit-text-stroke-color: #000000;caret-color: #000000;">这样的出道经历，一方面让小康品牌打响了知名度，另一方面也让小康这个品牌基本告别了高端汽车。但总体来说，当时依靠东风小康带动的整车业务，小康股份的日子过得还不错。</span></strong></p> 
<p style="padding: 10px;white-space: normal;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-size-adjust: auto;font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;line-height: 2em;background-image: none;background-size: auto;text-align: left;visibility: visible;background-position: 0% 0%;background-repeat: repeat;color: rgb(163, 163, 163) !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="color: #000000;font-family: PingFangTC-light;font-size: medium;letter-spacing: -0.4px;text-indent: 21px;-webkit-text-stroke-color: #000000;caret-color: #000000;">2016年6月，小康股份登陆A股时，当年其总营达到161.92亿元，汽车业务营收为150.09亿元，归母净利润5.14亿元，销售毛利率19.34%。凭借这样的表现，小康股份刚上市时曾一口气拉出18个涨停板，轰动一时。</span></p> 
<p style="padding: 10px;white-space: normal;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-size-adjust: auto;font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;line-height: 2em;background-image: none;background-size: auto;text-align: left;visibility: visible;background-position: 0% 0%;background-repeat: repeat;color: rgb(163, 163, 163) !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="color: #000000;font-family: PingFangTC-light;font-size: medium;letter-spacing: -0.4px;text-indent: 21px;-webkit-text-stroke-color: #000000;caret-color: #000000;">所以，从造车的底子来看，小康股份虽然以微车、经济型用车起步，但历经十多年发展，其在整车业务方面算得上经验丰富。有这样的行业基础，应该也是华为选择小康股份原因之一。</span></p> 
<p style="padding: 10px;white-space: normal;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-size-adjust: auto;font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;line-height: 2em;background-image: none;background-size: auto;text-align: left;visibility: visible;background-position: 0% 0%;background-repeat: repeat;color: rgb(163, 163, 163) !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong><span style="color: #000000;font-family: PingFangTC-light;font-size: medium;letter-spacing: -0.4px;text-indent: 21px;-webkit-text-stroke-color: #000000;caret-color: #000000;">当然，小康股份对于华为的吸引力，更重要的还是来自于在新能源汽车方面的技术积累，这也是资本市场看重的部分之一。</span></strong></p> 
<p style="padding: 10px;white-space: normal;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-size-adjust: auto;font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;line-height: 2em;background-image: none;background-size: auto;text-align: left;visibility: visible;background-position: 0% 0%;background-repeat: repeat;color: rgb(163, 163, 163) !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="color: #000000;font-family: PingFangTC-light;font-size: medium;letter-spacing: -0.4px;text-indent: 21px;-webkit-text-stroke-color: #000000;caret-color: #000000;">从2015年开始，小康股份就设立了新能源汽车设计院，开始进行包括控制系统和新能源汽车底盘等的研发。第二年，小康股份在美国硅谷成立SF Motors Inc，同时收购重庆东康新能源汽车有限公司，改名为<a class="project-link" data-id="440035" data-name="金康新能源" data-logo="https://img.36krcdn.com/20210812/v2_ad1994204bc74c579dae8e727c4d9963_img_000" data-refer-type="2" href="https://36kr.com/projectDetails/440035" target="_blank">金康新能源</a>。</span></p> 
<p style="padding: 10px;white-space: normal;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-size-adjust: auto;font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;line-height: 2em;background-image: none;background-size: auto;text-align: left;visibility: visible;background-position: 0% 0%;background-repeat: repeat;color: rgb(163, 163, 163) !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="color: #000000;font-family: PingFangTC-light;font-size: medium;letter-spacing: -0.4px;text-indent: 21px;-webkit-text-stroke-color: #000000;caret-color: #000000;">在上市之前，小康股份就已有24款纯电动专用车可享受工信部“免征购置税”的待遇。上市后，小康股份继续加大在新能源方面的投入，旗下拥有金康数字化工厂和SF美国工厂两大生产基地。通过一系列并购，目前小康已经拥有1000多项的全球技术专利及先进的制造工厂，而且还获得了国内生产和销售的双资质。</span></p> 
<p style="padding: 10px;white-space: normal;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-size-adjust: auto;font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;line-height: 2em;background-image: none;background-size: auto;text-align: left;visibility: visible;background-position: 0% 0%;background-repeat: repeat;color: rgb(163, 163, 163) !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="color: #000000;font-family: PingFangTC-light;font-size: medium;letter-spacing: -0.4px;text-indent: 21px;-webkit-text-stroke-color: #000000;caret-color: #000000;">不难发现，小康股份确实在积极布局新能源汽车，这从近年来持续增加的研发投入也能<a class="project-link" data-id="95377" data-name="得到" data-logo="https://img.36krcdn.com/20210807/v2_966db147ab4646ef82349f069ce61219_img_000" data-refer-type="2" href="https://36kr.com/projectDetails/95377" target="_blank">得到</a>证明。</span></p>

<p style="padding: 10px;white-space: normal;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-size-adjust: auto;font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;line-height: 2em;background-image: none;background-size: auto;text-align: left;visibility: visible;background-position: 0% 0%;background-repeat: repeat;color: rgb(163, 163, 163) !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="color: #000000;font-family: PingFangTC-light;font-size: medium;letter-spacing: -0.4px;text-indent: 21px;-webkit-text-stroke-color: #000000;caret-color: #000000;">而对于小康股份来说，能傍上华为这棵大树，对其未来发展当然有很大助力。但是，傍上华为也并不能解决所有问题，比如持续亏损和新车销量不济，似乎都比较棘手。</span></p>

<p style="padding: 10px;white-space: normal;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-size-adjust: auto;font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;line-height: 2em;background-image: none;background-size: auto;text-align: left;visibility: visible;background-position: 0% 0%;background-repeat: repeat;color: rgb(163, 163, 163) !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="color: #000000;font-family: PingFangTC-light;font-size: medium;letter-spacing: -0.4px;text-indent: 21px;-webkit-text-stroke-color: #000000;caret-color: #000000;">比如一度被寄予厚望，也是小康与华为首度合作的</span><span style="color: #000000;font-family: PingFangTC-light;font-size: medium;letter-spacing: -0.4px;text-indent: 21px;-webkit-text-stroke-color: #000000;caret-color: #000000;">赛力斯</span><span style="color: #000000;font-family: PingFangTC-light;font-size: medium;letter-spacing: -0.4px;text-indent: 21px;-webkit-text-stroke-color: #000000;caret-color: #000000;">SF5，交出的成绩单堪称惨淡。据乘联会数据显示，该品牌全年销量共计8169辆，还没有同样采取增程式技术的理想ONE一个月卖得多。</span></p> 
<p style="padding: 10px;white-space: normal;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-size-adjust: auto;font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;line-height: 2em;background-image: none;background-size: auto;text-align: left;visibility: visible;background-position: 0% 0%;background-repeat: repeat;color: rgb(163, 163, 163) !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="color: #000000;font-family: PingFangTC-light;font-size: medium;letter-spacing: -0.4px;text-indent: 21px;-webkit-text-stroke-color: #000000;caret-color: #000000;">那么，塞力斯为什么卖得这么惨？从品牌上来说，塞力斯来自于小康2016年在硅谷收购的SF Motors，在美国没搞出什么名堂，回国变成了赛力斯，品牌力不足，消费者不买单；其次，对于华为来说，这是其首次在汽车领域落地，各方面都在摸索阶段；最后，对于小康股份来说，其此前主要生产微车或经济型汽车，缺乏生产智能汽车的经验和技术。</span></p>

<p style="padding: 10px;white-space: normal;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-size-adjust: auto;font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;line-height: 2em;background-image: none;background-size: auto;text-align: left;visibility: visible;background-position: 0% 0%;background-repeat: repeat;color: rgb(163, 163, 163) !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="color: #000000;font-family: PingFangTC-light;font-size: medium;letter-spacing: -0.4px;text-indent: 21px;-webkit-text-stroke-color: #000000;caret-color: #000000;">小康股份随后进行了澄清和辟谣，但资本市场围绕在它周围的争议仍未消散。</span></p> 
<p style="padding: 10px;white-space: normal;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-size-adjust: auto;font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;line-height: 2em;background-image: none;background-size: auto;text-align: left;visibility: visible;background-position: 0% 0%;background-repeat: repeat;color: rgb(163, 163, 163) !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="color: #000000;font-family: PingFangTC-light;font-size: medium;letter-spacing: -0.4px;text-indent: 21px;-webkit-text-stroke-color: #000000;caret-color: #000000;">从财务数据上看，由于汽车销量不佳，小康股份在资金链上的压力正逐渐显现。2021年半年报显示，其有息负债总额为51.36亿元，占其净资产的70.88%。</span></p>
