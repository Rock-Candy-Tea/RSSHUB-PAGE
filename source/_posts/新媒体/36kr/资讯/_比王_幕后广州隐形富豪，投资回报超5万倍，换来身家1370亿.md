 <span style="font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;">6月10日，比亚迪股价大涨8.19%，报348.80元，总市值达1.02万亿元，成为创业板继宁德时代后的第二家市值破万亿的公司，有投资者将比亚迪称之为“比王”。</span> 
 <br> 
</section> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 24px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: 0.544px;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 0.5px;">成就<a class="project-link" data-id="1678523609854980" data-name="比亚迪" data-logo="https://img.36krcdn.com/20220331/v2_b97e2db3334d4ec4800582be94d562f5_img_000" data-refer-type="1" href="https://36kr.com/project/1678523609854980" target="_blank">比亚迪</a>的人有很多。有创始人王传福，他如果不放弃研究院处长职位，就没有现在的世界第二大新能源动力电池厂商和中国最大新能源车企；有巴菲特，2008年9月，巴菲特旗下伯克希尔.哈撒韦能源公司斥资18 亿港元，在港股认购比亚迪2.25亿股股票，14年来巴菲特一直持股未动，以比亚迪6月10日港股股价315.8港元/股来看，目前巴菲特这笔投资回报近40倍。有很多投资者因上述二人，长期相信比亚迪的价值，如今也已经得到收获。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 24px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: 0.544px;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 0.5px;">值得注意的是，比亚迪董事会中有一个人没有学历介绍，在比亚迪内部也不负责具体工作，但他却是比亚迪股东中持股数量仅次于王传福，为比亚迪的第三大股东。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 24px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: 0.544px;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 0.5px;">这个人不仅是比亚迪的副董事长、非执行董事，并且还是比亚迪股东融捷投资控股集团以及多家上市公司的董事长。此人还是王传福的表兄，如今的广州隐形富豪，比亚迪幕后的神秘人——吕向阳。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 24px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: 0.544px;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 0.5px;">据了解，吕向阳在比亚迪1995年成立时向王传福投资250万元，目前吕向阳直接持有比亚迪2.39亿股股份，并且吕向阳与张长虹夫妇通过共同持股的融捷投资控股集团有限公司持有比亚迪1.54亿股股份，吕向阳张长虹夫妇合计持有比亚迪股份比例为13.51%。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 24px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: 0.544px;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 0.5px;">按照比亚迪6月10日348.80元/股的股价计算，如今吕向阳所持比亚迪股份市值超1370亿元，27年后吕向阳的这笔投资回报高达54800多倍。</span></p> 
<h3 label="二级标题"><span style="color: #2A3342; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Segoe UI", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif, "Apple Color Emoji", "Segoe UI Emoji", "Segoe UI Symbol"; font-size: 18px; font-weight: 700; letter-spacing: normal;">吕向阳拿出250万，成了表弟的投资人</span></h3> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 24px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: 0.544px;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 0.5px;">吕向阳和王传福都是安徽无为人，二人是表兄弟关系，吕向阳生于1962年，比王传福大4岁。二人小时候家里很穷，王传福在哥嫂的支持下，考上了如今的中南大学，后来在中国北京有色金属研究院做到副主任。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 24px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: 0.544px;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 0.5px;">而吕向阳却早早辍学，16岁的时候，吕向阳进入了中国人民银行安徽分行开始工作。银行的工作很稳定，吕向阳很珍惜这个稳定的“铁饭碗”，工作也很努力，最后做到了支行负责人。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 24px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: 0.544px;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 0.5px;">90年代，流行下海掘金。1993年，31岁的吕向阳也选择南下经商，先后去了广州、深圳和香港，最初主要从事房地产、纺织品等传统行业的经营。凭借在银行十多年的经验，以及精明的头脑和极其广泛的人脉，吕向阳很快就积累了第一桶金。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 24px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: 0.544px;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 0.5px;">直到1995年，王传福找到了吕向阳，当时王传福放弃北京有色金属研究总院的安定工作，决心辞职创办一家生产电池的企业。但王传福手里并没有多少钱，创业起步阶段需要资金，王传福希望已经小有所成的吕向阳能够资助他。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 24px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: 0.544px;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 0.5px;">吕向阳很震惊，他深知创业艰苦，所以起初吕向阳并没有同意。他对王传福说，“你是国家干部，为什么要冒险下海呢？”王传福执拗地坚持，“我是研究电池的，我相信自己能做好，相信电池行业的潜力。”在王传福一遍又一遍地游说下，最终吕向阳给了他250万元启动资金。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 24px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: 0.544px;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 0.5px;">但创业艰难，谁能保证一定会成功呢！吕向阳和王传福二人商量，这250万元作为吕向阳对王传福的投资，王传福给吕向阳公司股份。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 24px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: 0.544px;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 0.5px;">于是，未来的锂电和新能源车巨头企业比亚迪就这样成立了。吕向阳看着表弟王传福和十几个创业伙伴在深圳莲塘一个破旧的车间里敲敲打打，白天搞研发，晚上打地铺。这种孤注一掷的“赌性”，让吕向阳觉得这笔投资值得。吕向阳也曾向媒体透露，因为信任王传福，才投资的比亚迪。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 24px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: 0.544px;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 0.5px;">好在比亚迪很善于抓住机会。比亚迪开始时主打产品为日本企业已经放弃的镍镉电池，但该产品在当时仍有很大市场需求。并以台湾最大的无绳电话代工企业“大霸”入手，迅速占领该产品市场，王传福也靠镍镉电池开始起家。随后的几年时间，比亚迪便将电池业务做到国内第一、世界第二。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 24px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: 0.544px;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 0.5px;">比亚迪业绩也在市场份额提升中飞涨，电池产业带来了强大的现金流和盈利能力。2001年至2003年，比亚迪净利润分别达到2亿元、6.53亿元和8.64亿元。比亚迪也凭借这项基础，在2002年春末，百富勤和工商东亚都各自承诺以更高市盈率比亚迪在香港上市。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 24px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: 0.544px;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 0.5px;">比亚迪上市后，吕向阳当初的250万元的投资早已变成几亿元。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 24px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: 0.544px;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 0.5px;">如今，比亚迪市值破万亿，很多人称赞“股神”巴菲特的投资眼光。殊不知，吕向阳才是投资比亚迪的最大赢家。</span></p> 
<h3 label="二级标题"><span style="color: #2A3342; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Segoe UI", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif, "Apple Color Emoji", "Segoe UI Emoji", "Segoe UI Symbol"; font-size: 18px; font-weight: 700; letter-spacing: normal;">吕向阳从产业投资转向资本运作</span></h3> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 24px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: 0.544px;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 0.5px;">吕向阳27年前做了王传福的“贵人”，从当前的回报来看，比亚迪市值大涨给吕向阳带来的账面上的浮盈，又何尝不是吕向阳的幸运呢？</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 24px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: 0.544px;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 0.5px;">不过，吕向阳外出闯荡至今快30年，也是凭借自身的努力和天赋，才闯荡出自己的一片天地。1995年，吕向阳创建了融捷投资管理集团（以下简称“融捷集团”），正式开启了自己的投资事业。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 24px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: 0.544px;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 0.5px;">吕向阳创办的融捷集团在27年的发展历程中不断向外界探索。截至当前，融捷集团旗下已有117家成员企业，对外投资事件21起，所投资产业涉及新能源产业、显示科技产业、教育科技产业、生物医药产业、资源开发与加工产业、科技创新投资与金融投资等十多个行业，产业基地和分支机构分布于全球众多国家和地区。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 24px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: 0.544px;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 0.5px;">融捷集团作为投资公司，前期投资主要都投向产业目标，比如早期的房地产、高分子化工、矿产等。但随着比亚迪的顺利上市，融捷集团开始与证券市场进行接触。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 24px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: 0.544px;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 0.5px;">吕向阳很早投资了少有人问津的锂矿。2005年，融捷集团对四川省甘孜州甲基卡固体锂辉石矿进行投资，率先抢占了全球新能源产业链中的锂资源。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 24px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: 0.544px;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 0.5px;">融捷集团旗下上市公司融捷股份，早前证券名称是“路翔股份”，2015年，吕向阳通过资本手段让融捷股份借壳上市。融捷股份的当前的主营业务为锂矿采选行业、锂盐及深加工、锂电设备，处在锂电上游。而2019年以前，市场对锂电的反馈并不积极，2020年4月还被实施了退市风险警示，成为*ST融捷。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 24px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: 0.544px;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 0.5px;">自2021年以来，随着全球锂资源紧缺和价格上涨，新能源汽车产业链上游锂矿及原材料供应商因锂价走高而业绩猛涨，处在赛道之中的融捷股份股价一路高涨，截至当前总市值已超400亿元。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 24px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: 0.544px;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 0.5px;">吕向阳因投资比亚迪获得巨大收益，比亚迪股价的价值更像是是吕向阳资本运作的趁手利器。几年前的借壳路翔股份以及恒天天鹅等资本运作，早年间让吕向阳得到“资本大鳄”的称号。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 24px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: 0.544px;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 0.5px;">近年来比亚迪股价的暴涨，也加快了吕向阳的运作。据统计，自2021年至今，吕向阳的融捷集团已经质押比亚迪股份43次，质押股份市值最高时近6亿元。对吕向阳的投资事业来说，比亚迪的股份无疑是融捷集团最大的抗风险工具。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px 24px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: 0.544px;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 0.5px;">不过，吕向阳并不经常出现在公众面前，一直非常低调。仅比亚迪股份这一项的价值，吕向阳张长虹夫妇的总资产就达到1370亿，如果加上融捷集团的资产，吕向阳的身家只会更多。</span></p>
