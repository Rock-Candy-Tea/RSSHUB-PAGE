<p>在国务院金融委会议相关利好消息刺激下，今日AH股双双大幅高开，延续昨日强劲表现，沪指高开1.4%，深成指高开1.9%，创业板指高开1.89%；恒指高开6.69%，恒生科技指数高开10.88%。</p> 
<p>国务院金融委会议提出，关于房地产企业，要及时研究和提出有力有效的防范化解风险应对方案，提出向新发展模式转型的配套措施。房地产板块全线大幅走强，天保基建、<a class="project-link" data-id="154093" data-name="苏宁环球" data-logo="https://img.36krcdn.com/20210808/v2_609a79b5d2a34244bc9d5046f24e4d29_img_000" data-refer-type="2" href="https://36kr.com/projectDetails/154093" target="_blank">苏宁环球</a>、新城控股等多股开盘涨停，港股融创中国盘中一度涨超60%。</p> 
<p class="image-wrapper"><img data-img-size-val="1093,1933" src="https://img.36krcdn.com/20220317/v2_5663f3042b17479eb1b12285295ddad3_img_jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p> 
<p class="img-desc">来源：Wind</p> 
<p>受地产股带动，基建板块表现活跃，浙江建投一字封停，此前已连续两日涨停。</p> 
<p>同样领涨的还有包括中药、新冠药物概念在内的医药股。<a class="project-link" data-id="571628" data-name="红日药业" data-logo="https://img.36krcdn.com/20210813/v2_f7f1826f72b74f9d97b2756251b87d19_img_000" data-refer-type="2" href="https://36kr.com/projectDetails/571628" target="_blank">红日药业</a>、盘龙药业、精华制药、<a class="project-link" data-id="67251" data-name="科伦药业" data-logo="https://img.36krcdn.com/20210807/v2_34ea3083ad784d39b451406b92e570d8_img_000" data-refer-type="2" href="https://36kr.com/projectDetails/67251" target="_blank">科伦药业</a>等多股早盘涨停，佐力药业、千金药业、<a class="project-link" data-id="78793" data-name="昆药集团" data-logo="https://img.36krcdn.com/20210807/v2_0b1e81610a2b48928016fd0fa3610885_img_000" data-refer-type="2" href="https://36kr.com/projectDetails/78793" target="_blank">昆药集团</a>、<a class="project-link" data-id="579746" data-name="尖峰集团" data-logo="https://img.36krcdn.com/20210814/v2_4a255d5383ad45b2bc26f21e1fe95e6d_img_000" data-refer-type="2" href="https://36kr.com/projectDetails/579746" target="_blank">尖峰集团</a>、富祥药业等跟涨。</p> 
<p class="image-wrapper"><img data-img-size-val="945,1439" src="https://img.36krcdn.com/20220317/v2_00c73870a3e644fd81ccdc1a2a77de05_img_jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p> 
<p class="img-desc">来源：Wind</p> 
<p>消息面，红日药业等多家中药企业产品被列入《新型冠状病毒肺炎诊疗方案（试行第九版）》.</p> 
<p>据<a class="project-link" data-id="3969151" data-name="人民日报" data-logo="https://img.36krcdn.com/20200916/v2_811751a081924fa9af8741ce120bd7bf_img_png" data-refer-type="2" href="https://36kr.com/projectDetails/3969151" target="_blank">人民日报</a>报道，今日，在上海疫情防控发布会上，张文宏解读新版新冠诊疗方案。他谈到抗疫要有3个“武器”：疫苗的充分注射；有效的抗病毒药物和其他药物，包括中医药等支撑；非常充足的医疗冗余度。</p> 
<p>与此同时，以食品饮料、餐饮旅游为首的大消费板块集体崛起，<a class="project-link" data-id="607048" data-name="春雪食品" data-logo="https://img.36krcdn.com/20210814/v2_b5462e27e3e2444ea7d0f006534a5925_img_000" data-refer-type="2" href="https://36kr.com/projectDetails/607048" target="_blank">春雪食品</a>、妙可蓝多、<a class="project-link" data-id="553555" data-name="岭南控股" data-logo="https://img.36krcdn.com/20210813/v2_92b576d0bc6d4297b7b060204f454ac2_img_000" data-refer-type="2" href="https://36kr.com/projectDetails/553555" target="_blank">岭南控股</a>、曲江文旅等涨停。</p> 
<p class="image-wrapper"><img data-img-size-val="1061,1617" src="https://img.36krcdn.com/20220317/v2_7a9109e528a0496eb80e37cb34f997ca_img_jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p> 
<p class="img-desc">来源：Wind</p> 
<p><a class="project-link" data-id="104179" data-name="财通证券" data-logo="https://img.36krcdn.com/20210808/v2_4e1936f62c544d2f934785dbe29ed878_img_000" data-refer-type="2" href="https://36kr.com/projectDetails/104179" target="_blank">财通证券</a>研报表示，经济内循环和促消费政策导向下，旅游业有望迎来政策支持红利期。疫情后国家各部门陆续出台多项政策，旨在促进消费、构建国内经济内循环，为旅游业中短期内的复苏和长期的品质化+智慧化+产业融合发展提供了政策基石和方向指引。</p> 
<p>此外，锂电股持续拉升，宁德时代盘中一度大涨超6%，宋都股份、<a class="project-link" data-id="155817" data-name="融捷股份" data-logo="https://img.36krcdn.com/20210808/v2_695f9c6e42d74b54ac4155fb95d0b69b_img_000" data-refer-type="2" href="https://36kr.com/projectDetails/155817" target="_blank">融捷股份</a>早盘涨停，蔚蓝锂芯、晶瑞电材、新力金融等跟涨。</p> 
<p>元宇宙、游戏板块表现强势，完美世界、三七互娱涨停，盛天网络、<a class="project-link" data-id="29221" data-name="巨人网络" data-logo="https://img.36krcdn.com/20210806/v2_3f66b25041a849c7924b11fcab15a1fa_img_000" data-refer-type="2" href="https://36kr.com/projectDetails/29221" target="_blank">巨人网络</a>、<a class="project-link" data-id="67275" data-name="宝通科技" data-logo="https://img.36krcdn.com/20210807/v2_f335aa51dd5f45ce813be9c6af7431cf_img_000" data-refer-type="2" href="https://36kr.com/projectDetails/67275" target="_blank">宝通科技</a>等跟涨 。</p> 
<p>半导体、光刻胶板块掀涨停潮，<a class="project-link" data-id="286583" data-name="容大感光" data-logo="https://img.36krcdn.com/20210810/v2_f78643ea28d34a7c9b27f576d6b41241_img_000" data-refer-type="2" href="https://36kr.com/projectDetails/286583" target="_blank">容大感光</a>、彤程新材、<a class="project-link" data-id="544909" data-name="江化微" data-logo="https://img.36krcdn.com/20210813/v2_4c81558c31fc48bb9784e60867cfcbcb_img_000" data-refer-type="2" href="https://36kr.com/projectDetails/544909" target="_blank">江化微</a>等多股涨停。</p> 
<p class="image-wrapper"><img data-img-size-val="990,1349" src="https://img.36krcdn.com/20220317/v2_9f575a8097a943328f5d7331b1a02ba2_img_jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p> 
<p class="img-desc">来源：Wind</p> 
<p>午间休盘，A股三大指数集体大幅上涨，沪指涨2.59%，深成指涨3.73%，创业板指涨4.38%；房地产、新冠药、光刻胶、游戏板块涨幅居前；北向资金净买入31.81亿元。</p> 
<p>午后，A股三大指数持续发力，深成指、创业板指盘中双双大涨4%。</p> 
<p>多只券商股午后冲高，国盛金控涨停，<a class="project-link" data-id="591391" data-name="华创阳安" data-logo="https://img.36krcdn.com/20210814/v2_24b170f142c84059963428a0db10888a_img_000" data-refer-type="2" href="https://36kr.com/projectDetails/591391" target="_blank">华创阳安</a>触及涨停，国联证券、<a class="project-link" data-id="39490" data-name="东方财富" data-logo="https://img.36krcdn.com/20210807/v2_c664bcde61844ffd968fea4d2f3d8ae6_img_000" data-refer-type="2" href="https://36kr.com/projectDetails/39490" target="_blank">东方财富</a>、国金证券等跟涨。消息面，中国结算宣布自4月起将股票类业务最低结算备付金缴纳比例自18%调降至16%。</p> 
<p>核电板块拉升走强，深南电A触及涨停，台海核电、<a class="project-link" data-id="360930" data-name="宝色股份" data-logo="https://img.36krcdn.com/20210811/v2_6a1d995a5b8b410cad90254338a3bcc1_img_000" data-refer-type="2" href="https://36kr.com/projectDetails/360930" target="_blank">宝色股份</a>、<a class="project-link" data-id="25118" data-name="中广核" data-logo="https://img.36krcdn.com/20210806/v2_69137726aeb548dea4245bd81ece3526_img_000" data-refer-type="2" href="https://36kr.com/projectDetails/25118" target="_blank">中广核</a>技、兰石重装等跟涨。</p> 
<p>今日收盘，A股三大指数集体收涨，沪指涨1.4%，深成指涨2.41%，创业板指涨2.87%；房地产、医药、建材板块领涨，电力、海运板块走低；北向资金净买入53.65亿元。</p>
