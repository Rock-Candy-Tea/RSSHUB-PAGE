<p style="margin-right: 16px;margin-left: 16px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;caret-color: rgb(0, 0, 0);color: rgb(0, 0, 0);line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="color: #000000;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 0.544px;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">蓝瓶咖啡的James Freeman选择了后者。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 16px;margin-left: 16px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;caret-color: rgb(0, 0, 0);color: rgb(0, 0, 0);line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="color: #000000;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 0.544px;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">2022年2月25日，中国大陆第一家蓝瓶咖啡在上海如约而至。这个在第三次咖啡<a class="project-link" data-id="457408" data-name="浪潮" data-logo="https://img.36krcdn.com/20210812/v2_079b6194026a457fb6ca0d50e1203979_img_000" data-refer-type="2" href="https://36kr.com/projectDetails/457408" target="_blank">浪潮</a>中脱颖而出的精品咖啡走上了桥头。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 16px;margin-left: 16px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;caret-color: rgb(0, 0, 0);color: rgb(0, 0, 0);line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 0.544px;color: #000000;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">关于蓝瓶咖啡，音乐出身的创始人James Freeman在选址和门店设计上，浪漫而艺术，在商业步伐上，审慎而严谨，但这一切，被具备商业运营和品牌操盘的首席执行官Bryan Meehan巧妙弥补。</span></strong></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 16px;margin-left: 16px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;caret-color: rgb(0, 0, 0);color: rgb(0, 0, 0);line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="color: #000000;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 0.544px;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">这让蓝瓶咖啡既充满Freeman浓烈的风格主义，更具备精品连锁的商业价值</span><span style="color: #000000;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 0.544px;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">。</span><span style="color: #000000;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 0.544px;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">你会看到，高端零售几乎苛刻的标准化和完美主义，以及个性品牌充满生机的社区性和互动性，在一个品牌的成长中巧妙兼容。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 16px;margin-left: 16px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;caret-color: rgb(0, 0, 0);color: rgb(0, 0, 0);line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="color: #000000;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 0.544px;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">这或许对中国同期发展的咖啡品牌具备独特的案例研究意义。</span><strong><span style="letter-spacing: 0.544px;font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light;font-size: 15px;">不同于星巴克、瑞幸的千店模式，也区别于Manner的集中区域扩张，蓝瓶咖啡短期内并不会挑起一场速度与</span><span style="letter-spacing: 0.544px;font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light;font-size: 15px;">规模之战。</span></strong></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 16px;margin-left: 16px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;caret-color: rgb(0, 0, 0);color: rgb(0, 0, 0);line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="letter-spacing: 0.544px;font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light;font-size: 15px;">而对M Stand、Seesaw等精品咖啡连锁来说，蓝瓶咖啡或许在服务、产品、创新上能启发更深层的品牌文化更新，</span><strong><span style="letter-spacing: 0.544px;font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light;font-size: 15px;color: #7A4442;">一个成长与社区周边紧密相连，在环保可持续上持续进发的品牌，未来可能会衍化成何种形状</span></strong><span style="letter-spacing: 0.544px;font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light;font-size: 15px;">。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 16px;margin-left: 16px;white-space: normal;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;font-size: 14px;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;caret-color: rgb(0, 0, 0);color: rgb(0, 0, 0);line-height: 1.75em;text-align: center;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light;caret-color: #FFFFFF;color: #FFFFFF;font-size: 36px;font-weight: bold;background-color: #B14F32;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"> 01 </span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 16px;margin-left: 16px;white-space: normal;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-family: -apple-system, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);caret-color: rgb(0, 0, 0);color: rgb(0, 0, 0);line-height: 1.75em;text-align: center;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 18px;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">刷信用卡创业，开标致505送货</strong></span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 16px;margin-left: 16px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;caret-color: rgb(0, 0, 0);color: rgb(0, 0, 0);line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="color: #000000;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 0.544px;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">2004年1月的某天，旧金山依旧阳光灿烂。蓝瓶咖啡的餐车前排起了30-40人的队伍。Freeman突然意识到某个高光时刻可能正在降临，「这事儿或许成了」。一年之后，他在距离Twitter总部几个街区的车库，开出了蓝瓶咖啡的第一家门店。</span></p>

<p style="margin-right: 16px;margin-left: 16px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;caret-color: rgb(0, 0, 0);color: rgb(0, 0, 0);line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="color: #000000;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 0.544px;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">2017年，瑞士食品巨头雀巢用约4.25亿美元收购了公司68%的股份，蓝瓶咖啡的估值一路上升到7亿美元。在此之前，他还是一位苦苦挣扎的单簧管演奏家。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 16px;margin-left: 16px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;caret-color: rgb(0, 0, 0);color: rgb(0, 0, 0);line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="color: #000000;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 0.544px;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">长期以来，Freeman痴迷于新鲜的咖啡。2002年左右，旧金山几乎没有一家咖啡馆出售的袋装咖啡上印有烘焙日期，这让痴迷于新鲜咖啡的Freeman决定自己动手。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 16px;margin-left: 16px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;caret-color: rgb(0, 0, 0);color: rgb(0, 0, 0);line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong><span style="color: #000000;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 0.544px;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">他在奥克兰Temescal街区的公寓附近，用每月600美金的价格租了一个186平方英尺的盆栽棚，又自驾到爱荷华州烘豆机制造商Diedrich那里淘了一台老式机器。</span></strong><span style="color: #000000;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 0.544px;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">这样一来，单次烘豆量就提升到了7磅，Freeman开着他那辆标致505，穿梭在街区间送货。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 16px;margin-left: 16px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;caret-color: rgb(0, 0, 0);color: rgb(0, 0, 0);line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="color: #000000;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 0.544px;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">如果当年没有勇气欠下15000美金的信用卡的债务，Freeman可能做不成蓝瓶咖啡。在地方管弦乐团演奏单簧管并不赚钱，收入只够勉强养家糊口，租大棚和烘豆机要钱，送货要油费，还要付给当地一位平面设计师「logo设计费」，这些都是成本。</span></p>
