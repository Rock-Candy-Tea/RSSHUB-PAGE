<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">距离<a class="project-link" data-id="481139" data-name="慕思" data-logo="https://img.36krcdn.com/20210812/v2_4d76b26ae8a1495a8145b643cf4b6199_img_000" data-refer-type="2" href="https://36kr.com/projectDetails/481139" target="_blank">慕思</a>企图在T9床垫推出智能化已经过去三年。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">根据数据显示，虽然2018-2020年慕思床垫业务营业收入逐年增长但营收占比有所下降。2020年其</span><strong><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">床垫营业收入达23.96亿元，占比降至54.22%。</span></strong></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">诚然，从2020年数据看，营收占比数据的下滑，大部分原因归于疫情对线下销售渠道的影响。截至2020年，慕思共开设1400余家经销商和3500余家专卖店，共计4900多家门店。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">而在疫情相对平稳的2021年，据慕思招股书显示，</span><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong>2021年上半年，慕思股份实现营收回到28.09亿元，净利润为3.26亿元。</strong></span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">根据iiMedia Research(艾媒咨询)数据显示，2016-2020年间，中国睡眠经济整体市场规模已从2616.3亿元增长至3778.6亿元，增长44.42%。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">有机构估算到2030年，睡眠市场将达万亿级规模。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">在智能家居行业赛道发展迅速，智能家居相关在业/存续企业已达到11.2万家的市场环境下，慕思能否稳站市场第一，似乎需要打上一个问号。</span></p> 
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class="rich_pages wxw-img js_insertlocalimg" data-backh="334" data-backw="500" data-ratio="0.668" data-s="300,640" data-type="png" data-w="500" style="width: 100%;height: auto;" src="https://img.36krcdn.com/20220313/v2_e5c54434c0b64d64b4f6aadb35de74b7_img_000" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p> 
<p><span style="color: #3F72F8; font-size: 20px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: left; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;">慕思的成本玄学</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">据财经新闻报道称，2021年上半年我国智能家居行业增速达到25%，国内智能家居行业销售收入首次突破1600亿元，同比增长25%，成为行业历史增长最快时期。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">市场规模方面，2016-2020年我国智能家居市场规模由2608.5亿元增至5144.7亿元，并预测2021年我国智能家居市场规模可达5800.5亿元。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">为此，随着床垫市场的规模逐渐增涨，</span><strong><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">2021年慕思也迈出了IPO的第一步。</span></strong></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">但在慕思上市之初就有媒体爆料，慕思品牌的床垫，成本900元售价却可以高达6000元。</span></p> 
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class="rich_pages wxw-img js_insertlocalimg" data-ratio="0.4410211267605634" data-s="300,640" data-type="png" data-w="1136" style src="https://img.36krcdn.com/20220313/v2_85fd83a1d3ce468096575f0e209ac60e_img_000" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">根据慕思旗舰店价格可知，床底价格最高在36279元，普遍价格在6000千元到1万元之间，相对于同行而言，价格更高。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">此外，就连一个普通的枕头也要卖到800-1000元。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">截至2021年6月末，慕思股份经销商数量为1589家。这一渠道的床垫单价一直在2000元左右；直营渠道近年来售价逐渐下调，2021年上半年平均售价6050元；电商渠道的床垫单价基本在2700元左右；向<a class="project-link" data-id="473074" data-name="欧派" data-logo="https://img.36krcdn.com/20200729/v2_1e366838832346ef8f97dc7b3e08998b_img_000" data-refer-type="2" href="https://36kr.com/projectDetails/473074" target="_blank">欧派</a>等合作商提供定制床垫的直供渠道价格最低，今年上半年仅有1258元。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">而根据招股书，在2021年上半年，其</span><strong><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">床垫产品的单位成本还不到1000元，床架产品的单位成本则在1800元左右。</span></strong></p> 
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class="rich_pages wxw-img js_insertlocalimg" data-ratio="1.5783333333333334" data-s="300,640" data-type="jpeg" data-w="600" style src="https://img.36krcdn.com/20220313/v2_884d69a360cb45ca836edf1a9f12d655_img_000" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">诚然，综合每年不菲的广告支出，产品巨大的价格差显然是销售费用综合所致。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">在慕思股份提交的招股书中披露，2018—2020年，慕思股份广告费用分别为3.45亿元、4.45亿元和3.96亿，分别占当期营业收入的10.9%、11.67%和8.96%。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">而同期同行可比公司的广告费用占营业收入比重的平均值则分别为4.95%、3.98%和3.78%。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">那么，在低成本高广告费用之下，慕思真的具备一定的产品技术吗？</span></p> 
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class="rich_pages wxw-img js_insertlocalimg" data-ratio="0.567219152854512" data-s="300,640" data-type="jpeg" data-w="1086" style src="https://img.36krcdn.com/20220313/v2_185794980e574ce8ab4724d75913ee28_img_000" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>

 <span style="color: #3F72F8;font-size: 20px;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-align: left;">的另一面</span> 
</section> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"></span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">实际上，多年研发费的投入，在产品专利上，慕斯目前一共拥有了798项专利，但其中光外观设计专利就占据了583种。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">例如慕思的T10 AI智能床垫，历经9年研发，可以利用AI检测人体数据，并通过慕思睡眠大数据来匹配睡眠参数，智能调节出适配人体体征的床垫软硬度。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">而目前慕思健康睡眠系统最新的T10型号，软硬件都可以实现深度自适应，随用户身体睡姿变化，提供自动检测的5D智能分区调节功能，两分钟内调节到最贴合用户的软硬度和形态。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">还能多维度采集睡眠信息，能够根据个人情况持续优化睡感。</span></p>
