<p style="margin-right: 10px;margin-left: 10px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light;white-space: pre-wrap;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">在我们生活中，有一种稳赚不赔的生意，简单点做一年赚几十几百万，放开来做一年赚几千万几个亿也很简单。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 10px;margin-left: 10px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light;white-space: pre-wrap;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">这个生意的逻辑就是利用人的信仰、情感来赚钱，站在生意模型的角度它是个好生意，但站在道德伦理层面做这个事情非常不妥，甚至还犯法。</span></strong></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 10px;margin-left: 10px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="color: #FF4C41;"><strong><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;font-family: Optima-Regular, PingFangTC-light;white-space: pre-wrap;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">不卖关子，这个生意就是做网上虚拟祈福、祭拜。</span></strong></span></p>
