<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">早在2018年之前，美国的头部科技公司就开始系统地布局自动驾驶，自动驾驶也不再仅仅是车的附属品。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">随着</span><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">自动驾驶技术的发展迭代，车的使用效率将大大提高，甚至将颠覆未来的出行方式和交通模式。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><img class="rich_pages wxw-img js_insertlocalimg" data-ratio="0.6538461538461539" data-s="300,640" data-type="png" data-w="1222" style src="https://img.36krcdn.com/20220323/v2_d0d45468975c418d8c8de2f396cf690b_img_000" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>

<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">创立以来，小马智行就成为了资本眼中的“香饽饽”。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">五年来</span><strong><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">小马智行基本保持每年1-2次融资，迄今为止已经完成了6轮融资</span></strong><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">。伴随着一轮轮融资的推进，小马智行在自动驾驶和智慧物流领域达成了众多里程碑。</span></p>

<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">目前，资本对自动驾驶行业的狂热已经褪去，对自动驾驶公司的要求也渐趋明朗。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">能够顺利完成本轮融资，</span><strong><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">小</span></strong><strong><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">马智行靠的不仅是自动驾驶行业的市场前景，更是因为其去年在技术与商业化落地所获得的较好成绩。</span></strong></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">年前，小马智行发布了第六代自动驾驶系统，并搭载于丰田旗下的S-AM MPV车型上。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">这款面向车规量产的第六代系统的发布足以体现小马智行的自动驾驶技术已经达到足够的成熟度和稳定性。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">楼天城表示，技术实力是获得此次融资的关键。事实上，小马智行自创立以来也一直在寻求技术的突破。</span></p>

<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">同时，</span><strong><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">小马智行也在不断积累测试里</span></strong><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">程，截至2021年，在全球范围内自动驾驶测试总里程数已超过1000万公里。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">此外，小马智行不断寻求在更多城市拿到复杂路况的测试机会，先继在加州、广州、北京、深圳拿到测试牌照。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">这就意味着小马智行能够在更复杂的路况和规则下进行测试，测试结果也更加精确，安全系数更高。</span></p>

<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">尽管小马智行的高管们很早就意识到了这一点，并在探索商业化落地之路上做出了很多努力，比如积极与车企寻求合作，布局自动驾驶出租车、小巴车、卡车等领域，涉足整车制造，但</span><strong><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;caret-color: red;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">目前看来这条商业化之路并没有想象之中的顺利。</span></strong></p>
