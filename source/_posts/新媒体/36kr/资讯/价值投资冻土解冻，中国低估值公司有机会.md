<section style="margin: 25px 8px;max-width: 100%;white-space: normal;line-height: 2em;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;box-sizing: border-box !important;"> 
 <span style="max-width: 100%;font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;background-color: #FFFFFF;font-size: 16px;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;box-sizing: border-box !important;">凯特勒说：“那是一个我们认真思考如何把传统价值投资——即本杰明·格雷厄姆(Benjamin Graham)的理论——应用到国际市场这样一个在当时还算新领域的机会。”凯特勒任Causeway Capital Management首席执行官，管理着450亿美元的资产。</span> 
</section> 
<section style="margin: 25px 8px;max-width: 100%;white-space: normal;line-height: 2em;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;box-sizing: border-box !important;"> 
 <span style="max-width: 100%;font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;background-color: #FFFFFF;font-size: 16px;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;box-sizing: border-box !important;">该公司的旗舰基金Causeway International Value (CIVVX)自2001年推出以来表现一直好于其跟踪的基准。</span> 
</section> 
<section style="margin: 25px 8px;max-width: 100%;white-space: normal;line-height: 2em;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;box-sizing: border-box !important;"> 
 <span style="max-width: 100%;font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;background-color: #FFFFFF;font-size: 16px;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;box-sizing: border-box !important;">正如凯特勒所说的，价值股经历了一个“漫长而寒冷”的冬天，表现一直落后于成长股。现在，面对通胀高企和利率上行，投资者正在从成长股转向价值股，多年冻土正在融化。</span> 
</section> 
<section style="margin: 25px 8px;max-width: 100%;white-space: normal;line-height: 2em;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;box-sizing: border-box !important;"> 
 <span style="max-width: 100%;font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;background-color: #FFFFFF;font-size: 16px;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;box-sizing: border-box !important;">凯特勒最近被《巴伦周刊》评选为2022年美国金融界最具影响力的100位女性之一，她近日在位于达拉斯的办公室接受了《巴伦周刊》的专访，谈到了如今的的市场环境、有吸引力的投资领域以及价值投资的前景等问题。以下是经过编辑的访谈内容。</span> 
</section>

<section style="margin: 25px 8px;max-width: 100%;white-space: normal;line-height: 2em;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;box-sizing: border-box !important;"> 
 <span style="color: #004EA2;"><strong><span style="color: #004EA2;max-width: 100%;font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;background-color: #FFFFFF;font-size: 16px;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;box-sizing: border-box !important;">《巴伦周刊》：对于投资者来说，这是一个非同寻常的时期，疫情已经持续了两年多，现在又出现了地缘政治冲突，你是怎么看市场的？</span></strong></span> 
</section> 
<section style="margin: 25px 8px;max-width: 100%;white-space: normal;line-height: 2em;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;box-sizing: border-box !important;"> 
 <strong><span style="max-width: 100%;font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;background-color: #FFFFFF;font-size: 16px;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;box-sizing: border-box !important;">萨拉·凯特勒：</span></strong>   
 <span style="max-width: 100%;font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;background-color: #FFFFFF;font-size: 16px;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;box-sizing: border-box !important;">这两场危机接踵而来的确不同寻常，通常两场危机之间会有一个间隔，可以看出现在投资者非常紧张不安，但推动市场的是企业盈利和流动性。在地缘政治冲突和全球央行开始进入货币政策紧缩周期等背景下，市场正在清醒过来，资金变少了，资产价格会由涨转跌，这只是一个下跌速度和幅度的问题。</span> 
</section> 
<section style="margin: 25px 8px;max-width: 100%;white-space: normal;line-height: 2em;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;box-sizing: border-box !important;"> 
 <span style="color: #004EA2;"><strong><span style="color: #004EA2;max-width: 100%;font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;background-color: #FFFFFF;font-size: 16px;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;box-sizing: border-box !important;">《巴伦周刊》：市场在出现大规模混乱过后通常会在一段时间里有不错的表现，你是怎样利用当前形势的？</span></strong></span> 
</section> 
<section style="margin: 25px 8px;max-width: 100%;white-space: normal;line-height: 2em;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;box-sizing: border-box !important;"> 
 <strong><span style="max-width: 100%;font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;background-color: #FFFFFF;font-size: 16px;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;box-sizing: border-box !important;">萨拉·凯特勒：</span></strong>   
 <span style="max-width: 100%;font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;background-color: #FFFFFF;font-size: 16px;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;box-sizing: border-box !important;">我经历过的第一次危机是1997年的亚洲金融危机，当时我正在关注亚洲市场，看到股票价格跌至它们资产负债表上的现金水平时感到非常迷惑，那是我见过的最大手笔的买入了。后来的危机也带来了同样的机遇，如今也不例外。</span> 
</section> 
<section style="margin: 25px 8px;max-width: 100%;white-space: normal;line-height: 2em;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;box-sizing: border-box !important;"> 
 <span style="max-width: 100%;font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;background-color: #FFFFFF;font-size: 16px;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;box-sizing: border-box !important;">当悲观情绪升温、其他投资者蒙受损失时，价值投资者可以有出色的表现，而且拥有量化风险控制的价值投资者做得更好。</span> 
</section> 
<section style="margin: 25px 8px;max-width: 100%;white-space: normal;line-height: 2em;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;box-sizing: border-box !important;"> 
 <span style="color: #004EA2;"><strong><span style="color: #004EA2;max-width: 100%;font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;background-color: #FFFFFF;font-size: 16px;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;box-sizing: border-box !important;">《巴伦周刊》：那么你在买哪些别人在抛售的股票？</span></strong></span> 
</section> 
<section style="margin: 25px 8px;max-width: 100%;white-space: normal;line-height: 2em;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;box-sizing: border-box !important;">

 <span style="font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;background-color: #FFFFFF;font-size: 16px;">我们试图确定最佳切入点，因为不能入场太早。在合适的时间增加特定的投资是优秀的投资组合经理与一般的投资组合经理的区别。</span> 
</section> 
<section style="margin: 25px 8px;max-width: 100%;white-space: normal;line-height: 2em;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;box-sizing: border-box !important;"> 
 <span style="max-width: 100%;font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;background-color: #FFFFFF;font-size: 16px;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;box-sizing: border-box !important;">银行股——尤其是欧洲银行股——跌了，因为欧洲距离地缘政治冲突很近，而且一些银行可能拥有俄罗斯、乌克兰和白俄罗斯的敞口。许多银行股已经大幅下跌，估值徘徊在2020年3月的水平，与2012年欧洲金融危机期间的估值差不多，但它们目前的资产负债表明显好于那个时候。</span> 
</section> 
<section style="margin: 25px 8px;max-width: 100%;white-space: normal;line-height: 2em;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;box-sizing: border-box !important;"> 
 <span style="max-width: 100%;font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;background-color: #FFFFFF;font-size: 16px;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;box-sizing: border-box !important;">一些银行已经经历了监管方面的考验，将以股票回购和派息的方式向投资者返还更多的过剩资本和未来利润。但2月24日冲突爆发之后投资者抛售了银行股，这带来了一个非常好的机会。</span> 
</section> 
<section style="margin: 25px 8px;max-width: 100%;white-space: normal;line-height: 2em;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;box-sizing: border-box !important;"> 
 <span style="max-width: 100%;font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;background-color: #FFFFFF;font-size: 16px;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;box-sizing: border-box !important;">在危机中，市场经常根据当前环境给股票定价，就好像当前环境会永远持续下去一样，我们的做法是把它看作一个周期，这是一个非常大的不同之处。</span> 
</section>

 <strong><span style="max-width: 100%;font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;background-color: #FFFFFF;font-size: 16px;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;box-sizing: border-box !important;">萨拉·凯特勒：</span></strong>   
 <span style="max-width: 100%;font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;background-color: #FFFFFF;font-size: 16px;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;box-sizing: border-box !important;">我们买了荷兰国际集团(ING)和裕信银行(UCG.意大利)的股票，这两家银行受到的抛售最严重，股价已经远低于有形账面价值。</span>

<section style="margin: 25px 8px;max-width: 100%;white-space: normal;line-height: 2em;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;box-sizing: border-box !important;"> 
 <span style="color: #004EA2;"><strong><span style="color: #004EA2;max-width: 100%;font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;background-color: #FFFFFF;font-size: 16px;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;box-sizing: border-box !important;">《巴伦周刊》：你还看到了哪些机会？</span></strong></span>

 <strong><span style="max-width: 100%;font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;background-color: #FFFFFF;font-size: 16px;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;box-sizing: border-box !important;">萨拉·凯特勒：</span></strong>   
 <span style="max-width: 100%;font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;background-color: #FFFFFF;font-size: 16px;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;box-sizing: border-box !important;">我们买了一些受疫情冲击严重的股票，因为我们认为这些公司的管理层有能力大幅改善经营状况，当疫情平息时，股价将大幅上涨，利润将比疫情前更高。</span>

<section style="margin: 25px 8px;max-width: 100%;white-space: normal;line-height: 2em;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;box-sizing: border-box !important;">

<section style="margin: 25px 8px;max-width: 100%;white-space: normal;line-height: 2em;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;box-sizing: border-box !important;"> 
 <span style="max-width: 100%;font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;background-color: #FFFFFF;font-size: 16px;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;box-sizing: border-box !important;">阿尔斯通去年进行了一笔不太顺利的收购，在业务整合方面遇到了困难。由于该公司和俄罗斯的关系，我们得以以更低的股价买入。阿尔斯通持有俄罗斯最大的铁道机车和铁路服务公司20%的股份，最新估值为4.8亿欧元（约合5.32亿美元）。即便这部分股份价值降到零，我们预计也不会对阿尔斯通的利润产生影响。我和我的同事认为，阿尔斯通的股价已经充分反应了俄罗斯相关风险，该股被大幅低估了。</span>

 <span style="max-width: 100%;font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;background-color: #FFFFFF;font-size: 16px;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;box-sizing: border-box !important;">在危机中表现出色的领域之一是医疗保健，尤其是制药行业。一些人可能认为它们在疫情期间表现更好，但有些公司是这样，有些则不然。但在地缘政治冲突这样的危机下，拥有巨额现金流和创新的制药公司能给投资组合带来稳定作用，而且一般而言这些公司不存在困扰其他行业的供应链问题和通胀带来的压力。</span>
