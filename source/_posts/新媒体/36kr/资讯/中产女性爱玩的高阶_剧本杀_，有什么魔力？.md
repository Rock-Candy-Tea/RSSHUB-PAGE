</section> 
<section style="font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;margin-left: 16px;margin-right: 16px;"> 
 <span style="font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 15px;">1968年的春天，美国戏剧理论家理查谢克纳在的《TDR戏剧评论》上提出了环境戏剧这一概念。</span> 
</section> 
<section style="font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;margin-left: 16px;margin-right: 16px;"> 
 <span style="font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 15px;">所谓环境戏剧，就是让演员穿梭在观众之间表演，使得观众能够更深层次的沉浸在故事里。观众也不仅局限于座位的观看位置，而是可以随意走动，用不同视角来观演。</span> 
</section> 
<section style="text-align: center;margin-bottom: 0em;margin-left: 16px;margin-right: 16px;"> 
 <img class="rich_pages wxw-img" data-galleryid data-ratio="0.712166172106825" data-s="300,640" data-type="png" data-w="2022" style src="https://img.36krcdn.com/20220504/v2_de1a1b09b1e74674bf4a7b6c4fc029ec_img_000" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"> 
</section> 
<section style="font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;margin-left: 16px;margin-right: 16px;"> 
 <span style="font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 15px;">由此，环境戏剧便演化成了沉浸式剧场，也发展成了如今相对成熟和流行的艺术形式。它将多媒体、虚拟现实、三维实境等高科技手段与传统演出相结合，视听体验更震撼、观演互动更充分。</span>

 <span style="font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 15px;">意犹未尽的她当天便决定二刷。</span> 
</section> 
<section style="font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;margin-left: 16px;margin-right: 16px;"> 
 <span style="font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 15px;">Lisa每个月在沉浸式剧场消费差不多达到两千，基本上每两周会去一次，每次出门仪式感拉满。</span> 
</section> 
<section style="font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;margin-left: 16px;margin-right: 16px;"> 
 <span style="font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 15px;"><img class="rich_pages wxw-img" data-ratio="0.6666666666666666" data-type="jpeg" data-w="720" src="https://img.36krcdn.com/20220504/v2_ab651bad4cbc4b6e8c942197c5021350_img_000" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span> 
</section> 
<section style="font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;margin-left: 16px;margin-right: 16px;"> 
 <span style="font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 15px;"><span style="font-size: 15px;">Lisa印象深刻的是根据泰坦尼克号的故事改编的沉浸式戏剧，剧场直接设在黄浦江的一艘邮轮上，门票就是船票。</span></span> 
</section> 
<section style="font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;margin-left: 16px;margin-right: 16px;"> 
 <span style="font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 15px;">“那天我穿了一条比较华丽的黑色吊带裙，本来还担心自己太夸张，但上船以后很庆幸自己精心打扮过，整个环境像一场盛大的派对，<strong>到场的女士们都是小礼服或者旗袍，恍惚间我都忘了第二天要回归打工人的生活。演出结束，还能在甲板上吹吹风，这种感觉太迷人了</strong>。”</span> 
</section> 
<section style="font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;margin-left: 16px;margin-right: 16px;"> 
 <span style="font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 15px;">虽然近些年剧本杀的舞美设计、服化道也越来越接近话剧的专业水准，<strong>但Sophia看来还是无法替代沉浸式戏剧。</strong></span> 
</section> 
<section style="font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;margin-left: 16px;margin-right: 16px;"> 
 <span style="font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 15px;">“我第一次观看是一场类似《风声》的谍战戏，每个人都有自己的身份，但又不同于普通的剧本杀，整个场地非常大，有整整一层楼，大概二十多个房间，审讯室、军械库一应俱全。演员的一对一互动震撼到我，审问拷打时的惨叫让我头皮发麻。”</span> 
</section> 
<section style="font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;margin-left: 16px;margin-right: 16px;"> 
 <span style="font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 15px;"><img class="rich_pages wxw-img" data-ratio="1.249609984399376" data-type="jpeg" data-w="641" src="https://img.36krcdn.com/20220504/v2_56151429bfeb476a850fc58daf8b0599_img_000" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span> 
</section> 
<section style="font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;margin-left: 16px;margin-right: 16px;"> 
 <span style="font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 15px;">在Sophia看来，沉浸式剧场更重要的是观感和剧情以及演员的互动，而不是像剧本杀那样找线索推理还原故事，场景也更具电影质感。</span> 
</section> 
<section style="font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;margin-left: 16px;margin-right: 16px;"> 
 <span style="font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 15px;">小鱼和她的团队已经称得上是“沉浸式剧场”老手，经常边看剧边团建。</span> 
</section> 
<section style="font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;margin-left: 16px;margin-right: 16px;"> 
 <span style="font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 15px;">“重庆这边儿的沉浸式剧场做的就很不错，比如知名的《重庆1949》，这剧场投资了8个亿，<strong>还是世界上最大的单一室内动态沉浸式表演剧场，现在几乎场场爆满</strong>。</span> 
</section> 
<section style="font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;margin-left: 16px;margin-right: 16px;"> 
 <span style="font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 15px;"><img class="rich_pages wxw-img" data-ratio="0.54" data-type="jpeg" data-w="1000" src="https://img.36krcdn.com/20220504/v2_01949cc9684a40a49b892d497fbea31e_img_000" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span> 
</section> 
<section style="font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;margin-left: 16px;margin-right: 16px;"> 
 <span style="font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 15px;">舞台和观众席分成了五个圆圈，而且还能三百六十度旋转，同一个时间点，不同的舞台在上演不同的剧情。”</span> 
</section> 
<section style="white-space: normal;margin-left: 16px;margin-right: 16px;"> 
 <span style="font-size: 15px;">沉浸式戏剧看多了，也能总结出不少经验。</span> 
</section> 
<section style="font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;margin-left: 16px;margin-right: 16px;"> 
 <span style="font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 15px;">作为过来人，Lisa建议千万不要为了图漂亮穿高跟鞋看沉浸式剧场，因为没有固定座位，又要追着演员跑，运动量比想象中要大很多。</span> 
</section> 
<section style="white-space: normal;font-size: 16px;margin-left: 16px;margin-right: 16px;"> 
 <span style="font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 15px;">阿黄有过观看途中受伤的经历，“沉浸式剧场的剧情总会有个高潮部分，那个时候场面肯定比较激烈一些，有些观众不注意的话就很容易受伤。<strong>我在一次观影时，就被其他观众不小心撞倒，正好旁边有个比较锋利的金属画框，划破了手。”</strong></span> 
</section> 
<section style="font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;margin-left: 16px;margin-right: 16px;"> 
 <span style="font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 15px;">“娱乐性强的沉浸式剧场往往会有人数限制，一场也就二三十个人，不会更多，这样也不拥挤。<strong>我个人不太喜欢那种一场动辄上百人的大剧场，一大堆人一起跑动，太容易受伤了，看剧情也很费力。</strong>” Sofia选择避开大剧场，躲开人群来享受沉浸式的快乐。</span> 
</section> 
<section style="font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;margin-left: 16px;margin-right: 16px;"> 
 <span style="font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 15px;">现在，小鱼和她的伙伴们已经有了很成熟的团队，也在筹划开展自己的创作。</span> 
</section> 
<section style="font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;margin-left: 16px;margin-right: 16px;"> 
 <span style="font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 15px;">“这个行业越来越卷了，做沉浸式剧场的人越来越多，大家的体验也在不断升级，好多地方都是电影大IP，<strong>你看《扬名立万》的拍摄地都被做成沉浸式剧场，不仅有沉浸式体验，还有魔术歌舞表演。</strong>说白了，沉浸式剧场除了贵，真没啥子大缺点。”</span> 
</section>

</section> 
<section style="font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;margin-left: 16px;margin-right: 16px;">

</section> 
<section style="font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;margin-left: 16px;margin-right: 16px;"> 
 <span style="font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 15px;">正如Punchdrunk剧团导演FelixBarrett所说：</span> 
</section> 
<section style="font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;margin-left: 16px;margin-right: 16px;"> 
 <span style="font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 15px;">“我们把演出带出剧场，带入空楼，试图打破一切常规、一切安全公式——像是如何买票，知道如何在观众席入座。<strong>所有现行的规则都消失了，观众将真正处于戏剧之中，从中学习，在更深处与之共振</strong>。”</span> 
</section> 
<section style="font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;margin-left: 16px;margin-right: 16px;"> 
 <span style="font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 15px;">感受不一样的世界，花钱做一场大梦。</span>
