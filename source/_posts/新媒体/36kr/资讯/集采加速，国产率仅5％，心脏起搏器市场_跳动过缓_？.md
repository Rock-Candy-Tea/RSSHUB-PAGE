  <p style="font-family: "PingFang SC", "Lantinghei SC", "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, Arial, "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Zen Hei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", "sans-serif"; text-align: justify; vertical-align: inherit; margin-right: 16px; margin-left: 16px; line-height: 1.75em; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="font-family: "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, "Hiragino Sans GB", "Apple Color Emoji", "Emoji Symbols Font", "Segoe UI Symbol", Arial, sans-serif;">2021年9月8日，京津冀地区“3+N”采购联盟（京津冀黑吉辽蒙晋鲁豫川黔藏等13省）开展冠脉药物球囊类和起搏器类高值耗材带量联动采购，其中山东只参加球囊采购，北京、山西、河南、贵州只参加起搏器采购。</span></p> 
  <p style="font-family: "PingFang SC", "Lantinghei SC", "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, Arial, "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Zen Hei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", "sans-serif"; text-align: justify; vertical-align: inherit; margin-right: 16px; margin-left: 16px; line-height: 1.75em; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="font-family: "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, "Hiragino Sans GB", "Apple Color Emoji", "Emoji Symbols Font", "Segoe UI Symbol", Arial, sans-serif;">随后，在2021年10月18日，陕西省联合甘肃、宁夏、新疆、新疆建设兵团、湖南、广西、海南等地省际联盟印发采购方案，开展双腔起搏器带量采购。</span></p> 
  <p style="font-family: "PingFang SC", "Lantinghei SC", "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, Arial, "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Zen Hei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", "sans-serif"; text-align: justify; vertical-align: inherit; margin-right: 16px; margin-left: 16px; line-height: 1.75em; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="font-family: "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, "Hiragino Sans GB", "Apple Color Emoji", "Emoji Symbols Font", "Segoe UI Symbol", Arial, sans-serif;">其中，在全身兼容MRI（磁共振成像）双腔起搏器中，波士顿科学、<a class="project-link" data-id="514357" data-name="雅培" data-logo="https://img.36krcdn.com/20210813/v2_cc0f1721e16044bdbed306c30e7c02d2_img_000" data-refer-type="2" href="https://36kr.com/projectDetails/514357" target="_blank">雅培</a>、<a class="project-link" data-id="3968938" data-name="美敦力" data-logo="https://img.36krcdn.com/20200916/v2_811751a081924fa9af8741ce120bd7bf_img_png" data-refer-type="2" href="https://36kr.com/projectDetails/3968938" target="_blank">美敦力</a>、百多力拟中选，美敦力报出最低价18871.62元，雅培报出最高价19879.27元；在非兼容MRI起搏器中，创领心律、乐普医学、波士顿科学、雅培、美敦力拟中选，乐普医学报出最低价12895.96元，美敦力报出最高价18190.29元。</span></p>

  <p style="font-family: "PingFang SC", "Lantinghei SC", "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, Arial, "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Zen Hei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", "sans-serif"; text-align: justify; vertical-align: inherit; margin-right: 16px; margin-left: 16px; line-height: 1.75em; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="font-family: "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, "Hiragino Sans GB", "Apple Color Emoji", "Emoji Symbols Font", "Segoe UI Symbol", Arial, sans-serif;">早在2019年，起搏器领域便出现了集采的身影。作为我国起搏器植入大省，江苏省在2019年率先开展了双腔起搏器的集采。首次集采力度温和，平均降幅约为15.86％。而<strong style="font-size: inherit;font-family: inherit;">在2021年5月，江苏省开展第二次双腔起搏器集采，平均降幅高达60％，均价6万元的全身MRI兼容双腔起搏器降价至2万元左右。</strong></span></p> 
  <p style="font-family: "PingFang SC", "Lantinghei SC", "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, Arial, "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Zen Hei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", "sans-serif"; text-align: justify; vertical-align: inherit; margin-right: 16px; margin-left: 16px; line-height: 1.75em; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="font-family: "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, "Hiragino Sans GB", "Apple Color Emoji", "Emoji Symbols Font", "Segoe UI Symbol", Arial, sans-serif;">从以往的集采事件中可见，我国起搏器集采一直处于较温和的状态。但江苏省第二次集采、省际联盟集采的力度都在加强。</span></p> 
  <p style="font-family: "PingFang SC", "Lantinghei SC", "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, Arial, "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Zen Hei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", "sans-serif"; text-align: justify; vertical-align: inherit; margin-right: 16px; margin-left: 16px; line-height: 1.75em; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="font-family: "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, "Hiragino Sans GB", "Apple Color Emoji", "Emoji Symbols Font", "Segoe UI Symbol", Arial, sans-serif;">在集采加速的情况下，起搏器产品自身有两大<a class="project-link" data-id="4263750" data-name="壁垒" data-logo="https://img.36krcdn.com/20210716/v2_63cfc02f48d341b99c257491f917fc19_img_000" data-refer-type="1" href="https://www.36dianping.com/space/4226801031" target="_blank">壁垒</a>——1、<strong style="font-size: inherit;font-family: inherit;">技术门槛高，具有竞争力的创新产品议价空间大</strong>，如2020年山东省集中带量采购起搏器时，有专家就远程监测型、MRI兼容型的双腔起搏器产品跟企业进行议价，13个产品平均降价16.86％，最大降幅31.78％；2、<strong style="font-size: inherit;font-family: inherit;">起搏器产品带有较强的长期服务性质</strong>，售价中包含有管理费、维修费，起搏器厂家需要供养工程师，如果价格下滑太厉害，可能会“吓跑”部分海外企业。</span></p> 
  <p style="font-family: "PingFang SC", "Lantinghei SC", "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, Arial, "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Zen Hei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", "sans-serif"; text-align: justify; vertical-align: inherit; margin-right: 16px; margin-left: 16px; line-height: 1.75em; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="font-family: "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, "Hiragino Sans GB", "Apple Color Emoji", "Emoji Symbols Font", "Segoe UI Symbol", Arial, sans-serif;">因此，起搏器不太可能和冠脉支架一样出现大降价，但利润空间的压缩仍是一大难题。</span></p> 
  <h3 label="二级标题" style="font-family: "PingFang SC", "Lantinghei SC", "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, Arial, "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Zen Hei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", "sans-serif";"><strong mpa-from-tpl="t" mpa-is-content="t" style="letter-spacing: 0px; caret-color: red;">治疗心跳过缓，</strong><strong mpa-from-tpl="t" mpa-is-content="t" style="letter-spacing: 0px; caret-color: red;">双腔起搏器最为常见</strong></h3> 
  <p style="font-family: "PingFang SC", "Lantinghei SC", "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, Arial, "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Zen Hei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", "sans-serif"; text-align: justify; vertical-align: inherit; margin-right: 16px; margin-left: 16px; line-height: 1.75em; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="font-family: "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, "Hiragino Sans GB", "Apple Color Emoji", "Emoji Symbols Font", "Segoe UI Symbol", Arial, sans-serif;">起搏器无疑是二十一世纪杀出的<a class="project-link" data-id="132192" data-name="一匹黑马" data-logo="https://img.36krcdn.com/20210808/v2_8f7b1249db2348a69fb7c9cc80cb45a1_img_000" data-refer-type="2" href="https://36kr.com/projectDetails/132192" target="_blank">一匹黑马</a>。</span></p> 
  <p style="font-family: "PingFang SC", "Lantinghei SC", "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, Arial, "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Zen Hei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", "sans-serif"; text-align: justify; vertical-align: inherit; margin-right: 16px; margin-left: 16px; line-height: 1.75em; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="font-family: "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, "Hiragino Sans GB", "Apple Color Emoji", "Emoji Symbols Font", "Segoe UI Symbol", Arial, sans-serif;">1984年美国职业工程师协会将心脏起搏器与半导体、激光等并列为上半世纪最杰出的十大发明。2001年因特网和起搏器获得美国工程最高奖。2021年4月，美国前副总统彭斯曾接受心脏起搏器手术。</span></p> 
  <p style="font-family: "PingFang SC", "Lantinghei SC", "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, Arial, "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Zen Hei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", "sans-serif"; text-align: justify; vertical-align: inherit; margin-right: 16px; margin-left: 16px; line-height: 1.75em; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="font-family: "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, "Hiragino Sans GB", "Apple Color Emoji", "Emoji Symbols Font", "Segoe UI Symbol", Arial, sans-serif;"><strong style="font-size: inherit;font-family: inherit;">心脏起搏器主要用于治疗心跳过缓的患者，是目前最有效的治疗手段。</strong>心跳过缓可能是由窦房结（SA）、房室结（AV）或心脏传导系统其他部分的功能障碍引起的。这类患者心脏排出量下降，重要脏器及组织供血不足，轻者影响生活，严重者会晕厥甚至是猝死。</span></p> 
  <p style="font-family: "PingFang SC", "Lantinghei SC", "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, Arial, "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Zen Hei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", "sans-serif"; text-align: justify; vertical-align: inherit; margin-right: 16px; margin-left: 16px; line-height: 1.75em; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="font-family: "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, "Hiragino Sans GB", "Apple Color Emoji", "Emoji Symbols Font", "Segoe UI Symbol", Arial, sans-serif;">随着心脏起搏技术发展，<strong style="font-size: inherit;font-family: inherit;">起搏器的功能也从单一治疗缓慢性心律失常发展到治疗心电紊乱（房颤、室颤等）和非心电性疾患（心衰等）。</strong>CRT-P（心脏再同步化治疗双心室起搏器）能纠正室间及室内不同步，从而改善心衰患者的症状并提高生存率。</span></p> 
  <p style="font-family: "PingFang SC", "Lantinghei SC", "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, Arial, "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Zen Hei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", "sans-serif"; text-align: justify; vertical-align: inherit; margin-right: 16px; margin-left: 16px; line-height: 1.75em; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="font-family: "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, "Hiragino Sans GB", "Apple Color Emoji", "Emoji Symbols Font", "Segoe UI Symbol", Arial, sans-serif;">心脏起搏是集生物工程、电子技术与临床于一体的治疗技术。心脏起搏器由脉冲发生器和电极导线组成。其中，脉冲发生器由集成电路和电池组成，内部有复杂的线路，控制着起搏器工作。而电极导线的一端连接到起搏器上，另一端则通过静脉到达心脏，并通过特殊结构固定在心肌壁上。</span></p> 
  <p style="font-family: "PingFang SC", "Lantinghei SC", "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, Arial, "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Zen Hei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", "sans-serif"; text-align: justify; vertical-align: inherit; margin-right: 16px; margin-left: 16px; line-height: 1.75em; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="font-family: "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, "Hiragino Sans GB", "Apple Color Emoji", "Emoji Symbols Font", "Segoe UI Symbol", Arial, sans-serif;"><strong style="font-size: inherit;font-family: inherit;">由脉冲发生器产生的电脉冲，通过电极导线传导到心脏，刺激心脏收缩，从而提高心率，缓解患者由于心动过缓引起的相关症状。</strong></span></p> 
  <p style="font-family: "PingFang SC", "Lantinghei SC", "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, Arial, "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Zen Hei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", "sans-serif"; text-align: justify; vertical-align: inherit; margin-right: 16px; margin-left: 16px; line-height: 1.75em; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="font-family: "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, "Hiragino Sans GB", "Apple Color Emoji", "Emoji Symbols Font", "Segoe UI Symbol", Arial, sans-serif;">根据放置位置的不同，起搏器可分为体外临时心脏起搏器和永久植入式心脏起搏器。临床上主要有两种情况会用到体外临时起搏器，一种是患者既往有缓慢性心律失常，在需要麻醉或手术的时候植入起搏器，防止因心率太低导致的各种症状；另一种是在患者植入永久性起搏器前，作为一个临时性的过渡。</span></p> 
  <p style="font-family: "PingFang SC", "Lantinghei SC", "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, Arial, "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Zen Hei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", "sans-serif"; text-align: justify; vertical-align: inherit; margin-right: 16px; margin-left: 16px; line-height: 1.75em; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="font-family: "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, "Hiragino Sans GB", "Apple Color Emoji", "Emoji Symbols Font", "Segoe UI Symbol", Arial, sans-serif;">而根据电极导线植入部位的差异，起搏器可分为单腔起搏器、双腔起搏器、三腔起搏器、四腔起搏器，其中单腔起搏器可分为VVI起搏器（电极导线的顶端电极置入在右室心尖部的肌小梁处）和AAI 起搏器（电极导线的顶端电极置入右心耳的梳状肌处）。</span></p> 
  <p style="font-family: "PingFang SC", "Lantinghei SC", "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, Arial, "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Zen Hei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", "sans-serif"; text-align: justify; vertical-align: inherit; margin-right: 16px; margin-left: 16px; line-height: 1.75em; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="font-family: "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, "Hiragino Sans GB", "Apple Color Emoji", "Emoji Symbols Font", "Segoe UI Symbol", Arial, sans-serif;">自1958年第一台起搏器植入后，目前全球每年约有上百万患者接受起搏治疗。<strong style="font-size: inherit;font-family: inherit;">根据《中国心血管健康与疾病报告2020》，我国2019年植入起搏器的患者比2018年增长9.3％，双腔起搏器占比近70％。</strong>在起搏器植入适应症方面，病态窦房结综合征的比例占50.08％，房室传导阻滞占43.52％，其他起搏器适应证占6.4％。</span></p>

  <p style="font-family: "PingFang SC", "Lantinghei SC", "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, Arial, "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Zen Hei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", "sans-serif"; text-align: justify; vertical-align: inherit; margin-right: 16px; margin-left: 16px; line-height: 1.75em; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="font-family: "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, "Hiragino Sans GB", "Apple Color Emoji", "Emoji Symbols Font", "Segoe UI Symbol", Arial, sans-serif;">中美两国的心律失常发病率大致相同，但中国的人口总量远高于美国。然而，在美国每百万人中有1000人植入心脏起搏器，而在中国每百万人中仅有60人植入起搏器。</span></p>

  <h3 label="二级标题" style><font face="Helvetica Neue, Helvetica, Hiragino Sans GB, Apple Color Emoji, Emoji Symbols Font, Segoe UI Symbol, Arial, sans-serif">乐普医疗</font></h3>

  <p style="font-family: "PingFang SC", "Lantinghei SC", "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, Arial, "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Zen Hei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", "sans-serif"; text-align: justify; vertical-align: inherit; margin-right: 16px; margin-left: 16px; line-height: 1.75em; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="font-family: "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, "Hiragino Sans GB", "Apple Color Emoji", "Emoji Symbols Font", "Segoe UI Symbol", Arial, sans-serif;">在三家头部企业之外，目前尚未有其它国产企业的起搏器产品获批或进入创新医疗器械审批程序。与大批量进入国内市场的海外企业相比，我国本土创新较匮乏。在高技术门槛的起搏器领域，进口垄断、集采加速，国内企业的生存空间似乎开始收窄，起搏器市场“跳动过缓”。</span></p>
