<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">其实回过头来看，在《人世间》之前国产剧从来没有停止过制造属于自己的“文化IP”。</span></p>

<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">比如</span><strong><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">新加坡版的《神雕侠侣》《白蛇后传》《东游记》和越南版的《射雕英雄传》</span></strong><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">等，质量也是见仁见智。</span></p>

<h2 label="一级标题" style><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 17px;letter-spacing: 0.5px;color: #485BF7;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">01 “中国文化”出海正当时</span></h2> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">2021年8月的一份在线调查显示，约有76%的全球受访者在过去两年内看过中国电视剧，其中43%的人已经养成了定期在流媒体平台上“打卡”看中国电视剧的习惯。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">在泰国、新加坡、马来西亚等亚洲国家，超过四成的人认为在流媒体平台上中文内容是不可或缺的部分。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">此外，在</span><strong><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">最受越南网友喜爱的十部海外电视剧榜单上</span></strong><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">，中国电视剧《以家人之名》、《三生三世十里桃花》、《离人心上》、《楼下女友请签收》、《冰糖炖雪梨》和《传闻中的陈芊芊》拿下6席。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">根据官方统计数据显示，2015年中国总计出口381部电视剧，价值3.77亿元，出口额自2008年以来首次超过进口额，紧接着2016年为5.1亿元，2017年为6.33亿元。</span></p>

<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">泰国视频平台MONOMAX购买的国产剧也有数十部。这些销往海外的并非全是老剧，不少剧集刚刚在国内播出，转头就在海外平台新鲜上架，比如2021年播出的《司藤》、《你是我的城池营垒》和《心跳源计划》等剧。</span></p>

<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">如果换一个时间维度</span><strong><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">，以目前的市场看，国产剧版权费仍然处于相对低廉的状态</span></strong><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">2021年最火热电视剧之一《长歌行》，在海外发行版权费15万美元一集。</span></p>

<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">此外，</span><strong><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">在亚洲地区，国产剧仍面临着韩剧这一强大的对手。</span></strong></p>

<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">那么，低版权费用又该如何改变呢？</span></p>

<h2 label="一级标题" style><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 17px;letter-spacing: 0.5px;color: #485BF7;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">03 国产剧离“出圈”还有多远？</span></h2> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">《人世间》被网友评为</span><strong><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">中国版的“请回答1988”</span></strong><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">，献上自来水的口评和推荐。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">但是这个以中国改革开放为背景，观察东北工人的典型中国式情感的剧集，怎么改变才能被国外观众共情？擅长动画制作的迪士尼能把握好细腻的情感吗？</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">这些都值得等待。</span></p> 
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class="rich_pages wxw-img js_insertlocalimg" data-ratio="0.43800178412132024" data-s="300,640" data-type="png" data-w="1121" style src="https://img.36krcdn.com/20220301/v2_ee12ddde56044f44b789b28f694d0686_img_000" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">而</span><strong><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">年代剧集在出海这条路上并不容易。</span></strong></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">根据统计，韩国TVING和泰国MONOMAX的国产剧版权购买情况，发现古装剧依然是出口的大头，比例分别占到了66.42%和40.24%。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">从2019年中国电视剧行业剧集TOP30类型分布来看，</span><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong>古装类型占比最高为37%；其次是都市剧占比为27%。</strong></span></p>

<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">诚然，如果想要获得更多海外市场对国产剧的认可，那么考虑海外“需求”将成为必要条件，但弊端也同样存在。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">对于本土用户而言，都市剧仍然存在大部分需求，一旦国内选择以古装剧为主，发展海外市场，则会失去一部分的本土用户。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">而且</span><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong>即使是古装剧，也并非屡试不爽</strong></span><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">国内口碑爆款正午阳光的男频古装剧<strong>《琅琊榜》</strong>，在北美市场上IMDb评分人数刚刚破千。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">国人眼中的宫斗剧天花板</span><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong>《甄嬛传》</strong></span><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">2015年上线Netflix，为了迎合北美市场观剧节奏，从76集缩减至6集，内容主线不完整，造成海外观众对这种含蓄冗长的宫斗戏难以理解。</span></p> 
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class="rich_pages wxw-img js_insertlocalimg" data-ratio="0.5559265442404007" data-s="300,640" data-type="png" data-w="599" style src="https://img.36krcdn.com/20220301/v2_89f7690ce3bc4b7888169625b0d38057_img_000" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">不仅是内容上文化差异造成了壁垒，制作、发行也成为制约中国剧集出海的原因。</span></strong></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">虽然，现阶段不少影视公司纷纷实施自己的国际化战略，如万达通过收购北美第二大院线AMC、澳大利亚第二大院线Hoyts以及美国传奇影业，来获得海外的影视制作经验，扩充自身在海外的营销发行渠道。</span></p> 
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class="rich_pages wxw-img js_insertlocalimg" data-ratio="0.5721812434141201" data-s="300,640" data-type="png" data-w="949" style src="https://img.36krcdn.com/20220301/v2_993ed6e3100b4337845f916545ce3756_img_000" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">此外，部分影视公司也纷纷在美国等地设立分公司，或是采取与海外公司合作制作影视作品的方式，以此了解并逐渐进入海外市场。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">但在欧美国家，目前国产影视作品还没有真正达到主流地位，因为国产影视作品的工业水准、播出方式和制作方式都与欧美不同，文化也存在一定差异，这对于国产剧同样是不可避免的挑战。</span></p> 
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class="rich_pages wxw-img js_insertlocalimg" data-ratio="0.6375" data-s="300,640" data-type="png" data-w="1280" style src="https://img.36krcdn.com/20220301/v2_d4c67566679a4b519d89de402136b365_img_000" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">换个角度看，如果面对海外市场，仅仅是发展单一类型的影视剧，那么未来国产剧在市场发展存在不利，为此最好的方式莫过于，扎实发展本土用户对影视剧的需求，其次发展海外。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">因为对于一部影视作品而言，</span><strong><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">若想获得观众认可关键还是内容质量需要过硬。</span></strong></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">尤其是进入已经拥有高度发达并成熟的工业体系的美国等市场，在剧情安排、拍摄技法、后期制作等方面均需保质保量。</span></p> 
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class="rich_pages wxw-img js_insertlocalimg" data-ratio="1.3261538461538462" data-s="300,640" data-type="png" data-w="650" style src="https://img.36krcdn.com/20220301/v2_22ad58f79be84228bbdbb01af1cea172_img_000" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">此外，目前改编热门网络小说绝不是长久之计，要想真正在国际市场上立足，还是需要改变目前的改编手段，将更多的精力投入到原创高质量剧本。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;letter-spacing: 0.544px;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Arial, sans-serif;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: #606060;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1.2px;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">虽然这段路并不好走，但绝不会输。</span></p>
