<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">有一部作品：（1）双方不是夫妻，却假装夫妻在同一个屋檐下生活；（2）有一个不是双方亲生的小孩作伴；（3）男方身负绝技，隐藏身份低调生活；（4）女方有个弟弟，还要来视察假结婚的婚姻状况；（5）各种势力交错混杂，却是一部搞笑日常作品。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><strong><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;color: #009C99;">请问这部作品是：A《间谍过家家》/ B《武林外传》。</span></strong></p>

<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">以上是动画《间谍<a class="project-link" data-id="1679775013245697" data-name="过家家" data-logo="https://img.36krcdn.com/20220401/v2_602f0edd77424621a399bcc75cba80e5_img_000" data-refer-type="1" href="https://36kr.com/project/1679775013245697" target="_blank">过家家</a>》播出后网友们流传的新段子。这部由霸权社WIT STUDIO和CLOVERWORKS制作、改编自远藤达哉同名漫画作品的4月新番，早在预告阶段就未播先火，备受期待。首话播出后，不论日本本土还是欧美国家，《间谍过家家》都达成了</span><strong><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;color: #009C99;">“落地即霸权”</span></strong><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">的成就。即便国内延迟播出大半个月，《间谍过家家》仍然在非独播情况下顺利达成B站首集首周最高纪录。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">目前，《间谍过家家》已在海外更新至第八集，国内更新至第五集，豆瓣评分稳定保持在9分以上，讨论越发高涨，已呈破圈之势。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">《间谍过家家》讲了一个怎样的故事？它因何赢得全世界观众的喜爱？市场为何需要这样的作品？</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;color: #888888;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: underline;">本文涉及少量剧透。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: center;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;"><img class="rich_pages wxw-img" data-galleryid data-ratio="0.18692307692307691" data-s="300,640" data-type="png" data-w="2600" style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;max-width: 100%;height: auto !important;vertical-align: bottom;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: center;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;" src="https://img.36krcdn.com/20220603/v2_0b3457701344493cad28b99c771bb5d1_img_000" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span><span style="font-size: 16px;"><strong><span style="font-size: 16px;margin: 0px;padding: 0px;color: #009C99;">优秀的故事基础</span></strong></span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">《间谍过家家》是那种靠几句话就能勾起观众好奇心的作品。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">一个间谍为完成任务不得已组建家庭，于是他领养了一个女儿，假结婚获得了一位妻子。而他并不知道，女儿有读心的超能力，妻子是战斗力爆表的杀手。他们互相隐藏着自己的秘密，在看似暗流涌动、实则温馨搞笑的日常中，渐渐发展出真正的家人感情。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><strong><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;color: #009C99;">强设定是《间谍过家家》的第一个巨大卖点，</span></strong><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">也是这部作品仅靠故事简介就能瞬间吸引观众注意的重要原因。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">从主人公身份上看，</span><strong><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;color: #009C99;">间谍、杀手、超能力者</span></strong><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">——商业大片最青睐这类“反日常系”设定，他们是天生的剧情“提味剂”，代表着可以让观众屏住呼吸的勾心斗角、波谲云诡，可以一饱眼福的飙车与枪战。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">《间谍过家家》的开局就是一场经典的街头情报交换，引出了男主人公“黄昏”的身份。作为一个熟练使用上百副面孔的顶级间谍，在最新的任务中却迎来了一个史诗级难题，组织要求他一周之内“生一个孩子”。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">原来，为接触这一次的目标对象，组织需要“黄昏”组建家庭。借助目标对象次子就读学院举办的联谊会，与目标对象建立联系。为此，“黄昏”去福利院领养了一个年纪合适的女孩阿妮亚，而为了顺利通过学院严格的考核，“黄昏”必须快速为女儿找到一位母亲。表面上是公职人员的单身女性约尔，为隐藏自己的杀手工作，刚好也需要通过结婚摆脱单身女性的身份。由此，二人不谋而合组建家庭。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">然而，间谍与杀手组成的奇葩家庭中，特殊的不只是这对“父母”。这一切秘密都被男主领养的女儿阿妮亚早早窥破。作为战争试验品的她，拥有读心的超能力，假夫妻双人组费尽心机隐藏的绝密，都被她尽收眼底。因此，网友们亲切地称其为</span><strong><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;color: #009C99;">“瓜神”</span></strong><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">，即走在“吃瓜”一线、站在信息“食物链”顶端的人物。</span></p>

<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: center;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;color: #888888;font-size: 14px;"> 《间谍过家家》</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">单从人物身份来说，《间谍过家家》的内容很容易显得太满。</span><strong><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;color: #009C99;">把这些较难驾驭且时常被过度使用的商业元素堆积到一起，通常情况下，观众们迎来的不是神作，而是烂片。</span></strong></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">然而，</span><strong><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;color: #009C99;">精巧的错位设计</span></strong><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">让《间谍过家家》成功在“套路化”的元素中脱颖而出。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">如果顶尖杀手“妈妈”实际上是温柔脱线的天然呆，读心超能力“女儿”实际上是热衷于看间谍题材动画片、智商不够内心戏来凑的超级萌娃。而拥有这两位合作伙伴的完美间谍“爸爸”，在任务过程中频频遭遇职业生涯“滑铁卢”。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">智商、武力、超能力——这一家人的“技能树”已经点满，具备一切实现任务的硬性条件，却因人物设定之间的错位和严重的信息差，演绎出时而耍帅、时而搞笑、时而温馨的嘀笑皆非的故事。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><strong><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;color: #009C99;">最重要的是，主人公们在“过家家”的情景中逐渐成为真正的家人。</span></strong><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">可以想见，“假戏真做”的爱情和亲情，未来都势必要经历来自真相和立场的考验。在反套路情节的惊喜之余，其实观众们真正等待的并非是故事目标的实现，而是渴望跟随着主人公们情感走向的变化，品味日常与反日常的跳跃交织，感受生活和家庭的可贵。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">用强设定元素刺激市场口味，用错位设计架构起故事核心、用普世的情感诉求引发观众共鸣，优秀的故事基础，已经决定了《间谍过家家》绝不可能是一部默默无闻的作品。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;"><img class="rich_pages wxw-img" data-galleryid data-ratio="0.18692307692307691" data-s="300,640" data-type="png" data-w="2600" style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;max-width: 100%;height: auto !important;vertical-align: bottom;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: center;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;" src="https://img.36krcdn.com/20220603/v2_cd5cd427776c4e6a9ec7741c4b6e199f_img_000" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: center;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="font-size: 16px;"><strong><span style="font-size: 16px;margin: 0px;padding: 0px;color: #009C99;">能打的漫画原作</span></strong></span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">5月22日，哔哩哔哩番剧官方微博宣布，《间谍过家家》开播22天播放量破亿，刷新了B站日本动画最快破亿纪录。这一纪录的前任保持者是2020年底大热的《咒术回战》，开播63天播放量破亿，而在B站审核延迟播放的情况下，《间谍过家家》仍然展现出碾压级别的破亿速度，令人叹为观止。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">在欧美国家，《间谍过家家》同样是大爆作品，首集破万，评分接近满分，刷新了欧美Reddit论坛榜单的新番纪录。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">5月30日，《间谍过家家》的原作漫画宣布单行本销量累计突破2100万部的消息。而在TV动画开播时该漫画销量为1250万部，即动画播出后两个月内，原作销量增加了850万部。对比《咒术回战》，2020年10月动画开播时原作销量为850万部，两个月后突破1500万部，可见《间谍过家家》动画化后扩大影响力的速度。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">即便动画如此火爆，《间谍过家家》播出后，仍有许多网友表示动画质量并未达到期待值。这种声音的出现，源于《间谍过家家》漫画原作的高质量和高人气。在</span><strong><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;color: #009C99;">刺猬公社（ID：ciweigongshe）</span></strong><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">的小范围调研中，粉丝们多认为《间谍过家家》的动画制作可列入一流梯队，而其漫画堪称该类型中的TOP级别。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">《间谍过家家》的原作是远藤达哉于2019年3月25日在网络漫画平台“少年Jump+”上开始连载的漫画，被视为是“少年Jump+”人气第一的王牌作品。早在连载初期，《间谍过家家》就吸引了大量关注，首话评论2000多条，第五话月阅览数突破300万。2021年，《间谍过家家》销量突破千万，打破了“少年Jump+”的作品销量纪录。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><strong><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;color: #009C99;">《间谍过家家》是作者远藤达哉在低谷期的一部康复之作。</span></strong><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">在《间谍过家家》之前，他曾创作《TISTA魔眼修女》《月华美刃》等暗黑风格向作品。《月华美刃》完结后，远藤达哉在很长一段时间里都只担任其他漫画家的助手，无法开启连载。在这样的情况下，其编辑林士平鼓励他在新作中作出改变，阻止远藤达哉接连创作黑暗向内容，《间谍过家家》由此变成了一部欢乐向漫画。</span></p> 
<p style="text-align: center;margin-bottom: 0em;margin-left: 8px;margin-right: 8px;"><img class="rich_pages wxw-img" data-galleryid data-ratio="1.6843657817109146" data-s="300,640" data-type="jpeg" data-w="1017" style src="https://img.36krcdn.com/20220603/v2_04949d7ac36a4d889c6e66f307736c51_img_000" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: center;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="color: #888888;font-size: 14px;">《间谍过家家》“名场面”</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">然而，如果翻开《间谍过家家》搞笑日常的背面，观众会发现它并不是一部纯粹的喜剧作品。恰恰相反，其故事背景和人物内核都是沉重的悲剧。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">作品设置在架空世界背景下，男主的父母和孩童时代的朋友都是死于战争，为了建立“让小孩子不再哭泣的世界”，男主选择成为间谍。女主同样年幼失去父母，独自一人艰难抚养弟弟，为了赚钱常年接受杀手训练，极度缺乏生活常识。而最为可爱天真的女儿则是战争实验的牺牲品，被神秘组织改造，又被多个抚养家庭抛弃。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">在近期公开的一段访谈记录中，远藤达哉提到：在《间谍过家家》的创作中，他放弃了原本想画的东西，而去描绘大家想看的内容，因此他并不喜欢漫画中的角色。然而，在漫画最近的更新中，粉丝们仍然看到远藤达哉花费了大量笔墨，展开男主黄昏的背景故事，借人物之口表达自己想要讲述的“反战”主题。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">从市场角度看，间谍是“佐料”，过家家是“主菜”。而从原作内容出发，作者最本质的表达欲仍然在冰山之下不时喷涌，供读者和观众想象与窥探。</span><strong><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;color: #009C99;">以悲剧为内核，以喜剧为外衣，让《间谍过家家》并未流俗于一部“快餐型”作品。</span></strong></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><strong><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;color: #009C99;"><img class="rich_pages wxw-img" data-galleryid data-ratio="0.18692307692307691" data-s="300,640" data-type="png" data-w="2600" style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;max-width: 100%;height: auto !important;vertical-align: bottom;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: center;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;" src="https://img.36krcdn.com/20220603/v2_35869951ff1d48e68e45fd84b03a396b_img_000" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></strong></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: center;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><strong><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;color: #009C99;font-size: 16px;">国民级潜力</span></strong></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">回忆上一个引发全网追更的动画番剧，还是2022年1月份的《国王排名》。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">《国王排名》主人公“波吉”符号化的人物形象，标志性的王冠和“V型微笑”，善良弱小的聋哑王子设定，让有关“波吉”的表情包在网络上疯狂传播，大范围破圈。然而，在网友们纷纷“破防”，作品频登热搜榜单之后，《国王排名》却高开低走、全线崩盘。故事发展到后期，B站《国王排名》刷屏的弹幕已经</span><strong><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;color: #009C99;">从“波吉好可爱”变成了“我不理解”</span></strong><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">，豆瓣评分从9.6分降至8.2分。</span></p> 
<p style="text-align: center;margin-bottom: 0em;margin-left: 8px;margin-right: 8px;"><img class="rich_pages wxw-img" data-galleryid data-ratio="0.5616641901931649" data-s="300,640" data-type="png" data-w="1346" style src="https://img.36krcdn.com/20220603/v2_01cc50a77e8a4a58acf689120ceec844_img_000" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p> 
<p style="text-align: center;margin-bottom: 0em;margin-left: 8px;margin-right: 8px;"><span style="text-align: justify;color: #888888;font-size: 14px;">《国王排名》</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">相对于《国王排名》，《间谍过家家》中并没有如波吉般高度符号化的角色。目前传播度最广的超能力女儿阿妮亚，虽然赢得无数观众的喜爱，但若不结合《间谍过家家》的故事内容，阿妮亚也只是一个在动漫中随处可见的萌系女孩形象，本质上并不具备出圈属性。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">即便《间谍过家家》还并未达到当时《国王排名》的出圈和传播效果，但目前作品的原作质量、口碑稳定度和热度增长趋势显然比后者更抗打。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><strong><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;color: #009C99;">如果说，《国王排名》是以“波吉历险记”为主要思路的子供向作品和成年人“童话”，那么《间谍过家家》则是以家庭为单位讲述的轻喜剧故事。</span></strong><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">不少网友将其与《家有儿女》《武林外传》《我爱我家》《蜡笔小新》《樱桃小丸子》等作品同时提及，把《间谍过家家》当作是这些经典作品的“间谍版”“新代餐”。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">正如本文开头的选择题，观众们巧妙地总结了《间谍过家家》与《武林外传》的共性，同样，《间谍过家家》和《家有儿女》也存在类似的共性：</span><strong><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;color: #009C99;">重组式家庭。</span></strong></p> 
<p style="text-align: center;margin-bottom: 0em;margin-left: 8px;margin-right: 8px;"><img class="rich_pages wxw-img" data-galleryid data-ratio="1.493421052631579" data-s="300,640" data-type="png" data-w="912" style src="https://img.36krcdn.com/20220603/v2_df9a8e26791b42e6bdb75c4e0956330a_img_000" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: center;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="color: #888888;font-size: 14px;margin: 0px;padding: 0px;">《家有儿女》</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">这类故事的优势在于，家庭中的每一个成员都带着各自的过去和心结，不同成员之间也面临着个人利益、家庭角色，乃至价值观之间的冲突，但他们仍然愿意融入到这个新集体之中，贡献力量，付出感情，相互取暖。他们可能都曾伤痕累累，却对共同创建美好的未来怀抱希望。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><strong><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;color: #009C99;">在这样的优势下，如果人物角色经得起推敲，故事就会在轻快的日常中丰满起来，让观众跟随着剧中人的悲喜而哭哭笑笑，甚至成为某一代人的集体性记忆。</span></strong><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">这类作品可能不会如热点爆款般迅速点燃网民的激情，却能被反复观看、长期咀嚼，具备国民级潜力。《间谍过家家》收获的全球热度，也在展示这类家庭轻喜剧作品的市场价值和普适意义。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">当然，《间谍过家家》原作目前还正在连载中，其剧情发展也在面临诸多观众的审视和市场的考验。但至少从当下情况来看，《间谍过家家》已经成为这一类型里绕不过的一部作品。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">《间谍过家家》动画的片尾曲是由星野源创作并演唱的。这首名为《喜剧》的歌曲，照应着这部作品欢乐日常表面下的忧伤与光亮。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">在这闹剧般的星球上，自诞生之日起，我便格格不入、泪水枯竭。直至我们辗转相逢，彼此怀揣秘密，佯装成普通<a class="project-link" data-id="1678518176330758" data-name="模样" data-logo="https://img.36krcdn.com/20220331/v2_fcf216cff9744f5890ec5cfbae409e5c_img_000" data-refer-type="1" href="https://36kr.com/project/1678518176330758" target="_blank">模样</a>走到一起。</span></p> 
<p style="margin: 0px 8px;padding: 0px;clear: both;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(51, 51, 51);font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, "system-ui", "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;font-size: 17px;font-style: normal;font-variant-ligatures: normal;font-variant-caps: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-align: justify;text-indent: 0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0px;-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px;text-decoration-thickness: initial;text-decoration-style: initial;text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="margin: 0px;padding: 0px;font-size: 15px;">那一天，我们在彼此心底，缔结下比血缘更为深厚的契约。我们手牵着手一起回家，问着“今天要吃点什么”的问题。</span><strong style="color: rgb(0, 156, 153);"><span style="font-size: 15px;">无论何时，和你在一起，便是喜剧。</span></strong></p>
