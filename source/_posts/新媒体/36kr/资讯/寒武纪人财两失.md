<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">一家行业里的独角兽，从声名鹊起到千夫所指，需要多久？拥有“AI芯片第一股”光环的寒武纪，只用了不到2年。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">3月15日，中科寒武纪科技股份有限公司发布了关于核心技术人员离职的公告，内容显示，核心技术人员梁军先生因与公司存在分歧，通知公司解除劳动合同。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">或受该消息影响，截至当日收盘，寒武纪股价报收66.02元/股，下跌18.38%。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">更不妙的是，大股东们信心渐失。龙虎榜数据显示，当日机构合计净卖出2257.96万元。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">倘若拉长时间线来看，寒武纪2020年7月上市首日开盘曾上涨290%，市值一度高达1194亿元，但好景不长，此后股价一路下挫，截至昨日总市值仅为254.12亿元，较最高点跌去约940亿元。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">一份公告带来波及面如此广的余震，势必在内容本身外，有更复杂的不利因素藏在表面之下。</span></strong></p> 
<h3 label="二级标题" style>存地留人？</h3> 
<p></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">依公告所示，梁军将不再担任寒武纪任何职务。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">梁军是谁？公开资料显示，他曾就职于<a class="project-link" data-id="25167" data-name="华为" data-logo="https://img.36krcdn.com/20210806/v2_cdf6feb963574c9cb91b2818615c6072_img_000" data-refer-type="2" href="https://36kr.com/projectDetails/25167" target="_blank">华为</a>、海思，是从业近20年的芯片架构专家，曾作为主架构师完成了多款高端复杂SoC芯片的架构设计，累计量产芯片超亿颗。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">2017年起，梁军入职寒武纪，任副总经理兼首席技术官。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">值得注意的是，早在今年1月18日就有公开信息显示，梁军向寒武纪公司董事会申请辞去副总经理兼首席技术官职务，担任前瞻芯片技术创新中心首席专家职务，继续从事技术创新工作。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">结合此次公告，意味着梁军彻底离开寒武纪。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">一次简单的人事变更，在业内造成如此影响力，梁军其人势必有特殊之处。</span></strong></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">众所周知，寒武纪之所以能在业界引起关注，乃至成功奔赴科创板，除了背后的资本推动，还与华为密切相关。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">2017年，华为旗舰手机Mate 10中的麒麟970芯片，集成了寒武纪的智能处理器IP产品1A，在AI芯片产品落地困难的大环境下，寒武纪借此实现了商业化落地。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">对此，为了避嫌，在此前回复<a class="project-link" data-id="120849" data-name="上海证券" data-logo="https://img.36krcdn.com/20210808/v2_3c1ce551589c41998f4d9fa653d9f233_img_000" data-refer-type="2" href="https://36kr.com/projectDetails/120849" target="_blank">上海证券</a>交易所《发行人及保荐机构回复意见》中，寒武纪特意把梁军与华为海思撇清关系。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">但作为华为旧部，在梁军加入的第一年，寒武纪就与华为终端芯片展开IP授权。有理由相信，这起合作中，他本人或起到关键作用。</span></p>

<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;text-indent: 0em;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;">考虑到自2019年来，华为未与寒武纪达成新的合作，自研战略之下，未来华为与寒武纪续缘的可能性也较小。</span><span style="font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;text-indent: 0em;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;">因此，在一些外部解读中，梁军或已成了“弃子”。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">不过，除了这种捕风捉影的说法，客观来看此次人事变动，还意味着寒武纪的核心技术人员目前仅剩三人，分别是陈天石、刘少礼、刘道福。</span></strong></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">因此，上述公告中特意提到：</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">公司已经建立了完备的研发体系，形成了专业的研发队伍，储备了丰富的专利技术，梁军先生的离职不会影响公司的技术创新，不会对公司整体研发实力产生重大不利影响。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">为了补充该论调，公告中特意摆出近三年来研发人员数量增长趋势：2019年12月31日，研发人员数量为680人；截至本公告披露日，研发人员数量为1228人，研发人员占总员工人数比例达80.37%。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">但需要了解的是，无数次经验证明，在科技创新和前沿领域，一个核心技术人才的作用是不可替代的，数量并不能覆盖质量上的差距。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">巧合的是，放之整个芯片行业来看，近年来频出企业高层“内斗”事件。</span></strong></p>

<h3 label="二级标题" style><span style="color: #262626; font-family: PingFangSC-Regular; letter-spacing: 0px; text-indent: 0em;"> “云边端”围城</span></h3>

<p><span style="letter-spacing: 0px; text-indent: 0em; font-size: 18px; font-weight: 600;">核心人员变更之外，寒武纪同样存在经营层面的隐忧。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif; text-indent: 0em;">2022年2月26日，寒武纪公布了2021 年度业绩快报，在报告期内，2021年营业总收入为7.21亿元，同比增长57.12%；归母净利润亏损达到8.47亿元，较上年同期亏损扩大94.98%。</span><br></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">在科创板同期几十家上市公司公布的财报数据中，寒武纪成为“亏损王” 。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">在此前的2018-2020年，寒武纪归母净利润亏损为0.41亿元、11.79亿元和4.35亿元。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">上述报告中，寒武纪把主要亏损原因归因于研发费用、管理费用中股份支付、销售费用的增长所致。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">一直以来，研发支出较大都是寒武纪持续亏损的主要原因之一。</span></strong></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">数据显示，2017年至2020年，寒武纪研发费用分别为 2986.19 万元、2.40亿元、5.43亿元 、7.68亿元，占营收比重分别为 380.73%、205.18%、 122.32%和167.38%。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">但在高额的研发投入外，寒武纪的产品线并没有足够的商业化落地能力。</span></p>

<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">先看终端，这块业务的销售对象主要是华为等手机厂商。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">数据显示，2017-2019年寒武纪对华为海思销售收入为771万、1.17亿、6877万，占到整个营业收入的98.95%、99.69%、15.49%。</span></p>

<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">在此前提下，边缘智能芯片及加速卡业务或难逃这般命运。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">2021年上半年，该业务收入8374.38万，占整个营业收入60.79%。国金证券测算，2021年全年，边缘智能芯片及加速卡业务仍贡献超过公司一半的营收。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">整体而言，在面临着像英伟达、英特尔等传统芯片厂商、跨界自研芯片科技巨头、初创芯片公司的多重竞争局面下，寒武纪需要围绕“云边端”四处救火。</span></strong></p> 
<h3 label="二级标题" style><strong><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">向何处去？</span></strong><br></h3> 
<p></p> 
<p label="正文" style><span style="font-size: 18px; font-weight: 600; color: #333333; font-family: "Segoe UI", "Lucida Grande", Helvetica, Arial, "Microsoft YaHei", FreeSans, Arimo, "Droid Sans", "wenquanyi micro hei", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Hiragino Sans GB W3", FontAwesome, sans-serif;">“云边端”陷入窘境后，寒武纪不得不找寻新的发力点。</span></p> 
<p label="正文" style><span style="font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 1px; color: #222222; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif; text-indent: 0em;">2021年1月，寒武纪创办了全资子公司寒武纪行歌（南京）科技有限公司，并借此正式开展车载智能</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">芯片相关业务。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">同年6月22日，寒武纪发布公告称，行歌科技注册资本拟由0.3亿元增至2.0亿元，未来将发展车载智能芯片。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">此后在2021世界人工智能大会上，陈天石披露寒武纪行歌车载智能芯片的关键数据：超200TOPS AI性能、7nm制程、车规级、独立安全岛、成熟软件工具链等等。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">对于寒武纪而言，其业务布局正在从“云边端”走向“云边端车”，但想要依靠此次布局进入此前未涉猎的汽车市场，或许并不是良方。</span></strong></p>

<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">现阶段，新能源汽车硬件配置升级迅速，车用计算芯片的算力将不断升级。作为智能驾驶核心硬件的芯片，自然引来一众目光。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">纵览全行业，智能驾驶芯片行业参与者较为多样化。有以恩智浦、德州仪器为代表的传统汽车芯片厂商，有高通、<a class="project-link" data-id="3969182" data-name="英伟达" data-logo="https://img.36krcdn.com/20200916/v2_811751a081924fa9af8741ce120bd7bf_img_png" data-refer-type="2" href="https://36kr.com/projectDetails/3969182" target="_blank">英伟达</a>等电子芯片巨头，有以<a class="project-link" data-id="132410" data-name="特斯拉" data-logo="https://img.36krcdn.com/20200729/v2_e76e3d3d44c440138f072b13bc84a6dc_img_000" data-refer-type="2" href="https://36kr.com/projectDetails/132410" target="_blank">特斯拉</a>为代表的车企，以及以地平线为代表的创业公司。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">论排交椅来看，<a class="project-link" data-id="3968654" data-name="英特尔" data-logo="https://img.36krcdn.com/20200916/v2_811751a081924fa9af8741ce120bd7bf_img_png" data-refer-type="2" href="https://36kr.com/projectDetails/3968654" target="_blank">英特尔</a>、英伟达等是较早布局智能驾驶芯片的企业，占据了全球智能驾驶芯片市场的较大份额，属于汽车芯片市场的第一梯队。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">高通和华为位于第二梯队，第三梯队则是其他的第三方芯片厂商，其中就包括地平线、云途、黑芝麻等国产公司。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">除此之外，越来越多的车企也开始尝试自主研发智能驾驶芯片。特斯拉就是其中的先行者。2019年，特斯拉抛弃英伟达，推出自研的14纳米自动驾驶芯片。这一度影响国内的造车新势力。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">结合已有资料，寒武纪仅可排于第三梯队中。</span></strong></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">现阶段，国内正在上演一股国产汽车芯片替代潮，有诸多厂商正在想办法代替国外芯片。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">巨头不可能吃掉所有市场，这自然是独角兽的机会，以地平线为例，2021年5月，<a class="project-link" data-id="66453" data-name="理想汽车" data-logo="https://img.36krcdn.com/20210807/v2_b75ffc526b0e4d938d580e2b3a18207c_img_000" data-refer-type="2" href="https://36kr.com/projectDetails/66453" target="_blank">理想汽车</a>发布改款理想ONE，芯片厂商从Mobileye换成了地平线的征程3芯片，实现L2级自动驾驶。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">但独角兽同样也面临着诸多挑战，车规级芯片要求严格，自动驾驶芯片更不能在任何情况下宕机，否则会危及驾驶安全。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">而对于寒武纪来说，对手则是自己。</span></strong><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">在失去先发优势后，能否把现有的技术积累迁移至车载芯片，在持续亏损之下，是否有足够的资金支撑新业务的拓展，都有待进一步观察。</span></p> 
<p style="margin-right: 8px;margin-left: 8px;outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(34, 34, 34);font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;letter-spacing: 0.544px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="outline: 0px;max-width: 100%;font-size: 15px;letter-spacing: 1px;visibility: visible;box-sizing: border-box !important;overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">这样来看，商业竞争就是这般无奈，寒武纪好似从一个围城，翻入了另一个围城之中。</span></p>
