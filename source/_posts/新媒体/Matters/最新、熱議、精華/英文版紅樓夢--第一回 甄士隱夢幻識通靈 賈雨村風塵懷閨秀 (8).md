<p><strong>"May I trouble to enlighten me," it said, "as to what wonderful merits you will bestow on me? And where do you mean to take me?"<br class="smart"></strong>「我可以麻煩您賜教，」它說：「您將贈予我什麼奇妙的功績？ 您打算帶我去哪裡？」<br class="smart"><strong>「不知賜了弟子那幾件奇處，又不知攜了弟子到和地方?望乞明示，使弟子不惑。」</strong></p><p>enlighten (v) 啟迪、闡明<br class="smart">merit (n) 優點、價值、功績<br class="smart">bestow (v) 贈予、給予</p><hr><p>"Don't ask." The monk smiled. "You'll find out all in good time." With that he tucked the stone into his sleeve and hurried off with the Taoist. But where they went no one knows.<br class="smart">「別問了。」 和尚笑道 ：「你有的是時間可以得知一切。」 他將那石頭塞進袖子裡，和道士一起匆匆離去，但他們去了哪裡沒有人知道。<br class="smart"><strong>那憎笑道：「你且莫問，日後自然會明白的。」說著，便袖攏了這石，同那道人飄然而去，竟不知投奔何方何舍。</strong><br class="smart"></p><p>find out (v) 找出、查明、得知<br class="smart">in good time 及早、有充裕時間地<br class="smart">tuck (v) 把......塞入<br class="smart">sleeve (n) 袖子<br class="smart"></p><hr><p>After no one knows how many generations or aeons, a Taoist known as Reverend Void, searching for the Way and immortality, came to Great Waste Mountain, Baseless Cliff and the foot of Blue Ridge Peak. His eye fell on the inscription on a large stone which was still discernible and he read it through. It was an account of the Stone's rejection for repairing heaven, its transformation and conveyance to the world of men by the Buddhist of Infinite Space and the Taoist of Boundless Time, and the joys and sorrows, partings and encounters, warm and cold treatment from others it had experienced there. On its back was a Buddhist verse:<br class="smart">不知過了多少世世代代，一位被稱為太虛尊者的道人，為了尋道與永生，來到了大荒山無稽崖青埂峰腳下。他的目光落在一塊大石上可識別的銘文，他讀了一遍，上面記述著石頭因未修補天空而被廢棄，由茫茫大士和渺渺真人將它轉形、轉化人世間，經歷過了悲歡離合，冷暖人情。 它的背面有一首佛偈：<br class="smart">       Unfit to mend the azure sky,   不適合修補蔚藍天空，<br class="smart">       I passed some years on earth to no avail;  我在地球上虛度了幾年光陰；<br class="smart">       My life in both worlds is recorded here;  我兩世的生活都記錄在此；<br class="smart">       Whom can I ask to pass on this romantic tale? 我可以請誰來傳遞這個浪漫故事？  <strong><br class="smart">后來，又不知過了幾世幾劫，因有個空空道人訪道求仙，忽從這大荒山無稽崖青埂峰下經過，忽見一大石上字跡分明，編述歷歷。空空道人乃從頭一看，原來就是無材補天，幻形人世，蒙茫茫大士、渺渺真人攜入紅塵，歷盡離合悲歡、炎涼世態的一段故事。后面有一首偈云:</strong><br class="smart"><strong>       無材可去補蒼天，枉入紅塵若許年。此係身前身後事，倩誰記去做奇傳？</strong><br class="smart"></p><p>generation (n) 世代<br class="smart">aeon (n) 永世、萬古、十億年期<br class="smart">reverend (adj) 可尊敬的、值得崇敬的     (n) 教士、牧師<br class="smart">void (adj) 空的、空無所有的     (n) 太空、空間、空虛感<br class="smart">immortality (n) 永生<br class="smart">inscription (n) 銘文、碑文<br class="smart">discernible (adj) 可識別的、看得清楚的<br class="smart">read through (v) 從頭到尾讀一遍<br class="smart">account (n) 描述、記述<br class="smart">rejection (n) 廢棄、剔除<br class="smart">transformation (n) 轉換、變化、變形<br class="smart">conveyance (n) 運輸、傳達、表達<br class="smart">infinite (adj) 無限的、無邊的<br class="smart">boundless (adj) 無限的、無窮的<br class="smart">verse (n) 詩歌、韻文<br class="smart">unfit for...(adj) 不適合...的     [疑似有誤的英文譯文 unfit to....]<br class="smart">azure (adj) 蔚藍的<br class="smart">avail (n) 效用、幫助、利益<br class="smart"></p><h2><strong>~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~</strong></h2><p>英文版紅樓夢學習紀錄</p><p>取材自大中華文庫漢英對照紅樓夢 (外文出版社、湖南人民出版社1999年出版)</p><p>譯者： 楊憲益、戴乃迭</p><p>這是我個人的中英文學習筆記，如有錯誤，敬請見諒並賜予指正。<br class="smart"></p>
