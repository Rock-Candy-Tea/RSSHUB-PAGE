<p>Life is like endless line.</p><p>A long line.</p><p>No one can see it exactly.</p><p>What is in front of us on this line.</p><p>Only when we stand on the position then we can know.</p><p>Because of people can’t see it clearly on this line.</p><p>The people always has different imagine and expecting while we walking on this line.</p><p>It comes what we expect sometime and it also comes some unexpected thing also.</p><p>Because of imagine</p><p>Imagine can create anything.</p><p>If the people lose the imagine, the people only can accept what the line give.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p><br></p><p>人生就像一條無止盡的線，一條很長的線。</p><p>沒有人可以很明確的看到，在這條線上有什麼東西在我們的面前。</p><p>只有當我們站到那個位置時，我們才有辦法知道。</p><p>因為人們無法在這條上看得清楚。</p><p>當人們走在這條線上時，人們總有不同的期待與想像。</p><p>有時會符合我們的期待，有時卻出現了意料之外的事。</p><p>因為想像。</p><p>想像可以帶來創造力。</p><p>如果人們失去了想像力，那麼人們只能夠接受這條線所給的東西了。</p><p><br></p>
