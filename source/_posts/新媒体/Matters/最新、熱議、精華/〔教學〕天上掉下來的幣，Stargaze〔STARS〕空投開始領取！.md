正在投票中。</p><figure class="image"><img src="https://assets.matters.news/embed/f1edf982-5c4a-4183-82ac-6bf97c7a3ca0.jpeg" data-asset-id="f1edf982-5c4a-4183-82ac-6bf97c7a3ca0"
