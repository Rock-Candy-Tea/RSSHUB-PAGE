<p>立場 x Oursky 為Likechain 第一批節點。</p><p>有關立埸新聞的簡介，請看[立場新聞 Stand News](<a href="https://thestandnews.com/about_us" target="_blank">https://thestandnews.com/about_us</a>)。有關Oursky 的請看[Oursky - Build Great Software Products](<a href="https://www.oursky.com/about/" target="_blank">https://www.oursky.com/about/</a>)。Oursky 為立場新聞的主要技術提供者。</p><p>我們認同一個開放的㡳層網絡有利於新聞肉容的傳播 ，而合理的激勵機制，更能鼓勵人們長期參與。因而參加了Likechain 成為驗證人。在驗證過程中的收益，全數用放thestandnews.com 的開發人員資薪上。意味大家委托這個節點就是實際在支持立場新聞的工作。</p><p>密匙持有人為@rickmak. Rick 是Oursky 的始創人之人。</p><p>---------</p><p>立場 x Oursky join Likechain as the first batch of validation node since genesis.</p><p>One can refer to [立場新聞 Stand News](<a href="https://thestandnews.com/about_us" target="_blank">https://thestandnews.com/about_us</a>) for intro of standnews. For Oursky intro, please refer to here: [Oursky - Build Great Software Products](<a href="https://www.oursky.com/about/" target="_blank">https://www.oursky.com/about/</a>) .Oursky is the primary the technical service provider for standnews.</p><p>We agree a open infrastructure for content provider benefit the journalism and communication. Incentive mechanism plays a important role for long term participation. Therefor we join as a validator. All the reward and commission will be used for paying the developer salary of maintaining thestandnews.com. It means your delegation is a support to Stand News.</p><p>Key holder and primary representative is @rickmak. @rickmak is the founder of Oursky.</p>
