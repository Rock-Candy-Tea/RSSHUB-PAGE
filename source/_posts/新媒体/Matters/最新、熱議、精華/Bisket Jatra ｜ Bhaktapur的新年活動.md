<p>爬完山休息夠了我們來到Bhaktapur，舊稱 Khwopa |  Newar 語。</p><p>Bhaktapur 的意思是 敬拜者之都 | city of devotees，中文讀者則多稱其故名——巴德崗 | Bhadgaon （有趣的是，印度也有一個名爲 Bhadgaon的城市）</p><p>每年的四月中，Bhaktapur 的Taumadhi廣場會出現一輛戰車。上城和下城的居民會在慶典中拉扯（車），把車子上的神明 Bhairav 和Bhadrakali 拉到自己所居住的地區。算是光明正大的拉攏神明（嗎）</p><p>拔河（神）比賽開始時，一些人會站上戰車呐喊打氣或凑熱鬧。此前也曾發生意外，有暴動、毆鬥、互丟石頭，有人從車上墜落。</p><p>旁觀的人也會被推倒甚或踩踏，像這樣：</p><figure class="embed-video"><div class="iframe-container"><iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/JcIxXE4wW4o?rel=0" allowfullscreen="true" sandbox="allow-scripts allow-same-origin allow-popups" frameborder="0"></iframe></div><figcaption><span></span></figcaption></figure><p>Covid也擋不了慶典。<a href="https://thehimalayantimes.com/kathmandu/19-injured-in-first-day-of-bisket-jatra" target="_blank">新聞</a>説，第一天就有19人受傷。爾後尼泊爾又爆了一波疫情。 </p><figure class="embed-video"><div class="iframe-container"><iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/PMXKCtYVyWU?rel=0" allowfullscreen="true" sandbox="allow-scripts allow-same-origin allow-popups" frameborder="0"></iframe></div><figcaption><span>2021年的Bisket Jatra </span></figcaption></figure><p>補充：</p><blockquote><strong>Bhairav</strong> and <strong>Bhadrakali</strong> are then placed in large chariots (locally known as Rathas) and pulled through crowds of cheering onlookers. During the festival people replay a drama passed on over the centuries.</blockquote><blockquote>The chariots, pause for a tug of war between the easterna and western sides of town and then move down a steep road leading to a river, where a 25-m high lingam (vertical pole) is erected.
