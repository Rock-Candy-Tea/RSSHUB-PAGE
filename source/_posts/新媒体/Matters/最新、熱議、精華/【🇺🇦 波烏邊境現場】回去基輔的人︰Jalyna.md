I am coming back to pick up my dogs. I have four dogs and one cat. I brought two dogs here and trying to go back to bring the rest. After cross the border, I want to find the transportation to Lviv and take train to Kyiv.</p><p>I arrived Poland on 11 March and coming back on 27 March. I will stay in Kyiv if my dogs won’t leave. I am not worried. The main thing is just don’t panic, to be prepared and organising everything. That’s it. It’s kind of sense ready for it. I just need to save my dogs.</p><p>(Crying) My friend and the child were shot in Kyiv. Another friend was killed during bombing. I hope that Putin’s army running out. I just hope the war will be ended as soon as possible.</p><p>(Specially thanks to Ukrainian Oleg for the translation)</p><p><br></p><p>#烏克蘭 #烏俄戰爭 #俄國侵烏 #ukraine #russiainvadeukraine #border</p>
