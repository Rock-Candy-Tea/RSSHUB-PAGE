<p>下面這些單字是常有人問我的單字，在此簡單解說，與有緣看倌分享。</p><h2><strong>awake (dj) 醒著的  (awake 後面不可接名詞)</strong></h2><p>Did  you lie awake last night, worrying about something? 你昨晚失眠，在擔心時麼事嗎?</p><h2><strong>awake awoke awoken (v) 醒、喚醒  (主要為文學用語)</strong></h2><p>She awoke to find that several inches of snow had fallen. 她醒來時發現雪已經下了好幾英吋。</p><p>Going back to his hometown awoke memories from his childhood. 返回家鄉喚醒他兒時記憶。</p><h2>awaken awakened awakened (v) 喚醒、喚起、激起   (正式用語)</h2><p>I was awakened by the sound of firecrackers. 我被鞭炮聲喚醒。</p><h2>awakening (n)  覺醒、認識</h2><p>a rude awakening 猛然醒悟</p><p>We expected a friendly reception but were in for a rude awakening. 我們以為會受到友善款帶，但猛然發現情況並非如此。</p><h2>wake woke woken (v) 醒、醒來 (wake up)</h2><p>I always wake up feeling hungry. 我睡醒時總是感到飢餓。</p><h2>wake (n) 守靈、守夜</h2><p>What should we do at a wake? 我們守靈的時候該做什麼呢?</p><h2>waken wakened wakened (v) 叫醒</h2><p>Please waken me at 5:00 tomorrow morning. 明天早上請在五點鐘叫醒我。</p><p><br></p><p><br></p>
