可見印尼流行歌壇也不容小覷。</p><figure class="embed-video"><div class="iframe-container"><iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/FKqrCNWmxIs?rel=0" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="true" sandbox="allow-scripts allow-same-origin allow-popups"></iframe></div><figcaption><span></span></figcaption></figure><h1><strong>2 Jhonni Blaze - Its Alright</strong></h1><p>近來喜歡的一首R&B抒情歌曲。現在有這樣純粹的流行節奏藍調，還真如時光倒流20年。</p><figure class="embed-video"><div class="iframe-container"><iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/9WFfiYLa-R4?rel=0" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="true" sandbox="allow-scripts allow-same-origin allow-popups"></iframe></div><figcaption><span></span></figcaption></figure><h1><strong>3 BENEE - Beach Boy</strong></h1><p>已經很少聽到這樣順耳的輕搖滾。美好輕柔的女聲，不複雜的背景音樂，就隨著旋律和陽光搖晃吧。</p><figure class="embed-video"><div class="iframe-container"><iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/JTK1nC8ptkA?rel=0" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="true" sandbox="allow-scripts allow-same-origin allow-popups"></iframe></div><figcaption><span></span></figcaption></figure><h1><strong>4 Ella Henderson - Brave</strong></h1><p>這首歌很有激勵人心之感。厚實的女中音，其實很適合搖滾樂啊! 雖然她這首歌並非搖滾歌曲，但現在的音樂風格混搭，界線已經不再明顯，反正歌曲好聽就好了。</p><figure class="embed-video"><div class="iframe-container"><iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/g6xvHG8nd5U?rel=0" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="true" sandbox="allow-scripts allow-same-origin allow-popups"></iframe></div><figcaption><span></span></figcaption></figure><h1><strong>5 Bebe Rexha - The Way I Are (Dance With Somebody) feat. Lil Wayne</strong></h1><p>這首歌大有向天后Whitney Houston致敬的意味。Bebe大概是這五年來冒出頭的女歌手裡頗受歡迎的新生代偶像。</p>
