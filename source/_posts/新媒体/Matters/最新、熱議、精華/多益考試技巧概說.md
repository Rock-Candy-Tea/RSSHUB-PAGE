<p>我的用意只是分享我的備考方式和學習經驗喔  </p><p>請各位看倌參考參考即可  不必完全接收 </p><p>但願不會出現負面言論</p><h2><strong>多益考試技巧概說  </strong></h2><h2><strong>Part 1 photographs</strong></h2><h2>1.快速瀏覽照片，注意人物、地點、動作級物件</h2><h2>2.聆聽選項並記下重要字詞</h2><h2>3.刪除錯誤答案</h2><h2>4.在下一題播出前選出答案</h2><p><br></p><h2><strong>Part 2 questions & responses</strong></h2><h2>who/whose/whom 詢問人物、名字、職稱、身分、關係等，例如</h2><h2> Who turned on the radio?</h2><h2> Whom should I go to ask for help?</h2><h2> Whose cell phone is this?</h2><p><br></p><h2>what  詢問事件、事物、日期、職業等，例如</h2><h2> What did the repairman say about the printer?</h2><h2> What is the new manager’s name?</h2><p><br></p><h2>where  詢問地方、位置、場所等，例如</h2><h2> Where will the wedding be held?</h2><h2> Will the wedding be held in a church or somewhere else?</h2><p><br></p><h2>when , what time/day/year, which time/day/year 詢問地方、位置、場所等。常出現的字詞包括 late, early, on time, delay, yet, still, start, begin 等，例如</h2><h2> When does the contract go into effect?</h2><h2> When is your return flight?</h2><h2> Does the concert begin at seven?</h2><p><br></p><h2>why , what’s the reason 詢問原因、理由等，例如</h2><h2> What’s the reason for moving the factory?</h2><h2> Why is he often late for work?</h2><p><br></p><h2><strong>which , which one, A or B 詢問選擇、可能性等</strong>，例如</h2><h2><strong> Which exit do we use, the north one or the south one?</strong></h2><h2><strong> Which ticket did you buy?</strong></h2><h2><strong> On which date is the conference scheduled?</strong></h2><h2><strong> Is the pattern stripped of dotted?</strong></h2><p><br></p><h2><strong>how about + V-ing / let’s + V 詢問意見、建議等</strong>，例如</h2><h2><strong> How about reserving a nonsmoking table?</strong></h2><h2><strong> Let’s not argue over details, OK?</strong></h2><p><br></p><h2><strong>how do you feel about…/what do you think about…/do you think… 詢問對方感覺、意見等</strong>，例如</h2><h2><strong> How do you feel about the carpenter’s work?</strong></h2><h2><strong> How about feel about taking a detour?</strong></h2><h2><strong> What do you think about teaching English to young children?</strong></h2><p><br></p><h2><strong>how long…/how much time…/how many days… 詢問時間有多長</strong>，例如</h2><h2><strong> How long will you be on vacation?</strong></h2><h2><strong> How much longer do you need to finish the work?</strong></h2><p><br></p><h2><strong>how much 詢問金額</strong>，例如</h2><h2><strong> How much will the new cell phone sell for in stores?</strong></h2><h2><strong> How much do you pay for rent?</strong></h2><p><br></p><h2><strong>how 詢問方法、程度等， 回答通常會提供某種方法</strong>，例如</h2><h2><strong> How can I get him to study hard?</strong></h2><h2><strong> How are you getting to Taipei?</strong></h2><h2><strong> How do you expect the expansion to happen if we don’t have the funding?</strong></h2><p><br></p><h2><strong>how often, how many times a day/week… 詢問頻率。常出現字詞包括 once in a while, twice every week 等</strong>，例如</h2><h2><strong> How often does the retailer send back a defective product?</strong></h2><h2><strong> How often does the inspection happen?</strong></h2><p><br></p><h2><strong>how many…/what’s the number… 詢問數量</strong>，例如</h2><h2><strong> How many orders did we get last week?</strong></h2><h2><strong> What is the number of violations are we allowed before the factory is fined?</strong></h2><p><br></p><h2><strong>Be 動詞的問句， 一般詢問。須注意時態及人稱變化。有時會省略 yes/no 直接以敘述句回答</strong>，例如</h2><h2><strong> Are you going to be honest with me?</strong></h2><h2><strong> Will the issue be dealt with tomorrow?</strong></h2><h2><strong> Was the customer satisfied with her purchase?</strong></h2><p><br></p><h2><strong>do 開頭的問句， 詢問對方意見或尋求幫助。常見問句形式包括 do you mind/know/like/think等</strong>，例如</h2><h2><strong> Do you know how to fix the computer?</strong></h2><h2><strong> Does your brother go hiking this weekend?</strong></h2><h2><strong> Do you think we can warp up earlier today?</strong></h2><p><br></p><h2><strong>完成式的問句， 詢問某事完成狀態</strong>，例如</h2><h2><strong> Have you tried on the new suit?</strong></h2><h2><strong> Has the mailman come today?</strong></h2><h2><strong> Have you given Mary a call?</strong></h2><p><br></p><h2><strong>can / is there any way / is it possible / is it OK / is it likely 等問句， 詢問可行性</strong>，例如</h2><h2><strong> Can you give a rough estimate?</strong></h2><h2><strong> Could the window be shut?</strong></h2><h2><strong> Is it OK I I leave the coffee and doughnuts outside the conference room?</strong></h2><p><br></p><h2><strong>附加問句， 詢問某事的揣測。語調上升表示對方想知道答案或相關資訊。語調下降表示對方並非真想知道或已知道答案。</strong>例如</h2><h2><strong> The meeting was rescheduled, wasn’t it?</strong></h2><h2><strong> We will send a self-addressed stamped envelope, won’t we?</strong></h2><p><br></p><p>(未完，待續......)</p>
