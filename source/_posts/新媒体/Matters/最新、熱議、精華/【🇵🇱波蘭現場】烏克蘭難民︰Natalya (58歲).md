After stayed for a short period of time with friends then I took bus to the border on 22 March. It took around 10 hours from Cherkasy to the border. </p><p>I lived on the side of Chernobyl. It was scared when started bombing and it’s hard to describe the feeling. I managed to leave Kyiv when the bombing started. My children and grandchildren still in Cherkasy and I came to Poland by myself.</p><p>I would like to go to Italy. I have friend there. I want to go there and get some work. I want to go back when the war end. During this one month, I couldn’t sleep. Bombing was happening in Cherkasy and we had to be in bomb shelter. No sleeping. Nervousness. It’s terrible. </p><p>I want to tell Ukrainian Army: To be patient. Stay strong for the victory. Victory not just for Ukraine but for the rest of the world.  </p><p>(Specially thanks to Ukrainian Oleg for the translation)</p>
