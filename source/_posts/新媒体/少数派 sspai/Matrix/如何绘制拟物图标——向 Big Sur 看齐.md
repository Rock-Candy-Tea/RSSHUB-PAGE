<div class="articleWidth-content" data-v-e1f0100a><div class="content wangEditor-txt minHeight" data-v-e1f0100a><h2 style="margin-left:0px;text-align:center;"><strong>01绘制底座</strong></h2><ul><li style="text-align:justify;">打开Photoshop，新建一个文档。</li></ul><p style="margin-left:0px;text-align:justify;">       我们推荐你创建大小为 512x512px 的正方形文档。</p><figure class="image ss-img-wrapper"><img src="https://cdn.sspai.com/2021/06/11/article/8e049653bbfcbaa7ec63ace33b715798?imageView2/2/w/1120/q/40/interlace/1/ignore-error/1" data-original="https://cdn.sspai.com/2021/06/11/article/8e049653bbfcbaa7ec63ace33b715798" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><ul><li style="text-align:justify;">使用<strong>圆角矩形工具</strong>，绘制下面的图形。</li></ul><figure class="image ss-img-wrapper"><img src="https://cdn.sspai.com/2021/06/11/article/a470a1ca609af0ce05742db86fa995c1?imageView2/2/w/1120/q/40/interlace/1/ignore-error/1" data-original="https://cdn.sspai.com/2021/06/11/article/a470a1ca609af0ce05742db86fa995c1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><ul><li style="text-align:justify;">对圆角矩形进行润色。进入<strong>图层样式</strong>，为其设置<strong>渐变叠加和投影</strong></li></ul><figure class="image ss-img-wrapper"><img src="https://cdn.sspai.com/2021/06/11/article/e85441b63ec77fad090acf3e4f211a81?imageView2/2/w/1120/q/40/interlace/1/ignore-error/1" data-original="https://cdn.sspai.com/2021/06/11/article/e85441b63ec77fad090acf3e4f211a81" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p> </p><h2 style="margin-left:0px;text-align:center;"><strong>02绘制小三角</strong></h2><p style="margin-left:0px;text-align:center;"> </p><ul><li style="text-align:justify;">同样使用<strong>圆角矩形工具，</strong>绘制如图三个形状，注意图层顺序。</li></ul><p style="margin-left:0px;text-align:justify;"><strong>    ！</strong>注意对齐中心的等边三角形。</p><figure class="image ss-img-wrapper"><img src="https://cdn.sspai.com/2021/06/11/article/d370e3ccaa381b595b8ecc7d4377d3e9?imageView2/2/w/1120/q/40/interlace/1/ignore-error/1" data-original="https://cdn.sspai.com/2021/06/11/article/d370e3ccaa381b595b8ecc7d4377d3e9" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><ul><li style="text-align:justify;">对小三角进行润色。进入<strong>图层样式</strong>，分别为每个圆角矩形设置三层<strong>内阴影。</strong></li></ul><figure class="image ss-img-wrapper"><img src="https://cdn.sspai.com/2021/06/11/article/801328077da493ed8b158ecd3355fee0?imageView2/2/w/1120/q/40/interlace/1/ignore-error/1" data-original="https://cdn.sspai.com/2021/06/11/article/801328077da493ed8b158ecd3355fee0" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><ul><li style="text-align:justify;">这时你一定发现了一个问题：<strong>和原图架构有些稍微不一样。原图的图层顺序非常微妙。</strong>要达到这个效果，我们只需要使用<strong>矩形选择工具</strong>将下方的横杠裁剪成两半，然后一个放上面，一个放下面。</li></ul><p style="margin-left:0px;"> <strong>   ！</strong>裁剪之前，注意将横杠的图层样式<strong>栅格化</strong></p><figure class="image ss-img-wrapper"><img src="https://cdn.sspai.com/2021/06/11/article/def6793c4ff0911bd66915e3511bf252?imageView2/2/w/1120/q/40/interlace/1/ignore-error/1" data-original="https://cdn.sspai.com/2021/06/11/article/def6793c4ff0911bd66915e3511bf252" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p style="margin-left:0px;text-align:center;"> </p><h2 style="margin-left:0px;text-align:center;"><strong>03绘制阴影</strong></h2><ul><li style="text-align:justify;">进一步分别对横杠进行润色：用<strong>钢笔工具</strong>绘制如图所示的<strong>淡蓝色阴影形状</strong>，为其减少一些透明度并对尾部进行<strong>模糊滤镜</strong>处理。然后，放置在对应的横杠下面。</li></ul><p style="margin-left:0px;">    <strong>！</strong>尽量避免使用纯黑阴影。</p><figure class="image ss-img-wrapper"><img src="https://cdn.sspai.com/2021/06/11/article/1c9955da28922259e59edbcfe23a51a9?imageView2/2/w/1120/q/40/interlace/1/ignore-error/1" data-original="https://cdn.sspai.com/2021/06/11/article/1c9955da28922259e59edbcfe23a51a9" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><ul><li style="text-align:justify;">就要完成了！最后一步便是再为小三角添加一层<strong>阴影</strong>。将三根横杠<strong>合并为一个组</strong>，为其添加<strong>图层样式>投影</strong>。</li></ul><p style="margin-left:0px;">    ！为了获得更好的效果，请使用<strong>多层投影</strong>。例如，图中我使用了<strong>三层投影</strong>。</p><figure class="image ss-img-wrapper"><img src="https://cdn.sspai.com/2021/06/11/article/2e165ba053a28e3377a41f5abc8c9af1?imageView2/2/w/1120/q/40/interlace/1/ignore-error/1" data-original="https://cdn.sspai.com/2021/06/11/article/2e165ba053a28e3377a41f5abc8c9af1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><ul><li style="text-align:justify;">完成！</li></ul><figure class="image ss-img-wrapper"><img src="https://cdn.sspai.com/2021/06/11/article/14b0cc5948b7e09adc14d30c2b94afec?imageView2/2/w/1120/q/40/interlace/1/ignore-error/1"
