<p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">著名工程师和发明家的名言（6）</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">武夷山 译</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">    Workflowmax网站于2020年4月提供了一些著名工程师和发明家的名言（博主：原文如此，其实有的语录的主人并非工程师，只是语录内容涉及工程或工程师），下面我翻译其中一些，与大家分享。这是第六部分。</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;"> </span></strong></span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"><strong>1.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-weight: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">  </span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;">“Science can amuse and fascinate us all, but it is engineering that changes the world.”</span></strong><strong><br> </strong>- Isaac Asimov，美国科普大师阿西莫夫</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">科学能逗乐大家。迷住大家，但改变世界的是工程。</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"><strong>2.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-weight: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">  </span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;">“Scientists study the world as it is, engineers create the world that never has been.”</span></strong><strong><br> </strong>- Theodore von Karman，冯.卡门,美国伟大的工程学家、钱学森的导师</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">科学家研究原本存在的世界，工程师创造迄今不存在的世界。</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;">3. “Manufacturing, science and engineering are…incredibly creative. I’d venture to say more so than creative advertising agencies and things that are known as the creative industries.”</span></strong><strong><br> </strong>- Sir James Dyson，1947年生，英国发明家</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">    制造、科学和工程......都具备难以置信的创造性。我想大胆地说，它们比创意广告机构和所谓创意产业都更有创造性。</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"><strong>3.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-weight: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">  </span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;">“Men build bridges and throw railroads across deserts, and yet they contend successfully that the job of sewing on a button is beyond them.”</span></strong><strong><br> </strong>- Heywood Broun，1879-1973，美国著名记者，文风幽默</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">    男人们建造桥梁，让铁路穿过了荒漠，但他们却成功地论证说，钉扣子这活他们干不了。</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"><strong>4.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-weight: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">  </span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;">“The ideal engineer is a composite… He is not a scientist, he is not a mathematician, he is not a sociologist or a writer, but he may use the knowledge and techniques of any or all of these disciplines in solving engineering problems.”</span></strong><strong><br> </strong>- N.W. Dougherty，1886-1977，美国土木工程师，曾担任田纳西大学工学院院长16年之久</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">    理想的工程师是个复合人才......他不是科学家，他不是数学家，他不是社会学家，也不是作家，但他可以利用这些学科中的任一学科或所有这些学科的知识与方法来解决工程问题。</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;"> 5.“The goal of science and engineering is to build better mousetraps. The goal of nature is to build better mice.”</span></strong><strong><br> </strong>- Unknown，佚名</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">科学与工程的目标是制造更好的捕鼠夹。大自然的目标是造就更厉害的老鼠。</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"><strong>5.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-weight: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">  </span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;">“All we know about the new economic world tells us that nations which train engineers will prevail over those which train lawyers. No nation has ever sued its way to greatness.”</span></strong><strong><br> </strong>- Richard Lamm,1935- ,美国政治家和律师,曾任科罗拉多州州长</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">    我们获得的关于新经济世界的知识告诉我们，培养工程师的国家将胜过培养律师的国家。没有一个国家是通过诉讼走向伟大的。</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;">7. “A good engineer thinks in reverse and asks himself about the stylistic consequences of the components and systems he proposes.”</span></strong><strong><br> </strong>- Helmut Jahn，1940- ，德裔美国建筑师</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">    优秀工程师是逆向思考的，他问自己，他打算使用的元件和系统会有什么样的风格性结果。</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"><strong>8.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-weight: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">  </span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;">“An engineer is someone who washes his hands before going to the toilet.”</span></strong><strong><br> </strong>- Anonymous，佚名</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">工程师是上厕所之前先洗手的人。 </span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"><strong>9.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-weight: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">  </span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;">“Knowing there is a structure, hidden or felt, to the random gives pleasure.”</span></strong><strong><br> </strong>- Cecil Balmond，1943- ，出生于斯里兰卡的英国设计师</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">随机事物也有隐藏的或可感知的结构，了解这一点产生愉悦感。</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">    10. <strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;"> “You never change things by fighting the existing reality. To change something, build a new model that makes the existing model obsolete.”</span></strong><strong><br> </strong>- Buckminster Fuller，美国发明家、哲学家和诗人巴克明斯特.富勒</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">    与现实抗争是改变不了现实的。要想改变事物，就构建一个使现存模型过时的新模型。</span></p><p style="margin: 18px 0px; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;">相关阅读</span></strong></span></p><p style="margin: 5px 0px;
