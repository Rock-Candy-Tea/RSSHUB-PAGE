宋体">在</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">MECO</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">期间，由于缺氧，深海大洋处于还原状态。</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">MECO</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">期间存留的</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">Mn<sup>2+</sup></span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">被氧化成</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">Mn<sup>4+</sup></span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">的氧化物，并沉积下来，在</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">MECO</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">的上方形成一层特殊的</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">Mn</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">异常。</span></p><p style="text-indent:28px;line-height:150%"><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family: 宋体">在</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">MECO</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">时期，新特提斯洋还在，但是已经不属于开放大洋环境，而是被陆地包围，类似于今天的地中海。于是这一地区的海水性质受到周边河流的淡水输入、有机质输入、营养盐输入等影响，信号会变得更复杂。在这一地区研究</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">MECO</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">，一定要考虑上述因素的影响。</span></p><p style="text-indent:28px;line-height:150%"><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family: 宋体">由于</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">MECO</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">升温的缓慢性，对于</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">MECO</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">的持续时间也会存在着一定的争议，比如，我们定义的</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">600kyr</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">的</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">MECO</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">主体暖期，是不是只是一个</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">~1.2Myr</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">更长暖期的一部分</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">?</span></p><p style="text-indent:28px;line-height:150%"><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family: 宋体">在</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">MECO</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">期间，陆地上的气候反应也并不一致。比如，在新特提斯地区、北美地区显示的是变暖变湿，而在亚洲内陆则变干旱。一方面，这反应了陆地对气候响应时的区域特性，另一方面，可能也反应在</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">MECO</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">期间，主要的天文调谐参数变弱，缺乏了全球的整体一致性。</span></p><p style="text-indent:28px;line-height:150%"><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">MECO</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">期间天文调谐参数确实发生了变化。研究表明，</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">MECO</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">刚好对应于</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">400kyr</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">和</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">2.4Myr</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">的</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">Eccentricity </span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">周期最小值，这和白垩纪大洋缺氧事件类似。也就是长</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">Eccentricity</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">的调谐变弱了，从而打乱了海洋中的碳循环过程。根据天文轨道参数的限定，</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">MECO</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">的最佳时期应该</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">40.2-39.7Ma</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">。在</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">MECO</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">刚刚开始的时候，在某些记录中确实也出现了</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">δ<sup>13</sup>C</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">短暂的负漂移，但是很快就被正偏所校正。</span></p><p style="text-indent:28px;line-height:150%"><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family: 宋体">在很大程度上，</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">MECO</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">的性质与白垩纪大洋缺氧事件（</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">OAE</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">）类似。比如，无论是</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">δ<sup>13</sup>C</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">正偏幅度和持续时间上都是如此。但是，</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">OAE</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">期间，海底完全缺氧，大量有机碳埋藏。但是</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">MECO</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">期间，虽然也缺氧，但是还没达到</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">OAE</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">的程度，此外，除了在</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">MECO</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">后期（</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">40.07-39.96Ma</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">）和</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">MECO</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">结束之后（</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">39.68-39.55Ma</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">），有生产力明显升高外，</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">MECO</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">前期并没有大量的有机质埋藏行为。</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">MECO</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">和</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">OAE</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">的最大区别之一在于背景温度，前者经过始新世降温后比后者的背景温度降低了很多。</span></p><p style="text-indent:28px;line-height:150%"><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family: 宋体">在这里，我们不得不面对另外一个问题，同时具有</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">400kyr</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">和</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">2.4Myr</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">的</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">Eccentricity </span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">周期最小值可不止</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">MECO</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">这一时期。我们可以罗列一下这样的特殊时期，看这些时期为什么有时发生</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">MECO</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">这样的事件，有时则没有。通过这样的对比，就有可能回答一个重要的气候边界问题。虽然外部环境出现了，但是地球自身的内部系统还没准备好，或者处于不同的状态（比如南大洋构造运动产生的海陆分布变化，或者新特提斯洋演化），那么地球整体输出的气候响应当然也不一样。这就像一个老师教很多学生，内容都一样，但是由于学生状况不同，成绩也及不一样。</span></p><p style="text-indent:28px;line-height:150%"><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family: 宋体">在</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">MECO</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">这种天文轨道参数条件下，季节性变化减弱，全球水文循环也减弱，这刚好也可以解释为什么</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">MECO</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">时期大陆风化变得不明显。</span></p><p><br></p>                    <br><br>
