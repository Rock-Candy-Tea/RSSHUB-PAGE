<p style="text-indent:28px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">前几天写了篇博客，谈了论文带来的快乐，有快乐必然就有不快乐，下面是论文写作与投稿中的糟心事：</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">1.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">     </span>想不出好的点子，有时这种状态能够持续好长时间，比如半个月、一个月。</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">2.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">     </span>想好的点子准备着手写了，查文献时突然发现别人的成果发表了。</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">3.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">     </span>数据计量结果不能支撑论文假设或理论，又找不到理由。</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">4.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">     </span>很好的点子，没有记录下来忘记了，后来过了一段时间，又想起来了，这是好的，问题是有的想不起来的就再也没有了。</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">5.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">     </span>很好的思路，但要进行跨学科研究，找不到合适的合作者。比如前段时间我想做评价哲学，再如最近想做会计方面的某个东东。</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">6.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">     </span>发现在某个领域，大多数人做得不对，又不能很明显地写论文反驳。</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">7.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">     </span>本来用得好好的计量软件，突然收费了，或者试用期过期了。</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">8.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">     </span>由于某种原因，希望已录用的论文早点发表，但就是千唤万唤不出来。</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">9.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">     </span>本来计划用某个时间段完成论文，突然各种杂七杂八的事情干扰，造成严重影响，关键是，破坏了初始写作的激情。</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">10.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">  </span>选题、数据、方法、写作，手把手的教学生，教不出来，并且搞得自己也不想看了。</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">11.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">  </span>学生论文不能实现自己的思路。</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">12.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">  </span>学生发来的论文，发现你根本无法用修订模式改，或者说，没法改，说人话，就是要重写。</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">13.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">  </span>发现学生论文的问题，男生还好一点，能批评一下，问题是男生总是很少。</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">14.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">  </span>学生拖延，对急性子的老师这一点很恐怖。</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">15.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">  </span>期刊提修改建议，要求更新数据到写论文的前一年，但该年无数据。</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">16.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">  </span>审稿专家没有看懂论文，提的修改意见可能是错的，又不敢得罪。</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">17.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">  </span>跨学科的研究，经济学还是有高度的，无论方法和视角，但投稿到某个学科，该学科规范性不够，但审稿专家还是将你藐视一通。</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left: 52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">18.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">  </span>关于跨学科的计量类论文，从定量研究方法角度，某些学科还处在襁褓中，正因为如此，投稿到该学科的期刊，反而容易受伤。</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">19.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">  </span>投稿时秒退。</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">20.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">  </span>莫名其妙的查重，已经专门写博客说明过了，现在查重已经严重影响论文质量。</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">21.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">  </span>从投稿至今6年了，某期刊网上系统显示所有已经投稿的几篇论文均在初审中，从来没有退稿，也没有录用，也没有任何反馈。</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">22.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">  </span>一篇论文自己认为牛得不行，但连续被退稿差不多5年（后来发了）。</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">23.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">  </span>现在期刊不反馈审稿意见很正常，习惯了，问题是已经改了三稿了，突然退稿。</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">24.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">  </span>七八年前，某C刊期刊录用的稿件因为换了主编，退稿了。我是电话问刊发情况，编辑不小心说出来的，该刊目前是C扩。</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">25.一不小心，投稿到假期刊了。</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">26.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">  </span>写得好好的，突然电脑出问题了，或者误操作，已经写的内容丢了不少。</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">还有很多，先写这么多，总之也习惯了，适应了，很平静。</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><br></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">                               2021.4.10 俞立平于杭州</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><br></p><p><br></p>                    <br><br>
