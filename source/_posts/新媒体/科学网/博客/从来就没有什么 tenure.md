<p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-family: "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 15px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 16px;">    最近，邯郸路事件持续发酵。一方面，各种角度的分析看得让人唏嘘；但另一方面，也可以说“日光之下本无新事”，类似的事情一直都有发生，只不过这次更激烈一点而已。</span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-family: "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 15px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 16px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 15px;">    </span>有心想评论一下，因为期末，杂事繁多，暂未动笔。稍有耽搁，就发现科学网博友把我想写的内容都写出来了，而且写的极到位。</span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-size: 15px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 16px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 15px;">   </span><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; color: #C00000;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Microsoft YaHei";"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 15px;"> </span>最初，我想聊聊国内的</span><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-family: Calibri;">"</span><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Microsoft YaHei";">伪tenure track制度"和国外实际</span><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Microsoft YaHei";">制度的不同，本质上是对国内简陋版特色tenure制度的批判。</span></span><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Microsoft YaHei";">聊这个内容的文章已经很多，到处都是。</span></span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-size: 15px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 16px;"><span style="font-family: "Microsoft YaHei"; overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 15px;">    </span><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; color: #C00000;"><span style="font-family: "Microsoft YaHei"; overflow-wrap: break-word;">后来，我想写写国内</span><span style="font-family: Calibri; overflow-wrap: break-word;">"</span><span style="font-family: "Microsoft YaHei"; overflow-wrap: break-word;">伪tenure track制度"的</span><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word;"><span style="font-family:Calibri">原因</span></span><span style="font-family: "Microsoft YaHei"; overflow-wrap: break-word;">，某种意义上给国内特色制度辩护，确实有不少现实的限制。</span></span><span style="font-family: "Microsoft YaHei"; overflow-wrap: break-word;">这部分内容也被王立新老师写了（<a href="http://blog.sciencenet.cn/home.php?mod=space&uid=2999994&do=blog&id=1290377" target="_blank" style="overflow-wrap: break-word; color: rgb(0, 102, 153); text-decoration-line: none;">http://blog.sciencenet.cn/home.php?mod=space&uid=2999994&do=blog&id=1290377</a>  ）<span style="font-family: "Microsoft YaHei"; background-color: #FFFFFF;">，连我想用的词“土壤”都是一样的。</span></span></span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-family: "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 15px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 16px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 15px;">    </span><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; color: #C00000;">再后来，我又想聊聊量化考核对科研的伤害，其实是质疑tenure track是否真有必要。</span>发现被蔡宁老师写了（<a href="http://blog.sciencenet.cn/blog-522469-915305.html" target="_blank" style="overflow-wrap: break-word; color: rgb(0, 102, 153); text-decoration-line: none;">http://blog.sciencenet.cn/blog-522469-915305.html</a>  ），和我想举的例子都完全一样。</span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-family: "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 15px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 16px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 15px;">    </span>自己想写的内容被别人捷足先登了，也没有什么遗憾，反而觉得自己可以省事了，并且有找到同志的感觉。<br><br></span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-family: Calibri; font-size: 15px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; color: #C00000; font-size: 16px;">***</span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-family: "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 15px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 16px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 15px;">    今天再聊点什么呢？聊聊我的对tenure本身的看法。</span></span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-family: "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 15px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 16px;">    关于tenure，当年初入学术圈的时候，第一次听说它，惊为天人：以为西方学术的世外桃源里居然真有铁饭碗存在。后来了解深了，逐渐发现tenure这东西也不过如此。</span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-family: "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 15px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 16px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; color: #C00000;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 15px;">    </span>Tenure并不重要的最大的原因在于：拿tenure并不经济。</span></span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-family: "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 15px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 16px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; color: #C00000;">    拿tenure的过程付出极大。通常只有最优秀的人才全力以赴耗费10年（包括读博）才能拿到。如果这些优秀人才能把拿tenure过程付出的时间和精力投入其它任何技术性行业，几乎毫无疑问都能确保他们取得领域领军技术性人才的位置，因为"头部效应"而赢得"事实上的tenure"。</span></span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-family: "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 15px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 16px;">    tenure是个投入极大，回报极小的东西。tenure是优秀教授应得的，而不是恩赐。在几乎所有的行业，事实上的tenure都是存在的，只不过不叫这个名字而已。而且更容易拿。想象一下，天赋不错并且苦练10年后的厨师、木匠、裁缝、程序员是什么状态。</span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-family: "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 15px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; color: #C00000; font-size: 16px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 15px;">    </span>另外，tenure本身也是假的。tenure看着像铁饭碗，也确实只是个铁饭碗，也能被打破压瘪：</span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-family: "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 15px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 16px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 15px;">    </span>学院的关闭（在国外高校并不鲜见）可能导致tenure丢失；</span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-family: "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 15px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 16px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 15px;">    </span>在有tenure的情况下，学院领导依然能以各种理由关闭你的实验室，分配给你无聊的行政工作，给你考核不及格，事实上赶你走。（比如前段时间自杀的英国帝国理工教授 </span><a href="http://bbs.gter.net/thread-1790636-1-1.html" target="_blank" style="overflow-wrap: break-word; color: rgb(0, 102, 153); font-size: 16px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word;">http://bbs.gter.net/thread-1790636-1-1.html</span></a><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 16px;">  ）</span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-family: "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 15px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 16px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 15px;">    </span>美国不少医学院教授的tenure只能保证40-60%的工资（我听说的数字，不保证准确）。</span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-family: "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 15px;"><br><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 16px;"> 还有很多意外发生的可能：</span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-family: "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 15px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 16px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 15px;">    </span>学术不端（比如学术造假）和违反学校规定（比如师生恋和性骚扰）可能导致tenure的丢失。（学术造假里有多少是主观造假？被控性骚扰的教授不少说自己是被冤枉的。）</span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-family: "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 15px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 16px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 15px;">    </span>美国的tenure教授可能因为和中国合作丢掉tenure，锒铛入狱。</span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-family: "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 15px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 16px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 15px;">    </span>交通事故、癌症和脑溢血（比如前段时间去世的几个中年科教领军人才）</span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-family: "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 15px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 16px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 15px;">    </span>总之，以为拿到tenure就万事大吉？这其实是非常危险的想法。</span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-family: "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 15px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 16px;"><br></span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-family: Calibri; font-size: 15px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; color: #C00000; font-size: 16px;">***</span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-family: "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 15px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; color: #C00000; font-size: 16px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 15px;">    </span>所以，如何看待tenure？</span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-family: "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 15px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; color: #C00000; font-size: 16px;"><span style="font-family: "Microsoft YaHei"; background-color: #FFFFFF; overflow-wrap: break-word; color: #C00000;">    tenure 和 编制在本质上是一样的。他们要求你付出巨大的努力，然后只以一个<span style="overflow-wrap: break-word;">并不优厚的待遇</span>给你一个并不存在的安全幻象；而真正的风险从来不曾远离。</span></span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-family: "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 15px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; color: #C00000; font-size: 16px;">    tenure更主要的是一种荣誉。</span><span style="color: #C00000; font-size: 16px;">作为一种安全保证，tenure的价值应该还不如3年工资的存款。</span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-family: "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 15px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 16px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 15px;"><span style="color: #C00000; font-family: Calibri; background-color: #FFFFFF;">***<br></span>    </span>认真审查一下自己：</span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-family: "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 15px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 16px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 15px;">    </span>看看自己的心理和生理是否健康。（邯郸路事件明显是这出了问题。）</span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-family: "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 15px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 16px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 15px;">    </span>审视一下自己的知识、能力和经济储备，是否能够胜任眼前和未来可能的工作，包括应对可能的离职。</span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-family: "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 15px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 16px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 15px;">    </span>心态放平稳。</span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-family: "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 15px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 16px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 15px;">    </span>如果一切顺利当然好；如果事事不顺也没关系，小心应付即可。应付不了也没关系，人有旦夕祸福，很多时候也没有办法： <a href="http://blog.sciencenet.cn/blog-522469-1193593.html" target="_blank" style="overflow-wrap: break-word; color: rgb(0, 102, 153); text-decoration-line: none;">http://blog.sciencenet.cn/blog-522469-1193593.html</a>  </span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); font-family: "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 15px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 16px;">    有首歌也唱过：从来就没有什么 tenure，也别想靠头衔编制；</span><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-size: 16px; font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei";">要创造生活的幸福，全靠我们自己！</span></p><p><br></p>                    <br><br>
