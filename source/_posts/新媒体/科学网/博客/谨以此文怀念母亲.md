style="font-size:19px;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'"> </span></p><p style="margin-top:10px;text-indent: 37px;line-height:35px"><span style="font-size:19px;font-family:楷体">学校放寒假了，我跟很多同事一样想利用假期呆在学校做点事。到</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">1</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family: 楷体">月</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">19</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family:楷体">日这天，哥哥来电话说母亲病了，希望我回家去。考虑快到年边了，想把手头的事再赶赶，让哥哥他们注意观察，当天没有赶回去。到了</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">22</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family: 楷体">日</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">(</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family:楷体">农历</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">12</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family:楷体">月</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">20</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family: 楷体">日</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">)</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family:楷体">，家里几个电话催促下，实在坐不住了就赶回家去。母亲见到我回到身边，很高兴。</span></p><p style="margin-top:10px;text-indent: 37px;line-height:35px"><span style="font-size:19px;font-family:楷体">同母亲、家人过了一个愉快的春节。到</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">2</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family: 楷体">月日5号</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">(</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family:楷体">正月初五</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">)</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family:楷体">这天，告别母亲回到学校，又开始手头的工作。初八家里来电话，我又回到老家，守在母亲的身边。但到了农历正月</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">14</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family: 楷体">日这天，学校马上要开学了，在家里实在坐不住，我又只得告别病中的母亲，返回长沙。临行前，母亲握着我的手哭了。我知道她老人家自己知道自己的时日不多，很想让我能够守在她身边。她哭了，依依不舍地望着我离去的身影……但我没办法，感觉压着的事越来越多，在家里没办法完成。只得强忍着告别母亲，又回到学校。</span></p><p style="margin-top:10px;text-indent: 37px;line-height:35px"><span style="font-size:19px;font-family:楷体">果不其然，回到学校第二天即收到一篇文章的审稿意见，编辑和</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">2</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family: 楷体">位审稿人提了一大堆的意见和建议，并要求</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">3</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family: 楷体">个星期完成修改，提交修改稿。我知道时间对我而言非常紧迫。就立马着手修改论文，回答审稿人的提问等等。可正月</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">19</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family: 楷体">日这天早晨，正在我忙碌修改论文还没来得及整理各种文件的时际，突然接到母亲去世的噩耗。我顾不上一切，立马往家里赶回。当天车到湘潭与衡阳之间，在长长的塞车队伍中，我忍不住把母亲去世的噩耗发在了朋友圈。原谅我吧，各位朋友。因为在母亲最后的时间里，我终究还是没有能够守在她身边；因为正月</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">14</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family: 楷体">日与母亲的告别已经成了我与她的永别。</span></p><p style="margin-top:10px;text-indent: 37px;line-height:35px"><span style="font-size:19px;font-family:楷体">安葬好母亲的亡灵，我带着疲惫不堪的身体于</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">2</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family: 楷体">月</span><span Roman','serif'">(</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family:楷体">正月</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'"> 26</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family: 楷体">日</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">)</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family:楷体">回到长沙。悲痛之中，还没缓过神来，于</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">28</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family:楷体">日收到编辑部的催稿信。我立马想起之前的论文修改之事，带着神志恍惚的精神状态又开始那篇论文的修改。仔细核对稿件、审稿人意见回复、清绘图件之后，于</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">3</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family: 楷体">月</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">1</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family:楷体">日凌晨将修改稿上传投出。令我十分惊奇的是，</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">3</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family:楷体">月</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman','serif'">4</span><span style="font-size:19px;font-family:楷体">日这天，收到了编辑发来录用的邮件。</span></p><p style="margin-top:10px;text-indent: 37px;line-height:35px"><span style="font-size:19px;font-family:楷体">今天，我以这篇学术论文献给逝去的母亲，怀念我的母亲。因为为了这篇论文，我没有能在母亲最后的日子里守在她的身边。但如果我一直守在母亲身边，这篇文章是不可能按时提交修改稿又如期录用发表的。故写下这些文字，怀念我的母亲。愿母亲在天之灵，理解我，原谅我。</span></p><p
