<p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">2021-08-09</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">，星期一</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体">中国科学院动物研究所，牛泽清博士</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体">今天从普兰县转点去札达县，全程约</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">349</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">公里。</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">8</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">点半左右，大家一起将行李装车，然后去街上的早餐店吃早餐。</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">9</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">点</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">15</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">分，我们从普兰县城出发，沿</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">G564</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">国道，先向东南方向</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">80</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">公里外的巴噶乡行进。</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202108/10/082003e1sp1sel626s5lxe.jpeg" title alt="2021-08-09-普兰县-1.jpeg" style="font-family: 楷体; text-align: justify; text-indent: 32px; white-space: normal;" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p><p style="text-align: center; font-size: medium; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px;"><font face="楷体">整理行装</font></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体">从普兰县城出来，我看到普兰县周边种植了许多树，路上还有许多当地的拉水车在给树浇水。普兰也在努力改善着环境，我想以后再来普兰，也许就会看到树木成林了。</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202108/10/082032yjbji7sikiygj6zz.jpeg" title alt="2021-08-09-普兰县-2.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p><p style="text-align: center; font-size: medium; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px;"><font face="楷体">高原造林</font></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体">由于区间限速，我们上午</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">10</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">点半左右才到了巴噶乡。从巴噶乡开始，我们改道</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">G219</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">国道，向狮泉河方向行进。走着走着，我看见了藏民心中的神山</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif"> - </span><span style="font-family: 楷体">刚仁波齐。可惜，今天天空不作美，神山被浓雾笼罩着，只露出一个山角来，但也不失它的神秘之感。</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202108/10/082058mkkswzsbzgkebsee.jpeg" title alt="2021-08-09-普兰县-3.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p><p style="text-align: center; font-size: medium; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px;"><font face="楷体">浓雾神山</font></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体">上午</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">11</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">点左右，我们已驶离普兰县，进入到噶尔县。前方修路，我们一行只能停下。海拔已近</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">4700</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">米，周围环境荒凉，路边也没有什么花草。金梁、小吴只好趁机翻起了石头，找寻着石头下的步甲类昆虫。不多一会儿，他俩还真收集了不少的步甲类昆虫。半个小时后，道路放行，我们继续上路。过了索多村，从</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">G219</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">改道</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">G565</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">国道。我们一直行驶在青藏高原的高原面上，路旁的山虽不高，但海拔却在</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">4500</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">米左右，体感特别累。我见小吴开着车，也不说话，他肯定更要累一些。沿途的山体呈现出多彩的颜色，但我们却没有太多的心情去欣赏它们了。</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202108/10/082329s9jjff9lm9yjxjyv.jpeg" title alt="2021-08-09-噶尔县-4.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p><p style="text-align: center; font-size: medium; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px;"><font face="楷体">远山胜景</font></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202108/10/082131icsu2998dkcu9kzk.jpeg" title alt="2021-08-09-噶尔县-1.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p><p style="text-align: center; font-size: medium; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px;"><font face="楷体">翻石头，找甲虫</font></p><p style="font-size: medium; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px;"><font face="楷体"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202108/10/082153g7y213aay0q01y72.jpeg" title alt="2021-08-09-噶尔县-2.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></font></p><p style="font-size: medium; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px; text-align: center;"><font face="楷体">大石头，大甲虫？</font></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体">下午</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">1</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">点左右，我们一行来到了龙噶拉达坂，海拔爬升至</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">5166</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">米。天空乌云密布，仿佛要下雨似的。我们因怕高反，在达板处也没敢过多地停留，匆匆拍了个照，就急忙驱车离开了。</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202108/10/082215smomkirrb3bk00r2.jpeg" title alt="2021-08-09-噶尔县-6.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p><p style="text-align: center; font-size: medium; font-family: DengXian; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体"><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: medium; text-align: justify; text-indent: 32px;">龙噶拉达坂</span></span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体">下午</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">3</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">点，我们已到札达县地界了。在札达土林观景台，大家驻车观景。我虽不是第一次来札达土林了，但再次站在土林前，还是深深地被大自然的神奇所感动。</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202108/10/082413k25l8gqdcqqhaawb.jpeg" title alt="2021-08-09-札达县-3.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p><p style="text-align: center; font-size: medium; font-family: DengXian; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体"><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: medium; text-align: justify; font-size: medium; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px;"><font face="楷体">铁线莲</font></p><p style="font-size: medium; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px;"><font face="楷体"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202108/10/082455dyy5pvlrkz6jdr7l.jpeg" title alt="2021-08-09-札达县-2.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></font></p><p style="font-size: medium;
