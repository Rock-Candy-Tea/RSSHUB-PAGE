<p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">人们在生活、学习、工作中，有时并不清楚该如何做才是合适的，单看别人怎么做，缺少必要的讲解或提示，往往会视而不见或不知其妙。例如，不少研究生撰写学位论文就是这种情况，虽然看了很多文献，但是，依然可能不知道怎么写才是恰当的。下面以我评阅的一篇硕士学位论文为基础，选择其中的若干典型错误，进行描述、分析、讲解和提示，希望对有关的人开展研究、撰写论文有所裨益。为了避免伤及论文作者，我做了一定的技术处理。按照惯例，先呈现我写的评阅意见，再进行典型错误的例子分析。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">该文以初中生</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的师生关系、同伴接纳、自我控制、学业拖延与</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">学业</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">成绩的关系为研究选题，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">符合</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">专业特点和要求。作者力图通过链式中介分析，较为全面地考察</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">初中生</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的师生关系、同伴接纳影响学业成绩</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">复杂机制，做出具有创新性的研究工作。然而，实际取得的效果</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">难</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">如人意。虽然该文的文献综述较为规范，但是，其他部分存在较多问题，特别突出的至少有：</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">其一</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">强</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">拆研究。该文呈现为研究一和研究二，其实只有一个研究，数据也是相同的，仅从分析内容上把</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">一个</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">研究强说成两个研究。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">其二</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">文题</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">不符。</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">表现</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">为研究一无法包含在该文的题目之中、未能论证清楚链式中介的相关问题，把师生关系和同伴</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">接纳</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">对学业成绩的影响分开分析</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">其三</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，学理失据。</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">师生</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">关系、同伴接纳与自我控制究竟是什么关系？该文认为师生关系、同伴接纳影响自我控制，然而，相反的关系似乎更合理。</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">该文</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的讨论</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">和</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">教育建议更是没有什么理论深度</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">其四</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，统计粗糙。</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">该文</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">没有介绍统计分析时可能进行的数据转换，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">从而，</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">使得</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">重要</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">变量的均值无法理解，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">过大</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的标准差也让人生疑。该文呈现了两个链式中介模型，估计作者也不太</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">明白</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">这样的模型是什么意思，从而，在报告时只能</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">机械</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">呈现</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">表格，</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">无法阐明对应的含义。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">其他</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">问题详见分页提示</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">（</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">这里从略</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">）。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">宏观地说，该文有如下四个典型错误——这些错误其实有一个共同根源。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">典型错误之一：热衷差异分析。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">该文专门设置研究一，对测试的基本数据进行人口统计学变量的差异检验。然而，这类内容</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">与该文的题目</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">没有关系</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">考察并呈现这样的结果，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">无异于</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">画蛇添足。同时，既然师生关系</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">和</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">自我控制存在年级差异</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">那么，随后的中介分析就应当考虑年级这个调节变量</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">但是，作者不再考虑年级变量</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">特别地，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">该文得出了像</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">“初三学生的师生关系优于初一和初二学生，初一学生的师生关系优于初二学生”这样的</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">结论</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">如果没有理论解释</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，此类结论是鲜</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">有意义</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">该文的确没有理论解释</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">也不太可能有理论解释。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">典型错误之二：没有研究目的。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">该文研究一讨论部分的</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">4</span><span style="font-family:宋体">个三级标题，均是</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">“</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">……</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的差异分析</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">”</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">研究二讨论部分的三级标题与结果部分</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">三级标题完全相同。这反映的是，作者分不清讨论与结果，把讨论与结果混为一谈，根本不知道讨论是理论层次的。这种错误产生的根源在于，作者没有发现并提出真正的研究问题，只能对测得的数据，机械地统计分析，生硬地描述结果，勉为其难地进行讨论。没有研究问题，其实是没有研究目的，从而，讨论自然就陷于无的放矢的窘境，也就是不知道要讨论什么。例如，该文对基本变量的人口统计学差异检验，正是如此。即使是中介分析，作者的理解也是浅层的，不清楚检验的模型究竟有什么理论价值和实践意义，当然很难讨论下去，体现在标题上，就是对结果亦步亦趋。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">典型错误之三：不懂研究意义。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">该文提到的</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">“实践意义”</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">是，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">“丰富此领域的研究质量”、“使该领域的研究内容更加全面”</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">不说</span>“丰富”与“研究质量”能否搭配</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">只说列出的两点，都不属于实践方面的意义</span>——其实，也不属于理论方面的意义。</span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">无论是学术型学位论文，还是专业型学位论文，都会分理论意义和实践意义两个方面来专门描写研究意义</span>——本来应该是论述，实际情况是描写。然而，很多论文呈现出来的，就是上述这类</span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">内容。</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">这反映的依然是，作者没有找到真正的研究问题，从而，无法确定自己的研究对相应领域的独特贡献。那种</span>“丰富<span style="font-family:Times New Roman">XXXX</span><span style="font-family:宋体">领域的研究”“进一步丰富</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">YYYY</span><span style="font-family:宋体">领域的研究”的说法，全然没有意义。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">此外，一些学位论文在描写研究意义时，显得定位不清。例如，学术型学位论文呈现实践意义的篇幅远远多于理论意义，而专业型学位论文给出理论意义的文字大大超过实践意义。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">典型错误之四：随意主观发挥。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">一篇论文的精华，在讨论部分，然而，该文在讨论方面存在太多问题。因此，这里再专门指出讨论时出现的不当。该文描述：</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">“随着年级的增加，师生关系更加亲密和谐”</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">这</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">与</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">文中呈现的</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">师生</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">关系得分</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">情况</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">不一致，说法</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">是</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">不可靠</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">“女孩</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">在生理上和心理上都应该成熟于同龄的男孩，她们往往有焦虑、易怒等不良情绪，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">她们</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">在行为上没有主动性</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">这有助于</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">男孩</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">获得</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">更高</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的同龄人认</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">可</span>”，</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">“在</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">学校学习的过程中没有具体的目标</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New
