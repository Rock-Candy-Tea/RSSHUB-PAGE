style="font-family:宋体">据服务员讲，宾馆西北不远处，有一喀斯特溶洞</span><span style="font-family:宋体">-</span></span><strong><span style=";font-family:宋体;color:#000000;font-weight:bold;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">艺云洞</span></span></strong><span style=";font-family:宋体;color:#000000;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，被誉为</span><span style="font-family:宋体">“仙迹胜景”。我们慕名前往，不巧，尚未正式开放。于是来到</span></span><strong><span style=";font-family:宋体;color:#000000;font-weight:bold;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">乃古石林</span></span></strong><span style=";font-family:宋体;color:#000000;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。这里，景观与大、小石林迥然不同。景区，石林呈黑色，雄伟而庄严，形态各异，有峰状、塔状、屏风状、城堡状、蘑菇状、动物状，等等。石林间，有两潭池水，清澈明净，玲珑俊秀。进入</span></span><strong><span style=";font-family:宋体;color:#000000;font-weight:bold;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">地下溶洞</span></span></strong><span style=";font-family:宋体;color:#000000;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，石钟乳、石笋、石柱等景观，形态多样，异彩缤纷。登上峰顶观看，绿树簇拥黑色石林，浩瀚磅礴，古朴粗犷。整个景区，集石林、湖泊、溶洞为一体，其造型之美，更胜</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;color:#333333;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">大、小石林一筹。</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;color:#000000;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">饱览美景之后，驱车回到昆明</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;color:#000000;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。</span></span></p><p><br></p>                    <br><br>
