Artist, too, must use<br> his “S” in order to “observe” the world,<br> his “A” (“intuition”) to sense<br> some basic ways to translate his<br> “observations”,<br> and his “M”<br> to derive his “results” in the form of<br> drawings, music, and so on.<br> Thus an Artist, too,<br> WHEN AT HIS BEST,<br> is a SAMite.</span></p><p style="line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-size: 16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体"> </span></p><p style="line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-size: 16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">    </span><span style="font-size: 16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">接着，她谈到健康的自由和病态的自由：</span></p><p style="line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-size: 16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体"> </span></p><p style="line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-size: 16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">Now consider a person<br> who is SOMETIMES or OFTEN like this:<br> SaM.<br> He is evidently relying very heavily on<br> his “intuition”, his “hunches”, his “emotions”,<br> hardly checking to see whether<br> the “observations” of the outside world (“S”)<br> and his own reasoning powers (“M”)<br> show his “hunch” to be correct or not!<br> And so,<br> precious as our “intuition” may be,<br> it can go terribly “haywire”<br> if not checked and double-checked<br> by “S” and “M”.<br> Thus, a person who<br> habitually behaves like this<br> is allowing his “S” and “M” to<br> become practically atrophied,<br> and is the wild, “over-emotional” type,<br> who is not only a nuisance to have around,<br> but is hurting himself and<br> not allowing himself to become<br> adjusted to the world he lives in.<br> Such a person,<br> with an exaggerated “A”,<br> and atrophied “S” and “M”,<br> has a feeling of “freedom”,<br> of not being held down by “S” and “M”<br> (“facts” and “reason”) ;<br> but, as you can easily see<br> this makes for Anarchy,<br> for a lack of “self-control” —<br> and can lead<br> to fatty degeneration from<br> feeling “free” to eat all he wants;<br> to the D.T.’s from<br> feeling “free” to drink all he wants;<br> to accidents because<br> he feels “free” to drive as fast as he wants<br> and to “hog” the road;<br> to a sadistic lack of<br> consideration for others<br> by feeling “free” to<br> kick them in the teeth for “nuttin'”;<br> to antisocial “black market” practices<br> from a similar feeling of “freedom”,<br> giving “free” reign to the “A”<br> without the necessary consideration of “facts” (“S”) and “reason” (“M”).<br> Needless to say this is a<br> PATHOLOGICAL FREEDOM<br> as against<br> a NORMAL, HEALTHY FREEDOM of<br> the well-balanced SAM<br> which is so necessary in society<br> in which EACH individual<br> must be guided by the SAM within himself<br> in order to avoid conflict with<br> the SAM in someone else.<br> This is something that<br> a bully does not understand —<br> that if he acts like a pathological sAm,<br> he induces sAmite-ism in others,<br> as in mob violence;<br> this is indeed a horrible “ism”<br> that can destroy a society as well as<br> individuals in it.</span></p><p style="line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-size: 16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体"> </span></p><p style="line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-size: 16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">    </span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">她又讨论了另一种不平衡：只看到分离的事实，看不到完整的大图景。</span></p><p style="line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-size: 16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">Similarly,<br> there is the Sam type:<br> he may be called the “tourist” type —<br> running around seeing this and that<br> but without the “imagination” (“A”)<br> or the reasoning power (“M”)<br> to put his observations together<br> with either heart (“A”) or mind (“M”),<br> but is concerned only with<br> ISOLATED BITS OF INFORMATION:<br> he is like the man who,<br> seeing a crowd had gathered,<br> wanted to know what happened.<br> and, when someone told him<br> “Ein Mann hat sich dem Kopf zerbrochen”<br> (It happened to be in Germany),<br> corrected the speaker’s grammar<br> and said “DEN Kopf!”<br> He knew his bit of grammar,<br> but what an inadequate reaction<br> under the circumstances.<br> don’t you think?</span></p><p style="line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-size: 16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体"> </span></p><p style="line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-size: 16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">    </span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">接着她描述了一些过分理性的人。</span></p><p style="line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-size: 16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">And there is also the saM type —<br> one who can reason (“M”)<br> but starts with perhaps<br> some postulate (“A”) favoring murder.<br> Such a man would make<br> a wonderfully “rational”<br> homicidal maniac or crook<br> who could plan you a murder<br> calmly and rationally enough<br> to surprise any who are not familiar with<br> this sAM type of pathological case.</span></p><p style="line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-size: 16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体"> </span></p><p style="line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-size: 16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">    </span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">总之，她这本书的宗旨就是要通过SAM来考察自身和他人的行为。</span></p><p style="line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-size: 16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">Thus SAM gives us a way of<br> examining our own behavior<br> and that of others,<br> taking into account the “facts” (“S”),<br> and using imagination and sympathy (“A”)<br> in a rational way (“M”).</span></p><p style="line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-size: 16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">Are you perhaps thinking,<br> “Well, this may be interesting,<br> but<br> why all this talk about SAM,<br> when you are writing a book about<br> Infinity?”<br> To which the answer is:<br> The yearning for Infinity,<br> for Immortality,<br> is an “intuitive” yearning (“A”):<br> we look for support for it<br> in the physical world (“S”),<br> we try to reason about it (“M”), —<br> but only when we turn<br> the full light of SAM upon it<br> are we able to make<br> genuine progress in considering<br> Infinity.</span></p><p style="line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-size: 16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体"> </span></p><p style="line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-size: 16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">    </span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">她最后强调，即使一个很平衡、不走极端的SAM人，也只满足了必要条件，而不是充分条件。</span></p><p style="line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-size: 16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体"> </span></p><p style="line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-size: 16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">There is only one more point<br> I must make here:<br> Namely, that<br> even being a well-balanced<br> SAMite —<br> and not a pathological anti-SAMite<br> like SAM, etc. etc. —<br> is NECESSARY but NOT SUFFICIENT.<br> You will probably agree that<br> it is further necessary<br> to have our SAM up-to-date.<br> For he is a GROWING boy,<br> and what was good enough for him in 1800<br> is utterly inadequate in 1953;<br> and unless the “S” is up-to-date<br> and the postulates (“A”)<br> and reasoning (“M”)<br> are appropriately MODERN,<br> we cannot make proper</span></p><p style="line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-size: 16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">s definitely NOT the<br> ANARCHICAL freedom of SAM,<br> but is a sort of<br> CONTROLLED FREEDOM —<br> controlled by facts and reason<br> and is therefore SELF-controlled<br> (by the SAM within us)<br> and hence implies<br> VOLUNTARY COOPERATION rather than FORCE.<br> Thus anyone who demands<br> “freedom unlimited” as his right,<br> is a pathological SAM,<br> a destructive creature;<br> whereas,<br> in mathematics<br> you will find the<br> CONTROLLED FREEDOM of SAM<br> and you will feel refreshed to see<br> how genuine progress can be made<br> with this kind of freedom.</span></p><p style="line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-size: 16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体"> </span></p><p style="line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-size:
