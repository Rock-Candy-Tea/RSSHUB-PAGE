font-family: 宋体;">）</span></span></strong></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">氨是食物和肥料的基本成分。大部分人工氨被用于生产农业肥料。氨也是一种重要的化学物质，具有广泛的工业用途，从生产洗涤剂到制冷剂。更重要的是，氨近年来引起了人们的广泛关注，因为它为燃料电池提供了氢的来源，而且它比氢更容易液化和运输。此外，氨本身可以作为发电的燃料，而不是石油和煤炭。所以生产氨的需求很大。</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><strong><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">“固定”氮是生产氨的重要步骤。虽然大气中的</span>80%<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">是由氮气组成的，但这种“自由”的氮只有转化为含氮化合物才能被利用，这种转化过程被称为“固氮（</span>nitrogen fixation<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）。如今，氨的生产严重依赖哈伯·博世工艺，但这是不可持续的，因为它消耗大量的化石燃料，并造成大量的二氧化碳排放，会造成严重的环境污染。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">为了寻找一种可持续生产氨的方法，</span>Michael K. H. Leung<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">教授和尚进博士（</span>Dr. Jin Shang<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">音译）联合，带领他们的团队开发了一种利用水和可再生能源在环境条件下固定氮的方法。联合研究小组面临的最大挑战是制造一种催化剂，使具有挑战性的多步骤固氮反应成为可能。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><strong><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">新型仿生光催化剂（</span><a href="https://phys.org/news/2021-10-photocatalyst-ammonia-atmospheric-nitrogen-room.html" target="_blank">New biomimetic photocatalyst</a></strong><strong><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）</span></strong></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">在自然界中，固氮酶</span>(<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">一种酶</span>)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">中的铁有利于结合和激活氮，叶绿素中的卟啉</span>(<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">一种有机化合物</span>)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">能有效地吸收阳光。受上述自然机制的启发，该团队开发了一种基于铁金属卟啉的金属有机框架</span>(metal-organic framework<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">简称</span>MOFs)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">光催化剂（</span>MOFs </span><a href="https://phys.org/tags/photocatalyst/" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">photocatalyst</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">）。</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">该仿生光催化剂厚度仅为</span>15 ~ 25 nm<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">，在环境温度和压力下，以阳光为动力，以水为还原剂，进行人工固氮，可产生氨。该团队在这种光催化剂中使用了</span>MOFs<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">，因为它在表面提供了更多的活性位点，用于吸附和活化氮，从而提高了氮还原反应的效率。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">该团队用这种光催化剂进行了实验，证明可以产生氨。</span>Michael K. H. Leung<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">教授说：“我们开发了一种新的光催化剂，在基于</span>MOFs<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">的光催化剂类别中，能够实现最佳的光催化固氮性能。它在</span>MOFs<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">中表现出最高的氨产率和最佳的水解稳定性（</span>hydrolytic stability<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）。”良好的水解稳定性意味着该光催化剂可以反复使用。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">通过这项研究，该团队探索了在他们的仿生光催化剂上进行光催化氮还原反应。尚进博士指出，从这项工作中获得的新知识将指导下一代</span>MOFs<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">基光催化剂的合理设计。他相信他们的发现将释放出开发各种卟啉基</span>MOFs<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">作为各种能源和环境应用的光催化剂的潜力。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">该团队希望这项开创性的研究能够启发催化领域的科学家和工程师探索和开发基于</span>MOFs<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">的仿生光催化剂，用于催化其他环境温度和压力下的化学反应，而不仅仅局限于人工固氮。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">Michael K. H. Leung<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">教授总结说：“通过无化石燃料过程生产能源和商品化学品是实现碳中和的理想方法。该研究开发出了利用阳光从大气中的氮和水中生产氨的技术，我们以可持续的方式获得无碳能源。”该团队相信他们的发现将有助于缓解日益紧迫的能源危机和环境问题。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">上述介绍，仅供参考。欲了解更多信息，敬请注意浏览</span><a href="https://doi.org/10.1021/acsnano.0c10947" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">原文</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">或者<a href="https://phys.org/news/2021-10-photocatalyst-ammonia-atmospheric-nitrogen-room.html" target="_blank">相关报道</a>。</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><a href="https://doi.org/10.1021/acsnano.0c10947" target="_blank" style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "arial black", "avant garde";">Abstract</a><br></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">The rational design of photocatalysts for efficient nitrogen (N<sub>2</sub>) fixation at ambient conditions is important for revolutionizing ammonia production and quite challenging because the great difficulty lies in the adsorption and activation of the inert N<sub>2</sub>. Inspired by a biological molecule, chlorophyll, featuring a porphyrin structure as the photosensitizer and enzyme nitrogenase featuring an iron (Fe) atom as a favorable binding site for N<sub>2</sub><em>via</em> π-backbonding, here we developed a porphyrin-based metal–organic framework (PMOF) with Fe as the active center as an artificial photocatalyst for N<sub>2</sub> reduction reaction (NRR) under ambient conditions. The PMOF features aluminum (Al) as metal node imparting high stability and Fe incorporated and atomically dispersed by residing at each porphyrin ring promoting the adsorption and the activation of N<sub>2</sub>, termed Al-PMOF(Fe). Compared with the pristine Al-PMOF, Al-PMOF(Fe) exhibits a substantial enhancement in NH<sub>3</sub> yield (635 μg g<sup>–1</sup><sub>cat.</sub>) and production rate (127 μg h<sup>–1</sup> g<sup>–1</sup><sub>cat.</sub>) of 82% and 50%, respectively, on par with the best-performing MOF-based NRR catalysts. Three cycles of photocatalytic NRR experimental results corroborate a stable photocatalytic activity of Al-PMOF(Fe). The combined experimental and theoretical results reveal that the Fe–N site in Al-PMOF(Fe) is the active photocatalytic center that can mitigate the difficulty of the rate-determining step in photocatalytic NRR. The possible reaction pathways of NRR on Al-PMOF(Fe) were established. Our study of porphyrin-based MOF for the photocatalytic NRR will provide insight into the rational design of catalysts for artificial photosynthesis.</span></p>                    <br><br>
