<p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style="font-family: Calibri;">1936</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">年，生物化学家亨里克·达姆</span><span style="font-family: Calibri;">(Henrik Dam)</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">发现了维生素</span><span style="font-family: Calibri;">K</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">，并以其在达姆的家乡丹麦语中促进血液凝结的作用而命名。维生素</span><span style="font-family: Calibri;">K</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">是一种叫做萘醌的分子。它可以以多种形式存在，但只有一种支持凝血——“还原性对苯二酚”形式，被称为</span><span style="font-family: Calibri;">VKH2</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">。</span><span style="font-family: Calibri;">VKH2</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">的水平在体内由维生素</span><span style="font-family: Calibri;">K</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">环氧还原酶</span><span style="font-family: Calibri;">(VKOR)</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">维持，这是维生素</span><span style="font-family: Calibri;">K</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">回收途径的一部分。</span><span style="font-family: Calibri;">Mishima</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">等人在《自然》杂志上发表文章，确定了一种不同的还原酶的身份，这种还原酶在另一个关键的维生素</span><span style="font-family: Calibri;">K</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">回收途径中发挥作用。</span><br></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">当需要治疗凝血时</span><span style="font-family:宋体">——例如，应对中风——</span></span><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;color: #0000FF;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">使用抗凝剂华法林抑制</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">VKOR</span></span></strong><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，从而降低血液中促进凝血的</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">VKH2</span><span style="font-family:宋体">水平</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">(</span><span style="font-family:宋体">图</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">1a)</span><span style="font-family:宋体">。华法林治疗虽然有效，但很容易导致华法林中毒，由于缺乏凝血而出现危及生命的出血。华法林中毒的标准临床治疗是服用高剂量的维生素</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">K</span><span style="font-family:宋体">，维生素</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">K</span><span style="font-family:宋体">通过华法林耐药还原酶降低到</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">VKH2</span><span style="font-family:宋体">，从而使凝血成为可能。</span></span><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;color: #0000FF;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">到目前为止，这种</span><span style="font-family:宋体">“非典型”还原酶的身份还不为人知。</span></span></strong></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;color: #0000FF;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体"><a href="https://www.nature.com/articles/d41586-022-02001-6" target="_blank">Long-sought mediator of vitamin K recycling discovered (nature.com)</a></span></span></strong></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;color: #0000FF;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202208/04/222921al1c51qgcihwqh5q.png" title alt="d41586-022-02001-6_23293716.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></span></span></strong></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">图</span>1 |<span style="font-family:宋体">维生素</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">K</span><span style="font-family:宋体">回收</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">a</span><span style="font-family:宋体">，在人体内，摄入的维生素</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">K (VK)</span><span style="font-family:宋体">被维生素</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">K</span><span style="font-family:宋体">环氧还原酶</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">(VKOR)</span><span style="font-family:宋体">还原，生成还原的对苯二酚形式的维生素</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">K, VKH2</span><span style="font-family:宋体">。这种形式的维生素</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">K</span><span style="font-family:宋体">作为</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">γ-</span><span style="font-family:宋体">谷氨酰基羧化酶</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">(GGCX)</span><span style="font-family:宋体">的辅助因子，该酶可以改变几种凝血因子以促进血液凝结，从</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">VKH2</span><span style="font-family:宋体">生成环氧维生素</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">K (VKO)</span><span style="font-family:宋体">作为副产品。然后，它可以通过同样涉及</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">VKOR</span><span style="font-family:宋体">的步骤回收回</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">VK</span><span style="font-family:宋体">。药物华法林抑制</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">VKOR</span><span style="font-family:宋体">，阻断循环，防止血液凝固。</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">b, Mishima </span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">等</span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">报道</span>FSP1<span style="font-family:宋体">酶也可以降低</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">VK</span><span style="font-family:宋体">促进凝血，但不被华法林抑制。</span></span><strong><span style="font-family: Calibri;line-height: 200%;color: #0000FF;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">他们还发现，通过这一途径产生的</span>VKH2<span style="font-family:宋体">可以消除一种叫做脂质过氧自由基</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">(LOO•)</span><span style="font-family:宋体">的自由基，这种自由基会促进一种被称为</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">ferroptosis</span><span style="font-family:宋体">的细胞死亡。</span></span></strong><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">这种相互作用产生了称为脂质氢过氧化物</span>(LOOH)<span style="font-family:宋体">的中间体，并将</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">VKH2</span><span style="font-family:宋体">循环回</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">VK</span><span style="font-family:宋体">。 </span></span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">目前进行这项研究的小组已经熟悉一种候选还原酶，即</span>FSP1<span style="font-family:宋体">酶。研究人员之前已经证明</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">FSP1</span><span style="font-family:宋体">是一种亚铁沉淀的抑制因子。亚铁沉淀是一种细胞死亡的形式，当自由基破坏脂质形成脂质过氧化物时就会触发这种现象。该研究小组已经证明，</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">FSP1</span><span style="font-family:宋体">利用分子泛素</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">(CoQ10)</span><span style="font-family:宋体">作为底物来清除细胞中的脂质自由基。在目前的研究中，</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">Mishima</span><span style="font-family:宋体">等人研究了</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">FSP1</span><span style="font-family:宋体">是否也可以降低维生素</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">K</span><span style="font-family:宋体">，维生素</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">K</span><span style="font-family:宋体">与</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">CoQ10</span><span style="font-family:宋体">在结构上有相似之处。 </span></span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">事实上，作者发现</span>FSP1<span style="font-family:宋体">降低了维生素</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">K</span><span style="font-family:宋体">，并且对华法林不敏感。在华法林中毒并接受高剂量维生素</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">K</span><span style="font-family:宋体">治疗的小鼠中，</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">Fsp1</span><span style="font-family:宋体">对防止致命的脑出血是必要的。此外，华法林处理过的人类细胞在缺乏</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">FSP1</span><span style="font-family:宋体">的情况下，只有最小的能力降低维生素</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">K</span><span style="font-family:宋体">水平。因此，</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">FSP1</span><span style="font-family:宋体">是长期寻求的非典型维生素</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">K</span><span style="font-family:宋体">减少的中介</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">(</span><span style="font-family:宋体">图</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">1b)</span><span style="font-family:宋体">。</span></span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">这一发现表明，在处理血栓和其他凝血障碍时，应考虑</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">FSP1</span><span style="font-family:宋体">。在人类中，编码</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">FSP1</span><span style="font-family:宋体">基因的遗传变异已经被鉴定出来，但它们对</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">FSP1</span><span style="font-family:宋体">功能的影响还没有被鉴定出来。人群中</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">FSP1</span><span style="font-family:宋体">活性的变化可能会降低华法林在</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">FSP1</span><span style="font-family:宋体">活性较高人群中的有效性。相比之下，</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">FSP1</span><span style="font-family:宋体">活性低的人可能需要更多的维生素</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">K</span><span style="font-family:宋体">来对抗华法林中毒。未来的研究将进一步探讨个体</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">FSP1</span><span style="font-family:宋体">功能、膳食维生素</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">K</span><span style="font-family:宋体">摄入量和华法林敏感性之间的相互作用。 </span></span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">接下来，</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">Mishima</span><span style="font-family:宋体">等人研究了维生素</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">K</span><span style="font-family:宋体">是否也可以作为</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">fsp1</span><span style="font-family:宋体">介导的过氧化物解毒的底物。第二种酶，谷胱甘肽过氧化物酶</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">4 (GPX4)</span><span style="font-family:宋体">，也可以消除脂质过氧化物，所以作者在实验中使用了一系列缺乏</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">GPX4</span><span style="font-family:宋体">的细胞和小鼠模型来消除该途径的影响。维生素</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">K</span><span style="font-family:宋体">以三种形式摄入——自然产生的叶醌</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">(PK)</span><span style="font-family:宋体">或甲萘醌</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">(MK4)</span><span style="font-family:宋体">，或合成的甲萘醌——作者发现这三种形式都可以抑制</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">gpx4</span><span style="font-family:宋体">缺失细胞的</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">ferroptosis</span><span style="font-family:宋体">。重要的是，在体外和体内，</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">FSP1</span><span style="font-family:宋体">还原酶活性对维生素</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">K</span><span style="font-family:宋体">的抗</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">ferroptotic</span><span style="font-family:宋体">作用至关重要。作者证明，</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">FSP1</span><span style="font-family:宋体">将维生素</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">K</span><span style="font-family:宋体">降低为</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">VKH2, VKH2</span><span style="font-family:宋体">作为一种抗氧化剂，清除自由基。这可以防止脂质过氧化并使维生素</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">K</span><span style="font-family:宋体">再生</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">(</span><span style="font-family:宋体">图</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">1c)</span><span style="font-family:宋体">。</span></span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">有趣的是，</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">Mishima</span><span style="font-family:宋体">等人发现，与</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">CoQ10</span><span style="font-family:宋体">相比，</span></span><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;color: #0000FF;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">维生素</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">K</span><span style="font-family:宋体">的</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">MK4</span><span style="font-family:宋体">形式在去除过氧化物和抑制</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">ferroptosis</span><span style="font-family:宋体">方面表现更好</span></span></strong><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。既然</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">FSP1</span><span style="font-family:宋体">可以同时使用这两种基质，那么一个明显的问题是，在体内哪种基质是首选的</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">?</span><span style="font-family:宋体">作者没有直接检验这一点，但从进化中有一些线索。</span></span><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;color: #0000FF;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">维生素</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">K</span><span style="font-family:宋体">在细菌和植物中是主要的电子载体，而在动物中，由于</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">CoQ10</span><span style="font-family:宋体">具有更高的氧化还原电位和丰度，大气的原始氧化作用似乎选择了</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">CoQ10</span></span></strong><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。这一信息表明，</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">CoQ10</span><span style="font-family:宋体">可能是动物体内首选的底物，作为一种治疗策略，高剂量维生素</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">K</span><span style="font-family:宋体">有可能抑制</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">ferroptosis</span><span style="font-family:宋体">。 </span></span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style="line-height: 200%; font-size: 16px;"><span style="font-family: 宋体;">当组织失去血液供应并随后恢复时</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">——这种破坏性的现象被称为缺血再灌注损伤</span><span style="font-family: Calibri;">(IRI)</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">——</span><span style="font-family: Calibri;">铁死亡</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">会造成相当大的伤害。在最后一组实验中，</span><span style="font-family: Calibri;">Mishima</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">等人表明，在肝脏或肾脏遭受</span><span style="font-family: Calibri;">IRI</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">的小鼠中，补充维生素</span><span style="font-family: Calibri;">K</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">可以抑制</span><span style="font-family: Calibri;">ferroptosis</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">，并防止组织损伤性炎症。此外，维生素</span><span style="font-family: Calibri;">K</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">还能防止神经元中的</span><span style="font-family: Calibri;">铁死亡</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">，证明了它在整个组织中的广泛作用。虽然这些研究的重点是在</span><span style="font-family: Calibri;">FSP1</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">背景下维生素</span><span style="font-family: Calibri;">K</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">抑制</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">铁死亡</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">的活性，但结果也提出了</span><span style="font-family: Calibri;">VKOR</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">是否能在某些背景下促进</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">铁死亡</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">抑制的问题。 </span></span></p><p style="margin-left: 0px; text-indent: 28px; line-height: 200%;"><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">这些发现表明，</span><strong><span style="font-family:宋体">使用维生素</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">K</span><span style="font-family:宋体">补充剂作为一种有效的手段，在高缺血风险人群</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">(</span><span style="font-family:宋体">例如那些有心血管疾病的人</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">)</span><span style="font-family:宋体">中避免</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">IRI</span><span style="font-family:宋体">具有令人兴奋的潜力。</span></strong><span style="font-family:宋体">维生素</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">K</span><span style="font-family:宋体">耐受性良好，</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">PK</span><span style="font-family:宋体">或</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">MK4</span><span style="font-family:宋体">剂型治疗华法林中毒没有发现毒性。然而，它是作为急性剂量服用的，长期服用的影响尚未确定。现在需要进行临床试验来测试这种应用——而且由于维生素</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">K</span><span style="font-family:宋体">已经被用于人体，这些可能很快就会发生。</span></span></p><p style="margin-left: 0px; text-indent: 28px; line-height: 200%;"><span
