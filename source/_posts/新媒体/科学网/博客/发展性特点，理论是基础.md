<p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">开展心理学研究，往往沿着一定的惯例，例如，从生活现实切入，把现象转化成变量，经过一系列探索，再回到生活现实进行检验。这样的研究路线，其实是哲学思想</span>“从实践到理论，再从理论到实践”的具体应用。编制一个心理量表，作法也是如此。如果能够在现实当中发现它测试的结果与已有观点一致，那么，这是对相应量表的更好检验。<span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Sonnentag & Wadian</span><span style="font-family:宋体">（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">2022</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）的第</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">4</span><span style="font-family:宋体">项研究，正是在现实当中检验道德捍卫倾向量表的效用。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">显然，该文的前面</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">3</span><span style="font-family:宋体">项研究，为道德捍卫倾向量表提供了相当强的信效度证据。在有道德问题的情境中，个体如果不反抗就会损害自己的价值观。个体反抗倾向的程度，可由道德捍卫倾向量表进行测试。为了扩展前面的研究以及道德捍卫倾向的已有文献，研究</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">4</span><span style="font-family:宋体">的目的是考察这个重要道德倾向的年龄相关趋势。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">此前关于道德捍卫倾向的所有研究，考察的都是青少年和成年早期个体，对有道德问题情境的原则性反抗（例如，</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Sonnentag & Barnett, 2016</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）。从而，已有文献中的一个重要缺口是，考察道德捍卫倾向是否随年龄而变化。有研究提示，个体对道德的理解在一生中都是发展的，道德身份和道德动机随年龄而增强（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Krettenauer & Victor, 2017</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">据此，研究者预测：个体的道德捍卫倾向将与年龄相关，并且，显示年龄相关的差异（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">age-related differences</span><span style="font-family:宋体">），也就是说，与年轻人相比，中年人和老年人面对有道德问题的情境，将会表现出更强的原则性对抗倾向。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">研究</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">4</span><span style="font-family:宋体">采用便利样本，被试为</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">283</span><span style="font-family:宋体">名美国成人，通过</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Amazon</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px">’</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">s Mechanical Turk</span><span style="font-family:宋体">线上平台招募。在数据分析前，剔除了那些没有通过质量保证题项的被试（</span></span><em><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: "Times New Roman";">n</span></em><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">=27</span><span style="font-family:宋体">），最后的样本量是</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">256</span><span style="font-family:宋体">人（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">55%</span><span style="font-family:宋体">为女性，</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">44%</span><span style="font-family:宋体">为男性），年龄为</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">19~75</span><span style="font-family:宋体">岁（</span></span><em><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: "Times New Roman";">M</span></em><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">=41.84</span><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><em><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: "Times New Roman";">SD</span></em><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">=14.19</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">研究者要进行一个分类变量</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">3</span><span style="font-family:宋体">个水平（即，</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">3</span><span style="font-family:宋体">个年龄组）的方差分析，推测存在中效应量（</span></span><em><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: "Times New Roman";">f</span></em><span style="font-size: 10px;"><sup><span style="vertical-align: super; font-family: "Times New Roman";">2</span></sup></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">=0.25</span><span style="font-family:宋体">），设定效力</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">0.80</span><span style="font-family:宋体">，显著性水平</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">0.05</span><span style="font-family:宋体">，用</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">G*Power</span><span style="font-family:宋体">进行事前效力分析，发现需要的最小被试量为</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">159</span><span style="font-family:宋体">。显然，</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">256</span><span style="font-family:宋体">人的样本量是合适的。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">样本中的大部分被试为欧洲裔美国人（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">80%</span><span style="font-family:宋体">），少部分被试为非洲裔（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">8%</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）、亚洲裔（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">5%</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）、西班牙裔（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">4%</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）或多种族（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">3%</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）美国人。总体而言，被试有良好的教育背景，</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">40%</span><span style="font-family:宋体">有</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">4</span><span style="font-family:宋体">年大学学位，</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">11%</span><span style="font-family:宋体">有研究生学位。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 宋体;">提供知情同意书后，被试填写道德捍卫倾向量表（</span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px">α</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">=0.93</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）和人口统计学问卷。实际上，填写这个问卷，是道德捍卫倾向年龄相关大型研究的一部分。被试作答线束，研究者对他们表示感谢，进行事后解释，并付</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">1</span><span style="font-family:宋体">美元的报酬。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 宋体;">结果表明，被试的年龄与他们在有道德问题情境中的反抗倾向呈正相关（</span><em><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: "Times New Roman";">r</span></em><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">=0.29</span><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><em><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: "Times New Roman";">p</span></em><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman"><.01</span><span style="font-family:宋体">，见图</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">1</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）。为了考察成人道德捍卫倾向的年龄相关趋势，研究者做了探索性方差分析，检验成年阶段的道德捍卫倾向是否存在年龄差异。采用三分法，尽量使各个组的样本量相等，把被试分成</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">3</span><span "Times New Roman";">F</span></em><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">(2,253)=10.55</span><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><em><span
