letter-spacing: 1px;">”</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">的关系，它准确而又完整地表达了这门新学科的内容。因此，这个建议顺利地被出席大会的学者们所接受。这次大会闭幕以后，遗传学家们也就名正言顺地称会议开始时仍称为的</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">“</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">杂交和植物育种会议</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">”</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">，改称为第三届国际遗传学大会。</span></span></p><p style="margin-bottom: 10px;text-indent: 33px;line-height: 125%"><span style="font-size: 20px;"><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">贝特森在创立遗传学术语方面所做出的贡献，英国遗传学家雷诺（</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">O.Raynor</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">）在</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">1961</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">年纪念贝特森诞辰一百周年时，曾专门谈道：</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">“</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">不管遗传学家在什么地方集会，他们总是使用贝特森所创立的术语。</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">”</span></span></p><p style="margin-bottom: 10px;line-height: 125%"><span style="font-size: 24px;"><strong><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">4 </span></strong><strong><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 黑体; letter-spacing: 1px;">发现</span></strong><strong><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">“</span></strong><strong><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 黑体; letter-spacing: 1px;">例外</span></strong><strong><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">”</span></strong><strong><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 黑体; letter-spacing: 1px;">，启迪来者</span></strong></span></p><p style="margin-bottom: 10px;text-indent: 33px;line-height: 125%"><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px; font-size: 20px;">孟德尔奠基的遗传学的迅速发展，促使着遗传学家更加深入地投身到动植物遗传实验的研究中，并试图去发现新的遗传现象。</span></p><p style="margin-bottom: 10px;text-indent: 33px;line-height: 125%"><span style="font-size: 20px;"><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">1906</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">年，贝特森和庞尼特在用香豌豆进行的杂交实验中发现，紫花和红花、长形花粉和圆形花粉这两对相对性状，在</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">F<sub>2</sub></span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">中的分离比例都各自符合孟德尔的</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">3:1</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">。将紫花、长形花粉和红花、圆形花粉的纯种植株作亲本，进行杂交，结果</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">F<sub>1</sub></span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">的表现型都是紫花和长形花粉。但是，</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">F<sub>2</sub></span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">所出现的</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">4</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">种表型，其比例却不符合孟德尔的</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">9:3:3:1</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">，其中紫长和红圆这两种亲本组合的实际个体数，所占比例分别远远超过</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">9/16</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">和</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">1/16</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">，而相应的紫圆和红长这两种重新组合的实际个体数，所占比例却大大少于</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">3/16</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">。他们把亲本的性状组合调换一下，即用紫花、圆形花粉和红花、长形花粉的变种植株杂交，实验结果与第一个实验相同，</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">F<sub>2</sub></span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">仍不符合孟德尔的</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">9:3:3:1</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">，结果依然是两种亲本组合的实际个体数所占比例分别远远超过</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">9/16</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">和</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">1/16</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">，而两种重新组合的实际个体数所占比例又大大少于</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">3/16</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">。</span></span></p><p style="margin-bottom: 10px;text-indent: 33px;line-height: 125%"><span style="font-size: 20px;"><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">当他们仔细分析这两个实验的资料时，却发现一个共同的遗传倾向，即：在</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">F<sub>2</sub></span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">的</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">4</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">种表现型中，类似亲本的表型总是远多于重组合的表型。也就是说，一个亲本所具有的两种性状在</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">F<sub>2</sub></span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">中常常连在一起遗传。这就意味着控制这两对相对性状的两对等位基因，在杂交子代中的组合并不是随机的，而是原来属于一个亲本的两种基因更倾向于出现在同一个配子中，也就是后来人们称为的</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">“</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">连锁遗传</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">”</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">现象。</span></span></p><p style="margin-bottom: 10px;text-indent: 33px;line-height: 125%"><span style="font-size: 20px;"><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">在香豌豆的紫花和红花、长形花粉和圆形花粉这两对相对性状的杂交实验中，所出现的重要的</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">“</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">例外</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">”</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">遗传现象，本来就是不同于孟德尔遗传定律的新发现。然而，由于贝特森本人的固执己见和保守的思想，使他对三年前美国遗传学家萨顿（</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">W.S.Sutton</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">）和德国实验胚胎学家鲍维里（</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">T.Boveri</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">）所提出的染色体学说，表示了极力反对。因此，贝特森也就不可能利用染色体的遗传理论，去深入地探究在香豌豆的杂交实验中发现的“例外”遗传现象之根本原因。贝特森更不可能想到在性状的连锁遗传中，还会有互换现象的发生。</span></span></p><p style="margin-bottom: 10px;text-indent: 33px;line-height: 125%"><span style="font-size: 20px;"><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">这样，在学术界曾经做出了不朽评论</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">“</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">珍惜你的例外吧！</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">”</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">的贝特森，最后终于被自己所发现的例外现象所迷惑。其结果，仅仅试图从狭隘的传统观念中，去寻找答案。最后只是提出了</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">“</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">相引</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">”</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">和</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">“</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">相斥</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">”</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">这两个名词术语，并没有给予理论上的任何解释。虽然新的遗传定律的发现与贝特森等人失之交臂，但是这个明显不同于孟德尔自由组合规律的</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">“</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">例外</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">”</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">，却为后来的遗传学家再次发现这种遗传现象的深入研究，提供了极为重要的启迪。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent: 33px;line-height: 125%"><span style="font-size: 20px;"><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">1912</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">年，美国遗传学家摩尔根（</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">T.H.Morgan</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">）和他的学生在果蝇的白眼和红眼、黄体和褐体这两对相对性状的遗传实验研究中，又一次发现了与当年贝特森等在香豌豆杂交实验中同样的遗传现象。摩尔根牢牢地抓住了这个例外，通过一系列精密设计的杂交实验，运用染色体理论，成功地解释出了这种</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">“</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">例外</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; letter-spacing: 1px;">”</span><span style="line-height: 125%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">遗传现象的根本原因，从而揭示出了遗传学上的第三个基本规律——连锁互换律，并由此奠定了细胞遗传学的坚实基础。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent: 33px;line-height: 125%"><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:125%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif';letter-spacing:1px"> </span></p><p style="margin-bottom:10px;text-align:right"><span style="font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px; font-size: 18px;">（原载《科学月刊》，本次发布作了标题改动、插图增添和个别文字的修订。）</span></p><p style="margin-bottom:10px;text-align:right;line-height:125%"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong><span style="line-height: 125%;
