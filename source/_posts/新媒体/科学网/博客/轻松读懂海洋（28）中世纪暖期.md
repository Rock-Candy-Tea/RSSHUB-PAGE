<p style="text-indent:28px;line-height:150%"><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">气候总体说来呈现波动特征，有降温就有升温，反之亦然。在长尺度上的波动具有一些周期性，英文叫做</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Calibri">P</span></span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px">eriodicity<span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">等等。在几十年尺度上的波动特征，常常叫做涛动（</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Calibri">O</span></span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px">scillation<span style="font-family:宋体">）。为啥不翻译成波动、震荡，而翻译成涛动？涛动听起来就很有文学底蕴。涛和波不完全一样，现在经常连用，叫做波涛，很容易让人联想起大海中波涛汹涌，一排排浪花从远处连绵不断地涌来。涛的本意是大波，从程度上比波纹要强烈。所以，涛动的含义是有一定的规模，而不是很小的或者可以被忽略的波动。成语涛声如雷，就不能变成波声如雷。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:28px;line-height:150%"><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">如果地势平坦，没有陆地分布，那么地球上大气的高压带和低压带也会成带状沿着纬向较为均匀地分布。但是，北半球存在着大块的大陆，把这些高低压带截断，而形成牛眼状闭环分布。在长期稳定存在的相邻的高低压带牛眼之间，一定存在着此消彼长的情况，并存在着一定的周期规律，这就是涛动。这种涛动还可以联想成磁场的正负异常分布，总是成对出现。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:28px;line-height:150%"><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">涛动的能量来源可以是海陆温度差。比如，在东亚和太平洋之间就会存在着海陆温度差，从而驱动一个东西向的</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">二级</span></span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">大气环流，造成北太平洋涛动现象</span>PDO<span style="font-family:宋体">。太平洋东西向也存在着温度差，由</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Calibri">W</span></span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px">alker<span style="font-family:宋体">环流控制。所以，在研究赤道太平洋区的时候，除了南北向的</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">ITCZ</span><span style="font-family:宋体">移动可以造成降雨变化，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">东西向的</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">W</span></span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px">alker<span style="font-family:宋体">环流变化也不能忽视。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:28px;line-height:150%"><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">在</span>LIA<span style="font-family:宋体">（</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Calibri">1</span></span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px">450-1850 </span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Calibri">CE</span></span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">）之前</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的中世纪，还发生过一段气候适宜期（</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">Medi</span></span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px">eval Climate Anomaly<span style="font-family:宋体">，</span><span style="text-indent:28px;line-height:150%"><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">结束，环境大变，生产力降低，瘟疫流行。这在某种意义上促进</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">了后期的欧洲文艺复兴，思想解放。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:28px;line-height:150%"><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Calibri">”</span><span style="font-family:宋体">早很多。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:28px;line-height:150%"><span style="font-family:宋体">类</span></span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">拉齐娜（</span>La Niña-like<span style="font-family:宋体">在</span>MCA<span style="font-family:宋体">和</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">LIA</span><span style="font-family:宋体">期间，涉及到了大西洋和北冰洋之间的水体相互交换，这肯定会造成北大西洋</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">AMOC</span><span style="font-family:宋体">的变化。但是，这又不引起南北半球的温度跷跷板效应，如何协调这种不匹配？</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:28px;line-height:150%"><span style="font-family:宋体">也就加强。反之，北冰洋水体活跃，</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">AMOC</span><span style="font-family:宋体">减弱。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:28px;line-height:150%"><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">结果显示，在</span>MCA <span style="font-family:宋体">初始期（</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Calibri">8</span></span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px">50-1000CE<span style="font-family:宋体">），</span></span><span style=";font-family:Symbol;line-height:150%;font-size:16px">D</span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px">R<span style="font-family:宋体">值很小，说明北大西洋的水占主导，表层</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">AMOC</span><span style="font-family:宋体">活跃。但是在</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Calibri">1</span></span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px">000CE<span style="font-family:宋体">之后，</span></span><span style=";font-family:Symbol;line-height:150%;font-size:16px">D</span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px">R<span style="font-family:宋体">开始增大，说明北冰洋的水开始占据主导，</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">AMOC</span><span style="font-family:宋体">减弱。这种情况一直持续到</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">LIA</span><span style="font-family:宋体">，并在</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Calibri">1</span></span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px">900CE<span style="font-family:宋体">之前达到最大值，说明</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">AMOC</span><span style="font-family:宋体">减弱非常明显。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:28px;line-height:150%"><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">其实，严格来讲，</span>AMOC<span style="font-family:宋体">减弱的说法不如</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">AMOC</span><span style="font-family:宋体">后撤更好理解。冰岛北面大陆坡刚好处于</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">NAC</span><span style="font-family:宋体">和北冰洋水团的锋面，所以该区被被冰洋海水占领，只能说明大西洋水的影响向南移动，此时</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">AMOC</span><span style="font-family:宋体">对欧洲的整体并未明显减弱，所以，在</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Calibri">1</span></span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px">000CE<span style="font-family:宋体">之后，</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">MCA</span><span style="font-family:宋体">依然还处于旺盛期。但是，至少说明，随后的</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">LIA</span><span style="font-family:宋体">并非没有前兆，而是在</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">LIA</span><span style="font-family:宋体">真正发生之前，北冰洋的水团已经在冰岛附近入侵。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:28px;line-height:150%"><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">任何事情都有前兆（</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Calibri">p</span></span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px">recursor<span style="font-family:宋体">）是最为合理的模式，否则，</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">NAO</span><span style="font-family:宋体">的正负相位突变很难理解。在</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Calibri">1</span></span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px">950<span style="font-family:宋体">年之后，</span></span><span style=";font-family:Symbol;line-height:150%;font-size:16px">D</span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px">R<span style="font-family:宋体">再次减弱，说明北大西洋的海水又开始返回冰岛北部，表层</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">AMOC</span><span style="font-family:宋体">变得活跃。</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">现今观测表明，</span></span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">在过去</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Calibri">1</span></span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px">00-200<span style="font-family:宋体">年，通过弗拉姆海峡（</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">Fram Strait</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）的大西洋水增多，与其它记录非常一致。现今的模式更趋向于</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">MCA</span><span style="font-family:宋体">模式，这是否是欧洲更热的标志？</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:28px;line-height:150%"><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">虽然上述记录清晰地显示，</span>MCA<span style="font-family:宋体">和</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">LIA</span><span style="font-family:宋体">和北大西洋表层</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">AMOC</span><span style="font-family:宋体">，也就是大西洋水团和北冰洋水团的交换有关系，但是具体到背后的物理机制，客观地说还没有完全的定论。如果北大西洋水团和北冰洋水团的锋面逐渐向南移动，在冰岛南部区域做类似的工作会有助于我们确定上述模型。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:28px;line-height:150%"><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">和</span>LIA<span style="font-family:宋体">类似，</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">MCA</span><span style="font-family:宋体">也是一种全球行为，热带海洋</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">SST</span><span style="font-family:宋体">的变化也深入参与其中。在太平区，热带</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">SST</span><span style="font-family:宋体">变化又会进一步和</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">NESO</span><span style="font-family:宋体">相关。比如，</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">MCA</span><span style="font-family:宋体">期间，印太地区</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">SST</span><span style="font-family:宋体">增加，而东热带太平洋则降温，增加东西太平洋的温度差，造成类拉齐娜状态，通过大气传导，让</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">NAO</span><span style="font-family:宋体">指标增加。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:28px;line-height:150%"><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">通过北非的石笋研究发现，</span>MCA<span style="font-family:宋体">期间，还存在内部的二级震荡行为，也就是</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">NAO</span><span style="font-family:宋体">虽然整体处于正的模式，但是也存在二级波动，对低纬度地区产生影响。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:28px;line-height:150%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Calibri">M</span></span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px">CA<span style="font-family:宋体">期间，欧洲降雨量减少，而在</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">LIA</span><span style="font-family:宋体">期间，欧洲降雨量反而增强，这种现象和</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">ENSO</span><span style="font-family:宋体">与</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">NAO</span><span style="font-family:宋体">之间的相互作用相关。不只是欧洲降雨少，在东亚地区，在</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">MCA</span><span style="font-family:宋体">期间东亚季风整体变强的大背景下，在</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Calibri">1</span></span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px">000-1100CE<span style="font-family:宋体">则发生了干旱行为。科学家把这种异常行为归结为印度洋</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Calibri">-</span><span style="font-family:宋体">西太暖池变化。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:28px;line-height:150%"><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">现今观测发现</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Calibri">1</span></span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px">970<span style="font-family:宋体">年以来，地中海地区变得干旱，而且在未来几年，温室效应增强，这一干旱趋势有可能加剧。虽然整体上说，</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">MCA</span><span style="font-family:宋体">期间，欧洲变得干旱，但是干旱指数具有强烈的空间不均一性。通过系统的数据编辑，科学家追踪过过去千年以来欧洲地区的干旱演化历史，这为预测未来的干旱趋势提供了新思路。研究发现，中北欧地区是收到干旱侵袭最严重的地区，这一地区的平均降雨量，或者干旱指数和</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">MCA</span><span style="font-family:宋体">和</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">LIA</span><span style="font-family:宋体">对应关系非常好。同时，研究也发现在空间上，降雨量或者干旱指标分布非常不均匀，显然这和大气环流分布密切相关。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:28px;line-height:150%"><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">通过对比</span>MCA/LIA<span style="font-family:宋体">、</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Calibri">B</span></span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px">A/YD<span style="font-family:宋体">、</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">H1</span><span style="font-family:宋体">、</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">OA</span><span style="font-family:宋体">事件，我们发现他们在全球的影响不一样。</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">LIA</span><span style="font-family:宋体">虽然幅度小，但是通过大气传播，全球都有响应。问题是，难道在</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">YD</span><span style="font-family:宋体">和</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">H1</span><span style="font-family:宋体">事件期间，大气就不在全球传播信号了吗？这当然不是，只不过这小幅度温度变化在</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">YD</span><span style="font-family:宋体">和</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">H1</span><span style="font-family:宋体">这种大规模降温事件中被淹没了</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:28px;line-height:150%"><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">另外在对</span>AMOC<span style="font-family:宋体">影响上，</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">MCA/LIA</span><span style="font-family:宋体">只影响了表层</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">AMOC</span><span style="font-family:宋体">的短暂变化，对深海环流没造成实质影响。</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">YD</span><span style="font-family:宋体">这种事件，对深层环流产生影响，但是并没有完全停止，其影响范围在北半球强。而对于</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">H1</span><span style="font-family:宋体">事件来说，对</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">AMOC</span><span style="font-family:宋体">的影响最完全，从浅部到深海几乎全都停止，造成南北半球跷跷板效应。</span></span></p><p><br></p>                    <br><br>
