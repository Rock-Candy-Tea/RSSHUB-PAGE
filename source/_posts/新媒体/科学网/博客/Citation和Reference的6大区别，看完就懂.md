<p style="text-align: center;"> <strong><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">共同点 </span></strong><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;"> </span></p><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">如果了解引文（citation）和参考文献（Reference）的共同点，就很容易理解它们之间的区别。</span></p><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">● 两者都表示学术文本的信息来源。<br></span></p><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">● 两者互相关联，引文（在正文中）对应参考文献（通常放在正文末尾，比如，在参考书目bibliography中）。</span></p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">  区别  </span></strong></p><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">1.  <strong>目的</strong>：引文是为了指出附加信息，参考文献是为了提供附加信息。</span></p><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">2.  <strong>位置</strong>：引文在正文中出现，参考文献在正文末尾按一定的的顺序罗列。</span></p><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">3.  <strong>信息量</strong>：引文提供有限的信息，参考文献提供全部详细信息。</span></p><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">4.  <strong>长度</strong>：引文较短，包括作者姓氏和出版年份，或作为脚注出现。相比之下，参考文献较长。</span></p><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">5.  <strong>相互引用</strong>：<span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px; color: #0070C0;">有些引文没有对应的文献（比如，个人电子邮件或未发表的数据），但每条参考文献都必须在正文中有对应的引文</span>。</span></p><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">  <strong>引文和参考文献举例</strong>  </span></p><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">本文从一篇具有代表性的学术论文中摘录部分内容，来说明引文和参考文献的差异。</span></p><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">下文中加粗的是引文：</span></p><blockquote><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">“We have yet fully to understand how typography should be designed when glance reading competes with other cognitive tasks, such as driving while using an in-vehicle display (Reimer et al. 2014) or walking while reading on a mobile device (Chen and Lin 2016).” </span></p></blockquote><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">在论文的末尾，References（参考文献）部分列出了参考文献，按作者姓名首字母拼音排序。列表中包括了以下两篇参考文献（即上方引文的参考文献）</span></p><blockquote><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">References</span></p><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">Chen, C.-M., and Y.-J. Lin. 2016. “Effects of Different Text Display Types on Reading Comprehension, Sustained Attention and Cognitive Load in Mobile Reading Contexts.” Interactive Learning Environments 24 (3): 553–571.</span></p><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">Reimer, B., B. Mehler, J. Dobres, J.F. Coughlin, S. Matteson, D. Gould, N. Chahine, and V. Levantovsky.2014."Assessing the Impact of Typeface Design in a Text-Rich Automotive User Interface."Ergonomics 57 (11): 1643–1658.</span></p></blockquote><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;"> 6. <strong>形式不同</strong>  </span></p><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">引文和参考文献的另一个区别是形式。引文可以包含字母（作者姓名）和数字（出版年份），也可以只有数字，而对应的参考文献通常只有一种形式。</span></p><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">上面提到的引文（为方便阅读，下面再次列出）包括作者姓名和出版年份。</span></p><blockquote><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">“We have yet fully to understand how typography should be designed when glance reading competes with other cognitive tasks, such as driving while using an in-vehicle display (Reimer et al. 2014) or walking while reading on a mobile device (Chen and Lin）</span></p></blockquote><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">注：以上这种引文格式被称为<span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px; color: #0070C0;">哈佛注释体系（Harvard system）</span>。</span></p><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">另一种引文体系只有数字没有姓名。请注意下面例子中加粗上标的“18”。</span></p><blockquote><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">“We focus on domestic food value chains for the simple reason that roughly three-quarters of all food is consumed in the same country as its raw commodity inputs were grown18.”</span></p></blockquote><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">在该论文的末尾，有一个带序号的参考文献列表，按出现的顺序排列，以下是第18条参考文献：</span></p><blockquote><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">D’Odorico, P., Carr, J. A., Laio, F., Ridolfi, L. & Vandoni, S. Feeding humanity through global food trade. Earth’s Future 2, 458–469 (2014).</span></p></blockquote><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">注：以上这种引文体系被称为<span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px; color: #0070C0;">温哥华注释体系（Vancouver system），常见于医学期刊和综述论文</span>。</span></p><hr><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202201/12/102618ziiuiu5zodi8iiyg.jpg" title alt="113658obo3kvsuais24qkd.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://www.cwauthors.com.cn/prices/?utm_source=sciencenetblog&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=snprice" target="_blank"><span style="text-decoration: underline; font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">论文润色</span></a><span style="text-decoration: underline; font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;"> | <a href="https://www.cwauthors.com.cn/_Translationservices/?utm_source=sciencenetblog&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=sntranslation" target="_blank">学术翻译</a> | <a href="https://www.cwauthors.com.cn/Servicesv2/?utm_source=sciencenetblog&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=snotherservice" target="_blank">其他发表支持服务</a> | <a href="https://www.cwauthors.com.cn/CharlesworthEditors/?utm_source=sciencenetblog&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=sneditor" target="_blank">英国编辑团队介绍</a></span></p>                    <br><br>
