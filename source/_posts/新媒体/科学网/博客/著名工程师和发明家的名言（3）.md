<p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;">著名工程师和发明家的名言（3）</p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;">武夷山 译</p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;">    Workflowmax网站于2020年4月提供了著名工程师和发明家的一些名言（博主：原文如此，其实有的语录的主人并非工程师，只是语录内容涉及工程或工程师），下面我翻译其中一些，与大家分享。这是第三部分。</p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;">  </p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;">1.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal;font-variant-east-asian: normal;font-stretch: normal;font-size: 9px;line-height: normal;font-family: 'Times New Roman'">  </span><strong><span style="font-family:宋体">“What we usually consider as impossible are simply engineering problems… there’s no law of physics preventing them.”</span></strong><br> - Michio Kaku，著名日裔美国物理学家加来道雄</p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;">    我们通常认为不可能实现的事物基本上都是工程问题......并没有哪条物理学定律妨碍其实现。</p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;">2.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal;font-variant-east-asian: normal;font-stretch: normal;font-size: 9px;line-height: normal;font-family: 'Times New Roman'">  </span><strong><span style="font-family:宋体">“The most important thing is to keep the most important thing the most important thing.”</span></strong><br> - Donald P. Coduto，美国加利福尼亚州立理工大学土木工程学教授</p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"> 最重要的事就是，使最重要的事始终保持为最重要的事。</p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;">3.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal;font-variant-east-asian: normal;font-stretch: normal;font-size: 9px;line-height: normal;font-family: 'Times New Roman'">  </span><strong><span style="font-family:宋体">“Aeroplanes are not designed by science, but by art in spite of some pretence and humbug to the contrary. I do not mean to suggest that engineering can do without science, on the contrary, it stands on scientific foundations, but there is a big gap between scientific research and the engineering product which has to be bridged by the art of the engineer.”</span></strong><br> - British Engineer to the Royal Aeronautical Society（一位英国工程师对皇家航空学会说的话）, 1922.</p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;">    飞机不是凭借科学设计出来的，而是凭借技艺设计出来的，尽管有一些相反的自我标榜和谎言。我的意思不是说，做工程可以不要科学，恰恰相反，工程立足于科学基础之上，但是，科学研究与工程产品之间有巨大的鸿沟，只有靠工程师的技艺方能跨越这个鸿沟。</p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;">4.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal;font-variant-east-asian: normal;font-stretch: normal;font-size: 9px;line-height: normal;font-family: 'Times New Roman'">  </span><strong><span style="font-family:宋体">“ A great bridge is a great monument which should serve to make known the splendour and genius of a nation; one should not occupy oneself with efforts to perfect it architecturally, for taste is always susceptible to change, but to conserve always in its form and decoration the character of solidity which is proper.”</span></strong><br> - Jean Peronnet（1708-1794），法国土木工程师，以建造石拱桥闻名于世</p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;">    大桥是伟大的纪念碑，应能使一个民族的辉煌和才华为世人所知；不过人们不应该致力于在建筑学上完善大桥，因为鉴赏口味总是容易改变，而应致力于在形态和装饰上都维护大桥的坚固性，这才是正理。</p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;">5.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal;font-variant-east-asian: normal;font-stretch: normal;font-size: 9px;line-height: normal;font-family: 'Times New Roman'">  </span><strong><span style="font-family:宋体">“An engineer is someone who is good with figures, but doesn't have the personality of an accountant.”</span></strong><br> - An Arts graduate's view of engineers，一位艺术专业毕业生对工程师的看法</p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;">工程师是对数字敏感的人，但其个性与会计师不同。</p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;">6.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal;font-variant-east-asian: normal;font-stretch: normal;font-size: 9px;line-height: normal;font-family: 'Times New Roman'">  </span><strong><span style="font-family:宋体">“ Architects and engineers are among the most fortunate of men since they build their own monuments with public consent, public approval and often public money.”</span></strong><br> - John Prebble</p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;">    建筑师和工程师属于最幸运的人，因为他们建造自己的事业丰碑之事却经过了公众的同意和政府的批准，往往花的还是公共财政的钱。</p><p style="line-height: 1.75em;"><strong><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">相关阅读</span></strong></p><p style="line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;color:#333333">著名工程师和发明家的名言（1），</span><a href="http://blog.sciencenet.cn/blog-1557-1284837.html" target="_blank"><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;color:#006699">http://blog.sciencenet.cn/blog-1557-1284837.html</span></a></p><p style="line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;color:#333333">著名工程师和发明家的名言（2），</span><a href="http://blog.sciencenet.cn/blog-1557-1285510.html" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体">http://blog.sciencenet.cn/blog-1557-1285510.html</span></a></p><p><br></p>                    <br><br>
