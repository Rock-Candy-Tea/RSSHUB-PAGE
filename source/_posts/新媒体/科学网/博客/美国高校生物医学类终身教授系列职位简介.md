<p style=";font-size: medium;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal"><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">【最近有不少关于大学 </span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">tenure </span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">职位</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif"> </span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">“</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">非升即走</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">”</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">的讨论，有人问起我美国大学的这套系统到底怎么回事】</span></p><p style=";font-size: medium;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal"><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif"> </span></p><p style=";font-size: medium;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal"><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">我在美国一个州立大学的兽医和生物医学学院任职多年后提前退休，并全职回国，经历过从</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif"> </span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">tenure-track Assistant Professor </span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">至</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">tenured Associated Professor</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">、</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">Full Professor</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">、和</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif"> </span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">Professor Emeritus全套</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">任职过程，也主持或参与过多个教授的招聘和之后 </span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">promotion & tenure </span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">（</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">P&T</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">）</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">committees</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">，可以对一般的州立大学的 </span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">tenure </span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">系统做个很简单的介绍。</span></p><p style=";font-size: medium;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal"><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif"> </span></p><p style=";font-size: medium;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal"><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">1</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">、新</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">tenure-track </span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">职位的设立：一般根据系、学院和学校的学科发展和教学需要确定，得到校办批准后设立。</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">Tenure-track  </span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">职位必须有特定的预算（工资和启动经费等），可以是系或学院的预算经费，也可以从学校甚至州政府争取部分或全部。</span></p><p style=";font-size: medium;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal"><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif"> </span></p><p style=";font-size: medium;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal"><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">2</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">、工资来源：根据学校，或系和学院情况。有单位全包的，也有单位保证部分工资的。不同单位差异可以很大，大多在保证</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">25% - 75% </span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">之间吧。一般来说，入职后前几年单位提供的比例高，然后逐年减少。</span></p><p style=";font-size: medium;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal"><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif"> </span></p><p style=";font-size: medium;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal"><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">3</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">、入职起点：可以是</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif"> </span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">tenure-track </span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">助理教授</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">, </span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">也可以是</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif"> </span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">tenure-track </span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">副教授。很优秀的可以直接给 </span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">tenure</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">，入职时就是</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif"> </span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">tenured </span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">副教授或正教授（</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">arrival on tenure</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">）。但需要入职前经系和学院的 </span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">P&T committees </span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">审查通过、学校批准。</span></p><p style=";font-size: medium;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal"><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif"> </span></p><p style=";font-size: medium;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal"><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">4</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">、所谓</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">“</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">非升即走</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">”</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">：一般情况下，大学助理教授的</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif"> </span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">tenure clock </span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">是</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">7</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">年（有些联邦或州的研究机构可以短至</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">5</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">年）。通常第</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">5</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">年申请</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif"> </span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">P&T</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">，优秀的可以提前申请。助理教授必须同时申请</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif"> </span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">P&T</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">，不能选择其一。如果没有通过，第</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">6</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">年可以再申请。第</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">6</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">年没有通过</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">P&T</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">的，会有</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">1-2</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">年时间找工作和离开。有特殊情况的，</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">tenure clock </span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">可以调整。</span></p><p style=";font-size: medium;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal"><br></p><p style=";font-size: medium;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal"><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">5</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">、给新招的助理教授提供帮助：我之前任职的学院，新招的助理教授会有一个由</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">3-4</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">位已</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">tenured</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">的教授组成的</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif"> </span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">mentoring committee</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">，为其提供帮助和指导。每年有个简单的评估，第</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">3</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">年有个中期评估。如有影响到</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">P&T</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">的不足地方，</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">committee</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">会尽量提供建设性意见。M</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">entoring committee</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">不保证成功，但保证给予指导和帮助、接受咨询。</span></p><p style=";font-size: medium;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal"><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif"> </span></p><p style=";font-size: medium;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal"><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">6</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">、助理教授权益（vs <span style="font-family: SimSun; font-size: 14px;">副教授、正教授</span>）：工作上，助理教授与副教授、正教授完全平等，都是独立的</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">PI</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">，都可以招博士生和博士后，可以作为</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">PI</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">申请各类科研项目。</span></p><p style=";font-size: medium;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal"><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif"> </span></p><p style=";font-size: medium;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal"><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">7</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">、科研为主的</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">P&T</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">至终身副教授：主要看申请人的学术能力、成就和潜力（</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">excellence in scholarship </span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">和 </span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">potential to develop an internationally recognized research program</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">），以及教学和服务。一般来讲，申请人建立了一个有特色的研究方向，研究有系统性和后劲（从发表的<span style="font-family: SimSun; font-size: 14px;">论文中体现</span>），有国家层面资助的科研项目（如在 NIH，NSF 和 USDA等通过竞争获得的项目），是 </span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">nationally recognized（参与项目评审、被邀请给学术演讲和大会发言等）</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">，也参与足够的教学并获不差的教学评估，</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">collegiality </span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">过得去，在单位和领域里也是个很好的 </span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">citizenship</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">，外审专家基本认可（一般会有5个以上美国和其他国家的专家），那么</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">P&T</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">基本没什么问题了。</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">P&T committee members </span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">不需要全票通过，但反对票不宜太多，反对票提出的弱点不太致命。</span></p><p style=";font-size: medium;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal"><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif"> </span></p><p style=";font-size: medium;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal"><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">8</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">、</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">P&T</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">竞争和名额：</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">P&T</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">没有同行竞争，也没有名额限制，主要是看申请人是否合格。同行竞争是申请入职时候，可以有</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">10</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">来个全球各地的竞争者，也可以有</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">1-2</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">百申请人。</span></p><p style=";font-size: medium;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal"><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif"> </span></p><p style=";font-size: medium;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal"><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">9</span><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: SimSun">、其他相关问题：上述是个简单的介绍。其他相关问题可以后续分别讨论。</span></p><p style=";font-size: medium;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal"><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif"> </span></p><p style=";font-size: medium;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal"><span style="font-size: 14px;font-family:
