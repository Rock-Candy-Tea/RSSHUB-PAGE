<div>   
<p style="text-indent:32px"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 宋体;">只要阅读一篇应用结构方程模型的论文，就会看到文中呈现的一系列拟合指数，它们都用符号表示，其中，</span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px">χ</span><span style="font-size: 10px;"><sup><span style="vertical-align: super; font-family: "Times New Roman";">2</span></sup><sup><span style="vertical-align: super; font-family: "Times New Roman";"></span></sup></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">总是排在第一。不过，有的研究者可能只是例行公事地给出这些拟合指数，并不理解对应的含义，亦不确切知道它们的作用，因此，流于模仿其他文献，列上拟合指数，按照有关资料提到的标准，接着描述</span>“这些拟合指数表明，模型拟合数据良好。”</span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 宋体;">实际上，进行结构方程模型分析，就是检验研究者提出的模型与样本数据的拟合情况，也就是整体模型的拟合程度。研究者一般用</span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px">χ</span><span style="font-size: 10px;"><sup><span style="vertical-align: super; font-family: "Times New Roman";">2</span></sup></span><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 宋体;">统计量作为结构方程模型整体拟合的检验。不过，</span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px">χ</span><span style="font-size: 10px;"><sup><span style="vertical-align: super; font-family: "Times New Roman";">2</span></sup><sup><span style="vertical-align: super; font-family: "Times New Roman";"></span></sup></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">检验对样本量具有依存性（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Bentler & Bonett, 1980</span><span style="font-family:宋体">），研究者就提出了评估模型拟合情况的其他拟合指数，作为</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px">χ</span><span style="font-size: 10px;"><sup><span style="vertical-align: super; font-family: "Times New Roman";">2</span></sup><sup><span style="vertical-align: super; font-family: "Times New Roman";"></span></sup></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">检验统计量的补充。可见，检验模型拟合数据程度的统计指标，原本就是<span style="text-indent: 32px; font-family: "Times New Roman";">χ</span><span style="text-indent: 32px; font-size: 10px;"><sup><span style="vertical-align: super; font-family: "Times New Roman";">2</span></sup></span><span style="text-indent: 32px; font-family: 宋体;">统计量，所以，报告结构方程模型的结果时，它位于最前面。其他拟合指数，可以分为<span style="font-size: 16px; text-indent: 32px; font-family: "times new roman";">3</span>类，分别是</span>增量拟合指数、绝对拟合指数、预测拟合指数（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Kline, 2005</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">增量拟合指数（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">incremental fit indices</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）度量一个模型对数据拟合的改进程度，是通过<span style="font-family: 宋体; text-indent: 32px;">比较一个具体结构方程模型与一个基线结构方程模型而得到的</span>。典型的基线比较模型是零模型（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">null model</span><span style="font-family:宋体">），在这个模型中，所有变量都是彼此独立的，也就是不相关的（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Bentler & Bonett, 1980</span><span style="font-family:宋体">），因此，又称独立模型（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">independence mode</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）。常见的增量拟合指数有：标准拟合指数（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Normed Fit Index, NFI</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）、增量拟合指数（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Incremental Fit Index, IFI</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）、非标准拟合指数（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Nonnormed Fit Index, NNFI</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）或</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Tucker-Lewis</span><span style="font-family:宋体">指数（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Tucker-Lewis Index, TLI</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）、比较拟合指数（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Comparative Fit Index, CFI</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）、简约比较拟合指数（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Parsimony Comparative Fit Index, PCFI</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）、相对非中心指数（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Relative Noncentrality Index, RNI</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">绝对拟合指数（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">absolute fit indices</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）度量一个结构方程模型对样本数据所发现的各种关系的解释程度。常见的绝对拟合指数有：</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px">χ</span><span style="font-size: 10px;"><sup><span style="vertical-align: super; font-family: "Times New Roman";">2</span></sup><sup><span style="vertical-align: super; font-family: "Times New Roman";"></span></sup></span><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: "Times New Roman";">/</span><em><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: "Times New Roman";">df</span></em><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 宋体;">比（</span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px">χ</span><span style="font-size: 10px;"><sup><span style="vertical-align: super; font-family: "Times New Roman";">2</span></sup><sup><span style="vertical-align: super; font-family: "Times New Roman";"></span></sup></span><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: "Times New Roman";">/</span><em><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: "Times New Roman";">df</span></em><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"> <span style="font-family:Times New Roman">ratio</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）、<span style="font-family: 宋体; text-indent: 32px;">拟合优度指数（</span><span style="text-indent: 32px; font-family: "Times New Roman";">Goodness-of-Fit Index, GFI</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; text-indent: 32px;">）、</span>调整的拟合优度指数（<span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: "times new roman";">Adjusted</span> </span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Goodness-of-Fit Index, AGFI</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）、</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">McDonald</span><span style="font-family:宋体">拟合指数（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">McDonald</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px">’</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">s Fit Index, MFI</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）或</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">McDonald</span><span style="font-family:宋体">中心性指数（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">McDonald</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px">’</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">s Centrality Index, MCI</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）、伽马帽（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Gamma Hat</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）、</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Hoelter </span></span><em><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: "Times New Roman";">N</span></em><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">、均方根残差（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Root Mean Square Residual, RMR</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）、标准化均方根残差（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Standardized Root Mean Square Residual, SRMR</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）、近似均方根误差（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Root Mean Square Error of Approximation, RMSEA</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">预测拟合指数（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">predictive fit indices</span><span style="font-family:宋体">），又称信息标准（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">information criteria</span><span style="font-family:宋体">），度量结构方程模型对来自同一总体的其他样本将会拟合的程度。常见的预测拟合指数有：</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Akaike</span><span style="font-family:宋体">信息标准（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Akaike</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px">’</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">s Information Criterion, AIC</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）、稳定性</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">AIC</span><span style="font-family:宋体">（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Consistent AIC, CAIC</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）、贝叶斯信息标准（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Bayesian Information Criterion, BIC</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）、预期交叉验证指数（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Expected Cross-Validation Index, ECVI</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">显然，结构方程模型的拟合指数十分多样。关于如何报告这些指数以及每个拟合指数的临界值，有不同的推荐标准（例如，</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Kline, 2005</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）。研究者通常把</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">IFI</span><span style="font-family:宋体">、</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">GFI</span><span style="font-family:宋体">、</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">AGFI</span><span style="font-family:宋体">、</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">MFI</span><span style="font-family:宋体">的值</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">>.90</span><span style="font-family:宋体">作为可以接受的临界值（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Bentler & Bonett, 1980</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）。后来，结构方程模型的研究者提出，把</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">.95</span><span style="font-family:宋体">作为一个更理想的水平（例如，</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Hu & Bentler, 1999</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）。同时，</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">SRMR<.10</span><span style="font-family:宋体">、</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">RMSEA</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px">≤</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">.05</span><span style="font-family:宋体">，表示模型拟合是可以接受的。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">关于这些指数的使用以及拟合结构方程模型的临界值一直存在争论（例如，</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Marsh, Hau, & Wen, 2004</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）。存在这类争论的一个原因是，从哪个指数是最佳的角度讲，研究发现是混杂的，拟合效果依赖于诸多研究特征，包括变量的个数（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Kenny & McCoach, 2003</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）、估计方法（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Hu & Bentler, 1998</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）、模型误设（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Hu & Bentler, 1999</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）、样本量</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">(Marsh, Balla, & Hau, 1996</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）。因此，研究者应当清楚，建议的临界标准是一般的指导方针，并不一定是最终的规则。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">按照</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Kline</span><span style="font-family:宋体">（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">2005</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）的观点，需要报告的拟合指数至少包括：（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">1</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px">χ</span><span style="font-size: 10px;"><sup><span style="vertical-align: super; font-family: "Times New Roman";">2</span></sup><sup><span style="vertical-align: super; font-family: "Times New Roman";"></span></sup></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">检验统计量及其对应的自由度、显著性水平；（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">2</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">RMSEA</span><span style="font-family:宋体">及对应的</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">90%</span><span style="font-family:宋体">置信区间；（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">3</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">CFI</span><span style="font-family:宋体">；（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">4</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">SRMR</span><span style="font-family:宋体">。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Hu & Bentler</span><span style="font-family:宋体">（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">1999</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）推荐，报告结构方程模型的发现时，使用两种指数的组合。具体而言，他们推荐使用</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">SRMR</span><span style="font-family:宋体">和如下的一个指数：</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">NFI</span><span style="font-family:宋体">、</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">IFI</span><span style="font-family:宋体">、</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">RNI</span><span style="font-family:宋体">、</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Gamma Hat</span><span style="font-family:宋体">、</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">MFI</span><span style="font-family:宋体">、</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">RMSEA</span><span style="font-family:宋体">。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">虽然有证据表明，</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Hu & Bentler</span><span style="font-family:宋体">（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">1999</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）的联合标准有助于将拒绝正确模型的可能性最小化，但是，也有证据表明，当用这些临界标准时，错设的模型可能会被认为是可以接受的。因此，关于需要报告的最少拟合指数，可以采用</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Kline</span><span style="font-family:宋体">（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">2005</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）推荐的方案。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">此外，结构方程模型是逐渐接近真相的，只要可能，研究者就比较竞争模型，并报告预测拟合指数（</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Worthington & Whittaker, 2006</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">）。最为重要的是，研究者应当总是基于相关理论来选择合适的模型。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">参考文献</span></span></p><p
