font-family: 宋体;">，在相对较短的合成途径中，关键序列是光化学反应级联</span><span style="font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 18px;">(photochemical reaction cascade)</span><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">，涉及多环中间体的一系列复杂重排。</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">萜烯类化合物（</span>Terpenes<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）是主要来源于植物的天然物质，具有很大的结构多样性。形式上，它们是由异戊二烯单元</span>(2-<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">甲基丁</span>-1,3-<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">二烯</span>)&#123; isoprene units (2-methylbuta-1,3-diene)&#125;<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">组成的。倍半萜烯类（</span>Sesquiterpenes<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）、萜烯类是由三个异戊二烯单元（</span>isoprene unit<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）组成的萜类化合物，是最大和最重要的子集。它们的基本框架由</span>15<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">个碳原子组成，这些碳原子通常是在几个碳原子环组成的系统中，可以非常不同的方式排列。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">一些稠环倍半萜烯类（</span>polycyclic sesquiterpenes<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）被用作香料（</span>scent<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）和调味品化合物（</span>flavoring compounds<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">），以及药剂。</span></span><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">近年来，从沉香</span><span style="font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 18px;">(agarwood also known as eaglewood)</span><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">和其他几种植物中分离出了一类结构复杂、具有抗糖尿病活性的倍半萜烯类化合物（</span><span style="font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 18px;">sesquiterpenes</span><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）。它们都有一个共同的中心框架，称为</span><span style="font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 18px;">prezizane</span><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">骨架（</span><span style="font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 18px;">prezizane skeleton</span><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）。由德国慕尼黑工业大学（</span><span style="font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 18px;">Technical University of Munich, Germany)</span><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）教授托尔斯滕·巴赫</span><span style="font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 18px;">(Thorsten Bach)博士</span><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">领导的一个团队</span><span style="font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 18px;">,</span><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">现在已经完成了这一天然产物家族成员之一的全合成，它就是沉香醇</span><span style="font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 18px;">B(agarozizanol B)</span><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">。全合成是指从基本的、可获得的起始材料中完全形成了天然产物。</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">prezizane<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">骨架是一个由三个碳原子环组成的系统</span>:<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">即一个六元环与一个五元环沿一条边相连，并通过另外两个碳原子桥接形成第二个五元环。合成这种结构的挑战是，此骨架可以以两种镜像形式——</span>(+)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">型和</span>(-)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">型存在。沉香醇</span>B(agarozizanol B)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">还有两个侧基，它们也必须有正确的空间定向。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">这个</span>11<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">步反应序列的起始点是一种茚酮衍生物（</span>indanone derivative<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">），这对于全合成来说是非常短的。这种化合物已经含有一个五元环和一个六元环的体系，尽管六元环是芳香环。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">缺失的碳原子以烯烃侧链的形式附着在茚酮上。该合成的核心是一个光化学级联反应，包括三个光驱动反应和一系列复杂的多环中间体的重排。第一步是生成一个应变四环框架，该框架包含沉香醇</span>B(agarozizanol B)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">的所有碳原子在正确的相对位置，并失去了六碳环不必要的芳香性。随后，通过中间体中一个三碳环的打开</span>(<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">环丙烷键的分裂</span>)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">，转化形成三环的</span>prezizane<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">骨架。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">托尔斯滕·巴赫说：“通过这种方式，我们获得了</span>(+)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">型和</span>(-)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">型的混合物以及纯</span>(+)-<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">沉香醇</span>B&#123;<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">（</span>+<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）</span>-agarozizanol B &#125;<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">，它与天然产物完全相同。我们最近开发的光化学级联反应已被证明在未来其它天然产物的合成中前景无限。”</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">上述介绍，仅供参考。欲了解更多信息，敬请注意浏览</span><a href="https://doi.org/10.1002/anie.202110009" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">原文</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">或者相关报道。</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><a href="https://doi.org/10.1002/anie.202110009" style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "arial black", "avant garde";" target="_blank"><strong>Abstract</strong></a><br></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">The prezizane-type sesquiterpene agarozizanol B was synthesized employing a photochemical cascade reaction as the key step. Starting from a readily available 1-indanone with a tethered olefin, a strained tetracyclic skeleton was assembled which contained all carbon atoms of the sesquiterpene with the correct relative configuration. The conversion into the tricyclic prezizane skeleton was accomplished by a strategic cyclopropane bond cleavage. Prior to the cyclopropane ring opening an adaption of the oxidation state was required, which could be combined with a reductive resolution step. After removal of two functional groups, the natural product was obtained both in racemic form or, if resolved, as the (+)-enantiomer which was shown to be identical to the natural product.</span></p><p><br></p>                    <br><br>
