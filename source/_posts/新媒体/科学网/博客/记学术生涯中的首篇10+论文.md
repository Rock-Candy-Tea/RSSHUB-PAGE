white-space: normal;" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><br></p><p><span style="font-size: 20px;">虽然本文从投稿到接受很顺利，只用了3个月的时间。但是，中间的过程，从构思，阅读海量文献，到反复修订，前后共计花了6年时间（2014年12月-2021年1月）。全文总计31页，正文接近2万字，包含8张图和2张表。所以，这篇文章的顺利接受给我最大的启发是<strong>机会是留给准备充分的人</strong>。</span></p><p><br></p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://bbs.sciencenet.cn/home.php?mod=attachment&filename=image.png&id=717442" title alt="image.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p style="text-align: center;"><br></p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://bbs.sciencenet.cn/home.php?mod=attachment&filename=image.png&id=717506" title alt="image.png" width="656" height="481" style="width: 656px; height: 481px;" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><br></p><p><span style="font-size: 20px;"><span style="font-size: 20px;">本文在投稿</span><em>Ageing Research Reviews</em><span style="font-size: 20px;">之前还有个插曲，我斗胆给<em>Trends in Genetics</em>主编写了Presubmission Inquiries。当然，</span><em><span style="font-size: 20px;">Trends in Genetics</span></em><span style="font-size: 20px;">大部分综述都是约稿，所以不出意外，本文</span><span style="font-size: 20px;">被主编很客气地回绝了。这个高峰就只有后面再去攀登了。另外，2020年我将mCAs中最受关注的嵌合性Y染色体丢失（mosaic loss of Y chromosome）单独整理成文，以</span></span><span style="font-size: 20px;"><strong style="font-size: 20px; white-space: normal;">Mosaic loss of human Y chromosome: what, how and why</strong></span><span style="font-size: 20px;">为题</span><span style="font-size: 20px;">发表在<em>Human Genetics</em>上（https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s00439-020-02114-w）。</span></p><p><span style="font-size: 20px;"><br></span></p><p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 20px;"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://bbs.sciencenet.cn/home.php?mod=attachment&filename=image.png&id=717513" title alt="image.png" width="778" height="209" style="width: 778px; height: 209px;" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p><p><span style="font-size: 20px;"><br></span></p><p><span style="font-size: 20px;">言归正传，本文的审稿人总共3位，其中之一是Po-Ru Loh。Po-Ru Loh是美国哈佛大学医学院统计遗传学和生物信息学领域的大牛，青年才俊。他基于UK Biobank的两项mCAs研究成果，分别于2018年和2020年发表于<em>Nature</em>上（https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-018-0321-x；https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-020-2430-6）。同时，Loh还开发了用于mCAs分析的软件</span><span style="font-size: 20px;">Mosaic Chromosomal Alterations software (MoChA)，现已对学术界</span><span style="font-size: 20px;">开放，可</span><span style="font-size: 20px;">免费使用(https://github.com/freeseek/mocha)。</span><span style="font-size: 20px;">从长相和名字推断</span><span style="font-size: 20px;">Loh</span><span style="font-size: 20px;">应该是东南亚华裔。谷歌学术画像显示其总被引 14,777，H-index 43。他给我的论文的评价很高：</span></p><p><span style="font-family: Verdana; font-size: 20px;"><br></span></p><p><span style="font-size: 20px;"><strong><span style="font-family: Verdana; font-size: 16px; color: #FF0000; text-decoration: none;">I want to commend the authors for writing a great review article. The authors have addressed all of my concerns and they have truly performed an incredibly extensive job summarizing a large amount of literature related to mCAs on very different topics. This review is going to be a great entry point for researchers and physicians who want to better understand the field of somaticism driven by mosaic chromosomal alterations.</span></strong></span></p><p><span style="font-size: 20px;"><strong><span style="font-family: Verdana; font-size: 16px; color: #FF0000; text-decoration: none;"><br></span></strong></span></p><p><span style="font-size: 20px;">另外一位审稿人是日本<span style="font-size: 20px; color: #222222; background-color: #FFFFFF; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">RIKEN Center for Integrative Medical Sciences的</span></span><span style="font-size: 20px; color: #222222; background-color: #FFFFFF; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">Chikashi Terao。2020年<span style="color: #222222; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 20px; background-color: #FFFFFF;">Terao和Loh在<em>Nature</em>背靠背发表了关于Biobank Japan中mCAs的研究论文（https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-020-2426-2）。</span></span><span style="font-size: 20px;">Terao对本文章的评价也还蛮中肯的：</span></p><p><span style="font-size: 20px;"><br></span></p><p><span style="font-size: 20px;"><strong><span style="font-family: Verdana; font-size: 16px; color: #FF0000; text-decoration: none;">This review by X. Dai and X. Guo is a very interesting and deep revision of the emergent field of mosaic chromosomal alterations (mCAs) and their impact in age-related diseases. Although clonal mosaicism has become a prevalent feature of aging genomes in recent years, a comprehensive and focused analysis on mCAs has been missing beyond the clonal hematopoiesis. Here, the authors provide an extended view of the causes and consequences of mCAs in aging-associated diseases. Importantly, mechanisms behind the association between mCAs and diseases, as well as strategies to rejuvenate mCAs and extend healthspan are discussed.</span></strong></span></p><p><br></p><p><span style="font-size: 20px;">我的直接感受是：文章被这样的两位业内大牛审稿，真的会学到非常多的知识，开拓自己的眼界。他们会把自己的新的想法在审稿过程中以Comments的形式全部无私地给出来，让我们直接加到文中进行讨论。他们的Nature文章我们反复看了数十遍，但还是会漏掉很多细节，或者对一些内容不是很理解。他们的Comments能让我们有一种茅塞顿开的感觉。所以，大家在投文章推荐审稿人时，不要害怕推荐大牛，因为如果文章有幸被他们审稿，真的会让文章有质的飞跃。</span></p><p><span style="font-size: 20px;"><br></span></p><p><span style="font-size: 20px;">第三位审稿人给的意见较少，评论一般：</span></p><p><span style="font-size: 20px;"><br></span></p><p><span style="font-size: 20px;"><strong><span style="font-family: Verdana; font-size: 16px; color: #FF0000; text-decoration: none;">Guo et al, reviewed mosaic chromosomal alterations (mCA), aka CHIP, in a comprehensive manner in this paper.
