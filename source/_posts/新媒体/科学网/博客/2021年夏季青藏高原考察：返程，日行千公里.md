<p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">2021-08-15</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">，星期日</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体">中国科学院动物研究所，牛泽清博士</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体">今天为了赶路，早上</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">7</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">点</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">15</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">分天刚亮，我和小吴就从格尔木出发了。</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体">沿格尔木黄河东路行驶大约</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">6</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">公里，从格尔木东上</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">G6</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">高速，一路向东，开向</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">683</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">公里外的西宁市。沿途的道路对我和小吴来说非常熟悉了，已多次从西藏返回北京都是走的同样的路：诺木洪、香日得、都兰、茶卡、共和县、湟源、倒淌河、多巴。到下午</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">3</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">点，我和小吴到了西宁市。时间尚早，我和小吴决定继续前行，过兰州，奔赴皋兰县。下午</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">6</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">点半，结束今天的长途旅行。</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202108/16/131503ul0v33pllc3vm0j8.jpeg" title alt="WechatIMG1073.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p><p style="text-align: center; font-size: medium; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px;"><font face="楷体">皋兰县</font></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体">一天近</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">11</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">个小时的长途奔波，为得就是能早日回到北京。下午</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">1</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">点</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">40</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">分左右，收到金梁发来的微信。知道队员们已乘机顺利回到北京，心感甚慰。我和小吴驾车估计还得两天才能回到北京。尤其是小吴，每天开车十多个小时，还得再辛苦两天。我每天坐车都感到有些疲惫，可想小吴开车会有多累。好在时间就两天了，过了这两天，我俩也就回到北京了。</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体;
