Laughlin</span></span><span style=";font-family:等线;color:#2C2D30;font-size:16px">所</span><span style=";font-family:等线;color:#2C2D30;font-size:16px">说，如果他知道</span><span #333333;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:等线">（</span><span style="font-family:等线">If I knew what leads one to the Nobel Prize, I wouldn’t tell you, but go get another one.）</span></span></p><p style="margin-top:0;margin-right:0;margin-bottom:0;margin-left:0;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;text-align:justify;text-justify:inter-ideograph;line-height:200%"><span style="margin-top:0;margin-right:0;margin-bottom:0;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;text-align:justify;text-justify:inter-ideograph;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">Levari</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">等人的研究颠覆了许多人的一个观念：</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">顶</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">级</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">专家</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">（</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">（</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">the best teachers</span><span style="margin-top:0;margin-right:0;margin-bottom:0;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;text-align:justify;text-justify:inter-ideograph;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">顺便插一句，现在的大学招聘教师其实也是基于这个观念，这从博士刚毕业，即使一堂课都没上过、一个学生也没教过，但只要手握一定数量的顶刊论文就可被争抢着进大学直接当教授可见一斑。</span></p><p style="margin-top:0;margin-right:0;margin-bottom:0;margin-left:0;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;text-align:justify;text-justify:inter-ideograph;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">研究人员首先</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">考察</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">了</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">人们是否真的</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">期望</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">顶</span><span style="font-family:等线">1092人（平均年龄35岁，606名女性）玩一个单词发现游戏，并预测建议者的成绩与其建议质量之间的关系。这些建议者已经玩了</span></span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">6</span><span style="margin-top:0;margin-right:0;margin-bottom:0;margin-left:0;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;text-align:justify;text-justify:inter-ideograph;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">结果表明，</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">超过一半的参与者表示，他们更</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">愿意</span><span style="margin-top:0;margin-right:0;margin-bottom:0;margin-left:0;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;text-align:justify;text-justify:inter-ideograph;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">人</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">的建议相比，</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">成绩最好</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">成绩</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">。他们指派一组参与者</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:等线">（</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">如何玩得好</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">的</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">建议</span><span style="font-family:等线">2064人，平均年龄33岁，890名男性），</span></span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">并分析</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">这些建议</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">对</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">新玩家成绩</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">的影响。</span></p><p style="margin-top:0;margin-right:0;margin-bottom:0;margin-left:0;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;text-align:justify;text-justify:inter-ideograph;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">结果表明，尽管</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">成绩</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">最好的</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">最好的</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">人</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">的建议</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">的</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">评价也较高，且接受建议确实提高了接受建议者的</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">成绩</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">换言之，成绩</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">最好的</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">人</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">的建议并不总是</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">最有</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">价值</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">。（</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">Tips from the top are not always worth top dollar.</span><span style="margin-top:0;margin-right:0;margin-bottom:0;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;text-align:justify;text-justify:inter-ideograph;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">必须指出</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">，在某些领域和某些情况下，</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">成绩最好的人提供</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">的建议可能会被证明是</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">最好</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">的。</span><span style="margin-top:0;margin-right:0;margin-bottom:0;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;text-align:justify;text-justify:inter-ideograph;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">为什么我们不能期望从最成功的人那儿学到最棒的方法和技巧呢？</span><span style=";font-family:等线;color:#8A8D96;font-size:16px"> </span><span style="font-family: 等线;font-size: 16px">研究人员提出了一些潜在的原因。包括：</span></p><p style="margin-top:0;margin-right:0;margin-bottom:0;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;text-align:justify;text-justify:inter-ideograph;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">意识觉知缺失（</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">Lack of conscious awareness</span><span style="margin-top:0;margin-right:0;margin-bottom:0;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;text-align:justify;text-justify:inter-ideograph;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">交流和观点采择困难（</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">Communication and perspective-taking difficulties</span><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">）。即使成绩最好的人有方法和技巧可以分享，他们也可能无法从新手的角度（或回忆他们曾经面临的挑战）来交流有用的信息。</span></p><p style="margin-top:0;margin-right:0;margin-bottom:0;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;text-align:justify;text-justify:inter-ideograph;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px">给出的建议太多。也许成绩最好的人实际上提供了有用的、可操作的建议，如果遵循这些建议，就会提高成绩。但由于建议太多，可能并没有完全被遵循，致使这些建议并不比其他成绩较低的人提供的有限建议更有效。</span></p><p style="margin-top:0;margin-right:0;margin-bottom:0;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;text-align:justify;text-justify:inter-ideograph;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:等线">正如作者指出的，他们的研究最主要的局限性在于侧重于</span><span style="font-family:等线">“如何做”的建议，这与“做什么”的建议大不相同。而这，恰恰可以证明老文的观察和体会，因为在老文看来，能否发</span></span><span style=";font-family:等线;color:#2C2D30;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:等线">顶刊论文，关键恰恰不在于</span><span style="font-family:等线">“如何做”，而在于“做什么”。</span></span></p><p style=";text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;text-align:justify;text-justify:inter-ideograph;line-height:200%"><span style="font-family: 等线;line-height: 200%;color: #333333;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px">诺贝尔生理或医学奖得主</span><span style=";font-family:等线;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:等线">彼得</span><span style="font-family:等线">·</span></span><span style="font-family: 等线;line-height: 200%;color: #333333;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px">梅达沃在《对年轻科学家的忠告》一书中写道：</span><span style="font-family: 等线;line-height: 200%;color: #333333;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:等线">“我可以很自信地说，任何科学家，无论年龄大小，要想取得重要的发现，都必须研究重要的问题。”</span></span></p><p style=";text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;text-align:justify;text-justify:inter-ideograph;line-height:200%"><span style="font-family: 等线;line-height: 200%;color: #333333;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:等线">这意味着，是否研究重要的问题（做什么），比如何取得重要的成果（如何做），重要得多。</span></span></p><p style=";text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;text-align:justify;text-justify:inter-ideograph;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:等线;line-height:200%;color:#2C2D30;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:等线">最后，打个很俗的比方，如何发顶刊论文有如如何投资发大财。我们即使向</span><span style="font-family:等线">“股神”</span></span><span style=";font-family:等线;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">巴菲特取经</span><span style=";font-family:等线;line-height:200%;color:#2C2D30;font-size:16px">如何投资，</span><span style=";font-family:等线;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">可能也没用，但盯住他在投资什么，有可能使我们一夜暴富。</span></p><p style=";text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;text-align:justify;text-justify:inter-ideograph;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:等线;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">推广讲，想</span><span style=";font-family:等线;line-height:200%;color:#2C2D30;font-size:16px">投资发大财，如何投资不重要，如果硬要说很重要</span><span style=";font-family:等线;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">（否则市面上不会随处可见有巴菲特名字或名义的投资书籍了），那也远不及投资什么重要。</span></p><p style="margin-top:0;margin-right:0;margin-bottom:0;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;text-align:justify;text-justify:inter-ideograph;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:等线;font-size:16px"> </span></p><p><br></p>                    <br><br>
