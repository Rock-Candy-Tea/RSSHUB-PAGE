<p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">我国有一些说法，表达对一个人的了解和评价，与时间有关，例如，路遥知马力，日久见人心。白居易的诗句更是充分揭示了这种现象：</span></span><span style="font-family: Arial;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:宋体">周公恐惧流言日，</span></span><span style="font-family: Arial;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:宋体">王莽谦恭未篡时。</span></span><span style="font-family: Arial;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:宋体">向使当初身便死，一生真伪复谁知？</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">当然，现实情况更为复杂，既有</span>“一日是贼，终身是贼”的俗语，又有“知错能改，善莫大焉”的劝导，显得彼此矛盾。那么，人们在评价别人的道德品质时，究竟更看重什么？</span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">特别地，人</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">们是如何评价</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">一个</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">人一生的道德品质的？虽然什么是好的个人观点可能是不同的，但是，回答这个问题的一种合理的方式是，把这个人一生所做的好事与坏事进行简单相加。</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">当然，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">可以</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">做得更精细，比如，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">考虑好的程度或坏的程度、涉及的事件的意义、受到影响的人数，从而</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">对各种行为加上合适的权重。那么，一个人对世界的影响就是这些做了适当加权的各种行为的总和，最后的得分可能是评估这个人总体而言如何好或如何坏的合理方式。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">然而，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">人</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">们可能并不遵循这种表面看来显得合理的策略评</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">价一个</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">人的总体道德。实际上，历史</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">和现实都</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">充满了相反</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">情形。一些故事</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">表明，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">有的人在生命的后期</span>“洗心革面”，在他们的最后日子里做了很多</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">好</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">事</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">；有的人在生命的后期</span>“晚节不保”，在他们的最后日子里做了一件坏事</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。这种改变，即使它们只能代表一个人整个一生的一小部分，通常也被描述成赎罪</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">或作恶</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的行</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">为</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，从而让大家用积极得多</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">或消极得多</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的方式来看待这个人的一生。这种</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">情形称称为</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">“生命终结”偏差</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">研究发现，人们把有积极结果的较长的不愉快经历</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">评定得比没有积极结果的较短负面经历更好</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">（</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px">Fredrickson & Kahneman, 1993; Kahneman, Fredrickson, Schreiber, & Redelmeier, 1993</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">）。</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">这样的时距忽视被认为是由一种更一般的高峰结果记忆偏差所致，即个体在最极端的高峰事件和最近的事件基础上形成对一种经历的总体评估</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">（</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px">Kahneman, Fredrickson, Schreiber, & Redelmeier, 1993</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">）。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">生命终结</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">本身显得在固化评估判断中具有独特的作用。对那些已经死亡的个体的评价要比对那些还活着的个体的评价更难以改变，这就是</span>“时间冻结效应”（</span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px">Eylon & Allison, 2005</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">）。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:28px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">研究显示，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">人</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">们在合计自己的经验及形成对别人的评价时存在偏差</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">个体</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">生命的终结显得在这样的评价中具有特殊的地位。</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">更精细的研究发现，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">这种</span>“生命终结”偏差仅仅适用于死亡前夕行为的有意改变，并且推测这种行为改变反映了个体“真实自我”的出现</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">（</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px">Newman, Lockhart, & Keil, 2010</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">）</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">这种偏差可能具有进化适应的意义，即个体最后的行为性质决定着他对</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">社会及有关群体</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的意义。个体是否对</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">人</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">们具有威胁，他</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px">/</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">她</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">最后的行为起</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">着</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">决定作用。同时，评价个体看重最后的行为，有利于起到警戒作用和榜样作用。当然，个体最后的行为是负面的时，对</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">大家</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的威胁最大，因此，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">人</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">们</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">对此</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">特别敏感。也就是说，个体由好变坏，生命终结时处于坏的状态，是</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">人</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">们最不提倡的，对这种</span>“一坏到底”或“晚节不保”的人，</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">人</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">们</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">也</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">是最痛恨的，体现在道德判断中，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">就会</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">得到最差的道德评价。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">如果把个体的行为改变描述得复杂一些，例如，由好变坏又变好、由坏变好又变坏，等，可以更为精细地考查这个问题。</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">此时，用功利论或道义论可能都不容分析了，而用道德账户理论来描述和解释，效果应当会更好。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">总之，当评价别人的道德品质时，人们显示出一种强烈的偏差，即，过于看重个体生命最后阶段的行为，即使这些行为是在相反行为长期存在的背景下出现的，也是如此。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span
