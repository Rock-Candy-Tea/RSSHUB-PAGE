<p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">道德问题具有情境性，个体是否具有道德责任（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Moral duty</span><span style="font-family:宋体">），取决于他</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">/</span><span style="font-family:宋体">她是否遇到道德情境。可以推测，在这个世界上，每时每刻都存在着需要人们伸出援手的状况，每分每秒都会发生需要人们伸出援手的事件。显然，不知道相应状况、不晓得相应事件的人们，不可能伸出援手，大家也没期望这样的人们伸出援手。也就是说，不知道、不晓得或者说没遇到道德情境，人们就没有相应的道德责任，大家也不会从道德层面去要求和评价个体。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">社会现实要复杂得多，就道德问题而言，</span>“道德滑坡”的说法不时就会被渲染一番，似乎真的人心不古了。在学术领域，也不乏相应的研究，甚至专门考察道德推脱问题。由文献可知，“道德推脱”来自心理学家班杜拉（<span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Albert Bandura, 1925-2021</span><span style="font-family:宋体">），是他</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">在道德主体理论中提出</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的概念，原文是</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Moral disengagement</span><span style="font-family:宋体">。他认为，“道德推脱”</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">包括一些社会认知机制，其作用是既能让个体保持道德标准又不至于陷入自责（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Bandura, 1991</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">对于来自国外的心理学术语，如果翻译得草率，可能会误导人。例如，</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Job burnout</span><span style="font-family:宋体">译为“职业倦怠”就是这样的不良一例。那么，</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Moral disengagement</span><span style="font-family:宋体">译为“道德推脱”是否也可能是这种情况呢？如下两例或许能带来一些启示。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">例一，若干年前，某高校一学院召开期末教工会议，同时，让大家签领课时费等报酬。会议结束，离场的教工走到门口，又纷纷无声地往回转，谁都不好意思出去</span>——原来，会议室门外摆着一张桌子，桌面放着写了“募捐”两个大字的纸箱，<span style="font-family:Times New Roman">3</span><span style="font-family:宋体">名学生各拿一沓募捐宣传单，恭敬地站在旁边。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">这是一个典型的道德情境，走到门口的教工必然遇到这个情境，也必然具有道德责任。特别地，刚领了报酬，哪名教工都不能以</span>“我身上没带钱”而推脱。或许，更真实的情形是，大家可能不知道捐出多少合适，只得脱离这个情境，离开门口，就像没遇到道德情境一样。</span></p><p style="text-indent:32px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">例二，十</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">年前</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">我的</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">研究生在用电车问题和天桥问题做实验时，有些被试看到实验材料后拒绝继续做</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">下去</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，她们不愿面对这样的假设情境。</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">从研究伦理来讲，允许被试随时退出实验。由此可见，不仅道德行为具有情境性，而且</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">道德判断</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">也有情境</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">性</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">现在分析，这样要求退出实验的被试，就是</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">回避道德</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">情境</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">哪怕相应的情境是假设的，非真实的。可以认为，她们当时其实</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">是在做出脱离</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">道德情境</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的反应</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">显然，说她们做出道德推脱的反应是不合适的。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">正因如此</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">我认为把</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Moral disengagement</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">译为</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">“道德推脱”</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">不甚恰当，译为</span>“道德脱离”则是</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">准确</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">毕竟，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">“道德推脱”还可能让个体有某种程度的愧疚，即“推脱”之后还会有一定的“道德余波”，但是，“道德脱离”就不是这样的了，“脱离”之后个体就可以完全不想“道德问题”了，即“道德脱离”是个</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">体将自己与道德情境彻底切割，使自己不再</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">有</span>“道德</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">责任</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">”。</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">毕竟，不是所有人都能承担各种情况的道德责任。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">或许可以认为，</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">道德</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">情境</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">性的</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">核心</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">就是个体的道德</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">责任</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，也就是说，在这样的特定情境中，个体会被赋予道德</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">责任</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，个体也往往会体验到相应的道德</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">责任</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。因此，实验中的被试不愿面对电车问题和天桥问题，就是要免除自己的道德</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">责任。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">如果当时询问她们</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">：在现实生活中，你遇到类似情形时，你会怎么做？尽管实验中的情境是假设的，但是，现实当中会有类似情形的，从而，这样的被试可能真的会遇到类似情形，那么，她们可能还是要做出免除自己道德义务的反应，例如，装作没有看到这种情境，或者迅速地</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">脱</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">离这种情境，就像实验中那样。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric"><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">实验当中，被试面对道德情境可以选择</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">脱离</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，现实当中呢？那些遇到需要帮助</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">情境</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">而</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">脱离甚至</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">逃离现场的人，做出的不也</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">正是</span>“道德脱离”反应</span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">吗？如此解读现实生活中的</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">一些社会</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">现象，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">可能是</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">更合理</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的。例如，它们只是表明人们存在个体差异，而不一定表明整个社会都</span>“道德滑坡”了。</span></p><p style="text-indent:32px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric"><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">可以将人们</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">大致</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">分成两种情况：其一，倾</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">向于</span>“道德脱离”的人，其二，倾向于“道德担当”的人。</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">显然，</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">每个人都会选择道德脱离或道德担当，但选择的程度不同，因此，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">要加上</span>“倾向于”</span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px">3</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">个字</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。倾向于道德脱离或道德担当的人，应当具有相应的人格特点，即这样的个体不单是在道德问题上脱离或担当，在其它各种问题上也会是这样的，据此就可以测量和区分这样的个体。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric"><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">接下来，可以考察他们何以如此？即个体表现出道德脱离或道德担当的内在机制是什么？包括心理机制与神经机制。了解了内在机制，就可以提出改</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">进</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的技术或措施。同时，能够区分出不同的道德类型者，对于职业选择和岗位安排也有实践意义。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 宋体;">参考文献</span></p><p style="text-indent: 32px;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 宋体;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: "times new roman";">Bandura, A. (1991). Social cognitive theory of moral thought and action. In W. M. Kurtines & E. J. L. Gewirtz (Eds.), Handbook of Moral Behavior Development (Vol. I) (pp. 45-103). Hillsdale, NJ: Erbaum.</span></span></p>                    <br><br>
