<p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">软骨具有再生能力</span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">当我们损伤了臀部、膝盖和肩膀的柔软组织，或者骨关节炎侵蚀了这些组织，导致我们移动时关节疼痛时，医生经常这样告诉我们</span>“<span style="font-family:宋体">软骨</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">一旦损伤，永远</span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">不愈合。</span>”<span style="font-family:宋体">。我自己也从整形外科医生那里听到过这种说法，他们解释说，软骨没有血液供应，无法将修复细胞和营养物质带到损伤部位。然而，活的组织无法取代受损的细胞，这一直让我觉得不太可能。事实证明，最近的研究表明，关节软骨确实有</span></span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">修复能力</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">，</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">虽然这种能力比较</span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">有限。</span></span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">对这种能力的新见解带来了治疗的希望，可以增强愈合或保护受损的软骨免受进一步恶化。</span></span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202206/14/094029ic60ce9wszbsb6rm.png" title alt="图片1.png" width="498" height="932" style="width: 498px; height: 932px;" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></span></span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">要</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">了解</span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">关节软骨，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">可</span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">想象一下鸡骨头末端坚硬的白色涂层。</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">软骨</span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的大部分是一种叫做细胞外基质的海绵状物质，是由软骨细胞泵出的水和纤维蛋白的混合物。杜克大学医学院的风湿病学家维吉尼亚</span>·<span style="font-family:宋体">克劳斯解释说</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">:“</span><span style="font-family:宋体">这是一种内在的再生</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">——</span><span style="font-family:宋体">新组织形成，旧组织被咀嚼和冲刷掉</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">。</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">这</span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">就像所有</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">身体</span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">组织一样</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">，</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">但</span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">牙釉质</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">除外</span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。</span>”<span style="font-family:宋体">但是，她强调，软骨的更新过程是缓慢的。成年人的</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">软骨</span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">组织确实没有血液供应。</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">但如果对关节施加压力或重量，这能导致</span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">营养物质的滑液进出</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">软骨。你可以想象一下海绵吸水的情况。</span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">克劳斯指出。</span></span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">“<span style="font-family:宋体">这就是为什么锻炼对关节健康如此重要，软骨吸收营养的方式是通过运动</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">实现的</span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。</span>”<span style="font-family:宋体">克劳斯是少数研究这种组织缓慢转变的科学家之一。在一项令人惊讶的发现中，她和她的团队在</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">2019</span><span style="font-family:宋体">年报告称，与修复和再生相关的蛋白质的产生因关节不同而不同</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">：</span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">踝关节比膝盖更</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">强</span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，膝盖比髋关节更</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">强</span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。克劳斯将这种梯度称为</span>“<span style="font-family:宋体">我们体内的蝾螈</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">”</span><span style="font-family:宋体">，他解释说，在蝾螈和其他可以再生断肢的动物中，</span></span><strong><span style="font-family: Calibri;line-height: 200%;color: #0000FF;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">这种</span></span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;color: #0000FF;font-size: 16px">自我修复再生</span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: Calibri;line-height: 200%;color: #0000FF;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">能力</span></span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;color: #0000FF;font-size: 16px">，</span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: Calibri;line-height: 200%;color: #0000FF;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">在脚上比在腿上更强大</span></span></strong><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。她的研究还表明，与修复有关的遗传物质在关节炎关节中比健康关节更丰富。克劳斯怀疑，就像蝾螈的肢体损伤会启动修复程序一样，骨关节炎也会启动人类的修复程序，尽管</span>“<span style="font-family:宋体">显然，我们现有的程序还不够</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">”</span><span style="font-family:宋体">。尽管如此，修复过程可能在踝关节起作用，她指出，踝关节比膝盖或臀部更不容易发生严重关节炎。还有其他证据表明，人类的软骨可以再生。一种名为关节撑开的手术正在进行测试，以促进膝关节炎患者的愈合，这些患者年纪太小，不适合进行全膝关节置换术。</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">(</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">人工</span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">假膝盖可以使用</span>15<span style="font-family:宋体">到</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">20</span><span style="font-family:宋体">年，之后必须更换。</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">)</span><span style="font-family:宋体">手术过程包括在膝盖上方和下方放置钉子，并使用外部设备持续六周，使上、下腿骨分离</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">5</span><span style="font-family:宋体">毫米。这就打开了关节空间。病人被鼓励步行，但该设备可以减少压力，所以膝盖沐浴在富含营养的液体中，而不会感到负担过重。荷兰研究人员已经证明，这种手术可以使关节软骨略有增加，减轻疼痛，这种好处至少持续两年，对一些患者来说，可以持续</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">10</span><span style="font-family:宋体">年之久。</span></span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">英国利兹大学的风湿病学家</span>Philip Conaghan<span style="font-family:宋体">说</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">“</span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">这项技术还需要更大规模的临床试验，但它是一个迷人的模型，</span>”<span style="font-family:宋体">。</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">Conaghan</span><span style="font-family:宋体">研究治疗关节炎的新药物，包括一种名为</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">sprifermin</span><span style="font-family:宋体">的生长因子，它似乎可以减缓一些患者软骨的丧失。他还在研究</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">canakinumab</span><span style="font-family:宋体">，这是一种炎症抑制剂，曾作为心血管药物进行测试，并显示出令人惊讶的副作用</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">:</span><span style="font-family:宋体">与安慰剂组相比，受体的关节置换明显减少。但</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">Conaghan</span><span style="font-family:宋体">警告说，由于修复过程缓慢且不确定，寻找能够使软骨增厚的药物是一项艰巨的任务</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">:“</span><span style="font-family:宋体">变化如此之小，即使使用最好的成像技术，也很难发现它。</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">”</span><span style="font-family:宋体">目前，增强力量的运动仍然是我们这些关节磨损的人的最佳策略。</span></span></p><p style="margin-left: 0px; text-indent: 28px; line-height: 200%;"><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">客纳罕建议</span></span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">在游泳池里走</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">。</span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">他说</span>:“<span style="font-family:宋体">强壮的股四头肌能大大减轻膝盖疼痛，不管你在做什么。所有的生命</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">需要</span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">强壮的肌肉。</span>”</span></p>                    <br><br>
