font-size: 20px;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"><br></span></span></p><p style="text-align: left;"><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun; font-size: 20px;">你充满激情、勇敢追梦，相信天生我材必有用，敢于直面科研难题、勇攀科研高峰。</span></strong></p><p><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun; background-color: #FFFFFF;"></span></span></strong><strong><span style="color: #333333; background-color: #FFFFFF; font-size: 20px; font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">自信人生二百年，会当水击三千里</span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;
