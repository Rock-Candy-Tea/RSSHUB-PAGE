<p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); text-indent: 2em;"><span style="text-indent: 2em;"></span></p><p style="margin-top:18px;margin-right:0;margin-bottom:18px;margin-left: 0;text-indent:32px;background:white"><strong><span style="font-size:14px;font-family:'Tahoma',sans-serif;color:#333333">How to draft a research hypothesis or research opening report?</span></strong></p><p style="margin: 18px 0;text-indent: 32px;background: white;overflow-wrap: break-word"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word"><span style="font-size:14px;font-family:'Tahoma',sans-serif;color:#333333">My first chatter is about research hypothesis (rationale, justification of a given study, or opening report in Chinese), which is the first step for us to conduct high impact research.</span></span></p><p style="margin: 18px 0;text-indent: 32px;background: white;overflow-wrap: break-word"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word"><span style="font-size:14px;font-family:'Tahoma',sans-serif;color:#333333">If you have had a right and feasible hypothesis, your research could be more than half way done. It seems very silly to discuss it since we are all "scientists" by training.</span></span></p><p style="margin: 18px 0;text-indent: 32px;background: white;overflow-wrap: break-word"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word"><span style="font-size:14px;font-family:'Tahoma',sans-serif;color:#333333">It is true that during our training, we as students usually spend months and months of time to search literature and prepare or write a review in the field before performing any real experiments. We all know that the research hypothesis is sole important and directly determines our research impact.</span></span></p><p style="margin: 18px 0;text-indent: 32px;background: white;overflow-wrap: break-word"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word"><span style="font-size:14px;font-family:'Tahoma',sans-serif;color:#333333">But often time, we do ignore the importance of research rationale before and during experiments, data collection and summary, or manuscript preparation.</span></span></p><p style="margin: 18px 0;text-indent: 32px;background: white;overflow-wrap: break-word"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word"><span style="font-size:14px;font-family:'Tahoma',sans-serif;color:#333333">For biomedical research, we can propose our research hypothesis in the following ways, which doesn't matter whether you area basic, translational, or clinic researcher:</span></span></p><p style="margin: 18px 0;text-indent: 32px;background: white;overflow-wrap: break-word"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word"><span style="font-size:14px;font-family:'Tahoma',sans-serif;color:#333333">1) Focus on the risk factor-mediated gene alterations. For example, lung cancer is associated with tobacco smoke; thus, we may hypothesize tobacco carcinogen-caused gene alterations (gene mutations, methylation, and other epigenetic changes);</span></span></p><p style="margin: 18px 0;text-indent: 32px;background: white;overflow-wrap: break-word"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word"><span style="font-size:14px;font-family:'Tahoma',sans-serif;color:#333333">2) Gene pathway-related gene alterations, e.g., p53 is often mutated in lung cancer; how about the role of the p53 up- or down-stream genes in lung cancer progression;</span></span></p><p style="margin: 18px 0;text-indent: 32px;background: white;overflow-wrap: break-word"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word"><span style="font-size:14px;font-family:'Tahoma',sans-serif;color:#333333">3) Mechanism of gene or drug action in a particular disease or organ site. For example, a gene is mutated or a drug worked well in a disease, so we will rationalize how and why. This kind of study specifically suits for GWAS data to provide hypothesis for further study of a particular gene in a given disease.</span></span></p><p style="margin: 18px 0;text-indent: 32px;background: white;overflow-wrap: break-word"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word"><span style="font-size:14px;font-family:'Tahoma',sans-serif;color:#333333">Taking home message is whatever we like to do needs a justification. In other words, we will rationalize why we perform a study and what implication we will expect to be reached in a given study.</span></span></p><p style="margin: 18px 0;text-indent: 32px;background: white;overflow-wrap: break-word"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word"><span style="font-size:14px;font-family:'Tahoma',sans-serif;color:#333333">But we will not rationalize that</span></span></p><p style="margin: 18px 0;text-indent: 32px;background: white;overflow-wrap: break-word"><span style="font-size:14px;font-family:'Tahoma',sans-serif;color:#333333">"A gene was abnormally expressed in one cancer but its expression is unknown in another cancer; thus, we performed this study";</span></p><p style="margin: 18px 0;text-indent: 32px;background: white;overflow-wrap: break-word"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word"><span style="font-size:14px;font-family:'Tahoma',sans-serif;color:#333333">or "A gene plays an important role in regulation of apoptosis in a cancer, but it is unknown whether this gene also plays a role in another cancer; thus, we performed the current study";</span></span></p><p style="margin: 18px 0;text-indent: 32px;background: white;overflow-wrap: break-word"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word"><span style="font-size:14px;font-family:'Tahoma',sans-serif;color:#333333">or "A gene is mutated in a cancer; thus, we assessed this gene mutation in another cancer".</span></span></p><p style="margin: 18px 0;text-indent: 32px;background: white;overflow-wrap: break-word"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word"><span style="font-size:14px;font-family:'Tahoma',sans-serif;color:#333333">These rationales were wrongly proposed because different diseases or cancers have the own risk factor, etiology, and pathogenesis. We can't just fish something for "novelty".</span></span></p><p style="margin: 18px 0;text-align: right;text-indent: 32px;background: white;overflow-wrap: break-word"><span style="font-size:14px;font-family:'Tahoma',sans-serif;color:#333333"> <span style="overflow-wrap: break-word">Medjaden editor</span></span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); text-indent: 2em;"><span style="text-indent: 2em;"><br></span>译文：如何起草实验假设或开题报告</p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); text-indent: 2em;"><span style="text-indent: 2em;">如果您有了正确的方向和可行的假设，您的研究就成功了一半。现在讨论这个似乎比较可笑，因为我们都是已经受过良好训练的“科研人员”。是的，在学生时代，我们在真正开始实验之前，就被训练着花费数月的时间寻找相关参考文献，用以准备一篇本研究领域的综述。</span><br></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); text-indent: 2em;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; text-indent: 2em;">我们都明白，一个好的实验前提，对文稿最终的发表影响力有多么重要，但是在实验、数据收集整理和论文的撰写中，大家仍旧经常忽略研究的立意。</span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); text-indent: 2em;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; text-indent: 2em;">对于生物医学类的研究，不论涉及到基础研究还是临床研究，以下几点意见都可供您在提出实验假设时进行参考：</span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); text-indent: 2em;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; text-indent: 2em;">1）关注风险因子介导的基因突变。例如：吸烟与肺癌有关，我们就可以假设烟草致癌物会导致基因突变（序列的变异，甲基化和其他表观遗传的改变）；</span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); text-indent: 2em;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; text-indent: 2em;">2）关注信号通路上的基因改变。例如：肺癌患者中</span><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; text-indent: 2em;">p53</span><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; text-indent: 2em;">经常有变化，那么肺癌的致病过程中，在</span><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; text-indent: 2em;">p53</span><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; text-indent: 2em;">上游或者下游的基因是如何变化的？</span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); text-indent: 2em;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; text-indent: 2em;">3）关注基因或者药物在特异疾病或组织中的作用机制。例如：我们知道一个基因突变或者一个药物对某种疾病有效，那么我们就需要了解为什么有效和如何生效。而这个理由也特别适合对</span><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; text-indent: 2em;">GWAS</span><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; text-indent: 2em;">分析文章后续的深入研究。</span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); text-indent: 2em;"> <span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; text-indent: 2em;">总之大家要记住：不论写哪方面的文章，都需要有坚实的研究依据。换句话说，我们需要指出我们做这个实验的原因，希望得到什么样的结论。以下是作者叙述实验依据中不合格的例子：</span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); text-indent: 2em;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; text-indent: 2em;"> 因为这个基因在某种癌症中表达正常，但是在另一种癌症中还尚无研究，所以我们进行了如下实验。不合格！</span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); text-indent: 2em;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; text-indent: 2em;"> 一个基因在某种癌细胞凋亡的调控中起到重要作用，但在其他的癌症中作用尚不知，所以我们进行了这个研究。不合格！</span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); text-indent: 2em;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; text-indent: 2em;">在某种癌症中一个基因突变了，但我们想研究一下在其他癌症中，这个基因是否会突变。不合格！</span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); text-indent: 2em;"> <span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; text-indent: 2em;">由于不同的疾病都有特异的风险因子，病因和发病机制，所以以上理由都是站不住脚的，大家不能用钓鱼策略来寻找文章的</span><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; text-indent: 2em;">“Novelty”</span><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; text-indent: 2em;">。</span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word;
