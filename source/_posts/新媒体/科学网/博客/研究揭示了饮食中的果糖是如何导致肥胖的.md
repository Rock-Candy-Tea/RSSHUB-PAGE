font-family: 宋体;">）。研究发现，喂食含有果糖食物的老鼠的绒毛比不喂食果糖的老鼠长</span>25%~40%<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">。此外，绒毛长度的增加与动物营养吸收、增重和脂肪积累的增加有关。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">上述论文的通讯作者、拉尔夫·纳克曼研究学者（</span>Ralph L. Nachman Research Scholar<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）、内分泌、糖尿病和代谢科（</span>Division of Endocrinology, Diabetes and Metabolism<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）医学助理教授以及纽约长老会</span>/<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">威尔·康奈尔医学中心的内分泌学家马库斯·达西尔瓦·冈卡尔维斯博士（</span>Dr. Marcus DaSilva Goncalves<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）说：“果糖（</span>Fructose<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）的结构不同于葡萄糖（</span>glucose<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）等其它糖类，它的代谢方式也不同。我们的研究发现，果糖的主要代谢物促进绒毛的伸长，支持肠道肿瘤的生长。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">研究者并不打算研究绒毛。该团队此前于</span>2019<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">年发表的研究发现，饮食中的果糖可能会增加结直肠癌小鼠模型的肿瘤大小，而阻断果糖代谢可能会阻止这种情况发生。由于果糖也可能促进小肠增生或加速生长，研究人员在显微镜下检查了喂食果糖或对照组饮食的老鼠的组织。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">正常低脂饮食、高脂饮食和添加果糖的高脂饮食来进行试验。第三组的老鼠不仅长出了更长的绒毛，而且比不含果糖的高脂肪食物的老鼠更肥胖。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">研究人员进一步观察了果糖代谢的变化，发现果糖的一种特殊代谢物，果糖</span>-1-<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">磷酸酯（</span><a href="https://medicalxpress.com/news/2021-08-uncovers-fructose-diet-contributes-obesity.html" target="_blank">fructose-1-phosphate</a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）相互作用，改变细胞代谢，促进绒毛的存活和伸长。去除丙酮酸激酶或果糖</span>-1-<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">磷酸酶后，果糖对绒毛长度无影响。先前的动物研究表明，果糖的这种代谢物也有助于肿瘤的生长。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">根据塞缪尔·泰勒的说法，从进化的角度来看，小鼠的观察是有意义的。他说</span>:<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">“在哺乳动物中，尤其是温带气候下的冬眠哺乳动物（</span>hibernating </span><a href="https://medicalxpress.com/tags/mammals/" style="text-decoration: underline; Simple sugar and sugar-sweetened beverage intake during adolescence and risk of colorectal cancer precursors. Gastroenterology 161, 128–142 (2021)." target="_blank">2</a></sup>. Dietary fructose metabolism begins at the epithelium of the small intestine, where fructose is transported by glucose transporter type 5 (GLUT5; encoded by <em>SLC2A5</em>) and phosphorylated by ketohexokinase to form fructose 1-phosphate, which accumulates to high levels in the cell<sup><a href="https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-021-03827-2#ref-CR3" title="Miller. M., Craig, J. W., Drucker, W. R. & Woodward, H. Jr. The metabolism of fructose in man. Yale J. Biol. Med. 29, 335–360 (1956)." target="_blank">3</a>,<a href="https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-021-03827-2#ref-CR4" title="Jang, C. et al. The small intestine converts dietary fructose into glucose and organic acids. Cell Metab. 27, 351–361 (2018)." target="_blank">4</a></sup>. Although this pathway has been implicated in obesity and tumour promotion, the exact mechanism that drives these pathologies in the intestine remains unclear. Here we show that dietary fructose improves the survival of intestinal cells and increases intestinal villus length in several mouse models. The increase in villus length expands the surface area of the gut and increases nutrient absorption and adiposity in mice that are fed a high-fat diet. In hypoxic intestinal cells, fructose 1-phosphate inhibits the M2 isoform of pyruvate kinase to promote cell survival<sup><a href="https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-021-03827-2#ref-CR5" title="Anastasiou, D. et al. Inhibition of pyruvate kinase M2 by reactive oxygen species contributes to cellular antioxidant responses. Science 334, 1278–1283 (2011)." target="_blank">5</a>,<a href="https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-021-03827-2#ref-CR6" title="Nguyen, A. et al. PKLR promotes colorectal cancer liver colonization through induction of glutathione synthesis. J. Clin. Invest. 126, 681–694 (2016)." target="_blank">6</a>,<a href="https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-021-03827-2#ref-CR7" title="Anastasiou, D. et al. Pyruvate kinase M2 activators promote tetramer formation and suppress tumorigenesis. Nat. Chem. Biol. 8, 839–847 (2012)." target="_blank">7</a></sup>. Genetic ablation of ketohexokinase or stimulation of pyruvate kinase prevents villus elongation and abolishes the nutrient absorption and tumour growth that are induced by feeding mice with high-fructose corn syrup. The ability of fructose to promote cell survival through an allosteric metabolite thus provides additional insights into the excess adiposity generated by a Western diet, and a compelling explanation for the promotion of tumour growth by high-fructose corn syrup.</span></p>                    <br><br>
