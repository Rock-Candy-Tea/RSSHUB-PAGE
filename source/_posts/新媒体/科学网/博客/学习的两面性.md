<p> </p><p style="line-height: 150%; text-indent: 32px;"><span style="background: #FFFFFF; line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 16px;"><span style="font-family: 宋体;">所有事物都像一张纸那样，有正反两个面，学习也是如此。学习的正面效应是增加知识和智慧；学习的负面效应是思想保守、大脑僵化。例如，</span></span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 16px;"> </span></p><p style="line-height: 150%; text-indent: 36px;"><strong><span style="line-height: 150%; letter-spacing: 1px; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 16px;"><span style="font-family: 宋体;">1. </span></span></strong><strong><span style="background: #FFFFFF; line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 16px;"><span style="font-family: 宋体;">物质世界</span></span></a><span style="background: #FFFFFF; line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 16px;"><span style="font-family: 宋体;">以及</span></span><a href="https://baike.baidu.com/item/%E7%B2%BE%E7%A5%9E%E4%B8%96%E7%95%8C/9653776" target="_blank"><span style="background: #FFFFFF; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 16px;"><span style="font-family: 宋体;">创造知识</span></span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 16px;"><span style="font-family: 宋体;">。</span></span><span style="line-height: 150%; letter-spacing: 1px; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 16px;"><span style="font-family: 宋体;">物理</span></span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 16px;"><span style="font-family: 宋体;">学的</span></span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 16px;"><span style="font-family: 宋体;">，</span></span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 16px;"><span style="font-family: 宋体;">智慧比知识更重要。</span></span><span style="color: #000000; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 16px;"><span style="font-family: 宋体;">在平凡中发现奇迹</span></span><span style="background: #FFFFFF; line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 16px;"><span style="font-family: 宋体;">的不是你所学过的</span></span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 16px;"><span style="font-family: 宋体;">知识</span></span><span style="background: #FFFFFF; line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 16px;"><span style="font-family: 宋体;">而是智慧。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 150%; text-indent: 32px;"><span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 16px;"> </span></p><p style="line-height: 150%; text-indent: 32px;"><strong><span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 16px; font-weight: bold;"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">2. </span></span></strong><strong><span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 16px;"><span style="font-family: 宋体;">对获得</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">“知识”强调过渡</span></span></strong></p><p style="line-height: 150%; text-indent: 32px;"><span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 16px;"><span style="font-family: 宋体;">传统的学习不重视科学的探究的过程，而是将知识</span></span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 仿宋; text-indent: 32px;"><span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 16px;"><span style="font-family: 宋体;">古往今来许多成功者既不是那些最勤奋的人，也不是那些知识最渊博的人，而是一些善于</span></span><span style="background: #FFFFFF; line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 16px;"><span style="font-family: 宋体;">从更高层面</span></span><span style="background: #FFFFFF; line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 16px; font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体;">用整体观点来看待事物，</span></span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 16px; font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体;">深度思考、</span></span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 16px;"><span style="font-family: 宋体;">变更思维方式</span></span><span style="line-height: 150%; text-indent: 32px;"><span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 16px;"><span style="font-family: 宋体;">传统学校的科学教育不是探究的过程，而是更频繁地作为成就的复述出现，这是不正确的。因此，</span></span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 楷体; font-size: 16px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体;">。</span></span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 16px;"><span style="font-family: 宋体;">”</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 150%; text-indent: 32px;"><span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 16px;"> </span></p><p style="line-height: 150%; text-indent: 32px;"><strong><span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体;
