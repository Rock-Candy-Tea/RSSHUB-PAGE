style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">不数日，那三个王子尽皆操演精熟，其余攻退之方，紧慢之法，各有七十二般解数，无不知之</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">”；</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">三个王子</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">“</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">较之初时，自家弄的武艺，真天渊也</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">”。</span></p><p style="margin-bottom:10px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">也就是说，只消数日，悟空就让</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">三个王子的武艺</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">相较之前有天壤之别</span><span style="font-family:宋体">——这样的能耐，远非当今有</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px">导师让</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px">研究生读研不到半年就发篇顶刊论文</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px">可以同日而语的</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">。这相当程度又说明，悟空的能耐足以颠覆</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">欧几里德</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">真言。</span></p><p style="margin-bottom:10px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;text-align:center;line-height:200%"><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px">六</span></strong></p><p style="margin-bottom:10px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">穿越一下：假设悟空在保护唐僧西天取经功成名就之后，到一所大学当博导，并恰巧跟爱因斯坦成为同事，他们俩谁更受学生欢迎</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">？</span></p><p style="margin-bottom:10px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px">爱因斯坦在柏林工作时期，是不如他的同事受学生欢迎的。主要原因是他</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px">对</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px">“工厂”</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px">式</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px">研究</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px">“</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px">毫无兴趣</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px">”。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px">弗兰克</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">写道，</span><span style="font-family:宋体">“</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">爱因斯坦并不愿意花过多精力在自己已经结束的课题上。他总是在寻找新的问题，探求新的答案。然而</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">，</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">愿意并且有能力自己思考这些新问题的学生是凤毛麟角的。</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">”</span></p><p style="margin-bottom:10px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">“他</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">敬佩的人有两种，一种是致力于解决问题的人；另一种是虽然自己无法解决，但能让世界知道问题的人。怀着这种对科学工作的态度，爱因斯坦没有带太多学生。</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">”</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px">而</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px">其他老师</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px">和学生</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">对</span><span style="font-family:宋体">“工厂”式研究“</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px">充满活力</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px">”</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px">，</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px">“</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px">他们全心全意投入研究</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px">，但却</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">忘记了在研究中更应该关注的问题，而把博士论文和一般论文看成终结目标</span><span style="font-family:宋体">”</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px">。</span></p><p style="margin-bottom:10px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">爱因斯坦若遇上悟空那样的同事，是否更难受学生欢迎，老文认为，同样取决于三个因素：时间，地点，学生。</span></p><p style="margin-bottom:10px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">假如你读博士，你选悟空还是爱因斯坦作导师？</span></p><p><br></p>
