<p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">使用连接词、转折词，或者某些常见单词或短语做引导，是使论文行文更加流畅的一个常见方法。不过，人们常常误解误用许多这类常见单词或短语，特别是非英语母语者。本文梳理了一些最为常见的引导性单词和短语，并提供了一些如何避免错用的建议。</span></p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">递进</span></strong></p><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">需要添加更多信息，同时避免句子过长，可以用表示递进关系的单词或短语，例如：</span></p><blockquote><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">The measured decrease in density suggested a significant loss of mass. <span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px; color: #0070C0;">Furthermore</span>, this was also confirmed by the spectrometry results.</span></p></blockquote><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">“Furthermore”这个单词通常表示简单的信息添加。此处还可以使用“additionally”、“in addition”、或简单的<span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px; color: #0070C0;">“further”</span>。</span></p><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">“<span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px; color: #0070C0;">Moreover</span>”也有类似用法，但要注意它也可表示后续内容对前面句子的增强，可以强调后面的信息更为重要，例如：</span></p><blockquote><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">To replicate these experiments would be difficult. <span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px; color: #0070C0;">Moreover</span>, it would serve no practical purpose.</span></p></blockquote><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">在科技论文中，经常看到将“at the same time”或“meanwhile”错误地使用。非正式语境下，这两个短语可以这样使用，但在科技论文中，最好是仅用它们的字面含义，即“simultaneously（同时）”，例如：</span></p><blockquote><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">During the reaction, a large peak appeared in the spectrum. At the same time, the pressure in the chamber increased by a factor of two.</span></p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">转折</span></strong></p></blockquote><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">学术写作中经常用“<span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px; color: #0070C0;">however</span>”这个词来强调转折关系。</span></p><blockquote><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">Almost all the samples survived the impact intact. However, one shattered completely.</span></p></blockquote><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">其他一些单词和短语也有类似用法。例如，“<span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px; color: #0070C0;">nonetheless</span>”可以用来表示“尽管如此”的意思，如下：</span></p><blockquote><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">The overall efficiency of this method is low. Nonetheless, it remains a popular approach to the problem.</span></p></blockquote><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px; color: #0070C0;">“Nevertheless”和“nonetheless”的含义相同，可互相替换</span><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">。“<span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px; color: #0070C0;">Even so</span>”也可以表示转折含义，虽略显非正式，但可接受。</span></p><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">最好<strong>避免使用“anyhow”、“still”或“all the same”来表示转折含义，因为它们非常不正式</strong>。</span></p><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">“<span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px; color: #0070C0;">In contrast</span>”这个短语可用于强调结果的不同，例如：</span></p><blockquote><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">The males spent on average 36% of their time engaged in territorial disputes. In contrast, the females showed no interest in these quarrels.</span></p></blockquote><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">此处可以用“<span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px; color: #0070C0;">conversely”替换“in contrast</span>”。</span></p><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">“On the contrary”这个短语也经常被错用；许多人经常使用这个短语，认为其与“in contrast”含义相同。但是，“<span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px; color: #0070C0;">On the contrary”一般表示“与预期相反”的意思</span>。例如：</span></p><blockquote><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">The reaction rate did not increase with temperature; on the contrary, it was found to decrease.</span></p></blockquote><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">值得注意的是，“<strong>on the other hand</strong>”等类似短语也常常被滥用，最好避免使用这个短语，尤其是前文没有使用“on the one hand”的情况下。</span></p><p style="text-align: center;"><strong><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">需要避免使用的其他词语</span></strong></p><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">还有几个引导性单词和短语最好在科技论文中避免使用。</span></p><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">尽管“nowadays”在严格意义上是正确的，但它偏口语化。相反，可以考虑使用更为确切的表达，比如：<span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px; color: #0070C0;">“in the last three years”、“since 2010”、“currently”</span>等。</span></p><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">“What’s more”这个短语在论文中也不够正式，可以替换为上述的递进词，比如“<span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px; color: #0070C0;">moreover</span>”或“<span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px; color: #0070C0;">in addition</span>”等。“Besides”也是如此，可以用更为正式的“<span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px; color: #0070C0;">moreover</span>”替换。</span></p><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">总结</span></p><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; font-size: 16px;">可以使用同义词词典变换引导词和短语，但要牢记，同义词的含义和正式程度会有细微差别。在同义词词典中，一个单词的所有同义词并非都可以替换该词。使用一个单词或短语之前，可以先查看例句，这样可以帮助你确定它是否适合论文写作的语境。</span></p><p><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei";
