Follow your eyes to wherever they lead you…and the world should begin to change for you.</span></cite><cite><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;font-style:normal"> </span></cite></p><p style="line-height: 1.75em;"><cite><span style="font-size: 16px;line-height: 150%;font-family: 宋体;font-style: normal">——Jerry Saltz，1951- ，美国艺术批评家</span></cite></p><p style="line-height: 1.75em;"><cite><span style="font-size: 16px;line-height: 150%;font-family: 宋体;font-style: normal">    </span></cite><cite><span style="font-size: 16px;line-height: 150%;font-family: 宋体;font-style: normal">博物馆是通向其他世界的虫洞。它们是带来陶醉体验的机器。博物馆把你的目光引向哪里你就奔向哪里......世界就会开始为你而改变。</span></cite></p><p style="line-height: 1.75em;"><cite><span style="font-size: 16px;line-height: 150%;font-family: 宋体;font-style: normal"> </span></cite></p><p style="line-height: 1.75em;"><cite><span style="font-size: 16px;line-height: 150%;font-family: 宋体;font-style: normal">A museum has to renew its collection to be alive, but that does not mean we give on important old works.</span></cite><cite><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;font-style:normal"> </span></cite></p><p style="line-height: 1.75em;"><cite><span style="font-size: 16px;line-height: 150%;font-family: 宋体;font-style: normal">——David Rockefeller，</span></cite><cite><span style="font-size: 16px;line-height: 150%;font-family: 宋体;font-style: normal">戴维·洛克菲勒，1915-2017，美国银行家</span></cite></p><p style="line-height: 1.75em;"><cite><span style="font-size: 16px;line-height: 150%;font-family: 宋体;font-style: normal">    </span></cite><cite><span style="font-size: 16px;line-height: 150%;font-family: 宋体;font-style: normal">博物馆必须更新其藏品方有活力，但这并不意味着放弃重要的古老藏品。</span></cite><cite><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;font-style:normal"><br> <br> </span></cite><cite><span style="font-size: 16px;line-height: 150%;font-family: 宋体;font-style: normal">Museums are managers of consciousness.
