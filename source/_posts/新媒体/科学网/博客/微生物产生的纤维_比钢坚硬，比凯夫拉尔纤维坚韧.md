font-family: 宋体;">），并用工程细菌生产它们。由此产生的纤维比一些天然的蜘蛛丝更坚韧。相关研究结果已经在《</span></span><a href="https://phys.org/journals/acs-nano/" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">ACS Nano</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">》杂志网站发表——</span>Jingyao Li, Yaguang Zhu, Han Yu, Bin Dai, Young-Shin Jun, Fuzhong Zhang. Microbially Synthesized Polymeric Amyloid Fiber Promotes β-Nanocrystal Formation and Displays Gigapascal Tensile Strength. </span><a href="https://phys.org/journals/acs-nano/" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">ACS Nano</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"> , 2021. </span><a href="http://dx.doi.org/10.1021/acsnano.1c02944" target="_blank" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">DOI: 10.1021/acsnano.1c02944</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">. </span><a href="https://pubs.acs.org/doi/10.1021/acsnano.1c02944" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">https://pubs.acs.org/doi/10.1021/acsnano.1c02944</span></a></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">确切地说，这种被称为“多聚淀粉样”纤维</span>("polymeric amyloid" fiber)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">的人造丝并不是由研究人员在技术上制造的，而是由麦凯维工程学院（</span><a href="https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2021/07/210720185821.htm" target="_blank">McKelvey School of Engineering</a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）能源、环境与化学工程系教授张福忠（</span>Fuzhong Zhang<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">音译）的实验室通过基因工程细菌制造的。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">张福忠以前也做过蜘蛛丝（</span><a href="https://phys.org/tags/spider+silk/" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">spider silk</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）研究。</span>2018<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">年，他的实验室改造了一种重组蜘蛛丝（</span><a href="https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2021/07/210720185821.htm" target="_blank">recombinant spider silk</a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">），使其在所有重要机械性能上都能与天然蜘蛛丝媲美。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">张福忠说：“在我们之前的研究之后，我想知道我们是否能利用我们的合成生物平台创造出比蜘蛛丝更好的东西。”</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">该研究团队，包括此论文的第一作者、张福忠实验室的博士生李景耀</span>(Jingyao Li<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">音译</span>)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">，修改了蜘蛛丝蛋白质的氨基酸序列，引入新的特性，同时保留了蜘蛛丝的一些吸引人的特征。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">与重组蜘蛛丝纤维有关的一个问题——无需对天然蜘蛛丝序列进行重大修改——是需要制造β纳米晶体（</span><a href="https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2021/07/210720185821.htm" target="_blank">β-nanocrystals</a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">），这是天然蜘蛛丝的主要成分，有助于增强其强度。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">张福忠说</span>:<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">“蜘蛛已经找到了纺出合适数量纳米晶体纤维的方法。但当人类使用人工纺丝工艺时，合成丝纤维中的纳米晶体含量往往低于天然丝纤维。”</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">为了解决这个问题，研究小组通过引入淀粉样蛋白序列重新设计了蚕丝序列，淀粉样蛋白序列具有形成β</span>-<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">纳米晶体的高趋势。他们用三种已被充分研究过的淀粉样蛋白序列作为代表，创造了不同的聚合淀粉样蛋白。由此产生的蛋白质比蜘蛛丝具有更少的重复氨基酸序列，这使得它们更容易被工程细菌生产出来。最终，该细菌产生了一种含有</span>128<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">个重复单位的杂交聚合淀粉样蛋白。用相似重复单位重组蛛丝蛋白已被证明是困难的。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">蛋白质越长，产生的纤维就越坚韧。这些重复</span>128<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">次的蛋白质产生了一种具有吉帕斯卡强度（</span>gigapascal strength<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）的纤维，这种纤维比普通钢更强。这种纤维的韧性</span>(<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">衡量断裂一根纤维需要多少能量的指标</span>)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">比凯夫拉尔（</span>Kevlar<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）纤维和之前所有的重组丝纤维都要高。它的强度和韧性甚至比一些报道的天然蜘蛛丝纤维还要高。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">在与能源、环境与化学工程系的</span>Young- Shin Jun<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">教授和她的博士生朱亚光（</span>Yaguang Zhu<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">音译）的合作下，该团队证实了聚合物淀粉样纤维的高机械性能确实来自于增加的β纳米晶体数量。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">这些新的蛋白质和产生的纤维并不是张福忠实验室高性能合成纤维的结局。他们才刚刚开始。张福忠说</span>:<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">“这表明，我们可以通过工程生物学生产出比自然界最好的材料更好的材料。”</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">上述介绍仅供参考，欲了解更多信息敬请注意浏览<a href="https://pubs.acs.org/doi/10.1021/acsnano.1c02944" target="_blank"><strong>原文</strong></a>或者<a href="https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2021/07/210720185821.htm" target="_blank"><strong>相关报道</strong></a>。</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><a href="https://pubs.acs.org/doi/10.1021/acsnano.1c02944" style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "arial black", "avant garde";" target="_blank">Abstract</a><br></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">The ability of amyloid proteins to form stable β-sheet nanofibrils has made them potential candidates for material innovation in nanotechnology. However, such a nanoscale feature has rarely translated into attractive macroscopic properties for mechanically demanding applications. Here, we present a strategy by fusing amyloid peptides with flexible linkers from spidroin; the resulting polymeric amyloid proteins can be biosynthesized using engineered microbes and wet-spun into macroscopic fibers. Using this strategy, fibers from three different amyloid groups were fabricated. Structural analyses unveil the presence of β-nanocrystals that resemble the cross-β structure of amyloid nanofibrils. These polymeric amyloid fibers have displayed strong and molecular-weight-dependent mechanical properties. Fibers made of a protein polymer containing 128 repeats of the FGAILSS sequence displayed an average ultimate tensile strength of 0.98 ± 0.08 GPa and an average toughness of 161 ± 26 MJ/m<sup>3</sup>, surpassing most recombinant protein fibers and even some natural spider silk fibers. The design strategy and the biosynthetic approach can be expanded to create numerous functional materials, and the macroscopic amyloid fibers will enable a wide range of mechanically demanding applications.</span></p><p><br></p>                    <br><br>
