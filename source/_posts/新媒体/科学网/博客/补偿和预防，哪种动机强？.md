<p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">人们会策略性地获得道德资格，即占据道德制高点，使自己显得是有道德的人，或者不使自己显得是不道德的人。如果人们多次地或一贯地占据道德制高点，也就是多次地或一贯地获得道德资格，那么，根据心理发展的内化理论，人们也容易成为或者早已成为有道德的人了。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">由前文介绍的两个实验（</span></span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px">Merritt, Effron, Fein, Savitsky, Tuller, & Monin, 2012</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">），不免让人产生疑问：</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">在</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">那样</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的研究中，白人胜任条件下的被试在</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">进行</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">因变量测量时</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">已经决定雇用白人候选人了，因此，指责别人的歧视是为了减轻笼罩在自己</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">心头</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的愧疚感。这样看来，被试把行为标定为种族主义不是为了</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">预防</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">将来</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">疑似</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">歧视决定的不安，而是为了补偿已经做出的歧视决定。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">研究者注意到了这个问题，于是做了</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">研究</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">三</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">直接操纵疑似</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">种族主义的威胁，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">检验</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">策略性道德资格效应。在</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">这个</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">研究中，为了使补偿效应起作用的可能性降到最小，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">研究者</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">让一些被试预期自己将来可能会得到关于他们种族态度的负性诊断反馈。这一操纵</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">在不让被试感到自己做了歧视行为的情况下，激起他们</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">抢先</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">确定自己没有种族主义</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的动机</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">被试是</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px">22<span style="font-family:宋体">名白人学生</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">其中</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px">13<span style="font-family:宋体">名女生。要求他们阅读一篇改编自《华盛顿邮报》的文章</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">内容是</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">关于内隐联想测验及其用于测量种族主义态度</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">方面</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的。该文把内隐联想测验描述</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">成</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">一种测量种族歧视的工具，并且指出那些</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">做了</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">这种测验的白人</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">常常吃惊地发现</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">自己在负性词语与黑人面孔之间怀有内隐的联想。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">在高威胁条件下，这篇文章就此结束。在低威胁条件下，这篇文章接着指出，心理学家对内隐联想测验存在很大争议，有些心理学家认为，它只是测量刻板印象觉知的一种方法，难以有效测量内隐态度。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">预研究发现，两种版本的文章都让学生预期</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">在内隐联想测验上会显示与黑人的中度负性联想，不过，那些阅读高威胁文章的学生</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">把这种联想解释得更能诊断种族歧视。已有研究表明，完成一项</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">声称可以</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">诊断种族主义的内隐联想测验对白人是一种威胁（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Frantz, Cuddy, Burnett, Ray, & Hart, 2004</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">读过文章之后，被试获知他们将在本研究后面部分完成内隐联想测验。在此之前，要求他们</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">用</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">1</span><span style="font-family:宋体">（</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">最胜任）</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">到</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">8</span><span style="font-family:宋体">（</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">最不胜任）</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的量尺，</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">评定一个咨询工作</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">岗位</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">8</span><span style="font-family:宋体">名求职者</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">——</span><span style="font-family:宋体">诡称</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">这</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">是</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">“</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">另一名研究者</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">”</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的研究</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">任务</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。在候选人的信息（例如，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">学业绩点</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">、专业）中有照片（年龄与魅力均匹配），它们显示候选人为</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">7</span><span style="font-family:宋体">名白人、</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">1</span><span style="font-family:宋体">名黑人。因变量是被试对黑人候选人的评定等级。实验者离开房间后，被试在计算机上完成这一测量</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">——</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">暗示被试，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">有</span>“另一</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">名</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">研究者</span>”</span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">会看到这些反应。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">研究者</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">又单独招募</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">研究者</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">预测，高威胁条件的被试，可能担心自己的内隐联想测验结果将会使自己显得是种族主义者，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">从而</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">将会比低威胁条件的被试更倾向于寻求道德资格。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">结果表明，</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">高威胁条件的被试对黑人候选人的评定（</span></span><em><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';font-size: 16px">M</span></em><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px">=3.00, </span><em><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';font-size: 16px">SD</span></em><span Roman';font-size:16px">(20)=3.43, </span><em><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';font-size: 16px">p</span></em><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><.01<span </span><em><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';font-size: 16px">SD</span></em><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px">=1.54<span style="font-family:宋体">预防</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">个体的行为往往是根据其道德轨迹记录而被评价的。预想到会这样，人们能够积极地塑造自己的轨迹记录，以</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span
