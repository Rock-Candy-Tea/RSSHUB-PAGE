<p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">有一位心理学家，与著名物理学家泡利保持着长期的友谊；默里与他相处</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">3</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。荣格向父亲提出犀利</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，父亲</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">通常是回答不上来的</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">认为自己的母亲是一个有能力的人，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">也</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">是一位好母亲，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">唯一存活的儿子。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">荣格</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的童年</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">是</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New style="font-family:宋体">荣格</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">有几次差点儿死掉，并且多病。</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">上学后，他</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">曾多次发生昏厥，结果休学在家</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">达</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px">6<span style="font-family:宋体">个多月。他很满意于疾病给自己带来的</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">这种</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">不去进行正规学习的自由和机会。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">有一次，</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">他听到父亲向别人诉苦：</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">“</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">这孩子将来可怎么办</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">荣格就</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">恢复了健康，重返学校。这次经历使</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">荣格</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">理解了神经病的意义：一个人之所以患病，是因为他有患病的愿望</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">荣格</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">本想成为一名考古学家，由于经济原因，他只能上</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">巴塞尔</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">大学。</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">该校</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">没有考古</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">专业</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，他就选择了学习医学。</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">1900</span><span style="font-family:宋体">年毕业后，</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体"><span style="font-family: 宋体; text-indent: 32px;">荣格</span>在一家精神病院当过助手，在</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">苏黎世</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">大学当过讲师，还开了一家私人诊所。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px">1907<span style="font-family:宋体">年</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，荣格</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">第一次</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">见到弗洛伊德。这次会面，是激动人心的，</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">二人都被对方及对方的工作所深深</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">折服</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">他们连续谈了</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">13</span><span style="font-family:宋体">个小时。</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">从此</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，他们</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">形成了一种个人与职业的密切关系。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">弗洛伊德</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">将</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">荣格</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">看成是自己</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">精神分析事业</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的继承人，用父亲对孩子的全部感情来对待</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">荣格</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。当国际精神分析学会成立时，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">弗洛伊德</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">力排众议，让</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">荣格</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">当了第一任主席。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">荣格也的确追随弗洛伊德的步伐。</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">1909</span><span style="font-family:宋体">年，</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">美国</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">克拉克</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">大学</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">荣格希望自己与弗洛伊德的关系是合作性质的，彼此都能追求自己的学术思想。他通过分析精神病患者的妄想与幻觉，相信存在带有普遍性的原型（<span style="font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 16px;">archetype</span>），从而，他对人格的看法与弗洛伊德就不相同了。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">当荣格继续表达自己的观点时，他与弗洛伊德的分裂就是不可避免的了。荣格</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">于</span>1913<span style="font-family:宋体">年离开了</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">弗洛伊德及其</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">学派。这一分裂，对</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">弗洛伊德</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">来说</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">是一个重大损失，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">他器重并悉心培养的接班人，弃他而去，他昏倒了两次。</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">对</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">荣格</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">来说，也是一次</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">精神</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">伤害</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">他陷入了一个广泛的</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">心理</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">失常时期，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family: 宋体; text-indent: 32px;">荣格</span>身材高大，肩膀宽阔。除了追求学术，他还喜欢划船和登山。荣格善于倾听、善于交流，可以轻松地与各种人打交道。作为一名具有非凡技巧的心理治疗师，荣格广受赞誉，吸引了世界各地的人前去拜访。他还获得了哈佛大学和剑桥大学的荣誉博士学位。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">荣格</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">荣格</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">认为</span></span><span Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">由原型构成。原型是以某种方式知觉世界的普遍的思想形式或倾向。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">其中的一种原型是</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">自身（</span>self<span style="font-family:宋体">）</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，它</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">代表</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">个体</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">努力追求</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">人格各部分的统一</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">真正的自身处于意识和无意识、理性和非理性的边界上。自身的发展是</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">个体人生</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的目标，只有当其他人格系统都充分发展之后，自身才开始出现</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">或者说显现</span><span style="font-family:宋体">——这就是自身实现（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">self-realization</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">如果做个比喻，只有当一朵花完全开放之后，方能显现它的样子，而它之所以能成为这个样子，是早就确定了的，只待真正充分绽放。同时，这朵花在开放之前，很可能由于各种原因，根本就没等到开放就枯萎了，或者即使开放了，但是，开放得并不充分、并不完整，那也是没有自身实现。后来，马斯洛在此基础上，提出了自我实现（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">self-actualization</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）理论。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent: 32px;"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">可以看出，荣格本人达到了自身实现——小时候孤独、乖僻、内向，后来则是善于交往、宽以待人、魅力四射。同时，自身实现，要求个人各方面均衡发展、协调发展、和谐发展、充分发展、全面发展，这对于当前的心理健康教育乃至整个教育，都具有重要的启发意义。</span></span></p>                    <br><br>
