overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">Results</span></strong>，也就是实验结果。第四，“D”是<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; color: #890303; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">Discussion</span></strong>（讨论）。一般的文章都是遵循IMRD格式，即使是《Nature》《Science》中的文章，虽然它可能没有IMRD这种格式的小标题，也遵循这样的顺序。当然，在引言之前还有标题、作者名字和单位、摘要、关键词。在讨论后面还有结论、致谢和参考文献。整体而言就是这样一个行文顺序，我们在讲课或者报告论文时，一般介绍主体的“IMRD”四个部分，这四个部分也是最重要的。</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><br></p></section><section><section><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">（三）一篇文章的基本结构：“痰盂”结构</strong><br></span></section></section><section><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">一篇好文章的总体结构就是要遵循“痰盂”结构，一些教授虽然可能没有听说过这个结构，但是在无意中也会使用这个结构。“痰盂”结构用中国人的传统讲法就是<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; color: #890303; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">“龙头、猪肚、凤尾”</span></strong>。</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">“龙头”也就是引言部分，是从一个吸引广大读者的宽泛的概念聚焦到本文要描述的实验，由大到小，所以是一个倒金字塔结构。当中称为“猪肚”，内容很丰满，有各种各样的实验内容、实验结果。最后是称为“凤尾”，包含的是讨论和结论，并从你得到的结果进行拓展，挖掘意义，高调收场，也就是由小到大，是一个金字塔结构。</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><br></p></section><section><section><span style="font-size: 18px;">下面我们就分三个板块来介绍“龙头”、“猪肚”、“凤尾”。<br></span></section></section></section></section><section><section><section></section></section></section><section><section></section></section></section></section></section><section><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><br></p></section><section><section><section><section><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">03</span></p></section></section><section><section><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: none; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; text-decoration-line: underline; font-size: 18px; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="text-decoration-line: underline; font-size: 18px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; color: #890303; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">引言部分（Introduction）</span></strong></span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: none; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; font-size: 18px; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">从General（宽泛）到Specific（具体）</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: none; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><br></p></section></section><section><section></section></section></section></section><section><section><section><section><section><section></section></section><section><section></section></section></section><section><section><section><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; text-align: left; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; text-align: justify;">（一）引言的目的（要实现的基本功能）</strong><br></span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; text-align: left; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">“龙头”就是引言部分，引言部分要实现几个基本功能：<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; color: #890303; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">第一</span></strong>，介绍你所要研究的领域，比如我刚才说的当今人类的癌症影响中，癌症就是一个领域，即背景知识介绍。<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; color: #890303; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">第二</span></strong>，解释你所研究的领域很重要，即推销，提高重要性，因为这个领域重要，所以你的文章才有可能发表。<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; color: #890303; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">第三</span></strong>，介绍别人以往的工作，把功劳给那些为你种树，让你来乘凉的前人。有一句话说“太阳底下没有什么东西是新的”，你的研究必然是建立在前人研究的基础上的，你需要提到别人的功劳，来引用他的文献。<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; color: #890303; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">第四</span></strong>，解释为什么要做这个工作，就是别人已经做了这个、做了那个，但是还有什么缺陷、不足，有什么gap（空档、空隙）需要你去填补。这一层意思又称为“gap statement”，即填补空白。<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; color: #890303; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">第五</span></strong>，介绍你现在进行的工作，就是你做什么、发现了什么，有什么意义和价值。这就是一个序或引言部分的写法，它要涵盖五层意思。</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">我们以研究抽烟对人得肺癌的影响为例陈述如下：第一点（To introduce the area of research），“近年来，肺癌成了第X大‘人体杀手’，而抽烟常被认为是病因之一。”第二点（To explain the importance of the research area），“搞清抽烟对肺癌的数学关联和模型对提升公众的认识和减少肺癌发生有重要作用。”第三点（To highlight the relevant, precedent works），“谁谁谁研究了抽万宝路对美国纽约州得肺癌的影响。谁谁谁研究了抽摩尔对法国人得肺癌的影响。”第四点（To justify the need for the current work），“但是抽红塔山对上海人得肺癌的数学模型没有研究过。”第五点（To introduce the current work），“有鉴于此，在本文中，我们选取上海6个三级医院得肺癌病人作为样本，研究抽红塔山的时间和量对上海人得肺癌的关联和数学模型。”这就是写作提纲，当然引言部分不只是这5句话，有些地方你可以进行扩充，添加内容。</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">（二）引言的最基本的写法</strong></span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">写引言部分的核心目的和基本功能（底线）是向读者和审稿人说清楚“我为什么要做这个研究”，能够自圆其说。而为了达到这个目的，有一个<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; color: #890303; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">“基本公式”</span></strong>：<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; color: #890303; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">首先说这个课题引起广泛兴趣，有重要的理论和应用价值；其次说谁谁谁做了什么，他们发现了什么，但是什么什么还不清楚，效果还不理想。这就是所谓的gap。再次说有鉴于存在的问题，我们在本文中用了什么方法做了什么，得到什么结果。这就是所谓的bridge the gap。</span></strong></span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">（三）写引言的要点</strong></span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; color: #890303; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">写引言的要点在于，把自己和别人区分开来。</span></strong>没有什么东西是完全新的，所以你必须要善于包装文章，把自己的卖点给说出来，也就是交代“市场”上已经有很多类似的文章了，为什么你的文章值得发表？例如，既然市场上可乐卖的很好，为什么佳得乐有生存空间？我们可以运用经济学理论中“市场细分原理”来解决这个问题。比如说，可乐是一种碳酸型饮料，而佳得乐不是碳酸饮料，是运动型饮料，喝了以后能够补充维生素，也有利于补充体内的水分。并且佳得乐不含咖啡因，这也是一种优势。这些就是佳得乐和可乐的区别，也是佳得乐的“卖点”（selling point）。</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">（四）如何师出有名（自圆其说）？</strong></span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">我们先看以下几种表述：</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; font-size: 18px; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">    <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%;"> > 虽然很多人研究了Y，但还有很多问题没有搞清楚</span></span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; font-size: 18px; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">     > 虽然很多人搞清楚了X，但是Y还是没有搞清楚</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; font-size: 18px; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">     > 虽然有几个工作研究了X，但关于Y的研究尚未报道</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; font-size: 18px; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">     > 虽然方法X达到需要的检测极限，但该方法昂贵且耗时</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; font-size: 18px; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">     > 需要额外的研究来证实这些前人报道的结果</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">这些话是不能随便说的，其言外之意在于，你能解决这些问题。写作时，你要确定自己的研究已经解决了什么问题，然后再倒推说这个地方有问题，也就是打下伏笔。如果你不能够解决这个问题，那就不要采用这样的表述，否则就成了审稿人攻击的对象。</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; color: #890303; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><br></span></strong></span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">（五）小结</strong></span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">引言部分的写作提纲是这样的：<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; color: #890303; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">第一步是介绍研究领域</span></strong>，这其中包括鉴别、界定研究领域，强调该研究领域的重要性，以及提供相关的基础背景，在提供的过程中需要引用相关的文献。<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; color: #890303; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">第二步是鉴别出一个gap或一群gaps</span></strong>。<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; color: #890303; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">第三步就是弥补空白</span></strong>。最后用一句“本文我们用什么方法做什么东西，得到什么关键的结果”这样的话结尾。<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; color: #890303; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">引言部分的总体意图就是从General（宽泛）到Specific（具体）。</span></strong></span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; color: #890303; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><br></span></strong></span></p></section><section><section><span style="font-size: 18px;">最后补充一点，<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; color: #890303; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">引言部分最好是问题导向</span></strong>。问题导向实际上也是一种“马后炮”。你在刚开始做实验时，其实对相关文献和实验内容并不熟悉，都是自己去摸索的。有时候导师给你的目标是A，但是你自己做完之后发现得到的是A’，也就是最终的东西与你原来设想的不一样。那么你就需要重新修改，对症下药。在写文章的时候，重新调整角度，找到关键的问题，也就是找到对应你的创新点的问题。<br></span></section></section></section></section><section><section><section></section></section></section><section><section></section></section></section></section></section><section><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><br></p></section><section><section><section><section><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">04</span></p></section></section><section><section><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: none; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; text-decoration-line: underline; color: #890303; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">结果部分（Results）</span></strong><br></span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: none; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; font-size: 18px; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">强调你的关键发现、提炼并表达趋势和规律、指出并解释反常的数据</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: none; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><br></p></section></section><section><section></section></section></section></section><section><section><section><section><section><section></section></section><section><section></section></section></section><section><section><section><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">（一）结果部分的基本概念</strong><br></span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; color: #890303; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">结论部分的基本概念就是逻辑性地阐述你的实验结果。</span></strong></span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">大致来说，就是将结论分为几个部分。这个可以是对实验不同变量引起的结果，或者研究的不同方面进行分别介绍。<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; color: #890303; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">在结论部分，要注意“分段”，也就是小标题的运用，并注意每段话表示一个意思，有一个主题句。</span></strong>比如你这段话讲了用红外光谱来检测，那么就专门讲红外光谱，不要牵扯进其他仪器的内容，也就是不要一段话讲两个不同的东西。此外，<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; color: #890303; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">整个结果部分要有逻辑性，每段话要注意起承转合。</span></strong>一般来说写Results基本上是看图说话，你出现5张图，你就按“12345”的顺序去写，这是一个基本套路。</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">（二）结果部分的注意</strong></span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">在报道数据的过程中注意实现三个功能：</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; color: #890303; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">第一，要向读者强调你的关键的发现。</span></strong>你的文章需要突出你的中心思想，强调关键发现，而不能平铺直述。比如介绍10张图，你不可能不分青红皂白地每张图都详细描述，花费500个字的篇幅，而是要对那些不重要的图一笔带过，例如说“见附件图3”就行了。</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; color: #890303; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">第二，从表观的实验数据中提炼出趋势和规律，并表达出来。</span></strong>比如在这张图中，你看到随着时间的变化，数据点先上升后下降，你也需要表达出这样的趋势，甚至需要对可能的原因进行简短的解释。</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; color: #890303; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">第三，如果有反常的数据，也需要指出来，并进行简要的解释。</span></strong>比如说我们环境系观测大气中铵离子的浓度，从5月1日到5月16日期间每天观察，但是在发表文章的图中，缺少了其中一天的数据点。那么就需要在文章中告诉审稿人，缺少的原因是因为当天停电了。如果没有解释，可能就会引起审稿人的疑惑，这种疑惑积累到一定程度就会导致文章不通过。</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">（三）小结</strong></span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">结论部分的写作提纲是：<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><br></p></section><section><section><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">（四）注意事项</strong><br></span></section></section><section><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; color: #890303; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><br></span></strong></span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">首先，需要注意在处理数据结果时，<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; color: #890303; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">并不是所有的数据都要用上去！</span></strong>写作的时候一般不要描述发现科学事实的曲折故事。不要说：“为了确定这个物质，我们查阅了大量书籍，从来没有报道过。于是我们到图书馆找了其它文献，发现了归属。”不要暗示“没有功劳也有苦劳”，而要直奔主题。</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">其次，<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; color: #890303; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">要避免使用一些词汇和短语</span></strong>。<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; color: #890303; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">第一，要避免使用俚语和行话</span></strong>。<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; color: #890303; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">第二，尽量简要</span></strong>，多余的内容会掩盖你的信息，使你的读者感到厌烦。<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; color: #890303; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">第三，省略空洞的短语</span></strong>，例如“As already stated”、“ It has been found that”等。<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; color: #890303; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">第四，避免多余的词语</span></strong>，例如“It is a procedure that is often used.”应该直接表述为“This procedure is often used.”“This is a problem that is……”应该表述为“This problem is……”。</span></p></section><section><section><section><section><section><br></section></section></section></section></section><section><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">05<br></span></p></section></section></section></section></section></section><section><section><section><section><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: none; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; text-decoration-line: underline; color: #890303; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">讨论部分（Discussion）</span></strong></span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: none; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; font-size: 18px; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">一篇好的文章不能仅仅停留在描述我们看到了什么，更需要介绍从中体会到什么永恒的真理。</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: none; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><br></p></section></section><section><section></section></section></section></section><section><section><section><section><section><section></section></section><section><section></section></section></section><section><section><section><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">（一）讨论部分的结构</strong><br></span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><br></strong></span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; color: #890303; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">第一步是讨论特定的结果。你需要提醒读者你的结果，并解释你的结果。</span></strong>在此过程中，你需要从你的实验当中提炼出几个要点。比如你是研究生物的，你在实验中加入一种药剂之后可以杀死某种物质，为什么能杀死呢？你就对此进行解释，因为分子中的某个基团能够跟某一个蛋白质结合，一旦缺失了这个蛋白质，该物质就没有办法生存了。那么是怎么证明的呢？你是通过做了200组实验，抽取了300个病人的样本，病人遍布五大洲的，因而是具有代表性的。此外，某某等人发现另外一种药物也具有这个基团，加入以后也能扼制蛋白质的产生，也具有效果，只不过你的效果是99%，而他的是80%。<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; color: #890303; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">要像这样讨论、比较自己和别人的区别，提出要点并提供证据。</span></strong></span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; color: #890303; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">讨论部分的总体意图就是从Specific（具体）到General（宽泛）。</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">讨论部分我归纳了四个要点：</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; font-size: 18px; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">不要漏引、错引，不要泛泛而引、为引而引</span></p></section></section><section><section></section></section></section></section><section><section><section><section><section><section></section></section><section><section></section></section></section><section><section><section><p style="margin-top: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">引用参考文献有五点主要功能（或者说目的）：</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 18px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; color: #890303; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">第一，把credit（功劳、贡献）诚实地分给前人和同行。</span></strong>例如指出谁谁谁曾经做过什么，他们发现了什么。</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; white-space: normal; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%;
