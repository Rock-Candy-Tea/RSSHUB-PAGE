<div>   
<p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">无论在现实当中，还是在网络世界，时不时就会出现攻击行为，有的人显得戾气很重，言行举止都弥散着攻击性。攻击行为，直接对被攻击者造成身心伤害，间接影响其他耳闻目睹者，可能给人留下挥之不去的阴影。因此，降低攻击性，对于社会和个体来说，都是非常重要的。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">提起攻击，人们很容易想到多拉德和米勒的挫折</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">-</span><span style="font-family:宋体">攻击理论，减少个体的攻击行为，一种可以考虑的策略是减少个体的挫折事件。然而，一方面，个体在社会上生活、学习和工作，挫折是难以避免的；另一方面，挫折不仅具有即时效应，而且具有潜伏效应，因此，攻击行为的出现可能更为微妙。显然，降低个体的攻击性，特别是攻击的思想、认知和情绪、情感，就是十分必要和关键的。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">如何降低攻击性呢？具体方法也有很多。例如，研究表明，听亲社会歌曲就是一种降低攻击性的有效方法（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Greitemeyer, 2011</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）。虽然这种方法很简单，但是，通过实证研究表明它的效应并不直观，涉及一些理论问题和研究技术。概括地说，这项研究共有</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">5</span><span style="font-family:宋体">个实验，逐步考察听亲社会歌曲降低攻击性的效应及内在机制。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">实验</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">一发现</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">听</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">亲社会</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">歌曲能够降低</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">攻击思想的通达性。</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">从广义的启动实验来讲，听</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">亲社会歌曲，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">可以</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">激活亲社会思想，提高亲社会思想的通达性</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">；听反社会歌曲</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">可以激活反社会（比如，攻击）思想，提高反社会思想的通达性。相比之下，接受一种启动条件，抑制某种思想的通达性，是困难的。因此，实验一背后的逻辑是，听亲社会歌曲</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，抑郁反社会</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">（攻击）</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">思想，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">从而</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">降低反社会</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">（攻击）</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">思想的通达性。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">实验</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">二</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">通过采用攻击</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">思想、</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">认知的</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">其他</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">测量</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">指标，</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">控制情绪与唤醒，重复并扩展了</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">实验</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">一</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">结果。</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">实验</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">三</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">表明，听</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">亲社会</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">歌曲</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">不仅</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">降低</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">攻击认知，而且</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">降低</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">攻击情感。</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">实验</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">四</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">发现，</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">听亲社会</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">歌曲能够减少攻击行为。实验</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">五</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">考察听亲社会歌曲减少攻击行为的内在机制，结果表明，</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">亲社会</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">歌曲降低攻击情感（</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">状态敌意</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">）</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">而降低的攻击情感（</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">状态敌意</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">）</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">减少攻击行为。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">从上可知，听亲社会歌曲能够降低攻击认知、攻击情感和攻击行为；特别地，中介分析显示，攻击情感而非攻击认知，是亲社会歌曲与（减少）攻击行为之间的中介变量。也就是说</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，亲社会</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">歌曲</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">对攻击行为的</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">降低</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">效应</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">是通过情感而非认知而起作用的。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">实际上，</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">亲社会</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">歌曲</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">对亲社会行为的效应也是由情感而非认知变量来中介的（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Greitemeyer, 2009</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">即，</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">虽然听亲社会</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">歌曲提高</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">了亲社会思想的通达性和移情</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">水平</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，但是只有移情</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">能够</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">引起助人行为。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">由于目前玩游戏的人似乎更多，不妨将听歌曲与玩游戏的社会效果</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">作</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">个</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">对照</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">研究显示</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，玩电子游戏对亲社会行为的效应是由认知而非情感变量来中介的。玩反社会电子游戏</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">会</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">增</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">强</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">攻击认知与情感，但是，只有攻击认知引发攻击行为（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Anderson & Dill, 2000</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）。同样地，玩亲社会电子游戏增</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">大</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">亲社会思想的通达性与移情，但是，只有亲社会思想通达性引发助人行为（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Greitemeyer & Osswald, 2010</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">所以</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">听歌曲和玩游戏对个体社会行为的影响过程是不同的，听歌曲</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">对社会行为的效应是通过情感</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">而</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">起作用，玩游戏</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">对社会行为</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的效应是通过认知</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">而</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">起作用。</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">例如，</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">听反社会</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">歌曲</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">通过增</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">大</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">攻击情感而增加攻击行为，玩亲社会电子游戏是通过减少攻击认知而减少攻击行为。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">了解这些内在机制，有助于指导歌曲创作者和游戏开发者在工作中更好地考虑社会效益，生产更多亲社会的歌曲和游戏；也有助于指导教育工作者在实践中更好地采取有效措施，更有针对性地开展有关活动，培养学生的亲社会性，降低学生的反社会性（比如，攻击性）。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span
