<p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">著名工程师和发明家的名言（5）</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">武夷山 译</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">    Workflowmax网站于2020年4月提供了一些著名工程师和发明家的名言（博主：原文如此，其实有的语录的主人并非工程师，只是语录内容涉及工程或工程师），下面我翻译其中一些，与大家分享。这是第五部分。</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-weight: normal;"> </span></strong></span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-weight: normal;">1. “The well being of the world largely depends upon the work of the engineer. There is a great future and unlimited scope for the profession; new works of all kinds are and will be required in every country, and for a young man of imagination and keenness I cannot conceive a more attractive profession. Imagination is necessary as well as scientific knowledge.”</span></strong><br> -Sir William Halcrow，英国著名水电工程师</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">    世界的福祉主要取决于工程师的工作。这个职业有伟大的前途和无限的施展空间；每个国家现在和将来都需要各种新工程项目。对于一个想象力丰富、热情洋溢的年轻人来说，我想不出一个比工程师更具吸引力的职业了。想象力像科学知识一样不可或缺。</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"><br></span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-weight: normal;">2. “There can be little doubt that in many ways the story of bridge building is the story of civilisation. By it we can readily measure an important part of a people's progress.”</span></strong><br> - Franklin D. Roosevelt，美国前总统罗斯福</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">    毫无疑问，从很多方面讲，桥梁建设的故事就是文明的故事。通过桥梁建设，我们可以很方便地测度一个民族的进步之一个重要侧面。</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"><br></span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-weight: normal;">3. “Engineering is quite different from science. Scientists try to understand nature. Engineers try to make things that do not exist in nature. Engineers stress invention.”</span></strong><br> - Yuan-Cheng Fung，冯元桢，美国国家工程院、美国国家医学研究院和美国国家科学院三院院士</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">    工程学与科学迥然不同。科学家试图认识自然。工程师试图造出自然界不存在的东西。工程师强调发明。</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"><br></span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-weight: normal;">4. “When engineers and quantity surveyors discuss aesthetics and architects study what cranes do we are on the right road.”</span></strong><br> - Ove Arup（1895-1988），出生于英国的著名丹麦结构工程师和企业家</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">    当工程师和建筑估算师讨论美学问题，而建筑师研究吊车的时候，我们就走在正确的道路上了。</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"><br></span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">5. “The life work of the engineer consists in the systematic application of natural forces and the systematic development of natural resources in the service of man.”<br> - Harry Walter Tyler（1863-1938），美国化学家</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">    工程师的毕生使命在于，为服务于人类而系统化地应用自然力和系统化地开发自然资源。</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"> </span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"> 6. “The scientist discovers a new type of material or energy and the engineer discovers a new use for it.”<br> - Gordon Lindsay Glegg，名著The Science of Design的作者</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">    科学家发现新材料或新能源，而工程师发现新材料和新能源的新用途。</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"><br></span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"> 7. “Can one think that because we are engineers, beauty does not <strong><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-weight: normal;">preoccupy us or that we do not try to build beautiful, as well as solid and long-lasting, structures? Aren’t the genuine functions of strength always in keeping with unwritten conditions of harmony?”</span></strong><br> - Gustave Eiffel（1832-1923），埃菲尔铁塔设计者</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">    人们的下列看法合适吗？就因为我们是工程师，美就不关我们的事？或者说我们不打算建造既结实耐久又漂亮的结构？力量的真实作用不正是遵守和谐这一不成文条件吗？</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"><br></span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-weight: normal;"> 8. “Engineering is not merely knowing and being knowledgeable, like a walking encyclopedia; engineering is not merely analysis; engineering is not merely the possession of the capacity to get elegant solutions to non-existent engineering problems; engineering is practicing the art of the organising forces of technological change… Engineers operate at the interface between science and society.”</span></strong><br> - Gordon Stanley Brown（1907-1996），美国著名电气工程师</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">    工程不仅仅意味着知识丰富，见多识广，好比活百科全书；工程不仅仅是分析；工程不仅仅是拥有能力对不存在的工程问题拿出漂亮的解决方案；工程是一种艺术实践——将技术变革的力量加以组织之艺术......工程师工作于科学与社会之界面上。</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"><br></span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-weight: normal;">9. “We shape our buildings, thereafter they shape us.”</span></strong><br> - Winston Churchill，英国前首相丘吉尔</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">    我们给建筑物赋形，然后建筑物形塑着我们。</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"><br></span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-weight: normal;"> </span></strong>  10. <strong><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-weight: normal;">“No one wants to learn by mistakes, but we cannot learn enough from successes to go beyond the state of the art.”</span></strong><strong><br> </strong>- Henry Pe</span><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">troski，美国普渡大学土木工程学教授和和历史学教授，围绕工程学主题发表了很多精彩论著</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">    没人愿意通过失败来学习，但是，通过成功所学到的东西不足以让我们超越目前的最高水平。</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"><br></span></p><p style="line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"><strong><span style="font-size: 16px;
