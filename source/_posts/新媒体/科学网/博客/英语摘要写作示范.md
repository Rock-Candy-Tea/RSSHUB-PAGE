<p>    最近暑假和国内合作者完成了一篇英文论文的初稿写作，在写作过程中积累了一些体会，我会逐步写成博客分享给大家。今天就讲讲摘要的写作。摘要写作重点在四要素：目的，方法，发现或结果，结论。当然有些期刊有自己的要求。这里的模板仅供参考。</p><p>    首先尽量使用主动语态叙说。因为研究已经结束，大部分情况下请使用过去式，少数如研究目的既可以使用现在时也可以使用过去式。但语法软件检查结果得分会不一样。因此时态语态一致最好。另外英语叙说要注意逻辑次序。</p><p>    研究目的叙说不要使用The purpose of this study was （is） 这样的写法， 而是直接说<strong>This study <strong style="white-space: normal;">invesitigated (examined) ...</strong>(</strong>有些文章喜欢使用this study aimed to 或aimes to，但从语言质量讲也是多余的，我承认我自己也用过，也不要使用research这个词，要用的话也用research study，以前讲过这两个词的区别，research指系统性研究，但一般论文都是发表某一局部研究结果，因此这里研究要用study ) 。 或把方法结合目的一起叙说，比如<strong>This study adopted </strong>(used)<strong> a</strong> (comparative, quasi-experimental,...) <strong>method</strong> <strong>to investigate the</strong> (relatiosnhip, effect, impact...) between X and Y。</p><p>    写完了这句话以后不要立即写结果显示，那样摘要会显得太干巴巴。可以说有多少人参与了实验Z number of  partcipants particpanted in the experiment. 或者数据是如何采集的。The research team designed (....), collected, processed, and anayzed data。</p><p>    然后再写结果显示<strong>The results showed that</strong> X and Y were positively correlated。但这里用词correlated和前面的目的里的relationship重复，因此最好使用别的动词，比如X generated effect on Y, X impacted Y。</p><p>    再给出结论，比如尽管某些研究已经有过零星的研究，但系统化地研究这两者之间的关系本研究属于首创。英文写的时候可以这样：Although some exisiting studies have examined X and Y1 or X and Y2，<strong>this study was the first to verify</strong> the relatiosnhip between X and Y 。因此重要意义在于填补了某方面的空白<strong>Therefore， this study filled a reserch gap</strong> in....。</p><p>    按我这个模板写出来的摘要，再使用grammarly （<a href="https://www.grammarly.com/" target="_blank">https://www.grammarly.com/</a>）检查语法，并使用AJE（American Journal Experts) 检查你的英文的逻辑表达和语言质量，（<a href="https://www.aje.com/arc/Free-Grammar-Check-for-Scientists-and-Academics/?gclid=EAIaIQobChMIo8u8n_zU-QIVgz-tBh3-xABLEAAYAiAAEgIvCvD_BwE%EF%BC%89" target="_blank">https://www.aje.com/arc/Free-Grammar-Check-for-Scientists-and-Academics/?gclid=EAIaIQobChMIo8u8n_zU-QIVgz-tBh3-xABLEAAYAiAAEgIvCvD_BwE）</a>  如果达到85-90%就是很不错了。</p><p>    下面是示例，比如我要研究X方法和学生对唱歌五要素的掌握和理解之间的关系或影响程度，结果显示X方法有正面影响。例子如下：</p><p style="text-indent: 32px;line-height: 200%"><strong><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:200%;font-family:'Times New Roman',serif;color:#0E101A">Abstract:</span></strong></p><p style="text-indent: 32px; line-height: 200%;"><strong><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:200%;font-family:'Times New Roman',serif">This study adopted a quasi-experimental method </span></strong><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:200%;font-family:'Times New Roman',serif">and instruments from the existing studies <strong>to investigate the effect of the X-method on students' abilities </strong>to master the five elements of singing. <strong>Fifty-five students participated in the experiment. The research study team designed </strong>a curriculum and syllabus for the experimental class. Through tracking the 8-week project, <strong>the research team collected, processed, and analyzed all data </strong>required for this study. <strong>The results showed that </strong>X-training positively impacted students' mastery of the five elements of singing. <strong>Although some existing studies have examined the relationship between</strong> X-method and breath control or resonance, <strong>this study was the first to verify the relationship </strong>between X-method and all five elements: breathing, phonation, resonation, registration, and articulation. <strong>Therefore, the study filled a research gap </strong>using X-method in singing teaching and training and added a new teaching tool to academic music programs.</span></p><p style="text-indent: 32px; line-height: 200%;">用这个模板写出来的这段摘要叙说逻辑清楚，没有重复使用动词，语态时态一致，使用AJE检查为8.7分高于提交给AJE的95%的文章。它说使用它们的专家服务（要付费的，最便宜一次大概是70美元，忘了也许90吧）也就是达到8.7分。This paper scored a <span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder;">8.7/10</span> which is in the <span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder;">95th</span> percentile of papers submitted to AJE.After editing by AJE, papers improve on average to a score of <span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder;">8.7</span> and move into the <span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder;">95th</span> percentile of research papers submitted to journals.<span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder;">Congratulations!</span> Your document is well written and does not need language editing.</p><p style="text-indent: 32px; line-height: 200%;">当然了，得高分还取决于写作者对英文词汇的运用和逻辑叙说的能力。</p><p style="text-indent: 32px; line-height: 200%;">希望对有志于发表英文期刊写作的人有所帮助。</p><p style="text-indent: 32px; line-height: 200%;"><br></p>                    <br><br>
