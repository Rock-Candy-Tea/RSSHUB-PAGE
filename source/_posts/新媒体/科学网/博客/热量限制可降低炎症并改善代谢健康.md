<p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:15px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:150%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">在不引起</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">营养不良的</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">条件下减少食物摄取，就是所谓</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">热量限制</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">，</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">具有长寿效应，不仅对健康寿命，而且对绝对寿命都具有延长作用，这是抗衰老领域最受广泛接受的观点。也因此产生了许多通过限制热量摄取的健康养生方法。如轻断食，隔日断食，甚至辟谷等。</span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">在人类的大部分进化过程中，高能量的营养物质都是稀缺的，所以主要的遗传途径进化成有利于增加高热量饮食的摄入，并在脂肪组织中以甘油三酯的形式储存能量。在这种储存卡路里的进化压力下，现代世界中高能量饮食的消耗导致了肥胖。肥胖是一种由慢性热量摄入和</span>IIMR<span style="font-family:宋体">干扰引起的多系统紊乱。文献表明，肥胖患者的</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">IIMR</span><span style="font-family:宋体">改变会导致慢性促炎状态，与</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">2</span><span style="font-family:宋体">型糖尿病、非酒精性脂肪性肝炎和心血管疾病等疾病有关</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">。相反，</span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">热量限制引起</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">的</span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">负能量平衡是延长多物种寿命和健康寿命的最有效手段</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">。</span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:15px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:150%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">但是科学家对热量限制的健康效应分子基础仍然继续进行研究，免疫系统特别是胸腺功能下降是衰老的典型表现，热量限制对免疫功能的影响是这一领域的重要问题之一。</span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:15px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:150%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">最近</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:Calibri">Spadaro</span><span style="font-family:宋体">等</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">将</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">两年内热量限制</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">14%</span><span style="font-family:宋体">人类和热量限制</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">40%</span><span style="font-family:宋体">小鼠免疫功能指标</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">进行了分析。</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">细胞分析和转录调查显示</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">，</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">胸腺功能改善</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">的同时，热量限制的人类受试者</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">血小板活化因子乙酰水解酶</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">(PLA2G7)</span><span style="font-family:宋体">基因的表达</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">下降，</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">该基因失活的</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">小鼠</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">减少了炎症，改善了衰老小鼠的胸腺功能和一些代谢功能的标志物。</span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:15px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:150%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">在啮齿类动物中，</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">40%</span><span style="font-family:宋体">的热量限制驱动的寿命延长导致生长、繁殖和免疫防御的权衡，这使得很难确定治疗相关的</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">热量限制</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">模拟目标。</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">该研究发现，</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">健康人群中约</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">14%</span><span style="font-family:宋体">的</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">CR</span><span style="font-family:宋体">持续</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">2</span><span style="font-family:宋体">年可改善胸腺生成，并与胸腺内异位脂质动员相关。</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">热量限制</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">诱导的脂肪组织转录重编程涉及调节线粒体生物能、抗炎反应和寿命的途径。</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">pl2g7</span><span style="font-family:宋体">编码血小板活化因子乙酰水解酶</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">(Pla2g7)</span><span style="font-family:宋体">的基因表达在</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">热量限制</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">人类中受到抑制。在小鼠中缺失</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">pl2g7</span><span style="font-family:宋体">显示胸腺脂肪萎缩减少，对年龄相关炎症的保护，降低</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">NLRP3</span><span style="font-family:宋体">炎症小体激活，并改善代谢健康。因此，</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">PLA2G7</span><span style="font-family:宋体">的降低可能介导</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">热量限制</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">的免疫代谢效应，并可能用于降低炎症和延长健康寿命。</span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:15px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:150%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"> </span></p><p
