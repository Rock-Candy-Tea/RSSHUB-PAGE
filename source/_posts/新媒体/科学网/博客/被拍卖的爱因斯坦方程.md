<p style="text-indent: 29px; line-height: 1.5em;"><span style="font-family: "times new roman";"><br></span></p><p style="text-indent: 29px; line-height: 1.5em;"><span style="font-family: "times new roman";"></span></p><p style="text-indent: 29px; text-align: left; line-height: 1.5em;"><span style="font-family: "times new roman";"></span></p><p style="text-indent: 28px; line-height: 1.5em;"><span style="font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">5</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">月</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">20</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">日，爱因斯坦的一封信在波士顿</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">RR</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">拍卖行卖了</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">1,243,707</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">美元，信的亮点在其中有公式</span><em><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">E = mc</span></em><sup><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">2</span></sup><span style="font-family: 宋体;">。据加州理工和耶路撒冷希伯来大学的老爱档案，有三份留着老爱手写质能公式的文件。这封信是第四件，从</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Ludwik Silberstein</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">后人流出；原信是老爱</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">1946</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">年</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">10</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">月</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">26</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">日写给物理学家</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">LS</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">的。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent: 28px; line-height: 1.5em;"><span style="font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">LS1871</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">年</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">5</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">月</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">17</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">日生在波兰华沙（一般文献说他生于</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">1872</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">年，因为他父亲拖了一年才报告他出生），与爱因斯坦一样在求学时代赶上了麦克斯韦的电磁理论和世纪之初的物理学革命。他游艺于数学物理，精通多种语言，做过普朗克和洛伦兹的翻译，他在</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">1907</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">年引入的复电磁矢量（<span style="font-family: "times new roman"; background: white;">即</span></span><strong><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; background: white;">E+iB</span></strong><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; background: white;">, </span><span style="font-family: 宋体; background: white;">又叫</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; background: white;">Riemann-Silberstein vector</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; background: white;">）是现代场论的基本量</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent: 28px; line-height: 1.5em;"><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">1914</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">年</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">LS</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">写过一本相对论，是相对论最早的英文读本之一。但他相信以太理论，</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">和</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Michelson</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">一起做过以太漂移实验。</span><font face="Times New Roman, serif">他</font><span style="font-family: 宋体;">质疑相对论是为了壮大（或打到）它。在那个革命的年代，科学家需要创新，更需要证明。</span></p><p style="text-indent: 28px; line-height: 1.5em;"><span style="font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-family: 宋体;">信的内容很简单，答复</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">LS</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">的一个问题：质量相同的两个物体组成的系统，一个系统两体相距无限远，另一个系统两体距离为</span><em><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">r</span></em><span style="font-family: 宋体;">，那么两个系统的质量差是多少？</span></span></p><p style="text-indent: 28px; line-height: 1.5em;"><span style="font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-family: 宋体;">老爱回答直截了当：</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Your question can be answered from the <em>E</em> =<em> mc</em><sup>2</sup>formula, without any erudition.</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">然后他给出了简单的计算。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent: 28px; line-height: 1.5em;"><span style="font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-family: 宋体;">更有意思的是最后一段：“</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">I am convinced that this (or a formula corrected with respect to the radius of the masses) cannot help in shedding light on atomic constants. For that one must first have a theory that contains the correct unification of gravitation and electricity.</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">”似乎LS想从这个问题引出原子常数的确定？老爱的观点是，那些自然常数只有在统一场论下才能解决。</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">100</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">多年过去了，这方面的进步还是零。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent: 28px; line-height: 1.5em;"><span style="font-family: "times new roman";"></span></p><p style="text-align: center; line-height: 1.5em;"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://bbs.sciencenet.cn/home.php?mod=attachment&filename=einstein%20e%3Dmcc.jpg&id=749465" alt="einstein e=mcc.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p style="text-align: left; line-height: 1.5em;"><span style="font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="text-indent: 28px; font-family: 宋体;">    关于两质点问题，</span><span style="font-family: "times new roman"; text-indent: 28px;"> </span><span style="text-indent: 28px; font-family: 宋体;">亚琛高等工学院的数学家</span><span style="text-indent: 28px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">E. Trefftz</span><span style="text-indent: 28px; font-family: 宋体;">（</span><span style="text-indent: 28px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">1888-1937</span><span style="text-indent: 28px; font-family: 宋体;">）在</span><span style="text-indent: 28px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">20</span><span style="text-indent: 28px; font-family: 宋体;">年代初也做过类似工作（</span><span style="text-indent: 28px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">The Static Gravitational Field of Two Mass Points in Einstein's Theory. <strong><em>Math. Ann</em>. 86,</strong> 317. 1922</span><span style="text-indent: 28px; font-family: 宋体;">），他认为找到了一个静态解，在两质点外没有其他奇点。场方程能否给出质量只集中在两个天体的静态宇宙，对大尺度宇宙结构是很重要的，所以它“引起了我的兴趣”。于是，老爱在</span><span style="text-indent: 28px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">1922</span><span style="text-indent: 28px; font-family: 宋体;">年</span><span style="text-indent: 28px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">11</span><span style="text-indent: 28px; font-family: 宋体;">月</span><span style="text-indent: 28px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">23</span><span style="text-indent: 28px; font-family: 宋体;">日发表了一个评论（《柏林普鲁士科学院物理</span><span style="text-indent: 28px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">-</span><span style="text-indent: 28px; font-family: 宋体;">数学部会议公报》，见全集第卷文件</span><span style="text-indent: 28px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">387</span><span style="text-indent: 28px; font-family: 宋体;">），随没有否定T的解，但认为它不能代表那样的静态宇宙图景。另外，</span><span style="text-indent: 28px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Weyl</span><span style="text-indent: 28px; font-family: 宋体;">和</span><span style="text-indent: 28px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Kottler</span><span style="text-indent: 28px; font-family: 宋体;">也曾独立发现过T的解，而且</span><span style="text-indent: 28px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Birkhoff</span><span style="text-indent: 28px; font-family: 宋体;">定理表明，这是带宇宙学常数的场方程的唯一静态解，其意义相当于老场方程的施瓦西解。</span><br></span></p><p style="text-indent: 28px; line-height: 1.5em;"><span style="font-family: "times new roman";"> </span></p><p style="text-indent: 28px; line-height: 1.5em;"><span style="font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">RR</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">拍卖行以前还拍过</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">3</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">件老爱给</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">LS</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">的信，其中两件关乎统一场论，应远比上面那封短信重要。即使从“艺术”说，老爱亲笔的场方程（可能还是唯一手迹）当然远比质能方程壮美和珍稀。可惜收藏者别有眼光，令其“收藏价值”不及</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">EMC</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">的</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">1/6</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">（成交价分别</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">$66, 898</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">和</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">152,920</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">）。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent: 28px; line-height: 1.5em;"><span style="font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">LS</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">在</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">30</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">年代得到场方程的一个特殊解，描述只有两个代表点质量的奇点的静态轴对称度规。</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">1933</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">年</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">12</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">月</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">17</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">日，老爱回信指出那样的解不存在。</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">LS</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">在</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">20</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">日回信坚持认为他是对的：</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; background: white;">Against your expectation, a statical solution with two (and, similarly, 3 or more) “singularities of simple polar character” does exist and, in view of its physical implications, it is imperative to deal with it in a fundamental way in order to uphold your gravitation theory. 24</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; background: white;">日，老爱回信接受奇点解，由此引发对点质量的疑虑，他寄希望于物质张量，或者形式上的唯象理论（暂时忘却它的物理），甚至（可能有些委屈地）想在奇点情形下得到很正确的运动法则（只要能小心确定奇点的特征）。最后他无奈地说，</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">In any case, your investigation clearly shows how careful one must be in dealing with singularities, and how empty a field theory is that allows singularities without exactly determining their character</span>。<span style="font-family: 宋体;">说这样的话，就等于说拿它没办法了。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent: 28px; line-height: 1.5em;"><span style="font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-family: 宋体;">既然老爱没办法，</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">LS</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">在</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">1935</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">年发表了他的度规，第二年</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">3</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">月还在</span><em><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Evening Telegram of Toronto</span></em><span style="font-family: 宋体;">向大众宣扬“对相对论的致命一击”（</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">“Fatal blow to relativity issued here”</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">）。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent: 28px; line-height: 1.5em;"><span style="font-family: "times new roman";"> </span></p><p style="text-indent: 28px; line-height: 1.5em;"><span style="font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-family: 宋体;">【</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">RR</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">拍卖的另一封给</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">LS</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">的信，写于</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">1934</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">年</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">9</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">月</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">20</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">日，原件为德文打印，老爱签名。信中主要说了普朗克的事（以及美国的犹太难民问题）。当时谣传普老向希特勒妥协了（他为犹太化学家</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Fritz Haber</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">去见过希特勒），老爱回答说普老（和他的朋友</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Max von Laue, Walther Nernst</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">）的行为正大光明，</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;
