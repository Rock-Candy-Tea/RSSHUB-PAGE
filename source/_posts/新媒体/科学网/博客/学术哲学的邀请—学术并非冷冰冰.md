max-width: 100%; font-size: 19px; font-family: 宋体; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">本来对于学术我一无所知，就像襁褓里的孩子，睁开双眸不断去试错、容错、纠错，一而再再而三。更像是牙牙学语、蹒跚学步一样，跌倒、爬起、再跌倒、再爬起，却从没有放弃过。这种求索的过程并非是一种漠视或者冷漠，更是一种生命的体验，生活从来不从学术中脱离，而学术亦不能从生活中剥离。</span></p><p style="font-size: medium; white-space: normal; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif; max-width: 100%; font-size: 19px; font-family: 宋体; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">我总是徘徊于学术的海洋中，久久不能离去。也曾迷茫、也曾困惑、也曾流离失所，正是这样的“曲径幽深”掺揉了拾级而来的学术顿悟，奠定了我这一生的教育生涯和学术生涯的方向。这其中裹挟着的多少不为人道的辛酸和泪痕。</span></p><p style="font-size: medium; white-space: normal; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif; letter-spacing: 0.544px; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); text-indent: 37px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-size: 19px; font-family: 宋体; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">正是这不为人道的辛酸和泪痕的经历成就了我对于学术的体验，学术并非冷冰冰，而是人文精神的“知识孪生”，或者人文精神造就学术，学术成就人文精神，不可或缺。人文精神涉及美学、文学、史学、哲学，这绝非空洞或虚无。缺乏人文精神，尤其是对于人的最深沉的认识，似乎会形成一种精神断层，这种断层会使学术不安与不稳？这种人文精神会使学者内心柔软、宽容、坚韧与刚毅，同样，反衬于学术，犹如余晖一样，映引出学术的张力与求索。</span></p><p style="font-size: medium; white-space: normal; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; min-height: 1em; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif;
