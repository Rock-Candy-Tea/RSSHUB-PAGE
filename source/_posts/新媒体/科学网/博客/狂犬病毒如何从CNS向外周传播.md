;line-height:28px;background:rgb(255,255,255)"><strong><span style="font-family: KaiTi;color: #0070C0;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 24px"><span style="font-family:KaiTi">今天要介绍的是狂犬病毒如何从中枢神经系统向外周传播。此文可让我们了解唾液腺在狂犬病毒传播中的核心或关键作用，以及感染发病后狂犬病毒在动物体内的分布情况。这些知识将有助于开发新的狂犬病实验室诊断方法。</span> </span></strong></h2><h2 style="margin-top:0;margin-right:0;margin-bottom:8px;margin-left:0;text-indent:41px;padding:0 0 0 0 ;line-height:28px;background:rgb(255,255,255)"><strong><span style="font-family: KaiTi;color: #0070C0;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 24px"> </span></strong></h2><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:43px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:SimSun;line-height:200%;font-size:20px"><span style="font-family:SimSun">狂犬病毒（</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">RABV</span><span style="font-family:SimSun">）的离心式传播，即病毒沿神经元途径从中枢神经系统</span></span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:200%;font-size:20px">(CNS)</span><span style=";font-family:SimSun;line-height:200%;font-size:20px"><span style="font-family:SimSun">到外周部位的传播，是</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">RABV</span><span style="font-family:SimSun">传播到其自然宿主的必要条件。作为</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">RABV</span><span style="font-family:SimSun">传播载体的猫狗等动物的唾液腺感染是口腔唾液（含</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">RABV</span><span style="font-family:SimSun">）实现病毒传播所必需的。唾液腺受面神经</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">(</span><span style="font-family:SimSun">通过下颌骨神经节或某些动物的</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">Langley</span><span style="font-family:SimSun">神经节</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">)</span><span style="font-family:SimSun">和舌咽神经</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">(</span><span style="font-family:SimSun">通过耳神经节</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">)</span><span style="font-family:SimSun">的副交感神经支配，也受颅颈神经节和传入</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">(</span><span style="font-family:SimSun">感觉</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">)</span><span style="font-family:SimSun">交感神经支配</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">(Emmelin,1967)</span><span style="font-family:SimSun">。将狗和狐狸单侧切除一部分舌神经和</span></span><span style=";font-family:SimSun;line-height:200%;font-size:20px">颅</span><span style=";font-family:SimSun;line-height:200%;font-size:20px"><span style="font-family:SimSun">颈神经节，感染</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">RABV</span><span style="font-family:SimSun">街毒后，这些去除了神经支配的唾液腺的病毒效价与对侧唾液腺的病毒效价相比非常低</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">(Deanetal.</span><span style="font-family:SimSun">，</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">1963)</span><span style="font-family:SimSun">。唾液腺上皮细胞受到广泛感染的证据是病毒沿着多个末端轴突传播的结果，而不是在上皮细胞之间直接传播的结果 </span><span style="font-family:Calibri">(Charlton, Casey, & Campbell, 1983)</span><span style="font-family:SimSun">。</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">RABV</span><span style="font-family:SimSun">抗原集中于粘液腺泡细胞（</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">mucous acinar cells</span><span style="font-family:SimSun">）的顶端区（</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">apical region</span><span style="font-family:SimSun">），超微结构研究显示病毒基质存在于</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;line-height: 200%;color: #2E3033;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 20px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:SimSun">基底区</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;line-height: 200%;color: #2E3033;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 20px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:SimSun">（</span></span><span style=";font-family:SimSun;line-height:200%;font-size:20px"><span style="font-family:Calibri">basal region</span></span><span style="font-family: SimSun;line-height: 200%;color: #2E3033;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 20px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:SimSun">）</span></span><span style=";font-family:SimSun;line-height:200%;font-size:20px"><span style="font-family:SimSun">，病毒在顶端区质膜上通过出芽（</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">budding</span><span style="font-family:SimSun">）进入腺泡腔（</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">acinar lumen</span><span style="font-family:SimSun">）和细胞间小管（</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">intercellular canaliculi</span><span style="font-family:SimSun">），偶尔会出现在分泌颗粒（</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">secretory granules</span><span style="font-family:SimSun">）的膜上 </span><span style="font-family:Calibri">(Balachandran & Charlton, 1994)</span><span style="font-family:SimSun">。唾液腺中的病毒滴度甚至可能高于</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">CNS</span><span style="font-family:SimSun">组织</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">(Dierks, 1975)</span><span style="font-family:SimSun">。</span></span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:43px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:SimSun;line-height:200%;font-size:20px"><span style="font-family:SimSun">除了唾液腺感染，在仓鼠（其乳鼠）离心传播模型中的证据显示，许多外周位点都同时涉及中央、外周和自主神经系统</span> <span style="font-family:Calibri">(Murphy, Harrison, Winn, & Bauer, 1973)</span><span style="font-family:SimSun">。视网膜神经节细胞层和受三叉神经感觉传入神经支配的角膜上皮细胞可见病毒感染。角膜浅层和深层上皮细胞均曾发现被感染 </span><span style="font-family:Calibri">(Balachandran & Charlton, 1994)</span><span style="font-family:SimSun">。角膜印模涂片中</span><a name="OLE_LINK1" href="http://blog.sciencenet.cn/undefined"></a><span style="font-family:Calibri">RABV</span><span style="font-family:SimSun">抗原的检测已被用作人类狂犬病的诊断试验</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">(Koch, Sagartz, Davidson, & Lawhaswasdi, 1975)</span><span style="font-family:SimSun">，且已知</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">RABV</span><span style="font-family:SimSun">可通过人类角膜移植传播。在皮肤活检中，可在毛发的</span></span><span style=";font-family:SimSun;line-height:200%;font-size:20px">司触觉的</span><span style=";font-family:SimSun;line-height:200%;font-size:20px">游离感觉神经末梢发现感染，这是在病人生前确诊狂犬病的最佳诊断方法之一。</span><span style=";font-family:SimSun;line-height:200%;font-size:20px">颈后皮肤富含毛囊，</span><span style=";font-family:SimSun;line-height:200%;font-size:20px"><span style="font-family:SimSun">抗原可能存在于</span><a name="OLE_LINK3" href="http://blog.sciencenet.cn/undefined"></a><span style="font-family:Calibri">RABV</span><span style="font-family:SimSun">离心传播中的实际作用，也不清楚该路径是否只在脊髓内病毒传播后很晚才开始发挥作用</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">(Hemachudha et al.</span><span style="font-family:SimSun">，</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">2013;Ugolini, 2011)</span><span style="font-family:SimSun">。</span></span></p><p
