font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">synthetic biology</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">）到化学工程再到人工智能（</span><a href="https://phys.org/tags/artificial+intelligence/" style="text-decoration: underline;
