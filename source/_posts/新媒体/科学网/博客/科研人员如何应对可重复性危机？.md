padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 宋体; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">可重复性危机出现的原因是什么？</span></strong><br></p><p></p><p style="line-height: 2em; margin-bottom: 20px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; letter-spacing: 1px; visibility: visible;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体; visibility: visible;">可重复性（</span>Reproducibility<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体; visibility: visible;">）是指研究人员使用先前发表的研究中相同的材料和方法而获得与先前同样的研究结果的可能性。可重复性是一个研究结论可信的必要条件。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em; margin-bottom: 20px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px; visibility: visible;">研究不可重复的原因可能有很多，比如科研人员出于获取资金赞助、终生职位的压力，有倾向性地挑选支持实验假设的数据发表。</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em; margin-bottom: 20px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; letter-spacing: 1px; visibility: visible;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">《</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #333333; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">Natur</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #333333; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">e</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #333333; font-family: mp-quote, -apple-system-font, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">》</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体; visibility: visible;">的问卷调查中，</span>60%<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体; visibility: visible;">以上的受访者也表示，出版压力和选择性报告是常见的原因；超过一半的人还指出存在先前的实验设计中设置的重复太少或统计功效过低的可能；也有小部分人认为存在其他障碍导致实验难以重复，如试剂的可变性等。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em; margin-bottom: 20px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 18px; letter-spacing: 1px; visibility: visible;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; visibility: visible;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 宋体; visibility: visible; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">学术界应该如何应对可重复性危机？</span></strong></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em; margin-bottom: 20px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; letter-spacing: 1px; visibility: visible;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; visibility: visible;">1<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体; visibility: visible;">、科研学者的努力</span></strong></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em; margin-bottom: 20px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">为了避免出现换个试验台、换个人，实验就做不出来的情况，研究人员在项目初期就应该进行实验可重复性规划，到临近发表时再试图编辑代码与记录实验细节，其工作量可能会令人望而生畏。</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em; margin-bottom: 20px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">本文推荐以下几种方式供作者参考：</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em; margin-bottom: 20px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; letter-spacing: 1px;">1<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体;">）研究人员可以尽早制定一个<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">数据管理计划</strong>（</span>Data Management Plan<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体;">，</span>DMP<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体;">）</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体;">，决定项目过程中在哪里存储、如何存储、如何跟踪和修改数据，这些都将对研究的可重复性产生重要影响</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体;">。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em; margin-bottom: 20px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; letter-spacing: 1px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体;">同时，因为书面的</span>DMP<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体;">可能是资助的条件之一，这样做还会帮助研究者获取资助。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em; margin-bottom: 20px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; letter-spacing: 1px;">2<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体;">）研究人员还可以将研究设计写成<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">注册报告</strong>（</span>Registered Report<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体;">）</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体;">并发表。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em; margin-bottom: 20px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">这样做的好处是，不仅能让研究设计接受同行评议收集有价值的反馈意见，而且只要试验时完全依照预先提交的报告中所描述的方法，不论结果是否与延期一致，文章都会在二轮同行评议后发表。</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em; margin-bottom: 20px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">虽然发表注册报告的做法比较新颖，但发表与预期结果不一致的实验也具有一定的科研价值，因此注册报告的形式已出现于人文学科、医学研究和社会科学等多个领域。</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em; margin-bottom: 20px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; letter-spacing: 1px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">2<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体;">、期刊的举措</span></strong></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em; margin-bottom: 20px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; letter-spacing: 1px;">1<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体;">）<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">为每篇论文创建追踪链接</strong>。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em; margin-bottom: 20px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">对已发表的结果进行更新，并报告试图复制该论文中关键发现的其他研究人员的努力。</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em; margin-bottom: 20px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; letter-spacing: 1px;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">2<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体;">）</span></strong><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">要求作者提供充分的实验细节</strong>。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em; margin-bottom: 20px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">实验假设可能在某些情况下不成立，研究人员因此锲而不舍地寻找能够使自己提出的假设成立的适当条件，即使这些条件的适用范围极窄。</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em; margin-bottom: 20px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">因此，期刊应在投稿指南中要求作者提供充分的实验细节，比如详细描述每次实验使用的试剂和动物来源等，这有助于未来研究人员在重复实验时能保持相同的实验条件从而提高研究的可重复性，为重现性研究节省大量的时间和资金。</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em; margin-bottom: 20px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">关于可重复性的讨论仍在进行当中。比如，判断实验重复成功的标准是什么？一篇论文中通常会测试多个假设，一个假设的结果不一致就认为实验无法重复吗？我们需要多少次实验才能得出论文不可重复的结论？</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;
