<p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 8px; margin-bottom: 8px; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; background: white;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 16px;">校园内的云南黄素馨开花了，成串金黄色的花朵<span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体; background: white;">映衬在碧绿叶丛中</span>，如飞瀑直下，惹人喜爱。早晨经过学生宿舍区，听见不少同学都说这迎春花真是很漂亮，这是搞混了迎春花和云南黄素馨这两种常见植物，此外探春也容易被误认为是迎春花。的确迎春花和云南黄素馨关系很近，同属于木犀科素馨属植物，而且花均为黄色，故易于弄混。</span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 8px; margin-bottom: 8px; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; background: white; text-align: center;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 16px;"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202203/14/113020kgagyd7v0bn9bb7y.jpg" title alt="IMG_20220314_092919.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 8px; margin-bottom: 8px; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; background: white;"><span style="font-size: 16px;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-family: "times new roman";">为什么大家第一想到的是迎春花？这与众多描写迎春花的诗词和其开花较早具有一定的关系。迎春花</span><span style="font-family: "times new roman";">因在百花之中开花最早，花后即迎来百花齐放的春天而得名。白居易的《玩迎春花赠杨郎中》“金英翠萼带春寒，黄色花中有几般？凭君语向游人道，莫作蔓青花眼看”、宋朝诗人董嗣在《迎春花》“杲破寒乘暖迓东皇，簇定刚条烂熳黄；野艳飘摇金誉嫩，露丛勾引蜜蜂狂”、以及韩琦在《迎春花》“覆阑纤弱绿条长，带雪冲寒折嫩黄；迎得春来非自足，百花千卉共芬芳”等都写出了迎春花的特点。</span></span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 8px; margin-bottom: 8px; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; background: white; text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 16px;"><span style="font-family: "times new roman";"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202203/14/113132fotofouu2dqu8qtc.jpg" title alt="IMG_20190311_162827.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></span></span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 8px; margin-bottom: 8px; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; background: white;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 16px;">黄素馨也叫野迎春，和迎春花、探春都同属于木犀科素馨属，由于其花的颜色和性状和迎春花有类似之处，故很多朋友常把它们认混。</span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 8px; margin-bottom: 8px; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; background: white;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 16px;">首先看迎春和云南黄素馨，二者叶均为对生，三出复叶或小枝基部具单叶，二者小叶性状基本类似，但二者之间区别明显；从开花时间看：迎春在早春（二月份）开花，意味着迎着春天开放；而云南黄馨开花时间稍微，一般在3月中旬开花；从开花特性看：迎春花是“花先叶开放”，也就是说在叶还没有长出前，花就开放了；而云南黄素馨为常绿灌木，其花盛开时满枝绿叶；从花形态来看：迎春花和云南黄素馨花均为漏斗状，但迎春花花筒稍长，可达到1.5厘米，而云南黄素馨花筒一般1厘米左右；迎春花一般花冠为1轮，其裂片5-6枚，花冠直径一般为2-2.5厘米；而云南黄素馨有时为重瓣花，裂片6-8枚，在裂片基部具有明显深棕色条纹，花冠直径也比迎春花大，可达到2-4.5厘米。</span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 8px; margin-bottom: 8px; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; background: white; text-align: center;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 16px;"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202203/14/113633mzhi35zjnl5fsnq5.jpg" title alt="图片1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 8px; margin-bottom: 8px; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; background: white;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word; font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 16px;">再来看探春：探春的小枝也是四棱状，但其叶是互生，复叶上小叶常为为3或5枚；探春花冠也为黄色，近漏斗状；但和迎春花和云南黄素馨相比，先叶后花，花期更晚，一般为5-9月。同时，探春花序为聚伞花序或伞状聚伞花序，有花3-25朵，其花冠管长为0.9-1.5厘米，但其花冠直径和云南黄素馨相比也小很多。</span></p><p style="overflow-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 8px; margin-bottom: 8px; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; background: white; text-align: center;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word;
