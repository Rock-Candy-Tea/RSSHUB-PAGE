style="line-height: 24px; font-family: 宋体;">羊肚菌是羊肚菌属真菌的总称，此类真菌因为头部凹凸不平，形似羊肚而得名，是世界最著名的四大食用菌之一。在我国，羊肚菌又被称为羊肚菜（河北）、羊肚蘑（辽宁）、麻子菌（陕西）、狼肚（甘肃）、羊肚子（山西）、蜂窝蘑（山东）、包谷菌（四川）等。</span></p><p><br></p><p white;">；顶端无孔口；表面不规则，由垂脊和横脊隔成众多凹坑，凹坑深浅及形状各异；颜色有黄色、淡黄褐色、褐色、棕褐色、灰褐色、黑褐色等。菌盖菌肉多为白色或淡黄色，厚约</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; color: #121212; background: background: white;">3~15cm</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; color: #121212; background: white;">，宽</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; color: #121212; background: white;">1.5~5cm</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; color: #121212; background: white;">，菌柄肉质厚</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; color: #121212; background: white;">1~2mm</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; color: #121212; background: white;">。羊肚菌的子囊一般含</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; color: #121212; background: white;">8</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; color: #121212; background: white;">个子囊孢子，单行排列，孢子表面光滑或带有纹饰，椭圆形、卵圆形、长椭圆形等。</span><br></p><p style="text-indent:32px;line-height:150%;text-autospace:none"><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;color:#121212;background:white">不同种的羊肚菌往往长得很像，难于区分。科学家们不得不利用核酸检测方法来识别物种。羊肚菌广泛分布于北半球温带至亚热带地区，中国科学院昆明植物研究所研究发现全球有约</span><span Roman','serif';color:#121212;background:white">40余</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;color:#121212;background:white">种，其中可栽培的有9种，分别是：<span style="font-family: 宋体;">妹羊肚菌</span><em><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">M. sextelata</span></em><span style="font-family: 宋体;">（</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Mel-6</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">），七妹羊肚菌</span><em><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">M. eximia</span></em><span style="font-family: 宋体;">（</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Mel-7</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">），九妹羊肚菌</span><em><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">M. exuberans</span></em><span style="font-family: 宋体;">（</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Mel-9</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">）、梯棱羊肚菌</span><em><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">M. importuna</span></em><span style="font-family: 宋体;">（</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Mel-10</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">）、欧氏羊肚菌</span><em><span style="font-family: 宋体;">和绒毛羊肚菌</span><em><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">M. tomentosa</span></em><span style="font-family: 宋体;">、</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Mel-13</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">、</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Mel-21</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">等</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">9</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">个物种，其中六妹羊肚菌、七妹羊肚菌和梯棱羊肚菌已经在我国初步实现了商业化栽培（杜习慧</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;"> 2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">，赵琪等</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;"> 2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">）</span>。</span></p><p style="text-indent:32px;line-height:150%;text-autospace:none"><strong><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;color:#121212;background:white">羊肚菌是自然界生物多样性的一员，包括：黑色系列和黄色系列，黑色羊肚菌一般生长在1600米以上高山一支可以分布到4000多米的原始林区；黄色羊肚菌一般分布在0-1000米的人为干涉稍大的生态系统。</span></strong><span style="color: #323232; text-indent: 2em; font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">羊肚菌生长需要事宜的生态环境，包括：</span><span style="color: #323232; text-indent: 2em; font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">植被、</span><span style="color: #323232; text-indent: 2em; font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">温度、湿度、降雨量和土壤等。</span></p><p style="text-indent:32px;line-height:150%;text-autospace:none"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong><span style="color: #323232; text-indent: 2em; font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">羊肚菌菌丝的孕育和生长都在土壤中进行，且原基和幼菇的形成、发育也需土壤持续提高营养</span></strong><span style="color: #323232; text-indent: 2em; font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">。</span></span><span style="color: #323232; text-indent: 2em; font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">因此，<span style="text-indent: 2em; font-family: 宋体, SimSun; color: #C00000; font-size:
