<p style="margin: 0pt; text-indent: 21pt;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 10.5pt; mso-spacerun: "yes"; mso-ascii-font-family: "Times New Roman"; mso-hansi-font-family: "Times New Roman"; mso-bidi-font-family: "Times New Roman"; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt;"><span style="font-family: 宋体;">十几年前的一天，一名由我校考到外校读研的学生回来找同学玩儿，见到我时聊了几句。她的导师是一位在国外任职的教授，在她读研的单位兼职带学生。她说，导师明确指出，论文在修改</span><span style="font-family: times new roman; font-size: 10.5pt; mso-spacerun: "yes"; mso-ascii-font-family: "Times New Roman"; mso-hansi-font-family: "Times New Roman"; mso-bidi-font-family: "Times New Roman"; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt;">40</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">遍之前，不要拿给他看。</span></span></p><p style="margin: 0pt; text-indent: 21pt;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 10.5pt; mso-spacerun: "yes"; mso-ascii-font-family: "Times New Roman"; mso-hansi-font-family: "Times New Roman"; mso-bidi-font-family: "Times New Roman"; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt;"><span style="font-family: 宋体;"><br></span></span></p><p style="margin: 0pt; text-indent: 21pt;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 10.5pt; mso-spacerun: "yes"; mso-ascii-font-family: "Times New Roman"; mso-hansi-font-family: "Times New Roman"; mso-bidi-font-family: "Times New Roman"; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt;"><span style="font-family: 宋体;">我第一次听说导师可以这样要求学生，当时就震惊了！我批阅自己开门弟子的论文时，几乎每个句子包括标点符号都进行了修改，在总结性评语中用了</span>“不堪卒读！”原来，是我的指导方法有误，我应该让学生把论文修改到他<span style="font-family: 宋体;">能达到的最高水平，再交给我批阅指导。</span></span></p><p style="margin: 0pt; text-indent: 21pt;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 10.5pt; mso-spacerun: "yes"; mso-ascii-font-family: "Times New Roman"; mso-hansi-font-family: "Times New Roman"; mso-bidi-font-family: "Times New Roman"; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt;"><span style="font-family: 宋体;"><br></span></span></p><p style="margin: 0pt; text-indent: 21pt;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 10.5pt; mso-spacerun: "yes"; mso-ascii-font-family: "Times New Roman"; mso-hansi-font-family: "Times New Roman"; mso-bidi-font-family: "Times New Roman"; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt;"><span style="font-family: 宋体;">我马上召集自己的研究生开会，传达这个精神。当然，我推测他们做不到修改</span><span style="font-family: times new roman; font-size: 10.5pt; mso-spacerun: "yes"; mso-ascii-font-family: "Times New Roman"; mso-hansi-font-family: "Times New Roman"; mso-bidi-font-family: "Times New Roman"; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt;">40</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">遍的程度，对</span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">40</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">遍的标准折半成</span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">20</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">遍；想想他们还是做不到，再折半成</span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">10</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">遍；感觉依然有难度，最后又折半为</span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">5</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">遍。同时，增加一条，必须请两位同门阅读——阅读的学生得签字。总之，学生要切实增强责任意识，提交给我的文字材料，必须是文通字顺的。</span></span></p><p style="margin: 0pt; text-indent: 21pt;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 10.5pt; mso-spacerun: "yes"; mso-ascii-font-family: "Times New Roman"; mso-hansi-font-family: "Times New Roman"; mso-bidi-font-family: "Times New Roman"; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt;"><span style="font-family: 宋体;"><br></span></span></p><p style="margin: 0pt; text-indent: 21pt;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 10.5pt; mso-spacerun: "yes"; mso-ascii-font-family: "Times New Roman"; mso-hansi-font-family: "Times New Roman"; mso-bidi-font-family: "Times New Roman"; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt;"><span style="font-family: 宋体;">我随后让学生专题学习《新华字典》附录中</span>“常用标点符号用法简表”和字典正文中助词“的”、“地”、“得”<span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">3</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">个字的用法。我告诫学生：遇到不懂的英语单词要查词典，遇到不懂的汉字也要查字典，不要轻易相信网上的材料。</span></span></p><p style="margin: 0pt; text-indent: 21pt;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 10.5pt; mso-spacerun: "yes"; mso-ascii-font-family: "Times New Roman"; mso-hansi-font-family: "Times New Roman"; mso-bidi-font-family: "Times New Roman"; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt;"><span style="font-family: 宋体;"><br></span></span></p><p style="margin: 0pt; text-indent: 21pt;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 10.5pt; mso-spacerun: "yes"; mso-ascii-font-family: "Times New Roman"; mso-hansi-font-family: "Times New Roman"; mso-bidi-font-family: "Times New Roman"; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt;"><span style="font-family: 宋体;">从此后，我的学生都得按照这样的要求去做，一级又一级，一届又一届。他们在文字表达方面都有长足进步，几个学生毕业后向我反馈说，读研期间的严格训练，使他们养成了认真负责的习惯，他们部门的文字材料，要请他们做最后的把关。</span></span></p><p style="margin: 0pt; text-indent: 21pt;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 10.5pt; mso-spacerun: "yes"; mso-ascii-font-family: "Times New Roman"; mso-hansi-font-family: "Times New Roman"; mso-bidi-font-family: "Times New Roman"; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt;"><span style="font-family: 宋体;"><br></span></span></p><p style="margin: 0pt; text-indent: 21pt;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 10.5pt; mso-spacerun: "yes"; mso-ascii-font-family: "Times New Roman"; mso-hansi-font-family: "Times New Roman"; mso-bidi-font-family: "Times New Roman"; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt;"><span style="font-family: 宋体;">我有些懊悔的是，当初自己心太软，没有采用修改</span><span style="font-family: times new roman; font-size: 10.5pt; mso-spacerun: "yes"; mso-ascii-font-family: "Times New Roman"; mso-hansi-font-family: "Times New Roman"; mso-bidi-font-family: "Times New Roman"; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt;">40</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">遍的标准。</span></span></p>                    <br><br>
