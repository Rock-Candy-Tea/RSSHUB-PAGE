style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:仿宋">针对上述挑战，</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">EFL</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height: 150%;font-family:仿宋">微凝胶墨水用以在不同种类的材料上直接打印制造的液态金属基柔性电子设备。相关工作</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">“</span><strong><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">Liquid Metal Microgels for Three-Dimensional Printing of Smart Electronic Clothes</span></strong><strong><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">”</span></strong><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:仿宋">，在液态金属微颗粒表面形成一层海藻酸镓外壳，机械破碎液态金属会加速这一反应过程，促进</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">Ga<sup>3+</sup></span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:仿宋">进入溶液中与海藻酸钠反应。很有趣的，由于微凝胶壳的包裹，这种液态金属微凝胶墨水可打印性能非常好，从玻璃、</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">PET</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height: 150%;font-family:仿宋">、织物到</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">PDMS</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:仿宋">都可以作为打印基底，极大地拓展了液态金属的应用场景。</span></p><p
