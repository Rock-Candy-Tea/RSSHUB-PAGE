font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">Bai-Qing Lv</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">音译）说：</span>“<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">在</span>EuTe<sub>4</sub><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">中，我们发现滞后的温度范围非常宽，超过</span>400 K<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">。实际数字可能要大得多，因为这个值受到当前实验技术能力的限制。这一发现立即引起了我们的注意，我们对</span>EuTe<sub>4</sub><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">的实验和理论表征对晶体中可能发生的滞后转变类型的传统观念提出了挑战。</span>”</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">滞后行为的一种表现形式是材料的电阻。通过冷却或加热</span>EuTe<sub>4</sub><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">晶体，研究人员能够将它们的电阻率改变几个数量级。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">努赫·格迪克实验室的博士研究生阿尔弗雷德·宗（</span><a href="https://journals.aps.org/search/field/author/Alfred%20Zong" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">Alfred Zong</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">音译）解释说：</span>“<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">在给定温度下，例如在室温下，电阻率的值取决于晶体过去是更冷还是更热。这一观察向我们表明，材料的电学特性以某种方式对其热历史有记忆，并且在微观上，材料的特性可以保留过去不同温度的特性。这种</span>“<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">热记忆</span>”<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">可以用作永久温度记录器。例如，通过在室温下测量</span>EuTe<sub>4</sub><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">的电阻，我们可以立即知道材料过去经历过的最冷或最热温度是多少。</span>”</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><strong><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">发现的怪事（</span></strong><strong><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;"></span></strong><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "arial black", "avant garde";"><strong><a href="https://news.mit.edu/2022/scientists-discover-mysterious-transition-electronic-crystal-0207?installer" target="_blank">Oddities found</a></strong></span><strong><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）</span></strong></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">研究人员还发现了滞后现象中的几个奇怪之处。例如，与晶体中的其他相变不同，他们没有观察到在大温度范围内电子或晶格结构的任何改变。</span><a href="https://journals.aps.org/search/field/author/BQ%20Lv" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">吕伯庆</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">补充道：</span>“<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">没有微观变化对我们来说看起来真的很奇怪。更神秘的是，与其他敏感地依赖于冷却或升温速率的滞后转变不同，</span>EuTe<sub>4</sub><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">的滞后回线（</span>hysteresis loop<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）似乎不受这个因素的影响。</span>”</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">研究人员的一条线索是电子在</span>EuTe<sub>4</sub><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">中的排列方式。</span></span><a href="https://journals.aps.org/search/field/author/Alfred%20Zong" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">阿尔弗雷德·宗</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">解释说：</span>“<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">在室温下，</span>EuTe<sub>4</sub><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">晶体中的电子自发凝聚成低密度和高密度区域，在原始周期晶格之上形成二次电子晶体。我们认为与巨大磁滞回线相关的奇怪现象可能与这种次级电子晶体有关，这种化合物的不同层在建立长程周期性的同时表现出无序运动。</span>”  </span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><a href="https://journals.aps.org/search/field/author/BQ%20Lv" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">吕伯庆</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">说：</span>“EuTe<sub>4</sub><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">的分层性质对于解释滞后现象至关重要。不同层中的次级晶体之间的弱相互作用使它们能够相对移动，从而在滞后回线中产生许多亚稳态配置（</span></span><a href="https://news.mit.edu/2022/scientists-discover-mysterious-transition-electronic-crystal-0207?installer" style="text-decoration: underline; font-family: 宋体;">）。</span>”</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">下一步是设计方法，除了改变温度，在</span>EuTe<sub>4</sub><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">中诱导这些亚稳态。这将使科学家能够以技术上有用的方式操纵其电气特性。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">努赫·格迪克说：</span>“<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">我们可以产生短于</span>10<sup>-12</sup><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">秒的强激光脉冲。下一个目标是在单次闪光后诱使</span> EuTe<sub>4</sub><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">进入不同的电阻状态，使其成为可用于例如计算设备的超快电子开关。</span>”</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-size: 18px; font-family: "arial black", "avant garde";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "arial black", "avant garde";">Abstract</span></a></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">Hysteresis underlies a large number of phase transitions in solids, giving rise to exotic metastable states that are otherwise inaccessible. Here, we report an unconventional hysteretic transition in a quasi-2D material, EuTe<sub>4</sub>. By combining transport, photoemission, diffraction, and x-ray absorption measurements, we observe that the hysteresis loop has a temperature width of more than 400 K, setting a record among crystalline solids. The transition has an origin distinct from known mechanisms, lying entirely within the incommensurate charge density wave (CDW) phase of EuTe4 with no change in the CDW modulation periodicity. We interpret the hysteresis as an unusual switching of the relative CDW phases in different layers, a phenomenon unique to quasi-2D compounds that is not present in either purely 2D or strongly coupled 3D systems. Our findings challenge the established theories on metastable states in density wave systems, pushing the boundary of understanding hysteretic transitions in a broken-symmetry state.</span></p>                    <br><br>
