<section><section><p style="text-indent: 2em; margin-top: 5px; margin-bottom: 10px; line-height: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; text-indent: 2em; margin-top: 5px; margin-bottom: 10px; line-height: 2em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong style="font-size: 14px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: DengXian; color: #0070C0;">而在科学研究的过程中，更多需要的则是探索性实验，尽管可以预先提出有一定依据的假设或猜想，但没有人能保证到底会发生什么，究竟会取得怎样的实验结果。</span></strong></p><p style="padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; text-indent: 2em; margin-top: 5px; margin-bottom: 10px; line-height: 2em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-family: DengXian; margin-top: 5px; margin-bottom: 10px; line-height: 2em;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: DengXian; font-size: 14px; box-sizing: border-box !important; margin-top: 5px; margin-bottom: 10px; line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-family: DengXian; font-size: 14px;">同样道理，</span><strong style="font-family: DengXian; font-size: 14px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 14px;"><strong><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 1px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">2. 对于不同个体的临床试验结果，无论阳性还是阴性，都需要如实报道</span></strong></span></p><p style="padding: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: DengXian; font-size: 14px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">在笔者前些年还是一名某学术期刊编辑时，曾收到一位作者的来稿，据其实验结果显示，一项已被多篇文献报道在小鼠等实验动物体内具有一定效果的治疗方案，在其实验过程中历经足够次数的重复之后，依然没有像已有文献报道那样显示出任何对于小鼠的治疗效果。</span></p><p style="padding: 0px; text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 5px; margin-bottom: 10px; line-height: 2em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); letter-spacing: 0.544px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; text-indent: 2em; margin-top: 5px; margin-bottom: 10px; line-height: 2em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; text-indent: 2em; margin-top: 5px; margin-bottom: 10px; line-height: 2em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-size: 14px; font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">“</span>实验结果<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-size: 14px; font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">”</span>部分；有些起补充说明作用的数据和结果，可以放在<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-size: 14px; font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">“</span>补充材料<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-size: 14px; font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">”</span>部分；而在论文的<span style="margin: 0px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #0070C0; font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">”</span>不够客观中肯。</span></strong><span style="font-size: 14px; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: DengXian; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">有时过于完美的数据和结果，也可能增加被质疑甚至被拒稿的风险。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent: 0em; margin-top: 5px;
