<p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">在心理学研究中，当把数量较多的指标合成数量较少的指标后，就应当使用合成的指标。在概念水平上，合成的指标比原来的指标要高。常见的合成技术是主成分分析和因素分析，得到的更高水平的指标分别称为主成分和因素。研究者把主成分分析当成因素分析的一种形式，将主成分简称为成分，从而，成分与因素成了可以替换使用的术语。此外，指标、指数、成分也可被视为同义词。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">在前文中，研究者（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Stanton, Sinar, Balzer, & Smith, 2002</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）通过主成分分析，把考察题项品质的</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">15</span><span style="font-family:宋体">个指数，合成为</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">4</span><span style="font-family:宋体">个成分，也就是</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">4</span><span style="font-family:宋体">个指数，分别为分布性指数、内部指数、外部指数、判断性指数。此后，考察缩减量表的指标时，即可采用这</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">4</span><span style="font-family:宋体">个指数，而不必直接使用原来的</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">15</span><span style="font-family:宋体">个指数。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">现在的问题是，这</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">4</span><span style="font-family:宋体">个指数对于缩减量表有何作用呢？研究者（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Stanton, Sinar, Balzer, & Smith, 2002</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）把这</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">4</span><span style="font-family:宋体">个指数作为预测缩减量表结果的预测因子，亦即变量，先计算这</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">4</span><span style="font-family:宋体">个变量的得分。这些得分能够反映每个题项在分布性、内部、外部、判断性品质上的位置。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">接着，评估这</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">4</span><span style="font-family:宋体">个变量预测题项保留在简版量表中的情况。把</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">72</span><span style="font-family:宋体">个题项的“工作描述指数”（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Smith, Kendall, & Hulin, 1969</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）缩减为</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">25</span><span style="font-family:宋体">个题项的简版量表，至少有</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">3</span><span style="font-family:宋体">种方法：第一种是组合分析，第二种是逐步回归，第三种是专业判断。在例文中，研究者希望在题项水平进行分析，就把这</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">3</span><span style="font-family:宋体">种方法作为题项水平的标准（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Stanton, Sinar, Balzer, & Smith, 2002</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）。“工作描述指数”包含工作内容、工资待遇、晋升机会、同事关系、主管情况等</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">5</span><span style="font-family:宋体">个层面，表</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">1</span><span style="font-family:宋体">是工资待遇层面的预测因子与题项保留标准。</span></span></p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://bbs.sciencenet.cn/home.php?mod=attachment&filename=image.png&id=874657" title alt="image.png" width="580" height="374" style="width: 580px; height: 374px;" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"> </span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">组合分析方法，可由一种软件来执行（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Stanton, 2000</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）。对于要缩减的量表，设定所需的长度，这个软件能够生成题项所有可能组合与完整量表之间相关的分布。也就是说，这种程序产生的题项组合，能够解释原初量表的变异性。例文研究者要求缩减的各个层面均有</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">5</span><span style="font-family:宋体">个题项，运行软件，计算各个题项在简版量表中出现在前</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">5%</span><span style="font-family:宋体">的次数。如果一个题项频繁地出现在前</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">5%</span><span style="font-family:宋体">，那么，它就能提供独特而有用的方差，有助于提高简版量表与完整量表之间的相关。研究者把题项出现在前</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">5%</span><span style="font-family:宋体">的百分比，作为线性回归分析中的优度标准（见表</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">1</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）。结果表明，外部题项品质得分（</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px">β</span><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: "Times New Roman";">=.23, </span><em><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: "Times New Roman";">p</span></em><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman"><.05</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）、内部题项品质得分（</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px">β</span><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: "Times New Roman";">=.47, </span><em><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: "Times New Roman";">p</span></em><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman"><.001</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）均能预测这个保留题项的标准，总的</span></span><em><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: "Times New Roman";">R</span></em><span style="font-size: 10px;"><sup><span style="vertical-align: super; font-family: "Times New Roman";">2</span></sup></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">=.34</span><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><em><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: "Times New Roman";">F</span></em><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">(4,67)=8.66</span><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><em><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: "Times New Roman";">p</span></em><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman"><.001</span><span style="font-family:宋体">；分布性、判断性题项品质得分均无显著的</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px">β</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">值，即不能预测题项是否出现在题项组合的前</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">5%</span><span style="font-family:宋体">。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 宋体;">逐步回归方法，在这里被当成一种纯粹的统计方法，可以对题项进入简版量表的优度进行排序。分析时，从与完整量表得分相关最高的一个题项开始，接着找到预测完整量表得分时对</span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px">Δ</span><em><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: "Times New Roman";">R</span></em><span style="font-size: 10px;"><sup><span style="vertical-align: super; font-family: "Times New Roman";">2</span></sup></span><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 宋体;">有最大增量的题项。继续这个过程，直到所有题项都进入回归方程。</span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">研究者（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Stanton, Sinar, Balzer, & Smith, 2002</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）把进入逐步回归的顺序作为显示题项优度的等级顺序（见表</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">1</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）。由于等级顺序不是正态的，不适合作为线性回归的因变量，研究者计算了题项品质因素得分与进入回归的等级顺序之间的</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Kendall </span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px">τ-</span><em><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">b</span></span></em><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">相关。结果表明，外部题项品质因素得分（</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px">τ</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">=-.19, </span></span><em><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">p</span></span></em><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman"><.05</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）、判断性题项品质因素得分（</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px">τ</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">=-.17, </span></span><em><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">p</span></span></em><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman"><.05</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）能够显著预测题项的等级顺序，分布性题项品质因素得分与内部题项因素得分均与题项等级不相关。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">专业判断方法，就是研究者把统计指数与专业判断结合起来进行分析的一种方法。在例文中，研究者采用</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Stanton</span><span style="font-family:宋体">等（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">2001</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）的研究结果来编码创建简版量表（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">AJDI</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）时从原量表中保留或删除题项的情况。在做出题项保留决定时，专业判断可以发挥很大作用。例如，在选择题项时，研究者的作用可以超过一些品质统计指数，以使层面内正向与负向措辞的题项达到平衡，以及避免不同层面题项内容的冗余。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">由于保留或排除一个题项的结果是二分的，研究者采用逻辑回归进行分析。结果表明，内部品质因素得分（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">B=1.46, </span></span><em><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: "Times New Roman";">p</span></em><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman"><.01</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）、外部<span style="font-family: 宋体; text-indent: 32px;">品质因素</span>得分（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">B=1.62, </span></span><em><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: "Times New Roman";">p</span></em><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman"><.01</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）、判断性<span style="font-family: 宋体; text-indent: 32px;">品质因素</span>（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">B=1.37, </span></span><em><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: "Times New Roman";">p</span></em><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman"><.01</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）都是显著的预测因子，</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px">χ</span><span style="font-size: 10px;"><sup><span style="vertical-align: super; font-family: "Times New Roman";">2</span></sup></span><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: "Times New Roman";">(4,</span><em><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: "Times New Roman";">n</span></em><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: "Times New Roman";">=75)=41.2, </span><em><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: "Times New Roman";">p</span></em><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman"><.001</span><span style="font-family:宋体">，分布性因素得分不能显著地预测题项保留。总的来说，内部、外部、判断性因素得分能正确预测</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">AJDI</span><span style="font-family:宋体">中</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">87%</span><span style="font-family:宋体">排除的题项和</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">68%</span><span style="font-family:宋体">保留的题项。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">总之，研究者用概括题项品质不同方面的因素得分，作为题项是否应当保留在简版量表中的预测因子。结果提示，题项的外部品质，即，外部效度关系，在缩减量表时有重要作用，判断性品质、内部品质也有明显作用。这也说明，缩减量表时，研究者的依据应当是有层次的。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span
