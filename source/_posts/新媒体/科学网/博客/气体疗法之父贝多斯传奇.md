style="font-family:Calibri">200</span><span style="font-family:宋体">年前英国科学家曾经吸入氢气，目的是治病</span></span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:32px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span 200%;color: #0000FF;font-size: 16px">是最早研究氢气人体效应的科学家</span></strong><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">。不过具有操作者是他的学生戴维。</span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:32px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">英国医生贝多斯非常传奇，他医学毕业后先在牛津大学做了几天化学讲师，不太成功，然后致力于吸气治疗结核，之前曾经把这个想法给达尔文的爷爷，著名博物学家伊拉斯谟斯</span><span style="font-family:宋体">·</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;color:#0000FF;font-size:16px">法拉第</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">，这个人后来发明了电动机和发电机，成了电学之父。</span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:32px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><img 651px;" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:32px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">一、贝多斯医生研究气体疗法的缘由</span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:32px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;color:#0000FF;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Calibri">1799</span><span style="font-family:宋体">年</span></span><span style="margin-left:0;text-indent:32px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Calibri">1788)</span><span style="font-family:宋体">提出人类疾病就是失去平衡，要么是过度兴奋，要么是过度抑制。</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">Brown</span><span Brown</span><span style="font-family:宋体">的理论之间提供了重要的联系，在这个理论中，氧气充当了兴奋因子。这种联系暗示了化学和医学之间尚未开发的潜力，这也成为了贝多斯的热情所在。</span></span></p><p style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">贝多斯</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">对化学的</span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">热情</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">也很快下降</span><span
