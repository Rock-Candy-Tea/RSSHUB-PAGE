font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">随着南极洲接近秋季，该地区的气象站周五（</span>2022<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">年</span>3<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">月</span>18<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">日）打破了记录。根据极端天气记录追踪者马克西米利亚诺·赫雷拉（</span>Maximiliano Herrera<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）的推特消息，高</span>2<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">英里</span>(3234 m)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">的肯考迪娅站（</span></span><a href="https://phys.org/news/2022-03-hot-poles-antarctica-arctic-degrees.html" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">Concordia station</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）温度为</span>10<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">度（</span>-12.2<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">℃），比平均温度高</span>70<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">度（</span>40 <span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">℃），而更高的沃斯托克站（</span></span><a href="https://phys.org/news/2022-03-hot-poles-antarctica-arctic-degrees.html" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">Vostok station</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）温度为</span>0<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">度（</span>-17.7<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">℃）以上，比其历史记录高出</span>27<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">度（</span>15<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">℃）。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">沿海的特拉·诺瓦基地（</span>Terra Nova Base<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）温度远高于冰点，为</span>44.6<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">度（</span>7 <span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">℃）。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">使美国科罗拉多州博尔德市美国国家冰雪数据中心（</span><a href="https://phys.org/news/2022-03-hot-poles-antarctica-arctic-degrees.html" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">National Snow and Ice Data Center in Boulder, Colorado, USA</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）的官员大吃一惊</span>,<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">因为他们关注北极温度</span>,<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">它比平均温度高</span>50<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">度</span>,<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">在北极（</span>North Pole<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）说。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">沃尔特·迈耶周五（</span>2022<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">年</span>3<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">月</span>18<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">日）晚上告诉相关媒体记者说：“它们是相反的季节。你不会看到南北</span>(<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">两极</span>)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">同时融化。这绝对是一件不寻常的事。”沃尔特·迈耶补充道</span>:<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">“这非常令人震惊！”</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">最近刚从南极考察回来的美国科罗拉多大学（</span>University of Colorado<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）的冰科学家泰德·斯坎博斯（</span>Ted Scambos<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）说：“哇！我在南极从未见过这样的事情。”</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">美国威斯康辛大学（</span>University of Wisconsin<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）气象学家马修·拉扎拉（</span>Matthew Lazzara<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）说</span>:<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">“当你看到这种事情发生时，这不是一个好迹象。”</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">马修·拉扎拉监测了东南极洲圆顶</span>C-ii<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">（</span>East Antarctica's Dome C-ii<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）区的温度，周五记录了</span>14 <span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">度</span>(-10<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">℃</span>)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">，而正常情况下是</span>-45 <span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">度</span>(-43 <span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">℃</span>):<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">“这种温度应该在</span>1<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">月份出现，而不是</span>3<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">月份。那里的一月是夏天。这是戏剧性的。</span>”</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">马修·拉扎拉和沃尔特·迈耶都表示，在南极洲发生的事情可能只是一个随机的天气事件，而不是气候变化的迹象。</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">但如果这种情况再次或反复发生，而且是全球变暖的一部分，那么就可能需要担心，他们说。也有媒体对南极暖流有报道。</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">根据美国缅因大学（</span>University of Maine<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）的气候再分析仪</span>(Climate Reanalyzer)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">基于美国国家海洋大气管理局</span>(U.S. National Oceanic Atmospheric Administration)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">的天气模型，周五整个南极大陆的温度比</span>1979<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">年至</span>2000<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">年之间的基准温度高了约</span>8.6<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">度</span>(4.8 <span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">℃</span>)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">。沃尔特·迈耶说，在已经变暖的平均气温上上升</span>8<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">度是不寻常的，就像整个美国比正常温度水平高了</span>8<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">度一样。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">与此同时，周五的北极整体气温比</span>1979<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">年至</span>2000<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">年的平均气温高</span>6<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">度</span>(3.3 <span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">℃</span>)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">相比之下，世界整体气温仅比</span>1979<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">据冰雪数据中心报道，南极洲在</span>1979<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">年创下了夏季海冰面积最低的记录，</span>2<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">月底的海冰面积缩小至</span>74.1<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">万平方英里</span>(<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">约</span>190<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">万平方公里</span>)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">2021<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">年</span>12<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">月</span>14<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">日有报道称，北极的变暖速度是全球其它地区的两到三倍</span>(</span><a href="https://phys.org/news/2021-12-highs-beaver-arctic.html" style="text-decoration: underline;
