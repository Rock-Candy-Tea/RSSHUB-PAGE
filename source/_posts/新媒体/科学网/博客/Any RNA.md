<p style="text-align:center"><strong><span style="font-size:19px">Any RNA</span></strong></p><p><span style="font-size:16px;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'"> </span></p><p><span style="font-size:16px;font-family:宋体">物理学中，宇宙中的所有粒子都能被分为两大类：费米子（</span><span style="font-size:16px;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">fermions</span><span style="font-size:16px;font-family:宋体">）和玻色子（</span><span style="font-size:16px;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">bosons</span><span style="font-size:16px;font-family:宋体">）。其中，电子是典型的费米子，它们无法处于同一状态；而光子是典型的玻色子，一样的光子能够聚集在一起。而在</span><span style="font-size:16px;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">2020</span><span style="font-size:16px;font-family:宋体">年，物理学家发现了第三类粒子：命名为任意子（</span><span style="font-size:16px;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">anyons</span><span style="font-size:16px;font-family: 宋体">）。任意子的特点是既不像费米子又不像玻色子，它处于一种中间状态。即这种粒子不会完全疏离，但聚集程度又远低于玻色子。详细情况可见该论文（图</span><span style="font-size:16px;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">1</span><span style="font-size:16px;font-family:宋体">），这里只是抛砖引玉一下。</span></p><p><span style="font-size:16px;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'"> </span></p><p style="text-align:center"><img style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:16px;font-family:宋体">图</span><span style="font-size:16px;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">1 </span><span 16px;font-family:宋体">）。有一类细胞，既不像上皮细胞，又不像间充质细胞，其特点介于上皮细胞和间充质细胞之间。如果类似于任意子，该细胞被称为任意细胞。</span></p><p><span style="font-size:16px;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'"> </span></p><p><span style="font-size:16px;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">RNA</span><span style="font-size:16px;font-family:宋体">和非编码</span><span style="font-size:16px;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">RNA</span><span style="font-size:16px;font-family:宋体">，分别编码蛋白质和不编码蛋白质。其中，</span><span style="font-size:16px;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">mRNA</span><span 宋体">。这些</span><span style="font-size:16px;font-family:'Times New Roman','serif'">RNA</span><span style="font-size:16px;font-family:宋体">在生物学中被称为</span><span style="font-size:16px;font-family:'Times New
