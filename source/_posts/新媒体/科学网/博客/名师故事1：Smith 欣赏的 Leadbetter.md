<p><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;">故事发生在1990年秋的北卡罗来纳大学教堂山分校（<span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">UNC-CH</span>）。我和统计系其他博一同学在上<span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">Walter Laws Smith</span>教授的随机过程课。</span></p><p><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><br></span></p><p><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;">有一次</span><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">Smith</span>教授说起，他三十多年前在剑桥大学出了一份考题，类似于我们刚刚考过的期中考试。有一个学生在答一道难题的部分写到（大意）：“<span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">Smith</span>教授</span>，我知道你对此题的标准答案是如<span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;">此</span>如此的（写了非常详细的步骤），但很不幸这个标准答案是错的。我的解法是如此如此。”<span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">Smith</span>教授很自豪地</span>告诉我们，这个学生就是<span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">Malcolm Ross Leadbetter</span>，<span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;">当时<span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;">也是<span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">UNC-CH</span></span>统计系</span></span>的教授。</span></span></p><p><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><br></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p><p><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">Leadbetter</span>教授的测度论是博一必修课，我虽然免修了，但我参加博士资格考试时，还是顺利完成了其中<span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">Leadbetter</span>教授</span></span></span>出的测度论题。博二我选了<span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">Leadbetter</span>教授</span>的极值理论（<span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">Extreme Value Theory</span>）和点过程（<span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">Point Processes</span>），都得了<span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">H</span> (<span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">High Pass, <span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai;">相当于</span>A</span>)。后来我在美国找教职，<span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">Leadbetter</span>教授</span></span></span></span></span></span>为我写了很好的推荐信。</span></span></span></p><p><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><br></span></span></span></p><p><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">Ross</span></span> Leadbetter</span>教授是新西兰人，本科毕业于<span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">Otago</span>大学，硕士毕业于剑桥大学（1958），<span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;">师从<span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">Smith</span>教授</span>获<span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">UNC-CH</span>的统计学博士（1963），随后1966年被<span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">UNC-CH</span>聘为终身副教授（见<span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;">期刊<span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">Extremes</span></span>2015年发表的对<span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;"><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">Ross</span> Leadbetter</span>的专访 <span style="font-size: 24px; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-family: times new roman;"><br></span></span></p><p><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;"><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;"><br></span></span></p><p><span style="font-size: 24px;
