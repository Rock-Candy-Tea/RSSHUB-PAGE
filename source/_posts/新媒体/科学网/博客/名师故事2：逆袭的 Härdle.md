<p><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;">1995年我博士毕业时，美国经济差导致大学的新教职太少，我找助理教授职位无果。我的导师<span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">J. S. Marron</span>教授（<span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">Steve</span>）就建议我去德国柏林，跟洪堡大学<span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">W. K. Härdle</span>教授<span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">（Wolfgang）</span></span>做博士后。</span></p><p><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><br></span></p><p><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;">当那年9月我见到</span><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">Wolfgang</span></span><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;">时，很惊讶。我读过他的很多论文，想象中他是身材高大，不苟言笑的德国人，而我当时见到的<span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;"></span><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">Wolfgang</span></span></span>是这样的</span></p><p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://bbs.sciencenet.cn/home.php?mod=attachment&filename=WHtalk.jpg&id=816102" alt="WHtalk.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p><p><br></p><p><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;"></span><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">Wolfgang</span></span>在北卡罗来纳大学教堂山分校<span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;">（<span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">UNC-CH</span>）</span>做过博士后，一见面就对我说：“<span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">You are from UNC, so I know the sort of things you can do</span>” 然后给了我<span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;">几篇</span>可加模型积分算法的论文读。不久他的另一个博士后<span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">Eric Severance-Lossin</span>（威斯康辛大学经济系的博士）抱怨论文被拒，<span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;"></span><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">Wolfgang</span></span></span>说换个期刊重投，<span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;">然后满不在乎</span>地大笑</span>，说“<span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">never be discouraged</span>”。</span></p><p><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><br></span></p><p><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;">第二年德州农工大学（<span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">TAMU</span>）的<span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">Raymond Carroll</span>教授（<span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">Ray</span>）获洪堡高级学者奖金，来</span>访问<span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">Wolfgang</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">Ray</span></span></span>去地铁站吃午饭，他说当年<span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">Wolfgang</span></span> </span>由于参加学生游行，被德语<span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;">国家（西德，奥地利，瑞士）</span></span></span></span>一些<span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;">右倾保守的</span></span></span></span>教授们打压。他1982年博士毕业时，没有哪个德语大学接受他做<span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">Habilitation</span>（德语国家教授资格），而不获得这个资格就不可能在德语国家找教职。后来<span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">Ray</span>就邀请<span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;"></span><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">Wolfgang</span></span>去<span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">UNC-CH</span>做博士后。</span></span></span></span></p><p style="text-align:center"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://bbs.sciencenet.cn/home.php?mod=attachment&filename=WH60s.png&id=816329" alt="WH60s.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></span></span></span></span></p><p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;">游行中</span>穿黑大衣的青年<span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">Wolfgang</span></span><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;"></span></span><br><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"></span></p><p><br></p><p><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;">说到<span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">Wolfgang</span><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;">这段</span>走麦城的经历，记得我对<span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">Ray</span>说“<span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">So that was before he was famous</span>”(那是他成名前)，而<span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">Ray</span>的回答是“<span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">He was always famous</span>”(他历来就是有名的)。确实，<span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-size: 24px; font-size: 24px;">和<span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">Steve</span>合作，一年就写出了5篇<span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">Annals of Statistics</span>论文，他获得过斯坦福大学等美国名校的聘任邀请。最终<span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">Wolfgang</span>去了比利时的鲁汶大学法语分校任教授，直到德国统一，才到洪堡大学担任商学院统计系主任（<span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">Lehrstuhl</span>，英语大致上<span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: times new roman;">Lecture Chair</span>,在德国的大学体制里，正教授不只是职称，而是自动带着系主任的官职），终于<span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;">“海归空降”，</span>成为了一名德国教授。</span></span></p><p><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><br></span></span></p><p><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 24px;"><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai;
