<p><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:107%;font-family:宋体"></span><strong><span style="font-size:19px;line-height: 107%;font-family:宋体">小编导读</span></strong></p><p style="text-indent:28px;line-height:32px"><span style="font-family:宋体;color:black;letter-spacing:1px">纳米加工是制造各种功能纳米材料的一个快速发展的领域。在过去的三十年里，随着纳米科学和纳米工程的发展，各种各样的化学和物理方法被开发用于纳米制造，</span><span style="color: black; letter-spacing: 1px; font-family: times new roman;">“Nanofabrication of Janus Fibers through Side-by-Side Electrospinning - A Mini Review”</span><span style="font-family:宋体;color:black;letter-spacing:1px">的文章，对</span><span style="color: black; color: black; letter-spacing: 1px;">（1）</span><span style="font-family:宋体;color:black;letter-spacing:1px">越来越小的皮米技术；</span><span style="font-family: times new roman; color: black; letter-spacing: 1px;">(2）</span><span style="font-family:宋体;color:black;letter-spacing:1px">更复杂的纳米结构和器件；以及</span><span style="font-family: times new roman; color: black; letter-spacing: 1px;">（3）</span><span style="font-family:宋体;color:black;letter-spacing:1px">更有序的纳米产品，例如各种无机纳米管阵列和定向纳米纤维。在这些方向中，</span><span style="color: black; letter-spacing: 1px; font-family: times new roman;">Janus</span><span style="font-family:宋体;color:black;letter-spacing:1px">纳米结构（如</span><span style="color: black; letter-spacing: 1px; font-family: times new roman;">Janus</span><span style="font-family:宋体;color:black;letter-spacing:1px">纳米纤维和</span><span style="color: black; letter-spacing: 1px; font-family: times new roman;">Janus</span><span style="font-family:宋体;color:black;letter-spacing:1px">纳米颗粒）的简易和可靠的制备是最困难的挑战之一。</span></p><p style="text-indent:28px;line-height:32px"><span style="font-family:宋体;color:black;letter-spacing:1px">如图</span><span style="color: black;
