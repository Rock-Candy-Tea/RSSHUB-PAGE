<p style="margin-top:5px;margin-right:0;margin-bottom:5px;margin-left:0;text-indent:32px"><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">理智与情感是人们心理的两个重要方面，二者需要彼此协调，才能保证个体有效地适应现实。然而，不少时候，人们的理智与情感却是不同步的，并且，情感远远滞后于理智，造成个体在理智上已经想通有关情况，但在情感上仍然拒斥对应情况的现象。这种情形，即为理通情拒效应。</span></span></p><p style="margin-top:5px;margin-right:0;margin-bottom:5px;margin-left:0;text-indent:32px"><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">一位初</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">中</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">二</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">年级</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的数学老师</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">在课堂上把一名男生小戈叫了起来：</span><span style="font-family:宋体">“你说，谁让你这样做题的？”小戈答道：“我爸。”老师听了，生气地说：“你爸？那你回家找你爸教去，别在学校听我讲了！”</span></span></p><p style="margin-top:5px;margin-right:0;margin-bottom:5px;margin-left:0;text-indent:32px"><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">从此，小戈在数学老师上课时，就不再听讲，作业更是应付了事。数学老师认识到自己前面说的话不合适，伤了小戈的心，也决定向小戈道歉。他酝酿多次，可是，面对小戈，他总是开不了口。他想写一封道歉信交给小戈，却又总是难以下笔。</span></span></p><p style="margin-top:5px;margin-right:0;margin-bottom:5px;margin-left:0;text-indent:32px"><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">在这个例子中，数学老师在理智上知道自己错了，也清楚通过道歉能够解决问题，然而，真要做出道歉的举动</span><span style="font-family:宋体">——无论是当面口头的还是书面文字的，他在情感上还是不能接受。没有真正的道歉行为，他和小戈之间的矛盾也就很难解决了。</span></span></p><p style="margin-top:5px;margin-right:0;margin-bottom:5px;margin-left:0;text-indent:32px"><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">通常，人们对这种情况的解读是：他还是过不了自己的心理这道关。显然，此处的心理是指情感，而非理智。也就是说，</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">人们存在情感力量强过理</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">智</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">力量的情况，</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">个体明明</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">知道怎么做是合适的，就是</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">由于情感因素而</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">难以这样做。</span></span></p><p style="margin-top:5px;margin-right:0;margin-bottom:5px;margin-left:0;text-indent:32px"><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">其他</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">类似的说法</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">至少还有，其一</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">：</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">道理</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">我都</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">懂，就是情感</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">上过不去</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">；其二，我</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">知道应该怎么做，就是面子上挂不住</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">；其三，</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">你说得都有道理，我也能够理解，但是，我还是不能接受。</span></span></p><p style="margin-top:5px;margin-right:0;margin-bottom:5px;margin-left:0;text-indent:32px"><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">尤其不妙的是，如果个体不懂得某些道理，那么，教他懂得这些道理，问题往往容易解决；但是，如果个体懂得某些道理，却在情感上拒斥，那就很麻烦，因为此时更难解决问题。</span></span></p><p style="margin-top:5px;margin-right:0;margin-bottom:5px;margin-left:0;text-indent:32px"><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">总之，人们</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">在情感上接受一个</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">说法或</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">现实，远比在</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">理智上</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">明白一个</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">说法或</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">现实，要困难得多</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">这种理智</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">与情感</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">不一致、</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">不同步</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的现象</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">对个体</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">来说，</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">是一种冲突、一种折磨</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">、</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">一种考验</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。</span></span></p><p style="margin-top:5px;margin-right:0;margin-bottom:5px;margin-left:0;text-indent:32px"><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:宋体">既然理通情拒</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:宋体">现象</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:宋体">是冲突、折磨和考验，那么，我们就应当积极寻求解决</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:宋体">的策略</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:宋体">。</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:宋体">例如，</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:宋体">一方面，</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:宋体">我们可以</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:宋体">培养</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:宋体">更</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:宋体">强的理智力量，面对问题，让理智处于优势地位，不使情感误事；另一方面，形成有益的行为模式，遇到问题，自然</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:宋体">反应就是</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:宋体">解决问题，而不是被情感因素所困。</span></span></p><p style="margin: 5px 0px; text-indent: 32px;"><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:宋体">更现实地讲，我</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">们可以熟记一些格言警句，例如，</span><span style="font-family:宋体">“冲动是魔鬼”、“人谁无过？过而能改，善莫大焉”，从而，一方面，我们尽量少做那些冲动的行为，另一方面，在有关格言警句的引导下，采取合理行动，有效解决问题——人们对行动理由的追求是很强烈的，而格言警句是极好的行动理由。</span></span></p>                    <br><br>
