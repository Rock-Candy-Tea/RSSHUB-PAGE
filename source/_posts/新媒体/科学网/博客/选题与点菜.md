宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px">目标，可以比前三种方式任何一种都奏效。关键要</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px">做到两点：一是目标定得好，例如发</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px">nature；</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px">二是咬定目标不放松，</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px">例如不</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px">能发</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px">nature</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px">的选题坚决不做，要求给题者</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px">（例如</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px">导师</span><span 16px">。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px">这方面</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px">，</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px">孙悟空</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px">是</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px">值得效仿的经</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px">典</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;font-size: 16px">。</span></p><p style="margin-right:0;margin-bottom:10px;margin-left:0;text-indent:0;padding:0 0 0 0 ;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;text-align:justify;text-justify:inter-ideograph;line-height:200%"><span 0;font-size: 16px">读研</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px">，</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px">在连续干了七年左右搬砖般的苦力活</span><span 16px">与众师兄学言语礼貌，讲经论道，习字焚香，每日如此</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 16px">）</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span 16px">“</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px">帽子</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px">”</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">等是没有任何问题的，问悟空</span><span style="font-family:宋体">“不知你学那一门哩</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: ;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;text-align:justify;text-justify:inter-ideograph;line-height:200%"><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">悟空很谦虚：</span><span style="font-family:宋体">“但凭尊师教诲，只是有些道气儿，弟子便就学了。”</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px">——这显然与“随便，由餐馆配，</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px">只是</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">要满足一定要求</span><span style="font-family:宋体">”无异。</span></span></p><p style="margin-right:0;margin-bottom:10px;margin-left:0;text-indent:0;padding:0 0 0 0 ;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;text-align:justify;text-justify:inter-ideograph;line-height:200%"><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">祖师先后抛出了</span><span style="font-family:宋体">“术”、“流”、“静”、“动”等旁门并逐一给悟空耐心讲解。悟空</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px">听不懂，</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px">也不细究</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px">。他</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">只始终如一问祖师同一个问题：</span><span style="font-family:宋体">“似这般可得长生么？”——相当于</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px">“</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px">做这玩意儿能</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px">发</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px">nature</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">么？</span><span style="font-family:宋体">”</span></span></p><p style="margin-right:0;margin-bottom:10px;margin-left:0;text-indent:0;padding:0 0 0 0 ;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;text-align:justify;text-justify:inter-ideograph;line-height:200%"><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px">祖师</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px">每每</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px">明确告知</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px">：</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px">“不能，不能！”悟空</span><span style="font-family: 16px">：</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px">“不学，不学！”</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px">——</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 16px">“学一个不老长生”</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 16px">是悟空拜师学艺前就明确的目标。</span></p><p style="margin-right:0;margin-bottom:10px;margin-left:0;text-indent:0;padding:0 0 0 0 ;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;text-align:justify;text-justify:inter-ideograph;line-height:200%"><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">众所周知，正是悟空</span><span style="font-family:宋体">“这般不学，那般不学”</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px">，才</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">让祖师</span><span style="font-family:宋体">“我亦喜悦”地“当传与你长生之妙道也”。</span></span></p><p style="margin-right:0;margin-bottom:10px;margin-left:0;text-indent:0;padding:0 0 0 0 ;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;text-align:justify;text-justify:inter-ideograph;line-height:200%"><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px">现实</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px">中</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: ;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;text-align:justify;text-justify:inter-ideograph;line-height:200%"><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px">悟空给我们启示：读研，若想取得大成就，与其被导师</span><span ;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;text-align:justify;text-justify:inter-ideograph;line-height:200%"><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: ;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;text-align:justify;text-justify:inter-ideograph;line-height:200%"><span style="font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px">最后，</span><span style="font-family:
