<p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:Calibri">CRISPR</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">是理想的</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">基因组编辑系统</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">，</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">通过</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">对这种系统的</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:Calibri">Cas9</span><span style="font-family:宋体">酶进化起源</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">进行分析</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，研究人员发现在</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">100</span><span style="font-family:宋体">多万种微生物基因组中的其他潜在编辑器</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">蛋白酶</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">。</span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">发表在</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">9</span><span style="font-family:宋体">月</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">9</span><span style="font-family:宋体">日的《科学》杂志上</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">的</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">这项研究，在一个名为</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">IscB</span><span style="font-family:宋体">的蛋白质家族中发现了新的编辑酶。这些蛋白质被认为是</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">Cas9</span><span style="font-family:宋体">酶的祖先，</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">Cas9</span><span style="font-family:宋体">酶被称为</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">CRISPR</span><span style="font-family:宋体">的分子剪刀</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">，</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">是该基因编辑系统的重要分子操作工具</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。在基因组编辑过程中，</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">Cas9</span><span style="font-family:宋体">与</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">RNA</span><span style="font-family:宋体">片段合作，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:Calibri">RNA</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">引导</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">Cas9</span><span style="font-family:宋体">酶找到并切割特定</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">DNA</span><span style="font-family:宋体">序列。这项技术依赖于</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">RNA</span><span style="font-family:宋体">作为引导系统，是其多功能性和广泛应用的一个关键原因，研究人员</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">可</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">轻松地将</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">Cas9</span><span style="font-family:宋体">定位到他们想要改变的基因组区域。</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">实现随心所欲编辑基因的目的，这是现代分子生物医学的重要技术革命。</span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">该研究的主要作者</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:Calibri">MIT</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的分子生物学家张峰说，发现其他能够切割</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">DNA</span><span style="font-family:宋体">的</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:Calibri">RNA</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">靶向酶，可能会为基因组编辑提供进一步的工具。</span><span style="font-family:宋体">“这些可编程蛋白质非常有用，超出了基本的生物学兴趣，”他说。“这种</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:Calibri">RNA</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">引导的</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">DNA</span><span style="font-family:宋体">识别机制很可能是大自然多次独立创造的</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">成果</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。</span><span style="font-family:宋体">”</span></span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">尽管研究人员已经将</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">CRISPR</span><span style="font-family:宋体">用于基因</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">编辑</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">，</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">这是</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">一种微生物</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">免疫</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">防御系统，可让细菌</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">和</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">古菌的单细胞生物通过发送</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">Cas9</span><span style="font-family:宋体">来</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">破坏</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">病毒和其他入侵者的</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">DNA</span><span style="font-family:宋体">。计算机研究表明，</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">Cas9</span><span style="font-family:宋体">可能是从</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">IscB</span><span style="font-family:宋体">家族蛋白质进化而来，</span></span><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;color: #0000FF;font-size: 14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">该家族由转座子或</span><span style="font-family:宋体">“跳跃基因”编码</span></span></strong><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，可在基因组中跳转到新位置。到目前为止，</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">IscB</span><span style="font-family:宋体">蛋白的</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">具体生物</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">功能还不清楚。</span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">张</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">锋</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">和同事们发现，负责编码</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">IscB</span><span style="font-family:宋体">蛋白质的</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">DNA</span><span style="font-family:宋体">通常位于一类</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">RNA</span><span style="font-family:宋体">分子的</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">DNA</span><span style="font-family:宋体">附近，他们称之为ω</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">RNA</span><span style="font-family:宋体">。他们还发现，一些</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">IscB</span><span style="font-family:宋体">蛋白可在ω</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">RNA</span><span style="font-family:宋体">序列指定位置切割</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">DNA</span><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">这</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">就像</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">Cas9</span><span style="font-family:宋体">及其引导</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">RNA</span><span style="font-family:宋体">一样。</span></span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">该团队继续研究另一个名为</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">TnpB</span><span style="font-family:宋体">的蛋白质家族，该家族被认为是另一种名为</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">Cas12</span><span style="font-family:宋体">的</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:Calibri">DNA</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">切片</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">CRISPR</span><span style="font-family:宋体">相关酶的祖先。他们发现，在ω</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">RNA</span><span style="font-family:宋体">的引导下，其中一些蛋白质也能切割</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">DNA</span><span style="font-family:宋体">。</span></span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">麻省理工学院</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">(MIT)</span><span style="font-family:宋体">的分子生物学家</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">，</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">该研究的第一作者之一苏米亚</span><span style="font-family:宋体">·坎南</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">(Soumya Kannan)</span><span style="font-family:宋体">说，数据库搜索结果显示，有</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">100</span><span style="font-family:宋体">多万个基因可能携带</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">TnpB</span><span style="font-family:宋体">蛋白的密码，有些生物含有这些基因的</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">100</span><span style="font-family:宋体">多个副本。</span></span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">特别重要的是，</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:Calibri">IscB</span><span style="font-family:宋体">基因不仅存在于细菌和古生菌中，还存在于藻类细胞内的光吸收叶绿体中。这是首次在真核细胞中</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">发现</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">这样的基因组编辑系统</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">。</span><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 14px">这一令人惊讶的结果表明，真核生物比之前认为的更广泛。</span></strong><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">“每次我演讲时，人们总是问我是否在真核细胞中看到了</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">CRISPR</span><span style="font-family:宋体">活动，”张说。“现在，我终于可以</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">回答</span><span style="font-family:宋体">“是的”</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">了。</span><span style="font-family:宋体">”</span></span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">在自然界中，这些基因可以执行各种功能，包括防御或调节其他基因的表达。在实验室中，这一发现可能会</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">让科学家设计出</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">大量编辑工具。</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">张</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的团队发现</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">IscB</span><span style="font-family:宋体">可以用来切割人类</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">DNA</span><span style="font-family:宋体">，尽管其效率低于</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">CRISPR-Cas9</span><span style="font-family:宋体">系统。但是张说，</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">IscB</span><span style="font-family:宋体">系统可以改进，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;color:#0000FF;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">指出</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">IscB</span><span style="font-family:宋体">蛋白的小尺寸可能会使它在某些应用中更容易使用。</span></span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">对于堪培拉澳大利亚国立大学的遗传学家</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">Gaetan Burgio</span><span style="font-family:宋体">来说，</span></span><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;color: #0000FF;font-size: 14px">这项研究的真正美妙之处在于它对理解进化的贡献，</span></strong><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">以及最终为</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">iscb</span><span style="font-family:宋体">这样一个大而普遍的蛋白质群赋予可能的功能。“这绝对令人着迷，”他说。“它填补了一个重要的</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">理论</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">空白</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">：</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">我们</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">过去</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">真的不知道这些</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">CRISPR</span><span style="font-family:宋体">系统是如何变成</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">CRISPR</span><span style="font-family:宋体">的。”</span></span></p><p><br></p>                    <br><br>
