line-height: 200%; text-align: justify;"><span style="font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 200%; font-family: 宋体; background: white;">中国科学院遗传发育所储成才研究组鉴定到一个花粉不育突变体</span><em><span style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 200%; font-family: 宋体; background: white;">。研究发现，</span><em><span style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 200%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; background: white;">post</span></em><span style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 200%; font-family: 宋体; background: white;">突变体花药绒毡层</span><span style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 200%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; background: white;">PCD</span><span style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 200%; font-family: 宋体; background: white;">过程受到抑制，使绒毡层降解受到抑制和推迟，导致花粉的败育（图 1）。</span><em><span style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 200%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; background: white;">POST</span></em><span style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 200%; font-family: 宋体; background: white;">基因编码一个全新的蛋白，仅存在于被子植物中。</span><em><span style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 200%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; background: white;">POST</span></em><span style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 200%; font-family: 宋体; background: white;">主要在花药发育的第</span><span style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 200%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; background: white;">8</span><span style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 200%; font-family: 宋体; background: white;">和</span><span style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 200%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; background: white;">9</span><span style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 200%; font-family: 宋体; background: white;">时期的绒毡层和小孢子中表达。转录组结果表明，</span><em><span style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 200%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; background: white;">POST</span></em><span style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 200%; font-family: 宋体; background: white;">可能通过脂质代谢过程的参与绒毡层</span><span style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 200%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; background: white;">PCD</span><span style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 200%; font-family: 宋体; background: white;">。</span><em><span style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 200%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; background: white;">post</span></em><span style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 200%; font-family: 宋体; background: white;">突变体除了雄性不育外，其颖壳出现了明显增大，暗示了细胞死亡和细胞分裂存在着一定关联，这一发现</span><span style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 200%; font-family: 宋体;">为深层揭示<span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; background: white;">绒毡层程序化细胞死亡过程</span>提供了全新的思路<span style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; background: white;">。有意思的是，精细调控</span></span><em><span style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 200%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; background: white;">POST</span></em><span style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 200%; font-family: 宋体; background: white;">不仅可以保持其花粉育性，且能增大水稻颖壳，增加库的大小，进而提高籽粒产量（图<span style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 200%; background: white; font-family: "times new roman";"> 2</span>）。因此，</span><span style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 200%; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; background: white;">POST</span><span style="font-size: 16px;
