<p style="margin-top:8px;margin-right:0;margin-bottom:16px;margin-left:0;background:white"><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">【创新型人才培养若干思考】</span></p><p style="margin-top:8px;margin-right:0;margin-bottom:16px;margin-left:0;background:white"><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">        2021</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">年</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">7</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">月</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">26</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">日，</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">2021</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">年博士点终于揭晓了，教育部网站上公示了《</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">2020</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">年学位授权审核结果公示》，根据公示情况，全国共有</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">361</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">个博士点获得授权公示，其中新增博士学位授权单位</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">34</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">个，获立项授权公示</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">43</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">个一级学科建设博士点，已有博士授权点高校新增一级学科博士点涉及</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">80</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">个点，共获立项授权公示一级学科建设博士点</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">229</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">个，其中获批较多的前三个一级学科博士点为物理学</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">23</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">个，数学</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">20</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">个，马克思主义理论</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">17</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">个）。</span> <span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">另外，专业博士学位点共涉及立项教育、电子信息等</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">14</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">个点，新增立项授权公示</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">89</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">个专业博士学位点。博士点批下去了，如何建设从某种角度来说比申请还难，博士点建设是否成功一个重要的标志是博士生的培养质量，从某种程度上来说，博士生是国家创新型人才的中坚力量，如何培养好，是一个值得全方位思考的课题。有以下思考：</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-top:8px;margin-right:0;margin-bottom: 16px;margin-left:24px;background:white"><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">1.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal;font-variant-east-asian: normal;font-stretch: normal;font-size: 9px;line-height: normal;font-family: 'Times New Roman'">    </span></span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">真正做到以生为本。作为导师一定要在科研方面全方位指导学生。虽然我国博士招生和培养方式的形成有其历史原因，但作为导师，所带博士硕士十几、几十个的，这放在世界范围内都是堪称奇观的。即使顶尖的藤校等名校，虽然招生指标没有硬性限制，但绝大多数的导师一年也至多招一到三个博士生，很少有突破这个极限的。因为人的精力毕竟是有限的，你要多带，就只能放羊了。在</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">developed</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">郭嘉看来，博士生是研究者</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">(researcher),</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">而不再是学生</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">(student),</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">必须学会培养自己的学术能力</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">,</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">并基于此进行独立研究</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">,</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">不能依赖任何人</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">,</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">也没有标准答案。导师与博士生之间的互动交流主要体现在定期的、一对一的学术研讨</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">(meeting)</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">上。在研讨中</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">,</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">学生将所有不解和疑惑的问题提出来请教</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">,</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">而导师则利用其学术知识和经验进行解答。</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-top:8px;margin-right:0;margin-bottom: 16px;margin-left:24px;background:white"><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">2.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal;font-variant-east-asian: normal;font-stretch: normal;font-size: 9px;line-height: normal;font-family: 'Times New Roman'">    </span></span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">真正做到以师为本。作为学校和科研机构，一定要充分了解导师，比如导师的科研方向和科研状态，导师的教学科研方面的需求，充分做好</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">“</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">后勤保障</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">”</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">工作。顶尖名校方面，教工分为</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">faculty</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">和</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">staff</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">，带博士生的</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">professor</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">属于</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">faculty</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">范畴，</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">staff</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">基本上对应着郭嘉大学的行政的角色，所有</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">staff</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">都围绕</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">faculty</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">，以</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">faculty</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">为中心提供服务。比如：</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">Professor</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">一般配备</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">secretary</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">和</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">teach assistant</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">，前者负责实验室日常事务的处理，后者负责上课有关事务处理比如作业批改等，所以教授一般会有更多的时间来处理自己的科研和教学。</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-top:8px;margin-right:0;margin-bottom: 16px;margin-left:24px;background:white"><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">3.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal;font-variant-east-asian: normal;font-stretch: normal;font-size: 9px;line-height: normal;font-family: 'Times New Roman'">    </span></span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">真正做到以科研为本。有了以上两条，实际上已经为以科研为本奠定了基础。当前，针对博士生的培养在各个培养单位都指定了一揽子规则规章制度，但这些给规章制度是否有利于科研，值得商榷。比如很多学校明确将博士生论文送审前置条件规定为必须在</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">**</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 宋体">期刊上公开发表或录用论文</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif">N</span><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family:
