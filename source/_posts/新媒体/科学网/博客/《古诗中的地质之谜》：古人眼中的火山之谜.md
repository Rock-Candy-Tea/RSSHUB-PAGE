overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 16px;">  所谓煤层自燃，指的是在天然条件下，露天煤矿、矿井内部以及储煤场等地的煤炭自发燃烧的现象。由于煤炭与空气具有一定的接触面积，会逐渐发生氧化作用，使得煤炭的温度升高，当累积的热量达到一定程度，导致温度达到了煤炭的燃点时就有可能引发煤炭自燃现象。</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; text-align: center; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://bbs.sciencenet.cn/home.php?mod=attachment&filename=640%20%282%29.jpg&id=951357" style="white-space: normal;" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><section><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 16px;"></span><br></section><section><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体;">  新疆的煤炭资源十分丰富，但这里也是遭受煤层自燃灾害最严重的地方之一，从北部的阿勒泰草原到南部的帕米尔高原都有煤炭自燃现象发生。根据新疆第五次煤田火区普查，截至</span>2020<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体;">年底，全区共有在燃火区</span>40<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体;">处、易发煤火风险区</span>37<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体;">处。</span>2015<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体;">年</span>4<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体;">月的一天，一则题为《新疆一工地出现火山口》的新闻引起广泛关注。事发地乌鲁木齐市西山的半山坡上有一个直径约</span>150<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体;">厘米的不规则圆洞，洞内冒出红彤彤的火光和滚滚热浪，令人望而生畏。（本文作者马志飞，原载于《百科知识》，转载请注明作者和出处。）但最终的调查结果表明，这个所谓的“火山口”与火山没有丝毫关系，而是地下煤层自燃形成的塌陷坑，其中冒出的火光、青烟和热浪正是地下煤层自燃的结果。</span></span></section><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体;"><br></span></span></p><section><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体;">  早在</span>6<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体;">多年前人们就已经观察到新疆的煤层自燃现象。</span></span></section><section><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体;"><br></span></span></section><section><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体;">  其实，在人类开始大规模开采使用煤炭资源以前，煤层自燃现象就已经存在。学者们根据新疆第四系黄土中发现的烧变岩碎片等资料推断，这里的一些煤田早在几十万年前就已经起火了。鄯善县的煤炭资源也十分丰富，该县的沙尔湖煤田属于特大型煤田，由于当地干燥少雨，昼夜温差大，风蚀现象较为严重，露天煤矿容易发生自燃。资料记载，</span>2010<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体;">年</span>6<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体;">月</span>25<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体;">日，因大风天气，鄯善县七克台镇东南</span>80<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体;">千米处的沙尔湖露天煤矿发生大面积火情。与沙尔湖煤田临近的哈密大南湖煤田也存在范围较广的自燃现象，钻孔资料表明，煤层的上覆地层因受到高温烘烤，变质形成的烧变岩特征十分明显。</span></span></section><section><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体;"><br></span></span></section><section><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 16px;">  当年岑参两度出塞，从长安（今西安）一路向西，沿途经过哈密、鄯善、吐鲁番、轮台，穿过茫茫戈壁，最终到达安西都护府治所龟兹，目之所见有煤层自燃现象也在情理之中。因此，诗中那些看似夸张的风景，不是诗人浪漫主义的浮想联翩，而恰恰是实实在在的叙事和纪实。</span></section></section></section></section></section></section></section></section></section></section></section><p><br></p>                    <br><br>
