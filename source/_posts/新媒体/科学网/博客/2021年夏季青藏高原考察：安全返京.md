<p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">2021-08-17</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">，星期二</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体">中国科学院动物研究所，牛泽清博士</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体">今天是返京行程的最后一天了。为了能在下午</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">1</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">点之前回到所里，我们今早特意起得更早。早上</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">6</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">点</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">40</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">分我俩就已起床，下楼结清住宿账单；早上</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">7</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">点，我俩已从盂县南上</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">G5</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">高速，奔向石家庄方向。</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体">早上的山间雾气很浓，从盂县出来，路过井陉，一路都是雾蒙蒙的，仿佛置身于仙境中一般。也多亏小吴驾车技术好，要不还真不敢在这样的天气开车急奔呢。</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202108/18/071348m88ytzyy8ujqydot.jpeg" title alt="WechatIMG2.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p><p style="text-align: center;
