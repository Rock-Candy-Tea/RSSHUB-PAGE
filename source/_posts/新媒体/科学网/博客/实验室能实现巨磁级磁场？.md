font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">兆特斯拉（</span>megatesla<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">简称</span>MT<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）磁场的强度是地磁</span>(0.3 ~ 0.5 G)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">的（</span>10-100<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）亿倍（</span>1–10 billion times<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">），只能在中子星或黑洞等天体附近观测到。这一结果将有助于在实验室中实现</span>MT<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">级磁场的雄心勃勃的实验，该实验目前正在进行中。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">自</span>19<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">世纪以来，科学家们一直努力在实验室中实现最高磁场。到目前为止，在实验室中观测到的最高磁场是千特斯拉</span>(kT)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">级。</span>2020<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">年，大阪大学的村上正松</span>(Masakatsu Murakami)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">提出了一个名为微管内爆</span>(microtube implosions<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">简称</span>MTI)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">的新方案，以产生</span>MT<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">级的超高磁场。用超强超短激光脉冲（</span></span><a href="https://phys.org/tags/ultrashort+laser+pulses/" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">ultrashort laser pulses</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">）照射微米大小的空心圆柱体，产生速度接近光速的热电子（</span><a href="https://phys.org/tags/hot+electrons/" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">hot electrons</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）。这些热电子向中轴发射了一个圆柱形对称的内壁离子内爆。应用一个平行于中轴的</span>kT<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">级的预先播种磁场</span>(pre-seeded </span><a href="https://phys.org/tags/magnetic+field/" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">magnetic field</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">，由于洛伦兹力</span>(Lorentz force)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">，使离子和电子的轨迹向相反的方向弯曲。在目标轴附近，那些弯曲的离子和电子轨迹共同形成了一个强大的自旋电流，产生了</span>MT<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">级磁场。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">在这项研究中，团队成员（</span><a href="https://phys.org/tags/team+members/" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">team members</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）之一迪达尔·肖科夫</span>(Didar Shokov)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">在大阪大学网络媒体中心（</span>Osaka University's Cybermedia Center<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）使用超级计算机</span>OCTOPUS<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">广泛地进行了三维模拟。结果发现，</span>MTI<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">产生磁场的性能与外部参数</span>(<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">如激光强度、激光能量和靶尺寸</span>)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">之间存在明显的比例法则（</span>scaling law<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">村上正松说：“我们的模拟显示，超高的巨火山磁场，过去被认为是不可能在地球上实现的，但是可以使用当今的激光技术来实现。目标磁场的标度规律和详细的时间行为有望促进大阪大学激光工程研究所（</span>Osaka University's Institute of Laser Engineering<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）的千万亿瓦特（</span>Peta-watt<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）激光系统“</span>LFEX<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">”的实验室实验，该实验目前正在进行中。”</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">Microtube implosions are a novel scheme to generate ultrahigh magnetic fields of the megatesla order. These implosions are driven by ultraintense and ultrashort laser pulses. Using two- and three-dimensional particle simulations where megatesla-order magnetic fields can be achieved, we demonstrate scaling and criteria in terms of laser parameters, such as laser intensity and laser energy, to facilitate practical experiments toward the realization of extreme physical conditions, which have yet to be realized in laboratories. Microtube implosions should provide a new platform for studies in fundamental and applied physics relevant to ultrahigh magnetic fields.</span></p><p><br></p>                    <br><br>
