<div>   
<p style="text-indent:32px;line-height:27px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">大学里的学习我们会遇到各种各样的挑战，各种各样的困难，更不用说创新</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">。</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">很多时候就一个问题</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">，</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">一个障碍</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">就</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">让我们看不见前面的路，没有方法，没有路径，只有一片漆黑，这就是一叶障目</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">的状态</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">。我曾经被一个</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">学术</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">问题卡壳了很长时间，没有一点进展，非常苦恼</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">。</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">后来被一个老师给予帮助解决了</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">让我好生感动并</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">感恩一辈子</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">，因为，</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">自那以后我的研究取得了快速的进展。</span></p><p style="text-indent:32px;line-height:27px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">我开始反思这个问题究竟是</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">怎么</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">缠绕着我，</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">使</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">我</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">裹足</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">不前的。其实</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">我们</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">所谓的学术问题，也</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">只</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">是一个关于对象分析的指标计算的常规问题。</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">实际上，在</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">后来的研究道路上，我也</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">经常遇到</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">国际学者</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">，他们</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">通过邮件请我帮忙分析</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">所在领域</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">研究对象的几个指标，并将分析结果给他们</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">。每每遇到这种情况，</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">我也都帮忙给解出来，并将分析工具</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">分享</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">给他们。</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">事后想想，我好震惊！是的呀，原来人家是怎么克服前进中的困难的？他们通过外包服务的形式将一叶障目的苦恼解除，一叶障目的那一片树叶对他们而言根本不存在，他们继续探索，往前行走，无惧于有限的一点无知。</span></p><p style="text-indent:32px;line-height:27px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">时隔多年，</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">他们现在也都成了</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">我</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">们这个圈子里的学术牛人，并反过来也给我们小组很多帮助，共同成长</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">。</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">一个问题，一叶障目</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">。但是，有</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">两个方法</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">：</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">一个是自己苦苦思考，长期努力，</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">或遭遇长时间未果的烦恼</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">，直到偶然获得帮助，从而得以扫除障碍</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">、</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">获得进展</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">为止；另一种</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">是国际</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">视野，在</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">全球</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">或者大范围</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">寻求帮助，并</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">与别人</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">同步前进</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">。后</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">者还有一个潜在的特质，</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">即姑且</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">不管某个障碍的存在，同步将研究主题的其他能够解决的问题先解决，不能解决的暂时放在</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">那</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">里，同时伴以大范围的求助</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">与问询</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">。我恍然想到我小时候的考试，老师不都是这么告诉我们的吗？孩子你去考试，能写的尽量写，不能写的最后有时间就去想，想不出来就交卷</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">。</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">多么简单的道理</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">！</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">到了</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">面对</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">研究与创新的时候，我们便忘掉了</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">这个</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">基本法则，丢掉了宝贵的法宝。由此我导出了去除</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">一叶障目</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">的三个法则</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">。</span></p><p style="text-indent:32px;line-height:27px"><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 16px">1.</span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 16px">专注于创新</span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 16px">。</span></strong><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">即</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">专注于我们自己能够解决的那个问题，而不为眼前的</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">其他</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">问题</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">或者</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">困难所磕绊，也就是不把</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">“障目</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">的叶子</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">”</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">放在我们的眼睛上。</span></p><p style="text-indent:32px;line-height:27px"><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 16px">2.</span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 16px">主动大范围寻求帮助。</span></strong><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">障目</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">的叶子只是一片叶子，犹如飞进我们的眼睛里的</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">虫子</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">，我们揉揉眼睛</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">却</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">怎么也没用</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">。</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">姑且</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">放慢</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">脚步，</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">但</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">仍然要向前走，并同时请人帮助我们去掉眼睛里的虫子即可。对于学术研究与各种工程创新，更是可以大范围甚至全世界寻求合作与帮助。</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">就某个主题而言，已经有了</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">那么多已经发表的论文</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">、</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">专利</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">和专著</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">，说明那些对我们而言困难的事情，对于全球范围的广大科研同伴而言，已经不是个事儿。</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">寻求合作是去除一叶障目的最好办法。</span></p><p style="text-indent:32px;line-height:27px"><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 16px">3.</span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 16px">黑匣子思维</span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 16px">。</span></strong><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">把</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">障目</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">的一</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">叶</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">转换成黑匣子，而不是黑蛾子。黑匣子思维告诉我们，</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">一叶障目</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">的</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">那</span><span style="font-family:宋体">“一叶”</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">代表着某种输入与输出的连接被暂时掩盖了，我们</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">暂时</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">的无知掐断了输入到输出的转换，但是我们个人的无知绝不</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">代表整个行业或者整个领域</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">的无知，我们只要将黑匣子问题交给其他研究人员去解决</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">便好，这是一个好主意。</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">这种黑匣子思维，也正是前面咱们提出的有限联系</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">假设</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">的推论</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">。</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">有限联系</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">假设</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">告诉我们，某个研究对象所牵连的维度、层级、节点都是有限的，我们关注我们能解决的几个节点与维度，而将</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">那些</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">与研究对象相连，或许也</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">可能</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">是紧密相连的</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">那个障目的一个节点</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">用一个黑匣子来代替</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">便</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">好</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">。</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">黑匣子交给对应的</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">T</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">oolbox，或者寻求别人的帮助而解决，这对我们的创动</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">训练</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">不构成任何实质性的威胁。</span></p><p style="text-indent:32px;line-height:27px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">由此我开始关注</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">求知</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">与创新的本质性区别。所谓</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">一叶障目</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">的那</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">“一叶”</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">，对我们来说，</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">只</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">是一个求知与学习的问题。</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">而</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">所谓求知与学习，也就是</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">意味着</span><span style="font-family:宋体">“</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">某个问题我不知道，或许你也不知道，但是这个世界上一定有人知道</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">”，这个</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">让自己</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">从不知道到</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">知道的过程便是学习。而所谓创新</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">，</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">也就是说</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">“</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">某个问题与方案，现在你不知道，我也不知道，世界上的任何人都不知道，或者说从来没去想过我们同处</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">的某个</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">黑暗空间，但是通过</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">探索</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">，总有一天我首先发现</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">、</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">我首先知道</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">、</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">并</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">想</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">让你和这个世界也都知道</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">”</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">，</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">这个过程</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">也就是所谓的创新了。</span></p><p><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">去除一叶障目是对团队力量的回归，去除一叶障目是对探索过程大体系的回归。去除一叶障目需要我们清楚的界定</span><span style="font-family:宋体">“一叶”的范围，并专注于“一叶”之外的工作，去除一叶障目是“功夫在诗外”的体现。</span></span></p><p><br></p>                    <br><br>
