<p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">著名工程师和发明家的名言（2）</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">武夷山 译</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">    Workflowmax网站于2020年4月提供了著名工程师和发明家的一些名言（博主：原文如此。其实有的语录的主人并非工程师，只是语录内容涉及工程或工程师），下面我翻译其中一些，与大家分享。这是第二部分。</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">  </span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">1.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">  </span><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;">“Normal people believe that if it ain’t broke, don’t fix it. Engineers believe that if it ain’t broke, it doesn’t have enough features yet.”</span></strong><br> - Scott Adams </span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">    普通人认为，若东西还没坏，就别修理它。工程师则认为，如果没坏，就说明它不具备足够多的功能。</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">2.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">  </span><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;">“Engineering is not only study of 45 subjects but it is moral studies of intellectual life.”</span></strong><br> - Prakhar Srivastav</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">工程学不仅涉及45门课程的学习，而且也是对知性人生的道德研究。</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">3.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">  </span><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;">“First rule of engineering; beware prototypes. Along with, avoid anything made by an engineer who doesn't have all his own fingers.”</span></strong><br> - Simon R. Green</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">    工程第一规则：对原型要小心提防。与此同时，避开手脚笨拙的工程师所做的任何东西。</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">4.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">  </span><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;">“The trick to having good ideas is not to sit around in glorious isolation and try to think big thoughts. The trick is to get more parts on the table.”</span></strong><br> - Steven Johnson</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">    获得好创意的诀窍不是不食人间烟火地傻坐着去天马行空地构想，而是弄一堆零件放在台子上。</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">5.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">  </span><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;">“[John] Kobak explained, 'The way you learn anything is that something fails, and you figure out how not to have it fail again.”</span></strong><br> - Robert S. Arrighi，《追求动力：美国国家航空航天局的1号和2号推进系统实验室》一书的作者</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">     [约翰]柯达解释说，“学习任何东西有一妙招：有东西失效了，你就设法弄清如何使它不再失效”。</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">6.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">  </span><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;">“Manufacturing is more than just putting parts together. It’s coming up with ideas, testing principles and perfecting the engineering as well as final assembly."</span></strong><br> - James Dyson（1947- ），英国发明家</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">    制造不单单是将零件装配在一起。它涉及：提出创意，对原理的测试，完善设计制造过程，及最后的装配。</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">7.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">  </span><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;">“Projects we have completed demonstrate what we know - future projects decide what we will learn.”</span></strong><br> - Dr. Mohsin Tiwana </span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">    我们完成了的项目表明我们了解了什么——而未来的项目决定着我们将学会什么。</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-weight: normal;">8</span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;">. “I don’t spend my time pontificating about high-concept things; I spend my time solving engineering and manufacturing problems.”</span></strong><br> - Elon Musk，埃隆.马斯克</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">    我的时间不是用于空谈高端概念，而是用于解决工程制造问题。</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">9.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">  </span><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;">“A good scientist is a person with original ideas. A good engineer is a person who makes a design that works with as few ideas as possible. There are no prima donnas in engineering.”</span></strong><br> - Freeman Dyson，弗里曼.戴森，出生于英国的著名美国物理学家</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">    优秀的科学家表现在有原创想法。优秀的工程师则表现在，能采用尽可能少的想法做出可行的设计。在工程中没有突出的主角。</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">10.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">  </span><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;">“There’s nothing I believe in more strongly than getting young people interested in science and engineering, for a better tomorrow, for all humankind.”</span></strong><br> - Bill Nye，美国机械工程师，著名电视科普专家</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">    我所坚信的事物，莫过于使年轻人对科学和工程发生兴趣，为了更美好的明天，为了全人类。</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">11.<strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;">“But remember this, Japanese boy... airplanes are not tools for war. They are not for making money. Airplanes are beautiful dreams. Engineers turn dreams into reality.”</span></strong><br> - Hayao Miyazaki，宫崎骏，日本著名动画师</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">    日本男孩，请记住......飞机不是战争工具。也不是挣钱的工具。飞机是美丽的梦。工程师使美梦成真。</span></p><p style="line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"> </span></p><p style="line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">相关阅读</span></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">著名工程师和发明家的名言（1），</span><a href="http://blog.sciencenet.cn/blog-1557-1284837.html" style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun; text-decoration: underline;" target="_blank"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">http://blog.sciencenet.cn/blog-1557-1284837.html</span></a></p><p><br></p>                    <br><br>
