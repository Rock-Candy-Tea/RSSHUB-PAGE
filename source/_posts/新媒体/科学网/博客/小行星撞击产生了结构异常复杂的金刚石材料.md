font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank">Credit: Pixabay/CC0 Public Domain</a></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em; text-align: center;"><a href="https://www.ucl.ac.uk/news/2022/jul/asteroid-impacts-create-diamond-materials-exceptionally-complex-structures" target="_blank"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202207/21/214615l45rqgpqlzcq35r2.png" title alt="asteroid_spacediamonds_newsstory_800x500.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a><br></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">据英国伦敦大学学院（</span><a href="https://www.ucl.ac.uk/news/2022/jul/asteroid-impacts-create-diamond-materials-exceptionally-complex-structures" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">University College London</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">简称</span><a href="https://www.ucl.ac.uk/news/2022/jul/asteroid-impacts-create-diamond-materials-exceptionally-complex-structures" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">UCL</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-family: 宋体;">）</span>2022<span style="font-family: 宋体;">年</span>7<span style="font-family: 宋体;">月</span>18<span style="font-family: 宋体;">日提供的消息，一项国际研究表明，小行星撞击产生了结构异常复杂的金刚石材料（</span></span><a href="https://phys.org/news/2022-07-asteroid-impacts-diamond-materials-exceptionally.html" style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank">Asteroid impacts create diamond materials with exceptionally complex structures</a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">）。</span><br></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">根据伦敦大学学院（</span><a href="https://www.ucl.ac.uk/news/2022/jul/asteroid-impacts-create-diamond-materials-exceptionally-complex-structures" target="_blank">UCL</a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）和匈牙利（</span>Hungarian<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）科学家领导的一项国际研究，小行星与地球碰撞引起的冲击波（</span>Shockwaves<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）会产生一系列复杂碳结构的材料，这些材料可以用于推进未来的工程应用。相关研究结果于</span>2022<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">年</span>7<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">月</span>19<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">日发表在《美国国家科学院院刊》（</span>Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）杂志网站——</span>Shock-formed carbon materials with intergrown sp<sup>3</sup>- and sp<sup>2</sup>- bonded nanostructured units. Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences, (2022). </span><a href="https://dx.doi.org/10.1073/pnas.2203672119" target="_blank" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">DOI: 10.1073/pnas.2203672119</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">. </span><a href="https://dx.doi.org/10.1073/pnas.2203672119" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">https://dx.doi.org/10.1073/pnas.2203672119</span></a></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">研究小组发现，大约</span>5<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">万年前小行星碰撞</span>(</span><a href="https://phys.org/tags/asteroid+collision/" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">asteroid collision</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">产生的高能冲击波形成的金刚石类</span>(</span><a href="https://phys.org/tags/diamonds/" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">diamonds</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">物质具有独特的特殊性质，这是由短期高温和极端压力造成的。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">研究人员表示，这些结构可用于先进的机械和电子应用，使我们能够设计出不仅超硬而且具有可调电子特性的可延展材料。</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">在这项研究中，来自英国（</span>UK<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）、美国（</span>US<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）、匈牙利（</span>Hungary<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）、意大利（</span>Italy<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）和法国（</span>France<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）的科学家们，使用了最先进的晶体学和光谱技术，对来自于</span>189<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">年在亚利桑那沙漠（</span><a href="https://www.ucl.ac.uk/news/2022/jul/asteroid-impacts-create-diamond-materials-exceptionally-complex-structures" target="_blank">Arizona desert</a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）中首次发现的迪亚波罗峡谷铁陨石（</span><a href="https://www.ucl.ac.uk/news/2022/jul/asteroid-impacts-create-diamond-materials-exceptionally-complex-structures" target="_blank">Canyon Diablo iron meteorite</a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）的朗斯代尔石</span>(mineral lonsdaleite)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">进行了详细的研究。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">朗斯代尔石以英国晶体学先驱、伦敦大学学院第一位女教授凯萨琳·朗斯代尔（</span>Dame Kathleen Lonsdale<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）的名字命名。此前，朗斯代尔石被认为是由纯六边形金刚石组成，有别于经典的立方金刚石（</span>classic cubic diamond<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）。然而，研究小组发现，它实际上是由纳米结构的金刚石和石墨烯类共生体</span>(<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">晶体中的两种矿物质一起生长</span>)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">组成的，称为</span><a href="https://www.ucl.ac.uk/news/2022/jul/asteroid-impacts-create-diamond-materials-exceptionally-complex-structures" target="_blank">diaphites</a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">。该团队还发现了堆叠错误，即原子层重复模式序列中的“错误”。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">上述论文的主要作者、</span>RCAS<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">地质和地球化学研究所（</span></span><a href="http://geochem.hu/index_en.html" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">Institute for Geological and Geochemical Research</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）彼得·内梅特（</span>P<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">é</span>ter N<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">é</span>meth<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）博士说：“通过识别石墨烯和金刚石结构之间的各种共生类型，我们可以更深入地了解小行星撞击期间发生的压力</span>-<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">温度条件。”</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">研究小组发现，由于金刚石和石墨烯界面上碳原子的特殊环境，石墨烯层（</span><a href="https://phys.org/tags/graphene+layers/" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">graphene layers</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）之间的距离是不寻常的。他们还证明，</span><a href="https://www.ucl.ac.uk/news/2022/jul/asteroid-impacts-create-diamond-materials-exceptionally-complex-structures" target="_blank">diaphite</a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">结构是导致先前无法解释的光谱特征的原因。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">该研究的合著者、</span>UCL<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">物理与天文学系（</span>UCL Physics & Astronomy<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）克里斯·霍华德（</span>Chris Howard<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）教授说：“这非常令人兴奋，因为我们现在可以使用简单的光谱技术检测金刚石中的</span>diaphite<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">结构，而无需昂贵而费力的电子显微镜。”</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">据科学家称，朗斯代尔石样品中所报告的结构单元和复杂性可发生在由冲击和静态压缩或气相沉积产生的其他多种含碳材料中。</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">研究合著者、</span>UCL<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">化学（</span>UCL Chemistry<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）克里斯托夫·萨尔兹曼（</span>Christoph Salzmann<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）教授说：“通过结构的可控层生长，应该可以设计出既超硬又有韧性的材料，并且从导体到绝缘体都具有可调的电子特性。因此，这一发现为具有令人兴奋的机械和电子特性的新型碳材料打开了大门，这些材料可能会产生新的应用，从磨料和电子产品到纳米医学和激光技术。”</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">“除了引起人们对所报道的碳结构的特殊机械和电子特性的注意之外，科学家们还对目前被称为朗斯代尔石的矿物的过于简单的结构观点提出了挑战。</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">研究人员还感谢已故合著者保罗·麦克米兰教授（</span>Paul McMillan<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">），他曾是</span>UCL<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">威廉·拉姆齐爵士（</span>Sir William Ramsay<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）的化学系主席，感谢他将团队召集在一起，感谢他对这项工作的不懈热情以及他对金刚石研究领域的持久贡献。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">上述介绍，仅供参考。欲了解更多信息，敬请注意浏览</span><a href="https://dx.doi.org/10.1073/pnas.2203672119" style="text-decoration: underline;
