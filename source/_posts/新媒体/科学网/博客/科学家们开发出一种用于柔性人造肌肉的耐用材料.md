font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">soft material</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">）考虑用作人造肌肉（</span><a href="https://phys.org/tags/artificial+muscle/" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">mechanical energy</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">），并在高应变条件下保持活性，这意味着它在重复工作循环后不会轻易失去形状和强度。虽然许多材料被认为是制造人造肌肉的竞争者，但具有高弹性能密度的介电弹性体（</span><a href="https://phys.org/news/2022-07-scientists-durable-material-flexible-artificial.html" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">dielectric elastomers</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">简称</span>DE<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）轻质材料因其最佳的柔韧性而备受关注。有视频显示高性能介电弹性体（</span>PHDE<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）薄膜和正在进行测试的致动器(详见<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><a href="https://sciencesources.eurekalert.org/multimedia/941068" target="_self">Fig.3</a>)</span></span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 18px;">。</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">介电弹性体是电活性聚合物，是由大分子组成的天然或合成物质，在电场（</span><a href="https://phys.org/tags/electric+field/" style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank">electric field</a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">）刺激下，大分子的大小或形状会发生变化。它们可以用作致动器，使机器能够通过将电能（</span><a href="https://phys.org/tags/electric+energy/" style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank">electric energy</a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">）转换为机械功来运行。</span><br></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">大多数介电弹性体由丙烯酸（</span><a href="https://phys.org/news/2022-07-scientists-durable-material-flexible-artificial.html" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">acrylic</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">）或硅树脂（</span><a href="https://phys.org/news/2022-07-scientists-durable-material-flexible-artificial.html" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">silicone</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）制成，但这两种材料都有缺点。虽然传统的丙烯酸介电弹性体（</span>acrylic DEs<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）可以实现高驱动应变，但它们需要预拉伸，缺乏灵活性。硅树脂类（</span></span><a href="https://phys.org/news/2022-07-scientists-durable-material-flexible-artificial.html" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">Silicones</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">）更容易制造，但它们不能承受高应变。</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">加州大学洛杉矶分校领导的研究团队利用商用化学品并采用紫外线（</span>UV<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）光固化工艺，开发出一种改进的丙烯酸基材料，该材料更柔韧、可调，更易于扩展，同时又不会失去强度和耐久性。虽然丙烯酸能形成更多氢键</span>(</span><a href="https://phys.org/tags/hydrogen+bonds/" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">hydrogen bonds</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">，从而使材料更易移动，但研究人员还调整了聚合物链之间的交联，使弹性体更柔软、更灵活。然后，将得到的薄、可加工、高性能介电弹性体膜（</span>PHDE<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）夹在两个电极之间，以将电能转换为运动作为致动器。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">每一层</span>PHDE<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">膜都像一根头发一样薄而轻，厚度约为</span>35 μm<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">，当多层膜堆叠在一起时，它们就变成了一个微型电动机，可以像肌肉组织一样工作，产生足够的能量来驱动小型机器人或传感器的运动。研究人员已经制作了一堆从</span>4<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">层到</span>50<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">层不等的</span>PHDE<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">薄膜。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">裴奇秉说：“这种灵活、多功能和高效的致动器可以为新一代机器人的人造肌肉打开大门，也可以为传感器和可穿戴技术打开大门，使其能够更精确地模拟甚至改善仿人运动和能力。”</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">上述图</span>2<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">（</span>Fig. 2<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）就是</span>UCLA<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">软材料研究实验室提供的一个直径约</span>1.2 cm<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">的跳跃机器人，装有</span>PHDE<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">致动器。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">安装</span>PHDE<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">致动器的人造肌肉可以产生比生物肌肉更多的兆帕力，并且它们也显示出比自然肌肉</span>3~10<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">倍的灵活性。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">多层软膜通常通过“湿法”工艺（</span><a href="https://phys.org/news/2022-07-scientists-durable-material-flexible-artificial.html" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">"wet" process</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）制造，该工艺涉及液体树脂的沉积和固化。但这一过程可能会导致不均匀的层，从而导致致动器性能不佳。因此，到目前为止，许多致动器仅在单层</span>DE<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">膜上获得成功。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">Dielectric elastomers are a type of electroactive polymer that can transform electrical energy into mechanical work. They are often made from silicone or acrylic elastomers. Silicone materials are easier to process but have lower strains, whereas acrylics require prestrectching to achieve high strains. Shi <em>et al</em>. designed a dielectric elastomer based on acrylates in which the selection of monomers, the use of both a short and long cross-linker molecules, and the ability to control hydrogen bonding enabled fine tuning of the stress-strain responses and viscoelasticity. The authors further developed a method to dry stack the elastomeric sheets without a loss of properties that is more compatible with current large-scale fabrication processes. —MSL</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "arial black", "avant garde";"><strong><a href="https://www.science.org/doi/10.1126/science.abn0099" target="_blank">Abstract</a></strong></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">Dielectric elastomers (DEs) can act as deformable capacitors that generate mechanical work in response to an electric field. DEs are often based on commercial acrylic and silicone elastomers. Acrylics require prestretching to achieve high actuation strains and lack processing flexibility. Silicones allow for processability and rapid response but produce much lower strains. In this work, a processable, high-performance dielectric elastomer (PHDE) with a bimodal network structure is synthesized, and its electromechanical properties are tailored by adjusting cross-linkers and hydrogen bonding within the elastomer network. The PHDE exhibits a maximum areal strain of 190% and maintains strains higher than 110% at 2 hertz without prestretching.
