<p style="margin-bottom: 8px;line-height: 150%;background: white;vertical-align: baseline"><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px">什么是修辞语言？用词语绘彩图</span></p><p style="margin-bottom: 8px;line-height: 150%;background: white;vertical-align: baseline"><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px">武夷山 编译</span></p><p style="margin-bottom: 8px;line-height: 150%;background: white;vertical-align: baseline"><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px"> </span></p><p style="line-height: 150%;background: white;vertical-align: baseline"><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px">    </span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px">Figurative language</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px">（修辞语言）指的是具有意义但不一定真实的词或短语。</span></p><p style="text-indent: 26px;line-height: 150%;background: white;vertical-align: baseline"><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px">如果你说</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px"> “That news hit me like a ton of bricks,”</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height: 150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px">（那条消息惊呆了我。字面意思是“那条消息像一吨砖头砸到我身上”），你就在使用修辞语言。 </span></p><p style="text-indent: 35px;line-height: 150%;background: white;vertical-align: baseline"><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px">同样，如果你说“He begged me to reconsider, but I had a heart of stone, and I refused”（他恳求我再考虑一下，但我心如铁石，断然拒绝）, 你也在使用修辞语言。 </span></p><p style="text-indent: 35px;line-height: 150%;background: white;vertical-align: baseline"><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px">有很多种使用修辞语言的方式，下面是几个常见类型。</span></p><p style="text-indent: 35px;line-height: 150%;background: white;vertical-align: baseline"><strong><span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;"><br></span></strong></p><p style="text-indent: 35px;line-height: 150%;background: white;vertical-align: baseline"><strong><span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; letter-spacing: 1px;">惯用语或成语</span></strong><br></p><p style="text-indent: 35px;line-height: 150%;background: white;vertical-align: baseline"><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px">惯用语这种表达是无法根据其每个单词的意义来理解的，它有自己独特的意义。许多惯用语（尽管不是全部）都是修辞语言的例子。</span></p><p style="text-indent: 35px;line-height: 150%;background: white;vertical-align: baseline"><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px">学生们离校时，老师对他们说“Hold your horses”（慢点走或悠着点。字面意思是“控制住你的马”）。</span></p><p style="margin-bottom: 5px;text-indent: 17px;line-height: 150%;background: white;vertical-align: baseline"><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px"> </span><strong><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:0"> </span></strong><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px">He was up in the air about whether to go to the concert.</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px">（是否去听音乐会，他还犹豫不决。字面意思是“在是否去听音乐会的问题上，他还悬在半空”）。 </span></p><p style="text-indent: 35px;line-height: 150%;background: white;vertical-align: baseline"><span style="font-size: 16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px">The dog ate the muffins in a flash. </span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing:1px">（狗迅速地吃掉了小松糕。字面意思是“狗电光石火般地吃掉了小松糕”）。 </span></p><p style="text-indent: 35px;line-height: 150%;background: white;vertical-align: baseline"><span style="font-size: 16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px">The senator was renowned for throwing his colleagues under the bus in election season. (</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px">这位参议员臭名昭著地惯于为了获得竞选优势而责备同事。字面意思是“这位参议员臭名昭著地惯于为了获得竞选优势而把同事扔到公交车底下去”)。 </span></p><p style="line-height: 150%;background: white;vertical-align: baseline"><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px"> </span></p><p style="line-height: 150%;background: white;vertical-align: baseline"><strong><span style="font-size:16px;line-height: 150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px">隐喻</span></strong><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px">（略。我曾写过“隐喻与明喻”，可参看：</span><a href="https://blog.sciencenet.cn/blog-1557-1333825.html" target="_blank"><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px">https://blog.sciencenet.cn/blog-1557-1333825.html</span></a><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px">）</span></p><p style="line-height: 150%;background: white;vertical-align: baseline"><strong><span style="font-size:16px;line-height: 150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px">明喻</span></strong><span style="font-size:16px;line-height: 150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px">（略）</span></p><p style="line-height: 150%;background: white;vertical-align: baseline"><strong><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:0"> </span></strong></p><p style="line-height: 150%;background: white;vertical-align: baseline"><strong><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:0">夸张</span></strong></p><p style="line-height: 150%;background: white;vertical-align: baseline"><strong><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:0">    </span></strong><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:0">夸张经常表达的是不可能或近乎不可能。</span></p><p style="text-indent: 34px;line-height: 150%;background: white;vertical-align: baseline"><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing:1px">She </span><a href="https://www.merriam-webster.com/words-at-play/very-actually-and-other-examples-of-semantic-bleaching" target="_blank"><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;color:windowtext;letter-spacing:1px">literally</span></a><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px"> was swimming in money. (</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px">她是有钱得不得了。字面意思是“她简直是在钱里面游泳”)。 </span></p><p style="text-indent: 34px;line-height: 150%;background: white;vertical-align: baseline"><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing:1px">Tickets sold out at light speed.</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px">（票顷刻售罄。字面意思是“票以光速卖光了”）。</span></p><p style="margin-bottom: 9px;text-indent: 38px;line-height: 150%;background: white;vertical-align: baseline"><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px">The show went on forever. (</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing:1px">这场演出持续了很久。字面意思是“这场演出似乎在永久进行)。 </span></p><p style="margin-bottom: 9px;line-height: 150%;background: white;vertical-align: baseline"><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing:1px"> </span></p><p style="margin-bottom: 9px;line-height: 150%;background: white;vertical-align: baseline"><strong><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px">拟人</span></strong></p><p style="margin-bottom: 9px;text-indent: 38px;line-height: 150%;background: white;vertical-align: baseline"><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px">We saw lights dance in the distance</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px">（我们看见远方有灯火闪烁。字面意思“我们看见远方有灯火在跳舞”。跳舞的主体应该是人。）</span></p><p style="line-height: 150%;background: white;vertical-align: baseline"><strong><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:0">    </span></strong><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px">The wind was howling all night. </span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px">（风整夜哀嚎。哀嚎的主体应该是人）。 </span></p><p style="text-indent: 35px;line-height: 150%;background: white;vertical-align: baseline"><span style="font-size: 16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px">The words jumped off the page. (</span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family: 宋体;letter-spacing:1px">这些生动的词语跃然纸上。字面意思是“这些词语从页面上蹦了下来”)。有意思的是，这个表达与中文的“跃然纸上”几乎完全同构。</span></p><p style="text-indent: 35px;line-height: 150%;background: white;vertical-align: baseline"><span style="font-size: 16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px"> </span></p><p style="line-height: 150%;background: white;vertical-align: baseline"><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px">    </span><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px">采用修辞语言可以使作者既显风趣，又能有效地将信息传递给读者。修辞语言给作家提供了用词语绘出彩图的工具。</span></p><p style="line-height: 150%;background: white;vertical-align: baseline"><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px"> </span></p><p style="margin-bottom: 8px;line-height: 150%;background: white;vertical-align: baseline"><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px">资料出处：</span><a href="https://www.merriam-webster.com/words-at-play/figurative-language" target="_blank"><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px">https://www.merriam-webster.com/words-at-play/figurative-language</span></a><span style="font-size:16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体;letter-spacing:1px">?</span></p><p style="line-height:150%"><span style="font-size: 16px;line-height:150%;font-family:宋体"> </span></p><p><br></p>                    <br><br>
