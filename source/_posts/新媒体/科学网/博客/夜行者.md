padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(13, 140, 166); font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); visibility: visible; line-height: 3em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #407600; visibility: visible; font-size: 20px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">昨夜我遇见了夜行者，他走进了茫茫的夜色之中。我只看见他的背影，模模糊糊，影影绰绰，有浓有淡。他有时就和黑夜融为一体，仿佛是把自己隐匿在了黑夜之中。有时候，因为他不停地行走，又把他的轮廓从黑色的、稠密的夜色中隔离出来。</span></p><p style="margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(13, 140, 166); font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); visibility: visible; line-height: 3em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #407600; visibility: visible; font-size: 20px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">夜行者的脚会踩上石头和荆棘，虽然坚硬厚实的土地是在他的脚下，这些石头和荆棘也会戳伤他的脚。但夜行者不能停止他的行走，他必须不停地走，因为他是夜行者。</span></p><p style="margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(13, 140, 166); font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); visibility: visible; line-height: 3em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #407600; visibility: visible; font-size: 20px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">夜行者会在夜晚遇见什么呢？鸟儿睡了，鱼儿也睡了吗？河流淙淙，是的，没有一条河流会在夜晚睡去，河流也像夜行者一样，永远不停息。夜行者于是就伴着河流，不停地行走。黑漆漆的夜里，河水会泛起微光，稀释黑色的夜。当河流泛起微光的时候，河岸上的小草也会轻轻地摇曳，仿佛与夜行者打着招呼。河岸上的树枝也会轻轻招手，给夜行者无言的抚慰。树上鸟窝里熟睡的鸟儿，在它的梦里遇见了不停行走着的夜行者。</span></p><p style="margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(13, 140, 166); font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); visibility: visible; line-height: 3em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #407600; visibility: visible; font-size: 20px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">夜行者从来都不会离开河流，如果那条河不在他的身旁，他会把它放在心里。夜行者或者伴着一条河，或者在心里装着一条河，行走，永远都不停息地行走。</span></p><p style="margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(13, 140, 166); font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); visibility: visible; line-height: 3em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #407600; visibility: visible; font-size: 20px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">夜行者可以停下他的脚步吗？如果夜行者停了下来，他就不是夜行者了。没有人会特别在乎，夜行者是不是一个人孤独地行走。在夜里，大多数人都会进入甜蜜的梦乡，那些没有进入梦乡的人，也会在温暖的卧室里就着明亮的灯光看电脑或者看手机。在这处处灯光和电网的时代，人们无暇顾及夜行者。夜行者只是遥远的黑影。</span></p><p style="margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(13, 140, 166); font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); line-height: 3em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #407600; font-size: 20px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">夜行者还是走在夜色里，是谁让夜行者成为夜行者的呢？或者就是美丽而明亮的白昼，是太阳他自己，让夜行者走进夜色，在圆圆的地球上，夜行者的脚步在丈量着，那是黑夜遇见白昼的距离么？</span></p><p style="margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(13, 140, 166); font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); line-height: 3em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #407600; font-size: 20px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">夜行者在黑夜里看到山峦的起伏，沉寂、稠密、模糊、高大，还有温柔，有谁知道夜的温情，也是这世界打开的小窗呢？夜行者走进荒原，走进旷野，他看见无边的旷野，那铺展开来的旷野，像是在表面上端着浅浅深深的黑夜，充满了神秘和广阔。夜行者在夜里行走，遇见了月亮升起的草地，草地上玲珑的小花，她们在黑夜里嬉戏，摇头晃脑，忘乎所以。当她们发现了夜行者，就羞涩地笑了，拘谨了一会儿。夜行者把自己藏在一颗树的后面，偷看着那些花儿再次高兴地玩耍。原来黑夜也拥有一切，夜行者就把这些花儿也装在了自己的心里。</span></p><p style="margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(13, 140, 166); font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); line-height: 3em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #407600; font-size: 20px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">夜行者走在旷野里，走在石头和荆棘之上，走在小路上，走在大路上，夜行者走在所有有路和没有路的地方。</span></p><p style="margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(13, 140, 166); font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); line-height: 3em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #407600; font-size: 20px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">夜行者心里装着河流，装着月亮和太阳，装着他看见过的花儿。夜行者独自走在黑色的夜里，夜行者知道还有很多的夜行者。夜行者没法结伴，但夜行者会把踩出来的路标记给另一个夜行者。</span></p><p style="margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(13, 140, 166); font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); line-height: 3em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; color: #407600; font-size: 20px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">每一个清晨，当鸟儿们睡醒之后，他们在树梢上啁啾歌唱，歌唱清晨和旭日，歌唱春天和秋叶，它们的歌曲里有梦中的夜行者。可它们却从来没有在黎明看到一个夜行者。夜行者的使命，就是在黑夜里行走。当黎明来临的时候，谁也认不出他们。如果我是一只会歌唱的鸟，我也从来没有在白天认出过夜行者。</span></p><p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202203/22/105857rjn6d2wfwktdlml2.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202203/22/105857rjn6d2wfwktdlml2.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><br></p>                    <br><br>
