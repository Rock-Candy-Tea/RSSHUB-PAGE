style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">近年来</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">在</span><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">“实践转向”的潮流中，</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">学术界已基本认同</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">知识</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">不仅是</span><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">“知”，也</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">包含</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">“</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">行</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">”</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:37px"><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">0-1是知识由知到行的第一步，主要是个人直觉式创新和少数人（包括天使轮投资者）在讨论中</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">逐步</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">形成的对于这一创新初步明晰的解释，是一种超常型隐性知识，并开始向超常型编码知识转化。（</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 14px;">方竹兰、于畅，知识经济与宏观管理新视角</span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';color: #0000FF"><a href="https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s/TIxXN_Q2xUPmcfAjNv55rw" target="_blank">https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s/TIxXN_Q2xUPmcfAjNv55rw</a></span><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312; font-size: 19px;">）</span><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312; font-size: 19px;">“1”高度浓缩、普适。</span></p><p style="text-indent:37px"><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">1-100，是知识之流，是“1”的延伸、拓展，同时也就是</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">逐步</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">嵌入于特定的语境和场景之中、地方化，物化或成为各种应用软件，直至最终为不同消费者在形形色色的</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">场景</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">中使用。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:37px"><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">前期是推理式创新，以超常型编码知识及其进一步完善为主。后期是扩散式创新，逐步演变为常规型知识，对应于人的</span><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">“无差别劳动”，同时逐步服务于特定对象，嵌入于特定场景，也就是形成地方性知识。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:37px"><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">总体而言，在知识论视野下，</span><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">0-1因位于知识流的上游乃至源头，拥有在先者对于在后者的权力，甚至生杀予夺。1-100的价值在于，充分挖掘和阐发0-1高度浓缩普适的超常型编码知识的内涵，使之转化为常规和地方性知识，</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">既</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">融入到知识整体之中，</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">又与形形色色无穷无尽的场景相结合，与社会相结合，</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">从而巩固和扩大新的产业链在产业整体</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">乃至整个社会</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">中的地位和作用。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:37px"><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">整个创新流程中的知识流，表现为隐性知识外显为在不同程度上拥有产权的地方性知识，以及超常型知识转化为常规型知识。其中，</span><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">0-1的超常型隐性知识在产业链的知识创新过程中发挥了决定性作用。1-100体现了知识的物化和社会化。知识的物化，指知识转化为具有某种物质形态的商品和服务，包括软硬件，以及所获得的利润和高附加值；知识的社会化意味着知识以地方化等方式和途径融入社会之中。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:37px"><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">简言之，</span></span><strong><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312;font-size: 19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">由</span><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">0-1到1-100，知识由超常到常规，隐性到编码，普适到地方</span></span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312;font-size: 19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">，</span></span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312;font-size: 19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">知识物化、硬件化，进而黑箱化</span></span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312;font-size: 19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">。</span></span></strong></p><p style="text-indent:37px"><strong><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312;font-size: 19px;background: #FFFF00"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">比产业的</span><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">0-1更重要的是，培育0-1的土壤，观念和制度的0-1，以孕育更多和层出不穷的0-1。</span></span></strong></p><p style="text-indent:37px"><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">0-1之“1”，不等于1-100之“1”。由前者到后者，科学家鲜有成功者，主要依靠有远见卓识的企业家。</span></span></p><p style="margin-top:10px;margin-bottom:10px;text-indent:37px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric"><strong><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312;font-size: 19px"><span href="https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s/IB3HrPKXwB6PVDQz08ifjA" target="_blank"><span style="text-decoration:underline;"><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312;color: #0000FF;font-size: 19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">确切可以归入</span><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">1的是第2、3两个阶段，</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span
