style="font-family:宋体">复合物对</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">NTpS</span><span style="font-family:宋体">去磷酸化的效率显著高于单独的</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">PP1C</span><span style="font-family:宋体">复合物中</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">PP1C</span><span style="font-family:宋体">和</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">SHOC2</span><span style="font-family:宋体">两个相邻的疏水区域并置可能会与</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">NTpS</span><span style="font-family:宋体">周围的氨基酸残基结合，从而提供选择性。</span></span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:150%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">增加</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">MRAS</span><span style="font-family:宋体">、</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">SHOC2</span><span style="font-family:宋体">和</span><span style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:150%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span </span></span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:150%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">另一个问题是，除了</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">RAF</span><span style="font-family:宋体">蛋白，</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">SMP</span><span </span></span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:150%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span
