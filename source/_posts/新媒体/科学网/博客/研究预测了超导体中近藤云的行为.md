<p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: 黑体, SimHei;">研究预测了超导体中近藤云的行为</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">诸平</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"> <a href="https://scx2.b-cdn.net/gfx/news/2021/study-predicts-the-beh.jpg" target="_blank"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202111/25/212830u6evw54sf61d4ofz.jpg" title alt="Snap1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><a href="https://phys.org/news/2021-11-behavior-kondo-cloud-superconductor.html" target="_blank"><span style="font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 18px;">Credit: Paşcu Moca.</span></a><br></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">据物理学家组织网（</span><a href="https://phys.org/news/2021-11-behavior-kondo-cloud-superconductor.html" target="_blank"><span style="font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 18px;">Phys.org</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）</span><span style="font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 18px;">2021</span><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">年</span><span style="font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 18px;">11</span><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">月</span><span style="font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 18px;">23</span><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">日报道，近年来，世界各地的许多物理学家都在研究混合纳米结构的行为。这些系统通常由两种或两种以上的材料组成。在这类结构中特别注意与超导和正常金属接触相互作用的磁性杂质。</span><br></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">过去的研究发现，当金属含有磁性杂质（</span><a href="https://phys.org/tags/magnetic+impurities/" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">magnetic impurities</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">）时，传导电子可以形成屏蔽云，本质上屏蔽了杂质的自旋。这种物理现象（</span><a href="https://phys.org/tags/physical+phenomenon/" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">physical phenomenon</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）被称为近藤效应（</span><a href="https://phys.org/news/2021-11-behavior-kondo-cloud-superconductor.html" target="_blank">Kondo effect</a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）</span>;<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">因此，产生的云被称为近藤云（</span><a href="https://phys.org/news/2021-11-behavior-kondo-cloud-superconductor.html" target="_blank">Kondo cloud</a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-family: 宋体;">虽然近藤云在正常系统中的行为已经被很好地理解，但它在超导材料存在时的性质还尚未被深入地探索。到目前为止，大多数物理学家认为杂化纳米结构中杂质自旋的筛选主要发生在筛选的量子相中，而不是在未筛选的量子相中。然而，匈牙利布达佩斯科技经济大学（</span>Budapest University of Technology and Economics in Hungary<span style="font-family: 宋体;">）和波兰亚当·米凯维奇大学（</span>Adam Mickiewicz University in Poland<span style="font-family: 宋体;">）的研究人员最近发现，虽然近藤状态不是在未屏蔽相中形成的，但近藤云（</span>Kondo </span><a href="https://phys.org/tags/clouds/" style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank">clouds</a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">）同时存在于屏蔽和未屏蔽的量子相中。</span><br></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">进行这项研究的研究人员之一伊勒内乌斯·魏曼（</span>Ireneusz Weymann<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）告诉</span><a href="https://phys.org/news/2021-11-behavior-kondo-cloud-superconductor.html" target="_blank">Phys.org</a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">工作人员说：“当一个人考虑单个自旋</span><em>S</em>=1/2<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">的杂质，比如一个量子点或一个分子，附着在金属接触点上，一个相关的近藤态在足够低的温度下在这样的系统中形成。在这种状态下，杂质的自旋被传导电子屏蔽，在杂质周围形成一个相关云</span>(<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">即所谓的近藤云</span>)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">。”</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">虽然一些研究人员检查了近藤云的特性，但直到最近才有团队开始对这些系统进行实验探索。其中最著名的一项实验研究（</span><a href="https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-020-2058-6" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">notable experimental studies</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）是由中国香港城市大学</span>(City University of Hong Kong)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">的</span>Ivan V. Borzenets<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">和他在世界各地其他研究所的同事进行的,详见<a href="https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-020-2058-6#auth-Ivan-V__Borzenets" style="white-space: normal; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank">Ivan V. Borzenets</a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">, </span><a href="https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-020-2058-6#auth-Jeongmin-Shim" style="white-space: normal; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank">Jeongmin Shim</a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">, </span><a href="https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-020-2058-6#auth-Jason_C__H_-Chen" style="white-space: normal; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank">Jason C. H. Chen</a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">, </span><a href="https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-020-2058-6#auth-Arne-Ludwig" style="white-space: normal; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank">Arne Ludwig</a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">, </span><a href="https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-020-2058-6#auth-Andreas_D_-Wieck" style="white-space: normal; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank">Andreas D. Wieck</a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">, </span><a href="https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-020-2058-6#auth-Seigo-Tarucha" style="white-space: normal; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank">Seigo Tarucha</a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">, </span><a href="https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-020-2058-6#auth-H__S_-Sim" style="white-space: normal; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank">H.-S. Sim</a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">, </span><a href="https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-020-2058-6#auth-Michihisa-Yamamoto" style="white-space: normal; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank">Michihisa Yamamoto</a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">. Observation of the Kondo screening cloud. </span><a href="https://www.nature.com/" style="white-space: normal; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank">Nature</a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">, 2020, 579: 210–213. </span><a href="http://dx.doi.org/10.1038/s41586-020-2058-6" target="_blank" style="white-space: normal; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">DOI: 10.1038/s41586-020-2058-6</a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">. </span><a href="https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-020-2058-6#article-info" style="white-space: normal; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank">Published: 11 March 2020</a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">. </span><a href="http://dx.doi.org/10.1038/s41586-020-2058-6" style="white-space: normal; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank">http://dx.doi.org/10.1038/s41586-020-2058-6</a>.</span></span><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-family: 宋体;">伊勒内乌斯·魏曼及其同事</span>2021<span style="font-family: 宋体;">年</span>10<span style="font-family: 宋体;">月</span>27<span style="font-family: 宋体;">日发表在《物理评论快报》(</span></span><a href="http://dx.doi.org/10.1103/PhysRevLett.127.186804" style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank">Physical Review Letters</a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-family: 宋体;">)的论文——</span>Cătălin Paşcu Moca, Ireneusz Weymann, Miklós Antal Werner, Gergely Zaránd. Kondo Cloud in a Superconductor. </span><a href="http://dx.doi.org/10.1103/PhysRevLett.127.186804" style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank">Physical Review Letters</a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">, 2021, 127: 186804. Published 27 October 2021. </span><a href="http://dx.doi.org/10.1103/PhysRevLett.127.186804" target="_blank" style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">DOI: 10.1103/PhysRevLett.127.186804</a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">. </span><a href="http://dx.doi.org/10.1103/PhysRevLett.127.186804" style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank">http://dx.doi.org/10.1103/PhysRevLett.127.186804</a>, <span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">是建立在之前的工作的基础上，阐明了超导体中的近藤云的行为。</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">伊勒内乌斯·魏曼解释道：“理解各种状态的空间扩展对于理解强电子相关性的基本方面是很重要的，这与各种相关材料有关。”</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">伊勒内乌斯·魏曼和他的同事最近的工作是纯理论性质的，因为他们采用了最先进的数值和解析重整群方法。这些技术使他们能够准确地预测超导材料内的近藤云的行为。</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">伊勒内乌斯·魏曼说</span>:<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">“当基态在近藤态和柴叶态（</span>Shiba state<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）之间发生变化时，我们所考虑的系统表现出量子相变。到目前为止，人们一直认为，筛查发生在近藤阶段。然而，相当引人注目的是，我们已经证明了近藤云也存在于未屏蔽的阶段。”</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">这组研究人员进行的分析和计算的结果还表明，近藤云的性质是普遍的，并在所谓的量子临界点表现出特征跳跃。在未来，他们的工作可以指导一系列直接研究超导体中近藤云特性的实验。</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">“我们现在计划将我们的工作扩展到更复杂的系统，在那里可以实现更奇异的基态，”伊勒内乌斯·魏曼补充说。</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px;
