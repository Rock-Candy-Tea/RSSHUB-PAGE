<p> </p><p style="text-indent:28px">   <span style="font-size: 20px;"> 做研究难免要写论文，走上这条路开始是生活所迫，到现在已经变成一种习惯，甚至某种程度上也变成了一种兴趣，写论文还是有一些乐趣的：</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">1.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">     </span>如果提出某种新的思想或理论，必然有一些新的概念，你可以自己命名，就像给自己的孩子起名字一样。</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">2.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">     </span>提出某个研究的问题或角度，看着它慢慢发展壮大，有越来越多的学者开展研究，比如宏观层面的创新质量，有点在丛中笑的味道了。</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">3.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">     </span>看到论文被新华文摘、人大复印、国研网等等转载，或者收录到某个有难度的数据库。</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">4.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">     </span>有时会很诧异地被评一个论文奖，尽管自己没有申报，所谓奖励级别也不高，挺好！干吗论文评奖要自己申报呢？</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">5.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">     </span>投稿时得到审稿专家的肯定，真的欣赏的那一种，心有灵犀的感觉真好。</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">6.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">     </span>被退稿多次，终于录用了。</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">7.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">     </span>看到自己的论文被期刊推荐为首篇论文，或者封面标题论文。</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">8.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">     </span>从开始有想法，到最后码字完成，看着排版，看着图、文，有种艺术家欣赏自己作品的感觉。</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">9.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">     </span>年终整理团队成果，难免要看看引用，看到高被引论文还是很欣慰的。</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">10.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">  </span>收录用稿通知，这是平时最开心的事情了。</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">11.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">  </span>用论文表明自己的探索性正确观点，岂止伟大？</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">12.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">  </span>开会与同仁交流，讨论你的某篇论文，对方提出建设性建议时，不是那种礼节性表扬你那种。</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">13.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">  </span>开会就某个选题做报告，有时会被自己的研究感动。</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">14.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">  </span>收到纸质期刊时。</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px"><span style="font-size: 20px;">15.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 9px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Times New Roman";">  </span>看到媒体某个热点引用自己的论文，或者推介自己的论文。</span></p><p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left:52px;text-indent:0"><span style="font-size: 20px;">下次写一篇博客，吐槽写论文。</span></p><p><br></p><p><span style="font-size: 20px;">                                                                    2021.4.7 俞立平 于杭州</span></p>                    <br><br>
