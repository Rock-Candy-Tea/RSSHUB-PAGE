<p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">2021-08-12</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">，星期四</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体">中国科学院动物研究所，牛泽清博士</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体">昨晚处理完所有事，确实有些晚了，我也累得倒头睡了。</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体">今天计划要从改则县返程到班戈县，全程大约</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">670</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">公里，海拔</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">4500</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">米左右。可想而知，今天又是一个艰苦的旅行。</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体">早上</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">8</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">点半，我们都已在住宿的酒店吃完了早餐，徐颖结完住宿的账。</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">8</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">点</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">50</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">分，大家分乘三辆车，驶离改则县城，沿</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">G317</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">国道，先向途中的尼玛县开进。</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202108/13/095231br4x6mxxbs66nbms.jpeg" title alt="2021-08-12-改则县-1.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p><p style="text-align: center; font-size: medium; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px;"><font face="楷体">准备出发</font></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体">上午</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">10</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">点</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">40</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">分，由于区间限速，我们停下车来等待时间。小吴趁此和金梁、宏亮将索朗师傅车上的所有标本归移到小吴的车上，明天我和小吴将带上标本奔安多县，其他队员去拉萨，所以得提前将标本归整好。归整时才知，徐颖早上结账时将她的手机丢在了酒店前台，江白师傅不得不开车回去跟徐颖取手机。唉，真是忙中出乱啊。等到大约上午</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">11</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">点半，江白师傅他们才回到我们停车的地方。小吴又忙了一阵，将江白师傅车上的标本也归移到他的车上。这才算是彻底整理好了所有东西，我们一起继续赶路。</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202108/13/095248mpp5esudc09ususp.jpeg" title alt="2021-08-12-改则县-2.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p><p style="text-align: center; font-size: medium; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px;"><font face="楷体">规整行李和样品</font></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体">车行不一会儿，我们一行就到了那曲地曲的地界了，但海拔一直维持在</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">4500-4700</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">米，也不见降低。到下午</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">2</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">点半左右，我们离尼玛县城大约还有</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">10</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">多公里，又一次停下车来，防止区间超速。小吴跑到坡上去采甲虫，总是利用一切可利用的时间采集标本。他真是一个敬业的人！</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202108/13/095326cgi3uuuph53a3g3y.jpeg" title alt="2021-08-12-尼玛县-2.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p><p style="text-align: center; font-size: medium; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px;"><font face="楷体">山坡寻甲</font></p><p style="font-size: medium; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px;"><font face="楷体"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202108/13/095402fcwjwppllzjuwkbl.jpeg" title alt="2021-08-12-尼玛县-1.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></font></p><p style="font-size: medium; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px; text-align: center;"><font face="楷体">高原生境</font></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体">下午</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">3</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">点</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">10</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">分，限速时间已到，可我们才刚到尼玛县，离班戈县还有</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">360</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">公里的路程呢。也没来得及在尼玛县吃午饭，大家只吃了几口零食，我们一行就一直赶路，想早点到达班戈县。</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体">也许是昨天太累，我今早从改则县出来，就感到胃里一直难受，路上我捂着肚子，强忍着，坚持着。小吴见我难受的样子，以为我感冒了。我知道小吴开车更累，告他没事，让他专注开车。他虽不说，但从他总嚼口香糖、也不时吸支烟就可看出他也在强打着精神呢。我真不想让他为我而分心。</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体">晚上</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">7</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">点，我们终于到了班戈县城。县城住宿管控疫情很严格，我们每个人填了知情单、出示了行程大数据信息才办妥了入住。小吴招呼大家去街上吃晚饭了，我有些难受，也没去吃晚饭。</span></p><p><span style="font-size: 16px;font-family: 楷体">小吴回来，专门给我买了吃的，他总是爱关心身边的人，我真心地谢谢他。</span></p><p><br></p>                    <br><br>
