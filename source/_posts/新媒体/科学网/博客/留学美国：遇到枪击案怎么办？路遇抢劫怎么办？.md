<p class="p1" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Helvetica Neue"; white-space: normal;"><strong><span style="font-size: 16px;">        </span></strong><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"><strong><span style="font-size: 16px;">遇到枪击案怎么办？</span></strong><span style="font-size: 16px;">这是我们学校师生每年必须要参加的培训。今天我们馆办公室邀请了学校的警察给我们馆员工作现场培训，回答问题，并带大家绕馆一周，实地考察各个现场，学会如何使用培训课学到的知识和技能逃生。我把这个过程和要点写下来，给留学生和访问学者参考。</span></span></p><p class="p1" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Helvetica Neue"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">        没在枪支泛滥的国家生活过的个人，对于公共场所如何逃生会比较陌生。我在微信上问了一位国内的亲友，他告诉我的第一反应就是躲藏起来。躲也是一个办法，但不是首选，根据我校警察制作的培训视频和“逃跑-躲藏-反击”（run-hide-fight）三字箴言，第一反应应该是逃跑。</span></p><p class="p1" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Helvetica Neue"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"><strong><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai;">1.逃跑（Run）</span></strong></span></p><p class="p1" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Helvetica Neue"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">a. 听到枪响，脑子里迅速产生一个逃生路线和计划。</span></p><p class="p1" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Helvetica Neue"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">b. 留下您的物品赶紧逃跑，钱财身外之物，命要紧。</span></p><p class="p1" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Helvetica Neue"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">c. 自己的手要在可见的位置。</span></p><p class="p1" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Helvetica Neue"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"><strong><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai;">2.躲藏（Hide）</span></strong></span></p><p class="p1" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Helvetica Neue"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">a. 躲在攻击者视线之外的区域。</span></p><p class="p1" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Helvetica Neue"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">b. 锁上门阻止枪手进入您的藏身之处。</span></p><p class="p1" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Helvetica Neue"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">c. 把随身的手机或任何其他制造噪音的设备静音。</span></p><p class="p1" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Helvetica Neue"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"><strong><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai;">3. 反击（Fight）</span></strong></span></p><p class="p1" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Helvetica Neue"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">a. 随时准备反击保护自己。</span></p><p class="p1" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Helvetica Neue"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">b. 反击作为最后的手段，并且仅在自己的生命处于迫在眉睫的危险中时使用。</span></p><p class="p1" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Helvetica Neue"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">c. 反击的效果以使射手失去能力为目标。</span></p><p class="p1" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Helvetica Neue"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">d. 使用物件攻击并向射手投掷物品。</span></p><p class="p1" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Helvetica Neue"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">1-2-3后，在安全的情况下拨打 911报警。<br></span></p><p class="p1" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Helvetica Neue"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"><strong><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai;">提供给 911 或执法部门的信息：</span></strong></span></p><p class="p1" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Helvetica Neue"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">• 活跃射手的位置。</span></p><p class="p1" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Helvetica Neue"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">• 射手数量。</span></p><p class="p1" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Helvetica Neue"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">• 射手的身体描述。</span></p><p class="p1" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Helvetica Neue"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">• 使用的武器数量和类型。</span></p><p class="p1" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Helvetica Neue"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">• 该地点的潜在受害者人数。</span></p><p class="p1" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Helvetica Neue"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"><strong><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai;">当执法人员到达时</span></strong><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai;">：</span></span></p><p class="p1" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Helvetica Neue"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">• 保持冷静并遵循指示。</span></p><p class="p1" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Helvetica Neue"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">• 放下手中的任何物品。</span></p><p class="p1" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Helvetica Neue"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">• 举手并张开手指。</span></p><p class="p1" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Helvetica Neue"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">• 始终保持双手可见。</span></p><p class="p1" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Helvetica Neue"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">• 避免向警察快速移动，例如为了自己安全而去抓住警察。</span></p><p class="p1" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Helvetica Neue"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">• 避免指指点点、尖叫或大喊大叫。</span></p><p class="p1" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Helvetica Neue"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">• 疏散时不要停下来向警官寻求帮助或指示。</span></p><p class="p2" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Helvetica Neue"; color: rgb(220, 161, 13); white-space: normal;"><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 16px; font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">        有关更多信息，请访问美国国土安全部如何应对枪击案 <a href="https://www.dhs.gov/active-shooter-preparedness" target="_blank">https://www.dhs.gov/active-shooter-preparedness</a><br></span></p><p class="p1" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Helvetica Neue"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai;">        <strong>遇到抢劫怎么办？</strong></span><span style="font-family: 楷体, 楷体_GB2312, SimKai; font-size: 16px;">最好的方法是举手投降，放下财物，把钱包递给他，并要求歹徒不要开枪。在美国，因为大家都有枪，你若拔枪，或做出掏东西的动作，歹徒以为你要拔枪，他会立刻开枪打你。也不建议把东西丢在地上转身就跑，因为歹徒会从背后开枪。对于背包的行人，应该举起双手，然后将包递给歹徒，在这种时刻，不要在意你的外表，生命是最重要的。建议民众做好一些最基本的心里准备，如果不幸遇到抢劫，要服从歹徒的命令。比如说一个人刚买了新车，歹徒要他的车子，如果说车主没做好心理准备的话，他会反抗，这样就很容易造成身体上的伤害。</span></span></p><p class="p1" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "Helvetica Neue"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">        另外，根据FBI的统计，<strong>引起枪击案前6位的因素</strong>分别如下：62%的枪手患有精神障碍，49%的枪手是财务状况出了问题，35%则是工作出了问题比如被炒鱿鱼了，29%是和朋友同事发生争执，27%是由于婚姻出了问题，22%是吸毒酗酒上瘾。<br></span></p><p class="p1" style="margin-top: 0px;
