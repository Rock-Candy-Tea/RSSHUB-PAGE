<p style="text-indent:24px;line-height:150%"><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">在整个全新世，胡焕庸线并不是一成不变的，也就是</span>EASM<span style="font-family:宋体">的深入内陆的最前沿边界也是变化的。当</span><span CE</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">），胡焕庸线就大致稳定在现今的位置。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:24px;line-height:150%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px;vertical-align:super"><span style="font-family:Calibri">1</span></span></sup><sup><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px;vertical-align:super">8</span></sup><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px">O</span><span style="text-indent:24px;line-height:150%"><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">研究石笋生长的降雨量，还可以用其生长</span></span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Calibri">—</span><span style="font-family:宋体">降水。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:24px;line-height:150%"><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">我们来举个例子，比如对于</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Calibri">8</span></span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px">.2ka<span style=";font-family:Symbol;line-height:150%;font-size:16px">d</span><sup><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px;vertical-align:super"><span style="font-family:Calibri">1</span></span></sup><sup><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px;vertical-align:super">8</span></sup><span style=";font-family:Symbol;line-height:150%;font-size:16px">d</span><sup><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px;vertical-align:super"><span style="font-family:Calibri">1</span></span></sup><sup><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px;vertical-align:super">8</span></sup><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:150%;font-size:16px">O</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">并不是纯的夏季风记录。</span></span></p><p 24px;line-height: 150%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">全新世适宜期（</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">H</span></span><span
