<p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">电休克治疗（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">electroconvulsive therapy, ECT</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）引起患者的杏仁核与海马回体积增大，是脑成像领域已知的最稳健效应之一（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Joshi et al., 2016</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）。脑区亚结构细分方面的最新进展，能够对脑结构和</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">ECT</span><span style="font-family:宋体">临床结果之间的关系提供新的见解。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">这个研究领域频频有论文发表，积累的文献已经能够进行元分析综述。有研究者（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Gryglewski et al., 2021</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）专门评估</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">ECT</span><span style="font-family:宋体">对抑郁症患者的脑体积效应，内容包括杏仁核、尾状核、海马回及其亚区的最近发现，以及与临床改善的可能相关。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">与以前元分析基于标准化均值差异（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">standardized mean difference</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）的计算相反，该文作者依赖前后</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">ECT</span><span style="font-family:宋体">研究的重复测量设计，使用标准化均值变化分数（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">standardized mean change scores</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）。据此，由变化的方差来标准化效应量，从而，这样的效应量不受被试间均值差异的影响。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">该文作者对</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">PubMed</span><span style="font-family:宋体">进行全面搜索，但只包括英文发表的完整论文，即，不包括摘要、海报、综述、评论或通讯。涉及的研究是采用磁共振成像技术，考察</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">ECT</span><span style="font-family:宋体">对抑郁症患者（重度抑郁症或躁郁症）脑结构的纵向效应，也就是至少有两个时间点（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">ECT</span><span style="font-family:宋体">一个疗程的前与后）。最后确定的分析数据来自</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">21</span><span style="font-family:宋体">项研究（共有</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">543</span><span style="font-family:宋体">名抑郁症患者），包括</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">6</span><span style="font-family:宋体">项（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">118</span><span style="font-family:宋体">名患者）海马回亚区研究。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">海马回（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">hippocampus</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）是大脑边缘系统的一部分，左侧和右侧各有一个，每个均可分为齿状回（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">dentate gyrus</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）、海马（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">cornu ammonis, CA</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）、下托（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">subiculum</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）等结构。依据细胞形态及皮质发育差异，海马又分为</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">CA 1</span><span style="font-family:宋体">、</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">CA 2</span><span style="font-family:宋体">、</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">CA 3</span><span style="font-family:宋体">共</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">3</span><span style="font-family:宋体">个扇形区。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">该文作者进行元分析的脑区有</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">7</span><span style="font-family:宋体">个，即海马回、杏仁核、尾状核，以及海马亚区</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">CA 1</span><span style="font-family:宋体">（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">cornu ammonis 1</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）、</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">CA 2/3</span><span style="font-family:宋体">、齿状回、下托。为了能够计算效应量，涉及的脑区最少要有</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">3</span><span style="font-family:宋体">个不同的样本研究。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">由于</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">ECT</span><span style="font-family:宋体">对脑结构的效应存在单侧化（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Gryglewski et al., 2019</span><span style="font-family:宋体">），该文作者对左、右、两侧的局部脑体积变化分别进行元分析，报告效应量、置信区间和显著性水平等。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">随机效应模型显示</span>——</span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">海马回：</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">ECT</span><span style="font-family:宋体">治疗后，左侧海马回有更大的体积（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">0.69, CI: (0.44, 0.93), p<0.001</span><span style="font-family:宋体">），右侧海马回有更大的体积（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">0.95, CI: (0.65, 1.26), p<0.001</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）；双侧海马回有更大的体积（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">0.72, CI: (0.46, 0.99), p<0.001</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">杏仁核：</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">ECT</span><span style="font-family:宋体">治疗后，左侧杏仁核有更大的体积（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">0.87, CI: (0.70, 1.04), p<0.001</span><span style="font-family:宋体">），右侧杏仁核有更大的体积（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">1.12, CI: (0.81, 1.43), p<0.001</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）；双侧杏仁核有更大的体积（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">1.02, CI: (0.86, 1.18), p<0.001</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">尾状核：用来分析双侧尾状核体积的研究有</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">4</span><span style="font-family:宋体">项，随机效应模型显示，</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">ECT</span><span style="font-family:宋体">治疗后，双侧尾状核的体积增大（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">0.44, CI: (-0.13, 1.01), p=0.13</span><span style="font-family:宋体">），但差异显著性检验不显著。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">对于海马亚区，随机效应模型显示</span>——</span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">CA 1</span><span style="font-family:宋体">：</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">ECT</span><span style="font-family:宋体">治疗后，左侧</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">CA 1</span><span style="font-family:宋体">有更大的体积（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">0.45, CI: (0.01, 0.88), p=0.04</span><span style="font-family:宋体">），右侧</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">CA 1</span><span style="font-family:宋体">有更大的体积（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">0.46, CI: (0.11, 0.80), p=0.007</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）；双侧</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">CA 1</span><span style="font-family:宋体">有更大的体积（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">0.46, CI: (0.11, 0.81), p=0.01</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">CA 2/3</span><span style="font-family:宋体">：</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">ECT</span><span style="font-family:宋体">治疗后，左侧</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">CA 2/3</span><span style="font-family:宋体">有更大的体积（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">0.51, CI: (0.17, 0.85), p=0.003</span><span style="font-family:宋体">），右侧</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">CA 2/3</span><span style="font-family:宋体">有更大的体积（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">0.55, CI: (0.06, 1.03), p=0.03</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）；双侧</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">CA 2/3</span><span style="font-family:宋体">有更大的体积（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">0.46, CI: (0.13, 0.91), p=0.009</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">齿状回：</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">ECT</span><span style="font-family:宋体">治疗后，左侧齿状回有更大的体积（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">0.78, CI: (0.42, 1.14), p<0.001</span><span style="font-family:宋体">），右侧齿状回有更大的体积（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">0.78, CI: (0.44, 1.13), p<0.001</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）；双侧齿状回有更大的体积（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">0.81, CI: (0.47, 1.15), p<0.001</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">下托：</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">ECT</span><span style="font-family:宋体">治疗后，左侧下托有更大的体积（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">0.51, CI: (0.18, 0.83), p=0.002</span><span style="font-family:宋体">），右侧下托有更大的体积（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">0.58, CI: (0.15, 1.03), p=0.009</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）；双侧下托有更大的体积（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">0.53, CI: (0.24, 0.82), p<0.001</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">此外，元回归显示，齿状回的增大与相关症状的改善之间存在负性关系（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">-0.27</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">总之，该文的结果表明，左右海马回、杏仁核、</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">CA 1</span><span style="font-family:宋体">、</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">CA 2/3</span><span style="font-family:宋体">、齿状回和下托，标准化均值改变分数在</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">0.34~1.15</span><span style="font-family:宋体">之间；模型没有发现尾状核体积的显著增大。该文作者的结论是，</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">ECT</span><span style="font-family:宋体">伴随着双侧海马和杏仁核体积的显著增加，不过，这与治疗结果无关；对于海马亚区，</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">ECT</span><span style="font-family:宋体">后体积增加最稳健的是齿状回，而这一效应与抗抑郁作用存在负相关，这需要进一步用个体患者的数据进行检验。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">参考文献</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Gryglewski, G., Baldinger-Melich, P.,
