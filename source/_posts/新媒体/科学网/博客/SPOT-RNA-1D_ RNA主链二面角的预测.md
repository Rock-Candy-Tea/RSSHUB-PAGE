<p style="margin: 0px 0px 14px; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;"><strong style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;"><span style="font-family: SimSun">导读：</span></strong><span style="font-family: SimSun;">许多疾病（包括癌症）跟非编码</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">RNA</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">相关，但是我们对它们的了解甚少，因为用于理解它们功能机制的结构很难被</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">X射线晶体衍射、核磁共振和冷冻电镜等物理方法所解析。没有机制的理解，就没有彻底解决疾病的手段，所以通过计算来预测RNA结构，大幅度提高其精度势在必行。长期以来，RNA结构的预测集中在二级结构（碱基配对），并把它用于三级结构预测的约束，但对决定整体结构的RNA主链二面角却束手无策，只能依赖于并不十分可靠的能量函数来优化，我们利用深度学习来预测主链二面角的工具将改变这个状态，这篇论文发表在 J. Chem. Info. Modeling</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;"> (2021)</span><span style="font-family: SimSun;">上</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">。</span><br></p><p style=";font-size: medium;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;white-space: normal"><strong><span style="font-family: SimSun"> </span></strong></p><p style=";font-size: medium;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;white-space: normal"><span style="font-family: SimSun">今年，信使</span><span style="font-family: SimSun">RNA(mRNA)在新冠疫苗研发上的巨大成功使RNA药物开发成为热点中的热点。同时，越来越多的非编码</span>RNA<span style="font-family: SimSun">被发现在许多</span>生命过程<span style="font-family: SimSun">的</span>调控<span style="font-family: SimSun">中起着极其关键的作用，从而发现与越来越多的疾病相关。然而所发现的功能非编码</span>RNA<span style="font-family: SimSun">分子还不到在人体里已经发现的非编码</span>RNA<span style="font-family: SimSun">的</span><span style="font-family: SimSun">10%，还有90%以上无法深入探索，因为它们的序列同源性弱、它们的结构我们一无所知。目前已知的实验方法对RNA结构的解析时间长、耗费大、困难多，所以提高RNA结构的计算预测精度是必由之路。</span></p><p style=";font-size: medium;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;white-space: normal"><span style="font-family: SimSun"> </span></p><p style=";font-size: medium;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;white-space: normal"><span style="font-family: SimSun">最近，</span>谷歌AlphaFold2在第14届蛋白质结构预测的比赛（CASP14）中对大部分的蛋白 实现了原子精度或接近原子精度的结构预测<span style="font-family: SimSun">，从而打破了长期以来以为计算</span>预测<span style="font-family: SimSun">不可能代替实验的迷思，对</span><span style="font-family: SimSun">RNA结构</span>预测<span style="font-family: SimSun">的前景带来了新的希望。</span>AlphaFold2<span style="font-family: SimSun">的成功主要依赖于最近几年通过深度学习方法，对蛋白质结构的主链以及蛋白质氨基酸之间的接触距离图的</span>预测<span style="font-family: SimSun">精度的大幅度改进。我们曾经发展了</span><span style="font-family: SimSun">Real-SPINE，世界上第一个用神经网络来预测蛋白质主链</span><span style="font-family: Symbol">f</span><span style="font-family: SimSun">和</span><span style="font-family: Symbol">y</span><span style="font-family: SimSun">二面角真实值的方法【</span><span style="font-family: SimSun">1】，也发展了目前蛋白质主链二面角预测的已知最高精确度的方法之一的SPOT-1D【2】。</span></p><p style=";font-size: medium;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;white-space: normal"><span style="font-family: SimSun"> </span></p><p style=";font-size: medium;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;white-space: normal"><span style="font-family: SimSun">相比蛋白质的两个主链二面角，</span><span style="font-family: SimSun">RNA要复杂得多。它的骨架主链是由磷酸</span>-<span style="font-family: SimSun">核糖组成，需要六个二面角（</span><span style="font-family: Symbol">a, b, g, e, d, z)</span><span style="font-family: SimSun">来描述</span><span style="font-family: Symbol">, </span><span style="font-family: SimSun">此外由于</span>RNA<span style="font-family: SimSun">结构稳定性的关键因素是碱基对的堆叠，碱基的配对决定了磷酸</span>-<span style="font-family: SimSun">核糖主链的走向，所以</span><span style="font-family: SimSun">连接</span><span style="font-family: SimSun">氮碱基支链二面角</span><span style="font-family: Symbol">c</span><span style="font-family: SimSun">也非常重要。问题是这么多</span><span style="font-family: SimSun">二面角</span><span style="font-family: SimSun">，只有几百个非同源</span><span style="font-family: SimSun">RNA结构可以作为训练集，能够预测准确吗？</span></p><p style=";font-size: medium;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;white-space: normal"><span style="font-family: SimSun"> </span></p><p style=";font-size: medium;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;white-space: normal"><span style="font-family: SimSun">我们基于过去发展预测</span><span style="font-family: SimSun">RNA二级结构（SPOT-RNA）【3，4】以及RNA溶剂可接触面积（RNAsnap）【5，6】的经验，利用扩张卷积神经网络（dilated convolutional neural network）来预测所有的主链及支链二面角。虽然只使用单个RNA序列作为输入信息，不同角度在三个测试集的平均绝对误差范围为14</span><span style="font-family: Symbol">°</span><span style="font-family: SimSun">-44</span><span style="font-family: Symbol">°</span><span style="font-family: SimSun">，不比单个蛋白质序列预测的</span><span style="font-family: Symbol">f</span><span style="font-family: SimSun">（</span><span style="font-family: Symbol">24</span><span style="font-family: Symbol">°</span><span style="font-family: SimSun">）</span><span style="font-family: SimSun">和</span><span style="font-family: Symbol">y</span><span style="font-family: SimSun">（</span><span style="font-family: Symbol">44</span><span style="font-family: Symbol">°</span><span style="font-family: SimSun">）结果差【</span><span style="font-family: Symbol">7</span><span style="font-family: SimSun">】</span><span style="font-family: SimSun">，相比之下，随机预测的平均绝对误差为</span><span style="font-family: SimSun">17</span><span style="font-family: Symbol">°</span><span style="font-family: SimSun">-62</span><span style="font-family: Symbol">°</span><span style="font-family: SimSun">。更重要的是预测角度比</span>RNA-Puzzles<span style="font-family: SimSun">里</span>（类似于蛋白质结构预测CASP比赛 的RNA结构比赛）<span style="font-family: SimSun">最好模型的角度误差还要小</span>，<span style="font-family: SimSun">表明预测的</span><span style="font-family: SimSun">RNA二面角，将可以象预测的蛋白质二面角一样，作为RNA三级结构预测的约束。</span></p><p style=";font-size: medium;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;white-space: normal"><span style="font-family: SimSun"> <img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://bbs.sciencenet.cn/home.php?mod=attachment&filename=image.png&id=728496" title alt="image.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p><p style=";font-size: medium;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;white-space: normal"><span style="font-family: SimSun">由于</span><span style="font-family: SimSun">RNA主链二面角是第一次被预测，预测的角度到底有没有实际使用价值成为审稿人怀疑的对象。文章首先被Bioinformatics杂志所拒，之后在</span>J. Chem. Info. Modeling<span style="font-family: SimSun">获得了六个人的审稿，最后用更多的测试集来衡量方法的鲁棒性，以及使用</span><span style="font-family: SimSun">RNApot 和RNA puzzles结构模型库证实了预测的角度能够用来判断RNA模型是否接近天然态了，而最终被接收。</span></p><p style=";font-size: medium;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;white-space: normal"><span style="font-family: SimSun"> </span></p><p style=";font-size: medium;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;white-space: normal"><span style="font-family: SimSun">我们组通过计算和实验的结合</span>，从事蛋白质/RNA的序列、结构与功能关系方面的基础 研究和生物分子检测、药物开发方面的应用研究。寻找在RNA方面有计算和实验相关经验的博士后、科研助理和助理研究员。</p><p style=";font-size: medium;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;white-space: normal"> </p><p style=";font-size: medium;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;white-space: normal"><strong><span style="font-family: webfont, serif;color: #C0504D;border: 1px none windowtext;padding: 0">论文链接：</span></strong><a href="https://pubs.acs.org/doi/10.1021/acs.jcim.1c00153" style="color: rgb(149, 79, 114)" target="_blank">https://pubs.acs.org/doi/10.1021/acs.jcim.1c00153</a></p><p style=";font-size: medium;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;white-space: normal"><span style="font-size: 19px;font-family: Monaco;color: #F2F2F2"> </span></p><p style=";font-size: medium;font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;white-space: normal"><span style="font-size: 13px;font-family: SimSun">【</span><span style="font-size: 13px;font-family: SimSun">1】</span><span style="font-size: 13px;font-family: 'Segoe UI', sans-serif;color: #37474F;background-color: white">B.
