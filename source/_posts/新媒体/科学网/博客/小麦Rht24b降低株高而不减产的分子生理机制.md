<p style="text-indent: 32px; text-align: justify; line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">小麦株高对产量有重要影响。降低株高不仅提高作物抗倒性还可增加产量潜力。半矮秆品种的培育和推广大大提高了小麦的产量，这是上世纪六七十年代绿色革命的重要标志。小麦绿色革命基因<em><span style="font-size: 18px;">Rht1</span></em>和<em><span style="font-size: 18px;">Rht2</span></em>已经广泛应用在小麦育种中，但是其在降低株高增强抗倒性的同时也抑制胚芽鞘伸长和种子活力，不利于小麦出苗，<em><span style="font-size: 18px;">Rht1</span></em>和<em><span style="font-size: 18px;">Rht2</span></em><span style="font-size: 18px;">也</span>显著降低了粒重及赤霉病抗性。因此，发掘新的株高控制基因是小麦改良的一个重要研究目标。</span></p><p style="text-indent: 32px; text-align: justify; line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 18px;">中国农科院作物所何中虎团队和我们团队合作克隆鉴定了位于<span style="font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; text-indent: 32px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">6A</span><span style="font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; text-indent: 32px;">染色体的</span>一个控制株高的主效QTL位点 <em><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Rht24</span></em>，多个自然群体检测表明，<em><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Rht24 </span></em>对株高具有显著的遗传效应。与<em><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Rht1</span></em>和<em><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Rht2</span></em>不同，<em><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Rht24 </span></em>矮秆等位变异 <em><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Rht24b </span></em>对胚芽鞘伸长无显著影响，且对赤霉病抗性也无负效应，是一个重要的矮秆基因。通过图位克隆方法了<em><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Rht24</span></em></span><span style="font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 18px;">基因，其</span><span style="font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 18px;">编码一个<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">GA2 </span>加氧酶，<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">TaGA2ox-A9</span>。矮秆等位变异<em><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Rht24b</span></em>导致了更高的<em><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">TaGA2ox-A9</span></em>表达水平，进而降低了茎秆中活性<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">GA</span>含量，导致株高降低。转基因系和近等基因系表型分析显示，<em><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Rht24b</span></em>降低株高但对产量无负效应。在拔节期<em><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Rht24b</span></em>降低茎秆中活性<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">GA</span>，但提高了叶片中活性<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">GA</span>含量，可见<em><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Rht24b</span></em>不仅影响<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">GA</span>代谢，且影响活性<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">GA</span>的时空分布。生理测定和转录组分析表明，<em><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Rht24b</span></em>可显著提高小麦氮肥利用效率和光合作用效率，推测<em><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Rht24b</span></em>引起的叶片活性<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">GA</span>的增加是其对产量无负效应的原因，并据此诠释了<em><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;"><em style="font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; text-indent: 32px; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Rht24b</span></em></span></em>调控株高和产量的模型（图<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">1</span>）。</span></p><p style="text-indent: 32px; text-align: justify; line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">利用小麦测序数据库进行等位变异调查发现，<em><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Rht24a</span></em>和<em><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Rht24b</span></em>是两个主要单倍型，基于功能标记遗传效应分析表明，<em><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Rht24b</span></em>在我国小麦主栽品种中显著降低株高且对产量无负效应。进化分析显示，<em><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Rht24b</span></em>起源于野生二粒小麦，早于绿色革命基因<em><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Rht1</span></em>和<em><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Rht2</span></em>。通过对来自世界重要麦区<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">1000</span>份小麦种质（我国主栽品种<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">486</span>个，我国地方品种<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">97</span>个，引进种质材料<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">417</span>份）分析发现：<em><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Rht24b</span></em>（<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">74.9%</span>）分布频率显著高于<em><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Rht1</span></em>（<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">29.7%</span>）和<em><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Rht2</span></em>（<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">33.1%</span>）；在<span style="font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; text-indent: 32px;">我国</span><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">583</span>份小麦种质中，<em><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Rht24b</span></em>（<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">89.2%</span>）分布频率高于<em><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Rht1</span></em>（<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">22.6%</span>）和<em><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Rht2</span></em>（<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">43.1%</span>），可见<em><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Rht24b</span></em>在自然选择和人工驯化中均受到了正向选择。我国<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">486</span>个主栽品种有<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">104</span>个，包括大面积推广品种如京<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">411</span>、济南<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">2</span>号、泰山<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">1</span>号、烟农<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">15</span>、繁<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">6</span>、繁<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">7</span>、川麦<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">107</span>、中麦<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">175</span>等只含有<em style="font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; text-indent: 32px; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Rht24b</span></em></span><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">而不含</span><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; text-indent: 32px;">有</span><em style="font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; text-indent: 32px; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Rht1</span></em><span style="font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; text-indent: 32px;">和</span><em style="font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify; text-indent: 32px; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">Rht2</span></em>。其中何中虎团队培育的中麦<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">175</span>已成为北方冬麦区第一大品种，累计面积约<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">5000</span>万亩。</span></p><p style="text-indent: 32px; text-align: justify; line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 18px;">这一研究不仅挖掘了一个具有重要育种价值的基因资源，也为解析<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">GA</span>调控植物形态建成和产量提供了重要线索，相关研究成果发表在New Phytologist 杂志上 (<a href="https://nph.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/nph.17808" target="_blank">doi: <span style="font-family: "times new roman";
