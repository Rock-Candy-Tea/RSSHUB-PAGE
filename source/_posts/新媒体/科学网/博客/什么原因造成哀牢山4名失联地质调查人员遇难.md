<p style="line-height: 2em;">     <span style="color: #333333; font-family: arial; text-align: justify; background-color: #FFFFFF;">在失联整整8天后，11月22日早晨，4名中国地质调查局昆明自然资源综合调查中心工作人员全部被找到，不幸的是，他们都已遇难。</span><span style="color: #333333; font-family: arial; text-align: justify; background-color: #FFFFFF;">11月13日，这4名工作人员在云南哀牢山开展野外作业时失联。</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="color: #333333; font-family: arial; text-align: justify; background-color: #FFFFFF;"><span style="color: #333333; font-family: arial; text-align: justify; background-color: #FFFFFF;"></span></span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; overflow-y: auto; max-width: 100%; outline: 0px; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word; clear: both; min-height: 1em; text-align: justify; line-height: 2em;"><span style="max-width: 100%; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word;">        4名调查人员的遗体被发现后，他们的身份和携带装备也被披露。</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; overflow-y: auto; max-width: 100%; outline: 0px; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word; clear: both; min-height: 1em; text-align: justify; line-height: 2em;"><span style="max-width: 100%; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word;">       失联的4人各有一<span style="max-width: 100%; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px;">台RTK设备</span>。按常理分析，他们只要按下设备按钮，救援人员就能根据卫星系统传回的数据找到4人所在的位置。<span style="max-width: 100%; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; color: #FF0000;">但据媒体报道，</span><span style="max-width: 100%; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px;"><span style="max-width: 100%; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px;">从14日至21日11时，他们从未打开过RTK设备</span>。</span></span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; overflow-y: auto; max-width: 100%; outline: 0px; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word; clear: both; min-height: 1em; text-align: justify; line-height: 2em;"><span style="max-width: 100%; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word;"><span style="max-width: 100%; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px;">       这里的报道显然不专业，因为这个情况下，并不是他们没有打开过RTK设备，而是因为没有移动公网的信号，打开RTK设备，后方也无法接收到定为数据。这里我们需要了解一下RTK的基本原理。</span></span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; overflow-y: auto; max-width: 100%; outline: 0px; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word; clear: both; min-height: 1em; text-align: center; line-height: 2em;"><span style="max-width: 100%; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word;"><span style="max-width: 100%; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px;">      <img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202111/22/233526ufbtwdtf7f1mm67v.png" title alt="RTK2.png" width="468" height="211" style="width: 468px; height: 211px;" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; overflow-y: auto; max-width: 100%; outline: 0px; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word; clear: both; min-height: 1em; text-align: justify; line-height: 2em;">                                                                             RTK一般长这个样子</p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; overflow-y: auto; max-width: 100%; outline: 0px; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word; clear: both; min-height: 1em; text-align: center; line-height: 2em;">         <span style="color: #222222; font-family: "pingfang sc", stheiti, "microsoft yahei", arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; background-color: #FFFFFF;">实时动态定位(RTK)是一种卫星导航技术，用于提高从基于卫星的定位系统(全球导航卫星系统，GNSS)获得的位置数据的精度。除了信号所包含的信息之外，RTK还使用载波信号相位的测量值，并依靠单个参考站或内插虚拟站来进行实时校正，以提供高达厘米级的定位精度。</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; overflow-y: auto; max-width: 100%; outline: 0px; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 2em;"><span style="color: #222222; font-family: "pingfang sc", stheiti, "microsoft yahei", arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; background-color: #FFFFFF;"> </span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; overflow-y: auto; max-width: 100%; outline: 0px; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 2em; text-align: center;"><span style="color: #222222; font-family: "pingfang sc", stheiti, "microsoft yahei", arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; background-color: #FFFFFF;"> <img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202111/22/233712sse2emaweg80fsa3.png" title alt="屏幕截图 2021-11-22 233657.png" width="295" height="292" style="width: 295px; height: 292px;" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; overflow-y: auto; max-width: 100%; outline: 0px; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 2em; text-align: center;"><span style="color: #222222; font-family: "pingfang sc", stheiti, "microsoft yahei", arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; background-color: #FFFFFF;">需要成对使用（基准站+移动站）</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; overflow-y: auto; max-width: 100%; outline: 0px; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word; clear: both; min-height: 1em; text-align: justify; line-height: 2em;"><span style="max-width: 100%; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word;"><span style="max-width: 100%; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px;"><span style="color: #222222; font-family: "pingfang sc", stheiti, "microsoft yahei", arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; background-color: #FFFFFF;">       <span style="color: #333333; font-family: arial, "pingfang sc", stheiti, "microsoft yahei", sans-serif; font-size: 18px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: justify; text-indent: 32px; white-space: pre-wrap; background-color: #FFFFFF;">RTK就是实时动态差分测量，其原理简单来说就是：基准站用一个固定坐标来做参考，以后基站每通过接收卫星算得一个坐标，就跟固定坐标作比对，得到差值，然后将这个差值发送给移动站，移动站用卫星接收到的坐标再减去基站发过来的差值就得到了改正后的坐标。</span></span></span></span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; overflow-y: auto; max-width: 100%; outline: 0px; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word; clear: both; min-height: 1em; text-align: center; line-height: 2em;"><span style="max-width: 100%; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word;"><span style="max-width: 100%; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px;"><span style="color: #222222; font-family: "pingfang sc", stheiti, "microsoft yahei", arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; background-color: #FFFFFF;"><span style="color: #333333; font-family: arial, "pingfang sc", stheiti, "microsoft yahei", sans-serif; font-size: 18px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: justify; text-indent: 32px; white-space: pre-wrap; background-color: #FFFFFF;"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202111/22/232832jj165c2966k6982z.png" title alt="RTK.png" width="458" height="399" style="width: 458px; height: 399px;" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></span></span></span></span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; overflow-y: auto; max-width: 100%; outline: 0px; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word; clear: both; min-height: 1em; text-align: justify; line-height: 2em;"><span style="max-width: 100%; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word;"><span style="max-width: 100%; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px;"><span style="color: #222222; font-family: "pingfang sc", stheiti, "microsoft yahei", arial, sans-serif; font-size: 18px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; background-color: #FFFFFF;"><span style="color: #333333; font-family: arial, "pingfang sc", stheiti, "microsoft yahei", sans-serif; font-size: 18px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: justify; text-indent: 32px; white-space: pre-wrap; background-color: #FFFFFF;">       基准站和移动站之间是<span style="box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word; max-width: 100%; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; font-family: arial, "pingfang sc", stheiti, "microsoft yahei", sans-serif; font-size: 18px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: justify; text-indent: 32px; white-space: pre-wrap; background-color: #FFFFFF; color: #FF0000;">有通信的</span>，最常用的办法是利用移动公网的4G DTU（数据传输单元）终端，基准站和移动站各接一个DTU，互相发送数据进行通信，得到更高精度的定位信息。对于野外作业人员使用的移动站RTK，可以做的很轻便，已经集成了4G DTU模块，只需要插sim卡就可以工作。把基准站放在后方控制中心，野外工作人员随身携带移动站，这样只要移动站RTK处于有移动网络的环境，它就可以发送位置信息，后方指控中心的人员就可以获得野外人员的位置。</span></span></span></span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; overflow-y: auto; max-width: 100%; outline: 0px; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word; clear: both; min-height: 1em; text-align: justify; line-height: 2em;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word; max-width: 100%; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; color: #333333; font-family: arial, "pingfang sc", stheiti, "microsoft yahei", sans-serif; font-size: 18px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: justify; text-indent: 32px; white-space: pre-wrap; background-color: #FFFFFF;">       但也许是这次野外工作人员对工作环境的估计不足，没有考虑到作业地点会没有移动公网信号，他们出发的时候没有随身携带卫星电话，在没有手机信号的时候无法及时求救，也许这才是惨剧发生的最主要原因。</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; overflow-y: auto; max-width: 100%; outline: 0px; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word; clear: both; min-height: 1em; text-align: justify; line-height: 2em;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; text-align: justify; text-indent: 32px; white-space: pre-wrap; background-color: #FFFFFF;">       我们注意到新闻报道里面的描述：<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; font-family: arial, "pingfang sc", stheiti, "microsoft yahei", sans-serif; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px;">从14日至21日11时，他们从未打开过RTK设备</span><span style="text-align: justify;">。”但是队员最后失联的时间是11月13日，说明不是他们没打开过RTK，而是因为手机没有信号了，RTK上的DTU也断网，无法回传位置信息。</span></span></span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; overflow-y: auto; max-width: 100%; outline: 0px; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word; clear: both; min-height: 1em; text-align: justify; line-height: 2em;"><span style="max-width: 100%; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-indent: 32px; white-space: pre-wrap; font-family: arial; text-align: justify; background-color: #FFFFFF; color: #FF0000;">地质考察的真实目的地是有手机信号的</span>，不然也不会带着RTK，反而不带卫星电话。但是因为进山之后气象条件变差，出现了降雨和大雾的情况，<span style="box-sizing: border-box;
