<p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">2021-07-23</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">，星期五</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体">中国科学院动物研究所，牛泽清博士</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体">也许是由于今天要转点去定结县陈塘沟，我和小吴</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">7</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">点左右就醒了。起床洗漱一下，我俩开始收拾行李和采集工具，归整好标本盒。我在群里发微信招呼大家将行李先搬至停车场去装车，我和小吴东西多，一次拿不上所有东西，只好分两次将所有东西搬运至停车场，装车后大家一起去吃早餐。</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体">吃罢早餐，</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">8</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">点半我们一行驱车出发，开始今天近</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">400</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">公里的转点路程。我们从亚东县城出发，沿着</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">G562 </span><span style="font-family: 楷体">先向康马县前行。由于区间限速，近</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">90</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">公里的路程我们足足走了</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">2</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">个小时。我们快到区间测速终点时，不得不停下车来等时间，以免超速。金梁在我们停车等时间时做起了伏卧撑来消磨时间。要不是途经怕里镇美丽的雪山令我们兴奋，还真不知这一段限速的路我们会怎么度过。</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202107/24/083345lh0sncds7sdlh7n4.jpeg" title alt="2021-07-23-亚东县-3.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p><p style="text-align: center; font-size: medium; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px;"><span style="font-family:楷体">美丽雪山</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202107/24/082845dnn1npbwapnnnpqc.jpeg" title alt="2021-07-23-亚东县-1.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p><p style="text-align: center; font-size: medium; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px;"><span style="font-family:楷体">高原俯卧撑</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体">行驶完这段漫长的限速路段，上午</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">10</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">点半左右，我们来到亚东县和康马县的分叉路口。在康马县嘎拉乡嘎拉夏村附近，路边有一片青稞田，田梗边有些石头。本来我们想在此处稍微休息一下，可金梁和小吴却在石头下发现了甲虫，两人忙着采集，不亦乐乎。</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202107/24/082941igf5wv55zwg3qg1f.jpeg" title alt="2021-07-23-康马县-5.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p><p style="text-align: center; font-size: medium; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px;"><span style="font-family:楷体">采集甲虫</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体">从康马县嘎拉乡嘎拉夏村起，我们就改道</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">219</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">国道嘎定线，向岗巴县方向行进。当行驶至康马县嘎拉乡嘎定线</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">4973km</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">处时，我和小吴又一次发现路边有大量的斜茎黄耆。这是我和小吴的福花啊！只要有开花的斜茎黄耆，就不愁采不到蜜蜂。此时正值中午时分，天气又格外的好，真是我俩采集蜜蜂的大好时机。小吴驻车，我和小吴分别沿道路两侧，开始狂扫起来。蜜蜂数量太多了，尤其有大量的分舌蜂，高兴得我和小吴手舞足蹈，尽情地挥网捕捉。半个多小时，我们俩就将两百多个收集管采集满了，还特意挑选了一些分舌蜂和准蜂浸泡在了液氮中。</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体">结束了嘎定线</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">4973km</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">处的采集，我们一行快速向岗巴县挺进。路上，小吴开车，我在副驾驶位上，一直忙着做针插标本，小吴还特意给我拍了个工作照。</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif"> </span><span style="font-family: 楷体">下午</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">2</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">点左右，我们驶入定结县县界。本来想在定结县给车加油，无奈离县城太远，而我们行驶的路上又没有加油站，我们只好硬着头皮沿</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">S514</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">向陈塘沟前行。行驶不一会儿，我们来到海拔</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">4888</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">米的尼拉山。</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">2018</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">年我和小吴也曾经路过尼拉山。今天天气好，让我们一睹尼拉山的风彩。小吴和我还在尼拉山口的国旗边分别留影。尼拉山附近风特别大，但小吴发现有熊蜂。为了捕捉几只熊蜂，小吴拿着扫网跑来跑去追逐熊蜂，累得脸都黑紫黑紫的。金梁也蹲在地上，努力在寻找着甲虫。但高海拔处采集的困难还是比较大的，付出的体力也要比低海拔处多</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">得多。在尼拉山停留约半个小时后，我们继续向陈塘沟前行。当行驶至</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">S514 20km</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">处时，我们又一次驻车，因路边又有斜茎黄耆。也不是我们只认识斜茎黄耆，而是西藏路边也只有斜茎黄耆开花招惹蜜蜂。这次停车采集</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">，想不到我和小吴竟然捕到了几只黄斑蜂。这是我俩今年出差一个月来首次采到黄斑蜂，真是提振士气啊。</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202107/24/083239kz1nx6p6y62i6yze.jpeg" title alt="2021-07-23-定结县-4.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p><p style="text-align: center; font-size: medium; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px;"><span style="font-family:楷体">尼拉山</span></p><p style="font-size: medium; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px;"><span style="font-family:楷体"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202107/24/083427b0hjmtmj0tl0ijwe.jpeg" title alt="2021-07-23-定结县-8.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p><p style="font-size: medium; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px; text-align: center;"><span style="font-family:楷体">高海拔扫网</span></p><p style="font-size: medium; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px;"><span style="font-family:楷体"><img style="font-family:楷体">尼拉山美景</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体">时间已是下午</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">5</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">点半了，我们离陈塘沟所在的陈塘镇还有近</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">80</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">公里的路程。不能过多地耽误时间了，需尽快赶往陈塘镇。沿</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">S514</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">急行，道路也变得越来越熟悉起来，</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">2018</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">年行驶过的路段，往事不油得浮现出来，让我和小吴感慨的是，</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">2018</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">年我俩在离陈塘镇</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">2</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">公里处时因大雨冲毁桥梁而无法通行，不得不折道而返。今天再次路经当年的桥梁时，顿时被新建的桥梁所震憾，车辆从新桥上平稳地通过，我们也顺利地到达了陈塘镇。陈塘沟的美景也顿时映入到了我的眼帘。</span></p><p
