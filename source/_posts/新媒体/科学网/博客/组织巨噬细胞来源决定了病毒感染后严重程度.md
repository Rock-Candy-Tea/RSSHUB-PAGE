<p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%">最近生物医学领域对巨噬细胞的类型研究非常热门，这主要得益于单细胞测序等技术的广泛应用。</p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">巨噬细胞（</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">macrophage cell</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）也称</span></span><a href="https://baike.baidu.com/item/%E7%BB%84%E7%BB%87%E7%BB%86%E8%83%9E/4409156" target="_blank"><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">组织细胞</span></span></a><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">（</span>histocyte<span style="font-family:宋体">），是由血液中的</span></span><a href="https://baike.baidu.com/item/%E5%8D%95%E6%A0%B8%E7%BB%86%E8%83%9E/10319973" target="_blank"><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">单核细胞</span></span></a><span style=";font-family:Calibri;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">穿出血管后分化而成的。单核细胞进入结缔组织后，体积增大，内质网和线粒体增生，溶酶体增多，吞噬功能增强。巨噬细胞的寿命因所在组织器官而异，一般可存活数月或更长。</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">器官组织内的巨噬细胞有两种基本类型，</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">胎儿单核细胞</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">和骨髓单核细胞。这两种细胞的功能存在差异。因此研究和分析具体器官内巨噬细胞的类型对于理解某些疾病的病理过程有很大帮助。最近《科学转化医学》对肺组织巨噬细胞的类型和病毒感染后的病理特征进行了研究。发现了非常有意思的现象。</span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">肺泡巨噬细胞来自胎儿单核细胞</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">(FeMo-AM)</span><span style="font-family:宋体">是至关重要的防御呼吸道病毒感染。在甲型流感病毒</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">(IAV)</span><span style="font-family:宋体">感染期间，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">胎儿单核细胞</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">会被耗尽，但目前尚不清楚该人群是通过</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">胎儿单核细胞的自我更新</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">还是通过骨髓源性</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">单核细胞</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">浸润来补充。</span> <span style="font-family:Calibri">Li</span><span style="font-family:宋体">等人使用多个小鼠模型研究了</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">A</span><span style="font-family:宋体">型流感病毒反复感染后</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">胎儿单核细胞</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">和</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">骨髓源性单核细胞</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">的长期表型、命运和功能。</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">肺组织巨噬细胞缺乏</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">最初被</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">胎儿单核细胞</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">的自我更新取代，但随着时间推移，被具有</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">更强</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">糖酵解和增殖能力的</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">骨髓源性单核细胞</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">所取</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">代</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">。</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">这可能是老年动物和人肺部感染容易发生的重要因素。</span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">多种肺损伤可导致驻留肺泡巨噬细胞</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">(AM)</span><span style="font-family:宋体">被骨髓单核细胞来源</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">(BMo) -AM</span><span style="font-family:宋体">取代。</span><span style="font-family:Calibri"> </span><span style="font-family:宋体">然而，这一过程的动态及其对呼吸道病毒感染的长期后果仍不清楚。</span><span style="font-family:Calibri"> </span><span style="font-family:宋体">使用几种小鼠模型和标记物明确跟踪胎儿单核细胞来源</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">(FeMo) -</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">巨噬细胞</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">和</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">骨髓源性单核细胞</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，我们建立了动力学和补充程度，以及它们对复发性甲型流感病毒</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">(IAV)</span><span style="font-family:宋体">感染的功能。</span><span style="font-family:Calibri"> </span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">胎儿单核细胞</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">的大量缺失导致了幸存者自我更新的快速补充，继而产生了</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">骨髓源性单核细胞</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。</span><span style="font-family:Calibri"> </span><span style="font-family:宋体">在几个月的时间里，</span></span><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;color: #0000FF;font-size: 16px">骨髓源性单核细胞</span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;color: #0000FF;font-size: 16px">逐渐胜过</span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;color: #0000FF;font-size: 16px">胎儿单核细胞</span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;color: #0000FF;font-size: 16px">，</span></strong><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">这是由于</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">后者</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">增加了糖酵解和增殖能力。</span><span style="font-family:Calibri"> naïve</span><span style="font-family:宋体">和经验丰富的</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">骨髓源性单核细胞</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">的存在给</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">甲型流感病毒</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">感染赋予了严重的病理，这与炎症前表型相关。</span><span style="font-family:Calibri"> </span><span style="font-family:宋体">此外，在</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">naïve</span></span><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;color: #0000FF;font-size: 16px">小鼠衰老后，</span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;color: #0000FF;font-size: 16px">胎儿单核细胞</span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;color: #0000FF;font-size: 16px">逐渐被</span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;color: #0000FF;font-size: 16px">骨髓源性单核细胞</span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;color: #0000FF;font-size: 16px">取代</span></strong><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">，这以细胞自主的方式导致了</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">甲型流感病毒</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">疾病的严重程度。</span><span style="font-family:Calibri"> </span><span style="font-family:宋体">总之，我们的结果表明，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">是巨噬细胞</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">起源</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">，</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">而不是训练决定了呼吸道病毒感染的长期功能，并为老年人感染的严重程度增加提供了解释。</span></p><p style="margin-left: 0px; text-indent: 28px; line-height: 200%;"><a
