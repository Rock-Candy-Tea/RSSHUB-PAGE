font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei";">发表不易，稿件通过评审需要修改，无论是大修，还是小修，其实都是好事。但是，有时候会碰到“难以处理”的审稿意见，查尔斯沃思编辑针对三种常见的情况，给出了建议，本文是最后一篇。</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei";"><br></span></p></section><section><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; text-align: center;"><span style="color: #0070C0; font-size: 16px; font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei";"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">03 </strong><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">如果审稿人给出了相互矛盾的审稿意见，该怎么处理？</strong></span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="color: #0070C0; font-size: 16px; font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei";"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><br></strong></span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei";">出现这种情况是可以理解的。大多数同行评审流程中，审稿人独立审稿。根据各自的专业背景和研究方向，不同审稿人在评审同一篇初稿时会采用不同的标准，因而要求阐明或修改的地方会不同，甚至相互矛盾。<br></span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei";"><br></span></p></section><section><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei";">随着期刊更加倡导多样化，以及审稿人身份日益多元化，评审意见必然会更新颖，也更可能会出现相互矛盾的修改要求。是不是很棘手？毫无疑问，是的。由此增加的多样性和视角层次是否值得？当然！<br></span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei";"><br></span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei";">出现这种情况时，首先，<strong style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">作者团队应该对审稿人的意见展开集体讨论</strong>。一位作者眼中的矛盾之处在另一位作者看来可能并非如此。作者投入大量的时间和精力完成了论文，也一定会顺利完成论文的修改。<br></span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei";"><br></span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei";">其次，<strong style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">确定是否能够按要求进行最重要的修改</strong>。如果不能解决每个问题，能至少解决主要问题吗？<br></span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei";"><br></span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei";">第三，<strong style="font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">试着协调相互矛盾的评审意见</strong>。在修改稿回复信中，礼貌地指出审稿意见的矛盾之处（编辑部可能不太清楚有此矛盾），并提出解决方案的建议。<br></span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei";"><br></span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; clear: both; min-height: 1em; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei";">始终对每位审稿人给出的每条评审意见表示尊重，并详细说明将如何按要求进行修改。即使你不能按要求修改，也要表明自己很重视审稿人的意见。<span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 微软雅黑, "Microsoft YaHei"; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; outline: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">如果评审意见无法调和，将矛盾告知期刊编辑，并向他们征求下一步的建议。</strong></span></span></p></section><hr><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202110/21/095619xmhh3z5xrvt5r8nt.jpg" title alt="科学网博客文章底部图片.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://www.cwauthors.com.cn/prices/?utm_source=sciencenetblog&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=snprice" target="_blank" style="text-decoration: underline;
