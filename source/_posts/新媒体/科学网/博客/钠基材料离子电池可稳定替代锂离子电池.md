font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">近十年来，科学家和工程师们一直在开发钠电池，用更便宜、更环保的钠取代目前锂离子电池中使用的锂和钴。不幸的是，在早期的钠电池中，一种被称为阳极（</span>anode<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）的成分会生长出针状的纤维，这种纤维被称为树枝晶（</span>dendrites<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">），会导致电池短路，甚至起火或爆炸。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><a href="http://www.utexas.edu/" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">UT Austin</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">最近取得的两项钠电池进展之一是，这种新材料解决了树枝晶问题，并能像锂离子电池一样快速充电。该团队</span>2021<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">年</span>10<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">月下旬已经在《先进材料》杂志网站上发表了他们的研究结果。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">设计这种新材料的科克雷尔工程学院沃克机械工程与应用研究实验室（</span>Cockrell School of Engineering's Walker Department of Mechanical Engineering and Applied Research Laboratory<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）的教授大卫·米特林</span>(David Mitlin)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">说：“我们基本上是同时解决了两个问题。通常情况下，充电越快，这些树枝晶就会长得越多。所以如果你抑制树枝晶生长，你就要更快地充电和放电，只有这样电池才会安全一些。”</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><a href="http://www.utexas.edu/" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">UT Austin</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">化学系和奥登计算工程与科学研究所</span>(Oden Institute for Computational Engineering and Sciences)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">的</span><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">格雷姆·亨克尔曼（</span><span style="font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 18px;">Graeme Henkelman</span><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）</span><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">教授，利用计算机模型从理论角度解释了这种材料为何具有独特的特性。</span></span><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">格雷姆·亨克尔曼</span><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">说：</span><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">“这种材料也很令人兴奋，因为理论上金属钠阳极的能量密度是所有钠阳极中最高的</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 18px;">。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 18px;">”</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">家用固定能源存储系统的需求正在上升，同时还需要消除风能和太阳能在电网中的涨落。与此同时，锂矿开采与提取因其对环境的影响而受到批评，包括大量地下水的使用、土壤和水污染以及碳排放。锂离子电池通常也使用钴，钴价格昂贵，主要在刚果民主共和国开采，对人类健康和环境有重大影响。相比之下，钠矿更便宜，也更环保。</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">大卫·米特林很看好这项新发明以及</span><a href="http://www.utexas.edu/" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">UT Austin</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">的其他发明，包括一种新的固态电解质，可以促进能量存储，这意味着钠电池可能很快就能满足不断增长的固定能量存储需求。</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">当可充电电池充电时，离子</span>(<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">如锂或钠</span>)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">从一个被称为阴极的组件移动到另一个被称为阳极的组件。当电池被用来发电时，离子从阳极回到阴极。</span></span><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">这种新的阳极材料被称为碲化钠锑（</span><span style="font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 18px;">NaSbTe</span><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）金属间化合物（</span><span style="font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 18px;">sodium antimony telluride intermetallic</span><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）</span><span style="font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 18px;">-</span><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">钠金属复合材料简称</span><span style="font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 18px;">NST-Na</span><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">，它是通过将薄片金属钠卷到碲化锑粉末上，将其折叠起来，并重复多次而制成的。</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">“想象一下做一种分层的糕点，比如传统希腊式馅饼（</span>spanakopita<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">），”大卫·米特林说。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">这个过程的结果是钠原子的分布非常均匀，这使得它比现有的金属钠阳极更不可能形成树枝晶或表面腐蚀。这使得电池更加稳定，充电速度更快，堪比锂离子电池（</span><a href="https://techxplore.com/tags/lithium-ion+battery/" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">lithium-ion battery</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">）的充电速度。它也比现有的钠离子电池具有更高的能量容量。</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">格雷姆·亨克尔曼说，如果钠电池中携带电荷的钠原子彼此之间的结合比它们与阳极之间的结合更强，它们就会形成不稳定，或者钠原子聚集在一起吸引更多的钠原子，最终形成树枝晶。他利用计算机模拟揭示了单个钠原子与新型复合材料</span>NST-Na<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">相互作用时会发生什么。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">“在我们的计算中，这种复合物结合钠的强度比钠本身结合的强度要大一些，这是让钠原子下降并均匀分布在表面的理想情况，并阻止这些不稳定性的形成，”格雷姆·亨克尔曼说。</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">该研究的两位主要作者、现任和前任大卫·米特林实验室研究生王义贤</span>(Yixian Wang)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">和董慧</span>(Hui Dong)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">分别制造了这种材料。由约翰·瓦特（</span>John Watt<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）领导的洛斯·阿拉莫斯国家实验室（</span>Los Alamos National Laboratory<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）的同事们描述了它的特性。该研究的其他作者是郝洪昌（</span>Hongchang Hao<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">音译）、刘鹏程（</span>Pengcheng Liu<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">音译）和德克萨斯大学奥斯汀分校的纳曼·凯泰尔（</span>Naman Katyal<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）。</span></span><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">大卫·米特林、王义贤及董慧已经和</span><span style="font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 18px;">UT Austin</span><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">一起申请了一项关于新型金属钠阳极材料的制造、结构和功能的专利。</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">Making sodium-ion batteries that last</span></a></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><a href="https://doi.org/10.1002/adma.202106005" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "arial black", "avant garde";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "arial black", "avant garde";">Abstract</span></a><br></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">Repeated cold rolling and folding is employed to fabricate a metallurgical composite of sodium–antimony–telluride Na<sub>2</sub>(Sb<sub>2/6</sub>Te<sub>3/6</sub>Vac<sub>1/6</sub>) dispersed in electrochemically active sodium metal, termed “NST-Na.” This new intermetallic has a vacancy-rich thermodynamically stable face-centered-cubic structure and enables state-of-the-art electrochemical performance in widely employed carbonate and ether electrolytes. NST-Na achieves 100% depth-of-discharge (DOD) in 1 m NaPF<sub>6</sub> in G2, with 15 mAh cm<sup>−2</sup> at 1 mA cm<sup>−2</sup> and Coulombic efficiency (CE) of 99.4%, for 1000 h of plating/stripping. Sodium-metal batteries (SMBs) with NST-Na and Na<sub>3</sub>V<sub>2</sub>(PO<sub>4</sub>)<sub>3</sub> (NVP) or sulfur cathodes give significantly improved energy, cycling, and CE (>99%). An anode-free battery with NST collector and NVP obtains 0.23% capacity decay per cycle. Imaging and tomography using conventional and cryogenic microscopy (Cryo-EM) indicate that the sodium metal fills the open space inside the self-supporting sodiophilic NST skeleton, resulting in dense (pore-free and solid electrolyte interphase (SEI)-free) metal deposits with flat surfaces. The baseline Na deposit consists of filament-like dendrites and “dead metal”, intermixed with pores and SEI. Density functional theory calculations show that the uniqueness of NST lies in the thermodynamic stability of the Na atoms (rather than clusters) on its surface that leads to planar wetting, and in its own stability that prevents decomposition during cycling.</span></p><p><br></p>                    <br><br>
