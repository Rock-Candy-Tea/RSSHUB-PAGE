font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">全息显示可以重建高质量的图像，真正的</span>3-D<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">视觉感知。它们被认为是连接真实世界和虚拟世界的终极显示技术，以实现身临其境的体验。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">然而，由于空间带宽产品有限，目前全息图信息的信息内容远远大于目前被称为空间光调制器</span>(<a href="https://phys.org/news/2022-03-stackable-holobricks-giant-3d-images.html" target="_blank">spatial light modulators</a>)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">的光引擎的显示能力。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">对于二维显示器，标准做法是将小尺寸显示器平铺在一起形成一个大显示器。这里正在探索的方法与此类似，但针对的是</span>3D<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">显示器，这是以前从未做过的。先进光子与电子中心</span>(<a href="https://phys.org/news/2022-03-stackable-holobricks-giant-3d-images.html" target="_blank">Center for Advanced Photonics and Electronics</a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">简称</span>CAPE)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">的主任、上述研究论文的通讯作者褚大平教授说：“将</span>3D<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">图像拼接在一起并非易事，因为最终的图像必须从各个角度和深度看都是无缝的。在真实空间直接平铺</span>3D<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">图像是不可能的。”</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">为了解决这一挑战，研究人员开发了全息砖单元（</span><a href="https://phys.org/news/2022-03-stackable-holobricks-giant-3d-images.html" target="_blank">holobrick unit</a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">），基于粗糙集成全息显示的角度平铺的</span>3-D<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">图像，这是大约七年前在</span>CAPE<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">与迪斯尼研究公司（</span><a href="https://phys.org/news/2022-03-stackable-holobricks-giant-3d-images.html" target="_blank">Disney Research</a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）共同开发的概念。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">每个全息砖使用高信息带宽空间光调制器进行信息传递，结合粗集成光学（</span><a href="https://phys.org/news/2022-03-stackable-holobricks-giant-3d-images.html" target="_blank">coarse integrated optics</a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">），形成具有较大的观看区域和视场的有角度的平铺三维全息图。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">仔细的光学设计确保全息条纹图案填满了全息砖的整个表面，从而使多个全息砖可以无缝地堆叠起来，形成一个可扩展的空间平铺全息图像</span>3-D<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">显示，既能宽视场角度，又能大尺寸。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">由研究人员开发的概念验证是由两块无缝贴合的全息砖组成的。每个全彩砖是</span>1024<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">×</span>768<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">像素，</span>40<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">°视场，每秒</span>24<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">帧，以显示全</span>3D<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">Here, we propose and demonstrate a modular holographic display system that allows seamless spatial tiling of multiple coarse integral holographic (CIH) displays called “holobricks”. A holobrick is a self-contained CIH module enclosing a spatial light modulator (SLM), a scanner, and periscopic coarse integral optics. Modular CIH uses a coarse pitch and small area but high-bandwidth SLM in conjunction with periscopic coarse integral optics to form the angularly tiled 3D holograms with large viewing areas and fields of view. The creation of periscopic coarse integral optics prevents the optical system from being larger than the holographic image and allows the holographic fringe pattern to fill the entire face of the holobrick. Thus, multiple holobricks can be seamlessly abutted to form a scalable spatially tiled holographic image display capable of both wide field-of-view angle and arbitrary large-size area.
