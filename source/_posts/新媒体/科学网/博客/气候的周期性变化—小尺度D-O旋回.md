Roman';line-height:150%;font-size:16px;vertical-align:super">18</span></sup><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';line-height:150%;font-size:16px">O</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">这种半球温度不对称现象，也会引起</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">ITCZ</span><span style="font-family:宋体">的引动。比如，北半球变冷，南半球变暖，</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">ITCZ</span><span style="font-family:宋体">就会向南移动，造成当地的水文变化。</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">ITCZ</span><span style="font-family:宋体">南移，北半球中低纬度地区降雨变少，而南半球中低纬降雨会降低。石笋记录可以清晰地解释出这一规律。对比南京地区的石笋记录和巴西的石笋</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">δ</span></span><sup><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';line-height:150%;font-size:16px;vertical-align:super">18</span></sup><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';line-height:150%;font-size:16px">O</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">记录，会发现完全反相关现象。</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">ITCZ</span><span style="font-family:宋体">是大气</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Hadley cell</span><span style="font-family:宋体">的下降沿，</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">ITCZ</span><span style="font-family:宋体">南移，会加强北半球的</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Hadley cell</span><span style="font-family:宋体">，同时减弱南半球的</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Hadley cell</span><span style="font-family:宋体">，同时使得南半球西风带向南移动。在此，我们会看到不但南北半球的海洋联动，大气也联动起来。二者共同决定这局部地区的气候变化。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:28px;line-height:150%"><span style="font-family:宋体;font-size:16px">D-</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">O</span><span style="font-family:宋体">冷期，南半球西风带向南移动并加强，使得海洋混合加强，</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">CO</span></span><sub><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px;vertical-align:sub"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">2</span></span></sub><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">被大量释放，南极区温度升高，海冰后退，更多的区域可以释放</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">CO</span></span><sub><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px;vertical-align:sub"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">2</span></span></sub><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。在</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">D-O</span><span style="font-family:宋体">旋回的冷期，北半球高纬度降温</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">~12</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">°</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">C</span><span style="font-family:宋体">，南极升温</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">1</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">°</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">C</span><span style="font-family:宋体">。形成了一个温度梯度带。在赤道南部的某些区域，肯定温度不升也不降。在北太平洋区域，我们最为关心的一个成分就是</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">NPIW</span><span style="font-family:宋体">。证据表明，在</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">D-O</span><span style="font-family:宋体">冷期，</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">NPIW</span><span style="font-family:宋体">生成量增加，比</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">LGM</span><span style="font-family:宋体">时期还要多。这个链条告诉我们，</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">AMOC</span><span style="font-family:宋体">和</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">NPIW</span><span style="font-family:宋体">增强。</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">NPIW</span><span style="font-family:宋体">增强，中层水和浅层水交换肯定加强，中层水富含</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">CO</span></span><sub><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px;vertical-align:sub"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">2</span><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span></sub><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">从而也会增加空气中的</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">CO</span></span><sub><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px;vertical-align:sub"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">2</span></span></sub><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">NPIW</span><span style="font-family:宋体">增强，</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">ITCZ</span><span style="font-family:宋体">南移，西北太平洋地区的降水减少，盐度增加。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:28px;line-height:150%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">经过南北极冰芯对比，我们也开始产生一些关于</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">D-O</span><span style="font-family:宋体">事件发生机制的思考。显而易见，在北极每次</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Henrich</span><span style="font-family:宋体">事件事件发生后，必然会出现</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">D-O</span><span style="font-family:宋体">事件，但是，没反过来不成立，并不是每次</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">D-O</span><span style="font-family:宋体">事件都对应</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Henrich</span><span style="font-family:宋体">事件。所以，我们不得不排除冰筏事件是引起每次</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">D-O</span><span style="font-family:宋体">事件的原因。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:28px;line-height:150%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">南北极的气候相关性，让我们把目光锁定到南极机制。比如，南极的海冰变化会引起</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">AMOC</span><span style="font-family:宋体">的变化，进而引起北极的温度变化以及相应的冰筏事件。这样想来，也很有道理。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:28px;line-height:150%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:150%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">从时间上来看，</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">D-O</span><span style="font-family:宋体">于是，科学家要开始思考，如果真的没有统一的气候变化机制，那么</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">D-O</span><span style="font-family:宋体">旋回是不是就是大气</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">-</span><span style="font-family:宋体">海洋</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">-</span><span style="font-family:宋体">海冰系统的内部固有的震荡行为？而且这种震荡行为在半寒冷的冰期最不稳定，气候系统中一点变化就可能导致</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">AMOC</span><span style="font-family:宋体">的停止，比如增加一点淡水，空气中的</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">CO2</span><span Roman';line-height:150%;font-size:16px;vertical-align:super">18</span></sup><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';line-height:150%;font-size:16px">O</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">总之，到目前为止，并没有一个完美的简单机制来统一</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">D-O</span><span style="font-family:宋体">旋回的变化。研究</span><span
