normal"> </p><p style="margin-bottom:0;margin-bottom:0;line-height: normal"><span style="font-family:SimSun">例如，我们观察自然界中植物的生长过程，比如一棵竹子。某一天这棵竹子</span>1<span style="font-family:SimSun">米高，</span>1<span (1+1/240)<sup>240  </sup>= 2.71264<span style="font-family:SimSun">。</span></p><p style="margin-bottom:0;margin-bottom:0;line-height: normal"> </p><p                             <span style="font-family:SimSun">（</span>3.1<span style="font-family:SimSun">）</span></p><p style="margin-bottom:0;margin-bottom:0;line-height: normal"> </p><p
