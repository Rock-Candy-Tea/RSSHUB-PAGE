Here, we report the synthesis of [Al(AlCp*)<sub>3</sub>]<sup>+</sup>[Al(ORF)<sub>4</sub>]<sup>–</sup> (RF=C(CF<sub>3</sub>)<sub>3</sub>) through a simple metathesis reaction between [(AlCp*)<sub>4</sub>] and Li[Al(ORF)<sub>4</sub>]. Unexpectedly, the [Al(AlCp*)<sub>3</sub>]<sup>+</sup> salt forms a dimer in the solid state and concentrated solutions.
