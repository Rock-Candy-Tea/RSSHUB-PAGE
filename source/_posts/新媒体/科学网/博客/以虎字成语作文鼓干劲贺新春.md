text-indent: 32px; background: white; line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun; font-size: 16px;">在攀登科学高峰前，要有足够的知识储备，才能<span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 宋体, SimSun; color: red;">如虎添翼<span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 宋体, SimSun; color: red;">虎狼之师</span><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 宋体, SimSun; color: #333333;">。</span></span></p><p style="margin-bottom: 0px; text-indent: 32px; background: white;
