<p style=";font-size: 15px;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal;-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto"><span style="font-family: DengXian">再论论文</span></p><p style=";font-size: 15px;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal;-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto"><span style="font-family: DengXian">许秋雨，</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">2021.3.22</span></p><p style=";font-size: 15px;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal;-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto"><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif"> </span></p><p style=";font-size: 15px;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal;-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto"><span style="font-family: DengXian">在学术界永远跳不出论文这两个字。特别对基础科研，科研人员发表论文就等同于科研人员的工作表现。所以科研人员都特别在意发表论文。</span></p><p style=";font-size: 15px;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal;-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto"><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif"> </span></p><p style=";font-size: 15px;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal;-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto"><span style="font-family: DengXian">要发表论文，当然需要期刊或者／和会议论文集。下面我就我自己最熟悉的期刊，即</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">IEEE </span><span style="font-family: DengXian">信号处理会刊，来展开分析一下。</span></p><p style=";font-size: 15px;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal;-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto"><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif"> </span></p><p style=";font-size: 15px;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal;-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto"><span style="font-family: DengXian">关于</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">IEEE </span><span style="font-family: DengXian">信号处理会刊，我以前也曾经提到过。在九十年代前，它叫</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">IEEE TASSP</span><span style="font-family: DengXian">。当时整个</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">IEEE</span><span style="font-family: DengXian">信号处理学会就只有这一个期刊发表原创论文，另外还有一个学会的通讯广告等期刊，即</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">IEEE SP Magazine</span><span style="font-family: DengXian">，它不发表原创成果。当时，</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">TASSP</span><span style="font-family: DengXian">是双月刊，每刊不到一厘米厚，一年只有六刊。到了九十年代，</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">IEEE TASSP</span><span style="font-family: DengXian">拆成至少三个期刊，一个是</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">TSP</span><span style="font-family: DengXian">，一个是</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">T</span><span style="font-family: DengXian">声和语音的，一个是图像</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">TIP</span><span style="font-family: DengXian">。且</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">TSP</span><span style="font-family: DengXian">由双月刊变成了月刊，一年有十二刊，每刊有时只有不到半厘米厚，有时一厘米厚。</span></p><p style=";font-size: 15px;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal;-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto"><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif"> </span></p><p style=";font-size: 15px;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal;-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto"><span style="font-family: DengXian">到后来，</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">IEEE</span><span style="font-family: DengXian">信号处理学会又增加了好几个会刊（</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">Trans</span><span style="font-family: DengXian">），现在应该大约有十个发表原创论文的期刊。同时</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">TSP</span><span style="font-family: DengXian">变成了每月双刊，一年有二十四刊，每刊有一寸厚。这样一算，现在</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">IEEE</span><span style="font-family: DengXian">信号处理学会发表原创论文期刊的总页数应该是八十年代的至少</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">100</span><span style="font-family: DengXian">倍之多。</span></p><p style=";font-size: 15px;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal;-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto"><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif"> </span></p><p style=";font-size: 15px;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal;-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto"><span style="font-family: DengXian">在八九十年代，</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">TSP</span><span style="font-family: DengXian">上发表了不少信号处理特有的比较好的论文，如变换，滤波器设计，反卷积，由部分信息恢复信号，自适应滤波等等。当时</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">TSP</span><span style="font-family: DengXian">发表的论文可读性都不错，大多经得起推敲。</span></p><p style=";font-size: 15px;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal;-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto"><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif"> </span></p><p style=";font-size: 15px;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal;-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto"><span style="font-family: DengXian">可是后来</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">TSP</span><span style="font-family: DengXian">里面的非常非常多的论文都经不起推敲了，除了堆上数堆理不清的、不想读的、很多情况下是错误的公式外，别无其它。正由于这样，且论文数量又多，现在已成了恶性循环，仔细读论文的人也越来越少了，以至于写不清楚的论文容易被接受，而写清楚的论文反而容易被拒稿。</span></p><p style=";font-size: 15px;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal;-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto"><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif"> </span></p><p style=";font-size: 15px;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal;-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto"><span style="font-family: DengXian">现在</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">TSP</span><span style="font-family: DengXian">里的论文错误率已经非常的高。有的论文的作者，跟着做的别人的论文还没有弄明白，就开始堆公式，写</span><span style="font-family: DengXian">论文</span><span style="font-family: DengXian">。别人的算法根本没理解，就改进别人的算法了，且还能在</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">TSP</span><span style="font-family: DengXian">上发表。尽管现在</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">TSP</span><span style="font-family: DengXian">比原来的</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">TASSP</span><span style="font-family: DengXian">多了至少十二倍的页数，但是有真正原创性的论文数量却远远不如八十年代的</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">TASSP</span><span style="font-family: DengXian">的多。当然，这是我个人的看法，一个跟踪此刊三十七年的老熟人的看法。</span></p><p style=";font-size: 15px;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal;-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto"><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif"> </span></p><p style=";font-size: 15px;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal;-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto"><span style="font-family: DengXian">我的天呢，</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">IEEE TSP</span><span style="font-family: DengXian">还算是口碑比较好的期刊啊！也许是因为此刊偶尔也会有真正好的论文出来么？！</span></p><p style=";font-size: 15px;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal;-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto"><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif"> </span></p><p style=";font-size: 15px;font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;white-space: normal;-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto"><span style="font-family: DengXian">自从要求交版面费后，学术论文已经完全变味了，发表论文再也代表不了科学研究。</span></p><p><br></p>                    <br><br>
