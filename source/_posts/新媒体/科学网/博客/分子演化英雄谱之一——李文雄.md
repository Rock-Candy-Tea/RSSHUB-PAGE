<h1 style="margin: 20px 0px 3px; font-size: 21px; font-family: DengXian; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-variant-caps: small-caps; letter-spacing: 0px; font-weight: normal; white-space: normal; text-align: center; line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">生命科学巨人第二辑-李文雄</span></h1><p><br></p><p class="p2" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 13px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-size: 20px;">这一期要介绍的是华人演化生物学家<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">-</span>李文雄。</span></p><p class="p2" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 13px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-size: 20px;">在求知阶段，少年李文雄的成长并非经典的逆袭式励志故事。天性乐观、兴趣广泛，善于思考是他的标签。他曾一度幻想成为小说家。高中时代他对未来充满了想象，对常识的本质感兴趣。虽然最好的科目是数学、化学、物理，但是他就像大多数年轻人一样，不知道自己未来该干什么。</span></p><p class="p2" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 13px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-size: 20px;">常言道<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">“</span>时势造英雄<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">”</span>。年轻人不知道该干什么，往往会先找个热门行业试水。青少年时代的李文雄，适逢台湾<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">60</span>年代经济起飞。<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">1961</span>年，<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">19</span>岁的他高中毕业，试图成为詹天佑一样的工程学家，投身建设浪潮。于是，中原大学的土木工程系成了他的首选。可是热爱理性思考和科学逻辑的他，很快意识到实用科目并非他的兴趣所在。大学二年级，他意识到自己更喜欢数学<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">, </span>但是苦于没有合适的数学研究生项目。研究生期间，他考取了中央大学地球物理专业。</span><span style="font-size: 20px;">钻研过土木工程、地球物理、数学等专业，似乎他还没有料到未来自己会成为一位著名生物学家。</span></p><p class="p2" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 13px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-size: 20px;">对数学感兴趣的李文雄决定继续念博士，他选择了应用数学也就是统计学方向。在数学的各种细分专业中，这在是一个热门选项。<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">1968</span>年他被<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">Brown</span>大学录取，开始了<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">4</span>年的博士生涯。布朗大学的学习经历非常愉悦也极为重要，他对随机过程理论产生了浓厚兴趣。<span style="font-family: "PingFang SC"; font-size: 20px; text-align: justify;">博士第二年，需要确定研究课题，该做什么的？</span>兴趣永远是成为好科学家的一面，更为重要的似乎是天时地利人和的“科学问题”。</span></p><p class="mb-0" style="box-sizing: border-box; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; line-height: 1.2; font-family: Georgia, serif; font-size: clamp(1.5rem, 2.1vw, 2rem); color: rgb(26, 26, 26); white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);"><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 20px;">70年代末，Walter Gilbert和Frederick Sanger独立的发明了DNA测序方法，分子遗传学的大门被缓缓打开。李文雄决定在这充满希望的领域探索一些重要的问题。同时幸运的是，他的导师Wendell Fleming也对群体遗传学感兴趣，而且他碰</span><span style="font-size: 20px; text-align: justify;">到一位来自东方的伯乐</span><span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal;">- Masatoshi Nei</span><span style="font-size: 20px; text-align: justify;">。</span></span><span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">Nei得知他的兴趣之后，</span><span style="font-size: 20px; font-family: "PingFang SC"; text-align: justify;">接纳他成为自己的学生，并告诉他概率论在遗传学领域将可以解决很多问题。</span><span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">1972</span><span style="font-size: 20px; font-family: "PingFang SC"; text-align: justify;">年，他们发表论文</span><span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">Total number of individuals affected by a single deleterious mutation in a finite population (American Journal of Human Genetics, 1972)</span><span style="font-size: 20px; font-family: "PingFang SC"; text-align: justify;">。该文章利用</span><span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">diffusion</span><span style="font-size: 20px; font-family: "PingFang SC"; text-align: justify;">方法来研究医学遗传学中的问题，即有限群体中单个有害突变在被清除之前，携带杂合子和纯合子突变的病人有多少。凭此论文和他的渊博学识，李文雄获得博士学位。</span></p><p class="mb-0" style="box-sizing: border-box; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; line-height: 1.2; font-family: Georgia, serif; font-size: clamp(1.5rem, 2.1vw, 2rem); color: rgb(26, 26, 26); white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);"><span style="font-size: 20px; font-family: "PingFang SC"; text-align: justify;"><br></span></p><p class="p2" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 13px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-size: 20px;"><span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">1973</span>年，李文雄在威斯康辛麦迪逊的<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">James Crow</span>实验室进行了一年的博士后研究。随后，前往德州<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">-</span>休斯顿大学担任助理教授。<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">4</span>年后，李发表重要论文<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">Distribution of nucleotide differences between two randomly chosen cistrons in a finite population (Genetics, 1977)</span>，解决了基于<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">Sewall Wright</span>无限等位基因模型的情况下，有限群体中等位基因保存的问题。凭借该论文，李获得了中性理论创始人<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">Motoo Kimura</span>的写信支持。据李回忆，该推荐信也帮助他获得晋升的机会。在<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">70</span>年代后期，<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">DNA</span>序列已经获得大量积累。李敏锐意识到<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">DNA</span>序列可能对于解决演化领域的核心问题具有关键意义，于是开启了他辉煌的<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">80</span>年代。</span></p><p class="p2" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 13px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-size: 20px;"><span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">1981</span>年，他与<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">Takashi Gojobori </span>和<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">Nei</span>合作，发表第一篇<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">Nature</span>文章<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">Pseudogenes as a paradigm of neutral evolution</span>。该文章设计堪称优雅，触及了两个演化流派的根本争论：选择论和中性论。基于选择论的预期，假基因的突变率会比较低，因为假基因的突变没有选择优势，故而不是正向自然选择的对象。相反，在中性论的角度，假基因的突变率会比较高，因为功能基因的重要性，故而受到负向自然选择（即纯化选择）的影响更强。他们的研究发现支持中性突变理论，在当时颇具影响力。<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">1985</span>年，李文雄与吴仲义在<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">PNAS</span>杂志发表文章<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">Evidence for higher rates of nucleotide substitution in rodents than in man</span>，第一次阐述了分子钟与世代间隔时间之间的关系。他们用编码基因比较人和鼠的演化速度，他们发现，无论是编码区的同义突变、非同义突变，还是基因的上下游区域，鼠的演化速度更快。该发现也更符合中性论而非选择论的预期。<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">1987</span>年，李文雄与<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">Masako Tanimura</span>发表文章<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">The molecular clock runs more slowly in man than in apes and monkeys</span>（<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">Nature, 1987</span>）。该文章与当时<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">Allan Wilson</span>等人认为的分子钟恒定观点产生冲突，被<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">Allan Wilson</span>拒稿。<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">Allan Wilson</span>是诺奖得主鲍林学生（<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">Arthur Pardee</span>）的学生，此时的<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">Allan Wilson</span>作为中性理论的证明者（之一）已经暴得大名。早在<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">20</span>年前，<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">Allan Wilson</span>已<span style="font-size: 20px; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">经<span class="s2" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal;">发现灵长类白蛋白演化速度恒定（</span><span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal;">Science, 1967</span>），被认为是分子演化中性理论的强力证据。</span>但是，中性演化理论的提出者<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">Kimura</span>也是李文雄文章的审稿人。<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">Kimura</span>认为文章具有说服力和创新性。该文章也修正了<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">Kimura</span>自身对于分子演化的认知。此前<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">Kimura</span>认为分子演化速率不依赖于世代时间，但是该文章说服了<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">Kimura</span>，证实了分子钟依赖于世代时间的观点更符合中性理论。</span></p><p class="p2" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 13px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-size: 20px;">整个<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">80-90</span>年代，李文雄实验室还进行了一系列有价值的探索。例如，<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">1987</span>与<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">Paul Sharp</span>发表了<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">“</span>密码子适应指数<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">”</span>。同年，发现了植物线粒体和动物线粒体演化的差异，即植物线粒体比核基因和叶绿体演化的速度都更慢。<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">1989</span>年，估计出了单子叶和双子叶分化时间（<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">170 Myr</span>），这一时间早于当时流行观点（<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">120 Myr</span>）。<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">1993</span>年他与<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">Yuxin Fu</span>在<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">Genetics</span>发表了用于检测<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">DNA</span>片段是否所有突变均中性演化的统计检验方法，也就是<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">DnaSP</span>软件中的<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">D, D*, F and F*</span>检验。该方法的巧妙性在于利用了系谱（<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">genealogy</span>）结构的内外枝受到选择的差异，即外枝（<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">external branch</span>）比内枝（<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">internal branch</span>）更容易受到自然选择的影响。<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">90</span>年代的李文雄更加关注分子实验研究，也发表了一系列有价值的研究。<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">1995</span>年在<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">Science</span>杂志发表文章《<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">Adaptive Evolution of Color Vision Genes in Higher Primates</span>》以及<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">1999</span>年在<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">Nature</span>杂志发表文章《<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">Trichromatic vision in prosimians</span>》均研究探讨色觉基因在高等灵长类的适应性演化和起源。</span></p><p class="p2" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 13px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-size: 20px;">除了对中性演化理论的证明，以及基于序列分析的表型分子演化研究，李文雄还<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">90</span>年代<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">-2000</span>年后还发表了新的演化理论如<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">“</span>雄性驱动理论（<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">male-driven evolution</span>）<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">”</span>。例如，<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">Male-driven evolution of DNA sequences</span>（<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">Nature, 1993</span>）和<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">Strong male-driven evolution of DNA sequences in humans and apes</span>（<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">Nature</span>，<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">2002</span>），以及在<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">PNAS</span>杂志发表的<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">Weak male-driven molecular evolution in rodents</span>，都阐述高级灵长类、啮齿类中<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">“</span>雄性驱动理论<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">”</span>的表现。<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">“</span>雄性驱动理论<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">”</span>认为雄性比雌性的突变率更高，因为雄性比雌性每一代的雄性减数分裂次数更多。雄性驱动演化在高级灵长类中比啮齿类中更强。人类基因组草图在<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">2001</span>年完成之后，<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">Nature</span>杂志邀请李文雄撰写分析人类基因组的文章。他和合作者用短短<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">6</span>周完成了<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">Evolutionary analyses of the human genome (Nature, 2001)</span>。该文章有一些有趣发现，也提出了一些难题，例如蛋白质结构域共享是非常普遍的，同时很多结构域的排列也是很保守的，因此重复基因的真实数量评估是一个难点，尤其对于短肽序列。</span></p><p class="p3" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 13px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-size: 20px;"><span class="s3" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";">在基因重复的研究方面，李及其合作者也有一些有价值成果。例如，聚焦于重复基因和突变的文章</span>Role of duplicate genes in genetic robustness against null mutations (Nature, 2003)<span class="s3" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";">，发现重复基因可以补偿丢失功能突变的效应。研究重复基因表达的文章</span>Duplicate genes increase gene expression diversity within and between species (Nature genetics, 2004)<span class="s3" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC";">，发现重复基因可以增加物种内和物种间的基因表达的多样性。</span></span></p><p class="p2" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 13px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-size: 20px;">近年来，李进入一些新的领域。例如，他试图利用<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">C4</span>植物光和效率高的特点，把<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">C4</span>基因引入<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">C3</span>植物，来增加作物产量。同时，他也聚焦于一些基础性问题，如利用新的测序方法，来大规模鉴定转录因子结合位点。</span></p><p class="p2" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 13px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-size: 20px;">虽然是一位科学家，李的业余生活也很丰富。他时常陶醉在音乐中，享受音乐带来的幸福和灵感。音乐是他长久的爱好。从德州工作的时候，他和朋友们组织了<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">“</span>彩虹合唱团<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">”</span>。每周二进行练习和表演，持续了近<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">15</span>年。此外，他几乎每天都进行游泳锻炼，保持了健康的体魄。</span></p><p class="p2" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 13px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-size: 20px;">未来希望李教授为我们带来新的理论和科学发现。</span></p><p class="p2" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 13px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-size: 20px;">后记</span></p><p class="p3" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 13px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-size: 20px;">-----</span></p><p class="p2" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 13px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-size: 20px;">那一年，我保送到了中科院昆明动物研究所，选择了貌似有趣的动物学专业，方向是哺乳动物系统进化（演化）。初进实验室，我无知茫然的眼神，便望向了师兄（现广州大学教授）的桌面。那里，赫然矗立了两本书。他看出了我的兴趣，便隆重给我介绍起来。一本是<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">Molecular Evolution and Phylogenetics</span>，作者是<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">Masatoshi Nei</span>和<span class="s1" style="font-size: 20px; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: Arial;">Sudhir Kumar</span>；另外一本是《分子进化基础》，作者李文雄。多年以后，在芝加哥大学访问求学期间，我多次在校园见到李文雄老师，才知道他是芝加哥大学教授。有一天，终于鼓起勇气对他说：李老师，很荣幸见到您，您是分子演化领域传说中的人物，能否跟您合个影？他笑呵呵的答应了。</span></p><p class="p2" style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 13px; text-align: justify; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; white-space: normal;"><span style="font-size: 20px;">随着研究的深入，我逐渐阅读到更多他过往学术文献甚至访谈记录。对其研究工作和重要性有了更深入的了解。我很好奇，他作为一个演化学家的成长和思想历程，以及如何推动了这个领域的进步。于是就有了上文，参考了公开的资料同时结合我个人的学术解读。</span></p><p class="p3" style="margin-top: 0px;
