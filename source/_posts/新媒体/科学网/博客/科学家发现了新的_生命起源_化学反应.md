font-family: "times new roman";">今年早些时候（</span><a href="https://phys.org/news/2022-02-role-cyanide-early-earth-extraterrestrial.html" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">complex molecules</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">）。</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">在今天的细胞中，氨基酸是由称为α</span>-<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">酮酸（</span></span><a href="https://phys.org/news/2022-07-scientists-life-chemical-reactions.html" style="text-decoration: underline; font-family: 宋体;">）的前体利用氮和称为酶的特殊蛋白质生成的。研究人员发现，有证据表明，α</span>-<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">酮酸很可能存在于地球历史的早期。然而，许多人假设，在细胞生命出现之前，氨基酸一定是由完全不同的前体醛类（</span></span><a href="https://phys.org/news/2022-07-scientists-life-chemical-reactions.html" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">aldehydes</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）而非α</span>-<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">酮酸生成的，因为进行转化的酶尚不存在。但这一想法引发了关于如何以及何时从醛类转变为α</span>-<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">酮酸作为制造氨基酸的关键成分的争论。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">拉马纳拉亚南·克里希纳穆尔西和他的同事成功地利用氰化物驱动其他化学反应（</span><a href="https://phys.org/tags/chemical+reactions/" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">chemical reactions</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）后，怀疑氰化物，即使没有酶，也可能有助于将α</span>-<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">酮酸转化为氨基酸。因为他们知道需要某种形式的氮，所以他们加入了氨——一种早期地球上存在的氮。然后，通过反复试验，他们发现了第三种关键成分：二氧化碳。有了这种混合物，他们很快开始看到氨基酸的形成。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">拉马纳拉亚南·克里希纳穆尔西说：“我们原以为很难弄明白这一点，结果比我们想象的还要简单。”如果你只混合酮酸（</span>keto </span><a href="https://phys.org/tags/acid/" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">orotate</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">拉马纳拉亚南·克里希纳穆尔西说：“我们下一步要做的是继续探索这种混合物会产生什么样的化学反应。氨基酸能开始形成小蛋白质吗？这些蛋白质中是否有一种会回来并开始作为一种酶来制造更多的氨基酸（</span><a href="https://phys.org/tags/amino+acids/" style="text-decoration: underline; However, biology employs transamination of α-ketoacids to synthesize amino acids which are then transformed to nucleobases, implying an evolutionary switch—abiotically or biotically—of a prebiotic pathway involving the Strecker reaction into today’s biosynthetic pathways. Here we show that α-ketoacids react with cyanide and ammonia sources to form the corresponding α-amino acids through the Bucherer–Bergs pathway. An efficient prebiotic transformation of oxaloacetate to aspartate via N-carbamoyl aspartate enables the simultaneous formation of dihydroorotate, paralleling the biochemical synthesis of orotate as the precursor to pyrimidine nucleobases. Glyoxylate forms both glycine and orotate and reacts with malonate and urea to form aspartate and dihydroorotate.
