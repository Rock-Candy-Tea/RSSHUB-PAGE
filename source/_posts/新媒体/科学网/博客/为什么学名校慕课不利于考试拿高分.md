style="margin-bottom:10px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">以老文的经历和体会，</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">由多个甚至无数个状态叠加而成</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">的</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">如</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">薛定格的猫</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;color: #333333;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:宋体">所处的状态是死和活的叠加态），</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">而考试是把叠加态变成塌缩态</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">，</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">也就是把无限种可能</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">塌缩</span><span style="margin-bottom:10px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style="font-family: 宋体;line-height: 200%;color: #333333;letter-spacing: 0;font-size: 16px">难怪，诺贝尔物理学奖得主、</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">）</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">里，我不久就学会了识别那种能导向基本原理的内容，而撇开其他许多东西不管，以免它们壅塞我的心智而使之远离精髓。当然，这里的问题是，为了考试，人们都得把这些废物统统塞进自己的脑袋，而不管自己愿意与否。这种强制的结果使我气馁，以致在我通过最后一科考试以后的整整一年内对科学问题的任何思考都深感不快。</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">”</span></p><p style="margin-bottom:10px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px">最近，</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;line-height:200%;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">一项发表在《自然</span><span
