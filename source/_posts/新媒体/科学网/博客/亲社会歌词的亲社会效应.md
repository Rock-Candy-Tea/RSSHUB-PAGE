<p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">当今时代，媒体资源丰富。然而，不同的媒体内容与风格，对受众的心理效应可能极为不同。以歌曲为例，有的听了让人向善，即具有亲社会效应；有的听了让人向恶，即具有反社会效应。如果歌曲具有亲社会或反社会效应，那么，起作用的可能主要是歌词。那些带有亲社会歌词的歌曲，即为亲社会歌曲。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">有研究者（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Greitemeyer, 2009</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）用</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">3</span><span style="font-family:宋体">个实验，考察亲社会歌曲的亲社会效应，结果发现，</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">相比于中性</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">歌曲</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">听亲社会歌曲</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">提高了亲社会思想的通达性，导致</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">了</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">更多的人际移情，促进了助人行为。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">在实验一中，研究者将</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">34</span><span style="font-family:宋体">名大学生（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">19</span><span style="font-family:宋体">名女生，</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">11</span><span style="font-family:宋体">名男生，</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">4</span><span style="font-family:宋体">名未报告性别）随机分配到亲社会歌曲条件和中性歌曲条件。每种条件下的被试，均听两名歌手的各一首歌曲；不同条件下，同一歌手的歌曲风格相同，歌词内容不同。被试听过歌曲，完成一项词干补笔任务，共有</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">18</span><span style="font-family:宋体">个词干。这些词干可以补成亲社会性的单词，也可以补成中性的单词。以每名被试补出的亲社会单词占全部补出单词的比例，作为亲社会思想通达性的指标。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">结果表明，听亲社会歌曲的被试组（</span></span><em><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">M</span></span></em><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">=0.21, </span></span><em><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">SD</span></span></em><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">=0.11</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）比听中性歌曲的被试组（</span></span><em><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">M</span></span></em><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">=0.14, </span></span><em><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">SD</span></span></em><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">=0.08</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）补出了更高比例的亲社会单词，</span></span><em><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">t</span></span></em><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">(32)=2.05, </span></span><em><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">p</span></span></em><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman"><.05, </span></span><em><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">d</span></span></em><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">=0.73</span><span style="font-family:宋体">，即歌曲类型对亲社会思想通达性的效应是可靠的。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">在实验二中，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">研究者将</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">38</span><span style="font-family:宋体">名大学生（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">27</span><span style="font-family:宋体">名女生，</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">11</span><span style="font-family:宋体">名男生）随机分配到亲社会歌曲条件和中性歌曲条件。告知被试，他们参加的是一项市场调查，要求</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">听过每首歌</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">曲（材料同实验一）</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，表明自己对</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">它</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的喜欢程度。接着，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">告知</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">被试另一项研究</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">也</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">要求</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">被试</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">阅读来自</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">其他</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">被试的</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">短文</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。因为原定来读</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">短文</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的被试没来参加这项研究，就</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">请他们</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">阅读这两篇</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">短文</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">并做出</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">回答</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">第一篇短文描述作者与女友分手了，并为此十分痛苦。第二篇短文描述作者在校内体育比赛中摔断了腿，生活极为不便，为此十分痛苦。要求被试读过短文，评定自己对短文作者的同情程度。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 宋体;">作者以歌曲类型为被试间变量，以短文内容（即，失恋与伤腿）为被试内变量，进行重复测量的方差分析，结果表明，歌曲类型有显著的主效应，</span><em><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: "Times New Roman";">F</span></em><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: "Times New Roman";">(1,36)=6.51, </span><em><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: "Times New Roman";">p</span></em><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: "Times New Roman";"><.05, </span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px">η</span><span style="font-size: 10px;"><sup><span style="vertical-align: super; font-family: "Times New Roman";">2</span></sup><sup><span style="vertical-align: super; font-family: "Times New Roman";"></span></sup></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">=.15</span><span style="font-family:宋体">，交互作用不显著，</span></span><em><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: "Times New Roman";">F</span></em><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: "Times New Roman";">(1,36)=0.22, </span><em><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: "Times New Roman";">p</span></em><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: "Times New Roman";">=.64, </span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px">η</span><span style="font-size: 10px;"><sup><span style="vertical-align: super; font-family: "Times New Roman";">2</span></sup><sup><span style="font-family:宋体">在实验三中，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">研究者将</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">90</span><span style="font-family:宋体">名大学生（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">55</span><span style="font-family:宋体">名女生，</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">35</span><span style="font-family:宋体">名男生）随机分配到亲社会歌曲条件和中性歌曲条件。告知被试参加一项音乐偏好的市场调查，接着让被试听歌曲（材料同实验一）并进行评定。然后，主试给被试</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">2</span><span
