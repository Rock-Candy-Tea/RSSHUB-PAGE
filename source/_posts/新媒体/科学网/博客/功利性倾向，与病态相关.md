<p style="text-indent:29px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">当前的道德判断研究，往往采用道德两难情境，并用功利论和道义论的框架进行分析。这样的框架，</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">赞同功利性</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">选项（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Koenigs et al., 2007</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:29px"><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">不过，由临床病人来概括往往是有问题的，因为他们的缺陷导致功利性判断</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">可能是通过与非临床病人在品质上不同的心理机制的。</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">据此，研究者</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">Bartels & Pizarro, 2011</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）对</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">208</span><span style="font-family:宋体">名大学生（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">101</span><span style="font-family:宋体">名女生）进行测试，考察他们</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">对</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px">14</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">个</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">天桥类道德</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">困境和</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">精神病态、无意义感、权术主义的反应，预测</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">功利性偏向与上述临床病人有关的心理特质相关</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">也就是</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">说，预测那些反映贬低生命价值、情感冷漠和好支配人等人格特质的变异性能够预测功利性偏</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">向</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">结果表明，</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">那些在精神病态（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">α=.86</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）、无意义</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">感</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">α=.90</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）和权术主义（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">α=.78</span><span style="font-family:宋体">）上得分更高的被试，在</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">道德</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">困境中显示了对功利性选项的更大偏向。把全部</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">14</span><span style="font-family:宋体">个困境合并时（</span></span><em><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';font-size: 16px">r</span></em><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px">=.38, .21, .35, </span><em><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';font-size: 16px">p</span></em><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><.05<span style="font-family:宋体">），整体分析的结果是这样；对单个</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">道德</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">困境进行分析时，大部分困境的结果也是这样</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:32px"><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">为了控制性别和社会赞许性对功利性偏向的效应，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">研究者</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">对每个</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">核心</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">个体差异变量单独进行多重回归</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">结果显示，</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">在控制性别与</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">社会</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">赞许性的情况下，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">它们</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">均</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">能预测</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">功利性偏向。功利性偏向与精神变态、无意义感、权术主义之间的关系是稳健的。</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">当</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">用</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">个体差异变量</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">、社会赞许性、性别作为预测因子，进行多重回归</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">时</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，结果发现</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">精神变态和权术主义均能独特地预测平均的功利性偏向（</span><span style="font-family:Times New Roman">β=.23</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">由此可见，</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">在非临床总体中</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">也</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">发现</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">了精神变态与功利性偏向</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的联系，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">这</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">困境中，至少有两种不同的路径导致赞同功利性选项</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">：</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">其一</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">是在做道德决策时喜欢理性精细化思维的倾向</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">；其二</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，是对造成</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">减弱</span></span><span style=";font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:16px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，就像在特定大脑</span></span><span
