<h3 style="word-wrap: break-word; margin: 17px 0px; padding: 0px; font-size: 1em; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; white-space: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 21px;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-size: 24px;">袁隆平与杂交水稻</span></span></h3><p style="word-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 21px; text-indent: 28px;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">民以食为天。水稻是中国最重要的作物。新中国建立以来，水稻技术的研究和试验一直持续不懈地进行着。袁隆平培育的杂交水稻是中国农业研究的一项重大自主创新。</span><a name="_ftnref1" title href="http://blog.sciencenet.cn/home.php?mod=spacecp&ac=blog#_ftn1" style="word-wrap: break-word; color: rgb(0, 102, 153); text-decoration: none;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; color: #0000FF;">[1]</span></span></a></p><p style="word-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 21px; text-indent: 28px;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">袁隆平，</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">1930</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">年</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">9</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">月生于北京，</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">1949</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">年８月考入了重庆相辉学院农学系（后并入西南农学院，现西南大学）。</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; background-color: #FFFF00;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">1953</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">年</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">8</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">月毕业后分配到湖南省安江农校任教，在长达</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">19</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">年的教学生涯中，袁隆平一面教学，一面从事生产实践、选择课题进行科学研究。</span></span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">由于开展杂交水稻研究的需要，袁隆平</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">1971</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">年</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">2</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">月调到湖南省农业科学院专门从事杂交水稻研究工作。</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">1984</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">年</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">6</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">月成立了全国性的杂交水稻专门研究机构－－湖南杂交水稻研究中心，后又成立国家杂交水稻工程技术研究中心，均由袁隆平任中心主任。</span></p><p style="word-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 21px; text-indent: 28px;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">20</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">世纪</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">30</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">年代，美国杂交玉米的成功开发，为其他作物的杂交繁育提供了极大的刺激，人们开始研究利用杂交优势来开发杂交水稻。水稻是严格的自花授粉作物，花器小，因此不可能用人工去雄杂交的方法生产大面积所需要的大量种子，最有效的方法是利用细胞质上的雄性不育现象。杂交稻种的商业化生产包括复杂的三系方法：（</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">1</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">）找出细胞质上的雄性不育系；（</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">2</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">）通过它和保持系来生产具有不育性的，但可望具有遗传特征的后代；（</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">3</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">）通过这些种子，用“恢复”系来生产具有正常自花授粉能力的种子。由于杂交种子生产的困难，国际上大多数研究者中止了它们的研究。</span><a name="_ftnref2" title href="http://blog.sciencenet.cn/home.php?mod=spacecp&ac=blog#_ftn2" style="word-wrap: break-word; color: rgb(0, 102, 153); text-decoration: none;"><sup style="word-wrap: break-word;"><sup style="word-wrap: break-word;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; color: #0000FF;">[2]</span></span></sup></sup></a></p><p style="word-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 21px; text-indent: 28px;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">１９６０年袁隆平从一些学报上获悉杂交高粱、杂交玉米、无籽西瓜等，都已广泛应用于国内外生产中。于是开始研究杂交水稻。</span><a name="_ftnref3" title href="http://blog.sciencenet.cn/home.php?mod=spacecp&ac=blog#_ftn3" style="word-wrap: break-word; color: rgb(0, 102, 153); text-decoration: none;"><sup style="word-wrap: break-word;"><sup style="word-wrap: break-word;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; color: #0000FF;">[3]</span></span></sup></sup></a><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">１９６０年７月，他在早稻常规品种试验田里，发现了一株与众不同的水稻植株。第二年春天，他把这株变异株的种子播到试验田里，结果证明了上年发现的那个“鹤立鸡群”的稻株，是地地道道的“天然杂交稻”。他想：既然自然界客观存在着“天然杂交稻”，只要我们能探索其中的规律与奥秘，就一定可以按照我们的要求，培育出人工杂交稻来，从而利用其杂交优势，提高水稻的产量。这样，袁隆平从实践及推理中突破了水稻为自花传粉植物而无杂种优势的传统观念的束缚。于是，袁隆平立即把精力转到培育人工杂交水稻这一崭新课题上来。</span></p><p style="word-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 21px; text-indent: 28px;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; background-color: #FFFF00;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">1962</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">年，袁隆平在学校图书馆的《参考消息》上看到了英美科学家沃森和克里克提出了</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">DNA</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">双螺旋结构而获得诺贝尔奖的消息。这年暑假他自费到北京，去中国农科院找当年他的老师了解国外研究的进展。</span></span></p><p style="word-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 21px; text-indent: 28px;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">在１９６４年到１９６５年两年的水稻开花季节里，他和助手们每天头顶烈日，脚踩烂泥，低头弯腰，终于在稻田里找到了６株天然雄性不育的植株。经过两个春秋的观察试验，对水稻雄性不育材料有了较丰富的认识，他根据所积累的科学数据，</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; background-color: #FFFF00;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">撰写成了论文《水稻的雄性不孕性》，</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">1966</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">年</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">2</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">月发表在中国科学院院刊《科学通报》上。这是国内第一次论述水稻雄性不育性的论文，不仅详尽叙述水稻雄性不育株的特点，并就当时发现的材料区分为无花粉、花粉败育和部分雄性不育三种类型。</span></span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">那期《科学通报》是“文化大革命”停刊前最后一期，<span style="word-wrap: break-word; background-color: #FFFF00;">袁隆平的论文幸好被国家科委一个局领导看见了，就发函下去，要求湖南省科委和安江农校支持袁隆平的试验。</span>从１９６４年发现“天然雄性不育株”算起，袁隆平和助手们整整花了６年时间，先后用１０００多个品种，做了３０００多个杂交组合，仍然没有培育出不育株率和不育度都达到１００％的不育系来。</span></p><p style="word-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 21px; text-indent: 28px;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">袁隆平总结了６年来的经验教训，并根据自己观察到的不育现象，认识到必须跳出栽培稻的小圈子，重新选用亲本材料，提出利用“远缘的野生稻与栽培稻杂交”的新设想。<span style="word-wrap: break-word; background-color: #FFFF00;">在这一思想指导下，袁隆平带领助手李必湖于１９７０年１１月２３日在海南岛的普通野生稻群落中，发现一株雄花败育株，并用广场矮、京引６６等品种测交，发现其对野败不育株有保持能力，这就为培育水稻不育系和随后的“三系”配套打开了突破口，给杂交稻研究带来了新的转机。</span></span></p><p style="word-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 21px; text-indent: 28px;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体; background-color: #FFFF00;">是将“野败”这一珍贵材料封闭起来，自己关起门来研究，还是发动更多的科技人员协作攻关呢？</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">在这个重大的原则问题上，袁隆平毫不含糊、毫无保留地及时向全国育种专家和技术人员通报了他们的最新发现，并慷慨地把历尽艰辛才发现的“野败”奉献出来，分送给有关单位进行研究，协作攻克“三系”配套关。</span></p><p style="word-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 21px; text-indent: 28px;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">1972</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">年，农业部把杂交稻列为全国重点科研项目，组成了全国范围的攻关协作网。</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">1973</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">年，广大科技人员在突破“不育系”和“保持系”的基础上，选用</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">1000</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">多个品种进行测交筛选，找到了</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">1000</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">多个具有恢复能力的品种。张先程、袁隆平等率先找到了一批以</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">IR24</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">为代表的优势强、花粉量大、恢复度在</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">90</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">％以上的“恢复系”。</span></p><p style="word-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 21px; text-indent: 28px;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; background-color: #FFFF00;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">1973</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">年</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">10</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">月，袁隆平发表了题为《利用野败选育三系的进展》的论文，正式宣告我国籼型杂交水稻“三系”配套成功。这是我国水稻育种的一个重大突破。</span></span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">紧接着，他和同事们又相继攻克了杂种“优势关”和“制种关”，为水稻杂种优势利用铺平了道路。</span></p><p style="word-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 21px; text-indent: 28px;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">1975</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">年，在数以百计的县，同时进行地区性的生产试验；</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">1976</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">年，杂交水稻开始大田生产。</span></p><p style="word-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 21px; text-indent: 28px;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">生产实践证明，杂交水稻一般比常规水稻增产</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">20</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">％左右。自</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">1976</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">年大面积推广以来，截止</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">1999</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">年我国杂交水稻已累计种植</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">35</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">亿亩，增产稻谷</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">3000</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">多亿公斤。在</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">2000</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">年前后的几年里，全国杂交水稻年种植面积</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">2.3</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">亿亩，约占水稻面积的</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">50</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">％，产量占稻谷总产量的近</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">60</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">％，年增稻谷可养活</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">6000</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">万人口。</span><a name="_ftnref4" title href="http://blog.sciencenet.cn/home.php?mod=spacecp&ac=blog#_ftn4" style="word-wrap: break-word; color: rgb(0, 102, 153); text-decoration: none;"><sup style="word-wrap: break-word;"><sup style="word-wrap: break-word;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; color: #0000FF;">[4]</span></span></sup></sup></a></p><p style="word-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 21px; text-indent: 28px;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">杂交水稻比常规矮种水稻具有约</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">15</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">％的产量优势。杂交水稻在中国得到快速的扩散，从</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">1976</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">年到</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">1987</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">年约有</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">30</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">％的水稻面积用于杂交水稻种植；从</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">1976</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">年到</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">2000</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">年，在全国推广面积达</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">2.3</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">亿公顷，增产稻谷</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">2 400</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">亿千克。</span><a name="_ftnref5" title href="http://blog.sciencenet.cn/home.php?mod=spacecp&ac=blog#_ftn5" style="word-wrap: break-word; color: rgb(0, 102, 153); text-decoration: none;"><sup style="word-wrap: break-word;"><sup style="word-wrap: break-word;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; color: #0000FF;">[5]</span></span></sup></sup></a></p><p style="word-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 21px; text-indent: 28px;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">九十年代后期，美国学者布朗抛出“中国威胁论”，撰文说到下世纪３０年代，中国人口将达到１６亿，到时谁来养活中国，谁来拯救由此引发的全球性粮食短缺和动荡危机？这时，<span style="word-wrap: break-word; background-color: #FFFF00;">袁隆平向世界宣布：“中国完全能解决自己的吃饭问题，中国还能帮助世界人民解决吃饭问题”。</span></span></p><p style="word-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 21px; text-indent: 28px;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">1976</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">年开始，杂交水稻在全国大面积推广，比常规稻平均增产</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">20</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">％。</span></p><p style="word-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 21px; text-indent: 28px;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">据统计，到</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">2006</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">年止，我国累计推广种植杂交水稻</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">56</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">亿多亩，增加亩产</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">5200</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">多亿公斤。近年来，全国杂交水稻年种植面积２．４亿亩左右，全中国年增产的稻谷可以养活</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">7000</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">多万人口。</span><a name="_ftnref6" title href="http://blog.sciencenet.cn/home.php?mod=spacecp&ac=blog#_ftn6" style="word-wrap: break-word; color: rgb(0, 102, 153); text-decoration: none;"><sup style="word-wrap: break-word;"><sup style="word-wrap: break-word;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; color: #0000FF;">[6]</span></span></sup></sup></a></p><p style="word-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 21px; text-indent: 28px;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">袁隆平在</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">1986</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">年，就在其论文《杂交水稻的育种战略》中提出将杂交稻的育种从选育方法上分为三系法、两系法和一系法三个发展阶段，即育种程序朝着由繁至简且效率越来越高的方向发展；从杂种优势水平的利用上分为品种间、亚种间和远缘杂种优势的利用三个发展阶段，即优势利用朝着越来越强的方向发展。根据这一设想，杂交水稻每进入一个新阶段都是一次新突破，都将把水稻产量推向一个更高的水平。</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">1995</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">年８月，袁隆平郑重宣布：我国历经９年的两系法杂交水稻研究已取得突破性进展，可以在生产上大面积推广。</span></p><p style="word-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 21px; text-indent: 28px;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">1998</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">年８月，袁隆平又向新的制高点发起冲击。他向朱总理提出选育超级杂交水稻的研究课题。朱总理闻讯后非常高兴，当即划拨</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">1000</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">万元予以支持。袁隆平为此深受鼓舞。在海南三亚农场基地，袁隆平率领着一支由全国十多个省、区成员单位参加的协作攻关大军，日夜奋战，攻克了两系法杂交水稻难关。</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; background-color: #FFFF00;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">经过近一年的艰苦努力，超级杂交稻在小面积试种获得成功，亩产达到</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">800</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">公斤，并在西南农业大学等地引种成功。目前，超级杂交稻正走向大面积试种推广中。</span></span><a name="_ftnref7" title href="http://blog.sciencenet.cn/home.php?mod=spacecp&ac=blog#_ftn7" style="word-wrap: break-word; color: rgb(0, 102, 153); text-decoration: none;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; color: #0000FF;">[7]</span></a></p><p style="word-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 21px; text-indent: 28px;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">2000</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">年，超级杂交稻实现百亩示范片亩产７００公斤的第一期目标，</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">2004</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">年，超级杂交稻实现百亩示范片亩产</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">800</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">公斤的第二期目标，现在正朝着百亩示范片亩产</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">900</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">公斤的第三期目标努力。</span></p><p style="word-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 21px; text-indent: 28px;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">杰出的贡献使袁隆平成了得奖专业户：</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">1981</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">年，袁隆平获得中国第一个国家特等发明奖；</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">2001</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">年，他又获得了首届国家最高科学技术奖……在国际上，他</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">14</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">次捧回大奖。荣获</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">2004</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">年世界粮食奖。</span></p><p style="word-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 21px; text-indent: 28px;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; background-color: #FFFF00;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">1995</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">年</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">5</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">月，袁隆平终于当选为中国工程院院士。</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">2007</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">年</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">4</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">月当选美国科学院院士。美国科学院院士中唯一的中国工程院院士。</span></span></p><p style="word-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 21px; text-indent: 28px;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">1998</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">年的一项无形资产评估，“袁隆平”三个字的品牌价值被估价超过</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">1000</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">亿元。</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">1999</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">年，中国发现的一颗小行星被命名为“袁隆平星”。</span></p><p style="word-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 21px; text-indent: 28px;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">袁隆平，“杂交水稻之父”。深受杂交稻惠泽的中国农民，用生动而朴实的语言<span style="word-wrap: break-word; background-color: #FFFF00;">称颂袁隆平为“当代神农”、“米菩萨”、“米财神”。</span></span></p><p style="word-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 21px; text-indent: 28px;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">2005</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">年</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">7</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">月，袁隆平院士提出<span style="word-wrap: break-word; background-color: #FFFF00;">“杂交水稻外交”</span>的建议，就是积极在发展中国家推广杂交水稻，帮助解决他们的吃饭问题，扩大中国的影响，以此促进双边关系的发展。袁隆平院士的这一建议，得到了党和国家领导人和有关部门的高度重视。</span></p><p style="word-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 21px; text-indent: 28px;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">2005</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">年</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">10</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">月</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">18</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">日，国务委员唐家璇会见袁隆平院士时说：“‘杂交水稻外交’将是我国‘走出去’战略的一项重要内容，也将是今后我国经济外交的一块王牌。”</span><a name="_ftnref8" title href="http://blog.sciencenet.cn/home.php?mod=spacecp&ac=blog#_ftn8" style="word-wrap: break-word; color: rgb(0, 102, 153); text-decoration: none;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; color: #0000FF;">[8]</span></span></a></p><p style="word-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 21px; text-indent: 28px;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";"> </span></p><p style="word-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 21px; text-indent: 28px;"><strong style="word-wrap: break-word;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; line-height: 28.5px; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 19px;">简短的小结</span></strong></p><p style="word-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 21px; text-indent: 28px;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">1 </span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">袁隆平的杂交水稻研究，不是完全凭经验的试试碰碰（</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">trial-and-error</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">），而是在遗传学理论指导的探索性研究。</span></p><p style="word-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 21px; text-indent: 28px;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">2 </span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">杂交水稻的研究，属于应用和开发研究，但袁隆平在杂交水稻的基础研究方面，作出了重大的贡献，如关于水稻雄性不孕性的研究，关于“三系法”的研究，以及关于杂交稻的育种从选育方法上分为三系法、两系法和一系法三个发展阶段的理论；以及野生稻群落的发现。<span style="word-wrap: break-word; background-color: #FFFF00;">这些基础研究就是“应用基础研究”，也属于司托克斯所谓的“应用激发的基础研究”，主要来源于社会需求的拉引。</span></span></p><p style="word-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 21px; text-indent: 28px;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">3</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">杂交水稻成功的创新，来源于技术创新与制度创新的有机结合。有人曾说，<span style="word-wrap: break-word; background-color: #FFFF00;">中国农民吃饭靠“两平”，一靠邓小平的责任制，二靠袁隆平的杂交水稻。从学术的观点看，前者指的是制度创新，后者指的是是技术创新。</span></span></p><p style="word-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 21px; text-indent: 28px;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">4 </span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">杂交水稻产生了巨大的经济效益。袁隆平主持研究的杂交水稻项目，在基础研究阶段的总投入不超过</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">200</span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">万元，但在其后的成果应用中却创造了巨大的经济效益和社会效应。</span><a name="_ftnref9" title href="http://blog.sciencenet.cn/home.php?mod=spacecp&ac=blog#_ftn9" style="word-wrap: break-word; color: rgb(0, 102, 153); text-decoration: none;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; color: #0000FF;">[9]</span></span></a></p><p style="word-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 21px; text-indent: 28px;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: "Times New Roman";">5 </span><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体;">杂交水稻的研究培养了大批人才，而且产生了很好的国际政治效益（“水稻外交”）。</span></p><p style="word-wrap: break-word; margin-top: 1.12em; margin-bottom: 1.12em; padding: 0px; font-family: Tahoma, Helvetica, SimSun, sans-serif, Hei; font-size: 14px; white-space: normal; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; line-height: 21px; text-indent: 28px;"><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体; background-color: #FFFF00;">6 袁隆平不仅是“把论文写在祖国的大地上”的典范，也是“把论文发在祖国的期刊上”的典范。</span></p><p><span style="word-wrap: break-word; font-family: 宋体; background-color: #FFFF00;"><br></span></p><p><br></p>                    <br><br>
