<p><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 14px"></span></strong></p><p><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 14px">   2</span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: Calibri;font-size: 14px">022</span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 14px">年</span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: Calibri;font-size: 14px">6</span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 14px">月</span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: Calibri;font-size: 14px">17</span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 14px">日，</span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 14px">在文德楼听新教师试讲之后，在</span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 14px">文德楼南出口楼下，与年轻教师交流，</span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 14px">表达</span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 14px">了我所理解的</span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 14px">关于大学课堂</span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 14px">的</span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 14px">一些观点</span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 14px">，</span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 14px">点点</span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 14px">感悟</span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 14px">分享如下</span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 14px">：</span></strong></p><p><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 14px"><br></span></strong></p><p style="margin-left:28px"><span style="font-family:Calibri;font-weight:bold;font-size:14px">1. </span><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 14px">润物细无声的课程思政。</span></strong><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">课堂教学肯定不是冷冰冰的知识传递或技能训练。事实上，教师的一句话、一举手、一投足中都包含着对学生的影响力。所举的例子、对社会和环境的关注与热爱、课堂活动的热情组织等都在无时无刻地释放我们的态度和观点。</span></p><p><span style=";font-family:Calibri;font-size:14px"> </span></p><p style="margin-left:28px"><span style="font-family:Calibri;font-weight:bold;font-size:14px">2. </span><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 14px">讲课要想方设法增加</span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 14px">课堂的</span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 14px">不可替代性。</span></strong><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">因为数字时代，</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">各种</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">数字资源来袭，知识获取的管道无数条</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">。</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">那么，学生</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">为什么要</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">听我们的课呢？那我们的课</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">一定</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">要具有</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">不可替代</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">性</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">才行。</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">为此，我们</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">就要想方设法在风格、随机性</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">、</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">因材施教</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">和语言魅力等</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">方面</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">注入</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">独特性，打造吸引</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">力，以增加不可替代性；</span></p><p><span style=";font-family:Calibri;font-size:14px"> </span></p><p style="margin-left:28px"><span style="font-family:Calibri;font-weight:bold;font-size:14px">3. </span><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 14px">课堂中要激发</span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 14px">学生说。</span></strong><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">力求打开学生的听</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">、</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">说</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">、</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">读</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">、</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">写</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">、</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">练等五大认知通道，要</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">力求</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">尝试让学生在说、问、讨论等交互活动中获得</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">、</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">理解</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">知识，并增加表达能力和人际交往能力</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">。</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">工程教育中要培养学生的沟通能力，然而</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">一般工程专业</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">没有专门的沟通课程，事实上也</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">没有多余学时开设</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">专门的沟通课程来学习</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">训练</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">沟通技能，我们每位教师</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">可</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">尽量尝试在本课程中增加学生与老师沟通以及学生之间相互沟通的频次和深度，如此提升学生的沟通能力。</span></p><p><span style=";font-family:Calibri;font-size:14px"> </span></p><p style="margin-left:28px"><span style="font-family:Calibri;font-weight:bold;font-size:14px">4. </span><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 14px">研究是工程教育的</span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 14px">基本毕业要求</span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 14px">，课堂教学也要贯彻研究能力的培养。</span></strong><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">提高学生提出问题、分析问题以及解决问题能力是人工智能时代对咱们</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">课堂教学</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">的新呼唤，</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">研究能力不是仅仅放在研究生阶段培养的，我们要在</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">本科教学中贯彻研究</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">与创新</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">能力的培养。</span></p><p><span style=";font-family:Calibri;font-size:14px"> </span></p><p style="margin-left:28px"><span style="font-family:Calibri;font-weight:bold;font-size:14px">5. </span><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-size: 14px">现代工具拓展。</span></strong><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">现代</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">社会，各种新</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">知识与</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">新</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">信息爆炸，通过各种软件工具可以增加认知的广度和深度，专业教学中</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">我们</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">要尝试贯彻</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">各种</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">软件工具使用的教育与辅导；同时，</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">教学本身</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">也要借助于自媒体等工具平台，不断延伸自己的辅导力量。给学生讲解的</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">点点滴滴之辅导</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">，尽量</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">尝试明晰主题并以一个个音视频</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">单位录制下来，放到</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">咱们的</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">学习通平台</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">上</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">，如此，通过<span style="font-family:Calibri">3</span></span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;font-size:14px">-5</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">年</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">的</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">积累</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">也能够</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">形成</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">比较壮观的</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">课程小资源。</span></p><p><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"></span><br></p><p><br></p>                    <br><br>
