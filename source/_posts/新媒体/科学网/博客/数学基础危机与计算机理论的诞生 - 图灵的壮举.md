<p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; -webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);"><span style="font-kerning: none">图灵的论著和工作为计算机理论的诞生奠定了基础，为了理解这一壮举的背景，需要回顾</span><span style="font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-font-kerning: none;">20</span><span style="font-kerning: none">世纪初那场著名的数学基础危机。</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); min-height: 19px;"><span style="font-kerning: none"></span><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; -webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);"><span style="font-kerning: none">欧几里得（</span><span style="-webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; -webkit-font-kerning: none;">前</span><span style="font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; -webkit-font-kerning: none;">325</span><span style="-webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; -webkit-font-kerning: none;">年</span><span style="-webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; -webkit-font-kerning: none;">）的《几何原本》一直被认为是最早用严格的逻辑结构建立学科体系的典范，但其不足之处也一直为历代学者所关心，其中一个具有争议的议题是第五公设，长达两千年之久所有企图推导出此公设的努力都失败，却促成了非欧几何的建立，引起了数学基础的重大危机，其核心问题涉及到：在什么终极基础上命题可以称为</span><span style="-webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-font-kerning: none;">“</span><span style="-webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; -webkit-font-kerning: none;">真</span><span style="-webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-font-kerning: none;">”?</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); min-height: 19px;"><span style="font-kerning: none"></span><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; -webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);"><span style="font-kerning: none">在人们寻找解决方案时，不得不将</span><span style="font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-font-kerning: none;">“</span><span style="font-kerning: none">真实性</span><span style="font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-font-kerning: none;">”</span><span style="font-kerning: none">和</span><span style="font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-font-kerning: none;">“</span><span style="font-kerning: none">可证性</span><span style="font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-font-kerning: none;">”</span><span style="font-kerning: none">脱钩：欧几里得几何公理系统的所有定理是</span><span style="font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-font-kerning: none;">“</span><span style="font-kerning: none">真实的</span><span style="font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-font-kerning: none;">”</span><span style="font-kerning: none">，换句话说，欧几里得几何学的合法性突然变得有问题。当然，证明仍然是检验新定理有效性的标准，但人们不能再依赖公理系统的论证。</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); min-height: 19px;"><span style="font-kerning: none"></span><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; -webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);"><span style="font-kerning: none">康托尔（</span><span style="font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-font-kerning: none;">1845-1916</span><span style="font-kerning: none">）正是为了给这些令人不安的发展提供一个明确的解决方案，发展了著名的集合论。由于他的开创性工作，使整个数学大厦有了新的基础，天空似乎又开始晴朗了。不幸的是，在康托里理论本身中很快就发现了悖论。</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); min-height: 19px;"><span style="font-kerning: none"></span><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; -webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);"><span style="font-kerning: none">于是，出现了三个相互竞争的数学流派，旨在应对这场危机。</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); min-height: 19px;"><span style="font-kerning: none"></span><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; -webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);"><span style="font-kerning: none">一，逻辑主义，形式主义与直觉主义【</span><span style="font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-font-kerning: none;">1</span><span style="font-kerning: none">】</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); min-height: 19px;"><span style="font-kerning: none"></span><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; -webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);"><span style="font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-font-kerning: none;">1</span><span style="font-kerning: none">，逻辑主义</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); min-height: 19px;"><span style="font-kerning: none"></span><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; -webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);"><span style="font-kerning: none">逻辑主义试图将数学归结为逻辑，使其受益于逻辑的严谨性，认为数的性质是推导出来的。这是英国哲学家和数学家罗素（</span><span style="font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-font-kerning: none;">1872-1970</span><span style="font-kerning: none">）与他以前的老师怀特海（</span><span style="font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-font-kerning: none;">1861-1947</span><span style="font-kerning: none">）合作进行的巨大计划。</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); min-height: 19px;"><span style="font-kerning: none"></span><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; -webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);"><span style="font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-font-kerning: none;">2</span><span style="font-kerning: none">，形式主义</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); min-height: 19px;"><span style="font-kerning: none"></span><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; -webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);"><span style="font-kerning: none">形式主义认为数学是一种智力游戏，在所开发的一系列操作中，数学家不必担心他所操作的符号的性质：对他来说唯一重要的是命题的非矛盾性和证明的一致性。数学家不必起始公理是真还是假，而只需问公理是否一致和完整。这一立场是由德国数学家希尔伯特（</span><span style="font-stretch: normal; margin-bottom: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); min-height: 19px;"><span style="font-kerning: none"></span><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; -webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);"><span style="font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-font-kerning: none;">3</span><span style="font-kerning: none">，直觉主义</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); min-height: 19px;"><span style="font-kerning: none"></span><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; -webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);"><span style="font-kerning: none">直觉主义倡导将数学建立在算术的基础上，认为算术是人类头脑创造性自由和对时间的原始直觉基础上的产物，拒绝某些经典的推理类型，因为它们在应用于无限集时导致矛盾。直觉主义的代表人物荷兰数学家</span><span style="font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-font-kerning: none;">Brouwer</span><span style="font-kerning: none">（</span><span style="font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-font-kerning: none;">1881-1966</span><span style="font-kerning: none">），为各种构造主义铺平了道路。</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); min-height: 19px;"><span style="font-kerning: none"></span><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; -webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);"><span style="font-kerning: none">二，图灵的贡献</span><span style="font-kerning: none">【</span><span style="font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-font-kerning: none;">2</span><span style="font-kerning: none">】</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); min-height: 19px;"><span style="font-kerning: none"></span><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; -webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);"><span style="font-kerning: none">这时年轻的图灵（</span><span style="font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-font-kerning: none;">1912-1954</span><span style="font-kerning: none">）出场了，投身于这场备受争议的数学基础问题中。图灵受过数学训练并精通逻辑，对于逻辑主义，按照他的说法，数是一个原始的数据，不能被还原为集合的特征。在这一点上，他同意直觉主义，只是他不能接受这个学派对数学世界的截断。在</span><span style="font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-font-kerning: none;">1935</span><span style="font-kerning: none">年的春天，</span><span style="font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-font-kerning: none;">22</span><span style="font-kerning: none">岁的他报名参加了形式主义的信徒麦克斯</span><span style="font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-font-kerning: none;">-</span><span style="font-kerning: none">纽曼</span><span style="font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-font-kerning: none;">(Maxwell Newman</span><span style="font-kerning: none">，</span><span style="font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-font-kerning: none;">1897-1984)</span><span style="font-kerning: none">在剑桥大学开设的课程。</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); min-height: 19px;"><span style="font-kerning: none"></span><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "PingFang SC"; -webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);"><span style="font-kerning: none">麦克斯</span><span style="font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-font-kerning: none;">-</span><span style="font-kerning: none">纽曼名声在外的是他在组合拓扑方面的工作，不过他也可能是剑桥大学在数理逻辑方面最有见识的人。纽曼整个课程的高潮是对哥德尔不完备性定理的证明。</span></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); min-height: 19px;"><span style="font-kerning: none"></span><br></p><p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; -webkit-text-stroke-color: rgb(0, 0, 0); background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);"><span style="font-family: "PingFang SC"; -webkit-font-kerning: none;">此外，纽曼的课程也涵盖了尚未解决的“判定问题”（</span><span style="font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-text-stroke-width: initial;">Entscheidungsproblem</span><span style="font-family: "PingFang SC"; -webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; -webkit-font-kerning: none;">“</span><span style="font-family: "PingFang SC"; -webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; -webkit-font-kerning: none;">机械过程</span><span style="font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; -webkit-font-kerning: none;">”</span><span style="font-family: "PingFang SC"; -webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; -webkit-font-kerning: none;">，纽曼指的不是一台机器。机器也许能够进行简单的算术，但几乎不能解决实际意义的数学问题。纽曼暗指的是后人称为</span><span style="font-family: "Trebuchet MS"; -webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; -webkit-font-kerning: none;">“</span><span style="font-family: "PingFang SC"; -webkit-text-stroke-width: initial; -webkit-font-kerning: none;">算法</span><span style="font-family: "Trebuchet MS";
