<p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;">著名工程师和发明家的名言</p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;">武夷山 译</p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><br></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;">    Workflowmax网站于2020年4月提供了一些著名工程师和发明家的名言（博主：原文如此，其实有的语录的主人并非工程师，只是语录内容涉及工程或工程师），下面我翻译其中一些，与大家分享。这是第一部分。理解或表达不妥之处，请博友指正。</p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"> </p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><strong>1.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal;font-variant-east-asian: normal;font-weight: normal;font-stretch: normal;font-size: 9px;line-height: normal;font-family: 'Times New Roman'">  </span></strong><strong><span style="font-family:宋体;font-weight:normal">“Engineers like to solve problems. If there are no problems handily available, they will create their own problems."</span></strong><br> - Scott Adams</p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;">    工程师喜欢解决问题。如果没有现成的问题可供研究，他们就自己造出一些问题。</p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><strong>2.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal;font-variant-east-asian: normal;font-weight: normal;font-stretch: normal;font-size: 9px;line-height: normal;font-family: 'Times New Roman'">  </span></strong><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">“Perfection is achieved, not when there is nothing more to add, but when there is nothing left to take away.”<br> -</span> Antoine de Saint-Exupery，安托万·德·圣-埃克苏佩里（1900-1944），<a href="https://baike.baidu.com/item/%E6%B3%95%E5%9B%BD/1173384" target="_blank"><span style="color: windowtext">法国</span></a>飞行员、作家</p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;">    增之一分则多余、损之一分则不足的时候，才算是是实现了完美。</p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><strong>3.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal;font-variant-east-asian: normal;font-weight: normal;font-stretch: normal;font-size: 9px;line-height: normal;font-family: 'Times New Roman'">  </span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-weight:normal">“The problem in this business isn’t to keep people from stealing your ideas; it's making them steal your ideas!”</span></strong><br> - Howard Aiken（1900-1973），计算机科学先驱，世界上第一台大型数字式自动计算机（IBM公司的马克一号计算机）的设计者</p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;">这个行当的问题不是阻止别人偷你的创意，而是让他们偷你的创意！</p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><strong>4.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal;font-variant-east-asian: normal;font-weight: normal;font-stretch: normal;font-size: 9px;line-height: normal;font-family: 'Times New Roman'">  </span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-weight:normal">“Math is my Passion. Engineering is my Profession.”</span></strong><br> - Wilfred James Dolor，美国卡内基梅隆大学人类感知实验室的工程师</p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;">    数学是我的挚爱。工程是我的专业。</p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><strong>5.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal;font-variant-east-asian: normal;font-weight: normal;font-stretch: normal;font-size: 9px;line-height: normal;font-family: 'Times New Roman'">  </span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-weight:normal">“The human foot is a masterpiece of engineering and a work of art.”</span></strong><br> - Leonardo da Vinci，达.芬奇</p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;">    人脚是一桩工程杰作，也是一件艺术品。</p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><strong>6.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal;font-variant-east-asian: normal;font-weight: normal;font-stretch: normal;font-size: 9px;line-height: normal;font-family: 'Times New Roman'">  </span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-weight:normal">“The fewer moving parts, the better." "Exactly. No truer words were ever spoken in the context of engineering.”</span></strong><br> - Christian Cantrell，美国软件工程师、作家</p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;">    “活动零部件越少，则越好。”“没错。在工程学语境内，从来没有哪句话比这句话更具真理性。”</p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><strong>7.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal;font-variant-east-asian: normal;font-weight: normal;font-stretch: normal;font-size: 9px;line-height: normal;font-family: 'Times New Roman'">  </span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-weight:normal">“When you want to know how things really work, study them when they’re coming apart.”</span></strong><br> - William Gibson，美国科幻作家</p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;">若你想知道事物到底是怎么工作的，那就在它们快要散架时对之进行研究。 </p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><strong>8.<span style="font-variant-numeric: normal;font-variant-east-asian: normal;font-weight: normal;font-stretch: normal;font-size: 9px;line-height: normal;font-family: 'Times New Roman'">  </span></strong><strong><span style="font-family: 宋体;font-weight:normal">“I’ve never seen a job being done by a five-hundred-person engineering team that couldn’t be done better by fifty people.”</span></strong><br> - C. Gordon Bell，计算机设计专家、微软公司荣休研究员、美国国家工程院院士、国家科学院院士</p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px 24px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;">    我从没见过有一件由五百人的工程团队做的事不能由50人来完成且做得更好。</p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;"><strong><span style="font-family:宋体;font-weight:normal">9. “A common mistake that people make when trying to design something completely foolproof is to underestimate the ingenuity of complete fools.”</span></strong><br> - Douglas Adams（1952-2001），英国著名作家</p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;">    人们在试图设计十足安全可靠的东西时易犯的共同错误是，他们低估了十足傻瓜的聪明程度。</p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;">10. “<span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun;">Strive for perfection in everything you do. Take the best that exists and make it better. When it does not exist, design it.</span>”<br> - Sir Henry Royce（亨利·莱斯爵士，1863-1933），英国工程师和汽车设计师，以生产高级轿车和航空发动机闻名，也是<a href="https://baike.baidu.com/item/%E5%8A%B3%E6%96%AF%E8%8E%B1%E6%96%AF/65611" target="_blank"><span style="color: windowtext">劳斯莱斯</span></a>公司创始人之一</p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 15px; background: white; line-height: 1.75em;">    做任何事都要追求完美。利用已有的最好的东西，并使之更好。如果尚且不存在最好的东西，那就设计一个。</p><p style="line-height: 1.75em;"> </p><p><br></p>                    <br><br>
