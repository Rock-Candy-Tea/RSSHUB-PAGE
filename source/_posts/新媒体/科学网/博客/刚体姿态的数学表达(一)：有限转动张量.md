line-height: 150%; text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 16px;">欧拉角是经典刚体动力学常用的广义坐标。它特别适合讨论章动角 </span><span style="font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 20px; white-space: normal; text-indent: 35px;"><em>e</em></strong></em></strong><sup>(3) </sup><span style="font-size: 16px;">相对</span><strong><em><strong style="font-family: "times new roman";
