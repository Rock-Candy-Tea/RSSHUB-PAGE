white-space: normal; text-indent: 41px; line-height: 24px; text-align: center;"><span style="font-family:楷体">地蜂访花</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 41px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体">中午</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">11</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">点半左右，阳光渐渐强了起来，白云也飘到了山顶，草丛上的露水也慢慢少了起来。站在草丛里，也能听到蜜蜂飞舞发出的嗡嗡声，我和小吴挥着扫网，忙着捕捉蜜蜂。大约用时两个小时，我俩就收获了不少的蜜蜂。小吴赶紧在车里做起了针插标本，十分兴奋。</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 41px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202107/31/093126neaimseyp3i3crrp.jpeg" title alt="WechatIMG821.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p><p style="text-align: center;
