font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank">UCF</a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">）的研究人员开发出了世界上第一台光学示波器（</span><a href="https://www.ucf.edu/news/ucf-develops-the-worlds-first-optical-oscilloscope/" style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank">UCF</a> <a href="https://www.ucf.edu/news/ucf-develops-the-worlds-first-optical-oscilloscope/" style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank">Team develops the world's first optical oscilloscope</a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">）。这是一种能够测量光的电场的仪器。该设备将光的振荡转换成电信号，就像医院的监控器将病人的心跳转换成电振荡一样。</span><br></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">迄今为止，由于光波振荡（</span><a href="https://phys.org/tags/light+waves/" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">light waves</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"> oscillates<span style="font-size: 18px; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">UCF</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">从事这项研究的物理学副教授迈克尔·奇尼</span>(</span><a href="https://www.nature.com/articles/s41566-021-00924-6#auth-Michael-Chini" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">Michael Chini</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">说：“光纤通信利用光使事情变得更快，但我们在功能上仍然受到示波器速度的限制。而我们的光学示波器可以将速度提高约</span>1<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">万倍。”</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">相关研究结果于</span>2021<span style="font-size: 18px;
