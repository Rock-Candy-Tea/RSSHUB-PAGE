<p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 14px;"><span style="font-family:Calibri">不了解国内是否有类似技术，但这种技术的临床应用，特别是在健康行业的应用价值，会十分巨大。因为能给患者和医生提供更精确的血管图像资料，特殊之处在于定位精准，图像清晰，生理数据多样。</span></span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 14px;"><span style="font-family:Calibri">2021</span>年，日本初创公司<span style="font-family:Calibri">Luxonus</span>制作了一张非同寻常的小腿内侧照片。<span style="font-family:Calibri">Luxonus</span>使用</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 14px;">的新型</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 14px;">光声<span style="font-family:Calibri">3D</span>扫描仪</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 14px;">获得这种</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 14px;">彩色的<span style="font-family:Calibri">3D</span>图像</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 14px;">能</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 14px;">显示了腿部大小血管的</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 14px;">精细</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 14px;">结构，</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 14px;">并能显示</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 14px;">血液流动</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 14px;">情况</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 14px;">。与任何其他类型的医学成像相比，</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 14px;">这些</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 14px;">细节是前所未有的。</span><br></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:Calibri;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">首席技术官孝说八木</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">说，</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:Calibri">Luxonus</span></span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">的技术是基于五年的三维血管成像研究项目的结果</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">，</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">是</span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">日本内阁府</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">在</span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">高风险、高影响力的研究和发展</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">方向投资的</span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">一个大型国家项目</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">。</span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202202/06/094943ommpanjhr93jrprb.jpg" title alt="d42473-021-00503-x_19867616.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:Calibri;font-size:14px"><a href="https://www.nature.com/articles/d42473-021-00503-x" target="_blank">A bold new vision (nature.com)</a></span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:Calibri;font-size:14px">Luxonus<span style="font-family:宋体">光声成像设备是一种新一代医学成像技术，可以产生更多、更详细的数据，对患者和操作人员来说风险更小、负担更少，而且成本明显低于超声波、计算机断层扫描</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">(CT)</span><span style="font-family:宋体">和磁共振成像</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">(MRI)</span><span style="font-family:宋体">。</span></span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:Calibri;font-size:14px">Luxonus<span style="font-family:宋体">的扫描仪通过将脉冲激光束定向到成像区域。在血液循环系统的情况下，血液中的血红蛋白在激光的作用下产生超声波。通过独特的半球探测器阵列进行扫描，可以创建各种不同方向的血管结构的</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">3D</span><span style="font-family:宋体">图像。产生的图像以前所未有的清晰度描绘了血管网络的结构，分辨率高达</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">0.1</span><span style="font-family:宋体">毫米。</span></span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:Calibri;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">光声成像技术有</span>20<span style="font-family:宋体">年历史了，但</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">Luxonus</span><span style="font-family:宋体">是为数不多的几家制造出能产生</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">3D</span><span style="font-family:宋体">图像的光声成像设备的公司之一。在为数不多的能够生成</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">3D</span><span style="font-family:宋体">图像的设备中，只有</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">Luxonus</span><span style="font-family:宋体">版本能够生成</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">18 x 29</span><span style="font-family:宋体">厘米的图像，深度为</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">3</span><span style="font-family:宋体">厘米。</span></span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:Calibri;font-size:14px">“<span style="font-family:宋体">我们还开发了使用高功率、两波长脉冲激光的实时图像重建技术，</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">”Yagi</span><span style="font-family:宋体">补充说。所提供的波长是</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">797</span><span style="font-family:宋体">和</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">835</span><span style="font-family:宋体">纳米或</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">756</span><span style="font-family:宋体">和</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">797</span><span style="font-family:宋体">纳米。与核磁共振成像和</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">CT</span><span style="font-family:宋体">不同，光声</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">3D</span><span style="font-family:宋体">扫描仪能够检测</span></span><strong><span style="font-family: Calibri;color: #0000FF;font-size: 14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">血液的氧饱和度</span></span></strong><span style=";font-family:Calibri;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，并区分血管和吲哚菁绿</span>——<span style="font-family:宋体">一种流行的医学诊断染料。此外，通过将阵列保持在同一位置，可以实现详细的</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">4D</span><span style="font-family:宋体">可视化，比如指尖动脉的实时运动和脉动。</span></span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:Calibri;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">与</span>MRI<span style="font-family:宋体">和</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">CT</span><span style="font-family:宋体">相比，光声三维扫描仪有很多优点。与</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">MRI</span><span style="font-family:宋体">和</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">CT</span><span style="font-family:宋体">不同，光声</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">3D</span><span style="font-family:宋体">扫描仪能够对静脉和动脉进行颜色区分，从而检测血液的氧饱和度。与</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">CT</span><span style="font-family:宋体">扫描不同，血管成像不需要造影剂，</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">而</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">X</span></span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">射线</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">和</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">M</span></span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;font-size:14px">RI</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">的</span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">强磁场对患者或机器操作人员都没有风险。也许最有希望的优势是</span>Luxonus<span style="font-family:宋体">成像可以在办公室进行。扫描可以很简单，把一只手放在检测板上，然后保持不动。</span></span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:Calibri;font-size:14px">Luxonus<span style="font-family:宋体">成像在活体动物研究中也有很大的应用潜力。植入小鼠体内的肿瘤，用动物用光声</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">3D</span><span style="font-family:宋体">扫描仪成像，揭示了肿瘤中血管生长。肿瘤持续血管化，以及</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">治疗效果评估</span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">，可以每天检查。同样，注射药物的扩散也可以被实时监测。</span></span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:Calibri;font-size:14px">Luxonus<span style="font-family:宋体">光声成像技术应用</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">潜力</span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">广泛。</span>Luxonus<span style="font-family:宋体">设备在疾病的检测和进展方面提供了特殊好处，如糖尿病、癌症、血管炎、动脉硬化、关节病和淋巴水肿。它还可以可视化皮肤、痣和头发的结构，无需活检，为皮肤科的应用打开了大门。在体育和健康科学中，运动对血管的影响</span></span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">也</span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">可以很容易地量化。</span></span></p><p style="margin-left:0;text-indent:28px;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric;line-height:200%"><span style=";font-family:Calibri;font-size:14px">Luxonus<span style="font-family:宋体">设备提供了一个前所未有的能力来观察微血管网络的结构和功能。</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">Luxonus</span><span style="font-family:宋体">的首席执行官</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">Sadakazu Aiso</span><span style="font-family:宋体">说</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">:“</span><span style="font-family:宋体">我们希望它能以一种安全、简单的方式研究血管和淋巴管，从而开启一门新的健康科学。</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">”</span><span style="font-family:宋体">在这一新的、统一的血管科学中，许多非侵入性的、低成本的疾病过程早期检测的机会可能会为患者和整个社会带来好处。</span></span></p><p><br></p>                    <br><br>
