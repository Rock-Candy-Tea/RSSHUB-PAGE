font-family: 宋体;">，是不允许电子存在的能量范围。现在，研究含铁、碲和硒的化合物的科学家发现，能带隙在材料表面上两个允许能带相交的点处打开。他们在冷却材料并用激光探测其电子结构时，观察到了这种意外的电子行为。他们的相关研究结果已经于</span>2021<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">年</span>1<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">CMPMS</a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-family: 宋体;">）布鲁克海文国家实验室的纳德</span>·<span style="font-family: 宋体;">扎基（</span>Nader Zaki<span style="font-family: 宋体;">）说：</span>“<span style="font-family: 宋体;">有一阵子，人们认为超导和磁场会相互影响。我们已经探索了一种材料，它可以同时具有超导和磁特性。</span>”</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">除了超导性和磁性外，某些铁基超导体还具有承载</span>“<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">拓扑</span>”<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">表面状态的正确条件。这些独特的电子态存在于表面（它们不存在于大部分材料中），反映了电子的自旋与其围绕原子核的轨道运动之间的强相互作用。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">电子光谱小组负责人、上述论文的通讯作者彼得</span>·<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">约翰逊（</span>Peter Johnson<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）说：</span>“<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">当您拥有具有拓扑表面特性的超导体时，您会对拓扑超导的可能性感到兴奋。</span>” “<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">拓扑超导性（</span></span><a href="https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2021/03/210317111817.htm" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">Majorana fermions</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">），它可以作为量子比特，是量子计算机的信息存储模块。</span>”</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">量子计算机有望大大加快计算速度，而这种速度在传统计算机上是不可能实现的。实现实用量子计算的挑战之一是量子位对其环境高度敏感。微小的相互作用会导致它们失去其量子态，从而丢失存储的信息。理论预测，存在于超导拓扑表面态中的马约拉那费米子（所寻求的准粒子）不受环境干扰，使其成为鲁棒量子位（</span>robust qubits<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）的理想平台。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">纳德</span>·<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">扎基、彼得</span>·<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">约翰逊及其同事将铁基超导体视为各种奇特现象和潜在重要现象的平台，着手了解拓扑、超导性和磁性的作用。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;"><img src="http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202103/20/104730dqnp7na5podnuz5p.jpg" title alt="gu genda.jpg" style="white-space: normal; font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 18px; float: left;" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><a href="https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2021/03/210317111817.htm" style="white-space: normal; font-family: 宋体;">音译）首先生长了铁基化合物的高质量单晶。然后，纳德</span>·<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">扎基通过基于激光的光发射光谱法绘制了材料的电子能带结构图。当来自激光的光聚焦到材料上的一个小点上时，来自表面的电子被</span>“<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">踢出</span>”<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">（即光发射）。然后可以测量这些电子的能量和动量。当他们降低温度时，发生了令人惊讶的事情。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">纳德</span>·<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">扎基说：</span>“此<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">材料正如我们所料显示出超导性，我们看到了与此有关的超导间隙。</span>” “<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">但是我们没想到的是，拓扑表面状态在狄拉克点（</span></span><a href="https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2021/03/210317111817.htm" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">类似于倒带视频并看到相同的事件序列以相反的顺序播放。但是在时间逆转下，电子自旋改变了它们的方向并打破了这种对称性。因此，打破时间反转对称性的一种方法是发展磁性，特别是铁磁性，这是一种磁性类型，其中所有电子自旋均平行排列。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">彼得</span>·<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">约翰逊说：</span>“<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">系统正在进入超导状态，看似磁性正在发展。</span>” “<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">我们必须假设磁性存在于表面区域，因为以这种形式不能共存。这种发现令人兴奋，因为该材料具有许多不同的物理性质：超导性、拓扑结构和现在的磁性。我喜欢说这是一站式购物。了解这些现象如何在材料中产生，可以为许多新的令人兴奋的技术方向奠定基础。</span>”</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">如前所述，该材料的超导性和强大的自旋轨道效应可用于量子信息技术。或者，材料的磁性和强自旋轨道相互作用可以实现电子中电流的无耗散传输（无能量损失）。可以利用此功能来开发功耗低的电子设备。</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">共同作者</span><a href="https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2021/03/210317111817.htm" style="white-space: normal; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank">CMPMS</a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）的物理学教授吴从军（</span>Congjun Wu<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">音译）提供了关于时间逆转对称性在表面区域如何破裂以及磁场是如何产生的理论见解。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">纳德</span>·<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">扎基说：</span>“<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">这一发现不仅揭示了拓扑超导状态与自发磁化之间的深层联系，而且还为铁基超导体中超导间隙功能的性质提供了重要见解，这是研究强相关非常规超导体时的一个突出问题。</span>”</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">在与</span><a href="https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2021/03/210317111817.htm" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">CMPMS</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">部门其他合作者的另一项研究中，实验团队正在研究样品中三种元素的不同浓度如何导致观察到的现象。貌似需要碲才能达到拓扑效果，过多的铁会泯灭超导性，而硒会增强超导性。</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"> Quirky Response to Magnetism Presents Quantum Physics Mystery</span></a></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><a href="https://www.pnas.org/content/118/3/e2007241118" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "arial black", "avant garde";">Significance</span></a></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">In the Fe-chalcogenide superconductors, topology, superconductivity, and magnetism all come together to provide a platform for a range of exotic quantum phenomena. Topology and superconductivity can interact to give topological superconductivity capable of supporting Majorana fermions that could prove to be the basis of future qubit technology. The large spin–orbit interaction associated with the topological phenomena combined with magnetism can potentially result in the quantum anomalous Hall effect although the superconductivity will work against such. It is important that we understand the relevant roles of the different phenomena. Here we provide evidence of time-reversal symmetry breaking and associated mass acquisition in a topological surface state at the superconducting transition, an observation highly suggestive of the formation of surface ferromagnetism.</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><a href="https://www.pnas.org/content/118/3/e2007241118" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "arial black", "avant garde";">Abstract</span></a></p><p style="margin: 0px 0px 24px; background: white; line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; color: #333333;">Topological superconductivity has been sought in a variety of heterostructure systems, the interest being that a material displaying such a phenomenon could prove to be the ideal platform to support Majorana fermions, which in turn could be the basis for advanced qubit technologies. Recently, the high-T</span><sub><span style="font-size: 18px; color: #333333;">c</span></sub><span style="font-size: 18px; color: #333333;"> family of superconductors, FeTe</span><sub><span style="font-size: 18px; color: #333333;">1−x</span></sub><span style="font-size: 18px; color: #333333;">Se</span><sub><span style="font-size: 18px; color: #333333;">x</span></sub><span style="font-size: 18px; color: #333333;">, have been shown to exhibit the property of topological superconductivity and further, evidence has been found for the presence of Majorana fermions. We have studied the interplay of topology, magnetism, and superconductivity in the FeTe</span><sub><span style="font-size: 18px; color: #333333;">1−<em>x</em></span></sub><span style="font-size: 18px; color: #333333;">Se</span><em><sub><span style="font-size: 18px; color: #333333;">x</span></sub></em><span style="font-size: 18px; color: #333333;"> family using high-resolution laser-based photoemission. At the bulk superconducting transition, a gap opens at the chemical potential as expected. However, a second gap is observed to open at the Dirac point in the topological surface state. The associated mass acquisition in the topological state points to time-reversal symmetry breaking, probably associated with the formation of ferromagnetism in the surface layer. The presence of intrinsic ferromagnetism combined with strong spin–orbit coupling provides an ideal platform for a range of exotic topological phenomena.</span></span></p><p><br></p>                    <br><br>
