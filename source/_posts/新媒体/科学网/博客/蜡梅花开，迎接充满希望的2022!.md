<p><span style="font-size: 16px;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; background: white;">最近，蜡梅开花了，这是为数不多的在该季节开花的植物，淡淡的花香给人一种美妙的感觉。蜡梅为蜡梅科植物，先花后叶，花被片</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; background: white; overflow-wrap: break-word;">15-25</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; background: white;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word;">，黄色或黄白色，有紫红色条纹，花被片圆形、长圆形或倒卵形等，内部花被片比外部花被片短，基部有爪。</span>关于蜡梅归属早在《本草纲目》就有描述，</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; background: white; overflow-wrap: break-word;">“</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; background: white;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word;">蜡梅，释名黄梅花，此物非梅类，因其与梅同时，香又相近，色似蜜蜡，故得此名</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">”</span></span>。</span></span></p><p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 16px;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; background: white;"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202112/31/213450csqeth5itqt06iqk.jpg" title alt="图片1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></span></span></p><p style="margin-top:10px;margin-right:0;margin-bottom:10px;margin-left:0"><span style="font-size: 16px;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; background: white;">“腊梅”还是“蜡梅”？武汉东湖梅园现有蜡梅</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; background: white; overflow-wrap: break-word;">100</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; background: white; overflow-wrap: break-word;">余种，蜡梅树达到</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; background: white; overflow-wrap: break-word;">3000</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; background: white; overflow-wrap: break-word;">余株，是观赏蜡梅的好去处。对于蜡梅的名称，有朋友认为该植物在腊月开花，故“蜡”应该为“腊”，从植物分类学上讲，“蜡梅”的写法不但没有错误，而且很确切，现在规范写法就是</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; background: white; overflow-wrap: break-word;">“</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; background: white; overflow-wrap: break-word;">蜡梅</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; background: white; overflow-wrap: break-word;">”</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; background: white;"><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word;">。</span>为什么腊月开花不叫“腊梅”而是叫“蜡梅”？这是因为蜡梅的花瓣一般呈现浅黄或者黄色，并且其表面有一层蜡质，摸上去有蜡质感，故名“蜡梅”。《花疏》中也有描述“蜡梅是寒花，人以腊月开，故以腊名，非也，为色正似黄蜡耳。”</span></span></p><p style="margin-top:10px;margin-right:0;margin-bottom:10px;margin-left:0"><span style="font-size: 16px;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; background: white;">蜡梅的花香：蜡梅花<span style="overflow-wrap: break-word;">香四溢，</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">“</span>多爇龙涎臭不如<span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">”</span>以及<span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;"> “</span>花香如梅<span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">”</span>的描述均说明蜡梅具有香气，甚至胜过龙涎。蜡梅的香气成分是什么？周明芹等研究表明，罗勒烯、芳樟醇和乙酸苄酯在蜡梅花香气成分中占比较大；周继荣和倪德江的研究也得到类似结论，香气成分中中的别罗勒烯、罗勒烯、乙酸苄酯和水杨酸甲酯的相对含量较高，其相对含量之和占总量</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; background: white;">80 %</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; background: white;">以上，香气成分中醇类化合物主要包括苯甲醇和芳樟醇。</span><span style="overflow-wrap: break-word;">单萜化合物芳樟醇主要由单萜合酶催化产生，</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; background: white;">Shang</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; background: white;">等对蜡梅基因组分析发现，单萜合酶</span><span style="font-family: "Times New Roman", serif; background: white;">TPS-b</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; background: white;">亚家族基因在蜡梅中发生了显著扩张，芳樟醇合成酶剂量上的变化而导致蜡梅特征香气芳樟醇的形成。</span></span></p><p style="margin: 10px 0px; text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 16px;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; background: white;"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202112/31/214155wjtificepzaww5t8.jpg" title alt="图片2.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></span></span></p><p style="margin: 10px 0px;
