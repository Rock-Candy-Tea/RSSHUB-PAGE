font-family: "times new roman";">据《健康世界新闻》（</span><a href="https://healthwnews.com/" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">年</span>7<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">月</span>30<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">日报道，百岁老人拥有独特的微生物组（</span>microbiome<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">），这可能有助于延长其寿命</span></span><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">。</span><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">百岁老人不易患与年龄有关的慢性病，</span><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "MS Gothic";">​​</span><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">更有可能从传染病中幸存下来。现在，一项新研究表明，活到</span><span style="font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 18px;">100</span><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">岁或以上的人拥有独特的微生物组，可以保护他们免受某些细菌感染，包括由多重耐药菌引起的感染。该研究结果已经在《自然》（</span><span style="font-family: "times new roman"; font-size: 18px;">Nature</span><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）杂志网站发表，可以帮助研究人员开发治疗慢性炎症和细菌性疾病的新方法。</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">日本庆应义塾大学医学院（</span><a href="https://healthwnews.com/health-news/centenarians-have-a-distinct-microbiome-that-may-help-support-longevity/" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">Keio University School of Medicine in Japan</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）的</span> Yuko Sato<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">、</span>Koji Atarashi<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">、</span>Nobuoshi Hirose <span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">和</span> Kenya Honda<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">以及美国麻省理工学院和哈佛大学博德研究所（</span></span><a href="https://healthwnews.com/health-news/centenarians-have-a-distinct-microbiome-that-may-help-support-longevity/" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">Broad Institute of MIT and Harvard</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）的</span>Damian Plichta<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">和</span>Ramnik Xavier<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">等研究人员研究了平均年龄为</span> 107 <span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">岁的</span>160<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">名日本百岁老人的粪便样本中的微生物。他们发现，与</span>85-89<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">岁以及</span>21-55<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">岁之间的人相比，百岁老人的几种细菌种类水平更高，这些细菌会产生称为次级胆汁酸（</span></span><a href="https://healthwnews.com/health-news/centenarians-have-a-distinct-microbiome-that-may-help-support-longevity/" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">Secondary bile acids</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">）的分子。次级胆汁酸由结肠中的微生物产生，被认为有助于保护肠道免受病原体侵害并调节身体的免疫反应。</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">研究人员接下来用百岁老人体内升高的次级胆汁酸处理实验室中常见的引起感染的细菌。一种叫做</span> isoalloLCA <span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">的分子强烈抑制了艰难梭菌（</span>Clostridioides difficile<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）的生长，艰难梭菌是一种抗生素抗性细菌，会导致严重的腹泻和肠道炎症。给感染了艰难梭菌的小鼠喂食补充有</span>isoalloLCA<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">的饮食同样抑制了病原体的水平。该团队还发现，</span></span><a href="https://healthwnews.com/health-news/centenarians-have-a-distinct-microbiome-that-may-help-support-longevity/" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">isoalloLCA</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">有效抑制了许多其他革兰氏阳性病原体（</span>gram positive pathogens<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）的生长或杀死了许多其他革兰氏阳性病原体，这表明</span>isoalloLCA<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">可能有助于身体维持健康肠道中微生物群落的微妙平衡。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">该研究的共同第一作者、麻省</span>-<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">哈佛博德研究所的计算科学家</span>Damian Plichta<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">说：</span>“<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">宿主与细菌不同过程之间的生态相互作用确实表明，这些肠道细菌具有维持健康的潜力。</span>”</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">来自世界不同地区的更大规模和更长期的研究可能有助于揭示长寿与胆汁酸之间的因果关系。与此同时，这项研究中鉴定的细菌可以帮助研究人员研究如何操纵胆汁酸来治疗由抗生素耐药细菌引起的感染。上述介绍，仅供参考。欲了解更多信息敬请注意浏览原文或者</span><a href="https://healthwnews.com/health-news/centenarians-have-a-distinct-microbiome-that-may-help-support-longevity/" style="text-decoration: underline;
