<p style="text-indent:37px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:宋体">不少人六十岁退休后就开始安享晚年，但有人退休后才创造了人生的高峰，他就是许渊冲先生。国际译联将</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">2014</span><span style="font-family:宋体">“北极光”杰出文学翻译奖授予了许渊冲，那时他已</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">93</span><span style="font-family:宋体">岁，而他的大部分创作，都是许先生退休以后开始的。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:37px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:宋体">读一首诗，欣赏一幅美景，再读懂诗人的意境与心情，又能将其用另外一种文字表达出来，还要做到押韵，这需要深厚的中外语言文学功底，还要有兴趣与爱好的支撑，精益求精的不懈追求。他被称为</span><span style="font-family:宋体">“诗译英法唯一人” ，在国内外出版中、英、法文著译六十本，包括《诗经》、《楚辞》、《李白诗选》、《西厢记》、《红与黑》、《包法利夫人》、《追忆似水年华》等中外名著，是有史以来将中国历代诗词译成英、法韵文的唯一专家。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:37px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:宋体">惊悉我国翻译界泰斗、北京大学新闻与传播学院教授许渊冲先生</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">6</span><span style="font-family:宋体">月</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">17</span><span style="font-family:宋体">日上午在北京逝世，享年</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">100</span><span style="font-family:宋体">岁。这里摘录一些许先生翻译的诗来纪念这位百年仍似少年的偶像。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:37px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:19px"> </span></p><p style="text-indent:37px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:19px">“千山鸟飞尽，万径人踪灭”</span></p><p style="text-indent:37px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:Calibri">From hill to hill no bird in flight</span><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">From path to path no man in sight</span><span style="font-family:宋体">；</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:37px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:19px"> </span></p><p style="text-indent:37px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:19px">“寻寻觅觅，冷冷清清，凄凄惨惨戚戚”</span></p><p style="text-indent:37px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:Calibri">look for what I miss</span><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">I know not what it is</span><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">I feel so sad</span><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">so drear</span><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">so lonely</span><span style="font-family:宋体">，</span><span style="font-family:Calibri">without cheer.</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:37px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:19px"> </span></p><p style="text-indent:37px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:19px">“海内存知已，天涯若比邻”</span></p><p style="text-indent:37px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:Calibri">If you have friedns who know your heart, Distance cannot keep you apart.</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:37px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:19px"> </span></p><p style="text-indent:37px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:19px">“欲穷千里目，更上一层楼”</span></p><p style="text-indent:37px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:Calibri">You will enjoy a grander sight, If you climb to greater height.</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:37px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:19px"> </span></p><p style="text-indent:37px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:19px">“两情若是长久时，又岂在朝朝暮暮”</span></p><p style="text-indent:37px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:Calibri">If love between both sides can last for aye, Why need they stay together night and day?</span></span></p><p style="text-indent:37px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:19px"> </span></p><p style="text-indent:37px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:19px">“人有悲欢离合，月有阴晴圆缺”</span></p><p
