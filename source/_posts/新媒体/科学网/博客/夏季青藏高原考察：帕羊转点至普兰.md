<p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">2021-08-07</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">，星期六</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体">中国科学院动物研究所，牛泽清博士</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体">昨天傍晚，帕羊镇的天空美伦美奂的。我特意到住宿旅店的房顶拍下了帕羊镇傍晚天空的美景。说真的，我还是第一次看到如此的美景呢。</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202108/08/084426p8riu5pbzugrgqyr.jpeg" title alt="2021-08-06-仲巴县帕羊镇-1.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p><p style="text-align: center; font-size: medium; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px;"><span style="font-family:楷体">帕羊火烧云</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体">今天早晨</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">8</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">点起来，大家都说昨晚由于帕羊镇海拔高，睡得都不好。旅店老板早餐给大家做了肉丝面和西红柿鸡蛋面。大家可能还是由于高反，吃得并不香。尤其徐颖和程瑞还买了氧气吸了起来。</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体">上午</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">9</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">点，我们一行乘车离开帕羊镇，沿</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">G219</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">国道，向西北挺进，目的地是阿里地区的普兰县。从帕羊镇出来，一直沿</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">G219</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">国道前行，大约走了一个半小时，才看到“藏西秘境天上阿里”欢迎您的门楼。从这里我们就离开西藏的日喀则地区最西面的仲巴县，正式进入到了阿里地区了。人们常说不到阿里，就算不上真正到过西藏。可你真到了阿里，你才会体会到啥是高海拔。这里海拔高度不低于</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">4300</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">米，要是你没有一个适应高海拔的经历，贸然直接来到阿里，恐怕高反会让你难受好几天的。</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202108/08/084508ud97474dpzyp4ysi.jpeg" title alt="2021-08-07-仲巴县2.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p><p style="text-align: center; font-size: medium; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px;"><span style="font-family:楷体">阿里秘境</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体">进入到普兰县地界，我们一行首先遇到疫情安检点的检查。好在我们进藏已近</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">50</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">天了，行程码记载着我们的活动，我们顺利通过了安检。上午</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">11</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">点，我们来到了公珠措附近。我们驱车来到湖边，只见湖边有厚厚的一层从湖里飘上来的飘浮物，人站上去感觉都绵绵的。小吴蹚着水在湖边试着采集钩虾，可公珠措里却没有钩虾。索朗师傅的车不小心陷入到湖边的泥坑中，大家费了九牛二虎之力，最后在江白师傅车上绞盘的帮助下，才将索朗师傅的车从泥坑里救了出来。真是有惊无险啊！</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202108/08/084540tyuio9chnyqrhhlu.jpeg" title alt="2021-08-07-普兰县-3.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p><p style="text-align: center; font-size: medium; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px;"><span style="font-family:楷体">疫情防控检查</span></p><p style="font-size: medium; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px;"><span style="font-family:楷体"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202108/08/084602dqjxqjvbbuv4mr44.jpeg" title alt="2021-08-07-普兰县-4.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p><p style="font-size: medium; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px; text-align: center;"><span style="font-family:楷体"><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: medium; text-align: justify; text-indent: 32px;">公珠措湖边漂浮物</span></span></p><p style="font-size: medium; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px;"><span style="font-family:楷体"><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: medium; text-align: justify; text-indent: 32px;"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202108/08/084641ga1v5xrx1bd3bvau.jpeg" title alt="2021-08-07-普兰县-5.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></span></p><p style="text-align: center; font-size: medium; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px;"><span style="font-family:楷体">试采钩虾不获</span></p><p style="font-size: medium; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px;"><span style="font-family:楷体"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202108/08/084706ebjda7hjeln1emmb.jpeg" title alt="2021-08-07-普兰县-6.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p><p style="font-size: medium; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px; text-align: center;"><span style="font-family:楷体">奋力推车</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体">脱险后，我们继续驱车前行。中午</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">12</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">点半左右，我们行驶至</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">G219</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">国道</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">3981</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">公里处，突见路边有弯弯曲曲的溪流。我和小吴说这儿可能有钩虾，小吴觉得这里的生境也像。于是他将车停在路边，拿上工具，下到溪流中寻找起了钩虾。我也走到溪流边，用收试试了水温。还真冷，难怪小吴每次采钩虾回来手指都发僵呢。我见小吴熟练地翻开溪流中的石头，迅速将漏网伸到下面一捞，再用水冲冲漏网，钩虾就被逮着了。不多一会儿，小吴、坤泽、徐颖一起就捞着了不少的钩虾。我看了一下，有几只还挺大的呢。</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202108/08/084741h6qyz22nnqyix2n6.jpeg" title alt="2021-08-07-普兰县-7.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p><p style="text-align: center; font-size: medium; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px;"><span style="font-family:楷体">溪流</span></p><p style="font-size: medium; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px;"><span style="font-family:楷体"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202108/08/084803k22znzee2292h191.jpeg" title alt="2021-08-07-普兰县-8.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p><p style="font-size: medium; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px; text-align: center;"><span style="font-family:楷体">收获钩虾</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体">采集完钩虾，今天算是有了初步的收获了，要是再采集一些蜜蜂或其它昆虫就锦上添花了。下午</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">2</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">点，我们一行也从</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">G219</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">国道转道</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">G564</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">国道了。行驶至</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">G564</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">国道</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">7</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">公里处时，路边出现许多粉红色的花。我和小吴驻车一看，是一种菊科植物，花上也有一些蜜蜂在飞。我俩拿上扫网直奔花丛，挥网捕起蜜蜂来。可这个地儿风大，海拔</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">4700</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">多米，没几下，我就感觉气喘嘘嘘的，累得够呛。倒是小吴体力还好，扫了一阵，捕到了一些褐胸分舌蜂和熊蜂，也算没白费功夫吧。</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202108/08/084828yeqzqwqkq3sgesdk.jpeg" title alt="2021-08-07-普兰县-9.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p><p style="text-align: center; font-size: medium; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px;"><span style="font-family:楷体">寻花觅蜂</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体">我们驱车向前又走了</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">10</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">公里，来到</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">G564</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">国道</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">17</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">公里处。小吴说他去年在这里采集过，于是我们停下来再次采集。我在车里针插刚才采到的蜜蜂，小吴拿扫网跑到路边的一处凹地，挥网采集。到下午</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">4</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">点，小吴真采到了不少的蜜蜂，尤其采到了丽切叶蜂和一种黄斑蜂。真是不错的收获啊。</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体; font-size: 16px;">钩虾采到了，蜜蜂也采到了，心满意足。我们一行驱车上路，赶往普兰县城。途经拉昂措时，我们特意停车，让大家看看美景。的确，西藏的美景太多了，有时美景美得让你都不敢相信自己的眼睛了。我也庆幸自己能有机会来西藏从事野外采集。累归累，但沿途能看到许多的美景，也算得上大自然给予我们野外工作者额外的奖赏吧。</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体;
