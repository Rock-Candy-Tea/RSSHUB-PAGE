<p style="margin: 0 0 10px;text-align: justify;font-family: 等线;font-size: 14px;white-space: normal;-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;line-height: 28px"><span style="line-height: 32px; font-size: 16px;">科学基金申请书也被</span><span style="line-height: 32px; font-size: 16px;">俗称</span><span style="line-height: 32px; font-size: 16px;">“本子”。本子的意思不言而喻</span><span style="line-height: 32px; font-size: 16px;">，</span><span style="line-height: 32px; font-size: 16px;">作业本、笔记本、剧本等装订成一本的东西都可以叫本子。据此，把</span><span style="line-height: 32px; font-size: 16px;">时下标书化的</span><span style="line-height: 32px; font-size: 16px;">基金申请书叫</span><span style="line-height: 32px; font-size: 16px;">“</span><span style="line-height: 32px; font-size: 16px;">本子</span><span style="line-height: 32px; font-size: 16px;">”</span><span style="line-height: 32px; font-size: 16px;">，尽管不一定贴切，</span><span style="line-height: 32px; font-size: 16px;">倒</span><span style="line-height: 32px; font-size: 16px;">的确形象。</span><br></p><p style="margin: 0 0 10px;text-align: justify;font-family: 等线;font-size: 14px;white-space: normal;-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;line-height: 28px"><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">把基金申请书叫</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px">“</span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">本子</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px">”</span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">，从口语化角度，老文认为没毛病。它折射出的问题也许是，申请书为什么要写成厚厚的一本？只写一页，充其量写三五页，不行吗？</span></span></p><p style="margin: 0 0 10px;text-align: justify;font-family: 等线;font-size: 14px;white-space: normal;-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;line-height: 28px"><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">本质上是行的，取决于想法的重要和创新程度。越重要和越创新的想法，越不需要说很多话</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">，所谓</span>“大道至简”也</span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">。</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">这从</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">科研成果</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">反过来看，</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">可见一斑。</span></span></p><p style="margin: 0 0 10px;text-align: justify;font-family: 等线;font-size: 14px;white-space: normal;-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;line-height: 28px"><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">诺贝尔奖的颁奖理由从来只有一句话，</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">但</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">那句话</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">必须有排他性，也就是</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">用在任何其他人身上都不合适。</span></span></p><p style="margin: 0 0 10px;text-align: justify;font-family: 等线;font-size: 14px;white-space: normal;-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;line-height: 28px"><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">例如，</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">相对论、光电效应、质能关系等这样的改变世界的成果</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">，</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">尽管只有区区几个字，但人人都明白它们属于爱因斯坦。后人</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">如果</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">再研究相对论、光电效应、质能关系等，不说一箩筐甚至几箩筐话，就很难说清楚他们工作的创新所在、重要几何了。</span></span></p><p style="margin: 0 0 10px;text-align: justify;font-family: 等线;font-size: 14px;white-space: normal;-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;line-height: 28px"><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">诺贝尔物理学奖得主爱德华</span>·珀塞尔在1950年1月向美国艺术与科学院提交了一份基金申请，主题是开展星际氢原子微波识别标志的实验探测工作。申请书很短，只有两页纸、几小段，后来被誉为“完美的申请”（A perfect proposal）。</span></p><p style="margin: 0 0 10px;text-align: justify;font-family: 等线;font-size: 14px;white-space: normal;-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;line-height: 28px"><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">据《今日物理》杂志报道，这份申请书之所以堪称完美，是因为它的崇高目标，它的明确愿景，它的清晰可见，它的诚实可信，以及它对科学界其他公民的尊重。当然，还因为它</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">在完成时</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">的产出：发表了一篇论文，开辟了一个新领域</span>——氢谱线射电天文学，这一领域</span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">后来</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">成为天文学的一个重要分支。</span></span></p><p style="margin: 0 0 10px;text-align: justify;font-family: 等线;font-size: 14px;white-space: normal;-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;line-height: 28px"><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">说</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">到</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">本子</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">，</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">看过</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">古装宫廷剧</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">（如《宰相刘罗锅》）的人很容易联想起</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">一句台词：</span>“有本奏来，无本退朝”。把基金</span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">本子写成</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px">“臣有一本”</span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">那样</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">的本子</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">如何？</span></span></p><p style="margin: 0 0 10px;text-align: justify;font-family: 等线;font-size: 14px;white-space: normal;-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;line-height: 28px"><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">首先</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">，两</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">种本子</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">都是发现了问题并提出解决问题的办法。</span></span></p><p style="margin: 0 0 10px;text-align: justify;font-family: 等线;font-size: 14px;white-space: normal;-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;line-height: 28px"><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">其</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">次</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">，两</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">种本子</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">都是往上呈，等待批准或否决</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">。</span></span></p><p style="margin: 0 0 10px;text-align: justify;font-family: 等线;font-size: 14px;white-space: normal;-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;line-height: 28px"><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px">“有本奏来，无本退朝”</span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">是一种严肃，是一种高效，而基金申请者如果有</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px">“臣有一本”</span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">那样的敬畏，必然尽全力研究真问题，真研究问题。</span></span></p><p style="margin: 0 0 10px;text-align: justify;font-family: 等线;font-size: 14px;white-space: normal;-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;line-height: 28px"><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span 14px;white-space: normal;-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;line-height: 28px"><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">更重要的是，基金</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">本子</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">如果像</span>“臣有一本”那样，站位高，提出真正关乎国计民生或人类命运的大问题，并写得言简意赅</span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">（</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">尽管也叫一本，其实是一页折成的一本</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">）</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">，</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">那么，它就如</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">珀塞尔</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px">“完美的申请”，</span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">不受资助都很难</span></span><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span style="font-family:等线">，不产出重要成果也不易。</span></span></p><p style="margin: 0 0 10px;text-align: justify;font-family: 等线;font-size: 14px;white-space: normal;-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;line-height: 28px"><span style="line-height: 32px;font-size: 16px"><span
