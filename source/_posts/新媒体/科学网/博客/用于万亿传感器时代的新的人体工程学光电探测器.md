font-family: 宋体;">），这是一种微小的基于半导体的电子元件，可以探测光并将它们转换成电信号。光传感器随处可见，从家用电子产品、医疗设备到光通信系统和汽车。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">近年来，光传感器的研究取得了显著的进展。科学家们一直在努力开发一种传感器，这种传感器可以检测到高动态范围的光，而且易于制造和节能。大多数用于具有成本效益的消费产品的光传感器都是节能的，但在外部环境中容易受到噪声和不必要的光信息的影响，这对它们的性能产生不利影响。为了解决这个问题，一些产品采用了信噪比更好的光频转换电路</span>(light-to-frequency conversion circuits <span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">简称</span>LFCs)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">。然而，大多数低氟碳化合物是由硅基光电探测器制成的，这限制了光探测的范围。此外，使用</span>LFCs<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">会导致芯片面积的损耗，这在设计紧致装置</span>(compact devices)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">的多功能电子电路时成为一个问题。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">现在，韩国仁川国立大学由</span>Sung Hun Jin<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">In this study, as system-level photodetectors, light-to-frequency conversion circuits (LFCs) are realized by i) photosensitive ring oscillators (ROs) composed of amorphous indium-gallium-zinc-oxide/single-walled carbon nanotube (a-IGZO/SWNT) thin film transistors (TFTs) and ii) phase-locked-loop Si circuits built with frequency-to-digital converters (PFDC). The 3-stage ROs and logic gates based on a-IGZO/SWNT TFTs successfully demonstrate its performance on flexible substrates. Herein, along with the advantage of scalability, a-IGZO films are used as photosensitive n-type TFTs and SWNTs are employed as photo-insensitive p-type TFTs for better photosensitivity in circuit level. Through the controlling a post-annealing condition of a-IGZO film, responsivities and detectivities of a-IGZO TFTs are obtained as 36 AW<sup>−1</sup> and 0.3<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">×</span>10<sup>12</sup> Jones for red, 93 AW<sup>−1</sup> and 3.1<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">×</span>10<sup>12</sup> Jones for green, and 194 AW<sup>–1</sup> and 11.7<span style="color: #333333; font-family: 宋体; font-size: 18px; background-color: #FFFFFF;">×</span>10<sup>12</sup> Jones for blue. Furthermore, as an advanced demonstration for practical application of LFCs, a unique circuit (i.e., PFDC) is designed to analyze the generated oscillation frequency (<em>f</em><sub>osc</sub>) from the LFC device and convert it to a digital code. As a result, the designed PFDC can exactly count the generated <em>f</em><sub>osc</sub> from the flexible a-IGZO/SWNT ROs under light illumination with an outstanding sensitivity and assign input frequencies to respective digital code.</span></p>                    <br><br>
