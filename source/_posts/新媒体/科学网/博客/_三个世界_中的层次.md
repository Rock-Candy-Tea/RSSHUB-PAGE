<p style="text-indent: 32px; line-height: 1.5em;"><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312;color: #222222;letter-spacing: 1px;font-size: 19px;background: #FFFFFF"><span line-height: 1.5em;"><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312;color: #222222;letter-spacing: 1px;font-size: 19px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">在一般的意义上，在</span></span><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312;color: #222222;letter-spacing: 1px;font-size: 19px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">波普尔所论的</span></span><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312;color: #222222;letter-spacing: 1px;font-size: 19px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">“三个世界”中都存在有“阶梯”：</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">在</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">“世界1”，</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">是</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">万物之间的</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">一种</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">普遍关系</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">主要指</span></span><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312;color: #222222;letter-spacing: 1px;font-size: 19px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">量子阶梯</span></span><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312;color: #222222;letter-spacing: 1px;font-size: 19px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">和不同运动方式之间的关系</span></span><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312;color: #222222;letter-spacing: 1px;font-size: 19px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">，</span></span><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312;color: #222222;letter-spacing: 1px;font-size: 19px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">在</span></span><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312;color: #222222;letter-spacing: 1px;font-size: 19px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">“世界3”</span></span><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312;color: #222222;letter-spacing: 1px;font-size: 19px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">是</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">关于自然界、人类社会和人自身的各种知识之间的普遍关系，</span></span><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312;color: #222222;letter-spacing: 1px;font-size: 19px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">知识阶梯</span></span><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312;color: #222222;letter-spacing: 1px;font-size: 19px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">；</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">在</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">“世界2”，</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">是</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">人类社会</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">的分层</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">和人</span></span><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312;color: #222222;letter-spacing: 1px;font-size: 19px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">的需求层次</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">。</span></span></p><p style="text-indent: 32px; line-height: 1.5em;"><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"> </span></p><p style="margin: 0px; text-indent: 40px; padding: 0px; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.5em;"><strong><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312;color: #222222;letter-spacing: 1px;font-size: 19px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">在空间上，低阶单一刚性封闭，属于</span><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">“低维”；高阶复杂多样柔性开放，相应于“高维”。</span></span></strong></p><p style="margin: 0px; text-indent: 37px; padding: 0px; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.5em;"><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">层次，看到这两个字，首先联想到的就是空间上的高低和大小关系，要素上的组成与被组成关系</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">，</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">以及</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">子系统、系统</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">和环境等等。</span></span></p><p style="margin: 10px 0px 0px; text-indent: 40px; padding: 0px; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.5em;"><strong><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312;color: #222222;letter-spacing: 1px;font-size: 19px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">世界</span><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">1：量子阶梯</span></span></strong></p><p style="margin: 0px; text-indent: 39px; padding: 0px; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.5em;"><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312;color: #222222;letter-spacing: 1px;font-size: 19px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">由低层到高层，越是高层，结构越是复杂，对称性破缺，所含的信息量越大。</span></span></p><p style="margin: 0px; text-indent: 39px; padding: 0px; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.5em;"><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312;color: #222222;letter-spacing: 1px;font-size: 19px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">氢原子中心对称、轴对称、平面对称，所有的</span></span><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312;color: #222222;letter-spacing: 1px;font-size: 19px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">空间要素</span></span><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312;color: #222222;letter-spacing: 1px;font-size: 19px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">全都对称；氢分子的对称性下降；水分子的对称性又进一步下降。层次越是升高，对称性越是下降。细胞没有对称性。对称性越高，相对来说层次越低，处于低维；而对称性越破缺，层次越高，处于高维。</span></span></p><p style="margin: 10px 0px 0px; text-indent: 40px; padding: 0px; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.5em;"><strong><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312;color: #222222;letter-spacing: 1px;font-size: 19px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">世界</span><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">2</span></span></strong><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312;color: #222222;letter-spacing: 1px;font-size: 19px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">的情况比较复杂。社会可以按影响力区分阶层，也可以按所掌握的知识区分为</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">科学家</span><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">-工程师-经济学和社会学家-社会管理人员-艺术家和思想家等序列</span></span><span style=";font-family:楷体_GB2312;font-size:19px"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">。</span></span></p><p style="margin: 0px; text-indent: 39px; padding: 0px; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.5em;"><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312;color: #222222;letter-spacing: 1px;font-size: 19px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">作为个体，可以有</span></span><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312;color: #222222;letter-spacing: 1px;font-size: 19px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">。</span></span><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312;color: #222222;letter-spacing: 1px;font-size: 19px;background: #FFFFFF"><span #222222;letter-spacing: 1px;font-size: 19px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">“</span></span><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312;color: #222222;letter-spacing: 1px;font-size: 19px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">舌尖</span></span><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312;color: #222222;letter-spacing: 1px;font-size: 19px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">”</span></span><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312;color: #222222;letter-spacing: 1px;font-size: 19px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">可以打破意识形态和国界的屏障；</span></span><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312;color: #222222;letter-spacing: 1px;font-size: 19px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">而作为自然演化的最高产物，心理需求则各具千秋，没有</span><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">“标准”，因人而异。</span></span></p><p style="margin: 0px; text-indent: 39px; padding: 0px; text-align: justify; line-height: 1.5em;"><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312;color: #C00000;letter-spacing: 1px;font-size: 19px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">意识作为自然界演化的最高产物，最美的花朵，不</span></span><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312;color: #C00000;letter-spacing: 1px;font-size: 19px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312;color: #222222;letter-spacing: 1px;font-size: 19px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">世界</span><span 楷体_GB2312;color: #222222;letter-spacing: 1px;font-size: 19px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">自然科学：理化天地生。物理学概念严谨、边界清晰；生物学进而生命科学概念模糊，与</span></span><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312;color: #222222;letter-spacing: 1px;font-size: 19px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">理化天地</span></span><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312;color: #222222;letter-spacing: 1px;font-size: 19px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">等</span></span><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312;color: #222222;letter-spacing: 1px;font-size: 19px;background: #FFFFFF"><span style="font-family:楷体_GB2312">其他学科交叉</span></span><span style="font-family: 楷体_GB2312;color: #222222;letter-spacing: 1px;font-size: 19px;background: #FFFFFF"><span
