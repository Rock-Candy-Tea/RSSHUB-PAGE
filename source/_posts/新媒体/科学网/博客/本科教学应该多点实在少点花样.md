font-family: 宋体, SimSun; font-size: 20px;">数据，以验证理论课的基本知识点。分析学生已经学习获得学分的课程内容，就不再赘述那些学生应该已经掌握了的基础知识点。</span></span></p><p style="margin: 5px 0px; text-indent: 37px; line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-family: 宋体, SimSun; font-family: 宋体, SimSun; font-size: 20px;">）。台下，一个个，满脸茫然。问</span><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 20px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">“</span><span style="color: #000000; font-family: 宋体, SimSun; font-size: 20px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">“</span><span style="color: #000000; font-family: 宋体, SimSun; font-size: 20px;">上过，是上过，</span><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 20px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">……”</span><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 20px;">这个概念都不知道，挂课了？</span><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 20px; font-family: "Times New Roman", serif;">”</span><span style="color: #000000; font-family: 宋体, SimSun; font-size: 20px;">再答</span><span style="color: #000000;
