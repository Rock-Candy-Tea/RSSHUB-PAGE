font-family: "times new roman";">近年来，化学家们已经找到了控制碳环（</span><a href="https://phys.org/tags/carbon+rings/" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">carbon rings</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）的方法，这些碳环被用于制造大量的药品和农用化学品，但大多数方法都很复杂，很难操作。它们通常也需要消耗大量的能源。在这项新的努力中，研究人员发现了一种方法，可以打破饱和杂环环中的</span>C<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">—</span>S<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">、</span>C<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">—</span>O<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">和</span>C<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">—</span>N<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">键，移除一个原子，之后只使用蓝光（</span></span><a href="https://phys.org/tags/blue+light/" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">blue light</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">），环重新闭合，减少一个原子。</span> </span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">为了进行反应，研究人员首先将一个芳香酮基团</span>(<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">附在杂环上</span>)<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">暴露在蓝色</span>LED<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">光（</span>blue LED </span><a href="https://phys.org/tags/light/" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">light</span></a><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）下。这引发了诺里什反应（</span>Norrish reaction<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">），其中一个碳杂原子键（</span>carbon-heteroatom bond<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）被断裂，导致环的打开和一个原子的排出。这是可行的，因为它导致了一种核心激进分子（</span>core radical<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）的产生。反应的第二部分涉及环的闭合，研究小组注意到，闭合过程与曼尼希反应（</span>Mannich reaction<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）类似。他们指出，要使反应起作用，光的波长必须与起始物质中的羰基（</span>carbonyl<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）完全匹配。他们还注意到，分子中没有原子被移除，也没有添加任何原子——结构只是简单地重新排列，留下了一个五元环。</span></span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">研究人员注意到，尽管反应很简单，但它是操纵药物化学基石的游戏规则改变者。他们还注意到，该反应可能与其他反应结合使用，提供</span>1-2<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">冲孔式反应（</span>1-2 punch sort of reaction<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">），一次进行不止一种修饰。他们用它来编辑多种知名药物，如甲氟喹（</span>mefloquine<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）和利米特罗（</span>rimiterol<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">），证明了这种反应的有效性。他们还指出，他们的新目标是找到一种方法，将芳香酮（</span>aromatic ketone<span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: 宋体;">）添加到饱和杂环上。上述介绍，仅供参考。欲了解更多信息敬请注意浏览</span></span><a href="https://science.sciencemag.org/lookup/doi/10.1126/science.abi7183" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">Shuffling nitrogen with a light push</span></a></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">Manipulation of carbon–nitrogen rings is integral to the synthesis of numerous pharmaceutical and agrochemical compounds. Jurczyk et al. report that photoexcitation of carbonyl-substituted cyclic amines can shift the nitrogen from inside to outside the ring framework. The reaction appears to proceed through a 1,5-hydrogen shift to the electronically excited carbonyl, which sets in motion the subsequent carbon–nitrogen and carbon–carbon bonding rearrangements. Several oxygen and sulfur heterocycles were applicable as well. Addition of a chiral phosphoric acid catalyst rendered the reaction asymmetric.</span></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";"><em><a href="https://science.sciencemag.org/lookup/doi/10.1126/science.abi7183" target="_blank">Science</a></em>, abi7183, this issue p. </span><a href="https://science.sciencemag.org/lookup/doi/10.1126/science.abi7183" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">1004</span></a></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><a href="https://science.sciencemag.org/lookup/doi/10.1126/science.abi7183" style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">Abstract</span></a></p><p style="line-height: 2em;"><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: "times new roman";">Saturated heterocycles are found in numerous therapeutics and bioactive natural products and are abundant in many medicinal and agrochemical compound libraries. To access new chemical space and function, many methods for functionalization on the periphery of these structures have been developed. Comparatively fewer methods are known for restructuring their core framework. Herein, we describe a visible light–mediated ring contraction of α-acylated saturated heterocycles. This unconventional transformation is orthogonal to traditional ring contractions, challenging the paradigm for diversification of heterocycles including piperidine, morpholine, thiane, tetrahydropyran, and tetrahydroisoquinoline derivatives. The success of this Norrish type II variant rests on reactivity differences between photoreactive ketone groups in specific chemical environments. This strategy was applied to late-stage remodeling of pharmaceutical derivatives, peptides, and sugars.</span></p>                    <br><br>
