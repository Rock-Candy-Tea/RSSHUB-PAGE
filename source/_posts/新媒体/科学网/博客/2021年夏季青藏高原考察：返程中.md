style="font-family: 楷体">点半左右都已吃完早餐，下楼装车，准备出发。</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202108/14/105004acysw2sws1pp8esn.jpeg" title alt="2021-08-13-班戈县-1.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p><p style="text-align: center; font-size: medium; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px;"><font face="楷体">班戈清晨</font></p><p sans-serif">8</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">点</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">40</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">分左右，我们一起出发，沿着</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">G317</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">国道行驶。本来计划大家到安多县叉道口分别时合个影，但我和小吴的车开得快，回头再看索朗和江白师傅的车时，他们已转道了。原来有另一条路可通拉萨，不需到安多县。电话联系索朗和江白师傅，告他们多注意安全，不必再跟我和小吴到安多县了。</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体">我和小吴沿</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">G317</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">国道行驶了约</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">2</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">个小时后改道</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">G109</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">国道，一直行驶在</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">4500</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">米左右的高原上。中午</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">12</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">点，我和小吴到了安多县城，花半个小时，我和小吴简单地吃了碗面充饥，也没敢耽误时间，匆忙上路，继续前行。</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体">下午</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">1</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">点一刻左右，</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">G109</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">国道修路，将我和小吴的车拦了下来。我下车看了一下，被拦下的车排起了长龙，恐怕一时半会儿不会放行。我用手机测了一下海拔，竟然已达</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">5010</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">米。小吴趴在了方向盘上打起了盹儿，我真想让他好好睡一会儿，他实在是太累了。</span></p><p style=";text-align: justify;font-size: medium;font-family: DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202108/14/105053s6amnjlz5ny04381.jpeg" title alt="2021-08-13-安多县-1.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p><p style="text-align: center; font-size: medium; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px;"><font face="楷体">修路堵车</font></p><p style="font-size: medium; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px;"><font face="楷体"><img DengXian;white-space: normal;text-indent: 32px;line-height: 24px"><span style="font-family: 楷体">半小时左右，道路放行，小吴又强打着精神，开车前行，准备翻越唐古拉山。下午</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">2</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">点</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">20</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">分，我和小吴翻过了唐古拉山垭口。垭口很冷，地面上还有积雪。我和小吴也没驻车，径直沿路行进。不一会儿，我俩已驶离西藏的北大门，进入青海省海西蒙古族藏族自治州境内。这时天空突然下起了雨，小吴也只好降低车速，沿着颠簸的</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">G109</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">国道，驶向雁石坪。下午</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">4</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">点左右，我和小吴到了雁石坪。附近的大车越来越多，路也越来越坑凹不平，车行驶起来颠得肚里的肠胃都疼。我和小吴算是咬着牙挺过了这段路，于下午</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">5</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">点</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif">10</span><span style="font-family: 楷体">分终于到达了沱沱河镇。</span></p><p style="text-align: center; font-size: medium; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px;"><font face="楷体">唐古拉山</font></p><p style="font-size: medium; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px;"><font face="楷体"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.sciencenet.cn/home/202108/14/105232hc17tp0er11zzzop.jpeg" title alt="2021-08-13-海西.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></font></p><p style="font-size: medium; white-space: normal; text-indent: 32px; line-height: 24px; text-align: center;"><font face="楷体">冒雨前行</font></p><p
