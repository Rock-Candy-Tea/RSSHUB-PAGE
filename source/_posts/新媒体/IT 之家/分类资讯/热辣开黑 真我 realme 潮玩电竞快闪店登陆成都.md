GT Neo3、真我 Buds Air2、真我手表 T1 等潮玩好礼。</p><p style="text-align: center;" data-vmark="d923"><img src="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2022/6/4356060b-a47e-4acf-b421-66fd91685aac.png" w="1080"
