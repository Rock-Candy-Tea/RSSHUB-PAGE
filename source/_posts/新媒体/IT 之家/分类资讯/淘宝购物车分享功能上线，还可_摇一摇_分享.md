<p data-vmark="5328"><a class="s_tag" href="https://www.ithome.com/" target="_blank">IT之家</a> 10 月 27 日消息，此前，淘宝内测了“购物车分享”新功能，随着天猫双 11 预售开启，今天凌晨，一键分享购物车功能正式上线了。</p><p data-vmark="c4b4"><img src="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2021/10/656ed461-f21e-49c2-afd5-e38322ecf60d.jpg" w="640" h="426" title="淘宝购物车分享功能上线，还可“摇一摇”分享" width="640" height="426" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p src="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2021/10/abee902a-f3ce-4eed-ae70-90355926cd16.jpg" w="595" h="1273" title="淘宝购物车分享功能上线，还可“摇一摇”分享" width="595" height="1273"
