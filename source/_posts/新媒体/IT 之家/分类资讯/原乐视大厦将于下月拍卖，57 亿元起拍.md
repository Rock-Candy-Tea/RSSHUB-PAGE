<p><a class="s_tag" href="https://www.ithome.com/" target="_blank">IT之家</a> 8 月 10 日消息 北京市朝阳区姚家园路 105 号 3 号楼 1-14 层不动产，即乐融大厦（此前名为乐视大厦）在 2019 年时遭司法拍卖，起拍价 6.78 亿元，不过由于“案外人对拍卖财产提出异议”而被叫停。</p><p>据阿里拍卖公告，乐融大厦将于 9 月 9 日被拍卖，起拍价为 <span class="accentTextColor">572845879.9 元</span>。</p><p><img src="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2021/8/b5dfdd82-b73f-4a28-aeca-a47807b43734.png" w="1020" h="800" title="原乐视大厦将于下月拍卖，5.7 亿元起拍" width="1020" height="643" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2021/8/ade84c4c-28a9-4a23-9f49-106967d3d952.jpg@s_2,w_820,h_995" w="1170" h="1420" title="原乐视大厦将于下月拍卖，5.7 亿元起拍" srcset="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2021/8/ade84c4c-28a9-4a23-9f49-106967d3d952.jpg 2x" width="1170" height="995" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>IT之家了解到，企查查 App 显示，目前乐视控股（北京）有限公司共有 12 条被执行人信息，当前被执行总金额<span class="accentTextColor">超 50 亿</span>。</p><p><img src="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2021/8/2e0131d4-674e-4238-9053-3b5fbe0797b2.jpg@s_2,w_820,h_1681" w="1170" h="2398" title="原乐视大厦将于下月拍卖，5.7 亿元起拍" srcset="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2021/8/2e0131d4-674e-4238-9053-3b5fbe0797b2.jpg 2x" width="1170" height="1681" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
