<p data-vmark="0b80"><a class="s_tag" href="https://www.ithome.com/" target="_blank">IT之家</a> 9 月 16 日消息 网友发现，花呗近期上线了账单助手，<span class="accentTextColor">新增账单管理、进度提醒、超额预警、还款建议等新功能</span>。</p><p data-vmark="dd51"><img src="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2021/9/34eb6378-178c-4218-aac4-61c8ad9d0d91.jpg@s_2,w_820,h_1822" w="1080" h="2400" title="花呗上线账单助手：倡导理性消费" srcset="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2021/9/34eb6378-178c-4218-aac4-61c8ad9d0d91.jpg 2x" width="1080" height="1822" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p data-vmark="9b96">据悉，用户可设定每月的预算金额，系统会每周播报已用额度，当快达到设定额度时就会做预警。</p><p data-vmark="4d51"><img src="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2021/9/8d631a3c-56be-47ab-bcae-6d7d88afde59.jpg@s_2,w_820,h_601" w="1320" h="968" title="花呗上线账单助手：倡导理性消费" srcset="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2021/9/8d631a3c-56be-47ab-bcae-6d7d88afde59.jpg 2x" width="1320" height="601" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p data-vmark="2f53">IT之家了解到，去年年底，为倡导理性消费，花呗也曾下调了部分年轻用户的额度，并上线了额度管理功能。蚂蚁集团对此回复称，花呗近期正在调整部分年轻用户的额度，倡导更理性的消费习惯。</p>
