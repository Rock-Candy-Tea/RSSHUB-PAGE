<p><a class="s_tag" href="https://www.ithome.com/" target="_blank">IT之家</a> 9 月 7 日消息 近日，小米公司与国家知识产权局其他一审行政判决书公开。</p><p><img src="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2021/9/bea7624f-90c4-4766-b18c-273b16380486.png" w="895" h="555" title="小米诉争商标“MiService”因与华为“MyService”近似被驳回" width="895" height="508" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>企查查 App 显示，法院查明该案诉争商标为小米公司“MiService 及图”，小米公司诉称该商标与华为公司 MyService 商标未构成近似且核定使用的商品不构成类似商品。</p><p>IT之家了解到，<span class="accentTextColor">法院认为诉争商标与华为公司商标在字母构成、读音上相近</span>，两商标若使用在同一种或类似商品上，容易造成相关公众对商品来源产生混淆、误认。判决结果为，驳回小米公司诉讼请求。</p><p><img src="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2021/9/560c0250-4aa7-4f7e-8ae8-602e04c2b208.jpg" w="690" h="1027" title="小米诉争商标“MiService”因与华为“MyService”近似被驳回" width="690" height="1027" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2021/9/59724345-8d3b-44ce-9820-8956ac8363b3.jpg" w="690" h="1300" title="小米诉争商标“MiService”因与华为“MyService”近似被驳回" width="690" height="1300" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2021/9/6345dc57-ba7a-418e-8da6-027d62cb25ee.jpg" w="690" h="472" title="小米诉争商标“MiService”因与华为“MyService”近似被驳回" width="690" height="472" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2021/9/74f72140-f05c-4435-8304-b3dd5d3cbf3d.jpg" w="690" h="452" title="小米诉争商标“MiService”因与华为“MyService”近似被驳回" width="690" height="452" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
