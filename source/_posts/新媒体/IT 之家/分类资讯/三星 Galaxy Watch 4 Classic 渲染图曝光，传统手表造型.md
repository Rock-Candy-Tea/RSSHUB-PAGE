UI Watch 的 Galaxy Watch。</p><p><img src="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2021/6/1442956a-6ced-4a4e-8c99-d2b046835fa5.jpg@s_2,w_820,h_461" alt="Apps for One UI Watch" title="三星 Galaxy Watch 4 Classic</span>。</p><p><img src="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2021/6/9b0812e2-bcdf-4d60-ab06-b4eb501afeae.jpg@s_2,w_820,h_430" title="三星 Galaxy Watch 4 Classic 渲染图曝光，传统手表造型"
