万高端手机的大规模智能工厂</strong>。</p><p style="text-align: center;" data-vmark="1fb0"><img src="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2022/5/a1a0a5fa-d3bd-45cf-8a05-517d0a0db4fb.png" w="1080" h="462" alt="小米"
