class="accentTextColor">坚决抵制“无底线追星”等行为，对发布诱导非理性追星等内容的帐号予以从严处理</span>。</p><p style="text-align: center;" data-vmark="846f"><img src="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2022/9/75b617d2-8f27-4ab0-8fe0-996ef024ebba.png?x-bce-process=image/watermark,image_aW1nL3dhdGVybWFyay9xYy9xYzE0My5wbmc=,g_3,x_14,y_14,a_0,t_100" w="927"
