月开启了集中整治。</p><p style="text-align: center;" data-vmark="ae24"><img src="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2022/1/6ea8d910-0d32-4ebd-ab83-68eca8646b21.png" w="809" h="448" alt title="B站公布 2021 年
