style="text-align: center;" data-vmark="a460"><img src="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2022/7/a3df1c98-117a-4747-8111-62884506c241.png" w="1440" h="711" title="vivo 双 C 口 80W 氮化镓 PD
