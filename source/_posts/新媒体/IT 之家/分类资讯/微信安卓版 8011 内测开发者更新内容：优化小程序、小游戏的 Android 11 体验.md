<span class="accentTextColor">8.0.11 的更新概要</span>。</p><p><img src="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2021/8/aa2c3e63-44cd-439a-91d3-3537149b45df.png" w="794" h="138" title="微信安卓版 8.0.11 <a class="s_tag" href="https://android.ithome.com/" target="_blank">Android</a> 11，需关注相关功能是否正常</p></li><li><p>4.websocket 性能优化</p></li></ul><h2>小游戏</h2><ul class="ai-word-checked
