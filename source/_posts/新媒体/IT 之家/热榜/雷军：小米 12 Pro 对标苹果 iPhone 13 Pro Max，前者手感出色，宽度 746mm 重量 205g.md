33g。</p><p data-vmark="1e20"><img src="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2021/12/bfbf016f-ad27-493e-8b52-ecd65d72fcea.jpg" w="690" h="518" alt="小米12 Pro对标iPhone 13 Pro Max" title="雷军：小米 12 Pro
