class="accentTextColor">荣耀 X20 5G</span>。</p><p><img src="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2021/8/819c1db2-a488-410a-8c1d-fb86b53d6ca4.jpg@s_2,w_820,h_273" w="1440" h="480" title="1899 元起，荣耀 X20 内存。</p><p><img src="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2021/8/c016c0c6-5054-479d-8b00-e8709ee8e15a.jpg@s_2,w_820,h_273" w="1440" h="480" title="1899 元起，荣耀 X20 5G 手机正式发布：搭载天玑 900 处理器"
