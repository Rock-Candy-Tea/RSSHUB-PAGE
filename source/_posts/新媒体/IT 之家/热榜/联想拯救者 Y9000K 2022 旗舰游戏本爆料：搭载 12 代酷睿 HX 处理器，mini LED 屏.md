显示屏。</p><p data-vmark="a238" style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2022/4/71c37892-fcdd-487c-abcd-381ce7326700.png" w="1440" h="1348" title="联想拯救者 Y9000K 2022
