4TB。</p><p><img src="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2021/8/fb944255-2f3d-43da-9308-32b9bd0f4a67.png" w="1440" h="864" title="联想官宣 ThinkPad P1 隐士 2021：系列首款 16 英寸移动图站" width="1440" height="492"
