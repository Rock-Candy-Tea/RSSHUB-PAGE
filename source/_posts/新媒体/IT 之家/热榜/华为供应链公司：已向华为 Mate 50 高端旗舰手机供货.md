同台亮相。</p><p style="text-align: center;" data-vmark="7bcc"><img src="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2022/8/fa911059-2499-4c5a-abee-aa48637050e9.png?x-bce-process=image/watermark,image_aW1nL3dhdGVybWFyay9xYy9xYzE0Mi5wbmc=,g_3,x_14,y_14,a_0,t_100" w="924" Gen1 4G 版）；Mate 50 Pro 并未延续 Mate 30 Pro 和 Mate 40 Pro 用了两代的瀑布屏。</p><p style="text-align: center;" data-vmark="6efe"><img src="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2022/8/1790e21e-a0f1-47cb-9375-baa8df4e14dd.png?x-bce-process=image/watermark,image_aW1nL3dhdGVybWFyay9xYy9xYzE0NC5wbmc=,g_3,x_14,y_14,a_0,t_100" w="936" HarmonyOS 3.0 系统，整体配置强于 P50 系列，但依然为 4G 机型。其中 Mate 50 Pro 后置星环相机模组，前置支持人脸识别解锁相机。</p>
