<p><a class="s_tag" href="https://www.ithome.com/" target="_blank">IT之家</a> 6 月 14 日消息 今年 3 月份，华为申请注册了“<span class="accentTextColor">小艺输入法</span>”商标，暗示该输入法即将发布。</p><p><img src="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2021/6/4b401069-1a14-49dd-a2f9-409a457a646b.jpg" w="650" h="631" title="华为小艺输入法曝光" width="650" height="631" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>近日，酷安网友 @湖北工业大学发言人 曝光了<span class="accentTextColor">小艺输入法的使用截图</span>，该输入法已上线早期版本。</p><p><img src="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2021/6/1f0a4260-0723-4953-a7db-7d827b42c1f6.png" w="732" h="527" title="华为小艺输入法曝光" width="732" height="527" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>从图中可以看出，<span class="accentTextColor">小艺输入法整体较为简洁，拥有四款主题配色</span>。不过实际测试使用，该输入法功能比较简陋，<span class="accentTextColor">中文输入目前也有很多问题</span>，比如 9 键无法连打词组。</p><p><img src="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2021/6/9a503d16-53ba-400c-84eb-8d754a28fbe9.png" w="368" h="230" title="华为小艺输入法曝光" width="368" height="230" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2021/6/8ea0f040-a832-4594-9ed5-bf012daf9cb5.png" w="391" h="309" title="华为小艺输入法曝光" width="391" height="309" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2021/6/075c3984-ae1c-4709-8709-c886a195ea5d.png" w="400" h="409" title="华为小艺输入法曝光" width="400" height="409" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2021/6/3d15686e-7f48-4eaa-95a3-9a1397fbc0e7.png" w="630" h="720" title="华为小艺输入法曝光" width="630" height="720" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>IT之家了解到，据酷安网友 @湖北工业大学发言人 测试，<span class="accentTextColor">该输入法可在华为应用市场英国区下载</span>，或者<a href="https://xiesicong.lanzoui.com/iifcQq626ni" target="_blank">点此</a>直接下载。</p>
