系统</span>。</p><p data-vmark="ec00"><img src="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2021/12/788acb83-b756-494f-948e-c71363f486e6.png" w="1176" h="932" alt title="联想 17 英寸 ThinkBook Plus data-vmark="a90f"><img src="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2021/12/339a6986-66ee-4ac0-bdcf-6e5b2e8c76b5.png" w="1170" h="925" alt title="联想 17 英寸 ThinkBook Plus 更多渲染图曝光：笔记本内塞入“平板”，运行 Win11"
