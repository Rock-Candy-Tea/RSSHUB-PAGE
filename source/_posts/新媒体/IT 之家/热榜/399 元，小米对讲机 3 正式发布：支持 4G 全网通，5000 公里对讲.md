class="accentTextColor">可双向语音通话</span>。</p><p style="text-align: center;" data-vmark="05b5"><img src="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2022/4/216d8f7c-b740-4d42-ba98-4d83b06be129.png" w="849"
