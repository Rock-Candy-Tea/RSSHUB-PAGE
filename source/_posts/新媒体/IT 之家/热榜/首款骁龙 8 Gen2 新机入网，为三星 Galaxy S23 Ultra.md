输出</strong>。</p><p style="text-align: center;" data-vmark="dc0d"><img src="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2022/9/0c1d8f72-78f5-4bf3-a5c2-644f0fa18187.png" w="1440" h="440" title="首款骁龙 8 Gen2
