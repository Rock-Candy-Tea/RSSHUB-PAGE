src="https://img.ithome.com/newsuploadfiles/2022/9/9478e79e-3de9-4342-8a51-5f25545396e1.jpg@s_2,w_820,h_546" w="1440" h="959" title="消息称苹果 iPhone 14 Pro“灵动岛”药丸挖孔屏将延续几代，并且一步步缩小"
