<p><a href="https://9to5mac.com/guides/aapl/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">Apple</a> has shared photos of its spectacular new <a href="https://9to5mac.com/2022/02/10/united-arab-emirates-apple-store-4/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">Al Maryah Island Apple Store</a>, ahead of tomorrow’s opening. It follows the <a href="https://9to5mac.com/2022/02/03/latest-apple-store-abu-dhabi/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">recent opening of the Yas Mall store</a>.</p>
<p>The latest Abu Dhabi <a href="https://9to5mac.com/guides/apple-store/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">store</a> has a dramatic water feature, blurs the boundary between interior and exterior, has a golden carbon fiber roof, and offers panoramic views of the skyline … </p>
<p><span id="more-789098"></span>
</p>
<p>The Cupertino company <a href="https://9to5mac.com/2022/02/10/united-arab-emirates-apple-store-4/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">recently teased the store</a>, revealing the cascading water feature, and a custom-painted barricade mural.</p>
<blockquote class="wp-block-quote">
<p>The barricade pays tribute to pearls and pearl divers, and what they represent for Abu Dhabi’s history and culture. “The pearl and the environment it is born out of is a great metaphor for the growth of the city, and its investment in its creative community that is driving full speed towards the future,” Apple said.</p>
</blockquote>
<p>The hoardings are now gone, and the store within is revealed.</p>
<blockquote class="wp-block-quote">
<p>Apple today previewed Apple Al Maryah Island, a new retail destination elevated above steps of cascading water. The new store creates a direct connection from The Galleria Al Maryah Island to the water’s edge, delivering the best of Apple with shoreline views amid Abu Dhabi’s prestigious financial district.</p>
<p>“We couldn’t be more thrilled to open the stunning Apple Al Maryah Island this Friday,” said Deirdre O’Brien, Apple’s senior vice president of Retail + People. “Our talented team is ready to welcome and delight customers from this vibrant community to our newest location in the Emirates” […]</p>
<p>Apple Al Maryah Island introduces several new design elements reflective of its surroundings. From the promenade, two ramps paved with Absolute Black granite stone  — the first use of this material at any Apple Store — cut through water and continue seamlessly inside the store, blurring the barrier between inside and out.  The store also features a golden carbon fiber roof locally sourced in the UAE.</p>
<p>Visitors entering from The Galleria Al Maryah Island Mall will walk through a mirrored stainless steel portal, enveloped by the sights and sounds of water jets cascading over exterior glass walls. A 72-foot-long mirrored foil ceiling creates a kaleidoscopic effect, with water falling down the walls, and reflections seen above.</p>
</blockquote>
<p>Then company also announced a unique set of three Today at Apple sessions, under the banner “Creativity Within.”</p>
<blockquote class="wp-block-quote">
<p>The sessions draw inspiration from the beautiful surroundings and creative culture of Abu Dhabi. Photo Walk: Fluidity and Motion with iPhone 13 Pro, co-created with local photographer Mohamed Somji. Music Walk: Liquid Soundscapes with GarageBand. Art Walk: Drawing Creativity Within Session Guide.</p>
</blockquote>
<p>Future sessions will also feature local creatives, and Apple has pulled together a special <a href="https://apple.co/3sNStYo" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">Apple Music playlist</a> featuring United Arab Emirates artists.</p>
