<p class="MsoNormal">临近年末了，欧美的新年假期要开始了，下周就没多少人工作了，<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>也赶在假期之前推出了<span lang="EN-US">Geforce Game Ready Driver</span>显卡驱动<span lang="EN-US">497.29</span>，优化了多款游戏支持，其中《地平线：零之曙光》支持了<span lang="EN-US">DLSS</span>，性能猛增<span lang="EN-US">50%</span>，推荐升级。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA Geforce Game Ready Driver 497.29</span>版显卡驱动发布，为本月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日发布的新游戏《<span lang="EN-US">GTFO</span>》提供了优化支持，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>同时也为《地平线：零之曙光》带来了<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA DLSS</span>技术，游戏性能提升可达<span lang="EN-US">50%</span>。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此外，新版驱动还更新了<span lang="EN-US">GeForce Experience</span>一键优化列表，添加了几款新游戏，具体如下：</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">-</span>《<span lang="EN-US">GTA</span>三部曲：终极版》</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US"> -</span>《<span lang="EN-US">GTA 3</span>》</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US"> -</span>《<span lang="EN-US">GTA</span>：罪恶都市》</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US"> -</span>《<span lang="EN-US">GTA</span>：圣安地列斯》</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US"> -</span>《<span lang="EN-US">Chorus</span>》</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">-</span>《<span lang="EN-US">Wartales</span>》</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US"> -</span>《魔兽世界：暗影国度》</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">-</span>《<span lang="EN-US">EVE Online</span>》</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">-</span>《上古卷轴<span lang="EN-US">Online</span>》</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">-</span>《地平线：零之曙光 完整版》</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">-</span>《雷霆一号》</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong><span lang="EN-US">497.29</span>版驱动<span lang="EN-US">Bug</span>修复列表：</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">1</span>、《微软飞行模拟<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>》游戏崩溃至桌面。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2</span>、《微软飞行模拟<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>》地面纹理显示紫色<span lang="EN-US">/</span>蓝色伪影。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">3</span>、《最高指挥官<span lang="EN-US">1/2</span>》移动鼠标会导致游戏性能下降。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">4</span>、移动鼠标会导致桌面卡顿一段时间。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">5</span>、在多显示器配置中，屏幕可能出现随机黑屏闪烁。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">6</span>、德语环境下，控制面板<span lang="EN-US">-</span>着色器缓存大小选项拼写错误。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>部分已知错误：</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">1</span>、《死亡循环》开启<span lang="EN-US">HDR</span>会导致游戏<span lang="EN-US">TDR</span>或崩溃。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2</span>、《索尼克全明星赛车：变形》在通过水下场景时会发生崩溃。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">3</span>、在从<span lang="EN-US">Windows</span>控制面板中打开<span lang="EN-US">HDR</span>或从<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>控制面板中切换<span lang="EN-US">G-Sync</span>后，鼠标指针会卡住。遇到问题的用户请用左<span lang="EN-US">/</span>右键单击鼠标，光标卡顿几秒后会恢复正常。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">4</span>、多显示器设置模式设置为<span lang="EN-US">dGPU</span>时，系统从<span lang="EN-US">s4</span>模式回复后这一设置无法保留。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">5</span>、<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA Image Scaling</span>应用到桌面时，系统冷启动时，桌面会移到左上角。<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>提醒不要将<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA Image Scaling</span>功能应用到桌面。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">6</span>、开启<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA Image Scaling</span>时，执行<span lang="EN-US">HDR</span>转换会导致使用<span lang="EN-US">DX11</span>的视频播放器损坏或系统挂起。</p>
