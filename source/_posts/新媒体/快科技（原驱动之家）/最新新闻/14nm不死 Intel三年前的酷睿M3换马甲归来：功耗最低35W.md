<p class="MsoNormal"><a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/779/779525.htm" target="_blank">日前有消息称微软正在准备一款低端的<span lang="EN-US">Surface Go3</span>平板</a>，使用的处理器是“<span lang="EN-US">Amber Lake”</span>架构<span lang="EN-US">Y</span>系列，其中低配版是奔腾金牌<span lang="EN-US">6500Y</span>处理器，高配版是<span lang="EN-US">i3-10100Y</span>，搭配<span lang="EN-US">8GB</span>内存。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">奔腾金牌<span lang="EN-US">6500Y</span>处理器是今年<span lang="EN-US">Q1</span>季度发布的新品，然而它被人挖出来其实是马甲版，实际上是<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年推出的酷睿<span lang="EN-US">M3-8100Y</span>处理器。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">这两款处理器都是<span lang="EN-US">2</span>核<span lang="EN-US">4</span>线程，<span lang="EN-US">Amber Lake Y</span>架构，<span lang="EN-US">14nm</span>工艺，基础频率<span lang="EN-US">1.1GHz</span>，加速频率<span lang="EN-US">3.4GHz</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不同之处在于奔腾金牌<span lang="EN-US">6500Y</span>的功耗更低，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>标准<span lang="EN-US">TDP</span>是<span lang="EN-US">5W</span>，但可配置<span lang="EN-US">TDP</span>最低<span lang="EN-US">3.5W</span>，最高<span lang="EN-US">7W</span>，而酷睿<span lang="EN-US">M3-8100Y</span>标准<span lang="EN-US">TDP</span>也是<span lang="EN-US">5W</span>，但最低<span lang="EN-US">4.5W</span>，最高<span lang="EN-US">8W</span>。</strong></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="14nm不死 Intel三年前的酷睿M3换马甲归来：功耗最低3.5W" h="406" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210830/ddf5b3fe-5fd7-4c42-ab05-11fe36d9c73e.png" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">换句话说，三年来酷睿<span lang="EN-US">M3-8100Y</span>换了新马甲，主要变化就是可配置<span lang="EN-US">TDP</span>功耗低了<span lang="EN-US">1W</span>，对低功耗设备来说有助于提升续航。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">至于为什么换马甲，应该是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>不再继续酷睿<span lang="EN-US">M</span>产品线了，低功耗产品还是继续用赛扬、奔腾品牌了。</p>
