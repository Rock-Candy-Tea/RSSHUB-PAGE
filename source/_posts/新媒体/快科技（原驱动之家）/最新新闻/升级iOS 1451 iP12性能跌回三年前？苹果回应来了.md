<p class="MsoNormal">还记得苹果的降速门吗？最近升级了<span lang="EN-US">iOS 14.5.1</span>系统的玩家也感觉“被降速”了，<a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/755/755852.htm" target="_blank"><span lang="EN-US">iPhone 12</span>的性能甚至不如三年前的<span lang="EN-US">iPhone XR</span></a>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">iOS 14.5</span>正式版于<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日推送，是<span lang="EN-US">iOS 14</span>推出以来最重大的一次升级，新内容包括<span lang="EN-US">APP</span>跟踪透明度功能、允许<span lang="EN-US">Apple Watch</span>用户免<span lang="EN-US">Face ID</span>解锁<span lang="EN-US">iPhone</span>、<span lang="EN-US">iPhone 12</span>系列的<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>双驻网支持、多重音色的<span lang="EN-US">Siri</span>、支持<span lang="EN-US">PS5/Xbox Series</span>手柄等。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不过，紧接着在<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，<span lang="EN-US">iOS 14.5.1</span>迅速降临，修复了<span lang="EN-US">APP</span>跟踪透明度重新启用后没有提示的<span lang="EN-US">BUG</span>、并修复了与<span lang="EN-US">WebKit</span>有关的严重安全问题。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">但是<span lang="EN-US">iOS 14.5.1</span>系统似乎有些问题，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>特别是<span lang="EN-US">iPhone 11</span>、<span lang="EN-US">iPhone 12</span>用户，不少人在网上抱怨称系统变卡了，甚至卡死，打《王者荣耀》等游戏也掉帧严重。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">之前有<span lang="EN-US">UP</span>主作了测试，<strong><span lang="EN-US">iPhone 11</span>和<span lang="EN-US">iPhone 12</span>的性能表现竟不如<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年前<span lang="EN-US">(2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">)</span>发布的<span lang="EN-US">iPhone XR</span>。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">对于质疑，有媒体询问了苹果官方客户，得到的回应称，苹果官方暂时没有接到“升级<span lang="EN-US">iOS14.5.1</span>性能下降<span lang="EN-US">”</span>的通知，也没有接到用户大规模反馈。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">对于第三方发布的信息，苹果不便评论，也无法对其准确性负责，苹果官方重视消费者反映的问题。”</p>
