<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">昨晚<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>解禁了<span lang="EN-US">RX 6600 XT</span>显卡，这是<span lang="EN-US">RNDA2</span>家族又一款新品，售价<span lang="EN-US">2999</span>元，主打<span lang="EN-US">1080p</span>游戏性能。现在<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>又发布了<span lang="EN-US">Radeon 21.8.1</span>驱动，首发支持新卡，并修复了一些小问题。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">RX 6600 XT</span>仍然采用<span lang="EN-US">RDNA 2</span>架构，不过核心从<span lang="EN-US">RX 6700 XT</span>的<span lang="EN-US">Navi 22</span>换成了规模稍小<span lang="EN-US">Navi 23</span>，原生<span lang="EN-US">32</span>组计算单元<span lang="EN-US">(2048</span>个流处理器<span lang="EN-US">)</span>、<span lang="EN-US">32</span>个光追单元，加速频率达到了<span lang="EN-US">2589MHz</span>，相比于<span lang="EN-US">RX 6900 XT/6800 XT</span>高出足有<span lang="EN-US">300MHz</span>之多。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">整卡功耗<span lang="EN-US">160W</span>，其中<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>核心功耗是<span lang="EN-US">145W</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">显存搭配<span lang="EN-US">128-bit 8GB GDDR6</span>，显存频率<span lang="EN-US">16GHz</span>，带宽<span lang="EN-US">256GB/s</span>。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210811/d14ded30-3f4d-4f8d-819d-5392999fe844.jpg" style="text-align: center;" target="_blank"><img alt="AMD发布Radeon 21.8.1驱动：首发支持RX 6600 XT显卡" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210811/Sd14ded30-3f4d-4f8d-819d-5392999fe844.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong><span lang="EN-US">Radeon 21.8.1</span>驱动升级说明：</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD Radeon Software Adrenalin 21.8.1</span>版显卡驱动发布，更新内容不多，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>主要为<span lang="EN-US">Radeon RX 6600 XT</span>显卡提供支持，同时也修复了后台运行<span lang="EN-US">FrameView</span>程序时《<span lang="EN-US">The Medium</span>》游戏启动崩溃的问题。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">上个版本的已知问题均未修复，还额外增加了两条，具体如下：</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">1</span>、<span lang="EN-US">Ryzen Master</span>自动更新功能失效。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2</span>、使用<span lang="EN-US">Radeon RX 6600 XT</span>显卡时，《<span lang="EN-US">Control</span>》在<span lang="EN-US">DX12</span>模式下会出现光线异常。</p>
