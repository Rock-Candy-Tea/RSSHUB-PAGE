<p>4月12日消息，据上汽大众官方消息，2022款途岳Tharu正式上市，<strong>新车共推出七款车型，<span style="color:#ff0000;">售价区间为15.66-21.46万元</span></strong>。</p>
<p>该车作为年款车型，与现款相比整体变化不大，依旧采用了Rock-Cutting岩刻设计理念，整个就是一“小途昂”。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220412/790c2de92d0c497790f48765acbb28bb.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="2022款上汽大众途岳上市：15.66万就能买到“小途昂” " h="1273" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220412/s_790c2de92d0c497790f48765acbb28bb.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>细节方面，新车前格栅与前大灯融为一体，进一步增强了前脸的视觉宽度，同时也有效地凸显了力量感。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220412/483c4fb03de444c59d5948b390a0bd0f.png" target="_blank"><img alt="2022款上汽大众途岳上市：15.66万就能买到“小途昂” " h="417" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220412/s_483c4fb03de444c59d5948b390a0bd0f.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>侧面和尾部同样变化不大，车身多用平直的线条勾勒增强了车辆的肌肉和力量感。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220412/b75026e4aaca40659f2d14eff06b3b86.png" target="_blank"><img alt="2022款上汽大众途岳上市：15.66万就能买到“小途昂” " h="433" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220412/s_b75026e4aaca40659f2d14eff06b3b86.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>途岳定位于紧凑型SUV，<strong>车身尺寸为4453*1841*1632毫米，轴距2680毫米</strong>，得益于MQB平台的优点，其内部空间表现较为优秀，最大容积可达1543升。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220412/a94ce3de221d4fa187aa47d9225fcc6d.png" target="_blank"><img alt="2022款上汽大众途岳上市：15.66万就能买到“小途昂” " h="411" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220412/s_a94ce3de221d4fa187aa47d9225fcc6d.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>内饰同样遵从了现款车型的设计风格，<strong>部分车型拥有10.2英寸全液晶仪表盘和9.2英寸镶嵌式中控屏</strong>，其中中控屏拥有多媒体交互系统，支持智能语音、导航、娱乐和控车等多种功能。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220412/1b3f0b5870fe46afb77eff3ceead2259.png" target="_blank"><img alt="2022款上汽大众途岳上市：15.66万就能买到“小途昂” " h="372" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220412/s_1b3f0b5870fe46afb77eff3ceead2259.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220412/5bd64b44af7746168752cccd4af7fa5a.png" target="_blank"><img alt="2022款上汽大众途岳上市：15.66万就能买到“小途昂” " h="426" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220412/s_5bd64b44af7746168752cccd4af7fa5a.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220412/d9d8667343274006aec039467dc9a725.png" target="_blank"><img alt="2022款上汽大众途岳上市：15.66万就能买到“小途昂” " h="384" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220412/s_d9d8667343274006aec039467dc9a725.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220412/b194cb72cec548159edfde3d0cf3f4b3.png" target="_blank"><img alt="2022款上汽大众途岳上市：15.66万就能买到“小途昂” " h="428" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220412/s_b194cb72cec548159edfde3d0cf3f4b3.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>安全配置方面，<strong>该车拥有ACC自适应续航系统</strong>，可根据车流状况调节车速，同时带有前后泊车雷达，12个超声波传感器等，即便是新手也能轻松完成停车。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220412/da056ef42ffd4d8f909ec6ad1c1d8957.png" target="_blank"><img alt="2022款上汽大众途岳上市：15.66万就能买到“小途昂” " h="376" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220412/s_da056ef42ffd4d8f909ec6ad1c1d8957.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>该车配备的是1.4T和2.0T两款发动机，与之匹配的均为7速双离合变速箱，相信这两款发动机大家并不陌生，其已经在大众家服役多年，经过了市场的检验，成熟度相当之高。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220412/2ffd21e6152a407c9cec7670fc7dbf45.png" target="_blank"><img alt="2022款上汽大众途岳上市：15.66万就能买到“小途昂” " h="366" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220412/s_2ffd21e6152a407c9cec7670fc7dbf45.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>不过需要注意的是，<strong>无论是1.4T还是2.0T发动机，均需要加注95号汽油</strong>，在用车成本方面会“吃粗粮”的车型要高一些。</p>
