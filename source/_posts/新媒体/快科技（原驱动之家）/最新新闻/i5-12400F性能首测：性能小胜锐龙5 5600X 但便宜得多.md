<div>   
<p>Intel 12代酷睿首发只有桌面版的K、KF系列六款型号和Z690主板，包括移动端在内的更多型号、更多主板，要到明年初才会和大家见面。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211107/3eda2acba60d4c91b6852e558abae4cb.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="i5-12400F性能首测：性能小胜锐龙5 5600X 但便宜得多" h="358" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211107/s_3eda2acba60d4c91b6852e558abae4cb.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p><strong>现在，有外媒提前拿到了一颗i5-12400F，性价比更加神奇，性能持平或小胜AMD爆款级的锐龙5 5600X，但是价格必然便宜得多。</strong></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211107/3f551dfa95154cbab3c13c6d734a5cb8.png" target="_blank"><img alt="i5-12400F性能首测：性能小胜锐龙5 5600X 但便宜得多" h="199" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211107/s_3f551dfa95154cbab3c13c6d734a5cb8.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211107/2bfc3b41f0044461b28dd2a2709b1ddf.png" target="_blank"><img alt="i5-12400F性能首测：性能小胜锐龙5 5600X 但便宜得多" h="456" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211107/s_2bfc3b41f0044461b28dd2a2709b1ddf.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="349" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>i5-12400F只有<strong>6个大核心</strong>，12线程，二级缓存7.5MB，三级缓存18MB，主频最高4.4GHz，撞到温度墙后会降至3.4GHz。</p>
<p>热设计功耗为65W，实测主板会自动设置1<strong>17W</strong>的最高限制，可能就对应PL2/MTP(最大加速功耗)。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211107/2a730a2fe5c2497da74875d2d1cc4cc4.png" target="_blank"><img alt="i5-12400F性能首测：性能小胜锐龙5 5600X 但便宜得多" h="476" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211107/s_2a730a2fe5c2497da74875d2d1cc4cc4.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211107/20c4850b22e24d11b107c46c79d89172.png" target="_blank"><img alt="i5-12400F性能首测：性能小胜锐龙5 5600X 但便宜得多" h="505" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211107/s_20c4850b22e24d11b107c46c79d89172.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>CPU-Z、CineBench R23单核成绩相比锐龙5 5600X分别领先11.7％、6.2％。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211107/f34e0454638a4746911e09dbaae2a236.png" target="_blank"><img alt="i5-12400F性能首测：性能小胜锐龙5 5600X 但便宜得多" h="508" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211107/s_f34e0454638a4746911e09dbaae2a236.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211107/d952f55b906a44ef895a298304707cb6.png" target="_blank"><img alt="i5-12400F性能首测：性能小胜锐龙5 5600X 但便宜得多" h="502" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211107/s_d952f55b906a44ef895a298304707cb6.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>多核成绩则基本部分彼此，差距不超过2％。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211107/7b9ebf82a2b640158b537ae04b4fed00.png" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="i5-12400F性能首测：性能小胜锐龙5 5600X 但便宜得多" h="509" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211107/s_7b9ebf82a2b640158b537ae04b4fed00.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>12款游戏平均性能领先接近2％。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211107/1d78c698e6314197b85b3989fb4a6857.png" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="i5-12400F性能首测：性能小胜锐龙5 5600X 但便宜得多" h="485" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211107/s_1d78c698e6314197b85b3989fb4a6857.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>H.264编码测试功耗也基本一致，只高了4W(大约3％)。</p>
