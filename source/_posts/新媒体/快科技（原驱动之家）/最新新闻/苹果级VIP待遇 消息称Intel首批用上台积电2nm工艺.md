<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">从<span lang="EN-US">Intel 7</span>工艺开始，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>未来中也要开始爆发模式，预计到<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年前的<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年中量产<span lang="EN-US">5</span>代<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>工艺，其中<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年就有<span lang="EN-US">20A</span>及<span lang="EN-US">18A</span>两代工艺，但这还不是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的极限，他们还会跟台积电合作，<span lang="EN-US">3nm</span>及<span lang="EN-US">2nm</span>节点上会跟苹果一样成为首发客户。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Digitimes</span>援引消息人士的话称，台积电下一代的工艺有两大客户，除了苹果之外，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>也是首批获得产能的客户，双方的合作不仅是<span lang="EN-US">3nm</span>工艺，还包括再下一代的<span lang="EN-US">2nm</span>工艺。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据台积电的说法，<strong><span lang="EN-US">3nm</span>工艺会在今年下半年试产，明年大规模量产，而<span lang="EN-US">2nm</span>工艺会在<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年试产，<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年开始量产，可能是下半年或者年底。</strong></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220422/71231e61-a9e4-438a-88fd-15392c17e2f7.png" style="text-align: center;" target="_blank"><img alt="苹果级VIP待遇 消息称Intel首批用上台积电2nm工艺" h="292" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220422/S71231e61-a9e4-438a-88fd-15392c17e2f7.png" style="border: black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">对于台积电与<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的合作，双方一直没有公开证实，不过<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>在<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月投资者会议的<span lang="EN-US">PPT</span>上提到了<span lang="EN-US">3nm</span>节点会有外包生产的，就是没点名是台积电而已。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不过业界认为两家的合作差不多是板上钉钉了，<span lang="EN-US">Intel CEO</span>基辛格去年底拜访了台积电，传闻<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月份还会再次举行会谈，但具体内容尚无证实。</p>
