<p style="color: rgb(34, 34, 34); font-family: ����, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; font-style: normal; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="font-size:14px;">过年期间，最硬气的流通货，毫无疑问要数白酒，觥筹交错时，免不了要喝酒助兴，那么现在白酒还能受到人们的青睐吗？哪些地方的人最能喝酒？报告数据来了。</span></p>
<p style="color: rgb(34, 34, 34); font-family: ����, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; font-style: normal; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="font-size:14px;">综合百度指数、抖音指数和头条指数来看，截至2022年2月1日，近一周有8个省份喝酒热情最高。</span></p>
<p style="color: rgb(34, 34, 34); font-family: ����, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; font-style: normal; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="font-size:14px;">其中，<strong>广东是唯一同时位居3个榜单前三名的省份。另外，河南、山东、江苏、河北、四川、浙江、湖北7个省份的热度也很高。</strong></span></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220203/aca8c9c8844e4924a4e892bdb61471d2.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="过年哪个省的人最爱喝酒？排名来了 广东、河南无争议" h="602" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220203/s_aca8c9c8844e4924a4e892bdb61471d2.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p style="color: rgb(34, 34, 34); font-family: ����, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; font-style: normal; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;"> </p>
<p style="color: rgb(34, 34, 34); font-family: ����, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; font-style: normal; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="font-size:14px;">换言之，以上这几个省份的人，喝白酒的需求量最大，也是最能喝酒的省份。</span></p>
<p style="color: rgb(34, 34, 34); font-family: ����, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; font-style: normal; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="font-size:14px;">此外，据《2022年货节京东超市礼盒消费报告》，白酒、坚果、牛奶、饼干、茶叶五大类礼盒购买量领先。</span></p>
<p style="color: rgb(34, 34, 34); font-family: ����, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; font-style: normal; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="font-size:14px;"><strong>同时，春节恰逢年轻人聚会用酒高峰，高端红酒成交额同比增加423%。</strong>从近3年各平台销售数据来看，年货节、春节期间酒类消费都有大幅增长。</span></p>
<p style="color: rgb(34, 34, 34); font-family: ����, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; font-style: normal; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="font-size:14px;">从历年“喝酒”在春节当周的搜索热度来看，疫情发生以来，大家喝酒的热情比之前低了。据百度指数，2011年至2022年，12年间“喝酒”的搜索热度在2019年达到最高，为2009。</span></p>
<p style="color: rgb(34, 34, 34); font-family: ����, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; font-style: normal; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;"><span style="font-size:14px;">之后在2020年春节下降了近5成。近两年，“喝酒”的热度逐渐恢复，但最新数据显示，<strong>2022年春节当周“喝酒”的搜索热度仍低于2019年，相当于疫情前的7成。</strong></span></p>
