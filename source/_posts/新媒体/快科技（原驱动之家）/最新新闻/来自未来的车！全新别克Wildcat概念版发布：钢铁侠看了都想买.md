<p>汽车工业发展到今天，同质化越来越严重，但还是会有那么一些让人眼前一亮的车型出现，由于过于惊艳，让人忍不住驻足多看几眼。</p>
<p>6月1日晚，别克在国内发布了别克GL8世纪和别克Electra-X纯电动SUV概念车，随后，其又发布了一款<strong>名为Wildcat纯电动概念车</strong>，而它的出现则给了汽车设计以更多的启发。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220602/614ca9c672944ffdaee259683cbcac22.png" target="_blank"><img alt="来自未来的车！全新别克Wildcat概念版发布：钢铁侠看了都想买" h="420" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220602/s_614ca9c672944ffdaee259683cbcac22.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>Wildcat曾经是别克旗下一款经典运动轿车，用它命名新车，显然是有致敬之意。事实上，Wildcat纯电概念车也确实做到了科幻中带有几分复古的特征。</p>
<p>具体来看，作为一款电动车，其采用了封闭式前脸，下方包围造型十分夸张，而左右两侧的大灯则是典型的别克家族化设计语言。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220602/53f765305fca40928fa3e70486492351.png" target="_blank"><img alt="来自未来的车！全新别克Wildcat概念版发布：钢铁侠看了都想买" h="432" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220602/s_53f765305fca40928fa3e70486492351.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>侧面是其精髓所在，该车定位于双门四座轿跑，其车门十分宽大，<strong>而其当车门打开时，该车侧车顶也会小幅升起</strong>，以方便进出车内，而缓缓下溜的车顶非常具有美式复古风。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220602/4fa5072286e14249af1f8f6c0b010a6f.png" target="_blank"><img alt="来自未来的车！全新别克Wildcat概念版发布：钢铁侠看了都想买" h="385" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220602/s_4fa5072286e14249af1f8f6c0b010a6f.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220602/dc4e0dc80ab8461183b1963b1d022251.png" target="_blank"><img alt="来自未来的车！全新别克Wildcat概念版发布：钢铁侠看了都想买" h="416" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220602/s_dc4e0dc80ab8461183b1963b1d022251.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>此外，该车的轮圈采用了多辐式镀铬设计，造型夸张，据官方介绍设计灵感<strong>来源于喷气式发动机的扇叶</strong>。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220602/c2b7913bb2df4d0caaf34e3945fa1d33.png" target="_blank"><img alt="来自未来的车！全新别克Wildcat概念版发布：钢铁侠看了都想买" h="426" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220602/s_c2b7913bb2df4d0caaf34e3945fa1d33.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>车尾线条十分简洁，并采用了掀背式设计，值得一提的是，其尾灯造型与沃尔沃非常相似。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220602/0fba8454fa554f1790fef0fc27de9023.png" target="_blank"><img alt="来自未来的车！全新别克Wildcat概念版发布：钢铁侠看了都想买" h="415" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220602/s_0fba8454fa554f1790fef0fc27de9023.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220602/91e7a3487d7f4997a3412fc959b5380d.png" target="_blank"><img alt="来自未来的车！全新别克Wildcat概念版发布：钢铁侠看了都想买" h="425" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220602/s_91e7a3487d7f4997a3412fc959b5380d.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>内饰更是相当科幻，方向盘设计极度简洁，前方是贯穿式的大联屏，风格有点像凯迪拉克LYRYQ锐歌，在精致镀铬、烤漆工艺的映衬下，显得格外具有格调感。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220602/985cfaa8a9684316af49a6bf7e10d96d.png" target="_blank"><img alt="来自未来的车！全新别克Wildcat概念版发布：钢铁侠看了都想买" h="352" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220602/s_985cfaa8a9684316af49a6bf7e10d96d.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220602/a976a195ad9642ee97ce0c8322b25c48.png" target="_blank"><img alt="来自未来的车！全新别克Wildcat概念版发布：钢铁侠看了都想买" h="432" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220602/s_a976a195ad9642ee97ce0c8322b25c48.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>此外，该车还取消了传统外置后视镜，<strong>而是采用了摄像头+显示屏的组合方式</strong>，再次有效提升了科幻感。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220602/8db484793903455c94cfc983e5d8b10c.png" target="_blank"><img alt="来自未来的车！全新别克Wildcat概念版发布：钢铁侠看了都想买" h="429" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220602/s_8db484793903455c94cfc983e5d8b10c.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>该车的动力并未公布，如果量产后保持80%概念车的风采，想必它必然会受到不少人的追捧。</p>
