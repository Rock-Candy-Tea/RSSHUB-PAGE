<p class="MsoNormal">中国已经建成了全球最大的<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>网络，去年底<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>基站超过<span lang="EN-US">142</span>万，今年将达到<span lang="EN-US">200</span>万基站，不过此前的<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>网络中打电话还要回落到<span lang="EN-US">4G</span>网络，现在中国联通宣布开通<span lang="EN-US">5G VoNR</span>，打电话也能用<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>电信日活动上，中国联通发布<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>新通信产品、全光高品质算力网络、<span lang="EN-US">“</span>银龄专享<span lang="EN-US">”</span>服务计划。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>其中<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>新通信产品，系中国联通基于全球最大<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>共建共享商用网络携手中国电信在全国<span lang="EN-US">125</span>个城市正式开通<span lang="EN-US">5G VoNR</span>（超高清音视频通话）服务，推出<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>新通信首批更高清、更安心、更智能、更炫酷的八大应用。</strong></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="不用回落4G 中国联通宣布125个城市开通5G VoNR通话" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220517/5bbfe63c-9f57-4958-9fc6-966957df4b6a.jpg" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">据了解，<span lang="EN-US">VoNR</span>全称为<span lang="EN-US">Voice over New Radio</span>，可以简单理解为<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>下的高清通话，和<span lang="EN-US">VoLTE</span>相类似。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">具体来看，<span lang="EN-US">5G VoNR</span>是指由<span lang="EN-US">5G NR</span>、<span lang="EN-US">5G Core</span>和<span lang="EN-US">IMS</span>端到端承载语音业务，相比<span lang="EN-US">VoLTE</span>，它有着以下优点：明显更低的延迟；改善通话质量；更快的通话连接。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在语音通话音质方面，<span lang="EN-US">5G VoNR</span>支持全新<span lang="EN-US">EVS</span>编解码，比<span lang="EN-US">4G VoLTE</span>更先进，通话音质更清晰，不会出现杂音或断流听不清楚等情况。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">同时，其还支持<span lang="EN-US">720P</span>分辨率高清视频通话，相比<span lang="EN-US">VoLTE</span>的<span lang="EN-US">480P</span>分辨率提升了一个等级。</p>
