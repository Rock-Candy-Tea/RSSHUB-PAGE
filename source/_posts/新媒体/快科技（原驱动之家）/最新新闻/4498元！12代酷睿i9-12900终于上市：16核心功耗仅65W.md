<p>发布一个半月之后，Intel 12代酷睿中最高端的标准版型号i9-12900，终于上架开卖了，<a class="f14_link" href="https://s.click.taobao.com/t?e=m%3D2%26s%3DP8iUdtdkRh0cQipKwQzePOeEDrYVVa64r4ll3HtqqoxyINtkUhsv0ASrH9mKcSSuOekYk1TnYuKAFxbgjdC6ZeCzWIQeYXW%2FhPf0wWVheIrPb%2FDduni%2F93dV8%2Fdh3m56SBaygToy7XngYycgkNdmo21XtcPgUcQzCLttUhmYolnokyAkShz1DJRaunZkkkfTIYULNg46oBA%3D&scm=null&pvid=100_11.178.152.72_52284_2511645365582441936&app_pvid=59590_33.43.114.79_803_1645365582437&ptl=floorId:2836;originalFloorId:2836;pvid:100_11.178.152.72_52284_2511645365582441936;app_pvid:59590_33.43.114.79_803_1645365582437&xId=2YYE9j403scNRBk8P84J6dbFQviFa85VOzo2DF2ygug6SmXP4R10sMkYy5EbxDMc0HEZzwhpmBoBO3qxGDmwdXRiRMeYmZDh4FT0grgwgpEf&union_lens=lensId%3AMAPI%401645365582%40212b724f_0b2f_17f176e5a79_7772%4001" target="_blank">价格为4498元。</a></p>
<p>相比之下，顶级旗舰版i9-12900K的价格为4999元，二者相差501元。</p>
<p>i9-12900同样是8大8小16核心24线程、14MB二级缓存、30MB三级缓存、UHD 770核芯显卡(32单元)，<strong>性能核频率从3.2-5.2GHz降低至2.4-5.1GHz，能效核频率则从2.4-3.9GHz降至1.8-3.8GHz</strong>，不过核显频率依然保持在300-1550MHz。</p>
<p>基准功耗从125W来到了65W，最大加速功耗则从241W降低至202W。</p>
<p>盒装版的i9-12900<strong>自带原装散热器RH1</strong>，这也是12代酷睿三款散热器中最高端的，自带灯效。</p>
<p>另一款类似的型号i7-12700将在明天上架销售，12核心20线程。</p>
