<p align="center"><a href="http://click.aliyun.com/m/1000253340/" target="_blank" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://icons.mydrivers.com/2021/news/ayun72890.png" width="600" height="74" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
           </p><p>2月1日，影驰发布了<a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/738/738214.htm" target="_blank">基于群联电子第二代主控E18的第二款PCIe 4.0 SSD HOF EXTREME</a>，形态M.2，容量1/2TB，持续读取速度突破7GB/s。</p>
<p><a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/742/742453.htm" target="_blank">根据快科技的实测</a>，这款造型优美的PCIe 4.0 SSD，是当今最快的产品。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210324/a63d99f70c6d45f98e76487d68c4c53d.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="影驰二代PCIe 4.0 SSD HOF EXTREME上架：狂飙7GB/s、1TB只需1799" h="377" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210324/s_a63d99f70c6d45f98e76487d68c4c53d.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>现在，它正式上架预售了：<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>1TB 1899元、2TB 3599元。</strong></span></p>
<p><strong>同时，3月29日之前在京东预付99元定金、天猫预付100元，3月30日结算尾款，就可以享受1799元的优惠价格。</strong></p>
<p>相比之下，上一代HOF PRO上架之初的价格为：500GB 1699元、1TB 2999元、2TB 4999元。</p>
<p class="tgbaobei"><b>购买链接：</b><a href="https://dt.mydrivers.com/qq.ashx?atr=1YW9" target="_blank">京东(1799元)</a>、<a href="https://dt.mydrivers.com/qq.ashx?atr=QKlD" target="_blank">天猫(1799元)</a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210324/4c541b7c57784839ad45b68ff0523190.png" target="_blank"><img alt="影驰二代PCIe 4.0 SSD HOF EXTREME上架：狂飙7GB/s、1TB只需1799" h="399" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210324/s_4c541b7c57784839ad45b68ff0523190.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210324/4362893e312c41548ec3587208660f3c.png" target="_blank"><img alt="影驰二代PCIe 4.0 SSD HOF EXTREME上架：狂飙7GB/s、1TB只需1799" h="399" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210324/s_4362893e312c41548ec3587208660f3c.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>影驰HOF EXTREME PCIe 4.0 SSD采用<strong>内盒+外盒套设计</strong>，其中外壳套左右双开口，推取毫不费力，配合全烫银的LOGO格调满满，同时还有原生PCIe 4.0的角标，方便玩家辨识。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210324/fe6d74a484a34cdc9a3b7a68f52db3ef.png" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="影驰二代PCIe 4.0 SSD HOF EXTREME上架：狂飙7GB/s、1TB只需1799" h="399" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210324/s_fe6d74a484a34cdc9a3b7a68f52db3ef.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>内盒采用翻盖设计，内置磁铁吸附，整体延续HOF系列莹白典雅风格，加入六边形元素全烫银LOGO。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210324/98d0421fa016412ead27c08132123799.png" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="影驰二代PCIe 4.0 SSD HOF EXTREME上架：狂飙7GB/s、1TB只需1799" h="399" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210324/s_98d0421fa016412ead27c08132123799.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>SSD本体采用全新外观设计，搭配名人堂的招牌白色、LOGO，奢华气息弥漫开来。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210324/c62dcf2117144229bc11b6879572c9a4.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="影驰二代PCIe 4.0 SSD HOF EXTREME上架：狂飙7GB/s、1TB只需1799" h="399" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210324/s_c62dcf2117144229bc11b6879572c9a4.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>M.2 2280形态，<strong>全新银色冰镜散热器，反光镜面，精细切割，辅以铝材+最高双层的8W导热胶。</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210324/65798e5f72cb49fa91f36fc0b0296ce3.png" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="影驰二代PCIe 4.0 SSD HOF EXTREME上架：狂飙7GB/s、1TB只需1799" h="398" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210324/s_65798e5f72cb49fa91f36fc0b0296ce3.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>白色定制PCB。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210324/f3dd82d105b841c9ab369093dd422a33.png" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="影驰二代PCIe 4.0 SSD HOF EXTREME上架：狂飙7GB/s、1TB只需1799" h="399" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210324/s_f3dd82d105b841c9ab369093dd422a33.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>群联PS5018-E18主控使用台积电12nm制程生产，三核心CPU，第四代LDPC纠错引擎，相比上代E16不但性能大增，同时功耗更低、发热更低。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210324/c72c7b5c0d7341988cb90961cbec22e9.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="影驰二代PCIe 4.0 SSD HOF EXTREME上架：狂飙7GB/s、1TB只需1799" h="399" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210324/s_c72c7b5c0d7341988cb90961cbec22e9.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>严格挑选3D NAND闪存、缓存颗粒，单颗256GB。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210324/6590b9036d7340dc956176977708226e.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="影驰二代PCIe 4.0 SSD HOF EXTREME上架：狂飙7GB/s、1TB只需1799" h="399" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210324/s_6590b9036d7340dc956176977708226e.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210324/7459f5056e854b9c9d36bf98db885b97.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="影驰二代PCIe 4.0 SSD HOF EXTREME上架：狂飙7GB/s、1TB只需1799" h="399" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210324/s_7459f5056e854b9c9d36bf98db885b97.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p><strong>PCIe 4.0 x4八通道，持续读写速度可达7GB/s、6.85GB/s，随机读写则可达700K/650K IOPS。</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210324/93a1ebbf5d074a48a80041cdd8d8d39e.png" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="影驰二代PCIe 4.0 SSD HOF EXTREME上架：狂飙7GB/s、1TB只需1799" h="393" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210324/s_93a1ebbf5d074a48a80041cdd8d8d39e.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>同时，影驰还公布了一系列官方测试，供参考。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210324/804dc891449b4011a7a0996fe689ed2c.png" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="影驰二代PCIe 4.0 SSD HOF EXTREME上架：狂飙7GB/s、1TB只需1799" h="574" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210324/s_804dc891449b4011a7a0996fe689ed2c.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>CrystalDiskInfo显示，待机状态下的温度仅为39度。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210324/a96ffd74f7884fe5ad7c68d660549d71.png" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="影驰二代PCIe 4.0 SSD HOF EXTREME上架：狂飙7GB/s、1TB只需1799" h="367" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210324/s_a96ffd74f7884fe5ad7c68d660549d71.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="402" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>CrystalDiskMark测试持续读取速度达到7229MB/s，写入则是6772MB/s，相比旗舰级PCIe 3.0 SSD轻松翻倍，比上代PCIe 4.0 SSD也提速了大约40％。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210324/a081da6913b74e7aae173c62a359ea57.png" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="影驰二代PCIe 4.0 SSD HOF EXTREME上架：狂飙7GB/s、1TB只需1799" h="469" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210324/s_a081da6913b74e7aae173c62a359ea57.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="499" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>AS SSD Benchmark测试随机读写速度达到690K、100K IOPS，<strong>总跑分第一次破万。</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210324/39963da1f60d4053b0e903508435fa31.png" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="影驰二代PCIe 4.0 SSD HOF EXTREME上架：狂飙7GB/s、1TB只需1799" h="639" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210324/s_39963da1f60d4053b0e903508435fa31.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="530" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>ATTO测试持续读取速度稳定在6.88GB/s，写入也稳定在6.30GB/s。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210324/08688431ee21461fa29958f4f8c7213f.png" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="影驰二代PCIe 4.0 SSD HOF EXTREME上架：狂飙7GB/s、1TB只需1799" h="396" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210324/s_08688431ee21461fa29958f4f8c7213f.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>PCMark 8测试中总跑分为5076，存储带宽为551.3MB/s。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210324/bde8aa4cfde7450a8f70e0b09c7aad4d.png" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="影驰二代PCIe 4.0 SSD HOF EXTREME上架：狂飙7GB/s、1TB只需1799" h="394" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210324/s_bde8aa4cfde7450a8f70e0b09c7aad4d.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>实际文件复制粘贴测试，速度稳定在1.8GB/s，而普通PCIe 3.0 SSD速度只有340MB/s，两者的速度相差五倍。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210324/c655a6e58c634b5186ed84dd9677db5c.png" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="影驰二代PCIe 4.0 SSD HOF EXTREME上架：狂飙7GB/s、1TB只需1799" h="539" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210324/s_c655a6e58c634b5186ed84dd9677db5c.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>89GB大文件解压缩测试，耗时1分51秒，而普通PCIe 3.0 SSD共耗时3分5秒。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210324/e983bf9c59cc4ef3984dfb55e643b226.png" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="影驰二代PCIe 4.0 SSD HOF EXTREME上架：狂飙7GB/s、1TB只需1799" h="450" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210324/s_e983bf9c59cc4ef3984dfb55e643b226.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>24.6GB设计文件导入测试，用时25.62秒，而普通PCIe 3.0 SSD用时39.60秒，差了超过一半。</p>
