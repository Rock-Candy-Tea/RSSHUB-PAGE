<p class="MsoNormal">前几天<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>发布的<span lang="EN-US">Q3</span>季度财报中，<span lang="EN-US">Radeon</span>显卡的销量超过预期，<span lang="EN-US">RX 6000</span>系列很受欢迎，该系列显卡还会支撑差不多一年，明年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月份才会有<span lang="EN-US">RX 7000</span>系列显卡，不过这次首发的可能是<span lang="EN-US">6nm Navi33</span>核心，中端而非旗舰卡。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的<span lang="EN-US">Navi3X</span>系列会是下一代<span lang="EN-US">RDNA3</span>架构，至少有<span lang="EN-US">Navi 31</span>、<span lang="EN-US">Navi 32</span>及<span lang="EN-US">Navi 33</span>三个核心，传闻颇多，但也比较乱，这里再重新梳理下。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Navi 31</span>是旗舰级显卡的核心，<strong>分为两个<span lang="EN-US">5nm GCD</span>模块、一个<span lang="EN-US">6nm MCD</span>模块，分别是计算节点、输入输出节点，集成多达<span lang="EN-US">15360</span>个流处理器、<span lang="EN-US">512MB</span>无限缓存，搭配<span lang="EN-US">256-bit GDDR6</span>。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Navi 31</span>核心的性能预计是<span lang="EN-US">Navi 21</span>的<span lang="EN-US">2.5-2.7</span>倍，也就是提升<span lang="EN-US">150%</span>以上，<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月份已经流片，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>但预计<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月份才有可能上市，</strong></span>据说是<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>产能要优先同期的<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>处理器。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Navi 32</span>核心也是<span lang="EN-US">2</span>个<span lang="EN-US">GCN</span>模块<span lang="EN-US">+1</span>个<span lang="EN-US">GCD</span>模块，不过流处理器单元减少到<span lang="EN-US">10240</span>个，<span lang="EN-US">192bit</span>位宽，<span lang="EN-US">384MB</span>无限缓存，明年<span lang="EN-US">Q1</span>季度流片，<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年春季发布。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">这两个高端核心的显卡，差不多就是<span lang="EN-US">RX 7900/7800/7700</span>系列显卡要等到后年初之后才能问世。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">最快问世的<span lang="EN-US">Navi3</span>系列显卡是<span lang="EN-US">Navi 33</span>核心，但它的架构跟两位大哥完全不同，<strong>没有采用多芯片封装，还是<span lang="EN-US">6nm</span>工艺，核心面积约为<span lang="EN-US">440mm2</span>，流处理器单元也不是传闻中的<span lang="EN-US">5120</span>个，而是<span lang="EN-US">4096</span>个，</strong>搭配<span lang="EN-US">128bit GDDR6</span>显卡，<span lang="EN-US">128MB</span>或者<span lang="EN-US">256MB</span>无限缓存，预计会在明年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月份上市。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">简单来说，明年的<span lang="EN-US">RX 7000</span>系列显卡中，首先问世的反而可能是<span lang="EN-US">RX 7600</span>系列的<span lang="EN-US">Navi33</span>核心，没有预期中的小芯片多模块封装，还是传统的<span lang="EN-US">6nm</span>单芯。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">大家最期待的高端<span lang="EN-US">RX 7000</span>系列显卡最快也要到<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年初了，会使用<span lang="EN-US">5nm+6nm</span>混合封装，性能提升也非常恐怖，<span lang="EN-US">1.5</span>倍以上的增幅，<span lang="EN-US">1.5</span>万个流处理器单元。</p>
