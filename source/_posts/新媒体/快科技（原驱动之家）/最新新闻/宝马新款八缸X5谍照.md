<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/9db8e5d20e294f9f906d9ad4e679caf6.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马新款八缸X5谍照" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/s_9db8e5d20e294f9f906d9ad4e679caf6.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/7fc70ba58ad247dca48d0081fb347796.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马新款八缸X5谍照" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/s_7fc70ba58ad247dca48d0081fb347796.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>这台X5的四出排气以及蓝色M高性能卡钳暴露了它的身份，新一代7系及X7所配备的S68八缸4.4升双涡轮增压发动机被下放至X5：</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/7d203de6827142529f97fba4ec38562e.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马新款八缸X5谍照" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/s_7d203de6827142529f97fba4ec38562e.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>现款最高规格X5的型号也由50i升级为X5 60i。尽管新设计的S68配备了48V轻混，电机可额外提供12马力@200牛·米动力输出，但动力系统整体功率指标保持不变，依然为523马力@750牛·米。</p>
<p>新款X5最重要的升级来自内部，与全新7系一样，改款后的X5将普及iDrive 8.0与曲面联屏。预计新款X5将于年底（海外）发布，明年春季交付实车。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/712c55bd5b8946428092fff008eacc56.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马新款八缸X5谍照" h="333" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/s_712c55bd5b8946428092fff008eacc56.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>另外，全新i5的谍照业已披露，显示该车外形与现款5系基本一致，只能从不翼而飞的尾部排气以及“电动测试车”标记看出这是一台基于现款5系的纯电中级轿车。</p>

<p>与新款X5的进化方向类似，i5相较于现款5系油车在内饰方面有天翻地覆般的变化，曲面联屏清晰可见，甚至配备了新7系的平底方向盘：</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/43211e38624c4a61a4957ecbbdf469c6.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马新款八缸X5谍照" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/s_43211e38624c4a61a4957ecbbdf469c6.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
