<p class="MsoNormal">今日股市收盘之后，<span lang="EN-US">A</span>股<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月份的答卷也来了，相比<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月份<span lang="EN-US">A</span>股市值增加了<span lang="EN-US">3.7</span>万亿，平均每个股民赚了<span lang="EN-US">1.95</span>万元。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据证券软件的数据，截至<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日，<span lang="EN-US">A</span>股总市值收报<span lang="EN-US">86.60</span>万亿。相比七月底，<span lang="EN-US">A</span>股增长了<span lang="EN-US">3.7</span>万亿市值。目前<span lang="EN-US">A</span>股大约有<span lang="EN-US">1,9</span>亿股民，平均下来每个股民本月赚了<span lang="EN-US">1.95</span>万元。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">8</span>月份<span lang="EN-US">A</span>股股市总体还是上涨的，但不同行业涨跌幅不一，<span lang="EN-US">28</span>个板块中涨幅最高的是为采掘<span lang="EN-US">(29.46%)</span>、有色金属<span lang="EN-US">(18.00%)</span>和钢铁<span lang="EN-US">(17.56%)</span>，跌幅前三为医药生物<span lang="EN-US">(-8.63%)</span>、电子<span lang="EN-US">(-6.97%)</span>和通信<span lang="EN-US">(-5.88%)</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>个股方面，位列牛股前三的公司依次为华辰装备<span lang="EN-US">(206.45%)</span>、兆新股份<span lang="EN-US">(169.91%)</span>和天音控股<span lang="EN-US">(161.90%)</span>，</strong></span>位列熊股前三的公司依次为<span lang="EN-US">*ST</span>星星<span lang="EN-US">(-56.09%)</span>、永福股份<span lang="EN-US">(-49.01%)</span>和康泰医学<span lang="EN-US">(-45.87%)</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不过要是算上新股，本月有<span lang="EN-US">40</span>只股票上市，涨幅最高的是能辉科技能辉科技，月涨幅为<span lang="EN-US">748.92%</span>。</p>
