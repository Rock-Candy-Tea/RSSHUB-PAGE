<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">昨晚小米发布了小米<span lang="EN-US">12</span>系列手机，其中小米<span lang="EN-US">12 Pro</span>不仅配备<span lang="EN-US">120W</span>快充，电池还做到了单芯<span lang="EN-US">4600mAh</span>，雷军表示要是双芯电池就只能做到<span lang="EN-US">4200mAh</span>，不过刚刚<span lang="EN-US">iQOO</span>宣布其<span lang="EN-US">iQOO 9</span>系列手机双芯电池就做到了<span lang="EN-US">4700mAh</span>容量。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">iQOO</span>官微刚刚公布了<span lang="EN-US">iQOO 9</span>系列手机的电池规格，<strong>首先是全系列<span lang="EN-US">iQOO 9</span>手机都标配<span lang="EN-US">120W</span>快充，无线快充最高<span lang="EN-US">50W</span>，这个跟之前的<span lang="EN-US">iQOO 8</span>系列是一样的。</strong></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="iQOO 9全系标配120W超级闪充：电池比小米12 Pro高出100mAh" h="1066" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211229/60afbffe-39f2-4f88-8814-86bbd0206788.jpg" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">电池容量也升级了，此前<span lang="EN-US">iQOO 8 Pro</span>最高<span lang="EN-US">4500mAh</span>电池，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>现在<span lang="EN-US">iQOO 9</span>系列做到了<span lang="EN-US">4700mAh</span>，依然是双电芯方案。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">昨晚的小米<span lang="EN-US">12</span>发布会上，雷军特别提到<span lang="EN-US">120W</span>快充下，双电芯电池方案只能做到<span lang="EN-US">4200mAh</span>电池，他们使用了自研澎湃<span lang="EN-US">P1+</span>单电芯方案做到了<span lang="EN-US">4600mAh</span>电池，不过<span lang="EN-US">iQOO 9</span>系列马上就跟进，<span lang="EN-US">120W</span>快充下双电芯依然有<span lang="EN-US">4700mAh</span>大电池，比小米<span lang="EN-US">12 Pro</span>还高<span lang="EN-US">100mAh</span>。</p>
