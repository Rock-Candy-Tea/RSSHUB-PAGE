<p>根据最新一期NPD月度数据可知，《艾尔登法环》面市以来，仅用2个月时间便在为期12个月的累计销量上战胜问世半年之久的年货CoD（使命召唤先锋）：</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220514/446736ff21b64101b7ceea618263df25.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《艾尔登法环》将年货挑落马下" h="315" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220514/s_446736ff21b64101b7ceea618263df25.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>四月销量冠军是当月发布的《乐高星际大战：天行者传奇》，面市月余的《艾尔登法环》退居次席：</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220514/a9241f72630842108672f94f30e0c98a.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="《艾尔登法环》将年货挑落马下" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220514/s_a9241f72630842108672f94f30e0c98a.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>菈妮不仅终结了CoD年货多年以来在北美地区的统治，同时也颠覆了发行商万代南梦宫的预期，实际销量较预期值膨胀数倍不止。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220514/dab7c3d8b339492e8b4e1ff2c07f9c5b.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《艾尔登法环》将年货挑落马下" h="900" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220514/s_dab7c3d8b339492e8b4e1ff2c07f9c5b.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>另外，知名舅舅Jeff Grubb在回复玩家的疑问时指出《星战绝地2》（Star Wars Jedi：Survivor）的档期应在明年2月或3月：</p>
<p align="center"><img alt="《艾尔登法环》将年货挑落马下" h="149" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220514/42ea2847bf3b4fb1b2c69a3f4f331f7e.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="588" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>由于《星空》意外延期，有玩家抱怨今年的XGP未免太冷清了吧，微软是不是该弄两个第三方大作来充数？</p>
<p>Jeff Grubb回应说，CoD与哈利波特游戏与索尼签有推广协议，协议明确规定这两部作品不得入库，留给XGP的选择委实不多，恐怕只能在EA或育碧身上动脑筋，比如年底发售的NFS？</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220514/60231defdb914dda9930809c38908093.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《艾尔登法环》将年货挑落马下" h="843" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220514/s_60231defdb914dda9930809c38908093.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
