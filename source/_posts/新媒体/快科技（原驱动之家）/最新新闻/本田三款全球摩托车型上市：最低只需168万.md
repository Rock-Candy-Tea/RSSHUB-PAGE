<p>近日消息，快科技从本田汽车官方获悉，旗下三款“全球摩托车型”迎来正式上市。</p>
<p>其中<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>Lead125车型的建议零售价为1.68万元；CM300车型的建议零售价为3.45万元；CBR400R车型的建议零售价为4.5万元。</strong></span></p>

<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220916/3734ec1c3f7b4705a77dcbfec42c084e.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="本田三款全球摩托车型上市：最低只需1.68万" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220916/s_3734ec1c3f7b4705a77dcbfec42c084e.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p><strong>Lead125</strong>在三款车型中售价最便宜的车型，其采用了踏板结构，设计思路较为简约，在前脸细节处融入了镀铬设计元素，可以提升车辆的精致感。</p>
<p>配置层面，新车将会搭载Honda eSP+四气门水冷发动机，还集成I-DLING STOP怠速启停以及ACG静音启动功能。</p>
<p>此外，Honda SMART Key智能钥匙、前置油箱，多功能仪表、37L大容量行李箱等也没有缺席。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220916/3d965bd62c674184b119bfc754e77433.png" target="_blank"><img alt="本田三款全球摩托车型上市：最低只需1.68万" h="418" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220916/s_3d965bd62c674184b119bfc754e77433.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220916/a722217f20904f29a55b5cda6ae689d8.png" target="_blank"><img alt="本田三款全球摩托车型上市：最低只需1.68万" h="388" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220916/s_a722217f20904f29a55b5cda6ae689d8.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p><strong>CM300</strong>车型在中国市场非常热门，除了造型复古之外，此次上新的新车拥有多款全新配色，比如牛油果绿、摩登灰等，从而彰显了更强的个性化定位。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220916/7949593760914afe84c044b9e6946f2d.png" target="_blank"><img alt="本田三款全球摩托车型上市：最低只需1.68万" h="396" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220916/s_7949593760914afe84c044b9e6946f2d.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220916/ba2fda587d3e4f5884752b5c31461345.png" target="_blank"><img alt="本田三款全球摩托车型上市：最低只需1.68万" h="396" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220916/s_ba2fda587d3e4f5884752b5c31461345.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220916/c323a3fd607843d8a7e5dac3c3e052bf.png" target="_blank"><img alt="本田三款全球摩托车型上市：最低只需1.68万" h="390" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220916/s_c323a3fd607843d8a7e5dac3c3e052bf.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>值得一提的是，该车690mm的座高也是它比较具备吸引力的一点，对女不少女性驾驶员而言也十分友好。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220916/f304a77c7de9462ba74ca4ff05c66cff.png" target="_blank"><img alt="本田三款全球摩托车型上市：最低只需1.68万" h="397" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220916/s_f304a77c7de9462ba74ca4ff05c66cff.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p><strong>CBR400</strong>则是全新力作，它采用了全包围车身设计，优化空气动力学表现，同时还集成了PGM-FI(Programmed Fuel Injection程序控制燃料喷射系统)程序设定的优化及直流式进气系统，加速更为灵敏。</p>
<p>该车上市后将与雅马哈R3、川崎Ninja400以及KTM RC390直面竞争，它的到来则为中排量放赛车市场诸如了全新的血液。</p>
