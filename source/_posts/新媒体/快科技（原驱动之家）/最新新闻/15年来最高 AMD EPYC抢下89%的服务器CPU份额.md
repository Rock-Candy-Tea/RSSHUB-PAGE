<p class="MsoNormal">自从<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>推出锐龙<span lang="EN-US">/</span>霄龙之后，<span lang="EN-US">Zen</span>架构<span lang="EN-US">x86</span>处理器终于可以让<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>有了翻身的希望，在桌面、服务器、移动等市场上收复失地，今年<span lang="EN-US">Q1</span>季度最大的突破则是服务器<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>市场，创下了<span lang="EN-US">15</span>年来新高。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据市场调研公司公布的<span lang="EN-US">Q1</span>季度报告，公布了<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>、<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>在不同领域的<span lang="EN-US">x86</span>处理器份额，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的整体份额从<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">Q1</span>季度的<span lang="EN-US">14.8%</span>提升到了<span lang="EN-US">20.7%</span>，拿到了五分之一的天下。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">具体来看，桌面份额从去年的<span lang="EN-US">19.2%</span>略微提升到<span lang="EN-US">19.3%</span>，笔记本处理器份额反而从<span lang="EN-US">19%</span>降低到了<span lang="EN-US">18%</span>，被<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>夺回去<span lang="EN-US">1</span>个百分点，这可能跟<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>缺货有关。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不过<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>收获最大的是服务器<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>市场，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>从<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">Q1</span>的<span lang="EN-US">5.1%</span>增长到<span lang="EN-US">8.9%</span>，要知道去年<span lang="EN-US">Q4</span>季度<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的服务器<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>份额也不过<span lang="EN-US">7.1%</span>，<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年底的时候才<span lang="EN-US">4.5%</span>，<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年底的时候只有<span lang="EN-US">0.8%</span>。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">总结来说，虽然大家喊<span lang="EN-US">AMD Yes</span>主要是在锐龙这边，但<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>这三年来最大的奇迹实际上是在服务器<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>市场上，份额增长了<span lang="EN-US">10</span>倍，几乎从零开始收复失地，而且这是利润最丰厚的市场。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">8.9%</span>的份额也是<span lang="EN-US">2006</span>年之后<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的最高水平了，不过这还不是巅峰，之前靠着皓龙处理器，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>只用了<span lang="EN-US">18</span>个月，在服务器<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>市场上的份额就从<span lang="EN-US">5%</span>提升到<span lang="EN-US">22%</span>，这也是<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>近期的目标——恢复皓龙时期的荣耀。</p>
