<p>今日下午，realme举行新品发布会，正式发布realme Q系列新成员——realme Q5 Pro。</p>
<p>ID设计上，realme Q5 Pro拥有夏日燃情、雪地漂移、魅影三款配色，同时，首次将具有强烈视觉冲击力和充满时尚感的棋盘格设计运用到realme Q5 Pro上。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220420/ab275e48c40c4ee18825c1e30831304e.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="骁龙870+80W闪充千元机皇！realme Q5 Pro发布：首发1799元起" h="163" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220420/s_ab275e48c40c4ee18825c1e30831304e.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p><strong>realme Q5 Pro正面配备一块6.62英寸AMOLED三星E4材料屏，分辨率为2400*1080，支持最高120Hz高刷，</strong>拥有720Hz超高触控报点率、1000Hz电竞操控引擎、16000级调光、100% P3广色域、106% NTSC色彩饱和度。支持夜光屏、护眼模式、超清视效等功能。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220420/d2eebd6931124b0bbc01e9ff7c188f15.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="骁龙870+80W闪充千元机皇！realme Q5 Pro发布：首发1799元起" h="163" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220420/s_d2eebd6931124b0bbc01e9ff7c188f15.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220420/c94bceea7f7d4dd68405f89b94569792.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="骁龙870+80W闪充千元机皇！realme Q5 Pro发布：首发1799元起" h="163" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220420/s_c94bceea7f7d4dd68405f89b94569792.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>核心性能上，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>realme Q5 Pro搭载目前安卓阵营备受好评的骁龙870处理器，</strong></span>最大支持8GB+5GB运存拓展。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220420/7e83d52acedb47d9aa651c0e990f2052.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="骁龙870+80W闪充千元机皇！realme Q5 Pro发布：首发1799元起" h="163" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220420/s_7e83d52acedb47d9aa651c0e990f2052.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>值得一提的，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>realme Q5 Pro还支持80W超速闪充，仅需33分钟即可充满5000mAh大电池。</strong></span>配合双电芯设计，双电芯串联技术，充电效率更高，放电更加安全。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220420/088d60afb20a4ac8bc8b53c6ddbd0ffd.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="骁龙870+80W闪充千元机皇！realme Q5 Pro发布：首发1799元起" h="163" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220420/s_088d60afb20a4ac8bc8b53c6ddbd0ffd.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>为了带来更出色的性能体验，realme Q5 Pro首次采用GT系列同款金刚石冰芯散热系统，拥有高达4129mm²不锈钢VC面积。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220420/3937632161c246b593de77c304fbd769.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="骁龙870+80W闪充千元机皇！realme Q5 Pro发布：首发1799元起" h="163" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220420/s_3937632161c246b593de77c304fbd769.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>影像方面，<strong>新机搭载6400万像素广角三摄旗舰级影像，</strong>前摄1600万像素广角镜头，可满足全场景拍摄需求。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220420/f01f8fc4fdea4d75ae3dbc50bc9b24a9.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="骁龙870+80W闪充千元机皇！realme Q5 Pro发布：首发1799元起" h="331" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220420/s_f01f8fc4fdea4d75ae3dbc50bc9b24a9.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>据介绍，realme Q5 Pro还配备了X轴Tactile线性马达，并搭载杜比全景声双扬声器。</p>
<p>体验部分，realme Q5 Pro搭载realme UI 3.0，支持全新自由浮窗、照片、视频分享的信息脱敏等功能，支持AI畅速引擎。</p>
<p>售价方面，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>realme Q5 Pro 6GB+128GB首发价1799元、8GB+128GB首发价1999元，8GB+256GB首发价2199元，</strong></span>将于4月27日0点正式开售。</p>
