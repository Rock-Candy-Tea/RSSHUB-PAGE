<div>   
<p>知名科技主播Austin Evans拆解了新近发布的CFI-1200型PS5，证实该机器不仅能为索尼节省物料成本，在电源效率方面也有长足进步（由左至右分别为首发1000型，去年面市的1100型，以及上月在澳洲悄然上市的1200型）：</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220907/c08b6ef271444fc8a89e844c120fc4b5.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="新款PS5拆解" h="383" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220907/s_c08b6ef271444fc8a89e844c120fc4b5.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>通过风扇选型是PS5玩家独占知识点——1200的扇叶造型确实有变化，但1200是否继续混用不同厂牌的风扇还很难说：</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220907/6590b73ed6ab4a89a376d7a9f81a4723.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="新款PS5拆解" h="313" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220907/s_6590b73ed6ab4a89a376d7a9f81a4723.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>散热窗测温：</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220907/3c553165559a4962981ff205ac81fbd1.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="新款PS5拆解" h="791" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220907/s_3c553165559a4962981ff205ac81fbd1.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220907/1031ad2a86c3498ba73513b23e480cb9.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="新款PS5拆解" h="777" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220907/s_1031ad2a86c3498ba73513b23e480cb9.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220907/fb9bb63ce5f5457a830d7188de8d71db.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="新款PS5拆解" h="806" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220907/s_fb9bb63ce5f5457a830d7188de8d71db.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>内部布局：</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220907/3e3dcf88d2b8415588c64a0a64c4b990.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="新款PS5拆解" h="287" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220907/s_3e3dcf88d2b8415588c64a0a64c4b990.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220907/96f8ac5416d04c399e179a8ffbf8c7b9.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="新款PS5拆解" h="283" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220907/s_96f8ac5416d04c399e179a8ffbf8c7b9.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>1200型重新设计了主板，不仅面积更小（成本更低），还增设一条热管为主处理器之外的元件散热（诟病许久的高烧内存？）：</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220907/504c3a1c30c04b98b877439f29524ef6.jpg" style="text-align: center;" target="_blank"><img alt="新款PS5拆解" h="272" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220907/s_504c3a1c30c04b98b877439f29524ef6.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220907/af3323e68de3433f9f1d1bfeb342ce70.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="新款PS5拆解" h="306" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220907/s_af3323e68de3433f9f1d1bfeb342ce70.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>物料成本最高的当然是配有大面积均热板的1000型，1100及1200在这方面均有不同程度的缩水，同时也带来明显的重量差异：</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220907/6c612172b9bf413d9aa9045df711ef54.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="新款PS5拆解" h="311" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220907/s_6c612172b9bf413d9aa9045df711ef54.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>1200型最大的进步在于功率消耗降至202瓦（1000型为218瓦，1100型为229瓦）：</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220907/502a7a5a467846f5855d116e04c404e5.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="新款PS5拆解" h="460" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220907/s_502a7a5a467846f5855d116e04c404e5.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>根据该视频提供的信息，1200型最大的缺点是CMOS电池深埋在系统内部，如果将来某一天要换电池，必须把主机大卸八块；1100型在这方面显然更人性化，方便玩家更换电池：</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220907/34f51f768fab439eb5a83a8fa9ce8a18.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="新款PS5拆解" h="415" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220907/s_34f51f768fab439eb5a83a8fa9ce8a18.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220907/3b4486573ba64637b3132eaa38848f23.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="新款PS5拆解" h="415" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220907/s_3b4486573ba64637b3132eaa38848f23.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>现在还不清楚主处理器在步进甚至制程方面是否有变化，只能等待主播作进一步拆解。</p>
