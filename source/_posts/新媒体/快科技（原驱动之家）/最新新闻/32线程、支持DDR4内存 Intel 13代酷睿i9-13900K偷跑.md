<p class="MsoNormal">前几天的<span lang="EN-US">CES</span>展会上，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>补全了<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Alder Lake</span>的产品线，并透露下一代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Raptor Lake</span>处理器已经出样了，没想到这两天就有<span lang="EN-US">13</span>代酷睿偷跑的消息了，最高<span lang="EN-US">24</span>核<span lang="EN-US">32</span>线程，依然支持<span lang="EN-US">DDR4</span>内存，但<span lang="EN-US">AVX512</span>还是会砍掉。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">网友已经在<span lang="EN-US">Intel GFX CI</span>库中发现了这颗神秘的处理器，<strong>不出意外地话就是下代旗舰酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i9-13900K</span>了，可支持<span lang="EN-US">32</span>个线程，基本坐实了之前泄露的<span lang="EN-US">8</span>大<span lang="EN-US">16</span>小核心的架构，</strong>总计<span lang="EN-US">24</span>核<span lang="EN-US">32</span>线程（<span lang="EN-US">8</span>大核支持超线程）。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">目前的样品频率只有<span lang="EN-US">1.8GHz</span>，这个显然是原型阶段的测试水平，考虑到<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿都可以做到<span lang="EN-US">5.5GHz</span>最高频率了，<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿优化之后超过<span lang="EN-US">5.5GHz</span>应该没问题。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">内存方面，测试用的还是<span lang="EN-US">DDR4</span>，<strong>显然<span lang="EN-US">13</span>代酷睿的时候<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>也会选择同时兼容<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>及<span lang="EN-US">DDR4</span>两种内存，</strong>毕竟今年底的话<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>也不会普及。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">还有一个关注点就是<span lang="EN-US">AVX512</span>指令集，<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿中<span lang="EN-US">AVX512</span>指令集被<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>及主板厂商通过升级<span lang="EN-US">BIOS</span>的方式禁用了，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">13</span>代酷睿现在的信息来看也是不支持的，</strong></span>这也没什么意外的。</p>
