<p class="MsoNormal">微软下个月就要发布<span lang="EN-US">Win11</span>系统正式版了，一大波系统都会升级，配套驱动也需要随之升级。今天<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>发布了锐龙芯片组最新版驱动<span lang="EN-US">3.09.01.140</span>版，旗下的多个芯片组正式支持<span lang="EN-US">Win11</span>系统。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD Ryzen Chipset</span>主板芯片组驱动，内含芯片组驱动和<span lang="EN-US">AMD Ryzen</span>电源管理程序（需要<span lang="EN-US">UEFI CPPC2 in Windows 10 May 2019 Update</span>）。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>新驱动版本号为<span lang="EN-US">3.09.01.140</span>，主要添加了对<span lang="EN-US">Windows 11</span>系统的支持。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">支持的芯片组型号如下：</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US"> - AMD A320 Chipset</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US"> - AMD B350 Chipset</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US"> - AMD X370 Chipset</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US"> - AMD X399 Chipset</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US"> - AMD B450 Chipset</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US"> - AMD X470 Chipset</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US"> - AMD A520 Chipset</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US"> - AMD B550 Chipset</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US"> - AMD X570 Chipset</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US"> - AMD TRX40 Chipset</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US"> - AMD WRX80 Chipset</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">支持的处理器型号如下：</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US"> - AMD Ryzen Threadripper PRO Processors</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US"> - AMD Ryzen 5000</span>系列<span lang="EN-US">Desktop Processor</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US"> - </span>第一代<span lang="EN-US">AMD Ryzen Threadripper Processor</span>（不支持<span lang="EN-US">Win11</span>）</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US"> - </span>第二、三代<span lang="EN-US">AMD Ryzen Threadripper Processor</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US"> - </span>第一代<span lang="EN-US">AMD Ryzen Desktop Processor</span>（不支持<span lang="EN-US">Win11</span>）</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US"> - </span>第二、三代<span lang="EN-US">AMD Ryzen Desktop Processor</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US"> - </span>第七代<span lang="EN-US">AMD A-Series Processor</span>（不支持<span lang="EN-US">Win11</span>）</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US"> - AMD Ryzen 3000G/4000G/5000G Series Desktop Processors with Radeon Graphics</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US"> - AMD Ryzen 2000G Series Desktop Processors with Radeon Graphics</span>（不支持<span lang="EN-US">Win11</span>）</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US"> - AMD Ryzen Mobile 3000U/C, 4000U/H, 5000U/H/HS Series Processors with Radeon Graphics</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US"> - AMD Ryzen Mobile 2000U Series Processors with Radeon Graphics</span>（不支持<span lang="EN-US">Win11</span>）</p>
