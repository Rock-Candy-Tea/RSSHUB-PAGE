<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">今年底，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>会推出集成<span lang="EN-US">128MB</span>缓存的<span lang="EN-US">3D V-Cache</span>增强版<span lang="EN-US">Zen3</span>处理器，主要用于应付<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Alder Lake</span>。明年<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>还会有全新的<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>处理器，现在就要出样了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">来自消息人士的爆料称，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>台积电今年<span lang="EN-US">Q1</span>季度就成立了项目组，用于生产首批<span lang="EN-US">AMD Zen4</span>处理器<span lang="EN-US">Genoa</span>样品，桌面版的处理器<span lang="EN-US">Raphael</span>则是在<span lang="EN-US">Q2</span>季度早期。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">从种种迹象来看，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>处理器应该很快会有样品测试了，考虑到这是明年下半年发布的产品，流片、测试再到最后的上市通常需要<span lang="EN-US">6-12</span>个月，时间点是差不多了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>公布的规划，<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>架构会是全新研发的，支持<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>、<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>，同时升级新的平台，桌面由<span lang="EN-US">AM4</span>升级到<span lang="EN-US">AM5</span>，服务器平台也会升级到<span lang="EN-US">SP5</span>。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="96核192线程 AMD Zen4处理器即将出样：升级5nm工艺" h="318" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210929/6cd76ba8-192e-448e-bdd6-68b3488f5e0e.png" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>会首先用于<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的<span lang="EN-US">EPYC</span>服务器产品线，代号<span lang="EN-US">Genoa</span>，<strong>早前泄露的信息基本上确认是会升级<span lang="EN-US">12</span>组<span lang="EN-US">CCD</span>，每组<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个核心，那就是总计<span lang="EN-US">96</span>个核心、<span lang="EN-US">192</span>个线程，比现在增加<span lang="EN-US">50</span>％。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">功耗方面，顶级型号默认<span lang="EN-US">TDP</span>为<span lang="EN-US">320W</span>，最高可达<span lang="EN-US">400W</span>，还可以在<span lang="EN-US">1ms</span>的瞬间达到恐怖的<span lang="EN-US">700W</span>，而现在的<span lang="EN-US">EPYC</span>处理器<span lang="EN-US">TDP</span>是<span lang="EN-US">280W</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">当然，桌面版的<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>处理器核心数应该不会升级，主流市场依然最多<span lang="EN-US">16</span>核<span lang="EN-US">32</span>线程，线程撕裂者系列倒是不好说，因为<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>明年也会升级酷睿至尊版，<span lang="EN-US">10nm</span>蓝宝石激流系列最多可以做到<span lang="EN-US">40</span>核<span lang="EN-US">80</span>线程，线程撕裂者系列或许会从最多<span lang="EN-US">64</span>核提升到<span lang="EN-US">96</span>核呢。</p>
