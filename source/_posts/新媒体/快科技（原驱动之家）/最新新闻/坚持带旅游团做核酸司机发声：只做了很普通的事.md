<p class="MsoNormal">日前宁夏司机带旅游团队做核酸一事引发热议，网友<span style="background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial; color: rgb(51, 51, 51);">赞其</span><span lang="EN-US" style="background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial; font-family: "Open Sans", sans-serif; color: rgb(51, 51, 51);">“</span><span style="background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial; color: rgb(51, 51, 51);">救了一座城</span><span lang="EN-US" style="background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial; font-family: "Open Sans", sans-serif; color: rgb(51, 51, 51);">”“</span><span style="background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial; color: rgb(51, 51, 51);">阻断了一条传播链</span><span lang="EN-US" style="background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial; font-family: "Open Sans", sans-serif; color: rgb(51, 51, 51);">”</span><span style="background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial; color: rgb(51, 51, 51);">。</span>现在这名司机首次对外发声，称自己只是做了很普通的事。</p>
<p class="newsinfo_vedio"><a class="mydrivers_player" href="https://weibo.com/2656274875/KDMA56sWb">视频</a></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">据央视新闻报道，此前宁夏吴忠本轮疫情累计通报新冠确诊<span lang="EN-US">5</span>例，其中有<span lang="EN-US">4</span>例来自同一个旅行团。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">按计划，该旅行团计划<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日在宁夏吴忠区就餐，次日乘高铁返回浙江。包车宁<span lang="EN-US">AE6865</span>的司机董文军在关注到近段时间内蒙、甘肃张掖等地的疫情动态后，坚持先带大家进行核酸检测。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">这个司机名叫董文军，日前他接受了媒体采访，<strong>称“我并没有想到会有这么高的关注度，我只是做了一件很普通的事。”</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">董文军表示，<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，我开着宁<span lang="EN-US">AE6865</span>号牌的商务车在河东机场接待杭州许某一行<span lang="EN-US">10</span>人的旅行团。接下来的<span lang="EN-US">2</span>天时间，我带着他们游玩了宁夏的水洞沟、沙湖、贺兰山岩画等景区。旅客们在出行前未做核酸检测，每到一个景区都出示行程码和健康码。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">14</span>日至<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，我拉着游客到内蒙古额济纳旗旅游。一路上，我作为司机并未与客人共同就餐，只是将客人从景区拉到酒店，再从酒店拉至餐厅。<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日下午，客人们根据网上大众点评，选择了在额济纳旗桐楠阁家常菜馆用餐。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">按照计划，我们的计划是下一站去张掖。<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>启程时，我一看抖音上有一对老夫妇已经有感染了，他去过额济纳旗、张掖，我就给客人说，我说大家可能走不了了，咱们首先要换一个行程，因为有夫妇已经感染了。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">我提议后，得到了一些游客的响应，当晚我们直接来到武威住宿。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，我带游客到中卫<span lang="EN-US">66</span>号公路。当时觉得<span lang="EN-US">66</span>号公路人应该不多，没有太大问题。从<span lang="EN-US">66</span>号公路出来以后，客人们打算去吴忠市区吃饭。我心里就想着额济纳旗是不是疫情严重了，我就领他们往吴忠市人民医院走，告诉大家最好做核酸检测。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">董文军提到，当时他提议做核酸的时候，<strong>大家没有反对，都很配合。</strong>“因为我跟他们说，回家之前最好做个核酸检测，这样能对得起家人，也对得起自己。”</p>
