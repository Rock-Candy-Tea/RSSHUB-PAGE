<p><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: Arial, "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", sans-serif;">近日，马斯克被爆料性骚扰空姐，Space X支付250000美元封口费一事引起热议，马斯克认为这是对他的蓄意攻击。</span><br style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: Arial, "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", sans-serif;">
<br style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: Arial, "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", sans-serif;">
<span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: Arial, "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", sans-serif;">一向立场反特斯拉的《Business Insider》撰文称，一位在马斯克私人飞机工作的空姐在得到这份工作后，被鼓励获得按摩师执照，以便她可以给马斯克按摩。</span><br style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: Arial, "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", sans-serif;">
<br style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: Arial, "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", sans-serif;">
<span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: Arial, "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", sans-serif;">她告诉一位朋友，在马斯克湾流G650ER的私人机舱里进行一次按摩时，马斯克向她提议了不可描述的事情。</span><br style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: Arial, "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", sans-serif;">
<br style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: Arial, "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", sans-serif;">
<span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: Arial, "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", sans-serif;">按照这位朋友的描述，这事情发生在2016年一次飞往伦敦的航程上，马斯克暴露了不可描述的东西，并问她是否可以做更多，并可以给她买匹马。</span><br style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: Arial, "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", sans-serif;">
<br style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: Arial, "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", sans-serif;">
<span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: Arial, "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", sans-serif;">2018年，这位空姐离开了这个工作岗位。</span><br style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: Arial, "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", sans-serif;">
<br style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: Arial, "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", sans-serif;">
<span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: Arial, "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", sans-serif;">在与马斯克亲自参加的调解员会面后，随从的投诉迅速得到解决。此事从未提交过法院或仲裁程序。<strong>2018年11月，马斯克、SpaceX和空姐签订了遣散协议，给予服务员25万美元的付款，以换取不就索赔提起诉讼的承诺。</strong></span><br style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: Arial, "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", sans-serif;">
<br style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: Arial, "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", sans-serif;">
<span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: Arial, "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", sans-serif;">据Insider报道，他们联系了马斯克征求意见，马斯克通过电子邮件要求更多时间来进行回复，并表示“这个故事还有很多”。</span><br style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: Arial, "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", sans-serif;">
<br style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: Arial, "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", sans-serif;">
<span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: Arial, "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", sans-serif;">马斯克写道，如果我倾向于进行性骚扰，这不太可能是我整个30年的职业生涯中第一次曝光，并称这个故事是“出于政治动机的热门作品”。</span><br style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: Arial, "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", sans-serif;">
<br style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: Arial, "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", sans-serif;">
<span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: Arial, "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", sans-serif;">Insider延长了截止日期，并重复了马斯克有发表评论的权力，但马斯克没有回应。</span><br style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: Arial, "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", sans-serif;">
<br style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: Arial, "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", sans-serif;">
<span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: Arial, "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei", "WenQuanYi Micro Hei", sans-serif;">SpaceX法律副总裁Christopher Cardaci通过手机回复说：<strong>“我不会对任何和解协议发表评论。”SpaceX没有回复对其一般媒体联系电子邮件地址的置评请求。</strong></span></p>
