<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">最近几天，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>及<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>都更新了驱动，为<span style="background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial; font-size: 9pt; font-family: 微软雅黑, sans-serif; color: rgb(52, 52, 52);">《狙击精英 <span lang="EN-US">5</span></span>》、《杀手<span lang="EN-US">3</span>》等游戏提供优化，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>现在也推出了<span lang="EN-US">Game On Driver 30.0.101.1994</span>版显卡驱动，这也是一版游戏优化驱动，同时还修复了<span lang="EN-US">Xe</span>显卡在《彩虹六号》等游戏中的崩溃、重启<span lang="EN-US">bug</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel Game On Driver 30.0.101.1994</span>版显卡驱动发布，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>主要为刚刚上市的《狙击精英<span lang="EN-US"> 5</span>》游戏提供优化，支持<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>第<span lang="EN-US">11</span>代酷睿处理器<span lang="EN-US">Xe</span>及更新型号显卡。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">同时，新版驱动还修复了如下<span lang="EN-US">Bug</span>：</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">1</span>、<span lang="EN-US">Xe  Discrete</span>显卡：《星际争霸<span lang="EN-US">2</span>：自由之翼》游戏中可能会出现纹理闪烁。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2</span>、第<span lang="EN-US">11</span>和<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿处理器：《彩虹六号：异种》在<span lang="EN-US">Vulkan</span>模式下，选择中等或更高画质时可能会出现游戏崩溃或系统重启。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">3</span>、第<span lang="EN-US">11</span>和<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿处理器：《怪物猎人：崛起》启动时可能需要着色器重编译。</p>
