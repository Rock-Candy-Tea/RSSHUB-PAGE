<p class="MsoNormal"><a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/776/776975.htm" target="_blank">刚刚<span lang="EN-US">iQOO</span>发布了最新的旗舰<span lang="EN-US">iQOO 8</span>系列</a>，现在价格也公布了，起价<span lang="EN-US">3799</span>元，顶配<span lang="EN-US">5699</span>元。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">具体来说，<span lang="EN-US">iQOO 8</span>价格如下：</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">8GB+128GB</span>，<span lang="EN-US">3799</span>元</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">12GB+256GB</span>，<span lang="EN-US">4199</span>元（预售期限时优惠<span lang="EN-US">100</span>元，到手<span lang="EN-US">4099</span>元）</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">该款手机<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日正式开售。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">iQOO 8 Pro</span>价格如下： </p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">8GB+256GB</span>，<span lang="EN-US">4999</span>元</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">12GB+256GB</span>，<span lang="EN-US">5499</span>元</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">12GB+512GB</span>，<span lang="EN-US">5999</span>元</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">iQOO 8 Pro</span>手机<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日<span lang="EN-US">10</span>：<span lang="EN-US">00</span>正式开售。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此外，全系享有最高<span lang="EN-US">24</span>期分期免息，以旧换新等尊享权益，权益使用规则以权益页面说明为准。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">最后还有一款产品，那就是iQOO 8 Pro这次支持了50W无线充电，<strong>iQOO也推出了立式风冷充无线电底座仅售329元，8.26当日限时279元！</strong></p>
