<p>谍照显示，新款3系的外观仅在前脸和尾部有所改动，车身其它部位并未覆盖伪装：</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210911/afa562250bc64e918b08ef02bb0399b2.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马新3系小改款" h="406" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210911/s_afa562250bc64e918b08ef02bb0399b2.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210911/e26f3ffbb5434f4985426751c4237879.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马新3系小改款" h="405" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210911/s_e26f3ffbb5434f4985426751c4237879.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>新3系将使用新设计的头灯并重新设计车尾部分，灯组轮廓更为锐利，车尾保险杠部分可能与现款不同：</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210911/933b24d5805c4c608fc745ec41c90669.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马新3系小改款" h="403" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210911/s_933b24d5805c4c608fc745ec41c90669.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210911/538b6a4d2c114df68bfca06ecea9d161.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马新3系小改款" h="404" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210911/s_538b6a4d2c114df68bfca06ecea9d161.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210911/1f145d3e1fcf4f3582845361d00acb50.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马新3系小改款" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210911/s_1f145d3e1fcf4f3582845361d00acb50.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>进气格栅可能也有微小变化，所幸不会长成像新M3那样。谍照摄影师声称车内仪表板顺应潮流做了更新，外界普遍猜测宝马会由上至下逐步将各车系车机屏幕更新为曲面屏，向电车内饰靠拢。</p>
<p>另外，慕尼黑车展上的“宝马世界”还展出了X5 VR6级别要人保护防弹车：</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210911/02c797c729f54665b5b0e0613b784821.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马新3系小改款" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210911/s_02c797c729f54665b5b0e0613b784821.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210911/a17f89bc455f4d73a7a6b40381685434.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马新3系小改款" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210911/s_a17f89bc455f4d73a7a6b40381685434.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210911/f373979028b24b4bb3a4db62b3a34a4b.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马新3系小改款" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210911/s_f373979028b24b4bb3a4db62b3a34a4b.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210911/55ef70dbfe4c4767aa4dca06251c9703.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马新3系小改款" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210911/s_55ef70dbfe4c4767aa4dca06251c9703.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>VR6级别可防住7.62毫米AK-47军用步枪子弹，底盘可抵御手榴弹袭击，整车可抵御四米外15公斤TNT当量爆炸冲击波。</p>
<p>X5 VR6沿用X5M及X5 M50i惯用的4.4升V8发动机，悬架经特别加强以应付额外的装甲重量。</p>
