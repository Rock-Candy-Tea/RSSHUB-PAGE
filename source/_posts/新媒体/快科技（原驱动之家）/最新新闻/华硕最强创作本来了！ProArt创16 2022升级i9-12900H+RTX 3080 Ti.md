<p>今日，华硕笔记本今日官宣ProArt创16 2022新品上市，该本采用酷睿i9-12900H+RTX 3080 Ti的强势组合，将于今晚20:00进行新品开箱全网直播。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220721/c62cf4f8fb934fe8ab0b1eca0e9e1cc0.png" target="_blank"><img alt="华硕最强创作本来了！ProArt创16 2022升级i9-12900H+RTX 3080 Ti" h="420" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220721/s_c62cf4f8fb934fe8ab0b1eca0e9e1cc0.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>核心方面，ProArt创16 2022搭载了目前Intel最强的<strong>i9-12900H处理器</strong>，CPU性能释放可达90W，14核心20线程、24MBL3缓存，最高睿频达5.0GHz。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220721/11dabec1f6a74211b64a3a8ab46cfc36.png" target="_blank"><img alt="华硕最强创作本来了！ProArt创16 2022升级i9-12900H+RTX 3080 Ti" h="531" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220721/s_11dabec1f6a74211b64a3a8ab46cfc36.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="544" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>不仅如此，ProArt创16 2022显卡最高可选<strong>RTX 3080 Ti</strong>，拥有6G GDDR6显存，7424 CUDA核心，频率为1395MHz，TDP最大175W，比RTX3080还高10W。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220721/bba2b76776ef4ab2a59797d50201f58e.png" target="_blank"><img alt="华硕最强创作本来了！ProArt创16 2022升级i9-12900H+RTX 3080 Ti" h="525" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220721/s_bba2b76776ef4ab2a59797d50201f58e.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="549" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>作为创作本，屏幕的重要性不然而喻，特别是平面设计、影视后期对屏幕的要求更高，新款ProArt创16 2022可选<strong>4K OLED屏</strong>，0.2s响应，550nit高亮度，100万:1对比度，DisplayHDR True Black 500认证，DeltaE≈1.5，还有低蓝光护眼认证、DC调光。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220721/6fe0f1b9aef04cf7a89ca7228f5fecb1.png" target="_blank"><img alt="华硕最强创作本来了！ProArt创16 2022升级i9-12900H+RTX 3080 Ti" h="621" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220721/s_6fe0f1b9aef04cf7a89ca7228f5fecb1.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="560" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>散热方面，该机采用华硕冰川Pro散热系统，双风扇六热管，液态金属导热，整机性能释放可达140W。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220721/67a0df7257124430b025da1f5f65155d.png" target="_blank"><img alt="华硕最强创作本来了！ProArt创16 2022升级i9-12900H+RTX 3080 Ti" h="705" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220721/s_67a0df7257124430b025da1f5f65155d.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="553" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>此外，ProArt创16 2022的<strong>独创的华硕旋钮</strong>是该机亮点，拥有超过70个功能，适配Adobe全家桶，提高创作者的工作效率。</p>
<p>售价方面，<strong>i9-12900H+RTX 3080 Ti+4K屏高配版本售价<span style="color:#ff0000;">29999</span>元。</strong></p>
