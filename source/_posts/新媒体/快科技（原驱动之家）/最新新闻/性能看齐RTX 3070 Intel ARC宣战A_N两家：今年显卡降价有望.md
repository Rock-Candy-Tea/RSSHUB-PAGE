<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年已经过去了，<span lang="EN-US">PC</span>用户感受最深的大概就是去年显卡的大涨价了，不论是<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的<span lang="EN-US">RX 6000</span>还是<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>的<span lang="EN-US">RTX 30</span>系列，价格都翻了一倍，光追游戏卡的门槛提升到<span lang="EN-US">3000</span>元以上，高端卡没有<span lang="EN-US">5000-6000</span>预算是拿不下来的。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年显卡市场会如何呢？相信大家最期待的是显卡价格恢复正常，这个事完全指望<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>及<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>两家是不行了，国外科技媒体<span lang="EN-US">Digitaltrends</span>把希望放在了<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>首款高性能独显<span lang="EN-US">ARC </span>“<span lang="EN-US">Alchemist”</span>系列上。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">它就是之前的<span lang="EN-US">DG2</span>独显，现在品牌定为<span lang="EN-US">ARC</span>，<strong>“<span lang="EN-US">Alchemist”</span>炼金术师是第一代产品，基于<span lang="EN-US">Xe HPG</span>架构，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>官方确认支持光线追踪及<span lang="EN-US">XeSS</span>技术，后者是类似<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA DLSS</span>的游戏技术，性能号称提升<span lang="EN-US">2</span>倍多。</strong></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="性能看齐RTX 3070 Intel ARC宣战A/N两家：今年显卡降价有望" h="293" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220101/08dd5c96-ce7e-4f24-9eec-16c22f7c7660.png" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>没有公布过首款<span lang="EN-US">ARC</span>显卡的具体性能，不过之前的爆料显示，该系列主打桌面和笔记本游戏市场，包含至少三种核心、六款型号，最高端的有<span lang="EN-US">512</span>个执行单元、<span lang="EN-US">4096</span>个核心，搭配<span lang="EN-US">256-bit 16GB GDDR6</span>显存，而最低端的只有<span lang="EN-US">96</span>个执行单元、<span lang="EN-US">768</span>个核心，仅搭配<span lang="EN-US">64-bit 4GB GDDR6</span>显存。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">性能方面，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">512</span>单元、<span lang="EN-US">16GB</span>的<span lang="EN-US">ARC</span>高端显卡传闻是对标<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>的<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3070</span>甚至<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3070Ti</span>，定位就比较高端了。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">至于价格，现在自然没有可信说法，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>之前强调他们的显卡并不会走低价路线，不过大家也不用预期价格太高，感觉会介于<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>及<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>之间，毕竟他们还是新来的。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年显卡市场最大的变数就是<span lang="EN-US">Intel ARC</span>显卡的加入，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>对<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>及<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>宣战会提升市场竞争，如果<span lang="EN-US">ARC</span>显卡性能如传闻那样很给力，逼迫<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>及<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>降价就有希望了。</p>
