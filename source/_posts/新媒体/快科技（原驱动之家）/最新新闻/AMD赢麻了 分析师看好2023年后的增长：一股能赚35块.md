<p class="MsoNormal">在<span lang="EN-US">x86 CPU</span>市场上，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>这三年来一直顺风顺水，不论是桌面、笔记本上的锐龙，还是服务器上的霄龙，份额一直在提升，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的业绩也有了明显改善。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">放眼未来两年，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的增长确定性还是很高，而且表现更超出预期，高盛分析师日前将<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的目标股价从<span lang="EN-US">89</span>美元上调到<span lang="EN-US">111</span>美元，至少还有<span lang="EN-US">20%</span>以上的增长空间，主要原因就是<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>未来几年中营收及利润率都会不断提高。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据他的测算，<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的<span lang="EN-US">EPS</span>每股盈利约为<span lang="EN-US">3.81</span>美元，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>到了<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年这一数字将提升到<span lang="EN-US">5.48</span>美元（约合<span lang="EN-US">35</span>元），比市场预期的<span lang="EN-US">EPS</span>指标分别高出了<span lang="EN-US">41%</span>、<span lang="EN-US">64%</span>，显示出高盛对<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>要比其他金融公司要乐观很多。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的信息，<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>财年营业额为<span lang="EN-US">97.6</span>亿美元，经营收入<span lang="EN-US">13.7</span>亿美元，净收入<span lang="EN-US">24.9</span>亿美元，<span lang="EN-US">EPS</span>每股收益<span lang="EN-US">2.06</span>美元。非<span lang="EN-US">GAAP</span>经营收入<span lang="EN-US">16.6</span>亿美元，净收入<span lang="EN-US">15.8</span>亿美元，摊薄后每股收益<span lang="EN-US">1.29</span>美元。</p>
