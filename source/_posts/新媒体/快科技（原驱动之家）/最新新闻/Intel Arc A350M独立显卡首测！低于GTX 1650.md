<div>   
<p>Intel Arc锐炫独立显卡目前仅限入门级移动端，首发上市产品是<strong>三星的Galaxy Boo Pro2</strong>，仅限韩国市场，所以第一批评测也都来自韩媒。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220410/f33fd3aa-7973-426b-8875-98aa5687ecdb.png" target="_blank"><img alt="Intel Arc A350M独立显卡首测！低于GTX 1650" h="325" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220410/Sf33fd3aa-7973-426b-8875-98aa5687ecdb.png" style="border: black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>该笔记本搭载了Arc A350M，最低端型号，6个Xe核心、6个光追核心，核心频率1150MHz，64-bit 4GB GDDR6显存，功耗范围25-35W。</p>
<p>它之上是Arc A370M，8个核心，频率1550MHz，功耗35-50W。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220410/6b9333832ce345279bef0d0f1fdfb16b.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="Intel Arc A350M独立显卡首测！低于GTX 1650" h="387" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220410/s_6b9333832ce345279bef0d0f1fdfb16b.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>首测的Galaxy Book Pro2还采用了12代酷睿i5-1240P处理器，4大8小12核心16线程，16GB LPDDR5内存，1080p 60Hz AMOLED屏幕，重仅1.17公斤。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220410/97b072c865b54d3a8b0cf76ff2f3fa1e.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="Intel Arc A350M独立显卡首测！低于GTX 1650" h="384" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220410/s_97b072c865b54d3a8b0cf76ff2f3fa1e.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>猜猜处理器和显卡在哪里？</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220410/c00d310926534d2da24a5f484b70c6f9.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="Intel Arc A350M独立显卡首测！低于GTX 1650" h="370" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220410/s_c00d310926534d2da24a5f484b70c6f9.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p><strong>左侧是i5-1240P处理器，右侧是Arc A350M显卡。</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220410/e33ba1c406464f01a72635e894ad311a.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="Intel Arc A350M独立显卡首测！低于GTX 1650" h="375" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220410/s_e33ba1c406464f01a72635e894ad311a.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>Arc A350M处理器，旁边是两颗三星显存，单颗2GB。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220410/d259e53a4a5d47c2ad2474b6b78a4ac5.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="Intel Arc A350M独立显卡首测！低于GTX 1650" h="381" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220410/s_d259e53a4a5d47c2ad2474b6b78a4ac5.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220410/7cd6edc3be8845808b56dc47e01398eb.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="Intel Arc A350M独立显卡首测！低于GTX 1650" h="378" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220410/s_7cd6edc3be8845808b56dc47e01398eb.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>Arc驱动面板内竟然无法显示正确的型号，自带驱动版本也不是最新的。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220410/09967ec93949474fa918d2d4a2184321.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="Intel Arc A350M独立显卡首测！低于GTX 1650" h="307" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220410/s_09967ec93949474fa918d2d4a2184321.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220410/448ca646b06649d8ab0995eff2525c6a.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="Intel Arc A350M独立显卡首测！低于GTX 1650" h="314" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220410/s_448ca646b06649d8ab0995eff2525c6a.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220410/57b2748fb7a34438b1977c672fd4a2ac.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="Intel Arc A350M独立显卡首测！低于GTX 1650" h="378" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220410/s_57b2748fb7a34438b1977c672fd4a2ac.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220410/58dc4677258442f4a829a7f01ba4fa75.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="Intel Arc A350M独立显卡首测！低于GTX 1650" h="384" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220410/s_58dc4677258442f4a829a7f01ba4fa75.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>3DMark测试中，<strong>Arc A350M Time Spy DX12得分在3000分左右，Fire Strike DX11得分则在7100-7400分范围，轻松超越25W MX450</strong>，相信面对最新的MX550也有战力。</p>
<p><strong><span style="color:#ff0000;">对比GTX 1650 50W则还差了很多，不过毕竟功耗释放也差了很多，而按照这样的表现，如果换成GTX 1650 Max-Q版本的话，Arc A350M应该是差不多的档次。</span></strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220410/8351bcd4b2254983808cea2a351f920d.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="Intel Arc A350M独立显卡首测！低于GTX 1650" h="319" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220410/s_8351bcd4b2254983808cea2a351f920d.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>另外，<strong>Arc A系列支持光线追踪，可以顺利完成Port Royal光追测试，得到了200分</strong>，聊胜于无，再配合XeSS超分辨率技术就可以同时获得更好的画质和帧率，只是该技术要到夏初才会开启。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220410/57e6480f7c4a436092c0db5c087b1122.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="Intel Arc A350M独立显卡首测！低于GTX 1650" h="405" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220410/s_57e6480f7c4a436092c0db5c087b1122.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>游戏测试中，使用1080p分辨率和低画质，可以发现<strong>实际运行频率最高可达2.2GHz</strong>，这也符合Intel之前的说法，<a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/824/824650.htm" target="_blank">即标称频率只是个最低平均值，实际会高得多。</a></p>
<p>但是，<strong>GPU频率并不是很稳定，GPU使用率也偏低。</strong></p>
<p><strong><span style="color:#ff0000;">《守望先锋》跑到了70FPS左右，《英雄联盟》超过100FPS，但是《PUBG》等游戏卡顿比较明显，另外有些游戏根本无法运行，包括《极限竞速：地平线5》、《使命召唤：冷战》等。</span></strong></p>
<p>总体来看，Arc A350M标志着Intel游戏独立显卡迈出了第一步，但前边的路还很长……</p>
