<p>之前由PS独占的《死亡搁浅》导演剪辑版将于3月30号登陆PC平台，定价40美元（198RMB）。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220128/f87bcd10ca994abe90f064d6eeb9d5f7.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="《死亡搁浅》导演剪辑版登陆PC" h="333" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220128/s_f87bcd10ca994abe90f064d6eeb9d5f7.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>之前购买了原始版本的玩家可付费升级，升级费用10美元（48RMB，需验证安装）：</p>
<p align="center"><img alt="《死亡搁浅》导演剪辑版登陆PC" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220128/90712850db1c4de2974c6ac5b4ae8c1b.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>导演剪辑版支持高帧率与带鱼屏，以及Intel的XeSS图形技术：</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220128/ac8f94a79d6f4b469526c81fabc15838.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《死亡搁浅》导演剪辑版登陆PC" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220128/s_ac8f94a79d6f4b469526c81fabc15838.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220128/55373cfaca2e46be861694f13d90b65b.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《死亡搁浅》导演剪辑版登陆PC" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220128/s_55373cfaca2e46be861694f13d90b65b.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220128/9bac9c693b28499588dee925303495bb.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《死亡搁浅》导演剪辑版登陆PC" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220128/s_9bac9c693b28499588dee925303495bb.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220128/c886c41d184140cb9f107f60073eb915.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《死亡搁浅》导演剪辑版登陆PC" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220128/s_c886c41d184140cb9f107f60073eb915.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>游戏增设一种新的“序列连拍”游戏内拍照模式，可捕捉到某个场景的连续画面：</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220128/0d5e6d4f441e4427bd08b75cf19fdd07.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《死亡搁浅》导演剪辑版登陆PC" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220128/s_0d5e6d4f441e4427bd08b75cf19fdd07.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220128/42ef4cfeae8e42c3bd7b2988d1d33295.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《死亡搁浅》导演剪辑版登陆PC" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220128/s_42ef4cfeae8e42c3bd7b2988d1d33295.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220128/3cb0e8d54c8c43a5a2293f88171dc4fa.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《死亡搁浅》导演剪辑版登陆PC" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220128/s_3cb0e8d54c8c43a5a2293f88171dc4fa.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220128/bd31eda1a89741c3a87965e7842a053b.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《死亡搁浅》导演剪辑版登陆PC" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220128/s_bd31eda1a89741c3a87965e7842a053b.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>为讨好玩家，游戏还追加了与V社Half-Life及波兰人《赛博朋克2077》相关的联动内容；为凸显诚意，游戏所有版本均附赠由Titan Books编辑发行的《死亡搁浅》原画集节选内容的数字版，背包补丁，力量手套（金/银），以及多种套装。</p>
<p>游戏现在Steam商店及EGS平台以三折新春优惠价促销：</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220128/4754d7143a974ce8b058e4bb7b85c2a1.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《死亡搁浅》导演剪辑版登陆PC" h="516" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220128/s_4754d7143a974ce8b058e4bb7b85c2a1.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>另外，由于PC爹爹对IO Interactive近期发布Steam版《Hitman 3》大加鞭挞，发行商迫于压力决定为Steam用户追加如下福利：</p>
<p>· 2月20号之前买《Hitman 3》标准版的用户，免费获得豪华版内容；<br>
· 2月20号之前买数字豪华版或三部曲的用户，免费升级至七宗罪收藏版。</p>
<p>《Hitman 3》PC版之前由EGS独占一年，独占期结束后移师Steam平台继续以原价发行，不仅不照顾之前买过1/2前作的老玩家，还搞出许多幺蛾子，什么标准、豪华、七宗罪，三部曲，三部曲豪华附加套……令人眼花缭乱目不暇接：</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220128/349b860c669d495898d85697fcfedc83.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《死亡搁浅》导演剪辑版登陆PC" h="970" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220128/s_349b860c669d495898d85697fcfedc83.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>IO Interactive现在是独立工作室，从史克威尔那里赎身成功后吃相愈发难看了。</p>
