<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">昨天凌晨的<span lang="EN-US">CES</span>展会上，<a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/807/807308.htm" target="_blank"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>发布了锐龙<span lang="EN-US">6000</span>系列处理器</a>，<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>升级<span lang="EN-US">6nm Zen3+</span>架构，<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>也升级<span lang="EN-US">RDNA2</span>架构，整体性能提升<span lang="EN-US">30%</span>，还可以提供长达<span lang="EN-US">24</span>小时笔记本续航。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">除了处理器方面的提升，锐龙<span lang="EN-US">6000</span>系列在<span lang="EN-US">IO</span>技术上也全面升级了，<strong><span lang="EN-US">PCIe 4.0</span>回来了，支持蓝牙<span lang="EN-US">5.2E</span>、<span lang="EN-US">Wi-Fi 6E</span>及<span lang="EN-US">HDMI 2.1</span>，不过最让人惊喜的是<span lang="EN-US">USB4</span>接口首发。</strong></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="AMD锐龙6000系列处理器首发 40Gbps的USB4接口来了" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220106/5599f1a4-e901-4efe-b2d3-ffc17e0f4279.png" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">为啥<span lang="EN-US">USB4</span>很重要？因为在过去的一两年中，<strong><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>在<span lang="EN-US">11</span>代酷睿上全面整合<span lang="EN-US">Thunderbolt4</span>（俗称雷电<span lang="EN-US">4</span>）接口，<span lang="EN-US">40Gbps</span>速度、<span lang="EN-US">100W</span>供电很好很强大，</strong><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">5000</span>系列笔记本相形见绌，厂商最多也只能做到<span lang="EN-US">USB 3.2 Gen2x2</span>，速度<span lang="EN-US">20Gbps</span>，供电也不统一。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Thunderbolt4</span>接口虽然可以兼容<span lang="EN-US">USB4</span>，但<span lang="EN-US">USB4</span>在<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿中依然不是重点，给了<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>锐龙<span lang="EN-US">6000</span>系列首发的机会。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">由于目前很多厂商的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">6000</span>系列笔记本还没公布详细规格，<strong>但联想新推出的<span lang="EN-US">ThinkPad Z13</span>及<span lang="EN-US">Z16</span>系列已经全面跟进了，分别配备了<span lang="EN-US">2</span>个、<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个<span lang="EN-US">USB4</span>接口。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="AMD锐龙6000系列处理器首发 40Gbps的USB4接口来了" h="449" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220106/12cd0a06-27f1-449f-8d81-ce275789a87d.jpg" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">与之前的<span lang="EN-US">USB</span>接口相比，<span lang="EN-US">USB4</span>全面采用<span lang="EN-US">Type-C</span>物理接口，正反插都行，速率可达<span lang="EN-US">40Gbps</span>，同样支持<span lang="EN-US">100W</span>供电输出，支持带宽共享，还兼容<span lang="EN-US">Thunderbolt3</span>（理论上也能兼容<span lang="EN-US">Thunderbolt4</span>），同时也支持<span lang="EN-US">DP 1.4</span>，做到了数据、视频及供电三位一体，轻薄本不需要专用电源就可以使用<span lang="EN-US">USB4</span>线<span lang="EN-US">+</span>充电头供电了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">按照业界的预期，<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">USB4</span>接口就会放量，至少高端笔记本上会成为标配。</p>
