<p><strong>小米旗下的Redmi品牌定位于高性价比，但这也不意味着为了控制价格而不顾品质，而是同时兼顾高性价比和高品质，良心满满。</strong></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/ea77382f63ae44baa3e35e8e27173859.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="RedmiBook Pro 14视频上手体验：11代酷睿撑起无短板的Redmi笔记本" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/s_ea77382f63ae44baa3e35e8e27173859.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center" style="display:none"><img height="0" src="https://g.cn.miaozhen.com/x/k=2237324&p=7sY6v&dx=__IPDX__&rt=2&pro=s&ns=__IP__&ni=__IESID__&v=__LOC__&xa=__ADPLATFORM__&tr=__REQUESTID__&vg=__AUTOPLAY__&nh=__AUTOREFRESH__&mo=__OS__&m0=__OPENUDID__&m0a=__DUID__&m1=__ANDROIDID1__&m1a=__ANDROIDID__&m2=__IMEI__&m4=__AAID__&m5=__IDFA__&m6=__MAC1__&m6a=__MAC__&m11=__OAID__&m14=__CAID__&m5a=__IDFV__&mn=__ANAME__&o=" twffan="done" width="0" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>比如今年2月份诞生的RedmiBook Pro系列，就是Redmi品牌历史上第一款高端笔记本产品，在屏幕、硬盘、内存、轻薄方面都不存在短板，堪称RedmiBook的里程碑之作。今天，我们就来体验一下<strong>14英寸版的RedmiBook Pro</strong>。</p>
<p class="newsinfo_vedio"><a class="mydrivers_player" href="https://weibo.com/tv/show/1034:4645799263666249?from=old_pc_videoshow">视频</a></p>
<p>它和RedmiBook Pro 15除了尺寸不同，最大区别就是处理器：15寸用的是11代酷睿高性能版i5-11350H，14寸的则是同属于11代酷睿，但是低压低功耗版的i5-1135G7。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/c94cd3b7e70b446fbe620d582e9c552b.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="RedmiBook Pro 14视频上手体验：11代酷睿撑起无短板的Redmi笔记本" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/s_c94cd3b7e70b446fbe620d582e9c552b.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>RedmiBook Pro 14采用微米级CNC一体精雕工艺，A面、C面、侧面由一整块金属锭精雕制成，身表面还有一道陶瓷喷砂金属阳极氧化工艺，摸上去手感细腻。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/2f2360c025364c6baffb6849eefe66d3.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="RedmiBook Pro 14视频上手体验：11代酷睿撑起无短板的Redmi笔记本" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/s_2f2360c025364c6baffb6849eefe66d3.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>14.1英寸的IPS屏幕，2560×1600分辨率，16:10比例，可以纵向显示更多内容，提高办公效率，另外色域100% sRGB。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/7e4d07c4c3b14669964563422e476015.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="RedmiBook Pro 14视频上手体验：11代酷睿撑起无短板的Redmi笔记本" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/s_7e4d07c4c3b14669964563422e476015.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>C面是传统的一体化成型全尺寸键盘，人性化布局，19.5mm键距标准，1.5mm舒适回弹高键程。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/3263c143ed154b838785f93d22437b2d.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="RedmiBook Pro 14视频上手体验：11代酷睿撑起无短板的Redmi笔记本" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/s_3263c143ed154b838785f93d22437b2d.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>D面有一排散热栅格。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/8d0a90cd5dda4df6bded5b600309d22e.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="RedmiBook Pro 14视频上手体验：11代酷睿撑起无短板的Redmi笔记本" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/s_8d0a90cd5dda4df6bded5b600309d22e.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>左侧除了有两个对称排布的Type-C接口外，还有一个HDMI与一个USB-A 3.0接口，其中两个Type-C接口均支持快充功能。更值得一提的是，<strong>右侧的Type接口同时还是雷电4高速接口</strong>，<strong>传输速度比USB 3.2快约4.4倍。</strong></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/3de948172ae448c887f5f536c0439078.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="RedmiBook Pro 14视频上手体验：11代酷睿撑起无短板的Redmi笔记本" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/s_3de948172ae448c887f5f536c0439078.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>右侧有一个3.5mm耳麦接口、一个USB 2.0接口和一个Type-C接口。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/8ccb9b1e43dd417da54079b9f241e036.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="RedmiBook Pro 14视频上手体验：11代酷睿撑起无短板的Redmi笔记本" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/s_8ccb9b1e43dd417da54079b9f241e036.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>RedmiBook Pro 14采用了Intel 11代酷睿低功耗家族的<strong>i5-1135G7</strong>，10nm工艺，4核心8线程，5MB二级缓存，8MB三级缓存，主频2.4-4.2GHz，<strong>集成全新Iris Xe核芯显卡</strong>，80个执行单元，整体热设计功耗28W。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/6a0e4967671e405cbec3f08724b77b14.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="RedmiBook Pro 14视频上手体验：11代酷睿撑起无短板的Redmi笔记本" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/s_6a0e4967671e405cbec3f08724b77b14.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>对比上代同等定位的i5-10210U，它在工艺、频率、缓存、核显等各个方面都有巨大的进步，还加入了对<strong>AVX512指令集的支持，处理性能提高一倍。</strong></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/384c51fe975041089d6d523c10a719de.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="RedmiBook Pro 14视频上手体验：11代酷睿撑起无短板的Redmi笔记本" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/s_384c51fe975041089d6d523c10a719de.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>它还是一颗为AI而生的处理器，Iris Xe核显、AVX512指令集、GAMN神经加速器都有AI加速功能，可以支持各种AI推理场景，比如图片处理、视频处理、远程会议等。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/25d22dfd976a4760bf792a955b4e1100.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="RedmiBook Pro 14视频上手体验：11代酷睿撑起无短板的Redmi笔记本" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/s_25d22dfd976a4760bf792a955b4e1100.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>CPU性能测试对比搭配i5-10210U的上一代RedmiBook 14。<strong>POV-Ray单核性能提升19％，多核则高达50％。</strong></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/dedf4f2c52d84123b9ada953b979877a.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="RedmiBook Pro 14视频上手体验：11代酷睿撑起无短板的Redmi笔记本" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/s_dedf4f2c52d84123b9ada953b979877a.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>wPrime 32M单线程提升3％，因为该项测试时间比较短，两款处理器短时间加速频率差不多；<strong>1024M多线程则猛增50％，</strong>1135G7高性能状态更持久。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/07d3e3fae6464aeabe9df642a82ced89.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="RedmiBook Pro 14视频上手体验：11代酷睿撑起无短板的Redmi笔记本" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/s_07d3e3fae6464aeabe9df642a82ced89.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p><strong>FritzChess国际象棋测试提升达到了不可思议的60％。</strong></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/2ee43691e5ee4a4097ea5258c5b45785.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="RedmiBook Pro 14视频上手体验：11代酷睿撑起无短板的Redmi笔记本" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/s_2ee43691e5ee4a4097ea5258c5b45785.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>核显测试，3DMark Fire Strike Extreme跑分为1530，好于10W残血版的独立显卡MX250，略低于28W满血版的MX250，<strong>对比上代24单元的UHD 630则提升多达200％。</strong></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/a01a172b27094e52ab4d676df38e4198.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="RedmiBook Pro 14视频上手体验：11代酷睿撑起无短板的Redmi笔记本" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/s_a01a172b27094e52ab4d676df38e4198.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>《最终幻想》测试1671分，基本和MX250同一档次。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/e7b019e23c304d7ba071fba5b3df4c5c.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="RedmiBook Pro 14视频上手体验：11代酷睿撑起无短板的Redmi笔记本" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/s_e7b019e23c304d7ba071fba5b3df4c5c.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>《坦克世界》测试8641分，换算帧率为52FPS。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/52eed4acc0254734b7fbe86a651fb83a.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="RedmiBook Pro 14视频上手体验：11代酷睿撑起无短板的Redmi笔记本" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/s_52eed4acc0254734b7fbe86a651fb83a.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>屏幕实测覆盖97%的sRGB色域、73%的Adobe RGB色域、73%的P3色域，最大亮度为263尼特，对比度大部分时候都能达到1500:1以上。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/cdb70d82155e4643b87556840e16cada.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="RedmiBook Pro 14视频上手体验：11代酷睿撑起无短板的Redmi笔记本" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/s_cdb70d82155e4643b87556840e16cada.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/62ac672c849942e29bc66e05f1d36d1e.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="RedmiBook Pro 14视频上手体验：11代酷睿撑起无短板的Redmi笔记本" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/s_62ac672c849942e29bc66e05f1d36d1e.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>雷克沙的NVme SSD顺序读写最高可以分别达到1.9GB/s、1.6GB/s。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/9a3077ed02a843fc96cda792ba6f462f.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="RedmiBook Pro 14视频上手体验：11代酷睿撑起无短板的Redmi笔记本" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/s_9a3077ed02a843fc96cda792ba6f462f.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>RedmiBook Pro 14内置56Wh大电池，PCMark 10现代办公场景、节能模式、50％亮度实测为<strong>9小时45分钟</strong>，足够支撑一天移动办公。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/a6ab5e0c8b5f4f2695695838a5a12a19.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="RedmiBook Pro 14视频上手体验：11代酷睿撑起无短板的Redmi笔记本" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/s_a6ab5e0c8b5f4f2695695838a5a12a19.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>AIDA64 FPU烤机测试，开始功耗达到了35W，之后一直稳定在29W，全核频率3.2GHz，温度93度。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/3732f03568fe4e64b91c2890f7c5d513.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="RedmiBook Pro 14视频上手体验：11代酷睿撑起无短板的Redmi笔记本" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/s_3732f03568fe4e64b91c2890f7c5d513.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>在笔记本领域，Intel 11代低功耗酷睿无可争议是一款单核性能非常强的处理器，因此可以拥有飞快的响应能力，不论是程序启动速度，还是办公软件运算速度，都远非以往的低功耗处理器可比。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/d53bcb25f5c14dfd9b661f18177ce19a.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="RedmiBook Pro 14视频上手体验：11代酷睿撑起无短板的Redmi笔记本" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/s_d53bcb25f5c14dfd9b661f18177ce19a.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>和上一代RedmiBook 14所搭载的i5-10210U相比，RedmiBook Pro 14配备的i5-1135G7处理器单核性能提升幅度在19%左右，多核性能提升幅度超过了50%，这其中既有Tiger Lake架构的功劳，RedmiBook Pro 14开放的28W TDP也是重要因素之一。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/39897ab76b624abe87fa0c522e88f3e3.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="RedmiBook Pro 14视频上手体验：11代酷睿撑起无短板的Redmi笔记本" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/s_39897ab76b624abe87fa0c522e88f3e3.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>核显方面，Iris Xe彻底旧貌换新颜，80组单元已经可以媲美MX250这样的独显，像《英雄联盟》、《坦克世界》、《守望先锋》这样的游戏都能在中高画质下获得非常流畅的游戏体验。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/7bfe35527765449fa7283a5edb2c0e5a.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="RedmiBook Pro 14视频上手体验：11代酷睿撑起无短板的Redmi笔记本" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/s_7bfe35527765449fa7283a5edb2c0e5a.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>这些数字的变化，反映在实际应用中，就是真切的用户体验的飞跃。如今的酷睿轻薄本，基本具备全能本的实力，无论日常多任务办公，还是各种影音娱乐，甚至是适度的内容创作、休闲游戏，都已经不在话下，不用担心跑不动软件，也大大推动了移动时代的进步。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/91276148922f43e2b519d559ee7e54c6.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="RedmiBook Pro 14视频上手体验：11代酷睿撑起无短板的Redmi笔记本" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210608/s_91276148922f43e2b519d559ee7e54c6.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
