<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">在年底发布<span lang="EN-US">RDNA3</span>架构的<span lang="EN-US">RX 7000</span>系显卡之前，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>今年中还会推出<span lang="EN-US">RX 6000 refresh</span>版，命名为<span lang="EN-US">RX 6x50 XT</span>系列，至少有<span lang="EN-US">3</span>款型号，包括<span lang="EN-US">RX 6950 XT</span>、<span lang="EN-US">RX 6750 XT</span>、<span lang="EN-US">RX 6650 XT</span>，主要是提升频率及显存。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">之前<span lang="EN-US">RX 6x50 XT</span>系列的官方渲染图也曝光了，依然是熟悉的造型，分别是三风扇、<span lang="EN-US">8+8</span>针接口，三风扇、<span lang="EN-US">8+6</span>针接口，双风扇、<span lang="EN-US">8</span>针接口。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>不同于<span lang="EN-US">RX 6000</span>系列公版设计，这次变成了整体全黑色调，只有顶部的<span lang="EN-US">Radeon LOGO</span>保持红色，证实了此前的传闻。</strong></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="AMD Yes！RX 6X50 XT系列显卡不会大涨价：性能提升10%" h="417" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220416/53a56ead-b964-41a0-9f7a-4647370f6021.jpg" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">RX 6x50 XT</span>系列统一升级为<span lang="EN-US">18GHz</span>显存，带宽提升<span lang="EN-US">12.5%</span>，而<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>核心频率预计也会提升<span lang="EN-US">200-300MHz</span>左右，整体性能提升大约为<span lang="EN-US">10%</span>，作为增强版算是可以了。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">最关键的还是价格，<span lang="EN-US">RX 6950 XT</span>显卡之前泄露的价格跟<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3090 Ti</span>基本一致了，约合<span lang="EN-US">2400</span>美元，换算成人民币约合<span lang="EN-US">1.5</span>万元，着实吓人。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在当前显卡跌价的情况下，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>要是大涨价显然不太合适，最新消息称<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>这次定价很现实，涨价是象征性的，<strong>其中<span lang="EN-US">RX 6750 XT</span>、<span lang="EN-US">RX 6650 XT</span>显卡建议价分别是<span lang="EN-US">499</span>、<span lang="EN-US">399</span>美元，涨了<span lang="EN-US">20</span>美元。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">RX 6950 XT</span>显卡售价会超过<span lang="EN-US">1000</span>美元，具体还不确定，不过相比<span lang="EN-US">RX 6900 XT</span>的<span lang="EN-US">999</span>美元也不会大涨。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不出意外的话，<span lang="EN-US">RX 6x50 XT</span>系列三款显卡会在<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月份发布上市，正好也刺激下当前的显卡市场。</p>
