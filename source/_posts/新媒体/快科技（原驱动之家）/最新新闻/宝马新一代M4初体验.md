           </p><p>热衷于M车评测的英国自媒体人Joe Achilles拿到了最新款M4（G82），开了一千英里后收获如下心得：</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210328/0d9dad03e17e4923ac649461093eb0c9.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马新一代M4初体验" h="338" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210328/s_0d9dad03e17e4923ac649461093eb0c9.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210328/21539b2d72dd46a4b19c3c4695762032.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马新一代M4初体验" h="354" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210328/s_21539b2d72dd46a4b19c3c4695762032.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210328/61cb573351eb462f93b3e55114e1d32a.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马新一代M4初体验" h="334" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210328/s_61cb573351eb462f93b3e55114e1d32a.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210328/658ff81cbf5544d7aa486fcd7eaf4c86.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马新一代M4初体验" h="341" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210328/s_658ff81cbf5544d7aa486fcd7eaf4c86.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>车辆配色为外丹泉石蓝（选装），内银石灰M桶椅（选装）。丹泉石蓝是编辑最喜欢的蓝色，随照度不同会变色，但超级不耐脏，除非天天擦，否则都不好意思见人。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210328/92836dde031c48c9b62b262021d57626.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马新一代M4初体验" h="351" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210328/s_92836dde031c48c9b62b262021d57626.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210328/825d452275444515a9347618315eef0b.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马新一代M4初体验" h="313" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210328/s_825d452275444515a9347618315eef0b.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210328/14e2e53918974b1bb894343fe580bb5b.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马新一代M4初体验" h="353" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210328/s_14e2e53918974b1bb894343fe580bb5b.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210328/05a84727e163428ca5cc2e07a18f090d.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马新一代M4初体验" h="327" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210328/s_05a84727e163428ca5cc2e07a18f090d.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>车内选装碳纤饰板，但选装M碳纤桶椅前要好好掂量一下——是的，承托及包裹性无敌，M徽标亮起来超级酷，坐姿极低，长途舒适性也好，但中间的鞍状突起能不能接受？</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210328/364071eade664d208068743106ee90f6.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马新一代M4初体验" h="340" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210328/s_364071eade664d208068743106ee90f6.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>对驾驶员来说问题不大——驾车时两腿呈自然分开状态，左脚在休息踏板，右脚控制油门和刹车，中间的凸起有助于固定大腿。副驾则是另一番情景，尤其副驾坐的是女伴。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210328/41e5857b8588414f9b3d7b484690af56.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马新一代M4初体验" h="365" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210328/s_41e5857b8588414f9b3d7b484690af56.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>评测编辑的妻子就抱怨她全程只能用一种坐姿，中间的凸起使其无法并腿向一侧斜靠（女性常用坐姿）。桶椅两侧高耸的侧翼对驾驶员来说也是一个问题，上下车免不了与之刮蹭。不到一周，银石色的Alcantara材质护翼就有发展为（牛仔裤）丹宁蓝的迹象：</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"> <a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210328/877375af3ba742ab84a80c0a7b85e4eb.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马新一代M4初体验" h="391" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210328/s_877375af3ba742ab84a80c0a7b85e4eb.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>采用八速ZF而不是双离合被人批评不够“M”，评测人承认少了个性，没有拳拳到肉之感；但提升了舒适性，换挡逻辑也无懈可击，除非出门就是赛道，否则还是ZF香。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210328/53f7ba46f69044e2abb1a053b23c51f4.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马新一代M4初体验" h="404" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210328/s_53f7ba46f69044e2abb1a053b23c51f4.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210328/42fd6016e3534e92ac7d6e65c1766e8e.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马新一代M4初体验" h="394" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210328/s_42fd6016e3534e92ac7d6e65c1766e8e.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210328/7101d41063f94ce9bf39953bc4094bc5.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马新一代M4初体验" h="354" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210328/s_7101d41063f94ce9bf39953bc4094bc5.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>过弯极好，出弯利落，极限很高，部分归功于前275/35 R19的米其林PS2性能胎——非缺气保用，因此舒适性与抓地力比宝马惯用的缺气保用胎要好。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210328/ae61373c63564a5aaac38b01991234fe.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马新一代M4初体验" h="340" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210328/s_ae61373c63564a5aaac38b01991234fe.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>油耗出人意料地好，巡航转速维持在1500RPM。混杂乡间道路及赛道，油耗不过是26MPG（英制每加仑英里数，合百公里11升）；温柔一点开，油耗可低至40MPG（百公里7升）。再结合大油箱，堪称长途小能手。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210328/cdd5f2aae5344537a6842dc2211d6a76.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马新一代M4初体验" h="360" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210328/s_cdd5f2aae5344537a6842dc2211d6a76.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210328/c83567800c4242ea9b8fb46105717764.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马新一代M4初体验" h="348" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210328/s_c83567800c4242ea9b8fb46105717764.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>后排极憋屈，空间表现甚至不如上一代（F82）。双门轿跑的溜背造型导致后排容易顶头，总之后排没法坐成年人。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210328/f449675f2a8345b39271fb6fcc1b4d8a.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马新一代M4初体验" h="335" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210328/s_f449675f2a8345b39271fb6fcc1b4d8a.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>以上只是初步印象，Joe Achilles许诺会发表更多更深入的驾驶感受。有读者回应说，评测视频14分14秒处出现的皇家邮政车是他在开，本来对新M4不感冒，看到实车后长草了。</p>
