<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日凌晨，<span lang="EN-US">realme</span>前几天发布的真我<span lang="EN-US">GT</span>大师探索版手机首销，创造了<span lang="EN-US">1</span>秒破亿的记录。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210727/d7401dbd-bf9d-4d02-8ef5-c3e36a0a4a8e.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="罗永浩力赞 realme真我GT大师探索版首销1秒破亿" h="990" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210727/Sd7401dbd-bf9d-4d02-8ef5-c3e36a0a4a8e.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">真我<span lang="EN-US">GT</span>大师探索版手机发布于<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>由日本设计大师深泽直人操刀设计，汲取旅行箱灵感，配备骁龙<span lang="EN-US">870</span>、三星<span lang="EN-US">120Hz</span>曲面屏、索尼<span lang="EN-US">IMX766</span>影像等，号称<span lang="EN-US">3000</span>元旗舰天花板，质价比之王。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">对于这一设计，罗永浩也作为嘉宾给出了点评，认为真我<span lang="EN-US"> GT</span>大师探索版：漂亮、工艺精细、特别、有新鲜感，他也很喜欢。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">配置方面，真我<span lang="EN-US">GT</span>大师探索版升级了骁龙<span lang="EN-US">870</span>处理器，最高<span lang="EN-US">12GB</span>内存，并可以通过内存扩展技术实现等效<span lang="EN-US">19GB</span>的内存容量，后台应用多了也不卡顿。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">屏幕方面，真我<span lang="EN-US">GT</span>大师探索版配备了三星<span lang="EN-US">6.5</span>英寸<span lang="EN-US">AMOLED</span>屏幕，支持<span lang="EN-US">120Hz</span>刷新率、三档自适应调节、<span lang="EN-US">480Hz</span>触控报点率，最高亮度<span lang="EN-US">1100nits</span>，并且采用<span lang="EN-US">COP</span>封装工艺，拥有极窄的下巴，同时还通过了<span lang="EN-US">HDR 10+</span>的认证，屏幕素质非常出色。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">影像也是真我<span lang="EN-US">GT</span>大师探索版的重头戏，前置<span lang="EN-US">3200</span>万像素索尼传感器自拍镜头，后置矩阵式三摄，主摄采用与索尼联合研发<span lang="EN-US">IMX766</span>传感器，<span lang="EN-US">5000</span>万超高像素，<span lang="EN-US">1/1.56 </span>英寸大底，进光量大幅提升，支持<span lang="EN-US"> OIS </span>光学防抖，全像素全向对焦，硬件级堆栈式<span lang="EN-US">DOL-HDR</span>，画质更清晰。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">还有<span lang="EN-US">1600</span>万像素索尼传感器超广角镜头，<span lang="EN-US">123</span>度超大视场角，支持超广角夜景模式，还有微距镜头。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">其他方面，<strong>真我<span lang="EN-US">GT</span>大师</strong>探索版<strong>还支持<span lang="EN-US">65W</span>超级快充、<span lang="EN-US">4500mAh</span>电池、</strong>双立体声扬声器、线性马达，增强版<span lang="EN-US">NFC</span>，提高了刷卡的成功率及识别率，体验更好。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">售价方面，<span lang="EN-US">8+128GB</span>版<span lang="EN-US">2899</span>元，到手<span lang="EN-US">2799</span>元，<span lang="EN-US">12+256GB</span>售价<span lang="EN-US">3199</span>元，到手<span lang="EN-US">3099</span>元。</p>
