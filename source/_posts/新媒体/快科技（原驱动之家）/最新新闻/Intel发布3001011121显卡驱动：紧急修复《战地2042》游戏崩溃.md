<p class="MsoNormal">这几天《战地<span lang="EN-US">2042</span>》正在测试，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>及<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>都推出了游戏优化驱动，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>这边也推出了<span lang="EN-US">30.0.101.1121</span>显卡驱动，主要是修复了<span lang="EN-US">Xe</span>架构显卡在运行《战地<span lang="EN-US">2042</span>》游戏时的崩溃问题。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>这个问题主要是<span lang="EN-US">11</span>代酷睿中<span lang="EN-US">Xe</span>显卡出现的，看<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的介绍，没提到<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Xe</span>架构的问题。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此外，它还修复了另外的两个<span lang="EN-US">bug</span>，具体如下：</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">1</span>、《古墓丽影：暗影》游戏中会出现轻微的图形异常。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2</span>、<span lang="EN-US">Intel Iris Xe</span>显卡：《古墓丽影：崛起》游戏中报错。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">这版驱动支持最新的<span lang="EN-US">Win11</span>系统，<span lang="EN-US">Win10</span>系统则包括<span lang="EN-US">Win10 v1809</span>到最新的<span lang="EN-US">Win10 21H1</span>，处理器支持<span lang="EN-US">6</span>代酷睿及之后的。</p>
