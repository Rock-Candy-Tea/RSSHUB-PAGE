<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>发布了截至<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日的公司<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>财年第三季度财报，营收为<span lang="EN-US">71.03</span>亿美元，与上年同期的<span lang="EN-US">47.26</span>亿美元相比增长<span lang="EN-US">50%</span>，与上一财季的<span lang="EN-US">65.07</span>亿美元相比增长<span lang="EN-US">9%</span>；净利润为<span lang="EN-US">24.64</span>亿美元，与上年同期的<span lang="EN-US">13.36</span>亿美元相比增长<span lang="EN-US">84%</span>，与上一财季的<span lang="EN-US">23.74</span>亿美元相比增长<span lang="EN-US">4%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不按照美国通用会计准则的净利润为<span lang="EN-US">29.73</span>亿美元，与上年同期的<span lang="EN-US">18.34</span>亿美元相比增长<span lang="EN-US">62%</span>，与上一财季的<span lang="EN-US">26.23</span>亿美元相比增长<span lang="EN-US">13%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">按照业务来算，三大部门的具体表现如下：</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>游戏收入：<span lang="EN-US">32.2</span>亿美元，而预期为<span lang="EN-US">31.8</span>亿美元，比去年同期增长<span lang="EN-US">42%</span>。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">数据中心收入：<span lang="EN-US">29.4</span>亿美元，而预期为<span lang="EN-US">26.9</span>亿美元，比去年同期增长<span lang="EN-US">55%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">专业可视化产品收入：<span lang="EN-US">5.77</span>亿美元，同比增长<span lang="EN-US">144%</span>，比上一季度增长<span lang="EN-US">11%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">汽车业务收入：<span lang="EN-US">1.35</span>亿美元，比去年同期增长<span lang="EN-US">8%</span>，比上一季度下降<span lang="EN-US">11%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在<span lang="EN-US">Q3</span>财季中，游戏显卡显然还是最重要的来源，营收增长<span lang="EN-US">42%</span>，主要动力就是旗下的<span lang="EN-US">GeForce</span>业务销售增长。</p>
