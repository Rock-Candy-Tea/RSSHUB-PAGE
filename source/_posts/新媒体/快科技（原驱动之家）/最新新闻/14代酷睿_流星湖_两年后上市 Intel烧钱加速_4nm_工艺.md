<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">很快<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>就要推出<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Alder Lake</span>了，除了大小核架构之外，还会升级<span lang="EN-US">Intel 7</span>工艺，也就之前的<span lang="EN-US">10nm SF</span>增强版。明年的<span lang="EN-US">13</span>代酷睿是<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿的加强版，代号<span lang="EN-US">Raptor Lake</span>，<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年就要轮到<span lang="EN-US">Meteor Lake</span>（流星湖），这是<span lang="EN-US">14</span>代酷睿，升级<span lang="EN-US">Intel 4</span>工艺。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">Intel 4</span>就是之前的<span lang="EN-US">7nm</span>工艺，官方已经统一改名，这一代会全面引入<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>极紫外光刻，能效比相比<span lang="EN-US">Intel 7</span>再提升大约<span lang="EN-US">20</span>％，明年下半年投产。</strong></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="14代酷睿”流星湖“两年后上市 Intel烧钱加速“4nm”工艺" h="481" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211025/e68919fb-308d-4c33-906d-464580918884.png" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="text-align: center"><img alt="14代酷睿”流星湖“两年后上市 Intel烧钱加速“4nm”工艺" h="345" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211025/f156d6b9-027d-4f74-9b49-2adf625786d7.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">首款<span lang="EN-US">Intel 4</span>工艺的产品就是<span lang="EN-US">Meteor Lake</span>，这代开始还会使用更先进的<span lang="EN-US">Foveros 3D</span>封装技术，将多个内核集成在一起。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据最新公布的示意图，<span lang="EN-US">Meteor Lake</span>主要有三个部分封装在一起，<strong>一是计算模块，二是<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>模块，多达<span lang="EN-US">96-192</span>个计算单元，三是<span lang="EN-US">SoC-LP</span>，应该是包含内存控制器、<span lang="EN-US">PCIe</span>控制器等输入输出部分，类似<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>锐龙<span lang="EN-US">/</span>霄龙里的<span lang="EN-US">IO Die</span>。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">目前<span lang="EN-US">Meteor Lake</span>处理器已经<span lang="EN-US">Tape in</span>，设计进入了后期阶段，预计明年下半年量产，<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年上市。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">考虑到<span lang="EN-US">Meteor Lake</span>这一代工艺升级更复杂，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>投入的资金也越来越多，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>官方财报中披露<span lang="EN-US">Q3</span>季度在这方面砸下<span lang="EN-US">4.55</span>以美元，约合<span lang="EN-US">29</span>亿人民币，随着产品不断进入后期，<span lang="EN-US">Inte 4</span>工艺的花费后续还会增多。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">研发制造先进工艺处理器真的是一个非常烧钱的过程，按照<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的花法，一年轻松超过<span lang="EN-US">20</span>亿美元，上百亿人民币才能打造一款高性能处理器。</p>
