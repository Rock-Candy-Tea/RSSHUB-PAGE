<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">作为全球<span lang="EN-US">NAND</span>闪存市场的一哥，三星在<span lang="EN-US">3D</span>闪存上又要领先其他厂商了，日前在三星技术论坛上，三星公布了第八代<span lang="EN-US">V-NAND</span>的细节，堆栈层数超过<span lang="EN-US">200</span>层，容量可达<span lang="EN-US">1Tbit</span>，<span lang="EN-US">512GB</span>容量的厚度也只有<span lang="EN-US">0.8mm</span>，可用于手机。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">三星的<span lang="EN-US">V-NAND</span>闪存现在演员发展到了第七代<span lang="EN-US">V-NAND V7</span>，堆栈层数<span lang="EN-US">176</span>层，<span lang="EN-US">TLC</span>版核心容量<span lang="EN-US">512Gbit</span>，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>而即将推出的<span lang="EN-US">V-NAND V8</span>层数将超过<span lang="EN-US">200</span>——三星没提到具体多少层，但之前的报道中指出是<span lang="EN-US">228</span>层，提升<span lang="EN-US">30%</span>左右，存储密度提升了<span lang="EN-US">40%</span>左右。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">V-NAND V8</span>闪存的单颗核心容量也从之前的<span lang="EN-US">512Gbit</span>翻倍到了<span lang="EN-US">1Tbit</span>，同时性能也更强，<span lang="EN-US">IO</span>接口速率从<span lang="EN-US">2Gbps</span>提升到了<span lang="EN-US">2.4Gbps</span>，性能更适配最新的<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>标准。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">得益于存储容量更大<span lang="EN-US">. V-NAND V8</span>闪存的厚度依然可以控制在合理水平，<strong>封装<span lang="EN-US">512GB</span>容量也不超过<span lang="EN-US">0.8mm</span>，可以用于新一代智能手机。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在未来，三星的<span lang="EN-US">V-NAND</span>闪存堆栈层数还会进一步提升，路线图中的目标是超过<span lang="EN-US">500</span>层，这被视为<span lang="EN-US">3D</span>闪存的极限，不过三星还在想法突破，最终能制造<span lang="EN-US">1000</span>层堆栈的<span lang="EN-US">3D</span>闪存。</p>
