<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">14nm</span>工艺的酷睿处理器一度是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的主力，现在随着<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿的上市，<span lang="EN-US">14nm</span>处理器逐渐要退市了，其中<span lang="EN-US">10</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">H</span>移动版处理器现在也大量退役，上市不过两年时间。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>日前发布通知，宣布了<span lang="EN-US">10</span>代酷睿笔记本处理器的退市计划，涉及的型号包括酷睿<span lang="EN-US"> i5-10200H</span>、<span lang="EN-US">i5-10300H</span>、<span lang="EN-US">i5-10500H</span>、<span lang="EN-US">i7-10750H</span>、<span lang="EN-US">i7-10870H </span>和<span lang="EN-US"> i9-10980HK</span>等，明年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日是最后的订单日，<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日是最后的发货日。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">酷睿<span lang="EN-US"> i5-10400H</span>、<span lang="EN-US">i7-10850H</span>、<span lang="EN-US">i7-10875H</span>、<span lang="EN-US">i9-10885H</span>、至强<span lang="EN-US"> W-10855M </span>和至强<span lang="EN-US"> W-10885M</span>系列则是<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日可订购，<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日前发货。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">这些处理器主要是<span lang="EN-US">14nm</span>工艺的<span lang="EN-US">Comet Lake-H</span>系列，实际上部分型号还是当前游戏本的主力，发布时间也不过两年，现在就退役了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>今年初<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>已经退役了<span lang="EN-US">Comet Lake-U</span>、<span lang="EN-US">Ice Lake-U</span>低功耗系列处理器，现在这一批退役后，<span lang="EN-US">14nm</span>在移动平台几乎销声匿迹了。</strong></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="发售仅两年 Intel退役10代酷睿H处理器：12代酷睿版即将发布" h="338" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211208/12880246-6580-419a-946c-05a5780efdb7.png" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>清退<span lang="EN-US">14nm</span>钉子户，主要原因还是新一代处理器已经成为主力，笔记本上<span lang="EN-US">11</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">TGL</span>系列遍地开花，明年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月份的<span lang="EN-US">CES</span>展会上，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>又要推出<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Alder Lake</span>的移动版了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">这一代CPU工艺、架构和桌面版相同，采用<span lang="EN-US">BGA Type 3</span>封装，尺寸为<span lang="EN-US">50×25×1.3</span>毫米，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>内部集成最多<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个<span lang="EN-US">P</span>核、<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个<span lang="EN-US">E</span>核，总计<span lang="EN-US">14</span>核心<span lang="EN-US">20</span>线程，</strong></span>同时整合<span lang="EN-US">96</span>单元的<span lang="EN-US">Xe</span>核显。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此前<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>表示<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿移动版已经发货给客户，显然新一代笔记本上市也不远了。</p>
