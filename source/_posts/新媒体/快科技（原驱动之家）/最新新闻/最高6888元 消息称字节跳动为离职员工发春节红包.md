<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">春节在即，不少公司都会给员工发过节红包，消息称字节跳动不仅给在职员工发了红包，那些一样离职的员工也同样收到了公司的春节红包，最高<span lang="EN-US">6888</span>元。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">据网友爆料，有部分离职的字节跳动员工已经收到了公司发的红包，不同级别的员工红包金额也不同，标准如下：</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>在职时间<span lang="EN-US">n≥3 </span>年 ，发放<span lang="EN-US">6888</span>元；<span lang="EN-US">1≤n<3</span>年，发放<span lang="EN-US">3688</span>元；<span lang="EN-US"> 6</span>个月<span lang="EN-US">≤n<1</span>年，发放<span lang="EN-US">1888</span>元；<span lang="EN-US"> 3 </span>个月<span lang="EN-US">≤n< 6 </span>个月，发放<span lang="EN-US">988</span>元，<span lang="EN-US">n<3</span>个月，发放<span lang="EN-US">588</span>元。</strong></span></p>

<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220129/b04b59907e2b4c169ad71e6b743c732e.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="最高6888元 消息称字节跳动为离职员工发春节红包" h="450" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220129/s_b04b59907e2b4c169ad71e6b743c732e.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">本次可获得该春节红包的员工范围为<span lang="EN-US"> 2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日至<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，因业务调整离开的中国大陆正式员工。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">红包将会通过在字节跳动任职期间的工资卡发放。若原工资卡已注销，可在<span lang="EN-US"> 2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日前，填写问卷更新银行卡账户信息，并在<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月底收到红包金额。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此前有知情人士爆料称，字节跳动<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年总营收约为<span lang="EN-US">580</span>亿美元（约合人民币<span lang="EN-US">3677</span>亿），同比增长<span lang="EN-US">70%</span>，略低于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年的营收涨幅，<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年字节跳动收入达<span lang="EN-US">2366</span>亿元人民币。</p>
