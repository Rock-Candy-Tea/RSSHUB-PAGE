<div>   
<p>近日，<strong>国产外设品牌怒喵科技官宣了旗下首款TWS耳机CyberBlade，并宣布将在8月30日正式发布。</strong></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220817/dbd4d751f0b449bf99c36c9ae0ccde31.png" target="_blank"><img alt="号称有信心击败苹果：李楠晒新耳机街头涂鸦版外观" h="511" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220817/s_dbd4d751f0b449bf99c36c9ae0ccde31.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>今天，<strong>怒喵科技创始人李楠在微博放出一组图片，展示了CyberBlade的涂鸦版外观，该版本在原版的基础上加入了大量街头涂鸦元素，在外观上与其他TWS耳机差异巨大。</strong></p>
<p>当然，比起张扬的外观，CyberBlade最引人注目的大概还是李楠对它“有信心击败苹果AirPods Pro”的评价。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220817/b5c5bf55d45b40dcb2bd7954b538a370.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="号称有信心击败苹果：李楠晒新耳机街头涂鸦版外观" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220817/s_b5c5bf55d45b40dcb2bd7954b538a370.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>根据李楠的介绍，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>CyberBlade在延迟上有望击败AirPods Pro，并表示，如果最后败给AirPods Pro，公司可能会取消这个产品。</strong></span></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220817/960b8dfe60a84e4596ba0d9514f5e702.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="号称有信心击败苹果：李楠晒新耳机街头涂鸦版外观" h="167" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220817/s_960b8dfe60a84e4596ba0d9514f5e702.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>值得一提的是，怒喵科技还发布了一则广告短片，致敬了苹果的经典广告《1984》，主角正是这款CyberBlade。</p>
<p>不难看出，<strong>怒喵的这则广告暗示现在的苹果已经变成了当年的IBM，而CyberBlade则会向当初的苹果一样，挑战AirPods Pro的行业地位。</strong></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220817/fb992c030bda4aa0a718e2ce149703b4.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="号称有信心击败苹果：李楠晒新耳机街头涂鸦版外观" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220817/s_fb992c030bda4aa0a718e2ce149703b4.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
