<p class="MsoNormal">前不久联发科在国内发布了天玑<span lang="EN-US">9000</span>处理器，除了更先进的<span lang="EN-US">X2 CPU</span>、<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>、<span lang="EN-US">APU</span>等架构之外，还首发支持了<span lang="EN-US">LPDDR5X</span>内存，这也是独一份，骁龙<span lang="EN-US">8 Gen 1</span>支持的依然是<span lang="EN-US">LPDDR5</span>内存。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">LPDDR5X</span>内存相比<span lang="EN-US">LPDDR4</span>内存来说频率更高，目前首发适配天玑<span lang="EN-US">9000</span>的<span lang="EN-US">LPDDR5X</span>内存来自于美光，此前美光也确认了他们跟联发科合作验证了自家<span lang="EN-US">LPDDR5X</span>内存与天玑<span lang="EN-US">9000</span>的兼容问题，首先验证的是<span lang="EN-US">7.5Gbps</span>的<span lang="EN-US">LPDDR5X</span>内存，随后还有更高的<span lang="EN-US">8.533Gbps</span>的<span lang="EN-US">LPDDR5X</span>内存，性能比上代<span lang="EN-US">LPDDR5</span>高出<span lang="EN-US">33%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>日前美光宣布已经开始出样<span lang="EN-US">LPPDDR5X</span>内存给客户，</strong></span>具体是哪家手机厂商还不得而知，不过美光强调他们的<span lang="EN-US">LPDDR5X</span>内存基于最先进的<span lang="EN-US">1α(1-alpha) </span>节点工艺，专为高端及旗舰智能手机而设计，可提供<span lang="EN-US">60GB/s</span>的超高带宽。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong><span lang="EN-US">60GB/s</span>的内存带宽意味着手机的内存带宽已经超过了当前的<span lang="EN-US">PC</span>内存，毕竟<span lang="EN-US">DDR4-3200</span>单通道是<span lang="EN-US">25.6GB/s</span>，<span lang="EN-US">DDR5-6400</span>带宽可达<span lang="EN-US">51.2GB/s</span>。</strong></p>
