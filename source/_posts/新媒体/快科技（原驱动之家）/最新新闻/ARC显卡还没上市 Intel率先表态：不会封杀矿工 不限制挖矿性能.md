<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的高性能独显已经定名为<span lang="EN-US">ARC</span>，中文名为锐炫，<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年初上市，性能最高可以摸到<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3070 Ti</span>级别。虽然游戏卡还没上市，但是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>日前率先表态他们不会封杀矿工，不限制显卡的 挖矿性能。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">显卡市场已经被<span lang="EN-US">AMD/NVIDIA</span>两家二人转了数十年，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>在<span lang="EN-US">23</span>年之后再次进军独显市场，<span lang="EN-US">DIY</span>玩家无论如何都是希望看到竞争更激烈的结果，所以<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>进军游戏显卡市场会有强烈支持度的。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">但在当前的显卡市场上，显卡挖矿是个绕不过去的问题，<span lang="EN-US">AMD/NVIDIA</span>两家都表态无法限制玩家购买显卡后的用途，<strong>不过<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>至少还做出点努力了，推出了<span lang="EN-US">LHR</span>版显卡，限制了挖矿算力——当然，<span lang="EN-US">RTX 30</span>系列显卡现在依然备受矿工欢迎，<span lang="EN-US">LHR</span>版也不例外。</strong></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="ARC显卡还没上市 Intel率先表态：不会封杀矿工 不限制挖矿性能" h="334" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211012/07fc461a-0e81-4ccc-ad74-2488d49a9170.jpg" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="580" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>对于显卡挖矿是什么态度呢？日前主管<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>显卡业务的<span lang="EN-US">Raja Koduri</span>及图形业务总经理<span lang="EN-US">Roger Chandler</span>接受了媒体采访。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal">在回应是否有硬件或者软件功能限制挖矿能力的问题时，<span lang="EN-US">Roger Chandler</span>称这个问题很难回答，但<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的<span lang="EN-US">Arc</span>显卡设计目的是玩家至上、创作者至上，所有的功能及优化都是为了确保为游戏玩家及创作者创造价值。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">至于软件锁定功能或者类似的事，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: Helvetica, sans-serif; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">Roger Chandler</span><span style="background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">表示</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: Helvetica, sans-serif; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">Intel</span><span style="background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">目前没有设计或者构建专门针对矿工的功能，这是一种人们能够在市场上买的产品，（限制挖矿能力）不是</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: Helvetica, sans-serif; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">Intel</span><span style="background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">的优先项。</span></strong></span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: Helvetica, sans-serif; color: rgb(62, 67, 62); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;"></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color: rgb(62, 67, 62); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">对于这一问题，</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: Helvetica, sans-serif; color: rgb(62, 67, 62); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">Raja Koduri</span><span style="color: rgb(62, 67, 62); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">也跟着明确了</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: Helvetica, sans-serif; color: rgb(62, 67, 62); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">Intel</span><span style="color: rgb(62, 67, 62); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">的态度，那就是</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: Helvetica, sans-serif; color: rgb(62, 67, 62); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">Intel</span><span style="color: rgb(62, 67, 62); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">不会做任何额外的工作。</span></p>
