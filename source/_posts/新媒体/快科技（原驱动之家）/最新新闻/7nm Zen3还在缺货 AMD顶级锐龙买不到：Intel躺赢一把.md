<p class="MsoNormal">今年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月初，<a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/819/819014.htm" target="_blank"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>发布了<span lang="EN-US">7nm Zen3</span>家族最后一个产品系列——线程撕裂者<span lang="EN-US">PRO</span></a>，这次主打<span lang="EN-US">OEM</span>市场而非消费级市场，毕竟最高<span lang="EN-US">64</span>核<span lang="EN-US">32</span>线程的锐龙对普通人来说没什么用处。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">线程撕裂者<span lang="EN-US">PRO 5000WX</span>系列可以看作是霄龙<span lang="EN-US">7003</span>系列的简化版，同样的<span lang="EN-US">7nm</span>工艺、<span lang="EN-US">Zen3</span>架构，内存都支持八通道<span lang="EN-US">DDR4-3200 ECC UDIMM</span>、<span lang="EN-US">RDIMM</span>、<span lang="EN-US">LRDIMM</span>，扩展连接有了完整的<span lang="EN-US">128</span>条<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 4.0</span>，相比上代<span lang="EN-US">Zen2</span>架构的线程撕裂者<span lang="EN-US">PRO 3000WX</span>系列多了八条，但不清楚是来自处理器本身还是芯片组。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>具体型号有五款，分别是<span lang="EN-US">64</span>核心<span lang="EN-US">128</span>线程的<span lang="EN-US">5995WX</span>、<span lang="EN-US">32</span>核心<span lang="EN-US">64</span>线程的<span lang="EN-US">5975WX</span>、<span lang="EN-US">24</span>核心<span lang="EN-US">48</span>线程的<span lang="EN-US">5965WX</span>、<span lang="EN-US">16</span>核心<span lang="EN-US">32</span>线程的<span lang="EN-US">5955WX</span>、<span lang="EN-US">12</span>核心<span lang="EN-US">24</span>线程的<span lang="EN-US">5945WX</span>，相比上代多了一个<span lang="EN-US">24</span>核心版本。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="7nm Zen3还在缺货 AMD顶级锐龙买不到：Intel躺赢一把" h="361" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220409/3ecc1940-1563-4662-92e3-52e33925323d.png" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">然而发布一个月，锐龙线程撕裂者<span lang="EN-US">PRO</span>系列还有个重要问题没解决，那就是缺货，来自英国媒体<span lang="EN-US">theregister</span>的调查称，美国有<span lang="EN-US">6</span>家<span lang="EN-US">PC</span>制造商及<span lang="EN-US">1</span>家渠道分销商告诉他们，线程撕裂者的供应在过去几个月中处于历史最低水平。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>目前这些<span lang="EN-US">OEM</span>厂商拿不到线程撕裂者处理器，只有联想公司有货，其他厂商要想拿到供应要等下半年了。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">就算联想是独家合作伙伴，但联想官网上开卖的也只有上代锐龙线程撕裂者<span lang="EN-US">3000</span>系列，而非最新的<span lang="EN-US">5000</span>系列，联想发言人也称<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>供应不是联想的问题。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的供应跟不上让这些<span lang="EN-US">OEM</span>厂商不得不寻求其他可能，<strong><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>这一波倒是躺赢了，因为他们的至强<span lang="EN-US">W</span>系列处理器重新获得了订单，</strong>要知道过去两年中主打工作站的该系列处理器实际上已经因为线程撕裂者竞争太激烈而没什么实质性更新了。</p>
