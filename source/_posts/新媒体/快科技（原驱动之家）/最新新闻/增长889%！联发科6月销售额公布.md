<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height:150%"><span style="font-family:宋体;

<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height:150%"><span style="font-family:宋体;

mso-ascii-theme-font:minor-latin;mso-fareast-font-family:宋体;mso-fareast-theme-font:

<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height:150%"><span lang="EN-US"> </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height:150%"><span lang="EN-US">2020</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:Calibri;mso-ascii-theme-font:minor-latin;

Calibri;mso-hansi-theme-font:minor-latin">年，联发科就以</span><span lang="EN-US">3.52</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:Calibri;mso-ascii-theme-font:minor-latin;

Calibri;mso-hansi-theme-font:minor-latin">亿颗的年出货量成功超越高通，位居全球芯片出货量第一名。随着产品愈发受到用户的欢迎，依靠中低端走量的联发科也有资金向高端领域发起冲击。事实证明，只要芯片做得好价格够低，消费者并不会在意使用谁家的芯片。</span></p>
