<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>斥资<span lang="EN-US">400</span>亿美元收购<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>的半导体交易这两天风云突变，美国<span lang="EN-US">FTC</span>联邦贸易委员会突然杀出来狙击收购，甚至用法律手段起诉这笔交易。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在<span lang="EN-US">FTC</span>采取法律行动之后，欧盟的反垄断审查机构也对<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>收购<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>一事做出了反应，宣布暂停审查这笔交易。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>很显然，欧盟在这个敏感时刻暂停审核并不是为了帮助<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>，而是补刀，这对<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>收购<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>并不利。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">再加上此前英国反垄断部门对这笔交易的二阶段审核表示出的消极态度，至此三大主要反垄断机构都不愿意看到<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>拿下<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>，这笔交易的变数增多。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">至于反对的原因，<span lang="EN-US">FTC</span>局长<span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">霍莉</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">·</span><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">维多瓦（</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">Holly Vedova</span><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">）</span>表示<span lang="EN-US"> “</span>这项提议的交易将扭曲<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>在芯片市场的活力，并允许合并后的新公司不公平地对待<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>的竞争对手。”</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不过<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>在<span lang="EN-US">FTC</span>起诉之后表示强烈反对，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>称该公司依然致力于保留<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>的开放授权模式</strong></span>，并确保其<span lang="EN-US">IP</span>产权可以被所有感兴趣的被许可人使用，不论是现在还是未来都会如此。</p>
