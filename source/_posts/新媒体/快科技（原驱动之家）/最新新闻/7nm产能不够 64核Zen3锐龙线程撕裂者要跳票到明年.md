<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的<span lang="EN-US">7nm Zen3</span>产品线已经布局差不多了，还没发布的就是锐龙<span lang="EN-US">Threadripper</span>线程撕裂者<span lang="EN-US">5000</span>系列了，之前传闻说是拖到今年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月发布，不过最新消息称显示它还会继续跳票，拖到明年。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Zen3</span>架构的线程撕裂者<span lang="EN-US">5000</span>系列传闻已久，预计有三款型号，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>分别<span lang="EN-US">64</span>、<span lang="EN-US">32</span>、<span lang="EN-US">24</span>核心，继续搭配<span lang="EN-US">TRX40</span>主板，顶级型号<span lang="EN-US">5990X</span>，<span lang="EN-US">64</span>核心<span lang="EN-US">128</span>线程，<span lang="EN-US">256MB</span>三级缓存，支持<span lang="EN-US">64</span>条<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 4.0</span>、四通道<span lang="EN-US">DDR4-3200</span>。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">热设计功耗最高还是<span lang="EN-US">280W</span>，其中，负责输入输出的<span lang="EN-US">IOD</span>热设计功耗都是<span lang="EN-US">80W</span>，<span lang="EN-US">CCD</span>计算模块部分则统一<span lang="EN-US">200W</span>，不区分核心数。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">至于跳票的原因也不让人意外，<strong>主要还是跟<span lang="EN-US">7nm</span>产能有关，现在<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的锐龙移动、<span lang="EN-US">EPYC</span>服务器版、<span lang="EN-US">PS5/XSX</span>主机版以及<span lang="EN-US">Navi2X</span>系列<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>芯片供应都很紧张，再发布一款<span lang="EN-US">Zen3</span>新品无疑会导致供应紧缺。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">还有一个因素就是<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>此前表示在产能紧张的情况下，会优先保证高利润产品，线程撕裂者系列的价格虽然比普通锐龙高很多，但是别忘了它们是跟服务器版<span lang="EN-US">EPYC</span>一个产品线，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">64</span>核<span lang="EN-US">EPYC</span>能卖几千美元，用于线程撕裂者<span lang="EN-US">5000</span>上，价格就只有一两千美元。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">最后就是<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>大概也评估了，拖到明年上市，友商那边也不可能拿出<span lang="EN-US">64</span>核的酷睿产品与之竞争，<span lang="EN-US">HEDT</span>这块市场上已经被<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>完全领先了。</p>
