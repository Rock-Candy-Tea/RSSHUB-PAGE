<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">按照<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的路线图，从<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年算起，他们要在<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年之前的<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年里掌握<span lang="EN-US">5</span>代<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>工艺，现在的是<span lang="EN-US">Intel 7</span>，今年要量产的还有<span lang="EN-US">Intel 4</span>，这还是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>首个<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>工艺。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据官方的信息，相比目前的<span lang="EN-US">Intel 7</span>工艺，<span lang="EN-US">Intel 4</span>工艺每瓦性能提升<span lang="EN-US">20%</span>，<strong>如果是对比之前的初代<span lang="EN-US">10nm</span>工艺，那么性能提升超过<span lang="EN-US">35%</span>。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="14代酷睿首发Intel “4nm”EUV工艺：对阵Zen4自信满满" h="338" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220530/c259949d-4291-492d-80a1-b96205f61809.png" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel 4</span>工艺会在明年的<span lang="EN-US">14</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Meteor Lake</span>上首发，后者也会是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>酷睿系列中首个大量使用<span lang="EN-US">3D Foveros</span>混合封装的处理器，主要有三个部分封装在一起，一是计算模块，二是<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>模块，多达<span lang="EN-US">96-192</span>个计算单元，三是<span lang="EN-US">SoC-LP</span>，应该是包含内存控制器、<span lang="EN-US">PCIe</span>控制器等输入输出部分。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">从规格来看，<span lang="EN-US">Intel 4+EUV</span>工艺很好很强大，不过大家最担心的还是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>是否会跳票，消息人士<span lang="EN-US">witeken</span>给出的爆料称，<span lang="EN-US">Intel 4</span>工艺的进展良好，过去几个季度中已经大规模启动建设。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此外，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>他还预测了明年的<span lang="EN-US">14</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Meteor Lake</span>的性能，认为即便<span lang="EN-US">IPC</span>提升<span lang="EN-US">12%</span>，对阵<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的<span lang="EN-US">zen4</span>也是足够的了。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="14代酷睿首发Intel “4nm”EUV工艺：对阵Zen4自信满满" h="399" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220530/615f0a2a-a733-48c0-8f95-13122ba1373d.jpg" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
