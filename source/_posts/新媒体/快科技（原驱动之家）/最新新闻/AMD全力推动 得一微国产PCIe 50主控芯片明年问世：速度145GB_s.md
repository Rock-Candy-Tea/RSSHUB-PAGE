<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>在去年底的<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿中首次支持了<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>，前几天<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>及<span lang="EN-US">AM5</span>平台也加入<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>支持，生态系统的基础已经有了，接下来会更多的<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>产品问世，比如<span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>硬盘。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">现在已经有多款<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>主控芯片，最热的当然是群联的<span lang="EN-US">E26</span>，前几天<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>官宣的<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>平台就是他们家的，<a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/835/835324.htm" target="_blank">国产品牌中英韧科技日前也发布<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0 SSD</span>主控<span lang="EN-US">IG5669</span></a>，顺序读取速度可高达<span lang="EN-US">14GB/s</span>，顺序写入也能跑到<span lang="EN-US">11GB/s</span>，随机读写则可高达<span lang="EN-US">300</span>万<span lang="EN-US">IOPS</span>、<span lang="EN-US">250</span>万<span lang="EN-US">IOPS</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">现在另一家国产主控厂商得一微也透露了他们的<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>主控芯片的进展，主要面向企业级市场，支持<span lang="EN-US">NVMe 2.0</span>、以及<span lang="EN-US">OCP Cloud Spec 2.0</span>，支持<span lang="EN-US">NVMe</span>分区命名空间（<span lang="EN-US">ZNS</span>）、开放通道<span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>，智能多流、原子写、智能运维等特性，支持<span lang="EN-US">ONFI5.0</span>接口。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>性能方面，该公司的主控芯片预计可提供超过顺序读<span lang="EN-US">14.5GB/s,</span>顺序写<span lang="EN-US">12GB/s</span>和超过<span lang="EN-US">300</span>万次<span lang="EN-US">IOPS</span>的性能。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">按照得一微的说法，这款<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>主控芯片会在<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年问世。</p>
