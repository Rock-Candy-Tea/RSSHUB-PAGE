<div>   
<p>这是外界首次获知换代X3的存在，该车型新近经历了一次改款，不疼不痒地更换了保险杠及尾灯造型，谈不上有多大变化。</p>
<p>但新一代X3毫无疑问将全面进化，不仅座舱采用类似iX的联屏形式，动力单元也将电气化。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220622/eaea3f906a67470db25f2cee2bd921c5.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马新一代X3首见" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220622/s_eaea3f906a67470db25f2cee2bd921c5.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220622/a964e54d77524c2bb45770c78d8f357a.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马新一代X3首见" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220622/s_a964e54d77524c2bb45770c78d8f357a.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220622/9ca19919b8bd49a09f402606114515a1.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马新一代X3首见" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220622/s_9ca19919b8bd49a09f402606114515a1.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>由于样车采用重度伪装，除进气格栅貌似大了一圈外，看不出具体造型。车身似乎有加长，但也可能是伪装效果。以机舱占比推算，新一代X3依然采用发动机纵置布局——除提供插电混动甚至纯电外，即便是最基础的型号也配备了48V轻混。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220622/1bab469efc1f4224bd5be5f601934654.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马新一代X3首见" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220622/s_1bab469efc1f4224bd5be5f601934654.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220622/b9b781a70e9142ac92da11ac76ade025.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马新一代X3首见" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220622/s_b9b781a70e9142ac92da11ac76ade025.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220622/6ec74b77383f45f98c9a07dd69032a12.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马新一代X3首见" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220622/s_6ec74b77383f45f98c9a07dd69032a12.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>尾部四出排气表明这是一台X3 M40i，头灯与尾灯均为临时方案，无法判断实车样貌。从伪装车到实车发布至少要间隔一年时间，然后才会有量产的消息，预计新一代X3应在2024年面市。</p>
<p>而新X5将比新X3更早亮相，伪装车已四处溜达了许久，伪装力度正逐步减弱：</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220622/881c3195aa1d4caf946064f489dfce80.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马新一代X3首见" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220622/s_881c3195aa1d4caf946064f489dfce80.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220622/18f32d5653224cdeac5c0461e7f45952.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马新一代X3首见" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220622/s_18f32d5653224cdeac5c0461e7f45952.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220622/847a0de45a2d4505b25b568f4f9ad1d9.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马新一代X3首见" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220622/s_847a0de45a2d4505b25b568f4f9ad1d9.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>新一代X5换用更细长的头灯——眯眯眼意味着高级，X7拥有最细长的眯眯眼，然后各车系随售价走低双眼逐渐睁开，新X1应该是眼睛睁得最大那位。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220622/ae04cecae18447cfa4a0172a6e7a3632.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马新一代X3首见" h="311" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220622/s_ae04cecae18447cfa4a0172a6e7a3632.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>预计新X5将在2023年面市，除iD8车机及双联屏外，动力系统全面普及48V轻混及插电混动。X5M将换用在XM平台得到验证的八缸4.4升混合动力单元，年底面市的新M2将是M家族最后一款百分百燃油车。</p>
