<div>   
<p>狗窝到底有多舒服，答案猫最清楚。</p>
<p>因为它总是会把狗子的床抢过来，自己睡。这操作让人费解，令狗气愤，但大家又无可奈何。只能看着它小小的身体，舒服地睡在巨大的床上。</p>
<p>狗子除了急得嗷嗷叫唤，也就只能希望人类主持公道。而猫呢？能动一下就算它大发慈悲了。</p>
<p align="center"><img alt="霸占狗窝的猫：脸皮厚到你无法想象" h="230" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220707/6f5347590d5748fdb663e14eb312c0ce.gif" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="408" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>狗子气到嗷嗷嚎叫，似乎已经在说明问题的严重性了，但猫不在乎。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><img alt="霸占狗窝的猫：脸皮厚到你无法想象" h="666" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220707/286bb5e6-3edb-4c1f-b3bb-ca8d1af4f8d2.png" style="border: black 1px solid" w="500" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>对猫来说，是龙你盘着，是虎你卧着。是狗，嚎完了去一边趴着。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><img alt="霸占狗窝的猫：脸皮厚到你无法想象" h="375" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220707/9f3ad817-de63-4b98-93c3-5768bc2bf084.png" style="border: black 1px solid" w="500" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>狗子：别干看着呀，这事儿你不管管？</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><img alt="霸占狗窝的猫：脸皮厚到你无法想象" h="500" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220707/5f022ed5-ae88-48f1-89a4-11339b9de71f.png" style="border: black 1px solid" w="500" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>是什么让一条大狗委屈巴巴地挤在一个小窝？</p>
<p>啊，原来是大窝让猫睡了。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><img alt="霸占狗窝的猫：脸皮厚到你无法想象" h="263" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220707/48efa3ecdfe048708efc9490e643d523.gif" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="340" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>猫的小床，只能让狗子放个脑袋。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><img alt="霸占狗窝的猫：脸皮厚到你无法想象" h="299" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220707/55fdc2b4-7cc5-47d6-90be-e2e019c978c5.png" style="border: black 1px solid" w="500" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>狗子委屈的眼神，猫哥看不到。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><img alt="霸占狗窝的猫：脸皮厚到你无法想象" h="236" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220707/6ba8022e-7ef6-4b14-889c-1df3068b2bf2.png" style="border: black 1px solid" w="500" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>看到床被小猫抢走，狗子气得直喷人类：你这个人长这么高，咋就不敢跟猫干一架呢？</p>
<p align="center"><img alt="霸占狗窝的猫：脸皮厚到你无法想象" h="340" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220707/b7086ad3c2ab403f9a8bfbd78445e793.gif" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="230" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>最后这只狗子被气得直往床下钻，准备和小猫睡上下铺。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><img alt="霸占狗窝的猫：脸皮厚到你无法想象" h="340" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220707/e19ff3cdcf3e40aea2b4e14bc9149e8a.gif" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="230" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>人类在询问猫咪床舒不舒服，然后顺着小猫的眼神，找到了一位受害者。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><img alt="霸占狗窝的猫：脸皮厚到你无法想象" h="405" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220707/e1ba92c26d3c4b958dc733d645ce1d44.gif" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="230" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>狗子经常考虑找个帮手来帮自己处理这个问题，但效果不大。「大哥，就是这猫抢我床。」</p>
<p>「这，这我也不敢吱声呀。」</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><img alt="霸占狗窝的猫：脸皮厚到你无法想象" h="375" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220707/c57661ca-ecc9-4bc1-b3de-c0a95148d2b3.png" style="border: black 1px solid" w="500" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>狗子：帮我。</p>
<p>猫：你试试？</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><img alt="霸占狗窝的猫：脸皮厚到你无法想象" h="624" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220707/a3580f86-ca00-45d3-925d-e4de4085c759.png" style="border: black 1px solid" w="500" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>狗子：「这个家难道不是你做主吗？你得支棱起来呀！」</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><img alt="霸占狗窝的猫：脸皮厚到你无法想象" h="281" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220707/c2b2c61c-8892-4efe-8c5f-7d142c3f8513.png" style="border: black 1px solid" w="500" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>猫妈妈正在忙着把自己的孩子放到狗窝里：</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><img alt="霸占狗窝的猫：脸皮厚到你无法想象" h="416" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220707/a683c2ff73744ed0a3765bab6e757bef.gif" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="230" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>它们相处得很融洽，除了狗子已经被挤到床外了。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><img alt="霸占狗窝的猫：脸皮厚到你无法想象" h="416" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220707/082dddabf8014d879d8e707c38f273f8.gif" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="230" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>就算只睡一个角落，这床今天也姓「猫」了。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><img alt="霸占狗窝的猫：脸皮厚到你无法想象" h="587" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220707/d6cac64f-a148-454e-a9e1-d35d2f73dc05.png" style="border: black 1px solid" w="500" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>狗子：「你非得睡中间吗？」</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><img alt="霸占狗窝的猫：脸皮厚到你无法想象" h="667" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220707/6c356bad-85fb-4874-9a14-ffd120ddb7e1.png" style="border: black 1px solid" w="500" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>这只狗子试图讲道理，但很快放弃了。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><img alt="霸占狗窝的猫：脸皮厚到你无法想象" h="501" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220707/a5b9262e-59ec-4ecf-a344-401a967957ad.png" style="border: black 1px solid" w="500" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>狗子向人类求助无果后，只能和猫换家了。</p>
<p align="center"><img alt="霸占狗窝的猫：脸皮厚到你无法想象" h="442" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220707/4bc323b7b8374686a9165016666a1bc5.gif" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="230" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>暗中观察，「我看看这猫走没走。」</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><img alt="霸占狗窝的猫：脸皮厚到你无法想象" h="637" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220707/8ade487c-7482-4a23-aed1-f5bccdbb97ae.png" style="border: black 1px solid" w="500" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>不过有时候盯上狗窝的不只是猫。「鸡哥你怎么回事，鸡哥？」</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><img alt="霸占狗窝的猫：脸皮厚到你无法想象" h="668" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220707/a8aae5ee-d6f6-42b2-9f00-5b245c03bc11.png" style="border: black 1px solid" w="500" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>两只狗子相互加油鼓励后，终于勇敢地向魔王猫发起了反抗，把腿儿伸了回去了。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><img alt="霸占狗窝的猫：脸皮厚到你无法想象" h="281" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220707/628e4926-7fa7-481b-9ad8-1d6f7c737dc6.png" style="border: black 1px solid" w="500" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>这次是真吵起来了，不过猫不在意，只觉得困了。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><img alt="霸占狗窝的猫：脸皮厚到你无法想象" h="230" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220707/fb2646583a72440e98241cb96987c7e2.gif" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="408" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>这只狗子简直怂出了境界，小心翼翼地倒车入库：</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><img alt="霸占狗窝的猫：脸皮厚到你无法想象" h="408" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220707/38daa7f24dbe424fa729545eb7371cc6.gif" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="230" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>不过好在也算是回窝了：</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><img alt="霸占狗窝的猫：脸皮厚到你无法想象" h="408" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220707/e4b630ed0ed24f928cd7bed38e10fd7c.gif" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="230" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>当然也有狗子忍不了，开始反击，直接坐在了猫身上：</p>
<p align="center"><img alt="霸占狗窝的猫：脸皮厚到你无法想象" h="340" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220707/85b09d857341455fb46c6d6247ba0291.gif" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="252" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>你占我狗窝，我抢你架子，来吧，互相伤害。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><img alt="霸占狗窝的猫：脸皮厚到你无法想象" h="667" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220707/943dee7b-365a-421b-a036-25eb2c513137.png" style="border: black 1px solid" w="500" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>小猫的脸被狗子的屁股挤歪了：</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><img alt="霸占狗窝的猫：脸皮厚到你无法想象" h="359" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220707/d88bde30-f0d8-43ff-9576-26cc709174a4.png" style="border: black 1px solid" w="500" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>最后小猫释放大招，不让我睡是吧，那今天就都别睡了。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><img alt="霸占狗窝的猫：脸皮厚到你无法想象" h="340" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220707/ec30828a27ab4d2b981024f160e78653.gif" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="340" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
