<div>   
<p>随着Intel 12代酷睿的发布，电脑内存也正式从DDR4时代开始向DDR5时代切换。</p>
<p>那么，在目前的环境下，DDR4与DDR5之间的对比究竟是什么样的？应该如何取舍？</p>
<p>今天就带来Intel平台的内存测试报告。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220306/c14a0bf9-b0cc-46e0-a780-af4d1852f32c.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="DDR4、DDR5究竟该选谁？Intel 11/12代酷睿深度测试" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220306/Sc14a0bf9-b0cc-46e0-a780-af4d1852f32c.jpg" style="border:black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p><strong>产品测试平台：</strong></p>
<p>首先来看一下产品的测试平台，这次用到了Intel 11代和12代CPU，分别为i9-11900K、i7-11700K、i5-11600K、i5-11400、i9-12900K、i7-12700K、i5-12600K、i5-12400，基本覆盖了现有Intel中高端CPU产品。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220306/5cca72f3-a258-4401-8b1c-bc703925cfdb.png" target="_blank"><img alt="DDR4、DDR5究竟该选谁？Intel 11/12代酷睿深度测试" h="571" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220306/S5cca72f3-a258-4401-8b1c-bc703925cfdb.png" style="border:black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>主板分别用了支持DDR5和DDR4的产品，DDR5的平台使用的是MAXIMUS Z690 HERO。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220306/b0f6221a-7ce1-4df1-a7fc-96801aa88074.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="DDR4、DDR5究竟该选谁？Intel 11/12代酷睿深度测试" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220306/Sb0f6221a-7ce1-4df1-a7fc-96801aa88074.jpg" style="border:black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>DDR4平台主板为ROG STRIX Z690-A GAMING WIFI D4吹雪。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220306/ac1d84f0-82d4-4438-8b13-f29b70c44634.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="DDR4、DDR5究竟该选谁？Intel 11/12代酷睿深度测试" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220306/Sac1d84f0-82d4-4438-8b13-f29b70c44634.jpg" style="border:black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>DDR4内存还是金士顿8G*4。实际运行频率是3200C14。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220306/ae4a9ef9-b2e0-409e-aabc-79a0e4495620.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="DDR4、DDR5究竟该选谁？Intel 11/12代酷睿深度测试" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220306/Sae4a9ef9-b2e0-409e-aabc-79a0e4495620.jpg" style="border:black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>DDR5内存是阿斯加特阿萨战士。需要注意的是这套内存是特挑的，所以不可以代表零售产品的实际表现。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220306/3b7f5359-53ab-451d-9915-981626521002.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="DDR4、DDR5究竟该选谁？Intel 11/12代酷睿深度测试" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220306/S3b7f5359-53ab-451d-9915-981626521002.jpg" style="border:black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>内存附赠了一双防静电手套，这个附件比较少见。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220306/a690476d-c1fe-4036-b8ee-cde6776769ed.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="DDR4、DDR5究竟该选谁？Intel 11/12代酷睿深度测试" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220306/Sa690476d-c1fe-4036-b8ee-cde6776769ed.jpg" style="border:black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>阿萨战士采用金属马甲设计。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220306/9d4656e1-efcc-4db2-8e78-2b1493287882.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="DDR4、DDR5究竟该选谁？Intel 11/12代酷睿深度测试" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220306/S9d4656e1-efcc-4db2-8e78-2b1493287882.jpg" style="border:black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>内存顶部有较长的导光条，可以实现ARGB调光。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220306/15a1eddc-1ad7-4a2e-bcd6-e4193af2a8b1.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="DDR4、DDR5究竟该选谁？Intel 11/12代酷睿深度测试" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220306/S15a1eddc-1ad7-4a2e-bcd6-e4193af2a8b1.jpg" style="border:black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
