<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">随着<span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>硬盘不断抢占消费级及企业级市场，<span lang="EN-US">HDD</span>机械硬盘再也没有好日子了，出货量已经连续下滑<span lang="EN-US">10</span>年了，巅峰还是<span lang="EN-US">2011</span>年泰国大洪水那一年，刚刚过去的<span lang="EN-US">Q1</span>季度中出货量又少了<span lang="EN-US">14%</span>，希捷、西数及东芝三家加起来也不过<span lang="EN-US">5400</span>万部。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据集邦科技公布的<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">Q1</span>季度<span lang="EN-US">HDD</span>硬盘报告，由于经济环境不好、客户端需求下滑，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">Q1</span>季度中<span lang="EN-US">HDD</span>硬盘出货量只有<span lang="EN-US">5300</span>万到<span lang="EN-US">5520</span>万部，环比下滑<span lang="EN-US">9.8%</span>到<span lang="EN-US">13.8%</span>，同比下滑<span lang="EN-US">14%</span>到<span lang="EN-US">17%</span>。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>目前<span lang="EN-US">HDD</span>硬盘市场主要剩下三家公司，其中希捷在<span lang="EN-US">Q1</span>季度出货约为<span lang="EN-US">2340</span>万到<span lang="EN-US">2420</span>万部，环比下滑<span lang="EN-US">9.6%</span>到<span lang="EN-US">12.6%</span>，同比下滑<span lang="EN-US">12%</span>到<span lang="EN-US">15%</span>，市场份额<span lang="EN-US">43.8%</span>到<span lang="EN-US">44.2%</span>，还是第一。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">西数<span lang="EN-US">Q1</span>季度出货量约为<span lang="EN-US">1970</span>万部到<span lang="EN-US">2050</span>万部，环比下滑<span lang="EN-US">5%</span>到<span lang="EN-US">8.8%</span>，同比下滑<span lang="EN-US">11.2%</span>到<span lang="EN-US">14.7%</span>，市场份额<span lang="EN-US">37.1%</span>到<span lang="EN-US">37.8%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">东芝是第三大<span lang="EN-US">HDD</span>硬盘厂商，当季出货约<span lang="EN-US">990</span>万到<span lang="EN-US">1050</span>万部，环比下滑<span lang="EN-US">18.2%</span>到<span lang="EN-US">22.9%</span>，同比下滑<span lang="EN-US">22.5%</span>到<span lang="EN-US">26.9%</span>，市场份额<span lang="EN-US">18.7%</span>到<span lang="EN-US">19%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">放在数年前，西数或者希捷一家的季度出货量就有<span lang="EN-US">4000-5000</span>万部，如今<span lang="EN-US">HDD</span>市场萎缩到之前的<span lang="EN-US">1/3</span>到<span lang="EN-US">1/2</span>，指望销量增长是不可能了，<strong>硬盘厂商现在只能做大容量，提高企业级销量了，消费级市场普及<span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>硬盘的趋势是定了。</strong></p>
