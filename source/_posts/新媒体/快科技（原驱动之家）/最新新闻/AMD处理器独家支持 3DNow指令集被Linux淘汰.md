<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>及<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>是两家仅剩的<span lang="EN-US">x86</span>巨头，过去的竞争中大部分指令集都是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>主导的，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>在<span lang="EN-US">x64</span>上赢得了先机，其他指令集就不一定有好运了，独家支持的<span lang="EN-US">3DNow</span>指令集现在也被<span lang="EN-US">Linux</span>淘汰，彻底作古了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">据报道，在<span lang="EN-US">Linux 5.17</span>内核代码的升级中，有关<span lang="EN-US">AMD 3DNow</span>指令集的支持已经被放弃，<strong><span lang="EN-US">X86_USE_3DNOW</span>选项被删除，</strong>它之前主要是用于支持旧款处理器，包括<span lang="EN-US">Geode LX</span>、<span lang="EN-US">K7 </span>和<span lang="EN-US"> Cyrix III</span>等。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">当然，<span lang="EN-US">Linux</span>删除<span lang="EN-US">3DNow</span>指令集也没什么稀奇的，也不让人意外，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>因为<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>早在<span lang="EN-US">2010</span>年就放弃了<span lang="EN-US">3DNow</span>指令集了，后续的处理器中已经没有这个指令集了，只有一些比较古老的<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>才支持。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">3DNow</span>指令集是<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>在<span lang="EN-US">1998</span>年推出的<span lang="EN-US">x86</span>指令集，主要跟<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的<span lang="EN-US">MMX</span>指令集竞争，首发于<span lang="EN-US">K6-2</span>架构处理器，并在<span lang="EN-US">1999</span>年的<span lang="EN-US">Athlon</span>处理器大放光彩。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不过<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>处理器一直没有支持<span lang="EN-US">3DNow</span>指令集，这是<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>处理器独家支持的，之后<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>又推出了<span lang="EN-US">SSE</span>指令集，逐渐统一了<span lang="EN-US">x86</span>扩展指令集，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>也不得不跟进，只在<span lang="EN-US">x64</span>指令集上扳回重要一局，后面推土机主推的所谓<span lang="EN-US">SSE5</span>指令集也被<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的<span lang="EN-US">AVX</span>指令集击败，没有成为主流。</p>
