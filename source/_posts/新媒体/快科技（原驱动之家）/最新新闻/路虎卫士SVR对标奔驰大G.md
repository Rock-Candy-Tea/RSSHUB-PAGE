<p>媒体在纽伯格林赛道拍到一辆神秘的路虎卫士，从单轮离地状态推测，这就是传闻多时的新款卫士SVR——路虎用来对标奔驰G63的V8版卫士：</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220412/c45a4dafb8ff45128ddf178a8588f830.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="路虎卫士SVR对标奔驰大G" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220412/s_c45a4dafb8ff45128ddf178a8588f830.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220412/e0d502973be04423a76dc5848d63ee2a.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="路虎卫士SVR对标奔驰大G" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220412/s_e0d502973be04423a76dc5848d63ee2a.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220412/8e952c197fcb4650900a8509d454d36a.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="路虎卫士SVR对标奔驰大G" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220412/s_8e952c197fcb4650900a8509d454d36a.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220412/75cc70069d1a4b6188a3087916b80511.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="路虎卫士SVR对标奔驰大G" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220412/s_75cc70069d1a4b6188a3087916b80511.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>若之前传闻成真，则新款卫士SVR配备宝马4.4升双涡轮增压V8发动机，账面数据600马力；或沿用现款5.0升V8发动机（518马力）——无论哪款发动机，都对得起车尾四出排气，更宽的挡泥板与轮拱，以及塞满整个轮圈的前刹车盘。</p>
<p>谍照中的新车基于卫士110，有报道指卫士90也有希望获得SVR待遇。路虎还在谋划新款卫士130车型，以及基于揽胜平台的豪华版卫士。</p>
<p>另外，对国内用户来说更现实的选择是日产Pathfinder，有报道指该车型有望年内实现国产化。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220412/22b6c532a06946a886bfbb01569d9826.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="路虎卫士SVR对标奔驰大G" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220412/s_22b6c532a06946a886bfbb01569d9826.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>北美2023款日产Pathfinder新增Rock Creek官方强化版本，标配全地形胎并抬升了车辆离地间隙。</p>

<p>Rock Creek车型拥有特别设计的前中网，前门及尾门独有标识；18寸轮毂配备东洋“纵驰（Open Country）”AT胎；底盘离地间隙增加1.5厘米，标配全时四驱。</p>

<p>但该车型标配的3.5升V6发动机预计难以引进，届时国产Pathfinder车型可能以2.0T四缸发动机+九速手自一体作为该车动力单元。</p>
