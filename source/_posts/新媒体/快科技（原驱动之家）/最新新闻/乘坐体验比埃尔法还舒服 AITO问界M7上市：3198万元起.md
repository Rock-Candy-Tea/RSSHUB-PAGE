<p>7月4日，由赛力斯和华为共同打造的AITO品牌第二款车型问界M7正式上市，<strong><span style="color:#ff0000;">新车共推出3款车型，售价31.98-37.98万元</span></strong>，将于8月份正式交付。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/4b8dda103e484bcfb669e43d81321f57.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="乘坐体验比埃尔法还舒服 AITO问界M7上市：31.98万元" h="358" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/s_4b8dda103e484bcfb669e43d81321f57.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/f9edf6b6df184fd1bf44f07cc19b85be.png" target="_blank"><img alt="乘坐体验比埃尔法还舒服 AITO问界M7上市：31.98万元" h="358" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/s_f9edf6b6df184fd1bf44f07cc19b85be.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>问界M7定位于中大型SUV，<strong>车身尺寸为5020*1945*1775毫米，轴距2820毫米</strong>，在主流的中大型SUV里，它的轴距确实不算长，不过较短的轴距能够有效减少转弯半径，提高车辆机动性。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/3dabb9a9810541b188cf21e12e3ac6ba.png" target="_blank"><img alt="乘坐体验比埃尔法还舒服 AITO问界M7上市：31.98万元" h="345" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/s_3dabb9a9810541b188cf21e12e3ac6ba.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/8b49252644c84585a8bf42e498514a4d.png" target="_blank"><img alt="乘坐体验比埃尔法还舒服 AITO问界M7上市：31.98万元" h="342" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/s_8b49252644c84585a8bf42e498514a4d.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>不同于问界M5，该车的前脸采用了全新的分体式设计，更加接近传统燃油车造型，有点早期大众的风格，确实谈不上惊艳，但看习惯之后也就能够接受了。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/0cf6955749b04c52bfe9c92bddba50e5.png" target="_blank"><img alt="乘坐体验比埃尔法还舒服 AITO问界M7上市：31.98万元" h="336" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/s_0cf6955749b04c52bfe9c92bddba50e5.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/20fda56a1f224770afc6a316efa5834b.png" target="_blank"><img alt="乘坐体验比埃尔法还舒服 AITO问界M7上市：31.98万元" h="318" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/s_20fda56a1f224770afc6a316efa5834b.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>侧面造型十分修长，车顶线条较为流畅，符合中大型SUV的定位，<strong>其配备了20英寸轮圈，视觉效果十分沉稳、大气</strong>，尾部与车头相呼应，同样采用了贯穿式尾灯，而且还用上了类似奥迪Q5的蚌壳式尾门。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/d3e9962eae8b4a36878ed8edc0152704.png" target="_blank"><img alt="乘坐体验比埃尔法还舒服 AITO问界M7上市：31.98万元" h="338" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/s_d3e9962eae8b4a36878ed8edc0152704.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/4eb50a0785494097acddb97e28a2c98d.png" target="_blank"><img alt="乘坐体验比埃尔法还舒服 AITO问界M7上市：31.98万元" h="346" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/s_4eb50a0785494097acddb97e28a2c98d.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/c7b4bec0dd36468e9b676629b1c34898.png" target="_blank"><img alt="乘坐体验比埃尔法还舒服 AITO问界M7上市：31.98万元" h="368" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/s_c7b4bec0dd36468e9b676629b1c34898.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>M7的内饰基本上延续了M5的极简风格，以驾驶者为中心，整体呈现出环抱式，<strong>同时配有10.4英寸的曲面全液晶仪表盘、15.6英寸HDR智能中控大屏</strong>，后者内置了华为鸿蒙智能车机系统，论流畅度和易用性在市面上是独一档的存在。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/9b55885a27fb4f42a3f4d1fc0de3d56b.png" target="_blank"><img alt="乘坐体验比埃尔法还舒服 AITO问界M7上市：31.98万元" h="321" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/s_9b55885a27fb4f42a3f4d1fc0de3d56b.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/f2072b27dadc44c29c5a7a9859c1d8b5.png" target="_blank"><img alt="乘坐体验比埃尔法还舒服 AITO问界M7上市：31.98万元" h="297" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/s_f2072b27dadc44c29c5a7a9859c1d8b5.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/4128916014ee4a90a91b266d6f78d000.png" target="_blank"><img alt="乘坐体验比埃尔法还舒服 AITO问界M7上市：31.98万元" h="320" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/s_4128916014ee4a90a91b266d6f78d000.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>此外，为了彰显豪华感，该车内使用了水晶电子档杆、高档Nappa真皮、木纹配饰及隐藏式出风口，而针对M5用户提出的一些呼声，M7同样进行改进，<strong>比如中控T型台做了掏空处理</strong>，可放下高下鞋子、平板电脑、折叠雨伞或手提包等等随身物品。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/74150e04f04f4651a5fb4ff7cc977c4c.png" target="_blank"><img alt="乘坐体验比埃尔法还舒服 AITO问界M7上市：31.98万元" h="346" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/s_74150e04f04f4651a5fb4ff7cc977c4c.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>M7采用了三排六座设计，前两排座椅支持电动调节、通风、加热和按摩等功能，<strong>其中第二排为独立式，拥有专属AITO零重力座椅</strong>，可支持语音一键零重力躺姿，还提供了触摸式多媒体与空调触控区，打造比丰田埃尔法还舒服的乘坐体验。</p>
<p>第三排的座椅靠背角度支持六档调节，让不同身材的乘坐者都能找到自己合适舒适的坐姿，还配备独立音响、空调出风口、杯架以及照明灯，保证的易用性和舒适感。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/08add5a3e9d54d68b07fd4c72aa38004.png" target="_blank"><img alt="乘坐体验比埃尔法还舒服 AITO问界M7上市：31.98万元" h="233" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/s_08add5a3e9d54d68b07fd4c72aa38004.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/ceb40878290d4c41a13330c1f5178ae4.png" target="_blank"><img alt="乘坐体验比埃尔法还舒服 AITO问界M7上市：31.98万元" h="250" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/s_ceb40878290d4c41a13330c1f5178ae4.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/7024db3029c44e7284eee2aa23735379.png" target="_blank"><img alt="乘坐体验比埃尔法还舒服 AITO问界M7上市：31.98万元" h="271" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/s_7024db3029c44e7284eee2aa23735379.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/af88fbaad81d4d459508838807422b3e.png" target="_blank"><img alt="乘坐体验比埃尔法还舒服 AITO问界M7上市：31.98万元" h="310" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/s_af88fbaad81d4d459508838807422b3e.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/932742f4dfa1405e91ea1b7949434ee2.png" target="_blank"><img alt="乘坐体验比埃尔法还舒服 AITO问界M7上市：31.98万元" h="309" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/s_932742f4dfa1405e91ea1b7949434ee2.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>为了保证车内的静谧型，问界M7采用低风噪车身+双层隔音玻璃+低噪声静音轮胎，配合秀的整车气密性，有效隔绝外部及路面噪音传递。</p>
<p>不仅如此，该车还配有主动降噪功能，一是采用业界领先的增程器主动降噪技术，二是采用转子修弧专利技术电驱，在怠速时纯开空调车内噪音低至35dB，增程器工作时仅38.7dB，<strong>120km/h高速状态下车内噪音也仅63dB</strong>，达到越级的静谧效果。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/8b140816daf640708eff07e2b1c78da3.png" target="_blank"><img alt="乘坐体验比埃尔法还舒服 AITO问界M7上市：31.98万元" h="330" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/s_8b140816daf640708eff07e2b1c78da3.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/65c9161d552d4c169b86b876f7e67072.png" target="_blank"><img alt="乘坐体验比埃尔法还舒服 AITO问界M7上市：31.98万元" h="330" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/s_65c9161d552d4c169b86b876f7e67072.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/665a862e30604ee28aea7e958f147128.png" target="_blank"><img alt="乘坐体验比埃尔法还舒服 AITO问界M7上市：31.98万元" h="336" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/s_665a862e30604ee28aea7e958f147128.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>动力方面，该车搭载了华为DriveONE纯电驱平台，前舱采用五合一增程器动力总成，分为后驱和四驱两种版本，<strong>其中后驱版零百加速7.8秒，四驱版版最大功率330千瓦，零百加速仅4.8秒</strong>，而采用了博世最新的制动系统，该车100-0公里/时刹停距离仅34.2米，足够优秀。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/ffe07bdca0e049d2a09c4587b81d6e25.png" target="_blank"><img alt="乘坐体验比埃尔法还舒服 AITO问界M7上市：31.98万元" h="331" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/s_ffe07bdca0e049d2a09c4587b81d6e25.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/90786e5964a744a0a61927b2dcb62cd5.png" target="_blank"><img alt="乘坐体验比埃尔法还舒服 AITO问界M7上市：31.98万元" h="293" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/s_90786e5964a744a0a61927b2dcb62cd5.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>至于说问界M7的续航，CLTC工况下，<strong>后驱版纯电续航里程可达230公里，满油满电1220公里，亏电百公里油耗5.8升；四驱版纯电200公里，满油满电1100公里，亏电百公里油耗6.3升</strong>。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/370b1aa3713d431cb79b9ff885410f25.png" target="_blank"><img alt="乘坐体验比埃尔法还舒服 AITO问界M7上市：31.98万元" h="323" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220704/s_370b1aa3713d431cb79b9ff885410f25.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>新车上市后，将与丰田汉兰达、大众途昂、理想ONE等中大型SUV展开竞争，凭借着鸿蒙座舱，以及问界M5打下的良好口碑，问界M7也能迎来不错的关注度。</p>
