<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年已经过去了，这个月底也是欧美众多上市公司发布<span lang="EN-US">Q4</span>及全年财报的日子，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>将在<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日发布<span lang="EN-US">Q4</span>财报。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>之前公布的<span lang="EN-US">Q4</span>业绩预告，<strong>当季营收将达到<span lang="EN-US">192</span>亿美元，非<span lang="EN-US">GAAP</span>下是<span lang="EN-US">183</span>亿美元，全年营收将达到<span lang="EN-US">777</span>亿美元，非<span lang="EN-US">GAAP</span>下是<span lang="EN-US">735</span>亿美元。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">需要说明的是，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>已经将<span lang="EN-US">NAND</span>闪存业务出售给<span lang="EN-US">SK</span>海力士，所以<span lang="EN-US">Q4</span>及全年业绩中已经不包括闪存业务，这也会影响<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>公司数十亿美元的营收。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">虽然大家都知道过去两三年中<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>受到友商的竞争压力增大了，但是<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">Q4</span>季度会有一次转折，因为<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿处理器在<span lang="EN-US">Q4</span>季度发布、上市了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿不仅升级了<span lang="EN-US">Intel 7</span>工艺，还带来了全新的<span lang="EN-US">Golden Cove</span>微内核及性能核<span lang="EN-US">+</span>能效核架构，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>这也是<span lang="EN-US">x86</span>历史上首次采用异构核心，而且<span lang="EN-US">IPC</span>性能提升了<span lang="EN-US">19%</span>，让<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>在多核、单核及游戏等性能上反超回来。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">这给了<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>极大的信心，以致于前不久<span lang="EN-US">Intel CEO</span>基辛格在采访中直接<span lang="EN-US">diss</span>友商，称<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿是一记重磅炸弹，我们重新掌控了游戏话语权。如今在消费级市场，<strong><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>仅出现在<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的后视镜中，并且永远不会再有机会反超我们的领先地位。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">如此评价友商，也让<span lang="EN-US">Intel CEO</span>的言论在媒体圈及粉丝圈中火了一圈，至于是不是真能让<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>看不到尾灯了，明天的财报会议上就会有更多信息放出。</p>
