<p class="MsoNormal">日前四川大学华西医院麻醉手术中心主任刘进向医院捐款<span lang="EN-US">1</span>亿元引发网络热议，刘进教授回应称这笔钱自己和家人花了是浪费。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">据报道，四川大学华西医院麻醉手术中心主任刘进向医院捐赠<span lang="EN-US">1</span>亿元，<strong>设立规培专项基金，用于激励住院医师、带教师资，提高住院医师临床能力。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">这笔资金来自刘进的科技成果转化所得，这也是我国首个由个人捐赠设立的专项规培发展基金。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">对于这笔数额不小的捐赠，<span lang="EN-US">65</span>岁的刘进坦言：<span lang="EN-US">“1</span>亿元，不是个小数目。<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>如果把这笔钱用于我们一个小小的普通家庭去度过更为舒适的退休生活，我们认为是一种浪费，</strong></span>而捐赠给住院医生规划培训的事业，更具有社会意义，更能体现我们的人生价值。因此我与夫人商量，做出了这个决定。<span lang="EN-US">”</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">刘进教授的<span lang="EN-US">1</span>亿元来自于他带领的科研<span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); letter-spacing: 0.4pt; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">团队完成的</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); letter-spacing: 0.4pt; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">2</span><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); letter-spacing: 0.4pt; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">次科技成果转化所得。</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); letter-spacing: 0.4pt; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;"></span></p>

background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">2020</span><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); letter-spacing: 0.4pt; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">年至</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); letter-spacing: 0.4pt; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">2021</span><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); letter-spacing: 0.4pt; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial;
