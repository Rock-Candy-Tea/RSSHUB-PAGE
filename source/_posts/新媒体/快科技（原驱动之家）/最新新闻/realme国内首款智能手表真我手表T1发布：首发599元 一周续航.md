<p class="MsoNormal">除了<span lang="EN-US">realme GT Neo2</span>及<span lang="EN-US">Q3s</span>手机之外，今天下午<span lang="EN-US">realme</span>还发布了一款智能手表<span lang="EN-US">realme</span>真我<span lang="EN-US">T1</span>，配备<span lang="EN-US">1.3</span>英寸圆形表盘，支持蓝牙通话，售价<span lang="EN-US">699</span>元，首发只要<span lang="EN-US">599</span>元。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">realme</span>真我<span lang="EN-US">T1</span>虽然价格不贵，但是功能齐全，配备了<span lang="EN-US">1.3</span>英寸圆形表盘，<span lang="EN-US">325ppi</span>高清<span lang="EN-US">AMOLED</span>屏，<span lang="EN-US">50Hz</span>全局高刷，<span lang="EN-US">50+</span>丰富表盘，大猩猩玻璃，不锈钢表框，三款潮流配色。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>它支持<span lang="EN-US">110</span>种运动模式、全面健康监测、蓝牙通话<span lang="EN-US">&</span>蓝牙音乐、<span lang="EN-US">5ATM</span>防水、磁吸快充、多功能<span lang="EN-US">NFC，支持支付宝支付</span>，而且续航时间长达一周，充电15分钟就可以用3天时间。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">realme</span>谦虚地表示，真我<span lang="EN-US">T1</span>智能手表<span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">可能是价位段首款不锈钢表框智能手表，可能是价位段首款蓝牙通话智能手表，可能是价位段首款高刷屏智能手表。</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;"></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">售价方面，</span><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>真我<span lang="EN-US">T1</span>智能手表售价<span lang="EN-US">699</span>元，不过活力黑版首发限时优惠价<span lang="EN-US">599</span>元。</strong></span></p>
