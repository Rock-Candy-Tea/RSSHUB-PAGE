<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日晚，宁德时代发布<span lang="EN-US">Q3</span>季度财报，当季营收<span lang="EN-US">292.9</span>亿元，同比增长<span lang="EN-US">130.7%</span>，净利润<span lang="EN-US">32.67</span>亿元，同比增长<span lang="EN-US">130%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>前三季度合计营收<span lang="EN-US">733.6</span>亿元，同比增长<span lang="EN-US">132.7%</span>，净利润<span lang="EN-US">77.51</span>亿元，同比增长<span lang="EN-US">130.9%</span>。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此外，宁德时代还公布了<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年股票期权与限制性股票激励计划<span lang="EN-US">(</span>草案<span lang="EN-US">)</span>，宁德时代拟向激励对象授予股票权益<span lang="EN-US">516.104</span>万份<span lang="EN-US">(</span>股<span lang="EN-US">)</span>，其中，首次授予的激励对象共计<span lang="EN-US">4483</span>人，首次授予股票权益为<span lang="EN-US">413.601</span>万份。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">宁德时代本次股票激励计划的激励对象是中层管理人员及核心骨干员工，首次授予股票期权的行权价格为<span lang="EN-US">612.08</span>元<span lang="EN-US">/</span>份，授予价格为<span lang="EN-US">306.04</span>元<span lang="EN-US">/</span>股。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>以行权价和授予价计算，宁德时代上述<span lang="EN-US">4483</span>名中层管理人员及核心骨干员工，平均每人可获得股票激励计划<span lang="EN-US">“</span>红包<span lang="EN-US">”28.34</span>万元。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">截至今日收盘，宁德时代涨<span lang="EN-US">2.85%</span>，报<span lang="EN-US">615.04</span>元，最新市值<span lang="EN-US">1.43</span>万亿。该股年内累计涨超<span lang="EN-US">75%</span>。</p>
