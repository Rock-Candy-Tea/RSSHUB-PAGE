<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的市值将近<span lang="EN-US">1000</span>亿美元，<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>的市值超过<span lang="EN-US">3500</span>亿美元，这两家公司过去一年股价都是暴涨，而<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>市值约为<span lang="EN-US">2700</span>亿美元，今年宣布换新<span lang="EN-US">CEO</span>后股价才回到去年的水平。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">相比其他两家，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>此前一度被各种看衰，高性能<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>计划前途未卜，<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>架构长期不升级，制程工艺也落后于台积电，<span lang="EN-US">14nm</span>工艺用了六年，桌面版<span lang="EN-US">10nm</span>工艺还得等到今年下半年。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不过也有金融机构看法不一样，瑞信分析师<span lang="EN-US">John Pitzer</span>日前发表报告，<strong>称<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>是所有芯片企业中股价最被低估的那个</strong>，值得买进。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">John Pitzer</span>认为<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的目标股价可达<span lang="EN-US">80</span>美元，比周三收盘的<span lang="EN-US">66</span>美元至少还有<span lang="EN-US">20%</span>的上涨空间。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日晚，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>正式发布了第三代至强可扩展处理器<span lang="EN-US">IceLake-SP</span>，升级了<span lang="EN-US">10nm</span>工艺，<strong>最多有<span lang="EN-US">40</span>个核心，<span lang="EN-US"> IPC</span>相比上一代<span lang="EN-US">20</span>核<span lang="EN-US">Cascade Lake</span>有<span lang="EN-US">20%</span>的提升</strong>，<span lang="EN-US">AI</span>性能相比上一代提升<span lang="EN-US">74%</span>，总体性能相比上一代平均提升<span lang="EN-US">46%</span>，相比第一代产品平均提升<span lang="EN-US">2.65</span>倍。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在数据中心处理器市场上，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的<span lang="EN-US">EPYC</span>霄龙份额依然远低于至强。<span lang="EN-US">IDC</span>分析师<span lang="EN-US">Shane Rau</span>指出，<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年第四季度，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>在服务器<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>市场的单位占有率为<span lang="EN-US">7.7%</span>，营收占有率为<span lang="EN-US">8.7%</span>。</p>
