<p class="MsoNormal">又一骁龙<span lang="EN-US">8 Gen 1</span>旗舰机定档了，刚刚<span lang="EN-US">iQOO</span>官方微博宣布将于<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日晚上<span lang="EN-US">19</span>：<span lang="EN-US">30</span>举行发布会推出<span lang="EN-US">iQOO 9</span>系列手机，主题为“新征程，无止境。“</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">iQOO 9</span>系列手机的规格及外观也曝光差不多了，有<span lang="EN-US">iQOO 9</span>及<span lang="EN-US">iQOO Pro</span>两款型号，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>其中正面采用中央挖孔直屏设计，后置超大大矩阵三摄设计，再加上白色素皮机身，辨识度极高。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">硬件方面，<span lang="EN-US">iQOO 9</span>系列将搭载骁龙<span lang="EN-US">8 Gen 1</span>芯片，并配备了双<span lang="EN-US">X</span>轴线性马达、双扬声器和双压感传感器的搭配，并配备了一块<span lang="EN-US">120Hz</span>刷新率的优秀屏幕。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">拍照方面，<strong><span lang="EN-US">iQOO 9</span>系列首发三星<span lang="EN-US">GN5</span>传感器，<span lang="EN-US">1/1.57</span>英寸，<span lang="EN-US">5000</span>万像素，</strong>单个像素尺寸<span lang="EN-US">1.0</span>微米，支持全像素双核对焦、<span lang="EN-US">8K60fps</span>视频拍摄，<span lang="EN-US">Pro</span>版本还搭载微云台技术。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在续航与充电上，<span lang="EN-US">iQOO 9</span>系列将搭载<span lang="EN-US">4700mAh</span>双电芯电池，且最高支持<span lang="EN-US">20Vx6A</span>的<span lang="EN-US">120W</span>超级闪充，与<span lang="EN-US">Type-C</span>的<span lang="EN-US">QC5</span>充电协议。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此外，<span lang="EN-US">iQOO</span>官方还表示，这款手机将会是新一代《王者荣耀》<span lang="EN-US">KPL</span>官方比赛用机，游戏表现值得期待。</p>
