<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>今年就要推出<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>架构的锐龙处理器了，桌面版最多还是<span lang="EN-US">16</span>核，不过<span lang="EN-US">EPYC</span>服务器版“<span lang="EN-US">Genoa”(</span>热那亚<span lang="EN-US">)</span>上了<span lang="EN-US">96</span>核<span lang="EN-US">128</span>线程，还有个专为高密度服务器设计的“<span lang="EN-US">Bergamo”(</span>贝加莫<span lang="EN-US">)</span>，上了<span lang="EN-US">128</span>核<span lang="EN-US">Zen4c</span>架构。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">再往后呢？当然是继续打“核战”——<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>预计<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年推出的<span lang="EN-US">Zen5</span>架构的<span lang="EN-US">EPYC</span>代号“<span lang="EN-US">Turin”(</span>都灵<span lang="EN-US">)</span>，最新消息称它会堆到<span lang="EN-US">256</span>核<span lang="EN-US">512</span>线程。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Zen5</span>的工艺正常来说会升级台积电<span lang="EN-US">3nm</span>，但是有可能因为产能不够，选择台积电<span lang="EN-US">4nm</span>工艺也有可能，一方面靠工艺升级带来的晶体管密度提升，另一方面靠着<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的小芯片设计，<span lang="EN-US">Zen5</span>堆上<span lang="EN-US">256</span>核还是可行的。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">从现在的<span lang="EN-US">64</span>核、<span lang="EN-US">96</span>核一路提升到<span lang="EN-US">256</span>核，<strong><span lang="EN-US">Zen5</span>处理器的功耗也免不了飙升，爆料称部分型号的<span lang="EN-US">TDP</span>可达<span lang="EN-US">600W</span>级别</strong>——这其实也不让人意外了，毕竟<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>也会跟<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>一样，未来几年里都会通过放宽<span lang="EN-US">TDP</span>功耗实现性能及规模的大提升。</p>
