<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">有关<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>处理器性能的参数中，频率一直是核心指标之一，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>酷睿在这方面一直占优，早就突破<span lang="EN-US">5GHz</span>，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>在月初的<span lang="EN-US">CES</span>展会上也官宣锐龙<span lang="EN-US">6000</span>达到<span lang="EN-US">5GHz</span>频率，两家现在都突破<span lang="EN-US">5GHz</span>了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">当然，总体上还是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>强一点，加速频率超过<span lang="EN-US">5GHz</span>很多了，<span lang="EN-US">CES</span>展会上<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>还发布了特殊的鸡血版——<span lang="EN-US">i9-12900KS</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">官方确认，<span lang="EN-US">i9-12900KS</span>的最高加速频率可以达到<span lang="EN-US">5.5GHz</span>，也就是单个大核所能达到的高度，相比于<span lang="EN-US">i9-12900K</span>提高了<span lang="EN-US">300MHz</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>同时，大核全核加速频率为<span lang="EN-US">5.2GHz</span>，相比于<span lang="EN-US">i9-12900K</span>高了足足<span lang="EN-US">400MHz</span>。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>这边桌面版锐龙<span lang="EN-US">5000</span>系列加速频率最高<span lang="EN-US">4.9GHz</span>，移动版锐龙<span lang="EN-US">6000</span>首次实现<span lang="EN-US">5GHz</span>频率，<strong>不过今年底的<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>做到了全核心<span lang="EN-US">5GHz</span>，</strong>只是<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>没公布具体的核心数，如果是<span lang="EN-US">8</span>核<span lang="EN-US">5GHz</span>也一个小惊喜了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">现在两家的处理器已经是全都突破<span lang="EN-US">5GHz</span>了，不过<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>似乎并不愿意开启频率之战，技术营销总监<span lang="EN-US">Robert Hallock</span>在<span lang="EN-US">CES</span>后的采访中表示，过去的两三年中，人们对频率的关注度实际上是下降的。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">他认为，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>如果人们意识到频率<span lang="EN-US">4.6GHz</span>到<span lang="EN-US">4.7GHz</span>的锐龙可以可靠地击败<span lang="EN-US">5.1GHz</span>到<span lang="EN-US">5.2GHz</span>频率的<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>，有<span lang="EN-US">500</span>到<span lang="EN-US">600MHz</span>的差距，这就说明频率并不是最重要的。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">当然，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>也有资本这么说，他们推出的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7 5800X3D</span>频率甚至降低到了<span lang="EN-US">4.5GHz</span>，但<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>称其游戏性能超过了酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i9-12900K</span>及自家的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">9 5900X</span>，是全球最好的游戏处理器。</p>
