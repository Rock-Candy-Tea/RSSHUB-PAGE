<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">前几天<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>公司发布通告，<a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/827/827558.htm" target="_blank">宣布提升显卡业务负责人、印度裔GPU大牛<span lang="EN-US">Raja Kudori</span>为公司<span lang="EN-US">EVP</span>执行副总裁</a>，后者之前是<span lang="EN-US">AMD RTG</span>显卡业务的掌门人，<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年加入了<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>公司，带领团队做出了<span lang="EN-US">Xe</span>架构<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">加入<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>公司之后，<span lang="EN-US">Raja Kudori</span>一直很受器重，实际上这五年来多次升职加薪，<strong><span lang="EN-US">EVP</span>副总的职务已经是整个<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的高层了，而不是局限于<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>显卡部门，可见<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>对<span lang="EN-US">Raja Kudori</span>的认可。</strong></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="印度高管立功 Intel：全球终于知道AMD/NV之外的GPU第三家了" h="480" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220422/f5333beb-6d48-43a6-8902-ec83db543190.jpg" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Raja Kudori</span>晋升副总是在<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>成功推出<span lang="EN-US">Arc</span>游戏显卡之后进行的，<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>正式推出了<span lang="EN-US">Arc A</span>系列移动显卡，主要是<span lang="EN-US">3</span>系列，首发两款型号，高端的<span lang="EN-US">Arc A370M</span>核心频率<span lang="EN-US">1550MHz</span>，<span lang="EN-US">64-bit 4GB</span>显存，功耗范围<span lang="EN-US">35-50W</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Arc A350M</span>精简到<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个<span lang="EN-US">Xe</span>核心、<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个光追单元，核心频率降至<span lang="EN-US">1150MHz</span>，显存不变，功耗范围则是<span lang="EN-US">25-35W</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的数据，<span lang="EN-US">Arc 3</span>系列的<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>性能相比<span lang="EN-US">96</span>单元的<span lang="EN-US">Xe</span>核显性能翻倍，但没有对比友商<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>及<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>的显卡，之前测试数据显示最低端的<span lang="EN-US">A350M</span>性能基本上跟<span lang="EN-US">GTX 1650 Max-Q</span>显卡相当，开局表现还不错。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">对于<span lang="EN-US">Raja Kudori</span>及<span lang="EN-US">Arc</span>显卡的表现，<span lang="EN-US">Intel CEO</span>还是挺满意的，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>在公告中他直接明确了<span lang="EN-US">Raja Kudori</span>及<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>业务的最大贡献——让全球注意到了在游戏市场上有第三个<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>玩家了。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">总之，<span lang="EN-US">Intel Arc</span>独显的意义也就是如此了，让以往只有<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>及<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIDA</span>才能玩的游戏显卡市场上出现第三个竞争者，或许开始不会改变显卡市场格局，但未来可期。</p>
