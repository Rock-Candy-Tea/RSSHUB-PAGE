<p class="MsoNormal"><strong><span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，韩国三大运营商宣布开通<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>，这是全球第一个商用<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>的国家，</strong>比美国运营商早了几个小时，显示了韩国在<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>上的野心。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">经过<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年的发展，<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>在韩国的普及率已经达到了<span lang="EN-US">20%</span>，<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>用户达到了<span lang="EN-US">1448</span>万，而韩国移动用户总计<span lang="EN-US">7110</span>万，这个普及率在全球也是相当高水平了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">5G</span>快速商用之后，韩国也在加速清退此前的网络，<span lang="EN-US">2G</span>网络首当其冲，韩国最大的无线提供商<span lang="EN-US">SK Telecom</span>去年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月份就申请关闭<span lang="EN-US">2G</span>，第二大运营商<span lang="EN-US">KT</span>电讯早在<span lang="EN-US">2012</span>年就关闭了<span lang="EN-US">2G</span>网络。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>第三大运营商<span lang="EN-US">LG Plus</span>公司关闭<span lang="EN-US">2G</span>网络的申请也被韩国信息通信技术部批准了，将于<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日淘汰<span lang="EN-US">2G</span>服务。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">截至<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月份，<span lang="EN-US">LG Plus</span>还有<span lang="EN-US">14</span>万<span lang="EN-US">2G</span>用户，在宣布关闭<span lang="EN-US">2G</span>网络之后，<span lang="EN-US">LG Plus</span>将提供手机及套餐补贴，吸引这些用户升级到<span lang="EN-US">4G LTE</span>或者<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>服务。</p>
