<p class="MsoNormal">在<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>与<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>竞争的将近<span lang="EN-US">50</span>年中，大部分情况下<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>都占不到什么优势，不仅产品力有问题，股票也长期不如<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>，<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年的时候甚至跌到了<span lang="EN-US">1.6</span>美元。然而当年<span lang="EN-US">A</span>饭要是狠心支持<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>一下，今年的收益是<span lang="EN-US">55</span>倍，财富自由唾手可得。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">财经网站<span lang="EN-US">Fool</span>日前算了一笔账，那就是<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年的时候花<span lang="EN-US">5000</span>美元购买<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的股票，现在能值多少钱？</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">计算过程不复杂，<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月份<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>股价跌到最低的<span lang="EN-US">1.61</span>美元，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">5000</span>美元大概能买<span lang="EN-US">3105</span>股，现在每股价格是<span lang="EN-US">91</span>美元，当年的投资现在变成了<span lang="EN-US">28.3</span>万美元，回报率超过<span lang="EN-US">5550%</span>，远远超过同期标普<span lang="EN-US">500</span>指数<span lang="EN-US">110%</span>的回报率。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">以上算的是<span lang="EN-US">5000</span>美元，如果当年有<span lang="EN-US">A</span>饭狠心押注一把，投入<span lang="EN-US">50000</span>美元的话现在就是<span lang="EN-US">280</span>多万美元了，<strong>超过<span lang="EN-US">1800</span>万人民币，足够财务自由。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">说到这一点，还得感谢<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>现任<span lang="EN-US">CEO</span>苏姿丰，她在<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年接下了时任<span lang="EN-US">CEO</span>罗瑞德的担子，那时候<span lang="EN-US">Zen</span>处理器已经在开发中了，但进度延期了，团队也不稳定，苏姿丰承担了很大的压力，一直到<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年初才完成初代锐龙<span lang="EN-US">/</span>霄龙处理器，奠定了<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>股价上涨<span lang="EN-US">50</span>多倍的基础。</p>
