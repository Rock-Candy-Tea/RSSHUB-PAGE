<div>   
<p>7月20日，林肯冒险家2.0T两驱阿拉斯加冰川限量版车型正式上市，<strong><span style="color:#ff0000;">新车售价为28.78万元，限量588台</span></strong>。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220720/e3cbcb31c7694c2c8eda07ae3e7b7400.png" target="_blank"><img alt="比三缸宝马X1香 林肯冒险家限量版上市：仅需28.78万" h="546" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220720/s_e3cbcb31c7694c2c8eda07ae3e7b7400.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>虽然是限量款，但该车在设计方面并未与普通版有太大的差别，所不同的是，其采用了阿拉斯加冰川限量版蓝色车漆，在光线发生变化时将会产生不同的色彩，显得更加具有迷幻色彩。</p>
<p>此外，新车还将配备全新的亮黑色19英寸密辐条轮圈，轮胎规格为轮胎尺寸为225/55 R19。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220720/ce62775106c6459fbb7d05ad55646f69.png" target="_blank"><img alt="比三缸宝马X1香 林肯冒险家限量版上市：仅需28.78万" h="396" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220720/s_ce62775106c6459fbb7d05ad55646f69.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>尺寸方面，冒险家定位于紧凑型SUV，<strong>长宽高为4615*1887*1630毫米，轴距2711毫米</strong>，比国产加长后的宝马X1（4565*1821*1620毫米，轴距2780毫米）还要大一些。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220720/6c65c0e4632f48059d8371746e517804.png" target="_blank"><img alt="比三缸宝马X1香 林肯冒险家限量版上市：仅需28.78万" h="422" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220720/s_6c65c0e4632f48059d8371746e517804.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>内饰来看，该车采用了独特的蓝黑配色，中控屏尺寸为12.8英寸，内置SYNC+智行互联系统，支持OTA升级。</p>
<p>此外新车还配备高级皮质座椅，7种味道的香氛系统、林肯Co-Pilot 360智能助驾系统和专属运动踏板。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220720/a1a0a7b9e9c64809bb526720e8cb85fd.png" target="_blank"><img alt="比三缸宝马X1香 林肯冒险家限量版上市：仅需28.78万" h="463" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220720/s_a1a0a7b9e9c64809bb526720e8cb85fd.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220720/0a9dcf898f344497b03ee057f2737d8f.png" target="_blank"><img alt="比三缸宝马X1香 林肯冒险家限量版上市：仅需28.78万" h="394" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220720/s_0a9dcf898f344497b03ee057f2737d8f.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220720/a51ae030fb014b589f3e5cd24dc3b18d.png" target="_blank"><img alt="比三缸宝马X1香 林肯冒险家限量版上市：仅需28.78万" h="514" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220720/s_a51ae030fb014b589f3e5cd24dc3b18d.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220720/7cf3a2d6de5d46b28f1453ca25f7661c.png" target="_blank"><img alt="比三缸宝马X1香 林肯冒险家限量版上市：仅需28.78万" h="541" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220720/s_7cf3a2d6de5d46b28f1453ca25f7661c.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220720/97d9a83ffccc4d398c1e2b7bd02a5ad3.png" target="_blank"><img alt="比三缸宝马X1香 林肯冒险家限量版上市：仅需28.78万" h="724" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220720/s_97d9a83ffccc4d398c1e2b7bd02a5ad3.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>动力方面，<strong>新车搭载了一台2.0T四缸发动机</strong>，最大可输出245马力，峰值扭矩390牛米，与之匹配的是8速自动变速箱。</p>
<p>28.78万元的美系豪华SUV，怎么看也要比同级的奔驰GLB、宝马X1和奥迪Q3更香，所以你有兴趣考虑下林肯冒险家吗？</p>
