<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">不出意外的话，两三周之后苹果就要推出<span lang="EN-US">iPhone 13</span>系列手机了，大家都说十三香，这一代改进、升级不少。不过最让人关注的还是价格，消息称<span lang="EN-US">iPhone 13</span>系列起步仍是<span lang="EN-US">5499</span>元，但<span lang="EN-US">1TB</span>顶配版要达到<span lang="EN-US">1.4</span>万元。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此前消息显示，<span lang="EN-US">iPhone 13</span>系列将会在<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日正式发售，并且全系<span lang="EN-US">4</span>款机型会同一天开售，分别是<span lang="EN-US">iPhone 13 mini</span>、<span lang="EN-US">iPhone 13</span>、<span lang="EN-US">iPhone 13 Pro</span>、<span lang="EN-US">iPhone 13 Pro Max</span>。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="iPhone 13系列手机价格曝光：5499起步 1TB皇帝版1.4万元" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210828/2879e1f4-deaf-46f0-9c78-8fac4de27667.jpg" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">售价方面，最新爆料给出的信息如下：</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong><span lang="EN-US">iPhone 13 mini</span>：<span lang="EN-US"> 4G</span>内存</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">64G 5499</span>元</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">128G 5999</span>元</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">256G 6799</span>元</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong><span lang="EN-US">iPhone 13</span>：<span lang="EN-US">4G</span>内存</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">64G 6299</span>元</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">128G 6799</span>元</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">256G 7599</span>元</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong><span lang="EN-US">iPhone 13 Pro</span>：<span lang="EN-US">6G</span>内存</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">128G 8499</span>元</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">512G 10499</span>元</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">1TB 13299</span>元</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong><span lang="EN-US">iPhone 13 Pro Max</span>版：<span lang="EN-US">6G内</span>存</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">128G 9299</span>元</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">512G 11299</span>元</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">1TB 14099</span>元</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>这个价格跟目前的<span lang="EN-US">iPhone 12</span>系列相比变化不大，起步<span lang="EN-US">5499</span>元，但因为<span lang="EN-US">Pro</span>及<span lang="EN-US">Pro Max</span>系列有<span lang="EN-US">1TB</span>版，价格要贵不少，分别达到了<span lang="EN-US">1.3</span>万、<span lang="EN-US">1.4</span>万元。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">综合已知爆料，此次<span lang="EN-US">iPhone 13</span>将全系配备小刘海显示屏，这也是苹果四年来首次将刘海区域缩小，将带来更大的屏占比，视觉效果有效提升，屏幕尺寸则依然维持前代的<span lang="EN-US">5.4/6.1/6.7</span>三种。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">另外，新增配色也将是<span lang="EN-US">iPhone 13</span>外观方面的一大亮点，<span lang="EN-US">iPhone 13</span>系列在保持传统的黑白两种配色之外，<strong>大概率还会新增两款名为日落金、玫瑰金的配色，</strong>虽说是新增配色，但是这两种颜色其实在此前的<span lang="EN-US">iPhone</span>中都曾出现过，加上供应链方面的证实，可能性非常大。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">配置方面，<span lang="EN-US">iPhone 13</span>全系都将升级为最新的自研<span lang="EN-US">A15</span>仿生芯片，此前消息称该芯片依然采用<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>工艺，但是却升级了台积电第二代<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>工艺，将会有效提升能效比，同时<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>性能也将进一步提升。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">除了在芯片端降低了功耗，<span lang="EN-US">iPhone 13</span>还将全系升级电池容量，其中<span lang="EN-US">iPhone 13 Pro Max</span>是<span lang="EN-US">4352mAh</span>，比<span lang="EN-US">12 Pro Max</span>的<span lang="EN-US">3687mAh</span>多出近<span lang="EN-US">700mAh</span>，<span lang="EN-US">iPhone 13</span>是<span lang="EN-US">3095mAh</span>，<span lang="EN-US">iPhone 13 mini</span>是<span lang="EN-US">2406mAh</span>。</p>
