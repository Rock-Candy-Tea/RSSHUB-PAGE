<p class="MsoNormal">最近一段时间，不少互联网企业都宣布取消<span lang="EN-US">996</span>、大小周制度，现在<span lang="EN-US">BOSS</span>直聘也跟进了，取消了大小周，并承诺员工薪酬不变。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">据报道，<span lang="EN-US">BOSS</span>直聘今日宣布，将从<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日起取消<span lang="EN-US">“</span>大小周<span lang="EN-US">”</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">恢复双休后，员工薪资中的<span lang="EN-US">“</span>周末加班费<span lang="EN-US">”</span>仍正常发出，薪资总额不变。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>据介绍，<span lang="EN-US">BOSS</span>直聘的薪资结构中，<span lang="EN-US">“</span>周末加班费<span lang="EN-US">”</span>部分约占员工年薪的<span lang="EN-US">20%</span>。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">与此前字节跳动等公司取消<span lang="EN-US">996</span>、大小周导致部分员工损失高额加班费不同，<span lang="EN-US">BOSS</span>直聘此番取消大小周相当于给员工加薪，或者说薪酬不变的情况下，一年多了将近<span lang="EN-US">1</span>个月的休息时间。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">BOSS</span>直聘<span lang="EN-US">2020 </span>年营收<span lang="EN-US">19.44</span>亿元、同比增长<span lang="EN-US">95%</span>，主要得益付费企业客户数增长，经调整净亏损<span lang="EN-US">-2.85</span>亿元、同比下降<span lang="EN-US">39%</span>，主要得益费用率下降；<span lang="EN-US">2021Q1</span>营收<span lang="EN-US">7.89 </span>亿元、同比增长<span lang="EN-US">179%</span>，经调整净亏损<span lang="EN-US">-1.28 </span>亿元、同比下降<span lang="EN-US"> 51%</span>。</p>
