<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">对于下一代显卡，大家要逐渐接受一个现实，那就是它们的功耗会大幅提升，以前觉得<span lang="EN-US">350W</span>就很高了，未来会突破<span lang="EN-US">500W</span>，<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>下代<span lang="EN-US">5nm Lovelace</span>核心的<span lang="EN-US">RTX 4090</span>显卡甚至能达到<span lang="EN-US">550W</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">目前<span lang="EN-US">8nm Ampere</span>核心的<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3090</span>显卡是<span lang="EN-US">350W TGP</span>功耗，指的是<span lang="EN-US">Total Graphics Power</span>整卡功耗，不再是以往单纯的<span lang="EN-US">TDP</span>热设计功耗，但实际测试来看<span lang="EN-US">350W</span>也不是显卡的功耗极限，超频版<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3090</span>有做到<span lang="EN-US">480W</span>的。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>下代<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>代号<span lang="EN-US">Lovelace</span>，升级台积电<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>工艺，按说能效要比三星<span lang="EN-US">8nm</span>好出不少，然而整卡功耗非但没有降低，反而会大幅提升，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>爆料达人称其<span lang="EN-US">TGP</span>功耗不低于<span lang="EN-US">550W</span>，比现在高出<span lang="EN-US">200W</span>。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">考虑到下一代针对<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>制定的插槽规范将供电能力提升到了<span lang="EN-US">600W</span>，未来的显卡功耗达到<span lang="EN-US">550W</span>级别也不是那么夸张。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不止<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>疯狂提升功耗，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的<span lang="EN-US">RDNA3</span>架构显卡也跑不了，根本原因还是为了下代显卡的性能大提升，<strong><span lang="EN-US">RDNA3</span>的旗舰<span lang="EN-US">Navi 31</span>核心说性能提升<span lang="EN-US">150%</span>以上，<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>的<span lang="EN-US">RTX 4090</span>显卡相比<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3090</span>显卡也是要翻倍的，</strong>整体算下来能效还是增长的。</p>
