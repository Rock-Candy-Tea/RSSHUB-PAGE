<p class="MsoNormal">大家都知道<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的酷睿处理器中只有<span lang="EN-US">K/KF</span>后缀的是解锁超频的，<strong>然而<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿中非<span lang="EN-US">K/KF</span>系列处理器被发现可以超外频，最近都被大家玩疯了，</strong>奔腾<span lang="EN-US">G7400T</span>这样的入门级<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>都超频到<span lang="EN-US">5.8GHz</span>了，频率猛增<span lang="EN-US">87%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">既然桌面版<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿都能实现外频超频了，那笔记本版的<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿是否能够超频呢，特别是<span lang="EN-US">45W</span>的<span lang="EN-US">Alder Lake-H</span>系列处理器？</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">针对这个问题，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>官方人员在社区回应了网友的提问，答案是否定的，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>表示只有酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i9-12900HK</span>这一款处理器是可以超频的。</strong></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="45W版12代酷睿处理器放开超频？Intel：仅有一款酷睿i9支持" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220129/061a63c1-fc9f-48d2-9023-56494cb61990.png" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i9-12900HK</span>是<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿移动版中的旗舰，<span lang="EN-US">6</span>大<span lang="EN-US">8</span>小<span lang="EN-US">14</span>核心<span lang="EN-US">20</span>线程，<span lang="EN-US">24MB</span>三级缓存，大核频率<span lang="EN-US">2.5-5.0GHz</span>，小核频率<span lang="EN-US">1.8-3.8GHz</span>，集成核显<span lang="EN-US">96</span>单元、<span lang="EN-US">1.45GHz</span>，最大加速功耗可达<span lang="EN-US">115W</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">同系列中还有一款酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i9-12900H</span>，规格一致，但是非<span lang="EN-US">K</span>后缀，不能支持超频，这件事说明<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>官方对超频的态度是没变的——<span lang="EN-US">K</span>（桌面版还有<span lang="EN-US">KF</span>系列）后缀的处理器才能超频。</p>
