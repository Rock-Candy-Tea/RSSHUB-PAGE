<p class="MsoNormal">去年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月份<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>宣布<span lang="EN-US">400</span>亿美元（约合<span lang="EN-US">2700</span>亿）收购<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>，现在已经过去了<span lang="EN-US">9</span>个时间了，约定的<span lang="EN-US">18</span>个收购期过半，现在交易已经提交给美国、欧盟、中国等地的监管部门审批。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">现在这个阶段很敏感，最近<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>公司<span lang="EN-US">CEO Simon Segars</span>也不断放口风，强调<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>的收购对<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>的发展至关重要，<strong>他甚至表示如果<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>继续保持独立运行，那才是坏事，因为公司将无法跟上客户需求，不能设计更复杂的芯片，满足不了日趋复杂的的多功能芯片的增长需求。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Simon Segars</span>表示，<span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">被</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">NVIDIA</span><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">收购了，后者可以提供更丰富的</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">IP</span><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">组合，推动未来更多更酷的应用交付。</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;"></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Simon Segars</span>的表态与<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>一直以来中立、独立运营的作风不符，而<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA CEO</span>黄仁勋日前也表态，与之前乐观看待收购<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>的态度也有了微秒的变化。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>黄仁勋表示，<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>并不是一定要收购<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>，<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>自己就发展的很好，也有自己宏大的发展战略，收购<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>是为了扩展<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>的生态系统，帮助<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>进入新的市场。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">从<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>的表态来看，黄仁勋希望能够消弭外界的担心，认为他们的收购可以帮助<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>扩展生态。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不过从另外的角度来看，与之前多次表示监管部门会通过反垄断审核的乐观态度不同，<strong>黄仁勋最新的表态也暗示了他们万一没有收购成功<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>，自己的发展也不会受到什么影响。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">结合两边最新的表态来看，在剩下的<span lang="EN-US">9</span>个月时间里，<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>收购<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>的交易不会如早前他们预测的那么顺利通过，不乐意看到<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>吞下<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>的公司及国家并不少，或许他们要认真考虑交易失败之后的事了。</p>
