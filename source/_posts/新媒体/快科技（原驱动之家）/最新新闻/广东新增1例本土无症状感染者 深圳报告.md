<p class="MsoNormal">据健康广东官方消息，<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日<span lang="EN-US">0-24</span>时，<strong>广东全省新增<span lang="EN-US">1</span>例本土无症状感染者，深圳报告。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">全省新增境外输入确诊病例<span lang="EN-US">3</span>例，广州报告，<span lang="EN-US">2</span>例来自沙特阿拉伯，<span lang="EN-US">1</span>例来自柬埔寨。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">新增境外输入无症状感染者<span lang="EN-US">13</span>例，广州报告<span lang="EN-US">10</span>例，<span lang="EN-US">2</span>例来自沙特阿拉伯，其余<span lang="EN-US">8</span>例分别来自加拿大、马来西亚、孟加拉国、柬埔寨、缅甸、伊拉克、喀麦隆和也门；</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">深圳报告<span lang="EN-US">1</span>例，来自中国香港；佛山报告<span lang="EN-US">2</span>例，分别来自菲律宾和乍得。新增出院<span lang="EN-US">2</span>例。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">截至<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日<span lang="EN-US">24</span>时，全省累计报告新冠肺炎确诊病例<span lang="EN-US">2409</span>例（境外输入<span lang="EN-US">1012</span>例）。目前在院<span lang="EN-US">44</span>例。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">据此前报道，<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日<span lang="EN-US">0-24</span>时，广东省新增<span lang="EN-US">1</span>例本土确诊病例，广州报告。<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>新增<span lang="EN-US">1</span>例本土无症状感染者，深圳报告。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">截至<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，<span lang="EN-US">31</span>个省（自治区、直辖市）和新疆生产建设兵团累计报告接种新冠病毒疫苗<span lang="EN-US">48334.3</span>万剂次。</p>
