<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>日前发布了最新的酷睿显卡驱动<span lang="EN-US">30.0.101.1404</span>版，支持了微软的<span lang="EN-US">CASO</span>技术，优化了<span lang="EN-US">Win11</span>下的系统响应速度，建议升级。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>显卡驱动<span lang="EN-US">30.0.101.1404</span>正式版发布，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>为使用<span lang="EN-US">Iris Xe</span>显卡（<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>第<span lang="EN-US">11</span>代酷睿处理器）的混合显卡系统带来了跨适配器资源扫描输出 （<span lang="EN-US">CASO</span>）技术的支持，可以优化<span lang="EN-US">Windows 11</span>系统的响应速度、提高带宽以及降低延迟。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">其他方面，新版驱动还修复了如下问题：</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">1</span>、<span lang="EN-US">Iris Xe</span>显卡（<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>第<span lang="EN-US">11</span>代酷睿处理器）：《孤岛惊魂：新曙光》、《<span lang="EN-US">ATLAS</span>》在<span lang="EN-US">DX11</span>模式下会出现轻微的图像异常。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2</span>、<span lang="EN-US">Iris Xe</span>显卡（<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>第<span lang="EN-US">11</span>代酷睿处理器）：《生化危机<span lang="EN-US">4</span>》间歇性崩溃或挂起。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">3</span>、<span lang="EN-US">Iris Xe</span>显卡：《彩虹六号：围攻》在<span lang="EN-US">Vulkan</span>模式下，基准测试会忽略全屏模式。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">4</span>、<span lang="EN-US">Intel UHD</span>显卡（<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>第<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿处理器）：以【克隆】模式连接<span lang="EN-US">8K</span>电视并将分辨率设置为<span lang="EN-US">4k</span>时会导致输出空白。</p>
