<p class="MsoNormal">今晚<span lang="EN-US">Redmi Note 11</span>系列正式发布了，<a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/792/792323.htm" target="_blank"><span lang="EN-US">Note 11</span>、<span lang="EN-US">Note 11 Pro</span>及<span lang="EN-US">Note 11 Pro+</span>三款手机到手只要<span lang="EN-US">1199</span>、<span lang="EN-US">1599</span>及<span lang="EN-US">1899</span>元</a>，性价比依然很高。这次发布之前就开始盲兽了，有<span lang="EN-US">12</span>万米粉选择相信<span lang="EN-US">Note 11</span>系列，他们也得到了回报——将会获得额外的<span lang="EN-US">99</span>元礼物。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在发布之前，<span lang="EN-US">Redmi</span>就开始预售<span lang="EN-US">Note 11</span>系列了，结果也有<span lang="EN-US">12</span>万粉丝下单。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">为了表达感谢，</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">Redmi</span><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">也为这些真诚的米粉赠送一份礼物——价值</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">99</span><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">元的礼物，感谢米粉的一路陪伴。</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>售价方面，Redmi Note 11 Pro 6+12GB版售价1799元，双11到手1599元，</strong>8+128GB版到手1899元，8+256GB版到手2099元。</p>
