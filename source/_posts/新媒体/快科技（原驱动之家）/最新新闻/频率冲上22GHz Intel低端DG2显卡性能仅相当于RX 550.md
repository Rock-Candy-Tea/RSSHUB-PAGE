<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的独显业务进展越来越快了，预计明年初的<span lang="EN-US">CES 2022</span>展会上就会正式发布。大家最关心的还是<span lang="EN-US">DG2</span>显卡的性能，现在入门级的<span lang="EN-US">DG2</span>测试曝光，频率超过<span lang="EN-US">2GHz</span>，不过性能水平相当于<span lang="EN-US">RX 550</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>新一代的<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>都升级了<span lang="EN-US">Xe</span>架构，其中核显锐炬、<span lang="EN-US">DG1</span>独显是<span lang="EN-US">Xe LP</span>版，最多<span lang="EN-US">96</span>个<span lang="EN-US">EU</span>单元，<span lang="EN-US">DG2</span>才是针对游戏开发的<span lang="EN-US">Xe HPG</span>架构，</strong>性能更强大，还支持光追——不过光追性能一直没啥消息。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">DG2</span>会有多个衍生版，从高到低分别是<span lang="EN-US">512</span>个、<span lang="EN-US">448</span>个、<span lang="EN-US">384</span>个、<span lang="EN-US">256</span>个、<span lang="EN-US">128</span>个、<span lang="EN-US">96</span>个<span lang="EN-US">EU</span>单元，此前传闻<span lang="EN-US">512</span>个<span lang="EN-US">EU</span>单元的旗舰级<span lang="EN-US">DG2</span>独显频率<span lang="EN-US">2.2GHz</span>，<span lang="EN-US">256bit</span>位宽，<span lang="EN-US">TDP</span>在<span lang="EN-US">225-250W</span>之间，性能介于<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3070</span>到<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3070 Ti</span>之间。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">至于低端卡，<span lang="EN-US">Geekbench</span>上泄漏了<span lang="EN-US">128</span>个<span lang="EN-US">EU</span>单元的低端<span lang="EN-US">DG2</span>显卡性能，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>频率达到<span lang="EN-US">2.2GHz</span>，<span lang="EN-US">OpenCL</span>性能<span lang="EN-US">13710</span>分，跟<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的<span lang="EN-US">RX 550</span>性能差不多。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">这个性能显然是不够看的，因为此前还有爆料称<span lang="EN-US">1.9GHz</span>的<span lang="EN-US">128 EU</span>单元<span lang="EN-US">DG2</span>显卡性能比<span lang="EN-US">GTX 1650</span>还要高，只能说明现在的驱动优化还不够。</p>
