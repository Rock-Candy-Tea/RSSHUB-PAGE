<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">从去年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿的<span lang="EN-US">Intel 7</span>工艺开始，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>未来几年会有一波集中爆发，短短<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年内就要推出<span lang="EN-US">5</span>代<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>工艺，一直到<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年的<span lang="EN-US">18A</span>工艺，各种路线图很好很强大。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">日前在美国银行技术大会上，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>高级副总<span lang="EN-US">S andra Rivera</span>也谈到了<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>未来的至强处理器路线图，<strong>今年要推出的是第四代至强可扩展处理器<span lang="EN-US">Sapphire Rapids</span>，使用的是<span lang="EN-US">Intel 7</span>工艺，<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿同代的<span lang="EN-US">Golden Cove</span>架构，还支持<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>、<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>等。</strong></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="Intel工艺进展太顺利：下下代CPU跳过4nm直接进入3nm" h="340" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220609/f7fd57a8-7905-420a-bb2c-f7e64adcffda.png" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Sapphire Rapids</span>处理器性能会很好很强大，不过跳票也是麻烦，原定去年就要发布的，之前官方说的是今年上半年，现在看今年下半年能出货就不错了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不过未来的至强<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>就顺利多了，尤其是是<span lang="EN-US">Intel 4</span>工艺量产之后，下下代的<span lang="EN-US">Granite Rapids</span>会全面使用<span lang="EN-US">P</span>性能核，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>原定是使用<span lang="EN-US">Intel 4</span>工艺的，但是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>测试之后认为<span lang="EN-US">Intel 4</span>工艺的升级版<span lang="EN-US">Intel 3</span>工艺更好，</strong></span>所以<span lang="EN-US">Granite Rapids</span>直接会上<span lang="EN-US">Intel 3</span>工艺，<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年量产。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="Intel工艺进展太顺利：下下代CPU跳过4nm直接进入3nm" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220609/1e8f6167-e88e-430c-9b01-f91354035374.jpg" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
