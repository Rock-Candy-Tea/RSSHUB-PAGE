<p class="MsoNormal">今天下午的发布会上，华米发布了多款<span lang="EN-US">Amazfit GTR</span>智能手表新品，健康监控功能也全面升级，<span lang="EN-US">Amazfit GTR 3</span>手表就带来了一键测量功能，需要<span lang="EN-US">60</span>秒就可以测量<span lang="EN-US">5</span>大健康指标。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">健康监测是智能手表的重要功能，以前心率、血氧之类的指标都要单独测试，现在华米表示<span lang="EN-US">GTR 3</span>智能<span style="font-family:"微软雅黑",sans-serif;mso-bidi-font-family:微软雅黑">⼿</span>表带来了全新的<span style="font-family:"微软雅黑",sans-serif;
mso-bidi-font-family:微软雅黑">⼀</span>键测量功能，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>只需<span lang="EN-US"> 60</span>秒，即可<span style="font-family:"微软雅黑",sans-serif;
mso-bidi-font-family:微软雅黑">⼀</span>次获得<span style="font-family:"微软雅黑",sans-serif;mso-bidi-font-family:微软雅黑">⼼</span>率、<span style="font-family:"微软雅黑",sans-serif;
mso-bidi-font-family:微软雅黑">⾎</span>氧、压<span style="font-family:"微软雅黑",sans-serif;mso-bidi-font-family:微软雅黑">⼒</span>、呼吸速率和<span style="font-family:"微软雅黑",sans-serif;
mso-bidi-font-family:微软雅黑">⼼</span>脏健康的重要身体指标数值报告。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此外，<span lang="EN-US">GTR 3</span>系列智能手表还使用了第三代<span lang="EN-US">BioTracker™</span>生物追踪光学传感，<span lang="EN-US">6PD</span>配置结合自研算法让<span lang="EN-US">OxygenBeats</span>血氧引擎和<span lang="EN-US">RealBeats</span>心率监测成功率和准确率提升至<span lang="EN-US">98%</span>，其中血氧升级到<span lang="EN-US">24</span>小时的智能监测，结合<span lang="EN-US">SomnusCare</span>睡眠引擎能够筛查疑似睡眠呼吸不畅全面监测不同阶段睡眠。</p>
