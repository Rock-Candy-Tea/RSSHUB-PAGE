<p>开着特斯拉Model 3去越野，这种事被一位敢想敢做的德国油管博主给干成了。</p>
<p>据了解，这位德国博主专做民间发明，有点类似国内的手工耿，只不过这位他的制造成本相当之高，每次都是投进去了相当的精力和资金，这回他将目光瞄准了特斯拉Model 3。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220611/de3a56ad0a9d456d9edc0937aca5fe44.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="谁说特斯拉不越野 “德国手工耿”改装履带版Model 3：重六吨" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220611/s_de3a56ad0a9d456d9edc0937aca5fe44.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>整体思路很简单，<strong>就是给它装上了钢制的履带</strong>，之后加上各种轴承和减速齿轮机构，最后把Model 3的驱动轴连接到履带的驱动轮上。</p>
<p>该车的悬架结构和负重轮均为原创，用了24根汽车上的避震桶作为缓冲装置，然后还加上了绞盘。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220611/e651b36e49c540b3bafa2ce42c998d7b.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="谁说特斯拉不越野 “德国手工耿”改装履带版Model 3：重六吨" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220611/s_e651b36e49c540b3bafa2ce42c998d7b.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220611/a5122a5c2df847ec98702824eb803567.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="谁说特斯拉不越野 “德国手工耿”改装履带版Model 3：重六吨" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220611/s_a5122a5c2df847ec98702824eb803567.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220611/7bab3d2aab00499b8035e90bb6cf78e4.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="谁说特斯拉不越野 “德国手工耿”改装履带版Model 3：重六吨" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220611/s_7bab3d2aab00499b8035e90bb6cf78e4.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>一段折腾之后，<strong>最终一台整备质量达到6吨，最小离地间隙80厘米的Model 3履带车</strong>诞生了。</p>
<p>为了让它的越野范更为正宗，博主还给该车装上了车顶行李架，外加四盏射灯。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220611/f196c0128b7f4f5caa10434c9bb3cc40.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="谁说特斯拉不越野 “德国手工耿”改装履带版Model 3：重六吨" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220611/s_f196c0128b7f4f5caa10434c9bb3cc40.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>装上了履带后的Model 3拥有强悍的越野能力，但自重达到了6吨，不知道充满一次电能够开出多远呢？</p>
