<p class="MsoNormal">进入<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年以来，猪肉价格已经持续下跌，部分地区跌破<span lang="EN-US">10</span>元一斤。不过上周猪肉价格开始止跌回升了，环比上涨<span lang="EN-US">0.3%</span>，是<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月份以来首次上涨。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">据商务部监测，上周<span lang="EN-US">(6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日至<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日<span lang="EN-US">)</span>全国食用农产品市场价格比前一周上涨<span lang="EN-US">0.9%</span>，生产资料市场价格比前一周上涨<span lang="EN-US">0.2%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>其中猪肉零售价格止跌回升。前期压栏大猪集中入市情况有所缓和，上周猪肉零售价格环比上涨<span lang="EN-US">0.3%</span>，<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月下旬以来首次回升。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">近期，中央和地方将启动猪肉储备收储工作，预计短期内猪肉价格仍有小幅上涨空间。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">截至上周，全国生猪均价<span lang="EN-US"> 16.16 </span>元<span lang="EN-US">/kg</span>，环比上涨<span lang="EN-US">26.92%</span>，同比下跌<span lang="EN-US">52.78%</span>；猪粮比<span lang="EN-US">5.70</span>：<span lang="EN-US">1</span>，环比增加<span lang="EN-US">27.95%</span>，猪料比价<span lang="EN-US">4.70</span>：<span lang="EN-US">1</span>，环比增加<span lang="EN-US">27.66%</span>；自繁出栏亏损<span lang="EN-US">133.70</span>元<span lang="EN-US">/</span>头，环比亏损减少<span lang="EN-US">74.60%</span>，同比亏损增加<span lang="EN-US"> 105.91%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>外购养殖出栏亏损<span lang="EN-US">1198.71 </span>元<span lang="EN-US">/</span>头，环比亏损减<span lang="EN-US">23.69%</span>，同比亏损加重<span lang="EN-US">203.48%</span>；</strong>本周仔猪均价<span lang="EN-US"> 31.35 </span>元<span lang="EN-US">/kg</span>，环比下跌<span lang="EN-US">6.79%</span>，同比下跌<span lang="EN-US">67.37%</span>；二元母猪价格<span lang="EN-US">57.02</span>元<span lang="EN-US">/kg</span>，环比下跌<span lang="EN-US">7.05%</span>，同比下跌<span lang="EN-US">25.54%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">首创证券表示，随着国家冻肉收储的开始，根据历史规律未来猪价有望平均上涨<span lang="EN-US">20%</span>以上。</p>
