<p class="MsoNormal">自从锐龙<span lang="EN-US">/</span>霄龙处理器上市之后，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>这三四年来的表现有目共睹，特别是<span lang="EN-US">7nm Zen2/Zen3</span>架构以来，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>在桌面、移动及服务器三大市场上不断创新高，前几天<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的财报会上还说连续<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个季度增加了份额，他们即将重返<span lang="EN-US">K8</span>时代的高点，份额已经回升到<span lang="EN-US">24.6%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">调研机构<span lang="EN-US">Mercury Research</span>发布了<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年第三季度<span lang="EN-US">x86 CPU</span>市场份额报告，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>其中提到<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>在整个<span lang="EN-US">x86 CPU</span>市场上的份额达到了<span lang="EN-US">24.6%</span>，</strong></span>环比上季度增加了<span lang="EN-US">2.1</span>个百分点。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">24.6%</span>的份额差不多就是<span lang="EN-US">1/4</span>的<span lang="EN-US">x86</span>天下了，<strong><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>历史上份额最高的是<span lang="EN-US">2006</span>年<span lang="EN-US">Q4</span>季度的<span lang="EN-US">25.2%</span>份额，正好超过<span lang="EN-US">1/4</span>份额，那时候<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的<span lang="EN-US">K8</span>处理器可谓如日中天。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">按照这个速度下去，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>今年<span lang="EN-US">Q4</span>季度超过历史最高水平应该没啥悬念了，<span lang="EN-US">15</span>年来重回巅峰。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在这个<span lang="EN-US">24.6%</span>的总份额中，笔记本业务的表现尤其亮眼，市场份额为<span lang="EN-US">22.0%(</span>不包括物联网设备<span lang="EN-US">)</span>，创下<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>历史新高。同比去年增加了<span lang="EN-US">1.8</span>个百分点。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的财报，<strong><span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年第三季度中<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>笔记本业务收入也创下了<span lang="EN-US">16.2%</span>的历史新高，</strong>季度增长<span lang="EN-US">1.3</span>个百分点，年度增长<span lang="EN-US">3.9</span>个百分点。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>在<span lang="EN-US">x86</span>市场上重回<span lang="EN-US">K8</span>时代巅峰之后，<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>还会有下一代新品，也就是<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>了，这一代不论工艺还是架构，亦或者是<span lang="EN-US">DDR5/PCIe 5</span>等先进技术都会升级，<span lang="EN-US">A</span>饭可以期待<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的份额冲击<span lang="EN-US">30%</span>大关了，这才是历史性的。</p>
