<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年的<span lang="EN-US">618</span>大战马上就要到最激烈的阶段了，各大厂商这两天放出了<span lang="EN-US">618</span>当天的优惠活动。今天威刚宣布<span lang="EN-US">618</span>期间旗下的内存、<span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>硬盘、机箱、电源等产品优惠信息，评价、晒单还有好礼，最高可抽<span lang="EN-US">iPad 10.2</span>寸平板电脑。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">618</span>日凌晨<span lang="EN-US">00</span>到<span lang="EN-US">23</span>：<span lang="EN-US">59</span>，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>威刚全天活动不停，抽送的奖品除了<span lang="EN-US">iPad</span>平板之外，还有<span lang="EN-US">XPG</span>战魂背包、<span lang="EN-US">XPG</span>战魂雨伞等。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">参与<span lang="EN-US">618</span>活动的产品中，<span lang="EN-US">600W</span>铜牌电源直降<span lang="EN-US">20</span>元，秒杀<span lang="EN-US">299</span>元，<span lang="EN-US">CR650</span>金牌模组电源直降<span lang="EN-US">60</span>元，秒杀价<span lang="EN-US">599</span>元，而多款机箱秒杀价也是<span lang="EN-US">299</span>元。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">大促的主力还是威刚的内存及<span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>硬盘，与<span lang="EN-US">ROG Strix</span>联名的龙耀<span lang="EN-US">D50</span>吹雪定制版内存上新，频率<span lang="EN-US">3600MHz</span>，有<span lang="EN-US">16GBx2</span>、<span lang="EN-US">8GBx2</span>两种套装可选。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">威龙、龙耀、万紫千红系列内存也有多个好价。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在<span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>硬盘上，<strong>威刚旗下的翼龙<span lang="EN-US">SX8200 Pro 1TB</span>直降<span lang="EN-US">200</span>元，秒杀价只要<span lang="EN-US">999</span>元，</strong><span lang="EN-US">SP580</span>系列<span lang="EN-US">SATA</span>硬盘<span lang="EN-US">480GB</span>版只要<span lang="EN-US">329</span>元，<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 4.0</span>版的龙耀<span lang="EN-US">S50 Lite 512GB</span>版只要<span lang="EN-US">549</span>元。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">详细情况可以关注官方旗舰店，这两天有意升级内存、<span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>及机电的玩家可以注意下了。</p>
