<div>   
<p>6月15日晚，在2022年蔚来产品发布会上，新款ES8、ES6和EC6正式发布，三款车型均支持搭载75千瓦时和100千瓦时的电池组，且支持买断和租赁两种方式。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220615/e39187a71bd743ba81aec969fa62caf3.png" target="_blank"><img alt="国产电动车高端玩家 2022款蔚来ES8、ES6、EC6上市：最高65.6万元" h="343" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220615/s_e39187a71bd743ba81aec969fa62caf3.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>价格方面，2022款蔚来ES8共推出五款车型，<strong>整车购买的价格为49.60-59.80万元（75千瓦时）和55.40-65.60万元（100千瓦时）</strong>，如果选择电池租用方案，那么搭载75千瓦时电池组的车型在整车购买的基础上降低7万元，100千瓦时车型降低12.8万元，电池月租费用则分别为980元和1680元。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220615/f53d71fce3fc46debe5e5e9d7ad252ae.png" target="_blank"><img alt="国产电动车高端玩家 2022款蔚来ES8、ES6、EC6上市：最高65.6万元" h="343" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220615/s_f53d71fce3fc46debe5e5e9d7ad252ae.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220615/67ab37cd420b4e25aa6d7efde507dee4.png" target="_blank"><img alt="国产电动车高端玩家 2022款蔚来ES8、ES6、EC6上市：最高65.6万元" h="341" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220615/s_67ab37cd420b4e25aa6d7efde507dee4.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>值得一提的是，此次蔚来新推出了领航版ES8，其使用了专属的马德拉红配色，同时配有专属的头枕压印、B柱签名板、气辉橙卡钳等。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220615/4496fc8959b04c48bd4ae8b781360ad3.png" target="_blank"><img alt="国产电动车高端玩家 2022款蔚来ES8、ES6、EC6上市：最高65.6万元" h="352" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220615/s_4496fc8959b04c48bd4ae8b781360ad3.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220615/482c345298d247ca8414e61ee303de38.png" target="_blank"><img alt="国产电动车高端玩家 2022款蔚来ES8、ES6、EC6上市：最高65.6万元" h="323" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220615/s_482c345298d247ca8414e61ee303de38.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220615/bd79cec89a724879a3091fafd98b8ce9.png" target="_blank"><img alt="国产电动车高端玩家 2022款蔚来ES8、ES6、EC6上市：最高65.6万元" h="346" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220615/s_bd79cec89a724879a3091fafd98b8ce9.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220615/8984775cc062494a9e7774d2d998bc98.png" target="_blank"><img alt="国产电动车高端玩家 2022款蔚来ES8、ES6、EC6上市：最高65.6万元" h="331" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220615/s_8984775cc062494a9e7774d2d998bc98.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220615/3009d95a4c46457b85b233a477f54d5d.png" target="_blank"><img alt="国产电动车高端玩家 2022款蔚来ES8、ES6、EC6上市：最高65.6万元" h="352" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220615/s_3009d95a4c46457b85b233a477f54d5d.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>2022款蔚来ES6共推出三款车型，<strong>整车购买的价格为38.60-49.60万元（75千瓦时）和44.40-55.40万元（100千瓦时）</strong>，选择电池租赁方案的话价格也会分别降低7万元和12.8万元，电池租赁费用与ES8相同。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220615/3772dde631fd4bf195dd734fd9688ae2.png" target="_blank"><img alt="国产电动车高端玩家 2022款蔚来ES8、ES6、EC6上市：最高65.6万元" h="350" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220615/s_3772dde631fd4bf195dd734fd9688ae2.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220615/c5e5999df5df48619b30f8aae924ec78.png" target="_blank"><img alt="国产电动车高端玩家 2022款蔚来ES8、ES6、EC6上市：最高65.6万元" h="342" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220615/s_c5e5999df5df48619b30f8aae924ec78.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>至于轿跑SUV蔚来EC6，它的定价与ES6比较接近，其同样推出了三款车型，<strong>整车购买的话价格为39.60-49.60万元（75千瓦时）和45.40-55.40万元</strong>，租赁价格也会分别降低7万元和12.8万元，月度费用同上。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220615/f8ef994f74054af18d03922672782c19.png" target="_blank"><img alt="国产电动车高端玩家 2022款蔚来ES8、ES6、EC6上市：最高65.6万元" h="346" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220615/s_f8ef994f74054af18d03922672782c19.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220615/f29a0e68ebf44bcb8fd4a7247460300e.png" target="_blank"><img alt="国产电动车高端玩家 2022款蔚来ES8、ES6、EC6上市：最高65.6万元" h="375" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220615/s_f29a0e68ebf44bcb8fd4a7247460300e.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220615/0060d0c79e43462ea133932238f12f4d.png" target="_blank"><img alt="国产电动车高端玩家 2022款蔚来ES8、ES6、EC6上市：最高65.6万元" h="390" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220615/s_0060d0c79e43462ea133932238f12f4d.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>值得注意的是，购买2022款ES8、ES6和EC6，用户可享受终身免费质保、终身免费换电、免费专属充电桩、终身免费道路救援、终身免费异地加电和终身免费互联网。</p>
<p>与老款相比，新款ES8、ES6和EC6最大的进步在于<strong>搭载了全新的智能座舱系统alder</strong>，其采用的是高通8155芯片，开机速度提升37%、CPU/GPU速度提升100%、软件冷启动速度提升55%、跟手流畅度提升18%，同时Nomi也得到了一定提升。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220615/cdcc30de27eb474981858b9233d8b46d.png" target="_blank"><img alt="国产电动车高端玩家 2022款蔚来ES8、ES6、EC6上市：最高65.6万元" h="310" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220615/s_cdcc30de27eb474981858b9233d8b46d.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>不止如此，新车行车记录仪摄像头升级为800W，支持4K录制， 环视摄像头升级为300Wx4，车内摄像头升级为250W。</p>
<p>让老车主感到心安的是，支持付费升级全新的alder智能座舱，<strong>其中不带5G功能的需要9600元，而带有5G功能的价格为12600元</strong>。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220615/1fbc7cafb4e64fb29ae2981b3c22c5f3.png" target="_blank"><img alt="国产电动车高端玩家 2022款蔚来ES8、ES6、EC6上市：最高65.6万元" h="250" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220615/s_1fbc7cafb4e64fb29ae2981b3c22c5f3.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220615/54f215c8972943d28aaa89e44ff81c6e.png" target="_blank"><img alt="国产电动车高端玩家 2022款蔚来ES8、ES6、EC6上市：最高65.6万元" h="241" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220615/s_54f215c8972943d28aaa89e44ff81c6e.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>2022款蔚来ES8、ES6和EC6将于6月预订、8月份交付。</p>
