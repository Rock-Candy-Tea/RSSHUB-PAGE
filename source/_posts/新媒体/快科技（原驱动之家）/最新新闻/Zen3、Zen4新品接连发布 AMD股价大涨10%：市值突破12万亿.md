<p class="MsoNormal">今天凌晨，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>举行了简短但很重要的发布会，推出了多款重磅产品，包括基于<span lang="EN-US">3D V-Cache</span>堆叠缓存的升级版霄龙<span lang="EN-US">7003x</span>系列处理器、<span lang="EN-US">5nm 96</span>核<span lang="EN-US">/128</span>核<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>、<span lang="EN-US">Instinct MI250/MI250X</span>加速卡等等，全都是跟数据中心市场相关的。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>处理器上市还要等一年多，不过根据<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的信息，<span lang="EN-US">EPYC Milan-X</span>系列的处理器很快就能上市，有四款新型号，其中<span lang="EN-US">EPYC 7773X</span>为<span lang="EN-US">64</span>核<span lang="EN-US">128</span>线程，热设计功耗<span lang="EN-US">280W</span>，频率<span lang="EN-US">2.2~3.5GHz</span>，三级缓存容量达到<span lang="EN-US">768MB</span>，也就是在原有<span lang="EN-US">256MB</span>的基础上通过<span lang="EN-US">3D</span>堆叠的方式增加了<span lang="EN-US">512MB</span>，是之前的<span lang="EN-US">3</span>倍。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>称，<span lang="EN-US">3D</span>缓存部分同样是台积电<span lang="EN-US">7nm</span>。性能方面，<span lang="EN-US">Milan-X</span>在数字逻辑仿真验证中，比非<span lang="EN-US">3D</span>缓存版提高了<span lang="EN-US">66%</span>。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的<span lang="EN-US">EPYC</span>处理器也赢得了越来越多的客户，除了之前已经有合作的谷歌、微软等厂商之外，这次<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>还拿下来改名后的<span lang="EN-US">Meta</span>公司（原来的<span lang="EN-US">Facebook</span>）的订单，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>他们找<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>定制了一批<span lang="EN-US">EPYC</span>处理器，基于<span lang="EN-US">EPYC</span>第三代产品共同定义了一个开放的、云规模的、单插槽服务器，旨在提高性能和能效。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">由于<span lang="EN-US">Meta</span>公司是现在的元宇宙热点，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>拿下<span lang="EN-US">Meta</span>的订单，推动股价大涨，一度涨超<span lang="EN-US">11%</span>，收盘也涨了<span lang="EN-US">10.14%</span>，股价站上了<span lang="EN-US">150</span>美元，<strong>市值<span lang="EN-US">1813.3</span>亿美元，约合人民币<span lang="EN-US">1.2</span>万亿。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">相比<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年创下的<span lang="EN-US">1.8</span>美元低价，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>公司<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年来股价上涨了<span lang="EN-US">83</span>倍之多。</p>
