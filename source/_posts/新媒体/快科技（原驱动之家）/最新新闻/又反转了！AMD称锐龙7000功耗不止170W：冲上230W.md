<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">前几天<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>在台北电脑展上公布了锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>处理器，基于<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>架构，单核性能提升<span lang="EN-US">15%</span>，频率超过<span lang="EN-US">5GHz</span>——实际上更高，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>已经确认<span lang="EN-US">16</span>核锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>也能跑上<span lang="EN-US">5.5GHz</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">伴随着性能的大提升，锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>处理器的功耗自然也会水涨船高，官方文档中提到了<span lang="EN-US">AM5</span>平台功耗可达<span lang="EN-US">170W</span>，比之前的<span lang="EN-US">105W</span>高出了<span lang="EN-US">65W</span>之多。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/834/834539.htm" target="_blank">前两天<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>才澄清说这个<span lang="EN-US">170W</span>功耗不是<span lang="EN-US">TDP</span>功耗</a>，是<span lang="EN-US">PPT</span>功耗（<span lang="EN-US">Package Power Tracking</span>），<span lang="EN-US">TDP</span>功耗实际上是<span lang="EN-US">125W</span>，比目前增长了<span lang="EN-US">20W</span>。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="又反转了！AMD称锐龙7000功耗不止170W：冲上230W" h="297" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220526/30a4c058-fa5b-41b0-952a-a75303caed45.png" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">然而事情到现在为止还没完，<span lang="EN-US">Anandtech</span>网站主编今天又从<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>那里得到了澄清信息，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>原来<span lang="EN-US">170W</span>的功耗也不是<span lang="EN-US">PPT</span>功耗，就是<span lang="EN-US">TDP</span>功耗，真正的<span lang="EN-US">PPT</span>功耗是<span lang="EN-US">230W</span>，也就是锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>的性能释放可以达到<span lang="EN-US">230W</span>。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">作为对比，当前<span lang="EN-US">AM4</span>平台的<span lang="EN-US">TDP</span>功耗是<span lang="EN-US">105W</span>，<span lang="EN-US">PPT</span>功耗<span lang="EN-US">142W</span>，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>将<span lang="EN-US">PPT</span>上限提升了将近<span lang="EN-US">90W</span>，<strong>这大概也是锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>频率可以突破<span lang="EN-US">5.5GHz</span>的关键了</strong>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">值得一提的是，在<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿上，高端处理器的功耗也不低，基础功耗有<span lang="EN-US">125W</span>，<span lang="EN-US">Turbo</span>功耗可达<span lang="EN-US">241W</span>，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的<span lang="EN-US">230W</span>功耗已经很接近<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿了。</p>
