<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>今年推出了两代酷睿桌面版，<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月份推出的是<span lang="EN-US">11</span>代酷睿系列，还是<span lang="EN-US">14nm</span>工艺，<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月推出的是<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿系列，升级<span lang="EN-US">Intel 7</span>工艺，<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>、<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>架构也全面更新，<span lang="EN-US">IPC</span>性能大涨。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">由于只间隔了<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个月，所以<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿发布之后，<span lang="EN-US">11</span>代酷睿处理器的地位有些尴尬，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>特别是还在用<span lang="EN-US">14nm</span>工艺，<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>核心数也从<span lang="EN-US">10</span>代酷睿最多<span lang="EN-US">10</span>减少到了<span lang="EN-US">8</span>核，市场营销上不占便宜。</strong></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="Intel确认：11代酷睿桌面版的CPU是印度工程师完成的" h="304" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211224/75fcc539-a683-4ede-8bda-6f8a32416865.png" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不过<span lang="EN-US">11</span>代酷睿也是有不少优点的，首先是<span lang="EN-US">IPC</span>性能提升<span lang="EN-US">19%</span>，采用了全新的<span lang="EN-US">Cypress CPU</span>架构，<strong>它实际上是<span lang="EN-US">10nm</span>工艺的<span lang="EN-US">IceLkae</span>处理器<span lang="EN-US">SunnyCove</span>核心的同款，所以<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>这次的改变实际上很有意思，那就是将<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>与工艺解耦，<span lang="EN-US">10nm</span>工艺的<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>核心也可以用于<span lang="EN-US">14nm</span>工艺。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">过<span lang="EN-US">14nm</span>与<span lang="EN-US">10nm</span>工艺的晶体管密度差很多，导致<span lang="EN-US">14nm</span>下塞不下那么多的核心，只能从上代的<span lang="EN-US">10</span>核减少到了<span lang="EN-US">8</span>核。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">对于<span lang="EN-US">11</span>代酷睿的改变，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>高管<span lang="EN-US">Adi Yoaz</span>在采访中确认了，<span lang="EN-US">11</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Cypress Cove</span>核心的移植是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>印度团队完成的。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">似乎这一切就能说通了呢？</p>
