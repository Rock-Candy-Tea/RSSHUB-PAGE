<p class="MsoNormal">新一代国产高性能掌机来了，壹号本宣布<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日将在<span lang="EN-US">B</span>站、抖音及微信三大平台发布<span lang="EN-US">OnexPlayer mini</span>壹号掌机。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">OnexPlayer</span>壹号掌机宣布其新品<span lang="EN-US">OnexPlayer mini</span>版的发布会将于<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日<span lang="EN-US">17:00</span>开始，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>此次发布会将在<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个平台同步直播，分别是<span lang="EN-US">B</span>站（账号：<span lang="EN-US">OnexPlayer</span>壹号掌机）、抖音（账号：<span lang="EN-US">OnexPlayer</span>壹号游戏掌机）、微信视频号（账号：壹号本科技）。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211216/0d630345a6d34d45a348cc20f3522d61.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="OnexPlayer mini掌机12月20日发布：升级11代酷睿i7处理器" h="461" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211216/s_0d630345a6d34d45a348cc20f3522d61.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>据悉此次发布的<span lang="EN-US">OnexPlayer mini</span>版屏幕只有<span lang="EN-US">7</span>英寸大小，重量轻至<span lang="EN-US">589</span>克，搭载Intel第十一代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i7-1195G7</span>处理器</strong>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">除了<span lang="EN-US">7</span>英寸的<span lang="EN-US">mini</span>版掌机之外，<span lang="EN-US">OnexPlayer</span>还会发布配套硬件，来辅助用户随时随地、更畅快的使用壹号掌机，并发挥出它的全部潜能。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">OnexPlayer</span>壹号掌机自今年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日发布其首款产品以来，截止目前已迭代<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个版本：<span lang="EN-US">OnexPlayer 1S</span>和<span lang="EN-US">OnexPlayer AMD</span>版，分别搭载<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>第十一代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i7-1195G7</span>处理器、<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7 4800U</span>处理器和<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7 5700U</span>处理器。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">这些机型均为<span lang="EN-US">8.4</span>英寸的高清大屏，<span lang="EN-US">2560*1600</span>分辨率，搭载<span lang="EN-US">16GB LPDDR4x 4266</span>频率内存和<span lang="EN-US">1TB M.2</span>接口（<span lang="EN-US">NVMe</span>协议）固态硬盘，性能强大，可以畅玩<span lang="EN-US">PC 3A</span>游戏。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">据悉，此次发布的<span lang="EN-US">OnexPlayer mini</span>版延续了<span lang="EN-US">8.4</span>英寸的优点，同时也满足了部分用户追求极致便携出行的多元化需求。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此次的新产品究竟会给用户带来怎样的游戏体验，又会呈现哪些惊喜，<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日<span lang="EN-US">17:00</span>的发布会，答案即将揭晓。</p>
