<p class="MsoNormal">除了外观设计全面升级，成为<span lang="EN-US">Note</span>系列有史以来最好看的设计之外，<span lang="EN-US">Redmi Note 11</span>系列这次在屏幕上也创造了一个首次——上了<span lang="EN-US">AMOLED</span>屏，而且是三星<span lang="EN-US">AMOLED</span>高刷屏，<span lang="EN-US">120Hz+360Hz</span>触控，钻石排列。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">具体来说，<span lang="EN-US">Note 11</span>系列采用了真空屏设计，孔径只有<span lang="EN-US">2.96mm</span>，边框窄到了<span lang="EN-US">1.75mm</span>，颜值很不错。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>屏幕方面，<span lang="EN-US">Note 11 Pro</span>及以上型号用上了<span lang="EN-US">OLED</span>屏，这也是<span lang="EN-US">Note</span>系列首次使用<span lang="EN-US">OLED</span>，来自于三星的<span lang="EN-US">6.67</span>英寸<span lang="EN-US">AMOLED</span>，<span lang="EN-US">120Hz</span>高刷，<span lang="EN-US">360Hz</span>触控采样率。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">更重要的是，这块<span lang="EN-US">AMOLED</span>屏是三星钻石排列的，大家不用纠结什么排列影响显示效果了。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal">其他方面，<span lang="EN-US">Note 11</span>系列的屏幕还支持阳光屏、<span lang="EN-US">SGS</span>护眼认证，纸质护眼模式更适合阅读，<strong>同时<span lang="EN-US">Pro</span>及<span lang="EN-US">Pro+</span>两款还支持康宁第五代大猩猩保护玻璃，防摔防刮花。</strong></p>
