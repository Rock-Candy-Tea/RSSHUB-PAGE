<p class="MsoNormal">当地时间周三，全球最大的光刻机公司<span lang="EN-US">ASML</span>发布了<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">Q4</span>季度及全年财报，受益于强烈的市场需求，<span lang="EN-US">ASML</span>的光刻机出货量及营收都创纪录了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">Q4</span>季度，<span lang="EN-US">ASML</span>公司实现净销售额<span lang="EN-US">49.86</span>亿欧元，毛利率<span lang="EN-US">54.2%</span>，，净利润<span lang="EN-US">17.74</span>亿欧元，当季净订单额<span lang="EN-US">71</span>亿欧元。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">至于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年，<span lang="EN-US">ASML</span>公司实现净销售额<span lang="EN-US">186.11</span>亿欧元，较上一财年增长<span lang="EN-US">33%</span>，净利润也从<span lang="EN-US">35.54</span>亿欧元涨至<span lang="EN-US">58.83</span>亿欧元，全年净订单额达到<span lang="EN-US">262.4</span>亿欧元，较去年同期翻了一倍，需求强烈。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年中，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">ASML</span>出货了<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>光刻机总计多达<span lang="EN-US">42</span>套，<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年是<span lang="EN-US">31</span>套，实现营收<span lang="EN-US">63</span>亿美元，算下来平均每套<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>光刻机要<span lang="EN-US">1.5</span>亿欧元，折合人民币<span lang="EN-US">10.8</span>亿元。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此外，<span lang="EN-US">Q4</span>季度新增的<span lang="EN-US">71</span>亿欧元光刻机订单中，除了当前<span lang="EN-US">NA 0.33</span>的<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>光刻机之外，<strong>还首次实现<span lang="EN-US">NA 0.55</span>高数值孔径<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>光刻机的销售，<a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/810/810304.htm" target="_blank">这<span lang="EN-US">1</span>个订单是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>贡献的</a>，</strong>这是<span lang="EN-US">ASML</span>下一代光刻机，预计<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年才能正式出货，售价据说达到<span lang="EN-US">3</span>亿美元，约合<span lang="EN-US">19</span>亿人民币。</p>
