<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>日前发布了<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿处理器，最高<span lang="EN-US">16</span>核<span lang="EN-US">24</span>线程，游戏性能领先锐龙高达<span lang="EN-US">30%</span>。今晚的发布会上，联想也发布了拯救者刃<span lang="EN-US">9000K 2022</span>游戏主机，升级<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代处理器，最高可解锁<span lang="EN-US">251W PL2</span>功耗，并配备了强大的<span lang="EN-US">360</span>水冷散热器。</p>
<p class="newsinfo_vedio"><a class="mydrivers_player" href="https://weibo.com/5981912323/KEZMK1pmQ">视频</a></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/792/792044.htm" target="_blank"><span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿处理器的详细介绍可以参考之前的文章</a>，刃<span lang="EN-US">9000K 2022</span>游戏主机最高可搭配酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i9-12900K</span>，<span lang="EN-US">16</span>核<span lang="EN-US">5.2GHz</span>频率。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">供电方面，刃<span lang="EN-US">9000K 2022</span>游戏主机配备了<span lang="EN-US">12</span>相供电，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>最高可解锁<span lang="EN-US">241W PL1</span>功耗及<span lang="EN-US">251W PL2</span>功耗，而默认的<span lang="EN-US">PL1</span>、<span lang="EN-US">PL2</span>功耗不过<span lang="EN-US">125W</span>、<span lang="EN-US">241W</span>，这意味着刃<span lang="EN-US">9000K 2022</span>游戏主机可以在<span lang="EN-US">PL1</span>阶段提供近乎翻倍的功耗输出，大幅提升性能。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">为了应付这么高的功耗，散热系统也全面升级了，最高可选<span lang="EN-US">360</span>水冷，散热能力达到<span lang="EN-US">241W</span>，<span lang="EN-US">3</span>层强化结构水管，<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年无漏水故障。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据联想公布的温度实测结果，刃<span lang="EN-US">9000K 2022</span>游戏主机在《孤岛惊魂<span lang="EN-US">6</span>》游戏中机箱外壳温度不超过<span lang="EN-US">35</span>度，<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>显卡温度<span lang="EN-US">70</span>度，比上代产品低了<span lang="EN-US">7</span>度、<span lang="EN-US">9</span>度，降温明显。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">其他方面，刃<span lang="EN-US">9000K 2022</span>游戏主机还升级了<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存，频率<span lang="EN-US">4800MHz</span>，带宽<span lang="EN-US">68GB/s</span>，最高容量可达<span lang="EN-US">64GB</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">扩展方面，标配<span lang="EN-US">1TB PCIe 4.0 SSD</span>硬盘，还有<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0 x16</span>插槽、<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 4.0</span>、<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 4.0 M2</span>插槽。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">另外还有<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个<span lang="EN-US">PWM/ARGB</span>接口、<span lang="EN-US">4</span>个<span lang="EN-US">SATA</span>接口、<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个<span lang="EN-US">USB 3.2</span>、<span lang="EN-US">4</span>个<span lang="EN-US">USB 2.0</span>接口，支持<span lang="EN-US">7.1</span>声道音频及<span lang="EN-US">2.5Gbps</span>有线、<span lang="EN-US">Wi-Fi 6</span>无线等等，</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">系统方面，刃<span lang="EN-US">9000K 2022</span>游戏主机已经预装<span lang="EN-US">Windows 11</span>正版系统。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">售价方面，<strong>酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i9-12900K+64GB</span>内存<span lang="EN-US">+RTX 3080 Ti</span>版售价<span lang="EN-US">26999</span>元，首发优惠价<span lang="EN-US">24999</span>元，酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i7-12700K+32GB</span>内存<span lang="EN-US">+RTX 3080</span>版售价<span lang="EN-US">16999</span>元，首发优惠价<span lang="EN-US">15999</span>元，酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i7-12700K+16GB</span>内存<span lang="EN-US">+RTX 3070 Ti</span>版售价<span lang="EN-US">13999</span>元，首发优惠价<span lang="EN-US">12999</span>元。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此外还有刃<span lang="EN-US">9000 2022</span>版，酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i5-12600K+16GB</span>内存<span lang="EN-US">+RTX 3060 Ti</span>版售价<span lang="EN-US">10999</span>元，首发价<span lang="EN-US">9999</span>元，双<span lang="EN-US">11</span>优惠<span lang="EN-US">300</span>元，到手<span lang="EN-US">9699</span>元。</p>
