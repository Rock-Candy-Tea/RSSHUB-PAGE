<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">由于大尺寸<span lang="EN-US">LCD</span>面板涨价，国内的面板双雄京东方、<span lang="EN-US">TCL</span>今年上半年的业绩暴涨，净利都是<span lang="EN-US">7</span>倍、<span lang="EN-US">10</span>倍地增长。不过以手机面板为主的维信诺就没那么好运了，上半年亏损<span lang="EN-US">7.2</span>亿到<span lang="EN-US">8.5</span>亿。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日晚，维信诺发表了<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年半年度业绩预告，公司<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年上半年营业收入约为<span lang="EN-US">13.50</span>亿元到<span lang="EN-US">15.50</span>亿元，其中<span lang="EN-US"> OLED </span>产品销售收入为<span lang="EN-US">13</span>亿元到<span lang="EN-US">14</span>亿元，较上年同期增加<span lang="EN-US"> 80%</span>到<span lang="EN-US"> 94%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong><span style="color:#ff0000;"><span style="background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">归属于上市公司股东的净利润亏损<span lang="EN-US">72000</span>万元到<span lang="EN-US">85000</span>万元</span>，去年同期则为盈利<span lang="EN-US">9249.13</span>万元。</span></strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color: black; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">虽然业务亏损了，但维信诺提到自<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日至本公告披露日，公司与某终端公司签署的日常经营类订单金额累计达到<span lang="EN-US">105908.55</span>万元，占公司最近一期经审计主营业务收入的<span lang="EN-US">50.20%</span>。</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color: black; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">订单执行将对公司<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年度的经营状况产生积极影响，有助于提高公司的技术水平和服务能力。</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color: black; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">维信诺的表态意味着这个大客户不仅贡献了<span lang="EN-US">10</span>亿元的大单，而且会对他们的技术及服务能力带来正面影响，<strong>业界认为这个大客户就是华为，他们不仅采购量巨大，而且标准高，有利于维信诺提升技术。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color: black; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;"><span lang="EN-US">此前也有媒体报道，维信诺在2019年已进入华为供应链，<strong>华为nova 8系列产品、荣耀30 Pro和荣耀30 Pro+采用的双曲面柔性AMOELD屏幕，均由维信诺提供。</strong></span></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color: black; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">除了华为之外，维信诺与品牌客户的供货合作持续加强，近期供货了市场热销的荣耀<span lang="EN-US">50</span>系列手机，并成为荣耀主力供应商。</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color: black; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">知名研究机构<span lang="EN-US">DSCC </span>首席执行官<span lang="EN-US"> Ross Young </span>指出，荣耀正在研发该品牌首款可折叠手机，也将使用维信诺的显示面板。此外，维信诺已实现成功供货中兴、努比亚、小米、联想等多家品牌客户主力机型。</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color: black; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">在智能可穿戴消费电子领域，维信诺与<span lang="EN-US">OPPO</span>、小米、努比亚、华米等终端合作密切，在细分市场优势明显。</span></p>
