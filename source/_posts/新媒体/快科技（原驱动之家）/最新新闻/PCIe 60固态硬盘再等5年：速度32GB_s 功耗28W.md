<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>最近发布的<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿首发支持了<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>，<span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>的性能可以从<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 4.0</span>的最高<span lang="EN-US">8GB/s</span>提升到<span lang="EN-US">16GB/s</span>，实际在<span lang="EN-US">14-15GB/s</span>，首次超过<span lang="EN-US">10GB/s</span>大关，已经是飞一般的感觉了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">再往后呢？<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 6.0</span>标准也在路上了，<span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>硬盘也得跟着升级，群联前不久提到他们已经在研发<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 6.0</span>标准的<span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>硬盘主控了，不过目前还没有公布什么技术细节。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">PCIe 6.0</span>的标准预计今年底完成，根据<span lang="EN-US">PCI-SIG</span>组织的信息，它的传输数据率最高<span lang="EN-US">64GT/s</span>，<span lang="EN-US">x16</span>带宽（双向）可达<span lang="EN-US">256GB/s</span>，两倍于<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="PCIe 6.0固态硬盘再等5年：速度32GB/s 功耗28W" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211103/4e30d072-19da-43cc-b242-d3c11579ec36.png" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>对于<span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>硬盘来说，使用的是<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 6.0 x4</span>，单向带宽依然有<span lang="EN-US">32GB/s</span>，同样比<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>硬盘翻倍，这个带宽快跟一些低端<span lang="EN-US">PC</span>内存有得一拼了。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不过对<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 6.0</span>硬盘来说，性能再次翻倍不是问题，最大的考验来自于散热，现在<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>的硬盘功耗已经上升到<span lang="EN-US">14W</span>，<strong><span lang="EN-US">PCIe 6.0</span>硬盘要直奔<span lang="EN-US">28W</span>，已经远远超过了被动散热的要求，</strong>甚至主动散热的要求也会相当高，毕竟<span lang="EN-US">28W</span>的功耗比得上部分<span lang="EN-US">CPU/GPU</span>芯片了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">至于<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 6.0 SSD</span>硬盘的问世时间，最快也要到<span lang="EN-US">2025-2026</span>年了，差不多还有<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年时间，希望这期间厂商能研发出更高效的散热技术，否则<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 6.0</span>硬盘会是机箱中另一个发热源。</p>
