<div>   
<p>北京时间12月23日，由华为和赛力斯联手打造的AITO品牌首款车型问界M5正式亮相，<strong>华为消费者业务CEO、常务董事余承东在介绍该车型时表示，</strong><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>新车是按照百万级豪车的标准打造而来的</strong></span>。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211223/cebae929f90a40618fb5a315d23d2500.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="华为余承东：AITO问界M5设计标准看齐百万级豪车" h="313" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211223/s_cebae929f90a40618fb5a315d23d2500.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>不同于赛力斯SF5，问界M5是华为全程参与设计的一款全新车型，长宽高分别为4770*1930*1625mm，轴距2880mm，定位于中型SUV。</p>
<p><strong>外观经过了重新设计，造型流畅自然，拥有0.32Cd的低风阻系数，比保时捷Macan（0.36Cd）还要低</strong>。同时共提供有松霜绿、冰晶灰、陶瓷白、鎏金黑和碧海蓝共五种车漆，以满足不同消费需求。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211223/ef87e0fa83f14cfb863cdf3d6ffca427.png" target="_blank"><img alt="华为余承东：AITO问界M5设计标准看齐百万级豪车" h="295" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211223/s_ef87e0fa83f14cfb863cdf3d6ffca427.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211223/c2f7237931644c6fa4082652a0b26742.png" target="_blank"><img alt="华为余承东：AITO问界M5设计标准看齐百万级豪车" h="294" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211223/s_c2f7237931644c6fa4082652a0b26742.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>问界M5是首款搭载了华为Harmony OS智能座舱车型，内部采用了环抱式设计，<strong>并配有15.6英寸中控屏、Nappa真皮座椅、HUD抬头显示、高级晶钻档把、华为SOUND 19单元声学设计和女王补妆镜</strong>等。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211223/fa0b1a386de24dec9c99a2eadbb30ac7.png" target="_blank"><img alt="华为余承东：AITO问界M5设计标准看齐百万级豪车" h="202" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211223/s_fa0b1a386de24dec9c99a2eadbb30ac7.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211223/c86a5d34887a453c914860d4fee5bb5c.png" target="_blank"><img alt="华为余承东：AITO问界M5设计标准看齐百万级豪车" h="201" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211223/s_c86a5d34887a453c914860d4fee5bb5c.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>不仅如此，新车还配有前后排双层加胶隔音玻璃，以及业界最快车载充电，可实现66W有线和44W无线充电，满足多种设备的充能需求。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211223/e3f3369aa4c44c6f83c06b7b280b309a.png" target="_blank"><img alt="华为余承东：AITO问界M5设计标准看齐百万级豪车" h="245" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211223/s_e3f3369aa4c44c6f83c06b7b280b309a.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p><strong>新车由HUAWEI DriveONE纯电驱增程平台打造</strong>，<strong>采用了前双叉臂、后多连杆悬架以及全铝合金底盘，簧下质量降低了30%，拥有良好的操控性，麋鹿测试达到75公里/时，仅次于保时捷Macan（76公里/时）</strong>。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211223/2cb9ed96e1284c5bb9b32ba00887c1ba.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="华为余承东：AITO问界M5设计标准看齐百万级豪车" h="317" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211223/s_2cb9ed96e1284c5bb9b32ba00887c1ba.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211223/d44b24d6d77c4ca3afa0c4c0089ffd6a.png" target="_blank"><img alt="华为余承东：AITO问界M5设计标准看齐百万级豪车" h="349" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211223/s_d44b24d6d77c4ca3afa0c4c0089ffd6a.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211223/053f3e35ffe941178e904089e04c2b0a.png" target="_blank"><img alt="华为余承东：AITO问界M5设计标准看齐百万级豪车" h="279" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211223/s_053f3e35ffe941178e904089e04c2b0a.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>动力方面，问界M5同样向百万豪车看齐，配备了1.5T四缸增程器3.0，热效率高达41%，<strong>0-100公里/时加速仅需4.4秒，满油满电综合续航里程达到了1195公里</strong>。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211223/61b0ff075829431abef0c8682389a9f0.png" target="_blank"><img alt="华为余承东：AITO问界M5设计标准看齐百万级豪车" h="276" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211223/s_61b0ff075829431abef0c8682389a9f0.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
