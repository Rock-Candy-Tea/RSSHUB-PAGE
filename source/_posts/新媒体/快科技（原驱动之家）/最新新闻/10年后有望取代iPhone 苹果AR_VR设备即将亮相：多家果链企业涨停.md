<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日，受苹果开发者大会召开，以及苹果<span lang="EN-US">AR/VR</span>操作系统曝光等消息的影响，苹果产业链概念股今日领涨两市，歌尔股份、东山精密涨停，蓝思科技、立讯精密涨超<span lang="EN-US">8%</span>，消费电子、芯片产业概念股全线拉升。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">日前有报道称，苹果的<span lang="EN-US">realityOS</span>商标被发现已经完成注册，而注册公司为<span lang="EN-US">“Realityo Systems LLC”</span>，该公司地址和<span lang="EN-US">macOS California</span>发布前使用的地址相同。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">realityOS</span>是一款针对<span lang="EN-US">AR/VR</span>设备的操作系统，即在苹果<span lang="EN-US">AR/VR</span>设备搭载。该商标申请日期都是<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，申请截止日期有<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日和<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日两个。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">苹果<span lang="EN-US">WWDC22(</span>苹果全球开发者大会<span lang="EN-US">)</span>将于<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日举行，而该商标的结束日期正是<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>这意味着苹果很有可能会在<span lang="EN-US">WWDC22</span>上发布关于这款设备的消息。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AR/VR</span>头显是苹果公司寄予厚望的一款产品。依据苹果分析师郭明琪研报，<span lang="EN-US">AR/VR</span>是苹果公司非常重要的战略产品，<strong>苹果<span lang="EN-US">CEO</span>库克希望<span lang="EN-US">AR</span>在<span lang="EN-US">10</span>年后彻底取代<span lang="EN-US">iPhone</span>。</strong></p>
