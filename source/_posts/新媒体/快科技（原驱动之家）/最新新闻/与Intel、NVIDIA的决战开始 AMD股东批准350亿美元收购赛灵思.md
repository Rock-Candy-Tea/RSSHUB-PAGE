<p class="MsoNormal">日前<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>股东、赛灵思的股东以压倒性优势双双批准了<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>收购赛灵思的交易，预计今年内完成，尚需完成全球监管部门的审批。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，<a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/720/720496.htm" target="_blank"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>宣布斥资<span lang="EN-US">350</span>亿美元（约合<span lang="EN-US">2350</span>亿元）收购<span lang="EN-US">FPGA</span>大厂赛灵思，</a>这次的交易没有现金，将全部使用<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的股票。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">合并后，苏姿丰博士将继续担任<span lang="EN-US">CEO</span>，赛灵思<span lang="EN-US">CEO Victor Peng</span>将加入新公司担任总裁，届时，至少两名赛灵思高管也将加入<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>管理团队。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">据悉，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>新公司将拥有多达<span lang="EN-US">1.3</span>万名工程技术人员，合并年研发投入<span lang="EN-US">27</span>亿美元。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>巨资收购赛灵思被认为是打造一个能与<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>、<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>相比的数据中心处理器业务，<strong>此前<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>公司<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年花费<span lang="EN-US">167</span>亿美元收购了<span lang="EN-US">Altera</span>，<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>则宣布<span lang="EN-US">400</span>亿美元收购了<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>公司。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">对<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>来说，旗下拥有先进的<span lang="EN-US">x86 CPU</span>及<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>业务，缺少的是<span lang="EN-US">FPGA</span>这样的芯片，整合赛灵思可以优势互补。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">近年来<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>在桌面、笔记本市场上不断扩展市场，但在数据中心市场上，<strong><span lang="EN-US">EPYC</span>霄龙的份额去年底才突破<span lang="EN-US">7%</span></strong>，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的至强<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>依然占据<span lang="EN-US">90%</span>以上的份额，任重道远。</p>
