<p class="MsoNormal">近年来，国产智能手机进步很快，国内市场上除了苹果占据部分高端市场之外，华为、小米、<span lang="EN-US">OPPO</span>、<span lang="EN-US">vivo</span>四大品牌几乎占了其他市场。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在今天召开的“华为开发者大会<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>（<span lang="EN-US">Cloud)</span>」”大会上，<span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">华为消费者业务软件部副总裁杨海松</span>也谈到了国产手机份额与利润的问题。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">杨海松表示，</span><span style="color:#ff0000;"><span style="background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;"><strong>中国手机厂家利润低的可怜，国产手机市场份额过半但仅仅获得</strong></span><strong><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;"> 10% </span><span style="background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">左右的利润。</span></strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">杨海松提到，在智能手机的销售中，苹果的市场份额为</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;"> 13%</span><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">，但所获利润达</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;"> 66%</span><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">，</span><span style="background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;"><strong>而中国及其他厂商的市场份额高达</strong></span><strong><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;"> 67%</span><span style="background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">，所获利润仅有</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;"> 17%</span><span style="background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">。</span></strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此前华为发布了2020年财报，2020年华为全球销售收入8914亿元，同比增长3.8%；净利润646亿元，同比增长3.2%；经营活动现金流352亿元，基本符合预期。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">从这个数据来看，华为的净利润率只有7%，华为手机的利润率没有详细数据，但看整体情况应该也不会高，在利润方面，国产厂商确实离苹果差很远，后者的净利润可达20%，iPhone手机的毛利率一直都是以卖一台赚一台著称。</p>
