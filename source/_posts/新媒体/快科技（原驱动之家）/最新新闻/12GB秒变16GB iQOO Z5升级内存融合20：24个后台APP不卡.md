<p class="MsoNormal">苹果<span lang="EN-US">iPhone 13</span>系列今年的内存容量也没变，依然最多<span lang="EN-US">6GB</span>，相比之下安卓千元机都上<span lang="EN-US">8GB</span>甚至<span lang="EN-US">12GB</span>内存了。今天下午的发布会上，<span lang="EN-US">iQOO</span>推出了<span lang="EN-US">iQOO Z5</span>手机，支持内存融合<span lang="EN-US">2.0</span>技术，<span lang="EN-US">12GB</span>内存秒变<span lang="EN-US">16GB</span>内存。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">内存融合技术已经不算什么新技术了，今年上半年<span lang="EN-US">iQOO Z3</span>发布时也支持了内存融合技术，不过当时是增加<span lang="EN-US">3GB</span>空间，<span lang="EN-US">8GB</span>秒变<span lang="EN-US">11GB</span>，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>现在的内存融合<span lang="EN-US">2.0</span>技术是<span lang="EN-US">+4GB</span>，<span lang="EN-US">8GB</span>变<span lang="EN-US">12GB</span>，<span lang="EN-US">12GB</span>变<span lang="EN-US">16GB</span>内存。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据<span lang="EN-US">iQOO</span>的介绍，<span lang="EN-US">8GB</span>内存变<span lang="EN-US">12GB</span>内存之后，后台<span lang="EN-US">APP</span>可同时保持<span lang="EN-US">18</span>个，<strong><span lang="EN-US">12GB</span>秒变<span lang="EN-US">16GB</span>内存之后，后台<span lang="EN-US">APP</span>可同时保持<span lang="EN-US">24</span>个，提升了多任务运行能力和<span lang="EN-US">APP</span>启动效率。</strong></p>
