<p class="MsoNormal">按照现在的计划，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>明年推出<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>处理器已经没跑了，支持<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>及<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>，技术升级很大。不过在下一代的<span lang="EN-US">Zen5</span>处理器还会有什么变化？<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>最新表态或许能从中管窥一斑。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="3nm工艺+大小核架构 AMD Zen5处理器还将探讨支持专用AI单元" h="235" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211013/a4c5c19d-0403-4b08-b90d-55d966a85242.jpg" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在日前的锐龙处理器五周年纪念视频采访中，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>首席营销官<span lang="EN-US">John Taylor </span>和技术营销总监<span lang="EN-US">Robert Hallock</span>也提到了未来的<span lang="EN-US">Zen</span>处理器架构方面的变化，除了传统的<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>架构改进之外，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>还在考虑加入固定单元的可能性。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">这个固定单元主要是给<span lang="EN-US">AI</span>人工智能、<span lang="EN-US">DL</span>深度学习加速的，在这方面其实也不新鲜了，友商<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>早在<span lang="EN-US">10</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Ice Lake</span>上就集成<span lang="EN-US">AI</span>单元了，现在的<span lang="EN-US">Tiger Lake</span>、桌面版的<span lang="EN-US">Rocket Lake</span>也有<span lang="EN-US">AI</span>加速单元，<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Alder Lake</span>中也会再次改进提升<span lang="EN-US">AI</span>单元。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">除了这一设计之外，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的<span lang="EN-US">Zen5</span>改变还是很多的，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>此前爆料称它会使用台积电的<span lang="EN-US">3nm</span>工艺，而且<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>架构也会引入<span lang="EN-US">big.LITTLE</span>大小核架构，<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>、<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>之类的技术也没跑了，预计会在<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年量产，隶属于锐龙<span lang="EN-US">8000</span>系列。</strong></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center"><img alt="3nm工艺+大小核架构 AMD Zen5处理器还将探讨支持专用AI单元" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211013/3a89cda3-db69-4925-984c-23cb08489352.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
