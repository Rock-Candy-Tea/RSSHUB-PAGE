<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">今天凌晨，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>发布了<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">Q1</span>季度财报，实现营收<span lang="EN-US">58.9</span>亿美元（预期<span lang="EN-US">55.2</span>亿美元），同比上升<span lang="EN-US">71%</span>，这也是公司历史上首个营收超过<span lang="EN-US">50</span>亿美元的季度。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">本季度营收中包括了收购赛灵思之后的部分营收，后者贡献了大约<span lang="EN-US">5.6</span>亿美元，不过排除这部分营收之后，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>整体业绩依然是大涨的，也超过了<span lang="EN-US">50</span>亿美元。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">利润方面，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>毛利润<span lang="EN-US">28.2</span>亿美元，同比大涨<span lang="EN-US">78%</span>，环比也增长了<span lang="EN-US">16%</span>，毛利率<span lang="EN-US">48%</span>，同比增长了<span lang="EN-US">2</span>个百分点，环比下跌<span lang="EN-US">2.4</span>个百分点，而净利润达到了<span lang="EN-US">7.86</span>亿美元，同比增长<span lang="EN-US">42%</span>，不过环比下滑了<span lang="EN-US">19%</span>。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的两大业务部门中，计算和图形部门贡献了<span lang="EN-US">28</span>亿美元营收，同比大涨<span lang="EN-US">33%</span>，环比也增长了<span lang="EN-US">8%</span>，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>表示锐龙<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>及<span lang="EN-US">Radeon</span>显卡表现强劲，<span lang="EN-US">ASP</span>均价继续提升，运营利润也达到了创纪录的<span lang="EN-US">7.23</span>亿美元，去年同期是<span lang="EN-US">4.85</span>亿美元。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">企业端、嵌入式暨半客制化部门贡献了<span lang="EN-US">25</span>亿美元营收，同比大涨<span lang="EN-US">88%</span>，除了自家的<span lang="EN-US">EPYC</span>服务器芯片之外，<span lang="EN-US">PS5</span>、<span lang="EN-US">Xbox</span>及<span lang="EN-US">Steam Deck</span>也贡献了两位数的增长。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此外，除了表现强劲的<span lang="EN-US">Q1</span>季度之外，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>对<span lang="EN-US">Q2</span>及全年的预期也提升了，<span lang="EN-US">Q2</span>预计营收将提升到<span lang="EN-US">65</span>亿美元，全年营收将从之前预计的<span lang="EN-US">215</span>亿美元大涨到<span lang="EN-US">263</span>亿美元，同时毛利率高达<span lang="EN-US">54%</span>。</p>
