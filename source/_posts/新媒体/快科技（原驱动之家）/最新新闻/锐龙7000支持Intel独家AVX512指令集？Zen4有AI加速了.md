<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>今天发布了锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>系列处理器，秋季上市，<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>升级<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>，<span lang="EN-US">IO</span>核心升级<span lang="EN-US">6nm</span>，还集成了<span lang="EN-US">RDNA2</span>架构的核显，支持<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>及<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>等新技术。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">有关锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>的新特性也在不断被挖掘，比如新的指令集，<strong>在发布会上<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>提到<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>会增加一些与<span lang="EN-US">AI</span>有关的加速指令集，</strong>不过<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>并没有提及细节。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">消费级<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>中增加<span lang="EN-US">AI</span>指令是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>在<span lang="EN-US">10</span>代酷睿处理器首次实现的，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>目前的新一代酷睿都有类似的功能，这些指令是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的<span lang="EN-US">AVX512</span>指令集中的一部分，可以提升<span lang="EN-US">AI</span>性能，增加个三五倍没有问题。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>支持的<span lang="EN-US">AI</span>指令应该也是差不多，甚至就是<span lang="EN-US">AVX512</span>中的，此前早有传闻称<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>会支持<span lang="EN-US">AVX512</span>了，不过消费级的<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>不一定是完整支持，支持其中部分加速指令集就可以。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">预计用于服务器市场的<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>架构<span lang="EN-US">EPYC</span>处理器才会支持更完整的<span lang="EN-US">AVX512</span>指令集，毕竟目前<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的<span lang="EN-US">AVX</span>指令集支持才到<span lang="EN-US">AVX2</span>，需要填补空白了。</p>
