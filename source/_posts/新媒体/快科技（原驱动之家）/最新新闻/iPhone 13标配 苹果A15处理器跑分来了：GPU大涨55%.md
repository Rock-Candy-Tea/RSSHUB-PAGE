<p class="MsoNormal">昨天凌晨苹果发布了<span lang="EN-US">iPhone 13</span>系列手机及<span lang="EN-US">iPad mini</span>平板，<a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/783/783398.htm" target="_blank">处理器升级为<span lang="EN-US">A15</span></a>，只不过<span lang="EN-US">Pro</span>版才是满血<span lang="EN-US">5</span>核<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>，其他型号的<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>阉割到了<span lang="EN-US">4</span>核<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">对于<span lang="EN-US">A15</span>的性能，苹果这次的描述非常让人迷茫，发布会现场对比的都是所谓安卓最强对手，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>号称<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>对比竞品性能领先<span lang="EN-US">50</span>％，<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>号称性能领先竞品<span lang="EN-US">30</span>％，但没有细节信息。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">好在在<span lang="EN-US">iPad mini 6</span>的介绍页面，苹果给出了<span lang="EN-US">A15</span>对比<span lang="EN-US">A12</span>（上一代<span lang="EN-US">iPad mini 5</span>）的性能对比数据，<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>提升<span lang="EN-US">40%</span>、<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>提升<span lang="EN-US">80%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">那对比<span lang="EN-US">iPhone 12</span>系列的<span lang="EN-US">A14</span>处理器有多大提升呢？今天已经有<span lang="EN-US">GeekBench</span>的的测试结果了，<strong>跑分的是<span lang="EN-US">iPhone 13 Pro</span>，但只有<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>单独跑分，其<span lang="EN-US">Metal</span>分数为<span lang="EN-US">14216</span>，与<span style="font-family:"MS Gothic";mso-bidi-font-family:"MS Gothic"">‌</span><span lang="EN-US">iPhone 12 Pro</span><span style="font-family:"MS Gothic";mso-bidi-font-family:
"MS Gothic"">‌</span>的<span lang="EN-US">9123</span>分相比提高了<span lang="EN-US">55%</span>。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">结合苹果的宣传，<span lang="EN-US">A15</span>处理器的<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>性能提升确实还是蛮多的，<strong>这方面没有挤牙膏，毕竟<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>还可以继续堆料。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">至于<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>性能，从苹果遮遮掩掩的表现及各大媒体的分析来看，相比<span lang="EN-US">A14</span>基本上没有提升，毕竟核心数及工艺基本没变化，苹果也有架构神优化了。</p>
