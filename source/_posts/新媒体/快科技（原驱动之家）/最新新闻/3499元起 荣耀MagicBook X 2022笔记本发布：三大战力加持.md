<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日晚，荣耀发布了<span lang="EN-US">MagicBook X 2022</span>笔记本，<a class="f14_link" href="https://www.hihonor.com/cn/laptops/honor-magicbook-x15-2022/" target="_blank">这是荣耀轻薄全金属笔记本的最新版</a>，有<span lang="EN-US">MagicBook X 14</span>及<span lang="EN-US">MagicBook X 15</span>两个系列，首发售价<span lang="EN-US">3499</span>元起。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong><span lang="EN-US">MagicBook X 2022</span>笔记本号称有三大战力加持，首先就是金属机身，采用高强度铝合金材质，</strong><span lang="EN-US">A</span>面则是金属喷砂工艺，亲肤质感，厚度低至<span lang="EN-US">15.9mm</span>，重量低至<span lang="EN-US">1.38</span>千克，便携性不错。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">其次就是护眼全面屏，有<span lang="EN-US">14</span>及<span lang="EN-US">15.6</span>寸两个版本，三边窄边框设计，分辨率<span lang="EN-US">1920x1080</span>，<span lang="EN-US">IPS</span>面板，通过了德国莱茵低蓝光及低频闪双重护眼认证。</p>


<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220420/5d683a4de24a46738b4d78e33859b0f8.png" target="_blank"><img alt="3499元起 荣耀MagicBook X 2022笔记本发布：三大战力加持" h="393" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220420/s_5d683a4de24a46738b4d78e33859b0f8.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>第三个战力就是荣耀的多屏协同了，这也是<span lang="EN-US">MagicBook</span>一直以来的特色，最新版支持<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个窗口并行，文件可以在手机与<span lang="EN-US">PC</span>上互相拖拽，可以跨设备编辑文件等等。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">其他方面，<span lang="EN-US">MagicBook X 2022</span>笔记本升级了<span lang="EN-US">11</span>代酷睿处理器，最高<span lang="EN-US">16GB DDR4</span>双通道内存及<span lang="EN-US">512GB SSD</span>硬盘，<span lang="EN-US">Xe</span>核显，支持<span lang="EN-US">FN+P</span>高性能模式，双热管散热。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此外，<strong><span lang="EN-US">MagicBook X 2022</span>笔记本还配备了指纹识别开机键、隐藏式摄像头及<span lang="EN-US">65W</span>便携充电器，支持多设备充电，搭配<span lang="EN-US">Win11</span>系统。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">售价方面，<span lang="EN-US">MagicBook X 14</span>的<span lang="EN-US">i5+16GB+512GB</span>版售价<span lang="EN-US">4299</span>元，首发价<span lang="EN-US">3999</span>元。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">MagicBook X 15</span>的<span lang="EN-US">i5+8GB+512GB</span>版售价<span lang="EN-US">3899</span>元，首发价<span lang="EN-US">3499</span>元，<span lang="EN-US">MagicBook X 15</span>的<span lang="EN-US">i5+16GB+512GB</span>版售价<span lang="EN-US">4399</span>元，首发价<span lang="EN-US">4099</span>元，现已预售，<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日正式开卖。</p>
