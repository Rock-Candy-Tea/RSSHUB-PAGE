<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">自从<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿首次在<span lang="EN-US">x86</span>桌面平台引入异构<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>之后，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>在<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>核战中已经处于不败之地，<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿是<span lang="EN-US">8</span>大<span lang="EN-US">+8</span>小，今年的<span lang="EN-US">13</span>代酷睿则会升级为<span lang="EN-US">8</span>大<span lang="EN-US">+16</span>小，<span lang="EN-US">14</span>代酷睿也是<span lang="EN-US">8+16</span>，直到<span lang="EN-US">15</span>代酷睿再次升级为<span lang="EN-US">8+32</span>，直接堆上<span lang="EN-US">40</span>核<span lang="EN-US">48</span>线程。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>代<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>路线图计划，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">15</span>代酷睿代号<span lang="EN-US">Arrow Lake</span>，预计在<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年问世，制程工艺除了自家的<span lang="EN-US">20A</span>之外，还有外部的<span lang="EN-US">N3</span>工艺，后者应该就是台积电<span lang="EN-US">3nm</span>，只要用于制造<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>模块，号称性能媲美独显。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">15</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Arrow Lake</span>的<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>部分使用<span lang="EN-US">20A</span>工艺，这是首款埃米级<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>工艺，支持<span lang="EN-US">RibbonFET</span>和<span lang="EN-US">PowerVia</span>这两项技术，每瓦性能上实现约<span lang="EN-US">15%</span>的提升。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="对决AMD Zen6 Intel 15代酷睿继续“核战”：8+32核来了" h="338" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220606/adb81f37-eec1-46c3-becd-6ac7e9ad5b71.jpg" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">由于时间还早，<span lang="EN-US">Arrow Lake</span>的具体规格还没啥确定消息，不过<span lang="EN-US">MLD</span>的爆料中称其微内核为<span lang="EN-US">Lion Cove</span>，<span lang="EN-US">IPC</span>性能相比<span lang="EN-US">Golden Cove</span>有双位数的提升，也就是超过<span lang="EN-US">10%</span>以上。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此外，<span lang="EN-US">Arrow Lake</span>使用的插槽也是<span lang="EN-US">LGA2551</span>，跟明年的<span lang="EN-US">14</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Meteor Lake</span>兼容。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">最后，<span lang="EN-US">Arrow Lake</span>处理器在<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年问世时，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>那边应该会到<span lang="EN-US">Zen 6</span>架构了，主要对手也是它。</p>
