<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>明年发布的<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>处理器继续堆料是没跑了，除了升级<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>工艺及<span lang="EN-US">96</span>核之外，内存系统也升级了，不仅支持<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存，通道数也提升到了<span lang="EN-US">12</span>通道，容量有望冲上<span lang="EN-US">12TB</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的<span lang="EN-US">Zen3</span>架构服务器芯片<span lang="EN-US">EPYC</span>现在支持的是<span lang="EN-US">8</span>通道<span lang="EN-US">DDR4-3200</span>，最高容量<span lang="EN-US">4TB</span>，而<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>也会上马<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存，根据<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>最新发布的<span lang="EN-US">EDAC</span>驱动补丁泄露的消息，<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>会支持<span lang="EN-US">12</span>通道内存，这一点就提升了<span lang="EN-US">50%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">再加上<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存频率的提升，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>这一升级首先带来了内存带宽的大幅提升，从<span lang="EN-US">Zen3</span>的<span lang="EN-US">204.8GB/s</span>提升到了<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>的<span lang="EN-US">460.8GB/s</span>。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此外，内存的容量也增加，一方面是通道数增加<span lang="EN-US">50%</span>，一方面就是<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存单条可以轻松做到<span lang="EN-US">512GB</span>，<span lang="EN-US">12</span>通道<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存容量可达<span lang="EN-US">6TB</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">但这还不是最高容量，考虑到单条还能做到<span lang="EN-US">768GB</span>以及每通道<span lang="EN-US">2</span>个内存，<strong><span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>的内存容量很容易冲上<span lang="EN-US">12TB</span>——这要比很多人的硬盘容量都高了。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">总之，随着<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>支持<span lang="EN-US">12</span>通道<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存，下代<span lang="EN-US">EPYC</span>服务器芯片可以轻松实现带宽及容量翻倍的规则，配合<span lang="EN-US">96</span>核<span lang="EN-US">192</span>线程处理器，性能再上一个台阶。</p>
