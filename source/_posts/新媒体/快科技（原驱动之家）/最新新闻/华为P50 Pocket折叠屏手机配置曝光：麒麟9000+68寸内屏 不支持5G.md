<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">华为已经官宣在<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日的冬季旗舰新品发布会上推出首款纵向形态折叠屏手机——华为<span lang="EN-US">P50 Pocket，关晓彤代言</span>，外观已经揭晓，现在硬件配置也曝光了，还是熟悉的麒麟<span lang="EN-US">9000</span>，依然没有<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">从定位上来看，<span lang="EN-US">P50 Pocket</span>不会是一款追求性能的折叠屏手机，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>不过整体配置依然很不错，处理器是麒麟<span lang="EN-US">9000</span>，但只支持<span lang="EN-US">4G</span>网络，现在还没有<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">屏幕方面，内屏是<span lang="EN-US">6.8</span>寸，外屏有个<span lang="EN-US">1</span>寸圆形副屏，可以当自拍取景器，还可以展示来电信息、时间、天气等信息。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211218/dd506a13-a8b7-45cc-a88d-3e6ab8ca3e2f.jpg" style="text-align: center;" target="_blank"><img alt="华为P50 Pocket折叠屏手机配置曝光：麒麟9000+6.8寸内屏 不支持5G" h="450" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211218/Sdd506a13-a8b7-45cc-a88d-3e6ab8ca3e2f.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>电池是<span lang="EN-US">4100mAh</span>容量，搭配<span lang="EN-US">66W</span>快充。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">拍照方面，<span lang="EN-US">P50 Pocket</span>后置三摄，主摄是<span lang="EN-US">IMX766</span>传感器，<span lang="EN-US">5000</span>万像素，还有<span lang="EN-US">1300</span>万像素超广及<span lang="EN-US">800</span>万像素长焦，支持<span lang="EN-US">3x</span>光学变焦。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">华为<span lang="EN-US">P50 Pocket</span>采用上下翻折设计，机身后盖拥有立体菱形花纹，机身背部为双圆环设计，分别为相机和圆形背屏，其中副屏可当做自拍取景器，整机极具辨识度，堪称是华为美学设计天花板。</p>
