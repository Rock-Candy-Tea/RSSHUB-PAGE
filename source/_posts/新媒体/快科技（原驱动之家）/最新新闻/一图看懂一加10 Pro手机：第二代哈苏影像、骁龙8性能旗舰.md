<p class="MsoNormal">今天下午，一加公司发布了一加<span lang="EN-US">10 Pro</span>手机，这是今年又一款骁龙<span lang="EN-US">8</span>旗舰机，也是一加公司第十代性能旗舰，除了升级骁龙<span lang="EN-US">8</span>处理器之外，还带来了第二代哈苏手机影像系统，<span lang="EN-US">2K+LTPO 2.0</span>高刷屏，售价<span lang="EN-US">4699</span>元起。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">硬件方面，<strong>一加<span lang="EN-US">10 Pro</span>自然是搭载了目前安卓最强的新一代骁龙<span lang="EN-US">8</span>旗舰平台，搭配<span lang="EN-US">+LPDDR5+UFS3.1</span>超速闪存，</strong>组合成目前最强的性能铁三角，还有一加的<span lang="EN-US">HyperBoost</span>全链路游戏稳帧技术，号称<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>效率提升<span lang="EN-US">36%</span>，单局游戏功耗降低<span lang="EN-US">5%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">屏幕方面，一加<span lang="EN-US">10 Pro</span>采用了三星顶级屏幕，拥有<span lang="EN-US">2K+</span>超清分辨率、<span lang="EN-US">120Hz LTPO</span>自适应屏幕刷新率、原生<span lang="EN-US">10-bit</span>色彩、<span lang="EN-US">1300</span>尼特峰值、<span lang="EN-US">HDR10+</span>认证，以及<span lang="EN-US">1000Hz</span>极限触控采样率（部分游戏场景下）。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220111/77acb0ac-b213-4e62-a422-655dccc93a7a.jpg" style="text-align: center;" target="_blank"><img alt="一图看懂一加10 Pro手机：第二代哈苏影像、骁龙8性能旗舰" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220111/S77acb0ac-b213-4e62-a422-655dccc93a7a.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">电池方面，一加<span lang="EN-US">10 Pro</span>支持<span lang="EN-US">SUPERVOOC 80W</span>有线超级闪充和<span lang="EN-US">AIRVOOC 50W</span>无线超级闪充，<span lang="EN-US">5000mAh</span>超大电池可以在<span lang="EN-US">32</span>分钟之内充到<span lang="EN-US">100%</span>，无线的情况下也可以在<span lang="EN-US">47</span>分钟之内充电<span lang="EN-US">100%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">影像方面，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>一加<span lang="EN-US">10 Pro</span>与哈苏合作打造了的第二代手机影像系统哈苏影像<span lang="EN-US">2.0</span>，后置三摄系统，</strong></span>规格分别为<span lang="EN-US">4800</span>万像素主摄<span lang="EN-US">+5000</span>万像素超广角<span lang="EN-US">+3.3</span>倍长焦镜头。</p>


<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220111/8c4e17f324ff4ff0bc69a04be24e9084.png" target="_blank"><img alt="一图看懂一加10 Pro手机：第二代哈苏影像、骁龙8性能旗舰" h="353" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220111/s_8c4e17f324ff4ff0bc69a04be24e9084.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">售价方面，一加<span lang="EN-US">10 Pro</span>拥有两种配色可选，分别是破界黑和万物青，提供三种存储版本，其中<span lang="EN-US">8GB+128GB</span>售价<span lang="EN-US">4699</span>、<span lang="EN-US">8GB+256GB</span>售价<span lang="EN-US">4999</span>、<span lang="EN-US">12GB+256GB</span>售价<span lang="EN-US">5299</span>，将于<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日上午<span lang="EN-US">10</span>点正式开售。</p>
