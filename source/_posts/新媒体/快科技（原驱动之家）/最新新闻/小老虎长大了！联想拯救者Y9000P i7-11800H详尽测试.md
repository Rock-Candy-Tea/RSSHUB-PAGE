<div>   
<p>2021年对Intel来说可以说是否极泰来的一年。2021年开局，Intel似乎依旧陷于万马齐喑的尴尬境地，CPU上无论桌面级、笔记本还是服务器都被AMD死死压住，产品线毫无还手之力。</p>
<p>但是随着5月Intel正式发布Tiger Lake-H45系列高性能移动平台，一场反击大幕也就此来开帷幕。</p>
<p>今天就带来基于Intel H45架构的i7-11800H测试报告。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210930/01d14d0b-8f54-4ef5-a8be-7201c732fe73.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="小老虎长大了！联想拯救者Y9000P i7-11800H详尽测试" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210930/S01d14d0b-8f54-4ef5-a8be-7201c732fe73.jpg" style="border:black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p><strong>产品整体外观：</strong></p>
<p>这次用到的测试产品是联想Y9000P，具体配置为i7-11800H+RTX 3060，是目前比较不错的游戏本配置。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210930/45c59ff5-0b20-47e2-ac0b-4d2794dc1083.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="小老虎长大了！联想拯救者Y9000P i7-11800H详尽测试" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210930/S45c59ff5-0b20-47e2-ac0b-4d2794dc1083.jpg" style="border:black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>开看一下笔记本整体，Y9000P采用的是传统架构，不支持平板模式。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210930/a70010d1-8963-4951-bdf6-cf2d1428e748.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="小老虎长大了！联想拯救者Y9000P i7-11800H详尽测试" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210930/Sa70010d1-8963-4951-bdf6-cf2d1428e748.jpg" style="border:black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>A面是拯救者这个系列经典的设计，上面有一个LOGO。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210930/a8719442-5958-4cd5-8651-23b459b40836.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="小老虎长大了！联想拯救者Y9000P i7-11800H详尽测试" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210930/Sa8719442-5958-4cd5-8651-23b459b40836.jpg" style="border:black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>笔记本A面下方则有2个出风口和中间若干IO接口。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210930/1defb8f5-350c-40e2-ab9d-b8aeb9998805.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="小老虎长大了！联想拯救者Y9000P i7-11800H详尽测试" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210930/S1defb8f5-350c-40e2-ab9d-b8aeb9998805.jpg" style="border:black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>B面则是笔记本屏幕，Y9000P比较有意思的采用了16寸 16:10的屏幕。整体屏占比很高，摄像头放在了额头上。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210930/1853809e-9ae9-43a9-9af4-78396c22eefa.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="小老虎长大了！联想拯救者Y9000P i7-11800H详尽测试" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210930/S1853809e-9ae9-43a9-9af4-78396c22eefa.jpg" style="border:black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>C面是键盘+触摸板，键盘上保留了完整的小键盘。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210930/0da9596b-11da-4b95-9c14-f855b30642d3.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="小老虎长大了！联想拯救者Y9000P i7-11800H详尽测试" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210930/S0da9596b-11da-4b95-9c14-f855b30642d3.jpg" style="border:black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>D面可以看到大面积的进风栅格，联想对笔记本的散热还是相当上心的。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210930/69abfdd4-b14d-43a9-b1cc-598ef1f56d78.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="小老虎长大了！联想拯救者Y9000P i7-11800H详尽测试" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210930/S69abfdd4-b14d-43a9-b1cc-598ef1f56d78.jpg" style="border:black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>屏幕最大开启角度约为135度。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210930/6357eaf5-53c3-4307-ad86-b9e932b11072.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="小老虎长大了！联想拯救者Y9000P i7-11800H详尽测试" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210930/S6357eaf5-53c3-4307-ad86-b9e932b11072.jpg" style="border:black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>Y9000P后部的IO接口图中左起为RJ45网络接口、雷电4（支持PD100W）、USB 3.0*2、HDMI、USB 3.0（支持关机充电）、适配器供电接口。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210930/e705a685-34af-4e80-a22b-b03664e376d2.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="小老虎长大了！联想拯救者Y9000P i7-11800H详尽测试" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210930/Se705a685-34af-4e80-a22b-b03664e376d2.jpg" style="border:black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>Y9000P左侧还有2个接口分别是雷电4（不支持PD）、3.5耳麦合一接口。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210930/7d4b8b8d-d10c-4b1d-8692-8622e5e66f79.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="小老虎长大了！联想拯救者Y9000P i7-11800H详尽测试" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210930/S7d4b8b8d-d10c-4b1d-8692-8622e5e66f79.jpg" style="border:black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>Y9000P右侧则为一个USB 3.0和笔记本摄像头的开关。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210930/9cd430ff-5f58-4409-b1ed-a457c4a5eaa0.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="小老虎长大了！联想拯救者Y9000P i7-11800H详尽测试" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210930/S9cd430ff-5f58-4409-b1ed-a457c4a5eaa0.jpg" style="border:black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>Y9000P同样支持模式切换可选性能、自动和静音三种模式。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210930/8a707589-66a1-4fb2-8615-022fb74b4dfb.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="小老虎长大了！联想拯救者Y9000P i7-11800H详尽测试" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210930/S8a707589-66a1-4fb2-8615-022fb74b4dfb.jpg" style="border:black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>Y9000P的宽度大约为36厘米。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210930/26eda734-e07e-4372-8aea-bf4916096992.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="小老虎长大了！联想拯救者Y9000P i7-11800H详尽测试" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210930/S26eda734-e07e-4372-8aea-bf4916096992.jpg" style="border:black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>Y9000P长度约为27厘米。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210930/9b3da809-69fc-4388-9d84-51546c964f87.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="小老虎长大了！联想拯救者Y9000P i7-11800H详尽测试" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210930/S9b3da809-69fc-4388-9d84-51546c964f87.jpg" style="border:black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>Y9000P厚度约为3厘米。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210930/d45b23ec-07a1-4887-86fe-5299f1239655.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="小老虎长大了！联想拯救者Y9000P i7-11800H详尽测试" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210930/Sd45b23ec-07a1-4887-86fe-5299f1239655.jpg" style="border:black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>Y9000P采用了比较大型的变压器。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210930/2f8af7ad-7bfe-47f3-98bb-45baf5ea5b41.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="小老虎长大了！联想拯救者Y9000P i7-11800H详尽测试" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210930/S2f8af7ad-7bfe-47f3-98bb-45baf5ea5b41.jpg" style="border:black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>变压器功率为300W，由台达电子代工。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210930/acfd0ed1-127f-4db5-92d9-e004850eca86.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="小老虎长大了！联想拯救者Y9000P i7-11800H详尽测试" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210930/Sacfd0ed1-127f-4db5-92d9-e004850eca86.jpg" style="border:black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>Y9000P本体的重量为2.5千克。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210930/033bf9bc-f7cf-43dc-af85-acc4078c09e0.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="小老虎长大了！联想拯救者Y9000P i7-11800H详尽测试" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210930/S033bf9bc-f7cf-43dc-af85-acc4078c09e0.jpg" style="border:black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>变压器重量也很大，重量约为1千克。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210930/770602c8-9017-4d4f-8b21-893bca084f2a.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="小老虎长大了！联想拯救者Y9000P i7-11800H详尽测试" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210930/S770602c8-9017-4d4f-8b21-893bca084f2a.jpg" style="border:black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
