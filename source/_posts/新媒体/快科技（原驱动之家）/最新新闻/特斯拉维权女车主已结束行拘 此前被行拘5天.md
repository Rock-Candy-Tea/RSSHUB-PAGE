<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">最近一周时间，特斯拉车顶维权事件闹得沸沸扬扬，其中一位女主车于上海车展期间在特斯拉展台站在车顶维权，被行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，今天已经结束。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">据报道，有记者从知情人士处确认，张女士行政拘留期满，<strong>已于今天上午解除拘留。 <span style="font-family:"MS Gothic";mso-bidi-font-family:"MS Gothic"">​</span></strong><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:"MS Gothic";mso-fareast-font-family:等线;
mso-fareast-theme-font:minor-fareast;mso-bidi-font-family:"MS Gothic""></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">4月20日，这名特斯拉用户在特斯拉车顶维权，引发网络热议，后被安保人员拖走。<span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:"MS Gothic";mso-fareast-font-family:等线;
mso-fareast-theme-font:minor-fareast;mso-bidi-font-family:"MS Gothic""></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>随后当地官方发布警情通报：张某（站车顶女车主）因扰乱公共秩序被处于行政拘留<span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:"MS Gothic";mso-fareast-font-family:等线;
mso-fareast-theme-font:minor-fareast;mso-bidi-font-family:"MS Gothic"">5</span>日。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">同时，警方提示：消费者应通过合法合理途径，表达诉求维护权益，切勿采取过激行为，否则将承担法律责任。<span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:"MS Gothic";mso-fareast-font-family:等线;
mso-fareast-theme-font:minor-fareast;mso-bidi-font-family:"MS Gothic""></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/753/753040.htm" target="_blank">上海车展维权女车主丈夫李先生通过社交平台对这次维权事件进行表态</a>，解释了为何妻子要站上车顶等相关问题。<span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:"MS Gothic";mso-fareast-font-family:等线;
mso-fareast-theme-font:minor-fareast;mso-bidi-font-family:"MS Gothic""></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">李先生表示，考虑到特斯拉副总裁陶琳可能参加上海车展，因此妻子想借此机会到车展上和陶琳见一面，反映情况，同时，<strong>最初并没有想过要做过激的行为，只想要一个说法。</strong><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:"MS Gothic";mso-fareast-font-family:等线;
mso-fareast-theme-font:minor-fareast;mso-bidi-font-family:"MS Gothic""></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">妻子抵达现场后，可能被工作人员认出正在拍视频，随后安保人员便对她进行拉扯并试图驱逐。<span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:"MS Gothic";mso-fareast-font-family:等线;
mso-fareast-theme-font:minor-fareast;mso-bidi-font-family:"MS Gothic""></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">李先生称，安保人员用雨伞遮挡不让拍摄，导致妻子被激怒，无奈之下才登上车顶。<span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:"MS Gothic";mso-fareast-font-family:等线;
mso-fareast-theme-font:minor-fareast;mso-bidi-font-family:"MS Gothic""></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">事件矛盾激化之后，特斯拉的态度也从之前的决不妥协变成了深夜致歉，多次发表公告愿意配合调查，并于<span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:"MS Gothic";mso-fareast-font-family:等线;
mso-fareast-theme-font:minor-fareast;mso-bidi-font-family:"MS Gothic"">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:
"MS Gothic";mso-fareast-font-family:等线;mso-fareast-theme-font:minor-fareast;
mso-bidi-font-family:"MS Gothic"">22</span>日晚上<span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:"MS Gothic";mso-fareast-font-family:等线;
mso-fareast-theme-font:minor-fareast;mso-bidi-font-family:"MS Gothic"">6</span>点公布了张某的行车数据。<span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:"MS Gothic";mso-fareast-font-family:等线;mso-fareast-theme-font:
minor-fareast;mso-bidi-font-family:"MS Gothic""></span></p>
