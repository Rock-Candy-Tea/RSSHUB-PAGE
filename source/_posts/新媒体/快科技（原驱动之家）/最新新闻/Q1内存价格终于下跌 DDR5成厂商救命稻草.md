<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">过去两年中全球半导体行业产能紧张，价格上涨，不过内存及闪存芯片是个例外，<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年迎来了熊市，价格都在下跌，其中<span lang="EN-US">Q1</span>季度内存<span lang="EN-US">ASP</span>均价跌了<span lang="EN-US">5%</span>左右，拖累了三星、美光等公司的业绩。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据中国闪存时候的统计，受<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>制于手机<span lang="EN-US">PC</span>两大消费终端需求疲软，<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年一季度全球<span lang="EN-US">DRAM</span>市场规模下滑<span lang="EN-US">3.4%</span>至<span lang="EN-US">239.3</span>亿美元。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">其中三星一季度的<span lang="EN-US">DRAM</span>销售收入达<span lang="EN-US">103.96</span>亿美元，环比增长<span lang="EN-US">0.8%</span>，市场份额达<span lang="EN-US">43.4%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">SK</span>海力士一季度<span lang="EN-US">DRAM</span>销售收入达<span lang="EN-US">65.61</span>亿美元，环比减少<span lang="EN-US">11.7%</span>，市场份额达<span lang="EN-US">27.4%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">美光一季度<span lang="EN-US">DRAM</span>销售收入达<span lang="EN-US">56.84</span>亿美元，环比增长<span lang="EN-US">0.9%</span>，市场份额达<span lang="EN-US">23.7%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">除了这三大厂商中，排名第四的南亚科技一季度<span lang="EN-US">DRAM</span>销售收入达<span lang="EN-US">7.13</span>亿美元，环比下滑<span lang="EN-US">7.3%</span>，市场份额达<span lang="EN-US">3.0%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">再往下就是华邦电子，一季度<span lang="EN-US">DRAM</span>销售收入达<span lang="EN-US">2.48</span>亿美元，环比减少<span lang="EN-US">4.4%</span>，市场份额<span lang="EN-US">1%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">由于内存价格下滑，<strong>厂商已经把<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存当作重点，主要是这种内存还是新产品，价格高</strong>，利润空间大，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>及<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的新平台今年都会把<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>当作重点来推。</p>
