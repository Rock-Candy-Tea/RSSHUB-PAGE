<p class="MsoNormal">前几天苹果已经宣布了新的发布会——<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日凌晨<span lang="EN-US">1</span>点举办特别活动，揭晓<span lang="EN-US">“</span>王炸级<span lang="EN-US">”</span>产品，这次的主角是新一代<span lang="EN-US">MacBook Pro</span>以及<span lang="EN-US">AirPods 3</span>等。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">新一代<span lang="EN-US">MacBook Pro</span>依然有<span lang="EN-US">14</span>寸及<span lang="EN-US">16</span>寸两个级别，这次升级到重点是屏幕，<strong>此前已经基本上得到确认的是采用一块<span lang="EN-US">120Hz</span>刷新率的<span lang="EN-US">mini LED</span>显示面板</strong>，至此<span lang="EN-US">iPhone 13</span>、<span lang="EN-US">iPad</span>及<span lang="EN-US">MacBook</span>系列都上了<span lang="EN-US">120Hz</span>刷了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">处理器是升级后的<span lang="EN-US">M1X</span>，规格也曝光差不多了，<span lang="EN-US">10</span>核设计，其中<span lang="EN-US">8</span>颗核心都是性能大核，<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>规模更是<span lang="EN-US">16</span>或者<span lang="EN-US">32</span>核。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">外观方面，新一代<span lang="EN-US">MacBook Pro</span>采用窄边框设计，正面下方没有了苹果<span lang="EN-US">LOGO</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">电池及充电是<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年新版<span lang="EN-US">MacBook</span>之后变化最大的一次，<span lang="EN-US">MagSafe</span>充电回归，充电模块重新设计，充电速度更快，其中<span lang="EN-US">14</span>寸机型电池容量增加，<span lang="EN-US">16</span>寸机型有所减少。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">除了这些主要变化，新一代<span lang="EN-US">MacBook Pro</span>还有很多细节升级，之前被人诟病的多个鸡肋功能也都取消或者升级了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>不太受欢迎的<span lang="EN-US">TouchBar</span>触控栏取消了，<span lang="EN-US">720P</span>摄像头这次也升级到了<span lang="EN-US">1080P</span>高清，视频通话效果改善了。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">原先<span lang="EN-US">8GB</span>起步的内存也大幅提升，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>标配<span lang="EN-US">16GB</span>内存及<span lang="EN-US">512GB</span>硬盘起步，</strong></span>最高可选<span lang="EN-US">64GB</span>内存，生产力大增。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">散热方面，<span lang="EN-US">14</span>寸<span lang="EN-US">MacBook Pro</span>也改进了，使用了更大的热管及散热器，还全系支持高功率模式，接电的时候可以提高性能。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">之前被吐槽的接口也有所改善，现在的<span lang="EN-US">MacBook Pro</span>主推<span lang="EN-US">4</span>个雷电接口，但其他接口较少，<strong>新一代<span lang="EN-US">MacBook Pro</span>配备了<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个雷电接口，还有<span lang="EN-US">MagSafe</span>充电接口、<span lang="EN-US">HDMI</span>接口及<span lang="EN-US">SD</span>读卡器接口，接口种类更丰富一些。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">总体来看，新一代<span lang="EN-US">MacBook Pro</span>除了配置大升级之外，很多细节也改进了，之前被吐槽的地方明显变了，值得期待。</p>
