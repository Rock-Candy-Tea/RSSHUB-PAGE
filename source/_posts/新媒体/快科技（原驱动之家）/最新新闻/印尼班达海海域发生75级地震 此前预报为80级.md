<p class="MsoNormal">据中国地震台网正式测定，北京时间<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日<span lang="EN-US">2</span>时<span lang="EN-US">25</span>分（当地时间<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日<span lang="EN-US">3</span>时<span lang="EN-US">25</span>分）在班达海发生<span lang="EN-US">7.5</span>级地震，震源深度<span lang="EN-US">200</span>千米，震中位于南纬<span lang="EN-US">7.75</span>度，东经<span lang="EN-US">127.60</span>度。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此前预报中，该处地震被报告为<span lang="EN-US">8</span>级。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>根据中国地震台网速报目录，震中周边<span lang="EN-US">200</span>公里内近<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年来发生<span lang="EN-US">3</span>级以上地震共<span lang="EN-US">13</span>次，最大地震是本次地震。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">据了解，班达海（<span lang="EN-US">Banda Sea</span>，印度尼西亚官方文字作<span lang="EN-US">Laut Banda</span>）位于南太平洋西部海域，总面积<span lang="EN-US">47</span>万平方千米，平均深度为<span lang="EN-US">3064</span>米，为印度尼西亚摩鹿加南部诸岛所环抱。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">西与弗洛勒斯<span lang="EN-US">(Flores)</span>海，西南与萨武<span lang="EN-US">(Savu)</span>海，南与帝汶海，东南与阿拉弗拉<span lang="EN-US">(Arafura)</span>海，北与塞兰海及摩鹿加海相连。南面是阿洛<span lang="EN-US">(Alor)</span>、帝汶<span lang="EN-US">(Timor)</span>、韦塔<span lang="EN-US">(Wetar)</span>、巴巴尔<span lang="EN-US">(Babar)</span>、塔宁巴尔<span lang="EN-US">(Tanimbar)</span>和卡伊<span lang="EN-US">(Kai)</span>等岛屿，北面是塞兰<span lang="EN-US">(Seram)</span>、布鲁<span lang="EN-US">(Buru)</span>和苏拉<span lang="EN-US">(Sula)</span>等岛群。</p>
