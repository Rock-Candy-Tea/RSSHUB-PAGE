<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">最近<span lang="EN-US">Chia</span>硬盘矿的火热，让<span lang="EN-US">HDD</span>硬盘大卖了一波，特别是超大容量的硬盘极为抢手，价格上涨<span lang="EN-US">2-3</span>倍。但是全局来看，<span lang="EN-US">HDD</span>市场还在继续下滑，<span lang="EN-US">Q1</span>季度出货只有<span lang="EN-US">6417</span>万块，环比下滑<span lang="EN-US">8.4%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">目前<span lang="EN-US">HDD</span>硬盘厂商主要剩下三家——希捷、西数、东芝，<strong><span lang="EN-US">Q1</span>季度中希捷出货<span lang="EN-US">2754</span>万块，环比下滑<span lang="EN-US">7.7%</span>，市场份额<span lang="EN-US">42.9%</span>，出货总容量<span lang="EN-US">139.5EB</span>。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">西数位列第二，出货<span lang="EN-US">2309</span>万块硬盘，环比下滑<span lang="EN-US">9.9%</span>，份额<span lang="EN-US">36%</span>，出货总容量<span lang="EN-US">109.04EB</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">东芝出货<span lang="EN-US">1354</span>万块硬盘，环比下滑<span lang="EN-US">7.5%</span>，份额<span lang="EN-US">21.1%</span>，出货总容量<span lang="EN-US">39.74EB</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>三家合计出货<span lang="EN-US">6417</span>万块，环比下滑<span lang="EN-US">8.4%</span>，但是<span lang="EN-US">HDD</span>硬盘容量增大，所以总的出货容量达到了<span lang="EN-US">288.28EB</span>。</strong></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal">这次硬盘下滑的主要是桌面及移动端，分别减少了<span lang="EN-US">8.8%</span>、<span lang="EN-US">15.2%</span>，这些领域正被<span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>硬盘侵蚀，但企业市场硬盘出货量大增<span lang="EN-US">19.3%</span>，出货容量也占到了<span lang="EN-US">197.16EB</span>，成为中流砥柱。</p>
