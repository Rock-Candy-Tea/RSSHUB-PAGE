           </p><p class="MsoNormal">日前中国移动、电信、联通三大运营商都公布了<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年报，三家公司去年的净利润超过<span lang="EN-US">1400</span>多亿，日赚大约<span lang="EN-US">3.9</span>亿元，大家贡献了多少？</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据他们的财报，<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年中国移动营业收入<span lang="EN-US">7681</span>亿元，<strong>增长<span lang="EN-US">3.0%</span>。净利润<span lang="EN-US">1078</span>亿元，增长<span lang="EN-US">1.1%</span>。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">中国电信营业收入<span lang="EN-US">3936</span>亿元，增长<span lang="EN-US">4.7%</span>。净利润<span lang="EN-US">209</span>亿元，增长<span lang="EN-US">1.6%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">中国联通营业收入<span lang="EN-US">3038</span>亿元，增长<span lang="EN-US">4.6%</span>。净利润<span lang="EN-US">125</span>亿元，同比增长<span lang="EN-US">10.3%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">简单计算下，三大运营商<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年合计营收约<span lang="EN-US">14655</span>亿元，净利<span lang="EN-US">1412</span>亿元，平均日赚约<span lang="EN-US">3.86</span>亿元。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">相较<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年，三大运营商日赚净利润同比增<span lang="EN-US">1.8%</span>，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>专家表示营收及利润增长并不明显，主要是跟<span lang="EN-US">4G</span>业务饱和、<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>建设开支过大有关。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此外，三大运营商还公布了移动用户数，截至<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月底，中国移动用户数达<span lang="EN-US">9.42</span>亿户，<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>套餐用户<span lang="EN-US">1.65</span>亿户；中国电信用户数达到<span lang="EN-US">3.51</span>亿户，<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>套餐用户<span lang="EN-US">8650</span>万户。中国联通用户数<span lang="EN-US">3.06</span>亿户，<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>套餐用户<span lang="EN-US">7083</span>万。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">这意味着三大运营商的<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>用户合计超过<span lang="EN-US">3.2</span>亿虎，差不多<span lang="EN-US">1/5</span>的用户已经转向<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>了。</p>
