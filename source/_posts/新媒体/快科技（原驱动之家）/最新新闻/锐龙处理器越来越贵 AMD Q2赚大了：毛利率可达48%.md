<p class="MsoNormal">全球半导体行业这下半年来遭遇产能紧张、缺货涨价的麻烦，但是对于<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>来说，已经过去的<span lang="EN-US">Q2</span>季度无疑会是大丰收，锐龙、霄龙及<span lang="EN-US">Radeon</span>显卡的<span lang="EN-US">ASP</span>均价提升，毛利率有望达到<span lang="EN-US">48%</span>的创纪录水平。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>公司将于<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日发布<span lang="EN-US">Q2</span>季度财报，还有一周时间，不过华尔街的金融分析师们普遍认为<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>在该季度的业绩表现强劲，将超过此前公布的营收指引。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">今年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月底，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>发布<span lang="EN-US">Q1</span>季度报告，营收为<span lang="EN-US">34.45</span>亿美元，与上年同期的<span lang="EN-US">17.86</span>亿美元相比增长<span lang="EN-US">93%</span>，与上一季度的<span lang="EN-US">32.44</span>亿美元相比增长<span lang="EN-US">6%</span>；净利润为<span lang="EN-US">5.55</span>亿美元，与上年同期的<span lang="EN-US">1.62</span>亿美元相比增长<span lang="EN-US">243%</span>，与上一季度的<span lang="EN-US">17.81</span>亿美元相比下降<span lang="EN-US">69%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>预计<span lang="EN-US">Q2</span>季度营收在<span lang="EN-US">36</span>亿美元，上下浮动<span lang="EN-US">1</span>亿美元，同比增长<span lang="EN-US">86%</span>，环比增长<span lang="EN-US">4%</span>，非<span lang="EN-US">GAAP</span>下毛利率可达<span lang="EN-US">47%</span>。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>这两年来财务指标显著提升的不止是营收，还有盈利能力，前几年的时候长期低于<span lang="EN-US">40%</span>，这对半导体公司来说是不健康，这两年稳步提升，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">Q1</span>季度是<span lang="EN-US">46%</span>，<span lang="EN-US">Q2</span>预期是<span lang="EN-US">47%</span>，不过分析师看得更高，认为会达到<span lang="EN-US">48%</span>。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">毛利率高了意味着<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的赚钱能力提升，主要原因是现在<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>高端产品上来了，特别是最新的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">5000</span>系列，<span lang="EN-US">ASP</span>均价比之前有看得到的增长，之前网友吐槽<span lang="EN-US">6</span>核的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">5 5600X</span>都卖到<span lang="EN-US">2000</span>多了，这也是<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>毛利率提升的关键。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">当然，即便是<span lang="EN-US">48%</span>的毛利率，相对友商来说还是低不少的，<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>的毛利率超过<span lang="EN-US">65%</span>，<span lang="EN-US">Intel Q1</span>季度的非<span lang="EN-US">GAAP</span>毛利率是<span lang="EN-US">58.2%</span>，不过<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>是<span lang="EN-US">IDM</span>企业，有自己的晶圆厂，成本本身就高，需要高毛利支撑。</p>
