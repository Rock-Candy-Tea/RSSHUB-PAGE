<p>曾为宝马设计X5的Frank Stephenson在一则播客节目中批评新2系简直不是宝马：“头灯相当怪异，格栅、下部通风口、引擎盖的设计语言各不相同，无法形成一个整体。”</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210807/49a74ba563dd44909731321c65eaea86.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="前宝马设计师评新2系" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210807/s_49a74ba563dd44909731321c65eaea86.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210807/0968e3f34a0d4e11bfc7272fecd3dc99.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="前宝马设计师评新2系" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210807/s_0968e3f34a0d4e11bfc7272fecd3dc99.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>某些Bimmer心仪的修长前车身在他看来过于“卡通化”，对于一辆小型双门轿跑来说比例完全不对，前轮需要向后退。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210807/583e9821cb524b32bee383d115625fe6.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="前宝马设计师评新2系" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210807/s_583e9821cb524b32bee383d115625fe6.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210807/6bf1aa119d704ccea6d3c2095af312af.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="前宝马设计师评新2系" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210807/s_6bf1aa119d704ccea6d3c2095af312af.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>“太多拐角，太多线条”，除非推倒重来，否则他看不出有什么办法补救。唯一能入Stephenson法眼的就是2系乘员舱，起码这个部分看上去“像宝马”。</p>

<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210807/ca0dadd69b3041708053c065187c6fc5.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="前宝马设计师评新2系" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210807/s_ca0dadd69b3041708053c065187c6fc5.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210807/490f7bf627b8482e9874a83237dc02c7.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="前宝马设计师评新2系" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210807/s_490f7bf627b8482e9874a83237dc02c7.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>

<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210807/6a1457ac2d364668a2e86af3ed6a62bc.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="前宝马设计师评新2系" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210807/s_6a1457ac2d364668a2e86af3ed6a62bc.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>Stephenson曾于九零及零零年代为宝马设计了新一代MINI与初代X5，他对4系大鼻孔尤其反感，表示新2系最大的可取之处就是沿用正常尺寸的格栅而非“海狸门牙”式大鼻孔。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210807/d29f0672684c46168037a86b8ab1bc85.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="前宝马设计师评新2系" h="332" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210807/s_d29f0672684c46168037a86b8ab1bc85.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="589" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>另外，新X1谍照显示这款跨界车侥幸未配备海狸门牙式格栅：</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210807/129d09e69c1d4403b2503fda3ebfff0e.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="前宝马设计师评新2系" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210807/s_129d09e69c1d4403b2503fda3ebfff0e.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210807/d296bd016f374657a7278552027ceb98.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="前宝马设计师评新2系" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210807/s_d296bd016f374657a7278552027ceb98.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>设计师根据这批谍照更新了想象中的渲染图，新设计的门把手参考了i4。平台代号U11的新一代X1将于年底正式宣布，明年交付。低配仍为sDrive前驱，高配型号为xDrive，预计可提供燃油、插混、纯电多种动力组合。</p>
