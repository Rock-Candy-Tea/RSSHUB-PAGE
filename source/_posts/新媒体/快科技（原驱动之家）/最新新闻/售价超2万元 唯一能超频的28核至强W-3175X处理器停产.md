<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">在<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的锐龙线程撕裂者疯狂堆核心的时候，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>为了与之竞争，在<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年推出了一款特殊的<span lang="EN-US">Xeon W-3175X</span>处理器，<span lang="EN-US">14nm</span>工艺、<span lang="EN-US">28</span>核<span lang="EN-US">56</span>线程，售价<span lang="EN-US">2999</span>美元，还是唯一解锁了超频的至强处理器。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Xeon W-3175X</span>的基准频率为<span lang="EN-US">3.1GHz</span>，睿频加速最高<span lang="EN-US">4.3GHz</span>，同时有<span lang="EN-US">38.5MB</span>三级缓存，内存支持六通道<span lang="EN-US">DDR4-2666</span>，并支持<span lang="EN-US">ECC</span>错误校验、<span lang="EN-US">RAS</span>特性，最大容量<span lang="EN-US">512GB</span>，可提供<span lang="EN-US">68</span>条<span lang="EN-US">PCIe</span>通道，热设计功耗高达<span lang="EN-US">255W</span>，封装接口为<span lang="EN-US">LGA3647</span>，需要搭配<span lang="EN-US">C621</span>芯片组主板。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">类似桌面可超频处理器，<strong>它也支持<span lang="EN-US">Intel Extreme Tuning Utility</span>、<span lang="EN-US">Intel Extreme Memory Profile</span>两大超频工具，</strong>可消除内存超频过程中的不确定性，并可利用<span lang="EN-US">AVX-512</span>指令集的比率偏移与内存控制器调整电压控制，获得最大化性能。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="售价超2万元 唯一能超频的28核至强W-3175X处理器停产" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211008/822ecffb-bdae-4794-9444-a3535ca8e022.jpg" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">按照<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的说法，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">Xeon W-3175X</span>与酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i9-9980XE</span>相比，在<span lang="EN-US">“</span>虚幻引擎<span lang="EN-US">”</span>中进行<span lang="EN-US">“</span>渗透器演示<span lang="EN-US">”</span>生成的速度提高了<span lang="EN-US">1.52</span>倍。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">问世三年来，<span lang="EN-US">W-3175X</span>处理器现在依然是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>最豪华的<span lang="EN-US">HEDT</span>发烧级处理器，虽然价格超过<span lang="EN-US">2</span>万元，而且需要搭配专门的主板，但它用于工作站的话还是很适合的。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不过<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>已经决定退役<span lang="EN-US">W-3175X</span>处理器了，<strong>最后的订单日是<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，最后的出货日是<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，一年之后彻底退出市场。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">虽然唯一能超频的至强<span lang="EN-US">28</span>核<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>没了，但<span lang="EN-US">I</span>粉也别伤心，<span lang="EN-US">W-3175X</span>不论发热还是组建成本都很高，<a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/763/763429.htm" target="_blank">明年<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>要推<span lang="EN-US">10nm</span>工艺的<span lang="EN-US">Sapphire Rapids</span>处理器了</a>，最多<span lang="EN-US">60</span>核<span lang="EN-US">120</span>线程，还支持<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>、<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>等先进技术，明年底也有望登陆桌面<span lang="EN-US">HEDT</span>平台。</p>
