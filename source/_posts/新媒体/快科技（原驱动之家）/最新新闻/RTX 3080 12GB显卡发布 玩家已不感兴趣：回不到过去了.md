<p class="MsoNormal">最近一段时间来，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>、<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>又推出了一波新显卡，<span lang="EN-US">A</span>卡这边有<span lang="EN-US">RX 6500 XT</span>及<span lang="EN-US">RX 6400</span>，<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>这边更多，除了笔记本显卡及复活<span lang="EN-US">RTX 2060 12GB</span>之外，桌面版还有<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3050</span>、<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3090 Ti</span>等，<a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/808/808749.htm" target="_blank">日前又推出<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3080 12GB</span>显卡</a>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">从规格上来看，<strong><span lang="EN-US">RTX 3080 12GB</span>采用了编号<span lang="EN-US">GA102-220</span>的新款核心，具备<span lang="EN-US">8960</span>个流处理器，</strong>相比于<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3080</span>增加了<span lang="EN-US">256</span>个，距离<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3080 Ti</span>也只差<span lang="EN-US">1280</span>个。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">同时还有<span lang="EN-US">70</span>个光追核心、<span lang="EN-US">280</span>个张量核心、<span lang="EN-US">280</span>个纹理单元，都增加了<span lang="EN-US">18</span>个，而核心频率维持在<span lang="EN-US">1440-1710MHz</span>，完全不变。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">显存容量增加之外，位宽也从<span lang="EN-US">320-bit</span>开放到完整的<span lang="EN-US">384-bit</span>，频率还是<span lang="EN-US">19GHz</span>，带宽因此达到<span lang="EN-US">912GB/s</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">售价方面，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>国内市场上七彩虹、微星、映众、索泰的非公卡已经上架，最便宜的<span lang="EN-US">9299</span>元，最贵的则要<span lang="EN-US">10499</span>元，差不多都在万元左右。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">RTX 3080 12GB</span>显卡发布会能给市场带来什么？国外同行<span lang="EN-US">hardwareluxx</span>日前发布了一篇评论文章，指出这次新卡发布之后，游戏玩家已经没什么兴趣了，因为当前的情况下，新卡不仅涨价，而且供应也跟不上，被炒作的价格更高。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不仅仅是高端卡如此，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的<span lang="EN-US">RX 6500 XT</span>、<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3050</span>显卡也会如此，<strong>还没上市销售就各种涨价，远高于<span lang="EN-US">AMD/NVIDIA</span>公布的建议价。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">想玩游戏的<span lang="EN-US">PC</span>玩家现在也改变主意了，与其花费<span lang="EN-US">300</span>多欧元买个<span lang="EN-US">RX 6500 XT</span>这样的显卡，还不如购买主机，比如<span lang="EN-US">Xbox Series X</span>，<span lang="EN-US">PS5</span>等。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>还有一件事是确定的，那就是显卡市场回不到<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年了，</strong>因为供应链已经全面提高了显卡价格水平（<span lang="EN-US">PS</span>：过去一年多中电子元件、芯片代工及物流、包装都涨价了），现在唯一的希望就是这种极端价格不会成为常态。</p>
