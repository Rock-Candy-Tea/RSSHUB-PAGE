<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">不出意外的话，今年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月份<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>就要推出<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Alder Lake</span>处理器了，升级<span lang="EN-US">LGA1700</span>插槽，带来全新的<span lang="EN-US">600</span>系芯片组。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">迎新就要辞旧，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>日前发布通知，<span lang="EN-US">B460</span>芯片组开始停产退役，而且进度很快，<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日是最后的订单日，<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日是最后的出货日。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">换句话说，<span lang="EN-US">B460</span>芯片组今年内就会停产，<strong>退役过程只有<span lang="EN-US">4</span>个月，</strong>以往产品退役至少有一年的过渡期。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">B460</span>是<span lang="EN-US">Intel 400</span>系芯片组中的主流，也普通玩家购买最多的主板型号之一，迄今为止也不落伍，搭配<span lang="EN-US">10</span>代酷睿仍是不错的选择，因为它跟<span lang="EN-US">H410</span>一样都是不支持<span lang="EN-US">11</span>代酷睿的。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">B460</span>之所以这么着急退役，估计跟年底的<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Alder Lake</span>有关，这代<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>升级<span lang="EN-US">Intel 7</span>工艺，<strong>首次带来大小核<span lang="EN-US">x86</span>架构，支持<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>及<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>，也会升级<span lang="EN-US">LGA1700</span>插槽，芯片组则是全新的<span lang="EN-US">600</span>系列。</strong></p>
