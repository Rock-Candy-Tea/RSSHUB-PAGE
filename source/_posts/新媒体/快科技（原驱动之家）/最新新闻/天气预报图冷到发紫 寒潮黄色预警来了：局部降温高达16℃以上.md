<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日晚上<span lang="EN-US">18</span>时，中央气象台发布寒潮黄色预警：受寒潮影响，<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日<span lang="EN-US">20</span>时至<span lang="EN-US">08</span>日<span lang="EN-US">08</span>时，我国自西北向东南将有一次大风降温天气过程，局部降温高达<span lang="EN-US">16℃</span>以上。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">值得注意的是，<strong>由于这次降温明显，中央气象台发布的天气预报图都变成紫色了，</strong>这还是很少见的，也说明了气温降幅有多大，大家不要硬抗了，秋裤穿起来。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">据中央气象台表示，大部地区气温将先后下降<span lang="EN-US">8</span>～<span lang="EN-US">10℃</span>，西北、内蒙古大部、华北、黄淮、江淮西部、东北地区南部等地部分地区降幅可达<span lang="EN-US">12</span>～<span lang="EN-US">14℃</span>，局地<span lang="EN-US">16℃</span>以上；</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">并伴有<span lang="EN-US">4</span>～<span lang="EN-US">6</span>级偏北风、阵风<span lang="EN-US">7</span>～<span lang="EN-US">8</span>级；<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日早晨最低气温<span lang="EN-US">0℃</span>线位于山东中部、河南中部至四川北部一带，华南北部最低气温将降至<span lang="EN-US">10℃</span>左右。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">预计<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日<span lang="EN-US">20</span>时至<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日<span lang="EN-US">20</span>时，新疆北部和东部、内蒙古西部、甘肃西部气温将下降<span lang="EN-US">8</span>～<span lang="EN-US">10℃</span>，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>其中新疆东北部和内蒙古西部部分地区降幅可达<span lang="EN-US">12</span>～<span lang="EN-US">14℃</span>，局地<span lang="EN-US">16℃</span>以上；</strong></span>并伴有<span lang="EN-US">4</span>～<span lang="EN-US">6</span>级偏北风，阵风<span lang="EN-US">7</span>～<span lang="EN-US">8</span>级，山口地区风力可达<span lang="EN-US">9</span>～<span lang="EN-US">10</span>级。</p>
