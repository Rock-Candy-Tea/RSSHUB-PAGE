<div>   
<p>今晚，iQOO召开Neo6新品发布会，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>开场官方便直接秀出了它1065001分的安兔兔跑分成绩。</strong></span></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220413/8f37987cecf048c79bb0d44d20b589ff.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="开场先秀跑分！iQOO Neo6安兔兔超百万" h="350" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220413/s_8f37987cecf048c79bb0d44d20b589ff.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>具体来说，<strong>CPU成绩为241598分，GPU成绩为486260分，MEM成绩为168806分，UX成绩则是168337分。</strong></p>
<p>iQOO Neo6跑分能够轻松突破百万，一方面是得益于骁龙8 Gen 1这颗旗舰级芯片，另一方面，超频版的UFS 3.1和增强版LPPR5也同样重要。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220413/043c9f6abc61440d8a7dac497eeb78f6.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="开场先秀跑分！iQOO Neo6安兔兔超百万" h="800" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220413/s_043c9f6abc61440d8a7dac497eeb78f6.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>根据iQOO官方数据，<strong>以810P分辨率运行一小时《原神》，iQOO Neo6的平均帧率能够维持在59.73，波动幅度较小。</strong></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220413/fc983cabaf1e4541b82d420af48af3af.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="开场先秀跑分！iQOO Neo6安兔兔超百万" h="800" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220413/s_fc983cabaf1e4541b82d420af48af3af.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>此外，iQOO Neo6还搭载了独立显示芯片Pro，通过芯片插帧技术分担GPU工作，进一步提升了游戏的画面与帧率表现。</p>
<p>同时，<strong>它有着高达46662mm²的散热面积</strong>，有效解决了高强度游戏中的发热问题。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220413/1c242db872b645f3af90f3eedcf0032f.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="开场先秀跑分！iQOO Neo6安兔兔超百万" h="800" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220413/s_1c242db872b645f3af90f3eedcf0032f.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
