<div>   
<p>Intel NUC迷你机家族日渐发展壮大，最新的NUC 11 Extreme更是突破极限，代号<a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/760/760142.htm" target="_blank">“野兽峡谷”(Beast Canyon)</a>的它体积达到了史无前例的8升，可以容纳更庞大、更高性能的硬件。</p>
<p>有外媒近日搞到了一台，并做了简单的拆解，一起来看。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210719/c7bcb3f70a514d49814594113a34dca6.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="Intel怪兽峡谷NUC拆解：8升容纳全长显卡、暴力风扇" h="519" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210719/s_c7bcb3f70a514d49814594113a34dca6.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>8升的体积严格来说已经相当不迷你，但是<strong>Intel说了，我们只做NUC，再大它也是NUC</strong>——射程再远也叫它火箭炮……</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210719/dd49628d68214b42b9f8d424a6bccfb6.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="Intel怪兽峡谷NUC拆解：8升容纳全长显卡、暴力风扇" h="504" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210719/s_dd49628d68214b42b9f8d424a6bccfb6.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>长方体造型，从表面纹理到侧面/顶部网格，再到前面板，到处都是六角形元素，正面的骷髅头标记还支持RGB LED。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210719/461cbac2c2d34da3aa4bba40c179fa8b.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="Intel怪兽峡谷NUC拆解：8升容纳全长显卡、暴力风扇" h="488" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210719/s_461cbac2c2d34da3aa4bba40c179fa8b.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>后部是供电接口和输入输出，包括<strong>一个2.5千兆或万兆网口、两个雷电4/USB-C、六个USB 3.0、一个HDMI 2.0b。</strong>镂空挡板处对应显卡安装位，<strong>最长12英寸(30.5厘米)。</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210719/32a97f27bd4f49668acb02c1b30b1476.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="Intel怪兽峡谷NUC拆解：8升容纳全长显卡、暴力风扇" h="398" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210719/s_32a97f27bd4f49668acb02c1b30b1476.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210719/f72c42d839854465977fd6e47f0c29f3.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="Intel怪兽峡谷NUC拆解：8升容纳全长显卡、暴力风扇" h="445" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210719/s_f72c42d839854465977fd6e47f0c29f3.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210719/d4c4c9486c034b3fb8fae2bb95fc4191.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="Intel怪兽峡谷NUC拆解：8升容纳全长显卡、暴力风扇" h="482" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210719/s_d4c4c9486c034b3fb8fae2bb95fc4191.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210719/11e29a47b41b4a05bdcb62d4455f5d74.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="Intel怪兽峡谷NUC拆解：8升容纳全长显卡、暴力风扇" h="398" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210719/s_11e29a47b41b4a05bdcb62d4455f5d74.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210719/11dd8c2c99d24fc59d1cd9f8b819fc36.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="Intel怪兽峡谷NUC拆解：8升容纳全长显卡、暴力风扇" h="398" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210719/s_11dd8c2c99d24fc59d1cd9f8b819fc36.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>左右侧板和顶部都可以拆开，拿下顶盖就可以看到<strong>相当暴力的三个并排风扇，酷冷至尊出品，92mm直径</strong>，可自行更换。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210719/7bd2d2eea4174dfbb03a4a724fa4607e.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="Intel怪兽峡谷NUC拆解：8升容纳全长显卡、暴力风扇" h="460" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210719/s_7bd2d2eea4174dfbb03a4a724fa4607e.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210719/ba5061a3fac243ea8f0002d3388f6077.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="Intel怪兽峡谷NUC拆解：8升容纳全长显卡、暴力风扇" h="277" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210719/s_ba5061a3fac243ea8f0002d3388f6077.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>内部布局紧凑，特别是为了容纳显卡，<strong>处理器、内存、硬盘、网络都整合在一张PCIe扩展卡形式的计算套件内</strong>，也就是左侧绘有骷髅头标识的地方。</p>
<p>M.2有三个，其一来自处理器，支持PCIe 4.0 x4，另外两个来自WM590芯片组，支持PCIe 3.0 x4/SATA。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210719/db70b7ad3d9f44269a7f82dc168c9357.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="Intel怪兽峡谷NUC拆解：8升容纳全长显卡、暴力风扇" h="474" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210719/s_db70b7ad3d9f44269a7f82dc168c9357.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>因为计算套件内零部件太多太集中，<strong>特意设置了一个涡轮风扇</strong>，散热效果应该不错，但噪音也会不小。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210719/5a8bcb7876a14df48ec3b3a596e6a83f.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="Intel怪兽峡谷NUC拆解：8升容纳全长显卡、暴力风扇" h="398" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210719/s_5a8bcb7876a14df48ec3b3a596e6a83f.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210719/1f89257fd7bd41ebac6b625faf55f047.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="Intel怪兽峡谷NUC拆解：8升容纳全长显卡、暴力风扇" h="398" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210719/s_1f89257fd7bd41ebac6b625faf55f047.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p><strong>电源来自全汉</strong>，SFX迷你规格，功率650W。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210719/003b83dbb4344372a233844b1d006ced.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="Intel怪兽峡谷NUC拆解：8升容纳全长显卡、暴力风扇" h="506" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210719/s_003b83dbb4344372a233844b1d006ced.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>处理器这一款<strong>自带最高端i9-11900KB</strong>，但不同于桌面14nm Rocket Lake家族的i9-11900K，<a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/759/759854.htm" target="_blank">它其实属于10nm Tiger Lake-H45系列</a>，BGA整合封装，8核心16线程，基准频率3.3GHz，单核睿频最高4.9GHz，TVB加速最高5.3GHz，24MB三级缓存，集成32单元核显，热设计功耗65W。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210719/4e7b1f4e0a0e453fad71459bdef8ce9a.png" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="Intel怪兽峡谷NUC拆解：8升容纳全长显卡、暴力风扇" h="667" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210719/s_4e7b1f4e0a0e453fad71459bdef8ce9a.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>同系列还有i7-11700B、i5-11500B、i3-1100B。</p>
