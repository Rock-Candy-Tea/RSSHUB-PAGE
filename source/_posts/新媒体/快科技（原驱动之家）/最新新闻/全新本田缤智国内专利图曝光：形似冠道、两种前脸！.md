<p>近日，全新一代本田缤智国内专利图曝光，结合此前网络上曝光的2022年东风本田新车规划可知，<strong>新一代XR-V/缤智将于今年内上市</strong>。</p>

<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220207/5b02526ece10402fb5c244fdf408bea6.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="全新本田缤智国内专利图曝光：形似冠道、两种前脸！" h="350" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220207/s_5b02526ece10402fb5c244fdf408bea6.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220207/ff973061858a4bdba2b0ce9da661a4dc.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="全新本田缤智国内专利图曝光：形似冠道、两种前脸！" h="229" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220207/s_ff973061858a4bdba2b0ce9da661a4dc.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220207/d692c2fbf8c14f06b8df237d35d9ddfd.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="全新本田缤智国内专利图曝光：形似冠道、两种前脸！" h="347" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220207/s_d692c2fbf8c14f06b8df237d35d9ddfd.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>从图片来看，新一代缤智整体造型变化较大，更加偏向于自家大哥冠道，视觉效果较宽。</p>
<p>值得注意的是，<strong>新车将提供两种不同风格的前脸供消费者选择</strong>，其中点阵式中网更为精致，而横条状格栅更有新能源车的感觉。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220207/3dfa7528ce9347bebca29f9bde952fe2.png" target="_blank"><img alt="全新本田缤智国内专利图曝光：形似冠道、两种前脸！" h="377" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220207/s_3dfa7528ce9347bebca29f9bde952fe2.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220207/e43cddb2fdfa4780a16008f2afcc812b.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="全新本田缤智国内专利图曝光：形似冠道、两种前脸！" h="399" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220207/s_e43cddb2fdfa4780a16008f2afcc812b.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>与现款缤智相同，新车同样使用了后门隐藏式门把手，车顶的线条比较平直，而直到后排接近尾部才有明显的溜背式效果，如此可保证车内乘客的头部空间。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220207/f06087e55bc74a33848033ad7a9e5afa.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="全新本田缤智国内专利图曝光：形似冠道、两种前脸！" h="399" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220207/s_f06087e55bc74a33848033ad7a9e5afa.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>不出意外，新车同样用上了贯穿式的尾灯，这点跟UR-V又有几分相像。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220207/4cbc62af29ac43b39340452d954a7b07.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="全新本田缤智国内专利图曝光：形似冠道、两种前脸！" h="399" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220207/s_4cbc62af29ac43b39340452d954a7b07.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>内饰未知，不过结合国外已经发布的车型官图可知，全新缤智内饰更加精致化，<strong>配备了全新样式的三幅式多功能方向盘，同时用上了新一代思域同款悬浮式中控屏</strong>。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220207/60e271d2df924f66b7bb37a388104c68.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="全新本田缤智国内专利图曝光：形似冠道、两种前脸！" h="450" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220207/s_60e271d2df924f66b7bb37a388104c68.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>动力部分，<strong>预计全新缤智将提供1.5L和1.5T两种汽油发动机</strong>，与之匹配的仍旧是CVT变速箱。</p>
<p>此外，新缤智在日本还提供双电机混合动力系统的e:HEV车型，至于国产后会不会保留混动版，目前仍旧是个未知数。</p>
