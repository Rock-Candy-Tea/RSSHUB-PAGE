<div>   
<p>今天，红魔游戏手机官方正式宣布，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>将与《变形金刚》合作联名，推出大黄蜂限量典藏版红魔7S Pro手机。</strong></span></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220708/4330184c06664cf69acc96fd58f87c14.png" target="_blank"><img alt="《变形金刚》IP联名！红魔7S Pro官宣关大黄蜂限量典藏版" h="360" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220708/s_4330184c06664cf69acc96fd58f87c14.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>这款联名款红魔7S Pro采用了大黄蜂标志性的黄黑撞色设计，机身背板以橙黄色为主色调，并搭配带有凯夫拉纹理的黑色先调整为点缀和装饰。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220708/bdd404782ecf426c8d081c7ed538e6ed.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《变形金刚》IP联名！红魔7S Pro官宣关大黄蜂限量典藏版" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220708/s_bdd404782ecf426c8d081c7ed538e6ed.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>同时，红魔7S Pro“氘锋银翼透明版”的透明后壳设计也在大黄蜂联名版上得到了延续，进一步增加了手机的科技感。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220708/8c8a73131ecf4a8884994f69f7eb5b3a.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《变形金刚》IP联名！红魔7S Pro官宣关大黄蜂限量典藏版" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220708/s_8c8a73131ecf4a8884994f69f7eb5b3a.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>除了手机本体外，<strong>此次推出的大黄蜂限量典藏版套装还包括了专门定制设计的包装盒、手机壳、氮化镓充电器、数据线、散热背夹、以及取卡针和一枚大黄蜂主题硬币。</strong></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220708/96c97e1109134d9fac66a2b9add876e0.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《变形金刚》IP联名！红魔7S Pro官宣关大黄蜂限量典藏版" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220708/s_96c97e1109134d9fac66a2b9add876e0.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>值得一提的是，此次的大黄蜂联名版并不是红魔与《变形金刚》IP的第一次合作，此前，官方就曾为红魔7系列推出了擎天柱与霸天虎的联名限定版。</p>
<p>此次的红魔7S Pro大黄蜂联名版，<strong>将在7月11日的发布会上，与系列其他产品同步亮相。</strong></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220708/3d50e316aed24e1f913d4caccdf7bac3.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《变形金刚》IP联名！红魔7S Pro官宣关大黄蜂限量典藏版" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220708/s_3d50e316aed24e1f913d4caccdf7bac3.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
