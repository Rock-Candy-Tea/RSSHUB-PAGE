<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">HPV</span>疫苗（人<span style="background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial; color: rgb(51, 51, 51);">乳头瘤病毒疫苗</span>）是全球目前唯一能预防宫颈癌症的疫苗，女性需求很大，现在国内又一个城市——石家庄宣布今年将为<span lang="EN-US" style="background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial; font-family: "Open Sans", sans-serif; color: rgb(51, 51, 51);">14</span><span style="background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial; color: rgb(51, 51, 51);">周岁女孩免费接种</span><span lang="EN-US" style="background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial; font-family: "Open Sans", sans-serif; color: rgb(51, 51, 51);">HPV</span><span style="background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial; color: rgb(51, 51, 51);">疫苗。</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，石家庄市第十五届人民代表大会第二次会议公告显示，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>会议上人大代表票决确定的石家庄市人民政府<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>件民生实事项目中包括：为全市<span lang="EN-US">14</span>周岁女孩免费接种<span lang="EN-US">HPV</span>疫苗。 <span style="font-family:"MS Gothic";mso-bidi-font-family:"MS Gothic"">​​​​</span></strong></span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:"MS Gothic";mso-fareast-font-family:等线;
mso-fareast-theme-font:minor-fareast;mso-bidi-font-family:"MS Gothic""></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在我国，宫颈癌是第二大女性恶性肿瘤，每年新发病例<span lang="EN-US">11</span>万左右。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">世卫组织建议，青春前期即<span lang="EN-US">9—14</span>岁是<span lang="EN-US">HPV</span>疫苗的最佳接种年龄，国家卫健委根据我国国情建议首要接种的目标人群是<span lang="EN-US">13—15</span>岁的女孩。然而，我国适龄女孩<span lang="EN-US">HPV</span>疫苗接种率不足<span lang="EN-US">1%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>国产上市的主要是<span lang="EN-US">2</span>价<span lang="EN-US">HPV</span>疫苗，不过国内有<span lang="EN-US">14</span>价<span lang="EN-US">HPV</span>疫苗在试验中。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">目前<span lang="EN-US">2</span>价<span lang="EN-US">HPV</span>疫苗对宫颈癌的保护性在<span lang="EN-US">70%</span>；<span lang="EN-US">9</span>价<span lang="EN-US">HPV</span>疫苗增加了<span lang="EN-US">5</span>个高危致癌的<span lang="EN-US">HPV</span>病毒型（另<span lang="EN-US">2</span>个为不引起癌症的<span lang="EN-US">HPV 6</span>型和<span lang="EN-US">11</span>型），其对宫颈癌的保护性大约在<span lang="EN-US">90%</span>。</p>
