<p class="MsoNormal">在先进半导体工艺上，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>目前最新的是<span lang="EN-US">10nm</span>工艺，已经落后于台积电、三星。新上任的<span lang="EN-US">CEO</span>基辛格决心用几年时间重新超越，未来几年将会投入<span lang="EN-US">200</span>亿美元建设先进工艺晶圆厂。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>下一个目标是<span lang="EN-US">7nm</span>工艺，这几天的台北电脑展上，基辛格称司已经完成<span lang="EN-US">7nm Meteor Lake</span>芯片的<span lang="EN-US">“Tape-in”</span>。<span lang="EN-US">Tape-in</span>在<span lang="EN-US">Tape-Out</span>（流片）前，大概是<span lang="EN-US">IP</span>模块完成设计验证阶段。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong><span lang="EN-US">Meteor Lake</span>（流星湖）是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>第一代<span lang="EN-US">7nm</span>客户端处理器，计划<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年开始出货，</strong><span lang="EN-US">7nm</span>数据中心处理器<span lang="EN-US">Granite Rapids</span>也会在同年交付。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">再往下就是<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>工艺了，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>官方是没有给出什么明确信息，不过有投行分析师透露了<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>工艺的水平，晶体管密度大约在<span lang="EN-US">400MTr/mm2</span>，也就是每平方毫米<span lang="EN-US">4</span>亿晶体管，差不多是<span lang="EN-US">Intel 10nm</span>工艺的<span lang="EN-US">4</span>倍了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">对比其他厂商呢？<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>专家指出台积电的<span lang="EN-US">2nm</span>工艺的晶体管密度也就是<span lang="EN-US">500MTr/mm2</span>，<span lang="EN-US">5</span>亿晶体管每平方毫米，双方的密度差距只有<span lang="EN-US">20%</span>左右，<span lang="EN-US">Intel 5nm</span>效能与台积电<span lang="EN-US">2nm</span>差不多。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">但是台积电的优势是在成本上，而且量产的时间也会更早，毕竟<span lang="EN-US">2nm</span>工厂已经取得土地，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>这边<span lang="EN-US">7nm</span>工厂还在建设中，<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>量产还早。</p>
