<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，西部数据<span lang="EN-US">WDC</span>发布了<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>财年<span lang="EN-US">Q3</span>季度财报，当季营收<span lang="EN-US">41.37</span>亿美元，上年同期为<span lang="EN-US">41.75</span>亿美元，同比下滑<span lang="EN-US">1%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">利润方面，运营利润<span lang="EN-US">3.17</span>亿美元，去年同期为<span lang="EN-US">1.53</span>亿美元，同比增长<span lang="EN-US">107%</span>，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>净利润达到了<span lang="EN-US">1.97</span>亿美元，去年同期为<span lang="EN-US">1700</span>万美元，同比大涨<span lang="EN-US">1059%</span>。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">西数目前的业务主要是<span lang="EN-US">HDD</span>机械硬盘及<span lang="EN-US">NAND</span>闪存，其中机械盘营收<span lang="EN-US">19.62</span>亿美元，相比去年同期的<span lang="EN-US">21.14</span>亿美元有所下滑，闪存业务贡献<span lang="EN-US">21.75</span>亿美元，相比去年的<span lang="EN-US">20.61</span>亿美元有所增长。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">机械盘出货量方面，<strong>希捷当季出货<span lang="EN-US">2320</span>万部<span lang="EN-US">HDD</span>硬盘，比去年同期的<span lang="EN-US">2440</span>万部进一步下滑，</strong><span lang="EN-US">ASP</span>均价为<span lang="EN-US">82</span>美元，比去年同期的<span lang="EN-US">85</span>美元略低。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">由于业绩超过市场预期，西数股价大涨<span lang="EN-US">7%</span>，此外西数预期第四财季销售<span lang="EN-US">44-46</span>亿美元，市场预期<span lang="EN-US">42.5</span>亿美元。</p>
