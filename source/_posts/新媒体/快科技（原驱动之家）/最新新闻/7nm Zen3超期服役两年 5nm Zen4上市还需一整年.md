<p class="MsoNormal">自从<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年推出<span lang="EN-US">Zen</span>架构锐龙处理器之后，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>承诺每年推出一代新品，不过<span lang="EN-US">7nm Zen3</span>这一代可能要长寿得多，<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月份发布，今年没有新品，继任者<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>要到<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年底才能发布，还有一整年时间。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>的消息一直牵动人心，因为这是<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>五年来的一次大升级，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>不仅升级<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>工艺和<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>架构，还要支持<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>、<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>、<span lang="EN-US">USB4</span>等先进技术。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">前不久的一次活动上，<a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/794/794880.htm" target="_blank"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>也宣布了<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>处理器的最新路线图</a>，包括<span lang="EN-US">Zen 4 Genoa</span>及<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen 4c</span>，代号<span lang="EN-US">Bergamo</span>（意大利港口贝尔格蒙）。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">从路线图上看，<span lang="EN-US">Zen 4c</span>略晚于<span lang="EN-US">Zen4 Genoa</span>（热那亚）推出，前者最大<span lang="EN-US">128</span>核，后者则是<span lang="EN-US">96</span>核。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">至于消费级的<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>，产品系列应该是锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>，官方没有透露明确的信息，<strong>不过笔记本上<span lang="EN-US">16</span>核、桌面上<span lang="EN-US">32</span>核还是非常有可能的。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">问题是<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>的<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>产能会优先用于服务器级的<span lang="EN-US">EPYC</span>霄龙，桌面版锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>要等到<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月份才能发布，还有比整整一年时间。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在这一年中，<span lang="EN-US">7nm Zen3</span>要超期服役了，<strong>它的升级换代周期至少<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年，是这几代中最长寿的了。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">当然，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>也不是说<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>之前完全没有新品，<span lang="EN-US">3D V-Cache</span>增强版的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">6000</span>系列也没跑了，应该是明年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月份的<span lang="EN-US">CES</span>展会上跟移动版锐龙<span lang="EN-US">6000 APU</span>一起发布，增加额外的<span lang="EN-US">128MB</span>缓存之后，锐龙<span lang="EN-US">6000</span>的游戏性能可提升<span lang="EN-US">15%</span>左右，足以从<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿中赢回不少份额。</p>
