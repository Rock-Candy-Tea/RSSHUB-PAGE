<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年了，<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>处理器随便买都是<span lang="EN-US">8</span>核起步了，高端点的甚至能上<span lang="EN-US">12</span>核甚至<span lang="EN-US">16</span>核，然而拖后腿的还是游戏厂商，即便是育碧最新上市的<span lang="EN-US">3A</span>大作《孤岛惊魂<span lang="EN-US">6</span>》，实测依然是吃<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>单核性能，多核支持不佳。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">跳票多次之后，《孤岛惊魂<span lang="EN-US">6</span>》已于<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日发售，画质技术还是很先进的，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>次世代主机支持<span lang="EN-US">4K/60</span>帧，<span lang="EN-US">PC</span>版有光追反射和阴影，<span lang="EN-US">Fidelity FX</span>（<span lang="EN-US">FSR</span>，<span lang="EN-US">CAS</span>），无锁帧，超宽屏幕，以及可调节的<span lang="EN-US">FOV</span>。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">硬件要求上，不考虑光追的话，最低配置只要锐龙<span lang="EN-US">3 1200</span>或者<span lang="EN-US">i5-4460</span>处理器，<span lang="EN-US">RX 560</span>及<span lang="EN-US">GTX 960</span>显卡，<span lang="EN-US">8GB</span>内存即可。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">推荐配置的话，<span lang="EN-US">1080p</span>、<span lang="EN-US">60fps</span>下需要的<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>是锐龙<span lang="EN-US">5 3600X/</span>酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i7-7700</span>，显卡则是<span lang="EN-US">RX Vega64</span>或者<span lang="EN-US">GTX 1080</span>，内存要求<span lang="EN-US">16GB</span>，这个配置也不算苛刻。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">实际表现如何呢？<span lang="EN-US">DSOGaming</span>使用了两套平台做了测试，一套高端的是酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i9-9900K</span>、<span lang="EN-US">16GB</span>内存及<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3080</span>显卡，另一套是<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>比较老的配置，配备了酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i7-3930K</span>、<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3070</span>显卡。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">经过验证，《孤岛惊魂<span lang="EN-US">6</span>》对<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>的单核性能要求很高，但多核支持不佳，<strong><span lang="EN-US">9900K</span>在<span lang="EN-US">1080p/2K</span>下都不能稳定跑<span lang="EN-US">60fps</span>，而在更早的<span lang="EN-US">3930K</span>处理器上，<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>占用率只有<span lang="EN-US">40%</span>，显卡占用率也只有<span lang="EN-US">60%</span>，中等画质也支持不了<span lang="EN-US">60fps</span>，性能严重浪费。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">总之，在<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年多核<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>如此普及的时候，游戏开发商似乎对多核<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>的优化依然不到位。</p>
