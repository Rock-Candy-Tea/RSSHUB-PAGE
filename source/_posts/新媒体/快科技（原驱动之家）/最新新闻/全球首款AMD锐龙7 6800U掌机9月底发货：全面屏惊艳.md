<p>这两年，AYANEO掌机相当活跃，尤其喜欢使用AMD锐龙U系列低功耗平台，最新款AYANEO 2更是首发了AMD新一代锐龙7 6800U。</p>
<p>现在，AYANEO宣布，<strong><span style="color:#ff0000;">AYAENO 2将在9月底首批发货，10月份大批量发货。</span></strong></p>
<p>另外，<strong>采用上一代锐龙5000U系列的AYANEO Air现已开始发货，8月份完成发货；AYANEO Air Pro 8月底发货、9月底完成发货。</strong></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220725/da55750477f64daca17b0099b562ccb1.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="全球首款AMD锐龙7 6800U掌机9月底发货：全面屏惊艳" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220725/s_da55750477f64daca17b0099b562ccb1.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220725/3ec27d983b6f49b48ccc107f89a7db59.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="全球首款AMD锐龙7 6800U掌机9月底发货：全面屏惊艳" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220725/s_3ec27d983b6f49b48ccc107f89a7db59.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>AYAENO 2采用的锐龙7 6800U基于6nm工艺、Zen3+ CPU架构、RDNA2 GPU架构，8核心16线程，频率2.7-4.7GHz，二级缓存4MB，三级缓存16MB，12个图形单元，频率2.2GHz，热设计功耗15-28W。</p>
<p>AYAENO 2为其搭配了<strong>全新的单风扇、双铜管散热架构</strong>，号称久玩不烫，还有大电池和快充。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220725/d77936027d874b989dc36954959c79bf.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="全球首款AMD锐龙7 6800U掌机9月底发货：全面屏惊艳" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220725/s_d77936027d874b989dc36954959c79bf.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220725/2b77c092e6f7448f9fb56011d7d0e749.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="全球首款AMD锐龙7 6800U掌机9月底发货：全面屏惊艳" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220725/s_2b77c092e6f7448f9fb56011d7d0e749.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>它还号称<strong>全球第一款全面屏掌机，7英寸IPS屏幕，分辨率1920x1080</strong>，正面覆盖一整块高级玻璃。</p>
<p>机身设计灵感来自婴儿睡姿，而且360度无外露螺丝，搭配独家的Master全能手柄，专业电竞级操控体验。</p>
