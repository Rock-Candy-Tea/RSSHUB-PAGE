<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">前几天<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>公布了<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Alder Lake</span>处理器的架构细节，这一代升级很多，除了<span lang="EN-US">Intel 7</span>工艺之外，还有首次大小核架构，桌面版支持<span lang="EN-US">8</span>大<span lang="EN-US">16</span>小，同时还支持<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存及<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">与此同时，<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿的主板也会升级，从目前的<span lang="EN-US">LGA1200</span>换成<span lang="EN-US">LGA1700</span>插槽，肯定不会兼容了，需要新的<span lang="EN-US">600</span>系芯片组。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此前我们知道<span lang="EN-US">600</span>系芯片组会有<span lang="EN-US">Z690</span>、<span lang="EN-US">B660</span>之类的，现在<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的芯片组驱动中一下子曝光了所有型号，具体如下：</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong><span lang="EN-US">X699</span>（<span lang="EN-US">HEDT</span>型号）</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Z690</span>（高端消费机型）</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">W685</span>（工作站高端机型）</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">W680</span>（工作站中档机型）</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Q670</span>（公司<span lang="EN-US">/</span>企业型号）</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Q670E</span>（公司<span lang="EN-US">/</span>企业笔记本型号）</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">R680E</span>（嵌入式设备企业型号）</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">H670</span>（中档消费机型）</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">B660</span>（中档消费机型）</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">H610</span>（入门消费机型）</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">H610E</span>（嵌入式设备入门机型）</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">可见<span lang="EN-US">600</span>系芯片组的覆盖范围很广，从<span lang="EN-US">W</span>系列工作站到<span lang="EN-US">Q</span>企业<span lang="EN-US">/</span>笔记本再到<span lang="EN-US">Z/H</span>消费级桌面平台全都有了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>这里面最让人兴奋的应该是<span lang="EN-US">X699</span>，因为<span lang="EN-US">X</span>后缀代表着酷睿<span lang="EN-US">X</span>系列，也就是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>最高端的消费级<span lang="EN-US">HEDT</span>平台，取代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i9-10980XE</span>系列的。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">自从<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年推出<span lang="EN-US">14nm</span>的<span lang="EN-US">Cascade-X</span>系列之后，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的<span lang="EN-US">HEDT</span>平台已经<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年多没升级了，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>主要原因是<span lang="EN-US">18</span>核的架构已经跟不上友商的<span lang="EN-US">64</span>核锐龙<span lang="EN-US">TR</span>平台了，所以<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>这两年事实上放弃了<span lang="EN-US">1000</span>美元以上的高端<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>市场。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="又要换插槽了 12代酷睿配套600系主板全曝光：酷睿X归来" h="428" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210822/339dcfa5-4e7b-41d7-85b9-01fec6721c3b.jpg" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">X699</span>的出现意味着新的酷睿<span lang="EN-US">X</span>平台也要来了，不过它应该不是基于<span lang="EN-US">Alder Lake</span>的，而是服务器级的<span lang="EN-US">Sapphire Rapids</span>，但<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>核心架构跟<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿的<span lang="EN-US">Golden Cove</span>同款，只是工艺还是<span lang="EN-US">10nm SuperFin</span>，而不是<span lang="EN-US">Alder Lake</span>的<span lang="EN-US">10nm Enchanced SuperFin</span>（现在改名为<span lang="EN-US">Intel 7</span>了）。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/754/754751.htm" target="_blank"><span lang="EN-US">Sapphire Rapids</span>系列的亮点也不少</a>，最多<span lang="EN-US">80</span>核心，支持<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>，最高<span lang="EN-US">8</span>通道，也支持<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>，<span lang="EN-US">TDP</span>上限从<span lang="EN-US">270W</span>提高到<span lang="EN-US">350W</span>，据说还能解锁<span lang="EN-US">400W</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">当然，用于桌面<span lang="EN-US">HEDT</span>平台的<span lang="EN-US">Sapphire Rapids</span>肯定会阉割不少，<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>核心估计在<span lang="EN-US">60</span>核左右的可能性更大，同时支持<span lang="EN-US">4</span>通道或者<span lang="EN-US">6</span>通道<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>。</p>
