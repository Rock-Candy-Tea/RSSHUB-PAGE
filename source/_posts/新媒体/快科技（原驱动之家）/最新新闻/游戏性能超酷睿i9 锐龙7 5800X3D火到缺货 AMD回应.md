<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">上周<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>正式解禁了一款锐龙<span lang="EN-US">5000</span>系列处理器新品——锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7 5800X3D</span>，它使用了<span lang="EN-US">3D V-Cache</span>技术，总缓存高达<span lang="EN-US">100MB</span>，提升了游戏性能，甚至超越了友商的酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i9=12900K</span>处理器，夺回了最快游戏处理器的称号。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7 5800X3D</span>海外售价<span lang="EN-US">449</span>美元，国内售价是<span lang="EN-US">3099</span>元，<strong>价格上也比酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i9</span>更有性价比，所以上市之后还是很受欢迎的，之前<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>高管确认美国市场上锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7 5800X3D</span>首发一天就售罄了。</strong></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="游戏性能超酷睿i9 锐龙7 5800X3D火到缺货 AMD回应" h="299" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220426/df23205c-e86e-4f9c-b919-d2d2a60ca528.jpg" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">欧洲市场上也差不多，锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7 5800X3D</span>很抢手，目前不少电商平台已经缺货，甚至还有黄牛加价炒作，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>对此<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>已经表示正在加快补货了，不过何时送到经销商那里就不好说了，当前情况下影响因素太多。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7 5800X3D</span>依然是<span lang="EN-US">8</span>核心<span lang="EN-US">16</span>线程，不过基准频率从<span lang="EN-US">3.8GHz</span>降低至<span lang="EN-US">3.4GHz</span>，最高加速频率从<span lang="EN-US">4.7GHz</span>降低至<span lang="EN-US">4.5GHz</span>，从而维持热设计功耗维持在<span lang="EN-US">105W</span>。</p>
