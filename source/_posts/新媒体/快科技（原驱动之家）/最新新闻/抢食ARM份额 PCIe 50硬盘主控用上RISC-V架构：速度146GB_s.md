<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>是低功耗芯片中的王者，近年来还想着抢<span lang="EN-US">x86</span>处理器的高性能市场份额，不过<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>也有自己的麻烦，那就是开源的<span lang="EN-US">RISC-V</span>。在高性能<span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>主控上，韩国公司<span lang="EN-US">FADU</span>就使用<span lang="EN-US">RISC-V</span>而非<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>架构，实现了<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0 x4</span>高性能。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">韩国初创公司<span lang="EN-US">FADU</span>日前宣布了自研的<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>主控芯片<span lang="EN-US">FC5161</span>，主要用于数据中心，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>支持<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0 x4</span>通道，读取速度<span lang="EN-US">14.6GB/s</span>，写入也有<span lang="EN-US">10.4GB/s</span>，随机读取<span lang="EN-US">IOPS</span>高达<span lang="EN-US">340</span>万，随机写入<span lang="EN-US">IOPS</span>也有<span lang="EN-US">73.5</span>万。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">目前<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>硬盘还比较少，东芝之前公布的新品性能甚至只有<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 4.0</span>的水平，<span lang="EN-US">FADUA</span>的这款<span lang="EN-US">PCie 5.0</span>主控的性能可以说是目前最强的之一了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">快科技获悉，他们的<span lang="EN-US">FC5161</span>主控采用的内核也公布了，不是常见的<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>架构，<strong>而是<span lang="EN-US">SiFive</span>公司的<span lang="EN-US">S5</span>系列<span lang="EN-US">RISC-V</span>内核，专门为嵌入式市场设备的。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在<span lang="EN-US">FADU</span>公司之前，虽然<span lang="EN-US">RISC-V</span>内核也被用于硬盘主控，但之前主打的多是低功耗，性能达到<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>顶流水准的非常少，这对<span lang="EN-US">RISC-V</span>的推广很有帮助。</p>
