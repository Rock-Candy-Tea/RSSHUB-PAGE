<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年对<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>来说很关键，除了<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>架构的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">/</span>霄龙处理器之外，显卡也要升级到<span lang="EN-US">Radeon 7000</span>系列，基于<span lang="EN-US">RDNA3</span>架构，首次采用小芯片架构，<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>及<span lang="EN-US">6nm</span>芯片合体，最新传闻功耗超过<span lang="EN-US">400W</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在未来一代显卡上，<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>及<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>殊途同归，两家都会通过大规模提升计算核心、增加核心面积、放宽<span lang="EN-US">TDP</span>功耗的方式来大幅提升性能，只是两家的具体实现方式不同。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>这边的<span lang="EN-US">RDNA3</span>架构显卡会使用小芯片设计，其中旗舰显卡<span lang="EN-US">RX 7900 XT</span>的<span lang="EN-US">Navi31</span>系列核心会采用双芯片封装，<strong>分为两个所谓的<span lang="EN-US">GCD</span>模块<span lang="EN-US">(</span>概念等同锐龙处理器里的<span lang="EN-US">CCD)</span>，<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>工艺制造，以及一个<span lang="EN-US">MCD</span>模块<span lang="EN-US">(</span>类似锐龙的<span lang="EN-US">IOD)</span>，<span lang="EN-US">6nm</span>工艺制造。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Navi 31</span>会集成多达<span lang="EN-US">15360</span>个流处理器<span lang="EN-US">(ALU</span>单元<span lang="EN-US">)</span>、<span lang="EN-US">512MB</span>无限缓存，分别是现在<span lang="EN-US">Navi 21</span>核心的三倍、四倍，而显存依然是<span lang="EN-US">256-bit GDDR6</span>，这些参数差不多了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">最新消息显示，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">RDNA3</span>架构显卡的频率依然可以达到<span lang="EN-US">2.4GHz</span>到<span lang="EN-US">2.5GHz</span>，<span lang="EN-US">FP32</span>浮点性能可达<span lang="EN-US">75TFLOPS</span>，这将是<span lang="EN-US">RX 6900 XT</span>显卡的 三倍，也就是说性能提升<span lang="EN-US">200%</span>。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不过这个主要是指浮点运算能力，游戏性能还要看光栅性能，提升幅度约为<span lang="EN-US">150%</span>，没有浮点那么强大，但也足够让人惊讶了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">RX 7900 XT</span>的性能很吸引人，但是代价也不小，<strong>爆料称其核心面积将达到<span lang="EN-US">800mm2</span>，而且功耗会超过<span lang="EN-US">400W</span></strong>，毕竟性能越高，功耗就低不了。</p>
