<p>7月27日，华为发布了旗下首款搭载HarmonyOS 3的打印机，HUAWEI PixLab V1彩色喷墨打印机。</p>
<p class="newsinfo_vedio"><iframe align="middle" id="kkj_video_play_iframe1" src="//blog.mydrivers.com/news/showvideo.aspx?tid=849818&ac=img&url=https://weibo.com/tv/show/1034:4798065534763043&k=1" frameborder="0" width="600px" height="400px" scrolling="no"></iframe></p>
<p>借助全新的HarmonyOS 3系统，华为再一次向我们诠释了什么是简单、智慧、稳定的打印机。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/b4bd0b0356c34d7ab33572501166b62a.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="简单稳定、前所未见！HUAWEI PixLab V1彩色喷墨打印机体验" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/s_b4bd0b0356c34d7ab33572501166b62a.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>此外，PixLab V1还给我们带来了诸如原色打印、AI字迹擦除等等一大堆令人惊喜的技术。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/27625a3674384e499d62e4adf0cef1e9.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="简单稳定、前所未见！HUAWEI PixLab V1彩色喷墨打印机体验" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/s_27625a3674384e499d62e4adf0cef1e9.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>HUAWEI PixLab V1延续了华为一贯的极简美学设计理念，放在家里任何地方，都能完美融入家居环境，除了隐藏式的触控面板外，整个机身都是白色。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/53c1f18da8734b0a90ebe83f25107746.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="简单稳定、前所未见！HUAWEI PixLab V1彩色喷墨打印机体验" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/s_53c1f18da8734b0a90ebe83f25107746.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>PixLab V1采用的是分离式墨仓设计，有红、黄、蓝、黑四个墨仓，使用前要先将墨仓安装进打印机。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/b5ac81e5b0c049218ebdad96c0a52b34.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="简单稳定、前所未见！HUAWEI PixLab V1彩色喷墨打印机体验" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/s_b5ac81e5b0c049218ebdad96c0a52b34.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>彩色墨盒的打印寿命是4000页，黑白打印的寿命是7500页。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/90d6db9c7acd40b3ab500abec527a91a.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="简单稳定、前所未见！HUAWEI PixLab V1彩色喷墨打印机体验" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/s_90d6db9c7acd40b3ab500abec527a91a.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>算下来，打印一页黑白文档，成本还不到4分钱，比绝大多数激光打印机成本更低，1个彩色喷头、1个黑色喷头，很简单就能装进去。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/22f7099f799748379f8c8deb02a6815f.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="简单稳定、前所未见！HUAWEI PixLab V1彩色喷墨打印机体验" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/s_22f7099f799748379f8c8deb02a6815f.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>针对喷墨打印喷头容易堵塞的问题，特别是断电状态下长期不适用，喷头更容易被灰尘堵塞，PixLab V1设计了三重防堵方案。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/3ca0cae842f14b43b779f72684b3e35e.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="简单稳定、前所未见！HUAWEI PixLab V1彩色喷墨打印机体验" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/s_3ca0cae842f14b43b779f72684b3e35e.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>在手机端，或者PC客户端，可以设置智能冲洗模式，定时清洗喷头。只要没有停电，即便是打印机进入了休眠模式，也会定时清洗喷头，大大降低了喷头堵塞概率。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/99ef460ac88040d78ef3cae2f1bf90cf.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="简单稳定、前所未见！HUAWEI PixLab V1彩色喷墨打印机体验" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/s_99ef460ac88040d78ef3cae2f1bf90cf.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>打印机在打击状态下，轻触触控面板即可唤醒操作。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/be9816a043db43388d265d6f3f478bd1.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="简单稳定、前所未见！HUAWEI PixLab V1彩色喷墨打印机体验" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/s_be9816a043db43388d265d6f3f478bd1.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>中间有一个ID复印按钮，这是专门用来复制各种证件的。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/e5e05f24319d4b1ba644814280641cf6.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="简单稳定、前所未见！HUAWEI PixLab V1彩色喷墨打印机体验" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/s_e5e05f24319d4b1ba644814280641cf6.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>华为在PixLab V1打印机中加入了“AI智慧校正”技术，无论怎么摆放证件，最终都能居中打印出来，给了强迫症患者一种莫名的舒适感，有了这个功能，打印证件的时候完全不需要操心，随便一扔，剩下的就交给华为PixLab V1。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/d21600a7621740968a6a45e223419319.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="简单稳定、前所未见！HUAWEI PixLab V1彩色喷墨打印机体验" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/s_d21600a7621740968a6a45e223419319.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>下面有一个HUAWEI Share图标，只需要你打开想要打印的照片或者文件，轻轻一碰就能开始打印了。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/74a5630a4864470f8b3e84180babcb77.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="简单稳定、前所未见！HUAWEI PixLab V1彩色喷墨打印机体验" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/s_74a5630a4864470f8b3e84180babcb77.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>1、极简配网：对于华为手机或者电脑的用户来说，连网变得极其简单，比如我们手上这台Mate 30 Pro，只需要把手机靠近PixLab V1，就能将打印机连上手机开始打印。整个过程不过20秒钟。华为的智慧生态恐怖如斯。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/c00bce1912844860935aa1e5b87d25f0.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="简单稳定、前所未见！HUAWEI PixLab V1彩色喷墨打印机体验" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/s_c00bce1912844860935aa1e5b87d25f0.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>2、共享打印：配好网络之后，只需要在智慧生活APP中，点击右上角，将打印机分享给亲朋好友，一人配网，全家都能共享打印。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/f29255b2dddf4e0196bdd9f9580fff63.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="简单稳定、前所未见！HUAWEI PixLab V1彩色喷墨打印机体验" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/s_f29255b2dddf4e0196bdd9f9580fff63.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>3、远程打印：在智慧生活APP中，开启远程打印。无论在任何地方，都可以放在家里的PixLab V1将手机里面的资料，试题打印出来。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/89a29ac27b2f4c29a8cf52d9f8e8a2f8.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="简单稳定、前所未见！HUAWEI PixLab V1彩色喷墨打印机体验" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/s_89a29ac27b2f4c29a8cf52d9f8e8a2f8.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>搭载了HarmonyOS 3的PixLab V1打印机，其智慧功能超乎你的想象。PixLab V1打印机加入了超级终端功能，打开手机的超级终端，一拉即合，所有的打印入口归一，想打什么，点击悬浮球即可，任何内容都可以直接打印出来。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/d0859a2ca8404d76aaf1053b1848aa92.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="简单稳定、前所未见！HUAWEI PixLab V1彩色喷墨打印机体验" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/s_d0859a2ca8404d76aaf1053b1848aa92.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>已经做过的试卷，想要重做一遍怎么办？</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/1e0dbed45dfc45aba9995b840e390ae2.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="简单稳定、前所未见！HUAWEI PixLab V1彩色喷墨打印机体验" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/s_1e0dbed45dfc45aba9995b840e390ae2.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>在智慧生活APP教育专区中，点击字迹擦除。把试卷放进打印机扫描，PixLab V1就能把试卷上的字迹全部擦除，然后将试卷重新打印出来，这效果跟全新的试卷没啥俩样。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/6659491608fd4d01b73eb70460ecfa31.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="简单稳定、前所未见！HUAWEI PixLab V1彩色喷墨打印机体验" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/s_6659491608fd4d01b73eb70460ecfa31.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>不同的显示设备，由于屏幕素质，显示原理的差异，同一张照片，在不同的屏幕上显示的色彩往往偏差很大。同时你也会发现，打印出来的照片，色彩和屏幕上的完全是2回事。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/047d0ce874d842cd8ccdd284167c9f63.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="简单稳定、前所未见！HUAWEI PixLab V1彩色喷墨打印机体验" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/s_047d0ce874d842cd8ccdd284167c9f63.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>华为基于3DLut色彩调优、色温跟随、产线孔径分级校准这三个技术，开发出了原色打印引擎。它能将华为终端设备上的色温同步到打印机上，实现了“所见即所得”的彩色打印效果。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/5cdfc8f7254c408e9520038e69a2f991.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="简单稳定、前所未见！HUAWEI PixLab V1彩色喷墨打印机体验" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/s_5cdfc8f7254c408e9520038e69a2f991.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>这就是我用PixLab V1原色打印技术，打印出来的照片，大家都能看到，和手机上的颜色几乎几乎做到了一模一样。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/8abf269842084ba087a21df27612dc86.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="简单稳定、前所未见！HUAWEI PixLab V1彩色喷墨打印机体验" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/s_8abf269842084ba087a21df27612dc86.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>想想以前我们是怎么使用打印机的，安装各种驱动，各种连接设置、动不动就连不上，PixLab V1我们体验了2天时间，我们常常会触发一种感触：打印竟然还能这样用...</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/9226f41344c54afcb73c66100e764fa5.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="简单稳定、前所未见！HUAWEI PixLab V1彩色喷墨打印机体验" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/s_9226f41344c54afcb73c66100e764fa5.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>不论是使用体验，还是智慧功能，PixLab V1都远远超越了市面上同价位的喷墨和激光打印机。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/495eddf5e7904b30a6a24532d3fa0f59.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="简单稳定、前所未见！HUAWEI PixLab V1彩色喷墨打印机体验" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220802/s_495eddf5e7904b30a6a24532d3fa0f59.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>华为将于8月3日正式开售，首发优惠100元，到手价1399元。不知道这样一款智慧加持的鸿蒙彩色打印机，能否打动你呢？</p>
