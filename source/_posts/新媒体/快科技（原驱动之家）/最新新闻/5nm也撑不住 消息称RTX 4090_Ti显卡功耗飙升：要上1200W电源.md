<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年最受期待的<span lang="EN-US">PC</span>产品中，<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>的<span lang="EN-US">RTX 40</span>系显卡名列前茅，预计今年下半年发布，升级台积电<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>工艺及<span lang="EN-US">Lovelace</span>架构，旗舰<span lang="EN-US">RTX 4090.</span>及<span lang="EN-US">RTX 4090 Ti</span>性能据说能翻倍。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此前消息称，<span lang="EN-US">5nm Lovelace</span>架构的大核心编号将是<span lang="EN-US">AD102</span>，将有多达<span lang="EN-US">12</span>个<span lang="EN-US">GPC(</span>图形处理集群<span lang="EN-US">)</span>、<span lang="EN-US">72</span>个<span lang="EN-US">TPC(</span>纹理处理集群<span lang="EN-US">)</span>、<span lang="EN-US">144</span>个<span lang="EN-US">SM(</span>流式多处理器<span lang="EN-US">)</span>，而每个<span lang="EN-US">SM</span>继续<span lang="EN-US">128</span>个流处理器<span lang="EN-US">(CUDA</span>核心<span lang="EN-US">)</span>，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>那么整体下来就有多达<span lang="EN-US">18432</span>个流处理器，相比现在的安培架构<span lang="EN-US">GA102</span>核心的<span lang="EN-US">10752</span>个核心增加了<span lang="EN-US">71</span>％，整体性能提升一倍。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">然而性能大涨的同时，<span lang="EN-US">RTX 40</span>系列显卡的功耗也在飙升，此前消息称其<span lang="EN-US">Total Graphics Power</span>整卡功耗不低于<span lang="EN-US">550W</span>，比现在高出<span lang="EN-US">200W</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在显卡功耗大涨的同时，对电源的要求也提升了，<strong>最新消息称<span lang="EN-US">RTX 4090/Ti</span>显卡搭配<span lang="EN-US">1000W</span>电源已经不够用了，至少要上<span lang="EN-US">1200W</span>电源。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">以往千瓦以上的电源跟主流<span lang="EN-US">DIY</span>玩家没什么交集，但是随着新一代显卡及处理器的到来，可能这都要变日常了，其实不用等到下一代产品，<span lang="EN-US">CES</span>上发布的<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3090 Ti</span>显卡推荐电源都要<span lang="EN-US">1000W</span>了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">如果今年底准备升级全套<span lang="EN-US">PC</span>平台，高端玩家有必要考虑下市面上<span lang="EN-US">1500W</span>级别的电源了。</p>
