<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">在<span lang="EN-US">14nm</span>节点之后，台积电、三星等公司率先了量产了<span lang="EN-US">7nm</span>、<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>工艺，而<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>在这场竞赛中落后了几年，去年才开始用<span lang="EN-US">10nm</span>（改名后为<span lang="EN-US">Intel 7</span>）取代<span lang="EN-US">14nm</span>，不过未来两年中<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>就有可能翻盘，凭借<span lang="EN-US">18A</span>工艺领先台积电的<span lang="EN-US">2nm</span>工艺。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">去年<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>新任<span lang="EN-US">CEO</span>基辛格上任之后，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>在半导体工艺上加快了脚步，虽然一方面在跟台积电合作<span lang="EN-US">3nm</span>甚至<span lang="EN-US">2nm</span>工艺代工，但另一方面也在加强自家晶圆生产，还重新成立晶圆代工部门<span lang="EN-US">IFS</span>跟台积电抢市场。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>跟台积电竞争的关键就是新一代工艺谁能更快量产，特别是关键的<span lang="EN-US">2nm</span>节点，台积电公布的进度是该工艺会在<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年试产，<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年开始量产，可能是下半年或者年底。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>至于<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>这边，转折点在<span lang="EN-US">20A</span>及<span lang="EN-US">18A</span>工艺时代，这是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的<span lang="EN-US">GAA</span>晶体管工艺技术，相当于<span lang="EN-US">2nm</span>及<span lang="EN-US">1.8nm</span>工艺，分别会在<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年上半年及<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年下半年量产，<span lang="EN-US">18A</span>工艺是提前了半年量产的，原定也是<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">这就有可能出现一个结果——<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>下半年的时候，台积电的<span lang="EN-US">2nm</span>工艺刚刚试产，<strong><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的<span lang="EN-US">1.8nm</span>工艺就量产了，芯片工艺上难得领先一次。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">持这一看法的不仅有<span lang="EN-US">The Register</span>、<span lang="EN-US">PC Gamer</span>等海外媒体，还有专业调研机构<span lang="EN-US">IC Knowledge</span>负责人<span lang="EN-US">Scotten Jones</span>，他也认为<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>将在<span lang="EN-US">18A</span>工艺节点战胜台积电。</p>
