<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，<a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/754/754106.htm" target="_blank">“天和核心舱”搭乘长征五号<span lang="EN-US">B</span>遥二运载火箭发射升空，</a>这是国内首个空间站，中国也成为全球第三个独立建造空间站的国家。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">由于“天和核心舱”重达<span lang="EN-US">20</span>多吨，所以这次发射使用的是长征五号<span lang="EN-US">B</span>火箭，专为空间站发射而改进的长征<span lang="EN-US">5</span>号家族火箭。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在发射升空之后，长征五号<span lang="EN-US">B</span>遥二运载火箭的残骸也会在这两天再入大气层，今天中国载人航天工程办公室公众号发布了相关信息。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">据悉，<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日<span lang="EN-US">7</span>时<span lang="EN-US">24</span>分，<strong>长征五号<span lang="EN-US">B</span>遥二运载火箭末级残骸轨道为：近地点高度约<span lang="EN-US">130km</span>，远地点高度约<span lang="EN-US">160km</span>，倾角<span lang="EN-US">41.5°</span>。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">预计再入时间为<span lang="EN-US">O5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日<span lang="EN-US">10</span>时<span lang="EN-US">12</span>分<span lang="EN-US">±15</span>分钟 ，再入区域中心点位于东经<span lang="EN-US">28.38°</span>，北纬<span lang="EN-US">34.43°</span>。 <span style="font-family:"MS Gothic";mso-bidi-font-family:"MS Gothic"">​​​​</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:"MS Gothic";mso-fareast-font-family:等线;
mso-fareast-theme-font:minor-fareast;mso-bidi-font-family:"MS Gothic""></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在此之前，专家们早就做过科普，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>地球<span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:"MS Gothic";mso-fareast-font-family:等线;
mso-fareast-theme-font:minor-fareast;mso-bidi-font-family:"MS Gothic"">71%</span>的面积都是海洋，火箭残骸再入大气层的时候大部分都会烧毁，</strong></span>几乎不可能会对地面产生影响。</p>
