<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>当前一代的游戏卡是<span lang="EN-US">7nm RDNA2</span>架构，计算卡是<span lang="EN-US">7nm CDNA</span>架构，下一代<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>应该会使用<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>工艺（官方一直没确认），不过<span lang="EN-US">Linux</span>代码中近日确认了另一件大事，那就是<span lang="EN-US">MCM</span>多芯片封装。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">由于摩尔定律越来越失效，提高芯片集成度不能只靠工艺微缩了，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>在<span lang="EN-US">7nm Zen2/Zen3</span>处理器中就开始使用<span lang="EN-US">MCM</span>多芯片封装了，<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>跟进也是板上钉钉的，除了游戏卡中的<span lang="EN-US">RNDA3</span>之外，计算卡的<span lang="EN-US">CDNA2</span>都会如此。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Linux</span>内核补丁中日前就泄漏了<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>下代计算卡的信息，应该是下一代的<span lang="EN-US">Radeon Instinct MI200</span>，<strong><span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>代号<span lang="EN-US">Aldebaran</span>（毕宿五星座，<span lang="EN-US">MI100</span>代号是大角星），由<span lang="EN-US">2</span>个<span lang="EN-US">MCM</span>芯片组成，每个芯片集成<span lang="EN-US">4</span>个统一内存控制器，后者又是<span lang="EN-US">8</span>通道，每通道支持<span lang="EN-US">2GB HBM2</span>或者<span lang="EN-US">HBM2e</span>显存。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">这么算起来，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">MI200</span>加速卡配备的显存将达到<span lang="EN-US">128GB HBM2/2e</span>，</strong></span>非常强大，成本估计也高的可怕，不过<span lang="EN-US">MI200</span>会用于<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>新一代的百亿亿次超算中，美国政府部门会买单，贵不是问题。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">MI200</span>加速卡是给<span lang="EN-US">HPC</span>用的，但它用上<span lang="EN-US">MCM</span>架构设计，意味着下代的<span lang="EN-US">RDNA3</span>游戏卡也会是双芯设计，此前爆料称其性能轻松翻倍，只不过配备的显存不会是<span lang="EN-US">HBM2/2e</span>这么奢侈，还是<span lang="EN-US">GDDR6/6X</span>了。</p>
