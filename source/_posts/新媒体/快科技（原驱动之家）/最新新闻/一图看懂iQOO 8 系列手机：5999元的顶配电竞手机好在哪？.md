<p class="MsoNormal">今晚<span lang="EN-US">iQOO 8</span>系列手机正式发布了，主要是<span lang="EN-US">iQOO 8</span>及<span lang="EN-US">iQOO 8 Pro</span>两款型号，前者是骁龙<span lang="EN-US">888</span>处理器，<span lang="EN-US">3799</span>元起，而后者是骁龙<span lang="EN-US">888 Plus</span>，同时还有<span lang="EN-US">2K OLED</span>屏，<span lang="EN-US">50W</span>无线快充及微云台拍照系统，售价<span lang="EN-US">4999</span>元起。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">iQOO</span>的用户都知道该系列手机主打电竞游戏，性能、触控及游戏优化是重点，但在屏幕、拍照等方面的规格有所折衷，但<span lang="EN-US">iQOO 8</span>系列这次不一样了。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210817/bef107adbd384c7b8d52470bc6082128.png" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="一图看懂iQOO 8 系列手机：5999元的顶配电竞手机好在哪？" h="437" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210817/s_bef107adbd384c7b8d52470bc6082128.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">以<span lang="EN-US">iQOO 8 Pro</span>为例，除了升级骁龙<span lang="EN-US">888 Plus</span>、<span lang="EN-US">LPDDR5</span>及<span lang="EN-US">UFS 3.1</span>闪存之外，<strong><span style="color:#ff0000;">还有<span lang="EN-US">3200x1440</span>分辨率的<span lang="EN-US">2K</span>屏，首发三星<span lang="EN-US">E5</span>材料，</span></strong>功耗降低了<span lang="EN-US">25%</span>，亮度可达<span lang="EN-US">1500nit</span>，还支持<span lang="EN-US">1-120Hz LTPO</span>自动刷新率，<span lang="EN-US">2K</span>及<span lang="EN-US">120Hz</span>可以同时开启。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">拍照上，<span lang="EN-US">iQOO 8 Pro</span>还升级了双主摄，今年<span lang="EN-US">X60 Pro</span>系列上主打的微云台技术下放，<span lang="EN-US">5000</span>万像素大底传感器及微云台防抖使得<span lang="EN-US">iQOO 8 Pro</span>的拍照及视频也是顶级水准的。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">其他方面，<span lang="EN-US">iQOO 8</span>系列表现也不俗，包括<span lang="EN-US">120W</span>有线快充，<strong><span lang="EN-US">iQOO 8 Pro</span>还支持<span lang="EN-US">50W</span>无线快充，</strong><span lang="EN-US">43</span>分钟就可以充满，无线快充上的遗憾也不补全了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">iQOO 8 Pro</span>系列售价<span lang="EN-US">4999</span>元起，<span lang="EN-US">12+512GB</span>顶配达到了<span lang="EN-US">5999</span>元，这样的价格对你有吸引力吗？</p>
