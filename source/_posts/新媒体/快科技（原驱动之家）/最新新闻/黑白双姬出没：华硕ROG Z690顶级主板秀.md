<p>11月20日至21日，核聚变2021 Tour广州站电玩嘉年华在广州保利世贸博览馆盛大召开。</p>
<p>作为DIY市场的领军厂商，<strong>华硕精心设计了RO姬、鬼灭之刃、赛车、天选姬四大主题互动体验区，带来了一系列硬核的ROG游戏装备，尤其是最新Z690 12代酷睿中主板、鬼灭之刃联名电竞成为焦点，RO姬、雪武战姬形态、鬼灭之刃真人COSer秀也纷纷亮相。</strong></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211121/56b9484405cd44bfb716992ce4483dd5.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="黑白双姬出没：华硕ROG Z690顶级主板秀" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211121/s_56b9484405cd44bfb716992ce4483dd5.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211121/edf6ea5fb0644315b91ddf528d6f8ff0.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="黑白双姬出没：华硕ROG Z690顶级主板秀" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211121/s_edf6ea5fb0644315b91ddf528d6f8ff0.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>RO姬互动区展出了<strong>黑、白色调为主的两套ROG全家桶</strong>，可以试玩《永劫无间》、《使命召唤：先锋》等人气大作，还有黑白RO姬真人COSer随机出没。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211121/a324bb051a3841f0bfdd0603ed1c5059.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="黑白双姬出没：华硕ROG Z690顶级主板秀" h="399" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211121/s_a324bb051a3841f0bfdd0603ed1c5059.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211121/e27833a8de674cc8a07c0e3f422f0601.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="黑白双姬出没：华硕ROG Z690顶级主板秀" h="399" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211121/s_e27833a8de674cc8a07c0e3f422f0601.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>黑色主机内部搭载Intel i9-12900K处理器、<strong>ROG MAXIMUS Z690 HERO主板</strong>、ROG STRIX雷鹰1000W电源、ROG STRIX LC飞龙II 360散热器、ROG SWIFT PG32UQ超胜显示器、ROG GT-AX6000路由器、ROG STRIX GeForce RTX 3090机动战士高达版显卡。</p>
<p>其中，主板创新加入矩阵式ROG LOGO，I/O盔甲加入全新Polymo动态灯效，20+1供电模组（最大电流90A），2个雷电4、5个M.2接口(其一支持PCIe 5.0)、Wi-Fi 6E和2.5有线网卡等。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211121/918301887a9c481ab48745327be5e388.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="黑白双姬出没：华硕ROG Z690顶级主板秀" h="900" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211121/s_918301887a9c481ab48745327be5e388.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211121/6564618ee12e4ec4933431e1af2afe8e.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="黑白双姬出没：华硕ROG Z690顶级主板秀" h="900" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211121/s_6564618ee12e4ec4933431e1af2afe8e.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211121/bfbb983f1daf4e8a831c494be0473ff0.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="黑白双姬出没：华硕ROG Z690顶级主板秀" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211121/s_bfbb983f1daf4e8a831c494be0473ff0.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>白色全家桶则是ROG STRIX HELIOS太阳神雪武战姬定制版机箱、Intel i7-12700K处理器、<strong>华硕Z690吹雪D4主板（ROG STRIX Z690-A GAMING WIFI D4）</strong>、ROG STRIX XG279Q-W吹雪显示器、ROG STRIX RTX3070 O8G WHITE显卡、ROG STRIX LC白龙360 RGB散热器、ROG STRIX雪鹰850W电源、RT-AX88U机动战士高达版路由器。</p>
<p>其中，主板主板延续了吹雪标志性的银白机能战甲，特别在芯片组散热装甲上加入蓝白配色的潮流ROG理线带，超大的I/O盔甲利用特殊双层亚克力材质，配合RGB灯效可呈现更具层次感的立体效果。</p>
<p>规格方面拥有16+1供电模组（80A），双8Pin ProCool II高强度供电接口，一体式I/O+VRM散热装甲和高品质导热贴，4个M.2接口均配备高效散热片，还有好评如潮的M.2便捷卡扣设计。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211121/427fafddbf2a420783308873123e8eb0.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="黑白双姬出没：华硕ROG Z690顶级主板秀" h="925" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211121/s_427fafddbf2a420783308873123e8eb0.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p><strong>至尊旗舰ROG MAXIMUS Z690 EXTREME</strong>也登场亮相，24+1供电模组，单相最大支持105A电流，双雷电4接口，万兆和2.5G双网卡，并创新加入AniMe Matrix光显矩阵屏，搭配灯效设置软件，可显示自定义图案及GIF动画，或是随音乐节奏变换的音效视觉动画。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211121/df99ff87a0a24d8ab511ee5db5ef26a3.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="黑白双姬出没：华硕ROG Z690顶级主板秀" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211121/s_df99ff87a0a24d8ab511ee5db5ef26a3.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211121/4722ca182ad24346a32813596dd52f16.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="黑白双姬出没：华硕ROG Z690顶级主板秀" h="399" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211121/s_4722ca182ad24346a32813596dd52f16.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211121/ef6f87d0afed458e80d85b57d04b010e.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="黑白双姬出没：华硕ROG Z690顶级主板秀" h="399" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211121/s_ef6f87d0afed458e80d85b57d04b010e.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211121/f2c09570799f448b8ae0ecb38591f660.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="黑白双姬出没：华硕ROG Z690顶级主板秀" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211121/s_f2c09570799f448b8ae0ecb38591f660.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>ROG鬼灭之刃体验区最引人瞩目的当属<strong>10台华硕鬼灭之刃全家桶</strong>，配备了TUF GAMING TUF501鬼灭之刃限定版机箱、<strong>TUF GAMING B560-PLUS WIFI鬼灭之刃限定版主板</strong>、TUF-RTX3060-O12G-GAMING鬼灭之刃限定版显卡、TUF GAMING LC 240 ARGB鬼灭之刃限定版水冷散热器、以嘴平伊之助元素定制的华硕RT-AX82U鬼灭之刃限定款电竞路由器、以灶门炭治郎元素定制的TUF GAMING鬼灭之刃限定版电竞鼠标、以祢豆子元素定制的TUF GAMING鬼灭之刃限定版电竞鼠标垫。</p>
<p>其中，主板正面以雷电的设计元素为主，活动式M.2散热片上加入了可爱的啾太郎的剪影，背面融入了漫画风的强调线条元素，自持华硕CPU性能增强技术2.0(APE 2.0)、双向AI降噪和DTS游戏音效定制技术。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211121/e59975fda35140f6987159f0392ef304.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="黑白双姬出没：华硕ROG Z690顶级主板秀" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211121/s_e59975fda35140f6987159f0392ef304.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>赛车区体验机搭载的赛车模拟游戏《神力科莎》，为其提供动力的则是<strong>长期稳居京东主板单品销量前列的华硕重炮手主板</strong>，以及TUF GAMING GT301火枪手机动战士高达版机箱、TUF GAMING LC破冰手240 RGB机动战士高达版散热器、TUF GAMING突击手750W电源、DUAL RTX3070 O8G显卡等。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211121/7d5b307290454dc49a3e1cd18c114d48.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="黑白双姬出没：华硕ROG Z690顶级主板秀" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211121/s_7d5b307290454dc49a3e1cd18c114d48.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>天选姬互动区则似乎还《使命召唤：先锋》、《孤岛惊魂6》两款3A大作。</p>
