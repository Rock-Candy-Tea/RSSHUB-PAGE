<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日晚，理想汽车发布<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">Q3</span>财报，当季营收营收<span lang="EN-US">77.8</span>亿元，同比增长<span lang="EN-US">209.7%</span>；净亏损<span lang="EN-US">2150</span>万元，亏损额同比收窄<span lang="EN-US">79.9%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>理想表示，第三季度新车交付量<span lang="EN-US">25116</span>辆，同比增长<span lang="EN-US">190%</span>，理想<span lang="EN-US">One</span>累计交付超过<span lang="EN-US">10</span>万辆。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">理想汽车<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年第三季度的研发费用为<span lang="EN-US">8.885</span>亿元（<span lang="EN-US">1.379</span>亿美元），较<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年第三季度的<span lang="EN-US">3.345</span>亿元增加<span lang="EN-US">165.6%</span>，较<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年第二季度的<span lang="EN-US">6.534</span>亿元增加<span lang="EN-US">36.0%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">研发费用较<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年第三季度及<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年第二季度增加主要由于研发人员增加导致雇员薪酬增加，及本公司新车型研发活动增加令有关开支增加。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">截至<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>理想汽车的现金及现金等价物、受限制现金、定期存款及短期投资总额为<span lang="EN-US">488.3</span>亿元（<span lang="EN-US">75.8</span>亿美元）。</strong></span></p>


<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211129/8d25c5edbdde4c85838b9bc64b9baa02.png" target="_blank"><img alt="理想汽车发布Q3财报：交付2.5万辆、营收增长209%" h="450" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211129/s_8d25c5edbdde4c85838b9bc64b9baa02.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月，理想汽车的北京制造基地正式开工建设，并计划于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年投入运营。该基地将成为理想汽车重要的豪华电动车制造基地。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">截至<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日，理想汽车拥有<span lang="EN-US">162</span>家零售中心，覆盖<span lang="EN-US">86</span>个城市，并于<span lang="EN-US">165</span>个城市运营<span lang="EN-US">223</span>家售后维修中心及理想汽车授权钣喷中心。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">针对<span lang="EN-US">Q4</span>季度，理想汽车预计<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年第四季度车辆交付量为<span lang="EN-US">30000</span>至<span lang="EN-US">32000</span>辆，较<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年第四季度增长<span lang="EN-US">107.4%</span>至<span lang="EN-US">121.2%</span>；收入总额为<span lang="EN-US">88.2</span>亿元（<span lang="EN-US">13.7</span>亿美元）至<span lang="EN-US">94.1</span>亿元（<span lang="EN-US">14.6</span>亿美元），较<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年第四季度增长<span lang="EN-US">112.7%</span>至<span lang="EN-US">126.9%</span>。</p>
