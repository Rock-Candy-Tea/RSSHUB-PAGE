<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Alder Lake</span>首次用上了大小核架构，这还是<span lang="EN-US">x86</span>桌面处理器的头一次，实际表现大家也看到了，大核心的单核性能强大，小核心表现也很不错，能效比极高。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿的架构设计只是个开始，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>过去几年一直坚持原生多核架构，所以<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>核心扩展数量不容易，这方面<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的锐龙采用了小芯片设计，每组<span lang="EN-US">CCD</span>有<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个核心，可以轻松堆出<span lang="EN-US">16</span>核、<span lang="EN-US">32</span>核及<span lang="EN-US">64</span>核处理器。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>接下来也会跟进，<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年才上市的<span lang="EN-US">14</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Meteor Lake</span>不仅会用上大小核<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>架构，还会上马小芯片设计，也会集成不同工艺的<span lang="EN-US">IP</span>核心。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="“4nm”酷睿用上大小核+小芯架构 Intel：灵活搭配满足定制需求" h="345" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211211/dcdaec68-8414-4384-9d20-bf7f84a339fa.jpg" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">目前可以确定的是，<strong><span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>计算模块的核心是<span lang="EN-US">Intel 4</span>工艺，也就是之前的<span lang="EN-US">7nm EUV</span>工艺，年中时候已经完成芯片“<span lang="EN-US">Tape-in”</span>，现在应该流片成功了。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">Meteor Lake</span>的<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>模块传闻是台积电的<span lang="EN-US">3nm</span>工艺，</strong></span>目前双方还在洽谈中，<span lang="EN-US">CEO</span>基辛格下周拜会台积电高层，不出意外的话可以定下来。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">其他模块，如<span lang="EN-US">IO</span>单元之类的还没信息，大概率不会上马很先进的工艺，<span lang="EN-US">Intel 7</span>工艺就不错了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">对于<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的这一转变，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>副总裁<span lang="EN-US">Sandra Rivera</span>在瑞银大会上表示，<strong>大小核以及小芯片技术的加入，使得<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>能够更灵活地满足客户需求，</strong>提供更丰富的定制产品。</p>
