<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Alder Lak</span>这周就要发布了，<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月份发布的<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>第一神软<span lang="EN-US">CPU-Z 1.97</span>版初步提供了支持，现在<span lang="EN-US">CPU-Z 1.98</span>版发布了，全面支持酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i-12000</span>系列<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>及<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存、<span lang="EN-US">XMP 3.0</span>了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据官方的更新日志，<span lang="EN-US">CPU-Z 1.98</span>版主要升级内容如下：</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">·改进了<span lang="EN-US">Alder Lake</span>处理器的验证过程</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">·支持<span lang="EN-US">AMX</span>指令集</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">·支持<span lang="EN-US">AMD 4700S</span>处理器</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>·支持<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i9-12900K/i7-12700K/i5-12600K</span>等<span lang="EN-US">Alder Lake</span>处理器及<span lang="EN-US">Z6XX</span>平台</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">·支持<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存<span lang="EN-US">SPD</span>及<span lang="EN-US">XMP 3.0</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">·支持<span lang="EN-US">AMD Radeon 6600 XT GPU</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据最新曝料，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>将在北京时间<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日<span lang="EN-US">0</span>点正式宣布<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿处理器、<span lang="EN-US">Z690</span>芯片组，并接受预订，主板厂商也可以开始宣传新品，包括外观、功能。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日<span lang="EN-US">21</span>点，性能评测正式解禁，同步上市开卖。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿处理器首发六款型号，都是<span lang="EN-US">K/KF</span>系列，具体包括<span lang="EN-US">i9-12900K</span>、<span lang="EN-US">i9-12900KF</span>、<span lang="EN-US">i7-12700K</span>、<span lang="EN-US">i7-12700KF</span>、<span lang="EN-US">i5-12600K</span>、<span lang="EN-US">i5-12600KF</span>。</p>
