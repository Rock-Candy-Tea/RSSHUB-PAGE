<p>今年是宝马M部门成立50周年，公司将揭示一系列新车，比如新一代M2与双座M4 CSL，但车迷最关心的还是M3旅行版。M粉为此已等了二十多年，上一台M3 E46旅行车诞生于千禧年之际。</p>
<p align="center"><img alt="宝马M3旅行车用上曲面屏" h="329" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220125/eb22ae4af4ba4f6f90ee952ad397e0be.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="585" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>按照宝马的说法，M3旅行版保留了M3的特质，同时还具备卓越的实用性，尾箱能塞下洗衣机。目标客户是那些注重长途舒适性，希望拥有更大后备箱空间，但又不喜欢SUV坐姿（X3M）的性能车爱好者。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220125/cc3cb0fafa49424ab5b70fbe9f08d013.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马M3旅行车用上曲面屏" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220125/s_cc3cb0fafa49424ab5b70fbe9f08d013.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220125/8f0a1cecfb96442cbdea5ec26f34c2a0.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马M3旅行车用上曲面屏" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220125/s_8f0a1cecfb96442cbdea5ec26f34c2a0.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>它的竞争对手是奔驰AMG C63 Estate，后者沿用A45的2.0T四缸机，靠辅助电机加成才能拼过M3的3.0T直列六缸机。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220125/84b8792049fa46b49fe0f914bec56fb4.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马M3旅行车用上曲面屏" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220125/s_84b8792049fa46b49fe0f914bec56fb4.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220125/b91ca359dfee4a75b21b1df695862005.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马M3旅行车用上曲面屏" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220125/s_b91ca359dfee4a75b21b1df695862005.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>目前全新M3旅行车正由公司设在瑞典的测试中心做冰面测试。尽管全车披着伪装，但鹰眼人士隔着挡风玻璃能分辨出橙色桶椅，以及与现款3系完全不同的仪表板轮廓：</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220125/108cff3639e4434c80fa6e28307d07b1.jpg" style="text-align: center;" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马M3旅行车用上曲面屏" h="750" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220125/s_108cff3639e4434c80fa6e28307d07b1.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220125/083c1ed423e146fca527ad909226aa55.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马M3旅行车用上曲面屏" h="750" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220125/s_083c1ed423e146fca527ad909226aa55.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220125/c4c3ef113e86417f9088ee2eca9c8df7.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马M3旅行车用上曲面屏" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220125/s_c4c3ef113e86417f9088ee2eca9c8df7.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>之前曾有谍照流出，依稀能看出隐藏在遮布下的双联屏轮廓：</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220125/e5303ef3c3b74486ae0e47fd887518bf.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马M3旅行车用上曲面屏" h="405" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220125/s_e5303ef3c3b74486ae0e47fd887518bf.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>捆绑双联屏的iDrive 8车机系统由宝马大型纯电SUV车型iX率先引入，之后又下放至i4等纯电车型。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220125/7564f7e1b217466da8e3cc65a2ed0a90.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马M3旅行车用上曲面屏" h="399" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220125/s_7564f7e1b217466da8e3cc65a2ed0a90.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>油车方面，下一代5系将配备iDrive 8，但3系未见报道。既然M3旅行版首开纪录，那3系中期改款迎来iD8是完全有可能的，iD8搭配双联屏将使目前还在用iD6/7车机系统的宝马车系瞬间落伍：</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220125/2eb86f0dc3c04d979022e74b2f21d810.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马M3旅行车用上曲面屏" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220125/s_2eb86f0dc3c04d979022e74b2f21d810.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
