<p>今晚，OPPO新品发布会如期举行，除了OPPO Watch 3，<strong>OPPO还为大家带来了新款无线耳机——OPPO Enco Air2i。</strong></p>

<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220810/c5db28c64718461e88387c79a1b524c9.png" target="_blank"><img alt="到手价119元！OPPO Enco Air2i真无线耳机发布：续航可达28小时" h="824" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220810/s_c5db28c64718461e88387c79a1b524c9.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="552" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>据介绍，这款耳机内置40mAh电池，单次播放可长达7小时，<strong>配合460mAh充电盒续航达到了28小时</strong>，较上一代续航增加4个小时。</p>
<p>音质上，这款耳机配备<strong>10mm超大复合镀钛发声单元</strong>，动力更加强劲，特制音腔配合高磁通量磁路令低频表现更佳。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220810/f272714fdd8f4735be34ae794a083c81.png" target="_blank"><img alt="到手价119元！OPPO Enco Air2i真无线耳机发布：续航可达28小时" h="388" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220810/s_f272714fdd8f4735be34ae794a083c81.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>值得注意的是，OPPO Enco Air2i支持AI通话降噪，即使在充斥着复杂环境音的户外，用户也能通话清晰，配合蓝牙5.2，全链路最低94ms延迟。</p>
<p>该耳机还支持Enco Live调音，AAC高清音频编码以及SBC音频格式，双击耳机还能进行遥控拍照。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220810/3be61fcf8b5f4911a9a78d9f67f752d2.png" target="_blank"><img alt="到手价119元！OPPO Enco Air2i真无线耳机发布：续航可达28小时" h="378" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220810/s_3be61fcf8b5f4911a9a78d9f67f752d2.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>此外，OPPO Enco Air2i有水晶白、曜石黑2款配色可选。<strong>售价为149元，预约到手价<span style="color:#ff0000;">119</span>元，新品今日开启预定，于8月16日正式开售。</strong></p>
