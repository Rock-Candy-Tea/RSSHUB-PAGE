<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">NAND</span>闪存已经从<span lang="EN-US">SLC</span>、<span lang="EN-US">MLC</span>发展到现在的<span lang="EN-US">TLC</span>、<span lang="EN-US">QLC</span>闪存为主的局面，由于技术原理不同，<span lang="EN-US">TLC</span>、<span lang="EN-US">QLC</span>的性能、寿命及可靠性都是在下滑的，未来还会有<span lang="EN-US">PLC</span>闪存，不过它可能是最不受欢迎的闪存了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">由于<span lang="EN-US">PLC</span>闪存能够存储<span lang="EN-US">5</span>个电位，容量比<span lang="EN-US">QLC</span>闪存还要再高出<span lang="EN-US">25%</span>，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>所以几大闪存厂商都在积极研发<span lang="EN-US">PLC</span>闪存，但是<span lang="EN-US">P/E</span>寿命估计会下降到百位数水平，比如<span lang="EN-US">500</span>次以内。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">从消费者角度来看，大家并不喜欢<span lang="EN-US">PLC</span>闪存，好消息是业界对<span lang="EN-US">PLC</span>闪存的问世时间也变得保守了，西数预计从<span lang="EN-US">QLC</span>到<span lang="EN-US">PLC</span>闪存的过程会变慢，<strong>至少<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年之前没戏，也就是说还要再等<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">西数认为，<span lang="EN-US">PLC</span>闪存硬盘上市很重要一点还得依赖下一代的<span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>主控芯片，由于<span lang="EN-US">PLC</span>需要更强的纠错能力，那主控芯片性能也要更强大，现在使用的<span lang="EN-US">ARM Cortex-R8</span>内核跟不上了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/711/711545.htm" target="_blank">去年<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>推出了更强大的<span lang="EN-US">Cortex-R82</span>架构</a>，支持了<span lang="EN-US">64</span>位指令集，最多支持<span lang="EN-US">8</span>核心，还支持完整的<span lang="EN-US">Linux</span>运行，<span lang="EN-US">DRAM</span>缓存容量提升到<span lang="EN-US">1TB</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">然而<span lang="EN-US">R82</span>内核的新一代<span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>主控要到<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年到<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年才能问世，而且首发主要用于高性能服务器、数据中心市场，不会很快就搭配<span lang="EN-US">PLC</span>闪存，所以<span lang="EN-US">PLC</span>硬盘还得等。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">对玩家来说，<span lang="EN-US">PLC</span>玩几年上市倒不是坏事了，一方面厂商可以继续优化<span lang="EN-US">PLC</span>闪存及主控的能力，另一方面大家也不用这么急着就为<span lang="EN-US">PLC</span>闪存纠结了。</p>
