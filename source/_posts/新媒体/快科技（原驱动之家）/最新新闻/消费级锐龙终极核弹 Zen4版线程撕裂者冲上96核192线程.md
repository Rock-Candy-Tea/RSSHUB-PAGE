<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的<span lang="EN-US">7nm Zen3</span>架构处理器已经完成布局，已经涵盖桌面级锐龙<span lang="EN-US">5000</span>、移动版锐龙<span lang="EN-US">5000H/U</span>、服务器级<span lang="EN-US">EPYC 7003</span>系列，<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月份还推出了面向工作站的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">Threadripper Pro 5000</span>线程撕裂者系列，最多<span lang="EN-US">64</span>核<span lang="EN-US">128</span>线程，已经是史无前例了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>今年下半年还会推出锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>系列，升级<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>架构，锐龙桌面版应该还是最多<span lang="EN-US">16</span>核<span lang="EN-US">32</span>线程，笔记本中也会有个最高端的版本<span lang="EN-US">Dragon Range</span>（龙岭），不出意外也会上<span lang="EN-US">16</span>核<span lang="EN-US">32</span>线程。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">最顶级的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">Threadripper Pro</span>就不同了，核心数还会继续飙升，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>最新爆料称<span lang="EN-US">Threadripper 7000</span>系列这次会用上<span lang="EN-US">96</span>核<span lang="EN-US">192</span>线程，该系列本来就脱胎于<span lang="EN-US">EPYC</span>服务器产品线，后者就是最多<span lang="EN-US">96</span>核的。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>版<span lang="EN-US">EPYC</span>会继续采用小芯片设计，集成了多达<span lang="EN-US">13</span>颗芯片，包括<span lang="EN-US">12</span>颗<span lang="EN-US">CCD</span>、<span lang="EN-US">1</span>颗<span lang="EN-US">IOD</span>，前者每<span lang="EN-US">3</span>颗一组，环绕在<span lang="EN-US">IOD</span>周围。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong><span lang="EN-US">Zen4 CCD</span>还是每颗一组<span lang="EN-US">CCX</span>、<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>核心，也就是总计可以最多<span lang="EN-US">96</span>核心<span lang="EN-US">192</span>线程，在<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>工艺加持下技术不是问题。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">当然，从本代的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">Threadripper Pro 5000</span>系列开始，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的线程撕裂者处理器就不是针对游戏玩家的，而是走专业生产力方向，<span lang="EN-US">96</span>核<span lang="EN-US">192</span>线程的<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>版线程撕裂者性能有望进一步提升，特别是高并行的工作负载中。</p>
