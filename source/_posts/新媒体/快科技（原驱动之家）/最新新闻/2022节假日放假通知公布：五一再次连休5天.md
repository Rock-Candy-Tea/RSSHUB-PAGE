<p class="MsoNormal">随着国庆节的国庆，<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年的全部节假日已经结束，再有就是<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年元旦了，意味着新一年假期开始了。今天国务院公布了<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年部分节假日的安排情况，春节国庆都是<span lang="EN-US">7</span>天，五一劳动节则是2020、2021之后再次连休<span lang="EN-US">5</span>天。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">国务院办公厅已经发布了放假通知，具体内容如下：</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">经国务院批准，现将<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年元旦、春节、清明节、劳动节、端午节、中秋节和国庆节放假调休日期的具体安排通知如下。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">一、元旦：<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日至<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日放假，共<span lang="EN-US">3</span>天。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">二、春节：<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日至<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日放假调休，共<span lang="EN-US">7</span>天。<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日（星期六）、<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日（星期日）上班。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">三、清明节：<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日至<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日放假调休，共<span lang="EN-US">3</span>天。<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日（星期六）上班。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>四、劳动节：<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日至<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日放假调休，共<span lang="EN-US">5</span>天。<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日（星期日）、<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日（星期六）上班。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">五、端午节：<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日至<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日放假，共<span lang="EN-US">3</span>天。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">六、中秋节：<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日至<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日放假，共<span lang="EN-US">3</span>天。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>七、国庆节：<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日至<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日放假调休，共<span lang="EN-US">7</span>天。<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日（星期六）、<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日（星期日）上班。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">节假日期间，各地区、各部门要妥善安排好值班和安全、保卫、疫情防控等工作，遇有重大突发事件，要按规定及时报告并妥善处置，确保人民群众祥和平安度过节日假期。</p>
