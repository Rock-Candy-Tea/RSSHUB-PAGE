<p class="MsoNormal">代号<span lang="EN-US">Cezanne</span>（塞尚）的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">5000</span>系列移动处理器已经成为<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年的主流，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>接下来<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>就要推锐龙<span lang="EN-US">6000</span>系列<span lang="EN-US">APU</span>了，消息称最快明年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月份发布。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">明年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月份是<span lang="EN-US">CES</span>展会，考虑到锐龙<span lang="EN-US">5000</span>系列<span lang="EN-US">APU</span>也是在<span lang="EN-US">CES 2021</span>上发布的，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>明年发布锐龙<span lang="EN-US">6000</span>系列<span lang="EN-US">APU</span>也不让人意外，毕竟友商明年<span lang="EN-US">CES</span>展会上大动作也不少，除了<span lang="EN-US">ARC</span>独显之外，预计<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Alder Lake</span>移动版也要在<span lang="EN-US">CES</span>上亮相了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">锐龙<span lang="EN-US">6000</span>系列<span lang="EN-US">APU</span>代号“<span lang="EN-US">Rembrandt”(</span>荷兰伟大画家伦勃朗<span lang="EN-US">)</span>，会有锐龙<span lang="EN-US">6000U/H/G</span>系列，<strong>不过首先上市的应该是<span lang="EN-US">15W TDP</span>的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">6000U</span>、<span lang="EN-US">45W TDP</span>的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">6000H</span>系列，</strong>锐龙<span lang="EN-US">6000G</span>系列还得等等，毕竟锐龙<span lang="EN-US">5000G</span>系列也是今年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月份才上市的。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">锐龙<span lang="EN-US">6000</span>系列<span lang="EN-US">APU</span>预计会升级<span lang="EN-US">Zen3+</span>改良型<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>架构，<strong><span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>核心也会放弃<span lang="EN-US">vega</span>改为<span lang="EN-US">Navi2</span>，也就是<span lang="EN-US">RDNA2</span>的，制程工艺升级台积电<span lang="EN-US">6nm</span>。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">其他方面，<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 4.0</span>是确定的，内存有说支持<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>，但<span lang="EN-US">DDR4/LPDDR5</span>应该也在，还有一个惊喜就是<span lang="EN-US">USB4</span>，最高速率也能达到<span lang="EN-US">40Gbps</span>，足以对抗雷电<span lang="EN-US">4</span>接口。</p>
