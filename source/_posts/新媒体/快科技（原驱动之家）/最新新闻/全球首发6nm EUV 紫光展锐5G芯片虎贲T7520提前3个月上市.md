<p class="MsoNormal">在<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>芯片上，除了高通、联发科、三星之外，现在国内厂商又多了一个选择——紫光展锐的虎贲<span lang="EN-US">T7520</span>，这款芯片将提前<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个月上市。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>据报道，紫光展锐<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>芯片虎贲<span lang="EN-US"> T7520</span>已于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>季度<span lang="EN-US">Full Mask</span>回片，将比计划提前<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个月上市。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此外，展锐首款<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>套片<span lang="EN-US">T7510</span>销售半年即破百万套，商用终端产品数量已超<span lang="EN-US">50</span>款。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">虎贲<span lang="EN-US">T7520</span>是紫光展锐基于<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>平台马卡鲁<span lang="EN-US">2.0</span>研发的<span lang="EN-US">5G SoC</span>处理器，<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日正式发布，已经获得了多家厂商的认可。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="全球首发6nm EUV 紫光展锐5G芯片虎贲T7520提前3个月上市" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210411/c2cc29ce-7d14-45ac-a3b0-8f0d9217cc91.jpg" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>虎贲<span lang="EN-US">T7520</span>全球首发<span lang="EN-US">6nm EUV</span>工艺制造，拥有多层极紫外光刻技术加持，</strong></span>工艺光源波长缩短到<span lang="EN-US">13.5nm</span>，接近<span lang="EN-US">X</span>射线的精度，从而带来了极高的光刻分辨率，成本、性能、功耗更加平衡，相比初代<span lang="EN-US">7nm</span>晶体管密度提高<span lang="EN-US">18%</span>，芯片功耗则可降低<span lang="EN-US">8%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">虎贲<span lang="EN-US">T7520</span>还号称是<span lang="EN-US">“</span>全球首款全场景覆盖增强<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>基带<span lang="EN-US">”</span>，支持<span lang="EN-US">6GHz</span>以下频段、<span lang="EN-US">NSA/SA</span>双模组网、<span lang="EN-US">2G</span>至<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>七模全网通、双卡双<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>、<span lang="EN-US">EPS</span>回落、<span lang="EN-US">VoNR</span>高清语音视频通话等先进标准和技术，<span lang="EN-US">SA</span>模式下行峰值速率可超过<span lang="EN-US">3.25Gbps</span>，上传则可达<span lang="EN-US">1.25Gbps</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">同时，它还支持<span lang="EN-US">5G NR TDD+FDD</span>载波聚合<span lang="EN-US">(</span>平均下行峰值速率提升<span lang="EN-US">30</span>％<span lang="EN-US">)</span>、<span lang="EN-US">3.5GHz+2.1GHz</span>频段上下行解耦<span lang="EN-US">(</span>覆盖半径提升<span lang="EN-US">100</span>％<span lang="EN-US">)</span>、<span lang="EN-US">3.5+2.1GHz</span>超级上行<span lang="EN-US">(</span>近点峰值速率提升<span lang="EN-US">60</span>％<span lang="EN-US">)</span>、<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>超级发射等，可解决增强型<span lang="EN-US">VR</span>、<span lang="EN-US">4K/8K</span>超高清视频直播等业务需要更大上行带宽的痛点。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">性能方面，虎贲<span lang="EN-US">T7520</span>集成了八个<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>核心，包括四个<span lang="EN-US">A76</span>、四个<span lang="EN-US">A55</span>，同时集成四个<span lang="EN-US">Mali-G57 GPU</span>图形核心，内存支持<span lang="EN-US">2×16-bit LPDDR4X 2133MHz</span>，存储则支持<span lang="EN-US">eMMC 5.1</span>、<span lang="EN-US">UFS 3.0</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">与此同时，它还有新一代的低功耗设计架构、基于<span lang="EN-US">AI</span>的智能调节技术，再加上<span lang="EN-US">SoC</span>多模融合、<span lang="EN-US">6nm EUV</span>统一，相比外挂<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>方案能效全面胜出，部分数据业务场景下功耗降低多达<span lang="EN-US">35</span>％。</p>
