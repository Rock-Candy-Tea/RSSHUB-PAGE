<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">除了<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>工艺跃迁式发展，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>业务这一年来也是重点，<span lang="EN-US">Xe</span>架构核显已经普遍升级，首款独显<span lang="EN-US">DG1</span>性能一般般，真正用于游戏市场上的是<span lang="EN-US">DG2</span>独显，消息称明年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月份的<span lang="EN-US">CES</span>展会上发布。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">最新爆料显示，有跑得快的国外同行收到了可靠的内幕消息，<strong><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>会在<span lang="EN-US">CES 2022</span>上正式发布<span lang="EN-US">DG2</span>独显，它基于专为游戏设计的<span lang="EN-US">Xe HPG</span>架构，工艺可能是台积电<span lang="EN-US">6nm</span>。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此外，<span lang="EN-US">CES 2022</span>展会上还会推出移动版的<span lang="EN-US">Alder Lake</span>处理器及搭配<span lang="EN-US">DG2</span>独显的<span lang="EN-US">Alder Lake-P</span>系列移动处理器，后者性能更强大。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">至于<span lang="EN-US">DG2</span>独显，此前爆料称<span lang="EN-US">Intel DG2</span>显卡共有五个版本，分别配备<span lang="EN-US">512</span>个、<span lang="EN-US">384</span>个、<span lang="EN-US">256</span>个、<span lang="EN-US">128</span>个、<span lang="EN-US">96</span>个执行单元，后续显示又增加了新版，备<span lang="EN-US">448</span>个执行单元，也就是<span lang="EN-US">3584</span>个核心<span lang="EN-US">(</span>流处理器<span lang="EN-US">/</span>着色器<span lang="EN-US">)</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">这样一来<span lang="EN-US">DG2</span>独显至少就有<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个版本了，<strong><span lang="EN-US">128</span>单元的性能据说略微领先<span lang="EN-US">GTX 1650</span>，<span lang="EN-US">448</span>单元的<span lang="EN-US">DG2</span>传闻逼近<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3070</span>。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">512</span>组<span lang="EN-US">EU</span>的<span lang="EN-US">DG2</span>显卡基础频率<span lang="EN-US">2.2GHz</span>，匹配<span lang="EN-US">16GB GDDR6</span>显存，<span lang="EN-US">256bit</span>位宽，热设计功耗在<span lang="EN-US">225~250</span>瓦，传闻性能在<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3070</span>到<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3070 Ti</span>之间。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">当然，这些性能对比现在还缺少实际测试来验证，即便理论浮点性能超过了<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>或者<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的<span lang="EN-US">DG2</span>独显还面临着驱动、游戏优化等难题，这可不是一年两年就能搞定的。</p>
