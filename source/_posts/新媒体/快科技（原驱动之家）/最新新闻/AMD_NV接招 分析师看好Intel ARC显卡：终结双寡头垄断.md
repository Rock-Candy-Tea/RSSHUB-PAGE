<p class="MsoNormal">前不久<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>正式宣布了旗下的游戏显卡品牌<span lang="EN-US">ARC</span>，<a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/776/776728.htm" target="_blank">中文名为锐炫</a>，<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年早些时候上市。此外<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>在上周的架构日上还公布了加速卡<span lang="EN-US">Ponte Vecchio</span>，<span lang="EN-US">1000</span>亿晶体管更加庞大。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>去年推出了<span lang="EN-US">Xe</span>架构的核显，现在游戏独显及<span lang="EN-US">HPC</span>加速卡也公布了，至此<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>在<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>上的布局差不多成型了，箭头直指<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>及<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>两家公司。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="AMD/NV接招 分析师看好Intel ARC显卡：终结双寡头垄断" h="600" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210823/4d0fd819-2962-4c8e-a413-28ebbae19992.jpg" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>此前一直宣称自己是唯一一家同时掌握高性能<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>及<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>的公司，</strong><span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>这边差点<span lang="EN-US">x86 CPU</span>，但正在收购<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>，并推出了自研的<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>高性能处理器<span lang="EN-US">Grace</span>，现在三家公司算是同台竞技了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">考虑到<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>市场比<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>市场门槛更高，开发也更复杂，那初生牛犊的<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>有多少胜算？</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在支持或者反对<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>做高性能<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>的问题上，华尔街的分析师这两年来意见并不一致，表示怀疑的不少，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>但摩根大通的分析师<span lang="EN-US">Harlan Sur</span>日前表态看好<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>业务，认为<span lang="EN-US">ARC</span>显卡可以在游戏及创作上跟<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>及<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>的显卡竞争，终结两家的寡头垄断局面。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">当然，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>最终能不能做到这一点，现在还没什么证据支撑，一切还要看<span lang="EN-US">ARC</span>显卡上市之后的竞争力再说。</p>
