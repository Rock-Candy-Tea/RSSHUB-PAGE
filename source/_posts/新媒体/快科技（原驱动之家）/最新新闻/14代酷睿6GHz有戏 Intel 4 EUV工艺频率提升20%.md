<div>   
<p><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>去年在<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Alder Lake</span>上量产了<span lang="EN-US">Intel 7</span>工艺，今年的<span lang="EN-US">13</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Rpator Lake</span>继续使用<span lang="EN-US">Intel 7</span>工艺，今年下半年则会量产<span lang="EN-US">Intel 4</span>工艺，这是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的首个<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>工艺，明年会在<span lang="EN-US">14</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Meteor Lake</span>上首发。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的“<span lang="EN-US">4nm EUV</span>“工艺有什么不同？在前不久的<span lang="EN-US">VLSI</span>大会上，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>公布了自家首个<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>工艺的一些细节，高性能库密度翻倍，<span lang="EN-US">16+2</span>金属层，同时还兼容<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的<span lang="EN-US">EMIB</span>及<span lang="EN-US">Foveros</span>封装技术。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>至于性能，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>也给出了具体的指标，同样的<span lang="EN-US">ISO</span>功耗下，相比目前的<span lang="EN-US">Intel 7</span>工艺，<span lang="EN-US">Intel 4</span>的频率提升<span lang="EN-US">20%</span>。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="14代酷睿6GHz有戏 Intel 4 EUV工艺频率提升20%" h="338" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220611/c05fd499-d5d4-4ffd-9925-f5ca2202264e.png" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>

<p class="MsoNormal">相比以往的性能提升，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>这次给出的说法确认了频率提升<span lang="EN-US">20%</span>，考虑到<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>频率已经超过<span lang="EN-US">5.2GHz</span>，这意味着<span lang="EN-US">14</span>代酷睿在功耗不变的情况下，频率冲上<span lang="EN-US">6GHz</span>是没问题的。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">之前我们也报道过，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>架构锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>的频率也很高，现在官方公布的数据中<span lang="EN-US">16</span>核全核加速到<span lang="EN-US">5.5GHz</span>都已经确认了，<a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/835/835704.htm" target="_blank">还有消息称内部的熔断频率实际上可达<span lang="EN-US">5.85GHz</span></a>，非常接近<span lang="EN-US">6GHz</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">从<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>及<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>两边的表现来看，新一代<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>的频率在超过<span lang="EN-US">5GHz</span>之后，下一个目标就要冲击<span lang="EN-US">6GHz</span>了，就看<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>及<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>谁能首先吃到螃蟹了。</p>
