<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>前不久低调推出了<span lang="EN-US">B2</span>步进的<span lang="EN-US">Zen3</span>架构锐龙<span lang="EN-US">5000</span>处理器，虽然规格、性能没什么变化，不过超频方面似乎有些小惊喜，功耗更低。接下来<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>还会有<span lang="EN-US">3D V-Cache</span>版的<span lang="EN-US">Zen3</span>处理器，下个月就要量产了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>本月底就要发布<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿了，单核、多核性能提升明显，超过<span lang="EN-US">Zen3</span>没跑了，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>迫切需要新品应对，但<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>处理器要到明年底才能问世，在此之前<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>会推出增强版<span lang="EN-US">Zen3</span>，重点提升缓存。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">这个产品就是前不久<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>展示过的<span lang="EN-US">3D</span>堆栈版<span lang="EN-US">Zen3</span>，<strong>每一个计算芯片上都堆叠了<span lang="EN-US">64MB SRAM</span>，官方称之为<span lang="EN-US">“3D V-Cache”</span>，可作为额外的三级缓存使用，这样加上处理器原本集成的<span lang="EN-US">64MB</span>，总的三级缓存容量就达到了<span lang="EN-US">192MB</span>。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="游戏性能再提升15% 消息称AMD的3D缓存版Zen3下月量产" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211021/6a15d9d0-4470-4a64-abc1-4dbe15707a5c.jpg" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">这个缓存加强版的处理器代号<span lang="EN-US">Brecken Rdige</span>，今年底问世，预计游戏性能可提升<span lang="EN-US">15%</span>，对付<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Alder Lake</span>应该不会吃亏。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据最新爆料，<span lang="EN-US">3D V-Cache</span>版的<span lang="EN-US">Zen3</span>处理器即将在<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月份开始量产，不过发布时间还不确定，之前有消息说是今年底，不过最大的可能还是明年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月份的<span lang="EN-US">CES</span>展会上，那时候还会有新一代锐龙<span lang="EN-US">APU</span>，桌面版及移动版正好一起进入锐龙<span lang="EN-US">6000</span>系列。</p>
