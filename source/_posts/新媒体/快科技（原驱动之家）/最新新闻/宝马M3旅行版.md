<p>上代E46 M3旅行版夭折后，大屁股爱好者等了二十多年终于与心心念的G81 M3旅行版见面：</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220623/359b2c74ae494eb990fecd332404d4ec.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马M3旅行版" h="450" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220623/s_359b2c74ae494eb990fecd332404d4ec.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220623/abf3876d6249409e8e0b46c764e0204e.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马M3旅行版" h="450" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220623/s_abf3876d6249409e8e0b46c764e0204e.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>相较于资源更充沛，战线更长的友商，宝马无力维持那么多产品线，只能集中力量专攻更赚钱的SUV车型，于是M旅行版便拖到了现在——即便如此，该车也不会在北美上市。宝马舍不得花钱为小众车型申请美国监管机构认证，等到宝马美国公司得知利润最高的M车系也有旅行款，已来不及补办认证手续了，于是北美Bimmer只能捶胸顿足干瞪眼。</p>
<p>以欧版车型为例，在德出售的G81 M3旅行版可提供十几种车漆，比如（不用加钱的）阿尔卑斯白、宝石青、摩天灰、布鲁克林灰、圣保罗黄、波尔蒂芒蓝……，（可能要加钱的）丹泉石蓝、碧玺灰、砂金石红，再到（一定要加钱的）磨砂波尔蒂芒蓝、磨砂灰、磨砂黑……，以下为个性化定制车漆白垩灰实拍图：</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220623/e5f63a14bad949729d572005c8014b0c.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马M3旅行版" h="1066" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220623/s_e5f63a14bad949729d572005c8014b0c.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220623/62a9f3c7bc844c45b05e14cffd0957cb.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马M3旅行版" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220623/s_62a9f3c7bc844c45b05e14cffd0957cb.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>轮毂为前19后20配置，胎宽及扁平比分别为275/35与285/30，这种扁平比不知该如何应对旅行。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220623/34507e03d66f47fe89c2473d5c732bab.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马M3旅行版" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220623/s_34507e03d66f47fe89c2473d5c732bab.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220623/d1ef04f9a61a4d208a07ac66f78b040e.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马M3旅行版" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220623/s_d1ef04f9a61a4d208a07ac66f78b040e.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220623/28c7920566a44400b52e7de7b1207ddf.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马M3旅行版" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220623/s_28c7920566a44400b52e7de7b1207ddf.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>M3旅行版B柱之前的部分与M3几乎一模一样，最销魂的部分当然是车尾：</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220623/85125cfc3ca84ca89f06f761b9c29eb8.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马M3旅行版" h="1066" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220623/s_85125cfc3ca84ca89f06f761b9c29eb8.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220623/54526a270d034727a6a793e40cdae225.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马M3旅行版" h="1066" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220623/s_54526a270d034727a6a793e40cdae225.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220623/303c38fa7cdc452a9f82e8856cc385ea.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马M3旅行版" h="1066" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220623/s_303c38fa7cdc452a9f82e8856cc385ea.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>尾箱空间分别为500（后排座椅直立）/1510升（后排座椅放倒），且尾门后窗可单独开闭：</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220623/417e9f4486b84324b88a3b74a67070ef.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马M3旅行版" h="1066" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220623/s_417e9f4486b84324b88a3b74a67070ef.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220623/a030a6e119fb4a8a865edb7547a452fb.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马M3旅行版" h="1066" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220623/s_a030a6e119fb4a8a865edb7547a452fb.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>相较于现款M3，旅行版车机升级至iD8.0，采用与iX/XM/i4一致的12.3+14.9曲面双联屏设计，同样可选碳陶刹车、捅肾碳纤桶椅与碳纤外观套件，且内饰材料可升级为全Merino真皮。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220623/f0246038e2f24747865ffcb9acdcf7b5.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马M3旅行版" h="1066" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220623/s_f0246038e2f24747865ffcb9acdcf7b5.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220623/6ac96ba900374225b714509b69997904.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马M3旅行版" h="1066" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220623/s_6ac96ba900374225b714509b69997904.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220623/f2e766ff78ee40dfb819f6543dfd9316.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马M3旅行版" h="1066" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220623/s_f2e766ff78ee40dfb819f6543dfd9316.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220623/5938a29d5fe2467e841cf454d62ae95f.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马M3旅行版" h="1066" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220623/s_5938a29d5fe2467e841cf454d62ae95f.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220623/fa8be9434a97468686dbb3895abfb24b.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马M3旅行版" h="1066" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220623/s_fa8be9434a97468686dbb3895abfb24b.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>M3旅行版的车身尺寸比3系旅行版大了一圈，但动力单元指标与M3一致，因此零百成绩要慢0.1秒（3.6秒 vs 3.5秒）。更要命的是，宝马找不到能盖住旅行版大屁股的碳纤车顶，只能用隐藏在亮黑配色（可选与车身同色）下的铝制车顶代替</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220623/9c980a2953654043bc409d474a76c909.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马M3旅行版" h="1066" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220623/s_9c980a2953654043bc409d474a76c909.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>旅行版车重涨至1865公斤，比M3重85公斤，只比M5轻30公斤，但M5标配4.4升八缸S63/S68发动机。</p>
<p>目前最重的宝马乘用车是年底发布的XM，2700公斤的车重直逼皮卡。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220623/4bc61155cba5453086605424722fe513.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马M3旅行版" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220623/s_4bc61155cba5453086605424722fe513.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>代号G81的M3旅行车预计九月开放预定，11月开始量产，起价97800欧元。</p>
