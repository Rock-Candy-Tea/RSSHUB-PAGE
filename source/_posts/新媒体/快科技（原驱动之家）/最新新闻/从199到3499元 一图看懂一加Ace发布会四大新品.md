<p class="MsoNormal">今晚的新品发布会上，一加公司发布了多款耳机、手机及散热配件，其中一加<span lang="EN-US">Ace</span>是他们主打游戏的性能王牌，配备了独家定制的天玑<span lang="EN-US">8100 Max</span>处理器，还带来了<span lang="EN-US">150W</span>长寿命超级闪充。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">这些新品具体来说如下：</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>一加<span lang="EN-US">Buds N</span>无线耳机：售价<span lang="EN-US">199</span>元</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">这款耳机采用搭载大尺寸<span lang="EN-US"> 12.4mm</span>超大低频动圈单元，支持杜比全景声，首次添加自定义均衡器，支持个性化定制音效。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">其单次续航达<span lang="EN-US">7</span>小时，配合充电盒提供至高<span lang="EN-US">30</span>小时的持久续航；耳机<span lang="EN-US">+</span>充电盒充电<span lang="EN-US">10</span>分钟，整体可提供<span lang="EN-US">5</span>小时听歌体验。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">一加云耳</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;"> Z2</span><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">蓝牙耳机：售价</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">199</span><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">元</span></strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">这款耳机搭载<span lang="EN-US">12.4mm</span>超感知低音动圈单元，搭配双阻尼独立音腔，；支持<span lang="EN-US">AI</span>通话降噪，自动识别和过滤不同环境声，动态减少通话噪音。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">支持<span lang="EN-US">IP55</span>防尘防水；采用无感知亲肤硅胶材质，配合人体工学弧形设计。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">充电<span lang="EN-US">10</span>分钟，听歌<span lang="EN-US">20</span>小时；<span lang="EN-US">220mAh</span>电池容量，<span lang="EN-US">30</span>小时超长续航，至高<span lang="EN-US">80</span>小时待机，远离电量焦虑。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>一加<span lang="EN-US"> 18W </span>冰点散热背夹：售价<span lang="EN-US">199</span>元</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">这个背夹采用<span lang="EN-US">18W </span>高制冷功率<span lang="EN-US">+</span>双通道超频制冷，<span lang="EN-US">18W </span>高功率（行业普遍为<span lang="EN-US">10W</span>），<span lang="EN-US">1800mm2</span>强劲制冷。单体最高可在<span lang="EN-US">60</span>秒内极速降温<span lang="EN-US">25°C</span>，最低降温至<span lang="EN-US">-3°C</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">负载状态下，手机侧边框最高降温<span lang="EN-US">13°C</span>，正⾯降温能⼒<span lang="EN-US">15°C</span>。双独立控制芯片，每一档都冰爽。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">一加<span lang="EN-US"> 18W </span>冰点散热背夹搭载双独立控制芯片，分别控制制冷片与风扇，做到分工明确、高效协同，使每一档位下制冷片都能处于最佳工作状态。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220421/1f5a0fa2-5669-45f2-83d5-2e21b9195c37.jpg" style="text-align: center;" target="_blank"><img alt="从199到3499元 一图看懂一加Ace发布会四大新品" h="300" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220421/S1f5a0fa2-5669-45f2-83d5-2e21b9195c37.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>一加<span lang="EN-US">Ace</span>手机：售价<span lang="EN-US">2499</span>元到<span lang="EN-US">3499</span>元</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">一加<span lang="EN-US">Ace</span>手机，采用独家定制了天玑<span lang="EN-US">8100-Max</span>处理器，还用上了长寿命的<span lang="EN-US">150W</span>超级闪充，配合<span lang="EN-US">HyperBoost</span>游戏稳帧引擎及独立芯片，主打高性能游戏，同时外观、拍照也没有短板。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">独家定制处理器天玑<span lang="EN-US">8100-Max</span>，完全拥有天玑<span lang="EN-US">8100“</span>神<span lang="EN-US">U”</span>所有特性的基础上，在以下三方面的能力上进一步大幅提升：游戏帧率稳定性大幅提升、<span lang="EN-US">AI</span>算力大幅提升、夜景视频降噪能力大幅提升。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">一加<span lang="EN-US">Ace</span>的<span lang="EN-US">8+128GB</span>版售价<span lang="EN-US">2499</span>元，<span lang="EN-US">8+256GB</span>版售价<span lang="EN-US">2699</span>元，<span lang="EN-US">12+256GB</span>版售价<span lang="EN-US">2999</span>元，<span lang="EN-US">12+512GB</span>版售价<span lang="EN-US">3499</span>元，<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日开卖。</p>
