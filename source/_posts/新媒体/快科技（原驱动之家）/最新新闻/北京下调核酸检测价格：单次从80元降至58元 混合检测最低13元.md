<p class="MsoNormal">全国新一轮疫情传播的很快，仅仅<span lang="EN-US">7</span>天时间就已经波及到了北京在内的<span lang="EN-US">7</span>个省市。为了提高核酸检测力度，今天北京方面宣布核酸检测降价，单次从<span lang="EN-US">80</span>元降至<span lang="EN-US">58</span>元。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">据报道，<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，北京市医保局会同多部门下发通知，自<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日起，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>北京市公立医疗机构新冠病毒核酸检测单样本价格由<span lang="EN-US">80</span>元降低至<span lang="EN-US">58</span>元<span lang="EN-US">/</span>次，<span lang="EN-US">5</span>样本、<span lang="EN-US">10</span>样本混合检测价格同步由每样本<span lang="EN-US">30</span>元、<span lang="EN-US">20</span>元降低至<span lang="EN-US">18</span>元、<span lang="EN-US">13</span>元。 <span style="font-family:"MS Gothic";mso-bidi-font-family:"MS Gothic"">​</span></strong></span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:"MS Gothic";mso-fareast-font-family:等线;
mso-fareast-theme-font:minor-fareast;mso-bidi-font-family:"MS Gothic""></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此前消息，北京市委宣传部副部长徐和建介绍，这轮京外关联输入疫情已波及昌平区、丰台区、海淀区。据北京昌平<span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:"MS Gothic";mso-fareast-font-family:等线;
mso-fareast-theme-font:minor-fareast;mso-bidi-font-family:"MS Gothic"">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:
"MS Gothic";mso-fareast-font-family:等线;mso-fareast-theme-font:minor-fareast;
mso-bidi-font-family:"MS Gothic"">25</span>日消息，截止到<span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:"MS Gothic";mso-fareast-font-family:等线;
mso-fareast-theme-font:minor-fareast;mso-bidi-font-family:"MS Gothic"">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:
"MS Gothic";mso-fareast-font-family:等线;mso-fareast-theme-font:minor-fareast;
mso-bidi-font-family:"MS Gothic"">24</span>日，昌平已累计报告确诊病例<span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:"MS Gothic";mso-fareast-font-family:等线;
mso-fareast-theme-font:minor-fareast;mso-bidi-font-family:"MS Gothic"">10</span>例。一起打牌者中已有<span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:"MS Gothic";mso-fareast-font-family:等线;mso-fareast-theme-font:

<p class="MsoNormal">据北京日报报道称，本次北京疫情跟旅游团、自驾游有很大的关系，有<span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:"MS Gothic";mso-fareast-font-family:等线;
