<div>   
<p>2020年度格莱美奖获得者莉佐（Lizzo）于当地时间8月28号出席MTV颁奖礼时以Jean Paul Gaultier礼服力压全场，风头盖过霉霉：</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220830/29b94222175548daa1f17bed9c5fccf7.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="莉佐礼服出场 众人啧啧称奇" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220830/s_29b94222175548daa1f17bed9c5fccf7.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>观众讥讽她“把窗帘穿来了”；“被降落伞缠住了”；“自带被褥，随时躺平”，莉佐对这些批评不以为然，她的造型师反驳道：“这可是时装，宝贝们，来自T台一线。”</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220830/4b6339af474b41f49ef80e58c8b54081.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="莉佐礼服出场 众人啧啧称奇" h="445" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220830/s_4b6339af474b41f49ef80e58c8b54081.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>这套海军蓝礼服出自Jean Paul Gaultier春季时装系列，莉佐与造型师知道网上有许多喷子，这套主打华丽哥特风的行头大概率被喷：</p>
<p align="center"><img alt="莉佐礼服出场 众人啧啧称奇" h="445" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220830/07f26630bb3f4dc2a104f1e92a45d0a5.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="586" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>不过莉佐久经战阵，她很清楚收益与风险成正比——在造型师的辅佐下，莉佐最终以Jennifer Fisher设计的耳环，Lael Osness品牌手套，搭配New Rock靴子，凑齐了这套震惊全场的红地毯专用行头：</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220830/f88ee45effbc474ab23d01eb0ce733ba.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="莉佐礼服出场 众人啧啧称奇" h="750" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220830/s_f88ee45effbc474ab23d01eb0ce733ba.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220830/bd43739017b14460ba8a7ca52fb67b8d.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="莉佐礼服出场 众人啧啧称奇" h="847" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220830/s_bd43739017b14460ba8a7ca52fb67b8d.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220830/a890aec154ee47e89ee4437948af7c6d.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="莉佐礼服出场 众人啧啧称奇" h="899" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220830/s_a890aec154ee47e89ee4437948af7c6d.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220830/26ad6a1e8f564bbca6e7b3915192c669.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="莉佐礼服出场 众人啧啧称奇" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220830/s_26ad6a1e8f564bbca6e7b3915192c669.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>霉霉的传统造型相形见绌，让出娱乐版头条：</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220830/776304c223404e5da39fdec231795a79.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="莉佐礼服出场 众人啧啧称奇" h="900" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220830/s_776304c223404e5da39fdec231795a79.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>另外，有报道指说唱歌手Megan Thee Stallion将加盟迪斯尼漫改剧集《女浩克》，该报道尚待迪斯尼方面确认：</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220830/348743c68e7744578c4a774176bd2b1e.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="莉佐礼服出场 众人啧啧称奇" h="750" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220830/s_348743c68e7744578c4a774176bd2b1e.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>现在还不清楚Meg准备扮演何种角色，有评论称以她的身形，可以不用特效直接出演女浩克。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220830/7a3bbba8a1b04017865ff00eb700c1da.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="莉佐礼服出场 众人啧啧称奇" h="600" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220830/s_7a3bbba8a1b04017865ff00eb700c1da.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>最后，耳光事件当事人克里斯·洛克上周日晚间在亚利桑那凤凰城登台表演节目时透露，电影学院邀请他主持明年的奥斯卡颁奖晚会，但他拒绝了：</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220830/75d0b5b083704b7b9a48142cb6433951.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="莉佐礼服出场 众人啧啧称奇" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220830/s_75d0b5b083704b7b9a48142cb6433951.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>“我俩不是一个量级的，拳击理事会不同意我与史密斯同台竞技”，洛克打趣道。</p>
<p>他将重返颁奖晚会比作重回犯罪现场——威尔·史密斯在2022届奥斯卡颁奖晚会上离开观众席上台打了洛克耳光。</p>
