<div>   
<p>今晚的华为全场景智慧生活发布会上，首个亮相的产品就是新一代智能手表——华为WATCH GT3。</p>
<p>首先何刚就为大家展示了华为WATCH GT3的外观设计，其完美传承了前代产品备受好评的外观设计。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211117/450a073ce8e74dbd9a789e9d1fd11165.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="全面屏手表！华为WATCH GT3实现超窄边框、屏占比提升12%" h="274" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211117/s_450a073ce8e74dbd9a789e9d1fd11165.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p><strong>其中46mm款采用实体数字表圈设计，正面搭载了一块1.43英寸的圆形屏幕，分辨率达到466*466，</strong>能显示非常清晰细腻的画面，背部则配备了陶瓷质感高分子纤维后壳。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211117/302e5988d894438b89efb43b8881ab0c.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="全面屏手表！华为WATCH GT3实现超窄边框、屏占比提升12%" h="274" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211117/s_302e5988d894438b89efb43b8881ab0c.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>42mm款同样延续了此前的曲面设计，但是屏幕的边框的到进一步提升，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>在尺寸不变的基础上，大幅增加了屏占比，相比前代提升了12%，屏幕尺寸达到1.32英寸，</strong></span>分辨率同样为466*466。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211117/ab0bfb3d9431404788da3a451847ac70.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="全面屏手表！华为WATCH GT3实现超窄边框、屏占比提升12%" h="275" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211117/s_ab0bfb3d9431404788da3a451847ac70.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211117/ee2fe701720d4ad099f830b91d0c1110.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="全面屏手表！华为WATCH GT3实现超窄边框、屏占比提升12%" h="274" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211117/s_ee2fe701720d4ad099f830b91d0c1110.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>值得注意的是，<strong>此次华为WATCH GT3还引入了此前华为WATCH 3系列上的3D旋转表冠，能随心的在系统界面中缩放界面，便捷滑动能操作。</strong></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211117/cfbd1738a1194789b2e92cb553db5c0c.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="全面屏手表！华为WATCH GT3实现超窄边框、屏占比提升12%" h="274" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211117/s_cfbd1738a1194789b2e92cb553db5c0c.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>根据官方介绍，此次华为WATCH GT3系列带来了两款共8中不同风格可选，各类用户都能找到最适合自己的外观风格。</p>
