<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>去年底在<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿上首发了<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存支持，只不过那时候<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存价格太高，<span lang="EN-US">4800MHz</span>的频率相对<span lang="EN-US">DDR4</span>也没什么性能优势，导致玩家对<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>接受度不高。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">今年<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>市场总算恢复生机了，前不久我们报道过，<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存价格已经在迅速下跌，桌面<span lang="EN-US">DDR5-4800</span>内存的<span lang="EN-US">32GB</span>套装现在只要<span lang="EN-US">154</span>欧元，每<span lang="EN-US">GB</span>价格不到<span lang="EN-US">5</span>欧元，一个月内价格跌了<span lang="EN-US">20%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>如果与去年底的情况相比，现在<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>的价格只有<span lang="EN-US">1/3</span>左右，当时每<span lang="EN-US">GB</span>价格还要<span lang="EN-US">15</span>欧元，如今跌了超过<span lang="EN-US">60%</span>。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="锐龙7000、13代酷睿发力 DDR5内存价格年底逼近DDR4" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220608/1ecee491-b679-455e-be2d-9ebbb5427e6f.png" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">下半年还会有更大的刺激力度，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>处理器直接放弃<span lang="EN-US">DDR4</span>支持，只支持<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存，频率拉升到<span lang="EN-US">5200MHz</span>以上，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的<span lang="EN-US">13</span>代酷睿虽然不会放弃<span lang="EN-US">DDR4</span>，但也会主推<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在这两家厂商的带动下，内存芯片厂商也加大了产能，<strong>下游的内存条公司也推出了更高频率的游戏用高性能<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存，频率普遍超过<span lang="EN-US">6000MHz</span>。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">来自供应链的消息称，<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存芯片的价格还会继续下降，<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年底的时候预计会接近<span lang="EN-US">DDR4</span>内存，推动<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存更广泛地被消费者接受，<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年的渗透率就会大幅提升。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">考虑到成本原因，今年指望<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>就比<span lang="EN-US">DDR4</span>便宜是不可能的，但是只要二者价格接近，玩家选择<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>的热情就会提升。</p>
