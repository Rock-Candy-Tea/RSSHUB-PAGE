<p class="MsoNormal">前几天<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>发布了最新的<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>工艺路线图，瞄准<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年的<span lang="EN-US">20A</span>工艺也首次亮相，会使用<span lang="EN-US">GAA</span>晶体管工艺，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>推出了<span lang="EN-US">RibbonFET</span>及<span lang="EN-US">PowerVia</span>两项革命性技术。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">接下来是<span lang="EN-US">Intel 18A</span>，预计<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年初投产，<strong>继续强化<span lang="EN-US">RibbonFET</span>，还有下一代高<span lang="EN-US">NA EUV</span>光刻，与<span lang="EN-US">ASML</span>合作。</strong></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="台积电2nm工厂2024年量产 与三星、Intel决战GAA晶体管技术" h="331" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210802/207da423-f4fe-44a2-a3c9-41c5a00c01dd.png" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>决心在半导体工艺上重回领导地位，台积电也面临压力，好消息是他们的<span lang="EN-US">2nm</span>工艺<span lang="EN-US">Fab20</span>日前获批。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">据介绍，<span lang="EN-US">Fab20</span>工厂将分为<span lang="EN-US">4</span>期厂房逐步动工，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>前<span lang="EN-US">2</span>期厂房预估会在<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年下半年完工并展开装机作业，<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年下半年可望进入量产阶段，<span lang="EN-US">2nm</span>工艺<span lang="EN-US">4</span>期厂房全部完工量产要到<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>～<span lang="EN-US">2026</span>年，总月产能将逾<span lang="EN-US">10</span>万片规模。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">台积电的<span lang="EN-US">2nm</span>工艺虽然没有详细规格流出，但也会上<span lang="EN-US">GAA</span>晶体管，台积电为此开发了新的<span lang="EN-US">nanosheet</span>纳米片技术，可以更好地控制阈值电压<span lang="EN-US">(Vt)</span>，波动降低至少<span lang="EN-US">15</span>％，将大大改善芯片设计、性能。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">除了<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>、台积电之外，三星的<span lang="EN-US">3nm</span>工艺工艺也会在<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年左右量产，尽管<span lang="EN-US">3nm</span>跟<span lang="EN-US">2nm</span>工艺还差了一代，但三星是首个进入<span lang="EN-US">GAA</span>晶体管工艺的厂商，依然不容小觑，这三大半导体巨头将在<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年有一场恶战，<span lang="EN-US">GAA</span>技术表现如何，三家厂商的表现让人关心。</p>
