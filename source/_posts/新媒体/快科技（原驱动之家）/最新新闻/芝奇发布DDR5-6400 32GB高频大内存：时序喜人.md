<p>创造<a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/811/811506.htm" target="_blank">DDR5-8888的超频记录</a>之后，芝奇今天又发布了一款高频率、大容量的DDR5内存套装，6400MHz、32GB的规格堪称梦幻，时序也非常喜人。</p>
<p>新内存包括<strong>Trident Z5 RGB幻锋戟、Trident Z5炫锋戟</strong>两个版本，<strong>频率都达到了6400MHz，容量均为单条16GB、双条32GB组成套装。</strong></p>
<p>同时<strong><span style="color:#ff0000;">，时序成功控制在CL32-39-39-102</span></strong>，甚至比几乎所有DDR5-4800起步频率的产品还要优秀，<strong>唯一代价就是电压加到了1.4V。</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220127/74199d3c812f46fcbddea6508e932cd4.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="芝奇发布DDR5-6400 32GB高频大内存：时序喜人" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220127/s_74199d3c812f46fcbddea6508e932cd4.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220127/9491c418f4744c298434296401f2edab.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="芝奇发布DDR5-6400 32GB高频大内存：时序喜人" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220127/s_9491c418f4744c298434296401f2edab.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>新内存专门针对Intel 12代酷睿、Z690平台设计，搭配i7-12700K、ROG MAXIMUS Z690 HERO成功通过了烤机测试。</p>
<p>近期上市，价格未公布。</p>
