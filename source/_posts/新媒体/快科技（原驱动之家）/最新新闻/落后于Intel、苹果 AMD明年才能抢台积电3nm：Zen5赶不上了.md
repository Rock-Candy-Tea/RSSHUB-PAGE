<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">根据台积电的信息，<span lang="EN-US">3nm</span>工艺今年下半年生产，不过明年才能大规模量产，<span lang="EN-US">2nm</span>则要到<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年才能量产，这两代工艺的<span lang="EN-US">VIP</span>客户都是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>和苹果，他们需求大，而且有钱，是台积电的优先客户。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">其他半导体公司要想拿到<span lang="EN-US">3nm</span>及未来的<span lang="EN-US">2nm</span>产能，都要等到苹果和<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>出货之后，日前<span lang="EN-US">digitime</span>爆料称，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>、<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>及联发科等公司也希望跟台积电谈判产能分配的问题，不过他们要到<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年底或者<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年某个时候才能开始谈判，首先是<span lang="EN-US">3nm</span>工艺，后面还有<span lang="EN-US">2nm</span>工艺，但肯定要比苹果、<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>晚很多。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不说更远的<span lang="EN-US">2nm</span>，单就<span lang="EN-US">3nm</span>工艺来看，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>要想拿到台积电的产能分配，<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年是没戏的，<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年就算有可能谈成，生产也要到年底了，比较可能的还是<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年才有机会拿到稳定的产能。</strong></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="落后于Intel、苹果 AMD明年才能抢台积电3nm：Zen5赶不上了" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220423/edb5cd87-b0b2-422e-a1cd-7ef1e7ac64a5.png" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">从这点来看，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>在<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>之后的下一代产品中，<strong><span lang="EN-US">Zen5</span>直接上<span lang="EN-US">3nm</span>的可能性并不大，除非<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>能等到<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年的年底再发布，<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>要战三代<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>处理器了。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">因此，更合理的情况是<span lang="EN-US">Zen5</span>继续使用台积电<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>工艺打磨，毕竟<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>首发的新东西很多，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>不会急于只用一代就放弃，<span lang="EN-US">Zen2</span>到<span lang="EN-US">Zen3</span>都是<span lang="EN-US">7nm</span>工艺，但<span lang="EN-US">Zen3</span>的架构改良提升还是很大的，<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>到<span lang="EN-US">Zen5</span>应该也会沿用这个路线。</p>
