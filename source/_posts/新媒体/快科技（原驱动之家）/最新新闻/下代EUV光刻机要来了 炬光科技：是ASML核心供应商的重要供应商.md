<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日消息，针对投资提问<span lang="EN-US">ASML</span>的新一代光刻机E<span lang="EN-US">XE:5200</span>是否使用了炬光科技的产品，炬光科技回应称公司是<span lang="EN-US">ASML</span>公司核心供应商<span lang="EN-US">A</span>公司的重要供应商。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">炬光科技表示，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>公司为半导体光刻应用领域提供光刻机曝光系统中的核心激光光学元器件光场匀化器，是荷兰<span lang="EN-US">ASML</span>光学设备核心供应商<span lang="EN-US">A</span>公司的重要供应商。相关商用情况请以官方披露为准。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">上周的<span lang="EN-US">Q4</span>财报会议上，<span lang="EN-US">ASML</span>公司确认将推出下一代的高<span lang="EN-US">NA EUV</span>光刻机，<span lang="EN-US">NA</span>值从<span lang="EN-US">0.33</span>提升到<span lang="EN-US">0.55</span>，进一步提高光刻分辨率，是制造<span lang="EN-US">2nm</span>及以下工艺的关键设备。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Q4</span>季度中，<span lang="EN-US">ASML</span>公司还新增了<span lang="EN-US">71</span>亿欧元的新订单，其中就有一套<span lang="EN-US">NA 0.55</span>高数值孔径<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>光刻机的销售，这是<span lang="EN-US">ASML</span>下一代光刻机，订购这个光刻机的是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>，据说成本高达<span lang="EN-US">3</span>亿美元，约合<span lang="EN-US">19</span>亿元。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">4</span>年来<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>实际上已经下单了<span lang="EN-US">6</span>台<span lang="EN-US">NA 0.55</span>的<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>光刻机，其中分为两种，<span lang="EN-US">Twinscan Exe:5000</span>系列主要用于工艺研发，产能输出是<span lang="EN-US">185WPH</span>，每小时生产<span lang="EN-US">185</span>片晶圆，<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年上半年交付。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">量产型的<span lang="EN-US">NA 0.55</span>光刻机是<span lang="EN-US">Twinscan Exe:5200</span>，<strong>产能提升到<span lang="EN-US">200WPH</span>，每小时<span lang="EN-US">200</span>片晶圆，预计会在<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年下线，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的<span lang="EN-US">20A</span>工艺正好是在<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年量产。</strong></p>
