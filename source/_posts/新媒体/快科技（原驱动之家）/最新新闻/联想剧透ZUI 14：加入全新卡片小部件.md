<p>今天，联想拯救者官方放出了ZUI 14的功能介绍视频，<strong>展示了新系统在UI、交互、功能等方面的提升。</strong></p>
<p class="newsinfo_vedio"><iframe align="middle" id="kkj_video_play_iframe1" src="//blog.mydrivers.com/news/showvideo.aspx?tid=852902&ac=img&url=https://weibo.com/5981912323/M1kEEFrY8&k=1" frameborder="0" width="600px" height="400px" scrolling="no"></iframe></p>
<p>从演示视频来看，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>ZUI 14最为明显的改动就是新增的“全能卡片”功能，该功能在呈现方式上与“小组件”功能类似，将大量信息在桌面呈现，提供了更强的便捷性与自定义空间。</strong></span></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220816/a9ab755252a346f394f2202f58268a33.png" target="_blank"><img alt="联想剧透ZUI 14：加入全新卡片小部件" h="335" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220816/s_a9ab755252a346f394f2202f58268a33.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>针对平板用户，ZUI 14则加入了新的快捷手势操作，<strong>允许在应用界面呼出底部应用栏，并可直接拖拽应用实现分屏与跨应用文件传输。</strong></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220816/0267a3746a274a33b7baa6c39bc9c7c1.png" target="_blank"><img alt="联想剧透ZUI 14：加入全新卡片小部件" h="336" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220816/s_0267a3746a274a33b7baa6c39bc9c7c1.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>此外，手机与平板在同一网络下可进行同步，实现文件与应用的异步传输。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220816/661569717e1b4090add38a253604f74c.png" target="_blank"><img alt="联想剧透ZUI 14：加入全新卡片小部件" h="338" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220816/s_661569717e1b4090add38a253604f74c.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>游戏方面，<strong>ZUI 14对游戏助手进行了优化，调整了呼出位置</strong>，并新增了多个增强功能，配合游戏自动更新等功能，提升用户的游戏体验。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220816/4f69b58ed1a84aa1b7ae67a43bfe5f3b.png" target="_blank"><img alt="联想剧透ZUI 14：加入全新卡片小部件" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220816/s_4f69b58ed1a84aa1b7ae67a43bfe5f3b.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>影像上，新系统加入了针对不同场景自动调整照片的AI摄影助手，并终于加入了动态照片的拍摄功能。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220816/69a07c595d7d4bd1bbc93b4148ace68a.png" target="_blank"><img alt="联想剧透ZUI 14：加入全新卡片小部件" h="338" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220816/s_69a07c595d7d4bd1bbc93b4148ace68a.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p><strong>ZUI 14将在8月18日晚的发布会上正式亮相</strong>，Y70等新品预计会首批收到更新推送。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220816/3780a686ac424375b9081adf6327ec3d.png" target="_blank"><img alt="联想剧透ZUI 14：加入全新卡片小部件" h="338" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220816/s_3780a686ac424375b9081adf6327ec3d.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
