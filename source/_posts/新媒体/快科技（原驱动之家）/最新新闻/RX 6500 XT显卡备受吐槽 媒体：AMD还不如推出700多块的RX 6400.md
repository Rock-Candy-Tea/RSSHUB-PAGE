<p class="MsoNormal">前不久<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>解禁了<span lang="EN-US">RX 6500 XT</span>，国行售价<span lang="EN-US">1599</span>元，本来应该是千元档显卡的救星，只不过这卡被吐槽很多，从性能到<span lang="EN-US">4GB</span>显存再到价格，大家对这款显卡的接受度并不高。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">当然，最关键的还是<span lang="EN-US">RX 6500 XT</span>的供应问题，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>之前表示只给<span lang="EN-US">4GB</span>显存及<span lang="EN-US">64bit</span>位宽就是为了限制挖矿，确保显卡能让游戏玩家买到，然而实际上<span lang="EN-US">RX 6500 XT</span>显卡也是加价卖出的，刚开始溢价甚至上千元，现在价格稍微缓和了点，加价一两百还能买到。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">RX 6500 XT</span>显卡遭遇的反对声是罕见的，尤其是<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>素以高性价比见长，这次<span lang="EN-US">RX 6500 XT</span>的表现让人很意外。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220131/70a1919a-d2f0-4c83-b1f2-801d62ec0b1d.png" style="text-align: center;" target="_blank"><img alt="RX 6500 XT显卡备受吐槽 媒体：AMD还不如推出700多块的RX 6400" h="331" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220131/S70a1919a-d2f0-4c83-b1f2-801d62ec0b1d.png" style="border: black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>应该如何挽回这次的局面？<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">neowin</span>网站提出了一个很有趣的想法，那就是<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>与其推<span lang="EN-US">199</span>美元的<span lang="EN-US">RX 6500 XT</span>显卡，还不如把<span lang="EN-US">RX 6400</span>显卡拿出来卖，售价约为<span lang="EN-US">109</span>美元，人民币<span lang="EN-US">700</span>元左右。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">RX 6400</span>显卡其实是跟<span lang="EN-US">RX 6500 XT</span>一起发布的，也都是<span lang="EN-US">navi24</span>核心的，只不过规格更低一些，从<span lang="EN-US">16</span>组<span lang="EN-US">CU</span>单元减少到<span lang="EN-US">12</span>组<span lang="EN-US">CU</span>单元，游戏频率也从<span lang="EN-US">2.6GHz</span>降至<span lang="EN-US">2GHz</span>左右，配备<span lang="EN-US">16Gbps</span>显存而非<span lang="EN-US">18Gbps</span>显存。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">预计<span lang="EN-US">RX 6400</span>显卡的性能跟<span lang="EN-US">RX 6500 XT</span>有一定差距，<strong>但它的功耗也只有<span lang="EN-US">53W</span>，而且还是半高设计的，</strong>充当亮机卡再合适不过，只要价格控制在七八百块左右，显然会比<span lang="EN-US">RX 6500 XT</span>更受欢迎。</p>
