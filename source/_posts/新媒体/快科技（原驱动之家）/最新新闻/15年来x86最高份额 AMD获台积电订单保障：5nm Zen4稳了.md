<p class="MsoNormal">根据调研公司<span lang="EN-US">Mercury Research</span>的最新数据，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>在整个<span lang="EN-US">x86</span>处理器市场上的份额已经回升到了<span lang="EN-US">22.%</span>，创造了<span lang="EN-US">2007</span>年来的最高纪录，接近<span lang="EN-US">2006</span>年<span lang="EN-US">25.3%</span>的峰值纪录。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">立下最大功劳的当然是<span lang="EN-US">Zen</span>架构处理器，<span lang="EN-US">Zen/Zen+</span>是初试身手，<span lang="EN-US">Zen2</span>架构性能追平，现在的<span lang="EN-US">Zen3</span>已经渐入佳境，单核多核性能都有优势，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>甚至战略性地减少低端<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>，重点改善利润了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">限制<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>份额继续提升的可能只有<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>缺货了，不过这个问题现在也得到了缓解，<strong><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>贡献的营收占到了台积电<span lang="EN-US">10%</span>以上，已经是<span lang="EN-US">VIP</span>客户了，现在双方的合作进一步加深，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>争取到了台积电更多的产能份额。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">据悉，<span lang="EN-US">7nm Zen3</span>的供应量会逐季增加，改进版的<span lang="EN-US">6nm</span>芯片也会如此，推动<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的<span lang="EN-US">x86</span>份额冲击<span lang="EN-US">25%</span>的记录。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">明年就要轮到<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>了，随着苹果开始转向<span lang="EN-US">4nm/3nm</span>工艺，台积电也可以给<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>分配更多的<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>产能，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>会在代号<span lang="EN-US">Genoa</span>的下一代<span lang="EN-US">EPYC</span>霄龙服务器处理器上首发，明年上半年<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>产品线会全向转向<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>，只要产能获得保障，<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>的市场前景几乎没人质疑。</strong></span></p>
