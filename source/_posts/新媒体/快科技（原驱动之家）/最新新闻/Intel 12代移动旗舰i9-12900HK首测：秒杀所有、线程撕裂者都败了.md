<p><a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/807/807326.htm" target="_blank">Intel近日正式发布了12代酷睿移动版</a>，一共多达28款型号，分为45W H系列、28W P系列、15W U系列三大类。</p>
<p>罗马尼亚媒体Lab501现在提前拿到了旗舰型号i9-12900HK，并放出了初步测试。</p>
<p>测试所用笔记本型号不详，只说内存是<strong>32GB DDR5-4800 CL40</strong>，显卡是RTX 3080 Ti，但因为驱动还不支持，所以没有测试显卡性能。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220109/dc7576b72df344b389500bc855b1abb5.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="Intel 12代移动旗舰i9-12900HK首测：秒杀所有、线程撕裂者都败了" h="597" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220109/s_dc7576b72df344b389500bc855b1abb5.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>CPU-Z已经可以完整识别i9-12900HK，6大8小14核心20线程，24MB三级缓存，大核频率2.5-5.0GHz，小核频率1.8-3.8GHz，集成核显96单元、1.45GHz，最大加速功耗可达115W。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220109/f9928d22f614424082123aab39aea026.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="Intel 12代移动旗舰i9-12900HK首测：秒杀所有、线程撕裂者都败了" h="606" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220109/s_f9928d22f614424082123aab39aea026.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220109/3676ee890ee748f6a1db1994a73c8f1a.png" target="_blank"><img alt="Intel 12代移动旗舰i9-12900HK首测：秒杀所有、线程撕裂者都败了" h="1206" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220109/s_3676ee890ee748f6a1db1994a73c8f1a.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="531" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>测试项目不多，但都是碾压所有。CineBench R15领先上代i9-11980HK 15.4％，领先锐龙9 5900HX 18.9％。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220109/2f241ccd0b854c9a8ef615ea62c0b0e6.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="Intel 12代移动旗舰i9-12900HK首测：秒杀所有、线程撕裂者都败了" h="526" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220109/s_2f241ccd0b854c9a8ef615ea62c0b0e6.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220109/13aab32e25814093a48cc3af4e762d7a.png" target="_blank"><img alt="Intel 12代移动旗舰i9-12900HK首测：秒杀所有、线程撕裂者都败了" h="1205" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220109/s_13aab32e25814093a48cc3af4e762d7a.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="531" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p><strong><span style="color:#ff0000;">CineBench R20更是分别领先16.8％、28.9％，事实上这样的成绩已经给超越了Zen架构的线程撕裂者1950X，后者可是16核心、180W的发烧产品。</span></strong></p>
