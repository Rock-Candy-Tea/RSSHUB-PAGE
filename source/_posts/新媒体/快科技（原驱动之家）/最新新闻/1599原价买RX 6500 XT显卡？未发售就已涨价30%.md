<p class="MsoNormal">在前几天的<span lang="EN-US">CES</span>展会上，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>及<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>都推出了新的千元级显卡，<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>这边是<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3050</span>，售价<span lang="EN-US">1899</span>元，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>这边是<span lang="EN-US">RX 6500 XT</span>，售价<span lang="EN-US">1599</span>元，将在<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日上市，然而原价买的可能又不大了，海外价格已经直奔<span lang="EN-US">2100</span>多元了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">RX 6500 XT</span>基于<span lang="EN-US">Navi24</span>核心，工艺从之前的<span lang="EN-US">7nm</span>升级为<span lang="EN-US">6nm</span>，内建<span lang="EN-US">16</span>组<span lang="EN-US">CU</span>（<span lang="EN-US">1024</span>个流处理器）和光追单元，<span lang="EN-US">16MB</span>无限缓存，游戏加速频率可达<span lang="EN-US">2.6GHz</span>，性能提升<span lang="EN-US">35%。</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">公版显卡采用单风扇设计，<span lang="EN-US">4GB GDDR6</span>显存（<span lang="EN-US">18Gbps</span>），单精度<span lang="EN-US">5.77T</span>，功耗<span lang="EN-US">107W</span>，单<span lang="EN-US">6Pin</span>外接供电，<span lang="EN-US">DP1.4+HDMI2.1</span>双输出，双槽位。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="1599原价买RX 6500 XT显卡？未发售就已涨价30%" h="338" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220107/8893e586-5570-4e51-b59d-ab93a74ab862.png" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>售价方面，<span lang="EN-US">RX 6500 XT</span>的<span lang="EN-US">SEP</span>建议价是<span lang="EN-US">199</span>美元，不过这不含税的，国内价格<span lang="EN-US">1599</span>元</strong>，然而显卡玩家经过这一年的折磨，应该知道厂商的建议价对最终售价已经没什么参考价值了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在欧洲市场上，已经有商家开始预售<span lang="EN-US">RX 6500 XT</span>，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>算上税的话要<span lang="EN-US">299</span>欧元，约合<span lang="EN-US">2154</span>元人民币，涨幅高达<span lang="EN-US">30%</span>，</strong></span>这还是没开卖的情况下。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">RX 6500 XT</span>最终价格能到多少，还要看实际性能及能效，以及<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的供货量，还得看矿工们是否中意这款显卡，反正<span lang="EN-US">1599</span>原价买显卡的难度非常高，估计第一波电商销售时拼手速才有戏。</p>
