<p>HTC将于14号宣布新款VR头戴装置“Vive Flow”，拥有令人错愕的激进外形设计。</p>

<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211013/4d12d8e2361940bc8c223c96ab8b3501.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="HTC新款头戴VR" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211013/s_4d12d8e2361940bc8c223c96ab8b3501.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211013/a6e6c62039a44de8b4611514eda6d6b0.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="HTC新款头戴VR" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211013/s_a6e6c62039a44de8b4611514eda6d6b0.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211013/5b60cf191af840c99eedb5de58697af4.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="HTC新款头戴VR" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211013/s_5b60cf191af840c99eedb5de58697af4.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211013/2db2127d37f34c3782d59094af668674.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="HTC新款头戴VR" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211013/s_2db2127d37f34c3782d59094af668674.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>Vive Flow内置600度（6.0D）屈光矫正装置，大部分近视用户无需借助第三方矫正镜片便能获得酣畅的视觉体验，仅凭这一点该装置便远胜Oculus Go：</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211013/4594ef694aa54858bd1e72e9bad4043b.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="HTC新款头戴VR" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211013/s_4594ef694aa54858bd1e72e9bad4043b.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>预计Vive Flow在售价方面不如Oculus Go那般亲民——可以说任何VR都不可能在性价比方面战胜Oculus Go，脸书基本上是以倾销手段推广Oculus Go，成本、补贴、出货量这三项指标碾压友商。</p>
<p>此外，从泄露的产品展示图上看，该装置居然内置冷却风扇，依稀还能见到热管的身影，表明该装置在算力与功耗方面绝非善茬：</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211013/e59319afa33c4de189d18e1e26879352.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="HTC新款头戴VR" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211013/s_e59319afa33c4de189d18e1e26879352.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>另外，三星为PS5用户推出新款980 Pro NVMe M2固态盘，本月29号发布。1TB版本售250美元，2TB售450美元。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211013/f0244869abf141e290f6e93c2abd2eb7.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="HTC新款头戴VR" h="399" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211013/s_f0244869abf141e290f6e93c2abd2eb7.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>新款实际上由老款叠加定制散热器而来，外形尺寸兼容PS5 SSD扩展槽（W24 x L80 x H8.6毫米），读速依然为7000MB/s，远高于5500MB/s推荐值。</p>
