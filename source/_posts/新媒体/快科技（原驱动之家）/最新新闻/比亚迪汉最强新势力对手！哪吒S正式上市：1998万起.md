<div>   
<p>7月31日晚，哪吒S正式上市，新车共推出8款车型，<strong><span style="color:#ff0000;">售价区间为19.98-33.88万元。</span></strong></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220731/bf25ac3050bb44d2a0a34b833479e117.png" target="_blank"><img alt="比亚迪汉最强新势力对手！哪吒S正式上市：19.98万起" h="323" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220731/s_bf25ac3050bb44d2a0a34b833479e117.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220731/2474ab1fcebb49cc834e268417138361.png" target="_blank"><img alt="比亚迪汉最强新势力对手！哪吒S正式上市：19.98万起" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220731/s_2474ab1fcebb49cc834e268417138361.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220731/f3c9b71c23e840509fd6f97db19bd2db.png" target="_blank"><img alt="比亚迪汉最强新势力对手！哪吒S正式上市：19.98万起" h="316" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220731/s_f3c9b71c23e840509fd6f97db19bd2db.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>哪吒S是哪吒全新自研山海平台首款落地车型，也是品牌旗下旗舰产品，定位于中型轿跑车，<strong>长宽高分别为4980*1980*1450毫米，轴距2980毫米</strong>，与比亚迪汉EV、小鹏P7、零跑C01等车基本上处于同一水准。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220731/c597c88d4f434af28cc28fd4172ff2a8.png" target="_blank"><img alt="比亚迪汉最强新势力对手！哪吒S正式上市：19.98万起" h="335" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220731/s_c597c88d4f434af28cc28fd4172ff2a8.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>前脸融合了燃油车和电动车的标志设计，封闭式前机舱盖，搭配下方的进气格栅，车头部位极具设计感。而狭长的日间行车灯和分体式大灯，配合中央发光的品牌LOGO，又为新车增添不少科技感。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220731/9cae41d5db514d48ba79a56ad35f897e.png" target="_blank"><img alt="比亚迪汉最强新势力对手！哪吒S正式上市：19.98万起" h="331" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220731/s_9cae41d5db514d48ba79a56ad35f897e.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>内饰最抢眼的莫过于“四屏联动”，<strong>包括17.6英寸中控屏、12.3英寸副驾驶娱乐屏、全液晶仪表盘和AR-HUD抬头显示</strong>，科技感十分强烈。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220731/8626b755fe5c4a9da96440655812d5c6.png" target="_blank"><img alt="比亚迪汉最强新势力对手！哪吒S正式上市：19.98万起" h="338" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220731/s_8626b755fe5c4a9da96440655812d5c6.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220731/8ec5d822508e4732982c67860612fd06.png" target="_blank"><img alt="比亚迪汉最强新势力对手！哪吒S正式上市：19.98万起" h="339" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220731/s_8ec5d822508e4732982c67860612fd06.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220731/6c4091a8bd5d45df926dcc4fbd9ba71d.png" target="_blank"><img alt="比亚迪汉最强新势力对手！哪吒S正式上市：19.98万起" h="331" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220731/s_6c4091a8bd5d45df926dcc4fbd9ba71d.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>此外，该车还采用了“悬浮式”多功能方向盘，这款车的设计团队以镂空和悬浮为主题安排设计了所有功能部件，意在带来体感触控自定义按键和超轻运动视感。</p>
<p>与其它造车新势力强调科技、极简的风格不同，<strong>哪吒S还注重豪华氛围的营造</strong>，座舱内通过Alcantara、木纹、皮革等元素，打造出更加豪华的“智能座舱”。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220731/dbaf8241b2ce47c3aa28c44723fd6e3b.png" target="_blank"><img alt="比亚迪汉最强新势力对手！哪吒S正式上市：19.98万起" h="333" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220731/s_dbaf8241b2ce47c3aa28c44723fd6e3b.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220731/50906379a1b24d7694c1e9f7e6865e76.png" target="_blank"><img alt="比亚迪汉最强新势力对手！哪吒S正式上市：19.98万起" h="345" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220731/s_50906379a1b24d7694c1e9f7e6865e76.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>在音响方面，官方透露，<strong>新车将配备21扬声器系统，功放最大输出1216W</strong>，包括12个主扬声器，1个重低音，4个顶棚扬声器，4个头枕扬声器，开车时还可享受听觉盛宴。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220731/32fe00d22d544a608ec5ae2d310e3234.png" target="_blank"><img alt="比亚迪汉最强新势力对手！哪吒S正式上市：19.98万起" h="346" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220731/s_32fe00d22d544a608ec5ae2d310e3234.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220731/c332869e08004b188d55d9dbfb96272d.png" target="_blank"><img alt="比亚迪汉最强新势力对手！哪吒S正式上市：19.98万起" h="333" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220731/s_c332869e08004b188d55d9dbfb96272d.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>智能安全和辅助驾驶上，哪吒S配备有毫米波雷达以及高清摄像头等装置，普通道路上支持L2+级辅助驾驶。</p>
<p>而针对更高需求的用户，可升级激光雷达，从而实现更高等级的辅助驾驶功能，<strong>比如记忆泊车、高速路况下的自动驾驶</strong>。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220731/e1b6aa60e93b47e19b5e17ccd45d731d.png" target="_blank"><img alt="比亚迪汉最强新势力对手！哪吒S正式上市：19.98万起" h="320" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220731/s_e1b6aa60e93b47e19b5e17ccd45d731d.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220731/404a9f07cf2743bca1e3bdf73d8d59f3.png" target="_blank"><img alt="比亚迪汉最强新势力对手！哪吒S正式上市：19.98万起" h="304" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220731/s_404a9f07cf2743bca1e3bdf73d8d59f3.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220731/4377c7efc40b4e7db163e8b7a86e1148.png" target="_blank"><img alt="比亚迪汉最强新势力对手！哪吒S正式上市：19.98万起" h="305" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220731/s_4377c7efc40b4e7db163e8b7a86e1148.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>据官方消息，新车将提供增程、纯电动两种版本车型，其中，<strong>增程版纯电续航高达310公里，综合续航更是达到了1160公里</strong>，基本上使用纯电模式就可以满足一周的通勤需求。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220731/d8cb094a2e10404aa7fa57cf85053fdb.png" target="_blank"><img alt="比亚迪汉最强新势力对手！哪吒S正式上市：19.98万起" h="327" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220731/s_d8cb094a2e10404aa7fa57cf85053fdb.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>纯电四驱版采用前后双电机，系统最大功率340千瓦，峰值扭矩600牛米，<strong>零百加速3.9秒，100-0公里/时刹停距离33.5米</strong>，底盘使用的是前双叉臂后五连杆结构，同时与保时捷联合开发、调校，操控感同级堪称无敌。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220731/8936b85e8c3e44b082d1376d0fb5a4dc.png" target="_blank"><img alt="比亚迪汉最强新势力对手！哪吒S正式上市：19.98万起" h="329" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220731/s_8936b85e8c3e44b082d1376d0fb5a4dc.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220731/5cc4780a06524cfe8e24ffe1faee1ac4.png" target="_blank"><img alt="比亚迪汉最强新势力对手！哪吒S正式上市：19.98万起" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220731/s_5cc4780a06524cfe8e24ffe1faee1ac4.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220731/631ca3b75f0b4404a2aa49fd75a777b7.png" target="_blank"><img alt="比亚迪汉最强新势力对手！哪吒S正式上市：19.98万起" h="331" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220731/s_631ca3b75f0b4404a2aa49fd75a777b7.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>纯电版分为三种续航版本，<strong>CLTC工况下，分别为715公里、650公里和520公里三种</strong>，针对电池安全，哪吒S搭载了自主研发HOZON EPT4.0电池恒温管理系统，实现了95%恒温占比，保证动力输出的同时提升电池使用寿命。</p>
