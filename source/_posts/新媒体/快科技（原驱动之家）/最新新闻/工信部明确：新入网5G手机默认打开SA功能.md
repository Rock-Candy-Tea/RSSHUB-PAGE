<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">5G</span>手机有<span lang="EN-US">NSA</span>、<span lang="EN-US">SA</span>双模之分，只有<span lang="EN-US">5G SA</span>网络才能提供最完整的<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>体验。日前工信部副部长刘烈宏表示，新入网<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>手机将默认开启<span lang="EN-US">5G SA</span>功能。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在昨天的<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>世界电信和信息社会日大会，刘烈宏副部长表示，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>新进网<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>终端将默认开启<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>独立组网<span lang="EN-US">( SA)</span>功能，加快基于<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>的新型消费终端成熟，推进独立组网模式规模化应用。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">刘烈宏表示，中国<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>发展取得领先优势，已累计建成<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>基站超<span lang="EN-US">81.9</span>万个，占全球比例约为<span lang="EN-US">70%</span>；<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>手机终端用户连接数达<span lang="EN-US">2.8</span>亿，占全球比例超过<span lang="EN-US">80%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">对于<span lang="EN-US">5G SA</span>的问题，三大运营商此前已经开始要求默认开启<span lang="EN-US">SA</span>功能，中移动要求从<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日起，终端默认打开<span lang="EN-US">SA</span>功能，不允许设置<span lang="EN-US">SA</span>开关。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">NSA</span>、<span lang="EN-US">SA</span>是<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>网络中的两种网络制式标准，前者依然需要<span lang="EN-US">4G</span>核心网，被视为过渡之选，<strong>但<span lang="EN-US">5G NSA</span>不能连接到<span lang="EN-US">SA</span>基站，会导致只支持<span lang="EN-US">NSA</span>的<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>单模手机不能通过<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>上网。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">相比之下，<span lang="EN-US">SA</span>则是连接到<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>核心网，功能更强大，是<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>发展方向。</p>
