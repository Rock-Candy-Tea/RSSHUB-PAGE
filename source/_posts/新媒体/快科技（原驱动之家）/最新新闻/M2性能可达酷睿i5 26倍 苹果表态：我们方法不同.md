<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">前几天的<span lang="EN-US">WWDC</span>大会上，苹果又发布了自研的<span lang="EN-US">M2</span>处理器，虽然还是<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>工艺，但是性能比<span lang="EN-US">M1</span>有了大幅提升，同功耗下性能提升<span lang="EN-US">18</span>％。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">18%</span>的提升实际上还是多线程的，单线程比<span lang="EN-US">M1</span>提升没多少，但是在苹果的营销对比下，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">M2</span>处理器的性能别说对比上代了，对比<span lang="EN-US">x86</span>架构的<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>酷睿处理器也是各种完胜。</strong></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="M2性能可达酷睿i5 26倍 苹果表态：我们方法不同" h="335" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220611/fb906858-b5f5-4f2b-bbba-729c8d67e5f6.jpg" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据苹果的说法，对比<span lang="EN-US">10</span>核心<span lang="EN-US">x86</span>笔记本处理器，<span lang="EN-US">M2</span>同等功耗下<span lang="EN-US">(15W)</span>性能领先<span lang="EN-US">90</span>％；对比<span lang="EN-US">12</span>核心笔记本处理器，<span lang="EN-US">25</span>％的功耗即可带来<span lang="EN-US">87</span>％的性能。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">10</span>个<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>核心，对比<span lang="EN-US">M1</span>增加两个，同功耗下性能提升<span lang="EN-US">25</span>％，最高功耗下性能提升<span lang="EN-US">35</span>％；对比<span lang="EN-US">10</span>核心笔记本处理器，同等功耗下<span lang="EN-US">(15W)</span>性能可达到<span lang="EN-US">2.3</span>倍，只需<span lang="EN-US">35</span>％的功耗即可媲美最高性能。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">官方号称，<strong>在新<span lang="EN-US">MacBook Air</span>上，<span lang="EN-US">M2</span>图片编辑性能比<span lang="EN-US">M1</span>快<span lang="EN-US">20</span>％、比八代<span lang="EN-US">i5</span>快<span lang="EN-US">5</span>倍，视频编辑性能比<span lang="EN-US">M1</span>快<span lang="EN-US">38</span>％、比八代<span lang="EN-US">i5</span>快<span lang="EN-US">15</span>倍。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在新<span lang="EN-US">MacBook Pro</span>笔记本上，<span lang="EN-US">M2</span>图片处理性能比<span lang="EN-US">M1</span>快<span lang="EN-US">39</span>％、比八代<span lang="EN-US">i7</span>快<span lang="EN-US">3.4</span>倍，游戏性能比<span lang="EN-US">M1</span>快<span lang="EN-US">39</span>％、比八代<span lang="EN-US">i7</span>快<span lang="EN-US">3.3</span>倍。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="M2性能可达酷睿i5 26倍 苹果表态：我们方法不同" h="447" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220611/4fb2f9e3-a23c-4d15-af04-507f170c9b52.png" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">15</span>倍<span lang="EN-US">i5</span>还不是最夸张的，更高的还有<span lang="EN-US">26</span>倍——<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>对比<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>款<span lang="EN-US">MacBook Air</span>，<span lang="EN-US">M2</span>在图形超采样性能上最高有<span lang="EN-US">26</span>倍的优势。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">然而，<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>款<span lang="EN-US">MacBook Air</span>，处理器为酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i5-8210Y</span>，基频只有可怜的<span lang="EN-US">1.6GHz</span>，这不仅是一颗<span lang="EN-US">14nm</span>双核产品，而且核显也是弱鸡的<span lang="EN-US">UHD 617</span>，这颗处理器实际上是当年<span lang="EN-US">Atom</span>水平。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">苹果这种选择性对比当然会让很多网友不满，不过站在苹果的角度来说，硬件技术高级副总<span lang="EN-US">Johny Srouji</span>表示，与行业内公司喜欢通过显著提升功率来获得性能相比，<strong>苹果的方法是不同的，苹果持续不断地关注节能性能，要在最大程度上降低功耗的同时最大程度提高性能。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此外，苹果对<span lang="EN-US">M2</span>的设计也有不同，<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>性能不是他们的关键，不是业界最快的，但神经网络及图形性能更重要，秒杀其他<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>性能数倍甚至数十倍的往往是这部分性能。</p>
