<p class="MsoNormal">今年大家看过多少电影了？据猫眼电影专业版数据，截至<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日<span lang="EN-US">17</span>时，当日大盘票房<span lang="EN-US">5663.8</span>万，营业影院<span lang="EN-US">8767</span>家，较上日增加<span lang="EN-US">804</span>家，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>全国影院营业率<span lang="EN-US">72.60%</span>，这也是<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月以来影院营业率首次超过<span lang="EN-US">70%</span>。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此前猫眼发布数据称，<strong>截至<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年全国院线电影总票房突破<span lang="EN-US">150</span>亿元。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">同时还公布了今年票房的<span lang="EN-US">TOP10</span>名单，吴京主演的战争大片《长津湖之水门桥》以<span lang="EN-US">40.62</span>亿的成绩位列第一，遥遥领先其他电影。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年国内电影票房<span lang="EN-US">TOP10</span>名单：</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">1</span>、《长津湖之水门桥》<span lang="EN-US">40.62</span>亿</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2</span>、《这个杀手不太冷静》<span lang="EN-US">26.23</span>亿</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">3</span>、《奇迹<span lang="EN-US">·</span>笨小孩》<span lang="EN-US">13.77</span>亿</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">4</span>、《熊出没<span lang="EN-US">·</span>重返地球》<span lang="EN-US">9.71</span>亿</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">5</span>、《穿过寒冬拥抱你》<span lang="EN-US">6.65</span>亿<span lang="EN-US">(</span>累计<span lang="EN-US">9.36</span>亿<span lang="EN-US">)</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">6</span>、《狙击手》<span lang="EN-US">6.01</span>亿</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">7</span>、《四海》<span lang="EN-US">5.42</span>亿</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">8</span>、《反贪风暴<span lang="EN-US">5:</span>最终章》<span lang="EN-US">4.81</span>亿<span lang="EN-US">(</span>累计<span lang="EN-US">6.28</span>亿<span lang="EN-US">)</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">9</span>、《李茂扮太子》<span lang="EN-US">4.62</span>亿</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">10</span>、《误杀<span lang="EN-US">2</span>》<span lang="EN-US">2.82</span>亿<span lang="EN-US">(</span>累计<span lang="EN-US">11.21</span>亿<span lang="EN-US">)</span></p>
