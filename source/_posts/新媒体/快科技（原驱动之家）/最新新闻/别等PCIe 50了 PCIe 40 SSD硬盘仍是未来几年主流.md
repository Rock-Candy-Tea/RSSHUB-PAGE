<p class="MsoNormal">去年底<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿处理器首发支持了<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>，速度相比<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 4.0</span>翻倍，<span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>的极限速度逼近<span lang="EN-US">15GB/s</span>，然而<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>距离普及还很遥远，未来几年里<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 4.0 SSD</span>硬盘依然会是主流。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">日前<span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>主控芯片巨头慧荣科技发布了<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">Q4</span>季度财报，营收<span lang="EN-US">2.64</span>亿美元，达到预期的高标，较上一季营收成长<span lang="EN-US">4%</span>，与前一年同期相比大幅成长<span lang="EN-US">84%</span>。第四季毛利率达<span lang="EN-US">49.9%</span>，税后净利<span lang="EN-US">6754</span>万美元。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年全年营收达<span lang="EN-US">9.22</span>亿美元，年成长率达<span lang="EN-US">71%</span>，税后净利<span lang="EN-US">2.19</span>亿美元。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">慧荣科技总经理苟嘉章表示，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">PCIe 4.0</span>在未来几年仍是<span lang="EN-US">PC</span>市场的主流规格，</strong></span>以目前公司在<span lang="EN-US">PC OEM</span>市场不断扩增的新案量来看，预期今年底到明年初，公司的<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 4.0 SSD</span>控制芯片将取得整个<span lang="EN-US">PC OEM</span>市场一半的占有率。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">从此前产业链的消息来看，PCIe 5.0硬盘今年会正式启动，2023年才有可能爆发性增长。</p>
