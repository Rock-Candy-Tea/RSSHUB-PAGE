<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>又要<span lang="EN-US">Yes</span>了，在过去的几年中他们抢占<span lang="EN-US">x86</span>市场份额的表现有目共睹，最新统计显示<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>在<span lang="EN-US">Q2</span>季度拿到了<span lang="EN-US">16.9%</span>的整体<span lang="EN-US">x86</span>处理器市场份额，创造了<span lang="EN-US">2006</span>年以来的最高记录。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2006</span>年的时候<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>还能靠着<span lang="EN-US">K8</span>大锤躺赢，口碑销量双赢，那可能是友商这么多年来最难受的日子，不过之后的事大家也都看到了，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的扣肉架构出来之后迅速翻身，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>则在<span lang="EN-US">K10</span>、推土机两代架构上翻车。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">好汉不提当年勇了，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>从<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年开始靠着<span lang="EN-US">Zen</span>架构又杀回来了，这几年在桌面、笔记本及服务器市场上稳步提升，<span lang="EN-US">x86</span>份额节节攀升。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据<span lang="EN-US">Mercury Research</span>发布的<span lang="EN-US">Q2</span>季度<span lang="EN-US">x86</span>处理器市场报告，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>在整个<span lang="EN-US">x86</span>市场的份额达到了<span lang="EN-US">16.9%</span>，环比增加<span lang="EN-US">0.8</span>个百分点，同比增长<span lang="EN-US">7.3</span>个百分点，创造了<span lang="EN-US">15</span>年来最高水平。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">具体来看，在客户端<span lang="EN-US">x86</span>市场上，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>份额<span lang="EN-US">15.8%</span>，环比增长<span lang="EN-US">0.7</span>个百分点，同比增长<span lang="EN-US">4.1</span>个百分点。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在服务器<span lang="EN-US">x86</span>市场上，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的份额也提升到了<span lang="EN-US">11.6%</span>，，环比增长<span lang="EN-US">0.8</span>个百分点，同比增长<span lang="EN-US">4.7</span>个百分点。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">以上还是<span lang="EN-US">Q2</span>季度的数据，<strong>下半年中<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>还能靠着<span lang="EN-US">7nm Zen3</span>恢复供应的资本进一步扩大市场份额。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此外，还要注意到<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>近来的变化，他们现在已经不是那么重视低端<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>市场上了，这一年来甚至减少了低价<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>的出货，重点放在了改善盈利上，高利润的<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>才是重点，不然市场份额还能增加更多。</p>
