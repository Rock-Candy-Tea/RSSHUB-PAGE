<p>今天下午，realme除了发布了骁龙870的千元机皇realme Q5 Pro之外，还带来了另外一款越级配置的<a class="f14_link" href="https://u.jd.com/ZwnVJp9" target="_blank">realme Q5</a>。</p>
<p>realme Q5采用一块6.6英寸1080P LCD直屏，支持120Hz刷新率，配别了侧面指纹识别。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220420/3e5c4935ced24a04badf1c743e72e4bb.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="60W智慧闪充捅破同级天花板！realme Q5发布：首发1299元" h="163" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220420/s_3e5c4935ced24a04badf1c743e72e4bb.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220420/29af9119c2954581b91c6bce6dc184cd.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="60W智慧闪充捅破同级天花板！realme Q5发布：首发1299元" h="163" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220420/s_29af9119c2954581b91c6bce6dc184cd.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p><strong>核心方面，realme Q5搭载了新一代骁龙695处理器，</strong>这是此前骁龙690的升级款，采用6nm工艺制造，配备主频为2.2GHz的Kryo660 CPU内核和Adreno619 GPU。</p>
<p>与前代产品相比，骁龙695的GPU性能提升30%，CPU提升15%，为realme Q5带来了性能支持。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220420/eaaff43095104340834a98f68318cf73.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="60W智慧闪充捅破同级天花板！realme Q5发布：首发1299元" h="163" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220420/s_eaaff43095104340834a98f68318cf73.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>快充和续航是realme Q5最大的亮点，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>realme Q5这次带来了越级的60W智慧闪充，16分钟就能充满一半的电量。</strong></span></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220420/1b77593f644049dd945e48ba59aae81a.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="60W智慧闪充捅破同级天花板！realme Q5发布：首发1299元" h="163" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220420/s_1b77593f644049dd945e48ba59aae81a.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p><strong>同时，realme Q5还内置了5000mAh超大电池，最长续航能达到29天，成为同价位超级续航王。</strong></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220420/8608742d9f824e19bd24df71c9295b45.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="60W智慧闪充捅破同级天花板！realme Q5发布：首发1299元" h="163" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220420/s_8608742d9f824e19bd24df71c9295b45.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220420/d4d186e2d738406995a24cec4a14e177.png" target="_blank"><img alt="60W智慧闪充捅破同级天花板！realme Q5发布：首发1299元" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220420/s_d4d186e2d738406995a24cec4a14e177.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>realme Q5这次还自研了行业首个波纹式的渐变镀膜工艺，把流光纹幻彩设计留在手机背壳上，推出了竞速黄昏、冰河斩浪、魅影三款配色。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220420/f950acdb81b447a98c4c3c7d051b5231.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="60W智慧闪充捅破同级天花板！realme Q5发布：首发1299元" h="163" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220420/s_f950acdb81b447a98c4c3c7d051b5231.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>价格方面，realme Q5推出了三个版本，首发期间可享受100元优惠政策，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>6+128GB到手价1299元、8+128GB到手价1499元、8+256GB到手价1699元。</strong></span></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220420/b49240bdd6ff438a8e503203ba5ca9d1.png" target="_blank"><img alt="60W智慧闪充捅破同级天花板！realme Q5发布：首发1299元" h="338" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220420/s_b49240bdd6ff438a8e503203ba5ca9d1.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
