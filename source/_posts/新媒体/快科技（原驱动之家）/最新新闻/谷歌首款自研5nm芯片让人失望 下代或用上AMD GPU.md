<p class="MsoNormal">全球科技巨头都有自己研发芯片的计划，苹果的<span lang="EN-US">M1</span>开局就很完美，谷歌也同样有自研的处理器，代号<span lang="EN-US">Whitechapel</span>，部件号<span lang="EN-US">GS101</span>，将用于自家的<span lang="EN-US">Pixel 6</span>系列手机中。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">从之前的爆料来看，<span lang="EN-US">Whitechapel</span>处理器采用三星<span lang="EN-US">5nm LPE</span>工艺生产，<span lang="EN-US">8</span>核<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>三丛集架构，分别是<span lang="EN-US">A78</span>、<span lang="EN-US">A76</span>及<span lang="EN-US">A55</span>核心，并集成安全处理单元<span lang="EN-US">Cital</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">然而<span lang="EN-US">Whitechapel</span>的整体规格让很多粉丝失望，特别是大家期待它能对标苹果的<span lang="EN-US">M1</span>芯片，可现在来看是不可能的了，甚至比不过骁龙<span lang="EN-US">888</span>，也就是骁龙<span lang="EN-US">870</span>的水平。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">既然如此，粉丝们就开始期待<span lang="EN-US">Whitechapel</span>的继任者，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>最新消息称<span lang="EN-US">Whitechapel</span>的下一代芯片有可能跟<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>合作，采用后者的<span lang="EN-US">Radeon GPU</span>授权，但不确定是<span lang="EN-US">RDNA2</span>、<span lang="EN-US">RDNA3</span>还是更新一代的技术了。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">这有点类似三星与<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>合作的新一代<span lang="EN-US">Exynos 2200</span>处理器，泄漏成绩显示其<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>性能很强大，远超骁龙<span lang="EN-US">888</span>、甚至好于苹果<span lang="EN-US">A14</span>，直接对标下一代的骁龙<span lang="EN-US">895</span>处理器了。</p>
