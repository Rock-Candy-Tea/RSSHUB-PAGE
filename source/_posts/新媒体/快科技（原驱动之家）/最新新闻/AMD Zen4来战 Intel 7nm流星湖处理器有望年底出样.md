<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>今年底会推出<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Alder Lake</span>处理器，升级<span lang="EN-US">10nm ESF</span>工艺，首次使用<span lang="EN-US">Golden Cove</span>大核及<span lang="EN-US">Gracemont</span>小核心组成的大小核架构，还有<span lang="EN-US">LGA1700</span>插槽，支持<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>、<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>等先进技术。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿有可能对战<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的增强版<span lang="EN-US">Zen3+</span>处理器，后者还有可能会用上<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>前不久宣布的<span lang="EN-US">3D</span>封装，集成额外的缓存来提高性能，等待明年的<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>架构。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="AMD Zen4来战 Intel 7nm流星湖处理器有望年底出样" h="345" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210614/cdb505a2-fba1-4dbc-a70e-a1fcd3779a5a.jpg" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>下下代的处理器也在准备中了，那就是<span lang="EN-US">7nm Meteor Lake</span>流星湖处理器，<strong>前不久<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>确认它已经<span lang="EN-US">Tape-In</span>，所谓<span lang="EN-US">Tape-in</span>在<span lang="EN-US">Tape-Out</span>（流片）前，大概是<span lang="EN-US">IP</span>模块完成设计验证阶段。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">流星湖处理器的进度可能比预期的要快，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>有爆料称今年底就可以看到<span lang="EN-US">Meteor Lake</span>的<span lang="EN-US">ES</span>工程样品，</strong></span>意味着<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>差不多只用半年时间就完成流片，速度快的不可思议。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">按照<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的计划，<span lang="EN-US">7nm Meteor Lake</span>处理器是在<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年开始出货，如果今年底就有<span lang="EN-US">ES</span>样品，那<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年出货的时间是相当保守的，理论上至少可以提前到<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年下半年。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不论如何，<span lang="EN-US">7nm Meteor Lake</span>与<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>处理器一战是跑不了的。</p>
