<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>、<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>为代表的逻辑工艺制程进入<span lang="EN-US">7nm</span>之后，<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>光刻工艺是少不了的，现在内存还停留在<span lang="EN-US">10nm</span>工艺级别，暂时没用到<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>工艺。不过三星、<span lang="EN-US">SK</span>海力士及美光也确定未来会用，其中美光的<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>工艺内存在<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年量产。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">美光<span lang="EN-US">CEO Sanjay Mehrotra</span>日前在采访中确认，美光已将<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>技术纳入<span lang="EN-US">DRAM</span>技术蓝图，将由<span lang="EN-US">10nm</span>世代中的<span lang="EN-US">1γ</span>（<span lang="EN-US">gamma</span>）工艺节点开始导入。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>美光<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>工艺<span lang="EN-US">DRAM</span>将会先在台中<span lang="EN-US">A3</span>厂生产，预计<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年进入量产阶段。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">注意，这个是<span lang="EN-US">1γ</span>工艺，不是之前的<span lang="EN-US">1y</span>工艺，<span lang="EN-US">10nm</span>级别的内存工艺中前三代是<span lang="EN-US">1x</span>、<span lang="EN-US">1y</span>、<span lang="EN-US">1z</span>，再往后是<span lang="EN-US">1a</span>、<span lang="EN-US">1</span>β、<span lang="EN-US">1γ</span>等。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在美光之前，三星及<span lang="EN-US">SK</span>海力士都更早进入了<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>节点，从去年底就开始部署<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>光刻工艺了，<strong>而且比美光更激进，最快在<span lang="EN-US">1a</span>工艺节点就会量产<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>内存芯片。</strong></p>
