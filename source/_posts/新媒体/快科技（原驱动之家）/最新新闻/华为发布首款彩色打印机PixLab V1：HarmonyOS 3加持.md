<p>今晚的HarmonyOS 3.0及全场景新品会上，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>华为带来了PixLab系列的首款彩色喷墨打印机：PixLab V1。</strong></span></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220727/8bb827c9b5024476841533f79b530b76.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="华为发布首款彩色打印机PixLab V1：HarmonyOS 3加持" h="262" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220727/s_8bb827c9b5024476841533f79b530b76.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>PixLab V1采用了原色打印引擎，支持3D Lut色彩调优，色温跟随以及孔径分级，在彩色打印时能够可靠的还原图片原本的色彩。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220727/aee1e80819db498cb1779b484a63272c.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="华为发布首款彩色打印机PixLab V1：HarmonyOS 3加持" h="262" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220727/s_aee1e80819db498cb1779b484a63272c.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>同时，PixLab V1还支持AI字迹擦除功能，<strong>用户只需要将批改完的卷子放进打印机，AI就能够自动擦除字迹，打印出一份空白的崭新卷子。</strong></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220727/5a4d35cdcfe44eac8de2a60655f3abb6.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="华为发布首款彩色打印机PixLab V1：HarmonyOS 3加持" h="262" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220727/s_5a4d35cdcfe44eac8de2a60655f3abb6.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>当然，<strong>作为首款搭载HarmonyOS 3的打印机，PixLab V1在超级终端生态上也有了突破式的提升。</strong></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220727/d5498ccd04eb413f95c1a8eb3076c4f0.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="华为发布首款彩色打印机PixLab V1：HarmonyOS 3加持" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220727/s_d5498ccd04eb413f95c1a8eb3076c4f0.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>连接上，用户只需要将手机靠近打印机，即可在弹窗中完成配网，而不需要复杂的配置调整。</p>
<p>在完成连接后，<strong>用户还能够一键打印手机上的文件，不需要逐个寻找打印按钮，且支持查看打印进度。</strong></p>
<p>其他诸如一碰打印、远程打印、智能防堵等功能，在PixLab V1身上，也是一应俱全。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220727/41d1aa0dfc044be1bd4c833976da088b.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="华为发布首款彩色打印机PixLab V1：HarmonyOS 3加持" h="262" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220727/s_41d1aa0dfc044be1bd4c833976da088b.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
