<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">前不久<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>宣布了全新的<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>工艺路线图，<a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/772/772105.htm" target="_blank">今年底的<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Alder Lake</span>所用的<span lang="EN-US">10nm ESF</span>工艺被改名为<span lang="EN-US">Intel 7</span></a>，变相实现了<span lang="EN-US">7nm</span>工艺升级，再加上成本大降<span lang="EN-US">45%</span>，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>在桌面及服务器领域来势汹汹。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD CEO</span>苏姿丰在日前的财报会议上接受了采访，谈到了双方的竞争情况，她对<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>非常自信，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>强调即便是现有一代的产品都非常非常有竞争力，</strong></span>客户非常喜欢<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的产品，市场需求也在不断增长。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">如果只谈霄龙在服务器处理器市场上的情况，<strong>苏姿丰则表示霄龙在多个细分领域处于市场领先地位</strong>——<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>当前的<span lang="EN-US">EPYC</span>三代是<span lang="EN-US">7nm Zen3</span>架构，<span lang="EN-US">64</span>核<span lang="EN-US">128</span>线程，<span lang="EN-US">128</span>条<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 4.0</span>，技术上确实领先不少。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="友商“7nm”换马甲 AMD CEO苏姿丰：不担心现有CPU竞争力" h="431" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210731/e8ea95e2-6b4d-4dc1-9d12-644bfd5a321f.png" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">今年下半年<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>有<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿，明年上半年有服务器版的<span lang="EN-US">Sapphire Rapids</span>处理器，用上改名后的<span lang="EN-US">Intel 7</span>工艺，<strong>号称每瓦性能比现在的<span lang="EN-US">10nm SF</span>工艺提升<span lang="EN-US">10-15%</span>，</strong>这对<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>确实会有一定的压力，因为<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的杀手锏<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>预计明年下半年才能问世，有一年的空缺。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的做法是今年底之前升级<span lang="EN-US">7nm Zen3</span>，前不久展示了<span lang="EN-US">3D</span>缓存技术，每一个计算芯片上都堆叠了<span lang="EN-US">64MB SRAM</span>，官方称之为<span lang="EN-US">“3D V-Cache”</span>，可作为额外的三级缓存使用，这样加上处理器原本集成的<span lang="EN-US">64MB</span>，总的三级缓存容量就达到了<span lang="EN-US">192MB</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">这个缓存加强版的处理器代号<span lang="EN-US">Brecken Rdige</span>，今年底问世，预计游戏性能可提升<span lang="EN-US">15%</span>，对付<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Alder Lake</span>应该不会吃亏。</p>
