<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">对于<span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>硬盘，从<span lang="EN-US">SLC</span>到<span lang="EN-US">MLC</span>，再到<span lang="EN-US">TLC</span>、<span lang="EN-US">QLC</span>闪存的进化是少不了的，前不久美光还首发了<span lang="EN-US">176</span>层的<span lang="EN-US">3D QLC</span>闪存硬盘，<span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>主控芯片一哥群联也表示<span lang="EN-US">QLC</span>将成为主流，在<span lang="EN-US">QLC</span>主控中也不会缺席。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">针对美光推出<span lang="EN-US">176</span>层<span lang="EN-US">QLD</span>闪存的硬盘一事，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>群联表示不会评价单一客户，但群联也看好<span lang="EN-US">QLC</span>闪存在两个世代后成为主流，在<span lang="EN-US">QLC</span>闪存主控市场上也不会缺席。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不过群联并没提到他们支持<span lang="EN-US">176</span>层<span lang="EN-US">QLC</span>闪存的主控芯片具体情况。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">两周前美光发布了<span lang="EN-US">2400</span>系列<span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>硬盘，首发了<span lang="EN-US">176</span>层<span lang="EN-US">QLC</span>闪存容量有<span lang="EN-US">512GB</span>、<span lang="EN-US">1TB</span>及<span lang="EN-US">2TB</span>三种，规格支持<span lang="EN-US">M.2 2230/2242/2280</span>，其中<span lang="EN-US">30mm</span>版依然有最高<span lang="EN-US">2TB</span>容量，这是其优势。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">性能方面，<span lang="EN-US">2400</span>系列硬盘读取<span lang="EN-US">4500MB/s</span>，写入<span lang="EN-US">4000MB/s</span>，随机读取<span lang="EN-US">650K IOPS</span>，随机写入<span lang="EN-US">700K IOPS</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">寿命方面，<span lang="EN-US">2400</span>系列<span lang="EN-US">512GB</span>版是<span lang="EN-US">150TBW</span>，<span lang="EN-US">1TB</span>版是<span lang="EN-US">300TBW</span>，<span lang="EN-US">2TB</span>版是<span lang="EN-US">600TBW</span>，<strong>这个写入寿命在<span lang="EN-US">QLC</span>中还不错，但与现有的<span lang="EN-US">TLC</span>闪存相比，<span lang="EN-US">2TB</span>版<span lang="EN-US">600TBW</span>只有一半的寿命，</strong>国产致态<span lang="EN-US">TiPro 7000</span>的<span lang="EN-US">2TB</span>都有<span lang="EN-US">1200TBW</span>寿命，后者是<span lang="EN-US">128</span>层的<span lang="EN-US">TLC</span>闪存。</p>
