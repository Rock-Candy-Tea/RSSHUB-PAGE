<p class="MsoNormal">此前全球第一大<span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>主控芯片公司群联预告了旗下首款<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>主控芯片<span lang="EN-US">PS5026-E26</span>的信息，现在这款芯片已经正式发布，最高速度可达<span lang="EN-US">12GB/s</span>，最高支持<span lang="EN-US">32TB</span>容量。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据群联的信息，<span lang="EN-US">PS5026-E26</span>主控支持<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0 x4</span>通道，支持<span lang="EN-US">NVMe 2.0</span>标准，采用台积电<span lang="EN-US">12nm</span>工艺，<span lang="EN-US">FCCSP 576</span>封装，核心面积<span lang="EN-US">16x16mm</span>，<strong>内部集成<span lang="EN-US">2</span>个<span lang="EN-US">ARM Cortex-R5</span>核心及<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个专用<span lang="EN-US">IP</span>核心，支持<span lang="EN-US">DDR4/LPDDR4</span>缓存，最高支持32TB容量。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>性能方面，<span lang="EN-US">PS5026-E26</span>读取速度<span lang="EN-US">12GB/s</span>，写入速度<span lang="EN-US">11GB/s</span>，<span lang="EN-US">4K</span>随机读取<span lang="EN-US">150</span>万<span lang="EN-US">IOPS</span>，<span lang="EN-US">4K</span>随机写入<span lang="EN-US">200</span>万<span lang="EN-US">IOPS</span>。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">从性能上来看，<span lang="EN-US">PS5026-E26</span>主控的读写速度并不算多高，此前三星、铠侠发布的<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>硬盘速度都冲上<span lang="EN-US">14GB/s</span>了，群联首款<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>主控显然要保守一些，只能做到<span lang="EN-US">12GB/s</span>，有可能跟主控芯片从上代的<span lang="EN-US">3</span>核<span lang="EN-US">R5</span>将为<span lang="EN-US">2</span>核<span lang="EN-US">R5</span>有关。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不过这款主控的随机性能很强大，最高可达<span lang="EN-US">200</span>万<span lang="EN-US">IOPS</span>，算是当前<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>中的拔尖水平，比<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 4.0</span>翻倍。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">按照群联的说法，PS5026-E26主控芯片会在今年下半年问世，还不知道哪家SSD公司会首发。</p>
