<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">从上周投资者会议公布的路线图来看，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>在<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年前的产品及技术路线图都非常激进，不仅要在<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年内掌握<span lang="EN-US">5</span>代<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>工艺，同时在外部委托的代工工艺上也要大升级，<span lang="EN-US">16</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Lunar Lake</span>的<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>核显要上台积电的<span lang="EN-US">2nm</span>工艺了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>公布的路线图，去年的<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿、今年的<span lang="EN-US">13</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Raptor Lake</span>的<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>核显部分都是自己生产的，跟<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>工艺同代，都是<span lang="EN-US">Intel 7</span>工艺。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="Intel GPU核显大升级 消息称16代酷睿直接上台积电2nm工艺" h="338" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220222/f2d5d963-6438-46e8-8f19-326610326480.jpg" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">从<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年的<span lang="EN-US">14</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Meteor Lake</span>开始就不一样了，因为架构大改，第一次采用非单一芯片设计，弹性集成多个小芯片模块，包括下一代混合架构<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>、<span lang="EN-US">tGPU</span>核显引擎、<span lang="EN-US">AI</span>加速单元，而且功耗非常低。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">这其中，<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>模块的工艺是<span lang="EN-US">Intel 4</span>，也就是之前的<span lang="EN-US">7nm EUV</span>工艺，而<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>核显单元是交给代工厂的，<strong><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>虽然没有提到具体名字，但<span lang="EN-US">tGPU</span>及<span lang="EN-US">N3</span>工艺的说法基本上可以证实是台积电<span lang="EN-US">3nm</span>工艺。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年的<span lang="EN-US">15</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Arrow Lake</span>的工艺更复杂了，<span lang="EN-US">Intel 4</span>及<span lang="EN-US">20A</span>工艺可能都有，<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>核显应该会继续使用台积电的<span lang="EN-US">3nm</span>工艺。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年就要轮到<span lang="EN-US">16</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Lunar Lake</span>，很多细节还没公布，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>工艺应该是升级版的<span lang="EN-US">18A</span>工艺，<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>核显工艺没说，但台湾媒体爆料称这是台积电的<span lang="EN-US">2nm</span>工艺。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">结合台积电之前的路线图来看，<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年他们将量产<span lang="EN-US">2nm</span>工艺，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>在<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年用上<span lang="EN-US">2nm</span>工艺也是很可能的，毕竟<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>很快就会成为台积电三大客户之一，仅次于苹果用上先进工艺的地位是可以确定的。</p>
