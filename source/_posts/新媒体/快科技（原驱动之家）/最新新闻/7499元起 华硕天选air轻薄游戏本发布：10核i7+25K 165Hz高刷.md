<p class="MsoNormal">今晚的“天选同萌 秀出高能<span lang="EN-US">”</span>华硕天选<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>新品发布会，除了天选<span lang="EN-US">3</span>、天选<span lang="EN-US">3 Plus</span>高性能游戏本之外，华硕还发布了新一代轻薄游戏本天选<span lang="EN-US">air</span>，配备了<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i7 10</span>核处理器及<span lang="EN-US">RTX 30</span>显卡、<span lang="EN-US">2.5K 165Hz</span>高刷屏，售价<span lang="EN-US">7499</span>元起。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">具体来说，天选<span lang="EN-US">air</span>搭载<span lang="EN-US">Intel 12</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i7-12650H 10</span>核<span lang="EN-US">16</span>线程移动处理器，其采用<span lang="EN-US">Aleder Lake</span>架构，具有<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个<span lang="EN-US">Performance Core</span>性能核<span lang="EN-US">+4</span>个<span lang="EN-US">Efficient Core</span>能效核的效能混合设计，能为年轻创作者的使用全面加速。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220211/ea1548ed-b778-47cb-b6a1-026a94ff4f99.jpg" style="text-align: center;" target="_blank"><img alt="7499元起 华硕天选air轻薄游戏本发布：10核i7+2.5K 165Hz高刷" h="353" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220211/Sea1548ed-b778-47cb-b6a1-026a94ff4f99.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">屏幕方面，<strong>天选<span lang="EN-US">air</span>搭载<span lang="EN-US">2.5K 165Hz 100% DCI-P3</span>专业广色域全能屏，</strong>还支持<span lang="EN-US">Adaptive-Sync</span>技术，可有效防止画面撕裂，并支持<span lang="EN-US">DC</span>调光，无频闪，保护眼睛健康。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="7499元起 华硕天选air轻薄游戏本发布：10核i7+2.5K 165Hz高刷" h="557" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220211/d5cdecdd-1cd3-4a25-8f91-2e2f7eb16c38.png" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">显卡方面，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>天选<span lang="EN-US">air</span>可选<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3050</span>到<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3070</span>在内的多款<span lang="EN-US">RTX</span>光追显卡，还支持华硕“双显三模”显卡切换，</strong></span>需要更强劲<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>性能的时候，可以选择独显输出模式，需要更长续航的场景下则可选择集显输出模式，还可选择混合模式自动平衡，三种模式满足更多样化的场景应用。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">其他配置还有<span lang="EN-US">2x8GB DDR5-4800</span>内存、<span lang="EN-US">512GB PCIe 4.0</span>硬盘等等，最高可支持64GB内存，预留M.2接口升级硬盘。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220211/59a1f924-9dc7-42ab-8823-c049f9ba6489.jpg" style="text-align: center;" target="_blank"><img alt="7499元起 华硕天选air轻薄游戏本发布：10核i7+2.5K 165Hz高刷" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220211/S59a1f924-9dc7-42ab-8823-c049f9ba6489.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">售价方面，<strong><span lang="EN-US">RTX 3050</span>版首发<span lang="EN-US">7499</span>元，<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3050Ti</span>版首发<span lang="EN-US">7999</span>元，<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3060</span>版首发<span lang="EN-US">8999</span>元，<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3070</span>版首发<span lang="EN-US">9999</span>元，<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月份正式开卖。</strong></p>
