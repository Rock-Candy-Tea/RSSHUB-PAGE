<p>5月18日，广汽传祺影豹R-style赛道版正式上市，<strong><span style="color:#ff0000;">新车共推出一款车型，售价13.68万元</span></strong>。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220518/1918ab8001a74a4a9a81bad6d6ee3a5c.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="大尾翼、四排气！传祺影豹R-style赛道版上市：仅13.68万元" h="348" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220518/s_1918ab8001a74a4a9a81bad6d6ee3a5c.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>与普通版不同，该车最大的亮点在于加入了诸多运动套件，因此更显运动和战斗感，以符合“R-style赛道版”的名称。</p>
<p>外观来看，该车车身颜色为当下十分流行的磨砂灰，前包围、侧裙以及后包围等处，使用了造型更为夸张的套件、扰流板，<strong>并用上了鲜艳的橙色装饰件</strong>。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220518/68550da1e04f40b0b0dd5ba5c50a3fe6.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="大尾翼、四排气！传祺影豹R-style赛道版上市：仅13.68万元" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220518/s_68550da1e04f40b0b0dd5ba5c50a3fe6.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>同时，中网、翼子板等处还使用了橙色的“R”徽标，以凸显该车的身份标识，并换上了熏黑多辐式锻造轮圈，搭配225/45 ZR18的米其林PS4运动轮胎。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220518/be859981f3754d6c852d4c0c1cdf87c4.png" target="_blank"><img alt="大尾翼、四排气！传祺影豹R-style赛道版上市：仅13.68万元" h="360" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220518/s_be859981f3754d6c852d4c0c1cdf87c4.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220518/444e8a82d364480e976b3d8d0fdee1ad.png" target="_blank"><img alt="大尾翼、四排气！传祺影豹R-style赛道版上市：仅13.68万元" h="439" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220518/s_444e8a82d364480e976b3d8d0fdee1ad.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>尾部更加具有赛车的感觉，尺寸夸张的扰流板和扩散器十分吸睛，底部是双边共四出排气，同时尾标上也使用了橙色“R”徽标。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220518/e78db2d6c6604fbcae0a2ddc5ca808ab.png" target="_blank"><img alt="大尾翼、四排气！传祺影豹R-style赛道版上市：仅13.68万元" h="426" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220518/s_e78db2d6c6604fbcae0a2ddc5ca808ab.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220518/64f0c2e9bd844c66a8afc4f01d84e4cd.png" target="_blank"><img alt="大尾翼、四排气！传祺影豹R-style赛道版上市：仅13.68万元" h="363" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220518/s_64f0c2e9bd844c66a8afc4f01d84e4cd.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>不只是视觉效果炸裂，得益于运动套件的加持，<strong>整车的风阻系数低至0.25Cd</strong>。</p>
<p>至于内饰，该车与普通版的区别并不大，与外观相同，同样使用了灰橙撞色设计，同时增添了大量的超纤麂皮，可增大摩擦力，帮助驾驶更好地固定坐姿。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220518/b3979ee33d84421780bed892e9a329c8.png" target="_blank"><img alt="大尾翼、四排气！传祺影豹R-style赛道版上市：仅13.68万元" h="422" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220518/s_b3979ee33d84421780bed892e9a329c8.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>动力部分，新车依旧搭载的是一台1.5T发动机，<strong>最大功率130千瓦，峰值扭矩270牛米</strong>，匹配7速湿式双离合变速箱，后悬架同样为多连杆独悬，不过通过优化动力软件，新车在驾驶风格、换挡平顺和声浪等方面会有一些提升。</p>
