<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>在去年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月底发布了<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿处理器<span lang="EN-US">Alder Lake</span>，首发了<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>及<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>支持，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>这边也在上周的<span lang="EN-US">CES</span>展会上跟进，锐龙<span lang="EN-US">6000</span>系列处理器也支持了<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存，今年底的<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>同样把<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>作为重点。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">考虑到内存升级换代的情况，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>在<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿上实际上是同时兼容<span lang="EN-US">DDR4</span>及<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存，甚至今年底的<span lang="EN-US">13</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Raptor Lake</span>系列中也没有放弃<span lang="EN-US">DDR4</span>，同样会支持<span lang="EN-US">DDR4/DDR5</span>两种标准。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">相比之下，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>在<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>上激进很多，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>锐龙<span lang="EN-US">6000</span>系列处理器上就没有<span lang="EN-US">DDR4</span>，<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>架构的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>系列会升级<span lang="EN-US">AM5</span>插槽，<span lang="EN-US">PPT</span>上同样只提到了<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存支持，<span lang="EN-US">DDR4</span>没有踪影。</strong></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="DDR4被淘汰 AMD称Zen4平台推广要看DDR5内存普及速度" h="338" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220110/d9039fdb-d754-4ef3-8e47-1000d8c4e97a.png" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">锐龙<span lang="EN-US">6000</span>主要面向笔记本市场，<span lang="EN-US">PC</span>品牌可以大量采购<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存，不支持<span lang="EN-US">DDR4</span>还能理解，但是<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>的<span lang="EN-US">AM5</span>平台要面向消费级市场，现在<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存价格相比<span lang="EN-US">DDR4</span>翻一倍甚至两倍，同时还没多少现货供应，这就很麻烦了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">桌面<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存的尴尬连<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>自己也很清楚，该公司代表在跟媒体的视频采访中也提到，<strong><span lang="EN-US">AM5</span>平台的推广要看<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存的价格及普及程度。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">按理说，锐龙<span lang="EN-US">6000</span>及锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>系列同时兼容<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>及<span lang="EN-US">DDR4</span>没有任何技术问题，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>之前也不是没有类似的过渡方案，不知道这次为什么比<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>还激进。</p>
