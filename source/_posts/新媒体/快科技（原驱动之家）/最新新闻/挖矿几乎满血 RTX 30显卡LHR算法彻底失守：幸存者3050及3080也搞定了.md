<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">最近两个月，显卡价格因为币圈的动荡而崩盘，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>及<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>的显卡价格暴跌，已经回归甚至跌破发行价了，<a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/830/830775.htm" target="_blank">不过本月初传来<span lang="EN-US">RTX 30</span>显卡<span lang="EN-US">LHR</span>算法被破解的消息</a>，引发巨大争议。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">破解团队<span lang="EN-US">NiceHash</span>开发者宣布，继成功解锁<span lang="EN-US">LHR</span>显卡<span lang="EN-US">70%</span>的原始算力（去年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月实现）后，经过<span lang="EN-US">9</span>个月的努力，几乎<span lang="EN-US">RTX 30</span>全系<span lang="EN-US">LHR</span>显卡已经被<span lang="EN-US">100%</span>破解。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不过他们当时的破解并不算完美，因为还有<span lang="EN-US">2</span>个显卡没搞定，那就是<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3050 8GB</span>及<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3080 12GB</span>，这两款显卡的<span lang="EN-US">LHR</span>算法是<span lang="EN-US">v3</span>版的，难度更高。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="挖矿几乎满血 RTX 30显卡LHR算法彻底失守：幸存者3050及3080也搞定了" h="318" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220520/788c6972-9e76-4229-988b-269cf06e80f8.jpg" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>现在这两个金汤也被攻破了，<span lang="EN-US">NiceHash</span>宣布破解了<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3050 8GB</span>及<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3080 12GB</span>的<span lang="EN-US">LHR v3</span>算法，挖矿算力恢复到了<span lang="EN-US">90%</span>左右。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">至此<span lang="EN-US">RTX 30</span>系列显卡的<span lang="EN-US">LHR</span>算法彻底失守了，虽然<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3050 8GB</span>及<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3080 12GB</span>的<span lang="EN-US">90%</span>算例对矿工来说可能还不完美，但<span lang="EN-US">10%</span>的差距影响不大了。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="挖矿几乎满血 RTX 30显卡LHR算法彻底失守：幸存者3050及3080也搞定了" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220520/8aaea561-74e0-4aef-8407-511ad64ce2a7.jpg" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">这件事对显卡价格走向会有多大影响？现在还不好说，不过目前为止，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>显卡总体上无限接近原价，<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>显卡依然存在较高的溢价空间，而且越是低阶显卡，涨幅依然很高，<span lang="EN-US">3060 Ti</span>是最夸张的，平均贵了多达<span lang="EN-US">40</span>％，另外<span lang="EN-US">3050 33</span>％、<span lang="EN-US">3050 32</span>％、<span lang="EN-US">3070 32</span>％。</p>
