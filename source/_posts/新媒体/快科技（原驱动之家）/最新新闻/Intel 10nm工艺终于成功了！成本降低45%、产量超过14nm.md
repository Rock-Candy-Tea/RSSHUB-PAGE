<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>今天发布了<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">Q2</span>季度财报，营收超过预期、<span lang="EN-US">PC</span>业务大涨的同时还有技术上的好消息，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>现在的<span lang="EN-US">10nm</span>工艺成本降低了<span lang="EN-US">45%</span>，并且产量也超过了<span lang="EN-US">14nm</span>工艺，用了<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年的<span lang="EN-US">14nm</span>有望退居二线了。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>在<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年的<span lang="EN-US">Broadwell</span>处理器首发<span lang="EN-US">14nm</span>工艺，按照之前<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年升级一次的节奏，本应该在<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年升级<span lang="EN-US">10nm</span>（最初应该是<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年，因为<span lang="EN-US">14nm</span>也跳票一年），然而<span lang="EN-US">10nm</span>工艺难产，<span lang="EN-US">14nm</span>就一直担当主力到今年，<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年之痒了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">10nm</span>工艺迟迟无法量产的问题在于成本高，根源则是良率低，因为在<span lang="EN-US">10nm</span>工艺上<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>用了很多新技术，包括<span lang="EN-US">SAQP</span>四重曝光、钴局部互连、有源栅极上接触<span lang="EN-US">(COAG)</span>等等，导致良率上不去，芯片成本降不下来，不能大规模量产。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>在<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年才搞定了<span lang="EN-US">10nm</span>工艺的量产，推出了<span lang="EN-US">Ice Lake</span>处理器，<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年推出了<span lang="EN-US">Tiger Lake</span>处理器，升级到了第二代的<span lang="EN-US">10nm SF</span>工艺，<strong>现在的成本已经降低了<span lang="EN-US">45%</span>，意味着良率有了很大的提升，生产不是问题。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">得益于此，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的<span lang="EN-US">10nm</span>工艺产能也超过了<span lang="EN-US">14nm</span>工艺，成为<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>新一代主力，今年底会推出<span lang="EN-US">10nm</span>工艺的<span lang="EN-US">Alder Lake</span>处理器，这次桌面版也会同步升级，这也意味着<span lang="EN-US">10nm</span>工艺产能的胜利。</p>
