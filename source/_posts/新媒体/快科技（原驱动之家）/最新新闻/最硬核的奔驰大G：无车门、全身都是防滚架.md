<p>把一台数百万元的奔驰AMG G63改装成“沙滩车”，你见过吗？</p>
<p>近日，奔驰御用改装品牌博速（Brabus）推出了一款基于AMG G63打造的<strong>博速900 Crawler</strong>，十分硬核。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220506/b289de2869f245338749ccdef95e23c4.png" target="_blank"><img alt="最硬核的奔驰大G：无车门、全身都是防滚架" h="432" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220506/s_b289de2869f245338749ccdef95e23c4.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220506/7b3b72e36910424c92e2337fb4d6c76f.png" target="_blank"><img alt="最硬核的奔驰大G：无车门、全身都是防滚架" h="399" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220506/s_7b3b72e36910424c92e2337fb4d6c76f.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>从官图来看，该车除了车头能够看出是奔驰大G之外，其他部分均变得十分陌生，全车连车门都没有，裸露在外的是红色防滚架，视觉效果相当炸裂。</p>
<p>据了解，为了轻量化，<strong>博速900 Crawler全车均使用了由碳纤维制成的外观件</strong>，此外，该车还配备了行程高达6.3英寸的减振器、20英寸的越野轮胎以及前400mm/后370mm的刹车盘，因此其拥有20.8英寸的巨大离地间隙，上山下河无所不能。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220506/ece2f2fa525640f483cb3729195d7c21.png" target="_blank"><img alt="最硬核的奔驰大G：无车门、全身都是防滚架" h="458" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220506/s_ece2f2fa525640f483cb3729195d7c21.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220506/0d80dd12cfcf4de280e62bbbeeafbc40.png" target="_blank"><img alt="最硬核的奔驰大G：无车门、全身都是防滚架" h="438" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220506/s_0d80dd12cfcf4de280e62bbbeeafbc40.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>内饰同样十分硬核，虽然保留了一体式贯穿屏幕，但中控屏貌似已被停用，而副驾驶前方则安装有额外的一块屏幕，此外，车内多使用红色的装饰，包括座椅、方向盘、刹车和油门踏板均如此。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220506/520b791dbaef4fcba31f1882ecb07828.png" target="_blank"><img alt="最硬核的奔驰大G：无车门、全身都是防滚架" h="438" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220506/s_520b791dbaef4fcba31f1882ecb07828.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220506/2a810d7936db4bd387c8ce8660e06fc0.png" target="_blank"><img alt="最硬核的奔驰大G：无车门、全身都是防滚架" h="439" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220506/s_2a810d7936db4bd387c8ce8660e06fc0.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220506/08642a94a7da492e8e6d0b19098300ee.png" target="_blank"><img alt="最硬核的奔驰大G：无车门、全身都是防滚架" h="414" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220506/s_08642a94a7da492e8e6d0b19098300ee.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220506/d0ecbefd52914646bcbd392ba3751487.png" target="_blank"><img alt="最硬核的奔驰大G：无车门、全身都是防滚架" h="428" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220506/s_d0ecbefd52914646bcbd392ba3751487.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>值得注意的是，由于是真正意义上可以拿去豁的车，所以其配备了四点式安全带，能更好地保障车内人员的驾驶安全。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220506/93916930159c46c996b3d0aa39950a0b.png" target="_blank"><img alt="最硬核的奔驰大G：无车门、全身都是防滚架" h="436" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220506/s_93916930159c46c996b3d0aa39950a0b.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>动力方面，<strong>AMG G 63的4.0T V8发动机被扩缸至4.5T</strong>，因此其拥有888马力、1050牛米的峰值扭矩，0-100km/h加速仅需3.4秒。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220506/2ea8407e5a344f3e9ce6f807fc53c5d0.png" target="_blank"><img alt="最硬核的奔驰大G：无车门、全身都是防滚架" h="433" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220506/s_2ea8407e5a344f3e9ce6f807fc53c5d0.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>据了解，博速900 Crawler将限量打造15台，<strong>每台售价74.90万欧元（折合人民币526.83万元）</strong>，将在2022-2024年间交付，如何，这台超级硬核的奔驰大G，有没有入得了你的法眼呢？</p>
