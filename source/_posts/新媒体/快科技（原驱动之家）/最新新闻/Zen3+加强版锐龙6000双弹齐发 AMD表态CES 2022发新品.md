<p class="MsoNormal">一个月后就是<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年了，年初就有<span lang="EN-US">CES 2022</span>展会，这是全球消费电子的风向标，<span lang="EN-US">AMD CEO</span>苏姿丰日前已经表态会在这次大会上展示公司近期的创新，发布锐龙<span lang="EN-US">6000</span>系列处理器的移动及桌面版几乎没跑了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">从最近几个月的爆料来看，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>在<span lang="EN-US">CES 2022</span>发布哪些产品其实也没悬念了，首先可以排除两款产品，<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>的<span lang="EN-US">RDNA3</span>显卡及<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>处理器，它们都要到明年下半年才能问世，<span lang="EN-US">CES</span>展会上不会是重点，最多是作为发布会结尾的彩蛋露下脸。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">第一个要发布的当属锐龙<span lang="EN-US">6000 APU</span>处理器，新一代的移动平台，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>代号<span lang="EN-US">Rembrandt</span>伦勃朗，照例也会有<span lang="EN-US">Rembrandt-H</span>高性能及<span lang="EN-US">Rembrandt-U</span>低功耗两个系列，前者<span lang="EN-US">TDP</span>可达<span lang="EN-US">45W</span>，后者是<span lang="EN-US">15W</span>。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">与现在的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">5000</span>系列<span lang="EN-US">APU</span>相比，锐龙<span lang="EN-US">6000 APU</span>使用的是<span lang="EN-US">6nm</span>工艺，<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>升级<span lang="EN-US">Zen3+</span>架构，<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>则升级<span lang="EN-US">navi2</span>架构，性能及能效要比现在用的<span lang="EN-US">vega</span>核心更强大，游戏性能值得期待。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">桌面版的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">6000</span>则是另外的产品，核心还是<span lang="EN-US">7nm Zen3</span>，主要是增加<span lang="EN-US">L3</span>缓存，现在是原生<span lang="EN-US">64MB</span>，<strong><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>会使用<span lang="EN-US">3D V-Cache</span>技术集成额外的<span lang="EN-US">128MB</span>缓存，总计<span lang="EN-US">192MB L3</span>，游戏性能最高提升<span lang="EN-US">25%</span>（《怪物猎人世界》），最少也有<span lang="EN-US">4%</span>（《英雄联盟》），平均在<span lang="EN-US">15%</span>左右。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">按照进度来看，这两款产品今年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月份应该量产了，明年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月发布，<span lang="EN-US">2-3</span>月份陆续上市。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不论是移动版还是桌面版，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">6000</span>都要面对<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Alder Lake</span>，<strong><span lang="EN-US">CES 2022</span>展会上<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>也会推出移动版<span lang="EN-US">Alder Lake</span>，</strong>由于大小核架构及<span lang="EN-US">19% IPC</span>性能提升的存在，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的压力不小，但也不是全都会输，需要<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>灵活对待。</p>
