<p class="MsoNormal">在上周的<span lang="EN-US">CES</span>展会上，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>除了推出锐龙<span lang="EN-US">6000</span>系列处理器，还首次公开展示了基于<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>系列处理器，今年底发布，升级<span lang="EN-US">AM5</span>插槽，支持<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>及<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>等先进技术。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">与现在的锐龙处理器相比，锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>除了<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>架构大改的优势之外，<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>工艺也会带来很多升级，毕竟<span lang="EN-US">7nm</span>工艺已经使用快三年了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">现在<span lang="EN-US">AMD CEO</span>苏姿丰还确认了一点，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>那就是<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>所用的<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>有点特别，是专门针对高性能计算做了优化的，跟其他所用的<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>不一定相同。</strong></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="Zen4可全核5GHz运行 AMD确认锐龙7000使用优化版5nm工艺" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220111/fa07c162-8ada-4e98-a300-aaf69f62a731.png" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>节点上，台积电推出了多款衍生版工艺，有<span lang="EN-US">5nm(</span>代号<span lang="EN-US">N5)</span>、<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>增强版（代号<span lang="EN-US">N5P</span>）、<span lang="EN-US">4nm</span>（代号<span lang="EN-US">N4</span>），<a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/803/803061.htm" target="_blank">以及去年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月才宣布的<span lang="EN-US">4nm</span>增强版（代号<span lang="EN-US">N4X</span>）</a>，首次使用<span lang="EN-US">X</span>后缀，强调为高性能运算专门开发，性能比<span lang="EN-US">N5</span>提升<span lang="EN-US">15%</span>，对比<span lang="EN-US">N4P</span>，在<span lang="EN-US">1.2V</span>下性能也提升了<span lang="EN-US">4%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>没有明确他们的<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>优化版具体是哪个工艺，<span lang="EN-US">N5</span>、<span lang="EN-US">N4</span>都可以排除，<span lang="EN-US">N4X</span>虽然听上去更符合<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的说法，但刚刚宣布，基本上也可以排除，最大可能还是<span lang="EN-US">N5P</span>工艺上针对<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>定制。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不管哪种工艺，<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>架构的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>系列处理器频率会有惊喜是没跑的，<strong>此前官方确认在微软的《光环》游戏中可以全核心<span lang="EN-US">5GHz</span>运行，尽管没有明确核心数，但是做到<span lang="EN-US">8</span>核<span lang="EN-US">5GHz</span>全核频率也会是极大的进步，值得期待。</strong></p>
