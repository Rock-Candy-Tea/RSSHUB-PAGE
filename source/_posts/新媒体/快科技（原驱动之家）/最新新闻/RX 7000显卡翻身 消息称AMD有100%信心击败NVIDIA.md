<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">除了<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>架构的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>系列处理器，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>今年还有一个重磅产品——<span lang="EN-US">RX 7000</span>显卡，使用<span lang="EN-US">RDNA3</span>架构，<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>及<span lang="EN-US">6nm</span>工艺都会有，大核心<span lang="EN-US">Navi31</span>会上双芯封装，集成多达<span lang="EN-US">7</span>颗小芯片。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">从某种角度来说，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的显卡更值得<span lang="EN-US">A</span>饭期待，因为过去几年中，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>虽然使用了先进的<span lang="EN-US">7nm</span>工艺，但在显卡竞争上依然敌不过<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>，<strong><span lang="EN-US">7nm RDNA</span>架构的<span lang="EN-US">RX 5000</span>系列性能也干不过<span lang="EN-US">12nm</span>的<span lang="EN-US">RTX 20</span>系列，<span lang="EN-US">RX 6000</span>的<span lang="EN-US">RDNA2</span>架构号称提升了<span lang="EN-US">50%</span>能效，结果还是输给<span lang="EN-US">8nm</span>工艺的<span lang="EN-US">RTX 30</span>系列。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">今年<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>也要上台积电的<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>甚至<span lang="EN-US">4nm</span>工艺了，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>这边也就同样的工艺水平，新一代的<span lang="EN-US">RDNA3</span>不拿出点绝活，恐怕压力更大。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>之前提到<span lang="EN-US">RDNA3</span>架构会再次提升<span lang="EN-US">50%</span>能效</strong>，但具体的架构细节还没有公布，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>不过最新爆料显示<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>不是说大话，<span lang="EN-US">RDNA3</span>架构的能效号称有<span lang="EN-US">100%</span>的信心击败<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">至于<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>怎么击败<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>，现在详情是不会有的，但是<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的信心是找回来了，大家就等年底两家新品发布了来看吧。</p>
