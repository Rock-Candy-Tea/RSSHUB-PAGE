<div>   
<p>Valve Steam Deck说是一个掌机，其实本质上就是个超迷你PC，近日已经正式支持Windows 11，相信未来还会有更多新玩法。</p>
<p>ETA Times就做了一个大胆的尝试，给它外接了一块独立显卡，还是顶级的RX 6900 XT！</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220412/62205e9b73b348f0813ef1f453ef96cb.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="大神改造Steam Deck外接RX 6900 XT！性能暴涨10倍" h="259" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220412/s_62205e9b73b348f0813ef1f453ef96cb.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p><a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/813/813675.htm" target="_blank">Steam Deck采用了定制版的AMD APU处理器</a>，基于7nm工艺、Zen2 CPU架构、RDNA2 GPU架构，和已经取消的Van Gogh(梵高)是同宗同源。</p>
<p>外接显卡最好的途径是雷电接口，其次是走PCIe通道，但是Steam Deck都不具备，ETA Times<strong>借用了M.2插槽。</strong></p>
<p>尝试多款不同显卡后发现，<strong>NVIDIA的无一成功，AMD的都能启动，毕竟是一家人。</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220412/92720d7996374b2187464825a31916df.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="大神改造Steam Deck外接RX 6900 XT！性能暴涨10倍" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220412/s_92720d7996374b2187464825a31916df.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220412/06dabf1168f842ebb8597d8bd98710b4.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="大神改造Steam Deck外接RX 6900 XT！性能暴涨10倍" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220412/s_06dabf1168f842ebb8597d8bd98710b4.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>3DMark Fire Strike测试中，Steam Deck原本只有4856分的图形成绩，接上RX 6900 XT之后达到了26855，<strong>多达5.5倍的差距。</strong></p>
<p><strong><span style="color:#ff0000;">3DMark Time Spy DX12测试中，跑分更是从1576飙升到15579，几乎10倍。</span></strong></p>
<p>这还是在Stream Deck CPU性能太弱，成为瓶颈的情况下。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220412/1fd7136649d14fbb85205d82c37baed8.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="大神改造Steam Deck外接RX 6900 XT！性能暴涨10倍" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220412/s_1fd7136649d14fbb85205d82c37baed8.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220412/776bcc4ff72544059bd35b29f1f92ca0.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="大神改造Steam Deck外接RX 6900 XT！性能暴涨10倍" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220412/s_776bcc4ff72544059bd35b29f1f92ca0.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>实际游戏中，<strong><span style="color:#ff0000;">接上RX 6900 XT就可以跑4K分辨率了，《巫师3》最高画质约108FPS，《GTA5》高画质约73FPS。</span></strong></p>
<p><strong>1080p分辨率和高画质下，《艾尔登法环》、《赛博朋克2077》可以跑出47-70FPS。</strong></p>
<p>再一次，孱弱的CPU性能限制了GPU的发挥。</p>
