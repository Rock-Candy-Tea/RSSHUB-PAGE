<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>现在的主力处理器是<span lang="EN-US">7nm Zen3</span>架构的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">5000</span>系列，今年底还会有<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>系列，<span lang="EN-US">Zen1</span>、<span lang="EN-US">Zen2</span>架构的锐龙都是几年前的产品了，好在<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>并没有忘记老朋友，时隔<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年半还更新了微代码。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">据<span lang="EN-US">phoronix</span>网站消息，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>日前通过社区更新了多款处理器的微代码，包括<span lang="EN-US">17h</span>、<span lang="EN-US">19h</span>家族处理器，其中<span lang="EN-US">19h</span>是目前的<span lang="EN-US">7nm Zen3</span>，<strong><span lang="EN-US">17h</span>家族则包括<span lang="EN-US">zen1</span>、<span lang="EN-US">zen+</span>及<span lang="EN-US">zen2</span>架构，也就是锐龙<span lang="EN-US">1000/2000/3000</span>系列。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">19h</span>的微代码更新之后大小没变，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">17h</span>的微代码升级之后容量为<span lang="EN-US">9700</span>字节，比之前的<span lang="EN-US">6476</span>字节增加了近<span lang="EN-US">50%</span>，上一次升级还是<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月份，现在过去差不多<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年半了。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>并没有公布升级日志，所以这次的升级内容不得而知，不过这么久了，升级新功能是不太可能了，很可能是修复一些漏洞提高安全性。</p>
