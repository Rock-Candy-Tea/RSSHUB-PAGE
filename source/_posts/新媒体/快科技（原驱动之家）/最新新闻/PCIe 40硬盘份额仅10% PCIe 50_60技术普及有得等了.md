<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>在<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">3000</span>系列处理器上就首发了<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 4.0</span>技术，现在已经两年半时间了，各大<span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>厂商也都有<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 4.0</span>旗舰级硬盘上市，但是去年该技术的份额也只有<span lang="EN-US">10%</span>而已，<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>及刚刚发布的<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 6.0</span>有得等了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">来自供应链的消息称，在<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年的时候，<span lang="EN-US">SATA SSD</span>硬盘份额依然超过<span lang="EN-US">1/3</span>，<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 4.0</span>只占<span lang="EN-US">3%</span>份额，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年才提高到了<span lang="EN-US">10%</span>，今年的普及速度还会加快，但份额也只有<span lang="EN-US">30%</span>左右。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>按照这个速度下去，<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 4.0</span>硬盘要想成为主流需要到<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">至于<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>在去年底的<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Alde</span>人<span lang="EN-US">Lake</span>上首发支持，三星、铠侠等公司也推出了<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>硬盘，速度可从目前的<span lang="EN-US">7GB/s</span>提升到<span lang="EN-US">14GB</span>甚至<span lang="EN-US">15GB/s</span>，性能轻松翻倍。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不过<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>初期市场是针对企业、数据中心的，消费级<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>硬盘在本月初的<span lang="EN-US">CES</span>展会上有过展示，但上市时间要到下半年。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">从产业链的消息来看，<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>硬盘今年会正式启动，<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年才有可能爆发性增长。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="PCIe 4.0硬盘份额仅10% PCIe 5.0/6.0技术普及有得等了" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220114/e988025c-ac20-4df2-bf98-cec663399d3b.png" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此外，这几天<span lang="EN-US">PCI-SIG</span>组织又发布了<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 6.0</span>标准，速度翻倍到<span lang="EN-US">64GT/s</span>，带宽<span lang="EN-US">8GB/s</span>，用于<span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>的话<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 6.0 x4</span>可提供最高<span lang="EN-US">32GB/s</span>的速度。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不过<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 6.0</span>跟<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>一样，初期都是面向高性能计算领域的，消费级普及又得等了，甚至未来会跟<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 4.0</span>长期共存。</p>
