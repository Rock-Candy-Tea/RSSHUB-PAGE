<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220624/790fb635563349d28608fe0dd93b9795.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《雷神4》首映式" h="800" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220624/s_790fb635563349d28608fe0dd93b9795.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>评论人士普遍认为《雷神4》贯彻了维迪提一贯手法，称赞该片提供了一场颠覆性的放纵狂欢，剧情与故事核心都很好，角色塑造给力。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220624/dc71f64863c344b8827d7fe2e7295361.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《雷神4》首映式" h="599" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220624/s_dc71f64863c344b8827d7fe2e7295361.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>媒体记者尤其赞赏扮演“屠神者格尔”的克里斯蒂安·贝尔，称赞他所扮演的格尔是这辈子见过的最令人毛骨悚然的银幕反派。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220624/2f41d5d0dcb04fcba2e148c0d39caf2f.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《雷神4》首映式" h="321" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220624/s_2f41d5d0dcb04fcba2e148c0d39caf2f.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220624/6fe3dc2a27094c47ada576672b9dbcf1.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《雷神4》首映式" h="583" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220624/s_6fe3dc2a27094c47ada576672b9dbcf1.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>批评者承认《雷神4》是漫威宇宙最滑稽的影片（对维迪提来说并无意外），贝尔一贯优秀，格尔压迫力十足。但剧情过于平铺直叙，故事核心平淡无奇，也许雷神的世界就是这副德行，抑或漫威的四平八稳路线在作怪。</p>
<p align="center"><img alt="《雷神4》首映式" h="913" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220624/cf140869cf2d480795c07884b5b57d51.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="581" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>银护一米八大长腿也有份出演该片并出席了首映式：</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220624/f1ff4d0c9f084b00bff17d90652347d4.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="《雷神4》首映式" h="810" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220624/s_f1ff4d0c9f084b00bff17d90652347d4.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220624/7adf1804c34c4f36b6fa389554ff8646.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《雷神4》首映式" h="833" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220624/s_7adf1804c34c4f36b6fa389554ff8646.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>如果波特曼有星云这等身高，也许就不用脚下垫高一英尺才能与锤哥配戏了：</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220624/42c66f129bb14307a6094920f5d41264.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="《雷神4》首映式" h="801" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220624/s_42c66f129bb14307a6094920f5d41264.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>蛰伏许久的大胸毒舌妹也现身首映式，她演过《雷神1》与《雷神2》，算是雷神班底的老熟人了：</p>
