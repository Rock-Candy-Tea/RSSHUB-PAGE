<p>《头脑特工队2》（Inside Out 2）将深入一位青少年的大脑，探究新的感情活动，定档2024年夏。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220910/392cbef7744041c6b78d2849ae7cd04b.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《头脑特工队2》定档2024" h="466" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220910/s_392cbef7744041c6b78d2849ae7cd04b.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>该片创作团队承自《恐龙当家》，该片编剧Kelsey Mann升任《头脑特工队2》的导演，该片主笔Meg LeFauve（右三）将为新片撰写剧本。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220910/54aa5f2c626e40759633791ffd53b611.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《头脑特工队2》定档2024" h="415" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220910/s_54aa5f2c626e40759633791ffd53b611.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>另外，《月光男孩》的导演巴里·詹金斯（Barry Jenkins）将为迪斯尼执导《穆法沙：狮子王》（狮子王前传），定档2024。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220910/affeac5647494a64a2b0a4f87352a779.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《头脑特工队2》定档2024" h="311" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220910/s_affeac5647494a64a2b0a4f87352a779.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>根据詹金斯的描绘，前传讲述穆法沙如何由流浪公狮成功晋级领地首领，观众将见识到青年版丁满及彭彭（狐獴与疣猪）。继19版《狮子王》后，塞斯·罗根再次为彭彭献声。19版《狮子王》以2.6亿美元预算攫取到16.6亿美元全球票房，成绩令人乍舌。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220910/2d1a8a620b43411dbffed2d3fd56e3f8.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《头脑特工队2》定档2024" h="412" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220910/s_2d1a8a620b43411dbffed2d3fd56e3f8.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
