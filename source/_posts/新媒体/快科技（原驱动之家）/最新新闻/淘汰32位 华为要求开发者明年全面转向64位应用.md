<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">32</span>位应用正在被淘汰，<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>宣布在<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年停止支持纯<span lang="EN-US">32</span>位处理器，苹果、谷歌也早就要求转向<span lang="EN-US">64</span>位<span lang="EN-US">APP</span>了，现在华为也要求开发者加快转变，明年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月份开始淘汰<span lang="EN-US">32</span>位应用，要提供<span lang="EN-US">64</span>位版，而且推荐<span lang="EN-US">AAB</span>格式打包。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据华为开发者联盟的消息，华为日前发布通知，要求开发者加快淘汰<span lang="EN-US">32</span>位应用，具体分为两个阶段，详细如下：</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">1</span>、自<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日起，在华为应用市场新上架<span lang="EN-US">/</span>升级的应用及游戏，必须包含<span lang="EN-US">64</span>位版，华为应用市场不再接收仅包含<span lang="EN-US">32</span>位版的应用；</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">2</span>、自<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日起，华为应用市场不再接收包含<span lang="EN-US">32</span>位版的应用<span lang="EN-US">(</span>即只能提交<span lang="EN-US">64</span>位版<span lang="EN-US">)</span>。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">除了淘汰<span lang="EN-US">32</span>位应用之外，华为也针对<span lang="EN-US">APP</span>应用的封装格式提出了建议，内容如下：</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">App Bundle</span>应用分发：<strong>上传<span lang="EN-US">aab</span>格式的软件包，<span lang="EN-US">App Bundle</span>在分发阶段将根据对应设备的<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>类型对<span lang="EN-US">aab</span>包进行拆分，</strong>打包成对应的<span lang="EN-US">apk</span>后进行灵活分发，并能有效减小应用包体积。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">仅上传<span lang="EN-US">64</span>位包体：您也可以直接上传<span lang="EN-US">64</span>位的<span lang="EN-US">apk</span>包体，以确保应用在<span lang="EN-US">64</span>位机型中的最佳性能体验。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">注：在过渡期间可以将<span lang="EN-US">32</span>位和<span lang="EN-US">64</span>位版打包在一起，以确保其在不同的<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>架构下的性能体验。</p>
