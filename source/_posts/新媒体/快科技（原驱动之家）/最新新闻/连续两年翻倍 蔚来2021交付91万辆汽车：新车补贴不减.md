<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，蔚来公布<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月及全年交付量。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月，蔚来交付新车<span lang="EN-US">10489</span>台，同比增长<span lang="EN-US">49.7%</span>；四季度共交付<span lang="EN-US">25034</span>台，同比增长<span lang="EN-US">44.3%</span>，实现连续七个季度正增长。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年蔚来交付新车<span lang="EN-US">91429</span>台，同比增长<span lang="EN-US">109.1%</span>，连续两年翻番。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">截至<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日，蔚来累计交付智能电动汽车<span lang="EN-US">167070</span>台。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此前财政部、工业和信息化部、科技部、发展改革委发布通知，<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年保持现行购置补贴技术指标体系框架及门槛要求不变；<strong><span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年，新能源汽车补贴标准在<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年基础上退坡<span lang="EN-US">30%</span>。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">针对补贴下滑一事，蔚来也公布了<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年购车补贴方案，<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日<span lang="EN-US"> (</span>含<span lang="EN-US">) </span>前支付定金购买<span lang="EN-US">ES8</span>、<span lang="EN-US">ES6</span>及<span lang="EN-US">EC6</span>，且在<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日前提车的用户，<strong>仍可按照<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年国家补贴标准享受补贴，差额由蔚来承担。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">据悉，蔚来<span lang="EN-US">75kWh</span>标准续航电池包版车型可享个人补贴<span lang="EN-US">11340</span>元、企业补贴<span lang="EN-US">7938</span>元；<span lang="EN-US">100kWh</span>电池包版车型则补贴<span lang="EN-US">12600</span>元，企业<span lang="EN-US">8820</span>元。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">蔚来<span lang="EN-US">ET7</span>将于<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日开启交付，新车此前已经公布了价格，<span lang="EN-US">ET7 </span>补贴前起售价<span lang="EN-US">44.8</span>万元，<span lang="EN-US">BaaS</span>方案补贴前起售价<span lang="EN-US">37.8</span>万元；首发版补贴前售价<span lang="EN-US">52.6</span>万元，<span lang="EN-US">BaaS</span>方案补贴前售价<span lang="EN-US">39.8</span>万元。</p>
