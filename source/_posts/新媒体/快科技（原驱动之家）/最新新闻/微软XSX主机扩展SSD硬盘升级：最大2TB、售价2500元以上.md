<p class="MsoNormal">在新一代<span lang="EN-US">Xbox Series X/S</span>（以下简称<span lang="EN-US">XSX/XSS</span>）主机上，除了标配的<span lang="EN-US">1TB SSD</span>硬盘之外，微软还联合希捷定制了游戏扩展专用<span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>硬盘，之前只有<span lang="EN-US">1TB</span>容量的。现在微软、希捷又推出了<span lang="EN-US">512GB</span>及<span lang="EN-US">2TB</span>的选择，售价<span lang="EN-US">139</span>、<span lang="EN-US">399</span>美元，分别折合<span lang="EN-US">889</span>、<span lang="EN-US">2552</span>元。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">XSX</span>的内置<span lang="EN-US">1TB</span>硬盘显然是不够用的，外置硬盘除了<span lang="EN-US">USB 3.2</span>接口的之外，微软还专门找希捷定制了一种扩展<span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>大小跟常见的<span lang="EN-US">U</span>盘差不多，甚至可以说更小一些，只有指甲盖般大小，容量也能达到<span lang="EN-US">1TB</span>。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">至于性能，微软还没公布这款<span lang="EN-US">1TB</span>迷你<span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>的具体速度，但官方表示它跟内部的<span lang="EN-US">NVMe</span>硬盘是匹配的，意味着速度也有<span lang="EN-US">GB/s</span>级别的，性能也会很强悍。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">之前容量只有<span lang="EN-US">1TB</span>，现在多了<span lang="EN-US">512GB</span>及<span lang="EN-US">2TB</span>的选择，售价<span lang="EN-US">139</span>、<span lang="EN-US">399</span>美元，<strong>分别折合<span lang="EN-US">889</span>、<span lang="EN-US">2552</span>元，而<span lang="EN-US">1TB</span>版售价<span lang="EN-US">219</span>美元，约合<span lang="EN-US">1400</span>元，不过这都是不含税价格，预计<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月份上市。</strong></p>
