<p>宝马的拥护者认为，当年公司以市场需求为借口，中止了超跑项目“Vision M NEXT”，这是一种短视甚至胆怯的行为。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220506/bdbba299c9a94148a873bb2e70536994.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马应该继续开发超跑" h="312" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220506/s_bdbba299c9a94148a873bb2e70536994.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>法拉利最近宣布的SP48 Unica定制车进一步激发了Bimmer的不满，法拉利成天折腾这些拉皮项目却不愁卖，说明需求不会自己找上门来：</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220506/81cf45b27f94428f941633c9eb7a1062.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马应该继续开发超跑" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220506/s_81cf45b27f94428f941633c9eb7a1062.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>

<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220506/3f77c4634970448a99665bae7c99b4ae.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马应该继续开发超跑" h="342" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220506/s_3f77c4634970448a99665bae7c99b4ae.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220506/c00c68a94ac1435cbdb466f707c21458.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马应该继续开发超跑" h="685" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220506/s_c00c68a94ac1435cbdb466f707c21458.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>宝马的Vision M NEXT当年赢得满堂彩却无疾而终，对不起公司所拥有的技术实力与目标用户群。</p>
<p>i8铩羽而归并非需求不存在，而是i8不够好。以当今纯电及混动技术而言，超跑的制造门槛大大降低了。复活Vision M NEXT能证明宝马的技术实力，进而提升i4及iX等纯电车型的产品形象。而不是去鼓捣臃肿的XM，贵为史上最笨重的宝马乘用车。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220506/4df5f2bbc21b4ecd82eb9867e5b18329.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="宝马应该继续开发超跑" h="329" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220506/s_4df5f2bbc21b4ecd82eb9867e5b18329.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>如今再来看这款宝马超跑，会发现《赛博朋克2077》里的湖女之剑与石中剑实际上是以这款车为原型：</p>
