<div>   
<p>今天，搭载苹果自研M2处理器的MacBook Pro 13英寸版已经正式发售。</p>
<p>在发售同期，<strong>有媒体便发布了M2版MacBook Pro的拆机照片，首次对外展示了这款产品的外观设计。</strong></p>
<p>在打开新MacBook Pro的后壳后，映入眼帘的是苹果一如既往整洁的内部设计风格，电池、散热铜管和其他元器件的排布都相当工整。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220624/c2532b33892547c0bba562f48424e0c2.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="M2版MacBook Pro迎来首拆：延续M1时代布局方案" h="399" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220624/s_c2532b33892547c0bba562f48424e0c2.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a><br>
M2版MacBook Pro</p>
<p>但对于见识过M1版MacBook Pro的用户来说，<strong>这一内部结构已经相似到了足以让人怀疑“是不是拆错了”的程度。</strong></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220624/5bae0f306fd7434985fc22b742c0fa7f.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="M2版MacBook Pro迎来首拆：延续M1时代布局方案" h="450" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220624/s_5bae0f306fd7434985fc22b742c0fa7f.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a><br>
M1版MacBook Pro</p>
<p>与老版本相比，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>新的MacBook Pro除了隐藏在散热片下的M2处理器外，几乎没有任何区别。</strong></span></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220624/142d90bba52c4b1d9e057a31d43a2a43.png" target="_blank"><img alt="M2版MacBook Pro迎来首拆：延续M1时代布局方案" h="292" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220624/s_142d90bba52c4b1d9e057a31d43a2a43.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>这意味着，<strong>和外观上沿用了M1时代的老模具一样，M2版MacBook Pro的内部设计也一样沿用了M1的“经典”布局。</strong></p>
<p>对于苹果和供应链来说，这使得在生产的过程中能够大量套用此前产品的模具，并有不少能够无缝替换的零部件，节省更多成本。</p>
<p>据悉，<strong>尚未正式发售的M2版MacBook Air将采用新的模具，这或许会让它的内部结构与M1版本相比有明显变化。</strong></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220624/7c079bdd9ee04ea1bcbb2e5c705ed4d7.png" target="_blank"><img alt="M2版MacBook Pro迎来首拆：延续M1时代布局方案" h="376" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220624/s_7c079bdd9ee04ea1bcbb2e5c705ed4d7.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
