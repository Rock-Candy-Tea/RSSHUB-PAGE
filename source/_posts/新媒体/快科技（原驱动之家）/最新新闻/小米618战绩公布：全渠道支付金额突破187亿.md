<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年的<span lang="EN-US">618</span>大战刚刚过去，现在各家的战报陆续公布，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>小米公司在官微表示今年全渠道支付金额突破<span lang="EN-US">187</span>亿。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据小米公布的信息，全渠道包括小米商城、小米之家、小米有品、各大授权电商及授权零售商等，统计时间是<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日<span lang="EN-US">20</span>点到<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日<span lang="EN-US">23</span>点<span lang="EN-US">59</span>分。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">作为对比，<strong>小米<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年的<span lang="EN-US">618</span>全平台累计支付金额突破<span lang="EN-US">150</span>亿元，时间点是<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日<span lang="EN-US">-6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日<span lang="EN-US">0</span>时<span lang="EN-US">0</span>分<span lang="EN-US">10</span>秒。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此前，今年<span lang="EN-US">618</span>开门红，小米仅用时<span lang="EN-US">3</span>分<span lang="EN-US">40</span>秒全渠道支付金额破<span lang="EN-US">5</span>亿元，<span lang="EN-US">14</span>分<span lang="EN-US">10</span>秒全渠道支付金额超<span lang="EN-US">10</span>亿元，<span lang="EN-US">4</span>小时全渠道支付金额破<span lang="EN-US">22</span>亿元。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">京东开门红！包揽京东手机品牌销量榜／手机全部单品榜销量／安卓旗舰手机榜销量<span lang="EN-US">3</span>项第一，斩获京东多价位段手机销量第一；</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">其中，<span lang="EN-US">Redmi K50</span>拿下京东<span lang="EN-US">2000-3000</span>元价位段销量第一；小米<span lang="EN-US">12</span>拿下京东<span lang="EN-US">3000-4000</span>元价位段销量第一。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">天猫开门红！斩获安卓手机销额第一，<span lang="EN-US">Redmi K50</span>斩获<span lang="EN-US">2000-3000</span>元价位段销量第一。</p>
