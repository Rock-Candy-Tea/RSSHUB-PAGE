<p class="MsoNormal">在<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年，有越来越多的内存厂商使用了合肥长鑫公司开发的<span lang="EN-US">DDR4</span>内存芯片，产能也在稳步增长，据悉<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年已达<span lang="EN-US">6</span>万晶圆<span lang="EN-US">/</span>月，消息称今年的产能将翻倍，达到<span lang="EN-US">12</span>万晶圆<span lang="EN-US">/</span>月的水平，同时还会有更先进的<span lang="EN-US">17nm</span>工艺<span lang="EN-US">DDR5/LPDDR5</span>等内存芯片。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">据介绍，合肥长鑫存储作为一体化存储器制造商，专业从事动态随机存取存储芯片<span lang="EN-US">(DRAM)</span>的设计、研发、生产和销售，目前已建成第一座<span lang="EN-US">12</span>英寸晶圆厂并投产，<span lang="EN-US">18</span>年研发国内首个<span lang="EN-US">8Gb DDR4</span>芯片，<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">Q3</span>季度量产，<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年多款国产内存已经用上了长鑫的颗粒。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">产能方面，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>长鑫在<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年、<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年分别实现了<span lang="EN-US">4.5</span>万片晶圆<span lang="EN-US">/</span>月、<span lang="EN-US">6</span>万片晶圆<span lang="EN-US">/</span>月的目标，<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年的产能目标是<span lang="EN-US">12</span>万片晶圆<span lang="EN-US">/</span>月，未来的产能目标是<span lang="EN-US">30</span>万片晶圆<span lang="EN-US">/</span>月。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">国产内存今年的产能目标在全球是什么水平？此前<span lang="EN-US">TrendForce</span>预测，<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年底全球内存产能将达到<span lang="EN-US">150</span>万晶圆<span lang="EN-US">/</span>月，三星产能超过<span lang="EN-US">55.5</span>万晶圆<span lang="EN-US">/</span>月，<span lang="EN-US">SK</span>海力士是<span lang="EN-US">36</span>万片晶圆<span lang="EN-US">/</span>月，美光也有<span lang="EN-US">35.5</span>万晶圆<span lang="EN-US">/</span>月。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">如果今年内国产内存产能如期建成，<strong>那么合肥长鑫能进入全球第四，</strong>不过与前三名的差距还很大。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><img alt="12万晶圆/月 消息称国产内存今年产能翻倍：17nm DDR5在路上" h="309" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220114/e4cc7759-4cd6-41ed-ac39-4dd0e1d6a338.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid" w="550" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">当然，国产内存扩增产能依然是好事，用网友的话说就是国内有自己的内存芯片生产，就不怕别人的内存厂失火、断电、地震等灾难了。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal">除了产能扩增，国产内存的技术水平也在追赶，目前长鑫量产的主要是第一代<span lang="EN-US">10nm</span>级别工艺<span lang="EN-US">10G1</span>，实际水平应该是<span lang="EN-US">19nm</span>，主要以<span lang="EN-US">DDR4</span>、<span lang="EN-US">LPDDR4</span>为主，<strong><span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年报道中提到研发<span lang="EN-US">17nm</span>及以下工艺的<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>、<span lang="EN-US">LPDDR5</span>等内存，这是<span lang="EN-US">10G3</span>代工艺，未来还有<span lang="EN-US">10G5</span>工艺，除了<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>、<span lang="EN-US">LPDDR5</span>之外还有<span lang="EN-US">GDDR6</span>显存。</strong></p>
