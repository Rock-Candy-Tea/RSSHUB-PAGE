<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年即将过去，明年的<span lang="EN-US">CES 2022</span>展会上<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>应该会推出<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3090 Ti</span>等多款显卡，继续填补<span lang="EN-US">RTX 30</span>系列市场空白，而明年下半年将会推出<span lang="EN-US">RTX 40</span>系列显卡，使用台积电<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>工艺。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>的下一代<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>包括<span lang="EN-US">Hopper</span>和<span lang="EN-US">Ada Lovelace</span>两大架构，均基于台积电的<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>工艺，其中<span lang="EN-US">Hopper</span>主要面向数据中心，服务<span lang="EN-US">AI</span>、云计算、元宇宙等场景，多新片<span lang="EN-US">MCM</span>设计，且会采用台积电的<span lang="EN-US">CoWos</span>扇出形封装，预计很快流片。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Ada Lovelace</span>则是游戏显卡<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>，对应<span lang="EN-US">RTX 40</span>系列，其中<span lang="EN-US">AD102</span>核心预计集成多达<span lang="EN-US">18432</span>颗<span lang="EN-US">CUDA</span>核心，比安培架构<span lang="EN-US">GA102</span>增幅超<span lang="EN-US">71%</span>，再加上频率将达到<span lang="EN-US">2.2GHz</span>，理论上<span lang="EN-US">FP32</span>浮点计算性能可达<span lang="EN-US">81TFlops</span>，对比<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3090 32TFlops</span>增加足足<span lang="EN-US">1.25</span>倍。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">RTX 40</span>系列升级很大，<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>的制造工艺也会转向台积电<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>，但是他们现在已经不是台积电的优先客户，优先级落后于苹果、联发科、<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>等厂商，要想获得稳定的<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>产能并不简单。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">业界消息称，<strong>台积电对苹果、联发科、<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>等三大客户的要求较低，不需要预付太多的保证金就能稳定产能，<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>这样的客户要想获得<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>产能订单，需要提前支付巨额预付款。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>的消息，他们在<span lang="EN-US">Q3</span>季度预付了<span lang="EN-US">16.4</span>亿美元的款项，未来还会支付<span lang="EN-US">17.9</span>亿美元，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>而整个长期订单预付款高达<span lang="EN-US">69</span>亿美元，</strong></span>远高于去年的水平。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">这<span lang="EN-US">69</span>亿美元的订单中不确定三星与台积电的比例，但是从工艺升级方向来看，找台积电买<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>产能显然占了大部分，可以说为了明年的<span lang="EN-US">RTX 40</span>系列显卡，<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>这一波也是付出真金白银了。</p>
