<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">10nm</span>可以说是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>史上最艰难的一代工艺了，原本<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年就该接替<span lang="EN-US">14nm</span>工艺，然而饱受良率<span lang="EN-US">/</span>产能折磨，直到现在的<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年才超过<span lang="EN-US">14nm</span>工艺，成本也降低了<span lang="EN-US">45%</span>，能承担大任了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">虽然<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>现在是从<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年的<span lang="EN-US">Ice lake</span>开始算第一代<span lang="EN-US">10nm</span>，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>然而真正意义上的第一款<span lang="EN-US">10nm</span>处理器是<span lang="EN-US">Cannon Lake</span>大炮湖，<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年底少量生产，上市的主要是酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i3-8121U</span>这一款，联想等个别厂商用在低端笔记本上，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>也有对应的<span lang="EN-US">NUC</span>电脑。</strong></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="大炮湖变炮灰 Intel彻底删除第一代10nm酷睿处理器支持" h="1080" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210813/c7a31371-917e-4de3-965b-c06916e9c129.jpg" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="455" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Cannon Lake</span>不仅工艺升级到<span lang="EN-US">10nm</span>，<strong>架构设计也可圈可点，是首款支持<span lang="EN-US">AVX-512</span>指令集的消费级<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>，</strong>核显也升级为<span lang="EN-US">Gen10</span>，然而上市的酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i3-8121U</span>因为工艺缺陷，实际上是屏蔽核显的，还需要搭配独显。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">正因为种种尴尬，酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i3-8121U</span>一直不受欢迎，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>自己也不待见，对它的存在没有高调宣传，<strong>去年开始删除<span lang="EN-US">Linux</span>代码支持，现在最新的一波<span lang="EN-US">Linux</span>内核图形驱动代码中彻底移除了<span lang="EN-US">Cannon Lake</span>的支持。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">当然，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>这波内核驱动代码升级主要是给<span lang="EN-US">DG2</span>独显做准备的，预计在<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年的<span lang="EN-US">CES</span>展会上发布，升级<span lang="EN-US">Xe HPG</span>架构，至少有<span lang="EN-US">6</span>款型号，性能最高可达到<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3070 Ti</span>水平。</p>
