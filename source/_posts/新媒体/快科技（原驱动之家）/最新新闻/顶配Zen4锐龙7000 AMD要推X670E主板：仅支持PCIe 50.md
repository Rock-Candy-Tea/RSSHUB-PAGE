<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">下周<span lang="EN-US">AMD CEO</span>苏姿丰就要在台北电脑展<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>上发表主题演讲，不出意外会正式发布<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>架构的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>处理器了，架构、工艺及性能全面升级。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据最新的爆料，锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>的旗舰新品是锐龙<span lang="EN-US">9 7950X</span>，<strong>至少是<span lang="EN-US">16</span>核<span lang="EN-US">32</span>线程，加速频率能冲到<span lang="EN-US">5.4GHz</span>，</strong>不过也有消息称它是<span lang="EN-US">24</span>核<span lang="EN-US">48</span>线程，现在还不能<span lang="EN-US">100%</span>确认，但是性能会很给力，<span lang="EN-US">IPC</span>及频率双双提升，有望赢回被<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿超越的单核及游戏性能。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>升级的同时，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>还会推出新的<span lang="EN-US">600</span>系芯片组，包括<span lang="EN-US">X670</span>、<span lang="EN-US">B650</span>及入门级的<span lang="EN-US">A620</span>等，据说<span lang="EN-US">X670</span>还是双芯设计，<strong>不过现在又爆出个<span lang="EN-US">X670E</span>，<span lang="EN-US">E</span>代表的是“<span lang="EN-US">Extreme”</span>，定位更高，主打发烧级游戏平台。</strong></p>


<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220520/97e8e09ea0e5405ea24ce321d9d47b08.png" target="_blank"><img alt="顶配Zen4锐龙7000 AMD要推X670E主板：仅支持PCIe 5.0" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220520/s_97e8e09ea0e5405ea24ce321d9d47b08.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">X670E</span>主板也只会支持<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>，跟<span lang="EN-US">X670</span>的<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 4.0</span>区别开，同时还有一些硬核超频功能，目前具体细节未知。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">总的来看，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>及<span lang="EN-US">600</span>系平台性能可期，但旗舰产品免不了更高端，也就是更贵，大家要做好准备。</p>
