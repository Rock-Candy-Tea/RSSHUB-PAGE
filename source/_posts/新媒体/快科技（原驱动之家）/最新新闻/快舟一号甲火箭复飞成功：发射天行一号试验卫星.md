<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">据央视报道，<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日<span lang="EN-US">10</span>时<span lang="EN-US">08</span>分，我国在酒泉卫星发射中心使用快舟一号甲运载火箭，成功将天行一号试验卫星发射升空。</p>
<p class="newsinfo_vedio"><iframe align="middle" id="kkj_video_play_iframe1" src="//blog.mydrivers.com/news/showvideo.aspx?tid=840901&ac=img&url=https://weibo.com/2656274875/LyRYQgULD&k=1" frameborder="0" width="600px" height="400px" scrolling="no"></iframe></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">卫星顺利进入预定轨道，发射任务获得圆满成功，该卫星主要用于开展空间环境探测等试验。</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: "Segoe UI", sans-serif; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;"></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">本次发射是快舟一号甲火箭的第<span lang="EN-US">15</span>次发射，总计<span lang="EN-US">13</span>次成功、<span lang="EN-US">2</span>次失利，<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日发射吉林一号高分<span lang="EN-US">02C</span>星出现故障，未能成功。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>上一次的发射是<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日发射<span lang="EN-US">GeeSAT</span>双星失利，这一次也是快舟一号甲复飞成功。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">快舟一号甲是航天科工火箭技术有限公司推出的一款小型固体运载火箭，具备国际通用接口，主要为<span lang="EN-US">300</span>公斤级低轨小卫星提供发射服务，具有飞行可靠性高、入轨精度高、准备周期短、保障需求少、发射成本低等特点。</p>
