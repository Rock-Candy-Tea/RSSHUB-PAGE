<p>微软宣布Xbox Series X造型迷你冰箱开放预购，首发销售权独家授予Target连锁超市及Target网站，19号开始预售，12月到货，定价99美元。</p>
<p align="center"><img alt="XSX冰箱开始预定" h="607" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211016/68cfad6a4a43432ba1949446cdd07385.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="583" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>英国及欧洲地区的Xbox粉丝可在GAME、GameStop、亚马逊等实体店及电商渠道购买。冰箱完全复制XSX光效，哑光外壳留有实际可用的USB端子：</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211016/27c43a2dca5d462b812214de768be457.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="XSX冰箱开始预定" h="600" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211016/s_27c43a2dca5d462b812214de768be457.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211016/8befb526ac9a4c0b892615b4f3b73581.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="XSX冰箱开始预定" h="600" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211016/s_8befb526ac9a4c0b892615b4f3b73581.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211016/3b734f30a8014e08900facab14ff1f2b.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="XSX冰箱开始预定" h="600" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211016/s_3b734f30a8014e08900facab14ff1f2b.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>冰箱外部尺寸462H x 232W x 232D毫米；内部尺寸352.3H x 174W x 175D毫米，较XSX主机略大；内部容积十升，可存放10至12罐355毫升规格饮料。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211016/c6ea4a286d9e4b65a4e2ac168cc9bc6a.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="XSX冰箱开始预定" h="600" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211016/s_c6ea4a286d9e4b65a4e2ac168cc9bc6a.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>冰箱为交直流两用，包装内附直流适配器，也许能车载？</p>
