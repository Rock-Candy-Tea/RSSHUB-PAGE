<p>随着Intel发布Rocket Lake 11代酷睿处理器，在主流桌面平台上都已经可以支持PCIe 4.0的M.2 SSD。</p>
<p>相对于过去的PCIe 3.0 SSD，即使是相对早期方案的PCIe 4.0，也可以对PCIe 3.0 SSD形成明显优势。今天就带来希捷酷玩520 SSD 2TB测试报告。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210610/3fc8ab69-b7e5-4002-81dd-a6e017e15676.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="希捷酷玩520 PCIe 4.0 2TB SSD实战：千万别挖矿" h="846" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210610/S3fc8ab69-b7e5-4002-81dd-a6e017e15676.jpg" style="border:black 1px solid" w="1269" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p><strong>产品开箱图赏：</strong></p>
<p>酷玩520的附件十分简单，就两份纸质说明书，SSD没有配原装散热片。不过比较有意思的是，<strong>希捷提供了一次保修期内免费数据恢复，这是比较少有的服务。</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210610/b36f8b58-c1c7-4540-9a59-cd647359f122.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="希捷酷玩520 PCIe 4.0 2TB SSD实战：千万别挖矿" h="846" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210610/Sb36f8b58-c1c7-4540-9a59-cd647359f122.jpg" style="border:black 1px solid" w="1269" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>酷玩520的尺寸规格为2280。接口为M.2 M-key，支持PCIe 4.0 X4。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210610/55de8f61-b9da-496b-aed7-81e1510483ac.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="希捷酷玩520 PCIe 4.0 2TB SSD实战：千万别挖矿" h="846" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210610/S55de8f61-b9da-496b-aed7-81e1510483ac.jpg" style="border:black 1px solid" w="1269" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p><strong>主控为希捷定制的STXZP010BE70，他是群联E16的定制版本。</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210610/1487ce32-0036-4368-a181-7b1522e4131c.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="希捷酷玩520 PCIe 4.0 2TB SSD实战：千万别挖矿" h="1000" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210610/S1487ce32-0036-4368-a181-7b1522e4131c.jpg" style="border:black 1px solid" w="1500" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>电源转换芯片是群联PS6106-22。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210610/88a4a44c-a735-4780-97a7-63766250ada5.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="希捷酷玩520 PCIe 4.0 2TB SSD实战：千万别挖矿" h="1000" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210610/S88a4a44c-a735-4780-97a7-63766250ada5.jpg" style="border:black 1px solid" w="1500" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>SSD的缓存正反面各一颗，<strong>是海力士的H5AN8G8NCJ-RVKC</strong>，是DDR4 2666频率单颗容量1GB。相比更常见的DDR3颗粒会更快。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210610/0a304b6f-261f-4f1e-9e10-e0c40a22d8db.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="希捷酷玩520 PCIe 4.0 2TB SSD实战：千万别挖矿" h="1000" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210610/S0a304b6f-261f-4f1e-9e10-e0c40a22d8db.jpg" style="border:black 1px solid" w="1500" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210610/73c9b6fc-61f7-49ca-ad66-e5a2717e0cf9.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="希捷酷玩520 PCIe 4.0 2TB SSD实战：千万别挖矿" h="1000" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210610/S73c9b6fc-61f7-49ca-ad66-e5a2717e0cf9.jpg" style="border:black 1px solid" w="1500" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p><strong>存储颗粒型号为TABHG65AWV，是东芝的3D TLC颗粒。</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210610/e76b47b8-f288-4c83-b22e-a563748ca7eb.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="希捷酷玩520 PCIe 4.0 2TB SSD实战：千万别挖矿" h="1000" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210610/Se76b47b8-f288-4c83-b22e-a563748ca7eb.jpg" style="border:black 1px solid" w="1500" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
