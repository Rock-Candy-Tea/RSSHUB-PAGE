<p>近日，联发科正式发布了<a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/803/803163.htm" target="_blank">新一代旗舰移动平台天玑9000</a>，拥有史无前例的强大性能、丰富功能。OPPO Find X系列新品、vivo新旗舰、Redmi K50系列都已经确认将会搭载。</p>
<p>对于新平台，大家最关心的自然是性能表现，尤其是和高通骁龙8 Gen1对比到底如何？</p>
<p><strong>我们快科技也拿到了一部天玑9000工程机，跑了一下安兔兔、GeekBench、GFXBench、PCMark四个基准测试，一起来提前了解下！</strong></p>
<p>当然了，这里使用的只是工程机，不代表实际产品的表现，而且按照惯例，随着平台的不断优化，性能也会持续得到提升。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211220/5d84865ab49f4d038e31a0a19a7c8304.png" target="_blank"><img alt="联发科天玑9000跑分实测首发！对比骁龙8 Gen1一胜一负" h="664" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211220/s_5d84865ab49f4d038e31a0a19a7c8304.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="299" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>  <a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211220/d8ac475ced764e3cbf903fb69439f550.png" target="_blank"><img alt="联发科天玑9000跑分实测首发！对比骁龙8 Gen1一胜一负" h="664" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211220/s_d8ac475ced764e3cbf903fb69439f550.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="299" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211220/5701452a965547d5921d0d16b1505769.png" target="_blank"><img alt="联发科天玑9000跑分实测首发！对比骁龙8 Gen1一胜一负" h="664" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211220/s_5701452a965547d5921d0d16b1505769.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="299" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a> <a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211220/c25277b6b8134654abe55182ebd889e0.png" target="_blank"><img alt="联发科天玑9000跑分实测首发！对比骁龙8 Gen1一胜一负" h="664" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211220/s_c25277b6b8134654abe55182ebd889e0.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="299" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p><strong><span style="color:#ff0000;">安兔兔突破了100万分</span>，相比于骁龙8 Gen1少了一两万分的样子</strong>，差距只在毫厘之间。</p>
<p>对比各个子项，<strong>GPU部分是唯一落后的，差距达到了12％，CPU、内存、UX则分别领先大约9％、7％、4％。</strong></p>
<p>跑分之后，<strong>温度从26℃升高到37℃</strong>，最终基本和之前跑的骁龙8 Gen1工程机差不多。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211220/d1ae0b9ae8044fbf9b165fbee29aeabd.png" target="_blank"><img alt="联发科天玑9000跑分实测首发！对比骁龙8 Gen1一胜一负" h="1333" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211220/s_d1ae0b9ae8044fbf9b165fbee29aeabd.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p><strong><span style="color:#ff0000;">GeekBench 5单核心1272分、多核心4243分</span>，对比骁龙8 Gen1前者差距不大只领先约4％，后者则超出了10％。</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211220/767982849c3f421abbd1f76d0e67fd15.png" target="_blank"><img alt="联发科天玑9000跑分实测首发！对比骁龙8 Gen1一胜一负" h="664" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211220/s_767982849c3f421abbd1f76d0e67fd15.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="299" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a> <a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211220/94678bcb758b497a9739ae099d82b3b9.png" target="_blank"><img alt="联发科天玑9000跑分实测首发！对比骁龙8 Gen1一胜一负" h="664" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211220/s_94678bcb758b497a9739ae099d82b3b9.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="299" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211220/ebbbb942dc0349af83af0b9d824cb1b8.png" target="_blank"><img alt="联发科天玑9000跑分实测首发！对比骁龙8 Gen1一胜一负" h="664" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211220/s_ebbbb942dc0349af83af0b9d824cb1b8.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="299" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a> <a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211220/d568c749f24b463b9c65746b8cc98e77.png" target="_blank"><img alt="联发科天玑9000跑分实测首发！对比骁龙8 Gen1一胜一负" h="664" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211220/s_d568c749f24b463b9c65746b8cc98e77.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="299" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p><strong><span style="color:#ff0000;">GFXBench 5曼哈顿、曼哈顿3.1、阿兹特克废墟Vulkan(High)、阿兹特克废墟OpenGL(High)分别为235FPS、159FPS、43FPS、42FPS</span>，对比骁龙8 Gen1分别落后约12％、11％、12％、2％，和安兔兔结果差不多。</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211220/033e66d65ee8457fa7be2e30318ea433.png" target="_blank"><img alt="联发科天玑9000跑分实测首发！对比骁龙8 Gen1一胜一负" h="664" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211220/s_033e66d65ee8457fa7be2e30318ea433.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="299" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a> <a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211220/17615cf43cf24ea29632ee1579496868.png" target="_blank"><img alt="联发科天玑9000跑分实测首发！对比骁龙8 Gen1一胜一负" h="664" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211220/s_17615cf43cf24ea29632ee1579496868.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="299" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p><strong><span style="color:#ff0000;">PCMark Work 3.0项目得分17660</span>，对比骁龙8 Gen1领先了大约2％，差距不大。</strong></p>
<p>总体而言，联发科天玑9000对比高通骁龙8 Gen1 CPU性能有领先优势，GPU则还有一定差距，整体基本在同一档次上，实际体验也不会有明显差别。</p>
<p>由于测试的是工程机，这次没有重点考察温度和发热情况，后续就看各家厂商的功力了！</p>
