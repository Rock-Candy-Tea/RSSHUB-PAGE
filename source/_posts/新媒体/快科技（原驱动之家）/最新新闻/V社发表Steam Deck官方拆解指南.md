<p>V社小心翼翼地表示，尽管从法理上说Steam Deck属于玩家个人财产且该设备本质上是一台PC，是PC就有自行升级部件的权利。但这东西设计得异常紧凑，不当拆解会招致财产损失甚至火灾。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211007/32d0e21aeeb3434cb7deaebb24852457.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="V社发表Steam Deck官方拆解指南" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211007/s_32d0e21aeeb3434cb7deaebb24852457.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>首先拆下八颗螺丝，然后用撬棒（或指甲）分离后盖：</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211007/be0288f48b104036ab331f9bb5e8cd65.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="V社发表Steam Deck官方拆解指南" h="453" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211007/s_be0288f48b104036ab331f9bb5e8cd65.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211007/0e65ab2073fd4b4289ebd8075403c4af.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="V社发表Steam Deck官方拆解指南" h="433" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211007/s_0e65ab2073fd4b4289ebd8075403c4af.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211007/8bfccd74d0444eb2a5854fe486ee3787.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="V社发表Steam Deck官方拆解指南" h="443" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211007/s_8bfccd74d0444eb2a5854fe486ee3787.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>V社确认所有型号均使用2230规格M2 SSD，但这枚M2固态盘藏在散热部件兼屏蔽罩下：</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211007/402e4465c5e74668913ee233a4376010.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="V社发表Steam Deck官方拆解指南" h="475" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211007/s_402e4465c5e74668913ee233a4376010.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211007/0477cc8e6a5f47ba9c8fdcf83c9655de.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="V社发表Steam Deck官方拆解指南" h="412" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211007/s_0477cc8e6a5f47ba9c8fdcf83c9655de.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>升级固态盘之前确认断开内部电池组，拧下一枚SSD螺丝即可取出这枚2230盘。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211007/7c089c98941e48c1aacde0b9e178abb4.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="V社发表Steam Deck官方拆解指南" h="324" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211007/s_7c089c98941e48c1aacde0b9e178abb4.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211007/91725de24bf749658255e90d25bdb508.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="V社发表Steam Deck官方拆解指南" h="381" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211007/s_91725de24bf749658255e90d25bdb508.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>V社在拆解视频中还演示了如何更换拇指摇杆，步骤比升级SSD简单得多，视频<a class="f14_link" href="https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1Qr4y117W6?share_source=copy_web" target="_blank">在此</a>。</p>
<p>另外，Gearbox总裁Randy Pitchford表示他指定了接班人Steve Jones接任Gearbox Software新总裁一职，他自己转任Gearbox影视工作室总裁。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211007/c214d632a0fa4f8c8e98e4c3ecbdad38.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="V社发表Steam Deck官方拆解指南" h="291" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211007/s_c214d632a0fa4f8c8e98e4c3ecbdad38.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>该部门专司影视节目，比如《无主之地》电影。游戏工作室将在新总裁Steve Jones的带领下继续开发3A级作品，受母公司Gearbox Entertainment挟制，Randy退居幕后扮演太上皇的角色。</p>
