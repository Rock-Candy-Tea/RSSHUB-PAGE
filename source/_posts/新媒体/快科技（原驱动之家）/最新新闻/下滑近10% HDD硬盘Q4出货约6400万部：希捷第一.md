<p class="MsoNormal">自从<span lang="EN-US">2011</span>年泰国大洪水之后，<span lang="EN-US">HDD</span>硬盘出货量就在逐年下滑，现在<span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>硬盘又挤占了笔记本、台式机及服务器的份额，去年<span lang="EN-US">Q4</span>季度<span lang="EN-US">HDD</span>硬盘出货量约为<span lang="EN-US">6400</span>万部，同比下滑了将近<span lang="EN-US">10%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height:150%">据调研机构<span lang="EN-US">Trendfocus</span>的数据，<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">Q4</span>季度中，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>全球<span lang="EN-US">HDD</span>硬盘出货量在<span lang="EN-US">6340</span>到<span lang="EN-US">6470</span>万部之间，相比<span lang="EN-US">Q3</span>季度减少了<span lang="EN-US">2.3-4.3%%</span>，比<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">Q4</span>季度则减少了<span lang="EN-US">7.7%-9.5%</span>，销量继续下跌。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height:150%">目前<span lang="EN-US">HDD</span>硬盘市场上只有三家厂商了，分别是希捷、<span lang="EN-US">WDC</span>西数及东芝，其中希捷在<span lang="EN-US">Q4</span>季度位列第一，出货<span lang="EN-US">2720-22770</span>万部<span lang="EN-US">HDD</span>硬盘，份额<span lang="EN-US">42.8%-42.9%</span>左右，出货量同比下滑了<span lang="EN-US">7.1%-8.8%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height:150%"><span lang="EN-US">WDC</span>西数在<span lang="EN-US">Q4</span>季度出货<span lang="EN-US">2350-2400</span>万部<span lang="EN-US">HDD</span>硬盘，同比下滑<span lang="EN-US">6.4%-8.3%</span>左右，市场份额<span lang="EN-US">37.1%</span>左右。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height:150%">东芝位列第三，<span lang="EN-US">Q4</span>季度出货<span lang="EN-US">1270-1300</span>万部<span lang="EN-US">HDD</span>硬盘，下滑<span lang="EN-US">11.1%-13.2%</span>，是三家厂商中下滑最严重的，份额跌落到<span lang="EN-US">20.0%-20.1%</span>左右。</p>
