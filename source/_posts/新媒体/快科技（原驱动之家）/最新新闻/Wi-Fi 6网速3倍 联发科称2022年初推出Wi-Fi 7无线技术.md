<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年是<span lang="EN-US">Wi-Fi 6</span>无线网络普及的关键一年，<span lang="EN-US">9.6Gbps</span>的理论速度让<span lang="EN-US">Wi-Fi</span>网速超越有线宽带，上网、游戏体验都很好。现在联发科也宣布<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年初将推出<span lang="EN-US">Wi-Fi 7</span>网络，此前数据显示其网速是<span lang="EN-US">Wi-Fi 6</span>的<span lang="EN-US">3</span>倍多。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">联发科<span lang="EN-US">CEO</span>、副董事长蔡力行日前表态，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>联发科在物联网领域，比如<span lang="EN-US">Wi-Fi 6</span>中相当成功，明年初将会推出<span lang="EN-US">Wi-Fi 7</span>技术，而联发科也会是其中的领导者之一。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此前消息称，联发科将在<span lang="EN-US">CES 2022</span>展会上发布<span lang="EN-US">Wi-Fi 7</span>网络技术，速度可达<span lang="EN-US">30Gbps</span>，是<span lang="EN-US">Wi-Fi 6</span>的<span lang="EN-US">3</span>倍，在<span lang="EN-US">Wi-Fi 6</span>标准的基础上引入了许多新的技术，比如<span lang="EN-US">320MHz</span>带宽、<span lang="EN-US">4096-QAM</span>、<span lang="EN-US">Multi-RU</span>、多链路操作、增强<span lang="EN-US">MU-MIMO</span>、多<span lang="EN-US">AP</span>协作等，体验比<span lang="EN-US">Wi-Fi 6</span>更好。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">与此同时，<strong><span lang="EN-US">Wi-Fi 7</span>网络芯片也会从目前的<span lang="EN-US">16/12nm</span>工艺升级到更先进的<span lang="EN-US">6nm</span>，</strong>有助于降低功耗及发热。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不过<span lang="EN-US">Wi-Fi</span>虽然有望在明年初问世，但距离制定最终标准及上市还有段时间，要到<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年左右，未来两年中<span lang="EN-US">Wi-Fi 6/6E</span>依然会是主流。</p>
