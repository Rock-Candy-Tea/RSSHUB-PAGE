<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>凭借锐龙处理器已经收复了部分<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>失地，显卡市场的形势依然要严峻许多，<span lang="EN-US">JPR</span>最新统计显示<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>在独显市场份额不过<span lang="EN-US">21%</span>，<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>依然独占<span lang="EN-US">79%</span>份额，而且性能也比<span lang="EN-US">A</span>卡强势。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不过<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>在显卡市场上的趋势还不错，出货量还是一直在增长的，<span lang="EN-US">RX 6000</span>系列的中高端显卡占比增加，也改善了<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>显卡部门的盈利水平。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>全球营销高级副总裁<span lang="EN-US">Ruth Cotter</span>透露的消息，过去<span lang="EN-US">18</span>个月<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>显卡在细分市场上一直是增长的，目前<span lang="EN-US">RX 6000</span>系列才进入大众市场，未来几个季度中<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>显卡的收入及份额还是会继续增长的。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong><span lang="EN-US">Ruth Cotter</span>表示，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>在显卡市场上也一样会重新夺回失去的市场份额。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">目前<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的<span lang="EN-US">RX 6000</span>系列显卡基于<span lang="EN-US">7nm RDNA2</span>架构，市场布局已经完成，但这一年来主要是遇到芯片产能紧张及缺货涨价的问题，导致不够普及。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">考虑到<span lang="EN-US">RDNA2</span>架构显卡在性能及光追上跟<span lang="EN-US">RTX 30</span>系列还有一定差距，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>明年推出的<span lang="EN-US">RDNA3</span>架构显卡会更给力，此前消息称下代旗舰芯<span lang="EN-US">Navi 31</span>系列会集成多达<span lang="EN-US">15360</span>个流处理器<span lang="EN-US">(ALU</span>单元<span lang="EN-US">)</span>、<span lang="EN-US">512MB</span>无限缓存，分别是现在<span lang="EN-US">Navi 21</span>核心的三倍、四倍。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">RDNA3还会搭配<span lang="EN-US">256-bit GDDR6</span>位宽，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>核心频率可达<span lang="EN-US">2.4GHz</span>到<span lang="EN-US">2.5GHz</span>，<span lang="EN-US">FP32</span>浮点性能可达<span lang="EN-US">75TFLOPS</span>，性能比<span lang="EN-US">RX 6900 XT</span>提升<span lang="EN-US">200%</span>以上。</strong></span></p>
