<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日晚，美国股市开盘就大跌，标普<span lang="EN-US">500</span>指数跌<span lang="EN-US">2.5%</span>，较高点回撤<span lang="EN-US">20%</span>，进入技术性熊市；道指跌超<span lang="EN-US">600</span>点。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>大型科技股普跌，亚马逊跌超<span lang="EN-US">5%</span>，特斯拉跌近<span lang="EN-US">4%</span>，苹果也跌了<span lang="EN-US">2.6%</span>。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">各版块中，能源业<span lang="EN-US">ETF</span>大跌超<span lang="EN-US">4%</span>，半导体<span lang="EN-US">ETF</span>跌近<span lang="EN-US">3%</span>，可选消费<span lang="EN-US">ETF</span>、科技行业<span lang="EN-US">ETF</span>均跌超<span lang="EN-US">2%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">中概股也同样不能幸免，阿里巴巴跌超<span lang="EN-US">10%</span>，蔚来汽车跌近<span lang="EN-US">9%</span>，哔哩哔哩跌超<span lang="EN-US">8%</span>，爱奇艺跌<span lang="EN-US">8%</span>，拼多多跌超<span lang="EN-US">7%</span>，百度跌超<span lang="EN-US">6%</span>，不过新东方逆势大涨超<span lang="EN-US">10%</span>，新东方旗下直播带货平台主播<span lang="EN-US">“</span>双语带货<span lang="EN-US">”</span>人气大涨。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据数据显示，<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日至<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，东方甄选直播间粉丝单日增长分别为<span lang="EN-US">44</span>万、<span lang="EN-US">77</span>万、<span lang="EN-US">30</span>万，合计交易额增长量分别为<span lang="EN-US">262</span>万元、<span lang="EN-US">1123</span>万元、<span lang="EN-US">522</span>万元。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">从近一个月的时间来看，东方甄选累计开播<span lang="EN-US">34</span>场，粉丝增加<span lang="EN-US">102.1</span>万，累计销售额<span lang="EN-US">4558.8</span>万。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">这也让新东方在线股价暴涨，在上周五就已经直线暴涨<span lang="EN-US">40%</span>，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>而今天开盘后更加生猛，涨幅一度扩大到超过<span lang="EN-US">100%</span>，股价超过<span lang="EN-US">12</span>港元。</strong></span></p>
