<p class="MsoNormal">最近<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>及<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>公司更新了几版显卡驱动，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>也推出了<span lang="EN-US">30.0.100.9894 beta</span>版显卡驱动，没什么功能、性能更新，主要是修复<span lang="EN-US">bug</span>、提升稳定性，<span lang="EN-US">11</span>代酷睿的<span lang="EN-US">Xe</span>显卡多个黑屏问题被修补了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>显卡驱动<span lang="EN-US">30.0.100.9894 Beta</span>版发布，修复了部分<span lang="EN-US">Bug</span>，提升了稳定性，具体如下：</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">1</span>、解决了使用<span lang="EN-US">Intel Iris Plus</span>显卡（第<span lang="EN-US">10</span>代<span lang="EN-US">Intel Core</span>处理器）时，<span lang="EN-US">Adobe Premier Pro</span>程序崩溃的问题。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2</span>、<span lang="EN-US">Intel Iris Xe</span>显卡（第<span lang="EN-US">11</span>代<span lang="EN-US">Intel Core</span>处理器）：《赛博朋克<span lang="EN-US">2077</span>》、《杀手<span lang="EN-US">2</span>》、《德军总部：新血脉》等游戏出现画面错误。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">3</span>、<span lang="EN-US">Intel Iris Xe</span>显卡（第<span lang="EN-US">11</span>代<span lang="EN-US">Intel Core</span>处理器）：《狂怒<span lang="EN-US">2</span>》在<span lang="EN-US">Vulkam</span>模式下使用<span lang="EN-US"> ALT + TAB</span>切换任务时会出现黑屏。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">4</span>、<span lang="EN-US">Intel Iris Xe</span>显卡：《方舟：进化生存》游戏载入时和《星际战甲》在<span lang="EN-US">DX12</span>模式下均会发生崩溃或挂起。</p>
