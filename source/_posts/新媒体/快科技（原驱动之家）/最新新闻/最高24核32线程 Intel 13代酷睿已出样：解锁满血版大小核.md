<p class="MsoNormal">今天凌晨的<span lang="EN-US">CES</span>展会上，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>发布了<span lang="EN-US">28</span>款<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿处理器桌面版，还有三大系列的<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿移动版，<span lang="EN-US">Alder Lake</span>这一代的布局差不多了，今年下半年还有<span lang="EN-US">13</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Raptor Lake，要迎战AMD的5nm Zen4处理器。</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在发布会上，<strong><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>客户端计算业务主管<span lang="EN-US">Gregory Bryant</span>确认<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>内部已经有<span lang="EN-US">Raptor Lake</span>处理器原型，</strong>可以运行<span lang="EN-US">Windows</span>系统，这意味着处理器完成度已经很高，进入了后期的测试阶段了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Gregory Bryant</span>还提到<span lang="EN-US">Raptor Lake</span>会具备更高的性能，不过具体提升多少是不会公布的。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此前<span lang="EN-US">Raptor Lake</span>处理器已经有过多次爆料，基本上可以确定的<span lang="EN-US">13</span>代酷睿会继续使用<span lang="EN-US">Intel 7</span>工艺<span lang="EN-US">(10mm</span>加强版<span lang="EN-US">)</span>，延续并强化混合架构设计，<strong>其中<span lang="EN-US">P</span>核<span lang="EN-US">(</span>大核<span lang="EN-US">)</span>采用新的<span lang="EN-US">Raptor Cove</span>架构，最多还是<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个并支持超线程，<span lang="EN-US">E</span>核<span lang="EN-US">(</span>小核<span lang="EN-US">)</span>则是加强版的<span lang="EN-US">Gracemont</span>，最多翻番到<span lang="EN-US">16</span>个但依然不支持超线程。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">Raptor Lake</span>处理器桌面版最多可以做到<span lang="EN-US">24</span>核<span lang="EN-US">32</span>线程，</strong></span>移动版也有类似的规格，大小核数量上创新高。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">其他方面，<span lang="EN-US">Raptor Lake</span>会支持更高频率的<span lang="EN-US">DDR5 5600MHz</span>内存，继续保留<span lang="EN-US">DDR4</span>，移动版还支持<span lang="EN-US">LPDDR5X</span>内存，集成<span lang="EN-US">Xe</span>架构核显，最多依然是<span lang="EN-US">32</span>组<span lang="EN-US">EU</span>单元。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><img alt="最高24核32线程 Intel 13代酷睿已出样：解锁满血版大小核" h="266" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220105/c4387dbc-d6e9-488c-9155-16f23ca38fe9.png" style="border: black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>

<p class="MsoNormal">对玩家来说，<span lang="EN-US">13</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Raptor Lake</span>最值得期待的就是全新大小核的性能及优化，它会支持新的电压调控技术——<span lang="EN-US">DLVR</span>数字线性稳压器。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的说法，使用<span lang="EN-US">DLVR</span>技术之后，<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>电压可以降低<span lang="EN-US">160mV</span>，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>对应的就是降低了<span lang="EN-US">20-25%</span>的<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>功耗，而<span lang="EN-US">21%</span>的电压下降大约就等效于提升<span lang="EN-US">7%</span>的性能了。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">如果<span lang="EN-US">13</span>代酷睿如传闻所说，那么今年升级的时候就有望解锁满血版的大小核性能，也是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>说性能更高的底气之一。</p>
