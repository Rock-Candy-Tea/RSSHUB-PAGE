<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年的<span lang="EN-US">GTC</span>大会上又要来了，<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>已经明确今年的大会将会在<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日到<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日举行，这次依然会是线上虚拟会议，创始人、<span lang="EN-US">CEO</span>黄仁勋会在<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日发表主题演讲，这次可能还会在老黄家厨房，正菜是<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>工艺的<span lang="EN-US">Hopper</span>架构加速卡。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">从<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年的<span lang="EN-US">GTC</span>大会开始，<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>都因为疫情原因选择线上举行<span lang="EN-US">GTC</span>会议，<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年黄仁勋在“厨房”里发布了全新的“<span lang="EN-US">Ampere”</span>架构、<span lang="EN-US">A100</span>加速器、<span lang="EN-US">DGX A100</span>工作站等重磅产品，而<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年的<span lang="EN-US">GTC</span>大会上没有新架构产品，吸引力不够。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">考虑到现在<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>两年一次的升级周期，<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年的<span lang="EN-US">GTC</span>大会显然到了推出新架构的时候了，<strong>这次应该会公布下一代计算架构<span lang="EN-US">Hopper</span>，台积电<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>工艺，相比现在的<span lang="EN-US">A100</span>核心的<span lang="EN-US">7nm Ampere</span>架构升级规模更大。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="5nm Hopper显卡即将上菜 NVIDIA官宣GTC大会3月线上举行" h="487" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220215/3a759675-a11d-4729-8511-1a9ae7b197ec.jpg" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Hopper</span>的大核心应该是<span lang="EN-US">GH100</span>，有望成为<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>历史上第一款<span lang="EN-US">MCM</span>多芯封装<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>，内部集成两颗芯片，预计总共拥有<span lang="EN-US">288</span>个<span lang="EN-US">SM</span>流式多处理器，相比<span lang="EN-US">A100</span>增加多达<span lang="EN-US">2.6</span>倍，同时内部架构也会大改。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">最近有消息称，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">Hopper</span>核心晶体管多达<span lang="EN-US">1400</span>亿个，面积核心达到了<span lang="EN-US">900</span>平方毫米，是有史以来最大的<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>。</strong></span>作为参考，<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>自家旗舰<span lang="EN-US">Ampere</span>架构的<span lang="EN-US">A100</span>为<span lang="EN-US">542</span>亿个晶体管，核心面积<span lang="EN-US">826mm2</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">5nm Hopper</span>显卡很好很强大，对应的游戏卡是“<span lang="EN-US">Ada Lovelace”</span>架构，也会上<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>，但不一定全是台积电<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>工艺，不过<span lang="EN-US">RTX 40</span>系列显卡今年是否发布也存在变数了。</p>
