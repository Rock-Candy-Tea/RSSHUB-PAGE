<p class="MsoNormal">除了发布<span lang="EN-US">Software Adrenalin Edition 22.5.1 WHQL</span>版驱动之外，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>还同时发布了一款全新的预览版驱动全称为<span lang="EN-US">AMD Software Preview Driver May 2022</span>，这个驱动是近期很少见的鸡血驱动了，特别是新发布的RX 6X50 XT系列，售价4199元的RX 6750 XT显卡游戏性能最多提升多达到<span lang="EN-US">24%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong><span lang="EN-US">AMD Software Preview Driver May 2022</span>驱动首先是支持了<span lang="EN-US">RSR</span>超分辨率技术</strong>，新的【锐化效果】选项可以将<span lang="EN-US">RSR</span>微调至自己想要的设置。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">其次，这版驱动优化了<span lang="EN-US">RX 6000</span>系列显卡的<span lang="EN-US">DX11</span>性能，对比<span lang="EN-US">22.5.1</span>版驱动，<span lang="EN-US">Radeon RX 6950 XT</span>显卡的<span lang="EN-US">DX11</span>性能提升了<span lang="EN-US">8%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">还有就是针对《死亡搁浅》和《看门狗：军团》游戏支持了<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的<span lang="EN-US">SAM</span>技术，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>导致游戏性能大涨，其中<span lang="EN-US">RX 6950 XT</span>显卡分别提升了<span lang="EN-US">10%</span>和<span lang="EN-US">13%</span>，<span lang="EN-US">RX 6750 XT</span>显卡分别提升了<span lang="EN-US">12%</span>和<span lang="EN-US">24%</span>，<span lang="EN-US">RX 6650 XT</span>显卡分别提升了<span lang="EN-US">6%</span>和<span lang="EN-US">6%</span>。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">SAM</span>全称<span lang="EN-US"> AMD Smart Access Memory</span>，是<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>推出的一种基于<span lang="EN-US">PCI-E</span>协议的技术，<span lang="EN-US">SAM</span>的作用是让<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>能够更高效访问显卡的显存，以此来提升游戏时的性能表现，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>官方此前公布的数据显示性能提升可观，不同测试中<span lang="EN-US">10%</span>到<span lang="EN-US">16%</span>都是可能的。</p>
