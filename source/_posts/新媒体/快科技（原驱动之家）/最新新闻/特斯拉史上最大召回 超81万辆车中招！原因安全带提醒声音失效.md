<p style="color: rgb(34, 34, 34); font-family: ����, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; font-style: normal; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;">2月3日消息，据美国国家公路交通安全管理局(NHTSA)网站显示，<strong>由于车辆启动且驾驶员未系好安全带时，蜂鸣器可能无法启动，特斯拉将在美召回超过81.71万辆汽车。</strong></p>
<p style="color: rgb(34, 34, 34); font-family: ����, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; font-style: normal; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;">召回车辆包括2021-2022年间生产的Model S/X、2017-2022年间生产的Model 3以及2020-2022年间生产的Model Y车型。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220203/ed7c23ea467c4db3a2791de82be351ed.png" target="_blank"><img alt="特斯拉史上最大召回 超81万辆车中招！原因安全带提醒声音失效" h="429" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220203/s_ed7c23ea467c4db3a2791de82be351ed.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p style="color: rgb(34, 34, 34); font-family: ����, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; font-style: normal; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;">这也是特斯拉史上最大召回，目前暂不知晓特斯拉通过何种方式进行召回，<strong>如果OTA解决不了故障，则意味着车主可能要驾车前往特斯拉售后。</strong></p>
<p style="color: rgb(34, 34, 34); font-family: ����, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; font-style: normal; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;">对于车主和售后中心员工而言，如此大规模的召回，双方都具有不小的压力。</p>
<p style="color: rgb(34, 34, 34); font-family: ����, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; font-style: normal; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;">此外，这也不是特斯拉的唯一召回，同日，NHTSA公布召回报告，“滚动停车”（rolling stop）功能，特斯拉将召回合计将近5.4万辆车。</p>
<p style="color: rgb(34, 34, 34); font-family: ����, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; font-style: normal; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;">“滚动停车”功能允许汽车，在监测到附近没有任何车辆、行人和自行车骑车的情况下，<strong>以最高每小时5.6英里（9公里）的车速，驶过路标显示四个方向都要求停车的“all-way stop”十字路口。</strong></p>
<p style="color: rgb(34, 34, 34); font-family: ����, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; font-style: normal; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;">NHTSA认为，特斯拉的上述软件功能违反了美国大多数州的交通法规，一旦真允许那样开车，交警会给司机开罚单,这样的功能增加了撞车的风险。</p>
<p style="color: rgb(34, 34, 34); font-family: ����, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; font-style: normal; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;">对于这两起召回事件，特斯拉方面还没有发布相应的情况说明，后续保持关注。</p>
