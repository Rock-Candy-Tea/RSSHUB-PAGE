<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">基于<span lang="EN-US">3D XPoint</span>技术的<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>傲腾被公认为最强<span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>，读写速度不一定是最快的，但是延迟、寿命远超<span lang="EN-US">NAND</span>闪存，去年底<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>推出了傲腾<span lang="EN-US">DC P5800X</span>系列<span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>，最新消息显示<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>将推出升级版傲腾<span lang="EN-US">P5810X</span>系列，但没有<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>，还是<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 4.0</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">新一代傲腾<span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>的消息不是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>方面曝光的，而是新罕布什尔大学互操作性实验室泄露的，显示<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>将推出傲腾<span lang="EN-US">DC P5810X</span>及<span lang="EN-US">P5811X</span>两款新品，支持<span lang="EN-US">NVMe 1.4</span>及<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 4.0</span>，前者是<span lang="EN-US">2.5</span>寸<span lang="EN-US">U.2</span>接口，后者是<span lang="EN-US">E1.S</span>标准，其他规格未知。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此前的<span lang="EN-US">P5800X</span>就已经上了<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 4.0</span>接口，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>按理说<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>已经在<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿以及今年上半年才发布的数据中心平台<span lang="EN-US">Sapphire Rapids</span>上都会支持<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>，自家最强<span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>竟然还没跟进<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>，确实让人有些意外。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不过这也可能跟<span lang="EN-US">P5810X/P5811X</span>的升级较小有关，看命名提升不大，应该是<span lang="EN-US">P5800X</span>的小改款而非新一代产品升级，<strong><span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>应该会留给更新一代的傲腾<span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>。</strong></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="最强SSD即将升级 Intel将推出傲腾P5810X：可惜没有PCIe 5.0" h="306" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220121/09cf70ab-66bc-4ef9-b3f1-f4c434541c12.png" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">回顾下<span lang="EN-US">P5800X</span>的性能，傲腾<span lang="EN-US">SSD P5800X</span>采用<span lang="EN-US">2.5</span>英寸<span lang="EN-US">U.2</span>形态，容量可选<span lang="EN-US">400GB</span>、<span lang="EN-US">800GB</span>、<span lang="EN-US">1.6TB</span>、<span lang="EN-US">3.2TB</span>，支持<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 4.0</span>，持续读写性能最高<span lang="EN-US">7.2GB/s</span>、<span lang="EN-US">6.2GB/s(</span>是上代的<span lang="EN-US">3</span>倍<span lang="EN-US">)</span>，<span lang="EN-US">4K</span>随机读写性能最高都是<span lang="EN-US">150</span>万<span lang="EN-US">IOPS</span>，<span lang="EN-US">4K 70/30</span>混合随机性能最高<span lang="EN-US">180</span>万<span lang="EN-US">IOPS(</span>是上代的<span lang="EN-US">3</span>倍<span lang="EN-US">)</span>，<span lang="EN-US">512bit</span>随机读取性能高达<span lang="EN-US">460</span>万<span lang="EN-US">IOPS</span>，<span lang="EN-US">4K</span>随机读取延迟低于<span lang="EN-US">6</span>微秒、随机读写延迟低于<span lang="EN-US">25</span>微秒<span lang="EN-US">(</span>比上代提升<span lang="EN-US">40</span>％<span lang="EN-US">)</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">除了读写性能指标让<span lang="EN-US">NAND</span>闪存固态盘望尘莫及，<strong>傲腾<span lang="EN-US">SSD P5800X</span>的寿命也异常惊人，达到了不可思议的每天<span lang="EN-US">100</span>次全盘写入，比上代的<span lang="EN-US">60</span>次增加了<span lang="EN-US">67</span>％。</strong></p>
