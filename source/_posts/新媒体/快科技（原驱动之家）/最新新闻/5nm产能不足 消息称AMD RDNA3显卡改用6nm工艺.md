<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>两大重磅产品除了<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>之外，还有下一代的<span lang="EN-US">RDNA3</span>架构显卡，<a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/755/755339.htm" target="_blank">有望用上<span lang="EN-US">Chiplets</span>小芯片封装</a>，规模比现在的<span lang="EN-US">RDNA2</span>翻倍，同时能效提升<span lang="EN-US">50%</span>，在性能上可以正面刚<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>下代显卡。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">按照之前的规划，<span lang="EN-US">RDNA3</span>应该是用上<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>工艺的，但是这事有点悬了，最新消息称台积电的<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>产能吃紧，明年可能没有这么多的量给<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>，而<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>显然会优先用于<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>处理器，<strong><span lang="EN-US">RDNA3</span>有可能改用<span lang="EN-US">6nm</span>工艺</strong>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">虽然看上去跟<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>工艺只差<span lang="EN-US">1</span>点，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>但台积电的<span lang="EN-US">6nm</span>工艺实际上是<span lang="EN-US">7nm</span>工艺的改进版，设计上是兼容的，密度提升了<span lang="EN-US">18%</span>，功耗降低<span lang="EN-US">8%</span>左右，性能几乎没什么提升。</strong></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center"><img alt="5nm产能不足 消息称AMD RDNA3显卡改用6nm工艺" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210615/8f6c518a-67cf-499c-9435-0dfc7868eafc.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">由此来看，如果使用<span lang="EN-US">6nm</span>工艺，那么<span lang="EN-US">RDNA3</span>架构的<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>芯片在性能及能效上的提升可能没有预期的那么大。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不过有一点，<span lang="EN-US">RDNA3</span>几乎可以确定会用小芯片设计，提升性能及能效不只是看工艺了，用<span lang="EN-US">6nm</span>工艺的影响可能会有所减少。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/761/761239.htm" target="_blank">考虑到近期有多款<span lang="EN-US">6nm</span>工艺的<span lang="EN-US">AMD Zen3+</span>处理器曝光</a>，看样子<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>确实是有计划使用<span lang="EN-US">6nm</span>工艺。</p>
