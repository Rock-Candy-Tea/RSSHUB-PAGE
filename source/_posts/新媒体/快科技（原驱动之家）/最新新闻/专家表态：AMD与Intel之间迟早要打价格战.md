<p class="MsoNormal">从去年底的<span lang="EN-US">Zen3</span>发布到现在的<span lang="EN-US">11</span>代酷睿上市，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>与<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>两家之间的竞争愈发激烈，<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>市场上的升级换代比之前更快，可惜的是<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>价格并没有跌下来。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>不仅没有跌价，两家厂商的主力产品甚至面临着缺货、涨价的问题，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">5000</span>系列一定涨幅达到<span lang="EN-US">30-50%</span>，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>这边的酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i9/i7</span>高端系列价格也很夸张。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">这主要是跟当前的芯片市场产能紧张有关，很多原材料也在涨价了，对<span lang="EN-US">DIY</span>玩家来说这并不是好事。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">但是从长远来看，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>与<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>之间的竞争还会更激烈，花旗银行分析师<span lang="EN-US">Christopher Danely</span>日前表示，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>两家厂商之间的价格战迟早要发生，只是目前还不知道何时会升温——对DIY玩家来说，最期待的这一天终于有望了。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">对于降价的问题，<strong><span lang="EN-US">Intel CEO</span>基辛格前几天在摩根大会上也有过类似的表态，暗示要降价</strong>——基辛格认为在服务器<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>市场上，采用竞争性定价也是合适的，虽然会影响公司业绩，但可以保住甚至增加市场份额。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据市场调研公司公布的<span lang="EN-US">Q1</span>季度报告，公布了<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>、<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>在不同领域的<span lang="EN-US">x86</span>处理器份额，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的整体份额从<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">Q1</span>季度的<span lang="EN-US">14.8%</span>提升到了<span lang="EN-US">20.7%</span>，拿到了五分之一的天下。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">具体来看，桌面份额从去年的<span lang="EN-US">19.2%</span>略微提升到<span lang="EN-US">19.3%</span>，笔记本处理器份额反而从<span lang="EN-US">19%</span>降低到了<span lang="EN-US">18%</span>，被<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>夺回去<span lang="EN-US">1</span>个百分点，这可能跟<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>缺货有关。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不过<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>收获最大的是服务器<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>市场，从<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">Q1</span>的<span lang="EN-US">5.1%</span>增长到<span lang="EN-US">8.9%</span>，要知道去年<span lang="EN-US">Q4</span>季度<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的服务器<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>份额也不过<span lang="EN-US">7.1%</span>，<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年底的时候才<span lang="EN-US">4.5%</span>，<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年底的时候只有<span lang="EN-US">0.8%</span>。</p>
