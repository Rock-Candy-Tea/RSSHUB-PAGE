<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">HPV</span>疫苗能预防<span lang="EN-US">HPV</span>病毒进而基本防止宫颈癌，这是全球唯一一个能明确预防癌症的疫苗。据报道，广东方面已经确认明年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月份开始针对<span lang="EN-US">14</span>周岁以下女生推广免费接种。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">广东省卫生健康委副主任、省中医药局局长徐庆锋介绍，广东启动全省适龄女生免费<span lang="EN-US">HPV</span>疫苗接种工作。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>该项目实施对象为具有广东省学籍、<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月起新进入初中一年级且未接种过<span lang="EN-US">HPV</span>疫苗的<span lang="EN-US">14</span>周岁以下女生，按照知情、自愿、免费的原则，进行<span lang="EN-US">HPV</span>疫苗免费接种。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">经综合评估，项目采用国产双价<span lang="EN-US">HPV</span>疫苗进行接种，<strong>接种第一针<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月后接种第二针，共接种两针，</strong>具体接种时间和地点由教育部门与卫健部门商定。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">目前全球已上市的<span lang="EN-US">HPV</span>疫苗有<span lang="EN-US">3</span>种，分别是英国葛兰素史克公司生产的针对<span lang="EN-US">HPV16</span>、<span lang="EN-US">18</span>型的双价疫苗，美国默沙东公司生产的针对<span lang="EN-US">HPV6</span>、<span lang="EN-US">11</span>、<span lang="EN-US">16</span>、<span lang="EN-US">18</span>型的四价疫苗和针对<span lang="EN-US">HPV6</span>、<span lang="EN-US">11</span>、<span lang="EN-US">16</span>、<span lang="EN-US">18</span>、<span lang="EN-US">31</span>、<span lang="EN-US">33</span>、<span lang="EN-US">45</span>、<span lang="EN-US">52</span>、<span lang="EN-US">58</span>型的九价疫苗。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">国内也有<span lang="EN-US">HPV</span>疫苗获批，<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，<span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">国家药监局</span>宣布批准厦门万泰沧海生物技术有限公司的双价人乳头瘤病毒疫苗（大肠杆菌）（商品名：馨可宁（<span lang="EN-US">Cecolin</span>））上市注册申请，该药是首家获批的国产人乳头瘤病毒疫苗，适用于<span lang="EN-US">9-45</span>岁女性。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此前据据厦门万泰消息，<strong>馨可宁的定价为<span lang="EN-US">329</span>元<span lang="EN-US">/</span>支，</strong><span lang="EN-US">9-14</span>岁 女性只需要接种<span lang="EN-US">2</span>针，<span lang="EN-US">15-45</span>岁女性接种<span lang="EN-US">3</span>针。</p>
