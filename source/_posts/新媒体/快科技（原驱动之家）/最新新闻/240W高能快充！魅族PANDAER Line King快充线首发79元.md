<p>魅族最近开启魅族PANDAER秋季新品上新周活动，推出手机壳、充电器、机械键盘等超30款新品。<strong>其中一款新品——PANDAER Line King快充线已正式开售，首发价79元起。</strong></p>

<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220922/15caee49065e422682348118a1f80558.png" target="_blank"><img alt="240W高能快充！魅族PANDAER Line King快充线首发79元" h="569" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220922/s_15caee49065e422682348118a1f80558.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="522" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>据介绍，这款数据线采用亮橙色编织线身设计，线长1.2米，外观十分亮眼。另有高密度编织工艺，长期使用也不会缠绕，不易起毛。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220922/32611931d0c8440cb176f2fe6e080e7f.png" target="_blank"><img alt="240W高能快充！魅族PANDAER Line King快充线首发79元" h="370" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220922/s_32611931d0c8440cb176f2fe6e080e7f.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="437" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>插头采用一体式合金设计，表面有专属镀金，高质量线材，配合全新抗折霓彩拖尾，大幅提升可靠性抗摇摆次数高达5000次。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220922/e5470936b48b429c921e7bcb6a7d0e0c.png" target="_blank"><img alt="240W高能快充！魅族PANDAER Line King快充线首发79元" h="575" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220922/s_e5470936b48b429c921e7bcb6a7d0e0c.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="521" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>同时，该产品<strong>支持240W电动功率，PD 3.1疾速充能</strong>，USB-C转闪电高能快充线支持MFi 认证，iPhone 8-14全系列快充兼容，30分钟即可将iPhone 14充至60%。</p>
<p>价格上，<strong>Type-C to Lightning版本售价<span style="color:#ff0000;">99</span>元，Type-C to Type-C版本为<span style="color:#ff0000;">79</span>元。</strong></p>
