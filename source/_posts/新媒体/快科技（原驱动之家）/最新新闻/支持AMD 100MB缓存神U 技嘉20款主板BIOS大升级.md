<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD 3</span>月份发布了<span lang="EN-US">7</span>款锐龙<span lang="EN-US">5000</span>系列处理器，<strong>其中锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7 5800X3D</span>是一款为游戏而生的神<span lang="EN-US">U</span></strong>，支持<span lang="EN-US">3D V-Cache</span>技术，带来了史无前例的<span lang="EN-US">100MB</span>缓存，国内售价<span lang="EN-US">3099</span>元，目前跌到<span lang="EN-US">3000</span>内了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不过锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7 5800X3D</span>增加缓存也不是没代价的，频率降低是已经知道的，基准频率从<span lang="EN-US">3.8GHz</span>降低至<span lang="EN-US">3.4GHz</span>，最高加速频率从<span lang="EN-US">4.7GHz</span>降低至<span lang="EN-US">4.5GHz</span>，从而维持热设计功耗维持在<span lang="EN-US">105W</span>。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><img alt="支持AMD 100MB缓存神U 技嘉20款主板BIOS大升级" h="427" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220517/95b41476-487d-4080-9624-c9cc6c2a6a58.png" style="border: black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在新一波锐龙<span lang="EN-US">5000</span>处理器发布之后，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>也联合厂商推出了新版<span lang="EN-US">BIOS</span>，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">5</span>年前的<span lang="EN-US">300</span>系主板也能升级支持锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7 5800X3D</span>了，此前微星的<span lang="EN-US">300</span>系主板已经全面升级，现在技嘉<span lang="EN-US">20</span>款<span lang="EN-US">300</span>系主板也升级了。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">技嘉主板的<span lang="EN-US">BIOS</span>升级内容如下：</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">1</span>、升级<span lang="EN-US">AGESA ComboAm4v2PI 1.2.0.7</span>，支持<span lang="EN-US">Vermeer</span>和<span lang="EN-US">Cezanne</span>核心的<span lang="EN-US">AMD 5000</span>系列处理器。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2</span>、支持<span lang="EN-US">Ryzen 7 5800X3D</span>处理器。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">以下是技嘉<span lang="EN-US">300</span>系主板的详细升级名单及<span lang="EN-US">BIOS</span>版本号，大家可以对照下自己主板的情况升级。</p>
