<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">今晚的新品发布会上，华硕也推出了多款灵耀系列轻薄本，其中灵耀<span lang="EN-US">X13</span>是一款<span lang="EN-US">13</span>寸轻薄本，仅重<span lang="EN-US">1</span>千克，配备了<span lang="EN-US">2.8K OLED</span>屏和<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7 6800U</span>处理器，<span lang="EN-US">8</span>核<span lang="EN-US">16</span>线程的配置依然能跑出<span lang="EN-US">19</span>小时续航。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">灵耀<span lang="EN-US">X13</span>采用金属机身，<strong>有夜蓝及瓷青两种配色，主打轻薄便携，重量只有<span lang="EN-US">1</span>千克，厚度<span lang="EN-US">14.9mm</span>，支持<span lang="EN-US">180</span>度开合。</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220523/f13b8cf1-de75-4d2a-9115-4b41b92531a5.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="仅重1千克 华硕发布灵耀X13轻薄本：锐龙8核CPU跑出19小时续航" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220523/Sf13b8cf1-de75-4d2a-9115-4b41b92531a5.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>

<p class="MsoNormal">硬件方面，灵耀<span lang="EN-US">X13</span>配备了锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7 6800U</span>处理器，<span lang="EN-US">8</span>核<span lang="EN-US">16</span>线程，加速频率<span lang="EN-US">4.7GHz</span>，<span lang="EN-US">TDP 15-28W</span>，整合<span lang="EN-US">Radeon 680M</span>核显，还有<span lang="EN-US">16GB</span>内存及<span lang="EN-US">512GB</span>存储。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">得益于<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>锐龙<span lang="EN-US">6000</span>系列的架构及功耗优化，就算搭配<span lang="EN-US">67WH</span>电池，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>灵耀<span lang="EN-US">X13</span>这样的轻薄本也能跑出<span lang="EN-US">19</span>小时续航，日常工作续航也有<span lang="EN-US">15</span>小时，还支持<span lang="EN-US">USBC</span>快充。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">屏幕也是灵耀<span lang="EN-US">X13</span>的一大特色，它配备了<span lang="EN-US">2.8K OLED</span>屏，<span lang="EN-US">16</span>：<span lang="EN-US">10</span>比例，支持触控，屏占比高达<span lang="EN-US">89%</span>，亮度可达<span lang="EN-US">600</span>尼特，支持<span lang="EN-US">100% DCI-P3</span>色域。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此外，灵耀<span lang="EN-US">X13</span>还配备了<span lang="EN-US">5.9</span>英寸的超大触控板，支持双天线<span lang="EN-US">Wi-Fi 6E</span>，支持<span lang="EN-US">DP</span>输出，<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个<span lang="EN-US">USBC</span>接口。</p>
