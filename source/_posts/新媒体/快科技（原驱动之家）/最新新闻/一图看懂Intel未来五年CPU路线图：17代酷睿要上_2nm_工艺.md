<p class="MsoNormal">上月底<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>发布了<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Alder Lake</span>，升级了大小核<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>架构，工艺也升级到了<span lang="EN-US">Intel 7</span>（之前的<span lang="EN-US">10nm</span>工艺），从一代开始<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>工艺也稳定下来了，未来四年中要掌握五代<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>工艺。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">伴随着新工艺出现的还有新一代酷睿，目前官方可确认的酷睿排到了<span lang="EN-US">14</span>代——<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年推出的<span lang="EN-US">Intel 4</span>工艺的<span lang="EN-US">Meteor Lake</span>流星湖，这是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>首款使用<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>光刻工艺的处理器，还会使用多芯片封装技术，亮点很多。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">再往后呢？爆料挺多，但也很散乱，好在<span lang="EN-US">3DCenter</span>网站汇总了一份表格，将<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>到<span lang="EN-US">2025/2026</span>年的<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>都标出来了，简单来看下。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">11</span>代、<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿都发布了，没什么可说的，接下来就是明年的<span lang="EN-US">13</span>代酷睿，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>代号<span lang="EN-US">Raptor Lake</span>，产品系列是酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i-13000</span>系列，大核升级为<span lang="EN-US">Raptor Cove</span>，最多<span lang="EN-US">8</span>核<span lang="EN-US">16</span>线程，小核还是<span lang="EN-US">gracemont</span>，但从<span lang="EN-US">8</span>核增加到<span lang="EN-US">16</span>核，总计是<span lang="EN-US">16</span>核<span lang="EN-US">32</span>线程，核显升级为<span lang="EN-US">Gen 12.2</span>，架构依然是<span lang="EN-US">Xe</span>。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">其他如<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>、<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>、<span lang="EN-US">LGA1700</span>插槽都没变，明年<span lang="EN-US">Q3</span>季度发布。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="一图看懂Intel未来五年CPU路线图：17代酷睿要上“2nm”工艺" h="345" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211116/c359e1ab-e3a9-4f63-8dfb-63adebb1cdce.jpg" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">再往后就是<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年的<span lang="EN-US">14</span>代酷睿，代号<span lang="EN-US">Raptor Lake</span>，核心数还没明确说法，制程工艺升级<span lang="EN-US">Intel 4</span>，兼容<span lang="EN-US">LGA1700</span>，<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年上半年发布。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">15</span>代酷睿代号<span lang="EN-US">Arrow Lake</span>，<strong>这代最大的变化是它可能会使用台积电的<span lang="EN-US">3nm</span>工艺代工，首次不是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>工艺生产，预计<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>到<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年问世。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">16</span>代酷睿代号<span lang="EN-US">Lunar Lake</span>，规格也不太确定，但工艺又回归<span lang="EN-US">Intel 3</span>，核显升级<span lang="EN-US">Gen13</span>，预计<span lang="EN-US">2023/2024</span>年问世。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">17</span>代酷睿代号<span lang="EN-US">Nova Lake</span>，这次变化又大了，<strong>工艺会使用<span lang="EN-US">Intel 20A</span>，这是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>在<span lang="EN-US">FinFET</span>工艺之后的第一次重大变革，首次进入埃米工艺时代，<span lang="EN-US">20A</span>差不多是<span lang="EN-US">2nm</span>工艺的水平，</strong>不过实际参数还是迷，要等很久才能公布。</p>
