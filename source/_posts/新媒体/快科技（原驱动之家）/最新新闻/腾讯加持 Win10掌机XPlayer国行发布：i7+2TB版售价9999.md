<p class="MsoNormal"><a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/756/756152.htm" target="_blank">前几天在海外火爆的<span lang="EN-US">Win10</span>掌机<span lang="EN-US">ONEX PLAYER</span>今天也在国内发布了</a>，配备了<span lang="EN-US">11</span>代酷睿处理器、<span lang="EN-US">2K</span>触控屏，售价<span lang="EN-US">5999</span>元起，顶配<span lang="EN-US">i7+16GB+2TB</span>版售价<span lang="EN-US">9999</span>元。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">具体来说，酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i5-1135G7+16GB+512GB</span>版售价<span lang="EN-US">5999</span>元，优惠价<span lang="EN-US">5699</span>元，酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i5-1135G7+16GB+1TB</span>版售价<span lang="EN-US">6399</span>元，优惠价<span lang="EN-US">6099</span>元。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i7-1165G7+16GB+1TB</span>版售价<span lang="EN-US">6699</span>元，优惠价<span lang="EN-US">6399</span>元，酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i7-1185G7+16GB+2TB</span>版售价<span lang="EN-US">10999</span>元，优惠价<span lang="EN-US">9999</span>元。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">ONEX PLAYER</span>掌机是深圳壹号本（<span lang="EN-US">One-Netbook</span>）设计的，该公司得到了腾讯的投资，研发了这款<span lang="EN-US">x86</span>处理器<span lang="EN-US">+Win10</span>系统的游戏掌机，本质上也是个超便携<span lang="EN-US">PC</span>，但外观是掌机风格，红黑配色，左右双摇杆、方向键、控制键一应俱全。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">硬件方面，<span lang="EN-US">ONEX PLAYE</span>掌机使用的是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的<span lang="EN-US">11</span>代酷睿处理器，<span lang="EN-US">10nm Tiger Lake</span>架构的，<strong>有酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i7-1185G7/1165G7</span>、酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i5-1135G7</span>等型号可选，最多<span lang="EN-US">4</span>核<span lang="EN-US">8</span>线程，加速频率最高<span lang="EN-US">4.8GHz</span>。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">显卡则是靠<span lang="EN-US">11</span>代酷睿的核显<span lang="EN-US">Iris Xe</span>，最多<span lang="EN-US">96</span>组<span lang="EN-US">EU</span>单元，性能水平可以看后面的官方<span lang="EN-US">PPT</span>，放在掌机中可以吊打其他产品，绝大多数<span lang="EN-US">3A</span>游戏能跑到<span lang="EN-US">60fps</span>以上。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">存储方面，<span lang="EN-US">ONEX PLAYE</span>掌机配备的是<span lang="EN-US">16GB LPDDR4-4266</span>、<span lang="EN-US">512GB/1TB M.2</span>固态硬盘，最高<span lang="EN-US">2TB</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">屏幕方面，<span lang="EN-US">ONEX PLAYE</span>掌机使用的是<span lang="EN-US">8.4</span>寸<span lang="EN-US">IPS</span>显示屏，分辨率高达<span lang="EN-US">2560x1600</span>，<span lang="EN-US">358PPI</span>，支持<span lang="EN-US">10</span>点触控，<span lang="EN-US">100% sRGB</span>色域。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">其他方面还有<span lang="EN-US">15300mAh</span>电池（<span lang="EN-US">59WH</span>）、<span lang="EN-US">65W</span>氮化镓快充、<span lang="EN-US">USB 4.0</span>接口、指纹识别、蓝牙<span lang="EN-US">5.0/Wi-Fi 6</span>等等，硬件规格非常强大。</p>
