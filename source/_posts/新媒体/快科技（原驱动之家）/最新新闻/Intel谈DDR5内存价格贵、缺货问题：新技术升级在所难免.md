<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">11</span>月底<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>发布了<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿处理器，除了升级<span lang="EN-US">Inte 7</span>工艺及大小核架构<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>之外，还首发了<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>及<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存支持，只不过<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存现在价格太贵，还缺货，被炒到了数千块，<span lang="EN-US">PC</span>玩家想升级也没招。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>是如何看待目前<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存的乱象呢？日前负责<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿开发的工程与验证总经理<span lang="EN-US">Guy Chalev</span>接受了采访，他回应了<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>价格高而且缺货的问题。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Guy Chalev</span>表示，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>这种情况在新技术升级中是在所难免的，所以<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>给客户提供了选择，<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿可以同时兼容<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>及<span lang="EN-US">DDR4</span>内存，<span lang="EN-US">OEM</span>厂商可以选择制造<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>或是<span lang="EN-US">DDR4</span>内存的主板。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">从<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的表态来看，他们认为<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>升级换代初期是免不了出现供应问题的，他们能作的是提供两种选择，这点上<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>已经尽力了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不过主板厂商出于自己的考虑，现在推出的<span lang="EN-US">600</span>系主板很乐意支持<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>，仅支持<span lang="EN-US">DDR4</span>的<span lang="EN-US">Z690</span>主板并不占多数，而且接下来要发布的平价主板<span lang="EN-US">B660</span>、<span lang="EN-US">H610</span>中支持<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>的也不少，这就让人有点疑惑了。</p>
