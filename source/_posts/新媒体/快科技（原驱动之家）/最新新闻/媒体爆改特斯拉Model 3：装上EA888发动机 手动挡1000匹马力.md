<p style="color: rgb(34, 34, 34); font-family: ����, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; font-style: normal; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;">过年期间，易车也没有闲下来，为所欲为的“马编”给我们带来了个大活，爆改特斯拉Model 3，给它装一个发动机。</p>
<p style="color: rgb(34, 34, 34); font-family: ����, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; font-style: normal; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;">据马编介绍，改装目前还没有进行，只是做出了初步规划，并且把发动机套在了车壳里，满足了一下视觉观感。</p>
<p style="color: rgb(34, 34, 34); font-family: ����, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; font-style: normal; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;">改装方案，是把Model 3的前备箱拆除，安上一个发动机，发动机选择的是明锐RS的EA888发动机，为什么没有选择V8，原因也是无奈，前备箱空间太小了。</p>
<p class="newsinfo_vedio"><a class="mydrivers_player" href="https://weibo.com/1750474095/LdJGg9ZEG">视频</a></p>
<p style="color: rgb(34, 34, 34); font-family: ����, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; font-style: normal; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;">这个EA888发动机，原厂马力不大，<strong>不过他们刷完程序后，可以达到700匹马力，同时Model 3自己的后驱电机保留，四驱情况下，车辆的最大马力可达1000匹。</strong></p>
<p style="color: rgb(34, 34, 34); font-family: ����, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; font-style: normal; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;"> </p>
<p align="center" style="color: rgb(34, 34, 34); font-family: ����, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; font-style: normal; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220203/3eb962c968084dd2b16ffce6f5782434.png" target="_blank"><img alt="媒体爆改特斯拉Model 3：装上EA888发动机 手动挡1000匹马力" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220203/s_3eb962c968084dd2b16ffce6f5782434.png" style="cursor: default; border: 1px solid black;" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p style="color: rgb(34, 34, 34); font-family: ����, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; font-style: normal; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;">而且车辆还有纯电、纯油、油电混动、用发动机发电等多种运行工况，可以说，全球第一台这样的特斯拉。</p>
<p style="color: rgb(34, 34, 34); font-family: ����, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; font-style: normal; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;">更刺激的是，车辆还是手动挡，<strong>预计后期只会为发动机匹配手动挡前驱，毕竟后驱的电机，再用手动挡控制，难度系数太高。</strong></p>
<p style="color: rgb(34, 34, 34); font-family: ����, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; font-style: normal; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;">目前，该改装方案已经立项，不过项目拨款还未到位，据改装总工程师“老陆”介绍，他已经借了十几个点的网贷，来推进改装进行。</p>
<p style="color: rgb(34, 34, 34); font-family: ����, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; font-style: normal; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;">保守估计，改装需要2、3个月的时间，而在五一劳动节前，能不能看到这台千匹马力的手动挡Model 3，就看制作团队给不给力了。</p>
