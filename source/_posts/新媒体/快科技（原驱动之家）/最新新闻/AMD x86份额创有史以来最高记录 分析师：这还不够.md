<p class="MsoNormal">数十年来，在<span lang="EN-US">x86</span>处理器的竞争中，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>一直都是老二，但是他们跟<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>之间的份额差距才更重要，前不久统计机构<span lang="EN-US">Mercury Research</span>公布了<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年四季度<span lang="EN-US">x86</span>处理器市场份额情况，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>以<span lang="EN-US">25.6%</span>的份额创造了有史以来的最高记录。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>上一轮的高峰还是<span lang="EN-US">2006</span>年，得益于速龙、皓龙在跟<span lang="EN-US">P4</span>、至强的竞争中处于有利位置，那时候<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>占整个<span lang="EN-US">x86</span>市场的份额超过<span lang="EN-US">25%</span>，</strong>不过之后随着<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>推出扣肉架构酷睿处理器，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的<span lang="EN-US">K10</span>及推土机两代<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>接连失利，份额就一路下滑了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">最近几年<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>靠着锐龙、霄龙处理器才逐渐翻身，并在<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">Q4</span>季度以<span lang="EN-US">25.6%</span>的份额收复了<span lang="EN-US">16</span>年前的失地。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不过四分之一天下的目标显然还不是终章，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">WestPark Capital </span>的分析师日前表示<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的份额还能涨，其中服务器<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>市场有望拿下<span lang="EN-US">25%</span>份额，现在只有<span lang="EN-US">15%</span>份额，历史最高也只有<span lang="EN-US">20%</span>左右。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">PC</span>电脑领域，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>现在的份额在<span lang="EN-US">18-20%</span>左右，未来有望达到<span lang="EN-US">30%</span>左右，要创造下一个记录了。</p>
