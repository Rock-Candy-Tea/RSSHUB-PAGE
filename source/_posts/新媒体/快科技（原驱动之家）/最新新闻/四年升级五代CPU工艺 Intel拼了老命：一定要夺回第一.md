<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">本月底即将发布的<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿处理器不仅会首发桌面版大小核架构，还会升级<span lang="EN-US">Intel 7</span>工艺，这个就是之前的<span lang="EN-US">Intel 10nm SF</span>增强版工艺，虽然换了名字，但是这对<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>来说是个起点，<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>工艺接下来要爆发。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Alder Lake</span>之后，<strong><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>下一个<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>节点是<span lang="EN-US">Intel 4</span>，也就是之前的<span lang="EN-US">7nm EUV</span>工艺，首款产品是<span lang="EN-US">Meteor Lake</span>，目前已经<span lang="EN-US">Tape In</span>，<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年正式问世。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel 4</span>之后还有<span lang="EN-US">Intel 3</span>，进一步优化<span lang="EN-US">FinFET</span>、提升<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>，能效比继续提升大约<span lang="EN-US">18</span>％，还有面积优化，<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年下半年投产，不过<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>没公布具体的<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>产品。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="四年升级五代CPU工艺 Intel拼了老命：一定要夺回第一" h="458" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211022/f5f1050a-5ab5-4bc8-bdcd-55861aa13080.png" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年则是最重要的一次升级，<span lang="EN-US">Intel 20A</span>工艺来了，放弃<span lang="EN-US">FinFET</span>晶体管，拥有两项革命性技术，<span lang="EN-US">RibbonFET</span>就是类似三星的<span lang="EN-US">GAA</span>环绕栅极晶体管，<span lang="EN-US">PoerVia</span>则首创取消晶圆前侧的供电走线，改用后置供电，也可以优化信号传输。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">接下来是<span lang="EN-US">Intel 18A</span>，预计<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年初投产，继续强化<span lang="EN-US">RibbonFET</span>，还有下一代高<span lang="EN-US">NA EUV</span>光刻，与<span lang="EN-US">ASML</span>合作。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>这就意味着<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>会在<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>到<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年的<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年时间里，不停地发布<span lang="EN-US">5</span>代<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>工艺，而且会有多次重大技术升级，不仅首发埃米级<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>工艺，还会首发<span lang="EN-US">ASML</span>的下一代<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>光刻机。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在今天的财报会议上，<span lang="EN-US">Intel CEO</span>也强调了这几年中升级<span lang="EN-US">5</span>代<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>工艺的重要性，还说<span lang="EN-US">18A</span>工艺已经吸引几家感兴趣的客户，当然<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>是不会公布具体名字的。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">如果进度没有延期，那么未来的<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年里可以说是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>史上工艺升级最快也是最重大的节点，<span lang="EN-US">14nm</span>挤牙膏那样的情况不会有了，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的目标就是<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年追赶对手，<strong><span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年的<span lang="EN-US">18A</span>工艺节点则会全面领先，夺回第一的位置。</strong></p>
