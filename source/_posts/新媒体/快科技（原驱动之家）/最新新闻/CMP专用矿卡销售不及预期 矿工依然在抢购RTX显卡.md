<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>今天凌晨发布了<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>财年的<span lang="EN-US">Q2</span>季度财报，<span lang="EN-US" style="background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial; font-family: "Open Sans", sans-serif; color: rgb(51, 51, 51);">65.07</span><span style="background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial; color: rgb(51, 51, 51);">亿美元</span>的营收同比大涨<span lang="EN-US" style="background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial; font-family: "Open Sans", sans-serif; color: rgb(51, 51, 51);">65%</span><span style="background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial; color: rgb(51, 51, 51);">，</span><span lang="EN-US">24</span>亿美元的净利润同比增<span lang="EN-US">282%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>具体来说，第二季度游戏业务营收为<span lang="EN-US">30.6</span>亿美元，同比大增<span lang="EN-US">85%</span>；</strong>第二季度数据中心业务营收为<span lang="EN-US">23.7</span>亿美元，同比增<span lang="EN-US">35%</span>；第二季度专业可视化业务营收为<span lang="EN-US">5.19</span>亿美元，同比大增<span lang="EN-US">156%</span>；汽车业务营收为<span lang="EN-US">1.52</span>亿美元，同比增<span lang="EN-US">37%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">毫无疑问，<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>上季度的优秀业绩与显卡大卖有关，特别是挖矿用的显卡，尽管<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>公开否认加密货币对显卡销售的重要影响。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="CMP专用矿卡销售不及预期 矿工依然在抢购RTX显卡" h="410" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210819/725af349-6c8c-4906-943b-38145935e32e.png" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="569" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不过上个季度中，<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>自家的<span lang="EN-US">CMP</span>专用矿卡确实没有达到预期，<strong>该公司预计会卖出<span lang="EN-US">4</span>亿美元的<span lang="EN-US">CMP</span>矿卡，实际上只销售了<span lang="EN-US">2.6</span>亿美元。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">至于为什么卖不动，除了币价大跌、国内封杀等意外因素之外，主要还是矿工对<span lang="EN-US">CMP</span>矿卡不感冒，因为现在的<span lang="EN-US">CMP</span>矿卡架构还是上一代的，挖矿能力上不如<span lang="EN-US">RTX 30</span>系列。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">更重要的是矿工们不傻，<span lang="EN-US">CMP</span>矿卡砍了视频输出、保修也只有<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个月，售价也不便宜，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>矿工还是青睐<span lang="EN-US">RTX 30</span>系列显卡，毕竟是完整功能的，而且随时可以出二手回血，<span lang="EN-US">CMP</span>系列矿卡就得砸手里。</strong></span></p>
