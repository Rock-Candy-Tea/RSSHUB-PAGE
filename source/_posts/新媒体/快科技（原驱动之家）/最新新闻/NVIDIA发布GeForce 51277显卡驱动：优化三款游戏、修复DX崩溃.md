<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>日前发布了<span lang="EN-US">GeForce Game Ready 512.77 WHQL</span>版显卡驱动，这是一款游戏优化驱动，主要为《<span lang="EN-US">Evil Dead: The Game</span>》、《杜蒙》和《吸血鬼：避世血族<span lang="EN-US">-</span>血猎》三款游戏提供优化支持。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">GeForce Game Ready 512.77 WHQL</span>版显卡驱动，主要为游戏《<span lang="EN-US">Evil Dead: The Game</span>》提供优化支持，<strong>通过<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA DLSS</span>技术可最大限度的提升游戏性能。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">同时，《杜蒙》和《吸血鬼：避世血族<span lang="EN-US">-</span>血猎》两款游戏也得到新版驱动的优化。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">Bug</span>方面，<span lang="EN-US">512.77</span>版驱动仅修复了【<span lang="EN-US">Adobe Premiere Pro</span>】软件中<span lang="EN-US">DirectX</span>相关功能引起的间歇性崩溃问题，未提及游戏方面的问题。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">另外还有<span lang="EN-US">3</span>条已知问题，具体如下：</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">1</span>、使用<span lang="EN-US">Ampere</span>核心的<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>且通过<span lang="EN-US">HDMI 2.1</span>接口输出音频<span lang="EN-US">/</span>视频时，<span lang="EN-US">Dolby Atmos</span>格式的音频可能会无法输出。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2</span>、使用<span lang="EN-US">Club 3D CAC-1085</span>转接器时，输出的最大分辨率为<span lang="EN-US">4K@60H</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">3</span>、使用<span lang="EN-US">GeForce RTX 3090 Ti</span>显卡时，系统无法识别<span lang="EN-US">Oculus Rift S/Pimax 8K x VR</span>设备。</p>
