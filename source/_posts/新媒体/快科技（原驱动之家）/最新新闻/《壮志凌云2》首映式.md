<div>   
<p>靓汤如约翩然而至，乘直升机降落至中途岛号后甲板，从碟中谍片场赶来参加《壮志凌云2》首映式：</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/6e32dcecfbe946c2acc8a67052170981.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="《壮志凌云2》首映式" h="375" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/s_6e32dcecfbe946c2acc8a67052170981.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/62469b7033b94688bc1cfca7dea94037.png" target="_blank"><img alt="《壮志凌云2》首映式" h="380" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/s_62469b7033b94688bc1cfca7dea94037.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/2d324d41c7ef4541b7630e1578a7cbdd.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《壮志凌云2》首映式" h="858" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/s_2d324d41c7ef4541b7630e1578a7cbdd.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>在片中饰演呆头鹅之子的爆裂鼓手与靓汤亲切交谈，两人之间的戏剧冲突扛起整部电影：</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/4f0c5a5947cd4fa3bba9e134a0834652.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《壮志凌云2》首映式" h="399" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/s_4f0c5a5947cd4fa3bba9e134a0834652.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>两人不仅身高差异明显，演技也有差别（猜猜谁的演技更好）。影片导演科辛斯基及制作人布鲁克海默携家人莅临首映式：</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/21caa0b372c247c38890e83abeaae56e.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="《壮志凌云2》首映式" h="856" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/s_21caa0b372c247c38890e83abeaae56e.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/fa9530ffc99347c09a9ea6ebb9d50272.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《壮志凌云2》首映式" h="388" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/s_fa9530ffc99347c09a9ea6ebb9d50272.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>（曾经的）玉女詹尼弗·康纳利在火红长筒靴的加持下率先登舰：</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/72f831ff49c6422fbb903fce16b34260.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《壮志凌云2》首映式" h="750" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/s_72f831ff49c6422fbb903fce16b34260.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/22431b5b60d54cee86a4fa25c25f0283.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《壮志凌云2》首映式" h="390" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/s_22431b5b60d54cee86a4fa25c25f0283.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>她在片中与靓汤演对手戏，依然是平民身份，但不再是武器专家而是酒吧老板。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/2d9b523f8fdf4afca105742be2aea65e.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《壮志凌云2》首映式" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/s_2d9b523f8fdf4afca105742be2aea65e.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/08186db3bf0b49a1953ca4ef531fb29f.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《壮志凌云2》首映式" h="750" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/s_08186db3bf0b49a1953ca4ef531fb29f.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>公开数据显示，靓汤一米七，我弗一米六九，身高相近的两人怎样才能拍出这种效果？原来奥秘在鞋底：</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/784e16a23d6f40deb38eabdc95a45d05.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《壮志凌云2》首映式" h="750" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/s_784e16a23d6f40deb38eabdc95a45d05.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>幻视（左一）是我弗的丈夫，右一是我弗（但不是幻视）的儿子：</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/4d894294949a46de8b2eec9786f26388.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《壮志凌云2》首映式" h="872" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/s_4d894294949a46de8b2eec9786f26388.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>广告狂人也有幸参演，他在片中的地位及作用类似前作中的“毒蛇”：</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/d0c98f947f1b41f6a752185c019be990.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="《壮志凌云2》首映式" h="839" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/s_d0c98f947f1b41f6a752185c019be990.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>雷帝嘎嘎为该片创作的歌曲“执我之手”业已上线，粉丝惊喜地发现她与汉斯季莫同为电影原声创作者：</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/0b68e57c3d9041848333e5392caeaaab.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="《壮志凌云2》首映式" h="600" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/s_0b68e57c3d9041848333e5392caeaaab.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/eae69831a6a8491c81e71ba58560e48f.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《壮志凌云2》首映式" h="600" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/s_eae69831a6a8491c81e71ba58560e48f.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/8802a1d686e9468eb2919308cccadd38.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="《壮志凌云2》首映式" h="748" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/s_8802a1d686e9468eb2919308cccadd38.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/1cac25b07c1349f9b90fc7ea3d54ebc9.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="《壮志凌云2》首映式" h="200" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/s_1cac25b07c1349f9b90fc7ea3d54ebc9.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>影迷将本片角色与前作剧照进行拼接，缅怀逝去的青春：</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/1c75e5211ecc495e957c352ae46ecdec.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《壮志凌云2》首映式" h="1076" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/s_1c75e5211ecc495e957c352ae46ecdec.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/cfca16092e7e49e89f25d65a2ca887d0.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《壮志凌云2》首映式" h="989" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/s_cfca16092e7e49e89f25d65a2ca887d0.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>首映式设在退役航母“中途岛”号的飞行甲板上，该船退役后成为类似海上博物馆的景点：</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/63293658260045b880f8b6394ed452b7.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《壮志凌云2》首映式" h="789" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/s_63293658260045b880f8b6394ed452b7.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/6b73853bb9ec4df3855edd44729de40d.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《壮志凌云2》首映式" h="398" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/s_6b73853bb9ec4df3855edd44729de40d.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>除拨冗参加首映式外，靓汤还携女弟子（呼号“凤凰”）造访最新一期戈登秀场，为该片作宣传：</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/f4d961a3004046c7b1fa7e6ebacabbf6.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《壮志凌云2》首映式" h="389" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/s_f4d961a3004046c7b1fa7e6ebacabbf6.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/383a530a7ddf4d56a686a08c69c410a4.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《壮志凌云2》首映式" h="404" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/s_383a530a7ddf4d56a686a08c69c410a4.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>为满足粉丝的热望，宣传单位十分体贴地设计了这个“执我之手”装置：</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/6abc84a22f514844a45cc77aed4eab1c.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="《壮志凌云2》首映式" h="800" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220505/s_6abc84a22f514844a45cc77aed4eab1c.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
