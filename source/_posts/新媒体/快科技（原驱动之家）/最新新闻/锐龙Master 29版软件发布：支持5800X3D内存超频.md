<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">前几天<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>正式发布了锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7 5800X3D</span>处理器，在原有锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7 5800X</span>基础上增加了<span lang="EN-US">64MB L3</span>缓存，总缓存高达<span lang="EN-US">100MB</span>，不过限制了<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>超频，游戏性能堪比酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i9-12900KS</span>，后者可要贵<span lang="EN-US">2500</span>元以上。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7 5800X3D</span>处理器发布之后，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>日前也更新了<span lang="EN-US">Ryzen Master</span>软件，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>最新版是<span lang="EN-US">锐龙Master 2.9.0.2093</span>版，给锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7 5800X</span>带来了内存超频功能，还有多个新功能升级。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">锐龙Master 2.9.0.2093</span>升级亮点：</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">增加了使用<span lang="EN-US"> AMD Ryzen 5000</span>处理器的台式机支持</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">为<span lang="EN-US"> AMD</span>锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7 5800X3D</span>添加内存超频支持</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">添加对<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>锐龙<span lang="EN-US">ThreadripperPRO 5000WX</span>处理器的支持</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">添加了错误报告工具，便于反馈和问题报告</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>为<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>锐龙<span lang="EN-US">5000</span>处理器和锐龙<span lang="EN-US">PRO 5000WX</span>处理器引入自动曲线优化器和手动曲线优化器</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">安装<span lang="EN-US">Ryzen Master 2.9.0.2093</span>软件需要<span lang="EN-US">X570</span>芯片组及锐龙<span lang="EN-US">3000</span>及以上处理器，<span lang="EN-US">Win10 2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月更新及以上系统。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><a class="f14_link" href="https://www.amd.com/en/technologies/ryzen-master" target="_blank">下载地址点此。</a></p>
