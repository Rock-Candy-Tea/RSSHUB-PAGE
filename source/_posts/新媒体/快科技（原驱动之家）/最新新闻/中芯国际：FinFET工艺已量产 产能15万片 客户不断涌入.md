<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日晚，中芯国际发表了<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">Q2</span>财报，<a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/774/774394.htm" target="_blank">营收<span lang="EN-US">13.4</span>亿美元，同比增长<span lang="EN-US">43.2%</span></a>，环比增长<span lang="EN-US">21,8%</span>，归母净利润为<span lang="EN-US">6.88</span>亿美元，环比增长<span lang="EN-US">332.9%</span>，同比增长<span lang="EN-US">398.5%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在财报电话会上，中芯国际联席<span lang="EN-US">CEO</span>赵海军也透露了公司的先进工艺的情况，表示“我们的<span lang="EN-US">FinFET</span>工艺已经达产，每月<span lang="EN-US">1.5</span>万片，客户多样化，不同的产品平台都导入了。<span lang="EN-US">(</span>这部分<span lang="EN-US">)</span>产能处于紧俏状态，客户不断进来。<span lang="EN-US">”</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据之前的报道，中芯国际的<span lang="EN-US">FinFET</span>工艺有多种类型，其中第一代<span lang="EN-US">FinFET</span>工艺是<span lang="EN-US">14nm</span>及改进型的<span lang="EN-US">12nm，目前1.5万片产能的主要就是14/12nm工艺</span>，第二代则是<span lang="EN-US">n+1</span>、<span lang="EN-US">n+2</span>工艺，已经试产，但产能不会有多大。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>根据中芯国际联席<span lang="EN-US">CEO</span>梁孟松博此前公布的信息显示，<span lang="EN-US">N</span>＋<span lang="EN-US">1</span>工艺和现有的<span lang="EN-US">14nm</span>工艺相比，性能提升了<span lang="EN-US">20%</span>，功耗降低了<span lang="EN-US">57%</span>，逻辑面积缩小了<span lang="EN-US">63%</span>，<span lang="EN-US">SoC</span>面积减少了<span lang="EN-US">55%</span>。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">从逻辑面积缩小的数据来看，与<span lang="EN-US">7nm</span>工艺相近。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">梁孟松博士也表示，<span lang="EN-US">N</span>＋<span lang="EN-US">1</span>代工艺在功耗及稳定性上跟<span lang="EN-US">7nm</span>工艺非常相似，但性能要低一些（业界标准是提升<span lang="EN-US">35%</span>），所以中芯国际的<span lang="EN-US">N</span>＋<span lang="EN-US">1</span>工艺主要面向低功耗应用的。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">而在<span lang="EN-US">N</span>＋<span lang="EN-US">1</span>之后，中芯国际还会有<span lang="EN-US">N</span>＋<span lang="EN-US">2</span>，这两种工艺在功耗上表现差不多，区别在于性能及成本，<span lang="EN-US">N</span>＋<span lang="EN-US">2</span>显然是面向高性能的，成本也会增加。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此外，梁孟松还透露，中芯国际的<span lang="EN-US">28nm</span>、<span lang="EN-US">14nm</span>、<span lang="EN-US">12nm</span>、<span lang="EN-US">N+1</span>等技术均已进入规模量产，<span lang="EN-US">7nm</span>技术开发已经完成，今年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月可以进入风险量产，<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>、<span lang="EN-US">3nm</span>最关键也是最艰巨的<span lang="EN-US">8</span>大项技术也已经有序展开，只等<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>极紫外光刻机到来，就可以进入全面开发阶段。</p>
