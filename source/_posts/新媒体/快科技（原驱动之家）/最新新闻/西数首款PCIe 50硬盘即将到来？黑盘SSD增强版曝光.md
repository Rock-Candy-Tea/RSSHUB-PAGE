<p class="MsoNormal">西数去年底推出了黑盘<span lang="EN-US">SN850</span>系列<span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>，这是西数首款<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 4.0</span>旗舰产品，速度达到了<span lang="EN-US">7GB/s</span>，日前网上又曝光了<span lang="EN-US">SN850X</span>黑盘<span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>，有可能是<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 4.0</span>增强版，不过也有可能是<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>的新品。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">除了<span lang="EN-US">SN850X</span>的型号之外，目前对这款黑盘<span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>的规格还是一无所知，通常来说<span lang="EN-US">X</span>后缀意味着性能提升，<span lang="EN-US">SN850X</span>可能会换用更强的主控及<span lang="EN-US">TLC</span>闪存，速度再冲高一下，读取速度<span lang="EN-US">7G/s</span>差不多到头了，<strong>但<span lang="EN-US">SN850</span>的写入只有<span lang="EN-US">5GB/s</span>左右，<span lang="EN-US">SN850X</span>有望提升到<span lang="EN-US">7GB/s</span>左右，成为最强<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 4.0</span>硬盘之一。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>不过也不排除<span lang="EN-US">SN850X</span>是<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>的可能，目前<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿已经支持了<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>，三星等公司已经开始布局<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>硬盘，西数也不能缺席。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">黑盘<span lang="EN-US">SN850X</span>硬盘的发布时间还没确定，不过明年初的<span lang="EN-US">CES</span>展会可能是个机会，届时是<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 4.0</span>还是<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>也能见分晓了。</p>
