<p class="MsoNormal">这几天适逢科隆游戏展，有多款游戏发布或者升级新技术。<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>日前也发布了<span lang="EN-US">Radeon Software Adrenalin</span>显卡驱动<span lang="EN-US">21.8.2</span>版，为《<span lang="EN-US">Myst</span>》等<span lang="EN-US">3</span>款游戏提供支持及优化，<span lang="EN-US">4K</span>性能提升大约<span lang="EN-US">6%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Radeon Software Adrenalin 21.8.2</span>版显卡驱动发布，主要为《<span lang="EN-US">Myst</span>》游戏进行优化。<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>使用<span lang="EN-US">Radeon RX 6800XT 16GB</span>显卡，开启光线追踪并设置为<span lang="EN-US">4K</span>分辨率中等画质时，对比<span lang="EN-US">21.8.1</span>版本游戏性能提升了约<span lang="EN-US">6%</span>。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">同时，新版驱动也为《异形<span lang="EN-US">:</span>火力小队》和《小小梦魇<span lang="EN-US">2</span>：增强版》游戏提供了支持。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此外，<span lang="EN-US">21.8.2</span>版还修复了之前版本的部分错误，具体如下：</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">1</span>、使用<span lang="EN-US">RX 6800XT</span>显卡并以<span lang="EN-US">H265/HEVC</span>编解码器录制时，<span lang="EN-US">Open Broadcaster Software</span>软件可能会无法结束录制。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2</span>、《<span lang="EN-US">F1 2021</span>》在分屏模式下会出现画面异常。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">3</span>、<span lang="EN-US">Radeon Software</span>软件有时候无响应。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">4</span>、使用<span lang="EN-US">Radeon RX 6600 XT</span>显卡时，《<span lang="EN-US">Control</span>》在<span lang="EN-US">DX12</span>模式下会出现光线异常。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">5</span>、<span lang="EN-US">Ryzen Master</span>自动更新功能失效。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>部分已知问题：</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">1</span>、使用<span lang="EN-US">Radeon RX 6800 XT</span>时，《<span lang="EN-US">Myst</span>》、《<span lang="EN-US">Kingshunt</span>》等游戏会出现黑屏，可通过禁用<span lang="EN-US">Performance Metrics Overlay</span>或<span lang="EN-US">Radeon Image Sharpening</span>临时解决。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2</span>、使用<span lang="EN-US">RX 500</span>系列显卡时，同时玩游戏和观看流媒体会导致驱动无响应。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">3</span>、使用多显示器并以【扩展】模式连接时，运行类似《<span lang="EN-US">PlayerUnknown’s Battlegrounds</span>》的<span lang="EN-US">DX11</span>游戏会导致<span lang="EN-US">AMD Radeon Software</span>软件崩溃或者卡顿。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">4</span>、使用<span lang="EN-US">RX 6700 XT</span>显卡时，长时间运行《地平线：零之曙光》可能会导致驱动超时或游戏崩溃。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">5</span>、启用增强同步会导致部分游戏或系统黑屏。遇到这一问题的用户请禁用增强同步功能。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">6</span>、<span lang="EN-US">Radeon RX Vega</span>系列显卡在连接两个分辨率<span lang="EN-US">/</span>刷新率差距较大的显示器时会出现闪屏。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">7</span>、<span lang="EN-US">Radeon performance metrics</span>显示过高或错误的闪存频率。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">8</span>、<span lang="EN-US">HEVC</span>编码的流媒体可能会出现画面发灰，遇到问题的用户请先使用<span lang="EN-US">AVC</span>编码。</p>
