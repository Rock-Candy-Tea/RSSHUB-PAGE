<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年的<span lang="EN-US">ISSCC</span>国际固态电路大会（国际三大半导体顶级会议之一）将于<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日举行，厂商会在这次大会上展示最新的半导体制造技术，其中<span lang="EN-US">SK</span>海力士会介绍最新的<span lang="EN-US">HBM3</span>内存技术，带宽从此前的<span lang="EN-US">819GB/s</span>提升到<span lang="EN-US">896GB/s</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">HBM3</span>已经是<span lang="EN-US">HBM</span>系列高带宽内存的第四代标准，之前三代分别是<span lang="EN-US">HBM</span>、<span lang="EN-US">HBM2</span>、<span lang="EN-US">HBM2E</span>，其中<span lang="EN-US">SK</span>海力士的<span lang="EN-US">HBM2E</span>在<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年七月全球首家投入量产。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">SK</span>海力士去年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月份就首发了<span lang="EN-US">HBM3</span>内存，提供两种容量，一是<span lang="EN-US">16GB</span>，二是<span lang="EN-US">24GB</span>，后者创下新纪录，内部通过<span lang="EN-US">TSV</span>硅穿孔技术堆叠了多达<span lang="EN-US">12</span>颗芯片，但是厚度依然控制在大约<span lang="EN-US">30</span>微米，相当于一张<span lang="EN-US">A4</span>纸的三分之一。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">当时的<span lang="EN-US">HBM3</span>内存带宽是<span lang="EN-US">819GB/s</span>，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>这次在<span lang="EN-US">ISSCC</span>大会上展示的则是更快的新品，带宽提升到了<span lang="EN-US">896GB/s</span>，增长了<span lang="EN-US">9%</span>。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">要知道<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>在<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年首发的第一代<span lang="EN-US">HBM</span>内存中，<span lang="EN-US">4</span>颗<span lang="EN-US">HBM</span>、等效<span lang="EN-US">4096bit</span>位宽才获得了<span lang="EN-US">512GB/s</span>的带宽，现在<span lang="EN-US">HBM3</span>一颗带宽就几乎翻倍。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">可惜的是，<span lang="EN-US">HBM3</span>内存越来越贵，除了数据中心<span lang="EN-US">GPU/CPU</span>之外，消费级显卡、处理器上恐怕是见不到了。</p>
