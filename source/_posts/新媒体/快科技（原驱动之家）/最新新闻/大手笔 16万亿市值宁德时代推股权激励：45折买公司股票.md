<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日晚，宁德时代发布公告，称公司董事会审议通过了股权激励计划，将向<span lang="EN-US">117</span>人发放股权<span lang="EN-US">/</span>股权激励，价格只有收盘价的<span lang="EN-US">4.5</span>折。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据议案，董事会认为公司<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年股票期权与限制性股票激励计划规定的预留授予条件已经成就。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据公司<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年第二次临时股东大会的授权，同意确定以<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日为预留授权日、预留授予日，向<span lang="EN-US">71</span>名预留授予部分激励对象授予<span lang="EN-US">51.3800</span>万份股票期权，行权价格为<span lang="EN-US">612.08</span>元<span lang="EN-US">/</span>份；</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">向<span lang="EN-US">46</span>名预留授予部分激励对象授予<span lang="EN-US">2.8940</span>万股限制性股票，授予价格为<span lang="EN-US">306.04</span>元<span lang="EN-US">/</span>股。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">截至<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日收盘，宁德时代报<span lang="EN-US">681</span>元<span lang="EN-US">/</span>股，上涨<span lang="EN-US">6.57%，</span><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>今年以来公司股价上涨<span lang="EN-US">94%</span>，总市值为<span lang="EN-US">15873</span>亿元，为<span lang="EN-US">A</span>股市值排名第二的上市公司。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>部分激励对象股票授予价为收盘价的<span lang="EN-US">4.5</span>折，期权行权价为收盘价的<span lang="EN-US">9</span>折。</strong></p>
