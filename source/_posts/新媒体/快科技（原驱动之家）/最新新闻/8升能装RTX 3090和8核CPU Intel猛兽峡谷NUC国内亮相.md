<p class="MsoNormal">最新一代的<span lang="EN-US">NUC</span>“猛兽峡谷”（<span lang="EN-US">Beast Canyon</span>）发布有段时间了，在日前的<span lang="EN-US">ChinaJoy</span>展会上，猛兽峡谷<span lang="EN-US">NUC</span>首次亮相，这次主打的是高性能，体积也达到了史无前例的<span lang="EN-US">8</span>升，能装下<span lang="EN-US">8</span>核<span lang="EN-US">16</span>线程酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i7/i9</span>、最高可配<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3090</span>显卡。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的<span lang="EN-US">NUC</span>最初是主打超迷你<span lang="EN-US">PC</span>市场的，功耗、发热、体积都非常小，然而这几代的<span lang="EN-US">NUC</span>性能越来越强，<span lang="EN-US">11</span>代酷睿处理器的猛兽峡谷这一代更夸张了，体积达到了<span lang="EN-US">8</span>升，比很多<span lang="EN-US">ITX</span>主机还要大了，但<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>表示这还是<span lang="EN-US">NUC</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">为了容纳更高端的硬件，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>猛兽峡谷<span lang="EN-US">NUC</span>电脑也一改之前方方正正的造型，变成了长方体，长宽高分别是<span lang="EN-US">357x189x120mm</span>，体积<span lang="EN-US">8L</span>，官方表示“体积小巧、总体积仅为<span lang="EN-US">8</span>升”。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">具体配置上，猛兽峡谷有<span lang="EN-US">2</span>个主要的配置，酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i7</span>版使用的是酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i7-11700B</span>处理器、酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i9</span>版是酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i9-11900KB</span>——两款<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>都相当有迷惑性，不是<span lang="EN-US">11</span>代酷睿桌面版，<strong>而是<span lang="EN-US">10nm</span>工艺的<span lang="EN-US">11</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Tiger Lake</span>，都是<span lang="EN-US">8</span>核<span lang="EN-US">16</span>线程</strong>，区别是前者是<span lang="EN-US">3.2G</span>到<span lang="EN-US">4.8GHz</span>，<span lang="EN-US">TVB</span>加速最高<span lang="EN-US">5.3GHz</span>，后者是<span lang="EN-US">3.3</span>到<span lang="EN-US">4.9GHz</span>，<span lang="EN-US">TVB</span>加速<span lang="EN-US">5.3GHz</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">除了<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>之外，<strong>其他配置是<span lang="EN-US">8GB DDR4-3200</span>、<span lang="EN-US">256GB NVMe SSD</span>，觉得偏低的可以升级，最高支持<span lang="EN-US">64GB+8TB</span>。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">其他方面，猛兽峡谷<span lang="EN-US">NUC</span>的配置也不错，包括一个<span lang="EN-US">2.5</span>千兆或万兆网口、两个雷电<span lang="EN-US">4/USB-C</span>、六个<span lang="EN-US">USB 3.0</span>、一个<span lang="EN-US">HDMI 2.0b</span>，自带三风扇机箱散热器、<span lang="EN-US">650W</span>金牌电源。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">值得一提的是，外观炫酷这事也少不了，不仅有酷酷的骷髅<span lang="EN-US">logp</span>，还支持<span lang="EN-US">RGB</span>信仰灯效。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">售价方面，配备酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i7/i9</span>版本的猛兽峡谷<span lang="EN-US">NUC</span>起售价分别为<span lang="EN-US">1150</span>、<span lang="EN-US">1350 </span>美元，分别折合<span lang="EN-US">7426 </span>、<span lang="EN-US">8717 </span>人民币，预计今年第三季度开售——国内高配版过万是跑不了的。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>注意，这些价格是不含显卡的。</strong></p>
