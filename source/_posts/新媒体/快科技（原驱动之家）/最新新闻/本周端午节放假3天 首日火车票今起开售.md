<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">据央视报道，因铁路客票预售期自<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日起已临时调整为<span lang="EN-US">5</span>天<span lang="EN-US">(</span>含当天<span lang="EN-US">)</span>，<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年端午假期首日<span lang="EN-US">(6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日<span lang="EN-US">)</span>铁路客票将于<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日起开售。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">假日车票预售时间安排为：<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，可购买<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日车票；<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日，可购买<span lang="EN-US"> 6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日车票；<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，可购买<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日车票。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">北京铁路提示，有出行需求的旅客，请提前了解出发地、目的地最新防疫政策，关注列车开行情况，合理规划出行。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>根据通知，端午节<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日至<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日放假，共<span lang="EN-US">3</span>天。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">按照规定，端午节<span lang="EN-US">3</span>天假期不用调休。端午过后今年假期余额还有<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天：中秋节<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日至<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日放假，共<span lang="EN-US">3</span>天；国庆节<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日至<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日放假调休，共<span lang="EN-US">7</span>天。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">另外需要注意的是，根据颁发的《重大节假日免收小型客车通行费实施方案》安排，在春节、清明节、劳动节和国庆节四个国家法定节假日，<span lang="EN-US">7</span>座及以下小型客车享受高速免费通行政策。所以，端午节高速公路不享受免费通行政策。</p>
