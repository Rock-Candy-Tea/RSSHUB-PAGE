<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年闪存逐渐从<span lang="EN-US">96</span>层过渡到了<span lang="EN-US">128</span>层为主，再往下就是<span lang="EN-US">170</span>层到<span lang="EN-US">200</span>层的了，每家闪存厂商的方案都有所不同，西数将在明年底量产<span lang="EN-US">BiCS6</span>代<span lang="EN-US">3D</span>闪存，堆栈层数提升到<span lang="EN-US">162</span>，而且接口速度翻倍。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在闪存市场上，西数跟东芝是合作研发、生产的，<span lang="EN-US">BiCS</span>技术其实主要是来自东芝，目前量产的主力是<span lang="EN-US">BiCS5</span>，<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月份发布，堆栈层数<span lang="EN-US">112</span>层，核心容量<span lang="EN-US">512Gbit</span>，接口速度<span lang="EN-US">1.066Gbps</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">BiCS6</span>闪存是下一代产品，堆栈层数最终确定为<span lang="EN-US">162</span>层，而非之前所说的<span lang="EN-US">170+</span>层，核心容量也提升到了<span lang="EN-US">1Tbit</span>，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>另外就是接口速度也将达到<span lang="EN-US">2.0Gbps</span>，相比上代翻倍，不过距离三星最新的<span lang="EN-US">8</span>代<span lang="EN-US">V-NAND</span>闪存的<span lang="EN-US">2.4Gbps</span>还有点距离。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据西数的计划，<span lang="EN-US">BiCS6</span>闪存将从明年初开始生产，<strong>但真正大规模量产要到<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年底了</strong>，现在的<span lang="EN-US">BiCS5</span>闪存还要过渡至少一年。</p>
