<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>昨晚发布了<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿，代号<span lang="EN-US">Alder Lake</span>，<a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/792/792044_all.htm#2" target="_blank">这代升级了全新的<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>架构</a>，并首次在桌面端引入了大小核架构，旗舰酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i9-12900K</span>拥有<span lang="EN-US">8</span>大<span lang="EN-US">8</span>小总计<span lang="EN-US">16</span>核架构，多达<span lang="EN-US">24</span>个线程。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">同时<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿还升级了最新的<span lang="EN-US">Intel 7</span>工艺，虽然本质上还是之前的<span lang="EN-US">10nm SF</span>工艺，但桌面版及服务器端接下来都会导入这个工艺，其晶体管集成度不输台积电的<span lang="EN-US">7nm</span>工艺，缩短与<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>锐龙处理器的代差。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">从<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>公布的性能测试来看，<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿的性能很好很强大，<strong>旗舰<span lang="EN-US">i9-12900K</span>面对锐龙<span lang="EN-US">9 5950X</span>，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>承认部分游戏确实稍有落后，也有的持平，但绝大部分都明显领先，最高幅度超过<span lang="EN-US">30</span>％。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="Intel强势归来 AMD展望2022：确保5nm Zen4依然领先" h="338" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211028/1e53cf09-cd98-447b-a13b-d16ee64fa666.png" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>这次强势回来，虽然现在还不能说就是王子复仇（具体评测还没解禁），能把<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的<span lang="EN-US">7nm Zen3</span>扫落马下，但是<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的竞争力显然是要削弱的。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal">根据之前泄露的信息，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>接下来会推出一波<span lang="EN-US">3D V-Cache</span>缓存加强版的<span lang="EN-US">Zen3</span>处理器，包括桌面端锐龙及服务器端的霄龙，前者估计在明年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月份的<span lang="EN-US">CES</span>展会上发布，后者已经确定在<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日的发布会上推出。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>提升缓存之后，按照<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>之前的数据，加强版的锐龙处理器游戏性能可提升<span lang="EN-US">15%</span>，将会缩短与<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿的性能差距，但不太可能再全面领先回来了。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的大招还得看明年的<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>架构，升级<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>工艺，也会支持<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>、<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>等技术，不过首先上市的是霄龙版，代号<span lang="EN-US">Genoa</span>，预计提升到<span lang="EN-US">96</span>核<span lang="EN-US">192</span>线程。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在日前的发布会上，<span lang="EN-US">AMD CEO</span>苏姿丰也谈到了<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>的进展，强调他们的路线图一如既往地执行，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>将确保<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>在下一代市场竞争上依然保持领先地位。</strong></span></p>
