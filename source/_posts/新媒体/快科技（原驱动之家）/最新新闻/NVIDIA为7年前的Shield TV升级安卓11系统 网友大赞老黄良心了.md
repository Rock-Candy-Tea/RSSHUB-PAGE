<p class="MsoNormal">虽然<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>在显卡涨价上的问题被网友吐槽，但是在软件系统升级上这次真的良心了一波，日前<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>为<span lang="EN-US">Shield TV</span>升级了安卓<span lang="EN-US">11</span>系统，这款设备发布于<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年，迄今<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年时间了，这波升级也让媒体及网友大赞NVIDIA良心。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">日前<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>发布通知，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>将为<span lang="EN-US">Shield TV</span>设备推送<span lang="EN-US">Software Experience Upgrade 9.0</span>，包括<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>版设备在内都会升级新系统，底层升级为安卓<span lang="EN-US">11</span>，还包括去年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月份发布的安卓安全补丁，修复了远程攻击可导致<span lang="EN-US">DDOS</span>的漏洞。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Software Experience Upgrade 9.0</span>还带了了一些功能升级，语音搜索支持谷歌键盘，用户可以通过<span lang="EN-US">Google Assistant</span>语音来搜索想要看的电影及节目。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此外，新系统还支持在<span lang="EN-US">Shield TV</span>上使用支持<span lang="EN-US">aptX</span>蓝牙耳机，还支持在睡眠时断开蓝牙耳机以省电的功能，额外自定义电源的节能设置等等，<span lang="EN-US">GeForce Now </span>会员也增加了对其他键盘和鼠标的支持等等。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong><span lang="EN-US">Shield TV</span>是<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>发布的电视机顶盒产品，<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年首发的版本采用了<span lang="EN-US">Tegra X1</span>处理器，</strong>内置<span lang="EN-US">3GB</span>内存和<span lang="EN-US">16/500GB</span>机身存储空间，支持<span lang="EN-US">4K@60fps 10bit VP9/H.265</span>视频解码、<span lang="EN-US">802.11ac Wi-Fi</span>以及蓝牙<span lang="EN-US">4.1</span>，运行定制版<span lang="EN-US">Android TV</span>操作系统。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">后续在<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年升级过处理器，<span lang="EN-US">Tegra X1+</span>采用台积电<span lang="EN-US">16nm</span>制造（上代是<span lang="EN-US">20nm</span>），<span lang="EN-US">CPU 8</span>核设计，分别是<span lang="EN-US">4x A57+4x A53</span>架构，搭配<span lang="EN-US">256</span>颗<span lang="EN-US">Maxwell GPU</span>，并有更高端的<span lang="EN-US">Shield TV Pro</span>，升级为<span lang="EN-US">3+16GB</span>存储、多出<span lang="EN-US">2</span>个<span lang="EN-US">USB 3.0 Type A</span>接口、可连接<span lang="EN-US">Plex</span>媒体库服务器等。</p>
