<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">在收购<span lang="EN-US">ATI</span>之后，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>一直自诩为全球唯一能提供高性能<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>及<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>的厂商，而对手<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>、<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>只是在<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>或者<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>上有优势。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">从<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年重返高性能<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>市场之后，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>靠着锐龙<span lang="EN-US">/</span>霄龙收复了部分失地，在桌面、笔记本、服务器<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>市场上给友商<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>带来了压力。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在显卡市场上，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>独显份额之前一度跌落<span lang="EN-US">20%</span>以上，随着<span lang="EN-US">7nm RDNA</span>及<span lang="EN-US">RDNA2</span>架构的<span lang="EN-US">Radeon RX 5000</span>、<span lang="EN-US">RX 6000</span>系列上市，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>在显卡市场上的劣势也弥补了不少。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">但是<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>在高性能<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>上的优势太强大了，游戏及数据中心揭示如此，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>缩短差距还有段距离。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">那<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>什么时候能够在<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>、<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>两大市场上同时正面刚友商<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>、<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>呢？这事没明确答案，因为还要看这三家厂商未来的技术及产品发展。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">开发了<span lang="EN-US">CapFrameX</span>测试软件的大<span lang="EN-US">V</span>似乎掌握了一些内幕消息，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>提到<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>在<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年就有可能击败<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>及<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>，但他不能给出详细信息。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">这个问题引发了网友的讨论，不过还没有什么实锤证据。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="A饭再等1年 Zen4/RNDA3在手的AMD真正崛起了" h="340" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210514/3102c7d7-72ad-4902-af16-d38fa5fbb5b8.jpg" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">A</span>饭假如能够等到<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>同时在<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>、<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>市场上击败<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>、<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>那<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的杀手锏只有可能是<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>处理器及<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>的<span lang="EN-US">RDNA3</span>显卡。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">对于<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>，它不仅会升级台积电<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>工艺，同时也是<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>全新平台（<span lang="EN-US">AM5</span>？）的开始，支持<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>、<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>，<span lang="EN-US">IPC</span>传闻提升<span lang="EN-US">20%</span>以上，<strong>甚至还有更夸张的<span lang="EN-US">40% IPC</span>提升。</strong></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="A饭再等1年 Zen4/RNDA3在手的AMD真正崛起了" h="368" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210514/08593205-27af-4f24-81a0-676950329ad8.png" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="text-align: center"><img alt="A饭再等1年 Zen4/RNDA3在手的AMD真正崛起了" h="336" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210514/7c6902d5-d96e-4820-b897-49002f489ead.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>方面则是靠<span lang="EN-US">RDNA3</span>架构，除了升级<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>工艺之外，预计会使用小芯片设计，<span lang="EN-US">IO</span>核心是<span lang="EN-US">7nm</span>工艺，计算核心是<span lang="EN-US">2</span>组<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>核心，每组至少<span lang="EN-US">80</span>组<span lang="EN-US">CU</span>单元，<strong>相比现在的满血<span lang="EN-US">RX 6000</span>系列翻倍规格，性能有望提升一倍。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">尽管现在还有很多不确定性，但是<span lang="EN-US">A</span>饭至少可以期待光明的未来，<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年或许就是重新扬眉吐气的日子了。</p>
