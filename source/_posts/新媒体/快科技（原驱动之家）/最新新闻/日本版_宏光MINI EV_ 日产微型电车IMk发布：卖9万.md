<div>   
<p>早在去年就有人曝光了日产微型电动车的相关谍照，而近日，这款车终于露出了庐山真面目。</p>
<p>新车基于日产IMk概念车打造，有望命名为SAKURA（日语译为樱花），<strong>其售价178万日元起（约合人民币9万元左右）</strong>。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220521/f2deebe25abb4837afdd72a8b8ca784d.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="日本版“宏光MINI EV” 日产微型电车IMk发布：卖9万" h="450" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220521/s_f2deebe25abb4837afdd72a8b8ca784d.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>日产IMk长宽高为3395/1475/1655mm，比国内的宏光MINI EV大了一些，采用了五门设计，造型同样方方正正，拥有较短的前悬。</p>
<p>而为了满足不同消费者的使用需求，<strong>该车还将提供15种外观颜色和3种内饰颜色可选</strong>。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220521/7f390d2aef264772bf7d9b73a24ccbbe.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="日本版“宏光MINI EV” 日产微型电车IMk发布：卖9万" h="450" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220521/s_7f390d2aef264772bf7d9b73a24ccbbe.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>其前脸使用了飞镖式的LED日间行车灯和灯带，同时拥有非常特别造型的轮圈，但目测尺寸较小，毕竟整车本身就不大。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220521/ed2a10a5a5de4b918d05b45cc07d3b6e.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="日本版“宏光MINI EV” 日产微型电车IMk发布：卖9万" h="450" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220521/s_ed2a10a5a5de4b918d05b45cc07d3b6e.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>不过该车内饰的设计相当有特色，整体较为简洁，甚至有种沃尔沃的性冷淡风，双辐式多功能方向盘一看就是日产的家族化风格。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220521/a19b218cb3f748a39f274254db57e22c.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="日本版“宏光MINI EV” 日产微型电车IMk发布：卖9万" h="450" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220521/s_a19b218cb3f748a39f274254db57e22c.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>不仅如此，其还配备有7英寸全液晶仪表盘、9英寸中控屏、多个储物空间及杯架，兼具了科技感和实用性。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220521/ff73a3b6b9eb4bf480df29ca39460866.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="日本版“宏光MINI EV” 日产微型电车IMk发布：卖9万" h="450" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220521/s_ff73a3b6b9eb4bf480df29ca39460866.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220521/cd0a7d9e32ce4a43a183d8c46479a0ec.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="日本版“宏光MINI EV” 日产微型电车IMk发布：卖9万" h="450" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220521/s_cd0a7d9e32ce4a43a183d8c46479a0ec.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220521/e60372abdc484ae396c33f66749d5adf.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="日本版“宏光MINI EV” 日产微型电车IMk发布：卖9万" h="450" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220521/s_e60372abdc484ae396c33f66749d5adf.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220521/868e3f6f5eef47c68f4f617672f62397.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="日本版“宏光MINI EV” 日产微型电车IMk发布：卖9万" h="450" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220521/s_868e3f6f5eef47c68f4f617672f62397.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220521/902a0c888936498ab14faca03a55fc88.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="日本版“宏光MINI EV” 日产微型电车IMk发布：卖9万" h="450" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220521/s_902a0c888936498ab14faca03a55fc88.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>虽然车型小，但新车还拥有至少L2级别的驾驶辅助系统，还带有V2H功能，可反向为家庭提供用电。</p>
<p>动力方面，该车最大功率47kW，峰值扭矩195N·m，电池包容量为20kWh，续航里程约180km左右(WLTC工况下)，同时提供生态、标准和运动三种驾驶模式可选，该车最高速度可达130km/h。</p>
