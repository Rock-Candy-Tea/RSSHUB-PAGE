<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>在去年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月底发布的<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿处理器上首发了<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存，正式开启了<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>时代，今年的<span lang="EN-US">13</span>代酷睿也会进一步支持，而<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>桌面版处理器也会加入<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>阵营。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>架构产品的特点可以用<span lang="EN-US">“</span>四个五<span lang="EN-US">”</span>来概括：首次采用台积电<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>工艺制造，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>平台上首次支持下一代<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存内存、<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>通道，首次引入新的<span lang="EN-US">AM5</span>封装接口。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的<span lang="EN-US">13</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Raptor Lake</span>也自然也少不了，而且会进一步优化支持，<strong>目前<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿默认支持的还是<span lang="EN-US">DDR5-4800</span>，消息称<span lang="EN-US">13</span>代会提升到<span lang="EN-US">DDR5-5200</span>甚至更高。</strong></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="13代酷睿、锐龙7000也带不动 DDR5内存今年仅10%份额：还是贵" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220415/2e142f68-6d99-44c1-89cc-29786f1940f3.png" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此前甚至有传闻称，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>对<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存的支持更激进，会推动厂商主要支持<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存而非成熟的<span lang="EN-US">DDR4</span>内存，而<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>这边也有类似的考虑，毕竟锐龙<span lang="EN-US">6000</span>移动版直接放弃了<span lang="EN-US">DDR4</span>内存。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>及<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>两大王者的带动下，今年的<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存占有率会比去年明显改观，但是因为之前的基数太低，<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存要想取代<span lang="EN-US">DDR4</span>成为主流还远得很。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据调研机构<span lang="EN-US">Omdia</span>预测，在整个<span lang="EN-US">DRAM</span>市场中，<strong><span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存市场占有率将达到<span lang="EN-US">10%</span>，而在<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年占有率将进一步提升至<span lang="EN-US">43%</span>，份额过半要等到<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年了。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">相对目前的<span lang="EN-US">DDR4</span>内存已经成熟稳定多年，<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存依然是新事物，三星、<span lang="EN-US">SK</span>海力士及美光等厂商还在技术及产能提升的早期阶段，导致<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存成本还是高不少，需要两三年的时间才能完成升级换代。</p>
