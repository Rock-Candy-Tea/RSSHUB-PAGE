<p><strong>昨日，Intel发布了锐炫移动显卡，同时预告锐炫桌面显卡限量版将于今夏发布。</strong></p>

<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220331/716e6e36122f40ecb19f3c5a9c5d5f0e.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="Intel锐炫桌面显卡限量版亮相：双风扇+发光logo 顶配16GB显存" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220331/s_716e6e36122f40ecb19f3c5a9c5d5f0e.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>这款显卡采用<strong>双风扇和双槽厚设计</strong>，预计是<strong>32Xe核心+16GB显存</strong>的顶配版，正面采用“<strong>透明探索</strong>”设计，侧面还有<strong>发光的Intel Arc logo</strong>。</p>
<p>不过，Intel暂未公布参数和规格。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220331/e99d1485c7e442cd943d11f3da7dc6be.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="Intel锐炫桌面显卡限量版亮相：双风扇+发光logo 顶配16GB显存" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220331/s_e99d1485c7e442cd943d11f3da7dc6be.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>据悉，锐炫A系列移动端显卡基于ACM-G10和ACM-G11两款GPU打造，前者拥有32个Xe内核和32个光追单元，后者拥有8个Xe内核和8个光追单元。</p>
<p>目前锐炫A系列移动显卡共有Arc 3/5/7三个系列，共5种型号。目前搭载Arc 300系列的笔记本现已面世，搭载Arc 700系列的笔记本将在初夏面世。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220331/5d56b99a759941f18ac760030e6ed9c5.png" target="_blank"><img alt="Intel锐炫桌面显卡限量版亮相：双风扇+发光logo 顶配16GB显存" h="375" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220331/s_5d56b99a759941f18ac760030e6ed9c5.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>性能方面，Intel以A370M和i7-1280P的核显进行了对比。<strong>A370M在多款游戏的1080p中或高画质下都可实现60fps以上的性能。</strong></p>

<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220331/ab611b85fcc74966ba58496a892880fb.png" target="_blank"><img alt="Intel锐炫桌面显卡限量版亮相：双风扇+发光logo 顶配16GB显存" h="325" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220331/s_ab611b85fcc74966ba58496a892880fb.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p><strong>在内容创作软件方面，A370M和Xe核显合力下，性能最高可达单独Xe核显的2.4倍。</strong></p>
