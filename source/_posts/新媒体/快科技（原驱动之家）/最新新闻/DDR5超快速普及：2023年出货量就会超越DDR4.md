<p class="MsoNormal">从<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年算起，<span lang="EN-US">DDR4</span>内存已经使用<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年多了，今年又到了一个关键节点，下半年<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存就会上市，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Alder Lake</span>这次要抢首发，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>也会支持<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>，但要到明年。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">每次内存标准升级换代，由于价格、配套平台、市场惯性等等原因，过渡期往往要很久，<span lang="EN-US">DDR4</span>取代<span lang="EN-US">DDR3</span>也用了至少两三年的时间，出货量上才超过了<span lang="EN-US">DDR3</span>标准。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>时代普及速度要快不少，今年底最多是有少量新品上市，<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年是各大厂商大规模切入<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存的第一年，普及率超过<span lang="EN-US">25%</span>，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>而行业调研公司<span lang="EN-US">Yole Developpement</span>预测显示，<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>的出货量就会超越<span lang="EN-US">DDR4</span>。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">换句话说，<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存只需要用一年左右的时间就超过<span lang="EN-US">DDR4</span>，占到<span lang="EN-US">50%</span>以上的份额，这个速度是之前没有的。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">至于<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存为何能够这么快速推动，很大一个原因是<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>及<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>两家竞争激烈，<strong>特别是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>在先进技术上不再保守，</strong>今年抢到了<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存的首发，在<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>上也同样如此。</p>
