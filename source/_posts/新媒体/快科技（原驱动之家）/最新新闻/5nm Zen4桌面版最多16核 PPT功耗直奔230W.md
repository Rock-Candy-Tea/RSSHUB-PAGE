<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>明年就要推出<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>处理器了，桌面版锐龙<span lang="EN-US">6000</span>系列代号为<span lang="EN-US">Raphael(</span>拉斐尔<span lang="EN-US">)</span>，最新信息显示依然最多<span lang="EN-US">16</span>核，也就是锐龙<span lang="EN-US">9 6950X</span>也是<span lang="EN-US">16</span>核<span lang="EN-US">32</span>线程的。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此前传闻的<span lang="EN-US">24</span>核<span lang="EN-US">48</span>线程桌面版锐龙已经落空，毕竟<span lang="EN-US">24</span>核已经深入到锐龙<span lang="EN-US">TR</span>这样的<span lang="EN-US">HEDT</span>平台处理器了，一个是没必要，另一个则是核心多了也会影响<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>频率，反而拖累游戏性能。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在锐龙<span lang="EN-US">6000</span>系列处理器上，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>还进一步解锁了<span lang="EN-US">TDP</span>功耗限制，目前锐龙<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>的功耗是<span lang="EN-US">65W</span>、<span lang="EN-US">95W</span>、<span lang="EN-US">105W</span>，<strong><span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>处理器则会增加<span lang="EN-US">120W</span>、<span lang="EN-US">170W TDP</span>的选择。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">但这还不是<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>的极限，这次还会有更高的<span lang="EN-US">PPT</span>（<span lang="EN-US">Package Power Tracking</span>）功耗，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>它跟<span lang="EN-US">TDP</span>的比例是<span lang="EN-US">1.35</span>：<span lang="EN-US">1</span>，也就是还可以再提高<span lang="EN-US">35%</span>的功耗空间，<span lang="EN-US">170W</span>的最多可以达到<span lang="EN-US">230W</span>功耗。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">从<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>提高功耗限制的情况来看，虽然可能导致实际功耗增加，但提高功耗上限也意味着<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>的性能更强大，频率会更高，目前<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">5000</span>也摸到了<span lang="EN-US">5.0GHz</span>的尾巴了，锐龙<span lang="EN-US">6000</span>系列轻松超过<span lang="EN-US">5GHz</span>上限。</p>
