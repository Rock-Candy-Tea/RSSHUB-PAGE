<div>   
<p>CES 2022大会上，Intel、AMD都发布了新一代笔记本移动平台，但新本上市还要一段时间，上手评测也还得等等。</p>
<p>近日，PCWorld有机会率先体验了Intel新旗舰i9-12900HK，并放出了跑分成绩。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220116/ccef91ec890b48f9bb0dd9064f1cd0bf.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="12代酷睿i9游戏本全球首测：57％优势领先上代锐龙9" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220116/s_ccef91ec890b48f9bb0dd9064f1cd0bf.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p><strong>测试笔记本是微星新款强袭GE76，模具基本没变，但加入了相变液金散热，还有RTX 3080显卡、32GB DDR5-4800内存，<span style="color:#ff0000;">显卡功耗最大释放从165W提高到175W。</span></strong></p>
<p>不过测试用本还是个工程样品，驱动、软件、固件都还不是最终版，成绩仅供参考。</p>
<p>i9-12900HK配置了6大8小14核心20线程，大核频率2.5-5.0GHz，小核频率1.8-3.8GHz，三级缓存24MB，集成96单元核显，基准功耗45W，默认最高释放115W。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220116/c4d50eae46e640268ab1ba266a10a582.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="12代酷睿i9游戏本全球首测：57％优势领先上代锐龙9" h="298" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220116/s_c4d50eae46e640268ab1ba266a10a582.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>对比笔记本一是微星现款强袭GE76，i9-11980HK处理器，<strong>RTX 3080显卡(165W)</strong>，32GB DDR4-3200内存，Extreme性能模式。</p>
<p>二是ROG枪神5 Plus，锐龙9 5900HX处理器，<strong>RTX 3080显卡(130W)</strong>，32GB DDR4-3200内存，Turbo性能模式。</p>
<p><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>当然，不同显卡功耗释放对比是不公平的，但我们重点看的是CPU性能，最后的游戏对比仅供参考。</strong></span></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220116/c91057da8058408d8d394b8b42dd72c0.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="12代酷睿i9游戏本全球首测：57％优势领先上代锐龙9" h="402" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220116/s_c91057da8058408d8d394b8b42dd72c0.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220116/4a6d3ac3daad4f81ac55673112ed0073.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="12代酷睿i9游戏本全球首测：57％优势领先上代锐龙9" h="406" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220116/s_4a6d3ac3daad4f81ac55673112ed0073.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220116/0006cc1f5baa4f6688527da9c02e2215.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="12代酷睿i9游戏本全球首测：57％优势领先上代锐龙9" h="393" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220116/s_0006cc1f5baa4f6688527da9c02e2215.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>先看一组<strong><span style="color:#ff0000;">单核性能，i9-12900HK比上代提升了15-30％，比竞品也领先27-28％。</span></strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220116/b18191c831b247aeae9f1e12fa0313c2.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="12代酷睿i9游戏本全球首测：57％优势领先上代锐龙9" h="398" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220116/s_b18191c831b247aeae9f1e12fa0313c2.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220116/51e238b4f1c940ca94ab8bd111dc4e7d.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="12代酷睿i9游戏本全球首测：57％优势领先上代锐龙9" h="405" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220116/s_51e238b4f1c940ca94ab8bd111dc4e7d.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220116/8273913e33f649cc9512c0ef328a1aed.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="12代酷睿i9游戏本全球首测：57％优势领先上代锐龙9" h="399" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220116/s_8273913e33f649cc9512c0ef328a1aed.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>再看<strong><span style="color:#ff0000;">多核性能，三个项目测试结果差异相当大，比上代提升16-41％，比竞品则领先23-57％。</span></strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220116/1cd90f7a6c8e40fdb5e23f217406fbe5.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="12代酷睿i9游戏本全球首测：57％优势领先上代锐龙9" h="401" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220116/s_1cd90f7a6c8e40fdb5e23f217406fbe5.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>CrossMark，Intel最近非常喜欢的项目，比上代提升21.5％，领先竞品36.2％。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220116/1e7bf2441aa24214af64c7e8f995f354.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="12代酷睿i9游戏本全球首测：57％优势领先上代锐龙9" h="406" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220116/s_1e7bf2441aa24214af64c7e8f995f354.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>UL Procyon Office是办公类测试，这次没有拉开差距，<strong>对比上代、竞品分别高出6.5％、5.0％。</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220116/be8e91dceba64da69c99ebbb31b5f1d9.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="12代酷睿i9游戏本全球首测：57％优势领先上代锐龙9" h="405" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220116/s_be8e91dceba64da69c99ebbb31b5f1d9.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>WebXprt 3里差距也不是很大，分别为9.5％、16.2％。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220116/19be999dc3d74d6f99848025fdeb65db.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="12代酷睿i9游戏本全球首测：57％优势领先上代锐龙9" h="413" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220116/s_19be999dc3d74d6f99848025fdeb65db.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220116/39cba00cd5b74534bb61903012ecfca4.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="12代酷睿i9游戏本全球首测：57％优势领先上代锐龙9" h="414" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220116/s_39cba00cd5b74534bb61903012ecfca4.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220116/f7297c89d31e401ca8fc950aeb412a74.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="12代酷睿i9游戏本全球首测：57％优势领先上代锐龙9" h="405" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220116/s_f7297c89d31e401ca8fc950aeb412a74.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p><span style="color:#000000;">游戏测试中，得益于更高的功耗释放，i9-12900HK全程近乎碾压，领先上代最多12％，领先竞品则最多近乎50％</span>。</p>
<p>当然，锐龙5000H的游戏性能确实也相对落后一些，就看锐龙6000H能不能迎头赶上了。</p>
