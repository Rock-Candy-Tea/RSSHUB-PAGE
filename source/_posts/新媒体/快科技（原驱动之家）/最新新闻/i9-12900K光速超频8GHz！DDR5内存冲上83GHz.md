<div>   
<p>Intel 12代酷睿尚未解禁上市，极限超频玩家已经玩嗨了，顶级高手HiCookie就将旗舰i9-12900K迅速超到了惊人的8GHz。</p>
<p>HWBOT的统计记录显示，<strong>10代酷睿i9-10900K、11代酷睿i9-11900K的最高超频记录分别只有7707MHz、7334MHz</strong>，都未突破8GHz大关，而且越来越低，14nm工艺显然扛不住了。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211103/64ee65abbfa74f40b094f80999fd7d67.png" target="_blank"><img alt="i9-12900K光速超频8GHz！DDR5内存冲上8.3GHz" h="402" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211103/s_64ee65abbfa74f40b094f80999fd7d67.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="403" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211103/46ed7dd937734c1e8035556629826a5d.png" target="_blank"><img alt="i9-12900K光速超频8GHz！DDR5内存冲上8.3GHz" h="402" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211103/s_46ed7dd937734c1e8035556629826a5d.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="403" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>12代酷睿用上了全新架构、Intel 7工艺(10nm加强版)，<strong><span style="color:#ff0000;">i9-12900K在液氮辅助下成功达到了8GHz，16个核心全部开启，CPU-Z检测显示核心电压已达1.812V。</span></strong></p>
<p>搭配内存是技嘉的一条DDR5 16GB，频率保持在默认的4800MHz，时序为40-39-39-76-115-2T。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211103/4d9a8fb07fc146b8950bcc7bc58d4274.png" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="i9-12900K光速超频8GHz！DDR5内存冲上8.3GHz" h="606" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211103/s_4d9a8fb07fc146b8950bcc7bc58d4274.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>收到内存，<strong><span style="color:#ff0000;">HiCookie还将这条技嘉DDR5超到了8.3GHz，对应时序放开到52-52-52-100-127-2T。</span></strong></p>
<p>搭配处理器还是i9-12900K，16核心全开，频率降到不足2GHz。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211103/294d548f12c8468aa9c38461538a1828.png" target="_blank"><img alt="i9-12900K光速超频8GHz！DDR5内存冲上8.3GHz" h="619" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211103/s_294d548f12c8468aa9c38461538a1828.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>这两次超频得到了技嘉的大力支持，<strong>主板都是技嘉Z690 AORUS TACHYON。</strong></p>
