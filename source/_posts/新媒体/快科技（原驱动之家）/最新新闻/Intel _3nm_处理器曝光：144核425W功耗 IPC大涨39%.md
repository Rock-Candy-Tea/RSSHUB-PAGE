<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年了，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿处理器布局也差不多了，今年还有<span lang="EN-US">13</span>代酷睿，未来的<span lang="EN-US">PPT</span>处理器可以看到<span lang="EN-US">17</span>代酷睿，至于服务器产品线中今年是<span lang="EN-US">Intel 7</span>工艺的<span lang="EN-US">Sapphire Rapids</span>处理器，也会上<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿同代的<span lang="EN-US">GoldenCove</span>核心架构。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">服务器处理器接下来如何走？<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>公布的信息不多，国外<span lang="EN-US">NXP</span>网站根据各方面的信息作了个推演，预估了<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>到<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年的至强处理器的规格及架构，如下所示：</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220121/c9d045d0-4953-4bdf-a8fa-b88f23ca7470.jpg" style="text-align: center;" target="_blank"><img alt="Intel “3nm”处理器曝光：144核425W功耗 IPC大涨39%" h="492" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220121/Sc9d045d0-4953-4bdf-a8fa-b88f23ca7470.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">从<span lang="EN-US">Sapphire Rapids</span>这一代开始，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的至强处理器都会走多芯片封装，今年及明年的至强都是<span lang="EN-US">4</span>个计算模块，每个最多<span lang="EN-US">16</span>核心，只不过<span lang="EN-US">Sapphire Rapids</span>只会启用<span lang="EN-US">56</span>个，明年的<span lang="EN-US">Emerald Rapids</span>系列会启用<span lang="EN-US">64</span>个核心。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">从<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年开始，<span lang="EN-US">Granite Rapids</span>系列的至强处理器会随着<span lang="EN-US">Intel 4</span>工艺（之前命名下的<span lang="EN-US">7nm+</span>工艺）的进步开始飙<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>核心，<span lang="EN-US">2</span>个计算模块就可以实现最多<span lang="EN-US">120</span>核，架构也升级为<span lang="EN-US">Redwood Cove</span>，<span lang="EN-US">IPC</span>性能提升<span lang="EN-US">35%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>到了<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年，至强处理器会升级到<span lang="EN-US">Diamond Rapids</span>系列，升级<span lang="EN-US">Intel 3</span>工艺，<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>核心数提升到<span lang="EN-US">144</span>个，架构升级为<span lang="EN-US">Lion Cove</span>，<span lang="EN-US">IPC</span>大涨<span lang="EN-US">39%</span>，支持的也是<span lang="EN-US">12</span>通道<span lang="EN-US">DDR6</span>内存，最大<span lang="EN-US">4TB</span>。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">当然，<span lang="EN-US">144</span>核的代价也不低，<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>功耗一路提升到了<span lang="EN-US">425W</span>，售价也达到了<span lang="EN-US">13000</span>美元，非常昂贵。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">从这个路线图来看，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的处理器会在<span lang="EN-US">Inte 4/3</span>节点实现一次飞跃，<strong>毕竟是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>首次使用<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>工艺，密度大幅提升，单个<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>核心数从现在的<span lang="EN-US">30</span>核左右翻倍到<span lang="EN-US">60</span>核甚至<span lang="EN-US">72</span>核以上，</strong>代价就是总功耗也飙升了，整体走的是大面积多核心高性能之路。</p>
