<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，红米发布了<span lang="EN-US">Redmi Note 11T</span>系列手机，这是<span lang="EN-US">Note</span>系列的性能小金刚，首发售价<span lang="EN-US">1699</span>元起，<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日开卖，现在小米之家跟顺丰同城达成了合作，平均<span lang="EN-US">1</span>小时就可以送达。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">顺丰同城发表消息称，<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，小米旗下品牌<span lang="EN-US">Redmi</span>发布两款智能手机新品<span lang="EN-US">Redmi Note 11T Pro</span>、<span lang="EN-US">Redmi Note 11T Pro+</span>，新品将于<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日开售，只要选择门店配送，即可享受<span lang="EN-US">3</span>公里内门店发货、顺丰同城极速配送上门的购物体验。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>同时，顺丰同城与小米之家已达成同城即配战略合作，服务目前已覆盖全国<span lang="EN-US">270+</span>城市、<span lang="EN-US">4200+</span>门店，在小米自有渠道下单后，实现<span lang="EN-US">“</span>平均<span lang="EN-US">1</span>小时<span lang="EN-US">”</span>即购即达。</strong></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center"><img alt="红米1699元性能小金刚即将开卖 “金牌快递”到位：1小时送达" h="286" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220526/b2fd378b-fcd9-4929-b428-50c4a914528f.png" style="border: black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Redmi Note 11T</span>系列共包含<span lang="EN-US">Redmi Note 11T Pro</span>和<span lang="EN-US"> Redmi Note 11T Pro+</span>两款手机。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Note 11T</span>系列搭载天玑<span lang="EN-US">8100</span>芯片，采用<span lang="EN-US">6.6</span>英寸<span lang="EN-US">LCD</span>屏幕，支持<span lang="EN-US">144Hz 7</span>档变速高刷，拥有<span lang="EN-US">20.5:9</span>修长比例，全程<span lang="EN-US">DC</span>调光<span lang="EN-US">+</span>定制背光灯防蓝光，原色屏<span lang="EN-US">+</span>环境色温感应<span lang="EN-US">+</span>杜比视界，是首款通过<span lang="EN-US">DisplayMate A+</span>认证的<span lang="EN-US">LCD</span>手机。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">影像方面，后置<span lang="EN-US">AI</span>三摄相机，<strong>包括<span lang="EN-US">6400</span>万像素主摄、<span lang="EN-US">800</span>万像素超广镜头、<span lang="EN-US">200</span>万像素微距镜头，并支持魔法分身、长曝光、移轴效果、电影模式等功能，可玩性更高。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">散热方面，<span lang="EN-US">Note 11T</span>系列搭载<span lang="EN-US">VC</span>液冷、<span lang="EN-US">7</span>层石墨组成的立体散热系统，<span lang="EN-US">VC</span>面积达到<span lang="EN-US">2268mm</span>，散热更加高效。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">续航方面，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">Note 11T Pro</span>内置<span lang="EN-US">5080mAh</span>电池，支持<span lang="EN-US">67W</span>闪充；<span lang="EN-US">Note 11T Pro</span>＋则内置<span lang="EN-US">4400mAh</span>电池，支持<span lang="EN-US">120W</span>闪充，</strong></span>采用小米自研澎湃<span lang="EN-US">P1</span>快充芯片，可轻松解决用户的续航焦虑。</p>
