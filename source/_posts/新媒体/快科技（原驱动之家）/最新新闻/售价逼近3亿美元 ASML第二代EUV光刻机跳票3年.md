<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">ASML</span>是全球唯一一家量产<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>光刻机的，台积电、三星、<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的<span lang="EN-US">7nm</span>、<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>及未来的<span lang="EN-US">3nm</span>、<span lang="EN-US">2nm</span>都要依赖<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>光刻机，单台售价超过<span lang="EN-US">1</span>亿美元，成本极高。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">ASML</span>的<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>光刻机目前使用的还是第一代，<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>光源波长在<span lang="EN-US">13.5nm</span>左右，物镜的<span lang="EN-US">NA</span>数值孔径是<span lang="EN-US">0.33</span>，发展了一系列型号。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">其中最早量产出厂的是<span lang="EN-US">NXE</span>：<span lang="EN-US">3400B</span>，产能有限，一小时生产晶圆是<span lang="EN-US">125PWH</span>，目前的出货主力是<span lang="EN-US">NXE:3400C</span>，产能提升到<span lang="EN-US">135WPH</span>，今年底还有<span lang="EN-US">NXE:3600D</span>系列出货，产能再进一步提升到<span lang="EN-US">160WPH</span>，不过价格也会提升到<span lang="EN-US">1.45</span>亿美元了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">现在第一代的<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>光刻机<span lang="EN-US">NA</span>指标太低，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>第二代<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>光刻机会是<span lang="EN-US">N XE</span>：<span lang="EN-US">5000</span>系列，其物镜的<span lang="EN-US">NA</span>将提升到<span lang="EN-US">0.55</span>，进一步提高光刻精度，半导体工艺突破<span lang="EN-US">1nm</span>工艺就要靠下一代光刻机了。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">然而<span lang="EN-US">NA 0.55</span>的二代<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>光刻机没那么容易，原本预计最快<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年问世，最新传闻称<span lang="EN-US">NXE:5000</span>系列跳票，<strong>而且一下子就跳票三年，要到<span lang="EN-US">2025-2026</span>年才有可能问世了。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不仅时间延期，<strong>二代<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>光刻机的价格也会大涨，预计轻松达到<span lang="EN-US">3</span>亿美元，</strong>是现有<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>光刻机的<span lang="EN-US">2-3</span>倍，这就意味着未来的芯片工艺成本极其昂贵，哪怕真能做到<span lang="EN-US">1nm</span>工艺，那高昂的成本也会让大多数公司退而却步。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">按照这样的发展下去，估计<span lang="EN-US">1nm</span>工艺的大客户就剩下苹果自己了。</p>
