<p class="MsoNormal">这几天<span lang="EN-US">AMD/NVIDIA</span>发布了新的显卡驱动，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>这边也没闲着，这次发布了<span lang="EN-US">30.0.100.9667 Beta</span>版驱动，还是首次进入<span lang="EN-US">30</span>系列，目前正在进行<span lang="EN-US">beta</span>测试。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">做为<span lang="EN-US">30</span>系首个驱动，升级的功能及改进的<span lang="EN-US">bug</span>还真不少，首先是新游戏支持<strong>，<span lang="EN-US">30.0.100.9667 Beta</span>版驱动主要为游戏《<span lang="EN-US">Chivalry 2</span>》提供支持</strong>，并修复了《堡垒之夜》的阴影问题。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在<span lang="EN-US">Linux</span>系统上，新驱动还还升级了<span lang="EN-US">Windows Subsystem for Linux (WSL)</span>驱动。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Bug</span>修复方面，主要针对<span lang="EN-US">11</span>代酷睿的<span lang="EN-US">Xe</span>架构显卡，<strong>修复了多个游戏在<span lang="EN-US">DX12</span>下的图形显示或者崩溃问题</strong>，还有就是准<span lang="EN-US">5K</span>分辨率下的黑屏，具体如下：</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong><span lang="EN-US">30.0.100.9667</span>版驱动<span lang="EN-US">Bug</span>修复列表：</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">1</span>、<span lang="EN-US"> Iris Xe MAX</span>显卡：《堡垒之夜》图形显示异常。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2</span>、<span lang="EN-US"> Iris Xe MAX</span>显卡：《刺客信条：英灵殿》和《漫威复仇者》在<span lang="EN-US">DX12</span>模式下会出现程序崩溃或无响应。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">3</span>、《赛博朋克<span lang="EN-US">2077</span>》在<span lang="EN-US">DX12</span>模式下会出现图形错误。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">4</span>、分辨率设置为<span lang="EN-US">5120x2160@50Hz</span>时会导致黑屏。</p>
