<div>   
<p>今年早些时候，曾有用户为Redmi品牌总经理卢伟冰留言，希望K50能够推出512GB版本。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220524/6bdd781017a94750aad99862af734869.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="12+512GB超大容量！Redmi K50新版本来了：到手价2899元" h="115" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220524/s_6bdd781017a94750aad99862af734869.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>在今天的发布会上，Redmi官方正式宣布，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>K50将推出12+512GB的超大容量版本，618期间到手价2899元，5月26日早10点开启预售。</strong></span></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220524/b32f7a5d12be4f95be9f7fc093e30a63.png" target="_blank"><img alt="12+512GB超大容量！Redmi K50新版本来了：到手价2899元" h="536" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220524/s_b32f7a5d12be4f95be9f7fc093e30a63.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>除了用户中呼声颇高的512GB存储空间版本外，<strong>在此次发布会上，K50系列还推出了纯白的“晴雪”配色，618特惠价2399元起。</strong></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220524/5c396efd403a457294157da2d191fa35.png" target="_blank"><img alt="12+512GB超大容量！Redmi K50新版本来了：到手价2899元" h="533" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220524/s_5c396efd403a457294157da2d191fa35.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>作为Redmi K系列的新品，K50搭载了一颗天玑8100处理器，<strong>该芯片采用台积电5nm工艺，由4颗Cortex A78大核和4颗Cortex A55小核组成。</strong></p>
<p>除此之外，K50还采用了一块6.67英寸的三星2K AMOLED柔性直屏，后置4800万主摄、800万超广角和200万微距，支持OIS光学防抖，电池为5500mAh，支持67W快充。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220524/1c3ca68232b241ada3db1f6460727866.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="12+512GB超大容量！Redmi K50新版本来了：到手价2899元" h="800" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220524/s_1c3ca68232b241ada3db1f6460727866.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
