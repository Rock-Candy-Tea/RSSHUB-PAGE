<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">今天凌晨的<span lang="EN-US">CES</span>发布会上，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>一股脑推出了锐龙<span lang="EN-US">6000</span>系列处理器、<span lang="EN-US">RX 6000M/S</span>显卡、<span lang="EN-US">Zen3 V-Cache</span>加成的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7 5800X3D</span>，<a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/807/807319.htm" target="_blank">还官方展示了<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>处理器</a>，命名为锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>系列，今年下半年发布。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>是<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>未来几年的重点，因为这一代不仅升级<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>工艺、<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>全新架构，<strong>还会首发<span lang="EN-US">AM5</span>插槽，变成<span lang="EN-US">LGA1718</span>阵脚，放弃<span lang="EN-US">PGA</span>插槽，支持<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>及<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>等新技术。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">对于<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>处理器，大家最关心的自然是其性能有多强，毕竟<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>去年推出的<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">IPC</span>提升明显，今年还有加强版的<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿处理器，<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>压力不小。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="升级AM5插槽 AMD演示5nm Zen4性能：光环游戏中全核5GHz" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220105/3e9bddbf-2056-4752-88f8-87617131ee57.png" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此前爆料称<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>的<span lang="EN-US">IPC</span>性能提升<span lang="EN-US">15%</span>甚至<span lang="EN-US">20%</span>以上，更夸张的说法也有，但无法证实，今天<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>官方明确的性能信息只有一点——<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>在光环游戏最新作<span lang="EN-US">Halo Infinite</span>中，<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>可以做到全核<span lang="EN-US">5GHz</span>运行。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>没有透露具体是多少核，但是以<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>的能力，<span lang="EN-US">4</span>核<span lang="EN-US">5GHz</span>对它来说毫无意义，<strong><span lang="EN-US">8</span>核<span lang="EN-US">5GHz</span>是有可能的，<span lang="EN-US">12</span>核<span lang="EN-US">5GHz</span>甚至<span lang="EN-US">16</span>核<span lang="EN-US">5GHz</span>则是超预期的。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此外，全核<span lang="EN-US">5GHz</span>玩游戏，<strong>意味着单核加速频率会高于<span lang="EN-US">5GHz</span>，</strong>如果能冲击<span lang="EN-US">5.5GHz</span>的高频，那么今年底的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>对战<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的<span lang="EN-US">12/13</span>代酷睿就更有胜算了。</p>
