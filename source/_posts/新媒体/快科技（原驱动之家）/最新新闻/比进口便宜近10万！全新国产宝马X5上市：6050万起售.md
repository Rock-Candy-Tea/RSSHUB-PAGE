<div>   
<p>3月31日晚，国产宝马X5正式上市，新车共推出四款车型，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>售价区间为60.50-77.50万元</strong></span>，起售价相比于进口车型（69.99万元）便宜了近10万元！</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220331/19c545b341da4ee8b4a071d01cd40bbc.png" target="_blank"><img alt="比进口便宜近10万！全新国产宝马X5上市：60.50万起售" h="327" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220331/s_19c545b341da4ee8b4a071d01cd40bbc.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220331/65615ca3cc0947539e53a1244a1208d5.png" target="_blank"><img alt="比进口便宜近10万！全新国产宝马X5上市：60.50万起售" h="318" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220331/s_65615ca3cc0947539e53a1244a1208d5.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>外观来看，国产宝马X5整体和进口标轴版变化不大，用上了宝马家族化的“大鼻孔”进气格栅、配有LED矩阵式大灯、并提供M运动套件，在两侧进口增加了一个新样式的镀铬饰条，用来强化豪华属性。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220331/99194e5b3c3a431d9bf6c9e7d79b21ee.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="比进口便宜近10万！全新国产宝马X5上市：60.50万起售" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220331/s_99194e5b3c3a431d9bf6c9e7d79b21ee.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220331/ca30a87168e84f869b7f31e54501d78b.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="比进口便宜近10万！全新国产宝马X5上市：60.50万起售" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220331/s_ca30a87168e84f869b7f31e54501d78b.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>新车内饰同样延续进口车型的设计，包括经典的三辐式方向盘、全液晶仪表和12.3英寸中控屏等均得以保留，<strong>并且还配备了宝马专属的水晶三件套</strong>（水晶挡把、水晶多媒体旋钮、水晶一键启动按键）。</p>
<p>此外，像是实木（或者仿木纹）装饰件、酒红色打孔真皮、哈曼卡顿品牌音响、多色车内氛围灯也均有配备，豪华感十足。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220331/267b4496d7a9401da476e46c1749c61f.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="比进口便宜近10万！全新国产宝马X5上市：60.50万起售" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220331/s_267b4496d7a9401da476e46c1749c61f.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>国产X5最大的亮点在于<strong>其加长了130毫米轴距，达到了3105毫米</strong>，与大哥宝马X7保持一致。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220331/a73cf9fc542e416395906267a500593d.png" target="_blank"><img alt="比进口便宜近10万！全新国产宝马X5上市：60.50万起售" h="327" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220331/s_a73cf9fc542e416395906267a500593d.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220331/77630c05384b4a49af515e4038f26cc0.png" target="_blank"><img alt="比进口便宜近10万！全新国产宝马X5上市：60.50万起售" h="353" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220331/s_77630c05384b4a49af515e4038f26cc0.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>动力部分，国产X5有两款动力，分别为X5 xDrive30Li以及X5 xDrive40Li，搭载B48 2.0T四缸发动机以及B58 3.0T六缸发动机，两款车型均匹配8AT变速箱，后者零百加速仅6秒。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220331/7f44e092d9af4f04ab1da72330ec8472.png" target="_blank"><img alt="比进口便宜近10万！全新国产宝马X5上市：60.50万起售" h="321" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220331/s_7f44e092d9af4f04ab1da72330ec8472.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>其中，<strong>30Li申报功率为180kW，40Li申报功率为245kW</strong>；而B48 2.0T高功率发动机已经国产，B58的3.0T六缸发动机，则由进口至国内组装后装车。</p>
<p>与老对手奥迪Q7和奔驰GLE相比，国产宝马X5占尽了先发优势：<strong>轴距最长、免关税后价格最低</strong>，上市后势必会从它们手中夺取不少的客户。</p>
