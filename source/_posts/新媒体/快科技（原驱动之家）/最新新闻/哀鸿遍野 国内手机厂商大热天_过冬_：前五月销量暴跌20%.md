<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">此前多家分析机构都表示<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年智能手机市场形势严峻，爆料大神郭明錤之前透露安卓厂商今年砍单<span lang="EN-US">1.7</span>亿部，现在中国信通院公布的数据也显示了手机市场上的惨淡，前<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月的手机出货量暴跌<span lang="EN-US">20%</span>以上。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据信通院公布的最新数据，<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月国内市场手机出货量<span lang="EN-US">2080.5</span>万部，同比下降<span lang="EN-US">9.4%</span>。其中，<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>手机<span lang="EN-US">1773.9</span>万部，同比增长<span lang="EN-US">6.0%</span>，占同期手机出货量的<span lang="EN-US">85.3%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">1-5</span>月，国内市场手机总体出货量累计<span lang="EN-US">1.08</span>亿部，同比下降<span lang="EN-US">27.1%。</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">其中，<strong><span style="color:#ff0000;"><span lang="EN-US">5G</span>手机出货量<span lang="EN-US">8620.7</span>万部，同比下降<span lang="EN-US">20.2%</span></span>，</strong>占同期手机出货量的<span lang="EN-US">79.7%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">品牌方面，<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月，国产品牌手机出货量<span lang="EN-US">1660.0</span>万部，同比下降<span lang="EN-US">14.0%</span>，占同期手机出货量的<span lang="EN-US">79.8%</span>，上市新机型<span lang="EN-US">23</span>款，同比下降<span lang="EN-US">8.0%</span>，占同期手机上市新机型数量的<span lang="EN-US">95.8%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1-5</span>月，国产品牌手机出货量累计<span lang="EN-US">9067.7</span>万部，同比下降<span lang="EN-US">30.6%</span>，占同期手机出货量的<span lang="EN-US">83.8%;</span>上市新机型累计<span lang="EN-US">160</span>款，同比下降<span lang="EN-US">3.6%</span>，占同期手机上市新机型数量的<span lang="EN-US">95.8%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">智能手机方面，<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月，智能手机出货量<span lang="EN-US">2055.9</span>万部，同比下降<span lang="EN-US">9.1%</span>，占同期手机出货量的<span lang="EN-US">98.8%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong><span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1-5</span>月，智能手机出货量<span lang="EN-US">1.06</span>亿部，同比下降<span lang="EN-US">27.0%</span></strong>，占同期手机出货量的<span lang="EN-US">98.2%</span>。</p>
