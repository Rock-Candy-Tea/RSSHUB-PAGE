<p class="MsoNormal">今年下半年<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>就要发布代号<span lang="EN-US">Alder Lake</span>的<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿了，升级<span lang="EN-US">10nm</span>工艺及<span lang="EN-US">Golden Cove</span>架构，首次使用大小核架构，最多<span lang="EN-US">8+8</span>核心<span lang="EN-US">24</span>线程，还会支持<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>及全新<span lang="EN-US">LGA1700</span>插槽。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">旗舰自然是酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i9-12900K</span>，此前信息显示，它采用<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个大核心、<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个小核心，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">QS</span>样品基准频率<span lang="EN-US">3.9GHz</span>、加速频率已经高达<span lang="EN-US">5.3GHz</span>，性能提升很明显，多核性能小幅超越<span lang="EN-US">16</span>核心的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">9 5950X</span>。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">除了性能大涨之外，<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿的价格还是最引人关注的，最新爆料显示国内已经有人偷跑了酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i9-12900K</span>处理器，<strong>价格在<span lang="EN-US">6900-7500</span>元，</strong>美元价格约为<span lang="EN-US">1065-1158</span>美元之间。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">通常来说，这种偷跑出来的<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>价格比较贵，实际价格可能在<span lang="EN-US">1000</span>美元内，而锐龙<span lang="EN-US">9 5950X</span>的建议价是<span lang="EN-US">799</span>美元，<strong><span lang="EN-US">i9-12900K</span>绝不会比这个便宜，预计售价是在<span lang="EN-US">800-1000</span>美元左右。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">当然，实际价格超过<span lang="EN-US">1000</span>美元，甚至达到<span lang="EN-US">1200</span>美元也不是没可能，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的<span lang="EN-US">10nm</span>成本不便宜，性能还有一定优势的情况下，价格肯定要涨。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">但是这还不是<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿的最大麻烦，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>爆料者结成安穗表示主板的价格比<span lang="EN-US">U</span>还贵</strong></span>——<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿升级<span lang="EN-US">LGA1700</span>插槽，搭配<span lang="EN-US">700</span>系芯片组，估计<span lang="EN-US">Z790</span>主板也不会便宜。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">再考虑到<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿还要上<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存，首发上市的价格也会比<span lang="EN-US">DDR4</span>贵不少，这样一来今年底组建<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿平台的价格会非常高，高端平台预计不会低于<span lang="EN-US">1.5</span>万元，尝鲜的代价不小。</p>
