<p class="MsoNormal">过去几年中，台积电成为全球工艺最先进的半导体制造公司，这也让<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>公司倍感压力，究其根源其实是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>这几年在<span lang="EN-US">14nm</span>到<span lang="EN-US">10nm</span>的换代上浪费了时间，<span lang="EN-US">14nm</span>工艺用了<span lang="EN-US">6</span>年才升级到下一代。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">对半导体制造来说，如期推出新一代工艺升级是非常关键的一步，不然错一代就会导致后面的工艺也跟着受影响，在这方面台积电的新工艺表现良好，基本上都是按照<span lang="EN-US">2-3</span>年升级一代，同代工艺不停改进来满足了客户需求。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="14nm牙膏式升级不再有 Intel 18A工艺被专家点赞：靠谱！" h="292" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220418/1d5ced2a-fb7c-46bc-aec5-3f025945ca6e.png" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">好消息是，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>在经历过之前<span lang="EN-US">14nm</span>到<span lang="EN-US">10nm</span>的阵痛之后，接下来也要进入一个爆发期了，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>他们之前公布的路线图中，要在<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年之前的<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年中连续量产<span lang="EN-US">5</span>代工艺，包括<span lang="EN-US">Intel 7</span>、<span lang="EN-US">Intel 4</span>、<span lang="EN-US">Intel 3</span>及<span lang="EN-US">20A</span>、<span lang="EN-US">18A</span>工艺，后两代还会上<span lang="EN-US">GAA</span>晶体管技术。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不仅技术升级，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>这次的量产时间也稳定多了，<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月份<span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: "Open Sans", sans-serif; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">Inte CEO</span><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">基辛格表示</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: "Open Sans", sans-serif; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">Intel</span><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">的先进工艺进展都是超预期的，其中</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: "Open Sans", sans-serif; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">18A</span><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">工艺提前量产半年，在</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: "Open Sans", sans-serif; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">2024</span><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">年下半年就能量产，并且还有</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: "Open Sans", sans-serif; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">2</span><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">个代工客户了。</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: "Open Sans", sans-serif; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;"></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">对于Intel这次的表现，<strong>行业分析师及芯片专家David Kanter也对Intel表示祝贺，认为Intel这一次提前量产还是很靠谱的。</strong></p>
