<p class="MsoNormal">在闪存市场上，三星、<span lang="EN-US">SK</span>海力士、东芝、西数、美光、<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>等美日韩公司占据全球主导，国内的长江存储在<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年首次量产<span lang="EN-US">64</span>层<span lang="EN-US">3D</span>闪存，现在<span lang="EN-US">128</span>层<span lang="EN-US">TLC</span>闪存也量产了，这与三星等国际巨头的差距缩小到了<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">长江存储是国内最主要的闪存研发制造公司，进入这个行业的时间不长，<strong><span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年才开始研发<span lang="EN-US">3D</span>闪存，<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年设计出了<span lang="EN-US">32</span>层<span lang="EN-US">3D</span>闪存，<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年首次流片<span lang="EN-US">64</span>层闪存，<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>季度才正式量产。</strong></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="国产128层TLC闪存已量产 与三星技术差距缩短到两年" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210803/bfe579e0-9c4a-4dbb-ad5f-cc17dca1ff68.jpg" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">但是长江存储追赶的速度很快，<span lang="EN-US">32</span>层及之前的闪存都是小规模试验，<span lang="EN-US">64</span>层<span lang="EN-US">TLC</span>才开始规模量产，并自主研发了全新的<span lang="EN-US">Xtacking</span>堆栈架构，同时还跳过了<span lang="EN-US">96</span>层闪存，直接进入到<span lang="EN-US">128</span>层闪存，去年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月份正式发布。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">长江存储表示，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">128</span>层<span lang="EN-US">QLC 3D</span>闪存（<span lang="EN-US">X2-6070</span>）是业内首款<span lang="EN-US">128</span>层<span lang="EN-US">QLC</span>规格<span lang="EN-US">3D NAND</span>，且拥有已知型号产品中最高单位面积存储密度，最高<span lang="EN-US">I/O</span>传输速度和最高单颗<span lang="EN-US">NAND</span>闪存芯片容量。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">值得一提的是，此次同步登场的还有<span lang="EN-US">128</span>层<span lang="EN-US">TLC</span>闪存（<span lang="EN-US">X2-9060</span>），单颗容量<span lang="EN-US">512Gb(64GB)</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">性能方面，长存透露，两款产品拥有<span lang="EN-US">1.6Gbps</span>的<span lang="EN-US">I/O</span>读写性能，<span lang="EN-US">3D QLC</span>单颗容量高达<span lang="EN-US">1.33Tb</span>，是上一代<span lang="EN-US">64</span>层的<span lang="EN-US">5.33</span>倍。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">据悉，两款产品均采用长存自研<span lang="EN-US">Xtacking 2.0</span>架构，外围电路和存储单元可堆叠，带来极佳的扩展性。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">原本<span lang="EN-US">128</span>层闪存在去年底应该就量产了，不过因为疫情等原因，<strong>最近才有厂商开始宣传<span lang="EN-US">128</span>层闪存的<span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>，意味着长江存储终于开始量产<span lang="EN-US">128</span>层闪存了。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">与国际水平相比，三星、<span lang="EN-US">SK</span>海力士都是在<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年中才量产<span lang="EN-US">128</span>层闪存的，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>这意味着长江存储与国际水平的差距已经缩短到了<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年，</strong></span>进度值得称赞。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">当然，三星、<span lang="EN-US">SK</span>海力士及西数、美光等公司今年已经开始量产<span lang="EN-US">176</span>层到<span lang="EN-US">192</span>层的闪存了，国产差距还是有的，但是追赶的进度很快。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">接下来长江存储面临的考验还是大规模量产，确保产能及良率稳步爬升，也会有越来越多的<span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>用上国产闪存。</p>
