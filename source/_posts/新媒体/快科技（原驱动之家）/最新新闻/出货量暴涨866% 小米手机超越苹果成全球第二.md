<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">7</span>月中旬，市场调研公司<span lang="EN-US">Canalys</span>发布报告称，小米首次超越苹果成为全球手机市场第二。现在<span lang="EN-US">IDC</span>也发布<span lang="EN-US">Q2</span>全球手机报告了，确认了小米超越苹果，全球排名第二，出货量大涨<span lang="EN-US">86.6%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据<span lang="EN-US">IDC</span>发布的季度手机追踪报告，<span lang="EN-US">Q2</span>季度中三星以<span lang="EN-US">5900</span>万台的出货量位列第一，市场份额<span lang="EN-US">18.8%</span>，出货量同比增长<span lang="EN-US">9.3%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>小米以<span lang="EN-US">5310</span>万台的出货量位列第二，首次挤下苹果，份额<span lang="EN-US">16.9%</span>，与三星差距不到<span lang="EN-US">2</span>个百分点，出货量同比增长<span lang="EN-US">86,6%</span>，增速是前五名中最猛的。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">苹果以<span lang="EN-US">4420</span>万台的出货量位列第三，份额<span lang="EN-US">14.1%</span>，出货量同比增长了<span lang="EN-US">17.8%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">OPPO</span>以<span lang="EN-US">3280</span>万台的出货量位列第四，份额<span lang="EN-US">10.5%</span>，出货量同比增长<span lang="EN-US">37%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">vivo</span>则以<span lang="EN-US">3160</span>万台的出货量位列第五，份额<span lang="EN-US">10.1%</span>，出货量同比增长<span lang="EN-US">33.7%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">这五大品牌占了全球<span lang="EN-US">71%</span>的份额，其他厂商合计出货<span lang="EN-US">9240</span>万台，全部品牌合计出货<span lang="EN-US">3.13</span>亿台，同比增长了<span lang="EN-US">13.2%</span>。</p>
