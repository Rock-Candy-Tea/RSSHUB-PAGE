<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">在过去几年中，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>在<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>性能上一直保持单核<span lang="EN-US">/</span>游戏上的领先记录，但<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的<span lang="EN-US">Zen3</span>架构锐龙<span lang="EN-US">5000</span>逆转了这一点，首次做到了单核、多核性能同时领先，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>酷睿丢掉了最快游戏<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>的称号。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">对于这一情况，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>显然是不服的，接下来要追回来，而且更加疯狂。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Alder Lake</span>不仅升级<span lang="EN-US">Intel 7</span>工艺，还会用上大小核架构，桌面版最多<span lang="EN-US">8</span>大<span lang="EN-US">8</span>小，总计<span lang="EN-US">16</span>核<span lang="EN-US">24</span>线程，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>旗舰酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i7-12900K</span>的<span lang="EN-US">GK5</span>跑分已经泄露，多核比前代大涨<span lang="EN-US">57%</span>，也超过了锐龙<span lang="EN-US">9 5950X</span>。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">单核性能上，<span lang="EN-US">Alder Lake</span>比目前的<span lang="EN-US">11</span>代酷睿提升不大，比锐龙<span lang="EN-US">9 5950X</span>高出<span lang="EN-US">12%</span>——这个性能优势是暂时的，<strong>因为<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>今年底还会推出缓存加强版的<span lang="EN-US">Zen3</span>，明年还会有<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>，单核性能很有可能再次逆转。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/777/777476.htm" target="_blank"><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>这边只能靠<span lang="EN-US">13</span>代酷睿了</a>，代号<span lang="EN-US">Raptor Lake，</span>大核心架构升级为<span lang="EN-US">Raptor Cove</span>，最多还是<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个，小核心架构延续<span lang="EN-US">Gracemont</span>，最多翻番至<span lang="EN-US">16</span>个，总计<span lang="EN-US">24</span>核<span lang="EN-US">32</span>线程。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">13</span>代酷睿的多核性能领先应该没悬念，单核性能则会继续加强，除了<span lang="EN-US">IPC</span>优化之外，<strong>频率也会冲到<span lang="EN-US">5.5GHz</span>，比当前最快的频率还要再高<span lang="EN-US">200MHz</span>。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">多核性能已经够强大了，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>为什么会如此疯狂追逐单核性能？此前的爆料分析认为<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>被<span lang="EN-US">Zen3</span>刺激了一波，一心想着给<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>还以颜色，<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿首次上新工艺，单核优化还不够，<span lang="EN-US">13</span>代酷睿则会挖掘极限，架构及频率双双提升，一定要在单核上领先<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>。</p>
