<p class="MsoNormal">最近又到了财报季，被股民称为中国<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>的国产<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>公司景嘉微日前公布了<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年业绩预告，预计<span lang="EN-US"> 2021 </span>年年度实现营业收入<span lang="EN-US">108,000</span>万元<span lang="EN-US">-120,00</span>万元，上年同期营业收入为<span lang="EN-US">65,377.21</span>万元，较上年同期增长<span lang="EN-US"> 65.20%-83.55%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">归属于上市公司股东的净利润在<span lang="EN-US">27,000</span>万元<span lang="EN-US">-32,000</span>万元，比上年同期增长<span lang="EN-US">30.27%-54.39%</span>。扣除非经常性损益后的净利润为<span lang="EN-US">24,100</span>万元<span lang="EN-US">-29,100</span>万元，比上年同期增长<span lang="EN-US">27.85%-54.38%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">除了业绩增长之外，国产<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>是最大看点。<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，景嘉微发布了<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>旗舰新品<span lang="EN-US">JH920</span>，<strong><span lang="EN-US">JH920</span>采用<span lang="EN-US">14nm</span>工艺，关键指标上，该芯片像素填充率为<span lang="EN-US">32G Pixels/s</span>，单精度浮点性能为<span lang="EN-US">1.5TFlops</span>，功耗为<span lang="EN-US">30W</span>。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">与之可比的是<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>的<span lang="EN-US">GeForce GTX 1050</span>，该<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>同样采用<span lang="EN-US">14nm</span>工艺，像素填充率为<span lang="EN-US">36.43G Pixels/s</span>，浮点性能为<span lang="EN-US">1.733TFlops</span>，功耗为<span lang="EN-US">75W</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>不过<span lang="EN-US">GTX 1050</span>显卡是<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年推出的了，景嘉微的国产<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>落后了<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年多，好处是功耗低了一半多。</strong></span></p>
