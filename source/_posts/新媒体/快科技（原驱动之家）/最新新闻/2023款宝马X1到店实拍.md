<p>根据到店实拍可知，新款X1分为七种配置，不要三缸车则汽油车里只有23i可选（对应于国内的25i，带48V轻混），零百加速七秒：</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220912/7b834781af6b44edb75f1c4da53705a3.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="2023款宝马X1到店实拍" h="347" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220912/s_7b834781af6b44edb75f1c4da53705a3.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>想飙直道，则有纯电X1可选：62千瓦时电池，313马力@500牛·米，零百成绩5秒6。此外还有两款插混（配备14.2或16.3千瓦时电池）以及两款柴油车可选，但后者国内不提供。</p>
<p>新款X1与老款相比，除外观显著改善外，还解决了几个长期以来X1客户所诟病的配置问题。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220912/919dec296ca14e809a6a119ffa8ca677.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="2023款宝马X1到店实拍" h="387" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220912/s_919dec296ca14e809a6a119ffa8ca677.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220912/1719a3213afc42ac98cc97bae114cf7d.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="2023款宝马X1到店实拍" h="335" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220912/s_1719a3213afc42ac98cc97bae114cf7d.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220912/d748772fa5504acda730196319772693.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="2023款宝马X1到店实拍" h="322" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220912/s_d748772fa5504acda730196319772693.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>新X1可选20寸轮毂（前后均为245/40 R20），这辐条样式放在七系上都不违和：</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220912/409d64a9cd37421fb5cde809522ca57f.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="2023款宝马X1到店实拍" h="386" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220912/s_409d64a9cd37421fb5cde809522ca57f.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>可选抬头显示，老X1即便顾客肯花钱也得不到HUD：</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220912/9cde7988428a477db9e17ceae71f9510.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="2023款宝马X1到店实拍" h="373" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220912/s_9cde7988428a477db9e17ceae71f9510.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>可选ACC自适应巡航，进气格栅可自动开闭：</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220912/ffbc37c2321d4c95a83b1e02d9429c68.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="2023款宝马X1到店实拍" h="401" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220912/s_ffbc37c2321d4c95a83b1e02d9429c68.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>毫米波雷达探头与车头进气格栅融为一体，与三、四、五系那种单独一大坨的样式相比更具设计感。</p>
<p>新X1采用内抠式门把手，除了不好用剩下的都是优点：</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220912/e0012dee811b405298836f9805024722.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="2023款宝马X1到店实拍" h="328" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220912/s_e0012dee811b405298836f9805024722.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>展车选配了M包围与黑色高光套件，丐车不可能这么好看：</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220912/5eb3b3cd7c03417aa07be4b2d9d4e182.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="2023款宝马X1到店实拍" h="343" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220912/s_5eb3b3cd7c03417aa07be4b2d9d4e182.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220912/15143e30906b42dbb25c66089bd1b0d3.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="2023款宝马X1到店实拍" h="357" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220912/s_15143e30906b42dbb25c66089bd1b0d3.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>受制于车身尺寸，行李箱容量在不放倒第二排座椅的情况下只有540升：</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220912/8c5eda63926a48a0a1d65f2c934ceaaf.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="2023款宝马X1到店实拍" h="310" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220912/s_8c5eda63926a48a0a1d65f2c934ceaaf.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>行李箱盖板下还有一个深坑，也许能放下折叠的童车：</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220912/f27bfab8b9c34b8dbb963871a36b3805.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="2023款宝马X1到店实拍" h="323" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220912/s_f27bfab8b9c34b8dbb963871a36b3805.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>国产化后，行李箱内部挂钩与左右耳朵网兜这种人性化附件通常会消失：</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220912/81f796030781401ba66d7c2bc74d3e6b.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="2023款宝马X1到店实拍" h="327" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220912/s_81f796030781401ba66d7c2bc74d3e6b.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>驾驶员侧门内饰板，比现款讲究多了：</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220912/a2aa0ce303f4431cb5ad2e10e5f872b4.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="2023款宝马X1到店实拍" h="359" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220912/s_a2aa0ce303f4431cb5ad2e10e5f872b4.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>缩水款曲面联屏，空调只能触控，无实体键：</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220912/6fc110541c1d4e2b834bde50fd89655f.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="2023款宝马X1到店实拍" h="395" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220912/s_6fc110541c1d4e2b834bde50fd89655f.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220912/6370a8558b99402f9c5b32700606bb09.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="2023款宝马X1到店实拍" h="347" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220912/s_6370a8558b99402f9c5b32700606bb09.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>立式手机无线充电板，带弹性夹紧装置：</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220912/e05b969005a3424da90d8acea4650dd7.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="2023款宝马X1到店实拍" h="358" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220912/s_e05b969005a3424da90d8acea4650dd7.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>再无大鸡腿之争，除M性能车外所有车系所有款型最终都会采用该样式拨杆，档次靠卜拎卜拎来区分。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220912/67c835d3faf14c84a2b95e80804d2b2b.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="2023款宝马X1到店实拍" h="360" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220912/s_67c835d3faf14c84a2b95e80804d2b2b.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>中央扶手箱只能放一套筷子：</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220912/1f0ac427a928479ea63e691642b2f67c.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="2023款宝马X1到店实拍" h="330" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220912/s_1f0ac427a928479ea63e691642b2f67c.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>座椅前后均为Alcantara材料，宝马正在逐步摒弃皮革——按船长的话来说，坐在一头死牛的屁股上有什么好？</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220912/a8304858824640e5ac7760597a938b35.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="2023款宝马X1到店实拍" h="328" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220912/s_a8304858824640e5ac7760597a938b35.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>

<p>对一台精致小车最起码的要求是（排名有先后）：无线CarPlay、名牌音响、HUD抬头显示、电尾门、AutoHold、座椅加热与方向盘加热（北方）、座椅通风（南方），无线充电……，新款X1做到了其中绝大部分——尤其是前三项，竞争力较老款X1高了一头。</p>
