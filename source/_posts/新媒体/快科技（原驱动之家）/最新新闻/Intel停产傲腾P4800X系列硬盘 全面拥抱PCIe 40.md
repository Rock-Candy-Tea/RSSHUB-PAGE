<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">去年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月份<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>宣布<span lang="EN-US">90</span>亿美元将闪存业务出售给<span lang="EN-US">SK</span>海力士，不过<span lang="EN-US">Optane</span>傲腾业务保留，这是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的看家神技，基于傲腾的存储产品将主打数据中心市场。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">现在<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>进一步调整了傲腾数据中心产品线，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">DC P4800X</span>系列傲腾硬盘开始停产、退役，最后的订单日是<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日是最后的出货日。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">涉及的<span lang="EN-US">P4800X</span>系列产品有<span lang="EN-US">10</span>多款，<span lang="EN-US">U.2</span>、<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 3.0</span>都有，容量涉及<span lang="EN-US">375GB</span>、<span lang="EN-US">750GB</span>及<span lang="EN-US">1.5TB</span>多种。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">DC P4800X</span>系列傲腾硬盘发布于<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月份，持续读写最高<span lang="EN-US">2.4GB/s</span>、<span lang="EN-US">2GB/s</span>，随机读写最高<span lang="EN-US">55</span>万<span lang="EN-US">IOPS</span>、<span lang="EN-US">50</span>万<span lang="EN-US">IOPS</span>、读写延迟<span lang="EN-US">10</span>微秒，但不知功耗是否会加大<span lang="EN-US">(375GB</span>的读写状态<span lang="EN-US">18W/</span>待机状态<span lang="EN-US">5W)</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">新版依旧提供<span lang="EN-US">PCI-E</span>半高半长扩展卡、<span lang="EN-US">U.2 2.5</span>寸<span lang="EN-US">/15</span>毫米盘两种样式，支持<span lang="EN-US">PCI-E 3.0 x4</span>、<span lang="EN-US">NVMe</span>，容量最高<span lang="EN-US">1.5TB</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">P4800X</span>系列停产主要原因还是有新产品替代了，<a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/730/730001.htm" target="_blank">去年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月份<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>发布了基于新一代傲腾存储介质的数据中心固态盘<span lang="EN-US">SSD P5800X</span></a>，号称同类产品世界最快，而且在寿命、延迟等方面依旧轻松秒杀<span lang="EN-US">NAND</span>闪存。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">该系列采用<span lang="EN-US">2.5</span>英寸<span lang="EN-US">U.2</span>形态，容量可选<span lang="EN-US">400GB</span>、<span lang="EN-US">800GB</span>、<span lang="EN-US">1.6TB</span>、<span lang="EN-US">3.2TB</span>，支持<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 4.0</span>，持续读写性能最高<span lang="EN-US">7.2GB/s</span>、<span lang="EN-US">6.2GB/s(</span>是上代的<span lang="EN-US">3</span>倍<span lang="EN-US">)</span>，<span lang="EN-US">4K</span>随机读写性能最高都是<span lang="EN-US">150</span>万<span lang="EN-US">IOPS</span>，<span lang="EN-US">4K 70/30</span>混合随机性能最高<span lang="EN-US">180</span>万<span lang="EN-US">IOPS(</span>是上代的<span lang="EN-US">3</span>倍<span lang="EN-US">)</span>，<span lang="EN-US">512bit</span>随机读取性能高达<span lang="EN-US">460</span>万<span lang="EN-US">IOPS</span>，<span lang="EN-US">4K</span>随机读取延迟低于<span lang="EN-US">6</span>微秒、随机读写延迟低于<span lang="EN-US">25</span>微秒<span lang="EN-US">(</span>比上代提升<span lang="EN-US">40</span>％<span lang="EN-US">)</span>。</p>
