<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">SK</span>海力士日前发布了<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">Q4</span>财报，当季营收达<span lang="EN-US">12.377</span>万亿韩元（<span lang="EN-US">1027.10</span>亿美元），上一年同期为<span lang="EN-US">7966</span>亿韩元，上一季度为<span lang="EN-US">11.805</span>万亿韩元，单季营收首次冲上<span lang="EN-US">12</span>万亿韩元。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在<span lang="EN-US">SK</span>海力士的业务中，<span lang="EN-US">DRAM</span>内存业务占总收入比例为<span lang="EN-US">71%</span>，<span lang="EN-US">NAND</span>业务占总营收比例为<span lang="EN-US">25%</span>，<span lang="EN-US">MCP</span>业务占比<span lang="EN-US">19%</span>，其他业务占比约<span lang="EN-US">4%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在该季度中，<strong><span lang="EN-US">SK</span>海力士的内存技术取得了重要突破，开始量产基于<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>技术的<span lang="EN-US">1αnm</span>工艺<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存芯片，核心容量<span lang="EN-US">24Gb</span>，也就是<span lang="EN-US">3GB</span>，轻松实现单条容量<span lang="EN-US">24GB</span>甚至<span lang="EN-US">48GB</span>的内存条。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">1αnm</span>工艺内存虽然使用的<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>光刻机成本高昂，但是有助于提高生产效率，是无法绕过的升级过程，<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年中<span lang="EN-US">SK</span>海力士也会继续扩大<span lang="EN-US">1αnm</span>工艺内存生产，基于<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>技术的<span lang="EN-US">1αnm</span>产量预计到年底达到<span lang="EN-US">25%</span>。</p>
