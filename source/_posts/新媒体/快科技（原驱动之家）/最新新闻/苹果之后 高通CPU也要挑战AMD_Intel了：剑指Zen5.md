<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">这么多年来，<span lang="EN-US">x86</span>架构几乎是<span lang="EN-US">PC</span>平台唯一的选择，<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>在统治移动平台之后已经开始挑战<span lang="EN-US">x86</span>的<span lang="EN-US">PC</span>地位了，苹果的<span lang="EN-US">M1</span>及<span lang="EN-US">M2</span>系列处理器已经取得成果，现在高通也宣布加入这一行列，<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年推出的自研处理器要跟<span lang="EN-US">Zen5</span>等<span lang="EN-US">x86</span>处理器碰撞一下子。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">高通<span lang="EN-US">CEO</span>安蒙日前在采访中透露了自研<span lang="EN-US">PC</span>处理器的情况，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>具体规格没公开，预计明年能提供给系统商设计，大量出货可能要到<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年了。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年的时候，<strong><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>要推出<span lang="EN-US">4nm</span>工艺的<span lang="EN-US">Zen5</span>了，</strong><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>那边也要进入<span lang="EN-US">15</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Arrow Lake</span>，制程工艺可能会上<span lang="EN-US">20A</span>，这是首款埃米级<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>工艺，支持<span lang="EN-US">RibbonFET</span>和<span lang="EN-US">PowerVia</span>这两项技术，每瓦性能上实现约<span lang="EN-US">15%</span>的提升。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">届时高通的<span lang="EN-US">PC</span>处理器要跟<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>及<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的新架构产品竞争了，期望从性能上超越<span lang="EN-US">x86</span>应该没戏，<strong>但高通的<span lang="EN-US">PC</span>处理器在能效上找到合适的竞争优势还是有可能的，跟苹果<span lang="EN-US">M1/M2</span>那样。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不过高通的<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>生态能不能发展起来还是让人担心的，苹果<span lang="EN-US">M1/M2</span>有自家的<span lang="EN-US">Mac</span>电脑及<span lang="EN-US">iPad</span>等设备支撑，高通的<span lang="EN-US">PC</span>处理器显然还是要靠微软的<span lang="EN-US">Windows</span>生态，系统及软件优化都是个麻烦。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">至少在高通最近几年发展的<span lang="EN-US">ARM PC</span>来看，这方面实在算不上成功。</p>
