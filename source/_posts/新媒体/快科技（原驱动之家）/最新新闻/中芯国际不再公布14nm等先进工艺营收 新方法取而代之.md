<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日晚，中芯国际发布了<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">Q1</span>季度财报，实现营业收入<span lang="EN-US">118.54</span>亿元，同比增长<span lang="EN-US">62.6%</span>；实现净利润<span lang="EN-US">28.43</span>亿元，同比增长<span lang="EN-US">175.5%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">值得一提的是，在这次的财报中，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>中芯国际决定不再公布具体每个工艺的营收占比，以往提到<span lang="EN-US">14nm</span>、<span lang="EN-US">28nm</span>、<span lang="EN-US">40nm</span>、<span lang="EN-US">55nm</span>、<span lang="EN-US">65nm</span>及<span lang="EN-US">90nm</span>、<span lang="EN-US">130nm</span>工艺等一系列工艺的数据不再有详细数据。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在上个季度中，<strong>最先进的<span lang="EN-US">28nm</span>及<span lang="EN-US">14nm FinFET</span>工艺则贡献了<span lang="EN-US">18.6%</span>的营收</strong>，上一年同期只有<span lang="EN-US">5%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">这是中芯国际将<span lang="EN-US">28nm</span>与<span lang="EN-US">14nm</span>工艺合并计算之后再一次改变工艺营收计算方式，中芯国际改用了新的方法来计算，用<span lang="EN-US">“</span>以尺寸分类<span lang="EN-US">”</span>替代了<span lang="EN-US">“</span>以技术节点分类<span lang="EN-US">”。</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">CEO</span>赵海军在业绩说明会中解释，<span lang="EN-US">8</span>英寸涵盖<span lang="EN-US">0.35</span>微米至<span lang="EN-US">90</span>纳米的制程，<span lang="EN-US">12</span>英寸涵盖<span lang="EN-US">90</span>纳米到<span lang="EN-US">FinFET</span>的制程，<strong>这样的披露方式更契合公司在同一工厂跨多个节点应用的运营弹性和业务特点。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">以晶圆收入分析来看，一季度智能手机业务占比继续下降至<span lang="EN-US">28.7%</span>；智能家居及其他业务占比分别为<span lang="EN-US">13.8%</span>、<span lang="EN-US">34.4%</span>，环比均显著增加。<span lang="EN-US">12</span>英寸晶圆收入占比继续增长至<span lang="EN-US">66.5%</span>，显示公司先进产能扩产有力。</p>
