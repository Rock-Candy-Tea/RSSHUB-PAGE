<p>1月20日，一汽丰田旗下最便宜的SUV车型卡罗拉锐放正式上市，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>新车共推出5款车型，售价区间为12.98-16.98万元</strong></span>。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220121/d5ba2eb3c4234124850e5b75481289e0.png" target="_blank"><img alt="一汽丰田最便宜的SUV上市！12.98万起售" h="278" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220121/s_d5ba2eb3c4234124850e5b75481289e0.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>卡罗拉锐放车身尺寸为4460/1825/1620mm，轴距2640mm，定位高于小型SUV奕泽、低于RAV4荣放，为入门型紧凑型SUV。</p>
<p>新车基于丰田TNGA平台打造，原型车为美版卡罗拉Cross，前脸使用了大尺寸的进气格栅，并配有大面积的黑色塑料保护件，增强跨界元素的同时，也能有效降低剐蹭带来的维修费用。</p>
<p>尾部非常工整，修长的车灯增加了车辆的横向视觉效果，<strong>后保险杠同样采用了黑色塑料件，作用与前保险杠相同</strong>。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220121/f70ab0d780734ac7be3745949c3e9590.png" target="_blank"><img alt="一汽丰田最便宜的SUV上市！12.98万起售" h="437" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220121/s_f70ab0d780734ac7be3745949c3e9590.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220121/d494589ed3aa4ec9a93c27a091b00e27.png" target="_blank"><img alt="一汽丰田最便宜的SUV上市！12.98万起售" h="416" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220121/s_d494589ed3aa4ec9a93c27a091b00e27.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220121/983537b30ae04f058714b6597f4ac6b3.png" target="_blank"><img alt="一汽丰田最便宜的SUV上市！12.98万起售" h="432" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220121/s_983537b30ae04f058714b6597f4ac6b3.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>内部造型基本上沿用了卡罗拉轿车的风格，谈不上有新意和创新，胜在实用和耐看。值得一提的是，传统的仪表盘中央有彩色行车电动显示屏，可以3D展现车辆的车门开启状态，还可以显示带有数字的胎压监测。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220121/b0591018c2444e55b915257a811c365a.png" target="_blank"><img alt="一汽丰田最便宜的SUV上市！12.98万起售" h="434" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220121/s_b0591018c2444e55b915257a811c365a.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220121/47bd900e5cb244d199333eeeb64f4bf5.png" target="_blank"><img alt="一汽丰田最便宜的SUV上市！12.98万起售" h="434" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220121/s_47bd900e5cb244d199333eeeb64f4bf5.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220121/ee6edabb005b4aab90276b0d7be6fe6c.png" target="_blank"><img alt="一汽丰田最便宜的SUV上市！12.98万起售" h="452" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220121/s_ee6edabb005b4aab90276b0d7be6fe6c.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>动力方面，卡罗拉锐放配备了一台2.0升直列四缸自然吸气发动机，依旧匹配CVT变速箱。在这台发动机的加持下，<strong>新车最大功率126千瓦（171马力），峰值扭矩203牛米，官方综合油耗百公里仅5.9L</strong>。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220121/7ace8aca95024f2ab366b84d30bf173b.png" target="_blank"><img alt="一汽丰田最便宜的SUV上市！12.98万起售" h="432" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220121/s_7ace8aca95024f2ab366b84d30bf173b.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>此次上市的仅为燃油版，后续会推出双擎版车型，此外，广汽丰田版“卡罗拉锐放”——锋兰达此前也已开启预售，预计上市后的价格会向卡罗拉锐放看齐。</p>
