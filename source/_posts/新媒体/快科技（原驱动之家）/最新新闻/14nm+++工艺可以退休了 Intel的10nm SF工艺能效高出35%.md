<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的<span lang="EN-US">14nm</span>工艺可以说是<span lang="EN-US">DIY</span>玩家的老朋友了，从<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年量产到现在都<span lang="EN-US">6</span>年了，期间<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>魔改了三代，最新的是<span lang="EN-US">14nm+++</span>工艺，依然是当前桌面酷睿处理器的主力。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">10nm</span>工艺好事多磨，<span lang="EN-US">2.7</span>倍的晶体管密度指标史无前例，但也带来了量产上的难题，导致<span lang="EN-US">10nm</span>工艺这两年才开始量产，去年开始生产的是<span lang="EN-US">10nm SuperFin</span>工艺（简称<span lang="EN-US">10nm SF</span>），也就是第二代<span lang="EN-US">10nm</span>工艺了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">10nm SF</span>工艺相比<span lang="EN-US">14nm+++</span>工艺到底有多大的提升呢？最近有<span lang="EN-US">B</span>站<span lang="EN-US">UP</span>主作了个测试，对比了酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i5-11400H</span>（<span lang="EN-US">10nm SF</span>工艺<span lang="EN-US">Willow Cove</span>架构）与桌面版的酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i5-11400</span>（<span lang="EN-US">14nm+++</span>工艺、<span lang="EN-US">CypressCove</span>架构）处理器的性能、功耗差距。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i5-11400H</span>在<span lang="EN-US">52W</span>功耗下频率<span lang="EN-US">3.52GHz</span>，酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i5-11400</span>在<span lang="EN-US">77W</span>功耗下频率<span lang="EN-US">3.4GHz</span>，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>这时候二者的<span lang="EN-US">R20</span>性能差不多，算下来<span lang="EN-US">10nm SF</span>的能效提升了<span lang="EN-US">35%</span>左右。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">当然，较真的话，酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i5-11400</span>的架构跟酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i5-11400H</span>的也不完全一样，但后者的<span lang="EN-US">WillowCove</span>架构基本上是改进了缓存系统，影响不大。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">排除这些细微差距，目前来看<span lang="EN-US">10nm SF</span>工艺的提升还是挺明显的，下半年就要上<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Alder Lake</span>了，除了会升级<span lang="EN-US">GoldenCove</span>高性能架构之外，传闻工艺也会进一步升级到<span lang="EN-US">10nm ESF</span>，也就是增强版的<span lang="EN-US">10nm SF</span>，值得期待。</p>
