<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的<span lang="EN-US">7nm Zen3</span>架构锐龙<span lang="EN-US">5000</span>处理器在过去一年中势头很猛，美国、欧洲及国内的畅销榜上几乎都是锐龙处理器，不过随着<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿的上市，<span lang="EN-US">Zen3</span>的优势正在被逆袭，其中<span lang="EN-US">12</span>核的酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i7-12700K</span>表现很猛。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i7-12700K</span>的规格很不错，<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个性能<span lang="EN-US">4</span>个能效核，<strong>总计<span lang="EN-US">12</span>核<span lang="EN-US">20</span>线程，比酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i9-12900K</span>少了<span lang="EN-US">4</span>个能效核，<span lang="EN-US">L3</span>缓存<span lang="EN-US">25MB</span>，少了<span lang="EN-US">5MB</span>，</strong>性能核频率<span lang="EN-US">3.6</span>到<span lang="EN-US">4.9GHz</span>，效能核频率<span lang="EN-US">2.7</span>到<span lang="EN-US">3.8GHz</span>，<span lang="EN-US">TB 3.0</span>睿频<span lang="EN-US">5.0GHz</span>，整合<span lang="EN-US">UHD 770</span>核显，<span lang="EN-US">Turbo</span>功耗<span lang="EN-US">190W</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i7-12700K</span>游戏性能不输酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i9</span>大哥，不过价格便宜不少，官方建议价也只有<span lang="EN-US">409</span>美元，但是上市<span lang="EN-US">4</span>个月来已经多次降价，美亚上现在只要<span lang="EN-US">338</span>美元，降幅20%左右，目前约合<span lang="EN-US">2137</span>元，性价比极高。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">得益于此，酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i7-12700K</span>最近在各大电商平台表现都很猛，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>英国电商<span lang="EN-US">Overclockers UK</span>销量第二，美亚上现在已经是最受欢迎的处理器，击败了<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7 5800X</span>、锐龙<span lang="EN-US">9 5900X</span>。</strong></span></p>
