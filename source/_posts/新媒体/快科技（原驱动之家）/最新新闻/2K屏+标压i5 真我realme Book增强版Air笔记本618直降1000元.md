<p class="MsoNormal">今天下午的发布会上，<span lang="EN-US">realme</span>真我不仅推出了真我<span lang="EN-US">GT Neo3</span>火影定制版等新品，还宣布了<span lang="EN-US">618</span>大促全面开启，其中<span lang="EN-US">realme Book</span>增强版<span lang="EN-US">Air</span>笔记本直降千元，16+512GB售价<span lang="EN-US">3699</span>元。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">realme Book</span>增强版<span lang="EN-US">Air</span>笔记本是一款轻薄本，厚度只有<span lang="EN-US">14.9mm</span>，重量<span lang="EN-US">1.37</span>千克，有天空青、海岛灰两种时尚配色。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220526/15a4f501-992b-4be6-9d7c-fdde4cc782bf.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="2K屏+标压i5 真我realme Book增强版Air笔记本618直降1000元" h="199" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220526/S15a4f501-992b-4be6-9d7c-fdde4cc782bf.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>核心配置上，<span lang="EN-US">realme Book</span>增强版<span lang="EN-US">Air</span>搭载第<span lang="EN-US">11</span>代酷睿高性能移动版<span lang="EN-US">i5-11320H</span>处理器，</strong>集全新<span lang="EN-US">Willow Cove CPU</span>架构、<span lang="EN-US">10nm SuperFin</span>制程工艺于一身，单核睿频高至<span lang="EN-US">4.5GHz</span>，性能强劲，更有<span lang="EN-US">16GB</span>内存、<span lang="EN-US">512GB</span>存储。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">显示方面，<span lang="EN-US">realme Book</span>增强版<span lang="EN-US">Air</span>配备了一块<span lang="EN-US">2K</span>超清屏幕，屏占比达到<span lang="EN-US">90%</span>，拥有<span lang="EN-US">400nits</span>峰值亮度、<span lang="EN-US">100% sRGB</span>高色域。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">另外，<span lang="EN-US">realme Book</span>增强版<span lang="EN-US">Air</span>拥有新一代<span lang="EN-US">VC</span>液冷散热技术，采用微米级黄光蚀刻微结构设计，配合超薄高转速双风扇，综合散热能力对比前代提升<span lang="EN-US">32.7%</span>，让<span lang="EN-US">realme</span>笔记本性能释放更加强劲。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>真我realme Book增强版Air笔记本：<a class="f14_link" href="https://dt.mydrivers.com/mv.ashx?txt=AQ15" target="_blank">京东3699元</a></strong></p>
