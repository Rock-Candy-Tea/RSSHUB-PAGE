<p class="MsoNormal">中秋假期已经开始了，很快还会有国庆<span lang="EN-US">7</span>天长假，总计有<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天假期。如何充分利用这次的假期呢？有人出游、有人探亲，要是不想浪费，利用好的话<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天假期就能赚到<span lang="EN-US">1</span>个月的工资了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">又到了一年一度的如何算加班工资的时候了，这次的中秋国庆<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天假期中有几种情况，具体如下：</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">多地人社部门已经明确，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>今年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日<span lang="EN-US">(</span>中秋节<span lang="EN-US">)</span>和<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日、<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日、<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日<span lang="EN-US">(</span>国庆节<span lang="EN-US">)</span>，这<span lang="EN-US">4</span>天是法定节假日，只要安排加班，就应当支付不低于<span lang="EN-US">300%</span>工资。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">三倍工资计算公式：月工资基数÷<span lang="EN-US">21.75</span>天<span lang="EN-US">÷8×</span>加班小时数<span lang="EN-US">×300%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此外，这<span lang="EN-US">4</span>天加班不能用补休来替代加班工资，必须发放<span lang="EN-US">3</span>倍工资。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">另外的<span lang="EN-US">6</span>天假期中，<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日、<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日、<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日、<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日、<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日、<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日这<span lang="EN-US">6</span>天如果安排加班但又不能安排补休的，应当支付不低于<span lang="EN-US">200%</span>工资。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">二倍工资计算公式：月工资基数÷<span lang="EN-US">21.75</span>天<span lang="EN-US">÷8×</span>加班小时数<span lang="EN-US">×200%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">如果中秋国庆假期都在加班，而且没有安排补休，那么，<strong>中秋国庆加班<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天，可以拿到相当于<span lang="EN-US">24</span>天的日工资，</strong>比一个月的<span lang="EN-US">22</span>天工作日收获还要丰盛。</p>
