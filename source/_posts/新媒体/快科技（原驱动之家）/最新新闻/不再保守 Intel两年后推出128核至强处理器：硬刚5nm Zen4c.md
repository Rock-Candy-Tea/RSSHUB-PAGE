<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">在过去的几年中，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>凭借先进的工艺及小芯片设计在多核上一路狂奔，桌面做到了<span lang="EN-US">16</span>核，服务器直接上了<span lang="EN-US">64</span>核，再下一代的<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>做到了<span lang="EN-US">96</span>核及<span lang="EN-US">128</span>核，后者将于<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年问世。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>这边的至强处理器此前因为原生多核的设计，核心数不能快速增加，导致<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>在多核上吃亏不少，不过接下来的两年<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>也要加速了，<strong><span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年也会推出<span lang="EN-US">128</span>核至强处理器，代号为<span lang="EN-US">Sierra Forest</span>。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">来捋一下未来的至强系列路线图，今年上半年推出了<span lang="EN-US">IceLake-SP</span>系列，<span lang="EN-US">10nm</span>工艺，最多<span lang="EN-US">40</span>核，原本今年还会有<span lang="EN-US">Sapphire Rapids-SP</span>系列，不过推迟到了<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年上半年，<span lang="EN-US">Intel 7</span>工艺，最多<span lang="EN-US">56</span>核。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">再往后还有<span lang="EN-US">Granite Rapids</span>处理器，升级<span lang="EN-US">Intel 4</span>工艺，详细规格还不太确定，应该是双芯封装，每组最多<span lang="EN-US">60</span>核心，但实际上会启用<span lang="EN-US">56</span>个，总计<span lang="EN-US">112</span>核心<span lang="EN-US">224</span>线程。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="不再保守 Intel两年后推出128核至强处理器：硬刚5nm Zen4c" h="291" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211127/6ce3c4c4-9db2-4021-b1b1-b6b3be3d9f9a.png" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">再后面就要到<span lang="EN-US">Sierra Forest</span>，这代至强又是一次大改，核心数直接上到<span lang="EN-US">128</span>核<span lang="EN-US">256</span>线程，跟<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4 Bergamo</span>一致，而且设计思路也差不多，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>都是针对高密度服务器而生的，<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>内核是改良过的，<span lang="EN-US">Bergamo</span>是精简后的<span lang="EN-US">Zen4c</span>，而<span lang="EN-US">Sierra Forest</span>则是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的能效核，可能是现在<span lang="EN-US">Alder Lake</span>酷睿中的<span lang="EN-US">Gracemont</span>或者改进版。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">总结来说就是，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>除了在高性能至强上跟<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>竞争之外，在<span lang="EN-US">Sierra Forest</span>这一代上也会提升<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>核心数跟<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>抢多核服务器市场，而且都是在<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年上市。</p>
