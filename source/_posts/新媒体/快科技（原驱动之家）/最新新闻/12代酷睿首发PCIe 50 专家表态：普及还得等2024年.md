<p class="MsoNormal">本月底<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>就要发布<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Alder Lake</span>处理器了，除了<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>架构及工艺升级之外，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>这波还要首发<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>，算是报了<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>前两年首发<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 4.0</span>的仇了，后者也会在明年的<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>上跟进<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">与现在的<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 4.0</span>相比，<strong><span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>单条通道速率翻倍到了<span lang="EN-US">32GT/s</span>，大约是<span lang="EN-US">4GB/s</span>，<span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>硬盘主控通常是<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0 x4</span>，速度理论上可达<span lang="EN-US">16GB/s</span>，相比现在的<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 4.0</span>硬盘提升一倍。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">前不久最大的<span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>主控公司群联宣布了<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>主控<span lang="EN-US">PS5026-E26</span>，明年下半年量产。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">东芝最近也展示过<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>硬盘的性能，与现有<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 4.0</span>的<span lang="EN-US">CM6</span>系列相比，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>下代硬盘的读取速度达到了<span lang="EN-US">14GB/s</span>，写入也有<span lang="EN-US">7GB/s</span>，分别提升<span lang="EN-US">103%</span>、<span lang="EN-US">67%</span>，同时随机读取性能也提升了<span lang="EN-US">79%</span>，读取、写入延迟则降低了<span lang="EN-US">77%</span>、<span lang="EN-US">50%</span>。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>硬盘看起来很好很强大，明年就有相关产品问世，不过业界专家对它的普及表示不乐观，服务器端可能会快一些，但消费级市场上要晚很多，因为<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>成本太高，消费级产品对售价很敏感。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>业内人士的看法认为，目前消费级<span lang="EN-US">SSD</span>还在处于<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 3.0</span>向<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 4.0</span>升级的阶段，明年能普及<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 4.0</span>就不错了，<strong><span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>普及要晚上一两年到，要到<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年才会在消费级市场上出货。</strong></p>
