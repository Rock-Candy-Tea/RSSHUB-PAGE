<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">自从去年<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>在<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿上首发了<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存之后，新一代内存闪亮登场，今年下半年还有<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的<span lang="EN-US">13</span>代酷睿及<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>加入支持，其中<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>更加激进，直接放弃了<span lang="EN-US">DDR4</span>，只支持<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">考虑到这一点，锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>的装机成本显然要比<span lang="EN-US">DDR4</span>平台高一些，不过好消息是现在的<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存价格也在下滑，而且跌幅很高，降低了锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>的装机成本。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="锐龙7000装机又省了 DDR5内存价格跌了50%：就差一点" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220618/94eba7c4-ee38-4530-a51f-87e9c3dc55e0.png" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">从德国市场上的价格统计来看，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>今年初<span lang="EN-US">16GB</span>套装的<span lang="EN-US">DDR5-4800</span>内存价格还在<span lang="EN-US">150</span>欧元以上，现在已经跌破<span lang="EN-US">100</span>欧元，部分甚至只要<span lang="EN-US">85</span>欧元，跌幅超过<span lang="EN-US">50%</span>。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">32GB</span>套装的价格现在也只要<span lang="EN-US">150</span>欧元了，算下来每<span lang="EN-US">GB</span>只要<span lang="EN-US">4.7</span>欧元，比<span lang="EN-US">16GB</span>套装的<span lang="EN-US">5.3</span>欧元还要划算。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">这一波内存降价的主力是美光的英睿达及金士顿两家大厂，不过降幅较多的主要是<span lang="EN-US">DDR5-4800</span>频率的，高频的<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存还是有点小贵，<strong><span lang="EN-US">16GB</span>套装的<span lang="EN-US">DDR5-5200</span>还要<span lang="EN-US">105</span>欧元，<span lang="EN-US">DDR5-6000</span>还要<span lang="EN-US">135</span>欧元，折合人民币<span lang="EN-US">740</span>、<span lang="EN-US">951</span>元。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">考虑到今年下半年还有几个月时间，<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存价格还是会继续降价，或许年底的时候就可以真正上车了，就差那么一点了，等等看。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="锐龙7000装机又省了 DDR5内存价格跌了50%：就差一点" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220618/9db570aa-30be-4711-ac1b-92f75657cc62.png" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
