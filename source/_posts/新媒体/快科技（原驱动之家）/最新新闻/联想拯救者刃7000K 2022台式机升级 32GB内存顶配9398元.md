<p class="MsoNormal">今年初联想发布了拯救者刃<span lang="EN-US">7000K 2022</span>台式机系列，全面升级<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿及<span lang="EN-US">RTX 30</span>系显卡，今晚拯救者刃<span lang="EN-US">7000K 2022</span>台式机的配置又升级了，官方还提供了定制服务，升级到<span lang="EN-US">32GB+1TB SSD</span>的顶配版需要<span lang="EN-US">9398</span>元。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">这次推出的新新配置主要是<span lang="EN-US">2</span>个版本，<strong>酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i5-12400+16GB+512GB+RTX 3060 LHR</span>版到手<span lang="EN-US">6799</span>元，酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i5-12600K+16GB+512GB+RTX 3060 Ti LHR</span>版到手<span lang="EN-US">9099</span>元，然后这两个版本还支持定制，总计有<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个版本的价格</strong>：</p>
<p>酷睿i5-12400+RTX 3060LHR显卡+16G DDR5+512GB SSD：6799元；</p>
<p>酷睿i5-12400+RTX 3060LHR显卡+32G DDR5+512GB SSD：7698元；</p>
<p>酷睿i5-12400+RTX 3060LHR显卡+16G DDR5+1TB SSD：7348元；</p>
<p>酷睿i5-12400+RTX 3060LHR显卡+32G DDR5+1TB SSD：8098元；</p>
<p>酷睿i5-12600K+RTX 3060TiLHR显+16G DDR5+512GB SSD：8099元；</p>
<p>酷睿i5-12600K+RTX 3060TiLHR显卡+32G DDR5+512GB SSD：8998元；</p>
<p>酷睿i5-12600K+RTX 3060TiLHR显卡+16G DDR5+1TB SSD：8648元；</p>
<p>酷睿i5-12600K+RTX 3060TiLHR显卡+32G DDR5+1TB SSD：9398元。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220528/21642eb493354dc08d89cbcc0f2c5ac5.png" target="_blank"><img alt="联想拯救者刃7000K 2022台式机升级 32GB内存顶配9398元" h="579" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220528/s_21642eb493354dc08d89cbcc0f2c5ac5.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>其他配置跟之前的版本一致，刃<span lang="EN-US">7000K 2022</span>采用了幻夜魔光灯效的侧透机箱，主板设计<span lang="EN-US">8</span>相供电，塔式风冷散热，九扇叶静音大风量，四套热管，最大散热能力<span lang="EN-US">170W</span>，搭配矩阵温控系统，六个测温点。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">内存标配<span lang="EN-US">16GB GDDR5</span>，四条插槽可扩展；硬盘自带<span lang="EN-US">512GB PCIe 4.0 SSD</span>，两条<span lang="EN-US">M.2</span>插槽可扩展。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">主板还提供两个<span lang="EN-US">3.5</span>寸硬盘位、一个<span lang="EN-US">2.5</span>寸硬盘位、五个<span lang="EN-US">USB 3.2</span>接口插针、四个<span lang="EN-US">USB 2.0</span>接口插针、两条<span lang="EN-US">PCIe</span>插槽，以及八个<span lang="EN-US">PWM</span>、<span lang="EN-US">ARGB</span>接口。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">前置接口两个<span lang="EN-US">USB 3.0</span>，后置接口两个<span lang="EN-US">USB 3.0</span>、一个<span lang="EN-US">USB-C 3.1</span>、四个<span lang="EN-US">USB 2.0</span>、一个<span lang="EN-US">HDMI 2.1</span>、四个<span lang="EN-US">DisplayPort 1.4a</span>、<span lang="EN-US">5.1</span>声道音频口、千兆网口，还有灯效切换按钮。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>拯救者刃7000K 2022台式机：<a class="f14_link" href="https://dt.mydrivers.com/mv.ashx?txt=ylxY" target="_blank">京东6799元</a></strong></p>
