<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日晚的春日新品观影会上，魅族推出了多款<span lang="EN-US">PANDAER NO.19</span>妙磁壳，售价<span lang="EN-US">89</span>元起。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">这次的<span lang="EN-US">PANDAER NO.19</span>妙磁壳实际上分为三个系列，分别是<span lang="EN-US">PANDAER NO.19</span>妙磁壳<span lang="EN-US">for</span>魅族<span lang="EN-US">16s/16s Pro</span>、<span lang="EN-US">PANDAER NO.19</span>妙磁壳<span lang="EN-US">for</span>魅族<span lang="EN-US">17/18</span>及<span lang="EN-US">PANDAER NO.19</span>妙磁壳<span lang="EN-US">for iPhone</span>。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220419/0c52585c-a398-4645-b6c7-8529c42e5df1.jpg" style="text-align: center;" target="_blank"><img alt="89/129元起 魅族发布PANDAER NO.19妙磁壳：99.9%抗菌技术" h="800" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220419/S0c52585c-a398-4645-b6c7-8529c42e5df1.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">PANDAER NO.19</span>妙磁壳<span lang="EN-US">for</span>魅族<span lang="EN-US">17/18</span>系列采用了魅族的<span lang="EN-US">mMagnet+</span>妙磁阵列，磨砂质感，还有限定版涂装，售价<span lang="EN-US">89</span>元。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220419/e3398e77-41eb-43fa-aa0b-778801e49ef0.jpg" style="text-align: center;" target="_blank"><img alt="89/129元起 魅族发布PANDAER NO.19妙磁壳：99.9%抗菌技术" h="800" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220419/Se3398e77-41eb-43fa-aa0b-778801e49ef0.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">PANDAER NO.19</span>妙磁壳<span lang="EN-US">for</span>魅族<span lang="EN-US">16s/16s Pro</span>有限定版涂装及磨砂触感，售价也是<span lang="EN-US">89</span>元。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220419/65b607b7-b34a-4766-9439-5c9641cbea2d.jpg" style="text-align: center;" target="_blank"><img alt="89/129元起 魅族发布PANDAER NO.19妙磁壳：99.9%抗菌技术" h="800" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220419/S65b607b7-b34a-4766-9439-5c9641cbea2d.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">PANDAER NO.19</span>妙磁壳<span lang="EN-US">for iPhone</span>售价<span lang="EN-US">129</span>元，比魅族自家的版本要贵，但材料也更复杂，具备<span lang="EN-US"> IML </span>工艺、磁吸和<span lang="EN-US"> PANDAER Guard+</span>抗菌三大特性，抗菌率可达<span lang="EN-US">99.9%，更适合果粉。</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220419/a053d918-6c90-4482-8c09-8601519c9438.jpg" style="text-align: center;" target="_blank"><img alt="89/129元起 魅族发布PANDAER NO.19妙磁壳：99.9%抗菌技术" h="800" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220419/Sa053d918-6c90-4482-8c09-8601519c9438.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>此外，魅族还推出了几款很有意思的新产品，PANDAER 超感钻石钢化膜，具备 98% 透光率，采用真空镀进口疏油层、0.33mm 电子级高铝玻璃和 Three Steps 装载辅助，即日开售，售价￥89。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220419/56db806e-7150-4fd5-b498-2bf187313f56.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="89/129元起 魅族发布PANDAER NO.19妙磁壳：99.9%抗菌技术" h="800" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220419/S56db806e-7150-4fd5-b498-2bf187313f56.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>PANDAER 光学级除菌速净大师，具备 PANDAER Clean 速净配方、超纤湿强纸、每盒100 片独立铝膜包装，售价￥69起。</p>
