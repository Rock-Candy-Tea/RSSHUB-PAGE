<p class="MsoNormal">今年的暑期档票房大战已经开始了，据灯塔专业版，截至<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日<span lang="EN-US">18</span>时<span lang="EN-US">23</span>分，<strong><span lang="EN-US">2022</span>暑期档<span lang="EN-US">(6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日<span lang="EN-US">-8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日<span lang="EN-US">)</span>总票房<span lang="EN-US">10.00</span>亿元</strong>，总场次<span lang="EN-US">582.4</span>万，总人次<span lang="EN-US">2828.1</span>万。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">其中票房前三的电影分别是：</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong><span lang="EN-US">1.</span>《侏罗纪世界<span lang="EN-US">3</span>》<span lang="EN-US">5.47</span>亿</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2.</span>《暗恋<span lang="EN-US">·</span>橘生淮南》<span lang="EN-US">1.20</span>亿</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">3.</span>《坏蛋联盟》<span lang="EN-US">1.02</span>亿 <span style="font-family:"MS Gothic";mso-bidi-font-family:"MS Gothic"">​​​​</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:"MS Gothic";mso-fareast-font-family:等线;
mso-fareast-theme-font:minor-fareast;mso-bidi-font-family:"MS Gothic""></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">从中可以看到，《侏罗纪世界<span lang="EN-US">3</span>》独占鳌头，贡献的票房超过了一半，遥遥领先其他电影，第二及之后的票房刚刚过亿级别，差距明显。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">目前的暑期档中依然没有多少热门大片，这也是《侏罗纪世界<span lang="EN-US">3</span>》能够票房独霸的关键。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><img alt="2022暑期档总票房破10亿：《侏罗纪世界3》独占鳌头 你看过几部？" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220618/badf228c-6c15-4ffd-9397-6676b88a2297.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">《侏罗纪世界3》片名为“统治”，是《侏罗纪世界》系列的完结篇，克里斯·帕拉特、布莱丝·达拉斯·霍华德继续主演，《侏罗纪公园》主演劳拉·邓恩、杰夫·高布伦、山姆·尼尔也回归，由《侏罗纪世界》导演科林·特莱沃若执导。</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">今年的暑期档电影就是这些了，大家看了多少部呢？</p>
