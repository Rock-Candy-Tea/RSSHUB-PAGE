<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>刚发了新驱动，<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>这边的新驱动也跟上了——<span lang="EN-US">GeForce 471.68</span>驱动发布，主要优化了国产武侠吃鸡游戏《永劫无间》，<span lang="EN-US">4K</span>游戏性能提升多达<span lang="EN-US">60%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA Geforce Game Ready Driver 471.68</span>版显卡驱动发布，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>主要为近期的热门武侠吃鸡游戏《永劫无间》提供优化，带来了<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA DLSS</span>和<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA Reflex</span>技术支持，并声称在<span lang="EN-US">4K</span>分辨率下可提升高达<span lang="EN-US">60%</span>的系统性能。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此外，新驱动还为《喋血复仇》公开测试版和《<span lang="EN-US">Psychonauts 2</span>》游戏提供优化支持，并添加了<span lang="EN-US">7</span>款新的<span lang="EN-US">G-SYNC</span>兼容显示器。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong><span lang="EN-US">471.68</span>版驱动<span lang="EN-US">Bug</span>修复列表：</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">1</span>、<span lang="EN-US">GeForce 10 </span>系列显卡：《蝙蝠侠：阿甘骑士》开启湍流烟雾会导致游戏崩溃。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2</span>、关闭<span lang="EN-US">V-SYNC</span>会导致《杀手<span lang="EN-US">3</span>》游戏画面随机闪烁。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">3</span>、《怪物猎人：世界》游戏画面闪烁。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">4</span>、《刺客信条：奥德赛》游戏中或者<span lang="EN-US">Benchmark</span>测试时会出现帧率下降。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">5</span>、《<span lang="EN-US">Topaz Video Enhance AI</span>》运行出错且发生驱动程序<span lang="EN-US">TDR</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">6</span>、开启<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA DLSS</span>会导致《<span lang="EN-US">D5 Render</span>》程序黑屏。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">7</span>、<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA SDR</span>设置为深色（<span lang="EN-US">10pbc</span>）时会导致桌面渲染错误。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">8</span>、<span lang="EN-US">PLA Benchmark</span>程序无法开启<span lang="EN-US">PhysX</span>物理加速。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong><span lang="EN-US">Windows 10</span>系统下部分已知问题：</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">1</span>、滚动页面会导致<span lang="EN-US">YouTube</span>视频卡顿。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2</span>、《全境封锁<span lang="EN-US">2</span>》出现残影。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">3</span>、《索尼克全明星赛车：变形》在通过水下场景时会发生崩溃。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">4</span>、与<span lang="EN-US">10-bit</span>相比，颜色设置为<span lang="EN-US">8-bit</span>时，<span lang="EN-US">DPC</span>延时较高。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">5</span>、不支持<span lang="EN-US">Samsung Odyssey G9</span>显示器。</p>
