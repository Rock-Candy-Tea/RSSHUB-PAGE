<p align="center"><a href="http://click.aliyun.com/m/1000253340/" target="_blank" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://icons.mydrivers.com/2021/news/ayun72890.png" width="600" height="74" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
           </p><p class="MsoNormal">日前<span style="background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial; color: black;">工信部发布《“双千兆”网络协同发展行动计划<span lang="EN-US">(2021-2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">)</span>》</span>，指出<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年时间实现<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>、千兆带宽基本普及的目标。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color: black; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">工信部表示，以千兆光网和<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>为代表的<span lang="EN-US">“</span>双千兆<span lang="EN-US">”</span>网络，能向单个用户提供固定和移动网络千兆接入能力，具有超大带宽、超低时延、先进可靠等特征，二者互补互促，是新型基础设施的重要组成和承载底座。</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">该计划提出要求，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>用三年时间，基本建成全面覆盖城市地区和有条件乡镇的“双千兆”网络基础设施，实现固定和移动网络普遍具备“千兆到户”能力。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">计划指出，千兆光网和<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>用户加快发展，用户体验持续提升。增强现实<span lang="EN-US">/</span>虚拟现实<span lang="EN-US">(AR/VR)</span>、超高清视频等高带宽应用进一步融入生产生活，典型行业千兆应用模式形成示范。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">千兆光网和<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>的核心技术研发和产业竞争力保持国际先进水平，产业链供应链现代化水平稳步提升。<span lang="EN-US">“</span>双千兆<span lang="EN-US">”</span>网络安全保障能力显著增强。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>具体目标如下：</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">1</span>、到<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年底</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">--</span>千兆光纤网络具备覆盖<span lang="EN-US">2</span>亿户家庭的能力，万兆无源光网络<span lang="EN-US">(10G-PON)</span>及以上端口规模超过<span lang="EN-US">500</span>万个，千兆宽带用户突破<span lang="EN-US">1000</span>万户。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">--5G</span>网络基本实现县级以上区域、部分重点乡镇覆盖， 新增<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>基站超过<span lang="EN-US">60</span>万个。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">--</span>建成<span lang="EN-US">20</span>个以上千兆城市。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2、</span>到<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年底</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">--</span>千兆光纤网络具备覆盖<span lang="EN-US">4</span>亿户家庭的能力，<span lang="EN-US">10G-PON</span>及以上端口规模超过<span lang="EN-US">1000</span>万个，千兆宽带用户突破<span lang="EN-US">3000</span>万户。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong><span lang="EN-US">--5G</span>网络基本实现乡镇级以上区域和重点行政村覆盖。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">--</span>实现<span lang="EN-US">“</span>双百<span lang="EN-US">”</span>目标：建成<span lang="EN-US">100</span>个千兆城市，打造<span lang="EN-US">100</span>个千兆行业虚拟专网标杆工程。</p>
