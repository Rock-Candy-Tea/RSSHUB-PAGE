<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220910/601c04dd2e4240fe97684dc6bfcbad0e.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="直降800元！真我GT2 Pro到手3199元：2K无极变帧屏" h="776" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220910/s_601c04dd2e4240fe97684dc6bfcbad0e.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="494" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>

<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220910/2fa6c14d84c445c1b163010f62cf34f9.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="直降800元！真我GT2 Pro到手3199元：2K无极变帧屏" h="935" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220910/s_2fa6c14d84c445c1b163010f62cf34f9.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="488" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>

<p>配备5000mAh电池，<strong>支持65W智慧闪充增强版，官方称充电至100%仅需33分钟。</strong></p>
