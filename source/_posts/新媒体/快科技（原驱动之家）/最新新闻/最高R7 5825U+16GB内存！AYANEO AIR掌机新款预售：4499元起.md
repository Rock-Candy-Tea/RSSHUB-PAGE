<p>近日，国内新锐掌机品牌AYANEO公布了一款新版本产品，为AYANEO AIR带来了Retro Powe经典配色，<strong>目前已经在<a class="f14_link" href="https://ayaneo.com.cn/goods/420" target="_blank">官网上架</a>，同步开启预售，售价449元起。</strong></p>
<p>其中，AYANEO AIR搭载R5 5560U+16GB+512GB，售价4499元；AYANEO AIR Pro搭载R7 5825U+16GB+1TB，售价6525元。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220701/ddcc3b048bd44008b729e5667474b17a.png" target="_blank"><img alt="最高R7 5825U+16GB内存！AYANEO AIR掌机新款预售：4499元起" h="401" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220701/s_ddcc3b048bd44008b729e5667474b17a.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>新配色在配置上与此前保持一致，作为AIR系列，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>最大的特色就是轻薄，标准版为重量为410g，厚度为18mm，体积小于Steam Deck，甚至比Switch更轻更小。</strong></span></p>
<p><strong>正面配备一块5.5英寸OLED屏，分辨率1920*1080，</strong>拥有109% NTSC、100% sRGB、96% ADOBE RGB、99% DCI P3，404 PPI。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220701/f369fcc5fcb743f3b8f3f9ec3cbe6fe3.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="最高R7 5825U+16GB内存！AYANEO AIR掌机新款预售：4499元起" h="599" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220701/s_f369fcc5fcb743f3b8f3f9ec3cbe6fe3.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>核心性能方面，AYANEO AIR标准版搭载AMD锐龙5 5560U处理器，采用7nm工艺制程，6核12线程，基础频率2.3GHz，最大睿频可达4.0GHz。核显为Vega 6，最高频率1.6GHz，配置高速M.2 2280 SSD。</p>
<p>Pro版则升级为R7 5825U处理器，以及1TB存储空间，整体性能更强。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220701/3632bca93ea5493aa612084a29c85dca.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="最高R7 5825U+16GB内存！AYANEO AIR掌机新款预售：4499元起" h="599" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220701/s_3632bca93ea5493aa612084a29c85dca.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>其他方面，AYANEO AIR内置7350mAh电池，内置X轴线性马达、蓝牙5.2、Wi-Fi 6、立体声音效，配备3.5mm耳机接口，两个全功能Type-C接口，均可视频输出支持DP1.4 4K60Hz。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220701/51256ac2a3ec4ecd937a57b0d2c9b902.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="最高R7 5825U+16GB内存！AYANEO AIR掌机新款预售：4499元起" h="1105" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220701/s_51256ac2a3ec4ecd937a57b0d2c9b902.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
