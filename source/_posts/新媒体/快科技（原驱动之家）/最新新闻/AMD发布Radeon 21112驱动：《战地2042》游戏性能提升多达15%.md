<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">A</span>卡战未来的时候到了，这次是<span lang="EN-US">Radeon Software Adrenalin</span>显卡驱动<span lang="EN-US">21.11.2</span>驱动，它让<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的<span lang="EN-US">RX 6900/6800/6700</span>系列显卡在新游戏《战地<span lang="EN-US">2042</span>》中提升多达<span lang="EN-US">15%</span>，小小地鸡血了一把。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>刚刚发布了<span lang="EN-US">Radeon Software Adrenalin 21.11.2 Optional</span>版显卡驱动，更新内容只有一项<span lang="EN-US">——</span>为《战地<span lang="EN-US">2042</span>》进行优化。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>官方提供的资料，《战地<span lang="EN-US">2042</span>》在<span lang="EN-US">4K</span>最高画质模式下，对比<span lang="EN-US">21.10.4</span>，使用新版驱动后，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的三款旗舰卡的游戏性能均有明显的提升，具体如下：</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">- Radeon RX 6900 XT 16GB</span>显卡提升了<span lang="EN-US">14%</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">- Radeon RX 6800 XT 16GB</span>显卡提升了<span lang="EN-US">15%</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">- Radeon RX 6700 XT 16GB</span>显卡提升了<span lang="EN-US">11%</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">另外，《战地<span lang="EN-US"> 2042</span>》已于<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日开启预载，将于<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日正式发售，登陆<span lang="EN-US">PC</span>，<span lang="EN-US">PS5</span>，<span lang="EN-US">PS4</span>，<span lang="EN-US">Xbox Series X/S</span>和<span lang="EN-US">Xbox One</span>平台，推荐已经购买用户下载更新。</p>
