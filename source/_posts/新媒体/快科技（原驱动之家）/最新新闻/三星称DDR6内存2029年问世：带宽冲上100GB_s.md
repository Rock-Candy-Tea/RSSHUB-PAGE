<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月份，随着<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿处理器发布上市，<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存也闪亮登场，未来几年里就要接替<span lang="EN-US">DDR4</span>了，不过三星也在准备下一代的<span lang="EN-US">DDR6</span>内存了，频率有望再次翻倍，预计<span lang="EN-US">2029</span>年问世。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">DDR6</span>内存现在肯定没什么规范之说，可以确定的是性能还会再次提升，我们知道<span lang="EN-US">DDR4</span>内存主流是<span lang="EN-US">2400-3200MHz</span>，<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>是<span lang="EN-US">4800-6400MHz</span>，<strong>而<span lang="EN-US">DDR6</span>内存的上限至少是<span lang="EN-US">12.8GHz</span>，比目前翻倍。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不过<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存已经规划到了<span lang="EN-US">8400MHz</span>，<span lang="EN-US">DDR6</span>内存的<span lang="EN-US">17GHz</span>也是有希望的。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">频率提升使得<span lang="EN-US">DDR6</span>内存的带宽水涨船高，<span lang="EN-US">DDR4-3200</span>单通道是<span lang="EN-US">25.6GB/s</span>，<span lang="EN-US">DDR5-6400</span>带宽可达<span lang="EN-US">51.2GB/s</span>，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">DDR6</span>轻松突破<span lang="EN-US">100GB/s</span>，首次冲上三位数，<span lang="EN-US">PC</span>上的双通道就能有<span lang="EN-US">200GB/s</span>带宽，</strong></span>这个速度比得上现在很多片上内存系统的，苹果的<span lang="EN-US">M1 Pro/Max</span>内存带宽就是<span lang="EN-US">200GB/s</span>到<span lang="EN-US">400GB/s</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">按照三星的计划，<strong><span lang="EN-US">DDR6</span>内存要到<span lang="EN-US">2029</span>年才能问世，</strong>还有七八年时间，现在先等着<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>成为主流再说吧。</p>
