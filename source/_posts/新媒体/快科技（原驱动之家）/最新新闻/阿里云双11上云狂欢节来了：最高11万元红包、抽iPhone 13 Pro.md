<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年阿里云双<span lang="EN-US">11</span>大促活动已经开始，活动分为两个阶段，已认证的注册会员都可以参加，红包最高<span lang="EN-US">11111</span>元，拉新还可以赢取<span lang="EN-US">iPhone 13 Pro</span>等在内的重磅礼品。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">从活动页面来看，阿里云双<span lang="EN-US">11 </span>上云狂欢节活动分为两个阶段：</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日<span lang="EN-US">-10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日的【预热期】亿元加油包抢先领、拉新赢<span lang="EN-US"> iPhone 13 Pro</span>、限量云小宝手办带回家！</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">01</span>日<span lang="EN-US">-11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日的【正式期】全场火热大促，热门产品全年底价，新老用户同享。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US"><strong>2021</strong></span><strong>年阿里云双<span lang="EN-US">11</span>大促主会场</strong>：<a class="f14_link" href="https://dt.mydrivers.com/qq.ashx?atr=188m" target="_blank">点此进入</a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211102/23c2b6c5fdf64aa48de0eacbfcfaa9eb.png" target="_blank"><img alt="阿里云双11上云狂欢节来了：最高1.1万元红包、抽iPhone 13 Pro" h="339" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211102/s_23c2b6c5fdf64aa48de0eacbfcfaa9eb.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>一、领取双<span lang="EN-US">11</span>加油包</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">阿里云官网已实名认证的注册会员用户皆可领取双<span lang="EN-US">11</span>加油包，该加油包分为个人用户和企业用户，可根据您的账户类型选择领取，同一用户活动期间只可领取<span lang="EN-US">1</span>次，不可重复参与。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">需要注意的是：</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">领券时间：<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日<span lang="EN-US">0</span>点<span lang="EN-US">-2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日<span lang="EN-US">24</span>点（大型活动期间如有调整，以届时活动页面展示为准）</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">券使用时间：<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日<span lang="EN-US">0</span>点<span lang="EN-US">-2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日<span lang="EN-US">24</span>点（需要注意：过期作废）</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>二、邀新大狂欢，<span lang="EN-US">iPhone13 Pro</span>、机械键盘、现金红包抽不停</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">阿里云官网实名认证用户皆可参与，活动期间内邀请<span lang="EN-US">1</span>名新用户注册即可得<span lang="EN-US">1</span>次抽奖机会，<span lang="EN-US">7</span>次封顶，<span lang="EN-US">100%</span>中奖。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">活动时间：<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日<span lang="EN-US">0</span>点至<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日<span lang="EN-US">24</span>点（大型活动期间如有调整，届时以活动页面实际展示为准）</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>参与步骤：</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">推广者在页面生成自己的邀请链接，并将其发送给好友（首次注册的阿里云新用户），邀请阿里云新用户完成注册即可获得<span lang="EN-US">1</span>次抽奖机会，每名参与者最多可获得<span lang="EN-US">7</span>次抽奖机会。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">第一步：点击“立即报名”按钮，报名参与活动成为活动推广者；</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">第二步：点击“立即邀请”按钮，将自动生成专属于你的分享链接 ；</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">第三步：将专属链接分享给你的好友（首次注册的阿里云新用户）；</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">第四步：你的好友通过你的分享链接，完成阿里云账户注册，即视为推广成功，可获得<span lang="EN-US">1</span>次抽奖机会！</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>本次活动的奖品有：</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">iPhone13 Pro 256G </span>手机（颜色随机）、机械键盘、福禄寿淘公仔套装、天猫精灵智能音箱方糖、<span lang="EN-US">50</span>元天猫超市卡、阿里云<span lang="EN-US">“</span>无影云电脑<span lang="EN-US">”</span>软件版、现金红包<span lang="EN-US">10</span>元、现金红包<span lang="EN-US">1</span>元等；</p>
