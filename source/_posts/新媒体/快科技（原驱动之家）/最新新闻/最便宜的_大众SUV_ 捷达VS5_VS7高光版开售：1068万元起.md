<p>近日消息，<strong><span style="color:#ff0000;">新款捷达VS5、VS7高光版</span></strong>正式开启预售，其中前者价格区间为10.68-11.58万元；后者价格区间为12.69-13.59万元。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220718/635da7ef9cfc46dfa2df53d9eff50407.png" target="_blank"><img alt="最便宜的“大众SUV” 捷达VS5/VS7高光版开售：10.68万元起" h="331" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220718/s_635da7ef9cfc46dfa2df53d9eff50407.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220718/fef8d237d25b427a8ac6ab48285451f2.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="最便宜的“大众SUV” 捷达VS5/VS7高光版开售：10.68万元起" h="246" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220718/s_fef8d237d25b427a8ac6ab48285451f2.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>相比在售车型，高光版针对造型及内饰细节进行了升级，除了经典的极地白、锰石黑车漆色外，这两款新车还加入了3种潮流车色，<strong>分别为苍柏青、跃动蓝、星河灰</strong>。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220718/adf842e6063844938952f934b1ce7286.png" target="_blank"><img alt="最便宜的“大众SUV” 捷达VS5/VS7高光版开售：10.68万元起" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220718/s_adf842e6063844938952f934b1ce7286.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220718/592390215b214c2898d54f55fb6a4f2f.png" target="_blank"><img alt="最便宜的“大众SUV” 捷达VS5/VS7高光版开售：10.68万元起" h="343" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220718/s_592390215b214c2898d54f55fb6a4f2f.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220718/963e2f521db74992a6c859793f8a86e4.png" target="_blank"><img alt="最便宜的“大众SUV” 捷达VS5/VS7高光版开售：10.68万元起" h="328" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220718/s_963e2f521db74992a6c859793f8a86e4.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>不仅如此，新车还配备了一系列的熏黑运动套件，包括前脸采用的“Y型雕刻铠甲式”高亮黑上格栅、熏黑的18英寸“Gaea”高亮黑铝合金轮圈、高亮黑外后视镜、车身同色门把手、高亮黑车顶+行李架等等，因此看上去更为运动和年轻。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220718/3f2043a326c547bfa48b2497b3f14b02.png" target="_blank"><img alt="最便宜的“大众SUV” 捷达VS5/VS7高光版开售：10.68万元起" h="308" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220718/s_3f2043a326c547bfa48b2497b3f14b02.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220718/f9d9fc4610ca487aa5f9b8345b47fee6.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="最便宜的“大众SUV” 捷达VS5/VS7高光版开售：10.68万元起" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220718/s_f9d9fc4610ca487aa5f9b8345b47fee6.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220718/4f75792967ed4629b9bd636df614c674.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="最便宜的“大众SUV” 捷达VS5/VS7高光版开售：10.68万元起" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220718/s_4f75792967ed4629b9bd636df614c674.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>内饰部分，新VS5高光版，为了让黑色内饰更有质感和个性，增加了全新蓝色饰条与缝线组合，再搭配3D立体图案门板，提升精致感与层次感，提升了车内的精致感与层次感。</p>
<p>新捷达VS7高光版黑色内饰则搭配玫瑰金色装饰条，辅以熏黑钢琴烤漆、菱形绗缝软质门板，一定程度上提升了车内的精致感，当然，跟豪华还是不怎么沾边。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220718/8364a7e6d4dd4ab9afd843ef3877310f.png" target="_blank"><img alt="最便宜的“大众SUV” 捷达VS5/VS7高光版开售：10.68万元起" h="415" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220718/s_8364a7e6d4dd4ab9afd843ef3877310f.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220718/7730355cbd594d46a80ec7312de864fe.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="最便宜的“大众SUV” 捷达VS5/VS7高光版开售：10.68万元起" h="449" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220718/s_7730355cbd594d46a80ec7312de864fe.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>动力部分，<strong>两款新车继续搭载1.4T发动机，最大功率110kW，峰值扭矩250N·m，</strong>与发动机匹配的是来自爱信的6速自动变速箱。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220718/d8a031bc676f4eac84944640b82dd7d4.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="最便宜的“大众SUV” 捷达VS5/VS7高光版开售：10.68万元起" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220718/s_d8a031bc676f4eac84944640b82dd7d4.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>作为大众旗下最为便宜的SUV车型，捷达VS5和VS7配备了EA211 1.4T发动机和爱信6AT的“黄金组合”，拥有较高的性价比，<strong>如果你预算有限，而又比较信赖合资品牌</strong>，那么这两款SUV或许能够成为备选项。</p>
