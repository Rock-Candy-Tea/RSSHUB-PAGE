<p class="MsoNormal">自从电竞显示器普及<span lang="EN-US">144Hz</span>之后，在高刷之路上已经一路狂奔，前不久的台北电脑展上<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>联合<span lang="EN-US">ROG</span>等才推出了<span lang="EN-US">500Hz</span>高刷的显示器，现在这个目标又要被刷新了，<span lang="EN-US">AUO</span>友达光电已经在开发<span lang="EN-US">540Hz</span>高刷的面板。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>这款超高刷面板的具体规格还没公布，很有可能是在<span lang="EN-US">500Hz</span>面板上超频而来的，属于继续优化的产品。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">除了<span lang="EN-US">540Hz</span>高刷，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>友达也在开发<span lang="EN-US">4K 240Hz</span>、<span lang="EN-US">2K 360Hz</span>高刷的电竞显示器面板，</strong></span>这种可能比<span lang="EN-US">540Hz</span>高刷面板更实用，前不久三星才首发了<span lang="EN-US">4K 240Hz</span>的显示器——奥德赛（<span lang="EN-US">Odyssey</span>）<span lang="EN-US">Neo G8 4K Mini LED </span>显示器。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">三星<span lang="EN-US">Odyssey Neo G8</span>搭载<span lang="EN-US">31.5</span>英寸<span lang="EN-US">4K Mini LED fast VA</span>面板，<span lang="EN-US">1000R</span>大曲率，支持<span lang="EN-US">240Hz</span>高刷新率，拥有<span lang="EN-US">1ms</span>灰阶响应时间，以及<span lang="EN-US">1000000:1</span>动态对比。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">这款显示器在国内的售价是<span lang="EN-US">10999</span>元，<strong>首发价<span lang="EN-US">9999</span>元。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">对玩家来说，高刷带来的视觉效果可能更好，不过冻着数百<span lang="EN-US">Hz</span>的高刷对平台的性能要求很高，之前有人测试过<span lang="EN-US">500Hz</span>高刷显示器，使用酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i9-12900K</span>、<span lang="EN-US">32GB DDR5</span>内存及<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3090</span>显卡在内的游戏<span lang="EN-US">PC</span>做了验证，哪怕是开最低画质，<span lang="EN-US">FHD</span>分辨率下帧率也只有<span lang="EN-US">343</span>帧。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">总之，就算有了<span lang="EN-US">540Hz</span>高刷的电竞显示器，要想达到这个性能也非常困难的，<strong><span lang="EN-US">RTX 3090</span>或者<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3090 Ti</span>级别的显卡很难，等下代的<span lang="EN-US">RTX 4090 Ti</span>或许有戏。</strong></p>
