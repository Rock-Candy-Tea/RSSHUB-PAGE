<p>今日晚间，Hi nova举行新品发布会，正式发布Hi nova 9系列新成员——Hi nova 9 SE，拥有一亿像素主摄、66W快充。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220419/745ccc1dff734c86b6f738db8743a6d3.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="2499元起！Hi nova 9 SE发布：一亿像素、66W快充全都有" h="199" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220419/s_745ccc1dff734c86b6f738db8743a6d3.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>ID设计上，Hi nova 9 SE在色彩上延续Hi nova系列的风格，提供梦幻冰蓝、梦幻贝母、亮黑色三种配色，后置镜头组为星环设计语言，仅7.94mm的机身配上3D玻璃后壳设计，带来出色的手感体验。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220419/8815bc0e60a5417bb203e6fc87a08d6c.png" target="_blank"><img alt="2499元起！Hi nova 9 SE发布：一亿像素、66W快充全都有" h="301" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220419/s_8815bc0e60a5417bb203e6fc87a08d6c.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220419/9982ebca77054b8d820c1d0499dd2298.png" target="_blank"><img alt="2499元起！Hi nova 9 SE发布：一亿像素、66W快充全都有" h="312" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220419/s_9982ebca77054b8d820c1d0499dd2298.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>Hi nova 9 SE正面配备一块6.78英寸全视屏，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>屏占比高达94.85%，支持120 Hz高刷新率和P3广色域，</strong></span>分辨率为2388*1080，387PPI。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220419/094f409a94b84ad5a754078bbdf4b0a0.png" target="_blank"><img alt="2499元起！Hi nova 9 SE发布：一亿像素、66W快充全都有" h="348" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220419/s_094f409a94b84ad5a754078bbdf4b0a0.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>据介绍，新机拥有1.05 mm超窄侧边框，屏幕上额头宽度仅1.1mm，在全屏观影或游戏时，带来沉浸享受。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220419/f158f024abdb4b4e85bdb20cd5bde86a.png" target="_blank"><img alt="2499元起！Hi nova 9 SE发布：一亿像素、66W快充全都有" h="343" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220419/s_f158f024abdb4b4e85bdb20cd5bde86a.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>此外，这块屏幕能够根据环境光亮度自动调整屏幕亮度，带来舒适的观阅体验，护眼模式能有效降低蓝光辐射，缓解用眼疲劳，保护视力。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220419/13b408f3def042beab6bf79329577121.png" target="_blank"><img alt="2499元起！Hi nova 9 SE发布：一亿像素、66W快充全都有" h="334" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220419/s_13b408f3def042beab6bf79329577121.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>影像方面，Hi nova 9 SE搭载一亿像素AI四摄系统，<strong>配备1.08亿像素主摄+800万像素超广角镜头+200万像素景深镜头+微距镜头，</strong>前摄为1600万像素镜头。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220419/1c435721c7a045f891321716efadfca1.png" target="_blank"><img alt="2499元起！Hi nova 9 SE发布：一亿像素、66W快充全都有" h="338" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220419/s_1c435721c7a045f891321716efadfca1.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>据悉，这颗一亿像素高清主摄，拥有1/1.52英寸传感器，F/1.9大光圈，支持9合1像素融合技术。在多帧合成技术的加持下，通过像素级画质处理算法，大幅度提升成像画质。</p>
<p>续航方面，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>Hi nova 9 SE内置4000mAh电池，支持66W快充，</strong></span>采用全新双低压电荷泵技术、三极耳电池技术等方案，大幅提升充电效率，改善电池循环寿命。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220419/de66b4c121254e1c9c9d8a3da0fd18f4.png" target="_blank"><img alt="2499元起！Hi nova 9 SE发布：一亿像素、66W快充全都有" h="311" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220419/s_de66b4c121254e1c9c9d8a3da0fd18f4.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>核心性能上，<strong>Hi nova 9 SE搭载高通骁龙6nm 5G芯片，支持双5G网络的使用，</strong>还可根据双卡状态，自动判断选择更优的5G网络进行上网。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220419/3da03b35db7d4df4942d3af00228d8d7.png" target="_blank"><img alt="2499元起！Hi nova 9 SE发布：一亿像素、66W快充全都有" h="336" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220419/s_3da03b35db7d4df4942d3af00228d8d7.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>其他方面，新机内置高导热的石墨烯材料，导热性能比普通石墨片提升30%。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220419/481a490bee77469a8766a2e64ce59d44.png" target="_blank"><img alt="2499元起！Hi nova 9 SE发布：一亿像素、66W快充全都有" h="319" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220419/s_481a490bee77469a8766a2e64ce59d44.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>价格部分，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>Hi nova 9 SE 8GB+128GB售价2499元、8GB+256GB售价2699元</strong></span>，将于4月23日上午10:08正式开售。</p>
