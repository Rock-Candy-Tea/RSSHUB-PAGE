<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">除了<span lang="EN-US">4400</span>万像素的前置、<span lang="EN-US">1</span>亿像素的后置镜头之外，<span lang="EN-US">vivo S10</span>系列这次也升级了硬件配置，处理器是联发科的天玑<span lang="EN-US">1100</span>系列，跑分可达<span lang="EN-US">67</span>万。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">天玑<span lang="EN-US">1100</span>采用台积电<span lang="EN-US">6nm EUV</span>工艺制造，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">4</span>个<span lang="EN-US">A78 2.6GHz</span>核心，<span lang="EN-US">4</span>个<span lang="EN-US">A55 2.0GHz</span>核心，</strong></span><span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>采用<span lang="EN-US">ARM G77 MC9</span>，频率<span lang="EN-US">836MHz</span>，同时支持双通道<span lang="EN-US">UFS 3.1</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">5G</span>网络功能上，天玑<span lang="EN-US">1100</span>集成<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>调制解调器，支持<span lang="EN-US">NSA/SA</span>双模的<span lang="EN-US">5G+5G</span>双卡双待和双<span lang="EN-US">VoNR</span>语音服务、<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>双载波聚合、<span lang="EN-US">5G UltraSave</span>省电技术等，可满足全场景<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>连网体验，包括<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>高铁模式、<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>电梯模式，即使在出差、电梯等特殊应用场景下，也能确保稳定和高速的<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>连接。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong><span lang="EN-US">5G UltraSave</span>省电技术还可以降低终端<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>通信功耗，带来更长效的<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>续航。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此外，天玑<span lang="EN-US">1100</span>搭载<span lang="EN-US">MediaTek</span>独立<span lang="EN-US">AI</span>处理器<span lang="EN-US">APU 3.0</span>，可充分发挥混和精度优势，灵活运用整数精度与浮点精度运算，达到更高的<span lang="EN-US">AI</span>应用能效。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">其他方面，天玑<span lang="EN-US">1100</span>还支持<span lang="EN-US">Wi-Fi 6</span>、<span lang="EN-US">4K 60fps</span>拍摄、<span lang="EN-US">1</span>亿像素镜头、<span lang="EN-US">AI</span>画质增强等等。</p>


<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210715/88ebd2e9ae064da69982a80b9400e206.png" target="_blank"><img alt="6nm EUV工艺 vivo S10全系满血天玑1100处理器：跑分67万" h="322" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210715/s_88ebd2e9ae064da69982a80b9400e206.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">跑分方面，<strong>得益于联发科天玑<span lang="EN-US">1100</span>处理器的强大性能，<span lang="EN-US">vivo S10</span>系列的跑分可达<span lang="EN-US">67.7</span>万分，接近市场上的高端旗舰机。</strong></p>
