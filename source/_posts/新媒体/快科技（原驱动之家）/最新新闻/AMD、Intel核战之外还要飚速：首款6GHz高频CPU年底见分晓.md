<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">最近几年来，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>及<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>处理器在核心数上展开了一波核战，现在主流桌面平台已经做到了<span lang="EN-US">16</span>核，今年<span lang="EN-US">13</span>代酷睿甚至会做到<span lang="EN-US">8P+16E</span>的<span lang="EN-US">24</span>核<span lang="EN-US">32</span>线程。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">现在<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>的核心数是够多了，下一步还有什么变化？传统的<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>频率大战又要接上了，目前官方出厂频率最高的是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i9-12900KS</span>，<span lang="EN-US">3.4GHz</span>基础频率、<span lang="EN-US">5.5GHz</span>加速频率。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">下一个目标是多少？之前有过<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>频率冲击<span lang="EN-US">6GHz</span>的传闻，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>现在网友<span lang="EN-US">OneRaichu</span>进一步报料称可能会有一款<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>以<span lang="EN-US">Turbo</span>加速频率的方式实现<span lang="EN-US">6GHz</span>频率，有人猜测这应该是酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i9-12900KS</span>的继任者。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">考虑到它提到了<span lang="EN-US">eTVB</span>（<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的热速度加速）模式，很有可能是<span lang="EN-US">13</span>代酷睿中的某个鸡血版，比如酷睿<span lang="EN-US">i9-13900KS</span>，这个系列<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>还会继续做下去。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="AMD、Intel核战之外还要飚速：首款6GHz高频CPU年底见分晓" h="600" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220621/f0bf8b34-79bf-4aca-8871-cb1b2d54a953.jpg" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">13</span>代酷睿基于<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿的架构及<span lang="EN-US">Intel 7</span>工艺进一步优化，频率冲上<span lang="EN-US">6GHz</span>还是有希望的，毕竟<span lang="EN-US">TDP</span>功耗还可以再放宽一些。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">除了<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>有可能抢得<span lang="EN-US">6GHz</span>频率的<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>首发之外，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>也同样有机会，即将问世的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>系列已经把全核频率提升到<span lang="EN-US">5.5GHz</span>，而且有台积电<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>工艺的制程优势，<strong><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>系列特挑一款达到<span lang="EN-US">6GHz</span>频率的处理器也不是没可能。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>频率飙车大战有望在今年底的新一代产品上看到了。</p>
