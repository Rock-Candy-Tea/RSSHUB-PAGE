<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>前几天发布了<span lang="EN-US">GeForce 496.49</span>版显卡驱动，优化了多个新游戏的支持，<a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/791/791759.htm" target="_blank">其中《骑士精神<span lang="EN-US">2</span>》通过支持<span lang="EN-US">DLSS</span>性能提升最多可达<span lang="EN-US">45</span>％。</a></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不过这版驱动也有些小问题，部分游戏出现了崩溃，<strong>日前<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>又发布了<span lang="EN-US">Geforce Game Ready Driver</span>显卡驱动<span lang="EN-US">496.61 Hotfix</span>版，基于几天前的<span lang="EN-US">496.49</span>版本，主要解决了部分游戏<span lang="EN-US">Bug</span>，</strong>具体如下：</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">1</span>、《<span lang="EN-US">Transport Fever</span>》和《<span lang="EN-US">Transport Fever 2</span>》游戏在启动时崩溃。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2</span>、《底特律：化身为人》游戏轻微卡顿。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">3</span>、《幽灵行动：荒野》游戏中会出现残影。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">4</span>、《<span lang="EN-US">WRC 9 FIA World Rally Championship</span>》、《<span lang="EN-US">RiMS Racing</span>》、《<span lang="EN-US">TT Isle of Man - Ride on the Edge 2</span>》等游戏在启动后很快会崩溃至桌面。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">遇到崩溃问题的玩家建议升级这版驱动。</p>
