<p>真我GT2大师探索版已官宣于7月12日正式登场，号称“年度质感旗舰”，现在该机已经开启盲订。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220705/924d80d0174c48a2850c57cba787a9bc.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="定金可退！真我GT2大师探索版盲订开启：限时100元抵200元" h="255" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220705/s_924d80d0174c48a2850c57cba787a9bc.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>据介绍，<strong>预定8GB+256GB版本，限时100元抵200元</strong>，此外预约还能够参与抽奖活动，有真我GT2大师探索版、真我Buds Air3 Neo耳机等奖品可拿。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220705/8d95056d342341fc9368e453612aca7d.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="定金可退！真我GT2大师探索版盲订开启：限时100元抵200元" h="233" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220705/s_8d95056d342341fc9368e453612aca7d.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>今日早间，真我GT2大师探索版外观曝光，该机采用硬箱造型的直角金属中框，背面采用环保素皮设计，手感细腻，并且还有金属铆钉装饰，辨识度拉满。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220705/932158eb2d9f421b80642efc3189d167.png" target="_blank"><img alt="定金可退！真我GT2大师探索版盲订开启：限时100元抵200元" h="301" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220705/s_932158eb2d9f421b80642efc3189d167.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>据此前爆料，真我GT2大师探索版将会搭载<strong>高通骁龙8+旗舰处理器，后置5000万主摄、5000万超广角和200万微距三摄</strong>，电池为5000mAh，支持100W闪充。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220705/d6a7c9dea7594c01a0bc3f94586f17b8.png" target="_blank"><img alt="定金可退！真我GT2大师探索版盲订开启：限时100元抵200元" h="340" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220705/s_d6a7c9dea7594c01a0bc3f94586f17b8.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>屏幕方面，<strong>真我GT2大师探索版采用6.7英寸中置挖孔柔性直屏</strong>，分辨率为2412 x 1080，刷新率为120Hz，从官方渲染图来看边框十分窄，机身也比较轻薄。</p>
<p>价格方面，徐起曾暗示，<strong>真我GT2大师探索版定价将在3000元左右</strong>，十分具有竞争力。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220705/09aaac1fc6674451ae747b00bf95e0fb.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="定金可退！真我GT2大师探索版盲订开启：限时100元抵200元" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220705/s_09aaac1fc6674451ae747b00bf95e0fb.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
