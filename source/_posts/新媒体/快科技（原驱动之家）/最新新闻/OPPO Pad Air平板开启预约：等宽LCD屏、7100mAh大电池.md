<div>   
<p><strong><a class="f14_link" href="https://www.opposhop.cn/cn/web/products/7228.html" target="_blank">OPPO Pad Air</a>平板今日正式在OPPO商城开启预约，不过官网没有显示该平板详细规格参数。</strong></p>

<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220513/bfd6ee41be124bd9aa7ff233aea246ef.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="OPPO Pad Air平板开启预约：等宽LCD屏、7100mAh大电池" h="476" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220513/s_bfd6ee41be124bd9aa7ff233aea246ef.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="597" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>在OPPO预售页面中可以看到官方提供的1元购价值1000元新品权益，消费者抢先预付1元，即可获得价值1000元的新品权益。</p>
<p><strong>新品权益具体包括价值129元的OPPO O-Fresh耳机、价值816元的2T云空间（12个月）和价值55元商城优惠券。</strong></p>
<p>据此前博主@数码闲聊站 爆料，这款平板搭载<strong>10.36英寸2K屏</strong>，分辨率为2000×1200，<strong>并且屏幕是等宽的LCD屏。</strong></p>

<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220513/c15355f514da48ed90c734450b9e5e11.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="OPPO Pad Air平板开启预约：等宽LCD屏、7100mAh大电池" h="482" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220513/s_c15355f514da48ed90c734450b9e5e11.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="594" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>新品平板还将会搭载<strong>骁龙680处理器</strong>，电池容量为7100mAh，支持18W快充，另外搭配四扬声器，支持杜比全景声，主打千元市场。</p>
<p>二月份，OPPO推出旗下首款平板电脑OPPO Pad，搭载高通骁龙870处理器，内置8360mAh大电池，支持33W超级闪充，配备杜比全景声和Wi-Fi 6，售价2299元起。</p>
<p><strong>新款平板应该会在打造轻薄机身上下功夫，便捷智慧操作意味着OPPO还会在系统生态上加入更多的体验。</strong></p>

<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220513/bee2d87f57f0451296060ac4f497f0a1.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="OPPO Pad Air平板开启预约：等宽LCD屏、7100mAh大电池" h="466" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220513/s_bee2d87f57f0451296060ac4f497f0a1.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="594" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
