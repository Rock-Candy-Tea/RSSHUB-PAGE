<p class="MsoNormal">在今天的中国闪存市场峰会上，长江存储首席运营官程卫华透露，<strong>长江存储<span lang="EN-US">64</span>层闪存颗粒出货超<span lang="EN-US">3</span>亿颗，<span lang="EN-US">128</span>层<span lang="EN-US">QLC</span>已经准备量产，<span lang="EN-US">TLC</span>良率做到相当水准。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">长江存储是国内唯一大规模量产了<span lang="EN-US">3D</span>闪存的公司，<span lang="EN-US">2019 </span>年初实现了<span lang="EN-US">32</span>层<span lang="EN-US">3D NAND</span>量产，其首创<span lang="EN-US">Xtacking</span>技术，顺利研发出<span lang="EN-US">64</span>层<span lang="EN-US">3DNAND</span>，并于<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月量产<span lang="EN-US">256Gb</span>（<span lang="EN-US">32GB</span>）<span lang="EN-US">TLC 3DNAND</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月份，长江存储宣布推出<span lang="EN-US">128</span>层堆栈的<span lang="EN-US">3D</span>闪存。<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>该公司称<span lang="EN-US">128</span>层<span lang="EN-US">QLC 3D</span>闪存（<span lang="EN-US">X2-6070</span>）是业内首款<span lang="EN-US">128</span>层<span lang="EN-US">QLC</span>规格<span lang="EN-US">3D NAND</span>，且拥有已知型号产品中最高单位面积存储密度，最高<span lang="EN-US">I/O</span>传输速度和最高单颗<span lang="EN-US">NAND</span>闪存芯片容量。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">值得一提的是，此次同步登场的还有<span lang="EN-US">128</span>层<span lang="EN-US">TLC</span>闪存（<span lang="EN-US">X2-9060</span>），单颗容量<span lang="EN-US">512Gb(64GB)</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">性能方面，长存透露，两款产品拥有<span lang="EN-US">1.6Gbps</span>的<span lang="EN-US">I/O</span>读写性能，<span lang="EN-US">3D QLC</span>单颗容量高达<span lang="EN-US">1.33Tb</span>，是上一代<span lang="EN-US">64</span>层的<span lang="EN-US">5.33</span>倍。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">据悉，两款产品均采用长存自研<span lang="EN-US">Xtacking 2.0</span>架构，外围电路和存储单元可堆叠，带来极佳的扩展性。</p>
