<p class="MsoNormal">自从<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年推出高性能的锐龙、霄龙处理器的<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年来，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>在<span lang="EN-US">x86</span>市场份额上已经收复了失地，份额超过<span lang="EN-US">20%</span>。目前桌面、笔记本市场的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>进展不错，服务器<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>市场上也有望重返巅峰，拿下<span lang="EN-US">25%</span>的市场。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">与消费级<span lang="EN-US">x86</span>市场相比，服务器<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>市场的突围更难，不仅仅是看性价比的，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>现在的<span lang="EN-US">7nm Zen2/3</span>架构的霄龙做到了<span lang="EN-US">64</span>核<span lang="EN-US">128</span>线程，性能给力，价格也有优势，也逐渐得到了微软、谷歌、<span lang="EN-US">FB</span>、亚马逊等大客户的支持。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">当然，大客户升级换代的过程要复杂很多，所以<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>现在服务器<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>市场上的份额才恢复到<span lang="EN-US">10%</span>左右，数年来已经增长<span lang="EN-US">5</span>倍，而<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>在皓龙时代创造的最高纪录就是四分之一天下，份额超过<span lang="EN-US">25%</span>。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="5nm Zen4在手 AMD将重返巅峰：夺回25%的服务器CPU市场" h="318" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210704/c98aee1f-842f-49ae-ae89-93e4e09186cd.png" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="text-align: center"><img alt="5nm Zen4在手 AMD将重返巅峰：夺回25%的服务器CPU市场" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210704/e65e3204-70a4-46a0-91af-8a274aa8f28b.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">美国银行日前发表报告，认为<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的市值在未来两年还会继续增长<span lang="EN-US">10-20%</span>，看好他们的关键就是<span lang="EN-US">EPYC</span>服务器<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>能够超越之前的巅峰纪录，增长空间很大。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>下一代<span lang="EN-US">EPYC</span>服务器<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>是<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>架构，根据最新的爆料，<span lang="EN-US">AMD Zen4</span>家族的霄龙将有两个分支，<b>一是官方早已公布的<span lang="EN-US">Genoa(</span>热那亚<span lang="EN-US">)</span>，可以视为标准版，最多增加到<span lang="EN-US">96</span>核心<span lang="EN-US">192</span>线程，继续和<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>正常竞争，对标计划明年发布的<span lang="EN-US">Sapphire Rapids</span>四代可扩展至强。</b></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">另外一个是<b>首次曝出的<span lang="EN-US">“Bergamo”(</span>也是意大利城市贝加莫<span lang="EN-US">)</span>，将会做到<span lang="EN-US">128</span>核心<span lang="EN-US">256</span>线程，<span lang="EN-US">Genoa</span>之后登场，大概在<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年底或<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年初，但对手已经不再是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>，而是苹果、<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>，竞争的是云<span lang="EN-US">SoC</span>市场。</b></p>
