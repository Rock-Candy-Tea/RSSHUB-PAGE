<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">据报道，5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日美股收盘，三日来连续重挫，标普<span lang="EN-US">500</span>指数收跌<span lang="EN-US">132.10</span>点，跌幅<span lang="EN-US">3.2%</span>，报<span lang="EN-US">3991.24</span>点，自去年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月来首次跌破<span lang="EN-US">4000</span>点，与道指齐创<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月来最低，从<span lang="EN-US">52</span>周高位回落<span lang="EN-US">17%</span>逼近熊市。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">道指收跌<span lang="EN-US">653.67</span>点，跌幅<span lang="EN-US">1.99%</span>，报<span lang="EN-US">32245.70</span>点，较<span lang="EN-US">52</span>周高位回落<span lang="EN-US">12.7%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">纳指、纳指<span lang="EN-US">100</span>和罗素小盘股均刷新<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月来最低。纳指收跌<span lang="EN-US">521.41</span>点，跌幅<span lang="EN-US">4.29%</span>，报<span lang="EN-US">11623.25</span>点。纳指<span lang="EN-US">100</span>收跌<span lang="EN-US">505.81</span>点，跌幅<span lang="EN-US">3.98%</span>。罗素小盘股收跌<span lang="EN-US">4.2%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">美国股市中的科技股及芯片股全面下跌，其中<span lang="EN-US">Meta</span>跌<span lang="EN-US">3.7%</span>，抹去<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日以来全部涨幅，亚马逊跌<span lang="EN-US">5.2%</span>，至<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月来最低。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>苹果跌<span lang="EN-US">3.3%</span>，回吐<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日来涨幅。微软跌<span lang="EN-US">3.7%</span>，至去年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月来最低。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">奈飞跌<span lang="EN-US">4.4%</span>，至<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月来最低。谷歌母公司<span lang="EN-US">Alphabet</span>跌<span lang="EN-US">2.9%</span>，至一年新低。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">特斯拉跌<span lang="EN-US">9%</span>，失守<span lang="EN-US">800</span>美元整数位，回吐<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日来全部涨幅。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">费城半导体指数跌超<span lang="EN-US">5%</span>并失守<span lang="EN-US">2900</span>点，连跌三日至一年新低。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>跌<span lang="EN-US">2.8%</span>，高通跌<span lang="EN-US">4%</span>。<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>跌<span lang="EN-US">9.4%</span>，回吐过去一周全部涨幅，<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>跌<span lang="EN-US">9.2%</span>，至去年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月以来最低。</strong></p>
