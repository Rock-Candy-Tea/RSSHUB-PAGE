<p>不知从什么时候起，当我们说一款手机的夜拍能力出色时，会说它能够媲美“夜视仪”。</p>
<p>而今天发布的iQOO 10 Pro，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>却通过自研V1+芯片的加持，实现了实时黑光夜视，做到了在开启相机后夜晚环境下也能看清场景，可以说是真成“夜视仪”了。</strong></span></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220719/1934fed03a134423a3a81afb4674206d.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="真成“夜视仪”了：iQOO 10 Pro实现实时黑光夜视" h="800" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220719/s_1934fed03a134423a3a81afb4674206d.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>通过该功能，即便是人眼已经看不太清的环境，iQOO 10 Pro也并能够获得不错的拍摄效果。</p>
<p>值得一提的是，除了夜视功能，<strong>iQOO 10 Pro在摄影的硬实力上也同样可圈可点。</strong></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220719/5d1e08b719fb4055a75c5e0639842f27.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="真成“夜视仪”了：iQOO 10 Pro实现实时黑光夜视" h="799" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220719/s_5d1e08b719fb4055a75c5e0639842f27.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p><strong>iQOO 10 Pro搭载了一颗5000万像素1/1.57英寸大底，并有微云台防抖支持</strong>；此外，它还搭载了一颗5000万像素150°鱼眼超广角镜头，以及一颗最大3倍光学变焦，40倍混合变焦的长焦镜头。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220719/e8f01f11d42c45c4a28e97a1a66b3c17.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="真成“夜视仪”了：iQOO 10 Pro实现实时黑光夜视" h="799" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220719/s_e8f01f11d42c45c4a28e97a1a66b3c17.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>此外，iQOO 10 Pro还支持XDR Photo、原色纯净夜景、地平线矫正、极夜夜景视频、追焦摇镜等特色功能。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220719/94269773a8c34c0b86a37a6003b83c59.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="真成“夜视仪”了：iQOO 10 Pro实现实时黑光夜视" h="879" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220719/s_94269773a8c34c0b86a37a6003b83c59.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
