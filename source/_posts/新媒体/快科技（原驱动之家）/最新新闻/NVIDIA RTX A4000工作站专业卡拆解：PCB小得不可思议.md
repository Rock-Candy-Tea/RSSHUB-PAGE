<p>显卡拆解稀松平常，但是你见过将工作站专业显卡大卸八块的吗？国外网友就拆了一块NVIDIA RTX A4000。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><img alt="NVIDIA RTX A4000工作站专业卡拆解：PCB小得不可思议" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210815/d1662469-0e07-4cbc-9204-93eba124d066.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p><a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/750/750779.htm" target="_blank">RTX A4000是今年4月发布的</a>，Ampere架构，GA104核心，完整的6144个CUDA核心，搭配256-bit 16GB GDDR6显存并支持ECC，功耗仅140W，四个DP1.4输出接口，采用典型的单个涡轮风扇暴力散热。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210815/8b955965a100420fbd30e4eedd2f4935.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="NVIDIA RTX A4000工作站专业卡拆解：PCB小得不可思议" h="430" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210815/s_8b955965a100420fbd30e4eedd2f4935.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>首先拿下外壳，露出内部的散热片和部分供电元件。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210815/2b3c7eead518474fb14c7ed9887e6bff.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="NVIDIA RTX A4000工作站专业卡拆解：PCB小得不可思议" h="297" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210815/s_2b3c7eead518474fb14c7ed9887e6bff.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>取下散热片就可以看到GPU核心，以及几乎全覆盖的支架。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210815/e6c84f6e6c0b4dcb91682281223241ac.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="NVIDIA RTX A4000工作站专业卡拆解：PCB小得不可思议" h="283" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210815/s_e6c84f6e6c0b4dcb91682281223241ac.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>涡轮风扇，专业卡标配，散热效率高，但转速高、噪音大。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210815/8363fe5f4a1f4924b7c4bb94a61fc104.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="NVIDIA RTX A4000工作站专业卡拆解：PCB小得不可思议" h="283" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210815/s_8363fe5f4a1f4924b7c4bb94a61fc104.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>去掉中间的支架，<strong>PCB就完整暴露了出来，可见尺寸非常之小，比很多迷你卡还要小</strong>，GPU芯片右侧只有显存和电感，然后就没了。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210815/1f874622edec4d65ac6da19a865917bb.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="NVIDIA RTX A4000工作站专业卡拆解：PCB小得不可思议" h="389" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210815/s_1f874622edec4d65ac6da19a865917bb.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>核心编号<strong>GA104-875-A1</strong>，RTX 3070 Ti上的则是GA104-400。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210815/cebabd3d533644159c7438cbf6736ee9.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="NVIDIA RTX A4000工作站专业卡拆解：PCB小得不可思议" h="371" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210815/s_cebabd3d533644159c7438cbf6736ee9.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>来自<strong>三星的GDDR6显存</strong>，编号K4ZAF325BM-HC16，单颗2GB，总共八颗，均在正面。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210815/8d16cdb4080b42a6954f28e7f54432cb.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="NVIDIA RTX A4000工作站专业卡拆解：PCB小得不可思议" h="430" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210815/s_8d16cdb4080b42a6954f28e7f54432cb.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>GPU核心对应的散热模组，可见硕大的铜底和热管。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210815/1c9fd87ecb79472fabab526b2c301bac.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="NVIDIA RTX A4000工作站专业卡拆解：PCB小得不可思议" h="303" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210815/s_1c9fd87ecb79472fabab526b2c301bac.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>最后是背部照，从这个角度也可以看出PCB的短小精悍。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210815/f013990dfc6b478ead2997efdc774e98.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="NVIDIA RTX A4000工作站专业卡拆解：PCB小得不可思议" h="321" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210815/s_f013990dfc6b478ead2997efdc774e98.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>PS：之前不少厂商发布了涡轮风扇散热的RTX 3090，但很快就消失了——NVIDIA不允许在非专业卡上这么做。</p>
