<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>公司去年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月份换了<span lang="EN-US">CEO</span>，有着<span lang="EN-US">30</span>多年资历的老将基辛格回归<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>担任<span lang="EN-US">CEO</span>，圆了十多年前出走<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>时的梦想，当时他还是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>首任<span lang="EN-US">CTO</span>，但在<span lang="EN-US">CEO</span>竞选上输给了前前前任<span lang="EN-US">CEO</span>奥特里尼。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">担任<span lang="EN-US">Intel CEO</span>的职位虽然只有<span lang="EN-US">11</span>个月，<a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/823/823435.htm" target="_blank">但是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>董事会为了说服他可是给出了超级丰厚的薪酬，</a><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>根据<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>提交给<span lang="EN-US">SEC</span>的监管文件，基辛格<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年的总薪酬高达<span lang="EN-US">1.7859</span>亿美元，约合人民币<span lang="EN-US">11.3</span>亿元。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">对于这样的高管来说，如此巨额的薪酬实际上是包括了大量股权奖励的，基辛格去年的基本工资只有<span lang="EN-US">110</span>万美元，奖金是<span lang="EN-US">175</span>万美元，期权奖励是<span lang="EN-US">2910</span>万美元，非期权补偿是<span lang="EN-US">511</span>万美元，其他补偿大概<span lang="EN-US">100</span>多万美元，而核心价格是<span lang="EN-US">1.4</span>亿美元的股票奖励，占了薪酬的<span lang="EN-US">8</span>成。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">问题来了，<span lang="EN-US">Intel CEO</span>去年收入有<span lang="EN-US">11</span>亿元多，那<span lang="EN-US">AMD CEO</span>苏姿丰的年薪是多少？</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">这也很好算，根据<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>提交的资料，<strong><span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年中苏姿丰的薪酬是<span lang="EN-US">2950</span>万美元，算起来都不到<span lang="EN-US">Intel CEO</span>的<span lang="EN-US">1/5</span>，连他的零头的不到。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在苏妈的收入中，基本工资是<span lang="EN-US">108</span>万美元，期权奖励是<span lang="EN-US">516</span>万美元，非期权奖励是<span lang="EN-US">319</span>万美元，其他补偿<span lang="EN-US">14</span>万，而贡献大头收入的还是股票奖励，大约有<span lang="EN-US">1930</span>万美元。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年中，苏妈在<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的薪酬是<span lang="EN-US">1335</span>万美元，<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年拿到了<span lang="EN-US">5853.4</span>万美元的总薪酬，约合<span lang="EN-US">4.1</span>亿人民币，主要是跟当时<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>股价大涨有关。</p>
