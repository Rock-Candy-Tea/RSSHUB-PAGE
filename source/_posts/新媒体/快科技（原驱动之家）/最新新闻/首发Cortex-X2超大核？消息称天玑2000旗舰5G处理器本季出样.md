<p class="MsoNormal">在推出天玑<span lang="EN-US">1000</span>系列<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>处理器之后，联发科在高端<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>手机市场上取得了突破，下一代旗舰处理器天玑<span lang="EN-US">2000</span>也酝酿很久了，不仅会升级台积电<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>工艺，据说还会首发<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>新一代<span lang="EN-US">CPU/GPU</span>架构，性能很强大。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年国内的<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>市场还会继续增长，联发科的天玑<span lang="EN-US">2000</span>这次瞄准了旗舰级市场，预计<span lang="EN-US">Q4</span>季度正式推出，比早前传闻的<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">Q1</span>季度提前了一个季度，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>本季度中就会出样给客户，预计小米、<span lang="EN-US">OPPO</span>、<span lang="EN-US">vivo</span>、荣耀等品牌都会采用。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">天玑<span lang="EN-US">2000</span>处理器除了升级<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>工艺，架构也会大变，业界消息称会首发<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>最新一代的<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>、<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>架构，比如超大核<span lang="EN-US">Cortex-X2</span>、大核<span lang="EN-US">Cortex-A710</span>、小核<span lang="EN-US">Cortex-A510</span>，<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>则是<span lang="EN-US">Mali-G79</span>，此前<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>宣称<span lang="EN-US">X2</span>相比于<span lang="EN-US">X1</span>整数性能提升<span lang="EN-US">16</span>％，机器学习性能则可以翻一番。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">天玑<span lang="EN-US">2000</span>是瞄准高通新一代处理器，如果传闻中的规格属实，<strong>超过骁龙<span lang="EN-US">888/888 Plus</span>应该无压力，但应该要比高通下代的<span lang="EN-US">4nm</span>骁龙<span lang="EN-US">895</span>处理器略低一些，</strong>继续打差异化策略。</p>
