<p>昨天，OPPO正式官宣新一代智能手表OPPO Watch 3，并表示它将全球首发高通骁龙W5可穿戴平台。</p>
<p>今天，<strong>根据最新消息，OPPO Watch 3能够带给我们的惊喜还远不于此。</strong></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220803/3f1a0e6b82ee4ff2b3ccea5e1495445f.png" target="_blank"><img alt="最强安卓版Apple Watch看来了！OPPO Watch 3集齐ECG、eSIM、NFC" h="504" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220803/s_3f1a0e6b82ee4ff2b3ccea5e1495445f.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>据悉，OPPO Watch 3系列在外观上将采用一块1.91英寸AMOLED LTPO方形表盘，搭配3D微弧双曲+可旋转表冠设计。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220803/4dcd0d9e46ef4688be75a3480e865935.png" target="_blank"><img alt="最强安卓版Apple Watch看来了！OPPO Watch 3集齐ECG、eSIM、NFC" h="553" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220803/s_4dcd0d9e46ef4688be75a3480e865935.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>功能上，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>它则会标配ECG、eSIM、NFC三大功能，在确保独立联网和虚拟钱包这亮相基本功能的同时，增加了心电图功能。</strong></span></p>
<p>此外，<strong>OPPO Watch 3还将搭载新的Apollo4 Plus旗舰级协处理器</strong>，在拥有更高图像显示能力的同时，也拥有更低功耗，作到了全智能5天、轻智能15天的长续航。</p>
<p>毫不夸张的说，在这套硬件配置的加持下，<strong>OPPO Watch 3将有望对标苹果的Apple Watch。</strong></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220803/c1b53ee22272486780fa088d42cc459d.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="最强安卓版Apple Watch看来了！OPPO Watch 3集齐ECG、eSIM、NFC" h="1066" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220803/s_c1b53ee22272486780fa088d42cc459d.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
