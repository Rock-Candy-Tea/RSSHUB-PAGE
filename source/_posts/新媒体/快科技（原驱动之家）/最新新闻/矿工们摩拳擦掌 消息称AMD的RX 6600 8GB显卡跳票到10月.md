<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的<span lang="EN-US">RX 6000</span>系列显卡已经到<span lang="EN-US">RX 6600 XT</span>了，再往下一款就是<span lang="EN-US">RX 6600</span>，<a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/781/781026.htm" target="_blank">此前已经确认是<span lang="EN-US">8GB</span>显存</a>，三风扇设计，不过发布时间比预期晚，要跳票到<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月份了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">今年显卡市场形势多变，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>及<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>两边的产品布局都有些意外，<span lang="EN-US">RX 6700</span>显卡一直没影子，<span lang="EN-US">RX 6600</span>显卡原本是跟<span lang="EN-US">RX 6600 XT</span>一起发布的，<strong>现在也明确发布时间，之前说拖到<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月，现在又说拖到<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月。</strong></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="矿工们摩拳擦掌 消息称AMD的RX 6600 8GB显卡跳票到10月" h="323" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210906/ab664af9-cb4e-4d1d-8bb9-df6fed8ee740.png" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">即便<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月能发布，<span lang="EN-US">RX 6600</span>显卡的供应量也不太乐观，因为<span lang="EN-US">7nm RDNA2</span>芯片成本不低，越做低端卡越没有利润，<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>那边连<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3050</span>显卡桌面版都一直没信呢，更何况<span lang="EN-US">7nm</span>芯片的<span lang="EN-US">RX 6600</span>显卡。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">考虑到<span lang="EN-US">RX 6600 XT</span>名义上定价<span lang="EN-US">2999</span>元，那么<span lang="EN-US">RX 6600</span>显卡应该在<span lang="EN-US">2500</span>元内，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>但是其性能差不多接近<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3060</span>，再加上还是<span lang="EN-US">8GB</span>显存，挖矿的工头早就盯上了，</strong></span>不出意外一上市就要被哄抬价格到<span lang="EN-US">4000</span>元左右，<span lang="EN-US">DIY</span>玩家太难了。</p>
