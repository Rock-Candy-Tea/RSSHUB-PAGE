<p align="center"><a href="http://click.aliyun.com/m/1000253340/" target="_blank" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://icons.mydrivers.com/2021/news/ayun72890.png" width="600" height="74" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
           </p><p class="MsoNormal">自从矿卡火了之后，游戏显卡市场就没好过，<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>也重新进入矿卡市场，推出了专用的矿卡，现在有比特币矿厂入手了，而且一下子就买了<span lang="EN-US">60%</span>的矿卡。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>全球最大的比特币矿厂<span lang="EN-US">Hut 8 Mining Corp</span>宣布他们已购买了价值<span lang="EN-US">3000</span>万美元的<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA CMP</span>矿卡</strong></span>，定于<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月下旬开始交付，预计将于夏季完成全面部署。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>据悉，这个新的挖矿系统可提供约<span lang="EN-US">1600 GH/s</span>的算力。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此外，该公司将使用<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>扩展其以太币和备用区块链挖矿工作，同时以比特币形式发放支出。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">今年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月底，<a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/741/741984.htm" target="_blank"><span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>宣布推出<span lang="EN-US">CMP</span>系列矿卡</a>，包括四款不同型号<span lang="EN-US">30HX</span>、<span lang="EN-US">40HX</span>、<span lang="EN-US">50HX</span>、<span lang="EN-US">90HX</span>，授权经销商包括华硕、七彩虹、<span lang="EN-US">EVGA</span>、技嘉、微星、同德、栢能，两款低端型号本季度末上市，两款高端型号下季度跟进。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">算力方面，<strong><span lang="EN-US">30HX</span>、<span lang="EN-US">40HX</span>、<span lang="EN-US">50HX</span>、<span lang="EN-US">90HX</span>的以太坊挖矿哈希算力分别为<span lang="EN-US">26MH/s</span>、<span lang="EN-US">36MH/s</span>、<span lang="EN-US">45MH/s</span>、<span lang="EN-US">96MH/s</span>，功耗分别为<span lang="EN-US">125W</span>、<span lang="EN-US">185W</span>、<span lang="EN-US">250W</span>、<span lang="EN-US">320W</span>。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此外，这些矿卡取消了视频输出接口，也不能运行<span lang="EN-US">3D</span>游戏，只为挖矿而生。</p>
