<p class="MsoNormal">台积电<span lang="EN-US">7nm</span>及以下的先进工艺现在成了各大芯片公司争抢的香饽饽，明年量产的<span lang="EN-US">3nm</span>工艺被<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>、苹果预定了，<span lang="EN-US">7nm</span>、<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>产能也紧张了。其中<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>明年也会回归台积电制造，<span lang="EN-US">RTX 40</span>系列会升级<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>工艺。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>去年底推出的<span lang="EN-US">RTX 30</span>系列显卡升级了安培架构，但除了服务器级的<span lang="EN-US">GA100</span>大核心是<span lang="EN-US">7nm</span>工艺之外，游戏卡用的还是三星<span lang="EN-US">8nm</span>工艺，尽管只差了<span lang="EN-US">1</span>个数，但<span lang="EN-US">8nm</span>实际上是<span lang="EN-US">10nm</span>工艺的改进版，差距是一代工艺。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">尽管<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>官方对三星<span lang="EN-US">8nm</span>工艺的表现表示满意，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>然而还是有消息称三星的产能不够，所以<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年的<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA GPU</span>芯片会转向台积电的<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>，更可能的是增强版的<span lang="EN-US">N5P</span>工艺。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">下代<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>同样分为两个方向，服务器级的代号<span lang="EN-US">Hooper</span>，游戏卡<span lang="EN-US">RTX 40</span>系列代号<span lang="EN-US">Ada Lovelace——</span>阿达<span lang="EN-US">·</span>洛芙莱斯女士，诗人拜伦唯一合法的女儿，第一位计算机科学家，编写了历史上第一个计算机程序，人称<span lang="EN-US">“</span>数字女王<span lang="EN-US">”</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">新架构的大核心编号<span lang="EN-US">AD102</span>，将有多达<span lang="EN-US">12</span>个<span lang="EN-US">GPC(</span>图形处理集群<span lang="EN-US">)</span>、<span lang="EN-US">72</span>个<span lang="EN-US">TPC(</span>纹理处理集群<span lang="EN-US">)</span>、<span lang="EN-US">144</span>个<span lang="EN-US">SM(</span>流式多处理器<span lang="EN-US">)</span>，而如果每个<span lang="EN-US">SM</span>继续<span lang="EN-US">128</span>个流处理器<span lang="EN-US">(CUDA</span>核心<span lang="EN-US">)</span>，那么整体下来就有多达<span lang="EN-US">18432</span>个流处理器！</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>相比于目前安培架构<span lang="EN-US">GA102</span>核心的<span lang="EN-US">10752</span>个，这一下子就增加了超过<span lang="EN-US">71</span>％！</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">规模提升再加上架构改进，<strong><span style="color:#ff0000;">传闻称<span lang="EN-US">RTX 40</span>旗舰<span lang="EN-US">RTX 4090</span>的性能将达到<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3090</span>的两倍，也就是提升<span lang="EN-US">100%</span>。</span></strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不过发布时间不太乐观，此前NVIDIA CFO暗示要到2023年才有新一代的GPU，目前来看，RTX 40系列最快明年Q4发布，更可能的是2023年Q1季度了。</p>
