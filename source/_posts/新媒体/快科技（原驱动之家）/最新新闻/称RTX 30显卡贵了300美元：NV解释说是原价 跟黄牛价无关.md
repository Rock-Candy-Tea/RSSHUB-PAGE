<p class="MsoNormal">日前<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>称玩家在<span lang="EN-US">RTX 30</span>系列显卡上多花了<span lang="EN-US">300</span>美元，<a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/822/822205.htm" target="_blank">这番表态立即引发了全球玩家的热议</a>，毕竟<span lang="EN-US">RTX 30</span>系列显卡的涨价很夸张，不过<span lang="EN-US">NVIDA</span>随后澄清说大家误会了，他们说的是显卡的原价，跟黄牛价无关。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">NVIDAI</span>这个表态是<span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">NVIDIA</span><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">高级副总裁杰夫</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">·</span><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">费舍尔</span>在之前的投资者会议上发布的。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;"></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">费舍尔提到<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>提供的价值比以往任何时候都多，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>根据<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>的数据，玩家升级到<span lang="EN-US">RTX 30</span>系显卡比前一代显卡多花了<span lang="EN-US">300</span>美元</strong></span><span lang="EN-US">——</span>换算人民币的话，差不多<span lang="EN-US">1911</span>元，这还是平均值。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="称RTX 30显卡贵了300美元：NV解释说是原价 跟黄牛价无关" h="318" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220325/aeb0dbe5-a7b8-4c8f-9e4a-f22e9627517c.png" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">过去的一年多中<span lang="EN-US">PC</span>玩家早就被显卡的高价所影响，一肚子火呢，<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>现在竟然把显卡涨价当做业绩来炫耀？所以这番话引发了玩家的不满，<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>公司不得不出来澄清，解释这个<span lang="EN-US">300</span>美元的含义。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">PCWorld</span>网站报道称，<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>给他们解释了这个<span lang="EN-US">300</span>美元是怎么回事，<strong>所为的贵了<span lang="EN-US">300</span>美元指的是建议价，而不是显卡的实际售价，他们指的是玩家在<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年花费<span lang="EN-US">400</span>美元购买了<span lang="EN-US">GTX 1070</span>显卡，那么在<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年会花费<span lang="EN-US">700</span>美元购买<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3080</span>显卡。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">然而原文提到，<span lang="EN-US">700</span>美元在<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年买<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3080</span>显卡是不可能的，由于供应短缺及黄牛炒作，<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3080</span>显卡的实际价格早就变了，在当前也要<span lang="EN-US">1200</span>美元。</p>
