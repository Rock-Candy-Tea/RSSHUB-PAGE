<p>四门电驱车的纽伯格林最快记录由保时捷Taycan Turbo于2019年创造（7'42.39），特斯拉最近交付的Model S Plaid有望打破这个记录。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210727/53a4c131f72e4bfcaa3140485cce40cb.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="特斯拉冲击纽伯格林新纪录" h="325" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210727/s_53a4c131f72e4bfcaa3140485cce40cb.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>拥有1020马力的高性能版Model S Plaid数周前开始交车，随即出现在纽伯格林赛道上。从谍照判断内部可能安装了防滚架，选配了21寸Arachnid轮毂和米其林轮胎。按照马斯克的说法，武装到牙齿的Model S Plaid有望将纽伯格林赛道记录缩短至不足七分钟，“堪称丰功伟绩”。</p>
<p>但保时捷手里还有Taycan Turbo S，其产品经理Lukas Kramer公开表示保时捷电车能在这条赛道上跑得更快；AMG也在谋划性能版EQS及EQE，电车圈速之争才刚刚开始……</p>
<p>此外，有人发现在纽伯格林赛道上飞奔的Model S Plaid居然使用传统式样方向盘：</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210727/e4b9d479e7a441a9ba0e635484bebd15.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="特斯拉冲击纽伯格林新纪录" h="325" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210727/s_e4b9d479e7a441a9ba0e635484bebd15.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210727/6ca53b74735d431faaf6be9ad6c8fa0f.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="特斯拉冲击纽伯格林新纪录" h="324" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210727/s_6ca53b74735d431faaf6be9ad6c8fa0f.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210727/a2922efe6dd74eb9b7baa2ca87ae9f3b.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="特斯拉冲击纽伯格林新纪录" h="324" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210727/s_a2922efe6dd74eb9b7baa2ca87ae9f3b.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>之前有人问过马斯克，Plaid是否提供传统式样方向盘，马斯克表示没有可能。</p>
<p>Plaid默认配备“Yoke”方向盘，式样接近方程式赛车。但车评人对Yoke的评价不高，认为这东西看着很酷，用起来一言难尽。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210727/45fb9dabc2494a3881838f78c94b25c1.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="特斯拉冲击纽伯格林新纪录" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210727/s_45fb9dabc2494a3881838f78c94b25c1.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>显然，特斯拉自己心里也没底，于是借着创纪录的机会，让车手对比两种方向盘，用圈速说话。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210727/b498a5cd489743b7a2408a1e2d792db5.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="特斯拉冲击纽伯格林新纪录" h="324" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210727/s_b498a5cd489743b7a2408a1e2d792db5.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210727/39108d687e6b4bcea58190004232a8ba.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="特斯拉冲击纽伯格林新纪录" h="325" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210727/s_39108d687e6b4bcea58190004232a8ba.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210727/0f81babe15b24cb0b9c2e80b48bc996c.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="特斯拉冲击纽伯格林新纪录" h="324" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210727/s_0f81babe15b24cb0b9c2e80b48bc996c.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>最后，路虎新款卫士可能换用宝马4.4升8缸涡轮增压发动机，账面数据远超卫士老款V8发动机（600+马力 vs. 518马力）。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210727/714f7d52aa35495ebff4668f8295f3a7.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="特斯拉冲击纽伯格林新纪录" h="400" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210727/s_714f7d52aa35495ebff4668f8295f3a7.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>V8款卫士名为“Defender SVR”，换发后的90短轴版其零百加速成绩有望达到4.9秒，极速240公里/小时。车价则逼近12.5万美元，明年问世。</p>
