<p>近日，外媒Wccftech汇总了可升级iOS15和iPad OS15系统的设备清单。该媒体认为苹果可能会放弃对iPhone6S、iPad Air2及更老设备的更新支持。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><img alt="iOS15升级设备清单曝光 iPhone6S老设备无缘更新" h="330" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210512/0fe4fb50-8f4d-48c1-9ddd-6579554b90c7.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid" w="550" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>详细可升级iOS15/iPad OS15的设备列表：</p>
<p>iPhone 12, iPhone 12 Pro</p>
<p>Phone 12 Pro Max</p>
<p>iPhone 12 mini</p>
<p>iPhone 11 Pro Max, iPhone 11 Pro, iPhone XS Max, iPhone XS</p>
<p>iPhone 11, iPhone XR</p>
<p>iPhone X</p>
<p>iPhone 8, iPhone 7</p>
<p>iPhone 8 Plus, iPhone 7 Plus</p>
<p>2020 iPhone SE 2</p>
<p>iPod touch seventh-generation</p>
<p>iPad Air 4</p>
<p>10.2-inch iPad 8</p>
<p>11-in. iPad Pro (1st and 2nd generations), 12.9-in. iPad Pro (3rd and 4th generations)</p>
<p>10.5-inch iPad Pro (1st generation), 12.9-inch iPad Pro (2nd generation)</p>
<p>iPad (5th generation), iPad (6th generation)</p>
<p>iPad mini (5th generation), iPad Air (3rd generation)</p>
<p>10.2-inch iPad (7th generation)</p>
<p>9.7-inch iPad Pro (1st generation)</p>
<p>12.9-inch iPad Pro (1st generation)</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><img alt="iOS15升级设备清单曝光 iPhone6S老设备无缘更新" h="341" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210512/a73898e1-aaa7-46ec-a657-b90fbae6be11.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid" w="550" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>不过，值得一提的是，该清单并未得到苹果方面的确认，具体升级计划需要以苹果官方公布的信息为准。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><img alt="iOS15升级设备清单曝光 iPhone6S老设备无缘更新" h="550" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210512/aa91614b-a2a3-4ebc-8489-8b349d5229d1.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid" w="550" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>据悉，iOS15会对控制中心、控制边栏、工具栏进行重新设计，新的设计界面将会于mac OS Big Sur风格接近，界面以简洁明快为主，小工具和控件也会变得更为紧凑。按照惯例来看，iOS15将会于今年秋季正式与大家见面。</p>
