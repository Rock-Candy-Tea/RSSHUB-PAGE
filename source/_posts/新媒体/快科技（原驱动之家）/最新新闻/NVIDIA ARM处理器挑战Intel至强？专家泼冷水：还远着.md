<p class="MsoNormal">在前两天的<span lang="EN-US">GTC 2021</span>大会上，<a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/750/750575.htm" target="_blank"><span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>推出了一款高性能<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>处理器——<span lang="EN-US">Grace</span></a>，主要用于高性能计算，包括超算、数据中心等等领域。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Grace</span>处理器的架构还很神秘，<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>公布的细节不多，但它<span lang="EN-US">ARM Neoverse</span>架构，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>搭配<span lang="EN-US">LPDDR5X</span>内存可提供超过<span lang="EN-US">500GB/s</span>的带宽，而且支持<span lang="EN-US">ECC</span>错误校验，通过缓存一致性<span lang="EN-US">NVLink</span>总线连接自家<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>，带宽可超过<span lang="EN-US">900GB/s</span>，同时两颗<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>之间的带宽超过<span lang="EN-US">600GB/s</span>。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Grace</span>宣布之后，瑞士国家超级计算中心<span lang="EN-US">(CSCS)</span>、慧与<span lang="EN-US">(HPE)</span>、<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>联合使用<span lang="EN-US">Grace CPU</span>、下一代<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA GPU</span>，正在共同打造号称世界最快<span lang="EN-US">AI</span>超算的<span lang="EN-US">“ALPS”(</span>阿尔卑斯山<span lang="EN-US">)</span>，算力高达<span lang="EN-US">20ExaFlops(</span>每秒<span lang="EN-US">2</span>千亿亿次计算<span lang="EN-US">)</span>，主要用于天气研究与预报、材料科学等。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>不是第一次第一次做<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>处理器，但这次是首次做高性能自研<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>处理器，那<span lang="EN-US">Grace</span>到底能给<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>带来什么意义？</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">有分析认为<span lang="EN-US">Grace</span>的问世，意味着<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>开始在<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>市场上挑战<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>，后者的至强处理器现在是高性能计算<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>中的王者，<span lang="EN-US">TOP500</span>超算中绝大多数是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>至强<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不过<span lang="EN-US">NIVDIA</span>挑战<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的目标不小，并不是所有人都看好，<strong>花旗银行分析师认为<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>处理器还确认广泛的生态系统，这会阻碍<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>的雄心。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">New Street Research</span>的分析师也是类似的看法，认为<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>的<span lang="EN-US">Grace</span>处理器只可能占领部分市场，<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>也不会显著扩大销售市场，受欢迎程度甚至比不上自家的<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>处理器。</p>
