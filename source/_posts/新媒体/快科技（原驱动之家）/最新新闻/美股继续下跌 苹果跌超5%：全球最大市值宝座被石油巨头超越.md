<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，美国股市继续下跌，三大指数集体收跌，领跌的纳指收跌<span lang="EN-US">3.18%</span>，报<span lang="EN-US">11364.24</span>点，创<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日以来收盘新低。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">标普收跌<span lang="EN-US">1.65%</span>，报<span lang="EN-US">3935.21</span>点，收创去年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日以来新低，周一自去年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日以来首次收盘跌破<span lang="EN-US">4000</span>点。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>科技股依然是下跌的重灾区，其中特斯拉收跌近<span lang="EN-US">8.3%</span>，收创去年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日以来新低。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">FAANMG</span>六大科技股中，奈飞跌近<span lang="EN-US">6.4%</span>、跌至<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月以来低谷，苹果跌近<span lang="EN-US">5.2%</span>，创去年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月以来新低，<span lang="EN-US">Facebook</span>母公司<span lang="EN-US">Meta</span>跌<span lang="EN-US">4.5%</span>，创<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月以来新低，微软跌<span lang="EN-US">3.3%</span>，创去年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月以来新低，亚马逊跌<span lang="EN-US">3.2%</span>，创<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月以来新低，谷歌母公司<span lang="EN-US">Alphabet</span>跌近<span lang="EN-US">0.7%</span>，创去年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月以来新低。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">其中坐拥全球第一大市值的苹果在连续下跌之后，市值第一的宝座已经被抢走，让给了石油巨头沙特阿美，周三盘中，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>沙特阿美的股价冲上历史最高水平，市值约为<span lang="EN-US">2.43</span>万亿美元，自<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年以来首次超过了苹果公司的市值；而苹果下跌<span lang="EN-US">4.4%</span>，至<span lang="EN-US">147.53</span>美元，市值为<span lang="EN-US">2.38</span>万亿美元，且日内跌幅不断扩大。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">今年以来，在一系列因素的影响下，全球最大石油公司沙特阿美（<span lang="EN-US">Saudi Aramco</span>）在<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年上涨了<span lang="EN-US">28</span>％，而苹果自年初创下<span lang="EN-US">3</span>万亿美元的记录之后多次下滑，股价今年跌了<span lang="EN-US">16%</span>。</p>
