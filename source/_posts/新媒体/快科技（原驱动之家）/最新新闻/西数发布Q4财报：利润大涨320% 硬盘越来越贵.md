<p class="MsoNormal">西数公司日前发布了<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>财年<span lang="EN-US">Q4</span>财报，截至<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，当季营收<span lang="EN-US">49.2</span>亿美元，同比增长<span lang="EN-US">15%</span>，环比增长<span lang="EN-US">19%</span>；净利润<span lang="EN-US">6.22</span>亿美元，同比增长<span lang="EN-US">320%</span>，环比增长<span lang="EN-US">216%</span>。每股摊薄收益<span lang="EN-US">1.97</span>美元，同比增长<span lang="EN-US">302%</span>，环比增长<span lang="EN-US">214%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">西数旗下主要有闪存及<span lang="EN-US">HDD</span>硬盘两大业务，其中闪存收入为<span lang="EN-US">24</span>亿美元，环比增长<span lang="EN-US"> 11%</span>，同比增长<span lang="EN-US"> 8%</span>，<span lang="EN-US">bit</span>出货量环比增长约<span lang="EN-US">4%</span>，平均售价增长约<span lang="EN-US">4%-7%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>硬盘收入为<span lang="EN-US">25</span>亿美元，环比增长<span lang="EN-US"> 28%</span>，同比增长<span lang="EN-US"> 22%</span>，<span lang="EN-US">EB</span>总出货量环比增长<span lang="EN-US">34%</span>，<span lang="EN-US">ASP</span>均价增长了<span lang="EN-US">18%</span>，达到<span lang="EN-US">97</span>美元。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">全年来看，西数营收达<span lang="EN-US">169.22</span>亿美元，同比增长<span lang="EN-US">1%</span>，<span lang="EN-US">GAAP</span>准则下，毛利率为<span lang="EN-US">26.7%</span>，营业利润<span lang="EN-US">12.2</span>亿美元，同比增长<span lang="EN-US">264%</span>；净利润<span lang="EN-US">8.21</span>亿美元，相较上一财年实现扭亏为盈，每股摊薄收益<span lang="EN-US">2.66</span>美元。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">从西数的财报来看，闪存在涨价，但增长幅度并不算高，只有<span lang="EN-US">4-7%</span>，反而是<span lang="EN-US">HDD</span>硬盘均价上涨更多，主要是因为大容量硬盘出货量越来越多，<strong>西数上季度中<span lang="EN-US">18TB</span>硬盘出货量增长了<span lang="EN-US">2</span>倍，占企业级硬盘出货量的一半。</strong></p>
