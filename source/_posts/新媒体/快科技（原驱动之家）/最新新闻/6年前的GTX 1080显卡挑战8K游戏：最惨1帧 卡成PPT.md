<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">GTX 1080</span>显卡已经是<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年发布的<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>旗舰了，现在当然过气了，不过性能用于<span lang="EN-US">1080p</span>游戏还可以一战，如果是挑战<span lang="EN-US">4K</span>甚至<span lang="EN-US">8K</span>游戏呢？</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">还真有人做了测试，油土鳖上有自媒体<a class="f14_link" href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=R0by3_I18D0" target="_blank"><span lang="EN-US">RandomGaminginHD</span></a>使用<span lang="EN-US">GTX 1080</span>显卡挑战了多款热门游戏在<span lang="EN-US">4K</span>及<span lang="EN-US">8K</span>下的游戏性能。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">对比的游戏是《赛博朋克<span lang="EN-US">2077</span>》、《孤岛惊魂<span lang="EN-US">6</span>》、《巫师<span lang="EN-US">3</span>》、《<span lang="EN-US">GTA</span>圣安地列斯最终版》、《极限竞速：地平线<span lang="EN-US">5</span>》这几款。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">简单说下结果，<span lang="EN-US">8K</span>低画质下的《赛博朋克<span lang="EN-US">2077</span>》平均帧只有<span lang="EN-US">9fps</span>，部分场景是<span lang="EN-US">7fps</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>《孤岛惊魂<span lang="EN-US">6</span>》中<span lang="EN-US">8K</span>画质下帧率只有<span lang="EN-US">1fps</span>，就像幻灯片一样，不可行。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">《巫师<span lang="EN-US">3</span>》这种老游戏中，<span lang="EN-US">8K</span>画质下也只有<span lang="EN-US">20fps</span>的平均帧，部分甚至低到<span lang="EN-US">15fps</span>，<span lang="EN-US">4K</span>平均帧能达到<span lang="EN-US">75fps</span>的。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">《<span lang="EN-US">GTA</span>圣安地列斯最终版》游戏中，<span lang="EN-US">4K</span>下有<span lang="EN-US">119fps</span>，很流畅，<span lang="EN-US">8K</span>下就只有<span lang="EN-US">32fps</span>了，部分场景会低至<span lang="EN-US">2fps</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">《极限竞速：地平线<span lang="EN-US">5</span>》游戏中，<span lang="EN-US">4K</span>中等画质下是<span lang="EN-US">86fps</span>，<span lang="EN-US">8K</span>下就只有<span lang="EN-US">34fps</span>了，低画质下可以提升到<span lang="EN-US">42fps</span>左右。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">总的来看，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">6</span>年前的<span lang="EN-US">GTX 1080</span>显卡挑战<span lang="EN-US">8K</span>游戏的结果很惨，个位数的帧数没啥可玩性一说了，部分游戏中<span lang="EN-US">8K</span>低画质还可以勉强超过<span lang="EN-US">30fps</span>。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">当然，这就是个尝试，毕竟<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3090</span>显卡拿来跑<span lang="EN-US">8K</span>游戏也不好轻松。</p>
