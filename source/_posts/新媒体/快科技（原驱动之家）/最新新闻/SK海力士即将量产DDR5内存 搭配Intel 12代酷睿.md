<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">今年下半年，最快<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月底就能升级<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存了，这一次<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Alder Lake</span>会首发支持<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>。<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">SK</span>海力士日前在财报会议上也表示未来几个月就要量产<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>内存。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>的标准已经发布一年多了，相比<span lang="EN-US">DDR4</span>继续提升性能、降低功耗，标准电压从<span lang="EN-US">1.2V</span>进一步降至<span lang="EN-US">1.1V</span>，<strong>最大单条容量从<span lang="EN-US">32GB</span>来到<span lang="EN-US">128GB(</span>单<span lang="EN-US">Die</span>密度<span lang="EN-US">64Gb)</span>，标准最高频率从<span lang="EN-US">3200MHz</span>翻番到<span lang="EN-US">6400MHz(</span>实际瞄准<span lang="EN-US">8400MHz)</span>，此时带宽达到<span lang="EN-US">51GB/s</span>，基本是<span lang="EN-US">DDR4-3200</span>的两倍。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">另外，<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>支持<span lang="EN-US">ECC</span>错误校验，不再需要专业平台。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">SK</span>海力士是全球三大<span lang="EN-US">DRAM</span>芯片公司之一，三星、美光此前都已经推出了<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>芯片，其中美光这次进度很快，<span lang="EN-US">SK</span>海力士对比之下反而慢了一些，今年下半年才会量产。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>今年的适配对象几乎只有<span lang="EN-US">Alder Lake</span>可选，这是<span lang="EN-US">Intel 12</span>代酷睿，<strong>升级<span lang="EN-US">10nm ESF</span>工艺——当然，这个工艺叫做<span lang="EN-US">Intel 7</span>了，</strong>并首次用上大小核架构，最多<span lang="EN-US">16</span>核<span lang="EN-US">24</span>线程。</p>
