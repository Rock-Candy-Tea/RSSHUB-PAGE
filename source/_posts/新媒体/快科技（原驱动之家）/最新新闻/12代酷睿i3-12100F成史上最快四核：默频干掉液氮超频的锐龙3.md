<p>Intel 12代酷睿着实彪悍，不仅仅是顶级的i9-12900K/KF，不仅仅主流级的i5-12400/F，就连低端的入门级的i3-12100/F都令人吃惊。</p>
<p>i3-12100/F是12代i3系列中的最低端型号，再往下就是奔腾赛扬了。它只有4个大核心，三级缓存12MB，频率3.3-4.3GHz，基准功耗58W，最大89W，价格仅97美元，自带RM1原装散热器。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220107/02b972ab2892454781771aabd5456227.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="12代酷睿i3-12100F成史上最快四核：默频干掉液氮超频的锐龙3" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220107/s_02b972ab2892454781771aabd5456227.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220107/c4611e834e7d49a1ae3c108a9d6bc1ed.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="12代酷睿i3-12100F成史上最快四核：默频干掉液氮超频的锐龙3" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220107/s_c4611e834e7d49a1ae3c108a9d6bc1ed.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>超频高手Alav Jonatha(LUCKY-NOOB)提前拿到了一颗i3-12100F，使用原装散热器，在默认频率下，搭配Z690主板、32GB DDR5-6200内存，进行了简单测试。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220107/8a8d255827ee4fd192a13ae660750817.png" target="_blank"><img alt="12代酷睿i3-12100F成史上最快四核：默频干掉液氮超频的锐龙3" h="481" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220107/s_8a8d255827ee4fd192a13ae660750817.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p><strong><span style="color:#ff0000;">Y-Cruncher (PI-1B)成绩为44.201秒，成为史上最快的四核处理器，只比水冷散热器的i3-12100慢了大约1.7秒钟，甚至超过了液氮超频到5.6GHz的锐龙3 5600G，而且快了几乎8秒钟，幅度达到了18％。</span></strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220107/1509d0a775d74deb8a1d335c5f17ec33.png" target="_blank"><img alt="12代酷睿i3-12100F成史上最快四核：默频干掉液氮超频的锐龙3" h="486" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220107/s_1509d0a775d74deb8a1d335c5f17ec33.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p><strong>CineBench R20四核排行榜上，它依然傲视群雄，只比液氮超频到5.6GHz的锐龙3 3100低了仅仅1分。</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220107/94893c7c0c104f728a1e642dc26eff1e.png" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="12代酷睿i3-12100F成史上最快四核：默频干掉液氮超频的锐龙3" h="477" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220107/s_94893c7c0c104f728a1e642dc26eff1e.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>GPUPI for CPU四核测试中，默频风冷中它还是最快的，甚至超越了液氮超到7GHz的酷睿i7-7740X。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220107/97f5dfa2531744b2b0cd9ef5b7f05d97.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="12代酷睿i3-12100F成史上最快四核：默频干掉液氮超频的锐龙3" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220107/s_97f5dfa2531744b2b0cd9ef5b7f05d97.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>另外有趣的是，<strong>至少在这块微星MEG Z690I UNIFY主板上，i3-12100F可以开启AVX-512指令集，但大概率后续会被封杀。</strong></p>
