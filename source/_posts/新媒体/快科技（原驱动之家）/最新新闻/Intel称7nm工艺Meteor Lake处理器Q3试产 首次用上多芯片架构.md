<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年对<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>来说异常重要，今年该公司的制程工艺终于走上正轨，现在已经传来多个喜讯——<span lang="EN-US">10nm</span>成本大降<span lang="EN-US">45%</span>，产能也超过了<span lang="EN-US">14nm</span>，成为新的主力，<span lang="EN-US">7nm</span>工艺进展良好，<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年随着<span lang="EN-US">Meteor Lake</span>首发。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Meteor Lake</span>是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>下下下代酷睿处理器，按照规划应该是<span lang="EN-US">14</span>代酷睿，中间隔着<span lang="EN-US">10nm</span>工艺的<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Alder Lake</span>、<span lang="EN-US">13</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Raptor Lake</span>，后两者使用的是<span lang="EN-US">LGA1700</span>插槽，<span lang="EN-US">Meteor Lake</span>传闻是<span lang="EN-US">LGA1800</span>插槽。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">虽然<span lang="EN-US">7nm</span>工艺跟最初宣布的进度相比也延期了至少<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年，不过这一次的进展不错，<span lang="EN-US">Meteor Lake</span>处理器今年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月份的时候已经完成芯片的“<span lang="EN-US">Tape-in”</span>。<span lang="EN-US">Tape-in</span>在<span lang="EN-US">Tape-Out</span>（流片）前，大概是<span lang="EN-US">IP</span>模块完成设计验证阶段。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><img alt="Intel称7nm工艺Meteor Lake处理器Q3试产 首次用上多芯片架构" h="345" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20210725/ab6bae27-dfee-4f9f-9cd9-57cb3f009503.jpg" style="text-align: center; border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">设计完成之后，下一步就要准备流片、验证了，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>人员提到<span lang="EN-US">Q3</span>季度<span lang="EN-US">7nm</span>的<span lang="EN-US">Meteor Lake</span>处理器就会在第一条<span lang="EN-US">7nm</span>生产线上试产（意味着最晚不过2个月时间），当然这还是很早期的工程样品，具体情况还没有什么信息。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">7nm</span>工艺会是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的一次翻身仗，虽然<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年量产的时候友商已经有<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>、<span lang="EN-US">3nm</span>工艺了，但是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的<span lang="EN-US">7nm</span>工艺晶体管密度达到了<span lang="EN-US">1.8</span>亿晶体管每平方毫米，技术水平追上台积电的<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>、三星<span lang="EN-US">3nm</span>工艺。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">对于<span lang="EN-US">Meteor Lake</span>处理器，除了<span lang="EN-US">7nm</span>工艺之外，这次还会用上<span lang="EN-US">Foveros 3D</span>封装技术，<strong>里面会集成不同工艺的<span lang="EN-US">IP</span>核心，这也是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>首次在主流桌面处理器用上多芯片封装技术，</strong>以前的胶水多核只是简单的<span lang="EN-US">2D</span>封装，现在是<span lang="EN-US">3D</span>封装多芯片结构了。</p>
