<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>前几天解禁了<span lang="EN-US">RX 6600 XT</span>显卡，售价<span lang="EN-US">2999</span>元的它不仅有着优秀的<span lang="EN-US">1080p</span>游戏性能，<a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/775/775689.htm" target="_blank">而且挖矿性能很好</a>，能效奇高，碾压其他显卡。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">硬件<span lang="EN-US">Up</span>主<span lang="EN-US">Dizzy Mining</span>将<span lang="EN-US">RX 6600 XT</span>的<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>核心频率降到<span lang="EN-US">1.2GHz</span>，显存频率拉到<span lang="EN-US">2.2GHz</span>，最终在以太坊计算器中输出<span lang="EN-US">32.6MH/s</span>的成绩，此时的<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>功耗仅<span lang="EN-US">55</span>瓦。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>测算对比发现，这样的能效可以说碾压了一切对手，包括<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3060 Ti/RTX 3080/Radeon VII</span>等。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">55W</span>功耗下就有<span lang="EN-US">32.6MH/s</span>的挖矿性能？算下来该卡的能效确实很无敌，这几天不少矿工都在抢购这款显卡，但奇高的挖矿效率是错误的。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">对于<span lang="EN-US">RX 6600 XT</span>挖矿能效的问题，<span lang="EN-US">3DCenter</span>指出其中的所谓<span lang="EN-US">55W</span>功耗是不准确的，因为这只是<span lang="EN-US">ASIC</span>芯片的功耗，整卡功耗还要再加上<span lang="EN-US">15-25W</span>，也就是功耗<span lang="EN-US">70-80W</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">按照这个水平来算，<strong><span lang="EN-US">RX 6600 XT</span>的挖矿能效在<span lang="EN-US">0.4</span>到<span lang="EN-US">0.46MH/s/W</span>之间，跟<span lang="EN-US">RTX 3060 Ti</span>的效率差不多</strong>，整体依然是很高水平，但不至于说碾压一切的领先。</p>
