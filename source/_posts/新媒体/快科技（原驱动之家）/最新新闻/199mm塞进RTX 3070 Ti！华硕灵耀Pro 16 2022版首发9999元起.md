<p>今天的华硕新品发布会上，华硕正式发布了灵耀Pro系列的两款新品。</p>
<p>其中，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>灵耀Pro 16 2022版作为定位轻薄便携的笔记本，重量仅有2.2kg，厚度也仅有19.9mm，首发价9999元起。</strong></span></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220907/e46cb4af816545a49bf7a7ee448b3bfe.png" target="_blank"><img alt="19.9mm塞进RTX 3070 Ti！华硕灵耀Pro 16 2022版首发9999元起" h="340" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220907/s_e46cb4af816545a49bf7a7ee448b3bfe.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>在仅有19.9mm的机身内，<strong>灵耀Pro 16 2022版塞进了一颗Intel i7-12700H处理器，以及最高RTX 3070 Ti显卡，整机性能释放最高可达140W</strong>，且最高内置32GB DDR5内存和1TB PCIe 4.0 SSD。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220907/02e5f1e574c8472d97dd67e65314ce1a.png" target="_blank"><img alt="19.9mm塞进RTX 3070 Ti！华硕灵耀Pro 16 2022版首发9999元起" h="342" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220907/s_02e5f1e574c8472d97dd67e65314ce1a.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>在拉高性能的同时，这款笔记本采用了华硕冰川Pro散热架构，能够确保电脑的散热效果。</p>
<p>显示方面，<strong>灵耀Pro 16 2022款采用了一款16英寸3.2K 120Hz的OLED屏</strong>，拥有90.3%的屏占比，100% P3广色域，并通过了HDR True Black 600认证，表现亮眼。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220907/dfc3d8e102874b7ea8f268932f98bd95.png" target="_blank"><img alt="19.9mm塞进RTX 3070 Ti！华硕灵耀Pro 16 2022版首发9999元起" h="338" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220907/s_dfc3d8e102874b7ea8f268932f98bd95.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>其他方面，这款笔记本拥有DialPad虚拟旋钮等华硕的“看家本领”，并有齐全的接口配置，<strong>能够胜任各类办公或娱乐场景。</strong></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220907/f1fc70abb9524c9aa8242ff4832ee82d.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="19.9mm塞进RTX 3070 Ti！华硕灵耀Pro 16 2022版首发9999元起" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220907/s_f1fc70abb9524c9aa8242ff4832ee82d.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
