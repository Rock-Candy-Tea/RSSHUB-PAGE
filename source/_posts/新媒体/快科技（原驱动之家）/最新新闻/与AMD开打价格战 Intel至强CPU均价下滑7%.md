<p class="MsoNormal">周五<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>公司发布了<span lang="EN-US">Q2</span>季度财报，<span lang="EN-US">PC</span>业务受益于销量大涨而创造了记录，部门营收首次超过<span lang="EN-US">100</span>亿美元，不过数据中心部门<span lang="EN-US">DCG</span>营收下滑了<span lang="EN-US">9%</span>，在这个领域受到<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的竞争压力越来越大，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>至强<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>也要降价了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的财报信息，<span lang="EN-US">Q2</span>季度中数据中心部门营收<span lang="EN-US">65</span>亿美元，同比下滑<span lang="EN-US">9%</span>，运营利润则从<span lang="EN-US">31</span>亿美元下滑到了<span lang="EN-US">19</span>亿美元，运营利润率从<span lang="EN-US">44%</span>跌到了现在的<span lang="EN-US">30%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>解释说，数据中心部门营收盈利下滑有成本增加的因素，特别是要准备<span lang="EN-US">7nm</span>工艺的生产，拖累了表现。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>值得注意的是，数据中心部门的出货量下滑了<span lang="EN-US">1%</span>，而且<span lang="EN-US">ASP</span>均价也同时下滑了<span lang="EN-US">7%</span>，意味着<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的至强<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>价格越来越低了。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/759/759068.htm" target="_blank">这也证实了之前<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的表态，</a>那就是在数据中心市场上<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>可以为了保住份额而开打价格战。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">当然，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>打价格战也是有底气的，一方面<span lang="EN-US">14nm</span>工艺发展多年，成本早就很低了，<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年时间都可以把折旧费摊完了，另一方面<span lang="EN-US">10nm</span>工艺也传来好消息，成本降低了<span lang="EN-US">45%</span>，这也是打价格战的关键。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的<span lang="EN-US">10nm</span>工艺至强代号为<span lang="EN-US">Sapphire Rapids</span>，<strong>最多可以做到<span lang="EN-US">80</span>核<span lang="EN-US">160</span>线程，核心数上跟<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的霄龙可以硬刚了，同时还支持<span lang="EN-US">8</span>通道<span lang="EN-US">DDR5</span>、<span lang="EN-US">PCIe 5.0</span>，还集成了<span lang="EN-US">64GB HBM2</span>内存，性能很强悍。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不过<span lang="EN-US">Sapphire Rapids</span>的问题在于又跳票了，明年上半年才能问世，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>明年要推<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>了，预计会升级到<span lang="EN-US">96</span>核<span lang="EN-US">192</span>线程，这个比赛又得重新开始。</p>
