<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日晚，中芯国际发表公告称，该公司有<span lang="EN-US">7.75</span>亿限售股将于<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日解禁，从中芯国际的股价<span lang="EN-US">58</span>元来算，这是一笔超过<span lang="EN-US">400</span>多亿的财富。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">中芯国际表示，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>公司此次限售股上市流通数量约为<span lang="EN-US">7.75</span>亿股，占公司截至<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日总股本的<span lang="EN-US">9.81%</span>，上市流通日期为<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日；解禁类型为公司首次公开发行战略配售限售股，限售股股东数量为<span lang="EN-US">27</span>名。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">中芯国际<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日上市，公司首次向社会公众公开发行<span lang="EN-US">A</span>股股票<span lang="EN-US">1,685,620,000</span>股（行使超额配售选择权之前），并于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日在上海证券交易所科创板上市。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">中芯国际表示，公司首次公开发行<span lang="EN-US">A</span>股股票超额配售选择权已于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日全额行使，对应新增发行股数<span lang="EN-US">252,843,000</span>股，由此发行总股数扩大至<span lang="EN-US">1,938,463,000</span>股。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">截至<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日收盘，中芯国际<span lang="EN-US">A</span>股股价<span lang="EN-US">58.30</span>元，市值超过4600亿元，<strong><span lang="EN-US">7.75</span>亿限售股意味着价值<span lang="EN-US">451</span>亿的财富可以套现，</strong>虽然不一定都会在<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日抛售，不过<span lang="EN-US">400</span>多亿市值解禁恐怕还是会对中芯国际的股价造成冲击。</p>
