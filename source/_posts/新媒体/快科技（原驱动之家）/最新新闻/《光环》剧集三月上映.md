<p>维亚康姆CBS旗下流媒体平台Paramount+将于3月24号发布备受期待的游戏改编剧集《光环》：</p>
<p align="center"><img alt="《光环》剧集三月上映" h="488" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220131/95059c63b3514406a8e3420948354e74.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="590" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>该剧已酝酿多年，至少八年前微软就打算先于游戏发布联动影视作品，为即将面世的超级大作《光环2》暖场。</p>
<p align="center"><img alt="《光环》剧集三月上映" h="861" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220131/a7213f65324244f9a09ff01843c44e2c.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>结果等到《光环2》面市大家连片花都没看到，Showtime频道最终决定将该题材发展为剧集并于2018年下订，19年10月开拍，先后换了两茬创制人，中途还因疫情原因暂停拍摄。2021年2月，母公司维亚康姆CBS决定该剧不在Showtime频道投放，驰援新生平台Paramount+：</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220131/cd33ddc3fc784538b94e33c31588b614.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《光环》剧集三月上映" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220131/s_cd33ddc3fc784538b94e33c31588b614.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>刚刚发布的<a class="f14_link" href="https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1ZR4y1T7gk?share_source=copy_web" target="_blank">最终版预告皮</a>宣告多年等待即将结束，玩家终于能在荧屏上见到狂霸酷帅巴勃罗所扮演的士官长与化妆师花费数小时所妆扮出的星盟对手：</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220131/a160db31e1bd49e3820e9f6687144fd2.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《光环》剧集三月上映" h="584" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220131/s_a160db31e1bd49e3820e9f6687144fd2.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220131/dfa216c072a1442ca81dc8c03b06c83e.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《光环》剧集三月上映" h="297" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220131/s_dfa216c072a1442ca81dc8c03b06c83e.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220131/bfe05c633326420096b55992cdab1428.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《光环》剧集三月上映" h="298" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220131/s_bfe05c633326420096b55992cdab1428.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220131/e626fd30e6cb427d97b0eaecdcfcdfd2.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《光环》剧集三月上映" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220131/s_e626fd30e6cb427d97b0eaecdcfcdfd2.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>除主角巴勃罗外，出演过《浪人》的Natascha McElhone扮演斯巴达计划创始人凯瑟琳·哈尔西博士，曾在《冰血暴》第二季中有精彩表现的Bokeem Woodbine出演索伦066：</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220131/639ba7e86a144cedbc8983538f132049.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="《光环》剧集三月上映" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220131/s_639ba7e86a144cedbc8983538f132049.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>曾在《求生之路》中为佐伊配音，并为多部《光环》游戏献声的Jen Taylor重拾她的老本行，演绎人工智能科塔娜：</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220131/b5f7c6eaba584242b72373ce53c39ee4.jpg" style="text-align: -webkit-center;" target="_blank"><img alt="《光环》剧集三月上映" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220131/s_b5f7c6eaba584242b72373ce53c39ee4.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>《光环》剧集由Paramount+平台独家播映，共九集，首集定于3月24日播出。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220131/0ef7fe5f812d45f7af6d8bd24cb0b873.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="《光环》剧集三月上映" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220131/s_0ef7fe5f812d45f7af6d8bd24cb0b873.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
