<p>今日下午，真我举行了新品发布会，<strong>广大网友心心念念的“年度质感旗舰”真我GT2大师探索版正式发布，起售价<span style="color:#ff0000;">3499</span>元。</strong></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220712/b5842048b6194c3dbd8e8df869cd2c21.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="真我GT2大师探索版五大技术首发：新内存、新“独显”" h="195" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220712/s_b5842048b6194c3dbd8e8df869cd2c21.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>新机一方面主打高颜值，复古硬箱设计引入户外潮流文化，而除了亮眼的外形，真我GT2大师探索版在用料上也诚意满满，首发行业五大技术：</p>
<p><strong>1、首发新一代X7独显芯片</strong></p>

<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220712/d8c79faa60a74835b9530f1bdc0c87a8.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="真我GT2大师探索版五大技术首发：新内存、新“独显”" h="195" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220712/s_d8c79faa60a74835b9530f1bdc0c87a8.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>GT2大师探索版首发了Pixelworks X7芯片。该芯片具有超低时延智能插帧技术，相比上一代独显降低60%。四倍游戏插帧技术和独显级游戏超分技术，带来高帧率、高画质、低时延、低功耗的流畅游戏体验 。</p>
<p><strong>2、首发LPDDR5X内存</strong></p>

<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220712/6ca4f9d36de740e08274b4fe9338d901.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="真我GT2大师探索版五大技术首发：新内存、新“独显”" h="195" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220712/s_6ca4f9d36de740e08274b4fe9338d901.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>据官方数据，LPDDR5X大幅度降低内存读写的功耗，玩游戏，功耗优化30%，长视频，功耗优化25%；短视频，功耗优化20%，综合功耗优化20%。</p>
<p><strong>3、首发超级N28自由四天线</strong></p>

<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220712/5f9b2ac0907846459fcceb64de134b7d.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="真我GT2大师探索版五大技术首发：新内存、新“独显”" h="195" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220712/s_5f9b2ac0907846459fcceb64de134b7d.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>真我GT2大师探索版在常用网络场景网速提升70%，支持自由天线切换技术2.0，信号强度提升1.5倍。</p>
<p><strong>4、首发全链路GaN百瓦闪充</strong></p>

<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220712/c94aea04ab5d4e44b59ea5636ca97550.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="真我GT2大师探索版五大技术首发：新内存、新“独显”" h="195" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220712/s_c94aea04ab5d4e44b59ea5636ca97550.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>内置GaN充电保护手机，电池体积降低64%，峰值功率器件发热降低85%。</p>
<p><strong>5、首发真隐藏式压感肩键</strong></p>

<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220712/14feb76a7c0943ed839db3359d432a27.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="真我GT2大师探索版五大技术首发：新内存、新“独显”" h="195" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220712/s_14feb76a7c0943ed839db3359d432a27.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>据了解，隐藏式肩键支持高灵敏度压感，可以在不影响手机颜值的情况下，提供电竞游戏体验，配合X轴线性马达，达到精准触感反馈。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220712/6727eb6f4a924fa7b3ccc2e513090601.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="真我GT2大师探索版五大技术首发：新内存、新“独显”" h="195" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220712/s_6727eb6f4a924fa7b3ccc2e513090601.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
