<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日，卫健委发表通告，<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日<span lang="EN-US">0—24</span>时，<span lang="EN-US">31</span>个省（自治区、直辖市）和新疆生产建设兵团报告新增确诊病例<span lang="EN-US">43</span>例。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">其中境外输入病例<span lang="EN-US">15</span>例（云南<span lang="EN-US">5</span>例，天津<span lang="EN-US">3</span>例，上海<span lang="EN-US">2</span>例，湖北<span lang="EN-US">2</span>例，广东<span lang="EN-US">2</span>例，辽宁<span lang="EN-US">1</span>例）；<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>本土病例<span lang="EN-US">28</span>例（福建<span lang="EN-US">20</span>例，其中厦门市<span lang="EN-US">17</span>例、莆田市<span lang="EN-US">3</span>例；黑龙江<span lang="EN-US">8</span>例，均在哈尔滨市）。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">无新增死亡病例。新增疑似病例<span lang="EN-US">2</span>例，均为境外输入病例（均在上海）。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">当日新增治愈出院病例<span lang="EN-US">37</span>例，解除医学观察的密切接触者<span lang="EN-US">499</span>人，重症病例与前一日持平。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">境外输入现有确诊病例<span lang="EN-US">511</span>例（其中重症病例<span lang="EN-US">4</span>例），现有疑似病例<span lang="EN-US">4</span>例。累计确诊病例<span lang="EN-US">8883</span>例，累计治愈出院病例<span lang="EN-US">8372</span>例，无死亡病例。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">截至<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日<span lang="EN-US">24</span>时，据<span lang="EN-US">31</span>个省（自治区、直辖市）和新疆生产建设兵团报告，现有确诊病例<span lang="EN-US">990</span>例（其中重症病例<span lang="EN-US">14</span>例），累计治愈出院病例<span lang="EN-US">90268</span>例，累计死亡病例<span lang="EN-US">4636</span>例，累计报告确诊病例<span lang="EN-US">95894</span>例，现有疑似病例<span lang="EN-US">4</span>例。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">累计追踪到密切接触者<span lang="EN-US">1183674</span>人，尚在医学观察的密切接触者<span lang="EN-US">17612</span>人。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">31</span>个省（自治区、直辖市）和新疆生产建设兵团报告新增无症状感染者<span lang="EN-US">14</span>例（均为境外输入）；当日无转为确诊病例；当日解除医学观察<span lang="EN-US">8</span>例（境外输入<span lang="EN-US">7</span>例）；尚在医学观察的无症状感染者<span lang="EN-US">348</span>例（境外输入<span lang="EN-US">340</span>例）。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">累计收到港澳台地区通报确诊病例<span lang="EN-US">28389</span>例。其中，香港特别行政区<span lang="EN-US">12167</span>例（出院<span lang="EN-US">11883</span>例，死亡<span lang="EN-US">213</span>例），澳门特别行政区<span lang="EN-US">63</span>例（出院<span lang="EN-US">63</span>例），台湾地区<span lang="EN-US">16159</span>例（出院<span lang="EN-US">13742</span>例，死亡<span lang="EN-US">841</span>例）。</p>
