<p class="MsoNormal">据报道，芬兰诺基亚日前宣布与全球最大的<span lang="EN-US">PC</span>企业联想集团达成了和解，双方在全球的专利诉讼将撤销。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">诺基亚没有透露双方专利合作的细节，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>只表示双方之间还有专利交叉协议，但是联想公司将会支付净差额，具体补偿金额就没有公布了。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">这次和解对双方来说都是好事，诺基亚拿到钱了，联想也了结了一系列官司，联想首席知识产权官约翰·穆格鲁表示，“达成的全球协议将使我们两家公司未来的合作能够造福全球客户。”</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">诺基亚手握<span lang="EN-US">2</span>万多项技术专利，其中<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>技术中标准必要专利就有<span lang="EN-US">3500</span>多个，其他公司很难绕开，此前三星等公司也一样被诺基亚起诉、和解。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">诺基亚从<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月份开始展开对联想的专利诉讼，称联想<span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">涉嫌侵犯其</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">20</span><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">项专利</span>，在美国、德国等多个国家都提起了诉讼，去年<span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">9</span><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">月底德国法院一度发表禁令，阻止联想销售任何使用诺基亚所谓的“</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">H.264”</span><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">技术的产品，当时慕尼黑地方法院裁定联想侵犯了诺基亚的</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">EP1433316</span><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;">号专利，并下达了禁止令，以及产品召回和损害赔偿。</span></p>
