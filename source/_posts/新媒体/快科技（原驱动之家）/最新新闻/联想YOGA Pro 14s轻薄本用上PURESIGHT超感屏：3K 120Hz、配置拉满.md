<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">5</span>月份联想会有一大波小新及<span lang="EN-US">YOGA</span>系列轻薄本发布，其中<span lang="EN-US">YOGA Pro 14s 2022</span>版除了会使用<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿及锐龙<span lang="EN-US">6000</span>两种处理器之外，屏幕也非常出色，联想确认它会用上<span lang="EN-US">PURESIGHT</span>超感屏，不仅拥有<span lang="EN-US">3K</span>高分辨率，还支持<span lang="EN-US">120Hz</span>高刷。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">联想<span lang="EN-US">YOGA</span>官微今天公布了<span lang="EN-US">YOGA Pro 14s 2022</span>版的屏幕细节，首先是分辨率达到了<span lang="EN-US">3K</span>，子像素分辨率<span lang="EN-US">250PPI</span>，实际上这是一块<span lang="EN-US">14.5</span>英寸的高分屏，<span lang="EN-US">3072*1920</span>分辨率与苹果<span lang="EN-US">MBP 16 2019</span>款是一样的。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>其次，<span lang="EN-US">YOGA Pro 14s 2022</span>版还支持<span lang="EN-US">120Hz</span>高刷，相比普通<span lang="EN-US">60Hz</span>更流畅。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">色彩方面，<span lang="EN-US">YOGA Pro 14s 2022</span>版支持<span lang="EN-US">10.7</span>亿色显示，硬件级色彩校准，<span lang="EN-US">100% sRGB</span>容积及覆盖。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">最后是护眼，<span lang="EN-US">YOGA Pro 14s 2022</span>版拥有<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>硬件级低蓝光护眼技术，支持<span lang="EN-US">DC</span>调光，支持环境光自适应及<span lang="EN-US">eyesafe</span>护眼。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">其他方面，<span lang="EN-US">YOGA Pro 14s 2022</span>版还支持<span lang="EN-US">G-Sync</span>，杜比视界、色准Δ<span lang="EN-US">E</span>＜<span lang="EN-US">1</span>，还支持触控操作，屏幕指标在同级别产品中可以说拉满了。</p>
