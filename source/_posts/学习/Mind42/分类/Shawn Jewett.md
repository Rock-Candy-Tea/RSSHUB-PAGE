From equipment to lumber, this service isdesigned to eliminate the countless phone calls it can take to find a rate that meets your expectations. TQ offers a web-based platform allowing the users to simply enter shipment details and instantly receive a free quote.  Whether it is a Full Truck load or Less Than Truck load, the system is customizable allowing the users to only pay for the specific footage and weightof their shipment. “We want to make it easy for people to instantly receive a free shipping quote, without having to waste time and money searching for an affordable price and a reliable company to have their goods transported.” Says President and CEO Shawn Jewett.  “Having been in the brokerage industry for many years, we understand how important it is for people tonot waste time trying to find a dependable company that stands behind pricing and customer satisfaction.” Users can sign up at no cost allowing them access to receive unlimited free quotes, book shipments and track and monitor all account and booking activity. Customer satisfaction is our number one goal.All of TQ’s carriers must comply with all regulations and laws set by the FMCA. TQ’s software monitors itscarriers’ performance and safety to ensure every carrier in our network performs to the highest standards. “Our mission is to provide individuals with a full-service solution to ship goods throughout the Nation affordably and safely. We want to be shippers first choice when looking for a transportation partner!” About Truck QuoteTruckquote.com is a Southern Oregon based company that offers an easy to use, FTL or LTL load quoting service that provides instant quotes, locates drivers and allows users to effortlessly book a load to be shipped. TQ’s tool is for everyone and was strategically designed to speed up the time it takes to get a quote, find reliable trucking at a competitive price and allow the user to track the shipment along the route.
