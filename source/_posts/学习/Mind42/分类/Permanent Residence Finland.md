                    Finland Permanent Residence Card permit is a document issued to non-EU citizens living in Finland. If you want to stay in Finland for a longer period than three months, you need a residence permit. If you plan to work, you may need a permanent residence card. A person intending to engage in an independent business or profession in Finland must have a residence permit for a self-employed person. Non-EU/EEA students need a student Residence Permit (student visa) for their studies in Finland.                </p>
