                    The Free Counseling For Cancer Patients and cancer support groups offer individualized assistance to all cancer patients and their families, based on their specific requirements. There are specialized programs for various types of cancer. During and after a cancer diagnosis, free assistance is provided on various concerns, including obtaining care, affording treatment, and obtaining emotional support. Whether peer or professional assistance, cancer support services are essential and provided at no cost through the programs.                </p>
