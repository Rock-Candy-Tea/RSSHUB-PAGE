                    Junior Engineer is a national-level examination held to recruit qualified candidates for the position of Junior Engineer (mechanical, civil, and electrical). The SSC JE Examination is divided into two papers, Paper I and Paper II. Paper I is an objective type test consisting of multiple-choice questions that is conducted online, whereas Paper II is a descriptive type test in which candidates must describe the given problem or statement. Candidates can begin filling out these admit cards on August 13 and the deadline is September 12.                </p>
