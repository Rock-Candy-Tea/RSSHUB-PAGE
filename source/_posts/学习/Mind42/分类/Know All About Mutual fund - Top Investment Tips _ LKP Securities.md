                    A mutual fund is a financial tool comprising a pool of money that is used to invest in securities like bonds and stocks. The fund collected from individual investors is allocated in various securities by fund managers in a way so as to earn fruitful returns from the investment. This return is then proportionately distributed to the investors. Thus, mutual funds allow you to invest in a professionally managed fund, making you a shareholder in the gains or losses of the fund.
