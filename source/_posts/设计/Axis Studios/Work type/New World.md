                    <p>At the Game Awards 2019, the new trailer for the upcoming open-world MMO New World, directed by Jon Yeo, was revealed. Developed by Amazon Games Studios, New World is set on a supernatural island known as Aeternum, where players aim to establish a foothold and uncover the mysteries of what happened in this strange land filled with undead monsters and relics of an ancient civilization. </p>
