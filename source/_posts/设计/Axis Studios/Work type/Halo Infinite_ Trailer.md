                    <p>Directed by Jon Yeo, our latest trailer for 343 Industries introduces a key new character as part of the much-anticipated Season 1 of Halo Infinite multiplayer. Having developed a close relationship and collaborated many times with 343 Industries, we were thrilled to deliver another cinematic release for the Halo franchise.</p>

<p>Focusing on the recollections of Spartan Commander Laurette Agryna, the trailer delves into her own heroic journey as a young girl caught in the crosshairs of a battle in the centre of London 2552 AD, her rescue by Spartans and her inspiration to become a Spartan herself.</p>

<p>Axis delivered fully animated CG, developing the character asset for Laurette showcasing nuanced facial animation and designed a futuristic London.</p>
