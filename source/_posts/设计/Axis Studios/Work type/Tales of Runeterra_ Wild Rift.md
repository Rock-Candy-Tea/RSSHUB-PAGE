                    <p>Axis joined forces with Riot Games once again to create a new <em>Tales of Runeterra</em> installment for the skills-and-strats 5v5 MOBA game, Wild Rift. Together, we introduced a band of daring and delightful Yordles.</p>

<p>Leveraging our expertise in bringing to life League of Legends champions, our team created atmospheric lighting and environments as well as endearing and comical character moments.</p>

<p>Follow the fluffy and mischievous Yordles beyond their homeland of Bandle City on the Yordle Expedition. Travelling through their magical portals, we revisit Piltover and Bilgewater as Tristana, Lulu and Corki team up to save the imprisoned Teemo, landing Graves and Twisted Fate in trouble...</p>
