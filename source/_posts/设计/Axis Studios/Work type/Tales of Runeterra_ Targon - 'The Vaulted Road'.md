                    <p>Following the success of the first season, we were over the moon to team up with Riot Games once again to create the next installment in the animated series ‘Tales of Runeterra’.</p>

<p>This episode continues to explore the lore of ‘Runeterra’, based on the fictional universe of League of Legends.</p>

<p>Set in the mystical and revered plane of Mount Targon, which boasts the highest peak in all of ‘Runeterra’, a Lunari scout escapes imprisonment with the help of her Solari lover. As the Solari sun and the Lunari moon deities meet in an eclipse, the transcendent bond of the lovers is immortalized beneath its shadow.</p>
