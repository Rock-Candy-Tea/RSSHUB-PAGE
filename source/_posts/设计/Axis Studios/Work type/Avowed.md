                    <p style="margin-left:0px;margin-right:0px;">Experience the world premier of the newest game from Obsidian Entertainment, <em>Avowed</em>!</p>

<p style="margin-left:0px;margin-right:0px;">We created this 45-second teaser trailer for the brand new <em>Avowed</em> game, a 3D RPG originally to be launched at the Xbox Games Showcase.</p>

<p style="margin-left:0in;margin-right:0in;">Working alongside the Buddah Jones agency for the first time to create the trailer for developer Obsidian Entertainment, Axis were responsible for a myriad of environments, characters, props and FX shots, from magic-wielding heroes to skeleton warriors. The trailer portrays the journey of an arrow across the landscape, tantalising fans with glimpses of the new world of <em>Avowed</em>. </p>
