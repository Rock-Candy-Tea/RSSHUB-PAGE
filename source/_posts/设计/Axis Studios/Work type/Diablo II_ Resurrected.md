                    <p>Diablo® II resurrected is a shot for shot remake created with Blizzard Entertainment for the timeless action RPG classic, now remastered and upgraded. </p>

<p>The team led by director Jon Yeo completed 7 cinematic sequences spanning over a total length of 27 minutes. Each shot is carefully and considerately recreated in line with the original to deliver the most faithful and rejuvenated presentation that does so much more than a simple homage to the original game.</p>

<p>Axis recreated every asset and shot for a modern audience, with a wealth of detail in every scene, in every monster, and in every character. All the key performances were captured over a four-day MOCAP shoot to lip-synced dialogue to add visual fidelity to the performances. </p>
