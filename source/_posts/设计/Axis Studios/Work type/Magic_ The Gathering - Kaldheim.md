                    <p><em>Kaldheim</em>, the sixth trailer created with Wizards of the Coast for Magic The Gathering introduces the latest card expansion of the same name. Directed by Abed Abonamous, Axis created the fully animated CG trailer introducing new characters and creating detailed character designs to bring MTG favourites to life in 3D.</p>

<p>Invoking Norse mythology, get ready to be plunged into a world of trolls, giant wolves, mystical realms and gruesome battles as we follow as our axe-wielding protagonist and fierce assassin Kaya.  But hidden in the branches of the cosmic World Tree, mysterious forces threaten the very fabric of the ten realms forever…</p>
