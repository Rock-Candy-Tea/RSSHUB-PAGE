                    <p><em>'Scrooge: A Christmas Carol',</em> produced by Timeless Films in association with Axis Studios and released by Netflix, is a fresh twist on Charles Dickens' classic festive tale.  Featuring the voices of Olivia Colman (“Landscapers”), Jessie Buckley (“Men”) and Luke Evans (“Crossing Swords”) the novel has been rejuvenated for the screen with "psychedelic, time-travelling and musical surprises".</p>
