                    <p>Directed by Stu Aitken, <em>Ikoria: Lair of Behemoths</em> is the latest trailer for Magic The Gathering’s Spring 2020 card expansion. This fantastical fast-paced monster battle was created for Wizards of the Coast, featuring green planeswalker Vivien Reid as she’s tasked with defending the city against giant monsters. Using her magic bow and arrow that shoots spectral wingcats, this heroic fight is not for the faint-hearted. </p>

<p>Our team employed fully animated CG, designing monsters that stayed faithful to the brand’s famous card-illustrations. Inspired by those iconic creatures in the cards, this epic battle means one thing - there’s always a bigger monster.</p>
