        <h2 id="73dd"><strong>Questions</strong></h2>

<p id="e07b"><strong>Tell us your own path from Designer to Creative Director.</strong><br><br>

I donтАЩt think I had the confidence to genuinely label myself a тАШtype designerтАЩ for a good five years into my career. I became a company director and together we grew the team. I didnтАЩt really notice that transition from designer to Creative Director until we grew from the 2 of us into 4 almost overnight. Our roles shifted, we had to create structure and that involved Jason and I stepping aside to define creative direction, consult over strategy, costs, timelines, finances, marketing, everything within the business really. ThatтАЩs when I first began guiding rather than doing, though I still do a lot of doingтАж I think you have to.         <p><strong>Tell us about some of the pros and cons of being a Creative Director.</strong><br><br>
You meet so many people from all walks of life, both client side and specialists that you can bring into your team.
