<p>盒马旗下眼镜品牌「盒睛睛」在上海浦东新区盒马 X 会员店正式亮相，盒马 CEO 侯毅与众多嘉宾到场剪彩。盒睛睛将为 X 会员提供免费验光、眼镜清洗、调整以及维修服务。据悉，盒马未来将进军时尚行业，计划探索健身、3C、宠物等领域。</p><div class="item-img"><span class="item-thumb img img-zoom"><i class="thumb " style="background-image:url(https://image.uisdc.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/04/db-20210428-3.jpg)"></i><img src="https://image.uisdc.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/04/db-20210428-3.jpg" alt="thumb" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></div>
