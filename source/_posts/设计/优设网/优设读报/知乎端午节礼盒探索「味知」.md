<p>今年端午，知乎推出「知味味知」端午节礼盒，谐音梗「味知」去探索「未知」。礼盒外观以绿色的粽叶为主色调，贯穿礼盒包装，礼盒内含粽子、艾草香囊、印章和饭盒，从味觉到嗅觉，兼具实用与趣味的双重玩味。</p><div class="item-img"><span class="item-thumb img img-zoom"><i class="thumb " style="background-image:url(https://image.uisdc.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/06/db-20210612-3.jpg)"></i><img src="https://image.uisdc.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/06/db-20210612-3.jpg" alt="thumb" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></div>
