        <h2>Game of War “Time”</h2>
    

<p><strong>Director:</strong> Benjamin Walsh          <strong>Agency:</strong> Untitled Worldwide          <strong>Production Company:</strong> Method Studios</p>

<p>Showing a detailed time-lapse environment with extensive CG by Method, the spot takes viewers on a visual journey as a plot of land gives rise to a medieval castle where rival factions fight over the territory. Method handled character animation, explosions, fire, smoke, matte painting, sky replacement, compositing and day to night transitions for the spot.</p>
