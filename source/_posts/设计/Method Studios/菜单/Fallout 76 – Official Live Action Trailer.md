        <h2>Fallout 76 – Official Live Action Trailer</h2>
    

<p><strong>Agency:</strong> GNET / Bethesda          <strong>Director:</strong> David Moodie</p>

<p>In the new :60 spot teasing Bethesda’s highly-anticipated online survival RPG Fallout 76, Method Studios created a stunning visualization of the in-game world of post-apocalyptic Appalachia. Incorporating live action actors into fully CG surroundings, the spot’s hyperreal look artfully showcases gameplay in a way that imagines what a group of friends might experience if they were able to step inside Fallout 76’s West Virginia wasteland.</p>

<p>Led by VFX Supervisor Jesse Bradstreet and Real-Time CG Supervisor Juan Sebastian Gomez, the project tapped Method’s deep VFX and immersive talents and capabilities, using a customized real-time pipeline in combination with traditional VFX techniques to speed creative iteration and achieve a distinct stylized look. </p>
