        <h2>Xbox Halo 5: Guardians: "The Hunt Begins"</h2>
    

<p><strong>Director:</strong> Henry Hobson          <strong>Agency: </strong>twofifteenmccann          <strong>Production Company:</strong> Furlined</p>

<p>Helping launch the latest installment of the “Halo” franchise, cinematic spots “A Hero Falls” and “The Hunt Begins” showcase devastation on a massive scale as viewers catch glimpses of Master Chief and Spartan Locke. With its efficient pipelines and methodologies for urban destruction effects, Method applied extensive VFX to practical footage shot around the world. Artists built character models based on high resolution meshes used for in-game production, accelerating turnaround and enabling a near-identical match with the game’s aesthetic.</p>
