        <h2>Game of War “Decisions/Reputation”</h2>
    

<p><strong>Director:</strong> Gary Shore          <strong>Agency: </strong>Untitled Worldwide          <strong>Production Company: </strong>Anonymous Content</p>

<p>Starring model Kate Upton, the live action campaign features a CG fire-breathing hydra and Minotaur created by Method amid the destruction of a medieval battle. Artists also created a full CG aerial shot with a digital army and complex environments as well as extensive smoke, fire and debris, integrating all elements with practical footage.</p>
