        <h2>Bethesda: Quake Champions: E3 Reveal Trailer</h2>
    

<p><strong>Clients:</strong> gnet, id Software, Bethesda</p>

<p>Method helped reintroduce the popular Quake franchise to a new generation of gamers with the launch trailer for new arena-style multiplayer game Quake Champions. Debuted at E3, the fully CG trailer was co-directed by Method’s Dan Glass and shows off the game’s frenetic action, unique characters, and their powerful weapons and abilities in incredible cinematic detail. Method utilized previz, motion capture, animation, and VFX.</p>
