<p style="-webkit-tap-highlight-color: rgba(26, 26, 26, 0.3)">　　<span><span>中证网讯（记者 吴娟娟）记者5月6日从华夏基金获悉，</span></span><span><span>华夏恒生科技指数ETF等首批</span></span><span><span>跟踪恒生科技指数的ETF</span></span><span><span>已于近日获批，很快将与投资者见面，</span></span><span><span>为投资者布局港股新经济核心资产提供利器。</span></span> </p>
<p style="-webkit-tap-highlight-color: rgba(26, 26, 26, 0.3)">　　<span><span>公开资料显示，恒生科技指数追踪在港交所上市的30</span></span><span><span>只</span></span><span><span>大型科技股，当前权重股包括</span></span><span><span>腾讯、阿里巴巴、美团、小米、</span></span><span><span>中芯国际</span></span><span><span>、京东、网易等</span></span><span><span>公司，汇集了互联网、电商、社交、消费电子、半导体、医疗健康、软件等科技行业中最优秀的代表性公司。</span></span></p>
<p style="-webkit-tap-highlight-color: rgba(26, 26, 26, 0.3)">　　<span><span>Choice数据显示，自2020年4月29日至2021年4月30日一年中，恒生科技指数的涨幅达65.41%，不仅大幅超越同期恒生指数16.88%和沪深300指数33.11%的涨幅，更是超越了同期纳斯达</span></span><span><span>克指数62.21%、创业板指52.23%的涨幅，成为全球科技</span></span><span><span>类宽基</span></span><span><span>指数的领跑者。</span></span></p>
<p style="-webkit-tap-highlight-color: rgba(26, 26, 26, 0.3)">　　<span><span>值得一提的是，</span></span><span><span>华夏基金不仅是中国</span></span><span><span>权益ETF</span></span><span><span>管理</span></span><span><span>规模</span></span><span><span>最大</span></span><span><span>的</span></span><span><span>基金公司，也是</span></span><span><span>境内</span></span><span><span>港股类E</span></span><span><span>TF</span></span><span><span>管理规模最大的基金公司</span></span><span><span>。</span></span><span><span>截至一季度末，华夏基金旗下港股类E</span></span><span><span>TF</span></span><span><span>产品数量共有6只，总规模2</span></span><span><span>26.84</span></span><span><span>亿元，市场占比4</span></span><span><span>2.3</span></span><span><span>%。</span></span><span><span>其中</span></span><span><span>，</span></span><span><span>华夏恒生ETF</span></span><span><span>（1</span></span><span><span>59920</span></span><span><span>）</span></span><span><span>是</span></span><span><span>境内成立</span></span><span><span>最早，也是规模最大的港股类E</span></span><span><span>TF</span></span><span><span>，</span></span><span><span>截至</span></span><span><span>一季度末，</span></span><span><span>规模达到1</span></span><span><span>26.61</span></span><span><span>亿元</span></span><span><span>。</span></span></p>
