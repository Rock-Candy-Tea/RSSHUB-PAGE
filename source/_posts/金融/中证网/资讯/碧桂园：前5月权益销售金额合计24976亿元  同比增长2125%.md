<p style="    ">　　<span style>中证网讯（记者</span><span style> </span><span style>董添）近日，碧桂园发布</span><span style>2021</span><span style>年</span><span style>5</span><span style>月销售业绩。</span> <span style>5</span><span style>月单月，碧桂园共实现归属公司股东权益的合同销售金额约</span><span style>573.8</span><span style>亿元，环比增长</span><span style>8.63%</span><span style>，同比增长</span><span style>2.26%</span><span style>。前</span><span style>5</span><span style>月，碧桂园共实现累计权益合同销售金额</span><span style>2497.6</span><span style>亿元，同比增长</span><span style>21.25%</span><span style>。</span></p>
<p style="    ">　　<span style>据了解，碧桂园已连续</span><span style>5</span><span style>个月实现同比、环比增长。</span> <span style>2021</span><span style>年开年以来，随着疫情负面影响逐渐褪去，此前积累的购房需求逐步释放，碧桂园销售业绩增长势头良好，继续处于行业领先地位。</span></p>
<p style="    ">　　<span style>5</span><span style>月</span><span style>13</span><span style>日，《福布斯》发布的"</span> <span style>2021</span><span style>年上市公司</span><span style>2000</span><span style>强榜单"显示，碧桂园位列第</span><span style>140</span><span style>名，实现连续第</span><span style>12</span><span style>年上榜。此前，《财富》</span><span style>2020</span><span style>年度世界</span><span style>500</span><span style>强榜单中，碧桂园排名升至第</span><span style>147</span><span style>名，居全球房地产行业首位。</span></p>
<p style="    ">　　<span style>截至</span><span style>2020</span><span style>年</span><span style>12</span><span style>月</span><span style>底</span><span style>，公司有息负债总额降至</span><span style>3265</span><span style>亿元，同比下降</span><span style>11.7%</span><span style>，期末平均融资成本为</span><span style>5.56%</span> <span style>，同比进一步降低。净负债率降至</span><span style>55.6%</span><span style>，连续多年低于</span><span style>70%</span> <span style>，低于行业均值。截至</span><span style>2020</span><span style>年底，碧桂园可动用现金余额达</span><span style>1836</span><span style>亿元。基于上述财务指标的持续改善，穆迪将碧桂园评级调升至投资级</span><span style>Baa3</span><span style>，标普也将公司评级展望由"稳定"调升至"正面"。</span></p>
