<p class="p1">　　<span class="s1" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">中证网讯（记者</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em"> </span><span class="s1" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">金一丹）</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">5</span><span class="s1" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">月</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">10</span><span class="s1" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">日，据合肥发布消息，</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">2022</span><span class="s1" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">年安徽省重大项目“云签约”活动上，合肥市经开区与蔚来就</span><span style="letter-spacing: 0.05em"> </span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">NeoPark</span><span class="s1" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">新桥智能电动汽车产业园区整车二期和关键核心零部件配套项目签署合作协议。该项目占地</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">1860</span><span class="s1" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">亩，根据协议将导入蔚来集团旗下全新中高端品牌智能电动汽车产品等，计划</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">2024</span><span class="s1" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">年建成投产。据了解，该新品牌将与特斯拉、大众等展开市场竞争。目前，新品牌核心团队搭建完成，首批产品已经进入关键研发阶段。</span> </p>
