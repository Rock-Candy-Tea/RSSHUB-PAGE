<div>　　 <span class="s1">中证网讯（记者</span><span class="s2"> </span><span class="s1">黄一灵）</span><span class="s2">2</span><span class="s1">月</span><span class="s2">25</span><span class="s1">日，记者从上交所获悉，近期，科创50、科创信息指数迎来本年度第一次定期调样。根据指数编制规则，科创</span><span class="s2">50指数</span> <span class="s1">调入和辉光电、纳微科技、百克生物、美迪西、威高骨科</span><span class="s2">5</span><span class="s1">只证券，调出</span><span class="s2">5</span><span class="s1">只证券。科创信息指数纳入和辉光电、瑞可达、力芯微</span><span class="s2">3</span><span class="s1">只证券，调出</span><span class="s2">3</span> <span class="s1">只证券。此次样本调整后，指数的代表性有所加强。</span>
<p class="p1">　　<span class="s1">公开资料显示，科创</span><span class="s2">50</span><span class="s1">发布于</span><span class="s2">2020</span><span class="s1">年</span><span class="s2">7</span><span class="s1">月</span><span class="s2">23</span><span class="s1">日，由科创板中市值大、流动性好的</span> <span class="s2">50</span><span class="s1">只证券组成，反映最具市场代表性的一批科创企业的整体表现。科创信息指数发布于</span><span class="s2">2021</span><span class="s1">年</span><span class="s2">8</span><span class="s1">月</span><span class="s2">16</span><span class="s1">日，是科创板首条主题指数，选取</span> <span class="s2">50</span> <span class="s1">只市值较大的新一代信息技术领域上市公司为样本股。</span></p>
<p class="p1">　　<span class="s1">本次调整后，科创</span><span class="s2">50</span><span class="s1">指数日均总市值</span><span class="s2">2.48</span><span class="s1">万亿元，占科创板整体的</span><span class="s2">44.6%</span><span class="s1">，较调整前上升</span><span class="s2">1.7</span><span class="s1">个百分点。</span> <span class="s2">50只</span><span class="s1">样本股</span><span class="s2">2021</span><span class="s1">年前三季度营业收入合计</span><span class="s2">2424</span><span class="s1">亿元，占科创板整体的</span><span class="s2">49.1%</span><span class="s1">，占比上升</span> <span class="s2">2</span> <span class="s1">个百分点；净利润合计</span><span class="s2">343</span><span class="s1">亿元，占比</span><span class="s2">50.3%</span><span class="s1">，上升</span><span class="s2">7.6</span><span class="s1">个百分。科创</span><span class="s2">50样本股所属</span> <span class="s1">行业仍以信息技术、新材料、生物医药等硬科技为主，</span> <span class="s1">按战略新兴行业分类，前三大行业为新一代信息技术、新材料、生物医药，权重分别为</span><span class="s2">56.1%</span><span class="s1">、</span><span class="s2">13.1%</span><span class="s1">、</span><span class="s2">12.3%</span><span class="s1">。</span></p>
<p class="p1">　　<span class="s1">科创信息指数日均总市值</span><span class="s2">1.73</span><span class="s1">万亿元，占科创板新一代信息技术公司整体的</span><span class="s2">67.9%</span><span class="s1">，较调整前上升</span><span class="s2">1.8</span><span class="s1">个百分点。</span></p>
