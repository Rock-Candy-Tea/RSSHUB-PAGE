<p class="p1">　　<span class="s1" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em;">中证网讯（记者</span><span class="s2" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em;"> </span><span class="s1" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em;">金一丹）</span><span class="s2" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em;">5</span><span class="s1" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em;">月</span><span class="s2" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em;">20</span><span class="s1" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em;">日，蔚来在新加坡证券交易所主板正式挂牌，股票代码为"</span><span class="s2" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em;">NIO</span><span class="s1" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em;">"。蔚来正式成为全球首家完成三地上市的汽车企业。</span> </p>
<p class="p1">　　<span class="s1">蔚来创始人、董事长、</span><span class="s2">CEO</span><span class="s1">李斌表示："今天是蔚来一个新的里程碑，在新加坡上市对蔚来全球业务发展计划意义重大。借助新加坡在全球资本市场的重要战略地位，蔚来进一步完善了全球资本市场布局；我们也将借助新加坡的国际经济与科技中心的优势，与新加坡本地科研机构展开深度合作，在新加坡建立人工智能与自动驾驶研发中心，进一步完善蔚来的全球研发与业务布局。"</span></p>
<p class="p3">　　<span class="s1">数据显示，</span><span class="s2">2022</span><span class="s1">年前四个月，蔚来累计交付新车</span><span class="s2">30,842</span><span class="s1">辆，同比增长</span><span class="s2">13.5%</span><span class="s1">。截至</span><span class="s2">2022</span><span class="s1">年四月末，蔚来已累计向用户交付</span><span class="s2">197,912</span><span class="s1">台智能电动汽车。</span></p>
<p class="p3">　　<span class="s2">2022</span><span class="s1">年，蔚来将陆续交付三款基于</span><span class="s2">NT2.0</span><span class="s1">技术平台的新车型：智能电动旗舰轿车</span><span class="s2">ET7</span><span class="s1">已于</span><span class="s2">3</span><span class="s1">月</span><span class="s2">28</span><span class="s1">日实现交付；</span><span class="s1">中型智能电动轿跑</span><span class="s2">ET5</span><span class="s1">预计</span><span class="s2">2022</span><span class="s1">年</span><span class="s2">9</span><span class="s1">月开启交付；</span><span class="s1">另有一款中大型</span><span class="s2">SUV ES7</span><span class="s1">将在今年发布并交付。</span></p>
<p class="p3">　　<span class="s1">蔚来研发投入不断加大。</span><span class="s2">2021</span><span class="s1">年，蔚来研发投入同比增长</span><span class="s2">84.58%</span><span class="s1">，达到</span><span class="s2">45.92</span><span class="s1">亿元。</span><span class="s2">2022</span><span class="s1">年，蔚来预计全年研发投入同比增长</span><span class="s2">1</span><span class="s1">倍，年底研发人员增至</span><span class="s2">9000</span><span class="s1">人。截至</span><span class="s2">2022</span><span class="s1">年</span><span class="s2">4</span><span class="s1">月底，蔚来申请中及已授权专利总数超过</span><span class="s2">4800</span><span class="s1">件。</span></p>
<p class="p3">　　<span class="s1">此外，蔚来也正加速推进充换电业务。截至</span><span class="s2">5</span><span class="s1">月</span><span class="s2">20</span><span class="s1">日，蔚来在全国范围内布局换电站</span><span class="s2">927</span><span class="s1">座，超充站</span><span class="s2">804</span><span class="s1">座，超充桩</span><span class="s2">4,424</span><span class="s1">根。目的地充电站</span><span class="s2">702</span><span class="s1">座，目的地充电桩</span><span class="s2">4,045</span><span class="s1">根，接入第三方充电桩总量超</span><span class="s2">490,000</span><span class="s1">根。</span></p>
<p class="p3">　　<span class="s1">据悉，蔚来计划，</span><span class="s2">2022</span><span class="s1">年中国市场将累计建成超</span><span class="s2">1300</span><span class="s1">座换电站，超</span><span class="s2">6000</span><span class="s1">根超充桩，超</span><span class="s2">10000</span><span class="s1">根目的地充电桩，持续布局，让加电比加油更方便。至</span><span class="s2">2025</span><span class="s1">年底，蔚来换电站全球总数将超</span><span class="s2">4000</span><span class="s1">座，同时向行业全面开放</span><span class="s2">NIO Power</span><span class="s1">充换电体系及</span><span class="s2">BaaS</span><span class="s1">服务，与行业及智能电动汽车用户分享</span><span class="s2">NIO Power</span><span class="s1">建设成果。</span></p>
