0.05em"> </span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">2021</span><span class="s4" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">年国庆长假期间，</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">15</span><span class="s4" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">个重点监测城市新房成交套数为</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">8309</span><span class="s4" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">套。成交面积为</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">92.44</span><span class="s4" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">万平方米，同比下降</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">19%</span><span class="s4" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">，与</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">2019</span><span class="s4" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">年基本持平。其中，</span><span class="s3" 0.05em">天长假成交</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">4193</span><span class="s4" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">套，同比下滑幅度达</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">41%</span><span class="s4" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">；</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">2</span><span class="s4" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">个重点三线城市，</span><span <p class="p3">　　<span class="s3">58</span><span class="s4">安居客房产研究院分析师许之静认为，预计四季度楼市调控将延续从严趋势。传统"金九银十"特有的开盘潮、抢房潮、售罄、秒光、上涨等火热场面很难再现。"金九银十"已成为历史，楼市调控下，楼市将较长时间维持平稳态势，买卖双方将脱离对季节的依赖。</span></p>
