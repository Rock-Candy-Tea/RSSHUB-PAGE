<p class="p1">　　<span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">中证网讯（记者</span><span class="s2" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em"> </span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">董添）</span><span class="s2" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">7</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">月</span><span class="s2" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">4</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">日，融创中国在港交所发布公告称，正采取适当措施以符合复牌指引及相关上市规则，</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">并将适时刊发进一步公告，让公众知悉最新进展。</span><span class="s2" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">1-5</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">月集团累计实现合同销售金额约</span><span class="s2" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">987.8</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">亿元，累计合同销售面积约</span><span class="s2" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">740.1</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">万平方米。公司正积极制定有利于所有利益相关方的债务解决方案，</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">并已委任财务顾问及法律顾问以协助评估集团的资本结构及流动性状况。</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">公司及财务顾问一直与相关债权人保持积极沟通，</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">以期在切实可行的情况下尽快就债务解决方案达成共识，</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">目前相关工作正在有序推进。</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">公司将根据上市规则及适用法律的规定适时作出进一步公告。</span> </p>
