<p class="p1">　　<span class="s2" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">中证网讯（记者</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em"> </span><span class="s2" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">董添）海螺水泥</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">3</span><span class="s2" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">月</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">25</span><span class="s2" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">日晚间披露</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">2021</span><span class="s2" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">年年报。报告期内，公司共实现营业收入</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">1679.53</span><span class="s2" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">亿元，同比下降</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">4.73%</span><span class="s2" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">；实现归属于上市公司股东的净利润</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">332.67</span><span class="s2" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">亿元，同比下降</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">5.38%</span><span class="s2" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">。拟每</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">10</span><span class="s2" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">股派发现金红利</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">23.8</span><span class="s2" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">元（含税）。</span><span style="letter-spacing: 0.05em"> </span> 　　</p>
