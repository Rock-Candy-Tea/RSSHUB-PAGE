<p style="    ">　　<span style>中证网讯（记者</span><span style> </span><span style>董添）</span><span style>3</span><span style>月</span><span style>31</span><span style>日晚间，按照自然资源部关于做好</span><span style>2021</span><span style>年度住宅用地供应分类调控工作中招拍挂出让住宅用地的有关要求，</span><span style>2021</span><span style>年，北京市将于</span><span style>3</span><span style>月、</span><span style>7</span><span style>月左右、</span><span style>11</span><span style>月左右分三批次发布商品住宅用地出让公告。</span></p>
