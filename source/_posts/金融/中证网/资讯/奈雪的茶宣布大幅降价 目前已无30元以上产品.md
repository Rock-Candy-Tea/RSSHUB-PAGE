<p class="p1">　　<span class="s1" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">中证网讯（记者</span><span class="s2" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em"> </span><span class="s1" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">潘宇静）</span><span class="s2" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">3</span><span class="s1" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">月</span><span class="s2" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">17</span><span class="s1" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">日，奈雪的茶宣布大幅降价，推出</span><span class="s2" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">9-19</span><span class="s1" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">元的“轻松系列”，并承诺将每月上新至少一款</span><span class="s2" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">20</span><span class="s1" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">元以下产品。目前，奈雪的茶已无</span><span class="s2" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">30</span><span class="s1" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">元以上产品，主力价格带整体下移。</span> </p>
<p class="p1">　　<span class="s1">其中，轻松葡萄、轻松芒果定价</span><span class="s2">19</span><span class="s1">元，相比对标的原有产品霸气葡萄、霸气芒果价格下调高达</span><span class="s2">10</span><span class="s1">元，该系列还将每月推出至少一款新品。经过此次调整，奈雪价格带整体下移至</span><span class="s2">9</span><span class="s1">元起，</span><span class="s2">14</span><span class="s1">至</span><span class="s2">25</span><span class="s1">元成其主力价格区间。</span></p>
<p class="p1">　　<span class="s1">奈雪的茶称，调价是品牌深入洞察消费者需求，并基于自身强大的供应链能力、数字化能力和品质控制能力，希望在更宽的价格带中为消费者提供更轻松、丰富的选择。</span></p>
<p class="p1">　　<span class="s1">据了解，奈雪的茶此次下调价格，可能是配合其拓店步伐，进入更多的新城市，扩展更多客群。截至</span><span class="s2">3</span><span class="s1">月，奈雪的茶已经在全国</span><span class="s2">80</span><span class="s1">多个城市开出近</span><span class="s2">900</span><span class="s1">家门店，门店数量仍在增长中。</span></p>
