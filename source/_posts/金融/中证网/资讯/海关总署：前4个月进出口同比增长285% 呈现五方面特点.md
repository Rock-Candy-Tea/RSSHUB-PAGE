<p style="white-space: normal; widows: 1; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">　　<span lang="ZH-CN">中证网讯（记者</span> <span lang="ZH-CN">赵白执南）海关总署网站</span>5<span lang="ZH-CN">月</span>7<span lang="ZH-CN">日消息，据海关统计，今年前</span>4<span lang="ZH-CN">个月，我国进出口总值</span>11.62<span lang="ZH-CN">万亿元人民币，同比增长</span>28.5%<span lang="ZH-CN">，比</span>2019<span lang="ZH-CN">年同期增长</span>21.8%<span lang="ZH-CN">。其中，出口</span>6.32<span lang="ZH-CN">万亿元，同比增长</span>33.8%<span lang="ZH-CN">，比</span>2019<span lang="ZH-CN">年同期增长</span>24.8%<span lang="ZH-CN">；进口</span>5.3<span lang="ZH-CN">万亿元，同比增长</span>22.7%<span lang="ZH-CN">，比</span>2019<span lang="ZH-CN">年同期增长</span>18.4%<span lang="ZH-CN">；贸易顺差</span>1.02<span lang="ZH-CN">万亿元，同比增加</span>149.7%<span lang="ZH-CN">。</span></p>
<p style="white-space: normal; widows: 1; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">　　<span lang="ZH-CN">具体来看，前</span>4<span lang="ZH-CN">个月我国外贸呈现以下特点：一是一般贸易进出口增长、比重提升。前</span>4<span lang="ZH-CN">个月，我国一般贸易进出口</span>7.16<span lang="ZH-CN">万亿元，同比（下同）增长</span>32.3%<span lang="ZH-CN">，占我外贸总值的</span>61.6%<span lang="ZH-CN">，比去年同期提升</span>1.8<span lang="ZH-CN">个百分点。其中，出口</span>3.84<span lang="ZH-CN">万亿元，增长</span>38.8%<span lang="ZH-CN">；进口</span>3.32<span lang="ZH-CN">万亿元，增长</span>25.5%<span lang="ZH-CN">。同期，加工贸易进出口</span>2.57<span lang="ZH-CN">万亿元，增长</span>18%<span lang="ZH-CN">，占</span>22.1%<span lang="ZH-CN">，下滑</span>2<span lang="ZH-CN">个百分点。其中，出口</span>1.62<span lang="ZH-CN">万亿元，增长</span>19.9%<span lang="ZH-CN">；进口</span>9560.9<span lang="ZH-CN">亿元，增长</span>14.9%<span lang="ZH-CN">。此外，我国以保税物流方式进出口</span>1.41<span lang="ZH-CN">万亿元，增长</span>29.2%<span lang="ZH-CN">。其中，出口</span>4951<span lang="ZH-CN">亿元，增长</span>40.7%<span lang="ZH-CN">；进口</span>9147.8<span lang="ZH-CN">亿元，增长</span>23.7%<span lang="ZH-CN">。</span></p>
<p style="white-space: normal; widows: 1; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">　　<span lang="ZH-CN">二是对东盟、欧盟和美国等主要贸易伙伴进出口均增长。前</span>4<span lang="ZH-CN">个月，东盟为我国第一大贸易伙伴，我国与东盟贸易总值</span>1.72<span lang="ZH-CN">万亿元，增长</span>27.6%<span lang="ZH-CN">，占我外贸总值的</span>14.8%<span lang="ZH-CN">。其中，对东盟出口</span>9505.8<span lang="ZH-CN">亿元，增长</span>29%<span lang="ZH-CN">；自东盟进口</span>7650.5<span lang="ZH-CN">亿元，增长</span>25.9%<span lang="ZH-CN">；对东盟贸易顺差</span>1855.3<span lang="ZH-CN">亿元，增加</span>43.6%<span lang="ZH-CN">。欧盟为我国第二大贸易伙伴，与欧盟贸易总值为</span>1.63<span lang="ZH-CN">万亿元，增长</span>32.1%<span lang="ZH-CN">，占</span>14%<span lang="ZH-CN">。其中，对欧盟出口</span>9746.9<span lang="ZH-CN">亿元，增长</span>36.1%<span lang="ZH-CN">；自欧盟进口</span>6504.2<span lang="ZH-CN">亿元，增长</span>26.4%<span lang="ZH-CN">；对欧盟贸易顺差</span>3242.7<span lang="ZH-CN">亿元，增加</span>60.9%<span lang="ZH-CN">。美国为我国第三大贸易伙伴，中美贸易总值为</span>1.44<span lang="ZH-CN">万亿元，增长</span>50.3%<span lang="ZH-CN">，占</span>12.4%<span lang="ZH-CN">。其中，对美国出口</span>1.05<span lang="ZH-CN">万亿元，增长</span>49.3%<span lang="ZH-CN">；自美国进口</span>3930.5<span lang="ZH-CN">亿元，增长</span>53.3%<span lang="ZH-CN">；对美贸易顺差</span>6538.9<span lang="ZH-CN">亿元，增加</span>47%<span lang="ZH-CN">。日本为我国第四大贸易伙伴，中日贸易总值为</span>7706.4<span lang="ZH-CN">亿元，增长</span>16.2%<span lang="ZH-CN">，占</span>6.6%<span lang="ZH-CN">。其中，对日本出口</span>3407.4<span lang="ZH-CN">亿元，增长</span>12.6%<span lang="ZH-CN">；自日本进口</span>4299<span lang="ZH-CN">亿元，增长</span>19.2%<span lang="ZH-CN">；对日贸易逆差</span>891.6<span lang="ZH-CN">亿元，增加</span>53.6%<span lang="ZH-CN">。同期，我国对“一带一路”沿线国家合计进出口</span>3.43<span lang="ZH-CN">万亿元，增长</span>24.8%<span lang="ZH-CN">。其中，出口</span>1.95<span lang="ZH-CN">万亿元，增长</span>29.5%<span lang="ZH-CN">；进口</span>1.48<span lang="ZH-CN">万亿元，增长</span>19.3%<span lang="ZH-CN">。</span></p>
<p style="white-space: normal; widows: 1; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">　　<span lang="ZH-CN">三是民营企业进出口增长、比重提升。前</span>4<span lang="ZH-CN">个月，民营企业进出口</span>5.48<span lang="ZH-CN">万亿元，增长</span>40.8%<span lang="ZH-CN">，占我国外贸总值的</span>47.2%<span lang="ZH-CN">，比去年同期提升</span>4.1<span lang="ZH-CN">个百分点。其中，出口</span>3.53<span lang="ZH-CN">万亿元，增长</span>45%<span lang="ZH-CN">，占出口总值的</span>55.9%<span lang="ZH-CN">；进口</span>1.95<span lang="ZH-CN">万亿元，增长</span>33.7%<span lang="ZH-CN">，占进口总值的</span>36.8%<span lang="ZH-CN">。同期，外商投资企业进出口</span>4.32<span lang="ZH-CN">万亿元，增长</span>20.3%<span lang="ZH-CN">，占我国外贸总值的</span>37.2%<span lang="ZH-CN">。其中，出口</span>2.26<span lang="ZH-CN">万亿元，增长</span>24.6%<span lang="ZH-CN">；进口</span>2.06<span lang="ZH-CN">万亿元，增长</span>15.9%<span lang="ZH-CN">。此外，国有企业进出口</span>1.77<span lang="ZH-CN">万亿元，增长</span>16.2%<span lang="ZH-CN">，占我国外贸总值的</span>15.2%<span lang="ZH-CN">。其中，出口</span>5136.4<span lang="ZH-CN">亿元，增长</span>9.8%<span lang="ZH-CN">；进口</span>1.25<span lang="ZH-CN">万亿元，增长</span>19.1%<span lang="ZH-CN">。</span></p>
<p style="white-space: normal; widows: 1; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">　　<span lang="ZH-CN">四是机电产品和劳动密集型产品出口均增长。前</span>4<span lang="ZH-CN">个月，我国出口机电产品</span>3.79<span lang="ZH-CN">万亿元，增长</span>36.3%<span lang="ZH-CN">，占出口总值的</span>59.9%<span lang="ZH-CN">。其中，自动数据处理设备及其零部件</span>4899<span lang="ZH-CN">亿元，增长</span>32.2%<span lang="ZH-CN">；手机</span>2920.6<span lang="ZH-CN">亿元，增长</span>35.6%<span lang="ZH-CN">；汽车（包括底盘）</span>577.6<span lang="ZH-CN">亿元，增长</span>91.3%<span lang="ZH-CN">。同期，出口劳动密集型产品</span>1.11<span lang="ZH-CN">万亿元，增长</span>31.9%<span lang="ZH-CN">，占</span>17.5%<span lang="ZH-CN">。其中，服装及衣着附件</span>2887<span lang="ZH-CN">亿元，增长</span>41%<span lang="ZH-CN">；包括口罩在内的纺织品</span>2856.5<span lang="ZH-CN">亿元，增长</span>9.5%<span lang="ZH-CN">；塑料制品</span>1869.6<span lang="ZH-CN">亿元，增长</span>42.6%<span lang="ZH-CN">。此外，出口钢材</span>2565.4<span lang="ZH-CN">万吨，增加</span>24.5%<span lang="ZH-CN">；成品油</span>2460.8<span lang="ZH-CN">万吨，减少</span>5.3%<span lang="ZH-CN">。</span></p>
<p style="white-space: normal; widows: 1; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">　　<span lang="ZH-CN">五是铁矿砂、大豆和铜进口量价齐升，原油、天然气等商品进口量增价跌。前</span>4<span lang="ZH-CN">个月，我国进口铁矿砂</span>3.82<span lang="ZH-CN">亿吨，增加</span>6.7%<span lang="ZH-CN">，进口均价为每吨</span>1009.7<span lang="ZH-CN">元，上涨</span>58.8%<span lang="ZH-CN">；原油</span>1.8<span lang="ZH-CN">亿吨，增加</span>7.2%<span lang="ZH-CN">，进口均价为每吨</span>2746.9<span lang="ZH-CN">元，下跌</span>5.4%<span lang="ZH-CN">；煤</span>9012.6<span lang="ZH-CN">万吨，减少</span>28.8%<span lang="ZH-CN">，进口均价为每吨</span>477.7<span lang="ZH-CN">元，下跌</span>6.7%<span lang="ZH-CN">；天然气</span>3945.9<span lang="ZH-CN">万吨，增加</span>22.4%<span lang="ZH-CN">，进口均价为每吨</span>2228.9<span lang="ZH-CN">元，下跌</span>17.6%<span lang="ZH-CN">；大豆</span>2862.7<span lang="ZH-CN">万吨，增加</span>16.8%<span lang="ZH-CN">，进口均价为每吨</span>3235.6<span lang="ZH-CN">元，上涨</span>15.5%<span lang="ZH-CN">；初级形状的塑料</span>1212.4<span lang="ZH-CN">万吨，增加</span>8%<span lang="ZH-CN">，进口均价为每吨</span>1.07<span lang="ZH-CN">万元，上涨</span>15.4%<span lang="ZH-CN">；成品油</span>803.8<span lang="ZH-CN">万吨，减少</span>14.9%<span lang="ZH-CN">，进口均价为每吨</span>3670.9<span lang="ZH-CN">元，上涨</span>4.7%<span lang="ZH-CN">；钢材</span>489.1<span lang="ZH-CN">万吨，增加</span>16.9%<span lang="ZH-CN">，进口均价为每吨</span>7611.3<span lang="ZH-CN">元，上涨</span>3.8%<span lang="ZH-CN">；未锻轧铜及铜材</span>192.1<span lang="ZH-CN">万吨，增加</span>9.9%<span lang="ZH-CN">，进口均价为每吨</span>5.58<span lang="ZH-CN">万元，上涨</span>29.8%<span lang="ZH-CN">。同期，进口机电产品</span>2.27<span lang="ZH-CN">万亿元，增长</span>21%<span lang="ZH-CN">。其中，集成电路</span>2100<span lang="ZH-CN">亿个，增加</span>30.8%<span lang="ZH-CN">，价值</span>8222.4<span lang="ZH-CN">亿元，增长</span>18.9%<span lang="ZH-CN">；汽车（包括底盘）</span>33.3<span lang="ZH-CN">万辆，增加</span>39.8%<span lang="ZH-CN">，价值</span>1170.4<span lang="ZH-CN">亿元，增长</span>46.9%<span lang="ZH-CN">。</span></p>
