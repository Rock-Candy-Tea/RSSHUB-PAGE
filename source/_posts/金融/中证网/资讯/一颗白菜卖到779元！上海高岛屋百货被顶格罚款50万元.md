<div>　　<span class="s1" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">中证网讯（记者</span><span class="s2" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em"> </span><span class="s1" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">董添）</span><span class="s2" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">3</span><span class="s1" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">月</span><span class="s2" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">26</span><span class="s1" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">日，一则“一颗白菜卖到</span><span class="s2" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">77.9</span><span class="s1" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">元，上海高岛屋百货被顶格罚款</span> <span class="s2" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">50</span><span class="s1" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">万元”的消息冲上热搜。上海市市场监管局通报显示，自</span><span class="s2" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">2020</span><span class="s1" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">年</span> <span class="s2" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">9</span><span class="s1" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">月起，汪某以上海农康蔬菜专业合作社名义与高岛屋百货公司签订联销合同，其将从农贸市场和生鲜小店等处购入的普通蔬菜进行分拣、包装后，以精品高端的定位高价对外销售。高岛屋百货公司未对上述内容进行审核。另查，自</span><span class="s2" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">2022</span> <span class="s1" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">年</span><span class="s2" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">2</span><span class="s1" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">月</span><span class="s2" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">14</span><span class="s1" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">日起，高岛屋百货公司对</span> <span class="s2" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">8</span><span class="s1" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">款商品开展促销活动，现场标价签标注的所谓“原价”均为虚构。</span> 
<p class="p1">　　<span class="s1">上海市市场监管局指出，汪某及高岛屋百货公司的相关行为涉嫌违反《中华人民共和国价格法》的相关规定，构成价格欺诈违法行为。3月26日，上海市市场监管局根据相关法律规定</span><span class="s3">已分别制发行政处罚听证告知书，对汪某处以</span><span class="s4">10</span><span class="s3">万元的顶格处罚，对高岛屋百货公司处以</span><span class="s4">50</span> <span class="s3">万元的顶格处罚。</span></p>
