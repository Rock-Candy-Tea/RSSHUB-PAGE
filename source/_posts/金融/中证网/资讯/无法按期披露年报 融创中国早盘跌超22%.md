<p>　　<span class="s1">中证网讯（记者</span><span class="s2"> </span><span class="s1">董添）</span><span class="s2">3</span><span class="s1">月</span><span class="s2">29</span><span class="s1">日，融创中国早盘股价大幅下跌，截至</span><span class="s2">10：</span><span class="s2">20</span><span class="s1">，公司股价最高跌幅达到</span><span class="s2">22.06%</span><span class="s1">。</span></p>
<p class="p2">　　<span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">融创中国</span><span class="s4" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">3</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">月</span><span class="s4" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">28</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">日晚间公告，</span><span class="s4" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">2021</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">年度业绩不能在</span><span class="s4" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">3</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">月底或之前刊发，公司股份将自</span><span class="s4" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">4</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">月</span><span class="s4" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">1</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">日起暂停买卖。</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">公告显示，考虑目前尚未完成的部分财务报表编制工作的预期工作量及所需时间；近期因国际评级机构下调公司评级引起有关境外贷款的问题，公司正积极与相关债权人沟通解决方案；及融创房地产境内公开债展期事项等对财务报表影响的不确定性，预期无法于本月底前完成</span><span class="s4" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">2021</span><span class="s3" style="letter-spacing: 0.05em">年度财务报表的编制。</span> </p>
