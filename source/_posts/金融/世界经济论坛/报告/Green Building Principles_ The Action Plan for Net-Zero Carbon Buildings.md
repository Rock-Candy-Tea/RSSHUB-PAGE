    <p>Collective action must be taken to accelerate the decarbonization of buildings, which contribute <a href="https://globalabc.org/resources/publications/2020-global-status-report-buildings-and-construction" target="_blank" rel="noopener">38% of all energy-related</a> greenhouse gas emissions. <i>Green Building Principles: The Action Plan for Net-Zero Carbon Buildings</i> offers a set of 10 principles to help companies deliver net zero carbon buildings and meet key climate commitments.</p>
</div>
<div class="st__content-block st__content-block--text">
    <p>Developed with JLL, the Principles and Action Plan outlined in this document aim also offer implementation strategies to decarbonize buildings at a portfolio level. This Action Plan draws on existing recommendations and signposts to an array of current targets to deliver this set of Principles at a global level while allowing for adaption to local contexts.</p>
