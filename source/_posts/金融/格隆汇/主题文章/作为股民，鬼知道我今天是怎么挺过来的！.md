src="https://img3.gelonghui.com/1703a-3b5b2be5-ba0f-41ba-bd1e-dcb5e984ac97.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><strong><span>人生三大幻觉，足球会赢，房价会跌，股票会涨。</span></strong></p><p><img src="https://img3.gelonghui.com/ce481-8daf86e1-ce69-4872-ae01-618ec84c8f46.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><span>所以真正的股民从不认输。</span></p><p><img referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><span>但经历这一波大涨大跌，现在无论追高的，抄底的，补仓的，做差价的，还是无论高手新手老手，全部都被市场摁在地板上使劲儿摩擦，就问还有谁不服？</span></p><p><img src="https://img3.gelonghui.com/a2817-cc1ec570-adc0-46eb-8077-f0b4794c72ae.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://img3.gelonghui.com/99237-f8f2d879-63aa-4b59-863a-102e5a976d58.png"
