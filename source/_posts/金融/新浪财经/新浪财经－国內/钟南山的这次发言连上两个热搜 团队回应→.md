<p class="ori_titlesource">　　原标题：钟南山的这次发言连上两个热搜，团队回应→ </p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">“因为没有病人了</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">三期临床试验只能在国外做”</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　近日，钟南山院士</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　受邀出席上海科技大学2021届毕业典礼</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　他的一段“凡尔赛”发言</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　连上两个热搜</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider202176/131/w578h353/20210706/0cc6-ksmehzs7922255.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">为什么我国疫苗研发看起来比较慢？</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">“因为没有病人了”</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在演讲中</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　谈及中国新冠疫苗的研发进展</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">钟南山表示，中国现在71个疫苗正在研发</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　9个在临床三期试验中</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　有两个是世界卫生组织批准紧急使用的</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　已经列入了紧急使用清单</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider202176/200/w1080h720/20210706/cfcb-ksmehzs7922259.jpg" alt="△图片来源：上海科技大学" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr">△图片来源：上海科技大学</span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　钟南山表示：</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“因为在中国由于防控有效</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　我们没有办法在中国</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　做第三期临床试验</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">因为没有病人了</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">所以三期临床试验只能在国外做”</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　不少网友感叹</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">“一张口就是老凡尔赛了！”</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">“这个凡尔赛超级喜欢！”</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider202176/565/w663h702/20210706/2db9-ksmehzs7922321.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">钟南山团队回应：</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">感谢用实际行动支持抗疫的每个人</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　今天（7月6日）上午</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　钟南山院士创新研究团队</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　官方账号@南山呼吸 </p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　回应此事，再度冲上热搜</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider202176/104/w640h264/20210706/2473-ksmehzs7828932.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">回应称</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">这种话语被网友说成凡尔赛</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">其实需要每一位</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">以实际行动支持抗疫的你</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　戴好口罩、做好社交隔离</p>
