<div>   
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　此前</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在线地图平台陆续开放台湾省地图权限</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在地图中搜索到的</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“山西刀削面”“山东水饺”“广西螺蛳粉”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　等店铺引起众多网友的关注</p>
<p cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">　　味道不会忘记</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　8日</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　台湾省地图上的“广东味道”也被有心人找到</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这是一家位于台湾新北市的经营了5年的</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　广东肠粉店<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">↓</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20220809/451/w800h451/20220809/edc4-2720c9bfefd2f4eed7a6f5e70460425b.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　据该店铺的店主介绍</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　五年前</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　他在一家广东师傅开的店铺里当学徒</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　学会了具有广东特色的肠粉、烧腊等传统菜式</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　2017年8月</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　他回到台湾新北市开了这家广东肠粉店</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“开店5年来生意都很不错</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">虽然我是台湾本地人</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">但做的肠粉是广东师傅教出来的正宗味道</font></font></font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　有顾客对该店铺评价称</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“很地道的广东肠粉</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　辣椒和酱汁的味道都很好”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　也有人留言说</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“之前在广东待过半年</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">回台湾非常想念广东的肠粉</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　没想到能吃到这么好吃地道的肠粉”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　除了这家广东肠粉店</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在台湾省的地图上搜索“广东”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">会发现</font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">台湾省多地都有粤菜馆</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　数量众多，品类丰富</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　广东粥、烧腊饭、早茶等应有尽有</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　仅在台北市就能搜到70余家“广东粥”</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20220809/591/w640h751/20220809/5e61-78a81de27341d8e163ad5f6c52c27c48.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　美食不仅是味蕾的连接</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　更是记忆的承载</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">在台北市还有一条街叫做</font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">“广州街”</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　广州街的附近是台北市有名的夜市</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在这里也能看到许多广州的特色元素</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20220809/284/w640h444/20220809/5f1d-db5e07f936396d74baf16333d1006155.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">　　每条路都是回家的路</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">在</font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">#地图可显示台湾省每个街道#</font></font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">话题</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“爆”上热搜之后</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20220809/496/w452h44/20220809/f3fb-262742af24fe9e9bd66f34f7bae7e86b.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　网友发现</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">通过手机地</font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">图软件搜索台湾省</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　除了可以看到完整显示的地图</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　以及详细的路况信息之外</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在地图软件上搜索“京台高铁”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">还可以显示</font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">京台高铁线路图</font></font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">（建设中）<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">↓</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20220809/103/w1080h2223/20220809/30a7-3b4d870e237c1a95b560a73a0604fe7c.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">京台高铁</font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">起点为北京南站</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　经廊坊、天津、沧州继续向南</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="5" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20220809/103/w1080h2223/20220809/251f-2a7515499e138eecefe6542571dd5042.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在台湾省设高铁新竹站</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">终点为高铁台北站</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="6" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20220809/115/w1080h2235/20220809/0bb1-04c0b05081152beb021c862803bef7d3.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一餐一食、一种熟悉的味道</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　都寄托着对祖国母亲的思念</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一街一巷、一条线路</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　都在诉说着根在哪里</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　两岸同胞日日夜夜深情守望</p>
