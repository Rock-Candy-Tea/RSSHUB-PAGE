<p cms-style="font-L">　　国庆节前最后一个交易日，A股半导体板块反弹明显，<span id="stock_sh688711"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688711/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">宏微科技</a></span><span id="quote_sh688711"></span>、<span id="stock_sh605358"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh605358/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">立昂微</a></span><span id="quote_sh605358"></span>、<span id="stock_sh600171"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600171/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">上海贝岭</a></span><span id="quote_sh600171"></span>、<span id="stock_sh688728"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688728/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">格科微</a></span><span id="quote_sh688728"></span>等个股表现抢眼。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　值得注意的是，在近期二级市场股价回落之际，机构却加强了对半导体产业链上公司的调研。Choice数据显示，近一个月来，<span id="stock_sz300327"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300327/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">中颖电子</a></span><span id="quote_sz300327"></span>、<span id="stock_sh688589"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688589/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">力合微</a></span><span id="quote_sh688589"></span>、<span id="stock_sz002138"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002138/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">顺络电子</a></span><span id="quote_sz002138"></span>、<span id="stock_sh688019"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688019/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">安集科技</a></span><span id="quote_sh688019"></span>、<span id="stock_sh688106"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688106/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">金宏气体</a></span><span id="quote_sh688106"></span>等获得众多机构调研。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/transform/461/w550h711/20211002/56ec-b2bc7566bf76d4bce785fbc10152980c.jpg" alt data-link referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">机构调研忙</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">三季度业绩可期</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　“市场低迷，更要加强调研！基本面良好的情况下，跌下来就是机会。”近期A股半导体板块低迷，有机构投资者表示，下跌就是机会，并加强了对半导体产业的调研。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　上市公司的投资者关系记录也体现出机构调研的忙碌程度。东财Choice数据显示，近一个月以来，共有28家半导体公司获得调研。其中，中颖电子以获得8次调研的高频率位列榜首，力合微、<span id="stock_sz003043"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz003043/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">华亚智能</a></span><span id="quote_sz003043"></span>、<span id="stock_sz002079"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002079/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">苏州固锝</a></span><span id="quote_sz002079"></span>各获得4次调研。调研记录显示，所有公司中，机构来访占比较高。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211002s/747/w1080h1267/20211002/5e6b-d963e98bac1c60e72b5f0d7a5bb5c820.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">部分公司接待调研频率较低，但接待数量却很客观。近一个月来，<span id="stock_sh603986"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603986/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">兆易创新</a></span><span id="quote_sh603986"></span>、<span id="stock_sh688012"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688012/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">中微公司</a></span><span id="quote_sh688012"></span>、<span id="stock_sh688766"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688766/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">普冉股份</a></span><span id="quote_sh688766"></span>虽仅接待一次调研，但接待量分别为381家、226家、100家；<span id="stock_sh688126"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688126/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">沪硅产业</a></span><span id="quote_sh688126"></span>接待2次调研，合计接待量为264家。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　半导体设备、电子化学品是国产半导体的薄弱环节。近一个月来，电子化学品板块中，有8家上市公司获得调研，其中，安集科技、金宏气体、<span id="stock_sh688268"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688268/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">华特气体</a></span><span id="quote_sh688268"></span>分别获得7次、4次、3次调研。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211002s/763/w1080h483/20211002/b493-85ec5bc3b7932842b9c4e131f49a48bb.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　扩展到整个电子产业，元件板块中，顺络电子、<span id="stock_sh603989"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603989/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">艾华集团</a></span><span id="quote_sh603989"></span>、<span id="stock_sh603738"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603738/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">泰晶科技</a></span><span id="quote_sh603738"></span>、<span id="stock_sz002938"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002938/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">鹏鼎控股</a></span><span id="quote_sz002938"></span>、<span id="stock_sz002916"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002916/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">深南电路</a></span><span id="quote_sz002916"></span>近一个月获得调研的次数分别为4次、3次、3次、3次、3次。光学光电子板块中，<span id="stock_sz002456"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002456/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">欧菲光</a></span><span id="quote_sz002456"></span>、<span id="stock_sz300303"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300303/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">聚飞光电</a></span><span id="quote_sz300303"></span>、深纺织关注度较高，分别获得5次、4次、4次调研。消费电子板块中，<span id="stock_sz002139"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002139/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">拓邦股份</a></span><span id="quote_sz002139"></span>、<span id="stock_sh605277"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh605277/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">新亚电子</a></span><span id="quote_sh605277"></span>、<span id="stock_sz300976"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300976/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">达瑞电子</a></span><span id="quote_sz300976"></span>、<span id="stock_sz002937"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002937/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">兴瑞科技</a></span><span id="quote_sz002937"></span>等获得较高关注度，机构调研活跃。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L color0">不仅如此，宏微科技、<span id="stock_sz002119"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002119/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">康强电子</a></span><span id="quote_sz002119"></span>、<span id="stock_sz002241"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002241/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">歌尔股份</a></span><span id="quote_sz002241"></span>、<span id="stock_sz002409"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002409/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">雅克科技</a></span><span id="quote_sz002409"></span>等上市公司三季报“预喜”。需求增长、产销两旺是半导体公司业绩增长的主因。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　比如，宏微科技披露，得益于光伏、新能源市场对IGBT需求快速增长，预计今年前三季度实现归属于上市公司股东的净利润3145.42万元至3359.96万元，同比增长94.41%至107.67%。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　康强电子预计，前三季度归母净利润为1.1亿元至1.3亿元，同比增长53.58%至81.5%；业绩增长主因行业景气度高，下游客户需求增加，公司部分产品供不应求，产销两旺；蚀刻引线框架产品的市场需求增长显著，使得业绩同比增长较多。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">多家原厂再提价</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">景气度逐季抬升</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　近两年，国产半导体设备、材料类公司突破良多，成长加速。比如，在半导体设备领域，<span id="stock_sz002371"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002371/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">北方华创</a></span><span id="quote_sz002371"></span>、中微公司在前道刻蚀领域已经进入先进行列，<span id="stock_sh600641"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600641/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">万业企业</a></span><span id="quote_sh600641"></span>的离子注入机连获订单。在半导体材料领域，<span id="stock_sh603650"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603650/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">彤程新材</a></span><span id="quote_sh603650"></span>旗下公司北京科华、<span id="stock_sz300655"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300655/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">晶瑞电材</a></span><span id="quote_sz300655"></span>的光刻胶进展可圈可点，安集科技、<span id="stock_sz300054"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300054/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">鼎龙股份</a></span><span id="quote_sz300054"></span>在CMP抛光液、CMP研磨垫领域的市场份额不断提升。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　今年以来，半导体景气度逐季抬升，相关上市公司业绩水涨船高。近日，记者广泛采访业内人士获得的共识是，半导体产业整体景气度至少持续至2022年年中。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">基于产业景气度持续高涨的预期，部分芯片原厂继续“涨价”：</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　9月27日，意法半导体对客户发出涨价函，将在今年最后一个季度提高所有产品线价格。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　赛灵思（Xilinx）通知客户，从11月份起，所有的versal系列价格提高10%，其他产品提价20%。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　安森美半导体表示，由于产品供不应求、原材料成本上涨等，将自10月份起，对部分产品进行提价，这是该公司今年第二次提价。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　“对国内半导体公司来说，市场渗透率提升、产品品类扩张是更长远的逻辑和看点。”对于A股半导体投资逻辑，<span id="stock_sh601688"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601688/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">华泰证券</a></span><span id="quote_sh601688"></span>原电子首席分析师、飞图创投合伙人纪攀峰认为，产业趋势比财报更为重要，技术壁垒、市场份额是非常关键的评判指标。他表示看好半导体设备、材料等领域，“半导体设备材料是国内比较薄弱的环节，也是未来市场提升最具有想象力的领域之一”。</p><!-- news_keyword_pub,stock,sz002079&sz002119&sz002138&sz002139&sz002241&sz002371&sz002409&sz002456&sz002916&sz002937&sz002938&sz003043&sz300054&sz300303&sz300327&sz300655&sz300976&sh600171&sh600641&sh601688&sh603650&sh603738&sh603986&sh603989&sh605277&sh605358&sh688012&sh688019&sh688106&sh688126&sh688268&sh688589&sh688711&sh688728&sh688766 -->
