<div>   
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">用火箭外壳技术</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">制造滑雪头盔？</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center color0">　　近日，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　2022年北京冬奥会开幕</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　进入倒计时，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　为助力“科技冬奥”，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L  align-Center">　　大连理工大学科研团队自主研发的一款高性能滑雪头盔，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　引来不少网友点赞：</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">堪称“黑科技、高颜值”！</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220105s/626/w400h226/20220105/aceb-b11eeeb8080988a4b59c6fda0379f585.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　据了解，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这款滑雪头盔</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">运用了航天薄壁结构设计科技，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　技术则来源于<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">程耿东</font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">院士指导、</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　王博教授牵头的科研团队</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center color0">　　十几年的研究积累。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　此前，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　他们曾利用这项技术</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　为我国<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">体积最大的大火箭——</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　长征五号运载火箭“胖五”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">成功减重1145公斤。</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper">
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://f.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220105s/426/w787h439/20220105/0e92-37ad3909110e6858b89c0145fcbb6e32.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
</div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在这款头盔的设计研发中，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　科研团队运用了</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">曲线加筋变刚度的设计技术，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　大幅提升了头盔的抗冲击变形能力，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　为运动员提供<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">更高的安全防护级别。</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220105s/626/w400h226/20220105/0339-cdfec65d0d9b3aa37089d2c0109d1281.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　同时，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　头盔的材料也有所创新，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在保持冲击韧度的同时，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">将刚度提高了4倍，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center color0">　　拉伸强度提高了3倍，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　大幅提升了头盔的抗冲击吸能效率，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　对运动员形成更好的保护。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220105s/626/w400h226/20220105/cca7-7acf59a28fd8832c991b72398bcc8280.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这款头盔还充分考虑到</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">亚洲人的头型特征。</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　团队采集了大量</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">国家队运动员的头型数据，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　并研发了设计软件，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　实现了运动员头盔的<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">定制化，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　有效改善了长时间佩戴的舒适性。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220105s/626/w400h226/20220105/c89d-2bf20d2c04dcab82bca9a6c08aa2be24.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　据介绍，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这款头盔重量<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">只有700g左右，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在不大幅增加厚度和重量的前提下，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">防护性能比欧洲标准至少提升了20%。</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="5" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220105s/626/w400h226/20220105/4111-59fffc51ef6d619cceb3191425738194.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L  align-Center">　　目前，这款头盔已顺利通过</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　欧洲滑雪头盔安全标准测试，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　为冬奥会滑雪相关项目</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　国家队训练提供安全保障，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">助力中国健儿夺金。</font></font></p>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　冬奥会<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">不仅一场体育赛事，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　也是一次科技盛会。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　记者注意到，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在这次“科技冬奥”中，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　还有不少高校的身影</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">↓↓↓</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　东莞理工学院参与研制的</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　个性化定制雪车头盔，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　将应用于</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">冬奥国家队项目训练与比赛，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">助力中国代表团取得理想成绩。</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="6" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220105s/424/w1044h180/20220105/cfb7-df737cf40acd3c922888b2921dcb2213.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="7" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220105s/290/w1080h810/20220105/e919-a1638671912a4d82b290592fe527e916.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　北京交通大学</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　李波教授团队的<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">风洞实验室，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">在训练中帮助运动员减小风阻影响，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　李静教授团队的</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　应急医学智能处置决策系统</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">也在现场“指挥”应急医学救援。</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="8" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220105s/53/w1080h573/20220105/c855-8a7800e57d54231e041bf7d101c7f315.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">共赴冰雪之约，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">为中国科技点赞！</font></font></p>
