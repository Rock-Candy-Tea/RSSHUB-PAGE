<p>　近期A股行情回暖、成交额放大，卖方也没有停下挖掘牛股的脚步。梳理研报数据发现，过去一周<span id="stock_sh603833"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603833/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">欧派家居</a></span><span id="quote_sh603833"></span>、<span id="stock_sz300450"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300450/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">先导智能</a></span><span id="quote_sz300450"></span>、<span id="stock_sz002304"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002304/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">洋河股份</a></span><span id="quote_sz002304"></span>的卖方关注度最高，此外上市后7连板的次新股<span id="stock_sh605499"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh605499/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">东鹏饮料</a></span><span id="quote_sh605499"></span>也被多家卖方机构看好。从卖方给出的目标价来看，有13只股票的预期上涨空间超50%，其中<span id="stock_sh600136"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600136/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">当代文体</a></span><span id="quote_sh600136"></span>、<span id="stock_sh600000"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600000/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">浦发银行</a></span><span id="quote_sh600000"></span>的空间最大，股价有望翻倍。</p>
