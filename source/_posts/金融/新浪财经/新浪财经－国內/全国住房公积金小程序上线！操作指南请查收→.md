<p class="ori_titlesource">　　原标题：全国住房公积金小程序上线！操作指南请查收→</p>
<p class="ori_titlesource">　　来源：国务院客户端</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　住建部推出的“全国住房公积金小程序”已经在国务院客户端小程序上线。手机上点一点，公积金转移接续轻松办理！</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">具体操作指南</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">第一步</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　扫描下方葵花码进入【便民服务】</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　然后点击“公积金”→“全国住房公积金”</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20211016/584/w683h701/20211016/c257-ab42c9776a2c0c25b899321d6a808ee5.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">第二步</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　点击底部“服务”页卡，进入后在“业务办理”选择“转移接续”</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20211016/579/w684h695/20211016/7fd4-f4f6696880d41f7c246138e0da681b64.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">第三步</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　进入这个页面后，填写相关信息后提交申请即可。</p>
