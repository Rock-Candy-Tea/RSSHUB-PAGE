<div>   
<p cms-style="font-L">　　财联社10月2日讯，国庆长假前4个交易日，数据显示，北向资金一共净流入69.67亿元，其中9月28日净流入47.86亿元，为4个交易日最多。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">白酒持续受青睐 消费占半壁江山</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　从近5个交易日北上资金的增持市值来看，排在前十位中，消费股占据了半壁江山，有4家白酒公司和1家食品饮料。其中，<span id="stock_sz000858"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000858/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">五粮液</a></span><span id="quote_sz000858"></span>增持市值最多，达18.54亿元，<span id="stock_sh600519"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600519/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">贵州茅台</a></span><span id="quote_sh600519"></span>被增持16.94亿元，<span id="stock_sh600809"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600809/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">山西汾酒</a></span><span id="quote_sh600809"></span>和<span id="stock_sz002304"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002304/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">洋河股份</a></span><span id="quote_sz002304"></span>分别被北上资金增持9.41亿和7.36亿元；<span id="stock_sh600887"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600887/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">伊利股份</a></span><span id="quote_sh600887"></span>最近5个交易日被北上资金增持14.18亿元。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/transform/550/w550h800/20211002/50af-a691edf3cae1b5550096af95c7fe0f0e.png" alt data-link referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　值得一提的是，在此前一周，北向资金已大幅增持了白酒板块，“茅五泸”均获得加仓，本周，白酒板块整体出现大涨，<span id="stock_sz000568"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000568/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">泸州老窖</a></span><span id="quote_sz000568"></span>本周上涨18.87%。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/transform/716/w550h166/20211002/babb-eef8755f5dbca0a04fabf4b6fa053b0c.png" alt data-link referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　对于北向资金的动向，<span id="stock_sz002736"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002736/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">国信证券</a></span><span id="quote_sz002736"></span>表示，从持仓占比的变化角度看，9月份外资加仓食品饮料、医药生物和公用事业等行业，减仓机械设备、电子和有色金属等行业。集中度上看，9月份行业、个股集中度均有所回升。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　国信证券认为此前一系列事件冲击影响最大的时刻已经过去， 9月份A股市场也有所企稳，各大主要指数普遍收涨，在这样背景下外资流入流出波动大幅降低，开始缓慢净流入。往后看一方面是目前A股市场在盈利好、估值低的组合下仍然具备非常高的投资性价比。另一方面是当前外资对A股配置比例依然处于明显的低配状态，因此长期来看外资持续净流入依然大有空间。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">大金融遭减持 两大银行股流出居前</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　在北向资金减持方面，大金融板块遭到大幅度减持，<span id="stock_sz000001"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000001/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">平安银行</a></span><span id="quote_sz000001"></span>和<span id="stock_sh600036"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600036/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">招商银行</a></span><span id="quote_sh600036"></span>分别被北向资金减持9.11亿和9.03亿元；<span id="stock_sh601318"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601318/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">中国平安</a></span><span id="quote_sh601318"></span>遭减持8.06亿元。而最近5个交易日，北向资金流出最多的是<span id="stock_sh600031"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600031/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">三一重工</a></span><span id="quote_sh600031"></span>，减持12.07亿元。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　此外，<span id="stock_sz000651"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000651/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">格力电器</a></span><span id="quote_sz000651"></span>、<span id="stock_sz300760"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300760/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">迈瑞医疗</a></span><span id="quote_sz300760"></span>、<span id="stock_sz000661"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000661/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">长春高新</a></span><span id="quote_sz000661"></span>等也受到了大幅度减仓。</p>
