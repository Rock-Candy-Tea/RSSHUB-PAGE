<p cms-style="font-L">　　来源：中国发展高层论坛</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　过去的2021年，世纪疫情与国际变局持续交织，我国“两个一百年”奋斗目标历史交汇，中国与世界在激荡中复苏，企业和个人在磨砺中前行。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　这一年，在中国，许多行业与企业迎接着全新的发展机遇和行业趋势；这一年，为中国，世界500强与行业领先企业的商业领袖们变中求新、变中求进，深耕中国，携手共进，共创未来。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　今年的“CDF之声”栏目邀请参会代表分享过去一年来自己与所领导的企业、所在行业的经历以及他们的感悟。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　今天做客“CDF之声”的是<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold color210">Visa公司董事长兼首席执行官艾克礼（</font></font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold color210">Alfred F． Kelly， Jr．）</font></font>。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　艾克礼首先向所有参加北京冬奥会和冬残奥会的优秀运动员表示了祝贺。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　艾克礼表示，Visa多年赞助奥运会、残奥会。他非常高兴能够见证运动员在北京展现出自己的最佳水平。Visa与合作伙伴为女性小微企业家提升商业技能的培训项目“冬奥有她”也入选了北京2022年冬奥会和冬残奥会“城市与区域发展遗产案例“。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　他指出，Visa植根中国发展已近40年，与各领域的合作伙伴通过数字支付技术的应用，支持中国的普惠金融、乡村振兴、人文交流等经济社会发展目标。Visa与本地伙伴合作开展的金融教育项目，已累计惠及一千多万中国农村居民。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　最后，艾克礼希望Visa能与中国持续加强合作，助力经济社会发展，继续传递“一起向未来”的精神力量。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　以下为中英文原文：</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　大家好。我是艾克礼，是Visa全球董事长兼首席执行官。很高兴能够代表Visa，与大家分享我对2022年中国发展高层论坛的展望与祝愿。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　首先，我要向所有参加北京冬奥会和冬残奥会的优秀运动员表示祝贺。Visa成为奥运全球TOP赞助商已超过35年，对残奥会的支持也超过20年。我们致力于赋能毕生为之奋斗的运动员在奥运舞台取得成功、实现梦想。今年，“Visa之队”的中国运动员包括自由式滑雪运动员谷爱凌；花样滑冰运动员隋文静、韩聪；以及单板滑雪运动员纪立家。非常高兴能够见证他们在北京展现出自己的最佳水平。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　我曾有幸多次参加中国发展高层论坛。参会的经历宝贵且难忘。在为期三天的论坛上，来自世界各地的政商界高层代表齐聚一堂，共谋全球化之道、探讨机遇和挑战。受疫情影响，今年我们仍无法线下相聚。但通过各方对共同面临的关键问题展开持续对话，论坛的主题精神将得以延续。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　作为一家使命驱动的公司，Visa致力于打造全球网络，成就你我，助力小微企业疫情复苏。2020年，Visa承诺三年内支持全球5000万家小微企业的数字化发展。我们正稳步朝着这个目标迈进。我们希望能赋能每一家企业，使他们从数字化转型中获益。通过Visa的全球网络，我们希望能尽可能连接更多的商户和消费者。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　Visa植根中国发展已近40年。今天，我们与中国57家领先的金融机构合作，使中国的消费者可以在境外和网上便捷支付。同时，我们也帮助本地商户受理Visa外卡产品。展望未来，我们预见中国数字商务发展的光明前景。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　上次访问中国时，我很荣幸亲临Visa北京创新中心的启动仪式。通过与本地初创企业的合作，我们共同为中国市场定制创新解决方案，这也充分体现了“在中国，为中国”。Visa北京创新中心也提供了一个平台，与我们的中国合作伙伴和中国同事，沟通分享来自全球市场的见解和实践。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　携手金融、科技、电子商务等领域的合作伙伴，我们通过数字支付技术的应用，支持中国的普惠金融、乡村振兴、人文交流等经济社会发展目标。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　多年来，Visa与本地伙伴合作开展的金融教育项目，已累计惠及一千多万中国农村居民，帮助他们更好地生活和工作。就在去年，Visa向论坛递交了一篇题为“发展数字普惠，助力脱贫攻坚，推动乡村振兴”的报告。报告呈现了我们与国务院发展研究中心开展的为期两年的研究成果。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　除此以外，Visa还携手中国妇女发展基金会和北京体育大学，共同推动冬奥举办地和京津冀区域协同发展。该项目提供了全面的商业技能培训，支持约5000名文旅行业的女性小微企业家，提升数字能力，对接冬奥机遇。该项目入选了北京2022年冬奥会和冬残奥会“城市与区域发展遗产案例“。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　Visa希望能与中国持续加强合作，助力经济社会发展。我们希望能通过今年的论坛，继续传递“一起向未来”的精神力量。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　最后，再次感谢主办方的邀请。希望明年能够在北京与大家见面！</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　Hello， I am Al Kelly， Chairman and CEO of Visa．</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　On behalf of our employees in China and around the world， I am pleased to share my thoughts and best wishes for the 2022 China Development Forum．</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　Let me begin by congratulating all of the talented athletes who recently competed in the Beijing Winter Olympic and Paralympic Games．  Visa has been an Olympic sponsor for more than 35 years， and a Paralympic sponsor for more than 20 years．  Our sponsorship helps to support the incredible athletes who dedicate their lives to train for the Games．  </p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　This year， we were pleased to support Team Visa athletes from China including freestyle skier Eileen Gu； figure skaters Sui Wenjing and Han Cong； and para snowboarder Ji Lijia．  It was a real pleasure to watch them all compete and perform at the highest level．</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　Turning now to the China Development Forum， I have had the great honor to attend CDF in person on multiple occasions．  It is always a valuable and memorable experience – three days of gathering with senior business and government leaders from around the world to discuss how we can best address global challenges and opportunities together．  Today， while the pandemic prevents us from gathering in person， the spirit of CDF lives on through sustained dialogue about the most critical issues facing our societies．  </p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　As a purpose-driven company， Visa is committed to uplifting everyone， everywhere， and helping small businesses recover from the pandemic．  In 2020， Visa committed to digitally enable 50 million small businesses globally in three years – and we are on track to meet that goal．  We want to help ensure that every business has the right tools to benefit from digital transformation， and we are on a mission to connect as many buyers and sellers as possible through our global network．  </p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　Visa has operated in China for nearly 40 years．  Today， we partner with 57 of China’s leading financial institutions to help Chinese consumers shop online and outside of China． We also enable local merchants to receive payments from foreign visitors using Visa products．  Looking ahead， we see a bright future for the continued development of digital commerce in China．  </p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　On my last trip to China， I had the honor of opening our Beijing Innovation Center where we work with local Chinese start-ups to create new custom solutions for the Chinese market – “In China” and “For China”． The Visa Beijing Innovation Center is also an ideal platform for sharing insights and best practices from around the world with our Chinese partners and colleagues．   </p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　Working together with a range of financial， technology and ecommerce partners in China， we have also applied digital payments technology to support social imperatives such as financial inclusion， rural revitalization， and people-to-people exchange．  Over the years， Visa and our local partners have helped more than 10 million Chinese rural residents improve their lives and work through financial literacy programs． In fact， last year， Visa submitted a paper to the China Development Forum exploring the topic of how digital financial inclusion helps drive China’s poverty alleviation and rural revitalization． The report highlighted the key findings of a two-year joint research project with the Development Research Center of the State Council．</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　And this year， Visa is working in partnership with the China Women’s Development Foundation and Beijing Sport University to help advance development in the Jing-Jin-Ji region that hosted the Winter Olympics． Through the program， as many as 5，000 women-led small businesses in the culture， travel and sport sector will receive comprehensive business skills training．  They are learning how to use digital technology and preparing for business opportunities brought by the Olympics． The program was featured as one of the legacy case studies which demonstrate the lasting impact of the Olympic and Paralympic Winter Games Beijing 2022 throughout the region．   </p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　Visa is committed to strengthening collaboration and promoting economic growth throughout China．  That is the spirit with which we approach CDF 2022， working “together for a shared future．”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　Thank you for the opportunity to participate． I hope to be able to be with you all in person next year． </p><!-- news_keyword_pub,stock, -->
