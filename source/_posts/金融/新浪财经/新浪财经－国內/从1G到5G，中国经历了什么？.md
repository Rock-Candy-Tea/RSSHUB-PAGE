<p>　　来源：国资小新</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　当你看到这行字的时候</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　我们便跨越万水<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">千山</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　完成了一场无声的对话</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　而这习以为常的一刻</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在30多年前</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">却几乎</font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">无法想象</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　30年</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　从1G的大哥大</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　到全球规模最大的5G网络</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　我们的通信技术高速飞奔</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　我们的生活方式也彻底改变</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　回忆过去的30年</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　你会看到一段怎样的历程？</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　值517世界电信日到来之际</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">小新为您带来</font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><span id="stock_sh600941"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600941/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">中国移动</a></span><span id="quote_sh600941"></span></font></font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">联合</font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">星球研究所</font></font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　共同出品的科普视频</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">《</font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">从1G到5G，</font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">中国经历了什么？</font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">》</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　用9分钟为你呈现</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">30年速度与激情</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　欢迎点击播放</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">▼</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　太阳系的边缘，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　距离地球超过230亿公里。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">飞行时速高达6万公里</font>的旅行者1号</font>探测器，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　用了<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">超过40年</font></font>才抵达这里，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　然而，它的信息从这里传回地球，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　却仅仅需要<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">约20个小时</font></font>。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/78/w550h328/20220517/3125-8c3cf4422a103228529a45986c001b01.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　因为用于传输信息的，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　是<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">以光速传播的无线电波</font></font>。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　如今，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这种电波不仅连通了地表之外的星际空间，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　更在地表之上，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　将全世界紧密相连。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/62/w550h312/20220517/4f22-04beafca3cf2924bf9ff41dfbc06de6b.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　截至2021年，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　全球仅移动通信用户人数已达到惊人的<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">53亿</font></font>，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　而在中国，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　更以<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">超过20%</font></font>的用户占比</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　以及<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">超过60%</font></font>的5G基站占比，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　位列<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">世界第一</font></font>。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/58/w550h308/20220517/6836-a41cb7e1d06679167ae5e77f2318f978.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　那么，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　中国是如何做到的？</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　从1G到5G，我们都经历了什么？</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　未来，又是怎样的呢？</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　首先，我们要从这条曲线开始说起。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/59/w550h309/20220517/be57-700e5b54bffd1aea59e5e1e495053339.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这是一段“简化版”的无线电波，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　通过改变它的振幅、频率和相位，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　便可以承载不同的信息。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　而一般频率越高，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　可利用的频段带宽就越大，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　传输容量也就越大。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="5" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/62/w550h312/20220517/5aea-2f55f7cb13f1da4a9ed550baeca54486.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　因此，根据频率的不同，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　无线电波被划分成了众多频率片段，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　用于完成不同的使命，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　包括无线定位、导航、广播以及卫星气象等等，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　再加上各种其他功能的占用，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　留给移动通信的频段已是十分有限。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="6" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/56/w550h306/20220517/3c03-63c59a2da16fe3bd0061b30806931936.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　如何<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">充分利用这些稀缺的频谱资源</font></font>，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这就是一代代移动通信追求的目标。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="7" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/62/w550h312/20220517/ccb6-275b45d2c51ab16c2af0b29246baa71e.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　然而，在30多年前，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　中国人在广州打通第一个移动电话时，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这样的探索基本都依赖于欧洲和美国。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　和当时大量涌入的进口商品一样，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　中国的<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">第一代移动通信技术（1G）</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　也是直接引进国外开发的模拟通信系统。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　之所以叫模拟通信，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　是指利用无线电波的振幅</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　或频率模拟声波的波动特征，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　来完成语音的传输。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　但此时，我们距离世界第一张商用1G网络，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　已落后了8年之久。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="8" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/59/w550h309/20220517/a5ff-3633e05ef7a52e3a4eb0fbbf303fbc03.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　不过，<span id="stock_sh600628"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600628/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">新世界</a></span><span id="quote_sh600628"></span>的大门已经打开，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　1992年，全球形势风云变幻，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　邓小平再次来到中国南方，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　坚定了全国“以科学技术为第一生产力”的决心和方向。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　次年，<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">中国第一个2G商用网络在浙江嘉兴开通</font></font>，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　距离全球首个2G网络商用</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　仅仅1年多时间。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="9" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/54/w550h304/20220517/27b4-7f491b94372b3daf7fb4326bcea0c499.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　和1G网络不同，这张2G网络引进的</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　是一种全新的通信标准<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">GSM</font></font>。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　其中无论是语音还是文字，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　都能被转化为一个个数字信号，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　再通过无线电波进行传输。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这意味着，移动通信不再局限于打电话，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　还能发短信，并且具备更好的保密性。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　不仅如此，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　曾经的1G网络是将有限的频段</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　直接划分给不同的用户使用，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　因此容量十分有限，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这称作<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">FDMA</font></font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">，</font></font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">频分多址技术</font></font>。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="10" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/gif_image/625/w400h225/20220517/0b8b-5dcb9373e384b9cb16e90280670848a1.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　但在GSM系统中，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　还能根据使用时间（时隙）的不同，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　将同一频段再次划分给新的用户，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　因此得名<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">TDMA，</font></font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">时分多址技术</font></font>。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="11" src="https://f.sinaimg.cn/finance/gif_image/625/w400h225/20220517/1e1f-105c3d89ecc9ed57352901acf8a505e5.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这样一来，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　2G网络对频谱的利用效率大大提高，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　容量明显提升，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　移动通信也不再是极少数人的特权，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　越来越多的人可以随时随地联系家人、朋友，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　去聊一聊自己的第一个双休日如何度过，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　聊一聊郎平是否能延续中国女排的辉煌，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　又或是刚刚回归的香港未来是什么模样。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　而转折，也在此时悄悄酝酿。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　中国向国际电信联盟提交了</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">我们第一个拥有自主知识产权的</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">3G标准TD-SCDMA。</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="12" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/60/w550h310/20220517/ccec-1defc1ea1b7c4e4c8ad034b46b4d015a.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　时间进入21世纪，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　北京申奥成功，中国加入世贸组织，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　三峡水库蓄水，中国人第一次进入太空……</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一个高歌猛进的时代已然到来。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　移动通信也不例外，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　我们开始通过手机连接网络，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　从这块小小的屏幕中触碰一个宏大的世界，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　我国的移动用户数</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　也首次超过固定电话用户数，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">移动通信开始成为主流。</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="13" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/58/w550h308/20220517/022a-c2b9ce9ba6de2687a50a225930d0dfd3.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　而到2007年，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　酝酿了多年的转折，终于到来。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　随着智能机的普及，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　人们对数据流量的需求被彻底引爆。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　随后，<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">3G在中国商用</font></font>。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　此后仅仅4年，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　速度更快的<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">4G横空出世</font></font>，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">中国主导的TD-SCDMA逐渐向TD-LTE演进，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">成为4G时代的国际主流标准之一。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　除支持更大的带宽外，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　随着运算能力的进步，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　4G网络能通过一种</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　更加强大的<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">频分多址技术（OFDMA）</font></font>，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　将原本单个用户使用的频段再次“划分”，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　并根据需求动态地分配给不同用户；</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　而通过更加先进的<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">天线阵列技术（MIMO）</font></font>，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　4G网络还能在物理空间上定向发送信号，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　让多个用户同时使用同一频段却不互相干扰，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　频谱效率再次提高。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="14" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/gif_image/625/w400h225/20220517/988d-9014eb9c8e8deebbd41eed80b1deff93.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　最终，在4G时代，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　移动支付、在线教育、短视频、</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　直播纷纷成为人们的日常，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　也真正开启了移动互联网的世界。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">那么，还能更快吗？</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　2019年，在浙江杭州，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　中国移动开通了</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">全国第一批满足商用标准的5G基站</font></font>，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这个“更高、更快、更强”的任务，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　将交给一个全新的5G时代。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这个时代，将使用更高频率的波段，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　最大带宽可达到4G的<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">5倍</font></font>。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="15" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/59/w550h309/20220517/e90a-53ad93accaaaa81179a49e34f03eb0b6.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这个时代，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　将使用更加优化的<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">多址技术</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　和更大规模的<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">天线阵列</font></font>，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　TDD技术中的上下行数据分配也更加灵活，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　从而获得更高的频谱效率。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="16" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/gif_image/625/w400h225/20220517/8728-2db5591897a8489eb3a2a17e2ce37400.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　再加上各种其他环节的提升，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　最终到来的5G时代，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　可达到<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">10倍于4G</font></font>的用户体验速率，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">10倍</font></font>的终端连接量，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　以及仅仅<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">1/10的时延</font></font>。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="17" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/gif_image/625/w400h225/20220517/6cc6-cc7391e769e9fce099b84fceda9ec0da.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　我们也终于不再遥望世界的背影，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　而是<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">在多项5G关键标准的制定中，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">发挥着主导的作用。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　今天，无论是在漠河的雪原，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　还是云南的雨林，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在世界之巅的珠穆朗玛，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　还是百米之深的矿井之下，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　中国大地上，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">上百万座5G基站覆盖了全国所有地级以上城市，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">构成了全球规模最大的5G网络。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　而拥有超过80万座基站，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　155项国际标准，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　3600项专利的<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">中国移动</font></font>，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　也成为了全球最大的5G运营商。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　此外，电信央企也开启了“共享”模式，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">中</font></font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">国电信</font></font>与<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210"><span id="stock_sh600050"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600050/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">中国联通</a></span><span id="quote_sh600050"></span></font></font>共建共享</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">超过76万座</font>5G基站，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　建成了全球规模最大共建共享5G SA网络；</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">中国铁塔</font></font>则承建了超139万<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">5G基站</font>，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　其中97%实现了共享。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这张庞大的5G网络，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　不仅能够带来全新的通话体验；</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　能支持我们流畅观看4k甚至8k清晰度的视频；</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　还能以更快的网络连接、更强的计算能力</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　和更大规模的终端连接量，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　构建一个综合<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">“连接+算力+能力”的</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">新型信息服务体系</font></font>，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　更好地服务全社会的生产生活，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　最终创造一个</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">“网络无所不达、算力无所不在、</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">智能无所不及”的数智化社会。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　但是，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这就是发展5G的全部意义吗？</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　当然不是。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　正如2G还未商用，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　3G概念就已提出；</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　4G刚刚落地，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　5G研究便拉开帷幕。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　再到如今，5G网络方兴未艾，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">6G的探索已然启动</font></font>。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一代代通信标准的升级，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　并非是为了满足人们当前的需要，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　而是为了未来奠定坚实的基础。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在这个未来里，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">由通信技术带来的各行各业的创新和变革，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">远比通信行业本身的发展更有价值。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　就像当我们回首1G到5G的历程，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　会发现，它不仅仅是一代人的青春和记忆，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　更是一个时代日新月异的见证，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　也是一个国家改头换面的进程。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　正如中国工程院院士邬贺铨曾说：</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“5G背后承载的，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　绝不仅仅是一个移动通信，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">它承载了中国高技术产业的希望，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">是我们国家是不是停留在低技术发展的阶段，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">能不能走到高技术、</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">走到世界的前面（的见证）。</font></font>”</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="18" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/59/w550h309/20220517/de01-dedc41c6ae6b7a7913e256e631450e71.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　当然，终有一天5G时代也将成为回忆，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　但这就是科技发展的规律。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　因为人类从未满足于停留在原地，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　我们的足迹终将走得更远。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　而到那时，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　我们彼此之间的连接，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　也必将延伸到一个</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　超越想象的广阔世界。</p><!-- news_keyword_pub,stock,sh600050&sh600628&sh600941 -->
