<p cms-style="font-L">　　4月19日上午，一头鲸鱼在浙江省宁波市象山县附近海域搁浅，当地渔政、消防等部门工作人员立即赶往现场开展救援。</p>
<!--video-list-->

<div class="video-2017" id="videoList0" style></div>

<!--/video-list-->
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">象山相关部门</font>发现鲸鱼搁浅后</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　派出五艘船进行救援</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　由于鲸鱼体型庞大</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　几种救援方式都失败了</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220419s/713/w456h257/20220419/978a-9a7ebf78bff99380578b5deccb92981c.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">海水退潮后</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">营救更加困难</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">只能先采取原地保护的措施</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220419s/636/w412h224/20220419/b865-0850343525dbe558824eb4cb358ca069.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　现场的救援人员没有放弃</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　不停地向鲸鱼身上泼水</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　保持其体表湿润</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　全力呵护</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220419s/702/w451h251/20220419/c620-604eb1d94eda290f09d4c85cf713262b.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　宁波鱼人水生野生动物救护中心</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　工作人员王亮亮告诉记者</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">经测量，鲸鱼体长约19米</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">尾部、头部等都有明显擦伤</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　目前已进行清创处理</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　还比较虚弱</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　只能尽量缓解它的不适</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220419s/702/w451h251/20220419/28d9-cd9c2c63667289c1cafb5d948cfd4fa2.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">专家分析原因</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　浙江海洋大学的赵盛龙教授分析</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　鲸鱼在近浅海搁浅</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　很有可能是因为本身受了伤</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　或者生命即将走向终点</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　身体条件不足以支撑其在远海生存</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="5" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220419s/702/w451h251/20220419/3c0d-c4b0784b731a9afa0015ed5cfbc7285f.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　工作人员用绳索把鲸鱼固定</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　预计到晚上涨潮后</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　船只从海上进来</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　再把鲸鱼拖带出去</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　赵盛龙教授解释</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　对于搁浅鲸鱼的救助</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　人类所能做的工作非常有限</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">“救助人员可以对其进行喷水保湿</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">确保鲸鱼呼吸畅通</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">希望鲸鱼能够撑过</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">退潮这一段最为艰难的时间”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　来源：新华社、宁波晚报</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L">记者：顾小立、张璇、</font><font cms-style="font-L">陈善君、张晓曦</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　监制：周年钧</p>
