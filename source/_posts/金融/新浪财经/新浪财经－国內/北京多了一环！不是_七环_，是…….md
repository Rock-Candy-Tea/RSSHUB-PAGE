<p>　　原标题：北京多了一环！不是“七环”，是……</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210"><!--article_adlist[<img src="https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_gif/azXQmS1HA7lCxiaicd7Au2P0LgZr4aQe8PCKmZl64lBUDzVE7F06CSMAxesRPz5KcEF2xJcgTPiccfltKKbIxQVcw/640?wx_fmt=gif" alt="宜：植树" cms-width="677" cms-height="74.0156" black-list="y"/>]article_adlist--></font></font></font>3月12日植树节</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　宜：认识北京的“新环”</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220312s/480/w240h240/20220312/4714-6f405f527753844e11d9674e4634f6e0.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“环”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这一特殊的符号</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">丈量着北京</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">这座“双奥之城”、千年古都的发展</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">更是这片土地上</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">与市民朝夕相处的坐标</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　北三环、南二环、西五环、东四环</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　……</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这里的衣食住行</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　总心照不宣地用“环”来标注</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220312s/218/w1080h738/20220312/8677-b8c127e5d2556a9c283e7a60b9a2c9e7.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　但你可能不知道</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">如今，北京又有一道“新环”悄然诞生</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　它可以随风舞动</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　也能悦人眼目</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　宛若一条“绿色项链”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这是一个由102座公园</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　串联而成的公园环</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　横跨北京的三环、四环、五环</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　闭合为一道绿意萌发的“新环”</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220312s/521/w1080h1041/20220312/b548-3c18446490bc9922c3a6764aee5337f7.png" alt="图为绿隔地区范围。（来源：“首都绿隔”）" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr">图为绿隔地区范围。（来源：“首都绿隔”）</span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　敲黑板！</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">“新环”</font></font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">学名“一道绿隔城市公园环”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">1987年便启动建设</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">用来防止城市建设无序扩张</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">为首都发展减量提质增绿</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　2012年以来</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　北京市的两轮百万亩造林绿化</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　加速推进“新环”建成</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220312s/563/w360h203/20220312/ad3b-b2a3b16c0616765d3f16b9b1bbccb7dc.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　截至目前</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“一道绿隔”地区310平方公里范围内</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　绿化面积约137.2平方公里</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　城市公园们</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一个接一个“手挽起手”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">“一环百园”的生态格局</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">让京华大地的“绿水青山”更加蔚为壮观</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　也让市民们休闲之时拥有了更多“小确幸”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　（此处应有五环之歌）</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220312s/35/w745h890/20220312/247c-068ab2dd3f742cfcfdbd73b6002d8620.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　悄悄告诉来北京游玩的小伙伴们</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　除了长城、故宫、颐和园之外</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这些各具特色的城市公园</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　也能让你“尽兴而归”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　赶紧来挖一挖“新环”里的宝藏吧</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="5" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220312s/380/w190h190/20220312/7a3e-ac83194f7e6982a4788fdf8b71e33b3d.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在大型“天然氧吧”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　奥林匹克森林公园</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　市民不仅可以</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　大口呼吸、清润心肺</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　更能感受“双奥之城”的魅力荣光</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="6" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220312s/229/w1080h749/20220312/9363-2332aa968979699a8afad1ba2e4941da.png" alt="2月4日，北京冬奥会火炬在北京奥林匹克森林公园传递。新华社记者 李贺 摄" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr">2月4日，北京冬奥会火炬在北京奥林匹克森林公园传递。新华社记者 李贺 摄</span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　无人车、智能步道、智能语音亭</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　……</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　散步的同时</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在海淀公园，邂逅人工智能</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　喜欢“潮玩”的朋友们</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　千万不要错过体验黑科技</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="7" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220312s/31/w480h351/20220312/2b23-afa3b2bb99abc9139d54d12b44d36d90.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　颇具“国际范儿”的</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　当属紧邻三里屯的朝阳公园</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　如画的风景</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　吸引着众多市民和国际友人</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　前去健身、游园</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="8" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220312s/794/w480h314/20220312/9bdf-17ebb81df301badf795fe024df1b9f5b.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　朝阳区十八里店乡的横街子公园</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　曾为疏解腾退后裸露的黄土地</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　它通过“消化”建筑垃圾实现华丽蜕变</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　如今成为自驾爱好者的秘密丛林</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="9" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220312s/290/w1080h810/20220312/335c-19232c5c93721bbf47f50f813d517dff.png" alt="图为横街子公园一角。（北京市园林绿化局供图）" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr">图为横街子公园一角。（北京市园林绿化局供图）</span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　总面积近30万平方米的霍营公园</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　地处北京人口密度较大的</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　回龙观、天通苑地区</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　为周边超100万居民打开了绿色空间</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="10" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220312s/200/w1080h720/20220312/2a88-b7dc01022fdb2111a181f8fa77c5bd22.png" alt="图为在霍营公园玩耍的小朋友。（来源：北青社区报）" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr">图为在霍营公园玩耍的小朋友。（来源：北青社区报）</span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　杜仲公园</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　结合中药“杜仲”的药用价值</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　以药用植物和养生文化为公园主题</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　打造中国范儿的“康养森林”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　中医药文化“发烧友”们一定期待地搓起了手</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="11" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220312s/201/w1080h721/20220312/42dd-914e8738cb67b527d9befd83ad600dd1.png" alt="图为杜仲公园一角。（北京市园林绿化局供图）" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr">图为杜仲公园一角。（北京市园林绿化局供图）</span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　10年间，随着“新环”落成</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　北京全市森林覆盖率达到44.6%</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　城市绿化覆盖率达到49%</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　野生动植物种类和数量也不断增加</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="12" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220312s/472/w302h170/20220312/0770-1e0a907d6c17f9f7ceda1b0f2b1f1bf7.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　与此同时</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“二道绿隔郊野公园环”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　生态格局也逐步显现</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　环首都国家公园正在规划</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="13" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220312s/229/w1080h1549/20220312/d1d3-5d18ca8c3a9a2144f6724984d16f0008.png" alt="《北京城市总体规划（2016年—2035年）》规划图。（北京市园林绿化局提供）" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr">《北京城市总体规划（2016年—2035年）》规划图。（北京市园林绿化局提供）</span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　未来</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　也许会有更多“新环”陆续亮相</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　大胆想象</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　我们或许可以听到《新五环之歌》</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="14" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220312s/259/w150h109/20220312/53a1-a3f0f86f176b45f1c271ec3542c0eaf1.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">绿水青山终不负</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">山川大地共起舞</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　枝芽新生、绿意盎然</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center strong-Bold color0">　　在北京</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">美丽的新画卷正徐徐展开</font></p>
