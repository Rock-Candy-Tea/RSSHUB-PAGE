<p cms-style="font-L">　　“医美茅”300亿巨额刚解禁，股东三年暴赚47倍后减持在即！下周40股将面临解禁，多只热门概念股在列（附股）</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　下周40只股将面临解禁，合计解禁市值320.53亿元。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　9月29日刚迎来超300亿大解禁的“医美茅”<span id="stock_sz300896"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300896/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">爱美客</a></span><span id="quote_sz300896"></span>，在节后第一天就有股东抛出减持计划。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　10月8日晚间，“医美茅”爱美客发布减持公告，称其收到第三大股东GannettPeak的《关于计划减持股份的告知函》，该股东共持有爱美客1296万股，此次拟减持不超过458.7万股，占公司总股本的2.12%。对于减持的原因，爱美客在公告中表示：系股东自身的资金需求。<font cms-style="font-L">按照爱美客前一个交易日599元/股来算，该次股东拟套现27.48亿元</font>。据悉，GannettPeakLimited是一家创投，该部分股权为爱美客上市前获得，交易对价约1.6亿元，与如今78亿元的市值相比，三年时间暴赚47倍。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　关于本次减持，爱美客表示，GannettPeak不属于公司控股股东、实际控制人，本次减持计划实施不会导致公司控制权发生变更，不会对公司治理结构及持续性经营产生影响。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/253/w550h503/20211010/b42b-9957440b8313bde7f7f692b025abdc31.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">值得关注的是，爱美客2020年全年实现营收7.09亿元，此次GannettPeak的套现金额几乎是去年公司营收的四倍。</font>爱美客2021年上半年营收6.33亿元，同比增长161.87%；归母净利润为4.25亿元，同比增长188.86%。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">40股面临解禁</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　下周将有40只股面临解禁，按照最新收盘价计算，合计解禁市值320.53亿元。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　解禁市值规模最大的是<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold"><span id="stock_sz300999"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300999/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">金龙鱼</a></span><span id="quote_sz300999"></span></font>。下周将有1.47亿股上市流通，主要是首发原股东限售股份和首发战略配售股份，解禁市值达106.15亿元。<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold"><span id="stock_sz000800"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000800/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">一汽解放</a></span><span id="quote_sz000800"></span>、<span id="stock_sz000875"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000875/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">吉电股份</a></span><span id="quote_sz000875"></span>、<span id="stock_sh600746"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600746/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">江苏索普</a></span><span id="quote_sh600746"></span></font>的解禁市值居前。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　22股的解禁压力较小，解禁市值均不足亿元。<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold"><span id="stock_sh688022"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688022/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">瀚川智能</a></span><span id="quote_sh688022"></span></font>的解禁市值最小，将有905.34万元的市值规模面临解禁。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　从解禁股数量占总股本的比例来看，<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold"><span id="stock_sz300694"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300694/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">蠡湖股份</a></span><span id="quote_sz300694"></span></font>的解禁比例居首，达到37.71%；<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold"><span id="stock_sz002786"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002786/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">银宝山新</a></span><span id="quote_sz002786"></span>、<span id="stock_sh688330"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688330/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">宏力达</a></span><span id="quote_sh688330"></span>、一汽解放、吉电股份</font>等股的解禁比例居前。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　解禁股数量占总股本比例不足1%的有13股。<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold"><span id="stock_sz002953"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002953/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">日丰股份</a></span><span id="quote_sz002953"></span>、<span id="stock_sh603127"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603127/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">昭衍新药</a></span><span id="quote_sh603127"></span>、<span id="stock_sz300486"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300486/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">东杰智能</a></span><span id="quote_sz300486"></span>、瀚川智能、<span id="stock_sh688158"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688158/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">优刻得</a></span><span id="quote_sh688158"></span>-W</font>的解禁比例较小。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">解禁前夕，多只股跑输大盘</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　证券时报·数据宝统计显示，下周将要解禁的40只股票，自9月以来股价平均下跌0.37%，跑输同期大盘（1.36%）。9月以来累计涨幅超过10%的有7股，分别是<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold"><span id="stock_sz300967"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300967/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">晓鸣股份</a></span><span id="quote_sz300967"></span>、<span id="stock_sz300830"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300830/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">金现代</a></span><span id="quote_sz300830"></span>、<span id="stock_sz300973"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300973/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">立高食品</a></span><span id="quote_sz300973"></span>、吉电股份、<span id="stock_sh603222"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603222/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">济民医疗</a></span><span id="quote_sh603222"></span>、日丰股份、<span id="stock_sh688663"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688663/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">新风光</a></span><span id="quote_sh688663"></span></font>。股价累积跌幅超过10%的有8股，<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">蠡湖股份、宏力达、<span id="stock_sz300540"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300540/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">深冷股份</a></span><span id="quote_sz300540"></span>、瀚川智能、江苏索普、<span id="stock_sz300971"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300971/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">博亚精工</a></span><span id="quote_sz300971"></span></font>跌幅居前。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　目前40只解禁股中，<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">一汽解放</font>的上半年归母净利润最多，公司上半年实现营收786亿元，同比增长14.19%；实现归属于上市公司股东的净利润32.69亿元，同比增长52.05%。据天眼查信息显示，一汽解放已于2021年9月9日成立一汽解放新能源汽车销售有限公司，注册资本2亿元。<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">金龙鱼、江苏索普、吉电股份、<span id="stock_sz002932"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002932/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">明德生物</a></span><span id="quote_sz002932"></span>、<span id="stock_sz002373"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002373/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">千方科技</a></span><span id="quote_sz002373"></span></font>的半年度归母净利润额居前。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　从净利润预计同比增长幅度来看，净利润同比增幅最大的是<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">江苏索普</font>，此外，<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold"><span id="stock_sh688368"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688368/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">晶丰明源</a></span><span id="quote_sh688368"></span>、济民医疗、<span id="stock_sh605338"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh605338/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">巴比食品</a></span><span id="quote_sh605338"></span>、<span id="stock_sh688315"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688315/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">诺禾致源</a></span><span id="quote_sh688315"></span>、明德生物</font>等9股的中报净利润同比均呈现翻倍增长。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">江苏索普</font>上半年归母净利润15.04亿元，同比增长17594.51%；营业收入39.18亿元，同比增长161.01%。近日，江苏索普发布三季报预告，预计2021年前三季度归母净利润为21.3亿元到21.8亿元，同比增加3901.44%到3995.37%。报告期公司主要产品醋酸及衍生品的价格持续高位，公司业绩大幅上升。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　下周解禁股中，<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold"><span id="stock_sz300427"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300427/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">红相股份</a></span><span id="quote_sz300427"></span>、深冷股份、蠡湖股份、优刻得-W、<span id="stock_sz000889"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000889/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">中嘉博创</a></span><span id="quote_sz000889"></span>、银宝山新</font>的上半年业绩下滑明显。</p>
