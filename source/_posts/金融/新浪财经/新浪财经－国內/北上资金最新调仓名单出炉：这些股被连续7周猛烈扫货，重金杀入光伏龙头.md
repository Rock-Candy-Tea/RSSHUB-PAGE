<div>   
<p>　　北上资金最新调仓名单出炉，这些股被连续7周猛烈扫货，重金杀入光伏龙头，锂电池龙头抛售最狠</p>
<p>　　本周，北上资金加仓幅度超50%的股票有49只。</p>
<p>　　本周沪指下跌0.06%，证券时报•数据宝统计显示，北上资金本周（06.07~06.11）累计成交5430亿元，成交净买入29.31亿元，已经连续9周持续买入A股筹码。其中，深股通合计净买入5.27亿元，沪股通合计净买入24.04亿元。</p>
<p>　　光伏政策带来新格局</p>
<p>　　北上资金重点加仓光伏龙头</p>
<p>　　本周共有38只个股上榜北上资金成交活跃榜榜单，所属行业来看，本周上榜的成交活跃股中，食品饮料行业最为集中，有7只个股上榜。成交金额最多的是<span id="stock_sh600519"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600519/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">贵州茅台</a></span><span id="quote_sh600519"></span>，本周累计成交128.64亿元；其次是<span id="stock_sz300750"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300750/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">宁德时代</a></span><span id="quote_sz300750"></span>，累计成交金额109.04亿元；成交金额较高的还有<span id="stock_sz000858"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000858/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">五粮液</a></span><span id="quote_sz000858"></span>、<span id="stock_sz300059"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300059/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">东方财富</a></span><span id="quote_sz300059"></span>等。</p>
<p>　　成交活跃榜个股中，6股获北上资金连续净买入，分别是<span id="stock_sh601919"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601919/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">中远海控</a></span><span id="quote_sh601919"></span>、东方财富、宁德时代等。东方财富已连续3日获净买入，期间累计成交金额46.24亿元，成交净买入13.35亿元，股价表现上，该股获北上资金连续净买入期间累计上涨1.62%。</p>
<p>　　以净买卖金额统计，本周上榜的个股中，共有22只股为净买入，净买入金额最多的是光伏龙头<span id="stock_sh601012"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601012/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">隆基股份</a></span><span id="quote_sh601012"></span>，本周净买入金额为30.08亿元，其次是<span id="stock_sz300274"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300274/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">阳光电源</a></span><span id="quote_sz300274"></span>、东方财富，净买入金额分别为9.60亿元、8.47亿元。净卖出个股中，净卖出金额最多的是锂电池龙头宁德时代，本周净卖出额为13.29亿元。</p>
<p>　　资金流向方面，有11只成交活跃股本周获主力资金净流入，净流入资金最多的个股是<span id="stock_sz002594"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002594/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">比亚迪</a></span><span id="quote_sz002594"></span>，主力资金净流入金额为25.20亿元，主力资金净流入居前的还有隆基股份、<span id="stock_sz000009"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000009/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">中国宝安</a></span><span id="quote_sz000009"></span>。</p>
<p>　　本周，北上资金买入最火热的概念莫过于光伏，隆基股份、阳光电源等龙头股明显净买入排行前列，主要因为光伏行业的多重利好。住房和城乡建设部等15部门发布《关于加强县城绿色低碳建设的意见》，其中提出构建县城绿色低碳能源体系，推广分布式光伏、智能光伏等清洁能源应用，提高生产生活用能清洁化水平，光伏下乡有望加速前行。另外，光伏行业协会也在本周召开座谈会，协会呼吁抵制对多晶硅、硅片产品的过度囤货、哄抬物价行为。市场分析人士指出，目前制约光伏的主要是硅料，一旦这个问题获得解决，光伏行业将迎来新的发展格局。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/325/w550h575/20210613/c314-krhvrxu2796160.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p>　　本周北上资金加仓股共716只，以持股数量增长幅度来看，加仓幅度超50%的股票有49只，超100%的股票有21只。</p>
<p>　　<span id="stock_sz002761"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002761/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">多喜爱</a></span><span id="quote_sz002761"></span>的加仓力度最大，最新持股数为62.23万股，与上周的持股数量相比，持股数量增长幅度为311.99%；紧随其后是<span id="stock_sh603876"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603876/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">鼎胜新材</a></span><span id="quote_sh603876"></span>，加仓幅度为293.42%；其他加仓幅度靠前的还有<span id="stock_sz300075"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300075/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">数字政通</a></span><span id="quote_sz300075"></span>、<span id="stock_sz300525"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300525/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">博思软件</a></span><span id="quote_sz300525"></span>、<span id="stock_sh688012"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688012/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">中微公司</a></span><span id="quote_sh688012"></span>、<span id="stock_sz300468"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300468/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">四方精创</a></span><span id="quote_sz300468"></span>、<span id="stock_sz002398"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002398/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">垒知集团</a></span><span id="quote_sz002398"></span>、<span id="stock_sz300256"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300256/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">星星科技</a></span><span id="quote_sz300256"></span>等。</p>
<p>　　从市场表现来看，本周北上资金加仓居前的20股中有14股上涨。<span id="stock_sz300287"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300287/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">飞利信</a></span><span id="quote_sz300287"></span>本周股价上涨32.02%，排名第一；涨幅较大的还有<span id="stock_sz002312"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002312/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">川发龙蟒</a></span><span id="quote_sz002312"></span>、数字政通、<span id="stock_sz300497"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300497/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">富祥药业</a></span><span id="quote_sz300497"></span>、四方精创等。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/393/w550h643/20210613/8702-krhvrxu2796236.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p>　　北上资金连续7周买入27股</p>
<p>　　北上资金连续4周买入138股，分行业来看，医药生物行业个股较多，有14只。其中，27股被北上资金连续7周加仓。</p>
<p>　　从持股比例变动情况来看，<span id="stock_sz300308"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300308/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">中际旭创</a></span><span id="quote_sz300308"></span>获加仓幅度最大，较7周前增持股数占流通股的比例为4.58%；其他连续加仓的还有<span id="stock_sh603939"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603939/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">益丰药房</a></span><span id="quote_sh603939"></span>、<span id="stock_sh600587"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600587/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">新华医疗</a></span><span id="quote_sh600587"></span>、<span id="stock_sh603639"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603639/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">海利尔</a></span><span id="quote_sh603639"></span>、<span id="stock_sz002706"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002706/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">良信股份</a></span><span id="quote_sz002706"></span>、<span id="stock_sh600742"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600742/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">一汽富维</a></span><span id="quote_sh600742"></span>等。</p>
<p>　　北上资金连续4周卖出122股。分行业来看，医药生物个股数量居前，有20只。从持股比例变动情况来看，与4周前相比，13股减仓比例超1%。<span id="stock_sz000338"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000338/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">潍柴动力</a></span><span id="quote_sz000338"></span>减仓幅度最大，相比4周前减仓了2.32%，其他减仓幅度较大的还有<span id="stock_sz300470"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300470/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">中密控股</a></span><span id="quote_sz300470"></span>、<span id="stock_sz002701"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002701/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">奥瑞金</a></span><span id="quote_sz002701"></span>、<span id="stock_sh603982"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603982/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">泉峰汽车</a></span><span id="quote_sh603982"></span>、<span id="stock_sz000825"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000825/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">太钢不锈</a></span><span id="quote_sz000825"></span>、<span id="stock_sh603279"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603279/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">景津环保</a></span><span id="quote_sh603279"></span>等。</p>
