<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">社保断缴会影响养老金的多少</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　养老保险</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“多缴多得，长缴多得”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　缴费年限越长</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　个人账户累计越多，养老金越高</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/117/w550h367/20220120/b633-e6f6b5ac7f7ebbe078546433beb1a946.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　如果您因为<font cms-style="font-L color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold color0">上学、离职</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　中断一段时间社保</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　之后继续参保</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　那么您前后<font cms-style="font-L"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">缴费的数额和年限时间</font></font>可以<font cms-style="font-L color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold color210">累计计算</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　但是如果您中断的时间越长</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　那么对应的累计缴费时间就会缩短</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　个人账户累计也会相对较少</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　最后<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">拿到手的养老金也会越少</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　如果断缴时间长</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　到法定退休年龄时<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">缴费年限不够</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　那您<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">领取养老金的时间就要相应延后</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一般情况下，您可选择</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">延长缴费时间至满足条件后</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">领取养老金</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　如果您在离职、</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　准备考研期间<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">不想中断</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　也可以选择</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">以灵活就业人员身份</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">参加职工养老保险</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">到了退休年龄，</font></p>
