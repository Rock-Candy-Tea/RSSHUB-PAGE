<p>　　原标题：哭笑不得！网逃人员找民警核实是否“网逃”……</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　人们常说“贼人心虚”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　不法分子干了见不得人的勾当后</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　是非常害怕被抓到</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　或者非常抗拒到派出所这些地方的</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　但是</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　近日，据江苏镇江新区警方介绍</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一名陕西男子</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　却将自己送进派出所</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　最终被警方轻松“拿下”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这是怎么回事呢？</p>
<!--video-list-->

<div class="video-2017" id="videoList0" style></div>

<!--/video-list-->
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　警方介绍</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　11月19日上午</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一名男子</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　走进镇江市公安局新区分局</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　丁岗派出所的办案大厅</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　随即很是“自信潇洒<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">”</font>地</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　把自己的身份证递给民警龚震</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">男子要求帮其查询</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">“自己是否是网络在逃人员”</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">同时胸有成竹地表示：</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">“我怎么可能会是网络逃犯呢？”</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211129s/251/w673h378/20211129/9a03-12acf8495fbc510c93ad8a3cfce04425.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　不查不知道，一查真可笑</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">男子真是一名网络逃犯</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　经询问才知</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　事发前</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　山西省太原市娄烦县公安机关民警</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　给该男子张某打电话</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　称他是网络逃犯要求其归案接受调查</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　张某接听后</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　认为一定是当地警方打错了电话</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　所以专程来派出所核实</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　警方告诉记者</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　经民警龚震核实发现</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　张某因涉嫌帮助信息网络犯罪</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　目前被山西太原警方列为网上逃犯</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　张某的真实身份水落石出后</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　当即被警方控制</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　目前</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　张某已被送往</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　山西省太原市第三看守所羁押</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“这波‘送人头’的行为</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　可是为警察蜀黍省了不少事情</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　当真让人笑哭！”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　派出所民警说</p>
