<p cms-style="font-L align-Center color210">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">制造假酒的手段有哪些？</font></font></p>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">加上茅台供应量始终处于供不应求的状态</font></p>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">那么 <font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">造假手法</font></font>都有哪些呢？</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">1</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">换标酒</font></font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="11" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20210826/704/w450h254/20210826/24c0-32c092009b0c8bcf0d3e71a4808e06d8.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">　　换标是常见造假方法的一种。造假者将瓶型相似的产品采用换标的方法来提升价值，一般是将高度酒标贴于低度酒瓶上。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">2</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">打孔酒</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="13" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20210826/84/w566h318/20210826/4b66-85b666ecbe74f6aa75c417011a427883.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">　　打孔是对跑酒、漏酒、少酒甚至是真瓶装假酒的酒品，在瓶身进行打孔注酒。而注入的都是劣质酒，千万不要饮用。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">3</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">拔头酒</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="15" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20210826/257/w715h342/20210826/d447-67b887bdd67417421a2cc2a6c686e749.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">　　拔头酒常见于近年生产的分段旋钮式瓶盖，造假者为向瓶内注入酒液故意避开瓶口封口处，直接从瓶盖封口下部将整个瓶盖拔下，达到不破坏原封口的目的。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">由于消费者对于茅台的偏爱</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">加上茅台供应量始终处于供不应求的状态</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="16" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20210826/218/w2048h1370/20210826/e5f0-de5e5009cef222a24a44075b8059a3f6.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一般而言 真酒销售越旺的地区</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">往往假酒销售</font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L align-Center color30"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color30">越</font></font></font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L align-Center color30"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color30">泛滥</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">那么 <font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">造假手法</font></font>都有哪些呢？</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="19" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20210826/704/w450h254/20210826/24c0-32c092009b0c8bcf0d3e71a4808e06d8.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center color210">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">我该如何维权？看这五步↓</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L">1、<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">保留购买凭证</font>，购买的酒、购买收据、发票或小票等做好备份。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L">2、<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">向购买酒所在的食药监局<a href="http://tousu.sina.com.cn/" target="_blank">投诉</a></font>，或向行政机关的工商局和社会团体的消费者协会投诉。</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="25" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20210826/220/w1098h722/20210826/2cb4-15c552724a581ba7a05b1d755fed1c2b.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　3、最终所购买的假酒被确认是在商家购买的假货时，工商局会责令商家罚款并完成退货退款，并要求商家按照购买价格三倍左右进行赔偿。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L">4、如果因喝了假酒对身体造成损害，可以通过消费者协会<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">向对方索要赔偿</font>。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L">5、协商不成，可以依法</font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">向法院提起诉讼</font><font cms-style="font-L">。</font></p>
