<div>   
<p>　　来源：国资小新</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　国资小新</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">5月10日1时56分，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">长征七号运载火箭</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">从海南文昌航天发射场点火起飞，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">成功把天舟四号货运飞船送入太空。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">中国空间站建造阶段首飞任务</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">取得圆满成功！</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/116/w550h366/20220510/fcb3-bcdfea33e2cb92201a0e44f61a50e90c.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　天舟四号货运飞船</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　采用自主快速交会对接模式，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　飞行约6.5小时后，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　与天和核心舱后向对接，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　转入三舱（船）组合体飞行状态。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/112/w550h362/20220510/aa51-e5d1f340dc1cd0a6397fe8aaafc4a164.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　天舟四号货运飞船</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　是<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">我国空间站建造阶段的首发航天器</font></font>，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　由中国<span id="stock_sz000901"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000901/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">航天科技</a></span><span id="quote_sz000901"></span>集团五院抓总研制，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　作用是为空间站</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　运输物资、补给燃料、下行废弃物，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　具备并网供电、应急通信等能力，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　确保空间站组合体在轨平稳运行。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/117/w550h367/20220510/9626-923f444d8c8ae97021b209fe26eb0654.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　本次任务中，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　天舟四号货运飞船</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　上行物资总重约<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">6.2吨</font></font>，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　将为<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">神舟十四号乘组</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在轨半年驻留、空间站组装建造、</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　开展材料科学、航天医学试验等空间站应用领域</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　提供物资保障。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/728/w550h978/20220510/9808-70480d7ed236e9ebdf84dffc2ebbcb98.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　今年，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　中国载人<span id="stock_sh603698"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603698/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">航天工程</a></span><span id="quote_sh603698"></span></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　还将执行<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">5次宇航发射</font></font>，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　把<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">2艘载人飞船</font></font>、</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">2个空间站实验舱</font></font>，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">1艘货运飞船</font></font>送入太空，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　完成我国空间站在轨建造。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/115/w550h365/20220510/2929-17dc6b779c5b2721955f11a99a067ba9.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　长征七号运载火箭</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　是我国新一代中型运载火箭，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　由航天科技集团一院研制，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　是一款两级半、全液氧煤油推进剂的火箭，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　可以把<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">1</font></font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">4吨有效载荷</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　送入中国空间站所在的近地轨道。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="5" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/483/w550h733/20220510/8784-d6393b6c40b9f7b48d8bc854f3c0380e.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　本次发射中，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　长征七号运载火箭</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　进行了<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">17项技术状态改进</font></font>，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　将<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">中国北斗卫星导航系统</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">升为卫星定位第一优先级</font></font>，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　火箭飞行可靠性提升至<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">0.9838</font></font>，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　达到世界先进水平。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="6" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/483/w550h733/20220510/9856-116189e6b7c66c59b2f7764de7a11a57.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　长征七号运载火箭试验队</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　优化测发流程，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　将测发天数压缩至<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">27天</font></font>，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">首次实现液氧、煤油推进剂并行加注</font></font>，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　发射前工作时长<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">缩短三分之一</font></font>，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　有效提高了发射场工作效率。</p><!-- news_keyword_pub,stock,sz000901&sh603698 -->
