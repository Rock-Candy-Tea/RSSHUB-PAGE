<p cms-style="font-L align-Center color210">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">二十四节气倒计时</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center color210">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">鸟巢上空现“立春SPRING”焰火 </font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　北京冬奥会开幕日</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　恰逢二十四节气中的</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　第一个节气——立春</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　倒计时表演以《立春》为题</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　孩子吹散蒲公英</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　白色的种子飞向空中</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　鸟巢上空出现“蒲公英”焰火</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　和中英文“立春”“SPRING”焰火</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　将一个天寒地冻的日子</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　命名为春天的起点</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　是因为中国人认为</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　极寒中常常孕育着新的生机</p>

<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="5" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/94/w550h344/20220205/b375-45eeb7cab87bffd2cb232a52a8efef9d.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center color210">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">国旗在普通中国人手中相传</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center color210">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">这个瞬间最直抵人心 </font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　国旗入场时</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　传递国旗的人</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　包括中国各行各业的代表、</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　国家功勋人员、56个民族的代表</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　他们都是普通的<span id="stock_sh603883"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603883/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">老百姓</a></span><span id="quote_sh603883"></span></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　用手手相传的方式</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　表达人民和国旗之间的情感</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="6" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/54/w550h304/20220205/08ac-8c1790a82a58bb8f54813dc27ac89831.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="7" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/117/w550h367/20220205/f1fd-0cf3bb9c849def3b08134c3dadc8f1ff.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center color210">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">升国旗奏国歌</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center color210">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">点赞伟大的祖国 </font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　经过普通中国人手手相传</p>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　现场响起嘹亮的国歌声</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　国旗在庄严的国歌声中升起</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　此刻，点赞伟大的祖国</p>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center color210">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">冰蓝色水墨从天而降</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center color210">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">幻化为“黄河之水天上来” </font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一滴冰蓝色的水墨从天而降</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　幻化为黄河之水</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　倾泻而下</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　画面的意境来自古老的诗句</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“黄河之水天上来”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这是中国人对母亲河的</p>
