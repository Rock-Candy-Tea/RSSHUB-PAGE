<div>   
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">没上骗子的当</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">还反手冻结了骗子53万元</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">近日，浙江嘉兴</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">一位老师的操作令人叫绝</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">他是怎么做到的？</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">骗子冒充领导加微信好友</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">体育老师靠细节识破</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">近日，浙江嘉兴的乐老师</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">收到了一条微信好友申请</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">对方自称是教育局的一位领导</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">并主动跟乐老师寒暄</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider2021715/512/w724h1388/20210715/db4f-44fd2bec5dc83b5f44b4b73ff60c65fe.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　然而，仔细一看</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">“领导”把他的名字都打错了</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">而且，从对方的言语中可以感觉到</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">对方似乎压根儿不知道他是体育老师</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">这些细节引起了乐老师的警觉</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">当天，经与学校及教育局核实</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">乐老师确认自己遇到了骗子</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">并立即报了警</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　警方建议他，可先与骗子周旋想办法要到尽可能多的银行账号</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">以帮助警方对其资金进行冻结</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">减少其他受害人的损失</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">反手冻结53万元！</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">他把骗子“骗了”</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　第二天，“领导”找到乐老师</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">要他帮忙办一件私事：</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">用他的卡作为中转</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">向“上级领导”转一笔钱</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">对方很快发来了一张</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">已向乐老师成功转账8万元的截图</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="7" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider2021715/500/w736h1364/20210715/511c-54512754ccf365147caef2c5181cf38d.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　乐老师一眼看出对方的转账截图假的</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">而且自己也没有收到任何钱</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">他将计就计答应下来，拖住骗子</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">并马上联系了警方👮♀️</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="8" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider2021715/674/w500h174/20210715/6d33-3199e6fc2c8a106d7b19376d3fbf865b.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="9" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider2021715/155/w510h445/20210715/77e8-dfe6c3287f1ce4a82e05e95d8827ee60.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">乐老师一直以各种借口</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">向对方表示转账失败</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　着急想要实施诈骗的“领导”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">在乐老师和警方的“套路”下</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0">陆续发来4个用于诈骗收账的银行账户</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0">警方随即对账户进行紧急冻结</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="10" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider2021715/419/w248h171/20210715/6c3e-ksmehzt4733362.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="11" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider2021715/387/w244h143/20210715/0f05-d762e8fffb6cb50a602fa055a06d9e1e.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="12" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider2021715/418/w243h175/20210715/06ea-ksmehzt4733389.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="13" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider2021715/376/w243h133/20210715/fc52-ksmehzt4733388.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　“我们反诈中心</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">紧急成立了资金查冻组</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">骗子每告诉一个诈骗账户</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">我们就紧急冻结一个账户”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">嘉兴市公安局南湖区分局</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">反诈中心教导员陈赛平说</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0">乐老师与民警一起</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0">用一上午时间</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0">成功冻结诈骗资金53万元</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">这波操作让网友直呼好家伙</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">“这次骗子遇上对手了”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">“太解气了”</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="16" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider2021715/420/w1080h140/20210715/804a-72caf71f2218b8244369a69c38d0e073.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="17" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider2021715/445/w1080h165/20210715/6404-a17c81b49e704e9b3a86d67bcf3189b0.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="18" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider2021715/437/w1080h157/20210715/6fe2-f21f269d594b6f6d6a0b3a99b92fe1d5.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="19" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider2021715/542/w1080h262/20210715/55f1-ksmehzt4733413.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">如何像乐老师一样机智？</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">记住警方这个小提示</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　经调查，这53万元来自6名受害者受害者信息仍在核实中</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">这些资金不日将依法返还</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">乐老师与骗子的斗智斗勇</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　帮他人挽回了损失警方特地赶到学校</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">送上一封感谢信</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="21" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider2021715/772/w1018h1354/20210715/35aa-ksmehzt4733438.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　警方也提醒大家⚠️</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">微信上遇到主动加好友的“领导”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">一定要保持警惕</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">最好核实对方身份</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">如果对方找各种理由让你帮忙转账</font></p>
