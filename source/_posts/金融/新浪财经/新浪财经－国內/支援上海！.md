<div>   
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　3月以来</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　受多重因素影响</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　上海本土疫情呈现快速发展态势</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　尤其是无症状感染者数量持续攀升</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　截至3月31日</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　累计报告感染者超过3万例</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/117/w550h367/20220402/e354-a48752b91f3d7348fc51e356fec0b6ec.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这个春天</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　上海遇到了困难</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一座以活力著称的城市</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　不得不慢下来</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　和病毒战斗</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/40/w550h290/20220402/55af-8ea44a9253dc4bffabb0f89fe6090545.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　无数的抗疫工作者</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　奋战在一线</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　不眠不休，加班加点</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">而全国各地的支援</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">也迅速赶来了</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">江苏来了！</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/gif_image/795/w512h283/20220402/845d-d5941c3ffe27cbed0cb8defa8a56470e.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　4月1日</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　第一批江苏省援沪医疗队</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　方舱队1026人出发</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　他们分别从南京、南通出发奔赴上海</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　医疗队到达上海后</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　将负责浦东临港方舱医院</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　部分病区的相关工作</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/gif_image/795/w512h283/20220402/93ee-abe3d35f6c83eeb48e7956c0b7d6b957.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　3月27日至4月1日</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　江苏先后共调派5000多名医务人员</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　支援上海疫情防控工作</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　江苏盐城各地</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　将新鲜<span id="stock_sz000061"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000061/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">农产品</a></span><span id="quote_sz000061"></span>和大米面粉发往上海</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　助力当地抗疫</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　仅3月31日</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　运往上海的蔬菜就有近400吨</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">浙江来了！</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="5" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/gif_image/33/w533h300/20220402/be90-804ea378619930758d10895b449c74b5.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　浙江大学医学院</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　附属第一医院、第二医院、邵逸夫医院等 </p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　7家省级医院和多家地方医院</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　先后派出数千名医护人员</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　全力支援上海</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　做好核酸采样</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　和集中隔离点的相关工作</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　浙江省嘉兴市嘉善县</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　杭州市富阳区永昌镇等地</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　紧急调集数万斤禽蛋果蔬等</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　运往上海</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="6" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/gif_image/33/w533h300/20220402/2bda-6dfe2ca9b72d4bc7c3f0eb84bf0a44bc.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">安徽来了！</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="7" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/gif_image/750/w480h270/20220402/2819-eff992864eb157d9933a7b527367f8f0.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　安徽阜阳、合肥等地</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　送上了大白菜、西兰花等</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　绿叶菜和其他农副产品</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　盛产中药材的安徽亳州</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　还捐出了4万盒疏风解毒胶囊</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　滁州驰援33万毫升血液</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　缓解上海疫情防控临床用血紧张</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　多地市医务人员、核酸检测队伍</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　相继抵达上海</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="8" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/gif_image/750/w480h270/20220402/2a56-127092474b476e66c42599a12c66941a.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">河南来了！</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="9" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/gif_image/750/w480h270/20220402/2317-c4a21b6438bda4ee3e056b62327dbe19.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　800吨各种优质新鲜蔬菜从郑州出发 </p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　驰援上海</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　捐赠抗疫一线 </p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　同时发出的</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　还有1000万只口罩</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="10" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/gif_image/750/w480h270/20220402/bd00-37e3747b9ad1a092620fef90add07f31.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　60余名司机志愿者</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　45辆大货车</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　装满河南人民的心意</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一路急驰</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">山东来了！</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　山东蔬菜瓜果种植面积最大的莘县</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　莘农之家联合社</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　每天为上海供应15吨新鲜蔬菜</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　莘县日当午瓜果蔬菜种植合作社</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　每天向上海发送1万份8斤<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">重</font>的“蔬菜包”</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="11" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/gif_image/29/w537h292/20220402/b62b-f83c8b2721d975e18bc5b0cd9e60e438.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这些发往上海的蔬菜</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　都经过了严格的抽检</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">四川来了！</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="12" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/gif_image/626/w400h226/20220402/d42a-7b572db81f6ec76b3f9184668a793348.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　散发着清香气息的莴笋</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　品质一流的莲花白、土豆、生菜、萝卜</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　还有都江堰特产大葱</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　以及时下当地最新鲜的春笋等</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　100多吨蔬菜</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　经过严格的安全检测后</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　被迅速装上爱心运输车辆</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　奔赴上海</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">云南来了！</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="13" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/gif_image/62/w554h308/20220402/25a7-2ed79ff69761443d2c7e5855eda4ba01.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　云南各地政府部门、企业、民众</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　积极筹集数百吨物资运往上</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　物资包括蔬菜、水果、坚果等</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　千里迢迢，驶向上海</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="14" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/gif_image/62/w554h308/20220402/0436-823a1a0b1d73666f804b5bc296ede523.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　还有湖北、贵州、江西……</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　都来了！</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">一方有难，八方支援</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">上海</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">一定会挺过去</font></p>
