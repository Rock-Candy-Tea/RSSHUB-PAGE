<div>   
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">新年开新局，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">1月1日，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">《中华人民共和国家庭教育促进法》</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">正式实施。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">也就是说，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">今后，</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">所有家长必须“依法带娃”了。</font></font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper">
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220104s/580/w690h690/20220104/3414-84c41ad8e720a293951131c3a5a009ff.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
</div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">从“家事”到“国事”</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">《中华人民共和国家庭教育促进法》</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">是继《教育法》、《义务教育法》</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">和新版《未成年人保护法》后，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">又一部教育领域的重磅法规，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">也是我国首次就家庭教育进行专门立法。</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">家庭教育是教育的开端，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">关乎未成年人的健康成长</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">和家庭的幸福安宁，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">但家庭教育具有私密性和个性化的特点，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">当“家事”上升到“国事”，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">该如何尊重家庭教育的自身规律？</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">立法的意图，</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">就是为“促进”家庭教育</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">而进行的“指引”和“赋能”。</font></font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220104s/179/w668h311/20220104/3f7e-6dfa3e4c994ee7c6d327b030a940c3e3.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">指引，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">是通过法律的方式告诉父母，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">如何当好一个合格的家长，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">引导家长</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">按照科学的方法和理念教育孩子。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">赋能，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">是指家长遇到困难和问题时，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">可以向公共体系获取相应的帮助，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">学习到相关的知识，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">然后来提升自己的教育能力。</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220104s/215/w668h347/20220104/c66f-3e69cf7241867ee69cafb64629923f02.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220104s/697/w666h31/20220104/f25d-55e2973067278ee20f6df7168550d546.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">相关部门如何介入？</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　既然立法是为“促进”家庭教育</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">而进行的“指引”和“赋能”，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">对于拒不履行家庭教育责任的家长来说</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">更多是纠偏，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">帮助家长更好地开展家庭教育。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">正因如此，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">该法在后续审议过程中，</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">删除了一审稿中的罚款、拘留等惩罚措施，</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">改为对家长的批评教育、劝诫制止、</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">予以训诫等措施。</font></font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper">
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220104s/690/w412h278/20220104/7429-c4cbff429d3cbc3c2521d9637a4b70ec.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
</div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">在四川泸州叙永县的</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">全国首个涉诉未成年人家庭教育指导工作站，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">刘某和张某夫妻俩正在接受家庭教育指导。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">双方的离婚纠纷，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">导致13岁的女儿产生厌学情绪，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">不间断逃学，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">但刘某和张某却放任不管，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">老师多次家访劝女孩返校也无效。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">于是，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">承办法官向这对夫妻发出</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">“责令接受家庭教育指导令”，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">指派两名家庭教育指导师对其进行指导。</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="5" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220104s/216/w667h349/20220104/ac7c-e7cc3b7211f058182ac2074d7ad79acc.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">2021年11月，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">全国法院系统首个设立的</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">涉诉未成年人家教指导工作站</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">在叙永县法院少审庭挂牌成立。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">如今，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">由叙永县团委、妇联、教体局等联合推荐的</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">14名家教指导员受聘领证上岗，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">他们分别是法官、学校心理咨询老师、</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">专业社工以及民政、妇联</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">和关工委等部门的工作人员，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">这张由家庭、学校、社会、司法四位一体</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">为未成年人撑起的“保护网”开始发挥作用。</font></font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="6" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220104s/217/w670h347/20220104/cadd-b53dd5ad10c21ca2deb6ecf87a463963.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="7" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220104s/702/w671h31/20220104/baf8-17d44b88ec2ea0a3be5b33bf0e8326fe.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="8" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220104s/698/w669h29/20220104/f621-ac272afe6e89938da2af6a10cf86d02c.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">新法与“双减”</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">新实施的《家庭教育促进法》的</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">另一大亮点是强调立德树人<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">。</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">而要想发挥家庭教育</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">在孩子品德教育</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">和人格养成方面的优势，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">还有一个问题无法回避，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">那就是“双减”。</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="9" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220104s/218/w671h347/20220104/5f9f-12129f2d768ecac684473e5f335df954.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">2021年7月，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">中办、国办印发</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">《关于进一步减轻义务教育阶段学生作业负担和校外培训负担的意见》，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">在“双减”背景下</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">出台的《家庭教育促进法》</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">自然肩负起落实“双减”的重任。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">《家庭教育促进法》规定：</font></font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="10" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220104s/161/w670h291/20220104/3858-f57cad0cc3828f25ea17681ceee52ca7.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">严管校外培训机构，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">提升学校教学质量，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">都有助于推进“双减”政策，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">但从某种意义上讲，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">家长才是真正让政策落地的关键。</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">专家表示，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">“双减”和《家庭教育促进法》的宗旨一致，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">都是为了立德树人。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">在这样的背景下，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">学校和家庭应协同确立孩子的教育价值观，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">家庭教育要配合学校，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">减少“短视化”、“功利化”的目标，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">把重心放在“育人”上；</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">学校也要对家长进行正确引导。</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="11" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220104s/218/w671h347/20220104/ea2b-3fb9ad002b5ec48d76b5cc97d029791c.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
