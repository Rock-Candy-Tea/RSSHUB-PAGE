<div>   
<p cms-style="font-L color0">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold color0">北上资金连续4周买入106股，医药生物行业个股较多，有10只。其中，21股被北上资金连续7周加仓。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">证券时报•统计，本周沪指上涨0.15%，北上资金一周累计成交6224.80亿元，创近5个月来新高。</font>成交净买入83.19亿元，其中，深股通合计净买入53.32亿元，沪股通合计净买入29.88亿元。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">有色金属行业获重点加仓</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">金融板块遭抛售</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　统计显示，本周北上资金持股量环比增加的有20个行业。<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">有色金属行业环比增幅最大，北上资金最新持有该行业43.75亿股，相比上周末增长5.65 %；环比增幅居前的有轻工制造、纺织服装等行业。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　8个申万行业遭北上资金减仓，综合行业减仓最为明显，减仓幅度为4.64%；其他减仓较多的行业还包括银行、非银金融等，金融板块本周成为北上资金重点抛售的方向。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　行业来看，28个申万一级行业中，本周上涨的有17个，新能源汽车相关的资源板块涨幅居前。有色金属、电气设备、国防军工、化工、汽车的涨幅位列前五，跌幅居前的行业为医药生物、休闲服务、食品饮料等。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd2021711s/141/w541h400/20210711/7abf-ksmehzt2217935.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">新能源汽车资源股大热获加仓</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">白酒股重新得宠</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">本周共有35只个股登上成交活跃榜，所属行业来看，本周上榜的成交活跃股中，电气设备、电子等行业最为集中，均有6只个股上榜。</font>成交金额最多的是<span id="stock_sz300750"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300750/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">宁德时代</a></span><span id="quote_sz300750"></span>，本周累计成交130.96亿元；其次是<span id="stock_sh601012"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601012/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">隆基股份</a></span><span id="quote_sh601012"></span>，累计成交金额100.78亿元；成交金额较高的还有<span id="stock_sz000858"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000858/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">五粮液</a></span><span id="quote_sz000858"></span>、<span id="stock_sh600519"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600519/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">贵州茅台</a></span><span id="quote_sh600519"></span>等。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L">以净买卖金额统计，本周上榜的个股中，共有20只股为净买入。净买入金额最多的是五粮液，本周净买入金额为16.78亿元；其次是<span id="stock_sh600309"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600309/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">万华化学</a></span><span id="quote_sh600309"></span>、<span id="stock_sh600111"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600111/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">北方稀土</a></span><span id="quote_sh600111"></span>，净买入金额分别为13.98亿元、7.22亿元。</font>净卖出个股中，净卖出金额最多的是<span id="stock_sz300274"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300274/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">阳光电源</a></span><span id="quote_sz300274"></span>，本周净卖出额为9.20亿元。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L">资金流向方面，有15只成交活跃股本周获主力资金净流入。净流入资金最多的个股是北方稀土，主力资金净流入金额为42.77亿元，主力资金净流入居前的还有<span id="stock_sz002460"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002460/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">赣锋锂业</a></span><span id="quote_sz002460"></span>、隆基股份。</font>主力资金净流出的个股有20只，净流出资金最多的是<span id="stock_sh601919"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601919/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">中远海控</a></span><span id="quote_sh601919"></span>，净流出资金为69.89亿元。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　受疫情、供应、货币放水等多方面因素影响，全球化工、资源行业的供需矛盾越来越尖锐，各种原材料大幅提价且很难看到价格回落的迹象，放大了化工、资源股业绩上涨的空间。A股已有85家化工类上市公司预告了2021年上半年业绩，业绩向好股的比率高达86%。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　此外，“碳中和”成为全球共识的背景下，电动车的需求、销量也在快速提升，乘联会发布最新数据显示，6月我<span id="stock_sh600617"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600617/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">国新能源</a></span><span id="quote_sh600617"></span>乘用车零售销量达到22.3万辆，同比增长169.9%，环比5月增长19.2%。这些积极数据，直接带动了上游资源需求，比如锂、钴、稀土等价格持续上涨。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　对市场情报十足敏感的北上资金，本周快速抢筹了相关个股，万华化学、北方稀土、赣锋锂业、<span id="stock_sz300014"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300014/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">亿纬锂能</a></span><span id="quote_sz300014"></span>等股名列北上资金加仓股前八，完全抢了重新得宠的白酒股的风头。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　梳理发现，从近期净买入情况看，有3股已被北上资金连续净买入，五粮液已连续5日获净买入，期间累计成交金额94.73亿元，成交净买入16.78亿元，股价表现上，该股获北上资金连续净买入期间累计下跌5.65%。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　五粮液本周累计净买入16.78亿元，成为北上资金加仓最多的个股，贵州茅台、<span id="stock_sh600809"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600809/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">山西汾酒</a></span><span id="quote_sh600809"></span>也获得了不同程度的加仓，一改过去数周北上资金连续大幅抛售白酒股的势头。作为A股压舱底的白酒股重新得宠，这也许是聪明资金转变对A股态度的重要表态，不过还需要时间来观察。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd2021711s/462/w645h617/20210711/e045-ksmehzt2217936.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">本周北上资金加仓股共792只，以持股数量增长幅度来看，加仓幅度超50%的股票有94只，超100%的股票有31只。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<span id="stock_sh600075"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600075/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">新疆天业</a></span><span id="quote_sh600075"></span>的加仓力度最大，最新持股数为198.71万股，与上周的持股数量相比，持股数量增长幅度为896.08%；紧随其后是<span id="stock_sz300080"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300080/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">易成新能</a></span><span id="quote_sz300080"></span>，加仓幅度为658.75%；其他加仓幅度靠前的还有<span id="stock_sz300087"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300087/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">荃银高科</a></span><span id="quote_sz300087"></span>、<span id="stock_sh603919"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603919/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">金徽酒</a></span><span id="quote_sh603919"></span>、<span id="stock_sz300696"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300696/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">爱乐达</a></span><span id="quote_sz300696"></span>、<span id="stock_sz300891"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300891/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">惠云钛业</a></span><span id="quote_sz300891"></span>、<span id="stock_sz300762"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300762/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">上海瀚讯</a></span><span id="quote_sz300762"></span>、<span id="stock_sh600966"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600966/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">博汇纸业</a></span><span id="quote_sh600966"></span>等。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　从市场表现来看，本周北上资金加仓居前的20股有15股上涨。<span id="stock_sz002108"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002108/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">沧州明珠</a></span><span id="quote_sz002108"></span>本周股价上涨38.13%，排名第一；涨幅较大的还有<span id="stock_sz002312"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002312/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">川发龙蟒</a></span><span id="quote_sz002312"></span>、荃银高科、<span id="stock_sh600316"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600316/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">洪都航空</a></span><span id="quote_sh600316"></span>、爱乐达等。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　北上资金减仓幅度超50%的有17股，<span id="stock_sz002111"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002111/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">威海广泰</a></span><span id="quote_sz002111"></span>、<span id="stock_sz002208"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002208/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">合肥城建</a></span><span id="quote_sz002208"></span>、<span id="stock_sz002658"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002658/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">雪迪龙</a></span><span id="quote_sz002658"></span>、<span id="stock_sz003009"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz003009/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">中天火箭</a></span><span id="quote_sz003009"></span>等股的减仓力度靠前。</p>

<p cms-style="font-L">　　从持股比例变动情况来看，<span id="stock_sh603195"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603195/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">公牛集团</a></span><span id="quote_sh603195"></span>获加仓幅度最大，较7周前增持股数占流通股的比例为4.39%；其他连续加仓的还有<span id="stock_sz300146"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300146/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">汤臣倍健</a></span><span id="quote_sz300146"></span>、<span id="stock_sh603605"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603605/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">珀莱雅</a></span><span id="quote_sh603605"></span>、<span id="stock_sz002410"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002410/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">广联达</a></span><span id="quote_sz002410"></span>、<span id="stock_sh601117"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601117/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">中国化学</a></span><span id="quote_sh601117"></span>等。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L">北上资金连续4周卖出206股。分行业来看，医药生物个股数量居前，有20只。从持股比例变动情况来看，与4周前相比，24股减仓比例超1%。</font><span id="stock_sz002015"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002015/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">协鑫能科</a></span><span id="quote_sz002015"></span>减仓幅度最大，相比4周前减仓了3.60%，其他减仓幅度较大的还有<span id="stock_sh600201"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600201/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">生物股份</a></span><span id="quote_sh600201"></span>、<span id="stock_sz300673"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300673/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">佩蒂股份</a></span><span id="quote_sz300673"></span>、<span id="stock_sh600171"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600171/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">上海贝岭</a></span><span id="quote_sh600171"></span>等。</p>
