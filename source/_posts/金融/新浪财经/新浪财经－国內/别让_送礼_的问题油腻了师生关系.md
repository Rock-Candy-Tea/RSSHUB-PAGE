<p>　　原标题：别让“送礼”的问题油腻了师生关系</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">今天几号？</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">9月6号了……</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">各位家长朋友们</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">再过四天</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">一年一度的教师节就到了</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">又要面对</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">宇宙终极命题——</font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">教师节</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">到底送不送礼？</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/168/w550h418/20210906/7620-554504c2062701ea7a9dc85fb617e25a.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">今年教育领域动作多</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">反内卷、反焦虑、反不必要的消耗</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">怎么给人民教师过节</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">也有了一些微妙的新变化</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/700/w540h960/20210906/a2aa-bf3ddfab21222ecad9a9debd45774ae8.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">近日</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">有网民反映</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">深圳某初中家委会提议为老师送礼</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">教育局马上调查并通报</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/98/w550h348/20210906/88a9-ab24888a45ba5008461b405090008623.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">其实家委会提议送给每个任课老师的礼物</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">只是养生杯1个和鲜花1束</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/635/w300h335/20210906/202e-6efff8be8f83e24a3a4e622eb22f4003.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">对这份善意与敬意表达感谢后</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">教育局重申教师不收礼</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">家委会退回了所有款项</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">这是个很有趣的事儿</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">一个保温杯一束鲜花</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">能腐蚀得了哪个人民教师</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">但教育局依然如此坚持</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">就是因为有些事情</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="5" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/675/w550h125/20210906/89d5-8dc5d7533d1b56c5dd5953e34fada4e0.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">正如网友直言：</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="6" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/47/w550h297/20210906/b758-3163f5f7dd8ca3cbf2fdab18054bdc92.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">有时候</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">我们过分迷信</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">“礼多人不怪”、“吃人嘴短、拿人手软”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">一边抱怨着不送不行</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">一边挤破了头往进送</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">其实这种心态</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">反而会让收礼的人腰杆子更硬</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="7" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/710/w550h160/20210906/63e1-3101aebf315aab49a9934d678690ad85.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">（2014年，黑龙江省依兰县一女教师因送礼问题谩骂学生被处分）</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">起初收得心虚</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">后来你不送还不得劲</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">会想方设法“拿”人一把</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">还有很多人并不想送礼</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">但总怕自己落于人后</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">你也随大流，我也随大流，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">久而久之</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">“大流”就变成了“恶流”</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">潜规则被搬上了台面</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">还有一些老师称</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">拒绝收礼对他们来说</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">也是一件非常头疼的事儿</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">几十个学生家长挨个拒绝一遍</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">会耗费巨大的时间精力</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">何况有些家长直接快递</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">或者小朋友眼巴巴地捧着礼物</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">根本连拒绝的余地都没有</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">真的是送不送让家长为难</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">收不收让老师心烦</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">恰逢今年的教师节马上到来</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">不如我们一起改变现状</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="8" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/738/w550h188/20210906/5333-d7c33373049e5346b952dfb116db0a06.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">老师把话挑明了“不收”！</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">家长放大胆子：别送！</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">一起过一个清淡消停节</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">别让“送礼”的问题</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">油腻了师生关系</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">你怎么看教师节送不送礼</font></font></p>
