<p>　　原标题：全国碳市场开市月成交3.55亿元</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/transform/300/w550h550/20210819/9528-e36814821976f82758ff68259a629390.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
