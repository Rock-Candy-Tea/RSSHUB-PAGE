<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">原标题：国家公园如何对公众开放？回应来了！</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">最近，国务院分别批复同意设立</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">三江源、大熊猫、东北虎豹、</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">海南热带雨林、武夷山等</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">第一批国家公园</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">国家公园如何对公众开放？</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">是否收费？</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">往下看，一同了解！</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">国家公园与传统自然保护区</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">有何区别？</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center"><!--article_adlist[<img src="//n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211022s/118/w60h58/20211022/25b5-242422b83956d96599d78b6d980dd8c0.png" style="max-width:640px" cms-width="30" cms-height="29"/>]article_adlist--></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">按照生态价值和保护强度</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">我国自然保护地体系</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">分为国家公园、</font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">自然保护区、</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">自然公园三个等级</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">其中，国家公园</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">是保护强度、</font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">保护等级最高的</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211022s/638/w408h230/20211022/754a-09a4b65ed31745399606da8b79d25279.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">从生态价值角度来讲</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">国家公园保护的</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">是具有国家代表性的</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">自然生态系统</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">具有全球价值和国家象征</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">而从保护强度来讲</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">国家公园的保护范围更大</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">生态过程更完整，管理层级更高</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211022s/638/w408h230/20211022/3ff1-5b428e16bc483993b81ca66caaf1fd92.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">国家公园对公众开放吗？</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center"><!--article_adlist[<img src="//n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211022s/118/w60h58/20211022/25b5-242422b83956d96599d78b6d980dd8c0.png" style="max-width:640px" cms-width="30" cms-height="29"/>]article_adlist--></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">国家林草局有关负责人表示</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">国家公园是人民的公园</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">坚持全民公益性</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">是国家公园的重要理念之一</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">国家公园内</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">青山常在、</font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">绿水长流、空气新鲜</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">是最好的自然课堂</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">也是最有吸引力的</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">生态体验胜地</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211022s/638/w408h230/20211022/2507-c508096e405dc71a830322425adc5048.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">国家公园</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">分为</font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">核心保护区和一般控制区</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">两个管控区</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">核心保护区只对科研开放</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">一般控制区可以开放旅游</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">满足公众进入自然、</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">亲近自然的要求</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">适当区域建设自然教育基地、</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">科普馆、野外观察点、露营区、</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">登山步道等</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">开展自然教育、自然游憩</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">和生态体验</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="5" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211022s/520/w849h471/20211022/22ce-922c720752fc40e025e95a0e9022225c.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L"><!--article_adlist[<img src="//n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211022s/107/w91h16/20211022/9aac-6a9779351ca14fa9241e9fe9dcc5fd35.png" style="max-width:640px" cms-width="80" cms-height="14.0625"/>]article_adlist--></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　国家林草局副局长李春良表示：要处理好保护与发展的关系，营造一个人与自然和谐共生的场景。我们要做到的就是生态保护和生态游憩、生态体验相得益彰。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">国家公园收费不？</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center"><!--article_adlist[<img src="//n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211022s/118/w60h58/20211022/25b5-242422b83956d96599d78b6d980dd8c0.png" style="max-width:640px" cms-width="30" cms-height="29"/>]article_adlist--></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">国家林草局有关负责人表示</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">原则上，公益性项目免费</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">经营性项目实行特许经营，少收门票</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="7" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211022s/638/w408h230/20211022/a41d-4864f246ea8420691102d4712a806006.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">在国家公园开展游憩体验</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">与大众旅游不同</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">国家公园</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">将根据环境容量等</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">合理确定到访游客承载数量</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">建立门票预约制度</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">科学测算运营成本</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">体现公益属性</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">严格控制门票等经营服务类价格</font></p>
