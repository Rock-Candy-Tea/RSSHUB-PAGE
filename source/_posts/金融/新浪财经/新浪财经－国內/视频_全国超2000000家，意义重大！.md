<p>　　原标题：全国超2000000家，意义重大！</p>

<p cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">此时是春耕备耕时节</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">各地的农民合作社</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">正忙着为农业生产作准备</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　2020年7月</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　习近平总书记在吉林考察时</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　高度评价了一家农民合作社</p>
<p cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">总书记为什么高度评价</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">这家农民合作社？</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">它是如何让黑土地变“活”</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">农民变富的？</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd2021329s/626/w400h226/20210329/40dc-knaqvqn1734344.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在吉林省四平市梨树县</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　年轻人大多选择外出务工</p>
<p cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">农忙时很多农民家里缺少人手</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　此外</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　很多农民曾采用</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　效率低下的传统耕种方式</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　并未使用先进的大型农机具</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd2021329s/626/w400h226/20210329/d6c2-knaqvqn1734343.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　2011年</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　农民卢伟</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　创办了农民专业合作社</p>
<p cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">用租赁、托管、带地入社的形式</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">把社员们的土地整合在一起</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">并雇专人来干农活</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">土地丰收之后再付给社员报酬</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd2021329s/626/w400h226/20210329/1d91-knaqvqn1734419.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">在解决<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">农民使用大型农机</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">不方便的问题上</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">农民专业合作社也花了一番心思</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">因为最初加入的农民不多</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">土地不集中连片</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">大型农机具派不上用场</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd2021329s/626/w400h226/20210329/eb90-knaqvqn1734423.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　于是</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　农民专业合作社就通过</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　采用玉米秸秆覆盖</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　还田免耕播种技术、<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">测土配方施肥等方式</font></p>
<p cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">提高粮食的产量</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">增加农民的收益</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">并以此激发农民加入的积极性</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">随着农民的陆续加入</font></p>
<p cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">土地逐渐连成了片</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">大型农机具就有了用武之地</font></font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd2021329s/626/w400h226/20210329/d3f6-knaqvqn1734509.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　如<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">今</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">梨树县共有</font><font cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">3244家农民专业合作社</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">适度规模经营面积</font><font cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">达150万亩</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">占全县耕地面积的38%</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　截至2020年11月</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　全国农民合作社达到<font cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">224.1万家</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">辐射带动近一半的农户</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">农民专业合作社</font></p>
<p cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">激活了农村资源要素</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">服务了农业发展</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">让众多农民从中</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">获得实实在在的好处</font></p>
<p cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">不断推动中国农业现代化</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">走得稳、迈得开</font></font></font></p>
