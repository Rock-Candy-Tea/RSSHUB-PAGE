<p cms-style="font-L">　　原标题：北向资金加仓“锂电茅”超17亿元！7800亿市值白酒龙头遭减仓超20亿元（附名单）</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　Choice数据显示，本周北向资金累计净流出88.27亿元，其中沪股通资金净流出17.05亿元，深股通资金净流出71.23亿元。本周北向资金对630只股票的持股数增加，对942只股票进行了减仓，其中对<span id="stock_sz300750"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300750/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">宁德时代</a></span><span id="quote_sz300750"></span>加仓金额最大，对<span id="stock_sz000858"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000858/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">五粮液</a></span><span id="quote_sz000858"></span>减仓金额最大。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">对39股<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">增</font></font>持超1000万股</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　本周北向资金仅交易4天，合计净流出近90亿元。具体来看，Choice数据显示，9月13日净流出38.24亿元，9月14日净流出41.28亿元，9月15日净流入21.40亿元，9月16日净流出30.14亿元。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　Choice数据显示，截至9月17日，北向资金持仓市值为2.53万亿元，本周持仓市值减少742亿元。本周北向资金对630股的持股数增加，对其中39股的持股增加数超过1000万股，持股增加数最多的<span id="stock_sh601872"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601872/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">招商轮船</a></span><span id="quote_sh601872"></span>增加了4671.49万股，对<span id="stock_sz300059"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300059/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">东方财富</a></span><span id="quote_sz300059"></span>、<span id="stock_sh601099"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601099/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">太平洋</a></span><span id="quote_sh601099"></span>持股分别增加4605.31万股、4083.76万股。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　从市场表现情况来看，本周北向资金持股增加数居前十股中仅有招商轮船、<span id="stock_sh600795"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600795/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">国电电力</a></span><span id="quote_sh600795"></span>上涨，涨幅分别为5.75%、3.50%；其他8只股票本周均下跌，<span id="stock_sh600010"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600010/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">包钢股份</a></span><span id="quote_sh600010"></span>跌幅最大，本周累计下跌9.87%。</p>

<p cms-style="font-L">　　从持仓市值变动情况来看，Choice数据显示，本周北向资金对20只股票的持仓市值增加超过5亿元，对<span id="stock_sz300760"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300760/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">迈瑞医疗</a></span><span id="quote_sz300760"></span>、<span id="stock_sh600406"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600406/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">国电南瑞</a></span><span id="quote_sh600406"></span>、<span id="stock_sh603259"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603259/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">药明康德</a></span><span id="quote_sh603259"></span>持仓市值增加金额居前，持仓市值分别增加37.25亿元、31.55亿元、30.04亿元。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　本周北向资金持仓市值增加额居前十的股票中，除<span id="stock_sz300347"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300347/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">泰格医药</a></span><span id="quote_sz300347"></span>、<span id="stock_sz300015"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300015/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">爱尔眼科</a></span><span id="quote_sz300015"></span>被北向资金分别减仓54.93万股、1205.00万股外，其他股票均被北向资金加仓，其中对国电南瑞、药明康德、<span id="stock_sh601615"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601615/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">明阳智能</a></span><span id="quote_sh601615"></span>的持股增加数超1000万股。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　从市场表现上来看，本周北向资金持仓市值增加额居前十的股票均上涨，<span id="stock_sh603659"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603659/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">璞泰来</a></span><span id="quote_sh603659"></span>涨幅最大，本周累计上涨20.28%。</p>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">加仓宁德时代超17亿元</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　从成交均价和加仓股数来计算，宁德时代为本周北向资金增持金额最大的股票，本周北向资金增持宁德时代335.96万股，合计加仓金额达17.50亿元。从明细数据来看，本周北向资金有2个交易日加仓宁德时代，其中9月14日加仓金额超过13亿元。9月份以来，北向资金显著加仓宁德时代，累计增持宁德时代79.41亿元。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　Choice数据显示，本周宁德时代累计上涨0.18%，截至9月17日，北向资金持有宁德时代1.64亿股，宁德时代股价为503元/股，总市值为11714亿元。</p>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">74股本周遭减仓超1000万股 </font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　在北向资金本周减仓近90亿元的情况下，本周有超900只股票被北向资金减仓。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　Choice数据显示，本周北向资金共计减仓942只股票，对其中74只股票的减仓股数超过1000万股。减仓股数最多的<span id="stock_sz000725"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000725/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">京东方A</a></span><span id="quote_sz000725"></span>被减仓1.08亿股，<span id="stock_sh603993"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603993/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">洛阳钼业</a></span><span id="quote_sh603993"></span>、<span id="stock_sz000002"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000002/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">万科A</a></span><span id="quote_sz000002"></span>被分别减仓9108.80万股、5570.34万股。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　从市场表现上来看，本周北向资金减仓股数居前十股中仅有<span id="stock_sh601600"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601600/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">中国铝业</a></span><span id="quote_sh601600"></span>上涨，累计涨幅为2.58%；其他股票均下跌，<span id="stock_sh600219"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600219/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">南山铝业</a></span><span id="quote_sh600219"></span>跌幅最大，累计周跌幅为9.77%。</p>

<p cms-style="font-L">　　从持仓市值变动情况来看，Choice数据显示，本周北向资金对46只股票的持仓市值减少超过5亿元，对<span id="stock_sz000333"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000333/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">美的集团</a></span><span id="quote_sz000333"></span>、<span id="stock_sz000001"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000001/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">平安银行</a></span><span id="quote_sz000001"></span>、五粮液持仓市值减少金额居前，持仓市值分别减少46.96亿元、46.85亿元、45.40亿元。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　本周持仓市值减少额居前十股除美的集团、<span id="stock_sh600036"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600036/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">招商银行</a></span><span id="quote_sh600036"></span>被北向资金分别加仓874.35万股、1688.08万股外，其他股均被北向资金减仓，其中平安银行、<span id="stock_sh601318"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601318/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">中国平安</a></span><span id="quote_sh601318"></span>、<span id="stock_sz000651"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000651/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">格力电器</a></span><span id="quote_sz000651"></span>、<span id="stock_sz002271"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002271/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">东方雨虹</a></span><span id="quote_sz002271"></span>、<span id="stock_sh600031"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600031/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">三一重工</a></span><span id="quote_sh600031"></span>被北向资金减仓超1000万股。可以看到，本周多只白马股遭遇北向资金减持。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　从市场表现上来看，本周北向资金持仓市值减少额居前十股均下跌，<span id="stock_sh600438"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600438/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">通威股份</a></span><span id="quote_sh600438"></span>跌幅最大，本周累计下跌15.74%。</p>
