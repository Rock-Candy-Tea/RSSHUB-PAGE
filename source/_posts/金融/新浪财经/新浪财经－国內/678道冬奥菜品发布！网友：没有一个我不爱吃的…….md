<p>　　原标题：678道冬奥菜品发布！网友：没有一个我不爱吃的……</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">西湖牛肉羹、玛格丽特披萨</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">日式照烧鸡排、韩式泡菜炒五花肉</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">北京烤鸭、葱爆羊肉</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">三鲜水饺、四喜丸子</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">宫保鸡丁、<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">扒牛里脊</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">马莱烤鸡和冬阴功汤</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">……</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　13日</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">2022年冬奥会</font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">运动员菜单</font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">发布</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">来自世界各地的</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">678道特色菜品上榜</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211115s/648/w681h2367/20211115/194b-1ac2472e26b91010e52e1c0d74391dbb.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　此次设置了<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">世界餐台<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">、亚洲餐台</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">中餐餐台、明档、清真餐台</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">披萨与意大利面餐台</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">沙拉台和调味品台</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">鲜果台、面包和甜品台</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　等12种餐台</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　比赛期间</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">每天将有大约200道菜品</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">供各国运动员食用</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">并以每8天为一个周期进行轮换</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　以满足不同的口味和饮食需求</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211115s/739/w1080h459/20211115/e4de-3bcd10a46df29d4e1153ba516760e10a.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这份菜单</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　邀请运动、营养和餐饮等</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　多个专家团队</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　针对食材成分、菜品规格</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　和制作流程进行了充分研究</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　菜品不但丰盛</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　还很用心</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　北京2022冬奥会延庆村总厨杨辰介绍</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">“冬奥会运动员菜品里不能有胡椒</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">由于胡椒属于食源性兴奋剂</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">所以不会出现</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在冬奥会运动员的菜单里</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　此外</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　香叶等48种厨房常见原料</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　也不能出现”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　网友评论：</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">“太馋了……”</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">“羡慕的泪水从嘴角流了下来”</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211115s/591/w534h57/20211115/2def-cb56a8504d582fce52b1fb35fde482b0.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211115s/584/w515h69/20211115/6c53-9f3a1ac3e512f2c7b7d00cecc4d23697.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211115s/561/w507h54/20211115/804c-df1e28c4e6299de7c85f5cb6109b79ca.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="5" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211115s/586/w526h60/20211115/e0ce-9e5c7c911225241dc451b9b60b4f0864.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="6" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211115s/590/w530h60/20211115/0351-607fee5888e881c729100d2d555b13e8.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">“同款菜单get”</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">“以后不知道吃啥，就按这菜单做”</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="7" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211115s/613/w555h58/20211115/189a-e55af160a9b1a30ed7a691e62456c18c.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="8" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211115s/572/w515h57/20211115/b0e0-4ebe913feef5a4e357c378b01af39386.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　而在疫情防控的背景下</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　北京冬奥组委也是毫不懈怠</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　组委会在运动员餐厅出入口</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　取餐流线、餐盘回收等关键点位</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　都摆放口罩、手套、酒精棉片</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　和免洗手消毒剂等物品</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　运动员进门后</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　设有智能保温取餐柜</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　实现人员分流就餐</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　此外</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　餐厅每层设有空间除菌仪</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　人机分离，减少人员接触</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="9" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211115s/739/w1080h459/20211115/e4de-3bcd10a46df29d4e1153ba516760e10a.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　食材安全可靠</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　服务细致周到</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　为了做好冬奥会这顿“大锅菜”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“双奥之城”北京诚意满满</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　信心十足</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　对于世界各国运动员而言</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　吃下去的是安心</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　感受到的是用心</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这浓浓的人情味儿</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　正是开放包容</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　严谨细致的“中国味道”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">祝愿每位参赛运动员都能吃好喝好</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">食堂里天天“光盘”</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">赛场上取得优异成绩</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">加油，咱们北京冬奥会见！</font></font></p>
