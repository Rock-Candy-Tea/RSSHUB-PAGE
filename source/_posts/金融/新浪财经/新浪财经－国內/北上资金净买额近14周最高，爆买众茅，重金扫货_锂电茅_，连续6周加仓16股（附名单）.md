<p class="ori_titlesource">　　原标题：北上资金净买额近14周最高，爆买众茅，重金扫货“锂电茅”，连续6周加仓16股（附名单） </p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　北上资金本周净买入额创近14周最高。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　截至9月3日，A股市场本周5个交易日成交额依然都在万亿以上，本周利好消息不断，工业母机、宣布设立北京证券交易所等，但三大指数涨跌不一，上证指数本周上涨1.69%，创业板指和深证成指均下跌。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　北上资金本周合计净买入279.27亿元，为近14周最高水平。其中沪股通本周净买入175.42亿元，深股通净买入103.85亿元。北上资金近期加仓意愿较为明显，近10个交易日呈连续净买入状态。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　相关人士对近期的盘面表示，板块轮动较快，市场维持高低板块之间的拉锯战。低位北交所概念包含了券商板块、参股新三板、创投概念、科技园区等方向，对盘面吸金效应比较明显。跷跷板效应下高位板块总体承压，北交所横空出世，光伏、风电、特高压均有所表现，高位大分歧下，白酒、医药等超跌的大消费，以及近期一直低迷的半导体再次小幅修复，后续持续性仍有待观察。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20210905/276/w738h338/20210905/77b6-8228aff6e07b7adcf86bfba829e3a31b.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　北上资金大举买入各种“茅”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　证券时报·数据宝统计显示，截至9月3日，北上资金最新持股市值达到2.49万亿元，较上周五（8月27日）增加127.62亿元。从各行业的持股数量来看，本周共有22个行业获加仓，最新持股数量较上周五加仓幅度居前的有通信、房地产、农林牧渔行业，加仓幅度均超过4%，医药生物、食品饮料分别获加仓2.23%、1.35%；周期防御性行业本周遭北上资金减仓，钢铁、国防军工分别减仓6.41%、3.38%。从更长时间来看，纺织服装、公用事业及建筑装饰3行业连续5周获北上资金加仓。</p>

<p cms-style="font-L">　　“医械茅”、“医美茅”等16股连续6周获加仓</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　从持股比例变动来看，本周共有792只陆股通成份股环比获加仓，占全部成份股比例刚过一半，加仓幅度前100股中，电气设备、化工、医药生物股居多。本周加仓幅度居前的有<span id="stock_sz000887"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000887/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">中鼎股份</a></span><span id="quote_sz000887"></span>、<span id="stock_sh603982"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603982/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">泉峰汽车</a></span><span id="quote_sh603982"></span>、<span id="stock_sh600956"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600956/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">新天绿能</a></span><span id="quote_sh600956"></span>，3股最新持股比例较上周五均加仓1.5%以上。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　本周获加仓的传媒股数量也不少，如<span id="stock_sz300413"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300413/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">芒果超媒</a></span><span id="quote_sz300413"></span>、<span id="stock_sz002555"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002555/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">三七互娱</a></span><span id="quote_sz002555"></span>、<span id="stock_sz002292"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002292/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">奥飞娱乐</a></span><span id="quote_sz002292"></span>等，其中芒果超媒最新陆股通持股3.61%，环比加仓0.44%。三七互娱最新陆股通持股0.77%，环比加仓0.08%，北上资金连续2周加仓该股，该公司自8月31日底部以来，股价累计反弹22.29%。本周环比遭减仓股包括<span id="stock_sh601068"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601068/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">中铝国际</a></span><span id="quote_sh601068"></span>、<span id="stock_sz002747"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002747/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">埃斯顿</a></span><span id="quote_sz002747"></span>、<span id="stock_sz300271"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300271/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">华宇软件</a></span><span id="quote_sz300271"></span>、<span id="stock_sh603681"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603681/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">永冠新材</a></span><span id="quote_sh603681"></span>等，前3股减仓幅度均超过2%。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　将时间轴拉长来看，自7月23日以来，共有26股连续6周遭减仓，<span id="stock_sh600426"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600426/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">华鲁恒升</a></span><span id="quote_sh600426"></span>、<span id="stock_sh601877"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601877/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">正泰电器</a></span><span id="quote_sh601877"></span>及<span id="stock_sz000656"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000656/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">金科股份</a></span><span id="quote_sz000656"></span>等5股最新持股较6周前减仓均超过1.5%，正泰电器市值超过1200亿元，公司是低压电器行业龙头企业，光伏新能源行业先行者，<span id="stock_sz002415"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002415/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">海康威视</a></span><span id="quote_sz002415"></span>、<span id="stock_sz000725"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000725/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">京东方A</a></span><span id="quote_sz000725"></span>及<span id="stock_sh601669"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601669/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">中国电建</a></span><span id="quote_sh601669"></span>3只千亿市值股，均遭北上资金连续6周减仓。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　与此同时，北上资金连续6周加仓16股，<span id="stock_sh603882"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603882/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">金域医学</a></span><span id="quote_sh603882"></span>、<span id="stock_sh688111"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688111/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">金山办公</a></span><span id="quote_sh688111"></span>最新持股较6周前均加仓3%以上，其中金山办公是国内领先的办公软件和服务提供商，市值超1100亿元。<span id="stock_sz002372"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002372/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">伟星新材</a></span><span id="quote_sz002372"></span>、<span id="stock_sz300685"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300685/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">艾德生物</a></span><span id="quote_sz300685"></span>较6周前加仓超2%。“医械茅”<span id="stock_sz300760"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300760/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">迈瑞医疗</a></span><span id="quote_sz300760"></span>获加仓1.72%，最新市值超4000亿元；“医美茅”<span id="stock_sz300896"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300896/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">爱美客</a></span><span id="quote_sz300896"></span>获加仓1.4%，最新市值在1100亿元以上。另外，<span id="stock_sz000895"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000895/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">双汇发展</a></span><span id="quote_sz000895"></span>、<span id="stock_sz300661"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300661/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">圣邦股份</a></span><span id="quote_sz300661"></span>也获得了不同幅度的加仓。</p>
