<p cms-style="font-L">　　核酸检测是实现疫情防控早发现早处置的有效手段，扫码查验是保证72小时核酸检测防疫要求落到实处的有力措施。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20220613/563/w360h203/20220613/eed7-e76c2fef9cee2782975450363db9b273.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　进入北京市各类公共场所和社区（村）严格落实测温、扫码、查证、查验72小时内核酸检测阴性证明“四件套”措施，不得以“亮码”代替“扫码”，严禁同行人员“一人扫码、多人进入”，确保“逢进必扫、逢扫必验、不漏一人”。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20220613/563/w360h203/20220613/5a9d-763375c2ba27209929c3f94e82d845b2.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20220613/563/w360h203/20220613/0fc7-f4e032822105717809190e78fcc3f0da.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　北京坚持严字当头、慎终如始，巩固来之不易的抗疫成果，坚定推进动态清零行动，守护人民群众生命安全和身体健康。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20220613/0/w1024h576/20220613/6276-39b9f3e72b097bad10c08007b59f86d0.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
