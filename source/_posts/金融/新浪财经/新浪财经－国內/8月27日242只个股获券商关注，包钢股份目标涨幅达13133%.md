<p class="ori_titlesource">　　原标题：8月27日242只个股获券商关注，<span id="stock_sh600010"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600010/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">包钢股份</a></span><span id="quote_sh600010"></span>目标涨幅达131.33%</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">每经AI快讯，</font><font cms-style="font-L">8月27日，券商给予评级的个股数共有242只，获得买入评级的个股数共有178只。在公布了目标价格的67只个股中，按照最新收盘价计算，预期涨幅排名居前的个股是包钢股份（600010.SH）、<span id="stock_sh688667"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688667/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">菱电电控</a></span><span id="quote_sh688667"></span>（688667.SH）、<span id="stock_sh601166"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601166/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">兴业银行</a></span><span id="quote_sh601166"></span>（601166.SH），预期涨幅分别是131.33%、94.84%、74.35%。</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/transform/756/w550h14606/20210828/93b4-3bd7a601e49894c15abf5363563724b7.png" alt data-link referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　从券商对个股的关注程度来看，有84只个股获得多家券商关注，券商关注数量排名居前的个股是<span id="stock_sh600809"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600809/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">山西汾酒</a></span><span id="quote_sh600809"></span>（600809.SH）、<span id="stock_sh603833"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603833/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">欧派家居</a></span><span id="quote_sh603833"></span>（603833.SH）、<span id="stock_sz300750"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300750/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">宁德时代</a></span><span id="quote_sz300750"></span>（300750.SZ）。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20210828/87/w900h787/20210828/7766-27bd46668ec739a6d8cd2ed7bc6a127e.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　从券商对行业的关注度来看，券商关注个股数居前的行业分别是医药生物、电子、化工。</p>
