<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">近日</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">孙海洋与被拐14年的儿子团聚一事</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">引发了全网关注</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/71/w550h321/20211208/8688-376ce38f3fa108d4f59f7eac806a361a.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">这14年间</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">在公安机关的不懈努力下</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">案件得以侦破</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">孙海洋在寻子过程中</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">也收到了无数陌生人的关怀与帮助</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">在湖南</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">袁先生因为与孙海洋</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">手机尾号仅有一数之差</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">多年来接到了许多误拨的电话</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">袁先生回忆</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">2015年</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">他第一次接到陌生电话</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">“对方也没说啥，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">直接了当地问了一句：</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">你小孩找到了吗？”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">袁先生回忆</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">当时还挺生气的</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">因为自己的孩子就在身边</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">但也因为这样一通电话</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">让袁先生知道了孙海洋寻子事件</font></font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/79/w550h329/20211208/7315-5a475e2178b5189c9dc028a13d5623eb.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">之后</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">袁先生接到的电话越来越多</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/gif_image/200/w640h360/20211208/c39a-033de9794e38967abe2c5942cf8680e2.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">“但只要有电话进来，</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">我也接。”</font></font></font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">每次有找孙海洋的电话</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">袁先生都会把正确的电话号码</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">转达给对方</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">并对他们提供线索表示感谢</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">这样一过就是好几年</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">袁先生坦言</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">也曾想过要不要换号码</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">但是</font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">担心换了号码后</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">错过可能有用的线索</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">于是就作罢了</font></font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="5" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/gif_image/200/w640h360/20211208/bdcc-0cfcde6e34d817ebfe8422bcdcf4e17c.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">这6年间</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">袁先生未曾与真正的孙海洋</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">取得联系</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">但他就像一个中转站</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">把来自全世界的爱心“中转”给孙海洋</font></font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="6" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/gif_image/200/w640h360/20211208/1722-e81eaac87fb04c202390f77749bc0f09.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">“感觉这是社会上一个</font></font></font></p>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">孙海洋与被拐14年的儿子团聚</font></p>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">祝你们平安健康”</font></font></font></p>
