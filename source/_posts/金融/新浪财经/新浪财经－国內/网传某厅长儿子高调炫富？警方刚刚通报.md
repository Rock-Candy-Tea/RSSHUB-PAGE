<div>   
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">又一“坑爹”的？</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">假的！假的！别被骗了！</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　近日</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一网友在网上声称</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　自己是吉林某厅长的儿子</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">发布一些</font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">“炫富炫父”</font></font></font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">的</font></font>不当言论</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　引起舆论关注</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　据网传截图显示</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　该网友在某群组中称</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">“其父是某某官员”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">“你们一辈子给我踩在脚下”……</font></font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center"></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　7月31日</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　吉林省长春市公安局朝阳区分局</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　发布警情通报</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">7月30日，长春市公安局朝阳区分局接到报案，<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">网民“下弦之睦 替补”和“上弦之贰”在互联网上冒充报警人身份诽谤其父亲韩某某</font>，造成不良影响。朝阳分局立即开展调查。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">经查，网民“下弦之睦 替补”和“上弦之贰”为同一人，真实身份为朱某某，</font></font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">与韩某某无血缘或亲属关系</font></font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">，也不认识韩某某，</font></font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">网上发布内容均系其捏造。</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">朱某某的行为已涉嫌违法，公安机关将深入调查并依法做出处理。</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220731s/585/w905h1280/20220731/0b64-039f4fb421fd540b351961054aee48fd.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">造谣者，依法严惩！</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L">综合：吉林省长春市公安局朝阳区分局、微博网友等，如有侵权请与本公众</font><font cms-style="font-L">号联系。</font></p>
