<p class="ori_titlesource">　　原标题：下周17.91亿股限售股解禁，解禁市值达316.84亿元（附名单）</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">每经AI快讯，</font><font cms-style="font-L">下周（5月3日至5月9日）一共有33家公司17.91亿股限售股解禁，解禁市值为316.84亿元，环比减少555.41亿元。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　下周四是解禁高峰，解禁规模达259.95亿元，占全周比例的82.04%。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　具体到个股，下周解禁市值排名前3的是<span id="stock_sz300014"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300014/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">亿纬锂能</a></span><span id="quote_sz300014"></span>、<span id="stock_sh603348"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603348/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">文灿股份</a></span><span id="quote_sh603348"></span>、<span id="stock_sh603019"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603019/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">中科曙光</a></span><span id="quote_sh603019"></span>，解禁市值分别为42.95亿元、39.92亿元、38.48亿元。</p>
