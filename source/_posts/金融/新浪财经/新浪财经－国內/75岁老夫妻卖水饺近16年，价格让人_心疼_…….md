<p>　　原标题：75岁老夫妻卖水饺近16年，价格让人“心疼”……</p>
<!--video-list-->

<div class="video-2017" id="videoList0" style></div>

<!--/video-list-->
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一碗水饺16个</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　不分荤素，每碗只卖3元</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　……</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　近日</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一对75岁老夫妻的平凡善举</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　感动了许多网友</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211222s/142/w1000h742/20211222/7ece-ae3912d18e4894c3b92e5ca6cd70e5de.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　姚传永和老伴胡多平</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在安徽省淮南市寿县的一个集市上</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　卖水饺将近16年</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　来店里吃饺子的顾客</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　大多是老年人</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　他们说</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这里的水饺比别的店实惠</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　起初</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一碗饺子卖一元钱</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　后来</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一碗水饺才卖两元钱</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">不久前</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　物价上涨</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">两位老人犹豫了很久</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　才将每碗水饺价格</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">从2元涨至3元</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211222s/112/w1000h712/20211222/aafc-8a36daecaeb4ac6cf99c36570758da7e.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　有人问夫妻俩</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“其他店随便一碗饺子、面条</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　都要七八元</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这价格能挣到钱吗”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　姚传永朴实地说</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">没有细算过成本和盈余</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">估摸着能赚一点</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“自己的房子</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　自己和面、擀面皮、包饺子</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　成本低很多”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“老人手里没什么钱</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　就是有点钱也舍不得花</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　我卖得便宜</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　他们才能吃得安心”</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211222s/217/w1000h817/20211222/484c-e8d79b994760e6b93466b0598acb9cb9.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　老夫妻说</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　自己的一儿一女都已成家</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　子女其实不希望他俩继续卖水饺</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　但为了那些已经处成朋友的老顾客</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　他们选择坚持</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　他们还表示</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　看见顾客吃上一碗热腾腾的水饺</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　就很开心</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211222s/90/w1000h690/20211222/3ffa-cc1fe4f6a03f140bb277f30cea725637.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">小小善举，</font></font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">汇成大爱</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　有网友将</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这个暖心故事发至网上后</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">#7旬夫妇卖3元水饺一份16个#</font></font>话题</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　很快上了热搜</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　网民点赞评论</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">“暖心又暖胃”</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0"> “朴实的老人，可贵的精神”</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">“<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">这是我看到最便宜</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">也最有爱心的水饺<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">”</font></font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211222s/738/w1000h538/20211222/21d2-a958fd3472186c3b3ae728692f90c527.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　老两口也<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">回应了</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">网民们的关心：</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">“我们这么大年龄了</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">不想当网红</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">出不出名都一样</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">水饺就卖3块钱一碗”</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">“只要乡亲们爱吃</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">我们家的饺子就行了”</font></font></p>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">微光成炬</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">温暖寒冬</font></font></p>
