<p>　　原标题：年终奖还没捂热呢，就辞职了</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　眼瞅着农历春节就要来了，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　最近的打工人圈里，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　流传着一个处理工作的万能金句——</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　年后再说！</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　大家对节日的期盼是多么热烈啊。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/399/w458h741/20220120/b436-ab4da5d0788ad653e6317f684b668d94.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　似乎“万事皆可年后再说”，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　只有一件事，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　不能等、不能拖，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　必须马上、立刻、现在，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　以及此时此刻就要说——</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　发年终奖！</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/640/w320h320/20220120/a011-520597449e153328a4a5501e11ad5201.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　尤其当年终奖与跳槽捆绑在一起时，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　它成了打工人最后的一丝牵挂。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　等着拿年终奖还是果断辞职？</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　熬还是不熬？</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这是个问题。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">与其苟延残喘不如纵情燃烧吧</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　除去假期，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一周七天，五天都是在工作，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　有的人和同事相处的时间＞</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　和家人相处的时间，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　工作顺心，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　很能提高幸福指数，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　比起年终奖，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　美丽心情更重要，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　新生代打工人似乎看得更通透。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/686/w550h136/20220120/b91b-97bc400ee0ce5051896a9e297b85fefe.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="5" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/683/w550h133/20220120/7fbb-e9b9c9d204757e1b97ff278a1b1a1b88.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　真的是上班如上坟。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">每天都哭、抑郁、不开心，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">每天被骂，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">干不下去，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">走进办公室，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">身体的每个细胞都在排斥，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">就要想想是不是该调整调整了。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　网友交流经验：</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　离职完才几天皮肤都发亮了，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　走路带风脸带笑容。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　年终奖在身心健康面前显得苍白而脆弱。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="6" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/795/w550h1045/20220120/b47f-6e97bc4a3f693f43a89bcedd1565d874.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">跟钱过不去？没有必要啊</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　经济账自然要算一算的。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">如果奖金很丰厚的话，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">可以等一等，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">苦哈哈干了一整年，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">好不容易熬到这个节骨眼儿，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">放弃了未免可惜。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">如果奖金微薄，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">跳槽到下家一两个月就能填平，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">年终奖显然就不是主要掣肘因素了，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">可以淡定地“先走为敬”。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　拿出计算器，好好算一算。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="7" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/420/w215h205/20220120/35c4-477c69f99505f121dd8bcd4e3bd74064.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">它或许不值得等待</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一寸光阴一寸金，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　寸金难买寸光阴。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　算经济账，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　也要算时间账。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　有些公司是热衷于“年后再说”的，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　而且不是马上“说”，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　而是拖上几个月，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　年终奖变成了年中奖。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="8" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/372/w550h622/20220120/5d4d-30968ac043a291a5f9e08ef1d19dd3ac.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　继续熬下去，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　你将再经历艰难的几个月，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　把钱熬到了，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　也把自己熬干了。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　而且年中拿到钱跳槽了，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　也很可能影响下一年奖金额度。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="9" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/242/w550h492/20220120/936a-72286921fc39b9fb0afbff0dd67ee042.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">落子无悔，三思而后行</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">年终奖是一时的收成，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">辞职跳槽则是职业生涯的重要转变，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">别因为年终奖，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">一叶障目，不见泰山。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　跳槽的原因是什么？</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　那些难以忍受的事情真的是无法解决吗？</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　是不是短时阵痛？</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　没有完美无缺的工作，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　对于下家是不是有了太高的幻想？</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　职业如何规划？</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　想要什么样的生活？</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　人心入局，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　落子无悔。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="10" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/202/w550h452/20220120/70c3-335133e4097703c553044b7bd431cf19.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">降薪跳槽都可以，还在乎年终奖？</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">有的人跳槽，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">工资不是主要考量标准，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">对年终奖也不太在意。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">这不一定和自身财力情况相关，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">更多是一种价值评判和选择。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　比如为了实现人生意义，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　做自己想做的事，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　或者是得到更多的安全感。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　可以从有年终奖的岗位</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　跳到没年终奖的地方，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　接受降职降薪，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在他们看来，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　水往低处流未尝不可，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　低处走得踏实，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　高处不胜寒啊。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">年龄越大，跳槽的心越小？</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　对于不少有多年工作经验</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　（年龄大）的打工人来说，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　是否跳槽，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　自己不能从心所欲。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在一些行业，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　门槛或隐或显地存在着，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　现实很残酷，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　留给自己的跳槽时间和机会不多了，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　更不能为了年终奖，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　苦熬等待，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　耗费时间。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="11" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/300/w550h550/20220120/f1f2-44bc108a3ffbc64c8f0570931a267f6a.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">年终奖很重要，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">但不是唯一重要选项，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">心有所向，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">不必金三银四，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">35+，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">也有逆势的突破口，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">关键是知道自己</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">想要啥，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">能干啥，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">该干啥。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">前行的人总会遇到坎坷，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">躺平的人才不会被绊倒，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">选择什么样的路，在于自己，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">艰难的路，没准儿越走越开阔，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">容易的路，没准儿越走越艰难。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">你的年终奖到位了吗？</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">有没有跳槽的打算？</font></p><!-- news_keyword_pub,stock, -->
