<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">厚厚的固体废物堆积</font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">近三层楼高</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">侵占黄河河道面积</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">相当于35个足球场</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">这触目惊心的场面</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">出现在陕西韩城</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/160/w550h410/20211217/bbaa-b57ff27c77ba38a970c93f3c2be396a5.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L">2021年11月4日，督察组暗查发现，黄河韩城龙门段违法倾倒固体废物，侵占河道违法建设  督察报告用图</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">这里</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">还曾有大禹治水和鲤鱼跃龙门传说</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">“新华视点”记者</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">近期跟随中央第三生态环境保护督察组</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">在陕西调查发现</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">位于黄河湿地省级自然保护区内的</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">黄河韩城龙门段干流河道</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">2013年至今</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">有逾百万立方米固废长期违法堆积</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">多年堆积形成的高台上</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">竟然还违法建有停车场、庙宇、生产装置等</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">在靠近黄河河面的地方</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">能看到大片干结龟裂的污泥</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">上边寸草不生</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/163/w550h413/20211217/c9b6-0795345fe9bf06e0152e0d4e2bc425c3.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">记者发现</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">这里的地面上</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">堆放着大量黑乎乎的泥样物质</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">捏在手里如橡皮泥</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">留在手上的黑渍很难清洗</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/163/w550h413/20211217/edbe-8e5e9800df933c0b1beea09ed32417b7.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这些固废</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　总量约125.9万立方米</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　违法侵占河道约378.5亩</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">相当于35个标准足球场</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这里还属于黄河干流河道行洪区</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　如遇特大洪水</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　堆放的各类固废和污水将被冲向下游</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　对下游三门峡库区形成较大生态环境风险</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　……</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">毫无疑问</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">这里的生态环境</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">遭到了严重破坏</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　可这里本来应该很美啊！</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在资料里我们看到</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　河道所在的黄河湿地省级自然保护区</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　是国内最大的灰鹤越冬地</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　也是候鸟重要觅食地、栖息地、繁殖地</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">触目惊心的反差</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">令人震惊的破坏</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">可这个问题，好像就解决不了一样</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">2013年至2018年间</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">国家有关部门先后10次致函韩城市</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">要求查处相关违法行为</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">恢复河道原貌</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/128/w550h378/20211217/00e5-43506d90a01febcd43de3047cd0b9aa5.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L">2021年11月3日，督察组暗查发现，2013年以来大量固体废物堆积在黄河韩城龙门段干流河道内 督察报告用图</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">但韩城市没有及时制止违法行为</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　相关问题始终未得到解决</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">还有基层干部辩解</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">非法倾倒固废属于历史遗留问题</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">2018年以后不曾发生过新的倾倒行为</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">但卫星影像的结果说不了慌：</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">2019年固废堆积面积仍在扩大</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　不仅如此</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">2018年，国家有关部门组织开展清理整治专项行动</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="5" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/gif_image/200/w640h360/20211217/3a0a-c36a10a16dee07547d40a61c329bc77c.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">但韩城市在整治过程中</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">避重就轻、搞变通</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">未按照要求扎实排查有关问题</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">对该区域内违法倾倒的</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">约125.9万立方米渣堆视而不见</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">违建的5.2万平方米停车场</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">仅排查出约0.7万平方米</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">违建的龙门煤化工应急火炬</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">大禹庙也未列入问题清单</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">“反馈的问题清单上有的才整改。</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">不在清单上的，就当不存在。”</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">一位督察人员说</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">此外</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">韩城市在相关整治工作</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">未达到标准的情况下</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">就对问题予以销号或上报销号</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">当地有关部门对占用湿地保护区的问题</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">长期以来也未依法进行处罚</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">督察组认为</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">韩城市生态优先、绿色发展理念树得不牢</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">履责不到位，工作不得力</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">致使相关问题长期存在</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">督察组现场要求整改</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">坚决杜绝在整改过程中引发新的问题</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper">
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="6" src="https://f.sinaimg.cn/finance/gif_image/200/w640h360/20211217/529d-8b0a9f1b48f6b82df10d27e5d99257a5.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
</div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">据了解</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">韩城市已成立专门的整改工作领导小组</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">对督察组指出的问题进行安排部署</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">清运工作已经启动</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">当地政府负责人表示</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">下一步将邀请行业专家进行专业指导</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">科学整改</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">督察组表示</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">将进一步调查核实有关情况</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">并按要求做好后续督察工作</font></font></p>
