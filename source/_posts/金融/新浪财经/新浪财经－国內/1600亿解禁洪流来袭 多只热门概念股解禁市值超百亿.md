<p cms-style="font-L">　　豪掷近20亿，锂电龙头投产新项目！1600亿解禁洪流来袭，多只热门概念股解禁市值超百亿</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">下周解禁市值规模超千亿元。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　7月9日晚，<span id="stock_sz300014"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300014/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">亿纬锂能</a></span><span id="quote_sz300014"></span>相继披露两个收购计划。<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">一份公告显示，亿纬锂能拟1.4亿收购金昆仑锂业28.125%股权，并分期投资不超过18亿元建设年产3万吨碳酸锂和氢氧化锂项目。</font>这家公司表示，此举有利于提升公司供应链稳定性，降低原材料价格波动对公司的不利影响。<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">另一份公告显示，亿纬锂能拟出资1.1亿收购大华化工5%股权，主要中意的是大华化工拥有大柴旦盐湖采矿权。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　值得一提，近一段时间以来，锂电概念是A股主要炒作的板块，而在市场火热期间，亿纬锂能再出手布局锂矿行业，无疑提振了股民做多信心。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　截至目前，亿纬锂能最新收盘价为117.58元/股，总市值为2220.45亿元，按照二季度末的股东户数来看，仍有10万余户持股。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/240/w550h490/20210711/aaab-ksmehzt1986303.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">三只热门概念股</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">解禁市值超200亿</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">下周解禁压力较大，将有34股解禁，按照最新收盘价计算，合计解禁市值1601.36亿元。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　34股中，解禁市值规模最大的是<span id="stock_sz300413"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300413/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">芒果超媒</a></span><span id="quote_sz300413"></span>，下周将有8.49亿股上市流通，主要是定向增发机构配售股份，解禁市值高达486.32亿元。<span id="stock_sh688981"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688981/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">中芯国际</a></span><span id="quote_sh688981"></span>和<span id="stock_sh688180"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688180/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">君实生物</a></span><span id="quote_sh688180"></span>-U的解禁市值分别为441.27亿元和258.85亿元。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　有11股的解禁压力较小，解禁市值均不足亿元。<span id="stock_sh600933"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600933/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">爱柯迪</a></span><span id="quote_sh600933"></span>的解禁市值最小，将有43.31万元的市值规模面临解禁。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">从解禁股数量占总股本的比例来看，<span id="stock_sh603713"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603713/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">密尔克卫</a></span><span id="quote_sh603713"></span>的解禁比例超50%，芒果超媒、君实生物-U、<span id="stock_sh603105"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603105/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">芯能科技</a></span><span id="quote_sh603105"></span>和<span id="stock_sh688165"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688165/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">埃夫特</a></span><span id="quote_sh688165"></span>-U等股的解禁比例居前。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　解禁股数量占总股本比例不足1%的有11股。其中<span id="stock_sh603939"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603939/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">益丰药房</a></span><span id="quote_sh603939"></span>、<span id="stock_sh600048"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600048/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">保利地产</a></span><span id="quote_sh600048"></span>和爱柯迪的解禁比例不足0.1%。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">解禁前夕</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">多股业绩翻倍增长</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　数据宝统计显示，<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">下周将要解禁的这34股，自6月以来股价平均下跌1.12%，跑赢同期大盘（-2.53%）。</font>6月以来累计涨幅超过10%的有6股，分别是<span id="stock_sz300850"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300850/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">新强联</a></span><span id="quote_sz300850"></span>、<span id="stock_sz300275"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300275/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">梅安森</a></span><span id="quote_sz300275"></span>、<span id="stock_sh600096"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600096/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">云天化</a></span><span id="quote_sh600096"></span>、芯能科技、<span id="stock_sh603005"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603005/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">晶方科技</a></span><span id="quote_sh603005"></span>和<span id="stock_sz300719"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300719/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">安达维尔</a></span><span id="quote_sz300719"></span>。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　股价累积跌幅超过10%的分别是<span id="stock_sh688656"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688656/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">浩欧博</a></span><span id="quote_sh688656"></span>、<span id="stock_sz000333"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000333/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">美的集团</a></span><span id="quote_sz000333"></span>、<span id="stock_sh605108"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh605108/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">同庆楼</a></span><span id="quote_sh605108"></span>、君实生物-U、<span id="stock_sh605199"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh605199/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">葫芦娃</a></span><span id="quote_sh605199"></span>、芒果超媒、<span id="stock_sz002705"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002705/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">新宝股份</a></span><span id="quote_sz002705"></span>和益丰药房。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">从这34股的一季报业绩来看，有30股在2021年一季度实现盈利。</font>美的集团的盈利最高，2021年第一季度营收约825.04亿元，同比增长42.22%；净利润约64.69亿元，同比增长34.45%。保利地产、中芯国际、芒果超媒、云天化和君实生物-U的一季度归母净利润额居前。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　从净利润同比增长幅度来看，净利润同比增幅最大的是<span id="stock_sz002932"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002932/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">明德生物</a></span><span id="quote_sz002932"></span>，2021年一季度营收约6.77亿元，同比增长2270.97%；净利润约3.41亿元，同比增长17448.99%。<font cms-style="font-L">还有8股的一季报净利润呈现翻倍增长，分别是云天化、<span id="stock_sz300622"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300622/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">博士眼镜</a></span><span id="quote_sz300622"></span>、浩欧博、君实生物-U、新强联、<span id="stock_sz300610"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300610/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">晨化股份</a></span><span id="quote_sz300610"></span>、中芯国际和晶方科技。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L">下周面临解禁的股票</font><font cms-style="font-L">中</font><span id="stock_sz300165"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300165/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">天瑞仪器</a></span><span id="quote_sz300165"></span>、同庆楼、<span id="stock_sz300849"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300849/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">锦盛新材</a></span><span id="quote_sz300849"></span>、<span id="stock_sh688309"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688309/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">恒誉环保</a></span><span id="quote_sh688309"></span>、<span id="stock_sh688027"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688027/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">国盾量子</a></span><span id="quote_sh688027"></span>和<span id="stock_sh688060"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688060/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">云涌科技</a></span><span id="quote_sh688060"></span>一季度业绩同比下滑。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">下周面临解禁的股票中，有5股已经发布了中报业绩预告，云天化、浩欧博、晨化股份和<span id="stock_sz002262"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002262/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">恩华药业</a></span><span id="quote_sz002262"></span>的中报业绩预喜，埃夫特-U的中报业绩预计续亏。</font></p>
