<div>   
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　雪花火炬台</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　二十四节气倒计时</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“破冰”五环</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">北京冬奥会开幕式</font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">连连刷屏、频登热搜</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/116/w550h366/20220205/89fd-2078c3f8a8de3cffd6e7f4c602559f98.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　开幕式为何有这些创意</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　节目设计理念是啥</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　2月5日</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　北京冬奥会开闭幕式总导演张艺谋</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">谈了这些</font>……</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/115/w550h365/20220205/dd75-d1361a47185d506208236d36da779023.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center color210">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">0</font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">1</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　关于火炬点燃方式</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　张艺谋说</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　中国美学特别讲究含蓄和空灵</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　讲究“以一当十”的留白风格</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在带有全世界参赛国和地区</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　名字的“雪花”簇拥下</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　小小火炬体现了<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">“星星之火可以燎原”</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/117/w550h367/20220205/57e7-6ed67de37b0711b2f9ae0e78e25ed8df.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center color210">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">0</font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">2</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　作为创作者</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　张艺谋对自己的要求很严格</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　通常不会给自己打特别高的分</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">但是我要给我们的团队</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">和昨晚开幕式呈现打100分</font>”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　他说</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　因为有了无数人的艰辛努力和创意创造</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　才有了昨晚的开幕式</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/106/w550h356/20220205/4239-bd52b9759ad5ae862cb1399b458e6eb2.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center color210">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">0</font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">3</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　张艺谋透露</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　开幕式结束后</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">“鸟巢”已开始筹备北京冬奥会闭幕式</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">他希望在闭幕式时呈现“快乐”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　因为相对开幕式来说</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　闭幕式更多是服务于运动员</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　他希望运动员们</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">能有快乐、圆满、</font>依依惜别的心情</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="5" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/116/w550h366/20220205/7439-ac74cb345a4939cc95370473ff61c996.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center color210">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">0</font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">4</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　张艺谋说</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　闭幕式的重点是<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">呈现“双奥”瞬间</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　因为“鸟巢”是唯一</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　举办“双奥”开幕式、闭幕式的场馆</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　他希望呈现出穿越历史的感觉</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　希望这个“瞬间”成为亮点</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="6" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/117/w550h367/20220205/8442-2a852d27233dd26481708345454428f2.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　除了北京冬奥会开幕式</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　有些场外花絮也引发广泛关注</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一名黎巴嫩运动员因“魔性”的步伐</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　被网友戏称为<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">“冬奥邓超”</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="7" src="https://f.sinaimg.cn/finance/gif_image/568/w648h720/20220205/ef6e-cac0a6f9018a16860be2a29e23cc6e4d.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“香港旗手好帅！”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　登上热搜的香港旗手</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　是短道速滑运动员朱定文</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　他说</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">在祖国举办的冬奥挥上区旗</font></font></font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">是他最大荣誉</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="8" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/116/w550h366/20220205/1b72-599f7a2d977a30361e67f743a9a5659b.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>

<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="9" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/63/w550h313/20220205/3567-03b30f817288fe72f55909c5c65cbf44.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">开幕式上</font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">光膀子、穿人字拖</font></font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">的旗手</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　是美属萨摩亚运动员内森·克鲁普敦</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　他身上抹的不是“防冷涂的蜡”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　而是<span id="stock_sh601099"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601099/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">太平洋</a></span><span id="quote_sh601099"></span>群岛盛产的椰子油</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="10" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/733/w440h293/20220205/4b78-41cabbe5f28c9e22bf32e0687b1b07d5.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　运动员入场环节</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　成为羽绒服<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">“种草现场”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　芬兰代表团被称为“企鹅同款穿搭”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　英国代表团大衣满满的“英伦风”</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="11" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/357/w550h607/20220205/b3f0-9f1600d022a4f24aeba6bc8727f03b9e.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　《闪亮的雪花》中</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“掉队的小鸽子”引起热议</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">表演者</font>徐书元说</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　我是一个掉队的小鸽子</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　然后我在人群中找到了姐姐</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　姐姐把我牵到了队伍里</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　网友纷纷留言：</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">一“鸽”都不能少</font></p>
