<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">有何特别之处？</font></p>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">模拟失重水槽</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">是最重要的</font></p>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">在水下进行训练的时候</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">通过给航天员的</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">颈部、手腕、腿部、腰部</font></p>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">来调整航天员身体的姿态</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">模拟在轨失重环境下的操作</font></p>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">利用虚拟现实技术</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">给航天员提供一个</font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">场景</font></p>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">以及出舱活动相关的</font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">设备</font></p>
