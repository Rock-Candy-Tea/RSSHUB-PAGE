<div>   
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　很多人做核酸时</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　都会张大嘴“啊”一声</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">但专家呼吁</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">测核酸时不要再<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">“啊”</font>了</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220325s/431/w1080h151/20220325/2755-2fd620fa55805b2ef2f391555c877861.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　近日</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　深圳国家感染性疾病</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　临床医学研究中心主任、</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　深圳市第三人民医院党委书记</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　刘磊在接受采访时提醒</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　做核酸的5点注意</p>
<!--video-list-->

<div class="video-2017" id="videoList0" style></div>

<!--/video-list-->
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">不要嚼口香糖、嚼槟榔、喝酒</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">咽部敏感者测前两小时最好不进食</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">保持一米距离不聊天</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">个人物品不要放在检测桌上</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">检测时最好不要发出声，不要喊“啊”</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220325s/378/w460h718/20220325/8376-0893a73412359a6736904ff8bd93845e.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　对于最后一点</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　专家表示：</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“有些阳性病人在‘啊’的时候</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　气溶胶就出来了</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　病毒就出来了</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　张开嘴时</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">你可以告诉自己在‘啊’</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">但是最好不要对外呼气</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">憋住一下呼吸</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　‘啊’一下主要是为了方便做出口型”</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220325s/86/w466h420/20220325/206b-b12868b31f1e3b93644cc0dc8ed97503.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220325s/518/w458h60/20220325/2ba9-2b98a28ddc918fad869db3923a4bd2a5.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220325s/530/w464h66/20220325/8ac9-e09181680f8dc328c9e2cba131947861.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　对此</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　有网友表示</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　是医务人员让“啊”的</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="5" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220325s/595/w1080h315/20220325/b84b-9ed9282be5d540802b884aacd06674e6.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　有些人确实得“啊”</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="7" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220325s/396/w1080h116/20220325/e230-abfdd9d124013c0512ea079e9692539d.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
