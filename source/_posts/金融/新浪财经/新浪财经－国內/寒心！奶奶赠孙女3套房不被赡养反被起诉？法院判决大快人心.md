<p>　　原标题：寒心！奶奶赠孙女3套房不被赡养反被起诉？法院判决大快人心</p>
<p>　　潇湘晨报</p>
<p cms-style="font-L"><!--article_adlist[<img src="data:image/gif;base64,iVBORw0KGgoAAAANSUhEUgAAAAEAAAABCAYAAAAfFcSJAAAADUlEQVQImWNgYGBgAAAABQABh6FO1AAAAABJRU5ErkJggg==" style="max-width:640px" cms-width="677" cms-height="141" black-list="y"/>]article_adlist--></p>
<!--article_adlist[<p cms-style="font-L">来源 | 每日经济新闻、荔枝新闻、新闻坊</p>]article_adlist-->
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　房子价值不菲</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　因此家中长辈的房产</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在一些小辈眼里，就成了香饽饽</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　3年前</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　十分疼爱孙女的苏州老人陆英</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">就将自己拆迁所得房产</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color30">全部给了孙女李某</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color30">双方约定李某需提供</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color30">一套安置房屋给老人居住</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center strong-Bold">　　同时承担赡养义务</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　但让老人意想不到的是</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　孙女李某得到房产后</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">就将老人晾在一边</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">从未去探望和照顾老人</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　因为无人照料，<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold color240">老人只得自己住进了养老院</font></font>，但自己的养老金无法支付养老院的费用，于是老人将自己居住的那套房产出租出去，<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold color240">用租金来补贴养老院的费用</font></font>。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211105s/117/w583h334/20211105/5665-2bdd69a4d7036263e74a617850d6e65f.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　谁知孙女李某得知上述情况后，不仅不同情自己奶奶的处境，反而认为自己的奶奶没有权力将房屋出租给别人，<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold color240">要求租客搬走，甚至还将租客起诉到法院</font></font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold color240">，并增列奶奶为第三人，</font></font>但被法院驳回。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211105s/198/w640h358/20211105/4783-48c775e76768d10a98f950fa6b1a364d.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　李某的做法让老人心寒不已，于是老人也起诉至法院，要求撤销对孙女的老宅拆迁份额赠与。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center"><!--article_adlist[<img src="//n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211105s/81/w40h41/20211105/5599-93bc5b40947bd49af331e557aef54553.png" style="max-width:640px" cms-width="30" cms-height="30.75"/>]article_adlist--></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L">法院认为，受赠人不履行赠与合同约定的义务，赠与人可以撤销赠予。<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">最终，法院判决支持老人的诉讼请求。</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　对于法院最终的判决</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　不少网友评论称<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">“大快人心”</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　有网友表示</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">“这下好了，有了这三套房子，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">老人可以安度晚年了！”</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211105s/396/w341h55/20211105/1f99-91c7a42a4cadf0d867587c098ab92f3d.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　还有网友称</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">“支持老人拿回房子，哪怕换成钱，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">在养老院也能过得自在。</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">看到最后还是感觉爽！</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">收回赠与！结局大快人心！”</font></font></p>
