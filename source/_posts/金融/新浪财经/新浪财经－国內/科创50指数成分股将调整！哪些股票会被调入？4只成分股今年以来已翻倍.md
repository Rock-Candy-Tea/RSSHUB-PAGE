<p cms-style="font-L">　　科创50指数样本年内再次迎来调整！</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　8月27日，上交所公告称，根据指数规则，经指数专家委员会审议，上交所与中证指数有限公司决定于9月13日起，对科创50等指数的样本进行调整，将<span id="stock_sh688063"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688063/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">派能科技</a></span><span id="quote_sh688063"></span>等5只个股调入科创50指数样本名单，将<span id="stock_sh688020"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688020/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">方邦股份</a></span><span id="quote_sh688020"></span>等5只个股调出。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　截至8月27日收盘，科创50指数今年以来累计上涨10.75%，近3个月累计上涨8.52%，其中有4只成分股走出翻倍行情。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">调整后指数科创属性增强</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　具体来看，本次将方邦股份、<span id="stock_sh688066"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688066/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">航天宏图</a></span><span id="quote_sh688066"></span>、<span id="stock_sh688098"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688098/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">申联生物</a></span><span id="quote_sh688098"></span>、<span id="stock_sh688389"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688389/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">普门科技</a></span><span id="quote_sh688389"></span>、<span id="stock_sh688567"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688567/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">孚能科技</a></span><span id="quote_sh688567"></span>调出科创50指数样本名单，将派能科技、<span id="stock_sh688536"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688536/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">思瑞浦</a></span><span id="quote_sh688536"></span>、<span id="stock_sh688608"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688608/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">恒玄科技</a></span><span id="quote_sh688608"></span>、<span id="stock_sh688777"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688777/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">中控技术</a></span><span id="quote_sh688777"></span>、<span id="stock_sh688819"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688819/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">天能股份</a></span><span id="quote_sh688819"></span>调入科创50指数样本名单。</p>

<p cms-style="font-L">　　此外，<span id="stock_sh688686"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688686/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">奥普特</a></span><span id="quote_sh688686"></span>、<span id="stock_sh688055"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688055/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">龙腾光电</a></span><span id="quote_sh688055"></span>、<span id="stock_sh688050"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688050/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">爱博医疗</a></span><span id="quote_sh688050"></span>、<span id="stock_sh688520"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688520/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">神州细胞</a></span><span id="quote_sh688520"></span>-U、<span id="stock_sh688200"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688200/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">华峰测控</a></span><span id="quote_sh688200"></span>进入科创50指数备选名单。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　中证君获悉，此次样本调整后，科创50指数的代表性和科创属性均有所增强。具体表现在五个方面：</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　一是对科创板市场的代表性提升。调整后科创50指数总市值2.43万亿元，占科创板比重46.8%，较调整前上升3个百分点。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　二是样本营业收入提升。调整后科创50成分股2020年营业收入2520亿元，占科创板比重50.67%，较调整前上升7.6个百分点。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　三是科技属性有所增强。调整后科创50成分股研发支出241亿元，占科创板比重54.2%，较调整前上升2.9个百分点。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　四是行业结构变化不大。按战略新兴行业分类，新一代信息技术、生物医药、新材料仍为前三大行业，权重分别为63.6%、14.1%、11.4%。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　五是个股权重分散度略有提升。指数前五大权重股为<span id="stock_sh688981"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688981/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">中芯国际</a></span><span id="quote_sh688981"></span>、<span id="stock_sh688111"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688111/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">金山办公</a></span><span id="quote_sh688111"></span>、<span id="stock_sh688012"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688012/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">中微公司</a></span><span id="quote_sh688012"></span>、<span id="stock_sh688036"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688036/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">传音控股</a></span><span id="quote_sh688036"></span>、<span id="stock_sh688169"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688169/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">石头科技</a></span><span id="quote_sh688169"></span>，合计权重31.7%，较调整前下降0.9个百分点。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">4只成分股今年以来涨幅翻倍</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　资料显示，科创50成分指数由科创板中市值大、流动性好的50只证券组成，反映最具市场代表性的一批科创企业的整体表现。科创50指数于2020年7月23日正式发布实时行情，指数以2019年12月31日为基日，基点为1000点。</p>

<p cms-style="font-L">　　今年以来，现有科创50成分股中，<span id="stock_sh688368"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688368/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">晶丰明源</a></span><span id="quote_sh688368"></span>（160.47%）、<span id="stock_sh688116"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688116/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">天奈科技</a></span><span id="quote_sh688116"></span>（157.63%）、<span id="stock_sh688005"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688005/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">容百科技</a></span><span id="quote_sh688005"></span>（143.20%）、<span id="stock_sh688599"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688599/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">天合光能</a></span><span id="quote_sh688599"></span>（133.91%）的累计涨幅超100%，位居前四，<span id="stock_sh688188"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688188/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">柏楚电子</a></span><span id="quote_sh688188"></span>以99.51%的涨幅紧随其后。</p>

<p cms-style="font-L">　　若把时间压缩至近三个月，区间涨幅翻倍的有天合光能和天奈科技，分别为219.09%和104.86%。此外，<span id="stock_sh688388"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688388/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">嘉元科技</a></span><span id="quote_sh688388"></span>、容百科技、<span id="stock_sh688065"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688065/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">凯赛生物</a></span><span id="quote_sh688065"></span>、<span id="stock_sh688122"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688122/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">西部超导</a></span><span id="quote_sh688122"></span>的区间累计涨幅超50%。</p>

<p cms-style="font-L">　　中证君注意到，天合光能和天奈科技上半年业绩均表现亮眼。天合光能2021年上半年实现营业收入201.88亿元，同比增长60.91%；实现归母净利润7.06亿元，同比增长43.17%。天奈科技实现营收5.23亿元，同比增长224.88%；实现归母净利润1.20亿元，同比增长267.36%。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">科创50ETF及联接基金已成立14只</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　截至目前，围绕科创50指数，市场已有相关指数基金产品。中证指数有限公司网站显示，相关产品共有14只（同只产品的联接A和联接C共算2只），产品类型包括联接基金和ETF，易方达、华泰柏瑞、华夏基金、工银瑞信、广发基金、国联安基金均有参与。</p>

</div>

<p cms-style="font-L">　　对于普通投资者而言，科创50指数相关基金产品是其参与科创“小巨人”的一大便捷和优选途径。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<span id="stock_sh601377"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601377/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">兴业证券</a></span><span id="quote_sh601377"></span>表示，8月份A股市场处于震荡颠簸期。前期涨幅过大、预期过高、交易过度拥挤的赛道股已经如期补跌，金融地产等低估值低成长传统产业板块出现补涨或修复。但是，基于中期基本面判断，系统性的风格切换很难出现，科创成长方兴未艾。建议趁颠簸期，继续掘金市场关注度不足的科创“小巨人”。</p>
