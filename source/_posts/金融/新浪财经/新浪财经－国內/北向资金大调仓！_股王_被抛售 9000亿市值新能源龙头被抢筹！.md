<p cms-style="font-L">　　从持有流通股比例情况来看，本周北向资金对10只个股的持有流通股比例增加1个百分点以上，其中对<span id="stock_sh603486"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603486/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">科沃斯</a></span><span id="quote_sh603486"></span>持有流通股比例增加2.32个百分点，对<span id="stock_sz300308"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300308/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">中际旭创</a></span><span id="quote_sz300308"></span>、<span id="stock_sh603786"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603786/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">科博达</a></span><span id="quote_sh603786"></span>持有流通股比例分别增加1.74个百分点、1.26个百分点。</p>
