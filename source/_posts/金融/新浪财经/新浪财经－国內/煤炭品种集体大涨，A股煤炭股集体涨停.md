<p>　　【煤炭品种集体大涨，<a href="https://weibo.com/search?containerid=231522type%3D1%26q%3D%23A%E8%82%A1%E7%85%A4%E7%82%AD%E8%82%A1%E9%9B%86%E4%BD%93%E6%B6%A8%E5%81%9C%23%26t%3D3">#A股煤炭股集体涨停#</a>】9月2日盘中，煤炭期货品种集体大涨，焦煤收盘价报2669元/吨，封住涨停；焦炭收盘价报3359元/吨，涨幅达到6.5%。现货方面，国内原料煤供应紧张，蒙古煤炭进口受疫情影响通关困难，8月以来，焦煤现货价格持续上升，优质主焦煤每吨累计上涨近千元。在期货大涨的带动下，A股煤炭板块走强，<span id="stock_sh600397"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600397/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">安源煤业</a></span><span id="quote_sh600397"></span>、<span id="stock_sh601001"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601001/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">晋控煤业</a></span><span id="quote_sh601001"></span>、<span id="stock_sz000983"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000983/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">山西焦煤</a></span><span id="quote_sz000983"></span>等多只个股涨停，其中<span id="stock_sh600740"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600740/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">山西焦化</a></span><span id="quote_sh600740"></span>近10天涨幅超过50%，表现强势。记者注意到，得益于煤炭价格高位运行，多家煤企上半年净利润翻倍，业绩表现亮眼。</p><!-- news_keyword_pub,stock,sz000983&sh600397&sh600740&sh601001 -->
