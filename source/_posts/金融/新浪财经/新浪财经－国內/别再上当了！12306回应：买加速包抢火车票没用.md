<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">又到</font>年末抢票时</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">一些第三方购票网站</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">推出所谓</font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0">“加速包”服务</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">宣称只要购买加速包</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">就能更快买到火车票</font></font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center"></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">买得加速包越多</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">抢票成功率更大</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">这真的有用吗</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold color240">12306回应抢票使用加速包</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold color240">并不能拥有优先购票权</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">铁路12306此前表示</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">所谓的“加速包”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">都是由第三方平台设计和提供的</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">并非铁路12306网站官方功能</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">不能保证给用户提供车票</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinanews/crawl/101/w550h351/20211220/2ff4-70af3a4f8c67709f1dc54128bbfca6f4.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">所以消费者使用的加速包</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">并不能拥有优先购票权</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">无论是哪款购票软件</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0">最终都要在</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0">铁路12306购票系统排队“上车”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">视频版讲解👇</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">此外</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">使用这些加速包还可能出现</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0">出票迟延、退改签手续费过高</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0">个人信息泄露、<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0">退款慢</font></font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">等风险</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold color240">12306特别提醒</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold color240">这个功能一定要开通</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">对于近期有出行计划的消费者</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">铁路12306特别提醒</font></p>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">为做好新冠肺炎疫情常态化防控</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">同时便于乘车人</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">及时接收到列车运行变更信息</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">旅客在购买火车票时</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">需要预留乘车人本人的</font></font></p>
