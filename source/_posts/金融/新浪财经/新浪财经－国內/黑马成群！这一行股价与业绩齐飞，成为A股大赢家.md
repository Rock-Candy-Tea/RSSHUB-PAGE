<p cms-style="font-L">　　有色金属板块成为今年黑马。全球货币持续宽松叠加疫情后经济复苏，大宗商品走出一轮大涨的行情，铜、铝、锌等基本金属价格涨声一片，锂、锰、钴、钼等小金属价格翻倍式上涨。有色板块景气周期还将持续多久？数据宝《金秋望远·穿越牛熊》系列，本期关注有色金属。</p>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">供需错配，有色金属价格大涨</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper">
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/0/w0h0/20210920/db0a-01c978afa636ac22fb7a0d1afc80d63f.svg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
</div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　2020年初，受疫情影响，需求端被抑制，有色金属价格几乎全线崩塌。随后全球央行大放水，在流动性冲击下，有色金属走出一轮波澜壮阔的上涨行情。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　今年上半年，各品种价格轮番走高<font cms-style="font-L">。长江有色市场铜上半年均价为6.68万元/吨，同比上涨50%；电解铝二季度以来价格攀升至2.2万元/吨，创2006年以来的价格新高；长江有色市场锌均价为2.2万元/吨，上涨29%。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　新能源汽车市场爆发性增长，上游与锂电池相关的金属材料供不应求，价格暴涨。上周，澳洲的锂精矿拍卖成交价是2240美元/吨（合约人民币1.4万元/吨），创历史新高。<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">下游电池级碳酸锂更是逼近17万元/吨</font>，六氟磷酸锂价格同比涨幅超过400%。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　有色金属上涨的背后是供需关系的极大不平衡。分品种来看，锂、钴、镍等电池材料受益于新能源车和储能双爆发，需求量大幅增长，同时供给端整体增量有限，行业持续处于供不应求状态。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　电解铝在碳中和以及能耗双控下新增产能受限，今年前七个月中国电解铝表观消费增长10.5%，远高于同期7.2%产量增长，需求增速远高于产量增速。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　铜受益于房地产行业旺季需求，同时海外铜矿因罢工、干旱等原因面临减产，供应预期收紧，铜价维持高位。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　稀土方面，近几年钕铁硼对稀土的消费量以5-10%的增速稳定增长，随着国内稀土行业整顿常态化以及环保核查的影响，指标外稀土产量不断收缩，短期内开采指标难有大增。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　在当前环保主题的大趋势下，新能源产业成为世界关注的焦点，新能源汽车产业也是有色金属行业未来最大的增长极。<font cms-style="font-L">未来铜铝镍等有色金属将成为新能源市场关注的焦点，需求量或迎来大幅增长。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">有色板块业绩爆发，总市值翻番</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper">
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/0/w0h0/20210920/db0a-01c978afa636ac22fb7a0d1afc80d63f.svg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
</div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　在产品价格上涨的推动下，上市公司盈利得到了明显提升。证券时报·数据宝统计显示，有色行业上半年营收同比增长43.78%，在周期股中仅次于钢铁行业，远高于全部A股的26.24%，<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">上半年归母净利润同比增长250%</font>，在28个申万行业中排名第五。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　个股来看，五成以上的有色股上半年归母净利润翻倍，<span id="stock_sh601600"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601600/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">中国铝业</a></span><span id="quote_sh601600"></span>、<span id="stock_sh600711"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600711/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">盛屯矿业</a></span><span id="quote_sh600711"></span>、<span id="stock_sh603876"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603876/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">鼎胜新材</a></span><span id="quote_sh603876"></span>、<span id="stock_sh600392"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600392/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">盛和资源</a></span><span id="quote_sh600392"></span>归母净利润增幅超10倍。长期来看，有色金属行业不乏盈利持续增长的成长股，以去年年报数据为准，<span id="stock_sh601388"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601388/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">怡球资源</a></span><span id="quote_sh601388"></span>近五年净利润复合增长率高达116%，<span id="stock_sz000807"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000807/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">云铝股份</a></span><span id="quote_sz000807"></span>、<span id="stock_sh601168"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601168/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">西部矿业</a></span><span id="quote_sh601168"></span>、<span id="stock_sh600531"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600531/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">豫光金铅</a></span><span id="quote_sh600531"></span>复合增长率均在90%以上。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　在过去的一年，<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">有色金属板块市值快速增长，最新总市值达到3.53万亿元，较去年同期增长112%</font>，远高于全部A股21.76%的市值增幅，也是唯一一个市值翻倍的行业。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　剔除去年以来上市的次新股，有色板块个股近一年平均涨幅70%，30只个股实现股价翻倍，<span id="stock_sh603260"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603260/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">合盛硅业</a></span><span id="quote_sh603260"></span>、<span id="stock_sz002466"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002466/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">天齐锂业</a></span><span id="quote_sz002466"></span>、<span id="stock_sh600338"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600338/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">西藏珠峰</a></span><span id="quote_sh600338"></span>涨幅最高，分别上涨629%、382%、359%。<span id="stock_sz002460"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002460/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">赣锋锂业</a></span><span id="quote_sz002460"></span>、合盛硅业、<span id="stock_sh600111"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600111/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">北方稀土</a></span><span id="quote_sh600111"></span>等7股成功站上千亿市值。</p>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">1</font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">1股</font>基金增持比例超过10%</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper">
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="6" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/0/w0h0/20210920/db0a-01c978afa636ac22fb7a0d1afc80d63f.svg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
</div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　高景气情况下，机构资金持续加仓有色金属行业。数据显示，<font cms-style="font-L">61只有色股二季度基金持股比例提升1%以上，11股提升超过10%</font>，<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold"><span id="stock_sz300986"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300986/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">志特新材</a></span><span id="quote_sz300986"></span>、<span id="stock_sh688269"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688269/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">凯立新材</a></span><span id="quote_sh688269"></span>、<span id="stock_sh688357"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688357/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">建龙微纳</a></span><span id="quote_sh688357"></span></font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">基金持股比例增加居前，分别增加37%、27%、20%。</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="7" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/746/w402h344/20210920/76fa-d7d2acfe2647ae43fb410135ae586b34.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　对于有色股未来趋势，机构依旧一致看好。38只有色股获得5家以上机构评级，<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold"><span id="stock_sh603799"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603799/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">华友钴业</a></span><span id="quote_sh603799"></span>、赣锋锂业、<span id="stock_sh688598"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688598/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">金博股份</a></span><span id="quote_sh688598"></span>、<span id="stock_sh688122"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688122/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">西部超导</a></span><span id="quote_sh688122"></span></font>获得20家以上机构推荐。根据机构一致目标价，<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold"><span id="stock_sz002203"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002203/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">海亮股份</a></span><span id="quote_sz002203"></span>、<span id="stock_sh601899"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601899/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">紫金矿业</a></span><span id="quote_sh601899"></span>、<span id="stock_sz000975"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000975/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">银泰黄金</a></span><span id="quote_sz000975"></span>、<span id="stock_sz300748"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300748/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">金力永磁</a></span><span id="quote_sz300748"></span>、<span id="stock_sz300395"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300395/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">菲利华</a></span><span id="quote_sz300395"></span></font>等14股目标价上涨空间超过30%。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　华创证券认为，宏观流动性风险已充分释放，后续金属将依据基本面差异走出结构型行情。碳中和背景下，电解铝持续紧平衡，铝价中枢有望持续上涨，带动公司业绩成长。<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">未来中<span id="stock_sh600617"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600617/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">国新能源</a></span><span id="quote_sh600617"></span>汽车销量高速增长较为确定，锂镍钴及铜箔等需求高成长可期，锂矿受资源限制或持续出现缺口。</font></p>
