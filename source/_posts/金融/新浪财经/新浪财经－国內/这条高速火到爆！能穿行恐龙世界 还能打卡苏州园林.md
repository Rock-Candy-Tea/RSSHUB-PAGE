<p class="ori_titlesource">　　原标题：这条高速火到爆！能穿行恐龙世界，还能打卡苏州园林 来源：中新社微信公众号</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　作者：唐娟 徐珊珊</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在高速公路上“穿越”侏罗纪，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　还能和恐龙来个“亲密接触”；</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　和<span id="stock_sz300024"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300024/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">机器人</a></span><span id="quote_sz300024"></span>共舞，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　再一脚踏入太空星际穿越；</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　移步换景，将苏州的园林尽赏……</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　小桥流水，能不忆江南？</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd2021424s/199/w700h299/20210424/2c28-kpamyii9012360.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd2021424s/100/w540h360/20210424/e594-kpamyii9012361.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　时速120公里，30分钟，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　就能赏过十里秦淮的风光，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　以及西津渡的旖旎。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　连地道的夫子庙小吃都不会错过……</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在别人看来，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　我是“吃喝”过了一个黄金周。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　其实，我只是走了一趟，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　300公里不到的沪宁高速。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　逛了逛沿路的6个服务区。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　便真的有种拥有全世界的满足感。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　不信！来，有图有真相！</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　沪宁高速南京出发第一站</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">黄栗墅服务区</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd2021424s/349/w700h449/20210424/492b-kpamyii9012410.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　黄栗墅服务区位于沪宁高速南京段。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　其建筑造型、设计风格，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　与夫子庙街区元素一脉相承。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd2021424s/429/w700h529/20210424/4bc7-kpamyii9012411.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd2021424s/364/w700h464/20210424/85d4-kpamyii9012441.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在这里，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　夫子庙传统饮食文化，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　与汤山地方特色有机融合，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　让你在高速上也能吃到家乡的味道。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">仙人山服务区</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="5" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd2021424s/376/w700h476/20210424/99d2-kpamyii9012440.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　第二站仙人山服务区，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　位于沪宁高速镇江段。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　服务区以“从西津渡到秦淮河”为主题，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　让短暂的服务区停留，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　变成一段穿越历史的小憩时光。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">窦庄服务区</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　沿着沪宁高速一路向东，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　常州与镇江交界处，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　便是窦庄服务区。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这个服务区，搬来了一座眼镜城。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="6" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd2021424s/403/w700h503/20210424/30c8-kpamyii9012491.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　因为，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　镇江丹阳眼镜全球闻名。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　全国七成的眼镜来自丹阳，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　原产地自然又好又便宜。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　不用下高速，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　就能享受到丹阳眼镜城的同款服务。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　来不来？</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">芳茂山恐龙主题服务区</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="7" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd2021424s/195/w700h295/20210424/86e1-kpamyii9012489.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="8" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd2021424s/366/w700h466/20210424/5647-kpamyii9012529.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="9" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd2021424s/366/w700h466/20210424/9633-kpamyii9012527.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="10" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd2021424s/100/w360h540/20210424/1e77-kpamyii9012551.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　走进服务区，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　随处可见的恐龙元素，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　仿佛让人走进侏罗纪公园。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　芳茂山恐龙主题服务区，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　位于沪宁高速常州段，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　毗邻有着“东方侏罗纪”美誉的</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　常州市中华恐龙园。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　服务区以“恐龙”为主题。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　除了提供休息、就餐、</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　娱乐、购物等综合服务功能。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　服务区内还有一座小型恐龙主题游乐场。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　此外，服务区还有真恐龙化石展出。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="11" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd2021424s/437/w700h537/20210424/b2fe-kpamyii9012552.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　就连电梯，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　都是模拟恐龙骨头装扮而成，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　乘坐电梯，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　就像是在恐龙的肚子里面穿行。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">梅村服务区</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="12" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd2021424s/384/w700h484/20210424/ef41-kpamyii9012580.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　沪蓉高速无锡段的梅村服务区，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　外观霸气侧漏，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　超大的建筑面积，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　宛如一个巨型购物中心。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这里的购物体验，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　你来了就知道。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">阳澄湖服务区</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　不入苏州城，尽览姑苏景。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　阳澄湖服务区，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　是全国首个江南园林主题的服务区。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在这里，不仅可以欣赏园林之美、</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　听昆曲、品美食，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　还可以触摸“黑科技”，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　甚至可以学习非遗文化。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="13" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd2021424s/421/w700h521/20210424/86af-kpamyii9012582.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　走进主体大楼，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　入口两侧是涵碧园林。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　涵碧园林左右是两片镜面水池，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　粉墙黛瓦倒映在镜面水池里，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　好似一副深邃的图画。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　河道上有五座沟通街区的桥梁，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　除去中间的主通道外，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　还有两座现代的玻璃桥和两座石桥。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="14" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd2021424s/425/w700h525/20210424/1c39-kpamyii9012615.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这两座石桥均来自江南乡间，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　历经近百年历史，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　由专人负责在当地拆解、编号、记录，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　搬到阳澄湖服务区后，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　按照图纸恢复原状，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　让游客感受到古色古香的历史感。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="15" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd2021424s/436/w700h536/20210424/5732-kpamyii9012616.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="16" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd2021424s/425/w700h525/20210424/45ff-kpamyii9012645.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　恍惚间，这是来到了城市科技馆？</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　难怪有人为了来服务区玩，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　特意跑了趟高速。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　如果五一假日的苏州园林太拥挤，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　那来服务区逛逛，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　似乎也不错！</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　沪宁高速公路1996年建成通车，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　连接上海与南京，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　它是国家高速公路网G42国道的组成部分。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　也是江苏省的第一条高速公路。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="17" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd2021424s/393/w700h493/20210424/5ff2-kpamyii9012644.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　沪宁高速，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　开启了江苏高速“开挂”建设的大幕，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　如今更成了江苏高速高质量发展的代名词。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　从中国北部、中西部，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　进入长江三角的流量均汇集于此。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　日均断面流量超13万辆次，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　让这里成为中国大陆最繁忙的公路之一，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　超大的人流车流，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　也让这条黄金通道的沿线休憩区，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　不只是“开水加泡面”。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="18" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd2021424s/425/w700h525/20210424/f59f-kpamyii9012675.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　经过近几年的改造升级，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　如今沪宁高速的服务区，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　各有各的颜值，各有各的玩法。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　服务区晋级成网红打卡地，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　引着男女老少来凹造型。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="19" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd2021424s/157/w700h257/20210424/8af6-kpamyii9012674.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一江春水向东流，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　沿着沪宁高速从南京一路向东，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　感受到的是穿行的速度。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　更是人们对美好生活的向往。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这个五一小长假，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　走，去江苏服务区“旅游”？</p><!-- news_keyword_pub,stock,sz300024 -->
