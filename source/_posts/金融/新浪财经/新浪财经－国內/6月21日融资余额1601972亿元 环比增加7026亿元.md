<p class="ori_titlesource">　　原标题：6月21日融资余额16019.72亿元，环比增加70.26亿元</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">每经AI快讯，</font><font cms-style="font-L">据交易所最新公布的数据显示，截至6月21日，沪深两市的融资融券余额为17507.63亿元，环比增加72.39亿元，其中融资余额16019.72亿元，环比增加70.26亿元。分市场来看，沪市两融余额为9422.06亿元，环比增加29.64亿元，深市两融余额8085.57亿元，环比增加42.75亿元。</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider2021622/543/w750h593/20210622/b56f-krwipar0583860.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　6月21日两市共有1135只个股有融资资金净买入。共有62只股票融资净买入额占总成交金额比例超10%，其中<span id="stock_sh688398"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688398/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">赛特新材</a></span><span id="quote_sh688398"></span>、<span id="stock_sh600790"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600790/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">轻纺城</a></span><span id="quote_sh600790"></span>、<span id="stock_sh600800"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600800/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">渤海化学</a></span><span id="quote_sh600800"></span>排名前三，占比分别为25.71%、22.34%、21.65%。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider2021622/659/w900h2159/20210622/de69-krwipar0583862.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　从融资资金净买入金额来看，共有22只个股净买入金额超亿元，其中<span id="stock_sz002594"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002594/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">比亚迪</a></span><span id="quote_sz002594"></span>、<span id="stock_sz002230"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002230/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">科大讯飞</a></span><span id="quote_sz002230"></span>、<span id="stock_sh600460"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600460/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">士兰微</a></span><span id="quote_sh600460"></span>排名前三，买入金额分别为4.78亿元、3.1亿元、2.55亿元。</p>
