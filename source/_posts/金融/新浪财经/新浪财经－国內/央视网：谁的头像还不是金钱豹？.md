<p>　　原标题 谁的头像还不是金钱豹？</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　再过几天，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　就是农历虎年了。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　没想到最近这段时间，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">本该属于老虎的风头，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">却被豹子抢了去。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　无论你用几G手机上网，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　是不是时常会刷到一只豹子头？</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20220126/789/w413h376/20220126/885d-7e9bf2a5628d381d471029105732198a.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　因为金钱豹有<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">“金钱暴”“金钱抱”</font></font>的谐音，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　寓意金钱暴多，被金钱拥抱，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　新的一年财源滚滚，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　所以很多网友把它</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　设为社交软件头像、用作表情包，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　还给它加了美颜，配上了新衣。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一时间，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　各种<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">“萌豹”、“美豹”、“口罩豹”</font></font>纷纷现身，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　社交媒体上金黄色一片，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　仿佛到了金钱豹大型社交现场。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　（老虎一定很无语）</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20220126/127/w588h339/20220126/46c2-a93624927344d1c49ba07b9f0451a138.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这只金钱豹出现在86版《西游记》续集的</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　第十二集《泪洒隐雾山》中。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　它在剧中没什么武器、法术，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　也没有背景、靠山，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　自称“南山大王”，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　用“分瓣梅花计”擒住了唐僧，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　两次用假人头骗过了孙悟空等，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　后被孙悟空用瞌睡虫迷倒，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　最终死于猪八戒耙下。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　金钱豹“本豹”怎么也没想到，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在原著中被“摩擦”得这么惨烈，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　却在2022年忽然翻身做了网红，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　扛起了招财进宝的重任。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20220126/396/w526h670/20220126/b2f6-fa2fa0142bf16136329dda068b866040.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一只豹子，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　怎么就莫名其妙地“火”了？</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　春节祈福、求财、求平安，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　是我国传统习俗的一部分。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　对联、窗花、年画，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　寄托着人们对新年的期盼。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　与此同时，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　网络也催生了无厘头的祝福方式。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">互联网文化和传统文化碰撞，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">让头像、壁纸、表情包成了祈福的新载体。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　经典著作里的虚拟形象经过二次加工，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　又活灵活现地出现在大众视野中，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　焕发出新的传播力。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　和当年锦鲤、孤寡青蛙、七夕布谷鸟</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　火爆网络一样，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　金钱豹那傲娇中略带邪魅的表情，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">映照出年轻网友渴望财富的心境。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　当社交媒体上充斥着</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　同一个头像、同一个话题、同一个表情包，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　代表着一种心照不宣的默契，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一种鉴别出“友军”后的欢乐，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一种化尴尬为自我调侃的情绪出口。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这种青年网友的群体共鸣，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　让金钱豹迅速成为刷屏的网红。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">过节偶尔“皮”一下，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">哈哈一笑，又有何妨。</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20220126/433/w865h368/20220126/5c03-a97e5f5a4d69c36643e0f8c85637723c.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　金钱豹的梗之所以能流传开来，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　说明众多网友认可</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“钱不是万能的，但没有钱是万万不能的”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　朴素道理。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">渴望财富乃人之常情，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">直接表达对金钱的渴望，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">不代表就是拜金，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">反而可能激发出一个人奋发向前的斗志。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　财富积累要有循序渐进的过程。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　有人说，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　何以解忧，唯有暴富。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　但一夜暴富的故事，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　更多是出现在影视剧中。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　现实生活中的暴富是小概率事件。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　即使出现，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　背后也有着长期积累，厚积薄发的逻辑。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　鼓吹让人一夜暴富的项目，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　很可能是让人变得更穷的骗局。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">生活没有多少捷径可走，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　只有一步一个脚印，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　踏踏实实做好眼前的工作，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　耐得住寂寞，潜心研究，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　才能走出属于自己的致富之路。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">在虎年即将到来之际，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">希望大家</font></font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">树立好自己的目标，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">以猛虎下山之势向其奔赴，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">用努力奋斗换来美好生活。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　你的头像换成金钱豹了吗？</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　评论区说说~</p>
