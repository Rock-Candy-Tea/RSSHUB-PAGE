<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　近日</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">浙江杭州</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">一名送快递的小哥</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">发现路上散落了</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">大量的<font cms-style="font-L">百元</font>人民币</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">捡起后发现有198张</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">报警后等候民警到来</font></p>

<div class="video-2017" id="videoList0" style></div>

<p cms-style="font-L">　　澎湃新闻记者：朱敏骏 编辑：胡宝秀 素材来源：相关人士 责任编辑：蒋格伟 校对：栾梦</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">随后</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">民警通知失主到派出所领取</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">撒钱男子来到派出所</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">称儿子的病在杭州治好了</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">捐8000元表示感谢</font></p>
<!--video-list-->

<div class="video-2017" id="videoList1" style></div>

<!--/video-list-->
<p cms-style="font-L">　　澎湃新闻记者：高亮 编辑：唐燕丽 素材来源：杭州日报 责任编辑：周琦 校对：张艳</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">据杭州交通918报道</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">前几天</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">快递小哥刘贵和一对情侣</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">在路面上看到一堆百元大钞</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">他们边报警边捡钱</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">将散落的19800元交给了警察</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">杭州市滨江区长河派出所民警</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">调取监控</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">发现当晚9点左右</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">有两名男子在事发地点</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">一名男子突然从口袋中</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">掏出一摞人民币抛洒向空中</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">随后拉着另一名男子离开了</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">民警立即对这两个人</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">进行视频追踪和人脸识别</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">但一直未能联系上</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://f.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220114s/708/w837h671/20220114/3b2c-45ccb2df2ce6d551663caccdba34f5cd.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">随后</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">这位撒钱的大哥来到长河派出所</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">说在网上看到消息来领钱</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">“撒钱哥”姓孙</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">天津人</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">为了给孩子治病</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">来杭州已经半个多月了</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">碰见民警</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">他先表达了自己的不好意思</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220114s/748/w480h268/20220114/18df-decde8380f0b1e059f44459ff214ad83.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“这一闹</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">都上新</font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">闻了</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">也给你们添了不少麻</font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">烦</font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">孙先生是个爽快人</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">觉得都“惊动”派出所了</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">就得来解决一下</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L"></font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">被问到为什么要撒钱</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　孙先生说</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　半年前</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　他们一家三口来杭州</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　不为工作也不为旅游</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　就是为了给1岁不到的孩子治病</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　原来</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　孙先生孩子刚出生没多久</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　就查出肠胃有严重的问题</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　当地看不好</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　网上查到</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　杭州当地的一位医生比较权威</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　索性开着车来求医</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　半年时间</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　医生真把孩子的病给治好了</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　身体在慢慢痊愈</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　正想回老家的时候</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　偏偏又碰上天津疫情</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　孙先生说：</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“天津最近要全员核酸</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　我们一下子回不去</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　家里老人都照顾不到</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　生活起居什么的都不方便</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　只能干着急．．．．．．”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　那天晚上</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　孙先生和朋友喝了酒</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　心酸、焦急、感激</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　几股情绪涌上心头</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　于是就把钱撒了</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　后来回到租的住处</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　他还发现自己兜里还有两百块</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“这2万元本来是用来在杭州治病的</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　我一直揣在口袋里”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　但现在治好了</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　想着以后也用不上了</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　索性撒了感恩杭州</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"></font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">既然杭州救了我儿子的命</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　我想这钱给杭州人</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　也没什么不对”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“嘛钱不钱的</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　乐呵乐呵得了”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　说着说着</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　孙先生眼眶就红了</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“没想到最后还是</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一分不少地回到了我手里”</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220114s/10/w463h347/20220114/5111-e5813cc25c431c808d5841acdd68c567.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在派出所里</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　民警再次把孙先生</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　丢掉的19800元现金做了清点</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　并郑重物归原主</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　孙先生也蛮感慨</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　向民警要来快递小哥刘贵的电话</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　表达了自己的感谢</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　刘贵也回答说</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　只要钱都在，就好了</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　临走前</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　孙先生又想到了什么</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　他从这沓现金里抽出了八千来块</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　再次交给民警</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　希望“委托长河派出所捐出去</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　留给有需要的孩子治病用”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　13日下午</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　民警赶到孙先生儿子就诊医院</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　将孙先生捐出的8300元</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　正式交到了医院工作人员手中</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　医院方面表示</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　救治孙先生儿子的医生</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　也听闻了此事</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　感谢之余</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　也会把这笔钱捐给</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　现在收治的一位小病人家庭</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　孩子家庭比较困难</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这笔钱也是非常好的一个帮助</p>
