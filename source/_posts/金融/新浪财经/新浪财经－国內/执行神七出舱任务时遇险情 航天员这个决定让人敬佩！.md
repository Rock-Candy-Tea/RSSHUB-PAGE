<div>   
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">2021年7月4日</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">中国空间站航天员首次出舱</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">刘伯明出舱后感叹“太漂亮了！”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">其实13年前执行神七出舱任务时</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">刘伯明就已经出征过太空一次</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">对于那一次出舱</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">他至今记忆犹新</font></font></p>
<!--video-list-->

<div class="video-2017" id="videoList0" style></div>

<!--/video-list-->
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center color210">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">意外！舱门拉不开</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">2008年，神舟七号成功发射</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">此次航天员的主要任务</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">是验证太空出舱行走</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">2008年9月27日下午</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">全世界的目光都聚焦在中国航天员</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">翟志刚、刘伯明和景海鹏身上</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">但意外发生了</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider202175/86/w1080h606/20210705/6058-krwipas6732861.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">回忆起当时的情景，刘伯明说</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">“我看翟志刚使尽了全身的力气拉舱门，</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">但舱门丝毫未动。”</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">翟志刚也万分着急</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">“如果门打不开，<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">我肯定就出不去，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">我要是出不去，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">神舟七号的飞行任务就没有完成。”</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://f.sinaimg.cn/spider202175/751/w480h271/20210705/005b-krwipas6732873.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">紧急关头</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">刘伯明<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">从舱内找到一根金属撬棍</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">递给了翟志刚</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">翟志刚后来感慨</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">“当通过辅助工具</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">把舱门撬开一条缝的时候，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">心情就像</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">瞬间打开了一个小窗户一样，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">心花怒放！”</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider202175/751/w480h271/20210705/4ffe-krwipas6733003.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center color210">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">惊险！火灾警报突然响起</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">然而，就在翟志刚准备出舱时</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">轨道舱突然响起了火灾警报</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">面对这样的生死考验</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">他们果断而迅速地作出了一个决定</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="6" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider202175/749/w480h269/20210705/adc1-krwipas6732995.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">按照出舱程序</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">翟志刚出舱后</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">首先要取回舱外的一块固体材料的试验模块</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">然后再展示国旗</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">那天，3名航天员临时决定</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">改变出舱程序</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">首先展示五星红旗</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">对于这一决定，刘伯明说</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">“我们执行任务目的是什么？</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">就是要出舱。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">那么它最具代表的，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">就是让五星红旗在太空高高飘扬，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">证明我们来到了太空。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">当时展示红旗，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">就是已经做好了牺牲的准备。</font></font>”</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="7" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider202175/751/w480h271/20210705/3845-krwipas6733100.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">致敬中国航天人</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">感谢你们的冒险与奉献</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">带我们驶向更辽阔的星辰大海！</font></font></p>
