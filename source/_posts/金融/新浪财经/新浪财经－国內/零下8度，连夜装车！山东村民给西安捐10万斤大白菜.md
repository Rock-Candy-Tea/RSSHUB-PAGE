<div>   
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">12月25日晚，零下8℃</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">山东菏泽村民王春凤</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">捐献10万斤大白菜，连夜装车</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">助力西安疫情防控</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">而没有菜的村民</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">纷纷自发义务前来打包装车</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">“我们没有菜，就是出把力！</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">冷是冷，但是心是热的！”</font></p>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">王春凤是成武县汶上集镇燕庄村</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">永晟果蔬种植专业合作社负责人</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">专门种植无公害蔬菜</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">看到西安疫情反复</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">王春凤当即决定把60亩大白菜</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">近10万斤无偿捐献给西安群众</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211227s/372/w750h422/20211227/f629-2f31d63928cf2b45299febb97672b6e2.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">王春凤的决定得到了</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">家人和当地政府的全力支持</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">村里60多人自发组成了</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">蔬菜装捡队伍</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">其中有八十多岁的老人</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">也有十几岁的孩子</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">这批大白菜将按西安市民实际需求</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">和疫情防控统一安排</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">尽快端上当地市民餐桌</font></p>
