<p>　　e公司讯，<span id="stock_sh601066"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601066/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">中信建投</a></span><span id="quote_sh601066"></span>研报指出，新东方在线旗下“东方甄选”抖音直播间目前粉丝突破1280万名，新东方未来在电商领域仍有较大发展空间，有望坐稳抖音电商直播龙头。东方甄选依托泛知识内容进行带货，展现出极强的用户吸引力与商业化潜力，一方面体现直播电商平台以内容为核心的变现空间潜力较大；另一方面，教育、出版等知识属性的公司有机会通过子建账号实现变现。推荐关注3条主线：1）直播电商平台：快手、抖音；2）电商服务商：<span id="stock_sz002291"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002291/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">星期六</a></span><span id="quote_sz002291"></span>、<span id="stock_sz300785"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300785/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">值得买</a></span><span id="quote_sz300785"></span>、<span id="stock_sz300792"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300792/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">壹网壹创</a></span><span id="quote_sz300792"></span>；3）出版类公司：<span id="stock_sz300788"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300788/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">中信出版</a></span><span id="quote_sz300788"></span>、<span id="stock_sh603096"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603096/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">新经典</a></span><span id="quote_sh603096"></span>。</p>
