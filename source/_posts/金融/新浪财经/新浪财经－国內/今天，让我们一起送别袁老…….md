<div>   
<p cms-style="font-L subpect-abstract">　　<font cms-style="font-L"><font cms-style="font-L">今日神州，风吹稻田千重浪，您可安心归去……</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">今天</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">“杂交水稻之父”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">“共和国勋章”获得者</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">袁隆平院士的遗体送别仪式</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">将在湖南省长沙市明阳山殡仪馆举行</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd2021524s/116/w550h366/20210524/aae2-kqpyffz0259408.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L">5月23日，湖南杂交水稻研究中心设立了吊唁厅，图为众多民众悼念袁隆平。杨华峰 摄</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">这是我国研究与发展杂交水稻的开创者</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">也是世界上第一个</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">成功利用水稻杂种优势的科学家</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">“袁隆平研发的杂交水稻</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">使数百万人脱离了饥饿</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">他是‘真正的粮食英雄’”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">联合国经社事务部这样评价</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd2021524s/366/w700h466/20210524/55a7-kqpyffz0259407.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L">5月23日，在长沙明阳山殡仪馆外，来自全国各地的数万民众冒雨排起了长龙送别袁隆平。杨华峰 摄</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">5月22日下午</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">袁老在长沙与世长辞</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">享年91岁</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">巨星陨落，举国悲痛</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd2021524s/365/w700h465/20210524/ded8-kqpyffz0259495.jpg" alt="图为民众提着行李箱前来献花。杨华峰 摄" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr">图为民众提着行李箱前来献花。杨华峰 摄</span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">当灵车缓缓驶出医院</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">在长沙街头的凄凄细雨中</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">聚集了大批自发赶来的市民</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd2021524s/366/w700h466/20210524/e90f-kqpyffz0259496.jpg" alt="运送袁隆平院士遗体的灵车专程绕行湖南杂交水稻研究中心及其居住地。中新社记者 杨华峰 摄" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr">运送袁隆平院士遗体的灵车专程绕行湖南杂交水稻研究中心及其居住地。中新社记者 杨华峰 摄</span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">灵车经过之处</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">人们把鲜花捧在胸前</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">车辆停驻鸣笛</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">向这个教科书中</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">陪伴我们长大的老人</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">高声道别：</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">“袁爷爷，一路走好！一路走好！”</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd2021524s/352/w700h452/20210524/85a0-kqpyffz0259577.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L">5月22日下午，江西省九江市德安县广大干部群众在德安县稻田广场的隆平雕像前敬献鲜花。戴和智 摄</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">消息传到家乡后</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">江西省九江市德安县人民</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">自发前往袁老雕像前敬献鲜花</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">表示沉痛哀悼</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="5" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd2021524s/366/w700h466/20210524/8647-kqpyffz0259576.jpg" alt="图为西南大学袁隆平塑像前，人们献花哀思。　何蓬磊 摄" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr">图为西南大学袁隆平塑像前，人们献花哀思。　何蓬磊 摄</span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">在其母校重庆西南大学校园内</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">短短两三小时内</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">其塑像下已铺了一层层的鲜花</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">……</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="6" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd2021524s/293/w700h393/20210524/af3e-kqpyffz0259644.jpg" alt="成都市民自发向袁隆平院士献花吊唁。成都市科技局供图" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr">成都市民自发向袁隆平院士献花吊唁。成都市科技局供图</span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">大江南北</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">无数群众以各种方式追悼缅怀</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">“长沙上万市民冒雨悼念袁隆平”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">“袁隆平雕像前的花海”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">“袁花花还没长大”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">“小理发店再没等来老顾客袁隆平”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">……</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="7" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd2021524s/366/w700h466/20210524/ffe1-kqpyffz0259645.jpg" alt="图为袁隆平吊唁厅前放满了鲜花。杨华峰 摄" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr">图为袁隆平吊唁厅前放满了鲜花。杨华峰 摄</span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">有人说</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">我好像和他有一面之缘</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">在课本上、在饭桌上、在人间里</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">有人说</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">袁老坚持到13点07分才离去</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">是想等我们吃完饭他才能安心离开</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">……</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">每一句，都饱含着人们对这个老人深深的不舍</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="8" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd2021524s/424/w524h700/20210524/04ae-kqpyffz0259881.jpg" alt="图为各式的话语表达对袁隆平的哀思。白祖偕 摄" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr">图为各式的话语表达对袁隆平的哀思。白祖偕 摄</span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">人们怀念他</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">因他不问名利</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">长期奋战在农业第一线</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">一生致力于杂交水稻研究</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">怀揣“禾下乘凉梦”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">“杂交水稻覆盖全球梦”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">让上亿人口摆脱饥饿</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="9" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd2021524s/366/w700h466/20210524/92e4-kqpyffz0259760.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L">5月23日，在长沙明阳山殡仪馆外，来自全国各地的数万民众冒雨排起了长龙送别袁隆平，图为民众手持采摘的鲜花向袁隆平遗像献花。杨华峰 摄</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">因他耄耋之年</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">仍坚持不懈，投身稻田</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">挑战自我，执着追求</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">要“继续干下去”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">“从‘90后’一直搞到‘百零后’”</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="10" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd2021524s/424/w700h524/20210524/13bc-kqpyffz0259989.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L">5月23日，民众到长沙明阳山殡仪馆送别袁隆平院士。图为一位小朋友为袁老献花。王昊昊 摄</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">因他热情生活，幽默乐观</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">总能与年轻人打成一片</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">说自己有十个“螺”笑呵呵</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">背九九乘法表</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">……</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="11" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd2021524s/366/w700h466/20210524/2b11-kqpyffz0259988.jpg" alt="湖南农业大学师生自发在校内袁隆平院士事迹展墙前悼念。李锦烽 摄" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr">湖南农业大学师生自发在校内袁隆平院士事迹展墙前悼念。李锦烽 摄</span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">“有些人，伟大到我们以为</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">他们没有生老病死”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">但斯人已逝，精神不朽</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">今日神州，风吹稻田千重浪</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">让这个从泥土里来的追梦人</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">安心地乘着白云归去</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="12" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd2021524s/786/w1080h2106/20210524/fe83-kqpyffz0260086.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">让我们道出最后一声再见：</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">袁老，一路走好！</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L"><font cms-style="font-L">作者：</font><font cms-style="font-L">彭宁铃</font></font></p>
