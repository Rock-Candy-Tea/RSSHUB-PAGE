<div>   
<p cms-style="font-L">　　今日上午，光伏龙头<span id="stock_sh601012"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601012/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">隆基股份</a></span><span id="quote_sh601012"></span>一改昨日暴跌态势，盘中一度涨超4%，消息面上，公司公告称，不会对美国单一市场产生重大依赖。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　板块方面，风力发电板块涨幅居首，新能源和电源设备板块也大幅上涨；煤炭开采、铁矿石板块则跌幅居前。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　截至午间收盘，上证指数上涨0.64%，深证成指上涨1.2%，创业板指上涨1.29%。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20211104/623/w363h260/20211104/b4e2-a34baec80dd9f04a13c138942b92b2c6.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">光伏板块涨势强劲</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　11月4日，隆基股份盘中一度涨超4%，截至上午收盘，隆基股份上涨2.8%，收于93.55元/股，半日成交额85.99亿元。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20211104/283/w456h627/20211104/4cf0-e02c3642641a2c344bf7caaab365f0d4.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　在此前的一个交易日，隆基股份盘中一度跌9.8%至90.18元/股，为今年3月15日以来出现的最大跌幅，市值跌破5000亿元。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　11月3日晚间，隆基股份发布公告称，公司对美国市场的发货仍在正常进行，以满足美国客户的订单需求。美国海关的暂扣令暂未对公司的经营造成重大不利影响，公司将持续关注和评估暂扣令对公司美国出货的影响。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　在隆基股份的股吧中，有网友表示：“隆基还是强啊，跌一天就涨了。”也有网友直称：“逆势拉升才是龙头的姿态。”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　除了隆基股份，光伏板块中的其他个股也表现活跃，<span id="stock_sh601908"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601908/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">京运通</a></span><span id="quote_sh601908"></span>、<span id="stock_sh603507"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603507/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">振江股份</a></span><span id="quote_sh603507"></span>涨停，<span id="stock_sz300118"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300118/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">东方日升</a></span><span id="quote_sz300118"></span>、<span id="stock_sh600732"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600732/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">爱旭股份</a></span><span id="quote_sh600732"></span>、<span id="stock_sz300724"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300724/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">捷佳伟创</a></span><span id="quote_sz300724"></span>等涨超7%。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20211104/44/w279h565/20211104/cf5d-0b71e55f43e8f2d1091224cbd9c8a8e6.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">风电板块批量涨停</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　此外，风力发电指数继续冲高，截至午间收盘，上涨7.55%，<span id="stock_sz300690"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300690/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">双一科技</a></span><span id="quote_sz300690"></span>、<span id="stock_sh601218"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601218/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">吉鑫科技</a></span><span id="quote_sh601218"></span>、<span id="stock_sh601615"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601615/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">明阳智能</a></span><span id="quote_sh601615"></span>、<span id="stock_sh603985"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603985/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">恒润股份</a></span><span id="quote_sh603985"></span>、<span id="stock_sh600163"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600163/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">中闽能源</a></span><span id="quote_sh600163"></span>、<span id="stock_sz002531"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002531/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">天顺风能</a></span><span id="quote_sz002531"></span>、<span id="stock_sz002487"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002487/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">大金重工</a></span><span id="quote_sz002487"></span>、振江股份等均涨停。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20211104/260/w347h713/20211104/4eba-d7e7bcb37a3d35aecb4f97c9ed8e9472.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<span id="stock_sh600030"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600030/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">中信证券</a></span><span id="quote_sh600030"></span>指出，结合明确的顶层政策规划指引与地方的执行力度跟进，在风电领域，看好2021年全年的增量政策红利和土地生产要素支撑，特别是来自广东、湖南、山东地区的政策支持，有望加速政策红利向基本面传导。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　今日上午，汽车板块也急速拉升，<span id="stock_sz000957"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000957/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">中通客车</a></span><span id="quote_sz000957"></span>、<span id="stock_sh600213"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600213/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">亚星客车</a></span><span id="quote_sh600213"></span>、<span id="stock_sz000980"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000980/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">*ST众泰</a></span><span id="quote_sz000980"></span>涨停，<span id="stock_sz000868"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000868/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">安凯客车</a></span><span id="quote_sz000868"></span>涨超8%，<span id="stock_sz002594"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002594/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">比亚迪</a></span><span id="quote_sz002594"></span>、<span id="stock_sh600686"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600686/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">金龙汽车</a></span><span id="quote_sh600686"></span>涨超5%。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20211104/277/w356h721/20211104/b35c-72c6105b566bbb7bd560333c45751e3f.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<span id="stock_sh601555"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601555/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">东吴证券</a></span><span id="quote_sh601555"></span>表示，汽车行业电动化+智能化渗透率持续超市场预期，趋势明确，看好自主崛起大行情。优质供给增加和双积分压力推动下，国内电动车渗透率提升速度持续超出市场预期。科技企业跑步入局将进一步加速汽车智能化进入摩尔定律阶段，迭代速率超市场预期。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">关注结构调整</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　从主力资金的流向来看，电气设备板块5日主力净流入额超250亿元，居于行业首位，电子和传媒板块5日主力净流入额分别为178亿元和119亿元。而有色金属、银行、休闲服务等板块的5日主力净流出额则居前。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="5" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20211104/742/w554h188/20211104/cbf9-8dfcfb8d6de890dd363b2d9c2e1a5e7e.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<span id="stock_sh601696"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601696/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">中银证券</a></span><span id="quote_sh601696"></span>表示，市场下行风险可控，关注结构调整。盈利方面，短期内需依然较弱，但市场预期相对充分，未来关注政策调整。流动性方面，海外紧缩周期开启，国内以量换价以稳为主。风险偏好方面，迷雾渐散，市场情绪有望边际修复。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<span id="stock_sh601878"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601878/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">浙商证券</a></span><span id="quote_sh601878"></span>表示，市场结构上，线索将从三季度的周期股复苏转向四季度的科技成长股，半导体引领科创板。三季报驱动下，积极把握国产替代主线下的黄金投资窗口，关注设备材料、设计、MCU等细分领域机会。（文中图片均来自Wind）</p>
