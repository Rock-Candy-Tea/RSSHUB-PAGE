<p cms-style="font-L ">　　澎湃新闻记者：高亮 编辑：周妍 素材来源：河南广播电视台民生频道 责任编辑：周琦 校对：张亮亮</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">据河南广播电视台民生频道，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">9月底，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">安阳市一名八旬老人在小区</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">被两只巨型犬咬伤。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">从监控视频可以看到，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L"><font cms-style="font-L">两只巨型犬</font>在经过老人身边时，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">突然上前咬了其臀部，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">并把她拖拽到路的一边。</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">狗主人试图拉住狗，但没有成功。</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">事发后，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">狗主人拒付赔偿，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L"><font cms-style="font-L">老人家属向帮扶节目《小莉帮忙》求助。</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211119s/564/w350h214/20211119/cc51-8fd790ffb1508b7300ddfcc4345dc083.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">根据节目发布的视频，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">被咬老人身上有明显的牙印和抓痕。</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper">
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211119s/309/w640h469/20211119/6c83-f86811938e18d878230fd9cf2e4725c3.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
</div>
<div class="img_wrapper">
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211119s/55/w616h239/20211119/389d-bf3199dc302098ff1678fbfdbf643bf0.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
</div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">有网友指出，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">狗主人是市监局经检支队队长王新刚，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">其所养的两只巨型犬，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">办理的是“心理辅导犬”狗证。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">女记者多次报道该事件却仍无进展，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">面对镜头痛哭引发关注。</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211119s/572/w640h732/20211119/ff00-502f0a3f0f63305bc4e2fa506322f7fd.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">今天（11月19日）下午，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">安阳市政府办向澎湃新闻证实，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">狗主人王新刚委托街道协调处理此事，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">市监局纪检监察部门已介入。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　此外，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　王新刚与副市长王新亭系亲属为谣言，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">“户籍都不在一个地方”。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">涉事公职人员王新刚自称，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">曾和被咬伤老人协调，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　但双方未达成赔偿事宜。</p>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">《小莉帮忙》官方微博发布此事进展，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L">称安阳市相关单位正在调查核实，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　结果会在调查清楚之后公布。</p>
