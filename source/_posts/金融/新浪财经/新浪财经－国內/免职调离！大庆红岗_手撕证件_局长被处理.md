<div>   
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center"></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">因为理发店做核酸检测“<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">不够配合</font></font>”，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">卫生计生综合监督执法局局长</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">撕毁了该店的卫生许可证</font></font>。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">3月25日，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">黑龙江省大庆市红岗区委区政府通报，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">该局长因<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">工作方法不当、简单粗暴被免职，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">并调离执法队伍。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">事件回顾</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center"></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">3月23日，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">大庆市红岗区一理发店店主称，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">因22日自己未做核酸检测，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">经与卫生监督执法局工作人员沟通</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">决定23日补做。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">但当天上午9时许，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">卫生监督执法局局长带队进店检查时，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">直接将店内卫生许可证撕坏，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">称要将店铺强制关闭一个月并进行罚款。</font></p>
<!--video-list-->

<div class="video-2017" id="videoList0" style></div>

<!--/video-list-->
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">该视频在网上流传后，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">红岗区委、区政府</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">第一时间成立调查组展开调查，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">同时责令涉事人王某某停止工作，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">配合调查。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">官方回应</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center"></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">3月25日，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">大庆市红岗区委区政府发布了</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">“关于红岗区卫生计生综合监督执法局干部</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">执法不当问题的调查处理情况通报”</font></font>。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">经查，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">3月23日上午，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">红岗区卫生计生综合监督执法局局长王某某</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">带领工作人员对区中心村疫情防控、</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">落实重点人群核酸检测等情况开展执法检查。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">9时20分左右，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">在中心村某理发店进行检查过程中，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">因该店2月以来</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">3次未能落实重点人群核酸检测政策，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">且当日进行相关执法检查时仍然不够配合，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">王某某情绪较为激动，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">发生了撕毁该店卫生许可证的不当行为。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">事件发生当晚，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">王某某本人深刻认识到自身错误，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">就执法不当行为</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">已经当面向理发店经营者高某某道歉，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">并取得高某某谅解。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">高某某也表示，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">今后将主动配合落实防控政策。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">鉴于王某某在执法过程中，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">工作方法不当，简单粗暴，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">在社会上造成严重不良影响，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">依据相关规定，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">给予王某某</font></font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">免职、</font></font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">政务记过、</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">调离执法队伍处理。</font></p>
