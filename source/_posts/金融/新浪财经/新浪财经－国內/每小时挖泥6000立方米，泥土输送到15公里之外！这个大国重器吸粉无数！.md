<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">有了万物之后，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">人类创造出器与物。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">人类运用它们，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">与大地、高山、大海、江河合作。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">过往的历史，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">继续积蓄成此刻建造的力量，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">连接未来。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">器物有形，心愿无边。</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L">中央广播电视总台财经节目中心大型纪录片</font><font cms-style="font-L color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold color330">《大国建造》</font></font><font cms-style="font-L">，讲述40个中国新地标的建造传奇！</font><font cms-style="font-L color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold color330">7月5日至10日每天19：30央视财经频道播出</font></font><font cms-style="font-L">，今晚播出第三集</font><font cms-style="font-L color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold color330">《锤炼成器》</font></font><font cms-style="font-L">，探秘超级工程背后的超级装备。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">1</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">世界上最长的高速公路隧道要来了！</font></font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">海拔4280米上的“死亡路段”或将被“拯救”！</font></font></p>

<div class="video-2017" id="videoList0" style></div>

<!--/video-list-->
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L">建设者们将用7年时间，建设</font><font cms-style="font-L color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold color330">世界上最长的高速公路隧道</font></font><font cms-style="font-L">，全长22公里的天山胜利隧道。</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider202177/435/w794h441/20210707/ece0-ksmehzs8999263.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L">高地应力、高地震烈度、高环保要求、高海拔施工，</font><font cms-style="font-L color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold color330">针对天山的地形条件打造的TBM胜利号为奇迹而生</font></font><font cms-style="font-L">，全球首创压注工法的新型硬岩掘进器，</font><font cms-style="font-L color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold color330">刀盘直径8.43米，接近三层楼房的高度</font></font><font cms-style="font-L">，可穿越多种复杂地质层。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">2</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">“水下走钢丝”，打开长江口！</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">世界最大河口整治工程，离不开它</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L">治理拦门沙，打开长江口，这是</font><font cms-style="font-L color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold color330">世界上最大、最复杂的河口整治工程</font></font><font cms-style="font-L">。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L">新海凤，耙吸式挖泥船的旗舰，日复一日，为确保12.5米的航道水深，人与装备始终坚守。</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider202177/438/w792h446/20210707/d26d-ksmehzs8999288.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L">双耙最大挖深45米，通过先进的自动控制系统，在水下实现精准挖泥，耙头就如同在</font><font cms-style="font-L color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold color330">“水下走钢丝”</font></font><font cms-style="font-L">。船舱泥沙容量</font><font cms-style="font-L color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold color330">相当于可同时填满9个国际标准泳池</font></font><font cms-style="font-L">。</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider202177/377/w795h382/20210707/68b0-ksmehzs8999285.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">3</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">每小时挖泥6000立方米，泥土输送到15公里之外！</font></font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">这个大国重器吸粉无数！</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L">天鲲号，</font><font cms-style="font-L color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold color330">亚洲最大自航绞吸船</font></font><font cms-style="font-L">，它拥有亚洲最强的挖掘系统，拥有世界第一的远程输送能力。</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider202177/439/w792h447/20210707/6d1d-ksmehzs8999309.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="5" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider202177/438/w794h444/20210707/bc01-ksmehzs8999308.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L">绞刀功率6600千瓦，每小时挖泥6000立方米；最大输送距离15公里</font><font cms-style="font-L">。如果从北京南站挖泥经过15公里管道投送，一周内就能填满一座水立方。</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="6" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider202177/428/w789h439/20210707/0fd6-ksmehzs8999353.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">4</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">1500吨高空巨无霸，三天建成一层楼</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold color330">成都绿地“468”项目，建成高度468米，超过现有成都第一高楼339米的电视塔。</font></font></font><font cms-style="font-L">楼顶上的巨大装置，就是高度的生长点，标志着成都开始创造西部第一高。</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="7" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider202177/436/w790h446/20210707/e038-ksmehzs8999352.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L">施工人员将驾驶1500吨的高空巨无霸，在300多米的高空，经历7个小时的空中漫步，完成4.4米的爬升，抵达新的作业面。5层楼高的巨型机器，最新一代空中造楼机，三天建成一层楼，这是中国首创的智能化施工装备集成平台。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">5</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">全长24公里，双向8车道！这条通道一经建成，深圳到中山只需30分钟</font></font>！</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L">继港珠澳大桥之后，又一项集桥、岛、隧一体的世界级</font><font cms-style="font-L">工程——双向8车道、长24公里的</font><font cms-style="font-L color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold color330">深中通道</font></font><font cms-style="font-L">，一经建成，</font><font cms-style="font-L color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold color330">深圳到中山只需30分钟。</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="8" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider202177/428/w787h441/20210707/2bcf-ksmehzs8999423.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold color330">深中通道沉管巨无霸，世界上首例特长钢壳沉管</font></font></font><font cms-style="font-L">，一节重约8万吨，超过“辽宁号”航空母舰的排水量，比港珠澳大桥还要宽9米。</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="9" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider202177/436/w792h444/20210707/5a51-ksmehzs8999422.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L">“一航津安1”是中国建设者历时三年，为深中通道量身打造的世界首艘沉管运输安装一体船。它具有巡航及偏移纠错功能，拖带8万吨沉管，航速接近6节，配备沉管沉放姿态控制系统，船与沉管在水中的每一个步骤，都得以清晰呈现。</font><font cms-style="font-L color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold color330">它的诞生，标志着我国海底沉管隧道施工，正式进入智能化时代。</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">中央广播电视总台大型纪录片</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">《大国建造》</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">7月5日至10日19：30</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">在</font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">CCTV-2央视财经频道</font></font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">重磅播出！</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">第三集</font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">《锤炼成器》</font></font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">今天19：30将播出，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">探秘超级工程背后的超级装备！</font></p>
