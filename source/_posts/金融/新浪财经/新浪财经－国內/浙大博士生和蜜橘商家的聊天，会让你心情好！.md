<div>   
<p>　　原标题：浙大博士生和蜜橘商家的聊天，会让你心情好！</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">“帮不了国家什么大忙</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">遇到就想出点力”</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">近日，一位蜜橘商家</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">和浙大博士生的聊天记录</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">冲上热搜第一</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211115s/779/w658h121/20211115/afca-0179ae91d8cfbf630c9bc23a48cdff48.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">陈凯是浙江台州涌泉镇西柯岙村</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">一位普通青年人</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">平时他和妻子经营着一家网店</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">卖自家和亲戚家产的橘子</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">网店规模不大，口碑不错</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">足够把收获的橘子销售出去</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">如今，他家的网店成了“网红店”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">销量迅速增长</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">这背后，有着温暖的故事</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">11月11日，网店来了位顾客</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">这位顾客对橘子的尺寸大小</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">问得格外仔细</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">“老板，我是浙大的研究生</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">要用橘子做科学实验”</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211115s/130/w720h1810/20211115/1728-b0777ea17d1fbca82b8dde62639a38d7.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">研究生？买橘子做科学实验？</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">陈凯听到这消息后，立即回复</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">“你们为国家做研究</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">我免费先寄一箱试试”</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">“帮不了国家什么大忙</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">遇到了就想出点力”</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">11月12日上午，按照同学的要求</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">陈凯寄出了第一箱约5公斤的蜜橘</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">同时，了解到实验需要使用的橘子</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">数量多达几百个</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">他又选了5箱10公斤的橘子寄出</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211115s/451/w720h1331/20211115/ec99-7eb35c6ed95ebac70957e4cbc5c64ee0.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">本以为故事至此就结束了</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">万万没想到，聊天记录传遍了网络</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">店里涌进来很多顾客来下单</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">几乎都是浙大学生</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">“以前一天发货就100箱左右</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">现在一天达到了2000多箱</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">没想到挺小的一件事情</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">能有这么大影响”</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211115s/496/w900h5196/20211115/d912-6c49d361495d19f34c2c0886450c99da.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">原来，这位研究生回去后</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">十分感激陈凯的无私帮助</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">同时也为陈凯那句话感动不已</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">就把自己的经历发在学校论坛</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">立即获得无数人点赞</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">在大学生们的力挺下</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">陈凯的网店销量持续攀升</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211115s/80/w720h960/20211115/aecf-3960fe898d7e46c7cca2bb1d64668230.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　现在，一进入陈凯的网店</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">客服就会先发来一段提醒</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">“请广大朋友们一定要理性消费</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">我们大家祝祖国越来越繁荣昌盛</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">祝大家天天开心，前程似锦</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">也祝广大学子们学业蒸蒸日上</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">毕业后事业<span id="stock_sz002251"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002251/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">步步高</a></span><span id="quote_sz002251"></span>升”</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="5" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211115s/645/w720h725/20211115/f4b6-d956d603f2fbf3f56e53cf85224128bc.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">网友纷纷为</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">友善的大学生</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">和朴实的陈凯点赞</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">“我们国家真的有最好的人民”</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="6" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211115s/378/w1080h98/20211115/8dd4-2c84d43e731d08a1ed6f7c0a8e442474.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
