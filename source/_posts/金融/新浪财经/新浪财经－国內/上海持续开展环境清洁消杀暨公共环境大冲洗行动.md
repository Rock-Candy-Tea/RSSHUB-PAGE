<p cms-style="font-L">　　原标题：上海持续开展环境清洁消杀暨公共环境大冲洗行动</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　上海市绿化市容局表示，本市正处于公共环境整治消杀攻坚阶段。近日，绿化市容行业再次开展环境清洁消杀暨公共环境大冲洗行动，进一步巩固大冲洗行动成果，使市容环境质量保持优良状态。详见↓</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220421s/290/w1080h810/20220421/eb2c-a2fe9bc68bdec3642d1ac33292d8c112.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220421s/289/w1080h809/20220421/6d45-821968a3d49ab561bcf905fa7f64b392.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　在本次行动中，全市共出动保洁人员7211人，小型保洁设备1038辆，道路机械化保洁设备750辆，消除死角盲区1308处，清除垃圾412.58吨。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220421s/782/w904h678/20220421/13aa-01305d61ffafe1eaabda52b14da29e17.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220421s/290/w1080h810/20220421/47df-9c2b4c775773c2a4f2fea60424746db4.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220421s/290/w1080h810/20220421/21ef-ef7620e3f6b5e1e662981a10b127e329.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">黄浦区</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　采取了“多车联动”组合作业，以机械化冲洗为主，对道路两侧隔离带、沟底、人行道等进行彻底冲洗，同时，加强飞行保洁动态管理，快速清理道路及居住区周边暴露垃圾。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="5" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220421s/290/w1080h810/20220421/c7cb-84c76dd60bc511e1c75d3354b6c04a6a.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="6" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220421s/290/w1080h810/20220421/a8e8-1fad2397ae28b896395c0ae5c2101236.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="7" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220421s/290/w1080h810/20220421/d292-d755dc795425b712533dc02d567959a9.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="8" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220421s/287/w1078h809/20220421/a2b5-5a513d4be02afafc95688a5d6b3e7660.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">静安区</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　在保障全域冲洗消杀的基础上，针对部分重点区域进行“再保洁、再提升”。对电话亭、邮箱、公交站点、便民座椅等城市家具进行冲洗消杀。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="9" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220421s/290/w1080h810/20220421/350e-c753ed0ef2e89b5792c50656bf12210b.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="10" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220421s/290/w1080h810/20220421/cd43-73faa334dd5a8af8edb89edac2cc9b49.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="11" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220421s/290/w1080h810/20220421/16dc-dbf1a96fd64f9eca12d319dadb96f63a.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="12" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220421s/290/w1080h810/20220421/96c2-6f77de231ebcf6ac18413d2de3b3d926.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">虹口区</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　对重点区域周边道路进行先立面后平面的精准消杀冲洗；采取“1+2”作业模式，即一辆冲洗车冲洗路面，两辆机扫车贴左右两侧沟底清扫，实现一体化消杀保洁。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="13" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220421s/289/w1080h809/20220421/0004-cd9022af42d09e75454ccdf26a8a5a79.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="14" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220421s/290/w1080h810/20220421/7bee-d721527b5653cba5bcf80e7e72534a76.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="15" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220421s/459/w719h540/20220421/dd41-16215da3dd176a69d56317d41ad45c85.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="16" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220421s/290/w1080h810/20220421/1b4c-daf3aad4b1a98e6d01b5c1cd231a614b.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">杨浦区</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　采用“机扫+机冲+人工”的方式，对花箱底部、绿化隔离带沟底等死角盲区进行重点整治。相关领导带队现场督查冲洗消杀落实情况，确保各项工作落到实处。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="17" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220421s/290/w1080h810/20220421/ea84-71bca24e95764677ebb086803ec8731e.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="18" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220421s/771/w898h673/20220421/c417-387a9bf78063fa846ddc12cab7798801.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">松江区</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　结合“常态消杀”和“重点消杀”，在“地毯式”全覆盖冲洗保洁的基础上，对重点区域增加冲洗保洁和消杀频次，对作业盲区进行延伸保洁，道路环境清洁状况明显改善。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">闵行区</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　改进了作业模式，对道路冲洗车进行改装，充分发挥小车机动性，对沿路非机动车道、隔离栏、废物箱、公交车站等进行整体消杀，提升作业效率。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="19" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220421s/290/w1080h810/20220421/7572-57202cc597057f2df54a447d397c7619.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="20" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220421s/291/w1080h811/20220421/e4b0-5036e0e24015cabacda01452ae0bbc49.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">青浦区</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　加强跨部门联动，申请办理一线人员临时出入证，按照就近便捷原则，调配现有机械化道路冲洗设备，有效调剂作业力量补充人员紧张区域，确保道路冲洗全覆盖，保障划片作业有序实施。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="21" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220421s/181/w1080h701/20220421/62bc-b37028252cc18d7ac436b9c44e9d5b98.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="22" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220421s/176/w1080h696/20220421/ff33-4e8283b989f4b4c978d71a31efcae45b.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="23" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220421s/166/w1080h686/20220421/4c96-6386f5b98c4748da8c6ad900a9c2396f.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="24" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220421s/146/w1080h666/20220421/5556-f8507fb19c495f2f4e8646d36a31eb62.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="25" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220421s/162/w1080h682/20220421/0737-3500a365d73ca829fa56782c3796028b.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　全面完成攻坚阶段任务后，4月22日-4月30日，本市绿化市容行业将进入城市公共环境清消行动巩固阶段，巩固两次的行动成果。</p><!-- news_keyword_pub,stock, -->
