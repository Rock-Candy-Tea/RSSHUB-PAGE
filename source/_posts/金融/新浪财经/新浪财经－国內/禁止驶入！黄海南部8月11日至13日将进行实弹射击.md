<p cms-style="font-L">　　新京报讯 据海事局网站消息，云航警0074/22，黄海南部，8月11日至8月13日每日9时至16时在34-52N 120-00E、34-52N 120-13E、34-44N 120-13E和34-44N 120-00E四点连线范围内进行实弹射击，禁止驶入。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　LYG0074/22，SOUTH YELLOW SEA GUN FIRING EXERCISES FROM 0100UTC TO 0800UTC DAILY FROM 11 TO 13 AUG． IN AREA BOUNDED BY THE LINES JOINING 34-52N 120-00E、34-52N 120-13E、34-44N 120-13E AND 34-44N 120-00E，ENTERING PROHIBITED． LIANYUNGANG MSA CHINA．</p>
