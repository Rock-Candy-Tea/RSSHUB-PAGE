<p>　　12306是24小时售票吗？解答来了！</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　从6月1日起</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　铁路客票预售期调整为15天</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　那么</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　12306App是24小时售票吗？</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　火车站到底几点开始售票呢？</p>
<p cms-style="font-L color210">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold color210">12306App是24小时售票吗？</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　铁路12306网站、12306App</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">售票改签服务时间</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">为每日5时至次日凌晨1时</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　注意</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　为了保障12306系统稳定运行</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">每周二23时30分至周三5时</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">进行系统运维巡检</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L color210">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold color210">几点可以买票？</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　预售期调整为15天</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　那么几点可以买票呢？</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　打开铁路12306App</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　点击“我的”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在出行向导里点击“<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">起售时间</font></font>”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　输入要查询的<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">车站名</font></font>和<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">日期</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　就可以看到该车站的车票起售时间</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">注意</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">各个站点的起售时间不同</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/59/w550h309/20220615/0159-b2c4593b065c80023f963206bbcd15ee.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/163/w550h413/20220615/f0a1-7213483f415c27110933ca9779910d69.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　举个例子</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　如果购买G2463次</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　北京北到呼和浩特东的火车票</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">北京北的起售时间是12时</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　如果你错过了北京北始发的车票</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　还可以尝试购买</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　相同车次途经清河的火车票</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">清河站的起售时间是16时</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L color210">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold color210">退票时间是24小时吗？</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　使用电子支付方式</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　购买的铁路电子客票</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　均可通过铁路12306网站</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　或车站指定窗口</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">在开车前办理退票手续</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　使用现金方式购票</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　或已打印报销凭证的铁路电子客票</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　可到车站指定窗口办理退票手续</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">注意</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　已打印报销凭证的铁路电子客票</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　办理改签、退票手续时</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　旅客须交回报销凭证</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">退票业务办理服务时间</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">为24小时全天候办理</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">退票截止时间为开车前</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center color210">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">乘车出行一定要规划好时间</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center color210">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">全程佩戴好口罩</font></p><!-- news_keyword_pub,stock, -->
