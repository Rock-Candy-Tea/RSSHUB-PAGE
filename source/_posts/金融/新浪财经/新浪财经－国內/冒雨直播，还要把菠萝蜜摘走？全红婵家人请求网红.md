<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　碾压式夺冠后，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　14岁的奥运冠军全红婵火出圈！</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/116/w550h366/20210809/2af4-46a5e09f0c94ebf246c825fd5949c8e0.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　然而，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">全红婵广东湛江的家门口</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">却聚集起不少网红</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">进行直播或录像．．．．．．</font></font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/472/w236h236/20210809/bde2-6bd59e0eeff1e2216d0e36a0c4d3f26d.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">超2000人打卡</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">网红冒雨直播</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">据@N视频消息，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　8月7日，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　全红婵夺冠后的第3天，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　记者来到全红婵的家乡</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　广东湛江麻章镇迈合村，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">发现入夜以后村里车辆依然络绎不绝，</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">不少主播冒雨在全红婵家外直播。</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">据现场的村民统计，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">按照</font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">一人一车</font></font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">进村登记，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">一天起码就有2000人</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">打卡过全红婵的家乡麻章镇迈合村。</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/44/w550h294/20210809/1e60-2d404a4a30d0dea6e69fca30b31c7798.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　某短视频平台上，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　以“冠军之家”为题的短视频一度刷屏；</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/467/w550h717/20210809/8a42-5588a8f16d3f0427a5e2e445538b602e.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　全红婵家门口有棵菠萝蜜树，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　也成为了网红们的焦点。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">有些网红甚至一边直播一边带货，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">还扬言要把全红婵家里的菠萝蜜摘走。</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/168/w550h418/20210809/32bb-7d0123cce03da677bf70dbaa8eb1daac.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center color210">　　全红婵家人请求网红“小点声”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　据广东电视台《DV现场》报道，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　面对家门口的一众网红，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　全红婵家人无奈请求，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">“请你们小声一点<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">，</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">已经骚扰到老人家两天没有睡了”。</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　迈合村委干部告诉记者，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　村民意见很大。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">迈合村的村口已立</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">“疫情防控，谢绝入村”的告示牌，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">但仍有网络主播在村口直播。</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">32个抖音帐号被永久封禁</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　8月8日晚，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“抖音安全中心”发布公告称，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　有极少数用户存在借奥运健儿营销、</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">蹭奥运健儿热度</font></font>的违规行为。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="5" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/191/w550h441/20210809/97d1-aef31cd121c7fa4cd93b4558a109127c.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　针对此类违规行为，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　抖音安全中心</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　下架违规视频3287条，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　处罚违规直播间106个，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">处罚违规帐号92个，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">其中永久封禁违规帐号32个。</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　对于部分网友去往奥运健儿家中聚集，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">甚至暴露家庭住址的行为，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　抖音呼吁要理性为奥运健儿加油。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="6" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/497/w550h747/20210809/47f4-5e484f7e76351f4202f49a803d027948.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">全红婵的人生路还很长，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">不过分打扰，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">就是最好的支持。</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　来源：中国青年报（ID：zqbcyol 整理：陈茜）综合南方都市报、N视频、抖音安全中心、观察者网（作者 郭肖）等。</p><!-- news_keyword_pub,stock, -->
