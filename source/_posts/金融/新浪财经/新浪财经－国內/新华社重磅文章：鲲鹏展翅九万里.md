<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　都能够从容应对、果断决策、</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　锚定目标、掌握主动</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　带领中国航船沿着正确方向坚定前行</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　实践一再证明并将继续证明</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　拥有富有远见、成熟稳定的</p>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　党的十八大以来</p>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　贯穿治国理政全过程各方面</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　与人民同呼吸、共命运、心连心</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　牢牢守好人民江山</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20220101/320/w1080h1640/20220101/ed44-bce1e025ee4e408d3c0411fed6bc9639.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　服务民族复兴、促进人类进步</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在坚守中国道路中</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　创造人类文明新形态</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在谋求天下大同中</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　构建人类命运共同体</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　习近平总书记以为全人类谋福祉的</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　世界级领袖担当</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　引领各国共同绘制出</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一幅追求和平、发展、</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　合作、共赢的壮美画卷</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　中华民族向世界展现的</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　是一派欣欣向荣气象</p>
