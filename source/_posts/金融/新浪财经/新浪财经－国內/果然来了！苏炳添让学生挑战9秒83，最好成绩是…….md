<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">“跑得最快的副教授”苏炳添</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">又开课了！</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　11月17日上午9点</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在暨南大学校本部旭日运动场</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　著名田径运动员、暨南大学体育学院</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　副教授<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">苏炳添的分享体验课</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">正式开讲！</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　此前，在东京奥运会上</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　苏炳添以9秒83的成绩</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一战惊人</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　不少网友猜测</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">苏老师的课</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">是不是要破9秒83的纪录</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">才能及格</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper">
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/128/w64h64/20211118/650e-56ce392a5c875e9b7affd3052ee294e4.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
</div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/630/w550h80/20211118/e3de-589bd1ad5589e5033c81cf32e29d749d.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center color0">　　今天，它终于来了！</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在17日的课上</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　苏炳添布置了一个挑战——</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">9秒83能跑多少米</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　听到这个挑战</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　现场学生发出一阵笑声</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/gif_image/199/w640h359/20211118/591a-158079a0ea08cb82b72f0aaae7c5d71d.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“什么是体育精神？</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　现在就是展现</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　你们体育精神的时候了！”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　进入9秒83挑战环节</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" src="https://f.sinaimg.cn/finance/gif_image/199/w640h359/20211118/3f7b-e9f0b2827a5864780ee99531e79fb767.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　据了解</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　当天男生第一轮的<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">最好成绩是80米</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="5" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/163/w550h413/20211118/757f-b76e3e3a028e57025738f906e346297e.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　尽管没有上过苏老师的课</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　但评论区中不少网友“交作业”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　并自曝测试成绩．．．．．．</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="6" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/747/w550h197/20211118/15c5-5bc47e43bca96b725a049949bed8c711.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="7" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/654/w550h104/20211118/1726-0a774e2d0e8e12f1037b68caa4485642.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="8" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/663/w550h113/20211118/1908-e84ad93156b7e7fda274780def16b39e.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="9" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/618/w550h68/20211118/5418-1336b5e70a0f69c39b87a25485487dc1.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　除了“983挑战”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　苏炳添课上还分享了练臀动作</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　并讲解了动作要点</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">“双脚可以稍微打开一些，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">这个动作如果做好了，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">也能练习你们的臀部哦~”</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="10" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/gif_image/375/w750h425/20211118/b002-cdb2db955a00c00e98c4ba282bcc930b.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　据了解</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　本次分享体验课</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　面向暨南大学全体学生</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">仅有100个名额</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　同学们要靠“抽奖”的方式</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　才能拿到本场公开课的入场券</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="11" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/458/w234h224/20211118/2f6c-f92a8dafb29df0b26bffd87df5299cf4.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　苏炳添透露</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　接下来暨南大学</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">将成立“苏炳添速度研究中心”</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　打造一个平台</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　帮助更多的运动员交流</p>
