<p>　　市值超千亿的消费股去年大受机构青睐，今年均出现了上涨乏力迹象。</p>
<p>　　为支持实体经济发展，促进综合融资成本稳中有降，<font cms-style="strong-Bold">中国人民银行决定于2021年12月15日下调金融机构存款准备金率0.5个百分点</font>（不含已执行5%存款准备金率的金融机构）。本次下调后，金融机构加权平均存款准备金率为8.4%。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/82/w550h332/20211206/323d-eacf0a1b915ca27b3dadbef26884c435.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></div>
<p>　　中国人民银行有关负责人就下调金融机构存款准备金率答记者问时表示，此次降准为全面降准，除已执行5%存款准备金率的部分县域法人金融机构外，对其他金融机构普遍下调存款准备金率0.5个百分点，同时考虑到参加普惠金融定向降准考核的大多数金融机构都达到了支农支小（含个体工商户）等考核标准，政策目标已实现，有关金融机构统一执行最优惠档存款准备金率，此次降准共计释放长期资金约1.2万亿元。</p>
<p cms-style="align-Center">　　<font cms-style="strong-Bold align-Center">跨界转型新能源股的</font></p>
<p cms-style="align-Center">　　<font cms-style="strong-Bold align-Center">股价逆袭</font></p>
<p>　　12月6日，沪深两市普遍高开低走，沪指尾盘失守3600点，创业板指收盘跌逾2%。早盘强势的券商板块大幅回落，<font cms-style="strong-Bold"><span id="stock_sh601377"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601377/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">兴业证券</a></span><span id="quote_sh601377"></span></font>涨停板被打开，<font cms-style="strong-Bold"><span id="stock_sh600918"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600918/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">中泰证券</a></span><span id="quote_sh600918"></span>、<span id="stock_sz002945"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002945/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">华林证券</a></span><span id="quote_sz002945"></span>、<span id="stock_sh601696"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601696/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">中银证券</a></span><span id="quote_sh601696"></span>、<span id="stock_sh601198"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601198/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">东兴证券</a></span><span id="quote_sh601198"></span>、<span id="stock_sh600621"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600621/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">华鑫股份</a></span><span id="quote_sh600621"></span></font>等股价由红盘翻绿。<font cms-style="strong-Bold">培育钻石、新型烟草、钠电池、CXO概念、盐湖提锂</font>等题材板块跌幅居前。</p>
<p>　　<font cms-style="strong-Bold"><span id="stock_sz000537"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000537/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">广宇发展</a></span><span id="quote_sz000537"></span></font>今日一字涨停。截至收盘，股价与历史高点仅差一分钱，最新市值突破400亿元。自宣布跨界转型之后，广宇发展一跃成为资金的宠儿，从去年的跌幅16%，到今年涨幅翻倍，该股完成股价逆袭。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/429/w550h679/20211206/033e-44b873d31afe349a19f5eaa1a9d9a030.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></div>
<p>　　12月6日早间，广宇发展公布《重大资产置换及重大资产出售暨关联交易报告书（草案）》，公告显示，<font cms-style="strong-Bold">公司将把旗下23家地产子公司出售或置换，以获取鲁能新能源100%股权</font>。此次资产处置完成后，公司主营业务将由原有房地产业务变更为新能源发电业务。</p>
<p>　　在本次重组之前，广宇发展已经面临经营困境。去年公司营收和净利润分别下滑14%、29.6%，今年前三季度，因受国内宏观经济环境、房地产行业环境、房地产调控信贷政策等叠加影响，本期交付项目数量、结转收入、利润较上年同期下降，加上计提资产减值准备等因素，前三季度业绩转为亏损状态。</p>
<p>　　今年前九个月，广宇发展股价并无起色，8月份一度创下六年来新低。<font cms-style="strong-Bold">证券时报·数据宝统计显示，自公司9月5日晚间首次发布筹划资产重组的提示公告，迄今过去近三个月时间，公司股价完成逆袭，期间累计上涨356.22%，全年累计涨幅265.28%</font>。</p>
<p cms-style="align-Center">　　<font cms-style="strong-Bold align-Center"><span id="stock_sh600734"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600734/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">*ST实达</a></span><span id="quote_sh600734"></span>股价七连板</font></p>
<p cms-style="align-Center">　　<font cms-style="strong-Bold align-Center">年内涨幅超2倍</font></p>
<p>　　数据宝统计显示，今日收盘涨停的股票不到70只，其中连续涨停的仅10只。连板股中有七成为ST股，如<font cms-style="strong-Bold"><span id="stock_sh600122"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600122/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">ST宏图</a></span><span id="quote_sh600122"></span>、<span id="stock_sh600260"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600260/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">ST凯乐</a></span><span id="quote_sh600260"></span><a href="https://wq.finance.sina.com.cn/company/detail/339" target="_blank">（维权）</a>、<span id="stock_sz002766"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002766/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">*ST索菱</a></span><span id="quote_sz002766"></span><a href="https://wq.finance.sina.com.cn/company/detail/193" target="_blank">（维权）</a>、<span id="stock_sz000835"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000835/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">*ST长动</a></span><span id="quote_sz000835"></span><a href="https://wq.finance.sina.com.cn/company/detail/246" target="_blank">（维权）</a>、<span id="stock_sz002356"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002356/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">*ST赫美</a></span><span id="quote_sz002356"></span><a href="https://wq.finance.sina.com.cn/company/detail/204" target="_blank">（维权）</a>、*ST实达、<span id="stock_sh600090"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600090/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">*ST济堂</a></span><span id="quote_sh600090"></span><a href="https://wq.finance.sina.com.cn/company/detail/273" target="_blank">（维权）</a></font>等。其中，*<font cms-style="strong-Bold">ST索菱、*ST实达</font>两者均录得七连板，是当前连续涨停次数最多的股票。与广宇发展一样，<font <div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/441/w550h691/20211206/c73f-3597107b9e697d561501adadfc463465.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></div>
<p>　　在A股市场上，能够长期处于上涨态势的股票并不多见，牛熊转换的现象时常出现。即便医药和食品饮料两大常青树板块，也难免出现被资金抛弃的情况。<font cms-style="strong-Bold"><span id="stock_sz300999"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300999/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">金龙鱼</a></span><span id="quote_sz300999"></span>、<span id="stock_sz000661"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000661/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">长春高新</a></span><span id="quote_sz000661"></span>、<span id="stock_sh603288"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603288/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">海天味业</a></span><span id="quote_sh603288"></span>、<span id="stock_sz300015"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300015/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">爱尔眼科</a></span><span id="quote_sz300015"></span>、<span id="stock_sz002304"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002304/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">洋河股份</a></span><span id="quote_sz002304"></span></font>等市值超千亿的消费股去年大受机构青睐，今年均出现了上涨乏力迹象。以“粮油茅”<font cms-style="strong-Bold">金龙鱼</font>为例，公司去年10月份登陆创业板，去年涨幅93.43%，今年1月份创下历史高点，但其后的第二、第三季度净利润均环比下滑，年内股价目前跌幅达到43.43%，最新市值回落到3317亿元。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/372/w531h641/20211206/3a50-63f00056cc5f713c2409da040eae1257.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></div>
<p cms-style="align-Center">　　<font cms-style="strong-Bold align-Center">130股完成股价逆袭</font></p>
<p cms-style="align-Center">　　<font cms-style="strong-Bold align-Center">化工行业居多</font></p>
<p>　　哪些股票今年完成逆袭了呢？数据宝统计显示，去年股价下跌，今年以来累计涨幅翻倍的股票，共计有130只。这些股票主要来自<font cms-style="strong-Bold">化工、机械设备、电子</font>行业，其中化工行业分布最广，共28股入围，如<font cms-style="strong-Bold"><span id="stock_sz300919"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300919/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">中伟股份</a></span><span id="quote_sz300919"></span>、<span id="stock_sz003022"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz003022/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">联泓新科</a></span><span id="quote_sz003022"></span>、<span id="stock_sh600256"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600256/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">广汇能源</a></span><span id="quote_sh600256"></span>、<span id="stock_sz000683"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000683/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">远兴能源</a></span><span id="quote_sz000683"></span>、<span id="stock_sh603077"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603077/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">和邦生物</a></span><span id="quote_sh603077"></span></font>等。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="5" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/84/w550h334/20211206/6e42-473259e5514a033caa068581513e4bb2.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></div>
<p>　　业内人士表示，行业景气度提升、业绩好转、加上政策催化，是上述130股股价逆袭的主要因素。钢铁行业的<font cms-style="strong-Bold"><span id="stock_sz000825"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000825/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">太钢不锈</a></span><span id="quote_sz000825"></span>、<span id="stock_sh600010"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600010/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">包钢股份</a></span><span id="quote_sh600010"></span></font>去年净利润均同比下滑，今年前三季度两者净利润分别同比增长5.35倍、21.6倍。化工行业的<font cms-style="strong-Bold">广汇能源、远兴能源、<span id="stock_sz000723"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000723/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">美锦能源</a></span><span id="quote_sz000723"></span>、和邦生物、<span id="stock_sz002274"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002274/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">华昌化工</a></span><span id="quote_sz002274"></span></font>等，去年净利润也为下滑状态，今年前三季度净利润均实现翻倍增长，其中<font cms-style="strong-Bold">华昌化工</font>净利润同比增幅达到56倍。</p>
<p>　　按照最新估值来看，不少股票仍处在较低估值水平。数据宝统计显示，上述130股中，16股滚动市盈率不到20倍，其中<font cms-style="strong-Bold"><span id="stock_sh688068"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688068/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">热景生物</a></span><span id="quote_sh688068"></span>、太钢不锈、<span id="stock_sh600746"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600746/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">江苏索普</a></span><span id="quote_sh600746"></span>、华昌化工、<span id="stock_sh603518"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603518/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">锦泓集团</a></span><span id="quote_sh603518"></span></font>等5股市盈率在个位数水平。市盈率最小的是<font cms-style="strong-Bold">热景生物</font>，目前滚动市盈率仅4.32倍。</p>
