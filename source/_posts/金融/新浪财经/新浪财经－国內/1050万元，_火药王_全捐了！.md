<div>   
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">近日</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">86岁的中国工程院院士王泽山</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">将他所获得的国家最高科学技术奖</font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">等</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">奖金共计</font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">1050万元</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">一次性捐赠给南京理工大学</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">用于奖励</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">在教学一线</font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">取得显著成效的教师</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">和学业优异的学生</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211203s/277/w1080h797/20211203/e6ed-21cf0c8a7ad160e96bbefefd38729323.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">潜心火炸药研究60多年</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">同行业的工作人员称王泽山为“火药王”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">而他自谦地说：“那是因为我姓王”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center color0">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">“国家需要的，</font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">就需要有人去做”</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">1935年，王泽山出生于吉林省吉林市</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">当时的东北正处于</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">日本侵略者的统治之下</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">家园沦陷让小小年纪的王泽山</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">懂得了</font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">“有国才能有家”</font></font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">的道理</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">高中毕业后，</font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">王泽山</font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">报考了</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">中国人民解放军军事工程学院（即哈军工）</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">全班不到20名学生当中</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">他是唯一自愿学习火炸药的人</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211203s/403/w636h567/20211203/3dcf-32596cf43207a34fef7b733ea0103d34.jpg" alt="△大学时期的王泽山" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr">△大学时期的王泽山</span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">火炸药研究领域狭窄、危险性高</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">但是王泽山坚信</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">“国家需要的，就需要有人去做！”</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">从此，研究火炸药</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">成了他的终身使命</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center color0">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">“我的生活</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center color0">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">已经跟科研分不开了”</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">王泽山科学研究的突破</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">从“废弃火炸药再利用”开始</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">他率先攻克了</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">废弃火炸药再利用的多项关键技术</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">把国家每年上万吨退役或废弃的火炸药</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">变成了二十余种</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">畅销国内外的军用和民用产品</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">他的研究成果</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">填补了该领域的技术空白</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper">
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211203s/768/w480h288/20211203/ef61-c34a7d8e6fc06f2d22b2ec247a027e1e.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
</div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">此后，王泽山向国际上难以攻克的</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">尖端技术发起挑战</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">通过研究发射药燃烧的补偿理论</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">王泽山发现了低温感含能材料</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">经过反复实验验证</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">采用这种低温感发射装药</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">武器膛压的温度感度</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">可</font></font>降低到3%以下</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">发射威力提高15%以上</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211203s/309/w639h470/20211203/200b-77e700faa2e7e42bcd83f01912d7ba88.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">凭借着火炸药领域的杰出贡献</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">王泽山</font>曾获得国家科学技术进步奖一等奖</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">并两次获得国家技术发明奖一等奖</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">他还获得了</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">中国科技界的最高荣誉</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">2017年度国家最高科学技术奖</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211203s/619/w400h219/20211203/f459-73a752a3572c799b865fb4e39a9e6f0e.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">王泽山认为，自己能力有限</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">一辈子只能做好一件事情</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">他说：“我除了能做火炸药研究</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">别的都不擅长</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">我的生活已经跟科研分不开了”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">曾经，矢志科研创新</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">如今，倾囊培育人才</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">每一份慷慨背后都有一颗赤子心</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">向王泽山院士致敬！</font></font></p>
