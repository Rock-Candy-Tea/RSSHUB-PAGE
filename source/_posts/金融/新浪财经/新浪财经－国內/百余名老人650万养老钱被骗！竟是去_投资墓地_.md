<div>   
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　近日</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　安徽合肥警方破获一起</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color240">以“投资墓地”为噱头</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color240">专门骗取老人“养老钱”的非法集资案</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　涉案金额超650万元</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　被骗老年人多达110余人</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/512/w300h212/20220518/df92-bafedcaf8a2293124d2f1e6dcc4e6273.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　2022年4月份</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　警方陆续接到群众报警</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　称在某公司投资墓地被骗数万元不等</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　经查</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　程某、季某招募人员</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在菜市场、超市门口</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　通过摆设展台、散发传单等方式</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color240">向老年人推荐“墓地销售”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color240">及“墓园投资”项目</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/125/w550h375/20220518/bd6d-6b6d0dbee37ea9b014ed252bc16de546.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　程某等人向老年人鼓吹</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　墓地投资利润丰厚</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color240">并公开宣称</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color240">“投资年化利率高达18%-30%”不等</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/63/w550h313/20220518/a0ad-a778eab033237a8e1dec63a92dabadad.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一些老人面对程某等人的</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　巧舌如簧，失去了戒备心</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　相信墓地投资可以稳赚不赔</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color240">纷纷把自己多年积攒的</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color240">养老钱交给程某</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一开始</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　参与“投资”的老人们</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　还可以每月领取利息</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　但半年后</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　程某等人就无法兑现承诺</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color240">一些老人感觉情况不对</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color240">随后开始报案</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　犯罪嫌疑人程某到案后</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　如实供述了自己通过</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“投资墓地”非法集资的犯罪事实</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　经查，程某在外地农村</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　经营一小型墓地</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　因位置偏僻</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　墓地销售情况惨淡</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color240">于是，程某便打起了</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color240">“让老年人来投资墓地”的歪主意</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">13日，</font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">程某、季某因非法吸收公众存款</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　被合肥市公安局高新分局</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　采取刑事强制措施</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　目前</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　已经追回部分受害人的投资款</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　案件正在进一步侦查中</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这些常见的养老骗局</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　转发提醒家中的老年人</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color240">一旦发现家中老人被骗</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color240">请及时拨打110报警</font></p>
