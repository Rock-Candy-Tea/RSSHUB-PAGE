<div>   
<p>　　来源：经济参考报</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在即将过去的2021年，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">“元宇宙”</font></font>和<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">“双碳”“破防”</font></font>等</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　成为网络热词。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/379/w550h629/20211231/95cf-8f507f213b958098193729b37fc18740.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　元宇宙资本、元宇宙社交、元宇宙旅游、</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　元宇宙音乐、元宇宙房产……</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/gif_image/390/w240h150/20211231/1866-fcfe38e69a7d7c3baedc82fe6324731e.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/266/w133h133/20211231/36b6-fbb7bb5dbcfcf66f7ff2a4b6b96e333f.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　从<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">科技巨头</font></font>跑步入场，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　到资本市场相关<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">概念股</font></font>持续暴涨，</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/480/w240h240/20211231/e180-6487eeb1deecce5427f2eb0d279b408e.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　对此，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　有人充满向往，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　有人保持观望，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　那么什么是元宇宙？</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">为何受到如此关注呢？</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　《经济参考报》记者，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　来到清华大学<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">元宇宙实验室，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　带您<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">沉浸式地体验</font></font>一下“元宇宙的世界”，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一起“浸入”“烧脑之旅”！</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　Go~~</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="5" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/34/w550h284/20211231/3825-e212508cc4bf1ce7579a3ea76d47803f.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="6" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/gif_image/638/w408h230/20211231/5e39-f17304ef92dd531c029c648f8374f3c3.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在元宇宙的想象中，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　人可以置身网络，</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="7" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/28/w550h278/20211231/ce9a-6eeabc915940d5a2abda7e666b5fa387.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">虚拟和现实</font></font>会变得密切融合。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="8" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/53/w550h303/20211231/ede0-b93b4d1a970e0812a53d225669e6589d.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　真人、虚拟人、<span id="stock_sz300024"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300024/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">机器人</a></span><span id="quote_sz300024"></span>，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　都会出现在同一时空中，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　社交方式也会发生巨大的变化。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="9" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/gif_image/638/w408h230/20211231/b785-9857eeca5794134c76aeb307871bd509.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　想喝奶茶？</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　走，去元宇宙里！ </p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="10" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/gif_image/638/w408h230/20211231/6440-4ec233eb1273a705a26107646643e645.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">清华大学新闻与传播学院新媒体研究中心沈阳教授表示，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　元宇宙概念<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">为何爆火</font></font>呢？</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　因为它具有很强的“科幻色彩”。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="11" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/gif_image/638/w408h230/20211231/a8d3-7f4a2877aacdf052b86c3c91d58d5a00.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　社会进步技术为本，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　但近些年互联网领域的技术概念有些枯竭；</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　普通用户对手机审美疲劳，期待新鲜事物；</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　资本缺乏新热点，需要新方向。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　但这里面要区分：</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">科学技术意义上的元宇宙、科幻中的元宇宙、泡沫中的元宇宙。</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="12" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/63/w550h313/20211231/90d0-98ccfb071002ce9e5cd0cd507fc3584c.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在“元宇宙“的想象中，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">扩展现实、区块链、数字孪生、人工智能</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">等</font>技术会变得更好应用，</p>
<div class="img_wrapper">
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="13" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/gif_image/706/w500h206/20211231/f8bb-3de587c3fb672e8b85e816d248a60348.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
</div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center strong-Bold">　　（行走的故宫文化）</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="14" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/48/w550h298/20211231/a92d-232dec6e7317dea69b9418ba3f0df712.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　旅行、购物、游戏娱乐．．．．．．</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　它承载了人们美好的交互愿景。 </p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　对于“元宇宙”概念在资本市场的持续火热，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">申银万国证券研究所首席市场专家桂浩明认为：</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　现阶段只有短线在关注，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　中线开始调研，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　但是能否持续，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　取决于许多方面：</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　如：技术的突破和政策的配合等。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0"> 建议：不要盲目追捧，要理性思考。</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="15" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/40/w550h290/20211231/f146-443e752aec86f084e36ce81e9a4e6bfb.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　记者发现，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　当前市场上存在部分打着<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">“元宇宙区块链游戏”、</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“云挖矿”等高科技幌子<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">诈骗钱财的APP。</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　不法分子谎称APP科技含量高、赚钱效果好，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　引人上钩，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　致使不少用户</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　跌入陷阱、遭受损失。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="16" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/347/w550h597/20211231/f7ff-88c7f2efe846dff41d8fe1692a946132.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">中央财经大学金融法研究所所长黄震表示，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　中国人民银行等多部门早有规定，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　无论是通过代“挖矿”或者其他形式的“挖矿”APP，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　只要有所谓的激励机制的代币或积分形式出现，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　且要通过货币或金融来进行变现交易的方式，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">都是属于非法集资或金融诈骗活动，同样构成犯罪。</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="17" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/gif_image/600/w300h300/20211231/d9c3-a7cc9dfc503a51b377e84772dcedcfa2.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　无论元宇宙距离现实有多远，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　我们都应该要把目光聚焦到科技本身，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　聚焦到现实发展本身。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“兴奋”的同时，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">要记得停下来，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">思考一下正确的方向。</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　你认为“元宇宙”会是</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　互联网的下一站吗？</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　别忘了，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　给我们留言、互动~~</p>
