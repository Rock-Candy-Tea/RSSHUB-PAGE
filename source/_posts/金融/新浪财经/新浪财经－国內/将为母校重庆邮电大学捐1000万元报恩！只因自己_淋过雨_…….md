<p>　　原标题：将为母校捐1000万元报恩！只因自己“淋过雨”……</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　12月17日下午</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　重庆邮电大学2020-2021学年</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　社会奖学奖教金颁奖仪式举行</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　多家捐赠企业代表参会</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　报道指出</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　今年，该校增设了</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">“照远承恩”基金</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">捐赠者是学校96级电信系</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">校友王军</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211221s/799/w1024h575/20211221/4b88-2f3e64429b04d8555641c4e57ba98862.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L">“照远承恩”基金颁奖现场</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这个奖学金背后</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　还有个不得不说的故事</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">基金名称来自父母名字</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">提醒学弟学妹要感恩</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　近日，重庆邮电大学</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">校友王军首次捐赠100万元</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">在母校设立奖学金</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">以10年为期</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">将累计捐赠1000万元</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　回报求学时学校和老师</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　给予自己的鼓励和关怀</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　据介绍</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">“照远承恩”基金的名字</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">是王军自己取的</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">父亲名叫王照娃、母亲名叫陈远慧</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">他将双亲姓名中各取一字</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“照远承恩”的意思是</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　感念父母的养育之恩</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　捐赠给母校是铭记母校</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　及老师们的培养关怀之情</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211221s/768/w440h328/20211221/1455-f7da6aae7a7dd54fb359120157114f6d.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　“照远承恩”基金是存本基金，每年孳息用于“照远承恩”奖学金的发放。“照远承恩”基金分为“科技创新”与“超越自我”两个大类，最多每年可奖励20名学生，获奖学生奖金1万元。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　其中，“科技创新”奖是在校期间立德修身、勇于创新，具有一定学术科研意识，具备较强学术科研素养和能力，在各类技术相关开源社区、组织领域等，或在科技竞赛、科技活动及学术论文发表等方面有较突出表现；“超越自我”奖是在校期间自立自强、发挥特长、追求卓越、乐于助人，在文化、艺术、体育、美育、劳育等竞赛活动中获得高级别奖励和荣誉，对身边同学有积极的引领和示范作用。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　王军也希望通过这个奖</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　助推学弟学妹早日成才</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　活岀最精彩的自己</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">“只因自己淋过雨，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">所以总想替别人撑伞”</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　据报道</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　捐赠人王军是来自新疆农场的学生</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　大二期间，王军突遭家庭变故</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　父亲不幸去世</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　母亲靠着卖冰棍、捡马粪</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　独力抚育兄妹二人</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这个时候他消沉敏感</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　是母校的老师一直鼓励他</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　给他精神动力</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　并通过学校的贫困救助</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　给他很多资助让他安心学习</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　并以优异成绩毕业</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211221s/287/w440h647/20211221/02aa-4b068b940304a2958fbbae8da566335b.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　王军曾经的老师黎伟回忆</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在校期间王军自强上进勤奋刻苦</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　毕业后以优异成绩进入大厂工作</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　因为对母校心怀感激</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　所以此次以个人名义捐赠1000万元</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　把“照远承恩”基金设立在母校</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　读大学期间</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　王军为了帮助更困难的同学</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　还曾主动提出将补助让出来</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　现在王军为母校学生设立奖学金</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　黎伟并不吃惊</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">“因为自己淋过雨，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">所以他总想替别人撑伞。”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">在评论区，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">这段故事感动了无数网友：</font></font></p>
