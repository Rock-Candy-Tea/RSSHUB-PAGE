<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">10月24日</font></p>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color240">外国舰机对我编队进行跟踪</font></p>

<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L">中俄海军首次海上联合巡航期间，日自卫队舰艇（右）对俄大型反潜舰（左下）和中方舰载直升机（左上）联合训练进行跟踪侦察（10月23日摄）。</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211026s/7/w1040h567/20211026/ae41-a2a26a46e780e8e3fad91fe44a12c4e8.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L">中俄海军首次海上联合巡航期间，日自卫队舰艇（右）对中方滨州舰（左）进行跟踪侦察（10月21日摄）。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">此次中俄海军海上联合巡航期间</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　南昌舰战士</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">用英语对外国舰机“硬核”喊话：</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">“我是中国海军101舰，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">我正在航行，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">与我保持安全距离。”</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">据悉</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">这是南昌舰今年第四次突破岛链</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">现场视频↓万吨大驱南昌舰战士，用英文喊话外国舰机</font></font></font></p>
