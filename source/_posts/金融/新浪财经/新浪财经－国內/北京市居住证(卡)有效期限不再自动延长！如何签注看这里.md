<p>　　原标题：北京市居住证（卡）有效期限不再自动延长！如何签注看这里</p>
<p>　　来源：首都之窗</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">大家都知道北京市居住证很重要</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">北京市居住证是小伙伴们在北京居住</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">作为常住人口享受基本公共服务和便利</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">通过积分申请登记常住户口的证明</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">2020年2月28日</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">北京市公安局推出了疫情防控期间</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">《北京市居住证》和《北京市居住登记卡》</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">有效期限自动延长的便民措施</font></p>

<p cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">要注意的是</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">市公安局自2021年6月1日起</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">停止执行</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">“居住证（卡）有效期限自动延长”</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">的临时措施</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">恢复居住证（卡）正常签注的要求</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">有效期限截止日期为</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">2020年1月1日（含）以后</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">目前仍未办理签注手续的</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">居住证（卡）持有人</font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">及时办理签注</font></p>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">北京市居住证签注</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">签注时间</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L"><font cms-style="font-L">按照前期时限规定，</font><font cms-style="font-L color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold color0">签注补办有效期限为两个月</font></font><font cms-style="font-L color0">，</font></font><font cms-style="font-L">2021年6月1日起至2021年8月1日（含），逾期未签注超1个月将停止居住证（卡）功能；逾期未签注超6个月将注销居住证（卡）。</font></font></p>
