<div>   
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　著名血液学专家</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　中国工程院院士王振义</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　今年已98岁高龄</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　但他仍坚持每周查房</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">早年间，他放弃申请专利</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">一盒救命药至今仅售290元</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">无数网友为之动容：</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">这就是医者仁心！</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220204s/416/w695h521/20220204/8816-0d5cd160e61bdc21a8c613f1e8575c11.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">“首先要解决病人的问题</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">我只想病人能好”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　上世纪80年代末</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　急性早幼粒细胞白血病</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　还是白血病中最凶险的一种</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　王振义为解决这一问题</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　带领团队攻坚克难，奋斗8年</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一改“杀死肿瘤细胞时</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　损害正常细胞”的疗法</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　引导肿瘤细胞“弃邪归正”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　让急性早幼粒细胞白血病</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　成为人类肿瘤治疗历史上</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　首个可被治愈的肿瘤</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　为了使更多病人得到救治</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　王老一开始就放弃了专利申请</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　而是将其公开</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　让全世界的患者受益</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">在我国，这盒10粒装的白血病“救命药”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">至今仅售290元，还纳入了医保</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">他说：“首先要解决病人的问题</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">我只想病人能好”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">98岁仍坚持“开卷考试”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　今年98岁的王振义院士</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　仍坚守岗位</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　他把查房称为“开卷考试”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　每周，他都会针对收治的病人案例</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　和学生提出的疑难杂症</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　查阅资料后和大家讨论处理方案</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　有时候，为了一个病例</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　王老要花3天时间查阅200多篇文献</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　做出30多页PPT</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　自2003年起，这样的“开卷考试”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　王老风雨无阻地进行至今</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“开卷考试是我很大的享受</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　开卷考试回来后</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　觉得我今天的工作做完了”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在一次查房的前一天</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　王老不慎摔伤了腰</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　当时，已经97岁的他</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　坚持在服用止痛片后参与查房</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220204s/748/w480h268/20220204/3331-f3345537e40d83c05a372b8e9f953d13.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">他一生捐款超千万元</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">把给病人捐款写进遗嘱</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　2020年，王振义院士获得</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　未来科学大奖“生命科学奖”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　他表示更希望把奖颁给年轻人</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　并把百万元奖金全部捐给扶贫基金会</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这些年，他获得的奖金超过千万元</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　几乎全部捐了出去</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　王老一生节俭</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　现在还住在医院提供的单元房里</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　王老说，等他百年之后</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这套房子是要还给医院的</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　他和医院签订了合同，子女不能继承</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　他还把给病人捐款的愿望写在了遗嘱里</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　王振义院士曾说</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“要有进取的雄心</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　像牡丹一样开得热烈</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　但要对名利看得很淡”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">王老今年许下的生日愿望仍是</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">“为人类做出一些贡献”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">致敬！</font></p>
