<p cms-style="font-L">　　割肉的要哭了？行业龙头最高跌逾30% 北上资金豪掷超6亿大买它，聪明资金逆市加仓股名单来了</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　北上资金逆市加仓这些股票。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　本周A股三大指数全线下跌，整体跌幅不算大，沪指周跌幅1%左右。个股涨跌方面，本周区间内上涨的个股共有2300家左右，而本周区间内下跌的个股则有1800余家，<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">其中数家行业龙头的跌幅较大，比如<span id="stock_sz002044"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002044/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">美年健康</a></span><span id="quote_sz002044"></span>、<span id="stock_sh600521"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600521/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">华海药业</a></span><span id="quote_sh600521"></span>、<span id="stock_sz300001"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300001/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">特锐德</a></span><span id="quote_sz300001"></span>、<span id="stock_sz300124"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300124/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">汇川技术</a></span><span id="quote_sz300124"></span>、<span id="stock_sz002352"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002352/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">顺丰控股</a></span><span id="quote_sz002352"></span>等。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　北上资金一周累计成交2766.17亿元，成交净卖出53.26亿元，分市场看，北上资金主要减持沪市个股，深股通合计净买入4.32亿元，沪股通合计净卖出57.58亿元。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　这已经是北上资金连续4个交易日净卖出了。值得欣慰的是，净卖出金额逐渐减少，最后一日降低到10亿元之内。</p>
<p cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">轻工制造行业获北上资金重点加仓</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　证券时报•数据宝统计显示，本周北上资金持股量环比增加的有11个行业，轻工制造行业环比增幅最大，北上资金最新持有该行业10.05亿股，相比上周末增长2.34%。环比增幅居前的有综合、国防军工、化工、机械设备等行业。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　17个申万行业遭北上资金减仓，公用事业行业减仓最为明显，减仓幅度为3.07%，其他减仓较多的行业还包括纺织服装、汽车等。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　从行业整体来看，近期北上资金操作呈现一定的短周期风格，<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">本周重仓的轻工制造、综合是上周的重点减仓方向，</font>在大盘方向不明朗的时候，北上资金进行调仓的过程中出现一定规模的短期投资也符合横盘走势的策略。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/164/w550h414/20210411/a322-knqqqmu5755116.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">白酒板块遭连续抛售，</font></p>
<p cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">机械龙头<span id="stock_sh600031"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600031/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">三一重工</a></span><span id="quote_sh600031"></span>获外资重仓</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　本周共有29只个股上榜成交活跃榜榜单，所属行业来看，本周上榜的成交活跃股中，食品饮料行业最为集中，有7只个股上榜。成交金额最多的是<span id="stock_sh600519"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600519/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">贵州茅台</a></span><span id="quote_sh600519"></span>，本周累计成交79.98亿元；其次是<span id="stock_sz000858"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000858/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">五粮液</a></span><span id="quote_sz000858"></span>，累计成交金额51.64亿元；成交金额较高的还有<span id="stock_sz000725"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000725/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">京东方A</a></span><span id="quote_sz000725"></span>、<span id="stock_sh601888"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601888/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">中国中免</a></span><span id="quote_sh601888"></span>、<span id="stock_sh601012"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601012/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">隆基股份</a></span><span id="quote_sh601012"></span>等。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　以净买卖金额统计，本周上榜的个股中，共有13股为净买入，<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">净买入金额最多的是三一重工，本周净买入金额为6.85亿元</font>，其次是<span id="stock_sh603288"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603288/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">海天味业</a></span><span id="quote_sh603288"></span>，净买入金额为3.01亿元，其他净买入金额居前的有<span id="stock_sz300750"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300750/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">宁德时代</a></span><span id="quote_sz300750"></span>、<span id="stock_sz002475"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002475/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">立讯精密</a></span><span id="quote_sz002475"></span>等。净卖出个股中，净卖出金额最多的是两市白酒龙头贵州茅台，本周净卖出额为36.51亿元。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　三一重工是机械设备龙头股，股价自春节后随大盘连续回调，最高跌逾30%。随着国内经济加速恢复，对工程机械的需求持续大增，行业整体向好。<span id="stock_sz000728"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000728/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">国元证券</a></span><span id="quote_sz000728"></span>表示，跟踪上游零部件厂商排产数据也可看出，4月挖机油缸、泵阀订单火爆，行业需求仍呈上行趋势，预计2021年二季度工程机械景气度仍将保持在较高的水平。地产、基建投资韧性十足，环保更新需求支撑挖机需求持续向好。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　继上周后，两市龙头贵州茅台带领白酒集体被外资抛售，周活跃的白酒股无一例外成为北上资金抛售的重点股。此外，此前几周尚显活跃的“碳中和”概念股本周也被不同程度的卖出，热度有所降低。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/97/w550h347/20210411/4f55-knqqqmu5755168.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　本周北上资金加仓股共714只，以持股数量增长幅度来看，加仓幅度超50%的股票有36只，超100%的股票有15只。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<span id="stock_sz300080"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300080/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">易成新能</a></span><span id="quote_sz300080"></span>的加仓力度最大，最新持股数为209.48万股，与上周的持股数量相比，增长167.1万股，持股数量增长幅度为394.24%；紧随其后是<span id="stock_sz002135"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002135/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">东南网架</a></span><span id="quote_sz002135"></span>，加仓幅度为378.43%；其他加仓幅度靠前的还有<span id="stock_sh603982"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603982/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">泉峰汽车</a></span><span id="quote_sh603982"></span>、<span id="stock_sz002978"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002978/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">安宁股份</a></span><span id="quote_sz002978"></span>、<span id="stock_sz300318"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300318/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">博晖创新</a></span><span id="quote_sz300318"></span>、<span id="stock_sh600860"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600860/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">京城股份</a></span><span id="quote_sh600860"></span>等。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　从市场表现来看，本周北上资金加仓居前的20股中有17股上涨，<span id="stock_sz000793"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000793/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">华闻集团</a></span><span id="quote_sz000793"></span>本周股价上涨26.11%，排名第一，涨幅较大的还有<span id="stock_sz300458"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300458/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">全志科技</a></span><span id="quote_sz300458"></span>、<span id="stock_sz300009"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300009/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">安科生物</a></span><span id="quote_sz300009"></span>、易成新能、京城股份等。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　北上资金减仓幅度超50%的有7股，<span id="stock_sz300366"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300366/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">创意信息</a></span><span id="quote_sz300366"></span>、<span id="stock_sh601127"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601127/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">小康股份</a></span><span id="quote_sh601127"></span>、<span id="stock_sz000761"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000761/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">本钢板材</a></span><span id="quote_sz000761"></span>等股的减仓力度靠前。</p>

<p cms-style="strong-Bold font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">北上资金连续7周买入19股</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　北上资金连续四周买入90股。分行业来看，医药生物行业个股较多，有12只。其中，19股被北上资金连续7周加仓。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　从持股比例变动情况来看，<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold"><span id="stock_sz002918"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002918/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">蒙娜丽莎</a></span><span id="quote_sz002918"></span>获加仓幅度最大，较7周前增持股数占流通股的比例为3.75%</font>；其他连续加仓的还有<span id="stock_sz002050"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002050/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">三花智控</a></span><span id="quote_sz002050"></span>、<span id="stock_sz002563"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002563/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">森马服饰</a></span><span id="quote_sz002563"></span>、<span id="stock_sh603489"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603489/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">八方股份</a></span><span id="quote_sh603489"></span>、<span id="stock_sz300496"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300496/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">中科创达</a></span><span id="quote_sz300496"></span>等。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　北上资金连续4周卖出114股。分行业来看，医药生物个股数量居前，有15只。从持股比例变动情况来看，与4周前相比，21股减仓比例超1%。<span id="stock_sz002973"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002973/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">侨银股份</a></span><span id="quote_sz002973"></span>减仓幅度最大，相比4周前减仓了6.5%，其他减仓幅度较大的还有<span id="stock_sz300274"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300274/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">阳光电源</a></span><span id="quote_sz300274"></span>、<span id="stock_sz002607"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002607/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">中公教育</a></span><span id="quote_sz002607"></span>、<span id="stock_sz002568"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002568/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">百润股份</a></span><span id="quote_sz002568"></span>等。</p>
