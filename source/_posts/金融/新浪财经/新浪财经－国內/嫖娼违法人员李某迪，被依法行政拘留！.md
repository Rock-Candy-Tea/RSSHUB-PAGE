<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　近日</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　朝阳公安分局接群众举报称</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　有人在朝阳某小区卖淫嫖娼</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　对此，警方依法开展调查</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　将卖淫违法人员陈某卉（女，29岁）</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　嫖娼违法人员李某迪（男，39岁）查获</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　经审查</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　上述人员对违法事实供认不讳</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　目前均已被朝阳公安分局</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　依法行政拘留</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　北京市公安局官方微博</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　@平安北京 发文写道：</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">“这个世界的确不止黑白两色，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">但一定要分清和划清黑与白。</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color330"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color330">这个，绝对不可以错……”</font></font></p>
