<p class="ori_titlesource">　　原标题：清华人，到底有多牛？</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color30"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color30">水木清华，钟灵毓秀</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color30"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color30">行健不息，薪火相传</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　今天，清华大学迎来110岁生日</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“清华人最高的荣耀</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　就是把自己奋斗的足迹印刻在</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　民族复兴的伟大征程上”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　让个人奋斗与国家发展同频共振</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一代代清华人不懈努力</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在各自领域中拼搏奋斗</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　↓↓↓</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">清华人钱学森：</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">“为国家民族做事就是最高奖赏”</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">钱学森</font>是中国航天事业的奠基人</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“两弹一星”元勋</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　1935年，他以航空系第一名的成绩</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　考取清华大学留美公费生</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　虽然身在美国</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　但钱学森初心不改，赤诚报国</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　美国人说他抵得上5个师</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　宁可枪毙也不让回国</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　他被软禁5年，用香烟纸发出求助信</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　周总理过问才得以脱身……</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　五年归国路，十年两弹成</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　钱老说：</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">“人民说我为国家、民族做了点事</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">就是最高奖赏”</font></p>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">清华人钱三强：</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">“科学没国界，科学家有祖国”</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">钱三强</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　中国原子能科学事业创始人</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“两弹一星”元勋</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　1948年，钱三强放弃法国优越生活回到祖国</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　倾注全部心血培养新一代学科带头人</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">获“两弹一星”功勋科学家荣誉的23人中</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">有15人由他动员回国</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">其中7人由他直接推荐到核武器研制一线</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　他曾说：</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color30">“<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color30">虽然科学没有国界</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color30"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color30">科学家却是有祖国的”</font></font></p>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">清华人邓稼先：</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">“如果有来世，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">我还是选择核武器事业”</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　邓稼先</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　中国第一颗原子弹和氢弹</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　理论方案设计者</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　1950年，邓稼先毅然决定</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">从美国回到百废待兴的新中国</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">接到秘密任务的他“消失”了28年</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　1964年10月16日</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　中国第一颗原子弹试验成功</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　1967年6月17日</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　中国第一颗氢弹又在罗布泊上空爆响</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　被授予“两弹一星功勋奖章”时</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　邓稼先已辞世整整13年</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　病危临终前，他曾对爱人说：</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color30"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color30">“要是有来世，我还是选择中国</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color30"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color30">选择核武器事业，选择你！”</font></font></p>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">清华人郭永怀：</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">他用血肉之躯保护绝密文件</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">郭永怀</font>，“两弹一星”元勋</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　60多年前，郭永怀拒绝美国军方邀请</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　回国投身核弹事业</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　并在清华主持开办力学研究班</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　培养尖端国防科技人才</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">1968年12月5日</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">飞机失事的最后10秒</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">郭永怀与警卫员紧紧抱在一起</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">用血肉之驱保护装有绝密文件的公文包</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　22天后，依据这份绝密文件</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　中国第一枚热核武器试验成功</p>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">清华人南仁东：</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">他放弃300倍工资让中国领先20年</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">南仁东</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“中国天眼”的主要发起者和奠基人</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">1994年</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">他放弃了高于国内300倍的薪资</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">毅然返回中国</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　决心建一个世界上最大的</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　单口径球面射电望远镜（FAST）</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　2015年，确诊肺癌的他</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　术后依然忍着病痛返回施工现场</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在南仁东和他的团队不懈努力下</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　终于在2016年9月25日</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　使FAST成功落成启动</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在“天眼”启用将满一年时</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　72岁的南仁东化为天上繁星</p>
