<p>　　原标题：曾霸气减租80万！深圳这位房东再上热搜→</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　还记着前年疫情期间</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　主动为租客减租80万元的</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color30"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color30"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color30">“深圳最美房东”林萍君</font></font></font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">吗？</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　最近她坚持给防疫人员送汤</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　又上热搜了！</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20220118/462/w1080h182/20220118/e6d2-4d5cd914d48b3ee1523eaec2e6461ab7.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center color0">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">一天送出去120份汤每次都换不同花样</font></p>
<!--article_adlist[<p cms-style="font-L"><img src="//n.sinaimg.cn/spider20220118/42/w20h22/20220118/6eba-0dd7abf6aef7bd7b3fed6fefdb98a6ce.png" style="max-width:640px" cms-width="14" cms-height="15.3906"/></p>]article_adlist-->
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　近日，深圳出现疫情</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在龙岗区坂田街道的林萍君</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　利用工作闲暇</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　主动为抗疫人员煲汤、送饺子</p>
<!--video-list-->

<div class="video-2017" id="videoList0" style></div>

<!--/video-list-->
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　1月13日中午</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　林萍君来到核酸采样点</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　将一碗碗热汤端给一线抗疫人员</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这次她送来的是猪肉白菜豆腐汤</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color30">因为担心工作人员吃得不方便</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color30">林萍君还细心地提前把骨头捞出来</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　晚上，她还给工作人员送饺子</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　并配上了老干妈和醋</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20220118/320/w640h480/20220118/7ae0-86f38007bce3b3318236058f498e9a3e.jpg" alt="林萍君送爱心餐饮到疫情防控一线" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr">林萍君送爱心餐饮到疫情防控一线</span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　实际上，从1月7日开始</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　林萍君就已经为抗疫人员送餐了</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　7日当天</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　她煲的是姜、鸡蛋、红枣和枸杞茶</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　林萍君说：</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“一大早我就去菜市场买菜</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　买了20多斤姜、红枣、枸杞</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　和150颗鸡蛋”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　当天上午和晚上各送了60份</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　此后，她又接连送了潮汕粿品</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　饺子、不同品种的汤等</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20220118/134/w640h1094/20220118/8339-237299254b3e68d9a05b91e9d7630ec8.jpg" alt="林萍君的朋友圈" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr">林萍君的朋友圈</span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　林萍君每天变着法子调整餐食</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　比如天气冷了，就煲骨头汤</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　加一点胡椒粉和指天椒</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　帮抗疫人员暖暖身子</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　喝着热汤的抗疫人员说：</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color30">“吃在嘴里，暖在心里！</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color30">这些爱心餐点让我们在一线力量更足”</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20220118/541/w800h541/20220118/cba4-9c43eb58fa443be0dab6cefe5c90eb3e.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<!--article_adlist[<p cms-style="font-L"><img src="//n.sinaimg.cn/spider20220118/41/w20h21/20220118/cc12-bc60910536f0bc6276d617042cf73977.png" style="max-width:640px" cms-width="14" cms-height="14.6875"/></p>]article_adlist-->
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center color0">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">前年疫情期间她顶着家庭压力减租约80万</font></p>
<!--article_adlist[<p cms-style="font-L"><img src="//n.sinaimg.cn/spider20220118/42/w20h22/20220118/6eba-0dd7abf6aef7bd7b3fed6fefdb98a6ce.png" style="max-width:640px" cms-width="14" cms-height="15.3906"/></p>]article_adlist-->
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　早在2020年疫情初期</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　林萍君就做了一件让大家惊讶的事</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　她主动为租户减免了约80万元的租金</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　被网友称赞为“最美房东”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　林萍君说：</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“当时减租80万，家庭是有压力的</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　但在那个时候就是想做点什么”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　网友纷纷为她点赞：</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“最美房东”“有心人”</p>
<!--article_adlist[<p cms-style="font-L align-Center"><img src="//n.sinaimg.cn/spider20220118/48/w24h24/20220118/550a-c55c7f5370fa9f646cb5bc539eef9595.gif" style="max-width:640px" cms-width="24" cms-height="24"/><img src="//n.sinaimg.cn/spider20220118/48/w24h24/20220118/550a-c55c7f5370fa9f646cb5bc539eef9595.gif" style="max-width:640px" cms-width="24" cms-height="24"/><img src="//n.sinaimg.cn/spider20220118/48/w24h24/20220118/550a-c55c7f5370fa9f646cb5bc539eef9595.gif" style="max-width:640px" cms-width="24" cms-height="24"/></p>]article_adlist-->
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20220118/539/w1080h1059/20220118/fd55-f452069fe7ec0056da3947bf0b7cd25d.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　林萍君老家在汕头潮阳</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　她在深圳坂田工作生活了近30年</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　她说自己的成绩</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　是坂田人民给她的</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color30">“吃水不忘挖井人</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color30">我的一个心愿就是</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color30">感恩和回馈坂田的居民”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　林萍君说</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　不管别人叫她“最美老板”还是“最美房东”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　她更愿意别人叫她一声</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">“最美深圳义工”</font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center"></font></p>
