<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　寻找方向，</p>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　坚决不欠员工一分钱工资；</p>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在底部积蓄力量；</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　有的顺势而为，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　意外打开了一扇窗；</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　困难重重，不言放弃，</p>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　没有迈不过去的坎；</p>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　面朝大海，</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　春暖花开！</p>
