<p cms-style="font-L">　　下周解禁股名单出炉。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">2股解禁市值超百亿</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　下周解禁压力相对较小，将有37只股解禁，按照最新收盘价计算，合计解禁市值615.64亿元。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L">37股中，<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">解禁市值规模最大的是<span id="stock_sz300433"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300433/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">蓝思科技</a></span><span id="quote_sz300433"></span>，下周将有5.9亿股上市流通，</font>主要是定向增发机构配售股份，解禁市值高达148.29亿元。</font><font cms-style="font-L"><span id="stock_sh688050"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688050/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">爱博医疗</a></span><span id="quote_sh688050"></span>的解禁市值也超过百亿，达到132.82亿元，解禁股为首发原股东限售股份和首发战略配售股份。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　有12股的解禁压力较小，解禁市值均不足亿元。<span id="stock_sz300606"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300606/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">金太阳</a></span><span id="quote_sz300606"></span>的解禁市值最小，将有21.81万元的市值规模面临解禁。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　从解禁股数量占总股本的比例来看，<span id="stock_sh603657"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603657/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">春光科技</a></span><span id="quote_sh603657"></span>的解禁比例居首，达到70.18%，爱博医疗、<span id="stock_sz300857"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300857/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">协创数据</a></span><span id="quote_sz300857"></span>、<span id="stock_sz300853"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300853/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">申昊科技</a></span><span id="quote_sz300853"></span>和<span id="stock_sh688586"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688586/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">江航装备</a></span><span id="quote_sh688586"></span>等股的解禁比例居前。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　解禁股数量占总股本比例不足1%的有9股。其中<span id="stock_sz300602"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300602/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">飞荣达</a></span><span id="quote_sz300602"></span>、<span id="stock_sh601872"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601872/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">招商轮船</a></span><span id="quote_sh601872"></span>、金太阳和<span id="stock_sh600682"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600682/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">南京新百</a></span><span id="quote_sh600682"></span>的解禁比例不足0.1%。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">解禁前夕，多只股业绩翻倍增长</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　数据宝统计显示，下周将要解禁的这37只股票，自7月以来股价的平均下跌0.99%，小幅跑赢同期大盘。7月以来累计涨幅超过10%的有7只股，<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">分别是飞荣达、<span id="stock_sz300648"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300648/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">星云股份</a></span><span id="quote_sz300648"></span>、<span id="stock_sz300748"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300748/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">金力永磁</a></span><span id="quote_sz300748"></span>、<span id="stock_sz002497"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002497/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">雅化集团</a></span><span id="quote_sz002497"></span>、<span id="stock_sz000723"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000723/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">美锦能源</a></span><span id="quote_sz000723"></span>、<span id="stock_sz002506"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002506/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">协鑫集成</a></span><span id="quote_sz002506"></span>和申昊科技。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　股价累积跌幅超过10%的有9只股，分别是<span id="stock_sz300373"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300373/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">扬杰科技</a></span><span id="quote_sz300373"></span>、招商轮船、蓝思科技、春光科技、<span id="stock_sz300209"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300209/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">天泽信息</a></span><span id="quote_sz300209"></span>、<span id="stock_sh603901"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603901/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">永创智能</a></span><span id="quote_sh603901"></span>、<span id="stock_sh603180"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603180/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">金牌厨柜</a></span><span id="quote_sh603180"></span>、爱博医疗和<span id="stock_sz300858"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300858/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">科拓生物</a></span><span id="quote_sz300858"></span>。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　目前37只解禁股中，有11股发布了中报业绩预测。从中报预告业绩中值来看，有9只股在2021年上半年实现盈利。美锦能源的盈利最高，公司业绩预增，预测净利润约11亿元至15亿元，同比增长1617.52%至2242.07%。公司业绩同比大幅增长的主要原因是煤炭，焦炭价格高位运行，公司年产385万吨的华盛化工项目中的焦炭项目已全部投产，主要产品产量较上年同期有所增加。扬杰科技、雅化集团、金力永磁、<span id="stock_sz000545"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000545/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">金浦钛业</a></span><span id="quote_sz000545"></span>和<span id="stock_sz002853"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002853/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">皮阿诺</a></span><span id="quote_sz002853"></span>的半年度归母净利润额居前。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　从净利润预计同比增长幅度来看，净利润同比增幅最大的也是美锦能源，此外，金浦钛业、<span id="stock_sz000803"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000803/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">北清环能</a></span><span id="quote_sz000803"></span>、金力永磁、扬杰科技、雅化集团和皮阿诺的中报净利润均预计呈现翻倍增长，其中金浦钛业的业绩增幅超过13倍，仅次于美锦能源。金浦钛业于7月14日上调业绩预告数据，预测2021年半年度扭亏，归母净利润1.1亿元至1.15亿元，比上年同期增长1380%至1430%。2021年上半年，钛白粉市场持续回暖，钛白粉销售价格较上年同期涨幅较多；公司把握经济周期上行机遇，外抓市场，内抓管理，现有产能得到有效释放，产量连创新高。</p>
