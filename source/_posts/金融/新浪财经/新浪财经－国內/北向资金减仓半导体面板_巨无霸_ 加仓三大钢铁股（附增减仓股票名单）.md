<p cms-style="font-L">　　原标题：北向资金减仓半导体面板“巨无霸”，加仓三大钢铁股（附增减仓股票名单）</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　本周（5月10日至5月14日）市场震荡上涨，上证指数、深证成指、创业板指分别累计上涨2.09%、1.97%、4.24%。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　Choice数据显示，本周北向资金有三个交易日出现净流出，两个交易日出现净流入，调仓动作明显。本周累计净流入27.12亿元，其中沪股通资金净流入7.25亿元，深股通资金净流入19.87亿元。本周北向资金对863只个股进行加仓，对678只个股进行减仓。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　南向资金本周加仓港股172.60亿港元，对264只港股进行加仓。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">加仓<span id="stock_sh601857"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601857/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">中国石油</a></span><span id="quote_sh601857"></span>超1亿股</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　Choice数据显示，截至5月14日，北向资金持仓市值为2.45万亿元，本周北向资金持仓市值增加约405亿元。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　本周北向资金共计加仓863只个股，对其中49只个股的加仓股数超过1000万股，加仓股数最多的中国石油被加仓1.07亿股，<span id="stock_sh601169"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601169/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">北京银行</a></span><span id="quote_sh601169"></span>、<span id="stock_sz000825"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000825/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">太钢不锈</a></span><span id="quote_sz000825"></span>分别被加仓7204.90万股、6079.84万股。截至目前，中国石油总市值为8073亿元。</p>

<p cms-style="font-L">　　从市场表现情况来看，本周北向资金加仓股数前十的个股涨跌参半，<span id="stock_sz000683"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000683/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">远兴能源</a></span><span id="quote_sz000683"></span>涨幅最大，本周上涨7.17%，<span id="stock_sz000898"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000898/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">鞍钢股份</a></span><span id="quote_sz000898"></span>跌幅最大，下跌13.85%。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">本周北向资金加仓股数居前十个股</font></p>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210"><span id="stock_sh600519"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600519/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">贵州茅台</a></span><span id="quote_sh600519"></span>、<span id="stock_sz000858"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000858/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">五粮液</a></span><span id="quote_sz000858"></span></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">持仓市值增加金额居前</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　从持有流通股比例情况来看，Choice数据显示，本周北向资金对6只个股的持有流通股比例增加1个百分点以上，其中对<span id="stock_sz002022"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002022/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">科华生物</a></span><span id="quote_sz002022"></span>持有流通股比例增加2.55个百分点，对<span id="stock_sh600141"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600141/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">兴发集团</a></span><span id="quote_sh600141"></span>、<span id="stock_sz300377"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300377/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">赢时胜</a></span><span id="quote_sz300377"></span>持有流通股比例分别增加1.47个百分点和1.18个百分点。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　从市场表现来看，本周北向资金流通股比例增加居前十个股中，太钢不锈、<span id="stock_sz000049"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000049/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">德赛电池</a></span><span id="quote_sz000049"></span>、<span id="stock_sh601636"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601636/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">旗滨集团</a></span><span id="quote_sh601636"></span>下跌，其他7只个股均上涨，涨幅最大的个股为<span id="stock_sz300339"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300339/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">润和软件</a></span><span id="quote_sz300339"></span>，本周上涨58.33%。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">本周北向资金流通股比例增加居前十个股</font></p>

<p cms-style="font-L">　　从持仓市值变动情况来看，Choice数据显示，31只个股的北向资金持仓市值增加超过5亿元，对贵州茅台、五粮液、<span id="stock_sh601888"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601888/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">中国中免</a></span><span id="quote_sh601888"></span>持仓市值增加金额居前，持仓市值分别增加111.48亿元、50.55亿元、45.27亿元。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　本周北向资金持仓市值增加额居前十的个股除<span id="stock_sz300059"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300059/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">东方财富</a></span><span id="quote_sz300059"></span>、<span id="stock_sz300760"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300760/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">迈瑞医疗</a></span><span id="quote_sz300760"></span>被分别减仓702.02万股、93.11万股外，其他8只个股均被北向资金加仓，对五粮液、<span id="stock_sh600276"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600276/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">恒瑞医药</a></span><span id="quote_sh600276"></span>、<span id="stock_sz000333"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000333/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">美的集团</a></span><span id="quote_sz000333"></span>、<span id="stock_sz300014"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300014/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">亿纬锂能</a></span><span id="quote_sz300014"></span>、<span id="stock_sz000625"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000625/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">长安汽车</a></span><span id="quote_sz000625"></span>加仓股数超500万股。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　从市场表现上来看，本周北向资金持仓市值增加额居前十的个股均上涨，长安汽车涨幅最大，上涨37.70%，涨幅最小的美的集团上涨1.81%。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">本周北向资金持仓市值增加额居前十个股</font></p>

<p><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold color210"><!--article_adlist[<p cms-style="font-L">来源：Choice</p>]article_adlist--></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center color210">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">这些股票被减仓</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　Choice数据显示，本周北向资金共计减仓678只个股，对其中34只个股的减仓股数超过1000万股。减仓股数最多的<span id="stock_sz000725"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000725/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">京东方A</a></span><span id="quote_sz000725"></span>被减仓2.62亿股，<span id="stock_sh600010"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600010/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">包钢股份</a></span><span id="quote_sh600010"></span>、<span id="stock_sh600031"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600031/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">三一重工</a></span><span id="quote_sh600031"></span>被分别减仓8334.74万股、6213.91万股。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　京东方A是半导体显示面板“巨无霸”企业，今年一季度净利润为51.82亿元，同比增长814.46%，最新总市值为2290亿元。公司股价去年累计上涨32.71%，但今年5月以来表现不佳，累计下跌10.23%。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">本周北向资金减仓股数居前十个股</font></p>

<p cms-style="font-L">　　从持有流通股比例情况来看，Choice数据显示，本周北向资金对8只个股持有流通股比例减少1个百分点以上，对<span id="stock_sh603259"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603259/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">药明康德</a></span><span id="quote_sh603259"></span>持有流通股比例减少3.22个百分点，对<span id="stock_sh603786"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603786/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">科博达</a></span><span id="quote_sh603786"></span>、<span id="stock_sh603587"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603587/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">地素时尚</a></span><span id="quote_sh603587"></span>持有流通股比例分别减少2.60个百分点、2.38个百分点。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">本周北向资金持有流通股比例减少居前十个股</font></p>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">南向资金净流入超170亿港元</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　恒生指数本周下跌2.04%，但南向资金积极抄底。Choice数据显示，本周南向资金净流入172.60亿港元，加仓264只港股。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　Choice数据显示，本周南向资金对38只个股的加仓股数超过1000万股，加仓股数最多的民银资本被加仓1.64亿股，小米集团-W、中国海洋石油分别被加仓1.64亿股、1.60亿股。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　从市场表现情况来看，本周南向资金加仓股数前十的个股多数下跌，跌幅最大的特步国际下跌9.69%。</p>

<p cms-style="font-L">　　从持仓市值变动情况来看，Choice数据显示，本周南向资金对15只个股的持仓市值增加超过5亿港元，对小米集团-W、金斯瑞生物科技、药明生物持仓市值增加金额居前，持仓市值分别增加61.87亿港元、16.70亿港元、14.02亿港元。</p>
