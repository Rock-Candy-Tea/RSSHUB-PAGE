<p>　　原标题 时间缩短72%！广东“90后”清华博士让核酸检测大提速→</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　黄忠良</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　是一名“90后”的清华大学工科博士</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　也是一名奔赴一线<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">抗疫</font>的“大白”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　今年4月以来</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　他两次主动请缨增援广州白云防疫工作</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">用工科思维优化核酸检测流程</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">把一整个村子</font></font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">所需的上门核酸采样</font></font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">时间</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">从9个小时压缩到了2.5小时</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　↓↓</p>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">1</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">“城中村”流动人口多</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">如何做到精细化管理？</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　广州市白云区人和镇明星村</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　是个典型的“城中村”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　流动人口多、名单底册杂乱</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　为了摸清村里情况</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　5月3日晚至5月4日凌晨</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　黄忠良和团队人员连续奋战</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　把居家隔离人员名单底册</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　按照地址和“户”整理汇总</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">运用数理统计技巧</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">建立起清晰的数据库</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">最终生成700多人的名单</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">为后续的统筹调度打下基础</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20220521/566/w781h585/20220521/888b-3f4d5bf3babb8a55878591892b08f78b.png" alt="△5月3日，黄忠良和同事在通宵核查隔离人员名单。" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr">△5月3日，黄忠良和同事在通宵核查隔离人员名单。</span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">2</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">一家一户敲门扫“核酸码”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">费时长怎么办？</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　黄忠良注意到</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　村里手机网络信号不好</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　医护人员扫描居民的“核酸码”费时很长</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“大家到处找信号</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　有时还要去隔壁家借WiFi”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　为缩短核酸检测时间</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　黄忠良决定将扫码环节前置</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　志愿者提前收集好居家人员的“核酸码”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　每天早上在村委扫码、配管</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这样医护人员上门时</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　只需进行咽拭子采样</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">每户减少半分钟</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">累计起来就是好几小时</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20220521/66/w1000h666/20220521/424e-fe42dc447ca4255c15d44481e5af7553.png" alt="△村民志愿者在村委提前进行扫码、配管" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr">△村民志愿者在村委提前进行扫码、配管</span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">3</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">面对复杂路线 </font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　医护人员上门如何不再“弯弯绕”？</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　前期，由于不熟悉路线</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　上门做核酸的医护人员</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　稍不留神<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">就</font>会在村里曲折的小巷中迷路</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　为了解决这一问题</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　黄忠良和工作人员</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">把村子划分成一个个小网格</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">每个网格由固定的</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">熟悉片区的志愿者负责带路</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20220521/427/w702h525/20220521/dca8-6ec3cac1211c3ebddaf8e35e7aad4d22.png" alt="△明星村按经济社划分网格，医护人员分组上门进行核酸采样" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr">△明星村按经济社划分网格，医护人员分组上门进行核酸采样</span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　同时，优化了上门路线</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　减少了医护人员在路上花费的时间 </p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20220521/618/w1000h418/20220521/ccaf-59120c177d0a04fb7b278be02fd66e24.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　支援明星村上门核酸采样工作的</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　广州市黄埔区中医院张泉军医生表示</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　得益于黄忠良顺畅的安排</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　医护人员的工作压力大大减轻了</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20220521/150/w1000h750/20220521/93c3-c25db6d9f3c1862ed37e920c9a3e5f65.png" alt="△医护人员给黄忠良竖起大拇指" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr">△医护人员给黄忠良竖起大拇指</span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　据了解</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　黄忠良平时就擅长破解各种难题</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在繁杂琐碎中寻找答案</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　他1993年出生</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　本科就读于中国科学技术大学</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　保研直博清华大学</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">是大家口中常说的“学霸”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　毕业后</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　他作为广州市纪委监委派驻的选调生</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在黄埔区永和街道基层锻炼</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　已有1年半时间</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　不少网友纷纷表示</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">“学以致用，为清华同学点赞！”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">“希望可以尽快大面积应用”</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="5" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider20220521/122/w718h1004/20220521/c367-a733bc3c5d13c6e0b042597ded70ea0e.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center color210">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">学以致用，</font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">科学抗疫</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center color210">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">黄忠良，好样的！</font></p>
