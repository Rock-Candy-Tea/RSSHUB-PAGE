<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">#本科毕业月平均起薪5825元#</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">蹿上微博热搜第一</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">引发网友热议</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">来自北京大学某课题组的</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">一份调查问卷显示</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">2021年</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">博士、硕士、本科、专科</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">月起薪算数平均值分别为</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">14823元、10113元、5825元、3910元</font></font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center"></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">中位数分别为</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">15000元、9000元、5000元、3500元</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">看到这组数据后</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">网友们议论纷纷</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">有人说</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">“博士/硕士还是有点用的”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">“难怪都跑去考研”</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220105s/308/w641h467/20220105/936f-f59f069539f1f2ec433e845b362d3cc0.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">也有网友说</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">“又来平均了”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">“金融和IT行业直接拉高了平均值”</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220105s/525/w619h706/20220105/e1ec-e5e8cdfc7ff0f12c8c7bc52c16cbda2e.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">也</font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">有人现场赋诗</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">“月薪一万八</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">每天累到趴</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">好想回老家</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">能陪爸和妈</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">能和狗玩耍”</font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center"></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220105s/651/w1080h371/20220105/1ab6-cbb46ea2e2260a2cbe335130b4aa6ba1.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">不过</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">也有部分网友认为</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">现实生活中</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">本科毕业生的平均月薪</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">应该在三四千元左右</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220105s/112/w1010h702/20220105/a9fa-b10baa020be9e01f086113dd2fbdda24.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">据悉</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">前述调查问卷</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">样本包括东、中、西部地区</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">19个省份的34所高校</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">样本量超过2万人</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">调查显示</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">一流大学建设高校的月起薪最高</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">为10827元</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">其次是一流学科建设高校</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">为7346元</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">第三是普通本科高校</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">为6043元</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">分学科门类看</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">工学、理学和交叉学科位居前三名</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">分别为8341元、7909元和7838元</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">在高考志愿中受热捧的</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">经济学和管理学</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">并没有在收入上显示出特别的优势</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">智联招聘近期发布的</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">《2021大学生就业力调研报告》显示</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">今年应届毕业生的期望薪资</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">有了明显的提升</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">主要集中在4000-6000元</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">此外</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">报告显示</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">在毕业生最期待从事的行业排名中</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">IT/通信/电子/互联网位居第一</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">排在其后的是</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">房地产/建筑业</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">文化/传媒/娱乐/体育</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">金融业</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">毕业生们这样选</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">自然是有道理的</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">要知道</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">金融、IT行业最多金</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">《中国企业招聘薪酬报告》显示</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">2021年第三季度</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">基金/证券/期贷/投资行业</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">月均工资达到12711元</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">成为本季度高薪行业之首</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">分职业看</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">管理岗、技术岗薪资领先</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">IT管理平均月薪25666元</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L">中新经纬综合自光明日报、中国新闻网、经济观察网、微博等</font></p>
