<div>   
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　清明将至</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　转眼间</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　袁隆平院士离开我们已近一年</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　4月2日，湖南长沙</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　袁隆平铜像在湖南省农科院揭幕</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　铜像守望着他一辈子挂念的稻田</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　上面刻着<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">“愿天下人都有饱饭吃”</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/117/w550h367/20220403/db5d-954f25fe2789c3bb01cd59d3053f7ebc.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　袁隆平夫人</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　为袁隆平铜像献花</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　她所戴口罩上写着</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">“人就像一粒种子，要做一粒好种子”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　让人泪目</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/575/w550h825/20220403/6a64-cbefb8ca7086676c6185fbf4021ae4bf.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　此前一天，4月1日</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　袁隆平夫人还与后辈</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　前往墓园祭扫缅怀</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　细雨中</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　袁老的家人和生前同事、朋友</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　还有自发前来悼念的市民们</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　鞠躬行礼，将稻穗敬献在墓前</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/116/w550h366/20220403/0f86-7220c3278f0fddac2646cca048f3628b.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一份成绩单在袁老墓前被读起</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">“（衡南县）又开启了第三次</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">双季亩产1500公斤高产攻关</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">为实现您的禾下乘凉梦</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">勇毅前行”</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/gif_image/514/w202h312/20220403/a1ca-a3a1e381f9ce2351574aad702d871aa4.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　前来悼念的市民</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　将写满寄语的卡片投入时空邮箱</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　还有许多网友在网上留言</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　寄托哀思</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/672/w550h122/20220403/6904-162e3b86f3bfe69d665a022eac040f07.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="5" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/118/w550h368/20220403/40e9-4969190e02532856c7daae7f07cd6ec8.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">风吹稻浪是您</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">灶前米香是您</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">怀念您，袁老！</font></p>
