<p>　　原标题：有色化工股遭遇重挫</p>
<p>　　【深圳商报讯】（记者 陈燕青 见习记者 邹晓）在连续大涨后，有色化工等强势股9月首个交易日遭遇重挫。截至昨日收盘，有色金属大跌逾6%，化工和煤炭均大跌3%。个股方面，<span id="stock_sz002460"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002460/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">赣锋锂业</a></span><span id="quote_sz002460"></span>、<span id="stock_sh603799"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603799/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">华友钴业</a></span><span id="quote_sh603799"></span>、<span id="stock_sz000831"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000831/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">五矿稀土</a></span><span id="quote_sz000831"></span>、<span id="stock_sz000635"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000635/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">英力特</a></span><span id="quote_sz000635"></span>、<span id="stock_sh600160"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600160/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">巨化股份</a></span><span id="quote_sh600160"></span>等数十只个股跌停。</p>
<p>　　国内商品期货昨日多数收跌，铁矿跌近8%，不锈钢跌4%，苹果跌逾3%，花生、热卷、纸浆跌逾2%，沪铜、螺纹、国际铜、棉纱、生猪、沪铝、沪锌、沪铅跌逾1%；焦煤涨2%，原油、硅铁、郑醇、焦炭、尿素、锰硅涨逾1%。</p>
<p>　　尽管昨日大跌，但有色板块年内至今依然大涨逾五成，超过20家公司年内股价翻番。其中，<span id="stock_sh603260"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh603260/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">合盛硅业</a></span><span id="quote_sh603260"></span>、<span id="stock_sz000762"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000762/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">西藏矿业</a></span><span id="quote_sz000762"></span>年内涨幅超5倍，<span id="stock_sh600338"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600338/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">西藏珠峰</a></span><span id="quote_sh600338"></span>、<span id="stock_sh600111"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600111/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">北方稀土</a></span><span id="quote_sh600111"></span>等年内大涨超3倍。化工板块方面，年内涨幅更是超过六成，<span id="stock_sz300343"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300343/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">联创股份</a></span><span id="quote_sz300343"></span>年内暴涨逾6倍，<span id="stock_sz000422"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz000422/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">湖北宜化</a></span><span id="quote_sz000422"></span>涨幅近5倍，<span id="stock_sz002407"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002407/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">多氟多</a></span><span id="quote_sz002407"></span>、<span id="stock_sz002709"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002709/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">天赐材料</a></span><span id="quote_sz002709"></span>等涨幅翻倍。</p>
<p>　　值得注意的是，在连续大涨后，<span id="stock_sh600096"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600096/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">云天化</a></span><span id="quote_sh600096"></span>、<span id="stock_sh600141"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600141/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">兴发集团</a></span><span id="quote_sh600141"></span>、湖北宜化等多只化工股同时公布减持公告。</p>
<p>　　对于有色化工的大跌，一位不愿具名的私募人士坦言，“周三市场呈现明显的风格转换，低位的金融地产股反弹，吸引了部分资金流入；叠加有色化工等资源股此前累计涨幅过大，一有获利盘出逃就容易出现大幅杀跌。从后市来看，周期股难有整体行情，大概率将进一步分化，估值合理且供需良好的公司仍有机会。”</p><!-- news_keyword_pub,stock,sz000422&sz000635&sz000762&sz000831&sz002407&sz002460&sz002709&sz300343&sh600096&sh600111&sh600141&sh600160&sh600338&sh603260&sh603799 -->
