<div>   
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　近日，在上海支援抗疫的</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　湖北武汉市肺科医院医生<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">姚利</font>、<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">李国飞</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　先后收到武汉房东的信息</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">“房租，免了！”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　原来，得知他们参与援沪</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　两人的房东不约而同决定：</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">减免房租</font><!--article_adlist[<img src="https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_png/DTobibL9LCHJVmYBIpa0r4tIFn64lnibbgBN1XIzqUXg4Q0zdEeSYMLDtLD1sOPInCg5MmFaLOicZehd2alG7RJaQ/640?wx_fmt=png" cms-width="20" cms-height="20" black-list="y"/>]article_adlist--></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">得知青年医生去支援上海</font></p>
<p cms-style="align-Center font-L color210">　　两位房东不约而同减免房租</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　武汉市肺科医院胸外科医生姚利</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　和院感科医生李国飞都是“90后”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　4月3日</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　他们随湖北援沪医疗队</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　前往上海新国际博览中心方舱医院支援</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　几天后</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">姚利收到来自武汉房东</font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">王姐</font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">的消息</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　原来，王姐从姚利的朋友圈得知</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　他正在上海支援</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">“</font></font></font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">给你免一个月的房租</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">想表达对你工作的支持</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">做好防护，平安回来！”</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220428s/556/w518h838/20220428/d33d-4a8cc0dbc21d091c52433e9d153093dd.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　王姐表示</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　2020年武汉抗疫时</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　她的丈夫也在一线奋战</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　因此深知医护人员的艰辛</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">“这点支持不值一提</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">希望上海疫情早日结束”</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220428s/316/w906h1010/20220428/ca46-0c4dc4aa11ea21917d655aeaa2b4cbdd.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　同在上海支援的李国飞</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　近日也收到来自房东张阿姨的消息</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">“小李，得知你去支援上海抗疫工作了</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">年轻人有这种勇气和作为很了不起</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">你去上海期间就不用交房租了</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">希望你平平安安！”</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220428s/264/w848h1016/20220428/d69e-b50d2e911fe50be1987f81b556fd6b6c.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">两位医生在援沪一线<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">奋战</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">“一定不辱使命”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　两位房东不约而同的举动</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　让两位援沪医生既意外又感动</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　他们表示，一定不辱使命</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">“房东给我们减免房租</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">这份情谊非常重</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">他们是用另外</font></font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">一种形式支援上海”</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　目前在<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">援沪一线</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　姚利主要负责</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　有基础疾病的新冠肺炎确诊病例的治疗</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“这些患者大多年纪比较大了</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　我们会充分了解他们的基础病史</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　再制定个性化的治疗方案”</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220428s/288/w1080h808/20220428/dcef-82c51b0e8e25f5efd0535817486faf03.png" alt="△姚利（中）和同事" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"><font cms-style="font-L">△</font>姚利（中）和同事</span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　李国飞主要负责</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　指导医护人员的个人防护</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“我们要全天做好监测</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　同事们在救治患者，我来保护大家</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这就是院感医生的使命”</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220428s/220/w776h1044/20220428/0859-e93c09af5a5c88709c77591a19b94697.png" alt="△李国飞（左）检查准备入舱的医务人员防护用品是否穿戴规范" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"><font cms-style="font-L">△</font>李国飞（左）检查准备入舱的医务人员防护用品是否穿戴规范</span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　减免房租的房东</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　奋战一线的医生</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">都在为“守沪”尽一份力</font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">心手相牵，<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">共同战疫</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">加油！</font></p>
