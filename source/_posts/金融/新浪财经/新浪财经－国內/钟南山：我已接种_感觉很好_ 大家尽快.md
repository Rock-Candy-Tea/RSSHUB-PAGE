<p>　　钟南山：我已接种，大家尽快</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">近日，钟南山院士接种了国产新冠疫苗</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">他在接种时竖起大拇指</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">并表示<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color30"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color30">“感觉很好”</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">↓↓↓</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/spider2021515/305/w448h657/20210515/3206-kpzzqna4405078.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">钟南山呼吁尽快接种疫苗</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">钟南山接种疫苗后表示</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">接种疫苗是非常好的实现群体免疫的方法</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">我国接种疫苗的比例较低</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">必须跟上形势，和世界其他国家一样</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">争取尽快能达到<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color30">70%至80%的接种率</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">截至5月13日</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">31个省（区、市）及新疆生产建设兵团</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">累计报告接种新冠疫苗<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color30">36691万剂次</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">钟南山呼吁大家尽快接种新冠疫苗</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">“希望大家一起努力，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">尽快接种疫苗，</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">为国家、世界，也为个人</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">预防新冠尽一份自己的努力”</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">响应<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">号召</font>，应接尽接</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color210"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">早日筑起免疫防线</font></font></p>
