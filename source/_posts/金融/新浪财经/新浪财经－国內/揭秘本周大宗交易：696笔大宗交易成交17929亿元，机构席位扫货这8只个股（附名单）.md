<p class="ori_titlesource">　　原标题：揭秘本周大宗交易：696笔大宗交易成交179.29亿元，机构席位扫货这8只个股（附名单）</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">每经AI快讯，</font><font cms-style="font-L">本周一共发生696笔大宗交易，其中41笔溢价，177笔平价，478笔折价，共成交179.29亿元，环比增加27.81%，涉及295家上市公司。</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/transform/185/w550h435/20210828/424a-3e9cdca490bf71d19dcf210106c71adb.png" alt data-link referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　本周大宗交易累计成交金额前3的公司是<span id="stock_sz300014"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300014/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">亿纬锂能</a></span><span id="quote_sz300014"></span>、<span id="stock_sh601919"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh601919/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">中远海控</a></span><span id="quote_sh601919"></span>、<span id="stock_sh688363"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688363/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">华熙生物</a></span><span id="quote_sh688363"></span>，累计成交金额分别为18.62亿元，7.56亿元，5.26亿元。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/transform/231/w550h481/20210828/8196-d73ea9900b1942582b7340198a6c6a6a.png" alt data-link referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　本周机构专用席位参与了134笔大宗交易，环比上周增加2笔。本周机构席位买入金额前3的公司为<span id="stock_sh600582"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600582/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">天地科技</a></span><span id="quote_sh600582"></span>、<span id="stock_sh688208"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh688208/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">道通科技</a></span><span id="quote_sh688208"></span>、<span id="stock_sz002050"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002050/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">三花智控</a></span><span id="quote_sz002050"></span>，机构买入金额分别为4.06亿元、2.18亿元、1.98亿元。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/transform/185/w550h435/20210828/32aa-4e9ac27a469a62c7dc776ae87f360c69.png" alt data-link referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/transform/333/w550h583/20210828/5da3-ce05f0ea913f40dee65dca33f36eb8c3.png" alt data-link referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　溢价交易方面，本周有8笔大宗交易溢价率超过10%。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/transform/231/w550h481/20210828/940b-85b30467965fd02af91b97a76ee27425.png" alt data-link referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　折价交易方面，本周有190笔大宗交易折价率超过10%。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/transform/231/w550h481/20210828/7c71-dff27cdda316e521b1f2ee2c57b47b6c.png" alt data-link referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　*文中所涉折溢价率均为相对前收盘的折溢价率</p>
