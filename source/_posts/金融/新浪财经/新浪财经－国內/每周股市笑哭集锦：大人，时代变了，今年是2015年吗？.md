<p cms-style="font-L">　　原标题：每周股市笑哭集锦：大人，时代变了，今年是2015年吗？</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　幽默，是股民必修的素质课。这阵子赚，那阵子亏，起起伏伏，就像人生，心态好才是王道。赚钱自然开心，没赚到钱也可以穷开心。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　一个坏消息，本周有六个工作日；</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　一个好消息，本周只有五个交易日！</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　松了口气，终于有一天不是无效上班了。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20210919s/503/w705h598/20210919/07e8-683c4638eac04fd0bb7281e0ce752980.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">今夕何夕？</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　周五收官，沪深两市连续第43个交易日突破万亿。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　连续43天？破万亿？</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　小编一阵恍惚，忍不住抬头看了眼桌上的日历：</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　这是2021年还是2015年，今夕何夕？</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20210919s/152/w549h403/20210919/aca3-89b5bcbae707589a191a6e8fd2d38b73.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　上一轮A股成交连续超过万亿元的纪录，还要追溯至2015年。当年的5月8日至7月8日期间，A股曾经连续43个交易日成交额突破万亿元。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　相同的数字，不同的心境。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20210919s/597/w300h297/20210919/8877-c4877099869a8341c1a7322795e15d08.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　掐指一算，即便下周一中秋休市，假设剩下的交易日每天都突破万亿，那么到“十一”前，A股有望连续50天成交破万亿。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　而7月1日以来，A股仅有1个交易日成交额低于万亿元。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20210919s/693/w540h153/20210919/2637-9de77635aa8a60a43ad26de874e86023.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　幸运儿7月20日：弱小、无助、瑟瑟发抖。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20210919s/368/w205h163/20210919/bb18-e5fb8fbc1a75b696ce446558f5365d49.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　从“七一”到“十一”，我大A有望用超过连续50天的万亿成交，交上一份完美献礼。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="5" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20210919s/384/w192h192/20210919/c7e0-8cd3097beb0747eb2a7e600be4e614c8.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">工作的意义就是补仓</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　紧接下来的周四，【基金大跌】以不可阻挡之势冲上了热搜第一。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="6" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20210919s/466/w627h639/20210919/f83b-1b003fe1d3f28475f3281fafc913840c.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　据非官方、不完全统计，这是【基金大跌】在热搜七八位震荡多次后，首次直线拉升冲上第一。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　对于这一现象，基民已经见怪不怪：哦：）</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="7" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20210919s/35/w486h349/20210919/835c-bfda5c3319f71850215ffd3f61858076.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　不卖是我最后的倔强：</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="8" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20210919s/192/w404h588/20210919/e1a4-b65a6e4e4d3b048d6e1b096bab4dd41c.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　咱啥也不敢说，啥也不敢问。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="9" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20210919s/590/w300h290/20210919/6fe6-4e7efcf1772bbe6b22115671f2f461bb.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="10" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20210919s/588/w300h288/20210919/b1be-bda1266687bee7da02ba79b4e1bceb56.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　相比基民的愁云惨淡，股民则显得云淡风轻：跟我们比，你们基民还太嫩了。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="11" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20210919s/609/w300h309/20210919/ad6a-87203ad292fdc96e7d4a56b151af2c73.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　有“基转股”同志表示，自从买了股票，心态都变好了呢~</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="12" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20210919s/642/w438h204/20210919/1001-1d2ef151b4fe00ebabfbe2280f47a224.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　而作为资深股民，我大A特色一定不能丢：亏钱算什么，诗作咏流传：</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="13" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20210919s/148/w520h428/20210919/e2c7-575326837dcee7a6f2cc86f04dd4a5d5.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　实在不行，咱换个赛道，基民股民携手奔向新生活。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="14" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20210919s/747/w538h209/20210919/5525-0617ed2ead45593ad2e2c672251e5d05.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">什么才是真正的“十三香”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　小龙虾还没过季，真正的“十三香”就来了。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="15" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20210919s/750/w428h322/20210919/fb0e-5144dbeff3204f839a6e77a858df1a24.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>

<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="16" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20210919s/74/w350h524/20210919/605a-1a8c0da1b0fb6b11092d8572a3def640.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>

<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="17" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20210919s/149/w500h449/20210919/51e6-916e60ae6290e995b604daf19ed97e8d.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="18" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20210919s/345/w500h645/20210919/7c10-4fddd8c1781756830774a07c121ae9c2.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　由此推测，下一代iPhone一定长这样：</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="19" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20210919s/563/w441h122/20210919/08da-3fa7a9d3144983c3db3c0cfd924b0a38.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="20" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20210919s/522/w900h422/20210919/7fdd-09441edd076fd7a27a540c1bdf0778ff.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　当然，为了区分12和13，颜色还是要多加几个的。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　作为猛男，怎么能少了粉色？</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="21" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20210919s/45/w391h454/20210919/ba2d-ddf3f26b1f289daefca8bb6595eea09e.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　蓝色党：我妙蛙种子才是最帅的！</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="22" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20210919s/288/w736h352/20210919/069a-d68c9fa477112c5a17f208916fe2bc56.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　颜色神马的都不重要，最重要的是：有充电器吗？有充电器吗？有充电器吗？</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="23" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20210919s/160/w413h547/20210919/ffe0-613a9ed7ba74f3831568a2ceb5022d54.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　好的，我也原谅了。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　做不到手机中最靓的仔，我们可以做最亮的仔嘛：</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="24" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20210919s/464/w382h82/20210919/3865-6196be6787f3655f97c63f4e8c8e7977.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　至于土得掉渣的文案，</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="25" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20210919s/769/w421h348/20210919/d33c-0b27499d11c7221902c6cad6e3e0ceb1.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　我只想说，正确运用标点符号，这水平我上我也行：</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="26" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20210919s/571/w363h208/20210919/50b7-90b1e1a1c561beeb0604f95a6d176279.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　看了这么多，你心动了不？</p>

<p cms-style="font-L">　　天上一天，地上一年。</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="28" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20210919s/553/w405h148/20210919/5dab-be22c0aa044b6cd361a22aa1da5b2a19.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
