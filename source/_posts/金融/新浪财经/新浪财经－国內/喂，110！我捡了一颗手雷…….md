<div>   
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">喂</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">你好，110</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">我捡回来了一枚手雷</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">不知道怎么办好</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">请您赶快过来吧……</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　近日</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　朝阳分局平房派出所</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　接群众报警</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　称在外出爬山时</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　发现了一枚疑似是手雷的物品</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220413s/49/w360h489/20220413/152f-e058e69b891af0939a024e5f320e31e8.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　事不宜迟</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　派出所立即调派警力赶赴现场</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　并请分局指挥中心</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　协调分局特警大队排爆车组</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　赴现场开展工作</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220413s/687/w400h287/20220413/966a-58139e8a76672fde4eef6b14b940571b.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　很快</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　民警抵达现场</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　报警人跟民警表示当天下午外出游玩</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">无意间发现一个铁坨</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">样子非常奇特</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　感觉有些好奇的报警人便随手将其捡起</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　回家的路上</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　经在网上查阅有关资料</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">他发现这个秤砣可能是一枚手雷</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　于是便拨打电话报了警</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220413s/75/w560h315/20220413/9969-9a9c6a98bbcbb2292667bb1704d39dff.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　民警迅速拉出警戒区域</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　并引导过往群众远离铁砣</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　经现场查看</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">该铁砣直径约5厘米、长约7厘米</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">表面布满锈迹</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">虽然引信已经丢失</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">但其外观完好</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　为防止发生意外</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">民警迅速疏散周边群众</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">并使用防爆围栏和防爆毯将手雷隔离</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220413s/75/w560h315/20220413/f464-8e46491d1a19defbdb4e6632b566e4a4.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　后经有关部门鉴定</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">该手雷已无引信、无引爆风险</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">应该为战争年代遗留在郊野山区的废弃物</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　现已移交上级部门进行销毁处理</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220413s/295/w640h455/20220413/b6dc-275b37de13ac13796ffbaa83f75ed39c.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　警方提示</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">市民在日常生活中，如发现疑似手雷、地雷、炮弹等可疑物品时，请您第一时间拨打110电话报警</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">一定要等待有关部门及专业人员到场处置</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">切不可因一时好奇而私自移动、碰触、观赏或把玩，以免发生意外</font></p><!-- news_keyword_pub,stock, -->
