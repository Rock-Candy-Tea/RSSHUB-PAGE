<p>　　原标题：曾霸气减租80万！这位“最美房东”再上热搜</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　还记着前年疫情期间</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　主动为租客减租80万元的</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">“深圳最美房东”</font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">林萍君</font></font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">吗？</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　最近她连日<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">给防疫人员送汤</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　又上热搜了！</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/643/w550h93/20220118/3ee8-edba236c863300972218d05ed68dadd9.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">一天送出去120份汤</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">每次都换不同花样</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　近日，深圳出现新疫情</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在龙岗区坂田街道的林萍君</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">利用工作闲暇</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">主动为抗疫人员煲汤、送饺子</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　1月13日中午</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　林萍君来到核酸采样点</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　将一碗碗热汤端给一线抗疫人员</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这次她送来的是猪肉白菜豆腐汤</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">担心工作人员吃得不方便</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">林萍君还细心地提前把骨头捞出来</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/163/w550h413/20220118/bbce-eb8a5e9b3766e2c702b40ccebc025d07.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　实际上，从<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">1月7日</font>开始</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　林萍君就为抗疫人员送餐了</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　7号当天</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　她煲的是姜、鸡蛋、红枣和枸杞</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">林萍君说</font>：</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“一大早我就去菜市场买菜</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">买了20多斤姜、红枣、枸杞</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">和150颗鸡蛋”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">当天上午和晚上各送了60份</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/gif_image/233/w446h587/20220118/2dc0-d4faee625dee01c8d18c53a8b921776b.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　此后，她又接连送了潮汕粿品</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　饺子、不同品种的汤等</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">林萍君</font>送餐的地方主要在住处周边</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">“送给我周边的一些采样点、卡口</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">因为我担心汤这些东西凉了”</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/gif_image/234/w467h567/20220118/8707-8fde7e0436750849859817990c4428ee.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">林萍君</font>每天变着法子调整餐食</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　比如天气冷了，就煲骨头汤</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　加一点胡椒粉和指天椒</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　帮抗疫人员暖暖身子</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　喝着热汤的抗疫人员说：</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">“吃在嘴里，暖在心里！</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">这些爱心餐点让我们在一线力量更足”</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/85/w506h379/20220118/6df0-7cb6b160a657f2d62d0453f2ed05566d.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">前年疫情期间 </font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">她顶着家庭压力减租约80万</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　早在2020年疫情初期</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">林萍君</font>就做了一件让大家惊讶的事</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">她主动为租户减免了约80万元的租金</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">被网友称赞为“最美房东”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　林萍君说：</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">“当时减租80万，</font></font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">家庭是有压力的</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">但在那个时候就是想做点什么”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　网友纷纷为她点赞：</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">“<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">善良的人</font>”“最美房东”</font></p>
