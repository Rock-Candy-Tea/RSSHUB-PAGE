<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>本周一共发生520笔大宗交易，其中48笔溢价，106笔平价，366笔折价，共成交129.77亿元，环比减少23.29%，涉及217家上市公司。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/a62d8e040e0b4e3f80c7be4e406ec4f6?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>本周大宗交易累计成交金额前3的公司是三棵树、中芯国际、韦尔股份，累计成交金额分别为14.63亿元，14.42亿元，7.38亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/4574655f4b5642a8996d27afb3be2fc8?nbddata-height=858&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>本周机构专用席位参与了93笔大宗交易，环比上周减少58笔。本周机构席位买入金额前3的公司为歌尔股份、东方雨虹、蓝思科技，机构买入金额分别为3.11亿元、2.39亿元、1.42亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/9a72ccfa9e1042f5b713821fb5e76384?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/1267475d8e6c466e9ea45aee936adf9b?nbddata-height=954&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>溢价交易方面，本周有7笔大宗交易溢价率超过10%。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/8cd5f0767dfd475aaf17cfda1b0c66df?nbddata-height=787&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
