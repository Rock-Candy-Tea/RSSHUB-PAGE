<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>4月7日，共有217只个股上榜龙虎榜，中钢国际龙虎榜净买入额最多，达1.93亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/a0add56e25954631b722c6107e573d1d?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有25只，净买入额前三的是南网能源、中钢国际、国轩高科，分别为5322.61万元、4295.2万元、2505.61万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/3511876d9bc545a090f6d12e9a2a1837?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有2只，华海药业的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入1264.2万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/0256faef153e402eba60cc09b4664682?nbddata-height=414&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有4只，美年健康的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入3742.9万元。</p>
