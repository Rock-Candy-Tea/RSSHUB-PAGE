<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>本周A股共84家公司购买理财产品99.59亿元，金额环比上周减少4.72%。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/b9221cc3-3b83-494c-81b7-fe96ac08752d?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>从买入金额上来看，购买理财产品金额排名前三的公司是蕾奥规划（300989.SZ）、高德红外（002414.SZ）、重庆水务（601158.SH），买入金额分别为6.10亿元、5.90亿元、5.00亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/bd82207b-cb1b-492c-a870-de412d25ebd6?nbddata-height=1423&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>从上市公司所属行业上来看，本周买入理财产品金额排名前三的行业分别是化工、电子、医药生物。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/f03f229b-835e-4c4b-bf56-1e3e77cf4d83?nbddata-height=1423&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>从上市公司所属地区上来看，本周买入理财产品金额排名前三的地区分别是广东省、浙江省、湖北省。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/6c996ab9-64e1-4535-b468-c199103b6277?nbddata-height=1303&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
