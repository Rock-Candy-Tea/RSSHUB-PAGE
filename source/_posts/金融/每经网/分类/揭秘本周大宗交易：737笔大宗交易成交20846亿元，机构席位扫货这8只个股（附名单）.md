<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>本周一共发生737笔大宗交易，其中42笔溢价，73笔平价，622笔折价，共成交208.46亿元，环比减少5.39%，涉及294家上市公司。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/87df78c479e344f49d5dfbf39496ac5f?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>本周大宗交易累计成交金额前3的公司是歌尔股份、明阳智能、锡业股份，累计成交金额分别为27.52亿元，8.08亿元，6.63亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/17c83d19807747cc9cf5902b5f9d54e4?nbddata-height=787&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>本周机构专用席位参与了155笔大宗交易，环比上周减少41笔。本周机构席位买入金额前3的公司为闻泰科技、海康威视、埃斯顿，机构买入金额分别为5.57亿元、4.58亿元、3.99亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/0405f4bba2e7478e836451876a39c6f7?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/a64b4754347c49baa7e13984a0e04ff8?nbddata-height=984&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>溢价交易方面，本周有2笔大宗交易溢价率超过10%。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/4bee1103ee974e6bbca32f3e443153e8?nbddata-height=787&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
