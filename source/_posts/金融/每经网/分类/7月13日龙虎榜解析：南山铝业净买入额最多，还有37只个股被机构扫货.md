<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>7月13日，共有267只个股上榜龙虎榜，南山铝业龙虎榜净买入额最多，达6.64亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/1328282b2ea149948f863084d5c0d803?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有37只，净买入额前三的是包钢股份、南山铝业、天际股份，分别为5.68亿元、4.74亿元、2.56亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/f820d71ff914401dbf2cedd991b39d0b?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有6只，南山铝业的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入2.79亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/bc80d2fb1515425ab42df188f0e11691?nbddata-height=798&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有8只，启明星辰的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入3.61亿元。</p>
