<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>7月19日，共有266只个股上榜龙虎榜，中兵红箭龙虎榜净买入额最多，达1.59亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/9db3b25b28db4394be4e460207350d76?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有30只，净买入额前三的是紫江企业、元利科技、中兵红箭，分别为6998.13万元、4787.59万元、3982.43万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/918270194e1f4c2ca13087b1100ae795?nbddata-height=1176&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有1只，紫江企业的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入2459.67万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/f9135174503b4e4799b334c908304a85?nbddata-height=318&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有13只，中兵红箭的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入1.14亿元。</p>
