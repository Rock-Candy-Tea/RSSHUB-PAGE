        <p><span>近日，浙江杭州</span>      </p>
<p><span>一名辅警突然脱岗半个月</span>      </p>
<p><span>派出所所长得知真相却说：</span>      </p>
<p><span>“奖金照发，还要奖！”</span>      </p>
<p><span>咋回事？</span></p>
<p><img src="https://tmp-file-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com//gov_img/2021/9/21/7004594255272693760.gov_img/2021/9/21/5453038169141864448.png" alt="5453038169141864448.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>            <p><strong></strong></p>
<p><strong><img src="https://tmp-file-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com//gov_img/2021/9/21/7956072978610847744.gov_img/2021/9/21/6544235264315872256.png" alt="6544235264315872256.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></strong></p>
<strong></strong>                   <p><strong><span>4个月前的这通电话</span></strong>          </p>         <p><strong><span>他期待了15年</span></strong>         </p>
<p><span>今年5月</span></p>      <p><span>杭州萧山闻堰派出所辅警叶灿飞</span>      </p>
<p><span>接到了红十字会的电话：</span>      </p>
<p><span>“您的造血干细胞</span>      </p>
<p><span>与一名白血病患者匹配成功</span>      </p>
<p><span>请问您是否愿意无偿捐献造血干细胞？”</span>      </p>
<p><span>叶灿飞脱口而出：“愿意！”</span></p>
<p><img src="https://tmp-file-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com//gov_img/2021/9/21/5012801699533595648.gov_img/2021/9/21/5108921641860800512.png" alt="5108921641860800512.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>      <p><img src="https://tmp-file-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com//gov_img/2021/9/21/8076703903668124672.gov_img/2021/9/21/5512443759820527616.jpeg" alt="5512443759820527616.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>      <p><span>△叶灿飞签署捐献同意书</span>      </p>
<p><span>从2002年至今</span></p>      <p><span>叶灿飞每年都坚持无偿献血</span></p>      <p><span>目前，他已无偿献血近3000毫升</span></p>      <p><span>2006年夏天</span></p>      <p><span>还是大学生的叶灿飞</span></p>      <p><span>听闻加入中国造血干细胞捐献者资料库</span></p>      <p><span>能救助血液病患者</span></p>      <p><span>他毫不犹豫答应了</span></p>      <p><span>“也许有一天，我可以帮到需要帮助的人”</span>      </p>
<p><img src="https://tmp-file-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com//gov_img/2021/9/21/529482935251041280.gov_img/2021/9/21/8377208614491225088.gif" alt="8377208614491225088.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>      <p><strong></strong></p>
<p><strong><img src="https://tmp-file-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com//gov_img/2021/9/21/7956072978610847744.gov_img/2021/9/21/6544235264315872256.png" alt="6544235264315872256.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></strong></p>
<strong></strong>         <strong><span>为了素不相识的她</span></strong>         <p><strong><span>叶灿飞开始每天跑步、跳绳</span></strong>          </p>         <span>接到电话后</span><span>的</span><span>4</span><span>个</span><span>月</span><p><span>叶灿飞调整作息</span></p>      <p><span>放弃夜宵、烧烤、碳酸饮料</span></p>      <p><span>坚持清淡饮食，还每天跑步、跳绳</span></p>      <p><span>“虽然平时身体没什么问题</span></p>      <p><span>但毕竟是去救人</span></p>      <p><span>肯定要把自己状态调整到最好”</span></p>      <p><span>捐献造血干细胞过程中</span></p>      <p><span>捐、受双方并不知晓对方的信息</span></p>      <p><span>叶灿飞只知道对方是一位小女孩</span></p>      <p><span>其他的一概“保密”</span></p>      <p><span>想到很快就能救助到患者</span></p>      <p><span>他每天都充满力量</span></p>
<p><img src="https://tmp-file-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com//gov_img/2021/9/21/5940320840401104896.gov_img/2021/9/21/9122991339438068736.png" alt="9122991339438068736.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>      <p><strong></strong></p>
<p><strong><img src="https://tmp-file-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com//gov_img/2021/9/21/7956072978610847744.gov_img/2021/9/21/6544235264315872256.png" alt="6544235264315872256.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></strong></p>
<strong></strong>                   <p><strong><span>突然请假半个月</span></strong>          </p>         <p><strong><span>母亲和儿女被“蒙在鼓里”</span></strong>         </p>         <p><span>9月13日，叶灿飞</span><span>入院注射动员剂</span></p>        <p><span>他向单位请了15天假</span></p>      <p><span>和许多捐献者一样</span></p>      <p><span>叶灿飞对家人选择了暂时隐瞒</span></p>      <p><span>除了妻子</span></p>      <p><span>他的母亲和儿女都被“蒙在鼓里”</span></p>      <p><span>“我想等捐献完成后</span></p>      <p><span>健健康康地出现在家人面前</span></p>      <p><span>再开开心心地告诉他们这件事”</span></p>
<p><img src="https://tmp-file-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com//gov_img/2021/9/21/2005197731427568640.gov_img/2021/9/21/2570245619366673408.png" alt="2570245619366673408.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>      <p><img src="https://tmp-file-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com//gov_img/2021/9/21/2304333405354484736.gov_img/2021/9/21/7673651313861529600.gif" alt="7673651313861529600.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>      <p><span><span>叶灿飞</span>的妻子包揽了大部分家务</span>      </p>
<p><span>忙前忙后地照顾着孩子</span>      </p>
