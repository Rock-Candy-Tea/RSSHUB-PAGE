<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>8月31日，共有309只个股上榜龙虎榜，特变电工龙虎榜净买入额最多，达5.15亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/ce91d79aab934bc2926f9db24e43403a?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有46只，净买入额前三的是特变电工、怡球资源、鄂尔多斯，分别为7.42亿元、5.96亿元、4.61亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/99e30783ce1048659ca291a0e9363b38?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有6只，特变电工的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入4710.71万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/ecbe83c2ca714b5c9ee1c1a5e30028d7?nbddata-height=798&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有15只，中泰化学的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入2.79亿元。</p>
