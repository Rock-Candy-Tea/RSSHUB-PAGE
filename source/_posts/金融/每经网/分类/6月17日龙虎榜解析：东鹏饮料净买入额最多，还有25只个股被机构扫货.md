<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>6月17日，共有252只个股上榜龙虎榜，东鹏饮料龙虎榜净买入额最多，达8.25亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/8998c2a1283f4f70aa75867299df95f9?nbddata-height=1176&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有25只，净买入额前三的是东鹏饮料、国民技术、佳创视讯，分别为11.39亿元、8002.69万元、2954.71万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/868b499975c24320a89e39787bbe99a2?nbddata-height=1110&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有8只，比亚迪的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入1.13亿元。</p>
