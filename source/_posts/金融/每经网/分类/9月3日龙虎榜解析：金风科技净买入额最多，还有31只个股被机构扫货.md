<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>9月3日，共有298只个股上榜龙虎榜，金风科技龙虎榜净买入额最多，达11.41亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/0ff30215ef9f43d5a49093bbe96d42ef?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有31只，净买入额前三的是中闽能源、华能国际、太阳能，分别为3.16亿元、3.14亿元、2.15亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/8657730190cb42a6b094b5cac253ceb2?nbddata-height=1176&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有5只，新安股份的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入9202.21万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/bcd7222e3e6647caae1a9c90b7520382?nbddata-height=702&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有14只，金风科技的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入4.33亿元。</p>
