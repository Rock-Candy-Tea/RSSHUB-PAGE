        <p>6月1日，乃万工作室官方发布微博表示，<span>近日有部分自媒体用户在新浪微博、抖音等平台散布关于乃万“谈恋爱”、“和男朋友闹分手”、“网暴素人”等的不实言论（详见下图）。</span></p>
<p><span><img class="nbdLazyload" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.nbd.com.cn/uploads/articles/images/1016637/1.jpg" alt data-imgscale="1.7575757575757576" data-imgwidth="580" data-imgheight="330" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br></span></p>
<p><span>在此，乃万工作室郑重声明，上述消息均为虚假信息，为别有用心之人编造出来的谎言。</span></p>
<p><span>针对以上谣言，我方工作室已启动相关法律程序，同时希望各大平台及用户不要传播不实信息，共同抵制网络谣言，感谢各位对乃万的关注。</span></p>
<p><span><img class="nbdLazyload" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.nbd.com.cn/uploads/articles/images/1016636/0088WV2Mly1gr2tg9z5bgj30n00nqwi7.jpg" alt width="630" data-imgscale="0.9691011235955056" data-imgwidth="690" data-imgheight="712" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p><span><img class="nbdLazyload" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.nbd.com.cn/uploads/articles/images/1016639/0088WV2Mly1gr2tg9j8ubj30mz0jwgr8.jpg" alt width="630" data-imgscale="1.1557788944723617" data-imgwidth="690" data-imgheight="597" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p>此外，<span>一微博名为“阿根廷进口大虾”的</span><span class="s-color-red">网友</span><span>晒出了</span><span class="s-color-red">乃</span><span class="s-color-red">万</span><span class="s-color-red">告</span><span>自己</span><span class="s-color-red">侵犯</span><span class="s-color-red">名誉</span><span class="s-color-red">权</span><span>的法律责任</span><span class="s-color-red">告</span><span>知书，原因是该</span><span class="s-color-red">网友</span><span>发布的某条内容涉及到侮辱、诽谤，侵害了</span><span class="s-color-red">乃</span><span class="s-color-red">万</span><span>合法</span><span class="s-color-red">权</span><span>益。现要求该</span><span class="s-color-red">网友</span><span>删除其相关内容。</span></p>
