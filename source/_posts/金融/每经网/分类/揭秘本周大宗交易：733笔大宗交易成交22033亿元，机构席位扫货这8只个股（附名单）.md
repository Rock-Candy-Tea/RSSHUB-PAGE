<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>本周一共发生733笔大宗交易，其中77笔溢价，139笔平价，517笔折价，共成交220.33亿元，环比增加22.89%，涉及310家上市公司。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/12ea88ea5d204275aa61d9a6f6da3b48?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>本周大宗交易累计成交金额前3的公司是万科A、荣盛石化、凯赛生物，累计成交金额分别为54.17亿元，6.01亿元，5.36亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/6483b6ee479541f682bd554ec700ffd2?nbddata-height=787&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>本周机构专用席位参与了196笔大宗交易，环比上周增加62笔。本周机构席位买入金额前3的公司为海康威视、震安科技、永兴材料，机构买入金额分别为3.61亿元、3.61亿元、2.57亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/bc854ead14b04a4296d381c7d7e9784c?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/a5539dd8766a44d08b6c55f56ae192e8?nbddata-height=990&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>溢价交易方面，本周有10笔大宗交易溢价率超过10%。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/181dddb6168b463f97fd88e805b9befe?nbddata-height=787&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>折价交易方面，本周有198笔大宗交易折价率超过10%。</p>
