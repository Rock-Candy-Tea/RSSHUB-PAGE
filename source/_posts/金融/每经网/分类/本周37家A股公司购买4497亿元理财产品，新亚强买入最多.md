<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>本周A股共37家公司购买理财产品44.97亿元，金额环比上周减少267.50%。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/1f032a65-4784-4ef1-ad2d-c70f04ca8b94?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>从买入金额上来看，购买理财产品金额排名前三的公司是新亚强（603155.SH）、浙版传媒（601921.SH）、蓝盾光电（300862.SZ），买入金额分别为7.00亿元、3.00亿元、3.00亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/aa87ed23-7afd-4037-8500-5f77988b208a?nbddata-height=1423&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>从上市公司所属行业上来看，本周买入理财产品金额排名前三的行业分别是化工、机械设备、传媒。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/8bda5b7c-3e5c-4595-863c-a767886d4365?nbddata-height=1243&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>从上市公司所属地区上来看，本周买入理财产品金额排名前三的地区分别是广东省、江苏省、安徽省。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/0b02eef9-156b-42bd-961e-18ed922c6e67?nbddata-height=883&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
