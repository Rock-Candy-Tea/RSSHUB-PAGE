<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>10月12日，共有41只个股上榜龙虎榜，东方电缆龙虎榜单日净买入额最多，达1.98亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/1c862806841948269031e7df11dc7a19?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/507bee7e54744455b7ff7ab4e137f3b1?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有25只，净买入额前三的是华致酒行、大金重工、吉电股份，分别为1.32亿元、3055.12万元、1244.94万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/100a5d44f1bb4912922625e53e5e3e09?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有2只，东方电缆的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入88.22万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/b7d4898d3e2844e5999ddeae429b766a?nbddata-height=414&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有4只，华致酒行的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入1555.47万元。</p>
