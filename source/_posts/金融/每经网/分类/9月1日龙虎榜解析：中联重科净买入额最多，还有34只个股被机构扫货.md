<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>9月1日，共有309只个股上榜龙虎榜，中联重科龙虎榜净买入额最多，达3.23亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/4e3e656ecc1c475f9cf1b19d8361f5b9?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有34只，净买入额前三的是东阳光、湖北宜化、快克股份，分别为2.2亿元、1.25亿元、1.22亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/03dde117571f433194297a83ecda6688?nbddata-height=1176&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有3只，东阳光的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入1.58亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/4a3c5eb1f08841c684ecd448e683fe41?nbddata-height=510&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有18只，赣锋锂业的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入1.27亿元。</p>
