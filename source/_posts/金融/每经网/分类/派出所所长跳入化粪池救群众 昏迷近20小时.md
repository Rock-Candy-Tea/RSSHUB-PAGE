<p>据贵州警事微信号9月18日消息，9月17日13时许，开阳县公安局南江派出所所长刘天周在出警回所途中经过南江村谷坝组时，发现一些群众焦急跑向一村民家中，口中还不断呼救。凭着职业的敏感性，刘天周立即赶往事发地，在得知系两名村民跌落家中化粪池的情况后，刘天周立即组织人员进行施救。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class="article-video-poster" src="https://rmrbcmsonline.peopleapp.com/upload/image/202109/rmrb_96411631951677.png?x-oss-process=style/w10" alt data-video-url="https://rmrbcmsonline.peopleapp.com/upload/video/mp4/202109/163195136585675790a8ddf913.mp4" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span>营救群众视频</span></p>
<p><span class="img-bitmap-box"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://rmrbcmsonline.peopleapp.com/upload/ueditor/image/20210918/a_623756812981628928.png?x-oss-process=image/format,jpg/auto-orient,1" alt width="720" height="405" data-bitmap="https://rmrbcmsonline.peopleapp.com/upload/ueditor/image/20210918/a_623756812981628928.png?x-oss-process=image/format,jpg/auto-orient,1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p>刘天周在化粪池洞口查看，两名村民已经失去意识，加之洞口太小无法使用楼梯下洞施救，群众的生命危在旦夕。</p>
<p><span class="img-bitmap-box"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://rmrbcmsonline.peopleapp.com/upload/ueditor/image/20210918/a_623756812981628929.png?x-oss-process=image/format,jpg/auto-orient,1" alt width="720" height="405" data-bitmap="https://rmrbcmsonline.peopleapp.com/upload/ueditor/image/20210918/a_623756812981628929.png?x-oss-process=image/format,jpg/auto-orient,1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p>“别进去，太危险了！我是所长，听命令！”话音刚落，刘天周不假思索就跳进了化粪池。化粪池里的沼气时刻攻击着他的胸腔，加上视线受阻，营救工作十分艰难。不久，刘天周成功将一名村民托举到洞外。</p>
<p><span class="img-bitmap-box"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://rmrbcmsonline.peopleapp.com/upload/ueditor/image/20210918/a_623756812981628930.png?x-oss-process=image/format,jpg/auto-orient,1" alt width="720" height="405" data-bitmap="https://rmrbcmsonline.peopleapp.com/upload/ueditor/image/20210918/a_623756812981628930.png?x-oss-process=image/format,jpg/auto-orient,1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p>“快！快！快！往上拉！还有一个人，我再下去！”说完这句话后，刘天周就因吸入大量沼气昏迷在现场。</p>
<p><span class="img-bitmap-box"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://rmrbcmsonline.peopleapp.com/upload/ueditor/image/20210918/a_623756812981628931.png?x-oss-process=image/format,jpg/auto-orient,1" alt width="720" height="405" data-bitmap="https://rmrbcmsonline.peopleapp.com/upload/ueditor/image/20210918/a_623756812981628931.png?x-oss-process=image/format,jpg/auto-orient,1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p><span class="img-bitmap-box"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://rmrbcmsonline.peopleapp.com/upload/ueditor/image/20210918/a_623756812981628932.png?x-oss-process=image/format,jpg/auto-orient,1" alt width="720" height="405" data-bitmap="https://rmrbcmsonline.peopleapp.com/upload/ueditor/image/20210918/a_623756812981628932.png?x-oss-process=image/format,jpg/auto-orient,1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p>经过在场民警和村民的合力救助，刘天周及另一名村民被先后救出并送往医院进行抢救。</p>
<p><span class="img-bitmap-box"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://rmrbcmsonline.peopleapp.com/upload/ueditor/image/20210918/a_623756812981628933.png?x-oss-process=image/format,jpg/auto-orient,1" alt width="720" height="407" data-bitmap="https://rmrbcmsonline.peopleapp.com/upload/ueditor/image/20210918/a_623756812981628933.png?x-oss-process=image/format,jpg/auto-orient,1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p><span class="img-bitmap-box"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://rmrbcmsonline.peopleapp.com/upload/ueditor/image/20210918/a_623756812981628934.png?x-oss-process=image/format,jpg/auto-orient,1" alt width="720" height="405" data-bitmap="https://rmrbcmsonline.peopleapp.com/upload/ueditor/image/20210918/a_623756812981628934.png?x-oss-process=image/format,jpg/auto-orient,1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p><span>在对被救群众和刘天周积极抢救时，他们的病房外，亲人和同事都在焦急等待着。</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span class="img-bitmap-box"><img src="https://rmrbcmsonline.peopleapp.com/upload/ueditor/image/20210918/a_623756812985823232.png?x-oss-process=image/format,jpg/auto-orient,1" alt width="720" height="405" data-bitmap="https://rmrbcmsonline.peopleapp.com/upload/ueditor/image/20210918/a_623756812985823232.png?x-oss-process=image/format,jpg/auto-orient,1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p><span class="img-bitmap-box"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://rmrbcmsonline.peopleapp.com/upload/ueditor/image/20210918/a_623756812985823233.png?x-oss-process=image/format,jpg/auto-orient,1" alt width="720" height="405" data-bitmap="https://rmrbcmsonline.peopleapp.com/upload/ueditor/image/20210918/a_623756812985823233.png?x-oss-process=image/format,jpg/auto-orient,1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p><span class="img-bitmap-box"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://rmrbcmsonline.peopleapp.com/upload/ueditor/image/20210918/a_623756812985823234.png?x-oss-process=image/format,jpg/auto-orient,1" alt width="720" height="405" data-bitmap="https://rmrbcmsonline.peopleapp.com/upload/ueditor/image/20210918/a_623756812985823234.png?x-oss-process=image/format,jpg/auto-orient,1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p>万幸的是，经过医护人员全力救治，目前刘天周已逐步恢复意识，正在重症监护室继续观察治疗。</p>
<p><span>为群众生命安全奋不顾身，</span>这是他的信仰，一起为人民好警察点赞！</p>
