<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>8月6日，共有276只个股上榜龙虎榜，多氟多龙虎榜净买入额最多，达4.71亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/c6c02736c184409eb2e5dd67e8cfa610?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有38只，净买入额前三的是紫光股份、中国宝安、川能动力，分别为2.91亿元、1.89亿元、1.35亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/0a929e70eca547d68f0a3100f158b49a?nbddata-height=1146&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有6只，广汇能源的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入7947.65万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/67aad53cc76d42b18b5bc83fe53ebc95?nbddata-height=798&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有11只，多氟多的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入2.5亿元。</p>
