<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>本周一共发生397笔大宗交易，其中76笔溢价，57笔平价，264笔折价，共成交113.63亿元，环比增加5.49%，涉及176家上市公司。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/052971d240a84a58a43e30b4c6267fc0?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>本周大宗交易累计成交金额前3的公司是宁德时代、智飞生物、海欣股份，累计成交金额分别为29.43亿元，6.72亿元，5.32亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/8d607cb4fe0b4e74b32f94be74b2debf?nbddata-height=1146&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>本周机构专用席位参与了103笔大宗交易，环比上周增加5笔。本周机构席位买入金额前3的公司为宁德时代、智飞生物、道通科技，机构买入金额分别为29.43亿元、6.72亿元、1.86亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/8a4527a33aa44bf9afba77108ea2dd31?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/4caaa4fbefd849059f344ff8e526cf32?nbddata-height=990&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>溢价交易方面，本周有19笔大宗交易溢价率超过10%。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/baa10b6869404c5db5542ee0c94306ca?nbddata-height=1092&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>折价交易方面，本周有114笔大宗交易折价率超过10%。</p>
