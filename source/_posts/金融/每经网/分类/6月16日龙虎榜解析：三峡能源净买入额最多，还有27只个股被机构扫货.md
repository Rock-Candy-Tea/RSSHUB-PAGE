<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>6月16日，共有249只个股上榜龙虎榜，三峡能源龙虎榜净买入额最多，达4.9亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/09bc6a8651e04b7580149c4d4c2d73d9?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有27只，净买入额前三的是三峡能源、东鹏饮料、飞利信，分别为3.69亿元、2.88亿元、5526.64万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/f3d1c0ff687b4fa19ffe3f5b2cd6401b?nbddata-height=1170&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有3只，海油发展的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入3213.14万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/c46a6c6e5bb840f3b64d83038b2f7ad3?nbddata-height=510&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有9只，雅化集团的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入5003.75万元。</p>
