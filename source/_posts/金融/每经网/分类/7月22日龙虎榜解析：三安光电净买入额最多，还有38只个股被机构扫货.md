<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>7月22日，共有268只个股上榜龙虎榜，三安光电龙虎榜净买入额最多，达21.54亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/00f14e21e79a431181b9e70e313a1eb4?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有38只，净买入额前三的是斯达半导、中国宝安、西藏矿业，分别为5.36亿元、2.54亿元、1.56亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/4b8c84a3ef0d441f9f5199c34b721561?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有5只，三安光电的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入5.03亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/63acc38838324d2daa67178c38112a58?nbddata-height=702&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有6只，全志科技的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入1.45亿元。</p>
