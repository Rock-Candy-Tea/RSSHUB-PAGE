<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>6月4日，共有236只个股上榜龙虎榜，盛新锂能龙虎榜净买入额最多，达2.37亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/6c3bb82450a64f78803c9be59167deab?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有19只，净买入额前三的是鼎胜新材、富满电子、富临精工，分别为7514.96万元、7481.07万元、6960.98万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/7c4b2494ad20475284db3531caff175e?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有4只，百联股份的沪股通专用席位净卖出额最大，净卖出1.2亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/7498f889e50b41f5897f3ec9076dba96?nbddata-height=606&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有5只，盛新锂能的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入1.01亿元。</p>
