<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>本周一共发生510笔大宗交易，其中67笔溢价，117笔平价，326笔折价，共成交142.91亿元，环比增加28.45%，涉及214家上市公司。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/f42e43fad73a4fef87fcf61a49ed62b8?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>本周大宗交易累计成交金额前3的公司是牧原股份、中环股份、太阳纸业，累计成交金额分别为18.03亿元，13.44亿元，9.61亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/ce9cb10430ab49f1a72c305d78d359c6?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>本周机构专用席位参与了143笔大宗交易，环比上周增加46笔。本周机构席位买入金额前3的公司为牧原股份、中环股份、京东方A，机构买入金额分别为14.45亿元、13.44亿元、4.35亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/77ec857cd8884e15a1048b3e79f138b6?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
