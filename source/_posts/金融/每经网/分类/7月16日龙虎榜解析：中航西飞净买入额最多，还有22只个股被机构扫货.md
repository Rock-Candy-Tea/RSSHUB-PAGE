<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>7月16日，共有258只个股上榜龙虎榜，中航西飞龙虎榜净买入额最多，达4.3亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/ba1e5b5bdef443f78087eb580813453f?nbddata-height=1176&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有22只，净买入额前三的是太钢不锈、中航西飞、同兴达，分别为1.84亿元、1.1亿元、9007.7万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/6fa237a1db994437acff0cbbcc76284d?nbddata-height=1140&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有2只，陆家嘴的沪股通专用席位净卖出额最大，净卖出1.75亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/78f8c07b37864f7c9655ca6e47c87768?nbddata-height=414&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有14只，太钢不锈的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入1.43亿元。</p>
