<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>6月11日，共有252只个股上榜龙虎榜，小康股份龙虎榜净买入额最多，达4.13亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/b4ca9b20c2064eff83be8d892d7447d4?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有20只，净买入额前三的是光启技术、德业股份、豫能控股，分别为4.87亿元、4014.71万元、1810.66万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/75b7d0e3104049398c346c43c8293fc7?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有3只，福斯特的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入2.16亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/5e76656526e8457ab774d4207b86229b?nbddata-height=510&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有8只，永太科技的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入1.11亿元。</p>
