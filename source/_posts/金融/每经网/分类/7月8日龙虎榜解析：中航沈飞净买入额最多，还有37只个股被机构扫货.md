<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>7月8日，共有265只个股上榜龙虎榜，中航沈飞龙虎榜净买入额最多，达5.85亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/07d6a4d8fb894c1b87eb793fde188283?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有37只，净买入额前三的是中航沈飞、精测电子、富临精工，分别为4亿元、2.4亿元、1.95亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/9eb8698c68bd4ca89c2ed55e1deb3380?nbddata-height=1176&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有3只，中航沈飞的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入3.54亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/bc78c22ab87c4c13a7ddefbb2abb8728?nbddata-height=510&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有11只，精测电子的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入1.47亿元。</p>
