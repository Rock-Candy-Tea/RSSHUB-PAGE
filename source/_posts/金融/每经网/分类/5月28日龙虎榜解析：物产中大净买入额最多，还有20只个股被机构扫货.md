<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>5月28日，共有234只个股上榜龙虎榜，物产中大龙虎榜净买入额最多，达2.2亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/b9147758bbb04175a0e3a49e02bbe8d7?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有20只，净买入额前三的是中伟股份、润和软件、恩捷股份，分别为1.07亿元、4521.2万元、3897.61万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/b047147a668f430ea2afc98e3fbc194f?nbddata-height=1176&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有2只，九洲药业的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入6113.56万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/24dd0a8d439d42d7b20067d7bfe4f2c8?nbddata-height=414&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有6只，恩捷股份的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入6533.35万元。</p>
