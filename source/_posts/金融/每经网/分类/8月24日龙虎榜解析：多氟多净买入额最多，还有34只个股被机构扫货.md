<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>8月24日，共有295只个股上榜龙虎榜，多氟多龙虎榜净买入额最多，达6.6亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/01406c47e84241a394d8f6ff61d23ce9?nbddata-height=1176&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有34只，净买入额前三的是怡球资源、鄂尔多斯、东方盛虹，分别为1.93亿元、1.85亿元、1.63亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/104d0a3e128849e492dc6b714c6b1e09?nbddata-height=1170&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有2只，鄂尔多斯的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入1.06亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/1d89ff6e268843f19127e97c03a058bf?nbddata-height=414&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有6只，多氟多的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入4.69亿元。</p>
