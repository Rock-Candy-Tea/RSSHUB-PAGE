<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>3月29日，共有213只个股上榜龙虎榜，深圳能源龙虎榜净买入额最多，达2.16亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/013100c2fe9245d2a479fb17d3a202db?nbddata-height=1140&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有33只，净买入额前三的是深圳能源、长源电力、朗姿股份，分别为6386.26万元、4111.43万元、2490.96万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/f5db10676d5545c191265bdab9588276?nbddata-height=1146&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有2只，新天绿能的沪股通专用席位净卖出额最大，净卖出1394.92万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/fe40e3cacca341088737cdfe67fbab3e?nbddata-height=414&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有10只，深圳能源的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入1.57亿元。</p>
