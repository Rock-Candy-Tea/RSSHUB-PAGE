<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>本周A股共72家公司购买理财产品68.76亿元，金额环比上周减少119.59%。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/32315046-8d12-4e33-bf6f-31b10f0737d3?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>从买入金额上来看，购买理财产品金额排名前三的公司是海信家电（000921.SZ）、安通控股（600179.SH）、密封科技（301020.SZ），买入金额分别为5.00亿元、4.50亿元、3.30亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/7240bbda-8520-422a-80b3-9aa79acc695d?nbddata-height=1423&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>从上市公司所属行业上来看，本周买入理财产品金额排名前三的行业分别是汽车、电气设备、机械设备。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/66c4a2fa-fd2e-45a9-a79c-70f9d1269a71?nbddata-height=1423&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>从上市公司所属地区上来看，本周买入理财产品金额排名前三的地区分别是广东省、山东省、上海。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/97c88fcf-456d-473c-8fe3-fb6e4c7860ca?nbddata-height=1423&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
