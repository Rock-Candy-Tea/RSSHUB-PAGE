<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>7月2日，共有260只个股上榜龙虎榜，金力永磁龙虎榜净买入额最多，达2.25亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/e5abaadddcf3410ab8377cccdde886ec?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有19只，净买入额前三的是江特电机、健麾信息、中钨高新，分别为7835.03万元、5988.56万元、3431.53万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/0dbbba89bcdb45c4a910dbc727f9fda5?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有3只，天地科技的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入6823.05万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/9210e19073104d57bcf377dd400d829e?nbddata-height=510&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有7只，金力永磁的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入1亿元。</p>
