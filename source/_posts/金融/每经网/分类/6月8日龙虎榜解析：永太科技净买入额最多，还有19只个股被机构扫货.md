<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>6月8日，共有250只个股上榜龙虎榜，永太科技龙虎榜净买入额最多，达1.29亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/b5135a9c91534f1da18bb796a7104ea7?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有19只，净买入额前三的是露笑科技、永太科技、润和软件，分别为4024.34万元、3679.84万元、1911.15万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/552d95f8a1b145f4b342c16a1c596a81?nbddata-height=1176&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有3只，一拖股份的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入5879.58万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/2c296a1f90a947d88a2b58c485383559?nbddata-height=510&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有10只，中国宝安的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入2.55亿元。</p>
