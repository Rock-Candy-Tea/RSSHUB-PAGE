        <p>3月29日，美股盘前，此前已经“大吐血”的中概教育股再度走低，四季教育跌9.4%，好未来、跟谁学跌超5%，网易有道、新东方跌3.7%。</p>
<p>今日，新华社发布评论文章《半月谈丨不能让资本把教育变成一场“内卷游戏”》。</p>
<p>文章称，<span>从广告电视宣传到互联网的大幅广告页面，从公交车站大屏到小区电梯小屏，教培机构似乎无处</span><span class="s-color-red">不</span><span>在。广告中，</span><span class="s-color-red">不</span><span>仅有</span><span class="s-color-red">“</span><span>别人家的孩子在上课</span><span class="s-color-red">”</span><span>的灵魂考问，还有用</span><span class="s-color-red">“</span><span>低价</span><span class="s-color-red">”</span><span>包装的消费诱惑。</span></p>
<p><span class="s-color-red">资本</span><span>是逐利的，放在哪个行业都如此。在占了小便宜的同时，我们也成为</span><span class="s-color-red">资本</span><span>盯上的</span><span class="s-color-red">“</span><span>流量</span><span class="s-color-red">”</span><span>。如果</span><span class="s-color-red">不</span><span>加有效约束，</span><span class="s-color-red">资本</span><span>将无可避免地以高获利为导向，无底线迎合短期</span><span class="s-color-red">“</span><span>提分</span><span class="s-color-red">”</span><span>的功利性需求，甚至</span><span class="s-color-red">不</span><span>惜制造群体性焦虑，</span><span class="s-color-red">把</span><span class="s-color-red">教育</span><span class="s-color-red">变成</span><span class="s-color-red">一</span><span class="s-color-red">场</span><span class="s-color-red">“</span><span class="s-color-red">内</span><span class="s-color-red">卷</span><span class="s-color-red">游戏</span><span class="s-color-red">”</span><span>，最后得利的只有</span><span class="s-color-red">资本</span><span>。</span></p>
<p>文章称，要提高准入门槛，<span class="s-color-red">不</span>是人人都<span class="s-color-red">能</span><span class="s-color-red">“</span>传道授业<span class="s-color-red">”</span>。同时要加强资金监管，保障学生与家长合法权益。最为关键的是，要节制<span class="s-color-red">资本</span>无底线逐利冲动，保证<span class="s-color-red">资本</span>在有序、可控的前提下助力<span class="s-color-red">教育</span>发展。政府机构也要积极引导校外培训机构健康、规范发展，引领<span class="s-color-red">资本</span>在良好的市<span class="s-color-red">场</span>秩序下为<span class="s-color-red">教育</span>行业的健康发展添砖加瓦。</p>
