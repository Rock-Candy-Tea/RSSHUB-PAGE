<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>6月22日，共有258只个股上榜龙虎榜，海信视像龙虎榜净买入额最多，达6.36亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/b83118133c164eca9bdb8f47724ecf75?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有28只，净买入额前三的是海信视像、福莱特、东方锆业，分别为5.83亿元、4.5亿元、3925.7万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/7a66ab5d24e2412aad74384f58433e35?nbddata-height=1176&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有4只，物产中大的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入1.68亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/66018899ce564d328a26a84418207fe2?nbddata-height=606&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有3只，五矿稀土的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入8787.12万元。</p>
