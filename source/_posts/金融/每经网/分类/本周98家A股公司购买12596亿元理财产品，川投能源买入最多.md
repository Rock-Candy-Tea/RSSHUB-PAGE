<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>本周A股共98家公司购买理财产品125.96亿元，金额环比上周减少1.84%。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/47d0b8f0-f96a-48c0-8bba-c6cd0bb8c112?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>从买入金额上来看，购买理财产品金额排名前三的公司是川投能源（600674.SH）、美畅股份（300861.SZ）、上工申贝（600843.SH），买入金额分别为15.10亿元、6.10亿元、5.50亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/6fee5aee-ce5f-4d12-b3ed-885f37a26b11?nbddata-height=1423&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>从上市公司所属行业上来看，本周买入理财产品金额排名前三的行业分别是机械设备、公用事业、化工。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/1e50e21e-1d7e-41f6-96fa-401704b8fe7e?nbddata-height=1423&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>从上市公司所属地区上来看，本周买入理财产品金额排名前三的地区分别是广东省、江苏省、四川省。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/41963cf8-7ff8-421f-93a0-6474e436bb7e?nbddata-height=1423&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
