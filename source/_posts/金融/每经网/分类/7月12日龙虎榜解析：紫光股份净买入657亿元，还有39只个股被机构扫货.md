<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>7月12日，共有270只个股上榜龙虎榜，紫光股份龙虎榜净买入额最多，达6.57亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/3470754474834bd0bd26ce7cef37d840?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有39只，净买入额前三的是西藏矿业、江淮汽车、赣锋锂业，分别为2.67亿元、2.61亿元、2.49亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/5b37c22adcb54945a708f1ac7c7f51c8?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有2只，威派格的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入333.9万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/754114c2f9a24ffebc29522aa697317e?nbddata-height=414&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有11只，紫光股份的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入2.29亿元。</p>
