<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>8月23日，共有284只个股上榜龙虎榜，川能动力龙虎榜净买入额最多，达5.2亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/9cf3933b66f94eb6ab1058cc2d743592?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有35只，净买入额前三的是华能国际、新华医疗、鲁北化工，分别为6.36亿元、3.44亿元、2.38亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/9e4f445231df43bab27c61e81341da01?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有6只，广汇能源的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入4825.36万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/d1d5d6e8891c4ca598c9209bbb0d0e2f?nbddata-height=798&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有9只，川能动力的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入2.64亿元。</p>
