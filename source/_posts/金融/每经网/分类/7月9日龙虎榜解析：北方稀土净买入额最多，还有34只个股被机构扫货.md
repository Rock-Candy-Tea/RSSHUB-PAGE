<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>7月9日，共有263只个股上榜龙虎榜，北方稀土龙虎榜净买入额最多，达9.2亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/9f7a9b25a07f4d4ea6d5a58184c7f382?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有34只，净买入额前三的是韵达股份、北方稀土、厦门钨业，分别为3.8亿元、2.83亿元、1.83亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/8fbd8fa179034c448c145b7a48984d53?nbddata-height=1176&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有6只，北方稀土的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入3.27亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/85b7c473b3eb4e468010e37e8d6c6936?nbddata-height=798&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有9只，格林美的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入2.14亿元。</p>
