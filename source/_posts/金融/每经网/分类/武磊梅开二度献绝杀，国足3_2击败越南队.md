        <p>据中国足球队微博，在刚刚结束的2022国际足联卡塔尔世界杯预选赛亚洲区最终阶段(12强赛）B组第3轮的比赛中，凭借张玉宁的补射破门和武磊的梅开二度，中国队以3:2击败越南队，取得中国队在12强赛中的首场胜利！</p>
<p><span class="img-bitmap-box"><img src="https://rmrbcmsonline.peopleapp.com/upload/ueditor/image/20211008/a_630866841106903040.jpeg?x-oss-process=image/format,jpg/auto-orient,1" alt width="720" height="422" data-bitmap="https://rmrbcmsonline.peopleapp.com/upload/ueditor/image/20211008/a_630866841106903040.jpeg?x-oss-process=image/format,jpg/auto-orient,1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p>本场比赛的进球全都出现在下半场。张玉宁补射为国足首开纪录，武磊头球建功，随后越南队连进两球扳平比分，武磊最后时刻破门上演绝杀。</p>
<p><span class="img-bitmap-box"><img src="https://rmrbcmsonline.peopleapp.com/upload/ueditor/image/20211008/a_630866841106903041.gif" alt width="472" height="267" data-bitmap="https://rmrbcmsonline.peopleapp.com/upload/ueditor/image/20211008/a_630866841106903041.gif" data-gifffer-width="472" data-gifffer-height="267" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p>最终中国男足3:2战胜越南，取得本届世预赛12强赛的首场胜利。</p>
<p><span class="img-bitmap-box"><img src="https://rmrbcmsonline.peopleapp.com/upload/ueditor/image/20211008/a_630866841106903042.jpeg?x-oss-process=image/format,jpg/auto-orient,1" alt width="720" height="480" data-bitmap="https://rmrbcmsonline.peopleapp.com/upload/ueditor/image/20211008/a_630866841106903042.jpeg?x-oss-process=image/format,jpg/auto-orient,1" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p>北京时间10月13日凌晨1点，国足将客场挑战沙特阿拉伯。</p>
