<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>7月14日，共有277只个股上榜龙虎榜，融捷股份龙虎榜净买入额最多，达2.42亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/e87d4aa528324543a58b15f2a16e434f?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有35只，净买入额前三的是五矿稀土、融捷股份、金城医药，分别为1.8亿元、1.68亿元、1.18亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/687589f26fb94ba6a28451cc27794e54?nbddata-height=1176&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有2只，金石资源的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入2540.41万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/37b9d62d415e4a6cbfad01cc17cc0e63?nbddata-height=414&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有13只，卫 士 通的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入5239.45万元。</p>
