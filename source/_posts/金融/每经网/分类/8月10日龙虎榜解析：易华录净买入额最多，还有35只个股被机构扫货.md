<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>8月10日，共有273只个股上榜龙虎榜，易华录龙虎榜净买入额最多，达2.36亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/83405fa98d1e469d9ca9a19ec3d80964?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有35只，净买入额前三的是上能电气、永和股份、新劲刚，分别为2.39亿元、1.11亿元、4969.88万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/bb329360554447cb95f10ee3102a54bb?nbddata-height=1176&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有1只，西藏珠峰的沪股通专用席位净卖出额最大，净卖出5024.87万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/608c8e3fd0e246b8a6be61375cdbe599?nbddata-height=318&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有7只，易华录的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入6684.9万元。</p>
