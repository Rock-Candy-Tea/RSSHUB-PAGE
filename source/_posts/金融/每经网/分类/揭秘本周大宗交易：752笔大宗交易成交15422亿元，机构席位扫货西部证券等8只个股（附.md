<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>本周一共发生752笔大宗交易，其中47笔溢价，136笔平价，569笔折价，共成交154.22亿元，环比减少1.95%，涉及263家上市公司。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/20cb74ceabb44f0fb7970fe6485d0df1?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>本周大宗交易累计成交金额前3的公司是君实生物-U、西部证券、中微公司，累计成交金额分别为14.46亿元，5.77亿元，4.48亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/68c1b97544d2406bb67cd4ef81327af0?nbddata-height=858&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>本周机构专用席位参与了145笔大宗交易，环比上周减少19笔。本周机构席位买入金额前3的公司为西部证券、首旅酒店、嘉元科技，机构买入金额分别为5.4亿元、1.95亿元、1.87亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/34f6cba1563747c0a4ea1cff40805b67?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/335f6a44e075434e8f72c163a612803e?nbddata-height=954&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>溢价交易方面，本周有6笔大宗交易溢价率超过10%。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/3891446686f84438a43d7ec24f70128c?nbddata-height=787&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
