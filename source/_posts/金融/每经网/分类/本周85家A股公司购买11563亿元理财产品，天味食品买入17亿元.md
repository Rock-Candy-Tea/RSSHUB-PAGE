<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>本周A股共85家公司购买理财产品115.63亿元，金额环比上周减少67.83%。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/9fba5b4b-24d8-4dd5-a1b8-415112967622?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>从买入金额上来看，购买理财产品金额排名前三的公司是天味食品（603317.SH）、深深房A（000029.SZ）、宏盛科技（600817.SH），买入金额分别为17.00亿元、13.00亿元、5.70亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/6cb961ce-1f6a-476f-8e81-01a2dc04b06e?nbddata-height=1423&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>从上市公司所属行业上来看，本周买入理财产品金额排名前三的行业分别是食品饮料、医药生物、房地产。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/ff3d5d3d-e04e-46fa-8741-4769115264f9?nbddata-height=1423&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>从上市公司所属地区上来看，本周买入理财产品金额排名前三的地区分别是广东省、浙江省、四川省。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/13bff0d6-0b3d-4aad-a9f0-c582851fec2d?nbddata-height=1363&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
