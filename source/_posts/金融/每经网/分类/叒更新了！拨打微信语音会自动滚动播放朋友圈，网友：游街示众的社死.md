        <p><span>微信又双叒更新了！这次…又是什么“神奇的”更新呢？</span>      </p>

<p><img src="https://tmp-file-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com//gov_img/2021/10/14/1072075164198195200.gov_img/2021/10/14/8076115583236786176.png" alt="8076115583236786176.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>      <p><img src="https://tmp-file-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com//gov_img/2021/10/14/4305446834833317888.gov_img/2021/10/14/4784882737518328832.png" alt="4784882737518328832.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>      <p><span><strong></strong></span></p>
<p><strong><img src="https://tmp-file-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com//gov_img/2021/10/14/8392215257919752192.gov_img/2021/10/14/6834358425567840256.png" alt="6834358425567840256.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></strong></p>
<strong></strong>      <p><span><strong><span>拨打微信语音时</span></strong></span>      </p>
<span><strong><span>会自动滚动播放朋友圈</span></strong></span>      <p><span>小编查询发现，这个是微信在9月底更新的新功能。此前仅在微信iOS更新了，但是现在部分更新到8.015版的安卓用户也可以使用该功能了。</span>      </p>
<p><span>更新了微信新版本的朋友发现，新版本除上线关怀模式、群聊折叠等功能外，还新增了一个语音彩蛋。</span>      </p>
<p><span>简单说就是，</span>       <span>当你在微信里用“语音通话”，拨打朋友电话时，将自动播放对方的朋友圈，</span>       <strong><span>并以“幻灯片”的</span></strong>       <strong><span>形式滚动播放</span></strong>       <span>。</span>      </p>
<p><span>经过测试，当向好友发起语音通话时，可以在通话界面中查看对方的朋友圈，不过</span>       <strong><span>只能看到对方三天内发出的朋友圈。</span></strong>      </p>
<p><img src="https://tmp-file-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com//gov_img/2021/10/14/2861108680154172416.gov_img/2021/10/14/9134224595844461568.png" alt="9134224595844461568.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>      <p><img src="https://tmp-file-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com//gov_img/2021/10/14/6752831415090084864.gov_img/2021/10/14/5732285161737677824.png" alt="5732285161737677824.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>      <p><span>另外，界面中间新增</span>       <strong><span>“看他的近况”</span></strong>       <span>按钮，点击还可弹出对方三天内的朋友圈动态，包括朋友圈文案、图片、定位。</span>      </p>
<p><img src="https://tmp-file-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com//gov_img/2021/10/14/7317843240957535232.gov_img/2021/10/14/459023656650539008.png" alt="459023656650539008.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>      <p><span><strong></strong></span></p>
<p><strong><img src="https://tmp-file-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com//gov_img/2021/10/14/5880212669799738368.gov_img/2021/10/14/3308466002826793984.png" alt="3308466002826793984.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></strong></p>
<strong></strong>      <p><span><strong><span>网友们吐槽：</span></strong></span>       <strong><span>游街示众的社死</span></strong>      </p>
<p><strong><span>找不到功能可以不开发</span></strong>      </p>
<p><span>微信官方并未在更新中提及此功能，现在似乎也不是所有用户都能体验到，但是一经曝光，立刻就让众多网友炸开了锅，</span>       <strong><span>直言这又是个要把大家游街示众的社死新功能…</span></strong>      </p>
<p><strong></strong>      </p>
<p><img src="https://tmp-file-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com//gov_img/2021/10/14/3451392370151000064.gov_img/2021/10/14/1647713322945851392.jpeg" alt="1647713322945851392.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>      <p><img src="https://tmp-file-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com//gov_img/2021/10/14/7820169136107932672.gov_img/2021/10/14/1234629543059944448.png" alt="1234629543059944448.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>      <p><span>有网友吐槽：屏蔽了谁这下都知道了，这个功能也太社死了吧……</span>      </p>
<p><span>网友们纷纷表示很担心如果朋友圈屏蔽对方了还能看到吗？还能看到就很尴尬了；分组可见呢，也能播放吗？如果对方权限了你，结果你打语音时看见了，那...</span>      </p>
<p><img src="https://tmp-file-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com//gov_img/2021/10/14/3955815274058650624.gov_img/2021/10/14/3061464543872856064.jpeg" alt="3061464543872856064.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>      <p><img src="https://tmp-file-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com//gov_img/2021/10/14/5625080718058447872.gov_img/2021/10/14/7307357576497269760.jpeg" alt="7307357576497269760.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>      <p><img src="https://tmp-file-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com//gov_img/2021/10/14/6200156884248229888.gov_img/2021/10/14/4715910556547942400.jpeg" alt="4715910556547942400.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>      <p><img src="https://tmp-file-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com//gov_img/2021/10/14/6036519476076665856.gov_img/2021/10/14/9200485188189998080.jpeg" alt="9200485188189998080.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>      <p><img src="https://tmp-file-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com//gov_img/2021/10/14/5518975488261214208.gov_img/2021/10/14/5690720223032882176.jpeg" alt="5690720223032882176.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>      <p><img src="https://tmp-file-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com//gov_img/2021/10/14/2936325090280504320.gov_img/2021/10/14/3068575225324870656.jpeg" alt="3068575225324870656.jpeg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>      <p><img src="https://tmp-file-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com//gov_img/2021/10/14/1712824802460394496.gov_img/2021/10/14/3154542088457037824.png" alt="3154542088457037824.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>      <p><span>经小编测试，</span>       <strong><span>分组可见的朋友圈在语音通话时，依旧是看不到的。</span></strong>      </p>
<p><span>还有网友已经脑补了大型社死现场的尴尬。比如甲乙丙三个人都认识，然后甲和乙在一起时，甲给丙发了语音通话，然后就可以看到丙最近的朋友圈状态，但是丙其实是屏蔽了乙的，那么当乙从甲的手机上看到了丙的朋友圈状态，该有多尴尬？</span>      </p>

<p><span>有网友表示，今天不小心在群里点了主任头像拍一拍，还撤不回，个个都来问我你拍一拍主任干嘛，又不说话（尴尬表情）。</span>      </p>

<p><span>不少网友祈祷下线“拍一拍”功能的时候，微信居然还上线了“炸一炸”，网友表示“这简直是社死的进阶版”。</span>      </p>
