<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>7月30日，共有274只个股上榜龙虎榜，天齐锂业龙虎榜净买入额最多，达3.26亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/dede6d70d81746208932a88abaa45844?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有31只，净买入额前三的是天齐锂业、宁波韵升、上能电气，分别为5.02亿元、1.4亿元、1.15亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/cd985cf5c9584c62b79ed7584fa8f027?nbddata-height=1146&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有2只，重庆啤酒的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入1.08亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/fe89240fe4794932be61eece81c02f9d?nbddata-height=414&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有4只，博晖创新的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入23.43万元。</p>
