<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>7月7日，共有262只个股上榜龙虎榜，南极电商龙虎榜净买入额最多，达2.51亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/0118986cee0c4512bbd754ae48da4bb0?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有29只，净买入额前三的是富临精工、仁和药业、川发龙蟒，分别为2.44亿元、1.14亿元、1.12亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/1705a65e05db4137a80e324aa650629a?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有3只，广汽集团的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入4197.01万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/8fe9f7862ea0490e93251801215d068c?nbddata-height=510&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有11只，紫光国微的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入1.79亿元。</p>
