<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>8月20日，共有291只个股上榜龙虎榜，N电信龙虎榜净买入额最多，达7.92亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/3f96288cdc6f4ba5ac07fd4aa11c3b3a?nbddata-height=1146&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有42只，净买入额前三的是盐湖股份、中直股份、旗滨集团，分别为4.03亿元、3.43亿元、2.86亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/62e79f0688e24da8bb0799eff17248ec?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有5只，中直股份的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入2.64亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/616e4021e46e4b9992e28a7a8c26b969?nbddata-height=702&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有14只，迈瑞医疗的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入6.79亿元。</p>
