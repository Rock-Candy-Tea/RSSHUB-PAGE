<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>8月27日，共有306只个股上榜龙虎榜，格林美龙虎榜净买入额最多，达6.64亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/11dd3715ea7b45b6a523b8a851338bd7?nbddata-height=1146&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有36只，净买入额前三的是中国化学、盛屯矿业、紫江企业，分别为5.95亿元、2.6亿元、1.77亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/74fd29d1a19a43eabacefd4090697fc8?nbddata-height=1146&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有6只，兖州煤业的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入1.89亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/8a05ce4dd0774f329de103d72bfa8e65?nbddata-height=798&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有8只，歌尔股份的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入3.66亿元。</p>
