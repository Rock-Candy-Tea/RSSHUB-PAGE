<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>本周A股共90家公司购买理财产品105.37亿元，金额环比上周减少62.65%。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/6a022661-fbd7-498b-bba7-c4643a1f85fc?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>从买入金额上来看，购买理财产品金额排名前三的公司是龙净环保（600388.SH）、君正集团（601216.SH）、林洋能源（601222.SH），买入金额分别为7.20亿元、7.00亿元、5.00亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/1dc20f9a-d0b5-4d7f-92c0-b5e8e9767ffa?nbddata-height=1423&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>从上市公司所属行业上来看，本周买入理财产品金额排名前三的行业分别是机械设备、化工、电子。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/6cc0df63-a7ff-470b-b182-d5cfb32039b1?nbddata-height=1423&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>从上市公司所属地区上来看，本周买入理财产品金额排名前三的地区分别是广东省、江苏省、浙江省。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/231f2a6f-5590-4c42-ab90-977105576efc?nbddata-height=1363&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
