<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>7月6日，共有269只个股上榜龙虎榜，江特电机龙虎榜净买入额最多，达2.86亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/cc34e7ff819a480ea1985ca6e61f6c96?nbddata-height=1146&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有30只，净买入额前三的是泰晶科技、泛微网络、三美股份，分别为1.86亿元、1.15亿元、8257.26万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/a727127b150041ce87b77668b630c4c8?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有3只，泛微网络的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入1351.59万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/cd0ea7662f84404098c0b802f48a4355?nbddata-height=510&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有5只，五矿稀土的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入9435.78万元。</p>
