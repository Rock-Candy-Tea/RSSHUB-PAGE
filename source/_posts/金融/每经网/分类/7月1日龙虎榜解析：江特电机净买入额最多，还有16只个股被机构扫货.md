<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>7月1日，共有253只个股上榜龙虎榜，江特电机龙虎榜净买入额最多，达1.5亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/cb8ce88ea5cd4c8cadce906846c7917d?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有16只，净买入额前三的是江特电机、三孚股份、共同药业，分别为1.04亿元、4339.17万元、1427.27万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/37fe0ebd08554446895b3d92fd376476?nbddata-height=1176&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有1只，物产中大的沪股通专用席位净卖出额最大，净卖出2.63亿元。</p>
