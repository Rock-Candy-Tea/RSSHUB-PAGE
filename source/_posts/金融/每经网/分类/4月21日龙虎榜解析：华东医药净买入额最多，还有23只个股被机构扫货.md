<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>4月21日，共有221只个股上榜龙虎榜，华东医药龙虎榜净买入额最多，达2.96亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/663f741c09eb42488fd9e940ccb8c26d?nbddata-height=1146&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有23只，净买入额前三的是华东医药、三只松鼠、朗姿股份，分别为1.63亿元、6763.56万元、2902.41万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/731340e01f754c7f9fd1bd1fe990c7c4?nbddata-height=1170&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有4只，格力地产的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入2001.27万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/199c3ded66eb488ba74e0fa40849a0e0?nbddata-height=606&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有5只，华东医药的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入1.16亿元。</p>
