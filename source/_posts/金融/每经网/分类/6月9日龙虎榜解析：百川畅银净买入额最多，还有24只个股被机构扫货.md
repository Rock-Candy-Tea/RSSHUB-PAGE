<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>6月9日，共有249只个股上榜龙虎榜，百川畅银龙虎榜净买入额最多，达4142.74万元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/7d695ae40d1c483189a061050a2a613d?nbddata-height=1176&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有24只，净买入额前三的是同为股份、立方制药、光启技术，分别为7523.27万元、3304.49万元、2987.48万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/408d1ae987b44e33ace93f5cc0a0d592?nbddata-height=1170&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有4只，光启技术的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入7860.08万元。</p>
