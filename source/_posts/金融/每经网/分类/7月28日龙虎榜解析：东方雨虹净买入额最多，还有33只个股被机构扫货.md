<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>7月28日，共有273只个股上榜龙虎榜，东方雨虹龙虎榜净买入额最多，达9.51亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/05a9f13abfe148378ddebfb43cb740f7?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有33只，净买入额前三的是北京君正、水井坊、金辰股份，分别为2.87亿元、2.79亿元、9082.47万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/d0b376eff905462daf546eca4f47d077?nbddata-height=1176&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有2只，凯乐科技的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入87.18万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/fbbea10281c54076898f32eb66c1e5a6?nbddata-height=414&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有8只，东方雨虹的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入11.72亿元。</p>
