<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>8月18日，共有288只个股上榜龙虎榜，广发证券龙虎榜净买入额最多，达3.71亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/903e589d80144665a4c4b3f744a5549c?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有29只，净买入额前三的是广发证券、东方电气、盛通股份，分别为1亿元、7687.85万元、6986.49万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/84ceb021926245448c157c6aec218ffc?nbddata-height=1176&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有5只，东吴证券的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入2.5亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/6553c96ee76e437280c51a8935224900?nbddata-height=702&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有7只，航发控制的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入9301.14万元。</p>
