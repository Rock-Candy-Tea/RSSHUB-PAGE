<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>8月25日，共有286只个股上榜龙虎榜，卫士通龙虎榜净买入额最多，达2.44亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/3fc20dfb2a234678bcad8e85b3a4c438?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有35只，净买入额前三的是卫 士 通、华峰铝业、黄河旋风，分别为3.06亿元、1.92亿元、1.53亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/af9397f0fdeb49aa9c0331f750dc57b9?nbddata-height=1146&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有3只，万业企业的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入347.56万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/4c2385f83b7447709613eb1104e2df51?nbddata-height=510&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有8只，卫 士 通的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入4448.52万元。</p>
