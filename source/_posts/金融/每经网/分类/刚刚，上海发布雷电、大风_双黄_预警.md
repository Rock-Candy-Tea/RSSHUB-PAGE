        <p>据上海发布，上海中心气象台2021年04月30日20时57分发布雷电黄色预警信号：受雷暴云团影响，预计6小时内全市大部地区将发生雷电活动。</p>
<p><span class="pic-con"><img src="https://rs1.huanqiucdn.cn/dp/api/files/imageDir/4610de5529936b6a976e5b5b875d6cab.png" alt data-alt data-upload-link="%7B%22cover%22%3A%22%22%2C%22desc%22%3A%22%22%2C%22id%22%3A%22vcd0k3_920024%22%2C%22size%22%3A272.87%2C%22width%22%3A480%2C%22height%22%3A480%2C%22url%22%3A%22https%3A%2F%2Fm1-1253159997.image.myqcloud.com%2FimageDir%2F4610de5529936b6a976e5b5b875d6cab.png%22%2C%22tags%22%3A%5B%5D%2C%22time%22%3A%22%22%2C%22mime%22%3A%22image%2Fpng%22%7D" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span class="pic-con">图片来源：“上海发布”微信号</span></p>
<p>此前稍早，上海中心气象台更新大风蓝色为大风黄色预警信号：受雷暴云团影响，预计未来6小时内全市将出现9-11级雷雨大风，风向偏北，大风蓝色预警信号更新为大风黄色预警信号。</p>
<p><span class="pic-con"><img src="https://rs1.huanqiucdn.cn/dp/api/files/imageDir/64e1679eb607af4c81ef77e6432e9739.png" alt data-alt data-upload-link="%7B%22cover%22%3A%22%22%2C%22desc%22%3A%22%22%2C%22id%22%3A%22a0ux37_856323%22%2C%22size%22%3A27.81%2C%22width%22%3A414%2C%22height%22%3A392%2C%22url%22%3A%22https%3A%2F%2Fm1-1253159997.image.myqcloud.com%2FimageDir%2F64e1679eb607af4c81ef77e6432e9739.png%22%2C%22tags%22%3A%5B%5D%2C%22time%22%3A%22%22%2C%22mime%22%3A%22image%2Fpng%22%7D" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p>
<p><span data-scene="strong">雷电防御指南：</span></p>
<p><span data-scene="strong">1、</span>停止在空旷的地方开展户外活动。</p>
<p><span data-scene="strong">2、</span>停止在江、河、湖、海游泳。</p>
<p><span data-scene="strong">3、</span>远离大树、墙角或有突出金属物的地方；不要将有金属尖角的物品朝天握在手中或扛在肩上。</p>
<p><img src="https://rs1.huanqiucdn.cn/dp/api/files/imageDir/edd674b39fd227e40f4007acfa355a9a.png" alt data-alt data-upload-link="%7B%22cover%22%3A%22%22%2C%22desc%22%3A%22%22%2C%22id%22%3A%221pzilt_627422%22%2C%22size%22%3A30.93%2C%22width%22%3A414%2C%22height%22%3A405%2C%22url%22%3A%22https%3A%2F%2Fm1-1253159997.image.myqcloud.com%2FimageDir%2Fedd674b39fd227e40f4007acfa355a9a.png%22%2C%22tags%22%3A%5B%5D%2C%22time%22%3A%22%22%2C%22mime%22%3A%22image%2Fpng%22%7D" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>大风防御指南：</p>
<p>1、关好门窗；加固室外彩钢板、脚手架、塔吊等。</p>
<p>2、外出时尽量不要在玻璃门窗、广告牌附近逗留。</p>
