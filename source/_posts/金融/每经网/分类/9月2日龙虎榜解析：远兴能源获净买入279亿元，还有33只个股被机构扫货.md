<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>9月2日，共有290只个股上榜龙虎榜，远兴能源龙虎榜净买入额最多，达2.79亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/ec1c7b98f29246138c6bbaeaf4532e45?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有33只，净买入额前三的是广发证券、中国交建、永太科技，分别为2.42亿元、1.21亿元、7591.16万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/50459d0dde02438983de22e890f5af82?nbddata-height=1176&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有4只，中国船舶的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入1.39亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/3e41c5360bab4d78b3e41e925f3eb53a?nbddata-height=606&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有13只，金风科技的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入1.23亿元。</p>
