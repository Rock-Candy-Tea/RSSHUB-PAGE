<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>本周A股共114家公司购买理财产品147.34亿元，金额环比上周增加23.30%。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/ee73ae7c-40c3-41b8-ba86-be98401e553d?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>从买入金额上来看，购买理财产品金额排名前三的公司是北大荒（600598.SH）、新亚强（603155.SH）、仲景食品（300908.SZ），买入金额分别为9.00亿元、8.00亿元、7.51亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/752ba78d-411a-4c11-9cbc-4d5de19ae45f?nbddata-height=1423&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>从上市公司所属行业上来看，本周买入理财产品金额排名前三的行业分别是电子、化工、食品饮料。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/8bb6306b-9fdc-4fcd-8d4f-4b8747e63a0e?nbddata-height=1423&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>从上市公司所属地区上来看，本周买入理财产品金额排名前三的地区分别是江苏省、广东省、浙江省。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/5b049603-77ee-4a14-bde8-8a338902fe18?nbddata-height=1423&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
