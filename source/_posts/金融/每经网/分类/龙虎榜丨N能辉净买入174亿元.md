<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>8月17日，共有284只个股上榜龙虎榜，N能辉龙虎榜净买入额最多，达1.74亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/08c2cfa143c242ed9606fb796273caca?nbddata-height=1176&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有21只，净买入额前三的是宇新股份、科士达、N能辉，分别为8909.31万元、4274.14万元、2369.64万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/4954a1b3501546ca9b011a3ad460f780?nbddata-height=1170&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有4只，大立科技的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入5992.09万元。</p>
