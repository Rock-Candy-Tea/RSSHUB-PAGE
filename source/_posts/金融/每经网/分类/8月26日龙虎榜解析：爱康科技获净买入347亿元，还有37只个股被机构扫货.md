<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>8月26日，共有297只个股上榜龙虎榜，爱康科技龙虎榜净买入额最多，达3.47亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/5d44d3a3df6a4826afdcd8b879127d89?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有37只，净买入额前三的是华峰铝业、联泓新科、马钢股份，分别为1.74亿元、1.35亿元、1.06亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/65276b29c673456daf4b2cd74d796892?nbddata-height=1146&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有6只，合盛硅业的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入4.43亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/25627c8ec08e41c0a10f34e9dfe46133?nbddata-height=798&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有11只，锡业股份的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入2.11亿元。</p>
