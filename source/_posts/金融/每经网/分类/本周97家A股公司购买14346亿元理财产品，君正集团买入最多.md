<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>本周A股共97家公司购买理财产品143.46亿元，金额环比上周减少87.31%。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/09146cfe-bb22-4eb4-b6bc-a83f0e71fff4?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>从买入金额上来看，购买理财产品金额排名前三的公司是君正集团（601216.SH）、中鼎股份（000887.SZ）、海信家电（000921.SZ），买入金额分别为8.12亿元、7.90亿元、7.00亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/d78743c0-926a-49ae-af38-1257eae8d5df?nbddata-height=1423&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>从上市公司所属行业上来看，本周买入理财产品金额排名前三的行业分别是汽车、化工、机械设备。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/94cf0d18-0c3a-43c8-ac4a-675df14e5701?nbddata-height=1423&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>从上市公司所属地区上来看，本周买入理财产品金额排名前三的地区分别是广东省、北京、安徽省。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/7e631d10-dcb4-4547-ba60-132a5a307776?nbddata-height=1423&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
