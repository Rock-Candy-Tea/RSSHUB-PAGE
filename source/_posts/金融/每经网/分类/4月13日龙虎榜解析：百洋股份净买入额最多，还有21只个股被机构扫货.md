<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>4月13日，共有222只个股上榜龙虎榜，百洋股份龙虎榜净买入额最多，达6339.65万元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/08d4fbe26db945f8b88575d7ee26b6b1?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有21只，净买入额前三的是豫能控股、创识科技、中电环保，分别为2676.58万元、2611.55万元、2107.15万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/2709f86081e94ddf8fcbb4a2ddb4fc58?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有3只，中国中免的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入5.43亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/1ecd789be7a949f4b1bdfcf6789ad063?nbddata-height=510&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有2只，聚龙股份的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入888.67万元。</p>
