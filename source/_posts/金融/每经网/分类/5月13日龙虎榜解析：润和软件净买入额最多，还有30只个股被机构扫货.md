<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>5月13日，共有239只个股上榜龙虎榜，润和软件龙虎榜净买入额最多，达2.4亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/e62259c67c294e41842fc00ca483f061?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有30只，净买入额前三的是爱普股份、润和软件、山西焦煤，分别为1.89亿元、4459.69万元、3708.19万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/27cd7d53cf7d4d30b9f8ac37c2b94a0f?nbddata-height=1146&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有4只，恒顺醋业的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入3369.66万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/6a6c1047fb644151820b2c03083b93f5?nbddata-height=606&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有11只，云铝股份的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入1.08亿元。</p>
