<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>4月1日，共有209只个股上榜龙虎榜，全志科技龙虎榜净买入额最多，达1.22亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/f81f61f0716d43c4b1ed65d9105681f7?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有20只，净买入额前三的是全志科技、华菱钢铁、西域旅游，分别为1.44亿元、7495.46万元、1426.43万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/7aa540000c7e4be3b593dd703795a52e?nbddata-height=1176&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有1只，今创集团的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入673.45万元。</p>
