<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>8月16日，共有293只个股上榜龙虎榜，北方稀土龙虎榜净买入额最多，达6.79亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/09ec14ea5c3a4f2494332b9be524d328?nbddata-height=1176&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有28只，净买入额前三的是N义翘、承德露露、东岳硅材，分别为1.02亿元、9724.38万元、9605.07万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/5a046e05586e4346be4f306af9f3aea5?nbddata-height=1176&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有3只，北方稀土的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入3.74亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/ce3bfa4bd9c14a4cba885c17e019f7d7?nbddata-height=510&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有9只，承德露露的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入1.33亿元。</p>
