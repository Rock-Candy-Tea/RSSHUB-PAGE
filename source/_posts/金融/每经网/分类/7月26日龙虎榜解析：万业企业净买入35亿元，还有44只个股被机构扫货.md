<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>7月26日，共有287只个股上榜龙虎榜，万业企业龙虎榜净买入额最多，达3.5亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/184d725254cb461e920fa4812b688992?nbddata-height=1176&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有44只，净买入额前三的是万业企业、联创股份、芭田股份，分别为2.85亿元、1.22亿元、1.21亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/15f7f5afcd2e4f28ae5e741a84806eee?nbddata-height=1176&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有3只，科沃斯的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入3.03亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/36f6f9234bc54adeb71febeeb0b08b54?nbddata-height=510&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有12只，利君股份的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入5287.73万元。</p>
