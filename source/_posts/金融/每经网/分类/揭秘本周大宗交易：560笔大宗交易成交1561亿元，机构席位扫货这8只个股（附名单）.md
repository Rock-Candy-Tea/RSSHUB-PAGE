<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>本周一共发生560笔大宗交易，其中30笔溢价，87笔平价，443笔折价，共成交156.1亿元，环比增加28.67%，涉及227家上市公司。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/7bf61658b8d148e1a0c4883f7aa55bbf?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>本周大宗交易累计成交金额前3的公司是长春高新、宁德时代、通威股份，累计成交金额分别为19.38亿元，8.84亿元，8.72亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/c05deef224d040a8821a7eb0f9de6112?nbddata-height=787&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>本周机构专用席位参与了117笔大宗交易，环比上周增加19笔。本周机构席位买入金额前3的公司为长春高新、通威股份、视源股份，机构买入金额分别为18.74亿元、8.72亿元、7.48亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/40764a128dcc4fc2ba2621f2ab173df9?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/82c969c12cf74028b5bd602bb49f5188?nbddata-height=984&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>溢价交易方面，本周有6笔大宗交易溢价率超过10%。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/663d63be8c2447f29acd6156a209db0e?nbddata-height=787&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
