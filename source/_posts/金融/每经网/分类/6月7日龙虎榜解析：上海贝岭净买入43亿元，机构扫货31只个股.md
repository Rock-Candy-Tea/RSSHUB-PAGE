<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>6月7日，共有256只个股上榜龙虎榜，上海贝岭龙虎榜净买入额最多，达4.3亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/8944b29ed771497e97d5822f60ad4da6?nbddata-height=1146&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有31只，净买入额前三的是万兴科技、新化股份、国民技术，分别为1.01亿元、6735.47万元、4077.75万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/5db6796ffd8b469ebf6e25f9954b20e6?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有2只，上海贝岭的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入2.22亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/8ef8a620f87b459788237c9eddb765e4?nbddata-height=408&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有6只，通富微电的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入1.56亿元。</p>
