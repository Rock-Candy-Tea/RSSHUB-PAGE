<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>6月15日，共有255只个股上榜龙虎榜，瑞芯微龙虎榜净买入额最多，达3.67亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/ad123b2aaaf941729f927e368531a279?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有32只，净买入额前三的是瑞芯微、鼎胜新材、国民技术，分别为6.26亿元、1.75亿元、2762.74万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/9aa43623606f4071bfe28ebaa74f9664?nbddata-height=1146&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有2只，鼎胜新材的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入388.7万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/d42bdf261801445ea440cd7435ae45b6?nbddata-height=414&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有6只，水晶光电的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入1.14亿元。</p>
