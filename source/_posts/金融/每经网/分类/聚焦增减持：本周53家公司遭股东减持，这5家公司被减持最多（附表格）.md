<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>本周A股共有10家公司发布了增持公告，共增持1.7亿股。53家公司发布了减持公告，共减持2.85亿股。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/e971360b5b004f5f9e052e7b82851b5f?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>从增持力度上来看，本周获股东增持比例（占流通股比）前三的公司是奥马电器、*ST目药、利安隆，增持比例分别为5.19%、3.3%、2.78%。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/0cb5a603252d4ac4ba44b2d2de8096a4?nbddata-height=787&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>从减持力度上来看，本周获股东减持比例（占流通股比）前三的公司是透景生命、仁东控股、英飞特，减持比例分别为4.41%、2.99%、2.72%。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/721fff8d99254ddc9f6ad377a8f6c503?nbddata-height=1387&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>从行业上来看，发布增持公告数量最多的行业是化工，发布减持公告数量最多的行业是建筑装饰。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/bd9ae880851047bbb93620b1888ac08f?nbddata-height=1567&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>*文中所涉增减持数据为上市公司本周公告数据，非本周实际增减持数据</p>
