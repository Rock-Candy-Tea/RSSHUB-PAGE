<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>本月A股共424家公司购买理财产品858.53亿元，金额环比上月增加12.82%。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/9215fefa-b39f-4a90-8ada-0dc3d5cbb32d?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>从买入金额上来看，购买理财产品金额排名前三的公司是潍柴动力（000338.SZ）、江苏国泰（002091.SZ）、古井贡酒（000596.SZ），买入金额分别为60.00亿元、48.37亿元、37.90亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/ec40b2a7-dace-4fc2-b3d2-79f3360da8de?nbddata-height=1423&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>从上市公司所属行业上来看，本月买入理财产品金额排名前三的行业分别是食品饮料、机械设备、汽车。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/ac8495e9-40cc-4c17-8899-660a930a3004?nbddata-height=823&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
