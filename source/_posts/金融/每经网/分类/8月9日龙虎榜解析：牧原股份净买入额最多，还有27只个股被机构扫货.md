<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>8月9日，共有264只个股上榜龙虎榜，牧原股份龙虎榜净买入额最多，达3.56亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/97b32ca34d104fc29741ddb65aebab28?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有27只，净买入额前三的是上能电气、牧原股份、西藏珠峰，分别为2.39亿元、1.7亿元、1.41亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/994135c469744922a1db7eaf1fdc2bf3?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有4只，精达股份的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入6100.29万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/e0f459e561ad4cbeba8ea306554f3762?nbddata-height=606&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有9只，牧原股份的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入1.47亿元。</p>
