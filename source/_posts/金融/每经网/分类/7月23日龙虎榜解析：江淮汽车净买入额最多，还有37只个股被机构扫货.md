<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>7月23日，共有269只个股上榜龙虎榜，江淮汽车龙虎榜净买入额最多，达3.3亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/7b115b0509e54b6695ab5d398e3ba21e?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有37只，净买入额前三的是瑞芯微、德业股份、华软科技，分别为3.18亿元、1.98亿元、1.92亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/a522459985ab465bae6321c76123e90f?nbddata-height=1146&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有5只，中泰证券的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入7025.09万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/d7d0691ebade48e2bf24ae786fcd05cf?nbddata-height=702&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有5只，鸿达兴业的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入1.53亿元。</p>
