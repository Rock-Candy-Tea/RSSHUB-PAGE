<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>8月13日，共有291只个股上榜龙虎榜，大族激光龙虎榜净买入额最多，达5.24亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/63b25eb56c68428ba0c4973ada5f6c2f?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有40只，净买入额前三的是永和股份、红星发展、科士达，分别为2.03亿元、1.81亿元、1.42亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/324c982adef44145a5472ab13791ba3f?nbddata-height=1170&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有3只，兴发集团的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入3.08亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/4baca8410e614a5a96b111dc0d3dbfda?nbddata-height=510&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有9只，大族激光的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入1.78亿元。</p>
