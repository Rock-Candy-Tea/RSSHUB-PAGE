<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>7月27日，共有266只个股上榜龙虎榜，九鼎新材龙虎榜净买入额最多，达1.67亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/ac46064d69c94b13b0b2e5cd46d7665b?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有29只，净买入额前三的是五矿稀土、图南股份、至纯科技，分别为1.74亿元、1.06亿元、1.04亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/0d1b10eb3d77405d99899ca4a5f1825e?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有3只，生益科技的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入496.82万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/f434779fcfd34fa2bc773471d9d597fd?nbddata-height=510&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有5只，云铝股份的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入7317.86万元。</p>
