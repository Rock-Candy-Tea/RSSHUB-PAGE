<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>6月21日，共有251只个股上榜龙虎榜，完美世界龙虎榜净买入额最多，达3亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/c03a669b9ebe427ea05ca662838cb96e?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有22只，净买入额前三的是九洲药业、精达股份、三星医疗，分别为2.69亿元、1.59亿元、1.52亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/22ffe6c70df44053a54fe3eda99411c4?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有7只，口子窖的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入2.78亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/ab606e9e350e4c2eb6ed92f2acee9e9f?nbddata-height=894&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有9只，完美世界的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入2.18亿元。</p>
