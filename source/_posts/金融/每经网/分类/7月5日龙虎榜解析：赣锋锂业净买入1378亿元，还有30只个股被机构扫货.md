<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>7月5日，共有265只个股上榜龙虎榜，赣锋锂业龙虎榜净买入额最多，达13.78亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/0f44b6507bf6447691d3eccd8966c3d4?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有30只，净买入额前三的是奥翔药业、赣锋锂业、西藏矿业，分别为1.95亿元、1.28亿元、1.02亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/ab4e66cb214d41bdb047a05de07c37fc?nbddata-height=1146&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有3只，水井坊的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入3632.78万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/e4c8cc5354a14f4eb1c2681199a55b6e?nbddata-height=510&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有15只，赣锋锂业的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入6.64亿元。</p>
