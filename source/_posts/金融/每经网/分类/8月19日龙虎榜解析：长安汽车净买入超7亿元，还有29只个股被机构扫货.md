<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>8月19日，共有276只个股上榜龙虎榜，长安汽车龙虎榜净买入额最多，达7.01亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/7c033441e52f4f51abda9169e91b6b0d?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有29只，净买入额前三的是西藏珠峰、永太科技、长城证券，分别为2.36亿元、7864.69万元、7152.42万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/dd053c35cefd4f56867ccc5b90bd5a58?nbddata-height=1146&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有2只，西藏珠峰的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入1065.9万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/9cf6bba6f057491f904b3bd7453912e4?nbddata-height=414&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有5只，长安汽车的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入3.03亿元。</p>
