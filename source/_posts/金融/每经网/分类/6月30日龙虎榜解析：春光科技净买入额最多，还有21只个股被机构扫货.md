<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>6月30日，共有259只个股上榜龙虎榜，春光科技龙虎榜净买入额最多，达8860.61万元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/d070ef2d84784ce4aa0fd1eba1042c98?nbddata-height=1176&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有21只，净买入额前三的是春光科技、永太科技、兴齐眼药，分别为1.01亿元、7855.86万元、5669.15万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/934c6b339f004796b09af558a95040c6?nbddata-height=1170&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有1只，中新药业的沪股通专用席位净卖出额最大，净卖出841.64万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/185dcf426fa5401d8d5f6763b1291d11?nbddata-height=318&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有1只，彩讯股份的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入660.41万元。</p>
