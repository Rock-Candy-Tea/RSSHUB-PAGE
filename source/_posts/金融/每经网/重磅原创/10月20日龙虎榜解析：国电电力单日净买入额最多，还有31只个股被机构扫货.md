        <p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span>10月20日，共有47只个股上榜龙虎榜，国电电力龙虎榜单日净买入额最多，达2.21亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/d3218afbd3644a0f9615e432a4fefce0?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/fbe0aa8638034776b839daab1fdbe3a4?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有31只，净买入额前三的是川恒股份、华能国际、天顺风能，分别为6300.5万元、4947.12万元、4462.33万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/27563b48e7e346b28be85d68cc1a3955?nbddata-height=1176&nbddata-width=900" width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有4只，国电电力的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入2.1亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/d1e677f5170a44ac8aca536dbeb18307?nbddata-height=606&nbddata-width=900" width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有6只，露天煤业的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入2298.28万元。</p>
