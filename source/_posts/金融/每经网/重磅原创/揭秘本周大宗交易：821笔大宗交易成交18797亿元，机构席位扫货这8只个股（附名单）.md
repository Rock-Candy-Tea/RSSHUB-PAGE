        <p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>本周一共发生821笔大宗交易，其中67笔溢价，99笔平价，655笔折价，共成交187.97亿元，环比减少9.83%，涉及321家上市公司。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/c9cc6a767fbc4303a750d9965ef8b159?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>本周大宗交易累计成交金额前3的公司是云天化、金风科技、金地集团，累计成交金额分别为9.24亿元，7.69亿元，6.88亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/a452fdc931304f68a5d4ff90a7d4efd4?nbddata-height=787&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>本周机构专用席位参与了131笔大宗交易，环比上周减少24笔。本周机构席位买入金额前3的公司为金地集团、中国宝安、特锐德，机构买入金额分别为5.26亿元、3亿元、2.13亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/697701dd70a146c0b36043f36a849bf6?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/36e9a9e0cccc480f8b17c7ca374b4cee?nbddata-height=948&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>溢价交易方面，本周有13笔大宗交易溢价率超过10%。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/1390ec97631b41c4898ae36de8976179?nbddata-height=787&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>折价交易方面，本周有229笔大宗交易折价率超过10%。</p>
