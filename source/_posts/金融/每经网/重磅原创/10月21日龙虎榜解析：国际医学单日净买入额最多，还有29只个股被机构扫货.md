        <p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span>10月21日，共有42只个股上榜龙虎榜，国际医学龙虎榜单日净买入额最多，达1.95亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/cd4c475f69ef4fb99a2de3a2b891e77b?nbddata-height=1176&nbddata-width=900" width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/ee91ba41f46747d9a708e74fa4fb2bd0?nbddata-height=1086&nbddata-width=900" width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有29只，净买入额前三的是常熟银行、兖州煤业、国际医学，分别为2.01亿元、6914.62万元、3004.91万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/61519c01b79940b19fcc6aec27ce5964?nbddata-height=1170&nbddata-width=900" width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有4只，兖州煤业的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入1.64亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/3c92b9fc70934373a5bf418126368e06?nbddata-height=606&nbddata-width=900" width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有3只，国际医学的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入1.06亿元。</p>
