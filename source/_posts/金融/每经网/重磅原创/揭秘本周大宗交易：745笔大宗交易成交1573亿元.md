        <p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>本周一共发生745笔大宗交易，其中53笔溢价，104笔平价，588笔折价，共成交157.3亿元，环比增加18.12%，涉及261家上市公司。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/b6f0adde65b5453eb04fd5f4c26824de?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>本周大宗交易累计成交金额前3的公司是君实生物-U、贵州茅台、洛阳钼业，累计成交金额分别为16.73亿元，8.1亿元，7.22亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/d3ab40a7f832422889bda7dee549cf6a?nbddata-height=858&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>本周机构专用席位参与了164笔大宗交易，环比上周增加12笔。本周机构席位买入金额前3的公司为赣锋锂业、七一二、海康威视，机构买入金额分别为5.12亿元、4.18亿元、3.87亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/1ba8783a93314253b4d0e87bdd626cec?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>

<p>溢价交易方面，本周有9笔大宗交易溢价率超过10%。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/84f025c8f55245a99b303a888675c312?nbddata-height=787&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
