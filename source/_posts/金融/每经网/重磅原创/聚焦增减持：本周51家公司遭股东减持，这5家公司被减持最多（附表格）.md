        <p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>本周A股共有6家公司发布了增持公告，共增持2.69亿股。51家公司发布了减持公告，共减持2.69亿股。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/cfcd9d52ea38470490dce8aff12b0ab9?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>从增持力度上来看，本周获股东增持比例（占流通股比）前三的公司是*ST同洲、中信证券、金刚玻璃，增持比例分别为6.32%、1.29%、0.91%。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/1eadfc5f8d67476797d3793ef8533533?nbddata-height=547&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>从减持力度上来看，本周获股东减持比例（占流通股比）前三的公司是左江科技、科沃斯、浙文影业，减持比例分别为6.13%、4.73%、2.29%。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/61769a4b94df481e904856f27a2a76eb?nbddata-height=1387&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>从行业上来看，发布增持公告数量最多的行业是家用电器，发布减持公告数量最多的行业是医药生物。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/ed2964e97a094fd988299407b707e099?nbddata-height=1447&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
