        <p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>本周一共发生527笔大宗交易，其中52笔溢价，66笔平价，409笔折价，共成交142.76亿元，环比增加7.66%，涉及237家上市公司。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/ab484e5b15944f5a8be55a5f5f4d940e?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>本周大宗交易累计成交金额前3的公司是玲珑轮胎、华友钴业、贵州茅台，累计成交金额分别为10.83亿元，6.89亿元，5.74亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/f5d6e769901d4a5d9b7cb15ff3b37eae?nbddata-height=787&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>本周机构专用席位参与了106笔大宗交易，环比上周减少19笔。本周机构席位买入金额前3的公司为玲珑轮胎、亿联网络、锐科激光，机构买入金额分别为10.83亿元、5.51亿元、3.52亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/7f686d8a142044bbb5507d5ddef58ed8?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/068057e18574478d903a248da1077def?nbddata-height=990&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>溢价交易方面，本周有13笔大宗交易溢价率超过10%。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/ecb5469fbe634d49945c029649ffa485?nbddata-height=787&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>折价交易方面，本周有158笔大宗交易折价率超过10%。</p>
