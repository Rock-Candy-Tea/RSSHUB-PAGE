        <p style="text-align: justify;"><span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，吉利汽车（<span lang="EN-US">00175.HK</span>）公布的销量数据显示，今年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月，吉利汽车销量约<span lang="EN-US">10.39</span>万辆，环比上升约<span lang="EN-US">18%</span>；<span lang="EN-US">1~9</span>月，累计销量约<span lang="EN-US">92.18</span>万辆，同比增长约<span lang="EN-US">5.3%</span>，全年<span lang="EN-US">153</span>万辆销量目标已完成超六成。</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">其中，领克汽车<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月销量约为<span lang="EN-US">1.58</span>万辆，环比增长约<span lang="EN-US">162%</span>。领克品牌成立五年来，累计总销量超<span lang="EN-US">57.79</span>万辆。在紧凑级市场占领一席之地后，领克品牌开始向上冲击。目前，领克品牌首款中大型<span lang="EN-US">SUV</span>领克<span lang="EN-US">09</span>开启预售，预售首日收获订单<span lang="EN-US">6597</span>辆。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class="nbdLazyload" src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://image.nbd.com.cn/uploads/articles/images/1079177/1634004156______20211011232116.jpg" alt width="630" data-imgscale="1.3333333333333333" data-imgwidth="3840" data-imgheight="2880" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><br><br></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span lang="EN-US">图片来源：每经记者 黄辛旭 摄</span></p>
<p style="text-align: justify;"><span lang="EN-US">9</span>月，吉利汽车旗下新能源车型成为一大看点。几何<span lang="EN-US">A</span>、几何<span lang="EN-US">C</span>、帝豪<span lang="EN-US">EV</span>、帝豪<span lang="EN-US">GSe</span>等新能源及电气化车型，<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月合计销量约<span lang="EN-US">1.12</span>万辆，同比增长约<span lang="EN-US">83.5%</span>，实现自<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月以来新能源汽车最高单月销量水平。<span lang="EN-US">1~9</span>月，吉利新能源及电气化车型累计销量约<span lang="EN-US">5.72</span>辆，同比增长约<span lang="EN-US">18.5%</span>。</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">从具体车型来看，<span lang="EN-US">SUV</span>车型仍为吉利汽车销量担当。<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月，吉利汽车旗下<span lang="EN-US">SUV</span>销量约为<span lang="EN-US">6.38</span>万辆，轿车销量约为<span lang="EN-US">3.94</span>万辆。由星瑞、星越<span lang="EN-US">S</span>、星越<span lang="EN-US">L</span>组成的<span lang="EN-US">CMA</span>架构高端产品阵营——“中国星”系列车型表现抢眼，其中，星瑞上市<span lang="EN-US">11</span>个多月，销量突破<span lang="EN-US">11</span>万辆；星越<span lang="EN-US">L</span>上市<span lang="EN-US">2</span>个多月，销量超<span lang="EN-US">2</span>万辆，并将在吉利西安制造基地正式下线。</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">当前，吉利汽车正加速新品投放和车型升级换代。例如，<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，紧凑级<span lang="EN-US">SUV</span>博越<span lang="EN-US">X</span>正式上市，新车搭载吉利银河<span lang="EN-US">OS</span>、百度自主研发鸿鹄芯片、<span lang="EN-US">Mobileye EQ4</span>智能辅助驾驶算力平台等，欲进一步提升吉利在紧凑级<span lang="EN-US">SUV</span>市场的竞争力。</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">海外出口方面，<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月，吉利汽车出口量为<span lang="EN-US">8013辆，<span lang="EN-US">1~9</span>月累计出口量约<span lang="EN-US">7.47</span>万辆，同比增长约<span lang="EN-US">94.1%</span>。其中，几何品牌<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月订单首次过万辆；领克品牌<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月出口至欧洲市场的发运量为<span lang="EN-US">2000</span>辆，<span lang="EN-US">1~9</span>月出口海外市场总发运量为<span lang="EN-US">7950</span>辆。</span></p>
