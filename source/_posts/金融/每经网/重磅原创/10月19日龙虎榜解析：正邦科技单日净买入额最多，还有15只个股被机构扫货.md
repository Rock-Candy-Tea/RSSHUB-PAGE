        <p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span>10月19日，共有39只个股上榜龙虎榜，正邦科技龙虎榜单日净买入额最多，达6921.74万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/ec787381d37245c09f65c2b7cd9b1489?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/1af9e97e975745518106bce2d629b4e8?nbddata-height=1182&nbddata-width=900" width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有15只，净买入额前三的是闽东电力、银星能源、华辰装备，分别为2452.61万元、768.06万元、493.09万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/085fc1808b85452a8e417eb547e58c4f?nbddata-height=1140&nbddata-width=900" width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有1只，新五丰的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入285.05万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/deec55e826c64e26af431f9cea1f0c9d?nbddata-height=318&nbddata-width=900" width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有5只，双汇发展的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入2.16亿元。</p>
