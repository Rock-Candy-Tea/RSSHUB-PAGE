        <p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>本周一共发生628笔大宗交易，其中67笔溢价，102笔平价，459笔折价，共成交133.17亿元，环比减少6.72%，涉及255家上市公司。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/3942750b71e842e6b32471aeb7d5f213?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>本周大宗交易累计成交金额前3的公司是绝味食品、韦尔股份、埃斯顿，累计成交金额分别为8.68亿元，6.06亿元，6.04亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/aa30fdbd22484fb6b56d7d63ce194b59?nbddata-height=787&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>本周机构专用席位参与了152笔大宗交易，环比上周增加46笔。本周机构席位买入金额前3的公司为绝味食品、埃斯顿、中油资本，机构买入金额分别为7.37亿元、6.04亿元、4亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/5e7b79ffb3d944838f61b32efc08b980?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>

<p>溢价交易方面，本周有6笔大宗交易溢价率超过10%。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/8f8512df02a74d60a01e8a4c153cfd0c?nbddata-height=787&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
