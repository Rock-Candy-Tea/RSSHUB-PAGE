        <p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>本周A股共72家公司购买理财产品152.46亿元，金额环比上周减少24.23%。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/3e8cc19a-0071-470e-981a-23953718be9b?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>从买入金额上来看，购买理财产品金额排名前三的公司是陆家嘴（600663.SH）、安图生物（603658.SH）、凤凰股份（600716.SH），买入金额分别为50.00亿元、26.70亿元、5.00亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/078a790c-7add-4ac9-8013-a304d75c3318?nbddata-height=1423&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>从上市公司所属行业上来看，本周买入理财产品金额排名前三的行业分别是房地产、医药生物、计算机。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/6034225b-4a96-4b18-aa1f-ba3e211d2b2c?nbddata-height=1423&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>从上市公司所属地区上来看，本周买入理财产品金额排名前三的地区分别是上海、河南省、广东省。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/431df075-a1b4-45b6-8aad-e6dd8322a29e?nbddata-height=1303&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
