        <p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>本周一共发生370笔大宗交易，其中30笔溢价，98笔平价，242笔折价，共成交88.67亿元，环比减少37.96%，涉及153家上市公司。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/a42bf040436e4151ae43db290488fa07?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>本周大宗交易累计成交金额前3的公司是药明康德、宁德时代、晨鸣纸业，累计成交金额分别为14.02亿元，10.63亿元，5.02亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/9a4c6c1b7a764d28b0fc2a438d97f5a3?nbddata-height=1110&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>本周机构专用席位参与了66笔大宗交易，环比上周减少77笔。本周机构席位买入金额前3的公司为七一二、农业银行、京东方A，机构买入金额分别为4.22亿元、1.04亿元、9601.74万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/235deeb140054dc8b2b7ea0f96211d31?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>

<p>溢价交易方面，本周有5笔大宗交易溢价率超过10%。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/d6f761e1d62b498ab8d4d5c0f5c8ad5a?nbddata-height=1122&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
