        <p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span>本周一共发生618笔大宗交易，其中38笔溢价，111笔平价，469笔折价，共成交125.83亿元，环比增加25.85%，涉及228家上市公司。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/22a3f34ac43e445092456b2b258899e3?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>本周大宗交易累计成交金额前3的公司是宁德时代、凯赛生物、广和通，累计成交金额分别为8.44亿元，6.81亿元，6.06亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/627ee451cb6f4a0ca7caf5367a76dfb4?nbddata-height=787&nbddata-width=900" width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>本周机构专用席位参与了127笔大宗交易，环比上周增加31笔。本周机构席位买入金额前3的公司为广和通、美的集团、金龙鱼，机构买入金额分别为5.29亿元、5.07亿元、2.02亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/ed6103a1deb447c59cc0ae01e3a0003a?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/75070576a98b419d8b3c9e747a8874af?nbddata-height=906&nbddata-width=900" width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>溢价交易方面，本周有6笔大宗交易溢价率超过10%。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/c7820367f2a1480393e58bc82f97d250?nbddata-height=787&nbddata-width=900" width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>折价交易方面，本周有101笔大宗交易折价率超过10%。</p>
