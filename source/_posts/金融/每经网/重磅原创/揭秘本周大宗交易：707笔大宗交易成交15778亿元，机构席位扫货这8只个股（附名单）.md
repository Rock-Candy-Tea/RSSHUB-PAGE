        <p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span>本周一共发生707笔大宗交易，其中50笔溢价，137笔平价，520笔折价，共成交157.78亿元，环比增加25.39%，涉及262家上市公司。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/db2f6c6341f543769bbe6fac1ac62e73?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>本周大宗交易累计成交金额前3的公司是恒瑞医药、美的集团、金风科技，累计成交金额分别为20.61亿元，19.8亿元，7.05亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/f93de1d9b3fe477fbc791dca8f5afd08?nbddata-height=787&nbddata-width=900" width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>本周机构专用席位参与了147笔大宗交易，环比上周增加20笔。本周机构席位买入金额前3的公司为金风科技、思瑞浦、美的集团，机构买入金额分别为4.77亿元、2.91亿元、2.38亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/d6f1f1497fa9496cb0050d6ca64fcdb4?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/973dd37bdcc24a919069208c2083c488?nbddata-height=906&nbddata-width=900" width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>溢价交易方面，本周有14笔大宗交易溢价率超过10%。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/7648909c333045d8ac61025f8599d43e?nbddata-height=787&nbddata-width=900" width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>折价交易方面，本周有182笔大宗交易折价率超过10%。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/da718664adf74774a78a3e87febd6ded?nbddata-height=787&nbddata-width=900" width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
