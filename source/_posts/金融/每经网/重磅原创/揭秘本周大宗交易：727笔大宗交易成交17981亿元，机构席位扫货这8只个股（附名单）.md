        <p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>本周一共发生727笔大宗交易，其中39笔溢价，127笔平价，561笔折价，共成交179.81亿元，环比增加15.19%，涉及273家上市公司。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/37ea8ce294344053b49812ca2a66058d?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>本周大宗交易累计成交金额前3的公司是智飞生物、恒瑞医药、视源股份，累计成交金额分别为12.85亿元，12.65亿元，6.61亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/c0a415fdc958415c9bb0fd118aa07c2d?nbddata-height=787&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>本周机构专用席位参与了140笔大宗交易，环比上周增加23笔。本周机构席位买入金额前3的公司为圣邦股份、隧道股份、卓胜微，机构买入金额分别为5.42亿元、3.28亿元、2.86亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/0b1e67e05d6c4ed693b4f68a6505f3ab?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>

<p>溢价交易方面，本周有2笔大宗交易溢价率超过10%。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/413b601615fb4d5f8c4fdfc747d28754?nbddata-height=787&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
