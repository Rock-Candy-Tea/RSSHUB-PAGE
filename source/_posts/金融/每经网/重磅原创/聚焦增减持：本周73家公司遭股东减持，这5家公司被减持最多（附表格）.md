        <p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>本周A股共有23家公司发布了增持公告，共增持3.24亿股。73家公司发布了减持公告，共减持3.81亿股。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/a4e9af37827440938435ef1fe12e69d3?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>从增持力度上来看，本周获股东增持比例（占流通股比）前三的公司是保税科技、易尚展示、金刚玻璃，增持比例分别为7.7%、3.3%、2.67%。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/af849ef255084e719dba08ae77e40f41?nbddata-height=1387&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>从减持力度上来看，本周获股东减持比例（占流通股比）前三的公司是瑞达期货、热景生物、国创高新，减持比例分别为4.29%、3.4%、3.06%。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/42b889bb1db741baaf1a5c42405a58b2?nbddata-height=1387&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>从行业上来看，发布增持公告数量最多的行业是交通运输，发布减持公告数量最多的行业是化工。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/9f0c89650c9c4dc49601da014e6df371?nbddata-height=1627&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>*文中所涉增减持数据为上市公司本周公告数据，非本周实际增减持数据</p>
