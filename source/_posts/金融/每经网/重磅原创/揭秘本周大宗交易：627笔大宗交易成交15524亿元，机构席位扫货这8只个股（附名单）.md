        <p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>本周一共发生627笔大宗交易，其中47笔溢价，151笔平价，429笔折价，共成交155.24亿元，环比增加19.63%，涉及268家上市公司。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/242b9a89b3c5458b9d0c7f1faff83020?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>本周大宗交易累计成交金额前3的公司是上机数控、中科创达、赤峰黄金，累计成交金额分别为23.48亿元，7.02亿元，6.18亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/26d7b58857d64ffdb537cae36ed80d04?nbddata-height=858&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>本周机构专用席位参与了123笔大宗交易，环比上周增加30笔。本周机构席位买入金额前3的公司为中科创达、西藏珠峰、海康威视，机构买入金额分别为7.02亿元、3亿元、2.11亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/e7ba3253768740eea98dbd3e6c16af4d?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>

<p>溢价交易方面，本周有8笔大宗交易溢价率超过10%。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/07d61ca07bca4c29aaa030fb74591832?nbddata-height=787&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
