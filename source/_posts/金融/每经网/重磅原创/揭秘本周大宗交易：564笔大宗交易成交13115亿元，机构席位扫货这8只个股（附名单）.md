        <p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>本周一共发生564笔大宗交易，其中45笔溢价，92笔平价，427笔折价，共成交131.15亿元，环比减少27.07%，涉及231家上市公司。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/8f67e2d8410c45139fc76f676166ec33?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>本周大宗交易累计成交金额前3的公司是北京君正、上海建工、工业富联，累计成交金额分别为6.63亿元，5.2亿元，4.32亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/c959dfcf207f473ba06a25ad4d6b1fc4?nbddata-height=787&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>本周机构专用席位参与了130笔大宗交易，环比上周减少10笔。本周机构席位买入金额前3的公司为上海建工、先导智能、华谊集团，机构买入金额分别为5.2亿元、3.86亿元、3.43亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/f749332a14ff4bb9aaf9876ee2ba5b93?nbddata-height=593&nbddata-width=750" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/9ce406fd5be3494bbbe2fad7e173bd9f?nbddata-height=990&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>溢价交易方面，本周有7笔大宗交易溢价率超过10%。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/6b45eb4fd8d1460783fac82eaf6e44a4?nbddata-height=787&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>折价交易方面，本周有130笔大宗交易折价率超过10%。</p>
