<p>业绩环比下滑股票元旦节后整体走势弱于上证指数，部分股票遭到北上资金抛售。</p><p>当前正值业绩披露高峰期，不少上市公司在业绩预告披露后股价有异动。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" data-imgqrcoded="1" width="500px" height="681.0185185185185px" style="width: 500px; height: 681.0185185185185px;" oldsrc="W020220124733429876184.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>据公告显示，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688309" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300671" code="300671">富满微</a>第四季度净利润预计亏损0.05亿元至0.15亿元，连续两个季度呈现下滑态势。</p><p>与机构一致预测的2021年净利润10.12亿元相比，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300671" code="300671">富满微</a>从平盘附近直线下挫，短短1分钟内逼近跌停板，回落幅度达到19%以上。截至收盘，公司股价跌幅15.23%。</p><p><a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration:
