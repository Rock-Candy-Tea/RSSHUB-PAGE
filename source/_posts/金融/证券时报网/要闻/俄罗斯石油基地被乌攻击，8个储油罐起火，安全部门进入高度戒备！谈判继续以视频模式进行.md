center;"><strong>西方援助机构：G20排除俄罗斯</strong></p><p style="text-align: style="text-align:center"><img data-type="png" _width="auto" wah-hotarea="click" src="https://news.stcn.com/news/202204/W020220401707944352478.png" alt="图片" data-fail="0" data-ratio="0.5625"
