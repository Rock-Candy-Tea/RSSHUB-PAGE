<p>证券时报网讯，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600030" code="600030">中信证券</a>指出，近期海外电解铝短缺加剧，铝价不断创出新高。2022年国内电解铝供需缺口预计扩大至200万吨，低库存背景下价格有望持续上涨，上调电解铝6个月目标价至25000元/吨。国内产能受限以及进口窗口关闭背景下，本轮铝价上涨的幅度和持续性均有望超预期，建议围绕两条逻辑布局：1）吨铝利润拉阔带动企业盈利回升；2）氧化铝和阳极企业盈利跟随改善。推荐<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000807" code="000807">云铝股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000933" code="000933">神火股份</a>和<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603612" code="603612">索通发展</a>，建议关注<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601600" code="601600">中国铝业</a>和<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600219" code="600219">南山铝业</a>。</p>
