<p>证券时报e公司讯，<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601236" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601236" target="_blank">红塔证券</a>(601236)11月3日晚间公告，公司作为原告，向原<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002174" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002174" target="_blank">游族网络</a>实控人林奇子女提起诉讼，涉案的金额2.67亿元。<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601236" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601236" target="_blank">红塔证券</a>指出，2020年10月14日，林奇从<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601236" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601236" target="_blank">红塔证券</a>获得融资借款2.3亿元，林奇死亡后，林奇持有的<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002174" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002174" target="_blank">游族网络</a>股票由其子女林小溪、林芮璟、林漓继承，因三人未偿还公司融资债务，<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601236" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601236" target="_blank">红塔证券</a>请求判令偿还尚欠的融资借款本息2.67亿元。</p>
