<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，1月13日有62只个股获机构买入型评级。其中，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600030" code="600030">中信证券</a>关注度最高，获7次机构买入型评级记录；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000001" code="000001">平安银行</a>，获6次机构买入型评级记录。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603713" code="603713">密尔克卫</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601689" code="601689">拓普集团</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603816" code="603816">顾家家居</a>均获5次机构买入型评级记录。从机构评级变动看，机构买入型评级记录中，有10条评级记录为机构首次关注，涉及<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601868" code="601868">中国能建</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002497" code="002497">雅化集团</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002847" code="002847">盐津铺子</a>等股。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202201/W020220114385615827878.png" title="获机构买入评级的个股.png" alt="获机构买入评级的个股.png" oldsrc="W020220114385615827878.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
