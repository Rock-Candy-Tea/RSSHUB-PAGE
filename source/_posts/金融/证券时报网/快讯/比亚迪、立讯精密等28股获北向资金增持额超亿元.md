<p>证券时报网讯，据wind统计显示，12月20日共有659只个股获北向资金持股量环比上一个交易日增加。以增持的股份数量和当日收盘价为基准进行测算，加仓股中，增持市值在1亿元以上的有28只，增持市值最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002594" code="002594">比亚迪</a>，最新持股量为7170.7万股，环比增加3.18%，增持市值为5.98亿元；增持市值较多还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002475" code="002475">立讯精密</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002241" code="002241">歌尔股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300014" code="300014">亿纬锂能</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601615" code="601615">明阳智能</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603501" code="603501">韦尔股份</a>等股。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202112/W020211221346389491066.png" title="北向资金增持市值排名.png" alt="北向资金增持市值排名.png" oldsrc="W020211221346389491066.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
