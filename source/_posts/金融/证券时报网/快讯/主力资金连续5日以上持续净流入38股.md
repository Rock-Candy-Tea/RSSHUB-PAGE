<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，截至8月31日收盘，共有38只个股主力资金连续5个交易日以上持续净流入，连续流入天数最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600586" code="600586">金晶科技</a>，该股已连续9个交易日净流入；连续净流入天数较多的还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600882" code="600882">妙可蓝多</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601838" code="601838">成都银行</a>等，主力资金分别连续流入9天、7天。</p><p>从股价表现看，主力资金连续流入期间，上涨的个股共有34只，涨幅居前的有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS605577" code="605577">龙版传媒</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002538" code="002538">司尔特</a>等。业绩方面，主力资金连续流入股中，有37股已经公布了半年报，净利润同比增幅较高的有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000045" code="000045">深纺织Ａ</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600586" code="600586">金晶科技</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600141" code="600141">兴发集团</a>等股。</p><p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202108/W020210831678966532681.png" title="主力资金连续流入个股.png" alt="主力资金连续流入个股.png" oldsrc="W020210831678966532681.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>责编：杨国强</p>
