<p>证券时报e公司讯，近日，多家银行高管公告以自有资金从二级市场买入公司A股股票，业内人士认为，高管们抛出“真金白银”可以在一定程度上起到稳定股价、提振市场信心的作用。今年以来，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601229" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601229" target="_blank">上海银行</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601963" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601963" target="_blank">重庆银行</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601187" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601187" target="_blank">厦门银行</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601916" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601916" target="_blank">浙商银行</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601077" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601077" target="_blank">渝农商行</a>等多家银行的董事、高管或股东增持股份。(央视财经)</p>
