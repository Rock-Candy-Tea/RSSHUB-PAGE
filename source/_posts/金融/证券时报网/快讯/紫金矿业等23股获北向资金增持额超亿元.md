<p>证券时报网讯，据wind统计显示，2月24日共有648只个股获北向资金持股量环比上一个交易日增加。以增持的股份数量和当日收盘价为基准进行测算，加仓股中，增持市值在1亿元以上的有23只，增持市值最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601899" code="601899">紫金矿业</a>，最新持股量为16.40亿股，环比增加4.29%，增持市值为7.42亿元；增持市值较多还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300274" code="300274">阳光电源</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002460" code="002460">赣锋锂业</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600522" code="600522">中天科技</a>等股。</p><p>从机构动向看，北向资金增持市值居前个股，近一个月获机构调研的有5只，机构调研次数较为频繁的有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002050" code="002050">三花智控</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300124" code="300124">汇川技术</a>等，期间分别获机构调研3次、2次；机构调研家数看，机构较为扎堆的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002050" code="002050">三花智控</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300124" code="300124">汇川技术</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002555" code="002555">三七互娱</a>，分别有336家、276家、23家机构参与调研。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202202/W020220225358372574592.png" title="北向资金增持市值排名.png" alt="北向资金增持市值排名.png" oldsrc="W020220225358372574592.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
