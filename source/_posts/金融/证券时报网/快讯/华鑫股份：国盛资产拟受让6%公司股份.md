<p>证券时报e公司讯，<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600621" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600621" target="_blank">华鑫股份</a>(600621)11月17日晚间公告，经<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600651" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600651" target="_blank">飞乐音响</a>组建的评审工作小组评审，确认上海国盛集团资产有限公司（简称“国盛资产”）符合公开征集转让的受让方条件，为此次股份转让的受让方。<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600651" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600651" target="_blank">飞乐音响</a>已与国盛资产签署股份转让协议。标的股份的转让价格为16.32元/股，转让总价款为10.39亿元。此次股份转让前，<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600651" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600651" target="_blank">飞乐音响</a>持有6%<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600621" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600621" target="_blank">华鑫股份</a>，股份转让完成后，国盛资产将持有9.986%<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600621" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600621" target="_blank">华鑫股份</a>，<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600651" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600651" target="_blank">飞乐音响</a>将不再持有公司股份。</p>
