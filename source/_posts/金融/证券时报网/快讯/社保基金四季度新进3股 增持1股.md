<p>证券时报网讯，据iFind统计显示，截止2月24日，2021年四季度末社保基金共现身6只个股前十大流通股东榜。其中，新进3只，增持1只。从持股比例看，社保基金持有比例最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300083" code="300083">创世纪</a>，持股量占流通股比例为5.7%；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002402" code="002402">和而泰</a>，社保基金持股比例为3.49%；持股比例居前的还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688368" code="688368">晶丰明源</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002092" code="002092">中泰化学</a>等。持股数量方面，社保基金持股量最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300083" code="300083">创世纪</a>，共持有7450.03万股，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002402" code="002402">和而泰</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002092" code="002092">中泰化学</a>等紧随其后，持股量分别为2797.52万股、2362.37万股。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202202/W020220224394155905434.png" title="社保基金四季度重仓股排行榜.png" alt="社保基金四季度重仓股排行榜.png" oldsrc="W020220224394155905434.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
