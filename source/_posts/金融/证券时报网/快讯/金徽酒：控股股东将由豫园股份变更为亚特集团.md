<p>证券时报e公司讯，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603919" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="603919" target="_blank">金徽酒</a>(603919)9月2日晚间公告，公司控股股东<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600655" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600655" target="_blank">豫园股份</a>及其一致行动人海南豫珠与亚特集团及其一致行动人陇南科立特分别签署《股份转让协议》，海南豫珠向亚特集团转让其持有的<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603919" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="603919" target="_blank">金徽酒</a>8%股份、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600655" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600655" target="_blank">豫园股份</a>向陇南科立特转让其持有的<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603919" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="603919" target="_blank">金徽酒</a>5%股份，转让价格均为29.38元/股。转让完成后，亚特集团及其一致行动人陇南科立特合计持有公司26.57%股份，公司控股股东将由<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600655" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600655" target="_blank">豫园股份</a>变更为亚特集团。为巩固亚特集团的控股权，本次股份转让完成后6个月内，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600655" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600655" target="_blank">豫园股份</a>将继续减持其持有的公司5%以上股份。</p>
