<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，8月30日共有644只股获北向资金持股量环比上一个交易日增加。以增持的股份数量和当日收盘价为基准进行测算，加仓股中，增持市值在亿元以上的有40只，增持市值最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601012" code="601012">隆基股份</a>，最新持股量为6.45亿股，环比增加1.91%，增持市值为11.76亿元；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002241" code="002241">歌尔股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600036" code="600036">招商银行</a>。</p><p>业绩来看，北向资金大手笔增持股中，有40股已经公布了半年报，净利润同比增幅最高的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002092" code="002092">中泰化学</a>，净利润同比变动幅度为728.82%。从机构动向看，北向资金增持市值居前个股，近一个月获机构调研的有12只，机构调研次数较为频繁的有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000661" code="000661">长春高新</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002092" code="002092">中泰化学</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600763" code="600763">通策医疗</a>等。</p><p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202108/W020210831350033211288.png" title="北向资金增持市值排名.png" alt="北向资金增持市值排名.png" oldsrc="W020210831350033211288.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>责编：孙孝熙</p>
