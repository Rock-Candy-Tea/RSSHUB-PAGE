<p>证券时报e公司讯，新股破发、弃购潮下，券商定价能力和专业能力有待提升。记者根据<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300033" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300033" target="_blank">同花顺</a>数据统计显示，截至4月24日，今年共有114只新股上市（包含北交所股票），上市首日跌破发行价的有32只，占比达28%。破发的股票主要集中在科创板和创业板。33只破发的公司中，从首发主承销商来看，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600030" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600030" target="_blank">中信证券</a>和<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601995" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601995" target="_blank">中金公司</a>首发主承销公司破发数量较多。此外，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688153" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="688153" target="_blank">唯捷创芯</a>-U、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688337" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="688337" target="_blank">普源精电</a>-U和<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688220" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="688220" target="_blank">翱捷科技</a>-U三只新股的上市首日跌幅最大，分别为30.93%、26.08%和20.99%，其首发主承销商分别为<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601066" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601066" target="_blank">中信建投</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601211" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601211" target="_blank">国泰君安</a>和大和证券（中国）、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600837" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600837" target="_blank">海通证券</a>。(经济参考报)</p>
