<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，今日可交易A股中，上涨个股有2515只，下跌个股有1859只，平盘个股131只。不含当日上市新股，共有82只个股涨停，9只个股跌停。以所属行业来看，涨停个股主要集中在电气设备、化工、机械设备、公用事业、电子等行业。</p><p>涨停股中，从收盘涨停板封单量来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600995" code="600995">文山电力</a>最受追捧，收盘涨停板封单有1.19亿股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600277" code="600277">亿利洁能</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601669" code="601669">中国电建</a>，涨停板封单分别有2625.63万股、2076.09万股。以封单金额计算，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600995" code="600995">文山电力</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ001219" code="001219">青岛食品</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000049" code="000049">德赛电池</a>等涨停板封单资金较多，分别有16.73亿元、5.82亿元、2.42亿元。连续涨停天数看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600995" code="600995">文山电力</a>已连收6个涨停板，连续涨停板数量最多。</p>
