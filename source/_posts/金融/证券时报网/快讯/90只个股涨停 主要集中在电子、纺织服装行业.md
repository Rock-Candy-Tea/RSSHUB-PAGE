<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，沪深两市可交易A股中，上涨个股有2304只，下跌个股有2060只，平盘个股182只。不含当日上市新股，共有90只个股涨停，10只个股跌停。以所属行业来看，涨停个股主要集中在电子、纺织服装、机械设备、医药生物、化工、汽车等行业。</p><p>涨停股中，从收盘涨停板封单量来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002269" code="002269">美邦服饰</a>最受追捧，收盘涨停板封单有2401.69万股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000572" code="000572">海马汽车</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600863" code="600863">内蒙华电</a>，涨停板封单分别有1989.33万股、1899.94万股。以封单金额计算，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603219" code="603219">富佳股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002432" code="002432">九安医疗</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300264" code="300264">佳创视讯</a>等涨停板封单资金较多，分别有2.36亿元、1.55亿元、1.33亿元。连续涨停天数看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603213" code="603213">镇洋发展</a>已连收10个涨停板，连续涨停板数量最多。</p>
