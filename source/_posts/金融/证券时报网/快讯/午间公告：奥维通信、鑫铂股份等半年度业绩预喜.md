<p>证券时报e公司讯，7月15日午间，多家公司披露半年度业绩预告：<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002231" code="002231">奥维通信</a>净利润预增120.71%至179.69%；<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ003038" code="003038">鑫铂股份</a>净利润预增40%至62.85%；赛意信息净利润预增10%至20%。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300289" code="300289">利德曼</a>预计盈利1200万元至1500万元，上年同期亏损595.76万元。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000876" code="000876">新希望</a>预亏29.5亿元至34.5亿元，上年同期盈利31.64亿元；<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002657" code="002657">中科金财</a>预亏1489.64万元至2234.46万元，上年同期亏损2615.42万元。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002931" code="002931">锋龙股份</a>拟1597.4万美元收购杜商精机49%的股权，实现全资控股。</p>
