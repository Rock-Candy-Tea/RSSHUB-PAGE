<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，近5个交易日（4月18日至4月22日）沪深两市约103家上市公司被机构调研。机构调研榜单中，共有48家公司获20家以上机构扎堆调研。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300760" code="300760">迈瑞医疗</a>最受关注，参与调研的机构达到551家；<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603605" code="603605">珀莱雅</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002216" code="002216">三全食品</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002841" code="002841">视源股份</a>等分别被280家、245家、220家机构调研。机构调研次数来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603856" code="603856">东宏股份</a>机构调研最为密集，共获机构5次调研。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000090" code="000090">天健集团</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000999" code="000999">华润三九</a>分别被机构调研3次、2次。</p><p>20家以上机构扎堆调研股中，近5日资金净流入的有14只，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603456" code="603456">九洲药业</a>近5日净流入资金0.44亿元，主力资金净流入最多；净流入资金较多的还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300760" code="300760">迈瑞医疗</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002028" code="002028">思源电气</a>等。</p>
