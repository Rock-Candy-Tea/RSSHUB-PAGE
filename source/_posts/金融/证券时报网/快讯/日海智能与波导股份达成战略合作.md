<p>证券时报e公司讯，日前，<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002313" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002313" target="_blank">日海智能</a>和<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600130" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600130" target="_blank">波导股份</a>达成了战略合作签约，<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002313" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002313" target="_blank">日海智能</a>集团CEO杨涛出席了本次签约仪式。并于同日在<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600130" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600130" target="_blank">波导股份</a>工厂举办了1000万片<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002313" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002313" target="_blank">日海智能</a>模组下线仪式。杨涛表示，未来<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002313" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002313" target="_blank">日海智能</a>将在无线通讯模组项目上与<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600130" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600130" target="_blank">波导股份</a>开展更加深入和紧密的合作，实现优势互补、合作共赢。</p>
