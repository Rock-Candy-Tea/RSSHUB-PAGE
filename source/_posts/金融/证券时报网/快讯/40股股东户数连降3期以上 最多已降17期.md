<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，285股公布了截至10月10日最新股东户数，相比之前各期，有40股股东户数连续减少3期以上，股东户数最多已连降17期。股东户数连降期数较多的有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300983" code="300983">尤安设计</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002456" code="002456">欧菲光</a>等股。</p><p>从机构动向看，股东户数连降股中，近一个月获机构调研的有9只，机构调研次数较为频繁的有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002456" code="002456">欧菲光</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000738" code="000738">航发控制</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002190" code="002190">成飞集成</a>等，期间分别获机构调研2次、2次、2次；机构调研家数看，机构较为扎堆的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300319" code="300319">麦捷科技</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002456" code="002456">欧菲光</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002013" code="002013">中航机电</a>。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202110/W020211015666289718193.png" title="股东户数连续下降股排行榜.png" alt="股东户数连续下降股排行榜.png" oldsrc="W020211015666289718193.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
