<p>证券时报网讯，据wind统计显示，10月18日共有1121只个股获融资净买入，净买入金额在千万元以上的有272只。其中，27只融资净买入额超1亿元。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600519" code="600519">贵州茅台</a>获融资净买入额居首，当日净买入7.68亿元；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603799" code="603799">华友钴业</a>，融资净买入14.55亿元。</p><p>此外，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002466" code="002466">天齐锂业</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600111" code="600111">北方稀土</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601899" code="601899">紫金矿业</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601600" code="601600">中国铝业</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688772" code="688772">珠海冠宇</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603986" code="603986">兆易创新</a>等股获融资净买入额超2亿元。</p>
