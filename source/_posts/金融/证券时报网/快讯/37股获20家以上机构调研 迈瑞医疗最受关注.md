<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，近5个交易日（4月15日至4月21日）沪深两市约86家上市公司被机构调研。机构调研榜单中，共有37家公司获20家以上机构扎堆调研。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300760" code="300760">迈瑞医疗</a>最受关注，参与调研的机构达到551家；<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002415" code="002415">海康威视</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002709" code="002709">天赐材料</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002216" code="002216">三全食品</a>等分别被367家、256家、245家机构调研。机构调研次数来看，有2家公司被机构调研3次，分别是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002003" code="002003">伟星股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000915" code="000915">华特达因</a>。</p><p>20家以上机构扎堆调研股中，近5日资金净流入的有13只，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300760" code="300760">迈瑞医疗</a>近5日净流入资金2.73亿元，主力资金净流入最多；净流入资金较多的还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002458" code="002458">益生股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002415" code="002415">海康威视</a>等。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202204/W020220422390733017082.png" title="近5日机构调研股一览.png" alt="近5日机构调研股一览.png" oldsrc="W020220422390733017082.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
