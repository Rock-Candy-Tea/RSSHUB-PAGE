<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，二季度末养老金账户共现身116只个股前十大流通股东榜。其中，新进38只，增持28只。二季度末养老金账户对<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002027" code="002027">分众传媒</a>的持股量最多，持股量为13359.49万股。从期末持股市值看，养老金账户期末持股市值在亿元以上的有67只股，分别是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600079" code="600079">人福医药</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002027" code="002027">分众传媒</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601877" code="601877">正泰电器</a>等。</p><p>持股比例方面，养老金账户持股比例最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002853" code="002853">皮阿诺</a>，合计持股量为890.74万股，占流通股比例10.19%；老金持股比例居前的还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688095" code="688095">福昕软件</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS605338" code="605338">巴比食品</a>等。从持有时间来看，养老金账户持股期限最长的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002126" code="002126">银轮股份</a>，已连续15个报告期出现在公司前十大流通股东名单中。</p><p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202108/W020210830494955411932.png" title="部分二季度养老金持有的个股.png" alt="部分二季度养老金持有的个股.png" oldsrc="W020210830494955411932.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>责编：孙孝熙</p>
