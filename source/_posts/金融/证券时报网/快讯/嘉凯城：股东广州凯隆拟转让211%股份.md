<p>证券时报e公司讯，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000918" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000918" target="_blank">嘉凯城</a>(000918)6月20日晚间公告，股东广州凯隆拟通过协议转让的方式将其持有的<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000918" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000918" target="_blank">嘉凯城</a>3812.93万股流通股股份（占<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000918" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000918" target="_blank">嘉凯城</a>已发行股份总数的2.11%）以2.043元/股的价格转让给曹升，总对价7789.82万元。本次协议转让为配合<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600030" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600030" target="_blank">中信证券</a>要求，用于归还广州凯隆在<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600030" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600030" target="_blank">中信证券</a>的质押债务本金、利息、违约金。</p>
