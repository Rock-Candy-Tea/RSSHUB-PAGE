<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计，近五个交易日的龙虎榜当中，89只个股出现了机构的身影，其中37只股票呈现机构净买入，52只股票呈现机构净卖出。近五日机构净买入前三名分别是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002756" code="002756">永兴材料</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603606" code="603606">东方电缆</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000862" code="000862">银星能源</a>。</p><p>与此同时，近五个交易日登陆沪深两市的龙虎榜个股中，营业部净买入较大的个股有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002049" code="002049">紫光国微</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600338" code="600338">西藏珠峰</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002665" code="002665">首航高科</a>。具体来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002049" code="002049">紫光国微</a>是资金最青睐个股，营业部净买入金额达6.23亿元，高居榜首。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202201/W020220104355524599789.png" title="近五日龙虎榜机构净买入金额最多个股.png" alt="近五日龙虎榜机构净买入金额最多个股.png" width="550" oldsrc="W020220104355524599789.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
