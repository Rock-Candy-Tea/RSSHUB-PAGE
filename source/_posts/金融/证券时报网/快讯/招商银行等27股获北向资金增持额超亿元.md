<p>证券时报网讯，据wind统计显示，1月17日共有827只个股获北向资金持股量环比上一个交易日增加。以增持的股份数量和当日收盘价为基准进行测算，加仓股中，增持市值在1亿元以上的有27只，增持市值最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600036" code="600036">招商银行</a>，最新持股量为15.34亿股，环比增加0.76%，增持市值为5.62亿元；增持市值较多还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002812" code="002812">恩捷股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300274" code="300274">阳光电源</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600763" code="600763">通策医疗</a>等股。</p><p>从机构动向看，北向资金增持市值居前个股，近一个月获机构调研的有3只；机构调研家数看，机构较为扎堆的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600900" code="600900">长江电力</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688036" code="688036">传音控股</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000792" code="000792">盐湖股份</a>。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202201/W020220118355920465110.png" title="北向资金增持市值排名.png" alt="北向资金增持市值排名.png" oldsrc="W020220118355920465110.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
