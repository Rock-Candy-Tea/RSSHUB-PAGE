<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，12月13日共有1284只股获融资净买入，净买入金额在千万元以上的有344只，其中19只融资净买入额超1亿元。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600887" code="600887">伊利股份</a>融资净买入额居首，当日净买入2.41亿元，其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600559" code="600559">老白干酒</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000723" code="000723">美锦能源</a>，融资净买入金额分别为2.39亿元、2.33亿元，融资净买入金额居前的还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002756" code="002756">永兴材料</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603259" code="603259">药明康德</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600519" code="600519">贵州茅台</a>等。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202112/W020211214359413698872.png" title="12月13日融资客净买入金额排名.png" alt="12月13日融资客净买入金额排名.png" oldsrc="W020211214359413698872.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
