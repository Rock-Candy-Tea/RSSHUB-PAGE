<p>证券时报e公司讯，<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000504" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000504" target="_blank">南华生物</a>(000504)12月10日晚间公告，公司与<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002411" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002411" target="_blank">延安必康</a>(002411)签署《资产收购意向协议书》，就<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000504" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000504" target="_blank">南华生物</a>收购<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002411" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002411" target="_blank">延安必康</a>持有的部分医药类资产达成初步共识。公司拟收购的资产具体包括<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002411" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002411" target="_blank">延安必康</a>下属部分制药企业、医药类流通企业的股权及/或部分生产车间所对应的资产，具体资产范围待尽职调查后进行协商确定。</p>
