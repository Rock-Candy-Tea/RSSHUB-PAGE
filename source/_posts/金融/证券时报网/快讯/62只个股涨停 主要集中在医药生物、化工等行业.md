<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，3月4日，沪深两市可交易A股中，上涨个股有1019只，下跌个股有3547只，平盘个股77只。不含当日上市新股，共有62只个股涨停，26只个股跌停。以所属行业来看，涨停个股主要集中在医药生物、化工、交通运输、汽车、传媒等行业。</p><p>涨停股中，从收盘涨停板封单量来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600056" code="600056">中国医药</a>最受追捧，收盘涨停板封单有3918.2万股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600190" code="600190">锦州港</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600734" code="600734">*ST实达</a>等，涨停板封单分别有3388.32万股、3332.77万股。以封单金额计算，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600056" code="600056">中国医药</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600190" code="600190">锦州港</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ301126" code="301126">达嘉维康</a>等涨停板封单资金较多，分别有5.79亿元、1.72亿元、1.57亿元。连续涨停天数看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600734" code="600734">*ST实达</a>已连收15个涨停板，连续涨停板数量最多。</p>
