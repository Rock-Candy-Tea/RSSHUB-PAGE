<p>证券时报网讯，据wind统计显示，2月23日共有825只个股获北向资金持股量环比上一个交易日增加。以增持的股份数量和当日收盘价为基准进行测算，加仓股中，增持市值在1亿元以上的有25只，增持市值最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601012" code="601012">隆基股份</a>，最新持股量为6.43亿股，环比增加0.86%，增持市值为3.96亿元；增持市值较多还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002594" code="002594">比亚迪</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000858" code="000858">五粮液</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300059" code="300059">东方财富</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601658" code="601658">邮储银行</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600958" code="600958">东方证券</a>等股。</p><p>从机构动向看，北向资金增持市值居前个股，近一个月获机构调研的有3只，机构调研次数较为频繁的有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002594" code="002594">比亚迪</a>等，期间分别获机构调研7次；机构调研家数看，机构较为扎堆的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002747" code="002747">埃斯顿</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002594" code="002594">比亚迪</a>，分别有18家、6家机构参与调研。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202202/W020220224353723573470.png" title="北向资金增持市值排名.png" alt="北向资金增持市值排名.png" oldsrc="W020220224353723573470.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
