<p>证券时报e公司讯，7月11日，两市股指低开低走，个股多数下跌；煤炭、有色等周期板块跌幅居前，电力等少数板块逆市上涨；截至午间收盘，沪指跌1.46%，深证成指跌2.25%，创业板指跌2.19%。电力板块逆市上涨，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600101" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600101" target="_blank">明星电力</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000601" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000601" target="_blank">韶能股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000600" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000600" target="_blank">建投能源</a>等涨停；医药股走强，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002864" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002864" target="_blank">盘龙药业</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603963" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="603963" target="_blank">大理药业</a>等涨停；钒电池概念大涨，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000709" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000709" target="_blank">河钢股份</a>等涨停；农业、银行等板块也逆市上涨；锂矿、化肥、煤炭、酿酒、有色、汽车等板块跌幅居前。两市约3500股下跌，约50股涨幅达到或超过10%。</p>
