<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，沪深两市可交易A股中，上涨个股有1079只，下跌个股有3353只，平盘个股103只。不含当日上市新股，共有81只个股涨停，8只个股跌停。以所属行业来看，涨停个股主要集中在汽车、传媒、轻工制造、医药生物、电子、机械设备等行业。</p><p>涨停股中，从收盘涨停板封单量来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002374" code="002374">中锐股份</a>最受追捧，收盘涨停板封单有4436.09万股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ001317" code="001317">三羊马</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002600" code="002600">领益智造</a>等，涨停板封单分别有2124.04万股、2006.19万股。以封单金额计算，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ001317" code="001317">三羊马</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000860" code="000860">顺鑫农业</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603501" code="603501">韦尔股份</a>等涨停板封单资金较多，分别有5.92亿元、5.39亿元、2.08亿元。连续涨停天数看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002374" code="002374">中锐股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600935" code="600935">华塑股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002766" code="002766">*ST索菱</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600734" code="600734">*ST实达</a>均已连收5个涨停板，连续涨停板数量最多。</p>
