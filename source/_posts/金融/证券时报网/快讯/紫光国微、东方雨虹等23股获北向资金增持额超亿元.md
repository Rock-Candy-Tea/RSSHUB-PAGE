<p>证券时报网讯，据wind统计显示，4月11日共有542只个股获北向资金持股量环比上一个交易日增加。以增持的股份数量和当日收盘价为基准进行测算，加仓股中，增持市值在1亿元以上的有23只，增持市值最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002049" code="002049">紫光国微</a>，最新持股量为1684.72万股，环比增加12.72%，增持市值为3.31亿元；增持市值较多还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002271" code="002271">东方雨虹</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000333" code="000333">美的集团</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300750" code="300750">宁德时代</a>等股。</p><p>从机构动向看，北向资金增持市值居前个股，近一个月获机构调研的有3只；机构调研家数看，机构较为扎堆的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600745" code="600745">闻泰科技</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603501" code="603501">韦尔股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000333" code="000333">美的集团</a>，分别有20家、20家、17家机构参与调研。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202204/W020220412356553262082.png" title="北向资金增持市值排名.png" alt="北向资金增持市值排名.png" oldsrc="W020220412356553262082.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
