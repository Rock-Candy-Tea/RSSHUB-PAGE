#2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603225" code="603225">新凤鸣</a>营业收入和净利润分别为447.7亿元、22.5亿元，同比增长21.05%、273.77%，扣非净利润为21.7亿元，同比增长445.04%。<a #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603225"
