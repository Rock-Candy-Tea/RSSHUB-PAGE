<p>证券时报e公司讯，银河证券最新研报表示，展望下半年，家电行业面临的原材料涨价，海运不畅等外部负面因素逐步减弱，稳增长背景下地产景气度边际改善，消费券发放、疫情压制的需求释放等因素推动家电需求改善。板块前期调整较多，估值处于历史较低水平，投资价值较高。建议关注两条主线，一、受益于基本面改善的传统家电龙头，推荐<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000333" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000333" target="_blank">美的集团</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000651" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000651" target="_blank">格力电器</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600690" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600690" target="_blank">海尔智家</a>和<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002508" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002508" target="_blank">老板电器</a>。二、关注景气度处于高位的细分行业龙头，推荐<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603486" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="603486" target="_blank">科沃斯</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688169" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="688169" target="_blank">石头科技</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603355" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="603355" target="_blank">莱克电气</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300894" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300894" target="_blank">火星人</a>。</p>
