<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，截至7月20日，有3家券商发布上半年业绩快报。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600837" code="600837">海通证券</a>营业收入最高，上半年营业收入233.85亿元；<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601878" code="601878">浙商证券</a>位居其后。</p><p>净利润来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600837" code="600837">海通证券</a>上半年净利润81.04亿元，盈利金额居首，其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601878" code="601878">浙商证券</a>，净利润为8.81亿元。增幅方面，公布业绩快报的上市券商中，净利润同比增长的有3家，增幅最高的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600837" code="600837">海通证券</a>，净利润同比增长47.8%。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202107/W020210720366994024286.png" title="公布业绩快报券商一览.png" alt="公布业绩快报券商一览.png" oldsrc="W020210720366994024286.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
