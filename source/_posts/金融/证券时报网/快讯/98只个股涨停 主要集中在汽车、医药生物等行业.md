<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，沪深两市可交易A股中，上涨个股有1615只，下跌个股有2837只，平盘个股97只。不含当日上市新股，共有98只个股涨停，4只个股跌停。以所属行业来看，涨停个股主要集中在汽车、医药生物、电气设备、轻工制造、电子、机械设备等行业。</p><p>涨停股中，从收盘涨停板封单量来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601868" code="601868">中国能建</a>最受追捧，收盘涨停板封单有1.82亿股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600318" code="600318">新力金融</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600935" code="600935">华塑股份</a>等，涨停板封单分别有6307.53万股、5942.72万股。以封单金额计算，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600318" code="600318">新力金融</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601868" code="601868">中国能建</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600935" code="600935">华塑股份</a>等涨停板封单资金较多，分别有7.69亿元、4.25亿元、3.71亿元。连续涨停天数看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000525" code="000525">ST红太阳</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603219" code="603219">富佳股份</a>均已连收6个涨停板，连续涨停板数量最多。</p>
