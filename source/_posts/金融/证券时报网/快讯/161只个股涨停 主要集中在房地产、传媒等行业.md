<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，沪深两市可交易A股中，上涨个股有3624只，下跌个股有896只，平盘个股51只。不含当日上市新股，共有161只个股涨停，13只个股跌停。以所属行业来看，涨停个股主要集中在房地产、传媒、计算机、建筑装饰、汽车、医药生物等行业。</p><p>涨停股中，从收盘涨停板封单量来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002265" code="002265">西仪股份</a>最受追捧，收盘涨停板封单有8282.71万股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601099" code="601099">太平洋</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002822" code="002822">中装建设</a>等，涨停板封单分别有4962.29万股、4908.49万股。以封单金额计算，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002265" code="002265">西仪股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002432" code="002432">九安医疗</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002822" code="002822">中装建设</a>等涨停板封单资金较多，分别有21.65亿元、3.55亿元、3.09亿元。连续涨停天数看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ001317" code="001317">三羊马</a>已连收16个涨停板，连续涨停板数量最多。</p>
