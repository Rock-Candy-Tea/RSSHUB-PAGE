<p>证券时报网讯，证券时报·数据宝统计显示，上周北上资金持股量环比增加的有11个行业，轻工制造行业环比增幅最大，环比增幅居前的还有综合、国防军工、化工、机械设备等行业。</p><p>个股来看，上周北上资金加仓股共714只，以持股数量增长幅度来看，加仓幅度超50%的股票有36只，超100%的股票有15只。其中，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300080" code="300080">易成新能</a>的加仓力度最大；其他加仓幅度靠前的还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002135" code="002135">东南网架</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603982" code="603982">泉峰汽车</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002978" code="002978">安宁股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300318" code="300318">博晖创新</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600860" code="600860">京城股份</a>等。</p>
