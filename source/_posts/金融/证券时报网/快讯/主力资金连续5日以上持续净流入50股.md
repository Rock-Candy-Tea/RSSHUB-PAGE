<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，截至8月26日收盘，共有50只个股主力资金连续5个交易日以上持续净流入，连续流入天数最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603901" code="603901">永创智能</a>，该股已连续11个交易日净流入；连续净流入天数较多的还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603587" code="603587">地素时尚</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS605336" code="605336">帅丰电器</a>等，主力资金分别连续流入8天、8天。</p><p>业绩方面，主力资金连续流入股中，有29股已经公布了半年报，净利润同比增幅最高的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600711" code="600711">盛屯矿业</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600586" code="600586">金晶科技</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600808" code="600808">马钢股份</a>等。</p><p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202108/W020210826659475339493.png" title="部分主力资金连续流入个股.png" alt="部分主力资金连续流入个股.png" oldsrc="W020210826659475339493.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>责编：孙孝熙</p>
