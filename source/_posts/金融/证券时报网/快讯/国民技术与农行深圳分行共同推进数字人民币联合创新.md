<p>证券时报e公司讯，1月20日，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300077" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300077" target="_blank">国民技术</a>股份有限公司与中国<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601288" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601288" target="_blank">农业银行</a>股份有限公司深圳市分行在深圳联合发布了业界首个基于<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=NEEQ835670" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="835670" target="_blank">数字人</a>民币硬件钱包的智慧租住创新应用成果。同日，双方签署了<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=NEEQ835670" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="835670" target="_blank">数字人</a>民币联合创新战略合作协议，并举行了“<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=NEEQ835670" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="835670" target="_blank">数字人</a>民币联合创新实验室”揭牌仪式。下一步，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300077" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300077" target="_blank">国民技术</a>将以此次合作为契机，携手农行稳步推进<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=NEEQ835670" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="835670" target="_blank">数字人</a>民币研发试点工作，围绕<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=NEEQ835670" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="835670" target="_blank">数字人</a>民币的运用开展更多创新研究，探索多种场景应用试点。</p>
