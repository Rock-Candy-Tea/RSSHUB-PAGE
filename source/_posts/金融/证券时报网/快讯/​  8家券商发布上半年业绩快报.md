<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，截至7月27日，共有8家券商发布业绩快报。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600837" code="600837">海通证券</a>营业收入最高，上半年营业收入233.85亿元，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600958" code="600958">东方证券</a>位居其后，营业收入为132.77亿元。</p><p>净利润来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600837" code="600837">海通证券</a>上半年净利润81.04亿元，盈利金额居首，其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601066" code="601066">中信建投</a>，净利润为45.29亿元。增幅方面，公布业绩快报的上市券商中，净利润同比增长的有7家，增幅最高的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601901" code="601901">方正证券</a>，净利润同比增长102.7%。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202107/W020210727384827489890.png" title="部分券商业绩快报.png" alt="部分券商业绩快报.png" oldsrc="W020210727384827489890.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
