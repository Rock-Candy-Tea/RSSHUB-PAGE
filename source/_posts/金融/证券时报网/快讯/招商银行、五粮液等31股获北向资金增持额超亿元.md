<p>证券时报网讯，据wind统计显示，2月11日共有604只个股获北向资金持股量环比上一个交易日增加。以增持的股份数量和当日收盘价为基准进行测算，加仓股中，增持市值在1亿元以上的有31只，增持市值最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600036" code="600036">招商银行</a>，最新持股量为16.9亿股，环比增加0.88%，增持市值为7.75亿元；增持市值较多还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601899" code="601899">紫金矿业</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000858" code="000858">五粮液</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002241" code="002241">歌尔股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600585" code="600585">海螺水泥</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601166" code="601166">兴业银行</a>等股。</p><p>业绩来看，北向资金大手笔增持股中，有5股已经公布了2021年业绩快报，净利润增幅较高的有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601888" code="601888">中国中免</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000001" code="000001">平安银行</a>，增幅为56.23%、25.6%。公布2021年业绩预告的共有16只，业绩预告类型来看，预增有13只。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202202/W020220214350545588797.png" title="北向资金增持的个股.png" alt="北向资金增持的个股.png" oldsrc="W020220214350545588797.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
