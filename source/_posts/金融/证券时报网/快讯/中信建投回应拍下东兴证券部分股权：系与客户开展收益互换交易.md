<p>证券时报e公司讯，在司法拍卖平台，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601066" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601066" target="_blank">中信建投</a>拍下了<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601198" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601198" target="_blank">东兴证券</a>4253万股股份。如<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601066" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601066" target="_blank">中信建投</a>最终持有该部分股权，将成为<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601198" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601198" target="_blank">东兴证券</a>第七大股东。此事引发市场关注。就此，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601066" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601066" target="_blank">中信建投</a>表示，“此举是客户与我司开展互换交易，希望以相对市价有一定折扣的成本获得<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601198" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601198" target="_blank">东兴证券</a>股票权益损益，我司通过法拍途径折价买入标的股票，完成了前述互换交易的对冲”。(券中社)</p>
