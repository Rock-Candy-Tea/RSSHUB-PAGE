<p>证券时报e公司讯，记者从<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688179" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="688179" target="_blank">阿拉丁</a>控股集团获悉，公司旗下<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688179" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="688179" target="_blank">阿拉丁</a>环保集团有限公司完成对中国节能环保集团旗下<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300140" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300140" target="_blank">中环装备</a>的战略投资。这是本月<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688179" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="688179" target="_blank">阿拉丁</a>能源集团完成对<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603126" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="603126" target="_blank">中材节能</a>战略投资后，半月内完成的第二起“央企系”A股公司投资。节能环保装备市场规模有望在2025年达到1.5万亿元。<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688179" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="688179" target="_blank">阿拉丁</a>投资<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300140" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300140" target="_blank">中环装备</a>增强了在节能环保装备领域的布局，进一步完善了产业链生态，并将利用资本和市场机制的优势助力<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300140" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300140" target="_blank">中环装备</a>行业整合。</p>
