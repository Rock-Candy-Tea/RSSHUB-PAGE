<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，近10个交易日（6月16日~6月29日）共有40家上市公司股份获高管增持。从增持金额看，近10日增持金额最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002351" code="002351">漫步者</a>，累计增持1850万股，增持金额合计2.23亿元；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600352" code="600352">浙江龙盛</a>，增持量为300万股，增持金额4128万元。</p><p>增持次数方面，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002958" code="002958">青农商行</a>增持最频繁，近10日共有9条增持记录，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600663" code="600663">陆家嘴</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600801" code="600801">华新水泥</a>等紧随其后，近10日分别有8条、6条增持记录。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002798" code="002798">帝欧家居</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300552" code="300552">万集科技</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS605168" code="605168">三人行</a>等期间也有两条以上的增持记录。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202106/W020210630447338541675.png" title="近10日高管增持股一览.png" alt="近10日高管增持股一览.png" oldsrc="W020210630447338541675.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
