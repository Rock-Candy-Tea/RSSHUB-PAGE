<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，近10个交易日（1月19日~2月8日）共有29家上市公司股份获高管增持。从增持金额看，近10日增持金额最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300529" code="300529">健帆生物</a>，累计增持208.98万股，增持金额合计1.05亿元；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300015" code="300015">爱尔眼科</a>，增持量为125.54万股，增持金额4220.71万元。</p><p>增持次数方面，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300015" code="300015">爱尔眼科</a>增持最频繁，近10日共有16条增持记录，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000661" code="000661">长春高新</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603060" code="603060">国检集团</a>等紧随其后，近10日分别有10条、7条增持记录。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002778" code="002778">中晟高科</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600048" code="600048">保利发展</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600476" code="600476">湘邮科技</a>等期间也有两条以上的增持记录。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202202/W020220209353483032657.png" title="近10日高管增持股一览.png" alt="近10日高管增持股一览.png" oldsrc="W020220209353483032657.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
