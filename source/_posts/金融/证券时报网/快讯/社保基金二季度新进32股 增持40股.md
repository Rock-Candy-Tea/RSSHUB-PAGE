<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计，二季度末社保基金共现身157只个股前十大流通股东榜。其中，新进32股、增持40股。社保基金新建仓股票中，从前十大流通股东名单中社保基金家数来看，社保基金家数最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300711" code="300711">广哈通信</a>，共有3家社保基金出现在前十大流通股东名单中。</p><p>从持股比例看，新进股中社保基金持有比例最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ003006" code="003006">百亚股份</a>，持股量占流通股比例为5.27%；持股比例居前的还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ003020" code="003020">立方制药</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002139" code="002139">拓邦股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300327" code="300327">中颖电子</a>等。持股量来看，社保基金新进的个股中，持股量最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600703" code="600703">三安光电</a>，共持有5300.94万股；<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600166" code="600166">福田汽车</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600110" code="600110">诺德股份</a>等紧随其后。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202108/W020210823410542342433.png" title="社保基金新进股一览.png" alt="社保基金新进股一览.png" oldsrc="W020210823410542342433.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
