<p>证券时报e公司讯，记者2月21日了解到，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601398" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601398" target="_blank">工商银行</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601288" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601288" target="_blank">农业银行</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601988" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601988" target="_blank">中国银行</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601939" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601939" target="_blank">建设银行</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601328" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601328" target="_blank">交通银行</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601658" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601658" target="_blank">邮储银行</a>六大家银行从今天起，下调了广州地区房贷利率。其中，首套房优惠审批利率从此前的LPR+100BP（5.6%）下调至LPR+80BP（5.4%），二套房优惠审批利率从此前的LPR+120BP（5.8%）下调至LPR+100BP（5.6%）。(澎湃新闻)</p>
