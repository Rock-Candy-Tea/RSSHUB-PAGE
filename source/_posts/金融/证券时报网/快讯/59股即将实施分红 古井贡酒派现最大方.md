<p>证券时报网讯，Wind统计显示，股权登记日为今日（6月10日）的共有59家上市公司。从这些公司的分红方案来看，每10股派现金额在1元（含税）及以上的共有46家。其中，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000596" code="000596">古井贡酒</a>派现最大方，每10股派现22元；<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600295" code="600295">鄂尔多斯</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603203" code="603203">快克股份</a>等紧随其后，每10股派现金额分别为20元、13元。</p><p>从送转比例来看，股权登记日为今天的即将实施分红公司中<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600295" code="600295">鄂尔多斯</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002179" code="002179">中航光电</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ301178" code="301178">天亿马</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688131" code="688131">皓元医药</a>每10股均送转4股，送转比例最高。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202206/W020220610366177281153.png" title="即将实施分红的公司一览.png" alt="即将实施分红的公司一览.png" oldsrc="W020220610366177281153.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
