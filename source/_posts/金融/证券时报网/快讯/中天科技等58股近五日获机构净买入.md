<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计，近五个交易日的龙虎榜当中，103只个股出现了机构的身影，其中58只股票呈现机构净买入，45只股票呈现机构净卖出。近五日机构净买入前三名分别是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603606" code="603606">东方电缆</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600522" code="600522">中天科技</a>、采纳股份。</p><p>与此同时，近五个交易日登陆沪深两市的龙虎榜个股中，营业部净买入较大的个股有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002434" code="002434">万里扬</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601618" code="601618">中国中冶</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603229" code="603229">奥翔药业</a>。具体来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002434" code="002434">万里扬</a>是资金最青睐个股，营业部净买入金额达2.48亿元，高居榜首。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202202/W020220208363468539792.png" title="近五日龙虎榜机构净买入金额最多个股.png" alt="近五日龙虎榜机构净买入金额最多个股.png" width="550" oldsrc="W020220208363468539792.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
