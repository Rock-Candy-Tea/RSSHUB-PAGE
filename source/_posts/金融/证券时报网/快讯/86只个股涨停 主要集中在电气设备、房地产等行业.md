<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，3月23日，沪深两市可交易A股中，上涨个股有2272只，下跌个股有2221只，平盘个股175只。不含当日上市新股，共有86只个股涨停，16只个股跌停。以所属行业来看，涨停个股主要集中在电气设备、房地产、机械设备、通信、化工、医药生物等行业。</p><p>涨停股中，从收盘涨停板封单量来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000063" code="000063">中兴通讯</a>最受追捧，收盘涨停板封单有7693.35万股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002060" code="002060">粤水电</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000671" code="000671">阳光城</a>等，涨停板封单分别有6865.84万股、5882.43万股。以封单金额计算，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000063" code="000063">中兴通讯</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002060" code="002060">粤水电</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603261" code="603261">立航科技</a>等涨停板封单资金较多，分别有20亿元、5.2亿元、5.1亿元。</p>
