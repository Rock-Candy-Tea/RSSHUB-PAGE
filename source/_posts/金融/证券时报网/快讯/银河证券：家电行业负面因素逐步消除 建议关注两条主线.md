<p>证券时报e公司讯，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601881" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601881" target="_blank">中国银河</a>证券研报表示，家电行业二季度由于国内部分地区疫情爆发、海外需求疲软等因素影响，营收增速放缓。展望未来，家电行业面临的原材料涨价，海运不畅等外部负面因素逐步消除，稳增长背景下地产景气度边际改善，促消费政策逐步落地、疫情压制的需求释放等因素推动家电需求改善。建议关注两条主线，一、受益于基本面改善的传统家电龙头，推荐<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000333" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000333" target="_blank">美的集团</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000651" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000651" target="_blank">格力电器</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600690" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600690" target="_blank">海尔智家</a>和<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002508" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002508" target="_blank">老板电器</a>。二、关注景气度处于高位的细分行业龙头，推荐<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603486" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="603486" target="_blank">科沃斯</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688169" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="688169" target="_blank">石头科技</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603355" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="603355" target="_blank">莱克电气</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300894" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300894" target="_blank">火星人</a>。</p>
