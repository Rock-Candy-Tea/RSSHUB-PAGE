<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，近5个交易日（2月15日至2月21日）沪深两市约208家上市公司被机构调研。机构调研榜单中，共有54家公司获20家以上机构扎堆调研。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002906" code="002906">华阳集团</a>最受关注，参与调研的机构达到250家；<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ301029" code="301029">怡合达</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000661" code="000661">长春高新</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300638" code="300638">广和通</a>等分别被142家、130家、107家机构调研。机构调研次数来看，有2家公司被机构调研3次，分别是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000338" code="000338">潍柴动力</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002158" code="002158">汉钟精机</a>等，机构调研最为密集。</p><p>20家以上机构扎堆调研股中，近5日资金净流入的有22只，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002709" code="002709">天赐材料</a>近5日净流入资金3.38亿元，主力资金净流入最多；净流入资金较多的还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000762" code="000762">西藏矿业</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000661" code="000661">长春高新</a>等。</p>
