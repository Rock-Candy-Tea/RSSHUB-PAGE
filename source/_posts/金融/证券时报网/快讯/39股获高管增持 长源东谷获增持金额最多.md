<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，近10个交易日（9月13日~9月28日）共有39家上市公司股份获高管增持。从增持金额看，近10日增持金额最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603950" code="603950">长源东谷</a>，累计增持140.16万股，增持金额合计2843.52万元；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603816" code="603816">顾家家居</a>，增持量为33.62万股，增持金额2081.08万元。</p><p>增持次数方面，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600387" code="600387">ST海越</a>增持最频繁，近10日共有11条增持记录，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000403" code="000403">派林生物</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600650" code="600650">锦江在线</a>等紧随其后，近10日各有11条增持记录。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601963" code="601963">重庆银行</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601318" code="601318">中国平安</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000661" code="000661">长春高新</a>等期间也有两条以上的增持记录。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202109/W020210929440976351624.png" title="近10日高管增持股一览.png" alt="近10日高管增持股一览.png" oldsrc="W020210929440976351624.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
