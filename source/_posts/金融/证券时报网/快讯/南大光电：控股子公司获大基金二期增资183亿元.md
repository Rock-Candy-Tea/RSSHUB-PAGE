<p>证券时报e公司讯，<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300346" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300346" target="_blank">南大光电</a>(300346)7月27日晚间公告，为加快光刻胶事业发展，控股子公司宁波<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300346" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300346" target="_blank">南大光电</a>材料有限公司（简称“宁波<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300346" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300346" target="_blank">南大光电</a>”）拟通过增资扩股方式引入战略投资者大基金二期。大基金二期将以合计18330万元的价格，认购公司宁波<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300346" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300346" target="_blank">南大光电</a>的新增注册资本6733.19万元。此次增资完成后，公司在宁波<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300346" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300346" target="_blank">南大光电</a>持股比例由71.67%降至58.53%。</p>
