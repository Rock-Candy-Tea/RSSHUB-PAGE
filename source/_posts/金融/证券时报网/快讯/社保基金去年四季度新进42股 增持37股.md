<p>证券时报网讯，据wind统计显示，截止4月6日，2021年四季度末社保基金共现身159只个股前十大流通股东榜。其中，新进42只，增持37只。社保基金重仓股中，从前十大流通股东名单中社保基金家数来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600426" code="600426">华鲁恒升</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600188" code="600188">兖矿能源</a>等2只股有4家社保基金集中现身。</p><p>从持股比例看，社保基金持有比例最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300638" code="300638">广和通</a>，持股量占流通股比例为8.76%；持股比例居前的还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603155" code="603155">新亚强</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ003038" code="003038">鑫铂股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688276" code="688276">百克生物</a>等。持股数量方面，社保基金持股数量在5000万股以上的共有15只，社保基金持股量最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601899" code="601899">紫金矿业</a>，共持有4.01亿股；<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ003816" code="003816">中国广核</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000629" code="000629">攀钢钒钛</a>等紧随其后，持股量分别为1.68亿股、1.6亿股。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202204/W020220406354823297359.png" title="社保基金四季度增持的个股.png" alt="社保基金四季度增持的个股.png" oldsrc="W020220406354823297359.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
