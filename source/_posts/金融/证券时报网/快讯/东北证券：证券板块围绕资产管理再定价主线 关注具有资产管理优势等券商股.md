<p>证券时报网讯，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000686" code="000686">东北证券</a>指出，上半年证券公司业绩维持高增，139家证券公司共实现营业收入2324.14亿元，实现净利润人民币902.79亿元，分别同比增长8.91%和8.58%。目前证券板块依然围绕资产管理再定价主线进行挖掘，继续推荐具备资产管理优势的<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600958" code="600958">东方证券</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000776" code="000776">广发证券</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601377" code="601377">兴业证券</a>，重点推荐资产管理、投行、自营、金融衍生品、经纪业务、国际业务持续领先的龙头券商：<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600030" code="600030">中信证券</a>。同时，随着北交所的正式推出，新三板业务领域布局深厚的券商率先受益，如<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000166" code="000166">申万宏源</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601066" code="601066">中信建投</a>等。</p>
