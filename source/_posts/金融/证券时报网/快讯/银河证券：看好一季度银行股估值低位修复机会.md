<p>证券时报网讯，银河证券指出，持续关注宽信用对银行经营环境改善、业绩稳健增长和资产质量优化的促进作用，看好一季度银行股估值低位修复机会，维持“推荐”评级。个股方面，跟踪两条主线：（1）区位经济优势明显、资产端增长动能强劲、资产质量同业领先的优质城农商行，推荐<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601009" code="601009">南京银行</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601128" code="601128">常熟银行</a>和<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600926" code="600926">杭州银行</a>；（2）中收贡献度高，财富管理业务领先，估值有望实现中长期溢价的个股，推荐<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600036" code="600036">招商银行</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000001" code="000001">平安银行</a>。</p>
