<p>证券时报e公司讯，记者获悉，3月3日，机器视觉产品提供商睿图智能宣布获得<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603960" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="603960" target="_blank">克来机电</a>的Pre-A轮战略投资。这是继睿图智能获得的新一轮融资。对于本次战略融资，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603960" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="603960" target="_blank">克来机电</a>表明了看好机器视觉在柔性自动化装备和智能<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300024" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300024" target="_blank">机器人</a>领域的发展趋势和机会。未来，睿图智能和<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603960" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="603960" target="_blank">克来机电</a>将在物流等领域有深度的产品拓展和合作。</p>
