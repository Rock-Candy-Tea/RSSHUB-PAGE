<p>证券时报网讯，据wind统计显示，11月1日共有807只股获北向资金持股量环比上一个交易日增加。以增持的股份数量和当日收盘价为基准进行测算，加仓股中，增持市值在亿元以上的有41只，增持市值最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300750" code="300750">宁德时代</a>，最新持股量为1.79亿股，环比增加1.11%，增持市值为12.74亿元；增持市值较多还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600519" code="600519">贵州茅台</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002241" code="002241">歌尔股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000100" code="000100">TCL科技</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000625" code="000625">长安汽车</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300124" code="300124">汇川技术</a>等股。</p><p>从机构动向看，北向资金增持市值居前个股，近一个月获机构调研的有7只；机构调研家数看，机构较为扎堆的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002241" code="002241">歌尔股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300124" code="300124">汇川技术</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002460" code="002460">赣锋锂业</a>。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202111/W020211102345457910087.png" title="北向资金增持市值排名.png" alt="北向资金增持市值排名.png" oldsrc="W020211102345457910087.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
