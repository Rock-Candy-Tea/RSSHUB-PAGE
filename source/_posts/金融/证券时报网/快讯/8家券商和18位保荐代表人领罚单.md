<p>证券时报e公司讯，证监会集中公布了二季度以来，证监会系统对投行业务采取的行政监管措施、自律惩戒措施，共有13条行政监管举措，8家券商和18位保荐代表人领罚单，涉及的机构有安信证券、<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002797" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002797" target="_blank">第一创业</a>证券承销保荐、<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000686" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000686" target="_blank">东北证券</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002926" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002926" target="_blank">华西证券</a>、宏信证券、<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600837" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600837" target="_blank">海通证券</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600109" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600109" target="_blank">国金证券</a>和<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000750" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000750" target="_blank">国海证券</a>。此轮领罚单的券商，和债券业务、首发（IPO）保荐业务把关不严有关，其中，债券业务“看门人”职责失守的占主导。另有3家券商的罚单和IPO业务相关，这包括<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000750" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000750" target="_blank">国海证券</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002797" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002797" target="_blank">第一创业</a>证券承销保荐和<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600109" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600109" target="_blank">国金证券</a>。(券商中国)</p>
