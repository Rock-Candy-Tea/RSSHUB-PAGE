<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计，近五个交易日的龙虎榜当中，106只个股出现了机构的身影，其中59只股票呈现机构净买入，47只股票呈现机构净卖出。近五日机构净买入前三名分别是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603606" code="603606">东方电缆</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600522" code="600522">中天科技</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002460" code="002460">赣锋锂业</a>。</p><p>与此同时，近五个交易日登陆沪深两市的龙虎榜个股中，营业部净买入较大的个股有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603229" code="603229">奥翔药业</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002654" code="002654">万润科技</a>、铜冠铜箔。具体来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603229" code="603229">奥翔药业</a>是资金最青睐个股，营业部净买入金额达1.97亿元，高居榜首。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202202/W020220207362548693961.png" title="近五日龙虎榜机构净买入金额最多个股.png" alt="近五日龙虎榜机构净买入金额最多个股.png" width="550" oldsrc="W020220207362548693961.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
