<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，近5个交易日（2月9日至2月15日）沪深两市约113家上市公司被机构调研。机构调研榜单中，共有26家公司获20家以上机构扎堆调研。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300036" code="300036">超图软件</a>最受关注，参与调研的机构达到289家；<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000963" code="000963">华东医药</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002475" code="002475">立讯精密</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300760" code="300760">迈瑞医疗</a>等分别被243家、223家、202家机构调研。机构调研次数来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002988" code="002988">豪美新材</a>机构调研最为密集，共获机构3次调研。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ301159" code="301159">三维天地</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002432" code="002432">九安医疗</a>等均被机构调研2次。</p><p>20家以上机构扎堆调研股中，近5日资金净流入的有7只，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002230" code="002230">科大讯飞</a>近5日净流入资金2.32亿元，主力资金净流入最多；净流入资金较多的还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002475" code="002475">立讯精密</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002908" code="002908">德生科技</a>等。</p>
