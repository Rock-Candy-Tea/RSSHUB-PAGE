<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，今日可交易A股中，上涨个股有1793只，下跌个股有2609只，平盘个股123只。不含当日上市新股，共有72只个股涨停，31只个股跌停。以所属行业来看，涨停个股主要集中在传媒、汽车、电子、化工、机械设备等行业。</p><p>涨停股中，从收盘涨停板封单量来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002096" code="002096">南岭民爆</a>最受追捧，收盘涨停板封单有4918.9万股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002170" code="002170">芭田股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002045" code="002045">国光电器</a>，涨停板封单分别有2305.34万股、2074.05万股。以封单金额计算，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002096" code="002096">南岭民爆</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002045" code="002045">国光电器</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603386" code="603386">广东骏亚</a>等涨停板封单资金较多，分别有6.92亿元、2.73亿元、2.25亿元。连续涨停天数看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600856" code="600856">ST中天</a>已连收9个涨停板，连续涨停板数量最多。</p>
