<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，二季度末养老金账户共现身46只个股前十大流通股东榜。其中，新进16只，增持9只。二季度末养老金账户对<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600352" code="600352">浙江龙盛</a>的持股量最多，持股量为3868.18万股。从期末持股市值看，养老金账户期末持股市值在亿元以上的有26只股，分别是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600352" code="600352">浙江龙盛</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603128" code="603128">华贸物流</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002850" code="002850">科达利</a>等。</p><p>持股比例方面，养老金账户持股比例最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688095" code="688095">福昕软件</a>，合计持股量为97.07万股，占流通股比例8.32%。养老金持股比例居前的还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002732" code="002732">燕塘乳业</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300196" code="300196">长海股份</a>等。从持有时间来看，养老金账户持股期限最长的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002878" code="002878">元隆雅图</a>，已连续12个报告期出现在公司前十大流通股东名单中。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202108/W020210823456941893903.png" title="二季度养老金持股明细.png" alt="二季度养老金持股明细.png" oldsrc="W020210823456941893903.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
