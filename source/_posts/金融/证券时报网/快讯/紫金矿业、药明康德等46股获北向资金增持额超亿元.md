<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，9月2日共有643只股获北向资金持股量环比上一个交易日增加。以增持的股份数量和当日收盘价为基准进行测算，加仓股中，增持市值在亿元以上的有46只，增持市值最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601899" code="601899">紫金矿业</a>，最新持股量为8.97亿股，环比增加12.96%，增持市值为11.22亿元；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600519" code="600519">贵州茅台</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603259" code="603259">药明康德</a>。</p><p>从机构动向看，北向资金增持市值居前个股，近一个月获机构调研的有11只，机构调研次数较为频繁的有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002585" code="002585">双星新材</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000932" code="000932">华菱钢铁</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603986" code="603986">兆易创新</a>等；机构调研家数看，机构较为扎堆的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300760" code="300760">迈瑞医疗</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603986" code="603986">兆易创新</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688012" code="688012">中微公司</a>。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202109/W020210903350165047463.png" title="北向资金增持市值排名.png" alt="北向资金增持市值排名.png" oldsrc="W020210903350165047463.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
