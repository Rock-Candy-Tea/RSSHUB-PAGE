<p>证券时报网讯，据wind统计显示，1月13日共有1021只个股获融资净买入，净买入金额在千万元以上的有227只。其中，10只融资净买入额超1亿元。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300750" code="300750">宁德时代</a>融资净买入额居首，当日净买入6.86亿元；<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600519" code="600519">贵州茅台</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600703" code="600703">三安光电</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002371" code="002371">北方华创</a>获融资净买入额超2亿元。融资净买入金额居前的还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000858" code="000858">五粮液</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300059" code="300059">东方财富</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002603" code="002603">以岭药业</a>等。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202201/W020220114341187703069.png" title="1月13日融资客净买入金额排名.png" alt="1月13日融资客净买入金额排名.png" oldsrc="W020220114341187703069.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
