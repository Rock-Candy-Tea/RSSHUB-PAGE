<p>证券时报e公司讯，<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000892" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000892" target="_blank">欢瑞世纪</a>(000892)12日晚公告，公司收到股东欢瑞联合的通知，欢瑞联合就诉讼事项与质权人<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601901" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601901" target="_blank">方正证券</a>达成和解。该诉讼事项涉及公司原实控人控制的公司股东欢瑞联合与<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601901" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601901" target="_blank">方正证券</a>的股权质押回购逾期违约纠纷，标的金额约6.4亿元。9月29日，申请执行人<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601901" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601901" target="_blank">方正证券</a>与被执行人欢瑞联合等达成执行和解协议。协议约定，赵枳程等提供执行担保，<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601901" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601901" target="_blank">方正证券</a>同意将涉案债务履行延期三年等内容。公司表示，本次和解缓解了欢瑞联合所持公司股份因质押违约存在的被强制平仓风险。</p>
