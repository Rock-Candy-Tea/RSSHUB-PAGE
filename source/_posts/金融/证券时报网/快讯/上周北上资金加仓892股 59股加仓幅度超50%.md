<p>证券时报网讯，证券时报·数据宝统计显示，上周北上资金持股量环比增加的有23个行业，电子行业环比增幅最高。环比增幅居前的还有综合、纺织服装、化工、传媒等行业。</p><p>上周北上资金加仓股共892只，以持股数量增长幅度来看，加仓幅度超50%的股票有59只，超100%的股票有23只。其中，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300366" code="300366">创意信息</a>的加仓力度最大；加仓幅度靠前的还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600295" code="600295">鄂尔多斯</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000567" code="000567">海德股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601330" code="601330">绿色动力</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600197" code="600197">伊力特</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300177" code="300177">中海达</a>等。</p>
