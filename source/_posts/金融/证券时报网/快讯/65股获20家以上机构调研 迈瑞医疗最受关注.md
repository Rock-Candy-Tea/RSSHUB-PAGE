<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，近5个交易日（12月31日至1月7日）沪深两市约194家上市公司被机构调研。机构调研榜单中，共有65家公司获20家以上机构扎堆调研。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300760" code="300760">迈瑞医疗</a>最受关注，参与调研的机构达到649家；<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688518" code="688518">联赢激光</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688556" code="688556">高测股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300496" code="300496">中科创达</a>分别被455家、380家、362家机构调研。机构调研次数来看，有2家公司被机构调研3次，分别是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002003" code="002003">伟星股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002171" code="002171">楚江新材</a>等，机构调研最为密集。</p><p>20家以上机构扎堆调研股中，近5日资金净流入的有12只，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002714" code="002714">牧原股份</a>近5日净流入资金7.34亿元，主力资金净流入最多；净流入资金较多的还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000762" code="000762">西藏矿业</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002873" code="002873">新天药业</a>等。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202201/W020220110348136248588.png" title="近5日机构调研股一览.png" alt="近5日机构调研股一览.png" oldsrc="W020220110348136248588.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
