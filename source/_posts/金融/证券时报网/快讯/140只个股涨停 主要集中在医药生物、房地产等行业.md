<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，3月21日，沪深两市可交易A股中，上涨个股有3192只，下跌个股有1316只，平盘个股152只。不含当日上市新股，共有140只个股涨停，3只个股跌停。以所属行业来看，涨停个股主要集中在医药生物、房地产、化工、机械设备、农林牧渔、建筑装饰等行业。</p><p>涨停股中，从收盘涨停板封单量来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002613" code="002613">北玻股份</a>最受追捧，收盘涨停板封单有6957.48万股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002060" code="002060">粤水电</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600736" code="600736">苏州高新</a>等，涨停板封单分别有5842.38万股、3026.91万股。以封单金额计算，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603261" code="603261">立航科技</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600196" code="600196">复星医药</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601607" code="601607">上海医药</a>等涨停板封单资金较多，分别有10.81亿元、6.7亿元、4.9亿元。</p>
