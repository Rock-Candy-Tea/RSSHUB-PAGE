<p>证券时报e公司讯，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600372" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600372" target="_blank">中航电子</a>(600372)6月10日晚间公告，公司拟以发行A股方式换股吸收合并<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002013" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002013" target="_blank">中航机电</a>。同时，本次换股吸收合并完成后，机载公司将其原持有<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600372" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600372" target="_blank">中航电子</a>的股份以及原持有<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002013" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002013" target="_blank">中航机电</a>的股份按照换股比例换成的<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600372" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600372" target="_blank">中航电子</a>股份对应的表决权委托给中航科工。另外，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600372" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600372" target="_blank">中航电子</a>拟采用询价的方式向包括中航科工、航空投资、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600760" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600760" target="_blank">中航沈飞</a>、航空工业成飞在内的不超过35名特定投资者非公开发行A股股票募集配套资金不超过50亿元。<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002013" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002013" target="_blank">中航机电</a>与<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600372" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600372" target="_blank">中航电子</a>的换股比例为1:0.6605，即每1股<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002013" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002013" target="_blank">中航机电</a>股票可以换得0.6605股<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600372" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600372" target="_blank">中航电子</a>股票。公司股票将于6月13日开市起复牌。</p>
