<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，近10个交易日（4月6日~4月19日）共有13家上市公司股份获高管增持。从增持金额看，近10日增持金额最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002241" code="002241">歌尔股份</a>，累计增持29万股，增持金额合计942.11万元；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002273" code="002273">水晶光电</a>，增持量为62.35万股，增持金额636.03万元。</p><p>增持次数方面，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002273" code="002273">水晶光电</a>增持最频繁，近10日共有5条增持记录；<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002056" code="002056">横店东磁</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002422" code="002422">科伦药业</a>等紧随其后，近10日分别有4条、3条增持记录。</p>
