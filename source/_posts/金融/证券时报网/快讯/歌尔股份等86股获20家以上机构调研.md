<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，近5个交易日（8月23日至8月27日）沪深两市约187家上市公司被机构调研。机构调研榜单中，共有86家公司获20家以上机构扎堆调研。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300896" code="300896">爱美客</a>最受关注，参与调研的机构达到503家；<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002241" code="002241">歌尔股份</a>被360家机构调研，榜单中排名第二。</p><p>机构调研次数来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300296" code="300296">利亚德</a>机构调研最为密集，共获机构4次调研。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002527" code="002527">新时达</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000629" code="000629">攀钢钒钛</a>等机构调研也较为密集，均被机构调研3次。机构扎堆股中，有80股已经公布了半年报，净利润同比增幅较高的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002407" code="002407">多氟多</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000301" code="000301">东方盛虹</a>，净利润分别增长1859.97%、1720.3%。</p><p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202108/W020210830341263064243.png" title="近5日机构调研股一览.png" alt="近5日机构调研股一览.png" oldsrc="W020210830341263064243.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>责编：孙孝熙</p>
