<p>证券时报网讯，据wind统计显示，1月24日共有787只个股获北向资金持股量环比上一个交易日增加。以增持的股份数量和当日收盘价为基准进行测算，加仓股中，增持市值在1亿元以上的有23只，增持市值最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600036" code="600036">招商银行</a>，最新持股量为16.41亿股，环比增加1.42%，增持市值为11.95亿元；增持市值较多还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601688" code="601688">华泰证券</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000858" code="000858">五粮液</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601166" code="601166">兴业银行</a>等股。</p><p>从机构动向看，北向资金增持市值居前个股，近一个月获机构调研的有3只，机构调研次数较为频繁的有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002142" code="002142">宁波银行</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300015" code="300015">爱尔眼科</a>等，期间分别获机构调研4次、2次；机构调研家数看，机构较为扎堆的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300073" code="300073">当升科技</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002142" code="002142">宁波银行</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300015" code="300015">爱尔眼科</a>。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202201/W020220125356535139484.png" title="北向资金增持市值排名.png" alt="北向资金增持市值排名.png" oldsrc="W020220125356535139484.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
