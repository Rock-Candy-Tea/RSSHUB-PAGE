<p>证券时报e公司讯，11月22日，有色金属板块全天表现活跃。Wind数据显示，截至收盘，申万一级有色金属行业指数涨超3%，今年以来涨超46%。当天，<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603799" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="603799" target="_blank">华友钴业</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000831" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000831" target="_blank">五矿稀土</a>等多股涨停，<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600549" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600549" target="_blank">厦门钨业</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002460" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002460" target="_blank">赣锋锂业</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002466" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002466" target="_blank">天齐锂业</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600259" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600259" target="_blank">广晟有色</a>等涨超4%。业内人士指出，有色金属板块表现活跃，与新能源市场的结构性行情关系密切。<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601066" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601066" target="_blank">中信建投</a>在有色金属行业2022年投资策略报告中指出，由于下游市场需求旺盛，锂镍锰等新能源金属景气度有望持续。(经济参考报)</p>
