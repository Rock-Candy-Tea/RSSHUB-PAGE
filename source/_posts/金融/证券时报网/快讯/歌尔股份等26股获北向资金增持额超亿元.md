<p>证券时报网讯，据wind统计显示，2月8日共有729只个股获北向资金持股量环比上一个交易日增加。以增持的股份数量和当日收盘价为基准进行测算，加仓股中，增持市值在1亿元以上的有26只，增持市值最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601318" code="601318">中国平安</a>，最新持股量为7.29亿股，环比增加1.44%，增持市值为5.48亿元；增持市值较多还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600036" code="600036">招商银行</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002241" code="002241">歌尔股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601166" code="601166">兴业银行</a>等股。</p><p>业绩来看，北向资金大手笔增持股中，有5股已经公布了2021年业绩快报，净利润增幅较高的有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600436" code="600436">片仔癀</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000001" code="000001">平安银行</a>，增幅为45.55%、25.60%。公布2021年业绩预告的共有8只，业绩预告类型来看，预增有7只。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202202/W020220209341844004843.png" title="北向资金增持市值排名.png" alt="北向资金增持市值排名.png" oldsrc="W020220209341844004843.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
