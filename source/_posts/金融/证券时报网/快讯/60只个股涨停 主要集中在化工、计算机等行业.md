<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，2月28日，沪深两市可交易A股中，上涨个股有1972只，下跌个股有2529只，平盘个股140只。不含当日上市新股，共有60只个股涨停，20只个股跌停。以所属行业来看，涨停个股主要集中在化工、计算机、交通运输、商贸零售、公用事业等行业。</p><p>涨停股中，从收盘涨停板封单量来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600190" code="600190">锦州港</a>最受追捧，收盘涨停板封单有7404.27万股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600734" code="600734">*ST实达</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002537" code="002537">海联金汇</a>等，涨停板封单分别有4349.21万股、2829.03万股。以封单金额计算，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603122" code="603122">合富中国</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600190" code="600190">锦州港</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002537" code="002537">海联金汇</a>等涨停板封单资金较多，分别有2.62亿元、2.58亿元、2.44亿元。连续涨停天数看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600734" code="600734">*ST实达</a>已连收11个涨停板，连续涨停板数量最多。</p>
