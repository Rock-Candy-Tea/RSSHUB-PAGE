<p>证券时报网讯，Wind统计显示，股权登记日为今日的共有61家公司。从这些公司的分红方案来看，每10股派现金额在1元（含税）及以上的共有41家，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688399" code="688399">硕世生物</a>派现最大方，每10股派现55元；<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603816" code="603816">顾家家居</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603203" code="603203">快克股份</a>紧随其后，每10股派现金额分别为8.2元、8元。</p><p>从送转比例来看，股权登记日为今天的即将实施分红公司中，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601012" code="601012">隆基股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603387" code="603387">基蛋生物</a>每10股均送转4股，送转比例最高。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202106/W020210622402853572622.png" title="即将实施分红的公司一览.png" alt="即将实施分红的公司一览.png" oldsrc="W020210622402853572622.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
