<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，近5个交易日（4月25日至4月29日）沪深两市约125家上市公司被机构调研。机构调研榜单中，共有79家公司获20家以上机构扎堆调研。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002241" code="002241">歌尔股份</a>最受关注，参与调研的机构达到457家；<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600745" code="600745">闻泰科技</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600885" code="600885">宏发股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002036" code="002036">联创电子</a>等分别被412家、379家、354家机构调研。机构调研次数来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002461" code="002461">珠江啤酒</a>机构调研最为密集，共获机构3次调研。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603856" code="603856">东宏股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002430" code="002430">杭氧股份</a>均被机构调研2次。</p><p>20家以上机构扎堆调研股中，近5日资金净流入的有39只，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002241" code="002241">歌尔股份</a>近5日净流入资金8.46亿元，主力资金净流入最多；净流入资金较多的还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600745" code="600745">闻泰科技</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002192" code="002192">融捷股份</a>等。</p>
