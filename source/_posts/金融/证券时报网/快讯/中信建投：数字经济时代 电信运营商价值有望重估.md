<p>证券时报e公司讯，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601066" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601066" target="_blank">中信建投</a>研报认为，近年来，电信运营商面向政企客户的产业数字化类业务快速增长。2021H1，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601728" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601728" target="_blank">中国电信</a>产业数字化业务收入501亿元，同比增长16.8%；<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600941" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600941" target="_blank">中国移动</a>DICT收入334亿元，同比增长59.8%；<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600050" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600050" target="_blank">中国联通</a>产业互联网业务收入280亿元，同比增长23.6%。其中，电信运营商云业务快速增长，2021H1，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601728" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601728" target="_blank">中国电信</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600941" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600941" target="_blank">中国移动</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600050" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600050" target="_blank">中国联通</a>分别实现收入103亿元、97亿元、28亿元，分别同比增长122%、118.1%、38.9%。产业数字化方兴未艾，电信运营商“十四五”期间产业数字化类业务仍有望保持较快增速，价值有待重估。</p>
