<p>证券时报网讯，据wind统计显示，12月7日共有779只股获北向资金持股量环比上一个交易日增加。以增持的股份数量和当日收盘价为基准进行测算，加仓股中，增持市值在1亿元以上的有43只，增持市值最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601318" code="601318">中国平安</a>，最新持股量为6.01亿股，增持市值为5.39亿元；增持市值较多还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600036" code="600036">招商银行</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600030" code="600030">中信证券</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603501" code="603501">韦尔股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601166" code="601166">兴业银行</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300014" code="300014">亿纬锂能</a>等股。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202112/W020211208378633807535.png" title="部分北向资金增持市值超亿元的个股.png" alt="部分北向资金增持市值超亿元的个股.png" oldsrc="W020211208378633807535.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
