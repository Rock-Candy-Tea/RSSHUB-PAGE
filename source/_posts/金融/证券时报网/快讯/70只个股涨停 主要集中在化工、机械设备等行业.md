<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，沪深两市可交易A股中，上涨个股有2752只，下跌个股有1716只，平盘个股121只。不含当日上市新股，共有70只个股涨停，21只个股跌停。以所属行业来看，涨停个股主要集中在化工、机械设备、计算机、医药生物、建筑装饰等行业。</p><p>涨停股中，从收盘涨停板封单量来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600927" code="600927">永安期货</a>最受追捧，收盘涨停板封单有4182.97万股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002480" code="002480">新筑股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002256" code="002256">兆新股份</a>等，涨停板封单分别有3701.6万股、1883.56万股。以封单金额计算，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600927" code="600927">永安期货</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ001296" code="001296">长江材料</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600668" code="600668">尖峰集团</a>等涨停板封单资金较多，分别有14.41亿元、4.39亿元、2.85亿元。</p>
