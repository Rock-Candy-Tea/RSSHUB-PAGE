<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，9月6日共有709只股获北向资金持股量环比上一个交易日增加。以增持的股份数量和当日收盘价为基准进行测算，加仓股中，增持市值在亿元以上的有40只，增持市值最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300750" code="300750">宁德时代</a>，最新持股量为1.54亿股，环比增加1.98%，增持市值为15.47亿元；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600570" code="600570">恒生电子</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002352" code="002352">顺丰控股</a>。</p><p>从机构动向看，北向资金增持市值居前个股，近一个月获机构调研的有12只，机构调研次数较为频繁的有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603986" code="603986">兆易创新</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000629" code="000629">攀钢钒钛</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000661" code="000661">长春高新</a>等；机构调研家数看，机构较为扎堆的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603986" code="603986">兆易创新</a>、爱美客、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300316" code="300316">晶盛机电</a>。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202109/W020210907347775602535.png" title="北向资金增持市值排名.png" alt="北向资金增持市值排名.png" oldsrc="W020210907347775602535.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
