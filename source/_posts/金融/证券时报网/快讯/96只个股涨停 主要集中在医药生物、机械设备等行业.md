<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，2月14日，沪深两市可交易A股中，上涨个股有2179只，下跌个股有2301只，平盘个股149只。不含当日上市新股，共有96只个股涨停，23只个股跌停。以所属行业来看，涨停个股主要集中在传媒、机械设备、医药生物、建筑装饰、化工、汽车等行业。</p><p>涨停股中，从收盘涨停板封单量来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603843" code="603843">正平股份</a>最受追捧，收盘涨停板封单有3401.69万股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000428" code="000428">华天酒店</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002542" code="002542">中化岩土</a>等，涨停板封单分别有3318.68万股、3280.71万股。以封单金额计算，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300363" code="300363">博腾股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603843" code="603843">正平股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002349" code="002349">精华制药</a>等涨停板封单资金较多，分别有3.3亿元、2.07亿元、1.94亿元。连续涨停天数看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002761" code="002761">浙江建投</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002878" code="002878">元隆雅图</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600078" code="600078">*ST澄星</a>均已连收6个涨停板，连续涨停板数量最多。</p>
