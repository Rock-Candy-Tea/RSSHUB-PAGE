<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，近5个交易日（1月6日至1月12日）沪深两市约156家上市公司被机构调研。机构调研榜单中，共有47家公司获20家以上机构扎堆调研。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688005" code="688005">容百科技</a>最受关注，参与调研的机构达到518家；<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000762" code="000762">西藏矿业</a>被223家机构调研，榜单中排名第二；<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002756" code="002756">永兴材料</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000739" code="000739">普洛药业</a>等分别被118家、96家机构调研。机构调研次数来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000762" code="000762">西藏矿业</a>机构调研最为密集，共获机构5次调研。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002345" code="002345">潮宏基</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300456" code="300456">赛微电子</a>等机构调研也较为密集，分别被机构调研4次、3次。</p><p>20家以上机构扎堆调研股中，近5日资金净流入的有16只，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000762" code="000762">西藏矿业</a>近5日净流入资金5.32亿元，主力资金净流入最多；净流入资金较多的还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002756" code="002756">永兴材料</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002714" code="002714">牧原股份</a>等。</p>
