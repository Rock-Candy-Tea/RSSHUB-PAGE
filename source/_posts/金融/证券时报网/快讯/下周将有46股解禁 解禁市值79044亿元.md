<p>证券时报e公司讯，下周将有46股解禁，按照最新收盘价计算，合计解禁市值790.44亿元。解禁市值超百亿的有3股，分别是<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600985" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600985" target="_blank">淮北矿业</a>、京东方A、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600733" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600733" target="_blank">北汽蓝谷</a>，解禁规模最大的是<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600985" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600985" target="_blank">淮北矿业</a>，下周将有15.22亿股上市流通，主要是定向增发机构配售股份，解禁市值达201.86亿元。从解禁股数量占总股本的比例来看，3股解禁市值超60%。<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603956" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="603956" target="_blank">威派格</a>的解禁比例居首，达到79.37%，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=NEEQ832735" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="832735" target="_blank">德源药业</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600985" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600985" target="_blank">淮北矿业</a>的解禁比例均超60%。(数据宝)</p>
