<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，今日可交易A股中，上涨个股有475只，下跌个股有3969只，平盘个股34只。不含当日上市新股，共有29只个股涨停，87只个股跌停。以所属行业来看，涨停个股主要集中在电气设备、公用事业、房地产等行业。</p><p>涨停股中，从收盘涨停板封单量来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS61868" code="61868">中国能建</a>最受追捧，收盘涨停板封单有3.15亿股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002305" code="002305">南国置业</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600200" code="600200">江苏吴中</a>等，涨停板封单分别有1993.23万股、1840.4万股。以封单金额计算，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS61868" code="61868">中国能建</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600200" code="600200">江苏吴中</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600733" code="600733">北汽蓝谷</a>等涨停板封单资金较多，分别有9.56亿元、1.47亿元、1.43亿元。连续涨停天数看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000993" code="000993">闽东电力</a>已连收4个涨停板，连续涨停板数量最多。</p>
