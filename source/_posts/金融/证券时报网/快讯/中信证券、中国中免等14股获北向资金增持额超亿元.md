<p>证券时报网讯，据wind统计显示，2月14日共有710只个股获北向资金持股量环比上一个交易日增加。以增持的股份数量和当日收盘价为基准进行测算，加仓股中，增持市值在1亿元以上的有14只，增持市值最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600030" code="600030">中信证券</a>，最新持股量为6.36亿股，环比增加14.82%，增持市值为20.36亿元；增持市值较多还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601888" code="601888">中国中免</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601012" code="601012">隆基股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300750" code="300750">宁德时代</a>等股。</p><p>从机构动向看，北向资金增持市值居前个股，近一个月获机构调研的有4只，机构调研次数较为频繁的有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300124" code="300124">汇川技术</a>等，期间分别获机构调研2次；机构调研家数看，机构较为扎堆的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300073" code="300073">当升科技</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300124" code="300124">汇川技术</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002709" code="002709">天赐材料</a>。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202202/W020220215351309391124.png" title="北向资金增持市值排名.png" alt="北向资金增持市值排名.png" oldsrc="W020220215351309391124.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
