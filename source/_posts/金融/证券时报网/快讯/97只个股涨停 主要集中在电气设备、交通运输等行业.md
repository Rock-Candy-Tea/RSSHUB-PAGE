<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，沪深两市可交易A股中，上涨个股有1351只，下跌个股有3084只，平盘个股134只。不含当日上市新股，共有97只个股涨停，8只个股跌停。以所属行业来看，涨停个股主要集中在电气设备、交通运输、传媒、建筑装饰、房地产、汽车等行业。</p><p>涨停股中，从收盘涨停板封单量来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002265" code="002265">西仪股份</a>最受追捧，收盘涨停板封单有8297.02万股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600396" code="600396">金山股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600863" code="600863">内蒙华电</a>等，涨停板封单分别有3044.14万股、3014.44万股。以封单金额计算，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002265" code="002265">西仪股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603071" code="603071">物产环能</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000049" code="000049">德赛电池</a>等涨停板封单资金较多，分别有17.92亿元、2.96亿元、2.31亿元。连续涨停天数看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ001317" code="001317">三羊马</a>已连收14个涨停板，连续涨停板数量最多。</p>
