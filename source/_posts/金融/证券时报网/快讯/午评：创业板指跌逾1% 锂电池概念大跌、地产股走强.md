<p>证券时报e公司讯，12月7日，两市高开低开，个股多数下跌。截至午间收盘，沪指涨0.12%，深证成指跌0.48%，创业板指跌1.43%。板块方面，低位的家居用品板块反弹，<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002572" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002572" target="_blank">索菲亚</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603801" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="603801" target="_blank">志邦家居</a>均涨停；受降准消息影响，地产股继续走强，<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000736" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000736" target="_blank">中交地产</a>等多只个股涨停；电力股临近午间收盘拉升，<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000791" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000791" target="_blank">甘肃电投</a>涨停；其他板块中，建材、家居、数字货币、培育钻石等涨幅居前。半导体、锂电池、有色、汽车电子等大跌。锂电池概念股中，<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002466" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002466" target="_blank">天齐锂业</a>跌逾7%，<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300750" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300750" target="_blank">宁德时代</a>跌逾5%。两市逾40只个股涨幅达到或超过10%。</p>
