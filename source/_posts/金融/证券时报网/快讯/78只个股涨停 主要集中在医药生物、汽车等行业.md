<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，沪深两市可交易A股中，上涨个股有1230只，下跌个股有3201只，平盘个股104只。不含当日上市新股，共有78只个股涨停，10只个股跌停。以所属行业来看，涨停个股主要集中在医药生物、汽车、化工、机械设备、农林牧渔等行业。</p><p>涨停股中，从收盘涨停板封单量来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600982" code="600982">宁波能源</a>最受追捧，收盘涨停板封单有2479.11万股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002263" code="002263">大东南</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002510" code="002510">天汽模</a>，涨停板封单分别有2125.43万股、1606.81万股。以封单金额计算，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603213" code="603213">镇洋发展</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603703" code="603703">盛洋科技</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ30014" code="30014">大富科技</a>等涨停板封单资金较多，分别有1.76亿元、1.45亿元、1.28亿元。连续涨停天数看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002047" code="002047">宝鹰股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603213" code="603213">镇洋发展</a>均已连收4个涨停板，连续涨停板数量最多。</p>
