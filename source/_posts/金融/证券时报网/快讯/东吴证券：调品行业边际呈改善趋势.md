<p>证券时报网讯，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601555" code="601555">东吴证券</a>指出，从需求恢复、库存去化及产品新鲜度上看，调品行业边际呈改善趋势，其中基础调味品优于复合料，基础调味品头部品牌优势显著，复合料头部扩张力度大，新品牌涌出背景下，竞争激烈。当前调味品行业虽因需求疲软、库存较高、社区团购冲击等因素短期处于调整期，但中长期看，调味品为好赛道，仍具备较大空间，头部企业经营能力强，市占率有望进一步提升，中长期可左侧布局。推荐<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603288" code="603288">海天味业</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600872" code="600872">中炬高新</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603027" code="603027">千禾味业</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600305" code="600305">恒顺醋业</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603317" code="603317">天味食品</a>、颐海国际。</p>
