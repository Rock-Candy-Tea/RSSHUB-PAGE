<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，近5个交易日（1月27日至2月9日）沪深两市约84家上市公司被机构调研。机构调研榜单中，共有45家公司获20家以上机构扎堆调研。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002050" code="002050">三花智控</a>最受关注，参与调研的机构达到328家；<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688058" code="688058">宝兰德</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688556" code="688556">高测股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688180" code="688180">君实生物</a>等分别被192家、190家、185家机构调研。机构调研次数来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002594" code="002594">比亚迪</a>机构调研最为密集，共获机构3次调研；<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600916" code="600916">中国黄金</a>被机构调研2次。</p><p>20家以上机构扎堆调研股中，近5日资金净流入的有8只，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002179" code="002179">中航光电</a>近5日净流入资金1.67亿元，主力资金净流入最多；净流入资金较多的还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002567" code="002567">唐人神</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603738" code="603738">泰晶科技</a>等。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202202/W020220210332482055360.png" title="近5日机构调研股一览.png" alt="近5日机构调研股一览.png" oldsrc="W020220210332482055360.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
