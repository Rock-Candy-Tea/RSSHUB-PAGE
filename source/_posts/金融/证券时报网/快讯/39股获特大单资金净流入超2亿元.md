<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，沪深两市全天特大单净流入64.34亿元。其中，39股特大单净流入超2亿元，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002594" code="002594">比亚迪</a>特大单净流入35.27亿元，特大单净流入资金居首；<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600031" code="600031">三一重工</a>特大单净流入资金15.93亿元，位列第二；净流入资金居前的还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600519" code="600519">贵州茅台</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300059" code="300059">东方财富</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000858" code="000858">五粮液</a>等。</p><p>所属行业来看，特大单资金净流入居前个股中，电子、机械设备、非银金融行业最为集中。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202108/W020210802673221011117.png" title="特大单净流入资金排名.png" alt="特大单净流入资金排名.png" oldsrc="W020210802673221011117.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
