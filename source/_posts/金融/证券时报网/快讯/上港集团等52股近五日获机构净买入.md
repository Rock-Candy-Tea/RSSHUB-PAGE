<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计，近五个交易日的龙虎榜当中，95只个股出现了机构的身影，其中52只股票呈现机构净买入，43只股票呈现机构净卖出。近五日机构净买入前三名分别是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600011" code="600011">华能国际</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300364" code="300364">中文在线</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600018" code="600018">上港集团</a>。</p><p>与此同时，近五个交易日登陆沪深两市的龙虎榜个股中，营业部净买入较大的个股有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601689" code="601689">拓普集团</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000860" code="000860">顺鑫农业</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS605111" code="605111">新洁能</a>。具体来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601689" code="601689">拓普集团</a>是资金最青睐个股，营业部净买入金额达5.9亿元，高居榜首。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202112/W020211210356733253790.png" title="近五日龙虎榜机构净买入的个股.png" alt="近五日龙虎榜机构净买入的个股.png" width="550" oldsrc="W020211210356733253790.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
