<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，沪深两市可交易A股中，上涨个股有1545只，下跌个股有2906只，平盘个股97只。不含当日上市新股，共有72只个股涨停，7只个股跌停。以所属行业来看，涨停个股主要集中在化工、计算机、机械设备、商业贸易、公用事业等行业。</p><p>涨停股中，从收盘涨停板封单量来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600318" code="600318">新力金融</a>最受追捧，收盘涨停板封单有3394.63万股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603219" code="603219">富佳股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000795" code="000795">英洛华</a>等，涨停板封单分别有2823.43万股、1499.61万股。以封单金额计算，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603219" code="603219">富佳股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600318" code="600318">新力金融</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600259" code="600259">广晟有色</a>等涨停板封单资金较多，分别有5.69亿元、3.76亿元、1.63亿元。连续涨停天数看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000525" code="000525">ST红太阳</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603219" code="603219">富佳股份</a>均已连收5个涨停板，连续涨停板数量最多。</p>
