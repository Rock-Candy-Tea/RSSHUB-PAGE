<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，近10个交易日（1月5日~1月18日）共有19家上市公司股份获高管增持。从增持金额看，近10日增持金额最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300015" code="300015">爱尔眼科</a>，累计增持107.91万股，增持金额合计3974.92万元；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300607" code="300607">拓斯达</a>，增持量为132.90万股，增持金额2186.88万元。</p><p>增持次数方面，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300015" code="300015">爱尔眼科</a>增持最频繁，近10日共有13条增持记录，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603950" code="603950">长源东谷</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601601" code="601601">中国太保</a>等紧随其后，近10日分别有9条、5条增持记录。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600048" code="600048">保利发展</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300607" code="300607">拓斯达</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002847" code="002847">盐津铺子</a>等期间也有两条以上的增持记录。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202201/W020220119368132531071.png" title="近10日高管增持股一览.png" alt="近10日高管增持股一览.png" oldsrc="W020220119368132531071.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
