<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，12月14日有23只个股获机构买入型评级。其中，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002032" code="002032">苏泊尔</a>关注度最高，获3次机构买入型评级记录；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601888" code="601888">中国中免</a>，获2次机构买入型评级记录。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002128" code="002128">电投能源</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603816" code="603816">顾家家居</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002074" code="002074">国轩高科</a>等股均获1次机构买入型评级记录。从机构评级变动看，机构买入型评级记录中，有5条评级记录为机构首次关注，涉及<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS605166" code="605166">聚合顺</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS605337" code="605337">李子园</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600018" code="600018">上港集团</a>等股。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202112/W020211215462584106915.png" title="获机构买入评级的个股.png" alt="获机构买入评级的个股.png" oldsrc="W020211215462584106915.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
