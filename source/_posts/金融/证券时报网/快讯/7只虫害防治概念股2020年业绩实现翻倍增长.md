<p>证券时报网讯，经证券时报·数据宝梳理，目前共有21只虫害防治概念股发布2020年业绩年报、快报或预告。从盈利水平来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600803" code="600803">新奥股份</a>2020年净利润为21.07亿元，净利润额居首；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600486" code="600486">扬农化工</a>。</p><p>从业绩增长幅度来看，7股在实现盈利的同时，业绩实现翻倍增长，分别是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603810" code="603810">丰山集团</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002915" code="002915">中欣氟材</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600731" code="600731">湖南海利</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300261" code="300261">雅本化学</a>等股。其中<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603810" code="603810">丰山集团</a>的业绩增长幅度最大。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202104/W020210402287183866609.png" title="部分虫害防范概念股.png" alt="部分虫害防范概念股.png" oldsrc="W020210402287183866609.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
