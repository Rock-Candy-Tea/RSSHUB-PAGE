<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，今日可交易A股中，上涨个股有1294只，下跌个股有3107只，平盘个股90只。不含当日上市新股，共有45只个股涨停，39只个股跌停。以所属行业来看，涨停个股主要集中在电子、有色金属、采掘、汽车、电气设备等行业。</p><p>涨停股中，从收盘涨停板封单量来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600071" code="600071">凤凰光学</a>最受追捧，收盘涨停板封单有5086.86万股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600856" code="600856">ST中天</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000498" code="000498">山东路桥</a>，涨停板封单分别有540.9万股、474.59万股。以封单金额计算，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600071" code="600071">凤凰光学</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS605111" code="605111">新洁能</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603290" code="603290">斯达半导</a>等涨停板封单资金较多，分别有15.26亿元、1.98亿元、1.05亿元。连续涨停天数看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600071" code="600071">凤凰光学</a>已连收7个涨停板，连续涨停板数量最多。</p>
