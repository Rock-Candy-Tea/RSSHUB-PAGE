<p>证券时报网讯，据wind统计显示，截止10月19日，在已经发布三季报的个股中，养老金共现身7只个股前十大流通股东榜。其中，新进4只，增持1只。持股比例看，养老金账户持股比例最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300761" code="300761">立华股份</a>，持股量为248.83万股，占流通股比例2.08%。养老金持股比例居前的还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603995" code="603995">甬金股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ02048" code="02048">宁波华翔</a>等。</p><p>持股数量方面，三季度末养老金账户对<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002299" code="002299">圣农发展</a>的持股量最多，持股量为835.27万股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600258" code="600258">首旅酒店</a>，持股量为800.35万股。从期末持股市值看，养老金账户期末持股市值在亿元以上的有4只股，分别是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002299" code="002299">圣农发展</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600258" code="600258">首旅酒店</a>等。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202110/W020211020373361860205.png" title="养老金三季度持有的个股.png" alt="养老金三季度持有的个股.png" oldsrc="W020211020373361860205.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
