<p>证券时报网讯，万和证券指出，激光雷达作为L3级别智能驾驶的关键传感器，是汽车智能化弹性较大的赛道，根据当前车厂车型规划和激光雷达厂商进展情况，激光雷达行业具备了量产基础并有望在2022-2023年迎来向上拐点，行业现阶段的关注在于禾赛科技、速腾聚创、大疆Livox、华为、Innovusion等半固态厂商的订单定点及实际交付情况。投资方面，产业快速成长期，上游确定性高，建议关注：1、已经涉足激光发射器、光电探测器领域并有所突破的公司，相关标的有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688167" code="688167">炬光科技</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688048" code="688048">长光华芯</a>；2、光学部件方面，国内供应链价值量占比不低且技术水平成熟，有望借激光雷达之东风率先收益，相关标的有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688195" code="688195">腾景科技</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603297" code="603297">永新光学</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002273" code="002273">水晶光电</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002222" code="002222">福晶科技</a>。</p>
