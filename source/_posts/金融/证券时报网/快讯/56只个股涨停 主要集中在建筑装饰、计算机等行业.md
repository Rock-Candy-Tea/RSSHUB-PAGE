<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，沪深两市可交易A股中，上涨个股有1340只，下跌个股有3180只，平盘个股88只。不含当日上市新股，共有56只个股涨停，64只个股跌停。以所属行业来看，涨停个股主要集中在建筑装饰、计算机、化工、食品饮料、传媒等行业。</p><p>涨停股中，从收盘涨停板封单量来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603176" code="603176">汇通集团</a>最受追捧，收盘涨停板封单有7402.75万股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ001227" code="001227">兰州银行</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000836" code="000836">富通信息</a>等，涨停板封单分别有5469.82万股、4138.06万股。以封单金额计算，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603176" code="603176">汇通集团</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002530" code="002530">金财互联</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ001227" code="001227">兰州银行</a>等涨停板封单资金较多，分别有5.2亿元、3.48亿元、3.09亿元。连续涨停天数看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603176" code="603176">汇通集团</a>已连收12个涨停板，连续涨停板数量最多。</p>
