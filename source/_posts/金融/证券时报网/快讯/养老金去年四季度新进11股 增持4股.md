<p>证券时报网讯，据wind统计显示，截止4月6日，2021年四季度末养老金账户共现身31只个股前十大流通股东榜。其中，新进11只，增持4股。去年年末养老金账户对<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600176" code="600176">中国巨石</a>的持股量最多，合计持股量为6282.08万股。其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600282" code="600282">南钢股份</a>，持股量为6024.95万股。从期末持股市值看，养老金账户期末持股市值在亿元以上的有21只股，分别是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600176" code="600176">中国巨石</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002850" code="002850">科达利</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002013" code="002013">中航机电</a>等。</p><p>持股比例方面，养老金账户持股比例最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000910" code="000910">大亚圣象</a>，合计持股量为2085.08万股，占流通股比例3.81%；养老金持股比例居前的还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002850" code="002850">科达利</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300772" code="300772">运达股份</a>等。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202204/W020220406381203652623.png" title="四季度养老金持股明细.png" alt="四季度养老金持股明细.png" oldsrc="W020220406381203652623.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
