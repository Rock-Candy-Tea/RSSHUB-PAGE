<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，今日可交易A股中，上涨个股有2914只，下跌个股有1385只，平盘个股155只。不含当日上市新股，共有122只个股涨停，5只个股跌停。以所属行业来看，涨停个股主要集中在化工、采掘、公用事业、有色金属、机械设备、电气设备等行业。</p><p>涨停股中，从收盘涨停板封单量来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600683" code="600683">京投发展</a>最受追捧，收盘涨停板封单有6190.37万股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000537" code="000537">广宇发展</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002610" code="002610">爱康科技</a>等，涨停板封单分别有4902.01万股、2961.36万股。以封单金额计算，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600683" code="600683">京投发展</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000537" code="000537">广宇发展</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600110" code="600110">诺德股份</a>等涨停板封单资金较多，分别有3.31亿元、2.86亿元、2.72亿元。</p>
