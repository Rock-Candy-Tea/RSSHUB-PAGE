<p>证券时报网讯，据wind统计显示，11月2日共有634只股获北向资金持股量环比上一个交易日增加。以增持的股份数量和当日收盘价为基准进行测算，加仓股中，增持市值在1亿元以上的有39只，增持市值最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601012" code="601012">隆基股份</a>，最新持股量为6.93亿股，环比增加1.90%，增持市值为12.89亿元；增持市值较多还有宁德时代、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601888" code="601888">中国中免</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600460" code="600460">士兰微</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601318" code="601318">中国平安</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603288" code="603288">海天味业</a>等股。</p><p>从机构动向看，北向资金增持市值居前个股，近一个月获机构调研的有8只；机构调研家数看，机构较为扎堆的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002241" code="002241">歌尔股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002460" code="002460">赣锋锂业</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002036" code="002036">联创电子</a>。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202111/W020211103360197207947.png" title="北向资金增持市值排名.png" alt="北向资金增持市值排名.png" oldsrc="W020211103360197207947.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
