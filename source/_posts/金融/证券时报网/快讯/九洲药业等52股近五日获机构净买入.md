<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计，近五个交易日的龙虎榜当中，97只个股出现了机构的身影，其中52只股票呈现机构净买入。近五日机构净买入前三名分别是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS605499" code="605499">东鹏饮料</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603893" code="603893">瑞芯微</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603456" code="603456">九洲药业</a>。</p><p>与此同时，近五个交易日登陆沪深两市的龙虎榜个股中，营业部净买入较大的个股有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600905" code="600905">三峡能源</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600460" code="600460">士兰微</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603589" code="603589">口子窖</a>。具体来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600905" code="600905">三峡能源</a>是资金最青睐个股，营业部净买入金额达8.89亿元，高居榜首。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202106/W020210622355919510100.png" title="近五日龙虎榜机构净买入的个股.png" alt="近五日龙虎榜机构净买入的个股.png" width="550" oldsrc="W020210622355919510100.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
