<p>证券时报e公司讯，4月8日下午，上海市药品监管局党组书记、局长闻大翔带队至<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688317" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="688317" target="_blank">之江生物</a>调研高通量全自动核酸检测平台—“青耕一号”的研发生产等情况。<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688317" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="688317" target="_blank">之江生物</a>董事长邵俊斌向闻大翔一行介绍了<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688317" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="688317" target="_blank">之江生物</a>最新获批三类医疗器械注册证的高通量全自动核酸检测平台“青耕一号”。同时闻大翔询问了<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688317" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="688317" target="_blank">之江生物</a>的新冠核酸检测试剂和抗原检测产品的研发注册及生产供应情况，了解了<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688317" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="688317" target="_blank">之江生物</a>内部防疫安全责任落实等情况，并针对相关诉求进行解答。</p>
