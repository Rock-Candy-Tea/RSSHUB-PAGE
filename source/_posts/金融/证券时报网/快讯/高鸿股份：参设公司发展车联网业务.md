<p>证券时报e公司讯，<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000851" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000851" target="_blank">高鸿股份</a>(000851)11月3日晚间公告，公司拟以现金出资3.5亿元设立新公司，发展车联网业务。据悉，新公司注册资本不超过7.2亿元，<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000851" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000851" target="_blank">高鸿股份</a>持股比例48.61%，除<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000851" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000851" target="_blank">高鸿股份</a>外，<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000851" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000851" target="_blank">高鸿股份</a>控股股东电信科研院以及其关联方大唐控股以车联网相关资产出资，非关联方投资人以现金出资。</p>
