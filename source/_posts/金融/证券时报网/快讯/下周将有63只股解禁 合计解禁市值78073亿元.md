<p>证券时报e公司讯，下周将有63只股解禁，按照最新收盘价计算，合计解禁市值780.73亿元。解禁市值规模最大的是<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300919" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300919" target="_blank">中伟股份</a>，下周将有0.96亿股上市流通，主要是首发原股东限售股份和首发战略配售股份，解禁市值达153.24亿元。此外，<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002032" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002032" target="_blank">苏泊尔</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000785" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000785" target="_blank">居然之家</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603587" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="603587" target="_blank">地素时尚</a>的解禁市值居前。</p>
