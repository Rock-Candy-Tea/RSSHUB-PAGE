<p>证券时报网讯，据wind统计显示，2月10日共有696只个股获北向资金持股量环比上一个交易日增加。以增持的股份数量和当日收盘价为基准进行测算，加仓股中，增持市值在1亿元以上的有39只，增持市值最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601318" code="601318">中国平安</a>，最新持股量为7.56亿股，环比增加2.36%，增持市值为9.44亿元；增持市值较多还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601899" code="601899">紫金矿业</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601688" code="601688">华泰证券</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002475" code="002475">立讯精密</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000001" code="000001">平安银行</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600585" code="600585">海螺水泥</a>等股。</p><p>业绩来看，北向资金大手笔增持股中，有5股已经公布了2021年业绩快报，净利润增幅较高的有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600030" code="600030">中信证券</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000001" code="000001">平安银行</a>，增幅为54.20%、25.60%。公布2021年业绩预告的共有19只，业绩预告类型来看，预增有17只。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202202/W020220211348522836569.png" title="北向资金增持市值排名.png" alt="北向资金增持市值排名.png" oldsrc="W020220211348522836569.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
