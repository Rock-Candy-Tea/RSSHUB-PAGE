<p>证券时报e公司讯，记者从业内获悉，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688179" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="688179" target="_blank">阿拉丁</a>控股集团旗下<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688179" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="688179" target="_blank">阿拉丁</a>能源集团有限公司日前完成对中国南方电网旗下<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ003035" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="003035" target="_blank">南网能源</a>的战略投资。<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ003035" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="003035" target="_blank">南网能源</a>是全国领先的工业节能、建筑节能、城市照明节能投资运营服务商；<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688179" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="688179" target="_blank">阿拉丁</a>在环保能源领域深耕多年，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688179" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="688179" target="_blank">阿拉丁</a>能源集团投资了光伏、风电、储能以及智慧能源等领域多家新能源企业。通过投资<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ003035" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="003035" target="_blank">南网能源</a>，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688179" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="688179" target="_blank">阿拉丁</a>加强了在综合节能领域的布局，进一步完善能源产业生态链，利用资本和市场机制优势，助力实现国家“双碳”目标。</p>
