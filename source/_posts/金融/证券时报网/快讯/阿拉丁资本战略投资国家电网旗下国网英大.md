<p>证券时报e公司讯，记者从<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688179" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="688179" target="_blank">阿拉丁</a>控股集团获悉，旗下<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688179" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="688179" target="_blank">阿拉丁</a>资本管理有限公司日前完成对国家电网有限公司旗下<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600517" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600517" target="_blank">国网英大</a>的战略投资。<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600517" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600517" target="_blank">国网英大</a>覆盖信托、证券、碳资产、电气等多类业务。<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688179" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="688179" target="_blank">阿拉丁</a>控股集团是一家致力于产业投资+产业运营的综合性平台，坚持金融投资和科技创新两个战略方向，聚焦环保能源与消费服务两条产业赛道。未来，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688179" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="688179" target="_blank">阿拉丁</a>资本将把握金融与产业融合发展的新趋势，有望与<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600517" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600517" target="_blank">国网英大</a>携手推动绿色金融与碳资产管理业务发展。</p>
