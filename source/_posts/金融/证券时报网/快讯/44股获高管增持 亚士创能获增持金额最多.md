<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，近10个交易日（7月14日~7月27日）共有44家上市公司股份获高管增持。从增持金额看，近10日增持金额最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603378" code="603378">亚士创能</a>，累计增持934.35万股，增持金额合计4.96亿元；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300292" code="300292">吴通控股</a>，增持量为1000万股，增持金额3040万元。</p><p>增持次数方面，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600650" code="600650">锦江在线</a>增持最频繁，近10日共有12条增持记录，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600099" code="600099">林海股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002958" code="002958">青农商行</a>等紧随其后，近10日分别有11条、8条增持记录。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000785" code="000785">居然之家</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300780" code="300780">德恩精工</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600000" code="600000">浦发银行</a>等期间也有两条以上的增持记录。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202107/W020210728501970822774.png" title="近10日高管增持股一览.png" alt="近10日高管增持股一览.png" oldsrc="W020210728501970822774.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
