<p>证券时报e公司讯，近期，工业和信息化部发布消息称，研究建立产业链供应链诉求应急协调机制，设立工业和信息化领域保运转重点企业“白名单”，集中资源优先保障集成电路、汽车制造、装备制造、生物医药等重点行业666家重点企业复工复产。记者获悉，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688012" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="688012" target="_blank">中微公司</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688082" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="688082" target="_blank">盛美上海</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688019" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="688019" target="_blank">安集科技</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688234" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="688234" target="_blank">天岳先进</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688126" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="688126" target="_blank">沪硅产业</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688180" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="688180" target="_blank">君实生物</a>和<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688202" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="688202" target="_blank">美迪西</a>等公司均在“白名单”之列。这些企业中多数在封控期便已在核心环节进行闭环运营管理，以保障重点领域持续运营。目前上述公司针对复工复产制定了方案。(第一财经)</p>
