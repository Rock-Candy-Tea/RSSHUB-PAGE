<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计，A股中，布局光伏建筑一体化（BIPV）相关产业的个股共55家。目前已有33家BIPV板块上市公司公布三季报，其中16家净利润同比增长，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300490" code="300490">华自科技</a>增幅最高，为354.7%；<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300040" code="300040">九洲集团</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000928" code="000928">中钢国际</a>紧随其后。</p><p>部分BIPV概念股相对估值较低，主要为BIPV后端的建筑行业个股和钢结构的钢铁板块个股，包括<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601886" code="601886">江河集团</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600496" code="600496">精工钢构</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000928" code="000928">中钢国际</a>等8股最新滚动市盈率不足15倍。13只BIPV概念股获5家以上机构评级，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601012" code="601012">隆基股份</a>最受机构关注，共有44家机构评级，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601615" code="601615">明阳智能</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688599" code="688599">天合光能</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601877" code="601877">正泰电器</a>分别获得18、17、15家机构评级。</p>
