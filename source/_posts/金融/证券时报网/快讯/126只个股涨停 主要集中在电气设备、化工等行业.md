<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，今日可交易A股中，上涨个股有3311只，下跌个股有1053只，平盘个股161只。不含当日上市新股，共有126只个股涨停，2只个股跌停。以所属行业来看，涨停个股主要集中在电气设备、化工、机械设备、汽车、电子、食品饮料等行业。</p><p>涨停股中，从收盘涨停板封单量来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002796" code="002796">世嘉科技</a>最受追捧，收盘涨停板封单有5497.88万股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000700" code="000700">模塑科技</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600522" code="600522">中天科技</a>，涨停板封单分别有2827.21万股、2333.19万股。以封单金额计算，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002796" code="002796">世嘉科技</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600522" code="600522">中天科技</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002202" code="002202">金风科技</a>等涨停板封单资金较多，分别有4.53亿元、3.43亿元、3.14亿元。连续涨停天数看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600856" code="600856">ST中天</a>已连收8个涨停板，连续涨停板数量最多。</p>
