<p>证券时报网讯，据<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300033" code="300033">同花顺</a>iFinD统计，近5个交易日（10月25日-10月29日）共有80只个股获北向资金连续5天增持。5天累计净买入金额在1亿元以上的有37只，其中16只累计净买入额超3亿元。获北向资金连续增持前5名的个股分别是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002709" code="002709">天赐材料</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600887" code="600887">伊利股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300750" code="300750">宁德时代</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600900" code="600900">长江电力</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600436" code="600436">片仔癀</a>。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002709" code="002709">天赐材料</a>获北向资金5天累计净买入约23.71亿元，位居连续增持榜首。</p><p>将时间拉长看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300750" code="300750">宁德时代</a>已连续12个交易日获北向资金净买入，合计净买入63.71亿元。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202111/W020211101411325339883.png" title="部分获北向资金连续5天增持的个股.png" alt="部分获北向资金连续5天增持的个股.png" width="550" oldsrc="W020211101411325339883.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
