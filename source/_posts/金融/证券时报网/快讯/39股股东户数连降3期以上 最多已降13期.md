<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，318股公布了截至8月31日最新股东户数，相比之前各期，有39股股东户数连续减少3期以上，股东户数最多已连降13期。股东户数连降期数较多的有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300983" code="300983">尤安设计</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ003035" code="003035">南网能源</a>等股。</p><p>从机构动向看，股东户数连降股中，近一个月获机构调研的有3只；机构调研家数看，机构较为扎堆的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002241" code="002241">歌尔股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ301013" code="301013">利和兴</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002152" code="002152">广电运通</a>。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202109/W020210902671596258261.png" title="股东户数连续下降股排行榜.png" alt="股东户数连续下降股排行榜.png" oldsrc="W020210902671596258261.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
