<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，71只个股今日获机构买入型评级。其中，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600919" code="600919">江苏银行</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600567" code="600567">山鹰国际</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603369" code="603369">今世缘</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603605" code="603605">珀莱雅</a>关注度最高，均有4次机构买入型评级记录。从机构评级变动看，今日机构买入型评级记录中，有20条评级记录为机构首次关注，涉及<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600048" code="600048">保利地产</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300824" code="300824">北鼎股份</a>等20只个股。</p><p>业绩方面，机构评级买入个股中，有69股已经公布了上半年业绩，净利润同比增幅最大的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000301" code="000301">东方盛虹</a>，公司上半年业绩实现净利润11.47亿元，同比增长1720.3%。净利润同比增幅较高的还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000807" code="000807">云铝股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600884" code="600884">杉杉股份</a>等。</p><p style="text-align:center"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202108/W020210826635915326482.png" title="机构今日买入型评级个股.png" alt="机构今日买入型评级个股.png" oldsrc="W020210826635915326482.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>责编：孙孝熙</p>
