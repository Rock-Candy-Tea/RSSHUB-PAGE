<p>证券时报e公司讯，<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600185" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600185" target="_blank">格力地产</a>(600185)8月5日晚间公告，全资子公司珠海保联与<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688289" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="688289" target="_blank">圣湘生物</a>此前签署股份转让协议，珠海保联拟将<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002022" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002022" target="_blank">科华生物</a>9586.30万股股份转让给<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688289" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="688289" target="_blank">圣湘生物</a>。因至今未完成股权转让过户事宜，无法达到交易各方的预期，珠海保联和<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688289" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="688289" target="_blank">圣湘生物</a>经友好协商，决定终止上述股份转让协议。公司将支持<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002022" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002022" target="_blank">科华生物</a>与<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688289" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="688289" target="_blank">圣湘生物</a>的业务合作，共同推进体外诊断“中国方案”，以推动中国体外诊断产业向更高层次、更高水平发展。</p>
