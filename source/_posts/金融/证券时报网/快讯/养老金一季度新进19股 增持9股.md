<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，一季度末养老金账户共现身38只个股前十大流通股东榜。其中，新进19只，增持9只。一季度末养老金账户对<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603128" code="603128">华贸物流</a>的持股量最多。从期末持股市值看，养老金账户期末持股市值在亿元以上的有21只股，分别是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002008" code="002008">大族激光</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600486" code="600486">扬农化工</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603128" code="603128">华贸物流</a>等。</p><p>持股比例方面，养老金账户持股比例最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688619" code="688619">罗普特</a>，持股量为207.04万股，占流通股比例4.83%。养老金持股比例居前的还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002732" code="002732">燕塘乳业</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300196" code="300196">长海股份</a>等。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202104/W020210427401563928660.png" title="养老金一季度持股明细.png" alt="养老金一季度持股明细.png" oldsrc="W020210427401563928660.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
