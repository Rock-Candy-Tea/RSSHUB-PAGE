<p>证券时报网讯，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002673" code="002673">西部证券</a>指出，上市猪企22年7月生猪出栏均价大幅上行，生猪养殖业务进入盈利区间。上市猪企7月出栏均价均已达到21元/公斤以上，目前出栏价下，上市公司生猪养殖业务均能获利，22H2猪企经营环境将较22H1大幅改善。此外，22H2上市猪企完全成本仍有一定的下降空间，利好全年业绩。猪企出栏均重环比小幅下降，显示上市猪企出栏策略稳定，压栏现象不明显。投资建议：把握生猪养殖板块的交易性机会，关注成长性强且经营稳健的生猪养殖企业：<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300498" code="300498">温氏股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002385" code="002385">大北农</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002567" code="002567">唐人神</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600975" code="600975">新五丰</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603477" code="603477">巨星农牧</a>等。</p>
