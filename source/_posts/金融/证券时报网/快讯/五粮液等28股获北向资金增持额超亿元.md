<p>证券时报网讯，据wind统计显示，11月24日共有755只股获北向资金持股量环比上一个交易日增加。以增持的股份数量和当日收盘价为基准进行测算，加仓股中，增持市值在1亿元以上的有28只，增持市值最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000858" code="000858">五粮液</a>，最新持股量为2.25亿股，增持市值为12.71亿元。增持市值较多还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600519" code="600519">贵州茅台</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000568" code="000568">泸州老窖</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002304" code="002304">洋河股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600111" code="600111">北方稀土</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300223" code="300223">北京君正</a>等股。</p>
