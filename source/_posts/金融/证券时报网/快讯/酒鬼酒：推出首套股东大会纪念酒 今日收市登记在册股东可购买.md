<p>证券时报e公司讯，从<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000799" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000799" target="_blank">酒鬼酒</a>获悉，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000799" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000799" target="_blank">酒鬼酒</a>公司2021年度股东大会将于6月27日召开。<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000799" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000799" target="_blank">酒鬼酒</a>公司推出首套<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000799" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000799" target="_blank">酒鬼酒</a>股东大会纪念酒，自6月26日至7月2日，面向持股股东限量发售。<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000799" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000799" target="_blank">酒鬼酒</a>股东大会纪念酒由内参大师酒、内参生肖酒（牛年）、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000799" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000799" target="_blank">酒鬼酒</a>（1956）和<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000799" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000799" target="_blank">酒鬼酒</a>（万里走单骑）4款产品组成，原价值7299元/套，股东优惠价仅为3699元/套。纪念酒仅面向<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000799" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000799" target="_blank">酒鬼酒</a>公司股东发售，即2022年6月20日下午收市登记在册的<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000799" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000799" target="_blank">酒鬼酒</a>公司股东均可购买，机构和个人投资者每个账号限购一套。</p>
