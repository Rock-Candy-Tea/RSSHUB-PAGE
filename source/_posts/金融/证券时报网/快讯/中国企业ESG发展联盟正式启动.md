<p>证券时报e公司讯，在由证券时报主办的“中国企业ESG发展研讨会”上，“中国企业ESG发展联盟”正式启动。证券时报、<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600958" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600958" target="_blank">东方证券</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002797" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002797" target="_blank">第一创业</a>证券、<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000750" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000750" target="_blank">国海证券</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000776" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000776" target="_blank">广发证券</a>、华夏基金和嘉实基金成为“中国企业ESG发展联盟”的发起会员单位。</p>
