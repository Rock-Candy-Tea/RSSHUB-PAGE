<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，沪深两市全天特大单净流入271.81亿元。其中，7股获特大单净流入超10亿元，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601012" code="601012">隆基股份</a>特大单净流入28.36亿元，特大单净流入资金居首；<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002594" code="002594">比亚迪</a>特大单净流入资金22.82亿元，位列第二；净流入资金居前的还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600010" code="600010">包钢股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600438" code="600438">通威股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600703" code="600703">三安光电</a>等。</p><p>所属行业来看，特大单资金净流入居前个股中，电气设备、有色金属、电子行业最为集中。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202108/W020210804665061963683.png" title="特大单净流入资金排名.png" alt="特大单净流入资金排名.png" oldsrc="W020210804665061963683.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
