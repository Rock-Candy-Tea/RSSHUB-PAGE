<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，近5个交易日（2月8日至2月14日）沪深两市约99家上市公司被机构调研。机构调研榜单中，共有24家公司获20家以上机构扎堆调研。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300036" code="300036">超图软件</a>最受关注，参与调研的机构达到289家；<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000963" code="000963">华东医药</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002475" code="002475">立讯精密</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300760" code="300760">迈瑞医疗</a>等分别被243家、223家、202家机构调研。机构调研次数来看，有2家公司被机构调研3次，分别是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002988" code="002988">豪美新材</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ301117" code="301117">佳缘科技</a>等，机构调研最为密集。</p><p>20家以上机构扎堆调研股中，近5日资金净流入的有5只，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000932" code="000932">华菱钢铁</a>近5日净流入资金3.42亿元，主力资金净流入最多；净流入资金较多的还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002196" code="002196">方正电机</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002563" code="002563">森马服饰</a>等。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202202/W020220215338635421914.png" title="近5日机构调研股一览.png" alt="近5日机构调研股一览.png" oldsrc="W020220215338635421914.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
