<p>证券时报网讯，据wind统计显示，11月25日共有733只股获北向资金持股量环比上一个交易日增加。以增持的股份数量和当日收盘价为基准进行测算，加仓股中，增持市值在1亿元以上的有25只，增持市值最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600519" code="600519">贵州茅台</a>，最新持股量为8923.52万股，环比增加0.19%，增持市值为3.39亿元；增持市值较多还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002460" code="002460">赣锋锂业</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300450" code="300450">先导智能</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600516" code="600516">方大炭素</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600438" code="600438">通威股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600276" code="600276">恒瑞医药</a>等股。</p><p>从机构动向看，北向资金增持市值居前个股，近一个月获机构调研的有10只；机构调研家数看，机构较为扎堆的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002241" code="002241">歌尔股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002709" code="002709">天赐材料</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300037" code="300037">新宙邦</a>等股。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202111/W020211126392990866448.png" title="北向资金增持市值排名.png" alt="北向资金增持市值排名.png" oldsrc="W020211126392990866448.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
