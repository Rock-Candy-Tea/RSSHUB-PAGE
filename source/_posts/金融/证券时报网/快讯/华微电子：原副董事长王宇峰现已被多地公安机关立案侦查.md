<p>证券时报e公司讯，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600360" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600360" target="_blank">华微电子</a>(600360)3月24日晚间披露澄清声明：<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600360" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600360" target="_blank">华微电子</a>原副董事长、控股股东上海鹏盛原法定代表人王宇峰涉嫌多起刑事犯罪，经上海鹏盛、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600360" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600360" target="_blank">华微电子</a>及董事长夏增文实名举报，王宇峰现已被多地公安机关立案侦查。在公安机关侦查过程中，王宇峰采取多种手段破坏<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600360" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600360" target="_blank">华微电子</a>正常生产经营，企图打击报复举报人、并逃避刑事责任。目前，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600360" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600360" target="_blank">华微电子</a>生产经营一切正常，公司产品市场需求旺盛，发展势头良好。</p>
