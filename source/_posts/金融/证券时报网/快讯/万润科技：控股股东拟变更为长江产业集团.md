<p>证券时报e公司讯，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002654" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002654" target="_blank">万润科技</a>(002654)5月13日晚间公告，按照湖北省国企改革实施方案要求，公司控股股东宏泰集团拟将其持有的<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002654" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002654" target="_blank">万润科技</a>2.02亿股股票（占<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002654" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002654" target="_blank">万润科技</a>总股本的23.62%）无偿划转至长江产业集团。本次权益变动完成后，长江产业集团将持有<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002654" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002654" target="_blank">万润科技</a>23.62%的股份，为公司控股股东，实际控制人仍为湖北省国资委。</p>
