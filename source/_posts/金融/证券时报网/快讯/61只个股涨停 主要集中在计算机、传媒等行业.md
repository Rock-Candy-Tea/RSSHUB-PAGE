<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，沪深两市可交易A股中，上涨个股有1375只，下跌个股有3107只，平盘个股127只。不含当日上市新股，共有61只个股涨停，54只个股跌停。以所属行业来看，涨停个股主要集中在计算机、传媒、煤炭、商贸零售、房地产等行业。</p><p>涨停股中，从收盘涨停板封单量来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603176" code="603176">汇通集团</a>最受追捧，收盘涨停板封单有1.14亿股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002717" code="002717">岭南股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600546" code="600546">山煤国际</a>等，涨停板封单分别有3599.05万股、3225.84万股。以封单金额计算，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603176" code="603176">汇通集团</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600546" code="600546">山煤国际</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002717" code="002717">岭南股份</a>等涨停板封单资金较多，分别有10.69亿元、3.2亿元、1.63亿元。连续涨停天数看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603176" code="603176">汇通集团</a>已连收15个涨停板，连续涨停板数量最多。</p>
