<p>证券时报e公司讯，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603098" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="603098" target="_blank">森特股份</a>消息，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603098" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="603098" target="_blank">森特股份</a>近日与广东<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002311" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002311" target="_blank">海大集团</a>股份有限公司签署《战略合作框架协议》，双方将通过各自的资源互配，在<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000591" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000591" target="_blank">太阳能</a>发电领域进一步深化业务合作。<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002311" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002311" target="_blank">海大集团</a>提供可供光伏开发应用的场地或屋顶资源，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603098" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="603098" target="_blank">森特股份</a>提供建筑光伏、水面光伏、地面光伏等各应用场景下的项目踏勘、设计、建设及运维等一体化解决方案服务，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603098" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="603098" target="_blank">森特股份</a>或其关联合作方将利用<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002311" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002311" target="_blank">海大集团</a>提供的场地或屋顶资源进行光伏电站投资。</p>
