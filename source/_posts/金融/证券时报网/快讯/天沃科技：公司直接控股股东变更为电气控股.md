<p>证券时报e公司讯，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002564" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002564" target="_blank">天沃科技</a>(002564)6月20日晚间公告，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601727" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601727" target="_blank">上海电气</a>拟向电气控股协议转让其直接持有的<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002564" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002564" target="_blank">天沃科技</a>15.24%股份。本次权益变动完成后，电气控股在<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002564" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002564" target="_blank">天沃科技</a>中拥有权益的股份数量（包括直接持股及拥有表决权的股份数量）合计为2.64亿股，占上市公司总股本的比例为30.34%，电气控股将成为公司的直接控股股东。本次权益变动后，电气控股由<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002564" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002564" target="_blank">天沃科技</a>的间接控股股东变更为直接控股股东，上海市国资委仍为<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002564" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002564" target="_blank">天沃科技</a>实控人。</p>
