<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，3月25日，沪深两市可交易A股中，上涨个股有1745只，下跌个股有2750只，平盘个股172只。不含当日上市新股，共有86只个股涨停，25只个股跌停。以所属行业来看，涨停个股主要集中在纺织服装、医药生物、建筑装饰、房地产、商贸零售等行业。</p><p>涨停股中，从收盘涨停板封单量来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002102" code="002102">ST冠福</a>最受追捧，收盘涨停板封单有3080.18万股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603261" code="603261">立航科技</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002486" code="002486">嘉麟杰</a>等，涨停板封单分别有3054.26万股、2584.6万股。以封单金额计算，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603261" code="603261">立航科技</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002864" code="002864">盘龙药业</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603209" code="603209">兴通股份</a>等涨停板封单资金较多，分别有18.58亿元、4.05亿元、2.14亿元。连续涨停天数看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600734" code="600734">*ST实达</a>已连收22个涨停板，连续涨停板数量最多。</p>
