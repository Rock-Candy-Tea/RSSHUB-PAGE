<p style="text-align: left;">证券时报网讯，华晟新能源HJT电池产线开始试产，电池最高效率接近24.4%，进展超预期。HJT电池提效降本路径明确，产业化与设备国产化快速推进，已布局并取得显著进展的设备龙头企业有望享受产业化初期较高的订单弹性。</p><p style="text-align: left;"><a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601696" code="601696">中银证券</a>指出，HJT电池效率提升与成本下降路径明确，目前设备国产化与HJT产业化快速推进，已布局并取得显著进展的设备龙头企业有望享受产业化初期较高的订单弹性，领先布局的电池片企业则优先受益于技术迭代初期的超额收益。HJT电池设备方面推荐<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300751" code="300751">迈为股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300724" code="300724">捷佳伟创</a>，建议关注<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603396" code="603396">金辰股份</a>；HJT电池片制造方面推荐<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600438" code="600438">通威股份</a>；同时建议关注辅材相关企业<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002079" code="002079">苏州固锝</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300842" code="300842">帝科股份</a>等。</p>
