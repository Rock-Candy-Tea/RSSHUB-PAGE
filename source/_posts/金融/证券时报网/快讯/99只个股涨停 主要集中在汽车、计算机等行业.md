<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，沪深两市可交易A股中，上涨个股有2884只，下跌个股有1525只，平盘个股141只。不含当日上市新股，共有99只个股涨停，5只个股跌停。以所属行业来看，涨停个股主要集中在汽车、计算机、化工、电子、医药生物、机械设备等行业。</p><p>涨停股中，从收盘涨停板封单量来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600935" code="600935">华塑股份</a>最受追捧，收盘涨停板封单有4860.95万股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002374" code="002374">中锐股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300311" code="300311">任子行</a>等，涨停板封单分别有3822.2万股、1700.12万股。以封单金额计算，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600935" code="600935">华塑股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002803" code="002803">吉宏股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300311" code="300311">任子行</a>等涨停板封单资金较多，分别有3.33亿元、2.42亿元、1.5亿元。连续涨停天数看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603219" code="603219">富佳股份</a>已连收7个涨停板，连续涨停板数量最多。</p>
