<p>证券时报网讯，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000686" code="000686">东北证券</a>指出，重组预期再升温，稀土行业格局有望进一步优化。1）上周国资委再次表示将深入实施稀土专业化整合，整合预期再升温。2）短期来看，年末指标偏紧+下游节前补货支撑下，供需矛盾支撑稀土价格高位。3）长期看，稀土正处于供需重塑的新时代起点，估值体系重构。稀土未来有望复刻历史上锂钴新能源需求高增的逻辑，而其供给端甚至比锂钴还要更优异，伴随价格中枢上移，企业或持续兑现高盈利，把握拥有全球定价权的核心资产价格重估机遇。相关标的：<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600111" code="600111">北方稀土</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000831" code="000831">五矿稀土</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600010" code="600010">包钢股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600392" code="600392">盛和资源</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600549" code="600549">厦门钨业</a>等。</p>
