<p>证券时报e公司讯，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000722" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000722" target="_blank">湖南发展</a>（000722 ）9月4日晚公告，公司于9月4日收到公司控股股东<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000722" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000722" target="_blank">湖南发展</a>集团通知：接上级主管部门通知，将<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000722" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000722" target="_blank">湖南发展</a>集团持有的<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000722" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000722" target="_blank">湖南发展</a>44.99%股权（2.09亿股）无偿划转至湘投控股。本次股权无偿划转完成后，公司控股股东将由<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000722" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000722" target="_blank">湖南发展</a>集团变更为湘投控股，实际控制人仍为湖南省国资委。</p>
