<p>证券时报e公司讯，下周将有40只股面临解禁，按最新收盘价计算，合计解禁市值320.53亿元。解禁市值规模最大的是<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300999" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300999" target="_blank">金龙鱼</a>。下周将有1.47亿股上市流通，主要是首发原股东限售股份和首发战略配售股份，解禁市值达106.15亿元。<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000800" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000800" target="_blank">一汽解放</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000875" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000875" target="_blank">吉电股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600746" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600746" target="_blank">江苏索普</a>的解禁市值居前。22股的解禁压力较小，解禁市值均不足亿元。从解禁股数量占总股本的比例来看，<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300694" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300694" target="_blank">蠡湖股份</a>的解禁比例居首，达37.71%；<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002786" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002786" target="_blank">银宝山新</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688330" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="688330" target="_blank">宏力达</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000800" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000800" target="_blank">一汽解放</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000875" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000875" target="_blank">吉电股份</a>等股的解禁比例居前。(数据宝)</p>
