<p>证券时报e公司讯，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ003038" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="003038" target="_blank">鑫铂股份</a>在机构调研时表示，公司未来会通过客户结构的调整和降本增效，不断提高盈利水平。在光伏领域，公司客户除了晶科能源、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601012" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601012" target="_blank">隆基股份</a>和<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002459" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002459" target="_blank">晶澳科技</a>，还有安徽大恒、正信光电、江苏东鋆、晋能集团等。除了光伏行业外，主要应用领域为轨道交通和汽车轻量化，客户有<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603111" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="603111" target="_blank">康尼机电</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603680" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="603680" target="_blank">今创集团</a>、无锡宏宇等，其中<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603111" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="603111" target="_blank">康尼机电</a>和<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603680" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="603680" target="_blank">今创集团</a>均为A股上市公司，公司也将继续深耕轨道交通和汽车轻量化领域，不断拓展优质客户，提高盈利能力。</p>
