<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，沪深两市可交易A股中，上涨个股有1715只，下跌个股有2716只，平盘个股130只。不含当日上市新股，共有97只个股涨停，20只个股跌停。以所属行业来看，涨停个股主要集中在化工、房地产、汽车、轻工制造、传媒、建筑装饰等行业。</p><p>涨停股中，从收盘涨停板封单量来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002265" code="002265">西仪股份</a>最受追捧，收盘涨停板封单有3120.26万股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600277" code="600277">亿利洁能</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002640" code="002640">*ST跨境</a>等，涨停板封单分别有2959.47万股、2445.02万股。以封单金额计算，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002265" code="002265">西仪股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600277" code="600277">亿利洁能</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600771" code="600771">广誉远</a>等涨停板封单资金较多，分别有3.14亿元、1.7亿元、1.64亿元。连续涨停天数看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600734" code="600734">*ST实达</a>已连收8个涨停板，连续涨停板数量最多。</p>
