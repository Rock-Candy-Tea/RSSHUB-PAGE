<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，沪深两市可交易A股中，上涨个股有1951只，下跌个股有2536只，平盘个股180只。不含当日上市新股，共有77只个股涨停，20只个股跌停。以所属行业来看，涨停个股主要集中在医药生物、化工、机械设备、建筑材料、国防军工等行业。</p><p>涨停股中，从收盘涨停板封单量来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600927" code="600927">永安期货</a>最受追捧，收盘涨停板封单有5621.56万股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600986" code="600986">浙文互联</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002665" code="002665">首航高科</a>等，涨停板封单分别有2883.56万股、2803.13万股。以封单金额计算，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600927" code="600927">永安期货</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600668" code="600668">尖峰集团</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002432" code="002432">九安医疗</a>等涨停板封单资金较多，分别有21.31亿元、3.02亿元、2.95亿元。连续涨停天数看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ001317" code="001317">三羊马</a>已连收17个涨停板，连续涨停板数量最多。</p>
