<p>证券时报e公司讯，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600503" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600503" target="_blank">华丽家族</a>(600503)5月26日晚间公告，公司与<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601688" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601688" target="_blank">华泰证券</a>签署股权转让协议，约定公司向<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601688" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601688" target="_blank">华泰证券</a>出售持有的华泰期货40%股权，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601688" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601688" target="_blank">华泰证券</a>以现金方式支付全部交易对价，共计15.9亿元。交易完成后，华泰期货将成为<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601688" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601688" target="_blank">华泰证券</a>全资子公司。此次交易构成重大资产重组。</p>
