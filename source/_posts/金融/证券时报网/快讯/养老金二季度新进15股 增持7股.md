<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，二季度末养老金账户共现身41只个股前十大流通股东榜。其中，新进15只，增持7只。二季度末养老金账户对<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600352" code="600352">浙江龙盛</a>的持股量最多，持股量为3868.18万股。从期末持股市值看，养老金账户期末持股市值在亿元以上的有23只股，分别是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600352" code="600352">浙江龙盛</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603128" code="603128">华贸物流</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002850" code="002850">科达利</a>等。</p><p>持股比例方面，养老金账户持股比例最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002732" code="002732">燕塘乳业</a>，合计持股量为751万股，占流通股比例4.82%。养老金持股比例居前的还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688619" code="688619">罗普特</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002206" code="002206">海利得</a>等。养老金重仓股中15只个股的前十大流通股东榜也有社保基金现身，养老金账户持有3708.68万股的<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603128" code="603128">华贸物流</a>，前十大流通股东中就有1家社保基金现身。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202108/W020210820528105152976.png" title="养老金持股明细.png" alt="养老金持股明细.png" oldsrc="W020210820528105152976.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
