<p>证券时报e公司讯，11月11日，<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600062" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600062" target="_blank">华润双鹤</a>一类抗癌药落地暨海口国家高新区与<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600062" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600062" target="_blank">华润双鹤</a>签约仪式在海口市举行。双方现场签署了《海口国家高新区管理委员会与<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600062" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600062" target="_blank">华润双鹤</a>药业股份有限公司合作协议》。<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600062" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600062" target="_blank">华润双鹤</a>董事长冯毅指出，<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600062" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600062" target="_blank">华润双鹤</a>将进一步加快创新转型，顺应海南省全面深化改革、鼓励科技创新的战略要求，开启了<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600062" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600062" target="_blank">华润双鹤</a>和海南省政府未来紧密合作的新篇章。</p>
