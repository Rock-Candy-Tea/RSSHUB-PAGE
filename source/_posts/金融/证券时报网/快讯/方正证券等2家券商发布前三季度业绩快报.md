<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，截至10月20日，共有2家券商发布业绩快报。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601901" code="601901">方正证券</a>营业收入最高，前三季度营业收入64.59亿元，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000728" code="000728">国元证券</a>位居其后，营业收入为37.08亿元。</p><p>净利润来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601901" code="601901">方正证券</a>前三季度净利润19.28亿元，盈利金额居首，其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000728" code="000728">国元证券</a>，净利润为13.26亿元。增幅方面，公布业绩快报的上市券商中，净利润同比增长的有2家，增幅最高的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601901" code="601901">方正证券</a>，净利润同比增长30.08%。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202110/W020211020361542365662.png" title="公布业绩快报券商一览.png" alt="公布业绩快报券商一览.png" oldsrc="W020211020361542365662.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
