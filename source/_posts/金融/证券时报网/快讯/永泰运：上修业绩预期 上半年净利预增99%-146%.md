<p>证券时报e公司讯，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ001228" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="001228" target="_blank">永泰运</a>(001228)7月13日晚间公告，上修半年度业绩预告，修正后预计上半年净利润1.22亿元至1.51亿元，同比增长99.22%-146.10%；此前预计净利润8932万元至9872万元。上修原因是公司解决了大量新客户的货物出运需求，集中体现在<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601018" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601018" target="_blank">宁波港</a>和<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601298" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601298" target="_blank">青岛港</a>业务的增长；美元升值带来汇兑收益。</p>
