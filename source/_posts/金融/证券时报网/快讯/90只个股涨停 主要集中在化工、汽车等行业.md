<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，沪深两市可交易A股中，上涨个股有3318只，下跌个股有1168只，平盘个股136只。不含当日上市新股，共有90只个股涨停，4只个股跌停。以所属行业来看，涨停个股主要集中在化工、汽车、机械设备、电气设备、电子、交通运输等行业。</p><p>涨停股中，从收盘涨停板封单量来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600500" code="600500">中化国际</a>最受追捧，收盘涨停板封单有2279.37万股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000723" code="000723">美锦能源</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002060" code="002060">粤水电</a>，涨停板封单分别有2125.28万股、1606.08万股。以封单金额计算，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300118" code="300118">东方日升</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000723" code="000723">美锦能源</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600500" code="600500">中化国际</a>等涨停板封单资金较多，分别有2.71亿元、2.66亿元、1.85亿元。连续涨停天数看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603213" code="603213">镇洋发展</a>已连收7个涨停板，连续涨停板数量最多。</p>
