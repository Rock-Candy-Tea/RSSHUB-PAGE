<p>证券时报网讯，据wind统计显示，3月1日共有673只个股获北向资金持股量环比上一个交易日增加。以增持的股份数量和当日收盘价为基准进行测算，加仓股中，增持市值在1亿元以上的有34只，增持市值最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600519" code="600519">贵州茅台</a>，最新持股量为8769.26万股，环比增加0.81%，增持市值为13.1亿元；增持市值较多还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601012" code="601012">隆基股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002460" code="002460">赣锋锂业</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600036" code="600036">招商银行</a>等股。</p><p>从机构动向看，北向资金增持市值居前个股，近一个月获机构调研的有3只，机构调研次数较为频繁的有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002050" code="002050">三花智控</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002475" code="002475">立讯精密</a>等，期间分别获机构调研2次、2次；机构调研家数看，机构较为扎堆的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002475" code="002475">立讯精密</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002050" code="002050">三花智控</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000625" code="000625">长安汽车</a>，分别有331家、19家、9家机构参与调研。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202203/W020220302346891264924.png" title="北向资金增持市值排名.png" alt="北向资金增持市值排名.png" oldsrc="W020220302346891264924.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
