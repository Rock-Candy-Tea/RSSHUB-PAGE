<p>证券时报e公司讯，日前，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300750" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300750" target="_blank">宁德时代</a>董事长曾毓群率队到访<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300274" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300274" target="_blank">阳光电源</a>集团总部，双方进行了座谈交流，就进一步深化<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300750" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300750" target="_blank">宁德时代</a>与<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300274" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300274" target="_blank">阳光电源</a>在储能等新能源领域的全球合作进行探讨，并正式签署战略合作协议。根据协议，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300750" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300750" target="_blank">宁德时代</a>与<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300274" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300274" target="_blank">阳光电源</a>将充分发挥各自优势，携手开拓全球光储一体化市场。双方将在储能系统产品创新及全球应用等方面扩大合作，共同推进产业协同，助力新能源行业可持续发展。</p>
