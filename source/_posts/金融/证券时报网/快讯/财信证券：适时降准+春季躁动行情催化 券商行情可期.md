<p>证券时报网讯，财信证券指出，适时降准+春季躁动行情催化，券商行情可期。货币政策边际放松、适时降准有望进一步释放流动性，提振市场情绪，从而提升券商估值水平，催化券商板块行情。维持行业“领先大市”评级。投资主线上，建议关注优质头部券商以及财富管理优势突出、以及新三板业务量居前的券商三条主线。投资主线一：关注经营稳健、资本实力强以及政策优势明显的头部券商。推荐<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600030" code="600030">中信证券</a>、关注<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601995" code="601995">中金公司</a>。投资主线二：关注顺应财富管理转型发展的券商。推荐<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300059" code="300059">东方财富</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601688" code="601688">华泰证券</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600958" code="600958">东方证券</a>。投资主线三：建议关注新三板业务量居行业前列的<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000166" code="000166">申万宏源</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601066" code="601066">中信建投</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS61555" code="61555">东吴证券</a>等。</p>
