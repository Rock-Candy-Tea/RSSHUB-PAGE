<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，近5个交易日（12月9日至12月15日）沪深两市约148家上市公司被机构调研。机构调研榜单中，共有34家公司获20家以上机构扎堆调研。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000516" code="000516">国际医学</a>最受关注，参与调研的机构达到179家；<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002738" code="002738">中矿资源</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688139" code="688139">海尔生物</a>、大北农等分别被152家、148家、96家机构调研。机构调研次数来看，有3家公司被机构调研4次，分别是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000811" code="000811">冰轮环境</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002003" code="002003">伟星股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002791" code="002791">坚朗五金</a>等。</p><p>20家以上机构扎堆调研股中，近5日资金净流入的有11只，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002003" code="002003">伟星股份</a>近5日净流入资金0.59亿元，主力资金净流入最多；净流入资金较多的还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300498" code="300498">温氏股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002859" code="002859">洁美科技</a>等。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202112/W020211216329614200131.png" title="微信截图_20211216090712.png" alt="微信截图_20211216090712.png" oldsrc="W020211216329614200131.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
