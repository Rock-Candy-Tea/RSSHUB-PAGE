<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，沪深两市全天特大单净流出266.52亿元。其中，24股特大单净流入超1亿元，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600036" code="600036">招商银行</a>特大单净流入4.58亿元，特大单净流入资金居首。净流入资金居前的还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000858" code="000858">五粮液</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601398" code="601398">工商银行</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002271" code="002271">东方雨虹</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600019" code="600019">宝钢股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002703" code="002703">浙江世宝</a>等。</p><p>从申万一级行业来看，今日有5个行业特大单资金净流入，银行特大单净流入规模居首，净流入资金10.18亿元；净流入资金居前的还有房地产、食品饮料、钢铁等行业。</p>
