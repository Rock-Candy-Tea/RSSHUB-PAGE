<p>证券时报e公司讯，伴随着<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600071" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600071" target="_blank">凤凰光学</a>的加入，年内终止重组个股也再添一例。经<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300059" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300059" target="_blank">东方财富</a>Choice统计，截至5月29日，年内已有25股先后宣布重组折戟，包括<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002652" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002652" target="_blank">扬子新材</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002816" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002816" target="_blank">和科达</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600318" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600318" target="_blank">新力金融</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS605286" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="605286" target="_blank">同力日升</a>等，数量上与去年同期持平。在上述个股中，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002355" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002355" target="_blank">兴民智通</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603988" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="603988" target="_blank">中电电机</a>两股重组标的交易价格在10亿元以上。就重组目的来看，基本均属横向整合、业务转型、多元化战略三类，其中横向整合居多。(北京商报)</p>
