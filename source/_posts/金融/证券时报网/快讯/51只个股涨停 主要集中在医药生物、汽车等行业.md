<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，3月14日，沪深两市可交易A股中，上涨个股有431只，下跌个股有4197只，平盘个股22只。不含当日上市新股，共有51只个股涨停，45只个股跌停。以所属行业来看，涨停个股主要集中在医药生物、汽车、通信、计算机、机械设备等行业。</p><p>涨停股中，从收盘涨停板封单量来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000788" code="000788">北大医药</a>最受追捧，收盘涨停板封单有3888.20万股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002550" code="002550">千红制药</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600056" code="600056">中国医药</a>等，涨停板封单分别有3731.46万股、3181.71万股。以封单金额计算，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600056" code="600056">中国医药</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000788" code="000788">北大医药</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603636" code="603636">南威软件</a>等涨停板封单资金较多，分别有7.32亿元、4.24亿元、2.87亿元。连续涨停天数看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600734" code="600734">*ST实达</a>已连收18个涨停板，连续涨停板数量最多。</p>
