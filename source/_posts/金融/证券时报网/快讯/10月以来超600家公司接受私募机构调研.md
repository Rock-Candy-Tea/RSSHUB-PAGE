<p>证券时报e公司讯，Choice数据显示，截至11月20日，10月以来，超600家公司接受私募机构调研，其中百亿级私募煜德投资合伙人蔡建军调研了<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002488" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002488" target="_blank">金固股份</a>，宁泉资本掌舵者杨东、睿郡资产董事长杜昌勇亲自调研了小市值公司<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300240" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300240" target="_blank">飞力达</a>，高毅资产首席投资官邓晓峰亲自调研<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000400" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000400" target="_blank">许继电气</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300316" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300316" target="_blank">晶盛机电</a>和<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002906" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002906" target="_blank">华阳集团</a>等，百亿级私募趣时资产掌门人章秀奇则调研了<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002050" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002050" target="_blank">三花智控</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300037" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300037" target="_blank">新宙邦</a>等公司，混沌投资董事长葛卫东也亲自调研了<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300260" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300260" target="_blank">新莱应材</a>。</p>
