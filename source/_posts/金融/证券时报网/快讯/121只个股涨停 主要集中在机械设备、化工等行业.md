<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，沪深两市可交易A股中，上涨个股有3255只，下跌个股有1293只，平盘个股82只。不含当日上市新股，共有121只个股涨停，5只个股跌停。以所属行业来看，涨停个股主要集中在机械设备、化工、轻工制造、通信、汽车、医药生物等行业。</p><p>涨停股中，从收盘涨停板封单量来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002374" code="002374">中锐股份</a>最受追捧，收盘涨停板封单有4371.04万股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600935" code="600935">华塑股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002600" code="002600">领益智造</a>等，涨停板封单分别有3431.98万股、2912.59万股。以封单金额计算，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ001317" code="001317">三羊马</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000723" code="000723">美锦能源</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603986" code="603986">兆易创新</a>等涨停板封单资金较多，分别有5.52亿元、4.21亿元、3.9亿元。连续涨停天数看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603219" code="603219">富佳股份</a>已连收8个涨停板，连续涨停板数量最多。</p>
