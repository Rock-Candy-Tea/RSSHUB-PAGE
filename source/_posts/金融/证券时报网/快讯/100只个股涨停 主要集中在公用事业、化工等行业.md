<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，今日可交易A股中，上涨个股有2591只，下跌个股有1792只，平盘个股143只。不含当日上市新股，共有100只个股涨停，7只个股跌停。以所属行业来看，涨停个股主要集中在公用事业、化工、传媒、机械设备、轻工制造、电气设备等行业。</p><p>涨停股中，从收盘涨停板封单量来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002354" code="002354">天神娱乐</a>最受追捧，收盘涨停板封单有1697.99万股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300266" code="300266">兴源环境</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600856" code="600856">ST中天</a>，涨停板封单分别有1525.7万股、1508.04万股。以封单金额计算，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002466" code="002466">天齐锂业</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603026" code="603026">石大胜华</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000009" code="000009">中国宝安</a>等涨停板封单资金较多，分别有5.82亿元、3.3亿元、2.16亿元。连续涨停天数看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600856" code="600856">ST中天</a>已连收10个涨停板，连续涨停板数量最多。</p>
