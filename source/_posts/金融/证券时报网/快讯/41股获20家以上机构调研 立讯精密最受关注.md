<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，近5个交易日（2月17日至2月23日）沪深两市约164家上市公司被机构调研。机构调研榜单中，共有41家公司获20家以上机构扎堆调研。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002475" code="002475">立讯精密</a>最受关注，参与调研的机构达到239家；<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002991" code="002991">甘源食品</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300124" code="300124">汇川技术</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002402" code="002402">和而泰</a>等分别被210家、193家、175家机构调研。机构调研次数来看，有2家公司被机构调研3次，分别是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000338" code="000338">潍柴动力</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300708" code="300708">聚灿光电</a>等，机构调研最为密集。</p><p>20家以上机构扎堆调研股中，近5日资金净流入的有14只，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002709" code="002709">天赐材料</a>近5日净流入资金5.62亿元，主力资金净流入最多；净流入资金较多的还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000762" code="000762">西藏矿业</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000661" code="000661">长春高新</a>等。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202202/W020220224343164084314.png" title="近5日机构调研股一览.png" alt="近5日机构调研股一览.png" oldsrc="W020220224343164084314.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
