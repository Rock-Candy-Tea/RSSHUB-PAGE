<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，沪深两市可交易A股中，上涨个股有2977只，下跌个股有1443只，平盘个股149只。不含当日上市新股，共有99只个股涨停，8只个股跌停。以所属行业来看，涨停个股主要集中在传媒、建筑装饰、机械设备、采掘、汽车、交通运输等行业。</p><p>涨停股中，从收盘涨停板封单量来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600466" code="600466">蓝光发展</a>最受追捧，收盘涨停板封单有5697.08万股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600396" code="600396">金山股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000552" code="000552">靖远煤电</a>等，涨停板封单分别有4725.44万股、3288.04万股。以封单金额计算，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002265" code="002265">西仪股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000723" code="000723">美锦能源</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600396" code="600396">金山股份</a>等涨停板封单资金较多，分别有5.03亿元、4.53亿元、1.8亿元。连续涨停天数看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ001317" code="001317">三羊马</a>已连收13个涨停板，连续涨停板数量最多。</p>
