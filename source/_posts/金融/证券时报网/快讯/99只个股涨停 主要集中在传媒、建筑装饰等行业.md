<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，2月8日，沪深两市可交易A股中，上涨个股有3630只，下跌个股有949只，平盘个股44只。不含当日上市新股，共有99只个股涨停，20只个股跌停。以所属行业来看，涨停个股主要集中在传媒、建筑装饰、化工、商贸零售、计算机、通信等行业。</p><p>涨停股中，从收盘涨停板封单量来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600715" code="600715">文投控股</a>最受追捧，收盘涨停板封单有2918.13万股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002037" code="002037">保利联合</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000856" code="000856">冀东装备</a>等，涨停板封单分别有2804.85万股、2481.35万股。以封单金额计算，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002037" code="002037">保利联合</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002878" code="002878">元隆雅图</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ003031" code="003031">中瓷电子</a>等涨停板封单资金较多，分别有3.78亿元、3.68亿元、2.82亿元。连续涨停天数看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002037" code="002037">保利联合</a>已连收5个涨停板，连续涨停板数量最多。</p>
