<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，沪深两市可交易A股中，上涨个股有2104只，下跌个股有2370只，平盘个股172只。不含当日上市新股，共有96只个股涨停，11只个股跌停。以所属行业来看，涨停个股主要集中在医药生物、房地产、传媒、交通运输、电气设备、建筑装饰等行业。</p><p>涨停股中，从收盘涨停板封单量来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600396" code="600396">金山股份</a>最受追捧，收盘涨停板封单有3579.64万股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002265" code="002265">西仪股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000665" code="000665">湖北广电</a>等，涨停板封单分别有2518.98万股、2448.31万股。以封单金额计算，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002265" code="002265">西仪股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002432" code="002432">九安医疗</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603888" code="603888">新华网</a>等涨停板封单资金较多，分别有4.5亿元、2.04亿元、1.78亿元。连续涨停天数看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ001317" code="001317">三羊马</a>已连收12个涨停板，连续涨停板数量最多。</p>
