<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计，近五个交易日的龙虎榜当中，108只个股出现了机构的身影，其中58只股票呈现机构净买入，50只股票呈现机构净卖出。近五日机构净买入前三名分别是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600496" code="600496">精工钢构</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002603" code="002603">以岭药业</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600986" code="600986">浙文互联</a>。</p><p>与此同时，近五个交易日登陆沪深两市的龙虎榜个股中，营业部净买入较大的个股有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600941" code="600941">中国移动</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000629" code="000629">攀钢钒钛</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300052" code="300052">中青宝</a>。具体来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600941" code="600941">中国移动</a>是资金最青睐个股，营业部净买入金额达20.15亿元，高居榜首。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202201/W020220112382705848152.png" title="近五日龙虎榜机构净买入的个股.png" alt="近五日龙虎榜机构净买入的个股.png" width="550" oldsrc="W020220112382705848152.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
