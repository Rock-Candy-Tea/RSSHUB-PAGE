<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，沪深两市全天特大单净流入44.88亿元。其中，8股特大单净流入超2亿元，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002466" code="002466">天齐锂业</a>特大单净流入9.5亿元，特大单净流入资金居首。净流入资金居前的还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300750" code="300750">宁德时代</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002031" code="002031">巨轮智能</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601012" code="601012">隆基绿能</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002056" code="002056">横店东磁</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002460" code="002460">赣锋锂业</a>等。</p><p>从申万一级行业来看，今日有15个行业特大单资金净流入，电力设备特大单净流入规模居首，净流入资金28.68亿元；净流入资金居前的还有有色金属、机械设备、汽车等行业。</p>
