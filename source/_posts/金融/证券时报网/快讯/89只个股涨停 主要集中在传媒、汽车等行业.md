<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，沪深两市可交易A股中，上涨个股有2237只，下跌个股有2174只，平盘个股156只。不含当日上市新股，共有89只个股涨停，5只个股跌停。以所属行业来看，涨停个股主要集中在传媒、汽车、建筑装饰、化工、机械设备等行业。</p><p>涨停股中，从收盘涨停板封单量来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002265" code="002265">西仪股份</a>最受追捧，收盘涨停板封单有2967.52万股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002699" code="002699">美盛文化</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601068" code="601068">中铝国际</a>等，涨停板封单分别有2656.63万股、1959.19万股。以封单金额计算，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002265" code="002265">西仪股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002699" code="002699">美盛文化</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002338" code="002338">奥普光电</a>等涨停板封单资金较多，分别有3.98亿元、1.53亿元、1.29亿元。连续涨停天数看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ001317" code="001317">三羊马</a>已连收9个涨停板，连续涨停板数量最多。</p>
