<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，沪深两市可交易A股中，上涨个股有3022只，下跌个股有1399只，平盘个股110只。不含当日上市新股，共有90只个股涨停，28只个股跌停。以所属行业来看，涨停个股主要集中在传媒、电气设备、计算机、汽车、机械设备、化工等行业。</p><p>涨停股中，从收盘涨停板封单量来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603703" code="603703">盛洋科技</a>最受追捧，收盘涨停板封单有5344.11万股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603213" code="603213">镇洋发展</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000536" code="000536">华映科技</a>，涨停板封单分别有2642.46万股、1987.64万股。以封单金额计算，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603703" code="603703">盛洋科技</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603213" code="603213">镇洋发展</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300199" code="300199">翰宇药业</a>等涨停板封单资金较多，分别有9.27亿元、2.76亿元、1.95亿元。连续涨停天数看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS605333" code="605333">沪光股份</a>已连收7个涨停板，连续涨停板数量最多。</p>
