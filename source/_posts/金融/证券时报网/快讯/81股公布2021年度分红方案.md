<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，81家上市公司公布2021年度分配方案。从派现金额看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ301047" code="301047">义翘神州</a>分红最慷慨，公司2月28日公布10转增9派100元（含税）的分配方案；<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688368" code="688368">晶丰明源</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300834" code="300834">星辉环材</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688200" code="688200">华峰测控</a>等每10股派现金额分别为40元、22元、21.5元。</p><p>送转方案来看，目前分配方案中包含送转的公司有20家，每10股送转比例最高的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ301071" code="301071">力量钻石</a>，公司拟10送转10股；<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ301047" code="301047">义翘神州</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ301003" code="301003">江苏博云</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300929" code="300929">华骐环保</a>送转比例为每10股送转9股、7股、6股。</p>
