<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，近10个交易日（11月3日~11月16日）共有27家上市公司股份获高管增持。从增持金额看，近10日增持金额最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300401" code="300401">花园生物</a>，累计增持245.42万股，增持金额合计3299.01万元；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300352" code="300352">北信源</a>，增持量为500.62万股，增持金额2915.47万元。</p><p>增持次数方面，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601825" code="601825">沪农商行</a>增持最频繁，近10日共有8条增持记录，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002936" code="002936">郑州银行</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300370" code="300370">ST安控</a>等紧随其后，近10日各有6条增持记录。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600801" code="600801">华新水泥</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300401" code="300401">花园生物</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300254" code="300254">仟源医药</a>等期间也有两条以上的增持记录。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202111/W020211117507087707948.png" title="近10日高管增持股一览.png" alt="近10日高管增持股一览.png" oldsrc="W020211117507087707948.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
