<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，近10个交易日（12月21日~1月4日）共有23家上市公司股份获高管增持。从增持金额看，近10日增持金额最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002563" code="002563">森马服饰</a>，累计增持805.68万股，增持金额合计5454.45万元；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300478" code="300478">杭州高新</a>，增持量为251万股，增持金额2469.84万元。</p><p>增持次数方面，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300400" code="300400">劲拓股份</a>增持最频繁，近10日共有13条增持记录，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600170" code="600170">上海建工</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002407" code="002407">多氟多</a>等紧随其后，近10日分别有7条、5条增持记录。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS605222" code="605222">起帆电缆</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002531" code="002531">天顺风能</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002936" code="002936">郑州银行</a>等期间也有两条以上的增持记录。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202201/W020220105374229823688.png" title="近10日高管增持股一览.png" alt="近10日高管增持股一览.png" oldsrc="W020220105374229823688.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
