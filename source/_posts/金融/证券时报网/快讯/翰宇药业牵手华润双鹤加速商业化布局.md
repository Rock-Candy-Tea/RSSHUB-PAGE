<p>证券时报e公司讯，近日，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300199" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300199" target="_blank">翰宇药业</a>与<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600062" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600062" target="_blank">华润双鹤</a>达成战略合作，双方将围绕产品市场拓展、推广销售等进行深度合作，促进双方业务共同发展。根据协议，双方作为重要的长期合作伙伴，结合<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300199" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300199" target="_blank">翰宇药业</a>的优势产品管线及<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600062" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600062" target="_blank">华润双鹤</a>优势区域的营销渠道和客户资源，大力推动翰宇核心管线领域产品创新，积极发挥<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600062" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600062" target="_blank">华润双鹤</a>在医药行业领导品牌的作用，充分扩大市场，进行产品的市场拓展、产品销售和学术推广，促进双方业务的共同发展。</p>
