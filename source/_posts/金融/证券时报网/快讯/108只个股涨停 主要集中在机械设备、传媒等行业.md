<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，沪深两市可交易A股中，上涨个股有2487只，下跌个股有1946只，平盘个股133只。不含当日上市新股，共有108只个股涨停，8只个股跌停。以所属行业来看，涨停个股主要集中在机械设备、传媒、化工、交通运输、汽车、医药生物等行业。</p><p>涨停股中，从收盘涨停板封单量来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601068" code="601068">中铝国际</a>最受追捧，收盘涨停板封单有2899.39万股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002374" code="002374">中锐股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600396" code="600396">金山股份</a>等，涨停板封单分别有2758.77万股、2450.83万股。以封单金额计算，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002265" code="002265">西仪股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300612" code="300612">宣亚国际</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002374" code="002374">中锐股份</a>等涨停板封单资金较多，分别有3.52亿元、1.88亿元、1.65亿元。连续涨停天数看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ001317" code="001317">三羊马</a>已连收11个涨停板，连续涨停板数量最多。</p>
