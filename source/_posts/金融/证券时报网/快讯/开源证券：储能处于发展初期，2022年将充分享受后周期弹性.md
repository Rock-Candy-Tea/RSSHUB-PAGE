<p>证券时报网讯，开源证券指出，储能处于发展初期，2022年将充分享受后周期弹性。（1）核心成长角度看，重点关注价值量占比高的环节（电池和储能逆变器环节成本占比较高），<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300750" code="300750">宁德时代</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002594" code="002594">比亚迪</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300274" code="300274">阳光电源</a>等受益；（2）确定性角度看，短期海外家储盈利较好，中长期关注电源侧储能打开增长的二次曲线，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688063" code="688063">派能科技</a>等受益；（3）弹性角度看，储能收入占比高、弹性好的公司包括电池领域的<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300438" code="300438">鹏辉能源</a>等，逆变器领域的<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS605117" code="605117">德业股份</a>等。</p>
