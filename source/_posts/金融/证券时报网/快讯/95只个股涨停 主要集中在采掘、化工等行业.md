<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，今日可交易A股中，上涨个股有1951只，下跌个股有2370只，平盘个股140只。不含当日上市新股，共有95只个股涨停，6只个股跌停。以所属行业来看，涨停个股主要集中在采掘、化工、有色金属、机械设备、公用事业、钢铁等行业。</p><p>涨停股中，从收盘涨停板封单量来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ001213" code="001213">中铁特货</a>最受追捧，收盘涨停板封单有5725.21万股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000537" code="000537">广宇发展</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000937" code="000937">冀中能源</a>等，涨停板封单分别有4460.11万股、4230.18万股。以封单金额计算，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000825" code="000825">太钢不锈</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ001215" code="001215">千味央厨</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ001213" code="001213">中铁特货</a>等涨停板封单资金较多，分别有3.79亿元、3.77亿元、3.59亿元。连续涨停天数看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600683" code="600683">京投发展</a>已连收5个涨停板，连续涨停板数量最多。</p>
