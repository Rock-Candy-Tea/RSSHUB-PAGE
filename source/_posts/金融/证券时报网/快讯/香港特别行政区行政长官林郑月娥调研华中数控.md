<p>证券时报e公司讯，据<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300161" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300161" target="_blank">华中数控</a>消息，11月30日下午，香港特别行政区行政长官林郑月娥等一行莅临武汉<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300161" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300161" target="_blank">华中数控</a>股份有限公司（简称<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300161" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300161" target="_blank">华中数控</a>）参观调研。她充分肯定了<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300161" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300161" target="_blank">华中数控</a>在国产高档数控系统研发、生产和推广方面所取得的重要成果，表示要积极推进香港制造企业与<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300161" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300161" target="_blank">华中数控</a>的交流合作，利用<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300161" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300161" target="_blank">华中数控</a>在智能制造的先进经验，共同推进香港特别行政区相关产业向工业4.0转型升级。</p>
