<p>证券时报e公司讯，最新披露的一季报透露了私募大佬葛卫东最新调仓换股动向。葛卫东在今年一季度大举减持了“云茅”<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600588" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600588" target="_blank">用友网络</a>4474.03万股，交易金额或近14亿元。葛卫东对<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600588" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600588" target="_blank">用友网络</a>的持股时间将近5年，这几年一直以加仓为主，就算减持也卖得比较少，此次这么大幅度砍仓，实属罕见，该股今年跌幅高达46.66%，一季度净利润亏损3.93亿元。总的来看，葛卫东今年一季度A股持股市值约80亿元左右，除了减持<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600588" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600588" target="_blank">用友网络</a>，还减持了<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002230" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002230" target="_blank">科大讯飞</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=NEEQ835185" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="835185" target="_blank">贝特瑞</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688023" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="688023" target="_blank">安恒信息</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603986" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="603986" target="_blank">兆易创新</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603236" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="603236" target="_blank">移远通信</a>等多只个股，新进<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688107" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="688107" target="_blank">安路科技</a>。                                          <img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/egs/202205/W020220503610388812182.png" oldsrc="W020220503610388812182.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">(中国基金报)</p>
