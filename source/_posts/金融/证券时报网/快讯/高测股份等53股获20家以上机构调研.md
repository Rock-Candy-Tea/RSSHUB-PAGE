<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，近5个交易日（1月26日至2月8日）沪深两市约115家上市公司被机构调研。机构调研榜单中，共有53家公司获20家以上机构扎堆调研。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002050" code="002050">三花智控</a>最受关注，参与调研的机构达到328家；<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688561" code="688561">奇安信</a>被272家机构调研，榜单中排名第二；<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688058" code="688058">宝兰德</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688556" code="688556">高测股份</a>等分别被192家、190家机构调研。机构调研次数来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002594" code="002594">比亚迪</a>机构调研最为密集，共获机构6次调研。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300622" code="300622">博士眼镜</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688561" code="688561">奇安信</a>等机构调研也较为密集，均被机构调研2次。</p><p>20家以上机构扎堆调研股中，近5日资金净流入的有6只，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002567" code="002567">唐人神</a>近5日净流入资金0.86亿元，主力资金净流入最多；净流入资金较多的还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603738" code="603738">泰晶科技</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002841" code="002841">视源股份</a>等。</p><p><br></p>
