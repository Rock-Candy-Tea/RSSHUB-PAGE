code="600036">招商银行</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600048" code="600048">保利发展</a>。增幅方面，135家公司营业收入同比增长，增幅最高的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603185" code="603185">上机数控</a>，去年实现营业收入109.06亿元，同比增长262.22%；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603392"
