<p>证券时报e公司讯，<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600673" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600673" target="_blank">东阳光</a>(600673)11月11日晚间公告，拟向广药出售<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600673" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600673" target="_blank">东阳光</a>药2.26亿股内资股股份，向广药全资子公司香港<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600673" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600673" target="_blank">东阳光</a>出售<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600673" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600673" target="_blank">东阳光</a>药2.26亿股H股“全流通”股份。上述拟转让股份合计4.52亿股，约占<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600673" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600673" target="_blank">东阳光</a>药总股本的51.41%。经友好协商，确定本次交易的<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600673" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600673" target="_blank">东阳光</a>药股份交易价格为37.23亿元。通过本次交易，公司将不再从事医药相关产品的生产和销售；公司将积极在变频储能、新能源汽车等新能源领域拓展新业务。</p>
