<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，近10个交易日（5月26日~6月8日）共有44家上市公司股份获高管增持。从增持金额看，近10日增持金额最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002517" code="002517">恺英网络</a>，累计增持906.79万股，增持金额合计3998.94万元；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000997" code="000997">新大陆</a>，增持量为39.39万股，增持金额602.43万元。</p><p>增持次数方面，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300273" code="300273">和佳医疗</a>增持最频繁，近10日共有12条增持记录，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300370" code="300370">ST安控</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601077" code="601077">渝农商行</a>等紧随其后，近10日各有7条增持记录。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601860" code="601860">紫金银行</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600697" code="600697">欧亚集团</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600801" code="600801">华新水泥</a>等期间也有两条以上的增持记录。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202106/W020210609434144082071.png" title="近10日高管增持股一览.png" alt="近10日高管增持股一览.png" oldsrc="W020210609434144082071.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
