<p>证券时报e公司讯，江苏<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600584" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600584" target="_blank">长电科技</a>股份有限公司（简称“<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600584" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600584" target="_blank">长电科技</a>”）声明，针对甬矽电子（宁波）股份有限公司的侵权和不正当竞争行为，<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600584" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600584" target="_blank">长电科技</a>发起一系列法律行动，旨在维护企业合法权益，推动行业健康发展。针对甬矽电子及部分高管采用不正当的竞争方式侵犯<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600584" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600584" target="_blank">长电科技</a>商业秘密，造成<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600584" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600584" target="_blank">长电科技</a>人才和技术流失，<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600584" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600584" target="_blank">长电科技</a>已提起不正当竞争诉讼。针对部分甬矽电子员工在<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600584" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600584" target="_blank">长电科技</a>任职期间即通过他人代持的方式持有甬矽电子的股份，违反<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600584" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600584" target="_blank">长电科技</a>的利益冲突政策和员工基本诚信和忠实义务的行为，<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600584" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600584" target="_blank">长电科技</a>已提起劳动仲裁和诉讼。</p>
