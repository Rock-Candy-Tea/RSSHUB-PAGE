<p>证券时报e公司讯，数据显示，全球持有<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600519" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600519" target="_blank">贵州茅台</a>最多的基金3季度减持<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600519" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600519" target="_blank">贵州茅台</a>。美国资产管理规模达2.6万亿美元的资管巨头-资本集团旗下多只基金日前披露9月底持仓信息。美洲基金-欧洲<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601099" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601099" target="_blank">太平洋</a>成长基金一季度末持有<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600519" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600519" target="_blank">贵州茅台</a>694.87万股，二季度降至655.36万股，三季度末持有<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600519" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600519" target="_blank">贵州茅台</a>进一步降低至582.32万股。与二季度末相比，美洲基金-欧洲<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601099" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601099" target="_blank">太平洋</a>成长基金三季度末持有<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600519" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600519" target="_blank">贵州茅台</a>股份数降低11.19%。(中国基金报)</p>
