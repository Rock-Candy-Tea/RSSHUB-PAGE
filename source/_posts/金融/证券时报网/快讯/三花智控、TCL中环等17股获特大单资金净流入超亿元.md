<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，沪深两市全天特大单净流出44.13亿元。其中，17股特大单净流入超1亿元，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600519" code="600519">贵州茅台</a>特大单净流入3.1亿元，特大单净流入资金居首；净流入资金居前的还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002050" code="002050">三花智控</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002129" code="002129">TCL中环</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000858" code="000858">五粮液</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002594" code="002594">比亚迪</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002805" code="002805">丰元股份</a>等。</p><p>从申万一级行业来看，今日有10个行业特大单资金净流入，电力设备特大单净流入规模居首，净流入资金13.96亿元；净流入资金居前的还有食品饮料、家用电器、汽车等行业。</p>
