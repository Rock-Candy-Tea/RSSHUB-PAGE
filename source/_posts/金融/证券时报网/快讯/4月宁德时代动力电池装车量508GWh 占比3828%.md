<p>证券时报e公司讯，5月11日，中国汽车动力电池产业联盟发布最新数据。数据显示，4月国内动力电池企业装车量前五名分别为<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300750" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300750" target="_blank">宁德时代</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002594" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002594" target="_blank">比亚迪</a>、中创新航、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002074" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002074" target="_blank">国轩高科</a>和<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300207" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300207" target="_blank">欣旺达</a>。其中，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300750" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300750" target="_blank">宁德时代</a>位列第一，单月装车量5.08GWh，占比38.28%；<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002594" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002594" target="_blank">比亚迪</a>的动力电池装车量4.27GWh，占比32.18%。</p>
