<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，今日可交易A股中，上涨个股有2477只，下跌个股有1890只，平盘个股101只。不含当日上市新股，共有108只个股涨停，17只个股跌停。以所属行业来看，涨停个股主要集中在化工、有色金属、机械设备、电气设备、交通运输、公用事业等行业。</p><p>涨停股中，从收盘涨停板封单量来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000937" code="000937">冀中能源</a>最受追捧，收盘涨停板封单有6971.02万股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000537" code="000537">广宇发展</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000630" code="000630">铜陵有色</a>等，涨停板封单分别有4170.39万股、3814.45万股。以封单金额计算，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ001215" code="001215">千味央厨</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000937" code="000937">冀中能源</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS605599" code="605599">菜百股份</a>等涨停板封单资金较多，分别有7.38亿元、5.45亿元、4.66亿元。连续涨停天数看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000537" code="000537">广宇发展</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ001215" code="001215">千味央厨</a>均已连收6个涨停板，连续涨停板数量最多。</p>
