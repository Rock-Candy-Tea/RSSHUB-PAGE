<p>证券时报e公司讯，近日，<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002152" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002152" target="_blank">广电运通</a>连续中标<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601998" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601998" target="_blank">中信银行</a>、宁夏银行、<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601997" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601997" target="_blank">贵阳银行</a>信创国产化软件建设项目，将通过全渠道业务平台iBank4.0产品，助推银行金融信创加速发展。在信创国产化的浪潮下，作为金融信创国产化的排头兵，<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002152" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002152" target="_blank">广电运通</a>结合多年银行智能设备终端硬件、跨平台软件的研发实施及部署经验，已经打造了全渠道业务平台iBank4.0产品。</p>
