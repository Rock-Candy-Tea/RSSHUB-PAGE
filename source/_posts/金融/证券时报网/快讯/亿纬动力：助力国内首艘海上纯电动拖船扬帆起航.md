<p>证券时报e公司讯，<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300014" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300014" target="_blank">亿纬锂能</a>消息，近日，国内首艘海上纯电动拖轮——“云港电拖一号”在江苏<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601008" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601008" target="_blank">连云港</a>港口交付试运行。该船由<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600150" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600150" target="_blank">中国船舶</a>第<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603712" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="603712" target="_blank">七一二</a>所与<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601008" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601008" target="_blank">连云港</a>港口控股集团、<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600150" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600150" target="_blank">中国船舶</a>第七〇八所联合设计，<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601008" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601008" target="_blank">连云港</a>港口控股集团轮驳分公司建造。是纯电池动力首次应用于海上拖轮，也是亿纬动力船用LF280电池在船舶领域应用的又一次突破。</p>
