<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，截止3月4日，2021年四季度末社保基金共现身10只个股前十大流通股东榜。其中，新进5只，增持3只。从前十大流通股东名单中社保基金家数来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300496" code="300496">中科创达</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688598" code="688598">金博股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688082" code="688082">盛美上海</a>等3只股有2家社保基金集中现身。</p><p>从持股比例看，社保基金持有比例最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ003038" code="003038">鑫铂股份</a>，持股量占流通股比例为7.61%；持股比例居前的还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300083" code="300083">创世纪</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300496" code="300496">中科创达</a>等。持股数量方面，社保基金持股量最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300083" code="300083">创世纪</a>，共持有7450.03万股，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002402" code="002402">和而泰</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002092" code="002092">中泰化学</a>等紧随其后。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202203/W020220304362047182679.png" title="社保基金四季度重仓股排行榜.png" alt="社保基金四季度重仓股排行榜.png" oldsrc="W020220304362047182679.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
