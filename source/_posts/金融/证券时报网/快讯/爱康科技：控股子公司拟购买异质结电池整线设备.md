<p>证券时报e公司讯，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002610" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002610" target="_blank">爱康科技</a>(002610)7月5日晚间公告，公司控股子公司与<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300751" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300751" target="_blank">迈为股份</a>签订采购合同，将向<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300751" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300751" target="_blank">迈为股份</a>购买两条<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000591" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000591" target="_blank">太阳能</a>210半片异质结电池整线设备，每条生产线的产能均超过600MW。完成交货后，公司将实现异质结电池自有产能约2GW。</p>
