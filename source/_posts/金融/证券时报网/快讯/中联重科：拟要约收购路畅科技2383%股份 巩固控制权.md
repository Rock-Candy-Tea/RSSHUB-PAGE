<p>证券时报e公司讯，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000157" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000157" target="_blank">中联重科</a>(000157)3月10日晚间公告，公司向<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002813" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002813" target="_blank">路畅科技</a>发出要约收购报告书摘要，此次要约收购前，公司已于2月23日受让并持有<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002813" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002813" target="_blank">路畅科技</a>29.99%股份，成为其控股股东。公司拟通过此次要约收购，增加对<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002813" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002813" target="_blank">路畅科技</a>的持股比例，进一步巩固对<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002813" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002813" target="_blank">路畅科技</a>的控制权。此次预定要约收购股份数量为2859.6万股，占<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002813" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002813" target="_blank">路畅科技</a>总股本的23.83%，要约收购价格为21.67元/股。</p>
