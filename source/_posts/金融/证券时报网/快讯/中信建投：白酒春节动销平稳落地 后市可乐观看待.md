<p>证券时报e公司讯，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601066" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601066" target="_blank">中信建投</a>研报认为，白酒春节动销平稳落地，后市可乐观看待。当前春节动销成为市场最为关注的核心因素之一，其与全国多地部分经销商沟通，认为春节动销整体平稳，苏皖市场表现较好，高端酒表现较为平稳。龙头酒企回款整体平稳，部分企业如古井、洋河等回款进度优于去年同期，春节动销受疫情影响较小，平稳落地后龙头企业全年业绩确定性提升，考虑名优酒企的综合竞争力不断提升，当前高端及次高端龙头估值具备安全边际，后市可乐观看待。重点推荐高端<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600519" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600519" target="_blank">贵州茅台</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000858" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000858" target="_blank">五粮液</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000568" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000568" target="_blank">泸州老窖</a>，次高端<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600809" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600809" target="_blank">山西汾酒</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000799" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000799" target="_blank">酒鬼酒</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600702" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600702" target="_blank">舍得酒业</a>等。</p>
