<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，9月1日共有801只股获北向资金持股量环比上一个交易日增加。以增持的股份数量和当日收盘价为基准进行测算，加仓股中，增持市值在1亿元以上的有57只，增持市值最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600887" code="600887">伊利股份</a>，最新持股量为8.42亿股，环比增加2.2%，增持市值为6.56亿元；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600519" code="600519">贵州茅台</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600438" code="600438">通威股份</a>。</p><p>从机构动向看，北向资金增持市值居前个股，近一个月获机构调研的有11只，机构调研次数较为频繁的有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002353" code="002353">杰瑞股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603986" code="603986">兆易创新</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600763" code="600763">通策医疗</a>等；机构调研家数看，机构较为扎堆的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300760" code="300760">迈瑞医疗</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603986" code="603986">兆易创新</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300316" code="300316">晶盛机电</a>。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202109/W020210902356393595200.png" title="北向资金增持市值排名.png" alt="北向资金增持市值排名.png" oldsrc="W020210902356393595200.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
