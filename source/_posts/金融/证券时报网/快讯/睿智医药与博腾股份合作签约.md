<p>证券时报e公司讯，3月10日上午，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300149" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300149" target="_blank">睿智医药</a>与<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300363" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300363" target="_blank">博腾股份</a>举办合作协议签署仪式。在此次签约仪式上，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300149" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300149" target="_blank">睿智医药</a>和<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300363" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300363" target="_blank">博腾股份</a>双方代表签署了关于凯惠药业（<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300149" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300149" target="_blank">睿智医药</a>旗下全资子公司）的股权转让协议。同时，以此次股权转让为契机，双方未来将展开业务的全方位合作。</p>
