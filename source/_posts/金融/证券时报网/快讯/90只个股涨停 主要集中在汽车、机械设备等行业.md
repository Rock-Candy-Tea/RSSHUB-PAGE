<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，沪深两市可交易A股中，上涨个股有3239只，下跌个股有1123只，平盘个股199只。不含当日上市新股，共有90只个股涨停，24只个股跌停。以所属行业来看，涨停个股主要集中在汽车、机械设备、化工、电气设备、公用事业、电子等行业。</p><p>涨停股中，从收盘涨停板封单量来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601212" code="601212">白银有色</a>最受追捧，收盘涨停板封单有4479.5万股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002265" code="002265">西仪股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600698" code="600698">湖南天雁</a>等，涨停板封单分别有3525.7万股、1412.44万股。以封单金额计算，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002265" code="002265">西仪股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600588" code="600588">用友网络</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600559" code="600559">老白干酒</a>等涨停板封单资金较多，分别有3.91亿元、1.9亿元、1.62亿元。连续涨停天数看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600734" code="600734">*ST实达</a>已连收9个涨停板，连续涨停板数量最多。</p>
