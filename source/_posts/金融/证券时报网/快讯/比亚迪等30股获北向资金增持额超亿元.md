<p>证券时报网讯，据wind统计显示，1月11日共有643只个股获北向资金持股量环比上一个交易日增加。以增持的股份数量和当日收盘价为基准进行测算，加仓股中，增持市值在1亿元以上的有30只，增持市值最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002594" code="002594">比亚迪</a>，最新持股量为7484.59万股，环比增加2.74%，增持市值为4.77亿元；增持市值较多还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000001" code="000001">平安银行</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600346" code="600346">恒力石化</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603259" code="603259">药明康德</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600406" code="600406">国电南瑞</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002371" code="002371">北方华创</a>等股。</p><p>从机构动向看，北向资金增持市值居前个股，近一个月获机构调研的有4只，机构调研次数较为频繁的有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002714" code="002714">牧原股份</a>等，期间分别获机构调研4次；机构调研家数看，机构较为扎堆的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002714" code="002714">牧原股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000568" code="000568">泸州老窖</a>。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202201/W020220112363797858635.png" title="北向资金增持市值排名.png" alt="北向资金增持市值排名.png" oldsrc="W020220112363797858635.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
