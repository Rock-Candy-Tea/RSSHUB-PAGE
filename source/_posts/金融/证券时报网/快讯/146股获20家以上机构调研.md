<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，近5个交易日（8月26日至9月1日）沪深两市约289家上市公司被机构调研。机构调研榜单中，共有146家公司获20家以上机构扎堆调研。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300760" code="300760">迈瑞医疗</a>最受关注，参与调研的机构达到704家；<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603986" code="603986">兆易创新</a>被554家机构调研，榜单中排名第二。机构调研次数来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300241" code="300241">瑞丰光电</a>机构调研最为密集，共获机构5次调研。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ003009" code="003009">中天火箭</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300358" code="300358">楚天科技</a>等机构调研也较为密集，均被机构调研3次。</p><p>20家以上机构扎堆调研股中，近5日资金净流入的有30只，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002241" code="002241">歌尔股份</a>近5日净流入资金7.32亿元，主力资金净流入最多；净流入资金较多的还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000932" code="000932">华菱钢铁</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300760" code="300760">迈瑞医疗</a>等。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202109/W020210902346255899803.png" title="近5日机构调研股一览.png" alt="近5日机构调研股一览.png" oldsrc="W020210902346255899803.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
