<p>证券时报e公司讯，1月6日，<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002434" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002434" target="_blank">万里扬</a>(002434)与<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002434" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002434" target="_blank">万里扬</a>集团等签署股权转让协议，前者以1.1亿元收购<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002434" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002434" target="_blank">万里扬</a>集团等股东合计持有的<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002434" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002434" target="_blank">万里扬</a>能源公司51%的股份。<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002434" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002434" target="_blank">万里扬</a>能源公司主要从事储能电站投资和运营等业务，2021年营收2770.49万元，净利润707.03万元。公司称此次收购有利于推动<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002434" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002434" target="_blank">万里扬</a>能源公司不断做大做强储能、电力市场现货交易等相关业务，为公司未来发展中创造新的业务和利润增长点。</p>
