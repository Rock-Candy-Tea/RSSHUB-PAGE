<p>证券时报网讯，据wind统计显示，12月23日共有683只个股获北向资金持股量环比上一个交易日增加。以增持的股份数量和当日收盘价为基准进行测算，加仓股中，增持市值在1亿元以上的有30只，增持市值最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300750" code="300750">宁德时代</a>，最新持股量为1.97亿股，环比增加0.39%，增持市值为4.75亿元；增持市值较多还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300059" code="300059">东方财富</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600699" code="600699">均胜电子</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002384" code="002384">东山精密</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601016" code="601016">节能风电</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002709" code="002709">天赐材料</a>等股。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202112/W020211224347560175433.png" title="北向资金增持市值排名.png" alt="北向资金增持市值排名.png" oldsrc="W020211224347560175433.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
