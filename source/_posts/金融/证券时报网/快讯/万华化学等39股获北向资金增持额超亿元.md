<p>证券时报网讯，据wind统计显示，10月20日共有725只股获北向资金持股量环比上一个交易日增加。以增持的股份数量和当日收盘价为基准进行测算，加仓股中，增持市值在亿元以上的有39只，增持市值最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600309" code="600309">万华化学</a>，最新持股量为2.27亿股，环比增加2.83%，增持市值为6.74亿元；增持市值较多还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300750" code="300750">宁德时代</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002747" code="002747">埃斯顿</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300751" code="300751">迈为股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601012" code="601012">隆基股份</a>、比亚迪等股。</p><p>从机构动向看，北向资金增持市值居前个股，近一个月获机构调研的有8只，机构调研次数较为频繁的有比亚迪等，期间分别获机构调研2次；机构调研家数看，机构较为扎堆的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002326" code="002326">永太科技</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600900" code="600900">长江电力</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000333" code="000333">美的集团</a>。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202110/W020211021360512867430.png" title="北向资金增持市值排名.png" alt="北向资金增持市值排名.png" oldsrc="W020211021360512867430.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
