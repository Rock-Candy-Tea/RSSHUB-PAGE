<p>证券时报e公司讯，数据宝统计，除去上市新股，8月22日至26日，A股市场将有37家上市公司面临限售股解禁。以个股最新收盘价计算，解禁市值合计1106.05亿元。从解禁市值规模来看，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601728" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601728" target="_blank">中国电信</a>占据半壁江山，高达539.56亿元，股份类型为首发原股东限售股份和首发战略配售股份。其后<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ301050" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="301050" target="_blank">雷电微力</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000031" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000031" target="_blank">大悦城</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002423" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002423" target="_blank">中粮资本</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603992" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="603992" target="_blank">松霖科技</a>解禁市值均超过50亿元。(数据宝)</p>
