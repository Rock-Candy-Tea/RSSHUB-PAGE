<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，近10个交易日（6月30日~7月13日）共有38家公司股份获高管增持。从增持金额看，近10日增持金额最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002791" code="002791">坚朗五金</a>，累计增持70万股，增持金额合计1.22亿元；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603816" code="603816">顾家家居</a>，增持量为24.7万股，增持金额1828.04万元。</p><p>增持次数方面，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603950" code="603950">长源东谷</a>增持最频繁，近10日共有13条增持记录，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600387" code="600387">ST海越</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002958" code="002958">青农商行</a>等紧随其后，近10日各有10条增持记录。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601916" code="601916">浙商银行</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002798" code="002798">帝欧家居</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002839" code="002839">张家港行</a>等期间也有两条以上的增持记录。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202107/W020210714355572137598.png" title="近10日高管增持股一览.png" alt="近10日高管增持股一览.png" oldsrc="W020210714355572137598.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
