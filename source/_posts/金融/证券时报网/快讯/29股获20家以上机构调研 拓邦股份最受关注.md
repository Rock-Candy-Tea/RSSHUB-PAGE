<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计，近5个交易日（7月25日至7月29日）沪深两市约114家上市公司被机构调研。机构调研榜单中，共有29家公司获20家以上机构扎堆调研。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002139" code="002139">拓邦股份</a>最受关注，参与调研的机构达到201家；<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688733" code="688733">壹石通</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ301101" code="301101">明月镜片</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688207" code="688207">格灵深瞳</a>等分别被184家、147家、134家机构调研。机构调研次数来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002807" code="002807">江阴银行</a>机构调研最为密集，共获机构3次调研。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300848" code="300848">美瑞新材</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300043" code="300043">星辉娱乐</a>均被机构调研2次。</p><p>20家以上机构扎堆调研股中，近5日资金净流入的有13只，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS605208" code="605208">永茂泰</a>近5日净流入资金1.47亿元，主力资金净流入最多；净流入资金较多的还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002139" code="002139">拓邦股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ003022" code="003022">联泓新科</a>等。</p>
