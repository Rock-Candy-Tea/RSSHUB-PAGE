<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，沪深两市可交易A股中，上涨个股有3282只，下跌个股有1097只，平盘个股156只。不含当日上市新股，共有114只个股涨停，6只个股跌停。以所属行业来看，涨停个股主要集中在电气设备、有色金属、汽车、化工、机械设备、电子等行业。</p><p>涨停股中，从收盘涨停板封单量来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002131" code="002131">利欧股份</a>最受追捧，收盘涨停板封单有2.39亿股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002717" code="002717">岭南股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002011" code="002011">盾安环境</a>，涨停板封单分别有1496.94万股、1083.05万股。以封单金额计算，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002131" code="002131">利欧股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002466" code="002466">天齐锂业</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600338" code="600338">西藏珠峰</a>等涨停板封单资金较多，分别有6.39亿元、3.42亿元、2.35亿元。连续涨停天数看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603213" code="603213">镇洋发展</a>已连收5个涨停板，连续涨停板数量最多。</p>
