<p>证券时报网讯，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601066" code="601066">中信建投</a>证券指出，火电板块至暗时刻已过，后续盈利能力有望触底回升。电力方面，我们仍然持续看好火电转型新能源运营的标的，包括<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600021" code="600021">上海电力</a>、华润电力和<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600483" code="600483">福能股份</a>，纯新能源运营公司建议关注<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600163" code="600163">中闽能源</a>。此外我们还推荐智能配电工程运维一体化企业<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300982" code="300982">苏文电能</a>，建议关注乌白电站有望注入提升公司盈利水平的水电龙头长江电力、受益于省内市场电价持续改善的<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600025" code="600025">华能水电</a>。天然气方面，我们推荐售气量不断提升，具备自有LNG接收站的城市燃气公司新奥能源、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601139" code="601139">深圳燃气</a>。</p>
