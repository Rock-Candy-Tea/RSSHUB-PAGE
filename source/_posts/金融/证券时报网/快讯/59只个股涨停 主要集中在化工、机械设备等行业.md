<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，今日可交易A股中，上涨个股有1063只，下跌个股有3335只，平盘个股74只。不含当日上市新股，共有59只个股涨停，41只个股跌停。以所属行业来看，涨停个股主要集中在化工、机械设备、电气设备、农林牧渔、食品饮料等行业。</p><p>涨停股中，从收盘涨停板封单量来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000537" code="000537">广宇发展</a>最受追捧，收盘涨停板封单有3823.13万股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ001215" code="001215">千味央厨</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600722" code="600722">金牛化工</a>等，涨停板封单分别有2059.63万股、1756.28万股。以封单金额计算，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ001215" code="001215">千味央厨</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600955" code="600955">维远股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000537" code="000537">广宇发展</a>等涨停板封单资金较多，分别有9.99亿元、5.48亿元、4.34亿元。连续涨停天数看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000537" code="000537">广宇发展</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ001215" code="001215">千味央厨</a>均已连收9个涨停板，连续涨停板数量最多。</p>
