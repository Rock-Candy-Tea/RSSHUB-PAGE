<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，3月28日，沪深两市可交易A股中，上涨个股有1782只，下跌个股有2744只，平盘个股143只。不含当日上市新股，共有73只个股涨停，39只个股跌停。以所属行业来看，涨停个股主要集中在房地产、医药生物、传媒、化工、建筑装饰等行业。</p><p>涨停股中，从收盘涨停板封单量来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600082" code="600082">海泰发展</a>最受追捧，收盘涨停板封单有4716.98万股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600156" code="600156">华升股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000514" code="000514">渝开发</a>等，涨停板封单分别有3313.99万股、3068.2万股。以封单金额计算，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603261" code="603261">立航科技</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603538" code="603538">美诺华</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603051" code="603051">鹿山新材</a>等涨停板封单资金较多，分别有7.41亿元、5.85亿元、3.31亿元。连续涨停天数看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600734" code="600734">*ST实达</a>已连收23个涨停板，连续涨停板数量最多。</p>
