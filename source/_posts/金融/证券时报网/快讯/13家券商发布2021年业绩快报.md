<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，截至2月17日，共有13家券商发布业绩快报。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600030" code="600030">中信证券</a>营业收入最高，去年营业收入765.70亿元，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601211" code="601211">国泰君安</a>位居其后，营业收入为426.69亿元。</p><p>净利润来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600030" code="600030">中信证券</a>去年净利润229.79亿元，盈利金额居首，其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601211" code="601211">国泰君安</a>，净利润为150.13亿元。增幅方面，公布业绩快报的上市券商中，净利润同比增长的有13家，增幅最高的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600958" code="600958">东方证券</a>，净利润同比增长97.27%。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202202/W020220217358088253563.png" title="公布业绩快报券商一览.png" alt="公布业绩快报券商一览.png" oldsrc="W020220217358088253563.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
