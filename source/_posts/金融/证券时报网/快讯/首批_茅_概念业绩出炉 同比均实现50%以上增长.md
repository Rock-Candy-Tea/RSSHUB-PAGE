<p>证券时报e公司讯，截至目前，已有8只茅概念股公告或预告三季报业绩，且同比均实现50%以上增长。具体来看，<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300896" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300896" target="_blank">爱美客</a>以144.09%的业绩增幅独占鳌头；<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603501" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="603501" target="_blank">韦尔股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002371" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002371" target="_blank">北方华创</a>随后，业绩均近乎翻倍；“券茅”<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300059" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300059" target="_blank">东方财富</a>增逾80%；<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002241" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002241" target="_blank">歌尔股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300124" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300124" target="_blank">汇川技术</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600763" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600763" target="_blank">通策医疗</a>幅度落在55%-66%区间；就连目前处于末位的“中药茅”<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600436" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600436" target="_blank">片仔癀</a>，业绩同比也有51.36%增长。(数据宝)</p>
