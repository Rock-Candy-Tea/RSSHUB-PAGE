<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，今日可交易A股中，上涨个股有3014只，下跌个股有1373只，平盘个股137只。不含当日上市新股，共有91只个股涨停，8只个股跌停。以所属行业来看，涨停个股主要集中在房地产、化工、汽车、机械设备、通信、电气设备等行业。</p><p>涨停股中，从收盘涨停板封单量来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002096" code="002096">南岭民爆</a>最受追捧，收盘涨停板封单有3018.17万股；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000671" code="000671">阳光城</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600090" code="600090">*ST济堂</a>，涨停板封单分别有2231.09万股、1698.02万股。以封单金额计算，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002096" code="002096">南岭民爆</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600566" code="600566">济川药业</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603893" code="603893">瑞芯微</a>等涨停板封单资金较多，分别有6.22亿元、2.5亿元、1.63亿元。连续涨停天数看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002096" code="002096">南岭民爆</a>已连收8个涨停板，连续涨停板数量最多。</p>
