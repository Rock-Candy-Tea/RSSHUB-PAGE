<p>证券时报网讯，证券时报·数据宝统计显示，截至目前，已有160家上市公司发布了2021年一季度报告，社保基金现身其中18股。持仓动向方面，一季度整体来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000933" code="000933">神火股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300636" code="300636">同和药业</a>获社保基金增持，新进<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603129" code="603129">春风动力</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600438" code="600438">通威股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002056" code="002056">横店东磁</a>3股。</p><p>从社保基金持仓市值来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600309" code="600309">万华化学</a>和<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600426" code="600426">华鲁恒升</a>位居第一和第二，分别为47.16亿和43.94亿，紧随其后是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601899" code="601899">紫金矿业</a>和<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600438" code="600438">通威股份</a>，均获社保基金持仓市值超10亿。同时，以上4股也是社保基金扎堆股。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202104/W020210419291853142867.png" title="一季度社保基金持股名单.png" alt="一季度社保基金持股名单.png" width="550" oldsrc="W020210419291853142867.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
