<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，近5个交易日（1月24日至1月28日）沪深两市约127家上市公司被机构调研。机构调研榜单中，共有54家公司获20家以上机构扎堆调研。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002050" code="002050">三花智控</a>最受关注，参与调研的机构达到328家；<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688561" code="688561">奇安信</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300253" code="300253">卫宁健康</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688058" code="688058">宝兰德</a>分别被272家、201家、192家机构调研。机构调研次数来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002594" code="002594">比亚迪</a>机构调研最为密集，共获机构4次调研。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300231" code="300231">银信科技</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300993" code="300993">玉马遮阳</a>等均被机构调研2次。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202202/W020220207341640262519.png" title="近5日机构调研股一览.png" alt="近5日机构调研股一览.png" oldsrc="W020220207341640262519.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
